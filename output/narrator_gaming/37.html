Ye Wuyu crouched there, a sorrowful expression on his face.

Because this time, there was only one jade pendant.

At the east entrance of the village, a large group of people had gathered.

Xu Fang sat silently to the side, his body covered in blood. Lin Qingqing was bandaging him, but he was still conscious.

Tu Su had also sustained serious injuries, and had just regained consciousness before fainting again, but at least his life was spared.

Only Bai Lu...

"What do we do? Crazy guy, can you save her? You were so amazing just now, surely you have a way to save her, right?"

"Yeah, yeah, crazy guy, think of something!"

"She's one of us from the village, we have to save her if we can."

A crowd surrounded Ye Wuyu, obviously impressed by his earlier display, where he had effortlessly dealt with that powerful person.

Such an amazing person, taken down by a simple gesture from Ye Wuyu.

Perhaps the "crazy guy" still had some powerful techniques that could save a life? Ye Wuyu's expression turned strange, and he patted the pale but beautiful face of the girl lying on the ground, then pinched her cheeks.

Afterwards, Ye Wuyu sat down on the ground, hands spread out.

He spoke bluntly.

"I'm the crazy guy, are you all idiots? How could I save someone? And this person is already ice cold, how could I save her?"

【No matter, the ignorant masses do not know that as a fox demon of the Azure Qiu royal clan, you have a life for each of your tails, you only need to wait a little.】

Ye Wuyu couldn't understand the commentary in his mind at all, nor did he bother to ponder why there was always a voice speaking in his head.

Because that was normal.

It was perfectly normal for a crazy person to have voices talking in their head.

Low sobs began to spread around.

When someone dies, crying is appropriate, it doesn't matter who is crying, as long as someone is.

Ye Wuyu rolled his eyes, curling up a bit, as he was very uncomfortable with this kind of atmosphere.

Staring at the Bai Lu lying on the ground, her chest pierced through, Ye Wuyu tilted his head.

Annoyed by the crying sounds, Ye Wuyu couldn't help but shout.

"Stop crying, stop crying! Let me try to save her, if I can't revive her, then you can cry."

The crying sounds quieted down, and Ye Wuyu recalled the incantation that Tu Su had used the day before.

If that half-dead chicken could be revived, then trying to save a person should be possible, right? Ye Wuyu thought for a moment, then his stiff fingers began to move.

Accompanied by a violent spasm in Ye Wuyu's body, everything then became dull and tasteless.

An invisible spiritual light shone upon Bai Lu's body.

【Yes, this is you, you had already calculated this step, stealing the beast taming inheritance was precisely for this moment, and from now on you will have an Azure Qiu royal clan fox demon as your guardian spirit.】

【The fox demon of the royal clan, each tail is a life.】

【And now, the life is yours.】
