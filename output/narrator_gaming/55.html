He handed Ye Wuyou a jade pendant, which was the key to getting out of the fifth layer of the celestial prison.

"Whatever you want to learn, he can teach you." These were the words left behind by Zhao Changhe.

Suppressing her emotions, Ye Wuyou opened the door and walked into the prison.

The locks were useless, at least for the existence of this layer, as the greater restraints came from the various silver needles and specially made chains on them.

There was nothing unusual in the prison, and the red-haired man was also sitting quietly on the bed, indifferent to Ye Wuyou's approach.

"Senior Fang, I am Ye Wuyou, and I..."

Ye Wuyou silently nodded.

"Where's the wine and meat?" Fang Cunshan asked.

"Huh?" Ye Wuyou looked at him, but saw the other's serious expression.

"Where's the wine and meat?" The same words were repeated.

Standing in the prison, Ye Wuyou didn't know why, but a nameless resentment arose in his heart.

"I didn't bring any wine or meat," he answered directly.

Fang Cunshan laughed, revealing his white teeth on his blood-stained face.

"No wine and meat? Then get the hell out of here," he said word by word.

The next moment, Ye Wuyou only felt his vision turn a bloody red, as if the surroundings had turned into a crimson mist, which then surged towards him like waves, and everything around him slowly rotated.

While he was at the center of the vortex.

His right eye emitted a strange light, and Ye Wuyou's figure trembled slightly, but soon regained his composure.

The red mist that ordinary people could not perceive was now slowly contracting and gathering around the red-haired man.

What had just disturbed his mind was not an illusion, nor anything strange, and it had nothing to do with his cultivation realm.

It was like a solidified aura of evil and killing intent.

Fang Cunshan sat on the bed, his cloudy eyes slightly less so, squinting as he eyed the young man in front of him.

Apparently, the fact that Ye Wuyou could regain his senses from that situation surprised him a bit.

But he's just at the first realm...

It's a bit interesting.

He was about to ask something, but saw the other had already walked out of the prison cell, and then didn't forget to close the door, his figure disappearing in the distance.

Leaving?

A trace of confusion and surprise appeared on Fang Cunshan's somewhat ferocious face.

He just left? What an unaccomplished thing, that old man Zhao Changhe must have misjudged.

He closed his eyes, and the fifth layer of the celestial prison fell into a strange silence again.

...

Ye Wuyou walked out layer by layer, and since Zhao Changhe had made arrangements, no one stopped him.

He gently touched his right eye, his eyes first pensive, but then slightly angry.

From the strange things in the celestial prison, to the fox spirit, the bell, and now this red-haired man, these people... how is it that every time something comes up, they have to disturb his mind?

Am I a public bus that anyone can just get on? But perhaps because he has been "boarded" too many times, and with his right eye, Ye Wuyou now has a certain resistance to the invasion of the mind.

Or is it just numbness? Ye Wuyou shook his head, it doesn't matter.

This person named Fang Cunshan, Ye Wuyou will definitely go find him again, but not today.

Today, there are other things to deal with.

Walking to the first layer, Ye Wuyou glanced around, but didn't see Uncle Wang.

However, there was a note left for him.

"The captain is back, I'll introduce you, we've already gotten the things and will divide them later, come have dinner at the Fuman Restaurant."

Captain?

What do I care about your captain? Ye Wuyou just remembered that Lin Qingqing's trio originally had one more person, who was not there before, otherwise he wouldn't have joined.

To be honest, he had no interest in their captain.

Others have fixed teams, and in the future, there will definitely be no shortage of people to do missions, and his hidden abilities, although many, are only at the first realm, so he will definitely be the one optimized out.

"Well, go have dinner, get the Jade Spirit Flower, and then give it to Lu Caiwi, it's also a way to make up for the previous incident with Xia Anmeng."

The Jade Spirit Flower can restore the spirit, and no matter what, although previously it was for the sake of survival, but by devouring the other's six-tenths of the soul force, causing the other to almost dissipate from the world, it was still somewhat wrong.

A private room at the Fuman Restaurant.

"Weiwei, you just take this Jade Spirit Flower, I've seen you searching for spiritual medicines to restore the soul, but now that you have it, you don't want it?"

Lin Qingqing was currently hanging on Lu Caiwi's body, her face buried in the neck and shoulder, greedily sniffing the other's scent.

Lu Caiwi sat upright in the main seat, her expression utterly cold, but she did not push away Lin Qingqing.

In her mind, she was still recalling the scene on the street just now.

Ye Wuyou and Lu Qingshan... one after the other went to that place...

She knew what that place was, and she also knew that it was normal for the man to go there...

But she was inexplicably unhappy.

Only to see three faces struggling to hold back their laughter.
