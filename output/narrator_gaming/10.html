"Ye Wuyu, are you even human?"

This sentence often marks the beginning of an argument or dispute, or a one-sided accusation.

But in this moment, it was just the literal meaning of the words.

Ye Wuyu had no mood or energy to deal with Xia Anmeng's craziness. He simply stared at the agonized expression on the woman beneath him, remaining silent.

"I have a technique called Soul Separation that can save my disciple..."

Hearing these words, Ye Wuyu's expression changed slightly. "Why didn't you say so earlier?"

The Soul Separation technique was something Xia Anmeng had used before. It was a high-level divine ability that only cultivators at the Xiantian Realm could perform.

As a remnant soul that had survived for hundreds of years, Xia Anmeng's mastery of spiritual powers was unparalleled. That was why she was able to split off a portion of her soul power and bestow it upon Lu Caiwai to resist the corrosion.

"But the prerequisite for this technique is that it requires a human to perform it," Xia Anmeng said calmly.

The scene fell silent for a moment, and Ye Wuyu almost burst out laughing from anger.

"You think I'm not human? Then what am I?"

"Bizarre," Xia Anmeng replied flatly.

Ye Wuyu was first stunned, then furious.

This pair of master and disciple were truly interesting.

First, the beautiful disciple pointed a sword at him and asked if he was even a thing. Now, this old hag who had lived for hundreds of years was asking strange questions, calling him bizarre.

Was it really that hard to be a human?

Xia Anmeng did not explain further. With a thought, the details of the Soul Separation technique appeared clearly in Ye Wuyu's mind.

Being able to see the bizarre, not fearing the corrosion, freely using others' divine abilities and techniques, and directly exposing one's own state...

Could such a person really be just a lowly prison guard who hadn't even reached the Foundation Establishment Realm? Moreover, the biggest doubt was that when Lu Caiwai first discovered him, his aura was completely gone.

Ye Wuyu's clumsy lies might have fooled Lu Caiwai, but they couldn't deceive Xia Anmeng, this thousand-year-old fox.

So she taught Ye Wuyu the Soul Separation technique.

After all, the bizarre is not human, not even a living being.

It has no spiritual soul!

Dare he use it?

Xia Anmeng waited with bated breath.

Ye Wuyu couldn't be bothered to argue with Xia Anmeng. He contemplated the technique in his mind.

Haha, he couldn't even understand it.

【You vile woman, to entrust the Soul Separation technique that only Xiantian Realm cultivators can perform to you, fearing that your spiritual soul will be annihilated. But you have such extraordinary natural talent, a mere Soul Separation technique, with a little modification, will become another extraordinary technique in the world.】

The complex technique gradually twisted and transformed, and new words appeared in Ye Wuyu's mind.

【Domination of Souls - Imprint of Life and Death】

This technique required the target to be still and unable to move.

He glanced at the trembling, agonized figure of Lu Caiwai beneath him, and silently muttered an "I'm sorry."

"Duang!"

Does it sound good? If it sounds good, then it's good.

Rubbing his slightly aching hand, without the slightest hesitation, Ye Wuyu began to perform the so-called improved Soul Separation technique.

This technique was clearly useful, as it had even elicited a dog's bark from the narrator.

As for it being called an improved, extraordinary technique in the world, Ye Wuyu couldn't see it. After all, he had no basis for comparison.

But he reckoned that if he could understand it, it must already be a remarkable thing.

Xia Anmeng watched the scene, frowning slightly.

He didn't ask her how to properly perform the technique, but instead knocked out his own disciple...So in the end, the bizarre one was still the bizarre one, after all.

But soon, she was stunned.

Because she clearly saw Ye Wuyu performing the Soul Separation technique.
