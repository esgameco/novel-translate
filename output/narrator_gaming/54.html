On the way back to the Heavenly Prison, Lu Qingshan and Ye Wuyou walked together for a stretch.

"Lu Qingshan, if I learn that divine art, what's the point of becoming your disciple?"

Ye Wuyou had already mastered the Heavenly Transformation Art, but he had not revealed it.

If others did not know the value of the divine art, how could Lu Qingshan not know?

To just take a glance and learn such a divine technique - if he were to openly admit to it, how would the other party think of him?

He knew Lu Qingshan might have some guesses, but as the latter had said, everyone has their secrets, and Lu Qingshan would not pry.

But the human heart is hard to fathom, who could guarantee anything?

Even if Lu Qingshan had no intentions towards him, what if others found out?

So he could only conceal this fact, and honestly prepare to go to the capital city later.

But for now, having learned the Heavenly Transformation Art, at least he no longer had to worry about the eerie corruption, and his life was not in danger.

Shouldn't he seize the opportunity to discuss the conditions?

Lu Qingshan softly said, "Not a disciple."

"Huh?"

"A successor."

Aren't disciple and successor the same thing?

Ye Wuyou pondered, but soon realized the other had diverted the topic.

"Lu Qingshan, you have to give me some hope, right? When old man Zhao wanted to take me as a disciple, he really made a big show of it, one good thing after another."

Lu Qingshan had his hands behind his back, walking slowly, while Ye Wuyou walked in front, occasionally stopping to urge him on.

Hearing Ye Wuyou's words, Lu Qingshan naturally knew what he meant, but his expression was only one of helplessness.

"If you truly can become my successor, I have the most complete library in the Great Yan for you to study..."

"No need, I don't like those things, I read the Spring and Autumn Annals."

What is the Spring and Autumn Annals? Lu Qingshan was slightly taken aback, but after thinking about it, he didn't know what to say.

This kid, how dare he negotiate conditions with me? But for some reason, he didn't feel averse to it.

Lu Qingshan's hand slowly opened within his wide sleeve, and a small black figure gradually materialized, then bowed deeply three times in succession.

Not just once like before, but three times in a row.

Huh... Lu Qingshan's expression became slightly surprised, then turned solemn and puzzled.

He had used the Heavenly Transformation to calculate Ye Wuyou's destiny.

The final scene was that Ye Wuyou would be unable to comprehend the divine art of the Heavenly Transformation, and ultimately settle for the divine art beneath the Heavenly Prison, but in doing so, he would also shed the eerie aura, living out his life as an ordinary Heavenly Prison guard.

So he had previously prevented Ye Wuyou from becoming his disciple, and actively asked if he was willing to accept another version of himself.

This was Ye Wuyou's destiny, and Lu Qingshan did not want to change it, for a normal cultivator to live peacefully in this relatively stable world would be a fortunate life.

But now, the previously clear destiny seemed to have undergone some changes.

His steps came to a halt.

"Lu Qingshan, why did you stop? I need to get back to the Heavenly Prison to complete my duties, and as my superior, you're the one slacking off here."

What is "slacking off"? The hand in the sleeve clenched slightly, and Lu Qingshan's face once again wore that carefree smile as he continued walking forward.

"Ye Wuyou, if you truly can master the Heavenly Transformation and become my successor, then you will be free to do as you please in this Great Yan."

This time it was Ye Wuyou's turn to stop in his tracks.

But he stopped not because of anything else, solely because Lu Qingshan's words had left him dumbfounded.

"Do whatever I want?" Ye Wuyou asked.

"Whatever you want," Lu Qingshan replied calmly.

This was really going overboard... Looks like Lu Qingshan was the real narrator here.

[Arrogant, there is actually such an arrogant person in this world, you sneer and directly mock him that you want to kill all under heaven, let's see if he dares to spout such nonsense.]

"Then what if I want to kill people?" Ye Wuyou's eyes flashed with a hint of coldness. He didn't mean anything by this, he just wanted to see how the other would respond to such a heretical statement.

"You can." Lu Qingshan's tone was serene, as if the words "kill people" were no different to him than stepping on an ant.

"Can I kill anyone?" Ye Wuyou asked, laughing bitterly.

Lu Qingshan nodded slightly, as if he had seriously considered Ye Wuyou's question.

"Except the one sitting in that chair, the rest are fair game," Lu Qingshan answered.

Lu Qingshan glanced at him, his footsteps uninterrupted.

"Well, not exactly, but..."

As if recalling something, Lu Qingshan joked.

"At most it's just a punch."

Ye Wuyou was stunned for a moment, although he was reluctant to admit it, but the other seemed to be telling the truth.

Lu Qingshan's expression became pensive, then he chuckled lightly and spoke in a level tone.

"A gentleman has his principles, and the word 'principles' is the dimension in which one acts. Kept in the heart, it becomes 'righteousness'. Ye Wuyou, I believe you will not become such a person."

Ye Wuyou had nothing more to say.

Arguing with a scholar was not a wise thing to do.

The two fell silent.

Until they reached a fork in the road just before the Heavenly Prison, Lu Qingshan stopped.

"After this month, I'll be going to the capital city again. You should come along, whether or not you can ultimately learn the Heavenly Transformation, comprehending the Twelve Divine Pillars will greatly benefit you..."

Seeing that Lu Qingshan was about to leave first, Ye Wuyou thought for a moment and asked.

"I want to quickly improve my cultivation level and learn divine techniques, is there any way?"

The eerie matter had been temporarily resolved, but Ye Wuyou had not forgotten that he was still just a novice at the first realm.

Although he had previously been able to slay even third-realm cultivators, that was while relying on the "Ghost" power. And most of the time it was through sneak attacks.

Without the unrestricted use of the "Ghost" spirit hand, Ye Wuyou, though reluctant to admit it, was indeed somewhat weak in his own strength.

The last desperate move of the man in the brocade robe had really opened Ye Wuyou's eyes, and he knew that every cultivator in this world had some hidden trump cards.

Hearing this, Lu Qingshan shook his head slightly, his expression calm as he spoke.

"Ye Wuyou, you've asked the wrong person, I don't fight."

Ye Wuyou's expression was a bit strange. He found that he could always hear some incredibly absurd words from Lu Qingshan's mouth.

You don't fight? Who was it that punched me into the wall in the Heavenly Prison before? But Lu Qingshan still pointed him in the right direction.
