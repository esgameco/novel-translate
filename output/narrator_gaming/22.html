The person in charge looked through the window at Ye Wuyou and then at the token in his hand.

"Is there anything wrong?"

"This task has already been taken, it's a Class B Group 5... oh, it should be called Class C Group 5 now."

Ye Wuyou: "?"

Of course he knew the task had been taken, and that they were just missing one person.

The task rank was not high, so it wouldn't be too difficult, and the reward was what he needed. It was perfect for him.

Seeing Ye Wuyou's puzzled look, the person in charge didn't say more, just commented "I see you don't know anything," and returned the token to him.

"Go, they are waiting in the third room on the right."

Xu Fang, formerly of Class B Group 5 but now demoted to Class C Group 5, was pacing back and forth in the small room, looking quite anxious.

Sitting beside him were two others - one with a pale, somber expression, currently resting with eyes closed.

The other was a woman, Lin Qingqing, wearing a black shirt, resting her face on one hand and looking slightly annoyed at the large man pacing.

Finally, after Xu Fang had paced back and forth about a hundred times, Lin Qingqing couldn't take it anymore and stood up, pointing at him angrily.

"Xu Fang, can you be quiet? You're just causing trouble without doing anything, it's driving me crazy."

The large man, as if finding an opponent, sneered and crossed his arms.

"I'm causing trouble? I'm trying to figure out a solution. We've had this task for a day and no one is willing to come, our reputation has fallen this low!"

"What reputation do you have!"

Lin Qingqing scolded furiously. "With that pig brain of yours, just being idle is the biggest help to us. Reputation? Do you even have one?"

"What do you mean I don't have a reputation? A month ago, who didn't know the name Xu Fang the Angry Gold Golem!"

However, Lin Qingqing seemed to have found his weakness, and immediately put her hands on her hips, laughing mockingly.

"Haha, you're just someone with more fame than ability. You want to know your reputation? It's that your fame exceeds your strength!"

"You, you, you..."

The burly man's face flushed red with anger, his Qi at the Second Realm involuntarily flaring up, looking furious.

Lin Qingqing puffed out her chest, not backing down.

The pale-faced man opened his eyes from his meditation, looked at the two, and sighed, as if he was already used to this.

Their Class C Group 5... things haven't been going well for them lately.

After all, a month ago, they were just promoted to Class B Group 5, and with that monstrous genius girl, they had accomplished remarkable feats.

Unfortunately, sometimes doing too much leads to too many mistakes.

A black bear demon disguised as a Fourth Realm injured, but actually a Fifth Realm, pretending to be captured, ultimately led to the incident at the Celestial Prison.

And their reputation has become utterly foul.

Although the Warden and everyone else said it wasn't their fault, the dead comrades and injured companions were still thorns in many people's hearts.

Without Lu Caiwi... they no longer qualified as Class B Group 5, and were demoted to Class C.

Who doesn't have a few good friends and companions? Having fallen to this state, they had already anticipated it in their hearts.

A knock on the door briefly interrupted the tense atmosphere in the room.

In an instant, all three turned their heads towards the door.

"Excuse me, is this Class C Group 5 that took the task of capturing a fox demon?"

Ye Wuyou looked at the room, his expression slightly puzzled.

For some reason, these three gave him the feeling that they were ready to fight each other even before going on the mission.

Combined with the strange words of the colleague who had assigned the task earlier, Ye Wuyou vaguely felt that this team was not quite right.

Seeing the three staring at him without speaking, Ye Wuyou thought for a moment and simply said "Excuse me."

But someone quickly...

"Bang."

The door was closed.

Lin Qingqing had now regained her composure. She stood in front of Ye Wuyou, leaning against the door with a faint smile.
