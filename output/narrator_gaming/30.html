Previously, this group of people in the prison had four members, one of whom was a woman...

Previously, it had indeed gone down the mountain to the village, not for any other reason, but because it had sensed a familiar aura.

The fool of the Qian Xuan Gate, that guy who was easily infatuated by a little trick.

At the time, the white fox was quite relieved, thinking that guy was probably the only remaining seedling of the Qian Xuan Gate, never expecting to encounter him again.

If he could be subdued, the chances of obtaining the true Qian Xuan Purple Gold Bell would increase significantly.

Unfortunately, the mantis stalking the cicada, with the yellow bird behind, his own sister also came, hiding her aura and learning the mystical techniques of humans.

The Qian Xuan Purple Gold Bell, what it wanted was not the bell itself, that was just a restraint set up by the Qian Xuan Gate people through mystical means.

What it truly wanted was the thing inside the bell.

The white fox had pondered for a long time, unable to break the restraint, so it had to hide it here, allowing the thing inside to quietly absorb the essence of the sun and moon.

The people of the Qian Xuan Gate did not dare to do this, keeping the bell securely locked up in a high place, obviously knowing that there was something extraordinary inside the bell.

This was a slow and steady process, the lifespan of a demon beast is very long, the white fox was in no hurry, wanting to quietly wait for the ancestral relic to be fully nourished, then the seal would naturally be broken.

But later on, everything became clear, the fox was mischievous, went down the mountain to gather and replenish, but was caught by someone in the brothel...

The white fox transformed, becoming a graceful and beautiful body, the black hair flowing down from the shoulders and neck, covering the slightly damaged area in the front.

The woman's expression was resolute, but at the moment, there was a hint of pain in her eyes, the hem of her pants was slightly rolled up, revealing a slender and pale leg bathed in moonlight, but with a few spots of crimson.

The white fox thought for a moment, deliberately adding a few more tears to the clothes, the damaged areas revealing a bit of skin, and a few fresh red lines.

"Lin Qingqing" finished these changes, then leaned back, half-leaning weakly against the tree, tilting her head slightly, with her hair covering half of her face.

The series of transformative actions were smooth and skillful, almost indistinguishable from the real Lin Qingqing.

In fact, it was even more graceful than the real Lin Qingqing.

"Hmph, flawless."

This was the fox demon's signature skill, the bread and butter of its livelihood.

Unless one surpassed the fox demon by several realms, even at the mid-Xian realm, they would not be able to see through the fox demon's disguise.

Ye Wuyu parted the withered branches, calmly gazing at the woman lying on the ground.

"Lin Qingqing" now had her eyes slightly closed, as if she had sensed someone approaching, and looked up with a pained expression.

Seeing Ye Wuyu, her expression first showed surprise, then quickly shifted to an appropriate look of delight.

She was truly delighted.

Earlier, she was too far away, and it was too dark in the mountain, so she couldn't see clearly.

Now that he was closer, she could see that this was a very handsome young man.

With such a handsome young man, the replenishment would be even quicker.

"It's you..." she said joyfully.

But that was all she could say.

She didn't know what Ye Wuyu's name was, and she didn't know what had happened to her earlier.

So she could only wait for Ye Wuyu to speak.

But Ye Wuyu didn't say anything, just standing in front of her, quietly looking at her.

Hey, this is not what she had imagined. In the night forest, with an injured and beautiful woman and a young cultivator, shouldn't there be a thousand topics to pour out their hearts?

In her gaze, the man's eyes were as calm as a dead pool of water, not even looking at the areas she had deliberately revealed.

But what hadn't she seen before?

I see, he's pretending, just keep up the act.

This must be the "innocent young scholar meets injured big sister" scenario.

Lin Qingqing wanted to get up, but after struggling a few times, a look of apology mixed with pain appeared on her face, and she said helplessly,

"Just now, I was careless and got injured, I'm afraid I can't walk anymore. Can you help me?"

"Okay." Ye Wuyu said.

"Lin Qingqing" seemed not to have expected Ye Wuyu to answer so decisively, and before she could react, she was suddenly embraced by him and then carried on his shoulder.

Hmm... this development.

She looked at the man's sturdy back, somewhat silent.

This is not normal.
