By the small river, next to the campfire, Xu Fang's snoring was as loud as thunder, sleeping soundly.

[Disturbing the mind, your snoring is louder than a pig! How can a cultivator be like a pig! You should just grab a watermelon knife and slaughter the pig right now!]

Sigh... Ye Wuyou lay by the tree, curling up.

Lin Qingqing was tapping her fingers, with a troubled look on her face, calculating something.

"I thought it would be just a few days, but in the end we ended up staying in that village for a week, lost four horses, and it will take two more days to get back. We only recovered the body of the demonic fox, this mission is not worth it at all."

Bai Changzai was lying beside her, though he should have been fast asleep, he now closed his eyes and said, "Well, as long as we can survive, that's good enough."

"You're not sleeping?" Lin Qingqing was surprised, but Bai Changzai didn't say anything more.

A rustling sound came from the side.

Lin Qingqing saw Ye Wuyou getting up from the ground, and just as she was about to say that he wasn't sleeping either, she saw the simple bandage on his face and the words got stuck in her throat.

"Your eye... I mean, when we get back to the Sky Prison, we can ask if any of the cultivators there have the ability to help you recover it?"

Her words carried a hint of apology.

She and the other two at the Qi Realm level had thought this was an easy mission, but Ye Wuyou was just there to make up the numbers.

In the end, they got lost in the village, and it was Ye Wuyou who led them out of the trouble, but he ended up losing an eye because of it.

Should she give him the entire reward for this mission? But no, the captain also needs the medicinal materials for healing spiritual wounds...

Hearing Lin Qingqing's words, Ye Wuyou didn't seem to mind, he just shook his head slightly and walked away.

"It's too noisy, I'll go for a walk."

"I'll go with you."

"And I'll go refill the water."

"Oh..."

[No, don't go, it's dark and windy, a lone man and woman, this won't end well.]

Damn, this is so awkward.

Sending Lin Qingqing away, Ye Wuyou walked through the small forest, following the sound of the stream to the riverside.

Standing by the water, he reached out and rubbed the bandaged side of his face, it was a bit itchy there.

What's going on? The strange sensation in his eye seemed to be getting more pronounced.

After hesitating for a moment, Ye Wuyou unwrapped the bandage.

Looking at his reflection in the water flow, at the black empty socket where his eye used to be, Ye Wuyou smiled wryly and tried to wrap the bandage back on.

But the cloth slipped from his hand and fell into the water, flowing away with the current.

Ye Wuyou stood there, his expression a bit off.

The lone remaining eye slowly looked down at his left hand, the hand that had just tried to grab the cloth.

The empty sleeve hung limply, swaying in the evening breeze.

Ye Wuyou's face finally showed a hint of surprise and bewilderment.

His left hand was gone.

But in the next moment, it wasn't just the left hand.

His hands, legs, waist, chest... all the way up to his head.

The man standing by the riverbank collapsed like a pile of building blocks, leaving only his empty clothes gently drifting to the ground.

...

"Huff, huff..." Ye Wuyou was now lying by the riverbank, his forehead beaded with cold sweat, his skin twitching as if in spasm.

He kept taking deep breaths.

The scene he had just witnessed felt like a dream, or something else. The cold river water splashing on his face brought a slight calmness to his mind.

But the next moment, Ye Wuyou's inner peace was shattered by what he saw in the reflection of the river water.

In the empty eye socket that had once been black, there was now a sinister red eye, the same demonic eye he had bitten and swallowed before.

A strange feeling came from Ye Wuyou's heart.

This eye, it was watching him.

Although it was now part of his own body, replacing the eye he had lost, Ye Wuyou could sense that this eye was looking at the river surface, looking at his own reflection.

As if to confirm his suspicion, the crimson eye even shifted, moving to the side.

Ye Wuyou immediately felt his vision also shift, no longer under his control.

Seeing this, Ye Wuyou quickly tried to regain control of his eyes, or rather, control of this one eye.

After a moment, the reflection in the river showed his eye blinking, and Ye Wuyou let out a sigh of relief.

He rolled the eye around, feeling that his vision was back to normal.

The "ghost" claw.

Now appearing on his own body?

But this ghost claw appearing on his own body, Ye Wuyu felt very uneasy.
