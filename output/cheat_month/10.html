Chapter 10: The First Time That Cannot Be Preserved

In a single day, shedding the mortal body and becoming a warrior!

Such a thing, even before coming to Heiyun Town, Huang Shiren had only heard of it, but had never seen it.

He even thought it was a rumor, hearsay.

Eh, now you've seen it.

"Clang!"

A precious sword hanging on the wall suddenly made a sound, and then the sword left its sheath, floating and killing towards Zhou Qing!

Spiritual sword control!

This is the ability of a soul cultivator, completely different from the methods of a warrior.

Zhou Qing also noticed this precious sword, his heart trembled, and he wanted to avoid it.

But the two ghost slaves suddenly became fierce, and there was also an invisible force rushing straight to Zhou Qing's head, making Zhou Qing appear dazed for a moment.

"Whoosh!"

The precious sword slashed across Zhou Qing's body, tearing his robe, blood spurting, and a deep wound appeared on Zhou Qing's body, with intense pain, Zhou Qing staggered and retreated a few steps.

Fortunately, he had just shifted his body slightly, otherwise the sword would have chopped on his neck.

"Just now that was... a mental impact?"

Before Zhou Qing could think more, Huang Shiren's sword and the two ghost slaves attacked Zhou Qing together again.

Zhou Qing hurriedly responded, but more wounds were added to his body.

"Hiss!"

The pain made Zhou Qing suck in cold air.

After Huang Shiren personally took action, Zhou Qing's situation immediately became extremely dire.

Although the two ghost slaves looked fierce, their attacks were actually straightforward. Even if Zhou Qing didn't have much combat experience, it wasn't a problem.

Confronting them head-on was fine, even random punches could beat an old master to death.

Anyway, with yang energy protecting his body, the two ghost slaves would be injured no matter what. In this way, the deficiency of lack of combat experience was temporarily concealed.

But facing Huang Shiren, Zhou Qing's disadvantage of lacking combat experience was fully exposed.

Moreover, Huang Shiren would give Zhou Qing a mental impact from time to time, adding insult to injury.

The Sensing Stage could resist the illusion power of the two ghost slaves, but it was difficult to completely defend against Huang Shiren, the ghost master.

But as the wounds increased and blood flowed out, a demonic intent suddenly rose in Zhou Qing's heart.

It seemed that a pair of ox demon eyes appeared in Zhou Qing's heart, and Zhou Qing could no longer feel pain, falling into a state of frenzy.

"You are a genius, with outstanding martial arts talent and strong will. If given time, even at the age of twenty-two, you may not be unable to become a martial arts master."

A cruel and excited smile appeared on Huang Shiren's face. His own talent was not good, but being able to strangle a genius made him feel happy.

While talking, Huang Shiren's attacks did not stop. Trash talk is trash talk, but killing must be done.

Zhou Qing was struggling to hold on, and the wounds on his body were increasing.

But Zhou Qing's coping level and combat experience were also improving at a visible speed.

Zhou Qing could indeed be called a genius, although it was created by artifacts.

Zhou Qing kept trying to approach Huang Shiren, but the ghosts blocked the way and the flying sword obstructed the path. Huang Shiren also kept changing positions.

Finally, Zhou Qing gritted his teeth. Damn it, if the magic weapon is exposed then so be it.

If he waited any longer, he would be chopped to death by this old pig dog.

With a thought, the pattern of the Three-Light Buddha Mirror appeared on Zhou Qing's right palm again.

Zhou Qing equipped this magic weapon that he had been hiding.

The scarce inner qi in his body was poured into the Three-Light Buddha Mirror. Although the energy was not compatible, it was still a kind of transcendent energy after all.

In an instant, Zhou Qing's body was enveloped by a layer of hazy white light.

At this moment, Huang Shiren's mental impact was like a stone sinking into the sea, and could no longer affect Zhou Qing.

Relying on the protection of the magic weapon's light, Zhou Qing's fists smashed into the two ghost slaves, beating them to wail continuously, retreating, and their figures became somewhat illusory.

The magic weapon infused with inner qi had much stronger power.

Then, Zhou Qing took a step forward, coming face to face with Huang Shiren. His right fist slammed towards Huang Shiren. In shock and anger, Huang Shiren thrust his sword towards Zhou Qing's fist.

Fist against sword, but at the same time, Zhou Qing kicked out, directly striking Huang Shiren's chest.

An ambush!

"Bang!"

Huang Shiren was sent flying by Zhou Qing's kick, crashing into the wall behind him, which collapsed from the impact.

A piercing pain shot through his right hand. The precious sword had pierced into Zhou Qing's fist, but not deeply.

The speed of a spiritually controlled weapon was extremely fast, making it difficult for Zhou Qing to dodge in such a small space.

The light of a magic weapon had an excellent defensive effect against forces like ghosts and souls, but its defense against physical weapons was somewhat lacking.

"Clang!"

The precious sword fell to the ground, and the two ghost slaves directly returned to the tattered small flag.

Huang Shiren looked extremely miserable, his body torn open, blood flowing everywhere. It was clear that he had already breathed his last.

Zhou Qing was stunned, the frenzied battle intent in his heart suddenly disappeared, and he became much more clear-headed.

Huang Shiren... died just like that?

Died at his hands?

He had killed someone?

His tightly clenched fist loosened, the light of the magic weapon slowly fading. Zhou Qing stared blankly at Huang Shiren's corpse, even temporarily ignoring the pain in his body.

Since learning that he had crossed over to such a world, Zhou Qing understood that sooner or later, there would be such a day.

But Zhou Qing hadn't thought that this day would come so quickly, only on the second day after his crossing...

Zhou Qing felt a bit conflicted in his heart. He didn't regret killing Huang Shiren, nor did he feel disgusted to the point of vomiting. He was unexpectedly calm, somewhat beyond his expectations.

But no matter what, he had ultimately lost his first time.

"Don't think too much, you were eliminating evil." A gentle voice spoke up, and someone patted Zhou Qing's shoulder.

Zhou Qing's body tensed, about to swing his fist to strike behind him, but his body was suppressed and he couldn't raise his fist.

Zhou Qing turned his head and saw his master's big face.

"Master, you... why are you here?"

Bai Tian put his hand down and said,

"Since I knew you were harmed by a villain, as your master, I naturally couldn't sit idly by."

"From the moment you left the martial arts hall, I secretly followed you."

"However, you did give me a surprise."

"Not bad, you are very outstanding, even more so than I anticipated."

Zhou Qing felt a warmth in his heart. He hadn't expected his master, as the head of the martial arts hall, to actually tail...

Ah no, to personally protect his disciple who had just been accepted today.

Previously, Zhou Qing's impression of this master was mysterious, and an extremely responsible martial arts instructor.

Directly imparting secret martial arts and devouring techniques, a ruthless martial arts teaching machine.

But now, in Zhou Qing's heart, his master had become even more vivid as a person.

Actually, Zhou Qing had long anticipated that someone would protect him after leaving the martial arts hall. He just hadn't expected Bai Tian to personally take action.

In the afternoon, while interacting with Bai Tian at the martial arts hall, Zhou Qing had already inquired about matters related to ghosts and those who control ghosts.

Bai Tian had also given a clear answer. The person controlling the ghosts to attack Zhou Qing must be a cultivator, but that cultivator's realm was definitely not high. After Zhou Qing tempered himself a bit more, he might be able to deal with them.

Bai Tian spoke with certainty. It was precisely because of Bai Tian's words that Zhou Qing followed those few big men to the Huang Residence. He had come prepared.

Otherwise, if someone asked Zhou Qing to come and he just recklessly agreed, do you think Zhou Qing is stupid?

It's just that Zhou Qing was now a bit worried. Would his master notice something from his use of the magic weapon?

What excuse should he make up this time?

"Zhou Qing, your background may not be simple."

"The power you suddenly erupted with just now seems a bit like the power of a bloodline," Bai Tian suddenly said.

"..."

Yes, Master, you are absolutely right.
