The Great Thousand Immortal Tree, with its mysterious origins, is incredibly ancient.

Zhou Qing was certain that visualizing the Immortal Tree was countless times better than visualizing the Yaksha.

Even if he said he would try to find a way to obtain a visualization picture from the officials during the day, Zhou Qing still didn't believe it could surpass this Great Thousand Immortal Tree.

The Great Qi government was very powerful, and they might have the legendary top-level visualization pictures.

But Zhou Qing definitely couldn't obtain those kinds of visualization pictures.

So this Great Thousand Immortal Tree was Zhou Qing's best and only choice.

If the visualization picture was inferior, then one would be at a disadvantage from the start, just like the difference in martial arts practice methods.

Could this Great Thousand Immortal Tree be inferior?

Of course not.

Standing in the dark space, Zhou Qing came to the closest spot to the Great Thousand Immortal Tree and began to meditate, visualizing this Immortal Tree.

Although the visualization picture in the Yaksha Visualization Method wasn't good enough, the knowledge recorded on it was universal and easy to understand. Zhou Qing was already very clear about the cultivation of the Visualization Realm.

In a daze and in a state of chaos, Zhou Qing felt as if he had become a tree.

Tree roots... tree body... tree trunk... tree leaves...

A tree with a shape almost identical to the Great Thousand Immortal Tree appeared in Zhou Qing's Soul Realm. The moment it manifested, a strange change occurred.

The gray mist surrounding Zhou Qing's Soul Realm surged, and then strands of gray mist poured into the "Immortal Tree". When they floated out again, they had become strands of transparent liquid lines.

These transparent liquid lines fell into the "puddle" at the center of the Soul Realm, strengthening the "puddle". At the same time, the originally colorful "puddle" gradually turned transparent.

This process continued for a period of time, and Zhou Qing's Soul Realm expanded a lot, and the "puddle" also grew.

At some point, Zhou Qing woke up, and the first thing he felt was that he was different.

It seemed as if he had entered another level of the world, leaving the mundane world behind.

[...]

[Soul Realm: Visualization Realm]

[Physical Realm: Flesh Realm (Initial)]

[Stamina: 2/2]

[...]

Opening the panel, two pieces of data had changed.

His soul cultivation had officially entered the Visualization Realm, and his stamina had recovered!

"Did I visualize for that long?"

Zhou Qing was a bit surprised. When he returned home, the sun had just set, but he didn't expect that when he finished visualizing, it was already past midnight.

But the result was gratifying; his soul had entered a new realm.

Zhou Qing looked at his Soul Realm and immediately noticed its expansion, and that "Immortal Tree" was so eye-catching.

Zhou Qing's Immortal Tree currently only had a rough shape and a hint of divine charm; it couldn't be as lifelike as the real thing.

The subsequent cultivation of the Visualization Realm was to perfect the visualized object in the Soul Realm until it was exactly the same as the original, possessing a complete divine charm.

At that time, the Visualization Realm would be perfected.

"Soul breakthrough, but stamina didn't increase. Is it only through martial arts breakthroughs, or does stamina only increase once per major realm? Or is it based on the cultivation path with the highest attainment?" Zhou Qing was a bit puzzled.

But this didn't affect him from entering the Immortal Tree secret realm and starting to chop the tree.

After two chops, two glowing orbs appeared in front of Zhou Qing.

[Rare Item: Chicken Blood Stone]

[Transformed by the power of the Immortal Tree, a stone soaked in the blood of the Eight Treasures Chicken. Cultivating while holding the Chicken Blood Stone can absorb the power of chicken blood, strengthening one's vitality and enhancing the physical body.]

[Weapon: Eagle Beak Spear (Fragment)]

[Transformed by the power of the Immortal Tree, a broken weapon. The spearhead has disappeared, leaving only the shaft, which is extremely hard.]
