A black and long shadow shot towards Zhou Qing like a sharp arrow, stirring up turbulent currents with extreme speed.

Zhou Qing's heart trembled as his spiritual power burst out from his body, striking the black shadow.

After a cultivator enters the state of visualization, with just a little practice, techniques like spiritual impact and controlling objects can be quickly mastered.

Under the spiritual impact, the black shadow slowed down for an instant, giving Zhou Qing reaction time.

Zhou Qing dodged to the side while the shaft of the Eagle Beak Spear appeared in his hand, fiercely smashing towards the black shadow.

"Whoosh!"

It actually dodged Zhou Qing's attack!

It was a black snake as thick as a baby's arm. Its scales were dark and glossy, its forked tongue flickering silently.

"Aquatic monster?"

Zhou Qing stared at the black snake. A snake that could evade his attacks was undoubtedly a monster.

"It seems you also have your eyes on this spirit plant..."

The black snake lightly swayed its tail and shot out again, its mouth open, fangs clearly visible.

Faced with the black snake's entanglement, Zhou Qing showed no intention of leaving.

Since he encountered this spirit plant, he definitely could not let it go.

In this section of the Cloud River near Black Cloud Town, there were no overly powerful monsters.

He still used the most basic soul attack technique, leading with a spiritual impact to affect the black snake's spirit.

Zhou Qing wielded the Eagle Beak Spear shaft with great prowess, but the black snake was simply too fast, dodging several of Zhou Qing's attacks. The environmental boost it received was too great.

Even with the Water Avoidance Pearl, compared to this kind of aquatic monster, Zhou Qing was at a great disadvantage in this environment.

However, Zhou Qing was very patient, not panicking or getting anxious. In the past few days, he had sparred with other senior disciples many times, accumulating some combat experience.

In the black snake's next charge, Zhou Qing unleashed his spiritual impact at maximum power, then actively used his hand to meet the black snake.

Seeing Zhou Qing not using the spear shaft and being affected by the spiritual impact multiple times, the black snake, with its low intelligence nearly gone, became extremely enraged and directly crashed towards Zhou Qing's hand.

I'll smash you to death!

A huge impact was transmitted from his hand, but delight flashed in Zhou Qing's eyes as he directly grabbed the black snake and brought the shaft down.

The black snake struggled frantically, but its stamina was far from its peak, and its speed had finally slowed.

There was no sound in the water, but the spear shaft solidly struck the black snake's body.

The black snake stiffened for a moment. Without hesitation, Zhou Qing landed a second blow.

The struggling force in his hand suddenly disappeared. The black snake was motionless.

Zhou Qing didn't understand much about monsters like the black snake, so to prevent it from faking death, Zhou Qing gave it a few more vicious hits, directly smashing the snake into several segments before feeling at ease.

Martial weapon fragments, if nothing else, were hard enough.

Are you stupid? I have Hundred Refined Gloves, what do you have?

Using your head to crash into a Hundred Refined weapon, don't tell me your snake head is also a Hundred Refined snake head?

Zhou Qing went to pick the spirit plant, then brought the several sections of the black snake's body and swam towards the river surface.

With the black snake dead, its blood spread out. Zhou Qing didn't dare linger in the river. If the smell of blood attracted other monsters, that would be terrible.

Most importantly, the glow of the Water Avoidance Pearl was already extremely faint.

Before Zhou Qing could even leave the Cloud River, the Water Avoidance Pearl lost its effect, and the cold river water slapped haphazardly on Zhou Qing's face.

Fortunately, he wasn't far from the surface, so Zhou Qing swam up by himself.

"Pah!"

Zhou Qing surfaced from the water, climbed up, his entire body already soaked through.

Wringing out the water from his clothes, looking at his fortuitous gain, Zhou Qing smiled.

He had obtained spirit plants before, but obtaining a spirit plant in this manner was a first.

Without delay, Zhou Qing prepared to return to the martial arts hall. But just then, three people saw Zhou Qing from afar and walked over in this direction.

Zhou Qing also noticed those three people and furrowed his brows.

They seemed to be... people from the Soaring Dragon Martial Arts Hall?

Zhou Qing had no intention of encountering them and prepared to leave from another direction.

However, Zhou Qing quietly re-equipped the Hundred Refined Gloves, and put one hand into the river, equipping the Eagle Beak Spear shaft under the water, then took it out.

Due to the game-like characteristics of the Immortal Tree Secret Realm, although storage spaces couldn't be made, dropped items could be taken out and used at any time.

Seeing Zhou Qing turn around and leave...
