"He actually broke through."

Several senior brothers and sisters surrounded Zhou Qing, pinching and touching him while expressing all kinds of sighs.

"Little junior brother has not only broken through in realm, his physique and inner breath can at least be compared to the peak of the Flesh Realm." Zhang Yuantao exclaimed in amazement.

"In other words, little junior brother has attained the strength of the peak Flesh Realm after practicing martial arts for seven days."

Yang Xing from the Soaring Dragon Martial Hall has been cultivating for four years and has only now reached the peak of the Flesh Realm!

In Black Cloud Town, this was already considered to be top-notch talent.

Comparing seven days to four years, the shock it brought to Zhang Yuantao and the others was earth-shattering.

"Looking at little junior brother's cultivation speed, I'm afraid his strength will quickly catch up to ours." He Feng laughed,

"Being a senior brother really puts on a lot of pressure. Looks like we need to work harder."

"Even if he ate spiritual plants every day and high-level monster beast meat every meal, practicing secret martial arts, he shouldn't be able to break through in seven days."

Bai Ruoyue was the most impacted and was very puzzled at the moment.

As for Bai Ruoyue's doubts, Zhou Qing could testify that it was indeed like this.

He ate spiritual plants almost every day, but before obtaining the Nine Aperture Golden Pill, he was still some distance away from breaking through.

Relying on spiritual plants alone, it was indeed impossible to break through in seven days.

But Zhou Qing had no way to explain this issue to Bai Ruoyue, so it could only be attributed to...

"Maybe I have extraordinary talent."

Bai Ruoyue glared at Zhou Qing, "Are you saying that as the eldest senior sister, my talent is not good?"

"Look, I just said a few more words and junior sister is already like this. Forget it, forget it, I spoke too much." Zhou Qing shook his head.

Bai Ruoyue raised her fist, "Are you looking for a beating?"

Everyone laughed and joked, the atmosphere was very lively.

Having a junior brother with outstanding talent was naturally something to be happy about. The relationship between martial siblings was very close, especially when the White Sky was still alive.

After completing today's martial arts lessons, Zhou Qing took his sixth senior sister Shen Yu and ran out, leaving Black Cloud Town and arriving at the side of the Cloud River.

"Little junior brother, can you really move freely underwater?" Shen Yu asked curiously.

"Of course, sixth senior sister will see in a moment."

Zhou Qing quietly equipped the Aqua Avoidance Pearl fragment.

But Zhou Qing was different, the Aqua Avoidance Pearl fragment allowed him to move as if walking on flat ground at the bottom of the water.

This virgin territory at the bottom of the water, rarely developed and inaccessible to other martial artists, Zhou Qing could fully explore.

With such a unique advantage, Zhou Qing naturally couldn't abandon it. Zhou Qing planned to come to the Cloud River every day in the future, as long as it didn't affect his own cultivation.

Shen Yu was a bit interested in this, so she followed Zhou Qing over to take a look, and also had the intention of looking after Zhou Qing.

What if he gets robbed again...

It was embarrassing to say, but Shen Yu was the youngest among the martial siblings, only sixteen years old, and yet Zhou Qing still needed this sixteen-year-old senior sister to look after him.

"Sixth senior sister, just stay here and don't move. I'll be right back."

With that said, Zhou Qing jumped into the river with a splash, then poked his body out and showed Shen Yu his dry clothes.

The dry Zhou Qing left Shen Yu so surprised that she couldn't close her mouth.

Afterwards, seeing that Zhou Qing hadn't come up for a long time, Shen Yu became a bit worried.

It's said that those who drown are good swimmers. Little junior brother is too good at this, could there be a problem?

While Zhou Qing had already dived a great distance, constantly observing his surroundings, not missing any place that might have spiritual plants growing.

At the same time, Zhou Qing was also very vigilant, guarding against any aquatic monsters that might appear.

The existence of aquatic monsters at the bottom of the Cloud River was precisely the reason why Zhou Qing decided to enter the Cloud River again only after his cultivation had broken through.

The risk of operating at the bottom of the Cloud River was still too great for someone who had just entered the Flesh Realm. The Black Profound Snake from last time was only equivalent to the early Flesh Realm.

Spiritual power spread out, revealing everything within a hundred meters in fine detail, making it convenient for Zhou Qing to search.
