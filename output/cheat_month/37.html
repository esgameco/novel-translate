Chapter 37: Further Teachings

Riding on a donkey and escorting a person, Zhou Qing and Yun Duo headed towards Heiyun Town.

"Zhou Qing, you're so amazing. None of the other martial artists I've seen are as good as you," Yun Duo said curiously, her steps light and quick. "That was Daoist magic. How did you break it?"

"It might be because martial artists have an advantage over Daoist priests," Zhou Qing said vaguely.

"Martial artists can overcome the Daoist magic cast by priests?" Yun Duo was puzzled.

The advantage martial artists have over priests refers to the suppression of souls by their yang energy and blood, but the Daoist magic cast by priests is different.

Not all Daoist magic is yin-based.

"Yun Duo, do you know about the art of animal transformation?" Zhou Qing changed the subject.

"Never heard of it," Yun Duo shook her head.

"Looks like we need to go back and ask Aunt Mo."

The wandering Daoist priest couldn't solve the art of animal transformation either.

Zhou Qing had carefully interrogated him and understood his situation.

This wandering Daoist priest was originally just a Contemplation Realm cultivator living in Tianyue County City.

But some time ago, someone approached him, helped him break through to the Out-of-Body Realm, and taught him the art of animal transformation, asking him to come to the Heiyun Town area to look for human-beasts.

In fact, the art of animal transformation is quite profound, and the wandering Daoist priest hadn't fully mastered it. To successfully cast it, he still had to rely on that wooden donkey figurine.

First, he would take the blood of a live donkey and smear it on the wooden figurine, then extract the soul of the live donkey and seal it in the figurine. After that, he could chant incantations to activate the magic and turn people into donkeys.

The wandering Daoist priest could only use the wooden figurine to cast the spell, but he didn't know how to undo it.

This wandering Daoist's skills were not even as good as Huang Shiren's back then. Huang Shiren at least knew how to control ghosts and had a soul sword.

Even the Out-of-Body Realm was useless. Under the scorching sun, souls that left the body would die even faster.

Who was behind him? That information couldn't be extracted.

Entering Heiyun Town, Zhou Qing and Yun Duo sent the dozen or so donkeys transformed from living people and the wandering Daoist priest to the garrison office, then headed straight for the peach grove to tell Lu Qingmo about the art of animal transformation.

"The art of animal transformation?" Lu Qingmo's eyes narrowed. "This is no ordinary Daoist magic. It actually appeared in Heiyun Town."

After saying that, Lu Qingmo closed her eyes. A few breaths later, she opened her eyes again, as if sensing something.

"It is indeed the real art of animal transformation, although somewhat incomplete, but it's not an ordinary illusion or delusion technique."

"Aunt Mo, can the art of animal transformation be undone?" Zhou Qing asked.

"It can be undone. Fortunately, this is not the complete art of animal transformation. Otherwise, if too much time passed, even gods and Buddhas couldn't save them, and they could only be donkeys for life."

Lu Qingmo said, "In the future, if you encounter similar situations, you can first give the affected person clear water to drink."

"If it's just an ordinary illusion or delusion technique, a trick to deceive the common folk, clear water can break it."

Zhou Qing listened carefully to Lu Qingmo's words, learning another useful piece of knowledge.

Lu Qingmo went into the attic and came out after a while, holding a yellow talisman in her hand.

"Burn the talisman paper into ashes, mix it with water, and feed it to those people. They will then regain their human form."

Water with added ingredients was needed to break the spell!

"Aunt Mo, isn't this art of animal transformation too terrifying? It can actually turn people into donkeys."

Transforming from one species to another, a true inside-out change, not just an illusion, was truly astonishing.

This was the magical Daoist magic.

"A complete art of animal transformation can do more than just turn people into donkeys. It can transform them into pigs, sheep, and other livestock as well."

Lu Qingmo shook her head, "However, this technique is extremely difficult to use on cultivators, so there's no need to be too afraid."

"Aunt Mo, there are still people behind that Daoist priest. If they find out we caught him..."
