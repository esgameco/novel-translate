"Scarlet Flame Divine Technique!"

The martial artist can directly achieve the realm of the Innate Grandmaster.

Unfortunately.

The powerful cultivator who created this Scarlet Flame Divine Technique only gained this enlightenment when he was about to pass away, so although he perfected the nine layers, he did not live to see the true qi self-generation.

So.

Strictly speaking.

The Scarlet Flame Divine Technique is not a transcendent martial art, but a top-tier martial art that directly points to the Innate Grandmaster realm.

"Grandmaster martial art!"

"This is a great surprise for me!"

An unstoppable smile appeared on Gu Qingfeng's face.

If all goes well.

Once he perfects the nine layers, he will have the opportunity to break through to the tenth layer of the Scarlet Flame Divine Technique, directly stepping into the Innate Grandmaster realm.

Gu Qingfeng believes that such an unparalleled technique that points directly to the Grandmaster realm is invaluable no matter where it is.

However.

Even with such an inheritance, Wen Jiao still chose to refine the evil entities into his body.

This also indirectly shows that the cultivation of the Scarlet Flame Divine Technique is arduous, and to truly perfect the nine layers, have the true qi self-generate, and step into the tenth layer to attain the Innate Grandmaster, is not a simple task.

What he currently lacks is a truly complete martial dao system.

With the Scarlet Flame Divine Technique at hand, this problem can be temporarily ignored before stepping into the Innate Grandmaster realm.
