Gu Qingfeng's expression changed slightly.

He was well aware that the imperial house of the Ji family had accumulated profound heritage over thousands of years, but he didn't expect them to have such profound resources.

Five grand patriarchs! This was an incredible concept.

Over the millennia, the true grand patriarchs who had appeared openly in the Nine Provinces were not many.

Now, the imperial house of the Ji family possessed five grand patriarchs, which showed the terrifying depth of their resources.

But upon further reflection, Gu Qingfeng felt that this was normal.

After all, the imperial house of the Ji family controlled the Nine Provinces. Even if there were immortal ancient families eyeing them hungrily, it was not entirely impossible for them to divert some resources to cultivate a few grand patriarchs.

"However, relying solely on these five grand patriarchs, it won't be that simple to change the current situation!" Gu Qingfeng mused.

The five grand patriarchs were indeed formidable, but now many immortal sects and ancient families were all eyeing the Nine Provinces. The possibility of this force, relying on the five grand patriarchs, successfully reversing the declining trend was not high.

However, the situation had reached this point.

The imperial house of the Ji family had no retreat.

If they did not expose their final trump card, the Ji family might be completely annihilated.

Therefore, regardless of the facts, these five grand patriarchs could not be hidden.

"Continue to keep an eye on the affairs in the Central Plains, and report any changes to me immediately!" Gu Qingfeng said indifferently.

The hidden guard immediately acknowledged the order and withdrew.

The appearance of the five grand patriarchs would inevitably push the situation in the Central Plains to a climax.

In Gu Qingfeng's view, the chaotic battle in the Central Plains would not take long to be completely resolved.

The only question was who would be the final winner.

But regardless, the imperial house of the Ji family was most likely not going to be that winner.

As the saying goes, the five grand patriarchs were indeed not weak.

But which of these immortal sects did not have profound resources?

Relying on the five grand patriarchs to block these ancient families was nothing but a joke.

Unless...

The imperial house of the Ji family had even more powerful resources, then it would be a different story.

However, in Gu Qingfeng's view, the current situation in the Central Plains also had some benefits.

After all, the imperial house of the Ji family had exposed such a trump card, which would inevitably attract the attention of all parties again, making Gu Yang's actions much easier.

Until they controlled the Qingyun State, they would have the qualifications to truly compete for the world.

With this thought, Gu Qingfeng continued his closed-door cultivation.

For him, there was nothing more important than improving his own strength at the moment.

"The five grand patriarchs of the imperial house of the Ji family, no wonder the Taixuan Dynasty was able to dominate the world for three thousand years, this kind of depth is probably beyond even the Ji family's own expectations!"

In the Southern Yang Commandery, when Ancient Cloud learned of this news, his expression was also filled with deep sighs.

As the top sect left behind by the Martial Ancestor, the Shenwu Sect currently did not have a single grand patriarch in existence.

Looking at the imperial house of the Ji family, they could produce five grand patriarchs at once.

From this, it was clear that the Shenwu Sect had indeed fallen into serious decline.

But this was an unavoidable situation.

Nominally the hegemon of the Wuzhou, but in the current world where Taoists were revered, the Shenwu Sect actually had little say in Wuzhou.

So the foundation of the Shenwu Sect could not be compared to the imperial house of the Ji family.

However, upon truly learning that the imperial house of the Ji family could produce five grand patriarchs, Ancient Cloud's heart could not help but be shaken.

As a swordsman who had already broken through to the grand patriarch realm, he was also slightly moved at this moment.

"Five grand patriarchs, if I could fight them, it would be a wonderful thing!"

After breaking through to the grand patriarch realm, the swordsman also wanted to have a battle with other grand patriarchs to test his own strength.

However, the swordsman was also aware that if he really went to the Central Plains, he might be suppressed by other immortal sects and ancient families before he even had the chance to fight the grand patriarchs.

So, this was just a thought for the swordsman.

Ancient Cloud smiled and said, "Senior Swordsman's cultivation is profound, having already reached the grand patriarch realm, you should be ranked among the top experts in the world, not inferior to the grand patriarchs of the Ji family.

And now the King wishes to compete for the world, in the future there will surely be many strong figures to obstruct him, so Senior need not worry about lacking opponents!"

Regarding this veteran expert, the swordsman, Ancient Cloud addressed him as a junior.

Not to mention that the swordsman was already a grand patriarch, while Ancient Cloud himself was only a half-step grand patriarch.

Just in terms of age alone, the swordsman was much older than Ancient Cloud, so the latter had to show him proper respect.

"Ancient Sect Master's foundation is deep, in my opinion, you should be able to break through to the grand patriarch realm in not too long."

Hearing Ancient Cloud's words, the swordsman looked at him and smiled slightly.

Ancient Cloud shook his head: "To break through to the grand patriarch realm, how easy is that!"

Although he was already a half-step grand patriarch, with only half a step left to the true grand patriarch realm, this half-step could still block him for the rest of his life if he was unlucky.

Throughout history, many brilliant experts had been stuck at the half-step grand patriarch realm, never able to take that final step and attain the grand patriarch realm.

This was also one of the reasons why many experts in their later years chose to cultivate demonic arts.
