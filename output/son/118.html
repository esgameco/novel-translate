Ten boxes of gold.

Each box of gold is roughly around two thousand taels.

Ten boxes of gold.

That would be twenty thousand taels.

Furthermore,

there are three boxes of martial arts manuals.

The Divine Fist Sect has been passed down for thousands of years, and has produced two eminent sect masters, which can be considered a top-tier major sect.

Therefore,

in terms of martial arts, the Divine Fist Sect is naturally not lacking in any way.

The three boxes of martial arts combined contain a total of several hundred volumes.

However,

when Gu Qingfeng briefly glanced through them, he found that most of these martial arts involve refining skin and refining blood, but once it comes to refining bones and above, the number of martial arts drastically decreases.

It can be seen that,

even with the foundation of the Divine Fist Sect, high-grade martial arts are not abundant.

Of course,

even though high-grade martial arts are not abundant, there are still a full twenty-six of them.

If Gu Qingfeng had not yet broken through to the Perfect Bone Refinement stage, then these twenty-six high-grade martial arts would be extremely important to him.

But now -

High-grade martial arts no longer have much use for him.

Perfect Bone Refinement!

The reason why the word "perfect" is used,

is to indicate that this stage cannot be further improved upon.

Even if Gu Qingfeng were to cultivate hundreds or thousands of high-grade martial arts in the future, they would not bring him any additional enhancement.

This,

is the difference between Perfect Bone Refinement and ordinary Bone Refinement.

However,

in addition to the high-grade martial arts, there are also a few supreme-grade martial arts and transcendent martial arts involving the refinement of organs.

Among them,

there are six supreme-grade martial arts,

three transcendent martial arts,

and -

one Patriarch-level martial art.

"The Prajna Sutra!"

"This is a martial art that directly points to the Patriarch-level!"

Gu Qingfeng's brow slightly raised.

This is the first true Patriarch-level martial art he has obtained.

Although the Crimson Flame Divine Skill can also allow one to break through to the Patriarch-level, in terms of grade, it is naturally not as good as the Prajna Sutra in front of him.

This is because the Prajna Sutra not only can allow one to break through to the Xiantian (Transcendent) realm, but it also directly points to the peak of the Xiantian realm.

This martial art is divided into thirteen levels.

The first nine levels are cultivation methods for those below the Patriarch-level.

The last four levels involve the complete Xiantian realm.

From the initial entry into the Xiantian realm to the peak of the Xiantian realm, the Prajna Sutra has comprehensive records.

It can be said that,

just based on this,

the Prajna Sutra is much more precious than the Crimson Flame Divine Skill.

Of course,

everything has two sides.

Although the Prajna Sutra has a higher upper limit, the difficulty in breaking through to the Patriarch-level is undoubtedly simpler with the Crimson Flame Divine Skill.

Afterwards,

Gu Qingfeng looked at his attribute panel.

...

Prajna Sutra (Not yet Learned)!...

This martial art has already been completely recorded in his attribute panel.

At this moment,

Gu Peng's gaze signaled the others to withdraw, then he took out a black iron box from his bosom, which seemed to be airtight, with only a small hole on top, seemingly for inserting a key.

"This is something that I found on the highest level of the Divine Fist Sect's library, where there were no extra martial arts, only this box.

I feel that this box is extraordinary, but unfortunately I couldn't find the key, so I've brought it for the Patriarch to take a look!"

Hearing this, Gu Qingfeng's expression remained unchanged as he took the box and examined it carefully for a moment, then used two fingers to forcefully pinch it, but found that the box did not budge at all.

Such a degree of toughness is enough to show the extraordinary nature of the black box. After all, Gu Qingfeng's cultivation has reached a transcendent and saintly level, and even the strength of his two fingers is heavier than a thousand catties, able to crush stone into powder with ease.

The secret technique of cultivating evil into the body.

In terms of preciousness, they are not inferior to the Prajnaparamita Sutra.

The Thirteen Layers of the Prajnaparamita Sutra.

If he could comprehend them, his strength could advance further.

Moreover.

As the pinnacle of the innate supreme art, each step of the foundation building, even if not perfected, should be extremely close to it.

In this way.

Gu Qingfeng's subsequent perfect marrow cleansing and perfect organ refinement would also become much easier.

Gu Qingfeng's entire blood energy was self-adjusting, and the difficulty of cultivating the corresponding level of martial arts with his perfect physical body was suddenly greatly reduced.

In less than a moment, Gu Qingfeng had successfully entered the first layer of the Prajnaparamita Sutra.
