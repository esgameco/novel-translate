The wind was cultivating the Heavenly Thunder Sword Technique.

The weapon in his hand was an extraordinary divine weapon, called the Heavenly Dipper Sword, which was a mid-grade divine weapon.

Mid-grade divine weapons were rare.

At least they were considered rare in the White Stone Dao.

This divine weapon originally belonged to the Iron Bone Gang, but after the Iron Bone Gang was destroyed, Gu Qingfeng found this divine weapon in their treasury. Perfect.

Because Gu Yang had broken through the Refine Skin realm, Gu Qingfeng obtained a mid-grade martial art called the Heavenly Thunder Sword Technique.

This mid-grade martial art was at the pinnacle level at this stage, much stronger than the ordinary mid-grade martial arts, on par with the Scorching Sun Divine Palm.

The difference was that the Scorching Sun Divine Palm was a cultivation of both internal and external aspects.

Not only could it temper one's qi and blood, improving the cultivator's attainment, but it also had a matching set of techniques. As for the Heavenly Thunder Sword Technique, it was completely focused on the techniques.

In terms of the exquisiteness of the techniques, it was even more advanced than the Scorching Sun Divine Palm.

Since obtaining the Heavenly Thunder Sword Technique, Gu Qingfeng had been constantly contemplating this supreme skill.

Unfortunately, the Heavenly Thunder Sword Technique was profoundly mysterious, and Gu Qingfeng had not been able to truly comprehend it so far.

Regarding this, Gu Qingfeng was not too concerned.

He was aware that his natural talent was not high, and his comprehension was not particularly strong. For him to master such a mid-grade martial art was naturally not an easy task.

Additionally, this time from the Chu family and the Iron Bone Gang, he had also obtained several other mid-grade martial arts, but Gu Qingfeng had no intention of cultivating these mid-grade martial arts for the time being.

After all, the essence of martial arts lies not in quantity, but in quality.

He had previously cultivated other low-grade martial arts mainly because his original martial art had reached a bottleneck and could no longer progress. But now, with the Heavenly Thunder Sword Technique still not mastered, cultivating other mid-grade martial arts would be a bit too greedy.

Furthermore, Gu Qingfeng had also passed on the complete Gu Family Fist to Meng Peng during this period.

Not only that, he had also passed on the Gu Family Fist to some of the guards who had joined the Gu family for a longer time and were more loyal.

With numerous mid-grade martial arts in hand, Gu Qingfeng no longer needed to cling to the Gu Family Fist alone.

The most important thing was that the Gu family now needed a sufficient number of powerful cultivators.

Otherwise, the vast Gu family would only have Gu Qingfeng at the half-step Refine Bone realm, and the rest were at the Refine Blood realm, which was simply not enough.

For this reason, Gu Qingfeng also bestowed ten Refine Blood pills to Meng Peng, allowing him to break through to the Refine Blood realm.

The remaining Refine Blood pills were mostly given to Gu Yang to accelerate his breakthrough to the Refine Blood realm.

As for himself, Gu Qingfeng rarely consumed Refine Blood pills for cultivation anymore.

Having reached the half-step Refine Bone realm, the effect of Refine Blood pills was limited.

Moreover, with the characteristics of the Scorching Sun Divine Palm, Gu Qingfeng could naturally advance to the Refine Bone realm without the need for any pills.

Therefore, it was better to use the Refine Blood pills to cultivate more powerful members of the Gu family.

Among them, Gu Yang was the main target of Gu Qingfeng's cultivation efforts.

Whether Gu Qingfeng could break through to higher realms depended to a large extent on Gu Yang.

In the blink of an eye, three days had passed.

Meng Peng came walking in from outside.

"Your subordinate pays respects to the family master."

Gu Qingfeng looked at the changes in Meng Peng's aura and saw the gleam in his eyes.

"You've broken through to the Refine Blood realm?"
