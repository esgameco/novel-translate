"The post-Grand Master realm, I have finally attained it!"

With the Blood Refining Sutra breaking through to the fifth realm, entering the ranks of Grand Master martial arts, his cultivation had also advanced, reaching the post-Grand Master realm.

This seemingly small breakthrough in realm, for Gu Qingfeng, represented a tremendous change in his strength.

Gu Qingfeng could now see the terrifying blood energy within him, even a single drop of ordinary blood was as heavy as a thousand catties.

Crushing mountains and splitting peaks

"What a pity!"

"There are no truly powerful experts in the Nine Provinces worthy of my full strength, otherwise I could see the true limits of my current abilities."

Gu Qingfeng's expression shifted slightly.

He continued to ponder the Blood Refining Sutra.

This martial art was extraordinary.

Gu Qingfeng had only reached the fifth realm of the eight-realm Blood Refining Sutra so far.

If he could cultivate the Blood Refining Sutra using his own abilities, he could save a lot of upgrade points.

Gu Qingfeng's cultivation breakthrough did not cause any commotion.

Advancing from the mid-post-Grand Master realm to the late-post-Grand Master realm, Gu Qingfeng did not show much outward change, and he would not openly boast about the growth of his own strength.

Everything returned to normal.

In addition to closed-door cultivation of the Blood Refining Sutra, Gu Qingfeng also guided Gu Shengnan, as well as Gu Xuan and Gu Xiu.

Perhaps aware that his young niece had broken through to the Blood Realm Perfection before the age of eight, Gu Xuan and Gu Xiu, although still young, also felt a sense of urgency, so they were very diligent in their cultivation.

Gu Xuan and Gu Xiu were a generation older than Gu Shengnan, with Gu Xuan being thirteen years old and Gu Xiu just over seven, the gap was quite significant.

Young people were always concerned about their reputation.

Moreover,

Gu Xuan and Gu Xiu had a reputation as geniuses since childhood in the Gu Family Manor, if they were surpassed by Gu Shengnan, it would undoubtedly be embarrassing.

Therefore,

The two of them became much more diligent in their cultivation.

As a result,

In just half a year, the two of them had successively broken through the Blood Realm Perfection and entered the Bone Refining Realm.

Gu Qingfeng looked at the information panel, his expression slightly surprised, then he shook his head and smiled.

"It seems I was too lenient in the past. Now that Shengnan is about to break through the Bone Refining Realm, it has put some pressure on them. But this is good, with pressure comes motivation.

Otherwise, with such abundant resources, it would be a complete waste!"

To be honest,

Gu Qingfeng did not expect that Gu Shengnan's displayed talent would put so much pressure on Gu Xuan and Gu Xiu. However, this was a good thing, as the two of them now had some pressure, at least they would not be as lazy as before.

The next day,

In the Gu Family Manor's main hall,

Gu Xuan and Gu Xiu came forward.

"Greetings, Father!"

"Speak, what is it?"

Gu Qingfeng glanced at the two of them. Now that they were at the age of scholarly pursuits, their physiques were much more robust than their peers.

The two of them had three parts resemblance to Gu Qingfeng and seven parts resemblance to Xu Yulian in their appearance. Their looks were not bad at all.

The difference was that Gu Xuan wore white robes, giving him the aura of a graceful young gentleman, while Gu Xiu was burly and robust, and even as a youth, one could sense a trace of fierce energy about him.

"Father, we two have recently broken through to the Bone Refining Realm, so we have come to ask Father to teach us some new martial arts!"

The two of them exchanged a glance, and it was Gu Xuan who spoke.

"New martial arts?"

Gu Qingfeng raised his brow.

"What do you want to learn?"

"I've heard that Father was once known as the Tyrant Saber in the jianghu, and your saber techniques have reached the realm of mastery, so I hope Father can pass on the Heavenly Saber technique to me."

Gu Xuan unhesitatingly expressed his wish.

Gu Xuan was not unfamiliar with Gu Qingfeng's reputation in the jianghu. And since he was particularly fond of saber techniques, he naturally hoped to receive Gu Qingfeng's personal instruction.

As for this,

Gu Qingfeng did not answer, but instead looked at Gu Xiu.

"What about you? What do you want to learn?"

"I want to learn the fist technique!"

Gu Xiu said in a deep voice.

Hearing this,

Gu Qingfeng nodded slightly: "As for the fist technique, I have a Grand Master martial art passed down from the Divine Fist Sect, the legendary Suzerain Technique of the Sun and Moon Fist.

If you can cultivate this fist technique to perfection, you can also refine the ultimate essence of the true meaning, and at the same time temper your own physical constitution and blood qi, it is truly profound and extraordinary!"

With that, Gu Qingfeng raised his hand and pointed, a surge of mighty true essence directly entered Gu Xiu's mind.

In an instant,

Gu Xiu felt the scene before him change, and suddenly the sun and moon appeared in the sky, the majestic celestial might pressing down, and he saw a figure of unparalleled prowess punching the mountains and rivers, truly worthy of being called the best under the heavens.

Immediately after,

Before Gu Xiu could react, a flood of memories surged forth, all related to the cultivation methods of the Sun and Moon Fist Technique.

After a long time,

Gu Xiu finally emerged from this torrent of memories, his eyes filled with awe.

"The Sun and Moon Fist Technique!"

He was both shocked by Gu Qingfeng's unimaginable methods, and amazed by the power of the Sun and Moon Fist Technique.

Although the information about the Sun and Moon Fist Technique had disappeared from the attribute panel, it did not mean that Gu Qingfeng had forgotten it.

On the contrary,

Gu Qingfeng's mastery of the Sun and Moon True Essence and various other supreme martial arts had never been lost, it just no longer appeared on the attribute panel.

The Sun and Moon True Essence was fused and forged into the creation of the Heavenly Saber technique, but the Sun and Moon True Essence was still under Gu Qingfeng's control.

At this moment,

Gu Qingfeng displayed the fully perfected Sun and Moon True Essence before Gu Xiu.

This peerless fist technique left the other party deeply immersed in it.

"I have passed on the Sun and Moon Fist Technique to you, but whether you can truly master this martial art depends on your own natural talent."

With that, Gu Qingfeng casually handed Gu Xiu a master's manual obtained from the Divine Fist Sect.

"This is a manual left behind by a master of the Divine Fist Sect, with a trace of master-level true essence remaining, which can be of some benefit to the cultivation of the Sun and Moon Fist Technique.

When the true essence in this master's manual is exhausted, or when you formally enter the Sun and Moon Fist Technique and cultivate the true master's essence, then come find me again."

"Thank you, Father!"

Gu Xiu firmly grasped the master's manual, his expression filled with excitement.

The power of the Sun and Moon Fist Technique was the ultimate fist technique he had always dreamed of.

Although Gu Xiu knew that Gu Qingfeng was renowned for his saber techniques, in the way of martial arts, it was not a matter of just cultivating the strongest technique, but whether it truly suited oneself.

Like the saber technique, Gu Xiu did not particularly like it, even if he were to obtain Gu Qingfeng's true saber technique transmission, his future accomplishments would be limited.

During his years in the Gu Family Manor, Gu Xiu had often received guidance from masters and discussed martial arts with others, and he already had his own unique perspective on the Dao of Martial Arts.

This level of martial arts insight was beyond the reach of ordinary jianghu cultivators.

"Cultivate well, and do not neglect your own cultivation, for the foundation of a martial artist lies in their cultivation."

Gu Qingfeng added a reminder.

To this,

Gu Xiu nodded in acknowledgement.

Thereafter,

Gu Qingfeng turned his gaze to Gu Xuan.

"You want to cultivate the saber technique, and I do have a few saber techniques, but among the Grand Master and above saber techniques, there are only two - the Tyrant Saber and the Heavenly Saber. Which one do you wish to cultivate?"

"May I ask Father, which of the two saber techniques is the strongest?"

Gu Xuan asked seriously.

Gu Qingfeng smiled faintly: "The Tyrant Saber Slash was a supreme Grand Master saber technique created by past jianghu masters. If cultivated to perfection, it can also reach the pinnacle of the Dao, like the Sun and Moon Fist Technique.

As for the Heavenly Saber, that is a truly supreme saber technique that I created based on various martial arts.

If you ask which saber technique is the strongest, then the Heavenly Saber would be the most powerful!"

Hearing this,

Gu Xuan unhesitatingly said: "I hope Father can pass on the Heavenly Saber technique to me!"

"Good!"

Gu Qingfeng did not hesitate, directly pointing out, and a terrifying Heavenly Saber true essence burst forth, instantly surging into Gu Xuan's mind.

This true essence power was at least ten times stronger than the Sun and Moon True Essence.

It took a full hour before Gu Xuan finally emerged from this torrent of true essence.

This vast amount of memories flashed through his mind like a panoramic view.

Although the Refine Blood Scripture had advanced, unfortunately, he still had not reached the pinnacle of the Grand Master Realm.

It seems that the two-level advancement of the Refine Blood Scripture was not enough to propel Gu Qingfeng's cultivation to a higher realm.

In his view, even if the Refine Blood Scripture reached the eighth level, whether it could truly break through his cultivation remains a question.

However, although there was no breakthrough in his cultivation, the growth in his strength was very real.

This change satisfied Gu Qingfeng greatly.
