In the dense forest valley, a military camp had been established.

This was a place that Gu Yang had secretly found and used for training the soldiers.

At this moment, there were 100,000 troops in this military camp.

Gu Yang stood on the command platform, looking at the 100,000 troops below, the silent and deadly aura permeating the area, igniting a heroic spirit within him.

This was Gu Yang's foundation.

And the capital for his future conquest of the world.

All the soldiers who had entered the military camp were carefully selected by Gu Yang. They had some talent in the martial dao, and the weakest among them had reached the Refining Skin Realm.

Some of the stronger ones had even stepped into the Refining Blood Realm.

Not to mention anything else, just the 100,000 Refining Skin Realm martial artists alone would be a terrifying force.

After all, many soldiers only had some martial prowess without truly entering the martial dao realm. 100,000 troops who had entered the martial dao, this kind of power would be considered elite no matter where they were placed.

"My fellow soldiers, the 100,000 Divine Martial Army is now marching towards the White Stone Path. As the saying goes, 'Cultivate an army for a thousand days and use it in a single moment.' Now is the time to test the fruits of our efforts!"

"For each enemy head you slay, you will be rewarded with 10 taels of silver. Slay 10 enemies, and you will be promoted to a Centurion and rewarded with 100 taels of silver, as well as a high-grade martial art. Slay 100 enemies, and you will be promoted to a Chiliarch and rewarded with 1,000 taels of silver, as well as an exceptional martial art."

10 taels of silver for one enemy head! 100 taels of silver for 10 enemy heads!

1,000 taels of silver for 100 enemy heads!

Many could already see the shimmering silver in front of them, not to mention the additional rewards of promotions and top-tier martial arts.
