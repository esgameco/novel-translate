Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The evil spirit revived, suddenly letting out a roar full of cruelty. Immediately after, the shadowy figure surged towards the shadow under Gu Qingfeng's feet like a tidal wave.

This was the ability of the shadowy figure.

It could hide within the shadows of others, and even manipulate them, turning them into its puppets.

However, Gu Qingfeng would not let the shadowy figure have its way.

Given the bizarre nature of the evil spirit, even though Gu Qingfeng was confident in suppressing the shadowy figure, he would not be careless.

Gu Qingfeng stomped his right foot on the ground, the powerful force causing the massive Zhenmo Prison to shake violently. Terrifying vital energy erupted, and the shadowy figure that was about to invade Gu Qingfeng's shadow suddenly let out a piercing, agonizing scream upon touching that scorching vital energy.

The shadowy figure retreated, fleeing towards the outside of the Zhenmo Prison.

"You think you can escape?" Gu Qingfeng sneered disdainfully, his Demon-Slaying Blade suddenly unsheathed, directly pinning the shadowy figure's body to the ground.

The vital energy power within the blade fell upon the shadowy figure, just like the spring sun melting the snow, causing the latter's body to gradually dissolve as it struggled and twisted, letting out one agonizing wail after another.

After a moment, the sound gradually faded away. On the ground, there was only a bit of black powder left - the existence of the shadowy figure had completely vanished.

"The evil spirit is dead!!" Gu Qingfeng's pupils suddenly constricted.

Even though he had anticipated this, when he actually witnessed the shadowy figure disappearing before his eyes, Gu Qingfeng still found it somewhat unbelievable.

The process of eliminating the shadowy figure had been so smooth, without any complications.

But soon, Gu Qingfeng felt at ease.

His level of cultivation had already undergone a transformation, no longer comparable to the past. The reason why the evil spirit was so difficult to destroy was that its level of cultivation was too high.

But now, it was different.

Their levels of cultivation were on the same plane.

Therefore, the problem of the evil spirit being difficult to destroy no longer existed.

After slaying the shadowy figure, Gu Qingfeng quickly released the other evil spirits.

Suppressing and sealing the evil spirits was a last resort.

In fact, if he could destroy the evil spirits, Gu Qingfeng naturally did not wish to seal them within the Zhenmo Prison.

After all, the existence of the evil spirits was like a time bomb - if one was not careful, it could bring about tremendous trouble.

Even though Gu Qingfeng had put in a lot of effort to create this Zhenmo Prison, there were still some hidden dangers.

Soon, the second evil spirit was released.

This time, the evil spirit seemed to be aware of the shadowy figure's demise, so it didn't even have the intention to fight Gu Qingfeng after emerging, directly rushing towards the outside of the Zhenmo Prison.

Unfortunately, Gu Qingfeng had already prepared himself at the side.

Seeing the evil spirit trying to escape, he immediately acted to slay it.

Subsequently, the third, fourth, and other evil spirits were released one by one, and then swiftly killed by Gu Qingfeng.

To be blunt, the evil spirits that could be sealed within the Zhenmo Prison were not that powerful to begin with.

With Gu Qingfeng's current Extreme Dao Sect Master cultivation, suppressing these evil spirits was an easy task.

Soon, all the evil spirits in the Zhenmo Prison were completely slain.

As he walked out of the Zhenmo Prison, he happened to see Hong Ye's solemn expression. When she saw Gu Qingfeng appear, the solemnity on her face dissipated somewhat.

"Your Excellency!"

"Alright, the matter of the Zhenmo Prison has been resolved. You don't need to guard this place anymore," Gu Qingfeng waved his hand, and Hong Ye inwardly heaved a sigh of relief upon hearing this.
