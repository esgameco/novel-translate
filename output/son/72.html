The Exorcism Bureau.

Jiang Qin returned.

Lu Wenxuan asked, "How did it go?"

"He didn't agree."

Jiang Qin shook his head slightly, and a cold glint immediately flashed in his eyes.

"However, the rumors in the jianghu are not true, this person is not at the Xisui realm, but has already stepped into the realm of a minor sect master of the Lianzang (Refining Impurity) cultivation."

"So I didn't attack him. A minor sect master of the Lianzang realm, and moreover a pure martial artist, perhaps he can be used as a nail to be inserted into the Taishan Dao."

"The various sects and aristocratic families will not tolerate the Gu Family Manor dominating the Taishan Dao, but if the lord of the Gu Family Manor is a minor sect master, then it's a different story.

Such a powerful expert, even those at the Yuling (Commanding Spirits) realm may not necessarily be his match. Looking at the entire Guangyang Prefecture, how many Yuling realm cultivators can there be!"

Jiang Qin ended with a cold smile.

Lu Wenxuan's eyes narrowed slightly: "A minor sect master of the Lianzang realm!"

He didn't expect that either.

In a small place like Baishi Dao, there could actually emerge a minor sect master of the Lianzang realm.

Not to mention Baishi Dao. Even in the flourishing martial arts of the Taishan Dao, with the numerous sects and forces in the jianghu, they have not seen the emergence of such a powerful expert at the minor sect master level.

Yet, a small place like Baishi Dao has actually produced a minor sect master, which is truly unexpected.

Among the seventy-two paths of the Guangyang Prefecture, the strength of the Taishan Dao can be ranked among the top ten.

In the past, the Taishan Dao was under the control of the Qingyang Sect, but now that the Qingyang Sect has been destroyed, the Taishan Dao has nominally fallen into the hands of the Gu Family Manor.

In this case, the various sects in the jianghu will certainly not turn a blind eye.

Just as Jiang Qin said, the people of the Guangyang Prefecture have all underestimated that person.

If he was only at the Xisui realm, it would have been fine, but a person who has already stepped into the Lianzang realm and can be called a minor sect master, the matter is not that simple.

"You did the right thing."

"A minor sect master is already not weak, at least in the Guangyang Prefecture, he can be considered one of the top experts."

"Since it is so, let them fight among themselves. We, the Exorcism Bureau, will sit firmly on the Diaoyu Terrace and have no need to intervene. The most urgent task at hand is to find the whereabouts of that evil spirit."

"As long as we can subdue that evil spirit, all other problems will not be problems."

Lu Wenxuan waved his hand, also agreeing with Jiang Qin's words, temporarily leaving the affairs in the jianghu unattended.

When two tigers fight, one is bound to be injured.

Regardless of who wins or loses, for the Exorcism Bureau, there is only benefit and no harm.

Gu Family Manor.

A few days after Jiang Qin's departure, another guest arrived.

"I am Zhuo Feng, greetings to the lord of the Gu Family Manor!"

In the main hall of the Gu Family Manor, a young man in white robes floated out, with a faint smile on his face.

Gu Qingfeng's calm gaze fell on Zhuo Feng, and information about the Zhuo family instantly appeared in his mind.

The Zhuo family!

A thousand-year-old aristocratic family in the Guangyang Prefecture. In terms of heritage, it is much stronger than the Qingyang Sect.

Although Zhuo Feng appears young, and his aura is hidden, with Gu Qingfeng's current eyesight, he can clearly feel the ageless, solemn aura and the faint evil essence emanating from the other party.

A cultivator!

This is a cultivator no weaker than the old patriarch of the Qingyang Sect. In an instant, Gu Qingfeng put all his thoughts in order and then said calmly, "May I ask what position you hold in the Zhuo family?"

"I'm afraid the lord of the Gu Family Manor will find it amusing, but I am currently an elder of the Zhuo family."

"So you are Elder Zhuo!"
