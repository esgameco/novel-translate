The Swordsman was heading towards the Blood Mountain Manor.

With each step he took, his aura became increasingly powerful.

The moment the Swordsman stepped into the Blood Mountain Manor, his aura reached its peak.

A True Meaning Master!

His qi and blood were as mighty as the blazing sun. The Swordsman's gaze towards the depths of the Blood Mountain Manor was resolute, and the dormant battle intent within him had now been fully awakened.

Even the iron sword on his back could feel this intense battle intent, and it trembled incessantly.

When he saw the sight of countless corpses strewn across the Blood Mountain Manor, the Swordsman's expression turned ice-cold.

"The evil entity has harmed my human race, it deserves to be killed!"

However, the Swordsman also understood.

The evil entity in the Blood Mountain Manor was capable of massacring an entire manor of civilians, so its strength must have reached a terrifying level, far beyond what he could currently handle.

After two hundred years of seclusion, the Swordsman had not truly reached the realm of a Grand Master, he was only at the half-step of a Grand Master.

Being a half-step Grand Master, he was considered a top-tier expert in the martial world. Many evil entities could be directly suppressed by him.

But when it came to subduing the evil entity of the Blood Mountain Manor, the Swordsman still lacked the confidence.

Because he was well aware of how powerful the evil entity was.

Five hundred years ago, the Swordsman had not participated in the previous battle, as he had not yet been born at that time. But his master, the founding patriarch of the Iron Sword Sect, had once taken part in that battle.

In the end, the Iron Sword Sect's patriarch had returned severely injured, and after teaching the Swordsman for some time, he passed away due to the unhealing wounds.

Precisely because the Swordsman understood the strength of the Blood Mountain Manor's evil entity, he had no intention of suppressing it with his own power.

His only goal was to delay it for some time.

As he delved deeper into the Blood Mountain Manor, the aura of the evil entity grew increasingly dense.

The Swordsman's expression became increasingly grave.

When he arrived before a certain mountain range, he saw that all the trees had withered, the ground had cracked, and a sinister and chilling aura permeated the air.

When the Swordsman looked at the gaping crevices in the earth, the endless darkness within them seemed like a devouring abyss that instilled fear, and the scattered corpses were arranged in a bizarre manner, their blood and essence completely drained.

Seeing this scene, the Swordsman's expression turned furious.

"Blood Sacrifice!"

"Someone has used the blood of the civilians to perform a blood sacrifice, which has led to the awakening of this evil entity!"

Before entering the Blood Mountain Manor, the Swordsman had thought that the evil entity had simply awakened due to the prolonged sealing, but now he realized that its resurgence was deliberately orchestrated.

And when he saw the attire worn by some of the corpses, the Swordsman's gaze turned cold once more.

"The Heaven Yang Sword Sect... the Fei family, they have the audacity to act in such a manner!"

Although he was not clear about the full details, seeing the bodies of the two sects among the many civilian corpses, the Swordsman could easily deduce the connection.

Evidently, the two sects had intended to use a blood sacrifice to awaken the evil entity here.

But it seemed they had miscalculated.

The evil entity had awakened and slaughtered the members of both sects.

And now it was poised to sweep across the entire Blood Mountain Manor, massacring all the civilians.

At this moment, the Swordsman noticed a figure in blue robes, and he couldn't help but be taken aback. He then spoke, "The evil entity has awakened in this place, sir, you should not linger here.

Given the strength of this evil entity, even Grand Masters would find it difficult to withstand. It is best for you to leave now, while there is still a chance to survive!"

In the Swordsman's view, the person in blue robes had likely sensed the disturbance in the Blood Mountain Manor and come to investigate. Although the Swordsman could not discern the depth of the other's abilities, he reckoned that even a powerful cultivator would not be a match for the evil entity.

Hearing the Swordsman's words, Gu Qingfeng turned his head and looked at him with a faint smile.

"If you know the evil entity is so formidable, why have you come here?"

"The Iron Sword Sect's principle is to eradicate evil entities. Now that an evil entity has awakened in the Blood Mountain Manor, how could I, as an elder of the sect, not come? I see that you, though young, have a remarkable aura. It would be a pity for you to perish here. It is better for you to leave while you still can."

The Swordsman had lived for centuries, and although he could not discern the full extent of Gu Qingfeng's abilities, he could tell that the young man was not ordinary.

Compared to the Swordsman, Gu Qingfeng was still a junior.

Upon hearing the Swordsman's words, Gu Qingfeng's expression shifted slightly.

The Iron Sword Sect! He had heard of this sect before.

After all, in the current world, truly pure martial arts sects were few and far between. The Iron Sword Sect was a prominent sect in the Southern Yang Prefecture, with a Xian-tier Patriarch at its helm, so Gu Qingfeng was not unfamiliar with it.

However, it seemed that the Iron Sword Sect's foundations were stronger than the rumors suggested.

At the very least, the cultivator before him was no weak individual.

In Gu Qingfeng's eyes, the Swordsman's aura was powerful, comparable to that of the Sect Master of the Divine Might Sect.

It was clear that the man was a half-step Grand Master.

And furthermore, he was a half-step Grand Master who had attained the True Meaning, not just a regular half-step Grand Master.
