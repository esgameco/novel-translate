The Five Viscera Classic (Not yet mastered)!

The Primordial Precious Mirror (Not yet mastered)!...

Gu Qingfeng took a glance at his attribute panel and was surprised to find that two martial arts had been added.

These two martial arts were the extraordinary martial arts sent by the Zhuo family.

The so-called extraordinary.

It is because when ordinary people reach the stage of refining the five viscera, they have already reached the limit of the human body and are stepping towards the extraordinary.

Therefore.

Martial arts involving the refinement of the viscera are also known as extraordinary martial arts.

Furthermore.

Among the two martial arts, Gu Qingfeng also understood that cultivators at the viscera refinement realm are also known as minor grandmasters.

The two martial arts.

The Five Viscera Classic is a supreme skill that directly targets the refinement of the five viscera.

The Primordial Precious Mirror, on the other hand, is slightly superior, only targeting the refinement of the four viscera.

However.

This so-called superiority is relative.

In the current situation where high-level martial arts are mostly controlled by sects and aristocratic families, not to mention extraordinary martial arts, even common top-grade martial arts are extremely rare.

At least.

For many jianghu forces like the Baishi Sect, so far, they only possess mid-grade martial arts that target blood refinement.

Obtaining top-grade martial arts.

Is no easy task.

If it were not for Gu Qingfeng's own advantages, the Gu Family Manor would probably be no different from other small forces, having little opportunity to take the first step.

From this, it can be seen.

Those ancient aristocratic sects are also secretly suppressing the growth of cultivators.

However.

These matters are currently unrelated to Gu Qingfeng.

The two extraordinary martial arts involving viscera refinement can at least serve as the inheritance of the Gu Family Manor, and they don't need to worry about the lack of cultivation methods before reaching the grandmaster realm.

Afterwards.

Gu Qingfeng spent several days observing the profundity of the extraordinary supreme skills.

Until this moment.

He finally truly understood how extraordinary the complete extraordinary martial arts are, and how arduous the refinement of the five viscera can be.

"No wonder there are few cultivators in the jianghu who have stepped into the minor grandmaster realm, refining the five viscera is like climbing a steep mountain, a single misstep can lead to death.

The path to the extraordinary is indeed not so easy to break through!"

Using his advancement points to break through the limit of the Heavenly Gale Divine Body, Gu Qingfeng had successfully entered the first realm of viscera refinement, but he had not yet felt the perilousness of the viscera refinement realm.

But now, after obtaining the viscera refinement martial arts and observing their cultivation methods, Gu Qingfeng realized that if there were no guidance from the martial arts of previous generations, the price that later generations would have to pay to create them from scratch would be incalculable.

It can be seen from this.

The emergence of every martial art is necessarily accompanied by a large number of sacrifices.

Otherwise.

Such martial arts would not be able to truly emerge.

And then.

There is the true intention of the grandmaster.

In the study.

Gu Qingfeng looked at the grandmaster's true intention painting hanging in the central position, and saw that it was a landscape ink painting, with only a solitary mountain divided into two parts appearing in the picture.

Apart from this.

There was nothing else.

After obtaining the grandmaster's true intention painting, Gu Qingfeng understood that this painting was not simple at all.

The painting seemed to contain an extremely profound aura.

Unfortunately.

The profundity of this painting.

Even though Gu Qingfeng observed it, he did not gain much harvest for the time being.

"According to Zhuo Feng's words, this contains the true intention of a grandmaster who is about to break through to the great grandmaster realm. The people of the Zhuo family have had this grandmaster's true intention painting for many years, but they have not been able to comprehend its profundity.

This shows that to unravel the profundity of the grandmaster's true intention painting is by no means a simple matter."

Gu Qingfeng murmured to himself.

However.

He was not discouraged.

Just because he could not comprehend it now does not mean he cannot comprehend it in the future.

As long as he continues to ponder it, Gu Qingfeng believes that he will eventually be able to unravel its profundity.
