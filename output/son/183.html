This kind of talk was not something a subordinate like him should interject.

Thereafter, Gu Qingfeng stretched out his hand and beckoned, and the Zhu Xie Sword in the center of the Whetstone Hall immediately responded and fell into his hand.

The Cultivating Sword Art had nurtured the Zhu Xie Sword for many years, and with the true essence and Gu Qingfeng's power refining it day and night in the Whetstone Hall, this divine weapon was now far beyond its former self.

Immediately after, Gu Qingfeng drew the sword, and the blood-red sword light illuminated the Whetstone Hall. Gu Yi only felt a chill, as if something terrible was staring at him, and his entire body was covered in goosebumps.

His gaze at the Zhu Xie Sword was filled with terror.

A single sword!

It made him feel such a strong sense of threat, showing the formidable power of the Zhu Xie Sword.

However, Gu Yi had already witnessed the divine might of the Zhu Xie Sword before, when it had slain an elder of the Tian Yang Sword Sect in the battle at the North Gate Pass, its strength being truly terrifying.

Under Gu Yi's astonished gaze, Gu Qingfeng flicked his finger, and a drop of golden blood shot out from his fingertip, embedding itself like a golden gem into the hilt of the Zhu Xie Sword.

In an instant, a golden light flashed on the Zhu Xie Sword, followed by a powerful and heavy aura that permeated outward.

The long sword was sheathed, and the aura dissipated.

With a wave of his hand, Gu Qingfeng handed the Zhu Xie Sword to Gu Yi.

"Tell Yang'er that this sword contains a drop of my essence blood, which can unleash its power three times, and even evil spirits can be slain by it!"

"Yes, sir!"

Gu Yi reverently accepted the Zhu Xie Sword, and although it was just a long sword, it gave him a sense of weight heavier than a thousand jin.

Thereupon, Gu Yi left, carrying the Zhu Xie Sword.

After Gu Yi's departure, Gu Qingfeng continued to ponder the martial arts, and the powerful aura of the true essence permeated and ravaged the Whetstone Hall. If there were any divine weapons that could sense this level of true essence power, they would tremble slightly.

If there were any strong cultivators who had mastered the true essence and entered the Whetstone Hall, they would discover that the divine weapons under Gu Qingfeng's constant illumination of the true essence had begun to develop a certain degree of spirituality, and each divine weapon was emanating a terrifying aura.

Such high-level divine weapons, if placed outside, would be enough to drive anyone crazy.

However, in the face of the Zhu Xie Sword, these divine weapons would seem somewhat inadequate.

In the days that followed, Gu Qingfeng continued to seclude himself in cultivation.

On the other side, Gu Yi had brought the Zhu Xie Sword to the capital city and handed it over to Gu Yang, also faithfully conveying Gu Qingfeng's words.

Hearing this, Gu Yang's expression immediately brightened.

"Good, with this sword, the Tian Yang Sword Sect will not be a concern!"

If Shen Wu Sect intervened, Gu Yang would have a ninety percent chance of being able to eliminate the Tian Yang Sword Sect. But now, with the Zhu Xie Sword in hand, his confidence was a full hundred percent.

Gu Yang absolutely believed in Gu Qingfeng's words.

The next day, the army set out.

One hundred and thirty thousand strong.

Gu Yang left thirty thousand troops to guard Taishan Prefecture, and the remaining hundred thousand troops marched towards Nanyang Prefecture in a grand display.
