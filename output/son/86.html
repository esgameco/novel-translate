the voices in the hall suddenly fell silent.

Yan Ren immediately bowed to the Crimson Flame Sect Master Ouyang Lie in the seat of honor, his voice full of grief.

"Please, Sect Master, avenge Peak Master Wen!"

"Please, Sect Master, avenge Peak Master Wen—"

The remaining elders of the Crimson Flame Peak also spoke in unison.

Ouyang Lie raised his hand, and the hall immediately fell silent, then the Crimson Flame Sect Master slowly spoke.

"Of course Wen Peak Master's revenge must be avenged, but the most urgent task right now is to first bring Wen Peak Master's body back."

"Why don't we put pressure on the government to have them send Wen Peak Master's body back to us?"

An elder proposed.

Ouyang Lie's expression was indifferent: "Although the court now seems weak, its accumulated strength over the years is not to be underestimated. At this critical juncture, we have no need to tear off our faces with the court.

Moreover, the local government of White Stone City will not offend the Gu family for the sake of our Crimson Flame Sect."

Ouyang Lie was well aware of the government's intentions.

Although the court is now weak, it is not something the Crimson Flame Sect can easily offend.

Acting without justification.

If they rashly put pressure on the government, it will only end up damaging the reputation of the Crimson Flame Sect.

Saying this,

Ouyang Lie looked at the Qingmu Peak Master Zhu Ming beside him and said, "Now I'll trouble Peak Master Zhu to go to White Stone Road and bring Wen Peak Master's body back."

"Yes!"

Zhu Ming, who had been silent all along, immediately nodded.

Yan Ren and the other Crimson Flame Peak elders still wanted to say something, but now that Ouyang Lie's words had fallen, they could only hold back the rest of their words.

When the Sect Master speaks,

it is naturally not something ordinary elders can oppose.

If Wen Jiao were still alive, the Crimson Flame Peak would at least have a powerful backer.

But now that Wen Jiao is dead, the strength of the Crimson Flame Peak has greatly declined, and at this time to further provoke Ouyang Lie's displeasure would only bring more trouble.

Where there are people, there is the jianghu.
