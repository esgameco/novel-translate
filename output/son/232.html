After all, he was only at the middle Xiwu Realm, and hadn't even fully cultivated the Wudu Realm. Compared to these experts who were casually Grand Ancestors or Shenbian Realm, he was no different from an ant.

However, the opportunity of the Great Saint was right in front of him, and if he didn't enter, he would be filled with regret.

As soon as Gu Xuan finished speaking, Gu Qingfeng suddenly stretched out his hand, and a beam of light instantly shot out from the manor, arriving in his hand in the blink of an eye.

"Take the Zhu Xie Sword with you into the secret realm, if you encounter danger, this sword can protect you."

Gu Qingfeng handed the Zhu Xie Sword to Gu Xuan, who was overjoyed upon hearing this.

"Thank you, Father!"

Gu Xuan understood the power of the Zhu Xie Sword. More importantly, by staying with Fu He, Gu Xuan had learned about some cultivation-related issues. The aura that long-term cultivators could contain around themselves was extremely powerful.

Since his father's strength was on par with the top Daogong Realm experts, the divine sword he had been refining day and night was certainly no ordinary weapon.

Afterwards, the two of them entered the Great Saint's secret realm.

At the same time, Gu Qingfeng also ordered Gu Wu and the others to enter the secret realm.

This time, Gu Yang only brought Gu Wu and Gu Dao, while the other two remained to guard the capital, in case of any other unexpected events.

As for the other people of the Divine Martial Empire, such as Shi Zhen and the Tiangao Army under his command, they had no intention of entering the Great Saint's secret realm.

After all, Shi Zhen was clear about his own situation.

Although Shi Zhen had succeeded in reaching the peak of the Grand Ancestor Realm by taking medicinal pills, among the experts entering the Great Saint's secret realm, the Grand Ancestor Realm was nothing more than an ant.
