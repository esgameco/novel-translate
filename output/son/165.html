In the Central State, in the imperial palace, in the Emperor's study, the Emperor of the Taixuan Dynasty, Ji Chunqiu, was sitting on the dragon throne, his appearance aged and exuding a sense of decline.

Before him was a memorial from Qing Yun State, the content of which was related to Guangyang Prefecture.

"Domineering Blade Gu Qingfeng! He slew the sect master of the Tianyang Sword Sect, Gu Yuetian, with a single sword strike. His cultivation has reached the Tong Shen Realm, possibly rivaling the Martial Ancestor from a millennium ago!"

"Furthermore, this person also possesses the means to eliminate evil spirits."

After reading the memorial for a long time, Ji Chunqiu let out a long sigh and handed it to the other members of the imperial family sitting in the study.

They took turns reading the memorial, and their expressions changed accordingly.

"Father, is this news truly real?" asked the Crown Prince, Ji Zhen'an.

Ji Chunqiu replied indifferently, "This news should be true. In the past, that Martial Ancestor preached to the world, and it was said that he had broken through the limits of the Grand Masters. However, since the Martial Ancestor's passing, there has been no one in the world who could reach that level of strength again."

"I didn't expect Guangyang Prefecture to produce such a powerful figure!"

Ji Chunqiu's tone carried a hint of emotion.

Hearing this, Ji Zhen'an cautiously asked, "Father, are you planning to try to win over this person?"

"Correct!" Ji Chunqiu nodded.

"Although our Taixuan Dynasty is founded by the Ji family, the cycle of the kingdom has been turning for three thousand years, a fate that no one can resist. The previous dynasty managed to last for six hundred years through its own foundations, but in the end, it could not escape its demise."

"Now, the warlords are vying for control of the Central State, wanting to destroy the foundation of our Taixuan Dynasty. If the dynasty falls, you all will have no chance of survival!"

This was a cruel truth, but it was the reality they faced.

The founding emperor of the Taixuan Dynasty was actually from a branch of the Ji family, and it was with the support of the Ji family that he was able to conquer the world and establish the Taixuan Dynasty.

Over the years, the Taixuan Dynasty has been constantly providing resources to the Ji family.

But now that the three-thousand-year cycle is about to end, the Ji family no longer protects the Taixuan Dynasty.

Without the protection of the Immortal Sect Families, the downfall of the Taixuan Dynasty is imminent.

Faced with this, Ji Chunqiu was unwilling to accept it.
