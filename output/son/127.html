"How interesting!"

Gu Qingfeng tried to cut his own skin and squeeze out a little fresh blood, only to find that the blood had a faint golden color and contained a powerful aura.

The strength of this aura was not just manifested in its power, but in the level of life, giving a sense of greater nobility.

"Transformation of the level of life!"

"Could it be that when entering the stage of perfect refinement of impurities, the warrior's level of life can begin to transform?"

Gu Qingfeng's expression changed slightly, and then he left the Grinding Hall and entered the Demon Suppression Prison.

The current Demon Suppression Prison.

It actually held three evil spirits.

Gu Qingfeng skillfully opened the golden coffin that sealed the Yin Gui, and then directly cut off a part of the Yin Gui, trying to extinguish the existence of the Yin Gui with his own vital energy.

When his vital energy collided with the remnant of the Yin Gui, it was like hot oil meeting cold water, instantly causing a violent reaction.

Vaguely, Gu Qingfeng could even hear a piercing wail coming from the remnant of the Yin Gui.

But he paid no attention to it, instead fully mobilizing his vital energy to continuously consume the remnant of the Yin Gui.

After a long time.

Gu Qingfeng's vital energy was almost exhausted, but there was no disappointment on his face, only an undisguised smile.

Because the small piece of severed finger representing the remnant of the Yin Gui had completely disappeared.

"It really works!"

Gu Qingfeng was overjoyed.

"Perfect refinement of impurities, causing changes in the heart, and then improving the warrior's level of life, the so-called rumor of evil spirits being immortal will also be completely broken.

My current level of life is probably higher than that of the so-called sorcerers, although it is still a bit lower than the evil spirits, but as long as I continue to improve my cultivation, truly slaying the evil spirits will no longer be a problem!"

Although at the moment, he had to use up his entire vital energy just to consume a small piece of the Yin Gui's severed finger, which still seemed to be a long way from truly eliminating an evil spirit.

But one thing must be understood.

Many things, the real difficulty is only at the beginning.

In the past, evil spirits were difficult to extinguish, and even a small piece of a severed finger was equally difficult to shake.

But now it's different.

Gu Qingfeng has been able to extinguish a piece of the Yin Gui's severed finger, and when his cultivation level improves, truly eliminating an evil spirit will certainly not be a problem.

This shows that the rumor of evil spirits being immortal can be broken.

In comparison.

Although sorcerers are powerful, they also do not have the ability to truly extinguish evil spirits.

If the evil spirits in this world have the highest level of life, then the sorcerers who bear the evil spirits must be one level lower than the evil spirits, while the warriors are half a level lower than the sorcerers.

This level of life has little to do with strength.

This is why.

Sorcerers can be stronger than warriors, but warriors can also slay sorcerers, but sorcerers cannot slay evil spirits when facing them.

Now.

With the transformation of his heart, Gu Qingfeng's level of life has further improved, already reaching half a level higher than sorcerers, only half a level lower than evil spirits.

As long as he can truly raise the five realms of refinement of impurities to perfection, Gu Qingfeng believes that his level of life will definitely transform again, reaching the same level as the evil spirits.

...

After leaving the Demon Suppression Prison, Gu Qingfeng felt that the sunlight had become much brighter.

The immortality of evil spirits.

It was like a huge mountain pressing on his heart.

After all, such an immortal existence, no matter how much it is suppressed, is ultimately useless.

So-called suppression.

Someday, there will always be the possibility of breaking the seal.

Back in the Grinding Pavilion, Gu Qingfeng continued to cultivate the Prajna Sutra.

According to his plan, once the five impure realms are completed, the subsequent synchronized cultivation of the impure realm techniques would be much less difficult.

With the difficulty of the Prajna Sutra, Gu Qingfeng was confident that he could complete the cultivation of the five impure realms in half a year.

However, when he actually started the cultivation, Gu Qingfeng found that things were not as simple as he had imagined.

In the past, whether it was the Chaos Precious Mirror or the Scarlet Flame Divine Skill, the cultivation of the five impure realms had been as if divinely assisted.

But this time, the cultivation of the Prajna Sutra was completely different. It was obscure, and every step was extremely difficult.

After half a month, Gu Qingfeng's expression was slightly gloomy.

"The difficulty coefficient of the Prajna Sutra cultivation has increased greatly, which should be related to the perfect refinement of the impure realms. My five viscera tempering is only one step away from perfect metamorphosis.

So to truly take this step, it will require time and gradual polishing, only then will there be the possibility of a breakthrough."

With this in mind, Gu Qingfeng's original plan to first cultivate the Prajna Sutra to the realm of the five impure realms perfection, and then use the promotion points to break through the Patriarch realm, was thwarted.

Immediately, Gu Qingfeng used another forty promotion points to directly upgrade the Prajna Sutra from the fifth layer to the seventh layer, skipping two realms.

In return, Gu Qingfeng had two more viscera perfectly tempered, making his entire strength even more terrifying, and his life level was also gradually rising.

"With my current blood and qi strength, I can directly grind away not only a Shadow Deception Finger, but even a Shadow Deception Palm, but to truly slay a Shadow Deception in one breath, I'm not quite capable of that yet."

Gu Qingfeng felt the changes in his own body. Compared to the time when only his heart had mutated, he was now much stronger.

However, this was still not enough. In Gu Qingfeng's view, this strength was still not sufficient to truly slay the evil spirits.

After all, in a life-and-death confrontation, who has the time to gradually grind down the evil spirits, as they are not inanimate objects and will not willingly submit to annihilation.

The evil spirits can run and resist.

Therefore, what Gu Qingfeng needs to do is to truly raise his life level to the same level as the evil spirits, so that slaying them would become much easier.

However, after glancing at the remaining three promotion points, Gu Qingfeng gave up the idea of further advancement.

From now on, he would wait. Wait for the Gu family's descendants to make a breakthrough, and then he could directly use the promotion points to completely refine the five viscera.

At that time, Gu Qingfeng could start to break through the Patriarch realm.

"With my current strength, although I cannot truly eradicate the evil spirits, if I were to face Shen Wudao again, I believe a single strike would be enough to slay him."

Gu Qingfeng's expression was confident.

Although he had not broken through the Patriarch realm, his strength was no longer comparable to the past.

The only uncertainty was that Gu Qingfeng did not know how his strength compared to the rumored great Patriarchs.
