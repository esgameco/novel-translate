He had always been aware of his dull innate talent, but now, with the appearance of the ancient immortal body, his talent had transformed into an extremely powerful one.

Even if there were no cultivation points, Gu Qingfeng could still rely on the ancient immortal body to ascend to higher realms.

Perfected Eighteen-Zhang Golden Body! Buddha Anomaly!

If there were any Buddhists present, they would undoubtedly prostrate themselves in reverence. However, this anomaly was small compared to the anomaly of the ancient immortal body, and it soon dissipated.

Gu Qingfeng opened his eyes, a glint of brilliance flashing in them.

However, this had not been truly achieved yet.

If a Great Dao Master could also comprehend the Nine-Level Dao Essence, their strength would undergo a qualitative transformation, and they could even be on par with the Nascent Soul realm experts.

In the past, the Sword Dao Patriarch had almost reached this level, but it was only after closing himself off for hundreds of years that he was able to barely take the final step and attain the Great Dao Master realm.

But even after that breakthrough, the Sword Dao Patriarch had still been stuck at the initial stage of the Great Dao Master realm, unable to advance further.

This showed how difficult it was to break through the Great Dao Master realm.

But now, Gu Qingfeng had ten Great Dao Master Promotion Pills and ten Nine-Level Dao Essence Promotion Pills, which could instantly create ten Perfected Great Dao Masters and allow them to comprehend the Nine-Level Dao Essence.

With such power, any force that possessed it would be able to sweep across the world, and even many immortal sects and ancient families might not be able to withstand the might of ten Great Dao Masters.

After that, there were also the other rewards.

Gu Qingfeng carefully examined them, his mind racing with various thoughts and plans for the future development of the Divine Martial Dynasty.
