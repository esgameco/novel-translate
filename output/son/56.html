In less than a breath, the two had exchanged dozens of moves.

With each collision, Gu Qingfeng felt a strange and icy aura entering his body through the palms of his hands, trying to freeze and devour his flesh and blood, but it was quickly dispersed by the scorching blood force.

On the other side, the Elder of the Azure Yang Sect was also not doing well.

He found that Gu Qingfeng's blood qi was terrifyingly strong, much more powerful than an ordinary Xiu Shu Realm cultivator, and his blood qi even contained an extremely scorching aura, completely conflicting with the sorcerer's power he had cultivated.

When the sorcerer's power invaded the other's body, this scorching power also entered his own body.

For a moment, the Elder of the Azure Yang Sect's expression looked unpleasant.

With a thought, the shadows on the ground suddenly erupted, countless black shadows like sharp swords piercing through the air, attacking Gu Qingfeng's vital points.

The latter, as if he had anticipated it, unleashed his protective spirit qi again, and all the shadow attacks were isolated by the protective spirit qi, but these shadow attacks were particularly strange and powerful, and even with the strength of the protective spirit qi, it could not withstand for more than a few breaths before it was shattered.

Seeing this, Gu Qingfeng's eyes turned cold, and his palms frantically struck out, unleashing his terrifying blood qi power, which, when colliding with the shadows, instantly dispersed and dissipated them like spring sunshine melting snow.

However, there were still shadows that broke through the blockade and directly pierced Gu Qingfeng's arm. The skin that could withstand the Lian Gu Quan's perfect attack was now completely defenseless.

The skin was broken open, and Gu Qingfeng could feel his flesh and blood being gradually devoured.

He directly grabbed the shadow with his right hand, activating the scorching Yang Palm Force, and the shadow that was originally devouring his flesh and blood was instantly severed, and Gu Qingfeng seemed to even hear a piercing scream.

"Kill!"

Gu Qingfeng's expression was ferocious, and in the next breath, he charged directly towards the Elder of the Azure Yang Sect.

This fierce momentum made the latter's expression suddenly change.

Immediately after, the Elder of the Azure Yang Sect also had a ruthless expression, and strange powers erupted from his body, the terrifying shadows under the sun's glare seemed to be magnified, twisting and struggling like evil ghosts in hell.

At the same time, the Elder of the Azure Yang Sect's previously plump skin suddenly shriveled, his eye sockets gradually sunken, his ten fingers' nails becoming long and sharp, his entire being transforming into a hellish evil ghost.

"This old man will devour you!!"

The Elder of the Azure Yang Sect let out a roar and engaged in battle with Gu Qingfeng again.

As for the Gu family side, they were also equally shocked.

Gu Qingfeng and the Qingyang Sect's Patriarch had become mere shadows, both of them unleashing their full power.

The shadows on the ground twisted and distorted, breaking through Gu Qingfeng's protective aura and leaving deep wounds on his body.

But Gu Qingfeng paid no heed to these injuries, his eyes fixed solely on the Qingyang Patriarch, his relentless attacks not diminishing in the slightest, putting immense pressure on the opponent.

"Boom!"

After another brief exchange, Gu Qingfeng seized an opening and struck the Qingyang Patriarch's chest with full force, the terrifying power capable of shattering mountains instantly caving in the opponent's chest.

The internal organs were instantly ruptured under such tremendous force.

The Qingyang Patriarch coughed up blood mixed with shredded viscera.

Such an injury.

If it had befallen an ordinary cultivator, they would have already perished. But as a Sorcerer, the Patriarch's life force was exceptionally strong, and even with such a fatal wound, he could still cling to life.

In this moment, the Qingyang Patriarch's gaze towards Gu Qingfeng finally turned to one of fear, sensing the aura of death from Gu Qingfeng's blood-soaked, unyielding form.

Immediately, the Qingyang Patriarch turned and fled.

"Too late to think of running now!"

The Scorching Sun Palm energy exploded.

"Ahhh!!!"

Immediately after, Gu Qingfeng grabbed the Patriarch's head and slammed it down onto the ground with tremendous force.

Boom--

The ground caved in, the rocks shattered. The Patriarch's skull seemed to crack under the immense power, his face covered in blood, his sunken eye sockets and withered body making him appear even more grotesque.
