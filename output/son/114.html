Since the demise of Seasonal Sect,

the Divine Fist Sect had been fully focused on gathering intelligence about the Gu Clan Manor.

So,

they believed they had a good understanding of the Gu Clan Manor's strength.

From the information gathered, Gu Peng was merely the outer hall master of the Gu Clan, just a minor Refining Blood Realm cultivator.

Even though Gu Peng had just destroyed a Refining Blood Realm cultivator with a single punch, suggesting his strength was not as simple as the rumors, Zhong Yang still did not take him seriously.

He struck out with a thunderous punch!

Seeing Zhong Yang's incoming destructive punch, Gu Peng's face twisted into a ferocious smile as he also struck out with his right fist.

The two fists collided.

The smile on Zhong Yang's face instantly turned to horror.

"No—"

He couldn't even finish his words before his arm suddenly exploded, flesh and bones flying everywhere. The residual force flung his body away like withered grass.

Upon landing,

Zhong Yang coughed up mouthfuls of blood, his eyes filled with dread as he looked at Gu Peng.

"No... impossible, your strength... strength..."

Before he could finish, Zhong Yang's pupils dilated, and he died with an expression of utter resentment.

This scene

left the Divine Fist Sect members utterly terrified.

Since the fall of Seasonal Sect, Zhong Yang, the deputy sect master, was the strongest of the Divine Fist Sect.

Yet,

this top expert was killed by Gu Peng with a single punch, how could they not be shocked?

After all,

the renowned "Overlord Saber" Gu Qingfeng had not even truly intervened, and just his subordinate had the power to instantly slay Zhong Yang - the implications of this were staggering.

"The Patriarch has ordered, not a single one of the Divine Fist Sect shall be spared!"

Gu Peng roared, his face filled with the excitement of slaughter and the thrill of his surging power.

Although on the way from Guangyang Prefecture, Gu Peng had already killed many bandits and thieves, the problem was that they were insignificant.

Not to mention Refining Bone Realm,

even Refining Blood Realm cultivators were few and far between.

So,

this battle with the Divine Fist Sect was the first time Gu Peng truly unleashed his perfected Refining Filth Fifth Realm strength.

One-punching the Refining Bone Realm expert Zhong Yang filled Gu Peng's heart with excitement.

This was the power he had yearned for! Those formidable figures he could barely look at before, he could now directly slay.

The moment the words left his mouth, Gu Peng charged into the Divine Fist Sect's group like a ferocious tiger, the power of the Refining Filth Fifth Realm erupting perfectly, each punch he struck taking a life.

The fist techniques he used were the Gu Clan's own.

But,

the seemingly ordinary Gu Clan fists, now supported by Gu Peng's mighty cultivation, had reached the point of transforming the rotten into the divine.

In that moment,

the Divine Fist Sect disciples were terrified by the slaughter.

Many outer sect disciples watching their own hall master wreaking havoc could not help but look at him with reverence.

Not a single survivor from the Divine Fist Sect!

within the Divine Fist Sect,

With the man's appearance, even under the bright daylight, an indescribable chill could be felt.
