At Gu Qingfeng's command, Gu Peng and the others immediately rushed towards the Divine Fist Sect.

Gu Peng's aura was surging, his presence as terrifying as an abyss. When he struck out with a punch, it directly caused the body of a Refining Blood Realm cultivator from the Divine Fist Sect to explode.

"Audacious!"

Zhong Yang stepped forward, his right fist falling with the force to shatter the heavens towards Gu Peng.

In his eyes, this Gu Peng was not worth mentioning at all. The true strong one in the Gu Family Manor was only Gu Qingfeng. The rest were not renowned in the jianghu and their strength was not that formidable.

Since the demise of Sect Master Qi, the Divine Fist Sect had been fully focused on gathering intelligence about the Gu Family Manor. So they believed they understood the strength of the Gu Family Manor quite well.

From the information they had gathered, Gu Peng was just a minor outer hall master of the Gu Family, a small Refining Blood Realm cultivator. Even though Gu Peng had just destroyed a Refining Blood Realm cultivator with a single punch, his strength did not seem as simple as the rumors, but Zhong Yang still did not take him seriously.

The punch struck out, shocking the heavens and earth!

Seeing Zhong Yang's incoming destructive punch, a ferocious smile appeared on Gu Peng's face as he also struck out with his right fist.

The two fists collided.

The smile that had just appeared on Zhong Yang's face instantly turned to horror.

"No—"

Before he could finish speaking, his arm suddenly exploded, flesh and bone scattered. The lingering force flung his body away like withered grass.

After falling to the ground, Zhong Yang coughed up mouthfuls of blood, his eyes filled with terror as he looked at Gu Peng.

"No... impossible, your strength... strength..."

Unable to finish his words, Zhong Yang's pupils dilated as he died, his face full of unwillingness.

This scene left the Divine Fist Sect members utterly horrified. Zhong Yang, the deputy sect master, was the strongest on the surface of the Divine Fist Sect after the death of Sect Master Qi. Yet he was killed by Gu Peng with a single punch. How could they not be shocked?

After all, the renowned "Overlord Saber" Gu Qingfeng had not even truly acted yet, and just his subordinate had the strength to instantly kill Zhong Yang. The impact of this scene was unimaginable.

"The Manor Master has ordered, not a single one of the Divine Fist Sect shall be spared!" Gu Peng roared, his face filled with a murderous intent and the excitement of his power's transformation.

Although Gu Peng had struck down many bandits and thieves on the way from Guangyang Prefecture, the problem was that they were insignificant. Not to mention Refining Bone Realm, even Refining Blood Realm cultivators were few and far between.

So this battle with the Divine Fist Sect was the first time Gu Peng truly revealed his perfected Refining Filth Fifth Realm strength.

Destroying the Refining Bone Realm's Zhong Yang with a single punch filled Gu Peng's heart with excitement. This was the power he had yearned for! Those formidable figures he had previously found difficult to face could now be directly slain by him.

As the words left his mouth, Gu Peng charged into the Divine Fist Sect members like a fierce tiger descending the mountain, the power of the Refining Filth Fifth Realm fully erupting. Every punch he struck took a life.

The fist techniques he used were precisely the Gu Family Fist. But now, supported by Gu Peng's powerful cultivation, the seemingly ordinary Gu Family Fist had reached the point of transforming the rotten into the divine.

In an instant, the Divine Fist Sect disciples were terrified by the slaughter.

Many outer hall disciples could not help but look at their sect master massacring everything with reverence in their eyes.

However,

in recent years,

Gu Qingfeng had few opportunities to take action.

Like slaying the weak, he had no interest in that, and the Demonic Buddha of the Nine Twists Path was too powerful, so finding a worthy opponent was not that easy.

Now,

Gu Qingfeng hoped that the Divine Fist Sect truly had a Sect Master.

This way,

he could truly witness the true power of a so-called Sect Master.

As time passed,

Many Sect Elders were killed by Gu Peng, and there were even those who directly gave up on resistance and wanted to surrender, but Gu Peng showed no mercy, whether they resisted or surrendered, he slaughtered them all.

The reason he did this was because Gu Qingfeng did not speak up.

If the other party did not speak up,

then the meaning was very clear.

Not a single survivor from the Divine Fist Sect!

Therefore,

At this time,

within the Divine Fist Sect,
