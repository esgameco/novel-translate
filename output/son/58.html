Inside the grand hall of the Qingyang Sect, Gu Qingfeng sat on the throne reserved for the sect master. His mind was focused on the item list, and with a single thought, a secret manual appeared in his hand.

Extraordinary martial arts!

The Heavenly Dipper Divine Body!

Gu Qingfeng did not hesitate and immediately opened the secret manual.

In an instant, the secret manual transformed into a stream of light and disappeared into his mind.

Immediately after, numerous memories surfaced in Gu Qingfeng's mind, as if he had cultivated the Heavenly Dipper Divine Body for many years. The twelve layers of the Heavenly Dipper Divine Body were all revealed.

Strictly speaking, the Heavenly Dipper Divine Body is the advanced and ultimate technique of the Heavenly Dipper Invincible Body. However, the two are not like the Chaos Iron Cloth and the Hundred Refinements Golden Body, where the former is a part of the latter.

Therefore, when Gu Qingfeng practiced the Heavenly Dipper Divine Body, the Heavenly Dipper Invincible Body did not disappear.

At the same time, Gu Qingfeng's qi and blood were stirred, and the aura around him visibly surged.

Washing Marrow Middle Stage!

Washing Marrow Late Stage!

Washing Marrow Perfection!

In less than a few breaths, Gu Qingfeng's cultivation had risen all the way to the Washing Marrow Perfection realm.

At this moment, Gu Qingfeng looked at his attribute panel and saw that a new martial art, the Heavenly Dipper Divine Body, had been added to the list.

...

Heavenly Dipper Divine Body (Twelve-layered Perfected Protective Qi, Level 9)

...

Afterward, Gu Qingfeng tried to activate the protective qi, and he saw almost tangible qi emerge from his body, which was several times stronger than the protective qi of the Heavenly Dipper Invincible Body.

Feeling the power of the protective qi, Gu Qingfeng's eyes gleamed with delight.

"With this level of protective qi, even if I were to stand still, the Qingyang Patriarch would have difficulty breaking through my defenses."

Indeed, the protective qi derived from the perfected Heavenly Dipper Divine Body was exceptionally powerful.

Afterward, Gu Qingfeng calmed his mind and looked at the Heavenly Dipper Divine Body on his attribute panel again.

"The Heavenly Dipper Divine Body's twelve-layered perfection can reach the peak of the Washing Marrow realm. If I were to break through its limit, I would be able to directly advance to the Refining Viscera realm!"

Refining Viscera! This was the pinnacle of the realm below the Innate.

This extermination of the Qingyang Sect had made Gu Qingfeng truly realize that in the current chaotic world, there were many powerful experts, and the strength of the Washing Marrow realm, although not weak, might not be that formidable.

Just to deal with the Qingyang Patriarch, he had to exert his full strength. If he were to face an opponent stronger than the Qingyang Patriarch, Gu Qingfeng was not confident in his ability to contend.

After all, the Qingyang Sect was just one of the sects in the Guangyang Prefecture. Who knows what other powerful existences might be lurking within the Guangyang Prefecture.

Furthermore, once the news of the Qingyang Sect's destruction spread, it would undoubtedly shake the Guangyang Prefecture. At that time, the Gu family would inevitably come into the sight of the major powers.

This was different from their small plot of land in the White Stone Dao. Once they reached that stage, the Gu family must have sufficient strength to support themselves.

Therefore, if he could break through to the Refining Viscera realm, many problems could be alleviated.

...

"Breakthrough!"

With a thought, Gu Qingfeng directly spent 40 Improvement Points to break through the limit of the Heavenly Dipper Divine Body.

Immediately, a flood of memories surged forth. In these memories, Gu Qingfeng had spent years cultivating the perfected Heavenly Dipper Divine Body, struggling to break through, and later traveled the world, competing with many powerful experts.

Is the power of the Refining the Viscera realm.

The Heavenly Canopy Divine Body has broken through its limits, and the Ninth Layer Protective Canopy Qi has advanced to the Indestructible Divine Body.

At this stage.

The Indestructible Divine Body.

Upon entering this realm, Gu Qingfeng's sense of security has greatly increased.

As for the Patriarch-level experts above the Refining the Viscera realm, their numbers would be even fewer.

After all, the Patriarch of the Azure Yang's strength is estimated to be at most the initial Marrow Cleansing realm.

Therefore.

After reaching the First Realm of Refining the Viscera, Gu Qingfeng believes that even if someone were to target the Gu Family, they would have to carefully consider whether they have the qualifications to do so.
