Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The two cultivators at the Fusion Shen Boundary level had fallen one after the other, causing the evil spirits within them to revive. In an instant, the Divine Martial Army fell into great chaos.

For ordinary people, evil spirits were an existence that could not be resisted.

Such existences were almost immortal.

So, when the evil spirits revived, they directly charged into the Divine Martial Army, ruthlessly slaughtering the soldiers and devouring their flesh and blood, causing the rest to shiver in fear.

This scene made Pei Jing's expression uncomfortable, and he was also slightly pale.

It was obvious that Gu Qingfeng's strength exceeded his estimation.

The fall of the three great elders of the Taiyang Sword Sect and the revival of the two evil spirits made Pei Jing realize that he was now in a dire situation.

In this battle, he had no chance of winning.

Seeing Gu Qingfeng charging towards him, Pei Jing immediately ordered a retreat while also having his men block Gu Qingfeng's path to create an opportunity for himself to leave.

"Since the Divine Martial King has come, why leave in such a hurry!" Gu Qingfeng laughed loudly, then let out a long howl, and his physical aura and blood suddenly exploded, sweeping across the battlefield like a blazing furnace. The terrifying might emanating from him made the other Divine Martial Army members dare not take a single step forward.

With a rumbling sound, Gu Qingfeng's body shook, and he suddenly shot up into the air, then came crashing down with the force of a mountain.

In an instant, the earth trembled, and the ground cracked. Within a ten-zhang radius, all the soldiers were unable to stand firm.

Gu Qingfeng paid no heed to this. He took a step, crossing dozens of zhang in a single breath, and appeared before Pei Jing.

Pei Jing felt the earth shaking, and his startled horse reared up, but he firmly gripped the reins, calming the horse down.

"Gu Qingfeng..." Pei Jing looked at the man before him, his expression surprisingly calm.

"If Brother Gu is willing to join hands with me, we can together conquer the world and rule it!"

At this point, Pei Jing realized that he had fallen into a desperate situation. In this battle, not only had the entire hundred thousand Divine Martial Army perished, but he himself might also be unable to escape.

Reaching this point, Pei Jing had to try his best to seize the last opportunity. After all, he had spent over twenty years building up his power, and he was unwilling to die here.

At the same time, Pei Jing also regretted that if he had been more cautious and not led the entire army to the front lines, the situation would not have become so dire. The loss of a hundred thousand troops was not a significant blow to his foundation of hundreds of millions of soldiers. And with such a vast army, he would not have feared a single great master.

But now, it was too late. Gu Qingfeng stood before him, and despite Pei Jing being a Xiantian Sect Master himself, he no longer had the courage to fight. The gap between them was too great, to the point of despair.

Hearing Pei Jing's words, Gu Qingfeng shook his head and laughed.

"You couldn't even subdue a White Stone Dao, yet you want to contend for the world? Victory and defeat are self-evident. If this is your last words, then Gu shall remember them!"

With that, Gu Qingfeng unleashed a slash.

The tyrannical blade cut through the air, and the blood-colored aura of the Demon-Slaying Blade reflected everything. The threat of death surged in Pei Jing's heart, and he roared, unleashing his true qi and blood.

The two forces collided, and Gu Qingfeng's attack swept through like an unstoppable force.
