Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The two Fusion Realm cultivators had fallen one after the other, causing the evil spirits within them to awaken. In an instant, the Divine Martial Army was thrown into great chaos.

For ordinary people, the evil spirits were an existence that could not be resisted.

These beings were almost immortal and indestructible.

So, the moment the evil spirits awoke, they directly charged into the Divine Martial Army, ruthlessly slaughtering the soldiers and devouring their flesh and blood, instilling a sense of inexplicable dread in the others.

This scene left Pei Jing's expression unsightly, and he was also slightly pale.

It was clear that Gu Qingfeng's strength had exceeded Pei Jing's expectations.

The fall of the three great elders of the Taiyang Sword Sect and the resurgence of the two evil spirits made Pei Jing realize that his own situation was now dire.

In this battle, he had no chance of winning.

Seeing Gu Qingfeng charging towards him, Pei Jing immediately ordered a retreat while also having his men block Gu Qingfeng's path to create an opportunity for himself to escape.

"Since the Divine Martial King has arrived, why leave in such a hurry!" Gu Qingfeng laughed loudly, then let out a long howl as his flesh and blood erupted with terrifying power, sweeping across the battlefield like a blazing furnace. The horrifying aura emanating from him caused the other Divine Martial Army soldiers to be struck with terror, not daring to take a single step forward.

"Boom!" Gu Qingfeng's body shook, and he suddenly shot up into the air, then came crashing down with the force of a mountain. In an instant, the earth trembled, and the ground cracked. Within a ten-meter radius, all the soldiers were unable to stand firm.

Gu Qingfeng paid no heed to this. He took a step, covering dozens of meters in the blink of an eye, and appeared before Pei Jing.

Pei Jing felt the earth shaking, and his startled horse reared up, but Pei Jing firmly gripped the reins, calming the horse down.

"Gu Qingfeng..." Pei Jing's expression surprisingly became calm.

"If Brother Gu is willing to join forces with me, we can together conquer the world and rule it side by side!"

At this point, Pei Jing realized that he had fallen into a hopeless situation. In this battle, not only had the entire hundred thousand Divine Martial Army been wiped out, but he himself might also be unable to escape.

Pei Jing was determined to seize the last opportunity to try and negotiate, as he had spent over twenty years building up his power, and he was unwilling to die here.

Gu Qingfeng shook his head and laughed. "You couldn't even subdue a White Stone Dao, yet you want to contend for the world? Victory or defeat is nothing to be said about. If this is your last words, then I shall remember them!"

With that, Gu Qingfeng unleashed a powerful slash. The blood-colored aura of the Tyrant Blade cut through the air, and the threat of death loomed. Pei Jing roared, unleashing his true qi and blood energy in a desperate counterattack.

"Boom!" The two forces collided, with Gu Qingfeng's attack overwhelming Pei Jing like a relentless torrent.
