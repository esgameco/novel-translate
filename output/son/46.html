This punch.

Clearly showed that he had already comprehended the divine rhythm of the martial art itself.

Gu Qingfeng's blood and qi transformed into a powerful qi, and when Gu Yang's punch landed on the qi, it was like hitting an extremely solid steel plate, the powerful recoil force making him involuntarily retreat several steps, his blood and qi surging unabated.

This scene.

Shocked Gu Yang.

He was well aware of the huge gap between himself and Gu Qingfeng, but he didn't expect the gap to be so great.

Even though he didn't use his full strength in that punch, he still couldn't break through Gu Qingfeng's blood and qi defense.

"The first move!"

Gu Qingfeng stood with his hands behind his back, his expression calm.

Gu Yang took a deep breath and immediately struck again.

This time.

He held nothing back.

An even stronger punch came crashing in, but it still failed to break through the defense of the blood and qi, and instead was injured by the powerful force.

At that moment.

Gu Yang suppressed the surging blood and qi, and struck out with a palm again, the powerful force causing the air to suddenly explode, as if a scorching aura accompanied it, the fierce and domineering palm heavily landing on the blood and qi.

In the next breath.

Gu Qingfeng's body trembled, and the blood and qi exploded, the terrifying impact force instantly sending Gu Yang flying.

In midair, Gu Yang barely stabilized his body, and after landing, he still couldn't stop his body, having to retreat several steps before finally coming to a stop.

"How was it?"

Gu Qingfeng had a faint smile on his face.

Gu Yang's face alternated between pale and red, and he finally lowered his head and said, "Father's cultivation is profound, and I am not worthy."

"My strength in the jianghu is not considered top-tier, and if you can't even break through my blood and qi, how can you have the qualifications to disregard everyone in the world?

The White Stone Dao may be small, but how do you know that the seemingly ordinary people are not hidden masters?

If you are always arrogant and conceited, you will only reap the consequences of your own actions.

Recognize your own strength, only then can you walk a long path!"

Gu Qingfeng admonished sternly.

"When I first came to the White Stone Dao, if I had acted like you, how could the Gu Family have reached today's status?

So before your strength is sufficient, you must learn to be low-key and hibernate, and only when your strength is strong enough can you truly disregard everything!"

Saying this, Gu Qingfeng's voice softened a bit.

"But your talent to enter the Refine Blood Realm at your age is indeed excellent. Cultivate well, and one day when you break through the Refine Bone Realm or even higher, you may not be completely without a chance."

"When I'm gone, the Gu Family will still need you to preside over the situation!"

Gu Qingfeng gave Gu Yang both a stick and a carrot, making his dejected face look much better.

"I understand, Father!"

"Good, you understand that's best. Well, there are some Refine Blood Pills here, just right for you to consolidate the foundation you just broke through. Go on and cultivate!"

Gu Qingfeng casually handed over eighty Refine Blood Pills.
