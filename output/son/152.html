These were ten Grandmaster Ascension Elixirs.

Among them:

Gu Qingfeng gave two Grandmaster Ascension Elixirs to Gu Peng and Xu Yulan.

For the former, it was to enhance his own strength, as his power needed to be elevated.

After all, as Gu Qingfeng's strength continued to grow, the realms he could involve himself in became higher. Merely refining the Dao Perfection realm would no longer be enough.

As for Xu Yulan, there's no need to say more.

Although Gu Qingfeng had been secluded in the Whetstone Hall these past few years, his feelings for her were quite complex.

Allowing her to advance to the Grandmaster realm, not only would it increase her lifespan, but also grant her a boost in strength.

When Gu Peng saw the Grandmaster Ascension Elixirs, he was stunned.

He had previously consumed the Breakthrough Elixir and Ascension Elixir, experiencing their profound mysteries.

However, even so, Gu Peng had never imagined that there would be an even more extraordinary elixir in the world.

A single elixir could allow one to directly break through the Grandmaster Perfection realm.

This was something Gu Peng had never conceived of before.

After all, Grandmasters were rare in the world, and the powerful experts who could enter this realm were as scarce as phoenix feathers and qilin horns.

But now, a single elixir could directly propel one into the Grandmaster realm, revealing the terrifying nature of such an elixir.

After breaking through the Grandmaster realm, Gu Peng's reverence for Gu Qingfeng only grew.

In the secret chamber of the Tianyang Sword Sect, an extremely sinister force was emanating, causing the blood-colored aura to surge in the sky, instilling a sense of unease in all the sect members.

This eerie power seemed to linger for a long time before gradually dissipating.

Thereafter, the door to the secret chamber opened, and the Sect Master of Tianyang emerged.

However, the Sect Master now wore a blood-colored robe, and an uncanny, icy aura permeated his brow, his eyes alternating between blood-red and normal.

The disciple standing guard outside the chamber had not even had a chance to speak when the blood-colored long hair pierced through his body, turning him into a dried corpse in just a few breaths.

When the sect elders arrived upon hearing the news, they were taken aback by this sight.

"Greet the Sect Master!" Gong Lei bowed his head as he faced the Sect Master's blood-red eyes, feeling a chill run down his spine.

Gong Lei could clearly see that the Sect Master's current state was vastly different from before his seclusion, as if he had become a completely different person.

Was it the awakening of an evil spirit? Or had his power spiraled out of control? Gong Lei did not know.

But one thing was certain: if he were to anger the Sect Master, he might very well meet his end here.

As for escaping, Gong Lei did not have the courage to even consider it.

Although he was a Fusion Realm cultivator, he was no different from an ant before the Sect Master's immense power.

After a long pause, Gong Lei finally felt the oppressive gaze disappear, replaced by the Sect Master's icy voice.

"Speak, what has happened during my seclusion?"

Hearing this, Gong Lei hastily replied, "Your Excellency, a few months ago, the Divine Warrior King Pei Jing died, and Elders Jiang Ning, Zuoqiu, and Cui Mingfeng all perished as well."

Upon hearing this, the Sect Master's eyes turned ice-cold, and the sinister, evil aura surrounding him intensified.

The Sect Master of the Tianyang Sect had emerged from seclusion this time, intending to gather a large number of living beings to cultivate himself and then break through to the realms above the Transformation Realm.

Ordinary people could not be compared to a Grand Master, even if there were millions of them, they would not be as useful as devouring a Grand Master.

If he could devour one.
