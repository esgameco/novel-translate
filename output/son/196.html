After Gu Qingfeng returned to the Gu Family Manor, the information panel kept updating constantly. When he saw the destruction of the Pei Family, his expression did not change much.

The Pei Family was destroyed.

This was an inevitable outcome.

The small Pei Family.

How much foundation could they have?

Even though the Pei Family was the governor of the Southern Yang Prefecture, controlling an entire prefecture, but with the death of Pei Jing, the Pei Family had already suffered a heavy blow. Now, how could the Pei Family withstand the power under Gu Yang's command.

So.

The destruction of the Pei Family was not something surprising.

It was only when he saw Gu Yang slaying a Metamorphosis realm cultivator with the Eradicate Evil Dagger that Gu Qingfeng's gaze showed some fluctuations.

Of course.

It was only a slight fluctuation.

Gu Qingfeng understood the power of the Eradicate Evil Dagger. He had nurtured this divine weapon with his Refine Sword Technique for many years, and it was constantly being tempered by the true essence. Now, he had even left a drop of his essence blood in the dagger, so slaying ordinary Metamorphosis realm cultivators was not a problem.

However.

If it were to face experts at the level of the Ancient Heavenly Emperor, it might not be enough.

But the Ancient Heavenly Emperor had died, and for the Heavenly Yang Sword Sect to produce a Metamorphosis realm cultivator was already difficult. The difficulty of producing another top-tier expert could be imagined.

"After this battle, the Heavenly Yang Sword Sect will no longer exist in the Qingyun State!"

Gu Qingfeng's expression was indifferent as he took out the jade bottle containing the severed finger. This was something he obtained after slaying the evil entity of the Blood Mountain Mansion.

In Gu Qingfeng's eyes, this severed finger should be on a similar level to the previous drop of blood.

The moment the finger appeared, Gu Qingfeng could see that it still emanated a vigorous life force, as if the finger in front of him was a living being, not a dead object.

There was a divine aura flowing on it, exuding a terrifying power.

When Gu Qingfeng focused his attention on the finger, he suddenly saw a vision of a desolate and ruined world, with countless corpses and skeletons piled up. A finger descended from the nine heavens, shattering the layers of void and sky, reducing all the bones and corpses to dust.

Suddenly, a blood light slashed through the endless void, coming towards him.

In an instant, the finger shattered, and blood splattered across the sky.

The scene then abruptly ended.

However, Gu Qingfeng remained dumbfounded at the spot. That blood light that slashed through the void and the finger seemed to be deeply imprinted in his mind, unable to be erased.

When Gu Qingfeng focused his attention and observed that blood light again, he finally saw what it truly was.

It was a blade!

To be precise, it was a powerful strike unleashed by a mysterious existence.

That strike.

Slashed through the heavens and the earth.

Slashed through the laws of the Dao.

And it also severed the finger in front of him.

The Heaven's Sword Technique that Gu Qingfeng had mastered was a true Grand Master martial art, but compared to this strike, it was like the difference between a firefly and the bright moon.

"This strike must have reached the level of transcending the Dao," Gu Qingfeng murmured to himself.

He was proud of creating the Heaven's Sword Technique, but now, compared to this strike, the gap was clearly revealed.

The cut on the finger still had lingering sword intent that refused to dissipate.

This sword intent, even just a wisp of it, was filled with a terrifying aura.

Gu Qingfeng had a premonition that if he could comprehend this sword intent and incorporate it into his own cultivation, the Heaven's Sword Technique could ascend to an even higher level.

This was a great opportunity! It was a true great opportunity. Just like the drop of blood left behind by the fallen Demonic Buddha of the Nine Curved Path, it was also a significant opportunity.

Gu Qingfeng understood that if he could refine that drop of blood, he would definitely gain immense power from it.

However, that drop of blood had given birth to a demonic entity, and Gu Qingfeng had no intention of recklessly attempting to harness such an uncontrollable power.

But the severed finger was different. The lingering sword intent on it could be comprehended to some extent.

Furthermore, Gu Qingfeng recalled the scene he had just witnessed, and his gaze towards the finger became more solemn.

"This finger, not knowing how many years it has been severed, can still retain such divine might. It is clear that the owner of this finger must be an extraordinary expert."

Beyond Grand Masters! This was undoubtedly the case.

But how much more terrifying this existence was compared to Grand Masters, he could not know.

"The Demonic Buddha of the Nine Curved Path contributed a drop of blood, and the evil entity of the Blood Mountain Mansion contributed this severed finger. It seems that the truly powerful demonic entities in the world may have various opportunities hidden within them."

Of course, this still needed further verification.

But if he had the chance...

Especially at the moment when the sword intent was about to strike him, Gu Qingfeng felt as if he was truly approaching death.

This was an opportunity!

But also a peril!

If ordinary people were to fear such sword intent, it would become their own inner demon, and they would never make any progress.

But if one could overcome the shock of such sword intent, there was hope to utilize it to temper oneself and advance further.

Clearly, Gu Qingfeng belonged to the latter category.

Although the inextinguishable sword intent was powerful, and the threat of facing death was terrifying, Gu Qingfeng had reached this point, his hands stained with countless lives, and he had never feared life and death.

Therefore, this inextinguishable sword intent deeply imprinted in his mind was completely transformed by Gu Qingfeng into the nourishment for the Heaven Sword, and various insights surged in his heart, allowing him to gain more understanding of the Heaven Sword he had created for the first time.

In the following time, Gu Qingfeng devoted himself to comprehending the inextinguishable sword intent, and only in his leisure time did he delve into the "Refining the Impurities" chapter of the Longevity Sutra.

It was just that the feeling of facing death and having one's soul seemingly slashed was not something that ordinary martial artists could endure.

Once or twice was fine.

This situation was something they had anticipated.

After all, no matter how powerful a Sect Master was, they were still human, and there would be a time when their strength was exhausted. Once they were surrounded by chaotic armies, even a Sect Master would have difficulty escaping death.

Seeing the change in the expressions of the others, Gu Yang remained much calmer.
