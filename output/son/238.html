Ominous aura!

Great opportunity! Gu Qingfeng looked at the bloodstained jade talisman, as if something on it had come alive, a sinister and cold aura suddenly erupted, and he felt as if he had fallen into endless darkness.

The next moment.

Gu Qingfeng burst forth with his vital energy, his terrifying blood energy power like the divine sun, sweeping away all the sinister and cold aura.

He had thought that demonic omens were just things that could not be mentioned, but now Gu Qingfeng realized that demonic omens were not that simple.
