"And now, as a Qi and Blood Grandmaster, I also possess a three thousand year lifespan!"

Gu Qingfeng's thoughts stirred, and then he used the Demon-Slaying Blade to cut his palm, allowing a drop of golden blood to flow out.

This blood, like liquid gold, contained a terrifying aura, originating from the transformation of his level of existence, not just a simple breakthrough in strength.

Looking at this drop of blood, Gu Qingfeng felt a sensation that was both familiar and unfamiliar.

The familiar part

Was that Gu Qingfeng could sense a power akin to that of demonic entities within this blood.

But the difference was

This power was fundamentally different from that of demonic entities, even though they existed on the same level of existence.

In this moment,

Gu Qingfeng understood

That he might now possess the ability to slay demonic entities.

But--

This would need to be verified.

Gu Qingfeng looked at the remaining 200-plus upgrade points and directly continued to improve his martial arts.
