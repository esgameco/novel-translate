Gu Qingfeng's pupils constricted slightly.

Even though he had mentally prepared himself, when he truly saw the change in his lifespan, his heart could not help but be shaken.

Ever since the moment he perfectly refined the impurities, Gu Qingfeng's lifespan had already broken through to two thousand years. Now, upon advancing to the Blood Qi Grandmaster realm, his lifespan has increased by another thousand years.

Now,

this is a lifespan of three thousand years.

"Transformation Realm cultivators have three thousand years of lifespan!"

"I, as a Blood Qi Grandmaster, also have three thousand years of lifespan!"

"Furthermore, with my perfected body refinement, my life realm has undergone another transformation, far surpassing that of mere cultivators!"

Gu Qingfeng's thoughts stirred, and then he used the Demon-Slaying Blade to cut his palm, allowing a drop of golden blood to flow out from the wound.

This blood, like liquid gold, contained a terrifying aura, which was the result of the transformation of his life realm, not just a simple breakthrough in strength.

Looking at this drop of blood, Gu Qingfeng felt a sense of familiarity and strangeness.

The familiarity

was that Gu Qingfeng could sense a power akin to that of demonic entities in this drop of blood.

But the difference was

that this power was fundamentally different from that of demonic entities, even though they were on the same life realm.

At this moment,

Gu Qingfeng understood that he might now possess the ability to slay demonic entities.

But—

this would need to be verified.

Immediately after,

Gu Qingfeng looked at the remaining 200-plus upgrade points and proceeded to further enhance his martial arts.

The difference was

that the power of this true qi was even greater than the true qi that had been self-generated by the Scarlet Flame Divine Skill before.

The Extreme Dao Grandmaster he had attained must be far more powerful than an ordinary Grandmaster.

Gu Qingfeng looked at the remaining upgrade points and in one breath, directly upgraded the Prajna Sutra to the thirteenth layer of perfection.

This level of true qi robustness was several times stronger than when he had just entered the Extreme Dao Grandmaster realm.

Gu Qingfeng had perfected the Prajna Sutra. By conventional martial arts standards, he should now be at the peak of the Innate Grandmaster realm.

However.

Now, in terms of the Extreme Dao Grandmaster, he naturally had not yet reached the peak of the Grandmaster realm.

After all.

Although Gu Qingfeng's true qi had broken through, his entire qi and blood power had only just entered the Grandmaster realm and had not yet reached the limit of this realm.

But when he saw the required upgrade points for the breakthrough, his expression changed slightly.

"One thousand upgrade points!"

If the Prajna Sutra could be broken through, it would allow him to directly step into the Great Grandmaster realm.

After all.

The upgrade to the Grandmaster realm only required fifty upgrade points.

Even the breakthrough to Great Grandmaster would normally only require one or two hundred upgrade points.

But now.

For the martial arts breakthrough.

It required a full one thousand upgrade points.

Instantly.

Gu Qingfeng abandoned the idea of breaking the limit.

"The more advanced the martial arts, the more upgrade points are required for the breakthrough!"

"Even for the Great Grandmaster, if I were to break through the shackles and step into an even higher realm, the required upgrade points would likely be even more."

Gu Qingfeng never thought that he would be stuck at the Great Grandmaster realm, even though it was currently the pinnacle of the martial arts world.

But.

With the attribute panel, breaking through the Great Grandmaster's shackles would not be too difficult, not to mention effortless.

However.

Gu Qingfeng himself had not yet reached that step, so he did not waste time on this matter.

The boat will reach the bridge when it arrives.

Take it step by step and see!
