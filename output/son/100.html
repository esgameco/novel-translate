Gu Qingfeng was increasingly driven to improve his own strength.

He didn't care at all about the changes in the outside world.

Just as Gu Qingfeng had anticipated, many prominent families and sects in Guangyang Prefecture suffered heavy losses, with numerous powerful experts falling at the hands of the evil Buddha.

As a result, the martial arts forces in the Guangyang Prefecture had suddenly declined significantly.

In these chaotic times, territory and resources were of utmost importance.

When the strength of Guangyang Prefecture declined, other martial arts forces outside of Guangyang began to covet this place, secretly dispatching people to infiltrate and gradually encroach upon it.

The thousand-year-old family sects under Guangyang Prefecture would not agree to this.

After all, these sects had taken root here for many years, and if they allowed other external forces to encroach, it would be undermining their own foundations.

They could not tolerate this situation.

Conflict was inevitable! Although Guangyang Prefecture had suffered heavy losses in martial arts strength, its deep heritage of a thousand years could still barely stabilize the situation for the time being.

On the other side, there were also other forces wanting to intervene in the White Stone Path.

However, after Gu Qingfeng personally stepped in with thunderous might, slaying several powerful experts, the reputation of the Tyrannical Saber once again spread, causing the various martial arts forces wanting to encroach on the White Stone Path to once again meet with failure.

The awe-inspiring reputation of a Half-Saint Master was truly not to be underestimated.

Moreover, the White Stone Path's ranking within Guangyang Prefecture was not very high, and its control over resources was extremely limited. It was not worthwhile for them to fight to the death with a Half-Saint Master just for the White Stone Path.

In the blink of an eye, two years had passed.

On this day, the Gu Family Manor was brightly decorated, with guests coming and going endlessly.

This was the day of the young master of the Gu Family's wedding.

At the banquet, many renowned martial arts forces of Guangyang Prefecture had come.

"I heard that the one marrying the young master of the Gu Family this time is an ordinary and common woman!"

"That's right, I heard that many forces wanted to marry into the Gu Family, but the young master didn't take a liking to any of them. That woman being able to marry into the Gu Family is probably truly flying up to become a phoenix!"

"In recent years, the reputation of the Gu Family has become increasingly renowned, and everyone knows the name of the Tyrannical Saber, Gu Qingfeng. Just look at the chaotic situation in Guangyang Prefecture, yet the White Stone Path remains stable as ever!"

"Ah, I wonder what kind of celestial being was able to be taken in by the young master..."

At the banquet, many people were whispering and discussing, mostly expressing envy towards the woman who had married into the Gu Family.

After all, the Gu Family was now an undisputed top force.

At least in the small realm of Guangyang Prefecture, the Gu Family's reputation was resounding, not inferior to those thousand-year-old family sects.

A Half-Saint Master was no joke.

Many forces wanted to marry into the Gu Family to strengthen their foundations, but they never had the chance.

Unlike other forces, with the Gu Family's current status, they could choose to either marry into other forces to expand their foundations, or seek stable development on their own.

As long as the Tyrannical Saber did not die, who would dare to harm the Gu Family?

"The newlyweds have arrived!"

When this voice sounded, all the discussions fell silent as everyone turned their gaze towards a certain direction. There, a pair of newlyweds were slowly walking in.

The man had an upright bearing, with sharp brows and bright eyes, exuding an aura of heroism.
