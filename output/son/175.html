Gu Yang nodded, and a smile appeared on his face.

Taishi Yi took his leave.

"Your Majesty, please take a look."

The content recorded in the intelligence was about Taishu Yi gathering a large army.

The other party thought this matter was kept secret, but it was all seen by the undercover guards. Taishu Yi would have such actions, and Gu Yang could understand it with just a little thought - the other party was coming to the Liuyun Manor to make preparations for this expedition.

From the very beginning.

Gu Yang had no intention of taking action against Taishu Yi.

The reason was very simple.

Taishu Yi was a true defector, whether it was sincere or not, Gu Yang also had to put on a good show outwardly.

So.

Gu Yang was not stingy at all, and directly granted Taishu Yi the position of the Prefect of Qingjiang Prefecture.

At the same time, this was also to tell others.

If they defect, Gu Yang will certainly not treat them badly.

Afterwards.

Gu Yang came to the central hall of the sand table.

On it.

The twelve prefectures of Taishan Prefecture have all been marked with his own flag.

During this period, Gu Yang led the army to conquer various places, and nine of the twelve prefectures of Taishan Prefecture have fallen under his control.

Now.

Taishu Yi has defected in the name of the Prefect, and the remaining three prefectures have also fallen under his control.

In this way.

The twelve prefectures of Taishan Prefecture have all become Gu Yang's.

Looking at the sand table in front of him, Gu Yang's expression was somewhat dazed.

At one time.

He spent a lot of time and resources, and could only barely cultivate a hundred thousand-strong army, lurking in the Baishi Road waiting for an opportunity.

But with the arrival of the Shenwu Army at the Baishi Road, everything underwent an earth-shattering reversal.

First, Gu Yang led a hundred thousand-strong army to defeat the Shenwu Army led by Pei Jing, and then he suppressed the entire Guangyang Prefecture with a thunderous force and proclaimed himself king.

Then.

It only took a few months to completely subdue the twelve prefectures of Taishan Prefecture.

Looking back now.

Gu Yang found that he had unknowingly grasped the territory of a prefecture, with an army of over a hundred thousand, and there were not a few powerful experts.

The grand master martial artists who were once high above, Gu Yang now had more than a dozen of them under his command.

Regarding this change.

Gu Yang was very clear.

It was not because of his own strength, nor because of his many plans, but because he was backed by a powerful figure who was revered as the supreme master of the martial arts world today.

"In this world, the strong are supreme!"

Gu Yang sighed inwardly.

A supremely powerful figure can change the pattern of the world.

Looking back now, Gu Yang found that he had been a bit negligent in his cultivation.

Possessing the territory of a prefecture, with abundant cultivation resources, and his own innate talent was not bad, but so far, he had only just broken through the middle stage of Refining Bone.

If it was in the past, the middle stage of Refining Bone would not be weak.

But if he looks at the Nine Provinces, this level of cultivation power is a bit insufficient.

Immediately.

Gu Yang made a decision.

Now that the entire Taishan Prefecture is under his control, the next step is to rest and recuperate for a period of time, and also to improve his own cultivation.

Not to say that he will raise it to a high level, but at least it should be decent.
