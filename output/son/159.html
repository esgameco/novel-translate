In the city.

Gu Qingfeng's expression remained indifferent.

Beside him, Gu Yang was still unaware of what had just happened, until he suddenly sensed a powerful surge of true qi from Gu Qingfeng, which made him feel greatly threatened.

Under the influence of this true qi, Gu Yang felt as insignificant as an ant.

However,

Seeing Gu Qingfeng's aura subside, Gu Yang couldn't help but ask, "Father, what happened?"

"Nothing, just a minor issue that I have now resolved," Gu Qingfeng replied calmly.

Although he spoke lightly, Gu Yang knew that the matter was far from simple.

But exactly what had happened, Gu Yang did not yet know.

Soon,

a city guard came to report the incident that had occurred in the city.

Upon hearing that Gu Qingfeng had killed an elder of an immortal sect from a distance, Gu Yang was stunned for a long time.

After a long while,

Gu Yang gradually regained his composure.

He never imagined that Gu Qingfeng's strength would be so formidable, to be able to slay an immortal sect elder with a single strike from a distance.

At this moment,

Gu Yang truly understood why Gu Qingfeng had said he was 100% confident in dealing with the ancient Gu Yitian.

Even the sect master of the Tianyang Sword Sect might not be able to accomplish such a feat.

Instantaneously killing an immortal sect elder! This was a completely different level from just killing an immortal sect elder.

Suddenly,

a person abruptly appeared in the hall.

Almost simultaneously,

Gu Qingfeng's sleeve fluttered, and a tyrannical and unparalleled gale of true qi struck out, causing the person to change expression and hastily try to block it, while also speaking.

"Senior Patriarch Gu, please do not attack, I mean no harm--"

Before the words could finish,

the gale of true qi had already reached him.

Although the person exerted his full strength to resist, he was still knocked back several steps, his entire body's qi and blood boiling violently, unable to calm down for a long time.

"Who are you, and why have you appeared here?"

Gu Qingfeng did not attack again, but his gaze towards the newcomer was cold, and a terrifying true intent seemed to lock him down, as if any movement from the other party would invite a devastating strike.

"I am Ancient Cloud, the sect master of the Shenwu Sect. I greet you, Senior Gu!"

Ancient Cloud dared not speak any further, deeply feeling his own insignificance under the oppressive true intent. If he had delayed even half a word, he might have been destroyed on the spot.
