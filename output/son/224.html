More importantly,

the forces behind Han An and the others involved the Guiyuan Empire, and Gu Yang did not dare to be careless.

Now that he had become the lord of a dynasty, Gu Yang naturally understood how powerful it was to wield the destiny of a dynasty.

If it was a dynasty, then it was already so powerful.

If it was an empire,

In the dim Heavenly Prison, there was a damp and mossy smell.

Gu Qingfeng and Gu Yang walked into the Heavenly Prison, without any other guards following them.

"This Heavenly Prison is divided into nine layers. The first five layers are for imprisoning ordinary criminals, while the last four layers are for masters and above."

This shows the preciousness of the mysterious iron.

If Gu Yang was not the lord of the Nine States, he would not have been able to collect so much mysterious iron to build the ninth layer of the Heavenly Prison.

When the two entered the ninth layer of the Heavenly Prison, the place was as bright as day.

In the two adjacent cells, an old man and a young man were imprisoned.

The two were covered in blood, their ribs pierced by iron hooks, but their wounds did not bleed.

After hearing the sound of someone approaching, the old man, Ke Lao, leaning against the wall, opened his cloudy eyes and looked at Gu Yang with a cold smile.

"Old man advises you to be sensible and let us go. The Guiyuan Empire is not someone you can offend, even if your Shenwu Dynasty has a Daogong Realm expert as its pillar."

"Furthermore, we have left soul tablets in the Guiyuan Empire. If we perish, the empire will immediately sense it."

"When the imperial army of the empire arrives in the Nine States, you will regret it too late!"

At this point, Ke Lao was not panicked at all, still trying to intimidate with the might of the Guiyuan Empire.

In his view, since Gu Yang had not killed him, he must be wary of the Guiyuan Empire, so he would not dare to act rashly.

After all, even in the Eastern Domain, the Guiyuan Empire was a major force. A mere Shenwu Dynasty would be insignificant in their eyes.

However,

faced with Ke Lao's words, Gu Yang's expression remained indifferent.

"I'll give you one more chance. Tell me the purpose of your coming and all the information about the Guiyuan Empire, and perhaps I can spare your life."

"Hmph!"

Ke Lao snorted coldly without answering.

Seeing this,

Gu Yang's expression suddenly turned cold, then he looked at Gu Qingfeng beside him.

"This person is a half-step Daogong Realm expert of the Guiyuan Empire. Unfortunately, his mouth is too stubborn, so I'll have to trouble the father to take care of him."

Hearing Gu Yang's words, Ke Lao finally looked at Gu Qingfeng beside him, his expression somewhat doubtful.

"Are you the Martial Saint Gu Qingfeng?"

This name,

Ke Lao was not unfamiliar with.

But in his calculations, this person should be a Shenbian Realm cultivator. If Gu Yang had not mentioned it, Ke Lao would have almost mistaken him for an ordinary person.

Thinking about this,

Ke Lao's heart was shocked again.

Even though his cultivation was now sealed, a Shenbian Realm cultivator should not be able to hide their strength from him, but now he could not see through the other party's true power, which was somewhat intriguing.

"It seems you have heard of my existence."

Gu Qingfeng stepped in front of Ke Lao, looking at him calmly.

To this,

Ke Lao remained silent.

Gu Qingfeng shook his head, "Judging from your appearance, even if I ask, you probably won't be willing to speak. Well, since you don't want to say it, then let me take a look myself!"

"You want to search my soul!"

Ke Lao seemed to have thought of something, his expression suddenly changing drastically, but then he sneered.

"You think you can search this old man's soul? Wishful thinking—"

However,

before Ke Lao could finish his words, a terrifying spiritual force from Gu Qingfeng had already forcefully invaded his sea of consciousness.

At that moment,

Ke Lao's face changed drastically, and his gaze became increasingly frightened.

"Daogong—"

As soon as he uttered these two words, his expression became dull.

At the same time,

Gu Qingfeng's spiritual sense was rampantly searching through the other's memories, and he soon found a lot of information.

The only regret was that the person in front of him was a half-step Daogong Realm expert. Although he had not truly opened the Daogong and cultivated his primordial spirit, his spiritual sense was still quite powerful.

And with the other party having lived for thousands of years, his memories were extremely long. Even if Gu Qingfeng wanted to thoroughly examine all his memories, it would be difficult to do so.

However,

despite this,

Gu Qingfeng still obtained the information he wanted.

"The Guiyuan Empire!"

"Imperial Worship!"

"Beast Taming Technique... Saint's Legacy!"

Gu Qingfeng murmured, his gaze towards the other party now filled with understanding.

In Ke Lao's memories, the Guiyuan Empire was established by a half-saint expert ten thousand years ago.

Since then,

the power of the Guiyuan Empire has been growing stronger.

Now,

although the Guiyuan Empire does not have a half-saint level expert as its pillar, it does have several Daogong Realm experts, and the one who controls the Guiyuan Empire, Emperor Han, is even a ninth-stage Daogong Realm expert.

As long as he does not encounter any unexpected calamity, he may very well step into the half-saint realm in the future.

At the same time,

Gu Qingfeng also understood why Ke Lao and the others were able to find the Nine States.

They knew the Beast Taming Technique, controlling a large number of fierce beasts to roam the Ancient Beast Mountain Range, and then based on the feedback from these fierce beasts, they were able to locate the Nine States.

It has to be said that

this kind of method is quite surprising to Gu Qingfeng.

It also indirectly explains the fundamental reason why the number of fierce beasts causing havoc in the Nine States has been increasing.

As for the purpose of the two, it was just as Gu Qingfeng had guessed - they came for the Saint's Legacy.

Even the Guiyuan Empire, which once had a half-saint as its pillar, highly valued the Saint's Legacy.

With the Saint's Legacy or without it, the difference in strength could be heaven and earth.

When Gu Qingfeng's spiritual sense withdrew, Ke Lao's face was deathly pale, and his gaze towards Gu Qingfeng was filled with both terror and mockery.

"You already know the strength of my Guiyuan Empire. If you release us now, there is still room for negotiation. Otherwise, it will be a dead end—"

"Bang!"

Before Ke Lao could finish speaking, Gu Qingfeng flicked his finger, causing his head to explode and die on the spot.

While Shenbian Realm experts could regenerate their severed limbs, they could not regrow their heads once they were destroyed.

"Ke Lao!!"

In the adjacent cell, Han An saw the headless corpse and could no longer suppress the terror in his heart. Looking at Gu Qingfeng, his face was filled with fear.

"Don't kill me!"

"I am the Sixth Prince of the Guiyuan Empire. If you kill me, my father the emperor will not let you off!"

Seeing that Gu Qingfeng did not respond, but instead came directly to his cell, Han An's heart became even more terrified.

"I'll tell you!"

"I'll say anything, just don't kill me... don't..."

As he spoke,

a warm flow had already leaked from Han An's crotch, the Sixth Prince of the Guiyuan Empire had been completely frightened out of his wits.

"I still believe what I saw."

Gu Qingfeng shook his head, then immediately used his methods to search Han An's mind.

Searching Han An's memories was much easier than searching Ke Lao's, after all, the gap in their realms was not small.

The former was only at the sixth stage of the Shenbian Realm, while the latter had already reached the half-step Daogong Realm.

Very soon,

Han An's memories were thoroughly searched.

Just as Gu Qingfeng was about to focus on searching for the cultivation techniques of the Guiyuan Empire, suddenly, a huge spiritual sense abruptly appeared.

"Who dares to pry into the legacy of my Guiyuan Empire? Seeking death!"

The spiritual sense erupted. Such a terrifying aura could crush ordinary Daogong Realm experts.

But Gu Qingfeng's eyes turned cold, and he responded with his own spiritual sense in kind.

To be honest, although he was currently only at the Dao Palace Realm Fourth Layer, his foundation was far beyond what a typical Dao Palace Realm Fourth Layer expert could match.

From the prohibition in Han An's sea of consciousness, it seemed that even the Dao Palace Realm Ninth Layer was not as strong as he had imagined.

Therefore, even if he were to face a Dao Palace Realm Ninth Layer expert, Gu Qingfeng would have the confidence to fight.

These words put Gu Yang's heart at ease, and his gaze towards Gu Qingfeng was filled with admiration.

As long as the Saint does not appear, there would be no major problems.

In that case, even if the Dao Palace Realm Ninth Layer expert of the Yuanxuan Imperial Dynasty were to come in person, there would not be much of an issue? Gu Qingfeng nodded in agreement under Gu Yang's expectant gaze.
