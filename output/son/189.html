"Your offspring 'Gu Yang' has been attacked by a Fusion Realm cultivator!"

"Your offspring 'Gu Yang' has slain the Fusion Realm cultivator with the Demon-Slaying Blade!"

Inside the Whetstone Hall, Gu Qingfeng glanced at the attribute panel, and when he saw the news of the Fusion Realm cultivator's attack, his eyes cooled slightly, then quickly returned to normal.

"Is the Fusion Realm cultivator from the Tianyang Sword Sect, or some other force?"

Gu Qingfeng murmured to himself.

Cultivators at this level of strength are much rarer than Xiantian Grandmasters.

Any Fusion Realm cultivator, regardless of their identity, holds a position of great power.

Even within the Immortal Sects, this level of existence can be considered as part of the upper echelons.

Therefore, when Gu Qingfeng received the news of a Fusion Realm cultivator's attack, the first thing he thought of was the Tianyang Sword Sect.

After all, apart from the Tianyang Sword Sect, he really couldn't think of anyone else who would use such a powerful expert to assassinate.

However, Gu Qingfeng didn't ponder for long.

As a guard entered and handed him the flying messenger's letter, all of Gu Qingfeng's doubts were resolved.

"The Commander of the Demon-Suppressing Bureau in Nanyang Prefecture, Nangong Yu!"

Regarding the existence of the Demon-Suppressing Bureau, Gu Qingfeng hadn't heard much about it for some time.

The Demon-Suppressing Bureau is an institution under the Taixuan Dynasty responsible for suppressing evil forces, with branches in every state and prefecture.

The Guangyang Prefecture previously had a Demon-Suppressing Bureau branch as well.

But since Gu Yang's uprising, the Demon-Suppressing Bureau in Guangyang Prefecture had disappeared without a trace.

Evidently, the Demon-Suppressing Bureau in Guangyang Prefecture neither had the ability to deal with Gu Yang, nor the intention to side with him, so they withdrew to preserve their strength.

Later on, as Gu Yang annexed the entire Taishan Prefecture, all the Demon-Suppressing Bureau branches in the region seemed to have vanished into thin air, leaving no trace behind.

Afterward, as Gu Qingfeng's strength grew, he didn't pay much attention to the Demon-Suppressing Bureau.

After all, the Demon-Suppressing Bureau was just an institution of the Taixuan Dynasty, no matter how powerful it might be.

Gu Qingfeng, with his own strength, had the right to ignore the existence of the Demon-Suppressing Bureau.

Until now, when the flying messenger from Nanyang Prefecture arrived, Gu Qingfeng was reminded of the Demon-Suppressing Bureau again.

However, with Gu Yang's Demon-Slaying Blade to protect him, a mere Fusion Realm cultivator attempting to assassinate him would be courting their own destruction.

To put it bluntly, the Demon-Slaying Blade that now bears Gu Qingfeng's power, in his eyes, could even slay a Nascent Soul cultivator, let alone a mere Fusion Realm cultivator.

Afterward, Gu Qingfeng turned his attention to the other contents of the letter.

After a moment, Gu Qingfeng's palm trembled, and he crushed the letter into powder.

Blood sacrifice of the common people.

The evil force sealed in the Blood Mountain Prefecture.

These pieces of information appeared, all of which made Gu Qingfeng's expression turn cold.

Evidently, the Tianyang Sword Sect is making a last-ditch effort.

Their reputation no longer matters, as they are trying to preserve the lineage of the Tianyang Sword Sect, which is why they are resorting to such desperate measures.

After all, even though the Immortal Sects require the life force and blood of the common people for cultivation, they rarely commit the act of massacring entire cities.

The cultivators view the common people as livestock to be harvested periodically, ensuring a continuous supply.

In contrast, if they were to kill everyone all at once, there would be no possibility of a steady, long-term supply.
