In the past, it would have been a heinous crime punishable by the confiscation of property and the extermination of the family, but now the court has come forward to enfeoff him, which is quite thought-provoking.

After thinking about it.

Gu Qingfeng also understood the key point.

"Boom!"

The Fist of Sun and Moon has reached Perfection.

The Ninth-Level True Meaning was born.

This year, Gu Qingfeng was 43 years old.

His martial dao realm had advanced to the Extreme Dao Master Middle Stage.

The perfection of a supreme martial art had greatly enhanced Gu Qingfeng's strength, allowing his original realm to take another step forward.

Compared to the Extreme Dao Master Initial Stage, after breaking through to the Extreme Dao Master Middle Stage, Gu Qingfeng felt that his vital energy and physical strength had become even more powerful than before.

At this point, the role of true qi had actually diminished significantly.

Gu Qingfeng was clear about the reason for this.

Martial artists emphasize the tempering of the physical body, with the cultivation of vital energy as the main focus, while the appearance of true qi was a bit of a detour.

After all, it is not easy for martial artists to cultivate vital energy, so once they reach the perfection of refining the viscera, the truly powerful experts who can break through to become Vital Energy Masters are few and far between.

Some martial artists who find it difficult to break through to the Vital Energy Master realm instead choose to cultivate true qi, thereby attaining mastery through an alternative method, becoming Innate Masters.

However, vital energy is still the foundation for martial artists.

This is why Vital Energy Masters are stronger than Innate Masters.

As for Extreme Dao Masters, they are the next level of transformation from Vital Energy Masters.

It can be said that the vital energy of Extreme Dao Masters is vastly superior to that of the so-called Vital Energy Masters.

"With my current Extreme Dao Master Middle Stage cultivation, even without the power of two great heavenly veins, I would be able to suppress the Ancient Overlord Emperor with ease!"

"If I were to activate the power of a heavenly vein, a single heavenly vein would be enough to instantly slay him!"

A gleam of determination flashed in Gu Qingfeng's eyes.

Unfortunately, compared to the existence that had left behind its bloodline, his cultivation was still lacking.

But these problems were not significant.

His cultivation would steadily accumulate step by step.

In the future, Gu Qingfeng would have the confidence to confront that Nine Heavens Buddha.

Before that, he must strive to continuously elevate his cultivation realm.

Each breakthrough to a higher level of Extreme Dao Master requires a substantial accumulation of cultivation.

Now that the Scripture of the Sun and Moon Fist had been perfected, further breakthroughs would require too much effort, so he needed to focus on other aspects to make progress.

With this in mind, Gu Qingfeng turned his gaze to the supreme martial arts of various sects. (End of this chapter)
