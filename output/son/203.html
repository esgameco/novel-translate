During this period, the information panel has also been constantly refreshing with new text.

"Your offspring 'Gu Yang' has received guidance from a Grand Master, and his cultivation has made some progress!"

When he saw that Gu Yang had broken through to the perfect Refine Bone Realm, Gu Qingfeng's expression did not change much.

After all, Gu Yang now has a large amount of resources at his disposal, and he has also received guidance from a Dao Grand Master, so his breakthrough in cultivation is only natural.

On the contrary, if Gu Yang had not made any progress in his cultivation, that would have been truly surprising.

Now that Gu Yang has broken through, Gu Qingfeng directly poured the extra 100 Promotion Points into the Pure Yang True Body.

In an instant, this Grand Master technique was upgraded from the initial stage to the perfect level by Gu Qingfeng.

"The Pure Yang True Body has reached perfection!"

"The Flesh Body has reached the 4th level of Purity Yang!"

"This technique is indeed extraordinary!"

After the Pure Yang True Body reached perfection, Gu Qingfeng felt the terrifying pure yang qi and blood within him, and his expression showed some surprise.

If this pure yang qi and blood power were cultivated by others, even if it could not trigger a breakthrough in their life realm, it could still make the cultivator's qi and blood far stronger than ordinary Grand Masters.

This shows that the expert who created the Pure Yang True Body had extraordinary vision and strength.

Unfortunately, the Pure Yang True Body could only be considered an ordinary Grand Master technique, as it could not trigger a breakthrough in the cultivator's life realm.

At most, it is just a powerful Grand Master technique, but has not reached a higher level of cultivation.

However, even so, the perfection of the Pure Yang True Body has made Gu Qingfeng's qi and blood much more refined, although it has not reached the level of breaking through the Extreme Dao Grandmaster realm, it is still stronger than before.

"To break through the Extreme Dao Grandmaster realm, I still need some accumulation!"

"But as long as I have enough Grand Master techniques, the difficulty of improving my cultivation in the Extreme Dao Grandmaster realm is not too great!"

Gu Qingfeng thought to himself.

Normally, to take a martial art from the initial stage to perfection only requires 200 Promotion Points.

200 Promotion Points, while not a small amount, is also not too much. At the current rate of progress, it should take about one or two years to accumulate enough.

The biggest problem is that higher-level martial arts are difficult to obtain, and can only be improved through the method of breaking limits.

Martial art limit breaking, the consumption required is beyond normal comprehension.

For example, for a Grand Master martial art, the normal promotion only requires 50 Promotion Points, but if it is to be broken through the limit, the requirement is ten times that.

Such consumption is self-evident.

Afterwards, Gu Qingfeng's gaze fell on the various martial arts in the Whetstone Hall.

Since the extermination of the Tian Yang Sword Sect and the conquest of the various prefectures of Qingyan State by the Guangyang Army, Gu Yang has been constantly confiscating martial arts from all over the world and sending them here.

But because Gu Qingfeng currently has requirements, he does not want any martial arts below the Grand Master level, so the martial arts sent by Gu Yang are mostly Grand Master martial arts.

These Grand Master martial arts include sword techniques, fist techniques, as well as other internal cultivation methods and body refining techniques.

However, most of these techniques are not worthy of Gu Qingfeng's attention, and if he were to cultivate them, they would basically be placed at the end.

After a long time, Gu Qingfeng's gaze fell on a martial art.

The Refine Blood True Classic! A Grand Master martial art! This was obtained by Gu Yang after the extermination of the Tian Yang Sword Sect, a truly top-tier martial art.

This technique is similar to the Pure Yang True Body, both focusing on refining the cultivator's qi and blood, and the "Refine Blood" in the Refine Blood True Classic refers to refining the cultivator's blood to possess terrifying power.

According to the records in the Refine Blood True Classic, if a cultivator can refine their blood to a sufficiently terrifying level, they may even be able to achieve the feat of "Dripping Blood Resurrection".

However, the so-called "Dripping Blood Resurrection" is merely a conjecture of the creator of this technique.

After all, even a Grand Master martial art, no matter how it is cultivated to the highest level of perfection, cannot truly achieve the feat of "Dripping Blood Resurrection".

"Dripping Blood Resurrection, the imagination of the creator of this technique is not bad, but relying on a Grand Master martial art to achieve this is a bit far-fetched.

However, if a Grand Master cannot do it, perhaps those above the Grand Master realm may be able to, as there is no absolute impossibility in such matters."

Gu Qingfeng thought of the drop of blood that gave birth to the Jiuqu Dao Demonic Buddha.

A single drop of blood had such divine power, perhaps those extraordinary experts could truly master the method of "Dripping Blood Resurrection" as the creator of the Refine Blood True Classic had imagined.

But these matters are too distant for Gu Qingfeng.
