The Heavenly Sun Sword Sect.

When the news of Elder Duan Ping's death reached the sect, the vast Heavenly Sun Sword Sect was shaken.

After all, any elder of the Heavenly Sun Sword Sect was a powerful cultivator at the Innate level.

Such powerful experts rarely met with untimely deaths.

Now that an elder had fallen, the impact was immeasurable.

In an instant, many powerful cultivators within the sect emerged from their seclusions and gathered in the main hall of the sect.

Just then, a figure suddenly appeared on the empty seat of the sect master, and an eerie, icy aura permeated the hall.

All the sect cultivators hurriedly stood up and bowed respectfully to the figure.

"Greetings, Sect Master!"

"No need for formalities," the Sect Master's hoarse voice replied.

The Sect Master's face was pale and devoid of any color, but his eyes were slightly reddish, as if filled with boundless demonic power, making it difficult to meet his gaze.

"Regarding Elder Duan Ping's death, what are your thoughts?"

The Sect Master's crimson gaze fell upon the crowd, and everyone felt immense pressure.

After a moment, a powerful cultivator spoke up solemnly, "My lord, Gu Qingfeng brazenly killed the elder, disregarding our Heavenly Sun Sword Sect. I believe we should use thunderous means to annihilate the Gu Clan Manor and restore the reputation of our sect!"

"Correct, the Gu Clan Manor must be destroyed!"

"Gu Qingfeng, for killing our sect's elder, he deserves to die!"

"What Saber Cultivator? A mere martial artist dares to be so arrogant, truly ignorant of his own mortality!"

As one person spoke, the others also chimed in, not even considering the Gu Clan Manor as worthy of their attention.

Indeed, the Heavenly Sun Sword Sect had the right to do so. As the true hegemon of the entire Qingyun Province, the Heavenly Sun Sword Sect was an immortal power, while the Gu Clan Manor and its predecessor, the Gu Clan, had only been established for a mere two decades.

The Sect Master spoke calmly, "The Shenquan Sect's elder was a Grandmaster hundreds of years ago, and later achieved the Enlightened Spirit Realm through demonic cultivation. Even without breaking through to the Fusion Spirit Realm, his strength is likely not far behind.

In terms of power, Shen Wudao is comparable to a True Meaning Grandmaster.

Since Gu Qingfeng was able to slay Shen Wudao, it means his strength is at least at the True Meaning Grandmaster level, perhaps even on par with a Half-Step Great Grandmaster!"

"Such a powerful expert, even a Fusion Spirit Realm cultivator might not be able to defeat him."

"Among those present, how many of you have reached the peak of the Fusion Spirit Realm, and who has the confidence to slay Gu Qingfeng?"

At these words, the hall fell silent.

The Sect Master's words had completely shattered their remaining pride.

The peak of the Fusion Spirit Realm was only equivalent to a Half-Step Great Grandmaster.

If the Saber Cultivator truly possessed strength comparable to a Half-Step Great Grandmaster, the outcome of a life-and-death battle would be uncertain.

"Furthermore, the Gu Clan Manor is located in Guangyang Prefecture. If our Heavenly Sun Sword Sect were to mobilize a large force, the imperial court might not turn a blind eye.

In the past, Gu Qingfeng did not display enough value, so the imperial court might have ignored him. But now, with a cultivator suspected to be at the True Meaning Grandmaster or even Half-Step Great Grandmaster level, the imperial court is likely looking for an opportunity to bring the Gu Clan Manor into their fold."

"Therefore, we shall set aside the matter of Elder Duan Ping for now."

"Within a hundred years, I will personally go and slay Gu Qingfeng!"

The Sect Master's words made the crowd's eyes light up.

"The Sect Master is going to break through?"
