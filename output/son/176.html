Gu Qingfeng, who was in seclusion in the Whetstone Hall, saw the content on the information panel and his expression changed slightly.

Immediately, he left the Whetstone Hall and went to the inner courtyard.

There, he saw Gu Shengnan being held in Xu Yulan's arms, and Zhong Li's expression was one of both joy and concern.

When they saw Gu Qingfeng arrive, both of them were startled.

"Husband (Father)!"

"Mm."

Gu Qingfeng waved his hand, and his gaze was fixed on Gu Shengnan the entire time.

The two-year-old infant had a round, chubby little face, making one have the urge to pinch her cheeks.

However, what Gu Qingfeng was concerned about was not these superficial details, but the powerful qi and blood force within her that was not normal for an infant.

Entering the martial dao!

This was something Gu Qingfeng had not expected.

After all, for normal children to cultivate the martial dao, they would need to be at least six or seven years old, when their foundation is somewhat stable, before they can begin cultivation.

If they are too young, hastily cultivating the martial dao can easily lead to problems.

Because infants are physically weak, how can they bear the power of the martial dao's qi and blood?

So, the moment Gu Qingfeng saw Gu Shengnan, he grasped her small hand and probed her condition with a trace of true qi.

After a while, Gu Qingfeng's expression eased somewhat.

On the other side, Xu Yulan's expression was also slightly tense: "Husband, is Shengnan alright?"

To be honest, when they saw Gu Shengnan cross the threshold of the martial dao and cultivate the martial dao's qi and blood, Xu Yulan was also somewhat surprised.

Hearing this, Gu Qingfeng shook his head: "The problem is not serious. Although her qi and blood are abundant, her meridians and bones are also different from ordinary people. And this qi and blood power will gradually and imperceptibly transform her. If nurtured over the years, it will bring great benefits."

"Now it seems that nourishing herself with innate true qi is also quite beneficial for an infant, but Shengnan is still too young to truly cultivate the martial dao. After all, the tempering of the martial dao, if practiced without sufficient age, will only harm oneself. But if she only nurtures herself with innate true qi, there is no major problem."

Hearing Gu Qingfeng's words, Xu Yulan and Zhong Li both visibly relaxed.

On the other hand, Gu Qingfeng was also somewhat surprised by Gu Shengnan's current situation.

After all, the foundation of the Gu Clan Manor is ultimately weak, not like those immortal sects and noble families that have a thousand-year heritage, where many things and experiences have been painstakingly explored by their ancestors and then inherited and perfected by later generations.

In comparison, the Gu Clan Manor is not on par with these immortal sects in this regard.

However, Gu Qingfeng has also indirectly confirmed one thing:

The reason why those immortal sects can endure for a long time is that they can continuously cultivate enough powerful experts.

Nourishing the physique with innate true qi, even those with mediocre aptitude can be forcibly transformed.
