Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Luo Yu suddenly dismissed the attendants and all the singing girls around him left the room. The other unrelated people also followed them out.

Seeing this, Chu Tianxiong's expression became solemn.

"According to the news, the Great Army of the Shenwu King has already occupied most of the Danyang Prefecture. At most within a year, the Great Army will conquer the entire Danyang Prefecture.

At that time, the Great Army of the Shenwu King will certainly march into the Taishan Prefecture.

Now the Taixuan Dynasty is nearing its end, and the Shenwu King has amassed three prefectures in just a few years. He has a high chance of contending for the Heavenly Throne in the future.

As long as we can first seize the Baishi Road, when the Great Army of the Shenwu King arrives, we can coordinate with the army to capture Guangyang Prefecture. When the Shenwu King ascends the supreme throne, you and I will be meritorious subjects following the dragon!"

Luo Yu's expression was grave, and his words caused Chu Tianxiong's face to change slightly, but he soon smiled and said, "It is an honor for the Chu family to serve the Shenwu King. Whatever needs to be done next, the old man will not hesitate!"

"The next step is simple - while dealing with the Qin family, we will gradually devour the other forces on the Baishi Road. When the Great Army of the Shenwu King arrives, I only need the Iron Bone Gang to be the sole voice of the Baishi Road."

"Then what about the Tianyun Sect--"

"You don't need to worry about the Tianyun Sect, Yun Changkong is already serving the Shenwu King like me."

Luo Yu smiled slightly.

Hearing this, Chu Tianxiong also felt relieved.

Afterwards, Chu Tianxiong said, "During this time, my Chu family has also been gradually targeting the Gu family, but the Gu family seems to have no reaction at all. Facing the constant pressure from my Chu family, the Gu family has not shown any response.

Is there a problem here?"

Mentioning the Gu family, there was a hint of apprehension in Chu Tianxiong's eyes.

He was not afraid of the Gu family's resistance, but rather that the Gu family would remain as silent as they are now.

As the saying goes, "A biting dog does not bark."

The more the Gu family remained silent, the more uneasy Chu Tianxiong felt.

Especially since the Gu family patriarch's strength was unfathomable. In Chu Tianxiong's judgment, the other party was likely a mid-stage or even late-stage Blood Refining cultivator.

Luo Yu's expression was indifferent: "You don't need to worry about the Gu family problem. You just need to gradually devour the Gu family. If Gu Qingfeng doesn't take action, then so be it. But if he dares to take action, I, the Gang Master, will deal with him.

Any who dare to resist the Shenwu King will not have a good end!"

Regarding the Gu family, Luo Yu never took them seriously.

In the past, when he sent people to recruit the Gu family, they were politely rejected. In Luo Yu's mind, the Gu family's fate had already been sealed.

Letting the Chu family secretly devour the Gu family was because the Iron Bone Gang was currently fully focused on dealing with the Qin family and had no time to spare.

Moreover, the Iron Bone Gang could not openly attack the Gu family right now. After all, there were still many neutral forces in the Baishi Road, and if the Iron Bone Gang publicly attacked the Gu family, it might cause the other neutral forces to feel threatened and side with the Qin family.

This situation was not what Luo Yu wanted to see.

Letting the Chu family devour the Gu family was because the Iron Bone Gang had suffered heavy losses in its fight against the Qin family over the years, and it needed to plunder resources from other directions to replenish its own losses.

Once the Qin family problem was truly resolved, the Iron Bone Gang would then turn its attention to dealing with these other forces, which would be much easier then.

Hearing this, Chu Tianxiong also felt relieved. With the Iron Bone Gang backing him up, the Gu family's problem was no longer an issue, even if their patriarch was formidable.

However, to Chu Tianxiong's disappointment, no matter how the Chu family secretly pressured them, the Gu family remained indifferent and ignored the loss of their business assets.

This feeling made Chu Tianxiong feel like he was punching cotton.

The problem was that since the Gu family did not respond, the Chu family could not make a direct move against them either.

After all, some things are different when brought out into the open compared to behind the scenes.
