Chapter 1

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The people in Linxian County were hurrying about, most of them wearing thick cotton jackets. Occasionally, there would be someone dressed lightly, shivering like sifted flour in the wind and snow.

In a certain courtyard, there was a young man dressed lightly, practicing punches in the wind and snow. Each punch was fierce and powerful, with wisps of white vapor seeming to rise from the top of his head - it was quite mysterious.

After a long while.

After finishing a set of punches, the young man's spirit stirred, and his gaze seemed to have an extra line of text that others couldn't see.

...

Name: Gu Qingfeng, Age: 22/80, Realm: Beginner Leather Refining

Martial Arts: Collapse Mountain Fist (Beginner) --

"To make the Collapse Mountain Fist reach small accomplishment, it seems it won't be done in a short time!"

The young man shook his head slightly.

His name was Gu Qingfeng. Before the age of sixteen, he was no different from ordinary people. After turning sixteen, he suddenly awakened the wisdom of his past life, gaining a memory of an additional lifetime.

After understanding that this was a world in chaos, with the collapse of rituals and music, Gu Qingfeng realized that strength was the foundation for his survival in this world.

So, he did not hesitate to exhaust his family's meager resources to successfully enter a martial arts school and become a disciple, learning a martial art called the Collapse Mountain Fist.

Although this martial art was only a beginner-level skill, it was temporarily sufficient for Gu Qingfeng.

Unfortunately, Gu Qingfeng's natural talent was average. After spending several years in the martial arts school, he still could not master the Collapse Mountain Fist. Later, due to being unable to pay the continued tuition fees, he had to leave the school. After wandering around, he eventually settled down in Linxian County.

It was not until a year ago that Gu Qingfeng finally managed to master the Collapse Mountain Fist.

And with the mastery of the Collapse Mountain Fist, he discovered that the "golden finger" he had obtained from his transmigration also appeared.

A simple attribute panel.

There was nothing else beyond that.

Gu Qingfeng had researched it many times, but could not find any additional functions for the attribute panel, as if it was just a simple display panel.

Over time, Gu Qingfeng gave up on this idea.

Just then, the attribute panel, which had remained unchanged for a long time, suddenly had an additional line of small text.

"Your first offspring has been born (can only be triggered once)!"

"Reward: 1 Upgrade Point!"

"Reward: 10 Qi Blood Pills!"

The sudden change startled Gu Qingfeng, as if he had witnessed something incredible.

The attribute panel now had two interfaces.

One interface was the just-displayed notification information, and the other was his original attribute panel.

The difference was that the attribute panel now had some changes compared to before.

...

Name: Gu Qingfeng, Age: 22/80, Realm: Beginner Leather Refining

Martial Arts: Collapse Mountain Fist (Small Accomplishment), Upgrade Points: 1 --

When Gu Qingfeng's gaze fell on the Upgrade Points, information naturally surfaced in his mind, allowing him to understand the function of the Upgrade Points.

Upgrade Points! As the name suggests, they are used to upgrade martial arts realms.

With this realization, Gu Qingfeng tried to use the Upgrade Point on the Collapse Mountain Fist.

Immediately, the Collapse Mountain Fist advanced from the Beginner realm to the Small Accomplishment realm.

Thereafter, a surge of powerful blood energy and strength erupted from Gu Qingfeng's body, and his skin became even more resilient, as if he had undergone a tremendous transformation.

At the same time, the memories related to the Small Accomplishment of the Collapse Mountain Fist suddenly emerged, like a series of vivid images.

An image appeared in Gu Qingfeng's mind, as if he had cultivated it countless times before.

When he looked at his own attribute panel again, Gu Qingfeng found that his realm had directly broken through from the initial stage of skin refinement to the intermediate stage.

"I've broken through!"

He was delighted.

It was at this moment that Gu Qingfeng truly discovered the real benefits of his attribute panel.

He had painstakingly cultivated the Collapse Mountain Fist for years, barely entering the introductory stage, but now with just a few points of improvement, he had broken through from the introductory stage to the minor accomplishment stage, and his realm had also risen to the intermediate stage of skin refinement.

In a small place like Linxian County, being at the intermediate stage of skin refinement was already quite formidable.

Afterwards, Gu Qingfeng also discovered that there was an item bag below the attribute panel, and the rewarded Blood Qi Pill was prominently listed there.

With a thought, a blue bottle appeared before his eyes.

Gu Qingfeng quickly grabbed the bottle, pulled out the stopper, and a rich fragrance wafted out, revealing the corresponding medicinal pill inside.

The Blood Qi Pill! Consuming it can enhance the warrior's blood qi and assist in their cultivation.

Gu Qingfeng had heard of such pills before when he was at the martial arts hall, but he had never truly experienced them.

After putting away the pill, Gu Qingfeng continued to study his golden finger.

From the prompts in the information panel and the changes in himself, he could confirm one thing - his golden finger seemed to be related to his descendant bloodline.

"The most urgent task is to find my illegitimate son!"

Gu Qingfeng pondered.

Finding his son was not difficult, as he had only broken through once in this world, and the other party was not from Linxian County, but this was not a problem.

After a simple cleanup, Gu Qingfeng shouldered his backpack and left the courtyard he had rented for a long time.

...

Ten days later, Gu Qingfeng returned to Linxian County, but this time he was carrying a baby in his arms.

During these ten days, Gu Qingfeng had traveled through countless mountains and rivers, and when he finally found his offspring, he discovered that the child's mother had already died in childbirth. Only an elderly couple remained, who were planning to abandon the infant, and that was when he found them.

Regarding the woman's passing, Gu Qingfeng felt a bit of sadness. Although they had little emotional connection and their relationship was due to some accident, he still couldn't remain calm upon hearing the news of her death.

After giving most of his remaining silver to the elderly couple, Gu Qingfeng left with the child.

This was a baby boy, and Gu Qingfeng named him Gu Yang.

After bringing him back to Linxian County, Gu Qingfeng began to worry about how to raise the infant. After all, he had never had any experience in raising children, and this was the first time he had his own offspring in both of his lifetimes.

There was another problem - his silver had already dwindled to almost nothing.

If he couldn't solve the silver issue, not only would he be unable to raise the child, he might even starve to death himself.

Thinking about this, Gu Qingfeng took a deep breath, as if he had made some decision, and holding the child, he went out again.

But this time, he went to a martial arts hall.

"I am Gu Qingfeng, and I would like to inquire if the hall is recruiting instructors?"

"Instructors?"

The martial arts hall staff member eyed the young man with a strange expression, especially when he saw the child in Gu Qingfeng's arms.

"There's no problem with becoming an instructor at the martial arts hall, but there's one requirement - your cultivation must be at least at the intermediate stage of skin refinement!"

Hearing this, a confident look appeared on Gu Qingfeng's face.

(End of this chapter)
