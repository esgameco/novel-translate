The Great Grandmasters' 800 years was already the limit for martial artists in the world.

Longevity!

For the vast majority of people, this was an irresistible temptation.

The lifespan of ordinary people was only about 180 years, and many were willing to exhaust their family fortunes just to extend their lives by a few years, let alone the difference of hundreds or thousands of years.

"One of the main reasons for the decline of the Martial Dao was that the lifespan of martial artists was not as long as that of Sorcerers!"

2,000 years -

Hearing this lifespan, Gu Qingfeng's heart was not particularly stirred.

After all, after achieving Perfect Body Refinement, his lifespan had already broken through 2,000 years, and later when he stepped into the ranks of the Extreme Dao Grandmasters, his lifespan even soared to 3,000 years.

Such a level of lifespan

was enough to be on par with the powerful figures at the Transformation Realm.

However,

strictly speaking, the Extreme Dao Grandmasters were only benchmarked against the Grandmasters, while the Transformation Realm was benchmarked against the Great Grandmasters.

If the Perfect Martial Dao was widely disseminated, it would be able to break the advantage of the Sorcerers' lifespan.

Combined with the elevation of the level of life, the position of the Sorcerers would undoubtedly be severely impacted, and even their eventual extinction would not be impossible.

Of course,

Gu Qingfeng would not rashly spread the Martial Dao he had created.

It was simply a matter of timing.

Moreover, the Longevity Sutra was not yet perfected, and if it was disseminated now, it would not have a great effect.

When the Longevity Sutra is further improved in the future, then disseminating it would have a different effect.

All these thoughts

flashed through Gu Qingfeng's mind in an instant.

Seeing his silence, Gu Yun thought Gu Qingfeng was doubting the truthfulness of his words, so he explained again.

"The words of this old man are not false. Nowadays, the world is corrupt, the order is in disarray, and the evil Sorcerers are causing havoc, making the common people unable to live in peace. This goes against the founding principles of our Divine Martial Sect.

In the past, the Martial Ancestor transmitted the Dao to the world, hoping to use the Martial Dao to resolve the calamities of the world. Alas, after the Martial Ancestor's ascension, no one was able to comprehend the legacy he left behind. As the current Sect Master of the Divine Martial Sect, I feel ashamed before my predecessors' dedication!"
