"A lowly servant?" Wen Jiao sneered contemptuously.

Immediately after, Wen Jiao opened his mouth and absorbed all of Stone Kai's vital energy and brain matter into his own body.

"The brain matter of the Blood Refining Realm is still a bit lacking in taste!"

"According to his words, the Patriarch of the Gu Family Manor should be a Marrow Cleansing Realm expert!"

"However, a Marrow Cleansing Realm cultivator is unlikely to make the Exorcism Pavilion and the Zhuo Family yield."

Wen Jiao's eyes turned ice-cold, and a sinister, savage smile appeared on his face.

Only Qi Polluting Realm cultivators would be able to truly command respect from powers like the Exorcism Pavilion and the Zhuo Family.

In the past, even before cultivating the Qi Polluting technique, Wen Jiao had already reached the Qi Polluting Realm. Now, having cultivated the Qi Polluting technique, he had long since surpassed the Spirit Controlling Realm.

Over the years, Wen Jiao had not only slain one or two Qi Polluting Realm cultivators.

This time, he had started with the Forging Sword Gate, intending to gain a deeper understanding of the Gu Family Manor's strength, to ensure that no problems would arise.

After all, caution is the mother of safety.

Wen Jiao's ability to survive until now was also due to his prudence and carefulness.

But now, he had already grasped the strength of the Gu Family Manor - a suspected Qi Polluting Realm cultivator, and a suspected Bone Refining Realm genius.

The remaining Blood Refining Realm cultivators were nothing more than ants in Wen Jiao's eyes.

"You killed the seeds I painstakingly cultivated, so now I shall have all of the Gu Family Manor accompany them in death. The vital energy and brain matter of a Qi Polluting Realm cultivator should be worth more than thousands of ants!"

Wen Jiao let out a cold, mocking laugh, and then his gaze fell on the outside of the hall, where he sensed other cultivators approaching the Forging Sword Gate.

In the next moment, his figure disappeared from the spot.
