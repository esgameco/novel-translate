Gu Qingfeng looked at the figure in the void, and the majestic holy aura permeated, making his entire body's qi and blood boil.

There was no fear in his eyes, only a strong fighting intent.

Gu Qingfeng also wanted to witness the strength of the Saint from the Primal Chaos Realm, to see how powerful such an existence could be.

At this moment.

Shangguan Hongtiān bowed respectfully to the Empyrean Saint, saying with a reverent expression: "A powerful expert has come to attack us, please, Saint, take action and slay this villain!"

"Allowed!"

The Empyrean Saint uttered a single word, devoid of any emotion.

Immediately.

The opponent grabbed the Empyrean Mirror and directly unleashed a world-destroying divine light, which in an instant pierced through the layers of void and pressed down towards Gu Qingfeng.

"Today, let this seat witness the true strength of the so-called Saint!"

Gu Qingfeng laughed loudly, the icy brilliance of the Slaying Evil Knife fully displayed, his terrifying qi and blood power surging towards the heavens, like a dazzling great sun, and in the moment the divine light descended, he swung out a fierce slash.

"Boom!"

The terrifying blade qi shattered the void, directly cleaving that world-destroying divine light.

The remaining momentum of the blade light surged towards the Empyrean Saint, who punched out, the two forces canceling each other out.

However.

In the moment the blade light dissipated, Gu Qingfeng took a step forward, his overwhelming blade intent bursting through the clouds, colossal power converging on his body, transforming into the Primal Chaos Heaven Sword and once again striking towards the Empyrean Saint.

At this moment.

A trace of emotion finally appeared on the Empyrean Saint's expressionless face.

Immediately.

He summoned the Empyrean Mirror, and towering raging flames swept across the heavens and earth, as if the great sun and golden crow had been born, and in the moment they spread their wings and took flight, endless true flames unfurled.

"This is the Golden Crow of the Great Sun!"

"It is said that the Empyrean Mirror was forged by the Empyrean Saint based on the legendary Golden Crow Divine Mirror, and now it seems the rumors are true!"

"Such divine might, it should be able to subdue even ordinary Saints!"

"Gu Qingfeng is also no ordinary person, even with the power of the Empyrean Saint manifested, he has not been directly suppressed, truly formidable—"

Watching the two figures clashing in the void, all the powerful experts were shocked.

The strength of the Empyrean Saint was not unexpected to them.

After all, although he had already perished, the power he left behind in the Saint Treasure was comparable to a true Saint.

But.

The power Gu Qingfeng displayed was truly astonishing to the various experts.

They had originally thought Gu Qingfeng was only a Half-Saint, but now it seems his strength was far beyond that.

Otherwise.

How could he contend with a Saint wielding the Empyrean Mirror?

One must know that.

A Saint possessing a Saint Treasure is the pinnacle of Saints.

As long as the power the Empyrean Saint left in the Empyrean Mirror was not exhausted, he would always be equivalent to a true Saint.

In this era where Saints do not emerge, such an existence is capable of sweeping away everything.

So.

Gu Qingfeng's ability to fight the Empyrean Saint was truly unbelievable to them.

...

Boom! Rumble!!

In the void, the Empyrean Saint wielded the Empyrean Mirror, unleashing dazzling solar divine lights, seemingly setting the entire space ablaze, the terrifying flames within capable of reducing any cultivator below the Saint Realm to ashes.

However.

Facing such attacks, Gu Qingfeng was not at all at a disadvantage, his towering qi and blood like a vast ocean, his unparalleled blade intent annihilating everything.

No matter how terrifying the Empyrean Saint's attacks were, they could not harm him in the slightest.

After a moment.

Gu Qingfeng's expression became somewhat disappointed.

"So this is the power of a Saint, it seems I have overestimated the so-called Saints!"

As the words fell, Gu Qingfeng's figure suddenly stopped, and at this moment, the Empyrean Saint also raised the Empyrean Mirror, a terrifying Golden Crow of raging flames converging and charging towards him.

The scorching waves were already surging towards him before the Golden Crow arrived.

Gu Qingfeng's expression remained indifferent as he looked at the approaching Golden Crow.

"It's time to end this!"

Before the words had fully left his mouth, he took a step forward, channeling all his qi and blood into the Slaying Evil Knife, and the boundless Heavenly Sword Dao Phantom coalesced in the sky, finally merging with Gu Qingfeng.

A single sword strike descended.

All was annihilated!

The Golden Crow was the first to be struck, its body instantly torn apart by the blade qi and dissipating into the sky as divine flames.

But after slaying the Golden Crow, the sword strike did not stop, continuing towards the Empyrean Saint.

The latter's expression changed slightly, having no choice but to summon the Empyrean Mirror to block.

In the next breath.

The long sword had ruthlessly struck the Empyrean Mirror.

"Crack!"

A crisp sound rang out.

Countless cracks appeared on the Saint Treasure, and in just a few breaths, it shattered completely.

The Saint Treasure was destroyed.

The Empyrean Saint's phantom dissipated.

The unstopped sword strike pierced deep into the void, as if cleaving the entire Ferocious Beast Mountain Range, causing the earth to crumble, the mountains to collapse, and countless fierce beasts to be buried.

"No... it's impossible, how could the Empyrean Saint be defeated!"

Seeing the Empyrean Mirror shattered and the Empyrean Saint's phantom vanish, Shangguan Hongtiān's face turned deathly pale, his eyes filled with disbelief.

What had he witnessed? The Empyrean Saint had been defeated!

And the Empyrean Mirror had been destroyed as well!

One must know that.

The reason the Shangguan Clan was able to stand as a Saint Clan and remain unshaken for so long was precisely because they had a Saint Treasure guarding them.

If there was no Saint Treasure, the Shangguan Clan might have been destroyed long ago.

After all.

When the Empyrean Saint was still alive, the Shangguan Clan had offended many forces, and it was only after the Saint's passing, leaving the Saint Treasure to guard the clan, that they were able to intimidate the various powers.

If they lost the Saint Treasure, the Shangguan Clan would no longer be worthy of being called a Saint Clan.

At the same time.

Many of the Shangguan Clan's past enemies, most likely, would seize the opportunity to strike.

Such a situation was definitely not what Shangguan Hongtiān wanted to see.

If he had foreseen today's events, Shangguan Hongtiān would never have used the Saint Treasure.

However.

Before Shangguan Hongtiān could lament for long, a frigid killing intent descended upon him, making his entire body instinctively tremble, and when he followed the source of that killing intent, he met Gu Qingfeng's cold gaze.

"Wait, you can't kill me..."

Shangguan Hongtiān's heart trembled, the fear of death instantly overwhelming all other thoughts, but before he could finish speaking, Gu Qingfeng had already brought down the sword.

In an instant.

This Shangguan Clan Elder who had reached the Dao Palace Realm's ninth layer was directly drenched in blood.

After slaying the Shangguan Clan, Gu Qingfeng's movements did not stop, a single sword strike transforming into countless blade qi, covering all the Shangguan Clan cultivators.

At this moment, a divine light suddenly surged from Shangguan Yushen, and a phantom emerged from him.

"Fellow Daoist, this is my Shangguan Clan..."
