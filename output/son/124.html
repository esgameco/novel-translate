"It's not just the Liuyun Stronghold, but there are shadows of the Blood Spirit Sect behind some other green forest forces as well," the dark guard said solemnly.

Hearing this, Gu Yang's expression turned slightly cold. "It seems that we have eliminated too many strongholds in this period, which has drawn the attention of the Blood Spirit Sect, so they have sent people to investigate."

Gu Yang had heard of the Blood Spirit Sect before.

The Taishan Prefecture has twelve counties, with Qingjiang County being the most prominent. The Blood Spirit Sect is an ancient sect within Qingjiang County.

What surprised Gu Yang was that the Blood Spirit Sect had extended its reach to Guangyang Prefecture at some point.

Normally, the jianghu (martial world) of a county would not allow external forces to interfere. Now that the Blood Spirit Sect has secretly infiltrated, if the news were to be leaked, it would invite hostility from other local powers in Guangyang Prefecture.

Of course, that was in the past. Nowadays, with the appearance of an evil Buddha on the Nine Turns Path, the strength of the sects and aristocratic families in Guangyang Prefecture has been greatly diminished, which has likely provided an opportunity for the Blood Spirit Sect to take advantage.

Gu Yang's understanding of the Blood Spirit Sect was limited.

He then looked at Gu Yi and said, "Have someone investigate the details of the Blood Spirit Sect, and have all the dark guards return, and for now, do not take any action!"

Since this round of eliminating the strongholds has drawn the attention of the Blood Spirit Sect, Gu Yang needs to be extra cautious. A thousand-year-old sect is not weak, and Gu Yang has deliberately concealed his identity without leaving any survivors. If his identity is exposed due to the Blood Spirit Sect, it would be a significant problem.

After all, the Gu family's foundation is currently too weak. In the past half year, although Gu Yang has been secretly recruiting troops and expanding his power, with White Stone City as the base, and gradually bringing the cities along the White Stone Path under his control, this force is still not enough.

Immediately, Gu Yang left with his people, taking away the abundant resources from the Liuyun Stronghold. In the dungeon of the Liuyun Stronghold, Gu Yang also found many civilians who had been imprisoned for a long time, most of them young and strong, but now on the verge of death from starvation.

Gu Yang did not personally intervene, but had the dark guards secretly open the dungeon and then led the people away.

Two days later, a middle-aged man arrived gracefully. Seeing the Liuyun Stronghold completely devoid of any living person, his expression remained calm. However, when he saw a tortured and disfigured corpse, his face darkened.

"Daring to kill the elders of my Blood Spirit Sect, I must find out who was behind this!" Yan Shaoyang's eyes turned icy cold.

The corpse was that of an elder sent by the Blood Spirit Sect to the Liuyun Stronghold.

Years ago, the appearance of an evil Buddha on the Nine Turns Path in Guangyang Prefecture had caused heavy losses to the various aristocratic sects, which gave the Blood Spirit Sect the opportunity to extend its influence into Guangyang Prefecture.

After that, the Blood Spirit Sect had secretly dispatched powerful cultivators to Guangyang Prefecture, covertly controlling various forces, in the hopes of gathering enough resources and population to support the sect's cultivation needs.

However, half a month ago, many of the green forest forces under the Blood Spirit Sect's control were suddenly eliminated. When the news reached the sect, they were enraged and immediately dispatched Yan Shaoyang and another elder to investigate the situation.

Yan Shaoyang was responsible for visiting the other forces, while the other elder was sent to a different area.
