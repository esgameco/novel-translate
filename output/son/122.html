Hearing this, a gleam instantly appeared in Gu Yang's eyes.

This was an extremely terrifying number.

So, being able to put out 2-3 million taels would already be their limit.

And if they used so much money, it might even affect the entire operation of the Gu Family Manor.

But now it's different.

9 million taels of silver, there is more than enough room for maneuvering.

"Immediately convert all the antique paintings and calligraphies into cash, then use 4 million taels of silver to purchase grains from various places. This matter must be handled with utmost caution and discretion, so as not to attract the attention of other powers!"

Gu Yang said at the end, adding another reminder.

If it were in a prosperous era, spending several million taels of silver in one place to purchase grains would cause quite a stir. Let alone now, in this chaotic time when grains are in short supply.

Not to mention spending 4 million taels in one place, even tens of thousands of taels would be enough to attract the attention of many powers.
