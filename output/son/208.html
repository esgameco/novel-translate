The Central Plains.

The void trembled.

A towering figure suddenly appeared in the sky, overlooking the city below. The people in the city immediately noticed the abnormality and looked up in shock.

"He's flying in the air!?"

"This is the capital of the Central Plains, how dare someone act so boldly!"

"This is the Sword King's Mansion, come down at once—"

Powerful martial artists with imposing auras looked up at the figure in the sky, their expressions a mix of shock and anger.

Flying in the air.

While this was an extraordinary feat, it did not shock them too much.

After all, masters at the Transcendent realm and above could achieve brief flight if they wished.

Moreover, this was the capital of the Central Plains.

Ever since the fall of the Taixuan Dynasty, it had fallen under the control of the Sword King, and the former imperial palace had become the Sword King's Mansion.

Currently, the capital not only had tens of thousands of elite soldiers, but also numerous powerful experts gathered here.

Even a grand martial dao master would find it difficult to escape.

"The Sword King's Mansion—"

Fu Zhao's expression was indifferent as he looked down at the city below, a flash of bloodlust in his eyes.

"With so many martial artists, their vital energies are truly abundant. If I can refine them all, I can completely stabilize the realm I just broke through, and perhaps even advance further!"

Seeing the many martial artists gathering below, Fu Zhao suddenly slammed down a palm, terrifying spiritual power coalescing into a massive palm strike that descended like a collapsing mountain, causing many martial artists to change expression in alarm.

"Danger!"

"Be careful!"

Martial artists roared and unleashed their primal true qi and terrifying vital energies, trying to block Fu Zhao's attack.

But alas, their struggles were futile against a Transcendent realm expert.

The terrifying power pressed down, causing the so-called master-level bodies to suddenly explode, dissipating into a mist of blood.

Immediately after, countless streams of vital energy surged towards the Fu Zhao in the sky.

A palm strike descended, shaking the city.

A horrifying palm print was deeply carved into the earth, countless martial artists and common civilians reduced to a bloody pulp by this single strike.

At this moment, an elder of the Divine Sword Sect appeared, looking up at Fu Zhao in the sky and coldly saying, "The Nascent Soul realm experts of the major sects are not allowed to personally take action. Are you trying to fight to the death with our Divine Sword Sect?"

He did not recognize Fu Zhao's identity, but from the latter's casual annihilation of many masters, he could tell the other was at least a Nascent Soul realm expert.

However, even facing a Nascent Soul expert, this Divine Sword Sect elder was unafraid.

After all, the Divine Sword Sect was a supreme immortal sect, its foundations far beyond what a mere Nascent Soul cultivator could contend with.

Yet, as soon as he finished speaking, Fu Zhao's expression turned disdainful, and with a casual flick of his finger, the Divine Sword Sect elder had no time to react before his physical body exploded, the evil energy within him also awakening.

"Mere Harmonious Soul realm insects daring to act recklessly before me!"

Afterwards, with a thought, Fu Zhao unleashed a terrifying power that enveloped the entire city, and behind him in the void, a bizarre and ominous figure stood tall in the sky, like a demonic god from the underworld descending.

In the next instant, the strange figure extended its right hand and grabbed the awakened evil energy, then stuffed it into its own mouth to chew.

This sight left everyone else ice-cold.

"Forbidden... Forbidden Area!!"

"This is the presence of a supreme being that has condensed the Forbidden Area!"

"Impossible, even at the peak of the Nascent Soul realm, one cannot condense a true Forbidden Area. Could you be a Transcendent realm expert—"

The moment they sensed the Forbidden Area, everyone could smell the scent of death.

The Forbidden Area! This was something only calamitous evil entities could condense.

But if a cultivator could condense the Forbidden Area, they would have to be at least at the Transcendent realm.

Now that Fu Zhao was displaying Forbidden Area techniques, it made these people feel a chill down their spines.

If he was truly a Transcendent realm expert, they were likely facing certain death.

At this moment, the Sword King had no choice but to step forward.

He looked up at the figure in the sky and humbly said, "I do not know how I have offended you, sir. If you tell me, I will gladly apologize!"

Although the Sword King was a regional lord commanding millions of troops, a single order from him could decide the life and death of countless civilians, but in the face of such absolute power, he could only bow his head.

"It's not about offending me, the fault lies in you contending for the imperial throne with my Taixuan Sect!"

Fu Zhao shook his head slightly, his words causing the Sword King's expression to turn shocked.

Clearly, the Sword King had not expected the other to be from the Taixuan Sect.

But before the Sword King could speak again, Fu Zhao pointed a finger, and the Sword King's body exploded, killed on the spot.

Eliminating the Sword King.

For Fu Zhao, it was as simple as crushing an ant.

In this world where the strong prevailed, so-called authority was just a trivial matter. In the face of absolute power, everything else was insignificant.

"Today, all of you shall die!"

Murderous intent flashed in Fu Zhao's eyes, and then a terrifying aura burst forth from him, causing many in the city to be directly shattered by the pressure, their bodies turning into a mist of blood.

The remaining masters and Harmonious Soul realm experts could barely withstand this pressure.

"Quickly, flee!"

An elder-level master, his expression horrified, wanted to escape the Forbidden Area.

As for confronting Fu Zhao, he did not have the courage. After all, at this level, eliminating masters was as simple as crushing ants, and any resistance would only lead to their own destruction.

Hearing this, the others also suddenly came to their senses and rushed towards the city gates.

In the void, Fu Zhao looked at the fleeing crowd with a disdainful expression.

Then he slammed down a palm, instantly killing many more martial artists and cultivators. He then extended his right hand, condensing terrifying spiritual power into a giant palm to grab a Harmonious Soul realm cultivator.

In the latter's terrified gaze, Fu Zhao clenched his fist, causing the cultivator to explode into a mist of blood.

"Die!"

The bizarre shadow behind him trembled, and Fu Zhao unleashed his true power with a palm strike, causing the centuries-old capital to crumble, annihilating all the martial artists and civilians within.

Millions of lives were extinguished, and the vast amount of vital energy surged into Fu Zhao, significantly strengthening his aura.

After a long while, a smile appeared on Fu Zhao's face.

"The vital energies of the masters are indeed remarkable. This has saved me a century of arduous cultivation!"

Previously, he had just entered the Transcendent realm, and his cultivation was not truly stable. But now, after massacring an entire city and refining the vital energies of its inhabitants, his unstable realm had been firmly consolidated.

Saving a century of arduous cultivation.

This was no exaggeration.

Then, Fu Zhao looked at the ruins of the capital, withdrew the Forbidden Area, and departed, flying away.

Half an hour later, in the ruins of the capital, some bloodstained figures emerged, looking at the direction Fu Zhao had left with terror-stricken expressions.

"Transcendent realm... this is the power of the Transcendent realm!"

"I must hurry back and report this to the sect leader!"

The master-level expert was completely terrified, not daring to linger in the ruins, and immediately fled in the opposite direction from where Fu Zhao had gone.

"Reporting to the esteemed lord!"

"An undercover agent has brought urgent news from the Central Plains, please take a look!"

In the Gu Family Manor, an undercover agent hurriedly arrived, hastily handing a secret letter to Gu Qingfeng.

Gu Qingfeng waved the agent away, then opened the letter.

Upon seeing the contents, his pupils slightly constricted.

"Annihilation of the capital!"

"All regional lords dead!"

"The Taixuan Sect has really turned the table over?"

The letter's contents stated that the Taixuan Sect's Grand Elder, Fu Zhao, had gone to the Central Plains and unleashed a massacre, completely annihilating the capital and slaughtering all the experts of the Divine Sword Sect, including the Sword King.

This was not all.

The capital's civilians and elite troops were all massacred by Fu Zhao.

One man annihilating an entire city! Fu Zhao's ruthless methods were evident.

However, this was not the full extent of Fu Zhao's actions.

After killing the Sword King, he directly attacked other regional lords, with the vast powers of the Central Plains nearly all being eliminated by him.

Now, the Central Plains had been reduced to a sea of blood and heads by Fu Zhao.

According to the undercover agent's report, Fu Zhao had slaughtered no less than tens of millions of people, truly incurring the wrath of heaven and earth.

After a long while, Gu Qingfeng took a deep breath.

"The law of the jungle!"

"When one possesses power that others cannot resist, the so-called rules and order become insignificant!"

Clearly, this was the case with Fu Zhao.

As the sole Transcendent realm expert in the world, he no longer cared about such things as order.

In the past, immortal sects that massacred the masses would at least find a reason, or be more discreet, and at least maintain a reputation.

But Fu Zhao's actions now showed complete disregard for these things.

Indiscriminate slaughter.

Even if it incurred the wrath of heaven and earth, he was utterly fearless.

The reason for this was simple - Fu Zhao was the only Transcendent realm expert in the world.

This alone was enough.

At the same time, Gu Qingfeng also realized something else.

Fu Zhao's absolute power crushing the regional lords was clearly to allow the Taixuan Sect to dominate the world. After this massacre, the previous chaos in the Central Plains would be completely settled.

After all, with millions of heads rolling and the Transcendent realm pressure looming, the other immortal sects and great families would not dare to utter a single word of dissent.

Unless...

These immortal sects and great families also had Transcendent realm experts.

But—

After learning of Fu Zhao's actions, Gu Qingfeng was certain that the remaining immortal sects and great families did not have any Transcendent realm experts.

If such experts existed, the entire world would have already been turned upside down by the Transcendent realm, not waiting for Fu Zhao to make a move.

"The situation in the Central Plains is settled, the next target of the Taixuan Sect must be the Qingyun State!"

"Then let me see, whether this so-called Transcendent realm can withstand my sword!"
