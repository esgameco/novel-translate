Gu Qingfeng took a quick look and finally found a master-level martial art that refines the physical body.

The Pure Yang True Body!

When mastered, it can make the flesh and blood purely yang and unyielding.

Although the Azure Yang Sect's Azure Yang Sutra can also cultivate pure yang qi and blood, when compared to this, the difference is a hundredfold or even more.

"Pure yang flesh and blood is the most effective against evil spirits, which shows that the ancestors of the Heavenly Yang Sword Sect who created this martial art also had the intention of suppressing evil spirits!"

"It's just a pity that the thoughts of later generations have ultimately changed!"

Gu Qingfeng shook his head slightly.

The slayer becomes the evil dragon.

From the existence of the Pure Yang True Body, it can be seen that in the beginning, the Heavenly Yang Sword Sect indeed had the idea of suppressing evil spirits, otherwise they would not have created such a supreme skill.

According to the introduction of the Pure Yang True Body, only a childlike body is most suitable for cultivating this supreme skill. If one who is not a childlike body cultivates it, it will be twice the effort for half the result, and it will be difficult to truly master it.

Speaking of which, Gu Qingfeng is no longer a childlike body.

However, with the attribute panel in hand, Gu Qingfeng is not afraid.

After Gu Qingfeng thoroughly studied all the cultivation methods and detailed introductions of the Pure Yang True Body, he finally gained an absolute in-depth understanding of this supreme skill.

"It seems that this Pure Yang True Body is not as simple as just requiring a childlike body, but the cultivation requirements are also extremely high. Only with the foundation of the Refining Viscera Five Realms complete can one cultivate this Pure Yang True Body."

Gu Qingfeng murmured to himself.

The Pure Yang True Body is different from other martial arts, this is a true master-level martial art.

Like other cultivated martial arts, they generally start from refining the skin, and then step by step cultivate to the upper limit of the martial art.

But the Pure Yang True Body is not like this.

This martial art starts from the master level and ends at the peak of the master level.

A martial artist who has reached the Refining Viscera Five Realms complete can enter the Pure Yang True Body at the initial stage of the Blood Master, and when the cultivation is complete, they can reach the peak of the Blood Master.

As a supreme martial art of the master level, this kind of martial art involving the Blood Master is undoubtedly much more precious.

Now that Gu Yang has brought many supreme skills, but the only one involving the cultivation of the Blood Master is this Pure Yang True Body.

Other master-level supreme skills are more like the Prajna Sutra, where the master focuses on cultivating the physical body before, and then cultivates the true qi after becoming a master.

These martial arts are not as consistent in refining the physical body as the Pure Yang True Body.

After a moment, Gu Qingfeng had memorized all the cultivation methods of the Pure Yang True Body, and then glanced at his own attribute panel, only to see the words "Pure Yang True Body (Unmastered)" added.

"Upgrade!"

Gu Qingfeng did not hesitate and directly used fifty upgrade points to upgrade the Pure Yang True Body to the entry stage.

With the breakthrough of the Pure Yang True Body, Gu Qingfeng felt a mighty blood force reviving from within his physical body, and his entire muscles and tendons became more refined, as if they had been tempered a thousand times.

The surging blood flow was like a raging river, rampaging and crashing through his physical body.

However, with Gu Qingfeng's current cultivation level, this torrential blood flow was not enough to shake his physical body at all.
