Now, it is a scene of mourning.

Numerous corpses were lying there, each with a rigid, terrified expression, as if they had seen a terrifying existence before their death.

In the main hall of the Zhenyang Sect, the wails of a woman and the frenzied, brutal laughter could be heard.

The Patriarch of the Zhenyang Sect, an old man, now had his four limbs twisted and broken, lying on the ground like a pile of rotten mud, his eyes bulging as he witnessed the unfolding scene.

A young woman in her prime had her limbs twisted and was being ruthlessly violated by a young man.

After a while, the young man's face revealed a satisfied smile, and he then casually snapped the woman's neck, throwing her lifeless body in front of the Patriarch.

"Good, I didn't expect this small place of Baishi Dao to have such a rare specimen, not bad, truly not bad!"

Ning Fu licked his lips, looking at the woman's corpse, still not fully satisfied.

The Patriarch's eyes were bloodshot as he roared angrily, "This is Baishi Dao, and you dare to wantonly destroy my Zhenyang Sect. The Gu Family Manor will not let you off!"

"Hmph, what Gu Family Manor? They are just ordinary martial artists, what can they do?" Ning Fu sneered, his gaze towards the Patriarch filled with cruelty.

"I've kept you alive until now because you are the only Qi Refinement realm martial artist in the Zhenyang Sect. I'll savor you last, for the blood and flesh of a Qi Refinement realm martial artist is not something ordinary people can compare to.

Consuming you, I might be able to further advance my cultivation!"

Although he was not a cultivator, he had practiced the divine arts of the Chiyun Sect, nourishing his body with the qi and blood of martial artists, cultivating himself to a semi-spiritual state.

In this way, when he truly refines the demonic arts, it will be much easier.

As he spoke, Ning Fu slowly approached the Patriarch, then bent down and, under the Patriarch's furious gaze, directly pierced his chest and extracted the heart from within.

"I come from the Chiyun Sect, and even if the Gu Family Manor's people are here, do they have the courage to touch me?"

"What audacious villain dares to speak such words? Perish!"

Just as Ning Fu finished speaking, a furious roar came from outside the hall, and a terrifying blade intent erupted, the sharp sword qi piercing into the hall, causing Ning Fu's expression to change drastically.

"Boom!"

Ning Fu had just made a move when he was blasted away by a powerful force.

"Pu-chi!"

An arm flew off, and the intense pain twisted Ning Fu's face.

At this moment, he saw a young man holding a sword calmly walking into the hall.

"Gu... Gu Young Master, please... please avenge... my Zhenyang Sect..."

The Patriarch's pupils dilated, his lips moving weakly, but his expression soon froze, and he breathed no more.

At this moment, Ning Fu, his left hand clutching his severed arm, looked at Gu Yang with fear in his eyes.

"You are Gu Yang... impossible, you are only at the initial Qi Refinement realm, how could you have such strength!"

Ning Fu couldn't believe it.

He knew of the existence of the Gu Family's young master, Gu Yang, and although the latter had a reputation for talent, he was only at the initial Qi Refinement realm.

Ning Fu himself, in his twenties, had already reached the peak of the Qi Refinement realm and qualified to break through to the Bone Refining realm.

But what truly shocked Ning Fu was the strength Gu Yang had just displayed.

That single strike, Ning Fu had no ability to resist at all.

Seeing Gu Yang's cold expression as he slowly approached, holding the sword, Ning Fu's face suddenly became filled with terror.
