Percentage-based damage vulnerability + percentage-based damage! This mechanism is quite special and a bit overpowered.

Although it requires the use of a fire-elemental spell to trigger, it's actually not that difficult for other mages to trigger.

Because even for ice mages, they only need to learn a basic fireball spell to be able to trigger the ice-breaking effect.

Dealing direct damage based on the target's maximum HP, if it's against monsters with extremely high HP but no ice resistance, the kill speed would be extremely fast.

It would even be much faster than just brute-forcing the damage with fire spells.

Then there's the 7th-tier ice spell.

The difference from fire and wind spells is also very large.

The 7th-tier fire and wind spells have very large areas of effect and powerful effects.

But this 7th-tier ice spell is actually a single-target spell.

[Soul Freeze]

{Type: Active Skill}

{Tier: 7th}

{School: Cryomancy}

{Cost: 28,000 Mana}

{Casting Time: 15 seconds}

{Cooldown: 30 minutes}

{Effect: Channeling horrific cold, trapping the target's soul in eternal ice, immobilizing them, preventing skill usage, and removing all beneficial effects. Duration is infinite.}

{Targets encased in the ice are immune to all damage except fire.}

{The ice will shatter when affected by 6th-tier or higher fire spells.}

{Note: Imprisoned in unbreakable ice for eternity.}

Another non-damaging skill.

But it extends the control duration from limited to infinite.

As long as it's not broken by fire, it's permanent control!

Lin Yi is very satisfied.

Because he now has mastery in three elemental schools.

Damage is not lacking at all.

What he was lacking was control skills.

Now the ice school has completely filled that gap.

After completing the ice school, Lin Yi looks at the other passive skill he gained from his second job advancement.

[Mana Shield]

{Type: Passive Skill}

{Tier: Initial}

{School: Universal}

{Effect: When you take damage, 10% of the damage is instead taken from your mana.}

A fairly decent survival skill.

For mages with far more mana than HP, this skill can be considered a 10% universal damage reduction.

Lin Yi starts allocating points.

After over 10 minutes.

This survival skill has been greatly enhanced.

[Indestructible Form]

{Type: Passive Skill}

{Tier: 7th}

{School: Universal}

{Effect 1: When you take damage, 40% of the damage is instead taken from your mana.}

{Effect 2: When you take elemental damage, based on your affinity for that element, you gain additional damage reduction, minimum 10%, maximum 100%.}

{Current Elemental Damage Reduction:}

{Fire: 80%}

{Frost: 80%}

{Storm: 80%}

{Effect 3: When you would take fatal damage that would reduce your HP to 0, that damage is fully absorbed by your mana instead.}

{After this effect triggers, your mana is reduced to 0 and your HP is set to 1.}

{This effect can only trigger once every 30 days, current remaining uses: 1}

So powerful!

Not only does it have high damage reduction, it even gives Lin Yi a once-per-30-days "get out of death free" card!

This skill directly maxes out Lin Yi's sense of security.

To be honest, Lin Yi did feel a bit of pressure when facing 200-level Aberrant Liches and similar bosses before.

After all, he wasn't truly invincible.

Even though his damage output far surpassed his current job level by orders of magnitude, his survivability has always been quite weak.

If it wasn't for the fact that Rui Wen Dai had unleashed that fatal Void Slash before he could finish chanting and use the Thousand Tempests Break, his Level 5 Gale Wings would have been completely unable to withstand that attack.

He might have ended up crashing and burning.

Now, if Lin Yi encounters a similar situation, or an even more intense one, he wouldn't be as panicked anymore.

Not only has his survival capability improved, but his control has also increased.

Even if he were to face an enemy he couldn't instantly defeat, escaping shouldn't be too difficult.

He has completed the upgrades for his two new skills.

Lin Yi still has over 500,000 skill points left.

More than enough to upgrade one of his Level 7 skills to Level 8.

It is indeed time to choose which one to upgrade.

However, Lin Yi is still considering which one to select.

While pondering this, Lin Yi has already entered the gates of Jiang City Second High School.

Walking into the teaching building, Lin Yi gradually realizes that something is amiss.

Where are the people?

How come there's not a single person around?

Normally, even if the entire Grade 12 has undergone their career awakenings, there would still occasionally be students returning to school to consult teachers about leveling up or skills.

The school administration would even organize activities for teachers to guide students in leveling up.

But it's impossible for both students and teachers to have completely disappeared, right?

Filled with questions, Lin Yi heads to the faculty building.

He knocks on the door of the principal's office.

"Come in," an aged voice responds from within.

Lin Yi pushes open the door.

The principal of Second High, named Zuo Qian, was also the one who presided over the entire Grade 12 career awakening ceremony.

Seeing Lin Yi, a look of surprise appears in the old principal's eyes.

"Student Lin Yi, you've finally emerged from the blow!"

"I'm so relieved!"

"You've always been my favorite student, and I truly didn't want you to fall into ruin!"

Zuo Qian scrutinizes Lin Yi up and down.

He can sense that the young man in front of him has grown significantly stronger.

Being a mage himself, Zuo Qian can feel a certain kind of spiritual connection between mages.

He can tell that Lin Yi definitely hasn't been wasting his time these past two weeks. This puts his mind at ease.

Lin Yi doesn't explain anything, but directly asks, "Principal Zuo, I heard my homeroom teacher was looking for me urgently, saying it's related to my future. What's going on?"

Zuo Qian takes off his reading glasses, stands up, and his expression turns serious.

"It is indeed related to your future."

"Let's discuss it as we walk."

With that, Zuo Qian leads Lin Yi out of the office.

Suddenly, a hauntingly melodious flute sound rings out, and the next moment, a shaggy-furred, somewhat battered-looking, unidentifiable hoofed beast appears.

This beast has deer-like antlers, but a more bovine-like face, and its four hooves resemble those of a horse.

A true mishmash of features.

Additionally, it has multicolored feathered wings protruding from its flanks.

As a Level 3 Awakened, Zuo Qian has gained the ability to learn mount skills and possess a mount space.

So this is clearly his mount.

"Get on, what are you waiting for!"

Lin Yi steps onto the stirrup at the back of the strange beast and climbs aboard.

The next moment, under Zuo Qian's control, the hybrid creature flaps its wings and takes to the sky, panting heavily as it does so.

Lin Yi: "..."

This "Four-in-One" beast is wheezing as if it's about to die any second.

Not to mention its flying speed, which is hardly any different from an electric scooter...

"Cough, cough, little Hua is getting on in years now. When it was younger, it used to fly so fast."

"Never mind the details, let's get to the main issue."

"To put it simply, this morning, an unknown abyss energy erupted in the leveling area on the east side of Jiang City, and a time-limited trial instance dungeon has appeared, one that can only be entered by mage class characters."

"So I had the teachers from each class notify all the students who have successfully awakened as mages, which of course includes you."

(End of chapter)
