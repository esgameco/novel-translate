Chapter 12

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Perhaps its body temperature was too high.

Lin Yi found that the emerald-green crystal stones near its body were actually starting to vaporize!

Lin Yi felt a bit sorry for it.

He quickly called for a stop.

The two burning flame-filled eyes of the Molten Flame Demon revealed an extremely humanized expression.

Just like a misbehaving child.

Yet it looked so burly and terrifying, the contrasting cuteness was gripping.

Lin Yi was also helpless.

He knew the Molten Flame Demon didn't do it on purpose.

However, in the subsequent instructions, he had the Flame Demon start excavating from the rock layers next to the cave wall crystals, and there were no more problems.

In no time, a large cluster of emerald-green crystals, along with some surrounding rocks, were extracted.

Lin Yi cleared out all the water and food from his spatial storage, then stored the crystals in his personal space.

Afterwards, Lin Yi sent the Molten Flame Demon back to the elemental plane, then crushed the return scroll.

After a dizzying whirl,

Lin Yi opened his eyes again to find himself back in the teleportation hall of Jiang City.

Walking out of the teleportation hall, Lin Yi headed straight home.

The spiritual items and the things dropped by the Tiger King in his backpack, Lin Yi planned to go to the trading house to check them out tomorrow.

Back home, Lin Yi had a simple dinner with his parents.

After the meal, he returned to his own room.

Lin Yi opened his panel.

The skill point balance that he had nearly exhausted this morning had now increased by forty to fifty thousand points again.

This constant feeling of having resources being given to him, it was a steady happiness!

Today he had obtained a new wind-attribute skill.

Should he put the skills of this system together to see the effect?

Reason told him that having more skill systems would definitely broaden his choices, and his combat power would definitely increase greatly.

Especially after witnessing the terrifying destructive power of the 7th-tier spell Heavenly Flame Dragon Breath.

Lin Yi had already fallen in love with that feeling of destroying everything with a flick of the hand.

If 7th-tier fire spells were already so powerful,

then what would 7th-tier wind spells be like? Lin Yi made up his mind, after all, upgrading to 7th-tier would only take a little over a hundred thousand skill points.

Just a day of grinding would be enough!

...

The next day.

Lin Yi woke up early as usual.

Overnight, his skill point balance had approached a hundred thousand.

Probably by noon, he would be able to give the new wind system skills a massive boost, directly advancing them rapidly.

Before that.

Lin Yi planned to first go stroll through the Life Profession Street, sell the large cluster of emerald-green crystal ore he had dug up yesterday, and see how much money he could get.

Then go, then go to the trading house to see if he could find a few mage skill books.

Finally, go to the Reincarnation Hall.

Accept the second job change quest.

After the second job change, he should unlock even more new skills.

With this plan in mind, Lin Yi went out the door and quickly arrived at the Life Profession Street of Jiang City.

Lin Yi found the best appraiser on this street.

He planned to first identify what exactly the emerald-green crystals he obtained in that cave were, so that he would have a rough idea of their value, so as not to be cheated by unscrupulous merchants when selling them.

"Young man, are you looking to have something appraised?"

An old man with white hair and glasses, looking scholarly and refined, smiled at Lin Yi.

This was the most respected appraiser in the entire Life Profession Street of Jiang City.

Lin Yi nodded, taking out a small piece of emerald-green crystal from his spatial storage.

The moment this crystal appeared, Lin Yi felt a cool breeze start blowing through the appraiser's hall.

The old man's face...

Here is the full English translation of the excerpt from the Chinese novel:

The smile froze for a moment, then turned serious.

"Young man, the thing you're holding in your hand is extraordinary!"

"May I take a look at it?"

Lin Yi nodded and handed the crystal to the old man.

The old man adjusted his glasses and began to examine it carefully, while the glow of his appraisal technique also lit up.

Soon, the old man looked at Lin Yi in amazement: "Incredible, this Wind Spirit Marrow is so pure, it's really quite rare!"

Lin Yi's heart stirred.

So this thing was called Wind Spirit Marrow.

"This type of mineral-rich spiritual object not only requires purity, but also the quantity you have on hand."

"Young man, what you showed me was probably just a small sample.

"You should have the entire crystal ore, and if the volume is large and the purity is at the level of this sample, then..."

Before the old man could finish his words,

Lin Yi took out a huge emerald-green crystal ore weighing twenty or thirty pounds.

The old man, who had just been appraising the value for Lin Yi, suddenly sucked in a cold breath, staring in disbelief!

He almost couldn't catch his breath, nearly passing out.

After a while, the old man recovered and said, "Young man, a Wind Spirit Marrow this large is no longer something that can be valued by the market price, it's already considered a collector's item."

"You should go to a specialized spiritual object recycling shop to inquire."

"Don't sell it to a trading house, they can only pay you by weight at the market price, you'll end up getting much less."

Lin Yi thanked the old man, paid the appraisal fee, and put the Wind Spirit Marrow back in his backpack, continuing to wander around the Profession Street.

...

More than half an hour later,

Lin Yi walked out of a store called the Treasure Pavilion.

This was a shop that specialized in recycling rare materials and spiritual objects, similar to the antique shops he was familiar with in his previous life.

At this moment, he had a Treasure Pavilion VIP black card in his pocket.

And there was also 120,000 Daxia coins in the card.

After selling that collector's grade Wind Spirit Marrow, the enthusiastic store owner even gave Lin Yi an extra 10,000 Daxia coins.

According to the owner, this 10,000 was just to make a new friend.

He insisted that if Lin Yi had any such good items in the future, he must come to him.

He would definitely buy them at a price far above the market rate.

Until he walked out of the store,

Lin Yi himself was a bit dazed.

How did he suddenly become so wealthy?

Daxia, as one of the strongest countries on the Blue Star,

has a currency value and circulation rate that is terrifying.

120,000 Daxia coins is truly a considerable sum of money.

It might take Lin Yi's father, Lin Guodong, three or four years of hard work in the mines to earn this much.

[Ding! Detected that your currency has exceeded 100,000 for the first time, your SSS-rank talent: Gift of the Gods has unlocked a new function!]

A prompt sounded in Lin Yi's mind.

Huh? New function??

Lin Yi quickly opened his talent interface.

Soon, Lin Yi understood what this new function was.

[Gift of the Gods]

[Effect: You gain 1 skill point per second.]

[New Function: You can now spend real-world circulating currency to purchase a 24-hour skill point reward bonus!]

[Note, the currency required for each subsequent purchase will increase!]

[Current purchase cost: 10,000 Daxia coins.]

(Chapter end)
