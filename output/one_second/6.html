Chapter 6

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

【You have killed 2 LV.10 Forest Brown Bears and gained 280 experience points (80 points bonus for killing higher-level monsters)】

【Due to the effect of your beginner's experience boost scroll, you have gained an additional 10% experience (135 points)!】

【You have obtained 8 Giant Python Scales!】

【You have obtained 8 Giant Python Fangs!】

【You have obtained 2 Bear Paws!】

【You have leveled up to LV.2! Gained 10 free attribute points!】

After another wave of system prompts, Lin Yi was enveloped in a white light as he leveled up!

Killing these high-level monsters provided a large experience bonus, and the experience boost scroll that Jiang Ya, the little rich girl, had given him earlier provided an additional 10% experience.

This round of monster hunting directly allowed him to reach level 2.

Lin Yi allocated all 10 of his free attribute points into the Spiritual attribute.

His mana limit increased by another 700 points.

However, Lin Yi looked at his mere 20 HP and still felt a lack of safety.

That's right, although his offensive power and alertness were maxed out, there was still the possibility of him capsizing in this high-level grinding area.

Especially if there were any stealthy monsters that could ambush him.

With only 20 HP, he was too fragile.

Although the underlying mechanics of this world were similar to a game, reality was not a game, and there were no second chances.

But Lin Yi had already prepared a countermeasure for this situation.

With the additional 700 mana, he was now able to implement his previous plan.

Lin Yi began chanting.

Under the 70% casting speed boost from "Divine Blessing Chant", his spell was completed in less than two seconds.

As Lin Yi finished casting, a massive hexagram magic array appeared on the ground in front of him.

The magical runes within the array constantly shifted, causing the entire earth to tremble.

Scorching lava erupted from the cracks in the ground.

Subsequently, a towering elemental creature, standing five or six meters tall and composed entirely of molten, scorching lava, appeared.

This was a level 6 fire magic spell, Molten Lava Demon! It was a summoning spell that called forth a Molten Lava Demon from the Elemental Plane of Fire. This summoned creature possessed numerous skills and was a powerful, high-HP tank, completely obedient to Lin Yi's commands.

Lin Yi could order it to move, attack, or do as he pleased - it was incredibly docile.

Watching his mana plummet from over 5,000 to zero, Lin Yi felt somewhat strained. That sensation of having his mana drained was not a pleasant one.

But when Lin Yi saw the Molten Lava Demon's attributes and skills, he felt it was all worth it.

【Molten Lava Demon】

【Level: LV.100】

【Type: Elemental Creature】

【HP: 500,000/500,000】

【Mana: 250,000/250,000】

【Strength: 800】

【Agility: 500】

【Spirit: 800】

【Vitality: 1,000】

【Skills: Lava Blast, Quaking Strike, Scorched Earth, Lava Shield, Self-Destruct】

A level 100 summoned creature! With a massive 500,000 HP, it was undoubtedly a terrifying force that could easily overwhelm any other monsters in the Howling Forest.

Defeating thousands of them would be no problem at all.

What Lin Yi was most interested in was the Molten Lava Demon's Lava Shield skill, which he could also benefit from.

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The Magma Demon summoned a molten lava shield to protect itself.

As the Magma Demon's massive elemental dual arms swung, a layer of scorching hot lava shield enveloped Lin Yi's body.

Perhaps it was because Lin Yi had an exceptionally high affinity for the fire element.

The unbearable high temperature of the lava only gave him a warm, cozy feeling.

[Lava Shield: A searing shield covering the body, can absorb 15,000 points of damage. When you are attacked, inflict 500 points of fire damage to the attacker.]

Fifteen thousand shield points.

And a counterattack effect.

Lin Yi felt completely at ease.

With this shield that could last for an hour, and the terrifyingly huge Magma Demon by his side, he was filled with a strong sense of security! Under the 7% mana regeneration effect, his just-depleted mana pool quickly refilled.

Lin Yi chanted again, summoning a second Magma Demon.

Then a third one.

Gazing at the three majestic, imposing Magma Demons before him, Lin Yi was highly satisfied.

Simultaneously summoning three was currently Lin Yi's limit, but as he leveled up and his affinity for the fire element increased, he would be able to summon even more.

But for now, having three Magma Demons summoned at once was already an incredibly formidable combat force.

After some contemplation, Lin Yi made a decision.

He gave orders to two of the Magma Demons.

He commanded those two Magma Demons to roam and patrol the entire Wailing Forest, slaying any monsters they encountered.

The experience and rewards from the monsters they killed would naturally accrue to Lin Yi.

It was essentially two fully automated leveling "plugins", constantly helping him find and kill monsters.

This was far more efficient than Lin Yi leveling up on his own.

The remaining Magma Demon, he would keep by his side as a bodyguard.

Receiving Lin Yi's orders, the two Magma Demons quickly flew off to other areas of the Wailing Forest.

As elemental beings, they had no legs, only an upper body.

Their mode of movement was more like floating and gliding through the air.

Despite their bulky appearance, their speed was not slow, at least several times faster than Lin Yi could run.

Even more terrifying was the Magma Demons' "Scorched Earth" skill - a range-type area-of-effect ability that constantly expanded molten lava and intense heat around them.

Lin Yi could clearly see that wherever the Magma Demons went, the ground was left scorched, and the trees in the Wailing Forest began to burn furiously, leaving no vegetation.

Any monsters that entered the Magma Demons' Scorched Earth zone were almost instantly reduced to charcoal, without even a chance to fight back.

Lin Yi continued deeper into the Wailing Forest, accompanied by the remaining Magma Demon.

Along the way, Lin Yi's mind was constantly bombarded with notifications.

They were all about how many monsters had been killed, and how much experience he had gained.

After just a few steps,

Lin Yi's body was suddenly enveloped in a series of white flashes.

[You have reached Level 3! Gained 10 free attribute points!]

Before he could even finish allocating those 10 new attribute points,

There were another five or six white flashes around him.

[You have reached Level 4! Gained 10 free attribute points!]

[You have reached Level 5! Gained 10 free attribute points!]

[You have reached Level 6! Gained 10 free attribute points!]

[You have reached Level 7! Gained 10 free attribute points!]

Lin Yi was dumbfounded!

What the hell did those two Magma Demons do?

How did he suddenly gain such a massive amount of experience to instantly level up 7 times?

Leveling up in this world doesn't seem too difficult after all... (Chapter ends)
