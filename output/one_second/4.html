Chapter 4

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

That is, while the power is extremely terrifying, the skill consumption is also very large! The chanting time is also very long.

Take the 7th-tier spell [Heavenly Flame Dragon Breath] that Lin Yi just obtained as an example.

[Heavenly Flame Dragon Breath]

{Type: Active Skill}

{Tier: 7th}

{Attribute: Fire}

{Consumption: 30,000 Mana}

{Chanting Time: 20 seconds}

{Cooldown: 2 hours}

{Effect: Summon the scorching dragon's breath to descend from the sky, igniting all targets within the dragon's breath range, dealing massive fire damage, and inflicting long-lasting dragon flame burn damage.}

{Remark: Wherever it passes, it will be scorched earth.}

This 7th-tier spell requires the consumption of 30,000 mana points to cast.

It also requires a very long 20-second chanting time.

Lin Yi currently only has a 100-point mana limit, and he can't even use this high-level skill.

So Lin Yi turned his attention to his two passive skills.

Especially [Efficient Spellcasting]! He hopes that this skill can bring him surprises after upgrading, at least allowing him to cast high-level spells!

Lin Yi switched his skill points to [Efficient Spellcasting], and then, just like he did when adding points to Fireball, he started frantically adding points again!

The "ding ding ding" prompt sounds never stopped.

More than ten minutes later, Lin Yi received the prompt for the final skill upgrade.

{Consume 100,000 skill points, your Super Mana Surge has been upgraded, and you have gained a new passive skill: Mana Storm!}

After consuming more than a hundred thousand skill points, Lin Yi upgraded the [Efficient Spellcasting] little skill to 7th tier.

Of course, its name is now much more majestic.

[Mana Storm]

{Type: Passive Skill}

{Tier: 7th}

{Attribute: Universal}

{Effect 1: The mana cost of all your spells is reduced by 70%.}

{Effect 2: Your maximum mana from all sources is increased 7-fold, and you regenerate 7% of your maximum mana per second.}

{Effect 3: Every 8 hours, you have a chance to cast any spell below 9th tier for free, current available uses: 1, maximum stacks: 3.}

{Remark: I am the approaching storm.}

Whew...

Lin Yi took a deep breath.

This is an amazing passive skill! And it just solved his pressing problem! The mana cost of all his skills is reduced by 70%, which means his endurance is much stronger now.

The cost of a [Heavenly Flame Dragon Breath] has been reduced from 30,000 to 9,000, which, although still high, is no longer out of reach.

More importantly, he has 3 free casting opportunities every day.

This way, he won't be unable to use crucial skills due to lack of mana.

Great! Lin Yi wasted no time and turned his gaze to the last remaining passive skill - [Rapid Chanting].

Another over 10-minute frenzy of adding points.

Lin Yi was starting to feel a bit tired.

Although the experience of frantically upgrading his own skills is wonderful and exciting,

it is truly monotonous and tedious.

The required operation is just clicking and clicking...

Lin Yi's hands were sore.

If other practitioners knew of Lin Yi's troubles at this moment, they would probably be furious!

They all had to work hard grinding dungeons for dozens or even hundreds of skill points.

Thinking carefully, weighing the pros and cons, trying to maximize the cost-effectiveness and benefits when upgrading their skills.

Lin Yi, on the other hand, just mindlessly added points, and that's it.

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Soon, another brand-new seventh-tier skill was forged.

【Divine Chant】

[Type: Passive Skill]

[Tier: Seventh]

[Attribute: Universal]

[Effect 1: When you cast spells below the fifth tier, the spell becomes instant-cast. For spells above the fifth tier, the casting time is reduced by 70%.]

[Effect 2: Your casting will not be interrupted by any skills below the seventh tier that cause interruption, silence, or anti-magic.]

[Effect 3: Every 8 hours, you have a chance to instantly cast any spell below the ninth tier. Current available uses: 1, maximum stacks: 3.]

[Note: Blessed by divine grace, chanting the words of the gods.]

Here it is!

Instant-cast spells have arrived! Lin Yi had previously anticipated that his two passive skills would become incredibly powerful after advancing in tiers.

But he didn't expect them to be this powerful.

And they even synergize with each other.

Costless casting + Instant-cast!

This is our hot-blooded combo technique!

If Lin Yi wanted to, he could even instantly cast a seventh-tier Heavenly Flame Dragon Breath right on the spot!

Although Lin Yi hasn't tested the power of this skill yet, he has a premonition.

If he were to unleash this seventh-tier fire magic attack,

It might end up causing destruction to half of Jiangcheng.

He's not some kind of misanthropic fanatic, so naturally, he wouldn't do something like that.

But to be honest, Lin Yi is already itching to try it out.

Since he can't brazenly unleash high-tier skills in the city, maybe he can go to the remote high-level training grounds and give it a try?

Lin Yi quickly gathered the various alchemy concoctions, red and blue potions, experience scrolls, and return scrolls he had prepared, and dumped them all into the 50-slot personal space he gained after awakening his profession.

Afterwards, Lin Yi left his home and went to the commercial street of the living professions in Jiangcheng, where he freeloaded a large amount of food and water sources.

Completely filling up his personal space.

Daxia places great importance on talent cultivation.

So as a newly awakened professional like Lin Yi, he enjoys educational benefits.

He only needs to tell these living profession vendors, "I'm a student,"

and show his student ID to freeload a large amount of basic supply items.

With all preparations complete, Lin Yi left the inner city of Jiangcheng and headed to the suburbs.

The suburbs were an endless grassland. The gentle breeze and pleasant weather made it a nice day.

Lin Yi scanned the area and saw many people around his age swinging various weapons, hunting harmless little animals like rabbits and marmots.

This was the lowest-level training ground outside Jiangcheng: the Peaceful Grassland.

The monsters in this grassland were generally level 1-4, and they were all non-aggressive creatures.

Even the weak rabbits and marmots would only flee when attacked, not fight back.

This place was a paradise for newbies.

So a large number of newly awakened professionals would choose to level up here.

However, the leveling efficiency was relatively low.

Because there were more people than monsters.

Most monsters would be instantly killed by the onslaught of attacks as soon as they respawned.

So some more ambitious people, or those with stronger professions and ample resources, would venture deeper into the grassland to challenge more dangerous and powerful monsters for higher leveling efficiency.

Lin Yi bypassed the popular leveling spots in the Peaceful Grassland and headed deeper into the grassland.

It took him half a day to reach his destination.

After half a day, Lin Yi saw the dense forest at the end of the grassland and heaved a sigh of relief.

That was the higher-level training ground - the Howling Forest.

The monsters in the forest were generally level 9 and above, and they were bloodthirsty and aggressive, actively attacking intruders.

(End of this chapter)
