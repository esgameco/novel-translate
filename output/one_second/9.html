The first stage, which is the current level that Lin Yi is at.

The second and third stages correspond to Intermediate and Advanced titles respectively.

The fifth stage is Archmage, and the sixth stage is Grand Archmage.

Beyond the seventh stage, they are called Sage Archmages or Mage Saints.

In short, once a practitioner reaches the seventh stage, they can be addressed as a Saint.

As for higher levels beyond that, even Jiang Xuan does not understand or have knowledge of them.

That is a realm he cannot access.

So generally speaking, powerful practitioners who can use skills of a certain level will have their job advancement match that.

Cases of low-level practitioners possessing high-level skills are extremely rare.

So seeing the Sixth-Tier Inferno Demon spell is enough to infer this conclusion.

Jiang Xuan nodded, "About decades ago, I had the honor of going to the Pantheon, the highest mage association in the Blue Star, to study."
