Chapter 18

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Sizzle, sizzle, sizzle! The plague of black water that binds the ankles of all professionals.

Under the scorching dragon's breath.

Light speed turns into wisps of black gas.

Scattered in the air, disappearing without a trace!

All people regain their freedom!

They also saw the same thing, a figure like a deity soaring high in the sky!

Yes, in their eyes, the one who can use such terrifying magic, instantly dispelling all plagues and gloom, has already become their savior god!

High in the sky.

Lin Yi let out a sigh of relief.

Damn, this high-speed flight, rushing and chasing.

Fortunately, he made it in time! This Death Knight, fortunately, did not die.

The moment Lin Yi saw the Death Knight Riven Dahl, he didn't think much.

He directly unleashed a flash of Heavenly Flame Dragon's Breath.

He was afraid that the professional players surrounding the boss below would snatch the kill.

If he had to talk about his current highest damage skill, it would undoubtedly be the Heavenly Flame Dragon's Breath.

Moreover, this skill is very straightforward, without any dragging attack windup.

It's just an ordinary dragon's breath slamming down.

Causing devastating strikes.

In addition, Lin Yi is not a homicidal maniac.

His current mastery of the fire element can even achieve the effect that the Heavenly Flame Dragon's Breath will destroy all monsters, but not harm the professionals at all.

This is also the reason why that group of over a thousand professionals, who had fallen into despair, did not suffer a single injury under such a terrifying seventh-tier dragon's breath!

After the dragon's breath, Lin Yi narrowed his eyes.

Huh? Not dead?

Didn't even lose much blood? This boss has some tricks! Lin Yi was a bit surprised.

He had previously thought that a Heavenly Flame Dragon's Breath might not be able to kill the Death Knight.

But at least it should have taken away half of his health, right? Now it seems that even if he fires a few more Heavenly Flame Dragon's Breaths, it might not work.

Lin Yi stared at the Death Knight Riven Dahl.

Under the influence of the [All-Knowing Eye],

His information soon appeared before Lin Yi.

[Death Knight Riven Dahl (Mutated)]

[Level: LV.200]

[Tier: King Tier]

[Race: Undead]

[HP: 9,970,000/10,000,000]

[MP: 100,000/100,000]

[Strength: 15,800]

[Spirit: 8,000]

[Agility: 8,000]

[Endurance: 15,800]

[Active Skills: Undead Army, Plague Wave, Death Rift Slash, Grip of Death, Charge, Corpse Explosion]

[Passive Skills: Undead Will, Indestructible Body (Mutated)]

[Weakness: Holy Light Damage]

Lin Yi was stunned.

Level 200? Wasn't it said to be level 60?

How did the level suddenly increase so much?

Of course, Lin Yi also noticed the "Mutated" word on the information panel.

But he had never heard of this mechanism in his textbooks before.

Hmm, does it mean a strengthened version of the boss?

However, a Heavenly Flame Dragon's Breath only caused 30,000 damage to him, which Lin Yi found a bit puzzling.

Fortunately, with the [All-Knowing Eye], Lin Yi soon found the clue from the Death Knight's skills.

The problem lies in the passive skill [Indestructible Body (Mutated)].

[Indestructible Body (Mutated)]

[Type: Passive Skill]

[Tier: 7th Tier]

[Effect: The power of the Abyss has strengthened your physique, granting you extraordinary damage resistance!]

[All damage you receive, except for Holy Light damage, is reduced by 50%, and the maximum single-instance damage you can take is 9,999 points.]

[You recover 5,000 HP per second.]

This passive skill is a bit overpowered.

The most terrifying part is the single-instance damage limit of less than 10,000 points.

No wonder a Heavenly Flame Dragon's Breath couldn't do much to him.

The dragon's breath attack came crashing down, but it only caused a little bit of damage.

Even the high single-target damage from the dragon's breath attack only dealt 9999 points of damage, and the subsequent blood loss was just from the burning damage.

After seeing the devastating passive skill of the Death Knight Riven,

Lin Yi suddenly felt relieved.

It's a good thing he had just upgraded his wind magic yesterday.

Otherwise, he would have had a hard time dealing with this boss.

It seems that a balanced multi-elemental development path is more effective in boosting combat power than single-element optimization.

Lin Yi used to look down on wind magic a bit.

The main reason was...

It felt a bit weak.

Just compare the basic third-tier spells.

Even without his current attributes and equipment, the triple damage of the Triple Flame Spear already exceeds 10,000 points.

But the nine strikes of the Gale Nine Blades don't even reach 5,000 points in total.

Although it attacks quickly and repeatedly,

it just feels too weak.

But the situation is different now.

When facing a boss like the Death Knight Riven who can withstand high single-target damage, the multi-hit wind magic becomes invaluable!

...

A few minutes ago, in the Cyan City.

At the Professional Association headquarters.

The lobby was still orderly and calm, with various professionals constantly coming to consult and handle matters.

But in the conference room, the top-level officials of the Cyan City Professional Association were all dripping with sweat.

"We can't handle it!"

"We absolutely can't handle it!"

"Based on the data comparison of the wild world bosses that have mutated in our Greater Summer region over the past century, their levels have generally increased by at least 3 times."

"That means the world boss in the Desolate Land right now is at least level 180!"

"I've been saying all along that once the only world boss around Cyan City mutates, it will be a ticking time bomb!"

"I didn't expect it to happen so soon!"

All the attendees had ashen faces.

The screen in the conference room was currently displaying the fully mutated Death Knight.

No one had a crisis response plan ready.

Finally, the chairman of the Cyan City Professional Association, sitting in the main seat, made the final decision.

"I've already sent a request for aid to the headquarters of the Professional Association in Tianhai City. They've dispatched a sixth-tier professional, and he's on his way here!"

"But even for him, it will take two hours to get from Tianhai to our Cyan City."

"In these two hours, we need to complete the evacuation of the citizens and defend against the undead army summoned by the mutated Death Knight."

"If we can't handle it properly, this will be a major disaster for our Cyan City!"

The vice chairman, a bald man, was stunned when he heard the chairman's decision to evacuate the Cyan City citizens.

"But... Mr. Chairman!"

"Sending the alarm now may cause unrest among the citizens, and if anything else happens..."

The bald man didn't even finish speaking before being abruptly interrupted.

"If the plague and corruption from the Desolate Land spreads to our Cyan City, it won't just be about your official hat, but whether you'll have to go work at a sewing machine for the rest of your life!"

"Are you truly that stupid, or just too eager to advance?"

The chairman's gaze was icy cold as he scolded the vice chairman mercilessly, publicly embarrassing him in front of everyone.

"Issue a level-one alert, then notify the city defense forces to prepare for the evacuation of the citizens!"

"Go do it now!"

As the Cyan City leadership made the decision to evacuate, a chilling alarm sounded throughout the entire city.

Countless professionals also received the regional broadcast, just like the previous incident in Jiangcheng when Lin Yi summoned the Inferno Demon.

They had no way to deal with such a high-level, powerful monster. They could only wait for the true powerhouses of the entire East River Province to arrive!
