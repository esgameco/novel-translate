Jiang Xian looked at Lin Yi with great interest.

"I've never seen you before, but I've heard your name so much that my ears are almost calloused."

"My granddaughter has mentioned you quite a few times."

"In fact, just this morning, she asked me to wait for you and arrange this trial for you."

Zuo Qian's eyes lit up when he heard this.

He never expected that Elder Jiang would actually know Lin Yi! This made things much easier.

Lin Yi felt a bit awkward.

The old man in front of him also had the surname Jiang.

Coupled with his words, it was not difficult to guess that Jiang Xian should be Jiang Ya's grandfather.

"Chairman Jiang, it's not very appropriate for you to openly give your acquaintances special treatment, is it?"

A somewhat cold voice came from behind Jiang Xian.

It was a young man dressed in a suit, wearing gold-rimmed glasses, with a somewhat chilly demeanor.

The other person standing beside him also chuckled lightly, "And to be frank, it's not that we look down on the educational level of your Jiangcheng, but rather that among the new students of this batch, there are probably only a few who can complete this trial. Instead of occupying the spots, they'll just be going to their deaths."

"It would be better to give way to the new students of our Tianshui Academy. We've come from far away, and we don't want to wait too long."

Jiang Xian heard this.

His expression also became unpleasant.

He had originally been very polite, adhering to the concept of "the guest is king" in welcoming this group that had come all the way from Tianshui.

It was just to let the new first-year mage profession students of their batch participate in this trial instance.

Lin Yi frowned slightly.

He really disliked the way these people were arrogant and looking down on others.

It was true that this group from Tianshui Academy were almost all second-class or above, and a few even reached the third-class.

But they weren't that strong either.

What were they pretending for?

Just as the few were conversing,

Suddenly, there was a cry of alarm from the entrance of the instance.

Following the sound, Lin Yi saw Jiang Ya, looking somewhat pale, being supported by a nearby practitioner, walking in a dazed manner.

Jiang Xian's expression changed, and he hurriedly went forward.

Even based on past experience, this mage profession trial was not considered particularly difficult.

But there was still risk involved.

Could it be that they had failed to complete the trial?

"Little Ya, you all...?"

Jiang Ya was clearly under great shock, her whole body trembling slightly, her complexion deathly pale.

Seeing her grandfather, Jiang Ya's eyes turned red, and tears immediately streamed down, "Grandpa, Second Uncle, Second Uncle... Wuwu!"

Jiang Xian's expression became completely gloomy.

He also noticed that this time, only three people had returned with his granddaughter - and one person was missing! That was Jiang Ya's Second Uncle, Jiang Xian's second son!

"What happened, Little Ya, don't cry, calm down and tell Grandpa!"

Jiang Ya couldn't stop trembling all over.

"In there... the monsters, there were too many!"

"We couldn't kill them all, and as long as we stayed in one place, more and more monsters would keep coming to attack us. There was almost nowhere to hide in the entire space."

"In the end, it was Second Uncle who gave us the last four instance escape scrolls, then drew the attention of those monsters, so that we could escape!"

"Second Uncle, he..."

"Wuwu wuwu!"

Jiang Xian and Zuo Qian, upon hearing this, both felt a chill run down their spines! After all, this trial instance was only open to mage profession practitioners.

If it was really as Jiang Ya described, with the monsters constantly searching for and attacking them,

Without tank professions to withstand the attacks, and without priest healers,

Even if the other four besides Jiang Ya were all second-class or even third-class, they still wouldn't be able to withstand such a high-pressure environment.

Looking at it this way, their initial assessment that this was only a D-level difficulty trial instance was completely wrong!

Now it seems, it was at least an A-level difficulty!

"Help... !"

"There are injured people here, is there anyone who can heal them?"

The most dreadful thing was the comparison.

"Principal Zuo, you don't need to apologize for things beyond your control."

"Even without a strategy guide or electronic map, this trial shouldn't be too difficult, right? I want to give it a try."

If this trial was truly only at the D-level difficulty, Lin Yi actually found it rather uninteresting.

Now, seeing so many people failing, he felt a spark of interest.

"But in the current situation, I doubt anyone would be willing to team up with you..."

Zuo Qian believed Lin Yi must have the capabilities, but he pointed out the awkward predicament.

In this every-man-for-himself environment, who would be willing to team up with him and enter the instance?
