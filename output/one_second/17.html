Of course, Lin Yi did not know that his sudden take-off from Wuhu had shocked so many people in the Azure City.

Soon, he flew up to the high skies of Azure City, with a wide-open view.

The entire Azure City was within his sight. And the surrounding leveling areas were also clearly visible.

With a single glance, Lin Yi saw the region hundreds of kilometers south of Azure City.

That barren and gloomy area should be the Desolate Land.

Lin Yi controlled the wind wings and began to fly towards the Desolate Land at full speed.

...

At the same time, in the Desolate Land.

The Desolate Land was not desolate at the moment.

A large number of professionals, like sharks that had caught the scent of blood, were flocking towards the central area.

That was the respawn point of the world boss, the Death Knight.

However, they still encountered strong resistance.

"The Thunder Legion Guild has booked the area, please leave, idle personnel!"

"The Star Sea Guild has restricted this area, please leave immediately!"

Countless professionals in the uniforms of various guilds were stopping any idle professionals who dared to approach the central area, not allowing them to get close at all.

"What the hell, is this your family's world boss?"

"Yeah, why don't you let us in?"

The guild members responsible for the blockade sneered.

"That's right, it's our Thunder Legion Guild's, you dare to try and get in?"

Faced with such unreasonable and tyrannical behavior, countless professionals were angry but dared not to speak up.

The Thunder Legion and Star Sea were the two major professional guilds in Azure City.

These two guilds often demarcated areas in popular leveling zones, allowing only their own guild members to level up, completely disregarding other professionals.

Some had tried to resist before, but the leaders of these two guilds were powerful professionals close to the fifth-tier, with elite troops under their command, so this situation had continued until now.

Now was the critical time for the Death Knight to respawn, and the two guilds naturally wanted to monopolize all the experience and loot.

Boom! Boom! Suddenly, the central area of the enclosure was filled with the rumbling sounds of skill collisions.

Everyone knew that the Death Knight Riven Dale had respawned! The elites of the Thunder Legion and Star Sea Guilds had begun to kill the boss.

However, this time the situation seemed a little different.

Because the sound of skill collisions soon subsided.

Subsequently, a wave of black pestilence permeated with a strong aura of death began to spread across the entire Desolate Land.

Suddenly, the professionals responsible for guarding the outer perimeter and restricting other professionals from entering were all panic-stricken.

They received distress messages from their guild members in the channel: "Something's wrong, the boss is calling for help, let's hurry over!"

Instantly, the members of the two guilds all rushed towards the central area of the Desolate Land.

The professionals who had been driven out to the periphery all revealed expressions of joy and followed them in a rush.

There was a major difference between a world boss and a dungeon boss.

That is, the distribution of experience points and skill points after the kill is based on the contribution to the kill.

The more damage you contribute to the world boss kill, the more skill points and experience points you will get in the division.

And the loot will only depend on who delivers the final blow.

The crowd surged towards the center of the Desolate Land.

The sight that met their eyes made everyone feel as if their throats were tightly grasped by the hand of death, unable to utter a sound! Countless professionals in the uniforms of the Star Sea and Thunder Legion Guilds were lying in disarray, maintaining the posture of fleeing outwards.
