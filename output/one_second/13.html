"It cost 10,000 skill points, and your Swiftwind Flash has been upgraded. You have obtained a new spell: Shocking Tidal Tempest!"

In less than half a day, Lin Yi's wind-elemental skill set has expanded from 1 to 7 skills.

Lin Yi quickly reviewed these skills, and he felt that the characteristics of wind magic were quite distinct.

If fire magic is simply about high damage, then wind magic combines speed, multi-hit attacks, light control, and some supportive abilities. The most obvious ones are speed and multi-hit attacks.

The 3rd-tier fire spell is called Triple Flame Spear, which summons three flame-forged spears from the sky to pierce and burn the enemy. The damage has 3 stages, which is already considered high among the fire spells Lin Yi has mastered.

But the 7th-tier spell Heavenly Flame Dragon's Breath only has the initial high-damage dragon's breath and the subsequent persistent burn damage.

In contrast, the 3rd-tier wind spell Gale Nine-Slash fires 9 extremely fast wind blades in 0.5 seconds. And the 7th-tier Shocking Tidal Tempest can inflict over a thousand strikes per second on targets within the area of effect.

However, what surprised Lin Yi the most were the two new 5th and 6th-tier skills:

[Gale Wings]

- Active Skill

- 5th Tier

- Wind Element

- Costs 500 MP/sec

- 1.5 sec cast time

- 5 min cooldown

- Gathers wind elemental power to cloak your true form, making it hard for others to see you.

- Forms a pair of wind wings, granting you high-speed flight.

- Also creates a wind barrier around you that can block 3 ranged attacks (up to 4th-tier strength).

- Note: "Gathering the gale, I take to the skies!"

Flight ability! This has been Lin Yi's long-cherished dream as a human - to fly freely in the sky. He never expected wind magic to have such a spell that allows mages to fly without relying on mounts.

As for the 6th-tier skill [Swiftwind Flash]:

[Swiftwind Flash]

- Active Skill

- 6th Tier

- Wind Element

- Costs 15,000 MP

- Instant cast

- 1 hour cooldown

- Requires a melee weapon

- Infuses your speed and your melee weapon's attack speed to the extreme, then unleashes a lightning-fast charge in one direction.

- Grants 100% dodge chance during the charge.

- After the charge, deals 99 strikes of your melee weapon damage plus 1 wind-elemental slash on all targets in the charge path.

- Max charge distance: 10 meters

- Max charge speed: 10 times the speed of sound

- Note: "Watch closely, this strike will be magnificent!"
