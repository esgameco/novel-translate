The moment Lin Yi saw this skill, he felt that this skill was undoubtedly the cool and stylish type.

Just like the countless animations he had seen in his previous life.

The swordsman who pursued speed as his lifelong pursuit.

Drawing the sword and charging, before the enemy could react, he had already slashed dozens of times!

Then sheathing the sword.

The enemies behind him slowly fell.

It was truly badass.

But why would this kind of skill appear in the mage's skill set?

As a mage, using this skill, I also need to wield a melee weapon.

Although the staff is also considered a melee weapon,

let me tell you, making me use the staff to frantically hit the opponent hundreds of times would feel very awkward!

Lin Yi was walking and looking at his new skill at the same time, and he just happened to arrive at the city center of Jiangcheng.

Here were located the most critical functional departments of the entire Jiangcheng city.

Lin Yi saw the most magnificent building with the largest flow of people, with the five big characters "Daxia Trading Hall" written on it.

Just like the Daxia Bank, the Daxia Trading Hall was also a state-owned functional department supported by the Daxia government in the background.

The trading chain covered the entire Daxia.

However, Jiangcheng was only a level 5 base city, and the connection between its branch and the headquarters was not completely non-existent, but almost non-existent.

The circulation of goods and prices were basically controlled by Jiangcheng itself.

Entering the door of the trading hall, passing through the bustling crowd, Lin Yi came to a self-service trading window.

Since the Calamity Era, the development of spiritual crystal technology in various countries on the Blue Star has become quite mature.

The self-service trading windows in the trading hall are the culmination of this.

Each self-service window had no one, only the blue spiritual crystals floating in the air, just like the one Lin Yi participated in during the job change ceremony.

This was Lin Yi's first time coming here.

He looked at how others were operating and quickly understood how to use the thing in front of him.

This spiritual crystal, as long as you touch it, a full-system screen will pop up.

Then you can operate on the screen.

Whether it's selling or buying, the small teleportation array on the trading counter can instantly deliver your goods.

Lin Yi found it very novel.

Online ordering, lightning-fast delivery, on-the-spot arrival, it's really amazing!

Lin Yi placed all the junk black iron equipment that had dropped from the Forest Tiger King and its tiger cubs and grandchildren under the spiritual crystal for scanning.

The spiritual crystal quickly gave an estimate.

Lin Yi's previous calculation was not bad, these equipment in the trading hall were just the national benchmark recycling price, not worth much money.

But fortunately, after a Heavenly Flame Dragon's Breath, there were still more than a dozen pieces of equipment.

After selling the dozen or so black iron equipment, Lin Yi's card had an additional more than 10,000 Daxia coins.

Subsequently, Lin Yi also sold the materials that had dropped from the monsters he had leveled up before in his personal space.

The most valuable ones were one or two special materials dropped by the Tiger King.

The spiritual crystal gave an estimate of two or three thousand.

The rest were basically just small change.

Finally, Lin Yi cleared his backpack, and the balance in his card rose from a few dozen to more than 20,000 again.

This money was not enough to buy another 24-hour afk skill point reward.

Lin Yi planned to see if he could find any good items in the trading hall.

He searched for mage skill books.

Sure enough, there were none.

Lin Yi thought for a moment and quickly understood.

On the one hand, the drop rate of skill books in low-level areas was really too low, too low, and Jiangcheng was a super remote small city, with not much total goods.

This also proved that Lin Yi's killing of the Tiger King yesterday and dropping a skill book was really incredibly lucky.

On the other hand, even if the goods were available, many people were also unwilling to come to the trading hall in this remote small city to trade.
