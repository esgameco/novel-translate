Lin Yi did not immediately go to check the effect of this new skill.

Instead, he glanced over and saw how many skill points were required to upgrade the "Fire Elemental Incarnation" skill from 8th tier to the next tier.

【9th Tier Forbidden Incantation - Calamity of the Eight Realms】

【Advancement Requirement: 1 million skill points!】

Whew...! It directly doubled again! 1 million skill points, that's too terrifying.

Even for him, without considering any cash shop accelerations, it would take nearly 12 days of grinding to obtain.

But 9th tier is already a forbidden incantation! The small goal he had set for himself earlier is actually very close now.

At most, he just has to wait it out for 12 days, and he'll be able to gather the 1 million skill points.

Afterwards, Lin Yi opened his skill menu.

He began to examine the new skill he had just obtained.

【Supreme Transcendence, Fire Elemental Incarnation】

【Type: Active Skill】

【Tier: 8th】

【Attribute: Fire】

【Consumption: None】

【Incantation Time: Instant】

【Cooldown: 3 days】

【Active Effect: Your understanding of the fire element has reached the pinnacle. You can choose to fuse with the fire elements of heaven and earth, entering a state of Fire Elemental Transcendence.】

【Fire Elemental Transcendence: You are immune to all physical damage, immune to all fire-attribute damage, and gain the ability to fly.】

【In this state, when you cast fire-attribute skills, your critical strike chance is increased by 100%, critical strike damage is increased by 100%, and you ignore 50% of the target's magic resistance.】

【In this state, all fire-attribute skills you cast become instant-cast and no longer consume mana.】

【The Transcendence state lasts for 3 minutes. During this time, you can freely manipulate the vast amounts of fire elements in the surrounding area, instantly raising the temperature to millions of degrees Celsius, triggering a terrifying explosion and inflicting massive fire damage.】

【Remark: I am the Raging Flames!】

When Lin Yi first saw the "Fire Elemental Incarnation" skill,

he had guessed that it was likely a state-type skill.

Similar to a superhero transformation, like a Kamen Rider.

Turns out his guess was pretty much spot on.

The description and effects of this 8th tier fire skill were practically a short essay.

After reading through it, Lin Yi couldn't help but exclaim inwardly that this skill was overpowered! It was an extremely rare composite skill.

On one hand, this skill also had damage capabilities.

In the Transcendence state, he could trigger a super explosion.

And the explosion's range and damage were likely even more exaggerated than the Heavenly Flame Dragon Breath.

At the same time, it also granted him a state.

This state could directly transform Lin Yi from a human flesh-and-blood body into a pure elemental life form.

In this state, all his fire-attribute spells would be guaranteed critical hits, increased in damage, instant-cast, and cost no mana! Bad news: this skill is a Frankenstein's monster.

Good news: It has stitched together everything that could be stitched!

Lin Yi immediately decided to try out this skill in the current instance.

Fire Elemental Incarnation, Supreme Transcendence required no incantation and consumed no mana.

Lin Yi just had to focus his thoughts.

He saw blue flames ignite at his fingertips.

The human characteristics of his fingers, nails, bones, and flesh rapidly disappeared.

The blue flames then spread, and in the blink of an eye, Lin Yi found that his hands were now burning with raging flames.

This transformation from flesh-and-blood to pure elemental fire was a very unique experience.

Not only was there no pain, but it also brought Lin Yi a great sense of novelty.

Lin Yi's rate of transcendence exceeded his expectations.

Almost in an instant, he felt his life form had already completed its metamorphosis.

He was no longer a carbon-based life, but had become an elemental life.
