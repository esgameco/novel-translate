There was no danger in the Celestial Tomb.

Xu Yan and Du Yuying, protected by the Xuan Wind Sword Qi, entered the Celestial Tomb without encountering any special situations.

Only the last door leading into the tomb chamber seemed to have something attached to it, a faint fluctuation, perhaps the remnant consciousness of a Celestial Warrior?

However, that remnant spiritual consciousness was very weak and did not possess any thinking ability, seeming only like a mark.

When Du Yuying approached, the faint spiritual consciousness fluctuated, and the tomb chamber door opened.

As the tomb chamber door opened, the spiritual consciousness immediately dissipated.

"For identity verification?"

Xu Yan pondered.

The tomb chamber was very spacious, with a central pool filled with dense spiritual energy, enveloping the entire chamber in a hazy spiritual mist.

Floating in the pool was a crystalline leaf-like spiritual herb, emanating a faint fragrance, and in the center of the leaf was a drop of blood-red liquid. It seemed to be a drop of essence blood!

Du Yuying looked at the drop of essence blood on the leaf in the pool, and a mysterious desire arose in her heart, wanting to absorb it into her body.

"Young Master Xu, that is what I need. Absorbing it will solve my problem."

Du Yuying spoke softly.

"There doesn't seem to be any danger here."

The Xuan Wind Sword Qi had scanned the tomb chamber, and if there were any remnant spiritual consciousness, Xu Yan would have immediately sensed it.

"Young Master Xu, I don't need the spiritual herb in the pool. You can take it for your cultivation. The pool contains abundant spiritual energy, which is also a good place for cultivation."

Du Yuying began to undress while speaking in a low voice.

Xu Yan's gaze remained calm, without the slightest change, and he nodded, saying, "I don't need the spiritual herb, it may not be suitable for me, but I can cultivate in the pool."

Du Yuying was wearing a thin, tight-fitting garment, revealing much of her jade-like delicate skin, stunningly beautiful.

Her jade-like face flushed red, and she stole a glance at Xu Yan, only to find that he was staring at the pool, as if the pool was more attractive than her.

He was not at all captivated by her beauty.

She felt a little disappointed, but also admired him greatly, "Indeed, Young Master Xu is pure-hearted and handsome."

She stepped into the pool and gently placed the drop of blood on her palm, absorbing it into her skin. The drop of blood seemed to contain a certain spiritual charm.

Under the nourishment of the pool and the spiritual herb, it had transformed into a drop of spiritual blood.

The spiritual blood slowly seeped into her delicate skin, causing a flush to spread across her fair skin, making her even more alluring.

Thump! Thump! Du Yuying felt her heartbeat accelerating, a warm current circulating in her body, as if her body was becoming more rounded, and some unknown defects were being repaired.

Spiritual energy surged into her body from the pool.

A sense of drowsiness overcame her, and Du Yuying's eyelids grew heavy, wanting to fall asleep.

With great difficulty, she raised her head and saw Xu Yan standing by the pool, observing the changes in her body. At that moment, her heart calmed, and she closed her eyes, falling into a deep slumber.

Xu Yan watched Du Yuying in the pool, sensing that she was undergoing a transformation, as if the defects in her body were being repaired.

Confirming that there was no danger, he stepped into the pool and sat down on the other side. The spiritual mist in the tomb chamber surged into his body like a whirlpool.

The spiritual energy in the pool also continuously flowed into his body. Xu Yan realized that the spiritual energy in the pool seemed to be endless, no matter how much he absorbed, it would never be exhausted.

"Could there be a spiritual crystal vein underneath the pool?"

Xu Yan was greatly surprised.

This Celestial Tomb should have other treasures to be found, and Xu Yan was in no hurry to search for them, instead choosing to settle down and begin cultivating.

He transformed into a Mountain-River Spiritual Body, and the terrifying spiritual energy constantly poured into his body, being frantically refined into true essence.

"This time, breaking through to the Small Perfection of the Penetrating the Profound Realm should not be a problem."

Xu Yan murmured to himself.

Time passed, and the spiritual mist in the tomb chamber had been completely absorbed by Xu Yan, while the spiritual energy in the pool continued to flow endlessly, showing no signs of drying up.

Xu Yan became increasingly convinced that the pool was built upon a spiritual crystal vein.

Considering that this was the Celestial Tomb, having a spiritual crystal vein here was not surprising.

At a certain moment, his aura surged, and he broke through to the Small Perfection of the Penetrating the Profound Realm.

"I've reached the Small Perfection of the Penetrating the Profound Realm. The distance to the Great Perfection is not too far, and even without using the pool for cultivation, I wouldn't be too far from breaking through to the Great Perfection."

Xu Yan got out of the pool. Du Yuying's physical defects had been completely repaired, and her aura had become stronger.

Great Perfection Grandmaster!

Shifting his gaze, Xu Yan examined the tomb chamber and saw that after the spiritual mist had dissipated, a section of the tomb chamber wall had been hollowed out.

In the recess, a coffin was placed.

He couldn't help but become curious. Why was the coffin placed in a recess instead of in the main tomb chamber?

"Young Master Xu!"

Du Yuying had woken up.

Her eyes were filled with excitement. The problem with her body had been solved, and her strength had greatly increased. More importantly, she felt that her innate talent had also improved significantly.

After getting out of the pool, she used her cultivation to evaporate the water on her body and put her clothes back on.

"Who was the owner of this tomb?"

Xu Yan asked as he approached the coffin.

He was very curious about the Celestial Warriors.

"It may be an ancestor, but I'm not sure. I only know this is the place left behind by my ancestors, where I can remedy my physical defects. The specifics, I'm not too clear about."

It seemed there was some secret here that she didn't know about.
