Xu Yan snatched the divine artifact from the Thousand Martial Hall and fled. Elder Blade tried to stop him, and was a little surprised - this powerful expert from the Myriad World Alliance actually came out to support the scattered cultivators.

"He really came out to back up the scattered cultivators!"

This obstruction allowed Xu Yan to escape far away, and there was no way Elder Blade could catch up to him. After Xu Yan broke through the Divine Origin Realm, his speed was like lightning - even the strongest experts couldn't catch up to him. Of course, Elder Blade and the Thousand Martial Hall were confident they could chase and kill Xu Yan, but that was just their inherent belief that divine artifacts couldn't be stored in a storage bag.

How could Xu Yan hide the Frigid Severance Blade and evade the pursuit? It was impossible!

However, the Thousand Martial Hall's people never imagined that while divine artifacts couldn't be stored in a storage bag, they could be kept in a storage ring!

Xu Yan looked at the Frigid Severance Blade, admiring its extraordinary craftsmanship.

"It's a good blade. If my Second Martial Brother doesn't want it, I can have Fourth Martial Brother refine it, the materials used to forge this blade are extraordinary!"

Divine artifacts were undoubtedly the most supreme treasures in the Spiritual Realm, and the foundation of the Transcendent Spiritual Sect.

"This trip was not in vain!"

Xu Yan was very satisfied, and directly stored the blade in his storage ring.

He had blocked the Thousand Martial Hall's mountain gate, slaughtered many of their geniuses, and seized a divine artifact. That scoundrel Huang Liang had shamefully claimed that his sword dao was stolen from the Thousand Martial Hall - now Xu Yan had finally vented this grievance.

Immediately, his divine avatar dissipated.

Since Tian Ji had escaped, the Thousand Martial Hall probably didn't want to start a major battle with the Myriad World Alliance at this moment, and thus didn't pursue Tian Ji.

"Where should I go next?"

Xu Yan pondered for a moment, then chose a direction and headed off.

"I'll find a city to stay in and gather news of the Spiritual Realm, and see how my Second Martial Brother is doing."

Xu Yan didn't take the Thousand Martial Hall's pursuit seriously at all.

Seizing the divine artifact was precisely to lure the Thousand Martial Hall to chase him, so that his journey across the Spiritual Realm would be more thrilling and full of challenges...

The news of Xu Yan blocking the Thousand Martial Hall's mountain gate, slaughtering their geniuses, and openly seizing their divine artifact, the Frigid Severance Blade, swept across the Spiritual Realm like a storm.

Since the Calamity of the Blood Demon, there had never been such a shocking event in the Spiritual Realm.

And it was the first time in history that someone had stolen a divine artifact from the Transcendent Spiritual Sect!

Xu Yan was truly a formidable hero!

In a certain noble family, a dignified man sighed and said, "If only my son could be like Xu Yan!"

He then turned to look at his own useless son, his face flushing red with anger as he grabbed a whip and lashed out viciously.

"You wretched son, if you had even one-hundredth of Xu Yan's abilities, I would be more than satisfied!"

In a certain great city, many cultivators gathered, all discussing the theft of the Thousand Martial Hall's divine artifact and the humiliation they suffered.

An elder suddenly sighed and said, "Among the countless geniuses in the world, Xu Yan is a model!"

Hearing this, a young man was immediately fired up, and declared boldly, "You're right, I will take Xu Yan as my role model. He dared to block and challenge the Transcendent Spiritual Sect, so I will go and block and challenge a top-tier sect!"

The onlookers watched the young man leave with a sense of resignation, feeling he was just going to get himself killed.

In the Great Proclamation City, Meng Chong stood on the arena, holding his blade. By now, no more geniuses dared to challenge him. All the powerful sects were livid, especially the Three Severance Tower.

One person had suppressed many top-tier sect geniuses.

The arrogant and disdainful sect geniuses had lost their overbearing aura.

Meng Chong sighed inwardly, these sect geniuses were too weak.

He had already reached the peak of the Divine Will Realm and was accumulating his foundation, preparing for his own transformation and breakthrough.

Seeing no more geniuses coming to challenge him, his gaze shifted to the Three Severance Tower's Sect Master and elders.

He had an idea.

"The Three Severance Tower's Sect Master's strength is indeed formidable, but he is not unbeatable. Even their elders, one-on-one, pose no threat to me. They may not even be able to accumulate their dao foundation well, and with my Unbreakable Golden Body, it's almost impossible for them to seriously injure me. Since that's the case, I might as well give it a try.

"Cultivating the physical dao requires constant tempering and self-improvement. With the Heaven Hammer Hundred Forges Technique boosting me, my foundation will accumulate even faster.

"I can't drag this on any longer. The Spiritual Realm is vast, and staying in the Great Proclamation City is meaningless. I also don't know how my Eldest Martial Brother is doing, I heard he recently blocked the Thousand Martial Hall's mountain gate.

"After finishing up here in the Great Proclamation City, should I also go and block the Thousand Martial Hall's mountain gate?"

Meng Chong muttered to himself.

With this in mind, he looked at the Three Severance Tower's Sect Master and scratched his head, grinning widely.

"The people of the Three Severance Tower must hate me to death and want to kill me, right?"

The onlooking cultivators all rolled their eyes. Wasn't that obvious? The Three Severance Tower's three top geniuses had all been killed by him, their corpses nowhere to be found. Of course they hated him to death!

Not just the Three Severance Tower, all the top sects' experts despised him!

The Three Severance Tower's Sect Master and elders glared at him with murderous intent, but seeing the orders passed down from the League of Provinces, they could only suppress their killing intent.

"I, Meng Chong, am giving you all a chance today. Three Severance Tower's Sect Master and elders, you can come at me now. I challenge you old fools!"

Meng Chong raised his blade and declared arrogantly.

The Three Severance Tower's Sect Master and elders were stunned, even doubting if they had heard correctly. Meng Chong was actually challenging them all together?

Where did he get such courage and arrogance?

The watching cultivators were all dumbfounded. Had Meng Chong become so arrogant after easily defeating all the geniuses, that he thought he could sweep away the Three Severance Tower's Sect Master and elders? This was just courting death!

"Unacceptable!" the League of Provinces' Sect Master hurriedly tried to dissuade him.

However, the Three Severance Tower's Sect Master wouldn't let this opportunity slip by, and directly descended onto the arena, sneering coldly, "Since you're the one challenging us, killing you won't violate the rules of the Genius Confrontation!"

"Foolish!" the League of Provinces' Sect Master and the others sighed.

Youthful rashness, not knowing one's own limits! Meng Chong looked at the Three Severance Tower's Sect Master, then at the elders surrounding the arena, and sneered, "One of you is not enough, have those old fools come as well."

The Three Severance Tower's Sect Master was stunned for a moment, this bald youth's brain didn't seem to be working properly.

Taking a deep breath, he nodded, and the Three Severance Tower's elders immediately descended onto the arena, surrounding Meng Chong.

Using their numerical advantage to bully him, these shameless acts didn't bother them at all, since it was Meng Chong who had challenged them.

"He's gone overboard!"

"Meng Chong is going to meet his demise in the Great Proclamation City."

"This is no longer youthful rashness, but arrogance beyond measure."

The onlooking cultivators sighed.

"Come at me!" Meng Chong threw out a punch, striking first.

"Kill!" The Three Severance Tower's Sect Master and elders unleashed their powerful auras, launching a fierce attack. The tremendous forces shook the arena, sending the onlookers flying into the air as the intense battle unfolded.
