Here is my full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Xu Yan nodded. These remnant souls, able to survive until now, were certainly not simple.

Fortunately, he had been cautious enough to completely destroy the opponent.

The fact that such remnant souls existed in the Tiankuyu Ruins meant that someone must have encountered them before, and may even have been bewitched by them.

After thoroughly searching the surroundings and confirming there were no omissions, Xu Yan continued his exploration.

This Tiankuyu Ruin was not particularly large, but not small either.

Carefully exploring bit by bit would still take several days to complete.

"Huh?"

Suddenly, he discovered a skull beneath the loose soil. Even more bizarre was that this skull was blood-red, as if it had been soaked in fresh blood.

With a wave of his hand, the skull floated up.

"I've finally waited for you!"

Faint red flames suddenly danced in the skull's eye sockets as it let out a sigh.

"I have been dormant for so long, just waiting for the destined one to pass down my legacy..."

"Do you have any divine artifacts or treasures?" Xu Yan interrupted to ask.

"What are divine artifacts? Young man, don't set your sights so low. As long as you obtain my legacy, forget about divine artifacts and treasures, you'll be able to choose from the supreme treasures of heaven and earth..."

The skull said proudly.

Xu Yan drew his sword, with the Xun Wind Sword Intent surrounding the skull, and said calmly: "Don't waste words or make empty promises. If you don't have divine artifacts then just die. I'm not interested in your legacy!"

The skull was a bit dumbfounded. How could this young man be so impetuous and short-sighted?

Had it misjudged him?

"Young man, do you know who I am? Missing out on my legacy will be the biggest regret of your life. For the sake of mere divine artifacts..."

Xu Yan couldn't be bothered to listen further and directly slashed down with his sword!

"Wait!" The skull hurriedly spoke up.

"Speak!"

Xu Yan's sword stopped less than three inches above the skull.

"There are divine artifacts. My skull itself is a divine artifact. If you receive my legacy, you can obtain my divine artifact..."

Crack!

The skull was split open by a sword strike. The dancing red flames seemed to reveal a bewildered look.

How did the sword suddenly come down?

"This is...?"

Then it was greatly shocked.

Whoosh! Like a howling gale, the red flames were completely extinguished before they could say anything more.

Xu Yan thrust his sword into the ground. The Xun Wind Sword Intent storm swept in all directions. After confirming the skull's remnant soul was not hiding, he finally sheathed his sword.

"What's going on with these remnant souls?"

Xu Yan pondered.

Whether it was the remnant soul on the small tree earlier, or this skull remnant soul, when they spoke, there was a bewitching power that sought to confuse one's mind.

He thought of the other geniuses who had entered the Tiankuyu Ruins this time. If they encountered such remnant souls, they would likely be easily bewitched.

"With Master here, even if they are bewitched it won't amount to anything."

Xu Yan continued exploring.

On top of Changqing Pavilion, Li Xuan suddenly looked towards the Tiankuyu Ruins with a thoughtful expression.

"These remnant souls are a bit strange. Why do they all like to target Xu Yan?"

The others hadn't encountered remnant souls like Xu Yan had. Among everyone, only Xu Yan had encountered them.

The remnant souls seemed to be specifically going after Xu Yan.

"These remnant souls don't belong to many people, but to one person, and moreover..."

Li Xuan took a deep breath. He had some guesses in his mind.

All the remnant souls in the Tiankuyu Ruins belonged to one person. They couldn't even be called remnant souls, but rather split souls!

Some powerful expert had split their soul in the Tiankuyu Ruins. After suffering heavy damage, it had further split into multiple remnant souls, existing in a special way.

Moreover, these remnant souls were unable to leave the Tiankuyu Ruins.

Once they left the Tiankuyu Ruins, they would be erased by the laws of heaven and earth.

"An outside invader?"

Li Xuan suddenly became interested. Since there were remnant souls, why not capture one to interrogate and find out the situation?

"It's targeting Xu Yan again. You really like fixating on my disciple, huh?"

Li Xuan raised his hand to grab, and a giant palm descended from the sky, grabbing towards the remnant soul that had just appeared.

Xu Yan looked at the jumping bead before him, with remnant soul aura permeating it. Just as he was about to take action to destroy it, he saw his master make a move.

On top of Changqing Pavilion, Li Xuan sat leisurely in his chair.

Above his head, a small Dahuang God Image appeared, with mysterious energy surging.

A light red bead floated in front of him, spinning round and round. Then a human face appeared on the bead.

The face wore a shocked expression.

"Who are you?" The face on the bead asked in a deep voice.

"Speak. Why did you target my disciple?" Li Xuan asked coldly, looking at the face.

The face's expression tightened, its brows furrowing as it said in a deep voice: "Since he is your disciple, let this matter end here. How about that?"

"Heh!" Li Xuan chuckled. "Explain clearly. If you do, there may still be room for negotiation. Otherwise, all this soul power of yours won't need to exist anymore."

"Why must you do this, sir? I merely saw that your disciple was very unique, different from the martial artists of this world, so I became curious. That's all."

The face's tone became somewhat cowardly.

"Is that all?" Li Xuan looked at him coldly. "Confess properly. Identity, background, intentions - explain it all clearly."

The face fell silent.

"I dare not offend you, sir. Let's end this here."

The face suddenly began to dissipate, disappearing in an instant.

It had chosen self-destruction rather than reveal anything, or perhaps it didn't want to provoke Li Xuan, who seemed unfathomable in its eyes.

It hid its identity as much as possible to avoid its true self being targeted.

"How decisive!"

Li Xuan wasn't particularly disappointed by this.

The other party was clearly no ordinary martial artist. Since it chose self-destruction rather than offend him, it showed that his Dahuang God Image plus mysterious aura had frightened the other party.

"If you're going to self-destruct, why bother hiding the rest of your soul power?"

Li Xuan's gaze turned towards the Tiankuyu Ruins as he raised a hand and lightly pointed.

Whoosh! A gentle breeze swept through the entire Tiankuyu Ruins. At this moment, all the remnant souls were swept out and dissipated in the breeze.

"Sir..."

A voice sounded, then fell silent.

Li Xuan's gaze was indifferent. Since nothing could be learned, and there was no more value, why keep them around? Naturally, he waved his hand to destroy them all.

The Tiankuyu Ruins expedition was over.

The gains were meager, almost nothing.

As for those remnant souls, they had completely disappeared. For a very long time to come, this Tiankuyu Ruin would not have anything special about it.

The Changqing Pavilion flying ship left the Tiankuyu Ruins. Fu Tianhai and the other Spirit Realm experts, along with the group of geniuses, also departed the Tiankuyu Ruins one after another.

"The God Bridge will open in two years. When the God Bridge opens, we can enter the other shore of the God Bridge," said the Great Zhou Emperor.

As for how the God Bridge opened, or how it existed, they didn't know.

Although they knew of the land on the other shore of the God Bridge, they didn't understand the circumstances there.

The existence of transcendent spirit sects was to discover spirit body geniuses and cultivate them to the Spirit Realm's limits.

Blood Spirit Child was quite ruthless too.

Furthermore, the core changes of the entire world, the core of the entire world, would all be within his grasp.

"The other side of the Divine Bridge, another region of Taicang, where the world's spiritual energy is more active and the spiritual qi is more concentrated."

Li Xuan pondered in his heart.

The Taicang World was divided into grid-like regions. The closer to the center of the Taicang World, the more active and concentrated the world's spiritual energy and spiritual qi.

The upper limit of martial arts was higher.

However, why was the Taicang World separated in this way? And why were the regions connected by portals and bridges, unable to freely come and go? What was the reason for this? Li Xuan was quite curious about this as well.

"Once we go to the other side of the Divine Bridge, we should know the answer, right?"

"Above the Divine Human who Refines the Spirit, there is the Heavenly Venerable. There's the Law Condensing Heavenly Venerable, the True King Heavenly Venerable, the Immortal Heavenly Venerable... The Divine Phase Realm should correspond to the Law Condensing Realm, but my martial way has surpassed the realms of Taicang martial arts starting from the Divine Origin Realm."

"The strength of the Divine Phase Realm corresponds to the True Refinement Realm, on par with the True King Heavenly Venerable. That being said, my strength is still somewhat inferior to the Immortal Heavenly Venerable."

There must be Immortal Heavenly Venerables on the other side of the Divine Bridge.

With his current strength, even if he could fight against an ordinary Immortal Heavenly Venerable, he would not be invincible on the other side of the Divine Bridge.

"Am I invincible below the Immortal level?"

Li Xuan muttered to himself.

The Immortal Heavenly Venerable was a huge leap, just like the Great Divine Human and the Divine Human who Refines the Spirit. It was a huge watershed.

What is immortality? It's precisely because of this that their lifespan is nearly infinite.

They can only be killed, and will not die due to reaching the end of their lifespan.

The Immortal Heavenly Venerable merges with heaven and earth, ruling over a region of the world as the venerable, but the Void-Breaking Realm breaks open the void of heaven and earth, is not bound by the laws of heaven and earth, and can traverse the space of heaven and earth..."

And the Void-Breaking Realm was not far off. Xu Yan was about to completely organize and thoroughly comprehend the martial methods of the Void-Breaking Realm!
