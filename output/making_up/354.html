Fortunately, he had been cautious enough to completely exterminate the other party.

This Heavenly Abyss ruin was not large, but not small either.

It would take a few days to thoroughly explore it bit by bit.

"Huh?"

Suddenly, he discovered a blood-red skull buried under the loose soil, which was even more eerie.

With a wave of his hand, the skull floated up.

"I've finally been waiting for you!"

Faint red flames flickered in the eye sockets of the skull, and it let out a sigh.

"I have been in seclusion for a long time, only waiting for the fated person to pass down my inheritance..."

"Do you have any divine treasures?" Xu Yan interrupted the other party's words and asked.

"Divine treasures? Young man, don't set your sights too low. As long as you obtain my inheritance, not only divine treasures, but the most precious treasures in the heavens and earth will all be yours to choose from..."

The skull said arrogantly.

Xu Yan drew his sword, the Xunfeng Sword Qi surrounding the skull, and said calmly, "Enough nonsense, and don't try to lure me. Without any divine treasures, you can just die. I'm not interested in your inheritance!"

The skull was a bit dumbfounded, how could this young man be so impatient and short-sighted?

Had it misjudged?

"Young man, do you know who I am? Missing my inheritance will be your greatest regret in life, all for some divine treasures..."

Xu Yan was tired of listening, directly slashing down with his sword. "Wait!"

The skull hurriedly spoke.

"Speak!"

Xu Yan's sword stopped just inches above the skull.

"There are divine treasures, my skull is a divine treasure. If you obtain my inheritance, you can also obtain my divine treasure..."

Crack! The skull was split open by a single sword strike, and the flickering red flames seemed to reveal a bewildered expression.

How could he suddenly strike down like this?

"This is...?" It was greatly alarmed.

Whoosh! Like a raging wind, the red flames were completely extinguished before it could say anything else.

Xu Yan stabbed his sword into the ground, and the Xunfeng Sword Qi swept through the area, confirming that the skull's soul had no more hiding places, before sheathing his sword.

"What's going on with these lingering souls?" Xu Yan pondered.

Whether it was the lingering soul on the small tree earlier or this skull's lingering soul, they had a bewitching power when speaking, trying to confuse one's mind.

He thought of the other geniuses who had entered the Heavenly Abyss ruin, if they encountered such lingering souls, they might easily be bewitched.

"With my master here, even if they are bewitched, it won't amount to anything."

Xu Yan continued his exploration.

On the Changqing Pavilion, Li Xuan suddenly looked towards the Heavenly Abyss ruin, with a pensive expression.

"These lingering souls are a bit strange, why do they keep targeting Xu Yan?"

The others had not encountered lingering souls like Xu Yan, he was the only one.

The lingering souls seemed to be specifically targeting Xu Yan.

"These lingering souls are not for many people, but belong to one person, or rather... not even lingering souls, but soul fragments!"

Li Xuan took a deep breath, he had a guess.

The lingering souls in the Heavenly Abyss ruin all belonged to one person, and they could not even be called lingering souls, but soul fragments!

A powerful expert had split their soul in the Heavenly Abyss ruin, and after suffering a heavy blow, the soul fragments were left behind in a special way.

And these soul fragments were unable to leave the Heavenly Abyss ruin.

Once they left, they would be erased by the laws of heaven and earth.

"An external invader?" Li Xuan suddenly became interested. Since there were soul fragments, why not capture one and interrogate it to know the situation?

"It's targeting my disciple again, so fond of keeping an eye on him, aren't you?"

Li Xuan raised his hand, and a giant palm descended from the sky, grabbing the soul fragment that had just appeared.

Xu Yan was about to strike down the flickering pearl-like soul fragment when he saw his master act.

On the Changqing Pavilion, Li Xuan was sitting leisurely in a chair.

A small Great Desolate Deity appeared above his head, and mysterious aura was surging.

A faint red pearl floated in front of him, spinning around, and then a human face appeared on the pearl.

The face was full of shock.

"Who are you?" The face on the pearl asked solemnly.

"Speak, why have you targeted my disciple," Li Xuan asked indifferently.

The face became solemn, frowning, and said in a deep voice, "Since it is your disciple, let's leave it at that, how about that?"

"Heh!" Li Xuan chuckled lightly. "Explain clearly, and maybe there's still room for negotiation, otherwise your soul force won't be allowed to exist anymore."

"Sir, I was merely curious about your disciple, as he is quite unique and different from the other martial artists in this world." The face spoke in a somewhat timid tone.

"Is that all?" Li Xuan looked at him indifferently. "Explain your identity, origin, and intentions clearly."

The face fell silent.

"I don't want to offend you, sir, so let's leave it at that."

The face suddenly began to disintegrate and vanished without a trace. It chose self-destruction rather than revealing anything, or perhaps it did not want to provoke Li Xuan, whose existence seemed unfathomable in its eyes.

"How decisive!" Li Xuan was not too regretful about this.

The other party was obviously not an ordinary martial artist, and since it chose self-destruction rather than offending him, it seemed that his Great Desolate Deity and mysterious aura had frightened it.

"Since it wants to self-destruct, why bother hiding the rest of its soul force?"

Li Xuan's gaze turned to the Heavenly Abyss ruin, and he lightly tapped with his hand.

Whoosh! A gust of wind swept through the entire Heavenly Abyss ruin, sweeping out all the remaining soul fragments, which then disintegrated and disappeared.

"Sir..." A voice sounded, then fell silent.

Li Xuan's gaze was indifferent. Since he could not get any information, they had no more value, so he simply waved his hand to extinguish them.

The expedition to the Heavenly Abyss ruin was over. The harvest was meager, almost nothing.

As for those soul fragments, they had completely disappeared, and this Heavenly Abyss ruin would not have any special features for a long time to come.

The Changqing Pavilion's flying ship left the Heavenly Abyss ruin, and Fei Tianhai and other Spiritual Domain experts, as well as a group of geniuses, also departed.

"The Divine Bridge will open in two years, and then we can enter the other side of the Divine Bridge," said the Great Zhou Emperor.

As for how the Divine Bridge opens and how it exists, they did not know.

Although they knew the place beyond the Divine Bridge, they did not understand the situation there.

The existence of the Transcendent Spirit Sect was to unearth Spiritual Body geniuses and cultivate them to the Spiritual Domain realm.
