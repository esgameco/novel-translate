Ice State, location of the Sky Cave.

A bed floated above the Sky Cave, surrounded by light gauze. A graceful figure could be vaguely seen lying lazily inside.

Around the bed, hundreds of beautiful women encircled it.

They had varied charms - some gentle, some fiery, some coquettish, some serene... Without exception, they all had light gauze covering their delicate bodies, in pink, white, black, green, yellow and other colors.

These people were all once warriors of the Snow Palace.

"Some prey has arrived," Mei Wu murmured with a smile.

Her alluring face was full of anticipation.

"My prey, are you all ready? Letting you die happily is the greatest mercy I can show you. How kind-hearted I, Mei Wu, am!"

Mei Wu smiled brightly.

"What is that?"

Wu Tiannan and the other experts arrived outside the Sky Cave. Looking at where the Snow Palace used to be, now transformed into a pitch-black cave, they were shocked.

"Is it the Sky Cave?" Xin Mengrou frowned.

"It seems to be the Sky Cave, but why would there be a Sky Cave at the Snow Palace? There's only one Sky Cave relic in the Spirit Realm!"

The master of Thunder Cloud Manor said in confusion.

Among these experts, Wu Tiannan was the only one who wasn't a Spirit Lord, so he was completely baffled. What was the Sky Cave?

"The Sky Cave is a mysterious cavern that doesn't exist on the ground. It's like an independent small space, or like a hole opened between heaven and earth."

"There's one Sky Cave relic in the Spirit Realm with some opportunities inside, but since too much time has passed, there are probably few opportunities left."

"The spots for the Genius Competition are for entering the Sky Cave relic."

Xin Mengrou explained to him.

"How did the Sky Cave come to be?" Wu Tiannan asked with furrowed brows.

"We don't know!" Xin Mengrou and the others shook their heads.

"The Sky Cave that appeared at the Snow Palace seems off. There's likely danger, so everyone be careful," the master of Spirit Control Manor said solemnly.

"Those are people from the Snow Palace!"

"Is that... the Jade Palace Master of the Snow Palace?"

"They are all..."

Seeing the crowd of beauties surrounding the Sky Cave, the Spirit Lord experts were all shocked.

Looking at them now, all the people of the Snow Palace seemed to have become somewhat wanton.

"Something's not right!"

"They're probably no longer their original selves, or their natures have greatly changed?"

"I never imagined the Jade Palace Master would be like this..."

An elder from Spirit Control Manor said painfully.

"Guests from afar, please forgive our poor hospitality!" the Jade Palace Master said with a charming smile.

"Since you've all come, why don't we have a deep exchange together?"

The elder from Spirit Control Manor's expression tightened. He stepped forward and said, "Let me go investigate what strange changes have occurred!"

With that, he rushed straight towards the Jade Palace Master.

"Forget it, he used to have a secret crush on the Jade Palace Master, and now..."

"Sigh, everyone keep watch. Let's hope nothing goes wrong!"

The other elders from Spirit Control Manor sighed.

The rest all watched intently, ready to act immediately if anything went wrong.

"Hiss, how could the Jade Palace Master be so... so..."

"Is this really the Jade Palace Master? Something seems off!"

The crowd of experts saw that as soon as the Spirit Control Manor elder approached, the Jade Palace Master happily welcomed him. Then she squatted down, pulled open the elder's pants with both hands, and opened her small mouth...

The sight left everyone dumbfounded and in disbelief.

"Ah!"

Suddenly, a scream of agony came from the Spirit Control Manor elder.

"Quick, attack! They are no longer their original selves. Be careful of them devouring..."

The Spirit Control Manor elder cried out in horror.

Boom! The master of Spirit Control Manor attacked fiercely, unleashing the might of his divine weapon.

But a faint pink light emerged, easily blocking the divine weapon's power. The Spirit Control Manor elder turned into a withered corpse.

"What a wonderful taste, so wonderful!"

The Jade Palace Master smiled enchantingly, her jade face flushed. She even burped.

At this moment, everyone's scalps tingled in horror!

"What kind of technique is this? Soul possession? The Jade Palace Master's own strength wasn't weak, how could she be possessed? Moreover, this technique is no longer the Jade Palace Master's..."

"When has the Spirit Realm ever had such a bizarre technique?"

"The appearance of the Sky Cave likely heralds the arrival of a crisis. We must attack and kill them!"

The master of Thunder Cloud Manor and the others said with solemn expressions.

"Let's attack!"

Xin Mengrou stepped forward. As she walked forward, dreamlike illusions spread out, blurring reality and fantasy.

"So strong!"

The master of Thunder Cloud Manor and the others' expressions tightened.

Boom! Wu Tiannan wielded his ruler and launched an attack from mid-air, striking directly at the Jade Palace Master.

When he made his move, the expressions of the Thunder Cloud Manor master, Spirit Control Manor master, Great Zhou Emperor and others changed slightly. Wu Tiannan had grown even stronger.

"Attack!"

The group of transcendent Spirit Lord experts wielded their divine weapons and attacked.

"So many wonderful flavors, how interesting!"

Mei Wu narrowed her eyes on the bed.

"Too Elusive Dream? Interesting!"

Her gaze fell on Xin Mengrou.

Boom!

The great battle erupted. The Thunder Cloud Manor master and the others were now the strongest group in the Spirit Realm. Wielding divine weapons, they attacked fiercely, unleashing torrents of violent power.

The Thunder Cloud Manor master wielded the Thunder Cloud Hammer divine weapon. Lightning illuminated the sky as he smashed down violently towards the crowd from Snow Palace.

Pink light gradually spread out, surging in all directions.

The Jade Palace Master and the others also began to attack. Their attacks immediately made the experts' hearts tremble.

The battle was intense.

Xin Mengrou fought against the Jade Palace Master. Her dream powers constantly shifted, hiding deadly intent between illusion and reality.

Wu Tiannan wielded his ruler, continuously striking out, beating back several Snow Palace warriors.

As everyone got closer to the Sky Cave, the bed behind the Snow Palace women also came into view. Looking at the figure on the bed, everyone knew that was the mastermind behind the scenes.

"Who are you, creating such calamity and bringing chaos to the Spirit Realm?" the Great Zhou Emperor asked sternly, crimson flames surging around his body.

"Mei Wu's hunting grounds naturally require deaths. You are all my prey."

Mei Wu laughed coquettishly.

Boom! Suddenly, the gauze on the bed lifted, and that graceful figure was revealed to everyone for an instant. In that moment, a delicate white hand reached out from the bed, grabbing towards the Great Zhou Emperor.

"Your Majesty, be careful!"

A top expert from Great Zhou beside the emperor cried out in alarm. He moved in a flash, instantly blocking in front of the emperor. His divine weapon struck out violently.

However, that delicate white hand directly broke through the violent attack and grabbed onto his neck.

Whoosh! Without any resistance, he was instantly pulled onto the bed.

Through the lifted gauze, everyone saw the beauty on the bed open her small mouth and lightly suck in the Great Zhou expert's soul.

"Ah!"

A terrified scream rang out.

Thud! The corpse was thrown out, falling among the Snow Palace women. One of the women caught the corpse and bit into the soulless body with her small mouth. In an instant, the corpse withered away.

This horrifying scene made the experts' hearts tremble in terror, their bodies shaking.
