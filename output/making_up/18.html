Chapter 18: A Top Martial Artist of the Jianghu, Killed with a Single Punch!

Xu Yan rode a tall, powerful horse as he left the city, with two large packages hanging from the horse's back. One contained the precious medicinal herbs needed for his cultivation, and the other held the precious tiger skin of the Crimson-Eyed Tiger.

"The young master of the Xu family is going to seek out another expert again."

"Judging by the packages, it seems he's bringing valuable gifts to offer the expert."

Discussions arose as Xu Yan rode past, for the foolish son of the wealthy family was once again going to seek out an expert.

Xu Yan paid no heed to these comments, feeling proud in his heart.

"A bunch of frogs at the bottom of a well, how could they know the vastness of the heavens and earth!"

After leaving the city, Xu Yan immediately noticed the hidden guards following him. His parents were surely worried about his safety, so they had the guards secretly protect him.

But these guards were far too weak. With Xu Yan's abilities, if he were to encounter any real danger, these guards wouldn't be able to save him - they'd likely be killed instantly.

About ten miles outside the city, Xu Yan spurred his horse and quickly entered a forest. With a little effort, he was able to lose the guards following him.

Confirming that no one was tailing him, Xu Yan returned to the small mountain village. His master disliked disturbances, so Xu Yan couldn't let anyone know the location of his master's secluded abode.

As the small town came into view, Xu Yan planned to rest there briefly before continuing on to the mountain village.

Suddenly, a figure rushed up from behind, leaping into the air and landing steadily in front of Xu Yan, blocking his path.

Xu Yan's brow furrowed. Judging from the intruder's agile movements, he was a top-tier martial artist in the jianghu. It seemed the newcomer had ill intentions.

"Hehe, young master Xu, I've finally found you. I won't waste time - hand over the medicinal herbs, and I'll spare your life!"

The man blocking the path was around forty years old, with limbs that were unusually long and thick compared to a normal person. However, his torso appeared thin and disproportionate, giving him a lanky, gangly appearance.

Sitting atop his horse, Xu Yan looked down at the man with disdain. "You?"

"You're courting death!" the lanky man growled.

He leaped into the air, his palm striking out towards Xu Yan's face.

Sitting calmly on his horse, Xu Yan didn't even furrow his brow. He could sense the power behind the man's palm strike, but it was nothing to him.

For cultivators of the Qi Kingdom, while they could shatter rocks and break through stone, their abilities were still quite ordinary to Xu Yan, who had already undergone five bone metamorphoses and was nearing the refinement of his true golden bones.

The strength of his qi and blood was far beyond what a mere top-tier martial artist could compare to. Even if he stood still and allowed the man to hack and slash at him, Xu Yan was confident he would emerge unharmed.

Facing the lanky man's palm strike, Xu Yan coldly shouted, "Begone!"

He raised his fist and struck out.

The fist collided with the palm strike.

The lanky man's eyes gleamed with malice, believing his powerful palm strike could easily shatter the young master Xu's arm, and then a follow-up strike would take his life. The precious medicinal herbs would then be his.

Boom!

However, upon impact, the man's expression suddenly changed drastically. His powerful strike had been crushed like paper, and his arm was instantly shattered.

The fist continued, slamming into the man's chest.

Boom!

The lanky man was blasted away, his chest caved in, his bones shattered, and his internal organs pulverized. He was dead instantly.

Xu Yan had killed a top-tier martial artist of the jianghu with a single punch. He felt exhilarated.

"Too weak!" he exclaimed. "Like a mere grasshopper, killed with a single punch."
