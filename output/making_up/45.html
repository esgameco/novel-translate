Chapter 45: I Wish to Appoint the Young Master as the Great Saint of the Heavenly Mother Sect

"You dog of an official, how dare you come to ransack my home? Are you tired of living?"

Xu Yan's face was filled with rage as he glared at the Prefect of Donghe. A scarlet-red dragon coiled around him, exerting a powerful pressure that forced the Prefect to sit down on the ground.

"My father worked hard to do some business and earn some money. Is that so difficult?

"Just because the Emperor's two sons are fighting over the throne, my Xu family has to be implicated? This is outrageous!

"My grandfather, the Xu family patriarch, is respected by the Emperor's third son. That is the Emperor's good fortune."

As soon as Xu Yan spoke these words, the expressions on Xu Junhe and his wife's faces changed drastically.

This was extremely disloyal and treacherous talk!

They could be executed along with their entire family for this!

The ambusher Kou Ruozhi, who was hiding outside the Xu family estate, was overjoyed upon hearing this. Seeing Xu Yan's majestic and awe-inspiring demeanor, a bold idea formed in his mind.

The Prefect of Donghe pointed a trembling finger at Xu Yan, "You... you are disloyal and treacherous! This is tantamount to rebellion. What crime shall you be punished with!"

"Seize them! Capture the father and son of the Xu family who intend to rebel. This is a great opportunity to accomplish a meritorious deed!" the Prefect roared.

Although Xu Yan had displayed formidable, godly power, there were always those who, blinded by the prospect of rewards, thought they could achieve glory by recklessly attacking him.

"Everyone, charge together! He is just one person, do not be afraid!" the officer leading the troops shouted, brandishing his weapon.

Quelling a rebellion and capturing the ringleader of the traitors would be an enormous feat, bringing wealth, status and high office within reach.

The Prefect of Donghe turned to Chen the Escort Leader and the others, saying, "All who participate in capturing this traitorous villain will be reported to the court, and great rewards, high offices and wealth will be yours. Seize this opportunity!"

Those were all top experts!

Chen the Escort Leader and the others exchanged glances, tempted but also hesitant.

"Archer troops!"

Outside the Xu family estate, rows of soldiers drew their bows, the cold arrowheads aimed at Xu Yan and the others.

Lady Xu's face turned pale as she trembled, "Yan'er, quickly escape! Don't worry about your father and I!"

"Mother, don't worry. Today, I will seek justice for our family!"

Xu Yan's eyes gleamed with icy coldness as he angrily said, "My father worked hard doing business, minding his own affairs. Yet because of the Emperor's family affairs, he is implicated. This is utterly unreasonable!"

His fury was beyond containment.

"If not for my father earning money through his business, where would I get the funds to pay the master's initiation fee?

"Without the family's wealth that my father accumulated, how could I have the means to cultivate the supreme martial arts?"

Facing the icy arrows and hundreds of soldiers, as well as the top experts, Xu Yan showed no fear. In his eyes, they were all mere insignificant insects.

A single strike of the Descending Dragon Palm could annihilate them all!

"Hmph, you are mere frogs in a well, unaware of the vastness of the heavens and earth. Today, I, Xu Yan, shall let you know what true martial arts are!"

Suddenly, an excited shout rang out, "Young Master Xu is right, the foolish old Emperor rules with incompetence and blindness. He deserves to be rebelled against!"

Thirteen figures leaped up from amidst the soldiers, soaring over their heads and landing in the center of the scene.

The leader, holding a goose-feather fan, had an excited expression on his face. The other twelve, wielding weapons, had cold, grim expressions.

"The Heavenly Mother Sect?" Xu Yan's brow furrowed.

He did not have much enmity towards the Heavenly Mother Sect, as long as they did not interfere with the interests of the Xu family. The news of their arrival had reached him earlier from the County Magistrate of Yunshan, who was a disciple of the Heavenly Mother Sect.

"The Twelve Pinnacles of the Tianmu Sect pay our respects to the Heavenly Sovereign!"

With such an invincible Heavenly Sovereign leading the Tianmu Sect, how could their grand ambition not succeed?

Xu Yan looked at the excited and fanatical Kou Ruozhi, completely bewildered.

What is this "Heavenly Sovereign" nonsense?

If I want to rebel, what does that have to do with you?

"What Heavenly Sovereign? Hold on, you all are from the Tianmu Sect, right? Don't tell me you're going to change the name to the Heavenly Sovereign Sect?"

Xu Yan opened his mouth, utterly perplexed.
