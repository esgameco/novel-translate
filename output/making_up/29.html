Chapter 29: Xu Yanming Comprehends the Cultivation Technique of the Blood Qi Realm

Although Xu Yan had entered the martial dao, his daily life remained the same - cooking when it was time to cook, feeding the chickens when it was time to feed them. Li Xuan also seemed to be as carefree as usual.

Behind the scenes, however, Li Xuan was racking his brains, trying to figure out how to create a cultivation framework for the Innate Realm, how to develop the theories for the Innate Realm, and even the cultivation technique.

In addition to the Innate Realm, he also needed to have a concept of the future cultivation direction.

In order to blindly create realms and cultivation techniques later on, he needed to lay a solid theoretical foundation.

If the cultivation technique was not mysterious enough, the cultivation practiced by Xu Yan would not be strong enough, and the realm of understanding would not be deep enough, which would not be sufficient for the prodigy Xu Yan to deeply comprehend.

If the technique was too mysterious and deviated from the framework, he was afraid it would not be able to connect to the next stage, and even more afraid that Xu Yan would not be able to comprehend it, getting stuck in the cultivation of the Blood Qi Realm.

"Damn, this is too difficult!" Li Xuan sighed secretly.

The pressure in his heart prevented him from giving free rein to his creativity, making him feel constrained in his creation.

If he just blindly created the Innate Realm cultivation technique, he was afraid Xu Yan would not be able to comprehend it and get stuck in the Blood Qi Realm cultivation, ultimately harming himself.

On Li Xuan's side, he was secretly troubled by the blind creation of the Innate Realm cultivation technique.

These past few days, Xu Yan had also been racking his brains trying to comprehend the four-line cultivation technique, but made no progress, feeling anxious and depressed.

"If I cannot even comprehend this simple cultivation technique, my master must be disappointed.

"What's the use of refining my jade-like bones? If I cannot comprehend the cultivation technique, how can I continue to cultivate the martial dao?

"The martial dao is boundless. If I cannot even comprehend such a simple cultivation technique, how can I cultivate to become stronger and reach higher realms of the martial dao?"

Xu Yan frowned and pondered deeply.

Suddenly, he remembered his master's words.

"My master said that as long as I try my best, not being able to comprehend it does not hinder my cultivation...

"I have been completely immersed in the cultivation technique, consuming my mental energy, and neglecting my actual cultivation.

"My master was actually telling me not to be too obsessed with it, but to find enlightenment in the process of cultivation itself..."

After this realization, Xu Yan's eyes lit up.

He immediately calmed his mind and began to cultivate, carefully feeling the circulation of his blood qi and its gradual strengthening, with a single-minded focus on the cultivation.

Occasionally, the four-line cultivation technique would float into his mind.

As he cultivated, Xu Yan gradually gained some enlightenment: "A thousand streams and ten thousand rivers flow into the great river... Each time I cultivate, my blood qi is increasing, growing bit by bit, just like the countless streams and rivers.

"Flowing for thousands of miles into the great ocean... The essence of this is the gathering of blood qi, becoming like the great ocean...No, it is the physical body becoming like the great ocean, containing the blood qi;

"Surging waves rise up in layers, the blood qi can shake the mightiest mountains - The blood qi cannot be calm, it must be like a raging current, like surging waves, only then can it fully stimulate the blood qi and effectively strengthen the physique.

"The blood qi can shake the mightiest mountains, does this not mean - the bones are like mountains, and the blood qi can shake them..."

Suddenly, enlightenment dawned on Xu Yan, and he felt that the meaning of the four-line cultivation technique had emerged in his mind.

At this moment, he vaguely comprehended and grasped the profound principles of the martial dao hidden within.

"The gathering of blood qi, like a raging current, like surging waves; striking the jade-like bones... The entire body becomes like a great ocean..."

Xu Yan fell into a state of deep contemplation, and his blood qi gradually began to flow like a raging current, layer upon layer of blood qi continuously pounding against his skeletal frame.

"I understand now!"

At a certain moment, Xu Yan's heart was filled with excitement as he finally grasped the profound martial dao principles hidden within the four-line cultivation technique.

He took a deep breath, and his bones began to tremble, with a rumbling sound resonating throughout his body, as if there were thunderous roars echoing within.

The sound was not loud, as if it was only reverberating within his body.

At a certain moment, strands of blood qi began to emerge, circulating in a new way.

Li Xuan summarized his experience, and the next time he edited the cultivation technique formulas, he needed to make them more profound, with a stronger artistic conception, and also be able to connect with the cultivation realm.

They couldn't be too broad.

Otherwise, it would be difficult to comprehend.
