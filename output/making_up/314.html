Wu Tian Nan was shocked, the senior spoke not of the Spirit Realm martial dao, but the Tai Cang Martial Dao!

"Senior, how can I break through this shackle?"

Wu Tian Nan asked respectfully.

"Go beyond the Spirit Realm, just as from the Inner Realm to the Spirit Realm, Tai Cang is the same."

Li Xuan said calmly.

The words cannot be said too directly and clearly, as a senior, his words must give a sense of profundity and deep meaning.

Wu Tian Nan murmured to himself, "Beyond the Spirit Realm, just like from the Inner Realm to the Spirit Realm, the Divine Bridge!"

At this moment, he thought of the Divine Bridge, perhaps that is the bridge leading out of the Spirit Realm.

Li Xuan continued, "Of course, there are also other methods, but for you, the difficulty would be too great."

He then raised his hand again, gathering the principles of heaven and earth, and drew a few strokes on the spiritual paper.

Several principles described by the power of the heavenly and earthly laws were imprinted on the spiritual paper, corresponding to the principles of the Tai Cang heaven and earth.

If one can comprehend and understand the principles on this spiritual paper, they can use it as a foundation to break through the shackle and refine the principles of heaven and earth into the body, surpassing the limit of the Spirit Realm.

However, achieving this is not something that can be done in a short time.

Since Wu Tian Nan has come, and had the chance to meet this senior, he will bestow upon him this opportunity.

If it were not for Xu Yan's guidance of Wu Tian Nan to come here, and Li Xuan happening to have just broken through, feeling elated, he would not have been so generous in bestowing this opportunity to Wu Tian Nan.

Today is his fortune!

Wu Tian Nan sat cross-legged, placing the spiritual paper on his knees, his spiritual consciousness carefully observing and examining it, as if he had entered into a profound and mysterious realm.

It was as if he could see the operation of the principles of heaven and earth, unfathomable and incomprehensible.

Moreover, they are all powerful experts from the Inner Realm who have converted to cultivate the Great Desolate Martial Dao.

As the Great Desolate martial artists become more and more numerous, and the Great Desolate Martial Dao is taught more and more widely, the trend is that the martial dao of the Inner Realm will eventually be eliminated.

The Great Desolate Martial Dao has already surpassed the Tai Cang Martial Dao.

Su Ling Xiu has comprehended the Divine Ability Realm.

With the breakthrough in the Dao of Alchemy Martial Dao, Li Xuan felt that his vitality was endless, his lifespan seemingly infinite, and his life force would never be exhausted.
