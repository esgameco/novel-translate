Zhu Liang and the Elders of the Yushen Sect were waiting solemnly.

The various attendants and disciples were all lined up to welcome them.

Their faces were filled with curiosity and excitement, many hoping that Xu Yan would be killed and their grievances avenged.

In midair, two figures stepped forward.

Seeing this, Zhu Liang and the others immediately became solemn. Leading the way was a young man with a cold, stern face and eyebrows like golden leaves - he was Lu Xingtian, the Second Elder of the Chimeng Sect, following half a step behind him. In the strict hierarchy of the Spiritual Realm, the Second Elder being half a step behind signified that Lu Xingtian would be established as the next Sect Master of the Chimeng Sect. And with his talent, the Chimeng Sect's strength would surely rise to a new level. "Greetings, True Transmitter Lu!"

The Elders of the Yushen Sect all paid their respects.

"Mm."

Lu Xingtian nodded indifferently, and the group entered the main hall and sat down.

"Get straight to the point, tell that Xu Yan to come and accept his death. I'm in a hurry, I'll kill him and then have to go back to prepare for my breakthrough."

Lu Xingtian spoke coldly.

"True Transmitter Lu, we will certainly convey this message, so that the cultivators of Yuzhou can also witness the grandeur of the True Transmitter Lu.

"Let those wandering cultivators know that the genius they see is nothing but a stray dog!"

Zhu Liang said respectfully.

"Good, do it quickly!"

Lu Xingtian nodded.

That day, the major sects and aristocratic families of the Spiritual Realm all received the news that the True Transmitter of the Chimeng Sect had arrived in Yuzhou, and would initiate a battle of geniuses, challenging Xu Yan to a duel! Instantly, the major sects and families were shocked - the Yushen Sect had actually invited the True Transmitter of the Chimeng Sect!

Lu Xingtian, with his Divine Radiance Treasure Body, one of the Ten Treasured Bodies, was a renowned genius. Having reached the Great Celestial Realm, he was known as the future number one powerhouse of Luozhou.

He had actually come to Yuzhou to fight Xu Yan.

Although Xu Yan had also killed Cultivators at the Refining Spirit Realm, in the eyes of the sect and family experts, they mostly believed that Xu Yan had used some special means.

And due to their inherent impressions, how could a wandering genius compare to a sect genius?

So in their eyes, the outcome of the battle between Xu Yan and Lu Xingtian was already determined - Xu Yan would definitely die!

They even suspected that Xu Yan might not even show up for the battle.

...

Wen Yong had finally returned to Yuzhou, arriving at the headquarters of the Myriad World Alliance, only to be shocked to find that the headquarters had been taken over.

After wandering through several secret places, he finally contacted Yu Gao.

In the manor, it was as usual.

Shen Haizhou hurriedly came, his expression somewhat surprised.

"Brother Xu, this is a big problem."

Xu Yan raised his eyebrows and asked, "What big problem has happened, has the Yushen Sect discovered the information you provided?"

"No."

Shen Haizhou shook his head, his expression grave. "The Yushen Sect has invited the True Transmitter of the Chimeng Sect of Luozhou to have a life-and-death battle with you!"

"The True Transmitter of the Chimeng Sect?"

Xu Yan was surprised.

Meng Chong and the others also gathered around, curious.

Shen Haizhou took a deep breath and said solemnly, "The Chimeng Sect is a top-tier sect in Luozhou. Its current True Transmitter, Lu Xingtian, possesses one of the Ten Treasured Bodies, the Divine Radiance Treasure Body. His talent is monstrous.

"At the Great Celestial Realm, he has reversed the order and killed Cultivators at the Refining Spirit Realm, his strength surpassing imagination.

"He recently killed three Refining Spirit wandering cultivators in Luozhou, fully awakening the potential of his Treasure Body, and is about to break through to the Refining Spirit Realm.

"The Yushen Sect has invited him to Yuzhou to initiate a battle of geniuses against you, Brother Xu."

Shen Haizhou detailed Lu Xingtian's feats, especially how he, at the Great Celestial Realm, had reversed the order and killed Refining Spirit Celestials - what a terrifying strength!

Xu Yan listened with great interest. "The True Transmitter of the Chimeng Sect, the Divine Radiance Treasure Body? Interesting, where is he planning to challenge me?"

After being in the Spiritual Realm for so long, he had finally encountered a true genius of the realm.

The Divine Radiance Treasure Body, one of the Ten Treasured Bodies.

"How can a Great Celestial reverse the order and defeat Refining Spirit Celestials? The might of the divine soul cannot be resisted by cultivators below the Refining Spirit Realm."

Fang Hao asked curiously.

As is well known, no matter how strong a Great Celestial is, they are absolutely no match for a Refining Spirit Celestial. Even if hundreds of Great Celestials joined forces, they would still find it difficult to contend with a Refining Spirit Celestial, the main reason being the might of the divine soul.

The pressure of the divine soul would suppress the cultivators, and those below the Refining Spirit Realm would find it difficult to withstand this pressure. Their speed, agility, and other abilities would all be suppressed. How could they fight under such circumstances?

"Lu Xingtian has the Divine Radiance Treasure Body, one of the Ten Treasured Bodies. His divine radiance has the ability to resist divine soul pressure. And as the True Transmitter of the Chimeng Sect, once he enters the Celestial Realm, he will have Refining Spirit Celestials to spar with.

"This has enhanced his resistance to divine soul pressure, and these top-tier sects also possess special techniques that can greatly reduce the impact of divine soul pressure.

"Having Refining Spirit Celestials to spar with from a young age, and with his Divine Radiance Treasure Body, he is no longer affected by divine soul pressure.

"The reason why top-tier sects are so powerful is due to their inheritance and foundations."

Shen Haizhou said solemnly.

Xu Yan and the others couldn't help but sigh. Those top-tier sects were indeed extraordinary. The notion that Great Celestials could not reverse the order and defeat Refining Spirit Celestials only applied to ordinary sects and families, and to wandering cultivators.

For the geniuses of top-tier sects, who had undergone rigorous training, cultivated powerful secret techniques, or possessed powerful Treasure Bodies, the might of the divine soul no longer had a significant impact on them.

Whether it was in terms of cultivation methods, cultivation environment, or the weapons they possessed, they were far beyond what wandering cultivators could compare to. This was the fundamental reason why sects and aristocratic families were so powerful and dominated the Spiritual Realm.

If even top-tier sects were so strong, how formidable would the geniuses of the supreme sects be?

"Very good, I was just wishing that Yuzhou had no geniuses, no opponents worthy of a battle. Since he is coming to challenge me, it's a good opportunity for me to experience the strength of a sect's True Transmitter."

Xu Yan said excitedly.

"Brother Xu, be careful, even though you may not necessarily lose to him, it's too dangerous if you show yourself."

Shen Haizhou hurriedly persuaded.

"This so-called battle of geniuses, although the rules state that in a battle between geniuses, the elders and protectors of the respective parties cannot intervene regardless of the outcome.

"But given the nature of the sects and aristocratic families, if their True Transmitter is in danger, they won't just sit back and watch.

"They won't even consider us wandering cultivators as worthy of their attention!"

Being from a prominent family himself, Shen Haizhou was naturally well-versed in the character of the sects and aristocratic families.

Xu Yan sneered, "Rules? If they don't abide by the rules, the one who will suffer will be them. I will definitely accept the challenge."

Shen Haizhou still wanted to persuade further, but Suling Xiu hummed and said, "They have protectors, they have powerful figures backing them up, but we also have a master! My master is invincible!"

At these words, Xu Yan and the others all nodded in agreement.

The master is invincible!

Shen Haizhou was stunned, and glanced at the mysterious young man leisurely reading books in the courtyard, and no longer tried to persuade further.

Li Xuan was delighted, Suling Xiu was right, with their master here, they need not fear the sects and aristocratic families not following the rules. They need not fear them bullying the weak.

"Then I'll go gather more information."

Shen Haizhou took a deep breath and said.

"Lu Xingtian, the Divine Radiance Treasure Body, I hope you won't disappoint me!"

Xu Yan sneered.

After Shen Haizhou left, Xu Yan went to his master.
