Chapter 33: Disciple, I will pass on the Subduing the Dragon Palm to you

Boom!

Li Xuan struck out with a palm, and his qi and blood transformed into a torrential flow that struck a tree dozens of yards away. The massive tree, as thick as a thigh, was instantly shattered to pieces by the palm strike, with fragments flying everywhere.

"The power is not bad, but the consumption of qi and blood is quite large.

"It's true that relying solely on the brute force of qi and blood is not enough. If I encounter an opponent of similar strength, I will definitely be at a disadvantage.

"I've already got an idea for this palm technique, I'll compile the palm technique formula and pass it on to Xu Yan."

Li Xuan let out a breath.

"What about the body technique? How do I pass it on? I don't know how to demonstrate it myself. Relying solely on brute strength won't show the subtlety of the body technique."

Thinking about the body technique problem, Li Xuan became troubled again.

"Am I overthinking this? Why do I need to demonstrate it to my disciple? Just pass on the formula and theoretical framework, and let him comprehend it himself."

Thinking this way, Li Xuan suddenly had an idea.

Outside the village, he found a flat area.

"How was the Bagua diagram drawn again?"

Li Xuan recalled the Bagua diagram he had seen in his previous life, and waved his hand, causing streams of qi and blood to flow out and draw it on the ground.

"I think it's like this."

Glancing at the Bagua diagram he had drawn on the ground, Li Xuan nodded in satisfaction.

When Xu Yan returned, he would let him cultivate the body technique here.

And at the same time, let him ponder the Bagua, who knows, he might gain some insights.

Everything was ready, just waiting for his disciple Xu Yan to return.

Li Xuan felt completely at ease.

...

Xu Yan returned home in less than ten days.

After violently pushing back the bandit's lair, he realized his lack of martial arts skills, and didn't stay at home for long, hurrying back.

"Master!"

Xu Yan respectfully paid his respects, pondering how to bring up the matter of asking his master to teach him martial arts.

"Mm."

Li Xuan nodded, stood up, and headed towards the outskirts of the village.

"Since you've returned, come with me."

Before his disciple could speak, he passed on the martial arts techniques, demonstrating the profound and inscrutable mastery of his teacher.

"Yes, Master!"

Xu Yan was puzzled, not knowing what his master had in store.

They arrived at a small hillside outside the village.

Li Xuan clasped his hands behind his back and said, "Disciple, although you have entered the martial dao, you only have a strong physique, but don't know how to utilize it or how to fight opponents.

"The martial dao is not just about reaching a certain realm, the more powerful the martial artist, the more exquisite and powerful the martial techniques and divine abilities they possess."

Hearing this, Xu Yan was extremely excited: "Master is going to teach me martial dao divine abilities? I'm no longer just a brute who only knows how to use brute force!"

Li Xuan turned around and looked at his foolish disciple, speaking in a deep voice, "Today, I will pass on to you the Subduing the Dragon Palm, which is both a martial technique and a martial dao divine ability. The higher the realm, the deeper the comprehension, the greater the power."

Since it was a martial dao technique, Li Xuan had originally wanted to call it the Eighteen Subduing the Dragon Palms, but then he thought the eighteen palms limit would be too restrictive, and might limit his disciple's room for comprehension.

So he simply called it the Subduing the Dragon Palm. The name didn't matter, the power was the key.

Moreover, he positioned the Subduing the Dragon Palm as something that could continuously improve with the increase in realm, reaching the level of a martial dao divine ability.

Whether he could succeed or not, it all depended on his disciple's comprehension.

"Subduing the Dragon Palm!"

Xu Yan was immediately excited: "Thank you, Master!"

"The dragon is a divine beast, possessing immense power and divine abilities... Disciple, do you know this?" Li Xuan asked.

"Master, I've read in an ancient storybook about the legends of the true dragon, which is the supreme symbol, its movements causing wind and thunder, making heaven and earth change color..."
