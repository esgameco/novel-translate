In the palace of the Zheng Kingdom, the Zheng Emperor led a group of powerful members of the royal family and several high-ranking Zheng officials, kneeling on the ground. In front of them, two figures sat on chairs.

One was a man, with a white beard and wearing a purple robe, exuding an imposing presence without anger.

The other was a beautiful woman, wearing a light green dress, her hair tied in a bun, with an indifferent expression, not uttering a word.

"The gate to the Spirit Realm has opened. Transmit the message to the Inner Realm. Those who hold the Jade Token may enter the Spirit Realm and go to the Spirit Sect and Aristocratic Families that the Jade Token belongs to. The Zheng Kingdom is responsible for escorting the holders of the Jade Token to the forces that the Jade Token belongs to," the man said calmly.

"Yes!"

The Zheng Emperor and the others were stunned. According to the original rules, it was not yet time for the gate to the Spirit Realm to open and allow the Jade Token holders to enter the Spirit Realm. Why was it opened early? Could it be that the gate to the Spirit Realm had already opened?

Thinking of this possibility, the Zheng Emperor broke out in a cold sweat and knelt on the ground. "Your Eminence, the gate to the Spirit Realm has already opened, and the people of the Water Star Palace have entered the Inner Realm."

The man looked at him indifferently and said, "Just act according to the rules, there's no need to report it."

"Yes, Your Eminence!"

The Zheng Emperor heaved a sigh of relief. Acting according to the rules meant that the warriors of the Spirit Sects and Aristocratic Families could be allowed to open the gate to the Spirit Realm and enter the Inner Realm.

The Zheng Emperor and the others bowed and withdrew, beginning to select personnel to be responsible for arranging the opening of the gate to the Spirit Realm and the escort of the Jade Token holders entering the Spirit Realm. This was the first time in nearly ten thousand years that the gate to the Spirit Realm was truly opened, allowing the warriors holding the Jade Tokens in the Inner Realm to enter the Spirit Realm and go to the forces that the Jade Tokens belonged to. There was no room for carelessness.

The task of the Zheng royal family was this. If there were any mistakes, the Zheng Kingdom would be ruined. Of course, as long as they fulfilled their responsibilities, even if someone attacked the Jade Token holders and the Zheng Kingdom was unable to protect them, they would not be punished. The superior Spirit Sect would pursue the attacker.

However, if the Zheng Kingdom did not fulfill its duties and was negligent, that would be a grave offense!

According to past rules, important Jade Token holders would naturally have the forces that the Jade Tokens belonged to escort them, not needing the Zheng Kingdom's escort. But the current situation was different, and it was unknown whether there were Jade Token holders in the Inner Realm.

"Yaron, I didn't expect that Taixuan Mountain would send you here. How is your venerable teacher?" the man asked the beautiful woman.

"The Azure King is thoughtful. My teacher is well," Yaron replied indifferently.

The Azure King, a powerful figure who had risen in the Great Zhou Kingdom in the past hundred years, was already in the late stage of Refining the Spirit. It was a foregone conclusion that he would one day reach the peak of Refining the Spirit.

The Azure King seemed to have gotten used to Yaron's indifferent attitude and continued to ask, "When your teacher entered the Inner Realm and slew the Blood Demon Patriarch, did he completely eliminate the Blood Demon?"

"The Blood Demon is dead. My teacher personally buried him," Yaron said coldly.

"The Blood Demon is cunning, rising three times after falling. Although your teacher's strength is unparalleled, and the Blood Demon was in a weakened state, the relationship between your teacher and the Blood Demon..."

The Azure King hesitated.

"Shut up!" Yaron suddenly said coldly.

The Azure King sighed and said, "You don't need to be angry. You should know that my grandfather, the previous Azure King, died at the hands of the Blood Demon in the Inner Realm. My father was mediocre and could not inherit the title of Azure King. I have painstakingly inherited the title of Azure King. The Blood Demon is a deep grudge for me. Although I trust your teacher, the Blood Demon is so cunning, and I'm most afraid that your teacher will be soft-hearted."

Yaron looked at him coldly and said, "My teacher has long since abandoned love and hatred, slaying the Blood Demon, cutting off worldly attachments, and breaking through the shackles..."

The Azure King was shocked, looking at Yaron, and took a deep breath, saying, "Your teacher has already taken that step?"

Yaron turned her head and no longer paid attention to him.

In the hall of the gate to the Spirit Realm, the two guardians were startled and hurriedly paid their respects, "Greetings, Your Majesty!"

The Zheng Emperor had actually come in person. Could it be that they had already determined that the young man was the Blood Demon, and the Emperor had come to punish him?

"Open the gate to the Spirit Realm, transmit the message to the Inner Realm. Those who hold the Jade Token may enter the Spirit Realm, for a period of one month," the Zheng Emperor ordered solemnly.

"Yes, Your Majesty!"

The two guardians heaved a sigh of relief. At the same time, they were puzzled. Why was the gate to the Spirit Realm opened early?

The gate to the Spirit Realm opened, not just a crack, but completely. At the same time, an elder following the Zheng Emperor stepped into the gate and entered the Inner Realm.

In the Northern Wilderness, the powerful figures of the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion were alerted by the brilliant light. They stood on the mountain peak, looking towards the gate to the Spirit Realm.

"Is this... the complete opening of the gate to the Spirit Realm?" the purple-capped man exclaimed in shock.

After a moment, a massive gate, with rippling light, stood towering between heaven and earth. A surge of dense spiritual energy flowed from the gate into the Inner Realm, causing the spiritual energy density within a hundred-mile radius to instantly double. The heavenly spiritual mechanisms in the area also became more active.

An elder walked out of the gate, frowning slightly, finding the spiritual energy in the Inner Realm too thin, and looked up towards the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion.

With a single step, he arrived at the peak of the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion. The purple-capped man saw him and immediately trembled, kneeling on the ground.

"Greetings, Your Eminence!"

The other powerful figures of the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion hurriedly knelt, not daring to offend the powerful figure behind the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion.

The elder looked down at the purple-capped man indifferently and said, "Transmit the message to the entire Inner Realm. Those who hold the Jade Token may enter the Spirit Realm, for a period of one month."

"Yes, Your Eminence!"

The purple-capped man's heart trembled, hurriedly responding respectfully. He was greatly shocked. Why was the gate to the Spirit Realm suddenly opened, allowing the Jade Token holders to enter? What was the Jade Token?

At this moment, the purple-capped man realized that he seemed to have missed this important information. If he had possessed the Jade Token, would his situation have been different?

After the elder delivered the message, he seemed to find the thin spiritual energy in the Inner Realm quite distasteful and turned to return to the gate to the Spirit Realm.

After the elder entered the gate, the people of the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion dared to stand up and hurriedly transmit the message through the channels of the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion to the entire Inner Realm.

As for what the Jade Token was, those who understood would naturally understand.
