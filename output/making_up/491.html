Martial artists who witnessed this scene assumed it was powerful underworld figures slaughtering their subordinates to blood refine and increase their strength, and didn't pay much attention.

The youth began hunting Immortal Heavenly Venerable, beginning his path of revenge.

The turmoil in the divine realm and the undercurrents could not affect the Qinghua Realm, naturally also not affecting Xu Yan and the others.

"It's time to prepare to break through to the Heaven Earth realm!"

Xu Yan exhaled, his expression excited. The Heaven Earth realm was now in sight.

Half a month ago, he had already reached the peak of the Void Breaking realm.

After consolidating for a bit, he prepared to break through to the Heaven Earth realm in one go before emerging from seclusion.

"Once entering the Heaven Earth realm, even Divine Lords are not to be feared. When I reach great accomplishment in the Heaven Earth realm, slaying Supreme realm martial artists will be commonplace."

Although the Heaven Earth realm was not yet at the very peak of the Cang Heaven and Earth, it was only one step away.

In the entire Cang Heaven and Earth, there were not even a hundred Realm Lord level experts.

"Once entering Heaven Earth, establishing the Dao is not far off."

Xu Yan was extremely excited. Those who establish the Dao transcend heaven and earth. His goal of transcending heaven and earth within a hundred years that he had once set was now within reach.

Just as he was about to activate the formation to shield all disturbances from the outside world and enter deep closed-door cultivation,

Roar! Suddenly, a howl rang out, followed immediately by a series of miserable screams.

Xu Yan frowned and came out of the closed-door cultivation room. He saw Ao Yuxue holding a stick in her hand, beating a blue flood dragon!

"Master, save me!" Yu Xiaolong hurriedly cried for help.

"What's going on?" Xu Yan asked with furrowed brows.

Ao Yuxue smashed Yu Xiaolong's head into the ground with another blow of her stick, angrily saying: "This useless flood dragon actually wants to transform into a true dragon. Doesn't he know what he looks like!"

Stone Two walked over and explained: "This guy Yu Xiaolong came back from the Nine Mountains Realm wanting to find you, but you were in closed-door cultivation and he couldn't find you. Unable to resist the thought of transforming into a true dragon, he went to provoke her."

Xu Yan suddenly understood. He looked at Yu Xiaolong and curiously asked: "You can transform into a true dragon? How do you transform?"

Yu Xiaolong excitedly nodded and said: "Of course I can. I'm a flood dragon, and flood dragons can naturally transform into true dragons. I just need true dragon blood and I should be able to transform."

"Is that so!" Xu Yan pondered.

Hearing this, Ao Yuxue became angry. She swung her stick and knocked Yu Xiaolong to the ground again. Then she reached out a hand to tug at Xu Yan's clothes, timidly saying: "He's a garbage flood dragon. Even if he transforms into a true dragon, he'll just be a garbage true dragon. He can't even take human form. If you bring along a garbage true dragon, it will be embarrassing.

You have me, this true dragon, and that's enough. I'm the most excellent among true dragons!"

Yu Xiaolong was beaten dizzy, but he hurriedly spoke up: "Master, if I transform into a true dragon, I'll definitely be more majestic and awe-inspiring. With me as your mount, you'll look more domineering when you go out. Whether I can take human form or not isn't important. The main thing is to look impressive!"

"Shut up!"

Ao Yuxue's dragon might pressed down. Yu Xiaolong immediately went limp on the ground, his whole dragon body softening.

Xu Yan pondered for a moment. It was just true dragon blood after all. If Yu Xiaolong really could transform into a true dragon, it would be a good thing.

Moreover, he cultivated the Great Demon method. After transforming into a true dragon, his strength would definitely increase further.

"It's just a few drops of blood. Just give it to Little Dragon."

So Xu Yan spoke up.

"I won't!"

Ao Yuxue became stubborn. Seeming to fear that Xu Yan would force her to give blood, she quickly added: "At most I'll make a deal with Sister Su!"

Xu Yan was a bit speechless. Ao Yuxue truly had some arrogance in her bones. She would rather make a deal with Su Lingxiu than give Yu Xiaolong a drop of true dragon blood.

From this, one could see how unworthy the flood dragon Yu Xiaolong was in her eyes.

Xu Yan didn't want to investigate the reasons for this. But since Ao Yuxue was so adamant, he couldn't possibly really hold her down and force her to give blood, could he? Moreover, if he really did that, this girl Ao Yuxue would definitely seek help from his junior sister Su Lingxiu.

"Alright, figure out how to resolve this yourself."

Xu Yan shook his head.

"Master, no! It's just one drop of blood!"

Yu Xiaolong wailed.

"Shut up!"

Ao Yuxue's dragon might pressed down again, suppressing Yu Xiaolong firmly.

Seeing this, Xu Yan couldn't help but click his tongue. Even if a flood dragon was several levels stronger, it would still be suppressed by a true dragon when they met. This showed how strong the bloodline suppression of true dragons was over flood dragons.

This was also why Yu Xiaolong was so obsessed with transforming into a true dragon. Even a garbage true dragon was much stronger than a flood dragon. At least it wouldn't be restrained like this.

With his cultivation of the Great Demon method, once he transformed into a true dragon, he would definitely be able to suppress other true dragons of the same level.

"Alright, figure out how to make a deal with her yourself. I'm going into closed-door cultivation. Don't disturb me."

Xu Yan shook his head, but he had given Yu Xiaolong a hint that he could make a deal with Ao Yuxue to obtain true dragon blood.

Hearing this, Ao Yuxue immediately breathed a sigh of relief. Her eyes gleamed with delight as she watched Xu Yan return to his closed-door cultivation room. Seeing Yu Xiaolong's despairing gaze, she immediately became smug.

Further away, Su Lingxiu had a frustrated look. She had been prepared to come out and save Ao Yuxue, using the opportunity to make a deal with her. But Senior Brother unexpectedly didn't give her the chance.

Xu Yan returned to his closed-door cultivation room. Hearing Yu Xiaolong's miserable howls outside, he shook his head. Forcibly making Ao Yuxue give blood was impossible now.

If he really did that, Su Lingxiu would come running over to make a deal with Ao Yuxue, and Yu Xiaolong's wish would still go unfulfilled in the end. Since that was the case, it was better to do nothing at all.

Activating the formation, Xu Yan began his closed-door cultivation to break through to the Heaven Earth realm.

"Once entering the Heaven Earth realm, one will know the mysteries of heaven and earth."

Xu Yan thought excitedly.
