Although that True King Celestial Venerable was not at peak strength, he was still a True King Celestial Venerable after all, far surpassing the previous Condensed Law Celestial Venerable in power.

In terms of strength, he was even more powerful than Mei Wu from before.

This showed that whoever struck was extremely formidable.

Looking towards the azure sea where blood light shot into the sky and figures emerged one after another, Li Xuan thought of the small booklet left behind by Blood Spirit Child.

"From today onwards, the Spirit Realm is the hunting ground of me, Blood Disciple 726 of the Netherworld Prison. From today onwards, the Spirit Realm is the Blood Realm. Those with decent talents and strength, if you want to live, become blood slaves.

"Those who wish to live, kneel and submit quickly!"

A cold and stern voice echoed out.

A powerful aura rose up from the azure sea, a blood light illuminating the horizon. Even from a great distance, one could feel the bloodthirsty qi.

Wu Tiannan and the others felt heavy-hearted. The speaker was too strong!

Even from afar, that bloodthirsty qi gave them a sense of terror, like an invisible mountain pressing down on their hearts!

This person had severed the divine bridge and slain a Celestial Venerable. Their strength far surpassed Mei Wu from the Ice State Heavenly Cave.

Moreover, those blood shadows must be the blood slaves they spoke of?

Netherworld Prison Blood Disciples? Could the Heavenly Cave of the Azure Sea Abyss be called the Netherworld Prison Heavenly Cave? Where exactly did these Netherworld Prison Blood Disciples come from?

The divine bridge had collapsed, would powerful experts come from the Divine Realm to rescue them?

"The Qinghua Realm is currently preoccupied and no one can save you. The divine bridge is broken, no one can come. Your fates are all in the hands of me, Blood Disciple 726.

"The useless will become blood food. Those with talent and decent strength can become my blood slaves and sacrifice the Spirit Realm with blood, only then will you have a chance to live.

"Your only path to survival is to submit to my Netherworld Prison!"

Blood Disciple 726's voice rolled out, piercing into people's hearts, as if plucking at the strings of fear.

As 726's voice rang out, rolling blood light seemed to dye the azure sea crimson. The bloodthirsty qi dyed half the sky red, even the spiritual energy of heaven and earth took on a faint red tinge.

"726? Is it the 726th Blood Disciple, or ranked 726th in strength?" Li Xuan pondered to himself.

726 was clearly this Netherworld Prison Blood Disciple's title, and could be considered their name. Whether it was ranked by strength or order was unknown.

However, Li Xuan was inclined to believe it was ranked by strength.

"What level of strength do the top ten Blood Disciples have, and what's above Blood Disciples?" Li Xuan sighed inwardly, this Netherworld Prison must have extremely powerful existences.

"The divine bridge is broken, does that mean we can't go to the Divine Realm?" Xie Tianheng said with some reluctance.

As for the blood light surging over from the azure sea, he wasn't worried at all. He was only upset about not being able to go to the Divine Realm.

"It's broken, so just reconnect it." Xu Yan said nonchalantly.

"Reconnect it?" Xie Tianheng's eyes lit up. "You can reconnect the divine bridge?"

Xu Yan shook his head and looked at his master, saying, "Master can reconnect it."

Xie Tianheng slapped his forehead, he had really been confused. Xu Yan was going to the Divine Realm, how could the senior not take action for the sake of his disciple going there?

"That Blood Disciple 726 is very arrogant and bloodthirsty. Where did these enemies come from? From the looks of it, they want to sacrifice the entire Spirit Realm with blood."

Xie Lingfeng said with furrowed brows.

Apart from Blood Disciple 726, those blood slaves were also extremely strong. Although not as powerful as that Condensed Law Celestial Venerable, they must be at the level of Condensed Law Celestial Venerables.

In the entire Spirit Realm, apart from the few in Changqing Pavilion, no one could match them.

If Blood Disciple 726 really succeeded, the Spirit Realm would surely become a land of blood, truly as they said, from now on the Spirit Realm would be the Blood Realm! A realm for nourishing essence blood!

The Ice State Heavenly Cave had brought great calamity to Cloud State and Great Marsh State, but the disaster brought by the Netherworld Prison Blood Disciples from this Azure Sea Abyss Heavenly Cave was even more terrifying.

However, because powerful experts were present, the Netherworld Prison Blood Disciples were destined to fail.

"Such dense blood energy, these blood slaves are like blood storage tanks. If refined, they would become pills that greatly nourish blood and flesh!" Su Lingxiu exclaimed.

Boom! The bloodthirsty qi rolled over, already reaching the shores of Rainbow State. The strong cultivators of the Spirit Realm felt a sense of suffocation, inexplicable fear arising in their hearts.

The blood light seemed to symbolize great calamity, sweeping towards Rainbow State, wanting to engulf the entire Spirit Realm.

Above the azure sea, blood-red figures wielding blood-red long sabers came killing.

Wu Tiannan's expression was solemn, Xin Mengrou's brows were also furrowed. Including the Thousand Martial Palace Master and other powerful experts, they all recalled the Blood Demon Calamity from before.

The Blood God Cult disciples had also surged with bloodthirsty qi like this.

However, the Blood God Cult disciples were far inferior to these blood slaves, lacking such intense bloodthirsty qi, and their entire bodies were not dyed blood-red as if soaked in fresh blood.

Wu Tiannan thought of the small booklet left by Blood Spirit Child. He was almost certain that Blood Spirit Child's strength back then would not have been weaker than these blood slaves.

It was only because he was unwilling to be controlled by others that he weakened his strength to erase his remnant soul. Even so, he still caused the Blood Demon Calamity in the Spirit Realm, sacrificing sects, clans and cities with blood.

One blood demon had caused such a huge disaster for the Spirit Realm. These blood slaves before them were not something the Spirit Realm could resist at all. Without powerful experts intervening, the Spirit Realm would surely perish, and cultivators would be sacrificed with blood!

"Blood food, wonderful blood food. Those with ambition, join my Netherworld Prison and enjoy the wonder of blood sacrifice together!"

Blood Disciple 726's voice seemed to carry a bewitching tone.

"I'll go meet him!" Xu Yan said with high fighting spirit atop Changqing Pavilion.

After breaking through to the Miraculous Realm, he could finally display the power of miracles.

Although Blood Disciple 726 was very strong, Xu Yan was not afraid at all. He wanted to truly challenge enemies stronger than himself.

Taking a step forward, Xu Yan instantly vanished from the flying ship.

"I'll go meet these blood slaves." Meng Chong rubbed his bald head with a smile.

"I'll go too!" Fang Hao nodded.

It was finally time to show their might.

"I'll go play around too." Su Lingxiu muttered.

Li Xuan remained sitting calmly in his chair, but his gaze was fixed on Xu Yan, paying attention to his battle with Blood Disciple 726.

Blood Disciple 726 could even slay True Kings, their strength far exceeded what Xu Yan could handle at his current stage.

However, Xu Yan's Yin-Yang Undying Sword Miracle was extremely powerful. As long as Blood Disciple 726 couldn't break through this miracle in one strike, they wouldn't be able to cause substantial harm to Xu Yan.

This was also Xu Yan's confidence in challenging stronger opponents.

Even if the opponent was twice as strong as him, Xu Yan had the confidence to remain undefeated, relying on the Yin-Yang Undying Sword Miracle!

"This is good too. Xu Yan's opponents have always been weaker than him. Now truly battling enemies stronger than himself will help him comprehend the martial dao and miracles, helping to increase his strength."

"An ant dares to block the way!"

In Hong Province, all the powerful cultivators of the Spirit Realm were shocked.

The expert hadn't made a move, but Xu Yan had? That person had shattered the God Bridge and killed Celestial Venerables, yet Xu Yan dared to directly challenge him?

Xu Yan's expression remained unchanged as he slashed down with his sword! Technique: Absolute Heaven-Severing Sword! In an instant, a black sword light seemed to descend from the sky, as if to extinguish heaven and earth, to extinguish everything! As this sword strike came out, the 726 Blood Disciple's expression tightened.

What kind of sword technique was this? It was so terrifying that if the opponent's strength was one level higher, he absolutely wouldn't be able to withstand this strike!

"You're too weak!"

"So strong! What kind of sword technique is this?"

"I don't know, but it's unheard of. As expected of the expert's personal disciple!"

All the powerful cultivators of the Spirit Realm were shocked.

However, the 726 Blood Disciple's slash was even stronger. The bloodthirsty blade light split open the black sword light and slashed directly towards Xu Yan!

"Not good, Xu Yan is in danger!"
