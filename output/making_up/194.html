Li Xuan returned from his boat trip, sitting in a chair and leisurely sipping spiritual tea and eating spiritual cakes.

The red cat was sprawled out in the spiritual fruit grove on the island, snoring loudly. In recent days, the red cat had been sleeping more and more, as if it was entering a stage of transformation.

Xu Yan was still sitting cross-legged on the mountain peak, continuing to comprehend the essence of the sword.

Xie Lingfeng was still consolidating the Heaven and Earth Bridge, constantly expanding and strengthening it, accumulating his own foundation, and also contemplating the Dao of the Sword, wishing to enter the realm of the Sword Heart Illumination.

Hu Shan and several Taoist master scholars had already initially refined the Heaven and Earth Bridge and were in the process of consolidating it.

Su Lingxiu continued to delve into the Medicinal and Martial Arts Classics, summarizing her insights, and according to her, she wanted to compile a new Medicinal and Martial Arts Classic.

Zhou Ying was in the final stage of breaking through to the Transcendent Realm.

Just at this moment, it was as if a storm was approaching, crossing the Cang River and sweeping towards Canglan Island.

The majestic might was not at all concealed.

More astonishing and shocking than the previous assault of over a hundred peak sect masters.

Li Xuan looked up, his gaze calm, his expression unchanged.

He had already reached the pinnacle of the Penetrating the Mystery Realm.

His strength was now far beyond what it was before. With a snap of his fingers, he could annihilate a Half-Step Celestial Being.

A mere four Half-Step Celestial Beings were nothing more than a flick of his fingers.

"They've arrived just in time."

Li Xuan revealed a smile.

Xu Yan was still comprehending the essence of the sword, and now that the enemy had arrived, including Half-Step Celestial Beings, it was the perfect opportunity for Xu Yan to temper his sword intent and truly grasp its essence.

The people on Canglan Island all looked up in shock.

Dozens of powerful experts were crossing the Cang River, unabashedly attacking Canglan Island.

"Who are they?"

Everyone was stunned. The last time, over fifty peak sect masters were all killed, yet someone still dared to come and seek their own demise?

Everyone's gaze fell on the four figures in the lead.

Even the sleeping red cat was awakened, raising its head and letting out a low growl towards the approaching group. If its strength allowed, it would have already charged over and killed them.

How dare they disturb its sleep!

"Master, who are these people?"

Su Lingxiu came out of the house, quite surprised.

Could it be that they came for the medicinal pills of the Evergreen Pavilion? Was the deterrence from the previous battle not enough? The majestic might was like a raging storm, and the dozens of peak sect masters all had their faces concealed and identities hidden.

"Hand over Su Lingxiu, and this battle can be avoided!"

A voice came from the group.

Su Lingxiu was taken aback. Was this for her sake?

The Concealed Pavilion's master was already dead, so who else would want to capture her? Just then, a sword beam flashed across, striking the one who had spoken.

"Die!"

Boom!

The group of peak sect masters' expressions changed, and they hurriedly moved to block the attack.

Xu Yan stepped forward, standing proudly, facing the group of powerful experts without the slightest fear.

"Either leave or die!"

Xu Yan spoke coldly.

He looked at the one who had just spoken, wearing a red robe and a blue mask, and there were nine others dressed the same way.

In front of these ten red-robed blue-masked figures, there was also a white-robed blue-masked person.

They came from the same faction.

He thought of the black-robed Concealed Pavilion, whose purpose was also to capture his shijie.

"Half-Step Celestial Beings?"

Xu Yan's gaze slightly narrowed as he looked at the four white-robed blue-masked figures. Four Half-Step Celestial Beings.

The white-robed blue-masked man's gaze was cold as he looked at the proud young man, calmly saying, "Xu Yan, your talent is extraordinary. There's no need for you to perish here. Hand over Su Lingxiu, and we will leave."

"Hmph, I, Xu Yan, have never been threatened by anyone. Mere Half-Step Celestial Beings are still being presumptuous. It's just the right opportunity to temper my Dao of the Sword with you!"

Xu Yan was not the least bit afraid, coldly chuckling.

"Since that's the case, then don't blame us."

The white-robed blue-masked man's gaze turned icy.

Boom! A frigid aura swept out, the blade light like icy frost, directly attacking Xu Yan.

The Cold Master had made his move! Two sword beams flashed out in front of Xu Yan, forming a life-and-death sword wheel that kept attacking, with a faint wind-like aura permeating it, merging into the sword light.

With the Cold Master's attack, the white-robed blue-masked man and the other three led the group of peak sect masters to descend upon Canglan Island.

"One is not enough!"

Xu Yan snorted coldly, raising a hand and unleashing a golden dragon's roar. The clouds and winds surged, the spiritual energy of heaven and earth constantly pouring into the golden dragon, making it seem almost sentient.

Boom! The golden dragon roared, charging towards the white-robed blue-masked man.

One of the other Half-Step Celestial Beings stepped out, meeting the golden dragon, joining the Cold Master in surrounding and attacking Xu Yan.

"He must be killed. If he escapes, it will be a great trouble in the future!"

The white-robed blue-masked man said solemnly.

Xu Yan was too monstrous. It had not been long since he slew the peak sect masters, yet he was able to hold his own against two Half-Step Celestial Beings.

However, the priority now was to capture Su Lingxiu. Once she was in their hands, they could then surround and attack Xu Yan, even if that meant letting him escape for now.

In addition to the two Half-Step Celestial Beings attacking, there were also ten peak sect masters joining in to surround Xu Yan.

Xu Yan was not the least bit afraid. He had not even exerted his full strength, not using the Mountain and River Sword Dao or the Mountain and River Sword Intent, but instead was continuously displaying the fledgling new sword intent in the midst of the battle.

A faint wind-like aura, merging into the sword light, as if permeating and extinguishing all spiritual consciousness.

Xu Yan fought fiercely with the two Half-Step Celestial Beings and the ten peak sect masters. Thunderous sounds echoed in the sky, even pressing down the waters of the Cang River.

On Canglan Island, Su Lingxiu bit her lip, looking at her master, her eyes slightly reddening.

"Master, it's because of me that I've brought you and my shixiong trouble."

Not only did the Concealed Pavilion want to capture her, but this unknown faction also wanted to seize her. What was the reason?

"Just focus on your cultivation. As long as your strength is powerful enough, this will not be a problem."

Li Xuan said calmly, without the slightest ripple.

It was likely related to Su Lingxiu's origins.

"Blue-Masked Ones!"

Xie Lingfeng spoke in a deep voice.

"What are the Blue-Masked Ones?"

Su Lingxiu asked curiously.

"One of the three most mysterious dark factions in the Inner Realm. All of them wear blue masks, and each Blue-Masked One is at least at the peak sect master level."

Xie Lingfeng said gravely.

And the white-robed blue-masked man was clearly extremely powerful, far beyond just a peak sect master.

"Little girl, come with me. Don't worry, I won't kill you. But if you don't comply, everyone here will die!"

The white-robed blue-masked man's gaze calmly looked at Su Lingxiu.

Then his gaze fell on Li Xuan, speaking indifferently, "No matter who you are, one person cannot monopolize everything. If you hand over the girl, we can discuss this."

Li Xuan looked up, the jade talisman in his hand turning, and he chuckled lightly, "I don't care about your goals, but before you came, you should have first measured your own abilities.

"Whether Half-Step Celestial Beings or Martial Dao Celestial Beings, to me, they are no different than ants.

"Very well, I shall not use my realm to intimidate you. Today, I shall let you frog-in-the-well fellows witness the might of one who has Penetrated the Mystery."
