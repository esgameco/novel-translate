Xie Tianheng was contemplating the Dao of the sword, but the enlightenment of the sword heart was still somewhat mysterious to him. He had learned about it from Xie Lingfeng, but even Xie Lingfeng himself did not fully comprehend the key points of the enlightenment of the sword heart, so he could not explain it clearly.

Therefore, although Xie Tianheng knew about the enlightenment of the sword heart, and Xie Lingfeng had told him everything he knew, it was still not easy for Xie Tianheng to comprehend it. He could not find the clue!

He couldn't help but feel a little troubled.

"Should I go ask that senior for advice?"

Xie Tianheng thought to himself.

That senior was highly respected, wise and broad-minded. If Xie Tianheng went to ask him for advice, he would surely be willing to point him in the right direction.

However, when Xie Tianheng thought back to how he had once stood up to that strong cultivator with an arrogant and overbearing attitude, he blushed and felt too embarrassed to face him.

Xie Tianheng felt that his skin was still too thin, so he could only sigh inwardly and continue to ponder and comprehend on his own.

Suddenly, a voice sounded beside him.

"To attain the enlightenment of the sword heart, you must first purify your mind, not be disturbed by external things, and have only the sword in your thoughts... Your talent in the Dao of the sword is not bad, and you have mastered many sword techniques, and can even comprehend the sword intent. You already have some experience.

"With the state of mind you have when comprehending the sword intent, immerse yourself in it and understand the meaning within, and the enlightenment of the sword heart will not be far away."

Xie Tianheng's heart trembled, someone had approached him without him even noticing! Immediately, he was greatly excited, for this voice was none other than that senior's!

He quickly looked up and sure enough, saw the senior standing in the distance, gazing at the rolling sea of clouds.

"Thank you, senior, for guiding me out of the confusion!"

Xie Tianheng hurriedly paid his respects.

"Mm."

Li Xuan nodded and continued to instruct Xie Tianheng.

Xie Tianheng was excited, as he had vaguely gained some understanding. He was confident that if he closed himself off for some time, he would surely be able to attain the enlightenment of the sword heart!

"Senior, have you come here with any instructions for me?"

Xie Tianheng asked respectfully after his excitement had subsided.

"Just wandering around, no particular instructions."

Li Xuan shook his head.

"If the senior has any instructions, Xie Tianheng will go through fire and water to complete them!"

Xie Tianheng said solemnly.

"Good intention."

Li Xuan nodded.

After instructing Xie Tianheng, Li Xuan did not linger too long on the Sword Saint Cliff, and left the mountain. His next destination was to go to the Seven Star Academy, now called the Great Wilderness Martial Arts Academy. Along the way, he leisurely enjoyed the scenery and observed the local customs and people.

Occasionally, he passed by some small villages and unexpectedly found a few young cultivators practicing at the skin-refining stage.

Feeling interested, he gave them some guidance.

That day, before he arrived at the Great Wilderness Martial Arts Academy, the Golden Book of the Dao on his Spiritual Platform suddenly opened, and a golden light surged out.

"Your disciple Xu Yan has comprehended the cultivation method of the Heart Sword Realm, and you have advanced to the Heart Sword Realm!"

In an instant, Li Xuan felt that his Dao of the Sword had undergone earth-shattering changes.

The Heart Sword Realm! All things can become the sword!

With a single step, the weeds floated and transformed into fierce, slashing swords, like a torrential flood of swords sweeping out, instantly piercing through a small hillside in front of him.

"The Heart Sword Realm!"

Li Xuan was greatly excited.

Xu Yan had finally comprehended the cultivation path of the second realm of the Dao of the Sword, the Heart Sword Realm.

Li Xuan believed that once Xu Yan broke through to the Divine Will Realm, his realm in the Dao of the Sword would also surpass the Heart Sword Realm.

"This is the true power of the Dao of the Sword!"

Li Xuan was very satisfied with the Dao of the Sword he had created.

"It's time to find an opportunity to pass on the third realm to Xu Yan."

Li Xuan had made up his mind.

"Your disciple Xu Yan has perfectly attained the enlightenment of the sword heart, and your Heart Sword Realm has been enhanced."

Xu Yan had perfectly attained the enlightenment of the sword heart.

The Dao of the Sword had advanced further.

"His strength is growing stronger every day!"

Li Xuan sighed with amazement, Xu Yan's strength was constantly increasing and growing stronger.

Continuing on to the Great Wilderness Martial Arts Academy, just as he arrived in the Seven Star City, the Golden Book of the Dao opened again.

"Your disciple Meng Chong's Dao of the Blade has reached mastery, and your Blade Soul has advanced to the Hidden Blade Realm."

Meng Chong's Dao of the Blade had also finally broken through.

"Your disciple Meng Chong has perfected the Tyrannical Blade Intent, and your Blade Intent has advanced to the second level."

Meng Chong's Blade Intent had also broken through.

"Meng Chong should be about to comprehend the first level of the Unbreakable Golden Body soon, right?"

Li Xuan was filled with anticipation.

After entering the Great Wilderness Martial Arts Academy, he remained very low-key, not attracting any attention, not even White Cloud and the others knew of his presence.

"So this is the former Seven Star Academy, dedicated to the transmission of the Martial Dao."

Li Xuan nodded as he watched the instructors teaching the students the Great Wilderness Martial Arts.

After teaching the students, these instructors would also immerse themselves in the comprehension and cultivation of the Great Wilderness Martial Arts.

However, the Grand Masters had turned to cultivate the Profound Dao, but had not yet broken through, and the strength of the Profound Realm was superior to the Grand Master Realm.

This made it impossible for the Grand Masters to switch to the Profound Dao, they could only switch to the Primal Dao first, and then break through to the Profound Realm.

As for the peak Grand Masters, they did possess the conditions to switch, but the cultivation method of the Profound Dao was not yet perfected.

Neither Xu Yan, nor Su Lingxiu, had much attention focused on this aspect.

Moreover, the Profound Dao had not yet been transmitted.

Li Xuan stayed at the Great Wilderness Martial Arts Academy, either guiding the instructors in their cultivation, or guiding the students.

He finally felt the sense of being a Martial Ancestor, transmitting the Dao and teaching disciples.

Half a month later.

"Your disciple Xu Yan has broken through to the Profound Realm Perfection, and your Divine Will Realm strength has been enhanced."

Xu Yan had broken through to the Profound Realm Perfection.

"Your disciple Meng Chong has broken through to the Profound Realm Mastery, and your Great Day Golden Body has doubled in strength."

Just a few days after Xu Yan, Meng Chong had also broken through.

"Your disciple Su Lingxiu has broken through to the Profound Realm Minor Success, and your Pill Dao and Martial Dao in the Profound Realm are now perfected, gaining further enhancement."

Su Lingxiu had also broken through to the Profound Realm Minor Success.

Li Xuan continued to guide the instructors and students in the Great Wilderness Martial Arts Academy, and anyone who received his guidance saw their strength rapidly soar.

He also noticed Du Yuying and her maid Cui'er, occasionally hearing the cute maid muttering about Young Master Xu...

"This girl has good talent and bloodline, and also possesses a special physique."

Li Xuan silently nodded, Du Yuying had already reached the Grand Master Realm.

After entering the Celestial Tomb and making up for the deficiencies in her body, her talent had further improved, and her strength had broken through rapidly.

At the Great Wilderness Martial Arts Academy, Li Xuan was a little regretful that he had not found a suitable disciple.

"It's time to go back."

He thought to himself.

Xu Yan had already reached Profound Realm Perfection, and if he did not accumulate a foundation, his body would undergo another transformation when he broke through to the Divine Will Realm. He was about ready to break through to the Divine Will Realm.

However, Xu Yan would certainly not choose to break through at this time.

He would continue to accumulate his own foundation, so that when he broke through to the Divine Will Realm, he could undergo another transformation and advancement.

"Now that Xu Yan has reached the Spirit Body, if he undergoes another transformation and advancement, will he surpass the Spirit Body?"

Li Xuan looked forward to it.

The higher the realm, the more time it takes to accumulate one's own foundation. To accumulate the foundation faster, the consumption of spirit medicines is indispensable.

Su Lingxiu should already be refining pills.

However, the spirit medicines in the Inner Realm are ultimately of a lower grade.

Li Xuan left the Great Wilderness Martial Arts Academy.
