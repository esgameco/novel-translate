He put away the storage bag, and just as the Jade Crystal Snake was about to breathe a sigh of relief, Xu Yan grabbed its neck again.

"The Qingtianjiao has been captured by me, and I am the victor. These things rightfully belong to me. I won't pursue the crime of you stealing them, but in the tomb, you attacked me, and that crime cannot be forgiven."

"The treasures are not on me... Ugh!" Its neck was grabbed again, almost crushed to death.

"Cough, cough!" The Jade Crystal Snake suddenly started coughing violently, sticking out its tongue.

"It's the Earth Dragon Qi! The legendary Earth Dragon Qi, said to be born from the great river's spiritual vein, has already surpassed the first-grade spiritual item level and is rumored to be a divine-grade spiritual item, or even a divine treasure!"

"Since this treasure is so precious, why are you telling me about it?" Xu Yan grabbed the Jade Crystal Snake's neck again, coldly saying, "Could this be a trap?"

The Jade Crystal Snake's entire body went numb. Could he please not grab its neck? "Listen to me, listen to me!"

Xu Yan released his grip again. "Speak."

The Jade Crystal Snake stuck out its tongue, panting. "Can you not grab my neck?"

"So you're trying to use me to help you get the Earth Dragon Qi. You're dreaming!"

Xu Yan sneered. This snake had attacked him before, and he would not let that grudge go.
