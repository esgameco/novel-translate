At this moment, Fang Hao revealed the wonders of his array techniques, which was naturally done with purpose.

The further one progresses in cultivation, the more resources are needed, especially for the unique martial arts he practiced. Whether upgrading the Qimen weapon box, refining artifacts, or cultivating arrays, all required consuming vast resources.

In the early stages, he relied entirely on his senior and second senior brothers to provide resources. Now that he had his own strength, it was time for him to earn resources for cultivation himself.

The Divine Realm lacked arrays for guarding the Heavenly Pit and defending against external enemies, where the marvels of arrays would be fully displayed.

This way, he could obtain an endless supply of cultivation resources to continuously research artifact refining and arrays, without worrying about resource consumption.

With such vast resources, and constant research into various artifact refining and array techniques, the speed of power increase and array improvement would enter a rapid growth phase.

The cultivation of Qimen martial arts, involving artifact refining, arrays and other aspects, consumed far more resources than pure martial arts or physical martial arts.

Now, this was an opportunity.

Li Xuan nodded silently. After the experiences with the Eternal Alliance, Fang Hao had also become more sophisticated, knowing how to seize opportunities and pave the way for future cultivation.

Even if array techniques were spread, it would not necessarily be a bad thing.

He would ultimately be the one to benefit.

Xu Yan and Meng Chong both had their own paths and knew how to pave the way for their own cultivation. They would both leave soon to venture into the Qinghua Realm and Divine Realm.

Whether pure martial arts or physical martial arts, one could better comprehend the true essence of martial arts through tempering, battle, and traversing the world.

Just as Xu Yan had rapidly advanced his martial arts by traveling throughout the Spirit Realm.

In the Divine Realm, he would surely take this path as well, which was also the correct path.

Fang Hao's cultivation relied entirely on resource provision, allowing him to freely display the mysteries of Qimen martial arts and research Qimen techniques, arrays, artifact refining, restrictions and so on.

In the Spirit Realm, the Eternal Alliance provided vast resources, which was why Fang Hao could advance so rapidly.

Su Lingxiu was the same, though she focused on alchemy and healing.

"Being an Imperial Advisor is not impossible, but I have a few conditions..." Fang Hao said with a smile.

"Please speak, young friend!" The Great Yue Emperor said joyfully in his heart.

"The conditions are quite simple. The way of arrays ultimately requires consuming quite a few materials..."

Fang Hao listed out his conditions one by one.

Finally he added: "I alone naturally cannot travel to all corners to set up arrays for various major cities. However, I can teach those with talent in arrays how to set them up, and even how to refine array instruments."

Qimen martial arts covered a broad scope, with arrays and artifact refining being just one aspect. Fang Hao also had the intention of spreading array and artifact refining knowledge.

Perhaps when his strength was great enough, establishing a sect would not be out of the question.

Of course, that was still too far off.

Upon hearing this, the Great Yue Emperor became extremely excited and nodded: "Good, everything will be according to Brother Fang's wishes. I will establish an Array Academy in Great Yue, with Brother Fang in full charge.

"No one in all of Great Yue may disobey Brother Fang's will. The resources of Great Yue will be at Brother Fang's disposal for setting up arrays!"

Fang Hao revealed a smile and said: "Good, it's a deal!"

Su Lingxiu took out several bottles and said with a beaming smile: "Great Yue Emperor, I have some medicinal pills here. Would you like to see their effects?"

The Great Yue Emperor's heart stirred. The disciple of a great expert was naturally extraordinary.

"Alright!"

He took the medicinal pills and looked. With his strength, he could naturally see their miraculous effects. What shocked him even more was that these pills seemed to use only ordinary spirit herbs, not divine herbs.

If divine herbs were used in refining, how much more miraculous would the effects be?

"What are Miss Su's conditions?" The Great Yue Emperor naturally realized the value of these medicinal pills. With enough of them, cultivators' speed of cultivation, breakthrough of bottlenecks, recovery from injuries, and replenishment during battle would all be greatly enhanced.

"Similar conditions to my junior brother!" Su Lingxiu said with a smile.

"Good!" The Great Yue Emperor agreed immediately.

Cheng Zhan was anxiously sweating, but could not interject. After all, he was only a True King Celestial Lord, while the Great Yue Emperor was one of the three supreme experts of the Qinghua Realm.

Fang Hao and Su Lingxiu had just formed a partnership with Great Yue, but what about the Myriad Thunder Sect?

The Myriad Thunder Sect needed them too.

He could only secretly decide to find Fang Hao and Su Lingxiu privately to discuss terms after the Great Yue Emperor left.

Fang Hao and Su Lingxiu had not actually considered the Myriad Thunder Sect. The reason was that the Myriad Thunder Sect was a martial arts sect, while the Great Yue Nation was not a sect. There were ultimately differences between the two.

Great Yue could be more free and better able to utilize their talents.

Moreover, if the Myriad Thunder Sect or Heavenly Martial Gate had any needs, the two of them could provide assistance as long as the price was right.

They were not bound exclusively to the Great Yue Nation.

Furthermore, how long they would stay in the Qinghua Realm was still unknown. They would eventually leave one day, and with the Divine Realm being so vast, they both wanted to travel and see more of it.

The Great Yue Emperor looked at Xu Yan and Meng Chong with great anticipation.

"Don't look at me. My martial path is different. I, Xu Yan, intend to travel the lands of the Divine Realm, to meet the geniuses and experts of this Divine Realm." Xu Yan said calmly.

"Me too!" Meng Chong said, rubbing his bald head.

The Great Yue Emperor was somewhat disappointed.

Next, without Xu Yan and the others needing to act, the Great Yue Emperor ordered the True Kings to plunder the resources of the Heavenly Pit, including things like Blood Cloud Gold, and deliver it all to the flying ship.

These were the spoils of war!

After exploring the Heavenly Pit, the flying ship departed. The Great Yue Emperor arranged for people to guard the Heavenly Pit and prevent invasion by enemies from the Netherworld.

Then they returned to the capital of Great Yue to deal with the turmoil in Great Yue.

The experts guarding Great Yue's Heavenly Pit finally had use for their abilities. They were dispatched to clear out rebels, blood disciples and blood slaves within Great Yue's borders, and suppress unrest at other Heavenly Pits.

Some experts were even sent to support the Myriad Thunder Sect and Heavenly Martial Gate.

Li Xuan and the others were arranged to stay in a separate courtyard of the Great Yue Imperial Palace. The Great Yue Emperor apologized to the great expert and then busied himself with state affairs.

In the courtyard, Cheng Zhan was negotiating terms.

Fang Hao and Su Lingxiu had also given their promises. Cheng Zhan was helpless - it was impossible to invite them to the Myriad Thunder Sect now.

However, there was still room for cooperation.

The turmoil of the Heavenly Pits was now entering the pacification stage. It was time to make arrangements for the lower sect geniuses who had come from the Spirit Realm.

The Spirit Controlling Manor group would belong to the Great Yue Dynasty. True Kings had already come to make arrangements.

Out of respect for the great expert, their treatment was improved and they no longer had to worry about being used as cannon fodder.

The Spirit Realm experts breathed a sigh of relief. Although they could no longer stay by the great expert's side, they had benefited from his influence with preferential treatment and would not become cannon fodder. That was enough.

Next was to cultivate diligently and strive for early breakthrough to Condensed Law Celestial Lord, then True King Celestial Lord, and even aim for Immortal Celestial Lord.

They had heard that among the Immortal Celestial Lords of Great Yue, one was formerly a spirit body genius from the Spirit Controlling Manor of the Spirit Realm.

The cultivators who came from the Spirit Controlling Manor were all placed under his command.

Cheng Zhan took the Thunder Cloud Manor group and Thousand Martial Palace group away. Those belonging to the Myriad Thunder Sect were sent there.
