Learning pill refining, even just one step, required repeated practice, so the consumption of medicinal materials would not be small.

In the practice stage, they used ordinary medicinal materials.

Once they became proficient, they would practice with precious medicines.

Finally, they would use spiritual herbs, while controlling the failure rate and improving the success rate.

This all required time to accumulate, and the refining methods for qi blood pills, nourishing qi pills, and healing pills had slight differences in each step.

They started with refining low-grade pills, such as those that could assist in refining bones, organs, or even perceiving the flow of qi and blood.

Under the powerful allure of the pill medicines, many aspiring martial artists wanted to join the Changqing Pavilion, but the conditions for joining were extremely strict, with limitations on age and talent.

As for the failed pills, following the principle of not wasting, they were all fed to the Red Cat.

Only after achieving a 100% success rate for each type of pill would they start refining the next type of pill, in order to reduce the failure rate when refining new pills.

Spiritual herbs, although seemingly abundant, could not withstand such wastage. The senior brothers had traveled a lot to obtain them.

Therefore, to minimize the failure rate when refining spiritual herb pills, Suli had very strict requirements for the success rate.

Another very important matter was to try to establish an intelligence system for the Changqing Pavilion.

Kou Ruozhi became even more excited upon hearing this.

That day, Kou Ruozhi took the few spirit crystals and spirit tickets given by Su Lingxiu, and left the Frontier Wilderness, heading towards the Iron Mountain County in the Inner Domain.

The spirit crystals and spirit tickets were naturally brought back by Xu Yan.

Su Lingxiu looked towards the direction of the Inner Domain, a flash of killing intent flickering in her bright eyes.

She was about to return to the Inner Domain.

She must uproot and completely exterminate the force of the Black-Robed Man!

Kou Ruozhi had entered the Blood Qi Realm, which theoretically corresponded to a Third-Grade Martial Artist, but he was much stronger than the Third-Grade Martial Artists in the Inner Domain. Except for the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion, there were definitely no Grandmaster Martial Artists in Iron Mountain County.

"There might not even be any Third-Grade Martial Artists. Once they break through to the Third Grade, the martial artists in Iron Mountain County would all leave this barren land," Su Lingxiu thought to herself.

With Kou Ruozhi's strength and methods, she believed he could quickly establish a foothold in Iron Mountain County and complete the task she had assigned him. Of course, building an intelligence network would be very difficult. It was absolutely impossible to achieve in the short term.

"I should break through to the Innate Realm now!" Su Lingxiu prepared to break through to the Innate Realm.

Her foundation was already sufficient, her comprehension of the Medicinal Martial Arts Classic had deepened, and she had also grasped the Thousand Butterfly Illusion Body Technique and the Xun Wind Spirit Needle. It was time for her to break through.

"Master, I'm going to break through to the Innate Realm," Su Lingxiu said as she poured tea for her teacher.

"Mm," Li Xuan nodded, playing with a jade ruyi scepter.

"Master, do you have any advice for my breakthrough to the Innate Realm?" Su Lingxiu asked.

Li Xuan pondered for a moment. Xu Yan had broken through to the Innate Realm by integrating sword intent into his body, undergoing a transformation into a Spirit Bone. Meng Chong was similar, also experiencing a significant transformation.

If Xu Yan were to break through to the Profound Realm, Li Xuan even suspected that he might undergo further transformation, with his Spirit Bone becoming even stronger, or perhaps even transforming into a Spirit Body. As for Su Lingxiu, who cultivated the Medicinal Martial Arts, she had not comprehended sword intent, blade intent, or needle intent. How would she undergo a transformation upon breaking through to the Innate Realm?

Li Xuan had long suspected that Su Lingxiu had a special physique. After learning about her origins and that her mother, a Grandmaster Martial Artist, had passed away due to excessive consumption after giving birth to her, Li Xuan suspected that Su Lingxiu's special physique was not complete, and her breakthrough to the Innate Realm would be an opportunity for her to transform and refine her physique.

He then spoke, "You cultivate the Medicinal Martial Arts. To undergo a transformation upon breaking through to the Innate Realm, you should focus on the Medicinal Arts. Refine the Long-Lived Bone, which has a continuous flow of vitality, and use it as the foundation to elevate yourself.

"Your two senior brothers transformed by tempering their sword intent and blade intent, but this is not your forte. Since you do not possess the comprehension of sword intent or blade intent, you should instead focus your efforts on the Medicinal Arts."

Su Lingxiu pondered for a while and then nodded, "Thank you, Master. I know what to do now."

Li Xuan nodded. The fact that Su Lingxiu could comprehend the method meant that her breakthrough to the Innate Realm would also result in a transformation. Perhaps it would elevate her special physique to a new level.

Su Lingxiu began preparing for her breakthrough to the Innate Realm. She spent a few days contemplating the medicinal formulas and eventually concocted one.

The main ingredients were three Sixth-Grade spirit herbs, with the auxiliary ingredients being Seventh-Grade herbs as well.

This was the highest-grade elixir she had ever refined, so she was extremely careful, not daring to be distracted in the slightest. If the refinement failed, the loss would be substantial.

Finally, the elixir was successfully refined, a total of nine pills, with an intertwining green-blue luster and a smooth, lustrous appearance, as if brimming with vibrant life force.

Li Xuan watched with a thoughtful expression.

Outside the manor, under a large tree on the mountain peak, Su Lingxiu sat in a cross-legged position, about to break through to the Innate Realm.

Xu Yan, Meng Chong, Xie Lingfeng, and Hu Shan were all watching from a distance.

Zhou Ying was both nervous and delighted, her doting gaze fixed on the young woman.

Breaking through to the Innate Realm was not difficult for Su Lingxiu, but whether she could use this opportunity to undergo a transformative ascension was the unknown.

The Heaven-Earth Bridge connected, and the Heaven-Earth spiritual energy entered her body. A faint blue glow began to emanate from her, containing a vibrant life force, and the tender buds of the large tree seemed to start growing rapidly.

Xie Lingfeng and Hu Shan looked on with envious expressions. In the Frontier Wilderness, they could cultivate and guide the Heaven-Earth spiritual energy into their bodies, as well as break through realms. This was the true essence of the martial dao.

The martial dao should not be constrained by rules and regulations.

In the Frontier Wilderness, they relied on medicinal pills to refine and cultivate the Qi-Condensing Elixir, and their cultivation speed was not slow either. However, they could never sense the Heaven-Earth spiritual energy, which left them feeling somewhat frustrated.

Su Lingxiu's blue glow grew stronger and more prolonged. Even from a distance, one could feel the vibrant life force emanating from her. Her aura was also undergoing a transformation. At the moment she was about to break through to the Innate Realm, a peculiar situation seemed to arise. The spiritual energy flooded into her body, but it did not condense into true Innate Qi.

Xu Yan frowned, silently observing and examining Su Lingxiu's condition.

Zhou Ying couldn't help but ask anxiously, "Young Master Xu, what's wrong with Miss?"

Even though she had only just reached the Blood Qi Realm, about to break through to the Greater Blood Qi Realm, she could still sense that something seemed amiss with Su Lingxiu's breakthrough.

"It seems that my shijie's body is passively absorbing the Heaven-Earth spiritual energy, like a thirsty person frantically drinking water," Xu Yan said, furrowing his brows in contemplation.

Zhou Ying couldn't help but feel worried. "Then what should we do?"

"Shijie has prepared some medicinal pills, so it should be fine," Xu Yan said, though he was also somewhat concerned.

Su Lingxiu had also noticed the situation in her body and was puzzled. She hastily placed a medicinal pill into her mouth. The pill instantly dissolved, and a vibrant life force began to emerge within her.

She took a deep breath and continued to operate her cultivation technique. The Medicinal Martial Arts Classic flashed through her mind, and she swallowed another pill, beginning to refine the Long-Lived Bone.

At the same time, numerous golden needles appeared, piercing into the acupoints all over her body. The needles trembled, as if connecting to the Heaven-Earth spiritual energy, stimulating the spiritual power of the acupoints.

Another pill was consumed, and the rich vitality, as well as a prolonged and intense energy, began to circulate within her body, stimulating the Long-Lived Bone.

At a certain moment, the Long-Lived Bone began to emit a faint blue glow, and its vitality became even more continuous.

Su Lingxiu discovered that her body seemed to be undergoing a transformation, and she felt a sense of affinity with the surrounding trees, as if she could control them with a mere thought.

The Heaven-Earth spiritual energy flooded into her body, no longer scattering but instead rushing towards her dantian, opening up a spiritual sea within her dantian.

Li Xuan walked over with a leisurely pace, watching the breakthrough of Su Lingxiu. Just as he had suspected, Su Lingxiu had a special physique, but it was not complete, limited by her environment and innate deficiencies.

However, she had refined the Long-Lived Bone, and now, with the assistance of the medicinal pills, her special physique not only became complete but was also undergoing a transformative ascension, about to transform into an even stronger physique.
