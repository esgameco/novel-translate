The Fenyun realm was also home to numerous powerful beings, yet it was still annihilated.

"This came as a huge shock to Taicang and the other five. They realized the gravity of the situation and went to investigate that powerful aura.

"But that aura seemed to be evading them."

Li Xuan was secretly astounded as he listened. The Fenyun realm, one of the seven great realms, was just annihilated like that? The lord of the Fenyun realm had just died like that?

"Or perhaps the annihilation of the Fenyun realm was part of some conspiracy?" Li Xuan pondered to himself.

Tianzi continued his account.

Taicang and the other five were unable to track down the source, and worried about the safety of heaven and earth, so they could only return. However, they all became more cautious, ready to provide support immediately if any of them encountered a crisis.

After an unknown amount of time passed, Wumo, whose power was second only to Taicang's, encountered a powerful enemy and was gravely injured, nearly perishing.

But Wumo also discovered the remnants of the fallen Fenyun, as well as clues about the attacker.

At some point, a divine palace had appeared in the unchanging realm, calling itself the Unchanging Divine Palace. It seemed to have wandered in from somewhere within the unchanging realm.

The annihilation of the Fenyun realm was connected to the powerful beings of this Unchanging Divine Palace.

Their goal was to invade and devour the seven great realms.

The existence in the Unchanging Divine Palace was so powerful that even Taicang could not guarantee he could match it.

As the situation developed and the crisis in heaven and earth became more urgent, Taicang and the others finally launched an attack against the Unchanging Divine Palace after thorough preparations.

"Taicang knew he might not survive this battle. Fearing that the Taicang realm would be invaded and annihilated, he left some contingencies in place.

"Those bastards Taicang and the others tricked me into coming here, gave me good food and fun things, but then locked me up here, just afraid I would be discovered.

"The Taicang realm is very special. Because of my existence as the spirit of this realm, to invade it, they would have to destroy me first. Otherwise, trying to invade or annihilate this realm would be extremely difficult.

"That battle was too brutal. Even though I didn't participate, I know Taicang died. He really died."

By the end, Tianzi couldn't help but start crying again.

Li Xuan fell silent. To think that even someone as powerful as Taicang had died.

Just how powerful were the beings of the Unchanging Divine Palace to be so terrifying?

"I can't get cocky or arrogant. My current power doesn't even match Taicang's, let alone those from the Unchanging Divine Palace. If I encountered them, I'd surely die.

"Even though I've transcended this realm, I should continue lying low in the Taicang realm until my power surpasses Taicang's and those from the Unchanging Divine Palace."

Li Xuan became more vigilant internally.

However, despite Taicang's fall so long ago, the Taicang realm was still intact. Although some changes had occurred, it hadn't suffered severe damage.

This showed how truly remarkable Taicang was. The powerful beings from the Unchanging Divine Palace likely didn't have an easy time in that battle either.

Tianzi wiped away his tears and started cursing again, "Taicang is such a bastard. To protect the living beings of this realm, he forcibly locked me up for so long.

"He said he'd be back soon, lied to me saying that person was no match for him, but he actually died.

"I'll never believe Taicang is invincible again. He's too useless. So what if this realm was gone? Why fight to the death for it?

"If I had the power, I'd change this realm. Taicang is dead, so this realm isn't his anymore!"

Tianzi was full of resentment towards Taicang at this moment.

He continued rambling on, describing more details about that battle and the changes to the Taicang realm.

In that battle, the powerful beings who followed Taicang suffered heavy casualties. Of the other five great realms, it was known that the Wumo realm and Mingyü realm still existed, but whether Wumo and Mingyü themselves had perished was unknown for now.

However, according to Tianzi's guess, they had most likely perished.

Now, both the Wumo and Mingyü realms were trying to invade the Taicang realm. There was a good chance that the remaining powerful beings from these two realms had allied with the Unchanging Divine Palace.

Of course, being locked up here, Tianzi couldn't know the specifics.

"After that battle, the Taicang realm was in turmoil for a long time, and some changes occurred. For example, my current location is in the borderlands, the frontier of the Taicang realm. There's no spiritual energy here, and the qi has become violent.

"I know why Taicang did this. With no spiritual energy, the borderlands appear to have suffered heavy damage. This diverts attention and prevents people from discovering I'm here..."

Tianzi sighed wistfully, but then said angrily: "But Taicang was too cruel. All the things he left for me are gone. Now I don't even have a bed left. How am I supposed to live from now on?"

Then Tianzi looked at Li Xuan expectantly and said, "Senior, do you think you could...?"

Li Xuan smiled in surprise and said, "This might actually be a good thing for you. Since you've been hidden away, why expose yourself now?

"Releasing you would be easy for me, but you should think about whether you really want to expose yourself after being hidden for so long."

Even someone as powerful as Taicang had died. Although the Taicang realm currently seemed safe, what if Tianzi's exposure attracted that powerful being from the Unchanging Divine Palace?

Li Xuan wasn't certain that he could oppose that powerful being from the Unchanging Divine Palace even if he mobilized the power of the entire Taicang realm while inside it.

After all, even with his current Realm Establishment level, his many divine abilities, the Wuxijidao Sword in hand, and the ability to harness the power of the Taicang realm, he still couldn't guarantee victory over Taicang.

So it was impossible to grant Tianzi complete freedom for now, but a plan could be arranged.

"What good does it do if I'm not resigned to this? I don't want to be locked up here for another moment. Taicang is such a bastard, dying and leaving me here to suffer alone."

Tianzi's resentment ran deep.

"Of course, I know the one I should hate most is that terrifying being. But that terrifying being is too strong. I can't beat him. Otherwise I'd want to kill him too."
