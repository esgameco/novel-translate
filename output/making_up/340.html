No matter what, their only choice was to pretend to know nothing and not disclose any information. Otherwise, not only would those Cultivated Spirit Celestials vent their anger on them, even that certain individual might not spare them.

After discussing and reaching an agreement, the two guardians quietly resumed their cultivation.

Guarding the Lingyu Domain Gate was an easy task, and the conflicts within the Yuzhou Martial Dao Realm did not affect them here. Whoever wanted to enter the Inner Domain could simply open the gate.

"The deadline set by those Transcendent Lingzong is almost here, isn't it? I wonder if there will still be any Jade Token holders entering the Lingyu Domain."

Nai the guardian asked.

"After this time, the Lingyu Domain Gate will likely be completely closed. It may take countless years before the Inner Domain can enter the Lingyu Domain again."

Ke the guardian nodded.

"Why completely close the Lingyu Domain Gate? Is it because of fear of the Blood Demon?"

"I don't know. Perhaps they think the Inner Domain is a lowly place with limited talents, so there's no need to pay attention to it anymore. Completely separating the two, not interfering with each other. Or it could be to prevent the Lingyu Domain from interfering with the Inner Domain."

"In that case, for the lowly Inner Domain, this may be a blessing in disguise."

The two fell silent.

Suddenly, a figure walked in.

Ke and Nai's expressions changed, and they hurriedly stood up, respectfully greeting, "Greetings, Senior!"

The newcomer was at least a Great Celestial Realm cultivator, and a powerful one from a certain Lingzong. The two guardians dared not look directly at him, keeping their heads slightly bowed.

"Mm, I need to go to the Inner Domain."

Xu Yan said indifferently.

"Yes, Senior!"

The two guardians hastily opened the Lingyu Domain Gate, not daring to delay even a moment.

Xu Yan looked at the two pillars in the grand hall, which emitted a faint glow and rotated slightly, causing the Lingyu Domain Gate to open.

"So this is how the Lingyu Domain Gate is opened."

After memorizing the method of opening the gate, Xu Yan remained silent and stepped through the grand hall, returning to the Inner Domain.

The two guardians were shaken in their hearts. What business did this Lingzong Senior have in going to the Inner Domain?

"We shall mind our own business and not spread any information. If anyone asks, other than that certain individual, we know nothing about who entered the Inner Domain!"

Ke the guardian spoke solemnly.

Nai the guardian nodded gravely.
