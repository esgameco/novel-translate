Dao Ancestor?

Upon hearing this, Taihe and Taikun's minds began to reel. What kind of being was this?

However, when Taimiao instructed the two to seek treatment for their injuries from Su Lingxiu, they couldn't help but feel a glimmer of hope.

If the wounds on their bodies could be healed, they would no longer need to constantly suppress themselves and endure the pain of erosion.

But then they recalled Su Lingxiu's excited expression earlier as she clamored about dissecting Mu Xie, and felt uneasy. Would that girl dissect them too?

Taimiao seemed to sense their concerns and said, "Don't worry, that girl is kind-hearted. She won't take your lives. To heal the wounds on your bodies, some sacrifice is necessary, isn't it?"

Then he looked towards a certain area of the Unchanging Realm, his gaze turning cold as he said in a deep voice, "With the Dao Ancestor in this world, the schemes of the Unchanging Divine Palace are destined to fail."

Taihe and Taikun nodded.

The Dao Ancestor's strength was beyond imagination, enough to protect this world.

"That's good then!" Taihe nodded.

If the world could be protected, his disciple wouldn't have to take the path of exile.

Tianzi's form appeared and looked at the two of them, saying with a pout, "You two old fogies, it's really rare that you're still alive. If you want to advance further, you'd better comprehend my Heavenly Dao well."

"What's the deal with your Heavenly Dao?" Taimiao asked curiously.

"Taimiao, you little woman, I'm not telling you!" Tianzi said with a haughty expression.

Taimiao glared at him in exasperation, "Why are you so petty? Big Brother Taicang is gone now, are you still holding a grudge over what happened back then? I gave you so many delicious treats and fun things back then, have you completely forgotten?"

Tianzi scratched his head, "Fine, I, the great Tianzi, am magnanimous. I won't quibble with you, little woman."

After a pause, he said proudly: "The Heavenly Dao is the great way of heaven and earth. Only a world with the Heavenly Dao is a perfect world. This is what the Dao Ancestor taught me.

"My Heavenly Dao can be enhanced without limit. As you cultivate and comprehend the Heavenly Dao, you can also become stronger along with it. As old acquaintances, I can give you some preferential treatment.

"However, this is preferential treatment I'm giving you, not bestowed by the Heavenly Dao itself. The Heavenly Dao is supremely impartial and beyond question.

"As the Heavenly Dao grows stronger, you will grow stronger too. As more and more powerful beings emerge in this world, the world itself will continuously expand and strengthen..."

Upon hearing this, Taimiao and the others were shocked.

If what Tianzi said was true, wouldn't the Heavenly Dao keep getting stronger, and the world keep expanding and strengthening? And their path of cultivation would be nearly endless?

The stronger the Heavenly Dao, the stronger the world, the stronger they would become.

And they, being at the pinnacle of power in this world, would continue to remain at the pinnacle in the future. Given enough time, surpassing Taicang in strength may not be impossible.

"Can it really be enhanced infinitely?" Taihe asked in disbelief.

"Theoretically, yes. Perhaps it will stop increasing only after the entire Unchanging Realm has been transformed into our world?

"But who knows, the Unchanging Realm seems boundless. When that time comes, we can ask the Dao Ancestor for guidance again." Tianzi shrugged.

The entire Unchanging Realm transformed into their world?

Taihe and the others couldn't imagine it. Before this world came into being, they had all traveled the Unchanging Realm with Taicang.

The Unchanging Realm seemed boundless. Even after eons of travel, they had not left the Unchanging Realm. In fact, they felt that despite their lengthy travels, they had only moved about in a small area of the Unchanging Realm.

Otherwise, where did the Unchanging Divine Palace come from? It must have come from beyond all the areas of the Unchanging Realm they had traveled.

Besides the Unchanging Divine Palace, whether there were other powerful beings or forces in the Unchanging Realm, or even other worlds, was unknown.

That beam of world-creating purple light may not have been the first in the history of the Unchanging Realm!

"Alright then, we'll ride on your coattails, Tianzi, to comprehend the Heavenly Dao, break through our limits, and increase our strength. Of course, that's assuming we truly have a chance to recover," Taikun nodded and said.

"Don't worry, you've endured for so long without dying. With Lingxiu's help, it'll just take some time to heal you," Tianzi said before happily walking away.

Taihe and Taikun were very powerful. If they comprehended and cultivated the Heavenly Dao, it could bring faster enhancement to the Heavenly Dao. After all, these two had followed Taicang for a long time, their knowledge far surpassed others, and their experience was broader.

In a small courtyard of the Qinghua Sect.

Li Xuan raised his teacup and drank leisurely, one hand playing with a jade ruyi scepter, relaxed and content.

Cai Linger stood gracefully to the side serving him, her beautiful eyes curiously looking at the figure suddenly brought here.

A row of bone spikes stood on its head, its eyebrows were curved and long, its eyes wide open with a faint green tinge. It seemed to be wearing a black leather outfit that looked smooth and shiny.

But that wasn't a leather outfit it was wearing. Rather, this being's skin was naturally like that.

This was an ugly humanoid creature, and it came from the Unchanging Realm, not any race or being from within this world.

Cai Linger had just witnessed the Dao Ancestor take action, reaching up to grab, and immediately bringing this creature down.

A creature grabbed by the Dao Ancestor must be very powerful. Although the Dao Ancestor's action seemed casual and effortless, this creature's strength must have already surpassed the Realm Lord level.

A World Lord level powerhouse! Cai Linger was shocked. Even a World Lord level powerhouse was casually grabbed by the Dao Ancestor. The Dao Ancestor's strength was truly unfathomable!

And how fortunate she was, what a great opportunity, to be able to become the Dao Ancestor's maid!

Mu Xie felt a bit dazed. Held by a hand, in an instant, he had arrived in this small courtyard. The gray energy surrounding his body had disappeared, revealing his true form.

What shocked him even more was that his powerful strength seemed to have vanished, and he couldn't sense even a trace of it.

However, he could still feel his own power, which meant his strength had only been sealed.

How the other party had sealed his strength, he couldn't perceive even a hint!

The small courtyard was ordinary. Under a large tree, a young man sat leisurely in a chair, contentedly drinking tea and enjoying pastries.

A stunningly beautiful woman stood by his side serving him, his maid.

But the young man looked ordinary, without any aura of a powerful being, as if he were just a common person, a young master from a wealthy family leisurely enjoying his maid's service.

That beautiful woman did have some strength, but not much. He could kill her with just a breath.

Mu Xie stared wide-eyed at the young man. He knew that the one who had taken action must be the young man sitting casually in the chair.

The more ordinary he appeared, the more it indicated that the other party's strength was immensely powerful, already beyond his perception.

"Who are you?" Mu Xie finally couldn't help but ask.

As the Ancestor of the Dao, constantly fighting and killing would damage his image. So when it was time to "educate", he needed to "educate" these uncivilized savages!

Mu Xiao, who had been glaring angrily at Feng Yan with bared teeth, was suddenly terrified. He looked in shock at Su Lingxiu, who was acting coquettishly and trying to curry favor. It was hard to imagine such a gentle and beautiful young woman saying such brutal things.

Feng Yan sighed beside him: "Now do you understand I was doing this for your own good? If you had kowtowed gratefully earlier, I would have taken you down and given you a broom to sweep. None of this would have happened!"

Su Lingxiu massaged her master's shoulders. "Don't worry, Master. I'll just dissect and study him a bit, I won't kill him. He's so powerful with such strong life force, he won't die.

The more Mu Xiao heard, the more his heart pounded in fear. Dissected but not dead?

Did that mean he would be wide awake as he was cut open and poked and prodded?

Just imagining it made Mu Xiao's whole body tremble involuntarily.

"If you're going to kill me, then kill me! To humiliate me like this is unbecoming of a powerful being!"

Mu Xiao shouted angrily, not realizing his voice was shaking slightly as he spoke.
