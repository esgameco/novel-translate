Under the shocked gazes of the martial artists from Tai Miao Sect, Xu Yan struck out palm after palm. In an instant, eighteen giant golden dragons roared forth.

They charged towards the attacking Jade Snow True Dragon.

The eighteen golden dragons attacked the Jade Snow True Dragon from all directions, immediately entangling it. In just a moment, the jade white true dragon was submerged among the golden dragons.

Immediately after, the furious roars of the Jade Snow True Dragon rang out, along with an extremely intense and violent dragon might. Terrifying true dragon techniques were also unleashed recklessly.

However, the eighteen golden dragons entangled it from above, below, left, right, front and back, intertwining with the Jade Snow True Dragon, rolling and coiling together in mid-air.

Boom! When one golden dragon was destroyed, Xu Yan would strike out to summon a new golden dragon to replenish it.

Old Man Xiao watched with wide eyes. That Jade Snow True Dragon had been entangled, and this kind of entanglement was somewhat shameful for a true dragon.

A total of nineteen giant dragons were intertwined together, eighteen of which were golden dragons, rolling and tumbling about. The jade white true dragon was struggling and roaring madly.

However, it was still unable to break free from the entanglement of the eighteen golden dragons. The scene looked as if eighteen giant dragons were assaulting a delicate jade dragon.

"What a sin!" Old Man Xiao exclaimed in astonishment.

Xu Yan continuously replenished the consumed golden dragons, sweat beading on his forehead. To defeat a stronger opponent with a weaker force and subdue this true dragon was still very pressuring.

The consumption was also enormous, but Xu Yan didn't mind this level of consumption.

"It won't work to keep entangling like this. This dragon has clearly lost its rationality and become extremely agitated, with anger and hatred filling its mind."

Xu Yan frowned.

This true dragon had clearly fallen victim to a scheme. Some people had deliberately lured it into the Divine Realm to create trouble and chaos.

"It must be the Heaven's Bane Earth's Shadow."

Besides Heaven's Bane Earth's Shadow, there was no other force capable of this.

To dare scheme against even a true dragon, the power behind Heaven's Bane Earth's Shadow must be very strong to dare offend a true dragon.

"If I kill the true dragon, it may cause some trouble, and it would play right into Heaven's Bane Earth's Shadow's hands. I refuse to let Heaven's Bane Earth's Shadow have their way."

Thinking this, Xu Yan pondered how to awaken this true dragon that had lost its rationality.

At this moment, Xu Yan's expression changed slightly. The true dragon that had been entangled into a ball and was struggling in mid-air suddenly opened its mouth.

The world's spiritual energy frantically poured into its mouth. Its jade white body radiated a hazy glow, as if a layer of white mist enveloped the true dragon's body.

Bang! One golden dragon after another was destroyed. Xu Yan's expression grew solemn as he struck out palm after palm, continuously replenishing the destroyed golden dragons.

"Is this dragon qi? True dragon breath?"

Xu Yan frowned deeply.

The Jade Snow True Dragon's breath at this moment didn't seem to be aimed at him, but was passively triggered.

Boom!

Suddenly, the world shook once, emitting a faint rumble. The sky above shone with light.

"The Dao Realm Stairway has opened!" The Tai Miao Sect Master exclaimed joyfully.

The opening of the Dao Realm Stairway meant that experts from the Dao Realm would soon descend to the Divine Realm. The true dragon calamity should also come to an end.

Although, based on the current situation, even without Dao Realm experts descending, the true dragon calamity would end. But the opening of the Dao Realm Stairway held a different significance.

Old Man Xiao looked up at the sky, his expression calm, but the corner of his mouth revealed a mocking smile.

"Let's see how you all struggle, scheme, and plot against each other!"

He laughed coldly in his heart.

In the Earth Cloud Realm, the clouds of mist covering the ground had already dispersed.

The huge heavenly cave mouth seemed to have been brewing for a long time. The powerful aura contained within was about to burst out.

Cracks had appeared in the light barrier sealing the heavenly cave mouth.

The Heaven's Bane expert guarding the heavenly cave had a flushed face as he struggled to maintain it.

Heaven Seventeen's expression was somewhat nervous. At this moment, a spirit bird flew over, bringing a message.

"Not good!"

Heaven Seventeen's expression changed drastically the moment he saw the message.

"That brat Xu Yan, how dare he ruin my grand plan!"

He could no longer sit still at this moment.

Xu Yan was subduing the dragon in the Tai Miao Realm! Although news that Xu Yan had already subdued the dragon hadn't arrived yet, he was now fighting evenly with the true dragon. The true dragon's unstoppable momentum had been instantly broken.

If Xu Yan's intervention suppressed the true dragon and caused the Tai He Realm to not open the Dao Realm Stairway, how could the plan succeed?

At this moment, Heaven Seventeen was shocked and furious, killing intent surging.

Boom!

However, at this moment, light flashed in the sky. He finally breathed a sigh of relief - the Dao Realm Stairway had opened.

"Prepare!" Heaven Seventeen said solemnly.

"Heaven Seventeen, watch for the right moment. As soon as the time is ripe, tell us to remove the seal!" A Heaven's Bane expert said with difficulty.

Heaven Seventeen nodded. He stared intently at the sky, waiting for the best moment.

In the Tai He Realm, at the location of the Dao Realm Stairway.

"Big Senior Brother is subduing the dragon. I really want to go see." Meng Chong said regretfully.

Jiang Buping nodded. Unfortunately, the two of them were currently waiting for the Dao Realm Stairway to descend and really couldn't be in two places at once. They could only regretfully miss Big Senior Brother subduing the dragon.

However, after Big Senior Brother subdued the true dragon, they would have a chance to see the true dragon's appearance.

Boom!

The steps in the sky became clearer and clearer, emanating an aura of heaven and earth. Then, at the end of the steps, the outline of a small world appeared faintly.

Finally, a step descended! The Dao Realm Stairway opened! The token in Wu Tiannan's hand gave guidance - he only needed to step onto the stairway to leave the Divine Realm and head to the Dao Realm.

Holding this token, one could ascend the stairway and enter the Dao Realm.

"Headmaster Wu, you should go up," Meng Chong said to Wu Tiannan.

Wu Tiannan hesitated and said, "My strength is low, and the Dao Realm must be very strong. Why don't I give this token to you?"

Meng Chong shook his head and said, "If I want to go to the Dao Realm, I naturally will go. Nothing can stop us. Headmaster Wu, since you have this token, you should be qualified. You should have a place to stay when you reach the Dao Realm."

"Alright, in that case, I'll go to the Dao Realm!"

Wu Tiannan thought about the person he was looking for. With no news in the Divine Realm, he could only try his luck in the Dao Realm.

"Meng Chong, Jiang Buping, now that the Dao Realm Stairway has opened, I hope you two won't..." The Tai He Sect Master said solemnly at this moment.

"Headmaster Wu is about to ascend the stairway. If you try to stop him, I won't be polite," Meng Chong interrupted.

"What's going on?" A voice came from atop the Dao Realm Stairway.

Meng Chong and Jiang Buping's expressions grew solemn. The person who spoke had extremely strong power.

"Reporting to the Emissary, a true dragon has fallen into the Divine Realm and is wreaking havoc everywhere. The Divine Realm is facing a great calamity!" The Tai He Sect Master said respectfully.

"What?"

A shocked voice came from the stairway.

Then, the stairway shook. Several figures were coming down the stairway.

Suddenly, Meng Chong and Jiang Buping's expressions changed drastically.

"Retreat quickly!"
