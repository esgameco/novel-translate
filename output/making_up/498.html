"Since you came to deliver treasures, I'll accept them!"

Meng Chong nodded. These Treasure Alliance guys really knew how to read the situation.

"Thank you, Young Master Meng!"

Hong Yi and the others were grateful, so moved that tears almost flowed from their eyes.

On the other side, the Revenge Alliance leader and others had resolute expressions, showing their determination to fight to the death. At this point, they could not retreat like the Treasure Alliance.

The slogan of slaying the Sword God and capturing the true dragon had already spread to all martial artists in the God Realm, while the Treasure Alliance's slogan had only been shouted a couple times as an afterthought.

Moreover, the Treasure Alliance could pin everything on them, but they could not do the same.

Xu Yan was a terrifying person. Since he had given them two paths and they didn't cherish the opportunity, now they could only fight to the death, perhaps with a slim chance of victory.

"Kill!"

A huge, ferocious giant mouth appeared between heaven and earth, swallowing towards them, as if it would devour both heaven and earth along with Xu Yan.

"Should we retreat?"

Ao Yuxue felt her heart pounding.

This attack was too strong. She couldn't withstand it at all and could only avoid its sharp edge.

After all, she was not yet an adult, just a young dragon, with strength only at the Heaven Fusion realm.
