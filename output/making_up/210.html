Li Xuan's spiritual energy consumption was not small, and it took him a long time to painstakingly write one-third of the patterns into the Great Dao Golden Book.

As he stopped writing the patterns, the feedback from the Great Dao Golden Book came.

Laws of Heaven and Earth (Incomplete):

Perfection Degree: Comprehension Difficulty:

When Li Xuan saw the feedback information given by the Great Dao Golden Book, he was immediately shocked.

Laws of Heaven and Earth?

On the Great Dao Golden Book, the perfection degree and comprehension difficulty of the Laws of Heaven and Earth were blank, without any evaluation.

Li Xuan took a deep breath, the Great Dao Golden Book did not give an evaluation because these Laws of Heaven and Earth were not created by him.

Therefore, the Great Dao Golden Book only gave feedback on the patterns, but did not give an evaluation.

Li Xuan withdrew his spiritual sense from the Great Dao Golden Book, his eyes filled with shock as he looked at the ancient Taiyang leather book in his hand. The patterns drawn on it were actually the Laws of Heaven and Earth? No wonder they were so profound, he couldn't understand them at all, his realm was too low, how could he understand? This also explained why the patterns gave people a sense of constantly changing.

"This is a true treasure!"

Li Xuan was deeply shocked.

The ancient Taiyang leather book contained the Laws of Heaven and Earth, if he could comprehend them, it would directly point to the profound mysteries of the origin of heaven and earth!

Since obtaining the ancient leather book, he had been studying the patterns on the first page, occasionally gaining some insights. Each time he focused on studying them, he would have a sense of tranquility.

The patterns depicted the Laws of Heaven and Earth, so it was not difficult to understand why the patterns had the outlines of landforms, environments, and so on.

The Laws of Heaven and Earth naturally encompass everything in heaven and earth.

The ancient leather book could contain the Laws of Heaven and Earth, which meant that the leather used must not be ordinary animal skin, but probably the skin of an extremely powerful beast.

"Taiyang."

As Li Xuan looked at the two characters "Taiyang" on the ancient leather book, he suddenly had some enlightenment.

Whether it was the Borderlands or the Inner Domain, including the Spiritual Realm, they were actually just a part of the Taiyang world. The heaven and earth he was in was called the Taiyang Heaven and Earth.

"This Taiyang book records the Laws of Taiyang Heaven and Earth, but it is unknown who authored it. Such a precious treasure, whether it was deliberately hidden or not, the signs all indicate that the martial arts power of the Taiyang world is absolutely strong, and may have declined for some reason.

"The Inner Domain, the Spiritual Realm... the gate of the Spiritual Realm is the portal between the Inner Domain and the Spiritual Realm, separating the two realms.

"Is this the original state of the Taiyang world, or was it deliberately done by someone?"

Li Xuan's emotions were in turmoil, and various doubts and mysteries surged in his mind.

"The Laws of Heaven and Earth, if comprehended, could one wield the Laws of Heaven and Earth? What a realm, what a powerful expert would be able to do that?"

Li Xuan sighed as he looked at the Taiyang book.

After his spiritual energy recovered, Li Xuan opened the Great Dao Golden Book again and wrote the first pattern of the Taiyang book into it, obtaining all the information of the first page of the Taiyang book.

Last time he wrote in one-third, the Great Dao Golden Book gave feedback on the incomplete Laws of Heaven and Earth.

If the complete patterns were written in, would it mean the complete Laws of Heaven and Earth? The first page was the Laws of Heaven and Earth, what were the patterns on the following pages? Writing the patterns consumed a lot of his spiritual energy, so Li Xuan had to write them in slowly, resting and recovering his consumed spiritual energy after each session.

On the Spiritual Platform, the Great Dao Golden Book was open, and Li Xuan's spiritual form seemed to stretch out a hand, drawing something on it.

It took him three days to finally write all the patterns on the first page into the Great Dao Golden Book.

As he stopped writing the patterns, the Great Dao Golden Book glowed with golden light.

Laws of Heaven and Earth (Partial):

Perfection Degree: Comprehension Difficulty:

Seeing the feedback information from the Great Dao Golden Book, Li Xuan let out a breath. The first page was the complete part of the Laws of Heaven and Earth, which meant the following patterns in the Taiyang book were also the Laws of Heaven and Earth?

"I have a book that records the Laws of Heaven and Earth in my hands, I must make use of it. I can start from the Laws of Heaven and Earth and create a new martial art."

Li Xuan was greatly excited.

The mystery of the Taiyang book had finally been solved.

However, the Laws of Heaven and Earth were too profound, even though he had reached the Spiritual Sense Realm, he could not comprehend the subtleties within.

But with the Great Dao Golden Book, he could do some creation.

"I have the Pure Martial Dao, the Flesh Martial Dao, and the Alchemy Martial Dao, and I've even created some elixirs. What's missing now is the Weapon Forging Martial Dao.

"The Weapon Forging Dao? How to forge weapons? I want to incorporate the Laws of Heaven and Earth into it and make use of it. But merely forging weapons is not enough.

"In addition to forging, I'm also missing formations and seals..."

Li Xuan fell into deep thought.

Forging, formations, seals... these were what he was currently lacking. How could he create them?

In the Inner Domain, there were forging masters who could forge precious weapons, but it was not a martial dao, and not powerful enough.

As for formations and seals, the Inner Domain had never heard of them.

Whether the Spiritual Realm had them, he did not know. At least for now, they did not exist in the Inner Domain.

As for the art of military formations, the formations in the Inner Domain were also very ordinary, far from the Dao of Formations he had in mind.

"The Demonic Martial Dao can be considered the fourth martial dao, it is not a cultivation path for humans, but for beasts. Once the Crimson Cat completes its transformation, this martial dao will emerge."

Li Xuan pondered, "'Weapon Formation Martial Dao'? 'Array Weapon Martial Dao'? That sounds a bit ordinary. Just forging weapons cannot display great power, but the Laws of Heaven and Earth can be used in the Dao of Formations.

"No, forging weapons can also be useful, like forging storage bags, storage rings, and so on. In addition to materials, the Laws of Heaven and Earth can also be utilized.

"However, the Laws of Heaven and Earth are too high-level, how can I apply them in a way that even those with low cultivation can use?"

The more he thought about it, the more Li Xuan felt troubled. Forging weapons without integrating it with formations would not be powerful enough, but if he integrated it with the Dao of Formations, the two did not seem to be of the same nature.

After pondering for a long time without any breakthrough, Li Xuan sighed and turned his attention back to the Taiyang book, flipping to the second page.

He decided to memorize all the patterns in the Taiyang book.

Although it was a bit difficult, and he could only remember the forms of the patterns without grasping their true profundity, once his realm was elevated, and he could truly comprehend the Laws of Heaven and Earth, he would naturally gain insights into their mysteries.

The patterns on the second page had a slightly different feeling compared to when he first saw them.

It was as if he was seeing a formation, or perhaps a trap formed by the laws of heaven and earth.

Vaguely, Li Xuan seemed to see some changes in the patterns.

"Formation, array..."

Li Xuan murmured a few words, and suddenly a flash of inspiration struck his mind.

"Bizarre Celestial Techniques."

In that moment, Li Xuan thought of the Bizarre Celestial Techniques, with 4,320 formations...

"I've found the new martial dao!"

Li Xuan became excited.

He had finally found a way to utilize the Laws of Heaven and Earth to create a new martial dao.

"Whether it's forging weapons or formations, using oneself as the formation, using oneself as the array, with weapons as the auxiliary... Forging, Formation, and the Great Celestial Array.

"Using all things as the array, using heaven and earth as the formation - the Bizarre Celestial Martial Dao!"

Li Xuan was invigorated in his heart. He had finally found the way to create a new martial dao.
