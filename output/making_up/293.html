The Xunfeng Sword Intent swept like a storm, the spiritual soul dissipated, and the physical body turned to ashes.

The spectating martial artists were all shocked. The Qianwu Palace was being slapped in the face, yet they did not unleash their thunderous might to subdue the intruder?

Xu Yan's eyes narrowed slightly, ready to flee at any moment. This elder's aura was stronger than Huang Liang's, he was definitely not Xu Yan's match.

"What, your Qianwu Palace has no more geniuses, only you old fellows left?"

Xu Yan looked at the elder contemptuously.

"How arrogant!"

"The rumors about Xu Yan's arrogance are true, I see it for myself today!"

This was a supreme expert, yet Xu Yan was treating him with such contempt.

Song Zeng's anger surged, but he suppressed it and said coldly, "The Qianwu Palace, the Superb Spirit Sect, holds an esteemed position. How can our sect's geniuses be challenged by you, a mere wandering cultivator?"

You must first defeat all the geniuses of Qianwu Prefecture, then come challenge our sect's geniuses. Otherwise, you have no qualifications!

Although Xu Yan had come all the way to their gate and killed two of their warriors, making them lose some face, but letting their geniuses fight him directly would make their geniuses look cheap, right? If not for the constraints of the genius competition, they would have slapped Xu Yan to death long ago.

However, the Qianwu Palace was being closely watched by Wu Tiannan, and if they violated the rules of the genius competition, Wu Tiannan would surely find a reason to strike them down.

The other Superb Spirit Sect sects might not necessarily join the Qianwu Palace in dealing with Wu Tiannan.

In the previous battle at the Wanxing Martial Arts Academy, it was the former Palace Master of the Qianwu Palace who struck down Wu Tiannan, but the latter actually managed to escape with his life.

And his strength had advanced further, reaching the pinnacle of the Spiritual Realm!

With such a powerful expert keeping an eye on them, the Qianwu Palace could only endure for now.

"The Qianwu Palace is very strong, their geniuses all have spiritual body talents, two realms higher than me. Even if I defeat them, their elders will definitely not sit still and will all rush at me.

"Alright, then I, Xu Yan, shall subdue all the geniuses of Qianwu Prefecture!"
