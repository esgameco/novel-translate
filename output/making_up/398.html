Although the impacts made his body shake, Meng Chong was not injured at all.

The assassin facing Meng Chong's ferocious assault seemed somewhat flustered, unable to defend properly.

Although he was a True King Celestial Venerable, Meng Chong kept closing in on him, as if wanting to engage in close combat. Faced with such a fierce attack, he became panicked for a moment.

His instincts told him that if Meng Chong got close enough for hand-to-hand combat, even as a peak True King Celestial Venerable, he would surely die.

Although each hit made Meng Chong's body shake and even stagger a bit, they still couldn't injure him.

Boom! Meng Chong grasped the sun, his blade wrapped in wind and thunder. At this moment he truly had the majesty of a god. In an instant, he suddenly lunged forward, his giant-like body abruptly returning to normal size.

This sudden change caught the assassin who was defending against Meng Chong's attack off guard. Meng Chong had already pounced on him.

"Not good!"

A sense of crisis arose in his heart, and he hurriedly retreated.

It was precisely because of this that the Wind Spirit Tiger, as king of the spirit beasts, would not casually punish this tiger.

After all, tigers were scarce! If one was killed, there would be one less.

It was also because of this that the red cat dared to take action today.

As a cultured tiger, the red cat was confident that although his current strength was not as great as the Wind Spirit Tiger's, his mind was sharper.
