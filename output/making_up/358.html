As Su Lingxiu broke through, the flying ship continued towards Zheng Kingdom in Jade State.

The current Jade State was no longer like when they first entered the Spirit Realm. Three great cities now stood in Jade State, connected by teleportation arrays, and were the most prosperous cities in Jade State.

Yu Gao and Shen Tai had become the strongest existences in Jade State, maintaining order there.

The Zheng Kingdom still existed independently, still maintaining its unique status. However, now it was not only under the rule of the Transcendent Spirit Sect, but also accepted the governance of the Eternal Alliance headquarters.

Only a small handful of the top experts in the Spirit Realm knew about the disappearance of the Spirit Realm Gate.

An order had been issued that no one was allowed to open the Spirit Realm Gate without permission.

They even falsely claimed that the strongest experts had taken action to seal the method of opening the Spirit Realm Gate, and that it could not be opened without obtaining a jade token.

"I wonder how the Great Wilderness is now?" Xu Yan was starting to miss his parents.

He had been in the Spirit Realm for so many years, but the mother-child bead he had left behind had not shattered, meaning no uncontrollable dangerous events had occurred.

The opening of the Divine Bridge was imminent, and he would be going to the other side of the Divine Bridge. After this parting, who knew when he would be able to return to the Great Wilderness again.

"It's good that the Spirit Realm Gate is closed, avoiding some potential crises."

With his parents in the Great Wilderness and Spirit Realm cultivators unable to enter, there naturally would be no threat to the pinnacle of the Great Wilderness, and his parents and family would not encounter danger.

Moreover, as time passed and his parents and family's strength increased, if they broke through to the Divine Sense realm, Spirit Realm cultivators would no longer be able to threaten the Great Wilderness.

They would also not be able to threaten the safety of his parents and family.

Although spiritual energy was sparse in the Great Wilderness, it merely meant cultivation would be slightly slower. Unlike cultivators in the Inner Realm and Spirit Realm, there was no issue of being unable to continue breaking through after reaching a certain level.

The Evergreen Pavilion flying ship arrived at the location of the Spirit Realm Gate.

Li Xuan looked down. The formation was still there. Although the Spirit Realm Gate was not open, in his eyes it was completely exposed, as if two forces of heaven and earth's laws had closed together, shutting the doorway.

He lightly pointed with his hand. With his Void Breaking realm strength, he rearranged the formation covering the Spirit Realm Gate.

After this arrangement, even if an Immortal Venerable came, they would not be able to open the Spirit Realm Gate.

The barrier between the Spirit Realm and Inner Realm was formed by the laws of heaven and earth. With an Immortal Venerable's great power, they probably would not need to go through the Spirit Realm Gate to enter the Inner Realm.

Therefore, Li Xuan planned to reinforce this barrier so that even an Immortal Venerable could not cross it.

"Master, I'll go open the Spirit Realm Gate," Xu Yan said.

"No need," Li Xuan shook his head. He raised a hand and pointed forward. Instantly, space shattered and a black void appeared in midair. The flying ship directly flew into it.

As the ship entered, the opened space began to close.

"This is Void Breaking. Observe and comprehend it well," Li Xuan said to Xu Yan and Meng Chong.

"Yes, Master!" Xu Yan and Meng Chong were shocked in their hearts. So this was Void Breaking? With just a light point, he broke open the void and directly traversed space!

Everyone on the Evergreen Pavilion was shocked and excited. They were honored to once again witness the senior slightly display his power.

Indeed! When suppressing the Charming Witch earlier, the senior had not even used a ten-thousandth of his power.

Boom! The flying ship passed through the void and emerged from a spatial rift, having crossed the barrier and entered the Great Wilderness.

They instantly felt how sparse the spiritual energy was, causing everyone accustomed to the abundant spiritual energy of the Spirit Realm to frown involuntarily.

"So this is Void Breaking," Xu Yan was still immersed in the shattering of space he had just witnessed.

"Void Breaking is difficult to comprehend. Master saw that I had broken through to the Divine Sense realm, so he demonstrated it to let me see what Void Breaking is, allowing me to more intuitively understand the profundities of the Void Breaking realm."

Xu Yan understood in his heart.

Li Xuan raised his head to look at the sky. The spiritual energy in the Great Wilderness had become a bit sparser, which was related to the separation from the Cang Tian world, but this situation was not unchangeable.

When his strength was insufficient, he could not do it.

Now, he could.

He already comprehended the laws of heaven and earth, and had gained insight into some of the Dao principles of heaven and earth. Although it would be somewhat difficult to raise the spiritual energy of the Great Wilderness by a level, it was not impossible.

"Since this is the Great Wilderness, I might as well take this opportunity to increase the spiritual energy concentration," Li Xuan decided in his heart.

The stronger the Great Wilderness became, the more he would benefit.

"We're finally back. Let's go to Canglan Island!" Xu Yan came back to his senses and said excitedly.

The Evergreen Pavilion flying ship turned into a beam of light, flying towards Canglan Island.

On Canglan Island, everything was as peaceful and tranquil as before.

Although Xu Yan had left the Great Wilderness, the deterrent still existed. With the support of the Great Wilderness Martial Arts Academy's experts, the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion and many other powerful figures, even if there were occasional disturbances, they would quickly be quelled.

The Great Wilderness had now become deeply ingrained in people's hearts.

Gradually, the term "Inner Realm" was no longer mentioned.

Especially children born in the past decade or youths who had only entered the martial path in the past decade, almost none of them knew the name "Inner Realm", only knowing the Great Wilderness.

Moreover, legends about the Great Wilderness were spreading among these youths.

Among the former Inner Realm cultivators, some adhered to Inner Realm cultivation methods, while others tried every means to convert to Great Wilderness martial arts. But regardless, the Inner Realm had gradually been abandoned and was rarely mentioned.

The Great Wilderness had unified and established uniform order and laws. The most renowned was undoubtedly the Great Wilderness Martial Arts Academy. Many young geniuses all wanted to enter the academy for intensive training.

The current Great Wilderness Martial Arts Academy was no longer the former Seven Star Academy. The requirements for admitting martial arts students had been further raised, and only geniuses could enter.

Starting from this year, the Great Wilderness had implemented a new policy.

Academies were established in every town and city throughout the Great Wilderness to popularize education and martial arts.

Moreover, the academies were clearly classified into different levels.

Children would enter school and advance to higher-level academies through examinations, ultimately aiming to be admitted to the Great Wilderness Martial Arts Academy.

Those who could enter the Great Wilderness Academy were all among the top geniuses of the entire Great Wilderness.

Martial arts sects still existed in various places, but some weaker sects had already faced inheritance crises.

Since everyone was cultivating Great Wilderness martial arts anyway, why would they need to join a sect and be bound by so many restrictions? In order to survive, sects with special strengths and appeal were racking their brains to research Great Wilderness martial arts techniques.

For example, certain powerful palm techniques, fist techniques, and so on.

Sects with deep foundations quickly researched techniques more suitable for Great Wilderness martial arts, using Inner Realm martial arts as reference and their original sect techniques as a basis.

Some sects even established their position by defeating one Great Wilderness cultivator after another with the techniques they had researched.

As the major sects became more competitive, this led to a rapid accumulation of various martial skills, secret techniques, and combat methods in Great Wilderness martial arts, becoming increasingly abundant with more and more martial techniques.

Everywhere in the Great Wilderness was competitive, except for Canglan Island, which remained as before.

In the Great Wilderness, apart from the Great Wilderness Academy, the other martial arts holy land was undoubtedly Canglan Island.
