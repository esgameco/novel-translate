"Is he using this to avoid being pursued by us?

Hu Shan analyzed it in this way.

Xie Lingfeng felt that it made sense. Blood Without Heart had suffered such a big loss in the Inner Realm, and had just fled to the Borderlands. He must have been low-key to avoid being pursued by Inner Realm martial artists.

To conceal his identity, he even went so far as to become someone's grandson?

He really underestimated Blood Without Heart's restraint.

Thinking about it this way, Xie Lingfeng narrowed his eyes and said to Guo Rongshan, "Send a message to your grandson, have him come to the capital to see me. Tell him he can't hide."

"Okay!"

Guo Rongshan did not hesitate and nodded in agreement.

This matter was no longer something he could control or resolve. Passing the message to his grandson, if it was truly impossible to defeat them, then let his grandson flee.

"Go slowly, my dear minister."

"Yes, yes, Father is right!" The Crown Prince apologized with a guilty expression.
