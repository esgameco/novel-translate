Meng Chong spent every day in the mountains and forests, contemplating the Wind and Thunder Vajra Fist, the thunderous might his master had displayed constantly echoing in his mind.

The Crown Prince felt that his search for the sage was not only for himself, but for the entire Qi Kingdom, for the Qi royal clan.

The Crown Prince looked around, now either having to explore left or right, or continue deeper, but going deeper would be dangerous.

He looked at the group, all of them were experts, including dozens of elite palace guards.

This team's combat power far surpassed the group the Minister's son had led. Even if they encountered danger, they wouldn't be completely defenseless.

Even if they had no ability to resist, with so many people, the monster probably wouldn't be able to drain them all before being full.

The Crown Prince steeled his resolve and said, "Continue forward in our exploration!

"Yes, Your Highness!"

The eunuch attendant nodded and passed down the order.

Inwardly, he was thinking, "I wonder if the martial dao can make one's roots grow back? Or, without the roots, will it affect cultivation?"

Although he had a physical deficiency, he still had a heart yearning for the martial dao.

The Crown Prince's group of over 300 people, equipped with the finest gear, encountered no real danger as they advanced. Ferocious beasts fled at their approach, and they easily slew any venomous snakes that appeared, the guards' leather armor and snake-repelling ointments keeping them safe.

"Where is the sage?"

The Crown Prince became somewhat dejected.

In the depths of the boundless great mountains, two figures were leisurely strolling, their feet not touching the ground as they moved through the forests.

Suddenly, a roar echoed out, and a spotted giant tiger leapt out.

The one walking in front was a young man in his twenties, who glanced at the spotted tiger and smiled, "This is a Crimson-Eyed Tiger?"

The middle-aged man behind him nodded, "Yes, young master!"

The spotted giant tiger, about to unleash its might, was cowed by the young man's glance, lying obediently on the ground like a docile large cat.

The young man chuckled, looking towards the outside of the boundless great mountains.

"So Blood Heartless really dared to flee, actually going to the remote borderlands, does he think no one would come to the borderlands to kill him?"

The middle-aged man cautiously said, "Young master, do not be careless. Blood Heartless is a sect master of the Demonic Cult, cultivating the rumored Blood Demon Classic that absorbs the essence blood of martial artists.

"His strength has likely recovered, or even increased somewhat."

Xie Lingfeng proudly declared, "Hu Shan, you are also a sect master. Even if Blood Heartless is stronger, with the two of us joining forces, we can certainly slay him!

"Moreover, I, Xie Lingfeng alone, am more than enough to kill him. Sect master martial artists are not something I haven't killed before!"

Hu Shan thought about it and felt there was truth to his words. The young master was one of the youngest and most renowned geniuses of the martial dao in the Inner Realm, his strength far exceeding ordinary martial artists.

And he himself was also a sect master.

Even if the young master alone couldn't kill Blood Heartless, with the two of them, Blood Heartless would undoubtedly die.

"Hu Shan, the reason I must go to the borderlands to slay Blood Heartless is precisely because there are people living there. Otherwise, the borderlands will become a living hell!"

Xie Lingfeng's expression became cold and grave as he said, "Blood Heartless has been in the borderlands for some time now, and he has likely already committed many atrocities."

Hu Shan nodded. Without any powerful experts in the borderlands, even an entire army would be nothing more than cannon fodder to Blood Heartless.

"Let's go, hurry to the borderlands!"

Xie Lingfeng said, soaring into the air and flying out of the boundless great mountains.

Hu Shan followed closely behind.
