In his joyful mood, Li Xuan summoned Shi Er and instructed him for a time.

Shi Er was overjoyed, as getting guidance from the master was a rare opportunity.

To receive guidance from the senior was a great honor.

It had barely been any time at all, yet he had already killed another Sect Master.

After finishing his guidance of Shi Er and Zhou Ying, Li Xuan took out an ancient leather book to calm his mind.

"Could it be that he's killed another Sect Master?"

Another Sect Master dead, but this time it was Meng Chong who killed him, not Li Xuan.

Li Xuan couldn't help but fall into contemplation. Could it be that his two disciples were Sect Master killers?

But the result was, Meng Chong had killed one with a single punch.

"This Sect Master was too weak!"

After waiting a while, with no more golden light appearing, it seemed the opponent had been dealt with, so he breathed a sigh of relief.

"After all, Sect Masters are not just cabbages, how could they be killed one after another in such a short time?"

Li Xuan chuckled wryly.

He focused intently on researching the first page of the ancient leather book, calming his body and mind.

As he continued studying the ancient book, Li Xuan found that whenever he immersed himself in researching it, his body and mind would become tranquil, and he would enter a state of "emptiness".

His mind would only be on the fine lines on the pages of the ancient book.

"This is indeed a good method to calm the body and mind."

He didn't know how long he had been engrossed in studying the ancient book when the golden light appeared again.

"Your disciple Meng Chong consecutively killed three Sect Masters with a single slash, your combat experience has greatly increased."

Li Xuan: ...

So Sect Masters in the inner domain were just like cabbages, huh? Back then, Xie Lingfeng and the others had said that Sect Masters were top experts, rare in the inner domain.

Were they just trying to flatter themselves? Li Xuan closed the ancient book and waited for the next golden light feedback.

But after this time, there were no more golden light feedbacks for the next two days.

"Master, I have already started tempering the Evergreen Bone," Su Ling Xiu happily jogged over.

Behind her, Kou Ruozhi was massaging her shoulders.

"Mm, try to enter the Martial Dao as soon as possible. After breaking through the Qi and Blood Realm, you can then attempt to refine dan pills with your Qi and Blood."

Li Xuan nodded.

"Master, when will my senior brothers return?" Su Ling Xiu missed her two senior brothers a bit.

"Master, do you think my senior brothers will bring back a large amount of spirit medicines?"

She was itching for some spirit medicines.

She even considered having Kou Ruozhi go to the Iron Mountain County early to try to get some spirit stones and buy spirit medicines to send back.

But Kou Ruozhi's strength was too weak, he could easily be tracked and discovered.

And the martial artists in Iron Mountain County, while not strong, the slightly capable ones had all left this barren place.

Li Xuan said cheerfully, "Don't worry, since I promised you, they will definitely bring back spirit medicines."

With so many Sect Masters killed, even if they just looted the corpses, they would be able to obtain a lot of spirit stones. How could they not bring back spirit medicines?

Moreover, Xu Yan was bringing the spirit medicines back for his parents and family.

"I hope my senior brothers come back soon."

Su Ling Xiu's face was full of anticipation.

"You should focus on entering the Martial Dao quickly, otherwise by the time there are spirit medicines, you may not be able to refine dan pills well due to your lack of cultivation, or the failure rate will be too high, wasting them."

Li Xuan reminded her.

Hearing this, Su Ling Xiu's heart tightened. "Master is right, spirit medicines are not like ordinary treasured medicines, refining them is much more difficult. With my current strength, relying only on the Spirit Fire Stone to refine, the control of the fire, the technique, the extraction of the spirit essence, etc. will all be restricted."

"Each spirit medicine is invaluable, the higher the grade, the greater the loss if I fail."

Thinking about this, Su Ling Xiu felt an urgent need.

"Master, within one month, I will definitely enter the Martial Dao!"

Su Ling Xiu said solemnly.

"Don't be too impatient, solid cultivation is the foundation."

Li Xuan instructed.

"Yes, Master!"

Su Ling Xiu respectfully nodded.

After that, Su Ling Xiu reduced her dan pill refining and invested most of her time in cultivation.

The dan pills she refined for her own cultivation were already sufficient.

The tempering of the Evergreen Bone was continuously progressing.

Li Xuan took a look, "You don't even need a month to enter the Martial Dao."

He had a rough estimate of Su Ling Xiu's cultivation.

"I wonder what kind of trouble Xu Yan and Meng Chong have gotten themselves into with the Sect Masters. At this rate, it won't be long before they start provoking the Grand Sect Masters, right?"

Li Xuan sighed inwardly.

The golden light appeared again.

"Your disciple Xu Yan extinguished a Sect Master with a single palm strike, your combat experience has increased."

It had only been a short while, and he had already killed another Sect Master.

Li Xuan was no longer surprised, he was already used to it.

"Your disciple Xu Yan extinguished a Sect Master with a single palm strike, your combat experience has increased."

"Your disciple Xu Yan extinguished a Sect Master with a single palm strike, your combat experience has increased."

He had already killed three Sect Masters.

Li Xuan remained calm, this was not the first time.

"Your disciple Xu Yan slew a Half-Step Grand Sect Master with his sword, your combat experience has greatly increased, and your sword intent has been enhanced."

Li Xuan: ...

Killing Sect Masters was no longer enough, he had started killing Half-Step Grand Sect Masters?

It wouldn't be long before he started killing Grand Sect Masters, right?

After slaying a Half-Step Grand Sect Master, half an incense stick later, the golden light appeared again: "Your disciple Xu Yan slew a Half-Step Grand Sect Master, causing a disturbance in the Thousand Star Lake."
