Li Xuan nodded and spoke.

"The Void Refinement Technique uses the void as the furnace to melt and refine the medicinal pills. This is the foundation of the Dao of Refinement. You must take this as a foundation and go on to comprehend the Dao of Refinement.

"Do not just stay at the level of the Void Refinement Technique. Go and contemplate more, for the Pill can be both medicinal pills and other things..."

Li Xuan said seriously, reminding her to go beyond just the Void Refinement Technique and comprehend stronger refinement techniques.

Su Lingxiu solemnly nodded and said, "Master, I understand. I will not be stuck at just the Void Refinement Technique. I will use it as a foundation to comprehend higher-level refinement techniques, to understand the Dao of Refinement."

Li Xuan nodded approvingly.

"Just like the Medicinal Martial Dao Classic, I already have new ideas. I believe that it won't be long before I can comprehend an even higher-level Medicinal Martial Dao Classic based on this."

Su Lingxiu said excitedly.

Li Xuan became even more pleased. "In that case, it won't be long before I can obtain an even higher-level Medicinal Martial Dao Classic?" He praised, "Well done, your realization pleases me."

After that, Li Xuan passed on the Void Refinement Technique to Su Lingxiu.

After learning the Void Refinement Technique, Su Lingxiu naturally could not immediately put it into practice, as she was only at the initial Innate Realm and still lacked the strength to use the Void Refinement Technique.

However, from the Void Refinement Technique, she also gained some insights into refinement techniques, and her hand-refining technique had improved even further.

The plan to refine the Heavenly Dew Marrow was postponed due to her comprehension of the Void Refinement Technique.

A few days later, Su Lingxiu's refinement technique had improved again, and her confidence in refining the Heavenly Dew Marrow had greatly increased. She was ready to begin the refinement.

"Junior sister, believe in yourself and do your best. Don't be afraid of failure. It's just one spirit herb, and even though it's a fifth-grade one, if we found one, we can find a second and third one."
