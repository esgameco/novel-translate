But for Old Man Xiao, at this moment, he felt like an ant facing the majestic might of heaven!

Even Taicang had never given him such a feeling! And Taicang was the foremost of the seven great world masters, with strength beyond imagination.

Not just Taicang, even that one from the Immutable Divine Temple couldn't give him such a feeling! The person before him had strength that already surpassed Taicang and that one from the Immutable Divine Temple.

Where did such a powerful expert come from? The Immutable Land? Was he related to the Immutable Divine Temple? Old Man Xiao was shocked beyond words, his eyes wide as he stared at the person sitting casually in the chair, even picking up a pastry to taste.

The impression he gave was like that of a pampered young master with beauties attending to him!

But at this moment, Old Man Xiao's heart was trembling. Xu Yan's words came back to him one by one, and now he actually started to believe that what Xu Yan said was true! Not just boasting!

Xu Yan and the others watched as Old Man Xiao's aura surged, his strength incredibly powerful. Even an Immortal Venerable would be like an ant before him.

Just as they were marveling at Old Man Xiao's formidable power, in an instant, that powerful aura dissipated.

Old Man Xiao hadn't even finished speaking when he fell silent.

Then they saw Old Man Xiao standing there dumbfounded, his body trembling slightly, his legs shaking, as if he had been frightened out of his wits! Xu Yan couldn't help but click his tongue, muttering: "Master didn't even do anything to him, why is he shaking? Old Man Xiao's courage seems a bit lacking!"

Meng Chong and the others nodded in agreement!

Master's tone was gentle, and he hadn't even revealed any powerful aura, still sitting there casually. He had merely calmed Old Man Xiao's aura, yet it scared him this much? "This old man, he's strong alright, but his courage is too small!" Su Lingxiu shook her head.

At this moment, Old Man Xiao had no mind to care about Xu Yan and the others' discussion. His heart was trembling as he forced himself to calm down, but for a long while he was unable to control the shock in his mind.

The higher one's realm, the broader one's knowledge, the more deeply one understands how terrifying this is! "Who... who are you?" Old Man Xiao asked in a trembling voice.

"Little friend, be polite, don't panic, just keep a normal state of mind!" Li Xuan's tone was still gentle.

Suddenly, Old Man Xiao felt his shoulder being patted lightly twice.

He still couldn't sense how the other party had patted his shoulder, and more terrifyingly, his trembling heart gradually calmed down.

It was as if those two pats had soothed the fear and unease in his heart.

Too terrifying!

What kind of expert could do this?

Although Old Man Xiao's body had stopped shaking and his mind had calmed down, he still stood there dumbfounded, not knowing how to face this situation.

Li Xuan's face still wore a gentle smile. Calming Old Man Xiao's uneasy emotions was merely a divine ability he had used. With his strength, how could Old Man Xiao resist the influence of his divine ability? Taicang couldn't do it because Taicang's martial arts were inadequate! He didn't have such marvelous and mysterious divine abilities like him!

"You... Senior, are you from the Immutable Land?" Old Man Xiao's tone unconsciously became respectful.

"Yes, and no." Li Xuan smiled and shook his head.

"May I ask how to address Senior?" Old Man Xiao gritted his teeth and asked.

"My name carries too much karmic weight, little friend, you cannot bear it." Li Xuan smiled faintly, his Dao Ancestor halo faintly visible.

"If you must have a way to address me, then you may call me Dao Ancestor."

Dao Ancestor!

It was like thunder roaring in Old Man Xiao's mind, his spirit shaken, unable to calm for a long time!

At this moment, he couldn't help but recall some things Taicang had once said.

The primordial chaos was immutable, heaven and earth were created, all things in the world were within untouchable, supreme rules. Life and death, illusion and reality, all kinds of marvelous and unfathomable abilities were within it.

Just like the martial arts he cultivated, all came from comprehending the mysteries, obtaining great opportunities, and then giving birth to wisdom in the primordial chaos.

He had experienced endless years, but had barely touched this Dao, not yet standing on it, standing firm, or going far! And what Taicang had said in the past was that formless yet omnipresent, mysterious and untouchable Dao.

Old Man Xiao was shocked beyond words. Taicang had not yet stood on that Dao, yet this person called himself the Dao Ancestor!

What an unfathomable existence this was! Old Man Xiao's whole person became dazed. Originally he was curious about Xu Yan's master, wondering who was planning something, to have taught such a monstrous disciple.

But now that he saw him, he was shocked beyond words!

Dao Ancestor?! At this moment, Old Man Xiao was a bit lost, as if his mind had suffered a huge impact. His mind was buzzing with the two words "Dao Ancestor".

Looking at Li Xuan, who was so profound and immeasurable that just glancing at him seemed to reveal the infinite Dao of the world, Old Man Xiao couldn't even raise a thought of doubt.

At this moment, a figure appeared in Old Man Xiao's mind.

Elegant and majestic, gentle yet dignified, as if everything in the world was within his grasp, as if all the martial arts in the world were his footprints.

Taicang!

Even though Taicang was dead, he had allowed the Taicang world to survive for long ages until now. Even that one from the Immutable Divine Temple had not appeared to occupy the Taicang world.

In Old Man Xiao's heart, although Taicang had been defeated, he was still superior to that one from the Immutable Divine Temple.

It was just that Taicang lacked time.

How could that dark, invisible figure in the primordial chaos compare to Taicang?

However, today, Taicang's invincibility, Taicang's majesty, received a strong impact in his heart! He had seen an existence stronger than Taicang, and much, much stronger.

It was as if all the Daos in the world were his footprints, and he called himself the Dao Ancestor!

For a moment, Old Man Xiao was unable to bear this huge impact. He felt as if his faith was collapsing, his whole person dazed.

"I want to be alone for a while," Old Man Xiao said dazedly as he turned to leave.

Xu Yan looked surprised and was about to call out to Old Man Xiao to ask what was wrong.

But Li Xuan waved his hand and said, "Let this little friend go calm down by himself."

Watching Old Man Xiao leave, Li Xuan smiled, his Dao Ancestor halo receding, his whole person returning to an ordinary, unremarkable appearance.

He understood Old Man Xiao's situation. It was nothing more than encountering someone stronger, more majestic, and more unfathomable than the one in his beliefs, and being unable to accept it for a moment.

Moreover, the person in his beliefs had a profound connection with him.

"Taicang, huh." Li Xuan sighed inwardly. Although Taicang was dead, his strength still became the belief of a martial artist like this.

Looking up at the sky, this world was opened up by Taicang.

And the Heaven Purple was formed from the remaining purple light when opening up the world.

"If he hadn't created the Heaven Purple, Taicang might have been even stronger," Li Xuan thought silently.

That ray of purple light contained great creative power.

The masters of the seven great worlds all obtained the creative power of the purple light and opened up worlds. Those masters of smaller worlds also obtained some purple light, just less of it.

The dragon calamity had caused quite a disturbance in the Divine Realm.

Although behind this was the plot of Tianshadiying, and Ao Yuxue was also a victim, she had indeed caused considerable trouble.

Ao Yuxue, a young dragon who had never experienced hardship, couldn't withstand this kind of situation. Naturally, she fell into it, her will collapsed, and she was easily influenced and lost her reason.

Her vigilance was also insufficient. After all, before this, Ao Yuxue had been a dragon in a greenhouse, never experiencing danger or harsh lessons!
