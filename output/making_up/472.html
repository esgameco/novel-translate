Li Xuan raised his hand, pointing at Tianzi, then at the sky, then at the ground, and said with a smile: "You are a spirit of heaven and earth, transformed from purple light!"

Upon hearing this, Tianzi was shocked, but at the same time no longer doubted Li Xuan's power. In fact, he even felt relieved.

Since Li Xuan knew his identity but hadn't done anything, it was clear he had no ill intentions.

Only a handful of people knew Tianzi's identity as a spirit of heaven and earth.

And even fewer knew he was transformed from purple light.

"So you really did know, senior. I was overthinking things," Tianzi said with a wry smile.

"You seem to have quite a story, young friend. Why don't you tell me about it?" Li Xuan took the opportunity to ask with a smile.

Tianzi was silent for a moment, then raised his hand to point at the sky and said: "Beyond heaven and earth is a place of primordial chaos that has not yet taken form. Senior, do you call it 'hundun' (chaos)?"

Li Xuan smiled and nodded, then shook his head. "Yes, and no!"

Beyond heaven and earth was indeed a state of primordial chaos, filled with unformed qi. Li Xuan's first thought was to call it "hundun", which is why he had used that term.

However, the fact that Tianzi asked this question meant they clearly didn't call it "hundun".

Li Xuan naturally didn't want to completely contradict what he had said before, but he also didn't want to entirely negate Tianzi's terminology. So he gave an answer that seemed to contain hidden meaning.

Some things shouldn't be stated too definitively, or there would be no room for maneuver. Li Xuan was already very skilled at this.

Tianzi didn't ask why it was both yes and no. Instead, his expression became more serious as he said in a complex tone tinged with reminiscence: "The primordial chaos beyond the heavens, Tai Cang and the others call it the 'Unformed Realm', or the 'Realm of Primordial Chaos'. In the past, the powerful beings within heaven and earth all called it the 'Outer Unformed Realm'...

"The Unformed Realm seems to exist eternally, inhabited by primal spirits. But they are like beasts - although they have great abilities and even innate talents, they use them crudely, without knowledge of cultivation, relying only on their own growth.

"No one knows when the beings of the Unformed Realm first came into existence, or how long they have existed. Even one as powerful as Tai Cang cannot trace back to see the ultimate origin.

"Of course, senior must know. If it were the past me, I would have curiously pestered you to explain everything clearly. But now, I no longer have such curiosity."

Towards the end, Tianzi's expression became somewhat melancholy.

Li Xuan listened silently, also feeling some emotion. The primordial chaos before heaven and earth was not too different from the "hundun" he knew.

He poured a cup of tea for Tianzi, acting as a listener.

As expected, once Tianzi started talking about these past events, he couldn't stop. He would continue on, telling the whole story.

Just as anticipated, after taking a sip of tea, Tianzi continued:

"Tai Cang was born in the Unformed Realm. According to Tai Cang, he doesn't know how he came into being, or what kind of being he was. At first he was in a daze, like those primal spirits of the Unformed Realm, his intelligence not yet awakened.

"Of course, at that time in the Unformed Realm, primal spirits were numerous. It was survival of the fittest - weak spirits were in constant danger of being eaten.

"Tai Cang said he witnessed the brutal slaughter of primal spirits. For some unknown reason, his intelligence gradually awakened and he slowly became clever. Moreover, his physical form gradually changed into the human-like shape he has now.

"By observing the battles between various primal spirits, both strong and weak, he gradually figured out how to hide, how to dodge, how to efficiently hunt primal spirits..."

Tianzi was slowly narrating the story.

The protagonist of this story was called Tai Cang.

From Tianzi's narration, it was clear he had an extremely close relationship with Tai Cang. Obviously, these events were not personally experienced by Tianzi, but were told to him by Tai Cang.

Li Xuan thought of Tianzi's origins - a spirit of Tai Cang's heaven and earth, transformed from the purple light of creation. Clearly, his birth was related to Tai Cang.

In fact, Tai Cang had likely created him.

"Tai Cang said he didn't know how large the Unformed Realm was. He had no fixed abode, wandering the Unformed Realm. He also encountered dangers. At that time, he had no concept of the passage of time or sense of time. He just drifted along, and because he had intelligence, he felt proud when facing other primal spirits.

"Later on, Tai Cang even stopped viewing himself as the same kind of being as those primal spirits, feeling he was a unique existence.

"After wandering for who knows how long, Tai Cang saw an immensely powerful primal spirit. But this spirit was already old, seemingly near death.

"Tai Cang observed this spirit until it died of old age, its corpse discovered and completely devoured by other primal spirits.

"Tai Cang said that scene shocked him greatly. It turned out that even if not killed, beings would still die of old age. Although he didn't know how long that spirit had lived, it had ultimately died.

"From that moment on, Tai Cang felt he should have some pursuit..."

At this point, Tianzi paused and looked at Li Xuan.

"Senior, do you know what Tai Cang's pursuit was?"

Li Xuan nodded slightly. He had already guessed some of it.

"You said Tai Cang was a very gifted young one. Tai Cang's talent is unmatched by anyone I know."

Tianzi let out a breath and said solemnly: "Tai Cang was the first cultivator in the Unformed Realm. His talent was extraordinary, and he pioneered the way of cultivation.

"Tai Cang cultivated in the Unformed Realm, absorbing the violent spiritual energy to practice, constantly exploring and improving.

"Just like that, after who knows how many years, Tai Cang became powerful. Those once-formidable primal spirits he couldn't match before, he could now easily hunt.

"As his cultivation deepened and he grew stronger, the range Tai Cang could traverse in the Unformed Realm also expanded. He could hunt increasingly powerful primal spirits.

"Until one day, he encountered primal spirits that also possessed intelligence..."

Tianzi was narrating Tai Cang's story, from his birth to his rise to power. Naturally, all these events had been told to Tianzi by Tai Cang himself.

Li Xuan listened silently, feeling more and more amazed. Tai Cang was truly legendary.

From the time Tai Cang encountered the first intelligent primal spirit, the entire Unformed Realm began to change. Intelligent beings started appearing more and more frequently.

These intelligent beings were constantly growing stronger. They were all influenced by Tai Cang, such as preferring to take human form.

For instance, they also figured out their own cultivation methods from Tai Cang's way of cultivation.

If one were to trace the origin of cultivation methods among the beings of the Unformed Realm, it seemed to start with Tai Cang. They were all influenced by him.

Tai Cang guided many beings in cultivation, and because of this, he gathered a group of followers around him.

From the beginning, when Tai Cang and the others were not the strongest in the Unformed Realm, to gradually reaching the pinnacle, there were very, very few primal spirits that could contend with them.

And besides Tai Cang, other extraordinarily gifted beings also appeared in the Unformed Realm.

"The Unformed Realm was chaotic, without heaven and earth. There wasn't even the concept of heaven and earth. Tai Cang and the others all lived in the Unformed Realm, wandering through it.

The appearance of Heaven and Earth was related to his birth.

Whether the purple light truly appeared from the edge of the Unformed Realm is now impossible to verify, but at the time, Taicang and others felt that the purple light seemed to appear from the edge.

The purple light illuminated the chaos, as if splitting the Unformed Realm in two, stretching across the entire realm. This was the first time the Unformed Realm was illuminated by light.

The appearance of the purple light also meant that Heaven and Earth were about to be born.

Everyone gained opportunities from the purple light, but how to use it was unknown.

Besides Taicang's realm, there were six other realms.

Taicang's strength was undoubtedly first, he had always been the leader.

The primordial spirits from outside the realms entered and gradually developed intelligence. Thus, spirits were divided into those of Heaven and Earth, and those of the Unformed Realm.

In fact, after the birth of Heaven and Earth, the term 'spirit' specifically referred to those with intelligence. 'Unformed spirits' referred to those without intelligence, meaning spirits that had not yet been enlightened.

The human race was also born at this time. All beings liked to exist in human form, which was influenced by Taicang.

Taicang taught the martial way, and cultivators appeared in the realms. The seven great realms interacted and learned from each other, with each realm seeking suitable places for cultivation.

Everything was harmonious and thriving. Taicang and the other six were the rulers of the seven great realms, with Taicang being supreme, worshipped by all cultivators.

The realms now had time, a record of years. I can't remember how many years passed since the creation of Heaven and Earth.
