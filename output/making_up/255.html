To lay the foundation for going beyond spiritual powers, using the laws of heaven and earth as his own.

"The Martial Dao Spiritual Shadows are about to break through the hundred thousand mark."

Li Xuan suddenly discovered that the Martial Dao Spiritual Shadows were just one away from breaking through the hundred thousand mark.

The Great Desolate martial artists were becoming more and more numerous, truly entering a stage of rapid development.

Finally, after a few days, the Spiritual Shadows broke through the hundred thousand mark.

"Your Martial Dao Spiritual Shadows have broken through the hundred thousand, and you have obtained the Divine Martial True Body."

Li Xuan was delighted. The Divine Martial True Body was formed by the condensation of a hundred thousand Spiritual Shadows. It was a manifestation, yet not like a manifestation. The Divine Martial True Body was connected to his main consciousness and was completely under his control.

More importantly, the Divine Martial True Body possessed nine-tenths of his own strength! "This is much stronger than the Spiritual Shadows. If the enemy is on par with me, but I have the Divine Martial True Body, it would be like two against one, and I would surely win!"

His strength had increased again.

Li Xuan was in an excellent mood.

"Your disciple Meng Chong has broken through the first minor success of the Indestructible Golden Body, and your Indestructible Golden Body has reached the first stage of perfection."

Meng Chong had broken through.

Soon after Meng Chong's breakthrough, Su Ling Xiu also broke through to the minor success of the Divine Consciousness Realm.

"Your disciple Su Ling Xiu has broken through to the minor success of the Divine Consciousness Realm, and your Pill Dao and Martial Dao Divine Consciousness Realm is now perfected."

The feeling of strength increasing was truly wonderful.

Among the four disciples, the weakest one was currently Fang Hao, but he was also about to break through.

Li Xuan summoned Fang Hao and pointed out to him things like treasures to be refined for convenient communication and transmission.

The spiritual domain communication techniques were relatively backward.

Although the Mother-Child Pearls could transmit some information, it was rather simple, like which child pearl was broken, representing what.

As for more detailed communication methods, they could only use a spiritual swallow, similar to the flying messenger of the inner domain. However, this communication method still had the risk of interception.

Although the spiritual swallow was fast, it was not instant, especially for cross-state communication, which might take several days.

Even within a single state, from south to north, although the spiritual swallow was fast, it would still take more than a day.

There was also the risk of being intercepted along the way.

The cost of cultivating the spiritual swallow was also not low. Constructing a communication system was very resource-consuming.

Even second- or third-rate Spiritual Sects and Aristocratic Families could not establish too extensive a communication system, only able to use it within their own territories.

The Mother-Child Pearls were used by Spiritual Sects and Aristocratic Families for emergency communication, such as when a pearl was broken, indicating a crisis or a call for help.

Since there were already refinements of talismans, formations, and prohibitions, how could communication treasures be lacking?

So Li Xuan pointed out to Fang Hao to refine communication-related items, so that they could communicate conveniently.

Even if they could not achieve instant communication throughout the Spiritual Domain, at least they should have the ability to communicate within a certain range.

After Li Xuan's guidance, Fang Hao was delighted and began to ponder the refinement of communication treasures.

Outside the Blue Scaled City in the Jade State,

"You two are also scattered cultivators, yet you are willing to serve the Spiritual Sects and Aristocratic Families? Do you really think the Spiritual Sects and Aristocratic Families will treat you as their own?"

Yu, the Alliance Master, said solemnly.

In front of the three of them were two Cultivated Spirit scattered cultivators, who were also somewhat renowned in the Jade State. However, during the Jade Divine Sect and the Su Family's search for Xu Yan and Meng Chong, they had recruited these two.

Now, the two of them were the Foreign Affairs Elders of the Jade Divine Sect! After discovering the Eternal Alliance, an organization of scattered cultivators, the Jade Divine Sect gave them a chance to prove their loyalty, and if they succeeded, they would truly become Elders of the Spiritual Sect, Martial Cultivators of the Spiritual Sects.

So, the two of them had apprehended members of the Eternal Alliance, which led to Yu, the Alliance Master, appearing to intercept them.

"Scattered cultivators have no future, the Spiritual Sects and Aristocratic Families are the right path. This Spiritual Domain is just like that, don't delude yourself that you can change the situation of the Spiritual Domain."

One of the Cultivated Spirit scattered cultivators said coldly.

"It is precisely because of people like you that we scattered cultivators can never rise up!"

The left protector said fiercely.

"Nonsense!"

The Cultivated Spirit scattered cultivator sneered, "How mighty was the Blood Demon in the past, and how did it end up? Do you think you can match the Blood Demon of the past?"

"You're the one talking nonsense! If it weren't for the betrayal of some people, how could the Blood Demon have changed its nature? How could it not have secured a place for us scattered cultivators?"

The right protector roared and rushed forward.

"Alliance Master, these scattered cultivator scums should be killed!"

As the right protector struck, the scattered cultivator's face changed drastically.

"Cultivated Spirit mid-stage, how is this possible!"

He was shocked in his heart and was about to flee.

Scattered cultivators, due to the lack of cultivation resources and even mediocre cultivation techniques, were mostly stuck at the initial stage of Cultivated Spirit, even after breaking through.

Their strength was generally weaker than the Spiritual Sects and Aristocratic Families' Cultivated Spirits.

A Cultivated Spirit mid-stage scattered cultivator was truly extraordinary, perhaps even inheriting some legacies.

Suddenly, a white light flashed, and the right protector's expression changed drastically as he frantically retreated.

Alliance Master Yu punched out, and with a thunderous sound, it collided with the white light, causing his figure to sway slightly.

At this moment, his expression was grave as he looked at the two figures in front of him.

"Jade Divine Sect Grand Elder, the Number One Expert of the Jade State, Zhu Liang!"

The Jade Divine Sect Grand Elder, Zhu Liang, had white light surging around him, his spiritual power undulating like ripples in the air.

He looked coldly at the Alliance Master.

"Are Xu Yan and Meng Chong your people?"

A scattered cultivator actually had the strength of the Cultivated Spirit late-stage, which was quite remarkable!

It seemed this scattered cultivator organization had grand ambitions.

"So what if they are? And so what if they aren't?"

The Alliance Master sneered.
