After these negotiations, the Changqingge officially entered the Tianbaoge, but was not swallowed up by it. The Changqingge retained full autonomy over its own affairs, with the Tianbaoge having no right to intervene.

As the Changqingge continued to grow stronger, there might come a day when it could completely control the Tianbaoge, becoming the hidden power behind it.

After reaching the agreement with the Tianbaoge, the Changqingge entered a phase of rapid expansion and development. Its pill refining facilities also began expansion, increasing production lines.

The pill refining apprentices were all from the Great Wilderness, carefully selected outstanding talents.

In addition to the Essence Gathering Pill, the Blood Qi Pill and Healing Pill also began to be sold.

The appearance of the Blood Qi Pill provided great assistance to the cultivation of martial artists below the First Rank, and quickly became highly sought after.

The Great Wilderness had also entered a high-speed development stage. While there were still less than a hundred martial artists who had broken through to the Blood Qi Realm, there were many who had reached the stages of Skin Refining, Bone Refining, and even Organ Refining.

It would not be long before the martial artists of the Great Wilderness entered a period of explosive growth.

However, the growth of Innate Realm martial artists could not be accelerated quickly. Not all martial artists could frequently use medicinal pills to assist their cultivation.

Most martial artists only used medicinal pills when they encountered bottlenecks in their cultivation.

Iron Mountain County had gradually seen the emergence of young people cultivating the Great Wilderness Martial Dao, and the Changqingge had also begun to recruit those with exceptional talents, training them as personnel for external expansion, as well as recruiting martial artists from the Inner Realm as auxiliary members.

The concepts of the Great Wilderness were constantly being instilled in the people of Iron Mountain County, to the point that they almost completely disregarded the Great Yue Kingdom, seeing the Great Wilderness as the true orthodox way.

The Great Wilderness Martial Patriarch had even become the martial faith that these martial artists aspired to, everyone wishing to become a figure like the Great Wilderness Martial Patriarch.

Under Kou Ruozhi's planning, this trend was spreading from Iron Mountain County to the surrounding Lanping Prefecture, gradually taking root in people's hearts.

There were even rumors that by worshipping the Great Wilderness Martial Patriarch, one's martial cultivation would be easier, with the chance to gain the Patriarch's favor and enhance one's martial dao fortune.

These rumors spoke of how certain individuals, after struggling in their cultivation of the Great Wilderness Martial Dao, only experienced a sudden enlightenment and rapid progress after worshipping the Great Wilderness Martial Patriarch.

As a result, the young men and women who began cultivating the Great Wilderness Martial Dao all started worshipping the Great Wilderness Martial Patriarch.

Li Xuan couldn't help but sigh, admitting that Kou Ruozhi really had a knack for this, no wonder he was able to revive and widely spread the teachings of the Heavenly Mother Sect back then.

Xu Yan had begun preparing for a breakthrough to the Profound Realm.

And on this day, news finally came from the royal court of the Great Yue Kingdom.

Lanping Prefecture and the portion of Yunfeng Prefecture bordering the Canglang River where Canglang Island was located would all be ceded to the Changqingge as compensation for the previous conflict.

At the same time, through the Tianbaoge's channels, 10,000 spiritual herbs were delivered.

Effective immediately, the entire area from Lanping Prefecture to Canglang Island, spanning half a prefecture, would be under the control of the Changqingge, with the Great Yue Kingdom relinquishing its governing authority and agreeing to assist the Changqingge in fully taking control of this region.

How to receive and control this half-prefecture area would naturally be handled by Xu Junhe and Kou Ruozhi, after they had thoroughly investigated the situation in Lanping Prefecture and that portion of Yunfeng Prefecture.

They quickly formulated a plan to consolidate control:

First, Lanping Prefecture and that half of Yunfeng Prefecture would be merged into the Great Wilderness, accepting the governance of the Great Wilderness and establishing a Great Wilderness Cabinet to manage its affairs.

To rally the people's hearts, it was announced that all martial artists within the Great Wilderness domain would enjoy a 30% discount on medicinal pills, and those who had made contributions to stabilizing the Great Wilderness would be rewarded with a bottle of Essence Gathering Pill, worth 12 pills.

At the same time, the establishment of the Great Wilderness Guard was announced, responsible for stabilizing and patrolling the Great Wilderness. Those who joined the Great Wilderness Guard would receive a Blood Qi Pill every month, and Grandmaster-level martial artists would receive two Essence Gathering Pills per month. They would also be granted the inheritance of the Great Wilderness Martial Dao, including the method for refining true qi for Grandmaster-level martial artists.

With this series of measures, the situation was quickly stabilized, and the Great Wilderness Guard was successfully established.

Of course, the consumption of medicinal pills would also be tremendous...
