Li Xuan was still sitting in the chair, looking at Xie Tianheng with a strange expression. This Xie Lingfeng's father was truly formidable!

Xie Tianheng drew his sword, his gaze resolute and solemn: "I, Xie Tianheng, have pursued the ultimate in the Dao of the Sword all my life. I believe that a true sword cultivator must dare to draw their sword against the strong, and dare to break through their limits!"

Li Xuan nodded inwardly. Xie Tianheng was a man with an unyielding spirit, and he also had remarkable talent in the Dao of the Sword. He was an arrogant and proud person.

Otherwise, he would not have so casually disregarded the powerful experts of the Fatian Alliance.

Xie Tianheng spoke with an arrogant air.

Li Xuan became curious. What had Xie Tianheng comprehended in the Dao of the Sword that could be even stronger than the Sword Dao he had created? How could he claim to be the first in history to open a new path in the Dao of the Sword?

"Then I shall see how you can claim to be the first in history in the Dao of the Sword."

Li Xuan chuckled.

"I will not disappoint you!" Xie Tianheng said solemnly.

"Then begin." Li Xuan remained seated, not even bothering to stand up.

Courage was admirable, to display his sword Dao before him.

Boom! As Xie Tianheng raised his sword, a fierce sword intent burst forth from him.

The sword intent grew stronger and stronger, shaking heaven and earth, converging on the long sword. In this moment, Xie Tianheng was like a towering sword standing between heaven and earth.

The ruthless and murderous sword intent gathered, like a raging sword storm!

Outside Canglan Island, the anxious Xie Lingfeng and the worried experts of the Cliff Sword Sect were all stunned.

What kind of sword Dao was this? A sword intent seemed to stand tall between heaven and earth, fierce and unyielding. Even Half-Step Celestials could not budge it in the slightest!

"What powerful sword Dao has the Sword Master comprehended?"

"Open a new path in the Dao of the Sword? No wonder the Sword Master has become so arrogant!"

The experts of the Cliff Sword Sect were all dazed.

Li Xuan looked at Xie Tianheng with some surprise. This Sword Master of the Cliff Sword Sect truly had remarkable talent in the Dao of the Sword, surpassing even Xie Lingfeng.

Apart from Xu Yan, Xie Tianheng had the greatest talent in the Dao of the Sword.

And Xie Tianheng was not just a Half-Step Celestial, but a peak Grandmaster.

With this fierce and powerful sword intent, he could easily disregard the pressure of a Half-Step Celestial.

No wonder Xie Tianheng had become so arrogant.

"I, Xie Tianheng, have comprehended the sword intent. Using intent as the sword, and the sword as the intent. This is a path in the Dao of the Sword that has never been seen before in history. I, Xie Tianheng, am the first to open a new path in the Dao of the Sword. Today, I dare to ask the senior for guidance!"

Xie Tianheng spoke with an arrogant air, as if he was the unrivaled master under the heavens.

Li Xuan smiled. Xie Tianheng had not comprehended sword will or sword heart, but he had comprehended sword intent. This sword intent was vast and fierce.

At this moment, Xie Tianheng was like a towering sword intent standing between heaven and earth.

Li Xuan sighed inwardly. If he had not appeared and created an even stronger Sword Dao, and if Xu Yan had not comprehended sword will, Xie Tianheng would truly be the first to open a new path in the Dao of the Sword.

At least, the first in the Inner Realm.

However, although the sword intent was powerful, in Li Xuan's eyes, it was still too dispersed and weak. It had intent but lacked will. It was ultimately just a powerful sword technique, not true mastery of the Dao of the Sword.

Xu Yan had comprehended sword will, and thus naturally understood sword intent. He just felt it was too weak, and thus directly cultivated an even stronger sword will.

Sword intent was not truly part of the Dao of the Sword in Li Xuan's view, but a powerful sword art.

"Although your sword intent is vast, it is still too ethereal." Li Xuan shook his head and waved his hand, condensing the spiritual energy of heaven and earth into a sword.

"In my eyes, you have not yet entered the Dao of the Sword, but you are indeed talented, surpassing many sword cultivators.

"This is the sword will. Only one who has comprehended sword will and sword heart can truly enter the Dao of the Sword and comprehend sword will.

"Try this sword and see how much you can comprehend."

Li Xuan said, waving his hand to send the sword of sword will towards Xie Tianheng.

The vast sword will of the Heavenly Sword burst forth.

"Sword will?" Xie Tianheng's brow furrowed. He struck down with his sword, and his powerful sword intent condensed into a giant sword, seemingly able to cleave anything.

However, as it came into contact with the sword will, it was like oil hitting a burning iron, easily broken through with a sizzling sound.

"Impossible!" Xie Tianheng was greatly shocked.

"Strike!" In that instant, Xie Tianheng's sword intent became even more violent, unleashing the myriad river sword techniques, his sword intent surging like a vast river.

Yet, it was utterly powerless against that sword.

He had been so confident that his sword intent could defy past and present, yet it now seemed so weak and incapable.

"Sword will?!" In this moment, Xie Tianheng was somewhat lost.

The more he struck, the more he felt the power and profundity of sword will. His sword intent seemed to be controlled by the sword will, having no offensive power at all.

It was as if one who had mastered sword will could easily subdue those sword cultivators without comprehending sword will.

Xie Tianheng was unwilling. The sword intent he had painstakingly cultivated through seclusion was so powerless? He struck with all his might, his sword intent growing more and more terrifying, its aura surpassing even that of a Half-Step Celestial.

"Although your sword intent is powerful, it is still just like a gust of wind. A strong wind can topple a tree or blow away a house, but it cannot move a huge boulder.

"Sword will, however, can make that boulder crumble to dust.

"You have been able to comprehend sword intent, which is already remarkable. If you can condense intent into will, and comprehend sword will, then..."
