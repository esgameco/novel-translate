Chapter 122: Fierce Battle with a Half-Step Grand Master, the Dragon's Roar in the Mountains and Rivers

A refined gentleman descended from the sky, his aura like a deep pool, as if he was accompanied by the might of the heavens.

Floating three feet off the ground, his footsteps did not touch the earth, and he gently waved his folding fan, refined and easygoing. Dou Yuying and the others were all shocked.

A Grand Master?

Xu Yan's expression also changed, his face becoming grave.

"A Grand Master? No, that's not quite right, it's because he's too weak? A trash Grand Master?"

The man in the purple robe had the aura of a Grand Master, but Xu Yan, who had fought against a Grand Master before, quickly noticed something was off.

"This Grand Master-like aura of heavenly might is much weaker, although it is still powerful. It is not impossible to defeat him, but it won't be easy either.

"Could this be a trash Grand Master? Is there really such a weak Grand Master?"

Xu Yan's expression was grave, but he was also puzzled.

Could it be that among the Grand Masters, there are some this weak?

"Even the weakest Grand Master would have ten times the strength of a Master, but this one is only about five or six times stronger than a Master. He is far from having ten times the power.

"Even compared to the weakest Master, like Yan Kuan, he does not have ten times the strength."

Xu Yan let out a sigh of relief.

The opponent was not undefeatable.

He was just puzzled as to why this Grand Master was so trash-like.

Dou Yuying stepped out of the carriage, looking at the man in the purple robe, her brows slightly furrowed, and after a long while, she said, "Lord Yin Hong of Canglan Island?"

"Indeed!"

Yin Hong smiled calmly. "Since Miss Dou knows me, then you should obediently come with me."

Yan Kuan came out of the carriage, his face deathly pale. His injuries had not fully recovered, and in his current state, he could still handle a First-Order Warrior.

But if Yin Hong attacked, he would be dead in an instant.

"Lord Yin Hong, do you dare to interfere in the affairs of the Dou family? Beware that after this matter is resolved, the Grand Master of the Dou family will come to demand justice!"

Yan Kuan said solemnly.

"Since I, Yin, have made a move, naturally I do not fear the Grand Master of the Dou family. Moreover, I am taking Miss Dou away on the request of an esteemed person. I naturally have the means to handle this matter.

"Otherwise, how would I, Yin Hong, dare to set foot here and take the young miss of the Dou family?"

Yin Hong smiled calmly.

"Lord Yin, you must think carefully. You are only a Half-Step Grand Master. Even if you eventually break through to become a Grand Master, do you think you can withstand the wrath of the master of the Dou family?"

Yan Kuan glared angrily.

Yin Hong had entered the Dou family's territory and was kidnapping the young miss of the Dou family. With the master's temperament, even if someone tried to mediate, he would not let it go easily.

Yin Hong's expression darkened as he coldly said, "You, a third-rate Master, what qualifications do you have to spout such nonsense in front of me? Do you wish to die?"

"You!"

Yan Kuan's face turned iron-blue with anger. If it were an ordinary Master who insulted him like this, he would definitely have a seventh-grade spirit herb prepared and ask Xu Yan to take the person down to vent his anger.

But this was a Half-Step Grand Master.

He was only one step away from becoming a Grand Master, and Xu Yan was ultimately just a Master-level warrior.

No matter how strong he was, he might not be able to handle this.

Yin Hong's aura became increasingly fierce as he coldly said, "Miss Dou, I don't want to kill anyone. Obediently come with me, or else... even the guardian you've summoned will die here!"

Dou Yuying's heart sank, and she took a deep breath, saying, "Alright, I'll go with you!"

"Young miss!"

Cui'er immediately broke down in tears.

"Cui'er, remember to deliver the owed spirit herbs to this young man."

Dou Yuying instructed.

"Yes, young miss!"

Cui'er was in tears.

"It's best to be sensible and not ruin the harmony between us."

Yin Hong chuckled lightly.

He was about to take Dou Yuying away when a voice suddenly came, which he found extremely humiliating to hear.

"You scared me for a moment, I thought you were a Grand Master, but it turns out you're just a Half-Step Grand Master, a half-baked one at that!"

Xu Yan had a look of sudden realization.

He had been wondering why this Grand Master was so weak, far below his understanding of Grand Masters.

It turns out he was just a half-baked one, with only part of a Grand Master's aura, but not a true Grand Master.

In that case, he need not fear him!

After being in the inner realm for so long, he had killed one Master after another, easily dispatching them with a single palm strike, without even needing to use his true strength.

This Yin Hong was a rare opponent.

"I have comprehended the true meaning of the Dragon's Roar, and my Sword of the Mountains and Rivers is not yet complete. Just as the mountains and rivers cannot be stagnant, I already have a direction to perfect it.

"I just need an opponent who can withstand my sword intent, battle with me, and surely I can perfect and gain a deeper understanding of a sword dao based on sword intent!"

At this moment, Xu Yan was inwardly excited.

The refined expression on Yin Hong's face suddenly turned cold. What did he mean by calling him a half-baked Grand Master who couldn't break through in one go?

Was this an indirect insult, calling him a waste?

Being stuck at the Half-Step Grand Master realm was something he had always been resentful about in his heart.

Now being mocked so directly, how could he not be enraged?

In an instant, his killing intent was palpable.

"Young man, you are courting death!"

Yin Hong opened his folding fan, and a mighty aura descended, with layer upon layer of surging power emanating from him, accompanied by the sound of crashing waves.

Dou Yuying's expression changed drastically.

Xu Yan was eager to try, but he still didn't forget that this was Dou Yuying's enemy, so he couldn't just jump in for free.

"Miss Dou, I'll help you fend him off. This half-baked one is not weak, much stronger than an ordinary Master. One seventh-grade spirit herb is not enough!"

Xu Yan said earnestly.

Dou Yuying was overjoyed. Since Xu Yan was willing to step in, it meant he could contend with a Half-Step Grand Master.

It made sense, as he could easily kill a Master with a single palm strike, so he was probably not much weaker than a Half-Step Grand Master.

"Five seventh-grade spirit herbs!"

Dou Yuying happily extended a delicate white hand.

"Okay, no problem!"

Xu Yan was delighted.

Another five seventh-grade spirit herbs, obtaining these herbs was not that difficult.

"Good, good, today, besides Miss Dou, all of you will die!"

Yin Hong was enraged.

They were openly negotiating and trading five seventh-grade spirit herbs to deal with him?

Did they think Yin Hong was so cheap?

If it weren't for the influential person behind him, who wanted Dou Yuying brought back unharmed, he wouldn't even spare Dou Yuying!

"Who are you trying to scare?"

Xu Yan was unperturbed.

As long as it wasn't a Grand Master, he had no fear!

Even if it was a Grand Master, if he couldn't defeat them, he could still escape.

Dou Yuying and the others quickly retreated, afraid of being caught in the crossfire.

Boom!

Yin Hong made his move, waving his fan, and a terrifying power like surging waves came sweeping towards them, its might shocking.

Xu Yan drew his sword, and the sword intent of the Mountains and Rivers surged forth, thundering as it cleaved through the rolling waves, overthrowing the mountains and rivers, annihilating all life!

Yin Hong's expression changed drastically!

What kind of sword technique was this?

Why did it have such a terrifying aura of slaughter, making him feel a chill down his spine.

"Cannot be careless!"

In that instant, he put aside his contempt, his expression becoming grave.
