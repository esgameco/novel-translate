"The Azure Sea has a Sword King? Where is he, and how powerful is he?"

Since he claimed to be the Sword God, the existence of a Sword King in the Azure Sea piqued his curiosity. He wondered how the other's sword dao would compare, though he presumed it would be roughly on par with the Thousand Sword Palace.

The woman was certain that this person was not the legendary Sword King, and she was greatly shocked. "When did the Azure Sea produce such a powerful sword cultivator?"

"The Sword King is in Yuntiancheng. He single-handedly defeated all the sword cultivators of the Azure Sea, sweeping through the sword dao of Yuntiancheng. "It is rumored that the Sword King is a very arrogant person. Although he says his sword dao has just begun, barely entering the door of the sword dao and not yet comprehending the true essence of the sword dao, in his eyes all the sword cultivators of Yuntiancheng are trash."

Xu Yan listened and couldn't help but feel this style was familiar.

He couldn't help but ask, "What is the name of this Sword King you speak of?"

The woman felt a bit embarrassed, "I only know him as the Sword King, but I don't know his actual name."

Just then, one of the escaped Refining Spirit cultivators, who was stronger than the others, was surrounded and about to be killed by the Blood Knife Bandits. He was only able to relax after Xu Yan stepped in and eliminated the bandits.

This cultivator then walked over and said, "The Sword King's name is Xie Tianheng!"

Xu Yan suddenly understood, no wonder the style was so familiar - it was Xie Tianheng! "So Xie Tianheng has even earned the title of Sword King."

Xu Yan sighed inwardly, it seems that in the past few years Xie Tianheng's strength has risen rapidly, likely reaching the ranks of the Supremely Powerful.

"Have you heard of Xie Lingfeng?" Xu Yan asked.

Since Xie Tianheng had achieved the title of Sword King, Xie Lingfeng, who also had exceptional talent, should have also carved out a reputation for himself in the sword dao.

"Is he not the son of the Sword King?" the cultivator asked thoughtfully.

Xu Yan nodded.

"The son of the Sword King was once a genius of Yuntiancheng, but alas, these past few years he has made no progress, and has even been surpassed by those he once defeated. His reputation as a genius has been tarnished, and he has long since faded into obscurity," the cultivator sighed.

"The fall of a genius, the son of the Sword King, has even been mocked as a waste," he lamented.

Xu Yan clicked his tongue. So Xie Lingfeng had fallen so far?

It was understandable. Xie Lingfeng had exceptional talent, and should have already perfected his Spirit Sense Realm cultivation. With that foundation, breaking through to the Refining Spirit Realm should not have been a problem.

But he had been unable to break through, while his former subordinates continued to advance, reaching the late and peak Refining Spirit Realm, naturally surpassing Xie Lingfeng.

No wonder he had faded into obscurity, and was even mocked as a waste.

"Brother Xie, just wait a little longer. I will come find you, and you will surely rise again, surpassing those who once served under you," Xu Yan muttered to himself.

It seemed the father and son of the Xie family had made quite a name for themselves in the Azure Sea Yuntiancheng, rather than staying low-key.

Xu Yan asked the cultivator for more information about the Xie father and son. The cultivator only knew that a few years ago, Xie Tianheng first made a name for himself by challenging a Refining Spirit Realm cultivator. At the time, Xie Tianheng was at the Great Celestial Realm, and everyone thought he would surely lose. But Xie Tianheng rose up powerfully, displaying extraordinary sword dao and defeating his opponent across realms. From then on, his reputation soared.

"It is rumored that the Sword King's wife has an extraordinary background, otherwise with the Sword King's former arrogance, he would have long been surrounded and beaten to death," the cultivator sighed.

Xu Yan nodded, understanding that Xie Tianheng's wife must be from the Spiritual Realm, though he didn't know how an Inner Realm cultivator like Xie Tianheng managed to marry a woman from the Spiritual Realm.

After asking a few more things about Xie Lingfeng and not getting much more information, Xu Yan bid farewell and left on the sea beast-drawn boat.

"Sword God? Could it be that I'm about to witness another sword dao showdown?" the cultivator muttered excitedly. Such a grand event, he couldn't afford to miss it. And if the Sword King were to hear that someone dared to call themselves the Sword God, he would surely be enraged...

In the Yuntiancheng of the Azure Sea, the Transcendent Spiritual Sect ruled the entire Azure Sea, even the Azure Sea Overlord Spiritual Race had to heed the orders of the City Lord's Mansion.

The Du family was the City Lord's family of Yuntiancheng. In this generation, the City Lord Du Tianhai's strength was extraordinarily powerful, rumored to have surpassed the previous City Lords.

He was undisputedly the number one power in the Azure Sea.

Although Yuntiancheng was under the control of the City Lord's Mansion, there were also many other factions. The Spiritual Race held important positions in the City Lord's Mansion, their status and influence were supreme.

There had long been rumors that from ancient times, Yuntiancheng and the Spiritual Race had a contract, jointly ruling the Azure Sea, and in certain times the Spiritual Race had to obey the orders of the City Lord's Mansion.

The specific content and terms of the contract were only known to the true inner circle of the City Lord's Mansion and the Spiritual Race's upper echelons.

Among the major factions of Yuntiancheng, the Myriad Stars Academy was one. After being driven out of the Eighteen Provinces, they established a martial arts academy in Yuntiancheng, and had since cultivated many renowned martial artists.

The Myriad Stars Academy's principal was rumored to have a deep friendship with the City Lord, so the academy was seen as having the backing of the City Lord's Mansion.

To this day, the Myriad Stars Academy's influence in Yuntiancheng, and even the entire Azure Sea, was already quite substantial.

The academy's students included geniuses from all over the Azure Sea, as well as scions of prominent Yuntiancheng families, especially those from declining noble houses.

There were also students from the Spiritual Race, who increasingly preferred to send their younger generation to the academy to cultivate.

A few years ago, two notable events occurred in Yuntiancheng:

Firstly, the crown prince of the Great Zhou Royal House was sent to the Myriad Stars Academy to cultivate. Secondly, Yuntiancheng's City Lord Du Tianhai suddenly gained a son-in-law and a grandson from the Inner Realm, through the City Lord's Jade Token.

The major powers of Yuntiancheng were all dumbfounded.

And those who had long admired the City Lord's daughter were completely stunned - how could they be surpassed by someone from the Inner Realm? Their goddess had actually married an Inner Realm man and had a son with him?

When this news spread among the major powers of Yuntiancheng, many couldn't help but recall a certain past event.

It was said that the City Lord's daughter had once gone to the Eighteen Provinces, reportedly because the scandalous affairs of the Wood Family's playboy had reached the Azure Sea, and the City Lord's daughter felt the Wood Family's playboy was too despicable, so she went to eliminate him.

The pursuit of the Wood Family's playboy was said to have led her all the way to the Inner Realm, and it took many years before she returned to Yuntiancheng.

As for whether she actually eliminated the Wood Family's playboy, there were no further rumors about that.

And the Wood Family's playboy seemed to have disappeared, with Yuntiancheng once rumoring that he had likely been killed by the City Lord's daughter.

The end.
