Chapter 94 - The Inner Realm and Spirit Crystals, Xu Yan Comprehends Again

Outside Yunshan County, Xu Yan, Xie Lingfeng, Meng Chong, and Hu Shan were sparring, while Li Xuan was sitting in a chair, watching the cultivation methods written by Xie Lingfeng and Hu Shan.

"The martial arts of the Inner Realm are also powerful, the Sect Master martial artists cannot be underestimated."

Looking at the cultivation methods, Li Xuan sighed inwardly.

In his eyes, both Xue Wuxin and Xie Lingfeng and Hu Shan were very weak.

Compared to Xu Yan, they were far inferior.

However, Xu Yan had a Jade Bone foundation, and after breaking through to the Primordial realm, he had transformed into a Dragon Soaring Mountain and River Spiritual Bone, and had also comprehended the Sword Intent.

He had also grasped the true meaning of the Dragon Palm.

His strength was naturally incomparable to Xie Lingfeng, a Sect Master of the Inner Realm.

But if one thinks that the martial arts of the Inner Realm are weak, that would be one-sided.

Of course, at the moment, there is no doubt that the martial arts he has created, the martial arts system he has established, is stronger than the martial arts of the Inner Realm.

But he cannot conclude that the martial arts of the Inner Realm are weak and have nothing to learn from.

"Someone like Shi Er, with a Bronze Bone foundation, may not necessarily be able to defeat Xie Lingfeng after breaking through to the Primordial realm."

Xu Yan silently evaluated.

With the cultivation methods as a reference, and knowing Xie Lingfeng's specific strength, he could easily assess the gap.

"Xie Lingfeng's talent is indeed very high, among the younger generation in the Inner Realm, he is one of the top ones. Shi Er is no match for him, which is not surprising, but he will not lose to that Hu Shan, and may even surpass him."

Li Xuan estimated the gap between the two.

Overall, the martial arts he has created are stronger and have more potential, especially in the foundation of martial arts, which is far superior to the martial arts of the Inner Realm.

It also sounds more mysterious.

No wonder Xie Lingfeng would think that the martial arts he cultivates are false.

"After this comparison, my martial arts completely surpass them. I wonder if the Sect Masters of the Inner Realm are truly the peak?"

Li Xuan looked at the cultivation methods in his hand.

The Sect Masters are already the peak.

Of course, it is not ruled out that Xie Lingfeng and the others have not yet accessed the secrets above the Sect Masters.

"I feel that the Sect Masters are not the peak, and Xie Lingfeng and the others have not yet accessed the secrets above the Sect Masters."

Li Xuan took out an ancient leather book.

This ancient leather book, although he could not understand it, Li Xuan was certain that it was not ordinary, and the level it involved should be very high.

With his current cultivation, he was still unable to see through it.

Every time he looked at the ancient leather book, he seemed to have different feelings, and the patterns he saw seemed to change.

He could only conclude that his cultivation level was still too low, and he could not see through the secrets within.

"The ancient leather book came from the imperial palace treasury of the Wu Kingdom, it is a relic left behind, which shows that the remote border regions also once had powerful experts."

The reason why the border regions cannot cultivate is that the spiritual mechanism of Heaven and Earth has been extinguished, but when the spiritual mechanism of Heaven and Earth was extinguished, this is no longer known.

Xie Lingfeng and the others also do not know.

"Within the same realm of Heaven and Earth, why is the spiritual mechanism of Heaven and Earth active in the Inner Realm, while it is extinguished in the border regions? Could it be that the spiritual mechanism is gradually withering, and as time passes, the Inner Realm will also approach the border regions where the spiritual mechanism of Heaven and Earth is extinguished?"

Li Xuan had a puzzled expression.

To cultivate the martial arts of the Inner Realm, one must have the spiritual mechanism of Heaven and Earth, only with the existence of the spiritual mechanism of Heaven and Earth can one sense the spiritual Qi, and the spiritual Qi of Heaven and Earth can be gentle and refined into the body.

"What is the spiritual mechanism of Heaven and Earth like?"

Li Xuan had never left the border regions, and after breaking through the Primordial realm, although he could sense the existence of the spiritual Qi of Heaven and Earth, he had never experienced the spiritual mechanism of Heaven and Earth.

"My martial arts can actually be cultivated without the existence of the spiritual mechanism of Heaven and Earth, this shows that the spiritual mechanism of Heaven and Earth is not a must for cultivation."

Li Xuan continued to flip through the ancient leather book.

Xu Yan's group returned from the sparring, and Xie Lingfeng and Hu Shan again asked Li Xuan to teach them martial arts.

They wanted to seize this rare opportunity to get guidance from an expert.

Li Xuan had already looked through the cultivation methods of the two, and as for guidance, with his Primordial realm cultivation, it was not difficult either.

The focus of the guidance was mostly on the refinement of true Qi.

Without the ability to refine true Qi, everything would be in vain.

"Do you know of the Taicangs?"

Li Xuan looked at Xie Lingfeng and asked.

Xie Lingfeng was taken aback, "Taicangs?"

He shook his head, "Never heard of it."

Li Xuan nodded silently, Xie Lingfeng had not heard of the Taicangs, and the ancient leather book had not appeared in circulation either.

Turning the jade talisman in his hand, Xie Lingfeng cupped his hands and said, "The junior will not disturb the senior's tranquility."

Li Xuan nodded.

Xu Yan was entertaining Xie Lingfeng and Hu Shan, and during the meal, he naturally asked about the Inner Realm, and Xie Lingfeng and Hu Shan began to introduce the Inner Realm and the cultivation of the martial arts of the Inner Realm.

Li Xuan listened silently.

As Xie Lingfeng introduced, he gained more understanding of the cultivation of the martial arts in the Inner Realm.

"The cultivation of martial arts in the Inner Realm starts from the Nine Grades, and can be assisted by medicinal pills and decoctions... If one can obtain spirit herbs, the effect of assisting cultivation will be even better.

"According to Xie Lingfeng, medicinal pills and decoctions cannot be taken too much, each dose must be taken at least seven days apart, so that the remaining medicinal power can be completely refined.

"Otherwise, the accumulation in the body will be detrimental to the cultivation of martial arts.

"As for spirit herbs, there are no such drawbacks, the spiritual power contained in the spirit herbs is pure, easy to refine, and will not remain, but spirit herbs are rare, and ordinary martial artists simply cannot afford them."

Li Xuan found that the Inner Realm did not have elixirs, they had alchemists, but they did not refine elixirs.

The spirit herbs are simply decocted and then consumed, and different spirit herbs have different medicinal powers, but there is no method to refine them into elixirs.

"Since the Inner Realm has no elixirs, if I create some elixir refining, if successful, it will definitely be a treasure for martial artists."

Li Xuan was greatly intrigued.

"For Sect Master and above martial artists, they also directly consume spirit herbs for cultivation, but generally the spirit herbs have little effect on Sect Master martial artists, and their cultivation still mainly relies on their own strength.

"Or they cultivate with spirit crystals, which can increase the speed of refining martial arts inner Qi, but spirit crystals are rare, and they are usually not extravagant to use for cultivation.

"In most cases, spirit crystals are used to increase the recovery speed of inner Qi after consumption during battles."

Xie Lingfeng talked about the cultivation of Sect Master martial artists.

"Spirit crystals?"

Xu Yan had a curious expression.

"This is a spirit crystal!"

Xie Lingfeng took out five spirit crystals.

Xu Yan picked up a spirit crystal, he could sense that the spiritual Qi contained in the spirit crystal was much gentler than the spiritual Qi he sensed from Heaven and Earth.

The spiritual Qi contained in a single spirit crystal was not much.

"Spirit crystals are naturally formed by Heaven and Earth, they are quite rare, and in the Inner Realm, they are the currency of martial arts, used to purchase spirit herbs, treasured weapons and other items."

Seeing that Xu Yan did not understand the origin of spirit crystals, Xie Lingfeng explained, "The formation of spirit crystals is actually similar to the jade mines in the border regions, the difference is that the spirit crystal mines in the Inner Realm are transformed from jade mines, the jade mines nurture the spiritual Qi of Heaven and Earth, and gradually transform into spirit crystals over time.

"The large spirit crystal mines are entirely spirit crystals, while most spirit crystals are mixed in the jade mines, because only a portion of the entire jade mine has transformed into spirit crystals."

Jade mines are very valuable in the Inner Realm, because most jade mines, to a greater or lesser extent, contain some spirit crystals.
