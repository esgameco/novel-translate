"I don't know!" Ziyun shook her head. "Don't ask anymore, I haven't even met that person, and I don't even know his name. I think my father has gone mad."

The prototype of the Heaven-Earth Bridge had been cultivated, and as long as he continued to refine and consolidate it, he would completely transform into a Primordial Realm cultivator.

Xie Lingfeng swallowed another pill and continued to cultivate the Heaven-Earth Bridge. As the Heaven-Earth Bridge stabilized, the Heaven and Earth spiritual energy was guided into his dantian sea of qi.

At this moment, he could confirm that the transformation was successful.

"I've succeeded!" Xie Lingfeng was overjoyed.

Li Yuan heaved a sigh of relief. Since the transformation method was confirmed to be feasible, the probability of Primordial Realm cultivators appearing in the near future had greatly increased.

However, the transformation required the assistance of pills, which inevitably imposed limitations. And the transformation method was now completely under the control of the Qingqing Pavilion.

"The martial cultivators in the Inner Realm are countless, and there are many geniuses. Perhaps after the transformation method is spread out, someone will have a flash of insight and discover a way to cultivate the Heaven-Earth Bridge without the need for pills?" Li Yuan mused to himself.

With Xie Lingfeng's successful transformation, several Martial Dao scholars at the Grand Master realm also prepared to transform to the Primordial Realm, and continue to research the differences between the Inner Realm Martial Dao and the Great Desolate Martial Dao.

Hu Shan also prepared to transform his cultivation.

Xie Lingfeng continued to stabilize and strengthen the Heaven-Earth Bridge. As for breaking through to the Profound Mysteries realm, he didn't want to think about it for now, as his foundation was still too shallow to achieve that.

He only wanted to improve his strength, to be able to defeat Grand Master realm cultivators with his perfected Primordial Realm cultivation.

Xu Yan, at the perfected Primordial Realm, could even kill peak Grand Masters.

Xie Lingfeng didn't set his sights too high, as long as he could defeat mid-level Grand Masters with his perfected Primordial Realm, he would be satisfied.

On Canglan Island, in a certain area was the Qingqing Pavilion's alchemy room, still under the charge of Alchemist Pan. The people from the Tianbo Pavilion came regularly to collect the pills and deliver the spirit herbs needed for alchemy.

Xu Yan continued to cultivate his sword intent, while Zhou Ying prepared to break through to the Primordial Realm.
