Chapter 22: Fierce Battle with the Maned Wolf (Seeking Readers)

Xu Yan confirmed that he had reached a bottleneck, and if he could not break through this bottleneck, he would be unable to refine his bones into jade-like quality!

He was just one step away, but he was stuck and unable to make any progress.

It was as if there was a barrier blocking him, and without breaking through this barrier, achieving the jade-like bones would be hopeless.

However, Xu Yan had no idea how to break through this barrier.

No matter how he circulated his qi and blood, or how he continuously refined his bones, it was all in vain.

"Is it because I haven't grasped the insight? Do I need to relax my mind and seek enlightenment to break through the barrier?"

"But I have a feeling that I need pressure, an internal and external interaction, to break through. However, I don't know what kind of pressure is required..."

Xu Yan fell into deep contemplation.

He decided to seek advice from his master.

Just then, Li Xuan came out of the house.

"Disciple, come here!"

Xu Yan perked up, thinking that his master had noticed his bottleneck and was here to guide him.

"Master!" he respectfully greeted.

Li Xuan's expression was somewhat serious as he said, "Disciple, after you hunted down the evil tiger in the Evil Abyss Forest, did you search the entire forest?"

Xu Yan was taken aback and shook his head.

"In the Evil Abyss Forest, besides the tiger, there is also a fierce beast. Be careful and take care of it!"

Li Xuan warned him solemnly.

Based on the description in the old cow's diary, that fierce beast was not ordinary, and if it came to the small village, it would be a grave threat.

Xu Yan's eyes lit up as he realized, "The Evil Abyss Forest has another fierce beast? Stronger than the Red-Eyed Tiger? I understand now, Master is telling me that the opportunity to break through is in the Evil Abyss Forest!"

"Battling the fierce beast, unleashing my potential, breaking through the barrier, the jade-like bones will be achieved!"

In that moment, a flash of insight struck Xu Yan's mind, and he suddenly understood what kind of pressure he needed to break through the barrier and achieve the jade-like bones.

The fierce beast in the Evil Abyss Forest was his chance!

He immediately nodded excitedly and said, "Master, I understand. I'll go take care of it right away!"

Xu Yan excitedly mounted his horse and headed straight for the Evil Abyss Forest.

"Why the hurry? I haven't finished speaking yet," Li Xuan said, somewhat exasperated.

This foolish disciple of his got so excited upon hearing about the fierce beast in the Evil Abyss Forest. Was he addicted to hunting?

"With many people, the fierce beast shouldn't be able to escape." Li Xuan sighed in relief.

Although he had learned that the external martial artists were not particularly strong, belonging to the lower martial realm, with a group of people, dealing with a fierce beast should not be a problem.

Even if the beast was powerful, it couldn't be that strong compared to the low-level martial artists, right?

...

Xu Yan rode his horse to the Evil Abyss Forest and began searching.

As he delved deeper into the forest, the trees grew increasingly massive, and his horse started trembling, slowing down and even wanting to retreat.

Xu Yan's spirit was roused, and he circulated his qi and blood, remaining alert to his surroundings.

Suddenly, a deep roar echoed, and a fiery figure burst out from behind a large tree, its fangs gleaming with a cold light.

It pounced directly at Xu Yan on the horse.

Xu Yan's eyes gleamed, and relying on his robust physique, he directly unleashed a punch.

A claw also came swinging at him.

Boom!

Xu Yan felt a tremendous force, and he was knocked off the horse.

The horse, terrified, immediately collapsed to the ground, trembling.

"How is this possible?" Xu Yan was shocked.

He was a cultivator who had nearly refined his bones into jade-like quality, with extraordinary strength.

Xu Yan staggered back a few steps, feeling a sharp pain in his chest.

As for the fierce beast, it was also pushed back two steps by the punch.

At this moment, Xu Yan looked closely and saw that the fierce beast's entire body was fiery red, especially the mane around its neck, burning like a raging fire.

It had the head of a wolf, emerald green eyes, and two fangs over a foot long that gleamed with a cold light. Its hind body was smaller than the front, but the hind legs were incredibly thick and sturdy.

Its front paws were like tiger claws, with sharp talons exposed, like small daggers.

Its body was more than half larger than a crimson-eyed tiger, standing there with an aura of ferocity. Its emerald green eyes gave off a sinister and savage feeling.

Being stared at by those eyes would make one shudder involuntarily.

At this moment, those emerald green eyes were fixed intently on Xu Yan.

It sensed a powerful life force from this human, which aroused its bloodthirsty desire.

"A Fire-Maned Wolf?!"

Xu Yan's heart was filled with awe. The appearance of this fierce beast was exactly the same as the one he had seen in an ancient storybook, a creature called the Fire-Maned Wolf.

It was rumored that the Fire-Maned Wolf was incredibly powerful, once rampaging into the city and causing countless deaths and injuries, and had retreated unscathed even under the siege of elite troops!

Unexpectedly, there was actually a Fire-Maned Wolf in this evil forest!

"Who says storybooks are all lies? Even the Fire-Maned Wolf has appeared!"

Xu Yan was greatly excited, his blood surging, his entire being filled with exhilaration.

The Fire-Maned Wolf, the legendary fierce beast.

Many had read about the Fire-Maned Wolf in storybooks, but no one took it seriously. A fierce beast rampaging into the city and retreating unscathed even under the siege of troops? That was too absurd!

But Xu Yan believed it now. From their earlier clash, the Fire-Maned Wolf's strength was indeed capable of retreating unscathed from a military siege.

Its formidable physique, agile speed, and immense power. Even Xu Yan felt some pressure, which showed how powerful the Fire-Maned Wolf was.

"This is my chance to break through!"

Xu Yan believed that the Fire-Maned Wolf was the key to his breakthrough to the Jade Bone realm. Battling the Fire-Maned Wolf, under such intense pressure, would allow him to break through the shackles.

"Come, today I, Xu Yan, will use your Fire-Maned Wolf's life to temper my Jade Bone!"

Xu Yan roared, his blood boiling, his body even emanating a faint jade luster, as he charged forward.

Roar!

The Fire-Maned Wolf let out a fierce howl and pounced at him, its massive paws swiping, its gaping jaws dripping with saliva as it viciously bit towards Xu Yan.

Xu Yan grabbed the Fire-Maned Wolf's paws with both hands, and before it could bite, he delivered a powerful kick to its body, sending it flying and crashing to the ground.

He then rushed forward and started raining down punches.

The Fire-Maned Wolf let out a roar, its eyes turning blood-red, an aura of savagery permeating the air, its fiery red mane now billowing like raging flames.

It pounced at Xu Yan again.

Boom!

Xu Yan was sent flying back, his heart filled with shock. How had the Fire-Maned Wolf's body become so scorching hot?

The Fire-Maned Wolf pounced down.

The man and the beast engaged in a fierce battle.

Xu Yan let out a roar, pushing the Fire-Maned Wolf back, then rushed in again, grabbing its paws and pinning it to the ground.

But the Fire-Maned Wolf kept kicking with its hind legs, each strike with tremendous force, shaking Xu Yan's blood and nerves with excruciating pain.

"Die!"

Boom!

Xu Yan mustered all his strength and flung the Fire-Maned Wolf away. His entire body was boiling with energy, and at this moment, a mist was rising from Xu Yan's skin, which was gradually turning red.
