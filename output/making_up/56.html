Cloud Mountain County.

Li Xuan brought Meng Chong back to the residence and let him choose a guest room to stay in.

"Get familiar with the environment first. Can you cook? Can you make chicken soup? You can, then go cook."

The first thing Meng Chong had to do after becoming a disciple was to cook!

"Senior, I've brought the things."

Shi Er respectfully came over.

"Put them down." Li Xuan nodded.

Shi Er placed five boxes on the table. Without Li Xuan moving, the five boxes opened by themselves as his vital energy surged.

This scene left Shi Er greatly startled, and he bent his waist even lower.

There were two nine-leaf spirit herbs and three thousand-year-old mountain ginseng.

Li Xuan was quite surprised. "The Heavenly Mother Sect is quite wealthy. Nine-leaf spirit herbs and thousand-year-old mountain ginseng are rare treasures, yet your Heavenly Mother Sect actually has such precious collections. That's quite remarkable."

Back then, Xu Yan became his disciple precisely because of the nine-leaf spirit herbs and thousand-year-old mountain ginseng, which deeply impressed him.

"Senior, although the nine-leaf spirit herbs and thousand-year-old mountain ginseng are rare, we can still find some in the boundless mountains. Our Heavenly Mother Sect has been forced to hide in the boundless mountains many times, and that's how we obtained these precious collections."

Shi Er respectfully explained.

"Boundless mountains?" Li Xuan's heart stirred.

"Senior, the boundless mountains are just two hundred miles away from here. They are called 'boundless' because no one can traverse these mountains - no one has been able to reach the end."

Shi Er raised his hand, pointing outside Cloud Mountain County.

Li Xuan pondered, "The small village I came to is right next to the boundless mountains. The evil forest seems to extend to the boundless mountains. Could it be that the fire-maned wolves came from the boundless mountains?"

He had some guesses, but whether it was true or not, he couldn't be certain yet.

"Continue."

Li Xuan picked up a nine-leaf spirit herb, gesturing for Shi Er to go on.

"Senior, the boundless mountains are called 'boundless' because they are endless. The deepest exploration was done by a few supreme martial artists, who went a thousand miles into the boundless mountains but still couldn't find the end.

"Due to the deadly miasma, they had to stop the exploration. Those who entered the boundless mountains and returned didn't go beyond a thousand miles, and those who never came back, no one knows what happened to them - they must have died in the mountains."

Shi Er respectfully explained, wondering if this great person might actually be from the boundless mountains.

After hearing this, Li Xuan's guess about the boundless mountains grew stronger.

The fire-maned wolves were clearly not ordinary wild beasts, and based on this, the martial prowess in this world should not be so low.

The truth might be on the other side of the boundless mountains?

Of course, these were just guesses. Whether it was true or not remained to be seen.
