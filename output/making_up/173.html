Things like the dark turmoil, the collapse of heaven and earth, the Martial Ancestor single-handedly stemming the tide, all were incorporated into the chronicle.

Li Xuan looked at the Great Wilderness chronicle, feeling motivated as well.

At this time, Xu Junhe was persuading the manager of the Heavenly Treasure Hall, using the Heavenly Treasure Hall's channels to spread the Great Wilderness chronicle to every corner of the inner domain.

Of course, under the guise of a story book, letting the Heavenly Treasure Hall sell it.

But who in the inner domain would read a story book?

The result was that this old fox Xu Junhe had edited a set of chronicles that secretly contained treasures and supreme martial arts. If one could decipher them, they would surely stand at the peak of the inner domain.

And with a single Tempering Qi Pill, he had persuaded the Heavenly Treasure Hall manager.

Even reaching an agreement with the Heavenly Treasure Hall in Iron Mountain County, handing over the sales agency of the Tempering Qi Pill produced by Long Qing Hall to be sold throughout the inner domain.

This was an extraordinary pill that assisted cultivators at the master level.

Li Xuan couldn't help but sigh, this Xu Junhe really had the ability.

Wanting to purchase the pill formula, and Xu Junhe directly gave him half of the formula, saying that the research and development had cost a lot, and he hadn't even recouped the investment yet, so he wanted to rely on the Heavenly Treasure Hall's help to sell it throughout the inner domain.

Completing a long-cherished wish, not in vain for establishing Long Qing Hall.

The Heavenly Treasure Hall manager, seeing Xu Junhe so generous, directly gave him half the formula without much thought, and agreed to sell the Tempering Qi Pill.

The profit was divided 7:3, with Long Qing Hall getting 7 and the Heavenly Treasure Hall 3.

After all, Long Qing Hall had it hard too, and by directly giving half the formula, the remaining half was what Long Qing Hall could earn back in the transaction.

The Heavenly Treasure Hall was very reputable and did not appear overbearing in cooperating with other business forces, which was also the reason for its excellent reputation in the inner domain.

It had never had a bad reputation.

Li Xuan could imagine that the Tempering Qi Pill would soon gain fame throughout the inner domain with the help of the Heavenly Treasure Hall's channels, and master-level martial artists would flock to it.

Long Qing Hall would certainly become renowned in the inner domain.

And there would be a huge influx of spirit herbs and spirit crystals.

As for the formula, what if he gave it to the Heavenly Treasure Hall?

Having the formula alone, without the know-how to refine the Tempering Qi Pill, how could they produce it?

Of course, Xu Junhe's use of the formula to induce the Heavenly Treasure Hall to help promote Long Qing Hall was with the approval of Suli Xiu.

After all, the formula was a crucial matter, and Xu Junhe would not use it as a bargaining chip if he could not guarantee it would be a complete success.

"Using the Heavenly Treasure Hall as a channel to accumulate quickly, and when the Heavenly Treasure Hall reacts, Long Qing Hall will have the strength to negotiate. As for relying on strength to suppress Long Qing Hall..."

Li Xuan shook his head, Xu Yanqi was about to break through to the Penetrating Realm, and no matter how strong the Heavenly Treasure Hall was, how could they compare? Strength was the foundation.
