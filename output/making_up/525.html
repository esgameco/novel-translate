The Celestial Purple Pearl was a supreme treasure, rumored to be formed from a strand of primordial purple light from the creation of the heavens.

No wonder Tianshai was so confident after obtaining this treasure, wanting to become the master of heaven and earth.

Normally, with Tianshai's methods and the Celestial Purple Pearl as a supreme treasure, the probability of seizing control of heaven and earth and ascending to become its master was very high.

Even if he couldn't fully control heaven and earth, he could at least obtain over 70% of its authority. In that case, Tianshai would become the strongest being in the Cang Heavenly Realm.

Unfortunately, Tianshai ultimately had limited knowledge of the Cang Heavenly Realm.

The Cang Heavenly Realm was different from the other six major realms. Besides some arrangements Cang had made to the realm, the more important reason was the existence of Tianzi.

Old Man Xiao, Taimiao and the others all knew about the special nature of the Cang Heavenly Realm, so they never had thoughts of obtaining authority over heaven and earth.

As for Cang leaving some contingencies for later generations to become the new master of heaven and earth, those conditions were too harsh. It required obtaining Tianzi's approval, and one still couldn't become the true master of heaven and earth due to Tianzi's existence.

He was the spirit of heaven and earth, with immense authority over the realm.

Although Old Man Xiao and Yu Yao didn't know Tianzi was the spirit of heaven and earth, they knew his identity was special, as if he had a feeling of being Master Cang's son.

Cang was quite indulgent towards Tianzi.

Boom! The Celestial Purple Pearl radiated purple light, and Tianshai's aura became powerful, with a special charm emerging.

At one point, the Shadow of Earth suddenly dispersed like clouds and mist, merging into heaven and earth, merging into the laws of heaven and earth.

Tianshai's expression was excited. The Celestial Purple Pearl was suffused with purple light, and he and the Shadow of Earth formed a kind of unified state.

Rumble!

The laws of heaven and earth seemed to come under control, and the power of the laws surged towards the Demon Shaman Realm, pulling it to fall.

Tianshai wanted to use the power of the Demon Shaman Realm to control more of the laws of heaven and earth. With the aid of the Celestial Purple Pearl, as a crisis in heaven and earth emerged, the laws of heaven and earth would not reject his control.

Because this could resolve the crisis.

This was also Tianshai's opportunity, although it was rushed this time and would inevitably cause a great calamity in heaven and earth, with countless lives lost.

However, as long as he became the master of heaven and earth, it would all be worth it. He would have the confidence to confront that mysterious powerful being!

Heaven and earth shook, and the laws appeared. Looking up, one could seemingly see rules surrounding heaven and earth, being controlled by a force.

The Celestial Purple Pearl emitted purple light, suffused with the charm of the Dao, as the Shadow of Earth dispersed into the laws of heaven and earth.

Rumble!

The Demon Shaman Realm had already touched the barrier of the Cang Heavenly Realm. The entire realm began to shake. The laws of Cang radiated light, wanting to shatter the Demon Shaman Realm.

At the same time, the Demon Shaman Realm seemed to be stimulated. Its laws surged as if about to pour out, releasing a powerful striking force.

Hum! A faint purple halo emerged from the laws of the Cang Heavenly Realm. The power of the laws gathered, transforming into a giant blade.

The aura around Tianshai grew stronger and stronger, as if the authority of heaven and earth was merging into his soul.

"Slash!"

Tianshai roared, wanting to use the laws of the Cang Heavenly Realm to slash apart the Demon Shaman Realm. As long as the Demon Shaman Realm was shattered, even if it fell into the Cang Heavenly Realm, the damage would be greatly reduced.

However, just as the laws of Cang transformed into a giant blade slashing towards the Demon Shaman Realm, Tianshai was shocked to discover that the laws of heaven and earth seemed to undergo some kind of change in an instant.

Moreover, they gradually slipped from his control.

Rumble!

The Demon Shaman Realm crashed into the barrier of Cang, about to collide with the Cang Heavenly Realm. At this moment, a vast and mighty power emerged.

The Cang Heavenly Realm seemed somewhat different.

The laws seemed to have changed. In a daze, it was as if heaven and earth were no longer in the same state as before.

Rumble!

"Impossible!"

Tianshai turned pale with fright. He completely lost control of the laws of heaven and earth. What shocked him even more was that he lost his connection to the Shadow of Earth.

It was as if it had perished! The authority of heaven and earth that had originally merged into his soul had disappeared. The familiar laws of heaven and earth had become unfamiliar.

That no longer seemed to be laws, but rather rules that were more powerful and harder to comprehend than the laws of heaven and earth.

It also contained supreme and majestic heavenly might.

Thud! The Demon Shaman Realm crashed in, but it was as if it had crashed into a huge vortex, or rather into a huge mouth.

At this moment, it seemed not that the Demon Shaman Realm was crashing into the Cang Heavenly Realm, but more like the Cang Heavenly Realm was devouring the Demon Shaman Realm.

All the realm lords were stunned, and even felt a bit scared.

The laws of heaven and earth had become unfamiliar, with a sense that they could not be offended, and a majestic heavenly might that would punish those who defied them!

"I still have a chance!"

Tianshai was unwilling to give up. He looked towards the Celestial Purple Pearl.

His aura surged as he infused power into the Celestial Purple Pearl. He wanted to activate this supreme treasure to reclaim the authority of heaven and earth that should have belonged to him.

The light of the Celestial Purple Pearl grew brighter and brighter. Tianshai had already infused 80% of his powerful strength, consuming a huge amount.

But at this moment, the immense power contained in the Celestial Purple Pearl was enough to break through the current situation and create an opportunity for him.

Just as Tianshai was about to activate the Celestial Purple Pearl and strike with all his might, something shocking happened again.

The Celestial Purple Pearl lost control and slipped from his grasp, transforming into a beam of purple light that vanished into the sky.

In this situation, Tianshai had a sad feeling of being used by the Celestial Purple Pearl, which had taken his powerful energy and then left him.

The Demon Shaman Realm continued to fall, while the Cang Heavenly Realm was also undergoing changes. The Dao Realm was no longer above the Divine Realm, and the Three Hundred Realms were also changing, losing their status as small worlds and completely merging with the Cang Heavenly Realm.

Although the Demon Shaman Realm was broken, it still contained powerful energy.

Its unstoppable momentum as it crashed in could not be blocked. Even though the Cang Heavenly Realm was devouring and eroding the Demon Shaman Realm, it could not resolve this crisis in such a short time.

"I can't control it anymore, it's going to crash in!"

Vaguely, all the realm lords seemed to hear a frustrated voice ring out.

In the courtyard, everyone watched this scene and sensed the changes in heaven and earth. Old Man Xiao had a look of shock and astonishment.

The Heavenly Dao!

He sensed the existence of the Heavenly Dao. The original laws of Cang had transformed into the Heavenly Dao. This realm had already undergone changes.

"The Heavenly Dao has been born. From now on, heaven and earth have the Heavenly Dao!"

Old Man Xiao muttered to himself.

Yu Yao was puzzled. What was the Heavenly Dao? What was going on with the Heavenly Dao? But this was not the time to ask about these doubts.

It seemed the Cang Heavenly Realm was devouring the Demon Shaman Realm, but the crashing force was too great. It would still crash into the Cang Heavenly Realm and still cause a significant calamity.

"The collision and fusion of realms, the mutual generation of yin and yang, is not a bad thing."

Li Xuan smiled slightly and waved his hand. The Cang Heavenly Realm suddenly changed. The wilderness of the 36 levels of the Divine Realm instantly merged together, appearing where the Demon Shaman Realm was falling.

The few living beings in the wilderness were all moved out.

When the Demon Shaman Realm crashed in, it would not cause much harm to life.
