At this point, Xu Yan's breakthrough to the divine ability realm was complete.

Besides Meng Chong, there was also Su Lingxiu.

Li Xuan was very curious to see what divine abilities Su Lingxiu would give rise to upon her breakthrough.

"The senior disciple has already broken through."

Meng Chong had a look of shock on his face, now feeling utterly insignificant before his senior disciple.

"The advancement to the divine ability realm is too powerful."

Meng Chong sighed, his blood boiling with the desire to quickly accumulate his foundation and break through to the divine ability realm himself.

"I feel the senior disciple won't be weaker than the Bewitching Witch."

Su Lingxiu spoke up.

"I wonder what divine abilities the senior disciple has attained."

After entering the divine ability realm, Xu Yan's strength was incomparable to before. Even the former Bewitching Witch held no fear for him now.

Sitting cross-legged on the clouds, Xu Yan vanished in an instant. Before Meng Chong and the others could react, there was suddenly another person beside them - Xu Yan!

"Senior disciple!"

Meng Chong was greatly shocked, his senior disciple had suddenly appeared without him even sensing it.

"So this is the power of divine abilities!"

Xu Yan sighed.

"Only by breaking through to the divine ability realm can one truly comprehend the wondrous nature of the martial dao."

Upon entering the divine ability realm, he finally understood that the true strength and profundity of the martial dao lay in the divine abilities. The cultivation before the divine abilities was merely the foundation, the preparation for attaining the divine abilities.

And the divine abilities were just the starting point of a new path in the martial dao. The martial dao was boundless! Now Xu Yan grasped this meaning more deeply.

"Senior disciple, what divine abilities have you attained?"

Su Lingxiu asked excitedly.

"I've only attained five divine abilities, nothing too impressive."

Xu Yan sighed.

He looked towards his master, wondering what his master thought of his attainment of five divine abilities.

"Not bad."

Li Xuan nodded, giving a middling evaluation. He couldn't let his disciple get complacent - he had to always make Xu Yan feel his performance wasn't good enough, so that he would strive even harder.

"As expected."

Xu Yan felt relieved, but also a bit disappointed.

"I'm still quite far from the level my master was at back then. At least I didn't disappoint him too much!"

In Xu Yan's heart, his master's neutral evaluation meant he hadn't completely failed, but also hadn't reached the heights his master had.

"I still need to work harder, strive to reach at least half of what my master accomplished in the next realm!"

Xu Yan inwardly resolved.

Meng Chong felt great pressure - his senior disciple was so strong, yet only received a "not bad" evaluation from their master. He would have to redouble his efforts.

Otherwise, he might not even get a "not bad" evaluation.

"Senior disciple, please demonstrate your divine abilities."

Su Lingxiu called out excitedly.

"Alright."

Xu Yan smiled, and in an instant, Su Lingxiu and the others were stunned.

The Long Qing Pavilion remained the Long Qing Pavilion, yet it seemed to be in a completely different world, with a sense that it could be annihilated at any moment.

"This is my sword dao divine ability, 'Sword Encompassing Mountains and Rivers'. Within my sword, the mountains and rivers exist, life and death are in my hands."

Xu Yan's voice rang out.

"So this is the power of divine abilities!"

Disciples like Dyu Yuying and Yun Miaoiao who cultivated the Vast Martial Dao were greatly shocked - the divine abilities were truly wondrous and inconceivable.

The power of the divine abilities exceeded all imagination!

Cai Lingyi's expression was grave. She knew her own strength was near the limits of the Spirit Realm, yet faced with this divine ability, she had no resistance at all.

If Xu Yan hadn't reminded them, if they hadn't known in advance that Xu Yan would unleash a divine ability, they might not have even realized they had already entered his divine ability.

As Xu Yan withdrew his divine ability, two sword lights appeared around him, constantly rotating.

"Sworn brother, attack me with your full strength!"

Xu Yan stood with his hands clasped behind his back, smiling.

"Okay!"

Meng Chong nodded.

Golden light surged from Meng Chong's body as he unleashed a powerful thunderous fist strike.

Boom! This was Meng Chong's full-force punch, shocking Cai Lingyi and the others with its ferocious power.

Seeing this punch, Cai Lingyi couldn't help but sigh that she would be unable to withstand such a violent attack.

Rumble!

However, this violent punch entered the whirlpool of sword lights surrounding Xu Yan, as if it had entered a vortex. It didn't harm Xu Yan, and the power of the punch didn't dissipate either.

"Sworn brother, continue!"

Xu Yan remained standing with his hands clasped behind his back.

Meng Chong unleashed punch after punch, over twenty in the blink of an eye.

These powerful, violent punches seemed to enter a vortex of sword lights, with no effect.

"Watch closely, this is my other sword dao divine ability, 'Yin Yang Inextinguishable Sword'!"

Xu Yan smiled, and with a single thought, the sword lights rotated, and a terrifyingly powerful thunderous fist strike shot out into the air.

This fist strike contained the accumulated force of Meng Chong's twenty-plus punches - its power was so great that even Meng Chong would be unable to withstand it.

That's why Xu Yan had directed it into the air.

Gulp!

Meng Chong wiped the sweat from his brow, utterly shocked. The combined force of his twenty-plus punches had been gathered into a single strike of such astonishing power.

"This sword dao divine ability is too strong."

In that instant, Meng Chong understood the incredible power of this divine ability of his senior disciple's.

It could return the enemy's attacks, and if faced with a group assault, it could even redirect the enemies' attacks to strike each other.

The Yin Yang Inextinguishable Sword, yin and yang never extinguished, endlessly cycling.

"I must cultivate, I must break through to the divine ability realm as soon as possible!"

Witnessing the power of the divine abilities, Meng Chong's blood burned with excitement, eager to quickly accumulate his foundation and break through.

"What kind of divine abilities will my martial dao of the physical body give rise to?"

Meng Chong was filled with anticipation.

He left to cultivate, determined to break through to the divine ability realm as soon as possible.
