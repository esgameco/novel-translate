Chapter 526

Here is my full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The world of witches and demons disappeared, but it didn't truly vanish completely. Rather, it merged into the Taicang world, just in an unexpected way.

The Taicang world was no longer the same world as before. It had transformed into a new world - the Great Wilderness world.

The Great Wilderness was divided into yin and yang, and the part from the witch-demon world became the Yin Realm of the Great Wilderness.

As the Heavenly Dao operated and yin and yang cycled, a new order of the world was born.

This process was gradual and could not be completed overnight. Time flew by swiftly, and in the blink of an eye, ten years had passed.

For powerful beings, ten years was but the snap of a finger.

The various realm lords who had been immersed in the world's changes and the division of yin and yang gradually awakened from their contemplation. Their hearts were greatly shaken as they sensed the somewhat unfamiliar world, feeling at a loss for a moment.

What alarmed them even more was that there seemed to exist some supreme rules in the world. These were no longer the original laws of the world. Even they could not defy these supreme rules.

This meant that from now on, these realm lord level powerhouses could no longer be truly supreme and unrestrained. Above them were inviolable supreme rules.

However, these supreme rules also seemed to be supremely fair and would not target them specifically. In fact, comprehending the supreme rules seemed to offer a way to increase one's strength.

It was a very contradictory state. On one hand, they were no longer supreme and unconstrained. On the other hand, it seemed a new path had opened up in the martial way, allowing them to continue advancing.

"Where did the Three Hundred Realms go?" a realm lord asked in a low voice.

"I saw some special places that seem to have been transformed from the Three Hundred Realms."

The realm lords who had awakened were exchanging information with each other.

At this moment, the once familiar Taicang world had become strange. Even more terrifying was that there was an extremely powerful being who could crush a realm lord with one hand, making them not dare to commit any offense.

They were afraid that one careless move would offend that mysterious powerful figure.

Although Realm Lord Wan Tao was not a top powerhouse, he was no weakling either. Yet he was turned to ashes with just a light pinch.

This was probably something only the Lord of Heaven and Earth could do.

"Where are Tianshai and Diying?" a realm lord asked in confusion.

All the realm lords began to carefully explore. They saw Ao Lie, Realm Lord Xiaoyao and others who were still in contemplation.

But Tianshai, Diying, and even the seven from Meiwu were nowhere to be seen.

"Could it be that Tianshai, Diying and Meiwu's group are all dead?"

"Have you noticed that a special place seems to have appeared in the world? That seems to be what the witch-demon world transformed into."

"Could Tianshai and the others be there?"

The realm lords discussed quietly among themselves.

At one point, an authoritative voice entered the ears of all the realm lords:

"The Great Wilderness world is divided into yin and yang, establishing the cycle of reincarnation. Tianshai and Diying defied the Heavenly Dao and are punished to enter the Yin Realm, responsible for building the Yin Realm to atone for their sins!"

The powerful beings were shocked.

The Heavenly Dao!

They looked towards the location of the Yin Realm. It was gloomy and dark there, with an eerie aura that made one's heart tremble.

The desolate Yin Realm was silent and vast, deep and frightening. Even realm lord level powerhouses would likely not be able to withstand staying there for long periods without their minds being affected.

Tianshai and Diying were actually punished to do hard labor in the Yin Realm. This was absolutely a huge torment.

They knew that even someone as strong as Tianshai could not resist this punishment.

The voice of the Heavenly Dao sounded in their ears again:

"Meiwu, Duwu, Liwu, Yanmo, Jumo, Bamo, and Chimo, since you came from the witch-demon world, and the Yin Realm was transformed from the witch-demon world, you seven are hereby punished to enter the Yin Realm from this day forward. Maintain order in the Yin Realm. Those who render meritorious service may atone for their sins and establish a foothold in the Yin Realm."

Meiwu's group of seven were also cast into the Yin Realm.

"From this day forward, the living beings of the witch-demon world shall enter the Yin Realm to enrich its vital energy and become beings of the Yin Realm."

The living beings of the witch-demon world were sentenced to the Yin Realm.

The realm lords who had allied with Tianshai now wore nervous expressions.

They worried about also being punished and sent to the Yin Realm. That place looked deep and gloomy, clearly not a good place to be.

In the Yin Realm, Tianshai wore an expression of unwillingness and dejection. A figure walked out from his body and stood silently as well - it was Diying! They had failed! After all their scheming, they thought victory was in their grasp and that no one but them could be the Lord of Heaven and Earth. But in the end they had failed.

The Taicang world had also become a thing of the past as of today.

The world now was the Great Wilderness!

He raised his head unwillingly, "If not for the Immutable Divine Palace, I, Tianshai, would not have been born in the wrong era. Otherwise, how could I have failed like this!"

Tianshai was proud.

He believed that he was born in the wrong era, missing too many opportunities and the right time to rise up. Moreover, he was not given enough time, which led to such a failure.

Tianshai subconsciously believed that the world's transformation and Taicang becoming the past was due to the Immutable Divine Palace's invasion.

A figure appeared before Tianshai - a youth wearing purple robes. Although he tried hard to appear dignified, he could not completely conceal his playful and mischievous nature.

"What does this have to do with the Immutable Divine Palace? From the moment you schemed to usurp the authority of Heaven and Earth, you were destined to fail," Tianzi said with a smile.

Tianshai was a talented individual. The Great Wilderness needed such talent, especially when facing the threat of the Immutable Divine Palace. Strong individuals like Tianshai could not be killed.

Moreover, the Yin Realm had just been established and needed major construction. This kind of hard labor could be left to Tianshai as punishment for him.

The necessary punishment could not be lacking.

Let him do hard labor for himself!

The more powerful beings in the Great Wilderness, the greater the feedback to the Heavenly Dao. This would allow him to become stronger and have a chance to confront that person from the Immutable Divine Palace. Only then would he have the power to make the Immutable Divine Palace experience the might of the Heavenly Dao.

Therefore, these powerful beings could not be easily eliminated unless their crimes were unforgivable. After all, these powerful beings were the nutrients that strengthened the Heavenly Dao.

Realm lord level powerhouses were ultimately not far from taking the next step. As long as they comprehended the Heavenly Dao, they could forge a new path.

This was the fastest way to enhance the Heavenly Dao in the shortest time possible.

Otherwise, cultivating new realm lord level powerhouses from scratch would take a considerable amount of time. Tianzi now needed to enhance the Heavenly Dao as quickly as possible.

Therefore, he only punished Tianshai and the others without eliminating them. If they performed well, they could even be rewarded. The Yin Realm needed strong individuals to maintain order.

"Who are you?" Tianshai asked in a deep voice.

At this moment, he felt the youth before him was extremely terrifying, emanating a majestic aura as if he were - the Heavenly Dao!

Tianzi smiled brightly, holding up a purple pearl in his hand.

"This is the Tianzi Pearl, a toy given to me by Taicang. My name is Tianzi, though of course, you can also call me the Heavenly Dao now!"

Tianshai's pupils constricted. He finally understood why the Tianzi Pearl had gone out of control - because it had been reclaimed by its master.

"You were in Taicang all along. How did I not know about you?" Tianshai's face was full of unwillingness.

He felt that he had been schemed against by Tianzi from the shadows.

"It's perfectly normal that you didn't know about me. Xiaoyao and Yuyao knew about me. If you had known about me, you wouldn't have had the delusion of trying to usurp the authority of Heaven and Earth."
