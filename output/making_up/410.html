This old man, Wu Tiannan had encountered him more than a dozen times. Each time he encountered the other party, he was lying not far from him.

At first, he thought it was just a coincidence.

After it happened again and again, Wu Tiannan was certain that the other party was deliberately following him.

Taking a deep breath, he was about to step forward and ask directly, when he saw the old man suddenly sit up, look up at the sky, with a surprised and astonished expression.

Wu Tiannan was puzzled. While remaining vigilant of the other party, he looked up at the sky but didn't discover anything.

And the other party didn't take advantage of him looking at the sky to suddenly attack.

"Strange," the old man muttered.

"Senior, why are you following me?" Wu Tiannan took a deep breath and asked in a deep voice.

The old man withdrew his gaze, looked at Wu Tiannan, and said with a smile: "I'm not following you, this is fate. Why is your luck so extraordinary, young man?"

Before Wu Tiannan could react, the old man was already in front of him, circling around him, clicking his tongue and saying: "Not simple, really not simple. The martial arts you practice are quite extraordinary, your luck is extraordinary. Who is your master?"

"I..." Wu Tiannan was shocked. The old man's strength was far beyond what he could compare to. If he wanted to kill him, it wouldn't take any effort at all.

"Could it be an Immortal Venerable?" he thought to himself, but for a moment he didn't know how to answer out loud.

Who was his master? His martial arts guide was not a master-disciple relationship, and his relationship with the senior expert was also not a master-disciple relationship. Strictly speaking, he had no master!

"This junior has no master," he said.

"Then who passed on your martial arts to you?" The old man asked with great interest.

"A senior expert passed it on."

"A senior expert? How does he compare to me?"

"Senior, you are not as good as him."

"Interesting." The old man smiled and said, "Your thick luck seems to be favored by heaven and earth. Was it bestowed by that expert you mentioned?"

Wu Tiannan was puzzled and shook his head, "I don't know."

He also felt that his luck seemed a bit too good. For example, some days ago, he saw two Immortal Venerables engage in a great battle. All the martial artists traveling with him died.

But he escaped unscathed, and even picked up a divine weapon! This kind of experience of turning danger into safety and obtaining treasures had happened more than once or twice. Now that the old man asked about it, Wu Tiannan couldn't help but wonder if his thick luck had something to do with the senior expert. Was it because he had successfully cultivated the divine ability passed on by the senior expert and established a martial arts connection with this senior expert, thus receiving karmic feedback?

After all, the senior expert was a supreme being who had transcended heaven and earth.

"Where is this expert you speak of?" the old man asked with interest.

Wu Tiannan pondered for a moment before saying, "He may be in the Qinghua Realm, or he may not be. I'm not sure."

The old man nodded.

He took out a token and stuffed it into Wu Tiannan's hand, saying, "This is for you. It's annoying to run into you everywhere, as if you're treating me as some kind of opportunity. Your luck is a bit ridiculous."

Wu Tiannan was dumbfounded. It was clearly this old man who had been following him, so how could he think that he was running into Wu Tiannan everywhere?

"What is this?" he looked at the token in his hand, feeling it was extraordinary but not understanding its purpose.

"When you go to the Tahe Realm, when the time is right, you silly boy will know the use of the token," the old man shook his head. How could there be someone with such luck in this world?

How strange.

"Senior, wait a moment. I want to ask you about someone," Wu Tiannan suddenly said.

"With your luck, you'll find them soon enough. No need to ask me. This old man doesn't know many people in the Divine Realm!" The old man waved his hand and left, disappearing without a trace.

Wu Tiannan had a helpless expression as he put away the token.

"The Tahe Realm, huh? The first and strongest realm of the Divine Realm. I'll go there sooner or later."

He set off again on his journey to find someone.
