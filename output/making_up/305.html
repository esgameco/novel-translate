Before the mountain gate of the Thousand Martial Hall, the battle of the geniuses was about to begin again, and the martial artists were greatly excited, ready to witness history once more.

"I thought the Blood Demon was arrogant and presumptuous enough in the past, but fortunately I'm still alive today to know that there are people beyond people. Xu Yan is even more reckless than the Blood Demon," an elder sighed and lamented.

Xu Yan was the first one to block the gate of the Transcendent Spirit Sect and issue a challenge.

"This is so exciting!"

Cui Huayu leaned out of the palace gate, holding a handful of spirit beans, tossing them into her mouth one by one, watching the impending battle with excitement.

On the arena, Xu Yan soared into the air, and the geniuses of the Thousand Martial Hall did the same, for if they fought on the arena, it would collapse in an instant.

Of course, the collapse of the arena did not concern the geniuses of the Thousand Martial Hall, but the arena was too close to the gate of the Thousand Martial Hall, and the aftershock of the battle would affect the surroundings.

Therefore, he followed Xu Yan and took the fight to the air.

"Xu Yan, a mere wandering cultivator, dares to challenge the supreme sect of the Thousand Martial Hall. Who gave you the courage? Today you shall die!"

The genius' aura surged, his saber light flashing, his strength far exceeding that of the previous five geniuses.

"Is this the genius from the Hundred Saber Gate, one of the Seven Sects?"

"Yes, it's him. I didn't expect his strength to have grown so much."

Among the martial artists, many recognized the genius who had taken action, from the Hundred Saber Gate, one of the Seven Sects, and was already renowned in the Thousand Martial State.

The Sect Master and the elders of the Hundred Saber Gate watched their genius with grave expressions. If he could kill Xu Yan, the status of the Hundred Saber Gate would rise greatly.

The geniuses of the Three Kingdoms, the Seven Sects, and the Eight Aristocratic Families had all entered the Thousand Martial Hall to receive a half-month of strength enhancement and the cultivation of powerful secret techniques.

In just half a month, their strength had undergone such a huge leap, the profundity and methods of the Transcendent Spirit Sect were truly inconceivable. To be able to dominate the Spiritual Realm for countless years, the foundation of the Transcendent Spirit Sect was beyond imagination.

Xu Yan's expression was calm. The opponent's strength had indeed increased greatly, but he was no longer at the Divine Will Realm, but had broken through to the Divine Origin Realm. Although he had just broken through, his strength was already far beyond what it was half a month ago.

He raised his right hand, the middle and index fingers together, and drew a sword in the air. A divine sword appeared.

"Presumptuous!" The Hundred Saber Gate genius was furious.

Xu Yan actually treated him with such disdain, not even bothering to draw his sword. He roared and unleashed a powerful slash.

Although he was angered by Xu Yan's disdain and arrogance, his first strike was his strongest attack, a torrential onslaught of saber light.

"Trifling tricks!" Xu Yan sneered, his two fingers lightly tapping, and the divine sword he had summoned instantly pierced through the Hundred Saber Gate genius' attack, shattering it.

The Hundred Saber Gate genius staggered back, his expression one of disbelief. Xu Yan reached out and grasped the divine sword, the sword intent already permeating the surroundings, unnoticed by the Hundred Saber Gate genius.

"It seems Xu Yan has grown stronger again!"

"Was he cultivating to enhance his strength in just half a month?"

The watching martial artists who had witnessed the previous battle were all amazed.

In just half a month, Xu Yan had grown stronger again.

"Have you noticed, Xu Yan's cultivation seems a bit different? His martial dao energy is rather unusual."

"I've noticed it too, it's quite different from the martial dao energy I'm familiar with, almost... divine?"

"Who knows what realm Xu Yan is in? It doesn't seem to be the Refined Spirit Celestial Realm."

Many martial artists were puzzled by Xu Yan's martial dao energy, as if it was not of the Spiritual Realm.

"There's nothing strange about it. Xu Yan is so strong, the martial dao technique he cultivates must be extraordinary. That is likely the source of his strength."

"Yes, his martial dao technique must be of an extremely high level, that's why he dares to confront the geniuses of the Transcendent Spirit Sect."

Some martial artists were not surprised, believing that Xu Yan's exceptional martial dao technique was the reason for his strength.

"In the past, the Myriad Star Martial Institute had cultivators who claimed to be researching unique martial dao techniques. Could it be that the technique Xu Yan cultivates is one they developed?"

"That's a possibility!"

Some martial artists who had experienced the Myriad Star Martial Institute era were also astonished.

On the Thousand Martial Hall gate, the Thousand Martial Hall Master and the others had gloomy expressions.

"Xu Yan has grown stronger again, and his martial dao technique..."

With their insight, they could clearly see that Xu Yan's martial dao energy was extraordinary, and they could not accurately determine his realm, whether it was truly the Refined Spirit Celestial Realm.

Of course, the powerful experts of the Thousand Martial Hall also believed that the unique martial dao technique Xu Yan cultivated was likely developed by Wu Tiannan and the other former Myriad Star Martial Institute cultivators.

"You are strong, but that's all!" The Hundred Saber Gate genius' expression turned cold. Xu Yan had easily shattered his attack, making him realize that no matter how he struck, he was no match for Xu Yan.

The only option left was to unleash the Burn Life Killing Intent! But once he used the Burn Life Killing Intent, he would also die.

There was no choice left now, he had to go all out.

Boom! The Hundred Saber Gate genius struck again, this attack even more ferocious, a hundred saber strikes combined into one, a hundred strikes merged into a single slash. This was his ultimate killing technique.

"So this is the Hundred Saber Gate's ultimate technique?"

"So powerful!"

The watching martial artists were full of admiration.

In the air above the arena, the layers of saber light converged in an instant, forming a single slash that seemed to split the heavens and earth.

"He's going to use the Burn Life Killing Intent!"

The Thousand Martial Hall Master and the others all tensed up.

Would the Burn Life Killing Intent be able to kill Xu Yan? Or at least severely injure him?

Xu Yan unleashed a sword strike, mountains and rivers appearing, the sword light layering, as if it could dissolve all attacks within the mountains and rivers.

A seemingly simple sword strike, yet it contained boundless killing intent.

"Now!" The Hundred Saber Gate genius' expression hardened, and suddenly a blood-colored aura surged from within him.

Terrifying killing intent descended, crossing the distance in an instant, appearing before Xu Yan!

This killing intent carried a sense of taboo.

The Hundred Saber Gate genius who had unleashed the Burn Life Killing Intent seemed to have lost his soul, his life force diminished by 99%, his physical body even sunken in.

But he had a smile on his face.

How would Xu Yan defend against this strike?

Once the Burn Life Killing Intent was unleashed, all the martial artists were horrified. What a terrifying secret technique!
