Seeing this scene, all the onlookers were stunned. Looking at the two intertwined dragons, the corners of their mouths twitched involuntarily.

No wonder the jade-white true dragon was almost crying with anger. This entangled posture was indeed somewhat indecent!

"You caused such a great disaster in the Divine Realm and thought you could just leave? You've been subdued by me now, so surrender."

The jade-white true dragon struggled, furious.

Then she became aggrieved, her voice carrying a crying tone: "You people are so despicable, taking advantage of my youth and ignorance. You bastard, humiliating me like this, I- I..."

At this point, the golden giant dragon had dragged the jade-white true dragon over. The jade-white true dragon seemed to be sobbing, having given up struggling.

However, the tremors in the Divine Realm continued. They all realized that great changes in the Divine Realm had begun, and it was all related to this true dragon.

Swoosh! A figure instantly came forward and said, "Young friend Xu, this dragon has committed heinous crimes and brought calamity to the Divine Realm. In my opinion, we should skin her alive, extract her tendons, and divide her flesh to eat!"

When the jade-white true dragon heard this, she immediately screamed in fright: "You dare? You all dare? My grandfather won't let you get away with this!"

However, she had no strength to struggle. At this moment, her eyes were filled with despair.

"Young friend Xu, you absolutely cannot spare this true dragon!"

"Yes, the evil of this dragon cannot be forgiven!"

"We implore Young Master Xu to severely punish this dragon, skin her alive, extract her tendons, eat her flesh and drink her blood!"

In an instant, emotions ran high. They wanted to skin the true dragon alive, extract her tendons, eat her flesh and drink her blood. They even invoked the greater good of the Divine Realm, and didn't forget to praise Xu Yan, saying they would erect statues of him, make his name known for thousands of years, calling him the savior of the Divine Realm's great calamity, and so on.

Old Man Xiao narrowed his eyes slightly, silently observing, but didn't speak.

The Sect Master of Tai Miao and others seemed lost in thought. Several elders were somewhat tempted, but were stopped by the Sect Master of Tai Miao's gaze.

Xu Yan's expression remained unchanged as he looked at the jade-white true dragon and said, "You see, they all want to eat you. Even if I let you go, in your current state, could you survive?"

"What- what do you want?"

The jade-white true dragon's voice was trembling, her eyes full of despair.

In her view, with so many powerful experts in an uproar, invoking the righteousness of the Divine Realm, seeking justice for the victims of the Divine Realm, and the temptation of erecting statues to make his name known for thousands of years, Xu Yan would surely agree.

She was doomed!

"If you want to live, open your mind."

"As long as I open my mind, you can protect me?"

"Of course. They're just a bunch of nobodies. Do they think they can threaten me?"

Xu Yan smiled calmly.

"Alright!"

At this point, she had no choice. And by opening her mind, no matter what method Xu Yan used, as long as her grandfather appeared, he would surely be able to remove it.

Xu Yan raised his hand and waved, imprinting a mark into the true dragon's mind. It was a small golden giant dragon, the dragon-subduing mark he possessed after mastering the Dragon Subduing Palm.

Once the true dragon was imprinted with the mark, she was thoroughly subdued! The true dragon only felt as if a small golden dragon was coiled in her mind, emanating a certain majestic aura that made her not dare to act rashly at all.

Her heart sank involuntarily.

"What kind of technique is this? It's too terrifying. And the golden dragons he manifested are almost indistinguishable from us true dragons. It seems to be a method that specifically counters us true dragons!"

Thinking of this, she became less confident about whether she could remove the golden dragon mark from her mind.

At this moment, the voices of angry people were growing louder and more numerous. The Immortal Heavenly Venerable Lords who had successively arrived also joined in after learning about the situation. Their eyes were fanatical as they stared at the flawless jade-white true dragon.

After Xu Yan imprinted the dragon-subduing mark, he waved his hand, and the Dragon Subduing Palm force disappeared, restoring the true dragon's freedom.

"Young Hero Xu..."

The crowd of Immortal Heavenly Venerable Lords, their eyes fanatical, wanted to say something more.

"Enough noise! All of you, shut up!"

A voice like thunder rang out.

Then sword light rose from all directions, filling the world with a sense of annihilation, as if in an instant, it could kill all the powerful experts.

"How I handle matters is none of your business!"

Xu Yan's gaze was cold, his sword intent soaring to the sky.

The uproar of voices immediately fell silent.

"Young Master Xu, this true dragon has caused such a great calamity and killed so many. How can you just let her off like this? Where is the face and dignity of our human race? Could it be that Young Master Xu, you would disregard the dignity and face of our human race..."

An Immortal Heavenly Venerable Lord began to speak righteously and indignantly.

Puff! A sword light slashed down, and before he could finish speaking, he directly turned to ash and scattered.

"What does your dignity have to do with me, Xu Yan? What, do you represent the human race now? Talking about dividing and eating at every turn. Do you think you can deceive me, Xu Yan, because I'm young and don't understand your intentions?

"Or do you think my sword is not sharp enough?!"

Xu Yan took a step forward, sword light soaring to the sky. The Ten Directions Annihilation Sword illuminated in all directions, filling the world with a sense of annihilation, as if anyone who dared to say otherwise would be annihilated to ash! At this moment, the clamoring martial artists suddenly felt a chill in their hearts. Their faces showed shocked expressions as they suddenly realized that Sword God Xu Yan was also a fierce person!
