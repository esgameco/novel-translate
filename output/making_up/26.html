Chapter 26 What a Demonic Disciple This Is (Seeking Continued Readership)

"Gulp!"

Feeling the terrifying aura of his master, Xu Yan's heart was trembling. The excitement and thrill of entering the martial dao had instantly subsided.

"I am too weak!

"In the face of my master, I am like an ant. I still have a long way to go!"

At this moment, Xu Yan felt incredibly insignificant. After just entering the door, what right did he have to be excited? What right did he have to be proud?

"It must be that my master saw me being arrogant, so he revealed a bit of his aura to let me know what true strength is.

"I must be humble, low-key, and diligently cultivate!"

Xu Yan restrained the aura around him and respectfully approached his master.

"Master, your disciple has entered the martial dao!"

Li Xuanyi had one hand behind his back, slightly trembling, which was from excitement, but his expression remained calm, still like a strict teacher.

"Mm."

He nodded slightly, revealing a look of contentment as he gazed at his foolish disciple.

"Did you see it? This is the aura at the threshold of mastery. Your master is showing you a glimpse."

Li Xuanyi said calmly.

"Master, I will be humble and diligently cultivate!"

Xu Yan said with an ashamed expression.

Li Xuanyi retracted the aura and looked at his disciple, saying contentedly, "I give you one year. That you could enter the threshold so quickly pleases me."

He stepped and sat down on the chair under the big tree. Xu Yan hurriedly brewed tea for his master and served him respectfully.

"The martial dao is boundless. You have just entered the threshold. You still have a long way to go. Let me ask you, is your heart resolute in the martial dao?"

Li Xuanyi asked solemnly.

"Master, my heart is resolute in the martial dao!"

Xu Yan knelt down with a resounding voice, his heart filled with excitement. "Master, are you truly going to take me as your disciple and teach me the supreme martial arts?"

"Good, very good. From today, you are my true disciple!"

Li Xuanyi looked very pleased.

Then, with an seemingly indifferent tone, he said, "My disciple, as you have cultivated arduously, do recall when you refined your skin, bones, and organs."

Regarding this demonic disciple, Li Xuanyi was truly curious about how he had managed to cultivate the fabricated techniques successfully.

When Xu Yan entered the martial dao, his golden finger appeared, and he directly achieved mastery of the techniques. Naturally, he also had the processes and methods of refining skin, bones, and organs in his mind.

These processes and methods of refining skin, bones, and organs were obviously insights that Xu Yan had gained himself.

They could not be said to be completely unrelated to the fabricated techniques he had, as they were derived from those. But ultimately, the fabricated techniques were fabricated, while the actual cultivation methods were real.

The two were different. The fabricated techniques could be seen as a set of illusory theories, while Xu Yan had realized those illusory theories and turned them into actual cultivation methods.

Only by understanding how Xu Yan had taken the fabricated techniques and derived the real cultivation methods could Li Xuanyi continue to edit the subsequent cultivation methods and martial dao realms.

Li Xuanyi felt that even fabricated techniques required a certain theoretical basis, even if it was illusory. The realms had to be connected and clearly tiered. They could not jump from the martial dao realm to the immortal cultivation realm.

That would be impossible to cultivate.

Li Xuanyi felt that these insights of his were likely conveyed by the golden finger.

"Yes, Master!"

Xu Yan heard this and his heart was filled with excitement. His master was going to guide him, to help him fully comprehend the cultivation process and experience the profundity of the martial dao.

If not for the master's hints, encouraging him to refine the jade bones, how could he have become so powerful now?

He never expected such a coincidence would lead the disciple to misunderstand.

"This foolish disciple is also a prodigy, actually comprehending the method to refine the jade bones and defeating the Fire-Maned Wolf!" Li Xuan sighed inwardly, this disciple was truly too much of a prodigy.
