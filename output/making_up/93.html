"Have I overlooked something and failed to fully comprehend it?"

"Bring out the cultivation method, let me take a look and test you two, then I will know if you have truly grasped the true meaning of the technique."

Li Xuan seemed to have already determined that they had not comprehended the true meaning of the cultivation method.

"Yes, esteemed elder!"

The two of them did not think much about it.

They had already determined that the cultivation method they practiced was a false martial art, and since it was not to be shared externally, they no longer cared.

Moreover, how could a high-level expert deign to look at such a crude false martial art technique?

"Please wait a moment, this disciple will write out the cultivation method."

Xie Lingfeng respectfully said.

"No problem."

Li Xuan nodded.

Shi Er brought over paper and brush for the two of them.

Xie Lingfeng and Hu Shan began to silently write down the cultivation method.

Li Xuan spoke again, "Write down all the cultivation methods you know, perhaps you two have only bitten off more than you can chew and failed to truly comprehend their true meaning."

"Yes, esteemed elder!"

The two of them perked up.

"What if the cultivation method I practice is not a false martial art? Perhaps I have simply failed to comprehend its true meaning and thus have not cultivated true martial arts?"

Thinking this, a look of excitement appeared in his eyes.

An hour later.

Xie Lingfeng and Hu Shan had written down all the cultivation methods they knew and practiced.

Li Xuan took a glance at them, truly just a glance, without even looking at the specific content of the cultivation methods.

As a high-level expert, what need was there to carefully examine them?

Especially since he had no intention of actually evaluating their cultivation methods.

"Whether true or false, as long as the martial arts heart is resolute, there will surely be attainment."

Li Xuan put down the cultivation methods and said calmly.

Xie Lingfeng and Hu Shan immediately became dejected.

"It seems these are false martial art techniques, the elder's words are just consolation."

Hu Shan's eyes grew dim, and the hope that had just been ignited seemed to have been extinguished.

"Esteemed elder, please guide us, on what path should this disciple's martial arts journey go?"

Xie Lingfeng prostrated himself on the ground and pleaded.

"Esteemed elder, please provide guidance!"

Hu Shan also prostrated himself on the ground.

Li Xuan considered his words carefully before slowly speaking, "You two, the foundation is already set, to start over would not be advisable... However, since you have cultivated... Let's call it true qi.

"The true qi is impure, thus your martial arts are not firm, and the foundation is not solid.

"The primary issue is to refine the true qi, and return to the right path of martial arts."

This was the strategy Li Xuan had already prepared.

"Elder, how should we refine the true qi?"

Xie Lingfeng respectfully asked.

When he was in the capital of Qi, he had discussed this problem with Xu Yan.

The only problem was that Xu Yan had not yet broken through to the Xiantian Realm, and could not provide a method.

As long as they could refine the inner martial qi into true martial qi, it would be the first and crucial step to return to the right path of martial arts.

If they could not refine it into true qi, everything would be in vain.

They could only continue cultivating the false martial art and break through to the Grand Ancestor Realm.

This was not what Xie Lingfeng wanted.

Even if he became a Grand Ancestor, what of it?

He would still be unable to enter the Sword Dao, unable to become a true martial arts cultivator.

"Refining true qi, it is both difficult and not difficult."

Li Xuan did not give a guarantee.

After all, how to refine the inner martial qi into true qi, he had derived it based on the foundation of refining blood qi into true qi.

Whether it would succeed, he could not guarantee.

Hu Shan did not quite understand, and asked, "Elder, what do you mean by that?"

Meng Chong couldn't help but speak up to explain, "My master's meaning is, whether you can refine true qi depends on your talent, and whether you can comprehend the cultivation method.

"You must know that for a cultivation method, some can cultivate it successfully, while others cannot even enter its door.

"This is why it is both difficult and not difficult."

Hu Shan heard this and his face flushed red with embarrassment as he lowered his head and said, "This disciple is truly stupid!"

He was somewhat panicked inside: "Could it be that I am unable to comprehend it, unable to refine true qi?"

Xie Lingfeng, on the other hand, was not worried. He firmly believed that he could refine true qi.

"Please, elder, teach me the method to refine true qi!"

He prostrated himself on the ground respectfully.

"Since we have met, so be it."

Li Xuan nodded.

"Thank you, elder!"

Xie Lingfeng and Hu Shan were greatly excited.

"Listen carefully..."

Li Xuan imparted the method of refining true qi to the two of them.

Xu Yan suddenly understood after hearing the cultivation method, so that's how it is! It's similar to refining blood qi into Xiantian true qi.

"Have you remembered it?"

Li Xuan asked after finishing the explanation.

"This disciple has remembered it!"

Xie Lingfeng respectfully replied.

Hu Shan opened his mouth, and finally nodded, "This disciple has also remembered it!"

Li Xuan glanced at Hu Shan, this guy clearly had not remembered it, but he was too lazy to bother with him. He would go ask Xie Lingfeng if he didn't understand.

This also revealed the difference in their talents.

"Thank you, elder, for imparting the technique!"

Xie Lingfeng respectfully kowtowed three times.

Hu Shan hurriedly followed suit, kowtowing three times forcefully.

"You may rise."

Li Xuan spoke.

These two truly understood etiquette!

The way they kowtowed was even more intense than when his own disciple bowed to him.

"This disciple will no longer disturb the elder's tranquility."

Xie Lingfeng cupped his hands and took his leave.

"Mm."

Li Xuan nodded slightly.

"Brother Xie, let's spar."

Xu Yan was greatly excited.

After breaking through the Xiantian Realm, he had not yet exchanged blows with Xie Lingfeng.

"Alright!"

Xie Lingfeng nodded.

He could gain insights from every sparring session.

Meng Chong also had an excited look on his face as he looked at Hu Shan, "Let's spar."

Hu Shan was still troubled, he hadn't fully remembered the cultivation method for refining true qi, and just now he didn't dare admit it in front of the elder.

"Okay."

He responded absentmindedly when Meng Chong invited him.

The four of them left the courtyard and went to the mountains outside the county city.

"Young master, I didn't fully remember the cultivation method for refining true qi, could you explain it to me again?"

Hu Shan approached Xie Lingfeng, embarrassedly speaking up.

Xie Lingfeng looked at him with a speechless expression.

"I... I didn't dare say it in front of the elder."

Meng Chong patted his shoulder and said, "Come, come, let me see the martial arts of an Inner Realm Ancestor Master."
