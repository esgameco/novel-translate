Especially now that he had broken through to the Heaven and Earth realm and his divine abilities had been enhanced, he should have no problem manifesting eighteen divine true dragons to entangle a Supreme realm true dragon.

He might not be able to fully subdue it, but he could ward off the dragon's threat.

As for endurance, after breaking through to the Heaven and Earth realm, Xu Yan feared prolonged battles least of all.

Xu Yan understood that martial artists of the same realm could vary greatly in strength, sometimes with vast differences between them. True dragons were no exception - even at the same level, their powers were not necessarily equal.

Even so, Xu Yan was confident that even if he couldn't win, he could easily escape.

After breaking through to the Heaven and Earth realm, his Traceless Thought divine ability had become even more powerful. If he wanted to leave, mere Supreme realm beings couldn't stop him. They wouldn't even be able to track his whereabouts, let alone chase and kill him.
