A young man was troubled by his inability to sense his qi and blood after three days of trying. He was anxious and dejected.

Gritting his teeth, he stood in a horse stance to sense his qi and blood, when suddenly he seemed to see a fleeting shadow approaching, and a voice sounded in his ear.

"The qi and blood move with the heart. Calm the heart, still the breath, and comprehend the self..."

The shadow extended a hand and tapped him, and in that instant, the young man felt a warm flow surge in his chest and abdomen. He was overjoyed, having sensed his qi and blood! "That must be the Martial Ancestor!"

The young man was filled with reverent admiration, having received the guidance of the Martial Ancestor.

Li Xuan sighed inwardly. The Hundred Martial Spirits Shadows were similar to clones, and even stronger than clones, with no restrictions. But limited by his own strength, the range of activity of the Hundred Martial Spirits Shadows was still limited.

"As my cultivation advances, they should ultimately be able to move freely outside, engage in combat, and not be restricted by distance."

Suddenly, an inspiration flashed in Li Xuan's mind.

"As my realm advances and my martial dao cultivation progresses, the ability to create martial dao clones should also be developed. But to obtain the ability to create clones is not easy."

Li Xuan pondered deeply, trying to figure out at what realm he could theoretically develop the ability to create something akin to clones, such as imbuing power into an object that could be carried and activated in times of danger.

"How can I construct a coherent theory that is also practically feasible? I need to ponder this thoroughly, or else the theory will be too vague and lack cohesiveness. It may take a long time for me to truly comprehend and realize it."

Thinking about this, Li Xuan's gaze turned to his Golden Finger. It was emitting a faint golden glow, but the outline of the Golden Finger was not yet visible.

"When I can clearly see the Golden Finger, perhaps I can try to utilize it to help develop this ability."

If he could use the Golden Finger as an aid, it should become much easier.

Since obtaining the Hundred Martial Spirits Shadows, Li Xuan would occasionally deploy them to wander the city, occasionally pointing the way for the young cultivators practicing martial arts.

The rumor that worshipping the Martial Ancestor could bring martial dao fortune, and that the diligent cultivators had a chance to receive the Martial Ancestor's guidance, became increasingly widespread and believed.

He had completely perfected himself, and was confident that upon breaking through, he would undergo another transformation.

Outside the Evergreen Pavilion, on a mountain peak, Wen Yan sat in meditation.

Wen Yan's breakthrough to the Penetrating Profound Realm did not involve any bottlenecks. If not for the need to consolidate his foundation and accumulate inner strength, he could have broken through to the Penetrating Profound Realm even when he perfected the Innate Realm.

Now, his breakthrough was a natural progression.

The spiritual energy of heaven and earth poured in, Wen Yan's true qi constantly refining within. The vast and boundless sea of qi in his dantian was swirling like a vortex.

As the sea of qi grew stronger, the first drop of true essence appeared.

The Heaven and Earth Bridge was also undergoing a transformation, seeming to become a bridge to the heavens, with a golden dragon lying beneath, the mountains and rivers depicted upon it, as if the masses were walking within.

Boom!

Wen Yan's entire being was in a state of sublimation. Although he did not cultivate the physical martial dao, his jade-like physique was the foundation, pure and flawless. When he broke through the Innate Realm, he had also transformed into an ethereal bone.

His physical body's strength and resilience was terrifyingly powerful.

The sword intent surged around his body, the mountains and rivers depicted within. At this moment, Wen Yan seemed to be within a mountain and river, or the mountain and river were within his physical body, forming a profound state.

As Wen Yan continued to break through, he was also interweaving and transforming.

At a certain moment, above the sea of qi in his dantian, it was as if a torrential rain was falling, the raindrops constantly dripping, wanting to fill the dantian.

Whoosh! The sea of qi in the dantian became a vast and mighty ocean, the true essence surging and crashing, forming wave after wave. And above the true essence, clouds and mists gathered, the wind whirling and stirring the clouds, forming a vortex.

The vortex continuously dripped raindrops, which were the true qi that had not yet condensed into true essence, as well as the spiritual energy pouring in from heaven and earth.

Wen Yan's breakthrough was ongoing, constantly in a state of sublimation and transformation.

At a certain moment, Meng Chong and the others watching Wen Yan's breakthrough were suddenly struck by an illusion - the one sitting in meditation was not Wen Yan, but a mountain and river.

Or perhaps the mountain and river were beneath Wen Yan, or Wen Yan was within the mountain and river.

"What's going on?" Su Lingxiu shook her head, and when she looked again, everything was as it had been.

Meng Chong pondered, was this a phenomenon that occurred during the senior brother's sublimation and transformation?

Boom!

Suddenly, in the midst of the surging spiritual energy of heaven and earth, a mountain and river image coalesced, majestic and desolate, seemingly filled with a sense of destruction and ruthless killing intent.

Any living being that entered this mountain and river would be annihilated within.

Harmonizing with the spiritual energy of heaven and earth, penetrating the profundities of the cosmos! This was the Penetrating Profound Realm!

"Senior brother has broken through to the Penetrating Profound Realm," Su Lingxiu said excitedly and with reverence.

When would she be able to break through to the Penetrating Profound Realm? She must redouble her cultivation efforts to strive for an early breakthrough to the Penetrating Profound Realm.
