"No wonder Xu Yan has such confidence, even the supreme experts may not be able to catch up with him."

On the Thousand Martial Hall sect, the powerful figures' faces turned gloomy, Xu Yan's movement technique was so swift that even ordinary supreme experts might not be able to catch up.

"From the Three Kingdoms Seven Sects and Eight Great Families, select a few geniuses, and in this half month, enhance their strength, and even refine a supreme killing intent for them."

The group of elders were startled, refining a supreme killing intent for the geniuses was actually cheating.

This was almost a method of mutual destruction.

Now they were going to use it on Xu Yan.

"The Spiritual Beast Clan, the Azure Sea Spirit Tribe, are threats, when the Spirit Bridge opens, they must be completely suppressed."

The Thousand Martial Hall Sect Master's gaze was cold and his killing intent was intense.

"Yes, Sect Master!"

The group of elders were solemn in their hearts, the Sect Master was going to completely change the pattern of the Spirit Realm when the Spirit Bridge opened.

The Thousand Martial Hall Sect Master turned to leave, but suddenly thought of something, stopped in his tracks, and then said, "In the sect, those Refining Spirit Realm experts who have been unable to successfully refine the Heavenly Earth Spiritual Mechanism and are getting old, it is also time for them to be loyal to our Thousand Martial Hall.

The group of elders became solemn upon hearing this.

The Sect Master actually wanted to use this move! "Sect Master, is it really necessary to do so?"

An elder asked in a deep voice.

"The Blood Demon Calamity, the reason it appeared was because we were not decisive enough back then, otherwise how could the Blood Demon have risen? Such a mistake must not be made a second time!"

The Thousand Martial Hall Sect Master said in a deep voice.

"Yes, Sect Master!"

Hearing this, the crowd felt a chill in their hearts.

The Blood Demon Calamity was the greatest crisis in the history of the Spirit Sect.

In the Sect Master's eyes, Xu Yan's threat level was already comparable to the Blood Demon back then?

The Thousand Martial Hall Sect Master stepped away.

The elders also began to arrange the subsequent countermeasures to deal with Xu Yan's challenge in half a month.

In the core area of the Thousand Martial Hall's back mountain, entering from a cave, after walking for only a few tens of meters, a huge underground hall appeared before their eyes.

This huge underground hall was directly excavated from a spiritual ore vein, and above the hall, the crystalline spiritual ore was permeated with a strand of profound aura.

The originally crystalline spiritual ore had colorful halos appearing on it, the halos constantly flowing and changing, as if reflecting the cycle of the four seasons of heaven and earth.

At the back of the hall were a number of small spiritual ore houses, five of which were closed, indicating that someone was in seclusion cultivation inside.

The Thousand Martial Hall Sect Master looked at it for a while, the profound aura that had permeated into the spiritual ore above the hall was being absorbed into the five spiritual ore houses.

"The Myriad World Alliance, Wu Tiannan, the foundation of the Transcendent Spirit Sect, how can you imagine it? The confrontation between geniuses is doomed to fail!"

The Thousand Martial Hall Sect Master thought with intense killing intent.

"In the past, the Myriad Star Martial Dao Academy recruited too many spiritual body geniuses, which allowed you to show a bit of your might, but the core geniuses of our Transcendent Spirit Sect have not even taken action yet.

"This time, let the scattered cultivators of the Spirit Realm, let you Myriad World Alliance see the strength of our Transcendent Spirit Sect's core geniuses!"

Wu Tiannan, you are just a rural bumpkin, thinking that you have cultivated some martial dao and reached the limit of the Spirit Realm, so you can act recklessly?

"You want to ascend the Spirit Bridge, I won't let you!"

Looking at the five spiritual ore houses, the Thousand Martial Hall Sect Master's expression relaxed, even if Xu Yan was prodigious, he could not be compared to the core geniuses of the Transcendent Spirit Sect.

The core geniuses of the Transcendent Spirit Sect had been rare in appearance in the Spirit Realm since ancient times, even if they had taken action, their names would not be spread, and no one would know about them.

"The existence of the Transcendent Spirit Sect is for the core geniuses... Wu Tiannan, you are just a frog in the well."

The Thousand Martial Hall Sect Master murmured a few words and entered an empty spiritual ore house to cultivate.

...

Xu Yan left the Thousand Martial Hall gate, preparing to find a place to break through, but suddenly his expression changed, and he looked back.
