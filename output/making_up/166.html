Xu Yan's blood boiled with excitement.

"Keep contemplating, the more you do, the stronger you will become, the more you can comprehend the supreme martial dao!"

Li Xuan was inwardly excited. He was not afraid of his disciples contemplating, but rather feared that they would not.

He now had the Innate Unbreakable Spiritual Bone and the Dragon Soaring Mountain and River Spiritual Body. If Suling Xiu's transformation could grant him a new constitution, what could he gain?

Li Xuan was confident that even if there were powerful innate constitution wielders, they would still be left behind by Xu Yan, who cultivated the powerful martial dao that Li Xuan had created.

Returning to the manor, Li Xuan sat down in a chair, leisurely waiting for the system's feedback on Suling Xiu's transformation.

Suling Xiu felt herself becoming increasingly ethereal, as if she had a natural affinity with plants and flowers. She could even clearly perceive the properties of medicinal herbs, including their age and characteristics, even for herbs she was unfamiliar with.

The heavenly and earthly spiritual energy continuously refined into her innate true qi, which was warm and gentle yet contained a fierce intensity. A faint blue flame of true qi appeared on her palm, the manifestation of the medicinal dao's innate realm.

Suling Xiu swallowed another medicinal pill, and her breakthrough had entered the final stage, her physical transformation complete.

"So this is the power of the Medicinal Dao's innate realm," Suling Xiu marveled, her strength rapidly increasing. Even peak sect masters would not be her match, though she was still inferior to Xu Yan and Meng Chong in terms of pure combat power.

However, this was still not enough. There were great masters and peak great masters within the Black-Robed Faction, and to deal with them, she needed to reach the Innate Realm Perfection and beyond.

Fortunately, with her two senior brothers, she could finally breathe a sigh of relief, knowing that even if a great master attacked, she could escape, or her senior brothers could handle it.

Suddenly, a blue radiance burst forth from Suling Xiu, and the plants and trees around her flourished, their leaves becoming even more vibrant.

She had broken through to the Innate Realm!

Her constitution had also completed its refinement and transformation.

"Your disciple Suling Xiu has broken through to the Innate Realm, and your Medicinal Dao has advanced to the Profound Realm," the system's feedback came.

"Your disciple Suling Xiu has broken through to the Innate Realm and transformed into the Azure Wood Spiritual Body. You have gained the Innate Spiritual Body."

The Azure Wood Spiritual Body! Suling Xiu's special constitution had transformed into this after her refinement.

"Innate Spiritual Body?" Li Xuan contemplated the changes in his body. With the Innate Spiritual Body, he could feel it perfectly harmonizing with his Innate Unbreakable Spiritual Bone. His physical body had become even stronger, and his affinity with the heavenly and earthly spiritual energy had skyrocketed, allowing the spiritual energy to constantly flow into him and instantly transform into his innate true essence.

To outsiders, it would seem as if the spiritual energy around him had simply become denser, without any obvious changes.

The Dragon Soaring Mountain and River Spiritual Body had been refined into the Innate Spiritual Body, inheriting all its characteristics and becoming even more powerful.

"My strength has increased again!" Li Xuan was greatly excited.

The true source of happiness was the growth of one's strength.

"This junior disciple is impressive, no wonder I took her in as my disciple and even killed a great master for her."

Li Xuan was filled with anticipation, eager to see what benefits Suling Xiu's transformation would bring him.
