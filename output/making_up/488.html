So Li Xuan smiled and said: "The Dragon Subduing Palm is a palm technique for subduing dragons, using dragons to subdue dragons. Can true dragons cultivate it? Theoretically yes, especially since you true dragons can take human form, so you can naturally cultivate it.

His disciple had already grown strong and was no longer a martial arts novice. It was time for him to be independent, so as his master, Li Xuan wouldn't interfere.
