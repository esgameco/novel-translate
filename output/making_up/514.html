"The Netherworld has already been broken to this extent. There's no need for it to exist anymore!" Blood Prison sighed somewhat.

Boom!

Bang! A corner of the realm shattered, and countless beings in the entire Netherworld Realm died instantly.

"How is this possible?!" The Blood Lords were all shocked. Why did the laws of the Netherworld Realm ripple open?

Boom! Blood Prison struck down with another palm, his figure walking step by step towards the Netherworld Realm.

The realm shattered a bit more.

The Blood Lords roared in anger, erupting with all their power. They joined forces to activate the laws and launched a fierce attack.

However, with just a stroke from the opponent, the laws of the realm became gentle, losing their attacking power. The remaining attacks from the five Blood Lords were easily neutralized by the opponent.

The gap between minor realm lords and a Lord of Heaven and Earth was ultimately too great.

Blood Prison walked step by step towards the realm, entering the Netherworld Realm. No matter how the Blood Lords activated the laws of the Netherworld Realm or launched attacks, they could not stop his footsteps.

"Your Excellency, it's you, isn't it? Are you going to destroy the Netherworld?" Suddenly, Blood Lords Ming and Yu stopped with bitter smiles. The other three Blood Lords fell silent.

Apart from Mingyu, the creator of the Netherworld Realm, no one else could smooth out the laws of the Netherworld Realm and not be attacked by them!

"Mingyu is dead. There is only Blood Prison, the guardian of the Immutable Divine Palace!" Blood Prison's figure paused as he spoke coldly.

"Your Excellency, you actually joined the Immutable Divine Palace!" Blood Lords Ming and Yu couldn't accept the reality before them for a moment. How proud and unyielding their lord had been back then.

No one could have imagined that he would submit to the Immutable Divine Palace.

"There's no such thing as joining or not joining. It's just mutual benefit. I'm giving you a chance to return under my command." Blood Prison's gaze fell on Blood Lords Ming and Yu as he spoke.

The two Blood Lords fell silent, raising their heads to look at the Netherworld Realm, filled with anxiety and the wailing of Netherworld warriors, Blood Kings, Blood Spirits, Blood Sons and others who had followed them.

"What about them? Your Excellency, you created the Netherworld Realm. All the beings in this realm exist because of you. Surely..."

Blood Prison spoke coldly: "Since I created this realm, and they exist because of my realm, their life and death are at my discretion. Out of consideration for our past relationship, I'll give you twelve spots. Choose your followers yourself."

Blood Lord Ming wanted to say something more, but Blood Prison coldly said: "Mingyu is dead, so naturally the Netherworld Realm should not exist. I am now Blood Prison, guardian of the Immutable Divine Palace. Do you understand?"

All five Blood Lords fell silent, struggling internally. Resistance would only lead to pointless death.

"We... are willing to follow Your Excellency!"

In the end, all five Blood Lords lowered their heads. This realm was created by Mingyu, and if even he had given up on it, what was the point in persisting?
