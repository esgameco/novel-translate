Chapter 6: The Stages of Refining Bones

"Refining bones has its own intricacies and is divided into different stages..."

Although Xu Yan's question about refining bones caught Li Xuan by surprise, he was not the least bit flustered.

After all, in his previous life, he was a writer of online novels, so his ability to fabricate stories was not bad. These past few days, after drinking the chicken soup cooked with nine-leaf spirit ginseng, his memory had become stronger and his mind sharper. He quickly thought of how he should fabricate the story.

"Today, I shall explain to you in detail the knowledge about refining bones.

"Refining bones is an extremely important foundation. One cannot be careless about it, as it is related to the strength of one's martial dao. If the bones are not sturdy, then the body will not be strong..."

Li Xuan's mind was racing as he began to fabricate a coherent story.

His theoretical explanations were delivered one after another, captivating Xu Yan, who nodded frequently, sometimes with a look of sudden enlightenment.

"So refining bones has so many intricacies. It's a good thing that Master has been constantly teaching me to be calm and patient, and not to be hasty. Otherwise, if I had rushed into refining bones without knowing the intricacies, wouldn't I have ruined my prospects in the martial dao?"

Xu Yan sighed inwardly.

"Indeed, I must heed Master's teachings, not be hasty, but be steady and contemplative!"

The more he listened, the more he felt that the profound nature of the refining bones stage. Once this stage was completed, his strength would surely increase several-fold.

"I have already mastered refining skin. Once I complete refining skin, I must settle down and carefully ponder the dao of refining bones before starting on that stage. I cannot be too eager and rush into it!"

Xu Yan made up his mind.

Li Xuan had fabricated a lengthy story, all the while observing Xu Yan's expression. Seeing Xu Yan listen so attentively, he was quite satisfied and nodded approvingly, saying, "My disciple, you must remember that starting cultivation slowly does not mean your future cultivation will also be slow.

"Just like building a house, the foundation is extremely important. If the foundation is not solid, the base unstable, it cannot withstand the wind and rain and will easily collapse. Cultivation is the same. Pursuing speed in the initial stages will actually limit your own potential and the future of your martial dao.

"Throughout history, there have been many geniuses who cultivated at a breakneck pace in the beginning, but later on, with an unstable foundation, their progress stagnated, and they reached their limits."

Li Xuan said these words with the intention of comforting Xu Yan. Although Xu Yan had not yet sensed his qi and blood, in the foundational stage, he need not pursue speed, as a solid foundation is the key.

Xu Yan, however, heard it as an encouragement. He thought, "Master is telling me that even though my cultivation speed may not match the ancient geniuses, as long as I lay a solid foundation, I can still surpass them in the end!"

"Master has such high hopes for me. I absolutely cannot let him down!"

"I must build a solid foundation and exceed those ancient geniuses!"

Xu Yan was deeply moved. He respectfully said, "Master, rest assured, your disciple will remember your teachings, not be hasty or impatient, and solidify the foundation. I will not disappoint you!"

Li Xuan nodded approvingly and said, "That you can understand the intention behind my teachings brings me joy."

He heaved a sigh of relief inwardly: "Finally, I've managed to deceive him again. Even if he doesn't sense his qi and blood for a month or two, he should be able to remain calm, especially after drinking the chicken soup cooked with nine-leaf spirit ginseng. His body should have grown stronger, and he must think it's due to his cultivation."

"But deceiving him can only work for a while. Especially when it comes to cultivation, and with Xu Yan's not-so-sharp mind, I still need to think of a way to extricate myself. After all, he has a powerful background."

Having stabilized Xu Yan's mindset, the rest would be much easier. Just let him continue cultivating. There was still a year to go.

"Master, you said refining bones is divided into stages. Are there any intricacies to that?"
