To advance against the tide, that is the true mark of a strong person, and he still lacked some courage.

Break through the heart's barrier and look down on the Spiritual Sect and Aristocratic Families!

Although the Mysterious Armory was not fully refined, he had refined some formation tools. The Illusory Confusion Formation, the Phoenix Fire Deadly Formation, and the even higher-level Bewildering Reversal Formation could all be deployed.

"Good! We brothers shall advance against the Refining Celestial Cultivators!"

Xu Yan had a smiling expression.

Li Xuan sighed inwardly, this elder brother Xu Yan was very capable, and he truly possessed the fearless and advancing spirit.

Peerless experts all rise up in this way.

Xu Yan has the bearing of a protagonist!

The four brothers left the manor and searched for a suitable place to set up the formation.

Fang Hao looked down and instantly found an excellent terrain, which was not only a good place for formation deployment but could also enhance the power of the Heaven and Earth Extraordinary Formation.

With the formation tools in hand, the formation was quickly set up. After the formation was completed, Fang Hao did not stop there.

He had a serious expression, beginning to draw restrictions, fusing them with the formation, and then deploying the Heaven and Earth Extraordinary Formation, fully utilizing the insights he had gained in the Extraordinary Martial Dao during this period.

"Elder brother, second brother, sister, wear these formation pearls, and you won't be affected by the formation."

He handed three formation pearls to Xu Yan, Meng Chong, and Su Ling Xiu.

"Good!"

The three of them took them.

Su Ling Xiu held an iron shovel, feeling a bit nervous, after all, they were about to fight the Refining Celestial Cultivators!

Fang Hao stood in the center of his Extraordinary Formation, waiting for the Refining Celestial experts to arrive. His palms were sweating, as this was the first time he had faced Refining Celestial Cultivators.

Xu Yan rose into the air, standing on a large tree, waiting for the enemy to arrive.

Meng Chong's figure disappeared into the formation.

At this moment, the formation had not yet fully activated and was in a hidden state.

An hour later, two figures flew swiftly from the sky.

Shu Lian Cheng and Ke Lin Ping, upon receiving the news, were transmitting messages back to the Jade Spirit Sect and the Shu Family while rushing to the outskirts of the Zheng National Capital, where Xu Yan had appeared.

Although Xu Yan and Meng Chong had joined forces to slay a Refining Celestial Cultivator, if they encountered one alone, they might be in danger. But with the two of them working together, there was no such concern.

"Unexpectedly, Xu Yan is actually hiding in the Zheng Nation. Could it be related to the Zheng Nation?"

Shu Lian Cheng said solemnly.

"The Zheng Nation does not participate in the martial arts conflicts of the Jade State. Although we have offered a bounty for Xu Yan and Meng Chong, they will not get involved, and they won't even pass the information on to us.

"Backed by the Transcendent Spiritual Sect, they are truly enviable!"

Ke Lin Ping said enviously.

Even if the Zheng Nation was indeed secretly sheltering Xu Yan, they dared not act against the Zheng Nation, except on the orders of the Transcendent Spiritual Sect. No power in the Spiritual Realm would dare to rashly attack the Zheng Nation.

"The Spiritual Realm's gate has been closed, and the Zheng Nation's task has also been lost. Could it be that they still..."

Shu Lian Cheng said, puzzled.

"The Spiritual Realm's gate has been closed, but that doesn't mean it no longer exists. It naturally needs guardians, and the Zheng Nation's task will continue."

Ke Lin Ping sighed.

It would have been better if this task had been given to the Jade Spirit Sect. With the Jade Spirit Sect's strength and the backing of the Transcendent Spiritual Sect, dominating the Jade State would be a matter of time, becoming a powerful sect among the top-tier Spiritual Sects.

Alas, the task belongs solely to the Zheng Nation's royal family! No one dares to interfere.

"Huh, is that... Xu Yan?"

Suddenly, Ke Lin Ping's expression changed as he said.

Shu Lian Cheng looked up and exclaimed with delight, "Yes, it's Xu Yan!"

The two exchanged a glance, immediately becoming excited, as with their strength, capturing Xu Yan would be no problem.

They immediately moved, their spiritual power surging out, rushing towards Xu Yan with lightning speed.

"Xu Yan, you will pay with your life!"

Shu Lian Cheng roared.

Xu Yan seemed to be taken aback, turning and retreating into the forest below.

"You can't escape!"

Ke Lin Ping said coldly.

Their spiritual power had already locked onto Xu Yan, and the two pursued him closely.

"They're here!"

Meng Chong's figure disappeared into a large formation, and Su Ling Xiu was nervous, tightly gripping the iron shovel, watching the two descending figures.

Fang Hao was even more anxious, his palms sweating, as this was the first time in his life he had seen the lofty Refining Celestial Cultivators, and the first time he would act against them.

With a rumble, the spiritual power surged as Shu Lian Cheng and Ke Lin Ping entered the formation. Fang Hao took a deep breath, stepping forward as the power of Heaven and Earth gathered.

The Extraordinary Formation was activated, with mists shrouding everything, directions becoming confused, and Phoenix Fire surging forth!

"Not good!"

Ke Lin Ping's expression changed, and he quickly retreated, but behind him was the terrifying Phoenix Fire, and the spiritual power he sensed was all shrouded in mist. He had lost his direction!

At this moment, it was as if he had fallen into a mysterious space.

Boom! Ke Lin Ping gripped a large axe in his hand and roared furiously, bringing it down towards the Phoenix Fire!

Rumble!

The Phoenix Fire surged, the axe's power terrifying.

Fang Hao's expression changed slightly, but he remained calm. He shifted his feet, and the Extraordinary Formation changed, the power of the formation surging, redirecting Ke Lin Ping's attack to where Shu Lian Cheng was.

Shu Lian Cheng, upon entering the formation, also sensed something was amiss and became alert, gripping a pair of short spears in his hands, warily watching his surroundings.

"Ke brother?"

He did not receive a response from Ke Lin Ping.

Suddenly, an axe beam slashed out from the mist.

"Not good!"

Shu Lian Cheng's expression changed, his two spears slashing out, and with a boom, he was forced to retreat two steps, though he had blocked the attack.

"Ke brother, it's me!"

He hurriedly called out in warning.

However, before he could finish speaking, another axe beam came slashing from the side.

Shu Lian Cheng hastily blocked it.

"What is going on here?"

He felt a chill in his heart, this place was extremely strange, his spiritual power was being interfered with, and he had lost his sense of direction.

Boom! Another axe beam came slashing.

Shu Lian Cheng's heart sank, realizing he couldn't continue like this, so he struck out towards the direction of the attack.
