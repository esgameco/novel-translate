Chapter 47: The Xu Family Calls the Shots in Donghe Prefecture

The crisis of the Xu family was revealed when Xu Yan's formidable power emerged, showing superhuman abilities, forcing Xu Junhe to consider some follow-up matters.

Collaborate with the Tianmu Sect and rebel together?

Xu Junhe dismissed this idea, as a rebellion by the Tianmu Sect would not succeed - they did not have the right timing, location, or people on their side.

There's no need to let his son act on behalf of others.

The Tianmu Sect is not worthy!

Moreover, the more crucial reason was that Xu Junhe understood his son.

Xu Yan was obsessed with the martial arts legends in story books, and now that he had truly cultivated and achieved results, he would undoubtedly focus more of his mind on martial arts cultivation.

After a rebellion, there would be many trivial matters that would require Xu Yan's involvement, which would delay his martial arts cultivation, a life Xu Yan did not wish to lead.

Naturally, Xu Junhe also did not want his son to be entangled in such trivial matters.

Therefore, the root cause of the struggle for the crown prince was in the capital Jing'an, and Xu Yan needed to go there to resolve it once and for all.

Suddenly, a long whistle sounded, coming from the direction of the Donghe Prefecture Magistrate's Office.

The Donghe Prefecture Magistrate quickly turned around, his expression changing repeatedly - the Donghe Prefecture Magistrate's Office had fallen?

Kou Ruozhi revealed a smile and cupped his hands towards Xu Junhe: "The Donghe Prefecture Magistrate's Office is now under the control of our Tianmu Sect. From today onwards, I, Kou Ruozhi, am the new Donghe Prefecture Magistrate."

The Donghe Prefecture Magistrate's face changed drastically, looking at Kou Ruozhi with shock and exclaiming, "You've even infiltrated the Magistrate's Office?"

Kou Ruozhi waved his feather fan and sneered, "You incompetent fools are only valued by the foolish old Emperor Qi. I Kou merely used a few simple methods."

Looking down on the Donghe Prefecture Magistrate, he continued, "You thought you could trap us like a fish in a jar, but did you not know that we were waiting for this opportunity?"

At this moment, dense footsteps came from the street, and a large group of soldiers surrounded them, led by the Donghe Prefecture Prefect!

"Your Excellency, the rebels have been apprehended."

The Prefect came before Kou Ruozhi and saluted.

He gestured behind him, and a servant brought forward a set of official robes.

Those were the robes of the Donghe Prefecture Magistrate.

Kou Ruozhi put on the official robes, then took out a thin mask and wore it on his face. In that instant, Kou Ruozhi's appearance was nearly identical to the Donghe Prefecture Magistrate.

"Throw all these impersonating Tianmu Sect rebels into the dungeon to await sentencing!"

Kou Ruozhi ordered in a voice barely distinguishable from the Donghe Prefecture Magistrate.

Thud!

The Donghe Prefecture Magistrate fell to the ground in shock, staring at Kou Ruozhi and exclaiming, "You want to impersonate me?"

"Is there a problem with that?" Kou Ruozhi chuckled.

The soldiers the Prefect brought had already disarmed and bound all the injured and fallen officials, and were now escorting them away.

"Get our Magistrate a new set of clothes and a new face."

Kou Ruozhi commanded.

One of the Tianmu Sect experts stepped forward and directly stripped off the Donghe Prefecture Magistrate's official robes, replacing them with a new set of clothes, and putting a mask on his face.

Now the Donghe Prefecture Magistrate had become the appearance of a different Tianmu Sect expert.

"Master Xu, how is it?" Kou Ruozhi asked with a hint of pride.

"Excellent tactics!" Xu Junhe praised.

"Master Xu, what do you think of our collaboration?" Kou Ruozhi asked with a smile.

"As for the collaboration, let's wait until you can completely control Donghe Prefecture," Xu Junhe replied calmly.

"Then Master Xu, please wait and see!" Kou Ruozhi stepped out, his gait indistinguishable from the Donghe Prefecture Magistrate's.

Evidently, he had been secretly imitating the Donghe Prefecture Magistrate for a long time.

The Tianmu Sect had actually infiltrated the Prefecture Magistrate's Office, and Kou Ruozhi was truly a formidable individual.

"Take them all away, do not disturb Master Xu!" Kou Ruozhi waved his hand and said.
