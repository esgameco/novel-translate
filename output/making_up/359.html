White Cloud was overjoyed, listening attentively, not wanting to miss a single word of Xu Yan's guidance, which was like pointing the way for these inner domain experts.

As long as there was a path, there was hope!

Although he could let go and not harbor any ill intentions, some others might not be the same.

For now, no one had developed any other thoughts, as they had not yet felt a sense of hopelessness about their future path.
