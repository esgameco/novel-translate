The demon might surged. Although it couldn't intimidate and subdue true spirits at the Supreme level, the Divine Lord level true spirits trembled under its demon might, unable to exert their full power, making them easy prey.

That horrifying killing intent was like countless steel needles aimed at him.

"Chi Mao, well done, keep going!"

Tian Zi was extremely excited.

"No problem!"

Although Chi Mao had subdued some true spirits, the number of untransformed true spirits driven by Mu Yao was simply too great, and the Great Wilderness Realm side was still at a disadvantage.

"Transforming true spirits into demons? Not bad!"

Li Xuan watched the changes outside the realm with some surprise.

Who would have thought the demon race could subdue true spirits so easily? This was an unexpected boon.

"It's been a long time since I've taken action. Since it's so lively outside the realm, I'll go join in the fun. It's a good opportunity to hone my martial arts."

Meng Chong rubbed his head and headed straight for outside the realm.

"I'll go join in the fun too!"

After all, if a Realm Lord retreated, the unrestrained Realm Lord level true spirits would cause a huge impact and could potentially cause the battle situation to instantly deteriorate.

The powerful fighters in the midst of battle suddenly saw a god-like figure walk out from within the realm.

A giant hundreds of zhang tall, his whole body shining with golden light, as if clad in armor, or as if surrounded by surging golden waves.

"It's the Heavenly God Meng Chong, he's already become so powerful!"

Meng Chong's appearance immediately caused exclamations.

Mu Yao's expression changed again as he looked at Meng Chong with uncertainty.

"What kind of martial arts is this? Did Tai Cang leave behind such powerful martial arts within the realm?"

He was somewhat shocked, "If Tai Cang hadn't died and lived until now, how strong would his power be?"

Thinking this, Mu Yao couldn't help but admire Tai Cang. If Tai Cang hadn't died, even the Untransformed Divine Palace probably wouldn't be able to deal with him now.

"Die!"

Meng Chong threw out a punch, directly blasting apart a true spirit! Seeing such a domineering and fierce scene, Mu Yao was even more shocked - this physical body was even more terrifying than untransformed true spirits! Meng Chong charged straight into the group of true spirits. Divine Lord level true spirits simply couldn't withstand a single punch from him. At this moment he was exerting his full power, freely displaying the might of his physical body martial arts.

As Meng Chong took action, all the martial artists, even the Realm Lords in the midst of battle, were deeply shocked. This physical body, this savage power, was truly terrifying to the extreme! If they were at the same level, could they even withstand one of his savage punches?

Hum! At this moment, another figure appeared, holding a sharp spear. As this spear emerged, many Divine Lord realm martial artists suddenly felt their souls tremble.

Even though this spear was not aimed at them, just looking at it made their souls uncomfortable!

"This is... martial arts targeting the soul and consciousness?"

Mu Yao was shocked again.

At this moment, as he looked towards the Great Wilderness Realm, a trace of wariness appeared in his eyes.

The changes in this realm had exceeded expectations!

As soon as Jiang Buping made his move, although not as savagely fierce as Meng Chong, spear light bloomed with each step he took, instantly annihilating the consciousness of untransformed true spirits one after another, leaving only their physical bodies behind.

"Let me help you recover a bit!"

At this moment, green light bloomed as a beautiful young woman stepped forward, seeming to leave behind endless vitality wherever she passed.

Her delicate fingers pointed, green needle light appearing and piercing into the body of an injured martial artist. In an instant, that martial artist's injuries were healed and even their expended energy was recovered.

At the same time, pills scattered out one after another.

Following that, several more green needle lights and pills landed on a Realm Lord who had expended a great deal of energy. In just a moment, that Realm Lord's energy was recovered and they were revitalized, beating back that true spirit.

What shocked the martial artists even more was that wherever the young woman passed, the vitality of true spirits was extinguished, as if their vitality had been transferred to the injured, instantly healing them.

Even more terrifying was that the young woman condensed an alchemy furnace in mid-air, extracting the vitality and essence blood of true spirits to refine into pills.

The originally injured martial artists who had no fighting strength were now shouting excitedly as they enthusiastically rejoined the battle, not sparing themselves at all, completely unafraid of being injured.

Divine ability: Sky-Holding Earth-Destroying!

Just based on Meng Chong's strike earlier, if he were already a World Lord, the power he could unleash - apart from that one person in the Immutable Divine Palace, the other experts of the same level in the Immutable Divine Palace probably couldn't withstand it.

"Fortunately, we came to probe this time. Now that these geniuses have come out, they shouldn't think about returning to their world. They must be killed here."

Thinking of this, Mu Yao felt somewhat relieved.

He continuously swung the whip in his hand, making cracking sounds. Those unconverted spirits seemed to be driven by it, becoming even more agitated and vicious. They let out roars as their momentum for attacking the world suddenly increased.

From the Immutable Land, unconverted spirits continued to arrive endlessly, joining the ranks attacking the world. Besides the spirits driven by Mu Yao, there were also some that originally wanted to enter the world. Sensing an opportunity now, they joined in as well.
