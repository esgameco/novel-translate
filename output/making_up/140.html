Truly worthy of being the Academy's Elder, a Grand Perfection Sword Dao Master, a single strike is extraordinary.

"So this is the technique that defeated my Sword Sovereign Cliff's Grand Perfection Master? It is truly fierce, I wonder how Xie Senior would deal with this move?"

Leng Qiu thought to himself.

He is a disciple of the Sword Sovereign Cliff, known as the genius second only to Xie Lingfeng, he came to the Seven Star Academy to spar with their Sword Dao, paving the way for his own breakthrough to the Patriarch realm.

Xu Yan shook his head and slapped out a palm.

The Golden Dragon roared and suddenly plunged into the layers of sword light, in an instant, the sword light connecting heaven and earth was shattered.

"Rotate!"

The elder's expression became solemn, the broken sword light suddenly began to rotate, this is one of the killing moves of the Heaven and Earth Frigid Light.

However, the fierce Golden Dragon directly struck the sword light that the elder had just started rotating, before the sword momentum could form its power, it was thunderously hit.

Boom! The sword light exploded and collapsed, and the Golden Dragon's tail swept the elder away.

Silence! The entire venue fell silent again.

Such a powerful move, yet it still couldn't withstand the young man's palm strike? What kind of technique was that Golden Dragon, truly powerful beyond measure.

The elder was knocked to the ground, feeling somewhat dazed.

For a moment, he was at a loss, his proud sword dao, actually so easily defeated? Against ordinary martial artists, it would certainly be invincible.

In Xu Yan's eyes, this move was not as good as Xie Lingfeng's Myriad River Sword Technique, let alone the Flying Rainbow Sword Technique.

If he were to face Xie Lingfeng, this Heaven and Earth Frigid Light would be shattered by a single Flying Rainbow Sword Technique.

So far, among the sword cultivators Xu Yan has seen, only Xie Lingfeng has truly grasped the threshold of the Sword Dao, cultivating the Sword Technique to the peak.

And he is also close to breaking free from the shackles of the Sword Technique.

"I wonder if Xie Senior has already attained the Sword Heart Enlightenment?"

Xu Yan thought to himself.

It should still not be the case, after all, to attain the Sword Heart Enlightenment is not an easy task.

The elder was dumbfounded, he had cultivated for half a lifetime, yet he had not even entered the door of the Sword Dao?

He opened his mouth wanting to refute, but as a loser, he had no right to refute. In the end, he sighed, "I have learned."

Leng Qiu stood in place, completely stunned by Xu Yan's words, he gained some new insights from that.

"Who is this person, his talent in the Sword Dao, could it be that he has already surpassed my Senior?"

Xu Yan nodded, the Seven Star Academy's Patriarch is not bad, he didn't fly into a rage or feel humiliated by the defeat, he just accepted it. He continued walking up.

The eighth layer.

The guardian was a Peak Patriarch.

Xu Yan had defeated the Seven Star Academy's Patriarch guardians one by one on his way up, and all were defeated with a single palm strike. Now the Seven Star Academy could no longer sit still.

The guardian of the eighth layer was among the top of the Seven Star Academy's Peak Patriarchs.

"Whew, it's Elder Ding Yan!"

The students of the Seven Star Academy exclaimed in shock.

"What, Patriarch Ding Yan?"

The surrounding martial artists were also shocked.

A young man actually alarmed the renowned Patriarch Ding Yan to come guard the platform, it's clear the Seven Star Academy felt the pressure.

Elder Ding Yan, with a fearsome reputation, in the peak of Patriarchs, he is ranked among the top thirty in the inner realm.

On the arena, a man in a wide robe and long sleeves, with three strands of long beard, stood elegantly, holding a three-foot long calligraphy brush, a precious treasure.

"Young friend, do you have the qualifications to enter the platform and spar with me?"

Xu Yan nodded, "Yes, you are the first one who has the strength to let me enter the platform and spar."

Ding Yan's strength is very powerful, not much weaker than the Half-Step Grand Patriarch Yin Hong.

And he is just a Peak Patriarch.

However, in Xu Yan's eyes, the so-called Half-Step Grand Patriarch is actually just an expert whose strength is not enough to break through to the Grand Patriarch realm in one go, so he can only break through halfway.
