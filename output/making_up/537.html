Blood Demon was furious and shocked.

He had also devoured Blood Demon's sub-soul, and since the sub-soul and Blood Demon were of the same origin, this meant Blood Extreme would not be restricted by Blood Demon's secret techniques for controlling the sub-soul, nor would he be actively rejected by Blood Demon's divine soul as a foreign invader.

Moreover, the runes on Blood Extreme's divine soul were transformed from a strand of Li Xuan's creative power, which was the key to ensuring Blood Extreme would not be destroyed.

Therefore, although Blood Demon was powerful, he now found himself in a bitter struggle.

Blood Extreme also marveled that as expected of a realm lord, he was indeed formidable. But he wasn't discouraged. As long as his divine soul couldn't be destroyed, he could wear him down slowly.

The final victor would surely be him.

He would replace Blood Demon and become the realm lord!

"Hehehe, don't resist anymore. You are me, and I am you. I obtained the Ancestral Dao's opportunity, which is equivalent to you obtaining it. Let me be the true you."

Blood Extreme continued to struggle with Blood Demon while speaking, continuing to deceive him.

Otherwise, if outside forces intervened, he would have a chance of failure, and the changes in the Cang Realm would be exposed.

This battle of divine souls couldn't be resolved in just a few days, but Blood Demon was confident that in the end, he would successfully devour the sub-soul and obtain everything it possessed!

"Hehehe, stop resisting futilely."

Blood Extreme laughed arrogantly. He had already taken control of half of Blood Demon's body. At this moment, Blood Extreme discovered that Blood Demon wasn't in the Immutable Palace.

This place seemed to be inside some mountain peak in the Immutable Land?

If so, there was no need to worry about exposure. He engaged in a fierce struggle with Blood Demon. Due to his initial advantage and Blood Demon's misjudgment of the situation, Blood Extreme controlled half of Blood Demon's body, and for the time being, the struggle was evenly matched.

Blood Demon continuously employed various secret techniques, constantly suppressing Blood Extreme. Gradually, he discovered that this sub-soul seemed quite strange, as if it had long since ceased to be his own sub-soul.

The thought of this possibility terrified Blood Demon. Had he been schemed against?!

The plan against Blood Demon was finally complete.

After all, Blood Extreme's divine soul, after her refinement and birth as a soul seed, had become complete and was no longer a remnant soul. Moreover, Blood Extreme had also once received Purple Light's creation.

The worst outcome would be sharing power with Blood Demon.

With the scheme against Blood Demon concluded, Su Lingxiu could finally spare time to ponder dissecting Mu Xie and study the differences of this powerful being from the Immutable Land.
