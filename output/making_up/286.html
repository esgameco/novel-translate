Li Xuan spoke solemnly, word by word.

Xu Yan was deeply moved. "Indeed, my master has transcended heaven and earth!"

Since his master had transcended heaven and earth, a mere flick of his finger could shake the heavens and earth. That's why his master suppressed his cultivation level, acting like an ordinary person, rarely using his full power, and only matching his opponents at the same realm.

So the ultimate goal of the martial path is to transcend heaven and earth?!

"No, my master said the martial path has no end, and transcending heaven and earth is not the final goal!"

Xu Yan thought excitedly.

"I will set a goal for myself - to transcend heaven and earth within a hundred years!"

At this moment, Xu Yan had set a martial arts goal for himself.

To transcend heaven and earth within a hundred years!

Li Xuan continued, "Beyond the divine ability realm is the Divine Manifestation realm, using one's own principles to control the principles of heaven and earth, transforming them into the divine martial arts..."

Word by word, he passed on the martial arts of the Divine Manifestation realm to Xu Yan, including the diagrams of the principles of heaven and earth that he had extracted, integrated, and designed in order to create the Divine Manifestation cultivation method.

Without these diagrams of the principles of heaven and earth, cultivating the Divine Martial Manifestation would be extremely difficult. But with these diagrams, it was as if one had grasped the flaws in the principles of heaven and earth, able to integrate one's own martial arts principles into them and manipulate the principles of heaven and earth.

This was a perfect match. As long as Xu Yan could comprehend it, the cultivation of the Divine Manifestation realm would become much easier.

Xu Yan carefully memorized the martial arts of the Divine Manifestation realm, including the diagrams of the principles of heaven and earth. Since this was not the first time he had comprehended the diagrams of the principles of heaven and earth, it did not take him too long to memorize them.

Li Xuan had carefully selected and repeatedly simplified the principles of heaven and earth in the diagrams to make them easier to memorize. Given Xu Yan's talents, it was not too difficult for him to memorize them after a short period of time.

As Xu Yan was carefully contemplating the martial arts of the Divine Manifestation realm, Li Xuan silently waited, not continuing to pass on the martial arts of the Transcendent Void realm yet.

He would wait until Xu Yan had completely memorized and gained some understanding of the Divine Manifestation cultivation method, then pass on the Transcendent Void realm.

"Master, I have memorized it," Xu Yan finally said, opening his eyes respectfully.

"Good!" Li Xuan looked very satisfied.

"Since you have memorized the martial arts of the Divine Manifestation realm, I shall now pass on to you the martial arts that surpass the Divine Manifestation realm," Li Xuan continued.

"Yes, Master!" Xu Yan's heart was filled with excitement.

The Divine Manifestation realm was already so powerful, able to use the principles of heaven and earth for oneself. What could possibly be beyond that?

"The realm beyond Divine Manifestation is the Transcendent Void realm, shattering the spatial dimensions of heaven and earth, traversing the void..."

Li Xuan explained the power of the Transcendent Void realm.

Shattering space, traversing the void.

Xu Yan was deeply shocked. Once he broke through to the Transcendent Void realm, a single step would allow him to directly traverse space, roaming freely through the void, covering thousands or even millions of miles in an instant.

Although he would not have transcended heaven and earth, he would have disregarded the spatial dimensions and principles of heaven and earth. One could say that at this realm, he would be free to roam within the heavens and earth.

"To shatter space and penetrate the principles of heaven and earth requires immense strength, which depends on the power of one's own martial arts principles..."

Li Xuan began to impart the martial arts of the Transcendent Void realm.

Using one's own martial arts principles to break through the principles of heaven and earth and roam freely through the void - this was the Transcendent Void realm. So the key was to cultivate one's own powerful martial arts principles.

Li Xuan carefully explained the Transcendent Void martial arts to Xu Yan in detail. Fearing that Xu Yan might miss something at this powerful realm, he even repeated the explanation.

Xu Yan was immersed in the incredible power of the Transcendent Void realm. In his mind, he could already envision the scenes.

A single step would shatter the spatial dimensions, his figure disappearing between heaven and earth, only to reappear from a distant space. Even a single palm strike could send a golden dragon crashing through space to attack a distant enemy.

The more he thought about it, the more excited and impassioned he became.

The Transcendent Void realm was truly formidable.

Once he reached the Transcendent Void realm, to return to the Inner Realm or come from the Inner Realm to the Spiritual Realm, he would not need to pass through the Spiritual Realm gates - he could simply traverse the space directly.

"Master, I have memorized it!" Xu Yan calmed his excited emotions and said respectfully.

"Good," Li Xuan nodded, extremely satisfied.

After passing on the martial arts, Li Xuan turned and left, not disturbing Xu Yan as he continued to contemplate and settle the teachings.

"The future of my martial dao legacy rests on you," Li Xuan thought to himself, filled with anticipation.

"If I were to reach the Transcendent Void realm, how powerful would I be?" Li Xuan mused inwardly.

Although he had created the realms, he could not fully imagine the power of the Transcendent Void realm.

After all, the ultimate strength of the Transcendent Void realm would depend on how Xu Yan comprehended and cultivated it.

"What kind of divine abilities would the Transcendent Void realm possess?" Li Xuan wondered. If he were in the Transcendent Void realm, could he simply shatter a spatial dimension and stand tall in the void? He could not even begin to fathom such terrifying power.

Otherwise, they were all trash!

His arrogance matched his strength.

After Yu Changfeng made his move, the Demon Slaughter from the Three Seclusion Tower also took action, challenging the talents of the Myriad World Alliance and decisively defeating his opponent.

The momentum of the Spirit Sect surged, and the disdain for wandering celestial talents was unceasing.

The clash of celestial talents had officially begun.

But the top celestial talents of the Transcendent Spirit Sect had not yet descended the mountain, not even having their names spread.

It was well known that the peak celestial talents of the Transcendent Spirit Sect were all Spiritual Body cultivators.

Only those with Spiritual Body talent could become the top celestial talents of the Transcendent Spirit Sect.
