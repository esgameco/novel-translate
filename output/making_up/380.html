The Ten Thousand Thunder Sect master.

A great master!

Unfathomably deep!

With the nine layers of arrays, plus two Immortal Celestial Lords stationed there, it was enough to resist a powerhouse at the Great Yue Emperor's level for at least a day and night without issue.

Li Xuan sat leisurely in his chair, his eyes slightly narrowed, appearing to be resting comfortably, but his mind was actually focused on the Golden Book of the Great Dao.

The fifth martial art was finally fully compiled, including the theoretical framework, martial arts techniques, and even the fusion of immutable qi to transform it into part of the soul.

What happened next would depend entirely on Jiang Buping himself.

Jiang Buping shook his head and woke up from his slumber once again. This time he breathed a sigh of relief - there were no more of those bizarre visions.

Where was he? Had someone rescued him? He stood up listlessly.

"You're awake," Stone Two appeared before him.

"Thank you for saving me, brother," Jiang Buping said gratefully.

"I'm not the one who saved you. Come with me," Stone Two led Jiang Buping to Li Xuan.

Xu Yan, Meng Chong, Su Lingxiu, Fang Hao and the others, hearing that Jiang Buping had awakened, all came over curiously.

Jiang Buping was a bit dazed, but he gratefully said, "I am Jiang Buping. Thank you all for saving my life!"
