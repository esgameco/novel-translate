When he heard they were spies, the Great Yue Emperor's face changed dramatically. The people sent to the Qimen Institute and Danyi Institute were all supposed to be highly trusted individuals.

Yet such people turned out to be spies. How many more spies could there be within Great Yue? How many spies could be lurking even around himself? Just thinking about it made him feel a chill in his heart.

"Your Majesty, we are not spies! We..."

The hearts of several Shadow members sank.

Their infiltration skills were naturally excellent. To have infiltrated to this level took years of gradual progress step by step.

Once suspected, under careful scrutiny, there was no way they could hide from an Immortal Lord.

"Whether you are or not, we'll know very soon!"

The Great Yue Emperor's gaze turned cold.

He had absolute faith in the guidance of the esteemed expert.

Cheng Zhan and the person in charge from Tianwu Gate felt their hearts sink. One of the sect members sent here to guard was a high-level figure in the sect.

If even such a person was a spy, it was unimaginable how deeply infiltrated the sect had become.

"Your Majesty, this matter is of utmost importance. I must report to the Sect Master. Before that, not a word can be leaked."

Cheng Zhan said solemnly.

"I understand," the Great Yue Emperor nodded.

Seeing this, the Shadow members knew they could no longer remain hidden. And in front of the two Immortal Lords - the Great Yue Emperor and Feng Yan - they had no chance of escape.

Once thoroughly investigated, there was a possibility their affiliated forces would be exposed.

Poof! Suddenly, the auras of several people instantly withered, their souls dissipating into nothingness. They committed suicide decisively, so quickly that even the Great Yue Emperor, an Immortal Lord, had no time to stop them.

Their suicide method was extremely powerful. Once activated, it led to instant death and soul dissipation, with no way to reverse it.

Besides their souls dissipating and life force fading, the bodies of the several people also rapidly shriveled, as if wind-dried.

All traces of their auras vanished completely.

The goal was to leave behind not a single clue! At this point, their identities as spies were confirmed.

Cheng Zhan and the person in charge from Tianwu Gate could no longer sit still. They personally set off to return to their sect, as a secret investigation of the sect was now necessary to root out any remaining hidden spies.

"Thank you for your guidance, esteemed one!"

The Great Yue Emperor respectfully bowed with clasped hands towards Li Xuan's direction.

Next, he began investigating the capital of Great Yue. But before that, the Great Yue Emperor and Sui Hongwu personally screened each of their trusted imperial guards. Only after confirming there were no issues did they dispatch the imperial guards to begin a covert search.

"Master, how did you know they were spies?" Su Lingxiu asked curiously.

Fang Hao also nodded, equally curious.

"Nothing can escape your master's eyes," Li Xuan smiled, then added: "If you cultivate the Minor Heavenly Dao Eye, you'll be able to detect it too."

"The Minor Heavenly Dao Eye is too profound. Although I have some understanding, I'm still just short of grasping it," Su Lingxiu said helplessly.

The Minor Heavenly Dao Eye was extremely mysterious. She still hadn't been able to fully comprehend it.

"Then let your master give you some more guidance," Li Xuan said with a resigned expression.

By now, he had actually developed the Minor Heavenly Dao Eye, so it wasn't just making things up anymore. He knew clearly in his mind how to cultivate it.

So Li Xuan guided Su Lingxiu and Fang Hao on how to cultivate the Minor Heavenly Dao Eye.

However, he only provided guidance rather than teaching it exactly as is. After all, he wanted Su Lingxiu to gain her own insights. Perhaps the Minor Heavenly Dao Eye she comprehended would have some differences?

A Minor Heavenly Dao Eye unique to the Danyi martial arts.

After guiding Su Lingxiu and Fang Hao, the two went back to continue their contemplation. It was unknown for now when they would be able to fully comprehend it and successfully cultivate it.

Li Xuan continued to memorize the Taisang Book's principles, while temporarily reducing the time spent on creating the Divine Ability Martial Classic. He allocated more time to perfecting the system of Extreme Soul martial arts, as well as refining martial methods beyond the Heaven and Earth realm.

In the Nine Mountains realm, Meng Chong emerged from a celestial cave. After evading an encirclement, he hid in a concealed location to consolidate himself.

"It's not forces from the Qinghua realm, nor is it one of the major forces of the Nine Mountains realm. What force is targeting me?"

Meng Chong wondered inwardly.

Those masked people had diverse methods and were vicious and ruthless. Many of their people had infiltrated various forces in the Nine Mountains realm.

After several encirclements, Meng Chong felt that next time, this masked force would likely try to trap him inside a celestial cave.

"I can't go to celestial caves for now."

Meng Chong pondered. He had to find a way to investigate the identities of his attackers.

"Senior Brother appeared at Mount Dagai before. I'll go to Mount Dagai and see if I can encounter Senior Brother."

Meng Chong made his decision. So he concealed himself and set off towards Mount Dagai.

"I've finally broken through."

Xu Yan emerged from his secluded cultivation place, looking relaxed.

He had broken through to the Divine Image realm.

Upon entering the Divine Image realm, his strength increased significantly again.

With a thought, a divine image appeared, with the power of heaven and earth's laws transforming into a landscape of mountains and rivers. Within the landscape, a golden giant dragon coiled.

Hum! The Yin-Yang Indestructible Sword emerged, as if enveloping the mountains and rivers. At this moment, Xu Yan's form was hidden within the divine image, with only the powerful divine image standing tall between heaven and earth.

"The fusion of divine image and divine abilities is truly marvelous. To harm me, one must first break through my divine image. Only after breaking the divine image can they truly face my real body."

"The power of the divine image, fused with the laws of heaven and earth, is in some sense similar to an Immortal Lord's unity with heaven and earth."

Xu Yan assessed his current strength. The powerful defense formed by his divine image and the Yin-Yang Indestructible Sword meant that ordinary Immortal Lords could not harm him.

"However, if I encounter a powerful Immortal Lord like the Great Yue Emperor, I would still be shattered in one hit. But I no longer fear weaker Immortal Lords like Sui Hongwu or Feng Yan."

Xu Yan instantly felt much more confident.

He was now invincible below the Immortal Lord realm.

And this was just the entry level of the Divine Image realm.

Powerful Immortal Lords like the Great Yue Emperor were rare even in the Nine Mountains realm.

Of course, Immortal Lords weaker than the Great Yue Emperor but stronger than Feng Yan were still very dangerous to Xu Yan.

But having reached this level, even if he couldn't win, he truly had the ability to escape.

"When I reach the peak of the Divine Image realm, I'll be able to face even Immortal Lords like the Great Yue Emperor without fear. They won't be able to do anything to me."

The Great Yue Emperor was considered strong among Immortal Lords, but certainly not the most top-tier. The Great Yue Emperor himself understood this.

"Once I enter the Void-Breaking realm, Immortal Lords will truly no longer be a threat to me."

Only by breaking through to the Void-Breaking realm could one truly disregard the threat of Immortal Lords.

The void-breaking power of the Void-Breaking realm could pierce through the space of heaven and earth. In a sense, it was perfectly suited to break through an Immortal Lord's power of unity with heaven and earth.

It was like a counter to an Immortal Lord's control over the power of a region of heaven and earth.

"Meiwu is at Thousand Fold Mountain. Should I go see her? With my current strength, I should be able to handle it."

Xu Yan pondered.
