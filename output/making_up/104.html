Shi Er seemed to be at the stage of completing the Purification of the Dantian, unable to break through the Qi and Blood Realm. Then she could refine a Qi and Blood Elixir, which was an elixir that could help break through the Qi and Blood Realm.

"In refining elixirs, do I need to add water?"

"In elixir refinement, it is to extract the essence and remove the impurities. Adding water may increase the impurities.

Xu Yan and Meng Chong had been staring at the elixir furnace, looking at their shidi. The furnace was already heated, but their shidi still hadn't added the medicinal materials. Was elixir refinement this complicated?

The two of them knew nothing about elixir refinement, and could only wait for their shidi's next move.

Suli Xiushen suddenly realized, this was elixir refinement, not just boiling medicinal pills.

How could she have overlooked that the elixir furnace was a precious artifact, not an iron furnace?

Regaining her senses, she saw that the elixir furnace was already heated, with a scorching hot airflow circulating around it, estimating it was about ready.

"The first step of the Qi and Blood Elixir is to put in a piece of Nine-Leaf Yuan Zhi, and refine it..."

Suli Xiushen followed the elixir refinement method she had comprehended, throwing a piece of Nine-Leaf Yuan Zhi into the elixir furnace, closing the furnace lid, and raising her delicate white hand, about to go and strike the elixir furnace to refine the medicinal ingredients.

"Second brother, when you use your Qi and Blood to strike the elixir furnace, you need to be slow and gentle, not forceful..."

The eldest brother was at the Xiantian Realm, his Xiantian True Qi too strong, she couldn't control it well, so she turned to Meng Chong.

"Okay!"

Meng Chong was extremely excited, raising his hand with a jade-like golden light covering it, his Qi and Blood circulating, lightly striking the elixir furnace.

Boom!

A muffled sound, the elixir furnace trembled slightly.

Suli Xiushen sensed the trembling of the elixir furnace, carefully comprehending the changes in the furnace. "Second brother, the Qi and Blood has increased a little.

"Good!"

Boom!

Meng Chong struck another palm.

Suli Xiushen slightly squinted her eyes, extending her delicate white hand to gently stroke the elixir furnace, feeling its trembling, as if she could sense the changes in the medicinal ingredients inside.

"Lighter on the Qi and Blood."

Suli Xiushen continued.

Meng Chong struck the elixir furnace again.

Afterwards, Suli Xiushen occasionally touched the elixir furnace, adjusting the force according to her inner feelings, making the strikes on the furnace have a certain rhythm.

"Okay!"

After nine strikes, Suli Xiushen sensed that the Nine-Leaf Yuan Zhi in the furnace seemed to have melted.

"Big brother, when I open the lid later, seal the furnace mouth, don't let the medicinal power inside evaporate."

"No problem!"

Xu Yan nodded enthusiastically.

Suli Xiushen opened the elixir furnace lid, and a true Qi sealed the furnace mouth, while Suli Xiushen threw the second medicinal ingredient into the furnace, a blood-red medicine made from tiger blood.

"It's too troublesome to open the lid every time, I'll just put the remaining medicines in together. For the first elixir refinement, it can be considered summarizing experience."

Suli Xiushen thought this way, and put all the remaining medicinal materials in the elixir formula into the furnace, then closed the lid.

She added three more Spirit Fire Stones to the elixir furnace, intensifying the flames.

This time, Suli Xiushen did not let Meng Chong strike the elixir furnace, but gently closed her eyes, carefully comprehending the elixir furnace. Her delicate white hands occasionally lightly struck and shook the elixir furnace.

The force kept changing.
