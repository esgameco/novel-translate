The Flame Demon's consciousness was destroyed, transforming into a giant web of flames that sealed off all directions. At this moment, the flames automatically gathered together, turning into a bright red flame that emitted a scorching aura.

Xu Yan reached out his hand and directly absorbed the flame into his mouth. With a light tap of his finger, layers of restrictions fell, sealing away the flame.

Although he was not a practitioner of the esoteric arts, he was familiar with restrictions and knew how to seal treasures. After all, the art of sealing was not too profound and not difficult to learn, so Xu Yan, Meng Chong, and Su Lingxiu had all learned sealing techniques.

After being sealed, the flame became gentle and no longer emitted a scorching aura, appearing like just a bright red flower.

Seeing this scene, Duwu's expression changed repeatedly. After all, this flame contained a trace of the Flame Demon's primordial fire. What method did Xu Yan use to subdue the flame so easily?

He had thought that even with the Flame Demon's consciousness dispersed, it would take Xu Yan considerable effort to subdue this flame. He never expected Xu Yan to subdue the Flame Demon's primordial fire so easily.

Meiwu had encountered trouble and was preoccupied. The Flame Demon had also been defeated. Only the injured Duwu remained, and alone he was no match for Xu Yan.

"Xu Yan, I hope you truly rise to power. Otherwise, you will surely die one day!" Duwu said coldly.

With a bang, he self-destructed this avatar.

At this point, the three powerful beings who had sent avatars had all failed, especially Meiwu, who seemed to have been ambushed and suffered greatly.

However, both Duwu and the Flame Demon had taken note of Xu Yan. If given the chance, they would surely kill him!

"I'll meet your true selves sooner or later," Xu Yan said dismissively of Duwu's threat.

"Breaking through the void, heaven and earth, establishing the Dao - once I break through to the Establishing the Dao realm, I should be able to deal with them, right?" Xu Yan muttered to himself.

"It's about time I broke through."

Now that he had obtained the treasure, it was time to prepare for breaking through to the Breaking Through the Void realm.

"Tianshadiying, just you wait. Once I break through to the Breaking Through the Void realm, we'll take our time playing," Xu Yan said with a cold smile.

Once he entered the Breaking Through the Void realm, Immortal Venerable Lords would no longer pose any threat to him. Even if surrounded by Immortal Venerable Lords, he would not fear them.

The power of Breaking Through the Void could easily shatter an Immortal Venerable Lord's control over heaven and earth.

This breakthrough would be a significant leap forward.

Xu Yan emerged from the celestial abyss and gazed out at the vast and deep Guangyou Realm. He decided to find a place to seclude himself and prepare for breaking through to the Breaking Through the Void realm.

It was also time to sort through the gains from this venture.

Especially that jade bamboo - it looked extraordinary and might be useful in comprehending the Establishing the Dao realm.

Xu Yan departed in an instant to find a suitable place for seclusion.

In the Qinghua Realm, Li Xuan had not gone to see Tianzi for some time. He couldn't rush getting more secrets of heaven and earth from Tianzi for now.

"Your disciple Xu Yan successfully ambushed a supreme being with your sword. You have obtained the Thousand Machinations Technique," came the sudden feedback from the Golden Book of the Dao.

Li Xuan was immediately overjoyed.

He had successfully ambushed Meiwu.

"The Thousand Machinations Technique, that will be useful."

The Thousand Machinations Technique was a special divine ability that could control power that had invaded an enemy's body from an infinite distance. It could also control this trace of power, constantly strengthening it and even hiding it.

As long as the enemy's strength was not vastly superior, they would be unable to eliminate the power of the Thousand Machinations Technique.

When fighting an enemy of similar strength, as long as the enemy was wounded by oneself, one could use the Thousand Machinations Technique to continuously invade the enemy, preventing their wounds from healing.

Of course, it could also be used to secretly ambush enemies. Once an enemy was ambushed, even if only lightly wounded, the Thousand Machinations Technique could be used to continuously damage the enemy's body and worsen their injuries.

Anyone affected by the Thousand Machinations Technique, unless they had an absolute advantage in strength to instantly suppress and eliminate its power, would find it like a persistent parasite, impossible to get rid of.

"Not bad, not bad. Let's try out the Thousand Machinations Technique," Li Xuan said with a smile.

He focused his mind slightly and instantly sensed the power of his wooden sword.

"Hiss, Meiwu's strength is so formidable!" In that instant, Li Xuan couldn't help but exclaim.

Meiwu's strength was even greater than his current level.

"Although stronger than me now, my sword is not so easily eliminated. And now with the Thousand Machinations Technique, Meiwu will never be able to get rid of this sword," Li Xuan chuckled.

With a thought, his fingers moved slightly, and a mysterious aura appeared, seeming to connect across an infinite distance.

A sword chime rang out inside Meiwu's body. In an instant, that sword began to move within her, constantly refining external power to strengthen itself.

Boom!

"Meiwu's strength is impressive!" Li Xuan sensed the powerful force suppressing the sword and exclaimed again.

"However, you are completely unfamiliar with my sword dao. Trying to eliminate it is a foolish dream."

Li Xuan's fingers danced as he sensed that sword in the depths of consciousness. When faced with Meiwu's power, it suddenly dispersed, becoming formless and traceless, evading Meiwu's power and regathering into a sword somewhere else in her body.

"Although I've finished reading the Primordial Book, I won't be bored in the future."

Li Xuan was in high spirits. Fighting this powerful being Meiwu across space was truly fascinating.

Moreover, the battlefield was inside Meiwu's body, which made it even more interesting.

"Hmm, Meiwu's aura is fluctuating violently. Is she so angry she's about to go mad?"

Li Xuan raised an eyebrow.

Meiwu's strength was truly formidable. If not for the Thousand Machinations Technique controlling that sword, with Meiwu's power and methods, she would be able to eliminate the sword given some time.

Of course, that time might not be too short.

Although the power of this sword was not enough to pose a fatal threat to her, it constantly caused trouble inside her body. Meiwu had to divert some of her attention and power to control this sword and prevent it from growing stronger.

This meant that Meiwu's strength would be diminished, putting her at a disadvantage when facing former powerful enemies.

"This Thousand Machinations Technique is really interesting."

Li Xuan was thoroughly enjoying himself.

He controlled the sword inside Meiwu's body, playing a game of hide-and-seek with her.

"Meiwu's strength is roughly equivalent to the Establishing the Dao realm."

Li Xuan had also made a judgment about Meiwu's strength - about equivalent to the Establishing the Dao realm. Of course, not his Establishing the Dao realm, but that of cultivators from the Great Wilderness.

After all, Li Xuan's strength was a hundred times that of Xu Yan at the same level. Once he entered the Establishing the Dao realm, he would be able to crush Meiwu.

"The Establishing the Dao realm is still much, much stronger than the Heaven and Earth realm."

Li Xuan sighed inwardly.

The further one progressed, the greater the power gap between each realm became.

"Meiwu is indeed outside this world, not within this heaven and earth."

Using the Thousand Machinations Technique to sense that sword, Li Xuan confirmed that Meiwu was not within the Taicang world, but outside it. However, she was constantly getting closer to the Taicang world.

"Driven mad with anger? But it's useless. You can't enter the Taicang world, and even if you could, you wouldn't be able to find me. I'm not afraid of you anyway."

Li Xuan remained calm and continued to provoke Meiwu with the Thousand Machinations Technique.
