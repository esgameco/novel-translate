"So this is the 'Enlighten the Heart and Observe My Cultivation of the True Intent'. Every minute detail of change is within my consciousness, and I can perceive the subtle changes in bone refinement without any leaks."

At this moment, he had another enlightenment. Regarding the subtle changes in the bones, the changes in every part of the body, and the circulation of qi and blood, they were all within his consciousness.

The efficiency of bone refinement had once again greatly improved.

"The two-sentence formula is by no means just this, I have not truly grasped its essence yet!"

Xu Yan sighed inwardly, the formula passed down by his master was truly profound.

Each time he felt that he had grasped the profundity.

But the result, a flash of insight, suddenly revealed that his understanding was still not enough!

"Now I have undergone eight transformations, and my entire skeleton has turned into golden bones!"

"Only the last transformation is left, and I will be able to have a complete golden skeleton!"

The diligent bone refinement over the past half month, especially the renewed enlightenment of the formula, has deepened his control over qi and blood, and the speed of bone refinement has also increased significantly. He has now undergone the eighth transformation.

His entire skeleton has turned golden.

The ninth transformation, to have a complete golden skeleton, is not far away.

Xu Yan continued to cultivate, he had a premonition that the golden bones would be refined within the next one or two days.

"Once I refine the golden bones, I will be on par with the ancient geniuses, and I can start refining the viscera.

"Mastering it within a year should not be a problem.

"As for the jade bones, my master said that it is not something that can be achieved through talent and perseverance alone, I cannot be too ambitious and ask for too much."

Golden bones are already extraordinary, as for jade bones, that is too difficult.

If he aims too high and wastes time trying to refine the jade bones, it may prevent him from mastering it within a year.

Since he has already reached the level of the ancient geniuses, what else is there to be unsatisfied with?

Therefore, Xu Yan plans to start refining the viscera after completing the golden bones.

He can begin to comprehend the method of refining the viscera.

So, Xu Yan was refining his bones while also recalling and deeply contemplating the method of refining the viscera that Li Xuan had taught him.

With the foundation of refining the skin and bones, refining the viscera is not difficult.

He already has the direction for refining the viscera, the only thing left is to deeply understand the profundity and mystery of the method of refining the viscera.

...

Li Xuan yawned and looked at the cultivating Xu Yan, sighing inwardly, "This foolish disciple, he is really persistent, and he can even keep his composure?"

He was puzzled, after all this time, he had not sensed any qi and blood from Xu Yan, but Xu Yan could still persist without any impatience.

He showed no signs of anxiety, depression, or shame in front of Li Xuan for not being able to sense the qi and blood.

He also did not continue to ask Li Xuan how to sense the qi and blood.

"This mentality of his is truly rare, not anxious or depressed, but persistently cultivating diligently every day. If there really is a cultivation method, with his diligence, as long as his talent is not lacking, he can become a strong cultivator, right?"

"If he knows that I have been deceiving him, and the cultivation method is fabricated, how would he react?"

Li Xuan felt that it's better not to expose it.

Xu Yan looks like an honest person, bullying an honest person too much, if he explodes, that would not be a joke.

Moreover, he is also a person of extraordinary background.

"Forget it, let him continue to play around, now the path out of the village is also safe, once the one-year period is up, I'll just send him away."

"Maybe I can even collect another master's gift?"

"I can't stay in the Eastern River Prefecture or even the Qi Kingdom anymore, I'll go to the Wu Kingdom."

Li Xuan had already planned his retreat route.
