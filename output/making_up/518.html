Here is my full translation of the Chinese excerpt:

He couldn't help but break out in a cold sweat, realizing he had made a mistake! His senior sister was far from the elegant and quiet appearance she presented on the surface. When it came to brutality, she was no less fierce than him! "Of all people to provoke, why did it have to be senior sister!"

Jiang Buping looked at the screaming White-Robed Divine Lord and shook his head with a sigh.

"Is this senior sister's unique burial technique?"

Watching Su Lingxiu's special burial method, it wasn't simply burying someone in the soil, but continuously scattering their soul and life force into the earth.

Once the life force and soul were completely dispersed, only an empty shell would remain, which would quickly decay and eventually turn to dust.

"I'll teach you to be frivolous and arrogant. Did you really think this young lady had no temper?"

Su Lingxiu continued to pound the man while filling in the soil, her technique skilled and extremely fast.

The White-Robed Divine Lord's screams grew weaker, his skin sagging as he aged rapidly, like a candle flame in the wind.

At this moment, the White-Robed Divine Lord's eyes were filled with despair and fear as he looked up at the young woman filling in the soil above him. She seemed like a demonic witch - where was that pure and flawless aura of natural grace now? He regretted why he had targeted this young woman, but when he first saw her, her peerless beauty and unique temperament had deeply attracted him!

Bang! Everything went dark as the soil completely covered his head.

His life force was draining away, and his weakened soul was also dissipating.

Su Lingxiu finished filling in the large pit, jumped up and stomped on the soil a few times, then clapped her hands with an excited expression, as if she had just done something she enjoyed.

"Junior brother, let's go!"

Putting away the shovel, Su Lingxiu continued on her journey in high spirits.

"Coming!"

Jiang Buping hurried to catch up. The soil where the Divine Lord had been buried became rich with vitality. In no time at all, lush green grass sprouted and began to grow rapidly.

Soon, it transformed into spirit herbs, and continued to evolve.

The buried Divine Lord was not only dead, but in a short time, even his bones had become nutrients for these spirit herbs.

Before long, this area would grow many divine herbs, including some that could nourish the soul.

"This is the place."

Arriving at the location Blood Extreme had mentioned, Su Lingxiu looked down and said with a smile: "That Blood Extreme is truly cunning and good at hiding. He stayed right here without leaving, and that Supreme from the Jiang family didn't even discover him."

This was where Jiang Feng had confined Blood Extreme's remnant soul.

Although Blood Extreme was no longer in his original confined position, he hadn't left. He had hidden beneath the original confinement spot, concealing himself inside a stone.

This stone was hidden under thick layers of soil, concealing its unique properties. Even a Supreme would not be able to detect the stone's special nature through the thick soil.

Blood Extreme's remnant soul was hiding inside the stone, relying on its special properties to conceal himself. This was why the Supreme from the Jiang family had been unable to find him.

However, this couldn't escape Su Lingxiu and Jiang Buping's notice.

"To be on the safe side and prevent him from self-destructing, we should use some techniques."

Fine green needles appeared in Su Lingxiu's hand. These needles were full of vitality, yet could merge with all things in nature without leaving a trace.

With a wave of her hand, the fine needles instantly sank into the ground, piercing the soil around the stone. The remnant soul of Blood Extreme inside the stone remained completely unaware.

"Senior sister, let me directly confine his soul. Once confined by me, he won't be able to self-destruct even if he wants to."

Jiang Buping spoke up.

"Junior brother, just watch senior sister's technique!"

A jar appeared in Su Lingxiu's hand. She opened the lid and turned the jar upside down.

Hum! In an instant, the vitality of the fine needles piercing the soil around the stone dissipated. At the same time, the stone had already entered the jar.

"This is another application of my Life and Death Reversal Divine Technique. What do you think, junior brother?"

Su Lingxiu tilted her head back, looking quite proud.

"Senior sister, this divine technique is truly remarkable!"

Jiang Buping exclaimed in admiration.

At this moment, the stone inside the jar moved, and a thread of blood-colored light cautiously probed out.

"Blood Extreme, you've been caught. Just stay put obediently."

Su Lingxiu said with a chuckle.

Bang! Blood light surged out from the stone, revealing Blood Extreme's appearance. Unlike the other part of Blood Extreme's remnant soul, this soul fragment had a faint trace of purple.

"You..."

Blood Extreme was shocked. He had hidden so deeply, yet was still discovered.

"What happened to that part of my soul?"

Blood Extreme then asked gravely.

Although the part of his soul he had sent out was stronger in soul power than this part, the true core was this portion of his soul.

He had sensed when that part of his soul was captured, but didn't know what method the other party had used to prevent that soul fragment from self-destructing.

Even later on, his connection to that part of his soul seemed to be blocked, as if it had disappeared.

This had made him feel relieved. Although losing that part of his soul was a heavy loss that damaged his foundation, at least this remnant soul had been preserved.

As a result, he was caught completely unaware and now found himself trapped in this jar, with a feeling that he couldn't self-destruct!

This greatly alarmed Blood Extreme.

"It's fine. You'll be able to reunite with that part of your soul soon."

Su Lingxiu said with a giggle.

Looking at Blood Extreme in the jar, it was as expected - Blood Extreme's core soul had also undergone transformation from the Primordial Purple Light.

Therefore, if this part of the soul disappeared, the Blood Extreme in the other jar would be able to sense it and even be affected.

"Don't resist. This might be an opportunity for you."

Having achieved her goal in coming to the Taikun Realm, Su Lingxiu was eager to return, after all, Blood Demon's soul was still waiting for her.

After capturing Blood Extreme's core soul, Su Lingxiu suddenly had an idea of how to trick Blood Demon, and she believed Blood Extreme would be willing to cooperate.

Returning to the Qinghua Realm, Su Lingxiu immediately entered a secret chamber and placed the two jars together.

"You finally came."

Seeing the core soul captured, the other remnant soul became excited.

Blood Extreme, however, felt his heart sink. The part of his soul he had sent out seemed to have developed consciousness beyond his original awareness.

In a sense, he had been betrayed by the other part of his soul.

Su Lingxiu didn't immediately merge the two Blood Extremes together. Instead, she began to study the core soul of Blood Extreme, especially the part related to the Primordial Purple Light.

She even wanted to try if she could extract a trace of the Primordial Purple Light.

After several days of research, Su Lingxiu found that she couldn't extract the Primordial Purple Light. Even if she refined this part of Blood Extreme's soul, she couldn't obtain the Primordial Purple Light. However, she could acquire some of its essence, which might be what Jiang Feng needed.

Su Lingxiu had no intention of refining Blood Extreme's soul. Instead, she used this as a reference to concoct pills with some of the Primordial Purple Light's essence, using the laws and principles of heaven and earth as the main ingredients.

After finishing her research, Su Lingxiu hurriedly set off for the Great Wilderness. By this time, Tianzi had already begun to capture Blood Demon.

Crashing the Demon Realm into the Cang Realm was no small matter. One misstep could even cause damage to the Cang Realm. If that happened, it would be even harder to deal with the coming crisis.
