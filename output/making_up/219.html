Li Xuan drew the terrain, topography, and the special features of the natural formation of the Hunting Demon Battlefield into a pattern, and incorporated it into the Mysterious Gate Martial Dao.

He also extracted some of the laws of heaven and earth from the first page of the Tai Cang Book, and integrated them into this pattern, forming a true grand formation of heaven and earth.

Or one could say, the grand scheme of heaven and earth.

As the laws of heaven and earth were integrated, this grand formation became more complete, and the pattern looked profoundly mysterious.

"I drew the unique terrain of the Inner Realm into a pattern, and then integrated the laws of heaven and earth, forming a formation diagram. With this formation diagram, it will be easier for me to comprehend the Mysterious Gate Martial Dao."

A flash of inspiration came to Li Xuan's mind.

The unique terrain of the Inner Realm is mostly found in the Cyan North region.

After leaving the Hunting Demon Battlefield, Li Xuan looked at the towering peaks of the Cyan North, as if they were the boundaries of the Inner Realm.

And the gate to the Spiritual Realm was located here, indicating that these peaks had some special properties.

Were they the boundary mountains?

Li Xuan walked among the peaks of the Cyan North, sketching the terrain and topography into patterns.

After returning, he would fill them with the laws of heaven and earth, step by step, to perfect them and draw out the formation diagrams.

Once the formation diagrams were completed, the Mysterious Gate Martial Dao would be almost fully created.

As long as he mastered the entry, the subsequent Mysterious Gate Martial Dao would be relatively easy to develop, as he would have a clear cultivation direction.

After wandering around the Cyan North, Li Xuan looked at the towering peaks, curious about what lay beyond them. So he climbed up one of the peaks.

Standing at the summit, the biting cold wind howled, and even a peak master would be unable to withstand the frigid conditions for long.

Looking out from the peak, he saw a vast expanse of mist and fog beyond the mountain range.

Taking a step forward, he had already crossed over the peak and entered the misty realm.

Li Xuan's spiritual awareness enveloped the surroundings, and he couldn't help but frown. All he could see was the mist, until suddenly, it cleared, and he found himself on a small hillside.

There was no icy wind or snow, only lush greenery and the sounds of insects and birds.

"Something's not right!" Li Xuan took a deep breath. He had just flown from the peak, and it couldn't have been more than ten li away, but when he looked back, he could vaguely see the towering peaks of the Cyan North in the distance, far beyond the ten li range.

"It's as if I've been teleported away."

Li Xuan's brow furrowed, but he became intrigued.

The peaks of the Cyan North were the boundaries of the Inner Realm, the edge of the realm, and crossing over them seemed to have teleported him to a distant location.

Was this a teleportation formation set up by someone? Or was it the work of the laws of heaven and earth?

Li Xuan leaned towards the latter.

He moved his body and headed back towards the peaks. Soon, he returned to the Cyan North region.

He crossed the peak again, but the result was the same - he ended up in a distant location.

Even with his full focus and spiritual awareness covering the area, he couldn't detect any changes.

Standing at the summit again, this time Li Xuan slowly walked down the mountain, using his physical steps to cross over.

Halfway down the slope, Li Xuan's gaze shifted, and within the coverage of his spiritual awareness, he seemed to detect a subtle change in the terrain below the peak.

This change was imperceptible to the naked eye.

"The laws of heaven and earth are circulating here!" Li Xuan took a deep breath.

He suddenly had a suspicion that there might be some issues with the laws of heaven and earth of the Tai Cang, and this place was the source of the problem, causing the leakage of the power of the laws of heaven and earth, forming a teleportation force.

This force could instantly transport people to distant locations.

"Although I'm only at the Spiritual Awareness Realm, my spiritual awareness has refined the will of heaven and earth, so I can detect these leaking powers of the laws of heaven and earth."

Li Xuan realized.

Even cultivators at the Refined Spirit Celestial level might not be able to observe these leaking powers of the laws of heaven and earth.

But his ability to sense them was due to the fact that his spiritual awareness had refined the will of heaven and earth, and he had also thoroughly memorized the laws of heaven and earth from the first page of the Tai Cang Book, which were the key factors.

The two were inseparable.

To verify his guess, Li Xuan continued walking down the mountain, silently observing the fluctuations of the laws of heaven and earth.

The subtle changes were manifested in his spiritual awareness.

"A few more steps, and I'll reach the teleportation point!"

Li Xuan's eyes lit up, and he stepped forward into the area he had observed.

In an instant, as if it was the most natural thing, he found himself outside the mountain peak.

"If I can draw the terrain below the mountain peak and the subtle changes into a formation diagram, can I comprehend the teleportation formation?"

Li Xuan was greatly excited.

Thinking and acting immediately, he returned to the mountain peak and continued walking down, his spiritual awareness covering the area and memorizing the subtle changes.

However, these subtle changes involving the powers of the laws of heaven and earth were extremely difficult to record.

Fortunately, Li Xuan had already memorized the pattern from the first page of the Tai Cang Book, so recording these subtle changes was just a matter of spending more time.

Li Xuan was in no hurry to return to Canglan Island, and he kept going back and forth on the mountain, recording the subtle changes and continuously drawing them into patterns.

"I must draw out this formation diagram. The Mysterious Gate Martial Dao is almost complete. I just need to find a suitable disciple to comprehend it."

Li Xuan was inwardly excited.

While drawing the formation diagram was difficult, it was not the most challenging part. The most difficult thing was finding a suitable disciple.

Geniuses were rare.

And the Mysterious Gate Martial Dao, whether it was formations, strategies, or weapon forging, all required exceptional talent.

People's talents had their own specialties, like Meng Chong's talent being more inclined towards physical martial arts, and Su Ling Xiu's talent being more focused on medicine...

"If I can't find one in the Inner Realm, I'll go to the Spiritual Realm. I'll definitely be able to find a suitable disciple."

Li Xuan thought to himself.

Once again, he returned to the mountain peak.

The formation diagram had already been drawn to 70% completion. With just a few more back-and-forth trips, he would be able to finish it.

Suddenly, on his spiritual consciousness, the Golden Book of the Dao opened, and a golden light surged forth.

"Your disciple, Meng Chong, has comprehended the first layer of your Unbreakable Golden Body. You have broken through the Unbreakable Golden Body!"

Meng Chong had finally comprehended the first layer of the Unbreakable Golden Body.

Li Xuan was overjoyed, as his strength had increased further.

At this moment, with the breakthrough of the Unbreakable Golden Body, Li Xuan realized the true power of the Unbreakable Golden Body.

In an instant, the Unbreakable Divine Armor appeared, and he seemed like a divine being descended to the mortal realm.

"Even small Celestial Beings can't harm a single hair on me!"

Li Xuan looked at himself, with the divine armor covering his body, and his majestic aura, and he was greatly excited.

With a thought, his body suddenly expanded, transforming into a towering six-zhang giant.

The Unbreakable Divine Armor covered him, and the six-zhang Unbreakable Golden Body was truly like a divine being descended, invincible and unstoppable.

"This Cloud Cocoon Silkworm Silk robe is truly remarkable, as it can withstand my transformation into a six-zhang giant."

The robe Li Xuan was wearing was made of Cloud Cocoon Silkworm Silk.

As soon as he transformed into the six-zhang golden body, the silkworm silk robe did not tear or break, but remained perfectly fitted to his body.
