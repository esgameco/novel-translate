After hearing Meng Chong's intentions, Du Yuan became puzzled. Looking for martial artists from the Spirit Realm? The Great Zhou Kingdom of the Spirit Realm was a subordinate power of the Great Yan Dynasty, and would send spirit body prodigies to the Great Yan Dynasty at regular intervals.

Spirit body prodigies were also top-tier talents in the Divine Realm.

It was said that due to certain factors, although spiritual energy was scarce in the Spirit Realm and the spiritual mechanisms of heaven and earth were not very active, with a low upper limit for martial arts, spirit body prodigies would still appear there.

Rumor had it that this was the origin of the name "Spirit Realm".

Du Yuan did not know the secrets behind this, but prodigies from the Spirit Realm had also broken through to become Immortal Celestial Lords.

The strongest among them, and the greatest backer of martial artists from the Spirit Realm, was the Marquis of Dongyang, whose strength was extremely powerful and who was a top existence even among Immortal Celestial Lords.

It was said that the Marquis of Dongyang, Xiang Zhen, had a thunder spirit body, and thus possessed extremely powerful offensive abilities that were tyrannical beyond compare.

Meng Chong was actually looking for prodigies from the Spirit Realm - could it be that he had a grudge against the Marquis of Dongyang? Thinking of this, Du Yuan's heart skipped a beat.

He cautiously asked, "Brother Meng, if I may ask, do you have a grudge against the Marquis of Dongyang?"

"Who is the Marquis of Dongyang?" Meng Chong raised an eyebrow.

"The Marquis of Dongyang, Xiang Zhen," Du Yuan added, fearing Meng Chong might not know the Marquis of Dongyang's name.

"Don't know, don't recognize him!" Meng Chong shook his head.

Hearing this, Du Yuan felt slightly relieved, and asked again, "Then may I ask, what business do you have with martial artists from the Spirit Realm, Brother Meng?"

"You're asking too much. You just need to tell me where they are," Meng Chong said, displeased.

After a pause, he added, "Don't worry, I'm not going to kill anyone." He was afraid Du Yuan might misunderstand and cause unnecessary conflict.

Hearing this, Du Yuan immediately felt relieved. As long as he wasn't going to kill anyone, things would be much easier to handle.

"Martial artists from the Spirit Realm are mostly gathered in Dongyang Prefecture. The Dongyang Martial Academy is responsible for recruiting prodigies from the Spirit Realm. Brother Meng can go to Dongyang Prefecture."

"Thank you very much!" Having obtained the information he wanted, Meng Chong didn't linger. He cupped his hands in farewell.

Watching Meng Chong's figure disappear, Du Yuan pondered for a moment, feeling somewhat uneasy.

"If Meng Chong is going to cause trouble for the Spirit Realm martial artists, and I provided the information, how could the Marquis of Dongyang let it go if he found out? I would surely be in trouble."

Du Yuan decided to pass the information to the Marquis of Dongyang, Xiang Zhen.

Moreover, he should also report Meng Chong's matter to the Great Yan Emperor. After all, such a dangerous person entering Great Yan territory could easily cause big trouble if not handled carefully.

Besides, the Great Yan royal family was also one of the behind-the-scenes forces of the Myriad Treasures Alliance. He must report to the Great Yan Emperor that Meng Chong alone had killed sixteen Immortal Celestial Lords of the Myriad Treasures Alliance. After all, the current losses of the Myriad Treasures Alliance had not implicated the Great Yan Dynasty.

Provoking Meng Chong without reason would be unwise.

Dongyang Prefecture was far from Biannan Prefecture, and Meng Chong was not hurrying to Dongyang Prefecture. Instead, he was traveling leisurely, enjoying the scenery of the Great Yan Dynasty.

Meng Chong even went to a Myriad Treasures Alliance branch in a certain prefecture city of the Great Yan Dynasty to buy some treasures as gifts for Zi Yun.

He specifically chose good-looking items.

As for the spirit crystals and such needed for the purchases, they were all looted from the Myriad Treasures Alliance, with the marks still not erased.

Too arrogant, too reckless, this was simply not taking the Myriad Treasures Alliance seriously at all. This was directly coming to their door to slap their face!

This was going too far!

"Young Master Meng, how about I give you a 40% discount?" The shopkeeper of the Myriad Treasures Alliance was screaming furiously in his heart, but his face wore a smile reserved for honored guests.

This person was dangerous. If not treated well, he might directly loot the branch. It was something he was capable of doing.

"Our Myriad Treasures Alliance branch belongs to the Great Yan royal family and is somewhat different from the Myriad Treasures Alliance branches in other realms," the shopkeeper hurriedly added.

He was afraid Meng Chong might not know the difference and loot this Myriad Treasures Alliance branch.

"Oh, so this is Great Yan's Myriad Treasures Alliance. Well then, that's fine, that's fine!" Meng Chong's face showed a look of disappointment.

The renowned Marquis of Dongyang, Xiang Zhen, had quietly returned to Dongyang Prefecture on this day.

"Why has the Marquis summoned us?" the Principal of Dongyang Martial Academy asked curiously.

Sitting at the head, a middle-aged man with a burly build and an imposing presence even without anger, now spoke with a serious expression, "You all know about this Divine God Meng Chong, right?"

The Prefect of Dongyang and other powerful figures were stunned, not understanding why Xiang Zhen suddenly mentioned Meng Chong.

Then, the Principal of Dongyang Martial Academy said with a shocked expression, "Marquis, could it be that His Majesty wants to participate in the Myriad Treasures Alliance's action?"

The Great Yan royal family was one of the major behind-the-scenes forces of the Myriad Treasures Alliance. Could it be that this time, the Great Yan royal family also wanted to join in surrounding and killing Meng Chong?

"No!" Xiang Zhen shook his head.

With an extremely solemn expression, he said, "You probably don't know, but sixteen Immortal Celestial Lords of the Myriad Treasures Alliance tried to ambush and kill Meng Chong, but they were all slain by him. Meng Chong's strength is terrifyingly powerful."

"His Majesty and the royal family have no intention of provoking Meng Chong, and the Myriad Treasures Alliance has not yet decided whether to continue seeking revenge."

Xiang Zhen paused, took a deep breath, and said, "I came here because Meng Chong has been inquiring about prodigies from the Spirit Realm. Although he has no grudges with us from the Spirit Realm, we must be cautious."

Upon hearing this, all the powerful figures present were shocked.

Meng Chong alone had killed sixteen Immortal Celestial Lords from the Myriad Treasures Alliance?

The Principal of Dongyang Martial Academy's face changed drastically, and he exclaimed in shock, "Marquis, do you mean that someone from our Spirit Realm might have offended Meng Chong, and he's come to seek revenge?"

If this were true, it would be a disaster for those of them from the Spirit Realm.

Given Meng Chong's overwhelming strength, even if all their Immortal Celestial Lords joined forces, they might not be able to defeat him.

"It's not necessarily so. Perhaps his enemy is not from among us who came from Great Zhou, but from other Spirit Realms?" Xiang Zhen shook his head.

As the strongest among the martial artists from the Spirit Realm, and the greatest backer of the Spirit Realm faction, Xiang Zhen had to make decisive decisions at crucial moments.

"If it truly is someone among us who has a grudge with Meng Chong, I only hope that this matter will not escalate and implicate the innocent."

The others nodded with solemn expressions.

This was unavoidable. After all, even if they joined forces, they couldn't resist such a powerful enemy, so naturally, there was no reason to stand up for any individual.

Otherwise, if they were implicated, it would be a disaster for all the Spirit Realm martial artists in the Great Yan realm.

They might even be completely suppressed because of this.

After all, they, who came from the Spirit Realm, were not without rivals in the Great Yan realm.

Their ability to establish themselves was related to their long-standing connection to the Great Yan royal family. They were like swords in the hands of the Great Yan royal family. As long as they didn't commit any major mistakes and get abandoned by the royal family, they could maintain their stable position.

"Don't spread this matter. Notify me immediately when Meng Chong arrives in Dongyang Prefecture," Xiang Zhen said in a deep voice.

"Yes, Marquis!" Everyone nodded.
