A group of people gathered together, enthusiastically discussing the affairs of the Wanbao Alliance - how many immortal lords had pursued someone but failed to catch them, and not only that, but one of their branch locations had even been looted opportunistically, among other things.

Zi Yun was unaware of all this, as she had been in secluded cultivation and generally didn't pay much attention to matters in the divine realm.

She listened for a while and roughly understood what had happened.

Essentially, the person being pursued had initially been framed for killing the disciple of the Changxun Realm Alliance leader, which then triggered a series of conflicts. The Wanbao Alliance had lost face and naturally refused to let the matter rest. Moreover, the pursued person's faction had never come to negotiate with the Wanbao Alliance to resolve the misunderstanding.

This led the Wanbao Alliance to conclude that the other party was likely an unaffiliated cultivator who had coincidentally obtained a powerful legacy, giving them such strength.

Zi Yun found it boring and was about to leave when she heard someone exclaim admiringly: "Meng Chong the Divine God is truly a prodigy - the first person to remain unscathed under pursuit by the Wanbao Alliance!"

Meng Chong?! Zi Yun was suddenly overjoyed. The person pursued by the Wanbao Alliance was Meng Chong?

"Senior brother, what does this Meng Chong you mentioned look like? Why is he so formidable?" Zi Yun suppressed her excitement and asked eagerly.

Although the person pursued by the Wanbao Alliance was undoubtedly the Meng Chong she knew - after all, there couldn't be a second person as incredibly talented named Meng Chong who was completely unafraid of the Wanbao Alliance - Zi Yun still couldn't help wanting to confirm again.

"It's said that this Meng Chong is bald with a muscular build. He's cultivated some unknown but extremely powerful technique that allows him to become like a giant, appearing divine, hence his title..."

The martial artist from Dongyang Martial Academy began describing enthusiastically, his eyes filled with admiration.

What martial artist doesn't aspire to become powerful?

"It really is Meng Chong!" Zi Yun was elated.

"My Meng Chong has only been in the divine realm for how long? Not even 30 years, and he's already famous throughout the realm!"

Zi Yun felt a surge of pride.

Her judgment had indeed been exceptional - the man she was in love with was incredibly powerful.

The group continued discussing Meng Chong enthusiastically. After a while, another female martial artist spoke up with an adoring expression: "Meng Chong may be strong, but he's not unrivaled. There's another who is handsome and elegant, with swordsmanship that reigns supreme in the world..."

Zi Yun knew immediately who this senior sister was referring to.

Senior Brother Xu Yan! It could be no one else but Senior Brother Xu Yan.

Sure enough, the group began discussing Xu Yan - the one who had slain the immortal lord Tianshadiying, who had single-handedly cut through a celestial rift with just his sword, killing all the experts comparable to immortal lords within, including one who was on par with a top-tier immortal lord.

Zi Yun was no longer bored and joined in the discussion, mostly inquiring about information on Meng Chong. However, towards the end, she became puzzled.

What about Su Lingxiu? Why hadn't her fame spread? On second thought, Su Lingxiu preferred peace and quiet, wasn't showy, and always stayed by her senior's side. Given how vast the divine realm was, it wasn't surprising that her name hadn't spread.

But what about Fang Hao? Why was there no mention of his fame either?

"Could it be that Fang Hao also dislikes showing off?"

Though puzzled, she didn't dwell on it much. Zi Yun's thoughts were entirely focused on Meng Chong.

After the group dispersed, Zi Yun returned to her residence. She was itching to go find Meng Chong but realized her current strength was still too weak.

"Meng Chong will come find me, right? It's been so many years."

Zi Yun sat by the window, resting her chin on her hand and staring blankly at the distant horizon, no longer in the mood to cultivate.

Meng Chong sighed as he entered the Great Yan Realm.

"I wonder where Zi Yun is."

Meng Chong scratched his head. The Great Yan Realm was so vast, finding one person would likely not be easy.

"It should be relatively easy to inquire about where the prodigies from the Spirit Realm have been settled. I'll ask the local governor of the Great Yan Dynasty."

Now with great strength and confidence, Meng Chong decided to directly seek out the local governor to inquire.

Although the Great Yan Dynasty was very powerful, Meng Chong didn't take it too seriously. If it really came to blows, while he might have difficulty cutting through the entire Great Yan Dynasty alone, he would have little trouble slaying several dozen immortal lords.

The head of a prefecture in the Great Yan Dynasty would certainly be an immortal lord, and one ranked among the strong within that realm.

Biannanjun was a border prefecture of the Great Yan Dynasty, bordering the wilderness. Governor Du Yuan had presided over Biannanjun for over a thousand years. His strength was at the late stage of the immortal realm, not far from perfection.

As the head of a prefecture, besides having to prevent the celestial rifts in Biannan from going out of control and needing to suppress them, he also had to prevent any unrest from occurring in Biannan.

Especially now, with the situation in the divine realm in turmoil and Tianshadiying stirring up trouble everywhere, even the Great Yan Dynasty had seen the emergence of some rebels, with Tianshadiying's shadow behind them.

However, the Great Yan Dynasty was at its peak, with strong power and numerous experts. Therefore, there was only some minor chaos, which could not shake the Great Yan Dynasty in the slightest.

Bordering the wilderness, Biannan naturally had to be vigilant and guard against powerful figures crossing over from the wilderness.

However, the wilderness was desolate. Even with people assigned to watch, it was impossible to have 100% control over those crossing the wilderness.

If a True King Celestial Lord entered, it wouldn't cause much impact. But if an immortal lord crossed the wilderness, unless they remained hidden and never took action, even the slightest movement would expose their traces.

Once exposed, if they hadn't entered the Great Yan Realm through the proper gates, they would have to explain their purpose for coming to the Great Yan Realm.

They would either be expelled or - die!

With the current turbulent situation, the Great Yan Dynasty showed no mercy in this regard, and they had the strength to back it up.

At this moment, in the Biannan governor's mansion, Du Yuan was looking at a new piece of intelligence.

This was information that had just been transmitted and had not yet spread widely.

"Sixteen immortal lords from the Wanbao Alliance pursued Meng Chong and have since vanished without a trace?"

Du Yuan was shocked upon seeing this.

Sixteen immortal lords - even for the powerful Wanbao Alliance, this was a devastating loss.

Moreover, among these sixteen immortal lords was a powerful late-stage immortal realm expert leading them.

"Were they killed by Meng Chong?"

Du Yuan found it hard to believe.

The Wanbao Alliance's pursuit of Meng Chong had been causing an uproar throughout the divine realm for several years now.

Meng Chong had been constantly evading, and his methods of evasion were quite miraculous. He could conceal and suppress his own aura, making it difficult even for immortal lords to track him.

"Could it be that a powerful figure behind Meng Chong took action?"

Du Yuan pondered, unwilling to believe this was Meng Chong's doing alone.

Even if Meng Chong had broken through to the immortal realm during the pursuit, how could he single-handedly slay sixteen immortal lords?

One of them was even at the late stage of the immortal realm.

Moreover, the experts of the Wanbao Alliance's treasure protection team were all skilled in joint attack techniques. When they joined forces, even the most top-tier immortal lords could only flee.

Each prefectural governor's seal was one of the cores of the Great Yan Dynasty's operation, and also one of the cores of the Great Yan Dynasty's control over the Great Yan realm.

Divine Meng Chong!
