"Before I die, I had wanted to see Waner one last time from afar. Since I can't see her, I no longer have any wishes left."

Mu Qiuliu's expression became calm.

"Please never tell Lingshou that I ever existed. It's as if I never existed at all!"

Mu Qiuliu said, then turned to leave.

Meng Shushu's expression was complex, and he sighed inwardly before speaking up, "Wait."

Mu Qiuliu turned back with a puzzled look.

"Although I don't know why you've become this way, since we've met, here are some pills for you. Take them and recover as much as you can."

Meng Shushu handed several pill bottles to Mu Qiuliu.

"They may not be able to restore you to your peak, but they can definitely help you recover to about 70-80% of your former self."

Mu Qiuliu shook his head and said, "It's useless, I know my own situation."

"Heh, these pills were refined by Lingshou. Even if your foundation is damaged or your spiritual body is collapsing, you can still recover with them. Your injuries are more severe, but you can still recover."

Meng Shushu sneered and continued, "If Lingshou personally treats you, your injuries would be nothing, and you'd be able to fully recover in just a few days."

Mu Qiuliu looked incredulous. "Really?"

"Of course!" Meng Shushu sneered. "Do you want to go find Lingshou for treatment? She hasn't seen you before, so she won't know you're her father."

"No need!" Mu Qiuliu shook his head and took the pills, saying, "If I see her, I'll only end up harming her. Let's part ways here, and may we never meet again!"

Meng Shushu fell silent. Mu Qiuliu's response had caught him off guard. He had expected Mu Qiuliu to express a desire to reveal his identity and ask Lingshou for treatment after learning he could recover to his peak.

Mu Qiuliu left, disappearing into the crowd, never to return.

Meng Shushu didn't know what to make of it. He admitted that he had softened at the last moment, which was why he gave Mu Qiuliu the pills.

Looking at the jade token in his hand, with its delicate patterns, Meng Shushu wondered what it was.

"This is what?" He shook his head, deciding to find an opportunity to give it to Lingshou.

Back at the Qingqing Pavilion, Lingshou was still catching up with Princess Xingwei. The trip to the Great Zhou capital had come to an end.

"We can't go to Bingzhou," Meng Shushu remembered Mu Qiuliu's warning, puzzled about what had happened in Bingzhou.

"Then we won't go," Lingshou said, and Meng Shushu was relieved, as Bingzhou was sparsely populated, and there was no need to go there. If Lingshou wanted to go, he would find an excuse to dissuade her, not because he feared danger in Bingzhou, but because he was worried that going there might uncover something about Mu Qiuliu, thus exposing Lingshou's and his own identities.

"Am I doing the right thing?" Meng Shushu was conflicted.

After all, Mu Qiuliu was Lingshou's biological father, but Meng Shushu didn't want Lingshou to bear any sadness or anger because of her father's existence, especially if Mu Qiuliu was not a womanizer.

If that were the case, Meng Shushu would have told Lingshou the truth.

"So be it," Meng Shushu sighed, holding the jade token as he went to the pavilion on the top of the Qingqing Pavilion.

"Senior!" Meng Shushu respectfully greeted Li Xuan.

He knew that Li Xuan had seen and heard everything about him and Mu Qiuliu.

"Mm," Li Xuan nodded.

"Senior, am I doing the right thing?" Meng Shushu couldn't help but ask.

"Right or wrong, it's in your heart. Let things take their course," Li Xuan said, shaking his head.

If Lingshou learned the truth about her father, she might be troubled and her carefree, happy life of cultivating alchemy and martial arts could be disrupted.

Since Mu Qiuliu had chosen to withdraw, he obviously didn't want to disrupt Lingshou's peaceful life with his presence.

"Senior, what is this?" Meng Shushu asked, placing the jade token on the table.

"I will give it to Lingshou," Li Xuan said simply, looking at the token.

As for what it was, he had no idea. He just avoided answering, as if telling Meng Shushu that his cultivation was too low to know such things.

"Thank you, Senior!" Meng Shushu didn't ask further.

If the token was given to Lingshou by the Senior, it would be taken more seriously than if he gave it to her himself. Otherwise, it might just be forgotten by Lingshou one day.

After Meng Shushu left, Li Xuan picked up the jade token and examined it closely. He was puzzled, wondering what this item was. The material of the token was not ordinary.

In terms of material alone, it was not inferior to the divine artifacts of the Spiritual Realm. But it was not a divine artifact.

The patterns on the token seemed to have no special meaning, just decorative.

Li Xuan pondered for a moment, then infused a wisp of spiritual energy into the token.

The jade token emitted a faint blue glow, and three words appeared on it: "Heavenly Vault Token!"

Along with these words, Li Xuan also saw a few lines of small text inside the token:

"When the token is activated, the Heavenly Vault will appear; the holder of the Heavenly Vault Token can enter the Heavenly Vault, and can also command the Heavenly Vault!"

Li Xuan withdrew his spiritual energy, falling into deep thought.

"The Heavenly Vault? Some kind of secret realm? This is the key to enter the Heavenly Vault, or the token to command it?"

He wondered.

What was the Heavenly Vault? As far as he knew, there was no such place in the Spiritual Realm, and he had never seen any records of it in the Great Zhou's archives.

"It's probably some kind of secret realm. When the Heavenly Vault appears, the token will activate and allow entry?" Li Xuan muttered to himself.

"I'll just give it to Lingshou. Whenever the Heavenly Vault appears, we'll find out then."

He shook his head, not dwelling on the Heavenly Vault token any further.

Even if it was a secret realm, it didn't hold much appeal to him, as he cultivated his own unique martial dao, not the martial arts of this world.

"I'll continue my grand martial dao endeavor."

Li Xuan shook his head and immersed himself in editing his martial arts scriptures and contemplating the Dao principles of the heavens and earth.

After seeing off Princess Xingwei, the Qingqing Pavilion also prepared to depart.

Zihui was reluctant to leave and returned to the Qing Wang Mansion.

"Where's our next destination?"

"Let's go to Tianling State and find the Red Cat. I've missed that chubby cat after all this time!"

Lingshou thought for a moment and said.

"Okay, then let's go to Tianling State!"

The Qingqing Pavilion's manor contracted, transforming into a flying ship that soared into the sky and vanished in the distance.

The onlooking powerful cultivators of the Great Zhou couldn't help but marvel at the Qingqing Pavilion's divine artifact, an unprecedented sight.

On the walls of the Great Zhou capital, Mu Qiuliu watched the flying ship depart, catching a glimpse of the young girl's figure in the distance. He was content.

Then his expression turned to anger.
