Although he wasn't afraid, as he had his master's divine talisman, he couldn't always rely on his master's divine powers to roam the cultivation world. In the end, he still had to rely on his own strength.

It should be time to comprehend it.

"But Fourth Senior Brother's reputation is a bit lacking in comparison."

However, the Jade State was ultimately too weak.

So naturally, it didn't attract as much attention.

The old man's aura was quite peculiar, and it gave Xu Yan a vague sense of danger.

The old man's hoarse and gloomy voice rang out.

"Someone from the Thousand Martial Pavilion?"

Xu Yan frowned, there was something off about this old man.

This time, by slightly revealing his traces, he had actually attracted such an old fellow.

"Where is the Cold Absolute Blade?"

The old man did not answer, but instead knitted his brows.

The Cold Absolute Blade was not on Xu Yan, could it be that he had discarded it?

But how was that possible, it was a Divine Artifact! Was it hidden somewhere?

"Tell me the whereabouts of the Cold Absolute Blade, and I'll grant you a quick death!"

"If you have the ability, then come and take this broken blade back."

The old man was greatly shocked.

That was not a storage bag, but something that could contain a Divine Artifact?

"Die!"

The old man let out a low roar, his white hair flying, and an unhealthy reddish glow appeared on his aged face.

A chilling aura mixed with a deathly presence surged forth, and a powerful punch was thrust towards Xu Yan.

Xu Yan unleashed a sword strike, enveloping the mountains and rivers.

Boom!

The mountains and rivers collapsed, and the old man closed in, unleashing another punch.

The killing intent was biting, and the aura of death permeated the area.

"Trifling tricks!"

Xu Yan sneered.

He didn't dare to fight for too long, or else the Thousand Martial Pavilion's Patriarch would catch up, and it would take a lot of time to shake him off.

Hum! In that instant, Xu Yan struck with his sword, combining the Gentle Wind Sword Intent with the Mountain and River Sword Intent. It appeared to be a single sword strike, but it was as if the mountains and rivers were being cleaved by a celestial being wielding a sword.

The old man's expression darkened slightly, and a gray light surged from his body, with the aura of death enveloping him.

At this moment, the temperature around them seemed to drop, and a bone-chilling aura of death permeated the area.

Whoosh! He thrust out a punch, and his aged body shriveled, as if his very soul was being consumed.

The old man's face did not show any pain, but rather a faint smile, with a sense of cold detachment.

Xu Yan's heart skipped a beat, this attack was no ordinary one, it was imbued with the aura of death. It seemed that the old man had accumulated a lifetime's worth of martial dao, all converging in this moment.
