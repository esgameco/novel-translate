Chapter 112 The Second Sword Intent

Xu Yan watched as Physician Pan broke through the Blood Qi Realm and became a martial artist. Although his foundation was only of Copper Bone, his strength seemed weak to Xu Yan, but in the Borderland, he was incredibly powerful.

The pinnacle masters of the jianghu could extinguish one with a snap of their fingers.

Xu Jun was also in the midst of a breakthrough. With the aid of medicinal pills, he had already completed the refinement of his viscera and was about to enter the door of martial dao in just a day or two.

"This Blood Qi Pill, just an ordinary medicinal pill, has such a miraculous effect. If it were a spiritual pill, how powerful would the effect be?"

"My grandfather is getting old, and his cultivation of the martial dao is slow. Moreover, my uncle and the others do not have good natural talents."

"As for my mother, even with the aid of medicinal pills, it will still take some time for her to enter the door. And the pills have already been consumed."

Xu Yan marveled at the effects of the medicinal pills and decided to take all the precious medicines at home to have his shijie refine them. He knew that refining pills consumed a lot of medicinal materials.

Especially for new pills, they could not be successfully refined in a single attempt.

Failing once would mean wasting an entire furnace of materials.

"Spiritual medicines, I lack spiritual medicines. When I go to the Inner Realm, the first goal will be to buy spiritual medicines. Only spiritual medicines are the true materials for refining pills."

Xu Yan thought to himself, his determination to go to the Inner Realm growing stronger once again.

Now, Physician Pan had already entered the martial dao, and his father was about to break through as well.

The other elite guards at home also had decent talents and were about to enter the door as well. After returning, he would have his shijie refine a batch of pills to quickly assist their breakthroughs.

The safety of the family had already been secured.

"When father breaks through, I'll go back and ask my shijie if she has any anti-aging pills or pills that can more gently and easily help mother break through the realm..."

Xu Yan thought of his mother.

He did not expect his mother to fight and brawl, as long as she had a certain level of strength. The most important thing was that cultivation could prolong her lifespan.

Li Xuan had surveyed the Endless Mountains and saw many groups of people entering the mountains to seek out immortals, but he did not discover any martial artists.

However, as he delved deeper into the Endless Mountains, closer to the Inner Realm area, he did discover fierce beasts like the Crimson-Eyed Tiger and the Fire-Maned Wolf.

"Were the Crimson-Eyed Tiger and Fire-Maned Wolf in the Evil Massacre Forest originally from the Endless Mountains? That Crimson-Eyed Tiger was a bit weak, not a pure breed?"

Li Xuan pondered.

"Since I've come this far, I can't return empty-handed."

With this thought, Li Xuan descended.

By the stream, a spotted giant tiger was crouching, its massive body not much smaller than an ox.

"Could this be the Crimson-Eyed Tiger King from the vicinity?"

Li Xuan muttered to himself, this tiger was truly gigantic.

The Endless Mountains near the Inner Realm had a faint presence of heavenly and earthly spiritual energy, which allowed fierce beasts to exist there, and their strengths were not weak.

"Roar!"

The Crimson-Eyed Tiger felt a weight on its back and realized someone had sat on it!

It roared in great anger and was about to flip over, but its neck was suddenly grasped, and the entire tiger was lifted into the air, as if a large cat had been picked up.

The Crimson-Eyed Tiger was terrified, curling up its tail and hind legs, letting out low growls, not daring to struggle or move, afraid of falling.

"Heh, this big cat is quite intelligent. Could it be that I randomly picked one of the smarter ones?"

Li Xuan chuckled in surprise.

He carried the Crimson-Eyed Tiger back to Cloud Mountain County.

Keeping a big cat as a pet wouldn't be bad. He didn't know if he could tame it, but if he could, he would keep it as a pet. If not, he could just cook and eat it.

The blood, flesh, bones, and even the tiger's penis could all be used for refining pills.

Back at the manor, Li Xuan put down the Crimson-Eyed Tiger, expecting it to roar in anger, but it simply rolled over on the ground, exposing its belly in surrender.

"Heh, this big cat is quite sensible."

Li Xuan was amused.

"Master, are you going to take its blood, flesh, and bones to refine pills?"

Su Ling Xiu came running over, excited.

"Let's keep it for now. If I can't tame it, then I'll slaughter it for refining."

Li Xuan patted the tiger's head.

"Okay."

Su Ling Xiu looked a bit disappointed.

"Stone Two, you're in charge of feeding it. If it doesn't obey, then slaughter it."

Li Xuan instructed.

"Yes, my lord!"

Stone Two looked excited and said, "My lord, I've learned beast taming before. I'll try to tame it."

"Go ahead and try."

Li Xuan was a bit surprised. Stone Two knew beast taming?

But he also knew that Stone Two's beast taming skills were just some common martial arts techniques, nothing extraordinary.

"In the future, if any failed pills are produced during pill refining, you can feed them to it, so as not to waste them. Whether it will be beneficial is up to the tiger itself."

Li Xuan thought for a moment and said.

"Understood, master!"

Su Ling Xiu nodded.

Stone Two heard this and hurriedly said, "My lord, I can eat the failed pills too."

Li Xuan said impatiently, "Not all failed pills are harmless to humans. If you eat them and end up with problems, don't come complaining to me."

Hearing this, Stone Two immediately shrank his neck, sheepishly saying, "Then I'll just let the tiger eat them."

Li Xuan sat down in the chair, looking at the Crimson-Eyed Tiger rolling around and acting cute, and after a moment of contemplation, he said, "From now on, I'll call you Red Cat."

"Roar!"

The Red Cat let out a low growl.

Li Xuan was amused, this old tiger seemed to have quite good intelligence.

Stone Two found a thick chain and put it around the Red Cat's neck, also bringing a whip for taming.

Su Ling Xiu used the Water Spirit Grass to refine a batch of pills, but two of them were failed pills, which she tossed to the Red Cat.

After eating the failed pills, the Red Cat's eyes became bright and shiny, constantly rolling around and exposing its belly to Su Ling Xiu, clearly showing that the pills had a good effect on it.

After refining the pills, Su Ling Xiu began to help Zhou Ying with her martial dao cultivation.

Zhou Ying's martial dao foundation was almost fixed, and the transition would take time. Without the aid of medicinal pills, the transition would be even more difficult.

Fortunately, her realm was not too high. After consuming the pills and accumulating Qi and Blood, she finally managed to sense the Qi and Blood, entering the Refining Skin stage.

With her original martial dao strength and the aid of the pills, the Refining Skin process was very fast, and she successfully completed the transition.

Su Ling Xiu let out a sigh of relief and began to focus on her own cultivation and research into the Dao of Alchemy.

That day, Xu Yan returned.

Li Xuan learned that Xu Yan's father had already broken through the Blood Qi Realm and entered the martial dao.

"Is it because there are too few cultivators, and their realms are too low, while my realm is too high, so I'm not getting any feedback?"

Li Xuan thought to himself.

After paying his respects to the master, Xu Yan handed over the medicinal materials he had brought back to Su Ling Xiu, as well as matters related to the pills.

"Shijie, do you have any anti-aging pills? I want to give them to my mother."

Xu Yan asked.

"Anti-aging pills?"

Su Ling Xiu pondered, various spiritual herbs appearing in her mind, and she gradually assembled an immature pill formula.

"There should be some, but anti-aging pills require spiritual herbs as ingredients, and I only have Water Spirit Grass on hand, which is not suitable for refining anti-aging pills."

Su Ling Xiu shook her head.
