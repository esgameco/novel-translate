As soon as the Jade Spirit Sect Sect Master entered the hall, the formation was activated, trapping the Sect Master inside.

Xu Yan struck out with a palm, and the golden dragon intimidated the Jade Spirit Sect executives and disciples, their souls suppressed by the majestic dragon aura, and they no longer had the ability to resist.

Facing a Refined Spirit Celestial, how could they resist? It would be unwise to fight against such a powerful expert, they could only leave it to the Sect Master or the Elders to deal with.

Time was pressing, Fang Hao's formation might not be able to trap the Jade Spirit Sect Sect Master for too long, and the other party, suddenly encountering the formation, would be cautious and focus on self-preservation rather than recklessly attacking.

If the time was too long, the other party would discover that the purpose was just to trap him, and he would surely break out of the formation with force.

After all, the Jade Spirit Sect Sect Master was a mid-stage Refined Spirit expert, only one step away from the late-stage.

Moreover, the Jade Spirit Sect Sect Master must have already crushed the mother-and-child pearl to call for help.

This visit to the Jade Spirit Sect was not for the purpose of killing.

It was for the Jade Spirit Sect's treasury! Where the treasury was located must be an important place, and Xu Yan and Meng Chong soon discovered a treasury of the Jade Spirit Sect.

Boom!

Facing such a powerful enemy, the treasury was still guarded by a Refined Spirit initial realm cultivator, the Jade Spirit Sect truly deserved its reputation as the number one sect in Yuzhou, with an unexpected number of Refined Spirit cultivators.

"Slice!"

Meng Chong instantly transformed into a sixty-foot giant, unleashing the Absolute Deity Slash.

And Xu Yan, with a sword strike, unleashed the Xun Mie Sword! Under the powerful martial arts that attacked the soul, the Refined Spirit cultivator let out a miserable scream, his soul torn apart and killed on the spot.

Boom!

The treasury was blasted open.

Xu Yan sighed in admiration.

Even if it was not the core treasury, the treasures inside were far more than the Dai family's, truly deserving the reputation of the number one sect in Yuzhou! A jar floated up, and with a sweep of Xu Yan's spiritual sense, the treasures in the treasury were instantly sucked into the jar.

This was a jar specially refined to plunder the Jade Spirit Sect's treasury, with a huge spatial capacity and an absorbing force. With a sweep of the spiritual sense, it could suck the treasures dry in a very short time.

"Hmm, there seems to be a hidden treasury here!"

Meng Chong suddenly discovered that there was something unusual in the core area of the Jade Spirit Sect's back mountain.

He punched out.

This punch, with his control of the force, instantly broke through the ground, revealing a treasure artifact door.

Xu Yan had rushed over, and with a sword strike, the treasure artifact door shattered.

"Is this one of the core treasuries?"

Xu Yan and Meng Chong were greatly excited, as the treasures hidden in this secret treasury were all extremely precious medicinal herbs and the like.

The storage bag was not as good as the storage ring, as it was mainly made of the Swallowing Toad as the material, and the medicinal herbs stored inside had a certain chance of losing their potency and being absorbed by the Swallowing Toad in the storage bag.

Therefore, the Spiritual Sects and aristocratic families would establish treasuries to store medicinal herbs and other treasures, rather than storing them in storage bags for a long time.

The jar flew out, and with a sweep of the spiritual sense, everything was sucked into the jar.

Boom, boom, boom! Xu Yan and Meng Chong began to plunder the Jade Spirit Sect extensively, clearing out all the spiritual herbs in the spiritual fields.

Whenever they encountered what seemed to be a core treasure area, they would simply punch it open, and in just a moment, the Jade Spirit Sect was in ruins, like it had been ravaged by bandits.

The Jade Spirit Sect executives and disciples were all terrified, even cursing inwardly, wondering what was wrong with the Sect Master, was he useless? Even now he still hadn't taken action? The Grand Elders, were they all fools, being lured away and not knowing the crisis of the sect?

"Hand over all the storage bags, or be killed without mercy!"

Xu Yan waved his hand, and eighteen golden dragons circled above the executives and disciples, their majestic dragon aura looking down on the people below.

"This is the Jade Spirit Sect, how dare you..."

An executive regained his spirit and spoke up angrily, trying to threaten them with the sect's power.

Poof! The dragon's tail swept across, and the person instantly turned to ash.

The sect was being attacked, and he still wanted to act tough and intimidate people by relying on the sect's power, such a fool didn't deserve to live.

"If you don't hand it over, die!"

The golden dragon descended from the sky, its eyes shining brightly, like a true dragon descending to the world!

"Hand over the storage bags, or die!"

Meng Chong's towering figure, clad in divine armor, was like a divine god in the sky, with a blade of light sweeping across the executives and disciples, ready to strike down at any moment.

"I'll hand it over, I'll hand it over, spare me!"

The Jade Spirit Sect executives and disciples, their faces deathly pale, hurriedly took out their storage bags and placed them in front of them.

Their lives were the most important! The golden dragon swept across, and all the storage bags were taken away.

Boom!

At this moment, the formation around the Jade Spirit Sect's main hall began to shake, the Jade Spirit Sect Sect Master was attacking the formation, trying to break out.

The transmission talisman also brought the news that Zhu Liang and the others were rushing back frantically.

"Shidi, let's go!"

Xu Yan and Meng Chong instantly appeared beside Fang Hao, who was now straining to control the formation, using the extraordinary Heavenly and Earthly Formation to bind the Jade Spirit Sect Sect Master inside.

"Okay!"

Fang Hao swept his spiritual sense, and the formation artifacts flew up and were stored in the weapon case.

Xu Yan and Meng Chong struck together, bombarding the Jade Spirit Sect Sect Master who had not yet fully revealed his form, and after the attack, the three of them disappeared into the sky in an instant.

In a moment, they had vanished into the distance.

The Jade Spirit Sect Sect Master let out an angry roar, fending off the attack, and as the mist dispersed, his spiritual power surged out, and he was suddenly stunned!

"Ah!"

An angry roar echoed through the Jade Spirit Sect! "Who, who was it!"

The Jade Spirit Sect Sect Master stood in the air, looking down at the Jade Spirit Sect, which was now in ruins, the treasury plundered, and even one of the core treasuries had been taken. The elder guarding the treasury had been killed.

The spiritual herbs in the fields had all been cleared out! At this moment, the Jade Spirit Sect was like it had been ravaged by thieves, the losses were devastating! "Sect Master, it was Xu Yan and Meng Chong!"

An executive wailed.

A lifetime of treasures, almost all of them in the storage bags, were gone! Damn it, they were even more ruthless than bandits!
