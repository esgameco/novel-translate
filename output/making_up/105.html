He even tried to interweave the true qi strands, yet keep them separate without merging.

"Once I comprehend the profound mysteries of the true qi, I will have grasped the cultivation method of the Profound Realm. When my Innate Realm is perfected, and I break through to the Profound Realm, it will be a natural progression!"

Xu Yan was greatly inspired, fully immersing himself in the task of dividing the true qi, seeking to uncover the profound secrets at its core.
