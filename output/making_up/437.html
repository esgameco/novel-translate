Meng Chong explained the effects and usage of each pill to Song You one by one.

Meng Chong had no use for these pills he was carrying at the moment. As for healing pills, he had more than enough. Besides, how could he easily get injured? With his powerful physical recovery ability, he didn't need pills for ordinary injuries. Even immortal heavenly venerable cultivators couldn't injure him severely enough to require healing pills.

These pills were just precautionary, but now they could be put to good use.

As for helping Song You, it was partly because his situation was similar to Meng Chong's own past experience. Secondly, the Song family was almost certainly a hidden force of the Tiansha Diying. Thirdly, they had a grudge against the Wanbao Alliance. Since there was an opportunity to retaliate against Tiansha Diying, why not take it?

Moreover, Meng Chong suspected that the Tiansha Diying force that framed him was very likely the Song family! The Song family was affiliated with the Wanbao Alliance and could better track the movements of Yan Shanhe and his disciples, making it easier to find an opportunity to strike.

Song You was already stunned. How could there be such miraculous pills in this world? He didn't doubt Meng Chong's words at all. At this moment, he was so excited that his eyes were filled with tears, his lips trembling, unable to express his gratitude.

Carefully putting away the pills, he finally said with tears of gratitude, "Brother Meng's great kindness, if Song You is lucky enough to survive, I will repay this great favor even if I have to be a beast of burden."

"You're too kind, it's just a small matter," Meng Chong said dismissively.

Then, he took out a formation disk.

"This is an Illusion Reversal Formation. Once activated, it can conceal your form and also confuse enemies, creating more opportunities for you. Of course, this formation disk is not high-level, it can only achieve the effect of surprise and can only confuse enemies once, so you must seize the opportunity."

Meng Chong took out another formation disk.

This formation disk was not high-level. If a true king heavenly venerable experienced it once, they would become alert, and the illusory power would have limited effect on true king heavenly venerables.

However, since the way of formations had never appeared in the Changyu Realm before, these true king heavenly venerables would certainly be confused when first encountering it, but there would only be one chance.

After being fooled once, they certainly wouldn't be fooled a second time.

But when facing unknown illusions, although they wouldn't be fooled, they would become cautious and not dare to act rashly. This was also an opportunity for Song You.

As for higher-level formation disks, Meng Chong naturally wouldn't give them to Song You to use. After all, they weren't that close. Giving Song You an outdated formation disk he couldn't use was already quite generous.

"Formation disk?" Song You was stunned again. What was this? He had never heard of it before.

He thought he was quite knowledgeable, coming from a big faction, but he had never heard of such miraculous pills, and now these formation disks and formations were also unheard of.

"The formation disk is very simple to use, you just need to do this..." Meng Chong explained how to use the formation disk.

"Do you understand?"

"I understand, thank you for your great kindness, Brother Meng!" Song You said excitedly.

"Take this mask."

Meng Chong handed a Diying mask to Song You, then instructed him on how to create a plan to deceive the Wanbao Alliance, making it seem like he was a secret agent of the Song family who had been plotting for years, looking for an opportunity to assassinate Yan Shanhe and his son without implicating the Song family.

As long as it succeeded, the Song family would definitely be implicated, and Yan Shanhe of the Wanbao Alliance would surely destroy the Song family in a fit of rage. This way, Song You's revenge would be achieved.

"Don't worry, Brother Meng, I know what to do. I will definitely succeed!" Song You said excitedly.

The dawn of revenge had appeared at this moment.

As long as he could get his revenge, even if he perished, it would be worth it! Song You began his action, first to confirm the whereabouts of Yan Shanhe and his only son Yan Long, and then to wait for a suitable opportunity.

Meng Chong also decided to stay in the Changyu Realm for now.

Yan Shanhe's only son Yan Long was the young master of the Wanbao Alliance in the Changyu Realm. With extraordinary talent, he naturally practiced the most top-tier cultivation methods and lacked neither cultivation resources nor guidance from immortal heavenly venerable cultivators.

Yan Long had an 80% chance of breaking through to the immortal realm, so Yan Shanhe doted on this only son excessively. He was absolutely Yan Shanhe's reverse scale.

Everyone in the major factions and among the powerful cultivators in the Changyu Realm knew that the person one must not offend in the Wanbao Alliance was Yan Long! For the safety of his only son, to ensure no accidents would occur, Yan Shanhe personally selected a rogue cultivator who was just a hair's breadth away from breaking through to the immortal heavenly venerable realm, bestowed upon him stronger cultivation methods and resources to help him break through to the immortal realm.

The price was to become Yan Long's guard.

Having an immortal heavenly venerable as a guard was a treatment that even the top geniuses of some major factions didn't enjoy.

This shows how much Yan Shanhe valued Yan Long, and also indirectly reflects the wealth and generosity of the Wanbao Alliance, being able to have an immortal heavenly venerable as an attendant.

Although the incident of Meng Chong robbing the Wanbao Alliance had occurred, few people were worried about Yan Long's safety, after all, Yan Long had an immortal heavenly venerable protecting him.

Yan Long was not in Baimu City, but traveling around.

On this day, just after leaving a city, a beautiful woman appeared by Yan Long's side, serving him. Several peak true king heavenly venerable guards were on alert, not allowing anyone to approach.

An immortal heavenly venerable was also protecting him in secret.

"I really envy Yan Long!"

"Worthy of being Changyu's number one young master, wherever he goes, there are genius beauties willing to serve him!"

"Extraordinary talent, noble birth, endless resources... I wish I had a good father!"

"Give it up, with your aptitude, if your father was Yan Shanhe, he probably would have sent you to some remote mountain long ago!"

The martial artists in the city looked at Yan Long's group leaving the city with envy and jealousy.

Suddenly!

"Meng Chong?!"

A low voice sounded.

"You protect the young master."

A figure burst forth with momentum, rushing towards a bald young man who appeared in mid-air.

"Hiss! Meng Chong actually wants to kill Yan Long? This is even more serious than robbing the Wanbao Alliance branch. Yan Shanhe might personally take action."

"Too arrogant, he's simply not putting Yan Shanhe in his eyes at all."
