The 726 Blood Disciple's form had already reached above the Abyss of the Azure Sea, just about to enter the heavenly rift, when suddenly the world darkened and space seemed to solidify.

The 726 Blood Disciple's form instantly froze, unable to move at all, as if he had been bound in place by space itself.

"What is this?"

A powerful expert! Could it be that experts from the Divine Realm had come to assist?

The divine bridge had already collapsed. Even if experts came to help, they would need to restore the divine bridge to set foot in the Spirit Realm in such a short time! However, the divine bridge had not been restored!

A giant hand reached down, as if scooping out a piece of the space where he was located.

To understand the existence of the heavenly rifts, the 726 Blood Disciple was undoubtedly a clue.

And what exactly was the Netherworld Prison? It was for these reasons that Li Xuan personally took action to capture the 726 Blood Disciple.

With his current strength, capturing the 726 Blood Disciple was merely a thought away, requiring no effort at all.

The 726 Blood Disciple was shocked to his core. In just an instant, he found himself appearing before a young man.

The man was sitting casually in a chair.

Beside him stood a stunningly beautiful woman, seemingly a beauty of the Sea Spirit race.

"You..."

The 726 Blood Disciple had a look of disbelief. How could such a powerful expert exist in the Spirit Realm? An Immortal Heavenly Venerable? The Blood Sons of the Netherworld Prison started at the level of Immortal Heavenly Venerable, and capturing him would also be effortless for them.

But the person before him seemed far beyond the level of an Immortal Heavenly Venerable.

"Netherworld Prison?"

Li Xuan's gaze looked down at the 726 Blood Disciple, his tone seemingly rather disdainful of the Netherworld Prison.

He raised a hand and directly pressed it on the 726 Blood Disciple's head.

No need for questioning, he would directly search the soul.

He had just obtained the soul-searching secret technique and hadn't used it yet. As for what damage it might cause to the 726 Blood Disciple, he naturally didn't care.

This was an invader, an enemy.

The 726 Blood Disciple felt his consciousness roaring, as if falling into some strange state. It seemed his memories and consciousness were being flipped through. He was terrified!

Realizing something was wrong, he roared inwardly, wanting to resist this terrifying power.

However, he couldn't muster even a trace of resistance.

Li Xuan's gaze was indifferent as the 726 Blood Disciple's memories gradually appeared in his soul search. But at a certain moment, a blood-colored light shone, as if some mysterious power had sealed off the secrets deep in the 726's consciousness.

Poof! In an instant, the 726 Blood Disciple turned into a blood mist and completely dissipated.

Li Xuan raised an eyebrow. Some of the secret memories in the 726 Blood Disciple's consciousness actually contained a kind of sealing power.

Once touched, it instantly activated, directly collapsing the 726 Blood Disciple's consciousness and destroying him.

"Very powerful."

Li Xuan felt a slight chill in his heart. To be able to instantly collapse the 726 Blood Disciple's consciousness and destroy him under his soul search showed that the power behind this was far beyond that of an Immortal Heavenly Venerable.

"The Blood Lord of the Netherworld Prison?"

Li Xuan pondered.

Although he was unable to search the core secrets of the 726 Blood Disciple's consciousness, he had obtained quite a bit of useful information.

The Netherworld Prison seemed to be some kind of world.

The 726 Blood Disciple was a servant, or perhaps a disciple, of the Blood Lord of the Netherworld Prison. Above the Blood Disciples were the Blood Sons.

As for the Blood Lord of the Netherworld Prison, he was mysterious and unfathomable. Li Xuan had only seen this name in the 726 Blood Disciple's memories. Details like appearance were probably core secrets that were lost with the collapse of the 726's consciousness.

"The heavenly rift of the Netherworld Prison?"

Li Xuan murmured to himself.

The 726 Blood Disciple had planned for this day for a very long time, but in the end he still failed.

"Enemies from beyond the heavens, huh."

Li Xuan sighed.

Heavenly rifts were holes in the world, cracks in this realm that had been eroded by external forces to form a rift.

Heavenly rifts varied in size. Small rifts were just tiny holes, like the one Xu Yan discovered in the Black Pool of the Azure Sea.

Large rifts, on the other hand, were vast in space.

The Abyss of the Azure Sea had once experienced a crack in the world, and under the permeation of the power of the Netherworld Prison, it had become a heavenly rift.

"Is Meiwu also from the Netherworld Prison?"

Li Xuan pondered.

He had already sealed the heavenly rift in Ice Province, and Meiwu could no longer enter.

"Is the Taisang world facing an external threat? The heavenly rifts are the channels for external enemies to invade. However, while external enemies can infiltrate through the rifts, the martial artists of this world cannot enter the enemy's territory and can only defend their positions."

Logically speaking, being sealed within this world seemed more likely to be a powerful invading enemy. Who had sealed it? The owner of the Taisang Book? If it was sealing a powerful enemy, why were there no powerful experts guarding it? Was it because the owner of the Taisang Book had sealed the enemy but perished in the process, leaving no one aware that a powerful enemy was sealed there? Li Xuan recalled that when the Wu Emperor spoke of that mysterious voice, he had mentioned the other party once raving madly about "liars" and such...
