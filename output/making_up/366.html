For this day, the 726th Blood Disciple had been planning for a long time, but in the end, he still failed.

Celestial Caves were cracks or holes in the world, formed by the invasion of external forces.

The Abyss of the Azure Sea had once experienced a spatial crack, and under the infiltration of the Underworld's power, it had become a Celestial Cave.
