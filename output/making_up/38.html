Chapter 38 Xu Yan Enlightened Again, the Agile Body Technique

After thinking about the body technique incantation, Li Xuan came to the Bagua diagram and said, "My disciple, you have been cultivating diligently these days, and your speed has improved, and your steps have become much more nimble.

"But you are still far from comprehending the body technique.

"Now that you have gained some insights into the body technique, it is time for me to pass down the body technique incantation to you.

"Remember it well."

Xu Yan stopped in his tracks and said excitedly, "Yes, Master!"

"The essence of the body technique lies in its lightness and swiftness. Being light means being silent and invisible, and being swift is like lightning..."

Li Xuan spoke in a lofty manner.

Xu Yan listened thoughtfully, vaguely understanding how he should comprehend the body technique.

"Light as a goose feather falling, swift as thundering strides leaving no trace!"

Li Xuan recited the incantation he had just composed, word by word.

"Disciple, ponder well on this incantation!"

After saying that, he turned and left with his hands behind his back.

"Yes, Master!" Xu Yan bowed.

By giving Xu Yan two lines of incantation, the rest was up to Xu Yan to comprehend the body technique. However, Li Xuan believed that his disciple would not disappoint him.

Xu Yan's mind was filled with the two lines of incantation, and the more he pondered, the more profound it seemed.

He looked up and saw that his master had already left. Just as he was about to continue his cultivation, he suddenly realized something.

"Master said that the power of the body technique lies in its lightness and swiftness...

"This is it? Master's steps are so leisurely and carefree, so light and comfortable..."

Xu Yan observed his master's departing figure, with his hands behind his back, his steps leisurely and comfortable, exuding a sense of effortlessness.

"Master's leisurely steps, is this an unconscious display of the body technique?

"No! Master's realm is so high that he has returned to the original simplicity. Although he seems to be walking slowly, he is actually concealing the supreme principles of the body technique.

"They say that teaching by words and by example, and Master just passed down the incantation to me. This leisurely gait must be the embodiment of his teachings!

"Yes! It must be so, I must comprehend the profound meaning hidden in Master's steps!"

After this realization, Xu Yan's spirits were immediately lifted.

He stared intently at his master's steps, and saw that his master's steps had a certain leisurely quality, light and carefree, yet also unrestrained and free.

The more he observed, the more he felt that his master's steps contained the profound principles of the body technique.

"Light as a goose feather falling, swift as thundering strides leaving no trace... I have gained some understanding."

Xu Yan watched his master's steps, and gradually, a flash of insight seemed to appear in his mind. The leisurely, light, and unrestrained steps blended with the incantation, and he vaguely grasped something.

But at this moment, Li Xuan's figure had already disappeared from Xu Yan's sight.

Li Xuan was in a very good mood at the moment. He had just composed two lines of incantation and passed them on to his disciple, and soon he would receive the feedback on the body technique.

Therefore, he walked with a leisurely and unrestrained gait, his whole being floating, and he even felt like humming a few songs.

He had no idea that his carefree and unrestrained steps had been observed by Xu Yan, who had engaged in a self-induced contemplation to comprehend the profound principles hidden within.

...

Since Xu Yan had contemplated the profound meaning behind his master's steps, when he studied the body technique incantation and practiced the body technique, the image of his master's leisurely, comfortable, and unrestrained steps constantly appeared in his mind.

Combining it with the incantation, he continued to gain new insights.

It was as if he only needed to break through a thin layer to fully comprehend the powerful body technique.

In the Bagua diagram, Xu Yan's steps became lighter, landing without a sound, and his turns became smoother. His leaps had a floating quality to them.

His figure flowed and maneuvered on the Bagua, his steps becoming increasingly light and agile, and his speed continuously increasing.

"I'm almost there!

"I feel that I'm just a step away from comprehending the body technique."
