Once one enters the Heaven and Earth Realm and understands the origin and principles of the Cang heaven and earth, even if an enemy is stronger within the Cang heaven and earth, they can't do anything to him.

Unless the opponent is powerful enough to destroy the Cang heaven and earth in a short time.

Li Xuan doubted whether such terrifying experts existed. Even for the owner of the Cang Book, it was unknown if they could achieve this.

After all, recording the principles of heaven and earth, depicting the principles and origin of heaven and earth, was a completely different concept from destroying an entire heaven and earth.

Even if it could be done, it wouldn't rely on powerful strength, but on understanding of heaven and earth, cleverly dismantling the entire heaven and earth.

Li Xuan had an expectant look on his face.

The Cauldron of Heaven and Earth!

"What a treasure!" Li Xuan was overjoyed.

This Cauldron of Heaven and Earth could refine even heaven and earth into pills.

"With the Extraordinary Gate Weapon Box, the Ultimate Soul Weapon, and now the Cauldron of Heaven and Earth, I have quite a collection of supreme treasures. I just lack a sword and a saber."

Li Xuan pondered briefly. A sword and saber might not be easy to obtain feedback for, but it didn't matter. With the sword techniques and saber techniques he had created, he didn't really need specific swords or sabers.

As long as his realm increased, he could directly transform heaven and earth into a sword.

"Using principles as a sword is also possible."

Li Xuan mused to himself.

"Only two principles left. These are the core of the entire outline of principles. Once these two principles are memorized, the principles of the entire heaven and earth will be complete.

"The origin of heaven and earth can also be understood."

Li Xuan looked at the last two principles on the Cang Book. These were also the most changeable and difficult to memorize principles.

Even at his current realm, staring at them for too long gave him a sense of dizziness, showing how profound and mysterious these two principles were.

"Finally memorized one principle, only the last one left."

Li Xuan immersed himself completely in memorizing the last two principles, not even knowing how much time had passed.

The last two principles were finally memorized, with only the very last one remaining. Once the final principle was memorized, he would be able to fully understand the operation of the Cang heaven and earth principles and comprehend the origin of heaven and earth.

"Just one last step."

Li Xuan was excited.

Now, he had a vague concept of the operation of heaven and earth principles and the understanding of the origin of heaven and earth.

He just needed to memorize the last principle, and instantly he would be able to integrate all the principles, connecting them completely.

Sudden feedback came from the Golden Book of the Great Dao.

Xu Yan had comprehended the cultivation method of the Holy Sword Realm.

Li Xuan was immediately excited. His sword realm had advanced once again.

"The Holy Sword Realm."

Experiencing the profundities of the Holy Sword Realm, Li Xuan sighed inwardly. His strength had increased again.

No sword practitioner could use a sword against him!

Li Xuan couldn't help but ponder.

"Sword Dao, Sword Dao. Beyond the Holy Sword, is it touching upon the sword of the Great Dao, truly cultivating the Great Dao of the sword?"

Li Xuan pondered for a moment and quickly had a general idea.

"Now that the Holy Sword Realm has been comprehended, the Heaven and Earth Realm can't be far off."

Li Xuan was full of anticipation.

Just as he was about to continue comprehending the principles, feedback from the Golden Book of the Great Dao came again.
