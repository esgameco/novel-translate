On Canglan Island, the discussion on how to convert the inner domain martial arts to the Great Wilderness Martial Arts had just concluded.

After research, the general direction of converting to the Great Wilderness Martial Arts had been clarified, but the implementation would be difficult.

"For those below the First Realm, to convert to the Great Wilderness Martial Arts, the first step is to cultivate the blood qi, and this difficulty is relatively low. If there are blood qi elixirs to assist, along with methods to refine the blood qi, it would not be too difficult.

"However, after the conversion, since the foundation was not solid from the beginning, the strength would be relatively weaker compared to the orthodox Great Wilderness martial artists."

Xu Yan summarized.

Zhou Ying's martial art was at the Fourth Realm after conversion, while in the inner domain, the Third Realm was when the martial arts foundation was truly solid. Therefore, conversion at the Fourth Realm was relatively easier.

But after the Third Realm, even with the assistance of blood qi elixirs and the refinement of blood qi, the martial arts foundation would be difficult to change, so they would be weaker than the orthodox Great Wilderness martial artists.

Nevertheless, they would still be stronger than the inner domain martial artists.

Therefore, once the conversion method was circulated, many martial artists below the First Realm would want to convert their martial arts.

But there was a prerequisite - the need for blood qi elixirs. This meant that not all martial artists could convert, as they might not be able to afford the blood qi elixirs.

"The essence of the Great Wilderness Martial Arts lies in the mastery of the Heaven-Earth Bridge. Even if one refines the true qi, without the Heaven-Earth Bridge, one cannot be considered a Great Wilderness martial artist.

"However, for martial artists at the Grand Master realm, their foundation is already fixed, so they cannot establish the Heaven-Earth Bridge, which is a problem."

Xu Yan sighed.

For Grand Master martial artists to convert, in addition to refining the true qi, the Heaven-Earth Bridge is the key. Without the Heaven-Earth Bridge, they cannot directly absorb the spiritual energy from heaven and earth to refine into true qi.

Instead, they can only transform it into the inner domain martial artist's internal martial qi, and then refine it into true qi through cultivation methods.

Peng Yuan and the other martial arts scholars were not discouraged, but rather excited, "We will continue to research, perhaps we can find an alternative method."

Xu Yan nodded. He would not spend too much time on the conversion method, as he had just broken through to the Penetrating Mystery Realm and had more martial insights to organize.

Next, Xu Yan guided Xie Lingfeng on sword dao cultivation, such as how to comprehend oneself and how to attain sword heart clarity.

Whether Xie Lingfeng could ultimately attain sword heart clarity depended on him alone, and Xu Yan had already provided all the guidance he could.

Sitting cross-legged on the mountain peak of Canglan Island, Xu Yan began to settle his mind.

Suddenly, he thought of Su Lingxiu.

"My shijie should have dissected those corpses, will she have any harvests? Can she refine a certain elixir to transform the meridians into a Heaven-Earth Bridge? Although it would only be a pseudo-Heaven-Earth Bridge, it would still possess the abilities of the Heaven-Earth Bridge, and thus allow the conversion to the Great Wilderness Martial Arts."

Xu Yan's eyes lit up, feeling that this method might be feasible. Once the Qingqing Pavilion moved to Canglan Island, he could have his shijie explore this.

In the inner domain, after the battle with the Qingqing Pavilion, it gradually became peaceful.

Although there were underlying currents, on the surface, the martial arts world remained harmonious and calm, with few conflicts.

Elixirs were selling well in the inner domain.

The methods of the Great Wilderness Martial Arts were also gradually spreading out.

On this day, the Seven Star Academy opened a Great Wilderness Martial Arts course, causing some ripples in the calm martial arts world.

Xu Yan was unaware of the underlying currents in the outside world, and even if he knew, he would not have cared.

He was currently settling his mind.

In his mind, the scene of his master's hand slashing and extinguishing the soul of the Hidden Pavilion Lord emerged.

That strike had directly annihilated the Hidden Pavilion Lord's soul. The mysterious fluctuations in that instant were etched in Xu Yan's memory, and he was now contemplating that mysterious power.

From the battle with the Blood Demon King, Xu Yan realized that if he mastered his master's soul-extinguishing technique, killing a Half-Step Celestial Being would not be too difficult.

"If the enemy also possesses the means to slay the soul and spirit, how should I defend against it?"

Xu Yan pondered deeply.

This was more difficult to defend against than any attack.

As Xu Yan contemplated, at one moment, two sword lights appeared before him, circling and rotating endlessly, as if they would never cease.

The mysterious fluctuations rippled within the sword wheel, then disappeared.

"No, I cannot integrate it into the sword wheel, I cannot unleash it."

Xu Yan's brow furrowed slightly.

With a thought, the Mountain-River Sword Intent emerged, the rivers and mountains flowing, as if encompassing the heavens and earth.

Immediately, the Mountain-River Sword Intent surged, and the outlines of the actual heavens and earth appeared within the illusory image - this was the second realm of the Mountain-River Sword Intent! After breaking through to the Penetrating Mystery Realm, Xu Yan could finally manifest the second realm of the Mountain-River Sword Intent.

"I should start from the sword intent. If I can comprehend the sword intent that can slay the soul and spirit, combined with the sword dao's killing techniques and martial arts divine abilities, the killing power would be even stronger! And not only can it attack, but it can also defend!"

Xu Yan's heart was stirred. He had found the direction for his cultivation.

The Life-and-Death Sword Wheel appeared again, merging into the Mountain-River Sword Intent. Within the mountain and river, the sword wheel revolved, with endless transformations between life and death, killing and changing.

For several days, Xu Yan tried to comprehend the sword intent, but he still had not made any breakthroughs.

Instead, the Life-and-Death Sword Wheel had become more complete and powerful, with more killing techniques.

And when unleashing the killing power, there was a faint aura of death.

"Is the direction I'm cultivating wrong? The Life-and-Death Sword Wheel is something I comprehended from the Taiji, but I've only grasped the superficial aspects."

Xu Yan pondered, sitting motionless on the mountain peak.

A gentle breeze blew, entering through the collar, filling his chest, and brushing past his sleeves.

In that instant, a flash of insight occurred in Xu Yan's mind.

"The Bagua contains the profound mysteries of heaven and earth. Xun, the wind, the wind enters through every crevice... I should use this as the direction to comprehend a new sword intent."

At this moment, Xu Yan became excited.

He had found the direction for his cultivation, and began to carefully contemplate the gentle breeze, with the Bagua diagram and its many mysteries emerging in his mind.
