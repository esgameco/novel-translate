The moon maiden was not the least bit afraid. She drew out a silver-white whip and cracked it, letting out a cold sneer. "You shameless old ghost of the Spiritual Sect, the moon maiden is not afraid of you!"

Two elders from the Jade Spirit Sect were about to rush towards the moon maiden, when suddenly a sword light slashed down like thunderous lightning.

Boom!

The two Jade Spirit Sect elders' expressions changed, and they hastily moved to block the attack, retreating at the same time.

"She's right, the time has not come yet. Why the hurry? Hurrying to your deaths?"

A cold voice rang out.

In midair, a young man appeared arrogantly.

Behind him were the burly Meng Chong and a slightly chubby young man.

Following closely behind the trio of Xu Yan, Meng Chong, and the other young man was a huge - was it a cat or a tiger? For a moment, the crowd couldn't quite make it out.

It looked like a magnificent tiger, yet it let out a "meow"...

Zhu Liang and the other powerful cultivators of the Jade Spirit Sect had grim expressions, glaring at Xu Yan, wishing to tear him to shreds!

Their gaze then fell on the few people sitting on the back of the giant cat behind Xu Yan.

They directly ignored Shi Er and the others, their gaze pausing briefly on Su Ling Xiu, then landing on Li Xuan - was this young man the one behind Xu Yan and Meng Chong?

In that instant, Zhu Liang and the others had murderous intent, exchanging a look with the powerful Cultivated Celestial cultivators of the Sha family, reaching a tacit understanding.

Today, Xu Yan must die, and all those related to him must die as well! The two Jade Spirit Sect elders who had originally planned to capture the moon maiden immediately retreated, and now it was a battle between geniuses.

In the eyes of the crowd, Xu Yan had come to his death. Xia Ming's true inheritance, the Divine Radiance Treasure Body of Lu Xin Ting - what a fearsome reputation. As a Celestial Immortal realm cultivator who could slay Cultivated Celestial realm cultivators, even if Xu Yan was extraordinary, he was still just a wandering cultivator.

How could a wandering cultivator compare to the geniuses of the Spiritual Sect? This was an established notion.

"So you are Xu Yan?"

The moon maiden looked at Xu Yan curiously.

"That's right!"

Xu Yan nodded, glancing at the moon maiden. He was slightly surprised, as the young girl's strength was not weak, and she even gave him the feeling of being stronger than Lu Xin Ting.

His gaze paused for a moment on the old man beside the young girl. Xu Yan's heart trembled slightly - the old man was very strong, the strongest he had seen since entering the Spiritual Realm. The old man looked at Xu Yan with a puzzled expression.

Not the genius cultivated by some acquaintance as he had guessed, and his aura seemed a bit unusual, as if the martial dao he cultivated exceeded the old man's understanding.

He looked up at the giant cat hovering in midair, arrogantly towering over everyone.

This giant cat was also somewhat unusual, seeming to be a spirit beast, yet lacking some of the aura of a spirit beast.

The young man sitting on the cat's back was calmly sipping tea, serene and unassuming, without any overbearing aura of a powerful cultivator, seeming quite ordinary.

"Not an acquaintance."

The old man frowned.

Li Xuan sat on the crimson cat, looking down upon the crowd of Cultivated Celestial cultivators below, not even considering them worthy of his attention.

The Protector of the Way of the Crimson Abyss Sect's Second Elder, his strength was not weak, actually reaching the peak of the Cultivated Celestial realm, though he had only just recently broken through.

Looking at the old man beside the moon maiden, Li Xuan began to ponder.

The old man's strength was very powerful, though both were at the peak of the Cultivated Celestial realm, the old man was significantly stronger than the Crimson Abyss Sect's Second Elder, and there seemed to be an unhealed old wound on him.

"A spirit body? It has just been shattered, and now his spirit body is in a broken state."

Li Xuan was slightly surprised.

"This girl, her talent is not bad."

His gaze swept over the moon maiden, realizing she was a spirit body genius.

"A bit inferior to Su Ling Xiu's spirit body, but still quite remarkable."

Spirit bodies were the pinnacle of talents in the Spiritual Realm, extremely rare.

Even Treasure Bodies were not commonly seen, especially top-tier Treasure Bodies.

This was why Lu Xin Ting's reputation was so great - he possessed one of the Ten Great Treasure Bodies, the Divine Radiance Treasure Body, and had fully awakened its potential.

His strength far surpassed that of cultivators at the same realm.

Apart from the old man, Li Xuan's gaze fell on the small palace atop a large tree, where the person inside, though only in the middle Cultivated Celestial realm, was actually stronger than the Crimson Abyss Sect's Second Elder.

"The true inheritance of the Transcendent Spiritual Sect? No wonder the Transcendent Spiritual Sect is so lofty, not even thirty years old, already in the middle Cultivated Celestial realm, and their strength nearly crushes the peak geniuses of the Spiritual Sects."

Although the young man in the palace was very strong, already in the middle Cultivated Celestial realm before the age of thirty, Xu Yan's current strength was still inferior to him.

But Xu Yan was not yet twenty years old...

Li Xuan saw through the cultivators around the arena - they were all just small fry, easily crushed. Even the old man beside the moon maiden was no exception.

Cui Hua Yu looked at Xu Yan, frowning and deep in thought, a rare sight as he was usually sickly.

"His aura is somewhat unusual, as if the martial dao he cultivates is quite special."

As a true inheritor of the Transcendent Spiritual Sect, Cui Hua Yu's vision and understanding far exceeded that of Zhu Liang and the others.

Xu Yan had not concealed his aura, so Cui Hua Yu was now intrigued.

Glancing up at the giant cat, he also slightly furrowed his brow.

"Not like a spirit beast, what is this?"

As a disciple of the Imperial Spiritual Sect, no one understood spirit beasts better than him.

"Perhaps, there will be an interesting show."

Cui Hua Yu murmured lazily, then reclined back down.

In the spotlight of all eyes, Xu Yan stepped into the arena, looking indifferently at Lu Xin Ting, and suddenly let out a sigh. "So you are the Crimson Abyss inheritor, the Divine Radiance Treasure Body Lu Xin Ting? I'm a bit disappointed, your reputation is so grand, but your strength is just this?"

Lu Xin Ting was taken aback, then his face flushed with anger, as golden light surged from his body.

All around, the cultivators fell silent, then quickly sucked in a breath. "Where does Xu Yan get the guts? Daring to look down on Lu Xin Ting's strength?"

"That's the Crimson Abyss inheritor, one of the Ten Great Treasure Bodies - the Divine Radiance Treasure Body. Yet in his eyes, he's not even worth mentioning?"

"Arrogant, far too arrogant! A mere wandering cultivator dares to be so presumptuous?"

Lu Xin Ting raised his golden halberd, pointing it at Xu Yan.

"Today, I'll let this lowly wandering cultivator, all the lowly wandering cultivators in the Spiritual Realm, know what a Spiritual Sect genius is!"

Boom!

In that instant, golden light surged from Lu Xin Ting, the golden radiance shining in all directions, his powerful aura shocking all the onlookers.

Zhu Liang and the other Cultivated Celestial cultivators all had grave expressions, inwardly marveling - truly a top-tier Spiritual Sect genius, truly one of the Ten Great Treasure Bodies.

At the Celestial Immortal realm, the aura he exuded was even more formidable than ordinary early Cultivated Celestial realm cultivators.

Lu Xin Ting's aura was surging, golden light flowing, his golden halberd shining with golden brilliance, the entire person like a divine god, his imposing presence...
