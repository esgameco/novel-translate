Even if they surrendered or betrayed the family, they would not meet a good end.

Rather than becoming slaves to others, it was better to fight to the death to protect their own interests!

After all, as long as the Immortal Realm ancestors were still around, the Wan family could not be completely destroyed!

The two Immortal Venerable experts who went to rescue him were also badly wounded, their strength greatly diminished.

As a result, the crisis facing the Wan family was now imminent. They had to seek help.

Nine Mountains Realm, Spirit Veneration Mountain, had now become demon territory.

The Wan family ancestor was no match for him!

Now, Red Cat Great Demon King was preparing to officially emerge and unify Nine Mountains Realm, establishing it as the realm of the demon race and widely inviting spirit beasts from the Divine Realm to join.

Great Canopy Mountain would be left as human territory within Nine Mountains Realm, not just the domain of the Wan family alone. Red Cat would take action to deal with Hengfeng Mountain, Thousand Fold Mountain, and Cloud Pool Mountain, putting an end to the chaos in Nine Mountains Realm.

It must be said that this big tiger truly had lofty ambitions, actually wanting to pacify the turmoil in Nine Mountains Realm and establish the foundation for the demon race in the Divine Realm.

The Wan family ancestor looked confused. "The demon race's supreme being cooperated with my Wan family?"

The Wan family ancestor looked puzzled. The demon race's supreme being had some connection with Wan Tianlin? Suddenly, two figures appeared in his mind.

Xu Yan and Meng Chong!

"Is it Meng Chong?" After all, with his towering physique, looking like a small giant when using martial arts, Meng Chong seemed more fitting for the title of demon race supreme being.

"That's the Second Supreme Being!" Red Cat said coldly.

The Wan family ancestor continued to be bewildered. Xu Yan was the demon race's supreme being? "Isn't Xu Yan human? Not a spirit beast?"

Red Cat gave him a disdainful look and said, "Who told you our demon race's supreme being must be a spirit beast?"

After all, if the ancestor of the demon race was human, what was strange about the supreme being of the demon race also being human?

Red Cat turned to leave, saying: "We'll begin in three days. Here are three pills as a gift for your Wan family. Three days is enough time for three people in your Wan family to fully recover."

The time has finally come for me, Great Demon King Red Cat, to shock the Divine Realm with my name.

At this moment, with the cultivation of great demon martial arts and the strengthening of demonic divine abilities, Red Cat's confidence had greatly increased. Killing an Immortal Venerable expert with one swipe of his paw would not be very difficult.

Although Tian Shiqi was very powerful and still not his match, he had his own trump cards and was not afraid at all!

Moreover, Jade Little Dragon and Little Ha had also broken through. Killing Immortal Venerable experts would be no problem for them either.

Nine Mountains Realm was in chaos. It was time to pacify and unify it, establishing the foundation for the demon race.
