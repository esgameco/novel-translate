Meng Chong's Golden Body apertures started circulating, and the chill surged into the apertures, nourishing the Golden Body. At this moment, Meng Chong suddenly gained some enlightenment.

The Great Sun Golden Body was extremely powerful and unyielding, but to advance and transform, he needed to accumulate a solid foundation, and the nourishment of the Golden Body apertures was crucial.

At this moment, Meng Chong realized that by using the chill to nourish himself, he could activate the yang vigor of the Great Sun Golden Body. Yin and yang interacting and transforming, he would be able to advance and break through.

"The breakthrough is right in front of me, the opportunity has appeared!"

Meng Chong was greatly excited.

Continuing forward, he suddenly saw a concave area at the base of the mountain wall, covered in a thick layer of ice.

The yin-cold energy was emanating from the concave area.

Vaguely, it seemed that under the thick ice, there was a broken corpse.

And the source of the chill was this corpse.

The remains of the powerful cultivators who had ambushed the Demon Lord, containing the yin-cold power they had cultivated, had diffused and accumulated in the concave area.

After such a long time, the icy chill was still bone-piercing. The power of this expert was truly terrifying.

Meng Chong looked around the ravine, feeling that this place seemed to have some special qualities that allowed the residual power of the experts to dissipate slowly.

It was precisely because of this that even after such a long time, the remaining power of the experts was still formidable.

"Choosing to ambush the Demon Lord here was likely also because of the special nature of this place."

Meng Chong thought to himself.

He walked towards the concave area in the mountain wall, and the yin-cold energy became even more piercing, like fine needles stabbing into his body.

"Meng Chong, what are you going there for? It's too cold."

Ziyun's delicate body trembled, her teeth chattering, her face paling slightly.

Meng Chong grabbed her and threw her back, saying, "I need to cultivate for a while, you stay out here for a bit."

Ignoring Ziyun's angry protests, he directly entered the concave area.

As soon as he entered, he felt the chill like needles constantly stimulating him, but the Great Sun Golden Body immediately started rotating, shining with golden light, his body like a human sun.

"This person died quite miserably!"

Meng Chong looked at the shattered corpse under the thick ice in the concave area, as if it had been slashed dozens of times.

He sat down cross-legged in the concave area and began to cultivate, preparing to break through to the Penetrating Mystery Realm.

Ziyun could only watch helplessly from afar, waiting for Meng Chong to finish his cultivation.

Lifting her head to look deeper into the ravine, there was a hint of caution in her eyes.

"The Demon Hunting Battlefield, will there really be a Jade Token? The Gate of the Spiritual Realm, it shouldn't be too far from here, but how to open it, why can't I see it?"

Ziyun took out a small notebook from her storage bag and began to study it carefully.

Time passed, and Meng Chong sitting in the lotus position had his body radiating golden light, and the ice in the concave area seemed to be melting.

The permeating yin-cold energy had disappeared.

Boom!

As Meng Chong's aura surged, the golden light grew stronger, and his aura became more powerful.

His bald head reflected the light, and he sat there like a golden arhat.

Ziyun put away the notebook and watched from afar, her eyes filled with joy.

The breakthrough of the Great Sun Golden Body was a foregone conclusion, and Meng Chong had broken through to the Penetrating Mystery Realm, and at the same time, he had advanced and transformed, becoming a Golden Immortal Body!

"My strength has increased!"

Meng Chong was exhilarated as he sensed the changes in his body.

"This advancement and transformation has made the Great Sun Golden Body even stronger, with a more solid foundation. This is the Penetrating Mystery Realm!"

Meng Chong stood up, carefully feeling the changes in his physical body.

The Golden Body apertures were filled with profundity, and with just a thought, he could manifest a thirty-foot golden body, becoming a small giant!

"With my current physical body, even treasured weapons would have difficulty harming me!"

Recalling his master's feat of single-handedly pushing back dozens of peak sect masters, Meng Chong reckoned that he could probably do the same now. Manifesting the thirty-foot golden body, becoming a small giant, and disregarding the attacks of great sect masters, directly pushing them away with brute force.

"This Demon Hunting Battlefield is a good place!"

Meng Chong looked towards the depths of the ravine.

Ziyun ran up to him, her face flushed, and she reached out to caress his sturdy and powerful muscles, which were filled with a formidable aura.

"Slap!"

Meng Chong slapped her hand away, frowning, "Keep your hands to yourself!"

Ziyun pouted and giggled, "Meng Chong, congratulations on your further advancement in strength!"

"Alright, let's go."

Meng Chong continued walking into the ravine, and not far away, he saw a fifth-grade spirit herb under the mountain wall.

The spirit herb was crystalline, blending into the snowy white, almost overlooking it.

Ziyun went forward to dig up the spirit herb.

As they continued forward, they discovered several more fifth-grade spirit herbs.

"This is strange, fifth-grade spirit herbs are extremely rare, how come there are so many here?"

Meng Chong frowned and said.

"It should be that the powerful cultivators who ambushed the Demon Lord had spirit herb seeds on them, which scattered and grew into fifth-grade spirit herbs over the years!"

Ziyun thought for a moment and said.

Meng Chong glanced at her, this woman knew a lot of things, she must know some secrets about the Demon Hunting Battlefield.

"What are you looking for here?"

Meng Chong asked solemnly.

"A Jade Token!"

Ziyun's expression became serious. "Finding the Jade Token can open the Gate of the Spiritual Realm, Meng Chong, why don't you come with me then?"

She looked at Meng Chong with hopeful eyes.

"We'll see."

Meng Chong replied evasively.

The Gate of the Spiritual Realm, what kind of place it was, was still unknown, and he felt that his current strength was still not enough.
