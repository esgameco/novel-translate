A faint bloody aura permeated the Serpent Peak Skyhole, as if there was a foreign essence condensing inside.

Every Skyhole guarded by a Blood Child was as vast as a small world.

"Although this is also a Netherworld Skyhole, it's somewhat different from the one in the Qinghua Realm. The bloody aura here is more intense, and there seems to be a foreign essence."

"Is it the aura of the Netherworld? Could it be that the fabric of space-time is more fragile here, allowing the Netherworld aura to gather without dispersing?"

As Xu Yan walked through the Skyhole, he noticed the differences and couldn't help but wonder silently.

"Could it be that treasures like the Blood Heart Fruit appear because the Netherworld aura gathers and merges with the essence of this world?"

Inside the entrance of the Serpent Peak Skyhole, a great city had been built. Any enemies from the Netherworld wanting to break out of the Skyhole would have to defeat this defensive city.

To some extent, the Dagaishan side held an advantage, otherwise they wouldn't have been able to build a defensive city inside the Skyhole.

In the center of the city was a tall tower, with a vague but powerful aura emanating from it. That was where the Wan family elder, an Immortal Venerable powerhouse, was stationed.

"The Blood Heart Fruit grows in the depths of the Skyhole, at the core of the Netherworld camp. It may exist in other places, but it's impossible to find. So the most efficient way is to go to the depths of the Skyhole to pick it."

Xu Yan looked towards the depths of the Skyhole, vaguely sensing some battles in the surroundings.

He had learned from other martial artists that the Wan family was planning to launch an attack against the Netherworld side in twenty days, pushing towards the depths of the Skyhole to harvest the Blood Heart Fruit.

The Wan family had offered generous rewards for this, and some unaffiliated True King Venerables were gathering to lend their strength.

"Twenty days is too long."

Xu Yan couldn't wait twenty days. Every day was extremely precious to him.

"In this Skyhole, only the Blood Child poses a threat to me. As long as I avoid the Blood Child, the rest of the Blood Disciples are not worth worrying about."

Xu Yan headed towards the depths of the Skyhole.

Since a major battle had just ended, there were only sporadic conflicts and skirmishes in the Skyhole. Most Blood Disciples and Blood Slaves had returned to their base, with only a few patrolling outside.

"Can I impersonate a Blood Disciple?"

Xu Yan suddenly thought of this question.

"Mimicking the aura of a Blood Disciple doesn't seem too difficult. I could blend in with their aura..."

Xu Yan pondered this idea.

He hadn't considered impersonating a Blood Disciple before, but now he suddenly had this thought in order to harvest the Blood Heart Fruit.

"However, Blood Disciples all have blood-red skin. Even the strongest Blood Disciples, while not as red, still have some redness to their skin.

"To impersonate a Blood Disciple, I first need to make my skin blood-red."

Mimicking the aura wasn't difficult, but how could he imitate the blood-red skin?

Xu Yan pondered, then thought of the first realm of martial arts, the Blood Essence Realm. If he could make his blood essence rise to the surface of his skin, wouldn't that turn it blood-red?

"It's worth a try."

Xu Yan became secretly excited. "I just need to avoid that Blood Child, and I definitely won't be discovered. That Blood Child won't pay attention to every Blood Disciple returning anyway. I'll pick the Blood Heart Fruit and leave immediately.

"Even if I'm exposed after getting the Blood Heart Fruit, I can still retreat."

Xu Yan believed that with his escape speed, he could return to the defensive city at the Skyhole entrance before the Blood Child could catch up to him.

The Wan family elder would naturally come out to resist the attacking Blood Child.

"I'll impersonate a patrolling Blood Disciple."

Xu Yan began searching for Blood Disciples in the Skyhole.

Finally, near the outer edges of the Skyhole's depths, he encountered a Blood Disciple leading a patrol of over a dozen Blood Slaves.

Whoosh! To avoid creating a big disturbance and risking the failure of his impersonation plan, Xu Yan immediately used his Sword Realm technique, instantly enveloping the patrol group.

Puff puff!

Those Blood Slaves instantly turned into bloody mist and perished.

"Roar!"

The Blood Disciple was shocked. He held his blade, striking out fiercely to try to resist the attack while calling for help.

But within the Sword Realm, no sound could travel outward.

Hum! The sword intent was sharp, and sword light swept through. In just a few breaths, that Blood Disciple was killed!

Xu Yan raised his hand and retrieved the Blood Disciple's blood bead, directly storing it on his person. He also took the clothes and the crimson long blade.

He changed into the Blood Disciple's clothes and hung the crimson long blade at his waist. After pondering for a moment, he slightly adjusted the muscles of his face, changing to roughly resemble that Blood Disciple.

Taking a deep breath, he stirred up his long-unused blood essence, turning his skin a light red color.

The aura around him gradually changed, matching the blood bead he carried. At this moment, Xu Yan was indistinguishable from a Blood Disciple.

"I shouldn't be discovered, right?"

Xu Yan examined himself, and after making sure there were no flaws, he set off towards the depths of the Skyhole, to the core of the Netherworld territory.

As for the small team of Blood Slaves he was supposed to be leading, if they disappeared, they disappeared. He could claim they were attacked and the Blood Slaves were killed.

This kind of thing had happened more than once or twice before.

In fact, Xu Yan had heard that some Blood Disciples, when injured and their blood power was severely depleted, would even devour their subordinate Blood Slaves to replenish themselves.

This wasn't even a secret in the Netherworld anymore.

The deeper he went into the Skyhole, the more intense the bloody aura became, reflecting all around, with a gloomy essence permeating the air.

Along the way, he encountered several patrol teams. They didn't say anything to him, this lone Blood Disciple, but their expressions seemed to assume he had encountered an attack, been injured, and devoured his subordinate Blood Slaves.

Huge blood-red stone houses were arranged in rows, forming lines. Looking out, one could see in the center of the stone house cluster a towering, enormous stone house with blood light flashing at its top.

That was where the Blood Child resided.

Around the Blood Child's towering stone house, the inner circle was where the Blood Disciples lived, while the outermost circle was for the Blood Slaves. Xu Yan noticed that the Blood Slaves didn't have individual stone houses to themselves.

Blood Disciples had their own independent stone houses. The strength of the blood light on the stone houses indicated the relative strength and status of their occupants.

"Which stone house is mine?"

Xu Yan suddenly pondered.

"Never mind, I'm here for the Blood Heart Fruit, not to live here. But where is the Blood Heart Fruit?"

Xu Yan entered the stone house cluster, trying to act as normal as possible, pretending to be familiar with the surroundings while secretly searching for the location of the Blood Heart Fruit.

"Oh? Is that a Blood Heart Fruit?"

Suddenly, Xu Yan saw not far ahead, in front of a Blood Disciple's stone house, a blood-red plant growing like a vine. The plant grew along the stone house, and an egg-sized, heart-shaped blood-red fruit hung from the vine.

Xu Yan strolled over casually, as if taking a leisurely walk, and came to the stone house, right under the Blood Heart Fruit. He examined the Blood Heart Fruit for a moment, then reached out and picked it.

Bang! The door of the stone house opened, and a Blood Disciple walked out, glaring at him angrily: "Eight Six Seven, this is mine!"

Eight Six Seven? Xu Yan's heart skipped a beat, then he understood - this was the code number of the Blood Disciple he was impersonating.
