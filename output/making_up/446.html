Apart from his disciples, only a few people possessed the life-saving jade talisman.

And these few people all had relatively close relationships and some connections with him.

Since giving out the life-saving jade talismans until now, no one had fallen into crisis and activated the talisman. This was the first time.

"It's not Xu Yan, nor Meng Chong, and not Jiang Buping either."

Li Xuan was somewhat surprised. His three disciples were all adventuring in the Divine Realm, facing powerful forces, and were more likely to encounter danger.

As a result, the first to trigger the life-saving jade talisman was actually not a disciple.

But rather, Du Yuying!

"She's the future daughter-in-law disciple. What crisis could she have encountered? She's a disciple of the Tai Miao Divine Sect, and Tai Miao Divine Sect is located in the Tai Miao Realm, controlling an entire realm, one of the great realms of the Divine Realm."

Li Xuan was a bit astonished.

Looking up towards the direction where the life-saving jade talisman was activated, his Heavenly Dao Eye perceived his own power emanating from the activated talisman.

With a slight gesture of his hand, he manipulated the Heavenly Dao rules for a moment. Instantly, a force of Heavenly Dao rules descended, empowering the talisman's power, raising it to the level of the Heavenly Realm in an instant.

With his understanding of the Cang Heavenly Realm and his mastery of the Heavenly Dao rules, even across vast distances, he could easily mobilize the power of the Heavenly Dao rules to empower it.

After all, that was his talisman, containing his power, and it was in the Divine Realm. With his current strength, mobilizing the Heavenly Dao rules to empower it was an easy task.

"Even if surrounded and attacked by a group of Immortal Venerable, it could easily kill them."

Li Xuan smiled calmly.

The power of the talisman began to recede, returning to Du Yuying's body, apparently having resolved the crisis.

...

Somewhere in the Divine Realm, an unkempt old man was lying on a large rock.

Suddenly, he jumped up.

Looking up at the sky, his brows furrowed tightly, with a look of surprise and uncertainty on his face.

"Who is mobilizing the power of Heaven and Earth? Who has such ability?"

At this moment, he was somewhat shocked.

"Who has secretly come to the Divine Realm?"

His brows furrowed tightly as familiar figures flashed through his mind, but none of them were related to the mobilization of the power of Heaven and Earth just now.

"Something's not right. To mobilize the power of Heaven and Earth so stealthily, unless it was me, others probably couldn't detect it. And those who could do this can be counted on one hand, but they..."

The old man frowned in deep thought.

"Could it be that someone survived?"

He was utterly perplexed, but those who could do this and prevent him from tracing the source of the mobilized power of Heaven and Earth were only a handful of people, and no one else.

And those few people had disappeared for a very, very long time.

"Could it be him?"

Suddenly, as if remembering something, he shook his head again: "Impossible. If it were him, he definitely wouldn't be so low-key, and he couldn't get out anyway."

Unable to figure it out, the old man's expression became somewhat solemn. An unknown existence was hidden in the world, and it was unclear whether it was good or bad.

"Those two bastards Tian Sha and Di Ying are quite capable of stirring up trouble. Let's see what they can stir up, hopefully they don't end up harming themselves."

Then, thinking of something, he sneered.

Lying back on the rock, "Forget it, if the sky is going to fall, then let it fall. Better not to worry about it."

Exhaling, he continued to sleep soundly.

In the Divine Realm, Tai Miao Realm.

"Great-grandmother, are you alright?"

Du Yuying supported Xin Mengrou, whose face was pale and who was badly injured.

"Cough, I'm fine."

Xin Mengrou took a deep breath, looking towards where the enemies had disappeared, her heart once again filled with shock.

"Great-grandmother, what should we do now?"

Du Yuying asked worriedly.

Although at the critical moment, she had activated the life-saving talisman given by the senior, easily killing the enemies, the subsequent troubles would definitely not be small.

Among the enemies, one was an elder of the Tai Miao Divine Sect.

"Return to the sect. If anyone asks, just say we were saved by a mysterious senior. Don't mention your life-saving talisman."

Xin Mengrou said, taking a deep breath.

What had just happened was too shocking.

That figure was like the supreme being of Heaven and Earth. With a wave of his hand, the might of Heaven and Earth was in his grasp. Immortal Venerables were like ants, wiped out with a wave of his hand, turned to ashes without leaving a trace.

Even top Immortal Venerables were the same.

"Great-grandmother, I understand."

Du Yuying nodded.

But there was still worry in her eyes. The internal strife of the Tai Miao Sect had gradually become apparent.

And great-grandmother cultivated the Tai Miao Dream Fantasy Dao, and had even developed some of her own insights and understanding from this Dao.

This was all thanks to the senior's guidance back then.

As a result, this matter had leaked out, and the powerful figures from another faction within the Tai Miao Sect intended to suppress the Tai Miao Dream Fantasy Dao, which led to this attack.

If it weren't for her wanting to go down the mountain this time to inquire about news of Xu Yan, accompanying her great-grandmother out, her great-grandmother would likely have been assassinated.

"Hmph, a bunch of old bitches, afraid that I'll become the master of the Tai Miao Dream Fantasy Dao. Just you wait!"

Xin Mengrou sneered.

Having survived this great danger, it also destined her rise.

Now, she was about to turn dreams into reality, to make the illusory real. Once she broke through, she would have an overwhelming advantage within the same realm.

And besides her, Yun Miaomiao was also cultivating the Tai Miao Dream Fantasy Dao, though it was another kind of dream fantasy dao. If she could truly succeed, her strength would also be extremely powerful.

"Great-grandmother, should we find an opportunity to pay our respects to the senior?"

Du Yuying asked expectantly.

Xin Mengrou looked at her, her face full of helplessness: "That kid Xu Yan isn't by the senior's side, you won't be able to see him if you go."

Du Yuying's jade face turned red, "Great-grandmother, what are you saying? I meant that you could go seek guidance from the senior, you would definitely be able to break through faster."

"Alright, I know what you and Miaomiao are thinking. You two, ah, your strength is too weak. If you don't work hard to cultivate, you'll only hold Xu Yan back.

"Forget it, no matter how you cultivate, you're destined to hold Xu Yan back. That kid is simply terrifying."

Xin Mengrou sighed.

Xu Yan's fame had already spread throughout the Divine Realm. He had single-handedly killed many Immortal Venerables and was now fighting against the powerful force of Tian Sha and Di Ying.

There was also Meng Chong, who was also incredibly fierce. Despite being wanted and pursued by the Myriad Treasures Alliance, he was still carefree and even occasionally raided branches of the Myriad Treasures Alliance.

Worthy of being the senior's disciples, they were all unbelievably strong.

"The Divine Realm is likely to see major changes. You should improve your strength as soon as possible."

Xin Mengrou and Du Yuying concealed their auras and hurried back to the Tai Miao Divine Sect.

Half an hour after they left, a figure appeared.

The newcomer wore a green long dress, her figure as if from a dream. After investigating the surroundings, she revealed a solemn expression.

"Not a single survivor!"

Three Immortal Venerables, one of whom was even a top Immortal Venerable, had all failed.

Moreover, they had been killed in such a short time.
