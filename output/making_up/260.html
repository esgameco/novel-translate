"So we've finally met!"

Shen Haizhou looked at Xu Yan and Meng Chong, sighing deeply in his heart.

These two sworn brothers were truly good at hiding. The Yushen Sect and the Shu family had searched all over Yuzhou, but they couldn't even find a trace of them. Instead, they discovered a hidden cultivator force.

And within this cultivator force, there were even Refining Spirit Celestials, not just one, shocking all the spiritual sects and noble families in Yuzhou.

After a round of attacks, this cultivator force seemed to have collapsed.

It was rumored that the leader of the cultivator force, a late-stage Refining Spirit cultivator, was severely injured by the Grand Elder of the Yushen Sect, Zhu Liang, and was left half-crippled, if not dead.

"Brother Shen, do you have any information on the spiritual ore mines of the Yushen Sect?" Xu Yan asked directly, without beating around the bush.

Shen Haizhou was delighted to hear this and took out a prepared map, spreading it out on the table. "This is the location of the Yushen Sect's spiritual ore mines. Among them, these two mines are the ones the Yushen Sect openly owns, but they are just small mines.

"These two are the core spiritual ore mines of the Yushen Sect, and there is a high possibility that the Spirit Soil Flowers are growing in the mines.

"This is the most crucial spiritual ore information I can currently provide. Whether there are even larger and more secretive mines, I'm not sure."

Shen Haizhou pointed out the marked mines on the map as he introduced them.

Xu Yan and Meng Chong's gazes fell on the two large mines. Their target must be the Yushen Sect's core large mines, especially since these two large mines were suspected to have Spirit Soil Flowers, a precious spiritual herb.

"Brother Xu, Brother Meng, the Yushen Sect's core large mines are guarded by Refining Spirit Celestials, so it's not possible to quickly empty the spiritual crystals. In my opinion, it's better to take these small mines first. Even that would be a significant loss for the Yushen Sect," Shen Haizhou said, swallowing hard.

Xu Yan and Meng Chong stared intently at the large mines, making Shen Haizhou feel uneasy.

If they really succeeded in seizing those large mines, the Yushen Sect would go crazy. They might even go so far as to seek outside help at any cost! Shen Haizhou was also worried that the information he provided might be leaked.

Although no one knew about his contact with Xu Yan and Meng Chong so far, and no one knew the information he had revealed, there was no such thing as an airtight wall in this world. He was afraid that if it was somehow leaked, he would be in trouble.

"The small mines are not interesting. Let's go for these two large mines," Xu Yan said excitedly.

Shen Haizhou swallowed again and said, "The mines are too large to empty completely. The person I'm looking for is nowhere to be found, and without his Swallow Mountain Toad, how can we quickly remove so many spiritual crystals? Unless... are you two after the Spirit Soil Flowers? But the existence of the Spirit Soil Flowers is just a rumor, not confirmed yet.

"Moreover, the Spirit Soil Flowers are deep within the mines. Once you enter, you may get trapped and be in grave danger if you can't get out quickly."

Xu Yan patted Shen Haizhou's shoulder and said, "These are just minor issues, Brother Shen. You don't need to worry. Your friend is no longer needed, as my sworn brother and I have a plan.

The Yushen Sect was renowned as the number one sect in Yuzhou, and their strength and foundation were truly terrifying. Even if the Yushen Sect was weak, they could still seek outside help. They had connections with powerful sects from other regions, and one could even say the Yushen Sect had a powerful backer.
