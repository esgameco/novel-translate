After saying this, he frowned again and said: "Why do you look so seriously injured?"

Yu Yao frowned and sighed: "I was careless and suffered a sneak attack. I've been recovering until now but haven't fully healed yet!"

Old Man Xiao became calm and composed.

He even looked forward to Tian Sha and the others being killed with one slap!

Boom!

In the Taihe Realm, a powerful aura emerged, and the majesty of the Realm Lord spread. Even across the vast distance, the Realm Lord's might still transmitted over.

This was intimidation, giving Old Man Xiao a warning.

"Xiao Yao, will you come meet me?"

The voice transmitted through the laws of heaven and earth.

Old Man Xiao directly ignored it and looked at Yu Yao, saying: "Your injuries haven't healed..."

At this moment, the entire Qinghua Sect was alerted, and those in cultivation walked out, including Wu Tiannan.

Wu Tiannan had just come out of his closed-door cultivation when he saw that graceful figure he had been thinking of day and night. He thought he was hallucinating and unconsciously rubbed his eyes, then his eyes turned red.

"Sister!"

He directly pounced over and hugged Yu Yao.

Old Man Xiao's mouth twitched. He couldn't bear to watch. Wu Tiannan, a grown man, was acting like a child right now.

Moreover, how long had Yu Yao lived?

And how old was Wu Tiannan?

Thinking of this, Old Man Xiao's gaze became a bit strange. He never thought Yu Yao would be this kind of person!

"Tiannan!"

Yu Yao also looked surprised, then her eyes showed a gentle expression.

"You've finally grown to this stage!"

Her tone was gentle and gratified.

Wu Tiannan was so excited he forgot himself. Holding Yu Yao's hand, he chattered on and on, recounting his experiences over the years and his feelings of longing.

"What a sour smell!"

Li Xuan sighed inwardly. He looked at Wu Tiannan, then at Yu Yao. "An older woman is worth gold bricks. How much older is this Yu Yao than Wu Tiannan?"

He didn't know how much older she was than Wu Tiannan, but Wu Tiannan had embarked on the path of martial arts because of her, and now he was already a Divine Venerable realm martial artist!

"Xiao Yao, leader of the Seven Heroes of Tai Cang, since when have you become so timid?"

Tian Sha's voice transmitted over.

Old Man Xiao continued to ignore it, but this voice finally interrupted Wu Tiannan's chatter. Now he realized that his sister's injuries hadn't healed! Wu Tiannan couldn't sit still anymore. Seeing Su Lingxiu, he hurriedly pleaded: "Miss Lingxiu, Tiannan requests that you treat my sister's injuries."

"Academy Head Wu, you're too kind!"

Su Lingxiu looked excited. The pills she had just refined could finally be put to use. She could test their effectiveness on this Realm Lord level powerhouse.

At this moment, Yu Yao realized that the people in this courtyard seemed a bit unusual, especially Xiao Yao's attitude. Something seemed off.
