The azure-colored flying boat shot through the hazy, cloudy sky without any hesitation, preparing to directly cross the Wuzhou and enter the Emerald Sea.

"This journey to the Emerald Sea, and then back to the Eighteen Realms, even if I don't attain the Divine Passage, I will at least reach the Perfected Spiritual Origin realm."

"Before the Genius Confrontation Battle begins, reaching the Perfected Spiritual Origin is a certainty. I hope I can accumulate enough foundation, so that after the Genius Confrontation, I can immediately break through to the Divine Passage realm."

Xu Yan pondered, once he enters the Divine Passage realm, not only the geniuses in the Spiritual Realm, but the entire Spiritual Realm, except for his master, would have no one who could match him. He could easily push aside the Transcendent Spiritual Sect.

"If I can accumulate enough foundation, and there's not much time left before the Genius Confrontation, then I'll break through after the Confrontation, a few more days won't make a difference."

Xu Yan muttered to himself.

Of course, whether he can accumulate enough foundation before the Genius Confrontation Battle arrives is still unknown. Even if he successfully obtains the divine treasure from the Emerald Sea, the accumulation of foundation still requires time to settle.

The Ice State, among the Eighteen Realms of the Spiritual Realm, is the one with the least human presence.

Covered in ice and snow, never melting for years, as far as the eye can see is a vast expanse of white, not even a trace of verdant plants and trees.

The occasional plants visible are mostly white, emanating a faint chill.

The Ice State is the place where the most potent icy and cold spiritual herbs are produced. If one were to encounter a non-white plant in the Ice State, it would undoubtedly be a divine-grade spiritual herb.

The Ice State has a spiritual sect, though not among the Transcendent Spiritual Sects, it is still one of the top-tier sects, rumored to have cultivated an extremely powerful expert who has grasped the Spiritual Mechanism of Heaven and Earth.

The Quiet Snow Palace, located deep within the Ice State, in the Quiet Snow Mountain Range, is the ruling spiritual sect of the Ice State.

The Quiet Snow Palace is one of the rare all-female spiritual sects in the Spiritual Realm, with not a single male warrior.

Furthermore, it is said that the women of the Quiet Snow Palace are all of unparalleled beauty, with icy skin and jade-like physiques, like enchanting flowers blooming amidst the ice and snow.

The bitter cold of the Ice State is such that even small Celestial Immortal realm cultivators are unwilling to reside there for long, which is why there are few cities or populated areas in the vast Ice State.

The disciples of the Quiet Snow Palace are said to be mostly selected from the other states, cultivating icy and cold techniques, which allows them to not fear the severe cold, and even thrive in such an environment.

The beauties of the Quiet Snow Palace have gained some fame in the Spiritual Realm, with rumors that spiritual sect geniuses have gone there to seek a bride, and occasionally one would gain the favor of a beauty.

There were once rumors of the peerless beauties of the Quiet Snow Palace, whose icy and alluring charm was most captivating even in the scorching heat, and that cultivators of fire-attributed techniques who married a Quiet Snow Palace beauty could experience the wondrous fusion of ice and fire.

However, after these rumors spread, the Quiet Snow Palace became greatly displeased, and since then, it has been rare for spiritual sect geniuses to be able to marry a Quiet Snow Palace beauty.

In the past, when the calamity of the Blood Demon swept through, the Ice State was not spared, experiencing a period of turmoil, with Blood Sect disciples coveting the beauties of the Quiet Snow Palace, leading to a series of major battles.

After the Blood Sect was annihilated and the Blood Demon retreated into the Inner Realm, the Ice State gradually faded from attention, and tales of the Quiet Snow Palace beauties have become scarce in the Spiritual Realm.

The Quiet Snow Palace has become like a forgotten place, as if it has entered a state of seclusion.

And at this moment, within the Quiet Snow Palace, most of the previous buildings have already disappeared.

Only one ice and snow palace remains, and around this palace, throughout the entire Quiet Snow Palace, are lush and vibrant divine-grade spiritual herbs.

Deep within the ice and snow palace, there is a black, vortex-like cave, which appears to be on the ground, but gives a sense of being a rift in the heavens and earth, rather than an earthly cave.

There is a mysterious aura emanating from this cave, and it seems to be gradually expanding, with a warm and moist feeling within.

Outside the ice and snow palace, a man in thin clothing, shivering in the cold wind, is swallowing a divine-grade spiritual herb.

This spiritual herb is blood-red, possessing the divine effect of greatly replenishing one's spiritual blood.

"This is the ninth one, I've recovered again, I should be able to hold on now."

The man is exceptionally handsome and graceful, but in the depths of his eyes, there is a hint of regret and fear.

He looks up at the Quiet Snow Palace, and in the sparse buildings, he can vaguely see the graceful figures of beauties, each with their own unique charm - some with imposing bearing, some delicate and pure, some gentle, some shy...

He has slept with all of them.

For over ten years.

Ever since he accidentally learned of a certain rumor and received a message that led him to the Quiet Snow Palace, he has never had a moment's peace, needing to heavily replenish himself every so often.

At first, he was greatly excited, wandering amidst the flowers and savoring the different beauties.

But later, it turned to terror.

And now, even a sense of numbness has set in.

Because all the beauties in the Quiet Snow Palace are the same person!

Their spiritual souls all belong to the same person! He even suspects that their physical bodies may also be the same person.

Of course, this is just a suspicion, the possibility is not very high, but the fact that their spiritual souls belong to the same person is 100% certain.

These beauties may have once been the disciples, elders, and others of the Quiet Snow Palace, but their spiritual souls have long been devoured, their physical bodies now inhabited by this single spiritual soul.

What terrifies him even more is that this spiritual soul is extremely unique, vastly different from the spiritual souls cultivated by the warriors of the Spiritual Realm.

These beauties, not only do they have different appearances, but even their personalities and temperaments vary greatly, and he has to deal with a different type of beauty every day, exhausting all his skills and sweet-talking to barely manage.

Just as he was about to catch his breath and find an opportunity to escape, the mastermind behind it all appeared.

Three years ago! Three years ago, he saw the breathtakingly beautiful, alluring, and soul-devouring mastermind.

Since then, he has been trapped in endless torment.

Raising his hand to touch his cheek, even after consuming the divine-grade spiritual herb, he still cannot fully recover, his cheeks slightly sunken.

"If this continues, I'll be drained to death!"

The man muttered to himself.

Although the experience is extremely pleasurable, and dying in such bliss could be considered the most wonderful way to go, he does not want to die!

The image of the gentle and jade-like woman flashes in his mind, the one he found most unforgettable among all the women he has encountered.

"When I left, you seemed to be pregnant, I wanted to stay with you until the child was born, and take you back home...but this unexpected incident occurred, and I had to return earlier.

"This one departure has trapped me here, perhaps you think I deceived you? But I truly did not lie to you, although I have had many lovers, you are still the one I love the most!"

The man murmured to himself.

Just then, a bewitchingly charming and seductive call came from the ice and snow palace, "Mu Mu, I want you, come quickly!"

Damn it! The man cursed inwardly, though it's not really a curse, as he knows he'll have to respond soon.
