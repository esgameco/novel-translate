Xu Yan spoke calmly, wanting to open the eyes of these frog-in-the-well cultivators.

This combination attack technique indeed had its unique merits.

It was as if five people were wielding five different sword arts.

In this way, it became Xu Yan alone, surrounding the five of them, rather than them surrounding Xu Yan.

"Could it be that Xu Yan's master is the legendary Celestial Venerable? No, even the legendary Celestial Venerable doesn't seem to have such wondrous techniques," Yaru pondered.

The battle continued, the profundity of the sword formation constantly unfolding. The five Qianwu Palace geniuses could no longer coordinate their attacks.

Moreover, they were hard-pressed to defend, constantly facing the risk of losing their lives.

It was clear that Xu Yan was not using his full strength to instantly slay the five, but rather using them to demonstrate the essence of the sword formation to the outside world.

The Qianwu Palace Elders had grim expressions.

The combination attack technique, under this sword formation, was so utterly weak.

"Have Wu Yuanzhun prepare to act," the Qianwu Palace Master said in a deep voice.

This battle had no surprises left.

Those five were doomed to lose.

Even at this moment, he had doubts in his heart. Even if the Celestial Artifact Cold Absolute Knife wielded by Wu Yuanzhun was used, could it truly heavily injure or even kill Xu Yan? Wu Yuanzhun, after all, was not an apex expert.

"Palace Master, with Wu Yuanzhun wielding a Celestial Artifact, can Xu Yan really withstand it?" the Saber Elder asked, disbelief written on his face.

"Has Xu Yan truly used his full strength?" the Qianwu Palace Master asked in a deep voice.

The Saber Elder was taken aback. That's right, Xu Yan had never truly used his full strength, always appearing to be at ease.

On the battlefield, Xu Yan was using his divine elemental incarnations to unleash the sword formation. At this moment, he had gained some new insights.

He was currently using the Qianwu Palace geniuses to validate his own realizations.
