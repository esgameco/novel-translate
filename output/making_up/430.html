Li Xuan was about to head to Wu when he suddenly became surprised.

With his increased power and insight into the Cangqiong Realm, he immediately noticed something odd through his martial arts connection with Wu Tiannan.

Wu Tiannan's body contained the destiny of the Cangqiong Realm, which allowed him to turn danger into safety and obtain opportunities even when facing powerful enemies.

"What's going on with this realm's destiny?"

"Should I go take a look?"

"Forget it, it's not a bad thing for Wu Tiannan after all."

Li Xuan glanced at the principles related to the realm's destiny, lost in thought.

"This is interesting. It seems the person Wu Tiannan saved back then was quite extraordinary, truly someone with great destiny."

"Being able to meet me and receive my guidance, he can be considered part of my sect. He really does have great fortune."

Thinking back on Wu Tiannan's experiences, Li Xuan couldn't help but smile.

After entering the Divine Realm, as Wu Tiannan's strength broke through, he began to touch upon the realm's destiny, fully awakening the destiny within him.

As a result, his cultivation speed increased rapidly, and he was even able to cultivate divine abilities.

No wonder Wu Tiannan was the first among the Cangqiong martial artists to cultivate divine abilities.

"He's not far from the Immortal Realm."

Li Xuan had an odd expression. If his guess was correct, after Wu Tiannan broke through to the Immortal Realm, he might even be able to break through beyond the Immortal Realm in the Divine Realm under the guidance of destiny.

"Is Wu Tiannan's martial arts guide there?"

Looking up at the sky, with his insight into the Cangqiong Realm, judging from Wu Tiannan's destiny, it seemed to have some karmic connection to that place.

"I'll give Wu Tiannan some more guidance when I have time. I should also go back to Wu to take a look. I'm curious about who that person in the stone house is."

Li Xuan felt excited inside.

He was about to uncover a mystery and satisfy his curiosity.

"Let Xu Yan consolidate for a while before passing on to him martial arts methods beyond the Realm Boundary."

A figure walked out of Li Xuan's body and sat down in a chair, relaxed and carefree.

This was his Extreme Soul Body.

Although there shouldn't be any accidents at the Qinghua Realm, Li Xuan still left behind his Extreme Soul Body just in case. If any special situations really did occur, it could suppress them in time.

Putting away the Cangqiong Book, Li Xuan vanished from the spot in an instant.

The Extreme Soul Body remained sitting in the chair as usual, relaxed and carefree. No one could tell it was another martial arts version of Li Xuan.

In the Great Wilderness, as spiritual energy increased, cultivation speed became faster and faster. The grade of spiritual herbs also improved. After years of development, the overall strength had long surpassed the former Inner Regions.

Moreover, almost everyone in the Great Wilderness now practiced martial arts.

The martial arts classics of the former Inner Regions had not been destroyed, but became a part of history, stored in the Great Wilderness Martial Arts Academy and some former martial arts forces.

At Sword Veneration Cliff, after Xie Tianheng and Xie Lingfeng left, the current masters of Sword Veneration Cliff were the father and son Hu Hai and Hu Shan. Both had cultivated sword intent and could be considered the number one swordsmen in the Great Wilderness.

Canglan Island, the holy land of the Great Wilderness, was surrounded by great formations. Every year, prodigies recommended by the Great Wilderness Martial Arts Academy would come here to cultivate for a month and learn more profound martial arts.

The alchemists of Changqing Pavilion held respected positions in the Great Wilderness. Artifact refiners had also begun to appear, all left behind by Xu Yan back then.

The entire Great Wilderness was developing vigorously, but conflicts were naturally unavoidable. However, everything was under control.

The borderlands remained special. Although spiritual energy was not scarce, it was violent, without the existence of natural energy.

But occasionally martial artists from the Great Wilderness would come here to cultivate and experience the violent spiritual energy.

Cangqiong martial arts could not be cultivated without natural energy, but Great Wilderness martial arts did not require it.

In the northern barbarian lands of the borderlands, a great leader had emerged, leading a large army to invade the former states of Qi and Wu, intending to unify the borderlands.

The northern barbarians, living in the harsh borderlands, did not yet know about the changes in the borderlands or the emergence of martial arts. After a period of intense internal turmoil, they reunified, and the Northern Barbarian Court produced its strongest leader in a thousand years.

Having accumulated sufficient strength, they finally embarked on the path of conquest, intending to unify the world.

When the Qi Emperor and Wu Emperor learned of this, they were excited. With just the two of them, they swept through the northern barbarian army, capturing the barbarian leader alive.

The hearts of all the northern barbarians were devastated. They had originally thought they could destroy Qi and Wu in one fell swoop and occupy the fertile lands, but they were swept aside by just two people!

The power they displayed was like that of gods! Even the true gods the barbarians worshipped and offered sacrifices to seemed like ants in comparison!

The northern barbarian leader was dumbfounded. Anyone with slightly weaker willpower would have been driven mad by the shock! After this incident, the northern barbarians changed. They began to submit to the two emperors of the Great Wilderness and become citizens of the Great Wilderness. The barbarian leader was even more decisive, kneeling down to become a disciple.

"The northern barbarians have strong physiques and can endure hardship. They are suitable for cultivating the Great Wilderness physical martial arts!"

And so, some talented northern barbarian prodigies were taught the Great Sun Golden Bell Technique and began to cultivate Great Wilderness martial arts.

But the vast palace was cold and quiet most of the time, only becoming lively during the days when Emperor Wu returned.

Usually, it was some former eunuchs who cleaned the palace.

The eunuchs who remained were basically those without much martial arts talent, unable to pursue the martial path, and could only spend the rest of their lives here.

Of course, these eunuchs actually did practice some martial arts, but their abilities were very poor. However, it was enough to give them sufficient physical strength to clean the palace and do some odd jobs.

When Emperor Wu returned, he would reward them with some medicinal pills.

The Great Wilderness had already abolished the eunuch system. The eunuchs serving Emperor Wu and Emperor Qi were all from the initial batch, with no new eunuchs.

Deep in the palace, there was a palace building that only Emperor Wu could enter.

Li Xuan took out a chair and sat down leisurely.
