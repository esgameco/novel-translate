Chapter 73 - Fierce Battle, Breakthrough of the Descending Dragon Palm (Seeking Monthly Votes)

Although Xu Yan's strength was not weak, and his Descending Dragon Palm technique was unparalleled in its ferocity, in terms of combat experience, he was naturally far inferior to Xue Wuxin.

At the moment when Xu Yan's Descending Dragon Palm struck out and a sudden opening appeared above his head, Xu Yan's heart jolted as he realized the problem.

However, this fleeting opportunity, for the battle-hardened Xue Wuxin who had experienced life-and-death battles, was enough to determine the outcome of life and death.

His figure soared through the air, and his long saber gleamed with a chilling light as it descended from midair.

In that instant, Xu Yan only felt his scalp tingle, his hairs stand on end, and a strong sense of crisis suddenly descended upon him. At this moment, it was too late for him to try to block the attack with a palm.

Involuntarily, an octagonal diagram flashed in his mind, and in that critical moment of crisis, he shifted his footsteps, his figure wavering and disappearing from the spot.

The next moment, he reappeared a dozen feet away.

Boom!

A deep gash appeared on the ground, where Xue Wuxin's saber had ruthlessly struck.

He was deeply shaken!

At such a critical moment, the opponent had actually evaded, and what kind of technique was that?

Xu Yan's heart was still pounding violently, his qi and blood boiling, and a huge dragon coiled around him, its aura growing increasingly powerful.

Just now, he had almost died!

If it hadn't been for the octagonal diagram that flashed in his mind in that crisis, giving him a sudden insight and allowing him to comprehend a new technique, that strike would have been impossible to evade.

At this moment, he was filled with shock and fury.

His figure moved, disappearing from the spot, and in an instant, he appeared beside Xue Wuxin, the Descending Dragon Palm striking out with a thunderous force.

Spatial Shift!

Boom!

Xue Wuxin's gaze was cold and sharp, his saber technique unfolding, the chilling sword qi criss-crossing and constantly pressing forward with overwhelming strength, continuously dissipating one Descending Dragon Palm after another.

Forced to constantly use the Spatial Shift technique to dodge and change positions, a few strands of blood-sucking vampire threads began to spread out from Xue Wuxin's body, covering the surroundings like a giant net.

Xu Yan's gaze grew serious; the opponent's strength was slightly superior to his own. Especially that ruthless swordplay and the eerie, icy aura, both were extremely bizarre.

If it weren't for his vast and blazing qi and blood that could ignore the invasion of the icy aura, he might have already become stiff-limbed with a greatly reduced combat ability.

Xu Yan frowned deeply, the battle growing increasingly fierce, his mind becoming clearer, with no more distractions, fully immersed in the fight.

The octagonal diagram appeared in his mind, and with a shift of his footsteps, he changed positions.

The Spatial Shift technique became more and more skilled with the progression of the battle.

Boom!

The mighty Descending Dragon Palm was once again shattered by one of Xue Wuxin's sword strikes.

The sharp sword light came slashing, and Xu Yan shifted his position again to evade.

At this moment, the incantation of the Descending Dragon Palm surfaced in his mind.

His qi and blood began to boil even more, his entire being seemingly in a state of excitement. Suddenly, he let out a roar, his two palms pushing out in an interchange, unleashing a massive Descending Dragon Palm force that split into eighteen giant dragons mid-trajectory, instantly enveloping Xue Wuxin.

The Descending Dragon Palm had reached its pinnacle!

Xue Wuxin grew increasingly alarmed in the battle, the opponent's realm should not be higher than his own, and his strength should not surpass his either.

Yet, that tyrannical palm force, with its astonishing might, even though he tried to cut it down, he found it somewhat difficult.

Moreover, the transformation of the dragon-shaped palm forces kept forming converging attacks against him.

"This kid is a bit troublesome, actually not fearing my blood demon qi at all, and even able to counter my blood demon qi!"

That blazing qi and blood kept dissipating his icy aura.

The more it was so, the stronger his desire grew. As long as he could devour the opponent, his strength would advance further.

He had never seen a warrior with such powerful qi and blood. That refined essence blood contained an immense spiritual aura.

Among the inner realm cultivators, no one could have such pure essence blood.

Even Grandmasters did not possess it!

The eighteen giant dragons converged in an attack, but Xue Wuxin's sword light surged, sweeping away the Descending Dragon Palm force in an instant, his speed reaching the pinnacle, ruthlessly striking towards Xu Yan.

Slash after slash, with his fierce attacks, Xu Yan once again fell into a disadvantageous position.

And the Spatial Shift technique was used more and more frequently, constantly evading, but he was not the least bit flustered, his gaze firmly fixed on Xue Wuxin.

His jade-like physique was shining brilliantly, his qi and blood seemingly endless.

"...Its majesty shakes the world, its wrath startles heaven and earth."

Suddenly, the incantation of the Descending Dragon Palm surfaced in his mind, and in the midst of the battle, he gained a sudden enlightenment.

"My Descending Dragon Palm, although its might is astonishing and seemingly tyrannical, it lacks the true 'majesty' and cannot startle heaven and earth.

'Its majesty shakes the world, its wrath startles heaven and earth.' Without wrath, how can it startle heaven and earth? My Descending Dragon Palm lacks 'majesty,' lacks 'wrath,' and wrath is what can startle heaven and earth?"

Xu Yan's mind became clear, the realization dawning on him, and in an instant, he seemed to have broken through a bottleneck in his understanding of the Descending Dragon Palm.

Abruptly, he halted his movements, and then thrust out a palm.

This palm strike was vastly different from the previous ones.

The moment the palm force was unleashed, it seemed to emit a whistling sound, a sense of chilling majesty radiating outwards.

This dragon-shaped palm force was more substantial, possessing an aura of divinity.

The speed had also increased explosively in that instant.

Roar!

The giant dragon opened its mouth, seemingly letting out a roar, and ruthlessly charged towards Xue Wuxin.

"Slash!"

Xue Wuxin's heart trembled, and he struck out with a sword, but this sword strike did not shatter the palm force. Instead, it carried a vast, surging might, like a towering mountain crashing down, the power undiminished as the dragon's tail swept across.

"Not good!"

Xue Wuxin's expression changed slightly, and he leapt up to evade the attack.

Boom!

Another giant dragon came rumbling in.

One dragon after another kept roaring in, each exuding a chilling majesty, and Xue Wuxin's heart tightened as he stared at Xu Yan in shock.

"Has he broken through?"

What a demonic prodigy!

Xu Yan exhaled the foul air from his chest, his eyes gleaming with a sharp light. He had comprehended it!

The Descending Dragon Palm had broken through its bottleneck, stepping into a new realm.

"Its wrath startles heaven and earth, I still cannot make the dragon-shaped palm forces truly 'wrathful,' truly possess the aura of 'wrath,' but I can manifest it in another way!"

Xu Yan looked at the Xue Wuxin who was being pressed in the converging Descending Dragon Palm forces, and now it was his turn.

Let this unknown warrior, who seemed to be at the Innate Realm, witness the true power of the Descending Dragon Palm.

"My current Descending Dragon Palm should be at the Second Realm, right? I have broken through the Descending Dragon Palm realm and entered the Second Realm!

"Unfortunately, I'm only at the Qi and Blood Realm, unable to fully unleash the power of the Second Realm.

"But it should be enough to deal with this guy!"

Xu Yan's mind was enlightened, realizing that he had cultivated the Descending Dragon Palm to the Second Realm.

Boom!

The eighteen giant dragons converged on Xue Wuxin, and just as Xue Wuxin was about to unleash his fierce sword light to try to cut down the palm force, his heart suddenly jumped.

Danger!

A strong sense of crisis instantly surfaced.

The battle experience forged in life-and-death situations made him decisively channel his qi and soar into the air, his sword light sweeping upwards.
