Suling Xiu headed towards the mountain peak.

"Huh, where's the eldest brother?"

She looked around in confusion, as the eldest brother usually meditated on the Dao of Martial Arts here.

But he was not there. As she passed by Xu Yan, her gaze swept over him, but she unconsciously dismissed him as just a rock.

She went back and forth on the mountain peak a few times, passing by Xu Yan twice, but still did not notice him.

"Master, master, the eldest brother has left."

Suling Xiu hurriedly ran to Li Xuan and said.

With a worried expression, "The medicinal pills I prepared, the eldest brother didn't take them with him. Why did he leave without telling us? Master, do you know the eldest brother has left?"

She unconsciously thought that Xu Yan had entered the Spirit Realm.

The medicinal pills she had specially refined for entering the Spirit Realm had not even been given to the eldest brother yet, making her somewhat dejected.

Li Xuan chuckled and said, "Your eldest brother is still on the mountain."

"No, he's not there, Master. I just went to look for him."

Suling Xiu shook her head.

"You all search carefully, go look for him again."

Li Xuan smiled.

"Okay, I'll go look for him again."

Suling Xiu blinked her eyes and ran back to the mountain peak.

This time, she searched meticulously, not missing a single rock or tree, but still could not find the eldest brother's figure.

"Master, I've looked at every rock and tree, but I still can't see the eldest brother."

Suling Xiu ran back and reported.

You've been mistaking him for a rock and ignoring him, how could you possibly find him?

Li Xuan couldn't help but sigh inwardly, the Merge with the Environment technique that Xu Yan had comprehended was truly extraordinary.

Suling Xiu's cultivation at the Penetrating Realm was far inferior to Xu Yan's, there was no way she could possibly detect Xu Yan's presence.

"How could your master be deceiving you? Go look for him again carefully."

Li Xuan smiled and said.

Suling Xiu pondered.

She ran to Meng Chong and said, "Second brother, the master said the eldest brother is on the mountain, but I've searched everywhere and can't find him. Come help me look."

Meng Chong paused, then stood up and said, "Okay!"

The two of them went to the mountain peak, and Suling Xiu looked around, asking, "Second brother, do you see the eldest brother?"

Meng Chong pondered and said, "No."

After circling the mountain peak, he asked, "Are you sure the master said the eldest brother is on the mountain?"

"The master said so."

Suling Xiu nodded glumly.

Meng Chong stood in place, carefully observing every blade of grass and tree on the mountain peak, his Dao of the Sword permeating the entire area, not missing a single detail.

Yet, he still could not find him.

He took a deep breath, greatly shocked. Had the eldest brother really comprehended this concealment technique?

This method of hiding one's presence was truly extraordinary. If one could reach this level, where even Heaven and Earth did not know of one's existence, it would not be strange for Meng Chong to be unable to detect him, as the eldest brother's cultivation was superior to his own.

Li Xuan had been observing the mountain peak the entire time. Meng Chong's method of using the Dao of the Sword to investigate the mountain was correct, but he had encountered Xu Yan, whose cultivation was higher than his, and whose Sword Dao surpassed his Dao of the Sword.

Xu Yan's third layer of Sword Dao had merged with the environment, replacing the surroundings with his Sword Dao. Meng Chong's Dao of the Sword was weaker than Xu Yan's Sword Dao, so naturally he could not detect the subtle changes.

Moreover, at this moment, both Meng Chong and Suling Xiu had already stepped into Xu Yan's Sword Dao. Even though Meng Chong vaguely felt something was off, he had not discovered the hidden danger.

"If this was an ambush or sneak attack, unless one is much stronger, it would be impossible to avoid it."

Li Xuan sighed inwardly. If Xu Yan wanted to launch a sneak attack, even if his cultivation was higher than Li Xuan's, he would still be unable to evade it.

On the mountain peak, Xu Yan withdrew his Sword Dao and revealed his presence.

"I'm here!"

Suling Xiu's eyes widened as she looked at him in astonishment, "Eldest brother, have you been here the entire time?"

"Yes."

Xu Yan nodded with a smile.

"I couldn't detect you at all."

Suling Xiu was shocked. She had come and gone several times, and searched thoroughly, but had always overlooked him.

"Eldest brother, have you comprehended it?"

Meng Chong asked excitedly.

Xu Yan shook his head, then nodded, "I've comprehended some of it, but have not yet fully grasped its true essence."

"Eldest brother, please teach me."

Suling Xiu said excitedly.

"Okay!"

Xu Yan passed on the Merge with the Environment technique to Suling Xiu and Meng Chong.

"It's so profound, no wonder the master said the breath suppression method he taught me was very ordinary."

Suling Xiu sighed in amazement.

She would need some time to comprehend the Merge with the Environment technique.

Since Xu Yan had already comprehended the Merge with the Environment technique, it was time for him to go to the Spirit Realm.

A few days later.

Xu Yan was preparing to go to the Spirit Realm, and Li Xuan's group was going to see him off to the Qianbei region, where the entrance to the Spirit Realm was.

Just then, a figure came rushing in.

It was the long-absent Meng Shu Shu.

"My cousin, it's been so long, you haven't been hiding from your debts, have you?"

Meng Chong looked at the travel-worn Meng Shu Shu and asked in surprise.

"Of course not, I discovered a treasure trove and got caught up there for a while."

Meng Shu Shu looked at the group, greatly shocked.

He had just come out of the treasure trove and heard about the series of changes in the Inner Realm, the magnitude of which gave him a sense of the world being turned upside down.

The powerful Great Yue Kingdom had disappeared, replaced by the Great Wasteland.

"Treasure trove?"

Meng Chong's eyes lit up as he put his arm around Meng Shu Shu's shoulder and asked cheerfully, "My cousin, did you gain a lot?"

He no longer cared much about the storage bag Meng Shu Shu owed him, since he already had one and was not short on money.

"A lot!"

Meng Shu Shu nodded excitedly, taking out five ancient-looking storage bags from his clothes.

"Look, these storage bags have larger spatial dimensions and can store more things than regular ones."

Meng Shu Shu grinned excitedly.

This trip had yielded a huge harvest, as he had stumbled upon an ancient relic.

"This one is for you, and this one is for Xu Shixiong!"

Meng Shu Shu handed one storage bag each to Meng Chong and Xu Yan.

He then stuffed one of the remaining three storage bags into Suling Xiu's hand, saying, "Inside are my harvest from this trip, the lowest grade is sixth-grade spirit herbs, and there's even one that seems to be a fourth-grade spirit herb!"

Xu Yan and the others were all surprised. What kind of treasure trove had Meng Shu Shu discovered to gain such a massive harvest, including a fourth-grade spirit herb that had never appeared in the Inner Realm before?

Meng Shu Shu then said mysteriously and excitedly, "Do you know, the biggest harvest I got this time was..."
