After leaving the spiritual medicine treasure land, due to concerns about the powerful experts from the black-robed person's faction, the three of them tried to move through the mountains and forests, hiding their tracks.

Arriving outside a city, Xu Yan and Meng Chong waited outside the city, while Meng Shushu disguised himself and went into the city to the Tianbao Pavilion to purchase spiritual medicine sealing talismans.

After Meng Shushu bought the sealing talismans and finished sealing the spiritual medicines, they set out to return to the territory of the Great Yue Kingdom.

Halfway there, a flying eagle descended.

It was the flying eagle from Sword Saint Cliff, following the flying eagle token on Xu Yan, carrying a letter from Xie Lingfeng inquiring if they needed any assistance.

Xu Yan wrote a reply letter and had the eagle take it back.

"This is the flying eagle from Sword Saint Cliff?" Meng Shushu exclaimed in surprise.

"Ah, you recognize it?" Xu Yan asked, puzzled.

"Elder Brother Xu, as a spiritual medicine explorer, I naturally need to be familiar with and understand the major powers. We would sometimes use their flying eagles for communication when cooperating, so I can naturally recognize them," Meng Shushu explained.

"I see!" Xu Yan understood.

"By the way, can you cultivate and organize a flying eagle communication network?" Xu Yan asked thoughtfully.

Since they needed to enter the inner domain, a convenient communication method like flying eagle transmission was indispensable. Relying on others' flying eagles was not a reliable long-term solution.

"I'm just a spiritual medicine explorer, how would I know about that?" Meng Shushu hurriedly shook his head.

"The Tianbao Pavilion can rent out flying eagle communication services. Many powers that can't build their own flying eagle network just rent from the Tianbao Pavilion, although the prices are quite high. Of course, you can also rent it for single-use temporary usage, and so far there haven't been any incidents of information leaks from the Tianbao Pavilion's flying eagle communication," Meng Shushu explained after thinking it over.

"The Tianbao Pavilion really is extraordinary, they have all kinds of businesses," Meng Chong sighed in amazement.

Xu Yan took out the jade token given by the woman in white clothes and asked, "Is this what you mean by renting the Tianbao Pavilion's flying eagle communication?"

"Yes, this jade token can only be used to obtain information from the Tianbao Pavilion, not to receive flying eagle transmissions. It's one of the cheaper options. Most powers use this, mainly to obtain news from the martial dao world, and it still costs over 100,000 spirit crystals per year," Meng Shushu nodded.

Xu Yan was secretly astonished. The Tianbao Pavilion was really formidable, just to obtain information would cost over 100,000 spirit crystals per year. How many powers were there in the inner domain? Even just within the Great Yue Kingdom, the number of powers was staggering, not to mention the vast regions outside the three major kingdoms.

It was clear that the Tianbao Pavilion's flying eagle information network was entirely funded by the various powers, without needing to invest their own resources.

After entering the Great Yue Kingdom, Xu Yan felt slightly relieved, as there were no peak Dao Ancestors chasing after them. It wasn't that he feared the Dao Ancestors, but he was worried that if a battle broke out, the heavenly drop marrow and several sixth-grade spiritual medicines could be damaged, which would be impossible to make up for, as fifth-grade spiritual medicines were unobtainable.

"Junior brother, for your safety, I will send you to Blue Peace Prefecture," Xu Yan said thoughtfully.

"Okay!" Meng Chong nodded.

He wasn't afraid of Dao Ancestor experts, as he could easily kill them with a single sword strike without damaging the spiritual medicines. But if half-step Dao Ancestors or even Dao Ancestors came, the situation would become dire.

The three of them headed straight for Blue Peace Prefecture. Blue Peace Prefecture was a border prefecture, extremely far from their current location. To reach Blue Peace Prefecture or Iron Mountain County would take a significant amount of time.

And to avoid any accidents, Xu Yan tried to remain as low-key as possible. Killing Dao Ancestors was not urgent, the spiritual medicines were the most important.

"Brother Meng, Elder Brother Xu, I'll hide in Blue Peace Prefecture for a while until the storm blows over. Then I'll continue searching for spiritual medicines. How can I contact you two in the future?" Meng Shushu asked as they were about to enter the borders of Blue Peace Prefecture.

Xu Yan pondered for a moment and said, "In the future, you can go to Iron Mountain County and contact my people, or you can contact Xie Lingfeng and have him relay the message."

Iron Mountain County was a border area, and after bringing the spiritual medicines back to the border region, the number of border cultivators would surely increase, and entering the inner domain was inevitable. Iron Mountain County would be the first stop for these border cultivators.

"I understand!" Meng Shushu nodded.

"My family member, work hard and try to earn a few storage pouches as soon as possible!" Meng Chong encouraged, patting Meng Shushu's shoulder.

"I'll try my best," Meng Shushu said, on the verge of tears. How many spiritual medicines did he need to collect to afford a storage pouch?

Just as they entered the borders of Blue Peace Prefecture, Xu Yan suddenly stopped in his tracks, extending his arm to block Meng Chong and Meng Shushu. His gaze was grave as he looked ahead.

A figure was approaching, step by step, and wherever it passed, the ground seemed to be scorched black, as if burned by fire.

It was a person wearing a scarlet red robe, with a hooded head covering their face, which was deathly pale. In their hand was a skeletal staff, the top of which was a skull with red flames flickering in the eye sockets and mouth.

A Dao Ancestor expert! Xu Yan took out the spiritual medicines from his body and handed them to Meng Chong, saying, "Junior brother, be careful."

"Elder brother, don't worry," Meng Chong replied solemnly, his hand resting on the hilt of his sword, vigilantly scanning the surroundings.

Meng Shushu stared wide-eyed at the Dao Ancestor, his scalp tingling and his body trembling slightly.

"My family member, your courage is too small!" Meng Chong sighed.

"He... he is the Demonic Butcher Sovereign!" Meng Shushu wailed.

Xu Yan and Meng Chong were both taken aback. The Demonic Butcher Sovereign?

One of the nine Demonic Sovereigns of the Demon Sect, known as one of the three most infamous Demonic Sovereigns. He was extremely brutal, cultivating a killing technique, and upon breaking through to the Dao Ancestor realm, he had slaughtered hundreds of Origin Realm cultivators and dozens of Dao Realm experts, refining their essence blood to reach the Dao Ancestor realm.

He was the most brutal of the nine Demonic Sovereigns, and his whereabouts were hidden, precisely to avoid being hunted down by other Dao Ancestors.

"No, the Demonic Butcher Sovereign couldn't be this weak," Xu Yan suddenly shook his head.

"Weak?" Meng Shushu was taken aback, swallowing hard. "Elder Brother Xu, the Demonic Butcher Sovereign is a peak Dao Ancestor, ranking among the top thirty in the inner domain."

"Then you must have mistaken him. He is only a mid-Dao Realm expert, not a peak Dao Ancestor. Although his strength is greater than the black-robed one I killed, he is still far from a peak Dao Ancestor," Xu Yan shook his head.

"So it's not the Demonic Butcher Sovereign, but one of his Dao Ancestor subordinates," Meng Shushu sighed in relief.

The Demonic Dao Ancestor slowly approached, his aura clearly locking onto Xu Yan, but he was not hurrying, showing no fear of the three of them trying to escape.
