"Success!"

Fang Hao wiped the sweat from his brow, looking at the circular disc in his hand with a smile.

"This money is really hard to earn!"

Fang Hao inserted the eight small auxiliary discs one by one into the main disc.

"I wonder what miraculous uses this thing has? That mysterious pattern seems to be one of disorientation and confusion?"

After pondering for a moment, Fang Hao was eager to try it out.

But he was also worried that the treasure he had forged might have uncontrollable or unknown dangers.

He looked towards the well in the courtyard.

"Little Ha, come out!"

A gray toad jumped out of the well and squatted on the ground, looking at him.

"Croak!"

Its lively eyes stared at Fang Hao, opening its mouth and croaking softly.

"Wait, I'm going to try this treasure. If anything uncontrollable happens, you swallow this auxiliary disc, understand?"

Fang Hao took out one of the auxiliary discs and instructed Little Ha.

If this auxiliary disc was swallowed, the entire treasure set would lose its balance and have a flaw.

"Croak!"

Little Ha nodded.

Fang Hao took a deep breath, his expression solemn. He felt that he might have forged something extraordinary.

He placed the auxiliary disc in front of Little Ha.

Holding the main disc, he input power into it, and with a hum, the seven smaller auxiliary discs inside the main disc suddenly flew out and landed in seven different places.

Hum! In an instant, the courtyard seemed to disappear, yet also not disappear.

The houses seemed to have changed positions, and even the well appeared to have moved.

The familiar courtyard now seemed disoriented, to the point that Fang Hao himself couldn't tell where the houses or the well were.

Looking at the spot where Little Ha had been squatting, he saw the forging furnace.

"That's not right, how is the forging furnace there?"

Fang Hao was greatly shocked. What kind of treasure was this? Why did it have such an effect? He tried to take a few steps forward, but found himself lost in the courtyard, unable to find his direction.

Lowering his gaze to the main disc, he took a deep breath, and a formation diagram appeared in his mind.

"This is controllable, the main disc is in my hand, I should be able to control it."

"Little Ha, swallow the disc."

Fang Hao gave the order.

However, he did not receive a response.

Fang Hao's heart sank, could something have happened to Little Ha?

"No, this disc shouldn't have any attack power, it only has the ability to disorient and confuse. Perhaps Little Ha simply can't hear my voice."

Taking a deep breath, Fang Hao stepped out, but suddenly stopped.

In front of him was a well, and a toad squatting on the ground, its lively eyes staring at the disc on the ground, waiting obediently.

"Little Ha?"

Fang Hao called out softly, but received no response.

He gently kicked the toad with his foot, but it was not soft, but hard.

"Is it a stone or a discarded treasure?"

Fang Hao was greatly shocked.

He shook his head and stared wide-eyed, and what he saw was clearly Little Ha.

"Why is this happening?"

This treasure was extraordinary! Fang Hao's heart raced as he remembered Li Xuan's words - if he could forge it, it would be an opportunity to change his fate. "Change my fate!"

Fang Hao took a deep breath, gently closing his eyes, and the formation diagram appeared in his mind.

The main disc was in his hand, but even though he stopped inputting power, the strange scene had not disappeared. He was searching for how to stop the power of the treasure.

The formation diagram flashed through Fang Hao's mind, each formation line appearing one by one.

"Here it is!"

Suddenly, Fang Hao opened his eyes, his expression excited.
