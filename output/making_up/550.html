Xu Yan chuckled and said, "Chopping off your head, you old turtle, shouldn't be too much of a problem!"

Canghai Turtle was shocked.

Canghai Turtle sighed inwardly. Meng Chong and the other two would likely surpass him before long as well.

He thought about how he had lived for such an incredibly long time, yet these people could casually surpass him after just a few decades or a hundred years of cultivation. It made him feel utterly useless. Even that streak of purple light from the world's creation wasn't much help - it had only given him a tiny bit of enlightenment!

Of course, Canghai Turtle knew that Xu Yan and the other three were able to increase their strength so rapidly and become such prodigies all thanks to the powerful martial arts passed down by their master and his excellent guidance.

Canghai Turtle and Meng Chong's group also revealed themselves, heading straight for the other two Immortal Temple Realm Lords. Canghai Turtle manifested his enormous body, with blue light surging like giant waves.

The Sword of Death! This was what Xu Yan had comprehended - the meaning and power representing death in the world.

He used all his secret techniques in that moment, even burning his own essence to create a powerful protective barrier.

As the gray-white sword approached, he sensed the intent of death.
