"Good, very good. It really was the Song family. Not content with killing my disciple, they even dared to plot against my son. The Heaven-Destroying Earth-Shaking Sect, is it? How wonderful!" Yan Shanhe's expression was extremely cold.

Yan Shanhe was filled with rage at this moment.

Originally, he hadn't paid much attention to the schemes of the Heaven-Destroying Earth-Shaking Sect, since there was a tacit understanding between the Myriad Treasures Alliance and the Heaven-Destroying Earth-Shaking Sect for the time being.

The Heaven-Destroying Earth-Shaking Sect wouldn't target the Myriad Treasures Alliance, and in return, the Myriad Treasures Alliance wouldn't target them or provide information about them to other forces.

Now that the Heaven-Destroying Earth-Shaking Sect had broken this understanding, the Myriad Treasures Alliance naturally had to retaliate!

The news that the Song family in Baimu City had been wiped out, that they were an important force of the Heaven-Destroying Earth-Shaking Sect in the Changyu Border Region, and that the Myriad Treasures Alliance had joined in to take revenge against the Heaven-Destroying Earth-Shaking Sect, instantly shook the entire Changyu Border Region.

By now, with both the Hundred Battles Hall and the Myriad Treasures Alliance taking action, the other members of the Heaven-Destroying Earth-Shaking Sect in the Changyu Border Region were stunned. Why had this suddenly happened? The time wasn't right yet, many plans hadn't been completed. How had the Hundred Battles Hall and the Myriad Treasures Alliance acted in succession?

It was becoming impossible to stay in the Changyu Border Region.

When Song You heard the news of the Song family's destruction, tears of excitement welled up in his eyes. "Father, Mother, I have avenged you!"

"And Brother Meng, I, Song You, will never forget this debt of gratitude!"

Now, he was the only one left of the entire Song family.

Moreover, the Myriad Treasures Alliance had put out a high bounty.

"So the Song family really was a force of the Heaven-Destroying Earth-Shaking Sect. How does it feel now that you've tried to frame me?" Meng Chong, who was on his way to Changyu City, sneered.

It was time to leave the Changyu Border Region and continue wandering through the various regions of the Divine Realm.

When he was still a thousand miles from Changyu City, just as Meng Chong was preparing to change his appearance to avoid searches and cross the border to leave, a figure suddenly appeared.

"Master!" Meng Chong was startled, then hurriedly bowed respectfully.

"If you have any questions about martial arts, you can ask now," Li Xuan said with a smile.

Li Xuan nodded. The questions Meng Chong asked were relatively simple, so there was no need for profound and mysterious answers. He could give some straightforward guidance.

So, Li Xuan carefully instructed Meng Chong on martial arts, especially regarding the third level of the Indestructible Body of the Great Sun and Stars, and finally on the realm of the Dao of the Blade.

As for divine abilities, he had explained most of what needed to be explained, and simply passed on a more complete manual of divine abilities to Meng Chong.

With communication talismans available for messaging each other, Meng Chong could discuss and seek advice from Xu Yan if he had any doubts, which made things much easier for Li Xuan as their master.

Walking leisurely while instructing Meng Chong on martial arts, they were not far from Changyu City now, and could see its border gate in the distance.

"Master, I understand now. I will definitely comprehend the third level as soon as possible!" Meng Chong said respectfully.

"Mm." Li Xuan nodded, then said, "Since that's the case, I'll pass on to you the physical cultivation technique beyond the Indestructible Body of the Great Sun and Stars."

"Thank you, Master!" Meng Chong said excitedly.

"Physical cultivation focuses on the body. The third level of the Indestructible Body of the Great Sun and Stars can truly achieve a state where each acupoint is like a star, forming a body like the universe. However, the stars in the acupoints are still just newborn stars.

"Gathering ten thousand stars, the boundless universe. I will pass on to you the Indestructible Body of the Boundless Universe."

Beyond the Indestructible Body of the Great Sun and Stars was the Indestructible Body of the Boundless Universe. After careful consideration, Li Xuan had decided on this name.

Gathering ten thousand stars to form the boundless universe! Meng Chong was excited in his heart, but his expression was solemn as he listened attentively.

Li Xuan began to explain the Indestructible Body of the Boundless Universe, how to strengthen the stars, how to gather the stars, and how to form the body of the boundless universe with ten thousand stars.

"The boundless universe establishes the way of the physical body, opening up the universe within the body..."

Li Xuan explained once, then let Meng Chong digest it on his own.

Meng Chong immersed himself in the Indestructible Body of the Boundless Universe, digesting its profundities and memorizing the profound methods of physical cultivation.

Suddenly!

Two figures flew over.

"Meng Chong, where do you think you're escaping to?"

It was the two Immortal Celestial Lords who had been pursuing Meng Chong.

Li Xuan cast an indifferent glance, then continued walking leisurely. When Meng Chong looked up, he saw that under his master's single glance, the two approaching Immortal Celestial Lords had turned to ash and scattered.

Gulp!

"To turn Immortal Celestial Lords to ash with just a glance, what realm must one reach to be able to do that?" Meng Chong marveled inwardly.

Master's strength was truly unfathomable.

"Were you able to remember it all?" Li Xuan asked casually.

"Master, there were some parts I couldn't memorize completely!" Meng Chong said with an embarrassed smile, scratching his head.
