He recalled those martial arts scholars, and a smile appeared on his face. He had shaken the inner domain, slaying peak great masters, so they must have all heard about it.

He should also be aware that he was not a martial arts master, but a pre-celestial realm martial artist.

The martial arts scholars all loved to delve into the martial arts, being obsessed with the martial way. When the true martial arts were presented before them, how could they not be moved? "A true martial artist is not a reckless person, as my master taught me, and I am a true martial artist."

Xu Yan was delighted in his heart.

After all, the Green Pine Pavilion lacked martial artists. It couldn't have him personally handle everything, nor could it disturb his master's peaceful and elegant life.

Therefore, the martial arts scholars were very suitable candidates.

Once they made a commitment, found their faith, they would undoubtedly defend it with their utmost effort, even sacrificing their lives for the Green Pine Pavilion.

However, the martial arts scholars were one of the foundations of the Seven Star Academy. To poach them would not be an easy task, and it might even lead to enmity with the Seven Star Academy.

The Seven Star Academy was undoubtedly very powerful. Although Xu Yan was not afraid, it would still cause him a lot of trouble, and this trouble would last for a while, even interfering with his master's carefree life.

However, since Pu Yuntian had invited him to the Seven Star Academy, and the academy's abbot wanted to see him, it was likely related to his previous discourse on the martial way.

Perhaps they could reach some form of cooperation.

After all, the Seven Star Academy was different from other forces, as its focus was on researching and propagating the martial arts.

"Brother, I'm going to challenge the Martial Arts Arena!" Meng Chong said excitedly.

"Good!" Xu Yan nodded.

With Meng Chong's strength, he could even kill peak great masters. Passing the Martial Arts Arena was a certainty.

After all, his strength was evident, even if the ninth-level arena was guarded by peak great masters.

The Martial Arts Arena of the Seven Star Academy was as lively as ever.

Today, there was a bald burly man who, just like Xu Yan before, directly wanted to challenge the upper levels of the arena.

This really angered the Seven Star Academy disciples guarding the arena.

Meng Chong didn't even bother to enter the arena, and with a flick of his finger, he sent the person flying.

This scene was very familiar.

All the martial artists, including the Seven Star Academy disciples, had a strange expression on their faces.

Could this bald reckless man be from the same school as Xu Yan?

They were just too similar.

Pu Yuntian sighed, "Little friend Xu, your disciple doesn't need to challenge the arena. The arena has no meaning for him."

Directly strangling peak great masters, this burly man came to challenge the arena - wasn't this just bullying?

"My disciple wants to enter the Scriptorium to study the martial arts classics, so he naturally has to challenge the arena," Xu Yan said seriously.

"For today, we'll make an exception and let your disciple enter the Scriptorium," Pu Yuntian said helplessly.

"That won't do. The rules of the Seven Star Academy must still be followed. We can't let people think that just because he's my disciple, he doesn't have to challenge the arena. Let them know that my disciple has the ability to pass the arena."

Xu Yan shook his head and refused.

Pu Yuntian opened his mouth, not knowing what to say. Weren't the rules meant to be broken? Now he was insisting on following the rules?

Unexpectedly, Meng Chong directly passed through the ninth-level arena, shocking all the spectators.

"This is the Scriptorium. The ninth level has been opened for you," Pu Yuntian said, leading Xu Yan and Meng Chong to the Scriptorium.

"Disciple, go directly to the ninth level. You can look at the lower levels another time," Xu Yan said.

"Okay!" Meng Chong nodded and entered the Scriptorium, heading to the ninth level.

"Little friend Xu, follow me. The abbot wants to see you," Pu Yuntian gestured for Xu Yan to follow.

Xu Yan followed Pu Yuntian to the core area of the Seven Star Academy.

"Little friend Xu, you are in the pre-celestial realm, not the master realm, right?" Pu Yuntian asked along the way.

"That's right, I've said so before," Xu Yan nodded.

Pu Yuntian nodded. "Little friend, from where did you receive your teachings?"

"My master has transcended the mundane world. Even if I tell you, you wouldn't know," Xu Yan replied calmly.

They passed through a refined courtyard area and entered a dilapidated small courtyard at the back of the Seven Star Academy.

In the courtyard, there was a seven-grade spiritual tea tree, over ten meters tall, with lush foliage and tender green buds.

Under the tree, there was a stone table and a few stools.

An old man with white hair was sitting on a stool, brewing tea.

Xu Yan's heart trembled. The old man's strength was very powerful, far exceeding the Fire Extermination Demon Sovereign, and he even gave the impression of being beyond the great master realm.

"Little friend, please sit," the old man said warmly, with a benevolent smile on his face. After Xu Yan sat down, the old man personally poured him a cup of tea.

"This tea is from the spiritual tea tree here. This spiritual tea tree is very ancient, rumored to have been planted by the founder of our Seven Star Academy..."

The old man introduced the spiritual tea tree beside them.

Pu Yuntian stood silently to the side.

Xu Yan drank a cup of tea and asked, "Senior, how should I address you?"

"White Cloud Empty, some people call me Old Man White," the Seven Star Academy abbot, White Cloud Empty, chuckled.

After that, White Cloud Empty seemed to be making casual conversation, introducing the Seven Star Academy while continuously refilling Xu Yan's tea, chatting for nearly half an hour.

He then asked, "May I ask, what realm is above the pre-celestial?"

At this moment, White Cloud Empty's expression became slightly solemn.

"Penetrating the Profound." Xu Yan put down his teacup and said, "Penetrating the profound mysteries of heaven and earth!"

"Penetrating the Profound?" White Cloud Empty murmured a few words to himself.

"Little friend, what are your thoughts on the martial arts of the masters?" White Cloud Empty asked.

Xu Yan found himself in a difficult position.

To directly say that it was a false martial art? The old man before him was extremely powerful, and if he got offended and went berserk, it would be quite troublesome.

Moreover, his master had not said that it was a false martial art.

After pondering for a moment, he said, "There is no view. Cultivation is different, and there is no true or false in the martial arts."

White Cloud Empty let out a wry laugh. Although Xu Yan said there was no true or false in the martial arts, in his eyes, the martial arts of the masters were indeed false martial arts.

"The martial arts have no true or false." White Cloud Empty smiled and said, "The founding of the Seven Star Academy was for the purpose of researching the martial arts, and many of the martial arts techniques currently circulating in the inner domain were originated from the Seven Star Academy.

"The mission of the Seven Star Academy is to research the martial arts and explore the true essence of the martial way. No matter what martial arts technique it is, we will research it without prejudice.

"Even if it is a murderous technique that harms the heavens and creates sins, we will not refrain from researching it. Perhaps there are things worth learning, and we can create new martial arts techniques."

The Seven Star Academy has contributed greatly to many secret martial arts techniques, some of which came at an extremely high price.
