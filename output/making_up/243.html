Fang Hao belatedly realized that something was amiss.

"How come I just said everything just now?"

The divine will is the will of heaven, and the will of heaven cannot be defied!

As a great master-level martial artist, how could he resist it?

He didn't even notice anything improper.

Fang Hao did not dwell on it further, but instead stared at the formation diagram, feeling that the patterns contained profound principles, far from ordinary.

"A great opportunity?"

Fang Hao muttered to himself.

He immersed himself completely in researching the formation diagram, pondering how to refine it into a treasure, and what the shape of the treasure should be.

"A treasure may not be able to contain the diagram, judging from the flow of the patterns, it seems to belong to a set? A main treasure, accompanied by a few auxiliary treasures, forming a combination?"

Gradually, Fang Hao began to get some ideas.

Since there were no restrictions on the treasures or their quantity, he could freely follow his own ideas.

Fang Hao threw himself wholeheartedly into the treasure refining, full of drive.

"Master, what is this?"Su Ling asked curiously as she approached.

"This is a battle puppet, but the one who forged it did not understand its essence, so it turned out quite useless," Li Xuan said, tapping a divine element into the puppet.

The battle puppet started moving, repeating the same few attack techniques.

Su Ling's eyes widened in amazement. "It can actually fight? But it's too crude, and the attack power is not strong, it can only deal with cultivators below the first rank."

"No, below the third rank. As long as one can dodge its attacks, they can find its weaknesses and defeat it. Only cultivators below the third rank, whose strength is not great, may be slain by its attacks," Shi Er, Zhou Ying, and Meng Shu Shu all nodded in agreement after taking a look.

"So it's quite useless," Li Xuan smiled. "It can still serve as a toy."

The battle puppet stopped moving, and Su Ling curiously infused some true qi, making it move again.

"Master, the battle puppet seems too fragile, it can hardly withstand much true qi, or it will collapse," Su Ling said with a frown.

Li Xuan nodded. Even a thread of his divine will was almost too much for it to bear.

"It's truly just a toy."

Su Ling soon lost interest in playing with it, but Shi Er took it to the side to play with.

"Fang Hao has extraordinary ideas and talent, he should be able to pass the test. This disciple, it seems that only fate can determine him," Li Xuan sighed inwardly.

Fang Hao's talent in treasure refining was truly extraordinary, be it the battle puppet or other treasures, they were beyond the current Spiritual Realm's capabilities. They were Fang Hao's own creations.

This showed his exceptional talent in this area, which was very suitable for cultivating the Mysterious Martial Dao.

However, the Mysterious Martial Dao encompassed not only treasure refining, but also formations, seals, and more. So it still needed to be tested. If Fang Hao could refine the formation diagram into a treasure, it would prove his talents in both treasure refining and formations.

"In the vast Spiritual Realm, finding a suitable disciple is not easy. I hope Fang Hao is the unique one," Li Xuan thought to himself as he opened the Great Dao Golden Book to review the Mysterious Martial Dao.
