The martial artists of Iron Mountain County were anxious and restless, and many of them had fled far away from the vicinity of the Qingqing Pavilion, with some even hiding in the mountains, waiting for the situation to pass before returning.

Undoubtedly, the most dangerous place in Iron Mountain County now was where the grand masters were, let alone the middle and lower-level martial artists who were not even at the third rank.

Even the slightest aftershock of a battle could reduce them to ashes.

Although Iron Mountain County was some distance away from the Qingqing Pavilion, the powerful figures who came would inevitably stay in the county.

As a result, the martial artists in the county had all temporarily left the county to find a place to avoid the turmoil.

Even the ordinary people had sensed that something was amiss and fled the county.

In just a few days, the county had become desolate.

The Tianbao Pavilion also had no guests coming and going, becoming cold and quiet.

On the top floor of the Tianbao Pavilion, a few people were sitting, sipping tea, completely unaffected.

Two of them, with graying hair, as if they had weathered the vicissitudes of life, were sipping tea while looking at the outline of the Qingqing Pavilion outside.

"What a grand spectacle! There hasn't been such a scene since the collapse of the dynasty," one of them said with a smile.

"Indeed!" the other nodded.

"The elixirs are truly profound, revolutionizing the cultivation of the martial dao, a groundbreaking innovation in the martial world that has attracted much attention. It's only natural."

"Do you think the Tianbao Pavilion will enjoy it exclusively, or just watch the unfolding events?"

The two chatted casually, unaffected by the pressure exerted by the various powerful factions.

"The Qi-Nurturing Elixir alone is not enough to make the Tianbao Pavilion abandon its principles. Let's see, the key is not the Qi-Nurturing Elixir formula or the Qi-Nurturing Elixir production method, but the person who created it.

"The Tianbao Pavilion has always valued talented individuals."

One of them spoke with a smile.

"Exactly, such a talented individual, if not recruited by the Tianbao Pavilion, would be a pity. So the Qingqing Pavilion can be divided up, but that person belongs to the Tianbao Pavilion."

The other nodded and smiled.

Acquiring one person was not difficult for them, no matter how many powerful figures gathered, as long as the Tianbao Pavilion wanted to protect someone, no one could touch them.

Since the various factions wanted the Qi-Nurturing Elixir, then so be it.

The Tianbao Pavilion's vision was not limited to the Qi-Nurturing Elixir.

In the Qingqing Pavilion, Su Lingxiu's expression was grave. She didn't expect that it would cause such a huge commotion, with many powerful figures from the inner domain gathering.

The manager of the Tianbao Pavilion had a smiling expression as he chatted with Xu Junhe.

"Brother Xu, the situation facing the Qingqing Pavilion is not good. As far as I know, the Grand Marshals of the Heavenly Robe Guard and the Heavenly Guardians of the Great Yue Kingdom have come, and they are all peak grand masters. Each of them has brought three grand master experts."

"Furthermore, the powerful figures revered by the Great Yue Kingdom's royal family have also come. Roughly estimating, there are more than fifty peak grand masters gathered here."

"Do you know what this means? Since the collapse of the dynasty, there has never been such a gathering of peak grand masters."

"Don't think that fifty is a lot. You have to know that after the collapse of the dynasty, countless powerful figures have perished, and the martial world has been resting and recuperating for a long time. The accumulated peak grand masters are not few."

"The collapse of the dynasty was over ten thousand years ago. Even if a peak grand master emerges every twenty years, you can imagine how many there would be. Let alone the inner domain, where it's impossible for a peak grand master to appear only once every twenty years."

Xu Junhe's heart trembled as he listened. He was not unaware of the significance of peak grand masters.

Even if his son could kill one with a single sword strike, he would need fifty strikes. Let alone the peak grand masters joining forces, how could they be so easily killed? One would be no match, let alone ten - it was too dangerous!

The only thing that gave him confidence was the powerful seniors.

"Fifty peak grand masters? They are overestimating the Qingqing Pavilion too much," Xu Junhe chuckled dryly.

"Brother Xu, the Qingqing Pavilion is doomed. Considering our friendship, you can come to the Tianbao Pavilion to seek refuge. As long as you come to the Tianbao Pavilion, your safety will be guaranteed."

The manager of the Tianbao Pavilion said with a smile, implying that the Qingqing Pavilion should give up the Qi-Nurturing Elixir and hand it over to the various factions.

The core members of the Qingqing Pavilion, mainly the ones who had developed the Qi-Nurturing Elixir, could join the Tianbao Pavilion, and their safety would be assured.

Xu Junhe was secretly shocked. The manager of the Tianbao Pavilion's implication was rather terrifying.

More than fifty peak grand masters had gathered, yet joining the Tianbao Pavilion could guarantee their safety. This showed how terrifying the Tianbao Pavilion's strength was.

"Brother Xu, the Tianbao Pavilion has always respected talented individuals and provided them with generous treatment, without restricting their freedom..."

The manager of the Tianbao Pavilion continued to smile.

"I appreciate your kindness, but the Qingqing Pavilion still has some foundation. We haven't reached that point yet. If we really have no other choice, I'll consider it."

Xu Junhe replied with a smile, not closing the door completely to avoid provoking the Tianbao Pavilion to join the faction targeting the Qingqing Pavilion.

"If you decide, just activate this signal flare, and the Tianbao Pavilion's experts will come to rescue you."

The manager of the Tianbao Pavilion left behind a signal flare.

After Xu Junhe thanked him, the manager left. Xu Junhe's expression became grave as he hurriedly went to the inner court of the Qingqing Pavilion.

"Fifty peak grand masters?" Meng Shushu's face turned pale. This was too terrifying.

How could Permit Yan alone withstand so many peak grand masters?

Kou Ruozhi was covered in cold sweat and said, "The inner domain is this powerful? The Great Yue Kingdom is too strong, and our Great Wilderness is too weak!"

However, he gritted his teeth and said, "If we get through this, I will definitely overthrow the Great Yue Kingdom!"

This was a life-and-death battle for the Qingqing Pavilion. If they won, they would officially rise to the ranks of the supreme powers in the inner domain. If they lost, they would lose everything.

Su Lingxiu hurriedly ran to Li Yuan's side and asked anxiously, "Master, you can deal with them, right?"

Fifty peak grand masters! She had thought they only needed to face the Great Yue Kingdom or the Black-Robed faction.

But now, many top factions had gathered, and even with the help of the Sword Immortal Cliff, the pressure was immense.

Li Yuan had a helpless expression. The number of peak grand masters in the inner domain was a bit too much, wasn't it?

However, upon further reflection, he didn't find it unexpected.

As the manager of the Tianbao Pavilion had said, after the collapse of the dynasty, the martial world of the inner domain had not experienced major conflicts and was in a period of recuperation. The number of peak grand masters, the pinnacle of the inner domain's martial arts, had naturally accumulated over the years.

Although they were not all peak grand masters, they were close. In the vast inner domain, there were no shortage of talented individuals who had reached the peak of martial arts over the years.
