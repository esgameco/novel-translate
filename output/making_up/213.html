"You're really timid. I wasn't really going to stew you!"

The aristocratic families of the Great Yue Kingdom announced that they no longer supported the Great Yue imperial court, and one high-ranking official after another in the capital rebelled, leading experts to attack the imperial palace.

What angered the Great Yue Emperor even more was that there were also traitors rebelling within the imperial palace!

At this moment, the Great Yue imperial court was undoubtedly in a state of power vacuum.

Although the Great Yue Kingdom had become the foremost of the three great nations in the Inner Domain, its strength and foundation were naturally not to be underestimated. Although they were caught off guard and their elites were mostly dispatched, and more than half of the high-ranking officials in the capital had rebelled, the foundation of the Great Yue imperial court was still revealed at this moment.

In the imperial court, there were still loyal high-ranking officials of the Great Yue Kingdom's imperial court. At this moment, they led experts to come to the aid, and one imperial court cultivator after another walked out of the imperial palace.

The peak Grandmaster Cultivators surged with momentum, and there was even an old prince whose aura was majestic, surpassing the peak Grandmaster Cultivators and approaching the realm of Half-Step Celestial Cultivator.

Unexpectedly, this old prince would soon become the Great Yue Kingdom's imperial court's pillar of the nation, a Half-Step Celestial Cultivator.

The rebelling group of high-ranking officials had their momentum instantly suppressed and began to fall into a disadvantageous position, about to be crushed at any moment.

Just then, a sword light flashed across, tyrannical and unmatched, slaying that old prince who was almost a Half-Step Celestial Cultivator with a single stroke!

The sword light did not stop, and after slaying the old prince, it continued to slay two peak Grandmaster Cultivators!

The originally confident Great Yue Emperor's expression changed drastically.

"Meng Chong!"

His heart sank to the bottom. The scene he had feared the most had finally arrived.

Meng Chong's appearance meant that this rebellion was being masterminded by the Long Qing Chamber, or rather, the Great Wasteland was taking over!

The Great Wasteland wanted to replace the Great Yue Kingdom!

"Kill!"

"Incompetent Great Yue Emperor, it's time to abdicate. The Great Yue is decaying, the Great Wasteland shall rise!"

"Restore the rightful order of heaven and earth, the Great Wasteland shall rise!"

Amid the shouts of the originally Great Yue Kingdom's high-ranking officials, the Great Yue imperial court retreated step by step. Seeing the situation turn unfavorable, the officials one by one turned against the imperial court and joined the rebel army.

On this day, the Inner Domain was shaken.

After thousands of years of establishment, with a deep foundation, the Great Yue Kingdom, the strongest of the three great nations, had collapsed.

The Great Wasteland was officially established!

From now on, there would be no more Great Yue in the Inner Domain, only the Great Wasteland!

The slogan "Restore the rightful order of heaven and earth, the Great Wasteland shall rise" also spread.

Cultivators of the Great Wasteland Martial Dao expressed their support one after another!

Suddenly, all the major forces in the Inner Domain turned their eyes to the Yan Kingdom and the Purple Cloud Kingdom.

The Great Wasteland was shining brightly, with the prospect of once again unifying the Inner Domain.

After replacing the Great Yue Kingdom, the Great Wasteland did not take further action, but instead focused on consolidating and stabilizing the Great Wasteland, pacifying the hearts of the Great Wasteland people, and freely spreading the Great Wasteland Martial Dao techniques.

The Transcendence Techniques also began to be passed on to the Grandmaster Cultivators who had joined the Great Wasteland.

The various major forces within the former Great Yue Kingdom borders voluntarily went to the Great Wasteland to register their sects.

The names of the two Great Wasteland Emperors also spread.

Of course, the Great Wasteland Cabinet was the one truly in charge of Great Wasteland affairs. The Cabinet Elders needed to be appointed by the two Emperors, but it was just a formality.

Many major forces knew that a major change was about to take place in the Inner Domain.

They also needed to consider how their own forces could be passed on.

The martial dao of the Inner Domain had currently reached the limit of the Half-Step Celestial Cultivator realm. To break through the martial dao to the Celestial Cultivator realm, one needed to enter the Spiritual Realm.

However, what the Spiritual Realm was like, no one knew.

After enjoying the treatment of the strong in the Inner Domain, to fall to the bottom tier in the Spiritual Realm, or even have to strive for Celestial Cultivator realm cultivation methods, was too great a gap for the Inner Domain's strong experts.

But the Great Wasteland Martial Dao did not have this limitation, and one could directly cultivate to a realm equivalent to the Celestial Cultivator.

This was a huge attraction.

The major forces in the Inner Domain were all considering their own path forward, while also paying attention to the movements of the Yan Kingdom and the Purple Cloud Kingdom.

The turmoil outside did not affect Canglan Island.

Meng Chong had returned.

"For the disciples who have cultivated the introductory Great Wasteland Martial Dao reaching one thousand people, you will obtain the Thousand Martial Spirit Shadows."

The Golden Book of the Dao opened, and golden light surged forth.

The Thousand Martial Spirit Shadows!

Li Xuan's heart was already unperturbed. This was nothing.
