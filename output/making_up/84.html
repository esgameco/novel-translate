This was his self-created lethal sword move!

It did not belong to Sword Saint Peak. Through many life-and-death battles over the years, he had created this ground-skimming sword light technique, which achieved the final victory by taking the enemy by surprise.

Under the cover of the raging sword qi, it had a startling effect.

Anyone who had seen this ground-skimming sword light had already died!

No one knew he had this hidden killing move!

Xu Yan's brow furrowed, but his figure flickered and dodged. Then he swung his sword, and amidst the raging sword qi, a series of ground-skimming sword lights shot out.

They were even stronger, faster, and more exquisite than Hu Shan's.

At this moment, Hu Shan had not yet realized that Xu Yan had unleashed his signature move.

Xie Lingfeng's pupils shrank, and a tumultuous storm surged in his heart. Wasn't this Hu Shan's killing move?

Could sword heart enlightenment really exist?!

"Be careful!" Xie Lingfeng hurriedly drew his sword, and a dazzling sword light shot out, blocking Xu Yan's ground-skimming sword lights.

Even so, one sword light still grazed Hu Shan's face, leaving a shallow sword mark.

Xu Yan sheathed his sword, standing there with an arrogant expression.

Hu Shan's lips trembled, his face deathly pale, his eyes wide with disbelief.

This was his self-created move!

As soon as he unleashed it, it was taken and used by the other party, and even better and stronger than his own.

What kind of monster was this? A cultivator this monstrous, who in the world of sword dao could be his match?

Who could dare to draw their sword against him?

Unless they had a cultivation realm far surpassing his, with absolute strength to crush him.

Otherwise, at the same realm, he would be invincible!

No, he could even fight beyond his realm!

At this moment, Hu Shan had no choice but to believe that what Xu Yan said was true - he had learned the sword techniques on the spot and could freely take and use them.

Xie Lingfeng felt a bit lost and dazed. This battle had dealt him such a heavy blow, he even began to doubt his own sword dao talent.

After a long while, he finally calmed down a bit, sheathed his sword, and cupped his hands, saying, "Senior Xu, it is I who have lost."

Although he technically hadn't lost yet.

But Xie Lingfeng knew he could not defeat Xu Yan. His sword techniques had been thoroughly seen through, and the only thing keeping him from losing was his slightly higher cultivation realm.

"You haven't lost yet, since I haven't defeated you," Xu Yan said thoughtfully.

Xie Lingfeng was very strong, proficient in sword techniques, he just lacked the sword heart enlightenment.

Although Xu Yan could see the weaknesses in Xie Lingfeng's sword techniques, the fleeting opportunities, with his current strength, he was not confident he could seize them.
