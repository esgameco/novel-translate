Chapter 10 - The Disciple's Engagement Broken Off (Please collect and continue reading)

The first day after Xu Yan's departure, Li Xuan got up early and went out, but he didn't see that diligent training figure, nor did anyone cook for him. He had to do the feeding of the chickens and the planting of the vegetables by himself.

He felt uneasy all over.

On the second day after Xu Yan's departure, Li Xuan started to feel anxious again.

"My foolish disciple, he couldn't have died in the evil demon forest, right?"

"He came back safely twice before, so he should be fine this time too, right?"

...

On the fifth day after Xu Yan's departure, Li Xuan came to the hillside outside the village, looking in the direction Xu Yan had left, but he didn't see anyone coming.

He heaved a sigh of relief.

"Even if I've been exposed, those who want to arrest me as a fraud wouldn't come so quickly."

"Will I really be exposed?"

"Sigh, that foolish Xu Yan isn't very smart, but since he comes from a good background, there should be smarter people, right?"

"If I get exposed, won't I be in trouble?"

Li Xuan's mind was in a mess, and he was quite worried.

...

On the tenth day after Xu Yan's departure, Li Xuan stood on the hillside, keeping an eye out, ready to hide and run if he sensed anything amiss.

"This disciple, he couldn't have died in the evil demon forest, and not returned home, right?"

"Or maybe he's been confined?"

The more he thought about it, the more muddled his mind became. Li Xuan cursed under his breath, "Fuck! It's not like I forced him to be deceived, it was his own lack of intelligence, how can you blame me for it?"

"I'm doing so poorly, it's so embarrassing!"

Li Xuan sighed, feeling that as a transmigrator, he was doing too poorly.

...

On the eleventh day, Xu Yan finally returned.

"Master, your disciple has returned!"

Seeing the respectful Xu Yan, and that only he had come back, without a group of people to arrest the fraud that Li Xuan was, Li Xuan felt a great sense of relief.

"Mm, good that you're back!"

His expression remained calm and indifferent, as if he had already known Xu Yan would return.

"Master, this is a small token from your disciple."

Xu Yan placed two long boxes on the table.

"That's thoughtful of you."

Li Xuan's expression remained tranquil as he reached out to open one of the boxes.

Inside was a sword, the scabbard entirely golden, engraved with auspicious clouds and auspicious beast patterns, inlaid with red, white, and blue gemstones, nine in total.

What a treasure!

Just a glance showed it was priceless, and Li Xuan was inwardly excited. This foolish disciple's background was even richer than he had imagined.

His face remained calm and indifferent, as if he wasn't moved at all.

He reached out and picked up the precious sword from the box. It was a bit heavy.

The scabbard was inlaid with nine gemstones on each side, a total of eighteen gemstones, each of which was of great value.

The hilt was inlaid with two larger red gemstones.

Li Xuan gently drew the sword out of the scabbard, and a golden light flashed before his eyes - this was a sword entirely forged of gold!

He sheathed the sword and placed it back in the box.

While Li Xuan's face remained calm and tranquil, his heart was already trembling with excitement.

"I've hit the jackpot. This sword can be passed down as a family heirloom. In the future, when I flee to the Wu state, I won't have to worry about anything."

His face revealed a slight look of approval as he said, "You have such a heart, your master is very pleased. Although this sword is mundane, it can still be enjoyed."

Hearing this, Xu Yan was immediately overjoyed and hurriedly said, "I'm so glad Master likes it!"

"Master truly is a hidden immortal. Such a priceless treasure of a sword, yet he remains completely unmoved, only thinking of it as something to play with."

His admiration for his master rose even higher.

Li Xuan opened the second box, and inside was a flawless jade ruyi scepter. His eyes lit up slightly - this jade ruyi scepter was clearly a treasure as well.
