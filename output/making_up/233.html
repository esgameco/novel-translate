"Perhaps it is related to the arrangements I made for my body to become a corpse-devouring monster after my death. In the end, they would only become bloodthirsty and savage corpse-devouring monsters.

Of course, whether these Xian Realm Cultivators had this plan in mind was still unknown.

Xu Yan lay motionless in the thicket of thorns, not immediately getting up to leave.

In less than half an hour, another wave of spiritual power swept through the area.

The Xian Realm Cultivator who had just left had returned.

Xu Yan did not hesitate upon hearing this, quickly arriving at the deep pool mentioned by the jade dragon, and diving into the water, entering the underground channel.

The passage was dark, and embedded in the rock wall at a distance was a moonstone.

From the shape and traces of the passage, it seemed to be a channel dug out by a snake-like creature.

"Be careful, there may be some spirit beasts living underground, and occasionally poisonous gas may seep out."

The jade dragon warned.

Xu Yan's sword intent manifested, enveloping him as he swiftly advanced.

Wen Yong leaned against the damp and dark rock wall, panting heavily, blood trickling from the corners of his mouth, his face deathly pale.

He trembled as he took out a few medicinal pills from his storage pouch and swallowed them.

"Who killed Dai Yingying? Isn't this framing me?"

Wen Yong's eyes were moist, he deeply regretted his impulsive outburst back then. Dai Yingying had been so overbearing, and in a fit of righteous indignation, he couldn't hold back.

Being hunted by the Dai family was within his expectations. After all, the great families were high and mighty, how could they tolerate a scattered cultivator who dared to threaten them.

But Wen Yong was not too worried about this, as he was a member of the Myriad World Alliance, the Dai family would not easily be able to kill him. At most, he could fake his own death to escape.

The Alliance had already prepared countermeasures for him.

But he never imagined that Dai Yingying would be killed, and he would become the prime suspect!

The Dai family had also determined that he was the murderer.

Not only had the great Xian Realm experts been dispatched, even the Xian Realm ancestor of the Dai family had personally taken action.

"I almost got caught!"

Wen Yong had a look of great fear.

"Why are the Jade Spirit Sect and the Shu Family pursuing this person?"

Wen Yong puzzledly took out a portrait.

This was an image he had snatched from a scattered cultivator who had almost mistaken him for the one being pursued.

Fortunately, Wen Yong reacted swiftly, striking the other down and taking the portrait.

"This brother is more capable than me. I'm only being hunted by the Dai family, but you are being pursued by the Jade Spirit Sect and the Shu Family. I hope you can escape."

Looking at the portrait of the young man, Wen Yong felt a sense of kinship as a fellow wanderer.

Gazing into the dark passage, Wen Yong's expression gradually became more determined.

"Dai family, I, Wen Yong, will return to kill you."

His face was grave, this underground passage stretched for thousands of miles, and there were hidden dangers.

With his injuries, his strength was only at five or six tenths of his full power. Whether he could safely pass through this passage would depend on his luck.

"Cough, cough!"

Wen Yong coughed a few times, the sound echoing in the passage.

Leaning on a sword, he stood up and casually crushed the portrait, looking into the deep passage with a determined gaze and a solemn expression.

"I, Wen Yong, will not die here."

Successfully entering this passage was his only hope of escape. Otherwise, with the Xian Realm Cultivators in pursuit, he would have almost no chance of survival.

Wen Yong proceeded cautiously, step by step, maintaining a vigilant state of mind.

"Didn't you say this passage was very hidden?"

Xu Yan looked at the figure of Wen Yong ahead and transmitted a question.

The jade dragon was also puzzled. "It should be, I only discovered this passage by chance. How does he know about it?"

Moreover, judging from his pitiful appearance, he seemed to be on the run.

"Another scattered cultivator who has offended a sect or a great family?"

Xu Yan silently followed behind Wen Yong.
