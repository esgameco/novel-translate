Kou Ruozhi was not far from mastering the martial dao.

However, the consumption of medicinal pills was also not small.

Su Lingxiu was almost daily refining pills, especially needing to refine qi and blood pills to supply to the Changqing Pavilion and for the use of the Xu family.

"Your disciple Su Lingxiu has broken through to the great perfection of the qi and blood realm, you have achieved the complete mastery of the pill dao and martial dao (Xiantian realm)."

Golden light emerged.

Li Xuan looked at Su Lingxiu, he was pondering a problem.

To cultivate martial cultivators in the borderlands and rapidly improve their borderland martial dao strength, medicinal pills are indispensable, and the required quantity of these pills is a considerable number.

Spirit herbs are secondary, after all, ordinary qi and blood pills do not require many spirit herbs to refine a large batch, or even a little, with spirit herbs as the main ingredient and precious herbs as auxiliary ingredients.

But relying on Su Lingxiu alone, how can she refine so many medicinal pills?

Even though she has reached the pinnacle in refining qi and blood pills, one furnace can refine dozens of pills, but with just her alone, she cannot sustain such a huge quantity.

Moreover, Su Lingxiu still needs to spend time cultivating the martial dao, studying the pill dao martial classics, refining higher-grade medicinal pills, and researching pill refining techniques, etc.

The borderlands are ultimately just a temporary dwelling place, entering the inner domain is inevitable, and the Changqing Pavilion will also enter the inner domain for development, focusing on the sale of medicinal pills.

It can be foreseen that when the medicinal pills are released, the inner domain martial cultivators will surely flock to them.

What will follow are the coveting from all sides, as well as various threats, and whether the Tianbaoge can sit tight is an unknown.

As for cooperating with the Tianbaoge, it depends on what price the Tianbaoge can offer.

Of course, no matter how they cooperate, the initiative will be in the hands of the Changqing Pavilion.

To have a large supply of medicinal pills, relying on Su Lingxiu alone, even if she works herself to death, she cannot supply enough.

Li Xuan thought of the assembly line production method, could medicinal pills also be done this way? The refining of medicinal pills is also divided into several steps, the complete pill refining technique is not easy to learn, but a single step is much easier.

In the final step, fusing and refining the extracted essence of spirit herbs, the medicinal pill is then produced.

And in this way, even if someone steals all the pill refining steps, it is still difficult to learn the pill refining technique, not to mention that the refining methods of each medicinal pill are different.

Those who are not pill dao martial cultivators cannot master the refining methods of various medicinal pills.

The sale of medicinal pills in the inner domain, the most common are only three types.

Qi and blood pills, needed by the majority of martial cultivators, even if there are differences in the inner domain martial dao, the efficacy of qi and blood pills can still assist them in cultivation.

Qi-nurturing pills, corresponding to master-level martial cultivators.

And healing-type medicinal pills.

The Changqing Pavilion only needs to produce these three types of medicinal pills, and it will be enough to obtain abundant resources in the inner domain.

They can also accept commissions, such as exchanging a certain amount of spirit herbs for the refining of a medicinal pill.

As long as the Changqing Pavilion is established in the inner domain, and the medicinal pills are promoted, once the martial cultivators have tasted the benefits, who would be afraid of not having spirit herbs?

Of course, in the beginning, the steps cannot be too big, otherwise the sudden and fierce impact will be very violent.

Li Xuan went to the inner domain to enjoy, not to constantly solve various troubles.

Therefore, only when the borderland martial dao becomes strong and forms a huge force, can they vigorously promote the medicinal pills, even if they face coveting and impact, they will have the strength to resolve it without Li Xuan's intervention.

Having thought this through, Li Xuan called Su Lingxiu over and pointed out the assembly line pill refining method to her, letting Su Lingxiu perfect it herself.

"This method of the master is good, I know how to do it now."

Su Lingxiu was overjoyed to hear this.

Next, she began to ponder how to implement the assembly line pill refining, first separating the pill refining steps, the refining method of each step, how to preserve the semi-finished products, and how to proceed to the next step, etc.

This is a complex process, and it also requires pill furnaces, which means finding people to forge them.

But before that, they need suitable pill refining martial cultivators to learn the separated pill refining steps, and the assembly line pill refining cannot be put on the agenda in the short term.

For now, the required medicinal pills are not many, and Su Lingxiu has recently taken some time to refine a batch, which is enough to sustain the needs for a period of time.

She is pondering the assembly line pill refining while also comprehending the Eight Trigrams and the various martial dao techniques in the pill dao martial classics.
