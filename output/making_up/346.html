Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The Great Zhe State was named for its abundance of mountains, rivers, and lakes. Many buildings were even constructed directly on the lakes.

One day, dozens of cultivator warriors rushed in from the Cloud State, bringing a shocking news to the Spiritual Sovereign of the Great Zhe State.

A disastrous flood was heading towards the Great Zhe State, the Cloud State had already been devastated with countless casualties!

The news shook the Great Zhe State. Many fled to the highlands in haste, while some remained skeptical.

That day, the rivers in the Great Zhe State suddenly swelled, and the flood arrived. Though weakened after passing through the Cloud State, the flood still submerged countless cities and towns, sweeping away those who couldn't escape in time.

The flood continued to spread, surging towards the Da Chuan State and Li State, but was significantly weakened after passing through the Cloud State and the Great Zhe State. The Da Chuan State, with its abundant mountains and rivers, was able to effectively block the flood's further advance.

Then, a shocking discovery was made - those who came into contact with the floodwaters, below the Patriarch realm, would experience symptoms of intense heat, reddened skin, and violent behavior. Even Patriarch realm cultivators afflicted would take days to recover.

In the Ice State, the snow had completely melted. Beautiful women, cultivators of the Quiet Snow Palace, were now walking in the rivers, their delicate figures barely covered by thin veils, occasionally revealing glimpses of their beauty.

These women were no longer themselves. A languid beauty lay on a couch, drifting downstream, emanating a soothing aura. This aura seemed to have a strange effect on the surrounding spiritual energy, as if disrupting the natural laws.

"Those who deceived me will meet a bad end," the beauty said coldly. "You think escaping the Ice State will save you?"

The black cave of the Quiet Snow Palace continued to expand, as if a black hole had appeared in the world.

Cultivators from the Cloud State encountered the women on the river and inquired about the changes in the Ice State. The women responded with coy laughter, luring the men onto their boats, where they began to...

The men were horrified to discover that the women were draining their spiritual energy and souls, growing stronger with each victim. One cultivator managed to break free, but the women's alluring voices continued to tempt him.

Realizing the Quiet Snow Palace had been corrupted, the cultivator fled to warn the higher authorities. More cultivators entered the Ice State, only to disappear as well.

The languid beauty reveled in her "hunting ground", eagerly awaiting the return of her "Wood Wood". The slaughter continued in the Cloud State, as all who encountered the Quiet Snow Palace women met a gruesome end, their bodies shriveled and lifeless.

As a result, the changes in Bingzhou, Yunzhou, and Dazhe State had not spread to Hongzhou quickly, and the Superb Spiritual Sect that ruled the Spiritual Realm was still unaware of the drastic changes that had occurred.

Cultivators from Yunzhou, Dazhe State, and Dachuan State were on their way to Hongzhou.

The battle of the genius cultivators was approaching its end.

The Wanshi Alliance was in the limelight, especially the junior alliance master Fang Hao, whose martial arts and methods once again shocked the entire Spiritual Realm.

At this moment, all the scattered cultivators seemed to have found their backbone, their morale was high, and they were determined to change the pattern of the Spiritual Realm and overthrow the rule of the Spiritual Sects.

On the Long Qing Pavilion's flying ship, Xu Yan took out the divine object he had obtained in the Cyan Sea. He left behind enough spiritual essence for himself and gave the rest to Meng Chong and Su Ling Xiu.

Li Xuan sighed inwardly, his senior brother Xu Yan was so capable, making an indelible contribution to his martial dao.

The battle of the genius cultivators continued, and Xu Yan had already begun to accumulate more spiritual essence to prepare for the breakthrough to the Divine Realm.

Meng Chong and Su Ling Xiu were striving to reach the Spiritual Element Realm.

"It's about to end," Li Xuan sighed, the grand battle of the genius cultivators was about to come to a close.

The Wanshi Alliance had truly stood up in the Spiritual Realm, completely breaking the pattern of the Spiritual Realm and shaking the rule of the Spiritual Sects.

But would the Superb Spiritual Sect just admit defeat? Li Xuan had some doubts, were they secretly preparing some moves? And would the other Spiritual Sects, including the Thousand Martial Hall, be willing to accept this humiliation and not seek revenge?

The Great Dao Golden Book opened, and golden light surged out.

"Your disciple Meng Chong has comprehended the first layer of the Unextinguishable Body of the Great Sun and Stars, you have broken through the first layer of the Unextinguishable Body of the Great Sun and Stars."

Boom!

Both the orthodox martial dao and the physical martial dao had reached the Divine Manifestation Realm.

And the medicinal martial dao would probably not be far behind.

Xu Yan had already explained the martial dao of the Divine Manifestation Realm to Su Ling Xiu.

Moreover, Li Xuan felt that Xu Yan seemed to have some insights into the martial dao of breaking through the Void Realm.

"Breaking through the Void Realm won't be too far away, right?"

Li Xuan looked forward to it.

The Emperor of the Great Zhou, Fu Tianhai, the Sect Master of the Thunder Cloud Sect, the Lord of the Yuling Manor, the Sect Master of the Thousand Martial Hall, and the Sect Master of the Tai Miao Sect stood in the air and announced the results of this genius cultivator competition.

"The number one genius cultivator of the Spiritual Realm is Xu Yan, the second is Meng Chong..."

"The top hundred genius cultivators of the Spiritual Realm have all obtained a spot, and in one month, they will all enter a treasure land in the Spiritual Realm..."

The Sect Master of the Thousand Martial Hall had a cold expression, his gaze turning to the Thousand Martial Hall camp, and the Elder Wielding the Sword nodded, indicating that they were ready.

The Emperor of the Great Zhou finished speaking and fell silent.

As for the matters of the Wanshi Alliance, they were unrelated to the Great Zhou, the territory of Shengzhou was directly ruled by the Great Zhou, and the Wanshi Alliance would not intervene.

As for the other territories ruled by the Spiritual Sects, he couldn't be bothered to care.

"The Wanshi Alliance's methods are extraordinary, especially the junior alliance master Fang Hao's array techniques, which are shocking, but if they think they can act recklessly and violate the rules of the Spiritual Realm, they are only seeking their own destruction.

The Spiritual Realm is ruled by the Spiritual Sects, and it cannot be violated!

As for whether it will be promoted to the top-tier Spiritual Sect or the same level as the Superb Spiritual Sect, the Sect Master of the Thunder Cloud Sect did not mention, apparently leaving a space for the Wanshi Alliance.

The entire venue fell silent.

Especially the cultivators from Yunzhou and Dazhe State, their expressions changed drastically.

"What happened?"

"Are there any people from Bingzhou?"
