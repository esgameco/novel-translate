With the might of a supreme expert, Huang Liang came stirring up the winds and clouds of the heavens and earth, his domineering aura unparalleled, yet he ended up dying like an ant! The stark contrast left Zheng Huang and the others dazed and bewildered.

Moreover, a thought they had never had before arose in their minds: Even the supreme experts of the Transcendent Spirit Sect are no more than this! The towering, god-like figure that stood tall in the heavens and earth was deeply imprinted in their souls, something they could never forget for the rest of their lives, and even the mere thought of it would make their souls tremble.

After the shock, Xu Yan excitedly asked, "Master, what martial art did you just display?"

It was so powerful, so overwhelming, as if it could prop up the heavens and earth, ready to split them open.

"Divine Ability!" replied Li Xuan.

Li Xuan was currently examining Huang Liang's storage bag, which he had left behind. After all, he was a supreme expert of the Transcendent Spirit Sect, so the storage bag might contain something valuable.

However, he was somewhat disappointed.

Inside the storage bag, there were only a few divine spirit herbs and three medicinal pills. Apart from that, there was nothing else.

The items he carried were likely all useful to Huang Liang.

The core treasures and cultivation techniques of the Thousand Martial Hall were obviously not carried on his person.

"So this is the Divine Ability!" Xu Yan was still in awe, it was just too powerful.

His blood was boiling, wishing he could break through to the Divine Ability realm immediately.

"Yes, my Divine Ability can manipulate the heavens and earth," Li Xuan said calmly, his expression unruffled. Inwardly, however, he was feeling extremely comfortable and relaxed. "Just now, I had already suppressed my Divine Ability to the lowest level, less than one-billionth-billionth-billionth of its full power. As for the heavens and earth, it's better not to speak of it."

Towards the end, Li Xuan shook his head with a sigh.

Xu Yan was deeply shaken, his eyes filled with awe.

"I understand now, Master's strength is too great. If you were to unleash even a fraction of your true power, the heavens and earth might not be able to withstand it.

"Yes, that's why Master is unwilling to overwhelm others with your cultivation. Whenever you act, you have to suppress your realm and strength.

"The reason is that you're worried that even the slightest leakage of your true power would cause upheaval in the heavens and earth, bringing calamity to all living beings!"

Xu Yan suddenly understood. His master had already reached such a terrifying level of power.

"Master, what's wrong with the heavens and earth?" Meng Chong scratched his head, his heart itching with curiosity as he asked.

"You'll know when the time comes. For now, focus on cultivating and improving your strength," Li Xuan waved his hand, unwilling to discuss it further.

With a wave of his hand, the formation reappeared.

"Alright, the ant Huang Liang is dead, let's get back to what we were doing."

Li Xuan took out the Taicangshu and prepared to continue his research into the laws of the heavens and earth.

With him taking action once, his disciples would be eager to cultivate like crazy again.

He looked forward to it.

"The realms above the Divine Ability are basically complete, but I should ponder the realms that come after as well."

Li Xuan thought to himself.

Seeing their master engrossed in his book, Xu Yan and the others did not disturb him further.

At this moment, Long Ming was completely dumbfounded. The way this powerful figure spoke was so profound and unfathomable.

He also had an itch in his heart, wondering what was wrong with the heavens and earth. Little Yue was even more curious, as her curiosity was greater than most.

"Big Brother, do you know what's wrong with the heavens and earth?" Suli Xiu couldn't help but ask.

As the eldest disciple, he would know the most about their master.

Meng Chong, Fang Hao, Zhou Ying, Shi Er, Meng Shu, and the three pet cats, Chihu, also gathered around.

Long Ming also couldn't help but join them.

Little Yue kept blinking her eyes, staring at Xu Yan, waiting for his answer.

Xu Yan pondered for a moment before solemnly saying, "You should remember this in your hearts. Master's intention is that the heavens and earth are very fragile. If he were to even slightly reveal his true strength or true aura, it would cause upheaval in the heavens and earth.

"Even the slightest display of his true power could make the heavens and earth collapse.

"That's why Master always suppresses his realm and strength when he acts, and tries to avoid acting if possible.

"It's all because he's worried about causing a calamity to the heavens and earth!"

Meng Chong and the others were stunned.

"No wonder Master usually doesn't act, and when he does, he always suppresses his realm and strength. Now it makes sense!" Meng Chong exclaimed in realization.

Chihu's back straightened, the entire tiger becoming more majestic. So the master was this powerful! Hearing Xu Yan's explanation, Meng Chong and the others were not only shocked, but also greatly excited.

As for Long Ming, he was dumbfounded and filled with disbelief.

Even the slightest display of true power, or the leakage of a trace of true aura, could cause a calamity to the heavens and earth?

What a terrifying existence! How could such a powerful being exist in this world?

He couldn't help but look back at the seated Li Xuan.

When he did, his body trembled. At that moment, he saw a figure as grand and majestic as the heavens and earth, mysterious and transcendent, as if it had already surpassed the heavens and earth.

Shaking his head, he even unconsciously rubbed his eyes, and when he looked again, Li Xuan appeared as ordinary as before.

However, every time his mind wandered, he would be shocked again when he looked back. It made him realize how unfathomable Xu Yan's master truly was.

"Could what Xu Yan said be true? Does such a terrifying existence really exist in this world?" Long Ming was dazed.

No wonder he didn't even consider the Transcendent Spirit Sect as worthy, treating them as mere ants.

This was not arrogance, but based on true power!

"Xu Yan's master is actually so powerful!" Little Yue exclaimed in amazement.

As for Li Xuan, he sighed inwardly, impressed by Xu Yan's imagination. He had only wanted to act a bit mysterious, but Xu Yan had already imagined him to be so powerful that even the leakage of his true aura could cause a calamity to the heavens and earth.

"With such powerful strength, I should have the hope of achieving what they've described. I need to make the later martial arts even more formidable, to truly transcend the heavens and earth.

"My disciples, can I truly become as powerful as you've described?" Li Xuan felt inspired.

He set a small goal for himself: to transcend the heavens and earth and become the kind of powerful existence his disciples had described.

...

Outside the manor, the group of cultivators from the Refined Spirit Sect who had been kneeling on the ground finally breathed a sigh of relief as the manor disappeared.

Nevertheless, they still reverently kowtowed three times before carefully backing away and leaving.

Huang Liang was dead!

The fall of the Thousand Martial Hall's supreme expert would undoubtedly stir up waves. The Spirit Realm was likely to become unsettled, and the Transcendent Spirit Sect would probably face another challenge.

This time, the opponent was an existence like a god or demon.

Huang Liang had died like an ant, so whether the Transcendent Spirit Sect's other supreme experts had the power to contend with this new threat was questionable.

Such a terrifying expert existed in this world.

"How terrifying!" Zheng Huang and the other experts were filled with trepidation.

Back in the imperial palace, Zheng Huang took a deep breath, pondering for a long time before beginning to write a secret letter to be sent to the Thousand Martial Hall. Whether they would believe it or not was not something he could control.

Thinking about the mysterious manor, so close to the capital city, sent a chill down his spine.
