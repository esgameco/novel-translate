Why did the sense of danger keep growing stronger?

Xu Yan nodded thoughtfully. Meng Chong was no ordinary martial artist after all, and although the sword formation was hidden, it was still detected by him. But since the Master was nearby, he didn't become vigilant.

In a flash, the sword formation was withdrawn, enveloping Su Lingxiu.

"Shimei, have you discovered anything?"

Xu Yan asked.

He was searching for a way to make the sword formation even more hidden and stealthy.

Su Lingxiu furrowed her brows and said, "I don't know why, but I feel the surrounding plants and trees have become rather sharp and dangerous."

"Is that so?"

Xu Yan nodded, and in a flash, the sword intent merged into the soil and even blended into the spiritual energy of heaven and earth.

"Huh, that feeling is gone, it's just... I feel something is a bit off."

Su Lingxiu said with a puzzled frown.

Meng Chong looked at the eldest disciple, his mouth twitching. He seemed to have figured it out - the eldest disciple must be up to something!

"Eldest disciple, have you realized something?"

Meng Chong asked curiously.

"The sword formation!"

Xu Yan exhaled, and in a flash, the sword formation appeared. Looking at the sword-shaped formations, the plants, soil, and everything seemed ready to transform into sharp blades, and each sword formation was rotating in a special pattern.

Meng Chong's scalp immediately went numb. So this was the sword formation?

Previously at the Dai family, Xu Yan had displayed the might of the Heart Sword Realm, where all things in heaven and earth could become swords. However, the sword formation operated in a profound way, making the attacks even stronger and the killing power more formidable.

"With this sword formation, if I were to fight Dai Sheng again, I'm confident I can kill him!"

Xu Yan withdrew the sword formation.

He had already reached the strength to slay a Cultivated Celestial in the initial stage.

"I feel like I'm about to break through, let me find a place to cultivate."

After bidding farewell to the Master, Xu Yan turned and left. He was not far from reaching the Shen Yi Realm.

"I'll go cultivate as well!"

Meng Chong let out a sigh. The eldest disciple could now slay a Cultivated Celestial in the initial stage on his own, so he also had to work hard on his cultivation.

Fortunately, he had just comprehended a cultivation technique that could assist his practice, so he could try it out.

Xu Yan and Meng Chong each found a place to cultivate.

As for Su Lingxiu, she did not leave, sitting beside Li Xuan, pouring tea for the Master while asking, "Master, how long will it take for my shidi to reach the initial stage?"

Li Xuan looked at Fang Hao in the valley and said, "The great momentum of heaven and earth is weakening, which means it is time for him to reach the initial stage."

Hearing this, Su Lingxiu looked at the strange formation set up by Fang Hao. The spiritual energy in the mountains was still gathering and slowly rising, far from being weakened.

"Master, what realm will my shidi reach after entering the initial stage?"

Su Lingxiu asked curiously.

Li Xuan pondered for a moment. Fang Hao was originally a Grand Master martial artist, and by relying on the momentum of heaven and earth to rebuild his martial foundation, he had become an Esoteric Martial Artist. His starting point was not low.

"The Tong Xuan Realm."

Fang Hao would enter the Tong Xuan Realm upon reaching the initial stage! Normally, Esoteric Martial Artists would enter the Xian Tian Realm at the initial stage.

But Fang Hao was the first Esoteric Martial Artist, and he already had a martial foundation, so his starting point would be higher.

"Normally, before reaching the initial stage, Esoteric Martial Artists also need to temper their bodies, strengthen their roots, and learn formations, strange realms, and the like. Only after completing these foundations can they meet the requirements for the initial stage.

"Although they can reach the initial stage directly, the cultivation before that is not necessarily faster than orthodox martial arts, physical martial arts, or alchemical martial arts.

"If the talent is insufficient, it may even be slower than cultivating other martial arts."

Li Xuan silently evaluated.

"After Fang Hao reaches the initial stage and receives the feedback from Esoteric Martial Arts, he will know the true cultivation path of Esoteric Martial Arts."

Li Xuan looked forward to it.

Half a month later, the momentum of heaven and earth began to weaken, and the treasures Fang Hao used to set up the strange formations of heaven and earth gradually showed cracks, almost reaching their limit.

"He's about to reach the initial stage, right?"

Li Xuan quietly watched.

A few more days passed.

Suddenly, Li Xuan revealed a smile. Fang Hao in the valley had become somewhat different.

On the spiritual platform, the Great Dao Golden Book opened.

"Your disciple Fang Hao, upon cultivating the Esoteric Martial Arts you created, has reached the initial stage of Esoteric Martial Arts, with strength a hundredfold of those at the same realm."

Li Xuan narrowed his eyes slightly, the Esoteric Martial Arts technique appearing in his mind.

But the feedback from the Great Dao Golden Book did not stop there.

"You have created the fourth martial dao, and your Esoteric Martial Arts have reached the Shen Yi Realm."

The Esoteric Martial Arts had reached the Shen Yi Realm. Now the four martial daos were: the Shen Yuan Realm of orthodox martial arts, the Shen Yi Realm of physical martial arts, alchemy martial arts, and Esoteric Martial Arts.

"Your disciple Fang Hao, in cultivating the Esoteric Martial Arts you created, has successfully refined a Heaven and Earth Esoteric Talisman. You have obtained a Xian Tian Esoteric Talisman."

At this moment, a mysterious esoteric talisman appeared on Li Xuan's bones.

"This!"

Li Xuan was somewhat surprised. He never expected Fang Hao to use this as the entry point for Esoteric Martial Arts, and to successfully cultivate it.

"I should have realized that for Esoteric Martial Artists to control formations, manipulate the great momentum of heaven and earth, and operate the strange realms of the Esoteric, they must use their own body as the core.

"And the only way to do this is to refine the Heaven and Earth Esoteric Talisman."

Li Xuan sighed deeply. The Esoteric Martial Arts had indeed taken an unconventional path, with a cultivation method completely different from the others.

This first Heaven and Earth Esoteric Talisman was just the foundation. The subsequent cultivation would be to refine the second, third, and more talismans within the body.

But each Heaven and Earth Esoteric Talisman needed to be cultivated to perfection before the next one could be refined.

Using the Heaven and Earth Esoteric Talismans to circulate true qi, true essence, divine will, and divine power... this would form the foundation of the martial dao, completely different from orthodox martial arts, physical martial arts, and alchemy martial arts.

This was the true embodiment of the unconventional Esoteric Martial Arts.

"Your disciple Fang Hao has comprehended the method of refining treasures, and your foundation in treasure refining is now complete."

"Your disciple Fang Hao has comprehended the method of formations, and your foundation in formations is now complete."

Xu Yan is refining his sword formations, perfecting them, and preparing to break through to the small success of the Spiritual Perception Realm.
