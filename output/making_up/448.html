Most of his time was spent on compiling martial arts, using the Golden Book of the Great Dao to compile martial arts above the Dao Realm level and perfect the entire martial arts system.

As his realm increased and his strength grew stronger, time became less and less sensitive to him.

The direction of martial arts after the Dao Establishment Realm was roughly determined. Even so, compiling the subsequent martial arts realms and methods was not an easy task.

After all, the later realms involved more profound issues, and had to maintain their inherent power. Therefore, Li Xuan had to repeatedly deliberate, delete and add, until he was satisfied.

"For the sake of my great martial arts undertaking, for the sake of my Dao Ancestor status, it's all worth it," Li Xuan thought silently to himself.

It was just compiling, not requiring him to cultivate arduously, so it wasn't considered too hard.

"Your disciple Meng Chong has broken through to the second level of the Great Sun Star Immortal Body. Your Great Sun Star Immortal Body has reached small success in the third level."

Meng Chong had broken through, entering the Void Breaking Realm.

"He's finally broken through," Li Xuan nodded.

"Your disciple Meng Chong has undergone extreme transformation, evolving into a Vajra Dao Body. You have obtained an Innate Immortal Dao Body."

"When that girl Su Lingxiu transforms, what kind of Dao Body will I obtain? Now it's already an Innate Immortal Dao Body."

Li Xuan was very curious about what kind of Dao Body the Golden Book would give him as feedback when Su Lingxiu broke through next. He looked up towards the location of the Great Mountain Heavenly Cave, now called the Great Mountain Divine Furnace. This heavenly cave had been completely transformed by Su Lingxiu into a divine furnace of heaven and earth.

Once netherworld creatures entered, they would be refined into pure blood essence. From outside the heavenly cave, it drew in extraterrestrial qi, which was now being melted and refined.

Su Lingxiu was currently inside the Great Mountain Divine Furnace, preparing for her breakthrough.

"Meng Chong has broken through, it's going to get lively again," Li Xuan anticipated eagerly.

After Xu Yan broke through, he began a killing spree. The number of immortal celestial lords who fell at his hands had already exceeded ten.

His fame spread throughout the Divine Realm. Even powerful immortal celestial lords changed color at the mention of Sword Immortal Xu Yan's name.

Meng Chong was hunted by the Myriad Treasures Alliance and encountered danger several times. This breakthrough would certainly not end well for them. He would surely kill until the Myriad Treasures Alliance changed color, kill until they bowed their heads.

"Jiang Buping is also about to break through."

Once Jiang Buping broke through, the Divine Realm would have another god of killing.

"Continue my great martial arts undertaking."

Li Xuan immersed himself in his martial arts undertaking again.

A year later, Su Lingxiu broke through to the Void Breaking Realm.

"Your disciple Su Lingxiu has broken through to the Void Breaking Realm. Her alchemy and medical martial arts have reached small success in the Heaven and Earth Realm."

Li Xuan began to anticipate what kind of upgrade he would receive when Su Lingxiu evolved into a Dao Body.

"Your disciple Su Lingxiu has undergone extreme transformation, evolving into an Evergreen Dao Body. You have obtained an Innate Great Dao Body."

At this moment, Li Xuan felt himself become stronger again. Due to the Innate Great Dao Body, it seemed as if Dao qi had been born within his body.

Although he had not entered the Dao Establishment Realm, his inner world seemed to have already laid the foundation of the Great Dao.

"A Great Dao Body, another upgrade," Li Xuan was delighted.

"With my current strength, I shouldn't be weaker than Meiwu, I might even be stronger."

Li Xuan's confidence greatly increased.

With an Innate Great Dao Body, small success in the Heaven and Earth Realm, and numerous powerful martial arts divine abilities, he could easily suppress Meiwu now.

"Even if Meiwu entered this world now, I could send her flying out."

Li Xuan was very excited.

"Indeed, only improving one's strength brings the greatest joy."

After Su Lingxiu's breakthrough, another year passed, and Xu Yan broke through to small success in the Void Breaking Realm.

On this day, with a loud boom, a burst of green light emerged from the Qinghua Realm, illuminating the entire sky. In an instant, the entire Qinghua Realm seemed to enter a mystical space.

The Qinghua Great Array was completed on this day.

The entire Qinghua Realm was within the great array, as if forming an independent space.

"The spiritual energy has increased by more than double, and is still increasing."

"Our Qinghua Sect will surely become the number one great sect in the Divine Realm."

"The spiritual energy is still increasing, I feel my bottleneck loosening, I'm going to seclude myself for a breakthrough."

"Do you sense that the heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms seem to have become more active?"

"Yes, I have that feeling too. The upper limit of the Immortal Realm seems to be breaking!"

"Great, we can finally break through the Immortal Realm's limit even in the Divine Realm."

Within the Qinghua Sect, the martial artists were excited and discussing enthusiastically.

The immortal celestial lords were even more overjoyed. The upper limit of heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms had been broken and was becoming increasingly active.

Moreover, it was slowly and continuously rising, which meant that the Immortal Realm was no longer the limit in the Divine Realm.

No, to be precise, the Immortal Realm was no longer the limit in the Qinghua Sect!

"Fang Hao is about to break through."

Li Xuan looked up towards the core of the Qinghua Great Array, where Fang Hao's body was shrouded in light.

Heavenly and earthly wondrous patterns were appearing, and the entire heavenly and earthly momentum was converging and pouring into his body.

This breakthrough was also a huge transformation for Fang Hao.

The Qimen martial arts would reach an even higher level.

As Fang Hao broke through, Li Xuan's Qimen martial arts also improved, reaching the Heaven and Earth Realm level.

With this, all five of his martial arts had reached the Heaven and Earth Realm.

Shortly after Fang Hao's breakthrough, Jiang Buping also broke through.
