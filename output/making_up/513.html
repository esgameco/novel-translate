The powerful beings from the Netherworld and the Witch-Demon Realm have been trying to enter the Cang Realm and fight against its strong ones for ages.

As time passed, the Cang Realm's advantage grew larger, and its powerful beings also increased in number.

After all, although there were some holes in the Cang Realm, it was still an intact world, and its laws of heaven and earth had not decayed. This was sufficient to support cultivators' practice, so strong ones would continue to emerge.

While Realm Lord level experts may not easily appear, there was no shortage of Supreme level experts.

In contrast, the Netherworld and Witch-Demon Realm had not produced any new Supreme level experts for a long time. The current Supreme level experts all belonged to the ancient generation.

The powerful beings of the Netherworld and Witch-Demon Realm had once joined forces to launch an attack on the Cang Realm, attempting to force its Realm Lords to make concessions. However, after a great battle, the Cang Realm repelled their joint attack by leveraging its numerical advantage in Realm Lords.

Nowadays, large-scale conflicts between the three realms no longer occurred. The powerful beings from each side occasionally made contact with each other, but the Cang Realm never yielded or made room for them to settle down.

Recently, the Netherworld had retracted the pathway of laws connecting to the holes in the Cang Realm that it had extended earlier. It seemed to have given up on the idea of letting Netherworld warriors enter the Cang Realm.

Boom! A sudden tremor came from the Netherworld. The realm, which had already partially collapsed, suddenly crumbled further in one corner.

The tremors rippled outwards, spreading through the Unchanging Land.

The Witch-Demon Realm, being closest to the Netherworld, was the first to sense it. One by one, powerful beings emerged and stood outside their realm.

Charming Witch, Earth Witch, Strength Witch, Flame Demon, Giant Demon, Tyrant Demon, Red Demon - the few remaining of the Seven Witches and Twelve Demons of the Witch-Demon Realm all appeared, looking towards the direction of the Netherworld.

"What happened? Why did another part of the Netherworld collapse?" Giant Demon asked in a deep voice.

Charming Witch frowned. At this moment, she showed no trace of her usual allure. Her expression contained only solemnity and fear.

"Did the Unchanging Divine Palace make a move? Even in its ruined state, the Netherworld is not something we have the power to crumble. Only the Lord of a Realm would have such strength!" Charming Witch said gravely.

"Is the Netherworld finished then? And will our Witch-Demon Realm be next?" Earth Witch and the others' expressions changed drastically.

At this moment, all seven of them looked towards the Cang Realm. To resist the Unchanging Divine Palace, only the Cang Realm had any possibility!

"Have those few from the Netherworld already perished?" Flame Demon frowned as he looked towards the Netherworld's location.

"I don't know!" Charming Witch shook her head.

"Could they have surrendered to the Unchanging Divine Palace?" Strength Demon asked hesitantly.

"Even if they surrendered, it would only buy them a short reprieve. Besides, we all know the Unchanging Divine Palace's style of action and their methods. They won't accept us, but will definitely invade to absorb our strength and steal the essence of our realms from us." Charming Witch sighed.

If surrendering to the Unchanging Divine Palace was an option, why would they stubbornly defend their realms in the face of mortal danger? It was simply because the Unchanging Divine Palace aimed to devour realms to achieve some goal. In a sense, the lords of these small realms and the living beings within were merely nutrients in the eyes of the Unchanging Divine Palace!

Boom! Suddenly, another corner of the Netherworld collapsed. Even across the vast distance, they could faintly sense a presence emerging.

"A Realm Lord!" The expressions of Charming Witch and the other six grew heavy. A true Realm Lord had made a move.

After lying dormant for such a long time, the Unchanging Divine Palace had finally recovered from the great battle of the past and begun to take action. And their first move was to send out a Realm Lord.

"Should we support the Netherworld?" Strength Demon asked solemnly.

"It's too late." Charming Witch sighed, "Even if we join forces, we may not be able to match that Realm Lord. By relying on the power of the Witch-Demon Realm, we could resist for a while, but it would take considerable time to move our realm over there. We won't make it in time."

"Let's find Tai Kun and Tai He. At this point, if the Cang Realm wants to survive, their only option is to accept us and fight the Unchanging Divine Palace together!" Tyrant Demon said coldly.

"That's right. At this point, this is the only path left. The Witch-Demon Realm should also move closer to the Cang Realm!" Charming Witch and the other six made their decision and returned to the Witch-Demon Realm.

Hum! The laws of the Witch-Demon Realm extended towards the holes in the Cang Realm. The laws began to contract, and the vast Witch-Demon Realm slowly started to move, drawing closer to the Cang Realm.

In the Cang Realm, Dao Domain, True Dragon Realm.

Ao Ming had already returned and was recounting the details of the dragon subjugation incident, as well as the powerful being behind Xu Yan.

"Xiao Yao?" Ao Lie's red whiskers trembled with rage as he furiously said, "Does he think it's still like the old days? Of the Seven Heroes of Cang, some are dead, some have disappeared, leaving only him and Yu Yao. He's all alone now, yet he still dares to act so presumptuously?"

"This is disregarding me, Ao Lie! Developing a method to subjugate dragons, what is he trying to do? This is bullying dragons too much! Does he really think I, Ao Lie, am easy to bully?"

Ao Lie was furious, his dragon aura rippling out. Even his dragon horns flashed with a sharp light.

"I didn't want to bother with that flirtatious woman Charming Witch, but this bastard Xiao Yao can't be let off so easily. I must demand an explanation from him and see just how capable this so-called leader of the Seven Heroes of Cang really is!"

Ao Lie stood up abruptly, speaking with uncontrollable anger.

Just then, a voice came:

"Brother Ao Lie, Tian Sha requests an audience!"

Ao Lie raised an eyebrow and gestured for Ao Ming to go welcome the guest. He sat on his throne, waiting for Tian Sha to arrive.

"Brother Tian Sha, what important matter brings you here?" Ao Lie asked directly after Tian Sha entered.

"I've come about Xiao Yao!" Tian Sha's gaze was solemn. "Xiao Yao has gone too far. He didn't contribute in repelling the Netherworld and Witch-Demon Realm several times, and now he's plotting for the authority over realms in the Divine Realm, even targeting the True Dragon Realm. This matter cannot be left unresolved."

Hearing this, Ao Lie immediately asked, "What's your opinion on this, Tian Sha?"

"I'm willing to lend Brother Ao Lie a hand in suppressing Xiao Yao. For this operation, I've already contacted some Realm Lords, and they won't interfere."

"I'm sure Brother Ao Lie knows that if we target Xiao Yao, Yu Yao, being one of the Seven Heroes, certainly won't stand idly by. I will block Yu Yao."

Tian Sha's eyes were cold.

"Do you have a grudge against Xiao Yao?" Ao Lie was very surprised.

"You could say that. What do you think, Brother Ao Lie?" Tian Sha neither confirmed nor denied.

Ao Lie fell into deep thought. It was no secret among the Realm Lords of the Dao Domain that Tian Sha was plotting for the authority over realms.

However, the authority over realms was not so easily obtained. Where did Tian Sha get the confidence from? Many Realm Lords were watching and waiting, even preparing to get a piece of the pie, absolutely not allowing Tian Sha to become too powerful alone.

Among the ancient small realm lords, Tai He, Tai Kun, and Tai Miao had shown no movement, made no statement on this matter, as if tacitly approving Tian Sha's actions.

"This cooperation to target Xiao Yao is fine, but in the Cang Realm, our True Spirit lineage received the favor of the Cang ancestors and was given a place..."

After all, the real crisis came from beyond heaven and earth. If they couldn't overcome it, everything would be for naught!
