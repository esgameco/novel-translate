The city of Yuntianbijhai became bustling with the arrival of Xu Yan.

On the top floor of the city lord's manor, Fu Tianhai stood in the corridor, with Xie Tianheng and Fu Yun standing on his left and right respectively.

"Xu Yan is indeed remarkable!" Xie Tianheng sighed.

Xie Tianheng gritted his teeth, feeling as if a huge mountain was pressing down on him, his whole body about to fall apart. His father-in-law's strength was truly too powerful.

However, he was still dissatisfied, snorting, "If I were at the same cultivation level as you, you would definitely not be my match!"

"Using cultivation level to oppress others is also a display of strength!" Fu Tianhai sneered.

"Hmph, if I were your age, my cultivation level would have surpassed yours by several levels. With so many cultivation resources at your disposal and cultivating for so long, yet your strength is still so little, I would be ashamed if I were you."

Xie Tianheng still stubbornly insisted.

Fu Tianhai clenched his five fingers, paused for a moment, and said, "You are too unfilial!"

Boom! "Fuck! Using your might to bully the weak, aren't you ashamed?"

"What's wrong with a father-in-law beating his son-in-law?"

Fu Yun looked helpless. When Xie Tianheng first arrived, he was very low-key and not at all arrogant, obedient to his father-in-law and trying to please him in every way.

She had even doubted for a time whether this was the same Xie Tianheng she had known before? But after his strength increased, he had finally reverted to his arrogant self.

"Father, go easy on him!"

Hearing this, Fu Tianhai struck even harder.

In the courtyard, after catching up with Xie Lingteng, Xu Yan began to teach him the divine elemental martial arts, and also took out some medicinal pills to assist Xie Lingteng's cultivation.

"Brother Xie, you have already reached the peak of the Divine Consciousness Realm and have accumulated some foundation, but it is still not enough. Accumulate more foundation, and when you break through, your self-transformation and ascension to the Divine Elemental Realm will make your strength even greater."

Xu Yan instructed.

For the next half month, Xu Yan guided Xie Lingteng in his cultivation and sword dao.

Until Xie Lingteng had accumulated enough foundation and began to seclude himself to break through to the Divine Elemental Realm, Xu Yan finally saw the long-absent Xie Tianheng and Xie Lingteng's mother, the city lord's daughter Fu Yun.

However, Xie Tianheng's face was bruised and swollen, as if he had been severely beaten.

Xu Yan couldn't help but be curious, who had beaten the Sword King of Beihai?

"Do you have any healing pills? Give me one, that old man hit too hard!" Xie Tianheng covered his face and said.

For several days, a force had been lingering on his face, preventing his injuries from healing.

With this appearance, he naturally could not go out, or else it would be a disgrace to the reputation of the Sword King. Xu Yan suddenly understood, so it was the father-in-law who had beaten him up.

He handed a bottle of medicinal pills to Xie Tianheng.

After another round of catching up, Xie Tianheng introduced Fu Yun, and when talking about Fu Yun, he was extremely proud.

In the Inner Realm, he had married the revered Spiritual Realm's young lady Fu Yun, unmatched by anyone in the Inner Realm, which was the reason he had looked down on everyone else back then.

"My sword dao has stagnated a bit, I always feel like I'm missing something to break through."

Xie Tianheng sighed.

He had come to seek Xu Yan's guidance on the insights of the sword dao.

Xu Yan naturally proceeded to guide Xie Tianheng on the sword dao.

Just after finishing guiding Xie Tianheng, a steward hurriedly came and said, "Miss, Luo Shao from the Wanxing Martial Arts Institute has requested to challenge Xu Yan, Young Master!"

"Luo Shao?" Fu Yun was greatly surprised.

Xie Tianheng frowned and said, "This guy, why is he challenging now?"

Xu Yan raised his eyebrows, curious, "How is Luo Shao's strength?"

"Luo Shao is known as the top genius of the Wanxing Martial Arts Institute, even called the number one genius of Beihai. It is said that he has already refined the aura of three heavenly and earthly laws."

Fu Yun introduced.

Xie Tianheng supplemented, "It is rumored that Luo Shao possesses a spiritual body."

Xu Yan became interested, he had long heard of genius cultivators with spiritual bodies, but had never had the chance to fight one. This Luo Shao actually had a spiritual body? No wonder he had already refined the aura of three heavenly and earthly laws.

Spiritual bodies are more closely connected to the spiritual mechanisms of heaven and earth, making it easier for them to refine the aura of heavenly and earthly laws.

Looking at the steward, he said, "Tell Luo Shao that he can challenge me, but only after he has defeated that Grand Commander, otherwise he is not qualified to challenge me!"

Although he was curious about the spiritual body genius, not just anyone was qualified to challenge him.

After defeating the Grand Commander, then he could come and challenge him, but if he couldn't even defeat the Grand Commander, he shouldn't even think about challenging Xu Yan.

"This..."

The steward looked at Fu Yun, and after getting her permission, hurriedly left.

Soon, the steward hurriedly came back again.

"Miss, Sea Wave from the Sea Spirit Tribe has requested to challenge the Young Master!"

Fu Yun was surprised again.

"Sea Wave is the top genius of the current Sea Spirit Tribe, rumored to have a distinguished bloodline, being the granddaughter of the current Sea Spirit Tribe Chief. It is said that she has also refined the aura of three heavenly and earthly laws, and is known as the twin genius of Beihai along with Luo Shao."

Fu Yun introduced.

"The usual rule, defeat the Grand Commander first, then I'll accept the challenge!" Xu Yan waved his hand dismissively.

The steward hurriedly left again.

The Grand Commander's face was very black, looking fierce as these two came to challenge him. He almost wanted to vomit blood.

The challengers were originally here to challenge Xu Yan, but Xu Yan had set a threshold, saying that only after defeating him could they challenge Xu Yan.

This really frustrated him.

He even regretted that he had interfered back then.

Now, everyone knew that he, the Grand Commander, was not Xu Yan's match!

"Grand Commander, please teach me!" Luo Shao said solemnly, holding a spear in his hand.

The gloomy-faced Grand Commander stared at Luo Shao and said, "I admit defeat, now you can go challenge Xu Yan."

Unexpectedly, Luo Shao shook his head and said, "I, Luo Shao, would never do such a deceitful thing. In a battle of geniuses, we must fight head-on. If I cannot defeat the Grand Commander, then I am not qualified to challenge Xu Yan.

"Grand Commander, please respect me!"

Seeing the unkempt Luo Shao with his thick, short hair and stubble, the Grand Commander almost vomited blood. Could he not be so serious! Damn it!

He glared at Sea Wave and said, "I admit defeat, you can go challenge Xu Yan now."

"Bullshit, I, Sea Wave, am going to be the Sea Spirit King, how can I stoop to such deceit? You, the Grand Commander, have no shame, but I, Sea Wave, have my pride!"

Fuck! The Grand Commander's face turned even blacker.

Sea Wave was the treasure of the Sea Spirit Tribe, even if she was rude, he could do nothing about it. This girl had a notorious temper, known throughout Beihai.

Taking a deep breath, he said, "Since that's the case, go ahead and fight."

Sea Wave held a azure trident, the divine artifact of the Sea Spirit Tribe. A look of anger crossed her pretty face as she said, "Grand Commander, are you looking down on us? Do you think you are qualified to let me and Luo Shao team up?"
