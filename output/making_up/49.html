Chapter 49: The Mighty Dragon Displays Its Prowess, One Man Sweeps Away Countless Troops

Xu Yan looked at the destroyed shops and felt a pang of heartache. Many of them were his family's shops!

"I acted rashly! I shouldn't have fought in the city. My family has so many shops here, and destroying even one results in a significant loss.

"And harming innocent people is even worse.

"My master said that the martial way is not about killing. Since I have the ability to control myself, I shouldn't have caused unnecessary casualties!"

The more Xu Yan thought about it, the more he felt that he had acted impulsively just now. He should have gone outside the city to fight.

As for capturing Jiang Pingshan, Xu Yan had no such intention. He was here to display his strength and give the old Emperor Qi a shock.

To single-handedly defeat the army he considered the pillar of the nation, the Shenwei Army, would be a big enough shock, wouldn't it?

From the rear of the Shenwei Army, a somewhat trembling voice was heard: "Good! We'll fight outside the city!"

Jiang Pingshan's voice was trembling. He could not believe this terrifying power that had single-handedly crushed the charge of the Shenwei Army.

And this person was once a potential son-in-law of his!

At this moment, a scene from the past flashed through his mind - Xu Yan's forceful words: "...one day, you will regret it deeply, you will not be able to reach up to him!"

Now, it seems that his daughter might truly not be able to reach up to this man.

But Jiang Pingshan did not believe that Xu Yan could be a match for the Shenwei Army.

In the streets of the city, the Shenwei Army could not fully display their abilities. But outside the city, that would be a different story.

That was the true battlefield of the Shenwei Army.

This time, to completely destroy the Tianmu Sect and help the Crown Prince establish his prestige, Emperor Qi had dispatched ten thousand Shenwei soldiers to be stationed and commanded here!

Ten thousand Shenwei soldiers, how could a mere mortal flesh and blood be their match?

"Retreat, gather outside the city!"

Jiang Pingshan calmed his trembling heart and issued the order.

The Shenwei Army began to withdraw. Although facing an opponent with inhuman strength like Xu Yan, the Shenwei soldiers still showed no sign of retreat.

As the Shenwei Army withdrew from the prefecture city, the civilians who had been confined to their homes also began to emerge, informing each other with expressions of shock on their faces.

The son of the wealthy family was going to challenge the Shenwei Army single-handedly?

Even more shocking was the fact that the prominent figures within the prefecture city found it hard to believe that Xu Junhe's son had cultivated such overwhelmingly powerful strength.

Where did he find such a reclusive master?

"Quickly, to the city wall!"

"Let's go, this battle will decide the fate of East River Prefecture and even the entire Qi Kingdom!"

Many of the prefecture city's prominent figures, under the protection of their guards and servants, rushed to the city wall.

"Let's go, to the city wall!"

Xu Junhe also spoke in a deep voice.

"Master, Madam, please!"

A guard brought a horse-drawn carriage over.

Xu Junhe held his wife's hand and entered the carriage, with the guards and servants surrounding the carriage as they headed towards the city gate.

"Master Xu, does your son have a chance?"

A voice came, and Xu Junhe looked up from the carriage window to see Kou Ruozhi and a group of Tianmu Sect elites following along to observe the battle from the city wall.

"Yan'er does not fight a battle he is not confident of winning," Xu Junhe replied calmly.

"Of course, I have full faith in the might of the Heavenly Honored Saint," Kou Ruozhi laughed heartily.

"Master Xu, about our cooperation..."

Kou Ruozhi had an expectant look on his face.

"We'll discuss that after the battle," Xu Junhe responded evasively.

The group soon arrived at the city wall.

Outside the city, the Shenwei Army was fully equipped, the soldiers clad in heavy armor, wielding long spears, with long sabers hanging at their waists, even their horses were armored.

"So this is the Shenwei Army, such a mighty army, how can one man be their match?"

The many prominent figures on the city wall, as well as the surging crowd of civilians, were all awestruck by the imposing might of the Shenwei Army.

Truly the pillar army of the Qi Kingdom!

"My dear, will Yan'er be alright?"

Xu Yan's wife couldn't help but worry as she looked at the formidable Shenwei Army outside the city.

"If Yan'er dares to fight, he must be confident. My dear, please rest assured!"

Xu Junhe was also anxious inside, but he tried to remain calm as he comforted his wife.

The group of Xu family guards, including Physician Pan, were also present.
