Although the Qinghua Realm had experienced a major invasion from the Netherworld Abyss, and the great battle had subsided with both sides returning to a state of confrontation, unrest still continued within the Qinghua Realm.

This large-scale invasion by the Netherworld Abyss had left the Qinghua Realm, especially the Great Yue Kingdom, feeling on the verge of collapse, causing some martial artists to defect to the Netherworld Abyss.

Now that the Netherworld Abyss had been repelled, those who had surrendered and already transformed into blood shadows were hiding everywhere, even causing chaos. They could no longer turn back.

Those who had secretly defected but had not yet transformed into blood shadows, blood slaves, or blood disciples lived in constant fear, worried that they would be exposed one day.

Some people had their weaknesses exploited by others and were being manipulated.

Although the Qinghua Realm was ruled by three major powers, there were also many other large and small factions. A reshuffling of the power structure was quietly taking place.

For the Great Yue Kingdom, Ten Thousand Thunder Sect, and Celestial Martial Gate, they did not care about the changes in those factions as long as the assigned tasks of guarding the abyss entrances in their respective areas could be completed.

In the territory governed by the Celestial Martial Gate, within the range of Iron Ridge Mountain, there was an abyss entrance that ranked above average in the Qinghua Realm.

This abyss entrance was guarded by one sect and two clans. During this recent attack, the patriarch of one clan had perished, weakening their strength.

The sect leader, seeing that they could not hold on and that the Qinghua Realm was in danger of falling, secretly defected to the Netherworld Abyss.

Before he could complete his mission as an insider, the Great Yue Kingdom defeated the Netherworld Abyss invasion and freed up powerful experts to support various regions. Several True King Celestial Venerables descended upon this place and joined forces to suppress this abyss entrance.

Unfortunately, his seemingly secret defection had been leaked. Someone was holding this information over him, and his authority was being stripped away step by step, leaving him helpless.

The sect was at a dangerous juncture.

The elders in the sect had all expressed dissatisfaction with him as the sect leader. He could only use the excuse that he had been secretly injured in the great battle, his strength had declined, and he had to make concessions to temporarily muddle through.

He even used the pretext of secluded cultivation to heal his injuries and no longer managed sect affairs.

"Sect Leader Wei, you know what to do, right?" In a secret valley somewhere on Iron Ridge Mountain, a man wearing a black robe and a mask on his face said with a sinister laugh.

Sect Leader Wei's expression was extremely gloomy as he said in a deep voice, "Your Shao family has defected to the Netherworld Abyss?"

The Shao family was one of the two clans of Iron Ridge, and also the faction that suffered the least damage in this Netherworld Abyss invasion.

"Sect Leader Wei, who told you I'm from the Shao family?" The masked man said with a cold laugh.

"Heh, you've been constantly seizing authority for the Shao family and expanding their sphere of influence in Iron Ridge. If you're not from the Shao family, who else could you be?" Sect Leader Wei said with a cold laugh.

"Sect Leader Wei, I advise you not to think too much. This plan must be completed, otherwise you know the consequences," the masked man said with a smile.

"The Iron Ridge Abyss cannot accommodate a Blood Child entering. Even if I'm willing to be an insider, a Blood Child cannot come in!" Sect Leader Wei said in a deep voice.

"The Qinghua Realm needs a Blood Child to back us up, but we don't need the Blood Child to come in. A Blood Disciple will do. Three days later, a Blood Disciple will enter the Iron Ridge Abyss. Your only task is to deceive the guardians and let the Blood Disciple enter the Qinghua Realm. Once the Blood Disciple enters the Qinghua Realm, it will be easy for them to advance to Blood Child," the masked man said solemnly.

Sect Leader Wei's expression changed slightly. He thought of Blood Child Tu, who had entered the Qinghua Realm as a Blood Disciple and performed a blood sacrifice on Qiyun Prefecture City to advance to Blood Child.

"What's wrong? Has Sect Leader Wei developed a sense of righteousness and is unwilling to do such a thing?" The masked man looked at the hesitating Sect Leader Wei and said in a sinister voice.

"Rest assured, I will handle this matter properly," Sect Leader Wei said, taking a deep breath.

At this point, he had no way to turn back.

"Very well, I await good news from Sect Leader Wei!" After saying this, the masked man's figure moved and disappeared from the spot.

"There's no turning back!" Sect Leader Wei muttered to himself before turning to leave.

Not long after the two had left, Xu Yan walked out from behind a large tree.

"The Qinghua Realm is truly chaotic, with no shortage of traitors," he said.

Both the masked man and Sect Leader Wei were True King Celestial Venerables with considerable strength. It would be quite difficult to kill them both.

"Go to the Iron Ridge Abyss. A Blood Disciple, huh? If I kill the Blood Disciple they've worked so hard to bring in, and then expose their identities as traitors..." Xu Yan had a plan in mind.

Directly confronting and killing a Blood Disciple would be quite challenging. Even though he was now at the great accomplishment stage of the Divine Ability realm, a Blood Disciple arranged to enter the Qinghua Realm and potentially repeat Blood Child Tu's feat would undoubtedly be powerful among Blood Disciples.

If he couldn't kill them in a direct confrontation, then he wouldn't confront them directly.

The turmoil at the Iron Ridge Abyss had subsided. On this day, it was the turn of Sect Leader Wei's sect to guard the Iron Ridge Abyss.

"Sect Leader, you've come?" The elder on guard duty said in surprise when he saw Sect Leader Wei appear.

"Yes, I'm going to check the abyss to prevent any Blood Disciples from invading again," Sect Leader Wei nodded.

After entering the abyss and reaching its depths, where another True King Celestial Venerable was on guard, he said, "I'll seclude myself here for a few days. You can return to the sect to rest for a few days."

"Yes, Sect Leader," that True King Celestial Venerable said after a moment of surprise before leaving.

Since the great battle, the sect's strength had declined, and the Sect Leader had repeatedly made concessions to the Shao family. The arduous task of guarding the abyss had mostly fallen on their shoulders.

Their rotation time was much longer than that of the Shao family's experts.

Perhaps the Sect Leader had come out of guilt, to temporarily take over guard duty for a few days.

Moreover, the Sect Leader was stronger than him, so there shouldn't be any problems with the Sect Leader guarding here.

Only Sect Leader Wei remained in the depths of the abyss.

He took out a Netherworld blood pearl and tossed it towards the blood membrane above. The blood pearl sank into the blood membrane.

"Is everything arranged?" After a while, a cold voice came through.

"My lord, everything has been arranged," Sect Leader Wei said solemnly.

"Very good!" Blood light shimmered, and a blood-colored figure descended from the blood membrane.

This Blood Disciple had pale red skin and a slightly pale face. His long fingers were like steel claws, giving off a faint cold light.

His aura was extremely powerful. Although far from a Blood Child, he was comparable to a peak True King Celestial Venerable.

"Well done!" The Blood Disciple nodded with satisfaction to Sect Leader Wei.

"It is my honor to serve you, my lord!" Sect Leader Wei said respectfully.

"Let's go."

"Yes, my lord. However, to avoid revealing your presence, please wear this armor," Sect Leader Wei took out a set of armor he had prepared in advance and handed it to the Blood Disciple.

The Blood Disciple put on the armor, which covered his entire body, leaving only his eyes exposed. But this alone wouldn't be enough to deceive the other martial artists guarding the abyss.

"My lord, please wait a moment while I send the others away," Sect Leader Wei said in a low voice as they approached the exit of the abyss.

"Mm," the Blood Disciple responded.
