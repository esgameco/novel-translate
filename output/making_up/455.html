The island was moving slowly.

It seemed like a floating island, but it was completely barren, without a single plant or tree visible.

The island was not small, spanning over ten miles in circumference, floating on the sea surface and moving slowly.

The "Ten Thousand Islands of the Azure Sea" refers to islands with martial arts forces and human settlements, not that the Azure Sea Realm only has ten thousand islands.

No one knows exactly how many islands exist in the entire Azure Sea Realm.

An island spanning ten miles in circumference is nothing special in the Azure Sea Realm, just a negligible small island.

What caught Xu Yan's attention was not the island itself, nor that it could move, but that this island was not actually a real island.

It turned out to be a huge black giant turtle in disguise.

Even though this giant turtle was trying its best to conceal its aura and blend into the sea, disguised as an island, it still could not escape Xu Yan's notice.

"It's not a native turtle!"

Xu Yan immediately became intrigued.

The aura on this giant turtle clearly did not belong to a spirit beast of the divine realm, nor was it a sea beast of the Azure Sea Realm, but rather a creature from beyond heaven and earth.

He thought of some rumors about the Heavenly Cave in the Azure Sea Realm, that some creatures had escaped from the Heavenly Cave and were hiding in the vast sea. Every time an Immortal Venerable of the Azure Sea Realm inspected the entire realm, they would discover some hidden outsiders.

With this giant turtle's disguise, an Immortal Venerable would definitely not be able to discover it!

"This turtle is interesting, I wonder if it's hiding any treasures from beyond the heavens!"

Xu Yan's eyes lit up.

He was quite interested in treasures from beyond the heavens.

After all, the Seven Wondrous Divine Flowers seemed to be treasures from beyond the heavens, and the jade bamboo in his hand was also a treasure from beyond the heavens.

Mei Wu was an existence from beyond heaven and earth, so her treasures naturally could not be from within the Cang Heaven and Earth.

Thinking of this, Xu Yan put away his flying boat and soared into the sky, flying towards the giant turtle.

As soon as Xu Yan appeared, the originally slowly moving giant turtle immediately stopped moving.

Thud! Xu Yan landed on the giant turtle's back, and the soil and rocks disguised as an island instantly crumbled away in a large area, revealing a patch of dark black turtle shell.

Thud thud thud! Xu Yan stomped his feet on the turtle shell, making thumping sounds. Each time his foot landed, a ripple of dark black light would spread out.

"Huh, this small island is really strange!"

Xu Yan stomped while pretending to mutter to himself.

The soil and rocks continued to crumble away, revealing an increasingly wide area of turtle back. The dark black turtle back seemed to contain some profound patterns, heavy and solid, indestructible.

"This turtle shell's defense is so strong!"

Xu Yan was secretly surprised.

His seemingly casual stomps each contained enough force to destroy an ordinary Immortal Venerable, yet the turtle shell remained unharmed, completely unaffected.

Let alone cracks, the entire turtle shell barely even felt any vibrations.

Boom!

The seawater surged up, and a turtle head emerged from the water, looking back at Xu Yan on its back. "Stop stomping, if you stomp any more, I'm going to die!"

This young man clearly discovered me, yet he's still pretending! The giant turtle felt somewhat helpless. I was careless, I didn't notice there was someone like him nearby, otherwise how could I have been exposed.

Xu Yan took out his sword and said, "Stomping definitely won't kill you. I want to try if one sword strike can break open your turtle shell!"

"Young man, if you have any conditions just say them directly. This old turtle really can't withstand your torment. Besides, I have no malicious intentions or hostility here, I've never harmed anyone!"

The giant turtle said with great distress.

"Since Brother Turtle is so straightforward, I won't be polite then!"

Xu Yan said with a smile.

While observing this giant turtle, its strength was at the Immortal realm, but its true power was probably stronger than even a typical peak Immortal realm cultivator.

In fact, Xu Yan suspected that no Immortal realm cultivator could break through its defense.

On the giant turtle's head was a patch of blue scales, with fine patterns like waves, and at a glance, it seemed as if one could see the waves surging.

Xu Yan's intuition told him that this giant turtle was extraordinary.

Sword intent surged from his body. In an instant, giant swords rose up from the sea surface, surrounding the area. More giant swords crossed in the air above, completely enveloping the giant turtle.

An aura of annihilation and killing intent rippled through heaven and earth.

The giant turtle raised its head to look at the giant swords formed from sea waves, with shock in its eyes.

It seemed as if there was a universe inside the turtle shell.

Heaven and earth's Dao essence was extremely mysterious, not as easy to capture as the essence of Dao principles. This involved the fundamental essence of the Dao of heaven and earth.

Without examining it closely, he put it away directly and asked, "Turtle brother, what should I call you?"

"Canghai!" The giant turtle replied after pondering for a moment.

"Canghai Turtle?" Xu Yan murmured. He had never heard of this turtle's name before.

He raised his hand and tossed a communication talisman to Canghai Turtle, saying, "Keep this communication talisman. If I obtain the Dao rhyme of heaven and earth, I will naturally contact you. If I have any questions, I will also use this to contact you."

Canghai Turtle was stunned, looking at the communication talisman, and asked, "How far can it communicate?"

"My junior brother said it can communicate anywhere within heaven and earth, but we haven't tested it yet. However, it can communicate within the divine realm," Xu Yan said. After finishing, his figure soared into the sky and disappeared in an instant.

Canghai Turtle looked at the communication talisman, somewhat dazed, muttering to himself: "When did the Taicang world have such an item? After leaving Canghai Turtle, Xu Yan took out a flying boat and drifted on the sea. He sat in the flying boat and took out the primordial turtle shell.

"How strong was Canghai Turtle in the past?" Xu Yan wondered curiously.

He didn't directly snatch it, but made a deal with Canghai Turtle. One of the most important reasons was that although Canghai Turtle seemed to be only at the Immortal realm, once it fought with all its might, it was impossible to gauge how strong it could become.

The flying boat drifted on the sea, and Xu Yan was immersed in claiming ownership and nurturing the primordial turtle shell, unaware of where the flying boat was drifting.

Time passed, and finally Xu Yan's consciousness formed a connection with the primordial turtle shell.

With a single thought, he could see the small world inside the primordial turtle shell.

The turtle shell contained its own small world, with rules operating. Weren't these rules formed from the insights the primordial turtle gained during cultivation?

Xu Yan was astounded.

However, such a powerful Canghai Turtle had fallen to this state. What great calamity had it encountered?

"Even with such powerful strength, one can fall and end up like this. I'm merely at the Void-breaking realm, I must be even more cautious and careful, and not be arrogant or conceited!"

Xu Yan admonished himself in his heart.

He also thought of the towering figure he had seen when crossing the wilderness.

Was the owner of this towering figure still alive? Or had they also perished? What terrifying enemy had they encountered?

"Only Master is truly invincible!"

Xu Yan sighed in his heart.

"This place is perfect for comprehending the Realm of Establishing the Way. When I nurture the primordial turtle shell into my mountains and rivers, it will further help me understand the Realm of Establishing the Way."

Xu Yan sat cross-legged in the primordial turtle world, his divine consciousness sensing the rules of the primordial turtle world, beginning to comprehend the Realm of Establishing the Way.

With a thought, a jade bamboo appeared, landing in front of him.

"Jade bamboo, primordial turtle world, with these as aids, I can quickly comprehend the Realm of Establishing the Way, and also lay the foundation for my breakthrough to the Heaven and Earth realm."

In the flying boat, there was only a small turtle shell, and this small turtle shell no longer emitted swirling light. Instead, it seemed as if a layer of moss had grown on the turtle shell, making it look like the shell of an ordinary turtle that had died.
