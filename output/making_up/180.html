However, since the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion had said so, they must be confident that the Long Qing Pavilion would ultimately seek aid and join the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion.

"The Heavenly Treasure Pavilion has taken too much. The Essence Qi Elixir is not something that can be monopolized by a single party, not even the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion," the Great Yue Emperor stood up and left.

He knew that in this battle for the Essence Qi Elixir, the Great Yue Kingdom alone could not contend with the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion. Only by uniting the major powers could they balance the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion.

They could no longer allow the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion to monopolize the Essence Qi Elixir.

On this day, the top powers of the Inner Realm, under the coordination of the Great Yue Kingdom, joined this operation.

This was an unspoken agreement - they could not let the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion continue to enjoy the benefits of the Essence Qi Elixir alone!

The Seven Star Academy still had no intention of getting involved in the conflict, maintaining its neutrality and the principle of teaching the martial dao, without interfering in the power struggles of the Inner Realm.
