The convoy continued moving forward.

But among the entourage, one person did not follow the convoy, but instead chased after in the direction Meng Chong and Zi Yun had left.

Sister Ping in the crowd was completely stunned.

That was the divine Meng Chong, and Zi Yun actually had such an intimate relationship with him.

The divine one's reputation was truly awe-inspiring!

Meng Chong and Zi Yun had already left East Yang Prefecture city. A respectful voice came from behind: "Young Master Meng, please wait a moment!"

Zi Yun looked back and saw it was an attendant from the Princess's entourage. She became alert, remembering that Meng Chong had said the Princess was thinking about his body.

"What is it?" Meng Chong asked, raising an eyebrow.

"Her Highness the Princess wishes to meet with Young Master Meng alone," the person said respectfully.

"No way!" Zi Yun jumped up and said. "Meet alone? What does she want to do? She wants to exclude me, that's absolutely not allowed!"

The corner of the attendant's mouth twitched. He said, "If Young Master Meng has no objections, Miss Zi Yun can naturally come along as well."

Zi Yun looked up at Meng Chong, her expression extremely nervous.

Meng Chong rubbed his head and asked, "When? Where?"

"Tonight!" The attendant gave the address and then respectfully departed.

"Meng Chong, you're going to meet her?" Zi Yun wrung the hem of her clothes with both hands.

"What are you afraid of? We'll go meet her tonight and that's it. That woman has some problems with her cultivation, we'll see if she has any treasures worth my guidance to offer up." Meng Chong chuckled and added, "You'll come with me to meet her tonight, maybe we can get some benefits."

Hearing this, Zi Yun immediately relaxed. Then she curiously asked, "What problems does she have with her cultivation?"

"You'll find out when we see her tonight. For now, tell me about what you've been up to these years."

"I spent most of my time in bitter cultivation..."

Zi Yun recounted her experiences, though there wasn't much to tell, since after coming to East Yang Martial Academy she had spent most of her time cultivating.

"Meng Chong, quickly tell me how you offended the Myriad Treasures Alliance, and how you went on a killing spree," Zi Yun said excitedly after finishing her own story, hanging off Meng Chong's body curiously.

"That's a long story..."

Meng Chong started from the Qinghua Realm, all the way to how he was framed by Heaven's Bane Earth's Shadow, how he was hunted by the Myriad Treasures Alliance, to breaking through and then slaughtering all the Myriad Treasures Alliance experts who came to hunt him.

The Azure Sea Realm. Past the wilderness was an endless sea, with waves crashing like thunder and occasional huge waves slapping against the shore.

Thousands of islands dotted the azure sea like stars. The islands were inhabited by humans, while the sea was the domain ruled by the Sea Spirit race. There was an unspoken understanding between them and they coexisted harmoniously.

In fact, there were even marriages between humans and Sea Spirits. Within the Azure Sea Realm, people with Sea Spirit bloodlines were not uncommon.

Cloud Sky Island was the largest island in the Azure Sea, as well as the most powerful force, ruling over the vast azure waters.

Among the thousands of islands in the Azure Sea, almost every island was home to a sect or clan. But strong islands ruled over weak ones, and Cloud Sky Island ruled over all the islands.

Cloud Sky Island was not exclusively human, but jointly governed by humans and Sea Spirits. They established the Azure Sea Elder Council, with every elder being an immortal heavenly venerable expert.

The Elder Council was also responsible for nurturing talented youths of the Azure Sea and mediating conflicts between various forces. Once the Elder Council made a decision, no one in the entire Azure Sea could or dared to defy it.

This meant it was the collective decision of all the immortal heavenly venerable experts in the Azure Sea Realm. No force or individual dared to challenge the will of all the immortal heavenly venerable experts of the Azure Sea Realm!

On the sea, there were large ships sailing towards Cloud Sky Island or trading between various islands. The shipping routes were mostly fixed and were the safest routes.

In the Azure Sea, besides Sea Spirits, there were naturally also sea beasts. But the sea beasts' strength was ultimately limited. There were no sea beasts that surpassed true king heavenly venerable experts, and they were all suppressed by the Sea Spirits.

However, the Azure Sea was not safe.

Because of the existence of sky rifts.

Within the Azure Sea Realm, there were three major sky rifts. The largest one was in the Azure Sea 100,000 miles from Cloud Sky Island, like a huge black abyss.

Besides the three major sky rifts, there were some other minor sky rifts, but they were not enough to pose a threat.

However, over the long years, with the sky rifts' periodic disturbances, no one knew how many otherworldly true spirits or enemies had escaped from the sky rifts and were lurking in the vast Azure Sea.

Even though immortal heavenly venerable experts would jointly patrol the Azure Sea at regular intervals, they still could not guarantee that they could root out and annihilate all the hidden foreign enemies.

Therefore, apart from the established routes, large ships would not easily venture into unfamiliar waters unless there were immortal heavenly venerable experts on board.

The realm gate of the Azure Sea Realm was not on Cloud Sky Island, but on a small island between the seventeen large islands near Cloud Sky Island.

The realm gate was at some distance from Cloud Sky Island and other islands. Even for an immortal heavenly venerable expert, it would take two quarter hours to reach it from Cloud Sky Island.

However, the realm gate was guarded by dozens of peak true king heavenly venerable experts and one immortal heavenly venerable expert sent by the Elder Council, rotating every ten years.

The Azure Sea Realm did not have very frequent exchanges with other realms. Usually only one person would cross the realm gate every three to five days.

As for martial artists from the Azure Sea Realm venturing to outer realms, they were relatively fewer.

On this day, figures crossed over the Azure Sea Realm gate one after another. They were all immortal heavenly venerable experts, but wore black robes enveloped in a special aura that blocked their aura as immortal heavenly venerable experts.

The Azure Sea Realm immortal heavenly venerable expert guarding the gate looked at the group, his gaze finally landing on one of them with a somewhat complex expression.

Finally he sighed and said, "I'm repaying the life-saving favor you did for me back then. You only have a quarter hour, otherwise people from Cloud Sky Island will surely come."

"A quarter hour is enough!" That person nodded.

The Azure Sea Realm immortal heavenly venerable expert waved his hand, wrapping up the already unconscious true king heavenly venerable experts and instantly disappearing to the nearest small island to the gate.

He gazed towards the waters where the realm gate was located, his brow slightly furrowed.

"Who are they ambushing? Twenty immortal heavenly venerable experts joining forces, that's quite a lineup. Could they be ambushing an expert above the immortal realm? But when did such an expert come from the divine realm?"

He was puzzled.

At the same time, he was also alarmed. "What kind of force is Heaven's Bane Earth's Shadow? Twenty immortal heavenly venerable experts came at once, truly incredibly powerful. And many of the immortal heavenly venerable experts seem unfamiliar."

At this moment, he seemed to think of something.

"Could they be from the Grand Unity Realm?"

He frowned, pondering something, his gaze heavy.

"If the celestial ladder of the Grand Unity Realm appeared, we would surely have sensed it. But we didn't sense any movement. Could it be that last time..."
