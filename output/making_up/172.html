Of course, perhaps the difficulty in breaking through the Penetrating Mysteries realm was even greater than breaking through to the grandmaster realm, which was yet to be known.

Xu Yan looked indifferently at the White Protector and said, "A peak great grandmaster, yet you hide and lurk, isn't that embarrassing? You don't even dare let people know the name of your faction, are you just a rat in the gutter?"

His gaze was extremely grave, his aura constantly increasing, a curved blade appearing in his hand.

Danger!

The White Protector sensed a strong sense of crisis from the youth.

Xu Yan sneered, "Today, I will slay you, this peak great grandmaster, just like killing a gutter rat!"

The gentle breeze transformed into sword intent, the mountains and rivers appearing, a golden dragon coiling within.

"Junior brother, leave those three to me!"

Xu Yan looked at the other three black-robed men.

"Brother, don't worry, I'll go slay them right away!"

Meng Chong grinned wickedly, completely ignoring the White Protector and rushing towards the other three.

After using a martial dao secret technique, that great grandmaster's aura had already become weaker, killing him would only take one or two strikes.

As for the other two, it would likewise only take one or two strikes.

The White Protector's expression changed slightly, but he dared not move an inch, staring intently at Xu Yan, not daring to be careless in the slightest.

The sense of crisis was growing stronger and stronger, the aura of death seemed to be surging into his chest.

This was a feeling he had never experienced since becoming a peak great grandmaster.

The White Protector said in a low voice.

"Three sword strikes! If my three sword strikes can't kill you, I'll spare your life!"

Xu Yan's body was surging with sword intent, a sword light flashing, the mountains and rivers appearing, as if the sky above the valley had transformed into a mountain and river landscape.

Even Sword Saint Cliff Xie Tianheng couldn't do it!
