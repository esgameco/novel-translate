The spirit realm was vast. Even with the combined efforts of transcendent spirit sects and the communication system built by the Eternal Alliance, it would still take considerable time to search the entire spirit realm.

Li Xuan was not in a hurry about this. The Changqing Pavilion flying ship remained in Hong Province, waiting for news from Xu Yan and the others.

"The spirit realm shouldn't have so many hidden dangers. There shouldn't be any more celestial caves," Li Xuan concluded in his mind.

However, conducting a search would ultimately be more reassuring.

Inside the Changqing Pavilion, Su Lingxiu was studying the Netherworld Blood Pearl.

The others were all diligently cultivating. Du Yuying, Yun Miaomiao and Zi Yun especially felt this way - the recent great battle had given them a sense that they were too useless. They had to strive to complete their cultivation as soon as possible, so that upon entering the divine realm, they could quickly prepare to break through to the Condensed Law Celestial Venerable realm.

Li Xuan was creating divine abilities, specifically eye techniques.

"Thousand Mile Eyes? The name's not strong enough. Heavenly Eyes? Illusion-Breaking Eyes? Void-Penetrating Divine Pupils?" Li Xuan muttered to himself as he pondered eye technique divine abilities. "Let's keep it simple - call it the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao. It can penetrate illusions and phantasms, captivate people's souls, observe the flow of time and space, and possess the might of the Heavenly Dao..."

Li Xuan's inspiration surged, and a powerful eye technique divine ability emerged.

Eyes of the Heavenly Dao!

"It's powerful, but not easy to create. And it would be difficult to comprehend... Let's consider the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao as an extremely powerful greater divine ability. But before perfecting it, I can create a reduced version of the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao.

"I'll call it the Lesser Eyes of the Heavenly Dao. It can serve as the entry requirement and foundation for cultivating the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao.

"That's the plan. First create the Lesser Eyes of the Heavenly Dao. Once it's comprehended and understood, it will be easier to create the true Eyes of the Heavenly Dao."

Li Xuan made his decision.

In his conception, while the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao was incredibly powerful, it would not be easy to create. Even if created, it would not be easy to comprehend.

This required an extremely high level of cultivation. Moreover, theoretically, the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao could not be used at low cultivation levels.

So, creating a foundational stage for cultivating the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao - the Lesser Eyes of the Heavenly Dao - would serve as a basis.

"Although the Lesser Eyes of the Heavenly Dao is not as powerful as the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao, it is still incredibly powerful at the current stage. The higher the cultivation level, the more powerful it becomes.

"Moreover, it's more suitable for Xu Yan to comprehend at his current level."

Li Xuan began creating the Lesser Eyes of the Heavenly Dao. Although he had created many martial arts techniques and divine abilities, gaining considerable experience, creating the Lesser Eyes of the Heavenly Dao was still not easy.

Simply making things up wouldn't work. There needed to be a theoretical framework, otherwise how could it be comprehended?

"Besides the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao, I should also create some other eye technique divine abilities. After all, there will be more and more people cultivating divine abilities in the future. The difficulty of cultivating the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao will certainly be extremely high, and only prodigious geniuses will be able to cultivate it.

"So, I should also create some weaker ones, or ones focused on specific abilities, such as killing enemies, seeing through illusions, confusing people's minds, and so on..."

Li Xuan began adding a new divine ability classification to the martial arts canon - Eye Technique Divine Abilities!

"There are myriad divine abilities, all created by me alone. No matter how much I create, it will be limited. So the most fundamental thing is to create a method for how to cultivate and comprehend divine abilities on one's own.

"This way, geniuses will be able to comprehend divine abilities on their own. As time accumulates, these martial arts divine abilities will inevitably become more numerous and varied."

Li Xuan suddenly realized that relying solely on himself to create divine abilities, no matter how hard he tried or racked his brains, would ultimately be limited.

Only by creating a path or method for cultivating and self-comprehending divine abilities could he allow future geniuses to comprehend on their own!

"I only need to create powerful divine abilities!"

At this moment, Li Xuan found his position in creating divine abilities.

While creating the Lesser Eyes of the Heavenly Dao, Li Xuan also began to ponder the fifth martial way. He already had some initial ideas for the fifth martial way.

However, it was not yet finalized, but he could think about and refine it to see if it was feasible.

Time passed as Li Xuan created the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao and pondered the fifth martial way. A month had already passed since the collapse of the divine bridge.

There was no movement from the divine realm, nor did any supreme beings come to repair the divine bridge.

It was unknown whether there were no supreme beings with such power, or if the Qinghua Realm in the divine realm was truly in chaos and had no time to attend to matters in the spirit realm.

"Master!"

Xu Yan and the others finally returned.

Li Xuan nodded. As he had expected, there were no other hidden dangers lurking in the spirit realm.

Wu Tiannan and other spirit realm powerhouses gradually returned to Hong Province, all waiting for the expert to take action, repair the divine bridge, and open the path to enter the divine realm.

Although the Qinghua Realm in the divine realm might be in chaos, it could not stop their determination to go to the divine realm.

Only by going to the divine realm could they reach higher levels of martial arts and witness the prosperity of martial arts in the divine realm.

"Since that's the case, let's go."

Li Xuan raised his hand and grabbed forward. With a rumbling sound, it was as if a hole had been torn open in midair! Rainbow light appeared again, and a bridge appeared before everyone's eyes.

The broken bridge seemed to be slowly recovering on its own.

A giant hand grasped the section of the bridge in Hong Province, like a chain, and flung it forward.

Boom!

The rainbow light spanned the azure sea, crossing it, as if reaching the other shore.

Hum! Suddenly, the divine bridge opened again.

Rainbow light swirled, and the divine bridge continuously solidified, appearing in midair, spanning the azure sea and reaching directly to the other shore.

The divine bridge was a rule of law, and not a strong one. It only had the ability to connect the spirit realm and the divine realm. With Li Xuan's current strength and understanding of the laws of heaven and earth, reconnecting the divine bridge was an easy task.

As the divine bridge appeared, everyone was excited and thrilled.

At the same time, they were grateful in their hearts that the spirit realm had an expert. Otherwise, it would surely have been a great disaster.

The spirit realm would likely have been destroyed!

The divine bridge was restored, but no powerful beings appeared on the divine realm side.

Logically, since the divine bridge had collapsed and was now restored, there should have been powerful beings from the divine realm coming to investigate.

But there weren't!

The hearts of the spirit realm powerhouses sank! Such a situation meant that the situation in the Qinghua Realm of the divine realm was not good.

"Let's go!"

Li Xuan said calmly.

The flying ship turned into a streak of light, landing on the divine bridge and heading towards the divine realm along the bridge.

Wu Tiannan took a deep breath and followed closely, stepping onto the divine bridge. His heart was both excited and a little nervous.

Was the person he was looking for really in the divine realm? Were they still alive?

All of this was unknown.

But no matter what, he would keep searching.

"Follow!"

The Emperor of Great Zhou and other supreme beings of the spirit realm looked at those geniuses and said sternly.

The group stepped onto the divine bridge. After stepping onto the bridge and walking for just a moment, the Hong Province end of the divine bridge began to dim and fade away.

Conveniently, the three major powers each took charge of guarding one Netherworld Abyss.

Open the border gate!

It was also an important channel for the 36 borders to seek help from each other.
