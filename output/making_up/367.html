The spiritual domain is vast and expansive. Even if the supreme Spiritual Sect Alliance and the communication system established by the Millennium Alliance were to work together, it would still take a considerable amount of time to thoroughly search the entire spiritual domain.

Li Xuan was not in a hurry about this. The Qingcheng Pavilion's flying ship remained stationed in Rainbow State, waiting for news from Xu Yan and the others.

"The spiritual domain should not have so many hidden dangers, there shouldn't be another Celestial Abyss."

Li Xuan made this judgment in his heart.

However, a thorough search would ultimately bring more peace of mind.

In the Qingcheng Pavilion, Su Ling Xiu was researching the Underworld Blood Pearl.

The rest were all diligently cultivating on their own. Du Yu Ying, Yun Miao Miao, and Zi Yun in particular felt a sense of inadequacy after the previous great battle.

They must strive to complete their cultivation as soon as possible, so that when they enter the Divine Realm, they can immediately set about preparing to break through to the Condensed Fa Venerable level.

Li Xuan was designing divine abilities, including eye techniques.

"Thousand-Mile Eyes? The name isn't strong enough. Heaven's Eyes? Eyes of Illusion Dispelling? Eyes of Void Perception?" Li Xuan murmured to himself, beginning to ponder eye technique divine abilities. "Let's keep it simple - call it the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao. It can penetrate illusions and phantoms, captivate the mind, perceive the flow of time and space, and possess the might of the Heavenly Dao..."

Li Xuan's inspiration surged, and a powerful eye technique divine ability emerged - the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao!

"It's powerful, but difficult to compose and comprehend... The Eyes of the Heavenly Dao shall be an extremely formidable great divine ability. But before perfecting it, I can compose a simplified version of the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao.

"Let's call it the Lesser Eyes of the Heavenly Dao. It can serve as the foundation for cultivating the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao. Once I've grasped and comprehended the Lesser Eyes of the Heavenly Dao, it will be easier to compose the true Eyes of the Heavenly Dao."

Li Xuan had made his decision. While the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao in his conception was supremely powerful, it was not easy to compose, and even after composing it, it would not be easy to comprehend. This required an extremely high realm, and theoretically, the lower realms would be unable to wield the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao.

Therefore, he would compose the Lesser Eyes of the Heavenly Dao as a foundational cultivation for the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao.

"The Lesser Eyes of the Heavenly Dao, while not as powerful as the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao, is still extremely formidable at this stage. The higher the realm, the greater its power.

"Moreover, it is more suitable for Xu Yan's current realm to comprehend."

Li Xuan began composing the Lesser Eyes of the Heavenly Dao. Although he had already composed many martial arts and divine abilities, and had gained much experience, composing the Lesser Eyes of the Heavenly Dao was still not easy.

Blind composition would not work - there had to be a theoretical framework, otherwise how could it be comprehended?

"In addition to the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao, I should also compose other eye technique divine abilities. In the future, there will be more and more people cultivating divine abilities, and the cultivation difficulty of the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao will undoubtedly be extremely high, only monstrous geniuses would be able to cultivate it.

"So I should also compose some weaker eye technique divine abilities, or those focused on specific abilities, such as killing enemies, perceiving illusions, or bewildering the mind..."

Li Xuan began adding a new divine ability category, eye technique divine abilities, to the martial arts canon.

"Divine abilities are countless, and it all depends on my own composition. No matter how hard I try, there will always be limitations. The most fundamental thing is to compose a set of methods for cultivating and comprehending divine abilities.

"In this way, talented cultivators will be able to comprehend divine abilities on their own. As time accumulates, these martial arts and divine abilities will become more and more numerous and diverse."

Li Xuan suddenly realized that relying solely on himself to compose divine abilities, no matter how hard he tried or how much he racked his brain, would ultimately be limited.

The key was to compose a path for cultivating and comprehending divine abilities, allowing future geniuses to comprehend them on their own!

"I only need to compose powerful divine abilities!"

At this moment, Li Xuan had found his positioning for composing divine abilities.

While composing the Lesser Eyes of the Heavenly Dao, Li Xuan also began pondering the fifth martial art. He already had some preliminary ideas for the fifth martial art, he just needed to refine and see if it was feasible.

Time passed as Li Xuan composed the Eyes of the Heavenly Dao and pondered the fifth martial art. It had been a month since the collapse of the Divine Bridge.

On the Divine Realm side, there had been no movement, and no powerful experts had come to repair the Divine Bridge.

It was unclear whether there were no experts with such power, or if the Divine Realm's Qinghua Realm was truly in great turmoil, leaving them no time to attend to the affairs of the Spiritual Realm.

"Master!"

Xu Yan and the others finally returned.

Li Xuan nodded, as he had expected, the Spiritual Realm did not harbor any other hidden dangers.

The powerful experts of the Spiritual Realm, such as Wu Tian Nan, had gradually returned to Rainbow State, all waiting for a powerful figure to repair the Divine Bridge and open the way to the Divine Realm.

Although the Qinghua Realm of the Divine Realm might be in great turmoil, it could not stop their determination to enter the Divine Realm.

Only by entering the Divine Realm could they reach higher martial dao realms and witness the flourishing martial arts of the Divine Realm.

"Since that is the case, then let's go."

Li Xuan raised his hand and made a grasping motion. With a thunderous sound, a rift appeared in the sky, and a rainbow bridge emerged before their eyes.

The broken bridge seemed to be slowly repairing itself. A giant hand gripped the Rainbow State end of the bridge, like a chain, and flung it forward.

Boom!

The rainbow light spanned the azure sea, reaching the other shore.

Hum! Suddenly, the Divine Bridge opened again.

The rainbow light gathered and solidified, appearing in the sky, spanning the azure sea and reaching the other shore.

The Divine Bridge was a law, and not a powerful one, it merely possessed the ability to connect the Spiritual Realm and the Divine Realm. With Li Xuan's current strength and understanding of the laws of heaven and earth, reconnecting the Divine Bridge was an easy task.

As the Divine Bridge appeared, everyone was excited and elated. They were also relieved, grateful that there was a powerful figure in the Spiritual Realm, otherwise it would have been a great calamity, and the Spiritual Realm might have been destroyed.

The Divine Bridge had been restored, but no powerful experts had appeared from the Divine Realm side.

Logically speaking, with the Divine Bridge having collapsed and now restored, the Divine Realm should have sent experts to investigate.

But there was nothing.

The powerful experts of the Spiritual Realm's hearts sank. This situation meant that the situation in the Divine Realm's Qinghua Realm was not good.

"Let's go," Li Xuan said calmly.

The flying ship transformed into a beam of light and landed on the Divine Bridge, heading towards the Divine Realm.

Wu Tian Nan took a deep breath and stepped onto the Divine Bridge, following closely. He was both excited and apprehensive in his heart.

Would the person he was looking for really be in the Divine Realm? Would they still be alive?

All of this was unknown.

But no matter what, he would keep searching.

"Follow me," the Great Zhou Emperor and other powerful experts of the Spiritual Realm said solemnly to the young geniuses.

The group stepped onto the Divine Bridge, and after walking for only a moment, the Divine Bridge on the Rainbow State side began to dim and fade away.
