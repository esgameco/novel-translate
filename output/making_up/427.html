Although Hong Ting had retreated under the Hundred-Eyed Evil Light of the Fallen Witch, he must have been affected, with his strength greatly reduced. It was the perfect time for an ambush.

Silently, he left the Fallen Witch Heavenly Cave to notify the assassins who had already been arranged, to find an opportunity to strike.

Boom!

In a city in the Long Cloud Realm, a branch of the Myriad Treasures Alliance suddenly collapsed.

A god-like figure, with a blade coiling with wind and thunder, instantly cleaved open the treasury. With a wave of his hand, he swept clean all the stored treasures inside.

Many martial artists were dumbfounded.

Too fierce, too bold - who was this formidable person who directly robbed a branch of the Myriad Treasures Alliance?

"Meng Chong, you're seeking death!"

An angry roar came from the sky.

Only then did the crowd realize that this was the fierce man hunted by the Myriad Treasures Alliance, the one called the God of War, Meng Chong! No wonder he was so bold.

"I'm a bit short on treasures, so I came to your Myriad Treasures Alliance to gather some. Not too much, right?"

Meng Chong sneered, his figure soaring into the sky and instantly disappearing into the distance.

"Where do you think you're going!"

The Immortal Venerable of the Myriad Treasures Alliance, seeing this branch turned into ruins, his eyes turned red.

As an Immortal Venerable responsible for hunting down Meng Chong, not only had he failed to kill him, but the other party had found an opportunity to destroy a branch. What a great shame and humiliation!

Where was the dignity of a mighty Immortal Venerable?

"You can say that if you can catch up."

Meng Chong sneered disdainfully.

His figure suddenly accelerated, disappearing from the Immortal Venerable's sight in an instant.

Changing his appearance and putting on a wig, he transformed from a burly brute into a tall, slender youth.

Meng Chong was already extremely skilled at this.

His aura also changed immediately. He swaggered into a city and began to scout out branches of the Myriad Treasures Alliance.

Since they were already at odds, there was no need to be polite anymore. He would make a big score, then leave the Long Cloud Realm and continue roaming the Divine Realm.

Accumulate enough treasures to build up his foundation, then break through and transform.

"Hm?"

Suddenly, Meng Chong overheard a group of martial artists discussing.

"Hong Ting of the Hundred Battles Hall is dead. They say he was killed by someone called Xu Yan."

"I heard that too. Hong Ting was hit by the Hundred-Eyed Evil Light in the Fallen Witch Heavenly Cave, his strength greatly reduced, which allowed Xu Yan to take advantage and kill him."

"This person is so bold, daring to assassinate a prodigy of the Hundred Battles Hall. You should know that Hong Ting was one of the Immortal Realm candidates of the Hundred Battles Hall, highly valued."

"Not only that, I heard Hong Ting's father is one of the top Immortal Venerables of the Hundred Battles Hall."

Hearing these discussions, Meng Chong raised an eyebrow.

"How could Big Senior Brother kill someone by taking advantage of their weakness? Moreover, Big Senior Brother never provokes others unprovoked. It seems this is another frame-up by the Heavenly Fiends and Earthly Shadows?"

Meng Chong's face darkened. These Heavenly Fiends and Earthly Shadows were truly persistent, and deeply hidden, with extremely deep plans. It wouldn't be easy to uproot them completely.

"The Hundred Battles Hall has already issued a challenge, asking Xu Yan to come to the Fallen Witch Heavenly Cave for a duel, life or death, to settle the grudge."

"This is really the style of the Hundred Battles Hall."

"Heh, they say this time an Immortal Venerable expert from the Hundred Battles Hall will take action. Xu Yan is definitely dead."

"Hmm, hasn't the Hundred Battles Hall always insisted on duels between those of the same realm? This time is an exception?"

"You don't understand. Hong Ting had a special status as an Immortal Realm candidate. And it's said that as long as Xu Yan can withstand ten moves from the Immortal Venerable of the Hundred Battles Hall, the grudge will be considered settled."

"That's an Immortal Venerable of the Hundred Battles Hall, you should understand. Forget ten moves, Xu Yan won't even be able to withstand one move. He'll certainly die!"

"What if Xu Yan doesn't accept the challenge?"

"Heh, if he doesn't accept, it'll be even worse. The Hundred Battles Hall will stop following the rules then. You should have heard what happened to someone who was too cowardly to accept a challenge before."

Hearing the discussions of the crowd, Meng Chong frowned. This Hundred Battles Hall seemed extremely powerful, even more so than the Myriad Treasures Alliance? Moreover, Hong Ting was an Immortal Realm candidate of the Hundred Battles Hall, and his father was a top Immortal Venerable of the Hundred Battles Hall. How could they let this go?

"I should contact Big Senior Brother about how to handle this Hundred Battles Hall matter."

Meng Chong took out a communication talisman and sent a message to his Big Senior Brother.

"Hundred Battles Hall? Hong Ting?"

Xu Yan had just come out of seclusion, preparing to go to the Fallen Witch Heavenly Cave. Seeing Meng Chong's message, he was stunned.

"Second Junior Brother, don't worry. It's just a small matter. It's not so easy to impersonate me to kill someone. Since the Hundred Battles Hall wants a duel, then let's have one."

"As soon as I make my move, they'll naturally know I didn't kill the person. If they still won't let it go even knowing I didn't kill him, then don't blame me for being merciless."

Xu Yan didn't take this seriously.

The Hundred Battles Hall was strong, but so what? After ending the communication with Meng Chong, Xu Yan continued towards the Fallen Witch Heavenly Cave. The martial artists of the Hundred Battles Hall had set the duel location outside the Fallen Witch Heavenly Cave.

"The Heavenly Fiends and Earthly Shadows are truly persistent. Since that's the case, let's have a contest then."

Xu Yan's gaze turned cold.

The prerequisite for counterattacking the Heavenly Fiends and Earthly Shadows was to find their people, control them with the Heaven's Will Divine Technique, and then use this to continuously expand his personnel within the Heavenly Fiends and Earthly Shadows.

Just like in the Nine Mountains Realm's Big Lid Mountain, where he ultimately destroyed the branch of the Heavenly Fiends and Earthly Shadows.

"Let's see what tricks you Heavenly Fiends and Earthly Shadows have up your sleeves."

Xu Yan pondered for a moment. If this trip went smoothly, the Hundred Battles Hall should also take action against the Heavenly Fiends and Earthly Shadows. If the Hundred Battles Hall insisted on believing he killed the person, then he would have to use some methods.

"I hope the Fallen Witch Heavenly Cave won't disappoint me. Mei Wu, you must remember to come find me!"

Xu Yan had an expectant look on his face.

Outside the Fallen Witch Heavenly Cave, the crowd looked at the people from the Hundred Battles Hall, shocked in their hearts.

Hong Ting had actually been killed! The one who did it was a prodigy called Xu Yan.

He had taken advantage of Hong Ting's weakness to kill him. Did he rely on a strong background, unafraid of the Hundred Battles Hall's revenge?

Outside the Fallen Witch Heavenly Cave, several men stood proudly, their battle intent blazing.

One of them held a spiked mace in his hand, his battle intent soaring to the heavens, seemingly turning this area into a battlefield filled with surging battle intent.

An Immortal Venerable of the Hundred Battles Hall, and not just an early-stage Immortal Realm one.

Even the Immortal Venerables guarding the Fallen Witch Heavenly Cave didn't show their faces now, afraid of being misunderstood as having something to do with Hong Ting's death.

"We'll wait one more day. If Xu Yan doesn't come, it means he has no intention of settling the grudge. Then don't blame my Hundred Battles Hall for bullying him!"

Luo Zhan said coldly.

His aura erupted, each spike on the mace in his hand radiating a sharp intent.

"Who is this Xu Yan? Which faction's prodigy?"

Not far from the Tuowu Heavenly Cave, in a temporarily built shelter, Xu Yan was quite surprised to see Hong Ting, the Hundred Battles Hall genius who was rumored to have been killed by a single sword strike to the throat.

"Hong Ting used a secret technique to fake death and escape, but he was also severely injured and remains in a coma," Luo Zhan said solemnly.

Xu Yan understood somewhat. Luo Zhan bringing him here was actually to confirm one last time whether he was truly the killer or not.

At this moment, Xu Yan sighed inwardly that his master's teachings were indeed great wisdom.

When killing enemies, one must crush their bones, scatter their ashes, and destroy their souls, giving the enemy not the slightest chance to escape! Tiansha Diying probably never expected that Hong Ting would have such a secret technique, thus escaping death. "That's right!" Luo Zhan nodded.

Xu Yan pondered briefly, then took out a medicinal pill and said: "I can wake him up, but I can't do anything about his injuries."

Seeing the Tuowu force in Hong Ting's body, Xu Yan was secretly alarmed. This strange force couldn't be underestimated. Once it invaded the body, it was extremely troublesome.

This was a strange force that directly eroded the will, making people degenerate and lazy.
