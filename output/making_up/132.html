Boom! The man in the black robe struck out, but the icy light did not dissipate. Instead, it transformed into countless sparks, enveloping him from all directions. "I've underestimated you!"

"Meng Shushu, are you seeking death?"

He quickly retrieved the long pen from Tian Jin's corpse, and while the two sect masters were hesitating, unwilling to make a move, he leapt into the depths of the mountain forest.

The man in the black robe's expression was icy cold, glancing at the other two sect masters, angrily shouting, "Don't just stand there, chase him! Don't destroy those two items, they are the keys to the treasure land!"

"No wonder he betrayed his friend, it turns out he was a poor sect master!"

Meng Chong despised Tian Jin.

Pocketing the spirit talismans, his figure moved, heading towards the direction Meng Shushu had fled.

He saw Meng Shushu in the distance, already nearly exhausted, while the three sect masters had formed a surrounding formation.

Meng Chong scratched his head, with the three-sided encirclement, Meng Shushu could only flee in one direction, and he wouldn't be able to escape far.

So, he went to the path Meng Shushu was most likely to take, sitting down on a large tree, waiting for Meng Shushu to arrive.

Soon, Meng Shushu came in a disheveled state, panting heavily, his face deathly pale, his injuries worsening.

"I can't escape this time!"

Meng Shushu felt his entire body weak, his breathing erratic, barely able to hold on.

Boom! The man in the black robe stepped forward, the other two sect masters also closing in to surround him.

"Meng Shushu, hand over the keys, surrender, or die!"

The man in the black robe spoke coldly.

"Forget it!"

Meng Shushu gritted his teeth, angrily saying, "You despicable people, you promised that I would explore the spirit herb treasure land for you, but not only did you not pay the agreed reward, you even want to control me to serve you. I, Meng Shushu, will never be your dog!"

He inserted the long pen into the ground, holding the treasure land keys in his hand, coldly saying, "Even if I die, you will never get the spirit herbs from the treasure land!"

He seemed ready to destroy the keys.

The man in the black robe, however, let out a cold laugh, saying, "Meng Shushu, how much strength do you have left? With your current abilities, can you destroy the keys?"

He stepped forward, his expression suddenly turning cold and fierce, looking towards a large tree.

"Who's there?"

Meng Shushu was startled, but remained unmoved, not turning to look at the direction the man in the black robe was shouting, still staring intently at him, inwardly scoffing, such a small trick would not fool him.

However, a familiar voice rang out, "Hmm, family member, it seems you're being chased? You look quite miserable!"

Meng Chong!

Meng Shushu's eyes welled up with tears of excitement, turning to look, and sure enough, he saw Meng Chong sitting on the large tree, with a curious expression.

"Please save me, brother Meng!"

Meng Chong had a hesitant look on his face, "Family member, your enemies seem quite formidable, this..."

"Two sixth-grade spirit herbs!" Meng Shushu hurriedly spoke.

Nothing was more important than his life, two sixth-grade spirit herbs, although rare, could still be found!

"What are you talking about spirit herbs? Your life, how can it be compared to spirit herbs? Since you're in trouble, I naturally won't just stand by and watch!"

Meng Chong spoke solemnly.

Two sixth-grade spirit herbs, just to settle the score, what was he thinking?

His figure moved, landing in front of Meng Shushu.

"Gentlemen, do me a favor and let this matter go, how about it?"
