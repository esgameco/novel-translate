Xu Yan walked among the high mountains and steep ridges of Dachuan, organizing his insights into the martial dao of the Shen Xiang realm while leaving Dachuan State.

"I wonder if my second junior brother is still in Dachuan State?"

Meng Chong had also come to Dachuan before, and the two had met once.

"Oh right, where did Xie Gong go?"

Xu Yan thought of Xie Lingfeng, since entering the Spirit Realm, there had been no news of Xie Lingfeng.

"After all these years, has Xie Gong already broken through to the Shen Yi realm, or even perfected the Shen Yi realm?"

Xu Yan pondered, after Xie Lingfeng broke through and perfected the Shen Yi realm, there were no more subsequent cultivation methods.

"Since I'm traveling the Spirit Realm, I might as well look for news of Xie Gong as well, and pass on the martial dao methods of the Shen Yuan and Shen Tong realms to him."

Xu Yan pondered for a moment, the next destination would be Yunshan State!

Tianling State, known as the State of Spirit Beasts.

Compared to Dachuan State, the mountains in Tianling State are higher, the forests denser, and the mountain ranges more continuous, with countless caves and underground rivers.

It is precisely because of Tianling State's unique terrain that it has become the State of Spirit Beasts, with 80% of the Spirit Realm's spirit beasts gathered in Tianling State.

In the central mountain range of Tianling State, ancient trees soar to the sky, and strange peaks are arrayed, this is the core area of the spirit beast clan, and also the territory of the super sixth-grade spirit beasts.

Few people dare to venture here, even the strongest of the Yuling Mansion would not easily set foot in this place.

Among the mountains, on the highest peak, a huge palace has been built, its golden roof shining brilliantly in the sunlight.

Upon closer inspection, one would find that this huge palace was carved out of a massive stone peak, and a rare golden material was poured onto the roof.

Surrounding the palace, various precious gems were inlaid, displaying an extravagant appearance.

On the left and right sides of the huge palace, there are two smaller palaces, each only one-third the size of the central one, as if guarding the huge palace in the middle.

Whoosh! A spirit beast flew from the mountains and landed in front of the huge palace, and its originally tens-of-meter-tall body shrank to about three meters before entering the palace.

This is a super sixth-grade spirit beast, its strength comparable to a cultivator who has refined the Heavenly Earth Spirit Mechanism.

Over the next few hours, more super sixth-grade spirit beasts came from all directions, and each one shrank its body before entering the palace.

On this day, all the overlord spirit beasts of Tianling State, all the super sixth-grade spirit beasts, gathered and entered this huge palace.

The spirit beasts that entered the palace were arranged in order of strength, either squatting or lying down, all facing upwards.

Above the hall, there were three chairs, one large and two small.

The large central chair was entirely golden, emanating a golden glow, and sitting on the huge chair was a mighty tiger with a large golden chain around its neck, exuding an awe-inspiring aura.

The mighty tiger sat upright on the chair, its paws resting on the armrests, looking down imposingly at the spirit beasts arranged below.

On the chair to the left of the mighty tiger, a jade-white dragon was coiled up, its head slightly raised, looking at the arranged spirit beasts below.

On the chair to the right, a toad was squatting, although much smaller than the mighty tiger, it still resembled a small mountain.

The one in the central chair was naturally the Red Cat.

On the left and right were Jade Little Dragon and Little Ha, since coming to Tianling State to roam, the Red Cat, as a cultural tiger, has been well-connected among the spirit beast clan.

When it was still weak, it gathered powerful backing to lay a foundation for its own growth.

Along the way, the Red Cat has now become the king of the spirit beast clan in Tianling State!

Its strength is sufficient to intimidate all the super sixth-grade spirit beasts, the super sixth-grade spirit beasts present are all its defeated opponents, all subdued by it.

Jade Little Dragon cultivated the Great Demon Technique, and has already broken through the shackles of its bloodline, transforming into a dragon, and its strength is now no weaker than a super sixth-grade spirit beast.

Little Ha, this Swallowing Mountain Toad, can almost be said to be the strongest Swallowing Mountain Toad in the history of the Spirit Realm, its strength comparable to a super sixth-grade spirit beast.

Its devouring ability, with its huge spatial power in its belly, has been unleashed, defeating many other super sixth-grade spirit beasts.

It has finally established a foothold in Tianling State.

The Red Cat is the Great Demon King, Jade Little Dragon is the Second Demon King, and Little Ha is the Third Demon King.

"We don't know why the Great King has summoned us, what are your orders?"

A super spirit beast spoke up from below.

"Today, I have summoned you all to announce a great matter, concerning the future and the rise of our spirit beast clan."

The Red Cat's divine sense fluctuated, issuing a mighty voice, fully displaying its aura as the Great Demon King.

The spirit beasts all perked up, not knowing what this king was going to order.

The Red Cat's eyes flashed with brilliance, and a golden light surged from its body, making it even more majestic and awe-inspiring, a powerful demonic aura shaking the hall.

The spirit beasts all felt a chill in their hearts, sensing this pressure, and an urge to worship arose within them, and this pressure continued to grow stronger.

This meant that the Great King's strength had increased once again.

"The spirit beast clan shall cease to exist, from today on, I shall establish the Great Demon Clan, we are demons, not beasts, from today on, Tianling State shall be the territory of the Demon Clan, and I am the Great Demon King."

The Red Cat's mighty voice echoed through the hall.

Demon Clan?! All the spirit beasts were stunned.

One of the spirit beasts spoke up, "Great King, we are originally spirit beasts, how can we forget our ancestors? This won't do!"

Slap! The Red Cat glared, its demonic aura surging, and Jade Little Dragon swept its tail, slapping that spirit beast and sending it flying, crashing to the ground.

"Insolent! Did the Great King allow you to speak?"

Jade Little Dragon thundered.

They had already cooperated tacitly, and the moment that spirit beast dared to question, they immediately took action to knock it away.

The Red Cat looked down at the spirit beasts below, coldly saying, "Forget your ancestors? Are you willing to be trapped in your bloodline, unable to make any progress, forever unable to surpass your ancestors?

"Moreover, were not your ancestors also just beasts? Are you willing to sink down into depravity?"

"I am the Great Demon King, establishing the Demon Clan, naturally I will pass down the Great Demon Techniques, break through the shackles of the bloodline, and walk the great path that belongs to our Demon Clan."
