Chapter 79: Xu Yan's Breakthrough, Meng Chong's Entry into the Martial Dao

Li Xuan stood up, his expression solemn, and said, "My disciple, your sword heart is clear, but that is only the foundation of the sword dao. You still have a long way to go, for the sword dao is endless, so do not be impatient!"

Xu Yan's expression became serious, and he took a deep breath, respectfully saying, "Yes, Master. I will certainly calm my mind and steadfastly advance on the path of the sword dao."

"Good!" Li Xuan nodded with a look of contentment. "Now that your sword heart is clear and you have entered the door of the sword dao, I shall point out the direction of the sword dao for you today."

Xu Yan listened attentively, his heart filled with excitement.

"After the sword heart, the second realm of the sword dao is the Heart Sword realm. 'My heart has a sword, and all things in heaven and earth can be my sword. When I encounter a mountain, the mountain becomes my sword; when I encounter rivers and lakes, they all become my swords... Even a single blade of grass or a tree can be my sword. The mountain has the might of the sword, the water has the might of the sword... All things in heaven and earth are the swords of my heart.'"

The second realm of the sword dao is the Heart Sword realm! Xu Yan's heart was stirred. All things in heaven and earth can be his sword? Mountains can be his sword, and rivers can be his sword as well? This was truly profound. In this moment, he felt a sense of his own insignificance.

The Heart Sword realm was so distant from him. What kind of sword dao realm was that?

Involuntarily, images emerged in his mind of raising his hand and summoning a great mountain to become a sword that would sweep away his enemies.

"My disciple, to break through to the Heart Sword realm, you must first comprehend the essence of the sword," Li Xuan said with a slight smile, seeing Xu Yan's flushed face and eager, passionate gaze.

Sounds quite profound, doesn't it? But the more profound it is, the more motivated Xu Yan becomes. He will never doubt that such profound things can be achieved. He will simply charge forward towards this goal, like a rooster that has been fed some stimulant.

Truly a good disciple! Li Xuan sighed, thinking that taking Xu Yan as his disciple in this lifetime was the best decision he had ever made.

"Yes, Master!" Xu Yan respectfully nodded.

The essence of the sword! Comprehending the essence of the sword!

"All things in heaven and earth can be used to comprehend the essence of the sword, but you must remember that when cultivating realms, attachment is the greatest enemy. Too much attachment will trap you in a cage and prevent you from truly comprehending the essence.

"And you must not blindly try to comprehend the essence of the sword, neglecting your own cultivation. Since your sword heart is clear, comprehending the essence of the sword is only a matter of time. Perhaps one day, while cultivating the Descending Dragon Palm, you will suddenly comprehend it.

"The essence of the sword is not something that can only be comprehended during sword cultivation," Li Xuan patiently instructed.

He couldn't let his disciple neglect the cultivation of the Descending Dragon Palm in his pursuit of the sword dao. Mastering both the palm and the sword was Xu Yan's true path.

"If you comprehend the essence of the Descending Dragon Palm, you may be able to apply it to the sword dao as well. And conversely, if you comprehend the essence of the sword, you may also be able to understand the Descending Dragon Palm. How much you can comprehend depends on you, my disciple."

Li Xuan pondered, thinking that he should also have Xu Yan try to comprehend the essence of the Descending Dragon Palm. If he truly grasped it, his strength would surely soar.

"I understand, Master," Xu Yan responded respectfully.

"Good, continue your cultivation." Li Xuan sat back down.

He would teach Xu Yan the realms beyond the Heart Sword realm after Xu Yan had mastered that realm. Passing on too much at once might hinder his disciple's comprehension. It was better to take it step by step, firmly grounded.

"Yes, Master!" Xu Yan nodded.

Li Xuan glanced at Meng Chong and said, "Your shidi (younger martial brother) has encountered a bottleneck. Go and spar with him, to stimulate his qi and blood and help him break through the bottleneck."

Xu Yan was momentarily taken aback, then turned to look at Meng Chong, understanding immediately.

When Xu Yan had broken through his own Gold Bone bottleneck and successfully tempered his Jade Bone, it was with the help of the Fire-Maned Wolf.

Meng Chong cultivated the Flesh Dao, and was stuck at the peak of the Gold Bone realm, unable to further temper his Gold Diamond Vajra Bone. He needed external force to refine his flesh, stimulate his qi and blood, and break through the bottleneck.

"Yes, Master," Xu Yan nodded.

Meng Chong cultivated the Flesh Dao, and to break through his bottleneck, the best method was external refinement to stimulate his potential and break open the bottleneck. The method was actually quite simple.

Of course, it depended on one's aptitude. If the aptitude was not good enough, no matter how much refinement and extraction, it would only end up harming one's foundation, and not break through the bottleneck.

Meng Chong's flesh aptitude was clearly very high, so he would not suffer any backlash. He would only have his potential ignited and his qi and blood boiling in the refining and extraction, breaking through the bottleneck and tempering his Gold Diamond Vajra Bone.

Previously, when Meng Chong broke through the Copper Bone bottleneck, it was because he was stimulated by Blood Wuxin's cold inner qi, igniting his potential and allowing him to break through.

"Shidi, come, let's spar," Xu Yan beckoned to Meng Chong.

"Okay!" Meng Chong nodded.

The two of them left the city of Yunshan County and went to the mountains outside to spar.

Ostensibly it was sparring, but in reality Xu Yan was refining Meng Chong's flesh, stimulating his potential to break through the bottleneck.

In the mountains outside Yunshan County.

Booming sounds rang out.

Xu Yan's qi and blood surged, and he struck out palm after palm, the scorching qi and blood continuously pounding against Meng Chong.

Meng Chong activated the Great Sun Golden Bell Armor, his flesh like a golden bell, emitting a humming sound as the qi and blood struck it, his entire body trembling.

"Not enough, make it stronger," Meng Chong said, flexing his muscles.

"Okay!" Xu Yan's brow furrowed. His shidi's flesh was truly formidable, worthy of the Flesh Dao.

Boom!

The qi and blood increased, enveloping Meng Chong.

After half an incense stick's time, Meng Chong finally couldn't hold on any longer, panting heavily, his entire body flushed red, his muscles trembling, his bones making slight sounds.

Refining the flesh, extracting potential, and breaking through a bottleneck was not something that could be accomplished in one or two tries.

"Shidi, that's enough for today, we'll continue tomorrow," Xu Yan said, withdrawing his hand.

"Okay, thank you, Shixiong (elder martial brother)!" Meng Chong replied.

The two returned to the courtyard, and Li Xuan glanced at them, silently nodding.

Meng Chong had not yet broken through the bottleneck and entered the tempering of the Gold Diamond Vajra Bone stage, but he had made some progress, the bottleneck loosening a bit.

Over the next few days, Meng Chong underwent Xu Yan's qi and blood pounding, but he still fell just short of breaking through the bottleneck.

Li Xuan thought that if they continued this method, Meng Chong would eventually break through, but it would take a long time.

So when Meng Chong returned from another round of flesh refinement, the bottleneck had loosened further, but still lacked that final push to break through completely. He needed a little stimulation.

Li Xuan took out a spirit crystal, recalling the method of absorbing essence blood from the Blood Demon Classic, and slightly adjusted it to absorb the spiritual energy from the spirit crystal using his true qi.

For Li Xuan's current strength, this was not a difficult task at all.

A wisp of spiritual energy was extracted from the spirit crystal, and Li Xuan flicked his finger, sending it into Meng Chong's body.

Sizzle!

In that instant, Meng Chong's qi and blood began to boil, as if stimulated, or as if receiving a supplement. Rumbling sounds echoed within his body.

Meng Chong was overjoyed.

He hastily circulated his qi, beginning to cultivate the Great Sun Golden Bell Armor.
