Li Xuan shook his head, this fat tiger Red Cat was getting smarter and smarter.

"It's time to compile the Qimen Martial Arts."

A new martial art should be compiled.

With the Heaven and Earth Principles from the Taicangshu as a foundation, compiling formations, arrays, and other Qimen techniques, although not easy, at least there was a reference.

"Qimen Martial Arts, lies in the 'qi', in the sword going against the grain..."

In Li Xuan's mind, the dragon veins of heaven and earth, the overall situation of the terrain, the array formations and prohibitions emerged.

Sitting in the chair, he opened the Daojin Book and began to write the initial techniques of the Qimen Martial Arts into it, receiving feedback from the Daojin Book.

Writing while extracting what was needed, writing down a Heavenly Principle.

He could already skillfully write the Heavenly Principle on the first page of the Taicangshu, but the principles on the subsequent pages, he could not yet do so as skillfully.

But it was enough for him to compile the early realms and techniques of the Qimen Martial Arts.

Before going to the Spirit Realm, he should also tour the Inner Domain, otherwise wouldn't it be a wasted trip? And the Qimen Martial Arts he compiled also need a suitable disciple to comprehend it, the Inner Domain is so vast, perhaps he can find the right person.

With Xu Yan and Meng Chong on Canglang Island, even if a Heavenly Demon were to come, it would only be a one-way trip.

The strength of the Lesser Heavenly Demons is just that.

Losing the advantage of the Heavenly Demon's divine might, where else can they be strong?

Thinking and doing, Li Xuan was originally going to leave riding the Red Cat pet, but found this fat tiger lying at Xu's mother's feet, acting cute and flattering, without a trace of the majesty of a great demon.

"Forget it, riding this big cat would be too eye-catching."

Li Xuan abandoned the idea and stepped out, leaving Canglang Island.

On the mountain peak, Xu Yan saw his master walking leisurely on the Cangjiang River, a jade talisman in his hand, one hand behind his back.

Master has gone out to wander the world.

"I must hurry and improve my strength, so that Master doesn't have to silently protect my family for me."

Xu Yan's gaze was determined.

"Heart Sword Realm, using all things as the sword, and Shenyi Realm, where the divine will goes, is the will of heaven... Divine Element, Divine Abilities..."

In his comprehension, Xu Yan was gradually gaining some insight.

He felt that he was about to comprehend how to cultivate to the Heart Sword Realm.

Meng Chong was perfecting himself, comprehending the Dao of the Sword.

The sword soul was growing stronger, the long sword resting on his knees slightly trembling, as if resonating with him.

The sword intent flowed, the domineering sword intent seemed to have become as light as the wind.

It would probably require an extremely long time.

But cultivating sword techniques, using them as a foundation to then enter the Sword Dao, would be much easier.

It's like breaking through one realm after another.

Moreover, after cultivating sword techniques, although not as powerful as sword intent, the strength would still increase greatly.

Zi Yun, a genius on par with him, had actually broken through to Great Grandmaster before him.

The Great Grandmasters on the Sword Saint Cliff had also started cultivating sword techniques day and night, this was the next level of the Sword Dao for the Sword Saint Cliff.

And the Sword Master Xie Tiangheng, after Xie Lingfeng explained the Sword Dao, also fell into contemplation.

"Sword Heart Enlightenment? Sword Intent?"

Standing on the peak of the Sword Saint Cliff, overlooking the sea of clouds, Xie Tiangheng's expression was calm, no longer showing dejection or having suffered setbacks.

Someone who could comprehend the Sword Technique Dao, unprecedented in history, would not be easily discouraged.

"Does the Spirit Realm have this Sword Dao as well?"

Xie Tiangheng muttered to himself.

Most likely not, they don't even have the Sword Technique Dao!

This is precisely why Xie Tiangheng was so arrogant, not taking the world lightly.

Because he had pioneered an unprecedented Sword Dao! But the trip to Canglang Island made him realize his own insignificance.

He had not even entered the Sword Dao!

Xie Tiangheng took out a pink jade bracelet, silently gazing at it, a flash of memories in his eyes, then took a deep breath and murmured, "I, Xie Tiangheng, have never submitted to anyone in my life, and if I say I will go find you, then I will!"

Putting away the jade bracelet, he looked back towards where Xie Lingfeng was, a doting look in his eyes.

Although his son was not as good as him, he was still an unparalleled genius in the Sword Dao.

"Sword Heart Enlightenment, Sword Intent, I must comprehend them!"

Xie Tiangheng's gaze was determined, if his son had comprehended them while he had not, wouldn't that be embarrassing?

Li Xuan was walking in the Inner Domain, appreciating the customs and people, experiencing the turmoil and waves of the martial arts world, while also looking for a suitable disciple.

He was also compiling the Qimen Martial Arts techniques.

"I should go take a look at the Sword Saint Cliff."

He thought of Xie Tiangheng, wondering if he had recovered from his setback.

Undeniably, Xie Tiangheng's talent was indeed formidable, to have actually comprehended the Sword Technique.

And using the Sword Technique as the foundation to enter the Sword Dao and comprehend the Sword Intent, it just perfectly completed the entire cultivation process of the Sword Dao.

From cultivating sword techniques, to cultivating sword techniques, to Sword Heart Enlightenment, to comprehending Sword Intent...

It had all been perfected.

"Guiding Xie Tiangheng a bit, consider it his contribution to completing the entire cultivation system of the Sword Dao."

Li Xuan had figured it out, the Sword Dao he had compiled before was too profound, the starting point was too high, lacking a solid foundation, lacking a complete system.
