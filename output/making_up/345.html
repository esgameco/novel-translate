"Hu Bubai, it's your turn to make a move," said the master of the Thousand Martial Palace, taking a deep breath and looking grimly at a young man with messy facial hair standing to the side.

"Alright!" Hu Bubai nodded.

"Niu Qiansheng, come back!" the palace master called out sternly.

Xu Yan smiled, not killing Niu Qiansheng but simply dispersing the layers of sword light and letting him return.

As Hu Bubai held his long spear, about to enter the battle, the palace master pondered for a moment and said, "Hu Bubai, decide victory or defeat, but don't fight to the death!"

He suddenly felt less confident. If by chance Hu Bubai was killed, it would be a big problem that he couldn't explain away. Every spirit prodigy was extremely precious.

Hu Bubai paused, then dragged his spear along the ground as he walked forward, saying, "Victory or defeat, life or death, it's all the same. I'll just fight with all my might."

As Hu Bubai walked out, the power of the laws of heaven and earth seemed to coalesce into a long spear, merging with the one he dragged on the ground.

"This youth is very powerful!" said the Great Zhou Emperor with a solemn expression.

"I never imagined the Thousand Martial Palace had such a prodigy. He's not far from reaching the next realm," Fu Tianhai sighed.

In the Tai Miao Sect camp, Du Yuying and Yun Miaomiao immediately became tense, staring intently at the battlefield while also turning to look at Xin Mengrou beside them.

"Ancestral Grandmother, that's Hu Bubai, right? How strong is he?" Yun Miaomiao couldn't help asking.

"He's decent. Not too far from the next realm," Xin Mengrou replied.

"Ancestral Grandmother, what is the next realm?" Du Yuying curiously asked.

Xin Mengrou was silent for a moment before saying, "Condensing the laws of heaven and earth into one's body, the realm of Law Condensation. It's not just gathering the power of heavenly laws, but condensing them into the body. The two are different, as different as heaven and earth.

"Condensing the laws of heaven and earth means possessing the might of those laws within oneself.

"Above Spiritual Refinement Celestial is Law Condensation Celestial Lord!"

Du Yuying and Yun Miaomiao were shocked, only now realizing that the realm above Spiritual Refinement Celestial was Law Condensation Celestial Lord! Condensing the laws of heaven and earth into one's body meant possessing their might.

"Ancestral Grandmother, are you a Law Condensation Celestial Lord?" Du Yuying asked curiously.

"Not quite yet," Xin Mengrou said softly, looking towards Changqing Pavilion.

The Law Pill bestowed by that senior had brought her just half a step away from condensing the laws into her body.

This was almost unthinkable in the Spirit Realm. It had already surpassed the cultivation limit possible there.

In the entire Spirit Realm, besides her, only one other person had reached this step - Wu Tiannan.

And Wu Tiannan had also relied on guidance from that senior.

"Xu Yan, you are very strong. I, Hu Bubai, have never been defeated in my life. I don't believe you can defeat me!" Hu Bubai said solemnly.

"Today you'll have to change your name to Hu Yibai (Hu One-Defeat)," Xu Yan smiled. Hu Bubai was very powerful, the strongest he had seen besides Xin Mengrou and Wu Tiannan.

"We'll see if you have what it takes!" Hu Bubai shook his spear and instantly attacked.

Xu Yan thrust out his sword. Mountains and rivers appeared, the Wheel of Life and Death materialized, sword formations emerged. In just an instant, Hu Bubai was covered in wounds.

"You've lost!" Xu Yan withdrew his sword, looking towards the Thousand Martial Palace and then around at the surroundings.

Silence! No one had expected Hu Bubai to be defeated so quickly and thoroughly.

Even Hu Bubai himself wore an expression of disbelief.

In his view, even if he lost, it should have been after an intense battle.

But as soon as he made his move, he saw sword light surrounding him, and with a few flashes, he was defeated!

"Senior Brother is so strong!" Su Lingxiu exclaimed in amazement.

The red cat sitting on the golden chair looked at the group of powerful beast cultivators and said, "Did you see that? This is the might of our Beast Clan's Supreme One!"

The beast cultivators all wore dumbfounded expressions.

Is our Beast Clan Supreme One really human?

"The Supreme One is mighty indeed!"

"Truly worthy of being our Beast Clan Supreme One!"

Never mind, as long as he's the Beast Clan Supreme One it's fine. Whatever the Great Beast King says must be right.

The beast cultivators praised admiringly, feeling extremely honored.

Li Xuan was not surprised by this. In the entire Spirit Realm, no one besides him was a match for Xu Yan.

Not Wu Tiannan, not even Xin Mengrou.

Xu Yan had already reached the peak of the Divine Origin Realm and accumulated profound foundations. He was just a tiny bit away from perfecting those foundations and breaking through to the Divine Power Realm.

"I am supreme among the Spirit Realm's prodigies. All of you prodigies, come at me together. I, Xu Yan, will suppress you all alone!" Xu Yan declared arrogantly, looking at the group of Spirit Realm prodigies.

Though his words were arrogant, at this moment, not a single prodigy stepped forward, nor did the crowd surge with anger.

Because Xu Yan had earned the right to say this!

Seeing no one respond to his challenge, Xu Yan suddenly felt bored. These Spirit Realm prodigies were all cowards.

"Xu Yan is ranked first on the Prodigy List. There's no need for further matches. Anyone who objects can issue a challenge," the Great Zhou Emperor announced.

No one objected!

Xu Yan left the battle arena, glancing at the Thousand Martial Palace. He felt bored with them too. Although he now had the power to suppress the palace, it no longer seemed necessary.

He had already reached the pinnacle of the Spirit Realm.

The Thousand Martial Palace master and others wore reluctant expressions, but were helpless. Even Hu Bubai had lost. Their only relief was that Xu Yan hadn't killed him.

"How strange that Xu Yan didn't kill him," Fu Tianhai said with a somewhat regretful look.

The Great Zhou Emperor gave him a disdainful glance and said, "Xu Yan is already a powerful expert. His mentality is completely different now. People like Hu Bubai are like juniors in his eyes - he can't be bothered to kill them.

"Besides, does Xu Yan even have a grudge with the Thousand Martial Palace? Even if there were past conflicts, he's vented his anger. He's already at the pinnacle of the Spirit Realm.

"He's not like certain people who take pleasure in bullying juniors."

Fu Tianhai rolled his eyes and said, "I just wanted to see a show. I wondered if those shameless Thousand Martial Palace fellows would open that illusory bridge after suffering such a big loss."

The Great Zhou Emperor couldn't be bothered to respond to him.

"Senior Brother Xu, why didn't you kill those Thousand Martial Palace guys?" Meng Shushu asked curiously on the Changqing Pavilion flying ship.

"In my eyes, they're already juniors. There's no fun in killing them," Xu Yan shook his head and said.

Meng Shushu: ...

Is this the mindset of a powerful expert? Or had Xu Yan already started to develop the mentality of a great master?

"It's my turn to make a move!" Meng Chong grinned happily, holding a knife in his hand as he instantly descended onto the battle arena.

Boom! As soon as he landed, he instantly transformed into a 90-foot golden body, covered in indestructible divine armor, like a god descending, majestic and awe-inspiring.

"I am the Knife Master Meng Chong, a Spirit Sect prodigy. Who dares to battle me?"

Such an imposing aura shook the surroundings.

"It's Meng Chong!"

"So strong! What do you think, is Meng Chong stronger or Xu Yan?"

"Definitely Xu Yan!"

A person walked out from the Thunder Cloud Manor, holding a purple hammer in his hand. Thunder clouds surrounded his body. As he walked out, the sound of thunder rumbled above his head.

Lightning crisscrossed, brewing a thunderstorm.

The scene of heaven and earth shattering shocked everyone.
