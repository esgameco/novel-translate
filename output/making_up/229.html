Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The once-mighty Qingtianjiao, unwilling to simply die, wanted to transform his post-death self into a corpse demon (shiliao).

He got his wish.

But not entirely.

For although he had become a corpse demon and awakened, the corpse demon Qingtianjiao was no longer the original Qingtianjiao.

His spiritual power had long since lost its former strength.

His original consciousness had not awakened either, with only some basic instinctual awareness remaining in his spirit, such as the desire to devour miasmas and flesh.

The flesh of martial artists held great allure for him, as if they were the most delicious delicacies in the world.

Perhaps he was awakened prematurely, unable to complete his final transformation, so his spiritual essence reassembled, but his original consciousness did not return.

The blue-backed, white-bellied Qingtianjiao had fine down growing between his scales, indicating his transformation was not fully complete.

Otherwise, he should not have had such down, and even if his original consciousness could not fully revive, at least a part of it should have.

The current Qingtianjiao was a corpse demon, no longer the original Qingtianjiao.

The overwhelming spiritual power suppressed the remaining geniuses, as miasmas and foul energies constantly surged into Qingtianjiao's body.

The young disciples of the Yushen Sect fled.

The remaining geniuses strained with all their might, yet still could not break free from the spiritual pressure.

"Corpse demon, Qingtianjiao has become a corpse demon!" exclaimed one of the geniuses in horror.

Corpse demons were extremely rare demonic beings in the Spiritual Realm.

They were usually born in certain special places, and only after a long time could they transform into corpse demons.

Ordinary corpse demons were not particularly strong. In the history of martial dao in the Spiritual Realm, there had never been a corpse demon as powerful as a Refined Spirit Celestial.

Once a corpse demon was born, it would have an innate thirst for blood, attacking and devouring the blood of martial artists and spirit beasts. They would quickly be exterminated by martial artists, with little chance to grow stronger.

In the history of the Spiritual Realm, the strongest corpse demon was only at the initial stage of the Great Celestial Realm.

Qingtianjiao was probably the strongest corpse demon in history.

Even more terrifying was that Qingtianjiao's transformation into a corpse demon was not accidental - it seemed to be his own deliberate arrangement for his post-death revival.

To live another life! How did Qingtianjiao know the method of transforming into a corpse demon?

The Spiritual Realm had never seen such a technique before. The group of geniuses were deathly pale with fear.

The powerful experts behind them must have never imagined that Qingtianjiao's tomb was deliberately arranged by Qingtianjiao himself to transform into a corpse demon.

The emergence of the tomb's traces meant that the transformation into a corpse demon was about to be completed.

Qingtianjiao was about to revive, living a second life as a corpse demon.

However, due to their intrusion, they had disrupted the final transformation, so although Qingtianjiao had become a corpse demon, his original consciousness had not revived.

Qingtianjiao coiled in midair, his eyes blood-red, his thirst for blood filling his gaze as he opened his huge mouth, staring at the remaining geniuses.

Xu Yan stood not far from Qingtianjiao, with a gentle wind swirling around him. The suppression of his spiritual power had no effect on him.

"With his spirit not complete or extremely weak, he can suppress those small Celestials, but if he faces a Great Celestial, he may not be able to suppress them.

"Now that Qingtianjiao has become a corpse demon, is he still useful?" Xu Yan fell into contemplation.

The reason he did not leave was that he had set his sights on Qingtianjiao. Meng Chong was a fleshly martial artist, and the essence of Qingtianjiao's flesh and blood could greatly help Meng Chong accumulate a fleshly foundation.

The current problem was whether Qingtianjiao, now a corpse demon, would still be useful.

"My junior brother's fleshly martial dao is more powerful than a spirit beast's physique. Even though Qingtianjiao has become a corpse demon, he might still be usable.

"Containing a powerful poison, he could also nurture the golden core apertures, and who knows, perhaps there could be some profound uses?"

Thinking this, Xu Yan decided to subdue Qingtianjiao. Even if he couldn't use him in the end, it wouldn't be a loss. Since he had encountered him, how could he let him go?

The sword was drawn. A sword descended, with mountains and rivers appearing, collapsing into a thunderous strike.

Thunderclap Sword! Qingtianjiao let out a low roar, and the huge mouth that was about to devour the geniuses suddenly spat out a black beam.

Boom! The black beam and the thunderous strike collided, the black beam shattering, but the thunderous sword was also weakened.

At this moment, Qingtianjiao's spiritual pressure had been broken by Xu Yan's sword.

The few geniuses suddenly felt reborn, hastily fleeing.

Xu Yan's intervention was not to save them, but to prevent Qingtianjiao from continuing to devour the miasmas and foul energies, lest his strength increase too much and become unusable.

Qingtianjiao's blood-red eyes stared at Xu Yan, letting out a roar as a black raging flame surged, the miasmas instantly condensing into a liquid. Raising his claws, he charged towards Xu Yan.

Hum! The Life-and-Death Sword Wheel appeared, spinning left and right to strike.

Xu Yan moved, shifting his position and appearing above Qingtianjiao's head, thrusting down with his sword. Although Qingtianjiao's original consciousness had not revived, his formidable might and powerful battle instincts remained.

His body twisting, another black beam was spat out. At the same time, the spiritual pressure constantly pressed down.

Hum! The Gentle Wind Sword Intent whipped up a gentle breeze, clashing with the spiritual pressure, locked in a stalemate.

"The spiritual power of a Refined Spirit Celestial is indeed extraordinary. With my current Gentle Wind Sword Intent, I cannot harm his spirit," Xu Yan sighed inwardly.

As expected, being able to resist the spiritual pressure was already sufficient. The Mountain and River Sword Dao unfolded, the Life-and-Death Sword Wheel shining brightly, continuously striking at Qingtianjiao, the two engaged in a fierce battle.

"Sss! So strong!" Shen Haizhou exclaimed in shock from outside the black swamp.

Xu Yan had casually slain his follower, and although Shen Haizhou knew Xu Yan was powerful, he never expected him to be able to contend with the corpse demon Qingtianjiao without being at a disadvantage.

"How is he able to resist Qingtianjiao's spiritual pressure?"

The spiritual pressure directly suppressed, and even below the Refined Spirit Celestial Realm, no one could resist it. Even Great Celestials who had already reached the Divine Realm were the same.

Yet Xu Yan was unaffected by Qingtianjiao's spiritual pressure?

"Could this brother be a Refined Spirit Celestial?" Shen Haizhou quickly dismissed this guess.

He did not sense any spiritual fluctuations from Xu Yan at all.

The few geniuses who had escaped with their lives were still shaken, looking at the battle raging in the sky above Black Rock Mountain, both shocked and relieved.

"Let's go, quickly return and inform the elders that Qingtianjiao has become a corpse demon."

This trip to Qingtianjiao's tomb had resulted in such a huge upheaval, and of those who came to explore it, less than ten people had survived. The rest had all died, either in the chaos of Black Rock Mountain's collapse, or in the jaws of Qingtianjiao, or from the sneak attack of the Jade Crystal Snakes.

Qingtianjiao's transformation into a corpse demon was undoubtedly a major event that would shake the martial dao world.
