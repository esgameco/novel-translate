He discovered that Su Lingxiu answered fluently, able to provide treatment methods for all kinds of patients.

Her medical skills were indeed remarkable, not just empty talk.

Li Xuan became energized. As for Su Wenxiu's cultivation of inner domain martial arts, he didn't really care, as it was only at the seventh rank. Reforming her martial arts would be easy.

Moreover, with a martial arts foundation, her cultivation would not be slow.

Li Xuan waved his hand, letting Shi Er leave, then stood up from the chair, playing with a jade scepter and circling around Su Lingxiu with a smile.

Su Lingxiu felt a bit panicked: "It's over, it's over, I've stepped on a nail. This time I can't bury the body!"

Zhou Yí's breathing was heavy, her fists clenched, as if she wanted to overcome the mountain in her heart and shield the young miss behind her. Her eyes had turned scarlet.

Li Xuan's aura was restrained as he chuckled, "Fourth rank in martial arts? From the inner domain?"

Zhou Yí took a deep breath and said in a muffled voice, "Yes, from the inner domain. I am Zhou Ying, responsible for protecting the young miss."

Li Xuan raised an eyebrow. This young girl's identity seemed to be not simple.

But he didn't care about his disciple's background, only whether they were suitable. The more background they had, the more willing he was to take them in.

"Inner domain martial artists don't usually come to this remote wilderness. Are you two fleeing here?"

Li Xuan had a guess in his mind.

Zhou Ying remained silent.

But she also felt relieved, as the other party was not those who were pursuing the young miss.

Su Lingxiu snorted twice and said, "It's not fleeing, just changing a place to live!"

Li Xuan was speechless.

He pondered for a while. Su Lingxiu had been in the remote wilderness for several years, after all, she had been the current Heavenly Mother of the Heavenly Mother Sect for years.

During all this time, no one had come to the remote wilderness to hunt them down.

Probably they didn't expect the two to flee to the remote wilderness.

Li Xuan evaluated in his heart that if he took Su Lingxiu as a disciple, he would certainly provoke the forces pursuing the two. Whether it was worth it or not.

"With my strength, I can easily defeat great masters. And if Xu Yan can comprehend a cultivation method beyond the Innate Realm, I will break through the realm."

"So the troubles behind Su Lingxiu are just minor troubles."

Of course, all of this was based on the premise that Su Lingxiu could comprehend the new martial arts system he had compiled. As long as she could comprehend it, the troubles behind her would be child's play.

"This young girl seems quite intelligent, and she started learning medicine at the age of four, her talent is monstrous. In the remote wilderness, I probably won't find a second person more suitable than her."

Li Xuan had made up his mind.

So he spoke, "Young miss, do you know about elixirs?"

Su Lingxiu shook her head.

"Elixirs, are they like those medicine pills?"

As she spoke, she reached into her bag and took out a bottle, pouring out a few round pills.

"Elixirs are not something as simple as these pills."

Li Xuan glanced at the smooth, white little pills in her delicate hand.

This young girl had talent, perhaps she could comprehend the martial arts system he had compiled.

So he said, "I see you have talent and intelligence. I have a set of Elixir Martial Arts here. Would you like to become my disciple?"

Li Xuan sat down in the chair.

"Master, Lingxiu pays respects to the master!"

Su Lingxiu immediately knelt down and kowtowed, as if afraid Li Xuan would change his mind.

"Young miss, you!"

Zhou Ying was a bit dazed. How did the young miss suddenly decide to take him as a master?

Li Xuan's mouth twitched slightly. This girl Su Lingxiu was really sharp, unhesitatingly becoming his disciple.

By becoming his disciple, she now had a powerful backer.

"Let me make it clear, you only have one year. If you fail to enter the door in one year, I will dissolve the master-disciple relationship, I don't accept useless disciples."

Li Xuan said sternly.

If Su Wenxiu could not comprehend the Elixir Martial Arts, he naturally couldn't provide her with a backing.

No need to invite those troubles.

But if she could comprehend the Elixir Martial Arts, then she would be a precious disciple!

It was related to the grand cause of his martial arts, whoever dared to cause trouble, he would crush them all!

"Yes, Master!"

Su Lingxiu respectfully replied.

One year was nothing, she believed that with her talent, entering the door in one year would be a breeze.

Zhou Ying, however, was anxious. "Young miss, in the remote wilderness, the spiritual energy has been extinguished, cultivation is impossible!"

Su Lingxiu was stunned, how could she have forgotten that?

"Master, you see?"

"My martial arts, how can they be compared to the inner domain? This talk of spiritual energy being extinguished and unable to cultivate, is all nonsense."

Li Xuan sneered contemptuously.

"You just need to remember, focus on comprehending the techniques I teach you. If you fail to enter the door in one year, we'll dissolve the master-disciple relationship."

Su Lingxiu's eyes lit up. "Master, will it really not affect my cultivation?"

"Of course!"

Li Xuan nodded.

"Then master, rest assured, I will definitely enter the door within a year!"

Su Lingxiu promised.

"Don't say too much."

Li Xuan smiled.

The Elixir Martial Arts were something he had pondered over, another martial arts system beyond just physical martial arts.

"Master, I started learning medicine at the age of four, and by six I was already curing diseases and saving people..."

Su Lingxiu said solemnly.

Li Xuan: ...

Looking at Su Lingxiu, he fell into deep thought. What was he doing at the age of six?

Playing in the mud!

Damn it!

The gap was quite large.

"Six years old, not bad. I vaguely remember that at the age of three, I had already fought and killed a fierce beast with my bare hands."

As a powerful and mysterious master, how could he lose to his disciple?

Li Xuan spoke without blushing or hesitation.

Su Lingxiu's mouth opened slightly as she looked at her master, falling into silence.

She doubted her master was boasting, but had no evidence.

"Master, truly impressive!"

She then revealed an admiring smile and flattered him.

"Mm."

Li Xuan nodded, quite pleased.

"You can stay here, I'll teach you the martial arts techniques tomorrow."

"Yes, Master!"

Su Lingxiu nodded.

She picked up an iron shovel and eagerly went to tidy up her own residence.

Shi Er came back.

The Heavenly Mother had actually become the disciple of the master?

But thinking about Su Lingxiu's monstrous talent, he felt it was only natural.

"Young miss, why did you take him as your master? The remote wilderness, how can cultivation be possible?"

In the room, Zhou Ying was cleaning and tidying up, worrying all the while.

"Aunt Zhou, what if it's really possible? The remote wilderness, do we have to stay here forever? Master is a powerful person. If I succeed in cultivating, I won't fear those people anymore."
