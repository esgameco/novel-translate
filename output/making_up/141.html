Only the number one sect in the inner realm, the Sword Sovereign Cliff, could possibly cultivate such a powerful and extraordinary sword dao prodigy.

Fu Yuntian nodded, "You can, follow me."

The rest of the martial artists mostly didn't follow, they didn't have the qualifications to go, and could only leave to spread what they had witnessed in the Seven Star City.

This young man is too much of a prodigy!

Defeating ten peak sect masters of the Seven Star Academy single-handedly.

Looking at the inner realm, it would be hard to find a second such monstrous young man.

Leng Qiu followed behind, this sword dao prodigy of a young man had almost become his idol of worship. He must find an opportunity to get to know him better.

If he could get some guidance from him, his own sword dao would certainly make a great leap forward!

The Seven Star Academy was in an uproar, a young man had cleared the nine-floor arena, defeated Ding Yan with a single sword strike, and defeated ten peak sect masters alone.

Countless students were shocked, where did this genius come from, to be so monstrous! They hurried towards the Storehouse, wanting to catch a glimpse of this young man.
