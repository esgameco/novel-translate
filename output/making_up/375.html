"Did someone save me?"

With such terrible injuries, who could have saved him? Who would be willing to use precious heavenly materials to help him recover?

Did an Immortal Venerable save him?

He shook his head and looked up with blurry vision. He was shocked.

What was that? A giant standing between heaven and earth? No! That was not an ordinary giant, but a divine demon as vast as heaven and earth?!

"My spirit must be damaged too, to have such hallucinations for the first time. Has the Undissipating Qi finally started affecting my consciousness? Thinking this, Jiang Buping's head drooped to one side as he withdrew his spirit power and fell into a deep slumber.

Standing between heaven and earth like a divine demon, with majestic divine might shaking in all directions - how could such a powerful being possibly exist? It must be an illusion, seeing a martial artist in such a form.

Jiang Buping felt some sadness in his heart. The Undissipating Qi had finally started affecting his consciousness. He really couldn't hold on any longer! He felt some reluctance! With this slight reluctance, Jiang Buping no longer forced himself to stay awake, but followed the feeling of drowsiness and fell asleep.

Moreover, Xu Yan, Meng Chong and Fang Hao seemed eager to go fight a battle in the abyss.

The crisis in Qiyun Prefecture had ended, so there was no need to go to Qiyun City. The flying ship turned around and flew towards the capital of Great Yue Nation.

Due to the long distance, even at full speed it would take some time to arrive.

However, since the crisis in Qiyun Prefecture had been resolved and the Blood Shadow Tower Lord was dead, the Great Yue Emperor and other powerful experts were sufficient to guard the abyss and prevent any major disasters, so there was no need to rush.

Although Blood Child Chu and his army of blood slaves had been eliminated, the abyss in Qiyun Prefecture had fallen. Now people needed to be arranged to guard it again.

The traitorous True King Venerables also needed to be killed one by one.

For this reason, Sui Hongwu apologized to Li Xuan, saying he needed to handle these matters personally, and Feng Yan would guide them instead.

With an Immortal Venerable taking action, the turmoil in Qiyun Prefecture would soon be quelled.

The flying ship turned into a stream of light, flying towards the capital of Great Yue Nation where the Netherworld abyss was located.

"This flying ship is a bit slow, and somewhat lacking," Fang Hao remarked with a sigh.

Limited by the materials obtained in the Spirit Realm, the Evergreen Pavilion flying ship was ultimately somewhat lacking and not quite matched to their strength. It needed to be upgraded.

Now that they had come to the Divine Realm, they could naturally obtain various divine objects and materials, enough to upgrade the flying ship to an extremely high level.

On the flying ship, with the Immortal Venerable Feng Yan present, Xu Yan and the others were all asking Feng Yan about matters of the Divine Realm, as well as the various heavenly materials and earthly treasures possessed by the Divine Realm.

After all, breaking through from the Divine Power realm to the Divine Form realm required an even greater accumulation, and needed more treasures of even higher grades.

It was imaginable that the further one progressed in cultivation, the rarer and more numerous the items needed to accumulate foundation would become. Preparations had to be made early.

Besides these, there were also matters concerning the abyss, especially whether there were unique treasures inside.

The secrets mastered by an Immortal Venerable expert were naturally far beyond what a True King Venerable like Cheng Zhan could compare to.

For example, Feng Yan knew much more about the Undissipating Abyss and Undissipating Qi.

Li Xuan was quietly paying attention, obtaining more information about the Divine Realm and the abyss from Feng Yan's mouth, while pondering the fifth martial art, compiling the theory and methods of the fifth martial art.

Feng Yan naturally held nothing back, after all these were the disciples of a great expert, obviously new to the Divine Realm and knowing little about it. This was also his opportunity to get closer to Xu Yan and the others.

Only those who had faced a great expert directly could know how unfathomably deep they were!

"In the three great Netherworld abysses of the Qinghua Realm, there is a unique divine material called Blood Cloud Gold, formed by the permeation of Netherworld blood energy over long years.

"Blood Cloud Gold can not only be forged into divine artifacts, but also has a special property - it can be integrated into one's essence blood. After being incorporated into the blood, it has the effect of replenishing essence blood.

"It can even be nurtured with essence blood to become a divine artifact exclusive to oneself. However, Blood Cloud Gold is not easy to obtain and is also extremely difficult to mine.

"Blood Cloud Gold is found near the main camp of the Netherworld. The closer to the core area, the higher the grade of Blood Cloud Gold produced. Since Blood Cloud Gold is not very useful for Netherworld martial artists, it is not mined..."

As Feng Yan introduced it, Xu Yan and the others became more and more interested as they listened.

Blood Cloud Gold was undoubtedly an extraordinary treasure, especially its property of being able to be incorporated into the blood for nurturing. If it was refined, or fused into weapons nurtured by oneself, it would certainly yield great benefits.

Moreover, Blood Cloud Gold was also one of the treasures that could accumulate one's own foundation.

They learned from Feng Yan that Blood Cloud Gold was not scarce in the three great abysses, but it was located in areas guarded by Netherworld Blood Children, making it difficult to enter and mine.

Only Immortal Venerables dared to venture in and hastily mine a piece.

It was precisely for this reason that Blood Cloud Gold was an extremely rare treasure in the Qinghua Realm, as even Immortal Venerables did not dare to linger for too long.
