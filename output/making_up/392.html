Regardless of whether Wan Tianlin came or not, Xu Yan would not let this matter rest.

He was already contemplating assuming a new identity and using the Blood Heart Fruit to lure out Wan Tianlin again.

At the Wan family residence.

Information about Xu Yan had already been delivered to Wan Tianlin.

"Entered the Snake Peak Celestial Cave, picked the Blood Heart Fruit..."

Wan Tianlin pondered.

Xu Yan's identity was still unknown, but it was almost certain he was not a martial artist from Mount Dagai.

It seemed he had specifically entered the Snake Peak Celestial Cave to obtain the Blood Heart Fruit?

"Something's not right!"

Wan Tianlin took a deep breath, his expression extremely cold. According to the information from Snake Peak Celestial Cave, the timing of Xu Yan's appearance and the Snake Peak Blood Son going berserk was too coincidental.

"Why did the Snake Peak Blood Son go so mad? He was even willing to fight to mutual destruction, all because this person appeared in the Snake Peak Celestial Cave..."

Wan Tianlin's gaze turned fierce.

The incident at Snake Peak Celestial Cave was caused by this person, and it was highly likely he had colluded with the Netherworld Prison.

However, thinking it over, if he had colluded with the Netherworld Prison, why would the Snake Peak Blood Son fight the clan elder to mutual injury for his actions? What was the purpose?

It brought no benefit to the Snake Peak Blood Son.

"He may not have colluded with the Netherworld Prison, but merely used them to weaken our Wan family, targeting us... The Blood Heart Fruit was probably not picked this time, but he merely entered Snake Peak Celestial Cave under the pretense of picking it, and used some method to enrage the Snake Peak Blood Son, causing him to attack madly..."

Wan Tianlin sensed the smell of conspiracy.

"Have our people returned?" Wan Tianlin suddenly looked up and asked.

"No!" the guard replied gravely.

"The situation looks grim!" Killing intent flashed in Wan Tianlin's eyes.

"Young Master, shall we still go to Jade Bamboo Slope?" the guard asked after a moment's thought.

"You go and see if he's there," Wan Tianlin said after pondering.

"Young Master, what if he is there?"

"Proceed with the trade as agreed, and get the Blood Heart Fruit first. If he's not there, then he clearly has a guilty conscience."

Wan Tianlin said with a cold smile, "Once we have the Blood Heart Fruit, the next step is to investigate the forces behind him. I want to uproot them completely!"

"I understand, Young Master!" The guard nodded.

"This time, two clan elders will follow in secret. You can rest assured."

For Wan Tianlin, the Blood Heart Fruit was the most urgent matter - it was an item he must obtain.

The Blood Heart Fruit in Snake Peak Celestial Cave had always been difficult to obtain. Moreover, the Snake Peak Blood Son had just gone berserk, and the clan elder was also seriously injured. There would be no opportunity to create a chance to pick the Blood Heart Fruit in the short term.

Therefore, the Blood Heart Fruit in Xu Yan's possession was extremely important to him.

"Also, have someone investigate who has obtained Blood Heart Fruits before, and who has entered the depths of Snake Peak Celestial Cave with the opportunity to pick them.

Make a list and conduct a secret investigation to see if there are any findings!"

Wan Tianlin thought for a moment and instructed.

The special and precious nature of the Blood Heart Fruit meant that those who obtained it would consume it to comprehend martial arts techniques, especially martial artists at bottlenecks.

Even if they didn't use it themselves, they would trade it immediately to obtain resources they urgently needed.

Even the Wan family had no stock.

To obtain a Blood Heart Fruit, they could only wait for war with the Netherworld Prison to create opportunities to pick them, and the quantity was limited - not everyone could obtain one.

Only geniuses like Wan Tianlin would receive special treatment, with people actively seeking to obtain Blood Heart Fruits for him. However, plans couldn't keep up with changes.

For now, to quickly obtain a Blood Heart Fruit, the only option was to trade with Xu Yan.

In a dark cave somewhere on Mount Dagai, several figures gathered.

"The plan is proceeding smoothly so far," the leader said in a deep voice.

Everyone in the cave wore masks. The leader's mask had an extra pair of fangs, giving it a fierce and murderous appearance.

He was also the strongest among them.

"Wan Tianlin wasn't killed, and Xu Yan didn't die either. How can you say the plan is going smoothly?" one of them asked in confusion.

"If it were so simple to kill Wan Tianlin and Xu Yan, we'd have to question whether this affair could cause enough turmoil.

Now the Wan family is investigating Xu Yan, and Xu Yan will certainly believe it was an assassination attempt by the Wan family. We just need to continuously intensify their conflict and deepen the hatred between them.

When the critical moment comes, we'll kill either Wan Tianlin or Xu Yan. Our goal will be achieved, and conflict between the Qinghua Territory and Nine Mountains region will inevitably break out!"

The man in the fierce mask said with a cold laugh.

"Xu Yan isn't one of the three major powers of the Qinghua Territory. Can he really cause conflict between both sides?" someone asked doubtfully.

"The name of Sword Saint Xu Yan is renowned in the Qinghua Territory, and he's an object of reverence for sword cultivators there. His identity is not simple. If I hadn't personally gone to the Qinghua Territory, I might not have been able to confirm his importance.

Now Xu Yan has come to the Nine Mountains region and wants to trade a Blood Heart Fruit with Wan Tianlin. This is a heaven-sent opportunity that can't be missed."

The man in the fierce mask said confidently.

"What's our next move?" a masked figure asked.

"Wan Tianlin urgently needs a Blood Heart Fruit now, and Xu Yan has one. They will certainly trade. Our next move is simple - further intensify the conflict..."

The man in the fierce mask coldly laid out the next steps of the plan.

...

When the appointed time to meet Wan Tianlin arrived, Xu Yan was surprised to see that the person who came was one of Wan Tianlin's guards from before.

And he had actually come to trade for the Blood Heart Fruit.

"These are the treasures you requested. Please check them," the guard said straightforwardly, his expression cold, as if they both tacitly understood something had happened between them.

Xu Yan nodded and didn't stand on ceremony. He checked the items, and after confirming everything was in order, he directly traded over a Blood Heart Fruit.
