"If that's the case, then so be it!" Xu Yan cupped his hands and turned to leave.

At this moment, the students of the Seven Star Academy seemed to have regained their senses.

Hu Shan had a look of envy on his face.

"What kind of Sword Sovereign Cliff's first genius is this? It's all just hype!"

"Today, I want to see how capable you Seven Star Academy students are. In my eyes, you are all trash!"

Hu Shan became excited and shouted, "My young master, Xie Lingfeng of the Sword Sovereign Cliff, is challenging all Seven Star Academy students under the age of thirty. In my young master's eyes, you are all trash!"

Du Yuying and the woman in white clothes were speechless.

On the Training Platform, Xie Lingfeng stood with his sword.

He didn't originally have these thoughts, but after being so disdained by the Seven Star Academy students, how could he endure it? After all, he was a renowned genius, and his pride and arrogance were high.

"Xu Yan alone broke through nine layers of the Training Platform. If I can't match that, then today I will subdue all the Seven Star Academy students under the age of thirty! These insignificant beings, how dare they mock me!"

Xie Lingfeng was furious.

The Seven Star Academy was in an uproar, and all the students were enraged. Such arrogance! Xie Lingfeng directly challenged the entire Seven Star Academy under the age of thirty, equivalent to suppressing his peers.

The departing Xu Yan had returned. He approached Hu Shan and asked, "Hu Shan, how about we make a bet on the outcome in the Seven Star Academy?"

"Huh? What kind of bet?" Hu Shan was taken aback.

Xu Yan was excited, as if he could see the opportunity to earn spirit stones. "Just bet on whether your brother will win or the Seven Star Academy will win!"

Hu Shan immediately understood, and the corner of his mouth twitched as he said, "That doesn't seem right."

"This is a great opportunity to make money!" Xu Yan was very confident in Xie Lingfeng.

"Alright then." Hu Shan thought about it for a moment and agreed.

Of course, he would be the one to handle this. If people knew that Xu Yan was betting on Xie Lingfeng, how could he make money?

So Hu Shan went to a large rock, placed his sword on it, and shouted loudly, "Today, my young master is challenging the students of the Seven Star Academy. I am confident that my young master can subdue all his peers. Who dares to make a bet with me?"

The gathered Seven Star Academy students were all holding back their anger. Xu Yan's arrogance was understandable, as he had the qualifications to be arrogant. But Xie Lingfeng, what right did he have to be arrogant? He was just a hyped-up genius.

"How do we bet?" A student immediately came forward.

"It's simple. If you bet on the Seven Star Academy winning, my sword will belong to the Seven Star Academy, representing my young master's defeat. If my young master wins, all your spirit stones will be mine!" Hu Shan looked at the students present and added, "You're only betting spirit stones, while I'm betting the reputation of the Sword Sovereign Cliff. Don't think I'm taking advantage of you. If you don't have the confidence to bet your spirit stones, you might as well just admit defeat."

These words stirred up the Seven Star Academy students.

"Bet it is! Let's see what Xie Lingfeng is capable of, challenging our Seven Star Academy!"

"Haha, we'll take the reputation of your Sword Sovereign Cliff!"

The students quickly opened their pouches and placed their bets. They were all betting on the Seven Star Academy winning. A pile of spirit stone pouches had accumulated in front of the large rock, and Xu Yan looked at them excitedly. This was another opportunity to make a big profit.

Xie Lingfeng on the Training Platform twitched his mouth. He suddenly felt the immense pressure. If he lost, he would not only lose the reputation of the Sword Sovereign Cliff, but also Xu Yan's money pouch!

He took a deep breath and coldly said, "Those below the Grandmaster level need not come up."

Boom! A Grandmaster-level aura was unleashed, shaking the surroundings.

The Seven Star Academy students were stunned. Xie Lingfeng was a Grandmaster-level cultivator! Considering his age, being a Grandmaster at around twenty years old, there were few in the Inner Realm who could match him. The rumors were not just hype.

"I'll go!" A Seven Star Academy student soared up and landed on the Training Platform. He was also a Grandmaster-level cultivator.

"Please proceed," Xie Lingfeng said coldly.

"Die!" The Grandmaster student raised his hand and struck with a sword.

However, a flash of sword light was seen, and his sword was knocked away, with a hole pierced in his shoulder.

"Next!" Xie Lingfeng said coldly.

The Seven Star Academy students were stunned into silence. One sword strike had defeated a Grandmaster! Fu Yunian's expression also became serious. Xie Lingfeng's strength was indeed not as good as Xu Yan's, but it could be said that his strength matched his genius reputation.
