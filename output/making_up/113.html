Chapter 113 The Heavenly Earth Dominating Saber, Splitting True Qi

Li Xuan looked at his second disciple, full of expectation, hoping that Meng Chong would comprehend a powerful saber cultivation technique, cultivating the saber with his heart and nurturing the saber's spirit.

Cultivating a trump card, a powerful hidden ace.

Xu Yan, who was standing aside, also fell into deep contemplation. Although his master spoke of saber cultivation, of nurturing the saber with one's heart and spirit, he cultivated the Sword Dao.

However, by drawing an analogy, he also had some ideas.

"To have a trump card, a hidden ace that one would not use unless absolutely necessary, this trump card, this hidden ace, once unleashed, can surpass one's own abilities."

Li Xuan slowly spoke.

"How can a martial artist not have a hidden ace? Only that which can surpass one's own abilities can be considered a true hidden ace, which can turn danger into safety in times of crisis.

"You have the intention to cultivate the saber, wanting to keep a lethal move for yourself, the idea is very good," Li Xuan praised.

Xu Yan and Meng Chong were both invigorated, their master was imparting precious martial dao experiences, which they must remember well.

"A martial artist must have a hidden ace!"

Meng Chong thought of his battle with the Blood Spirit Immortal, if he had not succeeded in saber cultivation, how could he have turned the tide and slain his opponent? That one saber strike could be considered his hidden ace!

"You have already entered the Saber Dao, today I shall impart to you the second realm of the Saber Dao."

Li Xuan solemnly said: "The second realm of the Sword Dao is the Heart Sword Realm, where all things can become the sword; while the second realm of the Saber Dao is the Concealed Saber Realm!"

Concealed Saber Realm!

Meng Chong listened respectfully, but his face showed confusion.

The second realm of the Sword Dao is the Heart Sword Realm, where the sword resides in the heart, and all things can become the sword.

Why then, is the second realm of the Saber Dao the Concealed Saber Realm?

Li Xuan said in a deep voice: "The Concealed Saber Realm, the saber is concealed within Heaven and Earth, there is no place in Heaven and Earth where there is no saber; Heaven and Earth is concealed within the saber, and I alone can establish Heaven and Earth with a single saber!"

Meng Chong's heart was shaken, murmuring to himself: "The saber is concealed within Heaven and Earth, there is no place in Heaven and Earth where there is no saber? Heaven and Earth is concealed within the saber, and I alone can establish Heaven and Earth with a single saber?"

He felt a domineering and overbearing aura from this.

There is no place in the world where there is no saber?!

Li Xuan solemnly said: "You must comprehend the Dao within it, to conceal is not to conceal, to conceal is also to conceal, it is to conceal the sharp edge and not reveal it, as well as to conceal the saber within the heart, to conceal the saber within Heaven and Earth, to conceal Heaven and Earth within the saber.

"Once you comprehend it, how vast is Heaven and Earth, where can you not go? Where is there no saber that you can use?"

Meng Chong's heart was shaken, feeling that it was too profound.

When will he be able to comprehend the second realm of the Saber Dao?

Given his current realm, it seemed too distant.

"Master, your disciple will remember it well!"

Meng Chong respectfully said.

Xu Yan also fell into deep contemplation, to conceal the sword within Heaven and Earth? It has a similar yet different meaning compared to the Sword Dao's all things can become the sword.

"Can I also conceal the sword within Heaven and Earth?"

Xu Yan murmured to himself.

Li Xuan glanced at his eldest disciple, "The Sword Dao is a Dao, the Saber Dao is also a Dao, continue to ponder and comprehend, you will surely have realizations."

These words were actually said for Xu Yan.

Given Xu Yan's prodigious talent and powerful imagination, he might be able to comprehend something new.

"Yes, Master!"

Xu Yan and Meng Chong respectfully bowed.

Li Xuan raised his hand and a serrated saber appeared in his grasp.

"The second realm of the Saber Dao is too distant for you, today I shall impart to you the Heavenly Earth Dominating Saber!"

Meng Chong was immediately excited.

The Heavenly Earth Dominating Saber!

"Watch carefully!"

Li Xuan gripped the serrated saber, his true qi circulating, the perfected domineering saber intent gathered, he raised his hand and struck out with a saber, the saber light traversing the vast sky, as if tearing apart Heaven and Earth.

A single saber strike, dominating Heaven and Earth.

To give his disciples space for realization, Li Xuan unleashed this strike with his full strength, infusing the perfected domineering saber intent with a trace of the ruthless and merciless sword intent that could overturn mountains and rivers.

He displayed this saber strike with an ability far surpassing Meng Chong and Xu Yan, naturally it was unparalleled in its domination, shocking the two disciples.

After the demonstration, Li Xuan inserted the saber into the ground.

"This saber strike, I displayed it with an ability almost equal to yours, this is the Heavenly Earth Dominating Saber, a single saber strike dominating Heaven and Earth!"

Li Xuan emphasized, the saber strike he just displayed was based on Meng Chong's current ability.

Meng Chong was greatly shocked.

Such a terrifying saber strike, was displayed with an ability almost equal to his own?

This Heavenly Earth Dominating Saber was truly powerful beyond measure.

That domineering aura that overawed Heaven and Earth had already deeply imprinted itself in his mind, under this saber strike, Meng Chong felt utterly insignificant.

"Yes, Master, your disciple will ponder it carefully!"

Li Xuan walked away with his hands behind his back, in a leisurely and carefree manner, "Mm, ponder it well."

Xu Yan watched his master leave leisurely, sighing inwardly: "Master's realm is too high, he can casually unleash it, wielding it freely, every action and movement contains the profound principles of the Dao of nature.

"Watching Master walk, so leisurely and unrestrained, with a great sense of freedom, when will I be able to reach such a realm?"

After imparting the second realm of the Saber Dao to Meng Chong, Li Xuan began to ponder the realms beyond the Profound Enlightenment Realm, how to compose them, how to refine the cultivation theories.

And to make it connect with the Profound Enlightenment Realm.

Meng Chong was cultivating the Heavenly Earth Dominating Saber, for the moment, he had not comprehended much.

Xu Yan took the Qi Blood Pill refined by Su Lingxiu and returned to the Eastern River Commandery, as well as going to the capital of the Qi Kingdom.

Li Xuan knew that with his family's martial dao entry secured, Xu Yan was eager to venture into the inner domain.

Su Lingxiu was cultivating and refining pills, she was tempering the Golden Bone, and the completion of the Golden Bone was not too far away.

Apart from pill refining and cultivation, she continued to ponder the Dao of Medicine, also beginning to comprehend the Golden Needle Technique, expanding upon the foundation of the Medicinal Classic.

"I've finally succeeded in refining it."

On this day, Su Lingxiu was greatly excited, the Evergreen Pill she had created was finally refined. With this pill, she felt her chances of tempering the Evergreen Bone had greatly increased.

Li Xuan was also in a good mood, if Su Lingxiu could temper the Evergreen Bone, his harvest would be even greater. Jade Bone + Golden Diamond Bone + Evergreen Bone, what kind of transformation would it undergo?

It would definitely be stronger than the current Innate Spiritual Bone.

On the hillside, Meng Chong gripped the precious saber, striking out one saber strike after another, the domineering aura of the Heavenly Earth Dominating Saber displayed by his master surfacing in his mind.

In the vast Heaven and Earth, only that one strike, the overbearing and domineering figure that overawed the world, and that one saber strike, as if tearing apart the heavens.

"The Heavenly Earth Dominating Saber lies in the aura of dominating Heaven and Earth, the overbearing and domineering attitude that overawes the world, but I still lack something, my saber intent is already unparalleled in its domination, but compared to that saber strike of my master's, it is still far behind."

Meng Chong contemplated, "My elder brother said, every word and action of my master conforms to the Dao, containing the profound principles of the Dao, I must observe and ponder more."

The image of his master surfaced in his mind, his steps leisurely, his bearing unrestrained, but on that day, when his master unleashed his strike, it was unparalleled in its domination, as if Heaven and Earth only had that one saber.

From leisurely and unrestrained, in an instant it became overbearing and domineering, with an aura of dominating Heaven and Earth, freely wielding it, no wonder his elder brother said his master was already close to the Dao.

Meng Chong closed his eyes, at this moment, the saber spirit became clear, the image of that saber strike surfacing in his mind, becoming increasingly distinct.

That aura of dominating the world, of slashing through the heavens, was gradually manifesting within him.

From childhood until now, although he had gone out many times to seek out experts, the time he spent away from home was never too long, except for that period when he cultivated in the small mountain village.
