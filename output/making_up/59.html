Chapter 59 Permian Yan's Journey to the Imperial Palace to Temper His Mind

The County Magistrate of Yunshan nodded and said, "Okay, we'll mobilize the congregation to find the precious medicinal herbs, but we'll also need to keep some for ourselves. These precious herbs might be useful for cultivating martial arts."

The old man in the hemp robe also nodded in agreement.

Shi Er then said, "Moreover, the Venerable One wishes to see the Heavenly Mother."

"Then pass the message to the Heavenly Mother and let her decide for herself," the old man in the hemp robe decided.

"Furthermore, the news of the Venerable One's presence in Yunshan County must not be leaked out, or else people from the Commandery City, or even the Capital City, will come to disturb the Venerable One!"

The County Magistrate said solemnly.

The group of experts from the Heavenly Mother Sect all nodded.

The opportunity is here, it's just a matter of who can seize it.

They didn't even tell people like Kou Ruozhi from the Commandery City.

Yunshan County was not very big, and Li Xuan had walked around for half an hour before returning to his residence.

Outside the courtyard gate, a figure was standing with a bent back, holding a box in his hands.

"County Magistrate of Yunshan?" Li Xuan asked in surprise.

These fellows, who usually avoided him like the plague, how come they were now coming to his door proactively?

"No, no, the Venerable One can just call me Little Mountain." The County Magistrate bowed and spoke in a very humble manner.

"I heard that the Venerable One is accepting disciples, so I've come to offer my congratulations. This is a small token of my appreciation, I hope the Venerable One will not disdain it!"

The County Magistrate held up the box in his hands.

"That's very thoughtful of you, very thoughtful!" Li Xuan took the box.

"Since there's nothing else, then I'll take my leave!"

The County Magistrate bowed and retreated.

Li Xuan nodded, the man was quite tactful.

Entering the courtyard, he opened the box to find a ginseng plant.

"This is not just any ordinary thousand-year-old ginseng, the Heavenly Mother Sect has quite a few precious medicinal herbs. My disciple's cultivation needs will be well taken care of now."

Li Xuan was very satisfied with this.

Just as he put down the ginseng, another person arrived.

Li Xuan sat in the chair without moving, letting Meng Chong go open the door.

The visitor was the old man in the hemp robe, also bringing precious medicinal herbs as a gift.

Li Xuan didn't stand on ceremony, directly accepting them.

"In the boundless great mountains, there are actually so many precious medicinal herbs, it must be quite extraordinary."

Li Xuan mused thoughtfully.

His guess was further confirmed.

Of course, the specifics were still unclear.

The group of experts from the Heavenly Mother Sect came to his door one by one to pay their respects, and Li Xuan received them all.

He would accept the gifts, but if the Heavenly Mother Sect dared to cause trouble under his banner, they better not blame him for being impolite!

These medicinal herbs could be considered the "ransom money" of the Heavenly Mother Sect's experts.

Meng Chong was contemplating the cultivation method of the Great Sun Golden Bell Cover in the courtyard, and had not made any breakthroughs yet. Li Xuan was not in a hurry.

In the evening, Shi Er came with a chicken.

However, he was quite tactful, not approaching Li Xuan directly, but handing the chicken to Meng Chong, smiling and saying, "Young Master Meng, this is the chicken you wanted!"

"Mm." Meng Chong took the chicken and went to slaughter and cook it into chicken soup.

Shi Er bowed to Li Xuan, who was sitting in the chair, and then left.

"Interesting, he's quite a clever fellow."

Li Xuan silently nodded as he watched Shi Er carefully close the courtyard gate behind him.

Having a runner like this could be useful.

Let's observe a bit more.

If he's clever enough and can get things done, he can be recruited as a servant.

After all, he can't always let his disciple do everything, since the disciple's main focus should be cultivation.

If the disciple doesn't diligently cultivate, how can Li Xuan improve his own strength?

In the Capital City of Qi, the shock brought by Yan's arrival had not yet subsided.

But the scions of many high-ranking officials, leading their guards and servants, had left the Capital City and gone to various great mountains in search of the Venerable One.

The imperial family had even mobilized all their intelligence forces and hidden spies, all for the sake of finding the Venerable One.

The Crown Prince was even preparing to personally set out to seek the Venerable One.

He had suffered the greatest losses in this upheaval.

Watching the position of Crown Prince, which he had almost grasped, slip away.

The ministers who had originally supported him now seemed to be avoiding him.

That nephew of his really was something, going to the inner palace so openly. Even the emperor had to maintain face.

Administering the lashes to the ministers was something only he could do.
