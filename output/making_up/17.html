Chapter 17 Five Transformations, the Golden Bone in Sight

In the secret chamber, Xu Yan took a stable stance and began to refine his bones.

As the cultivation technique operated, his qi and blood boiled, his body warming up, and his qi and blood started to continuously grow stronger.

"Indeed, the Crimson-Eyed Tiger is the king of tigers, containing a massive qi and blood!"

Xu Yan operated his qi and blood and began to temper them.

"The tempering speed has increased significantly!"

With the circulation of qi and blood, the internal and external merging, and continuous refinement, the copper bones started to emit a faint golden color.

"At the current speed, I can complete the first transformation in five days at the latest!"

"I can even go faster. The cultivation technique I've comprehended may not be complete, so I should continue to delve deeper into it!"

"Only by doing so can I accelerate the tempering of the golden bones!"

Xu Yan's gaze was resolute as he entered the state of forgetting the self and the object. He continued to ponder the cultivation technique while tempering his bones.

The next day, Xu Yan obtained the nourishing medicine concocted by Physician Pan, as well as the processed tiger skin, and returned to the secret chamber to cultivate.

"Indeed, the refining speed is even faster now!"

"I must purchase more precious medicines and more tonics to strengthen my qi and blood!"

Xu Yan was greatly excited, and according to the current refining speed, he might be able to complete the first golden bone transformation in a day and a half.

After the cultivation session, he found the manager of the Xu Family's business and instructed him to go and purchase precious medicines.

Being scolded was not a rare occurrence, and Xu Yan knew that his actions would bring some losses to the family. However, with the prospect of martial dao in sight, no matter how great the cost, he must pay it!

It was just a matter of some financial loss.

As long as he could enter the martial dao and truly become a disciple of a master, what did a bit of money matter?

Xu Yan looked at his mother with a pleading gaze.

"Mother!"

Xu Junhe's face turned green with anger and he sternly said, "Don't always look to your mother. I'm the one who decides this matter!"

"Why are you shouting so loudly? You're scaring Yan'er!"

Seeing her beloved son's pleading gaze, Xu Yan's mother immediately softened.

She glared at her husband disapprovingly.

"I..."

"My dear wife, you can't..."

"Of course not! This small amount of money is nothing."

"Then that's settled. Are you only concerned about money? That's our son, after all!"

"I..."

Xu Junhe deflated.

"Mother is the best!"

Xu Yan grinned.

Xu Junhe puffed out his cheeks and glared, but could only tacitly agree to Xu Yan's high-priced purchase of precious medicines.
