In the great mountains beyond Hong Ze's realm, Cang Hai Turtle was waiting for Xu Yan and the other three to return, while remaining vigilant of his surroundings, ready to send a message at any time.

This area, influenced by Hong Ze's realm, had an added darkness amidst the chaos, and was deathly silent with no true spirits to be seen.

"It's a bit uncomfortable staying here," Cang Hai Turtle sighed.

However, when he looked towards Hong Ze's realm, his heart was still trembling, though the feeling of fear had diminished somewhat compared to initially.

"I must overcome this fear!"

Cang Hai Turtle gritted his teeth and stared at Hong Ze's realm, wanting to use this to overcome the fear deep in his heart. Even if he couldn't completely eliminate the shadow in his heart, being able to reduce the fear would be great progress.

As time passed, Cang Hai Turtle gradually became delighted. The longer he stared, the more accustomed he became, and the fear in his heart kept diminishing.

"How much time has passed?" Cang Hai Turtle pondered. Had it been half a month since Xu Yan and the other three entered Hong Ze's realm?

"In the blink of an eye, so much time has passed? Why haven't they returned yet? I hope nothing has happened."

Cang Hai Turtle took out a communication talisman, preparing to send a message to inquire, but then thought, "If they are in the middle of comprehending something, wouldn't my message now be a disturbance? Besides, Xu Yan and the others are disciples of that person, they shouldn't be in any danger."

That person was an existence who could casually crush a true spirit comparable to a realm lord. In terms of strength, he was definitely not below the one from the Immutable Palace, and should be even stronger.

"I'll continue waiting. If they've really found an opportunity, they won't be able to return so quickly."

Thinking this, Cang Hai Turtle continued to work on overcoming his inner fear.

Somewhere in the Immutable Land, a huge true spirit was galloping, with three figures sitting on its back.

These three figures were different from ordinary martial artists, lacking some spiritual aura but with an added gloominess, as if they had never lived within the realms.

The three wore gray robes with the outline of a palace embroidered on the chest.

"What use is a dead realm? Why are we looking for it?" one of them asked, puzzled.

"We just need to complete the mission. As for what use a dead realm has, that's the business of the important figures," another shook his head and said.

The third person spoke in a deep voice: "The four people and one turtle we encountered were living beings from a realm. They're probably here for this dead realm too. If we can find the dead realm, we can also capture those four people and one turtle."

The first person who spoke asked confusedly: "Can people from the Tai Cang Realm actually leave their realm and have the leisure to search for the dead Hong Ze Realm?"

Since they were living beings from a realm, there was no other realm besides the Tai Cang Realm.

When they accepted the mission to search for the dead Hong Ze Realm, they had already learned that the Netherworld Realm had been refined by that Blood Prison Guardian, though it originally belonged to him anyway.

As for the Demon Shaman Realm, it had also disappeared, crashing into the Tai Cang Realm, seemingly causing no small disaster for the Tai Cang Realm.

In this situation, how could martial artists from the Tai Cang Realm have the leisure to search for the dead Hong Ze Realm?

"Perhaps the Tai Cang Realm, after being hit by the Demon Shaman Realm, has developed a major problem, and they want to find the dead Hong Ze Realm to fill this gap?" the third person said with a smile.

"That makes sense!"

The three continued riding the true spirit, searching for the Hong Ze Realm in the Immutable Land.

As for the Tai Cang Realm, that was the business of important figures. They could only follow orders.

At this moment, that mountain had an aura that made them tremble.

"The Great Wilderness is not fake!"

"Stop trying to deceive me. The Great Wilderness is fake, and the Dao Ancestor is also fake!"

"Hehehe, I've already said, the Great Wilderness is not fake. Tai Cang is already history. Now it's the Great Wilderness, called the Great Wilderness Realm. The Dao Ancestor is of course real. Inside the mountain, after a long struggle, Blood Demon finally understood that what he had drawn back was not his own soul fragment! No, to be precise, this soul fragment had undergone some change and had been usurped by an outsider.

Moreover, the other party intended to usurp his power and wanted to replace him! At this moment, Blood Demon knew that some unknown change had occurred in the Tai Cang Realm.

"I want to see how you'll replace me!"

Blood Demon was furious, but he had to admit that he couldn't do anything to the other party.

From the initial misjudgment, he had missed the opportunity and let the other party gain the upper hand. After the other party had stolen half of his body, it was destined that he couldn't suppress them.

Especially since this soul fragment was very strange. It had a quality that was of the same origin as himself and wouldn't be rejected by his laws, yet it wasn't under his control, and some secret techniques were ineffective against it.

The power of that mysterious "Dao Ancestor" was truly strong and inconceivable.

He was a realm lord, after all!

"Just because I can't do anything to you doesn't mean no one can!"

Blood light surged on Blood Demon's body as the blood pool disappeared. He prepared to return to the Immutable Palace to ask that person to take action and suppress the unknown soul in his body.

"Hehehe, you can't make that decision."

Blood Extreme laughed wickedly. Blood Demon stepped forward with one foot, but the other foot stepped backward in the opposite direction. As a result, Blood Demon's body did a split and sat on the ground.

"Hehehe!"

Blood Extreme laughed arrogantly, controlling the hand he possessed and raising it up, then slamming it towards the other hand that Blood Demon controlled, as if wanting to break Blood Demon's hand.

After all, it wasn't his own body, so he didn't care at all!

"How dare you!"

Blood Demon was furious, blood light surging on his body as he entered into another struggle for control of the body. As a result, Blood Demon's body rolled back and forth in the mountain, as if he was fighting with himself.

"You've already lost your chance. Now we share control of the body. You can't expel me, and I can't devour you for now. We should have a talk and reach some agreements, shouldn't we?"

Blood Extreme smiled very happily.

Although he hadn't succeeded in replacing Blood Demon and couldn't control all of Blood Demon's power and body, he had still occupied half.
