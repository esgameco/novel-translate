Tang Jinyan, accompanied by Ziyun Emperor, headed to the Thousand Martial Hall, where they were brewing plans for an action against the Evergreen Pavilion.

Leaving aside the miraculous medicinal arts of the pavilion, the Evergreen Pavilion itself was an astonishing divine artifact, and many powerful experts wanted to ascend the divine artifact and witness its extraordinary nature.

In the pavilion atop the Evergreen Pavilion, Li Xuan was seated calmly, and Su Ling Xiu was massaging his shoulders. "Master, I'm preparing to cultivate the pill-refining technique I've comprehended, and I need some special raw materials to practice with. I need your help."

"Your master doesn't have any heavenly materials or earthly treasures to give you," Li Xuan shook his head.

"It's not heavenly materials or earthly treasures, I want you to help me condense some heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms and laws of the Dao. I want to use the heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms as the main materials to cultivate a new pill-refining technique," Su Ling Xiu said with a smile.

"Alright," Li Xuan nodded.

The pill-refining technique Su Ling Xiu wanted to cultivate was the Heavenly and Earthly Divine Furnace Pill-Refining Technique that she had comprehended. She planned to use the heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms and laws of the Dao as the main materials to practice the Heavenly and Earthly Divine Furnace Pill-Refining Technique, and the pills refined would make even the strongest experts in the Spiritual Realm envious.

"Thank you, Master!" Su Ling Xiu was overjoyed.

She took out a few small bottles, which were used to store the heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms and laws of the Dao, and were refined by Fang Hao, containing seals that could lock up the spiritual mechanisms and laws of the Dao.

Li Xuan raised his hand and grasped, and the heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms condensed in his hand, continuously being poured into the small bottles.

He could even grasp and condense the laws of the Dao, let alone the heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms.

Su Ling Xiu plugged the bottles, sealing the heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms inside.

She then took out a few more small bottles.

Li Xuan raised his hand and grasped, and the laws of the Dao condensed, with a thread of the laws of the Dao being poured into each small bottle.

The reason he only poured a thread into each bottle was that he was worried that too much of the laws of the Dao would be beyond Su Ling Xiu's control, as this was her first time refining pills with the laws of the Dao, and she was using them to cultivate a new pill-refining technique.

"Thank you, Master!" Su Ling Xiu was excited, putting the small bottles away and hurrying off to refine pills.

As for the visiting martial artists, they were naturally received by Shi Er and the others, and Yue'er was busy assisting her, having no time to deal with these trivial matters.

As for Cai Lingier, she was no different from Li Xuan's maids, always serving Li Xuan, and had even started learning how to make delicious spiritual cakes and other delicacies.

The Evergreen Pavilion's flying boat formation had already been activated, and those who wanted to ascend the Evergreen Pavilion could only enter through the gate, and they needed to obtain permission to do so.

Shi Er and Meng Shushu were directly providing medical treatment at the entrance, and they even refused to let people in if they had no injuries.

When it was time to be overbearing, they would be overbearing. The Evergreen Pavilion had the confidence to do so.

Suddenly, a thunderous sound came from the refining room specially prepared for Su Ling Xiu in the Evergreen Pavilion's flying boat.

"Cough, cough!" Yue'er's originally delicate white face was now blackened by the smoke, and black smoke was still coming out when she coughed.

"Miss!" Zhou Ying was startled and hurriedly rushed into the refining room.

Su Ling Xiu's hair was a bit messy, but she was uninjured, with a sullen expression on her face. The furnace had actually exploded! Yue'er was even blackened by the charred spiritual medicine.

"Aunt Zhou, I'm fine," Su Ling Xiu shook her head and took out another bottle, preparing to refine the pills a second time.

Yue'er came over, holding the spiritual medicine, ready to add it to the furnace according to Su Ling Xiu's instructions.

Zhou Ying could only retreat from the refining room. Her strength was weak, and if an explosion occurred, Su Ling Xiu would have to protect her, making it even more dangerous.

With a wave of her hands, Su Ling Xiu's spiritual power transformed into a furnace, and the furnace flames burned, with a thread of heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms being thrown into the furnace, slowly breaking the seals that confined the spiritual mechanisms.

With a light sound, the refining room shook.

"It failed again," Su Ling Xiu looked at the black pill in her hand, a bit regretful, "If Crimson Cat were here, this pill wouldn't have been wasted."

After three failures, Su Ling Xiu finally succeeded in refining the pill using the heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms, and she was excited, as was Yue'er.

This meant that she could condense the heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms into her spiritual sea more quickly and easily.

"The heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms are actually everywhere. Even these spiritual medicines contain heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms, otherwise they couldn't be spiritual medicines. It's just that the heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms contained in them are extremely scarce.

"The higher the grade of the spiritual medicine, the more heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms it contains. But once the spiritual medicine is picked, the spiritual mechanisms will dissipate.

"We must find a way to completely seal the spiritual mechanisms contained in the spiritual medicines when picking them, so that they won't dissipate.

"Only then can we rely on the spiritual medicines to refine pills by extracting the spiritual mechanisms within them," Su Ling Xiu thought as she looked at the heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanism pill in her hand.

She could ask her master to condense the heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms for her pill refining. If she couldn't condense the heavenly and earthly spiritual mechanisms, wouldn't she be unable to refine pills?

So she needed to find a middle ground, even if the pill effects were a bit weaker, as long as she could refine the same type of pills without relying on the help of powerful experts.

As for refining pills with the laws of the Dao, there was no way around it - she had to rely on the help of powerful experts to condense the laws of the Dao.

"Maybe I don't have to rely on powerful experts, perhaps I can rely on formations to condense the laws of the Dao," Su Ling Xiu pondered, deciding to have Fang Hao research how to use formations to condense the laws of the Dao.

Outside the Evergreen Pavilion, there was a long queue, including many prodigies of the Great Zhou Kingdom and people of noble status, but they were being kept waiting, naturally causing some resentment. However, Shi Er and Meng Shushu paid no attention to it.

Li Xuan continued to immerse himself in the great martial dao, adding another divine technique to the Martial Divine Classic.

The theoretical framework for the realms beyond the Void Boundary had been further improved.

The subsequent cultivation methods for the physical martial dao had also been outlined, and the first layer had been compiled.

"Your disciple Xu Yan has comprehended the path of cultivating the Wisdom Sword Realm, you have broken through to the Wisdom Sword Realm!"

The Dao Golden Book opened, and golden light surged forth.

Li Xuan was stunned for a moment, then became extremely excited.

Such a mysterious Wisdom Sword Realm, such a profound and difficult-to-comprehend third realm of the sword dao, and Xu Yan had actually found the direction for its cultivation?

At this moment, his sword dao had broken through to the Wisdom Sword Realm, and the profound and mysterious realm had manifested.

"So this is the Wisdom Sword Realm!" Li Xuan sighed inwardly, no wonder it was the third realm of the sword dao he had compiled, so profound and unfathomable.

His cultivation had not advanced, but by entering the Wisdom Sword Realm in his sword dao, his strength had undergone a tremendous increase!

In an instant, a water sword condensed on the shore of Small Star Lake.

This sword seemed to have its own intelligence, as if it naturally grasped the Dao of the Water Sword.

On the shore of Small Star Lake, an eight or nine-year-old boy was holding a sword and swinging it, muttering, "I want to become the second Xu Yan, I also want to become a powerful swordsman!"
