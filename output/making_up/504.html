Every realm is the foundation for the next realm, so when creating a realm, one must first determine the general ideas and direction of the next realm in order to connect them and make the entire cultivation path smooth.

After all, it's no longer the early days when one could just make things up carelessly. Having transcended heaven and earth, one is now truly a powerful expert, a true master of martial arts. So the creation of each subsequent realm and cultivation method must be well-reasoned and justified.

They must conform to cultivation principles and connect properly between realms. Only in this way can one successfully comprehend and cultivate them.

Otherwise, if one gets stuck at a certain realm unable to comprehend or continue cultivating, wouldn't that block one's martial path? Therefore, Li Xuan put a great deal of effort into creating the cultivation beyond the Establishing the Dao realm, ensuring the realms connected while maintaining consistently powerful strength.

"Beyond Establishing the Dao is Creation!"

The Creation Realm! A faint halo of the Dao Ancestor flickered around Li Xuan as he gazed deeply and said, "Create heaven and earth to understand all living things, to perceive the Great Dao, to connect with the Great Dao!"

Xu Yan's heart trembled. Beyond Establishing the Dao was Creation - what did Creation mean?

Looking down at the mountains, rivers, plants, insects and birds below, he seemed to gain some vague understanding of what Creation was in that moment.

But he still didn't understand how to create heaven and earth.

Li Xuan continued speaking: "Creation is generating all things from nothingness, creating all things to know heaven and earth, to give birth to wisdom...

"Everything in the world originates from creation, and creation originates from the vast and profound Great Dao. Only by creating one's own heaven and earth, creating one's own Dao, can one resonate with the Great Dao and understand its meaning..."

Beyond Establishing the Dao was Creation.

After establishing one's own Dao and the Dao of heaven and earth, the next step was Creation.

Creating heaven and earth, creating oneself, to understand all living things and perceive the vast and profound Great Dao.

This was the future cultivation path Li Xuan had determined after repeated contemplation. It connected well with the Establishing the Dao realm while maintaining mysteriousness and power.

To create this Creation realm, Li Xuan had put in no small amount of thought. Even with the assistance of the Golden Book of the Great Dao, it still wracked his brains to fully develop this realm.

Moreover, Li Xuan had a feeling that the Creation realm was an extremely profound realm that could align with the Golden Book of the Great Dao.

After all, wasn't the feedback from the Golden Book of the Great Dao also a kind of creation?

As for the realms beyond the Creation realm, Li Xuan also had some concepts and was in the process of refining them.

"The Creation realm, ah! Once I enter the Creation realm, I'll be able to destroy powerful experts like Tai Cang with a wave of my hand. The Immutable Temple will no longer be a threat, right?"

Li Xuan thought excitedly.

The Creation realm was an extremely profound realm with the ability to create all things from nothingness.

The seven of Tai Cang used a mysterious purple light to create heaven and earth.

But if he broke through to the Creation realm, he would be able to create a heaven and earth in the immutable lands without relying on any treasures.

How could Tai Cang match such powerful strength?

Even that person from the Immutable Temple couldn't match it. So after breaking through to the Creation realm, Li Xuan felt he would truly have enough confidence.

"With the power of creation, I'll be a Dao Ancestor worthy of the name!"

Li Xuan thought to himself.

The Creation realm was a profound realm with extremely abstruse cultivation methods that relied entirely on self-enlightenment. However, Li Xuan had confidence in Xu Yan.

After explaining the theory and cultivation methods of the Creation realm once, Li Xuan let Xu Yan continue contemplating silently.

When explaining the Creation realm, Li Xuan's Dao Ancestor halo glowed with light, enveloping Xu Yan to enhance the power of transmitting the Dao and increase the listener's comprehension.

This was another wonderful use of the Dao Ancestor halo besides showing off.

"The mysteries of Creation cannot be memorized in a short time. Disciple, you must remember them well."

Li Xuan explained the Creation realm once again.

Afterwards, he asked Xu Yan if there was anything he didn't understand and explained those points in detail.

Even for a genius like Xu Yan, Li Xuan had to explain the Creation realm several times before he could fully memorize it.

As for when he would be able to understand the mysteries of Creation, Li Xuan couldn't predict.

"Disciple, you must remember the key points of the Creation realm. Understanding these key points means understanding the mysteries of Creation."

Li Xuan said solemnly.

"Master, this disciple will not disappoint you and will certainly comprehend the key points of Creation."

Xu Yan said respectfully.

"Good, listen carefully then."

Li Xuan held a jade ruyi scepter behind his back and looked up slightly at the sky, his Dao Ancestor halo glowing, fully displaying the demeanor of a Dao Ancestor.

"The Dao generates one qi from nothingness, from one qi produces yin and yang; yin and yang combine to give birth to all things, a single thought can sustain heaven and earth... These are the key points of Creation, contemplate them well."

After speaking, Li Xuan's form disappeared from the spot and returned to the courtyard, sitting leisurely in a chair.

"The Creation realm, ah, I've finally passed it on to Xu Yan. I hope he comprehends it soon."

Li Xuan sighed in his heart.

Without realizing it, he had gone from making up techniques carelessly to becoming a true Dao Ancestor.

"I should find time to guide Meng Chong, Su Lingxiu, Fang Hao, and Jiang Buping as well. Being a master isn't so easy."

Li Xuan couldn't help sighing again.

For the sake of his disciples' cultivation, he as their master had really put in a lot of effort.

"But it's all worth it."

In high spirits, Li Xuan guided the cultivation of Shi Er, Meng Shushu and the others, and also guided the cultivation of his maid Cai Ling'er.

Since awakening her Sea Spirit Clan royal bloodline, obtaining the Sea Spirit Clan royal legacy, and under Li Xuan's guidance, Cai Ling'er's cultivation speed had become faster and faster.

Now she was already at the Heaven Unity realm.

"Becoming a Realm Lord shouldn't be a problem."

Li Xuan made this judgment.

As for strength beyond the Realm Lord level, it would depend on Cai Ling'er's own fortune.

However, staying by the side of a Dao Ancestor like himself, breaking through the bottleneck of the Realm Lord realm was not impossible.

Of course, it would just take a longer time.

Surpassing the Sea Spirit Clan royalty was also not a problem.

"This girl has really grasped a great opportunity!"

Li Xuan sighed inwardly. Opportunities all lie in a single thought, and Cai Ling'er had grasped the opportunity by choosing to stay by his side as a maid.

This was the greatest opportunity of her life.

"That fellow Feng Yan also knows how to grasp opportunities."

Li Xuan chuckled to himself.

He liked these kinds of clever people who could see the path ahead clearly and knew how to seize opportunities.

"But has Blood Extreme, that half-crippled minor realm lord, also grasped his opportunity?"

Li Xuan had a strange expression.

Blood Extreme had become completely submissive, very cooperative with Su Lingxiu's research, seemingly determined to be Su Lingxiu's research subject. He even willingly endured excruciating soul pain to please Su Lingxiu, allowing her to observe the changes in his soul under extreme agony...

"What a ruthless person!"

Li Xuan sighed. For such a ruthless person to not grasp an opportunity seemed unreasonable.

Being able to stay by Su Lingxiu's side was also a kind of opportunity.

After all, Su Lingxiu would surely surpass Tai Cang in the future.
