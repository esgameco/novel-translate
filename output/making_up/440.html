Jiang Tianming raised an eyebrow, and Yun Yan'er also looked over.

"Oh, he's not dead yet?" Yun Yan'er said with a light laugh.

"If that's all, it's hardly a secret. You've come to disturb me for this..." Jiang Tianming's gaze gradually turned cold, murderous intent emerging.

Liang Quan was almost breaking out in a cold sweat. He hurriedly said, "Young Master, Jiang Buping has recovered! He hasn't lost his mind, and his strength is even greater than before!"

"What did you say?" Yun Yan'er suddenly stood up. "Impossible, he had already lost his mental faculties!"

Jiang Tianming's face also darkened as he stood up. "Liang Quan, you should know that Jiang Buping's loss of mental faculties and reduction to a useless person was confirmed by the clan elders."

"Young Master, Miss Yun, every word I say is true. When I saw him, I could hardly believe it myself. But the fact is, not only has he not lost his mind, his strength has increased. He seems to have cultivated some powerful soul technique. With one strike he killed an Outer Realm True Spirit Armored Beast..."

Liang Quan hurriedly reported the details.

"Young Master, I knew this matter was of great importance, so I rushed back immediately."

Jiang Tianming frowned, while Yun Yan'er's expression was grave. She opened her mouth to speak several times but stopped.

"Have you reported this to anyone else?" After a long while, Jiang Tianming asked.

"Young Master, I came straight here to report to you first. I haven't told anyone else!" Liang Quan said respectfully.

"Good, very good. I appreciate people like you who know how to act appropriately. Stay here in this courtyard and follow me from now on. You won't be short of rewards." Jiang Tianming said with satisfaction.

Liang Quan had come to pledge allegiance to him, and he was a clever person too.

"Thank you, Young Master!" Liang Quan was overjoyed.

From now on, he would be one of the followers of the Jiang clan's young master. His status would be far higher than even the patriarch of his own clan.

"Mm, you may go now." Jiang Tianming nodded.

"I take my leave!" Liang Quan retreated respectfully.

"Brother Tianming, how is this possible? Could it be someone who just looks like him? After all, people who look alike do exist in this world." As soon as Liang Quan left, Yun Yan'er couldn't help but speak.

"When one's soul is tainted by immutable qi, loss of mental faculties is inevitable. Even immortal realm experts, let alone true kings or heavenly venerables, can't avoid this fate if tainted.

"Jiang Buping's soul being tainted by immutable qi was a confirmed fact. The clan elders all verified it at the time. That's why Jiang Buping was abandoned and expelled.

"Now he's recovered? This is no ordinary matter!" Jiang Tianming's expression was extremely grave.

Jiang Buping being sabotaged, leading to his soul being tainted by immutable qi, was no longer a secret. The clan elders all knew about it. But since he had become useless with no value, there was no need to investigate further.

As for Yun Yan'er, she would certainly marry into the Jiang family, because she was the "culprit". If the Yunshang Sect Master didn't agree, the Jiang clan would have an excuse to take action.

Crippling one of the Jiang clan's peerless geniuses deserved some kind of accountability.

Those who knew the inside story naturally understood, and how could Jiang Buping himself not know who had sabotaged him? So once he rose to power, he would surely come to demand an explanation.

"Brother Tianming, what should we do now?" Yun Yan'er asked with a frown.

"Kill him, of course!" Jiang Tianming said coldly.

"Yes, kill him. We were careless before." Yun Yan'er nodded happily.

"This is a small matter. Even if he's reached the immortal realm, let alone not having reached it yet, he's sure to die. Don't worry, I'll handle this." Jiang Tianming said with a reassuring smile.

"Mm-hmm!" Yun Yan'er nodded.

"To be able to recover even after being tainted by immutable qi, Jiang Buping, what secrets do you have? If I could obtain them..." Jiang Tianming pondered.

Who wouldn't be tempted by the ability to dispel immutable qi and recover from it? What kind of opportunity or technique was this? In the depths of Yun Yan'er's eyes, there were also some ideas.

The Taikun Border remained peaceful. The chaos of the Heavenly Abyss had not erupted for several hundred years, entering a relatively calm period.

On this day, two immortal realm experts left a certain courtyard of the Jiang clan.

Not long after these two immortal realm experts left, another immortal realm expert departed from the Yunshang Sect, crossing the border.

Yuanping Border.

Jiang Buping had just finished cultivating, his expression elated. He had finally integrated another trace of immutable qi, strengthening his soul and improving his Extreme Soul Martial Arts.

"Taikun Border, Jiang family, Yun Yan'er, just you wait. I, Jiang Buping, will definitely wash away all humiliation. Your so-called glory is nothing but a joke in my eyes."

Jiang Buping clenched his fist, his gaze fierce.

"I'll hunt down that Outer Realm True Spirit comparable to the immortal realm."

Jiang Buping was excited.

With one experience under his belt, hunting a second immortal realm Outer Realm True Spirit was naturally not a problem.

Boom!

As this Outer Realm True Spirit fell, the other weaker Outer Realm True Spirits were startled and hurriedly fled the Heavenly Abyss, returning to the outer realm.

However, Jiang Buping knew that before long, more Outer Realm True Spirits would descend.

For these Outer Realm True Spirits, the Heavenly Abyss was a treasure trove, far more comfortable than the outer realm. If the Heavenly Abyss wasn't restricted, Outer Realm True Spirits above the immortal realm would likely have entered long ago.

The martial world of Yuanping Border was in an uproar.

Someone with strength below the immortal realm had hunted and killed two immortal realm Outer Realm True Spirits. Although it was through sneak attacks, it was still unprecedented.

News of Jiang Buping cultivating an extremely powerful soul technique naturally spread throughout Yuanping Border. One who could cultivate such a powerful soul technique must be a peerless genius from a great border.

It was precisely because of this that even though some immortal realm experts coveted the technique, they didn't dare to make a move.

Jiang Buping continued to train in Yuanping Border. When immortal realm Outer Realm True Spirits appeared in the Heavenly Abyss again, he entered to hunt them, while also spending more time collecting and extracting immutable qi.

The name "Spear God" also spread throughout Yuanping Border.

Some people wanted to befriend Jiang Buping and find out which great border he came from, but Jiang Buping remained aloof, maintaining an unapproachable demeanor. After a while, no one came to bother him anymore.
