"Young Master Xu," the woman in plain clothes said softly as she approached.

"You've come at the right time. This is for you, to thank you for your help last time. If you ever have any trouble, I can lend you a hand."

"Young Master Xu, you're too kind. Please keep the jade token."

The woman's eyes seemed to hold a smile.

"No, I don't want it!" Xu Yan pushed the jade token back into her hand.

He didn't like owing favors to others, and since he no longer needed it, he naturally wouldn't keep it.

The woman felt a little regretful, but she didn't insist that Xu Yan take it. Instead, she took out a bag and handed it to him, saying, "This is a gift for you, Young Master Xu. Xu Yan asked in puzzlement, "A gift?"

This was quite a generous amount - 300,000 spirit crystals, plus valuable spirit medicines. He wondered why she was thanking him.

"Young Master Xu, you killed the Killing Skeleton, so I've come to thank you. The Killing Skeleton had been terrorizing my Wushuang Hall for a long time, and we had a bounty on him. Now that you've killed him, you've avenged those from my Wushuang Hall who lost their lives to him. So I naturally want to express my gratitude to you."

The woman seemed very relieved and satisfied that the Killing Skeleton had been killed.

"The Killing Skeleton again?" Xu Yan was delighted. It seemed this Killing Skeleton was greatly hated. Although he hadn't been able to obtain any spirit crystals from him, only a skeleton staff, the bounty he received for killing him was a windfall.

"Then I won't be polite." Xu Yan readily accepted the gift.

"Young Master Xu, where are you going?" the woman asked with a smile after Xu Yan had taken the items.

"To take over Canglan Island," Xu Yan replied frankly.

"Then if I want to contact you, how should I do it?"

"You can go to Canglan Island, or have Xie Brother relay the message," Xu Yan said after a moment of thought.

"Young Master Xu, if you have the time, please come to the Wushuang Hall for a visit," the woman said expectantly.

"I'll take a look when I have the time," Xu Yan replied evasively.

He no longer needed to temper his mind, so there didn't seem to be a need to go to the Wushuang Hall. As for the beautiful woman? Beautiful women were not as interesting as the Dao of the Sword!

After the woman left, Xu Yan and the others continued on their way to Canglan Island.

"I didn't expect the Killing Skeleton to be worth so much. Killing him has really made me a fortune!" Xu Yan marveled.

Xie Lingfeng's mouth twitched. That woman from the Wushuang Hall was clearly just coming to give gifts. The excuse about the Killing Skeleton killing people from the Wushuang Hall was just a pretext.

The Wushuang Grand Master was also a peak Grand Master, whose strength was not much weaker than the Fiend Extermination Venerable.

He could only sigh that the truly extraordinary prodigies were welcomed wherever they went, especially by women.
