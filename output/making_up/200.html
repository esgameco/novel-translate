At this moment, they all recalled the rumors of the martial artists of the world hunting down the Demon Lord, although the details had been deliberately erased.

But their forces still had the remnants of those rumors.

The Grand Empress Dowager of the Great Yue Kingdom had a complex expression. The Demon Lord Blood Spirit, a being that suppressed the world, was rumored to be a master beyond the Grand Masters.

It turned out to be true! Li Xuan suddenly realized, the one he had almost killed Meng Chong was the legendary Demon Sect Demon Lord!

A Martial Dao Celestial Being expert.

Li Xuan calmly smiled and said, "That insect was just a remnant soul, trying to possess others to rise again, but was killed by my disciple."

"Attack, kill him!"

The Purple-Robed Azure-Faced, the Grand Empress Dowager of the Great Yue Kingdom, and others shouted in a deep voice.

Rumble!

With a wave of his hand, the Heavenly Earth Saber that dominated the world slashed through the sky, unstoppable.

With a punch, the wind and thunder raged, destroying everything! Xie Tian Heng and other Sword Saint Cliff experts were all shocked.

This was the might of the Profound Mysteries?

With their strength, they could naturally sense that the power Li Xuan was displaying did not have the pressure of a Celestial Being.

This meant that he was truly fighting at the same level!

Facing so many enemies, he did not use his realm to bully others, what an extraordinary figure! The Subduing Heaven Alliance's half-step Celestial Being experts, along with the peak Grand Masters, launched a terrifying attack, as if they could shatter the Cangjiang River and erase Canglan Island!

Immediately after, the thousand-zhang golden dragon thundered into the Subduing Heaven Alliance.

The unparalleled saber light, fists like wind and thunder, with every gesture, people were killed like ants! Li Xuan's gaze was indifferent, and in the blink of an eye, he unleashed the Mountain River Sword Dao, the Heavenly Earth Saber, the Descending Dragon Palm, and the Wind Thunder Golden Fist.

In just a moment, the Red-Robed Azure-Faced was blasted to pieces, the Grand Empress Dowager of the Great Yue Kingdom was destroyed, one peak Grand Master after another turned to ash and dispersed.

With every step forward, dozens of martial artists were reduced to ash.

On Canglan Island, everyone was shocked.

The might of the Profound Mysteries, so powerful? Xu Yan was already terrifyingly strong, able to easily kill half-step Celestial Beings after entering the Profound Mysteries.

But in comparison, the difference was too great, like a hundredfold!

Her master was a mountain that she could only look up to, unable to climb over.

The Subduing Heaven Alliance's experts were all terrified, roaring furiously and frantically unleashing their secret techniques.
