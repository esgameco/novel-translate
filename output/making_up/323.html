"This is getting interesting. Are they trying to kill that spiritual essence cultivator?"

Meng Chong shook his head, not in the mood to watch. Such assassinations and vendettas were constantly unfolding in the Spiritual Realm, and he had encountered several before.

"No good!" The cultivator who had refined Heaven and Earth's spiritual essence spoke in a grave tone, his aura exploding to the extreme.

"Manager Aqiu!" A cry rang out, and then a graceful purple figure rapidly fled.

The other party had obviously planned this in advance!

Even someone as strong as Manager Aqiu was likely doomed.

Even knowing she was no match, Zi Yun was unwilling to submit to her fate and prepared to fight to the death, when suddenly a massive fist struck from below.

"Who!" The old man blocking Zi Yun's path was alarmed, his aura erupting, about to flee, but that punch was too powerful, too fast, leaving him no time to dodge or retaliate.

Boom! He was instantly shattered and turned to dust.

Zi Yun was dumbfounded, such a fierce and powerful punch reminded her of someone.

"Meng Chong!" She looked towards the source of the strike, her expression one of disbelief.

That burly, muscular figure, that bald head, all told her that it was Meng Chong!

Meng Chong originally didn't want to get involved, but when he heard Zi Yun's name, he couldn't help but be moved, thinking it couldn't be a coincidence.

It turned out that Zi Yun was indeed being pursued. He immediately took action and killed that old man.

And the other party had even impersonated a Myriad Ages Alliance cultivator, trying to frame and implicate him.

Although he was not part of the Myriad Ages Alliance, his shidi (sworn brother) Fang Hao was the junior alliance master, the future alliance master. He had a deep connection with the Myriad Ages Alliance.

Zi Yun hurriedly looked up and said.

"Alright."

Meng Chong took to the air, and in an instant, he was on the battlefield.

At this moment, the one called Manager Aqiu was heavily injured and barely hanging on.

Boom! Meng Chong punched out, wind and thunder surging, the massive fist like condensed wind and thunder, with the might of a single punch completely annihilating the attackers.

Manager Aqiu was stunned, turning back to see Zi Yun clinging to a burly man, and he couldn't help but be surprised.

"Princess Zi Yun!"

"I'm fine!" Zi Yun breathed a sigh of relief, but seeing Manager Aqiu's severe injuries, if not treated promptly, it might even lead to the collapse of his foundation.

"Meng Chong, do you have any medicinal pills?"

Zi Yun asked.

"Here." Meng Chong handed her a bottle of pills, helplessly saying, "Can you not cling to me like this?"

Zi Yun smiled happily, getting off Meng Chong, pouring out a pill and giving it to Manager Aqiu. "Manager Aqiu, quickly take this to heal your injuries."

"A friend of mine!" Zi Yun's flushed face was filled with excitement. After giving the pill to Manager Aqiu, she said, "Manager Aqiu, you should go back to the imperial capital. Report this to my father the King, and he will escort me to the Qingqing Pavilion, so there won't be any more accidents."

Manager Aqiu hesitated, but Zi Yun urged, "Hurry back, he is much stronger than you."

Manager Aqiu didn't insist further.

After Manager Aqiu left, with no one else around, Zi Yun excitedly jumped up and hugged Meng Chong, asking, "Meng Chong, did you come to find me?"

"I suppose so." Meng Chong smacked his lips, "You're living quite well."

"It's alright, I'm now part of the Azure King lineage in Great Zhou. My foster father is the Azure King, but the rest of the Great Zhou imperial clan actually look down on me, thinking I'm from a lowly place in the Inner Realm and have disgraced them.

Zi Yun snorted coldly.

"This time, I'm lucky to have met you, Meng Chong, otherwise I would have been in danger. That Myriad Ages Alliance is so vicious, thinking the Azure King Mansion is easy to bully, so they targeted me."

Thinking of the recent crisis, Zi Yun gritted her teeth in anger.

"It has nothing to do with the Myriad Ages Alliance. Those assassins' methods are more like they come from a top-tier Spiritual Sect, or even a Transcendent Spiritual Sect."

Meng Chong shook his head.

"Where are you going?"

He asked Zi Yun.

"To the Qingqing Pavilion, of course. I've missed you, and Lingshou... and the elders."

Since Meng Chong said it had nothing to do with the Myriad Ages Alliance, Zi Yun didn't dwell on that issue.

"In that case, let's go together. I'm also going back to the Qingqing Pavilion."

Meng Chong nodded.

The two left the battlefield and headed towards the current location of the Qingqing Pavilion.

"Meng Chong, you're so amazing. Even in Great Zhou, I've heard of your reputation."

Zi Yun looked at him with admiration.
