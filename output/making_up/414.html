Here is my full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Li Xuan sat motionless, but a figure emerged from him.

It was identical to him, but had an extremely powerful aura, with an added sense of dominance.

"Since you have now entered the gate, today your master will demonstrate something to let you see what is meant by extreme soul martial arts," the extreme soul body said, looking at Jiang Buping.

"Yes, Master!"

Jiang Buping had a shocked expression on his face. He looked at the extreme soul body, then at his master still sitting leisurely in the chair, unable to tell for a moment what exactly was going on.

Su Lingxiu widened her beautiful eyes, staring up and down at the extreme soul body. After looking for quite a while, she found that this did not seem to be a divine element avatar or other type of分身 [fēnshēn, split body/avatar].

It was as if this was the master himself.

"Master, this is..."

Li Xuan smiled calmly and said, "How could you possibly understand your master's techniques? This is not an avatar, this is also your master's body."

Without further explanation, the extreme soul body looked towards the location of Dayue Heavenly Cave and said with a smile, "Let's go."

"Yes, Master!"

Su Lingxiu was extremely excited, and Jiang Buping's face was full of excitement - the master was going to take action.

Shi Er and the others felt the same way.

Feng Yan's face was full of anticipation, while also being shocked at how unfathomable this senior expert was.

At Dayue Heavenly Cave, although Fang Hao had set up a great formation using the power of heaven and earth, it was still under intense attack, as if it could be breached at any moment.

Below Dayue Heavenly Cave, several Blood Sons were taking action. Behind the cave entrance were dense crowds of blood slaves and blood disciples.

Boom!

Suddenly, a powerful blood-colored light transformed into a crimson long saber, slashing over fiercely. On the saber, it was as if crimson flames were burning.

As this slash was unleashed, thousands of blood slaves and over a dozen blood disciples behind it were refined into blood to fuel the power of this strike.

"Block it!"

Along with this slash came powerful attacks from the Blood Sons.

The group of True Kings and Heavenly Venerables hurriedly joined forces, using the power of the formation to resist this mighty slash.

The Dayue Emperor's face was extremely gloomy.

"I don't believe your Netherworld blood slaves can be endless, that you can refine them into blood at will!"

He gritted his teeth.

Had the Netherworld gone mad? Did they intend to exhaust the Netherworld's strength just to invade the Qinghua Realm?

Otherwise, why refine so many blood slaves on such a large scale? Wouldn't they be afraid of damaging the Netherworld's power? He couldn't help but recall the brutal great battle of the past.

The Netherworld had used similar invasion techniques before. In the end, the Qinghua Realm martial artists had used secret techniques to burn their own lives one after another. At a terrible cost, they had finally driven the Netherworld back.

Since then, the Netherworld had never used this kind of invasion technique again.

Now that they were using this invasion technique again, was it to force Qinghua Realm martial artists to trade life for life, depleting Qinghua Realm's overall strength? He couldn't help but think of Tianshadiying's purpose - this was likely related to Tianshadiying.

"Have all the Netherworld forces that invaded the Nine Mountains Realm's Heavenly Caves been transferred to invade our Qinghua Realm?"

The Dayue Emperor's face was extremely ugly.

Currently, the Nine Mountains Realm was in turmoil, making it difficult for the Qinghua Realm to request aid.

And requesting aid from other realms beyond the Nine Mountains Realm would involve a long journey, requiring passage through the Nine Mountains Realm. This meant that even if they successfully sent out a request for aid, it would take considerable time for reinforcements to arrive.

"This time, the casualties won't be as severe."

The Dayue Emperor looked at the great formation. With the formation's assistance, the casualties shouldn't be as massive.

Then his expression changed again.

His figure moved instantly, appearing in front of Fang Hao. His great halberd struck out, blocking a crimson blade slashing over from inside the Heavenly Cave.

This blade was actually formed from dozens of blood disciples refined into blood.

Had the Netherworld gone mad?

Even with numerous blood disciples, they shouldn't be consumed like this. To tear open the formation at such a great cost - would they refine Blood Sons into blood next time to unleash killing techniques?

Boom!

Under the combined defense of the formation and the Dayue Emperor's attack, this slash was blocked.

However, the Dayue Emperor's face changed drastically again the next moment.

At the rear of the Heavenly Cave, a Blood Son was walking forward, approaching the cave entrance. As he got closer, blood-colored flames surrounded him and his aura grew stronger and stronger.

Then, the blood slaves around him transformed into streams of blood-colored light, pouring into his body.

By the time this Blood Son reached the entrance of the Heavenly Cave, he had transformed into a crimson, cold, and murderous long saber.

The lead Blood Son raised his hand and grasped this crimson long saber. With a gloomy gaze, he looked at the Dayue Emperor and Fang Hao.

"Not good! Brother Fang, retreat quickly!"

The Dayue Emperor's face was pale with shock.

This saber formed from one Blood Son and thousands of blood slaves refined into blood was unimaginably powerful. Even he would have difficulty blocking it without using secret techniques.

The expressions of the other several Undying Heavenly Venerables also changed. One of them stepped forward, his aura surging, "Your Majesty, this subject will exhaust his cultivation to fight to the death with him!"

At this point, they had no choice but to fight desperately. Otherwise, the formation would definitely be breached, and the Netherworld enemies would surely take the opportunity to invade the Qinghua Realm.

Fang Hao also had a shocked expression. Had these Netherworld Blood Sons gone mad? How could they be so crazy?

In that instant, he felt somewhat helpless - his strength was ultimately still too weak.

Otherwise, how could he be threatened by a mere single slash?

As these thoughts flashed through his mind, the power of heaven and earth surged like great waves, heavenly laws descended, and light flowed over Fang Hao's body. The Qimen Weapon Box appeared, and various divine weapons flew out one after another.

He prepared to set up a great Qimen formation of heaven and earth to resist this slash from the Netherworld Blood Son.

At this moment, several figures descended.

"Master?!"

Fang Hao was stunned. He looked at his master, somewhat puzzled as to why his master seemed a bit different.

Li Xuan nodded, looking towards the bottom of the Heavenly Cave, especially at the saber formed by the Blood Son refining himself. He turned back to look at Jiang Buping and said, "Your master will only take action this one time. How much you can learn and comprehend depends entirely on yourself."

Jiang Buping's expression was solemn as he respectfully nodded and said, "Yes, Master!"

When the Dayue Emperor and others heard this, they were immediately overjoyed.

The senior expert was going to take action - Dayue Heavenly Cave would surely be swept clean! Looking at Jiang Buping, they couldn't help but feel extremely grateful. If the senior expert wasn't teaching his disciple, he probably wouldn't have taken action. It was all thanks to Jiang Buping.

At the bottom of the Heavenly Cave, that Netherworld Blood Son holding the crimson long saber had a somewhat grave expression. His gaze was fixed on Li Xuan's extreme soul body, sensing an intense feeling of danger.

Li Xuan raised his hand, and the Extreme Dao Soul Weapon appeared in his grasp.

As soon as the Extreme Dao Soul Weapon appeared, the Dayue Emperor and others had shocked expressions and hurriedly averted their gazes.

It was too terrifying!

Just looking at it for a moment gave them a feeling of their souls being torn apart.

What kind of divine weapon was this? Jiang Buping looked at the Extreme Dao Soul Weapon in his master's hand, then looked at his own Extreme Soul Divine Weapon, and immediately sighed - the difference was too great, far too great.

"This is the Extreme Dao Soul Weapon. You still need to work hard to nurture yours. Your soul weapon can only be considered basic."

Li Xuan gently stroked the Extreme Dao Soul Weapon with one hand.

His gaze fell on that Blood Son with the grave expression. With an expressionless face, he said calmly, "Your Netherworld likes to refine blood. Now I'm giving you a chance - continue refining blood, or you won't have another opportunity!"

"Kill!"

With a roar, they unleashed their strongest attack with the long swords in their hands.

They executed the great killing technique refined by blood!

Jiang Buping gripped the spear in his hand tightly, his eyes wide open, staring at his master's move. This spear thrust seemed ordinary, but it was actually the pinnacle of the Extreme Soul Martial Way.

Da Yue Huang and the others were even more shocked.

As the spear thrust out, slow and calm, a cold glint flashed at the entrance of the Heavenly Cave the instant the three Blood Children attacked.

This cold glint was not seen by the physical eye, but felt by the soul!

Puff! The eyes of the three Blood Children instantly became hollow. Their just-unleashed attacks dissipated, as if their souls and consciousness had been completely annihilated.

Li Xuan stepped forward, holding his spear as he entered the Heavenly Cave.

Such powerful Netherworld martial artists had almost broken through the cave's defenses, yet the elder had defeated all enemies with just one spear thrust.

Even more terrifying was that the elder seemed to have suppressed his own cultivation, striking out only at the Immortal Venerable realm?

Li Xuan nodded. This was another divine ability Jiang Buping had cultivated: Devouring Abyss!
