"That's right, it's the Hongze World!"

The Ancient Sea Turtle exclaimed excitedly.

The desolate world was dark and empty, with an aura of death permeating it. Looking from afar, it was just a vast expanse of darkness that even obscured the primordial chaos.

"Be careful, there may be some unexpected changes in the Hongze World," Jiang Buping said solemnly.

"Yes, be careful, especially watch out for the powerful experts from the Immortal Palace," the Ancient Sea Turtle nodded vigorously.

The flying ship slowed down and activated its concealment formation, silently approaching that patch of darkness.

As they got closer, they could gradually make out the outline of an enormous world.

But unlike the Great Wilderness which stood like a bright pearl amidst the primordial chaos, this world was gray and lifeless, even more devoid of vitality and desolate than the mountains formed from dead True Spirits.

Xu Yan's eyes glowed as he silently observed the distant world, probing for any hidden dangers.

Meng Chong, Fang Hao and Jiang Buping did the same.

The flying ship slowed down even more, occasionally hiding in small mountains along the way to temporarily avoid detection, then slowly approaching again. The closer they got to the Hongze World, the stronger the sense of desolation and bleakness became.

There were no Immortal True Spirits in this area.

It seemed even the Immortal True Spirits were unwilling to approach this lifeless world, unable to bear its desolation.

"This lifeless world seems to have an ominous aura amidst its bleakness," Xu Yan murmured.

The Ancient Sea Turtle's voice trembled slightly, "Should we still approach? After all, there's never been a lifeless world before. We don't know what changes might occur in a world after it dies. What if there really is something inauspicious?"

"Old Turtle, if you're scared, just wait for us here. The world is already dead, so what's there to fear even if it's inauspicious? The more unknown, the more worth exploring!" Meng Chong patted the Ancient Sea Turtle's head.

"A little fear is normal, just overcome it!" the Ancient Sea Turtle said with an awkward smile.

"Then stop shaking. If we're attacked, I doubt you'll be able to respond, let alone control the flying ship!" Fang Hao shook his head.

"I'm shaking from excitement, not fear!" the Ancient Sea Turtle insisted stubbornly.

"Sure, keep being excited then." Fang Hao chuckled.

As the flying ship got closer to the Hongze World, the sense of desolation and bleakness washed over them like a tide. It was as if a dark aura enveloped the entire world.

Xu Yan and his three fellow disciples silently gazed at the world before them, looking at the uneven ground devoid of any signs of life, without a single plant growing.

It was as if an ominous aura permeated the entire world.

In front of the Hongze World was a large mountain. This mountain did not seem to be formed from a dead True Spirit, but rather appeared to be a mountain from within the Hongze World that had broken off and now accompanied it.

"Let's stop briefly on that mountain!" Xu Yan said solemnly.

The lifeless world seemed to be shrouded in an ominous aura. Moreover, the people from the Immortal Palace were also searching for the Hongze World, so they couldn't afford to be careless.

Xu Yan had already observed that mountain. It also appeared desolate, but there were no potential dangers or signs of Immortal Palace experts lying in ambush.

"O-okay!" The Ancient Sea Turtle's voice shook even more, his control of the flying ship's formation disk trembling slightly.

Xu Yan glanced at him. This turtle had clearly been traumatized in the past. Even after countless years, he still couldn't control his trembling when approaching the Hongze World.

He finally understood why the Ancient Sea Turtle had given up on the potential opportunities in the Hongze World.

It was simply because the Ancient Sea Turtle was too afraid. The Hongze World had become a source of fear for him. Unable to control his fear, even the greatest opportunity would turn into disaster.

The flying ship slowly approached the mountain. As they got closer, Xu Yan and the others continued to focus their attention on the Hongze World ahead.

Suddenly, a thunderous boom rang out. The Ancient Sea Turtle, who was controlling the flying ship, was so startled that he instantly retracted into his shell with a whoosh.

Thud! The Ancient Sea Turtle fell onto the flying ship. In an instant, he seemed to lose all signs of life, turning into an ordinary dead turtle - just an empty shell! The formation disk of the flying ship also fell.

Xu Yan and the others: ...

Fang Hao picked up the formation disk and looked at the Ancient Sea Turtle, who was using his turtle breath technique to play dead. He was speechless.

This turtle was really too cowardly!

"What was that sound just now?" Meng Chong scratched his head in confusion.

"It seems to be a battle!" Xu Yan's expression was solemn. That thunderous sound had come from extremely far away, yet it still reached them.

Moreover, just from the rumbling sound, they could tell it must be an extremely powerful battle. The combatants must be at the level of World Lords.

"Could it be two True Spirits fighting?" Jiang Buping asked in surprise.

Battles breaking out between True Spirits wasn't uncommon.

Boom! Another sound came, this time continuous, as if from some powerful offensive techniques clashing.

"It doesn't sound like True Spirits fighting," Xu Yan said gravely.

True Spirit battles were always accompanied by roars, but apart from the rumbling of combat, there were no roars to be heard.

Moreover, judging from the sounds, it seemed more like a battle between powerful experts using mighty secret techniques.

"Old Turtle, stop playing dead and come out!" Xu Yan kicked the turtle shell.

After a long while, a head finally emerged from the shell.

"What happened?" The Ancient Sea Turtle's eyes darted around, looking ready to retract into his shell again at any moment.

"There are powerful beings fighting in the Immortal Realm!" Xu Yan said solemnly.

By now, the flying ship had landed on the large mountain, with its body embedded into the mountain to conceal it.

"Powerful beings fighting?" The Ancient Sea Turtle was puzzled. After listening for a while to the gradually weakening sounds coming from extremely far away, he finally emerged completely from his shell, returning to his normal form.

"Ahem, that was a survival technique. You all reacted too slowly. In the Immortal Realm, as soon as you sense any unusual changes, the first thing to do is hide yourself and conceal your aura. This is all experience!" The Ancient Sea Turtle said with a serious expression.

Xu Yan couldn't be bothered to argue. This turtle was not only cowardly, but refused to admit it, always finding excuses.

"Alright Old Turtle, we understand. But listen to those sounds - it's clearly powerful beings fighting, and they must be at the World Lord level. Besides the Immortal Palace and the former Seven World Lords, are there other World Lord level experts in the Immortal Realm?" Xu Yan patted the Ancient Sea Turtle's head as he asked.
