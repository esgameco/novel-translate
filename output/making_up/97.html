Chapter 97 As Long as You Bury the Person, They Won't Do Evil Again

The Great Sun Golden Body, opening thirty-six golden body acupoints, with eighteen acupoints in one small cycle, is the cultivation of the Innate Realm.

After the eighteen acupoints are opened and nourished to form a cycle, although one cannot transform into a three-zhang golden body, once it is set in motion, it can also make the physical body grow larger by one circle.

When the thirty-six acupoints form a cycle and the Heavenly Talisman Profundity is opened, one can transform into a three-zhang golden body.

This already belongs to the realm beyond the Innate, which only the most powerful can possess.

Li Xuan finished explaining the Great Sun Golden Body, then went on to a familiar topic, further deepening his disciple's awareness, saying, "What I teach you is the Dao. Even if it is a martial arts cultivation method, it is the foundation of the Dao.

"So you must re-awaken, not focus on the form, but comprehend the true meaning of the cultivation method. Only then can you succeed in cultivation."

The cultivation method he compiled is like a basket, with not much inside it, requiring the disciple to comprehend and fill the basket.

Only then will it be complete.

He has only given the disciple a basket, and when the disciple fills it, he will obtain the full harvest.

Therefore, after passing on the cultivation method to the disciple, he must make the disciple understand that he must fill the basket.

"The profundity of the Great Sun Golden Body lies in opening the acupoints, in nourishing the acupoints. Each person's nourished acupoints are not exactly the same. One person's nourished acupoint may have the profound power of rapid injury recovery, while another person's nourished acupoint may have a different profundity."

Li Xuan began to supplement the possible loopholes in the golden body acupoints he had compiled.

For example, he said that this acupoint, after being nourished, can make the physical body's injuries recover quickly.

If Meng Chong opens and nourishes it, but the acupoint's profundity is not as such, wouldn't it damage the image of this senior master?

Li Xuan would not leave such loopholes, so he told the disciple that the profundity of the opened and nourished acupoints of each person is different, and some profundities may not be nourished by certain people.

In this way, no matter what profundity Meng Chong opens and nourishes, or even if he fails to nourish any profundity, it will not be considered a mistake in the master's teachings.

After all, he has already said that the profundity of the opened and nourished acupoints of each person is different, and some may even fail to nourish any profundity.

Not every acupoint can be nourished to have profundity.

"The human body does not only have thirty-six acupoints. If you discover other acupoints during your cultivation, do not be surprised. If you can open them and complete the cycle, you can also open them.

"However, the thirty-six acupoints are the great acupoints of the golden body, the foundation."

Li Xuan said solemnly.

After all, the acupoints are not just thirty-six, and if Meng Chong discovers acupoints outside the thirty-six during his cultivation, and his master did not mention them, it may damage the image of the all-knowing master.

"Yes, Master!"

Meng Chong was now in an excited state.

The Great Sun Golden Body is truly immensely powerful.

"You must ponder and cultivate diligently, do not be negligent in your own cultivation. Temper the sword with patience, be calm and composed, and you will naturally have insights."

After explaining the Great Sun Golden Body, Li Xuan spoke in a stern teacher's tone.

"Yes, Master, I understand!"

Meng Chong nodded.

"Martial Dao is boundless, step by step, cultivate steadfastly. The Great Sun Golden Body is just the starting point on the path of Martial Dao."

Seeing his disciple like a chicken with its blood stirred up, still in an excited state.

Li Xuan said solemnly.

Meng Chong was startled, hurriedly calming his excited mood, respectfully saying, "Yes, Master, I understand. I will cultivate steadfastly without haste or rashness!"

"Good, continue your cultivation."

Li Xuan nodded, then his figure disappeared in an instant.

Meng Chong took a few deep breaths to calm himself down.

"In my eyes, the Great Sun Golden Body is already very powerful, but the Master said it is just the starting point on the path of Martial Dao. I cannot be complacent or hasty!

"Martial Dao is boundless, beyond the Great Sun Golden Body, there are even stronger things waiting for me!"

Meng Chong's gaze was determined, and he sat down again to begin his cultivation.

While cultivating, he also tempered the sword and began to ponder the Great Sun Golden Body.

"The cultivation of the Great Sun Golden Body is to open the acupoints. How to open the acupoints, I need to ponder carefully..."

Just as he had previously pondered the Great Sun Golden Armor, Meng Chong began to ponder the Great Sun Golden Body. Now he is only at the peak of the Qi and Blood Realm, still some distance from the Innate Realm.

So he was not impatient.

He cultivated the Great Sun Golden Armor while tempering the sword, and also took time to ponder the Great Sun Golden Body.

First, he familiarized himself with the thirty-six acupoints. The cultivation of the Great Sun Golden Body also starts from opening the Heavenly Bridge and establishing the Dantian and Sea of Qi, but the subsequent cultivation is different from the orthodox martial arts.

The cultivation focuses on the golden body acupoints. It needs to be determined which acupoint to open first.

Meng Chong was pondering the Great Sun Golden Body.

As for Li Xuan, he returned to the courtyard and finally heaved a sigh of relief, handing the basket of the Great Sun Golden Body to Meng Chong, waiting for him to comprehend and fill it.

"It should be fine, right? Meng Chong's insight is also good, comprehending it should not be a problem, just a matter of time."

Li Xuan pondered carefully.

He felt that Meng Chong should be able to comprehend the Great Sun Golden Body.

"Next, I will pass on the cultivation method beyond the Innate Realm to Xu Yan."

Li Xuan felt quite helpless.

Fortunately, he is now a true martial artist, and with the reference of the inner domain martial arts cultivation methods, he has great confidence in the cultivation method he has compiled.

Rather than saying he has confidence in his own compiled cultivation method, it is better to say he has confidence in Xu Yan!

"Xu Yan should be able to comprehend the cultivation method. The cultivation method beyond the Innate Realm should not be a problem for him. My martial arts undertaking depends on him to open up."

Li Xuan sighed.

In the past, he just casually compiled it, but Xu Yan was able to comprehend and cultivate it successfully.

Now the cultivation method he has compiled is more complete, the theory is more solid, the levels are clearer, with reason and evidence. With Xu Yan's prodigy talent, cultivating it should not be a problem.

Although it is said that the higher the realm, the more difficult the cultivation.

After all, the cultivation method is more complete now.

"When Xu Yan returns, I will pass on the cultivation method beyond the Innate Realm to him."

Xu Yan went to the capital of Qi State to guide those people under his maternal grandfather.

"About a few more days, he should be able to break through to the small success of the Innate Realm, right?"

Li Xuan estimated that Xu Yan would break through to the small success of the Innate Realm in a few more days.

He glanced at the arduously cultivating Shi Er.

His Impure Refinement was about to be perfected.

"The difference in talent is truly huge."

Li Xuan sighed inwardly.

Even after Shi Er's Impure Refinement is perfected, to break through the Qi and Blood Realm and succeed in the martial arts, it will still take some time for him to overcome the bottleneck of the Impure Refinement.

"This is like the bottleneck from the Fourth Grade to the Third Grade in the inner domain martial arts?"

Li Xuan thought to himself.

With nothing to do, he took out the ancient leather book and opened the first page, as usual, carefully studying it.

"I have a feeling that if I can understand some clues, I will surely gain something.

"This Taisui, is it a cultivation method?

"But it doesn't seem like a cultivation method, sometimes it looks like a map, like a terrain, or like a ghost talisman..."

The ancient leather book is obviously not simple, but he has not been able to understand its secrets.
