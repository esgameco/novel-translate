To become the Heavenly Dao, there was another crucial step - Tianzi must transform into principles himself, using himself as the core to integrate and operate the principles of heaven and earth.

Tianzi was the spirit of heaven and earth, so it was not difficult for him to transform into principles without losing his self-awareness. However, he would ultimately have to give up his current physical body.

"Once you successfully become the Heavenly Dao of Daxia, extend to the Spirit Realm, Divine Realm, and finally the entire world..."

Tianzi was extremely excited, clenching his fists. "I will definitely succeed. I will become the Heavenly Dao. Taicang is dead. From now on, this is the Daxia world, my world!"

Tianzi screamed in agony, cursing and swearing as his body gradually dispersed, finally turning into a ball of purple light.

At a certain moment, the purple light merged into the world. In a trance, it seemed to have transformed into a principle, but this principle was somewhat different from the others.

On this day, the warriors of Daxia all looked up in confusion, not knowing why, but feeling that the sky seemed somewhat different.

They couldn't quite put their finger on how it was different. It was as if there was a heavenly majesty contained within.

"Hahaha, I'm finally free, I'm finally carefree..."

A voice that no one could hear rang out in the Daxia world. Tianzi looked at every blade of grass and tree in Daxia, at every cultivating person, extremely excited and finding it all very interesting.

"Oh right, I'm the Heavenly Dao now. I need to have dignity, I need to have rules..."

Tianzi took a deep breath and began to establish the rules of the Heavenly Dao, taking the first step to becoming the Heavenly Dao.

Li Xuan had already taught him how to establish the rules of the Heavenly Dao and what rules to establish, so Tianzi just needed to follow his instructions.

Li Xuan looked at the sky of Daxia with a bright smile, watching as Tianzi was transforming into the Heavenly Dao, and couldn't help but feel anticipation.

"The Heavenly Dao ah, Taicang had no Heavenly Dao, but Daxia has a Heavenly Dao. Those who covet Daxia will ultimately have to bear the might of the Heavenly Dao."

Although Daxia's strength had progressed rapidly, its foundation was still shallow. The spiritual energy had indeed improved, but due to the short time, there was not enough accumulation, and there was a lack of heavenly and earthly treasures.

Now that Tianzi had become the Heavenly Dao of Daxia, his primary task was to quickly increase Daxia's strength, allow heavenly and earthly treasures to appear in Daxia, and even expand Daxia's territory.

Li Xuan had taught Tianzi everything about how the Heavenly Dao strengthens the world, how to establish the rules of the Heavenly Dao, what kind of rules are beneficial to both the Heavenly Dao and promote the strengthening of the world, and how to promote more efficient cultivation for warriors.

And now, Tianzi was becoming the newborn Heavenly Dao, in the process of transformation, in the process of establishing the rules of the Heavenly Dao. This required some time and process.

But it wouldn't take too long for Tianzi to become the Heavenly Dao of this part of the Daxia world. After he became proficient in operating the rules of the Heavenly Dao and improved Daxia's strength, he would gradually and continuously expand, eventually incorporating the entire world.

In the future, there would only be the Daxia world, and no Taicang world.

Taicang was dead, Daxia should be established! From the moment Tianzi shouted this sentence, the fate of the Taicang world was determined.

"This world, after all, is now related to me," Li Xuan sighed with emotion.

"Those guys in the Dao Realm, they think too much. Wanting to fight for the position of the world's master is purely wishful thinking. Just wait to bear the might of the glorious Heavenly Dao in the future."

Li Xuan was gleeful inside.

Tianzi had been locked up for so long, while those realm lords had been living comfortably. How could Tianzi tolerate this? He would definitely not spare those realm lords from trouble.

Daxia was changing, and this change was silent and imperceptible. The warriors of Daxia couldn't even detect how the changes in Daxia were different from before.

After all, since the increase of spiritual energy in Daxia, changes in the world had been constant.

"He's about to become the newborn Heavenly Dao," Li Xuan was full of anticipation.

The remaining shackles on Tianzi didn't need his help to remove. As Tianzi became the Heavenly Dao, he could slowly remove the shackles himself as the Heavenly Dao expanded.

Li Xuan was waiting for feedback from the Golden Book of the Great Dao. The Heavenly Dao was his creation, and it was also his bestowal of a new concept to this world, so he would surely receive generous feedback.

"It should allow my strength to further improve," Li Xuan was secretly excited.

Nothing was more satisfying than an increase in strength.

Li Xuan couldn't be bothered to pay attention to the changes in the Divine Realm anymore. He was in Daxia, guiding Tianzi on how to transform into the Heavenly Dao, how to be a good Heavenly Dao, and how to establish the rules of the Heavenly Dao.

These rules were not only the foundation of the world's operation but also the source of the Heavenly Dao's strength. They also needed to have the fundamental principle of "supreme impartiality". All things in the world...
