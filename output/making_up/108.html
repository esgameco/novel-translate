Chapter 108 Meng Chong's Qi and Blood are Complete, a Dangerous Aura

In the capital of the Wu Kingdom, after Meng Chong, there was another uproar. The ministers trembled in fear, and the Wu Emperor's face was deathly pale.

The renowned elder of the jianghu, Senior Wu, had single-handedly stormed the imperial palace and slashed open a long gully with a single stroke, shocking the ministers and the Wu Emperor.

The imperial guards were all shivering in fear, not daring to step forward.

Even Senior Wu's disciple was hiding far away, realizing that something was off with his master.

In the court, the Blood Spirit Patriarch casually crushed a hapless minister to death and sat on the imperial throne, resting a serrated knife by his feet. The Wu Emperor stood pale-faced before the ministers.

This Senior Wu was even more ferocious than Meng Chong, ready to kill at any moment.

"So you are the Emperor of the Wu Kingdom?" the Blood Spirit Patriarch looked down at the Wu Emperor.

"Yes, yes, Senior Wu, what are your orders?" the Wu Emperor replied tremblingly.

His heart was filled with resentment. As the sovereign of a kingdom, he was now like a servant, awaiting his master's commands.

"Find a man named Meng Chong, and bring him to me!" the Blood Spirit Patriarch bared his teeth.

The Wu Emperor heaved a sigh of relief and hurriedly said, "No problem, I'll have someone find him immediately. Meng Chong has no respect for his elders, and I've long been dissatisfied with him.

"Senior Wu, please wait a few days, he will soon come to the capital of the Wu Kingdom."

The Blood Spirit Patriarch stood up, "Very good, this seat awaits your news. If you fail to find the man, you all... will become my food!"

"Although the taste is a bit lacking, it's still better than nothing!"

The Blood Spirit Patriarch walked past the Wu Emperor, casually crushing another hapless minister to death, and then left.

The Wu Emperor was completely drained, collapsing to the ground. The rest of the ministers were in the same state.

Their former lofty status had changed since the Meng Chong incident, and today was even more unbearable, almost costing them their lives.

All the ministers felt utterly humiliated.

In the face of absolute power, their so-called authority was so easily shattered.

"Dismiss the court!" the chief eunuch hurriedly ordered the ministers to leave.

He supported the Wu Emperor, "Your Majesty, what are we to do?"

"Find Meng Chong, notify Meng Chong that I have a request for him, and let him come to the capital!" the Wu Emperor gritted his teeth.

If Meng Chong didn't come, the consequences would be unimaginable. And he knew that given Meng Chong's character, if he heard that the Emperor had a request, he would surely come.

"Tell Meng Chong that there is a martial arts expert in the capital of the Wu Kingdom!" the Wu Emperor paused and then said solemnly.

"But... we don't know where Meng Chong is," the chief eunuch said worriedly.

"Have the Heavenly Mother Sect spread the news, the jianghu will have its own channels of information," the Wu Emperor pondered for a moment.

"Yes, Your Majesty!" the chief eunuch ordered it to be done.

In a secluded palace of the Wu Kingdom, the Wu Emperor entered an ancient stone house.

"You said this place cannot be cultivated, but one martial artist after another has appeared. What's going on?" a mysterious voice sounded from the stone house after a while.

"Haha, you're trying to trick me again, impossible, absolutely impossible, I won't fall for it," the voice continued.

The Wu Emperor took a deep breath and said solemnly, "I'm not tricking you. A renowned figure from the jianghu of my Wu Kingdom came to the palace today, wanting to find a martial artist.

"His strength is extremely terrifying, and he even drinks blood!"

The stone house fell silent for a while, and then the mysterious voice spoke again, "Really? You're not lying?"

"I can swear an oath!" the Wu Emperor replied.

"There's no reason for it. The remote border, the spiritual mechanisms of heaven and earth have already died, how could martial artists appear? If the spiritual mechanisms of heaven and earth were to recover, I would definitely come out," the mysterious voice muttered to itself.

"You taught me the cultivation method, let me try it, and then I'll know. Even if I can't cultivate, you won't lose anything," the Wu Emperor seized the opportunity to speak.

"Haha, I knew it, you're trying to trick me, all of you are liars, wah... all liars, a group of liars, how can there be so many liars in this world..." the mysterious voice became somewhat frantic.

The Wu Emperor frowned, once again getting nothing.

"I'm not lying to you, martial artists have truly appeared, I swear!" the Wu Emperor pleaded.

"Liars... wah, liars, I won't fall for it, I definitely won't, I'm so smart, I won't fall for it..."

"Just consider it, teach me something a bit inferior, or even just an entry-level cultivation method, I really haven't lied to you, martial artists have truly appeared..."

The Wu Emperor's tone carried a hint of begging.

However, the mysterious voice ignored him, repeatedly saying, "Liars, all liars..."

The Wu Emperor left the stone house helplessly and with discontent.

Meng Chong felt his treasured sword trembling, almost in sync with his own heartbeat. Whenever his palm gripped the hilt, it felt as if it was a part of his own body.

It was as if this sword had been forged bit by bit by him, imbued with consciousness and a soul.

"It's almost time!" Meng Chong had an intuition that the day to draw the sword was not far away.

When the sword was unsheathed, it would be the day the sword's spirit awakened.

"I feel that under my nourishment, this sword contains tremendous power. When I draw it, it may be able to slay a Sect Master!" Meng Chong thought to himself.

After nourishing the treasured sword for so long, his qi and blood had been constantly circulating. The sword had accumulated tremendous power under his nourishment.

When this sword was unsheathed, it would surely shake the heavens and the earth.

"I also have a clue about the Great Sun Golden Body, I'm almost able to comprehend it," Meng Chong thought.

At a certain moment during his cultivation, his body flashed with iridescent golden light, and a bell-like sound resonated within his body.

The Great Sun Golden Bell was complete!

"My qi and blood are now complete, I only need to fully comprehend the Great Sun Golden Body, successfully nourish the sword, and then I can start working on breaking through to the Xiantian Realm!"

Meng Chong's heart was filled with excitement.

The Xiantian Realm was not far away.

In the courtyard, Li Xuan was studying an ancient leather book, while also keeping an eye on Su Ling Xiu's cultivation.

"This girl's bone refinement speed has finally slowed down."

Su Ling Xiu was currently refining golden bones, and the speed had finally slowed down. To refine the golden bones to perfection would require a significant amount of time.

However, Su Ling Xiu was concocting medicinal pills to assist her bone refinement, so her cultivation progress was not too slow.

"At this rate, after her golden bones are perfected, she will encounter another bottleneck. How to break through that bottleneck and refine the evergreen bones, that is a problem," Li Xuan also fell into contemplation.

Whether Su Ling Xiu could refine the evergreen bones was related to his own gains, so he was also pondering how she could refine the evergreen bones.

"The characteristic of the evergreen bones is eternal vitality, and the general methods cannot refine them. As for medicinal pills..."

Li Xuan mulled it over but had no clue.

He could only let Su Ling Xiu try to comprehend it herself. He had already passed on the Medicinal Dao Classics to her, and with her talent, she might be able to find a solution.

"Your disciple Meng Chong, the Great Sun Golden Bell is complete, your physical body has been enhanced, and your true qi has been elevated."
