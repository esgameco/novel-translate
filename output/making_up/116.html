Chapter 116 The Inner Realm, I, Xu Yan, Have Arrived

The Red Cat stared with wide eyes, seeming a bit confused, as if unable to understand what Li Xuan was saying.

"After all, it is a fierce beast. Although it appears to have intelligence, how could it possibly comprehend such profound things?"

Li Xuan shook his head and smiled wryly.

He had been overly idealistic, actually thinking that the Red Cat could comprehend the Dao of the Great Demons.

He played with the jade scepter in his hand and left with a leisurely pace.

Outside the manor, on the hillside.

Meng Chong sat cross-legged, fully immersed in the contemplation of the Great Sun Golden Body. He was just a step away from fully comprehending the cultivation method of the Great Sun Golden Body.

The senior brother had gone to the Inner Realm, and he too yearned to go there.

As for why he did not go to the Inner Realm immediately, aside from feeling that his strength was still not enough, there was another more important factor - his Qi and Blood Realm could not take flight.

Without entering the Primal Realm, he could not truly unleash his Furious Thunder Soaring Body Technique.

"The Great Sun Golden Body, after connecting the Heavenly and Earthly Bridge, opens the Qi Sea. With the Qi Sea as the foundation, it opens the Golden Body Acupoints. In the Primal Realm, eighteen Golden Body Acupoints are opened, forming a small cycle.

"Each acupoint can nurture the profundities of the physical body. With all eighteen acupoints opened, one can also possess a portion of the transformations of the Great Sun Golden Body, although not to the extent of a thirty-foot Golden Body, a ten-foot Golden Body should be no problem."

Meng Chong's mind flashed with insight, and the mysteries of the Great Sun Golden Body began to unfold before him.

The Golden Body Acupoints had been determined, and he even discovered some other acupoints through his own comprehension.

"The Great Sun Golden Body is right before my eyes."

Meng Chong was extremely excited.

He did not know how many days he had spent in contemplation on the hillside, but at this moment, he had finally comprehended the Great Sun Golden Body!

Sitting in a chair, he looked at an ancient leather book while using a bamboo cane to point at the acupoints on the Red Cat's body. "Red Cat, these are acupoints. If you can channel the power of the medicinal pills you've eaten into one of these acupoints, you might gain some benefits!"

The Red Cat's tail swished as it lay on the ground, its gaze seeming to understand yet not understand.

Suddenly, a golden light emerged.

Li Xuan's heart rejoiced, Meng Chong had comprehended the Great Sun Golden Body.

"Your disciple Meng Chong has comprehended the Great Sun Golden Body you created. He has entered the Great Sun Golden Body."

Boom!

In that instant, Li Xuan felt that his own acupoints were opening, nurturing profound powers. His physical body was rapidly enhanced, strengthening by more than tenfold in a moment.

"So this is the Great Sun Golden Body!"

Li Xuan had already obtained the complete cultivation method of the Great Sun Golden Body. The martial dao system of his physical body, from the Qi and Blood Realm to the Primal Realm, had all been fully developed.

In the Primal Realm Great Sun Golden Body, eighteen acupoints are opened, but as a beginner, he had only opened three acupoints. These three acupoints were constantly nurturing profound powers, continuously nourishing his physical body.

"With my current physical body, even if I stand still, a Sect Master would be unable to harm me."

Li Xuan was overjoyed. This Great Sun Golden Body he had created was quite powerful.

His strength had taken another leap forward.

The day when he could crush a Great Sect Master with a single hand was not far away.

Now that Meng Chong had comprehended the Great Sun Golden Body, his breakthrough to the Primal Realm was also imminent.

"When Xu Yan broke through, he transformed into the Dragon Soaring Mountain and River Spiritual Bone. Will Meng Chong also undergo a transformation?"

Li Xuan pondered this.

Xu Yan's talent was simply too monstrous, and he was the pioneer of the martial dao. Li Xuan suspected that he possessed great fortune, belonging to the category of a Child of Fate.

That was why his breakthrough to the Primal Realm resulted in a transformation.

Meng Chong ultimately lacked a bit compared to Xu Yan, so he might not necessarily undergo a transformation.

"Once Meng Chong breaks through, and my Great Sun Golden Body in the Primal Realm is fully mastered, with my strength further enhanced, I'll be able to easily defeat Great Sect Masters with a single punch or palm strike. Even if faced with dozens of Great Sect Masters surrounding me, I'll be able to handle them with ease."

Thinking this, Li Xuan became extremely excited.

After obtaining the complete Great Sun Golden Body technique, he now looked at the Red Cat and had some new ideas. Tapping the Red Cat's body with the bamboo cane, he said, "I'll teach you the Dao of the Great Demons. You must cultivate it diligently. Even if you're just a pet, I can't let you be completely useless. Especially with your huge body, you're not even a bit cute.

"If you can't comprehend it, I might just have to consider stewing you!"

With a threatening tone, he looked at the Red Cat with a smile.

The Red Cat's eyes widened, and it trembled in fear.

"I'm telling you, you have to comprehend it well..."

Li Xuan tapped the Red Cat's body again, carefully explaining the new Dao of the Great Demons to it word by word.

He did not expect the Red Cat to remember it all at once. He would repeat it to it every day. If the Red Cat's intelligence was indeed decent, it should be able to remember it over time.

If the Red Cat truly cultivated the Dao of the Great Demons, would that mean he had created the martial dao of the Great Demons? Would he also receive some kind of feedback?

Li Xuan was filled with anticipation.

In the capital of the Qi Kingdom.

Guo Rongshan looked at the letter, then opened the box, revealing a statue inside.

The statue was posed with one hand behind the back and the other holding a jade scepter, slightly tilting the head upwards, exuding an aura of a transcendent immortal.

The facial features were only roughly outlined, but they gave a sense of youthful handsomeness and refinement.

If Li Xuan saw this statue, he would find that although the facial features were only roughly outlined, the shadow of his own likeness could still be vaguely discerned.

"The Ancestor of the Martial Dao, the Venerable Teacher? The Martial Dao Saint!"

Guo Rongshan's expression was solemn and reverent as he carefully lifted the statue and placed it on the table.

"Also, copy a cultivation technique. I will present it to the Emperor in the palace."

Guo Rongshan continued.

"Father, that cannot be done!" Guo Yunkai exclaimed in alarm.

"Do you think the techniques have not already been leaked?" Guo Rongshan looked at him. "The remote borders lack martial dao, but we are now ahead of the curve. Actively passing on the techniques has a different meaning than passive leakage. Do you understand?"

"What does Father mean?"

Guo Rongshan pointed to the letter on the table. Guo Yunkai picked it up and read it, then let out a sigh of relief. "I understand now, Father!"

The Qi Emperor had been feeling a bit more at ease lately, but he still felt constrained.

He had never defied the Emperor.

"Brother Guo, what matter do you have? You can make your own decision, and I will not refuse!"

Xu Yan's heart was filled with excitement. After briefly orienting himself, he headed straight for the nearest city to the boundless mountains.
