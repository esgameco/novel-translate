Yin Hong's face was gloomy and dark. He had one hand behind his back, trembling slightly. In that moment just now, he had almost died!

That sword strike was too powerful!

"Young man, who are you? You must think carefully before getting involved in this matter. Can you bear the consequences?" Yin Hong said ominously.

He knew that continuing the battle would not lead to victory. In fact, he might even end up in a dangerous situation.

Xu Yan raised an eyebrow and said, "What, are you trying to threaten me?"

Yin Hong sneered and looked at the approaching Du Yuying and the others. "The Du family is too deeply involved in this matter. Even Grand Masters don't dare to get involved easily."

"You have obstructed me from taking the Du girl. You have offended the influential person behind me. You must think carefully about the consequences. Someone will come to settle the score with you," Yin Hong said coldly.

Du Yuying frowned slightly and said softly, "Young master, don't worry. My Du family will resolve this matter for you."

Yin Hong sneered, "Young man, do you think you can rely on the Du family? Being a protector is not that easy. The influential person behind me, the Du family cannot handle them!"

Du Yuying furrowed her brows. For Yin Hong, a half-step Grand Master, to mention an influential person, it was clear that the person behind the scenes was extraordinary.

She couldn't help but be suspicious. Yin Hong and the mastermind behind the attack on her were obviously not the same person.

Xu Yan frowned, muttering to himself, "I'm just trying to earn some spirit stones and spirit herbs. How hard can that be? The person behind this guy is obviously not simple. If they want to cause me trouble, I'll have to be careful wherever I go. There might even be a Grand Master coming after me."

He needed to earn the spirit stones and spirit herbs, and he had already escorted Du Yuying here. After a fierce battle, how could he back down now?

Moreover, even if he retreated, the other side would not let him off.

"Young master, you don't need to worry. You can rest at my Du family for a while. After this storm blows over, I guarantee no one will involve you in this matter," Du Yuying said solemnly.

However, Xu Yan was not interested. He was here to explore the inner realm, not to ally with any particular force.

Yin Hong further threatened, "Young man, this matter involves too many people. Although your strength is not weak, I advise you to withdraw. If you insist on getting involved, you will be making an enemy of the influential person behind me. Think carefully about the consequences."

Xu Yan sneered, "Threaten me? Are you worthy of that? Is the influential person behind you worthy?"

A half-step Grand Master in the Dao of Martial Arts could not directly break through to the Grand Master realm. He must be a waste. How dare he threaten him? Who gave him the courage?

Without further ado, Xu Yan thrust out his sword, and the mountains and rivers appeared, the dragon's roar resounding.

This was the sword dao he had just comprehended - the Mountains and Rivers, Dragon's Roar!

Such a good opponent to refine his sword dao, how could Xu Yan let him go?

Yin Hong's expression changed drastically. He knew the terrifying power of this sword strike. With his folding fan in hand, he unleashed wave after wave of surging tidal forces, while also inwardly gathering his spiritual focus to resist the dreadful influence of the dragon's roar.

Even so, the moment the dragon's roar sounded, he felt as if he was being drawn into a world of mountains and rivers, as if he was about to be buried in them.

"Ah!" Yin Hong roared angrily, constantly unleashing his full power, the surging tides rolling, allowing him to break free from the mountains and rivers and quickly retreat.

Sizzle!

His robe was slashed open on the chest, and he was furious and alarmed.

"Dare to threaten me? A half-baked thing, who gave you the courage?" Xu Yan struck out with sword after sword.

The dragon's roar sounded continuously, the mountains and rivers constantly appearing, relentlessly attacking Yin Hong, who was forced to dodge and evade, disheveled and in disarray. His purple robe was almost reduced to rags, and there were fine blood traces on his shoulder.

However, he was still a half-step Grand Master. Although he was in a sorry state, he had not suffered any fatal injuries.

"I can't continue like this. This young man's sword technique is bizarre, and it seems to be getting stronger the more we fight. If I'm not careful, I might end up falling at his hands!" Yin Hong's heart was filled with trepidation, and he had already developed the intention to retreat.

Du Yuying and the others were deeply shaken. What kind of sword technique was this, so powerful and seemingly containing some profound celestial mystery, forcing Yin Hong, a half-step Grand Master, into such a sorry state?

This was a half-step Grand Master, a terrifying existence far surpassing ordinary Sect Masters. And Yin Hong was said to be one of the best among the half-step Grand Masters, a figure that ordinary Grand Masters could not easily defeat.

Yet now, he had lost his elegant demeanor, his hair disheveled, his purple robe reduced to rags hanging on his body, with criss-crossing blood traces visible. Although the wounds were not deep, just shallow blood traces, it was enough to see how perilous Yin Hong's situation was.

If he was even slightly careless, he might fall right then and there.

Xu Yan raised an eyebrow, amazed at Yin Hong, "This guy is really strong. I can't seem to kill him!"

After this fierce battle, Xu Yan now knew his current strength level within the inner realm. He was stronger than a half-step Grand Master, but weaker than a Grand Master.

However, to kill a powerful half-step Grand Master like Yin Hong was not an easy task!

Boom!

Yin Hong suddenly unleashed a powerful strike, explosively retreating to put distance between them, ready to flee at any moment.

"Young man, since you insist on getting involved in this matter, from now on, there will be no place for you in this Great Yue Kingdom, or even in the martial arts world of the inner realm. Anyone who dares to disrupt the plans of the influential person behind me will die without exception, even if you are a Grand Master!" Yin Hong said with a gloomy face, bringing up the influential person behind him as a threat.

Xu Yan raised an eyebrow. The influential person behind Yin Hong must be of extraordinary identity, otherwise he would not have said such words.

"Hmph, a half-baked thing can only rely on his background to scare people. What a waste. The 'influential person' in your eyes, what is that to me? Does he have the qualifications to threaten me?" Xu Yan sneered contemptuously.

"Good, good, good!" Yin Hong laughed angrily. "Since you are so arrogant and do not take the influential person behind me seriously, dare you reveal your name?"

Xu Yan's expression was proud and disdainful as he looked at Yin Hong. "Why wouldn't I dare?"

Inwardly, he was pondering, "Brother Xie did say that in the inner realm, I can use his name to resolve any issues. The Sword Sovereign Cliff must be a top power in the inner realm. Can the influential person behind this guy be stronger than the Sword Sovereign Cliff? We'll see how useful Brother Xie's name is this time. I hope he wasn't just boasting."

Xu Yan was not afraid of the influential person behind Yin Hong. He just didn't want this to interfere with his initial goals of earning spirit stones and collecting spirit herbs, which were his top priorities upon entering the inner realm.

Once he had achieved his foundation and even broken through to the Profound Realm, he would not fear provoking these major powers.

Xie Lingfeng of Jianxun Cliff was renowned as one of the three youngest grand masters in the inner realm, and the number one among the younger generation in the Dao of the Sword.

Since Xie Lingfeng was involved, the Du family's matter would certainly be dropped.

Xu Yan was astonished. Xie Lingfeng's reputation was so effective?
