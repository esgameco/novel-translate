Since an agreement had been reached, at the crucial moment, they would need to be close to the Taicang realm to exert all the power of the witch-demon world to resist the enemy.

Xu Yan looked up towards the outside of the realm. Due to the restrictions of the laws of heaven and earth, except for realm lord level experts, no other martial artists from the Taicang realm could transcend the realm and enter the Unchanging Land.

So now, Xu Yan also could not leave the Taicang realm to enter the Unchanging Land.

"Witch-demons? Is Meiwu going to enter Taicang?" Xu Yan muttered to himself.

To be honest, Meiwu had helped him somewhat and given him some treasures.

"I wonder where Meiwu's jade bamboo is," Xu Yan thought to himself. If just a small branch of that jade bamboo was already so extraordinary, the complete jade bamboo must be even more remarkable, definitely a supreme treasure.

Moreover, that jade bamboo seemed to be related to one of the seven realm lords, the lord of the Qingling realm. It was also called Qingling jade bamboo!

Xu Yan continued to comprehend the essence of heaven and earth. Although his comprehension at the Creation realm seemed to have hit a bottleneck, he was about to break through to small success in the Heavenly realm.

Time passed, and Meiwu and the others began to enter the Taicang realm. For a time, the Dao domain became lively.

Tianshai's two realms were enough to accommodate the martial artists from the witch-demon world, as there weren't that many living beings in the witch-demon world to begin with.

In a broken realm, even the laws were decaying. If too many living beings were born, it would consume more of the realm's resources. Meiwu and the others had long been deliberately suppressing the number of living beings.

But because of this, the lower limit of strength for the witch-demons was relatively higher, as they had been cultivating for a longer time.

Tianshai's two realms were divided into a demon realm and a witch realm, which in a sense also separated the witches and demons of the witch-demon world.

"It's quite lively," Li Xuan silently observed the living beings from the witch-demon world transferring to the Taicang realm, without interfering.

Tianzi also smiled. The more powerful experts and living beings there were in the Taicang realm, the more beneficial it was for him as the Heavenly Dao.

"If we could devour the witch-demon world and incorporate it into Taicang, the Taicang realm would quickly improve by a level," Tianzi thought to himself.

He felt some regret that he currently had no way to incorporate the witch-demon world, even though he operated the entire realm and had become the complete Heavenly Dao of the Taicang realm. He would still need some assistance to incorporate the witch-demon world.

"That Blood Demon fellow is quite interesting," Tianzi turned his attention back to Blood Demon.

In the Taikun realm.

Jiang Feng stood quietly in front of a weathered stone platform.

"Master, Meiwu and the others have already entered Taicang," a somewhat tired voice came from within the stone platform.

"Has the situation already reached this point? Feng'er, if Taicang cannot be protected, take that hand and escape into the Unchanging Land. Follow the direction I gave you and flee as far as you can."

The tired voice sighed.

"Yes, Master!" Jiang Feng nodded, then after a moment of silence, added, "Master, Blood Ji's remnant soul may have disappeared."

"If it's gone, then it's gone," Taikun said indifferently.

"But Blood Ji's soul still contained a trace of the Primordial Purple Light. Perhaps it could help Master recover from your old injuries?" Jiang Feng said somewhat reluctantly.

"Difficult! A mere trace of the Primordial Purple Light's intent is not enough to help your master recover. Don't worry about it. When the great battle begins, your master will help you escape," Taikun said with a smile.

Sorrow appeared in Jiang Feng's eyes. After a long silence, he asked, "Master, what about Tianshai? Can he really obtain the authority of heaven and earth?"

The stone platform was silent for a long time before Taikun's voice sounded, "I don't know where his confidence comes from, but what Tianshai cannot achieve, Big Brother Taicang indeed left this opportunity to become the realm lord. But since it hasn't appeared until now, something unexpected must have happened.

"Moreover, that Tianzi fellow isn't dead yet. Getting Tianzi's approval isn't so easy.

"In fact, once Big Brother Taicang died, this realm had no hope. The fact that it has survived until now is thanks to Big Brother Taicang's greatness.

"Even if a second Taicang realm lord were to appear, they wouldn't be able to resist."

Jiang Feng fell silent.

The stone platform shook slightly, and a scroll flew out.

"After such a long period of sedimentation and organization, I've managed to draw this map. These are the Unchanging Lands I once traversed, including the location of my birthplace.

"After you escape into the Unchanging Land, use this to explore and return to my birthplace. Try to find Big Brother Taicang's birthplace using the method I told you. Perhaps there will be some opportunities.

"If you truly find an opportunity and become powerful, you can seek revenge if you have enough strength. Otherwise, don't think about revenge."

Taikun rambled on as if making final arrangements.

"Master!" Jiang Feng choked up.

He was of humble birth, orphaned at a young age, when he fortunately encountered his master who was wandering the realm. From then on, he embarked on the path of the strong, until he became a realm lord and took charge of the Taikun realm!

"Go now, prepare for the crisis. Taihe has probably made similar arrangements for Cangli. You can go with Cangli, at least you'll have a companion to support each other."

Taikun's voice sounded even more tired.

Jiang Feng nodded and was about to turn and leave when he suddenly thought of something and said, "Master, Tianshai has joined forces with Ao Lie, seemingly targeting the Carefree Realm Lord. The Carefree Realm Lord seems to be plotting something in the Divine Realm. Should we help the Carefree Realm Lord?"

"Carefree? Wasn't he lazy and uninvolved, indifferent to life and death? How is he plotting something now? Feng'er, you don't need to worry about it. Someone will naturally help Carefree, and besides, Tianshai and the others aren't capable of killing Carefree."

Taikun said with some confusion.

"Yes, Master!"

Jiang Feng nodded and turned to leave.

Inside the stone platform, the aged Taikun had deep wrinkles on his face, as if he had little life force left. A gray energy swirled around him, constantly eroding him.

As an ancient minor realm lord, one of the earliest to follow Taicang, he had once obtained the Primordial Purple Light, and his strength ranked at the forefront among the minor realm lords.

He was one of the main powerhouses who participated in that battle back then, and he even killed a minor realm lord level expert from the Unchanging Divine Temple.

In the final battle, he witnessed with his own eyes that last moment. He rushed into the battlefield, willing to fight to the death, but was affected by the aura of that one from the Unchanging Divine Temple, resulting in his current state.

If not for that palm strike that pushed him out of the battlefield at the last moment, he would have likely perished.

"Big Brother Taicang, even if the realm is destroyed, I won't let you be forgotten," Taikun murmured to himself.

In the Taihe realm, also a major realm in the Dao domain, the one in charge was not the ancient minor realm lord Taihe, but his personal disciple Cangli.

Cangli was once called the Eighth Prodigy of Taicang by some minor realm lords, rumored to have received guidance from Taicang himself.

"Master!" At the foot of a hill, Cangli bowed respectfully.

"Since the crisis has arrived and the Taicang realm has reached its end, it's impossible to resist. They haven't witnessed the final battle, so they can't understand," Taihe's voice came from within the hill.

In the Taimiao Realm, amidst a sea of colorful flowers, there seemed to be a towering figure standing.

In the Yuyao Realm.

"Cough, cough!" A few light coughs sounded as the Yuyao Realm Lord suddenly opened her eyes and looked in a certain direction.

"Diying, you've finally appeared!"

With a step, she instantly arrived outside the Yuyao Realm.

"The Divine Realm? Is this aimed at Xiaoyao?"

Yuyao gave a cold laugh and was about to head to the Divine Realm when her expression suddenly changed. She looked up.

Two masked men blocked her path.

"Lord Yuyao, you'd better rest quietly and recover," one said solemnly.

Although they were only weak realm lords, they were still realm lords.

Now that the Diying Realm had fully merged with the Divine Realm, and the Dao Realm was unifying with the Divine Realm, even realm lord level experts could descend to the Divine Realm.

"Why meddle in others' affairs? We should just watch the game played by the powerful," another realm lord said with a smile.

Xiaoyao indulging his disciple to kill a realm lord's son had indeed broken the rules between them.

"It's said Meiwu has a grudge against Xiaoyao!"

As Meiwu emerged, Duanwu, Liwu, Yanmo, Jumo, Bamo, and Chimo also appeared in succession.

Boom!

Suddenly, everyone was startled and looked towards the Taimiao Realm.

A figure walked out of the Taimiao Realm, dreamlike and unreal, yet giving off an air of unrivaled dominance.
