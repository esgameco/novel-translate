Hearing the discussions of the senior and junior sisters, the male Taimiao Sect disciples' faces turned green with envy.

Especially that "Junior Brother Yu", he was completely disheartened! The sudden commotion had shaken the entire Taimiao Sect, with powerful experts attacking Taimiao Sect - were they from the Myriad Worlds Alliance? Yaren's figure flickered as she appeared on the battlefield, her expression changing drastically.

Xu Yan?

Looking for people?! Yaren was a bit helpless, Shu Yuying and Yun Miaomiao were involved in Master Xin Mengrou's secret matters, so these elders didn't know about them when they entered Taimiao Sect.

"Stop!"

Yaren's figure flickered as she came to Xu Yan's side. "I'll take you to the people you want to see."

Xu Yan raised an eyebrow, the sword formation disappearing. He recognized this Taimiao Sect woman, she had been observing the battle at the gate of the Thousand Martial Hall back then.

"Junior Sister Yaren, you..."

The Dharma Protecting Elder was alarmed by Xu Yan's strength, and couldn't help frowning at Yaren.

"Xu Yan's acquaintances are with the Master."

The Dharma Protecting Elder frowned, why didn't she know about this?

"But Xu Yan..."

However, thinking of Xu Yan's arrogance, not only did he break into Taimiao Sect, disregarding the rule of not allowing outsider men to enter the mountain gate, he had even blasted away an elder - this was a direct provocation!

If they just let it go, where would Taimiao Sect's dignity be?

"I said at the mountain gate that I came to visit an acquaintance, but your gatekeeper was yelling that I, Xu Yan, was here to block the Taimiao Sect mountain gate, which is not my fault!"

Xu Yan interrupted her words.

Although he had entered the Taimiao Sect mountain, it was ultimately Taimiao Sect's responsibility.

Of course, Xu Yan thought about it, even if the Taimiao Sect gatekeeper had asked him who he was visiting, they probably wouldn't have been able to find Shu Yuying and Yun Miaomiao, and he would have still barged in.

In the end, it was still inevitable that they would come to blows.

The Dharma Protecting Elder paused, having the guarding warriors summoned, but Xu Yan disdained confronting them, looking at Yaren and saying, "Where is it? Lead the way, or have them come see me."

He came here for two reasons - one was to see the acquaintances, the other was to inquire about Xie Lingfeng's whereabouts.

"You..."

Yaren was about to let Xu Yan wait while she reported to her master, but suddenly paused, changing her words: "Just follow me."

She turned and led Xu Yan towards the peak of Taimiao Mountain.

"Junior Sister Yaren, you..."

The Dharma Protecting Elder's expression changed, the mountain peak was the core area of Taimiao Sect, even ordinary elders couldn't go up there, let alone an outsider.

However, her expression suddenly froze, and she didn't say anything further.

Xu Yan raised an eyebrow, it seemed someone had secretly transmitted a message to her, was it Xin Mengrou? Along the rugged and narrow mountain path, heading towards the peak, he saw some elegant courtyards on the way, all residences of Taimiao Sect's powerful experts.

The closer to the peak, the more flowers bloomed by the path, becoming even more beautiful, the purple and red hues faintly visible in the misty clouds, having a hazy, dreamlike beauty.

"Go up by yourself, at the foot of the peak, there are two elegant courtyards, that's where the people you're looking for reside."

Yaren stopped and said.

"Okay!"

Xu Yan nodded, not at all timid, nor fearing any potential plots, his strength was his foundation. Even if his own strength was insufficient to deal with any hidden dangers, he still had the divine talisman of his master's divine abilities.

The higher up Taimiao Peak, the more ethereal it felt, the more unreal.

"Hm?"

Xu Yan suddenly stopped in his tracks, looking towards a certain spot on the mountain peak, his brow slightly furrowed. That place seemed a bit unusual.

"The secret place of Taimiao Sect?"

Without the curiosity to explore it, after all, this was Taimiao Sect's secret, he continued stepping up the mountain.

The two courtyards had come into view.

Elegant courtyards, blooming with gorgeous flowers, and standing in front of the courtyard gates were two beautiful women.

They were Shu Yuying and Yun Miaomiao.

After so many years, the two women had become even more beautiful and charming.

Especially Yun Miaomiao, compared to when she was in the Matchless Pavilion, she was even more captivating, as if constantly plucking at one's heartstrings.

"Young Master Xu!"

Shu Yuying revealed a happy smile, her jade-like face flushing red.

"Young Master Xu!"

Yun Miaomiao's voice reached him, directly entering his heart, stirring his heartstrings.

"Miss Shu, Miss Yun, it's been a long time."

Xu Yan revealed a smile, stepping forward, but suddenly halting his steps!

Something's not right! He took a deep breath, his gaze becoming focused, his spiritual sense spreading out, but the scenery he saw contained no problems.

"Young Master Xu, please come in."

Shu Yuying quickly came over, wanting to lead him into the courtyard.

But Yun Miaomiao also quickly approached, the two women glaring at each other in the middle, seemingly arguing through spiritual transmission.

The two women's expressions grew colder, their hostile postures becoming increasingly obvious.

"Young Master Xu first met me, so he should first reminisce with me!"

"Hmph, when Young Master Xu came to my Matchless Pavilion before, I wholeheartedly entertained him, so he should first reminisce with me."

They were about to fight to decide the winner!

Xu Yan frowned, carefully examining the surroundings, he felt like he had fallen into some kind of illusion, or perhaps a dream.

But what his spiritual sense saw, what his eyes saw, were all real, the courtyards were also real, it was just that the two women seemed to be fake?

"Something's not right, what kind of technique is this?"

Xu Yan's heart tightened, this was no ordinary martial arts attack technique, but a kind of illusion, a dream-like state, similar to an illusion technique, yet not a pure illusion technique.
