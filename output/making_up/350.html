"The master is telling me to maintain a heart of exploration. The path of the Dao is long and arduous, only by keeping the spirit of seeking can one expand one's horizons and find one's own Dao."

Xu Yan suddenly understood.

Li Xuan nodded with a look of satisfaction, "I'm glad you comprehend the master's intention."

Hearing the master's words, Su Ling Xiu, though still curious, did not dare to ask further, feeling dejected.

"Please, senior, lend a hand and save the Spirit Realm!"

Upon seeing the flying ship of the Green Pine Pavilion, Wu Tiannan became greatly excited.

"Please, senior, save the Spirit Realm!"

The Sect Master of Thunder Cloud and others prostrated on the ground, pleading loudly.

At this point, this was their only hope.

Wu Tiannan and Xin Mengrou believed that the powerful expert of the Green Pine Pavilion could save the Spirit Realm, and they had long suspected that this senior might be the existence of the True Sovereign.

Even the Sect Master of the Thousand Martial Pavilion and the others had to prostrate on the ground.

Whatever grievances, they paled in comparison to their lives.

They had to seek help, no matter what.

"Let's go take a look at the Heavenly Abyss."

Li Xuan said indifferently.

The flying ship headed towards the Heavenly Abyss.

Wu Tiannan and the others were greatly excited, was the senior going to suppress the Demonic Sorceress?

The Sect Master of the Thousand Martial Pavilion and the others also had an expectant look, what kind of power did this mysterious senior possess? Could it be stronger than the True Sovereign? Could it be the True King that the True Sovereign mentioned?

As for what the True King was, they were not too clear. In their knowledge, the Refined Law True Sovereign was already at the pinnacle.

What realm was the True King? As they approached the Heavenly Abyss, the aftermath of the battle had already dissipated.

The floating bed was hovering above the Heavenly Abyss, and the warriors of the Serene Snow Palace were once again standing in front of the bed.

Within the bed, a figure had just stopped struggling.

A delicate white hand was grasping the neck of the True Sovereign, having devoured the True Sovereign's divine soul and refined it into her own cultivation.

The True Sovereign's body was gradually withering away.

Gulp!

Those who witnessed this scene felt their hearts tremble, the mighty True Sovereign had also had his divine soul drained.

The Demonic Sorceress looked towards the flying ship, her brows slightly furrowed. What was this divine artifact? She did not hurry to attack, but was instead refining the True Sovereign's divine soul to enhance her own strength.

From the depths of the Heavenly Abyss, streams of pink energy were constantly surging up, entering her body, making her appear more bewitching and powerful.

"Please, senior, slay the great evil of the Heavenly Abyss!"

Xin Mengrou respectfully said.

"No need to rush."

Li Xuan glanced over, relieved in his heart. The Demonic Sorceress's strength was not too formidable, he could easily destroy her with a single strike.

However, her power was derived from the Heavenly Abyss. Unless he could block the Heavenly Abyss, he would not be able to completely annihilate her.

To block the Heavenly Abyss would require some effort.

Li Xuan's gaze shifted, his brows slightly furrowed. The Demonic Sorceress seemed a bit unusual, as if she was not a flesh and blood being.

"Similar to a clone? But not quite, it seems to be a body born in a certain way."

Li Xuan pondered.

"Senior, what is this?"

Xin Mengrou was puzzled, why was he not hurrying?

"I will act when her strength reaches its peak."

Li Xuan said calmly.

"The senior is truly remarkable, this is confidence, this is the bearing of a powerful expert, not rushing into danger!"

Wu Tiannan sighed inwardly.

The group of powerful experts all felt relieved.

The senior directly gave the Demonic Sorceress the chance to refine the True Sovereign's divine soul, which showed his unwavering confidence and immense strength.

The Spirit Realm is safe now!

The Demonic Sorceress will no longer be able to wreak havoc in the Spirit Realm!

At this moment, everyone eagerly anticipated the senior's powerful strike to slay the Demonic Sorceress.

Xu Yan looked at the Heavenly Abyss, his expression one of astonishment.

This was exactly the same as the cave he had seen in the Black Abyss of the Emerald Sea, the only difference being that the cave in the Black Abyss was much smaller, while this Heavenly Abyss was gigantic.

So this was the Heavenly Abyss? He couldn't help but step out of the Green Pine Pavilion's flying ship and approach the Heavenly Abyss.

Looking at it now, the cave seemed like a massive hole between heaven and earth, as if a powerful force had pierced through the heavens and earth, leaving behind this enormous abyss.

This was the Heavenly Abyss! "The Heavenly Abyss, a hole in the heavens and earth?"

"Transcending Boundaries, not bound by the laws of heaven and earth, not constrained by the spatial dimensions of heaven and earth, breaking through the void of heaven and earth, traversing the void of heaven and earth.

"It has similarities with this Heavenly Abyss.

"The difference is that the power of Transcending Boundaries, while crossing the void of heaven and earth, does not leave behind a massive hole between heaven and earth.

"The existence of the Heavenly Abyss is due to the endless power that props open the laws and spatial dimensions of heaven and earth, allowing the Heavenly Abyss to persist.

"It's as if the heavens and earth have been pierced through with a hole?"

Xu Yan had a dawning realization, gaining a deeper understanding of the Dao of Transcending Boundaries. He felt that he was just a step away from comprehending the Dao of Transcending Boundaries.

Step by step, he approached the Heavenly Abyss, wanting to take a closer look and truly understand how this Heavenly Abyss was formed by piercing through the heavens and earth.

The Heavenly Abyss in the Black Abyss of the Emerald Sea was too small, limiting his insights. This massive Heavenly Abyss would allow him to gain a clearer understanding.

"Big Brother, what is he doing?"

Meng Chong asked in bewilderment.

The others were also puzzled. Was Xu Yan going to fight the Demonic Sorceress? He was indeed very strong, the peerless genius of the Spirit Realm, and his strength could even rival the True Sovereign.

But the True Sovereign had been killed, and after the Demonic Sorceress had devoured the True Sovereign's divine soul, her strength had grown even more. How could Xu Yan be her match?

However, Li Xuan's brows raised slightly, his heart secretly excited.

"Xu Yan is gaining insights into the Dao of Transcending Boundaries by looking at the Heavenly Abyss. This means he is about to break through the Transcending Boundaries realm very soon.

"In that case, I can pass on the cultivation methods beyond the Transcending Boundaries realm to him earlier.

"Perhaps it won't be long before he comprehends the Dao of Mystical Enlightenment as well!"

Li Xuan was filled with anticipation. Xu Yan was already not far from the realm of Mystical Enlightenment.
