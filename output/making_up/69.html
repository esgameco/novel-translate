Chapter 69: Meng Chong: The Great Sun Golden Bell is Extremely Powerful

The Innate Realm has already transcended the mundane. In the eyes of ordinary people, there is little difference between them and the divine.

A lifespan of thousands of years is actually within a reasonable range.

Absorbing and exhaling the spiritual energy of heaven and earth, the Innate True Qi is endless, so the longevity is naturally extraordinary.

And if the foundation is Jade Bone Innate, the lifespan will naturally be even longer.

Li Xuan was extremely excited. Why did he cultivate the martial dao?

The fundamental reason was to become powerful, to achieve immortality!

This is the ultimate meaning of the martial dao.

"I am now at the entry stage of the Innate Realm, the Innate True Qi seems endless, and I already have the cultivation methods of the Innate Realm. Next, I should create the cultivation methods above the Innate Realm.

"I need to determine the realm and connect it well with the Innate Realm, so that it can form a complete system."

Li Xuan was in a state of excitement.

His disciple is so powerful!

"Xu Yan has not yet broken through the Innate Realm, he does not know the profundities of the Innate Realm. Even if I create the cultivation methods above the Innate Realm for him now, he would not be able to comprehend them.

"It is impossible for him to comprehend the cultivation methods across realms."

Li Xuan fell into deep thought.

So the cultivation methods above the Innate Realm can only be comprehended by Xu Yan after he breaks through the Innate Realm.

Without breaking through the Innate Realm, even if one comprehends the cultivation methods of the Innate Realm, without personally experiencing the changes of the Innate Realm, it is impossible to use that as a foundation to comprehend the cultivation methods above the Innate Realm.

However, he can start working on preparing the next realm now.

"With Xu Yan's cultivation speed, it should take about a month for his Qi and Blood to be complete, and it will take some time to break through the Innate Realm. Within two months, Xu Yan should be able to break through the Innate Realm!"

Thinking about this, Li Xuan was filled with anticipation.

Once Xu Yan breaks through the Innate Realm, he will receive the feedback from the Godly Manual again.

Directly reaching the peak of the Innate Realm!

"It's time to work on the next realm, to create the cultivation methods!"

Li Xuan was greatly excited.

Meng Chong had refined to the Bronze Bone stage. He felt his own immense strength, his physical body like a golden bell covering him. He clenched his fist and felt that he could easily kill a top martial artist of the jianghu with a single punch.

"Shi Er, come here!"

Meng Chong called out.

"Young Master Meng, what are your orders?"

Shi Er asked with a smile on his face.

He had finally grasped the threshold of refining the skin through these days of arduous cultivation, and his heart was filled with excitement.

"Come, punch me!"

Meng Chong patted his chest.

"Huh?"

Shi Er was dumbfounded.

"Stop spacing out, come, punch me!"

Meng Chong urged.

"Is that really okay?"

Shi Er hesitated.

What if he ends up injuring the person?

"Do you think you can hurt me? Stop the nonsense, hurry up!"

Meng Chong looked at him disdainfully.

"Alright then!"

Shi Er took a deep breath, stepped forward, and fiercely punched Meng Chong's chest.

Boom!

At that moment, Shi Er felt as if he had punched a huge mountain, his fist hurt a bit, and there was a recoiling force that made his arm numb!

"Is that all? Use your full strength!"

Meng Chong felt that this punch was like a tickle to him.

"Okay, I'll try again!"

Shi Er's eyes narrowed, he let out a loud roar, and punched Meng Chong's chest with all his might.

This punch, he had used his full power.

He was a top martial artist of the jianghu, having mastered the Powerful Strength.

A full-power punch could split rocks and break stones.

However, when it landed on Meng Chong's chest, it was still like punching a huge mountain, giving Shi Er a feeling of an ant trying to shake a mountain.

The recoiling force made him retreat two steps, and his entire arm felt numb.

Gulp!

Meng Chong has become this strong already?

In such a short time, he can completely ignore the attacks of a top martial artist of the jianghu?

"Did you really use your full strength?"

Li Xuan was wandering around Yunshan County when a voice suddenly caught his attention.

"Thank you, senior. I will purchase the secret manual."

"Hmm, since we have met by fate, a hundred taels of silver will do."

"Here is the payment, senior."

The young master handed over the silver and received the secret manual.

"Ponder and cultivate well!"

This is truly outrageous!
