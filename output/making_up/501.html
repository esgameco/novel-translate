Jiang Buping asked curiously, "Does Blood Extreme have a grudge against you?"

Old Xiao was taken aback, "That's impossible. Blood Extreme is rumored to have perished a very long time ago. How old are you? How could he possibly have a grudge against you?"

"From Jiang Tianming to the one just now, his shadow was present in both cases. And the secret techniques he taught were all meant to restrain me. How could this be a coincidence?"

Jiang Buping explained the whole situation and his speculations.

"Oh, is that so?" Old Xiao raised his eyebrows, quite surprised. After pondering for a while, he asked, "Are you a descendant of the Jiang family of Taikun?"

Jiang Buping nodded.

"If that's the case, I have some guesses. Blood Extreme was killed by Taikun, and the Taikun disciple Jiang Feng was your ancestor. His targeting of you may be to take revenge on Jiang Feng?"

But even Old Xiao felt this reasoning was too far-fetched.

"I'm not the prodigy of the Jiang family, and I've fallen out with them. Shouldn't he be supporting me more? This doesn't make sense," Jiang Buping frowned.

"Perhaps he discovered your talent is too monstrous, and wants to nip the bud of Jiang Feng's monstrous descendant to take revenge on Jiang Feng. That's why he's targeting you?" Old Xiao continued to speculate.

"Is that so?" Jiang Buping frowned, feeling the possibility was low. No one else knew about his innate divine soul. Could a Blood Lord like him actually see through the special nature of his soul?

"We might as well ask him directly instead of guessing back and forth."

Su Lingxiu's delicate white finger tapped on the jar, and Blood Extreme, confined inside, was able to move.

Blood Extreme was decisive. As soon as he realized he could move, his first action was to self-destruct!

Puff! The jar turned blood red in an instant. In a flash, Blood Extreme's figure disappeared from the jar, leaving only a cloud of blood mist.

"You can't die in the Soul Capturing Jar!"

Su Lingxiu's delicate white finger tapped the jar again. Blood Extreme, who had turned into blood mist, gradually solidified and regained his form.

Blood Extreme now had a shocked expression. Everything he had experienced had completely exceeded his knowledge and imagination. Being confined as a remnant soul and unable to self-destruct, he could understand.

However, when the confinement on his remnant soul was lifted and he had already self-destructed, he still couldn't die and was reconstituted.

Even though he was once a Realm Lord level expert, he had never heard of such methods. He was so shocked that his soul trembled.

"Impossible, absolutely impossible!"

Blood Extreme let out a low roar, and with a bang, he exploded. A thick cloud of blood energy rolled in the jar, shaking the jar.

"I told you, you can't die in the Soul Capturing Jar!"

Su Lingxiu's delicate white finger tapped the Soul Capturing Jar again. The exploded blood energy in the jar condensed once more, and Blood Extreme's form reappeared.

"What is this? How are you able to prevent me from self-destructing?"

Blood Extreme was incredibly shocked.

Beside them, Old Xiao also had a shocked expression, staring at the Soul Capturing Jar without blinking. Just this small jar not only confined Blood Extreme's remnant soul inside, but even prevented Blood Extreme from self-destructing.

It's worth noting that Blood Extreme was a Realm Lord level expert. To be precise, Blood Extreme was the lord of a small world, one of the earliest strong supporters of the Netherworld.

He had once obtained a strand of purple light, which made Blood Extreme even more powerful than ordinary Realm Lords and difficult to kill.

His soul was also somewhat stronger.

But such a powerful expert was now confined in a transparent jar like an exhibit, unable even to self-destruct.

Su Lingxiu giggled, "I'm an alchemist cultivator! How to treat souls, how to keep souls from dying, these are all my specialties. This Soul Capturing Jar is an artifact I created by combining my skills as an alchemist cultivator with Fourth Junior Brother's artifact refining methods, various restrictions, my Heavenly Divine Furnace, and my divine abilities.

"It took me quite a while to refine this Soul Capturing Jar. It has a special property: besides confining souls, it can prevent souls from dispersing and consciousness from leaving the jar.

"As long as your soul is in the jar, your consciousness cannot leave it. No matter how you try to self-destruct or self-detonate, unless you can shatter the jar, your soul and consciousness will always remain diffused inside the jar.

"And I can easily reconstitute the diffused soul and consciousness. Do you understand? The deeper principles are beyond your comprehension. You just need to know that now, unless you can shatter the jar, no matter how you try to self-destruct, you can't die!"

Blood Extreme unconsciously swallowed. He looked at the transparent jar with disbelief. What kind of miraculous technique could create such an artifact?

Now, upon careful sensing, he realized the special and miraculous nature of this jar.

When it needed to confine, it could isolate his consciousness and cut off all perception of the outside world.

At the same time, it could lift the confinement, allowing him inside the jar to perceive everything outside, yet still unable to break out of the jar.

"I, Blood Extreme, admit defeat. Kill me or torture me as you wish. But confining me in a jar like this, humiliating me like this, is this the behavior of a true man?"

Blood Extreme glared angrily.

Su Lingxiu blinked her bright eyes and extended a delicate white finger to poke the jar. Blood Extreme immediately felt a slight force, like a small finger, poking his soul body.

"Are you blind? How am I a man?"

Blood Extreme's face darkened. Then he saw Old Xiao and said through gritted teeth, "You little Xiao brat, if you want to kill me then kill me. How dare you humiliate me like this? Let your disciple kill me. I, Blood Extreme, admit defeat!"

He instinctively assumed that Su Lingxiu was Old Xiao's disciple, and that this Soul Capturing Jar must have been created by Old Xiao.

As the foremost of the Seven Heroes of Taicang, with exceptional talent and most valued by Taicang, it would make sense for him to have such abilities.

"You think too highly of me. How could I possibly have the ability to teach such an outstanding disciple?" Old Xiao shook his head.

Blood Extreme was stunned. Xu Yan and the others were not Old Xiao's disciples?

"Then whose disciples are they? Let their master come. Kill me or torture me as you wish. We are beings of the same level, why humiliate me like this!"

Blood Extreme then angrily said.

Old Xiao looked at Blood Extreme with disdain and said, "Don't flatter yourself. How dare you claim to be on the same level as the senior? What are you? Not to mention you, even the Netherworld doesn't qualify to stand on the same level as the senior!"

Blood Extreme was stunned for a moment, then his pupils contracted as if he had realized something. He exclaimed in a lost voice, "Is it Senior Taicang? He didn't perish?"

In this world, those who even the Netherworld's World Lord couldn't stand on the same level with were, in a sense, only two people.

One was the expert from the Unmelting Divine Palace.

The other was Taicang!

And from Xiao's words, it obviously couldn't be the one from the Unmelting Divine Palace. Since it was...
