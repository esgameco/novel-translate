When fellow disciples reunite, it's natural to recount their experiences.

"How dare the Heaven Killers and Earth Shadows attempt to assassinate my junior brother! One day we will destroy them!" Xu Yan said with a cold smile.

"So that force is called the Heaven Killers and Earth Shadows. When my strength breaks through, it will be time for revenge against them!" Meng Chong said angrily, rubbing his bald head.

"Junior brother, how is your training of the Small Heavenly Eye going?" Xu Yan asked.

"Still lacking a bit," Meng Chong sighed.

The Small Heavenly Eye was too profound. Although he was just a tiny bit away from comprehending it, that tiny bit seemed like an insurmountable hurdle. Moreover, since arriving in the Nine Mountains Realm, he had been constantly evading assassination attempts by the Heaven Killers and Earth Shadows, with no free time to calm his mind and carefully contemplate.

"I have some Blood Heart Fruits here that can aid in comprehending techniques. Junior brother can take them to comprehend the Small Heavenly Eye. You should be able to break through," Xu Yan said, taking out three Blood Heart Fruits.

After explaining the miraculous effects of the Blood Heart Fruits, he added, "Junior brother, if you have any divine abilities you want to cultivate, you can use these to comprehend them as well."

"Many thanks, senior brother!" Meng Chong was overjoyed.

With the Blood Heart Fruits, he was confident he could comprehend the Small Heavenly Eye. As for divine abilities, Meng Chong also had some he needed to cultivate.

The divine ability Heaven's Will Stabilizes the Spirit was not within Meng Chong's cultivation scope, as he was a physical body martial artist, and this type of divine ability was not his forte.

Next, they set up a concealment formation. Meng Chong took the Blood Heart Fruits to comprehend the Small Heavenly Eye and cultivate divine abilities, as well as accumulate his foundation in preparation for breaking through.

As for Xu Yan, he also began to organize his martial dao, but did not take the Seven Wonders Divine Flower. After all, once he took it, he would need to go into seclusion for a period of time to gain insights.

Currently, Meng Chong needed to comprehend techniques and break through realms. Even with the concealment formation, there was still a chance of leaking their whereabouts, so Xu Yan needed to protect him.

Although it wasn't the right time to take the Seven Wonders Divine Flower, Xu Yan wasn't just idly standing guard. He was organizing his martial dao, especially the use of several divine abilities. He was also pondering something he had been considering for a long time - how to utilize the Immutable Qi.

As Meng Chong went into seclusion to cultivate and Xu Yan protected him, the Heaven Killers and Earth Shadows who had been pursuing Meng Chong suddenly found that his trail had disappeared.

Xu Yan's whereabouts had also vanished once again.

Meanwhile, the Wan family was conducting an internal investigation, rooting out infiltrated Earth Shadow members. Once these Earth Shadow members realized they had been exposed, they immediately self-destructed without any hesitation.

This made the Wan family's powerful experts feel a chill in their hearts.

How decisive!

They also realized the huge threat posed by the Heaven Killers and Earth Shadows.

They immediately issued a bounty throughout the entire Great Canopy Mountain region, offering rewards for information on the Heaven Killers and Earth Shadows, as well as for killing their members.

With the Wan family's actions, there was another wave of turmoil within the Heaven Killers and Earth Shadows.

The Five-Leaf Heaven Killer in charge of the Great Canopy Mountain branch had a grave expression. Although no Wan family experts had attacked that fake branch, meaning their initial guess was correct - the Wan family had deliberately shown weakness, using Wan Tianlin as bait to lure them out - the Wan family's actions made him realize there was a traitor within the Heaven Killers and Earth Shadows!

Otherwise, how would the Wan family know the name "Heaven Killers and Earth Shadows"?

In the cave, the Five-Leaf Heaven Killer swept his cold gaze over everyone present and said in a deep voice, "Why has a traitor appeared in the Great Canopy Mountain region? You are all from this area. From now on, all missions must be reassigned. We must root out the traitor!"

The Heaven Killers and Earth Shadows members now had solemn expressions. A traitor had appeared internally?

They were all willing to sacrifice themselves to restore the glory of heaven and earth, so why would someone betray them? Why would someone's faith waver?

"Sir, all Earth Shadows are cultivated from childhood. Every member is carefully selected and nurtured, with firm beliefs. They shouldn't betray us..." one person said in a deep voice.

The Five-Leaf Heaven Killer was silent for a moment. All members of the Heaven Killers and Earth Shadows organization were cultivated from childhood, including himself who was nurtured by an old Heaven Killer. Their faith was solid, and there had never been betrayals or doubts about the great mission of the Heaven Killers and Earth Shadows.

To restore the glory of heaven and earth!

But why had someone now abandoned their faith and betrayed them?

"Some people's faith has wavered. They think it's impossible to restore the glory of heaven and earth, so they betrayed us for their own selfish interests. That's because they are short-sighted and don't know the plans of the Heaven Killers and Earth Shadows.

"Now, the time has come. The mission to restore the glory of heaven and earth will be fully advanced, not just in the Nine Mountains Realm..."

As the Five-Leaf Heaven Killer spoke solemnly, a hint of madness appeared in his eyes.

"Have the people infiltrated in various forces prepare to assassinate important figures and instigate conflicts. Also prepare to enter the Heavenly Cave in the guise of Nine Mountains Realm forces to discuss some cooperation matters.

"Lastly, prepare to organize manpower to occupy the realm gates and attack the Qinghua Realm..."

The Five-Leaf Heaven Killer began issuing orders.

"For this operation, I will report to the Seven-Leaf Heaven Killer and obtain his permission!"

Hearing this, the Heaven Killers and Earth Shadows members became excited, their eyes showing a mad glint.

"Yes, sir!" they responded enthusiastically.

"Sir, should we continue pursuing Meng Chong and Xu Yan?"

"Kill them! How can we not kill them? We must kill them!" the Five-Leaf Heaven Killer said coldly.

At Heng Feng Mountain, Tian Seventeen sat at the head of the great hall.

A figure walked in, wearing a mask engraved with seven leaves.

"Sir, I have returned!" The newcomer bowed respectfully.

Tian Seventeen nodded. Although he wore a seven-leaf mask, his status far surpassed that of a Seven-Leaf Heaven Killer. He had come to Heng Feng Mountain in the Nine Mountains Realm only temporarily to take the place of this Seven-Leaf Heaven Killer.

He had another purpose - to come for the Wind Spirit Tiger.

"How did it go?" Tian Seventeen asked in a deep voice.

"In reply to sir, everything went smoothly. The Netherworld Prison and Demon Witches have reached an understanding. The chaos in the Divine Realm can begin."

As he spoke the last part, this Seven-Leaf Heaven Killer's eyes were filled with fanaticism.

"Very good!" Tian Seventeen nodded.

"Sir, the Five-Leaf branch wants to take some actions..."

He relayed the Five-Leaf Heaven Killer's plans.

Tian Seventeen nodded and said, "The time has come. Let them do as they wish. We're not afraid of revealing the name of the Heaven Killers and Earth Shadows anymore. It's about time for us to appear in the world."

"Yes, sir!" The Seven-Leaf Heaven Killer was excited.

"You continue to oversee things here."

"Yes, sir!"

After giving his instructions, Tian Seventeen took a step and instantly vanished.

"You've come!"
