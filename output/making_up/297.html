"It's an elixir that can enhance one's strength!"

"This elixir is a bit too much, here, Little Gold, you take this one pill, and I'll keep the rest."

Zhu Xingzheng took out one pill and gave it to the golden-horned deer, keeping the rest for himself.

"No, this is mine!"

The golden-horned deer became a bit irritable.

Slap! Zhu Xingzheng slapped it on the head, "Do you think I'm greedy for your elixir? I, Zhu Xingzheng, walk the righteous path, how could I do such a thing?

"I'm doing this for your father's sake. This elixir is for your father. Now that there's an opportunity, if your father takes the elixir, he might have a chance to become a super sixth-rank spiritual beast.

The golden-horned deer wanted to say something, but Zhu Xingzheng slapped it on the head again, angrily saying, "How can you have no filial piety? I'm considering your father, and you don't even think about being filial?

"Little Gold, how can you be such a deer?

"If Mistress Su and Mistress Yue know that you are such an unfilial deer, how will they see you?

"This... this... this won't work."

The golden-horned deer was dumbfounded, this time it lost 70%, it was really painful.

Honoring one's father is not supposed to be like this.

Zhu Xingzheng said with a look of disappointment, "Little Gold, that's your father, he's about to become a super sixth-rank spiritual beast, he needs more elixirs. Once your father reaches the super sixth-rank, it means you have a super sixth-rank father as your backing.

"70% is not too much. Besides, if Mistress Su and Mistress Yue know how filial you are, they might bestow more elixirs upon you. With this give and take, the amount of elixirs you obtain will actually increase."

The golden-horned deer blinked its eyes, its brain couldn't quite turn it over for the moment, but it felt that Zhu Xingzheng's words made a lot of sense.

"Alright then!"

It nodded and agreed.

"That's more like it! You need to be a filial deer. Look at how filial Mistress Su is to her master, if they know you are a filial deer, they will naturally have a good impression of you, and the elixirs bestowed upon you will increase."

Zhu Xingzheng patted the golden-horned deer's head, nodding in satisfaction.

Disciples were roaming outside, and Li Xuan had simply changed locations to continue his carefree and leisurely cultivation of the Heavenly and Earthly Principles, continuing to compile the Divine Martial Classics.

And every now and then, the Great Dao Golden Book would provide feedback, either that Xu Yan had slain a Refining Spirit Celestial, or that Meng Chong had slain a Refining Spirit Celestial.

The Shen Family was a cultivator family, and with this identity, they could obtain some intelligence from other cultivator families and sects.

So there was no need to expose their relationship, as it would not be good for either side if it was revealed.

After stabilizing the situation in Jade State, the Jade State Alliance officially occupied a place.

The scattered cultivators truly had a place.

Among the eighteen states of the Spiritual Dao Realm, except for the Myriad Worlds Alliance's headquarters in Chenzhou, the remaining seventeen states truly gave the scattered cultivators a place, and achieved equal treatment with the cultivator families and sects, only Jade State has done so.
