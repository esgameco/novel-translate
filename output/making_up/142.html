Xu Yan sat down cross-legged with a solemn expression, looking at these martial arts scholars - they were all people obsessed with the martial arts, perhaps with unique understandings of the martial way.

Or they might have cultivated some extraordinary martial arts techniques.

"Strange, there isn't a single one who cultivates the true martial arts?"

Xu Yan glanced at these martial arts scholars, secretly surprised, "Could it be that the Seven Star Academy also doesn't have the true martial arts? If that's the case, the inner domain martial artists are truly pitiful!" These were all fanatical martial arts enthusiasts, if he told them the truth that what they cultivated was false martial arts, they probably wouldn't be able to bear the blow.

"So, I'll just expound on some martial arts fundamentals, and won't mention the true and false, lest they can't handle it.

"Although they cultivate false martial arts, but through long-term research, they might have some unique insights."

Xu Yan thought this way, and his spirits were suddenly lifted, looking forward to this martial arts discussion, hoping to gain some harvest.

"For the martial arts discussion, should the young friend first expound on his own understanding of the martial arts, or should we start first?" asked one of the martial arts scholars.

"You all are older, so you can start first," Xu Yan replied.

"Then let me start the discussion. I've always believed that the martial arts are about empowering oneself, pursuing one's own transcendence. Starting from the Nine Grades of Body Refinement, strengthening the physique, enhancing one's strength..."

The first martial arts scholar began to expound on his own understanding of the martial arts, explaining each martial arts realm one by one.

Xu Yan listened silently. Although he had a general understanding of the inner domain martial arts, the specific differences between each realm were not detailed.

Listening to this martial arts scholar's exposition, his understanding of the inner domain martial arts became more detailed.

"Master didn't talk about the true and false martial arts for a reason. This inner domain martial arts is actually a complete martial arts system, just not perfect enough.

"Or you could say, they haven't cultivated to the fundamentals.

"Like the Nine Grades realm, it belongs to body refinement, just strengthening the physique, enhancing strength and toughening the bones and tendons, but without tempering the skin, bones, and viscera.

"It seems they only cultivate on the surface, without delving into the depths."

Xu Yan sighed as he listened.

The first martial arts scholar had expounded from the Nine Grades realm all the way to the Grandmaster realm.

This was the first time Xu Yan had a complete understanding of the cultivation of inner domain martial artists. He sighed inwardly, "Although the system is complete, the cultivation always gives a sense of being superficial, so calling it false martial arts is not wrong."

One by one, the other martial arts scholars expounded on the martial arts, and each martial arts cultivation had its differences, but they all did not transcend the framework of the inner domain martial arts.

"Young friend, it's your turn now!" the last martial arts scholar, after finishing his own understanding of the martial arts, smiled and looked at Xu Yan.

Xu Yan nodded. This martial arts discussion was not without harvest for him. These martial arts scholars were obviously more knowledgeable about the inner domain martial arts than the martial artists outside, and their research was deeper.

It gave Xu Yan some enlightenment.

"The foundation of the martial arts is the most important. My master once said, 'The tree that can fill the arms grows from the tiniest sprout; the tower of nine storeys rises from a heap of earth.' Therefore, the beginning of the martial arts lies in refining the skin, refining the bones, refining the viscera..."

Xu Yan spoke solemnly.

The group of martial arts scholars were dumbfounded, and Fu Yunian beside them also had a bewildered expression.

Refine the skin? Refine the bones? Refine the viscera? How to cultivate? "Young friend, young friend, please stop for a moment, what is this refining the skin, refining the bones, refining the viscera? Is this how the martial arts are cultivated from the beginning?" asked one of the martial arts scholars in bewilderment.

"Of course!" Xu Yan nodded solemnly and said, "The true martial arts are about tempering oneself from the inside out and from the outside in, an all-round refinement, to transcend the mundane, to achieve immortality!"

The group of martial arts scholars swallowed hard, this vision was a bit too high.

Throughout history, who has achieved immortality?

"Young friend, tell us more about this refining the skin, what is it about?" asked one of the martial arts scholars curiously.

This cultivation method they had never heard of had piqued their curiosity.

"Refining the skin, the first step is to sense the blood qi, and use the blood qi to temper the skin. Once the skin is refined, even ordinary knives and swords will find it difficult to harm..."

Xu Yan explained.

"Impossible!" exclaimed one of the martial arts scholars excitedly. "Even for a Third Grade martial artist, relying solely on their own physique, they would not be able to achieve the feat of being unharmed by knives and swords. According to what the young friend said, this is just the first step of martial arts cultivation, it's absolutely impossible."

The rest nodded in agreement.

Xu Yan looked at them with a pitiful gaze, they were frogs in a well, truly terrible. When he told them the sky was vast, they immediately jumped up and denied it, claiming the sky was just this small!

"What kind of gaze is that?" the martial arts scholar with dark circles under his eyes said unhappily.

"Being unharmed by ordinary knives and swords after refining the skin is not strange at all. As for what you said about Third Grade martial artists, their cultivation method is different, the toughness of the skin is only slightly stronger than ordinary people, naturally able to withstand the harm of knives and swords.

"Just because you have never seen or heard of it, does not mean it does not exist."

Xu Yan retracted his gaze of looking at a frog in a well.

"Impossible, I absolutely do not believe it, and that blood qi, how to activate and temper the skin, it's not credible!" the martial arts scholar shook his head.

"Don't interrupt, let the young friend continue to speak," another martial arts scholar said unhappily.

"Okay, you continue!" Xu Yan continued, "After refining the skin, it's time to refine the bones. Refining the bones can be divided into Iron Bones, Copper Bones, Gold Bones, and Jade Bones. Iron Bones are just ordinary, Copper Bones are already quite good, while Gold Bones, even if buried underground for thousands of years, will still remain indestructible. As for Jade Bones... I don't think there's a need to say more."

The group of martial arts scholars listened in a daze, hearing about Iron Bones, Copper Bones, Gold Bones, it all sounded so mysterious and incredible, it didn't seem credible at all.

They looked at Xu Yan with skepticism, this young man, could it be that he had read some fabricated novels and was now confused? Fu Yunian also had an expression of surprise.

"Continue!" the martial arts scholars said, suppressing their curiosity.

"After refining the bones, it's time to refine the viscera... When the tendons and bones thunder, the blood qi is like a fierce storm, then it reaches the Blood Qi Realm... Connecting the Heaven and Earth Bridge, inhaling and exhaling the spiritual energy of Heaven and Earth, refining it into the Primordial True Qi, that is the Primordial Realm!"

Xu Yan expounded the martial arts completely.

The group of martial arts students all stared with wide eyes, completely dumbfounded.

"Are you just making this up?" one of the martial arts scholars couldn't help but speak up.

This was just too mysterious, talking about tendons and bones thundering, blood qi like a fierce storm, connecting the Heaven and Earth Bridge, inhaling and exhaling the spiritual energy of Heaven and Earth.

Blood Qi Realm, Primordial Realm?

Where in the martial arts were there such realms!

"How could this be made up?" Xu Yan looked at them with the gaze of looking at frogs in a well, and stretched out his hand, "Look at my arm, knives and swords can't harm it."

"Impossible, what you said is not realistic at all," a martial arts scholar shook his head.

If the martial arts Xu Yan expounded really existed, then what they cultivated would be so weak in comparison.

Both in terms of the mystical nature and the terminology, it was several levels apart.

"Come, let's discuss and see if what I said is true or false."

Xu Yan smiled happily.

"Okay, let's discuss then."

"Of course, Brother Xie said that in the Inner Realm, I can use his name for anything!"

"You must have misunderstood, Brother Xie allowing you to use his name doesn't mean..."

How could his teacher brother let someone use his name like this?
