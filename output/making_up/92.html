Chapter 92 The Incomprehensible Ancient Leather Book, Xie Lingfeng Arrives

Li Xuan flipped through the ancient leather book, page by page. There were a total of eighteen pages, each with a different pattern drawn on it.

But every time he looked at a page, the first time it seemed to be some kind of terrain or other pattern, the second time it felt like random scribbles.

The third time, it seemed to have transformed into another pattern.

The more he looked, the more confused and incomprehensible it became. He was even unsure what exactly was being depicted in the drawings.

"This book is somewhat special, but I can't make sense of it," Li Xuan fell into deep thought.

He closed the ancient leather book and looked at the two characters "Tai Cang", puzzled: "Tai Cang, what does that mean?"

Li Xuan couldn't help but open the book again, and this time he felt the patterns on the pages were slightly different, even though the lines had not changed.

"Could it be some kind of martial arts technique? But it's too profound, beyond my current realm to understand the hidden meanings?"

Li Xuan was puzzled.

"If it is indeed a martial arts technique, would it be even more powerful than the ones I've created?"

He flipped through the book again.

"But it doesn't seem like a martial arts technique, it feels more like a map?"

He closed the book and put it away, playing with the jade ruyi in his hand, carefully pondering the content of the ancient book.

To his surprise, every time he recalled the patterns on the book, they seemed to be different, as if constantly changing.

"Strange, what is this exactly?" Li Xuan frowned and couldn't help but take out the book again.

As always, the first time he looked at a page was different from the second time, but he couldn't quite put his finger on why.

"This ancient leather book is rather peculiar. It's not quite a technique, not quite a map, not quite a talisman - it's just strange. Perhaps when my cultivation reaches a higher level, I'll be able to make sense of it."

Li Xuan fell into contemplation. Since even he couldn't understand it, his disciple Meng Chong definitely couldn't either, as he only saw some lines without any meaning.

"What about my senior disciple Xu Yan? Maybe he can discern something from it?"

Li Xuan made up his mind. Xu Yan was so talented, perhaps he could uncover something from this mysterious book.

The day after Meng Chong returned, Xu Yan also came back from the Eastern River Commandery. Under the guidance of his sword intent, Xu Junhe had begun refining his dantian.

The strength of the Xu family was all on the rise, and the progress was not slow.

Li Xuan sat in a chair, several books piled on the table beside him. He was currently reading a book on medicinal alchemy.

"The Borderlands lack the concept of elixirs, only having some simple medicinal concoctions, and no theory of monarch, minister, assistant and guide..."

The more Li Xuan read, the more he felt that the new martial arts system he had envisioned was feasible.

The ancient leather book lay on the table.

Xu Yan came to pay his respects: "Master!"

Li Xuan nodded, and Xu Yan's gaze was drawn to the ancient book.

"Master, what book is this?"

As a lover of novels, Xu Yan had read too many of them, and perhaps no one was more familiar with books than him. This ancient leather book looked like a rare treasure.

He immediately became interested.

"Take a look."

Li Xuan said casually.

Xu Yan excitedly picked up the ancient book.

"Tai Cang?"

Seeing the two big characters on the cover, he curiously asked, "Master, what does 'Tai Cang' mean?"

"When your cultivation reaches the appropriate level, you'll naturally know."

Li Xuan evaded.

Xu Yan opened the book and looked at the criss-crossed lines, which seemed to have a certain pattern, outlining an unknown shape.

It was somewhat like a map, or perhaps a terrain?

But he couldn't make sense of it. He flipped through the second page, the third page...

After going through the entire ancient leather book, Xu Yan was still puzzled, and asked, "Master, what are these lines? Is it some kind of treasure map?"

Towards the end, Xu Yan's eyes lit up.

Li Xuan saw Xu Yan's reaction and knew that he also couldn't discern anything from the book, not even realizing the special nature of the patterns on the pages.

He only saw simple lines.

"If even my disciple can't understand it, is it because my realm is too low? Forget it, I'll just keep it for now."

Li Xuan thought to himself.

He waved his hand to retrieve the ancient book, saying, "When your cultivation and realm reach a higher level, perhaps you'll understand."

"Don't think too much about it. Focus on your cultivation first. In a few days, I'll pass on a cultivation technique beyond the Foundation Establishment realm to you."

Li Xuan knew his disciple had a vivid imagination, so he added this to prevent him from speculating too much and neglecting his cultivation.

"Yes, Master!"

Xu Yan didn't think too much about it. After paying his respects, he went out to the city outskirts to cultivate and exchange insights on the Bagua with Meng Chong.

...

In the capital of Qi Kingdom.

Xie Lingfeng had been pondering the theory Xu Yan had mentioned, wanting to achieve sword heart clarity. First, he needed to have a tranquil mind, unperturbed by external matters.

"Seemingly having no woman in one's heart, is this actually a state of mind where one is not disturbed by external things? What kind of realm is this?"

Xie Lingfeng was quite perplexed.

He vaguely felt that he understood it to some extent, yet also didn't fully grasp it.

"My comprehension is too poor. Xu Gege has explained it so clearly, yet I still haven't been able to understand it."

He sighed inwardly.

Suddenly, an idea occurred to him.

"Xu Gege said he used the Qi Emperor's harem to temper his mind and improve himself... Perhaps I can do the same? By refining my mind and achieving clarity, I may have a chance to attain sword heart clarity.

"Only by attaining sword heart clarity can I truly step into the true path of the sword!"

Xie Lingfeng couldn't sit still anymore. He went to find the Qi Emperor, hoping to borrow the beauties of the imperial harem to refine his mind.

"Your Highness, do you have any commands for me?"

The Qi Emperor was overjoyed when he learned that Xie Lingfeng wanted to see him.

This is an opportunity! Perhaps he can obtain some martial arts techniques?

Xie Lingfeng looked at the Qi Emperor, finding it somewhat difficult to broach the subject, as borrowing the imperial harem to refine his mind was indeed a rather delicate matter. He was bashful and didn't know how to start.

Seeing Xie Lingfeng's hesitation, the Qi Emperor was even more delighted. He must have a request, and a good one at that. So he smiled and said, "Your Highness, just say whatever you need, and I will do my utmost to fulfill it!"

"Since that is the case, then I will just be direct."

Xie Lingfeng thought that since Xu Yan had also borrowed the imperial harem, the Qi Emperor should not find it too much of an issue.

So he said, "The thing is, I recently wish to refine my mind. I've heard that Xu Gege used the beauties of the imperial harem to temper his mind, with excellent results. So I've come with the same intention."

Fuck!

The Qi Emperor cursed inwardly: "What kind of so-called 'Your Highness' is this? Good things he doesn't learn, but he learns from that little bastard Xu Yan?

"That's my imperial harem, all my concubines, and he wants to borrow them to refine his mind?

"What does he take me for?

"This is too much, I can't stand it anymore!"

The inner realm cultivation techniques would soon be in his hands.

Xie Lingfeng and Hu Shan were both Sect Master-level cultivators from the Sword Sovereign Cliff, a major power. Their cultivation techniques were definitely extraordinary in the inner realm, at the pinnacle level.

Finally, he could have a glimpse of the inner realm martial arts, providing some reference for his own cultivation.

The Blood Demon Classic was ultimately a demonic cultivation technique, and it was incomplete. Although there were some useful aspects, it was still limited.

Xie Lingfeng and Hu Shan's hearts were now tense, finally about to meet this mysterious eminent person.
