In the Dao Realm, a great battle erupted. Tai Miao fought alone against Deng Wu, Chi Mo, and Ba Mo, yet still held his own.

Yan Mo moved, preparing to intercept Yu Yao. Unexpectedly, a white light flashed around Yu Yao's body. Without needing to pass through the Shadow Realm, she directly crossed the barrier between the Dao Realm and God Realm, descending into the God Realm.

Seeing this, Tian Sha understood why Shadow had failed before - at the critical moment, Yu Yao had escaped from the Dao Realm.

"A disciple of Tai Cang!" Tian Sha muttered to himself.

For Yu Yao to have such an ability, she must have received a treasure bestowed by Tai Cang that allowed her to flee the Dao Realm.

Tian Sha continued forward. Mei Wu was even more murderous, her expression devoid of charm and filled only with killing intent! The other realm lords all watched Tai He and Tai Kun realms, but there was no movement from those two realms.

Jiang Feng did not emerge, nor did Cang Lie.

The realm lords were all puzzled. Would they just watch as Tian Sha grew more powerful? Would they not intervene to assist the Xiao Yao Realm Lord?

"Let's go to the God Realm and take a look. The Xiao Yao Realm Lord is extremely powerful. Surely we can't allow internal strife to weaken us at this critical time?" one realm lord sighed and suggested.

"Let's go see," the others agreed.

Among these realm lords, many had secretly reached agreements with Tian Sha, but they did not know about each other and remained mutually wary.

The battle between Tai Miao and Deng Wu, Ba Mo, and Chi Mo continued, though all four had become more restrained.

In a certain treasure ground in the Dao Realm, Xu Yan exhaled, having finally broken through to the early stage of the Heaven and Earth realm.

"With my current strength, I no longer fear even Supreme experts."

Except for Realm Lord level experts, or being surrounded by multiple Supreme experts, he could now act without fear.

Suddenly, Xu Yan sensed some battle fluctuations.

"Such intense tremors - could it be a battle between Realm Lords?"

At this thought, Xu Yan became excited and silently left his secluded cultivation spot.

"It really is a battle between Realm Lords. What happened?"

Xu Yan hid in a certain place, secretly observing.

"They've all left!"

He saw Tian Sha depart, as well as the figures of Mei Wu and Yan Mo, all seemingly heading towards the God Realm.

"Are they going after Master?"

Xu Yan raised an eyebrow.

"Tian Sha has left, Mei Wu has left too. Has she already brought the Jade Bamboo to Tai Cang?"

Since Tian Sha had left and no Realm Lord remained to guard, he no longer needed to be cautious.

"The Shadow Realm is Tian Sha's territory. They targeted me before, which can be counted against Tian Sha. It's reasonable for me to go take some treasures from Tian Sha's realm, right? Consider it Tian Sha's blood money."

Xu Yan muttered to himself.

Tian Sha's realm must be full of treasures. Mere Supreme experts couldn't stop him, and Xu Yan was confident that the laws of Tian Sha's realm wouldn't detect his presence.

He immediately took out a communication talisman to contact Seventy-Three, inquiring about the situation in Tian Sha's realm and learning about the locations of important treasures.

In Tian Sha's realm, among the various realms of the Dao Realm, there was an imposing atmosphere with many powerful experts. Tian Sha himself struck fear throughout the Dao Realm, secretly subduing many weaker realm lords. In the entire Dao Realm, apart from a few major realms like Tai Kun and Tai He, the other realm lords did not dare to directly oppose Tian Sha's realm.

Although Tian Sha had descended to the God Realm and no Realm Lord remained to guard his realm, the Supreme experts in Tian Sha's realm were not worried at all.

With Tian Sha realm's strength, no Supreme expert would dare to provoke them.

As for other realm lords, they valued their status and naturally wouldn't take advantage of this opportunity to act against Tian Sha's realm. Moreover, Tian Sha's deterrent power was extremely strong. Weaker realm lords didn't dare offend him, while powerful realm lords wouldn't do something so beneath their dignity.

Furthermore, there were unwritten rules between realm lords.

So within Tian Sha's realm, the Supreme experts continued their secluded cultivation or handled affairs as usual, or waited for orders from Tian Sha.

No one knew that an uninvited guest had already entered Tian Sha's realm.

Even Seventy-Three, a Divine Venerate, did not know Xu Yan had already entered. He only thought Xu Yan wanted to further understand the situation in Tian Sha's realm to prepare for future actions.

He never imagined Xu Yan would enter Tian Sha's realm at this time.

"This is the place."

In a certain location in Tian Sha's realm, Xu Yan's figure appeared, his face showing excitement.

"Tian Sha is really wealthy!"

He sighed inwardly. As expected of a renowned Realm Lord of the Dao Realm, Tian Sha's realm was truly rich, with numerous divine medicines and all kinds of heavenly materials and earthly treasures in astonishing quantities.

In an instant, this area of Tian Sha's realm seemed unchanged, still appearing as it originally did, but all those heavenly materials and earthly treasures had already been plundered by Xu Yan.

Using his Heaven and Earth realm power, Xu Yan had replicated this place and displayed it here again, but it was false, not real.

Without careful investigation, it would be impossible to discover that the heavenly materials and earthly treasures here had disappeared.

Of course, it definitely couldn't fool a Realm Lord. If a Supreme realm expert was a bit more careful, they could also find flaws. But given the current situation in Tian Sha's realm, those Supreme experts never imagined someone would come to plunder heavenly materials and earthly treasures.

Therefore, even if they came here, they would only glance around without looking carefully, thus overlooking it and not discovering the flaws.

"It can be maintained for a while, but that's enough."

Xu Yan continued on to the next treasure ground. After all, even with the mysteriousness of the Heaven and Earth realm, replicating and displaying scenes of heaven and earth couldn't last long, and the power would slowly dissipate.

Moreover, Xu Yan had no intention of letting these replicated scenes of heaven and earth continue to be displayed for long.

A great thief had entered Tian Sha's realm, but the Supreme experts of Tian Sha's realm were completely unaware.

"Tian Sha is truly wealthy. This will be considered your blood money."

Xu Yan was extremely excited.

"This is the place. After plundering here, I'll go take a look around Mei Wu's territory to see if the Azure Spirit Jade Bamboo is there."

He arrived at a treasury in Tian Sha's realm. This was where Tian Sha's realm stored many supreme treasures, guarded by Supreme realm experts.

Xu Yan silently appeared behind the guarding Supreme expert.

"Whoosh!"

Suddenly, mountains, rivers, heaven and earth enveloped the area, sealing off the surroundings without any fluctuations. A sword pierced into the body of this Supreme expert!

"You!" The Supreme expert paled in shock, his face full of disbelief.

To be sneaked up on from behind without any awareness - even a Shadow Supreme couldn't accomplish this.

Only the Shadow Realm Lord could have such ability, but why would the Shadow Realm Lord use such methods to assassinate him, a Supreme realm expert?

Moreover, the Shadow Realm Lord wasn't an enemy!

"There's another one!"

Xu Yan looked towards a certain spot. The treasury wasn't guarded by just one Supreme expert - there was another Supreme expert hidden in secret. Moreover, this Supreme expert was a Shadow Supreme, skilled in stealth and concealment.

Ordinary Supreme realm martial artists probably couldn't detect his presence even if they came here.

"Whoosh!"

Xu Yan used the same technique, easily dealing with this Supreme expert. Then he easily broke through the treasury's defenses and entered.

The Primordial Turtle Shell appeared, directly sweeping all the items in the treasury inside. Xu Yan immediately retreated.

The theft in Tian Sha's realm couldn't be concealed for long.
