Of course, there are still differences.

After all, one is divine nature, the other is medicinal properties.

Time passed imperceptibly, the divine nature eroding the flesh and blood, while the internal qi in the physical body surged, contending with the divine nature, like two fierce beasts, fighting for control within Li Che.

This process of the divine nature and internal qi colliding and canceling each other out was also the process of tempering the physical body.

Hum—! The combined power of the two, flowing through the boiling blood, circulating along the Small Heavenly Circuit, washing the fragile meridians over and over again.

Sizzle, sizzle, sizzle...

Gradually, Li Che felt a kind of pain like being skinned and tendons being pulled, and sweat beaded on his forehead.

In the depths of his heart, a sense of inexplicable anger seemed to be born!

Anger erupted from every inch of his flesh and blood, like a volcano that had accumulated to the extreme, rushing towards his mind!

As if wanting to drill into his brain nerves!

The divine nature... wants to enter the brain!

Wants to drill into his mind! This won't do!

Li Che's heart skipped a beat, the brain... that is the most precise and mysterious part of the human body! Once damaged, it would be too late to regret!

According to the description of the divine foundation technique, this should be the final hurdle!

As long as he can withstand it, the divine nature will become docile and condense into the divine foundation wheel.

Li Che hurriedly mobilized the qi in his dantian, gathering it at the Baihui acupoint in his brain, blocking the divine nature's charge! Boom—! One collision, like the thundering of heaven and earth, the great bell directly collided with his ears in a surprising way!

Dizzy and dazzled, stars flashing before his eyes! As soon as Li Che had just adjusted, the Furious Amitabha divine nature gathered another force, like a howling angry dog, and attacked again.

This collision was even more deafening, like the spring thunder of the heavens bursting in his ears.

Li Che's whole body trembled slightly.

The divine nature like a river gathered again and launched the third attack!

Just like a waterfall rushing up three thousand feet, the great river hanging upside down!

Boom—! The countless internal qi and divine nature collisions had a feeling of the heavens and earth collapsing.

His head was buzzing incessantly.

It seemed to have passed in an instant, yet it also seemed extremely long.

Li Che slowly opened his eyes, his entire body flushed red, gradually calming down, the large veins on his back trembling like a dragon.

In the Nirvana inner landscape, the divine nature returned to calm, the original [Furious Amitabha] river-like divine nature disappeared without a trace, replaced by a circular platform.

Evenly thick, like a flattened cylinder, purely condensed from divine nature.

The divine nature emanating from it was far more powerful than the original [Furious Amitabha] divine nature.

A wisp of divine nature was equivalent to the previous ten or even hundreds of wisps!

In the inner landscape, [Howling Hundred Ghosts] and [Startled Heart Firm and Strong], the two divine natures, were both suppressed to the extreme.

[Three-Eyed Furious True Lord Divine Foundation]

Top-grade of the Eight Extremes!

Metamorphosis in position!

Li Che exhaled a breath, the airflow surging like a sharp blade, cutting through the air.

With a thought, the divine foundation wheel, like a millstone, appeared behind his head, no matter how he moved, it would remain behind his head.

The wrathful divine natures in the world, like mosquitoes attracted to a light source, flocked and continuously fused into the divine foundation wheel, strengthening it.

"The divine foundation wheel has been successfully forged, it can be considered to have stepped into the initial stage of the divine foundation, but we cannot be careless, according to the description of the divine foundation technique, the newly forged divine foundation has the possibility of disintegration, the higher the position of the divine foundation, the easier it is for the divine foundation master to be counterattacked, so it still needs time to stabilize the divine foundation wheel..."

"Ordinary Ten Extremes divine foundation wheels take about ten days to stabilize, the Nine Luminaries require thirty days, and the Eight Extremes divine foundation wheel requires a hundred days to stabilize the wheel!"

Li Che's lips curled slightly upward.

He opened his eyes, his gaze slightly glowing, like a mirror washed clean of all dust, reflecting the flowing light of heaven and earth.

He was surprised to find that his five senses had become even more sensitive, as if they could be manipulated, the perception could be extended, extended to the extreme, reaching a distance of ten meters! "Is this the 'divine sense' that the divine foundation cultivators speak of? Because of the impact of the divine nature on the Baihui acupoint in the brain?"

Li Che happily manipulated the divine sense, trying it out, like a child who had just obtained a new toy.

This was different from his use of the [Chess Sage] Dao fruit's heavenly chessboard perception.

The heavenly chessboard, that was a kind of overlooking perspective to observe everything.

It only required the consumption of divine nature to peek.

While the divine sense did not require the consumption of divine nature, but rather a refinement and transformation of the five senses and six consciousnesses, like an extension of the physical organs.

The divine foundation realm, compared to cultivating nature, is indeed a great transformation.

Suddenly, Li Che's ears caught an extremely faint sound of cracking.

The three spirit baby wooden sculptures that had absorbed the blood of the three sacrifices and five livestock, under the dried blood on the surface, began to crack, and then crumbled.

Three streams of divine nature gushed out from them, merging into Li Che's divine foundation wheel.

Li Che sensed them for a while, and was surprised.

They were actually three techniques.

One was a divine nature secret technique, one was a divine nature martial art...

And one was a method for advancing the divine foundation!

...

Fei Lei City, Inner City.

After breaking through the divine foundation, Li Che returned to his study room with the divine nature chess pieces.

Sitting in the chair, Li Che couldn't help but smile.

In three years, from just cultivating nature to forging the divine foundation... this speed, he reckoned that in Fei Lei City, or even in the entire Jin Guang Prefecture, there might not be many who could match him. Furthermore, he closed his eyes, his mind connecting to his own divine foundation wheel, and words seemed to float on it.

These were the techniques obtained from the three broken spirit wooden sculptures.

It was as if after breaking through the divine foundation using the sacrificial method, he had also obtained the corresponding techniques.

Divine nature secret technique [Furious True Lord Killing Gaze], divine nature martial art [Furious True Lord Three Blade Sword]! The three sculptures gave him three techniques! And there was one more, the method for forging the divine foundation together, which he had already known from the analysis of the Thousand Slivers.

The quality of the divine nature secret technique and divine nature martial art were both lower grade of the Eight Extremes, which really made Li Che extremely delighted.

Of course, the higher the position, the more difficult the cultivation.

But for the divine nature secret technique, Li Che was indeed a bit troubled.

However, for the divine nature martial art, with the [Dragon Elephant Golden Armor] Dao fruit, Li Che would only feel at ease.

Smiling, he withdrew his divine sense from the divine foundation wheel.

With a thought.

[Dao Fruit: Unsullied Heart (LV2, 0%)]

Looking at the information displayed in the flowing light.

Li Che was in a great mood, forging the [Three-Eyed Furious True Lord] divine foundation had directly caused a huge surge in the maturity of the Unsullied Heart Dao fruit, even directly breaking through the obstruction, reaching LV2 level.

"It seems... in addition to slowly accumulating the divine nature of the Unsullied Heart, forging the divine foundation is also a way to quickly improve the maturity of the Dao fruit. If I forge a few more divine foundations, I should be able to mature the Unsullied Heart Dao fruit to LV3, and obtain the embryonic form of the divine power, right?"

"I'm really curious what the divine power embryonic form of the 'Unsullied Heart' will be?"

Li Che's heart was a bit itchy.

And with the [Unsullied Heart] Dao fruit reaching LV2 level, Li Che's mind moved, and he could actually completely conceal the aura of the divine foundation.

The [Dragon Elephant Golden Armor] Dao fruit could conceal the blood and qi, the [Unsullied Heart] could conceal the divine nature, the [Chess Sage] Dao fruit could investigate the terrain and situation, the [Immortal Craftsman] Dao fruit could make hidden weapons...

Wow! As Li Che summarized it, he felt that he was going further and further down the path of the old fox.
