Regarding letting Xixia enter the inner city's Xu Family's Shenxiu Academy, Li Che did not have as much resistance as one might have imagined.

The child has grown up, it's not possible to keep her confined forever.

That would not be good for the child's growth.

The Shenxiu Academy... it's just like when the child in the previous life went to Miaomiao's class.

"Ache, so you've agreed to it?"

Xu You saw Li Che's agreement, and couldn't help but feel a bit delighted.

Old Chen, who was sitting on the rattan chair beside them, also nodded his head: "Nowadays, the environment outside the inner city has changed greatly from the past, it has indeed become much more perilous. The Lingying Sect is stirring, the spirit children in the outer city have already dwindled to almost nothing, they have even started targeting ordinary children..."

"The inner city will indeed be much safer than the outer city, and although Xixia is a bit young, her spiritual attainments are extremely high. It would be good for her to start cultivating her spiritual nature earlier."

Old Chen had always avoided entering the inner city, just wanting to live peacefully in the outer city. But now, he had spoken up to express his opinion.

Li Che then called over Zhang Ya and informed her of the idea.

"My lord, you and Master Xu can decide this matter... it's just that Xixia is so young, I'm still quite worried about her."

Zhang Ya had some concerns. After all, Xixia was only two and a half years old.

"Don't worry, although I can't enter the Shenxiu Academy, I can, I will go with her and keep an eye on her, ensuring Xixia's safety."

Xu You spoke up proactively. Since this was his suggestion, naturally he had to pay attention to Xixia's safety.

Xixia was going to the Shenxiu Academy now.

The whole family was busy with the preparations, even Li Che, who had a somewhat wistful and nervous feeling about it.

He made a small wooden book box for Xixia, using extremely light wood materials, so that it wouldn't feel heavy when she carried it on her back.

Zhang Ya was busy sewing new clothes for Xixia, planning to dress her up beautifully before she went to the Shenxiu Academy, so that she wouldn't lose face there.

Three days later.

A light drizzle, a chilly breeze blowing, early spring had just arrived, still carrying a bit of a cold edge.

Xu You personally summoned the Xu Family's carriage, and together with Li Che, they got into the carriage while carrying Xixia.

"Goodbye, mother! Wait until I kick the Shenxiu Academy's butt!"

Xixia, carrying the small book box that Li Che made for her, leaned out of the carriage window, waving goodbye to the tight-lipped, full of reluctance Zhang Ya.

This was Xixia's first time leaving her mother to go on a trip. Zhang Ya was indeed very reluctant to part with her.

As the carriage wheels crushed the puddles on the ground and gradually drove away,

Xixia looked at her mother, who was shrinking in her large eyes, her little mouth puckered, and she started to wail loudly.

Li Che hugged her and comforted her, taking out a sugar-coated hawthorn, and the little girl's longing for her mother dissipated in less than three seconds.

"Grandpa Chen said that when Xixia goes to the Shenxiu Academy, she has to use her little fists to protect herself, so that no one will bully Xixia!"

Xixia clenched her little fists, licking the sugar-coated hawthorn while still having unshed tears in her eyes, speaking seriously.

Li Che smiled and ruffled her hair.

"If anyone bullies you, remember to tell dad..."

"Dad is best at persuading with reason."

When they arrived at the gates of the Shenxiu Academy, Li Che was prohibited from entering. After entrusting Xixia to Xu You's care, Li Che went to the independent workshop that Xu You had arranged for him in the inner city.

This place was well-equipped, Li Che could work here during the day, and then go pick up Xixia from the Shenxiu Academy after school.

The independent workshop was not very large, after all, the inner city was precious land. The workshop Xu You arranged for him was a single-courtyard building, with some stone locks and wooden stakes, where he could practice martial arts in his free time.

In the following days, Li Che's life became somewhat regimented - waking up in the morning to send Xixia to the Shenxiu Academy, working and practicing martial arts in the inner city workshop during the day, and then going to the Shenxiu Academy gate in the evening to pick up Xixia and return home.

Of course, in between, Li Che would still occasionally don his cute cow-faced mask and straw hat, going out in the cold, drizzling rain to hunt for experience babies of the Lingying Sect.

Apart from that, Li Che also quietly started paying attention to and inquiring about the situation of Simo Bai.

"He's been holed up and not come out for over two months, staying in the deep courtyard of the Simo family... It seems his injuries are not light."

Inside the workshop, Li Che held a carving knife, carving on the wood he had gathered, the wood chips falling to the floor like drifting snow.

[Immortal Craftsman (Lv3, 10%), Dragon-Elephant Vajra (Lv2, 20%), Untainted Heart (Lv1, 7%)]

Now, he could control the size of each part of his body at will, more in tune with his heart.

Oppressive and confined.

He took a straw hat, put it on his head, stuffed the just-carved smiling cow mask into his chest, walked into the workshop, and wrapped the improved "Wood Raven" in a black cloth, placing it in a backpack.

After putting on an enhanced black elastic shirt, he looked up at the dark clouds in the sky.

"It's going to rain."

"Go early, come back early..."

"I still need to pick up Xixia from school."
