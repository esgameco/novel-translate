The hurried footsteps came to an abrupt halt outside the courtyard.

Rain mixed with light snow drifted down from the sky above. Li Che put away the page of the Tongluo Technique into the Qiankun Space, then turned to look towards the direction of the courtyard gate.

A light knocking sound echoed, and Li Che got up to open the door.

He saw a towering figure wearing a wide-brimmed hat outside the door, with a majestic aura that felt scorching hot, as if burning with inner fire.

Tousled black hair, knotted muscles, and sinews like an enraged giant python.

Standing there, it was as if he was silently raging, howling.

Yet the imposing figure knocked politely, which made Li Che's attitude a bit more amiable.

"Sir, who are you?"

"Master Li, I come from the Mansion of the City Lord, my name is Cao Qingyuan." The burly man's gaze flashed as he quickly glanced around the courtyard, not finding any other presence, causing his thick brows to furrow slightly.

"Cao Qingyuan..." Li Che was taken aback.

He then cupped his hands in salute: "This humble one is Li Che, greetings to the Young City Lord."

"Master Li, there's no need to be so courteous. Since you have become a renowned wood carving master, you are an honored guest of our Mansion. My father has specially sent me to invite you to lend your skills."

The mighty Cao Qingyuan smiled and returned the greeting, his manner quite humble.

However, Cao Qingyuan's aura of fury exerted great pressure on Li Che, making him feel like facing an enraged Buddha.

The Young City Lord of Feiluo, Cao Qingyuan! A Spiritual Foundation Cultivator!

It was no secret that Cao Guang, the City Lord of Feiluo, had a son who had reached the Spiritual Foundation realm. In the past, he was known as one of the Four Young Masters of Feiluo, but now that Cao Qingyuan had cultivated to the Spiritual Foundation, that title no longer befitted him.

"Young City Lord, I apologize, but I am currently at a critical juncture in my Bone Tempering, about to break through the Blood Transformation Realm, so I may not be able to take on your task."

Li Che politely declined Cao Qingyuan's request.

But Cao Qingyuan didn't mind, smiling and saying, "No problem, Master Li can focus on your Blood Transformation. As long as you can complete this wood carving within a month."

"The design for the wood carving is here, and I've already paid a down payment of a hundred gold leaves. The remaining four hundred will be delivered once you've finished the work."

A Spiritual Foundation Cultivator visiting in person, with a relatively amiable attitude and not demanding an unreasonable deadline.

Li Che understood that this was a subtle way to block his refusal.

If he rejected again, it would seem ungrateful.

After some thought, Li Che no longer refused, taking the design and the hundred gold leaves.

Seeing this, Cao Qingyuan's face broke into a smile. He pressed down his hat brim, took one last lingering look at the independent workshop, then turned and left.

Looking at the wood carving design in his hand, Li Che narrowed his eyes.

"Eighteen Maiden Lotus Worshipping the Wrathful Mahayana Image..."

Cao Qingyuan returned to the Mansion of the City Lord.

Removing his hat, he slowly stepped into the room.

Though it was daytime, the entire room was shrouded in darkness. Rows of white candles were lit, forming a staircase-like arrangement, and beside each candle was a small wooden sculpture carved from red wood.

Illuminated by the flickering flames, each wooden sculpture seemed to be bleeding.

The eerie candlelight flickered, shifting between yellow and green.

Cao Qingyuan's heart felt as if it was being gripped, and even as a Spiritual Foundation Cultivator, he dared not breathe freely.

"Has that Li Che accepted the commission?"

A faint, hoarse voice came from the depths of the mansion. Cao Qingyuan respectfully replied, "Yes, father, he has accepted."

"Good... The Xu family had already sold him to our Mansion. Since he has accepted the commission, it saves the trouble of having you capture him."

"As long as he completes the carving, that will be enough..."

"Father, what if he accepts the commission but doesn't carve it?"

The room fell silent for a moment, before the voice slowly responded, "He dares not..."

"Furthermore, for the Thousand Buddha Feast, we still lack three Spiritual Children. The An family has one, the Yang family lost theirs, but... the Xu Family Spiritual Cultivation Monastery has two more..."

"The Xu Family has two?"

"One is that Li Che's daughter, and the other is a powerful Spiritual Child... Hehe, a buy one get one free..."
