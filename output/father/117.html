The Cooperation of Great Wealth and Nobility?

Li Che raised his eyebrows and glanced at Li Qingshan.

He could smell something unusual about this "great wealth and nobility" cooperation - it might suit his tastes well, because the form of address had changed from "little Li Che" to "Ox Demon," indicating that Li Qingshan's talk of great wealth and nobility cooperation was to be done with the identity of Ox Demon, not Li Che.

Li Che could more or less guess that Li Qingshan wanted to cooperate with the identity of Ox Demon, and wanted to cooperate in which aspect.

He took a sip of hot tea, chewing on the emerald green tea leaves that had entered his mouth, and the bitter taste spread across his tongue.

Li Che smiled and said, "Senior, speak plainly, what is this great wealth, I'm very interested."

Wealth and nobility, who wouldn't be interested?

Now, Li Che was in great need of resources, and if he could get a few more Eight Extremes Divine Essence Crystals, Nine Yang Martial Elixirs, and supreme martial arts, that would be even better.

Li Qingshan also narrowed his eyes and looked at Li Che.

To be honest, the shock Li Che brought to him was no less than Xixia's bone tempering at the age of three.

After all, Li Che had only cultivated for three years, and the time he spent truly stepping into the martial dao and beginning to nurture his divine nature was not much different from the time Xixia took to complete her bone tempering.

A 22-year-old spirit child, from just entering the martial dao to the point of being able to kill a sect master in just three years. How terrifying?!

Calculating this talent as a genuine spirit child, it would be equivalent to a three-year-old spirit child killing a sect master!

Compared to this, Li Che's daughter Xixia no longer seemed so monstrous.

Of course, Li Qingshan also knew that he couldn't calculate it this way.

But there was no doubt that Li Che and his daughter Xixia... their talents were absolutely terrifying! Li Qingshan pondered carefully, but he couldn't figure out the reason, because it was unreasonable...

Xixia could be understood, after all, Li Che was monstrous, so his daughter being monstrous made sense.

But why was Li Che's talent so monstrous?

Bloodline inheritance? Late bloomer?

Before, Li Che was just a mud-treading nobody! Working as a porter in a wood carving shop, earning a miserable living of only dozens of large scenery copper coins a day.

How could he suddenly achieve such a reversal?

Could it be... that there really is a sudden enlightenment technique in the world, like the Peng bird soaring into the clouds? All one can say is that Li Che may have obtained the inheritance of some powerful expert.

Li Qingshan was not interested in these things, everyone has their own opportunities, he would not covet anything, he Li Qingshan also had his own opportunities.

Li Qingshan withdrew his strange gaze and took a deep breath. "Ox Demon..."

"I know all about your actions since you came to the prefecture city, I can only say... well done in killing!"

"The forces in the prefecture city are a mixed bag, each with their own schemes, they should be killed off, so they won't be full of themselves and always try to stir up trouble."

Li Qingshan took a sip of tea and sneered.

"You killed the Fishery Gang, those subordinate forces are all related to the Corpse God Sect... the Fishery Gang's composition needs no further explanation."

"Jing Wudao has placed his treasure on the Corpse God Sect, planning to be the vanguard for the Corpse God Sect's advance into the Golden Light Prefecture, and in the future he can gain great benefits, even obtain the 'Cursed Copper Corpse' refining technique of the Corpse God Sect, and build his own flesh body to the level of the 'Cursed Copper Corpse'."

Li Che's heart trembled slightly: "Cursed Copper Corpse?"

He had activated the Tower Ascension Technique and used the temple god's chess pieces to unleash power that could kill the Cursed Iron Corpses, but their toughness had already exceeded his imagination.

The Cursed Copper Corpse, how terrifying could it be? Li Qingshan narrowed his eyes: "The Cursed Copper Corpse is not ordinary, the low-grade Cursed Iron Corpses already possess the strength of a sect master, and if they are top-grade Cursed Iron Corpses, they can even rival a perfected sect master, and may even be harder to deal with than a perfected sect master, because their flesh bodies are unparalleled, indestructible and painless, like the most perfect death soldiers, and once they achieve the Cursed Copper Corpse, they can even be comparable to a great sect master!"

"Great sect master?!"

Li Che took a deep breath.

In the Qi and Blood Martial Dao, after opening the meridians, the next step is to become a True Qi Sect Master, refining the essence of the martial dao into oneself, the perfect fusion of true intent and qi and blood, forming an even more powerful killing force.

And the great sect masters, they refine spirit auras, which are even more terrifying, with the power to split mountains and rivers with a casual gesture! "Yes, spirit aura great sect masters... they are martial dao elites who have realized the path of their own martial dao and comprehended the true meaning of heaven and earth. Every great sect master, even if they do not cultivate divine nature, can easily escape the divine essence pressure of divine sages, and easily flee to save their lives."

"Any great sect master, even if they do not cultivate divine nature, can dominate a region and become a powerful expert, and have no fear of divine sages, and can even easily kill them."

"Normally, when comparing martial dao to divine nature cultivation, a pure spirit aura great sect master is roughly equivalent to a top-tier divine sage, and compared to divine sages, they are a bit weaker."

"After all, to become a divine sage... the flesh body must also refine spirit aura, reaching the great sect master realm."

"Without becoming a great sect master, one cannot become a divine sage."

Li Qingshan said softly, his eyes flickering with a hint of longing and pursuit.

"Unless one cultivates to the realm of heaven and human unity among the great sect masters, one can then, relying solely on pure martial dao, contend with divine sages."

Pure qi and blood martial dao, competing with divine sages! Such a realm, how grand and magnificent, it is hard not to be captivated by it.

"Of course, in the present era, pure martial dao great sect masters are extremely rare, most of them will also cultivate divine nature, after all, comprehending heaven and earth through divine nature is much easier and smoother."

Li Qingshan laughed, with a hint of wistfulness in his eyes.

"Those who have reached the great sect master realm relying solely on their own comprehension are few and far between."

He came back to the main topic, looking at Li Che, or rather, Ox Demon.

"You killed the Fishery Gang, most of them were those who had been imprinted with the Corpse Curse. Can you sense who has been imprinted with the Corpse Curse?"

Li Qingshan's gaze was intense as he looked at Li Che.

Li Che furrowed his brow, then smoothed it out and nodded.

"I can sense it... and therefore struck them down."

"The Corpse God Sect has set its sights on my daughter, all those who have been imprinted are not innocent, they shall be killed without mercy."

There was an icy killing intent in Li Che's words.

Li Qingshan clapped his hands and stroked his beard, laughing heartily.

"Excellent."

"Being able to sense the Corpse Curse imprint, it seems you are naturally sensitive to corpse qi. Normally, when a cultivator is imprinted with the Corpse Curse and has entered the Nirvana Palace, the divine nature can conceal it, and it is impossible to see it in ordinary circumstances..."

"You can see through it, this is an advantage, this is the foundation of our cooperation to achieve great wealth and nobility!"

Li Qingshan narrowed his eyes, a sharp intent flashing in his eyes. He didn't care how Li Che was able to sense it, he just needed Li Che to confirm that whenever Ox Demon set his sights on a Fishery Gang cultivator who had been imprinted, it was not a guess, but because he had the ability to identify the Corpse Curse imprint, and that was enough.

"Senior, how do you want to cooperate?"

Li Che narrowed his eyes.

"Not only the Fishery Gang, but the other two major gangs, the five great families, the five major sects... even the divine sect branches... may all have the possibility of having traitors who have allied with the Corpse God Sect!"

"This prefecture city is filled with filth, with corpse qi flowing everywhere, making one want to vomit."

"You point out the filth to me, and I will clean it up myself."

Li Che immediately understood.

This was to have him confirm who had been imprinted with the Corpse Curse, and then... strike them down?! "The idea is pretty good... but a bit difficult, isn't it?"

"Like the Fishery Gang, that Fishery Gang leader Jing Wudao has been imprinted with the Corpse Curse, he is a true divine sage cultivator, if you and I go to clean him up... we may end up being the ones cleaned up."

Li Che said it directly.

When he acted alone, he could instantly teleport and escape using the flying thunder chess pieces, even a divine sage cultivator might not be able to catch him.

But if they acted as partners...

If he ran, his partner might not be able to escape.

Although Li Qingshan was powerful, against a true divine sage, he might have a hard time.

"You little brat, do you think it's just the two of us acting? Do you think we don't have any companions... no, partners?"

"When we clean up, we'll naturally start from the illusory realm..."

Li Qingshan's eyes flashed with a rebellious look: "Moreover, give me another year, divine sages..."

"So what?"

She no longer listened to Old Chen's words, the old and young constantly bickered and quarreled, shouting "little stinker" and "old stinker" back and forth.

In the days to come, everything returned to normal.

Li Che resumed his regular life.

During the day, he went to the Astronomical Observatory to stand watch, and after getting off work, he went to the workshop courtyard to practice martial arts, cultivate, and carve wood.

The Fishery Gang had been wiped out, the once mighty gang, with the escape of their leader Jing Wudao, had completely fallen into desolation.

The Fishery Gang's forces were then devoured and divided up by the various major powers in the prefecture city, although the Fishery Gang still existed in name, it had actually been completely destroyed.

Perhaps it was the deterrence brought about by the Fishery Gang's downfall, or perhaps it was the lingering aura of the divine sage battle, as well as the suppression of Li Qingshan's reputation upon his return.

The entire prefecture city had become much more stable.

Listening outside Flower Lane, there was no longer any force keeping an eye on it.

For a moment, looking at the empty Heaven and Earth Chess Board, Li Che even felt a bit unaccustomed to it.

However, he still habitually arranged the Heaven and Earth Chess Board to ensure the situation around, so that if there was any abnormality, it would provide feedback.

Stability was the first priority for Li Che to establish himself and make a living in this world.

Inside the workshop courtyard.

The Heaven and Earth Chess Board was shrouded, isolating all external auras.
