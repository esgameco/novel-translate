"A Lingying statue?"

Li Che's eyes were ice-cold. So it was true...

Even after moving into Xu's woodcarving shop and under Xu's protection, they still could not completely avoid the Lingying Sect's coveting of Xixia! The Lingying Sect had emerged right after Xixia's first birthday celebration!

"They just won't leave us alone..."

Li Che clenched his fist, his eyes brimming with murderous intent.

"This Lingying statue has not appeared since we moved into the Xu family estate. But it showed up today... The only outsiders who had access were the workers and cooks summoned from Spring Wind Pavilion."

"But those workers and cooks were all arranged by Manager Sun to be sent out of the Xu family estate, not allowed to linger. So they can be ruled out. Li Che took a deep breath, feeling the winds and snow around him grow even colder.

His deep, dark pupils reflected a chilling glint.

"Which means... this Lingying Sect disciple... is most likely an insider at Xu's woodcarving shop!"

There was a member of the Lingying Sect within Xu's woodcarving shop! A chill ran down his spine, seeping into his body through his pores.

"This possibility... is very high, very high indeed!"

"I've been a bit too careless... Is there really any place that is absolutely safe?"

Li Che shook his head, glaring at the glaring Lingying wooden statue on the ground, his expression ice-cold: "Who among the woodcarving shop could be a Lingying Sect member?"

Faces flashed through Li Che's mind, but he still could not pinpoint the culprit.

Shaking his head, Li Che grabbed the wooden statue glaring up at him. His surging blood caused his fingers to suddenly glow with a golden light, and with a "crack", the wooden statue was crushed to pieces.

"I will find you..."

"Do not underestimate the determination of a protective father."

"A Lingying statue... appeared outside your courtyard?"

"No, I should say it is certain to happen. The Lingying Sect... wants to shake the rule of the major powerful families in Feilei City, so they must plant their people. And faith infiltration is indeed the most effective method."

Old Chen said solemnly, "Don't worry, I'll go investigate. This is not too difficult to find out. Becoming a Lingying Sect believer is not easy to hide. If I find out, I'll tell you right away. You should also be on the lookout, be more vigilant in your daily life."

"Third Master has been dispatched out of the city to eradicate the mysterious temple evils, so during this period... the shop doesn't have a main decision-maker, which is the best time for them to make their move."

"But even if we know who it is, I can't act directly before they make a move on little Xixia..."

Old Chen frowned as he spoke.

Li Che expressed his understanding.

While the Lingying Sect has spread in Feilei City, and people may detest becoming their believers, they would not go out of their way to actively eliminate them.

After all, before they cause trouble, it is just freedom of faith.

"But at least knowing who it is will give me some peace of mind, and I can be more vigilant."

Li Che said sincerely.

Old Chen, hearing this, also felt it made sense.

Old Chen's investigation took three days.

During these three days, Li Che increased his cultivation time, as the feedback from the "Dragon Elephant Golden Armor" Dao fruit had ignited his cultivation fervor.

Three days of arduous cultivation had brought the maturity of the "Dragon Elephant Golden Armor" to 5%. In a state of fully unleashing the Dao fruit's power, it was more formidable than the perfected Skin Refinement, with strength reaching a thousand jin.

However, it was still just perfected Skin Refinement.

In the training field, Li Che was drenched in sweat, his skin flushed with a reddish glow, the air hazy with heat waves.

He put down the stone lock, his eyelids lowering.

"Now I want to break through to Meridian Opening, but I need the Meridian Opening cultivation method... Yet I cannot go to Xu Beifeng, since the cultivation I've shown him is only perfected Skin Refinement."

In the past two days, Xu Beifeng had come to inspect his cultivation, and Li Che had only displayed his perfected Skin Refinement.

He had not fully revealed his perfected Skin Refinement.

Even so, Xu Beifeng was already greatly astonished - in just one year's time...
