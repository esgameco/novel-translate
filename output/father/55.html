Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Li Che's series of actions were fluid and extremely familiar.

The speed was at its peak, from the sudden attack to killing Si Mu-bai, it took around ten breaths.

But the confrontation within this short period of time was extremely dangerous. If Li Che could not withstand the spiritual impact of Si Mu-bai's divine nature, it might have been him lying on the ground at this moment.

The only regret Li Che had was that there was not much good stuff on Si Mu-bai's corpse.

The imagined divine cultivation method, the superior inner strength technique, and so on, all left no trace, not even a golden leaf! Unreasonable! This should not be the appearance of a young master from a prestigious family!

Boom!!! Li Che gathered his spiritual energy, his foot heavily stomping the ground, instantly causing a wall of water to explode like rain. His figure had already shot out like a cannon.

The "Sleeping Dragon Elephant" was also driven to its limit by him, his entire being seemingly erased of all presence from the mortal world.

"Stop!!!"

A piercing scream burst forth from the Golden Radiant Pavilion!

Three figures broke through the air, both surprised and furious, parting the rain curtain, rushing over rapidly. The rain curtain was instantly torn apart! Powerful divine will, sweeping across the sky, whipping up wind and rain! They were wearing masks of a tiger, a leopard, and a goat respectively...

Under the rain, they appeared extremely ferocious!

Surging divine will intertwined, as if forming a majestic river-like phenomenon around them!

Li Che lowered his hat, not uttering a single word in response.

Like a black shadow, he swiftly moved along the wall in the raging wind and rain.

Tap tap tap tap...

His footsteps were extremely light, each step barely causing a ripple in the rainwater.

Yet despite this, Li Che still felt the three figures were getting closer and closer! Not only that, from the direction of the outer city, a scorching and terrifying divine aura was also sweeping over and pressing down.

He was like a conspicuous beacon, unable to hide at all.

Even if he fully unleashed the Sleeping Dragon Elephant...

"I've been exposed the entire time... Even the concealment and bone-hiding of the Sleeping Dragon Elephant cannot help me escape..."

Li Che immediately thought of the jade pendant he had obtained from the corpse.

It must be that jade pendant...

That had exposed his whereabouts! And these people had actually abandoned Si Mu-bai's corpse and were directly coming after him...

This undoubtedly sent out a signal.

The jade pendant...

A good thing!...

...

At this moment, the rain seemed to have completely distorted. Boom! With a stomp of his foot, his figure abruptly drew a large circle in the rain curtain.

The accumulated water gushed out like a water wall in front of him, everything seemingly frozen.

"Hahaha... It's the Ox Demon... There's something good..."

A figure wearing a cheerful laughing child mask, carrying a long spear on their back, parted the rain curtain above their head like an umbrella.

Intense divine will permeated the surroundings, strangely bringing a joyful emotion, as if every raindrop contained happiness and delight, making one... unable to resist the urge to laugh heartily.

Li Che's brows furrowed, his eyes extremely ice-cold, but under the divine aura's infection, he couldn't help but want to open his mouth and laugh.

The rain, seemed to have frozen.

The torrential rain abruptly congealed in the air, controlled by the powerful divine will.

Aside from the figure in the child mask, there were two other figures, standing on either side, blocking his escape path.

The remaining two were armed with waist-hung blades, one with a burly bear-like physique, glaring with fury, their divine will seemingly igniting the rainwater!

Li Che had already recognized this person...

One of the Three Divine Hunters, the [Wrathful Amitabha] Zhao Xuan-hai!

The other two...

Li Che's eyes under the ox-mask glanced at the two.

These two should be the other two members of the Three Divine Hunters.

[Awe-Inspiring Radiance] Zhang Xiang-yang, [Chaotic Joy Bewitching Spirit] Zhou Ruo-guan!

Xu You had previously briefed Li Che on the divine bases mastered by the Three Divine Hunters, as their characteristics were too distinct, making them easily identifiable.

[Wrathful Amitabha], [Awe-Inspiring Radiance], [Chaotic Joy Bewitching Spirit]! The three divine base sequences were imparted by the imperial court.

They had now unleashed their divine powers, no longer caring about concealment.

"The Three Divine Hunters... All have the identity of the Spiritual Infant Sect, this Spiritual Infant Sect..." Li Che's expression under the mask was extremely gloomy.

This city... is rotten to the core!

The sky was windless and rainless, divided by the divine powers.

It was as if three raging rivers were obstructing and spanning before him.

"Ox Demon, Ox Demon..."

"I never expected that Si Mu-bai would actually die at your hands..."

"Young Master Fei Lei, haha, he died so miserably... Dismembered into countless pieces of flesh... Haha, well done!"

The figure wearing the cheerful laughing child mask stared at Li Che.

Just as Li Che was struggling and thinking.

His towering figure, his qi and blood raging like a dragon, his big tendons trembling and roaring...

The Ox Demon's arms trembled with big tendons, the surging qi and inner strength, were crazily infused into the strange mechanism he was shouldering, aiming at them...

You have divine nature...

I have firepower!

"Enjoy the fireworks!"
