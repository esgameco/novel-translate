In the inner city of Feilei City, the gloomy clouds had gathered for a long time, and the winter thunder suddenly boomed.

Once again, fine drizzle like cattle hair fell from the sky, mixed with flurries of snow. Drifting and drifting, it shrouded the entire world in a haze, but this haze was suddenly torn and shredded by a series of piercing, agonizing screams.

It was as if an invisible sword was wildly dancing and slicing through the heavens and the earth.

A figure with completely white hair, burly and robust, walked like a mountain of flesh down the long street. With each step, the entire blue stone street trembled and shook, and every quiver of flesh and blood seemed to be accompanied by countless twisted, shrill cries of shock and alarm.

The newcomer did not cover his face with a mask, his white hair scattered, his figure as towering as a mountain.

The flow of his qi and blood intertwined, seeming to transform into a roaring dragon.

His scarlet eyes appeared to be oozing bloody tears, filled with pain, sorrow, and even more so, murderous intent...

The Yang family... had almost been wiped out.

His obedient grandson, Yang Kaihe, who had the most hope of forging a divine foundation... had been killed.

All the experts of the Yang family had been killed off!

His family's century-old undertaking had ended in failure...

He was in so much pain! He had just sent Yang Kaihe to the Xu family to capture a spirit child, how could he have died?

With so many experts in the Yang family, how could they have been completely wiped out? How could his family's century-old undertaking have... dissipated into thin air? Only he, an old man, was left, the family was gone.

"Ox Demon... Ox Demon!!!"

The low roar burst forth from the throat of the Yang family patriarch. His body suddenly swelled up like an inflated balloon, growing larger and larger!

A divine foundation the size of a millstone appeared behind him, constantly emanating the divine nature of "Shock and Hardness". The divine nature almost condensed into substance, like the tentacles of an octopus, gradually falling onto the robust old man's body like a mountain of flesh, piercing into his flesh and blood, causing his consciousness to descend into madness.

Boom...

With each step, the blue stone street sank, forming sunken footprints and spider web-like cracks.

Step by step, he walked on.

Leaving behind dense footprints and cracks.

Suddenly.

The foot that was about to step down stopped abruptly, and the ripples of the splashed rainwater, as if congealed, spread and rippled towards the surroundings, freezing in the air.

The old patriarch of the Yang family, the Qi Condensation cultivator Yang Jing, suddenly raised his head.

His white hair flew, his scarlet eyes staring fixedly at the long street.

There...

A towering figure in black clothes, wearing a hat, stood quietly in the wind and rain on the long street.

The hat was raised, revealing a cute, wet ox-faced mask.

"Ox Demon..."

"Ox Demon—!!!"

The white-haired, robust old patriarch of the Yang family, Yang Jing, opened his mouth and let out a piercing, horrifying scream! Countless raindrops, countless water droplets bouncing on the ground, scattered into a hazy water mist under the high-decibel, terrifying scream!

"Mm... It's me."

The hat, mask, black clothes, the towering Ox Demon, the intertwining of qi and blood, faced the convulsing, roaring old patriarch of the Yang family.

He responded calmly, as if casually greeting a neighbor.

The convulsing scream abruptly stopped.

Yang Jing's scarlet eyes, controlled by the "Shock and Hardness", seemed to be slightly stuck and hesitant at this moment.

But very soon, the ubiquitous shrill screams burst forth, creating a brilliant air impact wave like a dragon, shattering the dense rain.

"So you are the Ox Demon!"

"The Ox Demon who killed my whole family!"

As if the air was twisted and blurred.

The divine foundation millstone-like Yang Jing rose behind him, his disheveled hair and violent ferocity like a mountain blocking the sky.

Blocking all the light, it suddenly appeared above the Ox Demon.

Boom—! The pile of fatty fists thundered and crashed against the Ox Demon's crossed arms! Ripping and tearing...

The sleeves of the elastic clothes burst open, revealing the steel-like muscles that were as dense as a dragon. But...

Yang Jing's crazed will, stimulated by the divine nature, slightly paused, his blood-red eyes widening slightly...

Because, the Ox Demon in front of him, who had not yet forged a divine foundation, had actually...

Withstood his punch without budging an inch!
