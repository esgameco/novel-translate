The warm sun of the winter day, rarely breaking through the dark clouds, sheds a fine and gentle light.

Ten miles outside Flashing Thunder City.

A bamboo forest.

The stomach churned, crushing and refining the Serpent Blood Warrior Pill he had swallowed, extracting the powerful medicinal power and refining it into true qi, circulating it through the small heavenly circulation meridians.

The five fingers clenched like hooks, and a sudden gust of wind arose, as if a white tiger was roaring behind Li Che!

The White Tiger Raging Wind Fist! This martial art technique, refined by Li Che to the Transformation Realm, has been further improved by him, becoming an excellent martial art with formidable power.

The LV3 [Dragon Elephant Golden Vajra] has greatly enhanced his martial arts talent! To cultivate the true essence of the Martial Dao, the martial art must reach the "Pinnacle Martial Art" level.

Although the White Tiger Raging Wind Fist and the Xu family's Eight-linked Bone-shattering Spear have been refined by him to the level of excellent martial arts, they still lack the hope of cultivating the true essence of the Martial Dao and reaching the Pinnacle level.

Furthermore, divine martial arts cannot cultivate the true essence of the Martial Dao, only pure blood qi martial arts can give rise to the true essence of the Martial Dao.

The blood qi gradually subsided, the crushed Warrior Pill slowly being digested.

Transformed into a surging energy, it strengthened Li Che's internal organs and gave rise to a dragon-like inner qi.

Boom! As Li Che practiced the martial arts, the movement of the qi and blood accelerated the release of the medicinal power of the Warrior Pill.

After completing the White Tiger Raging Wind Fist, Li Che clenched his five fingers, and the air silently tore apart, spitting out a strange long spear with blood-colored patterns engraved on it, the Nine Suns Pinnacle divine weapon, the Bloody Shocking Spear! With the divine weapon in hand, the swinging motion scattered fierce winds and surging qi! Amid the wailing, there seemed to be a piercing roar echoing through the mountains and forests! Even without divine power, the divine weapon's hardness, toughness, and sharpness far surpassed ordinary weapons.

As a blood python roared and burst out, the air rang out with eight consecutive shattering sounds, and Li Che put away the Bloody Shocking Spear.

"The last Serpent Blood Warrior Pill... should be enough. My Martial Dao cultivation has reached the peak of the Qi Vein Realm, my inner qi as mighty as a dragon. I only need the Mysterious Vein Technique to break through the next realm."

Li Che exhaled a turbid breath.

Without hesitation, he opened his mouth and swallowed the last Serpent Blood Warrior Pill.

At this point, all the Warrior Pills obtained from the Yang family's treasure trove had been consumed, using up a fortune of tens of thousands of taels of resources, successfully pushing his cultivation to the peak of the Qi Vein Realm.

As the medicinal power of the last Warrior Pill was completely refined and absorbed by his physical body, the entire courtyard was filled with scorching qi and surging heat waves.

Li Che opened his eyes, his gaze shining.

"The foundation of my physical body is now solid. Even without activating the Wrathful Golden Vajra, I can easily defeat ordinary Mysterious Vein martial artists..."

"It seems I can now attempt to forge the Three-eyed Wrathful True Lord Divine Foundation..."

To forge the divine foundation, it ultimately requires more than just a passionate heart.

According to the divine foundation technique imparted by the temple deity, many things need to be prepared. It is more akin to a ritual.

Li Che compared the Shocking Forceful Divine Foundation technique he obtained from the Yang family's ancestor, and aside from the different preparation materials, the ritual is the same.

The most important thing is the need to prepare three divine objects, such as wooden carvings, jade carvings, or scrolls. But it is recommended to use wooden carvings, preferably from spiritual wood.

In the Yang family ancestor's collection, there is a wooden block made of a spiritual wood called "Spiritual Flower Willow", which, although not as valuable as the "Blood Flesh Red", is much cheaper in price.

As for the design of the wooden carving, the divine foundation technique also provides a template.

Li Che himself is a master wood carver, so this is not a difficult task. As for the other three sacrificial animals and their blood, it only requires time to prepare.

Gathering his blood qi and only revealing his refined and perfected Tempering Bone cultivation, Li Che changed into a clean black outfit, graceful and gentle, grasped the Flashing Thunder chess piece, and returned to the set anchor point, the study in his own courtyard.

Today is a big event in Flashing Thunder City.

It is the day when City Lord Cao Guang holds a banquet at the Golden Brilliance Pavilion for the Divine Sect's Recruitment Envoy. All the major families and clans of Flashing Thunder City will bring their spiritual children to be evaluated by the Divine Sect's Recruitment Envoy, hoping they can be recruited into the sect.

In previous years, in addition to the Divine Sect's recruitment of spiritual children, the Great Scenery Dynasty's official authorities would also recruit them. However, with the expansion of the Mysterious Temple's calamity, the court has abandoned the recruitment of spiritual children in various places, leaving it to the major families and clans to cultivate them on their own. The Divine Sect, on the other hand, has not given up and often sends recruitment envoys.

Some powerful families may even hide their talented spiritual children, but most of the smaller families and common households are hoping their children can enter the Divine Sect and change their fate.

The status of the Divine Sect in the hearts of the Great Scenery commoners is truly extraordinary.

However, how many spiritual children Flashing Thunder City will have this year is unknown.

Arriving in the inner room, his wife Zhang Ya was braiding Xixia's hair.

After waiting for a while, Li Che, along with his wife Zhang Ya, who had finished dressing up, took Xixia and went out.

Li Qingshan and Lü Chi had already gone to the Golden Brilliance Pavilion, as they were representatives of the Divine Sect and naturally could not travel with Li Che's family.

Old Chen was not idling either, carrying his large wine gourd, following behind, intending to join the lively crowd as well.

After leaving the alley, the elder brother's family, who had also moved to the inner city, was already waiting in the cold wind.

The elder brother was still wearing his scholar's robe, with a cotton-padded jacket over it, puffing on a dry tobacco pipe.

Cousin Li Zhengran's family was waiting nearby. Li Che's nephew Li Chengzhou was already three years old, more tranquil than Xixia, with a fair and delicate appearance, already showing signs of handsomeness.

Seeing Li Che's family, Cousin Li Zhengran immediately showed a smile. Thanks to Li Che's help, his family was living extremely well in the inner city.

As soon as Xixia came out of the alley, she broke free from Li Che's hand and ran to Li Chengzhou's side.

"Brother Chengzhou!"

Xixia familiarly took Li Chengzhou's hand.

Li Chengzhou's fair face instantly turned bright red, and he was a little flustered for a moment.

"Brother, you look so good." Xixia stared at Li Chengzhou.

Li Chengzhou instinctively struggled a bit, retreating towards his mother-in-law Liu Chunmeng.

"Chengzhou, don't be afraid. Sister is just playing with you." Liu Chunmeng quickly comforted him.

Xixia didn't mind, smiling and approaching.

Li Che paused for a moment, unable to speak.

This girl... She's already a face-ist at such a young age? The way she treats Lü Daitou is not like this at all.

Li Che pursed his lips, suddenly startled.

Wait a minute, could it be that the blonde one is my daughter? Along the way, the originally frightened inner city residents, who had not dared to go out much due to the Yang family's extermination, were now gathering, all carrying children and heading towards the Golden Brilliance Pavilion.

Li Qingshan had already arranged for Lü Chi to wait outside, and when he saw Li Che's group, he led them into the Golden Brilliance Pavilion and seated them at a table covered in red cloth.

"Why are there so many people?" Li Che was slightly surprised.

"The number of children in Flashing Thunder City... seems to be more than I imagined."

Li Che had originally thought that after the Spiritual Infant Calamity incident, the number of children in Flashing Thunder City would have decreased greatly.

Seeing Xixia holding Li Chengzhou's hand, Lü Chi's expression was not very good. He seemed to have never received such treatment before.

Hearing Li Che's words, Lü Chi shook his head with his Nezha-style hairstyle and said, "Don't be fooled by the large number of children. There are actually very few spiritual children..."

"The Spiritual Infant Calamity only wants spiritual children. If they are not spiritual children, the Spiritual Infant Calamity won't even look at them."

Lü Chi's words were simple and direct.

The group sat down to eat the banquet, which was set up by City Lord Cao Guang using public funds, so Li Che ate quite happily. Although he had amassed a huge fortune from the Yang family, there is a different kind of joy in freeloading.

After the banquet was half over, City Lord Cao Guang gave a brief speech, and then Li Qingshan stood up, his gaze sweeping across the Golden Brilliance Pavilion and the bustling crowd outside.

He saw many curious children blinking their eyes, and some even brought their infants in swaddling clothes, hoping to try their luck.

However, Li Qingshan remained impassive and said calmly, "Next year, in the second month during the Spring Equinox, the Qianyuan Divine Sect will open its doors to recruit spiritual children. All children under the jurisdiction of the Jinhuang Prefecture who are three years old or above, and are tested to be spiritual children, can enter our Divine Sect."

Li Qingshan's voice, though not loud, resounded throughout the Golden Brilliance Pavilion and beyond, reaching the ears of almost everyone.

Instantly, the crowd began to stir.

"Of course, in addition to spiritual children, those with good martial arts aptitude can also obtain the opportunity to go to the prefecture city and participate in the recruitment examination."

Li Qingshan added.
