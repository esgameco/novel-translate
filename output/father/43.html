Thud! Thud! The muffled footsteps, like bells and drums, approaching from afar.

The thick snow on the ground, mixed with rainwater, stirred up rippling waves like a shaking quilt, spreading out from the distance and crashing onto the blue stone steps in front of Xu's wood carving shop, erupting into waves of snow like crashing tides.

Wave after wave, as if each step would stir up such ripples.

The wailing cries, circling endlessly, came from the other end of the long street to this end.

As if the gates of hell had been opened, the wailing ghosts...

Howling out, walking the human world at night! In the alleys.

The old man listened to the approaching footsteps, feeling the intense spirituality mixed in the footsteps, gently patting the child Lü Chi beside him.

"Go, foolish child."

"For a martial artist to get involved in the battle of a spiritual cultivator... is too much."

The old man said softly.

Hearing this, Lü Chi's eyes showed no fear, but rather burned like a raging fire.

"Master, leave it to me!"

"Consider it an early surprise for my unmarried junior sister!"

Lü Chi grinned crookedly, revealing a dazzling smile.

The next moment, he took out a thumb-sized wooden sculpture from his bosom. Though small, it was a vivid carving of a child holding a red-tasseled spear, with a bun hairstyle, wearing a red belly warmer, and surrounded by red silk.

Evidently the work of a great master.

Glaring eyes, angry gaze, domineering...

Lü Chi pressed the thumb-sized wooden sculpture against his brow, and suddenly there were sparkling particles of spirituality floating out from the sculpture, the spirituality contained within.

All of it was absorbed into Lü Chi's brow, as the wooden sculpture crumbled into dust and sand slipping through his chubby palms.

A red dot appeared on Lü Chi's brow, his eyes burning with fury!

"My unmarried junior sister is going to die miserably!"

"Ah! I'm so angry!"

"Prince, help me!"

Lü Chi shouted, his anger surging, his eyes burning, his aura rising rapidly.

The old man smiled, flicking his finger, and a red silk ribbon flew out, wrapping around Lü Chi's body.

Lü Chi's face was completely obscured by the spirituality of raging fury that emanated from him!

The swirling snow and wind enveloped him, the red silk ribbons coiling around him, as if he was stepping on a tornado, a terrifying sight. The old man stroked his beard and nodded slightly.

The spirituality of fury.

[The Furious Third Prince]!...

The pattering of winter rain mixed with snow pellets rained down from high above, falling on the oiled paper umbrella, bouncing like marbles.

Under the umbrella, a figure in white robes, whiter than the snow, with a handsome yet eerie beauty, eyes filled with melancholy, a tear mole at the corner of the eye, as if about to pour out all the sorrow in the world.

The strands of hair on his forehead gently swayed, revealing Sī Mù Bái's tear-stained face.

Holding the umbrella with one hand, he walked step by step, each step causing the snow on the ground to roll and surge forward, clearing a clean path for him to walk.

He was strolling leisurely towards Xu's wood carving shop.

Unconcerned about the situation inside the wood carving shop, no matter how tragic and urgent it might be.

Whether it was Zhao Chuán Xióng having his head severed by the Ox Demon, or the four blood-exchanging martial artists battling and chasing the Ox Demon, the heart-pounding pursuit...

None of it caused his rhythmic steps to show the slightest panic.

"Zhao Chuán Xióng is dead."

Sī Mù Bái pursed his lips, the vivid tear mole seeming to exude even more profound sorrow, his tear-filled voice tinged with grief.

"How unfortunate... Dead is dead, I'm so afraid, so afraid that Zhào Xuán Hǎi will come trouble me..."

"The divine constable killed me, I'm so afraid, so sad... Wuwuwu..."

Sī Mù Bái was shrouded in sorrow, cultivating his spiritual nature, using emotional states as the basis to activate his spiritual powers. Refining one's emotions is a must-learn lesson for every spiritual cultivator.

Beneath the tear-filled sorrow, his eyes were clear and lucid.

Sī Mù Bái looked at the Xu wood carving shop in the snowy night, the sorrow mixed with gloom, for the Ox Demon had appeared.

During the day, Zhao Chuán Xióng had brazenly taken Chén Dà Bǎo away, and now caused a commotion outside the Xu wood carving shop, clearly provoking the Ox Demon's attack.

But even Sī Mù Bái had underestimated the Ox Demon's rate of power increase.

The remnant of the [Weeping Demon-Subduing] spiritual power he had left on Zhao Chuán Xióng transmitted back the information, letting him know that the Ox Demon had already exchanged blood!

"So fast... This Ox Demon, who is he really?"

Sī Mù Bái was truly curious.

But no matter how he racked his brain, he could not find a match for the Ox Demon in his mind.

Whether martial artists or spiritual cultivators, none seemed to fit the Ox Demon.
