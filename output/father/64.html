Snow flakes were swirling in the sky above.

Li Che's spirit was like a chessboard that encompassed the area centered around the small courtyard. His pure heart and mind were slowly being consumed, but Li Che clearly captured the figure of Zhao Xuanhai stepping into the alley.

Zhao Xuanhai's appearance here made Li Che realize that the Xu family must have sold him out. They had sold him to the Lordship's manor. Previously, Zhao Xuanhai had represented the Lordship's side and wanted to recruit Li Che to carve wooden statues, but at that time he had been blocked by Xu Heli.

But now, Zhao Xuanhai had come to Li Che's independent workshop shortly after Li Che had revealed his identity as a master wood carver. Was it a coincidence? It didn't matter, Li Che didn't care.

In fact, Li Che had vaguely anticipated Zhao Xuanhai's arrival. After all, in this world, one must always be prepared for both good and bad outcomes. Coupled with the Xu family's unusual attitude today...

Li Che had some ideas and was on guard.

And now, Zhao Xuanhai had indeed arrived.

Tilting his head up, Li Che felt the icy coldness of the heavens and earth. It was the bitter cold of the twelfth lunar month.

Raising his head, the swirling snow flakes fell from the sky, landing on the chessboard woven by the spiritual nature of the "Chess Saint" Dao Fruit.

Like being scorched by glowing embers, they instantly evaporated!

"The Xu family... the affection is exhausted," Li Che murmured softly.

The "Chess Saint" Dao Fruit had activated, and under his observation, Zhao Xuanhai's every move was under Li Che's control.

"Zhao Xuanhai, a cultivator of spiritual nature as calm as a river, has not yet stepped into the divine foundation... shall we have a battle?" Li Che whispered.

Since Zhao Xuanhai had come today, he would stay. As an expert of the Infant Sect, Li Che would show him no mercy. This was such a good opportunity...

If he didn't keep him here, he would eventually become a great trouble to Xixing.

"Can I keep him here?" Li Che looked down, as if overlooking the chess pieces on the board from high above.

No, he must keep him here...

If all else fails, he would use the six thousand "Wood Raven" mini-guns he had saved up over the past half year to blast him to death!

As for the troubles that keeping Zhao Xuanhai would bring...

Perhaps some people might think that Niu Mo and Li Che had a deep connection...

But so what? As long as Zhao Xuanhai could not enter his courtyard, could not open the door to the courtyard.

Separated by a single door, separated by heaven and earth.

Whether Zhao Xuanhai lived or died, what did it have to do with Li Che?

The one who would kill him...

would be Niu Mo.

Li Che took out a cute and adorable ox-faced mask he had prepared earlier from his pocket and gently covered his face with it.

A familiar and comforting feeling welled up within him.

Raising his head, his jet-black hair fluttered sharply, and his gaze instantly contained towering killing intent.

"Zhao Xuanhai..."

"Your ox-faced uncle is here again."

...

...

Creak.

Stepping on the soft, snow-covered cobblestone ground of the alley, a faint creaking sound was heard.

"Li Che, born a commoner, trained in martial arts for three years, and has now reached the Tempering Perfection realm, quite impressive, a martial arts prodigy, and also a wood carving genius..."

"But mere Tempering Perfection is not enough to be feared. Becoming a master wood carver may have given him some spiritual cultivation, but according to the information provided by Xu Beihu, Li Che has not cultivated the Xu family's 'Suspended Thought Flowing Sword', so his spiritual cultivation is still lacking."

"That is also not enough to be feared."

Zhao Xuanhai's physique was burly, and the heavy saber at his waist weighed eight hundred jin, its killing power extremely strong when wielded by his flesh and blood.

Zhao Xuanhai not only had strong spiritual cultivation, but he was also formidable in the martial arts, possessing initial Qi Vein cultivation, with an extraordinary physique of dragon sinews and tiger bones.

To subdue and capture the Tempering Perfection realm Li Che would be a simple task for him.

Even if Li Che was a martial arts genius, he was still only at the Tempering Perfection realm, there was no way he could escape.

Suddenly.

A heavy step down, and instantly, the snow throughout the shabby alley exploded, rippling outwards and slamming heavily against the walls and tiles, shattering into countless snow powder.

Zhao Xuanhai, wearing an angry child's mask, had his coarse black hair seemingly piercing the air.

Under the mask, his gaze swept around wildly, his divine sense in his Nirvana Realm trembling frantically, like being targeted by a terrifying venomous snake! His every pore stood on end!

Creak...

His ear twitched, his pupils constricting like a snake's, as he heard an extremely faint sound, as if it was from far away...

Yet also very close.

That was...

The sound of a bowstring being drawn! It was as if an divine archer had their bow fully drawn, locking onto him from all directions, sealing off all his escape routes and paths forward...

He was completely exposed, trapped in the archer's sights! The slight friction between the arrows and the long bow was like thunderclaps exploding beside Zhao Xuanhai's ears.

"Who?!"

Zhao Xuanhai roared, his blood and qi suddenly erupting, the "Furious Amitabha" divine ability exploding forth at that instant!

However, in the next moment.

The falling snowflakes were pierced and shattered into powder.

A dark-colored luminous flow, like the roar of a fighter jet, accompanied by the rumbling of thunder, shot forth...
