Gold Light Manor City.

Fallen Flower Alley, a small workshop courtyard.

Li Che sat cross-legged in the courtyard, tilting his face upwards, allowing the snowflakes to fall on his cheeks.

The scorching blood coursed rapidly under his skin, the bursting heat instantly melting the snowflakes into water.

"My Xixia is four years old now."

Four years old...

Time flies so quickly, in the blink of an eye, the little girl who was once just a tiny infant is now a lively and energetic young girl of four.

Li Che lowered his eyelids slightly, the corners of his lips curling up into a smile.

Watching his daughter grow up safely and gradually mature.

As an old father, he felt a sense of pride and joy.

Xixia was born on a snowy day, so whenever the snow falls, it means her birthday is drawing near.

Today is Xixia's fourth birthday.

At the age of four, it also means the fifth Dao fruit should appear.

He was not in a hurry, gently closing his eyes, quietly waiting.

At the same time, he reviewed his cultivation progress so far.

In the inner realm of his Nirvana, the eight divine foundations floated, each emitting a brilliant glow, the powerful divine nature surging and roaring like a storm.

The seven emotional divine natures had all been forged into divine foundations, seven divine foundations...

Li Che shook his head slightly, sighing a little.

The "Dragon Elephant Golden Vajra" had enhanced his martial dao talent, making him a martial dao prodigy, unparalleled in horizontal tempering.

Now, the martial dao had indeed not let down this Dao fruit, stepping into the Furnace Sect Master realm.

And he had also mastered three supreme martial arts, one of which was the self-created Dragon Elephant True Essence, which could integrate the true essences of other supreme martial arts into it.

The third was the low-grade supreme art, Azure Thunder Body, obtained from the Shangguan Family.

At the Sect Master realm, in addition to tempering the body, one must also refine the five viscera and six bowels! Integrating the true essence into the five viscera and six bowels, making them unyielding, cultivating both externally and internally, without any flaws, one can then assault the Great Sect Master realm!

And true essence can only be born from supreme martial arts, so Sect Masters mostly cultivate one supreme martial art! Similarly, there are levels of entry, minor accomplishment, great accomplishment, master, and transformation, and if one supreme martial art is cultivated to the master level, true essence can be born, and if cultivated to the transformation level, the true essence can be transformed into a true form! The combination of martial dao true essence and divine image power is extremely terrifying! This is also the realm of the many divine image ancestors in the Gold Light Manor City.

They have immersed themselves in the martial dao for countless years, slowly grinding one supreme martial art to the transformation level.

But that's all there is to it, to break through to the Great Sect Master, one supreme martial art reaching the true form level is far from enough.

The martial dao true form is just a basic requirement to become a Great Sect Master.

"The martial dao path is long and arduous..." Li Che let out a sigh.

He is now just at the initial Sect Master realm, just beginning to refine the five viscera and six bowels, to advance to the Great Sect Master realm, it should still take several more years...

As for the cultivation of supreme martial arts, Li Che is not worried, his talent is not bad, with gradual cultivation, he will surely reach the true form level!

And the Dragon Elephant True Essence, fusing various true forms, who knows what kind of power it will transform into?

Li Che couldn't help but feel some anticipation and curiosity.

Regarding this supreme art that was born from the "Dragon Elephant Golden Vajra" Dao fruit and self-created by him, Li Che still harbored great expectations.

His mind stirred.

Flowing light appeared before his eyes.

...

[Name: Li Che]

[Bonds: Li Nuanxi (daughter)]

[Dao Fruits: Immortal Craftsman (lv3, 88%), Dragon Elephant Golden Vajra (lv3, 55%), Untainted Heart (lv3, 15%), Chess Saint (lv3, 10%)]

[Nascent Divine Abilities: Immortal Craftsman·Thousand Splitting Hands, Dragon Elephant Golden Vajra·Ascending the Tower, Untainted Heart·Restraining the Divine]

[Prompt: For every year the bonded object grows up safely, a new Dao fruit can be obtained]

...

The familiar interface appeared before his eyes.

The peaceful and stable cultivation, the overall improvement was not small, the maturity of the "Immortal Craftsman" Dao fruit had already reached 88% of lv3, very close to lv4.

During this period, he had set about manufacturing five "Dragon Fang Bodhi" bullets, engraving on them a spiral pattern that could accelerate the gathering of divine power.

Making the penetrating power and the explosive divine power of the bullets even stronger! But that's all, it's still very difficult to improve the maturity of Immortal Craftsman.

And the production of ordinary divine wooden sculptures can hardly improve the Immortal Craftsman standard, unless he enters the mysterious temple and captures the divine nature of the temple deity into the wooden sculpture, only then can he accelerate the improvement of Immortal Craftsman.

Li Che also plans to busy himself with the upcoming tasks for a while, and then start working on improving the "Immortal Craftsman" Dao fruit to lv4.

In fact, Li Che really enjoys this kind of stable and peaceful life.

As long as he cultivates, carves, and nourishes his divine nature, it's simply too comfortable.

Tranquil and beautiful.

Unfortunately, the tree wishes to be still, but the wind does not stop, this world has never been a peaceful and stable world.

Even the Gold Light Manor City is full of surging undercurrents.

Factions fighting each other openly, the five major sects also constantly plotting against each other, the aristocratic families also competing for interests, frictions never-ending.

The Temple Guardianship Bureau is completely focused on the three ominous temples outside the city, which are full of potential great terrors at any time.

The City Lord's Mansion and the Temple Guardianship Bureau, as well as the Celestial Observatory, are not on the same page, seemingly eyeing the Divine Sect greedily.

Within the Divine Sect, there are also the evil people of the Corpse Divine Sect.

The Corpse Divine Sect is also corroding the high-level experts of the major forces in the Gold Light Manor City, like a venomous snake opening its bloody maw, wanting to swallow the entire city.

The world... has never been stable.

Only when one's own strength is strong enough, strong enough to support a stable realm...

Can one truly achieve carefree enjoyment, watching the winds and clouds, and savoring life.

Li Che opened his eyes and exhaled a turbid breath.

After being so calm for so long, it was the calm before the storm.

As the Four-Armed Furious Third Prince's Mysterious Temple shook, the Mysterious Spire expanded, and when the powerful experts of the Qian Yuan Divine Sect arrived to open the Furious Third Prince's Mysterious Temple and conduct the temple deity resonance test...

The storm might no longer be able to be suppressed.

When he went through the sect assessment before, Li Qingshan said that the temple deity resonance test would probably be a year after the sect assessment, so after Xixia's birthday, it should be the temple deity resonance test.

"The temple deity resonance test... Li Qingshan said that this time it's going to be big, different from before. In the past, the temple deity resonance, the Qian Yuan Divine Sect's main sect would bring the top-notch spiritual children from the four prefectures and the state capital to test together."

"But this time, in addition to the Cloud State, the ten branch sects under the jurisdiction of the Lingnan Dao's capital city, even the capital city itself will dispatch powerful experts, bringing spiritual children..."

"For the inheritance of the Four-Armed Furious Third Prince!"

Li Che's gaze flickered.

The Eight-Armed Furious Third Prince's Mysterious Temple outside the Gold Light Manor is of the highest rank, even among the Four Sovereign Mysterious Temples, it belongs to the top-tier.

Therefore, the shaking of the Mysterious Spire and the emergence of the Third Prince's inheritance have attracted the attention of all forces.

And the temple deity of the Third Prince's temple is a child, so the matter of temple deity resonance will naturally be highly valued by all parties.

This temple deity resonance... has attracted the attention of the entire Lingnan Dao.

The Corpse Divine Sect had already laid its roots in the Gold Light Manor City, also because of the fame of the Four-Armed Furious Third Prince's Mysterious Temple.

Just as Li Che was pondering and summarizing, completing the review of his own cultivation.

Hum—

Li Che only felt that the heavens and earth suddenly became extremely quiet!

Thump thump thump! Violent pulsations were transmitted from within his chest, the Dao fruits of "Immortal Craftsman", "Dragon Elephant Golden Vajra", "Untainted Heart", and "Chess Saint", all four of them were violently palpitating!

As if welcoming a new companion!

The scene in front of him shifted, the familiar scene, like a mirror emerging from the water, slowly reflecting...

Roar—

The vast and profound earth, as if ancient deities were roaring in fury, in the boundless heaven and earth, a precious tree towered up to the clouds!

Countless leaves trembled, flashing countless streams of light! A seven-colored fruit, accompanied by seven-colored radiance, hung on the treetop.

The growth scenes of Xixia this year flashed by like a panorama, reflecting the vast heaven and earth! Li Che's black hair and bright eyes gazed into the distance.

Sparkling with crystalline brilliance, there was curiosity and expectation.

Xixia's birthday, the day he opens his Dao fruit blind box...

Of course, he was very much looking forward to it! A year of guardianship, finally reaped the harvest today.

Xixia is four years old, the fifth Dao fruit!

It has arrived!

...

[Your daughter is four years old, with some twists and turns, tenacious and unyielding, safe and sound, and has obtained the Dao fruit "Immortal in the Painting"]

...

The prompt converged into flickering golden light, dazzlingly bright.

The fifth Dao fruit! Li Che knew that if his daughter was safe and sound, his future... Dao fruits would only become more and more.

And the attributes of the Dao fruits, what they specifically would be, he could not determine, nor could he fathom.

Just like opening a blind box, very exciting.

Just like this time, seeing the name of the fifth Dao fruit, Li Che was as bewildered as when he first saw the "Chess Saint" Dao fruit.

Immortal in the Painting? Is this going to make him a talented scholar wielding the wolf-hair brush?

His brows furrowed slightly.

Seems to have figured out some of the different implications of the Dao fruits.

"Profession?"

"Are these Dao fruits going to let me ascend to divinity through different professions?"

Li Che smiled amusedly.

Of course, it's not necessarily the case, because the "Untainted Heart" Dao fruit does not belong to the profession category, so it should not be generalized, there is still probability.

The interface has given him opportunities, binding him to his daughter's bonds, and for every year his daughter lives safely, he can obtain a Dao fruit.

This Dao fruit is just nascent, without any maturity, he needs to slowly accumulate the maturity.

The origin of this opportunity is very profound.

Because his cultivation has grown, his contact with the world has also deepened.

He has seen the temple deities, seen the overlapping mythologies, so when his mind trembles, he can't help but wonder...

The Dao fruits he has obtained, bound to his daughter, is it because his daughter's identity is special?

My Xixia...

Could she be the reincarnation of some great being?

Li Che closed his eyes, his mind violently trembling, this thought once born, he could not suppress it at all, as if a cold feeling had instantly enveloped his entire body.

He dared not think about it deeply.

He slowly opened his eyes.

"Even if she is the reincarnation of a great being... so what?"

"The Dao fruits are in my possession, and I, Li Che... am Xixia's father!"

"Even if there are some plots and schemes behind it, I only wish for my daughter to be safe and sound."

Li Che's gaze gradually calmed down, the coldness in his body also dissipated, and in his chest, it was as if a furnace was burning, radiating scorching heat.

"As long as I become strong enough..."

"I will fear no evil spirits!"

Become stronger!

Only then can he protect his daughter, protect his wife, protect the family that belongs to him! [Immortal in the Painting (Dao Fruit): Blood as ink, divine nature condensed into spirit, immortals in the painting, painting all things to life]

His mind trembled slightly.

The introduction of the new Dao Fruit "Immortal in the Painting" appeared before his eyes.

"Immortals in the painting, painting all things to life..."

Li Che narrowed his eyes, he was still a bit bewildered about this introduction.

But he can be sure that this Dao Fruit will involve the use of blood and divine nature.

"Just try it and you'll know."

[Dao Fruit (Immortal in the Painting): lv1, 0%]

Li Che glanced at it.
