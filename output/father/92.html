Heroes?

Mu Popo was slightly stunned: "In this world... heroes never live long."

The soft cushions in the carriage may have been bumpy, but it did not provide any comfort. Li Qingshan held Mu Popo's hand, even though her skin was no longer as smooth and delicate as a young girl's.

Yet Li Qingshan could not bear to let go.

Gently playing with her long fingers.

"It's true that heroes never live long."

"But that guy is different... if we don't consider his ruthless killing methods, he can be considered a hero."

Li Qingshan smiled.

Mu Popo immediately understood who Li Qingshan was referring to.

She couldn't help but recall the time when the Xu Family Spiritual Cultivation Monastery was attacked. When she was in endless despair and planned to perish together with theYang family members...

A burly figure stepped forward, shattering theYang family's plan to seize the spiritual children, and saving those innocent children.

Niu Mo, a person with ruthless methods and a fierce appearance, an... hero.

At least, in Mu Popo's eyes, the Niu Mo who saved the children was a hero!

"Niu Mo? Does he... dare to get involved in this 'Thousand Buddha Feast'?"

Mu Popo frowned and couldn't help but ask.

Cao Guang had been preparing for the Thousand Buddha Feast for a long time, several years in fact, whether it was developing the Spiritual Infant Sect, plundering and capturing spiritual children, or spending a lot of money to invite wood carvers from various clans to create spiritual infant wood carvings...

Years of planning, all for the sake of this Thousand Buddha Feast.

Mu Popo was not stupid, she was well aware that Cao Guang was definitely doing this for the sake of advancing his own strength.

Cao Guang was likely hoping to borrow the power to break through the divine realm from the temple gods during this Thousand Buddha Feast.

Sacrificial rituals were quite normal in this world.

When Mu Popo was young, she had traveled all over the country and seen too many sacrificial rituals.

The fishing villages by the river would use young girls or female children, along with three sacrificial animals and five livestock, to worship the river god.

The mountain villages would also worship the mountain lord and mountain gods.

The desert castles and oases also worshipped the desert gods.

And there were even more who worshipped the mysterious temples, even though the divine nature of these temples would influence various places and give birth to evil spirits that would wreak havoc.

But the temples still housed gods, dead gods, who were still gods.

Many powerful cultivators, through sacrificial rituals and worshipping the gods, had obtained power beyond their own and achieved breakthroughs in their cultivation, they didn't care whether the gods were strange or evil.

Mu Popo knew that Cao Guang was also performing such sacrificial rituals.

But what could she do?

She couldn't stop him at all, she was just an old person with weakened divine nature and depleted vital energy, a mortal who had never established a divine foundation.

Li Qingshan seemed to know about the situation Mu Popo had encountered, and smiled: "Why wouldn't he dare? You think he'll die? That kid's talent for staying alive... is quite strong."

Li Qingshan thought of Li Che's concealment techniques, and the endless Tang clan's hidden weapons that could kill divine foundation cultivators...

Even though the Thousand Buddha Feast was something Cao Guang had been preparing for years, and even though Cao Guang had prepared many backup plans.

But...

Niu Mo might not die.

Niu Mo's methods, just a glance shows he's no good person, a calamity that lasts for a thousand years, how could he die easily? "Besides, don't I have the old man?"

Mu Popo looked at the arrogant and self-assured Li Qingshan of the past, and couldn't help but laugh out loud.

"You know Niu Mo? Know his true identity?"

Mu Popo asked curiously.

Who is Niu Mo?

She was a bit curious, Li Qingshan seemed to know Niu Mo's true identity.

Suddenly, the spirited horses neighed, the hoofbeats on the muddy ground became more and more urgent, mixed with the sound of wind and rain beating on the carriage, continuous and uninterrupted! The leisurely Li Qingshan who was holding Mu Popo's hand did not answer her question.

He suddenly straightened up, his eyes shining brightly.

"Here they come."

Li Qingshan lifted the curtain and looked out at the gloomy sky, smiling lightly.

Mu Popo was stunned.

The next moment.

She saw Li Qingshan lift the carriage curtain and walk out, lightly stepping on the tip of his foot, and in the horrified gaze of the carriage driver, he floated and landed on top of the galloping horse.

Even though the spirited horse was bobbing its head up and down as it ran, its wet mane flying wildly.

It did not affect the old man standing on its head at all.

The howling wind and rain, like the opening of a grand play, tore open in front of the old man standing on the horse's head.

Woo woo woo—

The heavens and earth seemed to emit a heavy wail! Like a terrifying beast roaring and growling! Countless raindrops burst open, forming a white hazy wave.

On top of the waves, was a black ram's horn hammer!

The center of the ram's horn hammer protruded a sharp spike, like a short spear, wanting to pierce the sky! In the distance, on the rain-shrouded earth, a figure threw out this hammer, then far away cupped his hands in salute, then merged into the rain curtain like water.

The old man raised his hand.

The ram's horn hammer shot towards him, and was firmly grasped in his hand with a thunderous sound.

The overwhelming divine nature surged out from the ram's horn hammer, like a furious beast charging against the heavens and earth! In an instant, the rain in the sky and earth seemed to have paused.

"The Ram's Horn Heaven-Shattering Hammer... only brings me a portion of divine nature, but it's enough."

"To deal with some rotten fruits... it's enough."

Li Qingshan gripped the ram's horn hammer, his blue robe fluttering, his hunched shoulders slowly straightening up.

As if filled with spirit, he stretched out a lazy yawn towards the heavens and earth.

Sitting in the carriage, Mu Popo's eyes were stunned.

In this moment, that hunched figure, seemed to have returned in her eyes to the past, when he rode a white horse and roamed the rivers and lakes.

Seeing that vigorous and heroic young man in blue robes.

That year.

The young man had spirit, and the beauty was like jade.

Flying Thunder City.

In the city lord's mansion, under the eight-cornered eaves.

Wind and rain, the heavens and earth were hazy, in the distance the cold mountains were shrouded in smoke, like ink splashes on a landscape painting.

Cao Guang had changed into a clean brocade robe, adorned with gold and silver, standing with his hands behind his back on the city tower.

His gaze was deep, as if concealing sharp weapons.

A blurry figure appeared, its aura heavy and sunken, with a sword hanging at the waist, emanating a powerful sword qi, it was Cao Guang's son, Cao Qingyuan.

"Have you found your second uncle's body?"

Cao Guang looked out at the rain curtain in the sky and earth, speaking faintly.

Cao Qingyuan's face was icy cold, with a trace of grief flashing in his eyes, he shook his head: "No, the night rain washed away too many traces, your second uncle's body could not be found, only a few scattered and exploded pieces of flesh..."

"Scattered flesh..."

Cao Guang's lips trembled, his face twitching.

Confirmed...

How ruthless! Li Qingshan! The Ram's Horn Heaven-Shattering Hammer... Li Qingshan!

Cao Hetu must have encountered Li Qingshan's deadly hand, instantly shattered and exploded into scattered flesh.

According to the rumors that had returned to the mansion, this Ram's Horn Heaven-Shattering Hammer Li Qingshan was extremely ruthless, his attacks were violent, the opponent either exploded or was smashed to pieces...

Cao Hetu... his bones were gone.

"Second uncle..."

Cao Qingyuan was full of grief.

But Cao Guang turned around, his gaze icy cold, his face accompanied by the rolling thunder in the dark clouds, even appearing somewhat ferocious.

"What are you grieving for? Prepare the Thousand Buddha Feast, do you think Li Qingshan has been in Flying Thunder City for so long, and suddenly left for no reason?"

"He's giving us an opportunity to perform the sacrificial ritual, he must come back, once the ritual is one-fifth complete, he will come to kill!"

Cao Guang's face was ice cold, the words that came out of his clenched teeth made Cao Qingyuan's heart tremble.

"He... is he feigning departure?"

"He knows, he's in the city, I won't perform the ritual, and moreover, he can only use his vital energy now, although he has the cultivation of a grandmaster, he can't hammer me to death."

Cao Guang said coldly.

"So, he must have drawn divine nature from the divine sect's mountain gate... he's just going outside the city to collect divine nature, while also giving us an opportunity to perform the ritual..."

Cao Guang closed his eyes, his body trembling slightly.

"If I don't perform the ritual, the Li Qingshan who has obtained divine nature... I won't be able to withstand even one of his hammer blows, I'll be smashed to pieces, and everything will be over then."

"He's forcing me..."

"Openly and secretly... forcing me!"

Cao Guang's words were like thunderous shocks in Cao Qingyuan's ears, making him tremble and involuntarily take two steps back.

"Does he know that if we perform the ritual, it will completely awaken the temple god..."

"Dare he face the fully awakened mysterious temple god?!"

Cao Qingyuan's voice had become louder.

Cao Guang let out a breath, his voice slightly bitter: "Perhaps... he never took us into account at all..."

"Is he aiming... at the temple god all along?"

Cao Qingyuan paused.

"Is he so arrogant?"

Cao Guang suddenly smiled bitterly, his gaze tinged with malice, as if he was unwilling to recall a long-buried scar.

"This is nothing compared to the arrogance I've seen..."

Shaking his head.

Cao Guang's thoughts returned, looking at Cao Qingyuan indifferently.

"I've already guessed his purpose, since Li Qingshan is so reckless... I'll go along with his recklessness."

"Today, I'll perform the ritual, as long as the ritual is one-fifth complete, if my cultivation breaks through... Li Qingshan may not be able to harm me."

"Although he is among the top ten in the divine foundation realm, most of his divine nature is left in the mysterious temple in the sect's mountain gate... the divine nature he can mobilize is not more than one-fifth."
