Crack!

Rumble!

They looked towards the direction of the lightning and thunder.

After landing outside the gang's courtyard, he lifted his foot and began walking calmly.

With each step, the earth seemed to tremble in sync, like the pounding of a drum against their chests, causing the Black Snake Gang members' blood to nearly freeze and their heartbeats to be pulled and connected.

Pah! One sturdy warrior couldn't endure it, drenched in sweat, coughing up blood and collapsing to the ground, the fear in his eyes uncontrollable.

Cat-faced mask, ram's horn hammer...

Ram's Horn Shattering the Heavens, Li Qingshan!

The Black Snake Gang members' souls were shaken, for they knew all too well the weight of Li Qingshan's name.

That ram's horn hammer, how many experts had it killed in the capital city? How much of their blood had it tasted! Compared to the Ox Demon, they were even more terrified of this long-infamous existence!

The raindrops falling from the sky were completely twisted and scattered before even reaching Li Qingshan's body by the invisible spiritual energy.

A faint laughter drifted out from behind the cat-faced mask.

"It's a pity you didn't take the chance to kill me while I was away from the capital city..."

The voice echoed in the deathly silent Black Snake Gang courtyard.

No one dared to answer.

The Black Snake Gang members knew that Li Qingshan was not actually conversing with them.

The two deputy gang leaders of the Black Snake Gang, terrified to the point of despair, their legs trembling, directly threw down their weapons and knelt on the ground.

"Spare us..."

"Lord Li, please spare us!"

Perhaps taking the lead, the Black Snake Gang members one by one threw down their weapons and knelt on the ground, no longer having any thoughts of resistance.

The eyes behind Li Qingshan's mask glanced over them indifferently.

Clenching his five fingers.

The Ram's Horn Shattering the Heavens hammer, crackling with lightning arcs, suddenly shot back from the deep pit it was embedded in, shattering the heads of the two deputy gang leaders on its return, spraying blood and brain matter everywhere.

With a clap, Li Qingshan grasped the Ram's Horn Shattering the Heavens hammer. Ignoring the Black Snake Gang members, he stomped his foot, causing his majestic blood and qi to erupt, as if transforming into three raging spiritual dragons roaring and surging into the heavens!

The Primal Spiritual Sect, the Grandmaster's supreme skill of Body Tempering, the Eight Dragons Overturning the River!

Boom! The entire earth instantly shattered, with countless crisscrossing cracks rolling, and countless scattered stones being blown into the air by the rising heat from below.

Li Qingshan, wrapped in the three raging spiritual dragons of blood and qi, charged out like an ancient fierce beast! A house instantly exploded!

"Li Qingshan—!"

A heavy voice, tinged with fear!

It was the Fishing Gang's Void Appearance Realm deputy leader, Liu Jinsong!

Hidden here, he had originally set up a heavenly net to target the Ox Demon, cooperating with the experts of the Corpse Deity Sect.

The majestic true qi rampaged and surged, sweeping away all the scattered debris from the exploded house. Li Qingshan stood there, gripping the ram's horn hammer with one hand.

With him as the center, the surrounding airflow was forcefully squeezed out by the three raging spiritual dragons of blood and qi!

Rising into the air, the blurry and illusory divine form of Liu Jinsong had an extremely unsightly expression on his face.

"The Ox Demon slaughtered seven forces under my Fishing Gang, and I set this trap just to kill the Ox Demon! Why are you, Li Qingshan, interfering?!"

"I am a wandering Grandmaster, who dares to stop me?!"

Behind the mask, Li Qingshan laughed.

The next moment, exhaling like thunder, his voice like a startling wild goose! Without any nonsense, Li Qingshan knew who the Ox Demon was, and the reasons behind the Fishing Gang's forces being slaughtered, he had also learned the ins and outs from the intelligence.

This Fishing Gang... colluding with the Corpse Deity Sect, harming the common people, cultivating evil arts, they deserved to die!

Today, upon returning to the Capital City, he wanted to instill fear, to display his might!

To make those people in the Capital City remember his Ram's Horn Shattering the Heavens hammer once again!

Li Qingshan took a step forward, the three raging spiritual dragons wildly dancing, like flickering flames! It seemed as if countless lightning arcs were erupting and surging, instantly sweeping forth with his spoken words! "Who dares to stop me?!"

Rumble! The ram's horn hammer broke through the air, in an instant, like a giant hammer striking a drum, the heavens and earth as the drum, countless thunderbolts rising, the lightning arcs soaring into the clouds, like a thunderstorm lashing the mortal realm! Just one strike, overwhelming and suppressing! Within a hundred zhang, everything was dazzlingly bright with thunderbolts! The thunderbolts entwined with the Grandmaster's blood and qi dragons, making the heavens and earth seem to lose their luster in this moment! Liu Jinsong's illusory divine form was dimmed and obscured by this strike! This strike! Such a strike! The divine powers of the six realms spanned the heavens and earth, coiling within the hammer's shadow, Li Qingshan's spirit, true qi, and heroic bearing all vented through this single strike!

I'll die!

I'm going to die!

Liu Jinsong roared, pushing his hands forward forcefully, his divine appearance seeming to condense in the heavens and earth, glaring angrily at the mortal realm.

However, the cat-masked Li Qingshan merely let out a snort of laughter.

The rumbling sound of the hammer echoed, accompanying his laughter as it reverberated through the heavens and earth.

"Divine Appearance? Just an illusion, and what divine foundation have you forged..."

"What divine foundation have I forged then?"

"My divine foundation was earned by exchanging my life before the temple gods..."

"Do you think you can compare to me?"

In the darkness, Li Yue, wearing a cute ox mask, restrained his spiritual energy, blending into the surroundings, watching Li Qingshan's actions from afar.

"So powerful?!"

Li Yue's pupils contracted slightly, his heart shaken.

Just feeling the faint surging thunderbolts, he felt his physical body go numb, as if his very cells had been shocked into paralysis!

Shattering the darkness with thunderbolts! Dispersing all evil spirits!

Tyrannically powerful!

Li Yue knew that the Li Qingshan in Fei Lei City had not yet fully integrated his divine powers, relying solely on his physical body, and it seemed his physical blood and qi could not be freely unleashed either.

Yet even then, he had smashed three Corpse Deity Sect's Cursed Iron Corpse experts.

Li Yue had thought that with Li Qingshan's divine powers now in place, his blood and qi unleashed, he wouldn't be that much stronger...

But the gap turned out to be so huge!

Li Qingshan... was just at the divine foundation realm! However, Li Yue soon calmed down, his eyes narrowing, his mind churning with countless thoughts.

Finally, he let out a breath, rumbling like rolling thunder.

His figure instantly merged into the darkness.

Silently.

Pah! In the night sky, countless falling spring rain were shattered and exploded into a dense mist! The illusory divine form, like a porcelain sculpture, covered in cracks, then exploded into pieces, dissipating into the heavens and earth.

The Fishing Gang deputy leader Liu Jinsong let out a miserable howl, spraying out mouthfuls of blood that echoed through the night sky, tearing through the darkness!

One strike fell, the thunderbolts in the pitch-black night sky bloomed like a spider web, as if shattering the very void!

Liu Jinsong's body was sent flying in midair, his bones and joints cracking and popping, spewing out thick, mercury-like blood from his mouth in an uncontrolled manner! Thud!

Liu Jinsong crashed onto the long street, shattering numerous blue bricks, the ground sinking, billowing clouds of dust spreading out in a circular pattern.

Just one strike, and the Void Appearance Realm Liu Jinsong was defeated!

In the sunken pit on the long street, Liu Jinsong clutched his chest, tottering. His eyes bulging with rage, he let out a roar.

Just one strike, and he had been beaten bloody, if they truly fought, he would definitely be hammered to death!
