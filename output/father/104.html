"Has this boy been to the Three Prince Temple outside the city?"

The white-robed Vice Supervisor's robe fluttered without wind as he narrowed his eyes at the serene wooden statue placed on the tea table.

Although... it was only at the Tenth Rank level, its divinity was quite weak.

But for a master woodcarver, through a wooden sculpture, one could sense what the carver wanted to express - a meaning conveyed through the technique, lines, and patterns.

The reason why they are masters is that they have mastered an art to the extreme, and if they want to seek a breakthrough, they can only present and express it differently in terms of meaning.

Some masters of freehand ink painting, with just a few strokes, may seem like random splashes of ink at first glance, but upon careful observation, a unique beauty, a beauty of meaning, can be discovered.

Carving... is the same.

Gongyang Liancheng and Lady Gong Yunli were both bewildered by the Vice Supervisor's words.

"Vice Supervisor, are you referring to the 'Furious Eight-Armed Prince of Hell' temple, the strange temple outside the city?" Lady Gong Yunli couldn't help but ask.

The Vice Supervisor, dressed in white, had a delicate appearance, looking like a handsome young man of seventeen or eighteen. He gave Lady Gong Yunli an impatient glance. "Little Gong, don't be brainless..."

"Are there other Prince temples outside the city?"

Hearing this, Lady Gong Yunli immediately felt embarrassed. She looked down at her own prominent bosom, and the Vice Supervisor... just loved to tell the whole truth.

"He must not have gone in there, nor seen the temple's deity..."

"Li Che's blood cultivation is only at the level of blood refinement, and his divinity is only as pure as a stream... He cannot even enter the Mysterious Pavilion, let alone the strange temple."

"Even if we... were to forcibly enter the strange temple while bearing the temple deity's pressure, we might not be able to reach the temple altar and see the furious eight-armed Prince of Hell deity."

Gongyang Liancheng spoke up in a deep voice from the side.

"That's indeed the case..."

"Even if that little fellow has hidden his cultivation, he couldn't have hidden so much."

The Vice Supervisor nodded, walked up to the wooden sculpture, and raised his finger, gently caressing the carved lines.

He closed his eyes, and with each inch his fingers slid across the sculpture, it seemed to evoke a strange sensation that made the hair on his body stand on end.

"This wood carving assignment was set by me. I spent a few breaths of time simply roughing out the initial form, but this boy... not only perfectly continued my carving ideas and techniques, but also in the latter half, infused his own understanding and feelings."

"He must never have seen the true deity, yet he was able to depict something almost identical to it through his own imagination."

"This boy... is like me."

The Vice Supervisor slowly opened his eyes, a gentle smile curling at the corners of his lips.

He then said, "This boy... has the qualifications of a Special-Grade Guest Official."

These words immediately shocked Gongyang Liancheng and Lady Gong Yunli.

This evaluation... was too high!

Guest Officials are usually divided into three grades: Third, Second, and First. A First-Grade Guest Official, placed in the Astronomical Observatory of the capital city, would even have a status surpassing theirs as Junior Supervisors.

Because of the identity of a First-Grade Guest Official, even if they went to other cities or even provincial capitals, they would still be able to get by.

But they were only Junior Supervisors of the Astronomical Observatory in the Golden Light Prefecture. If they left the Golden Light Prefecture and went to other prefectures, they would naturally be a grade lower. If they went to provincial capitals or even the Great Deity Capital, they would be two or three grades lower!

Let alone... a Special-Grade Guest Official!

Lady Gong Yunli was even more disbelieving, shaking her head, her ample bosom heaving up and down.

"Vice Supervisor, are you... excessively praising a newcomer! You can't just flatter him because he was recommended by Li Qingshan!"

Gongyang Liancheng regained his senses, his face darkening.

Oh, little Yunli, how can you speak to your superior like that? Can you say such words?

Sure enough, the Vice Supervisor's brow furrowed. "Excessively praising? Am I flattering a newcomer because of Li Qingshan?"

"Little Gong, do you remember how many articles there are in the total regulations for formal Astronomical Observatory officials jointly drafted by the Chief Supervisor and Vice Supervisor of the Astronomical Observatory in the Divine Capital?"

Lady Gong Yunli: "There are a total of 108 Astronomical Observatory regulations."

"You remembered very well. Go back and copy them 20 times. Tomorrow, bring them to me for inspection. If there is even one word missing or wrong, you will be fined 10 Divine Essence Crystals."

Lady Gong Yunli was suddenly alarmed, gritting her teeth in resentment. Settling personal grudges, how unreasonable! The Vice Supervisor's eyes narrowed as he glared at Lady Gong Yunli.

"30 times!"

Lady Gong Yunli immediately lowered her unwilling head, forgetting that this old man had a similar heart-reading divine art.

"Vice Supervisor, I still have to say, casually proclaiming someone has the qualifications of a Special-Grade Guest Official... is too much flattery of a newcomer!"

Lady Gong Yunli quite liked Li Che, a good woodcarving master, who was amiable and had a gentle temperament. She didn't want this young man to be crushed by such high praise.

After saying this, Lady Gong Yunli quickly tucked her white robe and hurriedly slipped away.

The Vice Supervisor remained silent.

He looked at Gongyang Liancheng.

"The Vice Supervisor has keen eyes and is right. I also feel that this boy has limitless potential and the qualifications of a Special-Grade Guest Official!"

Gongyang Liancheng loudly expressed his agreement, not being as brainless and iron-headed as Lady Gong Yunli.

Opposing a superior, what's the point? Gongyang Liancheng's motto was...

One flattering word is worth a thousand in gold!

"Remind little Gong not to spread this evaluation around. Calling it excessive flattery of a newcomer... I'm afraid she'll let it slip first."

The Vice Supervisor was weary. His two subordinates, one was as stubborn as iron, the other as flattering as could be...

They were truly both fragrant and foul.

Gongyang Liancheng naturally understood, solemnly nodding, "The Vice Supervisor's consideration is to be prepared for the unexpected. No wonder you are the Supervisor. Your subordinate is sincerely..."

"Shut up and leave."

Gongyang Liancheng made a deep bow, hands lowered to the ground, and slowly retreated.

Within the tea room, the fragrance of tea lingered.

Tranquil as usual.

The Vice Supervisor's fingers caressed the statue, and in a moment of reverie, he seemed to see the figure of someone in red robes, stirring the vast sea...

"Recommended by Li Qingshan..."

"Such talent, not sent to the Divine Sect, but instead sent to the Astronomical Observatory to be a Guest Official... for what purpose? In another five years... it will be the time when the Furious Three Prince of Hell Temple's Mysterious Pavilion shakes and forms a calamity..."

"The Corpse God Cult..."

"It's a turbulent time."

The Vice Supervisor withdrew his hand from touching the statue and closed his eyes, rubbing his temples.

Suddenly, his fingers paused, and his slender eyes opened.

"Or perhaps... this Li Qingshan fellow is suspecting a schism in the Divine Sect?"

Becoming a Third-Grade Guest Official of the Astronomical Observatory, he could enjoy the Observatory's resources while also obtaining many good things.

Most importantly, he had obtained a tiger skin, enough to make many powers in the prefecture city wary of him. It could ensure the safety of his little family for some years.

For Li Che, that was enough.

He didn't want much, just a peaceful and stable life.

Quietly watching his daughter grow up, watching her cultivation reach maturity.

This was a simple and modest wish of an old father.

Li Che was in a good mood as he walked on the wide street in the prefecture city, wide enough to accommodate ten horse-drawn carriages side by side.

Inhaling the fresh air after the rain, basking in the spring breeze that soothed the heart.

Li Che planned to go to the留香阁 (Lingering Fragrance Pavilion) to order some good wine and dishes, go back and celebrate, especially the roast duck from Lingering Fragrance Pavilion, get a few more, as his daughter and wife both loved to eat it.

However, Li Che did not let his guard down. After all, he had killed Qin Yuqing...

Although he was sure he had left no clues related to himself, and even if the Qin family really came to his door, without evidence, they wouldn't be able to do anything to him with his status as a Guest Official of the Astronomical Observatory.

But there are no absolutes in this world, what if the Qin family produced another Qin Yuqing-like venomous woman? The kind who thinks they can kill you just because they want to, and even the passing dog should be stabbed...

So Li Che still had to be vigilant, not to let his guard down. Even in the prefecture city, this world was not safe, and perhaps for a long time, he would have to tread carefully.

Oh yes, Li Che's mind stirred, the Chess Sage Dao Fruit trembled, and the Heaven and Earth Chess Board's enveloping spread expanded.

Sure enough, the three Qin family's gray-faced secret agents who had been tracking and investigating him had disappeared without a trace.

With Qin Yuqing's death, these three people must have returned to the Qin family to report the situation.

Fortunately, they had provided him with proof of his entry into the Astronomical Observatory, proof that he could not have harmed Qin Yuqing, as he had shot her in a different direction in the Astronomical Observatory Treasure Hall.

After ordering the wine and dishes, Li Che did not immediately return to his own small courtyard, but first went to another courtyard he had purchased, the workshop courtyard.

Sitting in a chair, heating water, and brewing a pot of tea.

Amid the swaying spring breeze, sipping a good tea, his mood became even more beautiful.

Then, he began to organize the gains he had obtained after becoming a Third-Grade Guest Official of the Astronomical Observatory.

His mind stirred.
