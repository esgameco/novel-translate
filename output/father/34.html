Li Che brought his uncle's family back to the Xu family's estate. They first stayed in his own courtyard for one night, and the next day he went to find the steward, Xun.

Since Xu You had given his approval, Xun the steward did not give them any trouble. Although Xun had become rather cold towards Li Che after the loss of his son, upon further reflection, Xun Changbiao had originally wanted to sacrifice Xixia to pray for a son.

The fault lay with his son, not with Li Che's family who were the victims.

After smoothly resolving the issue of his uncle's accommodation, Li Che breathed a sigh of relief.

Recalling the oppressive feeling when the Lingying Cult followers were marching on the streets, Li Che couldn't help but furrow his brows slightly. The entire Lingying Cult... they all looked like bad people. They would eventually become a disaster.

In the workshop, Li Che paused his carving knife, his black hair flecked with wood shavings, and gazed at the drifting snowflakes in the courtyard, mentally adding the entire Lingying Cult to his personal "must eliminate" list, with the Lingying Patriarch at the very top, circled and highlighted.

Day by day, time passed.

Ever since the bounty on the Ox Demon was raised to 500 taels, aside from helping his uncle's family, Li Che had chosen to keep a low profile and not take any further action.

He had tried to track down the constable Zhao Chuanxiong, but unfortunately, Zhao seemed to know Li Che was trying to kill him and had become cautious and sly. He never appeared alone, and even when traveling, he was accompanied by several constable subordinates. At night, he did not return home, hiding somewhere unknown.

Li Che couldn't find an opportunity to strike, so he gave up.

Perhaps due to the death of that Baguazhang expert who had reached the Tempering the Bones realm, the outskirts of Flying Thunder City seemed to have settled down for a while. But gradually, news of more children being killed started to spread.

As long as there were people, evil would never cease.

Time slipped through the fingers like sand, turning from spring to summer in the blink of an eye.

Unknowingly, half a year had flown by.

At the Xu Family Woodcarving Workshop.

Li Che took a deep breath, blowing away the wood shavings on the carving. Amidst the swirling dust, a "Nine Children Embracing the Lotus" statue emerged, lifelike and vivid before him.

Old Chen had also carved a Nine Children Embracing the Lotus wooden sculpture before, but the one Li Che had carved was even more lively and expressive, with a unique artistic conception, as if the nine children carved were truly alive, playing and frolicking, their voices almost audible.

This was the result of his "Immortal Craftsman" Dao Fruit reaching Level 3, imbuing the work with an even richer artistic essence.

"Ah Che, your skills have really improved! You'll probably be promoted to a Fourth-Grade Woodcarver in the next evaluation," said Xu You, standing at the workshop entrance in a silk white robe, holding a jar of wine and smiling warmly.

"As a Fourth-Grade Woodcarver, you'll be able to freely come and go between the inner and outer cities. Ah Che, have you considered moving your whole family into the inner city?"

Xu You asked.

Hearing this, Li Che thought about the experts swarming in the inner city, and how the true powerhouses of the Lingying Cult were said to be within. Moving there would be too risky.

Just as Old Chen had said, the living conditions in the outer city were a bit worse, but being far from the vortex of intrigue, it was more carefree and peaceful.

Seeing Li Che remain silent for a long time, Xu You waved his hand: "Nevermind, let's not talk about this topic. If you moved to the inner city, it would be a hassle for me to come drink with you."

As a member of the family who was not favored and had lost the chance to compete for the position of clan leader, staying in the outer city was like being exiled. Xu You, since returning from clearing out the mysterious temple evil in the outer city, seemed to have taken a liking to Li Che, often coming to drink with him. At first, it may have been with the intention of cultivating him, but over time, he had genuinely come to see Li Che as a friend.

Coming to mooch meals at Li Che's little courtyard had become a familiar routine.

After work, Li Che and Xu You returned to the small courtyard together.

The loquat tree in the courtyard was lush and verdant.

Little Xixia was under the loquat tree, gripping a wooden stick, happily shouting and giggling nonstop.

One and a half weeks old Xixia was lively and intelligent, almost like a three-year-old child - full of boundless energy, active and agile, her differences from a normal infant becoming increasingly apparent.

"Daddy!"

Seeing Li Che return, Xixia's chubby little face lit up, and she ran towards him on her bare little feet, her topknot bouncing.

She came to an abrupt stop in front of Li Che and Xu You, her head almost colliding with Li Che's leg.

"Daddy, did you bring sugar-coated haws for Xixia to eat? Where are they?"

Xixia blinked her big eyes, already searching around.

But after looking all around, she couldn't find them, and her puffed-out chest deflated in disappointment.

Li Che laughed and picked up the dejected Xixia. "That was what I promised you the day before yesterday. Didn't I bring you sugar-coated haws just yesterday?"

Xixia paused, tilting her head. "Really? I forgot the taste of the sugar-coated haws from yesterday..."

She stuck out her tongue, licking her lips.

Xu You couldn't help but laugh. "Uncle's got you, your daddy didn't buy you any. Your Uncle Xu did."

As if performing a magic trick, Xu You suddenly produced a sugar-coated haw, and Xixia cheered excitedly.

Old Chen pulled over a bamboo chair, sitting under the loquat tree to enjoy the cool breeze. Seeing this, he couldn't help but shake his head and smile. "Third Master, you mustn't spoil little Xixia so much. Eating sugar-coated haws every day will ruin her teeth."

Xixia heard this and pouted. "I'm not little Xixia, I'm Warm Xixia, Li Wanxixia!"

She muttered and grumbled, her words still carrying a touch of childish sweetness.

Outer City, Listening to Rain Alley.

In the courtyard rented by Li Che.

Suddenly, the Dao Fruit [Immortal Craftsman] and [Dragon Elephant Golden Armor] in his chest cavity pulsed slightly! The familiar scene appeared before his eyes.

On the vast earth, a precious tree towering into the clouds rose up, its countless leaves trembling and sparkling, and on it, a seven-colored fruit slowly formed, the fruit glowing and flowing, reflecting the many growth scenes of Xixie over the past year.

With long black hair, Li Che's eyes were bright.

Another year of expectation has finally blossomed at this moment.

Xixie is two years old, the third Dao Fruit...
