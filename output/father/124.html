But for Zhang Ya, the mother, if she didn't have this permit to enter and exit, she would have to be separated from her daughter for a very, very long time...

Cultivators cultivate without regard for days and months, and as a mother, how much Zhang Ya must miss Xixia.

"Xixia, three days later, prepare for the ceremony of becoming my disciple, remember to address me as 'Master' then."

"Okay, Master!" Xixia called out cheerfully.

Nan Liehuo was at a loss for words, then suddenly burst into laughter, truly a clever and quick-witted little rascal.

"The Qianyuan Shenzhong Sect, the Jinguang Branch Sect, the entrance examination..."
