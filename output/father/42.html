Each increase in strength is a leap!

"Who are you exactly..."

Zhao Chuanxiong kept spitting out fresh blood, staring fixatedly at that adorable ox-faced mask.

If this was a martial artist from Feilei City, with such rapid progress, it would be impossible to keep it hidden, the news would have spread throughout the entire city! Even if it was a powerful clan, they would be going crazy over it! But there is no such person, there is no information about this person in the entire Feilei City.

Li Che looked at Zhao Chuanxiong indifferently, not responding, his cold eyes like two sharp blades of frost in the winter.

The arm that had crushed Zhao Chuanxiong's chest suddenly withdrew, spraying a gush of blood, about to strike down again.

The terrifying power of a blood exchange warrior, one more punch, and Zhao Chuanxiong believed he would die! "The fish has been hooked! Save me!!!"

Zhao Chuanxiong let out a roar like thunder, expelling all the air he had left, the thunderous sound echoing all around!

The eyes behind Li Che's mask remained unmoved.

Indeed, Zhao Chuanxiong was just bait, a lure to hook him in, Simo Bai had colluded with the Lingying Sect... The one they actually wanted to eliminate was him.

This Ox Demon who dared to seek freedom in Feilei City, under the control of the Lingying Sect.

After shouting, Zhao Chuanxiong poured all his remaining strength into rolling into the snowy ground, trying to escape.

He was afraid.

The Ox Demon's unwavering gaze terrified Zhao Chuanxiong.

He knew...

The Ox Demon had known all along... that this was an ambush to fish him out! But he still wanted to swallow this bait!

Damn it! Zhao Chuanxiong had underestimated the Ox Demon's determination to kill him...

How could it be so strong? What grievance, what resentment? Just because they had bullied him a bit in Liu's courtyard with the Bagua Staff back then? Didn't the Ox Demon even suffer any injuries? Yet he could hold such a deep grudge! His physique is so towering and mighty, yet his heart is so small!

Li Che looked indifferently at the rolling Zhao Chuanxiong in the snow, his bloodied palm suddenly raised, the five fingers hooking, making a screeching sound like chalk on a blackboard in the night. Barely visible silver threads suddenly sprang up.

In the twinkling of an eye, the air burst, a wailing cry like a ghost's lament. Pshh!

On the ground...

Scalding blood spurted up three feet high!

Zhao Chuanxiong's head and neck were torn apart, his bulging eyes still flashing with resentment and bewilderment.

Why? Why! Even in death, Zhao Chuanxiong still had endless resentment and anger! In this moment, the only one Zhao Chuanxiong hated was Simo Bai!

He had endured for so long, why force him to come to his death?! You Simo Bai, you bastard, using him as bait, but making him the fish! Hateful!

The silent threads swayed, coated with round blood droplets.

In the twitching, the blood droplets exploded into a blood mist.

Thousands of threads! Around the mighty Ox Demon, countless silver threads intertwined like electric currents, whipping the air, tearing the drifting snowflakes.
