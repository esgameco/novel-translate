Suddenly, dark clouds revealed azure lightning like a spider's web, the bulging veins on the skin of the ultimate warrior! The torrential rain poured down, shrouding the entire Flying Thunder City.

The incident at the Golden Brilliance Pavilion was so abrupt that the entire inner city was instantly plunged into a state of stagnation.

Those unaware of the various forces at play dared not make a sound or move.

In the Xu Family Mansion, in the Inner Cultivation Monastery.

Xu You, who was sitting under the eaves reading the "Great Landscape Record", was suddenly startled awake by the thunderous roar in the sky, his body covered in cold sweat. He looked towards the direction of the Golden Brilliance Pavilion, his eyes narrowing slightly.

From afar, the four massive divine foundations, as grand as the sun, were still radiating the utmost brilliance!

Any cultivator of divine nature would have sensed this. Xu You's divine cultivation was not high, and at the age of twenty-four, he had just barely reached the "Cultivating Nature like a Stream" stage.

Although he had stepped into the second realm of Cultivating Nature, he had already lost the possibility of assaulting the divine foundations.

Unlike his elder brother Xu Heli, whose talent was extraordinary, and who had been named one of the Three Young Masters of Flying Thunder City, had already cultivated his "Suspended Thought Flowing Sword" divine foundation to the "Cultivating Nature like a River" stage, not far from forging a divine foundation.

"A battle of divine foundations?"

Xu You was struck by lightning, instantly realizing that a great event was about to unfold in Flying Thunder City.

He did not ponder further on the specifics of what was happening.

He quickly turned his gaze to the Inner Cultivation Monastery, sweeping over the young disciples quietly cultivating their nature, and his gaze finally rested on the well-behaved Xiu Xiu, feeling relieved in his heart.

In fact, Xu You was not the only one who had noticed.

In the Inner Cultivation Monastery, an elderly Xu family matriarch slowly approached, one of the senior divine cultivators responsible for teaching the young disciples, a powerful figure who had cultivated their nature to the "River-like" stage.

"Ayu, lock the doors of the Inner Cultivation Monastery, and do not let anyone in. Come back and stand guard with me to protect the safety of the children."

The old matriarch, holding a phoenix-headed cane, her face wrinkled like old tree bark, was extremely serious.

Xu You hurriedly nodded and agreed.

"I wonder how Ache is doing in the workshop... I hope he stays put and does not go out of curiosity to watch the commotion."

"The clash of divine cultivators, even the slightest backlash, can be fatal, dooming one to eternal damnation..."

Xu You shook his head, suppressing the worry in his heart.

"Xiu Xiu, come to your Uncle Xu."

Xu You waved to Xiu Xiu.

Xiu Xiu was very obedient and immediately ran to Xu You's side.

But behind Xiu Xiu, a large-headed boy immediately followed.

"Chizi, come quickly, stay behind me, and we'll be safe!" Xiu Xiu stretched her neck and waved.

Xu You glanced at the large-headed boy following behind Xiu Xiu.

This boy had a Nezha-style braid, which, combined with his large head, was quite eye-catching.

He couldn't help but take another look.

"Sumu Grandma, whose disciple is this?" Xu You couldn't help but ask.

The old Sumu, leaning on the phoenix-headed cane, glanced at the Nezha-braided boy and coldly smiled.

"Entrusted by an acquaintance..."

Li Che, wearing a straw hat and a bovine-faced mask, his eyes burning with anger, the elastic clothes tightly binding his body, seemed to outline every pulsing vein and knotted muscle!

He stomped down hard! The vital energy rolled like thunderous rage!

Like a sudden explosion of a bomb!

The standing water at his feet was instantly drained, like a giant stone thrown into a lake, the rippling water wall reaching several feet high, the bluestone tiles on the ground cracking and deeply indented! Unprecedented terrifying power was unleashed at this moment.

The raging air currents, in the turbulent waves of water!

A figure shrieked and shot out, the splashing semi-transparent water distorting into a blurred human form, the divine energy violently fluctuating and manifesting.

Simo Bai, soaked to the bone like a drowned rat, had long lost the calm and composure he had when facing Li Che.

As he was single-mindedly trying to flee towards the city outskirts...

But he never expected that halfway, such a terrifying vital energy would suddenly erupt!

Transcending Meridians realm! A Transcending Meridians martial artist... had ambushed him in advance?!

Whose family's?

This secret technique was passed down by the divine foundation's old patriarch, and under the right time and place, no divine cultivator... could possibly track his whereabouts during the evasion!

Splash...

Simo Bai slid several meters in the street water, half-kneeling on the ground, panting heavily, the rain water distorting as it streamed down his face.

He stared intently at that burly figure.

The black, trembling, powerful veins, like magma-red vital energy!

The pure and mighty condensed spiritual energy rushing towards him!

"Ox Demon!!!"

Simo Bai stared at the majestic aura, the vital energy burning the rain like mist, and the hazy heat rising, his eyes filled with undisguised murderous intent! But in his eyes, there was also a deep sadness, countless tears rolling down his cheeks.

Simo Bai never imagined... that the one blocking his path would be the Ox Demon, a self-proclaimed hero that he should have easily eliminated. How dare he obstruct a "River-like" divine cultivator like himself? Although he now sensed the Ox Demon's aura had reached the ultimate Transcending Meridians level.
