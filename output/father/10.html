Li Che gripped the wooden figurine of the spirit infant, his gaze flickering with uncertainty.

Since the other party had set their sights on him and produced this eerie wooden spirit infant figurine as a mark, Li Che naturally dared not take the risk. What if he went out to work, and the other party forcefully broke into his humble abode and attacked? He would surely regret it then.

"The safest place... would naturally be the woodcarving workshop!"

"I am now a master woodcarver, with my own workshop. I can have Xixia and Xiaoye stay at my workshop..."

"Mm, stable and safe!"

After making this steady and secure plan, Li Che called his wife Zhang Ya to wake up.

"My lord, you were too exhausting last night, pent up for too long, weren't you..." Zhang Ya's fair face flushed slightly, and her gaze at Li Che seemed to be brimming with water.

Li Che smiled and gently ruffled his wife's disheveled hair.

"My lady, pack up and bring Xixia, and come with me to the woodcarving workshop... Stay with me at work these two days."

Li Che said.

Zhang Ya was momentarily stunned, go to the woodcarving workshop and work together? "My lord, today is your first day as a woodcarving master... Is it really appropriate for me and Xixia to come along? Won't it displease the shopkeeper?"

Li Che shook his head: "No, it won't. The workshop is the private space of the woodcarving master, and it's fine for family members to stay there. Besides, I'll explain it to the shopkeeper."

Hearing this, Zhang Ya no longer said anything, and began to pack up, bringing the wide-eyed Xixia who was playing on the bed.

After carefully bundling up Xixia, the family set out towards Xu's Woodcarving Workshop, braving the wind and snow.

Li Che protected his wife and daughter, the biting wind howling as he tightened the cotton coat around his wife, wrapping Xixia to shield her from the cold.

At the same time, Li Che's peripheral vision constantly scanned the surroundings, his heart highly alert.

Seeing her husband's manner, Zhang Ya's heart suddenly chilled, knowing that something must have happened, but since Li Che didn't say anything, she didn't ask either, only silently worrying in her heart.

The couple, with their child, crossed the snow-covered cobblestone street.

"The holy spirit teaches, cleansing the mundane, the law of reincarnation without worry..."

"It takes great wisdom to succeed without being taught..."

The sound of ringing bells and beating drums echoed, as the procession of the Spirit Infant Cult, with their captivating chants, approached from the distance.

On the raised platform, the three-headed, six-armed wooden figurine of the Spirit Infant grew increasingly ominous amidst the incense smoke.

Li Che even vaguely felt that the gaze of the wooden figurine of the Spirit Infant Master seemed to be directed towards his family, a hazy gaze constantly watching them, making him feel like he had a target on his back!

"My lord..." Zhang Ya looked at the procession of the Spirit Infant Cult, her expression clearly anxious.

"Don't be afraid, I'm here."

"Don't look, just keep walking."

Li Che patted his wife, embracing her slender shoulders as they headed straight for the woodcarving workshop.

But Li Che couldn't help but feel that the cold wind was swirling behind them, as if there was a figure constantly following them.

The sense of crisis was like ink slowly diffusing in a pool, gradually darkening the entire pool.

Fortunately, they soon arrived at Xu's Woodcarving Workshop.

The moment they stepped into the workshop, Li Che felt his body relax. He looked back towards the distance and his eyes narrowed, sure enough, he saw a dark shadow flashing around the corner.

"Leichunlan?"

Li Che's chest heaved violently, and he couldn't help but be overcome by the urge to kill.

"Ache?"

Suddenly, a familiar voice sounded, and Chen Daobao, holding a gourd and drinking, saw Li Che and his wife and daughter.

This entourage... made him pause for a moment, but old Chen was shrewd, and he quickly connected it to the matter of the Spirit Infant Cult that Li Che had mentioned before, eyeing him knowingly.

"Come in first."

Old Chen's expression became solemn as he ushered Li Che and his family into the workshop.

Li Che arranged for Zhang Ya and Xixia to stay in his own workshop, then came to find Old Chen.

Old Chen sipped his gourd wine, glancing at Li Che: "You've been targeted?"

Li Che nodded, taking out the wooden spirit infant figurine that had appeared at his doorstep the previous night.

The moment he saw the wooden spirit infant figurine, Old Chen's expression became grave, and he even stopped drinking: "You did the right thing, bringing your wife and daughter to the woodcarving workshop, your home... is no longer safe."

"The Spirit Infant Cult, the Spirit Infant Cult... giving you this wooden figurine means they have assessed your daughter as a 'spirit infant'!"

Old Chen took the wooden figurine, his expression extremely serious.

Li Che's expression also changed involuntarily.

"They must have been watching you all along. This 'three-headed, six-armed spirit infant figurine' only appeared last night?"

"It seems they knew you had become a master woodcarver at Xu's, and knew you would move into Xu's compound, so..."

Li Che's expression darkened: "They want to strike first?"

"Yes, you're fortunate enough to have moved your wife and daughter here in time..." Old Chen clenched his fist, and the wooden spirit infant figurine instantly cracked and shattered into a pile of wooden pieces.

"You said someone was following you? Was it that midwife who has harmed many children?"

"I couldn't see clearly... I can't be sure."

"Considering we're about to become friendly neighbors... I'll take you to take a look, see if it's that brainwashed midwife..."

Old Chen gulped down a mouthful of strong old wine, slightly narrowing his eyes as he spoke.

Hearing this, Li Che couldn't help but feel delighted.

He knew that Old Chen's change in attitude was because of the woodcarving talent he had displayed, able to imbue the woodcarvings with such profound meaning - this talent, it seems, was even more important than he had imagined!

Li Che settled his wife and daughter in the workshop, instructing Zhang Ya not to wander off, and Old Chen had an experienced master help keep an eye on them, then the two of them went out of the workshop, braving the wind and snow with their hats pulled low.

Grinding the skin, replacing the blood... Old Chen had reached an unimaginable realm, and his inner strength was also extremely extraordinary.

Exhaling the hot breath in the snowy wind, Li Che pressed down on the hidden crossbow in his sleeve, his face as cold as water.

Not afraid of the thief, but afraid of the thief remembering...

Thinking that the lovely Xixia had actually been targeted by these villains, Li Che's heart was filled with intense brutality.
