The wind outside the city seemed to be much colder, blowing through the body and taking away the warmth, piercing into the spine.

Li Che wore a cute ox-faced mask, silently looking at Li Qingshan who wore a cat-faced mask.

Mask-wearing person looked at mask-wearing person, the atmosphere seemed to have a touch of peace.

"What are you looking at? Learned from you, this old man was once an upright person too..."

Li Qingshan stood with his hands behind his back, speaking indifferently.

Wearing the mask, he seemed to have become much colder, his emotions a bit unruly.

The mask isolated the facial features, giving people a sense of releasing the hidden emotions in their hearts.

Just like Li Che, every time he wore the cute ox mask, his body and mind would feel incredibly relaxed, doing anything, killing anyone... everything became uninhibited, without any restrictions.

"The meridians are unblocked? In three days' time... not bad at all."

"Since it's like this, let's go take a look at the Mysterious Temple, I should have some chance now."

"Go quickly and come back before dark, you must leave the temple."

Li Qingshan said in a deep voice, the next moment, with a tap of his toes, a surging force erupted from his feet, and the small old man's body turned into a straight black line, shooting out rapidly on the white snowy official road.

Li Che's body swayed, the inner qi that broke through the meridians surged through his legs, quickly following.

Whoosh whoosh whoosh -

The howling wind rushed by, the airflow rushing past the sides of the mask!

The scenery outside the city flashed by in the eyes like lightning.

The old man's speed was extremely fast, and Li Che found it quite difficult to keep up, after all, he had not used the peerless tempering of the "Dragon Elephant Golden Armor", just silently following like this.

In fact, the fleeing Li Qingshan in front was already greatly surprised.

"The foundation is extremely stable, he has entered the qi meridians, his inner qi is vast and long-lasting... indeed not bad."

Li Qingshan praised inwardly.

This is really a good seedling, just with such martial arts talent, even if Li Che is not a 22-year-old spiritual child, Li Qingshan would be willing to guide him into the Divine Sect.

The two rushed for about a quarter of an hour.

Li Qingshan then slowed down his speed.

His body paused, gracefully landing on the branches of an old pine tree covered in snow, the cat-faced mask, his two eyes shining brightly.

Li Che followed closely, also controlling his inner qi, imitating Li Qingshan's posture to land on the old pine.

Li Qingshan looked at Li Che and said, "You have entered the Qianyuan Divine Sect, most likely you will be under my tutelage, so... since you've come to the Mysterious Temple, let me take you to experience it first."

Li Che did not say much, just nodded, looking up and seeing the temple obscured by the dense forest, only a corner of the eaves visible, the vermilion lacquer color, almost like freshly smeared blood.

"This Mysterious Temple is a Tenth-Grade one, it's best to enter during the day, at night the temple deity will become restless, and if you wake up the temple deity, it won't be easy to deal with."

"I was once constrained by the awakened temple deity for two months... quite annoying."

"When the temple deity awakens, it will release its divine nature, and this divine nature will invade the surrounding living beings, causing them to mutate into evil spirits under its control. The evil spirits of a Tenth-Grade Mysterious Temple won't have divine foundation-level power, the problem is not big, you should be able to try clearing them."

"It's a good opportunity for you to consolidate your qi meridians and qi blood."

Li Che listened and did not speak further, his body flashed and rushed towards the front dense forest.

Controlling his inner qi, leaving no trace in the snow.

As if parting the obstructing forest, a lonely temple suddenly came into Li Che's view.

The temple had carved beams and painted eaves, upturned corners, the black tiles were dotted with white snow, slightly dilapidated, the vermilion lacquer covering the walls and eaves seemed to emanate a pungent bloody smell.

Li Che's gaze fell on the main gate of the temple, shifting slightly upwards, he saw a plaque.

Inscribed: Mysterious Temple of the Angry Three-Eyed True Lord Spiritual Child Branch.

Just a glance, Li Che felt the wrathful divine nature in his Nirvana Aperture start to become restless, as if it wanted to break free from his control and drift towards that Mysterious Temple!

However, after operating the divine nature technique, Li Che regained control of the divine nature, and with the "Untainted Heart" pounding in his chest, he was able to break free from this constraint.

And at the moment of breaking free, Li Che's pupils contracted.

Unknowingly, he had already appeared at the door of the Mysterious Temple, and one foot had even been lifted, almost stepping into the temple! Countless black qi spread out from the temple, wrapping around his feet, ice-cold and bone-piercing!

In the distance.

The small old man wearing the cat-faced mask, standing on the pine branch and preparing to take action, uttered a surprised sound.

"The first time encountering the Mysterious Temple, he was actually able to break free from the temple deity's attraction to the divine nature by relying on himself... such strong willpower!"

In front of the temple.

After breaking free from the temple deity's spiritual attraction in the Mysterious Temple, the black qi wrapped around his legs was washed away by his qi and blood.

Then, evil spirits emerged from the Mysterious Temple.

They were a group of corpses with black membranes and black spider webs covering their bodies, their eyeballs rolled back.

Their speed was extremely fast, their defense was extremely strong, but now that Li Che's meridians were unblocked and his inner qi was consolidated, his combat power had become even stronger, and he mastered the White Tiger Raging Wind Fist at the Transformation Realm, easily defeating these evil spirits.

After turning these evil spirits into a pile of rotten flesh, the small old man Li Qingshan floated over to his side.

"Not bad, these evil spirits are the elite guards of the temple. The evil spirit corpses of the Tenth-Grade Mysterious Temple all have Replacement Blood Realm strength, their flesh bodies are stronger than Replacement Blood, and they know no pain, they are not easy to deal with..."
