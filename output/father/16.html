The next moment, his mind was stimulated by the Dao Fruit [Dragon Elephant Golden Armor].

Li Che's original height of around 1.75 meters directly grew to around 1.9 meters after activating the Dragon Elephant Golden Armor. His blood and flesh also swelled up, becoming more solid and robust, exuding an extremely oppressive aura.

"Power..." The eyes behind the cute ox mask flashed, looking at his palms that contained terrifying power like fans. He clenched his fists tightly, vaguely causing a howling wind.

"It's as if it contains the power of the dragon and elephant..."

"Let's call this transformation the 'Golden Armor Change'," Li Che murmured softly.

He felt that at this moment, he was like a giant elephant in the jungle, possessing the terrifying might to trample fierce tigers and lions.

This is just the change in physical strength brought by the Dao Fruit alone...

Unparalleled in forging!

As the second son of the Sun family manager, Sun Changbiao's courtyard after splitting the family was naturally excellent, being a three-wing compound with a spacious and bright courtyard.

Sun Changbiao held an umbrella and returned to the courtyard after work.

Letting out a sigh, his expression became somewhat gloomy.

"Chen Dabao has set his sights on me... so fast, he's come to find me so quickly."

Standing in the courtyard, Sun Changbiao's expression was unsettled. He had noticed that Old Chen was monitoring him, and even though he had tried to hide it, the fact that he had joined the Ling Ying Sect could not be concealed.

After all, he had personally gone to the Ling Ying Sect's altar to burn incense.

"Well, since he's monitoring me, if I don't make a move, Chen Dabao also won't dare to kill me... without evidence, he won't make a move."

"What I need to do is just pass on the information, wait for the opportunity... with a Ling Ying, once it is delivered to the altar, my son's wish will definitely be fulfilled!"
