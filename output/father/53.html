The howling wind and the slanted rain poured down from the high sky, splattering against the earth below.

Washing over the mortal realm!

The entire Golden Pavilion seemed to be shrouded in a curtain of rain, its surfaces blooming with countless hazy outlines. The suspended pavilions, with their curtains fluttering wildly in the rushing airflow stirred up by the downpour, were like a swirling dance of twisted dragons.

Simo Bai's complexion was ashen, his face full of a look as if he were on the verge of tears, like an aggrieved young mistress who would burst into sobs at the slightest touch.

He had taken a seat in the suspended pavilion he had reserved in advance.

As one of the Three Young Masters of the Fei Lei Clan, he previously never needed to make reservations, but ever since he was mysteriously attacked by a cultivator and had his divine nature diminished, and the news spread that his elderly grandfather's divine foundation was on the verge of collapse...

Now, even coming to the Golden Pavilion required advance booking for him.

The charcoal stove boiled the water, the bubbles rolling up and down. He added the finest spring tea leaves to the pot, and the dry tea leaves instantly unfurled, their verdant essence blooming like flowers.

The rich tea aroma intertwined with the wind and steam flowing through the hall.

Simo Bai's eyes were downcast, even with a hint of ferocity. Guo Zhan had died... He had suffered a grave injury, his divine nature knocked down, plummeting from the realm of "Cultivating the Spirit like a River."

This filled him with both anger and sorrow...

But this was not the worst of it. As if the heavens were raining down on a leaky roof, his elderly grandfather, in order to pursue the culprit for him, had gone to the mysterious temple outside the city, only to suffer a devastating blow, his divine foundation teetering on the verge of collapse. If he did not use his divine foundation's power in this lifetime, he could still eke out a few more years of life, still dominating the Fei Lei Clan. But once he did, he would undoubtedly perish.

Worst of all, the news of his grandfather's damaged divine foundation had already spread within Fei Lei City.

The Yang, Xu, An families, the imperial court... one by one, they were eyeing the Sima Clan, which was about to lose its patriarch, like a juicy, fragrant roast.

"The divine cultivator who attacked me, the Fourth-Rank 'Wrathful Prince,' could not have come from Fei Lei City. Most likely, he was from the Divine Sect..."

"Calculating the days, in about a year, the Divine Sect will send people to recruit spirit children. But with the changes in the Spirit Infant Academy and the mysterious temple, the Divine Sect must have noticed something and sent people earlier than usual."

"Fei Lei City is, after all, just a small city. The [Grieving Demon-Subduing] technique I cultivate is the best divine art in Fei Lei City, but it's only at the Nine-Star level. Compared to the Fourth-Rank... the gap is too vast, far too vast..."

Simo Bai clenched his fist. Although he was furious, tears kept flowing uncontrollably from his eyes.

There was a time when he had forced Zhao Chuanxiong to be the bait, to lure in that Ox Demon.

But now, it was as if the wheel of karma had turned, and he too was being forced to be the bait...

At this moment, Simo Bai also began to understand Zhao Chuanxiong's feelings a little.

Being the bait... there was always the risk of being devoured.

That kind of suspending one's life on a hook, walking the line between life and death...

It truly tested one's mental state and courage.

Simo Bai forced himself to calm down, but when he picked up the exquisite tea that the beautiful courtesan had personally poured for him, his trembling fingers betrayed his inner turmoil.

The curtain rose and fell...

Rose and fell again.

But when it rose once more, he saw one figure after another, wearing masks, appear around the Golden Pavilion, their powerful divine auras bursting forth from their bodies.

Some had sword lights swirling, stirring up thoughts, some had towering fury like a Buddha, some had a terrifying presence like an armored deity.

They wore all kinds of masks, tigers, leopards, goats...

Perhaps because of the uproar caused by the Ox Demon's cute ox-faced mask.

Making those who lurked in the shadows of Fei Lei City and committed acts of violence now habitually wear masks.

Simo Bai looked at these people, his hand no longer trembling, but with a bitter smile.

"Xu Hele, An Ruosu, Yang Kaihe... Do you think just by wearing masks, I won't know who you are?"

"Your divine auras, even if you turn to ash, I would still recognize them. Wearing masks is nothing but self-deception."

"Since you dare to come and encroach on my Sima Clan, why not do it openly?"

Simo Bai said coldly.

However, no matter how he rebuked and questioned them, only his own voice echoed in the heavens and earth.

No one answered him.

Simo Bai's gaze flashed with a sharp edge, and the next moment, nine palm-sized Grieving Infant wooden figurines slid out from his sleeve pocket. With a flick of his hand, they floated in the air.

"Since you've come... then all of you, go to your deaths!"

And in the instant he flung out the nine Infant wooden figurines,

Simo Bai's white robes billowed as he plummeted downwards into the rain curtain, his divine aura bursting forth as he executed a divine secret art, his entire being seemingly melting into a tear sliding down a cheek, fleeing swiftly out of the Golden Pavilion!

Simo Bai now had only one thought: Escape! The bait must also save itself!
