Li Che, dressed in black attire, wearing a wide-brimmed hat, his expression extremely grave.

Accompanying him was his uncle's family.

The group trudged through the increasingly turbulent wind and snow, not even having time to be alarmed by the skirmish that had just broken out in the small courtyard. The family just wanted to flee to Xu's woodcarving shop.

Walking along the stone-paved street, due to the inclement weather, there were few passersby, most of them hurrying with their heads down.

The entire way, everyone remained silent, no one spoke.

Suddenly.

Li Che's steps paused, the accumulated snow pushed out into a small mound by the sudden stop of his feet.

Through the hazy wind and snow, there was a lingering scent of smoke.

The sound of gongs and drums, suona horns, and devout chanting came from the other end of the long street, forming an eerie symphony that made the hairs on one's neck stand on end.

"The holy spirit teaches, to wash away the mundane, the reincarnation without worry method, requires the highest wisdom to succeed without being taught..."

The mournful melody drifted over from the wind and snow.

There was a bare-chested burly man wearing a child's mask, carrying a sedan chair.

On the sedan chair was placed a three-headed, six-armed spirit infant statue, its three faces expressing anger, sorrow, and joy... its eyes lively, as if real eyeballs were embedded.

Marching in orderly formation, there were dozens of them, the congregation members processing, the spirit infant paraded through the streets! There were also women with graceful figures wearing child masks, carrying flower baskets, accompanying the music, scattering flower petals, vying with the wind and snow.

Li Che's brow furrowed, he instructed the uncle's family behind him not to look and to keep their heads down as they pressed on.

However, this time the spirit infant street procession of the congregation did not simply pass by Li Che as before, without incident.

In the blinding snowfall, these spirit infants saw the uncle's family, saw the nephew Liang Chouzhu in his aunt Liu Chunming's embrace, and seemed to have become excited.

They danced in strange steps, wearing children's masks, tilting their heads and twisting their bodies like raging hell demons, step by step approaching and drawing nearer.

An oppressive atmosphere permeated the heavens and earth, swept up in the wind and snow, assaulting them!

Uncle Li Liang was drenched in cold sweat, his legs trembling.

Cousin Li Zhengran's eyes were wide with alarm, gripping a chopping knife tightly.

The aunt and aunt Liu Chunming were terrified beyond measure, having never seen such a spectacle, almost on the verge of tears, tightly embracing the frightened Liang Chouzhu.

Li Che took a step forward, his blood surging, his skin flushing red, revealing his cultivated and perfected abilities.

"In broad daylight, what are you trying to do? Forcibly seize the child?!"

"Do you not consider the imperial court, the laws of the land?!"

Li Che roared with great force, momentarily cowing the congregation members.

But the gongs, drums, and suona horns sounded again, and these masked congregation members once more surrounded and drew closer, the greedy and eerie glow flickering in the children's masks.

Li Che clenched his fists.

He thought... they're wearing masks! These congregation members kept getting closer, and vaguely, there seemed to be a unique aura permeating, disrupting the spirit, affecting people's emotions!

Li Che's heart raced... spiritual energy? Not dense, only a trace, but for ordinary people, it was still extremely deadly!

Suddenly.

The wind and snow erupted, a figure rushed in from the distance, falling before Li Che and the uncle's family with the force of a cannonball.

Powerful blood energy surged, internal energy gushing out, kicking up great winds and waves, the gale-force airflow dispersing those approaching spirit infant procession congregation members.

"I am... the third shopkeeper of Xu's woodcarving shop, Xu You."

"Gentlemen, in broad daylight, this kind of intimidation is a bit excessive. Feiluo City... has not yet reached the time when the Spirit Infant Congregation can do as they please."

The figure in white fur cloak, snow-white clothing, with flowing black hair, spoke indifferently.

Li Che was taken aback, never expecting the arrival to be the long-unseen third shopkeeper Xu You, who had disappeared after going out of the city to hunt demonic beasts. Now he appeared in such a situation.

Xu You's blood energy burned like fire, his internal energy gushing wildly, standing there, his explosive power utterly terrifying.

Those spirit infant procession congregation members scattered in all directions, the dispersed spiritual energy also vanishing without a trace in the wind and snow.

No dialogue, no words.

The procession members, carrying the spirit sedan, stepping to the dance, scattering flower petals, accompanied by the gongs, drums, and suona horns, gradually faded away, disappearing into the wind and snow.

The evil aura also seemed to have retreated along with them.

The uncle's family finally let out a long sigh of relief, the aunt in particular feeling as if she had narrowly escaped disaster, unable to hold back her tears.

Li Che clasped his hands in gratitude: "Many thanks, Third Shopkeeper."

Xu You turned around, his face more weathered than before, the handsome scholarly features now bearing a scar.

He glanced at Li Che and smiled: "I had just returned from the city outskirts after dealing with the sinister temple's evil creatures, and then encountered this situation..."
