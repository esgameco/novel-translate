The struggle for power, father killing son. New69book.com

Everything did not know where it started from, perhaps it was from the moment when the child Anse was happily taken by his father Redley to set foot in the temple of God, and then saw his father looking at him with an extremely disappointed gaze.

It is also possible that when Yesaier awakened his innate talent, the fate of killing each other among flesh and blood had already been determined.

But at this moment, there is no need to dwell on it anymore.

This drama has finally fallen.

"Thump!"

Redley pulled out his scepter and threw it on the ground, the bone scepter making a dull sound.

In an instant he staggered, and the guards around him immediately panicked and surrounded him.

"Your Majesty!"

"The Wise King."

He forcefully leaned on the guards beside him to prevent himself from falling, after gasping for a few breaths, he regained his strength and waved to have Anse's corpse carried away.

Turning back.

Looking at the queen sitting on the ground with her mouth open but unable to cry out, Redley's eyes were full of apology.

For a mother to witness her own child die in front of her eyes is truly too cruel, and the one who killed her child is her own husband, the child's biological father.

"I'm sorry!"

"I'm really...very sorry."

Redley kept apologizing, even though he himself was not much better off.

He hugged the queen and left the ruins.

He walked with some difficulty, but his arms were holding her with all his might. New69book.com

He even bent down, which for the usual Redley would be a lack of kingly dignity, but at this moment he did not care.

"Let's go!"

"Let's go back!"

All the Trifoliate people inside and outside knelt on the ground, everyone could feel Redley's sadness, even though there was no expression on his face, nor did he show any sign of weakness.

The queen began to be unable to eat anything, not even able to drink water, and fell into a coma and unconsciousness.

"Anse."

"Where have you run off to? Why haven't you come home yet?"

"Did you go play in the sea again? I told you not to go."

"Bune."

"You and your older brother are being naughty again, I definitely won't let you off today."

In her dream, she kept calling out the words she said to her children when they were young, her expectations for the children shortly after they were born, her scolding of the naughty pranksters as they grew up.

At that time she was still young, with the children surrounding her, innocent and lovely.

Redley stayed by her side, listening to her say these words, holding her hand.

Until a few days later, the queen suddenly woke up and regained consciousness.

This was the last glimmer of life.

Redley also understood that she had reached the end of her life.

For a moment, Redley's face became extremely ugly, as if he was about to cry, but when the queen opened her eyes and looked at him, he forced a smile again. New69book.com

"You're awake."

The queen also felt that her days were numbered, her consciousness extremely clear, she looked at Redley, and then at his hand holding hers.

"No need to apologize, I don't blame you."

"I just...can't accept it..."

"Why did Anse become like this?"

"How did everything become like this?"

The queen's voice grew softer as she spoke: "When Yesaier returns, pass the throne to Yesaier!"

"You too..."
