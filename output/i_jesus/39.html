Sally held a flower pot in one hand and grasped a freshly caught Sun Cup in the other. It was very obvious that compared to the one in the flower pot, the newly caught Sun Cup was much more beautiful.

"Gurgle gurgle!"

Sally threw away the one from the flower pot and planted the newly caught one inside instead.

Her petite figure squeezed through the sea of flowers, under the dense Sun Cups.

Under the sunlight, the flower cups she passed kept swaying, like waves on the sea.

"Clack! Clack!"

She climbed up the steps of the pyramid.

Sally's human form was not tall, so the climbing process felt extra amusing. She had to lift her legs extremely high with each step, as if using all her strength, almost shouting heave-ho, with her body swaying left and right.

She arrived under the statue, placing the flower pot on the altar as usual, as if offering a sacrifice to the deity.

Year after year, day after day, she did this every single day.

She was about to climb onto the altar herself and sit beside the statue when suddenly, a starry light bloomed from above and a faint voice came from overhead.

"Beautiful flowers."

Sally looked up. The statue emanated ribbons of light, and a shadowy figure enveloped in radiance slowly emerged from within.

The deity had awoken at last.

Sally didn't climb up anymore, letting go of her hands and standing still. She leaned forward, hands behind her back, eyes wide open.

"Gurgle gurgle!"

Indeed, she looked just like a fish blowing bubbles.

This made the radiant figure who had stepped down from the altar laugh, patting Sally's shoulder, then gazing at the Sun Cup.

The newly born intelligent consciousness within the Sun Cup, unable to comprehend the warmth of this moment, was about to be scared out of its wits.

It had just escaped from the clutches of the Demon God, only to appear before another terrifying existence beyond its ability to perceive.

It had never encountered such a horrifying being, beyond its ability to even imagine.

What its consciousness sensed was a towering celestial star standing at the end of time and outside the universe, its dazzling radiance indescribable, as if spanning an unimaginable distance to traverse here from realms far beyond the Earth.

Sally ran ahead of Yin Shen and arrived at the seaside. Yin Shen's figure followed her leisurely through the bushes.

Sally stood by the sea, turning her large green eyes towards Yin Shen, waiting for him to come over.

When Yin Shen came before her, she turned into a beam of light and leapt into the vast ocean.

"Boom!"

Huge waves surged up.

The lovely little girl Sally had just been instantly revealed her true form.

It was a 70-80 meter tall giant monster, its large and small horrifying green eyes rolling, countless tentacles stretching out from the sea and waving in the sky.

The monster carried Yin Shen away from the God-blessed land, just as it had brought Yin Shen here long ago.

"Go to the ocean trench over there."

Above the vast sea, Yin Shen's gaze penetrated through the trench to see the Crown of Wisdom in the abyss below.

With a thought, the crown began floating up from the dark, cold depths of the sea.

Eventually it broke through the surface, hovering before Yin Shen.

The Crown of Wisdom gave off a faint glow, accompanied by a soft ringing sound.

It seemed the departed Laidley was communicating with Yin Shen in this way.

Yin Shen shook his head helplessly, "Laidley."

"You promised to let go, so let go completely!"

"There's no need to restrain your descendants anymore. Let it all end here."

"End it!"

The Crown of Wisdom glowed faintly, drifting slowly towards Yin Shen. It seemed to want to be worn on his head, but ultimately shrank back in fear, unable to approach.

It seemed to feel itself unworthy of being worn by a god.

In the end, it shrank into a small black ring on Yin Shen's right ring finger.

Yin Shen raised his hand, standing in the middle of the ocean, using the power of the Crown of Wisdom to call out to the entire world.

Rays of light pierced through space from the ends of the universe, gathering above this ocean like a starry river surrounding the deity.

The scattered threads of wisdom, power, and mythical factors, the power dissipated when the royal clan and Trifoliums died - all returned at the summons of the Crown of Wisdom.

Finally, they all fell into the Crown of Wisdom.

The originally black Crown of Wisdom took on a dark golden hue, imbued with a breath of immortality.

As Yin Shen lowered his raised hand, the radiant light enveloped the black ring, leaving no trace visible.
