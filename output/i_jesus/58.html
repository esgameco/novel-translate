"Shall we not proceed further?" Star looked at Polo.

"No need to go further. We've traveled so far, we should take a rest," Polo turned and gently said to Star.

Polo looked at Star, whose once beautiful white bone armor had become rough and tarnished, and the starry radiance that once covered her had also dimmed.

Star had aged, while Polo remained largely unchanged from before.

Both Polo and Star knew that this journey of theirs would eventually reach its end.

Star smiled: "Then let's stop here!"

They found a beautiful sandy beach by the sea and built a house with a courtyard.

The courtyard was covered by a golden dome, woven from the petals of sun cups, brilliant and beautiful.

Polo loved flowers, and Star would sit in the courtyard every day, gazing out at the sea through the window.
