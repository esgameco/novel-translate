At dawn, the first rays of the sun were just rising on the horizon.

A figure emerged from the beach, crawling out in a disheveled state. He tightly gripped a set of bone scriptures in his hand, his gaze fixed on the distant town of Tito.

"It can't be."

"They're all dead."

A group had traveled thousands of miles to carry out this mission, but in the end, he was the only one who made it out alive.

Although the Trileaf people could survive underwater, those people had all been crushed by the collapsing ground or trapped inside, and there was no way they could have escaped.

Even he had narrowly escaped from within, despite being a high-ranking priest. He could not withstand the force of the earth's collapse.

His palm caressed the words on the bone scriptures, and the tense, oppressive mood instantly relaxed.

"Fortunately."

"The bone scriptures were brought out."

No matter how many people died, as long as he could bring out this object, it would all be worth it.

He quickly ran with the bone scriptures, as if someone was waiting for him in the distance.

However, halfway through, he suddenly stopped.

His gaze was somewhat complex, but it quickly stabilized.

In that brief moment, he made a certain decision.

He took out the bone tablets and quickly flipped through the content.

There was not much text on the bone tablets, and he soon finished reading them. He finally learned the secret left behind by the great poet Tito.

His face showed a look of sudden enlightenment, yet also a sense of shock.

"So!"

"The secret left by the great poet is this, there really is a Dream Fairy that can fulfill any wish."

Untying the rope that bound the bone scriptures, he removed the last bone tablet and then re-tied the bone scriptures.

The leader made his way through the coast and came to a stretch of reef.

Behind the reef stood a tall, three-leaf male, his bone armor pure and flawless, with a Ruhei brand on his forehead like an eye.

This clearly indicated that the other party was of extraordinary status, if not a king, then at least a prince, otherwise he would not have the ability to control the Ruhei giant.

The leader knelt humbly before him and presented the bone scriptures.

"Your Highness! This is the bone scriptures left behind by the great poet Tito!"

"For two hundred years, it has been sleeping under the castle of the Tito family, and now it has finally reappeared in the world."

"And now, it belongs to our Volcano Kingdom."

The prince eagerly took the bone scriptures and, after a glance, excitedly said:

"The descendants of the Tito family were not wrong, the great poet really left behind the final chapter."

"Hahaha!"

"The secret that the Star Realm Kingdom could not keep, the thing they could not obtain, is now ours, the Volcano Kingdom."

The four major royal bloodline families - Silen, Samo, Seler, and Hosen - represent the meanings of starlight, desert, ocean, and volcano, and are the beautiful blessings and wishes of King Laideli for his children.

They established the Star Realm Kingdom, the Samo Kingdom, the Seler Kingdom, and the Volcano Kingdom respectively.

And these people who pretended to be the Samo Kingdom's caravan were actually soldiers of the Hosen family of the Volcano Kingdom.

Especially the leader, who was a priest of the royal lineage.

The prince of the Volcano Kingdom asked him, "Why did you make such a commotion? Didn't I tell you to be careful?"

"Where are the others?"

The leader replied, "They're all dead."

The prince nodded, "Although it's not perfect, the fact that you were able to obtain the bone scriptures left by the great poet is already good enough!"

"You have completed your mission, and upon your return, glory and rewards will surely await you."

As the prince said this, he put away the bone scriptures. But at the same time, the bone whip in his hand suddenly lashed out unexpectedly towards the caravan leader.

The caravan leader reacted extremely quickly, deflecting the bone whip with his Ruhei sword.

Turning the sword, he swung out a sword flower to knock away the three bone spears that were stabbing towards him from behind.

His superb combat skills and early precautions showed that he was no ordinary person.
