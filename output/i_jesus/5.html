Yin Shenshou placed his hand on the giant eye of the fusion monster, as if adhering to an enormous cold crystal. His will extended through the pupil, and he commanded in an imperative tone:

"Shed your shell for me!"

One tentacle after another extended from beneath the coiled abdomen, gradually lifting the shell off its back.

The enormous shell was even larger than the temple, placed on the steps in front of it.

The fusion monster crouched beneath, as if offering a gift to a deity.

Yin Shenshou originally just wanted to touch this shell, but he found that after his hand made contact, the shell actually began floating up.

With a shift of his thoughts, the shell floated higher and higher.

He could control this thing.

Yin Shenshou felt like he had found a great toy, this was the first time he could touch and directly manipulate something, as if it were a part of his own body, going wherever he willed it.

Yin Shenshou tried moving other things but found it had no effect.

The only thing he could move was this shell.

Finally, following his thoughts, the huge shell continuously shrank.

When he held it in his hand, this shell was like a horn.

As if blowing it would play the melody of life.

This truly was...

A miracle.

Yin Shenshou gained an initial understanding of these mythical creations: "They can be controlled from a distance and change size."

"Things imbued with my power are no longer bound by scientific laws," Yin Shenshou guessed.

The power of the Trifolians was not just this, there should be even more undiscovered abilities and uses.

But how to use his own power to imbue other things, giving them mythical power, Yin Shenshou still did not understand the key.

He was imprisoned in the crevice of space-time, only able to anchor himself and this universe through the Trifolian Leadley and the fusion monster. His preliminary plan was to create more Trifolians, to see if he could form more anchors.

If he could regain a physical body, that would be even better.

Living like a stray ghost was truly too terrifying.

Yin Shenshou held the shell and stood up.

He saw the fusion monster watching him. Without its shell, the fusion monster looked even more terrifying.

Yin Shenshou looked at the fusion monster: "Since you can change your form, transform into a shape that looks more comfortable!"

"This appearance is quite frightening too."
