"The child chosen by the gods."

The believers of the gods chased after and beside the Ruhui giant monster, either embracing their chests or singing the Redley Covenant loudly.

Like a group of madmen.

They cheered and rejoiced, they shouted with all their might.

The power of faith is so sacred, yet can be so ridiculous.

Just a few days ago, Xing was a sinner, but now she has transformed into the chosen one of the gods.

She received everyone in the palace and became the new master of this God-Descended City.

Then those people looked at Bolo beside Xing with fear, that terrifying, indescribable mythical life, the messenger of the gods.

"Xing!"

"You stay with them, I'll go take a look inside the palace."

"I've seen the palace of the Redley King, but I haven't seen the palace here yet."

Bolo liked liveliness and new things.

Yet he disliked these tedious rituals, feeling that these cumbersome rituals and the scheming for power were like shackles that bound people, making them feel heavy all over.

Bolo didn't even bother with the flattery from the nobles of the Xince Kingdom and turned to leave.

The noble families of the God-Descended City looked at each other, their hearts filled with trepidation.

They didn't understand where they had offended the messenger of the gods, but they also didn't dare...

The sky was clear, without a cloud for miles.

In front of the royal palace of the Divine Descent City, the people of the Xin Kingdom had gathered to witness the ceremony. They were blocked by a line of soldiers at the bottom of the steps, gazing up at the high palace. Even though the person they were anticipating had not yet appeared, they were already excitedly boiling with passion.

"Rumble!"

Wearing a crown, Xing walked out from inside the palace, step by step coming to the front of the stairs, allowing everyone to see her.

One by one, the royal bloodline families came out, followed by the nobles of the Divine Descent City and the lords of various cities and towns.

"The great king of Xin."

"Xing!"

"We will forever be subjects under your scepter and crown, obeying the will bestowed upon you by the gods. May you lead Xin towards an even farther and brighter future."

"For the celebration of the queen's birth."

One after another, phantasmal fireworks shot up into the sky from behind and exploded.

At the bottom of the steps in front of the palace, row after row of Trifolians knelt down.

Like waves extending into the distance.

"The queen!"

"The child chosen by the gods."

"..."
