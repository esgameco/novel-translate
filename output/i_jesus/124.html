Sander decided to leave his hometown.

He wanted to go see the City of Anhol, to see the legendary Sky Temple and the Holy Mountain.

Perhaps then, he would know what to do.

He came to the small hut where Stan Tito once lived, wanting to bid farewell to the Master.

But he didn't expect to see a group of people searching for something here, led by a priest, with a minor noble following behind.

"It's him."

"My lord."

"This slave used to come here often, he must have taken the things from the hut."

His former master, the lord of the fishing village, recognized him.

Sander felt uneasy and immediately turned to leave.

But the other side had no intention of letting him go, and hurriedly chased after him with a group of people.

Sander swung a stone hammer to fight back, his quick reflexes from the power of wisdom allowed him to quickly knock down the other three men. The priest, although only a junior priest, had obviously stronger combat skills than him.

It wasn't long before Sander was forced to roll on the ground to dodge, and the things in his backpack spilled out.

And the most conspicuous among them, of course, was the Sun Flower with its petals curled up.

Instantly, the movements of everyone present stopped, and the minor noble pointed at the object, his hands trembling.

"The Forbidden Flower."
