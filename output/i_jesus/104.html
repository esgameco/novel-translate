The army of the Samo Kingdom was slow to react. By the time they had finally gathered their main forces to attack the Kingdom of Xinglu, the latter had already advanced into the territory of the Kingdom of Celuo.

But this time was different from before. The royal bloodline of the Samo family was evidently anxious, launching an all-out offensive without sparing any cost. They quickly breached and captured the fortified Stone Forest City.

Within a few days, they had broken through and reached the foot of the Sacred Mountain.

According to the plan of the Queen of Xinglu, the Sky Temple had long prepared the Death Star to staunchly defend this place.

The natural barrier of the Sacred Lake and the geographical advantage of the Sacred Mountain were enough to hold them off for a day or two.

However, two days before the Samo Kingdom's army arrived, an unexpected incident occurred.

The Death Star suddenly surged out from the Sacred Lake, rampaging and destroying the fishing grounds within, then crawling onto land and moving away in the distance. No matter how they called out, it did not turn back.

The entire City of the Divine Servants and the Sky Temple were in an uproar.

"How could this happen?"

"How could the Death Star suddenly lose control?"

"It's over, it's over."

The people of the City of the Divine Servants were thoroughly panicked. Without the Death Star, how could they possibly defend this place?

As expected.

As soon as the Samo Kingdom's main army arrived, the desert worms on the frontline immediately launched an attack and breached the city gates. A long line of people began fleeing from the back roads.

At the foot of the Sacred Mountain, there were refugees everywhere.

They had abandoned the Sky Temple and the City of the Divine Servants.

If it weren't for the fear of damaging the Sky Temple, the Samo Kingdom would have been able to occupy the city even faster.

At this moment, huge waves began to rise on the surface of the Sacred Lake in the distance.

A terrifying giant beast emerged from the water, its iron-clad body crashing onto the ground and carving a thousand-meter-long trench, kicking up a violent cloud of dust.

With just a single strike, it brought a catastrophic defeat to the army of the Kingdom of Celuo at the foot of the mountain.

"Hiss!"

A black magic fern suddenly grew out of the ground, transforming into a hundred-meter-tall giant plant in the blink of an eye, entwining the Celuo sea monster.

This was another giant monster of the Samo family - the Moon Magic Fern, the only Ruhei giant monster that had evolved along the plant evolutionary path.

"Woo woo!"

A rumbling sound came from deep beneath the Sacred Mountain, and a terrifying giant insect burrowed out, engaging in battle with the desert worms of the Samo family.

The burrowing magic insect and the Celuo sea monster arrived just in time, helping to turn the tide of the battle.

Smoke and dust rose in the distance, and the main armies of the Kingdom of Xinglu and the Volcanic Duchy were also rushing back.

Heniel stood atop the head of the burrowing magic insect, raising his sword and pointing at the controller of the desert worms.

People change with the situation and the passage of time, and Heniel's transformation, having grasped power, was earth-shattering. His confidence and the aura surrounding him had swelled to an unbelievable degree.

"King of the Samo family."

"You either destroy the temple of the divine, and then engage in the final battle here."

"Do you dare?"

"Or quietly leave this place and end this war."

The king of the Samo family did not become angry at Heniel's provocation, but calmly asked, "Where is the King of the Celuo family?"

Heniel did not tell the truth: "Her Majesty the Queen is integrating the Kingdom of Celuo and will return with the sky giant beast at any moment."

However, the king of the Samo Kingdom said, "But the information I received is that the Queen of Xinglu is dead."

Heniel laughed loudly, "The Celuo family dares to say such nonsense just to lure you in."

"On what basis?"

"What power do they have to harm the great Queen of Xinglu?"

The king of the Samo Kingdom hesitated, also doubting the authenticity of the news. How could the weakest Kingdom of Celuo, relying on just one giant monster, have the power to kill the mighty Queen of Xinglu?

The situation was at an impasse.

The Samo family did not dare to destroy the temple of the divine and the City of the Divine Servants, and staying here to wear them down was also not a long-term plan, as this was the heartland of the Kingdom of Xinglu - it would ultimately benefit the enemy.

The Samo Kingdom felt they had no opportunity and no determination to engage in the final battle at the foot of the Sacred Mountain.

After hesitating for a while, they ultimately retreated and withdrew.

Seeing the Samo Kingdom retreat, the entire City of the Divine Servants erupted in cheers.

"Her Majesty the Queen has returned!"

"Her Majesty the Queen has returned."

"The Samo Kingdom has retreated, we have won, we have won."

Inside the temple and throughout the City of the Divine Servants, everyone was overjoyed, flooding the streets to celebrate.

Even the fleeing nobles and soldiers had hurried back, joyfully welcoming the return of the Queen.
