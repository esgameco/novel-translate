The premeditated ones came for the final chapter left behind by the great poet.

The patriarch of the Tito clan was stepped on the palm of his hand, letting out agonizing cries.

"I really don't know!"

"The Tito clan has never had these things, I've never heard of these things before."

The caravan leader even used mind-reading, but found that the patriarch of the Tito clan truly knew nothing.

Seeing that he couldn't dig out anything, he looked at the other party with great disappointment.

"After two hundred years, not only have you forgotten the glory of your ancestors, but even the inheritance and secrets of the past have been almost completely severed."

"A group of fallen fat worms, you are simply desecrating the holy name of the great poet."

The patriarch of the Tito clan saw the other party raise the Ruhei sword high, and he let out a shrill voice.

"You killed the descendants of the great poet, the glorious and brilliant patriarch of the Tito clan."

"Retribution will surely come."

"The Star Realm Kingdom will not let you off, and Her Majesty the Queen will not let you off either."

The other party sneered and said, "Of course the great poet Tito is inviolable and sacrosanct, but his glory belongs to him alone."

"You foolish insects who shamelessly call yourselves the descendants of the saints, daring to call yourselves glorious and brilliant."

"What are you?"

"With the royal bloodline, you call yourselves the descendants of the saints, but for a hundred years, you haven't even produced a single priest."

"Decaying and falling to this state, do you still deserve to be called the name of the great poet Tito?"

He said, and then thrust a sword along the seam of the armor into the other party's body.

The fat worm twitched a few times and then stopped moving.

From the beginning, the caravan leader had no intention of leaving any survivors.

He despised these fat worms who had desecrated the holy name of the great poet, but he knew how great the influence of the Tito clan's reputation was.

Although through generation after generation of being excluded and suppressed by the Sky Temple and the royal bloodline families, the Tito clan had long since declined and decayed, but just hearing their title of the descendants of the saints, he knew how troublesome it would be.

If he left behind any aftermath, it would be a dead end for him.

Even the person behind him dared not guarantee his safety.

"Search!"

"In the records left behind by the three sons of the great poet Tito, the great poet had his eldest son come here to find the hiding place of the Star Queen and the divine messenger Baro before he ascended to the divine realm, and then hid all his secrets there."

"That place must be here."

The caravan leader led his men to search everywhere, and finally discovered something unusual in front of a wall in the castle's underground warehouse.

"Found it."

There was a pattern of stars carved on the wall, not elaborate but not ordinary either.

The other people accompanying him felt and tapped the wall.

"There's nothing here?"

The caravan leader said, "It's an illusion cast by the use of the Sun Cup!"

The caravan leader stretched out his hand and made a grasping motion towards the wall.

The originally high wall began to twist and rotate, opening up a passage.

"Rumble!"

Dust rose, and the stone slabs on the ground collapsed one by one.

The passage led directly to the depths of the underground.

As they walked deeper into the passage, a faint golden glow gradually appeared in the darkness ahead.

"What is this?"

After approaching, they found that the underground was filled with Sun Cups.

The Sun Cups had bloomed, with dreamlike flowers swaying and their pollen floating in the space, emitting a bioluminescent glow.

Looking at it, it was like falling into a dreamscape.

"Sun Cups!"

"There are so many Sun Cups here."

"The Tito clan's underground has a sea of Sun Cups."

They were excited, but they all suppressed it.

Any one of these Sun Cups would be priceless to ordinary three-leaf people or even nobles.

But with their clear purpose, they knew that compared to what they had come to find, the term "priceless" was already insignificant.

The treasure hidden deep in the flower sea, the precious legacy left behind by the great poet Tito.

That was the thing that the royal bloodline families were all eagerly coveting and drooling over.

Because...

It was the supreme object related to the divine.
