The Volcano King, who commanded the Lava Monsters, paused for a moment. Hearing that tone, he finally recalled who stood before him.

"It's you, Heniel!"

"How did you get the Burrowing Worms? And where is Weiss?"

Heniel's voice was calm. "What do you think?"

Heniel laughed. "Of course, it's because..."

"I want you dead!"

The King was practically going mad. This lowly bastard, this commoner born of black mud. How dare he, how dare he.

"You madman!"

"Because of you, the Hosen Clan lost the capital, lost hundreds of their kin."

"The Volcano Kingdom lost the foundation it had built up over hundreds of years. Do you know that?"

Heniel did not speak, responding only with mocking laughter. The more the King lost his composure, the more Heniel wanted to see.

"Hahaha!"

The great battle atop the volcano was about to erupt. With a command, the Volcano King made the Lava Monster spew scorching hot red lava.

The lava swept across the ground, but the Burrowing Worms immediately burrowed underground to avoid it. However, as soon as the Lava Monster tried to leave the volcano, the Worms would surge out to block it, their fangs and tentacles latching on.

The Lava Monster possessed power far exceeding the Burrowing Worms, once the greatest deterrent of the Volcano Kingdom. But now, faced with the Worms' abilities, it could not unleash that power.

Heniel controlled the Burrowing Worms, trapping the King and the Lava Monster on the path down the mountain. The King grew increasingly anxious, while Heniel became more and more at ease.

"Hum hum hum~"

Another volcanic eruption was coming, a full-scale one this time.

Lava surged out like tidal waves, cascading down from the heights in towering, dozens-of-meter-high waves. Thick smoke rose hundreds of meters, tinged with scarlet.

The towering lava flows rolled down, engulfing the entire mountain peak, closing in on the battling Burrowing Worms and Lava Monster.

However, the Burrowing Worms burrowed underground at the last moment.

The Lava Monster was not so fortunate. The terrifying lava consumed it, its monstrous screams echoing for dozens of miles.

The Volcano King watched the lava pouring down from above, and the objects being swept away in the billowing smoke.

He let out a final roar: "Heniel!"

The name he had once given to that bastard child, "Black Mud."

He never imagined that one day, this "Black Mud" would become the terrifying black swamp that would completely devour the Volcano Kingdom and the Hosen Clan.

In the distance, Heniel, controlling through spiritual projection, smiled.

"Ah!"

"It's all gone now," he said, gazing at the destruction, the fire, and the lava engulfing the capital.

With this single act, he had killed nearly all the rulers of the Volcano Kingdom. All those who could oppose him, those who looked down on him from above, had perished here.

This was exactly what Heniel wanted.

The great armies of Samu Kingdom and their Monsters were being held back at the Volcano Kingdom's borders by the Star Realm Kingdom, engaged in an even more intense slaughter and battle there. Four Luhe Monsters and tens of thousands of soldiers clashed, with the Star Realm Queen and Samu King personally leading the fight.

Samu Kingdom had received the news and wanted to rush in to rescue the Hosen Clan, but the Star Realm Kingdom naturally would not let them have their way.

They just never expected everything here to end so quickly.

"Lord Heniel," a line of priests and nobles stood behind Heniel. These priests all had royal bloodlines, but were sidelined members or bastards, whom Heniel had been cultivating for a long time.

They had once been frustrated and unfulfilled, but now followed Heniel in his rise.

And these nobles were local lords and aristocrats - they saw in Heniel the opportunity to enter the core of power.

In their eyes, Heniel saw excitement, ambition, greed, and desire.

"Desire is not shameful. Wanting more is not wrong," he said.

"From today on, everything here belongs to us. I will share power and glory with you all."

Everyone knelt before Heniel, chanting his name.

But at that moment, there was movement from the volcano again.

A savage, hollow sound echoed through the sky - the roar of a Monster.

The Lava Monster, consumed by the volcano, had revived. Its insect-like carapace gradually shed, and the Luhe Monster regained its true form.

It crawled across the land, heading towards the distant sea.

Without the Luhe mark, no Three-Leaf person could bind and control it anymore.

Heniel recalled the divine messenger he had encountered, and the words she had spoken:

"The gods will reclaim the power of destruction they bestowed upon the Three-Leaf people."
