Yin Shen walked through the Sea of Sun Cups, where thousands upon thousands of golden flower cups were blooming together, releasing golden pollen.

"Whew!"

The wind blew across the golden island, and the pollen gathered into a whirlpool.

This should have been a scene like a fantasy fairy tale, accompanied by an intoxicating floral fragrance that captivated people.

But Yin Shen could not smell the floral fragrance, nor could he feel the wind brushing past him.

The gene fragments within the Sun Cups were devoured by the mythical blood of the Crown of Wisdom, and the Crown of Wisdom, accompanied by the mythical blood of Leadley, also began to change. It transformed from the initial mythical blood into a more perfect form, and the power of the Wisdom Authority also underwent a metamorphosis.

Unlike the initial Wisdom Authority, which only had an effect on consciousness and thinking, the Wisdom Authority was now completely manifested and became a divine power that could directly interfere with the external world.

With a thought from Yin Shen, the City of Divine Grace, which was submerged beneath the sea of flowers, began to move.

"Rumble!"

Yin Shen raised his hand with the ring, and he could clearly feel that the entire City of Divine Grace was grasped in his palm.

It seemed as if he only needed to exert a little force, and he could lift up the entire City of Divine Grace.

A smile appeared on his face: "It's this powerful?"

His surprise was not only due to the strength of this power but also because he could now directly interface with reality through this power.

Although he still lacked a physical body, after gaining this power, he could finally directly interfere with reality.

Yin Shen walked through the ruined city, strolled across the sea of flowers, and stood atop the valley.

He no longer needed to rely on the power of others to grasp other things in this world.

However, while mastering this power, Yin Shen also felt that this power was still far from perfect.

Not that it wasn't powerful enough, but it lacked a sense of refinement and qualitative change.

It was like comparing a water wheel powered by water to a steam engine or an internal combustion engine, not to mention electric or nuclear power.

"It should be able to become even more powerful and more miraculous."
