Behind the palace, there was a spacious and magnificent palace room. This was originally the place where the sculptures of King Yeser, the King of Wisdom, were displayed. Yeser inherited his father's hobby of sculpting.

At this moment, this magnificent palace room had been completely emptied and turned into an indoor garden.

The people of the City of God's Descent had found the most fertile soil from the bottom of the sea and laid it on the ground. The Cup of the Sun would also be transplanted here.

"Be careful!"

"This is a gift from God. If there is any mistake, it would be blasphemy against the divine. We cannot afford any mistakes."

Shlode's voice came from outside, and a team of Trifolians carried in a rectangular planter.

The temple had not yet begun construction, and Shlode, the high priest, was only a nominal title.

His most important task at the moment was to nurture the Cup of the Sun.

If the divine object bestowed by God died in his hands, let alone the position of high priest, he would surely have to bear the wrath of King Yeser, the King of Wisdom.

Shlode carefully transplanted the Cup of the Sun into the soil.

The other priests following Shlode asked, "Will it come back to life?"

Shlode believed firmly, "Since God has bestowed the Cup of the Sun upon us, He will surely allow it to bloom here."

It was not known whether it was Shlode's words that came true, or whether the Cup of the Sun's vitality was simply strong from the beginning.

The broken Cup of the Sun regained its powerful vigor in the soil. This divine flower not only came back to life but also produced two offshoots on the side.

They...
