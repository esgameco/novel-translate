The Palace of Wisdom had undergone reconstruction time and again. Now it had become not just a castle over twenty meters tall, but at the very top was also equipped with a lighthouse-like structure.

Inside the tower was a type of stone discovered near an undersea volcano that would radiate a brilliant glow at night, allowing the Trifolians in the ocean to find their way home.

The sun rose.

In the dawn, Leadriki walked alone up the spiraling stone steps of the castle, step by step from the lowest level up to the very top lighthouse of the Divine City.

He looked down on all his creations with a satisfied smile on his face.

He spread his arms and in an old, parched voice, spoke the most vigorous words of his youth:

"I am Leadriki."

"The Wisdom King of all living beings."

Power burst forth, light surging.

All the Trifolians who had come out in the morning saw an unusual sight.

Countless rays of light scattered out from the lighthouse, replacing the stars and covering the sky of the Divine City.

Finally.

All the rays of light fell back into Leadriki's body, merging into the symbolic crown on his head.

"Wisdom is the most powerful myth in the world."

"The divine has bestowed wisdom upon me."

"Today I shall imbue this crown with wisdom, so that all who wear this crown may bask in divine glory, shepherding living beings with wisdom on behalf of the divine."

Leadriki's originally dark gray crown gradually turned pure black.

Then.

It fell off.

In an instant, Leadriki seemed to have aged even more severely.

He cradled the crown, a smile on his face.

"The Crown of Wisdom."

He was fading away, but his will and ideals would be passed down through this crown.

This supreme divine instrument of the path of wisdom was thus born. It would rule over all Trifolians generation after generation, and then countless future civilizations and intelligent races as well.
