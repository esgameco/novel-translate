Heneir gripped the scepter of Xin Sai, and he suddenly recalled the scene he had just witnessed of Stan Tito.

At that time, he was still just a small figure who had risen up by seizing the Ruhei Giant, a bastard child who was despised and scorned by others.

Due to the tense war and political situation, he gained the favor of the Queen of the Stars, and in the palace, he received the Queen's enfeoffment, becoming a nominal Duke of the Volcano Kingdom.

Because at that time, the Volcano Kingdom still belonged to the Hosen family.

Stan Tito stood among the crowd, just as out of place as Heneir, both of them disliked by the people.

At that time, Heneir immediately noticed this legendary lucky son.

Was it because he lacked the aura of nobility?

No.

It was because he was simple and pure.

He called himself a marionette, not knowing what he wanted, but he was a very pure person.

He could only simply distinguish right from wrong, knowing that he should repay the Queen's grace, and that he should stand up for his friends in times of trouble.

However.

Such a person should not have appeared at the noble banquet.

What did he say to him back then?

He remembered.

"When the day comes when no one calls you the lucky son anymore, you will no longer be a marionette."

"At that time."

"You will become a truly powerful person, worthy of being worshipped and feared by all."

Heneir laughed: "Unexpectedly, you have become such a person."

He reminisced and laughed, then turned and walked towards the Sky Temple.

From this moment on, he was the King of Xin Sai.

But this throne was not as lofty as he had imagined, allowing him to act recklessly.

Without the Ruhei Giants, in the era when the bloodline monarchy was terminated, anyone could become the king.

Just as Stan Tito had said, if he could not do a good job as the king, others would come to do it.

He had just stepped up the stairs and looked up to see the statue of the Xin Sai deity in the temple.

Suddenly, the sky burst forth with dazzling light, rotating and projecting downwards.

In the sky above the sacred lake, a vast dreamlike starlight poured out from another realm, forming a vortex in the sea of clouds.

A huge door rose up, accompanied by dreamlike colorful light.

"What is that?"

"The gate to the divine realm?"

Heneir looked puzzled, but also pensive.

He did not expect that at this moment, the gate to the divine realm would open.

But he also looked towards the distant direction of the sea, vaguely guessing something.

In front of the gate of the divine realm, as the light gradually faded, a divine messenger in golden robes appeared under the giant door.

She stood silently, her gaze towards the mortal world.

With a wave of the divine messenger's hand, a beam of light from the divine realm shone upon the mortal world, enveloping the dissolving figure of Stan Tito.

Stan Tito's figure re-solidified within the light, looking up at the gate to the divine realm that had opened for him.

The ethereal voice of the divine messenger came from the other world, echoing in the realm of Xin Sai.

"Stan Tito."

"Congratulations, you have passed the test, and you will receive the grace you desire."

The wind lifted the golden hair of the divine messenger, and the divine dreams painted on her robe transformed into bubble projections of various scenes, such as snowy nights, forests, and weaving kingdoms.

Seeing her, it was as if one was seeing beauty itself, involuntarily evoking a longing in the heart.

"And from now on,"

"the gate to the divine realm will be open to you."

"Every Trilobe person and Abyss Dweller who is about to die will embark on a pilgrimage and enter the eternal dreamlike starry sea."

"Along with their memories, they will forever return toâ€”"

"the divine realm."

After saying this, the giant gate behind the divine messenger thunderously opened, revealing the world of the divine realm.

The vast and endless dreamlike starry sea, with the sun-like divine cup and the dream-like moon formed by the wisdom crown at the highest point of the starry sea.

And beneath the starry sea, the legendary land bestowed by the gods.
