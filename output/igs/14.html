Ge Xiaolun: "Uuru Metal Great Sword!"

Ge Xiaolun was delighted to hear this, it was a precious treasure.

Indeed, he had advanced from a planetary level to a stellar level, so it was time to change his weapon. The Void Greatsword awakened with the power of the Divine River was just a semi-finished product. In his recent battles with the Heavenly Scum, if he hadn't been careful, the Void Greatsword would have been damaged.

Ge Xiaolun: "I'll just go get it, I wouldn't dare let Brother Thanos spend money."

Ge Xiaolun had cleared the 15th floor of the Bowing Dragon Tower, and after purchasing a batch of standard equipment, he still had a lot of points left.

1,000 points for a Crossing Token, he could afford that.

Long Ming: "Xiaolun, I'll go to the Marvel world with you."

Long Ming's words surprised both Ge Xiaolun and Thanos. Over the past year, the three of them had been frequently exchanging items. Buying a Crossing Token for 1,000 points to go to a friend's world, exchange items, and then return - this was a way to bypass the group's transaction fees.

After all, directly trading in the chat group would incur service charges.

This time, the group leader wanted to go to the Marvel world, the two were curious about his purpose.

Thanos: "I eagerly await the arrival of the group leader!"

Ge Xiaolun: "Group leader, you're going to Marvel? Come to think of it, I'm the only one in the group who hasn't met the group leader in person, this time is a coincidence."

Ge Xiaolun: "Big shot, what are you going to do in the Marvel world this time?"

Driven by curiosity, Ge Xiaolun couldn't help but ask.

Long Ming: "Oh, I'm going to see Thanos."

Long Ming: "@Thanos, I'll borrow your Infinity Stones to study them a bit, don't worry, it won't affect your snap."

Thanos: "No problem."

Thanos readily agreed. Seeing the group leader in person would allow him to learn from him again.

A year ago in the training arena, although the group leader only guided him for a dozen minutes, those precious minutes helped him find his future path and no longer rely on the Infinity Stones.

A true teaching is worth more than a thousand books, as the saying goes.

After a year of arduous cultivation and exploring secret realms for treasures, his strength had progressed at an astonishing speed.

With his current power, he was confident that he could personally wipe out half the life in the universe. This was also the reason he had not continued searching for the Infinity Stones this past year.

Just now, the group leader mentioned the snap.

Gathering all six Infinity Stones, a single snap should be able to erase half of all life, huh.

This method was brilliant, perfectly aligning with his previous ideas.

Indeed, he must collect all six Infinity Stones first, doing it himself would be too slow, too slow.

Thinking of this, Thanos said in the group that he would go prepare, then his avatar went offline.

On the Sanctuary II spaceship, Thanos closed the chat group interface and suddenly stood up, his battle armor rustling.

Looking at the Black Order who had returned empty-handed half a year ago, he issued an order, "Corvus Glaive, you and Superstar go to Midgard one more time, bring back the last two Infinity Stones. This time, you are only allowed to succeed, no failures allowed."

"Understood?"
