Traversing the endless chaotic sea, the distant Douluo Star.

Divine Realm, the Palace of the Divine King.

Divine King Tang San had just finished handling the affairs of the next term, sitting on the Divine King's throne, enjoying the divine fruits brought by the divine servants, squinting his eyes and savoring the gentle breeze from the Divine Realm.

At this moment, a notification sound rang in his mind.

"Ding! You have received an invitation to the Eternal Chat Group!"

"Do you wish to join?"

"Warning: This group carries certain risks, please act within your capabilities. The consequences of group members joining will be borne by themselves, this group will not be responsible."

"The Eternal Chat Group of the Myriad Heavens?"

The voice directly rang in Tang San's mind, making him feel doubtful.

His first thought was, who dares to trifle with him, the Divine King.

Swoosh! Tang San's soul power was released, with the Divine King's Palace as the center, scanning a radius of 500 meters back and forth.

However, he found nothing.

"Is this the work of an evil soul master, daring to offend the might of the Divine King?"

Tang San's face turned grim.

He was actually unable to investigate the origins of this Eternal Chat Group.

"Hmph, no matter who you are, you have invited death!"

Tang San's brows and eyes twisted, filled with anger.

He wanted to see who dares to play tricks on him, and directly selected to join the group.

As the Divine King, joining the Eternal Chat Group would be the most appropriate.

Let him see, what kind of chat group dares to call itself eternal.

Super Mechanical Scientist World.

"A chat group?"

"Interesting!"

Han the Technician didn't expect that after just harvesting a batch of "leeks", and preparing to break through to the Super A-Rank, he would receive an invitation to a group called the Eternal Chat Group.

He already had a "golden finger", and now with this chat group, he felt extremely intrigued.

"The Myriad Heavens Eternal, this name sounds mighty, but even reaching the peak of Super A-Rank is not truly eternal, right?"

Han the Technician didn't hesitate and immediately clicked to join.

More "golden fingers" are always better, the more the merrier.

Marvel World.

Sanctuary spaceship.

On the control room throne sat a massive and majestic being, his towering and muscular form covered in sturdy muscles and thick armor, emanating a powerful aura. His skin was a deep purple with a metallic sheen, as if forged from solid stone.

This person was the infamous Cosmic Administrator, the mighty Thanos.

"Periodic annihilation of half of all life, and the universe shall be eternal!"

Thanos spoke with great confidence.

He was just short of two Infinity Stones, and already knew the whereabouts of the final two.

Soon, he would be able to realize his ideal, cleansing half of all life in the universe.

The will of the universe, he was the Celestial Mandate.

The Eternal Chat Group, a mysterious look flashed in Thanos' eyes, and he promptly clicked to join.

Super God Academy Universe.

Ge Chongming, who was daydreaming, heard the chat group invitation and thought it was from one of the brothers in the Xiong Bing Lian, so without much thought, he directly agreed to join.

After clicking to join, Ge Chongming finally reacted.

Looking at the familiar chat group interface, he felt a bit bewildered.

He couldn't help but wonder, has Old Ma's technology become this amazing? Or is this some kind of alien technology?

The super computer of the Super God Academy didn't give any warning either.

Ah well, whatever.

Although Ge Chongming was a bit simple-minded, he wasn't stupid.

He glanced at the group name and the three people who joined with him.

He took the initiative to greet them.

The known most godly loser in the universe: "Hello everyone, it's a pleasure to meet you all."

Ge Chongming's old-fashioned group greeting, if Zhao Xin, one of the Three Scoundrels, saw it, he would definitely say, "There's an honest person here, everyone quickly bully him."

Ge Chongming looked at the group members, feeling a bit curious about this Eternal Chat Group.
