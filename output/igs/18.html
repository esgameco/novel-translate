The shadowy figure of Wumuhou and the illusory figure of the Superstar gradually became more substantial.

It wasn't long before the two souls had fully recovered, and since they had no physical bodies, they were now floating on the deck.

After regaining consciousness, Wumuhou and the Superstar saw Thanos and the Goddess of Death in front of them, and they quickly knelt down.

"Thank you, Goddess."

"Master, we have failed."

The two first expressed gratitude to the Goddess of Death, then looked at Thanos with a bitter tone.

Thanos looked at the two useless fellows, snorted coldly, and then inquired about the course of events.

Wumuhou then recounted the ins and outs of the matter in detail.

That day, Thanos had sent them two to Midgard to collect the last two Infinity Stones. At first, it went smoothly. On the spaceship, they used the Space Stone to open a portal and directly arrived in Midgard.

Upon arriving in Midgard, they worked together and immediately subdued the sorcerer who possessed the Time Stone, but due to the existence of a spell, they were unable to forcibly take the Time Stone.

So they simply took the sorcerer, along with the Stone, and the next step was to find the Mind Stone.

The owner of the Mind Stone was a being called Ultron, who had discovered them through the network ahead of time, and then he directly contacted a bald guy to ambush them.

When they went to find him, they were directly controlled by that bald guy.

Unable to move, they could only be slaughtered.

Later, the two were tortured to death in the dungeon. The sorcerer they had controlled was also tortured to death with them, so now the four Infinity Stones had fallen into the hands of the one called Ultron.

After listening to the account of Wumuhou and the Superstar, Longming's mouth twitched, "This plot sounds a lot like the 'What If' plot."

In the 'What If' plot, Thanos' sending the Stones was a case of "light gift, heavy meaning."

After Ultron obtained all the Stones, he first massacred the entire planet, and then even discovered the hidden observer and crushed him.

On the deck, looking at the two floating souls, Longming found them very annoying, pinched his thumb and middle finger, and then snapped his fingers.

Snap! A surge of energy emerged, instantly enveloping the souls of Wumuhou and the Superstar.

Flesh and blood regenerated.

It wasn't long before the two were completely reborn.

The Goddess of Death had only recalled the souls of Wumuhou and the Superstar, and she could not give them bodies.

"Hurry up and thank the Holy Lord God." Thanos saw the two standing there dazed, walked up, and suddenly slapped them.

Thanos dared not let his subordinates directly call Longming's name, and in his quick wit, he thought of Longming's other title.

With a slap, Wumuhou and the Superstar snapped out of their shock, quickly bowed to Longming, and repeatedly thanked the Holy Lord God.

Since Thanos didn't explain, they didn't dare to ask anything further.

"Also, from now on, Death will be your Mistress."

Hearing Thanos' words, the two were momentarily at a loss, but after thinking about it, they understood.

No wonder the Goddess had pulled them back from hell.

Afterwards, the two were overjoyed in their hearts. If the Goddess was the Mistress, then their legion's actions would have Thanos' approval.

Seeing the expressions of the two, Thanos knew what they were thinking, but he didn't say anything, just waved his hand to dismiss them.

Thanos turned around and said in a muffled voice to Longming beside him, "The group leader is deeply sorry, and his subordinates have failed in their duties. I will go and retrieve the Infinity Stones."

He suppressed the rage in his heart, needing to vent.

Ultron, huh? Daring to steal the universe.
