Putting down the research in his hands, he opened the chat group panel.

He quickly glanced through the chat records of a few people.

This triggered his distant memories from his previous life.

Finally, a thousand years ago, he successfully advanced to the Immortal Realm.

He directly reached the level of Sealed King Immortal!

Longming: "Since everyone has introduced themselves, let me introduce myself as well."

Longming: "I am Longming from the Muqiu Star, a transmigrator from the Blue Star. @Ge Xiu @Han Xiao, although you two are not from the same Blue Star as me, you can still be considered fellow countrymen."
