The Power Stone may still suck you in.

Among the six Infinity Stones, the Power Stone undoubtedly has the greatest energy storage capacity, even more than the other five combined.

It's no wonder it can serve as a backup power source for the other five Stones.

After fully comprehending the Power Stone, Long Ming did not feel much surprise, and he felt it was only natural. Thinking about it, the Marvel universe is just one of the countless worlds, how could it compare to the legendary Primordial Chaos?

The previous anticipation was just that - anticipation.

As for the other four Stones, Long Ming's comprehension progress from highest to lowest is: Soul, Mind, Time, and Reality.

The Soul and Mind Stones, with Long Ming's powerful divine soul, were not difficult to analyze, and he could be said to have basically grasped the general idea.

The Soul and Mind Stones are the key to the birth of life in the Marvel universe, but this universal structure is something that Long Ming can only comprehend to a certain extent for now, as it is not very useful to him.

After all, what he needs most now is to understand the basic structure of the Marvel universe, so that he can use it as a reference to perfect the structure of his inner world. As for the issue of life, perhaps he can learn from it after the divine nation has been fully upgraded.

As for the Time Stone, it contains the laws of time.

Although Long Ming has not fully grasped the laws of time, he has already gained some insight. The lower-level law of light, which is a sub-law of the time law, he had already mastered long ago.

He is only one step away from fully comprehending it.

As for the time laws within the Time Stone, they are not too profound. While it is somewhat difficult for Long Ming to comprehend, he is not completely clueless about it.

As the Stone that stabilizes the time of the Marvel universe, the Time Stone is useful to him, but the usefulness is not that great.

Perhaps it is because the time laws of the Marvel world are not powerful enough, the time laws contained within are too superficial, and many of the powerful time abilities cannot be realized, to the extent that Long Ming does not even have the greed to bring it back to his own world.

Finally, there is the Reality Stone.

On the planet, Long Ming held the Reality Stone in his hand, his expression somewhat puzzled. He never imagined that the most difficult one to comprehend would be the Reality Stone.

The other Stones can still be comprehended, but this Reality Stone is completely incomprehensible.

Long Ming recalled its abilities - to turn the illusory into reality, and to modify the rules.

These two abilities are simply heaven-defying. He doesn't know if it can even define concepts.

If it can, then possessing a Reality Stone would be invincible.

Long Ming tried various methods, but still could not get a grasp of it. Although he could easily destroy it, destroying it did not allow him to gain any insights.

"Could it be that this Reality Stone is used to maintain the dimensions of the Marvel universe?"

This idea quickly took root in Long Ming's mind.

After all, the Marvel universe has many dimensions, the most famous being Dormammu's Dark Dimension and Mephisto's Hell Dimension, some as vast as an entire universe, others as small as a coffin.

The Marvel universe is a combination of countless dimensions and the real universe.

Could it be that the Reality Stone is used to maintain the balance between the dimensions and reality? Long Ming felt that he could test it out. Coincidentally, the broken universe he obtained from the Five Primal Deities has not yet found a place to verify his conjecture, especially in the real universe, it is not easy to conduct experiments, but in other dimensions there is no such concern.

Find a dimension without any living beings, and test the Reality Stone and the broken universe together.

Without further ado, Long Ming rose and released his divine sense, beginning to search for the entrance to other dimensions.
