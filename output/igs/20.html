Of course, the Roaring Dragon wouldn't let him succeed, jumping back several meters, and at the same time, he retracted the golden and diamond weapons back into the World Ring.

"Pig, you're courting death." Seeing the Roaring Dragon's actions, Tony Shit-Head finally understood that this despicable guy had been toying with him from the beginning to the end.
