They even discovered a large pile of spirit grasses and herbs.

200 spirit stones!

"What?"

"Buying a storage bag?"

Lü Lingyun was stunned for a moment.

She then said: "Storage bags are very complex. Do you understand the intricacies?"

Yu Xiao and Wei Biao looked at each other, completely clueless.

"You two rough men probably don't know how to bargain either. I'm afraid you'll be slaughtered like fat pigs!"

Lü Lingyun said irritably.

She then sighed heavily: "Fine, fine, I have nothing urgent to do anyway. I'll go with you!"

That would be great!

Storage bags were indeed expensive and very important. They couldn't be careless about it. Being overcharged wasn't a big deal, but buying a storage bag with quality issues would be.

Lü Lingyun had better judgment than the sworn brothers.

With her help in appraising, they could avoid many pitfalls.

So the Three Friends of Spring Thunder left the martial arts hall and entered the cultivators' market.
