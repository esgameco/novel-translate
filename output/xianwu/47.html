Yu Xiao had now reached the sixth rank of blood energy, entering the middle three ranks and joining the ranks of experts in just over half a year!

To witness his rise to prominence.

Their names might even be recorded in the annals of history!

Yu Xiao was also satisfied with his cultivation progress. Since the last hunting trip, he had been cultivating diligently, using a total of five Blood Essence Pills to achieve this breakthrough.

Yu Xiao couldn't help but mock himself again.

When Senior Brother Wei Biao planned to break through to the late stage of Blood Refining.

He had only planned to prepare five Blood Essence Pills at first!

Yu Xiao's true martial arts talent was far inferior not only to true prodigies like Yu Daniu, but even to Wei Biao.

In fact, he was just average.

He couldn't be said to have no aptitude at all, but he was certainly no uncut jade or raw talent either.

Now seeing the admiration on Wang Zhong and Tang Yang's faces, and the worship in Bai Yajie's eyes, he knew these people's misunderstanding of him was getting deeper and deeper.

But this wasn't a bad thing.

The image Yu Xiao had always crafted for himself was one of extraordinary talent.

This way, when his martial arts reached great heights and shocked the nation in the future, it wouldn't seem incongruous. And it could also effectively increase his prestige.

Yu Xiao held a family banquet to celebrate.

He raised a cup of spirit wine and toasted the eight masters: "Thank you all for your guidance these many days. Without your painstaking instruction over the past half year, my martial arts could not have improved so quickly!"

First, practice the Spring Thunder Fist ten to twenty times every day to consolidate his blood energy.

Second, refine the Green Light Sword to be able to wield and control it.

Third, work hard on Qi cultivation, striving to approach the second level of Qi Refinement.

His senior brother was still in seclusion.

But it didn't matter.

The martial arts hall had gained a new member, Lü Lingyun.

She was a third-level Qi Refinement cultivator and her theoretical knowledge was much stronger than Wei Biao's.

So Yu Xiao asked her for guidance on Qi Refinement and techniques for refining the Green Light Sword.

In the inner courtyard of the Spring Thunder Martial Arts Hall.

Yu Xiao made a hand seal and shouted lightly: "Rise!"

A small green sword flew out from his sleeve, instantly growing several times larger, turning into a green light. However, it failed to hit the wooden stake and directly pierced into the courtyard wall.

"You have barely mastered this magical weapon, but to make it respond to your will as if it were an extension of your arm, you still need to continue refining it with spiritual power for some time."

Cultivators' magical weapons couldn't be used just by picking them up.

In most cases, from obtaining an unfamiliar magical weapon to using it skillfully, it required a considerable amount of time for spiritual power refinement and adaptation.

Yu Xiao had been refining the Green Light Sword for half a month.

He could barely activate it.

Lü Lingyun continued: "Moreover, your spiritual power is still too weak. You can only activate the Green Light Sword four or five times at most. You need to reach at least the second level of Qi Refinement to truly unleash its power in actual combat!"

Second level of Qi Refinement?

Even though his blood energy had broken through, Yu Xiao still felt it was quite challenging.

As the two were talking, suddenly a powerful surge of blood energy was released from a certain direction in the martial arts hall.
