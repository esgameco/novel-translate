After all, this is still a world of the survival of the fittest, and the West still has many plantations using black slaves.

The four maids had been educated and trained since childhood to serve the master.

Being a maid, although of a lower status, at least ensured food and clothing.

Of course, Yu Xiao was not a moral degenerate either, and would not force the servants to do things they were unwilling to do, or ruthlessly exploit them like cattle and horses.

The Fate points remaining were 94.

He had no plans to bring more for now.

All these seeds were used for self-cultivation.

Hiring dozens of laborers was to take care of the crops.

In the future, based on this, he would cultivate second-generation rice varieties and hand them over to the villagers to plant.

Although the quality might be slightly inferior compared to the first generation, even if it was diluted for five or eight generations, it would still be ten times better than ordinary rice, and would certainly have great competitiveness in this great dynasty.

If he could brew spiritual rice wine with the spiritual rice,

He would easily dominate the domestic and foreign markets.

He reckoned that with this as a foundation, it would not be difficult to gain the titles of "Huang Jiu King" and "Tea King".

Not only could he further gain prestige, but he could also obtain more social status, and then enter more industries, which fit his persona as an industrialist, and could also increase his standing in this world.

"What's the rush? I'm just about to get going!"

Yu Xiao felt his strength had recovered, changed out of his training clothes into formal attire, and rode in a sedan chair to leave.

The small town of Xiaohe had only one main street, with dozens of shops on both sides, from barbers and laundries to tea houses and restaurants, to weddings and funerals, providing a full range of services, truly a small but complete town.

On a street corner, lanterns were hung and firecrackers were constantly popping.

There were many onlookers.

Fifty-some thin and sallow-faced children, wearing brand new clothes, with a touch of excitement mixed with nervousness on their small faces, were looking around, waiting for something.

"Adoptive Father has come!"
