The group of elders from the Heavenly Mechanism Pavilion finally regained their composure, taking a deep breath to calm their racing hearts. They then straightened their robes and followed Nangong Jing.

"Nangong Jing, leading the elders of the Heavenly Mechanism Pavilion... pays respects to the Immortal Emperor of Longevity."

Nangong Jing bowed respectfully and was about to lead the group of elders to kneel down.

Lu Changsheng smiled faintly and interrupted, "No need for such formalities, please be seated."

Nangong Jing and the others paused, then sat down on their knees in front of Lu Changsheng.

The atmosphere fell silent. The group of elders, who usually held high positions, were now bowing their heads, very reserved.

Nangong Jing glanced at the old tortoise, then his gaze lingered on Lu Changsheng for a moment before he lowered his head, as if letting out a sigh of relief.

"Immortal Emperor, after a million years, you... have finally returned."

Lu Changsheng picked up the gourd and took a sip, then smiled mysteriously, "Judging by your reaction, you must know where I've been these past million years?"

Nangong Jing shook his head and said respectfully, "Nangong could never know the heavenly secrets related to the Immortal Emperor."

Lu Changsheng looked up at the frozen palace in the sky and said, "Precisely because the Immortal Emperor's heavenly secrets are so difficult to discern, you immediately thought of me, since those four fellows don't have the time to deal with such trivial matters."

Nangong Jing was silent for a moment, then suddenly said, "The other four Immortal Emperors' lifespans are about to end, with the longest only having ten thousand years left. But you, Immortal Emperor, still have an abundant and vigorous life force, truly living up to your title of the Immortal of Longevity."

Lu Changsheng gave him a sidelong glance and chuckled lightly, "What are you implying by bringing up the lifespans of those four fellows?"

"Immortal Emperor, Nangong believes that with your abilities, you must already know about the obstruction on the Divine Path by the Ancestral Heavenly Realm. And Nangong has calculated that a great calamity is about to descend upon the Immortal Realm... the Immortal Emperors don't have much time left."

Nangong Jing spoke directly.

The old tortoise was taken aback. He was not surprised to hear about the Divine Path, as he had already known about it and even fought with the people from the Divine Path before. But a great calamity? What was that?

However, he did not immediately raise any doubts. He knew that the Heavenly Mechanism Pavilion would not say such things lightly, as they were experts in this field.

Lu Changsheng looked at Nangong Jing and said, "Your lifespan is not long, is it? It's because of this, isn't it?"

Nangong Jing smiled calmly and said, "This is the responsibility of every generation of Pavilion Masters. Nangong is grateful that after a million years, you, Immortal Emperor, have finally appeared. This way, the Immortal Realm will have a chance to face the great calamity."

Lu Changsheng nodded, not saying anything further. As the Immortal Emperor of Longevity in the Central Immortal Realm, he had an obligation to help the Immortal Realm in times of crisis.

"Immortal Emperor, may you accompany Nangong to the Heavenly Mechanism Pavilion? Some of the content Nangong has calculated has been recorded on the Heavenly Mechanism Mirror." Nangong Jing asked respectfully.

"Alright." Lu Changsheng agreed.

Seeing this, Nangong Jing felt relieved. He led the group of elders to stand up.

Immediately, a simple imperial carriage was summoned, inviting Lu Changsheng and the old tortoise to board.

In a flash,

the imperial carriage disappeared from the city wall.

The frozen crystal palace in the sky remained, as if it had existed since ancient times.

.........

The Sweeping Yang Immortal Realm's Inspection Headquarters had been frozen, and all the personnel, from the Copper Plate Inspectors to the Immortal King-level Headquarters Masters, had perished.

This news was spreading like wildfire, not only in the Sweeping Yang Immortal Realm, but also in the Chaotic Star Immortal Realm and the Martial Immortal Realm.

The three major Immortal Realms were already in a state of turmoil...
