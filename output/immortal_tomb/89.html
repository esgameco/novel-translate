"Not saying? Then I'll just have to search your soul myself!" The Fierce Soaring Snake let out a bloodthirsty smile and raised his hand towards the Second Elder, who was then suddenly flung towards him.

Without a word, he was about to press his finger to the Second Elder's forehead, when suddenly an old, hunchbacked man appeared between the Fierce Soaring Snake and the Second Elder, using two fingers to lightly pinch the Fierce Soaring Snake's index finger...

And then decisively twisted it!

Crack!

The Fierce Soaring Snake's index finger was completely snapped off and detached.

This all happened in an instant.

"Tsk!"

The Fierce Soaring Snake hissed in pain, retreating in shock, and glared at the hunchbacked old man, growling, "Who are you? Are you the one who's been interfering with my Demon Realm's affairs in Lishui Empire?!"
