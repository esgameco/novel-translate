"Esteemed guests, our Changsheng Tavern is the same as other taverns. Since you are drinking, you should relax and drink freely. Being overly constrained is not what the master...the proprietor likes," Tong Xin said with a smile.

Jiang Wuji furrowed his brows and slowly stood up, saying, "Old Man Zhan, what wind has blown you to my Jiang Clan today?"

The woman beside him supported his wrist as they entered the hall.

"I, the clan leader, have heard that you, Jiang Wuji, suffered a setback in the Black Abyss Domain east of the Scar of the Sky, so I've come to check on you..."

Zhan Xingmang sat down in a seat casually, chuckling mysteriously.

The woman stood silently behind him, but upon hearing that Jiang Wuji had suffered a setback in the Black Abyss Domain, a trace of surprise flashed in her eyes.

The hall fell silent for a moment.

The faces of the upper echelons were slightly stiff and unnatural.

Jiang Wuji said in a cold voice, "This news should have taken some time to spread to the Holy Immortal Domain, Old Man Zhan. You, the clan leader of the Zhan Clan, truly have an iron grip on information."

Zhan Xingmang nodded slightly, neither confirming nor denying.

Seeing this, Jiang Wuji was extremely angry, and his killing intent towards that Lu Changsheng grew even stronger. He then said in a deep voice, "Old Man Zhan, if you've come here to mock me, then your purpose has been achieved. There's nothing else, so please leave. I, the clan leader, won't see you off."

"Hmph," Zhan Xingmang waved his hand slightly and smiled, "Old Jiang, you've misunderstood. With our close relationship, how could I possibly mock you? As these words fell, including Jiang Wuji, everyone in the hall tensed up.

Jiang Wuji maintained his composure and coldly chuckled, "Haha, your Zhan Clan's information is so good, yet you don't even know this?"

Zhan Xingmang simply looked at him, not speaking.

Jiang Wuji paused, then pursed his lips and said, "I'll tell you, then you can leave. My clan still has a meeting to hold."

"Jiang Chu and Jiang Chong, I don't know where they heard that the Longevity Wine Hall in Qingxuan Town of the Black Abyss Domain has a peerless vintage. They are both obsessed with alcohol, so they sneaked off there. Who would have thought that such an incident would happen?"

"Old Jiang, then I'll take my leave. I'll come visit again next time."

"Hmph." Jiang Wuji angrily waved his sleeve, his expression somewhat unsightly, and he growled, "Everyone, sit down. Let's start the meeting!"

In the distant Qingxuan Town.
