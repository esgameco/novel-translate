The Longevity Wine Hall has already made a name for itself thoroughly in Qingxuan Town!

Although the trend was inevitable due to the deliciousness of the Xihun Liquor, the Lord of the Heavenly Snake Manor kneeling outside the Longevity Wine Hall also greatly accelerated the progress of the Longevity Wine Hall in Qingxuan Town.

Just two days after opening, the Longevity Wine Hall had become known to everyone in this Qingxuan Town that housed nearly 200,000 cultivators.

Many stores that previously wanted to unite and target the Longevity Wine Hall suddenly lost their momentum, filled with fear.

Since the Lord of the Heavenly Snake Manor had knelt, they no longer dared to act rashly.

Naturally, the Longevity Wine Hall had already become the dominant force in Qingxuan Town...

Although it was just a wine hall without a bunch of people and without bullying the weak, the principle of "the strong are respected" is eternal.

Even in the following two days, the cultivators were increasingly shocked to see the Lord of the Heavenly Snake Manor still kneeling there.

On the fourth day, at dawn.

Many cultivators casually passed by the courtyard of the Longevity Wine Hall, then their gaze seemed to inadvertently look towards the courtyard gate.

They saw the Lord of the Heavenly Snake Manor standing upright at the gate, like a guardian deity, vigilantly scanning the surroundings.

Under this rather oppressive gaze, the passing cultivators did not dare to even take a breath.

The fifth, sixth, seventh, and eighth days were the same!

The crowd finally understood, shocked beyond measure!

Goodness, the Lord of the Heavenly Snake Manor was guarding the Longevity Wine Hall...

A gatekeeper?

For the overlord of a top force in Qingxuan Town to become a watchdog, this enormous change left the crowd unable to react, feeling as if they were dreaming.

During this period, Left You and the Elders of the Heavenly Snake Manor also came to visit their Lord, but were eventually chased away by Left Qoushan.

Half a month later.

Qingxuan Town was still as orderly as before.

The business of the Longevity Wine Hall was still booming every day, with the Xihun Liquor increasing from the initial daily limit of 200 jars to 500 jars!

The crowd was greatly excited, feeling grateful towards the wine hall, especially for people like Su Qianghe the drunkard, as Luchang Sheng was like their reborn parent.

Due to the large daily flow of people in Qingxuan Town, the news of the Longevity Wine Hall with its peerless good liquor gradually spread to some other towns in the Black Sha Domain...

Luchang Sheng, who had been staying in the wine hall for a long time, finally came out of the wine hall.

The motionless Left Qoushan by the door also had a slight change in expression, bowing and saying, "Senior."

"You all have come to patronize my wine hall, no need for such formalities," Luchang Sheng said with a light smile, gently waving his sleeve, and a gentle force lifted the crowd.

The crowd bowed slightly, feeling something unusual.

In this Black Sha Domain, the ordinary cultivators were all arrogant, but who would have thought that this proprietor had such overwhelming strength in Qingxuan Town, yet was still so amiable!

It was a rather different experience.

"Senior... Who are you?" Left Qoushan asked cautiously.

Luchang Sheng looked up at the sky and smiled, "I've been in Qingxuan Town for a while, and haven't had a chance to stroll around. Today the sunshine is just right, the breeze is not oppressive, it's perfect."

Left Qoushan hurriedly said, "Senior, shall I lead the way for you?"

"It's fine," Luchang Sheng smiled and stopped him, then turned his head to look at Peng Fang.

The implication was obvious - he wanted to guide Luchang Sheng.

Luchang Sheng looked at him meaningfully and smiled, "Alright."
