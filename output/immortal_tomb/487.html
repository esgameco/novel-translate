In the eternal night city, the fluctuations of the laws were becoming increasingly intense.

The cultivators hurriedly retreated, leaving the field to the three major factions.

Even many cultivators fled outside the eternal night city, and after deploying various defensive measures, they looked back at that place in shock.

No one had expected that the Three Tigers Gang would actually say they were going to attack.

"Three Tigers Gang, have you gone mad? You actually want to go to war with us over a hall master? Do you know the consequences of going to war?"

The Fierce Lion Faction leader's eyes were gloomy as he shouted, "Moreover, the death of your hall master has nothing to do with us...!"

Saying this, the Fierce Lion Faction leader's eyes suddenly changed, and he coldly laughed, "Hehe, the lord knows, the so-called death of the hall master is just a pretext. You Three Tigers Gang have been waiting for this day for a long time..."

The three gang leaders of the Three Tigers Gang had to admit that the Fierce Lion Faction leader was very sharp.

"Fierce Lion, what do you mean?" the Violent Elephant Sect Master frowned and asked.

At the same time, many puzzled gazes also turned to him.

The Fierce Lion Faction leader laughed loudly, "The so-called seeking justice for the hall master is just an excuse, a pretext. The Three Tigers Gang has long wanted to start a war. Their ambition is not limited to this; they want to dominate the Eternal Night Island!"

What?!

The crowd was shocked!

Many cultivators who had been living on the Eternal Night Island immediately looked at the Three Tigers Gang with some hostility!

If this were the case, they would definitely stand on the side of the Fierce Lion Faction and the Violent Elephant Sect to resist the Three Tigers Gang!

Seeing this, the three gang leaders of the Three Tigers Gang had a gloomy look in their eyes. If these many local cultivators stood on the Fierce Lion's side, it would be extremely unfavorable for their Three Tigers Gang.

"Big Brother, we can't delay any longer. Delaying further won't do us any good!"

"Yes, Big Brother, while the situation is still unclear, we should directly subdue the two factions. As long as we subdue them, everything will be under the control of our Three Tigers Gang."

Hearing this, the big gang leader couldn't help but glance at the members of the gang. Sure enough, he also saw that the expressions of the members had changed somewhat.

He then suddenly raised his hand and shouted loudly:

"My Three Tigers Gang is upright and righteous. How could we have such ambitions? Fierce Lion! Stop deceiving the masses! Today, our gang leader will help Big Bear and hundreds of brothers take revenge! Come at us with your lives!"

As soon as he finished speaking, the big gang leader rushed towards the slightly changed Fierce Lion.

The second and third gang leaders then attacked the Violent Elephant Sect Master.

The members of the Three Tigers Gang also shot out like arrows released from a bow!

At this moment, the battle was instantly ignited, and there was no room for the crowd to continue guessing blindly!

"Haha! Good!" The Fierce Lion Faction leader threw his head back and laughed loudly, then his gaze became fierce:

"Since it's like this, then from now on, only one side can exist on the Eternal Night Island - us, the Fierce Lion Faction and the Violent Elephant Sect! Fierce Lion Faction, kill!"

"Violent Elephant Sect, kill!" The Violent Elephant Sect Master clenched his fists tightly, and his clothes were instantly torn apart by his bursting muscles.

Soon, the Eternal Night City erupted in light, with various laws emerging one after another, as there were no shortage of cultivators who had mastered the laws, the only difference being their level of proficiency and mastery.

The ordinary disciples of the factions had relatively weak law comprehension, and their law attributes were generally not strong either.

But once they reached the level of hall masters, they would have considerable combat power, with their laws either filled with offensive power or highly functional!

The next moment, one of the hall masters of the Fierce Lion Faction made a hand seal, connecting his consciousness to heaven and earth, and shouted, "Law of Mist, cover me!"

Centered on him, a mighty mist suddenly spread out in the Eternal Night City, quickly enveloping the entire city.

"Huh?! This, this is the Law of Mist, the law power of the Third Hall Master of the Fierce Lion Faction!"

"Sss, you can't see anything... But what's the use of that? If both sides can't see each other, won't they just be blindly striking around?"

"Hehe, you're wrong. Do you really think the Law of Mist is that simple? As the one who released this law, the Third Hall Master of the Fierce Lion Faction has achieved a certain level of mastery in the Law of Mist. He can freely control the density of the mist, and this mist also contains a deadly poison."

"If that's the case, in the mist, the Three Tigers Gang's vision is obstructed, but the Fierce Lion Faction and the Violent Elephant Sect are not affected?"

"Exactly!"

"How do you know so much about the Law of Mist?"

"Hehe, I'm not that capable. I've also comprehended the Law of Mist, but my cultivation is far behind the Third Hall Master of the Fierce Lion Faction."

"......"

At this moment, the entire Eternal Night City was shrouded in the drifting mist.

Except for the members of the three major factions, all other cultivators had already fled the Eternal Night City and were watching from afar, their hearts filled with terror.

"Big Brother, this mist seems to contain a deadly poison. Look at those flowers, grasses, and trees..."

Chen Pang pointed at a spot in horror. Xiao Chai looked over, and sure enough, he saw that the plants withered the moment they came into contact with the mist.

At the same time, the members of the Three Tigers Gang inside the city were somewhat panicked. They immediately activated their life-saving methods to isolate themselves from the mist, but unfortunately, apart from the hall master-level figures, they couldn't withstand it at all, and many of them immediately had their legs turn weak.

"Gui Lang, what are you waiting for? Quickly disperse the poisonous mist!"

The big gang leader shouted during his battle with the Fierce Lion Faction leader.

Gui Lang was the second hall master of the Three Tigers Gang. He was stunned for a moment.
