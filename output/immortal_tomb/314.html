The Sword Ancestor and the others were shocked, involuntarily looking up at their own ancestor, only to see the smile on their face gradually disappear.

Seeing this, they felt a chill in their hearts - Lu Changsheng was courting death!

To be honest, when they first saw their ancestor's attitude, they were afraid that Lu Changsheng would shake hands and make peace with their ancestor.

Lu Changsheng's heart stirred a bit as he asked, "Where is your true body?"

Sword Wuhui paused momentarily, then a scroll appeared in his hand, which he tossed to Lu Changsheng.

He had been away from the Eternal Celestial Realm for so many years that he no longer had much affection for the Ancient Desolate Divine Sword Clan. If not for the ancestral imprint he left behind being summoned this time, he might have even forgotten that he had once established such a clan.
