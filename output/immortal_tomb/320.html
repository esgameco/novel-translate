Everything was happening in the vast and high heavens, which people like Duan Jiuxiong could not see clearly, and they could only judge by the sounds coming from the heavens.

Although they did not understand what was specifically happening, they could grasp the general situation through the sounds.

It seemed that the Ancient Clan was losing in the battle against the Heavenly Dao Lord, and the so-called "Summoning the Ancestor" should be the last resort.

Some cultivators had witnessed the destruction of the Ancient Desolate Sword Clan in the past, so they deeply understood what "Summoning the Ancestor" meant - it meant that the Ancient Clan had been pushed to the brink by the Heavenly Dao Lord.

The heavens.

Seven figures slowly appeared, like tangible entities. They wore different attires, but they all had a simple and pure aura, a sense of returning to the roots.

However, Lu Changsheng, who was far away in the Moon-Star Realm, could see them clearly. Among the seven, five were inferior to the Sword Immortal Sword Wuhui.

Only two of them might be comparable to Sword Wuhui.

"Greet the ancestors!"

When the powerful members of the Ancient Clans discovered that their ancestors had appeared, the suppressing power of the Heavenly Dao suddenly disappeared from their bodies. Overjoyed, they hurriedly paid their respects to their own ancestors: "Greet the ancestors!"

The seven figures, however, did not pay attention to the Ancient Clan experts. After exchanging slight nods with each other, they turned to the two Heavenly Dao Lords and slightly cupped their hands in a simple greeting.

A graceful lady with a gentle smile asked, "May I ask what has happened, esteemed Heavenly Dao Lords?"

She was the ancestor of the Ancient Moon Blood Clan, Blood Moon Ru.

After Blood Moon Ru asked, the other six figures flashed a glance but did not speak.

At this time, the two Heavenly Dao Lord's illusory figures seemed to slightly bow, and one of them chuckled:

"Hehe, you seven, you should ask your own descendants about what has happened."

Hearing this, the seven people frowned slightly and looked towards the powerful members of the Ancient Clans.

The powerful members of the Ancient Clans, however, were already panicked by the friendly attitude their ancestors showed towards the Heavenly Dao Lords. An ominous premonition arose in their hearts!

It seems they had underestimated the Heavenly Dao Lords!

"Speak," said a bare-chested burly man in a calm tone.

He had a bald head and an extremely exaggerated muscular build, making it clear that he was the ancestor of the Ancient Saint Clan, Mang Tian.

"Ancestor, we eight clans only destroyed five Boundless Heavenly Dao at the Middle World level, but they also annihilated the Ancient Desolate Sword Clan... They actually demanded an explanation from us, and of course we were unwilling..." the clan leader of the Ancient Clans said cautiously and tremblingly.

Huh? The Ancient Desolate Sword Clan was destroyed? What about Sword Wuhui?

The seven figures' expressions showed slight surprise as they swept their gaze over, and indeed they did not see any powerful members of the Ancient Desolate Sword Clan.

"Insolence," Mang Tian growled. "Merely destroying five Boundless Heavenly Dao at the Middle World level? How dare you speak such arrogant words? Have you become so conceited from staying in this void for too long?"

The other six people also looked at them coldly.

They did not expect the Ancient Clan they left behind in the Eternal Vast Realm to have become like this. They must maintain face in front of the Heavenly Dao Lords, as it would not be wise to make enemies with them because of these Ancient Clans.

After all, the Ancient Clans left in this realm were not important to them. If not for the ancestral seals, they would have almost forgotten about them.

The powerful members of the Ancient Clans felt distressed and speechless. They never expected that after shattering the precious ancestral seals, the situation would turn out like this...

The ancestors were actually siding with the Heavenly Dao.

The seven figures looked at the clearly disgruntled powerful members of the Ancient Clans and shook their heads. The Ancient Clans they left behind were truly short-sighted and fearless in their ignorance.

The Heavenly Dao was not as simple as they thought.
