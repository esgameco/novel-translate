On the eastern city wall, Cloud Zeshuang and the officials seemed to sense something, and they quickly looked up to see a beam of light descending towards them.

"Your Majesty, that is the Piercing Cloud Carriage!!"

An official exclaimed hurriedly.

The Piercing Cloud Carriage was the exclusive palace carriage of the Ninth Prince, Cloud Aoxie, which the officials were all too familiar with.

The officials knew that this meant the Ninth Prince and the Qin Saint had returned, and most importantly, the distinguished personage that His Majesty had mentioned should be in the carriage at this moment!

Cloud Zeshuang pursed his lips, even as the ruler of a nation, he also felt nervous at this moment.

After all, what Ye Xunwang had said earlier was too shocking.

For a mere servant to be able to crush them...

As for his younger brother's strength, Cloud Zeshuang was more familiar with it than anyone.

In the blink of an eye, the light flashed and slowly descended onto the city wall.

The first thing Cloud Zeshuang and the officials saw were Cloud Aoxie, Ye Xunwang and others outside the carriage, as well as a strange old man...

Cloud Zeshuang's gaze lingered on the old turtle for a moment, immediately guessing that this person must be the terrifying servant...

He then quickly shifted his gaze to the carriage, where a blurry figure was now visible behind the curtain.

At the same time, Cloud Zeshuang saw Ye Xunwang and his son frantically signaling to him with their eyes, and he quickly reacted, hurriedly bowing with the officials and saying:

"I am Cloud Zeshuang, the ruler of the ancient Cloud Lan Kingdom. Today, I have specially led the officials to welcome you, the arrival of the distinguished personage is a blessing for the capital city, and an even greater blessing for the ancient Cloud Lan Kingdom!"

On the carriage, apart from the listless old turtle, Cloud Aoxie and Ye Xunwang and the others also bowed.

Soon, the curtain was lifted, and Lu Changsheng walked out from it, looking at the robed Cloud Zeshuang and the officials before him, and said softly, "You may rise."

"Yes, distinguished personage."

Cloud Zeshuang's heart relaxed slightly, and he led the officials to rise, finally getting a clear look at the young man standing on the carriage, hands behind his back.

He was a young man, simple and unadorned, except for the gourd at his waist, with only his azure robe fluttering in the wind, making him seem even more transcendent.

Cloud Zeshuang and the others were stunned by Lu Changsheng's calm and unfathomable aura, and for a moment they didn't know what to say, nor dared they look into those deep eyes, just standing there stiffly.

Ye Xunwang saw this and quickly cleared his throat, saying, "Your Majesty, the distinguished personage must be weary from the journey, we should invite him to rest in the palace first."

Cloud Zeshuang quickly nodded and smiled, "Yes, yes, distinguished personage, please follow me..."

"Outrageous!! How dare you block my royal brother!!"

But before Cloud Zeshuang could finish his sentence, a furious shout suddenly came from a direction.

Immediately, Cloud Zeshuang's expression changed slightly, and Ye Xunwang, Cloud Aoxie, and many officials also stiffened.

Anyone could tell that this was the voice of the Crown Prince, Cloud Aotong.

Well, what was the Crown Prince trying to do in this situation? Was he looking for trouble?

Wasn't the King's order that no one was allowed to approach this place?

Now it's done, it must have left a bad impression on the young man in azure!

Cloud Zeshuang looked at Lu Changsheng, his face slightly pale, and said with difficulty, "Distinguished personage..."

A faint smile appeared on Lu Changsheng's face as he said, "It's fine, you deal with it first, there's no hurry."

Seeing that Lu Changsheng was not angry, the people instantly relaxed their tense nerves!

Their situation was exactly the same as when Cloud Aoxie and Ye Xunwang had descended upon the Heavenly Fragrance City, with Guo Xiong and Zhu Guoyuan.

They were afraid of making Lu Changsheng uncomfortable in any way.

"Yes, the distinguished personage is magnanimous." Cloud Zeshuang cupped his hands and forced a smile, then his expression darkened as he shouted, "Bring that rebellious son, Cloud Aotong, to me!"

The officials trembled, inwardly understanding that the Crown Prince was truly in trouble this time, as this was the first time they had heard the King address any prince by their full name, which showed how enraged he was.

The officials guessed that if it weren't for the presence of the young man in azure, the King would have unleashed his fury, but now he could not do so.

At the same time,

"Crown Prince, the King has ordered us to bring you over."

Two imperial guards received the order and headed towards the Crown Prince Cloud Aotong.
