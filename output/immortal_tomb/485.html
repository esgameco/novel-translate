In a restaurant, Lu Changsheng casually found a seat by the window, then ordered two pots of wine and a few dishes of spiritual energy.

Lu Changsheng thought for a moment, his mind entering the Great Dao Demon Subduing Tower, arriving at a corner where a turtle shell was lying quietly.

Lu Changsheng kicked it with his foot, infusing it with some qi while chuckling, "Old Turtle, wake up and have a drink with me?"

Hm?

A turtle head poked out of the shell, seeming a bit disoriented. But after seeing Lu Changsheng, it got startled and suddenly transformed into a human form, trembling as it said:

"M-Master!"

Lu Changsheng waved his hand, then brought the Old Turtle out of the Great Dao Demon Subduing Tower, appearing in the restaurant.

"Sit, have a drink with me," Lu Changsheng said with a smile.

The Old Turtle regained its senses, looking around the place with a wary gaze, not knowing where it was. It then eyed Lu Changsheng suspiciously before carefully sitting down.

The Old Turtle poured wine for Lu Changsheng, then poured a cup for itself. After Lu Changsheng took a sip, the Old Turtle picked up the cup and took a careful sip as well: "Master, how long was I unconscious... and where is this place?"

The Old Turtle could feel that this was no longer the Qiankun Heavens, damn it, it hadn't even entered the Void Ancient Realm yet and had already passed out.

Lu Changsheng picked up his chopsticks, taking a bite of the dish and chewing it slowly before swallowing and saying with a smile, "You weren't out for long. This is no longer the Qiankun Heavens, but the Three Thousand Realms, and we are currently in the Sea of Principles."

The Old Turtle nodded slightly, then carefully looked at Lu Changsheng and mustered up the courage to say:

"Master, please be honest with Old Turtle - was it because I smuggled some personal... items last time that led to such a harsh punishment?"

Otherwise, it wouldn't have been so disastrous.

Lu Changsheng raised his gaze to look at the Old Turtle, saying in a deep tone, "You think I brought in personal grievances?"

The Old Turtle gave an embarrassed smile. "Just joking."

But Lu Changsheng said, "That's right, I did bring in personal grievances. Is that not allowed?"

The Old Turtle's expression changed slightly, his body starting to tremble. "Of course it's allowed!"

Seeing the frightened Old Turtle, Lu Changsheng suddenly spoke earnestly:

"Old Turtle, do you not know your own worth? In the Qiankun Heavens' Heavenly Abyss, you couldn't even handle a small demonic creature - this truly disappoints me."

"I've forgotten how long you've been following me, but it must have been a long time. As my mount, if you don't cultivate diligently and instead indulge in women all day, how can you expect me, your master, to take care of everything for you?"

"If you continue to decline, even Tongxin and Lindong will surpass you."

Hearing this, the Old Turtle was filled with shame, wanting to find a crack in the ground to hide in.

It seems he had misjudged his master. Of course a supreme cultivator like his master wouldn't be petty.

"Master, rest assured. From today on, Old Turtle will stay away from beauties and focus on cultivation, not letting down your teachings."

The Old Turtle bowed his head.

Lu Changsheng glanced at the Old Turtle. "If I remember correctly, you've said this many times before."

The Old Turtle was immediately embarrassed.

Lu Changsheng gave a faint smile. "Your lifespan is also very long, you should have gained some insights."

The Old Turtle hurriedly said, "Yes, yes, yes. Beautiful women are like fleeting clouds and red flowers that wither - no matter how beautiful the appearance, it is just an ugly skeleton supporting it in the end... Eventually, the red flowers will fade and the dry bones be lost."

The Old Turtle actually understood the principles, after all, it had lived for so long. But but...

Lu Changsheng put down his chopsticks and said softly, "This is the Sea of Principles. As long as you cultivate diligently, with your speed, it should be quite simple."

The Old Turtle was delighted, quickly thumping his chest in guarantee. "Master, rest assured!"

"Don't be so quick to be happy. I'll tell you, if I find you slacking off again..."

This oath was quite vicious.

Hearing this, Lu Changsheng was initially a bit puzzled, but after reacting, his face darkened slightly, and he waved his hand to let the old turtle sit down.

The old turtle scratched his head, sat down awkwardly, and asked puzzledly, "Master, what about Tongxin, Linzong, and the others?"

"I have temporarily dispersed them in the Qiankun Heaven." Lu Changsheng rubbed his temples.

The old turtle nodded and quietly drank his wine, no longer speaking.

"Old turtle." Lu Changsheng said indifferently, "The final destination of this voyage will be the place of law comprehension, which will be a good opportunity for you to grow stronger."

Profound techniques are powerful, but a small number of powerful laws are not necessarily weaker than profound techniques, and may even surpass them.

For example, the Light Profound Technique and the Space Law, although one is a profound technique and the other is a law, the Light Profound Technique may not necessarily be able to defeat the Space Law.

After all, profound techniques and laws are both at the Void Realm, and profound techniques are only slightly more advanced than laws.

Since profound techniques have not surpassed the Void Realm, they have not surpassed laws by much, and some top-tier laws can surpass profound techniques with their powerful abilities and characteristics.

Such as the Time System, Space System, Power System, Destruction System, Fate System, and so on...

The old turtle paused, quickly saying, "Master, rest assured!"

Although he said this, the old turtle was inwardly anxious. Comprehending the law is simple, but what if the law he comprehends is trash? The old turtle suddenly felt a little lacking in self-confidence.
