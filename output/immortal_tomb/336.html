The eyes of the people in the Chiyang Palace suddenly sharpened, and the Grand Elder stared solemnly at the man in blue robes!

A... Daoist Immortal?

"Sect Master!!"

Peng Fang and the other Xiaoliao Sect disciples were shocked and hurriedly paid their respects. Tong Xin and Lin Dong were extremely excited - with the Sect Master's involvement, today's matter would no longer be a problem.

Huh? This person is the Sect Master of the Xiaoliao Sect?

The elder of the Chiyang Palace's eyes flickered, but he still did not feel too much panic in his heart.

So what if he is a Daoist Immortal realm expert?

Not to mention that the life of the Sect Elder of their sect is currently in his hands right now, more importantly, he believes that the other party definitely fears the Chiyang Palace and would not dare to make a big fuss about this!

"Hehe, so you are the Sect Master of the Xiaoliao Sect? Perhaps the actions of the Chiyang Palace today were a bit presumptuous..."

But before the elder of the Chiyang Palace could finish speaking, Yin Jiudui coldly interrupted:

"Nonsense, how is this presumptuous? We of the Xiaoliao Sect came here, and you said this is your Chiyang Palace's territory. Didn't we say we were willing to compensate?

But you made an outrageous demand, asking for our sect's core cultivation technique. Isn't that insatiably greedy! After being rejected by me and the Second Elder, you immediately resorted to violence without a second thought!!"

What?!

They actually wanted the Xiaoliao Technique?!

The executive disciples of the Xiaoliao Sect were all shocked, staring unbelievably at the elder of the Chiyang Palace.

Seeing this, the elder of the Chiyang Palace lowered his head, his expression gloomy for a moment, then he ignored Yin Jiudui and looked at Lu Changsheng with a smile:

"Sect Master of the Xiaoliao Sect, as they say, 'no fight, no acquaintance'. This old man admits that our actions today were a bit excessive. How about this, this old man will release your Sect Elder, and we can just let this matter go?"

Even though he knew the Sect Master of the Xiaoliao Sect before him was a Daoist Immortal expert, he was still confident!

Upon hearing this, the members of the Xiaoliao Sect had extremely unsightly expressions, but the fact was that their Second Elder, Su Qianghe, was still in the other party's hands.

Today, unless they were willing to disregard Su Qianghe's life, it would be very difficult to kill this elder of the Chiyang Palace.

"Let it go?" Lu Changsheng smiled slightly, "Why do you think I would agree to just let it go?"

Hearing the Sect Master speak, the members of the Xiaoliao Sect watched intently, as for the current situation, they were helpless and did not know what the Sect Master...

"Hehe." The elder of the Chiyang Palace smiled benevolently, slowly saying, "Sect Master of the Xiaoliao Sect, I'm sure you don't want to offend our Chiyang Palace, especially since your Sect Elder is still in my hands..."

"And then?" Lu Changsheng asked calmly.

The elder of the Chiyang Palace's expression changed slightly, being straightforward, this Sect Master of the Xiaoliao Sect, although appearing calm and indifferent on the surface, made him inexplicably feel an increasing sense of pressure!

"And then naturally..."

"Crawl over here."

But before the elder of the Chiyang Palace could finish speaking, he was interrupted by Lu Changsheng!

Crawl over?

The elder of the Chiyang Palace's face flushed with anger, how dare this Sect Master of the Xiaoliao Sect insult him!

But at that moment, he felt his four limbs lose control, and the hand strangling Su Qianghe's neck also stiffened and loosened. He suddenly felt a sense of terror rise in his heart, and he looked up at Lu Changsheng with a horrified gaze!

Afterwards, the elder of the Chiyang Palace, under the horrified gaze of everyone present, slowly prostrated on the ground and crawled towards Lu Changsheng.

"G-Grand Elder..."

The members of the Chiyang Palace were all bewildered by this scene, they looked unbelievably at the crawling Grand Elder.

But at this moment, the heart of the elder of the Chiyang Palace was already filled with fear!

He wanted to stop himself, stop this humiliating action, but no matter how hard he tried, he was powerless to do so!

His mind had never been as clear as it was at this moment.
