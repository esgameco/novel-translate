The crack that pierced the heavens also gradually became clearer.

Outside the Longevity Tavern, the people sitting cross-legged in line on the ground perked up, the sky was finally going to brighten!

Some of the people at the front of the line looked back at the queue, and couldn't help but take a sharp intake of breath!

They saw a dense mass of heads swaying, it seemed to be over ten thousand people......

The line even appeared to still be growing......

With this sea of people, the Longevity Tavern would not be able to accommodate them all in a short time.

However, they all knew that as long as they held their place in line, they would eventually get their turn.

Just then, the massive queue suddenly became restless.

The cultivators at the very front were puzzled, and turned around to look, their pupils then suddenly constricting.

In their line of sight, twelve figures were slowly approaching them.

What shocked them was that the four leading figures were the leaders of the four top powers in Qingxuan Town.

The Heavenly Snake Manor, Su Qianghe.

The Giant Axe Gang, Su Qianghe.

The Shadowy Pavilion, Zhao Wuou.

The Controlling Puppet Dao, Yin Jiukui.

"No way... the leaders of the four top powers have all come, it seems they're here for the Longevity Tavern, are they going to fight it out?"

"What about the soul-cleansing wine I've been longing for..."

"Well, what can we do? If you don't want to die, just quietly slip away. We've been waiting all night for this, how unlucky."

"Don't worry, after yesterday, you should all understand that the Tavern Keeper is likely at the Xian Lord level. Since they dare to open this tavern, it means they are confident. I don't think the four top powers will be able to easily take over the Longevity Tavern."

"Xian Lord or not, these four top power leaders are all Xian Lord bigshots. Forget it, if the Longevity Tavern is gone, I'll risk my life to snatch a bottle of the soul-cleansing wine!"

"......"

The cultivators frantically transmitted messages, their words mostly filled with tension.

Normally, they would be quite happy to witness something like this - the four top power leaders making a move. It's not their business after all.

But when it came to the soul-cleansing wine they had been longing for, it was a different story.

They couldn't help but feel that the four top powers were ruining their good thing!!

At the same time,

Peng Fang in the queue lowered his eyelids, a hint of murderous intent rising in his heart.

If these power leaders targeted the Longevity Tavern today, he would definitely intervene!

His gaze was extremely far-sighted, if this tavern was gone, where would he get his soul-cleansing wine in the future?

That was unacceptable!

Without the soul-cleansing wine, he wouldn't be able to survive at all!

A mere Sixth-Rank Xian Lord, against three Fifth-Rank Xian Lords, heh...

Peng Fang sneered inwardly...

......

"Haha, the queue is indeed very long, is this soul-cleansing wine really that good to drink?"

The Giant Axe Gang leader Su Qianghe laughed heartily, his muscular body dark and chiseled, exuding a powerful aura.

Yin Jiukui's eyes flickered as he smiled, "What good wine haven't we tried? He looked towards Su Qianghe and Zhao Wuou.

If one wants to survive in Qing Xuan Town, they must lower themselves before the leaders of the four top powers.
