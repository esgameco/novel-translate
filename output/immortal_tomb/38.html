Before the pupils of the ancient crane-like elder could even contract, the Second Elder suddenly stood up, his eyes burning with excitement and exclaimed,

"An exquisite Fire Spirit Root! This is an exceptionally rare exquisite Fire Spirit Root!!"

An exquisite Fire Spirit Root!

The innocent boy looked at the kneeling youth with incomprehensible wonder, his heartbeat accelerating!

What is the concept of an exquisite Fire Spirit Root? He had already become a direct disciple of the ancient Immortal of the Azure Cloud Sect with his superior Thunder Spirit Root, so an exquisite Fire Spirit Root... he dared not even imagine.

Has the Azure Cloud Sect ever produced an exquisite spirit root before?

This kind of spirit root would incite a fierce competition among all the sects in the cultivation world.

"Exquisite Fire Spirit Root, what is that?" Although puzzled, Lin Dong also felt that he was not too inferior.

"Senior, senior, senior, do you mean..."

The gaze of the ancient crane-like elder towards Lu Changsheng was intense, and his speech was even somewhat stammering due to excitement.

"Senior! I can do it too..." At this time, the Second Elder also looked at Lu Changsheng with anticipation, his fingers even turning white from gripping tightly.

At the same time, he sighed inwardly, thankful that the Sect Master did not come to Moyu Town this time, otherwise this prodigy disciple might have been taken in as a direct disciple.

Hearing these two immortal-like elders address Lu Changsheng as "senior", Lin Dong was shocked again... Could it be that his teacher is also an immortal? And an even more powerful one at that.

At this moment, Lu Changsheng looked at Lin Dong and said,

"Isn't that the immortal you admired that night? He is the one."

Saying that, he nodded slightly towards the ancient crane-like elder.

Lin Dong looked at the ancient crane-like elder, his pupils contracting to the size of needles, his mind momentarily blank.

This, this white-bearded old man is actually the immortal from that night!

Now that he thought about it, the voice was indeed extremely similar.

"As for who Lin Dong wants to take as his teacher, let him choose for himself," Lu Changsheng did not immediately reject the Second Elder's request.

"Thank you, senior!" The Second Elder bowed to Lu Changsheng, then looked at Lin Dong and said solemnly, "I am the Second Elder of the Azure Cloud Sect. If you take me as your teacher, I will pour all my resources into you!

The best dwelling, the best cultivation techniques, the best spells, the best elixirs, the best... all will be yours!!"

Seeing the Second Elder's determination, the ancient crane-like elder's heart ached, but he also knew the importance of the exquisite Fire Spirit Root. Moreover, this exquisite Fire Spirit Root is practically tailor-made for alchemy.

And more importantly - this is recommended by the senior himself, if he says there is no connection between the boy and the senior, he would not believe it.

This young man has a connection with the senior, if he, Gu Helin, takes him as a disciple, does that mean he also has a connection with the senior?

Damn it, he must not let this happen!

His thoughts were very strong, but what he said was: "I, Gu, do not have as many grand words as the Second Elder, but I can only say that if you take me as your teacher, I will definitely be a good master."

Lin Dong did not hesitate at all, first giving an apologetic look to the Second Elder, then looking at Gu Helin, lightly knocking his forehead three times and said, "Master."

He did not know what dwelling, elixirs, cultivation techniques, or spells were, he only knew that the sentence "seek longevity, and even more seek the heroic spirit within" seemed to be engraved in his bones.

"Haha, good." Gu Helin paused, then stroked his beard and smiled.

The Second Elder sat down dejectedly, shaking his head and sighing, "Oh well, oh well."

No matter whose disciple he is, he is still a disciple of the Azure Cloud Sect.

"Alright, Lin Dong, come stand beside your senior disciple behind me."
