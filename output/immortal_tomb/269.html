The master was telling the truth. What was Tong Xin compared to Li Shuyan, who could even arm-wrestle with Immortal Emperors?

Tong Xin's heart had just warmed up, but when he heard the last part, his face fell.

Was there such a way to comfort someone?

Grandpa Turtle, no, that old rascal was really bad.

"Phew." At this moment, Li Shuyan straightened up, his heart still pounding: "Master, you are right. Thank you for your timely intervention, or else I would have been lost."

After following Lu Changsheng, Li Shuyan had also changed the way he addressed him.

Lu Changsheng looked at him, "And now?"

What now? Old Turtle and Tong Xin were puzzled.

Li Shuyan pursed his lips and smiled, bowing to the Azure Robe, then boldly surveying the endless void and the twinkling stars, saying:

"Master, thanks to you, I now understand that we cultivators should dare to contend with Heaven, contend with Earth, and contend with ourselves, and not be intimidated by the endless void."

Old Turtle and Tong Xin listened in awe.
