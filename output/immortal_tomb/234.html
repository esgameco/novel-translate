He had been a bit bored staying in the clinic all the time, so he wanted to go out for a stroll, since there were no patients at the moment anyway.

"Teacher, are you going out?" Tongxin quickly asked, subtly implying that he wanted to accompany the teacher.

"You and Xiaotong should just stay in the clinic. When there's nothing to do, cultivate properly. As for Xiaotong, read medical books and memorize medicinal herbs," Luchengsheng said as he stepped out, then disappeared.

"Yes," Tongxin and Xiaotong quickly responded.

"Xiaotong, you should hurry and read the medical books. The teacher has instructed me to test you on them later," Tongxin said, waving his hand as he turned to go back inside.

"Okay," Xiaotong replied, hugging the medical books.

Tongxin paused his steps and turned around, frowning as he asked, "Those two cultivators just now wanted to take you to cultivate immortality, why didn't you go with them?"

Xiaotong opened her mouth, then after a moment, softly said, "I just like to study medicine."

Tongxin's gaze flickered, but he didn't say anything more and went back inside to cultivate.

...

In an area far from Yanyu Town.

Su Lianyi, carrying the human stick Xu Jiao, was rushing away at lightning speed, her pretty face anxious, occasionally looking back, fearing that the azure-robed cultivator would come chasing after them!

Su Lianyi was truly frightened. Who knows how immense the pressure was for her in front of that azure-robed cultivator!

In all these years since becoming the Holy Lady of the Upper Clear Palace, she had never been so afraid before, it was a feeling of losing control of her life.

Afterward, the more she thought about it, the more she dreaded it.

That azure-robed cultivator's cultivation... it was probably beyond her ability to estimate.

Such a powerful expert was actually hidden in the Yanyu region...

She suddenly snapped back to her senses!! Why was Xiaotong working as a medicine attendant in that clinic, could it be that the azure-robed cultivator had discovered Xiaotong's Jade Maiden Profound Body?

"Ugh~" At this moment, Xu Jiao's painful groan came.

Su Lianyi cut off her thoughts and looked at the human stick beside her... Xu Jiao spoke with a strained voice, "Holy Lady... that person's sword, not only did it sever my four limbs, but it also cut away my ten thousand years of cultivation, causing me to fall from the Golden Immortal Realm to the Celestial Immortal Realm..."

Xu Jiao's face was deathly pale, full of agony as she spoke.

What?! Ten thousand years of cultivation!

Su Lianyi was shocked, quickly checking, and sure enough, it was true.

Just now, she had been so preoccupied with her own thoughts that she didn't notice Xu Jiao had truly fallen to the Celestial Immortal Realm...

Looking at the pitiful Xu Jiao at the moment, and then thinking about the Jade Maiden Profound Body matter... her gaze flickered, and a strange feeling welled up in her heart.

If Xu Jiao returned to the palace, she might very well tell the Sect Master about the Jade Maiden Profound Body...

At this moment, Su Lianyi's previously wavering and undecided thoughts, the hesitation between the sect and herself, suddenly tilted slightly.

Seeing Su Lianyi remain silent, Xu Jiao couldn't help but continue to grit her teeth, her face full of resentment.
