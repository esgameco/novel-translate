The space fell into an eerie silence...

Luo Ge behind Lü Changsheng also felt somewhat puzzled, but soon realized that these inspectors were probably waiting for Jinyang's arrival. It seems this senior in blue robes had them somewhat at a loss.

"Summon your patriarch, I wish to have a word with him," Lü Changsheng said impatiently to the insignificant insects.

As soon as the words left his mouth, the crowd stood dumbfounded.

But soon, an overwhelming imperial aura suddenly descended, suppressing the entire space!

Boom!!

All their faces were filled with fear!

Especially the golden-badge inspectors, who felt their world spinning. Never in their lives had they experienced such panic and great terror!

The young man in blue robes before them was none other than - the Immortal Emperor Changsheng himself!!

"This palace would like to see which fool dares to defy the... imperial might!"

Lü Changsheng materialized before Jinyang, looking down at him.

"I did not know the Immortal Emperor had graced us with his presence. Please forgive my lack of proper welcome!" Jinyang trembled as he bowed his head.

He was racking his brain, unable to understand why the long-missing Immortal Emperor Changsheng would come to his Inspection Palace, and how Luo Ge could be connected to the Immortal Emperor.

Immortal Emperor!!

The inspectors' minds went blank, finally realizing the truth.

But which Immortal Emperor was this... Could it be their Southern Immortal Realm's Yin-Yang Immortal Emperor?

Lü Changsheng looked at him and stated calmly, "For obstructing the ascension channels in the borderlands for tens of thousands of years, you deserve punishment. This need not be elaborated further."

Jinyang's heart suddenly jolted!

His expression turned to one of terror!

He never imagined that such a trivial matter would catch the attention of Immortal Emperor Changsheng!

In his previous thoughts, even if Immortal Emperor Changsheng had been missing for a million years, the obstructed ascension channel was just in the borderlands of the Southern Immortal Realm. How could it possibly be noticed by the Immortal Emperor all the way in the Central Immortal Realm?

"Immortal Emperor, I beg your forgiveness! Jinyang, Jinyang...!"

Jinyang kowtowed, his tone extremely panicked, but he didn't know how to explain himself.

Outside the Heavenly Prison, it was silent.

The crowd listened to their patriarch's humble pleading, their hearts tingling.

Their patriarch was a figure of importance in the entire Southern Immortal Realm, standing at the peak in the Soaring Yang Immortal Continent.

Yet, faced with an Immortal Emperor, he was as lowly as dust.

"According to the First Immortal Edict, this Inspection Palace... should no longer exist," Lü Changsheng said.

Jinyang was terrified to the core of his soul, kowtowing desperately and wailing,

"Immortal Emperor! Jinyang harbored delusions and violated the edicts you yourself had formulated. I deserve death a thousand times over, but the Inspection Headquarters of the Soaring Yang Immortal Continent is a direct force under the Immortal Emperor's Palace of the Southern Immortal Realm. Please, Immortal Emperor, have mercy for the sake of the Yin-Yang Immortal Emperor!"

The inspectors were stunned, their gazes filled with shock. This was the long-missing Immortal Emperor Changsheng, the one shrouded in the greatest mystery in the four immortal realms.

He had returned.

Lü Changsheng smiled. "Do you think even the venerable Yin-Yang would dare to ask for my favor?"

Jinyang's heart trembled violently. Yin-Yang, the full name of the Yin-Yang Immortal Emperor - in the four immortal realms, only the one before him would dare to address him so directly.

"Immortal Emperor... I-I-I-I...!" Jinyang was completely at a loss.

He had never imagined this would happen, nor that he would incur the wrath of Immortal Emperor Changsheng.

"If you have anyone to blame, blame your own patriarch," Lü Changsheng said, sweeping his gaze over the dazed inspectors.
