"This Venerable, as the Lord of the Great Dao, is not unworthy of you all."

"Clang!!"

Finally, Lu Changsheng let out a long sigh, slowly sitting down in a cross-legged position and closing his eyes.

His body disintegrated, dissolving into countless points of light, merging into the Creation Pillar, nourishing the Creation Pillar's wounds.

The onlookers watched the gradually fading, gradually illusory blue-robed man with tears in their eyes, and bowed down in a grand manner!

"Dao Venerable!!!!!"
