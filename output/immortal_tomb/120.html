The old turtle looked at the group of soul collectors with some surprise, giving them a thumbs up and praising, "Well done, you must be the newly promoted soul collectors, right?"

The soul collectors were shocked!

How did this old man know they were newly promoted!

"We don't care who you are, please leave the soul realm, this place is not for the likes of you living beings!"

The lead soul collector tightly gripped the soul lock in his hand, sternly shouting.

Without knowing the other party's background, they dared not act rashly, and could only try to politely expel them.

Lu Changsheng smiled without saying a word.

The old turtle had initially wanted to slap these soul collectors away with a palm strike, but then thought that they were just doing their jobs, so he didn't make a move for the time being.

Just then,

A soul body swooped in from the distance, the voice arriving before the soul itself!

"Who dares to trespass the soul realm!"

The soul collectors all heaved a sigh of relief, their leader had finally arrived, they were about to be unable to hold on.

When the soul body arrived, the old turtle was slightly surprised, then revealed a meaningful smile.

"Well, if it isn't the old butler from the Moli Manor? Doing quite well for yourself after death, becoming the head of the soul collectors."

More than six hundred years ago, the old turtle had not personally witnessed the butler's death, as he had been slapped out of the spiritual realm by Lu Changsheng at the time.

But he quickly understood, this old butler must have ridden on Lu Changsheng's coattails, otherwise he would have already been reincarnated, how could he be comfortably serving as the head of the soul collectors?

The old butler stiffened, looking at the young man in blue with disbelief and joy, exclaiming, "Master!! Master!!!"

Hearing this, the soul collectors were dumbfounded, shocked one after another.

This young man in blue, it turned out he had a relationship with their leader!

The next moment, the old butler excitedly went over and respectfully bowed down, "Master, it's been so long!"

Seeing this, the soul collectors trembled, realizing that it was not the young man in blue who had a relationship with their leader, but rather their leader who had a relationship with this young man.

And this young man seemed to be of great importance, this much they could confirm.

Instinctively, they all knelt down as well.

For safety's sake, it was better to kneel first and then speak.

"It seems you've been doing well these past years," Lu Changsheng said with a smile to the old butler.

Encountering an old friend in a strange land, the old butler's heart was filled with excitement as he deeply bowed, "It's all thanks to Master's grace."

Although he didn't know exactly how powerful Lu Changsheng was, but from the Soul Emperor's earlier behavior, he could more or less understand.

Not to mention, the Soul Warden was the supreme ruler of this vast soul realm after all...

Lu Changsheng nodded slightly.

After the joy, the old butler lowered his head and tremblingly asked, "Master, over six hundred years... I've never seen Lord Chen's soul in the soul realm, he... he..."

After becoming the head of the soul collectors in this domain under the Soul Warden's instructions, he had ordered all the soul collectors to keep a close watch for Chen Dadao's soul, so that if it was found, they could properly accommodate him, so he wouldn't have to reincarnate.

But it had been over six hundred years!

The old butler was very puzzled, logically speaking, Chen Dadao should have died a long time ago.

Strangely, his soul had never been seen.

The old butler had wanted to apply to check the register of the living and the dead in the mortal realm, but with his current position, his authority was not enough.

In any case, he was very worried about Chen Dadao.

"You don't need to worry about that, after your death, Chen Dadao has already embarked on the path of cultivation," Lu Changsheng said with a light smile.
