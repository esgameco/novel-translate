In the Lord's Palace of the Black Abyss Domain,

Lu Changsheng followed Xing Tian as they walked towards a certain place.

Xing Tian's footsteps were a bit hurried, indicating his inner urgency.

Lu Changsheng casually asked with a smile, "What is your beloved daughter's name?"

Xing Tian's footsteps suddenly slowed down, realizing he had lost his composure. He then slightly bowed and replied with a smile, "Senior Lu, she takes her mother's surname, and her name is He Xitong."

Lu Changsheng nodded slightly without saying anything else.

Soon, the two arrived at a palace.

Lu Changsheng swept a glance at the palace, noticing that Xing Tian had placed a layer of seal on it. Without any doubt, this seal was quite powerful, and it would be difficult for anyone in the Heavenly Scar to break it.

Except for him, of course.

Xing Tian waved his hand, and the seal disappeared. He then made an inviting gesture, his eyes full of hope, "Senior Lu, please."

Lu Changsheng nodded and stepped in, with Xing Tian closely following behind.

Upon entering the palace, a refreshing fragrance immediately wafted into their nostrils. Lu Changsheng scanned the luxurious decorations, and his gaze finally settled on a ice bed in the center, which was emitting a white mist.

On the ice bed, there was a stunningly beautiful woman lying quietly, her hands placed on her abdomen, her eyes closed, and her brows slightly furrowed.

"Senior Lu, Tong'er, she..." Xing Tian's face turned slightly pale, and he seemed deeply pained.

Lu Changsheng gently waved his hand, then stepped up to the ice bed, his eyes fixed on the woman. There was no surprise or astonishment in his gaze.

This woman, could she be the same little Tong from the Lingchen Realm in the past? However, compared to the pure and simple little Tong from the Lingchen Realm, this woman on the ice bed was dressed in an exquisite phoenix robe, exuding a graceful and elegant aura.

Lu Changsheng tilted his head and asked, "How long has your beloved daughter been in this state?"

Xing Tian immediately replied, "Tong'er has been in this state for almost three million years..."

Lu Changsheng's eyes flickered, "You mentioned the Rebirth Flower before, and I know there is a place with an abundance of them."

Xing Tian's body suddenly trembled, and his eyes shone with a glimmer of hope!

"However..." Lu Changsheng suddenly turned around and looked at him, speaking softly, "However, the Rebirth Flower will not only fail to help your beloved daughter, but it will also harm her."

This unexpected turn of events left Xing Tian momentarily at a loss for words. He stammered, "Senior, could you please tell me why?"

Lu Changsheng walked towards the exit of the palace, asking as he went, "Do you know the reason behind your beloved daughter's current condition?"

Xing Tian glanced at the ice bed where He Xitong lay, then quickly followed Lu Changsheng. "Senior, I don't know. Three million years ago, Tong'er suddenly fell into this state."

After Xing Tian had placed the seal on the palace, Lu Changsheng said, "Her condition is something that cannot be cured by any means in the world, because... this is not something that can be interfered with by others."

"..." Xing Tian was perplexed.

"This is a Myriad Worlds Tribulation, and she must overcome it on her own." Lu Changsheng smiled as he brushed his long hair.

Myriad Worlds Tribulation? Xing Tian was even more confused. Although he considered himself experienced, he had never heard of this "Myriad Worlds Tribulation" before.

But the literal meaning was clear to him.

He did not doubt the truth of Lu Changsheng's words, for he knew that Lu Changsheng's abilities and knowledge were far beyond his own.

Xing Tian then respectfully asked, "Senior Lu, does this Myriad Worlds Tribulation take place in the realm of dreams?"

"No," Lu Changsheng shook his head and said, "The Myriad Worlds Tribulation is real."

Real?! Xing Tian was stunned.

But Tong'er's body has been under his protection all this time!

Lu Changsheng did not bother to explain Xing Tian's bewilderment and continued, "More than 1,400 years ago, I once encountered a little Tong in a small world called the Lingchen Realm..."
