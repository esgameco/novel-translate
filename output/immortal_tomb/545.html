The Tianwu Sect, what trash is that?? If these words were to spread, the entire Star Luo Empire would be in an uproar.

"Old Xu, I'm going to get my fishing gear, let's go fishing?"

Huang Yulang and the old madam, along with the girls, also hurriedly returned to the Qingqing Pavilion!

In the blink of an eye, only Wang Shu and the others were left kneeling in front of the Qingqing Pavilion.

One elder also went to carry Lin Elder, and then hurriedly left this place of trouble along with the other elders!

The painful cries of Song Ren and Lin Elder could be heard all along the way!

"So do you know what to do next?" Lu Changsheng said indifferently.

Since the other sects' elders hadn't really provoked him, Lu Changsheng naturally had no intention of killing them. He was not the type to indiscriminately slaughter the innocent.

Huh?
