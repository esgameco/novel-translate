"Shopkeeper, shopkeeper..." Su Qianghe looked worried, hesitating to speak.

Hearing this, Left Qi Ran on the side felt her tense nerves relax a bit. She didn't know why, but she had an intuition that this shopkeeper should not be provoked!

Left Qiu Shan's body trembled, and he laughed dryly, "The shopkeeper is joking."

"Joking?"

Lu Changsheng's voice paused, and he said indifferently, "Kneel down."

Left Qiu Shan suddenly raised his head, thinking he had heard wrong, but the next moment, his expression changed drastically!

In the shocked and bewildered gaze of the crowd, he bent his knees and knelt down heavily on the ground with a thud!

"Lord Qiu, you..." Yin Jiukui was stunned, but before he could finish,

Left Qiu Shan, kneeling and trembling, said, "Senior, it's Qiu Shan... I was presumptuous."
