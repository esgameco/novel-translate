Although the hundred million years in the tomb were not considered long for him, and did not delay anything, this account still needed to be settled.

But how did his strength increase so greatly during that hundred million years of slumber?

Lu Changsheng gently rubbed his fingers, his brow slightly furrowed.

His eternal life body did not require cultivation, and with the flow of the great river of time, his strength would grow slowly but steadily each day.
