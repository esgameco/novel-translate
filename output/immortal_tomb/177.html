Jiang Wumang's face suddenly stiffened. He looked at Lu Changsheng from afar, giving a bitter smile, with a hint of regret in his eyes. The last remaining hilt in his hand then fell off, and his body in the longevity robe split into two and then disintegrated into nothingness.

The heavens and the earth were stunned, and everyone swallowed their saliva in shock. Even the Jiang Clan's venerable ancestor, despite unleashing such an overwhelming might, could not withstand the sword qi of the young man in blue for even a moment!

"Ancestor! Ancestor!!"

Jiang Wuji's expression changed drastically, and the scions of the Jiang Imperial Clan also had bloodshot eyes at that instant.

A desolate atmosphere permeated the heavens and the earth.

The azure sword qi continued to advance, heading towards the Jiang Imperial Clan!

"Shopkeeper! Vengeance's son is inside... can you..."

At this moment, Left Qishan could no longer hold back, and he knelt down in front of everyone, pleading desperately.

Seeing this, Left Qizi also knelt down, and the elders of the Tiansha Mansion followed suit, gradually connecting the dots and guessing what had happened.

"It's you... Left Hengzhi!!"

Jiang Wuji in the sky suddenly fixed his gaze on Left Qishan, his eyes filled with hatred. The accidental death of his daughter Jiang Xuan had always been a thorn in his heart, and he had piled all the blame on Left Qishan.

If it weren't for this presumptuous young man, his daughter would not have died!

The Jiang Imperial Clan's elders also reacted, but at the moment, they had no time to focus on this matter - their Jiang Imperial Clan was about to perish!

Jiang Zhou heard the shout and his heart suddenly jumped. He looked at Left Qishan with a confused expression.

What was going on...?

At this moment, everyone suddenly noticed that the speed of the azure sword qi in the sky had slowed down...

Peng Fang saw this, glanced at Left Qishan, and sighed softly. He stepped forward and looked at Jiang Zhou, speaking in a solemn tone:

"He is your biological father. Back then..."

Peng Fang quickly recounted the past events involving Left Qishan and the Jiang Imperial Clan.

Everyone in the heavens and earth was stunned, not expecting such a big secret to be hidden within.

Connecting the dots, they suddenly understood.

So that's why Jiang Chu and Jiang Chong appeared in the Black Sha Domain - they were actually searching for the Tiansha Mansion's master, Left Qishan!

It turned out that the Jiang Imperial Clan's princess was accidentally killed by her own father, Jiang Wuji...

Jiang Wuji's face turned black. He never expected that at the moment of the Jiang Imperial Clan's downfall, such an ugly secret would be exposed!

The heavens were unjust!

At this time, the old matriarch of the Jiang Clan, who had been silent all along, stepped forward and shouted: "It's you, daring to climb the high branches of our Jiang Imperial Clan. I've always believed that Xuan'er was only blinded! You... are not worthy, just as you were in the past, and will always be!"

Left Qishan's face turned pale, gritting his teeth.

As for Jiang Zhou, he was dumbfounded. He stared at Left Qishan, never expecting this man to be his father...

This was something he couldn't digest at the moment, and even if he could, what did it matter? They were all going to die anyway...

"Since the truth has been revealed... then you shall all die."

At this moment, Lu Changsheng let out a light laugh. His eyes flashed, and the azure sword qi suddenly surged in speed!

Jiang Wuji and all the scions of the Jiang Imperial Clan were immediately on high alert, their expressions tense, and a glimmer of uncontrollable despair appeared in their eyes.

Seeing this, Left Qishan's eyes filled with tears, and he kowtowed frantically, pleading, "Shopkeeper, Vengeance's son... can you..."

Lu Changsheng remained expressionless, as if he had not heard anything. The azure sword qi only grew more and more chilling.

"Phew—"

Even though the Proprietor of the inn didn't target them, he was truly terrifying.

The once prosperous Jiang Clan had become history in such an absurd way...

Who could have imagined it?

Previously, in the Scar of the Heavens, the major clans were at their peak.

But from now on... everyone knew that the strongest in the Scar of the Heavens was the Proprietor of the Blue Mysterious Town's Black Calamity Domain, Lu Changsheng.
