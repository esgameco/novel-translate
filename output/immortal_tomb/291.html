In the southwest of the Ancient Wasteland King City, there were countless cultivators standing in the vast and open space.

In front of them, there was a massive sand curtain that stretched from the sky to the ground. The sand and gravel on it were rolling, making it look like a waterfall made of sand.

This was the first station of the Celestial Overlord Assembly - the Secret Realm of Sand.

In fact, the Secret Realm of Sand was not the real Celestial Overlord Assembly, it was just a screening process. The true Celestial Overlord Assembly would only begin after the selection of the top 1,500 participants.

Only the top 1,500 would have the qualifications to participate in the final Celestial Overlord Tea Gathering, where they would compete against each other to determine the top 10.

The Secret Realm of Sand was essentially prepared for the Celestial Overlords at the Immortal Ancestor Realm. Under normal circumstances, out of the top 1,500, 1,020 would be from the Divine Yang Realm and the Divine Origin Realm, while the remaining 9,000-plus Celestial Overlords would be competing for the 480 remaining spots, which was an extremely difficult challenge.

Lu Changsheng and the others had also arrived. They quietly observed the scene from a corner, and the others did not dare to speak, only discussing the matter in hushed tones and guiding Fu Shiyu and the others.

At this moment, hundreds of figures appeared in front of the sand waterfall, and all eyes turned to them.

"Wow, it turns out to be the people from the Mansion of the King of the Ancient Wasteland City!"

"That's not surprising, since the Ancient Wasteland Sword Clan has chosen the Ancient Wasteland King City as the venue for the Celestial Overlord Assembly, naturally they would let the City Lord's Mansion take full charge of it."

"The middle-aged man in the lead must be the City Lord of the Ancient Wasteland King City, Duan Jiuxiong, right?"

"It should be him. Lord Duan has always been quite mysterious and rarely makes public appearances. This Celestial Overlord Assembly has finally brought him out, which is not an easy feat."

"Hehe, Lord Duan is a peak expert under the Ancient Wasteland Ruins and the Ancient Wasteland Sword Clan. His cultivation must have reached a terrifying level..."

Lu Changsheng's eyes were closed under the bronze mask, as if he was resting. However, Li Shuryan and Liu Tu, who were in the Divine Yang Realm, looked at Duan Jiuxiong with grave expressions, sensing his overwhelming strength that seemed undefeatable. As for Fu Liancai and Liu Qing, who were only in the Divine Origin Realm, they did not feel much.

At this moment, Duan Jiuxiong took a step forward, raising his hand slightly. His expression was cold and stern, but he exuded the aura of the lord of the central great city of the Ancient Wasteland Ruins, without any sign of timidity before the countless people.

The entire venue fell silent, as everyone knew that Lord Duan was about to speak.

Duan Jiuxiong's stern face revealed a slight smile as he said, "I am Duan Jiuxiong, the City Lord of the Ancient Wasteland King City. I believe many of you here are familiar with me, so I won't introduce myself further."

Numerous voices of praise immediately arose:

"Lord Duan is too humble. In our Ancient Wasteland Ruins, there is only one King City, so who wouldn't know Lord Duan?"

"Lord Duan, you are too modest. Rest assured, we will all behave properly and maintain good order during the Celestial Overlord Assembly. Whoever dares to cause trouble, I'll be the first to deal with them!"

"Lord Duan, please start already. We can't wait any longer!"

Hearing this, Duan Jiuxiong let out a hearty laugh and waved his hand, shouting, "All qualified Celestial Overlords, enter!"

In the next moment, countless streams of light surged forward from the vast sea of people.
