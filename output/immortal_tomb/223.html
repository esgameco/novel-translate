The disgruntled voice of Su Qianghe rang out,

The three, Left Choushan, stood in the distance, suppressing their laughter as they watched the flushed-faced Su Qianghe being subdued.

He truly cares more about the wine than his own life.

Soon, they saw Su Qianghe frantically gesturing towards them, and they awkwardly walked over.

"Ahem, Grand Elder, the Third Elder was just momentarily tempted by the taste. Please forgive his small transgression."

Peng Fang wiped the wine from the corner of his mouth, glanced at Su Qianghe, withdrew the suppression, and said indifferently,

"It's been ten years, Third Elder, you seem to have always had a lot of opinions about me, the Grand Elder."

Hearing this, the panting Su Qianghe grumbled unhappily, "Grand Elder, you're using your position to oppress us. If we report this to the Patriarch..."

Upon hearing this, Peng Fang laughed, both proudly and disdainfully, "Hmph, do you really have that chance? In all these years, when has the Patriarch ever summoned you individually? The Patriarch has always had me, the Grand Elder, convey his instructions, so don't try to threaten me with that again, it's useless!"

Peng Fang had become more cunning. Ever since he discovered that Lu Changsheng never summoned Left Choushan and the others, he has been drinking his wine with a clear conscience, occasionally generously sharing a bit with the four of them.

After all, these four Elders can't see the Patriarch, can't overpower him, and their status and authority aren't as high as the Grand Elder's.

Heh, he can handle them as he pleases.

Su Qianghe paused, then quietly said, "Grand Elder, at least give us two jugs? Giving us one cup at a time, what's the point? Aren't you just teasing us to death?"

Hearing this, Peng Fang's eyes rolled, and suddenly three jugs of wine appeared in his hand, which he pushed in front of Left Choushan and the others, smiling, "Ah, Second Elder, Fourth Elder, Fifth Elder, here, one jug each, but don't let the Third Elder drink any."

Left Choushan and the others were delighted and immediately agreed.

Su Qianghe's face looked like he had eaten shit. Ah, he finally realized that the Grand Elder was deliberately targeting him!

Could it be that he still harbors a grudge over the incident ten years ago when Su Qianghe exposed his hidden stash of fine wine in the extreme cold ice field?

What a narrow-minded person!

"Hmph! I'm not playing with you guys anymore!"

Su Qianghe angrily shook his sleeve and turned to leave.

Just as he turned around, Su Qianghe saw the blue-robed man who had appeared out of nowhere, and he paused in surprise, then quickly knelt down in reverence, "Patriarch!!"

Peng Fang, who had not yet swallowed the wine in his throat, choked a bit, his face changing. He and Left Choushan quickly came over and respectfully knelt down, "Patriarch."

Lu Changsheng looked at the eager-eyed Su Qianghe, then at the unnaturally pale Peng Fang, and finally sniffed the air, detecting the scent of wine. He then came and sat down to the side, smiling, "Grand Elder, I see your complexion is ruddy and you reek of wine. It seems you've been living quite comfortably of late."
