The junior cultivators were eager, chattering noisily, pushing and shoving one another.

Seeing this, Gu Helin felt slightly embarrassed, and sighed inwardly that the ascension tribulation of a great cultivator could actually trigger such an economic boom, a wave of scrambling for spirit stones.

"Uh, sir, what do you think?" Gu Helin looked over at Lu Changsheng, unable to make the decision himself.

If the Patriarch Turtle could speak, Gu Helin knew that these junior cultivators would have long been driven away by him.

"Uh..." The crowd of cultivators were stunned, then glared hatefully at the little black guy, quickly bowing and retreating.

Since the senior predecessors had chosen, they had no choice but to obey, daring not to cause any trouble.

The little black guy pointed at himself excitedly, "Senior, can I really?"

Gu Helin smiled, "Of course you can. We are indeed not very familiar with the Western Regions, so let you lead the way."

He then handed over a mid-grade spirit stone.

The little black guy was stunned, trembling as he accepted it. He had been doing odd jobs outside the Meiyu Trade Association for two and a half years, but this was the most spirit stones he had ever received. "Senior, isn't this too much? Little black guy can't bear it."

Gu Helin smiled, "It's fine, as long as you guide and introduce the Western Regions well for the sir, the spirit stones won't be lacking for you!"

"Sir?" Hearing this, the little black guy instinctively looked at the leading Lu Changsheng, quickly cupping his hands and greeting, "Greetings, sir!"

The little black guy walked beside them, nodding and bowing as he introduced, his expression full of excitement, clearly the mid-grade spirit stone had given him great motivation!

Along the way, Old Turtle wanted to chat with the little black guy a few words, but alas, he was almost going crazy.

Chu Xiran, on the other hand, had been quietly standing by the side, not even bothering to say a word.

"Hehe, little black guy, I suppose the sir must be tired now. Do you know if the Meiyu Trade Association has any suitable resting places? Remember, it should be quiet."

Gu Helin said with a smile.

"Of course there is! For quiet places, it must be the Lateautumn Residence. Senior predecessors, follow little black guy."

The little black guy performed a salute, then bounced and skipped as he led the way, looking like a happy chicken.

Gu Helin's eyes lit up. Having a local guide was indeed more convenient, then he looked at Lu Changsheng.

"Then let's go to the Lateautumn Residence. As usual, we'll rest there for two days, and then go to the Xiaoyao Sect."
