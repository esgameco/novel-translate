There were only two days left before the start of the Nine Realms Sect Competition.

At this very moment, a gentle and low-pitched voice descended from the sky in the Extreme Cold Glacier,

"Attention all sects, there are only two days left, everyone, keep up the effort..."

Hearing this, the sects rushing through the Extreme Cold Glacier were stunned.

The voice that made the announcement used to be extremely stern and loud, but why was it so gentle this time?

This left the sects completely perplexed.

However, they had no time to dwell on this, as the time they had left was truly limited.

.........

On the afternoon of the second-to-last day, sects gradually arrived at the central area one after another.

Everyone was disheveled and in disarray.

When they saw the magnificent palace suspended in the air before them, they finally heaved a sigh of relief.

They began to sit down and restore their spiritual energy.

Among them, the Blood Flame Sect and the Rainbow Pavilion had also successfully arrived, but the Forestry Gate was still nowhere to be seen.

Finally, at the very last moment of the final day,

just as the Carefree Sect entered,

Boom!!

A bell suddenly rang out.

Many sects that had not yet arrived were immediately dejected, especially those who were just short of the destination, who were utterly dispirited.

The members of the Carefree Sect looked around, their hearts slightly shocked,

because the total number of sects that had arrived before the deadline was less than one-tenth of the initial number.

Just traversing the Extreme Cold Glacier had eliminated more than nine-tenths of the sects!

"The sects that did not make it to the central area can either retreat or continue to come here to observe. Although they have lost the qualification, they have gained experience, and I believe that in the next Grand Competition of the Myriad Sects, you will make further progress," a gentle voice resounded from the sky, reaching the ears of all the sects.

Hearing this, the countless sects that had lost their qualification in the Extreme Cold Glacier quickly calmed their emotions and continued towards the central area. Since they had come this far, they naturally couldn't just turn back, and they had to observe the competition.

.........

In the central area of the Extreme Cold Glacier, less than a thousand sects stood solemnly, their eyes frantically scrutinizing each other, each harboring their own thoughts.

After all, the sects present at this moment were their true opponents!

Outside the central area, there were even more sects observing, dense and numerous, nearly ten times the number inside.

In the Blood Flame Sect's camp,

"Sect Leader, the Carefree Sect has also successfully arrived!"

Elder Chen He, with the corner of his eye, spotted the Carefree Sect, and his heart was shaken as he transmitted a message.

"I've seen it," Fan Hong's face was grave as he said, "The Carefree Sect has indeed been hiding their true strength..."

At this moment,

the doors of the suspended palace in front opened, and seven figures slowly walked out.

Seeing this, everyone was startled.

Soon, the seven people stopped, and the one in the lead, Fu Ming-yan, took a step forward, looking down at all the sects.

His gaze swept across everyone at an incredible speed, and he heaved a sigh of relief when he did not see the strange and powerful man in blue.

Then he smiled faintly and said, "I am the Ninth Elder of the Mysterious Fire Sect, and I will be presiding over this Nine Realms Sect Competition."

The Mysterious Fire Sect, a First-Rank Sect!!

Everyone present had their pupils constrict, their hearts pounding in fear.

No one had expected that this Nine Realms Sect Competition would be presided over by an Elder of the Mysterious Fire Sect, which was such a high-ranking sect!

"Hehe, the rewards for the top hundred in this Nine Realms Sect Competition will be bestowed by my Mysterious Fire Sect," Fu Ming-yan waved his hand, about to continue speaking.
