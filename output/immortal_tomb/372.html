Experts at the Mystic Immortal Realm are invaluable to any tribe. It can be said that from now on, the Human Tribe will definitely become the public enemy of all tribes in the Divine-Demonic Battlefield...

What merit or virtue do the humans possess?

Furthermore, according to Fang Yin's description, the Immortal Dao Companion's strength is absolutely above the Mystic Immortal Realm! Even in the Mystic Immortal Realm, he is an extraordinary existence.

Ah, so that's how it is.

This time, the humans have provoked the wrath of all tribes. It would be impossible to appease them. Even if they hand over the Crystal Hearts, it would not change the fate of their tribe's annihilation...

"Lord Fengxing, the matter has already happened, it is irreversible. The Crystal Hearts... we cannot hand them over!" Han Jinlong transmitted his voice solemnly.

Jiang Wuxin narrowed his eyes and nodded slightly. The Gold Wheel King's thoughts were the same as his.

Even if they hand over the Crystal Hearts, it would not change the fate awaiting the Human Tribe... There's no need for that.

It's just that, the Immortal Dao Companion is really...

Han Jinlong and Jiang Wuxin had a somewhat numb expression, thinking about Luo Chengsheng trapped in the tower.

They felt a bit uneasy about it.

No wonder the Immortal Dao Companion had previously asked them what would happen if all the Crystal Hearts were seized...

Now they understood. It turns out that he had been planning this all along.

The Immortal Dao Companion's descent upon the Human Tribe with the Eternal Longevity Body was a great blessing, but soon after, he brought a great calamity upon them...

Jiang Wuxin and Han Jinlong did not respond to Shen Luo, but instead glanced at the towering pagoda before them, and simultaneously gritted their teeth, saying, "Return to the tribe!"

Thereafter, the humans quickly departed under the gloomy gazes of the other tribes. Due to the rules, no one hindered their departure.

However, the eyes of the surrounding tribes were flickering with anticipation.

They had already imagined the scene of the Human Tribe being suppressed by all the tribes.
