The city wall was quiet at the moment.

The group of ancient cranes looked blankly at the uniquely-looking hunchbacked old man...

Cen Yuezhen, when did such a character come here again?

Not to mention anything else, just that move earlier was enough to shock them!

After all, the one in front of them was an eighth-level demonic cultivator on par with an Infant Celestial Ancestor!

"Who are you? How dare you interfere in the affairs of my demonic realm?!"

The porcupine growled hoarsely, he and the Heavenly Wolf were now facing a formidable enemy!

Because they couldn't see through this hunchbacked old man at all!

The old turtle stroked his beard, put his hands behind his back, his expression calm and his tone disdainful,

"How dare I interfere in the affairs of the demonic realm? If it weren't for not wanting that nuisance Hunhun to buzz in my ear like an annoying bee, just based on your actions today in disturbing my master's business, your demonic realm... even if it's overturned a hundred times, it wouldn't be enough to appease it!"

At these words,

Not only Heavenly Wolf and Porcupine, even the disciples like Ancient Crane Lin and Tong Xin were all shaken violently!

Outrageous, too outrageous!

Leaving aside the credibility of what this hunchbacked old man said, Ancient Crane Lin was now more curious about who this master he mentioned was?

Apart from that, just based on his earlier ability to counterattack an eighth-level demonic cultivator, it was enough to prove his strength, then his master behind him...

Terrifying to even think about.

"Arrogant!!!"

The porcupine glared at the old turtle and roared.

"Ridiculous words, overturning our demonic realm a hundred times? Not to mention our demonic lords, even if any one of the five great demonic kings came out, they could crush you!" Heavenly Wolf also growled unhappily.

But undeniably, their hearts were extremely grave at the moment.

However, even if they couldn't beat him, they still thought the other party definitely couldn't keep them here!

"Aiya, you two little..."

The old turtle raised an eyebrow, about to say something,

"Do you believe I'll seal your mouth? Bickering with two little demons, don't you find it embarrassing?"

A cool voice suddenly came from the direction of the Magistrate's Mansion...

The old turtle was startled and kept bowing and scraping, the calm expression on his face replaced by an awkward smile,

"Hehe, yes, yes, master, don't be angry."

Seeing the hunchbacked old man who had just been so arrogant now acting like a fawning dog,

Everyone was dumbfounded.

The voice of the master...

This powerful hunchbacked old man, was actually the servant of the master?!

The master he had mentioned earlier, was actually the master...

But why had they never seen this hunchbacked old man before?

"You, you..."

Porcupine and Heavenly Wolf snapped out of their daze, their gazes trembling as they looked towards the Magistrate's Mansion,

They were completely panicked now.

This hunchbacked old man... the master he had spoken of earlier actually existed, and was right here in this town!

If this hunchbacked old man was so strong, they couldn't even imagine how powerful his master must be?

"Run!!!"

Without hesitation, Porcupine and Heavenly Wolf retreated in unison!

"Want to run? Did you ask old turtle first? You two little..."

The old turtle's gaze was indifferent, with a wave of his hand, two streams of dark green light shot out, their speed terrifying!

Whoosh!!

Porcupine and Heavenly Wolf, in the midst of their escape, suddenly sensed something, and turned back abruptly, their faces changing drastically!!

The terrifying power contained in those two streams of dark green light was beyond their comprehension!

They had greatly underestimated this hunchbacked old man!

"You, who are you, no, we've cultivated for thousands of years to successfully transform, don't kill us, don't!!!"

Heavenly Wolf and Porcupine's eyes finally revealed infinite terror! Their tone even had a begging quality.

But,

It was too late.

The dark green light flashed and disappeared in an instant, and they suddenly lost consciousness, their bodies being ruthlessly destroyed into nothingness.
