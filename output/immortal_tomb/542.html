After a simple lunch, the three of them - Han Yue, Lü Changsheng, and the others - paid their respects to Lü Changsheng and quickly returned to Changsheng Academy.

"Mr. Lü... about Xuan..."

Lü Changsheng waved his hand, "It's nothing, let's just enjoy the music."

"Yes."

Huang Yulang quickly responded and sat down to accompany Lü Changsheng, drinking wine and listening to the music, though he sighed inwardly from time to time.

Lü Changsheng swirled the wine in his cup, listening to the music, his gaze curiously fixed on the direction outside the town.

At the same time, outside Jixiang Town, an uninvited group of sixteen people, mostly elders and middle-aged men, had arrived.

These people were the senior members of the various sects, apart from Elder Wangsu and Steward Song from the Tianwu Sect.

"Hehe, you all arrived quite quickly..."

Wangsu looked around, hands behind his back, and chuckled coldly.

As an elder of the number one sect in the Star Luo Empire, he now had an aura of a big shot in front of the other sect members.

However, Wangsu was not being arrogant, as he was only an outer sect elder of the Tianwu Sect.

The other sects had all sent their inner sect elders, which showed how much they valued the Earth Martial Vein.

Wangsu did not look down on these people, but he also did not take them too seriously, as he had the backing of the Tianwu Sect.

But behind Wangsu, Steward Song's expression became worried!

He was concerned that in order to obtain the Earth Martial Vein, these sect elders might not buy the Tianwu Sect's story, and if that happened, it would be disastrous!

If the sects found out that Wangsu and he had deliberately concealed the existence of the Earth Martial Vein for their own benefit... not only Steward Song, but even Elder Wangsu would be in trouble.

Alas, they had acted too hastily! Now that it had come to this, they could only take it one step at a time and hope the sects would remain rational.

"Hehe, so it's Elder Wangsu. We've long heard of the renowned name of Elder Wangsu from the outer sect of the Tianwu Sect, and now that we've met, you truly live up to your reputation."

"Elder Wangsu, we've heard that a shocking three Earth Martial Veins have appeared in this small town, but your Tianwu Sect has only brought back one, leaving the other two behind. Could it be that you've intentionally left them for us other sects?"

"Haha haha, how generous! No wonder the Tianwu Sect is the number one martial sect in Star Luo Empire!"

"Hehe, Elder Lin, the way you put it, it seems the Tianwu Sect has always taken most of the Earth Martial Veins that have appeared in the Star Luo Empire. It looks like this time, the Tianwu Sect is willing to let the other sects have a taste."

"..."

The various sect elders discussed among themselves, their tones sarcastic and mocking.

Normally, they would not dare to speak to the Tianwu Sect in such a way.

But this time was different.

For the sake of these two Earth Martial Veins, the sects had temporarily formed an alliance.

No matter what, they could not let the Tianwu Sect snatch these two Earth Martial Veins away again!

At this moment, the sect elders also breathed a sigh of relief.

As seasoned elders, they naturally had deep schemes. They could see at a glance that Wangsu had not reported these two Earth Martial Veins to his sect.

This Wangsu, he has great ambitions, wanting to make a big contribution, hehe...

Otherwise, with the Earth Martial Veins being two in number, the Tianwu Sect would not have just left them behind, would they?
