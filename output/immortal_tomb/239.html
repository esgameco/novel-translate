If the senior in green was determined to kill him, then he would perish today with no chance of survival.

But if the senior gave him a chance to make amends, not only would he survive, he might even be able to get close to the senior.

Just from drinking that tea, Yang Qiu knew that this senior in green had cultivation at an extremely high level, the likes of which he had never seen before!

In the primordial Divine Continent, to be so fortunate as to encounter such a powerful figure in this small domain of Tuochang was an opportunity to defy fate!

"Celestial precious tea?"

Lu Changsheng smiled mysteriously. He picked up the teacup and slowly poured the tea onto the ground. "This is just ordinary tea I use to entertain patients, how can it be precious?"

Hearing the sound of the tea hitting the ground, Yang Qiu looked up in astonishment, staring dumbly at the tea spilled in front of him. He was extremely pained!

To just... pour out the celestial precious tea like that?

If the conditions were different, he would have wanted to lick the tea off the ground clean.

He swallowed hard and said hoarsely, "This tea may be ordinary to a great cultivator like you, but to a humble disciple like me... it is a priceless treasure."

As the sect master of the Yang Yuan Sect, he felt he had used up all his flattery today in Yun Yu Town.

Yang Qiu didn't expect that his talent for flattery would come out so naturally and elegantly at this critical moment.

Normally, whenever the elders of his sect tried to flatter him, he would feel disdainful. But today...

He had thought that as the sect master, he could maintain a dignified bearing in front of anyone in Tuochang.

He realized he was wrong, and very, very wrong!

When faced with a true supreme expert, he not only failed to remain calm, but couldn't help but engage in excessive flattery.

"How do you plan to make amends?" Lu Changsheng asked leisurely.

Yang Qiu was stunned for a moment, then burst into joy. He excitedly said, "Senior, I will personally bring the despicable Su Lianyi to you for you to deal with!! That wretch has offended you, deserving of death a thousand times over. Her sect won't be able to protect her either!!"
