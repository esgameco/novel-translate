Seeing that Fu Liangcai's face had turned slightly gloomy, Ouyang Nuo's eyebrows furrowed, and he pretended to be sorrowful, saying:

"Patriarch Fu, your Fu Clan has suddenly received such tragic news, and as the Patriarch, I deeply feel the sorrow... Ah, in those years, the old ancestor of the Fu Clan was so graceful, but in the end, he could not escape the ravages of time..."

Beside them, Xu Chong and Gao Teng's mouths twitched.

This Ouyang Nuo, he really kills people and then rubs salt in the wound.

He was clearly harboring evil intentions, yet he still put on this yin-yang weird act... Who was laughing so happily just now?

Fu Liangcai raised his eyebrows, seeing that Ouyang Nuo actually made a joke about the recently departed old ancestor, and his anger rose from his heart as he shouted:

"Ouyang Nuo, don't pretend anymore!"

Ouyang Nuo paused, then his expression quickly changed from sorrow to joy, and he smiled brilliantly:

"Alright, then I won't pretend anymore, Patriarch Fu. Let's open the window and speak plainly. I'm sure you know why we're here, so what is your Fu Clan's attitude?"

The crowd was stunned, knowing that the main event was about to begin!

"Attitude?" Fu Liangcai grinned and said, "If you want to attack, then just attack, no need for so much nonsense. My Fu Clan will never surrender, even if we are destroyed, we will make you suffer some losses!"

After saying this, he coldly waved his sleeve and disappeared into the Fu Clan's main gate!

The crowd was in an uproar.

Ouyang Nuo's face suddenly turned gloomy. This Fu Liangcai... really has a good backbone!

What he just said, of course, he had no intention of sparing the Fu Clan,

he just... wanted to see Fu Liangcai beg for mercy.

What a pity.

Ouyang Nuo suddenly turned his head and looked at Xu Chong and Gao Teng, "You two, are you willing to follow my Ouyang Clan to attack the Fu Clan?"

Xu Chong and Gao Teng hesitated when they heard this.

"Heh, you two don't worry, I won't let you work for nothing. After the Fu Clan's resources are divided, each of your clans will get one-tenth!" Ouyang Nuo raised one finger and said lightly.

"Okay!!" Xu Chong and Gao Teng no longer hesitated and immediately agreed.

Getting one-tenth is also a lot, and for them, it's like finding free money! There's absolutely no risk!

"Haha, great! Wonderful!"

Ouyang Nuo laughed wildly, then looked at the Fu Clan in front of him and shouted loudly:

"Since this Fu Liangcai is so unreasonable, then today the entire Fu Clan... will be wiped out without leaving a single chicken or dog!!"

Ouyang Nuo's loud shout was very resonant,

even Li Shuyan, who was dozing off in the guest room outside, heard it.

He coughed awkwardly and looked fearfully at the room...

This person is arrogant, a real "wiping out without leaving a single chicken or dog".

At the same time!

Fu Liangcai's loud shout also echoed in this space: "Activate the Clan Defense Formation!"

"Rumble—Whoosh!"

Accompanied by countless multicolored light beams shooting in all directions of the Fu Clan, intersecting with each other, a thick barrier quickly formed like a giant bowl covering the Fu Clan.

Ouyang Nuo saw this scene, his heart slightly constricted. He was very familiar with the Clan Defense Formation, his Ouyang Clan also had one.

It's precisely because he's familiar with it that he knows how difficult it is to break.

Strange, what is that Liu Qing fellow doing? He hasn't arrived yet, and if it weren't for waiting for Liu Qing, he would have started the attack long ago!!

Just then, several Liu Clan elders led a group of Liu Clan disciples, including Liu Xing, finally arrived.

"Where's Liu Qing? What are you Liu Clan up to?"

Ouyang Nuo's heart relaxed a bit when he saw this, and he immediately frowned and shouted.

"Clan Leader Ouyang, sorry about that, our clan had a bit of a situation, and the Clan Leader is still dealing with it, so he asked us and the Young Master to come first. He said he'll hurry over as soon as he's done." The Liu Clan elder said apologetically with a bow.

"That Liu Qing fellow, really dropping the ball at a critical moment." Ouyang Nuo snorted coldly, waving his hand, "Nevermind, we won't wait for him anymore. Attacking this Clan Defense Formation will take a bit more time without him, but it's no big deal."

"However, I'll tell you all, if Liu Qing is late, when it's time to divide the spoils of the Fu Clan, your Liu Clan will get one-tenth less!"

The few Liu Clan elders' expressions changed slightly, with some subtle discontent.

"Hmph." Ouyang Nuo coldly snorted on the surface, but was actually laughing inwardly.

Liu Qing, don't blame me, this one-tenth is something you let slip yourself.

The next moment, he suddenly soared into the air, his robe fluttering.
