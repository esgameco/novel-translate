In the spacious main hall, Tiger Battle and Jie Wuqing were sitting across from each other.

Jie Wuqing said indifferently, "Tiger Battle, stand down."

"I've met Lu Changsheng, but I can't figure out his intentions, so it's best not to act rashly for now."

"Lu Changsheng? You mean that mysterious cultivator the Sovereign encountered in the Immortal Mausoleum?"

"..."

Jie Wuqing fell silent.

She didn't know why this Tiger Battle always kept coming back to the topic of the turtle.

Jie Wuqing didn't say anything else, but in Tiger Battle's puzzled gaze, she raised her right sleeve with her left hand and shook it, revealing it to be empty.

Tiger Battle abruptly stood up, asking with surprise, "Your right arm..."

"Was severed by that Lu Changsheng, and there is a strange power lingering around the stump, so my arm won't grow back or reattach," Jie Wuqing said indifferently.

Tiger Battle slowly sat back down, saying solemnly, "I didn't expect there to be such a formidable expert hidden in the Spiritual Dust Realm. But don't worry, when we launch the war, I will personally help you get your revenge and make that Lu Changsheng pay the price."

Jie Wuqing was taken aback. She didn't expect that after she had said all that, Tiger Battle would still be plotting to seize the Immortal Mausoleum.

"Do you think if Lu Changsheng intervenes, we can still succeed?" Jie Wuqing said through gritted teeth.

Tiger Battle smiled mysteriously, "Don't worry, I've recently allied with a partner, and Zhang Youdao is about to ascend soon. At that time, conquering the Spiritual Dust Realm will be a piece of cake."

"Do you remember that disappeared tomb in the Immortal Mausoleum group? That Lu Changsheng came out from there!" Jie Wuqing suddenly said emphatically.

Tiger Battle was stunned for a moment, then threw his head back and laughed heartily, "Hahaha!"

Jie Wuqing's expression changed slightly, "You!"

"Who told you that?" Tiger Battle asked with a smile.

"I had guessed it before, and then he confirmed it for me," Jie Wuqing said expressionlessly.

Clap, clap, clap, clap, clap, clap...

Tiger Battle clapped his hands, his face serious, "Mm-hmm, yes, whatever he says is the truth, but such a ridiculous thing, even you, the mighty Demon Sovereign, actually believe it... Tsk tsk."

Bang!

Jie Wuqing slammed her hand on the table and stood up, coldly saying, "Do as you please, if you want to attack, then go ahead, this time the Demon Realm will not interfere!"

After saying that, she was about to leave.

At this moment, Tiger Battle sneered, "Jie Wuqing, the matter of plotting the Immortal Mausoleum was first brought up by you, and now it's you who wants to back out. Isn't that laughable?"

Jie Wuqing paused.

Tiger Battle sneered, "You can back out if you want, I won't force you, but you have to promise me one thing."

"What is it?"

Tiger Battle said indifferently, "When I conquer the Spiritual Dust Realm and it's time to break open the Immortal Mausoleum, I need to borrow your blood-gathering magic technique. However, even if the Immortal Mausoleum is opened, you can only get a tenth of the secrets inside."

Jie Wuqing's eyes flashed as she said, "We'll talk about it again when you've conquered the Spiritual Dust Realm."

After saying that, her figure disappeared.

Tiger Battle fell silent for a moment, his face full of stubble.
