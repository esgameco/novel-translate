The dense and gloomy forest surrounded them.

"Ugh..."

At this moment, they no longer had any thoughts of competing for points. Survival was the top priority!

Soon, the disciples from the three sects had all gathered. Some were supporting each other, while others were carrying the bodies of their fallen fellow disciples. Many had died in the previous battle between the three sects.

Xiao Yan waved his hand and shouted, "Let's go..."

The disciples of the three sects heaved a sigh of relief. They had thought it might be someone else, but it turned out to be this person.

They remembered that during the previous battle between the three sects, Xiao Yan had sought help from this person, but he had not responded.

Thinking about this, the Tianlong Sect disciples looked at Luo Changsheng with a slightly displeased expression.

Xiao Yan quietly glanced at Luo Changsheng, his gaze flickering, as if he was pondering something.

Upon hearing this, the Tianlong Sect disciples were not very happy, wondering why they should bring this person along.
