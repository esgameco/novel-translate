Unbelievable!

Two disciples have followed the Immortal Emperor Changsheng!

The people's gazes towards Ancient Crane Forest became increasingly cautious.

Regarding this, Ancient Crane Forest sighed slightly, knowing that this was all because of the Master's doing.

A person, even with the slightest connection to Immortal Emperor Changsheng, would likely be able to walk arrogantly in the Immortal Realm.

Of course, Ancient Crane Forest was not the kind of person to bully others with his power.

He patted Chen Dadao and then looked at the Sect Master of the White Moon Sect, bowing and asking, "Sect Master, should my disciple Dadao be able to become an inner disciple?"

"No!"

The Sect Master of the White Moon Sect's expression changed slightly as he refused.

He then said, "Craneforest, making your disciple an inner disciple would be wasting his talents. In our sect's view, he should directly be promoted to become a core disciple!"

The crowd all nodded and smiled, agreeing.

Ancient Crane Forest was stunned.

Core disciple, that was already the highest level of White Moon Sect disciples...

He had struggled for over a thousand years, and was only now a true disciple...

Yet this little Chen Dadao had surpassed him?

Moreover, Chen Dadao was a newcomer, suddenly becoming a core disciple, he would surely become the target of many. This might not be good for him.

Seeming to know what Ancient Crane Forest was thinking, the Sect Master hurriedly said sternly, "Craneforest, don't worry, this Sect Master knows what you're thinking. Today, even the Patriarch is here, and the Elders are also here. Let's see who dares to have any objections to your disciple becoming a core disciple! Anyone with objections will be expelled from the sect!"

After saying this, he tentatively asked, "Craneforest, the sect is currently lacking an Elder. Would you like to take on that role?"

The Sect Master was a bit worried, fearing that Ancient Crane Forest might want the position of Sect Master. If he did, he would have to give it to him.

Seeing the Sect Master say this, Ancient Crane Forest could only acquiesce, immediately saying, "Since the Sect Master has said so, then let my disciple become a core disciple."

Without any resistance, becoming a core disciple was naturally excellent. Not to mention the other privileges, the resources would be greatly tilted in his favor.

As for the Elder position...

Ancient Crane Forest also nodded in agreement. Although he was not one to fight, he was not stupid. Sometimes borrowing the Master's influence could allow him to live more comfortably.

He even felt that if he had proposed to become the Sect Master, the Sect Master, out of deference to the Master's might, might have agreed...

"Haha, good!" The Sect Master heaved a sigh of relief, affectionately putting his arm around Ancient Crane Forest's shoulder and laughing, "Craneforest, tomorrow our sect will hold a promotion ceremony for you and your disciple. At that time, let all the influential figures in the Langtian Domain come to observe!"

So quickly?

Ancient Crane Forest was shocked and asked puzzledly, "Sect Master, my disciple has just arrived. Shouldn't he first mine for three years according to the rules?"

The Sect Master: "Rules are dead."

The crowd: "Yes, Elder Crane, rules are dead."

Ancient Crane Forest: "..."

In the Central Immortal Realm.

"We pay our respects to the Immortal Emperor Changsheng!!!"

The unified respectful exclamation resounded in the supremely magnificent Immortal Emperor's Palace!

In a corner of the palace,

Tongxin and Lin Dong were hiding behind a golden pillar, their heads poking out, watching the scene with awe and being completely immersed in it.

Before their eyes were hundreds of kneeling figures, each of whose auras were overwhelming, each in the most reverential posture.

Above the palace, a figure in azure robes stood tall.

It was not Luchang Sheng, but who else could it be.

Luchang Sheng looked down upon the many figures, saying indifferently, "Today, this Emperor has only one matter, and that is to abdicate the position of Immortal Emperor of the Central Immortal Realm."

As the words fell, all the people in the palace trembled in their kneeling posture, not daring to speak.

In the other four Immortal Realms, the people present would have the right to speak and dare to speak. But in the Central Immortal Realm, no one dared!

The difference between Immortal Emperors was clear at a glance.

Luchang Sheng's supreme majesty was evident.

Luchang Sheng finished speaking, and with a wave of his hand, the Immortal Emperor's Decree that had been hanging high in the palace suddenly flew into his hand.

The Immortal Emperor's Decree trembled in his hand, as if wanting to express something.

Luchang Sheng smiled slightly, "Do you know the purpose of the Immortal Emperor's Decree? It is to assist one Immortal Emperor after another."

Time flowed on, never ceasing.

In the blink of an eye, another ten years had passed.

This was the two thousand and eleventh year since Luchang Sheng had emerged from the Immortal Tomb in the Mortal Realm.

The Heavenly Scar, this was the name of a certain realm, a dazzling world.

In the boundless azure sky, there was a rift that stretched from an unknown origin to an unknown destination, as it had been since ancient times.

No matter when or where, as long as one looked up, they could see the black rift in the sky, which looked as if it had been cleaved by a sword.

It was said that if one could no longer see the rift in the sky, it meant they were no longer within the Heavenly Scar realm.

However, this was just a legend, for the Heavenly Scar was too vast, and so far, no one had been heard of leaving it.

As for whether those powerful figures had left, that was unknown.

To the east of the Heavenly Scar, there was a notorious land of chaos, known as the Black Calamity Domain, where countless ruthless and evil people gathered.

The name "Black Calamity Domain" struck fear in the hearts of ordinary people.

Common folk dared not venture there.

Today, in a small town within the Black Calamity Domain, a new inn had opened for business.

The inn was not large, even a bit small, but its name was boldly arrogant!

It was called... the Longevity Inn?
