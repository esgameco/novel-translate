We have come to offer you a toast."

As the saying goes, "Don't slap a smiling face," Lu Changsheng also nodded slightly at them, but did not raise his cup.

"Don't waste your effort, your innate qualities are not suitable for cultivating immortality," the silent Lu Changsheng suddenly put down his cup and said slowly while looking at Chen Dadao.

Chen Dadao and the steward were suddenly struck with dread!

The immortal truly lived up to his reputation, as he had already known their purpose even before they had said anything!

But soon, the composed Chen Dadao's pupils contracted, and he trembled as he asked in a low voice, "Sir, am I really not suitable for cultivating immortality?"

Lu Changsheng nodded slightly.

Chen Dadao immediately staggered, his eyes losing their luster.

"Magistrate..." The steward behind him hurriedly supported him.

The commotion naturally attracted the attention of the other patrons, as well as the innkeeper and the waiter.

The other patrons first looked at Lu Changsheng with reverence, then curiously at the tightly wrapped Chen Dadao and the steward, not knowing the meaning of their actions.

The innkeeper frowned slightly, as someone had disturbed the young man in blue robes while he was drinking, so he discreetly signaled the waiter.

The waiter hurriedly went over and frowned at Chen Dadao and the steward, saying, "Gentlemen, please do not disturb the other patrons' drinking."

"It's fine," Lu Changsheng said softly.

The waiter paused, then silently withdrew.

"Sir, do you have a place to rest in this Lianyu Town? If not, may we invite you to our humble abode to stay for a while?"

Although Chen Dadao's heart was dejected, he still thought that if he could invite the immortal to his residence, it might be a rare opportunity.
