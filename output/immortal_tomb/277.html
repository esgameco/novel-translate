Outside the Fu clan, in the distance was a vast crowd of onlookers, their eyes flickering as they watched.

In front of the main gate of the Fu clan stood the Xu clan and the Gao clan, with several thousand people from each clan, mostly high-ranking and elite combat forces.

"Hehe, Xu Chong, Gao Teng, are you two so impatient?"

Suddenly, Fu Liangcai appeared on the steps of the Fu clan's main gate, sneering at the two leaders below.

The patriarch of the Fu clan has come out!

The crowd stirred, their eyes fixed on Fu Liangcai, wanting to see a trace of trepidation on his face.

But to their disappointment, even though the Fu clan was in dire straits, Fu Liangcai's expression remained calm.

"Hehe, Patriarch Fu, your Fu clan is declining, no longer worthy of being one of the three great clans of Jinyun City. You should just abdicate and make way for others, so as not to trouble us..." Gao Teng said with a cold smile on his lips.

Xu Chong also had a mocking expression, but his heart was heavy.

He discreetly observed the overall situation of the Fu clan out of the corner of his eye.

He and Gao Teng had not attacked immediately because they feared the Fu clan's protective formations.

After all, the Fu clan was one of the three great clans, and even in their decline, they could not be taken lightly.

Fu Liangcai naturally knew what they were thinking, so he replied calmly, "Abdication is impossible... If you have the strength, just attack us directly, why the empty talk?"

After saying this, he glanced in the direction of the Ouyang and Liu clans in the city, his gaze heavy with gravity.

To be honest, Xu and Gao clans were not even on Fu Liangcai's radar!

Xu Chong and Gao Teng's faces darkened with anger, about to speak, but were interrupted by Fu Liangcai's raised hand, with a meaningful implication:

"And even if you succeed in conquering my Fu clan, can you two clans really swallow it?"

This was the situation he wanted to see. Now he really didn't want to waste too much energy and trump cards on these Xu and Gao clans, the court jesters.

He only wanted to save all his strength to deal with Ouyang Nuo and Liu Qing.

At this moment, Fu Liangcai's gaze suddenly sharpened.

The crowd of onlookers outside had also become somewhat chaotic, parting to the sides to make way for a wide path, as if for someone to pass through.
