The elder's eyes flickered, temporarily not showing any intention of releasing Su Qianghe in his grasp.

He was clear in his heart that if he really let go, it would be difficult for the Crimson Yang Palace people to leave the Carefree Sect today.

He was puzzled, why did the fat man in the late-stage Spirit Sun Realm give him a sense of danger!

And the commotion here should have been heard, so more and more people from the Carefree Sect were likely to come this way.

"Grand Elder, save us..." The two middle-aged men cried out in agony, as they felt the grip on their necks tightening, and if it continued, their necks would be snapped.

"Grand Elder, don't bother with me, kill those two and then keep all the Crimson Yang Palace people here! I, a mere Spirit Origin Realm, am worth exchanging for them!" Su Qianghe shouted, his face flushed red.

Peng Fang seemed to have not heard, and said indifferently, "Are you sure you won't let go of our sect elder?"
