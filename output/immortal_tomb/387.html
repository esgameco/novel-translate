Hunhui's brows furrowed, although he was the ruler of the Ghost Realm, he still felt a chill in his heart being looked at by this Reincarnation Demonic Child.

The demonic child: "....."

Lu Changsheng's expression turned cold: "So you're saying I have no significance?"

Before the demonic child could say anything, the Great Dao's voice suddenly exploded in his mind!

The demonic child let out a miserable howl, even startling the nearby Hunhui.

After all, the Great Dao's voice he had just amplified a bit should have been enough to overwhelm the demonic child.
