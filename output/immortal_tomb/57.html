Chapter 57

Here is my translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Boom!

Lightning struck the mountain peak, but it did not hit the Ancient Crane Forest.

This lightning bolt was not the tribulation of the Ancient Crane Forest, but rather it was caused by Lu Changsheng being startled and losing control.

Lu Changsheng looked at the increasingly gathering tribulation clouds in the sky, and then turned his head to look at the Ancient Crane Forest, saying softly,

"Why are you still spacing out? The Nascent Soul tribulation is about to come."

"Ah, yes, yes, Master... Then the Crane Forest will go first."

The Ancient Crane Forest respectfully looked at Lu Changsheng, shook the messy thoughts in his head, and with a tap of his toes, flew up into the air, standing in midair.

He looked up at the tribulation clouds, his expression becoming solemn.

"Master, this little Heavenly Dao is afraid, thinking that this Ancient Crane Forest is closely related to you, so it specially sent down the Forty-Nine Heavenly Tribulation to please you, haha."

The old turtle looked up at the sky and laughed.

The Forty-Nine Heavenly Tribulation has four waves, each with nine bolts, a total of thirty-six bolts.

One wave is more terrifying than the last.

The Forty-Nine Heavenly Tribulation usually only appears when Nascent Soul cultivators break through, and under Lu Changsheng's influence, the Ancient Crane Forest's Gold Core breakthrough to Nascent Soul has caused this...

Whether it is a blessing or a curse, he does not know.

If he can ultimately withstand this Forty-Nine Heavenly Tribulation, he will benefit greatly.

Lu Changsheng couldn't be bothered to respond to the old turtle, he just stood with his hands behind his back, quietly looking up.

At the same time,

The tribulation clouds gathered more and more, the lightning crackling, as if it were the end of the world!

Even Chen Dadao and the steward on the distant city wall were dumbfounded, a sense of fear welling up in their hearts.

"Lord, there seems to be a figure standing in midair under that terrifying thunder cloud."

The steward pointed in that direction, squinting his eyes.

The distance was far, but the naked eye could still see it clearly, especially under the pitch-black tribulation clouds, the figure of the Ancient Crane Forest stood out particularly prominently.

"That's right, that should be the Immortal Crane. Weathering the tribulation, weathering the tribulation, it seems becoming an Immortal is not that easy after all." Chen Dadao sighed.

The steward tilted his head curiously, "But didn't the Master always seem quite carefree?"

Chen Dadao: "..."

...

Rumble rumble...

The Ancient Crane Forest looked at the tribulation clouds in the sky, his face as ugly as if he had eaten shit!

The Forty-Nine Heavenly Tribulation!

It shouldn't be, his natural talent, although not bad, but it's not to the point where his Gold Core breakthrough to Nascent Soul would trigger the Forty-Nine Heavenly Tribulation, right?

Facing the Forty-Nine Heavenly Tribulation, the Ancient Crane Forest suddenly lost some confidence.

The various tribulation-weathering methods he had prepared before may not be enough...

Rumble rumble...

At this time, the tribulation clouds began to roll violently, heralding the arrival of the first wave of tribulation!

And the Ancient Crane Forest also felt that he had been completely locked on!

He took a light breath, his eyes becoming as sharp as a hawk's.

The first wave, he chose to resist head-on!!

Otherwise, he would not be able to withstand the next three waves at all!

Rumble rumble! Crack!

Suddenly, nine bolts of lightning as thick as fingers, shimmering in a pale gold color, intertwined and struck down!

The Ancient Crane Forest's eyes narrowed, and he frantically mobilized the abundant spiritual energy in his body, even the Gold Core in his abdomen was trembling!

A thick spiritual energy barrier enveloped his body, and he did not retreat but advanced, rushing towards the nine lightning bolts!

In just a moment, the spiritual energy barrier dissipated, and the nine lightning bolts struck him.

Emitting dazzling light and the smell of scorched black smoke,

The Ancient Crane Forest's face turned pale, and he fell from the sky.

Just as he was about to hit the ground, he forcibly stabilized his body, blood seeping from the corners of his mouth.

He looked inside at the slightly cracked Gold Core in his abdomen, but there was not a trace of joy.

He then flipped his palm, put a few medicinal pills in his mouth, wiped the blood at the corner of his mouth, and soared back into the sky.

"Master, judging from the situation, it seems he may have a hard time."

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The old turtle shook his head and laughed.

"The new 99 book can be found at 99shux.com."

Lu Changsheng moved his mouth, spitting out the date pit in his mouth, then tossed in another one, continuing to eat and observe.

In the sky, the ancient crane Linlei was panting heavily, exhausted.

He looked at the rolling storm clouds, knowing that the second wave of thunder tribulation was about to arrive.

"They really don't give one a chance to catch a breath, do they..."

Rumble! Crack!

Suddenly, nine streaks of faintly golden lightning, each as thick as three fingers, descended from the sky.

Linlei lowered his head to look at the armor on his chest, gritted his teeth, and forcefully injected his spiritual energy into it!

Then... he charged forward again!

In an instant, the nine bolts of lightning connected with him...

Sizzle! Sizzle! Sizzle!

The armor on his chest erupted in a brilliant light, completely enveloping Linlei.

But in just a few breaths, the armor was shattered to pieces.

The lightning struck Linlei's body, and he only felt his mind go blank before plummeting down from the high sky again.

- Boom!

If the old turtle hadn't gently caught him, he would have died in this second wave, and it would have been from the fall...

"Pah!"

Linlei violently coughed up a mouthful of fresh blood, his expression somewhat wilted, his entire body disheveled and tattered, his clothes in rags, his entire person blackened by the tribulation lightning.

His previous immortal bearing had been completely swept away under the terrifying thunder tribulation.

Today, in facing the tribulation, he had left Tongxin and Lindong behind at the Zhenzhu Manor, precisely because he had considered this.

Linlei laboriously took out a medicinal pill, not caring what it was, and forcefully swallowed it down, his complexion gradually improving somewhat.

But before he could even think, the storm clouds in the sky rolled and became even more violent!

Linlei's heart jumped, and he quickly rose up into the air.

Gritting his teeth, a battle flag appeared in his hand, and he waved it fiercely.

"Formation, arise!"

Hum hum hum!

The battle flags Linlei had previously set up below began to vibrate violently, and beams of light shot out from thirty-six directions, connecting in the sky to form a light shield.

A sense of indestructibility floated upon it, making it clear that this was the defensive formation Linlei had prepared earlier to withstand the thunder tribulation.

Rumble rumble!!

Nine bolts of lightning, each as thick as a forearm, ruthlessly descended!

Linlei's pupils contracted, feeling that under this overwhelming divine might, he would be reduced to ashes in the next moment!

The power and divine might they carried were too immense, and this was only the third wave!

The nine bolts of lightning struck the formation in an instant!

The battle flag in Linlei's hand snapped with a crack, and the thirty-six formation flags below also broke one after another.

The formation dissipated, and the tribulation lightning surged towards Linlei!

"Master!"

In the distance, on the city wall, Tongxin and Lindong saw this and were greatly alarmed.

Just now, they had heard the increasingly loud thunderclaps and could no longer resist, disobeying their master's orders to come out.

Chen Dadao and the steward were also deathly pale. They had never seen such terrifying lightning.

Even without being in the midst of it, they could feel the world-destroying power of those nine tribulation bolts.

It was unimaginable what would become of the immortal cultivator struck by them.

Their hearts were gripped with dread.

At the same time, in a distant place, vast black mists were billowing.

"Hehe, very good, I can sense the Abyss Demon Spear is in that direction," Zhao Shenyuan had just said when he heard the distant thunder. His eyes flickered, and he disappeared from the spot.

The next moment, he appeared in the sky.

He looked over, his gaze slightly solemn.

"The Forty-Nine Heavenly Tribulation? Could it be that there is a Transcendent cultivator undergoing tribulation?"

Then a mocking smile curved his lips. "How fortuitous, arriving just in time."

He waved his hand towards the black mists below.

"Come, follow me!"
