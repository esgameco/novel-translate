Dragon Tribe Ao Xing?

"Luchang Sheng."

"Luchang Sheng..." Ao Xing murmured to himself.

This surname was unheard of.

"It's truly the battlefield of the warring gods and demons, the Dragon Clan... should be terrifyingly powerful, right?" Li Shuyan exclaimed in awe, recalling the figures he had just seen.

However, the old turtle did not seem to have heard him, but instead frowned and said, "How strange, I feel like I've heard the four words 'Dragon Clan Ao Xing' somewhere before."

Li Shuyan was startled, and turned his head in shock, "Senior Turtle, you feel the same way?"

Li Shuyan solemnly nodded.

The old turtle's eyes flickered, this was definitely not a coincidence, it seems the matter was not that simple.

Lu Changsheng, who was walking in front, sighed lightly and turned around, pointing upwards, "Instead of racking your brains, you two might as well take a look up there."

Huh?

The old turtle and Li Shuyan were stunned, and looked up at the sky in confusion.

What did he want them to see? Apart from the prominent three major rankings, there was nothing else in the sky...

Quack?

But the next moment, the two of them simultaneously let out a duck-like cry, suddenly enlightened as they looked at the ranking in the middle.

The Divine Body Ranking, third place, the Primordial Dragon Body, Dragon Clan Ao Xing!

Goodness, no wonder it sounded familiar, it turns out that young man was on the ranking, and even ranked among the top three on the Divine Body Ranking!

They had previously focused most of their attention on the Myriad Tribes Ranking and the Individual Combat Strength Ranking, only briefly glancing at the Divine Body Ranking.

"Wow, so strong!" Li Shuyan exclaimed in shock.

To think that upon entering the Battlefield of Gods and Demons, they would encounter such a powerful figure on the rankings. Ah, it seems that his Divine Yang Realm is still too weak in this Battlefield of Gods and Demons.

Li Shuyan couldn't help but feel a little discouraged.

To be honest, the Dragon Clan members they had just faced, not to mention that Ao Xing, even the other clansmen had given him a sense of being undefeatable.

"What's wrong, Shuyan? Feeling discouraged?" The old turtle asked with a smile.

Li Shuyan gave a bitter smile, "Senior Turtle, ever since I followed the Master out of the Ancestral Heavenly Realm, I have been feeling discouraged everywhere I go, be it the Eternal Vast Realm or the Battlefield of Gods and Demons. In the end, I come from the barren Ancestral Heavenly Realm, my foundation is too shallow."

"Take it slow," the old turtle comforted, patting his shoulder.

Lu Changsheng continued to walk on with his hands behind his back. Seeing this, Li Shuyan collected his thoughts and followed the old turtle.

"Li Shuyan," Lu Changsheng suddenly called out.

"Ah, Master, I'm here," Li Shuyan quickly ran to Lu Changsheng's side, keeping half a body's distance and bowing.

"How is the Myriad Flames Blazing Body cultivation going?" Lu Changsheng asked.

Li Shuyan hesitated, carefully saying, "To be honest, I don't know. It's just that the flame intensity in the Eternal Vast Realm's Flame Mountain... is a bit insufficient."

Lu Changsheng nodded slightly, "As long as you have flames strong enough to refine the body, and cultivate the Myriad Flames Blazing Technique to great accomplishment or even perfection, the physical strength may not be inferior to the Primordial Dragon Body on the Divine Body Ranking."

Li Shuyan was shocked. Although he did not know how powerful the Primordial Dragon Body was, for it to be ranked third on the Divine Body Ranking, it must be extremely formidable. Yet the Master said the Myriad Flames Blazing Technique could match it?

He was overjoyed!

But just as his smile was about to blossom, Lu Changsheng poured a bucket of cold water on him:

"Of course, that's only in terms of physical strength. The Myriad Flames Blazing Technique is after all just a cultivation method, while those on the Divine Body Ranking possess special constitutions. Strength is only one aspect, the more important thing is that they have other unique qualities."

Li Shuyan's smile faltered slightly, and he respectfully said, "Master, as long as the physical strength can match the Divine Body, I am already very satisfied with the power of the Myriad Flames Blazing Technique."

Lu Changsheng paused in his steps, turning to look at him and saying indifferently, "Are you truly satisfied?"
