Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

For tens of millions of years,

Lu Changsheng has traversed many worlds, riding on the back of an ancient turtle, witnessing all kinds of people and strange occurrences.

Recently, Lu Changsheng also went to the Four Directions Immortal Realm, and casually tossed Ancient Crane Forest and Chen Dadao into the Great Dao Demon-Subduing Tower, joining the Carefree Sect and keeping company with Tong Xin Lin.

However, while in the Four Directions Immortal Realm, the Heavenly Dao had come to him, saying that Ancestor Heavenly God Realm's Xing Tian had been looking for him.

After some contemplation, Lu Changsheng once again rode the ancient turtle and traveled to the Ancestor Heavenly God Realm.

When Xing Tian saw Lu Changsheng, the resolute middle-aged man suddenly broke down in tears, almost smearing them on Lu Changsheng's clothes.

Without a word, Lu Changsheng gave Xing Tian a kick, which calmed him down. Xing Tian then hurriedly explained the situation to Lu Changsheng, kneeling and begging for his help. He said he would do anything if Lu Changsheng was willing to help.

It turned out that Xing Tian's daughter, He Xitong, had finally completed her tribulation of a myriad lifetimes. This should have been a joyous occasion, but before Xing Tian could rejoice, a mysterious old woman descended upon the Ancestor Heavenly God Realm and forcibly took He Xitong away.

Xing Tian knew the old woman was up to no good, as even the Divine Emperors and the eleven Guardians were no match for her. In the end, Xing Tian could only watch helplessly as the old woman took He Xitong away, leaving behind the words "If you have the ability, come find me in the Qiankun Heavens."

The Qiankun Heavens... Xing Tian was utterly despairing, having no idea where or what the Qiankun Heavens were, let alone how to find them.

At this time, the mysterious and all-capable Lu Changsheng became Xing Tian's last hope. Xing Tian had repeatedly gone to the Four Directions Immortal Realm to inquire about Lu Changsheng, but always to no avail. In the end, he could only beseech the Heavenly Dao to inform him if there was any news of Lu Changsheng.

Finally, his perseverance paid off, and he got the news he had been waiting for.

After learning of the situation, Lu Changsheng was indeed a bit hesitant, but when he thought of the little fruit-selling girl in the Lingchen Realm and the young lady who had studied medicine at his clinic in Smoke and Rain Town in the Ancestor Heavenly God Realm, he ultimately nodded in agreement.

Seeing this, Xing Tian was immensely grateful and his hopeless heart was filled with hope once more! Without further delay, Lu Changsheng, accompanied by Xing Tian's farewells, rode the ancient turtle and departed the Ancestor Heavenly God Realm, embarking on a quest to find the so-called Qiankun Heavens.

In the boundless void, searching for such a world was akin to finding a needle in a haystack. But this was no challenge for Lu Changsheng, who, armed with the details gleaned from his conversation with Xing Tian, quickly pinpointed a certain star.

This turned out to be a world even greater in scale than the Divine-Demon Battlefield. Considering Xing Tian's description of the old woman, it seemed this world was no simple place.

And so, Lu Changsheng calmly rode the ancient turtle towards this world.

Since he had promised Xing Tian, he would certainly help, but he was in no rush, for after some calculations, he determined that He Xitong was not in immediate danger.

...

In the Qiankun Heavens, in the Cloud Mist Ancient Nation, in the City of Heavenly Fragrance, by the shores of the Secluded Pavilion Lake, a young man in azure robes was quietly playing the zither, with an old, hunched figure standing behind him like a loyal guard, his gaze filled with a tinge of bitterness.

The soothing, melodious sounds of the zither echoed along the lakeside, drawing a crowd of people who were all entranced, their eyes glazed over as they immersed themselves in the music that seemed to cleanse the soul.

Many young ladies had their eyes shining with admiration, gazing longingly at the transcendent young man in azure.

"Wow, the Zither Immortal has appeared again! So handsome!!"
