But she knew, in her lifetime, that azure figure wielding a sword would always be deeply etched in their minds.

Inside the carriage, Lu Changsheng was leaning back in the seat, his feet propped up on the window, and he leisurely replied, "Linglong Heaven."

Linglong Heaven?

Lu Changsheng saw this and a stream of light flashed into Li Shuyan's mind.

Li Shuyan paused, then immediately understood, and quickly began to adjust the direction of the flying carriage, speeding towards a void.
