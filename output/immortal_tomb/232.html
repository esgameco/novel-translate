The woman could tell at a glance that the two women in front of her were cultivators.

In this Yanyu Town, although almost all of them were ordinary people, they still occasionally saw the dazzling lights of cultivators passing through the sky.

It was because they had heard that there was a dangerous place far away from their Yanyu Town.

Of course, for cultivators it was not far, but for ordinary people like them, it was extremely far.

Who were Su Lianyi and Xu Jiao? As ordinary people, their emotions could not escape their perception even with the slightest change.

Through the woman's strange behavior, they knew that she must know something.

Su Lianyi's eyes flickered slightly, and she said softly with a smile, "Don't worry, we are Tong's friends, and we have no ill intentions. We've come to bring her a great opportunity."

A great opportunity?

The woman's gaze was still suspicious, but she also knew that cultivators could soar through the clouds and perform magical arts, which were beyond the reach of ordinary people like them.

"You two immortal ladies, I really don't know much about it. You'd better ask someone else," the woman said with a forced smile, then quickly left, bowing slightly.

Xu Jiao's gaze changed slightly, wanting to stop the woman, but Su Lianyi held her back, "Never mind, let's ask someone else."

Xu Jiao had no choice but to give up, muttering, "Holy Lady, why do we need to be so polite to these ordinary people?"

At this moment, a group of old men walked by, their voices full of complaints:

"Ah, Tong has been working as a medicine boy in the clinic. What are we going to do now?"

"Yes, it will be very difficult to buy medicine from Doctor Lu now. We can't let Tong know about this, or I'll lose face."

"There's only one solution. Next time, one of us will distract Tong, and the rest of us will quickly go buy the medicine!"

Tong? Clinic?

Su Lianyi and Xu Jiao exchanged a delighted glance and walked over.

"Gentlemen, wait a moment," Xu Jiao called out directly.

The old men stopped and looked over, seeing two women with an immortal aura approaching them. They immediately recognized them as cultivators and hurriedly bowed.

"I..." Xu Jiao was about to speak, but was interrupted by Su Lianyi, who asked with a smile, "Gentlemen, we just overheard you talking about a clinic. Do you know where it is?"

She had learned to be smart. Since Tong was working as a medicine boy in a clinic, it would be better to ask about the clinic directly.

Asking about Tong directly might arouse the suspicion of these old men.

"You two immortal ladies are looking for Doctor Lu?" one of the old men asked with a smile.

Su Lianyi's eyes flickered slightly, and she nodded, "That's right, we are here to find Doctor Lu."

The old men looked at each other, not finding anything unusual about it. After all, the banner hanging outside the Longevity Clinic said, "Curing the illnesses of the common people and cultivators."

Having cultivators come to their door was quite normal.

They only felt a sense of admiration. Doctor Lu was truly skilled!

Even cultivators were coming to him for treatment.

Combining this with the rumors about the Chen family and the rise of Luo Valley, they couldn't help but sigh. Doctor Lu's medical skills were truly remarkable!

Then an old man pointed in a direction and said with a smile, "You two immortal ladies, Doctor Lu's clinic is that way."
