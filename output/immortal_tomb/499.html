The Chu Clan Chief's expression became extremely unsightly!

Seeing this, the Chu Clan Chief suppressed his emotions and began to ponder, and the venue became quiet for a moment.

At the same time, at the entrance of the Elf Clan.

A young man in blue robes and an old man appeared, which were the summoned Lü Changsheng and the Old Turtle.

The Old Turtle waved his hand and said, "That's right, I and the master have come to attend the ceremony. Hurry up and have your Clan Chief come out to welcome us!"

The two male Elf Clan cultivators' pupils suddenly contracted.

How arrogant was this?

The leaders of the nine peak forces were already in the venue, and the President of the Maritime Merchants Association and the Holy King of the Land were also among them.

They really couldn't think of anyone in the Three Thousand Realms, other than the three Dao Realm Patriarchs of the Demon-Subduing Valley, the Nine Abyss Underworld Palace, and the Chu Clan, who would have the qualifications to have the Elf Clan Chief personally welcome them.

And those three Dao Realm Patriarchs, the two male Elf Clan cultivators had all seen them before.

Clearly, the young man in blue robes was not among them!

Of course, more importantly, according to the rules, they could no longer enter the venue at this time.

"Sorry, according to the rules, after the ceremony has begun, no one is allowed to enter the venue. How about this, you two can first come with us to the clan to rest?"

One of the male Elf Clan cultivators apologized.

The Old Turtle's gaze narrowed, he was afraid that Lü Changsheng would be impatient, and he was about to strike down these two Elf Clan cultivators.

The two Elf Clan cultivators were taken aback, and immediately took up a defensive stance, ready to face a powerful enemy.

At this moment, Qing and Lin hurriedly arrived, and as soon as they arrived, they saw this scene that made their hearts tremble!

"Senior, please wait!"

Hearing this, the Old Turtle frowned and looked at the approaching Qing and Lin, slowly putting down his hand.

"Miss Qing, Miss Lin!"
