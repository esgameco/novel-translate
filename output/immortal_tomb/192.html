Seeing the cultivators laughing uproariously, Cai Xu and Huang Bin's expressions changed slightly!

Saying that, he pulled the silent Huang Bin and left.

Leaving behind the still-laughing crowd.

"Tch! To be just a lowly menial, look how proud they are! Tomorrow, we'll go to the Xiao Yao Sect and see just how remarkable this unheard-of sect really is!"

"......"

Cai Xu and Huang Bin walked quickly, hearing the mocking behind them. They sneered inwardly, waiting for tomorrow when these people would no longer be laughing. They would surely be envious of the two of them, just thinking about it made them feel elated.

Immersed in their thoughts, the two walked hurriedly, almost colliding with a person coming towards them in the bustling street.

"Excuse us, fellow Daoist, we weren't paying attention," Cai Xu apologized quickly.

Huang Bin also scratched his head, looking embarrassed.

They looked at the blue-robed man in front of them, and a sense of unease began to rise in their hearts, feeling that this person was not simple, exuding an indescribable pressure.

Lu Changsheng looked at the two calmly.

Cai Xu and Huang Bin trembled inexplicably, realizing they were blocking the way, so they quickly moved aside.

Lu Changsheng stepped forward, passing between the two, and soon disappeared into the crowd.

"A-Bin, I don't know why, but that blue-robed man just now gave me such a strong sense of pressure... My heart almost jumped out of my throat."

Cai Xu turned and looked at the crowd, still shaken.

Huang Bin nodded, that feeling of being suppressed by a superior was truly inexplicable, even though they didn't know this person.

"Little Black, let's go home quickly. Tomorrow we have to return to the sect, and the sect is also holding a disciple recruitment day, so there will be some excitement to see."
