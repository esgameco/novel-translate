Lu Changsheng suddenly said, "The other night when the demons attacked, did you see the immortal that appeared?"

This father and son had been immersed in their emotions, but this abrupt question instantly pulled them out of it.

The young man's brow furrowed slightly, and he said in a bright tone: "Sir, of course I saw him. The immortal was so majestic! Although my vision wasn't great at the time, everyone could vaguely see that the immortal just waved his sleeve, and those demons were all wiped out!"

The young man's tone was very excited.

However, Lu Changsheng shook his head and said, "I'm not talking about the immortal that appeared later, but the one before that."

He was naturally referring to the ancient Crane Forest.

The immortal before?

The young man paused for a moment, then became equally excited and said,

"The previous immortal was also very powerful. Under the moonlight, he flew on a sword, unleashing a thousand blades at once! He was exactly like the immortal I've always imagined in my heart! Especially that line about seeking longevity, but more seeking the heroic spirit within! He was simply too cool!"

"Although that immortal almost lost, he was still very formidable."

Seeing the young man's admiring expression, Lu Changsheng smiled and asked, "Then do you want to cultivate immortality?"

"I do, of course I do! If I could become an immortal, I might be able to cure my father's paralysis!"

The young man raised his head abruptly, but soon became dejected and lowered it again: "But it's impossible."

"Your father treats you this way, yet you still think of him first?" Lu Changsheng said indifferently.

The middle-aged man lowered his head, a flash of shame in his eyes.

"I know that immortal, and if you want to cultivate immortality, I can introduce you to him." Lu Changsheng looked at the young man.

The young man was stunned, looking at the man in the blue robe in disbelief.

It was like a dream.

After this interaction, he didn't think the man in blue was joking with him.

But rather, it was true...

The middle-aged man's expression changed slightly, quickly looking at the young man, his eyes filled with hope, and said, "Dongzi, your father hopes you will go cultivate immortality!"

The young man was bewildered, "Father, what will happen to you if I leave?"

Lu Changsheng squatted down, rubbing his knuckles, and quietly watched them.

The middle-aged man's face was bleak, and a bitter smile appeared on his face: "Dongzi has taken care of his father for so long, that's enough... In the future, let the father fend for himself."

"No, no way!" The young man suddenly stood up, his eyes reddening, and pleaded to Lu Changsheng, "Sir, I really want to cultivate immortality, but if I leave, my father will die. Can you please take him with me so I can take care of him?"

A flash of joy appeared in the middle-aged man's eyes, but it quickly disappeared.

"I've already said, why do you still have to consider him?" Lu Changsheng smiled.

The young man lowered his head and said softly, "No matter whether my father is as the sir said, he is still my father, an undeniable fact. As a son, I should filial."

Lu Changsheng pursed his lips and looked at the 11-year-old young man in front of him, nodding slightly: "Good."

Then he looked at the middle-aged man and said, "I have a elixir here, given to me by an immortal. It can revive the dead and heal your paralysis with ease, even a mortal can take it. Do you want it?"

The middle-aged man was stunned for a moment, but in an instant, his eyes flashed with a fanatical light, nodding frantically.

He was tired of this inhuman life!

"I can give you the elixir, but after that, you and your son will have no more relationship, and you will never see each other again in this life."

Lu Changsheng said softly.

This would be an excellent blessing for the two of them.

One could cultivate immortality, the other could be healed.

If it weren't for Lu Changsheng, the young man and the middle-aged man's fate would probably be tragic.

"Good! I'll do as the sir says!" The middle-aged man did not hesitate, nodding decisively!

"Father..."

The young man looked blankly at the middle-aged man.

"Dongzi, in the future when you step onto the immortal path, your father and you will be different as immortal and mortal. It's better for you to sever this earthly connection, and it's for your own good."

The middle-aged man's gaze was evasive.

Lu Changsheng nodded, and silently flipped his hand, a elixir pill appearing in his palm.

He handed it to the middle-aged man and said, "Alright, after you take this elixir pill, you will no longer have any relationship with your son."

"Okay!"
