The streets of Yeyecheng were in an uproar.

"Have you heard? The Three Tigers Gang has summoned all their members, and it seems all three gang leaders are there, exuding a murderous aura!"

"Whew, what exactly happened? I've been feeling that the Three Tigers Gang has been acting strange today, as if they're searching for something everywhere..."

"Hehe, you don't know? It seems the Three Tigers Gang's Grand Elder has run into some trouble."

"What? The Grand Elder has run into trouble? How did you find that out?!"

"Tsk, many people know about it. Today, a lot of people in the city have been stopped by Three Tigers Gang members and asked if they've seen their Grand Elder or something. What do you think?"

"Ah! Judging from the Three Tigers Gang's actions, could it be that..."

"That's right, the Three Tigers Gang's Grand Elder has most likely been killed, and the culprit... I'm sure you all have your guesses, huh?"

"..."

Just as the people were discussing animatedly, a fierce shout rang out across the entire Yeyecheng:

"Raging Lion Sect! Savage Elephant Sect! You two factions, come out!"

"This... This is the voice of the Three Tigers Gang's Grand Leader!"

Instantly, Yeyecheng was in an uproar, and everyone was stunned.

"Hmph, Three Tigers Gang Leader, you've provoked us Raging Lion Sect and Savage Elephant Sect without reason today. If you don't give us an explanation, our Sect Leader and Sect Master won't let this go!"

The next moment, two streams of light rushed towards the Three Tigers Gang from different directions in Yeyecheng.

Seeing this, the crowd was excited, knowing that these were the forces of the Raging Lion Sect and Savage Elephant Sect, and they hurried to gather towards that area.

At the same time, Captain as well as Chen Pang, Xiao Chai, and other cultivators who knew the origins of the matter also frowned and headed in that direction.

After all, when there's no clue, it's better to watch the dog-eat-dog show. Especially this kind of feeling of being the only sober one among the drunk, it's quite delightful.

Watching the dogs bite each other, isn't that wonderful?

After all, among these three factions in Yeyecheng Island, there's not a single good one.

Meanwhile, in the restaurant.

Hearing the commotion, the old turtle's expression became slightly puzzled, not understanding what was happening.

"Master, what are they doing, making such a ruckus so late at night?"

The old turtle was worried that these people would disturb Lu Changsheng, and thus spoke.

If it were in the past, with the old turtle's temperament, he would have definitely launched a thunderous attack and taught those noisy fellows a lesson.

But now, alas, his strength doesn't allow it! No choice!

If he goes to discipline others, and ends up getting beaten up instead, that would be too embarrassing.

The old turtle still has a good sense of self-awareness.

Lu Changsheng glanced at the old turtle with a faint smile and said:

"I do know a bit about it. It seems that one of the Three Tigers Gang's Elders has died."

"Master, could it be that you...?" The old turtle looked at Lu Changsheng and then spoke in a low voice.

Lu Changsheng's expression was indifferent as he said, "Of course not."

The old turtle smiled awkwardly, but in his heart, he still had some doubts.

...

In front of the Three Tigers Gang in Yeyecheng, the crowd had gathered, forming a sea of people. However, the scene had become quiet, and everyone was staring intently at the center.

Captain, Chen Pang, Xiao Chai, and the other cultivators were mixed in the crowd. Coincidentally, their positions were not far apart, and they all noticed each other.

They nodded at each other in acknowledgment, and then watched the show.

In the center, the three factions stood in a three-way standoff, coldly eyeing each other, their auras rising, as if they were about to engage in a fierce battle at any moment.

"Three Tigers Gang Leaders, your Three Tigers Gang has provoked us today with such words. If you don't give an explanation, my Raging Lion Sect won't let this go easily!"

The Raging Lion Sect Leader said coldly with his hands behind his back.

"That's right, my Savage Elephant Sect is the same! Since you Three Tigers Gang entered Yeyecheng Island, you've been arrogant and lawless. What, are you now declaring war on us two factions?"

The Savage Elephant Sect Master shouted loudly, his muscular body bulging, as if his clothes were about to burst.

In fact, the Raging Lion Sect and Savage Elephant Sect had already been very accommodating towards the Three Tigers Gang, because the Three Tigers Gang's strength was indeed formidable.

This was also the reason why the Three Tigers Gang dared to directly confront the two factions over the death of their Grand Elder.

The Three Tigers Gang, with their three Gang Leaders, the Grand Leader could single-handedly fight against one faction leader, while the Second and Third Leaders together could barely hold their own.
