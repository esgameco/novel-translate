As the man in blue robes passed by, he did not cause much of a commotion.

Because that night when he exorcised the evil, Lu Changsheng appeared in the sky, and given the distance and the darkness, the common people's vision could not clearly make out his face.

However, it still drew the attention of many commoners, for it was his bearing.

Lu Changsheng strolled casually on the street, looking around here and there.

Occasionally, he would stop by the stalls on the two sides of the street and buy some snacks to taste.

"Little girl, how much are these fruits?"

Lu Changsheng squatted down and asked about the unknown red fruits in the two baskets.

The little girl stared blankly at the man in blue robes, feeling a bit nervous, "Brother, how many do you want?"

Lu Changsheng's eyes flickered, and he smiled lightly, "If they are delicious, I can buy all of them. May I try one first to taste the flavor?"

"Ah?" The little girl was stunned, seeming a bit at a loss, but then she reacted with delight, "Of course, of course you can!"

Lu Changsheng nodded slightly, reached into the basket, and picked up a red fruit, which was small enough to be held between two fingers.

He then popped it into his mouth under the little girl's expectant gaze.

Lu Changsheng pursed his lips and smiled at the girl, "This fruit is sour at first, then sweet."

The little girl's black eyes widened, not understanding what this brother meant. How could she not know the taste of these red fruits, when she had secretly eaten them many times before?

She just wanted to know if this brother found them delicious. Even if he didn't buy all of them, if he could buy some, that would be good...

"Not bad, they're delicious. I'll take both these baskets. How much do they cost?"

Lu Changsheng asked the girl.

"Okay! Brother, please wait a moment, let me calculate it," the girl exclaimed happily, and then started carefully counting on her fingers.

Lu Changsheng was in no hurry, grabbing a handful of red fruits and eating them as he waited.

After a moment,

the girl opened her small palms towards Lu Changsheng. Her index fingers spread apart, she said seriously, "Brother, ten copper coins will do."

Lu Changsheng took fifteen copper coins from his pocket and handed them to the girl, then bent down to pick up the two baskets of fruits, ready to leave.

The girl stared blankly at the handful of copper coins in her hands, counting them carefully with both hands, then hurriedly said, "Brother, you gave me too much!"

Lu Changsheng paused, then softly said, "The extra five coins are for the carrying pole and baskets."

With that, he carried the two baskets of fruits and gradually walked away under the girl's bright gaze.

The carrying pole and baskets were hardly worth five copper coins...

The girl tightly clutched the copper coins in her hands. After all, she was just a child, and she quickly broke into a happy smile.

Today, she had actually earned fifteen copper coins! Wow!

Could it be that in just a short while, Xiao Tong had been tricked?

"Hehe, Grandma, all the red fruits were bought by a brother. Look, this is the fifteen copper coins he gave me."

Xiao Tong happily opened her palms to show the old woman.
