After all, there were so many chains.

Lu Changsheng raised his eyebrows, "But you have committed such grave sins by killing dozens of lives in the great realms. If I were to release you, wouldn't that be a crime worthy of death?"

But Lu Changsheng said, "However, it's still far from enough, those were dozens of lives in the great realms after all..."

The boy's expression changed slightly, his lips moving, not knowing what to say.

He admitted that he was an unparalleled demon.

But...

He naturally wasn't afraid of this boy, he was just teasing him a bit.

This kind of unparalleled demon was quite interesting.

Lu Changsheng wanted to challenge himself, to see if he could redeem this unparalleled demon.

If he could redeem him and have him do good deeds after being released, then letting him out wouldn't be a big deal.

"No, no, I won't go against you, Big Brother. You are like a second father to me, and your strength is formidable. I'm no match for you!" the boy hurriedly said.
