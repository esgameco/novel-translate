"Lord Sword Sovereign, this method is truly marvelous!"

Mantiankong stood up and clapped his hands excitedly. The others also revealed relieved smiles on their faces. If the Sword Sovereign could do this, perhaps the human race could safely weather this great calamity!

Hearing this, the crowd fell silent for a moment.

Jian Wuhui paused briefly before softly saying, "Wind Venerable, your compassion is admirable, but we must first ensure the survival of our own race. This method is the only way to preserve the greatest interests of the human race..."

"This method can only work through the strong subjugating the weak. Are we really going to distribute the Jade Hearts to the races ranked below us? If we do that, can those lower races help us withstand the pressure from the top hundred clans?"

"Even if we give the Jade Hearts to the top hundred clans, the remaining 7,666 would still not be enough to be divided among all the races. And even if we divide them evenly, the human race would still face annihilation in the end."

"I'm sure you all can foresee these consequences."

"The Sword Sovereign speaks the truth," Jiang Wuxin said no more, knowing that he had indeed been overly compassionate.

The Sword Sovereign was right - to preserve the human race, it was better to give more to the top hundred clans, using the strong to subdue the weak, rather than dividing the resources equally among all the races.

At this point, Yan Jueyi asked, "Lord Sword Sovereign, how shall we deliver the Jade Hearts to the top hundred clans then?"

It was not feasible to send people individually, as it would be dangerous, not to mention that many of the great clans were located far from the human race.

Jian Wuhui shook his head: "We cannot send them now. Whether we have enough time is another matter, but the most important thing is that if the ones sent to deliver the Jade Hearts have their souls searched, then the human race will be utterly doomed."

The crowd fell silent, realizing the gravity of the situation. What they had discussed earlier sounded simple, but the actual implementation was extremely difficult.

"Then what should we do?" Yan Jueyi asked, perplexed.

Jian Wuhui said solemnly, "We need not hurry to send out the Jade Hearts. We will wait until the day when all the races surround our human race..."

Hearing this, the crowd felt a chill in their souls. The encirclement of all the races...just the thought of it was terrifying.

At this moment, Han Jinlong softly inquired, "Lord Sword Sovereign, even if the other races do not know how many Jade Hearts we possess, as you mentioned, if they decide to search our souls, what shall we do then?"

Before that happened, they needed to consider every possible contingency and prepare accordingly.

The hall fell silent for a moment.

Then Jian Wuhui spoke again, "When that time comes, even if they search your souls, they will most likely target one of you. I have a method that can erase your memories regarding the number of Jade Hearts, though it will cause a slight pain."

The crowd was overjoyed. Of course they could endure a bit of pain if it meant the Sword Sovereign had a solution.

Seeing their reaction, Jian Wuhui nodded slightly and called out, "Hongyan."

In the next moment, the crowd saw a beam of red light enter the hall and transform into a beautiful woman.

The people looked at the woman in puzzlement. They recognized her as the Sword Sovereign's mount, but they did not understand why she was involved in erasing their memories.

Countless eyes were now focused on the southeast direction of the Divine-Demon Battlefield, which was the territory of the Human Tribe.

Immediately, all the tribes began to mobilize. After completing their preparations, they quietly waited in their tribes.

They were waiting for the movements of the top hundred tribes, or even the top ten peak tribes.

As soon as the top ten tribes made a move, they would immediately set out!

Although the Human Tribe ranked only in the thousands, it was not an ordinary tribe, with five Overlords and the existence of the Annihilation Sword Sect Master.
