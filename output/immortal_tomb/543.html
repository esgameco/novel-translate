Apart from Jiang Yanfeng's high status and position, the more important reason was that Wangsu thought that if he could get on good terms with Jiang Yanfeng, it would be much easier to seize the Earth Vein later.

The other sect elders snapped out of their daze and hurriedly paid their respects as well.

"Mm." After looking at Wangsu, Jiang Yanfeng's eyes flickered, then he turned to the crowd and asked in a stern voice, "Have any of you seen my sect's Fifth Elder?"

Fifth Elder?

Even Wangsu and Song Ren were puzzled, their eyes flashing with uncertainty.

Seeing this, Jiang Yanfeng pursed his lips, his heart becoming conflicted and confused.

They've all just arrived...

Will they join forces to assassinate him, Jiang Yanfeng, next?

Jiang Yanfeng's eyelid twitched violently, gritting his teeth inwardly. It was just as he had guessed!

The Tianwu Sect and the other sects had colluded together to target his Daoyan Sect, killing the Fifth Elder, and now they wanted to join hands to kill him as well!

No, although he was the strongest, he couldn't take on so many opponents. If they had some hidden trump card, he would be in deep trouble. He had to quickly leave this place and report to the Sect Master!

Jiang Yanfeng was anxious, unleashing his full potential, and disappeared in a flash of light.

The crowd watched his hasty and panicked retreat, stunned.

"Uh, looks like he really had an urgent matter to attend to, even forgetting about the Earth Vein?"

The other sect elders shook their heads, unable to figure out what had happened, and could only stare at Wangsu and Song Ren as they entered Jixiang Town.

Since the Daoyan Sect had given up, let them give up. As long as they could subdue Wangsu, the others wouldn't dare to come and snatch the Earth Vein back, right?

At the same time, miles away, Jiang Yanfeng looked back and, seeing no one was pursuing him, stopped and took out a communication talisman, panting heavily:

"I suspect that the Tianwu Sect has colluded with the other sects to kill our Fifth Elder!!"

"Sect Master, what exactly has happened? Why are they targeting our Daoyan Sect?"

"Sect Master, our Daoyan Sect is now in a precarious situation, on the verge of disaster! Please think of a solution!"

"I won't return to the sect for now, I'll stay here and wait for your instructions."

Jiang Yanfeng said all this in one breath, then put away the communication talisman, cautiously looking around before hiding in a secluded spot.

Yin Quan had just been in a meeting with the elders, when the communication talisman suddenly started to glow and heat up.

Including Yin Quan, the Sect Master, who felt a chill run down his spine!
