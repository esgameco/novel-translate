Luchang Sheng stood with his hands behind his back, gazing into the vast sky, silent.

Nangong Jing respectfully stood behind him.

His eyes flickered slightly, as Luchang Sheng had not moved or spoken for a while.

He was somewhat perplexed.

But the next moment, Nangong Jing's eyes suddenly widened!

He saw a towering, illusory figure slowly coalescing in front of them...

"Immortal Emperor Changsheng."

A hollow, ancient voice sounded in his ears.

Nangong Jing knew that this voice was coming from the towering illusory figure.

Could this be...

He looked at the towering illusory figure with a hint of disbelief.

Luchang Sheng said directly, "Return to him the lifespan you took when he peeked into the Heavenly Secrets."

Seeing Luchang Sheng's directness, the towering illusory figure seemed to fall silent for a moment.

After a while, it said, "Immortal Emperor Changsheng, peeking into the Heavenly Secrets always comes at a price..."

Luchang Sheng waved his sleeve lightly and said calmly, "I am currently serving as the Immortal Emperor in the Central Immortal Realm. Nangong Jing's peeking into the Heavenly Secrets can be seen as indirectly helping me. Since I have said so, do you understand?"

The towering illusory figure wavered for a moment, then finally said, "Alright, I'll do this favor for the Immortal Emperor Changsheng... But once he breaks through to the Immortal Emperor realm, this lifespan..."

Luchang Sheng smiled and said, "Once Nangong Jing breaks through to the Immortal Emperor realm, you can naturally take back this lifespan."

Nangong Jing was stunned.

So it could be done this way... How is this any different from cheating?

When he becomes an Immortal Emperor, his lifespan will increase greatly, and the portion returned will be insignificant.

"Then go ahead and return the lifespan to him," Luchang Sheng said calmly.

The towering illusory figure nodded, and Nangong Jing suddenly felt an extremely powerful surge of vital energy flooding his body, reviving all the exhaustion within him.

"Thank you, Immortal Emperor Changsheng, thank you, Heavenly Dao..."

Nangong Jing bowed gratefully.

The towering illusory figure did not pay attention to Nangong Jing, but looked at Luchang Sheng and said hesitantly, "Immortal Emperor Changsheng, there is a huge breach in the barrier of one of the Four Realms..."

Luchang Sheng's eyes flashed, knowing that it was caused by the punch he had thrown in the Southern Immortal Realm, and he smiled, "What do you mean?"

The towering illusory figure paused, then gradually faded away...

"Nothing... I just hope that in the future, Immortal Emperor Changsheng will be a little more restrained. The Four Realms cannot withstand such turbulence from you. If you were to throw a few more punches, the Four Realms might be riddled with holes, and it would be a hassle for me to repair them."

Nangong Jing heard this with great shock, but the next moment, his vision went dark.

When his sight returned, he was back in the Dao Sect.

"You may leave. I need to rest."

Luchang Sheng said and walked inside.

"Yes..."

Nangong Jing took a deep look at Luchang Sheng's retreating back, bowed respectfully, and turned to leave the courtyard. He was deeply shaken.

The Heavenly Dao of the Four Realms seemed to cower before Immortal Emperor Changsheng. At this point, even if someone said Luchang Sheng was the father of the Heavenly Dao, Nangong Jing would be willing to believe it.

Especially the Heavenly Dao's last few words, they were truly shocking.

What did it mean by "a few more punches" and the Four Realms would be "riddled with holes"? This was too terrifying.
