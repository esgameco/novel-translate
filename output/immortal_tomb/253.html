Lu Changsheng looked into the void, "Waiting for a few people."

Waiting for a few people? Xiao Tong was surprised, as she scanned the empty surroundings, it didn't look like there were any people around.

But she also quieted down and didn't dare to ask more.

Chu Lingxian and the others were also stunned, staring unbelievably at the blue-robed man standing in the cold wind, whose robes were fluttering.

Long... long...

The Old Turtle, with tears streaming down his face, rushed over with open arms, wanting to give him a hug in his excitement.

Lu Changsheng's brow slightly furrowed as he kicked the Old Turtle, sending him crashing to the ground.

Boom!

The Old Turtle's agonized cry made Chu Lingxian and the others' scalps tingle, and they immediately snapped out of their daze. They hurriedly stepped forward and bowed excitedly, "Greetings, Immortal Emperor Changsheng!"

Immortal Emperor Changsheng?

Beside them, Xiao Tong was shocked, shocked and shocked again, as she tilted her head and looked at the blue-robed man with reverence.

Although she didn't understand, she was deeply shaken.

She never imagined that in addition to being the doctor of the medical clinic, the sir also had another identity.

Lu Changsheng looked at the dirt-covered group with a faint smile and said, "Judging from your state, it seems you haven't been having a very pleasant time these past few days, have you?"

Chu Lingxian and the others couldn't help but feel a lump in their throats when they heard this.

Yes, they had been through so much hardship.

At this time, the Old Turtle, clutching his abdomen, got up from the ground and said unhappily, "Master, you have no idea, those beasts have been so ruthless. Ever since we parted ways on the Divine Path, me and Lingxian have been hunted down day and night."

Lu Changsheng glanced at him and said, "If you had spent the time improving your cultivation instead of being lazy, would that not have been better?"

"Cough, Master is right, from now on the Old Turtle will definitely not be so playful anymore," the Old Turtle said awkwardly with a slight bow.

Indeed, all this time, he had never really focused on cultivating, always thinking that with his long lifespan, he could do it another day.

After all, these Immortal Emperors were far more powerful than him.
