Han Yue snapped out of his daze and glanced at his still stunned parents.

Han Tie and Wu Cuilan also regained their senses and looked at Han Yue.

Han Yue instantly understood the meaning in his parents' eyes, and his heart felt slightly relieved. He then respectfully said:

"Thank you, Lord Zhen, for your concern. However, Han Yue is not tired or in a hurry. The ten-li journey is just the right distance for Han Yue to exercise his body..."

Cao Yulong was taken aback, and Huang Yulang and the others were also stunned, then they looked at Han Yue with disbelief!

Rejected?

This half-grown child, Han Yue...

Did he know what he had just rejected?

That was a house in Jixing Town!

A house in Jixing Town was something that countless commoners outside the town could only strive for their entire lives, and might still not be able to obtain!

And the house bestowed by the Lord was no ordinary house, how could it be compared to that?

Ye Xiaoxiao and Xiao Fan looked at Han Yue anxiously, wishing they could speak up and accept it on his behalf!

Though Han Yue was bowing, his small figure still appeared somewhat upright.

Han Yue knew what he had rejected.

A house in Jixing Town.

But Han Yue knew that one should not receive unearned rewards.

His teacher had said, everything must be earned through one's own efforts, only what one obtains by oneself is truly one's own.

There is no one in the world who would help oneself without reason.

If he were to accept the house bestowed by the Lord without any merit, Han Yue knew that he and his parents might not be able to live there at ease.

How could one eat a windfall with a peaceful mind?

"Han Yue, are you sure about this?" Cao Yulong asked solemnly.

But anyone could see that Cao Yulong's gaze had changed, now filled with admiration.

Huang Yulang and the others also looked at Han Yue and his parents with admiration.

This is true poverty of the body but not of the spirit...

Perhaps some would consider this as affectation, false high-mindedness, to reject the wealth presented before them would be foolish, utterly foolish!

But Cao Yulong and the others could clearly see that Han Yue's family had not a hint of pretense, and after rejecting the house, they showed no reluctance or discontent.

"Thank you, Lord Zhen, Han Yue is grateful." Han Yue deeply bowed.

Cao Yulong looked at Han Yue for a moment, then nodded and said, "Good! Han Yue, the Lord has kept this house for you. Whenever you feel ready, come to the Lord's manor and claim it."

Before Han Yue could speak, Cao Yulong raised his hand and continued, "Don't refuse again. The Lord can assure you that the house is given to you without any other ulterior motives."

Since Cao Yulong had said so, Han Yue naturally did not dare to say anything else, and could only bow and reply, "Yes, Han Yue understands."

"Haha, good." Cao Yulong laughed heartily, then looked at his own child, his gaze filled with disappointment, but he still led him back towards the town.

Cao Boyu knew he had disappointed his father, and did not dare to speak, only feeling sorrow, discontent, dejection, and even self-doubt in his heart.

He, the son of the Lord, was utterly useless.

Huang Yulang and the others also nodded slightly towards Han Yue's family and returned to the town.

Ye Xiaoxiao and Xiao Fan made a funny face at Han Yue, then ran back to the town, as it was already mealtime and they were quite hungry.

"Father, Mother," Han Yue suddenly lowered his head, his tone somewhat melancholic.

"Hmm?" Han Tie and Wu Cuilan exchanged a glance, each taking one of Han Yue's arms, their expressions puzzled.

"Father, Mother, is Tie'er really so foolish, to reject the Lord's house? You could have lived in a good house, and I... I..."

Han Yue said this while covering his face with his hand, starting to sob.

Han Tie and Wu Cuilan's hearts ached. They squatted down, patting his head and comforting him with a smile:

"No, Tie'er, you did well. Your father and mother are proud of you. We believe that in the future, we can live in a good house through our own efforts!"

"It is we, your parents, who have let you down..."

Suddenly, the sound of someone eating noodles rang out.

Huh?

This immediately pulled the three of them out of the sorrowful atmosphere.

They looked over and saw that Luchang Sheng had somehow arrived, standing before them, holding a steaming bowl of noodles in his hands.
