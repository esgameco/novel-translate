"So you've only achieved half of it, is it still too early to break through?"

Ye Zhui Xing was surprised and puzzled as he asked, "Brother, what do you mean by that?"

"I've only just barely stepped into the Divine Realm, isn't that still too early?" Ye Zhui Yue sighed bitterly.

Hearing this, Ye Zhui Xing was deeply shaken, and he quickly comforted, "Brother, you're already very strong. Even if you only achieve half the Divine Realm, that is still the Divine Realm, far surpassing the top ancient clans."

The Divine Realm was the absolute ruler of the Void Ancient World, even the entire Qian Kun Heavens!

Cultivators at the Divine Realm had lifespans exceeding a hundred million years, which was an extremely terrifying milestone.

Ye Zhui Yue shook his head, "Zhui Xing, even just half the Divine Realm is as difficult as ascending to the heavens. Do you know how long it took me to reach this point?"

Ye Zhui Xing asked, "How long?"

Ye Zhui Yue raised three fingers.

Ye Zhui Xing said, "Three hundred thousand years? That's quite normal."

Ye Zhui Yue shook his head again.

Ye Zhui Xing tentatively asked, "Could it be three million years? That's still not too long if you grit your teeth."

Ye Zhui Yue replied, "Wrong, it's three million years."

What?! Three million years?!

Ye Zhui Xing was dumbfounded!

Just to reach half the Divine Realm, it was this difficult?

Three million years, the world had changed drastically, and for many low-level cultivators, everything would have been long gone.

"Haha, so you see, there's no point in discussing this. Perhaps one day an opportunity will arise, but if I follow the rules, it will indeed take around three million years, more or less."

Ye Zhui Yue waved his hand and said, "Alright, let's not talk about this anymore."

"Brother, you've already reached the threshold of the Divine Realm, surpassing many others. Unlike me, I'm still lost in the dark," Ye Zhui Xing comforted him and prepared to leave.

"Hmph, you're so comfortable, managing the Cang Wu Pavilion, lying there half-dead every day. Your life is much easier than mine," Ye Zhui Yue sneered.

Ye Zhui Xing's face flushed red, and he felt embarrassed to stay any longer. He bowed and then turned to leave.

Just then, he heard Ye Zhui Yue say behind him:

"Zhui Xing, when I've finished summarizing my experiences, I'll share the insights I've gained about reaching the Divine Realm threshold with you."

Ye Zhui Xing paused.

"Thank you, Brother."

As Ye Nan Tian flew in front, his eyes flashed, and he turned his head with a slight smile, "Elder Lu, what do you think of our Ye Clan?"

Lu Changsheng replied without hesitation, "It's just average."

Just average?

The top ancient clans in the Void Ancient World numbered only around a hundred!

Ye Nan Tian didn't realize that Lu Changsheng's "average" was already a relatively positive evaluation.

"What are you trying to say?" Lu Changsheng asked, with a hint of a smile on his face.

"I... Cough, cough, never mind." Ye Nan Tian was about to say something, but he held back.
