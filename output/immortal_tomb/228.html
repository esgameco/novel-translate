Tongxin, who was standing by, was shocked. Although he had already mentally prepared himself, he still did not expect the doctor's medical skills to be so extraordinary!

If it were him, he would never be able to achieve this. He could only gently nourish the child's five zang organs with immortal qi, or concoct a healing elixir, but the child might not be able to withstand the potency of the elixir.

The woman cried tears of joy, lowering her head to press her face against the child's face.

The big man also snapped out of his shock, letting out a long sigh of relief. He affectionately ruffled the child's head, then looked at Luchang with moist eyes, almost kneeling down again, but was once more lifted up by Luchang, who smiled and said,

The big man felt a little embarrassed, but then said gratefully: "Doctor Lu, you truly deserve it. If it weren't for you, I don't know what would have happened to my child..."

Luchang smiled, winked at the child in the woman's embrace, then went to lie down on the rocking chair.

Seeing this, the woman discreetly nudged the dazed big man.

The big man understood. He recalled his previous words, his heart trembling, but then he looked at the healthy child and steeled his resolve, saying, "Doctor, tonight I will sell my family property..."

But before he could finish, Luchang raised five fingers: "Five copper coins."

The big man and the woman were puzzled for a moment, then their eyes widened in disbelief.

It was so cheap, just the cost of two meals.

While the two were still stunned, Tongxin reached out his hand and chuckled, "Uncle, Auntie, five copper coins."

"Okay, okay, little doctor." The big man hurriedly took out five copper coins and handed them to Tongxin.

Then the two of them deeply bowed to Luchang and left.

"Sir, our clinic charges so little for treatment..."

Tongxin weighed the five copper coins in his palm and muttered.

These five copper coins were only enough to buy ten steamed buns.

"The common people have their own value, and the cultivators have their own value," Luchang softly said with his eyes closed.

Tongxin suddenly understood.
