Xing Tian's pupils contracted into needle-like shapes, and he couldn't help but swallow his saliva quietly.

Due to the movement of his Adam's apple, it was slightly cut by the sword tip, and a scarlet thread immediately flowed out from his throat.

He thought that Lu Changsheng's strength might not be ordinary, but he never imagined that it had reached a level that he couldn't understand, a level that he couldn't resist, a level that could instantly kill him!

This is... impossible!

How could someone become so strong within a million years?

"Now, how about we discuss the conditions? The condition is that you tell me about them, and I won't kill you."

Lu Changsheng smiled slightly.

Xing Tian was stunned, and his heart felt bitter.

Under the chasm-like gap in strength, the roles had instantly switched.

However, he also knew that Lu Changsheng had no intention of killing him, or else he would have died just now.

He replied dejectedly, "In this Heavenly Scar, there were three people who participated in that matter: the ancestor of the Jiang Clan Imperial Family from the Holy Immortal Realm, the ancestor of the Zhan Clan Imperial Family, and the Beast Ancestor from the Extreme Gloomy Forest."

Hearing this, Lu Changsheng nodded slightly, and the long sword in his hand disappeared. After turning his back, he said, "You want a drop of my blood, but I won't give it to you. However, I can take a look at your daughter for you."

Xing Tian's body trembled, and he looked up in astonishment.

He had originally thought that the matter was over, and he should be grateful that Lu Changsheng didn't kill him. But he never expected that...

His original condition was to try to obtain a drop of Lu Changsheng's blood, but now Lu Changsheng had directly mentioned it, leaving him extremely shocked.

He didn't know how Lu Changsheng knew about this.

"Thank... Thank you, Senior Lu." Xing Tian bowed respectfully. After knowing that Lu Changsheng had the power to instantly kill him, he also gained a lot of respect for him.

As for the matter of his daughter, although he wouldn't give the blood, but was willing to help take a look, it was already very good.

Xing Tian thought that since Lu Changsheng was so powerful, he must have more methods than him.

"If I were to heal your daughter, would you then leave the Heavenly Scar and return to the Ancestral Heaven Deity Realm?" Lu Changsheng suddenly said.

At these words, Xing Tian abruptly stood up, his face full of disbelief.

"Senior Lu, how do you know about the Ancestral Heaven Deity Realm?"

After all, he was originally from the Ancestral Heaven Deity Realm, and he was also a prominent figure there.

He had crossed the vast distance to come to the Heavenly Scar for more than a million years, just to find the Rebirth Flower to save his daughter.

But he had found nothing.

Lu Changsheng shook his head without answering. He then asked, "Since you think my blood can save your daughter, why didn't you come to target me a million years ago? Your strength is much stronger than theirs..."

Xing Tian recovered from his shock, and after a long time, he humbly confessed, "Senior Lu, at that time, I came to the Heavenly Scar with my daughter, and my mind was only focused on her. I didn't have much time to pay attention to them. Moreover... there is no such thing as an immortal being in the world. The lifespan of living beings may be more or less, with some living for hundreds of years, thousands of years, tens of thousands of years, or even millions or billions of years. But... there will be no immortal beings."

"To be more realistic, even the Deity Emperors of the Ancestral Heaven Deity Realm cannot achieve immortality. This Heavenly Scar is only slightly higher in level than the Four Directions Immortal Realms... How could there be an immortal being?"

As he said this, Xing Tian smiled bitterly, then realized something and his expression changed slightly, looking at Lu Changsheng apologetically, "Senior Lu, Xing Tian is straightforward and didn't mean to look down on you."

"It's fine." Lu Changsheng nodded expressionlessly and continued to ask, "If there are no immortal beings, then why have you come to find me this time?"

Xing Tian's face showed sadness. "Senior Lu, to be honest, Xing Tian is really out of options. After more than a million years, I still can't find the Rebirth Flower. I feel that my daughter has been acting strangely lately, and I'm afraid something unexpected might happen. That's why I came to find you, it's just a desperate measure."

Rebirth Flower?

Lu Changsheng's gaze showed a slight surprise. Is this thing really that rare?

He remembered that there were many of them by the Rebirth Lake in the Soul Realm...

Just as Lu Changsheng was about to say something, he looked up at the sky and leisurely smiled, "The Jiang Clan Imperial Family has sent three ants. Can you handle it?"

Xing Tian said solemnly, "I can."

He knew that Lu Changsheng was using him as a helper, but he dared not refuse, nor could he!

Not to mention Lu Changsheng's terrifying strength that completely overwhelmed him, his and his daughter's lives were also essentially in Lu Changsheng's hands...
