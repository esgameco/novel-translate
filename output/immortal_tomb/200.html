But how could this be? Wasn't he the Sect Master? Why was he standing in the third row?

The leader of the Xiaoyao Sect was actually a fat man? Could it be that this fat man was the true Sect Master of the Xiaoyao Sect?

Chen He pondered for a moment, and still decided to inform the Pavilion Master Fan Hong, who was meditating in front.

"Pavilion Master, it's the Xiaoyao Sect!"

Huh? What sect?

Fan Hong was startled, opening his eyes and turning around, looking puzzled at Chen He.

Chen He's gaze was solemn, tilting his head slightly to indicate.

Seeing this, Fan Hong turned his head to look, and his gaze immediately sharpened.

So this was the Xiaoyao Sect?

He scrutinized the fat man who was obviously the Sect Master, and the more he looked, the more his gaze became serious.

This Xiaoyao Sect Master, he actually couldn't see through him...

It seems this Xiaoyao Sect really has some substance to it.

Fan Hong quickly withdrew his gaze, afraid of attracting the attention of the Xiaoyao Sect.

Now in this Extreme Cold Glacier, there were too many sects, it was not appropriate to establish an opponent without reason!

"There are too many sects here, let's not bother with them for now. When we reach the Extreme Cold Glacier, if we encounter the Xiaoyao Sect again, we'll deal with it then," Fan Hong said in a calm voice.

Hearing this, Chen He expressed his full agreement.

They did not know that while Fan Hong was probing Peng Fang, Peng Fang had already noticed, and he looked puzzled at the Blood Flame Pavilion's Fan Hong.
