The ground was covered with layer after layer of white cloth-covered bodies...

Chen Dadao's forehead was beaded with cold sweat. He swept his gaze across the scene - no more, no less, exactly sixty-seven bodies.

"Lord, the entire Qian household has been killed, and the bodies are all here."

The garrison commander bowed slightly, with a hint of self-blame in his tone. In his eyes, as the garrison commander, the Qian household was attacked right under his watch, and he could not escape the blame.

Chen Dadao asked in a stern voice, "Were they killed by humans or demons?"

The garrison commander hesitated for a moment, then gritted his teeth and replied firmly, "Not by humans."

Chen Dadao took a deep breath and walked to the nearest body, crouching down to lift the white cloth.

A shriveled corpse appeared before the three men's eyes. The expression on the face was frozen in terror, and there were no wounds on the entire body...

Chen Dadao swallowed hard and continued to uncover the second, third, and fourth bodies.

"Lord, the cause of death is exactly the same for every body - they are all shriveled, as if their entire blood supply has been drained, and their expressions are full of terror, as if they saw something extremely horrifying before their deaths," the garrison commander explained, pursing his lips.

Chen Dadao slowly stood up, his legs visibly trembling. He scanned the surroundings and said in a hoarse voice, "Many towns in the eastern part of the Water Emperor's Empire have been plagued by demons... Now it seems that our Moyu Town has also been affected."

The garrison commander's expression changed - he didn't know about this news.

The steward said, "News of the demon-plagued towns has been completely suppressed. I guess the Empire is afraid that it will have a widespread impact and disrupt the people's livelihoods. The lord and I only learned of it through some informal channels a few days ago."

The garrison commander nodded stiffly, then looked at Chen Dadao and said with difficulty, "Lord... The demon is now within Moyu Town, and it may even... be watching us in secret at this very moment!"

Hearing this, Chen Dadao and the steward felt a chill run down their spines as they looked around, a sense of terror overwhelming them.

"And to have killed sixty-seven people in such a short time, it's likely not just a single demon," the garrison commander said solemnly.

Chen Dadao murmured, "Not just a single demon..."

The steward frowned and said, "Garrison commander, if you suspect the demons are still in the Qian household, why haven't they attacked us yet? We are mere mortals, and they could easily kill us in an instant, right?"

Chen Dadao's eyes lit up, and he pointed at the steward, exclaiming, "You're right!"

He realized the steward's mind was quick.

The garrison commander shook his head and chuckled, "Steward, you're mistaken."

Both Chen Dadao and the steward were taken aback, and they listened as the garrison commander continued:

"The lord and the steward are unaware, but the garrison and town guards have already studied the habits of demons. While they could easily kill us mortals, demons often enjoy striking unexpectedly, reveling in the element of surprise and creating various forms of panic, delighting in people's fear. Even though they possess the power to destroy us, they still prefer to play a cat-and-mouse game."

Chen Dadao and the steward were stunned, realizing there was such a perspective.

"Lord, based on the garrison commander's words, the demons seem to enjoy having people in a state of fear, so they may not make a move for the time being. However, we can't be certain. What should we do next?" the steward asked Chen Dadao bitterly, as there were still countless civilians outside the Qian household waiting for an explanation.
