Ye Zhuiyue first looked a bit flustered at the fierce battle ahead, seeing the fighting was in complete chaos, and no one was paying attention to his Ye Clan, so he relaxed a little.

He then quickly looked at Lu Changsheng, suspiciously asking, "Elder Lu, what is this..."

Lu Changsheng did not say much, only saying, "Your Ye Clan should not rush to act for now, just observe for the time being."

Ye Zhuiyue felt his brain itching, his face full of astonishment: "Why, why?"

"It's up to you. If you trust me, just do as I say."

Lu Changsheng glanced at him and then walked to the side, taking out some fruits and tossing them into his mouth, watching the battlefield like an indifferent bystander.

Ye Zhuiyue's eyes flickered, his expression conflicted. After staring at the calm Lu Changsheng for a while, he made a decision and sent a voice transmission to Ye Nantian.

Hearing the voice transmission, Ye Nantian's expression became strange, but of course, he dared not disobey the order of the ancestor, so he issued the order.

After receiving the order, the members of the Ye Clan gave Elder Lu a meaningful look, thinking that Lu Changsheng was really... Indeed, in the chaos of the battle, no one would notice them slacking off for a while.

Soon, the entire Ye Clan stood aside with Lu Changsheng, staring intently at the earth-shattering battle in front of them, as if it had nothing to do with them.

"Cough, big brother, is this Elder Lu's intention?" Ye Zhuixing came to Ye Zhuiyue's side and coughed dryly to ask.

"Mm." Ye Zhuiyue nodded.

Ye Zhuixing asked suspiciously, "What does Elder Lu mean?"

"Elder Lu didn't say, but I can feel that there must be some hidden reason..." Ye Zhuiyue's eyes flickered.

Ye Zhuixing pursed his lips, unable to help but look at the blue-robed man eating fruits as if he was at his own home, and whispered, "Big brother, I don't know why, but I'm starting to feel that Elder Lu is not simple at all..."

Ye Zhuiyue did not speak again. In any case, although this tactic was a bit of a slacker, it could greatly reduce the casualties of the Ye Clan for the time being, so why not?

Boom!!!

The battle here was gradually reaching a climax!

Every moment, countless people were falling!

The bloody scent in the heaven and earth was becoming more and more dense, and the blood mist in front of them could be clearly seen!

The most intense part of the battle was between the ancestor-level figures of the two super ancient clans, Hong Wanjun and Ouyang Mo!

Without any exaggeration, most of the casualties here were caused by the aftershocks of their battle!

The battle at the Divine Void Realm, even the aftershocks, was terrifyingly powerful, and more importantly, the two of them had not even used their full strength yet.

At this moment, Ouyang Mo turned his head and glanced at the direction of the He Clan, then turned to look at Hong Wanjun with a bloodthirsty smile:

"Hong Wanjun, what's the point of this mutual exploration? The Divine Void Realm has already vaguely grasped a glimpse of the Dao, why don't we directly clash in the Dao?"

"Come, old man will not let you win!" Hong Wanjun glared.

At the same time, on the Ye Clan side, Ye Zhuiyue murmured longingly, "The Dao... Today, I actually have the chance to witness the clash of the Dao."

Ye Zhuixing became curious and asked, "Big brother, what is the Dao? Is it very powerful?"

Ye Zhuiyue sighed, "Zhuixing, you may not know that the Divine Void Realm is called the true watershed. Apart from the longevity of a hundred million years, the most important thing is that they have already grasped a glimpse of the power of the Dao!"

"Just a glimpse of the power of the Dao is so powerful?" Ye Zhuixing exclaimed.

Ye Zhuiyue smiled, "Of course, my brother is almost at the Divine Void Realm, and it is also because he has grasped the power of the Dao, though it is only a tiny, tiny bit, far less than the true Divine Void Realm experts."

Ye Zhuixing nodded thoughtfully, then focused his attention on the battle.

On the side, Lu Changsheng, upon hearing this, had an expressionless face.

The Dao, the bridge connecting reality and the supreme Dao of heaven and earth, but...

A tiny bit of the Dao, what is that? Even the true Dao is nothing in his eyes, at most it is at the Void Realm.

After all, above the Dao, there is still the Profound Principle, which is a higher bridge than the Dao.

And above the Profound Principle, there is the Order, which already belongs to a realm beyond the Void Realm, able to give birth to all things.

Suddenly, Lu Changsheng's eyes widened!

How did he know this information so clearly... How did he know all this...Strange.
