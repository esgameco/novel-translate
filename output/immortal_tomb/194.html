Zhu Xingqiu's domineering words fell,

The entire venue fell completely silent.

The cultivators immediately understood that the terrifying aura that had just flashed by was released by him......

Shocked, they were guessing whether this elder was an elder of the Xiaoyao Sect, or even the Sect Master?

After all, that aura......

Zhu Xingqiu glanced at Cai Xu and Huang Bin, then looked down at the crowd and said indifferently, "Those of you who have failed the assessment, leave quickly, don't occupy the space."

Hearing this, many Xiaoyao Sect disciples immediately began to clear the venue with cold expressions.

They were the kind of people from the Black Sha Domain, so their temperament was naturally different from ordinary people.

And now, backed by the big tree that is the Sect Master, they felt that they did not need to care about anyone's face out there! Nor did they need to give any unpleasant people a good face!

They were very clear about how powerful their own Sect Master was!!

Seeing this, many cultivators who had failed the assessment and were unwilling to leave could only leave one by one, filled with fear.

And at this moment,

The people of the Huoyan Hall and Sengluo Gate finally arrived!!

Their conspicuous sect uniforms quickly attracted everyone's attention, their pupils constricting, their hearts inexplicably excited, especially many of the cultivators who had failed the assessment and missed the chance to join the Xiaoyao Sect.

They couldn't wait to see the Xiaoyao Sect embarrassed!

At first, when they saw the Xiaoyao Sect treating them well, they were all excited and wanted to join the Xiaoyao Sect.

But after being washed out, their hearts became unbalanced, and they wanted to see the ugliness of the Xiaoyao Sect.

"Sss, that seems to be the Grand Elder Chen He of the Huoyan Hall..."

"The other elder should be the Grand Elder Zheng Shan of the Sengluo Gate, right?"

"Hehe, it seems that the Xiaoyao Sect has been too prosperous lately, affecting the interests of the major sects. This is to discipline the Xiaoyao Sect."

"......"

Countless queuing assessment disciples frantically transmitted voice messages to each other, making way, their eyes flickering as they remained silent.

This scene, Zhu Xingqiu on the high platform seemed to have not seen it at all,

The many examiners in the assessment venue also seemed to have not seen it, and continued to carry out the assessment in an orderly manner.

Suddenly, the atmosphere of the entire venue became a bit eerie.

The people of the Huoyan Hall and Sengluo Gate were slightly taken aback, and their expressions soon turned ugly.

The Xiaoyao Sect completely ignored them.

What a big tune!

Chen He and Zheng Shan exchanged a gloomy look.

Chen He took a step forward, looking at the obviously dominant Zhu Xingqiu on the high platform, and said with a strange smile, "We of the Huoyan Hall and Sengluo Gate have come to visit, but you have ignored us. Is this the hospitality of your Xiaoyao Sect?"

Zhu Xingqiu was still looking at the assessment venue, without any intention to speak, not even bothering to look at them...

Ridiculous!

He, a person about to be promoted to Immortal Saint, what was there to say to these two mere Golden Immortal Realm elders?

There was a huge realm difference between them!

That's right, he admitted that he looked down on these two!

The other few executives in the assessment venue also closed their eyes and sat in meditation, with an indifferent air.

Seeing this, the Xiaoyao Sect disciples smiled knowingly, they were very clear about the thoughts of Elder Xingqiu and the few executives.

Immediately, one of the Xiaoyao Sect disciples stepped forward, first bowing to Zhu Xingqiu,

Then turned to face the slightly taken aback Huoyan Hall and Sengluo Gate crowd and smiled, "Hospitality? When have we of the Xiaoyao Sect ever invited you Huoyan Hall and Sengluo Gate? It is you elders who came uninvited on the day we were recruiting disciples in the Xiaoyao Sect, isn't that a bit impolite?"
