Hu Zhan and Jiang Yuan both stiffened and turned their heads. A young man in blue robes was sitting there cross-legged, like a ghost, and they hadn't noticed him at all...

Lu Changsheng!

The hall fell into an eerie silence.

"Lu... Fellow Daoist Lu... Haha."

Hu Zhan laughed dryly, also glancing at Lu Changsheng's shoulders, which were empty this time, without the tortoise.

Jiang Yuan's eyes flickered rapidly, somewhat trembling.

This was the second time he had seen the young man in blue, but this time, the pressure he felt was immense!

"Sit." Lu Changsheng smiled.

He then picked up the wine jar and poured himself a cup of tea, as if he were in his own home.

Hu Zhan and Jiang Yuan exchanged a glance, then slowly sat down.

The two who had just been adversaries quickly reached a consensus with a single glance.

If this Lu Changsheng had ill intentions, they would definitely join forces against him first!

"Don't be nervous, I won't do anything to you." Lu Changsheng soothed.

But the more he said this, the more Hu Zhan and Jiang Yuan felt their bodies stiffen.

As the Demon Venerable of the demonic realm and the Elder of the Purple Extreme Thunder Peak, they were now constrained in every way.

Whether it was their aura or something else, they were suppressed to the extreme.

Lu Changsheng looked at Hu Zhan, pausing until Hu Zhan became nervous, then said, "You little demon tiger, learn a thing or two from that Demon Lord, and use your brain a bit more."

Hu Zhan's expression changed drastically. His true form had actually been seen through!

The Four Celestial Realms...

He was inwardly extremely relieved that he had been relatively stable, otherwise it would have been a matter of life and death.
