As soon as Shen Ting took a step, an illusory staircase appeared under his feet, and he slowly ascended to the high point.

And below him were the countless cultivators of all tribes, like countless stars.

Shen Ting flicked his sleeve, looking down at the cultivators with an arrogant smile on his face, and said:

"I, the Celestial Lord, was born into the great Celestial Tribe. Since birth, I have been at the top, the strongest among my peers!"

As he said this, Shen Ting turned his head to look at Bi You and Ao Zhengtian on the stone pillars.

To be honest, among the top ten peak great clans, only Bi You and Ao Zhengtian could be barely considered in his eyes.

In the past, perhaps it was difficult to suppress them, but now, he and these two were no longer in the same world.

Bi You and Ao Zhengtian's expressions were extremely unsightly...

Yes, this was the feeling!
