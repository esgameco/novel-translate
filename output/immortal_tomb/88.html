Eastern Domain.

Not only the Lishui and other empires, many places in the Eastern Domain have also experienced war.

Wave after wave of merciless beast tides ravaged the land.

If it were not for all the sects of the Eastern Domain's cultivation world mobilizing, the casualties would have reached a horrifying degree.

However, since the Eastern Domain did not prepare its defenses in advance, even though they reacted in time, the casualties were already severe.

"A few years ago, the Azure Cloud Sect had already warned about this, but no one believed them. Now look, the Eastern Domain has suffered tremendous losses! So many common people have died, including many with spiritual roots. In the coming period, the sects of the Eastern Domain will fall into decline and be left far behind by the other domains!"

"What's the use of saying this now? It's better to quickly exterminate the demonic beasts and minimize the losses. The Five Great Demonic Kings of the Demon Realm are sweeping all before them, practically unstoppable. Where is the Purple Extreme Thunder Peak? As the hegemon of our Eastern Domain, why are we not seeing any of their people!"

"Ah, I don't know. If the Purple Extreme Thunder Peak doesn't act, the Eastern Domain will surely fall."

"Damn it... at a time like this, what the hell is the Purple Extreme Thunder Peak doing!"

"......"

Countless regrets, urgency, doubts and curses reverberated throughout the Eastern Domain.

Meanwhile.

The capital of the Lishui Empire.

"Roar!!"

The terrifying beast roar shook heaven and earth, causing countless buildings to collapse. The ground was dyed crimson with fresh blood, with corpses strewn everywhere.

The severed heads, limbs, eyeballs, and various other dismembered remains left by the demonic beasts were a horrifying sight.

The pungent stench of blood madly invaded the nostrils, assaulting everyone's senses.

"Disciples, follow this Elder to slay them!"

"Yes!"

An Elder from the Azure Cloud Sect led a group of disciples to battle the horde of beasts.

Each strike was earth-shaking, with blood spraying in all directions - a truly brutal scene.

"Immortal, save us!!!"

The King of Lishui, along with a group of officials and palace beauties, kowtowed on the ground, their faces filled with terror.

The terrifying demonic beast tide had suddenly attacked, killing and injuring countless citizens in the capital in an instant. At this rate, their capital would be destroyed.

Boom!!

Lin Dong smashed the demonic beast in front of him with his staff, then turned around and impatiently shouted,

"All of you, shut up! The Second Elder and his disciples are still out there exterminating the demons. You'd better stay put obediently. Say one more word, and I'll feed you to the beasts!"

The King of Lishui and the officials dared not speak another word.

Lin Dong snorted coldly, looking at the raging battle scene, his eyes losing focus.

The sudden invasion of the demonic beasts was so unexpected. Now... the entire Eastern Domain was plunged into a brutal war.

Demonic beasts were everywhere, infiltrating every corner.

Lin Dong did not know where his father was at the moment...

"Roar!"

Suddenly, a beast roar interrupted Lin Dong's thoughts. He saw a dozen or so ordinary demonic beasts charging towards him, and his grip on the staff tightened, his gaze turning fierce!

The next moment, staff in hand, he swiftly pounced forward!

"You damned things! Die for me!"

.........

In the capital's prison.

"Master, the jailer said that a demonic calamity has broken out outside. Judging from those terrifying beast roars, it seems to be quite serious."

The old butler, holding the cold iron bars, looked towards the prison cell opposite.

Chen Dadao leaned against the prison wall, disheveled and listless. His lifeless eyes regained some focus as he said self-mockingly, "In that case, being locked up in this prison is actually quite safe. At least my life is not in immediate danger for the time being."

He lowered his head and continued, "Manager, the current disaster is exactly the same as what you predicted that day... Sometimes, I'm really impressed by your foresight. If only I had known earlier... Nevermind, there's no such thing as foreknowledge in this world."
