The night sky covered the heavens, in a snowy mountain range.

A black gate suddenly appeared, and after Luchang Sheng and the others passed through, the black gate vanished again.

"Huh? Where are we? I don't remember it being even half an hour..."

Shi Wushi looked around and scratched his head, his gaze meeting only darkness and white snow.

The others also looked to Luchang Sheng, puzzled.

The area beyond the Five Great Immortal Realms was vast, and they only knew the path leading to the Divine Road, having not explored much else.

Luchang Sheng sat down on the ground, waving his hand to conjure a campfire. The warm glow immediately enveloped them, and he smiled, "We're now only halfway to the Divine Road, so we have plenty of time. Everyone can rest here for a while."

What?

In just a short while, they were already halfway to the Divine Road?

Shi Wushi and the others exchanged shocked glances.

"Tch, so-called Immortal Emperors, a bunch of country bumpkins. How could you possibly understand the master's capabilities?"

The old turtle patted his own back, then calmly sat down, acting all high and mighty.

Hearing this, the others felt slightly embarrassed, but soon gathered around the campfire and sat down.

"Haha, it's been a while since we've all sat together like this," Chu Lingian said with a smile, looking at Luchang Sheng.

Feng Jiu pursed her lips, her gaze also turning to Luchang Sheng. She then suddenly stood up and bowed deeply, sincerely saying, "Immortal Emperor Changsheng, without you, we can't even imagine how difficult the Immortal Tribulation and the Divine Road would have been. We've been relying on you all this time."

A moment of silence fell.

Chu Lingian and the others also stood up and bowed.

Seeing this, the old turtle inwardly cursed, these brats have made him feel awkward about just sitting there. Helplessly, he also stood up.

Luchang Sheng warmed his hands by the fire and said with a gentle smile, "As the Immortal Emperor of the Central Immortal Realm, this is simply my duty, no need for thanks."

"Sit down."

Everyone sat back down.

Shi Wushi scratched his head and changed the topic, laughing, "Immortal Emperor Changsheng, where did you go for the past hundred million years? We were all so worried about you back then."

Luchang Sheng shook his head, not answering the question, but instead said, "You are about to go to the Ancestral Heaven Realm. I'm afraid you will still face pressure there."

Chu Lingian clenched his fist and said in a stern voice, "Immortal Emperor Changsheng, don't worry. Once we're in the Ancestral Heaven Realm, under the Ancestral Heaven Emperor's watchful eye, those people won't dare to act so recklessly against us."

"That's right, they won't dare to be so outrageous anymore!" Shi Wushi also had a fierce look in his eyes.

"Once we establish a foothold there, we'll definitely repay them a hundredfold!" Ancient Yin and Yang gritted his teeth and said.

The Divine Road had been obstructed, and the Underworld Demon Clan had come to devour them, wanting to destroy the Five Great Immortal Realms. Anyone subjected to such oppression would be unable to endure it, let alone these Immortal Emperors of the Immortal Realms.

"Eh? But Immortal Emperor Changsheng, aren't you coming to the Ancestral Heaven Realm with us?" Shi Wushi suddenly realized and asked, puzzled.

Chu Lingian and the others also narrowed their eyes, looking at Luchang Sheng.

"The Ancestral Heaven Realm, I will not go there for now," Luchang Sheng said calmly.

The others wanted to ask more, but they saw Luchang Sheng wave his hand to extinguish the campfire.

The old turtle also quickly gestured for them to be silent.

Chu Lingian and the others were slightly surprised, and they all concealed their auras, making their bodies appear hazy and transparent, completely hiding themselves.

The surroundings fell silent once more.

Only a cold wind blew, creating a faint rustling sound.

Shhh...

Faint footsteps could be heard echoing around them.
