"Big Dao, you and the butler are still chatting. Since it's still early, let's set off quickly," a woman's voice came from the carriage behind.

"Oh, okay!" Chen Dadao snapped out of it, ordered the coachman, then sat down with the butler and pulled down the curtain as the carriage slowly started moving.

Just then, a melodious female voice and hurried footsteps sounded outside.

Chen Dadao was taken aback, immediately shouting, "Stop!"

The carriage came to a halt.

Chen Dadao lifted the curtain and saw a strange woman standing by the carriage, panting as she clutched her knees, looking at him anxiously.

"Dadao, who is this girl?!" came the irritated voice of Chen Dadao's wife from the carriage behind.

"Ah, madam, this is a local resident of the town. She might not have attended the recent sendoff ceremony and has come to see us off today. Isn't that right, butler?" Chen Dadao hastily made an excuse.

The butler quickly agreed, "Yes, yes, yes!"

Hearing this, the woman in the carriage behind pulled the curtain shut and fell silent.

Chen Dadao heaved a sigh of relief, then asked the woman puzzledly, "Miss, who are you?"

The woman composed herself, occasionally glancing at the carriage behind, then said cautiously, "Lord Zhen, are you... leaving Moyu Town?"

Little Tong had brought some fruits to sell in Moyu Town today and happened to hear that the Lord Zhen was being relieved of his post. She had hurried to the town gate to wait for him.

Chen Dadao and the butler exchanged a look, then he smiled and said, "That's right, miss. Do you have something to say?"

Little Tong bit her lip and said hoarsely, "May I... see my brother?"

Brother?

Chen Dadao and the butler were puzzled. What brother? This woman couldn't be quite right in the head...

"Uh, miss, I really don't know who this brother of yours is. If you have nothing else, then we'll be on our way," Chen Dadao said with a forced smile, about to lower the curtain, as he didn't have much time to spare.

Little Tong urgently said, "Lord Zhen, it's your son!"

Before the bewildered Chen Dadao and butler could respond, an angry voice came from the carriage behind.

"Chen Dadao! When and where did you have a son with some vixen? If you don't give an explanation today, I'll kill myself right here!"

Chen Dadao panicked, glaring angrily at Little Tong, then frantically signaled to the old butler.

The old butler understood, turning back and solemnly stating, "The master has always been upright. This servant can vouch for the master with his life."

"Hmph." A cold snort came from the carriage, and no more sounds were heard.

Chen Dadao sighed heavily, wiping the sweat from his brow, and finally began to feel impatient with the strange woman in front of him.

Anyone would be in a terrible mood after being so disrupted.

He had almost been driven to the brink of divorce because of this!

"Miss, as you can see, I have no son. I must take my leave."

Chen Dadao lowered the curtain and commanded, "Depart!"

Little Tong's eyes reddened. Not knowing when she would see him again, she gathered her courage and cupped her hands around her mouth, shouting, "Yes, there is! Three years ago, when Moyu Town was plagued by demonic beasts, my brother took me to a courtyard in the Lord Zhen's residence. He was wearing blue robes, and I clearly remember his warm smile that thawed my heart in that cold winter!"

"Stop!!!"

Chen Dadao's expression changed, and he suddenly waved his hand.

The coachman pulled the reins, and the horses reared up, coming to a halt.

Seeing the carriage stop, Little Tong immediately felt relieved and ran over.

"Dadao, what are you-"

"Shut up!" Chen Dadao cut off the impatient words of the woman in the carriage with a stern face.

As for how peerless that good wine is, Helin doesn't know, after all, no one has succeeded in naming it before.

At the end, Gu Helin muttered softly.

"Bullshit, naming it is just a simple matter, who can't do it? Old Turtle can do it too! It's decided, it'll be called Turtle Grandpa Restaurant! The owner of this restaurant has to agree, whether they like it or not!"

The old turtle snorted coldly, sounding quite tyrannical.

"Ahem."

Lu Changsheng suddenly coughed lightly.

The old turtle reacted and his expression changed, quickly squeezing out a chrysanthemum-like smile and flattering, "Uh... no, no, it should be called Life Master Restaurant, haha, right, owner?"

Slap!

Lu Changsheng lightly slapped the old turtle on the head, sending him spinning dizzily like a top, seeing stars in front of his eyes, until he finally stabilized himself by grabbing onto Gu Helin next to him.

Lu Changsheng looked at the plaque silently and said, "Continue."

Gu Helin was so scared that his legs were shaking, he swallowed and said, "What the Turtle Senior said is not bad, it's true that naming it is something anyone can do... but, but all this time, no one has been able to successfully carve the characters onto the plaque... It's not that the naming is difficult, but that plaque is extremely strange."

Lu Changsheng stood with his hands behind his back, looking at the plaque, silent.
