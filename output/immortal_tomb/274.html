"What? Fu Shiyu, are you not satisfied?"

Liu Xing sneered coldly, mocking, "That's just the facts!"

Suddenly, a sword appeared in Fu Shiyu's hand, pointing directly at Liu Xing: "Liu Xing, you need not waste words. Today, dare you fight me? Whether dragon or insect, the world will judge for itself!"

Liu Xing's expression changed slightly. She didn't expect Fu Shiyu to suddenly become so unyielding. Although the Fu Clan had declined, this young master Fu Shiyu was indeed quite formidable.

To be honest, she was not certain she could defeat Fu Shiyu.

If she lost, her reputation would be greatly damaged!

But if she didn't accept the challenge, she would still lose face, which was unbearable.

The voices in the area quieted down, and countless gazes swept back and forth between Fu Shiyu and Liu Xing.

Many people's gazes towards Liu Xing already carried a hint of subtle mockery.

Sensing this, Liu Xing's slightly flushed face twitched, as she prepared to accept the challenge.

But just then,

Fu Liancai and a group of elders suddenly appeared by Fu Shiyu's side.

Whoa, the Clan Leader of the Fu Clan and the Fu Clan's upper echelons!

The crowd was stunned, slightly bowing their bodies. Although the Fu Clan had fallen, they were still not ordinary people to be trifled with!

Ouyang Jun, Liu Xing and the others also had their expressions change slightly. They didn't expect Fu Liancai, the Clan Leader, to actually come in person...

"Father!" Fu Tao hurriedly stepped forward, exclaiming joyfully.

"Clan Leader!" The clansmen also felt relieved in their hearts, cupping their fists in salute.

They knew that since the Clan Leader had arrived, there would be no more trouble today.

"Mm." Fu Liancai nodded slightly, his gaze, along with the elders', focusing on Lu Changsheng and Li Shueyan.

Fu Liancai's eyes flashed slightly as he looked deeply at the bronze mask, then smiled at Fu Shiyu, "Shiyu, who are these two?"

Fu Shiyu hesitated for a moment, glancing at Lu Changsheng, then said softly, "Father, now is not the time for idle chatter."

Hearing this, Fu Liancai nodded amiably towards Lu Changsheng and Li Shueyan, then turned around.

Looking at Ouyang Jun and Liu Xing, he narrowed his eyes slightly and said indifferently,

"Since the Clan Leader has not been greeted, is this how your elders have taught you etiquette?"

Ouyang Jun and Liu Xing's hearts suddenly jumped!

Facing the Clan Leader, it was impossible for them not to be nervous, after all, they were just juniors!

Ouyang Jun and Liu Xing couldn't withstand the pressure and were about to perform the greeting.

Suddenly, two more middle-aged men in embroidered robes appeared!

Boom!

The crowd was dumbfounded at this sight!

The Ouyang Clan Leader, the Liu Clan Leader!

What kind of day was this, that the three major Clan Leaders of Jingwun City had gathered together!!

Li Shueyan's hand holding Zhan Xu tightened!

These three Clan Leaders... were actually on par with the Saint-Emperor Realm experts of the Ancestral Celestial Realm!!

Their cultivation had almost caught up to his.

This Yongzhou Great Realm was truly terrifying. Upon just arriving, he had already encountered such figures.

It was as if they were everywhere.

Li Shueyan could hardly imagine how many more such powerful experts existed in this Yongzhou Great Realm - it was truly a daunting thought.

He had once been a Mirror Observation Monk of the Ancestral Celestial Realm, but now that he had come to this Yongzhou Great Realm, it seemed he would have great difficulty reaching the upper echelons here...

Thinking this, Li Shueyan glanced at the silent Lu Changsheng who seemed to be just watching the spectacle.

He felt slightly relieved.

At least, he had the Senior.
