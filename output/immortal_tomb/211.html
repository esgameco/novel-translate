"Elder Lei," said Lu Ye, dressed in a red robe, his appearance aged, his entire body seemingly exuding a scorching heat.

Li Tai Ling had the appearance of a middle-aged man, but he was lean, his gaze flashing like a sword.

However, there was one thing they had in common - both of them carried the aura of a superior.

"Please, have a seat," Lei Cheng Dong said with a smile, waving his hand, then coming to sit down in the seat opposite them, pouring himself a drink.

Lu Ye was the first to smile and say, "Elder Lei, you've misunderstood. Our sect and Elder Li are not here to mock you. As the number one sect in the human domain, how could we dare to do so?"

Li Tai Ling nodded slightly, his temperament seeming more reserved compared to Lu Ye.

Lei Cheng Dong raised his cup and smiled, "You two are too kind."

Seeing this, Lu Ye and Li Tai Ling raised their cups in return.

After taking a few sips,

Lu Ye suddenly said, "Elder Lei, that Xiao Yao Sect... is no simple matter."

The Ninth Elder of his sect, Fu Ming Yan, had clearly stated upon returning from the Extreme Cold Glacier that he had witnessed the affairs of the Xiao Yao Sect!

Abruptly broaching the main topic, Li Tian Ling's expression remained unchanged, but his gaze became more solemn. At their level, there were no mindless fools.

"The Xiao Yao Sect is naturally no simple matter," Lei Cheng Dong said, lowering his head to pour more wine as he spoke. "Previously, during the Nine Rank Sect Competition, Feather just happened to go to the Extreme Cold Glacier for a trip and witnessed the Xiao Yao Sect."

Lu Ye and Li Tai Ling exchanged a glance, neither of them continuing the conversation, but waiting for Lei Cheng Dong to continue.

Lu Ye was inwardly furious, though. Good, so Fu Ming Yan hadn't mentioned the matter of the Junior Elder of the Lei Sect, perhaps because he had forgotten about it in the face of the Xiao Yao Sect's affairs.

Lei Cheng Dong looked up at the two of them, or more precisely, at Lu Ye, and smiled, "Your Ninth Elder of the Xuan Huo Sect must have told you all about it."

Lu Ye silently nodded.

Of course he had told him, even mentioning that an elder of the Xiao Yao Sect had cursed the Xuan Huo Sect as bullshit...

Li Tai Ling, however, was somewhat puzzled. He currently knew nothing about the Xiao Yao Sect, and Lu Ye had never told him.

Lei Cheng Dong looked at Li Tai Ling and smiled, "Hehe, all the elders are at the Immortal Venerable realm, how could the Xiao Yao Sect be simple?"

Li Tai Ling's gaze sharpened.

Such strength... far exceeds that of a second-rank sect.

Although Li Tai Ling was inwardly surprised, his expression remained impassive as he tentatively probed, "Their strength is indeed impressive, but their foundation is still somewhat lacking compared to our first-rank sect."

Lu Ye glanced at him and smiled, "You're not wrong, but there's one thing you've overlooked... which is that the Patriarch of the Xiao Yao Sect has never been seen by anyone to this day."

Upon hearing this, Lei Cheng Dong's expression became more serious.

The unknown... is something to be wary of.

During this period, although he had been residing in the Thunder Ancestor Hall, it didn't mean he hadn't done anything.

He had sent out many elders to investigate the Patriarch of the Xiao Yao Sect, but to no avail.

This person was like a non-entity in the world,

truly... mysterious.

Seeing Lei Cheng Dong seemingly lost in thought, Lu Ye stood up and smiled, "Elder Lei, the reason we came to see you today was to inform you of this matter, but it seems you already know. Then our sect and Elder Li shall take our leave and rest. The battle across ranks is about to begin, so we won't disturb you further."

Hearing this, Li Tai Ling also stood up.

"Good, thank you," Lei Cheng Dong said, rising and cupping his hands in a salute.

Lu Ye and Li Tai Ling returned the gesture and then proceeded to leave.

However, Li Tai Ling turned back to look at Lei Cheng Dong one more time,

and Lei Cheng Dong looked back,

their gazes meeting and then quickly parting.
