The various sects had only just regained their senses, and they looked at the Xiaoyao Sect's camp with reverence, then carefully shifted their gaze to the side of Leifeng and the others...

The current junior sect master of the Great Day Thunder Sect was right here, after all...

Fu Mingyan lightly pressed his dry lips, glancing out of the corner of his eye at Leifeng, his heart filled with an indescribable feeling.

The Xiaoyao Sect had just had some conflicts with his Xuanhuo Sect, and he never expected them to run into the Great Day Thunder Sect so soon.

This was truly...

Their overall strength might not be as good as their Great Day Thunder Sect, but it was definitely far superior to a second-grade sect!

Leifeng paused for a moment, then suddenly raised his head and looked towards the Xiaoyao Sect, cupping his hands and smiling from afar, "Fellow cultivators of the Xiaoyao Sect, this junior sect master will wait for your arrival within the sect, we shall meet again in three months."

With that, he and the other three disappeared from the scene.

With such a major event happening, Leifeng had no mood to watch the competition of the ninth-grade sects.

As the junior sect master, he had to hurry back to the Great Day Thunder Sect and report this to his father.

The Xiaoyao Sect was no simple matter, his Great Day Thunder Sect could not afford to be careless.

...

In the Xiaoyao Sect's camp,

Tong Xin and Lin Dong's gazes were grave - this junior sect master of the Great Day Thunder Sect was no ordinary person.

At this time, Peng Fang waved his hand, "Let's go."

Moments later,

the Xiaoyao Sect also departed.

In the central region of the extreme cold ice field, only Fu Mingyan and the rest remained, along with the densely packed sects.

Everyone was silent,

the shocking event that had just occurred made the competition feel somewhat tasteless to them.

They knew that the upcoming competitions of the eighth, seventh, and sixth-grade sects, as well as the fifth, fourth, and third-grade sect competitions,

would be far less eye-catching and dazzling compared to the confrontation between the Xiaoyao Sect and the Great Day Thunder Sect.

The battle between a ninth-grade sect and a first-grade sect,

this fight, perhaps the entire region and even the heavenly domain would pay attention to it.

After all, such unprecedented level-jumping was unlikely to happen again in the future.

"Ahem." Fu Mingyan cleared his throat, interrupting everyone's thoughts. He said indifferently, "Sects, prepare yourselves, the competition is about to begin!"

Immediately, the expressions of the various sects became solemn.

They also wanted to finish quickly and then rush to the Lightning Continent to witness the level-jumping battle!

...

In the sky above the extreme cold ice field, the Xiaoyao Sect's spirit ship was slowly gliding.

On the deck, Zuo Qishan looked at Peng Fang and asked,

"Grand Elder, are we going directly to the Lightning Continent?"

Hearing this, Su Qianghe and the others also looked at Peng Fang, as the Sect Master was not here, the Grand Elder was the primary decision-maker.

Peng Fang looked at the golden characters in the sky, pondering, "Hmm, the Sect Master must already know of this matter, let's head directly to the Lightning Continent."

"Understood." Zuo Qishan and the others responded.

The next moment, the spirit ship abruptly changed direction, then with a thunderous roar, it shot out, disappearing into the distance as a point of light.

At the same time, in the mist below,

Lu Changsheng's eyes peered through the ice mist, gazing far away at the direction where the Xiaoyao Sect's spirit ship had vanished.

Then, with a casual tear in space, he leisurely stepped in.
