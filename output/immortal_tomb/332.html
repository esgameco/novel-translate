"A human city? Could it be that there are also an underground city and a celestial city?" The old turtle had some doubts in his heart.

Seeing this, the crowd immediately became spirited, the city guards were going to subdue this foreign race!

Yes, how could a foreign race just wander around in their human territory? Moreover, many foreign races have joined forces to suppress their human race, so they are particularly displeased with foreign races!

Among the human race, there are foreign races, but apart from Lord Sword Sovereign's mount that can transform into a human form and move freely in human territory, what other mount or foreign race can transform into a human and move around? Isn't that just asking for trouble?

It's not just like this in the human race, the territories of other foreign races generally don't allow foreign races to move around either.

The old turtle raised his eyebrows, well, they want to get rough?

Green light suddenly flashed from the fingertips of his sleeves, if these two city guards dared to get rough, he wouldn't hold back...

Sigh, it can't be helped, do I have to cause a massacre right after arriving in the human race?

Li Shuyan saw this and also secretly activated the Wanjian Yanxue technique, his skin turning red-hot and smoking. He was naturally on the old turtle's side.

Although he was part of the human race, he hadn't developed a strong sense of belonging since just arriving at the Divine-Demon Battlefield.

Huh, mount? Mount?

After all, being able to have a foreign race mount meant that this person's strength was not to be underestimated.

The crowd watched with their eyes twitching.

Well, to be so arrogant in their human territory, isn't that a bit too much?

They had never seen such a strong-willed foreign race mount before, Lord Sword Sovereign's mount didn't seem to be this unruly, right?
