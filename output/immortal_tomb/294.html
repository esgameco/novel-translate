One after another, the celestial champions were teleported out, either with bruised faces and swollen bodies or covered in blood.

The teleportation seemed to never cease.

From this, everyone could imagine how intense the battles must have been within the secret realm of the sands!

Fortunately, all those who had been teleported out so far were celestial champions at the Immortal Ancestor Realm.

So far, no celestial champions above the Divine Origin Realm had been teleported out.

As long as the celestial champions of the Divine Origin Realm did not unluckily encounter the top 20 elite champions, they would basically not encounter any accidents.

This was the Life Jade!

It was the one he had specially made for his son, Miu Lun, and carried with him.

The extinguishing of the light... meant that Miu Lun had...

Miu Hantian's eyes instantly turned bloodshot!! Lun'er!

Amidst countless bewildered gazes, he abruptly surged out from the crowd and stood before Duan Jiuniong.

"Clan Elder!"

The Miu Clan's elders were stunned and hurriedly followed after him.

At this moment, the surrounding people were discussing in an uproar:

"Ah, it's the Clan Elder of the Miu Clan, Miu Hantian! What's he doing...?"

"I don't know, but the Clan Elder's state seems a bit off..."

"What happened? Could it be that something unexpected happened to the Clan Elder's son inside?"

Duan Jiuniong's brows furrowed slightly. He naturally recognized Miu Hantian, as the Miu Clan held some weight in the Royal City. He asked in confusion, "Clan Elder, what's the matter?"

Miu Hantian slightly bowed, then opened his palm to reveal the lifeless Life Jade.

"Life Jade?" Duan Jiuniong was a remarkable figure, so he naturally recognized the Life Jade, but his gaze became even more puzzled. He couldn't quite comprehend what Miu Hantian meant.

At this time, the Miu Clan's elders who had gathered around Miu Hantian had changed drastically in expression.

Their young master had died?

A murderous aura emerged on Miu Hantian's face as he said in a deep voice, "City Lord Duan, this is the Life Jade of my son..."

Duan Jiuniong paused, asking in bewilderment, "Your son entered this secret realm of the sands?"

Miu Hantian closed his eyes and nodded.

Duan Jiuniong pursed his lips and said, "Clan Elder, please accept my condolences."

The surrounding people and many celestial champions were shocked. Miu Lun, a Divine Origin Realm champion, had died???

Miu Hantian gritted his teeth and transmitted a message, "City Lord Duan, please do me the favor of opening the secret realm of the sands so that I may quietly enter!"

Duan Jiuniong was slightly taken aback, and transmitted a low voice, "Clan Elder, I understand your current state of mind, but please be rational. Your actions would be breaking the rules of the Celestial Champion Assembly!"

Miu Hantian paused, speaking in an extremely low tone, "City Lord Duan, Miu Lun was my only son. How can you ask me to be rational?"

Duan Jiuniong's expression darkened, and his tone also became less pleasant, "Clan Elder, the Celestial Champion Assembly is not hosted by this City Lord. If you break the rules and are discovered and blamed by the Ancient Desolate Sword Clan, will your Miu Clan be the one to bear the consequences? If you say your Miu Clan will bear it, then I can immediately let you in!"

Upon hearing the words "Ancient Desolate Sword Clan", Miu Hantian suddenly calmed down, and beads of cold sweat even appeared on his forehead.

He apologetically said, "City Lord Duan, I'm sorry. I was a bit hasty in losing my beloved son."

Duan Jiuniong glanced at him and waved his hand, saying, "It's fine, as long as you understand the importance. By the way, Clan Elder, there's something I'm not sure if I should say or not."

"City Lord, please speak without reservation," Miu Hantian said sadly.

"This City Lord does have some impression of your Miu Clan's Miu Lun. He was also a top existence in the Divine Origin Realm."
