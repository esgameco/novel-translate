A majestic old man in a yellow robe stood outside the palace, looking at the black-armored man in front of him and said calmly:

"Xing Tian, summon the other guardians."

"Yes, Divine Emperor." Xing Tian bowed in response.

With a thought, he immediately transmitted the message to the other guardians.

In no time, eight armored figures appeared almost simultaneously.

The eight men placed their right hands over their left chests and bowed: "We, the First Guard, pay our respects to the Divine Emperor!"

Yin Yao's eyes flashed with a smile as he said, "Rise."

"Thank you, Divine Emperor!"

The guardians stood up.

Xing Tian took his place at the head of the guardian formation.

The nine of them were the nine great guardians under the Divine Emperor of the Ancestral Heavenly Realm, each with extraordinary cultivation and power!

In the Ancestral Heavenly Realm, no one other than the Divine Emperor himself could appoint them.

Yin Yao scanned the nine great guardians with a slightly satisfied expression, then squinted his eyes and said, "You shall accompany me to the Heavenly Sound Monastery. As for Monk Jing Guan... it's truly a pity."

He was well aware that Jing Guan had most likely perished in a calamity.

After all, in this Ancestral Heavenly Realm, aside from himself, there were only a few who had the ability to kill Jing Guan: the old Taoist from the Heavenly Evolving Taoist Sanctuary, the old demon who fell into the Abyss of Fallen Demons, and the ancient demon from the Endless Demonic Abyss.

It could be said that these three were the only ones who posed a threat to Jing Guan, but even they did not have the power to kill him.

Even for Yin Yao himself, killing Jing Guan would not be an easy task.

Jing Guan's death would be beneficial, as it would prevent him from attaining Buddhahood, which would not be advantageous for the Divine Emperor's Palace.

The upcoming ceremony to praise Jing Guan was a must-attend event. There were two reasons for this: first, to showcase the grandeur of the Divine Emperor's Palace, and second, to take the opportunity to suppress the various peak powers that would gather.

Lately, there had been undercurrents among the peak powers in the Ancestral Heavenly Realm, and some even seemed to have colluded with the Abyss of Fallen Demons and the Endless Demonic Abyss!

It seems that the Divine Emperor had not shown himself for a long time...

"Xing Tian, send the order to all the Divine King Halls. Let the Divine Kings put aside all their current affairs and attend the praising ceremony," Yin Yao said.

Xing Tian bowed, "Divine Emperor, I have already issued the order earlier."

"Good!" Yin Yao's gaze turned towards the clouds outside the palace, his eyes seemingly piercing through the mountains and rivers, "Depart for the Heavenly Sound Monastery."

"As you command!"

The six major Divine Realms and countless forces had learned of this earth-shattering event.

The Soul Realm.

"Hiss!" The old butler and the group of soul servants watched the scene before them with a sharp intake of breath.

In front of them, there was a golden-glowing soul body, hands clasped, eyes closed, slowly drifting towards the shores of the Reincarnation Lake.

Wherever it passed, all the soul bodies frantically fled and scattered, causing chaos.

When had they ever seen such a soul body?

They could only watch helplessly as the golden-glowing soul body drifted towards that direction, not daring to obstruct it.

The golden light that enveloped it was extremely oppressive to them. If they approached any closer, they had a premonition that their souls would be scattered.

"This... This... We must find a way to notify the Soul Emperor immediately!! You all stay here and watch! I'll go tell the head of the Huiyou Gate."

The old butler hurriedly flew in one direction.

But before he could go far, a middle-aged man with fiery red hair and a single horn on his forehead suddenly appeared in the area!

"Soul... Soul Emperor!" The soul servants trembled, as if seeing a savior, and quickly bowed down, respectfully calling out.

The old butler's soul body paused and quickly turned back.

The Soul Meng paid no attention to the old butler and the others, striding towards the golden-glowing soul body. In the next moment, he appeared before the golden-glowing soul body, looking down at it.

The golden-glowing soul body still had its hands clasped, eyes tightly closed, as if trying to pass through Soul Meng's body and continue deeper.

Soul Meng raised his hand towards the golden-glowing soul body, fingers spread wide, and a complex blood seal flashed in his palm. "Stop."

The next moment, the golden-glowing soul body halted in its tracks!

The golden-glowing soul body opened its eyes, looking up at the red-haired middle-aged man with a puzzled expression, hands still clasped. "I... Who am I?"
