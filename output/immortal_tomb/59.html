Damn, he had been careless.

"You... who are you exactly?"

Zhao Shenyuan's tone was very unstable, his mind completely in disarray. How could there be such a mysterious and powerful cultivator in the Lingchen Realm?

If it were before, he might still think the other had some hidden treasure.

But the fact that the demon spear was broken was right in front of him, he was not a fool, naturally understanding that the young man in the robe must be an absolute powerhouse!

"Old Turtle, you take care of it."

Lu Changsheng glanced in the direction of Moyu Town, waved his sleeve, and disappeared from the scene with Gu Helin.

"Hehe, you little demon are quite brave, daring to point a spear at the master?"

The old turtle looked at Zhao Shenyuan with a sinister smile on his face.

"Who are you guys exactly..."

Zhao Shenyuan asked in a somewhat low voice, his demonic qi surging, ready to attack or flee at the slightest sign of trouble.

"If you say the master is a Immortal Emperor, Grandpa Turtle is afraid you won't understand. I can only tell you that the bigshots in the great world you'll ascend to will have to respectfully serve the master..."

The old turtle sneered lightly.

Zhao Shenyuan's soul shook, his mouth slightly open, his face suddenly turning deathly pale.

But soon, he shook his head and said in a deep voice, "Nonsense!"

"Hehe, frog in the well."

The old turtle's eyes suddenly became bloodthirsty, looking like a complete villain!

He stretched out his palm and lightly pressed it towards Zhao Shenyuan...

The next moment,

Boom!!

Zhao Shenyuan's expression changed drastically, his body suddenly pressed down to the ground, like a lamb awaiting slaughter.

The previously somewhat calm him was now completely panicked and terrified, his eyes filled with fear.

Pitter-patter...

"Oh ho, can't move anymore?" The old turtle walked in front of Zhao Shenyuan and looked down at him indifferently, "This is only one-hundred-thousandth of Grandpa Turtle's pressure, and you can't even handle it?"

Zhao Shenyuan's pupils trembled violently, a great fear rising in his heart.

He regretted it, if he had known there would be such a terrifying variable, he wouldn't have come...

"Even an old servant like Grandpa Turtle is this strong... Now do you know the identity of the person your spear was just pointing at?"

As the old turtle chuckled, he slowly raised his foot.

Hearing those horrifying words, Zhao Shenyuan's heart was filled with immense fear.
