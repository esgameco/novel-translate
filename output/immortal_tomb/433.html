"Greetings, Ninth Prince."

Cloud Ao Xie nodded and stepped in quickly.

Afterwards, Cloud Ao Xie came to the back garden in a familiar manner, and saw a young lady feeding fish in the pavilion.

Cloud Ao Xie pursed his lips and walked over, calling out with a smile, "Ke Ning."

Ke Ning put down the feed in her hand. "Elder brother."

Cloud Ao Xie nodded and sat down beside her, smiling, "Aren't you bored staying in this Weiyang Pavilion every day? How about coming with this prince to stroll the streets?"

Ke Ning sat down across from Cloud Ao Xie and shook her head, "No need, thank you for the kind offer, elder brother."

"You..." Cloud Ao Xie sighed helplessly, but soon suppressed his smile. "Ke Ning, I've troubled you this time."

As he said this, Cloud Ao Xie looked at Ke Ning, but could not see any emotion on her face.

"Elder brother, do you know about the esteemed person from the Observation Pavilion?" Ke Ning suddenly asked.

Huh? The Observation Pavilion?

Cloud Ao Xie was taken aback, but immediately knew who Ke Ning was referring to.

He then blurted out, "How do you know of that esteemed person's existence?"

Logically speaking, Ke Ning has been secluded in this Weiyang Pavilion, indifferent to everything, so her information should have been cut off, right?

"A few days ago, that esteemed person played the zither..."

Ke Ning then briefly recounted the events, omitting the part about her going to the Observation Terrace.

Hearing this, Cloud Ao Xie suddenly understood.

A few days ago, he had just gone to Wanhua Garden with Senior Turtle, coincidentally missing it.

"I see, Ke Ning, that esteemed person is indeed a formidable existence. Ah, not to mention that esteemed person, even their servant Senior Turtle is an astounding existence. Uncle Ye's strength, I'm sure you're aware of it too, he can even withstand one move from Senior Turtle."

Cloud Ao Xie wasted no time in explaining the terrifying nature of Lu Changsheng.

After he finished, Cloud Ao Xie expected Ke Ning to be shocked, but found that her expression only showed some surprise, nothing more.

"Ke Ning, aren't you shocked?" Cloud Ao Xie couldn't help but ask.

"Elder brother, of course Ke Ning is shocked." Ke Ning hesitated for a moment before continuing, "It's just that Ke Ning has personally witnessed that esteemed person's abilities, so I've become a bit accustomed to it."

Ah? Personally witnessed?

Cloud Ao Xie was dumbfounded!

To be honest, apart from witnessing the青衣大人's (the esteemed person in blue)'s zither skills at Youying Lake that day, he didn't know anything else.

He only knew that Uncle Ye was extremely powerful based on what he had heard.

"Tell me the details."

Seeing that Cloud Ao Xie was all ears, Ke Ning briefly recounted the events of that day.

"Sss! The shattered door was reconstructed and looked exactly the same as before?"

Today, he finally learned of the true strength of the esteemed person in blue, even though it was from Ke Ning's account, but he believed that Ke Ning would not speak of mere jokes.

"Mm-hmm, elder brother, you haven't told me where that esteemed person came from yet," Ke Ning asked curiously.

Feng Changxuan's eyes flickered, and he and the two generals bowed, "Greetings to King Yunlan."

Huh?

Yunzao Qing nodded slightly.

Seeing this, Feng Changxuan smiled in satisfaction, his heart burning with excitement.
