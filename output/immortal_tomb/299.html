Outside the Sand Secret Realm, the crowd that had been observing dispersed like the receding tide towards the Ancient Wasteland Royal City.

Everyone knew that the Sand Secret Realm was just the appetizer, a screening process to select fifteen hundred people. The real show would be at the Heavenly Prodigy Tea Gathering.

The Tea Gathering, it sounded so harmonious, but in reality, the Tea Gathering would be filled with overt and covert struggles. After all, deciding the top ten at the Tea Gathering was no laughing matter.

Blood Demon Moon glanced at Lu Changsheng out of the corner of her eye, then silently headed towards the Ancient Wasteland Royal City.

"Hehe, Blood Immortal Lady, how about we cooperate for the Tea Gathering five days from now?"

Just then, Nangong Wenxin approached Blood Demon Moon, fanning himself elegantly.

"Nangong, this young master also wants to cooperate with the Blood Immortal Lady, why don't you make way?"

Lai Qing quickly followed, smiling at Blood Demon Moon.

Hearing this, Nangong Wenxin's face flashed with displeasure, but he quickly composed himself, smiled without saying a word, and kept his eyes fixed on Blood Demon Moon.

At the upcoming Heavenly Prodigy Tea Gathering, the heavenly prodigies would generally seek cooperative partners, as going at it alone would be rather difficult.

The highly sought-after top-tier heavenly prodigy ranked fifth, Blood Moon, was undoubtedly the best cooperative partner. Her background may be temporarily mysterious, but more importantly, Blood Moon was the only female among the top ten. And she was a great beauty to boot.

Although her lower face was veiled and not visible, her enchanting eyes and slender figure were enough to captivate.

In the distance, Evil Exterminator and Invisible Shadow clicked their tongues in annoyance. They had also wanted to cooperate with Blood Moon, but now that they were beaten to it, they immediately disappeared.

Blood Demon Moon withdrew her gaze from Lu Changsheng and without even sparing a glance at Nangong Wenxin and Lai Qing, indifferently said a single sentence before vanishing.

"Worthy?"

Nangong Wenxin and Lai Qing's faces suddenly stiffened.

After a moment of silence,

Nangong Wenxin suddenly smiled, waving his folding fan, and sneered at Lai Qing, "That's right, what are you, the Lai Clan young master, worthy of cooperating with the Blood Immortal Lady?"

Lai Qing's anger flared, and he shouted, "Nangong Wenxin, stop with the sarcasm. The Blood Immortal Lady clearly meant you! You effeminate man, you're not worthy!"

Nangong Wenxin's face darkened. "I'm talking about you! And who are you calling effeminate? Say that again if you dare!"

Lai Qing coldly smiled, not wanting to get further entangled with him, and vanished in a flash.

At the same time, outside the Sand Secret Realm, most of the people had already left.

Only Lu Changsheng, Li Shuoyan, and the people from the Fu and Liu clans remained.

And the Miu Clan as well.

That's when Miu Hantian and the others moved, approaching Lu Changsheng's side.

Liu Tu, Fu Liangcai, and the others were instantly alarmed, as if facing a great enemy!

But soon, their eyes bulged in shock.

"Young Master Hantian of the Miu Clan and the elders pay respects to the esteemed one."

Miu Hantian took the lead, bowing ninety degrees in salute.

The Miu Clan elders also hurriedly knelt down. "Greetings, esteemed one!"

At this moment, the north wind howled, the only sound being the gulping of Liu Tu and the others.

They knew full well that the person before them was from a royal clan!

What was going on?

Involuntarily, their gaze shifted back to Lu Changsheng's back, realizing they had greatly underestimated him.

Compared to Liu Tu and the others, Fu Shiyu and the three were even more bewildered. The Miu Clan?

The Miu Lun who had been killed by their senior, should he be the young master of the Miu Clan?

But but... how did their senior know the Miu Clan?

Fu Shiyu and the others felt a tightness in their hearts, unsure of how the Miu Clan elders would react if they knew their son had been killed by their senior.

Boom.
