If this Life Spirit Heavenly Lotus truly lived up to its name, then it would be truly defying the heavens...

To forcibly pull an ordinary person into the immortal path, this was nothing short of defying the heavens and changing one's fate.

It was an absolute reversal of destiny.

Tong Xin and Lin Dong were also filled with astonishment.

Chen Dasao stood there dumbfounded, his eyes becoming blurred from moisture...

"Dasao, let me ask you one more time, do you still wish to cultivate immortality?"

"Master, this immortality... I will not cultivate it anymore..."

The scene of the old steward's passing and his last conversation with Lu Changsheng was vividly etched in his mind.

That was also the last time they met.

"Venerable Gu, you keep this Heavenly Lotus. Dasao is already advanced in years, I don't want to waste this precious treasure."

Chen Dasao forced a smile and pushed the wooden box back.

Gu Helin furrowed his brows and said sternly, "Let you keep it, and you keep it. What's with this fussiness? I will carry out the Master's instructions to the letter!"

Saying that, he exerted strands of spiritual power to restrain the dazed Chen Dasao, and shouted at Tong Xin and Lin Dong, "You two, what are you still standing there for? Hurry up and grind the Life Spirit Heavenly Lotus into powder, and brew it into a tea for him to drink!"

"Yes, Master!"

Tong Xin and Lin Dong immediately sprang into action, quickly grinding the Life Spirit Heavenly Lotus into powder, then adding it to the tea, stirring it, and finally forcing the entire concoction down Chen Dasao's mouth, their movements fluid and seamless.

In the next moment,

A scorching heat surged up from Chen Dasao's dantian, and he felt as if his entire body was on fire, his qi and blood in a state of violent turmoil.
