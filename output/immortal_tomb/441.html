The old turtle gestured to the dancing girl with his eyes, then quickly got up from the bed, put on his clothes with a wave of his hand, and then went to open the door a little, where he saw a figure about to leave.

It was indeed Zhu Xingqiu. What was he doing here?

The old turtle's eyes flashed, and he asked suspiciously, "Grand Steward, what are you doing here?"

Zhu Xingqiu was stunned for a moment, turned around, and saw the old turtle, looking confused: "Senior Turtle? Senior?"

The old turtle coughed dryly and stepped out of the room without a change in expression. "You haven't answered why you are here."

Zhu Xingqiu bowed and then explained the matter to the old turtle in detail.

A moment later, the old turtle, who had understood the whole situation, pointed at Zhu Xingqiu and said unhappily, "Well, so you are the so-called Divine Spirit Realm cultivator from the ancient Wind Luan Kingdom!"

Zhu Xingqiu gave an embarrassed smile.

The old turtle stared at him for a long time, until Zhu Xingqiu felt puzzled, then shook his head and said, "You'd better leave quickly. At a time like this, coming to find your master, are you not tired of living?"

Zhu Xingqiu's face turned pale, and he could only nod. Just as he was about to leave, the old turtle suddenly asked, "Do you know where Elder Peng and the others, as well as Li Shuryan and the children, are now?"

Zhu Xingqiu shook his head and sighed, "Senior Turtle, after the Sect Master sent us out that day, the Grand Elder just gave a few casual instructions, and everyone went their separate ways. How could I know where they are?"

"Alright, you'd better leave quickly and behave yourself!" The old turtle waved his hand.

"Yes, Senior Turtle, then I'll take my leave." Zhu Xingqiu bowed slightly and then disappeared.

The old turtle smacked his lips, no longer interested, tightened his belt, and then sent the dancing girl away with some treasures.

Yun Zeqing did not hesitate and immediately led his troops to the Wind Luan Kingdom. Now was the best time to annex Wind Luan, as the Wind Luan monarch had been killed, and the elite troops had been completely destroyed.

After a few months, the ancient Wind Luan Kingdom was successfully merged into the Cloudy Mist Kingdom.

Yun Zeqing was excited in his heart. He issued a general amnesty, and for the next hundred years, the towns and cities of his Cloudy Mist Kingdom would have a 30% tax reduction.

In the Cloudy Mist Palace,

Yun Zeqing held a grand banquet, inviting all the officials. Of course, Yun Zeqing went to the Observation Pavilion in person to invite Lu Changsheng, but he was rejected.

Yun Zeqing thought it was because of the previous banquet that left a bad impression on Lu Changsheng, so he repeatedly assured that nothing like the previous banquet would happen again.

Seeing that Lu Changsheng still refused, Yun Zeqing could only bow and withdraw, finally ordering that whatever was served at the banquet should also be arranged at the Observation Pavilion.

At the banquet, cups and plates were exchanged, and Yun Zeqing suddenly noticed that Ye Xunwang's expression did not seem too good. His eyes flashed with puzzlement as he asked, "Brother, do you have something on your mind?"

Ye Xunwang came back to his senses and did not hide it, sighing and saying directly, "A few days ago, the family contacted me."

What?

Yun Zeqing was stunned and stammered, "Brother, how long have you been away from the family? Why did they suddenly contact you, and what's more puzzling is how they were able to contact you after all these years?"

Ye Xunwang pressed his lips, and a palm opened, revealing a red glow slowly emerging from his palm. "At first, I was also surprised, but later I found out that the family could contact me because of this hidden bloodline mark in my body. Not only can the family contact me through this, but I estimate they can also clearly know my location."

Huh?

Yun Zeqing stared in shock at the red glow in Ye Xunwang's palm, a bloodline mark?
