Hmm?

The King of Lishui furrowed his brows, and the officials also exchanged glances.

Seeing this, Dong Ge glanced at the King of Lishui's expression, and hastily stood up, squinting his eyes at Chen Dadao and shouting,

"Little Chen, what's wrong with entering the courtyard? It's an honor for the King to hold an early meeting here, you should quickly step aside! Don't block the King's path."

Chen Dadao nodded at Dong Ge, thanking him for the good intentions, but still firmly stated, "The King cannot enter here."

Dong Ge paused, and with a sigh, said no more.

The expression on the King of Lishui's face fluctuated for a moment, but then he suddenly smiled and asked while looking into the courtyard, "Why?"

Chen Dadao's expression was conflicted, without the clever steward by his side, he couldn't think of a suitable reason at the moment!

"Could it be that your Garrison Master's Mansion is hiding some secret? Today, I the King must go in and take a look."

The King of Lishui indifferently glanced at Chen Dadao, then led the officials into the courtyard...

Yesterday, he still thought this Garrison Master Chen was not bad, worthy of great use, but today he has the audacity to defy him!

Chen Dadao snapped out of his daze, only to find that the King of Lishui and the officials had already entered the outer courtyard!

He hurriedly rushed in, urgently shouting, "Your Majesty, you cannot enter, you cannot enter!"

Of course, the King of Lishui heard his low roar, but he ignored it, and was even prepared to punish this Garrison Master!

This is simply disrespectful!

But soon, the King of Lishui and the officials halted in their steps, staring blankly at the man in blue robes fishing in the pavilion.

His figure was as motionless as a sculpture, exuding a sense of detachment from the world.

That demeanor, they were all too familiar with - the aura of an immortal!

Even compared to the immortals they had seen before, his aura seemed purer, more ethereal!

At this time, Chen Dadao anxiously ran to Luchang Sheng's side, kneeling and trembling as he said,

"Sir, I really couldn't stop the King and his men... I've disturbed your tranquility, and I deserve to be executed for my crime!"

The outer courtyard fell silent.

The King of Lishui and the officials were all staring blankly at this scene, and soon, a sense of trepidation arose in their hearts.

They were not fools, and in just a moment, they understood the whole situation.

The King of Lishui now hated this Chen Dadao!

If he had just directly said there was an immortal here, he wouldn't have dared to boldly enter with so many officials!

Nor would they be in this predicament now!

"We pay our respects to the Immortal!"

The King of Lishui hurriedly led the officials in bowing down.

The King of Lishui trembled as he said, "We did not know the Immortal was here, and barged in so brazenly. Please forgive us!"

Chen Dadao watched as the King of Lishui, who had been so high and mighty just a moment ago, now became so frightened, and his respect for Luchang Sheng grew even deeper.

Involuntarily, his desire to cultivate the Dao also became stronger, but... it seems he no longer has the chance.

If time could turn back, and he could choose again, he would still make the same choice as before!

He... has no regrets.

"You may rise," Luchang Sheng said lightly, gazing at the water.

"Thank you, Sir!" Chen Dadao let out a big sigh of relief and slowly stood up.

"We thank the Immortal!"

The King of Lishui and the officials were also delighted, and prepared to stand up...

But soon their faces turned pale, as they found that no matter how hard they tried, their knees could not leave the ground, let alone stand up.

"Did I tell you to stand up?" Luchang Sheng spoke softly.

The King of Lishui and the others trembled inwardly.

Indeed, he is an Immortal. Without even moving, he has suppressed them.

After that, they dared not speak further, afraid that at this moment, any words might incur his wrath.
