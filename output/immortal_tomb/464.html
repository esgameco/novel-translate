The Heavenly Astral Tower of the He Clan.

He Lian and the venerable ancestor of the He Clan, He Yan Ci, brought the expressionless He Xi Tong to the top of the Heavenly Astral Tower.

"Star Master, the Millennium Calamity Body has been brought here," He Lian said to the man in the black robe.

However, He Yan Ci stared intently at the man in the black robe, his eyes flickering. As the clan leader, He Lian had of course already had an in-depth discussion with this mysterious black-robed man.

Through what He Lian had told him, He Yan Ci naturally also knew of the unfathomable mystery surrounding this black-robed man, and at the same time his heart was filled with vigilance and dread towards this black-robed man who had inexplicably shown them warmth.

The black-robed man looked at He Xi Tong, but his expression could not be seen under the black robe. He quickly averted his gaze and said, "Venerable Ancestor of the He Clan, time waits for no one. Quickly enter the Extreme Refinement Formation with the Millennium Calamity Body."

He Yan Ci glanced at He Xi Tong beside him, then nodded slightly, and then went into the Extreme Refinement Formation, sitting down in a cross-legged position.

Afterwards, He Xi Tong also calmly entered the Extreme Refinement Formation, sitting down on the ground, and suddenly said, "Don't forget the heavenly oath you made."

He Lian's heart stirred, and she replied calmly, "Don't worry, as the clan leader, I naturally won't break a heavenly oath."

He Xi Tong was then relieved, and closed his eyes in resignation.

"Are you ready? I'm going to activate the Extreme Refinement Formation!" the black-robed man urged.

He Yan Ci's eyes narrowed, and he transmitted a message to He Lian with an unchanged expression, "Alian, keep a close eye on the black-robed man's movements. If anything is amiss, stop him immediately!"

"Ancestor, don't worry," He Lian responded, but in her heart, she felt bitter. The black-robed man was so mysterious, if he truly had malicious intentions, could she really stop him?

"It seems you're ready." The black-robed man slightly opened his hands, and the complex patterns on the ground suddenly flashed with dazzling multicolored light!

The black-robed man continued, "Clan Leader, you don't need to stay here. Go down and prepare to command your He Clan disciples to activate the Sky-Shielding Formation."

He Lian's gaze changed slightly. She couldn't leave the Heavenly Astral Tower, so she said, "Star Master, don't worry, the activation of the Sky-Shielding Formation has already been arranged for the clan elders."

The black-robed man looked at He Lian and said mockingly, "Clan Leader, it seems you don't really trust me..."

He Lian forced a smile and said, "Star Master, how could the clan leader not trust you?"

"Hehe, it's fine if you want to stay in the Heavenly Astral Tower, but are you sure the Ouyang and other ancient clans can hold them off?" the black-robed man said with a hint of a smile.

He Lian fell silent for a moment, then said, "The refinement of the Millennium Calamity Body will be completed in no more than three days. Just three days, the clan leader believes in Ancestor Ouyang and the others."

At this moment, the black-robed man seemed to have forgotten about the existence of Lu Changsheng, and said with a mysterious smile, "Then do as you wish... But Clan Leader, remember to inform your clan elders that the Sky-Shielding Formation should not be activated now, but at the most critical moment of refining the Millennium Calamity Body..."

"Okay, the clan leader will wait for the Star Master's command."
