Here is my translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The Sect Master was soul-searched?!

The elders' pupils shrank, and they exchanged glances.

When did this happen?

"Still not saying? Don't worry, just speak up, and I will help you take care of it!"

Shen Jing Tao's gaze was sharp, and his brow was gloomy.

The current Sect Master of his Qing Yun Sect was actually soul-searched, which was a great humiliation, as if they didn't even consider Shen Jing Tao at all.

How dare they!

Moreover, he was now at the Nascent Soul stage, so naturally, he had the confidence.

The elders felt Shen Jing Tao's anger and were as timid as cicadas in winter.

The Patriarch was angry.

Faced with such questioning, Shen Miao Jun could only bitterly say,

"Zi Ji Thunder Peak, Jiang Yuan."

The elders were stunned, and their ears seemed to be filled with thunderous sounds.

In the Eastern Domain, or even the entire Immortal Cultivation World, who doesn't know the name of the Patriarch of Zi Ji Thunder Peak, Jiang Yuan?

"Cough, what is the story here, Miao Jun?"

Shen Jing Tao quickly suppressed the anger on his face, coughed dryly, and asked somewhat embarrassedly.

If the one who soul-searched was that Jiang Yuan, then it's temporarily settled...

The strength is not enough, even if he gets beaten, he must stand straight.

Shen Miao Jun was not surprised by the change in Shen Jing Tao's expression, then said softly,

"Three years ago..."

After Shen Miao Jun finished recounting the cause and effect,

the faces of the elders were already filled with anger.

To soul-search without reason, this is simply unreasonable. Don't they know how much damage soul-searching can do to a cultivator?

A Tribulation Transcendence expert, can they just do as they please?

And that Jiang Yuan, after soul-searching, also knew the truth, but in these three years, there was not the slightest indication, how hateful...

"So, I won't go to observe the Ascension Tribulation this time, Miao Jun said in a hoarse voice. Even if I go, it would be useless, and if I encounter that Jiang Yuan, I..."

Shen Miao Jun looked at Shen Jing Tao.

What troubled her the most was that since she was soul-searched three years ago, her cultivation path seemed to be visible to the naked eye.

Shen Jing Tao was silent for a long time, then sighed towards the sky,

"Miao Jun, it is this Patriarch's uselessness."

If he were also at the Tribulation Transcendence stage, then Jiang Yuan would certainly not dare to be so reckless!

Unfortunately, there are no "ifs".

"Patriarch, don't blame yourself, after all, Jiang Yuan is the overlord of the Immortal Cultivation World in the Eastern Domain, and our Qing Yun Sect is just a small sect, naturally we can't provoke him," Shen Miao Jun comforted.

Shen Jing Tao waved his hand, somewhat powerlessly: "Miao Jun, don't worry, this Patriarch will try his best to help you find some heavenly treasures to repair your spiritual sense. Here is a spiritual herb that can help with spiritual sense recovery, you take it first."

After saying that, he flipped his hand and handed a blue flower emitting a misty aura to Shen Miao Jun, sighed, and then disappeared.

"Ganda Flower."

The elders were shocked as they looked at the blue flower in Shen Miao Jun's hand, inwardly exclaiming in amazement.

The Patriarch really has such a precious treasure.

Shen Miao Jun looked at the flower in her hand and also sighed.

The Ganda Flower is quite effective in repairing spiritual sense, but it's a pity that there's only one.

For her severely damaged spiritual sense, the effect would be somewhat limited.

She then looked at the Grand Elder and said,

"Grand Elder, you are now at the Initial Nascent Soul stage, you should go with the Patriarch to observe the Ascension Tribulation."

The Grand Elder was slightly taken aback, opened his mouth, and finally bowed and said, "Yes."

"As for the other elders, go guard the Mundane Realm, do not let your guard down."

"Yes."

Soon, the elders in the hall left, only the Ancient Crane Forest was still hesitating to speak.

"Hmm? Ancient Elder, do you have anything else?" Shen Miao Jun asked curiously.

Ancient Crane Forest took a light breath and said in a solemn voice, "Sect Master, I have just passed through the Heart Demon Tribulation, and the Nascent Soul Thunder Tribulation may come soon..."

He was confident that the Demon Sovereign and Demon King would not be able to hold back then!
