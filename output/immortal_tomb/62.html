Here is the full English translation of the excerpt from the Chinese novel:

The laughter sounded extremely grating in the ears of the villagers who had stepped forward. Their bodies were trembling violently, yet they stubbornly kept their heads lowered, not daring to look at the helpless figure of Gu Helin.

"Hehe, Gu Venerable, so what now? I will ask you one last time, are you willing to join my demonic realm? Leave this mortal realm of warmth and coldness behind! I will help you exterminate these despicable ants!"

At this time, the Great Earth Dragon Bear finally asked this question again.

All that he had done before was just to set the stage for this moment.

As soon as these words were spoken, the villagers who had stepped forward suddenly raised their heads, looking at the Great Earth Dragon Bear with disbelief and terror in their eyes.

"Didn't you say you would spare us?!"

"You went back on your word!"

"......"

The Great Earth Dragon Bear impatiently shouted, "Shut up!"

The demonic beasts also let out terrifying roars.

Instantly, the villagers were so frightened that they dared not make a sound, their eyes filled with a lifeless despair.

"No, I will not join the demonic realm," Gu Helin said as he stood up and patted his hands.

Everyone was stunned, their pupils trembling wildly, their hearts filled with bitterness to the extreme.

"Huh? Have you gone mad? Even after all this, you still insist on protecting these ants?" The Great Earth Dragon Bear's pupils contracted as he roared angrily.

"It's not that I'm protecting them, I simply don't want to join your demonic realm," Gu Helin said expressionlessly.

"Alright then, since that's the case, you can die together with them. What a waste of your pill-refining skills." The Great Earth Dragon Bear took a deep breath, and as he leaped forward, he sneered, "All demons, hear my command! You have five breaths of time to crush them, Gu Helin is mine!"

"Yes!!" The demonic beasts obeyed the order, emanating a terrifying aura as they surged towards Moyu Village.

This horrifying scene stunned everyone, and then they instinctively began to flee in all directions.

However, due to the chaos, some people tripped and fell, leading to a stampede.

Cries of anguish filled the air.

"Master! You must escape! Don't worry about them or me and Lin Dong!" Tong Xin shouted at Gu Helin.

The aura of the Great Earth Dragon Bear was too terrifying, the master couldn't possibly resist him, but escaping should be no problem.

"You two, retreat."

Gu Helin turned and smiled gently at Tong Xin and Lin Dong, and then, before their horrified gaze, his abdomen began to swell...

"Master... no!"

Tong Xin and Lin Dong rushed towards Gu Helin, tears streaming down their faces.

Even the Great Earth Dragon Bear was stunned by this sight, and hastily retreated!

Although he was on par with a late-stage Nascent Soul cultivator, being faced with an early-stage Nascent Soul self-explosion, he would certainly sustain heavy injuries.

"Don't come over here!" Gu Helin shouted at Tong Xin and Lin Dong.

Tong Xin and Lin Dong ignored him, their eyes filled with determination.

Just as the situation was at a critical juncture,

Lu Changsheng appeared like a ghost in front of Gu Helin, gently tapping his abdomen with a finger.

Instantly, the swollen abdomen quickly subsided.

"Master! You... " Gu Helin was stunned for a moment, his face bitter.

He never expected Lu Changsheng to intervene, never expected...

"Master!" Tong Xin and Lin Dong slammed on the brakes, their faces filled with joy, but their tears continued to flow, they had been truly terrified.

Lu Changsheng glanced at the chaotic scene, and with a light wave of his sleeve, all the demonic beasts, villagers, and everyone in various poses were frozen in place!

The world suddenly fell silent.

Lu Changsheng's gaze turned to Gu Helin, scrutinizing him for a moment, and then softly asked, "Even after all this, you still disregard your life. Is it worth it?"

Gu Helin was taken aback, his eyes reddening as he smiled and said, "It is."

Lu Changsheng nodded slightly, without saying anything further.
