"Master, when shall we go?" the old turtle asked cautiously.

The old turtle was a bit afraid that Luchengsheng would disdain him for being weak and not take him along.

The old turtle knew that the "worms" on the so-called Creation Pillar must be terrifyingly powerful.

Perhaps even the master would be in danger, and as the master's mount, he must accompany him, even if it meant death.

Luchengsheng looked at the old turtle for a while and said, "After you have mastered the laws of space, destruction, and yin-yang, we will go. You are still a bit weak now."

In fact, Luchengsheng would most likely take the old turtle even without him asking.

As an immortal celestial turtle, the old turtle had extraordinary talents. If he could thoroughly comprehend the three supreme laws and evolve into the Dao or even higher, he could be of great help.

The Creation Pillar was immensely, overwhelmingly huge! It could support the endless void and nurture countless galaxies, showing just how colossal it was!

From the moment Luchengsheng took the old turtle as his mount, it had been predestined.

"However, the 'worms' from the endless void outside the Creation Pillar, capable of gnawing at the foundation of the endless void, must be extremely powerful. Are you afraid, old turtle?" Luchengsheng suddenly chuckled lightly.

This was the first time Luchengsheng had used the word "extremely" to describe his opponents!

Perhaps one or two or three or a few of them would be no problem for Luchengsheng, but the worms on the eight Creation Pillars must be countless!

The old turtle swallowed hard, then trembled as he spoke:

"Master, the old turtle fears nothing, not even death! If I can be of any help to you, it would be the greatest honor of my life."

What the old turtle really wanted to say was that the endless void belonged to all, and if anything happened to it, the countless beings within would all perish!

Why must the master go alone to face this? Why not gather the top experts of the endless void to confront it together?

Luchengsheng seemed to understand what the old turtle was thinking, and said, "It's too far. The Creation Pillar is at the end of the endless void, a place no one can ever reach."

The old turtle understood and fell silent. If the master said it was far, then it must be truly, extremely far.

After all, in the old turtle's understanding, the endless void was boundless.

Except for the master, who else could know the endless void had an end?

"Old turtle, I shall tell you that any being who dies within the order and causality of the endless void, I have the supreme means to revive them!

But at the Creation Pillar, if they die by the hands of those 'worms', that would be true death, and even I would be powerless to save them," Luchengsheng said softly.

The old turtle unhesitatingly replied, "Master, rest assured. If it comes to that, the old turtle has lived enough."

Luchengsheng looked deeply at the old turtle. "Let's go."

"Yes."

The old turtle quickly flew forward.

Luchengsheng's gaze became hazy as he looked at the sea of laws.

No wonder he had knocked the old turtle unconscious and placed him in the Demon-Subduing Tower, no wonder he had left the Xiaoyao Sect disciples to roam in the Qiankun Heaven.

His words about the Xiaoyao Sect being a burden were just a subconscious protection.

If the Xiaoyao Sect disciples went to the Creation Pillar, with their strength, they would most likely all perish.

Whether it was Tongxin, Lin Dong, Guhelin, Chen Dadao, or the elder executives like Peng Fang, they would most likely all die.
