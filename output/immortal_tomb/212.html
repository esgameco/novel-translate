When Leiyu and Leiting appeared in Jinglei City, it immediately caused a huge commotion!

The current junior sect master and holy maiden of the Great Day Thunder Sect had actually shown up!

"Tsk, the battle beyond their level is happening the day after tomorrow, yet the junior sect master and holy maiden still have the heart to come out and play?"

"I think you're the one who's seriously ill. Do you think they're from the Carefree Sect? They're from the Great Day Thunder Sect, the mightiest sect in the human domain!!"

"That's right, and not to mention, if they're not out playing, are they cultivating? What could they possibly cultivate in just two days? Moreover, this is the style of a great sect, showing that the Great Day Thunder Sect doesn't even consider the Carefree Sect worthy of their attention."

"Oh, okay."

"......"

At the same time,

Leiyu and Leiting were strolling down the street, with the two guardians from the Extreme Cold Ice Field following behind them.

The streets were bustling with people.

Leiyu and Leiting's expressions were quite unpleasant, as they were followed by a dense crowd of people, all looking at them excitedly or with reverence.

"Guardian Zhao, disperse these cultivators," Leiyu ordered, sighing helplessly, "It's really troublesome. We just want to go out and relax, but we're being gawked at like monkeys. Isn't that right, sister?"

Leiting: "Mm-hmm!"

"Yes, junior sect master,"

The guardian Zhao following Leiyu immediately obeyed the order, quickly surveying the surroundings and shouting, "No one is allowed to follow! If this guardian discovers anyone still following... hehe."

As he spoke, a terrifying pressure akin to that of a Celestial Venerable suddenly erupted, vanishing in an instant!

The crowd felt their scalps tingle, and fear appeared in their eyes as they scattered in all directions, not daring to continue following.

However... after distancing themselves, they still couldn't help but watch from afar.

Seeing this, Guardians Zhao and Lin frowned, intending to deal with these cultivators.

Leiyu stopped them, a handsome smile appearing on his face: "It's fine, Guardian Zhao. Let them be, as long as they don't get too close."

Hearing this, Guardian Zhao sighed inwardly, bowing respectfully: "Yes, junior sect master."

Afterwards, the four of them continued strolling down the street, with no dense crowd following behind them anymore, making Leiyu much more comfortable.

The main reason was that life in the sect was too boring. The disciples were too respectful towards him, and he didn't have a single person he could chat with comfortably. Even though his temperament was calm, the disciples still kept their distance from him.

Not to mention the disciples, even those Celestial Venerables and Immortal Saints were extremely courteous to him!

Perhaps, this is the trouble of being the junior sect master of the Great Day Thunder Sect.

Involuntarily, Leiyu's thoughts turned to that snowy night in the Extreme Cold Ice Field, and the young man in blue robes by the campfire.

Although their interaction was limited, that young man in blue was quite nice.

Not only did he not fear Leiyu's identity as the junior sect master of the Great Day Thunder Sect, but he had also generously gifted them a jar of delicious wine that night.

And that jar of wine... was truly delicious beyond words!

Thinking about it now, Leiyu still couldn't get over the lingering taste.

At the time, his sister and the two guardians had also drunk it, and they all praised it endlessly.

However, they didn't encounter him again in the central region of the Extreme Cold Ice Field later on.

Reminiscing this, Leiyu sighed regretfully.

But the next moment, his wandering and indifferent gaze suddenly widened...

"Junior sect master, it's him!!"

The startled voices of the two guardians suddenly rang in Leiyu and Leiting's ears.

Even the originally listless Leiting's eyes lit up!

It's him!

That night's wine was truly delicious!

"What are you shouting about? Of course I've noticed," Leiyu said calmly, a look of delight appearing on his face as he quickly strode towards the wine house.

Leiting and the two guardians hurriedly followed.

"Heavens, the junior sect master and holy maiden are actually heading towards the Encounter Immortal Pavilion!"

"Whew, the business at the Encounter Immortal Pavilion is going to be booming from now on!"

"......"

Countless discussions arose on the street, and everyone wanted to follow them in, but remembering Guardian Zhao's earlier display, they dared not continue following.
