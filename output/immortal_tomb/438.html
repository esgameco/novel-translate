"Your Majesty, it's the Great Army of Feng Luan!"

The officials cried out in pale-faced alarm.

Suddenly, all the troops and guards were on high alert!

Yun Cang stared intently at the approaching Great Army of Feng Luan, his gaze gradually growing more serious. He turned to Ye Xunwang beside him and asked:

"My wise brother, can you sense it as well?"

"Yes, such a subtle yet captivating aura, it must be an expert at the Divine Spirit Realm without a doubt," Ye Xunwang replied solemnly.

Yun Cang shook his head and wryly smiled, "Now I understand."

"Your Majesty, what do you mean?" Ye Xunwang asked puzzledly.

Yun Cang spoke calmly, "Since the ancient Feng Luan nation has experts at the Divine Spirit Realm, they must want to swallow our ancient Cloud Mist nation. Even if Princess Ning is married off, the Great Army of Feng Luan will still attack us soon."

Ye Xunwang nodded without further comment. Although he was not a native of Cloud Mist, he had lived there for a long time. Moreover, given his close relationship with Yun Cang, he naturally did not wish for Cloud Mist to fall.

At this moment, Feng Changxuan's eyes gleamed with pride and emotion.

He never expected his father would mobilize such a formidable force just to rescue him...

"Hehe, Yun Cang, now do you know the weight of this prince in the ancient Feng Luan nation?" Feng Changxuan sneered at Yun Cang.

But Yun Cang had no time to pay attention to him, for the Great Army of Feng Luan had already arrived above the Eastern Gate!

At this moment, the Great Army of Feng Luan and the Cloud Mist Army faced each other, an overwhelming aura of deadly seriousness sweeping through the heavens and earth!

Suddenly, the Great Army of Feng Luan parted to the sides, and two imperial palanquins flew to the forefront.

The Feng Luan monarch emerged from the palanquin, standing on the platform and smilingly cupping his hands towards Yun Cang, "Cloud Mist monarch, it's been a while!"

Yun Cang's eyes narrowed slightly as he cupped his hands in return, his smile not reaching his eyes, "The Feng Luan monarch visits my Cloud Mist without prior notice, and I couldn't prepare a proper welcome."

The sudden attack by the ancient Feng Luan nation was truly unexpected by Yun Cang, leaving him in a very passive position.

Due to Feng Luan's surprise attack, Yun Cang had no time to gather troops from other regions, so the two armies were not too far apart in strength.

The ancient Feng Luan nation truly gave them no chance at all...

"Hehe, we're old friends, what's the need for preparation?" The Feng Luan monarch waved his hand casually, exuding a sense of magnanimity.

But down below, Feng Changxuan felt a chill in his heart...

Because Feng Changxuan realized that since his father's arrival in the Cloud Mist nation, he had not looked at him even once...

What's going on? That night in the blue robe, and now his father, it's as if they're deliberately ignoring him?

Or could it be that his father, Feng Changxuan, has been abandoned?

Thinking this, Feng Changxuan's heart grew even colder, and he couldn't help but call out tentatively, "Your son Changxuan pays respects to the King!"

But to his surprise, the Feng Luan monarch still did not look at him.

Feng Changxuan's heart immediately sank.

The people of Cloud Mist also had uneasy expressions, a bad premonition arising in their hearts.

Could it be...

Suddenly, a sword appeared in Yun Cang's hand, pressing against Feng Changxuan's neck as he smiled, "Feng Luan monarch, what do you think of this?"

Feeling the cold blade against his neck, Feng Changxuan trembled and looked pleadingly at the Feng Luan monarch, "Father..."

"Hehe, what do I think?" Seeing Yun Cang lay his cards on the table, the Feng Luan monarch looked at Yun Cang indifferently, "Once I have your Cloud Mist in my grasp, my son Feng Changxuan will be the unparalleled hero who sacrificed himself for the great Feng Luan!"

Feng Changxuan's face turned deathly pale, finally understanding his fate.

Goodness, just because he was in Yun Cang's hands, his father, in order to avoid being constrained by Yun Cang, completely ignored his own son?

And he even beautified it as "sacrificing himself for the great Feng Luan"...

Father, this is truly filial piety extinguishing one's own flesh and blood! Your son does not want this so-called heroic sacrifice!

The people of Cloud Mist, on the other hand, had somber expressions. This was exactly the scene they did not want to witness.

The Feng Luan monarch's ruthlessness truly showed that he had the Cloud Mist nation in the palm of his hand.

Yun Cang's gaze shifted to the other silent imperial palanquin beside the Feng Luan monarch, and he asked solemnly, "I wonder what great favor the Feng Luan nation has promised the esteemed one, that he would help Feng Luan against my Cloud Mist?"

The palanquin remained silent, its occupant showing no intention of responding to Yun Cang.

The Feng Luan monarch, however, hesitated, not wanting Yun Cang to interact too much with the Divine Spirit expert.
