This was the time for court proceedings.

The Lishui Grand Hall was in a great commotion, lacking the solemn atmosphere of the past!

"In the past, demonic creatures mostly appeared alone, but this time there are not just one, and they are coming in droves. Some towns have already seen many of their people suffer."

"Many of the common people have gone from hopeful to lamenting in anguish."

"Yes, if this continues, the Lishui Empire will surely be devastated."

"......"

At this moment, the first elder on the left discreetly glanced at the gloomy-faced king on the high seat, then stood up and spoke loudly:

"Silence! What kind of decorum is this commotion in the Lishui Grand Hall? Who doesn't know what you all have said? Now everyone should be thinking of strategies, with helping the king resolve his worries as the top priority!"

As the words fell, the Lishui Grand Hall suddenly fell silent.

Seeing this, the expression on the face of the Lishui king on the high seat slightly relaxed, and he looked at the elder with satisfaction, then spoke in a calm voice,

"All my ministers can come forward and share your thoughts, but it must be orderly, not chaotic."

The people looked at each other, and finally a middle-aged official stood up and bowed, saying, "Your Majesty, as for the demonic creatures, we mortals are naturally powerless... This matter, just like before, still requires... the intervention of the immortals."

Hearing the title of "immortals", the people were all startled.

The common people may not know that there are immortals in the world, but all those who can come to the court know of the existence of immortals!

At the same time, the immortals have warned that their existence must not be made public to the secular world.

The people remembered this well, and treated it as a secret.

In the past, whenever there were demonic troubles, it was basically the immortals who handled them, after all, they paid tribute every year, and it was only natural for the immortals to protect the region.

It's just that this time, the immortals have been slow to come, and they are somewhat panicked.

Seeing the hopeful gaze of the officials, the Lishui king shook his head and sighed, "When the demonic trouble first started, the king had already sent people on fast horses to the Qingyun Sect to request the immortals to come out of seclusion, but until now, there has been no news..."

Hearing this, the officials all deflated, their faces gradually turning pale.

If this continues, their lives will also be in danger.

"What about the injured? Didn't I order a report on both the dead and injured?"
