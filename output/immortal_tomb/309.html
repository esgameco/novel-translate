The Eternal Realm, the Ancient Wasteland Ruins.

In the sky, a streak of light flashed, heading towards an unknown destination.

On the massive airship, there were only a dozen or so figures.

These were the people, including Lu Changsheng, who had come out of the Ancient Wasteland King City.

At this moment, the deck was relatively quiet, and everyone was looking, consciously or unconsciously, towards the figure in blue standing by the railing.

Nangong Wenxin, Li Qing, and the other prodigies were filled with trepidation, for they knew how powerful the person before them was.

Sword Cliff, the steward of the Ancient Wasteland Sword Clan, and two other young men stood respectfully there.

After being controlled by Lu Changsheng, they now only obeyed Lu Changsheng, just like Blood Demon Moon.

However, there was no sign of anything unusual about them.

"How long until we reach the Ancient Wasteland Sword Clan?"

Lu Changsheng asked indifferently while looking outside.

Sword Cliff hurriedly stepped forward and respectfully said, "My lord, the Ancient Wasteland Sword Clan is hidden in a secluded space in the far north of the Ancient Wasteland Ruins. At our current speed, we should arrive in about three days."

"Mm." Lu Changsheng nodded, then suddenly turned to look at Nangong Wenxin and the other prodigies.

"Senior!"

Nangong Wenxin and the other prodigies were startled and quickly knelt down.

"When we arrive at the Ancient Wasteland Sword Clan, behave yourselves."

"Senior, rest assured!"

Nangong Wenxin and the other prodigies immediately promised.

In truth, they were also struggling with mixed feelings. They had finally waited for the Prodigy Assembly to become Sword Attendants and join the Ancient Wasteland Sword Clan. Yet, this incident had occurred.

They were not fools and knew that Lu Changsheng was heading to the Ancient Wasteland Sword Clan. When they arrived, the clan would certainly not have a peaceful time.

They did not know what would happen... Sigh.

In any case, they had an ominous premonition in their hearts.

Lu Changsheng looked at Sword Cliff and asked, "Tell me about the Ancient Wasteland Sword Clan."

Sword Cliff hesitated for a moment, not knowing where to start.

"My lord, do you want to know about the clan's strength or...?"

"Strength," Lu Changsheng said.

"Well..." Sword Cliff hesitated slightly, then carefully said, "My lord, there are about a few hundred cultivators at the Godly Vault Realm in the clan..."

Hiss!

Nangong Wenxin, Li Shuo, and the others sucked in a cold breath.

A few hundred Godly Vault Realm experts?!

No wonder they are one of the Eight Ancient Clans. Excluding the Ancient Wasteland Sword Clan, the top powers in the entire Ancient Wasteland Ruins are just that many at the Godly Vault Realm.

Lu Changsheng frowned slightly and waved his hand, "Forget about the Godly Vault Realm trash. Tell me about the stronger ones."

The Godly Vault Realm, he had already experienced it in the Ancient Wasteland King City - it was just trash and weak.
