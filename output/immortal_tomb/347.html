They all came here long, long ago, because this world was very suitable for war.

This world was only named the Divine-Demon Battlefield after the arrival of the myriad races.

The human race did not have a large presence here initially, but gradually grew in numbers through reproduction, developing to its current scale.

Since they lacked in quality, they made up for it in quantity.

Seeing this, Han Jinlong tilted his head back and drank it all in one gulp. His eyes then flickered as he tentatively said:

"Fellow Daoist Lu, since you have just arrived at the Divine-Demonic Battlefield, you must not have heard of the Seven-Colored Jade Pagoda, have you?"

The Seven-Colored Jade Pagoda?!

Zhou Dafu beside him trembled. It had indeed been a long time since the last battle for the Jade Heart. Could it be that the Seven-Colored Jade Pagoda was about to start again?

Such momentous news was naturally very difficult for small cultivators like them to know about, unless an announcement was made.

Lu Changsheng did not rush to speak, but quietly looked at Han Jinlong, waiting for him to continue.

The old turtle had long since retracted into its shell and fallen asleep, its dull cries gradually fading.

Although Zhou Dafu had his head lowered, his ears were already perked up.

Han Jinlong gently rubbed his fingers and said, "The Seven-Colored Jade Pagoda is quite a grand event, and also a small gambit among the myriad tribes. Within the Seven-Colored Jade Pagoda, there is the Jade Heart, which has immeasurable uses..."

"What kind of uses?" Lu Changsheng took a sip of his wine.

Han Jinlong suddenly glanced at the bowed head of Zhou Dafu and said, "You should know, come, tell Fellow Daoist Lu."

"Yes, yes, yes..." Zhou Dafu raised his head, looking at Lu Changsheng with reverence, then lowered his head again and respectfully said:

"Senior, the Jade Heart is rightfully considered a supreme treasure. Its most important function is that it can greatly increase the possibility of late-stage Divine Dao cultivators breaking through to the Divine Profound Realm!"

Han Jinlong nodded slightly. Indeed, when it came to this, the preciousness of the Jade Heart was self-evident.

The Divine Profound Realm was the core combat power in the Divine-Demonic Battlefield, and was so for any tribe.

Moreover, the Jade Heart had an even more important function for the current situation of the Human Tribe, aside from helping Divine Dao cultivators break through to the Divine Profound Realm!

Lu Changsheng nodded to indicate he understood, then looked at Han Jinlong with a half-smile and said:

"So you're telling me about this Seven-Colored Jade Pagoda... for what purpose?"

Han Jinlong coughed dryly, feeling gloomy in his heart. He had a strange feeling that somehow this young friend Lu seemed to know his thoughts.

He then picked up his folding fan, snapped it open, and said while fanning himself, "Fellow Daoist Lu, you don't know, but I accidentally obtained news that the Seven-Colored Jade Pagoda is about to open soon."

Zhou Dafu's pupils suddenly shrank. The Seven-Colored Jade Pagoda was about to open?

At that time, there would be another fierce battle among the tribes!

Seeing that Lu Changsheng did not seem inclined to speak, Han Jinlong smiled and said, "Fellow Daoist Lu, I observe that your strength is not bad. Do you have any interest in participating in the Seven-Colored Jade Pagoda?"

He couldn't help but have a vague feeling that if this young friend Lu were to participate in the Seven-Colored Jade Pagoda, there might be some surprises.

"What? Whether I can participate in the Seven-Colored Jade Pagoda is not something you can decide, is it? I've just arrived at the Divine-Demonic Battlefield and heard that the Human Tribe has five Overlords. Are you one of them?" Lu Changsheng said leisurely.

"I... of course I'm not. If I were an Overlord, how could I be here discussing this with you? But Fellow Daoist Lu, don't underestimate me. I can still say a few words in front of King Jinlun and Abbot Gunsheng."

Hearing this, Zhou Dafu's heart raced, and he looked at Han Jinlong with shocked sidelong glances.

Lu Changsheng shook his head, looked deeply at Han Jinlong, then grabbed the turtle shell and stood up, smiling as he said, "I do have some interest in things I haven't tried before, but... you said it's useless unless Gold Wheel King or Abbot Gunsheng come to invite me. Perhaps I might consider it then."
