The young boy, Tong Xin, saw the young girl standing there dazed, and hurriedly urged, "Xiao Tong, this is the teacher, quickly pay your respects!"

Xiao Tong snapped out of it and quickly bowed. When she straightened her frail body, her black eyes were still flashing, as if she was still trying hard to recall something.

After a moment, she opened her mouth, trying hard to say something, but no sound came out.

"Tong Xin, the brush and ink." Lu Changsheng said softly.

In fact, he knew what the little girl wanted to ask, but he would still use paper and pen, which would also help her get familiar with holding a brush.

"Ah, yes." Tong Xin immediately understood, and with a flip of his hand, brush, ink, paper and inkstone appeared on the table. He dipped the brush in ink and handed it over, "Xiao Tong, write down what you want to say on this."

Xiao Tong took the brush awkwardly, gripping it like a wooden stick, not sure if she was holding it correctly.

"It's like this." Tong Xin demonstrated, also holding a brush.

Xiao Tong nodded, and then wrote a few crooked characters on the paper, "Teacher, why do I feel like I've seen you somewhere before?"

Lu Changsheng walked out from behind the counter, looking at the words on the paper, and said, "Have you been to Yanyu Town before?"

Xiao Tong shook her head confusedly. As far as she could remember, she had always been in Yanyu Town.

"Then that's it, Tong Xin and I just arrived in Yanyu Town yesterday, how could you have seen me before?" Lu Changsheng said calmly.

Xiao Tong wrote a few more words, then bowed and stepped aside.

She no longer dwelled on the illusory familiarity, and based on her own situation, it must have been a hallucination.

Lu Changsheng looked at the words on the paper, "Teacher, Xiao Tong has overstepped", and nodded slightly, then went back to lie down on the rocking chair, saying, "Tong Xin, first take her to get familiar with the medical house."

"Yes, teacher." Tong Xin responded, then looked at Xiao Tong and smiled, "Come, I'll take you to the herb garden in the backyard first."

Xiao Tong obediently nodded, glancing at Lu Changsheng in the rocking chair one more time, then followed Tong Xin to the backyard.

Lu Changsheng slowly closed his eyes, couldn't help but think of Xing Tian, whom he had crossed paths with in the Heaven's Scar.

The daughter of Xing Tian, Wan Shi Jie, had encountered him again in another reincarnation.

If he could meet Xing Tian during this trip to the Ancestral Heavenly Realm, perhaps this time Xing Tian would have the chance to see his daughter who was undergoing tribulations.

...

In the evening,

Lu Changsheng casually tossed a medical book in front of Xiao Tong, saying, "The information about all the herbs in the herb garden is written in this book. Memorize it by heart within ten days, and Tong Xin will test you then."

"Yes, teacher." Tong Xin quickly responded.

Xiao Tong opened the medical book, looking at the various diagrams and dense annotations, her gaze determined.

She then hugged the medical book and bowed to Lu Changsheng.

Tong Xin had thought the young girl might find it difficult, as the book was so thick, but he was pleasantly surprised...he couldn't help but feel satisfied.

It seems that this little herb girl he randomly picked up on the street indeed has a good temperament.

Lu Changsheng nodded slightly, then went behind the curtain to the inner room to rest.

Tong Xin looked at Xiao Tong and waved his fist, smiling, "Xiao Tong, I forgot to ask the teacher to treat your voice. But don't worry, I'll ask the teacher about it tomorrow. Keep up the good work, but remember, when it's about time, you should sleep."

He then quickly went to his own room to cultivate, as he was somewhat anxious about cultivation, after all, Lin Dong's mocking face would occasionally surface in his mind.

The lights in the medical house flickered, everything was very quiet, only the sound of Xiao Tong turning the pages of the medical book could be heard. Her small face was full of seriousness.
