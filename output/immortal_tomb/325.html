Cai Xu and Huang Bin were practicing the Carefree Technique, but suddenly they noticed a young man in blue robes appear in the middle!

The two of them abruptly stopped, dumbfounded!

The surrounding disciples who were watching Cai Xu and Huang Bin were also stunned, and they rubbed their eyes, thinking they were seeing things.

The Sect... Master?

"Your cultivation of the first volume of the Carefree Technique still has a long way to go," Lu Changsheng stood with his hands behind his back, speaking softly.

"Sect Master!"

With the disciples' exclamation, the entire training field fell silent, and all the disciples present looked over in bewilderment.

The Sect Master? What Sect Master?

But when they saw the young man in blue robes standing in the air, their minds were shaken, and they hurriedly descended from the sky.

"Outrageous!! What are you all still standing there for? Hurry and pay your respects!!"

Just as the disciples were dazed, the five Elders led by Peng Fang suddenly appeared on the training field.

The five Elders first looked at Lu Changsheng with delight, then quickly led the now alert disciples to kneel down.

Lu Changsheng moved, and he stood on the ground of the training field, smiling faintly, "Rise."

"Yes." The crowd quickly stood up, their gazes burning with admiration as they looked at Lu Changsheng.

This was their Sect Master!

At this time, Li Shuyan also appeared on the training field, marveling at the scene before him.

When he followed Lu Changsheng into the Linglang Heavenly Realm, he found that this place was far inferior to the Ancestral Heavenly Realm.

But his master's sect actually had five Transcendent Realm experts. He could tell that the five had reached their limits, and their disciples' cultivation levels were also quite impressive.

No wonder this is the sect established by his master.

Lu Changsheng looked at Peng Fang and ordered, "Elder, gather all the disciples of the Carefree Sect to assemble here. I have something to say."

"Yes!" Peng Fang immediately responded respectfully, then turned and shouted, "The Sect Master has ordered, all disciples of the Carefree Sect are to assemble at the training field!"

His words echoed throughout the Carefree Sect.

Instantly, the Carefree Sect was shaken!

Whether it was the two Holy Sons, Tong Xin and Lin Dong, or the Protector Executives like Zhu Xingqiu, they were all stunned.

They never expected the Sect Master to suddenly return.

So in a moment, many disciples who were still cultivating quickly withdrew from their cultivation state and hurried towards the training field.

Countless streams of light appeared in the sky above the Carefree Sect.

They also knew that the five elders had stagnated after reaching the so-called Divine Origin Realm, and continuing to stay in the Linlang Heavens would only waste their time and life.

However, they couldn't just let them leave either, for two reasons: one, they were the Xiaoyao Sect's upper echelon and couldn't depart, and two, they themselves didn't know where to go even if they left the Linlang Heavens.

Lu Changsheng looked at the expressions of the crowd and smiled slightly. The Xiaoyao Sect could not remain in the Linlang Heavens forever, as he had initially intended to establish the sect and take responsibility for it.
