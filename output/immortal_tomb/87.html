"Ugh...ugh...!!"

The Patriarch of the Eternal Celestial Sect stared blankly at the vast ruins below, his outstretched hand frozen in place, his pupils trembling wildly.

In just a moment,

She...her sect...

Tens of thousands of disciples and elders...

The Eternal Celestial Sect's heritage of hundreds of millions of years...all gone.

The elderly Shen Mu also trembled as he looked on. With a single palm strike, the vast expanse had collapsed - a power he had never witnessed before!

The Immortal Emperor...the most mysterious of the Five Great Immortal Emperors, the Eternal Life Immortal Emperor, was too terrifying.

"What have you done!!!"

The elder abruptly turned his head and angrily questioned the Patriarch of the Eternal Celestial Sect.

If not for something happening, how could the remote southern immortal realm have drawn the presence of the Eternal Life Immortal Emperor, the supreme figure?

It was even more impossible for the Eternal Life Immortal Emperor to personally appear and destroy the Eternal Celestial Sect.

Moreover, even when he was still a Terrestrial Immortal, the Eternal Life Immortal Emperor had vanished without a trace...

If he had not been fortunate enough to see the Eternal Life Immortal Emperor's portrait and have it deeply imprinted in his mind, he might not have recognized him today, and his sudden death would have been utterly inexplicable.

The Patriarch of the Eternal Celestial Sect said dejectedly, "I...I blocked the ascension channel..."

Hearing this, the elder sharply inhaled!

The immortal rule that no one in the four directions of the immortal realms could block the ascension channel was clearly one established by the Eternal Life Immortal Emperor himself, wasn't it?

"You...you..." The elder trembled as he pointed at her, momentarily at a loss for words.

After a moment, the elder roared, "Why did you do this, and how dare you do it!"

The Patriarch of the Eternal Celestial Sect lowered her head, speechless.

"Tell me who is behind this, and then self-destruct, and this Emperor will not act."

At this moment, Lu Changsheng looked down at her calmly.

The Patriarch of the Eternal Celestial Sect's soul trembled. The pressure from the Immortal Emperor was as heavy as a thousand catties. In the presence of the Immortal Emperor, she was but a lowly ant in the dust.

She trembled and knelt down. "Immortal...Immortal Emperor, can you..."

Seeing this, Lu Changsheng's brow furrowed slightly, and the Dao Eye's blue light began to flicker as it remotely searched the Patriarch of the Eternal Celestial Sect's memories.

Instantly, the Patriarch of the Eternal Celestial Sect froze in place, her eyes vacant.

After a few breaths, the blue light in Lu Changsheng's eyes disappeared, and he murmured, "Interesting..."

"Immortal...Immortal Emperor..." The Patriarch of the Eternal Celestial Sect's head was splitting, but she still struggled to beg for mercy, her head in her hands.

The earlier awe had completely vanished, and now she was like an ordinary woman, disheveled and utterly helpless.

Lu Changsheng waved his sleeve indifferently.

The Patriarch of the Eternal Celestial Sect then abruptly vanished from this space, erased into nothingness!

"Immortal Emperor, my Eternal Celestial Sect has violated the immortal rules and deserves its fate...I only wish to see Your Majesty in person at the end of my life, and die without regrets."

The elderly Shen Mu smiled reverently, bowed his body, and dissipated into particles.

With his lifespan already at its end, he departed this world with exceptional grace.

Lu Changsheng flickered and appeared at the ruins below, standing before Chu Xiran and Zhang Youdao.

Chu Xiran and Zhang Youdao hurriedly knelt down and prostrated themselves.

"Immortal...Immortal Emperor."

Chu Xiran's chest heaved violently, her breathing extremely rapid. As an insignificant being like her, she had never dared to hope that she would one day see the Eternal Life Immortal Emperor, the one who had disappeared for over a million years and now reappeared.

Recalling their encounter at the Nameless Tavern in the Spiritual Dust Realm, and even unknowingly pouring that Cyan Sea Immortal Wine for the Eternal Life Immortal Emperor, it all felt like a dream. Yet, at the same time, she was filled with trepidation.
