Lu Changsheng walked while looking at the scroll in his hand, or it would be more appropriate to call it a route map.

"The Divine-Demonic Battlefield..." Lu Changsheng murmured softly as he looked at the huge mark on the route map.

Even though he was immortal, there were still countless places he had not been to, after all, the boundless void could be said to be infinite.

According to what Jian Wuhui said, the Divine-Demonic Battlefield should be the place where all tribes wage war, which sounded quite interesting.

From Jian Wuhui's words, it was not difficult to see that the human race was not doing well there.

Lu Changsheng's gaze slightly changed, although he was not sure whether he was part of the human race or not, he had always been dealing with the human race, and had made a few human friends in the past, but they had long since died in the river of time.

Therefore, he later became alone, no longer making friends, feeling that it was unnecessary.

After all, they would all die eventually, no need to add sorrow for the subsequent separation of life and death.

"Can you still find your home?" Lu Changsheng lowered his head and asked casually.

Li Shueyan and the Blood Demon Yue were taken aback.

The Blood Demon Yue quickly reacted, realizing that the master was asking her, so she hurriedly bowed and said, "Master, Demon Yue... can't remember."

Lu Changsheng turned around and stared at the Blood Demon Yue without speaking for a long time.

Li Shueyan obediently lowered his head.

The Blood Demon Yue, seeing that Lu Changsheng did not speak for a long time, carefully raised her head and saw Lu Changsheng quietly looking at her.

This was the first time she had clearly seen her master's appearance since the ancient Demon Sword Clan was destroyed.

Handsome and extraordinary, transcendent like a fallen immortal, with the appearance of a gentle young immortal, and when he smiled, he was like a big brother next door.

It was really difficult to compare him to the overlord who had destroyed the ancient Demon Sword Clan.

"Master... Master..." the Blood Demon Yue lowered her head, her voice slightly trembling.

"Blood Demon Yue, do you know why I summoned you from the Ancient Moon Blood Clan?" Lu Changsheng asked softly.

"I don't know." The Blood Demon Yue had just answered when she suddenly remembered the destruction of the ancient Demon Sword Clan, her pupils shrank, and she quickly looked up at Lu Changsheng.

Lu Changsheng: "This time, destroy the Ancient Moon."

The Blood Demon Yue's mind shook, and she said in a low voice, "Demon Yue respects and obeys any decision of the master."

At this moment, she clearly knew what she was saying.

But... she just couldn't resist, her heart only wanted to serve the master infinitely.

Lu Changsheng nodded and smiled, "You will also die then."

The Blood Demon Yue's pupils trembled, but she still said, "Demon Yue respects and obeys any decision of the master..."

Next to her, Li Shueyan slightly pursed his lips, his heart somewhat complicated.

Lu Changsheng nodded slightly, looked up at the sky, and after learning about the Divine-Demonic Battlefield, he had a strange feeling in his heart, as if he wanted to go and take a look...

Thinking of this, he also lost his playful mood and did not want to stay in the Eternal Void Realm for long.
