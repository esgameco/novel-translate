In a room of the restaurant.

Lu Changsheng and Han Jinlong sat facing each other.

Lu Changsheng looked at the solemn and majestic armored man in front of him and smiled:

"Have I had any dealings with Jinlun Wang before?"

Han Jinlong's heart jumped, and he shook his head and smiled lightly: "Of course the King has never had any dealings with you, Lu Xiaoyu."

Han Jinlong was not too bothered by Lu Xiaoyu directly addressing him as Jinlun Wang, after all, he was a newcomer to the Divine-Demonic Battlefield.

"So why has Jinlun Wang come here today? As one of the Five Overlords of the human race, your true self appearing in this Xianhe Restaurant has certainly given Lu a great honor."

Lu Changsheng chuckled lightly.

Han Jinlong's eyes flashed with a hint of surprise.

Although this was the second time he had seen Lu Xiaoyu, the current situation was completely different from the first time!

The first time, Lu Xiaoyu didn't know that the young man in the black robe was him.

But now, he himself had appeared in person, and this Lu Xiaoyu was still as calm and indifferent as before...

Didn't he see the scene of thousands of people worshiping him outside?

His open appearance in the restaurant was not only to give face to Lu Xiaoyu, but also to let him know the weight of Jinlun Wang as one of the Five Overlords of the human race.

But it seems... it didn't have much effect.

"Lu Xiaoyu, before we discuss why I came here today, I want to ask you one thing - are you not afraid of me?"

Han Jinlong looked straight at the young man and asked.

"Why should I be afraid of you?" Lu Changsheng asked back.

Han Jinlong paused, then smiled and said, "I am one of the Five Overlords of the human race, after all..."

As he spoke, he slowly released a trace of his oppressive aura towards Lu Changsheng...

Then he was stunned to find that Lu Changsheng didn't have any reaction at all, not even a slight frown.
