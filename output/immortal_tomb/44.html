The Immortal Tomb in the Mountain Range.

"I was buried here before," Lu Changsheng said calmly, standing in the middle of the tomb group at the spot where he had been buried previously, his hands behind his back.

On his shoulder, the old turtle looked at him quite mysteriously and curiously asked, "Master, where is your tomb?"

"I erased it," Lu Changsheng replied.

The old turtle nodded its head and scanned the other tombs, "There are no epitaphs on any of these tombs."

"Mm-hmm."

"Master, aren't you curious? Shall we dig up these tombs and take a look?" the old turtle said, quite intrigued.

After countless years, the old turtle's state of mind was no longer easily stirred by things.

But this matter had aroused its great interest and curiosity.

To be able to bury Lu Changsheng for over a million years, that was impressive.

Lu Changsheng did not speak, his gaze flickering slightly.

At this moment, a burly man with a full beard suddenly appeared in the cemetery.

"I just can't believe it, a tomb in the Immortal Tomb actually disappeared into thin air!"

Tiger Battle cursed and grumbled, and just as he was about to take a step, he paused in mid-air, not coming down.

He saw a young man in blue robes standing quietly in the tomb, looking at him like a ghost.

A cultivator?

Tiger Battle's eyes flashed, and he immediately tried to probe the other's aura...

But he was shocked,

Impossible, as a mighty demonic lord, how could he not be able to discern the realm of a human cultivator?

In the Spiritual Dust Immortal Realm, aside from Zhang Youdao, Qin Fuming, and Jiang Yuan, the few who had reached the Tribulation Crossing realm, who else was there?

This person must have some treasure that conceals his aura...

Tiger Battle was somewhat puzzled for the moment, deciding not to bother this young cultivator in blue, and instead started to carefully observe the tombs one by one as he walked around.

"Master, this little demon's true form is actually the Roaring Heavenly Tiger, I didn't expect there to be one in this realm," the old turtle chuckled.

The Roaring Heavenly Tiger clan was quite famous in the various immortal realms.

However, of course, the one in front of them was too weak, any Roaring Heavenly Tiger from the immortal realms could easily crush him.

Lu Changsheng nodded slightly, not saying anything.

With his Great Dao Eyes, what couldn't he see?

He even knew that this tiger demon was the lord of the Demon Realm.

But since it had no connection with him at the moment, he naturally wouldn't destroy it for no reason.

All lives, whether human or various demonic beings, were more or less the same in his eyes.

However, at times, he couldn't help but lean a bit more towards the human race, after all, he was human himself, wasn't he?... Actually, Lu Changsheng wasn't entirely sure.

Tiger Battle strolled slowly, looking at one tomb after another, and soon came to stand in front of Lu Changsheng, his towering figure a full head taller than Lu Changsheng.

He inexplicably glanced at Lu Changsheng, then turned his gaze to the spot in front of Lu Changsheng, his expression immediately changing!

Sure enough, a tomb had disappeared!

In his memory, there had definitely been a tomb here!

But now it was empty, extremely strange!

"Hey, I suspect you also come here often, do you know there was a tomb here before?" Tiger Battle couldn't help but point to that spot and ask Lu Changsheng.

"I'm not 'hey'," Lu Changsheng said with interest.

Tiger Battle's face darkened, and he suppressed his temper and said, "Fellow Daoist, do you know there was a tomb here before?"

Lu Changsheng said softly, "Could it be that you misremembered?"

"Impossible!!"
