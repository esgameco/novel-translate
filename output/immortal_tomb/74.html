In just a moment, the tribulation clouds dispersed, and the thunderbolts stopped, giving the impression that they had fled.

The old turtle took a deep breath, hating this stupid Heavenly Dao to the core!

He was not afraid of the master, as the master was invincible, but he, the old turtle, was also very strong, okay?!

The old turtle didn't notice that all the cultivators below, and even the entire Tianyun Trading Company, were gaping in astonishment, their jaws dropped to the ground.

Many mocking smiles had frozen on their faces.

In the tavern, Chu Xirou and Gu Helin also had their pupils dilated...

The world fell silent, without a single sound.
