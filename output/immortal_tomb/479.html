It was not long after the Sea Transport No. 3 had set sail when the captain personally came to the deck and, amidst many questioning gazes, found Lü Changsheng and respectfully said:

"My lord, this place is not befitting of your noble status. We have a top-tier suite on the Sea Transport No. 3, please allow me to escort you there."

The cultivators heard this and their eyes were filled with envy! They had heard of the top-tier suites before - they were not for public sale, but were used by the Sea Transport Guild to accommodate distinguished guests!

Today, they were able to witness it.

However, the cultivators could only admire it, and did not dare to feel jealous or have any objections. The young man in the green robe completely deserved it. After all, in the end, it was all about one's own strength.

Chen Pang and Xiao Chai silently watched, but their minds were still a bit dazed after the previous events. They never expected that the random person they had struck up a conversation with was actually the most formidable figure on the ship...

At the same time, Lü Changsheng opened his eyes, looked at the respectful and anxious captain standing before him, and although he had initially wanted to refuse, he seemed to have suddenly remembered something. He then turned his head to look at Chen Pang and Xiao Chai, and smiled:

"You two were saying you wanted to stay in the luxurious suite, right? Come along."

Lü Changsheng then stood up, looked at the captain, and gestured for him to lead the way.

"Yes, yes, yes... My lord, please!"

The captain understood, made a gesture inviting them, and then turned and led the way.

As for Chen Pang and Xiao Chai, who were huddled there, their minds were completely blank, their lips opening and closing to express their inner turmoil.

They never expected that their casual remark earlier had been remembered by such an important figure.

The two were both amazed and moved, but also at a loss. How could people like them possibly be qualified to stay in a top-tier suite?

Chen Pang glanced at his older brother, then looked at Lü Changsheng's retreating back and said in a trembling voice:

"M-m-m-my lord, we... we'll just stay here with my big brother, it's fine."

Lü Changsheng paused in his steps, then continued to follow the captain, saying, "Don't dilly-dally."

"Yes, yes..." Chen Pang and Xiao Chai were startled, quickly agreed, and then hurried to catch up, under the envious gazes of the other cultivators.

"Again, these two... How did the lord come to know them?"

"I vaguely remember, I think these two were sitting in the same spot as the lord when they first boarded the ship."

"Goodness, these two are so lucky, to be able to stay in the top-tier suite on the Sea Transport ship. I don't know if I'll ever have the chance in my lifetime."

"Don't dream about it. How could the likes of you stay there? The Sea Transport Guild is no ordinary guild, they even have branches in the Dao Realm, and only figures of great importance can stay in the top-tier suites. You, who haven't even grasped the Dao, how could you possibly?"

"Hey, why are you talking like that, as if you're some great expert? If you're so amazing, then why are you out here on the deck with the rest of us?"

"I never said I was amazing. I'm just telling you not to have unrealistic fantasies!"

"Tch!"

"..."

...

The captain soon brought Lü Changsheng and the others to the entrance of the top-tier suite.

"My lord, this is it."

The captain said respectfully, then inserted a jade token into a formation, and the door to the room opened.

The captain reverently held the jade token out to Lü Changsheng: "My lord, this is the room key. Please keep it safe."

Lü Changsheng took the key, and with a faint smile, asked, "How much is the room fee?"

The captain was startled, and hurriedly bowed, saying, "My lord, you jest. Your stay is the honor of the Sea Transport Guild, there is no need to speak of room fees..."

"Good, you may go now." Lü Changsheng nodded slightly and then stepped into the room.
