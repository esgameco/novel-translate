What?! They didn't get any Void Mushrooms?

The elders looked at each other in dismay!

The elders even suspected that Shiyu and Fu Tao were lying.

After all, the Ancient Desolate Sword Clan and the Unbound Celestial Dao, even five World-level ones... All of this was too ethereal and absurd for them.

"Father, the situation at the time was like this..." Shiyu quickly recounted the details of what had happened.

Afterwards, the crowd was deeply shaken and fell even more silent.

Fu Liangcai frowned and asked, "After a sword chant, that suspected expert from the Ancient Desolate Sword Clan and the five Unbound Celestial Dao just disappeared??"

Fu Tao patted his chest nervously and said, "Yes, Father, they probably went to fight in another void space. Fortunately, the void is vast, and we are like specks of dust, unlikely to attract their attention... "As for the Void Mushrooms, never mind. With such an incident happening, not only you two, even I and the Elders probably wouldn't dare to linger in the Void anymore."

Fu Liangcai sighed regretfully, then asked, "Oh yes, Shiyu, how did you two encounter those two people?"

Shiyu's gaze shifted.

The crowd also knew that Fu Liangcai was asking about that mysterious man in blue robes.
