The Immortal Emperor of Longevity!

That imperial title from memory suddenly surfaced in everyone's minds!

The Three Great Immortal Sages of the Central Immortal Realm, the various Immortal Kings were stunned on the spot. That was their elusive master, but...

This imperial title also awakened the memories of countless mortals in the Sand City, they revealed sorrowful expressions and knelt down involuntarily.

"Oh, our most revered Immortal Emperor of Longevity, where are you now? Can you once again save us as you did when you established the Immortal Statutes to protect the mortals all those years ago!"

"Haha, the Immortal Emperor of Longevity from the Central Immortal Realm? Even if he's here, what can he do? Does he have the power to save you all? Before the might of my Yuhun Demon Clan, any Immortal Emperor is just a dog!"

Youshi sneered and waved his hand, ordering, "Continue the slaughter!"

"Just dogs?"

Just as the Yuhun Demon Clan was about to make their move, a cold voice resounded through the heavens and earth, echoing in everyone's ears!

Everyone was stunned, looking around in bewilderment.

Only Shi Wushi, Gu Yinyang, Hunqiu, Nangong Jing, the powerhouses of the Central Immortal Realm, as well as Leigu, Chu Xie, and Zhang Youdao were suddenly shaken to the core!

This voice was so familiar to them...

They hurriedly looked around, their heartbeats quickening.

In the next moment, a young man in blue robes gradually materialized, standing with his hands behind his back, his expression indifferent.

Seeing this figure that had not been seen for hundreds of millions of years, Shi Wushi was stunned, his eyes instantly brimming with tears. For some reason, the pent-up frustration in his heart dissipated greatly, and he trembled...
