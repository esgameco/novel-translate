The Venerable Great Dao.

Hunhui and the others stared at Lu Changsheng in shock for a long time, and once they regained their senses, they had no time to think about anything else. A mysterious guidance led them to kneel down in a daze, as if their minds had gone blank.

It was the Reincarnation Demon Child who was dumbfounded, his mouth gaping open, and he asked, "Big Brother, are you telling the truth?"

Lu Changsheng did not respond, but instead placed his hand on the Reincarnation Demon Child's head and said, "How about I take you with me to purge the worms on the Creation Pillar?"

The Reincarnation Demon Child's eyes lit up with joy, and he looked up slightly, "Good... good!"

"I'll let you live a little longer, and after we've purged the worms, I'll kill you," Lu Changsheng said with a smile.

"Good... good! Thank you, Big Brother, thank you!!" The Reincarnation Demon Child had no objections, and was even so excited that tears glistened at the corners of his eyes.

Lu Changsheng smiled slightly, and with a wave of his hand, the radiant Great Dao Demon-Subduing Tower appeared, and he said meaningfully, "I am, in fact, the master of this Great Dao Demon-Subduing Tower."

The Reincarnation Demon Child was stunned, and he looked up at Lu Changsheng, sniffing.

Ah, so that's how it was...

In fact, the Reincarnation Demon Child had hated the master of the Great Dao Demon-Subduing Tower in the past.

Because the master had once imprisoned him in the tower, and he had wasted so much time!

He could have devoured countless worlds and become powerful, and then quickly returned to the Creation Pillar.

But at this moment, the Reincarnation Demon Child no longer hated, nor dared to hate.

In addition to the awe of Lu Changsheng's identity,

More importantly, he said he would take him along to purge the monsters!

The Reincarnation Demon Child naturally had the utmost respect.

"Great Dao Venerable, Hunhui does not know what the Creation Pillar is, nor what those monsters are, but since it concerns the endless void, how can it be the affair of the Great Dao Venerable alone? Hunhui... requests to fight!"

Hunhui suddenly clasped his fists to his forehead and bowed respectfully.

He finally understood why, from the first time he met Lu Changsheng, he had an indescribable sense of familiarity and an irrepressible feeling of awe!

It turns out... it all makes sense!

"The Four Great Spirit Generals (Thirty-Six Demons) request to fight!"

Immediately afterwards, the high-ranking members of the Soul Realm also respectfully shouted.

The Reincarnation Demon Child watched this scene, his eyes trembling deep within.

Although he knew that Hunhui and the others were doing it for the sake of the entire endless void, it was also a way to help him take revenge, and he could not help but be moved.

He deeply understood how powerful those monsters were!

Those monsters were not only individually strong, but their numbers were also terrifying, and they were spread across the Creation Pillars!

And what he had seen before was only the monsters on one of the Creation Pillars.

The Reincarnation Demon Child had once heard those monsters say that the eight Creation Pillars of the endless void had all been invaded by them... so one can imagine the number of monsters on the eight Creation Pillars...

Thinking of the terrifying scene, the Reincarnation Demon Child gritted his teeth.

In fact, even if he went with the Big Brother, the Reincarnation Demon Child had no confidence at all, none whatsoever.

Lu Changsheng glanced at Hunhui and the others, pondering.

"Big... Big Brother, the monsters on the Creation Pillar are really, really strong, you... you..." The Reincarnation Demon Child couldn't help but speak out of selfishness.

Lu Changsheng's brow furrowed slightly. Of course he knew that the monsters from the endless void were strong.

Lu Changsheng lowered his head and looked at the Great Dao Demon-Subduing Tower slowly rotating in his palm, and smiled, "No matter how strong they are, I will destroy them. As the Lord of the Great Dao, all things revere me."

Bring the Myriad Worlds Calamity Body.
