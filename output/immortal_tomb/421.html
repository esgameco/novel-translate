The Yooting Lake was deathly silent.

Everyone stared blankly at Cloud Ao Xie and Ye Xun Wang kneeling there.

Despite the scorching sun, the crowd felt as if they had been plunged into the depths of a freezing winter, their bodies shivering as if trapped in an icy cellar.

This reversal was too sudden, and no one could react in time.

Cloud Ao Xie's four subordinates were also stunned by this sudden scene, but after regaining their senses, they quickly knelt down as well!

Guo Xiong, the estate manager, and Zhu Gu Yuan and the others were shivering, their throats constricted, especially Guo Xiong and the estate manager, their faces deathly pale without a trace of blood!

Boom, boom, boom, boom, boom...

Suddenly, the sound of countless people kneeling resounded, as all the citizens of Yooting Lake dropped to their knees in a grand, sweeping motion!

There was no other reason - it was simply because the Ninth Prince and the Peerless Musician had knelt, and they all dared not stand!

Seeing this scene, Zhu Qingqing's gaze became hazy and trembling, her heart surging with waves of emotion!

The Peerless Musician, he...

Cloud Ao Xie, seeing the courtyard so quiet, grew increasingly fearful. He glanced sideways at Ye Xun Wang beside him, knowing that Ye Xun Wang could not help him either. He then raised the thorns on his head again and cried out in a trembling voice:

"Yesterday, it was Ao Xie... reckless, short-sighted, failing to recognize the true dragon. If the Esteemed One can forgive his anger, Ao Xie... would not mind dying!!"

The crowd, kneeling on the ground, heard Cloud Ao Xie's words again, sensing the fear in his tone, and felt a chill run down their spines.

The Peerless Musician... just what kind of extraordinary being was he!

The mighty Ninth Prince of the Cloud Lian Ancient Kingdom, the most beloved by the king, was actually willing to atone with his life!!

Thinking about it deeply filled them with dread, profound dread!

"Creak~"

Just as the crowd was shuddering at the thought, the courtyard gate opened.

Lu Changsheng slowly walked out, his hands clasped behind his back, with Old Turtle following closely behind and bowing.

Sensing the movement, everyone in the area trembled!

They all knew it must be the Peerless Musician coming out, but no one dared to look up!

Only Cloud Ao Xie, still holding the thorns high, stared at the young man in blue with narrowed pupils.

Cloud Ao Xie had expected the extraordinary person Ye Xun Wang spoke of to be a venerable elder with an immortal demeanor, but he never imagined it would be such a refined and handsome young man.

Although... Cloud Ao Xie also knew that one could not judge a person's depth by their appearance alone.

Cloud Ao Xie merely glanced at him and then lowered his head, not daring to look again.

At this time, Zhu Qingqing, who was kneeling in the closest position to Lu Changsheng, felt her heart racing with great nervousness and awe.

If she had initially harbored some romantic fantasies about the Peerless Musician, then from the moment the Ninth Prince begged for forgiveness, she no longer dared to indulge in such foolish dreams.

Between her and the Peerless Musician, there was an insurmountable chasm!

If the Ninth Prince Cloud Ao Xie was the moon in the sky, then she, Zhu Qingqing, was but a firefly on the ground.

Then, how could she, Zhu Qingqing, be compared to the Peerless Musician... perhaps she was not even worthy of being called dust...

"Esteemed One, Ao Xie was blind and ignorant, deserving of the gravest punishment..." Cloud Ao Xie said hoarsely, his hands holding the thorns trembling rapidly.

Lu Changsheng's gaze swept over the countless citizens of Yooting Lake kneeling before him, and after a moment, he softly said, "Forgiven."

Instantly, an irresistible aura of gentleness enveloped the tearful Cloud Ao Xie, Ye Xun Wang, and the entire crowd of Yooting Lake.

Only Guo Xiong and the estate manager remained kneeling.

After their initial bewilderment, Guo Xiong and the estate manager seemed to realize something, and their faces suddenly turned deathly pale, quickly shivering and drenched in cold sweat!

"Esteemed One's magnanimity, Ao Xie is deeply grateful for Your forgiveness of Ao Xie's reckless ignorance!" Cloud Ao Xie, though spared from death, was still uneasy. He put away the thorns and bowed again, his attitude extremely respectful.

Due to standing up, Ye Xun Wang also finally got a clear look at Lu Changsheng's appearance and bearing, once again marveling in his heart.

At this moment, Lu Changsheng did not look at Cloud Ao Xie, but calmly gazed at Guo Xiong and the estate manager.

Gradually, gradually, everyone noticed this scene!

The crowd was puzzled...
