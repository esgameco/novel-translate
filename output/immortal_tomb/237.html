Upper Clarity Palace.

These days, Yun Mi was very troubled.

She had exhausted all her methods but still couldn't ferret out the mole among the elders.

At this time, in the main hall of the Upper Clarity Palace.

Yun Mi sat in the main seat, with many elders standing on both sides, their heads bowed in silence, brows furrowed.

Recently, the elders were also troubled.

They didn't know what medicine the Mistress had taken wrong, insisting on finding some mole among them.

Heavens and earth, they were truly innocent!

Born as Upper Clarity Palace disciples, they would die as Upper Clarity Palace ghosts - how could they betray the palace?

"Mistress, our loyalty is as bright as the full moon. Why do you... don't make the hearts of us elders grow cold..."

The Grand Elder struggled for a long time before standing out and bowing as he spoke.

The other elders were also puzzled when they heard this. They had all been questioned, so what else could be done?

Could it be that they would have to undergo soul-searching?

If the Mistress really made them undergo soul-searching, they would be thoroughly disheartened.

Yun Mi rubbed her brow and sighed. "Lianyi and Xu Jiao went out and were attacked by a mysterious expert. Xu Jiao perished, and even Lianyi was almost... Besides this palace and the elders, who else knew that Lianyi went out?"

The elders were shocked to hear this.

So it was their Upper Clarity Palace's Saint Girl who had almost been attacked?

Xu Jiao's death, after all, was also the death of an inner disciple, and they had heard about it, but didn't pay much attention.

In their Upper Clarity Palace, inner and outer disciples died every now and then, which was normal.

Only when a core disciple died would they pay close attention.

They just didn't expect Xu Jiao to have been attacked together with the Saint Girl.

No wonder the Mistress was suspicious of them...

Not to mention the Mistress's suspicion, after learning of this, they all started glancing at each other, secretly becoming vigilant and wary.

"What do the elders suggest?" Yun Mi said faintly.

The elders' eyes flickered, this was too difficult, they had even questioned themselves, where could they find a solution?

"Lianyi is our Upper Clarity Palace's Saint Girl. She almost met with disaster, this matter must have an explanation." Yun Mi's eyes narrowed as she swept her gaze over the crowd and continued, "The only two sects that have enmity with our Upper Clarity Palace are Qiqing Valley and Yangxuan Sect..."

"In three days, the elders will accompany this palace to Qiqing Valley to demand an explanation."

Since she couldn't ferret out the mole, she would directly confront them.

As for Yangxuan Sect, Yun Mi temporarily excluded them, as she vaguely knew that that fellow Yuan Yang Qiu had been infatuated with Lianyi for a long time, so the possibility of him attacking Lianyi was not high.

How did she know this?

Because Yuan Yang Qiu had mentioned more than once that he wanted to become Lianyi's cultivation partner, but had all been rejected by her.

"Yes, Mistress."

The elders immediately responded, even if they had doubts and didn't understand why the Mistress had determined that Qiqing Valley was the attacker, rather than Yangxuan Sect.
