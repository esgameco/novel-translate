"Hehe, Shuyan, where did you and the master go during the previous period, and what did you do?" The old turtle finally had time to ask curiously.

"Oh, oh." Li Shuyan nodded in realization, then glanced at the countless stars in the void and replied, "Recently, I went with the master to a place called the Eternal Cosmos Realm..."

Quickly, Li Shuyan detailed everything that had happened in the Eternal Cosmos Realm to the old turtle.

The old turtle nodded from time to time, and was also somewhat shaken. The Eight Ancient Clans, the Divine Element, the Divine Yang, the Divine Storehouse, the Divine Dao?

Li Shuyan is now in the middle stage of the Divine Yang realm, and he can easily control himself. So what realm is he in now? The Divine Storehouse or the Divine Dao?

"Senior Turtle, may I ask, what realm have you reached? The Divine Storehouse or the Divine Dao?" Sure enough, Li Shuyan couldn't help but ask.

Hearing this, the old turtle frowned slightly. Based on the distinctions between the Divine Yang, Divine Storehouse, and Divine Dao that Li Shuyan had just mentioned, he found that none of them matched his own situation.

Moreover, he had only felt that he had become much stronger before, but he had never encountered anything specific like "one brilliant sun, two brilliant suns" and so on.

"Uh, I don't know," the old turtle said awkwardly.

Don't know?

Li Shuyan looked at the old turtle suspiciously, but didn't pursue the matter. He guessed that the old turtle should be at the middle stage of the Divine Storehouse or the Divine Dao.

Otherwise, he wouldn't have felt a sense of difficulty in resisting the old turtle.

Now that he has reached the middle stage of the Divine Yang realm, and has also cultivated the Myriad Forging Flame Technique bestowed by the master, he should not be afraid of a cultivator at the initial stage of the Divine Storehouse realm.

Moreover, he has not yet fully comprehended the Myriad Forging Flame Technique, otherwise he would be even more powerful. This body cultivation technique bestowed by the master seems to have been prepared for him to fight beyond his level.

"Okay, Senior Turtle, a thousand years is a bit long, so I'll start cultivating first."

Li Shuyan said and immediately entered a state of cultivation. Seeing this, the old turtle stretched out his hand, wanting to say something, but thought about it and decided not to.

He originally wanted to chat with Li Shuyan a little more, but considering Lu Changsheng in the flying chariot, he simply gave up.

Sighing regretfully, the old turtle also closed his eyes and entered a state of cultivation. His surroundings were illuminated by a series of dark green lights, making him appear indestructible.

The flying chariot became completely quiet, continuously shuttling through the endless void, passing through one layer of stars after another.

During this time, Li Shuyan would occasionally withdraw from his cultivation state and inject spiritual energy into the flying chariot to provide continuous power.

Time flowed like a river of sand, day after day, year after year.
