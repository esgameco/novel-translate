Ever since he escorted his elder sister back home yesterday, his whole person seemed to have been bewitched, and he still hadn't recovered until today.

Just then, Fu Tao saw Fu Shiyu coming out of the courtyard gate. Her complexion was still a bit pale, but much better than yesterday.

"Sister, you've finally recovered, you scared me to death."

Fu Tao hurried over and smiled bitterly.

"It's nothing, Xiao Tao, I made you and Father worry." Fu Shiyu shook her head.

He had wanted to ask since yesterday, but seeing Fu Shiyu's condition at the time, he had temporarily put it aside.

Fu Shiyu nodded, then suddenly remembered something and asked, "What about the Ouyang Clan?"

Hearing the words "that senior", Fu Shiyu's expression changed slightly again.

"Sister?" Fu Tao was suspicious.

Fu Shiyu shook her head, walking ahead with a heavy heart.

Fu Tao frowned and hurried to catch up.

He felt that his sister was just not right, but he couldn't quite put his finger on what was wrong.

Inside the Fu Clan.

The area of Lu Changsheng's guest room had long been designated as an absolute forbidden zone by Fu Liangcai.

No one was allowed to approach, not even the elders, not even Fu Shiyu and Fu Tao, nor even Lu Changsheng himself under normal circumstances.

Outside the guest room,

Li Shuyan stood there as usual, closing his eyes to cultivate when there was nothing to do, like an unmoving statue for ten years.

"Shuyan."

At this moment, Lu Changsheng's voice sounded from within the room. Li Shuyan was startled, and his eyes suddenly opened wide.

He quickly turned and pushed the door open, only to see Lu Changsheng sitting there leisurely drinking wine, with the bronze mask placed beside him.

He hurried over and bowed, "Sir, you called for Shuyan."

Lu Changsheng smiled and poured him a cup of wine in his dazed gaze, "Sit."

"Uh, yes, thank you sir." Li Shuyan was suddenly overwhelmed with gratitude.

"Saint Origin realm at the eighth layer, with your cultivation, you'll only be able to barely make it to the middle tier at that Supreme Cultivator Assembly..."

Lu Changsheng said lightly after taking a sip of wine.

Although he had mentally prepared for this, Li Shuyan still felt a shock, and a bitter smile even appeared at the corners of his mouth.

Thinking that he, a peak figure of the Ancestral Divine Realm, had come to this Eternal Heavenly Realm, and would only be able to rank in the middle tier among those supreme cultivators, it was truly embarrassing...

"Sir, it's Shuyan's inadequacy." Li Shuyan sighed, putting down the cup of wine he had just picked up, having lost the desire to drink for the moment.

"Isn't the reason you followed me to get stronger?" Lu Changsheng shook his head and smiled.

Li Shuyan pursed his lips, and with difficulty said, "Sir, for you to have brought Shuyan out of the Ancestral Divine Realm, to witness the boundless void and the Eternal Heavenly Realm, Shuyan is already deeply grateful. Shuyan dares not ask for anything else."

"Oh, that's a pity, I was going to help you get stronger. Since you don't aspire for it, then forget it." Lu Changsheng said regretfully.

Li Shuyan suddenly stood up, bending at a 90-degree angle!

"Sir! You've misunderstood Shuyan's meaning!"
