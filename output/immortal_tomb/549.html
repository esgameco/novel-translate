Jixiang Town, Changsheng Academy.

Zui Qianchun was stunned, looking at Lu Changsheng's retreating back, wanting to say something but unable to.

Teacher, you said you wanted me to be the assistant, so why are you leaving like a free-handed boss?

"Yes, Grandpa Xu still has the noodle shop to run."

Grandpa Xu actually knows about the source of ink? And he could even see that the three of them have a source of ink at a glance?

Ah, is this real or not!

Han Yue and the others' pupils contracted, feeling that the Grandpa Xu in front of them had become so unfamiliar.

It doesn't match the kind-hearted Grandpa Xu at all.

No wonder... No wonder the teacher made Grandpa Xu the assistant teacher, the teacher must have his own reasons.

It turns out Grandpa Xu has been hiding so deeply!

He's actually also a brush cultivator!

And obviously, a rather powerful brush cultivator!

"Grandpa... Grandpa Xu, so your noodle shop is not open anymore?" Ye Xiaoxiao asked softly.

"Two things don't interfere with each other," Zui Qianchun casually said without further explanation, and then solemnly said, "Then let's prepare for the class. From now on, you can still call me Grandpa Xu."

"Okay..."

Han Yue and the others answered in a dazed manner, until now, their minds were still a bit muddled.

How did Grandpa Xu, the noodle seller, suddenly become a powerful brush cultivator?

The sun rose higher.

In front of the Changsheng Academy, Cao Boyu's small body swayed unsteadily, drenched in sweat, his face and lips pale.

His knees hurt, a piercing pain.

As the young master of the Magistrate's mansion, he had never experienced such suffering before.

Cao Boyu had almost given up several times, but when he thought of Huang Xuan, Han Yue, Ye Xiaoxiao, Xiao Fan and the others, he gritted his teeth and persevered.

He, Cao Boyu, was no inferior to anyone!

More and more passersby stopped at the academy gate, their gazes filled with both worry and empathy as they looked at the young master Cao Boyu.

This couldn't go on, he might even die at this rate, after all, he was just over ten years old!

The townspeople were anxious, repeatedly trying to persuade him.

But at this moment, Cao Boyu couldn't hear them at all, completely immersed in his own world, a world that was gradually spinning.

His knees were completely numb, and his strength was exhausted.

Just then, Lu Changsheng returned carrying a bag of fruits. The townspeople were delighted, like seeing a savior, but they didn't dare say anything, only looking at him hopefully.

Lu Changsheng didn't even glance at Cao Boyu, walking straight towards the academy.

Suddenly, there was a "bang" sound.

Lu Changsheng stopped in his tracks.

Because a small hand had grabbed the hem of his pants.

Cao Boyu had collapsed to the ground, tightly clutching Lu Changsheng's pant leg, hoarsely pleading, "Sir... please..."

Lu Changsheng looked back and smiled, "You're the first one to grab my pants."

Cao Boyu trembled and let go of his grip.

"Come to the academy to audit the class tomorrow, and we'll see how you perform."

Lu Changsheng said, then tossed a fruit precisely into Cao Boyu's hand before entering the academy.

Cao Boyu just lay on the ground, staring dazedly at the fruit in his hand, his eyes brimming with tears, his vision gradually blurring.

Soon, his eyes closed, and he passed out.

But the fruit was still tightly gripped in his unconscious hand, as if letting go would mean losing this hard-won opportunity.
