Today, the Qingyun Sect was more lively than usual.

The disciples had previously seen many spiritual boats approaching from the horizon.

At this time, there were dozens of figures in the main hall, all of them elders from the various sects in the Eastern Domain, as well as the elders of the Qingyun Sect itself. Fortunately, the Qingyun Sect's main hall was extremely spacious and could easily accommodate them.

"Hmm, why don't I see the venerable elder of your sect?"

"Yes, the old master, this time he came to have some medicinal pills refined, but where is he?"

"......"

The elders of the various sects were all looking for the figure of Gu Helin, but to no avail, so they turned to the Qingyun Sect elders with a smile.

It was clear that Gu Helin had a certain reputation in the immortal cultivation world.

"Hehe, esteemed elders of the various sects, Helin is still in the mortal realm at the moment," the Grand Elder said politely.

"I see." The elders of the various sects nodded.

At this moment, Shen Miaoyun slowly entered the hall from outside.

"Sect Master Shen."

The elders of the various sects hurriedly cupped their hands in greeting.

Shen Miaoyun swept her gaze across the elders in the hall, her face smiling, but in her heart, she sighed.

Indeed, there were too few sects willing to believe them, and the elders of the various sects numbered no more than fifteen...

The Eastern Domain was so vast, with countless large and small sects, yet only these few had come, and apart from a few sects like Feixing Valley that were roughly on par with her Qingyun Sect, the rest were mostly small sects.

Soon, Shen Miaoyun sat down on the dais.

As soon as she sat down, the Five Elders of Feixing Valley stood up.

"Sect Master Shen, could you please tell us what happened before that made your Qingyun Sect believe that the Demon and Devil Realms are plotting to seize the immortal tombs?"

Hearing this, the elders of the various sects looked intently at Shen Miaoyun on the dais.

After all, they had come here, which would undoubtedly take up a lot of their time. Wouldn't it be better to use that time to seclude themselves and search for treasures?

"Grand Elder, you can tell them," Shen Miaoyun said indifferently.

The Grand Elder bowed slightly and then looked at the elders of the various sects and began to speak: "Esteemed elders, the matter is as follows..."

A moment later,

The elders of the various sects frowned deeply in thought. It turned out that Gu Helin was the first to discover this matter.

And according to what the Grand Elder of the Qingyun Sect had just said and analyzed, combined with the unusual demonic calamities in the Lishui Empire in recent years, it seemed that this was indeed the case.

"Sect Master Shen, the Patriarch has ordered that I lead our disciples to guard Lishui and the surrounding secular empires for three years. If any unusual demonic calamities occur during this period, we will jointly spread the truth of this matter. But if no such demonic calamities occur during this period, we will return to the valley."

The Five Elders relaxed their brows and cupped their hands solemnly.

The other elders of the various sects also nodded in agreement. Indeed, the truth of this matter would have to be seen in the aftermath.

"Good, then I thank the esteemed elders for their efforts."

Shen Miaoyun stood up and bowed slightly.

"Sect Master Shen, then we shall take our leave."

Soon, the elders of the various sects paid their respects and one by one left the main hall, gathering their disciples and leaving.

"Sect Master, in three years, the Demon and Devil Realms will surely cause trouble again. When that time comes, the elders will see it with their own eyes and will surely believe!"

The Grand Elder looked at the departing elders and then at Shen Miaoyun, smiling excitedly.

Shen Miaoyun's face was expressionless, without a trace of joy.

Three years was not a long time for the Demon and Devil Realms. Whether they would cause trouble or not was uncertain, as they had once laid low for five years.

If the Demon and Devil Realms did not cause any trouble in these three years, then the Qingyun Sect would become a laughingstock.

"You may all leave, I am weary."

Shen Miaoyun waved her hand listlessly and closed her eyes.

The elders quietly withdrew.

"Sigh."

Shen Miaoyun sighed, waved towards the tea on the table, and the tea flew into her hand. She took a sip and opened her eyes.

Then she was stunned.

An unassuming middle-aged man in plain robes was standing in the main hall, looking at her indifferently.
