Four blurry figures swiftly darted across.

"Is that the Qingxuan Town ahead?" Jiang Chu asked.

"Haha, yes, it is. This journey has been quite tiring for you two elders Jiang," Kunlun Yu said apologetically.

Jiang Chu slightly furrowed his brows but didn't say anything. Instead, the usually more silent Jiang Chong asked, "Your Black Abyss Domain has about twenty major cities and over a hundred small towns, and the distances between them are so far. Why don't you establish teleportation arrays?"

Kunlun Yu and the Vice Palace Master exchanged a bitter smile.

Establishing teleportation arrays would certainly be more convenient. Who wouldn't know that?

Kunlun Yu sighed helplessly, "Elder Jiang, the cities and towns in the Black Abyss Domain have their own powers. They won't agree to it."

The Vice Palace Master also said, "Yes, long ago, some major cities did want to establish teleportation arrays to consolidate the small towns in the Black Abyss Domain. But this place is filled with ruthless people. If their biggest interests are threatened, the cities and towns will unite against the outside. It's very difficult to intervene. Later, the Domain Lord even issued a decree prohibiting the establishment of teleportation arrays between the major cities and towns in the Black Abyss Domain, and it has been that way since then."

Hearing this, Jiang Chu and Jiang Chong nodded in understanding. It made sense for the Black Abyss Domain.

"Haha, we'll soon arrive at Qingxuan Town. I wonder how extraordinary this famed Washing Soul Liquor really is?" the Vice Palace Master licked his lips and chuckled.

Huh?

Jiang Chu and Jiang Chong's brows furrowed at this.

Seeing this, Kunlun Yu quickly turned his head and sternly said, "Vice Palace Master! Our main task is to help the two Elders Jiang find someone. Drinking is just a side matter. Don't mix up the priorities!"

The Vice Palace Master's expression changed slightly, and he replied in a hoarse voice, "Palace Master, Elders Jiang, I naturally know the priorities."

...

Peng Fang had spent a large sum to buy a small courtyard near the Longevity Wine House.

He didn't know where he got a rocking chair, which he placed in the courtyard under the sunlight and lay down on it.

"Ah, the innkeeper really knows how to enjoy life. This rocking chair is indeed comfortable, rocking back and forth~" Peng Fang sighed contentedly.

Then he looked towards the Longevity Wine House, where a long queue was lining up.

Peng Fang clicked his tongue and started counting on his fingers, soon frowning worriedly.

"Ah, according to the queue list, I'll have to wait for over ten days before I can drink the Washing Soul Liquor... How am I going to endure this?"

"Hm?"

Suddenly, Peng Fang's brows raised, and he sat up from the rocking chair. His gaze was fixed on the distant horizon.

"A Nine-Rank Immortal Venerable, a One-Turning Immortal Emperor, and two Two-Turning Immortal Emperors?"

Peng Fang's eyes flickered, and he slowly lay back down. He wanted to see what purpose these four people had in coming to Qingxuan Town...

Thinking of Lu Changsheng, he couldn't help but wonder if these people were here for the innkeeper. He was certain he had nothing to do with them. Other than the unfathomable Lu Changsheng, Peng Fang really couldn't think of anyone else in Qingxuan Town who could attract such powerful Immortal Emperors.

Oh well, he'll just observe the situation quietly.

With that thought, Peng Fang turned on his side on the rocking chair, his eyes fixed unblinkingly on the Longevity Wine House.

...

On the streets of Qingxuan Town, the four figures were walking slowly, their gazes discreetly scanning the faces of every person they passed.

The copper mirror in Jiang Chu's hand showed no reaction.

Kunlun Yu smiled and asked, "Elder Jiang, we've traveled so far. Shall we go to the Longevity Wine House for a drink first?"

The Vice Palace Master's eyes lit up at the mention of this, and he swallowed hard, feeling a little tempted.
