Because as soon as the cultivators in this last row were looted, they would then be able to fully understand the intentions of the sea cultivators, which was also their own fate - death, or a slim chance of survival...

"Hey, you all! Hand over the treasures on your bodies, quickly!" the ruthless sea cultivator growled.

The other cultivators in the last row silently opened their storage spaces, and the sea cultivators, seeing this, were slightly satisfied. Then they glared viciously at Luchang Sheng and the other two: "And you three, hurry up!"

Chen Pang's lips trembled, terrified, and then he exchanged a glance with Xiao Chai. The two of them tremblingly opened their storage spaces.

On this side of the Three Thousand Worlds, they were also from humble origins, and in fact had hardly any treasures on them. So even if their worldly possessions were robbed, they were not too concerned about it.

At this moment, what they wanted most was to survive.

"Hmm?"

At this time, Luchang Sheng, who had been standing still without any action, caught the attention of all the cultivators and sea cultivators!

Uh, this azure-robed cultivator...

"I told you to open your storage space, can't you hear?! Are your ears deaf?!"

The sea cultivator lost his patience, glaring coldly at Luchang Sheng.

Beside him, Chen Pang and Xiao Chai suddenly became anxious, and in their worry, they hurriedly transmitted a message to Luchang Sheng:

"Fellow Daoist Luchang, as long as the green hills remain, there will be no shortage of firewood. Let's just hand over the treasures to him. Even if we give them, we may not necessarily live, but if we don't give them, we will definitely die!"

"Fellow Daoist Luchang..."

Chen Pang and Xiao Chai's transmission was filled with tension and worry. After they finished, seeing that Luchang Sheng remained unmoved, their hearts became even more anxious.

"Bang!"

Suddenly, the ruthless sea cultivator unexpectedly exploded into a mist of blood!

The entire Sea Transport Number Three fell into deathly silence!

Not only were the stunned cultivators amazed, even the group of sea cultivators stared with disbelief at this scene!

Chen Pang and Xiao Chai were shaken, their pupils contracting. They discreetly glanced at the calm Luchang Sheng, their gazes filled with bewilderment and shock.

"Who? Who was it! Come out!!"

The demonic cultivator leader regained his senses and angrily glared at the group of cultivators, who hurriedly shook their heads and backed away, indicating it was not them.

Due to the cultivators' retreat, Luchang Sheng, Chen Pang, and Xiao Chai, who were standing still, quickly became the focus of attention!

Immediately, all gazes turned towards them again!

Chen Pang was terrified, his calves trembling, and Xiao Chai was not much better off.

The pressure they felt at this moment was only known to themselves.

"Since it wasn't you two, why are you just standing there?"

Luchang Sheng suddenly turned his head and lightly asked the two.

Chen Pang and Xiao Chai opened their mouths, hesitant, not knowing what to say.

They had actually already vaguely guessed that it was Luchang Sheng who did it, although they didn't know how Luchang Sheng accomplished it. But now they had no time to ponder further.

They only knew that although they had only known Luchang Sheng for a short time, they instinctively could not bring themselves to abandon him.

In their line of work, they valued camaraderie.

"Was it you?!"

At this time, the demonic cultivator leader coldly stared at Luchang Sheng, gritting his teeth and asking.

Luchang Sheng's mouth curled up slightly, and with a finger forming a sword, without any further ado, he lightly slashed out!

"Jing!"

Only a sword's cry resounded in the vast sea.

Immediately, countless sword beams criss-crossed in the space, illuminating the dark night like broad daylight!

Swoosh swoosh swoosh swoosh swoosh--!

Rumble rumble--

In the next moment, those nine massive sea cultivator ships suddenly split into four or five pieces, collapsing and emitting a huge commotion, before gradually sinking into the depths of the sea!

Everyone stared blankly at this scene, their bodies shivering with cold!

Chen Pang and Xiao Chai exchanged a horrified glance, then carefully glanced at Luchang Sheng's side profile, and finally cautiously moved a distance away.

The group of sea cultivators stared blankly at this scene, their bodies slightly trembling. They had a premonition that this time, they might have stepped on a steel plate!

If it was just the destruction of nine ordinary ships, they would not have felt this way.
