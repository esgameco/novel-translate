Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

In the main hall of the Qingyun Sect.

Shen Miaojun sat silently on the high seat, her expression blank, her face slightly pale.

The aftereffects of having her soul searched by Jiang Yuan three years ago had not eased up even now, and she feared she would never be able to advance to the Divinity Realm in this lifetime.

The incident of her soul being forcibly searched was known only to her, and she had not told anyone about it. She knew that telling anyone would be useless, only adding to her troubles.

"Sect Master, as expected, all the sects in the Eastern Domain are now mocking us," the Grand Elder sighed as he looked at the silent Elders.

Now they did not even dare to go out and play chess or have tea with their old friends.

Shen Miaojun stood up and said indifferently, "Let them mock, there will come a day when they regret it."

Gu Helin opened his mouth, then sighed.

Just then, Shen Miaojun's eyes flickered, and a talisman appeared in her hand, burning.

Immediately, a figure appeared in the hall.

"The Patriarch of the Xitong Carefree Abode will ascend to the Tribulation in the third month, and all Nascent Soul and above cultivators are welcome to observe!"

Ascend the Tribulation, Tribulation of Ascension?!

The Elders in the hall exchanged shocked glances, taking in sharp breaths.

"Sect Master, this is wonderful news. The Patriarch of the Carefree Abode ascending the Tribulation will surely sweep through the entire cultivation world, and the other sects will no longer have the time to focus their attention on our Qingyun Sect."

Gu Helin stepped forward to soothe.

For some reason, he had always felt that there was something off about Shen Miaojun these past three years. As if... something was missing.

Just as Shen Miaojun was about to speak, a middle-aged stranger suddenly appeared in the hall.

"Patriarch!"

Everyone exclaimed in surprise!

The Patriarch had come out of seclusion!

Could it be that he had successfully advanced to the Divinity Realm?

They were shocked, but also felt a sense of reassurance.

"The affairs of the sect will be handled by the Grand Elder. Miaojun, come with me to the Western Domain to observe the Tribulation of Ascension."

Shen Lingfeng looked at Shen Miaojun and smiled.

"Patriarch, I..."

Shen Miaojun hesitated.

"Hmm?" Shen Lingfeng's gaze turned puzzled, then he swept his eyes over the Elders and said with a smile, "As for the matter of the demonic and evil realms plotting to seize the immortal tomb, I already know about it. No wonder you all are like this. In the future, they will understand that you should just focus on your own cultivation, and not worry about the opinions and words of others."

The Elders were stunned, then a warm current flowed through their hearts. They had thought the Patriarch would scold them, but the outcome was different from their expectations.

"Patriarch, I will not go to observe the Tribulation of Ascension," Shen Miaojun said hoarsely.

Not go?

A flicker of puzzlement passed through Shen Lingfeng's eyes as he said in a serious tone, "Miaojun, do you not know how greatly observing the Tribulation of Ascension can benefit cultivators at the Nascent Soul, Divinity, and even Tribulation Realm? This is a rare opportunity that comes once in a lifetime."

The Elders were also dumbfounded. Yes, why wouldn't she go?

Especially the Grand Elder, who was the second Nascent Soul cultivator in the Qingyun Sect after Shen Miaojun. He was eager to go.

Feeling the gazes of the people around her, Shen Miaojun's body began to tremble slightly, and she pressed her lips tightly together.

"Hmm?"

Just then, as if he had sensed something, Shen Lingfeng's gaze changed slightly. In a flash, he appeared in front of Shen Miaojun, tapping her forehead with his finger.

Soon, in the bewildered looks of the Elders, he shouted sternly, "Your spiritual sense is severely damaged, you've actually been soul-searched? Tell me, who was it? I'll tear him apart!"
