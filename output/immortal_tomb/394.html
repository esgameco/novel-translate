Human Realm.

The five Human Realm Overlords gathered, their eyes trembling as they gazed at the hazy sky.

"Ascending to the Divine..." Jiang Wuxin murmured. "Literally, it should mean breaking through to the Divine Realm, otherwise the Heavenly Dao wouldn't be involved."

The expressions of Jian Wuhui and the others gradually became grave. If the Divine Lord truly broke through... the consequences would be unimaginable.

Han Jinlong said helplessly, "No wonder the myriad races have not descended for a long time, no wonder even the top ten great clans don't care about their own holy sons and daughters - it turns out they're waiting for the Divine Lord to break through the Divine Realm..."

Man Tian pursed his lips, "With Lord Lu around, we might have had a chance even against the leaders of the top ten great clans, but the Divine Realm..."

As he spoke, Man Tian's expression became extremely bitter.

It wasn't that he lacked confidence in Lu Changsheng's strength - everyone had witnessed Lu Changsheng's power.

It was just that the three words "Divine Realm" were filled with unparalleled oppression.

After all, it was a legendary realm.

The Divine Realm, the Divine Realm, it must be the true Divine Beings.

Jian Wuhui said in a solemn voice, "Don't think too much about it. What's meant to come will come. Now we can only pray that the Divine Lord's breakthrough fails."

Hearing this, the group fell silent, furrowing their brows and focusing on the situation. The cultivators of the entire Human Realm were also in a state of extreme tension, except for the old turtle who was sleeping soundly.

...

At the same time, in the Divine Realm.

Above the sky, in addition to the Divine Elemental, there seemed to be something else...

"The shackles of the Divine-Demonic Battlefield have been opened, and I have also helped you introduce the Mysterious Spiritual Energy. Whether you can break through now depends on you," the Heavenly Dao said faintly.

Divine Lord felt his heart pounding as he sensed the Mysterious Spiritual Energy in the air, completely different from the Divine Elemental.

Regarding the Divine Realm, he had already gained insights. Now, all he had to do was to absorb the Mysterious Spiritual Energy in the air, and he might be able to smoothly ascend to the Divine Realm!

However, the process of absorbing the Mysterious Spiritual Energy was also very dangerous.

In addition to the nine great clan leaders, another dangerous aspect was when he replaced the Divine Elemental within his body with the Mysterious Spiritual Energy. If his body was not compatible or there was a problem in any step, it would be fatal.

Soon, Divine Lord's gaze sharpened, no longer hesitating. He looked at the illusory Heavenly Dao and cupped his hands, "Thank you, great Heavenly Dao, for introducing the Mysterious Spiritual Energy."

The Heavenly Dao shook his head, glanced at the Imprisoning Divine Formation on the side, and then looked deeply at Divine Lord before dissipating.

Divine Lord's expression became grave. He immediately sat down to begin absorbing the Mysterious Spiritual Energy, not daring to delay.

He had to hurry and absorb all the Mysterious Spiritual Energy in the air before Bi Youao, Zhen Tian, and the other great clan leaders broke through the Imprisoning Divine Formation!

Although Divine Lord knew that Bi You and the other great clan leaders were not proficient in formations, he couldn't be sure that no accidents would occur. After all, to become the leaders of the top ten great clans, they must have some tricks up their sleeves.

Today was indeed somewhat dangerous, but he would seek fortune in the midst of danger. As long as he succeeded in breaking through to the Divine Realm, he could lead the Divine Realm Tribe to greater glory!

At that time, not only the Beast Tribe and the Dragon Tribe, even the entire Divine-Demonic Battlefield would have to kneel before him, the Divine Lord!

Divine Lord even had a feeling that as long as he attained the Divine Realm, he might even be able to arm-wrestle with the Heavenly Dao of the Divine-Demonic Battlefield!

Thinking of this, a scorching heat arose in Divine Lord's heart. He then took a deep breath, assumed a stance, and began to focus on absorbing the Mysterious Spiritual Energy.

Gradually, his face became pale, and his expression was agonized. Replacing the Divine Elemental with the Mysterious Spiritual Energy felt like having one's tendons and bones torn apart, just like ordinary people experiencing muscle cramps.

The Divine Realm Tribe members watched the Divine Lord, who had clearly entered a state of cultivation in the sky, not daring to make a sound. If it weren't for the continuous rumbling of the black thunderbolts crashing into the Imprisoning Divine Formation and the clanging of the chains, it would have been eerily quiet.

...

"Sigh! At this moment, Divine Lord is probably already beginning to break through to the Divine Realm, while we are still trapped in this Imprisoning Divine Formation! Damn it! Damn it!!"

Inside the Imprisoning Divine Formation, one of the clan leaders finally lost his composure and started cursing.

The other clan leaders all had solemn expressions, remaining silent. They could understand his feelings.

Because if Divine Lord succeeded in his breakthrough...
