As soon as Lu Changsheng took his seat, Yun Zeao returned to his seat, smiling and saying, "Everyone, let's drink and enjoy the dance!"

"Yes, Your Majesty!"

The officials quickly responded.

The banquet then officially began, and the officials chatted and enjoyed themselves. However, the corner of everyone's eyes in the hall kept stealing glances at the young man in blue robes who was quietly drinking and watching the dance.

The old turtle sat behind Lu Changsheng, his eyes shining as he watched the dancing girls, occasionally glimpsing the white skin revealed by the swaying robes. He nodded his head from time to time, seemingly evaluating them in his mind.

To be honest, the beauties in the royal palace were indeed quite good, although not much different from those in the Wanhua Garden, and even a little inferior. But the palace beauties had one advantage - they lacked a hint of worldliness.

Thinking of this, the old turtle suddenly beckoned to Ye Xunwang.

Ye Xunwang, who was constantly keeping an eye on Lu Changsheng's side, naturally noticed it immediately and quickly went to the old turtle's side. "Senior Turtle, what are your orders?"

"Hehe." The old turtle first let out a flirtatious laugh, then said solemnly, "Little Ye, Grandpa Turtle thinks that dancing girl is quite good, both in her dance and her looks."

As the old turtle spoke, his gaze indicated the lead dancer in the sheer veil.

Ye Xunwang paused for a moment, then followed the old turtle's line of sight and immediately understood. He then transmitted a message, "Senior Turtle, don't worry. As soon as the banquet is over, I will arrange for that dancer to meet you..."

Hearing this, the old turtle stroked his beard and looked at Ye Xunwang approvingly, "The child is educable."

"Hehe," Ye Xunwang laughed awkwardly.

At the same time,

After clinking cups with the Prime Minister Zhang Dao, Wind Changsheng glanced in the direction of Lu Changsheng, saying meaningfully, "Prime Minister Zhang, this person is..."

Zhang Dao's hand holding the cup paused, then he smiled calmly and said, "Crown Prince, that gentleman... I don't know him either, I only know he is an honored guest of ours in Yunlan, and the King and Lord Ye both hold him in high regard."

Honored guest?

Wind Changsheng gave a cold smile. It shouldn't be just an honored guest, right?

Judging from Yun Zeao's earlier behavior, this was no simple honored guest. Yun Zeao's attitude towards him was one of deep respect.

Even in the face of Wind Changsheng, the Crown Prince, Yun Zeao's attitude was not as respectful.

While Wind Changsheng was curious about the identity of the young man in blue robes, he also had the desire to converse with him.

No matter what, a person who could make Yun Zeao show such an attitude must have some background.

If he could befriend this person, it would only bring benefits and no harm.

Moreover, the Fengluan Ancient Kingdom was now in its heyday, and Wind Changsheng believed this young man would be more than happy to befriend the Crown Prince.

Wind Changsheng made up his mind, then smiled and said, "Prime Minister, please continue to enjoy your drink. I will go and have a chat with this gentleman."

As Wind Changsheng spoke, he filled his cup and then walked over.

Yun Zeao was just about to lead the officials in toasting Lu Changsheng, when he saw Wind Changsheng heading towards Lu Changsheng with a cup, and his heart skipped a beat!

What is this Wind Changsheng up to?

Yun Zeao instinctively wanted to stop him, but in such a short distance, Wind Changsheng had already reached Lu Changsheng in the blink of an eye, and it was too late.

Ye Xunwang, Yun Daoxie, and the others frowned and their heartbeats quickened.

They did not expect Wind Changsheng to suddenly make this move.

After the initial worry, Yun Daoxie suddenly thought of something, and his eyes gradually narrowed. Wind Changsheng, you'd better not court death, or you might offend the gentleman.

At this moment, the entire hall had fallen silent, with only the soft music echoing through the hall.

The dancers noticed the change and quietly retreated.

Wind Changsheng approached Lu Changsheng, first sizing him up, then holding his cup and arrogantly said, "I am Wind Changsheng, the Crown Prince of the Fengluan Ancient Kingdom. May I ask your esteemed name?"

The old turtle saw that Lu Changsheng's cup was empty, and quickly came over to refill it.

Lu Changsheng raised the cup and took a light sip, then indifferently said, "The dance was going so well, why did it stop?"

From beginning to end, Lu Changsheng's gaze never fell on the Wind Changsheng standing before him, as if the person in front of him was not a person, but just a wisp of air.

Yun Zeao's face paled at this, and he immediately shouted angrily, "Outrageous! Where are the dancers? Who told you to stop? Hurry up and continue dancing!"

Everyone in the hall was startled and on edge.

He was the Crown Prince of Fengluan now!!

Your Majesty, the young man in blue robes is someone we cannot provoke, please think carefully!

Your Majesty, the Fengluan Kingdom is someone we cannot provoke, please think carefully!

The two sides had completely different thoughts!

As for Yunze Cang, he had no hesitation at all, and decisively waved his hand:

"Bring the enemy Crown Prince to the dungeon and await further orders!!"

Feng Changxuan staggered, looking at Yunze Cang in disbelief.

"Why? Why?"

Old Turtle looked at Feng Changxuan and sneered, "Why? You will soon understand."

Old Turtle finished speaking and looked at Yexin Mo meaningfully before leaving the hall.
