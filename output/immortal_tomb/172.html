Wind, Fire, and Thunder Envoys?

He underestimated, or rather, they all underestimated. The Proprietor and this fat man are both so powerful, yet they still come to this Qingxuan Town, isn't this just bullying the weak?

Incomprehensible, truly incomprehensible.

"Then, I'll leave it to the senior," Left Chou Shan Chou said respectfully.

"Senior, that's the gist of my grievances with the Jiang Clan Imperial Clan," Left Chou Shan Chou said through gritted teeth.

If his strength allowed it, he really wanted to tear Jiang Taiqi apart!

"You little guy, I didn't expect this..." Peng Fang exclaimed in surprise.
