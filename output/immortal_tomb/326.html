Seeing the disciples who seemed somewhat at a loss, Lu Changsheng's mouth curved into a slight arc as he said:

"In this Linglang Heavens, the Carefree Sect is already the strongest. Without any rivals or sense of crisis, it is difficult to make further progress. Since I have built the Carefree Sect, as the Sect Master, I naturally have to consider you all."

"The path of cultivation seems to be endless. Are you seeking that elusive and ethereal longevity?"

"Or do you wish to stop at the Linglang Heavens, waiting for your realm to reach its limit, and then fade away in the vast river of time?"

Longevity.

The people on the martial field were momentarily dazed. The word "longevity" seemed to be an aspiration that was lofty and ethereal at any realm.

From the Refining Qi stage, they had heard of longevity. Some cultivators cultivated immortality to fly freely, while others cultivated to uphold justice and eliminate demons in the world.

But there was one thing that was the common dream of all cultivators - that was longevity.

But does true longevity really exist in the world?

Whether it was Li Shuoyan or the five Elder Peng, their eyes were filled with bewilderment at this moment.

All along, from the Refining Qi stage, through Foundation Establishment, Golden Core, Nascent Soul, and even reaching the current Divine Elemental and Divine Yang realms, their lifespans had only increased incrementally.

Longevity remained elusive and ethereal.

Beside them, the old turtle looked at the perplexed crowd, then stole a glance at Lu Changsheng on the high platform.

He did not feel that longevity was ethereal, after all, his master was an immortal. Yes, his name was fitting.

And as the mount of his master, he was the only immortal heavenly turtle in the world. However, he could not be compared to his master, whose eternal lifespan was truly immortal.

As for himself, he was only nearly immortal. Perhaps in the distant future, he too would fade away in the river of time.

Of course, he was also aware that as his opponents grew stronger, his own strength was no longer sufficient. That's why he had been cultivating frantically recently.

Otherwise, it would be a joke if the immortal heavenly turtle was killed by someone.

Although his life was extremely resilient and the chance of being killed was small, even if he was on the verge of death, he would slowly recover. But if the opponent was strong enough, they could still suppress and imprison him.

This was similar to how his master was suppressed and imprisoned in the immortal tomb by those mysterious figures thousands of years ago due to his insufficient strength at the time.

Speaking of this, the old turtle couldn't help but wonder, since his master had returned, had he resolved those mysterious figures?

...

"To the Sect Master! We do not wish to stop at the Linglang Heavens!"

Quickly, the people on the martial field shouted while bowing.

Especially the five Elder Peng, their faces were filled with excitement!

No one here understood the pain of reaching the limit of their realm better than these five.

Lu Changsheng nodded slightly and said, "The path ahead is long and arduous. That is a world full of wonders and perils, where myriad tribes contend."

Myriad tribes contend!

What a grand and magnificent world that must be!

The expressions of the crowd were greatly shaken!

Ever since the Carefree Sect had dominated the Linglang Heavens, their spirits had been lacking. But in that moment, it had returned.

And in their understanding, there were only a few simple categories of tribes, such as humans, demonic beasts, and evil monsters.

But myriad tribes? It was simply unimaginable.

"As for the specifics, I'm not entirely sure either. We'll know once we get there," Lu Changsheng said.

Elder Peng hesitated slightly, then gathered the courage to ask the question on everyone's mind:

"Sect Master, when will we go, and how will we go? What about the sect?"

After Peng said this, the crowd looked around the Carefree Sect's premises, their eyes filled with reluctance. After all, this was their sect's headquarters.

"That's simple. I will move the entire sect. For the time being, you can stay in the sect and do as you please. When we arrive at the destination, I will..."
