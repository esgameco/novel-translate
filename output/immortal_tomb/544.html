Here is my translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Outside the Qiuqing Pavilion, there was a large crowd gathered. It was because Zuichenci had brought Wangsu and the others here. Wangsu and the others looked around the Qiuqing Pavilion suspiciously, wondering why this old man had brought them to this place of romantic charm.

Han Yue and Ye Xiaoxiao were still just children - how could they be in a brothel?

"This is the place, you all go in yourselves," Zuichenci said with a smile.

"If we go in and don't see those two children, do you know what will happen to you?" an elder of the sect glared at Zuichenci and demanded.

"If they really are inside, how could old Xu dare to deceive you all? If they are not there, old Xu is at your disposal," Zuichenci sighed and cupped his hands.

Cao Yulong and the other townspeople frowned, not knowing what old Xu was up to, but they were very worried - if old Xu offended these people, he would likely have no place to be buried.

Some people, concerned for old Xu, quietly left the crowd and ran towards the Changsheng Academy, intending to seek help from Lu Changsheng.

Suddenly, a huge commotion erupted - the wall of the Qiuqing Pavilion was blasted open, startling the townspeople. Two figures were then shot out like cannonballs, crashing heavily to the ground. They were Songren and the elder, coughing up blood, their bones completely shattered except for their skulls.

Wangsu and the others were shocked, realizing there must be a powerful expert within the Qiuqing Pavilion. At this moment, a young man in blue robes slowly approached the entrance, accompanied by Huangyulang, the madam, and some girls and customers. Upon seeing this young man, Wangsu and the others felt a chill and their legs began to weaken.

"Mr. Lu," Cao Yulong and the townspeople hurriedly greeted.

Lu Changsheng glanced at Zuichenci, who felt guilty and coughed, avoiding his gaze. Lu Changsheng spoke softly, "I was just listening to the music, and you all came barging in, forcing the courtesans to stop playing, disturbing my enjoyment, and disrupting everyone else's mood. Who gave you the nerve? Was it old Xu?"

Wangsu quickly pointed at Zuichenci, saying, "Yes, yes! This old wretch did it on purpose to lure us here!"
