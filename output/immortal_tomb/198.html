"Little black guys, Abin, you two must quickly break through to the Earthly Immortal Realm. Nowadays, you two are dragging down the lower limit of the sect's strength."

This really shocked them.

For them, even the outer disciples now have high status, not to mention the inner disciples, and the special identity of the Holy Son afterwards.

That is an existence they look up to!

Hearing this, Cai Xu and Huang Bin's faces flushed slightly, and they glanced at each other awkwardly.

Indeed, they were really dragging down the sect's standards.

No, with two and a half years left, they must cultivate diligently. In any case, they must break through to the Earthly Immortal Realm!

"Hehe, don't worry everyone, Abin and I will go cultivate right away, we definitely won't drag the sect down!"

Cai Xu smiled and left with Huang Bin in a hurry.

"Haha haha." The miscellaneous disciples saw this and burst out laughing.

"You bunch of miscellaneous disciples, don't think you don't have to cultivate! The Grand Competition of the Ten Thousand Sects is something you all have to participate in! Whoever laughs again, will lose their spirit stones for this month!"

At this moment, the voice of the Miscellaneous Peak executive suddenly rang out.

The smiles on the faces of the miscellaneous disciples instantly froze...

"Yes!"

They respectfully bowed and quickly disappeared without a trace!

They now know that the executives of their sect are no ordinary executives!

At least they are all Transcendent Immortal Realm and above in cultivation!

It's better to run for their lives!

on the mountain path,

Tong Xin and Lin Dong were walking together.

The disciples they encountered all respectfully greeted them, and the two responded with gentle smiles.

"Junior brother, are you about to undergo the Tribulation?" Tong Xin asked, tilting his head to look at Lin Dong's profile.

Hearing this, Tong Xin's expression fell, and he punched Lin Dong's chest, sighing, "You little rascal, you're really too fast... Damn it."

As Lin Dong's junior brother, his cultivation had caught up to his senior brother, causing an imbalance in his heart.

Lin Dong pretended to be in pain, taking a step back, and then smiled, "Senior brother, you can't blame me for this. You were just playing around during the previous period."

Over these two and a half years, all the talk and news in the Human Realm revolved around the Grand Competition of the Ten Thousand Sects. But during this period, all the sects had fallen silent, as everyone knew they were focusing on cultivation.

Though it was only two and a half years, no one dared to slack off at this critical time. Humans always tend to make a last-minute effort, which is normal. But it's undeniable that some were able to unleash their potential under this immense pressure and urgency.

For example, a year after Lin Dong successfully broke through to the Great Luo Golden Immortal realm, Tong Xin also managed to do the same. And Little Hei and A Bin finally broke through to the Earth Immortal realm as well.

Thus, the minimum cultivation of the Carefree Sect had finally risen from the Tribulation Crossing realm to the Earth Immortal realm. Though their aptitude was average, the Carefree Sect's abundant resources, combined with their intense efforts under pressure, allowed them to achieve this.

Two and a half years passed in the blink of an eye, especially for cultivators.

Today, countless spiritual ships streaked across the sky of the Human Realm, all heading towards the Extreme Cold Ice Field - the venue for the Nine Rank Sect Competition of the Grand Competition of the Ten Thousand Sects. The Nine Rank Sect Competition of the Human Realm would be held in the Extreme Cold Ice Field, while the locations for the Heavenly Realm and Earth Realm were unknown.

Ultimately, the top three Nine Rank Sects from each realm would face off in the final showdown.

In the region south of the Canglan Continent, the Blood Flame Pavilion, the Forestorm Gate, and the Rainbow Pavilion had set off early that morning, riding spiritual ships towards the Extreme Cold Ice Field. After all, with so many people, and the long distance, it would take some time to reach the Extreme Cold Ice Field.

Along the way to the Extreme Cold Ice Field, they would encounter many other sects, and conflicts and battles might even break out before they even reached the Extreme Cold Ice Field.

...

At the Carefree Sect's training ground, over ten thousand figures stood in neat formation, the atmosphere solemn.

Seeing this, Peng Fang and the others exchanged a smile.

Peng Fang, with his bulging belly, couldn't help but laugh heartily, "Relax a bit, you all don't need to be so serious. The early battles between the Nine Rank Sects are just child's play to our Carefree Sect."

Hearing this, the disciples felt relieved, their expressions easing.

That's right, with their Carefree Sect's strength, going to the Extreme Cold Ice Field was just a formality, like a pig pretending to be a tiger.

Afterwards, Peng Fang conjured a spiritual ship in his hand, infusing it with spiritual power, then flung it high into the sky.

The next moment, a spiritual ship that could cover the sky and block the sun hovered in the air, darkening the heavens.

This spiritual ship was large enough to accommodate their tens of thousands of people.

"Everyone, board the spiritual ship!" Peng Fang commanded in a low voice.

"Yes!"

Moments later, on the spiritual ship in the sky.

Peng Fang and the other elders and executives stood at the bow railing, their gazes flickering as they looked down at the Carefree Sect below.

Tong Xin, Lin Dong, and the other disciples stood behind them, their countless eyes gazing down at their own sect.

Peng Fang stood at the very front, taking a deep breath, and leading everyone in a respectful bow, about to say, "Sect Master, we..."

But before he could finish, a faint voice drifted from the back mountain of the Carefree Sect, carried by the wind:

"As my Carefree Sect's disciples go forth, they need not hold back against any.

If provoked, destroy. If challenged, slay."
