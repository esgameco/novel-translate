When Lü Changsheng walked back to the academy, the second class of the morning was just about to begin.

Han Yue, Ye Xiaoxiao, and Xiao Fan were already sitting properly, with the textbook "Cultivating Qi Technique" placed on the desks in front of them.

"How is the cultivation of the Cultivating Qi Technique going?" Lü Changsheng asked calmly from the podium.

Ye Xiaoxiao and Xiao Fan stammered, but it was the usually poorly-studied Han Yue who raised his hand and said, "Teacher, the student has already sensed the existence of qi!"

Hearing this, Ye Xiaoxiao and Xiao Fan were shocked.

So fast?

"Correct." Lü Changsheng nodded slightly, "Sensing the book qi is only the first step. The next most important thing is to absorb the book qi into the ink source."

Ink source!

The three of them were startled. They of course knew what the ink source was, as it was written in the Cultivating Qi Technique.

The ink source is not something everyone has, because the ink source can only appear in the dantian after being blessed by the saints of heaven and earth. The ink source is the core power of book cultivation.

With the ink source, one can be considered a true book cultivator, and the larger the ink source, the purer and more abundant the book qi, the stronger the book cultivation will be.

The ink sources of Han Yue, Ye Xiaoxiao, and Xiao Fan were currently in their initial state, extremely small, precisely because they had not yet refined the book qi into it.

"The attack methods of book cultivation mostly rely on the ink source, so you must diligently cultivate. Furthermore, whether it's the Mysterious Brush, the Spiritual Brocade, or the Heaven and Earth Unification Diagram, they all require a robust ink source for you to be able to wield them."

Lü Changsheng said calmly.

"Yes, teacher!" The three of them responded quickly.

Lü Changsheng nodded, sat down in his seat, and said, "The realms of book cultivation are quite simple, with only five major realms."

Han Yue, Ye Xiaoxiao, and Xiao Fan perked up, listening attentively. At the same time, they looked at the young man with a slightly dazed gaze - it was as if they had also become book cultivators and were officially beginning their cultivation, all of it like a dream.

Lü Changsheng continued:

"The Mortal Book Realm is when the ink source is the size of a container, and the protective brush is no more than ten zhang tall.

The Mysterious Book Realm is when the ink source is the size of a pool, and the protective brush exceeds ten zhang.

The Earth Book Realm is when the ink source is the size of a river, and the protective brush exceeds a hundred zhang.

The Heavenly Book Realm is when the ink source is the size of an ocean, and the protective brush exceeds a thousand zhang.

The Saint Realm is when the ink source is crystallized, and the protective brush exceeds ten thousand zhang."

Mortal Book Realm, Mysterious Book Realm, Earth Book Realm, Heavenly Book Realm, Saint Realm!

Han Yue, Ye Xiaoxiao, and Xiao Fan's gazes were burning with intensity, their breathing quickened.

So powerful!

"Teacher, does that mean Master Lü, Master Mo, and Master Zui are already at the Saint Realm?" Han Yue asked with a flushed face.

Ye Xiaoxiao and Xiao Fan also listened intently, as they had just seen Master Lü and Master Mo the day before.

"Yes, but they are no longer ordinary Saints, but the three Heavenly and Earthly Saints."

Lü Changsheng smiled, but did not elaborate further.

Han Yue, Ye Xiaoxiao, and Xiao Fan naturally did not dare to ask more, but they now knew the extraordinary status of the three Masters.

Ye Xiaoxiao suddenly asked curiously, "Teacher, we now know about the ink source, but what is the protective brush?"

Han Yue and Xiao Fan were also curious.

"The protective brush is both offensive and defensive, and as important as the ink source. If the ink source is the core power stored in book cultivation, then the protective brush is the medium through which the book qi is unleashed in battle. The size of the protective brush also symbolizes one's strength."

After Lü Changsheng's explanation, Han Yue, Ye Xiaoxiao, and Xiao Fan fell into contemplation.

Now that they had the ink source, how could they also see their protective brushes?

"Just focus on your cultivation, and when you have absorbed the book qi, you will naturally be able to see your protective brushes."

Huh? Sect Master, are you serious?
