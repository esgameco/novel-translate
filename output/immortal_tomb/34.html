Outside the town of Moyu, some of the sleeping civilians in the tents had distorted, agonized expressions on their faces, their limbs convulsing, as if they were trying to wake up, but... they just couldn't open their eyes.

A dream.

In front of their eyes, it seemed to be the endless riverbank, but they couldn't see the color of the water clearly, and the entire world was unusually blurry and deathly silent.

Only the continuous sound of the flowing water echoed in their ears...

Whooshing... whooshing...

Their inner hearts began to grow restless, only wanting to run, to keep running.

But no matter how they tried, they couldn't escape, the river was still the same river, the world remained deathly silent, and aside from the river, they couldn't see anything clearly, yet the sound of the rushing water in their ears grew even more violent!

They finally fell into despair, a great fear welling up...suppressed to the extreme...

"Let, let me out!!" In the dream, the people huddled on the ground, howling in despair, like enraged beasts on the verge of collapse.

"Hehe hehe~"

A strange dark shadow appeared before the people, letting out a sinister, smug laugh.

"Ghost, ghost!" A numbing sensation filled the people's minds, infinite fear enveloping them.

"Hehe, ghost? Oh no no no, we are the Nightmare Demons..." the dark shadow chuckled ominously.

"Nightmare Demons... what is that..." The people's heads throbbed with pain, as they gritted their teeth and cried out.

"It's... it's invading your dreams, and then making you become fearful, and we'll devour the dreams filled with your terror... Mmm, just thinking about it makes my mouth water..."

The dark shadow hugged itself, cackling maniacally, as if in the throes of an addiction.

"Demons, you are demons!"

"Hehe hehe, ah yes, that's right, the more fearful you are, the more delicious this dream becomes, come, continue..."

"Ahhh!!!!"

But in the dreams of many other sleeping civilians, the situation was different...

When the Nightmare Demon tribe entered the dreams, they were perplexed.

There was a transparent barrier in front of them, blocking their way.

They could only watch through the barrier as the civilians dreamed pleasant dreams.

They couldn't stand seeing this, they wanted to see fear! The fear of the people!!

Subsequently, they frantically clawed and raged outside the barrier, trying to break through this strange barrier, but no matter how they tried, not even a ripple appeared on the barrier...

"What is this barrier? Could it be that some cultivator is meddling?"

Many Nightmare Demons quickly regained their composure and prepared to escape the dream realm to report to their tribal leader!

But then... they were horrified to discover that not only could they not interfere with the dreams, they were also unable to escape the dream realm!

It was as if they were trapped in a small cage!

At that moment!

In all the dreams, the small space confining the Nightmare Demons, simultaneously flashed with a dazzling light!

This was an ultimate brightness that the Nightmare Demons, who abhorred daylight, had no time to be frightened before they all vanished into nothingness.

And the civilians, under this mysterious protection, continued to dream pleasant dreams.

However, those without the barrier to block the Nightmare Demons were killed in their dreams.

In the backyard of the Zhenmain's mansion,

Fruit after fruit was tossed into Luchengsheng's mouth, his pupils flickering with a faint blue glow in the darkness...

This was his Dao Eye, which could see the cycle of reincarnation, life and death, and the emptiness of the world.

Among these civilians, some had committed grave sins in their previous lives, and naturally, he had no intention of saving them.

"It seems I'll have to go to the Soul Realm soon, I wonder what that fellow Hunhui has been up to, letting so many sinful people reincarnate as humans again."
