In the Chengzhu Manor, in a luxurious room.

The unconscious Yun Aoye slowly regained consciousness.

Yun Aoye's eyes first gradually focused, then he stared blankly at the ceiling for a moment, before suddenly sitting up in alarm: "Summon someone!"

Boom!

The four subordinates outside quickly pushed open the door and hurried to Yun Aoye's side, bowing respectfully: "Your Highness!"

Yun Aoye glanced sideways at the four subordinates, frowning as he demanded: "Where am I? What happened?"

Yun Aoye's memory cut off right when he was about to scold that old servant, and he couldn't recall anything after that.

Yun Aoye felt very puzzled, wondering how he could have suddenly lost consciousness like that.

The four subordinates trembled slightly, and one of them respectfully replied:

"Your Highness, you are now in the Chengzhu Manor. As for why you are here, it was at the behest of the Qin Saint..."

As he spoke, the subordinate lowered his head even further.

He had already spoken very tactfully, not daring to directly say that it was the Qin Saint who had knocked Yun Aoye unconscious.

Yun Aoye was taken aback, but not entirely surprised, as he had vaguely guessed that it was Uncle Ye who had knocked him out.

After all, in this Tianxiang City, aside from Uncle Ye, who else would dare and have the ability to knock him unconscious?

The only question was, why did Uncle Ye want to knock him out?

Could it really be just because of the other party's musical skills?

Yun Aoye admitted that he might not be able to understand Uncle Ye's obsession with musical skills.

After a moment of silence, Yun Aoye pursed his lips and asked, "Where is Uncle Ye?"

Thump thump thump...

At this moment, Ye Xunwang entered the room.

"The Qin Saint!"

The four subordinates immediately bowed in reverence.

Although they were Yun Aoye's trusted and capable subordinates, they understood Ye Xunwang's weighty status in the ancient Kingdom of Yunlan, and naturally did not dare to show the slightest negligence.

Even if Yun Aoye and Ye Xunwang issued orders simultaneously, they would unhesitatingly obey Ye Xunwang's!

The four subordinates, having some foresight, bowed and then retreated, closing the door behind them.

After a brief silence in the room, a bitter smile appeared on Yun Aoye's face: "Uncle Ye, why..."

Of course, Yun Aoye could never get angry at Ye Xunwang, as aside from fearing him, he also held him in the utmost respect.

Ye Xunwang came over and sat down, taking out the ancient zither, gently caressing its broken and disheveled strings.

"Aoye, come with me to Youting Lake again tomorrow."

Yun Aoye paused, then excitedly said:

"Uncle Ye, are you going to discipline that arrogant master and servant? Good! Let's set out immediately!"

As he spoke, Yun Aoye quickly got up from the bed, unable to wait.

Ye Xunwang's brow furrowed, "Insolent!"

Huh?

Yun Aoye was taken aback, and although he was puzzled in his heart, he still lowered his head, not daring to look directly at Ye Xunwang, as he rarely saw Uncle Ye angry.

Ye Xunwang looked steadily at Yun Aoye. "Aoye, never dare to say such things again. If the royal family of the capital is destroyed, you will be a sinner for all eternity, and then it will be too late for regret."

Huh? This is so serious?

Yun Aoye was stunned again!

In his entire life, Yun Aoye had never felt as lost as he did today.

"Uncle Ye... Aoye does not understand."

Yun Aoye said bitterly.

Ye Xunwang caressed the ancient zither in front of him, closing his eyes as he said:

"That gentleman's servant could suppress me, your Uncle Ye, to death with a single palm strike..."

As he said this, Ye Xunwang, disregarding Yun Aoye's frantically widening eyes, his voice even slightly trembling, continued:

"Then do you think, the master behind him, is what kind of existence?"

Yun Aoye stood there dumbfounded, his pupils trembling violently!!

Yun Aoye understood Ye Xunwang, he was not the kind of person to make such a huge joke.

From Ye Xunwang's words, he could hear that he had already been disciplined by that servant...

Yun Aoye suddenly recalled his previous words and attitude at Youting Lake,

and his body finally began to tremble uncontrollably!!

His own death would not matter, after all, Yun Aoye had lived a life of glory and wealth.

But if he were to implicate the entire capital and the royal family, he would be forever recorded in history, and reviled for all eternity in the ancient Kingdom of Yunlan!

Thinking of this, Yun Aoye staggered, barely managing to support himself on the table, his complexion deathly pale, trembling as he said:

"Uncle Ye, what should I do?"

Ye Xunwang looked at the terrified Yun Aoye and solemnly said, "Apologize."

Yun Aoye, no longer caring about the dignity of the royal family, hurriedly nodded: "Okay, okay, I'll apologize, I'll apologize!!"

Ye Xunwang felt somewhat relieved, as although Aoye was usually carefree, he could still discern what was important. He then said, "Don't rush, that gentleman has already been disturbed by us today, it's not appropriate to go again. Tomorrow, tomorrow we'll go."

"Okay... okay, I'll listen to Uncle Ye." Yun Aoye said hastily.

"Mm." Ye Xunwang nodded, then lowered his head to look at the broken strings on the ancient zither, his eyes filled with longing.
