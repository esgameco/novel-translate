"Grandma..." Little Tong's eyes were red as she gently held the old woman's frail hand.

Lu Changsheng was still there, just as he had been on the day the butler died, quietly watching from the side.

"Little Tong, you were born with a hard life. You were still young when your parents passed away, and your grandfather also passed away when you were three. You were selling fruit with Grandma at such a young age, those years were hard on you."

The old woman had a benevolent smile on her face, but her expression was somewhat ashamed.

Little Tong frantically shook her head, sobbing, "It wasn't hard at all, not even a bit! Grandma gave me all her love!"

The old woman proudly stroked Little Tong's cheek, "Hehe, my little Tong is successful. Although the days were tough, you still managed to become a government official and let Grandma enjoy a few years of comfort."
