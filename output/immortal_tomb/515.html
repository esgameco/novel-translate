Seeing this, the onlookers' hearts suddenly raced, thinking, "Could this blue-robed man be our Dao Master?"

Those who had seen Lu Changsheng but had not yet perished, such as Lei Yu, Lei Ting, Chu Lingian, Jian Wuhui, and many others, were all plunged into profound shock, their minds in a daze!

Dao Master!!

It's actually the Dao Master!

Recalling the past, they had once sat and drank with the Dao Master, even... everything seemed so unreal, so dreamlike.

But soon, they became deeply worried - there was no room for them to think of anything else at the moment!

They were worried about Lu Changsheng. Although Lu Changsheng had never been defeated in their eyes, the enemy he now faced was clearly different!

In the Carefree Sect.

The Sect Master... was actually the Dao Master.

No wonder the Sect Master had never been defeated, no matter how powerful the enemy was, he would always be stronger.

No wonder the Sect Master was so carefree and unrestrained - it was because he was truly like that.

No wonder they had been left here for so long - it was not that the Sect Master had forgotten them, but that he was protecting them.

Tong Xin's eyes were red-rimmed, his heart trembling, "Master, please don't let anything happen to you."

At the Eighth Creation Pillar.

The countless Devouring Void Sacred Demons sneered at the blue-robed man before them, looking at him as if he were a mere plaything.

Each member of the Devouring Void Sacred Demon Clan was fierce and powerful in battle!

This time, the Eight Sacred Demon Gods, the hundreds of Sacred Demon Emperors, and the thousands of Sacred Demon Lords had all gathered. How could this man be defeated?

Even if he was the Dao Master, so what?

"Haha, the Dao Master of the Boundless Void? Daring to come alone, how brave!"

Seviel unfurled his seven pairs of great wings, mocking the blue-robed man.

The other seven Sacred Demon Gods also looked at the blue-robed man with amusement, while harboring intense killing intent!

No matter what, this man was truly obstructing them!

Returning between the Creation Pillars would waste too much time!

They only wanted to quickly absorb all the energy from the Creation Pillars and leave this place!

Lu Changsheng's expression remained calm as he silently sent Old Turtle and the others out from the Great Dao Demon-Subduing Tower.

"Master!"

"Dao Master!"

As soon as Old Turtle and Hunhun came out, they saw the countless Devouring Void Sacred Demons and their pupils immediately shrank, stunned - so many?!

Lu Changsheng said indifferently, "I originally planned to defeat them one by one, but the situation has changed. When the time comes, don't stray too far from me, or I may not be able to take care of you."

Originally, he had planned to leave Old Turtle and the others in the Great Dao Demon-Subduing Tower, but he needed to use the tower.

"Yes!"

Old Turtle and Hunhun nodded solemnly in response.

As for Reincarnation Magic Child, his eyes were bloodthirsty - so many Devouring Void Sacred Demons, he was so excited, the blood of hatred surging wildly!

He knew that the Sacred Demon Lords and Sacred Demon Emperors among them were extremely powerful, but if he could kill even one, it would be worth it, and if he could kill two, it would be a huge gain!

As for the countless Sacred Demon soldiers, he didn't even consider them a threat at all!

"Xiao Tong, you are the weakest, you don't need to do anything, just stand behind me."

Lu Changsheng tightened his grip on the long sword, speaking indifferently.

"Yes." He Xitong responded respectfully, her gaze bitterly resigned. She knew that her Eternal Calamity Body might be useful to the Master, but the feeling of being so powerless was very unpleasant.

Old Turtle and Hunhun stood in a line beside Lu Changsheng, their gazes gradually turning fierce!

The eight Sacred Demon Gods paused, then looked at each other and burst into laughter!

"Hahaha hahaha hahaha!!"

Suddenly, Seviel roared, "Kill!!"
