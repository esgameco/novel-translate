Here is the full English translation of the excerpt from the Chinese novel:

Peng Fang retracted his gaze and lowered his head to rub his plump belly, marveling inwardly that the innkeeper was indeed an extraordinary person, completely unfathomable in his intentions.

After surveying the interior of the inn for a while, Zuo Qizhan's gaze couldn't help but linger on Lu Changsheng's face, her eyes flickering, lost in thought.

And at that moment, she was startled to see that the young man in blue had somehow opened his eyes and was gazing at her pensively...

Those calm eyes were like a deep abyss, captivating her.

Zuo Qizhan's face stiffened, and she hastily shifted her gaze elsewhere, her heart pounding.

Lu Changsheng remained expressionless, his eyes closing again.

"Esteemed guests, the drinks are here! This is the 'Soul-Cleansing Brew', and since it's our opening day, you can have a free cup. But it won't be free after today."

At this, Tong Xin came running out, carrying a wine jug and smiling cheerfully.

Although the guests didn't outwardly show it, they were inwardly quite disdainful. Just a cup of ordinary wine, and they were making such a fuss about it. "Soul-Cleansing Brew"? What a strange name, they'd never even heard of it!

However, when Tong Xin removed the jug's lid, the expressions on the guests' faces changed dramatically!

An indescribable aroma of wine instantly permeated the entire inn!

Peng Fang also unconsciously swallowed, his expression shaken. When had he ever encountered such a fragrant wine? If he were to have a single cup of this, he would gladly spend his entire fortune...

Including Zuo Qizhan, the guests all licked their lips, their throats working.

It was rare for cultivators to not enjoy drinking wine.

Next, Tong Xin proceeded to pour a cup for each of them, not too much, not too little, and the measurements were all the same.

The guests stared at the crystalline liquid in their cups, dumbfounded. Could they really be allowed to drink such an exquisite vintage for free?

They were somewhat skeptical of their good fortune.

Seeing this, Tong Xin inwardly gloated. This fine wine was the master's recipe, but he and Lin Dong had brewed it themselves. After tasting it, they had been utterly astounded - how could there be such a delicious wine in the world! The master truly lived up to his reputation, and they knew that with just this one cup, he could make countless wine enthusiasts bow in reverence.

"M-Master, we... we can really drink this?" Peng Fang trembled as he held up the wine cup, glancing uncertainly at the reclining Lu Changsheng.

The others were the same, looking at Lu Changsheng with a dreamlike expression. If the innkeeper were to suddenly deny them the drink now, they felt they would die of craving.

Lu Changsheng, eyes closed, smiled and said, "Of course you may."

Hearing this, the guests became exhilarated, placing the cups under their noses to savor the aroma, then carefully taking a sip.

The wine lingered in their mouths, madly stimulating their taste buds, and the ultimate fragrance filled their nostrils.

Before their eyes, it was no longer an inn, but a place filled with birdsong and floral scents, and the mysterious air seemed to cleanse some of the sins from their bodies, leaving them feeling refreshed and at ease.

"'Soul-Cleansing Brew'... the name is quite apt, this is... such a wonderful wine!" Peng Fang came to his senses, gazing at the wine in his hand with an enraptured expression.

Zuo Qizhan also praised it profusely, her face flushed with two spots of color.

At this moment, the guests were reluctant to finish their drinks, their eyes gleaming with the desire to preserve the remnants of this exquisite vintage.

Seeing this, Tong Xin apologized, "Esteemed guests, please finish your wine. It's our inn's rule that the wine cannot be taken out of the premises."

The guests inwardly bristled, but when their gaze fell upon the reclining young man in blue, they immediately sobered up.
