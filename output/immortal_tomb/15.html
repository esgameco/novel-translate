Too many, too many deaths. No city had a death toll below three hundred...

"The heavens truly want to destroy my Lishui Empire..."

"Moyu Town."

Moyu Town...

The main hall fell silent, and everyone frowned tightly.

"I understand!!"

Suddenly, an elderly official exclaimed excitedly, interrupting everyone's contemplation.

"Oh? Elder Dong, what are your thoughts? Please share," the King of the Water Nation asked, looking at the elderly official.

Aside from this demonic calamity, this elderly official had indeed understood a great deal in the past, and his thoughts were easy to grasp, able to come up with angles that ordinary people couldn't.

The other officials were also surprised to look at Dong Ge.

"Of course, I know what you are all worried about, we will go to the Moyu Town area first to take a look, and if it is indeed safe, then we will also have the people from the south, west, and north move there if demons start to appear there as well."

The King of the Water Nation said hoarsely, this was the only option left...

As for the immortal from the Qingyun Sect, time did not allow him to wait any longer...
