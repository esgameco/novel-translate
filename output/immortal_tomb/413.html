The City of Heavenly Fragrance.

On the shores of the secluded Pavilion Lake, a courtyard had appeared, no one knew when. In the bustling City of Heavenly Fragrance, hardly anyone had noticed it.

Even if they did notice, they would not be surprised, thinking it was just a wealthy family who admired the Immortal Musician buying a plot of land by the Pavilion Lake.

To wait for the next visit of the Immortal Musician and occupy the prime location to listen.

And at this moment, this courtyard was quite lively.

"Tong Xin, that area is a bit empty, go plant some flowers and plants there."

"Xiao Dong, for the pond over there, put in some rockeries and small fish fry."

"Da Dao, tidy up the backyard."

"Helin, and you few elders, are you just going to rely on your old age? Don't just stand idle, go work on the courtyard as well."

"......"

The old turtle walked with his hands behind his back, occasionally reaching out to point and gesture.

Indeed, Tong Xin and the others were all released by Lu Changsheng from the Great Dao Demon Pagoda.

After going through such a long period of cultivation in the Great Dao Demon Pagoda, their auras had all become more restrained, and no one knew what realm they had reached.

At first, when they were released by Lu Changsheng, they were completely dazed.

The saying "cultivation knows no time" was true, even they did not know how long it had been, a few years, decades, hundreds of years, or even thousands of years?

Until the old turtle told them that the outside world had already passed tens of thousands of years, and they were no longer on the Deity-Demon Battlefield, except for the elder Helin and Chen Dadao who had entered later, the rest were all stunned.

Terrifying indeed!

After a moment, the group had almost finished tidying up the entire courtyard and sat down together in the courtyard.

Except for the old turtle's mocking expression, Tong Xin and the others were dazed, still immersed in the terrifying passage of time, finding it difficult to recover.

"Tens of thousands of years, it really passed in the blink of an eye... I finally understand the ruthlessness of time," Peng Fang sighed as he patted his plump belly.

The others also nodded in agreement, as for the flow of time, they had not noticed it at all, and a hint of fear for the future appeared in their eyes.

In other words, even if they were diligent and cultivated tirelessly, they could never catch up with time, and would eventually be extinguished in the river of time, it was only a matter of time.

All things must perish, and no one can exist forever.

The old turtle sneered, "It's true that the outside world has passed tens of thousands of years, but the time you spent in the time-distorted Great Dao Demon Pagoda was probably far more than that."

Hearing this, the group was shaken again and could only smile bitterly.

Tong Xin scratched his head and suddenly asked, "By the way, Senior Turtle, what about the Deity-Demon Battlefield?"

The others also looked at the old turtle, but Helin and Chen Dadao were not very clear about the Deity-Demon Battlefield.

The old turtle waved his hand, "The Human Clan has risen to the top of the Myriad Clan Ranking, as for the former number one Deity Clan, it has already been destroyed by the Master in a flick of his hand, not worth mentioning."

The old turtle only briefly explained, not wanting to go into too many other details.

Hiss!

The group couldn't help but take a deep breath, at least in their impression, the Deity Clan was a massive force with countless powerful experts, and it was actually destroyed by the Patriarch just like that?

Helin and Chen Dadao silently exchanged a glance, their eyes filled with shock. They may not understand much, but they could still grasp that the Patriarch was as invincible as ever, no matter where he was.

Creak~

At this moment, a young man in blue robes pushed open the door and entered.

The group looked over, their hearts suddenly racing, and they quickly stood up and saluted, "Patriarch!"

Lu Changsheng came to the main seat and sat down slowly, smiling faintly, "Please, have a seat."

"Yes..."

The group sat down rather stiffly.

Although the one before them was the Patriarch they most revered and worshipped, their faith, which made them feel somewhat restrained.

Lu Changsheng looked at Su Qianghe and asked, "Third Elder, how long has it been since you last had a drink?"

Su Qianghe was stunned for a moment, then his expression suddenly turned ecstatic, and he instinctively stood up.
