She knew that if she was taken away by Yuanyang Qiu, she would face a fate worse than death!

But there were two reasons that firmly restrained him:

One, he had promised the senior to personally bring Su Lianyi before him. If Yunran interfered, it would be difficult to accomplish.

Two, the senior was clear-sighted, and Yuanyang Qiu had experienced it firsthand. If he deliberately aggravated the conflict between the senior and the Upper Clarity Palace, it would be disastrous.

A few days later,

Three figures appeared outside the Smoke and Rain Town.

They were Yuan Yang Qiu, Yun Mi, and the tightly bound Su Lian Yi.

At this time, Su Lian Yi's face was deathly pale, her spirit depressed and filled with fear.

During the journey to Smoke and Rain Town, she had been tormented in body and mind, tormented by the sense of impending doom.

She thought back to when she came to the clinic before, and how the azure-robed cultivator had been turned into a human stick...

Then what about her?

She was afraid of being chopped into countless pieces of meat.

The more Su Lian Yi thought about it, the more terrified she became, her very soul trembling.

Yun Mi's expression was one of doubt and vigilance as she surveyed the secluded little town before her. She found it hard to imagine that there was a powerful figure here who could withstand even one move from Yuan Yang Qiu.

"Hehe, Sect Master Yun, do you see that over there? That is the clinic where the senior resides," Yuan Yang Qiu gestured towards the southwest. "When we're in front of the senior, you'd better be careful and attentive. Let's go!"

With that, he grabbed the trembling Su Lian Yi and disappeared in a flash.

Yun Mi took a deep breath and followed closely behind.
