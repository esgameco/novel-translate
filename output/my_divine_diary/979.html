Chapter 979

On top of a barren mountain, Cong Renfei's brows were furrowed as he diligently attempted the Wudaoist's cultivation of the heart, trying to step through the door of the Wudaoist's cultivation.

However, no matter how hard he tried, he just couldn't get anywhere.

On this day, he felt the Yuan Qi permeating around him and secretly said, "Could it be that outsiders really don't have the means to perform martial arts cultivation?"

After thinking about it, he shook his head and said, "It won't be, I feel that if the martial artists of this world are able to use a power like Yuan Qi, then I must be able to do it as well, and there must be a way to skip the step of 'mastering oneself' and instead utilize the Heaven and Earth Yuan Qi directly."

After cultivating according to the martial Taoist's cultivation method for a period of time, Cong Renfei was already able to perceive the existence of Yuan Qi without using his divine mind, but he could only perceive it, but he was unable to mobilize the Yuan Qi of heaven and earth into his body.

According to the words of Heavenly King on the Cloud: a wastrel without roots cannot communicate with nature, just as a cup without a bottom cannot hold water.

Cong Renfei suddenly thought of Master Wei's operation a few months ago, and couldn't help but think, "Master Wei should be studying Yuan Qi as well, if it's Master Wei's words, what method would he use?"

He would never have imagined that Su Hao was directly stuffing a ball of highly concentrated natural yuan qi into his chest and forcibly blocking it to allow it to naturally fuse.

Even if he thought of it, he wouldn't dare to do so.

After all, although he possessed a soul brain, his body was just an ordinary 'spirit body', and there was no way to recover quickly with 'flesh and blood augmentation' like Su Hao's [God] if he played with it.

After thinking about it, Cong Renfei got up and walked towards the humble house, and after seeing the Heavenly King Yunshang who was sitting aside watching a small movie, he said, "Old man Yunshang, my cultivation is still not working, or else how about you give me a little bit of yuan qi to be used as my start-up capital?"

Although Heavenly King Yun Shang's eyes were glued to the screen, watching the male and female protagonists inside perform vigorously, his mind was thinking of other things.

He couldn't forget about the instantaneous fading of Heaven and Earth Yuan Qi and the big explosion that happened a few months ago.

He thought of many possibilities, but couldn't figure out what had happened at all.

When he went to the location of the explosion, there was nothing else but an immense and incomparable explosion crater.

Now when he heard Cong Renfei's question, his eyes finally moved away from the screen, "That won't work, the yuan qi is mine, no one else can control it, the moment it enters your body, it can blow you up into a pile of broken meat, you want to take the risk, but I don't want to, no way."

He is still inseparable from Cong Renfei, because after the computer is dead, he needs Cong Renfei to help him change the battery ......

Cong Renfei is also very admiring this little old man, he clearly taught this old man how to fast forward, but this old man watching the movie is hard to see the end from the beginning.

The Heavenly King on the Clouds was silent for a moment, then ventured out, "Get lost!"

After saying that, he turned his head to focus on watching a small movie.

Cong Renfei was stunned, "You ...... don't believe me?"

The Heavenly King on the Clouds spewed out a puff of air from his nose and didn't say anything.

Cong Renfei waved his hand and sighed, "You have no chance with the True God."

After saying this, he went out on his own, closed his eyes and opened the mall, wandering around.

Some time ago, he got a large amount of points, he hadn't thought about what to buy, and he liked to browse the mall when he had free time.

Shopping, he suddenly found two new products in the mall, one named 'Innate Yuan Qi', one named 'Heaven and Earth Yuan Qi', he instantly froze.

"What? Innate Yuan Qi is sold in the mall? When did this thing come into existence?...... It couldn't be that Master Wei put it up for sale after he researched it thoroughly!"

He quickly opened the item description and read it carefully, quickly confirming that the Yuan Qi spoken of here was the martial dao Yuan Qi he wanted to learn.

Cong Renfei's head was filled with black lines, "If I knew that the mall sells it, why do I still need to tempt this old man to teach me with a small porno? Just buy it directly and it wouldn't be over."

Looking at the price tag again, two thousand points of credit, nothing to hesitate, change!

Soon his hand more a ball of light white light, as soon as it appeared it automatically sank into his palm, along the palm to the heart position, and fused with the heart.

Cong Renfei's divine mind observed and found that this ball of innate energy was able to integrate into the bloodstream and transported to every part of his body through the blood vessels.

"Doesn't this make it Innate Yuan Qi? I can carry out martial artist cultivation now, hehehe!"

When Cong Renfei opened his eyes, it was slightly blurred in front of him, and the figure of the Heavenly King on the Cloud suddenly appeared in front of him, staring blankly at him.

Cong Renfei heatedly smiled.

Heavenly King on the Clouds couldn't help but ask, "Kid, what's going on with the Yuan Qi inside your body?"

Cong Renfei shrugged his shoulders and said, "There's nothing to tell, because you wouldn't believe me even if I told you."
