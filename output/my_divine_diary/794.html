Chapter 794

Here is my translation of the Chinese excerpt to English:

Time passes slowly.

The boundless universe seems unchanged, still dotted with stars as before.

But in the subtleties, drastic changes occur incessantly.

Countless civilizations rise and fall, countless brilliances of wisdom burst forth, even illuminating a patch of the universe.

But ultimately, they inevitably extinguish, and then eternal darkness.

Almost all intelligent life explores one question: What kind of life can be eternal?

Perhaps in the depths of the universe, there is already an answer.

In the haze, Suhao's consciousness gradually revived.

He first perceived his own body, a warm current surging from all over into his brain, making his whole body comfortably warm.

The power of his body was also under his control.

Before long, Suhao gained enlightenment: "I have successfully reincarnated again, gradually mastering this fleshly body. Indeed, it did not disappoint me - I, Suhao, have successfully reincarnated again. This is luck, but also inevitability.

I can sense that this body is extremely powerful! Now, let me patiently wait a moment, until I fully adapt, until my mental power fully returns."

During this period just after reincarnating, Suhao was at his most vulnerable, where a slight misstep could plunge his consciousness into slumber.

"I must persevere."

Suhao remained still and quiet, maintaining his original posture until his consciousness fully awakened.

He sensed something different.

"My current bodily state...doesn't feel quite right! My current posture seems to be curled up, curled up in a narrow space, restraining my whole body. Why is this?"

"Could it be...that I'm a prisoner?"

He pondered, then cautiously opened his eyes a crack to survey the surroundings.

However, he could see nothing, only a dim yellowish glow faintly entering his eyes.

Suhao: "???"

He forced his eyes fully open, constantly adjusting focus and trying to turn his head to gather more information.

A spherical space? No, more like an ellipsoid...

After struggling for a while, he gained enlightenment.

"It seems...I'm inside an egg. I've reincarnated as some kind of oviparous animal...Indeed, my automatic consciousness-matching technique has taken effect!"

Although, Suhao was still quite surprised, never expecting to truly reincarnate as a non-human being.

As for what creature he reincarnated as, he just needed to enter the Bouncing Realm to see clearly.

After realizing he was in an eggshell, Suhao temporarily set his mind at ease. For him, this was good news - at least he didn't need to worry about any imminent life-threatening danger.

If there was any danger, the mother would surely guard over him, right?

He tested the current fleshly body's strength, thinking: "The body's power is extremely formidable. I could break out of the shell at any time and take out any would-be egg roasters."

Steady!
