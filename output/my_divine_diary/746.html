Chapter 746

Here is my translation of the excerpt from the Chinese novel:

Suhao did not know that after glaring away the mushroom girl, he had caused a mushroom person to lose its precious love.

He walked slowly up to the blue crystal, controlling the tail behind the mushroom head to rise up. He opened his eye petals, aiming at a protruding point on the crystal, like a baby latching onto a plastic nipple.

The eye petals enveloped the blue crystal head, with the dense neural tentacles tightly adhering to the blue crystal. In that instant, a huge stream of information violently rushed in, causing the mushroom person controlled by Suhao to shudder involuntarily.

In a daze, Suhao entered a grayish-white world, unable to make out what was near or far.

After a moment, his perceptual vision flickered with dense white dots, like an old TV with static.

Then the world scene began to gain color, and the white dots began to twist and gradually change and flow.

One by one, the colors pieced together, inexplicably forming a scroll painting.

The painting was blurred at first, but gradually became clear...

Then Suhao found himself as if in a sky full of balloons. These balloons were colorful, varied in size, gorgeous with intricate and beautiful patterns, emitting a faint glow around them...

Suhao looked at the scene before him in a daze, uncertain: "Are these...planets?"

At this moment, he was like a sprite floating in mid-air, able to freely pass through these spheres. With just a thought, he could pass through the not-so-large gaps between these spheres and arrive at any place he saw.

Suhao stared at a distant yellow sphere, and with a thought, his vision blurred slightly before he arrived in front of this sphere.

He murmured in amazement: "So this is the 'Star Viewing World'? It's too incredible! And these dense spheres must be planets!

It's as if...these planets are servers, interconnected to build a vast network world, and I'm now inside this network world.

Against the backdrop of the starry sky, though separated by vast distances, they are extremely close in this network world."

The planets interconnected to build a vast network?

Only someone with holes in their brain would dare think that way!

But is it possible?

What Suhao saw indeed brought him a strong enough shock to give him a whole new perspective on this universe.

Or rather, he saw another facet of this universe.

"From my view, the distances between galaxies in the universe are immense, but from another angle, the distances may not be so far.

I need more angles to observe the universe, like the 'spiritual eye'. Perhaps I can make the blue crystal into a spiritual eye, allowing me to enter this wondrous Star Viewing World anytime."

Suhao examined the yellowish-brown planet before him, about the size of a cruise ship, clearly seeing the shifting winds and clouds on its surface.

He couldn't help but try to reach out and touch this seemingly small planet, but forgot he had no hands at the moment...

With a thought, the yellowish-brown planet suddenly enlarged before his eyes. His vision blurred for an instant, and when it cleared again, he had entered the planet's atmosphere.

It was like clicking a magnifying glass button.

Suhao seemed to have mastered controlling his vision, rapidly roaming over this planet, crossing over mountains, through storms, into the underground, swimming through magma...

However, no matter what he did, the surrounding environment had no effect on him - he was merely an observer.

In other words, this planet seemed to be constructed from a set of data, synchronizing information corresponding to the actual planet in real-time.

Suhao only had viewing rights, not modification rights.

Suhao thought: "I see, so this is the Star Viewing World! Just by staying in the Star Viewing Divine Temple, I can freely roam the planets of the universe! This ability is too powerful!

So to prevent the Compound Eye Race from stealing their vision technology, humans adopted the 'deception' policy.

Of course, Suhao's shielded mini-world may be effective against the 'Star Viewing World', a point he could only verify later!

While marveling at the power of the 'Star Viewing World', Suhao couldn't help but mock the so-called Compound Eye Race.

With such an advantageous environment and innate conditions, they still ended up like this - it's too disgraceful!

Don't think about it, don't think about it!

But in their shoes, Suhao couldn't be certain he would definitely soar either. In any case, he still felt pity for the Compound Eye Race."

Suhao left this planet and passed through a few more, gradually adapting to moving in the Star Viewing World.

Finally, he turned towards the massive, softly glowing white giant - a star.

This was a star! Presented to Suhao in this way.

Suhao had been curious about stars for a long time but never had a chance to see them up close. Now with this opportunity, he naturally wouldn't let it pass.

With a thought, he instantly arrived before the star and, without hesitation, plunged right in.

Like diving into water, his vision transformed into a brilliant golden expanse.

This was probably the star's atmosphere, where Suhao knew terrifying high temperatures raged, but this heat had no effect on him whatsoever.

Continuing downwards...

Before long, Suhao found himself stuck, unable to approach the star's interior no matter what.

"What's going on? Unable to withstand the star's raging energy and disconnected? It seems the Star Viewing World can't observe every celestial body...I was originally going to take a look inside the black hole at the center of the nebula, but that was presumptuous."

He idled around the star's surface, carefully observing the conditions, pondering whether tossing his 'Star Shard Cube' onto the star was feasible...

The answer was cruel: the Star Shard Cube would likely vaporize before even landing.

Stars were not something humans could challenge.

...

After who knows how long, Suhao controlling the mushroom person released the eye petals gripping the blue crystal, gradually waking from the Star Viewing World.

He coiled the 'eye petal' tail in front of his eyes, watching the eye petals slowly close, unable to hold back a light laugh: "Dream Stone, Eye Petals, Star Viewing World...it's all too fascinating!"

Then he disengaged control of this body and returned to the Bouncing Realm space.

With a wave of his hand, a huge blue crystal network structure appeared in the Bouncing Realm space, emitting a faint blue glow.

This was the blue crystal structure he personally recorded across the entire Divine Planet with his divine consciousness, with little deviation overall.

The more Suhao looked, the more satisfied he became: "What secrets are hidden in this blue crystal? I can't wait to uncover them!"

Thanks to the powerful Eight-Armed Race forcing the entire Nebula System to interconnect, this deeply buried Compound Eye Race was flushed out.

Otherwise, even if Suhao was reborn into the Nebula System, he may never have found this wondrous planet!

For Suhao, just obtaining this massive blue crystal alone made his rebirth after waiting hundreds of millions of years worthwhile!

Recalling his previous life ending at just two or three years old, Suhao slowly felt regretful: "Back then I experienced so many worlds, but how many good things did I miss..."

Just thinking about it made his heart ache and bleed.

...

After Suhao released control of the mushroom person named Changtong, it woke up dazed with a head full of questions: "Huh? When did I fall asleep? Why did I end up next to the Dream Stone?"

Then it looked around and quickly found that beautiful figure.

That was its mushroom person partner - they had a very good relationship...

"Crack!"

In the next moment, the mushroom person Changtong petrified, then shattered into pieces falling to the ground.

What did it see? Its beloved sweetheart, actually kissing another male mushroom person...
