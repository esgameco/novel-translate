Chapter 699

Here is my translation of the Chinese excerpt into English:

Three years later. The small world of the dormitory.

Su Hao walked through a spacious factory, his eyes scanning left and right, observing the motionless [Eight-Handed Gods] and [Insect Gods] standing in the small partitions on both sides.

The vast majority were [Eight-Handed Gods], still looking the same as before.

There were only five [Insect Gods], and their appearance looked rather special: overall similar to the other [Gods], they were all wrapped tightly in pink crystal shells covering their bodies. Their bodies resembled a scorpion covered in hooks, with a dinosaur-like head, ferocious fangs, six segmented limbs, and two huge sickle-shaped pincers extending from each side, making one shudder just looking at them. Two pairs of transparent, razor-sharp wings grew from their backs...

If the [Eight-Handed Gods] looked 'weirdly cruel', then the [Insect Gods] looked 'ferocious'!

However, the [Insect Gods]' nervous systems were much weaker compared to the [Eight-Handed Tribe], less smooth to control, and not very practical, but they made good experimental subjects.

These two rows of densely packed [Gods], a total of one hundred, were one of Su Hao's achievements over the past three years.

The reason there were only one hundred was not because Su Hao could not make more, but because he had exhausted the experimental subjects of the Eight-Handed Tribe and no longer had the opportunity to capture more.

However, there was no need to dwell too much on this, as for him, these one hundred [Gods] might not even be usable.

Not only had the number of [Gods] increased, but their quality had also been greatly optimized.

All the [Gods] in the two rows had mastered core abilities such as 'Deity Realm Cultivator', 'Core Perception Compound Runes', 'Spatial Abilities', and 'Source Mage'.

As long as Su Hao activated the 'Full Immersion Control System' in the small black room, he could immediately take complete control of any one of the [Gods].

The [Eight-Handed God] he was currently using was even more powerful. After three years of training and adaptation, it could now use advanced spatial skills such as 'Second-Level Space' and 'Small World', further enhancing its survival abilities by another level.

It could be said that it was only slightly weaker than his main body, with little difference overall.

Su Hao turned around, very satisfied with his masterpieces. These [Gods] were the source of his confidence.

He walked out of the factory and entered another area to observe the current state of the Mother Bug.

The area where the Star-Eating Bugs resided was isolated by Su Hao because the Mother Bug was too fertile, and the Star-Eating Bugs could reproduce too quickly.

In just three short years, they had eaten away nearly half of the soil in his small world.

Who could have imagined that ordinary soil and rocks would transform into a group of living bugs, all because of a basketball-sized bug egg?

Now, in the isolated small space, there was no soil or other materials left, only densely packed bugs.

Su Hao didn't even have the desire to count how many there were.

After the Mother Bug realized there were no more resources left in this area to continue reproducing, it began producing large hot air balloons, spraying bug eggs upwards.

Without exception, the sprayed bug eggs could not break through the isolation of the small world and fell back down from above...

The Mother Bug was still the same, hiding amidst the surrounding bugs, quietly producing. However, now lacking materials, its production rate had dropped to freezing, only one egg per day.

However, what Su Hao was concerned about was not how the Mother Bug produced, but how it simultaneously controlled the vast swarm of bugs.

During this period of observation, Su Hao made a major discovery: as the number of bugs increased, the Mother Bug's intelligence gradually rose from initially acting based on instinct to now having a certain level of intelligence, comparable to that of a three or four-year-old child.

And the secret Su Hao wanted to find lay in the increase of the Mother Bug's intelligence.

How did the Mother Bug's intelligence increase?

Their way of becoming smarter was very special: the neural network stacking method!

Simply put, the Mother Bug did not cultivate the bug eggs one by one, but in pairs.

Inside the two bug eggs being cultivated simultaneously, there was a small area that was exactly the same, including the motion of particles, all synchronized.

As time passed, the synchronized part gradually evolved into a nervous system.

After the bug eggs matured, one of them was expelled from the body, while the other remained in the mother's body. After developing to a certain stage, the other parts were invaded and digested, leaving only the synchronized nervous system, which was then transferred to the Mother Bug's brain and integrated with it...

This was the way the Mother Bug controlled the bugs, and also the reason why the Mother Bug became smarter.

In theory, as long as the Mother Bug kept producing new bugs, its brain would continue to grow, and its intelligence would continue to increase without limit...

This method of becoming smarter by stacking neural networks through reproduction was something Su Hao dared not even imagine!

The Star-Eating Bug race truly deserved to rule an entire star domain through their own strength.

For this, Su Hao gave a sincere praise: "Awesome!"

As for the principle behind the complete synchronization of the two neural networks...

Su Hao thought of a term - quantum entanglement.

He thought to himself: "Perhaps it's related to this theory I studied before, but I don't know exactly how it's implemented. The Star-Eating Bugs have great research value inside and out!"

Just as Su Hao was thinking of feeding the Mother Bug some food to observe its egg-laying process, the broadcast from the Yuanxing Ship sounded.

"In two hours, the ship will shut down the warp engine. All personnel must immediately enter the adaptation cabin and prepare."

He instinctively looked up: "It seems we've arrived at the human homeworld, Blue Light."

...

The moment the broadcast sounded, the crew on the entire spaceship cheered excitedly, their faces filled with barely contained excitement.

The agonizing three-year wait finally erupted at this moment, and some soldiers even shed tears of joy, unable to hold back.

Because everyone knew they had successfully returned home!

After twenty years, what would things be like now?

However, upon returning, only eight hundred people remained. Six hundred soldiers had closed their eyes forever in the unknown depths of space...

Not to mention how excited and sad the crew was, the ship's speed gradually slowed down, appearing in a chaotic asteroid belt, with no sign of Blue Light. The Yuanxing Ship was just outside the asteroid belt.

The Yuanxing Ship sent an arrival signal, then skillfully passed through the asteroid belt, heading towards the empty space on the inside.

It was only then that the crew on the Yuanxing Ship slowly calmed down.

They suddenly remembered that shutting down the warp engine did not mean they had arrived at Blue Light, only that they were approaching.

To return to Blue Light, they would still need to travel for at least a month, going through countless checkpoints and undergoing numerous inspections. The time could easily be extended to over two months...

They got excited too early!

After the extreme excitement, the crew became dejected, looking utterly miserable, losing interest even in their daily training, their minds fully occupied with the hope that time would pass quickly.

As the crew had expected, every day there were human small spacecraft approaching to board the ship, carrying various instruments to inspect all aspects of the vessel.

Not only that, but all personnel on the Yuanxing Ship, including Su Hao, had to undergo blood tests and comprehensive medical examinations...

A blood test? If he handed over a tube of [Eight-Handed God] blood, who knows what they would analyze it as.

They might not even consider him a human anymore.

Su Hao's operation was simple. He drew some blood from his main body, modified it with a 'Gene Regression Liquid', and then let the awe-struck inspection officers draw it away.

He passed smoothly and was still considered a human.

After another two months, the Yuanxing Ship, escorted by many warships, finally arrived in front of a yellow-green planet and slowly entered its atmosphere.

This yellow-green planet was the human homeworld, 'Blue Light'!

For many years, the Eight-Handed Tribe had been searching for a planet that emitted a blue glow...

As the Yuanxing Ship entered the atmosphere, countless cameras focused on this ship that had been on patrol for twenty years.

Large and small screens across the planet all broadcast live videos of the Yuanxing Ship entering the atmosphere, with countless people staring intently at the all-black cruiser.

Over the years, this cruiser named the Yuanxing Ship had repeatedly become the hottest topic among humans.

Because from this warship, many battle videos had been transmitted back that made all humans' blood boil with excitement!

Unbelievable!

In everyone's eyes, this was no longer just a cruiser, but a warship.

On this epic warship was a superhero of humanity.

His name was Xu Shiqiang!
