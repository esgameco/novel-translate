Another five hundred years passed.

Their doppelgangers, having explored at least a few billion light years outward, could be said to be very far from the Cross Star, almost beyond imagination.

Only to see that the pictures showed a small ancient town, many humans wearing simple hemp clothes with long hair were moving to and from the town, a bustling scene.

At the same time, after observing, he quickly confirmed that the humans in this world were able to become 'travelers'.

"That's you, Traveler Hometown."
