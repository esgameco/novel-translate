Chapter 701

Here is my translation of the excerpt from the Chinese novel:

After returning, every member of the Yuanxing ship became a living hero.

From a broad perspective, the heroes belonged to all of humanity, receiving welcome from all humans.

But from another angle, the heroes belonged to their own families.

So, after the welcoming ceremony ended, the crew members walked out of the military port one by one, heading towards their waiting families.

The living crew members reunited passionately with their loved ones, overflowing with happiness.

For the deceased crew members, their remains and belongings were returned, their families outwardly proud of the heroes, with excited tears glistening, but inwardly gloomy and grieving.

Doing the same thing, surviving or perishing, made a complete difference.

However, they should be content.

If not for the Yuanxing ship's great achievements and meritorious service, even the deceased crew would have been ignored by all.

This was already the best possible outcome for the dead.

As Su Hao walked, scanning the crowd, from 'Xu Shiqiang's' memories he knew his parents were still alive on Languang and he had a younger sister, so he expected his family in this world would come to meet him.

His eyesight was excellent, but after scanning the crowd, he did not find any familiar faces from his memories.

As he wondered about this, a call came from the side.

"Brother!"

Su Hao turned towards the voice to see a middle-aged woman around 40 years old staring at him from outside the fence, her eyes brimming with tears, so excited she was about to cry.

Su Hao was stunned: "This is...my sister?"

It's no wonder he didn't recognize her immediately, because in his memories his sister was a 17 or 18 year old young girl who hadn't even graduated when he left Languang.

Now looking at her, she had become a middle-aged woman, completely different from the chattering, manic girl in his memories.

Su Hao's sister was named Xu Ruyun. Her appearance was still pretty in line with the previous Xu Shiqiang's.

Standing beside her was a tall, burly middle-aged man, perhaps her husband? On the other side was a 15 or 16 year old girl who resembled Xu Ruyun, probably her daughter.

These two were looking at Su Hao with excited, curious gazes, clearly the fact that 'superhero Xu Shiqiang is my great uncle' had a strong impact on them.

On the other side were two elderly white-haired people - Su Hao looked again and recognized them as his original body's parents.

The lively young girl had become a middle-aged woman, the black-haired parents had turned white-haired, all in just twenty years.

Su Hao walked over, and his sister and brother-in-law immediately came to greet him.

These relatives of Xu Shiqiang were so excited to reunite that in the end they couldn't say a word...

The sense of separation after twenty years made the atmosphere fall into an awkward silence.

Looking around, other crew members were hugging their families directly.

It was Su Hao who smiled lightly and said, "Let's go, no need to be polite!"

This reunion belonged to 'Xu Shiqiang' and his family. Su Hao himself didn't feel much.

His thought was, since he was occupying Xu Shiqiang's body, he didn't mind giving Xu Shiqiang and his family a good ending.

After reuniting with his family today, starting tomorrow, he would do his own thing.

After Su Hao spoke, the atmosphere became much more relaxed, although his words "no need to be polite" still sounded a bit strange to Xu Ruyun and the others.

The group got into a car and slowly drove into this huge human city - Victory City.

And the atmosphere gradually became more harmonious.

Xu Shiqiang's family was just an ordinary, not wealthy family who couldn't afford expensive enhancement drugs.

So in twenty years, Xu Shiqiang's parents and sister had slowly aged.

Only Xu Shiqiang, by joining the military, had the qualification to receive enhancement drug injections, not only making his body extraordinarily strong, but greatly extending his lifespan and slowing aging.

So although Xu Ruyun and the others had mentally prepared, when they saw Su Hao's overly youthful face, they still felt a sense of space-time dislocation.

Moreover, Xu Ruyun was surprised at how handsome her brother had become!

She knew her brother - his looks were just average, his tall stature made him look passable, but he would never be associated with the word "handsome."

But after twenty years of absence, her brother had become a man of top-tier appearance and demeanor.

Most shockingly, it was still Xu Shiqiang's face - she could recognize it was her brother at a glance.

The same face, but giving a completely different feeling?

And through Xu Ruyun's words, Su Hao also learned the general situation.

Everyone had been waiting for Xu Shiqiang's return. As they waited, she started a new family, had a daughter, and their parents slowly aged.

Then suddenly one day, news of him came back - overnight he became a human hero, and their family was given the title and treatment of a hero's family.

The Commonwealth rewarded them a large sum of money, arranged a standalone villa in Victory City, and most importantly, they received the qualification for enhancement drug injections.

Unfortunately, after age 30, injecting enhancement drugs would have an extremely high mortality rate, so in the end only Xu Ruyun's daughter Yonghau received the enhancement.

Not long ago, the human leader suddenly visited their home in person...scaring them terribly, as they thought Su Hao had unfortunately sacrificed himself.

In general, as long as one made corresponding contributions to humanity, the Commonwealth did not mind promoting them fully and giving matching treatment.

From Su Hao's perspective, the structure of this human society, under the pressure of the Eight-Armed Race, was relatively vibrant and in a stage of development.

The wealthy did have great privileges, even able to receive enhancement drugs and longer lifespans as a priority, but the path for ordinary people to rise was not closed - as long as they contributed to humanity, whether technological or military achievements, they could obtain whatever they wanted.

Of course, this path did not seem fair to many, since most people exhausted all their efforts but still could not make sufficient contributions to humanity.

But considering the overall social environment and human predicament, it was impossible to be fair to everyone, and this was already an extremely good approach.

The reunion dinner went relatively smoothly. The niece Yonghau seemed to have completely opened up, curiously asking this and that across the table, her eyes full of admiration.

The key point was, her great uncle was so handsome, all the fantasies of a young girl seemed to be realized in him.

"Great Uncle, did you really kill a lot of Eight-Armed monsters?"

"Great Uncle, are the Eight-Armed monsters as brutal, cruel and stupid as in the movies?"

"Great Uncle, the Eight-Armed ships are so powerful, how did you defeat them?"

"Great Uncle, on the ship, were you the strategist or the warrior? I heard you could take on a hundred alone, and I also heard with just one of your strategies, you captured five Eight-Armed ships!"

"Great Uncle you're amazing!"

"Great Uncle, you're my idol, and the idol of all my sisters too! They're so jealous of me! They said they want to meet you!"

"Great Uncle, let me tell you a secret, those sisters of mine said that if you don't mind, they're all willing to marry you together. Hee hee hee!"

"Hmph! I've also noticed a lot of boys looking at me weirdly..."

...

Once little Yonghau opened up, her mouth was like a machine gun, chattering non-stop.

It seemed in this short hour or two, she wanted to release all the excitement from years of "my great uncle is a superhero."

She remembered when she was very young, she was just a sensitive, self-conscious little girl. But she didn't know from when, her life changed - she was no longer someone others looked down on, she was reborn, as if becoming the happiest little princess in the world...

All of this was brought to her by her great uncle.

The way she looked at her great uncle, besides "admiration" and "reverence," there seemed to be... a human-shaped diamond and gold?

Su Hao just smiled lightly: "As long as you're all happy."

...

Su Hao had three small worlds - one was the previous "research small world" left in the dorm where all the "Gods" were placed; one was the former cage for the Eight-Armed Race prisoners, now used to raise Star-Devouring Worms; the last was used to store the five captured Eight-Armed warships.

That very night, Su Hao found a new location and placed the "research small world" thousands of meters underground in the rock layer beneath Victory City.

The "Worm Small World" was moved by Su Hao to a barren rocky planet he had visited before, in case something happened and the worms escaped, at least they wouldn't affect Languang.

As for the "Warship Small World," Su Hao did not move it, leaving it on the planet where he had originally attacked the supply station.

The next morning, Su Hao went alone to the military affairs office, submitted a request for transfer, and according to the staff's instructions, submitted a specific report.

Under the admiring gazes of everyone, he walked out of the military affairs office.

With his divine sense, he locked onto the captain's position and headed straight for the captain.

At this time, the captain was still hugging his young wife asleep. Su Hao didn't care about that.

Under Su Hao's control, his voice precisely entered the captain's ear.

The captain jerked awake with a start.

His wife complained: "Gong Liang, what are you doing, so startled!"
