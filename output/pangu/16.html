As Gu Daoyi woke up, the various Dao images reflecting the heavens began to dissipate.

Gu Daoyi glanced at them and immediately understood. He didn't care about the dissipation of the Dao images, as they were merely formed from the Dao rhyme, Dao meaning, Dao wonder, and so on that had dispersed while he was comprehending the Dao.

"Daoyi, you're finally awake." Huang Weiyang's voice rang in Gu Daoyi's ears, her tone full of joyful surprise.

"How long was I comprehending the Dao?"

"Half a quarter of an hour."

Gu Daoyi nodded slightly. As soon as he came out, he discovered that the time didn't match, which is why he asked.

In the Fuxi space, he comprehended the Dao for nine days without eating, drinking, or sleeping. Although he was an ordinary person, nourished by the Dao Qi, he didn't feel the slightest bit of fatigue or hunger. Now, he was still in high spirits and looked radiant.

The surrounding crowd was still there. If he had comprehended the Dao for nine days, these people should have dispersed. They couldn't have stayed here to watch the whole time, after all, they didn't know how long he would be comprehending the Dao.

"Daoyi, did you obtain the inheritance of Emperor Fuxi?" The stunningly beautiful girl in the pink dress asked curiously.

Gu Daoyi nodded gently.

A trace of shock appeared on Huang Weiyang's face. Although she had guessed it early on, hearing Gu Daoyi personally admit it still shocked her greatly.

She was born in the ancient era. Emperor Fuxi was also a supreme being of the imperial realm in the ancient era. He had attained the Dao tens of thousands of years earlier than her mother, the Nine Heavens Phoenix. She had heard many legends about Emperor Fuxi.

She never thought that in this era, she would meet a descendant of Emperor Fuxi, and this person was someone she was willing to follow.

How could Huang Weiyang not be shocked?!

Their conversation was not deliberately concealed. The countless descendants of the Gu family surrounding them all heard it and couldn't help but gasp.

"The Divine Son is so amazing!"

"Heaven-defying! The Divine Son's comprehension is simply heaven-defying!"

"Emperor Fuxi's inheritance has finally reappeared after several eras."

"Hahaha~ The Divine Son was born into our Gu family. This is the Dao blessing our Gu family."

"With the Divine Son here, our Gu family will surely achieve immortal glory."

After the shock, the numerous disciples of the Gu family discussed enthusiastically, their expressions full of excitement as they began to imagine the future.

The ancient Gu family had shaken the Primordial world for countless years and had never declined. Several supreme beings of the imperial realm had emerged from it, and its foundation was unfathomably deep.

It could be said that the Gu family was also the most top-notch among the ancient aristocratic families, and now there was still an Emperor guarding it.

Below that, there was the Sixth Patriarch Gu Yuanwu, a quasi-emperor with stunning talent and world-shaking aptitude. He had a good chance of attaining the Dao and becoming an Emperor.

Then there was the current head of the family, Gu Taichu, known as the White-Robed God King. With a white robe of unparalleled elegance, it was said that he was about to break through to the Great Sage realm. His potential was even greater than the Sixth Patriarch's, and his hope of attaining the Dao and becoming an Emperor was even higher.

And now, their Gu family's Divine Son had been born. His comprehension was heaven-defying. In the future, barring any accidents, he would certainly attain the Dao and become an Emperor, or even an immortal, overlooking the cycles of time and forging the Gu family's immortal glory to its pinnacle.

Amidst the discussions, Gu Daoyi had already risen and was walking out with Huang Weiyang, his expression calm.

He had comprehended the ancient Fuxi stele at the ancient Dao comprehension site. Next...
