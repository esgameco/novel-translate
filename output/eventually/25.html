At the dining table, the cat stood up on its two hind legs, crossing its front paws across its chest, like a dignified young lady cat.

Its face was filled with speechlessness.

She had even worried about it to the point of not bothering to pretend to be silent, but unexpectedly, the situation was completely the opposite of what she had imagined.

Xue Jing's appearance was too deceptive, refined to the point that anyone who saw him for the first time would think he was a scholarly, intellectual type of man, even though the cat had already seen that his muscles were quite well-trained, it subconsciously thought he was the one who would be bullied.

"Thank you for worrying about me so much, Little Wen," Xue Jing said lightly with a smile.

Afterwards, when he saw the cat standing upright with its arms crossed, he found it utterly adorable and couldn't help but reach out to pinch its cheeks.

This fight was worth it, and he even got an extra reward.

Without any intention, the cat, who was unwilling to open its mouth, ended up speaking up because it was concerned about the bloody scent of the gangsters' razor that clung to him.

While feeling touched, Xue Jing also couldn't help but find it amusing.

"...Don't call me Little Wen, you must respectfully address me as Grand Mistress!"

The cat, whose real name was Ning Fengwen, slapped Xue Jing's hand away, blushing and indignant.

In Xue Jing's eyes, the young girl's voice had no intimidation at all, and he nodded and said, "Okay, Grand Mistress Wen."

He then reached out his hand again, stroking the cat's head back and forth.

If this were a manga, Ning Fengwen's forehead would now be throbbing with veins. She tried to use her two white glove-like paws to stop Xue Jing's caress, but it was to no avail, and she said angrily, "Show some respect for your elders!"

When she was pretending to be a cat before, it was fine, she could deceive and comfort herself that she was just a cat, and being petted wasn't a big deal, and she even enjoyed it a bit.

But now that her identity was completely exposed, being "frivolous" like this again made Ning Fengwen feel a sense of shame as a human being, spreading from the tips of her toes to the top of her head.

Xue Jing noticed that she was indeed a bit anxious, so he withdrew his hand. He asked curiously, "Elders? Little Wen, how old are you?"

The cat was dissatisfied, "You must call me Grand Mistress. Anyway, I'm older than you."

As Ning Fengwen said this, her tone was a bit hesitant, but Xue Jing didn't notice it. He stroked his chin and said, "The Dragon Cult Grand Mistress... is that the one reported in the news?"

Earlier, he had heard that name in the news reports, apparently it was a "disguised cult organization of a criminal syndicate" that had been destroyed by the Kang De Security Company.

Huh? My cat is actually a mafia boss?

The cat hugged its chest proudly, letting out a haughty hum with its young girl's voice, "That's right, the very same Dragon Cult. Scared, aren't you?"

Finding it cute, Xue Jing couldn't resist again, reaching out both hands to cup the cat's head, left and right, and started kneading its cheeks, laughing, "Scared, scared, Grand Mistress."

The cat gritted its teeth, "You're not scared at all! Hurry up and let go!"

Xue Jing withdrew his hands and asked, "The news said the Dragon Cult has been destroyed by Kang De Security Company, so are you now all alone?"

The cat raised its paw, gesturing to the pot of chicken soup, indicating for Xue Jing to serve it a bowl, then said, "Well... you could say that for now."

Xue Jing picked up the ladle, scooped half a bowl of chicken soup, and then added a big chicken leg, placing it in front of the cat.

"Then what are your plans for the future?"

The cat tore off a bite of the tender, soft and glutinous chicken meat, chewed it, and a very anthropomorphized expression of enjoyment appeared on its face.
