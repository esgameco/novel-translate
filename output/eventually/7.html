This cat, did it not learn how normal cats meow? How can it meow like that, so strained?

Xue Jing was being straightforward, planning to have a good chat with the cat, find out what exactly it is, why it can speak, what the white gold ring on its tail is, how to find something similar, and why this anomalous talking cat ended up in the stray cat world, nearly getting crushed.

However, the cat in front of him had an innocent expression, as if saying "I don't understand what you're talking about at all", just like an ordinary cat.

"Meow~~"

"Don't pretend, I heard everything, speak properly."

"Meow?"

"Just say it, how many cat treats do you want, just say it."

"Meow~"

The cat licked its paws, indifferent.

Xue Jing raised an eyebrow: "You're still pretending?"

He placed the cat on his lap, with one hand pressing on the back of its neck, and the other reaching towards the base of its tail.

This is one of the areas with the densest nerves on a cat's body, and most cats really enjoy being stroked there, involuntarily arching their backs.

Xue Jing's nimble fingers kept scratching and clawing at the fur around the tail base, and the little black cat's eyes immediately widened.

"Meow!!!"

It let out a cry that was almost agonizing, its hindquarters involuntarily raised high, its limbs starting to struggle, but it was firmly held in place by Xue Jing's grip on its neck, unable to move, only meowing incessantly.

After a while, Xue Jing stopped his movements, and the cat had already given up struggling, lying on Xue Jing's lap, completely limp, sticking out its tongue, eyes rolled back, panting, looking like it had been thoroughly played with.

But even after suffering such torment, it still didn't say a single word from start to finish, maintaining the appearance of just being an ordinary little cat.

It made Xue Jing a little self-conscious.

Could it be that the "Godly Absorption" skill on his character panel had drained away its ability to speak human language? Has it truly become an ordinary cat? If it weren't for that strained meow when it woke up, Xue Jing would have believed it.

But Xue Jing really didn't know what to do with it.

As long as the cat didn't intend to have a frank and open exchange with him, and just kept pretending, he couldn't really resort to cruel torture to force it to speak.

He had just received the cat's favor, his moral bottom line, no matter how flexible, wouldn't allow him to do such a thing.

Shaking his head, he spoke: "Alright then, let's leave it at that for now, are you hungry?"

The cat let out a weak "meow".

Xue Jing took it as a sign that it was hungry.

He placed the cat on the sofa, then stood up and went to the kitchen to get a large bowl, came to the table, opened the supermarket bag, took out the bag of cat food, tore it open from the mouth, and poured it into the bowl.

Placing the bowl filled with cat food in front of the cat, Xue Jing had expected the cat that had probably suffered a lot in the stray cat world to immediately start eating enthusiastically.

Unexpectedly, the cat just glanced at the cat food in front of it, clicked its tongue, and unhesitatingly turned its head away, not even looking at it again.

"Huh?"

Seeing this scene, Xue Jing suddenly had a thought.

This reaction... doesn't seem quite right.

This is not the reaction of a picky cat disliking the taste of the food, but more like... a reaction of the food in front of it not even being within its dietary considerations.

Xue Jing's mind flashed with insight, suddenly realizing.

He might have misunderstood from the very beginning.

His mind recalled the words the cat had spoken earlier.
