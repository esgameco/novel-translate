"Ah!!"

A few minutes later, everyone was panting heavily as they stopped, staring at the distant figure of the student in school uniform.

They really couldn't catch up! And the moment they stopped, Xue Jing also stopped and slowly turned around.

A sense of foreboding arose in the hearts of the group.

Their premonition soon came true.

In the streets of South City, there was no shortage of all kinds of trash.

Xue Jing picked up a 500ml glass beer bottle from the ground.

Seeing this, the hoodlums immediately panicked.

"Fuck, find cover quickly!!"

"Make way, let me hide here!"

"Scatter, scatter quickly!"

In the chaos, the glass beer bottle flew with a whistling sound, precisely hitting the head of a tall, thin youth who failed to dodge in time.

"Bang!"

"Ah!!"

The tall, thin youth covered his head, blood constantly flowing out from between his fingers. "He's out of stuff, hurry up and get him!"

He seemed to be a small leader, and seeing his order, the people around him quickly emerged from their hiding places.

But by this time, Xue Jing had already run far away.

The group looked at each other, unsure whether they should chase him.

"What are you standing there for, chase him!" the tall, thin youth shouted angrily.

Seeing this, the group had no choice but to chase after Xue Jing again.

After chasing for three or four times, with full-force running, most of the group's stamina was running low, and this time they didn't chase for long before most of them started to slow down.

And as soon as they slowed down, Xue Jing immediately noticed and stopped in his tracks.

"This..."
