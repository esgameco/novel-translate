Qingcheng High School is the affiliated high school of Qingcheng University.

Unlike Xue Jing's previous life, this planet called Distar, although the national powers can generally be found corresponding to it, many things are vastly different.

The biggest difference is that in the late 19th century, an event that affected the entire globe, now known as the 'Death of God', occurred.

It was also because of that event that the Anno Domini calendar system with the birth of Jesus as the starting year officially ended, and was replaced by the 'New Era' calendar system with the year after the death of God as the new starting year, while the history before the Death of God was called the 'Old Era' and the 'Old Times'.

Currently, it is the 267th year of the New Era.

Xue Jing was deftly twirling a pen in his hand, seemingly focused on the teacher, but his mind was actually wandering.

He opened his skill panel, and there was not a single skill related to studying.

As a student in the elite class of a key high school, his academic performance was quite outstanding. Based on the activation conditions of his other skills, he should have been able to easily activate at least the basic Lv1 skills in subjects like Chinese, math, physics, politics, and history.

But on his panel, none of those were present.

'Perhaps... these courses can only be considered "knowledge", and not true "skills"?'

Xue Jing speculated.

The skills recognized by the skill panel should be specific abilities, while subjects like Chinese and math are just knowledge stored in the brain, and cannot be considered skills.

'If that's the case, then attending class is kind of a waste of time...'

Xue Jing was rapidly twirling the ballpoint pen in his hand, considering whether he should fake an illness and skip class to grind his skills, when he suddenly noticed that the "Charm" skill on his panel was gaining experience again.

[Someone has developed feelings for you, Charm experience +1]

[Someone has developed feelings for you, Charm experience +1]

He discreetly turned his head and looked around, and his gaze met that of a bespectacled female classmate who had been sneaking glances at him. She flushed with embarrassment and quickly averted her eyes, pushing up her glasses in a gesture of concealment, her cheeks reddening.

Xue Jing felt a bit helpless.

This "Charm" skill was one he had just activated a couple of days ago.

As the name suggests, it's a skill that enhances one's charm and attractiveness to others.

The effect is that his mannerisms and expressions will become more charming, and as the skill level increases, his physique and appearance will also undergo subtle improvements, becoming more well-proportioned and good-looking.

However, this skill... he didn't need it.

In this life, he can be summed up in six words:

Naturally a soft rice ball with a godly physique.

He doesn't need something like Charm at all, as his mere presence is already like a human aphrodisiac.

Yet, this is the skill that he least needs, but also the easiest one for him to level up.

Because one of the conditions for it to gain experience is if someone develops feelings of "like" or "love" towards him. The stronger the emotion, the more experience it gains.

Furthermore, if he actively does things that make people develop a fondness for him, the experience gain is even greater. For example, when he accepted a towel from a junior girl during physical fitness testing, he directly gained 45 points.

This is why, without even deliberately grinding the "Charm" skill, he had already reached Lv2 in just two days.

Just casually strolling on the street can gain him a decent amount of experience, so naturally his leveling is fast.

'If only the other skills were this easy to level up as well...'

Xue Jing thought as he twirled the pen.

This student not only had excellent grades - after all, elite class students were all outstanding - but his physical appearance was also exceptionally good.

Whenever there were activities that required finding a few students to make a good impression, pulling him in would add a lot of prestige.

And unexpectedly, his athletic ability was also quite impressive.

Mr. Du patted Xue Jing's shoulder and said with a smile.

Xue Jing instinctively wanted to refuse, as he didn't have the intention to compete. But suddenly, he seemed to have thought of something, pondered for a moment, and then nodded, "Since the teacher is saying so, then I'll participate."
