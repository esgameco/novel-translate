Shock!

On the playground.

Students still waiting for the test, or those who had just gone through the spiritual power test, also noticed the anomaly and looked towards the school gate.

Dozens of people poured into the school, heading towards the playground.

In the front row stood four middle-aged men, dressed ordinarily in long robes over undergarments, which were official custom-made uniforms.

The second row had four more people than the first.

The eight people were all in their late forties or fifties, with some elders looking venerable.

The third row had even more people, about twenty or so, each with a decent spiritual demeanor, only concealing their essence inwardly!

The fourth row was the last, with the most people standing, about thirty. The driver and the smoking uncle were also in this row, but standing on the far left.

He was a Martial Saint!

A Martial Saint could not even stand in the second row, let alone the first.

Then what status did those standing in the front two rows hold??

The highest rulers of the Dragon Kingdom?

Teacher Jing'an was an associate professor at the Dragon Academy. Above him were professors, associate deans, and deans!

The four deans were the nation's important figures, promoting martial arts curricula, advocating martial arts for all citizens. The martial arts textbooks used in the past century were almost all written by these four.

The writing of the Six Realms of Body Refining, Bone Forging, Tendon Changing, Purifying the Filth, and Blood Exchanging also owed some credit to them.

The four deans of the Dragon Academy had simultaneously left the imperial capital and come to Linjiang.

They attached great importance to the future of martial arts that had emerged in Linjiang.

"Don't panic, since the deans of the Dragon Academy are here, we no longer need to worry about Linjiang's affairs."

After all, Teacher Jing'an was an associate professor at the Dragon Academy. After a brief daze, he quickly organized the four exam officials to conduct the next round of testing.

The huge testing device was carried onto the table. The college entrance exam instrument for testing spirit power was similar to the ordinary spirit power tester, only much larger.

That liquid crystal display screen was over one meter tall, able to accurately show each student's exact spirit power value.

Teacher Jing'an took a scoring sheet and wrote "Qualified" for the spiritual power test section, before looking at the name on the sheet.

"Next, I'll call out a name, and that person come up for spirit power testing."

"Ning Que!"

One of the hundred students who had just tested spiritual power stepped out from the crowd.

"Ning Que from Class 2, Grade 12!"

"So he's Ning Que?"

"From the Class 2 of Grade 12, it seems this person's ability must be above ours."

The other Linjiang high school exam class students revealed envious looks.

How could they not envy?

Class 2, Grade 12!

That was the super strong class!

The other classes, 1, 3, and 4, were all regular classes.

The super strong class had forty-five students, all dragons and phoenixes among men, and Ning Que was one of the top among them.

He strode past the crowd and stood under the spirit power tester, naturally extending his arm into the device.

The four exam officials and Teacher Jing'an had solemn expressions, looking at this arm as if they had seen a ghost, their eyes revealing an unimaginable gaze.

"How is this possible?"

"Are you sure this is the arm of a high school student?"

Moreover, a Grade 12 student?

Pure white!

Smooth!

Not a single impurity!

As high school students, in order to boost their spirit power values to 1 and become martial artists, they would check their spirit power periodically.

But this student named Ning Que had no needle marks on his arm!

Did he never test his spirit power value?

Teacher Jing'an could only keep this doubt in his heart.

The tester secured Ning Que's arm, and a fine needle shot out, piercing his index finger.

Huh?

The screen on the tester did not react, the spirit power value did not display.

This scene!

Was captured by the surveillance cameras and projected onto screens at other college entrance exam sites.

"The spirit power tester in Linjiang is broken!"

"Sure enough, there are problems with the Linjiang exam, no wonder it has attracted such high-level attention."

"This is just the first test taker, and they've already encountered such a major issue. Linjiang is rotten and needs to be cleaned up!"

"..."

Teacher Jing'an looked at Ning Que and adjusted the tester's power to maximum.

The other four exam officials flinched.

They all understood Teacher Jing'an's intention.

"Try again."

Ning Que did as instructed, and this time when the needle shot out, it instantly pierced his index finger.

At the same time, the over one-meter tall display screen on the tester lit up.

1.8!!

Boom!

This number violently struck the hearts of the other exam students.

A spirit power value of 1.8, exceeding a martial artist by a whole 0.8 units.

Not many nationwide exam students could calmly watch the screens, at most just a dozen or so.

"2.0!"

"3.3!"

"5.8!"

"8.9!"

"9.9!"

The numbers on the tester's display screen jumped rapidly, they could only see the string of numbers leaping madly.

Each new number display made their hearts sink further.

As fellow high school exam students, how could the gap be so huge??

Among all Grade 12 students nationwide, ninety-nine percent had spirit power values below 1, only the remaining one percent were martial artists!

And now, even those remaining one percent martial artist high schoolers had their mentalities shattered.

9.9!

That was the peak of a martial artist's spirit power, just 0.1 more would be a Master's spirit power!

"Boom!"

It was as if an invisible sound blasted all the high school exam students across the nation, watching those display screens at their respective exam sites!

"10.8!"

"10.9!"

"12.3!"

After breaking through the spirit power value of 10, the number on the screen finally stopped at 12.3 without further jumping.

Teacher Jing'an and the four exam officials instinctively looked at the tester, thinking it must be broken.

A Master??

The first exam student was already a Master??

This?!!

Teacher Jing'an raised his head to look at the distant crowd.

He saw the four deans, seeming to plead for help from them, but that group remained silent, watching the playground without reaction.

Just a single Master!

During their time in power, they had seen too many youthful prodigies.

Or perhaps it would be more accurate to say they themselves were martial arts prodigies - an eighteen-year-old Master was nothing rare to them.

Seeing his plea for help was futile, Teacher Jing'an could only continue the testing.

"Next, Feng Buping."

Ning Que's spirit power value had just reached 12.8, how could he possibly compare?

He extended his arm into the tester, and soon the display showed a string of numbers, eventually settling at 5.3!

The youth looked disheartened, merely glancing at the spirit power value before hurriedly leaving the exam site.

"5.3? This is a 5.3 spirit power value?"

Dumbfounded!

The other students watching the Linjiang exam were all stunned.

They had thought this test taker's spirit power value would be around 0.98, otherwise why would he act so awkward and shy.

It was only when the value appeared that they realized how greatly mistaken they were.

He was actually also a martial artist, and moreover his spirit power value reached 5.3!!

A mid-level martial artist!

Unimaginable that such a shy and awkward person would be a mid-level martial artist.

"Dong Yixiang, spirit power value 5.8!"

The subsequent test results opened everyone's eyes wide.

Not a single Linjiang high school exam student had a spirit power value below 5.3!
