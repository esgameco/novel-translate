The First Martial Saint!

He had lived for nearly three hundred years. Two hundred years ago, he stepped into the realm of Martial Saint, and only then did Martial Saints appear across the ocean.

He was ranked first. In the Dragon Kingdom, his strength was the strongest, having completed three blood condensations!

This was his publicly known level of power, but in reality, he had already completed the fourth blood condensation, living out another lifetime, which allowed him to maintain his youthful appearance of forty years old.

He was secretly observing these two Martial Saints in the dark.

The Mayor of Lijiang!

Secretary Wen!

The auras of these two Martial Saints were concealed, impossible to observe.

But it was precisely this feeling that made the First Martial Saint frown.

"Lijiang's Martial Saint is very powerful, is this the power after cultivating the Divine Body? Starting off with two blood condensations!"

Not only the Mayor of Lijiang and Secretary Wen, but also the three Martial Saints behind them.

If these five Martial Saints joined forces, even the First Martial Saint felt he might be trapped in the Ancestral City of Lijiang.

The three of them simultaneously stopped in their tracks and looked behind them!

There, the Principal was fawning over a young teacher in conversation.

That teacher was the Martial Ancestor.

"The Crown Prince of the Gu Family! Gu Zixiu!"

The First Martial Saint stepped forward, walking towards Gu Jiuqing.

The Mayor and Secretary Wen moved to flank him, sandwiching the First Martial Saint in the middle.

If the First Martial Saint made any unusual moves, they would attack him together.

The two corporate leaders behind also came forward.

"This time it's my turn. There are two matters, the first is to pass down the method to cultivate magical powers."

The First Martial Saint had just walked behind the Principal when he heard Gu Jiuqing's voice.

He actually stopped in his tracks, as if he couldn't believe what he had heard!

He looked at the Mayor and Secretary Wen surrounding him on both sides and let out a wry laugh.

"You two Martial Saints, why are you so tense? The Martial Ancestor is preaching, I'm just curious."

"As fellow Earthlings, if I had malicious intentions towards humans, not to mention my Dragon Kingdom, even humans on Earth would have perished when the first interdimensional portal appeared."

The First Martial Saint stated his stance.

The several Martial Saints laughed heartily, but did not let down their guard.

The First Martial Saint didn't mind, as he looked at this young Martial Ancestor and took the initiative to greet him.

"Teacher Gu?!!"

He inquired tentatively. Gu Jiuqing nodded slightly. Who was this person?

Why had he never seen him before?

He had met the other high-ranking figures in Lijiang.

"I am the First Martial Saint, Teacher Gu must have heard of me, right?"

The First Martial Saint said proudly.

Throughout the Spiritual Qi Renaissance era, his fame could be said to have overshadowed half an era!

The one billion people of the Dragon Kingdom were all his followers.

If this Martial Ancestor was from the Dragon Kingdom, there was no way he hadn't heard of him.

Gu Jiuqing looked at the middle-aged man before him, the corners of his mouth curling up slightly!!

The true ruler of the Dragon Kingdom had finally appeared before him.

The twenty-three provinces and five capital cities of the Dragon Kingdom were very different from the regime during the Spiritual Qi Renaissance period.

After individual combat abilities reached a certain extreme, one person became an entire nation.

Just like the Sword Sect, the Sect Leader was the number one person in the Sword Sect. With him around, the elders of the Sword Sect could not have other intentions.

Under the Sect Leader were the two-in-one and three-in-one Celestial Masters at the peak. Under them were the Peak Masters, followed by the Elder Sword Immortals.

And in the Dragon Kingdom, with the First Martial Saint being the strongest individual, the entire Dragon Kingdom had to follow his lead.

After him were the eight Vice Princes of the eight great families in the imperial capital, the top four and bottom four families.

They nominated a Prince to be the open Emperor of the Dragon Kingdom.

After the eight Princes were the Presidents of the Dragon Academy of Sciences, whose status was highly esteemed and did not participate in the regime. They were ranked in the second and third tiers.

The third tier consisted of the capital cities of the twenty-three provinces, whose Chairmen could command their own forces.

This was how the current Dragon Kingdom was structured in the Spiritual Qi Renaissance era.

"Mmm!"

Gu Jiuqing nodded.

Having drawn out the First Martial Saint, Gu Jiuqing didn't need the students of Class 2 Grade 3 to continue relaying his message when he preached in the Dragon Kingdom later. It would only take one sentence.

The First Martial Saint smiled, "Teacher Gu just said there is a way to cultivate magical powers? Is that true?"

The other Martial Saints frowned.

Since the Martial Ancestor said so, it must be true.

What was the First Martial Saint doubting?!

Gu Jiuqing did not respond, but continued, "The method to cultivate magical powers is very simple, it is called the Root Cultivation Method."

The First Martial Saint frowned!

But he did not speak again, and like the other Martial Saints, he listened to Gu Jiuqing's teachings.

"Cultivating the first strand of magical power is most crucial. As long as you cultivate the first strand of magical power, you can open your Dantian, and thereafter refine spiritual qi into magical power to store in your Dantian."

"The Root Cultivation Method is to, under specific conditions, perfect yourself and then no longer continue cultivating, but instead stabilize yourself, reaching a state of fullness and overflow."

Gu Jiuqing's explanation was simple, "It's like a bottle. When the bottle is filled with water, if you don't expand the bottle but keep pouring water into it, the water will overflow. This overflowing water is magical power."

Gu Jiuqing pointed at Wen Daihua.

Wen Daihua promptly walked in front of Gu Jiuqing.

"It's like her, Wen Daihua is currently at the Sect Master realm."

"After you perfect the Sect Master realm, don't try to break through to the Great Sect Master realm. From the day you perfect it, build up your physical body, elevating your essence, energy and spirit to the utmost. In about one to three months, you will overflow your first strand of essence, energy and spirit. After this strand resonates with heaven and earth, it will become your first strand of magical power!"

This method was very simple and easy to practice. It was popular for a period in the ancient Primordial Era.

But it was not as good as the Heaven-Defying techniques.

So in the Primordial Era, this method could not be passed down.

Only when the path to Celestial Immortality was blocked did some three-in-one Patriarchs comprehend other Celestial Immortality methods, thereby rediscovering this method.

The Mayor and the others looked excited after hearing this.

Only the First Martial Saint had doubts. Could this really allow one to cultivate magical powers?

He had never tried it before, so he didn't know if this method was real or not.

If it was real!

Just this method alone could allow the Dragon Kingdom to become the number one power on Earth, suppressing the forces across the ocean!

The First Martial Saint couldn't help but speak up, "It's very difficult to cultivate magical powers. Teacher Gu, where does this method come from?"

Gu Jiuqing looked at the First Martial Saint again.

Where did it come from?

This was the first time someone had asked him the origin of this method in front of him.

"Insolent!"

A voice suddenly rang out.

A Martial Saint flew in from outside the school.

"First Martial Saint, this is the Ancestral City of Lijiang, not the imperial capital or any other capital city. Mind your words."

The President of the Martial Arts Association's figure landed in the school.

His aura was immense, golden feathered wings condensed on his back. He had already filled in seven vertebrae with the Golden Crow Divine Body technique!

After filling three vertebrae, one could complete the first blood condensation!

Six vertebrae allowed the second blood condensation.

Nine vertebrae, twelve vertebrae, fifteen vertebrae allowed the third, fourth and fifth blood condensations respectively!

After cultivating fifteen vertebrae and completing five blood condensations, one could officially step into the Celestial Martial realm and become a Terrestrial Immortal.

The First Martial Saint sensed the President's aura, his expression changing repeatedly.

This was a Martial Saint on par with him.

"You've also completed four blood condensations?"

The President did not respond, but bowed to Gu Jiuqing instead.

"Greetings, Teacher Gu!"

Didn't he see?

These Martial Saints were so respectful towards Gu Jiuqing. The First Martial Saint was powerful, but if they attacked together, suppressing and killing him would not be difficult.

Gu Jiuqing was their deity.

Surrounding their deity was surrounding their own divine authority.

The First Martial Saint's tone softened as he looked at the young Gu Jiuqing, finally placing Gu Jiuqing in the position of a "deity".

"Mmm, I'm not doubting the method Teacher Gu passed down, but methods to cultivate magical powers are not only rare on Earth, they are also rare in other worlds."

Other worlds!

Gu Jiuqing became interested, hearing about other worlds for the first time when Elder Gu came to see him.

Interdimensional portals could lead to other worlds.

"Other worlds? Can the First Martial Saint elaborate?"

Gu Jiuqing was courteous, not using harsh words, but adopting a inquiring tone to ask the First Martial Saint.

The First Martial Saint had entered interdimensional portals many times and was the most qualified among the Seventy-Two Sages to comment on other worlds.

The First Martial Saint nodded, his voice slightly weighed down, "Over a hundred years ago, the Great Elephant Venerable of the Great Elephant Kingdom across the ocean discovered a crack in a valley, and from there the reason for the Spiritual Qi Renaissance was found!"

The Spiritual Qi leaked out from the interdimensional rift!

And by passing through this rift, one could enter another world.

"Over the past hundred years, there have been four interdimensional rifts on Earth. The Dragon Kingdom's Shennongjia has one. Over the years, I have entered and exited the Shennongjia interdimensional rift many times."

Gu Jiuqing asked curiously, "Are the four interdimensional portals leading to the same world, or different ones?"

The First Martial Saint shook his head, "I'm also not very clear. The world the Shennongjia interdimensional portal leads to is very vast, but also very quiet. Once I walked for three months before reaching a city."

The world of the Shennongjia interdimensional portal was like the ancient era!

Vast lands with sparse population, cities separated by great distances. There could be a string of cities that were all ruins!

"This world is very bizarre. We've coded it as the Bizarre World."

The Bizarre World?

Gu Jiuqing was puzzled.

"According to the natives there, there are bizarre things outside the cities that appear at night, so they need to take shelter in the cities. Moreover, in every household within the cities of this Bizarre World, they light an oil lamp."

"No cultivators?"

First spread the dharma to the ominous world, let the people there know the benefits of the dharma transmitted by Gu Jiuqing. Another realm.

This concerned Gu Jiuqing's cultivation. Regardless of who dared to obstruct this, he would not hesitate to unleash the power of the Great Wilderness!!

Harvest the sun!
