"The lunar base has been built for a month already, are we finally going to start the lunar mission today?"

Many of the lunar cultivators looked towards the enormous object inside the base.

That was a manned rocket!

This manned rocket was built by the T20, equipped with the latest technology, but with one flaw - it was single-use only, capable of going to the moon but unable to return!

As a result, it could greatly reduce the rocket's weight, thereby increasing the number of people going to the moon.

"I thought the lunar program had stalled. The daily allowance for the lunar program is quite good, much better than being a cultivator."

"What? Aren't you a cultivator yourself?"

Among these one hundred lunar personnel, one person stood out.

Bai Muqian!

She did not converse with the other lunar crew. This morning, she had just come out of the cafeteria when she was notified to report to the base.

She looked up at the bright sky, the moon nowhere to be seen.

"Perhaps I can cultivate the Yin-Yang Divine Body on the moon!"

Collect the lunar yin essence on the moon, balance it with the sun's yang essence, and develop the Yin-Yang Divine Body!

"I wonder how cultivating the Dragon and Elephant Skill would be on the moon?"

Bai Muqian had already steadily entered the Refining Bones realm, a Refining Bones grandmaster. She was refining the 206 bones in the human body, and with the help of the Dragon and Elephant Skill, she had already refined twenty-eight of them!

At this pace, she could complete the Refining Bones cultivation within a year and advance to the Refining Tendons level!

Too fast!

The cultivation speed of the Dragon and Elephant Skill was too fast.

If she could get help from the Yin-Yang Divine Body, Bai Muqian's cultivation could accelerate even further!

"Beep beep beep ----"

The sound of horns rang out not far away. Several military trucks stopped outside the lunar base, and a large group of people rushed out from inside the base to welcome them at the entrance.

Bai Muqian saw the mayor, who had only appeared in the news before!

The mayor himself came out to welcome them.

"That's the mayor, and the mayor's secretary, they're here too!"

"Oh my, aren't those the chairmen of the three major corporations? These three chairmen control Linjiang's economy, and these three big shots have also come!"

"Isn't that the head of the security department?"

"Wow, so many people coming out together to welcome us, are they here to receive some high-ranking officials from the Dragon Kingdom?"

All eyes turned towards the entrance of the base, and soon they saw a young man walking towards the base, surrounded by a group of people.

"He's so young? Is this a prince from the imperial capital?"

"A prince? It should be, but didn't Linjiang impose a lot of blockades? How did a prince manage to come to Linjiang?"

"..."

Bai Muqian looked at this young prince and her heart skipped a beat.

"Gu Zixiu!"

That was the young master of the Gu family, Gu Zixiu, the prince of the imperial capital.

Why did he also come to the lunar base?

A thought arose in Bai Muqian's mind, "Could the lunar base have been Gu Zixiu's idea?"

A profligate son?

Bai Muqian wanted to associate this term with Gu Jiuqing, but no matter how she thought about it, she could not connect Gu Jiuqing with being a profligate son.

"Could there be some other purpose behind going to the moon?"

In this era of spiritual energy's revival, was going to the moon to search for spiritual energy?

Bai Muqian stared fixedly at that figure.

Gu Jiuqing finally saw the big thing for the lunar mission - the manned rocket!

This manned rocket would fly to the moon today, and was expected to land on the moon five days later, on the sixth day of the sixth month.

Behind Gu Jiuqing stood Gu Lao'er.

He was quite apprehensive!!

Facing him were the high-ranking officials of Linjiang City.

These officials meant nothing compared to his Gu family's status.

But the cultivation levels of these officials!

They were all Martial Kings!

The mayor of Linjiang City, a Martial King. This Martial King's qi and blood had formed a blazing furnace, and even from a distance, he could feel that this Martial King's five viscera had been refined to the utmost, with the five primordial qi intertwining!

With every breath, it was like the sound of thunder, cleansing the physical body.

"Such profound attainments, this level of Martial King could rank among the top ten in the imperial capital. At most three years, and he could break through to the Blood Condensation Realm!"

There were levels of strength among Viscera Refining Martial Kings. The weaker Martial Kings had just begun viscera refining, refining the five viscera once, which would greatly increase their strength.

Then using the sound of thunder to temper the physical body, their strength would enter a period of explosive growth.

Martial Kings at the third step had the five primordial qi intertwining within the five viscera, completely dissolving the viscera and intestines!

Even the heart would be dissolved, transformed into a heart deity residing in the heart's position!

At the third step, Martial Kings could soar through the air using their physical bodies, and even survive in outer space without needing to breathe oxygen or worry about pressure issues.

Most Martial Kings in the imperial capital and the twenty-three provinces were stuck at the first viscera refining step.

There were very few Martial Kings at the second thunder tempering step!

As for third step Martial Kings, their strength was immense, and each one was a priceless treasure to various powers.

In this era where Martial Sages had entered other realms to explore, third step Martial Kings were the strongest.
