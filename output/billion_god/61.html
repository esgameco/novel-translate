The main subject was right in front of him. Gu Jiuqing held the letter, feeling a bit nervous.

He didn't know!

Whether the Celestial Goddess had seen this letter or not.

This was just an ordinary letter, without any infusion of magical powers or divine abilities.

Anyone could read it.

As if hearing Gu Jiuqing's thoughts, a voice came from the Celestial Goddess in the distance.

"Young man, do not worry. This palace does not stoop to such petty behavior."

Gu Jiuqing felt awkward.

His elder sister had arranged too many potential spouses for him.

First it was the Goddess of the Luo River, and now came this Celestial Goddess!

Had Gu Linglong forgotten the Celestial Goddess's identity?

She was the disciple of a major sect. Even if he took a fancy to the Celestial Goddess, she could not possibly marry him.

Unless!

He married into her family!

There was still a slim possibility.

In the letter, a newly introduced name, Chu Xiangyu, would be the third potential spouse that Gu Linglong would introduce to Gu Jiuqing.

Gu Jiuqing was now somewhat apprehensive.

The Goddess of the Luo River was the goddess of the northern lands! Her reputation resounded throughout the north, and behind her was the power of an influential northern aristocratic family!

And the status of the Celestial Goddess was even more terrifying. Backed by the Celestial Palace, she was the future supreme leader of the next generation of the Celestial Palace. She might even cultivate into a deity in the future. At the very least, she was a formidable figure close to godhood, capable of being ranked among the four greatest powers in the world!

Then what about Chu Xiangyu?

The status of this potential spouse should not be low either.

[By the way, little brother, your calligraphy and paintings have received a good response in the Great Zhou. When will you send some of your works to your sister? Over these years, I have done well in business and opened up trade routes across the nine states, even reaching the barbarian tribes beyond.

After some time, I will send you a sum of spirit stones.

It has been a few months already. How is your cultivation going? Have you made any progress in the Three Zhang Golden Body technique? Cultivating this method requires comprehending Buddhist dharma. With your mastery of the arts of zither, chess, calligraphy and painting, you should be able to comprehend the Buddhist dharma quickly.

I have already purchased a genuine painting of the Buddha from a barbarian. I will send it to you along with the next delivery.]

The letter also mentioned at the end:

[You are not young anymore. You and I are the only ones left in the Gu family. While I am out working hard, you need to take on the responsibility of continuing our family line. But do not be too picky. If you take a fancy to someone, just tell me, and I will help you get her.]

Apart from the useless first half of this letter, the second half revealed a lot of information.

Sister would send another large sum of spirit stones!

Gu Jiuqing was preparing to open the path to the Blue Star and the Moon, and activating the ancient teleportation formation required spirit stones! This could resolve Gu Jiuqing's worries.

And there was also the genuine painting of the Buddha!

The pages recording the Three Zhang Golden Body technique had been taken away by the Goddess of the Luo River.

But the cultivation method was already imprinted in Gu Jiuqing's mind.

The reason he had not taken out this cultivation technique was that he was worried the people of the Blue Star might not be able to cultivate it successfully.

With the genuine painting of the Buddha, allowing the people of the Blue Star to comprehend the true meaning of the Buddha, there was a slim possibility they could successfully cultivate the Three Zhang Golden Body technique.

Gu Jiuqing put away the letter, and only then looked towards the Celestial Goddess.

She had walked over to a wall inside the divine palace.

The wall had been pierced through, leaving a huge hole.

"This must have been pierced through by sword energy."

The Celestial Goddess looked towards the hole, and saw the innermost cultivation room beyond it.

"You, young man, were not careful enough during your cultivation, almost destroying your own palace."

"If I were to be with you in the future, wouldn't I be suffering for no reason?"

Seeing that Gu Jiuqing had finished reading the letter, the Celestial Goddess joked.

"However, you only joined the Sword Sect two or three months ago, yet you have already cultivated such fierce sword energy."

The Celestial Goddess suddenly changed her tone. "This is not sword energy, this is sword intent. You are cultivating the flying sword pellet technique, imbuing your sword intent into flying swords. Formless like water, bending around your fingers. You have already completed the cultivation of the flying sword pellet technique."

Gu Jiuqing was startled.

Just by looking at this damaged wall, the Celestial Goddess had deduced Gu Jiuqing's level of cultivation!

Such insight was astonishing.

"There was a temporary breakthrough, which allowed me to condense the flying sword into a pellet."

The Celestial Goddess looked again at the hole in the wall.

She reached out and touched the edge of the hole, as if sensing the remnants of the sword intent left behind.

"This sword intent!"

"This sword intent should be no less than the fifth level of sword intent."

The Celestial Goddess looked at Gu Jiuqing and parted her ruby lips. "You have already cultivated to the semi-immortal sword realm!!"

The semi-immortal sword realm at such a young age?

Just from less than three months of sword cultivation??

The Celestial Goddess was greatly moved.

Gu Jiuqing did not deny it.

"It cannot compare to the cultivation of the Celestial Goddess!"

After his travels in the illusory realm, Gu Jiuqing's insight had greatly improved. He looked at the star halos surrounding the Celestial Goddess!

This Celestial Goddess was enveloped in starlight, her mysterious gauze robes forbidding any prying eyes.

She was too mysterious, no less so than Sixian and Senior Martial Brother Liang Xiao!

Sixian's insight was unmatched in the world, yet such a peerless genius had joined the Sword Sect! Gu Jiuqing could not fathom it.

And then there was Senior Martial Brother Liang Xiao!

Renowned as the madman of the Nameless Peak, he had cultivated the divine demon visualization technique, only to sever it later! Studying demonic arts, he had planted three hundred seeds of divine abilities on Mount Wuzhou!

It took three hundred and sixty-five divine warriors to suppress his celestial aperture, and there were still major artifacts suppressing him on Mount Wuzhou!

A figure at the celestial realm could easily slay those at the tribulation realm, defying the heavens with ease.

And the mystery surrounding this Celestial Goddess before him seemed no less than those two...
