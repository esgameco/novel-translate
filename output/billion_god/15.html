Gu Jiuqing learned about the issue of physical shackles from Xia Qingzhou.

Shedding mortality and building a foundation - the more times one sheds mortality, the more solid the foundation that is forged.

However, relying solely on one's inner energy to cleanse the mortal body, there is an ultimate limit to how many times one can shed mortality.

This is not the limit of an individual's mortal body, but the limit of the entire human race.

The four gates of the Great Zhou in the southeast, northwest, northeast and southwest guard against the invasion of demons and monsters. Outside of the Great Zhou in the desolate territories, demons and monsters run rampant, causing chaos with their evil energy.

Demons and monsters are born with innate magical powers, with overwhelmingly strong abilities.

The gap between humans and demons is too large. Even if humans can somehow break through their shackles, the shedding mortality realm can only be achieved so many times.

"Qu Yaozhou has shed mortality three times. Chaotianque can borrow the power of the ancient Qu family ancestor, but his mortal body cannot withstand so much power. Do not underestimate Chaotianque!"

So here is the problem.

Gu Jiuqing's expression changed. More and more people would cultivate the Dragon and Elephant Skill at Linjiang, and more would use it to break through the martial artist realm.

Just the inner energy born from their cultivation of the Dragon and Elephant Skill would be an astronomical number.

And him!

He would gain all the cultivation bases of the Linjiang martial artists, hundreds? Thousands? Tens of thousands?

With such an immense amount of inner energy combined and gathered within Gu Jiuqing's body, could his mortal form withstand it?

This was a huge problem, a matter of life and death for Gu Jiuqing.

"So being too powerful is also a bad thing."

Gu Jiuqing sighed inwardly. It seems joining the inner sect of the Sword Gate is a must.

He cannot resolve this issue by himself, he can only seek help from the higher-ups of the Sword Gate.

Qu Yaozhou!

He must die!

When he dies, it will shake the Sword Gate, and more Sword Gate higher-ups will appear.

At that time, I will gain more attention from the Sword Gate higher-ups.

"Young Master Gu, do you feel confident about tomorrow's battle?"

Xia Qingzhou asked with a hint of worry.

She was a disciple of the Xia family in the Ancient Guiyuan City, studying zither, chess, calligraphy and painting from a young age, well-versed in various secret classics and books, all for the purpose of joining the Sword Gate today.

Disciples from prestigious clans had such profound foundations.

They almost all knew the information about the ancient cultivation clans of the city, including Qu Yaozhou of the Qu family.

In the few days Xia Qingzhou had spent with Gu Jiuqing, she had become quite interested in him.

She did not want Gu Jiuqing to fall at the foot of Sword Gate Mountain!

"The little Qu brat is nothing to speak of."

Gu Jiuqing stood up and walked towards the flying carriage.

Seeing Gu Jiuqing's confidence, Xia Qingzhou was no longer worried.

However, Ye Fei, Fan Shaoli, and Qing Yang Bai frowned, not understanding why this young master of the Gu family was so arrogant.

"The Gu family rose to prominence relatively late. The elders of the Gu family all died in an ancient ruin, leaving only Gu Jiuqing and Gu Linglong."

"Gu Jiuqing loves zither, chess, calligraphy and painting, lingering in various academies, and has good relationships with several great scholars of the Great Zhou."

"Gu Linglong has average talent, five years older than Gu Jiuqing. After the Gu family elders died, Gu Linglong went into business, frequently traveling between the northern lands and the Great Zhou."

"The Gu family's ultimate skill is the Three Yin Soul-Slaying Blade! Condensing the power of the Great Yin, forming the Soul-Slaying Blade. The first ancestor of the Gu family fortuitously entered an ancient teleportation formation, arriving on the moon. Using the moon's yin essence, he greatly accomplished the Soul-Slaying Blade, establishing the Gu family in the Tianyuan Ancient City."

"Apart from the Three Yin Soul-Slaying Blade, the Gu family also has the Six Harmonies Boxing and the Celestial Snake Skin-Shedding Art, but these skills are inferior to the Dragon and Elephant Skill."

"Where does his confidence come from?"

Chaotianque, Chaotianque!

What does Gu Jiuqing have to defeat him?

Outside the flying carriage, Steward Xie had been waiting for Gu Jiuqing all this time.

This old servant had followed Gu's father for many years, but had never entered an ancient ruin, thus preserving his life.

Eight days had already passed in the nine-day great examination, and he had been waiting here for eight days.

"Young Master!"

Seeing Gu Jiuqing, Steward Xie's expression was tense and anxious.

"You are too reckless. Qu Yaozhou of the Qu family accomplished the Chaotianque realm at a young age."

"Alas, it's this old servant's fault for not informing the young master of the backgrounds of the great clans, leading to this predicament."

"Chaotianque can borrow the power of an ancient Qu family ancestor. With Qu Yaozhou's three sheddings of mortality, once he uses Chaotianque, he can fight at four times the shedding level!"

Each shedding greatly enhances one's strength, speed and physical qualities.

Four sheddings is overwhelmingly stronger than three.

"Tomorrow is the ninth day. Young Master, why don't you just stay inside the carriage? If anyone comes looking for you, this old servant will refuse them."

This was the only option.

Steward Xie could not bear to watch the Gu family's only male heir die before his eyes.

The young master's talent had far exceeded Steward Xie's imagination. If the young lady knew about this, she would surely be moved.

He could already envision the young lady's joyful expression.

Regardless, he had to protect the young master.

"I have my ways. You don't need to say more, Old Xie."

Gu Jiuqing stepped onto the carriage, turning back to Steward Xie.

"You've been with the Gu family for many years, but I still don't know what cultivation realm you've reached?"

Gu Jiuqing did not know Steward Xie's real name, but over the years, Xie had always been by his side.

Within Tianyuan Ancient City, he had never run into any trouble!

Except for this Sword Gate examination!

Steward Xie was stunned, smiling awkwardly.

"Why does the young master ask?"

"This old servant's cultivation is not high, incomparable to the old master."

Gu Jiuqing's father had reached the Immortal Body realm of the Unbroken realm.

So at most, Steward Xie's cultivation was at the peak of the Unbroken realm, or the Energy realm of releasing the Heavenly Mechanism?

"What about my sister?"

Steward Xie smiled awkwardly again, "The young lady started her business five years ago and rarely returns home, so this old servant is unaware."

"Then what was my sister's cultivation five years ago?"

"At that time, it took the young lady a year to complete five sheddings of mortality. She only cultivated energy shortly before leaving to travel the northern lands."

In five years, how far could Gu Linglong's cultivation have progressed?

It seems inquiring about his own family was not going to work.

"It's fine, I'll get some early rest today for the great battle tomorrow."

Gu Jiuqing entered the flying carriage.

The flying carriage contained hidden mysteries - though not a celestial abode, it had been expanded, larger than the blue star room Gu Jiuqing lived in.

It was still early, so Gu Jiuqing did not rush into the blue star.

He sat cross-legged on the bed, continuously gathering his thoughts, trying to enter a meditative state.

Soon, he could sense the vague image within the Mud Pill Palace again.

It was a round, full, plump fruit-like object, a bit like a watermelon but much smaller.

Gu Jiuqing could confirm that this perfectly round, imaginary fruit form had never appeared in the Mud Pill Palace before he transmitted the Dragon and Elephant Skill into the blue star.

It only manifested after he first transmitted the skill, becoming more solidified after receiving the cultivation bases of the high school students.

"Could this plump fruit be an unaccomplished Dao Fruit?"

No matter how Gu Jiuqing pondered, he could only guess it was related to the Dao Fruit.

The Sutra on Upholding the Ten Virtuous Precepts states: "To see all Buddhas, hear the Dharma and attain the Dao Fruit, possess the six spiritual penetrations, and roam the ten directions."

To see the Buddhas, one must hear the great Dharma, attain the Dao Fruit, cultivate the six spiritual penetrations of Buddhism, and be able to travel everywhere.

"The Dao Fruit, the fruition of enlightenment, but this is a Buddhist concept, an illusory path. Perhaps this was the path of cultivation in the previous era, but in this era, there is no such thing as the Dao Fruit in the path of cultivation."

Shedding mortality, building a foundation, releasing the Heavenly Mechanism to condense energy, forging the Unbroken realm to refine the Immortal Body, comprehending Heaven and Earth to refine the Celestial Being, understanding yin and yang to create the Celestial Abode, passing tribulations for eternal life!

None of the realms involved the manifestation of a Dao Fruit!

But Gu Jiuqing had a feeling that this was a real, genuine Dao Fruit, the Dao Fruit he had condensed from cultivating the Dragon and Elephant Skill.

This Dao Fruit contained all the insights from the twenty-two forms of the Dragon and Elephant Skill.

And at all times!

Random insights into cultivation would spontaneously appear within this Dao Fruit, including insights into the Dragon and Elephant forms as well as scattered insights into sword arts.

Gu Jiuqing's thoughts touched the Dao Fruit, and in an instant, myriad insights assailed his mind!

"So that's why these past few days my cultivation insights haven't manifested physically - they've all been accumulating within this Dao Fruit!"
