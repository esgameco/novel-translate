A jade-like youth of peerless elegance, not inferior to Liang Xiao in the slightest.

The jade-like youth had starry eyes containing brilliant lights - the manifestations of his cultivation.

"This true disciple must be the Third Elder's newest student, Si Wuxie!"

Si Wuxie?

Though peerlessly elegant then, he paled in comparison to his present divine youthful splendor.

He looked like a celestial youth, starlight flickering in his eyes - the after-images of secret cultivation.

"Si Wuxie is cultivating the Great Primordial Star Sword Classic, condensing star sword intent."

"Strange, he joined the Third Elder with the Jade Sword Intent. Why is he now cultivating the Great Primordial instead of the Five Phases Sword Intent?"

Liang Xiao was surprised.

Gu Jiuqing studied Si Wuxie.

Beneath the youth's gentle smile hid a celestial heart.

He calmly allowed Gu Jiuqing's scrutiny.

First, he bowed to Liang Xiao.

"Si Wuxie greets the true disciple of Nanting Peak!"

Then he turned to Gu Jiuqing, his smile growing warmer.

"I never imagined the Gu Clan would produce another sword genius like Senior Gu Jiuqing."

He studied Gu Jiuqing curiously, his smile deepening.

"Worthy of being the Sword Genius, worthy of Nanting Peak's true disciple!"

Gu Jiuqing felt a chill.

He sensed gazes sweeping over his body!

Si Wuxie withdrew his gaze, eyes still smiling.

"Has the true disciple come to select a flying sword?"

"My master asked me to lead the two seniors into the Sword Tomb."

"Seniors, please!"

Si Wuxie was courteous and warm, addressing Gu Jiuqing as "senior."

The three proceeded, Si Wuxie leading, with Gu Jiuqing half a step behind Liang Xiao. They soon arrived at the Sword Tomb.

The Sword Tomb!

A dense forest of flying swords, each one embedded in earth and air. Some stuck in rocks, others in empty space, with only their sword qi leaking out.

The densely packed flying swords exuded an aura like a giant furnace before their eyes.

"This is the Sword Sect's Sword Tomb, containing 109,600 flying swords. The lowest grade is most numerous, followed by the middle grade, with over a thousand top-grade swords."

"Please enter and select a sword, Senior Disciple."

Si Wuxie stood outside, his clear gaze showing no greed toward the myriad swords.

Yet obtaining a top-grade sword and refining its sword qi could greatly bolster one's sword intent!
