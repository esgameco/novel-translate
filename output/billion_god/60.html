At the Sword Gate Divine Mountain, a disciple of the Sword Gate stepped out of the courtyard and looked up at the heavens and earth. A streak of starlight scattered across the Sword Gate.

It was the Star Maiden, treading on starlight as she walked through the void.

Two elders accompanied her on either side, explaining matters of the sect to her.

"It's the maiden from the Star Palace, descending upon the Sword Gate!"

"What? The Star Maiden herself?"

"No wonder she has such unparalleled beauty and elegance. It's said that just the 81 cultivators following the Star Maiden..."

"Those 81 followers are all elites from great clans, even including disciples from the Longevity Clan. They all follow the Star Maiden!"

The disciples of the four great sects all have a large number of followers.

These followers follow the sect disciples from an early age. When those disciples eventually become the supreme patriarchs, the followers will be rewarded for their service and may become elders of the great sects.

Among the Star Maiden's 81 followers, two are from the Longevity Clan, and one is from a noble family of the Great Zhou Dynasty.

The names of these three followers are known throughout the world. Yet they willingly became followers of the Star Maiden, which speaks to her ability to win people's hearts.

"Swish swish swish————"

The brilliant starlight transformed into a gauzy robe draped over the Star Maiden's body. Her black hair flowed like a waterfall, her fair skin was delicate enough to be blown away. Her jade peaks stood proudly above all others.

Her slender waist traced a perfect curve, with a large expanse of skin exposed on her back.

Her long, slender legs were exquisitely delicate, like jade carved from mutton fat. With each step she took, starlight scattered, transforming into twinkling stars!

She became the focus of the Sword Gate!

On each of the true transmission islands, the palaces opened their gates, and true disciples emerged, gazing at the Star Maiden in admiration of her stunning beauty.

The two elders flanking the Star Maiden were none other than the Lotus Sword Immortal and the River Mountain Sword Immortal.

Within the Sword Gate, the Lotus Sword Immortal's seniority far exceeded the other elders, as three of her true disciples had already cultivated to the Sword Immortal realm!

In the presence of the Lotus Sword Immortal, even the River Mountain Sword Immortal's sword intent paled in comparison.

"Thank you, senior sword immortals, for leading the way!"

The Star Maiden's cultivation was nothing compared to the two sword immortals.

But she represented one of the four great sects - the Star Palace!

Her arrival at the Sword Gate represented the Star Palace itself!

The two sword immortals hurriedly said, "It is only proper. The Star Palace and Sword Gate have long been close friends. Our two patriarchs would often drink wine together in merriment. We naturally must continue this friendship."

The true form of the River Mountain Sword Immortal had descended in person!

This was an elderly sword immortal with one foot already in the grave. The sword intent condensed around him within three feet, with images of rivers and mountains swirling, vast rivers flowing, and the realm of towering peaks and flowing waters pervading.

"May I ask if the maiden's descent upon the Sword Gate is at the behest of the Star Palace's patriarch?"

The Lotus Sword Immortal trod upon lotus flowers, each petal etched with delicate sword patterns, as if the sword intent had blossomed into flowers.

A chilling coldness emanated from the Lotus Sword Immortal.

The Star Maiden shone brilliantly like the stars, graceful and proud in her bearing.

The Lotus Sword Immortal, however, was an aloof beauty, untouchable to mortals.

Her eyebrows were long and willowy, her eyes slightly narrowed like sharp sword edges.

"This senior sword sister jests. This princess comes to the Sword Gate seeking an old friend."

An old friend?

The two peerless sword immortals exchanged a glance.

They had never known of anyone in the Sword Gate who could claim friendship with the Star Maiden.

Since the previous Star Child fell, the Star Palace had spent hundreds of years pursuing the culprit, never expecting to establish a new Star Maiden three hundred years ago.

This maiden's talents were average at best!

All could see her mediocre aptitude!

Three hundred years of cultivation, yet she had never reached the Celestial Realm. With such meager talents, how could she become a sect's disciple?

Rumors claimed the Star Maiden was the reincarnation of a great immortal, a primordial deity reborn!

Her unique identity was the only reason the Star Palace's patriarch bestowed her the title of Star Maiden.

But this year, news occasionally spread of the Star Maiden being suppressed.

They could not help but doubt again the claim that she was a reincarnated primordial deity.

"Which of my Sword Gate's disciples has the honor of being the maiden's old friend?"

The River Mountain Sword Immortal was curious.

Could it be one of the sword prodigies? But the sword prodigies had been in secluded cultivation with the patriarch for so many years, older than even the Star Maiden's age.

If not a sword prodigy, then perhaps one of the true sword immortals?

The Star Maiden trod upon starlight, gazing at the Sword Gate's peaks and islands, finally settling her gaze upon a mountain shrouded in sword aura.

"I presume that is the Sword Gate's famed Name Courtyard Peak?"

The Name Courtyard Peak of the Sword Gate!

Where the former senior elder cultivated the Sword Furnace, reaching the realm of the present age's Sword Saint.

Among the older generation, it had become a legendary tale.

"Indeed, that is the Name Courtyard Peak."

The River Mountain Elder replied, puzzled as he looked at the Star Maiden.

"Then the old friend I seek resides there."

Starlight rippled through the air, the gauzy robe empowered by the stars formed a halo around her body, strands trailing behind her, portraying her as a celestial deity descending to the mortal realm.

She flew towards the Name Courtyard Peak, the two elders following at her sides.

"Could the maiden's old friend be Gu Jiuqing?"

The Lotus Sword Immortal hesitated briefly before asking.

"Correct, it is that paramount sword cultivator!"

So it was him!

The two sword immortals had guessed correctly.

The Name Courtyard Peak, Gu Jiuqing, this paramount sword cultivator was an old friend of the Star Maiden.

This news was of utmost importance, and the two peerless sword immortals' expressions turned grave.

On the True Transmission Island of the Heavenly Sword Palace.

Gu Jiuqing and Lu Yuan stood on the palace steps.

Starlight streamed down in brilliant strands, scattering dazzling beams endlessly. The three major formations of the Heavenly Sword Palace seemed to have vanished by half, allowing the starlight to descend unimpeded.
