Here is my full translation of the Chinese excerpt into English:

The vast heavens, boundless and immense, the starry sky within reach.

The howling gales whipped by fiercely, propelling the divine moon ship forward, stirring up the starry realm.

"Boom!"

The divine ship jolted, its sails rippling, scattering fragments of the brilliant moon.

The sword disciples on the deck were shaken and staggered, hastily using their physical strength or magical powers to anchor themselves to the deck.

Once they steadied themselves, they noticed changes in the surrounding sky.

Wisps of azure mist drifted beneath the starry expanse.

The azure fog swirled around, crisscrossed by lightning snakes, with flashes of electricity flickering above.

"Rumble——————"

A blinding white light flashed, thunder tearing through.

"This is the Ganfeng Layer, we've now flown out of the Ganfeng Layer."

"So this is the legendary Ganfeng Layer? Ancient tales say the heavenly realm was built above the nine heavens' Ganfeng!"

Many disciples approached the deck's edge, leaning over to gaze down at the Ganfeng Layer.

The azure Ganfeng winds intertwined, colliding with the ship's hull as thunderous forces howled forth. The nine-headed thunder dragons absorbed most of the thunderbolts, but some still struck the ship.

A number of inner disciples took action, these sword-inheriting disciples wielding great swords, stepping into the void to refine the thunder talismans from the thunder pools!

The activated talismans devoured the thunderbolts surrounding the ship. After three or four breaths of time, the moon divine ship finally emerged from the nine heavens' Ganfeng Layer.

Xia Qingzhou gazed at the receding Ganfeng Layer in awe.

"Legend says the ancient eighteen celestial emperors built the heavenly realm upon the Ganfeng Layer, overlooking the great wilderness."

At this moment, the great wilderness appeared several times smaller. If there were transcendent experts present, unleashing their divine sight, they could even pierce through this entire realm!

This was the main reason the heavenly realm was constructed above the nine heavens - to allow the celestial emperors to oversee the great wilderness with ease.

Beside him, Qing Yangbai felt enlightened.

"I never imagined I would one day witness the legends, beholding with my own eyes where the ancient heavenly realm once stood."

The sword disciples all let out exclamations of wonder.

The primordial era seemed to lie just before them, close enough to touch.

Yet tens of thousands of years had passed since that ancient time. The old heavenly realm had fallen eighteen times over, the world utterly transformed, everything changed.

"Why are those senior martial brothers sitting down?"

Xia Qingzhou wondered.

Those senior inner disciples, having refined the ancient thunder talismans, sat cross-legged on the deck.

From their bodies, dazzling halos flickered - some senior disciples emanated over two hundred such halos from their fleshly bodies. Each brilliant halo transformed into an ancient, mythical beast, devouring the starlight raining down from the cosmos.

Above the great wilderness was the Ganfeng Layer of the nine heavens, which blocked most of the starlight.

But now, having broken through the Ganfeng Layer, the moon divine ship was fully bathed in starlight.

Cultivating the star energy arts was undoubtedly the best choice at this moment.

"The senior martial brothers are collecting starlight, absorbing it into their celestial apertures!"

Under the seniors' guidance, the other inner disciples followed suit.

Xia Qingzhou and Qing Yangbai also sat on the deck, breathing in the starlight and refining it into their own magic powers.

Over these past few months, having successfully comprehended the heavenly mysteries, these two inner disciples had become magic power cultivators.

In the ship's cabin...

The Sword Immortals Wind Sword, Fierce Sun Sword, and Cold Sword burst through the brilliant moon barrier, instantly flying into the nine-headed thunder dragons' bodies.

The three figures trod upon the crown of the myriad-zhang thunder dragons, facing the vast starry expanse, circulating their full magic powers to temper their fleshly bodies with the starlight!

Halos lit up one by one, each Sword Immortal opening over three hundred and fifty celestial apertures.

Starlight poured into the halos, their bodies generating divine radiance - the three Sword Immortals appeared like three celestial beings, riding true dragons through the nine heavens and ten earths.

Gu Jiuqing withdrew his gaze, looking back at Lu Yuan behind him.

"With such a great opportunity, why don't you go out and cultivate for a while too, Disciple Brother?"

"This journey is far, gathering starlight to temper your cultivation would bolster your powers."

A magic power cultivator instructing a non-celestial cultivator!

Lu Yuan found nothing strange about this scene.

After a moment's thought, he responded, "Then this disciple brother will go gather starlight to forge the Sun Divine Body!"

Lu Yuan cultivated the Sun Divine Body, requiring the essence of the great sun. At this moment, the sun's essence was many times denser than in the great wilderness.

He set down his sword case and inverted his body before exiting the cabin.

Inside the cabin, only Gu Jiuqing remained.

Strands of magic power dripped from Gu Jiuqing's body, appearing like silken threads that wrapped around the sword case.

Within his dantian, a gate fully opened, and the flowing magic power descended into the dantian.

His dantian expanded, reaching nine zhang!

The silken strands of magic power coalesced into physical form, weaving together like silkworm threads into a vast ocean.

With a thought from Gu Jiuqing, the strands converged, condensing into an enormous cocoon.

"The Origin Pill!"

This was the Origin Pill!

Without refining any external magic power, solely using the power flowing from the Myriad Mysteries Gate, Gu Jiuqing condensed an Origin Pill.

Ripples emanated from the Origin Pill as it burst from the dantian, soaring up to the Non-Celestial Mountain.

The primordial bodies circling the Non-Celestial Mountain stirred!

The Golden Crow primordial essence coalesced into a three-legged golden crow!

The Yin-Yang primordial essence became a roc.

The two primordial avatars descended from the Non-Celestial Mountain, obstructing the cocoon Origin Pill.

But the cocoon did not evade, surging forward instead.

"Boom boom boom————"

"Rumble rumble!!"

The Golden Crow and Roc struck the cocoon repeatedly. With each collision, the pill became more condensed.

The massive cocoon visibly shrank at an astonishing rate, finally reduced to the size of a grindstone!

The cocoon's outer strands, initially separate threads of magic power woven together, could no longer be distinguished.

"So this is the first cycle Origin Pill?"

Using one's own primordial bodies to temper the Origin Pill?

Then how did cultivators without such primordial bodies refine their pills?

Gu Jiuqing looked inwardly at his fleshly body. With a thought, the myriad dragons and elephants circling the Non-Celestial Mountain flew towards the grindstone-sized first cycle Origin Pill.

The elephant essences merged into one massive dragon-elephant divinity, with an elephant's head, eighteen arms, and a dragon's tail whipping behind.

The deity acted, its eighteen arms transforming into iron hammers that struck down upon the Origin Pill relentlessly.

"Bam bam bam!!"

The hammers pounded the pill, compressing it smaller and smaller.

"The texts state that the Origin Pill is compressed from magic power. Without changing the total power, the smaller the pill, the more concentrated and refined the energy within."

"The Origin Pill flies up to the Non-Celestial Mountain, then falls back into the dantian, dispersing into magic power again. The cycle repeats, tempering it into the ninth cycle Origin Pill."

This was the magic power realm's method of Origin Pill refinement.

Previously, when Senior Martial Brother Liang Xiao instructed Gu Jiuqing to choose a magic power attribute, he had declined. So he did not know the specific magic power cultivation methods.

But this was how the Origin Pill was tempered!

Indeed, no mistake!

"The first cycle Origin Pill can circle the Non-Celestial Mountain once. The second cycle twice, the third cycle four times, the fourth cycle eight times, and so on."

"Condensing a higher-grade Origin Pill, ranked seventh or above, allows projecting the pill outwardly to be used as a magic treasure, its might no less than ordinary divine weapons."

Gu Jiuqing's Origin Pill had not even completed a circuit around Non-Celestial Mountain before being "pummeled" by the two primordial avatars. Now thousands of dragon-elephant essences hammered it further.

The grindstone-sized pill was first compressed to the size of a palm, then a thumb, and finally a grain of rice.

The dragon-elephant deity gathered the power of two thousand two hundred dragon-elephant essences.

How formidable was such force?

Gu Jiuqing could not compare it, but it was enough to temper the Origin Pill.

The pill even emitted sparks from the hammering!

After a while more, the thumb-sized pill shrank to a grain of rice.

"If this continues, I won't even be able to see my Origin Pill!"

Gu Jiuqing hurriedly halted the dragon-elephant deity and returned the pill to his dantian.

Within, the rice-grain sized pill dispersed, strands of magic power finer than hair scattering into the dantian.

The power flowing from the Myriad Mysteries Gate converged with these tempered strands, reforming into a vast ocean.

Once the ocean filled the nine-zhang dantian, Gu Jiuqing repeated the process, summoning the dragon-elephant deity to temper the Origin Pill ascending to Non-Celestial Mountain.

Exiting his introspection, Gu Jiuqing gazed at the sword case before him.

The Taiyi Divine Sword! Forged from the primordial material Taiyi Imperishable Divine Gold, the embryo of a divine weapon.

Lu Yuan had already passed down the Taiyi Divine Sword's refinement method to Gu Jiuqing.

Over the past months, Gu Jiuqing prepared to refine the Taiyi Divine Sword, placing it upon his own Non-Celestial Mountain.

"I don't know what heavenly method Senior Martial Brother Liang Xiao used that allowed me to wield the three thousand-grooved divine weapons without any refinement!"

Divine weapons required refinement, imprinting one's own aura upon them before borrowing their power.

Gu Jiuqing cradled the sword case, infusing it with his aura, magic power, blood essence, sword intent, following the prescribed method to refine the Taiyi Divine Sword.

This was a long, gradual process. The more powerful the divine weapon, the longer the nurturing and refinement took.

Time passed bit by bit. Five days later...

The great wilderness beneath the moon divine ship had shrunk several more times.
