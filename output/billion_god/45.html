The living quarters of Senior Martial Brother Liang Xiao were in ruins, completely lacking the appearance of a disciple's residence in the Sword Sect's inner sect. It looked more like an ancient cave dwelling that had just emerged.

As soon as Gu Jiuqing entered, he felt a chill all over his body!

Huge pillars supported the roof beams, with strange demonic figures coiled around each stone pillar.

A three-eyed demon coiled around one pillar, so lifelike it seemed to still be alive!

There was also an eight-armed demon, its eight arms tightly hugging the pillar, with four legs exposing white bones that had turned into four small stone pillars.

Another demon had three heads, six arms, seven tails and eight eyes, coiled around a pillar.

This was the first time Gu Jiuqing had seen so many demons. He wasn't afraid, but instead looked at them with curiosity.

"Senior Martial Brother, did you bring all these demons back from the Primordial Chaos?"

Senior Martial Brother Liang Xiao shook his head and placed the Demonic Prince into a coffin!

There were actually many coffins hidden in Senior Martial Brother's living quarters.

Some coffins already had the forms of buried demons inside.

"Most of these demons invaded the Northern Lands or entered the Great Zhou kingdom. Senior Martial Brother was confined by our master, otherwise he wouldn't dare enter the Primordial Chaos without first investigating the Sword Furnace."

"If our elderly master emerges from seclusion, you'll have to vouch for me!"

"I only went to the Primordial Chaos for the sake of my junior martial brother!"

Senior Martial Brother spoke as he placed the Demonic Prince's true form into the coffin.

The Demonic Prince was horrified, his twelve arms struggling wildly to break free of his restraints.

His Buzhou Mountain had been shattered and his spine sealed, leaving all his divine powers and abilities unusable. He could only watch in horror as he was buried in the coffin.

"This is an ancient ice coffin from the era of the Celestial Soldiers, their burial place from antiquity."

The Celestial Soldiers were buried in these ancient ice coffins.

Just how many of these Celestial Soldier coffins had Senior Martial Brother stolen?

Gu Jiuqing could see over a hundred coffins!

"The Demonic Prince's physical body is too perfect, did you know that, Junior Martial Brother? On this trip to the Primordial Chaos, I saw the offspring of several Demonic Emperors vying for supremacy!"

Senior Martial Brother even revealed a look of desire.

Gu Jiuqing could understand Senior Martial Brother's feelings at this moment.

It was like Gu Jiuqing receiving an invitation from a great scholar, or seeing the calligraphy of a legendary calligrapher, the paintings of a master artist, or hearing the music of a virtuoso.

"Unfortunately, there were too many Demonic Princes. That eldest Demonic Prince's power exceeded even mine."

"But after some time, once I've constructed the final Celestial Palace, I'll capture that eldest Demonic Prince and use him to brew an elixir bath for my junior brother!"

Senior Martial Brother took action!

His ten long fingers, much longer than ordinary people's, unleashed streams of sword intent from their tips, each one unique.

Senior Martial Brother had cultivated too many sword intents, each reaching the highest level.

The cold ice sword intent activated the ancient ice coffin, instantly sealing the Demonic Prince's physical body.

After a breath, the Demonic Prince's spiritual energy was also sealed!

"Fortunately, after waiting several days, those Demonic Princes dispersed. This Demonic Prince should be the seventh son of the Demonic Emperor. He had a Tribulation-transcending giant by his side."

A Tribulation-transcending giant? That was a Tribulation Realm expert, even stronger than the Celestial Palace Realm.

Senior Martial Brother had actually snatched the Demonic Seventh Prince right under the nose of a Tribulation Realm expert?

"Indeed, to become a true disciple of the Sword Sect, one cannot be ordinary."

Gu Jiuqing set aside his disdainful thoughts.

"Capable people emerge everywhere in the world. I should not consider myself the foremost in the Mortal Transcendence Realm."

Gu Jiuqing became a bit more humble.

"Hmph, that Demonic giant noticed me and tried to use the Demonic Seventh Prince as bait. But I saw through the ploy and struck with lethal force, subduing him at the Primordial Chaos Cliff!"

"Unfortunately, that Demonic giant had only just transcended his first Tribulation. Like the other giants I've killed, his physical body and bloodline held no research value!"

Senior Martial Brother looked at the Demonic Seventh Prince in the coffin.

His sword intents intertwined the five elemental energies, forming five sword qi that entered the Demonic Prince's five viscera. In no time, five primordial deities emerged from the viscera.

Senior Martial Brother casually held the five deities in his hands to study them.

"Not bad, not bad. The quintessence of the five elements has congealed so brilliantly, three to four times more powerful than an ordinary Celestial Palace cultivator."

"Now let me see how the Demonic Emperor's bloodline is constructed!"

Senior Martial Brother devoted himself fully to researching the Demonic Emperor's bloodline.

With a rumbling sound, his spiritual power acted, one palm striking the Demonic Seventh Prince's crown aperture. A burst of blood essence shot out, instantly melting the ancient ice intent.

A thick demonic aura descended from the blood essence, filling the dilapidated cave.

"Roar————————"

Layer after layer of seals were undone at this moment!
