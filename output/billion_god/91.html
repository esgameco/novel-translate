Not Old Peak!

The spring water tinkled.

Every strand of spring water was crystal clear, flowing between the mountains, able to reflect the beautiful scenery amid the mountains.

On the moon there were rarely any trees or plants, but on this main peak, there were spiritual plants and grasses everywhere, with ancient trees soaring to eight hundred zhang high!

Forming an expanse of primordial ancient forest.

This scene made one feel awe.

The two threaded through the ancient forest. From deep within the ancient forest, occasionally sounds would transmit over, those were living beings inhabiting the ancient forest.

"But this is on the moon, besides us, could there really be other living beings?"

Lu Yuan exclaimed in astonishment!

The foremost venerable elder of the first mountain ridge of the Eight Formations Ridge had died.

Only then did the disciples of the Constellations Pavilion and Sword Gate dare to set foot here!

But this second mountain ridge, those with insight could discern that this was the place where a venerable elder from the Primordial Era sat in meditation until death.

Who would dare trespass?

Within the Sword Gate, starting from the outer door disciples, besides cultivation, they needed to study the scriptures.

The inner door disciples had to also master the arts of zither, chess, calligraphy and painting.

Every Sword Gate disciple, if placed in the great Zhou dynasty, would at least be a scholar. If they loved learning, passing the imperial examinations would be no problem.

This was the daily routine of the disciples of the four great sects. Lu Yuan was not like Gu Jiuqing who ascended in a single step. When he was an inner door disciple, he studied all kinds of scriptures and ancient books, knowing many widely known legends.

The chaotic era eight thousand years ago was also called the Primordial Era.

Anyone who joined the Sword Gate for a year or two would know about this.

So those moving beings could not possibly be disciples of the Sword Gate or Constellations Pavilion.

"Could they be wandering immortal cultivators?"

Lu Yuan shuddered!

If that was truly the case, their lives would be in peril.

Gu Jiuqing shook his head, "It's impossible to be immortal cultivators. The sounds coming from deep within the ancient forest were sometimes hurried, sometimes quiet. Those immortal cultivators would have no need to be so restless!"

Gu Jiuqing slightly furrowed his brows, the spiritual energy within his body coalesced together, forming a spirit pill!

The various origins on Buzhou Mountain, including the two thousand two hundred dragon and elephant beasts, had all been stirred!

Mouthfuls of thousand-grooved divine weapons surged with spiritual energy, activating the divine weapons.

The two passed by ancient tree after ancient tree, with various wondrous fragrances wafting over. They dared not be distracted, vigilantly watching their left, right, front, back, above and below.

After a long while, Gu Jiuqing and the others had walked to the main peak. After going a bit further, they would be able to see the Not Old Spring on Not Old Peak.

"Swish——"

From a large tree, leaves fell down one after another. Some of the fallen leaves landed on the clear spring flowing between the mountains, while others fell onto the ground.

But before they even hit the ground, they transformed into wisps of spiritual energy, merging into the void of the mountain ridge.

The spiritual leaves of spiritual plants could not last long.

"There's something in the tree!"

Gu Jiuqing stared at this ancient tree. Amid the dense branches, a rabbit the size of a palm was staring right at them!

The rabbit was snow white, with red eyes. Its body brimmed with spiritual energy that merged with the moonlight, which was why they hadn't discovered it earlier.

"It's a moon rabbit!"

Gu Jiuqing blurted out.

There were moon rabbits on the moon - they were spiritual beasts, celestial spirits. Taking the moonlight as their foundation, they condensed into the form of moon rabbits. Every moon rabbit was a celestial spirit!!

Their lifespans were practically endless. Without needing to cultivate, they were comparable to pure yang paragons who had crossed the nine thunderclap tribulations.

The moon rabbit flickered and hid back into the forest. Sounds transmitted over from even further away.

The earlier noises had been made by the moon rabbit.

"The Not Old Spring connects through Not Old Peak. On this mountain peak, even celestial spirits like the moon rabbit can manifest!"

The spiritual essence of the Not Old Spring was too dense!

Gu Jiuqing and Lu Yuan set their minds at ease and continued walking.

Very soon, the two arrived at the summit of Not Old Peak.

A vast expanse of spring water!

Reflected on the mountaintop, it was crystal clear. The moonlight rippled across it, causing various visions to appear on the spring water's surface.

A naturally formed Dao rhyme emerged that could lead to the great yin Dao!!

Lu Yuan inwardly sighed. He cultivated the sun divine body, unless he switched to cultivating the yin divine body, this innate Dao rhyme had nothing to do with him.

Gu Jiuqing stared at the innate Dao rhyme!

The Dao rhyme formed naturally, then dissipated on its own. The formation of each Dao rhyme was different. If one could comprehend even a bit of it, their cultivation would soar.

But Gu Jiuqing did not linger. He stepped forward and arrived before the Not Old Spring.

He did not care whether the Not Old Spring held any dangers. His spiritual energy flowed out, forming a large arm that directly plunged into the Not Old Spring.

The spring water rippled, disrupting the innate Dao rhyme. But no matter how long he searched within the Not Old Spring, he could not find the eight divine weapons that Senior Martial Brother Liang Xiao had said he planted there.

"It's not here?"

Gu Jiuqing looked around blankly. Could it be that besides this Not Old Spring on the Eight Formations Ridge, there was another Not Old Spring?

Or could it be that the divine weapons Senior Martial Brother Liang Xiao planted under the Not Old Spring had been taken away by someone?

Lu Yuan circled around, his gaze gradually becoming horrified. He pointed at the surface of the Not Old Spring, where a blurry figure was gradually condensing into physical form!!

The spring water surface was sinking down.

This spring, not even two meters wide, as the blurry figure appeared, the spring water grew less and less, as if being absorbed by this blurry figure.

In just a few breaths of time, the spring water revealed the bottom, and the figure solidified into form.

It was a humanoid figure!!

It sat within the Not Old Spring. Under the Not Old Spring was only a thin layer of spring water left. Within this entire Not Old Spring, besides this humanoid living being that had condensed into form, there was nothing else.

"A humanoid! A living being!"

Lu Yuan's hair stood on end. The divine sword in his hand surged as he was so startled that he immediately summoned the sword on the spot. Seeing Gu Jiuqing motionless beside him, he hurriedly moved in front to protect him.

Not Old Peak!

The spring water flowing down from this mountain peak flowed in reverse, flying up to the summit and pouring into the humanoid living being.

"In the ancient texts, I found many mentions about the Not Old Spring," Gu Jiuqing's voice rang out calmly.

"It is said the Not Old Spring contains spiritual essence. There are tales of people who drank from the Not Old Spring and had their cultivation explosively increase, with their fleshly body, spiritual energy, and even divine soul all strengthening. Even more bizarre is that they could randomly comprehend various divine abilities and insights."

"Just now when I saw the innate Dao rhyme on the surface of the Not Old Spring, I had an inkling of this."

The humanoid living being did not move, still facing away from Gu Jiuqing.

Gu Jiuqing looked at this figure and exclaimed in amazement, "Who could have imagined the Not Old Spring was actually formed from a senior cultivator."

Huh?

Lu Yuan was dumbfounded.

The Not Old Spring was formed by this humanoid living being?

How was that possible?

There was not the slightest connection between the two.

After hearing Gu Jiuqing's words, the humanoid living being's figure suddenly trembled.

A piercing sound rang out.

"Kaka kaka————"

"Kaka kaka————————"

The humanoid living being swayed, absorbing all the Not Old Spring water on Not Old Peak.

"Old man..."

His voice sounded very strange, as if he hadn't spoken for a long time.

"Just now I was quite disappointed. Old man thought someone had mistakenly wandered into Not Old Peak."

The ancient voice lingered melodiously.

He was admitting to Gu Jiuqing's deduction!

An Eight Formations Elder!

It was this person.

Gu Jiuqing was astonished.

So Senior Martial Brother Liang Xiao had planted the eight divine weapons within this Eight Formations Elder.

The Not Old Spring was formed by the Eight Formations Elder.

Gu Jiuqing guessed that the Eight Formations Elder had been invaded by demonic energy. He fused together his spiritual energy, divine soul, fleshly body, and all his divine abilities.

Forming a pool of spring water!

This pool of spring water contained the Eight Formations Elder's entire cultivation.

The ancient texts recorded that anyone who drank from the Not Old Spring was actually plundering the Eight Formations Elder's cultivation and gaining his divine abilities!!

Then why didn't Senior Martial Brother Liang Xiao inform him of this beforehand?

"But when you condensed your spiritual energy, old man was even more disappointed."

Disappointed!

He was so disappointed.

How many years had he waited??

Five hundred years??

Six hundred years??

Or eight hundred years?

He only remembered that on Not Old Peak, he had cycled through reincarnation nine thousand times!!

His "body" flowed down from the mountaintop of Not Old Peak, then flowed back up from the mountain ridge to the summit.

After waiting all those years, the one who came was not him!!

The living being's disappointment actually triggered a celestial phenomenon.

The celestial phenomenon's range was not large, just enough to envelop Not Old Peak.

A chilling wind howled. A cold aura assailed them. The spiritual plants on the mountain instantly withered in an instant.

Tree after ancient tree transformed from lush green to rotten wood. The green leaves dulled and rapidly disintegrated.

Rays of light also fled from Not Old Peak in succession.

Those were the moon rabbits!!

Several moon rabbit celestial spirits escaped from Not Old Peak, fleeing far away.

The feeling of disappointment lingered, this ambiance permeating the fleshly body down to the soul.

Gu Jiuqing hurriedly summoned his spiritual energy to cleanse his body, dispelling the disappointment ambiance.

"Alas, why did the one who came not turn out to be him, but you instead!"

The "him" the living being spoke of should be referring to Senior Martial Brother Liang Xiao.

Since Senior Martial Brother Liang Xiao did not come, this peerless elder from the Primordial Era felt such disappointment.

Gu Jiuqing looked at the living being and respectfully bowed from afar.

"Senior Martial Brother instructed me to come to the Not Old Spring to retrieve the eight divine weapons he planted here long ago!"

The living being moved!

In the next moment, his entire body completely took form, transforming from the illusory into the real and tangible.

He slowly turned around.

A living being without any facial features stood in the position of the Not Old Spring.

"Back then, he did not put it that way when he took away my face."
