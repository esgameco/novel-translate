Talents!

Cultivators in the Sword Sect do morning and evening lessons every day, gathering purple mist and moonlight.

This is an unchanging principle passed down for millennia.

But the Blue Star people?

Each one is like Senior Martial Brother Liang Xiao, not bound by convention, opening up all kinds of new cultivation methods.

The Blue Star's spiritual energy is far inferior to the Primordial Chaos, but the future accomplishments of the Blue Star people will definitely surpass the Primordial Chaos cultivators!

Gu Jiuqing continued browsing the Linji Forum.

Dozens of new Martial Kings also appeared!

The Linji Forum was filled with joyous news.

"How much will my strength increase this time?"

Gu Jiuqing looked at the Linji Forum and couldn't help but wonder.

At the Radio Station Building.

"That person's home computer has been turned on."

Today the editor suddenly found me and told me to put the book on pricing.

Originally it was rising, but the reading numbers dropped these past few days.

I hesitated for a long time. At only 150,000 words, there's no need to price it yet, I could keep it free.

So the editor gave me a few days. I'm begging for more readers on Monday and Tuesday. If the reading numbers are high enough to reach the Three Rivers ranking, I can continue free for another week.

If not, I'll have to put it on pricing.

I beg all readers to support me, don't abandon it. Once priced, I guarantee at least 10,000 words of updates! It will definitely be exciting!
