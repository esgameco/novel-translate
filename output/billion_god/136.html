The Eastern Wilderness! Wind and Thunder Valley.

As a perilous area on the eastern edge of the Great Zhou Dynasty, the Wind and Thunder Valley was actually the safest place.

Because it was guarded by the Tao Sect.

The Tao Sect had three Celestial Venerables - Expounding Celestial Venerable, Intercepting Celestial Venerable, and Supreme Celestial Venerable! The Expounding Celestial Venerable emerged earliest, followed by the Intercepting Celestial Venerable, and then the Supreme Celestial Venerable.

Additionally, the strongest expert in the world, the Tao Ancestor, guarded this place.

For thousands of years, demons and monsters did not dare to forcefully enter the Wind and Thunder Valley.

This allowed the Eastern Wilderness to gradually prosper. The number of influential clans around the Wind and Thunder Valley increased, and there were even many Tribulation experts who came to the Wind and Thunder Valley to brave the Pure Yang Tribulation.

Thunderous lightning rolled across the sky. Elder Patriarchs at the Tribulation Realm ascended into the heavens, summoning divine thunderbolts.

This scene did not attract much attention.

Only...

This Tribulation lasted for an unusually long time.

When the first Tribulation thunderbolt struck, the disciples of the clans in the Wind and Thunder Valley paid no heed.

The same was true for the second and third Tribulation thunderbolts.

But after the fourth Tribulation thunderbolt fell, some cultivators finally noticed the abnormality.

They saw that deep within the Wind and Thunder Valley, dark clouds gathered, lightning flashed, and even human-shaped lightning appeared. Thunder dragons emerged, transforming into ancient giants pressing down on the world.

"What is this ominous sign? Why is the Tribulation manifestation within the Wind and Thunder Valley so terrifying?"

One after another, the Elder Patriarchs of the clans stood at a distance, observing this Tribulation.

"To summon the Tribulation and temper one's Primordial Spirit, one must face at least one Tribulation. But generally, Tribulation experts would summon two or three Tribulations."

"So how many Tribulations has the one deep in the Wind and Thunder Valley summoned now?"

"The sixth? Or the seventh?"

Under their watchful eyes, another Tribulation thunderbolt struck, shaking the Wind and Thunder Valley.

The ominous signs in the Wind and Thunder Valley attracted the observation of the clan Elders, and eventually even the Elders of the Long Life Clan were astonished.

"Just who is this Tribulation expert? To summon so many Tribulations at once?"

The Tribulation Realm!

It was divided into nine levels!

Meaning one had to pass through nine Tribulations and perfectly cultivate two hundred and fifty-six Pure Yang Thoughts.

And in each Tribulation, the number of thunderbolts summoned varied, depending on one's own state of cultivation.

So generally, when Tribulation experts faced their first Tribulation, they would test the waters by summoning one Tribulation thunderbolt and use their fleshly body, powers, divine abilities, and divine weapons to resist this divine thunderbolt.

After gauging the strength of the divine thunderbolt, they would summon the second one.

Generally speaking, the divine thunderbolts would grow stronger.

When facing the second divine thunderbolt, they would use their powers, divine abilities, and divine weapons to dispel most of the thunderbolt's force, allowing the remaining part to bathe their Primordial Spirit and accept the divine thunderbolt's baptism.

This required precise control. If not handled properly, the Primordial Spirit could be obliterated by the thunderbolt's force, resulting in death and the dissipation of one's cultivation.

"This Tribulation expert must have already passed through seven or eight Tribulations, otherwise it wouldn't be so fierce."

To summon so many divine thunderbolts, they believed he must be a Ninth Tribulation expert.

After several more divine thunderbolts struck deep within the Wind and Thunder Valley, the ominous signs finally dissipated.

The strange phenomena in the sky vanished, and the Wind and Thunder Valley became quiet again.

They wanted to enter the depths of the Wind and Thunder Valley to find this Tribulation Elder.

But after passing through a Tribulation, there would be a period of weakness. Although the Primordial Spirit would absorb Pure Yang energy, it would also enter its weakest state.

"Let's wait. When this Elder emerges from seclusion, we'll know who was braving the Tribulation."

The clan Elders and Long Life experts were all paying attention to that Tribulation expert deep within the Wind and Thunder Valley.

In the depths of the Wind and Thunder Valley.

Senior Brother Liang Xiao retrieved his Primordial Spirit from above his head.

This was his second Primordial Spirit. After bathing in the divine thunderbolts, half of its head had turned into a Pure Yang state. After a breath or two, a thought fell from the Primordial Spirit.

It was a Pure Yang Thought the size of two thumbs!

Liang Xiao, who was previously at the First Tribulation level, had now entered the Tribulation Realm again today.

He retrieved his Primordial Spirit, feeling a wave of weakness. He smiled faintly as the inner energy in his body circulated, cleansing his Primordial Spirit with his essence, energy, and spirit.

Visibly to the naked eye, Liang Xiao's Primordial Spirit grew larger, now over twenty-two zhang tall!

Liang Xiao's period of weakness passed in just a few breaths.

"In a few more days, I should be able to face the Second Tribulation."

Liang Xiao planned to greatly increase his cultivation within the Wind and Thunder Valley. He felt that the situation in the Wilderness was changing, and with his current strength, he was inferior even to a Pure Yang Patriarch!

"After passing through eight Tribulations with this Primordial Spirit, I can directly break through to the Ninth Tribulation Realm!"

With two Primordial Spirits, each containing one hundred and twenty-eight Pure Yang Thoughts, combining them would reach two hundred and fifty-six.

Allowing him to directly step into the Ninth Tribulation Realm.

Then he would use the Ninth Tribulation to directly cultivate to the Pure Yang Realm.

"Oh? You haven't left yet?"

A voice came from a distant mountain peak, entering Liang Xiao's ears.

Liang Xiao's eyelids twitched, and countless divine eyes appeared on his face.

He used his divine eyes to scan the heavens and earth, only seeing an old Taoist walking down step-by-step from the mountain.

It was an elderly Taoist monk wearing a grey robe, holding a Buddhist staff, with a Taoist crown on his head and a face full of wrinkles.

He looked at Liang Xiao with a hint of bewilderment.

"Alas, back then your father passed through all nine Tribulations in a single day, his Primordial Spirit became Pure Yang, his fleshly body Pure Yang, his inner energy Pure Yang, directly achieving a Three-in-One state. Who knows how many experts at the Deity Realm he alarmed."

He seemed to be recalling a scene from three thousand years ago.

The divine eyes on Liang Xiao's face trembled, his pupils constricted, and the tiny hand within his Immovable Demon King Eye retracted, not daring to move!

"I thought you might also achieve the Three-in-One state today."

"It seems you're far inferior to your father."

The old Taoist sighed, as if saying Liang Xiao had wasted his time.

If he had known earlier, he wouldn't have come down from the mountain.

"You know that old fellow?"

"It seems you're even older than him?"

Liang Xiao looked at this divine mountain, his countless eyes filled with dread. This was the first time he could not actively control his divine eyes!

These eyes seemed to have lost their effect, unable to perceive any information about the old Taoist.

He retracted all his divine eyes and used his Eight Trigrams Divine Eye to observe the old Taoist.

"Oh? The Eight Trigrams Divine Eye is on your face?"

The old Taoist finally showed a hint of surprise.

Liang Xiao did not respond to the old Taoist, instead saying on his own:

"The three Celestial Venerables of the Tao Sect - the Supreme Celestial Venerable took the title one thousand six hundred years ago, the Intercepting Celestial Venerable emerged three thousand six hundred years ago. Five thousand years ago, the Expounding Celestial Venerable walked the world."

"I don't know which Celestial Venerable you are? The Expounding Celestial Venerable? Or the Intercepting Celestial Venerable?"

For the first time in his life, Liang Xiao felt a sense of dread.

This old Taoist was at least a Two-in-One cultivator! No weaker than the Eight Trigrams Patriarch at his peak.

The old Taoist smiled faintly. He waved his hand towards the white clouds in the sky, and the clouds transformed into a giant lotus pedestal.

The old Taoist stepped onto the cloud pedestal and sat down. Seeing this, Liang Xiao also stepped into the void and sat across from the old Taoist on the white cloud.

"Hahaha————"

The old Taoist laughed heartily, "You know! In the world today, there are no more than two people who dare to sit before this old Taoist."

Liang Xiao's scalp went numb!

At this moment, he finally guessed who this old Taoist was.

"Who are the two?"

"The first is the Heavenly Empress of the Demonic Dao."

The old Taoist shook his head, "At that time, she had just obtained a branch of the Primordial Lunar Tree and wanted to challenge this old Taoist, so she sat before me."

He seemed to recall something, and the smile on his face grew more intense.

"Well, the Heavenly Empress had just achieved the Three-in-One state and obtained a branch of the Lunar Tree, so she was bold in her youth. But afterwards, this old Taoist invited her to the Wind and Thunder Valley many times, yet she never came once."

"This Heavenly Empress has lost her manners."

The old Taoist was reprimanding the Heavenly Empress for losing her manners!!

Though there were no ominous signs in the surrounding world, Liang Xiao felt his Mud Pill Palace tremble. The Primordial Spirit he had just tempered through the Tribulation was frozen in his sea of consciousness, unable to move!!

Though there were no signs of heavenly might, heaven and earth were still one. Every word and action was the order and rule of heaven and earth!

Liang Xiao sat upright, as obedient as a good child, not daring to speak recklessly.

"Could it be the Buddha Patriarch of the Buddhist Sect?"

The old Taoist shook his head.

"Then it must be the Ancestral Patriarch of the Sword Sect, the Primordial Sword!"

"That brat from the Sword Sect is too weak in cultivation. He wouldn't dare meet this old Taoist, let alone sit before me."

The old Taoist shook his head again.

"The Star Lord?"

"That old fellow from the Sword Sect?"

The old Taoist continued shaking his head, "The Star Lord doesn't dare face this old Taoist. He has the Future Stars Scripture and knows this old Taoist wants to kill him, so he doesn't dare meet with me alone!"

"Jiang Xingyun achieved the Three-in-One state in a single day three thousand years ago and stepped into the Deity Realm, but even he doesn't dare sit before me now!"

"Hmm, perhaps in another three thousand years, your father might dare to sit before me."

Every sentence from the old Taoist made Liang Xiao tremble.

After four sentences, Liang Xiao's back was completely drenched in sweat.

The old Taoist nodded, "After guessing for so long, you've finally got it right."

"It was over nine hundred years ago when that Zhou emperor came to see this old Taoist. But he didn't really sit before me, as the Zhou emperor knelt in front of this old Taoist."

The Zhou emperor!

Knelt before this old Taoist?!!

That was the founding emperor of the Great Zhou Dynasty, a Three-in-One expert.
