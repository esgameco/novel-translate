Gu Jiuqing stared at the spirit veins!

These past few days, he had pondered for a long time.

"I'm still young now, and my cultivation is not enough. Although the Luo Shen is not bad, it's not something I can subdue at the moment!"

Thinking about the Luo Shen, Gu Jiuqing only felt a bit flustered.

"Injecting these two spirit veins into the Blue Star is the best choice."

Yes!

Gu Jiuqing had already made his decision.

He was going to inject these two spirit veins into the Blue Star.

He sat cross-legged, entering meditation and contemplation. The Dao fruit from the Mud Bead Palace appeared.

These past few days, the scenes within the Dao fruit had been constantly evolving. They were the insights and cultivation realizations from the 800,000 people of Linjiang practicing the Dragon and Elephant Skill and the Art of Drawing Swords, all condensing into the Dao fruit!

Gu Jiuqing did not immediately absorb the cultivation and insights.

He had completed eight Sheddings of Mortality and wanted the ninth Shedding of Mortality, which required a qualitative change!

"It's almost here, it's almost here. As long as I inject the resources I've obtained this time into the Blue Star, my strength will reach new heights in a short period of time."

Gu Jiuqing stretched out his hand, grabbed the two jade boxes, and activated the Dao fruit. His vision went black, and then the world became clear again.

The Blue Star!

Gu Jiuqing opened his eyes and appeared in the bedroom. On his bed, there were two additional jade boxes, which were the ones containing the two spirit veins.

Releasing the spirit veins was simple. Gu Jiuqing didn't care where these two spirit veins would end up.

He got up and opened the jade boxes, casually tearing apart the talisman seals inside!

Without the talisman seals suppressing them, the two spirit veins instantly broke free from their seals and slithered out of the window in streaks of light.

Flashes of light flickered, like meteors streaking across Linjiang. On this day, especially in this neighborhood, people saw two unusually flickering rays of light.

However, these two rays of light flashed and disappeared into the vast night sky in an instant.

After completing all this, Gu Jiuqing returned to the bedroom and opened his computer.

The Blue Star, 8 PM. This was the first time Gu Jiuqing had descended to the Blue Star at this time. He first opened the Linjiang Forum and browsed it casually, then opened the search bar.

"Huh? It can still be used?"

Wasn't Linjiang sealed off?

How was he still able to search?

[Buzhou Mountain, before the arrival of the high martial artists, was a mythical mountain in the legends of the Blue Star, said to be the spine of the primordial god Pangu transformed.]

As Gu Jiuqing read the introduction of Buzhou Mountain, he became more and more astonished.

After obtaining the Buzhou Forging Method from the Qu Family, Gu Jiuqing had memorized this book of scriptures verbatim, greatly enhancing his understanding of cultivation.
