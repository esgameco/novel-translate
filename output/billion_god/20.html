Below Sword Gate Pass, many young men from the northern lands showed looks of surprise and doubt. They had never heard of the Seal Cultivator or Bright Court, nor did they know who the Venerable Taishang Elder was.

But!

No matter where, the position of Venerable Taishang Elder was above ordinary elders. The status of this Seal Cultivator was much higher than even those Sword Immortals.

Gu Jiuqing did not hesitate and bowed to the white-robed Sword Immortal.

"Gu Jiuqing is willing!"

The status of a Seal Cultivator was the highest level in the Sword Sect.

Being accepted as a disciple under a Seal Cultivator would make his future actions much more convenient.

With this Venerable Taishang Elder as his backer, even the old ancestor of the Qu family would not dare act against him.

The white-robed Sword Immortal smiled slightly, "Good, good, from now on you will be the second disciple of the Bright Court Sword Furnace. Your senior martial brother will take you back to the Bright Court!"

Gu Jiuqing wanted to bid farewell to the steward Xie, but the white-robed senior martial brother had already acted.

A heaven-spanning talisman fell, its divine light forming a golden bridge laid at Gu Jiuqing's feet. A thread of brilliance from the divine bridge entered Gu Jiuqing's body.

Gu Jiuqing involuntarily stepped onto the divine bridge, and in just a few steps, he had crossed the void and arrived beside his white-robed senior martial brother.

A force carried Gu Jiuqing, and atop a flying craft, he descended into the Sword Sect's divine mountain.

When observing the Sword Sect's divine mountain from outside Sword Gate Pass, one could only see its magnificence. But when Gu Jiuqing flew into the Sword Sect, he discovered the true grandeur of the divine mountain.

The divine mountain was immense, containing worlds within, with small realms cultivating inside the Sword Sect. At first glance, there were rays of luminous clouds crisscrossing the azure sky, as well as numerous floating islands suspended around, and many mountain peaks emanating terrifying sword auras.

This was the scene of the highest reaches of the Sword Sect.

Gu Jiuqing looked down at the base of the divine mountain!

That was where the outer court disciples and inner court disciples dwelled. The outer courtyard buildings were interconnected, while the inner courtyards were tens of miles apart. Some inner court disciples had even excavated cave abodes to live in and cultivate.

There were disciples cultivating with dragon and elephant auras above their heads, tempering their fleshly bodies, or transforming into Blazing Demon forms. Some disciples inhaled the spiritual qi of heaven and earth, refining it into their own magic powers!

Other disciples forged divine bodies, crafting invincible ones, becoming battle gods!

The Shedding Mortality Realm, Magic Power Realm, and Divine Body Realm cultivators were the most numerous inside the Sword Sect.

Soon, the two courtyards below could no longer be seen clearly as Gu Jiuqing had arrived above the divine mountain's peak.

There were marks of divine abilities, sites of great battles, and traces left behind by divine weapons, all clearly visible.

"These are the marks of a great battle left behind when the Primordial Demon Tribe tried to invade the northern lands and fought my Sword Sect three thousand years ago," the white-robed Sword Immortal sighed.

"Three thousand years ago, the Sect Master at the time joined the Sword Sect. Within nine days of the great test, he comprehended a supreme sword intent and was accepted as a disciple by the Ancestral Patriarch."

This was part of the Sword Sect's history that Gu Jiuqing did not know before. Now that he was also a disciple, he had to engrave this history in his heart.

The Sword Sect's Ancestral Patriarch was an almost godly existence who had only suffered an injury ten thousand years ago, with his great tribulation coming three thousand years ago.

When the demons outside Sword Gate Pass learned of this, they launched a massive invasion of the Sword Sect, causing devastation to the living beings of the northern lands. Even the Great Zhou Dynasty sent a million troops to guard the area.

In this battle, although the Sword Sect was victorious, the Ancestral Patriarch passed away prematurely. During this period, the Sect Master at the time shone brilliantly, slaying demons and butchering local deities. The number of demons, semi-deities, and nature spirits killed by his hands was countless.

"Do you see this handprint on that mountain peak over there?"

Before them, an isolated mountain peak had an enormous black handprint, dozens of feet across.

"This is the mark left behind when the Sect Master at the time slew a nature spirit with a single palm strike."

The Sword Sect Master!

Surpassing the previous, more formidable than the Ancestral Patriarch's power.

But!

Why did such a powerful existence not become a deity?

Gu Jiuqing voiced his doubt.

The white-robed Sword Immortal shook his head, "Becoming a deity is too difficult. Our Sword Sect has three Sword Sages who have cultivated to the final realm, even completing the cultivation of all realms, but they still cannot reach the deity realm."

Gu Jiuqing wanted to inquire further, but the white-robed Sword Immortal spoke again.

"Junior Martial Brother, becoming a deity is not something I can attain, but you have the prospect of becoming one. When the Sect Master returns, he will personally tell you about this!"

"Or if Master awakens, Master will also tell you."

What?

Master?

That never-seen Master was also an almost godly powerful figure?

The sword furnace!

A sword furnace contains countless sword intents.

Like the one before them, shining brightly, as if it were a celestial furnace!

Simply put, it was a blazing fire pile in the sky, shaped like a sword, over a hundred meters tall.
