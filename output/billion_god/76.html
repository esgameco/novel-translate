Inside the exam hall, no one knew when Teacher Le Qing and Teacher Da Yang had stood by the window. They were pointing at the strange sight in the distant sky.

It seemed they could see the scene of the martial arts competition.

"I think I just saw a high school sophomore student go up to participate, but got slapped down with a palm strike."

"Teacher Da Yang, that's nothing. I just saw my little niece enter the arena. Alas, before the match even started, she got so scared she burst into tears."

"Huh? Teacher Le Qing, you mean your little niece who is still in middle school?"

"Yes, which other niece do I have? I only have one."

Teacher Da Yang chuckled, "My granddaughter is seven this year and has started practicing Dragon and Elephant Skill. I reckon in six years at most, she'll become a martial artist."

The two suddenly fell silent.

This era!

It will become the era of the youth!

High school martial artists will be everywhere. Middle school martial artists will be commonly seen. Even elementary student martial artists will become the norm.

The two turned around at the same time, looking towards the exam hall.

It turned out to be the 1.9 meter tall exam invigilator glaring at them.

Teacher Da Yang and Teacher Le Qing left the window and walked back to the front and back of the classroom.

They showed no respect at all towards this invigilator from the provincial capital.

Rude!

Arrogant!

When had Lu Jiuling ever seen such rude proctoring teachers?

This was the college entrance exam! Yet these two teachers from Linji High were chatting in the exam hall?

Huh?

No, wait!

The grandmasters had already completed refining the skin, flesh and bones, and started refining the tendons. Once the tendons were refined, making the bones and tendons hum in unison, they could enter the Transformation Realm.

The autumn wind had yet to stir, but the cicadas had already sensed it. Mosquitoes could not land on their bodies, and water could only submerge up to their knees. At the peak grandmaster level, they could even tread on water.

With one glance, Lu Jiuling sensed the subtle state of the two Linji teachers.

"Grandmasters!"

They were actually two grandmasters??

The high school teachers in Linji City were two grandmasters??

They were grandmasters! Did they really need to teach at a high school?

Lu Jiuling was stunned. The events from earlier had already left his mind.

He walked over to the side of this female student again.

"Crazy, this world must have gone crazy."

Lu Jiuling was seeing the exam questions for the first time too.

When he saw the last big question, he paused for a moment.

Lu Jiuling was a talented martial arts prodigy. In high school sophomore year, he had already cultivated into a martial artist, entering university one year early as a freshman at Jiangnan University.

In the fourth year, he completed refining the flesh and started refining the bones, becoming a Master.

A Master could teach at a university as a lecturer.

Three years later, Lu Jiuling finished refining his bones and began refining his tendons!

Until now, he was still stuck at the Tendon Refining Realm, as it was too difficult for a grandmaster to break through to become a Martial Monarch.

He had been stuck for so long, he didn't know if he would ever reach the Organ Refining Realm in his lifetime.

"The path of martial arts has six realms, which is enough!"

For Lu Jiuling, whether the path of martial arts had an end or not was the same.

But then!

He was stunned to see this student casually extending the path of martial arts further.

Something called the Human Immortal Realm, which was above the martial artist realm. It was further divided into the Earth Immortal and True Earth Immortal sub-realms.

The human spine was called the Imperfect Mountain, and by opening the Imperfect Mountain for cultivation, one could refine a divine body.

As for Earth Immortals, they would open the 365 spirit apertures within the body, corresponding to the number of stars in the heavens!

Crazy! This must be crazy!

The body's aperture points have not even been proven, yet this high school senior directly stated the definite number of 365 spirit apertures in the body.

He really wanted to call it nonsense.

But!

This student actually started writing down the cultivation method for the Earth Immortal Realm.

He hurriedly walked over to Yang Miaoling's side. By the time he stood there, Yang Miaoling had already finished writing the Golden Crow Divine Body cultivation method.

"Visualize the Great Sun Golden Crow, gather the essence of the Great Sun, condense the origin, and open the first vertebra of the human body."

This was a complete cultivation method.

After writing the Golden Crow Divine Body method, this student even started writing the method to open the 365 Heavenly Spirit Apertures!

The opening methods for 333 spirit apertures were densely written across the entire exam paper, even covering the draft paper!

In the end, Yang Miaoling wrote the opening methods for the last few spirit apertures on the first page of the exam paper.

Rustle——————

She straightened the exam papers, put down her pen, looked at the clock on the podium which showed there was still half an hour left, just enough time to submit.

"Teacher, I want to submit my papers."

Submit?

What?

Lu Jiuling stared blankly at the black words on the white papers.

That's right, he was proctoring an exam right now.

And written on the exam papers were the shockingly brilliant Golden Crow Divine Body cultivation method and the Heavenly Spirit Aperture Opening method - the path of martial arts extended further! The Earth Immortal, True Earth Immortal realms were the future path!

He had even started to believe that the path of martial arts had been successfully continued!

Until Yang Miaoling spoke up, he finally snapped out of it.

"Teacher, I also want to submit!"

"Me too!"

One voice after another almost rang out at the same time.

One student after another stood up, placed their exam papers on the podium, and walked out of the exam hall.

Lu Jiuling!

He was still a bit dazed.

Teacher Le Qing organized the papers on the podium. By the time Lu Jiuling fully regained his senses, the entire exam hall was empty of students. Even those two Linji teachers had finished sealing and packing up the exam papers.

It was over?
