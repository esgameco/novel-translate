The original dantian, under the scouring of the divine power, was constantly shaken open. The three-zhang tall Zhongmiao Gate was enveloped by divine power.

Beneath this fully opened Zhongmiao Gate, Gu Jiuqing's thoughts were scoured into a vague form.

One breath of time!

Two breaths of time!

By the third breath of time!

This thought took a step and entered the Zhongmiao Gate.

The divine power was like a river, as if treading upon the long river of years. Gu Jiuqing directly walked into the Zhongmiao Gate.

Infinite and endless!

Boundless and unrestricted!

Immeasurable!

Various profound truths enveloped his entire body, and Gu Jiuqing's thoughts also became increasingly solidified.

"Is this the Zhongmiao Gate within the Zhongmiao Gate?"

This gate was covered in rust stains and looked exactly the same as the first Zhongmiao Gate Gu Jiuqing saw, also three-zhang tall.

Around this gate, there was no flow of divine power.

Gu Jiuqing stared at this gate for a long time before looking at the scene within the Zhongmiao Gate. Apart from the infinite and endless divine power surging into the Zhongmiao Gate and flowing into the dantian, there were no other strange sights!

The divine power seemed to appear out of thin air and pour into the dantian!

Gu Jiuqing wanted to take a step forward to observe this ancient gate up close. As he took a step, going against the flow, his entire thought trembled.

The vague figure began to split apart!

An immense pressure fell down!

He hurriedly stopped his steps and looked at the Zhongmiao Gate in shock.

"With my current strength, I can't even get close to the second Zhongmiao Gate!"

Gu Jiuqing's thoughts dissipated, passing through Buzhou Mountain from within the dantian, all the way to the thirty-fourth vertebra, and surging into the Niyuan Palace.

[Xitianji is to release divine power from within the Zhongmiao Gate to strengthen oneself. The more Zhongmiao Gates that are opened, the more divine power can be condensed. I will fully push open the first Zhongmiao Gate! The cultivation realm of divine power should be the first.]

[Apart from this, one can also borrow external forces to condense divine power, refining one's vital essence or elixirs and great medicines into divine power. Due to special reasons, I cannot cultivate divine power by relying on external objects. I can only rely on the Zhongmiao Gate or draw in the divine power of the people of the Azure Star.]

[The cultivation of the divine power realm is divided into three levels: gaseous, liquid, and solid states. Gaseous divine power in the dantian, liquid divine power in the dantian, and solid divine power in the dantian! Upon reaching the third level, one can condense a Yuandan. The lowest is a one-turn Yuandan, and the highest is a nine-turn Yuandan.]

But!

Gu Jiuqing looked inward at his dantian, and the divine power flowed back like seawater, while others leaked out from the Zhongmiao Gate in strands and threads. He, on the other hand, had an entire expanse flowing out!

Gu Jiuqing's divine power condensed into a liquid state, and he could now attempt to condense a Yuandan.

"Has the junior brother not yet accomplished Xitianji? So much time has passed."

"Has the junior brother already walked the path of the Fire Demon? If he has walked the path of demonic cultivation, wouldn't this fleshly body be wasted? We should slice it up while the junior brother's fleshly body has not yet dispersed its essence."

"It's been ten days now, the tenth day, and the junior brother has still not awakened?"

"..."

In the cave dwelling, Gu Jiuqing sat cross-legged on the ground.

Before him, a white-robed young master stared fixedly at Gu Jiuqing.

"Why don't we first slice off one of the junior brother's fingers? With the junior brother's powerful fleshly body, it will take no more than three days for him to regrow a finger."

"Alas, that won't do. If the master emerges from seclusion and learns that I sliced off one of the junior brother's fingers, he will definitely throw me into the Sword Furnace!"

Senior Brother Liang Xiao looked troubled, revealing a pained expression.

"Junior brother! You're awake!"

Senior Brother Liang Xiao rose from a ten-thousand-year ice coffin and landed before Gu Jiuqing.
