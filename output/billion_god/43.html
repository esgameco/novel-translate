Some had even comprehended sword forces!

Within the Dao fruit, the insights into the Unsheathing Sword Technique mutually verified each other, discarding the false and retaining the true.

Ancestral teachings are insufficient, the heavenly Dao is not to be feared.

In the sword schools, a full Sword Immortal was one who had comprehended one complete strand of sword intent.

Comprehending five portions of a sword intent was considered a half-step Sword Immortal.

In the true transmissions of the sword schools, even those elite disciples who cultivated the Sword Dao for hundreds or even thousands of years were mostly stuck at the half-step Sword Immortal level!

Gu Jiuqing had cultivated the Sword Dao for less than a month and had already become a half-step Sword Immortal. If this cultivation speed was spread, it would undoubtedly cement his status as the paramount figure of the Sword Dao.

"And there's still my heaven-slicing sword intent!"

Gu Jiuqing could sense the richness of the sword intent in his palm. Perhaps after some more time, the heaven-slicing sword intent would elevate another grade.

Surpassing the upper-rank sword intent?

Gu Jiuqing didn't know what level that would be, but it seemed it would shake the sword schools and allow him to obtain more resources.

The sword intent in his palm continued slowly elevating. The brilliant heaven-slicing sword intent flowed through his five fingers, graceful like water, gentle like a spring breeze. Gu Jiuqing extended his hand, and the five portions of sword intent in his palm instantly soared, transforming into a spiritual sword.

"This spiritual sword represents all my insights into the Unsheathing Sword Technique!"

Once the sword intent was damaged, Gu Jiuqing's sword cultivation would plummet.

Hence the existence of sword pills!

Refining the sword pill with one's essence, nourishing the sword pill with one's life force.

Using the hard materials of precious swords to protect the sword intent - this was the origin of the flying sword technique.

The insights of seventy million people of the Blue Star had been absorbed by Gu Jiuqing alone.

The sword intent in his hands had also elevated to the peak of five portions, just a hairbreadth away from six portions of sword intent.

The soaring sword intent dissipated the translucent spiritual sword.

Gu Jiuqing sighed, "Finally, I've refined all the cultivation and insights accumulated in the Dao fruit."

This elevation in his strength allowed Gu Jiuqing to stand at new heights.

Five hundred strands of Dragon Elephant Force, three innate celestial phenomena, the half-step Sword Immortal realm of five portions of sword intent, having shed the mortal form ten times!

Yet currently Gu Jiuqing was only at the Form Shedding realm, having not even cultivated true energy.
