The first emperor of the Great Zhou dynasty excavated nine tripod cauldrons from ancient ruins.

He divided the realm into nine provinces based on the nine cauldrons, forming the current nine states.

Qing Province was in the north, connected to the Northern Lands.

Close to the Northern Lands, there was a great long-living aristocratic family, the Tian Mu Dafeng Family!

"The Tian Mu Dafeng Family has an ancient lineage, much older than even my Lu Family's inheritance. It is said that the old ancestor of the Tian Mu Dafeng Family attained the Pure Yang realm during the Primordial Chaos period, but later died in the chaos three thousand years ago."

Three thousand years ago, that chaos occurred in the Northern Lands, affecting the nearby Qing Province as well. One supreme ancient ancestor after another assisted the Sword Sect in blocking demons from entering the nine provinces.

The Tian Mu Dafeng Family perished in this chaos.

Three thousand years later, the Dafeng Family still had not recovered its former peak.

"The Dafeng Family cultivates the Wind Spirit Body, Thunder Wind Spirit Body, and various wind-attribute spirit bodies. Combined with their unique Thunder Refinement methods, their powers are profound, on par with disciples of the Xingchen Court!"

The Wind Thunder Divine Chariot hovered in the air, with the Tian Mu Immortal Realm right below!

The immortal realm floated three feet off the ground, shrouded in immortal mist clinging to the mountains. A vast fog enveloped the heavens, with a waterfall cascading down from the main peak of the Tian Mu Immortal Realm.

This waterfall was the spiritual vein of the Tian Mu Immortal Realm!

"Such dense spiritual qi!"

Gu Jiuqing exclaimed in surprise.

This single spiritual vein was three times denser than the two spiritual veins Gu Linglong had given him combined.

Just this spiritual vein alone could allow the Dafeng Family to nurture peerless talents.

The flying horse carriage descended outside the Tian Mu Immortal Realm, and the two arrived beneath the heavenly gate!

The contours of a gate vaguely took shape, but when Gu Jiuqing approached the heavenly gate of the Tian Mu Immortal Realm, there were no Dafeng Family members guarding it.

The heavenly gate stood wide open, allowing a view into the immortal realm's scenery.

Mountains and rivers surged, indistinguishable from the swirling mists as to whether it was the mists or terrain undulating.

There were also palaces and pavilions soaring amidst the clouds, like heavenly immortal palaces!

Gu Jiuqing frowned. "Are long-living aristocratic families so overconfident that they don't even need to guard their gates?"

Lu Yuan shook his head. As a long-living aristocratic family that even possessed an immortal realm, the Dafeng Family was only slightly weaker than the Four Great Sects and the Great Zhou imperial clan. Even the Nine Provinces Governor's forces were comparable to such aristocratic families.

Lu Yuan felt a bit displeased!

His Senior Brother Gu had arrived, so even though the Dafeng was a long-living family, they should still come out to pay respects.

He stepped forward, his blood essence undulating. His entire body was like an inflated ball, gathering one of the eight primordial origins.

"Where is the Tian Mu Dafeng Family, the long-living aristocrats?"

"A true transmitter of the Sword Sect has graced the Dafeng Family, yet no one from the family has come to welcome us?"

Lu Yuan sounded arrogant, like a dog relying on its master's power.

But!

This was indeed the manner a true transmitter of the Sword Sect should conduct themselves. Lu Yuan did not exaggerate at all, if anything he was being cautious.

When a true transmitter of the Sword Sect traveled the realm, in the Great Zhou they represented the continuation of the Sword Sect's lineage.

Aside from showing some respect to the Great Zhou imperial clan and the other Three Sects, Sword Sect disciples did not need to show deference to anyone else.

Whoosh——————

This sound echoed through the Tian Mu Immortal Realm, dispersing some of the clouds.

After three breaths of time, finally a Dafeng Family member flew towards Gu Jiuqing and the others.

"Something's not right!"

"Kid, be careful. The Tian Mu Dafeng is an ancient aristocratic family that has produced many enlightened immortals. I've met that old Dafeng ancestor before - a very talented Pure Yang ancestor."

"Though weaker than me, he also attained a pure yang yuanshen. His family could not have declined so much as to not even have a gatekeeper."

The Eight Primordial Origins Elder from the Mud Pill Palace warned.

Lu Yuan did not care, but still transmitted the message to Senior Brother Gu.

The person flew unsteadily through the mountains, swaying from side to side as if just learning to fly and not used to controlling their fleshly body, wasting a lot of spiritual power scattering into the world.

This Dafeng member looked even younger than Gu Jiuqing, but had already cultivated to the Spiritual Power Realm.

"Qing Yang of the Dafeng Family greets the true transmitter of the Sword Sect!"

Qing Yang?

Lu Yuan was startled. "Senior Brother Gu, this is Qing Yang, the younger brother of Senior Brother Feng!"

Senior Brother Feng, the Sword Immortal Feng Ruyi, was originally from the Qing Province's Tian Mu Dafeng Family.

So this Qing Yang before them was over a hundred years old as well, right?

As if seeing Gu Jiuqing's surprise, Qing Yang openly said, "But I'm not as the senior imagines."

He made a gesture of invitation, leading Gu Jiuqing and Lu Yuan into the heavenly gate.

The heavenly gate's formation had long since disappeared, but this was their territory, so Gu Jiuqing and Lu Yuan could not simply enter without the host's permission.

"I am from the Dafeng Family's ninth branch, twelfth sub-branch. Feng Ruyi is my elder brother, but his father is not my father."

Oh?

Gu Jiuqing immediately understood.

So Senior Brother Feng and this Qing Yang had the same mother, but she had remarried, likely to another Dafeng Family member.

Qing Yang flew awkwardly towards the Tian Mu Immortal Realm, but had recovered to normal.

Celestial clouds of qi rippled underfoot, and there were eight immortal cavern worlds standing atop the mountain peaks.

"These are the eight great immortal caverns of the Tian Mu realm, self-contained worlds brimming with dense spiritual qi - the inheritance grounds of my Dafeng Family."

Qing Yang did not hide anything, looking at Gu Jiuqing's puzzled expression.

"What brought the two senior sword cultivators to my Dafeng Family today?"

"Do you need me to notify the clan elders?"

As a disciple of the Sword Sect, he could only lead them into the Tian Mu Immortal Realm.

"It's nothing major, we just wanted to borrow your Dafeng Family's ancient teleportation array to travel toYu Province."

Going from the Tian Mu Dafeng Family to Yu Province, then using Yu Province's teleportation array to reach Youzhou Province would be a shorter route, saving travel time even though bypassing one province.

Upon hearing this, Qing Yang laughed heartily.

"So you wish to use my Dafeng Family's ancient teleportation array? If the two senior sword cultivators want to use it, then please do. There's no need to report this to the clan elders."

Qing Yang pointed to one of the immortal caverns in the mountains below.

"There are eight great immortal caverns in my Dafeng Family's Tian Mu Immortal Realm, named after the seasons, sun, moon and stars."

"The ancient teleportation array is inside the Star Cavern!"

The eight immortal caverns were arranged in order, with the Star Cavern being the last one.

This cavern world was vast, with rippling void space around it forming a huge vacuum zone. Entering it would lead into the Star Cavern.

Gu Jiuqing and Lu Yuan followed behind Qing Yang, but they also sensed something amiss.

"We haven't seen any other Dafeng Family members on the way here."

"Senior Brother, the Eight Primordial Origins Elder just told me that the eight immortal caverns of the Dafeng Family are not named after the seasons, sun, moon and stars, but rather correspond to the eight primordial origins of Heaven, Earth, Water, Fire, Mountain, Wind, Thunder and Marsh. The Pure Yang ancestor of the Dafeng Family lost a debate to the Eight Primordial Origins Elder long ago."

After that debate loss, the Dafeng ancestor opened these eight immortal caverns named after the eight primordial origins, intending for his disciples to transcend the Eight Primordial Origins Elder's realm!

That was the origin of these eight caverns.

Yet Qing Yang was from the Dafeng Family, how could he not know even this basic background?

Gu Jiuqing's expression remained calm as he followed Qing Yang into the final immortal cavern.

Void ripples, this cavern world was self-contained.

Though not large, the dense spiritual qi here surpassed even the sword peaks.

Not far away, an ancient teleportation array flickered with light.

"Senior sword cultivators, this is my Dafeng Family's ancient teleportation array."

Qing Yang pointed to the ancient array, then asked in puzzlement, "May I ask the names of the two senior cultivators? After you use the teleportation array, Qing Yang can report it to the clan elders."

Lu Yuan raised his brows.

It really was an ancient teleportation array!

Perhaps they had overthought things?

The Dafeng Family's inheritance was so ancient, it wasn't impossible for someone to intentionally conceal the origins of the eight primordial caverns.

"This is my Sword Sect's True Transmitter Gu Jiuqing, the current Sword Dao's paramount."

Lu Yuan introduced Gu Jiuqing but did not introduce himself.

Upon hearing this, Qing Yang's expression changed.

He had heard of Gu Jiuqing's name before.

"So it's the senior from Mingtingfeng Peak!"

Qing Yang immediately bowed to Gu Jiuqing. "My apologies!"

Qing Yang remained courteous and gracious, a redeeming quality of this aristocratic family member.

Just as Gu Jiuqing was about to step onto the teleportation array, Qing Yang blocked his path.

"What's this? The paramount of the Sword Dao looks down on me?"

"Why not ask me first?"

Qing Yang's expression turned strange, a hint of a smile in his eyes as he stared at Gu Jiuqing.

"Whoosh——————"

At this moment!

Within the eighth immortal cavern, the heavens and earth transformed as countless rays of light dissipated into a vast black fog that swept over, enveloping everything in a dense obscuring mist. Qing Yang's figure dissolved!

A human skin sloughed off Qing Yang's body, revealing the form of a demon underneath.

The upper body of this demon was human-like, while the lower body was shrouded in swirling smoke forming "legs".

It had horns on its head, long black hair billowing behind like a cape.

"A demon!"

Lu Yuan blurted out in shock!

They had encountered a demon within the Great Zhou's borders?

"This is impossible! How could there be demons inside the Nine Provinces?"

The Four Great Sects guarded the four desolate regions, blocking demons from the Wilderness from entering the Nine Provinces.

Nine hundred years ago, when demons suddenly appeared in the Nine Provinces, they were all slain by the Dao Ancestors and Buddha Ancestors joining forces.
