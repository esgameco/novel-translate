Chapter 139

Here is my full translation of the Chinese excerpt to English:

This ambush!

It was targeting the supreme leader of the sect!

So the question arises.

Will this third-ranked expert in the world be able to overcome this killing catastrophe?

If he cannot make it through, all the disciples of the Sword Sect will die.

It's not just one or two, nor is it one hundred or one thousand, but all the disciples up and down the Sword Sect's sacred mountain will be massacred!

Gu Jiuqing's heart felt heavy.

His figure appeared in Junzhou Prefecture, the closest city to the Zhendou Pass.

"Baixed!"

The true disciples of the Sword Sect travel the world in order to compete in the Four Sects' Grand Competition, so most are in the Youzhou region, adjacent to the ancient ruins.

As for the inner door disciples of the Sword Sect, they travel in Qingzhou and Junzhou when walking the world.

These two provinces are closest to the Sword Sect, so they don't need to go that far. Walking around in these two provinces is considered completing the inner door trial.

Therefore, the ancient city of Baixed is the final stop for the inner door disciples of the Sword Sect.

The name "Baixed" comes from a hundred years ago when, according to legend, there was a young man in the ancient city who admired the Jixia Academy but had no way to study there.

One day, the grand minister of Jixia Academy, Banshannuo, happened to pass through this place and met the young man.

Banshannuo, in his capacity as the grand minister, recommended the young man to enter the academy.

The young man expressed a hundred thanks, hence the ancient city got its name Baixed.

And this young man was Xuantianji!

With such a story, the ancient city attracts many literati and scholars who gather in Baixed City, seeking a great mentor.

Therefore, apart from Junzhou Prefecture, Baixed City is the most prosperous city in Junzhou.

Cultivators compete here, and literati compose here.

The inner door disciples of the Sword Sect also come here to compete against heroes from all over.

But today!

An anomaly occurred on the arena stage.

"It's bad, it's bad."

"A Sword Sect disciple was killed!"

This voice in the ancient city stirred up towering waves, causing the commoners on the streets to shudder, while some cultivators hurried towards the arena.

The arena stage in Baixed City was specially built for cultivators.

Cultivators could compete on the stage to determine superiority, but usually no one would cause loss of life.

Let alone causing the death of a Sword Sect disciple.

That's the Sword Sect, one of the four great sects in the world. No matter who competes against a Sword Sect disciple, they would be extremely cautious.

On the huge arena stage in the center of Baixed City, a corpse lay there.

It was a disciple of the Sword Sect!

This Sword Sect disciple had a precious sword pierced through his chest, with sword marks on it, but it was not activated!

Sword Sect disciples are masters of the sword, yet this one died by the sword of another sword cultivator.

This is a tremendous humiliation.

"Tsk tsk, lacking in skill, unable to withstand even one of my sword strikes."

"Is this what a Sword Sect disciple is?"

"Swish ————————"

The sword cultivator on the corner of the stage waved his hand, retrieving the precious sword from the chest of the Sword Sect disciple into his palm.

Strands of sword intent drifted in the air.

One strand of sword intent!

Two strands of sword intent!

Three strands of sword intent!

Up to six strands of sword intent before stopping.

A half-step Sword Immortal stood on the stage, looking around. Those who met his gaze quickly averted their eyes, not daring to look directly at him.

"This person seems to be Taibai Xian from the Taibai Clan of Junzhou!"

"The Taibai Clan? Isn't that tens of thousands of miles away from Baixed City? How did such a person come to Baixed City?"

"Ah, this is uncertain. I've heard that disciples from other provincial clans have also rushed to Junzhou, and incidents like today's have happened not just once or twice in other cities!"

"What? You mean Sword Sect disciples have been killed by other cultivators? And not just one or two?"

Heavens!

This news is too shocking.

Sword Sect disciples were actually killed in the ancient city?

And more than just one or two cases.

"Hmph——————"

A sword cultivator in the crowd let out a cold snort.

The one dead on the stage was his good friend!

Yue Qingfeng's expression was unsightly.

When the true disciples of the Sword Sect descended the mountain to compete for the Dragon and Tiger Ranking, these inner door disciples also took the opportunity to leave the mountain early.

Yue Qingfeng joined the Sword Sect many years ago and has cultivated to the Unbounded Realm!

His friend Tusanming had similar cultivation, both having refined fifteen vertebrae to the Unbounded Realm.

"Tusanming!"

"Is dead!"

He recalled the bits and pieces of when he and his brother joined the Sword Sect, studying the Celestial Snake Shedding Technique under the mountain, shedding mortal skin twice a day, completing four sheddings in nine days to successfully join the inner door!

At that time, they were hailed as the twin prodigies of the Sword Sect!

But in the Sword Sect, there were too many so-called "twin prodigies" like them.

Decades passed, and they were still stuck in the Unbounded Realm, with no hope of reaching the Celestial Palace!

This time they descended the mountain, Yue Qingfeng and Tusanming planned to test their swords against the world, regaining their confidence from the past through repeated sword battles.

After leaving the mountain, they found that they had become the former peerless geniuses of yesteryear.

Wielding their swords, no one could match them!

They fought eighty bouts in Baixed City and won all eighty.

Only in this eighty-first bout did they lose!

Tusanming even died by the opponent's sword.

"Junior Brother Ye, go and retrieve Senior Brother Tu's corpse!"

"We're returning to the Sword Sect."

Yue Qingfeng's voice was cold. He only knew that Tusanming was dead, and he wanted to take Tusanming's body back to the Sword Sect to be buried on the sacred mountain.

Ye Fei emerged from the crowd. He didn't dare look at this Taibai Xian again.

Taibai Xian, ah, was even more powerful than his senior brothers Yue and Tu, a half-step Sword Immortal. He didn't dare look at him again, fearing he might be killed by Taibai Xian's sword.

This time, he followed Yue Qingfeng in descending the mountain to broaden his horizons by relying on the strength of his two senior brothers.

Xia Qingzhou, Qingyang Baiduo Yue!

He had left the mountain to travel the world, hoping to increase his experience by relying on the power of these two senior brothers.

Little did he expect that even his senior brothers would fall here.

Trembling, he carried Senior Brother Tu's corpse, about to leave the stage.

A strand of sword intent firmly locked onto him.

"Oh? You're also a Sword Sect disciple?"

A mocking voice sounded.

But Ye Fei didn't dare speak up or confront him.

That was the ridicule of a half-step Sword Immortal; he didn't dare open his mouth.

"When did Qi Refining Realm disciples of the Sword Sect start wandering the world?"

"Never mind, never mind. Since you've stepped onto the stage, I assume you want to have a bout with me?"

The voice from behind grew louder, and the sword intent became denser, causing Ye Fei to feel as if a myriad of needles were piercing his back.

"Take out your sword and let's have a duel!"

Taibai Xian shamelessly mocked Ye Fei.

He deliberately left Tusanming's corpse there. With his strength, he could easily slash it into hundreds of pieces with a single sword strike.

But he didn't!

He needed someone to collect Tusanming's remains.

Then he would have an excuse to continue killing Sword Sect disciples.

Killing Sword Sect disciples with the sword!

Such an opportunity was hard to come by even once in a thousand years.

He wanted to kill a few more Sword Sect disciples to prove his invincible will.

"Taibai Xian! You've gone too far!"

Yue Qingfeng's face was ashen, his chest heaving - he was furious.

"Killing my Sword Sect brother, and still daring to insult my Sword Sect cultivators, aren't you afraid that the Sword Immortals of my sect will come to Junzhou and wipe out your entire Taibai Clan?"

Taibai Xian's expression changed slightly, but then he smiled faintly.

"That depends on whether you have the chance to leave Baixed City alive!"

Huh?

A killing intent suddenly erupted, locking onto Yue Qingfeng.

Auras intertwined, energy threads pulled, and the sword in his hand flew out!

At the same time, six strands of sword intent manifested in midair, forming a sword array that slashed out another sword strike.

Taibai Xian, standing on the stage, was actually trying to kill someone off the stage.

This violated the rules.

"You!"

Yue Qingfeng spat out a sword pill, using the Rootless Water Finger technique to turn it into a flying sword, its sword array spanning the heavens and earth!

The flying sword collided with the precious sword!

The precious sword trembled!

The flying sword remained motionless.

But the two sword intent arrays clashed, and Yue Qingfeng's sword intent was severed, with sword aura sweeping towards him from all directions.

"I'm doomed!"

Six strands of sword intent were far too powerful for him.

In the battle between sword cultivators, once the sword intent was unleashed, it determined life and death!

"Crack!"

A sound suddenly rang out, and the sword array in front of Yue Qingfeng shattered, the sword light scattering and retracting.

Taibai Xian revealed a look of shock.

His six strands of sword intent were actually blocked?

"Who! Who dares to ruin my good show!"

After his initial shock, Taibai Xian regained his composure.

Killing Sword Sect disciples!

He had someone backing him up!

No matter who came, they would die.

"Noisy!"

A cold voice emerged from the crowd as a young man in blue robes and a blue shirt stretched out a finger, pointing it at Taibai Xian!

With that one pointing finger, ripples appeared in the space before him.

Taibai Xian's head exploded through the void!

"Boom——"

His head burst apart, his soul extinguished. The Unbounded Realm half-step Sword Immortal collapsed lifeless on the stage.

Dead!

Just like that, he's dead?

Yue Qingfeng looked at the headless corpse on the stage, then turned to look at this young man in blue robes.

"Senior Brother Gu!"

With one glance, he recognized this cultivator as their sect's true disciple, hailed as the paramount genius of the sword path, Senior Brother Gu.

Gu Jiuqing nodded slightly, then looked towards Ye Fei carrying the corpse.

"Brother Ye, long time no see!"

Ye Fei kept his head lowered. He had seen Gu Jiuqing's figure but immediately looked down again.

Now that Gu Jiuqing had addressed him by his real name, Ye Fei could only raise his head.

"Senior Brother Gu, it has indeed been a long time."

His voice was bitter.
