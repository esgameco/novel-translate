The little figure on Liangxiao Shifu's brow moved.

The little figure stood upright in the vertical eye on his brow. On the little figure's face, one eye emitted strands of Buddhist light, as if the Immovable Bright King was seated there.

Thousands of rays of light scattered, sweeping over the entire Sword Tomb.

"The third king-grade flying sword is hidden beneath these three flying swords."

"These three flying swords have evolved into the Three Talents Flying Swords in the form of Heaven, Earth and Man. The king-grade flying sword lies beneath the Three Talents Flying Swords."

Upon hearing this, Gu Jiuqing looked towards the depths of the Sword Tomb.

There, the Three Talents Flying Swords trembled, and the Three Talents Array of Heaven, Earth and Man was manifested. Three sword auras containing the true intent of Heaven, Earth and Man were faintly emanating sword intent!

Those were flying swords that had manifested sword intent! Their grade was so high, just half a step away from king-grade.

And beneath these three flying swords was the true king-grade flying sword.

Siwuxie stood up and stepped into the Sword Tomb. His eyes were like the starry sky, with brilliant stars spanning billions of miles.

He pointed at the flying sword beneath his feet.

This flying sword looked plain and ordinary, with only a faint sword aura leaking out.

With Gu Jiuqing's perception, he could only tell that this was a low-grade flying sword without any special characteristics.

But as Siwuxie pointed at it, the flying sword suddenly emanated terrifying sword auras, releasing an aura of slaughter, as if it wanted to slaughter all living beings in heaven and earth.

"The Slaughter Sword. This flying sword was born eight thousand years ago, inside the Sword Tomb, even before the Third Patriarch had cultivated the Five Phase Sword Furnace."

Each sentence from Siwuxie made Liangxiao Shifu look sideways in astonishment.

Eight thousand years ago was the era of their ancestral founder.

It was only three thousand years ago that the current Sword Sect leader became the supreme master.

Siwuxie even knew the secrets from eight thousand years ago?

The Slaughter Sword retracted its slaughter aura and became inconspicuous within the Sword Tomb.

Gu Jiuqing memorized the position of this flying sword.

Among all the flying swords now, this one had the strongest spiritual nature and the greatest power.

"Shifu, please!"

The little figure in Liangxiao Shifu's vertical eye pointed one hand to the sky and one to the earth, striking a pose of supreme arrogance.

The Immovable Bright King's true eye flashed, and the little figure instantly transformed into a Bright King statue!!

Composed of countless eyeballs covering its entire body, it pierced through the Sword Tomb.

Under the full scrutiny of the Immovable Bright King's true eye, Liangxiao Shifu searched the Sword Tomb over and over again, even scouring the void multiple times, before finally locating the sixth king-grade flying sword.

The fifth king-grade flying sword was hidden in the central position of the Sword Tomb, closest to the Five Phase Sword Furnace below, absorbing the richest five phase sword intent.

Faint traces of the five sword auras flickered on its body.

"The fifth king-grade flying sword has nurtured the great five phase sword intent. Why hasn't your sect taken this flying sword?"

Liangxiao Shifu pointed at the fifth king-grade flying sword and inquired.

Logically speaking, for the true disciples of the Third Patriarch's lineage who cultivated the five phase sword intent, possessing a king-grade flying sword imbued with the five phase sword intent would be extremely beneficial, potentially even creating a new Sword Immortal.

Yet this flying sword had remained in the Sword Tomb for many years, untouched by anyone.

Siwuxie shook his head, "I have no need for this flying sword."

"And the other senior brothers have never discovered this flying sword, so this king-grade flying sword has stayed within the Sword Tomb."

Hmm?

Liangxiao Shifu frowned.

Indeed, although the Third Patriarch was a divine figure in the sword path, his true lineage had no successors.

It was best for the fifth king-grade flying sword to remain guarding the Sword Tomb.

"It's your junior brother's turn now."

Siwuxie shook his head again, "There are only five king-grade flying swords in the Sword Tomb, Shifu, what do you think?"

Only five king-grade flying swords?

Gu Jiuqing looked at the positions of the five king-grade flying swords and had already made his choice.

"Hahaha, Junior Brother Wuxie, your insight can match my talents."

In the Sword Tomb, there were only five flying swords!

Siwuxie could accurately state this answer, earning Liangxiao Shifu's approval.

He looked at Gu Jiuqing, "Junior Brother, you've identified the locations of the five king-grade flying swords. Now go and choose the one most suitable for you."

Liangxiao Shifu had gone through so much trouble!

He deliberately competed with Siwuxie to uncover all the king-grade flying swords' locations, just to let Gu Jiuqing make the selection!

Upon hearing this, Siwuxie showed no reaction, his eyes still full of smiles.

Watching Gu Jiuqing step forward...
