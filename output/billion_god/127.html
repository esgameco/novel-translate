"Then, when the four patriarchs were guarding the four dangerous areas, the First Emperor of Great Zhou divided the nine states and established the Great Zhou Dynasty!"

"Three thousand years ago, Jian Taichu fell. Nine hundred years ago, the First Emperor of Great Zhou died prematurely."

The Eight Trigrams Patriarch briefly recounted the history of the recent antiquity, searching his memories, but still could not find any information about the giant figure with the red furry arm that had emerged from Ancient Mountain.

"So the being suppressed under Ancient Mountain could be an existence from the ancient era!"

It might even be an ancient deity!

This was the first time Gu Jiuqing had encountered an ancient deity from such a close distance!!

Within his body, in the area of his five viscera.

The five deities formed from the primordial qi of his five viscera stirred, as if wanting to fly out of his flesh to observe the deity!

Gu Jiuqing forcefully suppressed this feeling. This time, he had come to Ancient Mountain to seek the Three Great Scourges' treasures.

With three thousand thoughts stilled, Gu Jiuqing gazed towards the distant cliff. At the edge of the cliff, there was a smooth wall surface, and the divine gold that Lu Yuan had picked up was located below this wall.

"Disciple, use your Eight Trigrams Divine Eye to take a look at this wall!"

Lu Yuan did as instructed, offering the Eight Trigrams Divine Eye, which could penetrate the void.

"Senior Brother, there are traces on this wall!"

Lu Yuan was greatly alarmed. The Eight Trigrams Patriarch took over the Eight Trigrams Divine Eye.

"The Three Great Scourges! They're inside this wall!!"

In the divine eye, three blurred figures appeared on the wall.

Gu Jiuqing was delighted.

Just as he had guessed.

During the Primordial Era, the Three Great Scourges roamed the wastelands, plundering ancient treasures.

One day, they came to Ancient Mountain, suspecting there were treasures here.

They ascended the peak from the other side of Ancient Mountain, advancing without encountering the red-furred ancient human.

But after entering this cliff area, they disturbed the ancient human, leading them to be trapped within this wall!

But why could this wall imprison the Three Great Scourges for thousands of years?

Gu Jiuqing approached the cliff's edge again, this time more cautiously. He looked at the wall and noticed a piece of Taiyi Divine Gold embedded in it, about to fall off!!

Those pieces of divine gold were actually distress signals sent out by the Three Great Scourges!

"The wall is smooth. Is it possible that this isn't actually a wall?"

Gu Jiuqing stepped into the void, landing on the other side of this "wall."

It turned out the wall was very thin, like a cicada's wing.

It was a mirror!!

"An ancient divine mirror! This is an ancient divine mirror that trapped the Three Great Scourges on Ancient Mountain."

The Three Great Scourges encountered the ancient human here. Upon discovering the ancient divine mirror, they panicked and hid inside it, not expecting to be unable to leave!

Gu Jiuqing deduced what had happened during the Primordial Era, piece by piece, almost arriving at the true story.

"Save..."

"Save me!"

Weak voices came from within the wall.

Gu Jiuqing was overjoyed!

The Three Great Scourges were indeed inside.
