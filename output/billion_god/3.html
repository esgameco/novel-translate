Class 2 of Grade 3, inside the classroom!

Clouds of mist and rosy clouds emerged, a hazy atmosphere was born!

This was an extraordinary scene of cultivation.

Forty-five students practiced the Dragon Elephant Skill together, going through the eight forms of the Dragon Elephant Skill one by one. The forty-five students looked like forty-five giant elephants, walking around in the classroom.

All kinds of crackling sounds kept ringing out, startling the people outside the classroom.

However, it was noon now, so the other students did not disturb Class 2 of Grade 3. But when they passed by, they all felt an intense heat.

But this was normal.

For martial cultivators, their blood and qi were vigorous. There were even videos widely circulated of Blood Martial Saints whose single drop of essence blood could crush an entire building.

Yang Miaojing was the class monitor of Class 2 Grade 3. To be able to take on this role, she was most likely the strongest martial cultivator in the class.

Yang Miaojing had a tall figure, wearing a long blue robe, with her hair tied up high in a bun with a hairpin. As she practiced the eight forms of the Dragon Elephant Skill, the qi and blood in her body coalesced, vaporizing quite a bit!

The dispersed qi and blood intertwined behind her, actually forming a giant blood-red elephant!!!

The elephant rampaged wildly, the qi and blood elephant's ripples were three to four times larger than Yang Miaojing's figure, even reaching the ceiling.

The elephant uprooted divine mountains!

The elephant trod the void!

The elephant pushed divine cities!

Trampling like an elephant!

The divine elephant soared!

The elephant transcended heaven and earth!

No laws, no elephant!

As the eight martial forms unfolded one by one, Yang Miaojing's face flushed red, her entire skin a rosy white, and her long dress was soaked through, accentuating her curves.

"Huff——————"

She forcefully exhaled a mouthful of turbid air, which condensed into a sharp arrow flying out three feet before dissipating after hitting the wall.

A stain was also left on the wall.

"My qi has actually become so strong?"

Yang Miaojing looked at the stain on the wall in disbelief.

That was the stain from her exhaled turbidity - the sign of a martial cultivator refining their skin!

Yang Miaojing's skin was as smooth as jade, without a single blemish, as if any birthmarks or pigmentation had vanished.

Just her jade-like skin alone showed that Yang Miaojing was a true martial cultivator.

"I have a feeling that I've now broken through the realm of a martial cultivator and become a true martial artist."

Martial cultivators refine their skin, martial artists refine their flesh - qi and blood at level 1 was the boundary between the two.

To get into university, one's qi and blood had to exceed 1!

And in the entire Lijiang area, there were only twenty to thirty people who got into university each year.

These twenty to thirty people were the dragons and phoenixes among humans, one picked out of thousands, even one out of ten thousands of talents.

Among them, some came from schools, while others were disciples from Lijiang's martial artist clans.

Going to school?

That was just a path of martial arts opened for the poor.

According to Yang Miaojing's previous rate of cultivation, it would take three months for her to possibly break through to qi and blood level 1, but that was only a possibility.

But now!!

Her qi and blood had definitely exceeded level 1.

And this was just from half a day of practicing the eight forms of the Dragon Elephant Skill.

The Dragon Elephant Skill actually had twenty-two forms in total.

Looking at her delicate, fragile skin, Yang Miaojing didn't hesitate to take out a small qi and blood testing device from her desk.

"Class monitor, are you going to test your qi and blood?"

Forty-four figures immediately surrounded her.

The students of Class 2 had all practiced the eight martial forms of the Dragon Elephant Skill, and they clearly felt the changes within themselves.

A foul odor wafted out from their bodies, each student expelling impurities, but they didn't care about that now, all eyes on Yang Miaojing.

"The martial art passed down by Master Gu is too terrifying. I've only practiced for an afternoon, but I can already feel my strength has increased greatly."

"Me too!"

"Same here!"

"With Master Gu's profound teachings, we might all be able to get into university by practicing the Dragon Elephant Skill in the three months before the exam."

Silence!

The classroom fell silent, the forty-four students all staring at the qi and blood testing device in Yang Miaojing's hand.

They held their breath, wanting to verify their speculation. Just by looking at the class monitor's qi and blood level, they would know if it was real or just an illusion.

The qi and blood testing device was about the same size as a mobile phone, with an LCD screen on the front and a smooth jade-like back.

There was also a protruding button on the back of the testing device. Yang Miaojing gripped it in one hand, then pressed the protruding button.

A fine needle shot out, instantly pricking her index finger.

A drop of blood was absorbed by the testing device.

Yang Miaojing tested her qi and blood with this device every half month. The last test was 0.983. In this half month, according to her usual progress, her qi and blood would probably increase to around 0.985.

Under Yang Miaojing's gaze, and that of the whole class, the LCD screen on the qi and blood testing device suddenly lit up, black numbers starting to flash rapidly.

0.584!

0.775!

0.946!

0.985!

In an instant, her qi and blood level surpassed 0.983. Yang Miaojing's eyes went wide, staring at the LCD screen in disbelief.

Too fast!

The numbers were jumping too fast. She had been using this qi and blood testing device for almost three years now.

It was a gift from her grandfather when she had just entered high school.

Previously, the testing device's numbers would start from 0.1 and take a full minute to reach 0.9. But now it had instantly jumped to 0.98!!!

The black numbers leapt again, and the surrounding students leaned in closer, surrounding Yang Miaojing without any gaps.

"0.989!"

"It's 0.989! Heavens, the class monitor's qi and blood increased by 0.05 in just half a month?"

"The class monitor might become a martial artist before the exam!"

On the path of martial arts, the further one goes, the harder it becomes. Most of the students in Class 2 had qi and blood levels above 0.9, but increasing it by 0.1 was extremely difficult.

Even given three to four years, they might still be unable to break through to 1!

And once past thirty years old, one's qi and blood would start declining. Martial artist was the most basic realm, with martial cultivators refining their skin, martial artists refining their flesh, and above that were bone refiners!

On the high martial planet, everyone practiced martial arts. Only by becoming a martial artist before eighteen did one have a chance to reach the bone refiner realm.

"No, the numbers on the qi and blood testing device jumped again."

Or perhaps even a Tendon Refining Grand Master?

"Teacher Gu truly lives up to being a genius from the imperial capital."

"Teacher Gu asked us to write an 800-word reflection on the Dragon and Elephant Skill. Why don't we just go home early? We still have Teacher Gu's class tomorrow morning."
