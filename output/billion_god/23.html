Taking the elevator downstairs, Gu Jiuqing saw many elderly men practicing morning exercises in the community.

This was the first time Gu Jiuqing went out so early on Planet Blue.

Men in their fifties, sixties, and even over seventy years old, moved as lightly as swallows, practicing the Dragon and Elephant Skill on the small paths.

The twenty-two postures of the Dragon and Elephant Skill were performed one by one, puffs of mist emanating from their bodies, making the whole community seem like a dream.

Some elders held long swords, repeatedly making the motions of drawing and sheathing their swords.

That was the practice of the Sword Drawing Technique.

"The Sword Drawing Technique and the Dragon and Elephant Skill have been promoted nationwide? Even the community I live in is covered."

Gu Jiuqing smiled slightly.

These elderly men, their diligent practice was really adorable.

After walking along the main road for a while, Gu Jiuqing exited the community and looked back.

Row upon row of high-rise buildings stood before his eyes, with people standing on many balconies practicing the Dragon and Elephant Skill and the Sword Drawing Technique.

This was an extraordinary sight. After the advent of the High Martial Era, such scenes existed but were not common.

Unlike this morning, when households everywhere rose early to cultivate.

The streets on both sides had much less pedestrian traffic, and many shops were closed.

Especially the clothing stores - everyone was cooped up at home cultivating, who had time to go out and buy clothes?

However, the restaurants were doing much better business than usual.

Practicing the Dragon and Elephant Skill consumed a great deal of energy and blood essence, so apart from nutrient solutions, the only option was to replenish through food.

"The detailed twenty-two posture version of the Dragon and Elephant Skill for only 3,688! Don't miss it as you pass by."

"The true explanation of the Sword Drawing Technique, how to easily complete 300 sword draws in a day!"

"Want to get D-grade nutrient solutions for 3,001 each? Join my Celestial Martial Academy, and you can buy one every day!"

...

Gu Jiuqing looked at the drastically changed streets with some bewilderment.

He had only been away from Planet Blue for two or three days, but the transformation of Linji was too drastic.

He had looked up nutrient solutions online before - the lowest grade for cultivating martial artists cost 3,000 each. And that was just for body refiners!

C-grade nutrient solutions would be even more expensive, but now they were a full ten times cheaper!

"Abolish the Hundred Schools, uphold only the Dragon and Elephant!"

Linji was promoting the Dragon and Elephant Skill on a large scale, wanting the entire population to rapidly increase their strength in a short time, even controlling resources like this.

"Linji is different from Northern Territory. Linji is mainly composed of the Martial Alliance, the municipal government, and the commercial associations. Only by the three giants joining forces could the current situation manifest."

Gu Jiuqing looked towards the more prosperous streets in the distance.

The flow of vehicular traffic had disappeared. This was the morning rush hour, but there were hardly any pedestrians on the road!

The few pedestrians walked briskly in groups of three or five. In contrast, Gu Jiuqing's pace was slow - he was overtaken by two or three waves of people walking from one end of the street to the other.

"But for me, this is a good thing!"

Gu Jiuqing had transmitted the Dragon and Elephant Skill and Sword Drawing Technique to Class 2 of Grade 3. When he had the students take photos afterwards, he knew these skills would spread to Linji.

But he didn't expect it to happen so quickly!

"Now that the higher-ups of Linji are aware of my transmission, when will they try to make contact with me?"

Gu Jiuqing thought of it!

He stood at the end of the street and turned to look into the distance.

There, a school building rose up - it was Linji High School!

"Or perhaps, they have already begun trying to make contact with me."

The youth withdrew his gaze, his steps firm as he walked step-by-step towards the school.

"He's appeared!"

"The target has appeared!"

"Target detected! Approaching the school gate."

"It's Gu Zixiu, he's come!"

At the school gate, numerous high-definition cameras turned, all focused on the area right in front of the gate.

High building, secret room...
