Outside Jianmen Pass stretched an endless wilderness, while atop Jianmen Pass stood the sacred mountains of Jianmen. Inside Jianmen Pass were the thirteen ancient cities of the northern lands, with flying horse-drawn carriages stopping within.

The youths from the ancient cities flew toward Jianmen Pass, riding in the flying horse carriages.

The carriages revealed the backgrounds of these youths from the ancient cities. One carriage was pulled by three dragon horses - each powerfully built steed emanating a faint glow, radiating a clear divine light in the night.

Inside the carriage, Qu Yaozhou's hands had been fully restored.

Before him stood an elderly man. "That kid from the Gu family went too far."

This was an old man from the Qu family, over a hundred years old, but through cultivation arts he appeared only around thirty.

He stood before Qu Yaozhou, his gaze flickering as strands of spiritual power flowed down, constantly circulating through Qu Yaozhou's physical body.

"Today is the great examination at Jianmen. If you are injured, it will be nearly impossible for you to complete the second Spiritual Transcendence."

Gu Jiuqing, he was trying to ruin Qu Yaozhou's path forward.

For a declining Gu family to dare attack the young master of the Qu family.

"Gu Jiuqing has completed two Spiritual Transcendences in a row. If it was truly through his own talents, then surely within the next six days he will definitely complete the third Transcendence!"

Three Transcendences allows entry into the inner sect of Jianmen.

Qu Yaozhou gritted his teeth in hatred. He absolutely would not allow Gu Jiuqing to become an inner sect disciple of Jianmen.

"Yaozhou, there are many talents in this world. Jianmen guards the northern wilderness at Jianmen Pass, but those coming this time are merely youths from the thirteen ancient cities of the northern lands."

"Look farther ahead on the path of cultivation. It is not talent that matters, but an unwavering heart."

The Qu family elder spoke calmly. He had experienced too many storms in his life. Talents? This world certainly did not lack for talents. Moreover, those who could walk the path of cultivation, were they not all sons of heaven?

"In the next few days, quickly complete the three Transcendences. The old patriarch has sent me a message - at that time, he will take you as his disciple in person."

That old patriarch! Taking me as a disciple?

Qu Yaozhou was overjoyed. That was a Jianmen elder, the strongest of the Qu family patriarchs. To be accepted by this elder as a disciple, what was Gu Jiuqing in comparison? How dare he challenge Qu Yaozhou?

Qu Yaozhou hurriedly nodded. The elder left, and the ghostly light on the flying horse carriage dimmed before extinguishing.

9pm, Gu Jiuqing prepared to leave his rented room. His residence was not far from the school, just a ten minute walk away.

Along this ten minute path, Gu Jiuqing saw more than ten martial arts schools lining the street.

"Elite Martial Arts School - our head instructor won the championship at the 3rd Linjiang Martial Arts Competition, a veteran Bone Refining master!"

"No need for 99.8 or 98, just 9.8 and you get a chance for guidance from a martial champion!"

"Only 10 spots left, no, 9 spots now."

"The instructors at Dragon Tiger are all university-graduated martial artists. Increasing inner energy by 0.1 in 3 months is no dream!"

"Tuition is only 6666. Two enroll together for a 10% discount! Three for 20% off!"

...

It was madness. The martial arts should not be transmitted recklessly, yet on the martial planet of Gaowu, martial cultivation was popularized among everyone.

Walking down the street, Gu Jiuqing noticed every pedestrian showed signs of having cultivated martial arts. Compulsory elementary education included six years of basic martial training. With some effort, one could test into middle school for another three years.

High school was the watershed - only the diligent could enter and strive for university admission.

At the school gates.

"Teacher Gu? You're here again today?"

"Haha, I envy Teacher Gu. So young yet already a school teacher. I heard the principal wants to promote your title."

Old Yang and Old Jin were well-informed to even know this kind of news.

Gu Jiuqing felt awkward. Title? What was that about?

"Oh right, I almost forgot - today is the fitness test. All the senior third and fourth year students have to participate."

Old Yang and Old Jin looked towards the sports field, where a large group of third-year students had already gathered.

Soon, shrill screams rang out from the field, followed by roars from the crowd.

This prompted the school security to intervene, with police cars entering the sports ground. It was a while before the field finally calmed down.

With doubts in mind, Gu Jiuqing entered the office.

The office was in an uproar. Besides Gu Jiuqing, there were three other teachers present.

Teacher Le Qing, a female martial artist around thirty years old. Constant training kept her in peak physical condition - she had reached the pinnacle of Flesh Refining.

Then there was Old Teacher Yang, over fifty years old. His inner energy had severely deteriorated, though rumor was he used to be a Bone Refining master! Thus, he commanded the most authority in the office.

The third teacher was even younger than Gu Jiuqing.

On the martial planet of Gaowu, Gu Zixi was eighteen when he tested into university. Four years later he graduated from the Imperial Capital University, then came to teach at Linjiang School.

This young Teacher Tong was two years Gu Zixi's junior, also a martial artist.

"A martial artist! At such a young age? Impossible!"

"The school must have made a mistake."

Teacher Le Qing lay slumped over her desk, staring at the report on her computer in disbelief.

The fitness test results must be wrong. Otherwise, how could there suddenly be two martial artists?

1.037! Yang Miaojing!

1.025! Ning Que!

These two students from Class 2 had inner energy exceeding 1 - the level of a martial artist.

But it didn't stop there.

The other students in Senior Grade 3 Class 2 had also experienced massive increases in inner energy.

0.9921! Fang Linli!

0.9914! Lan Bai!

0.9948! Ke Jing!

0.9977! Wen Daihua!

0.9994! Lu Qing!

In the 45 students of Senior 3 Class 2, the class president Yang Miaojing and vice president Ning Que had become martial artists, while the remaining 43 were all martial apprentices.

Not just two martial artists emerging from one class, the rest were all martial apprentices?

This was insane!

How was this possible?

Old Teacher Yang and young Teacher Tong also stared at the fitness test results on their respective computers.

They scrolled down to the last student in Senior 3 Class 2.

"Martial apprentices! They're all martial apprentices!"

"Their inner energy has all surpassed 0.99!"

"How can there be so many martial apprentices? What happened to Class 2's students for them to improve so much?"

If it was one or two students with a major increase, they could accept it.

But now, it was the entire class whose scores had risen dramatically.

Old Teacher Yang had taught for many years, leading several senior classes and producing three university students. He knew full well how difficult it was to cultivate to the martial artist realm before age eighteen.

Yet the report in front of him showed two martial artists.

Impossible! Absolutely impossible.

"Teacher Gu, you're here."

Teacher Le Qing noticed Gu Jiuqing, her expression changing. Senior 3 Class 2 was Gu Jiuqing's class, but he had taken leave half a month ago, with Le Qing taking over teaching them.

She could confirm that just two days prior, the students were still normal.

So how did they become so different just after two days?

"What exactly is going on..."
