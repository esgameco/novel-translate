Gu Jiuqing, dressed in a long blue robe stained with blood, stood calmly on the ground. The sound of a sword chant was emanating from him.

A sword!

Sword force!

Sword aura!

Sword intent!

The three fundamental aspects of the sword path blossomed simultaneously, slashing the heavens and extinguishing the earth, piercing through the void. The extraordinary phenomenon appearing this time was dozens of times more magnificent than when Si Wuxie comprehended the jade sword intent.

His blue robe dyed with blood fluttered noisily. The youth from the northern lands only felt his eyes dry, as an overwhelming intent to kill emerged from nowhere.

"This sword intent contains the meaning of slashing the heavens and extinguishing the earth. In this sword test, the jade sword is inserted into the trial stone, possessing only the jade sword intent. How could the intent to slash the heavens arise?"

Ye Fei focused his gaze, allowing his eyes to dry and shed clear tears, still staring fixedly at Gu Jiuqing's body.

He felt it!

He felt that boundless sword intent.

A wisp of sword aura coiled around Gu Jiuqing's index finger.

The glowing sword aura did not harm Gu Jiuqing at all, instead bringing a sense of warmth.

The sword aura continued to flow, the sword force naturally forming. The heaven-slashing sword force condensed within, and after this wisp of sword force reached full condensation, it finally converged into a strand of sword intent!!

Whoosh——————

The sword aura dispersed from Gu Jiuqing's body, and an illusory great sword formed behind his back.

This sword could slash the heavens!

Hence, it was called the heaven-slashing sword intent.

"Is this the sword intent of the Unsheathing Sword Art?"

Gu Jiuqing seemed dazed, as if inquiring and affirming at the same time.

Within his Mud Pill Palace, the illusory fruition swayed, and insights from cultivating the Unsheathing Sword Art condensed onto the golden flying sword, touched by Gu Jiuqing's thoughts.

There were too many insights into the Unsheathing Sword Art, with new ones converging every moment. The heaven-slashing sword intent was born from the Unsheathing Sword Art, the most quintessential sword intent!

A strand of sword intent rose, the boundless sword aura dispersing. The insights within the fruition continuously converged, causing this strand of sword intent to grow and condense more sword intent, visibly to the naked eye.

"This is not the jade sword intent. This kind of sword intent is too profound. If sword intents are also graded, this sword intent would at least be of the highest grade!"

Xia Qingzhou's beautiful eyes flickered incessantly. As the heir of the Xia family, she understood the secrets of the sword path.

She also knew about the cultivation of the sword path.

The beginning of the sword path involved using one's blood energy or spiritual power to stimulate the true comprehension of the sword path, transforming it into sword aura. Achieving this step meant stepping onto the path of cultivating the sword path.

The next step was to condense the force of the sword path and comprehend its true comprehension. If one could comprehend a strand of sword force, they could be called a sword cultivator.

One strand of sword force, two strands of sword force, three strands of sword force... After ten strands of sword force, they would condense into one complete sword force.

Starting from one complete sword force, one would continue to condense two complete sword forces, three complete sword forces... After reaching ten complete sword forces, they could finally converge into one strand of sword intent!

One could imagine how difficult it was to transform sword aura within nine days, then condense ten complete sword forces into one strand of sword intent.

Those sword cultivators within the sword sect might not be able to condense a single strand of sword intent in their entire lives.

This was the gap between a sword prodigy and an ordinary sword cultivator.

One was like a bright lamp in the eternal night, while the other group of sword cultivators fell in the eternal night, unable to emit even a glimmer of light.

"The Gu family is about to rise!"

Xia Qingzhou's expression changed, finally turning into a sigh.

The four of them had sought out Gu Jiuqing early on, intending to join forces and enter the sword sect. Although they also wished to become sword prodigies, only they knew how difficult it was to become a true sword prodigy!

Unexpectedly!

Fan Shaoli gripped his folding fan tightly, crushing the dragon bone within.

A sense of powerlessness!

For the first time, this feeling arose in the hearts of these four young masters from great clans.
