Mingting Peak.

Outside a palace that appeared very magnificent on the outside.

Gu Jiuqing was standing under the main gate of this palace.

"Junior Brother Gu Jiuqing has come to visit Senior Brother Liang Xiao!"

The palace gates opened automatically, and a demonic aura was released from within, with strands of demonic qi swirling around. The sword intent on Gu Jiuqing's body breathed, extinguishing the demonic qi!

He frowned tightly, looking at the pitch-black passage, but still entered Senior Brother Liang Xiao's cave abode.

The cave abode was dark, even darker than the last time Gu Jiuqing entered his senior brother's cave abode, and as soon as he entered the main hall, a wave of chilling air erupted.

Inside the dilapidated cave abode, huge stone pillars supported the roof beams.

The demonic beings coiled around the pillars had disappeared, and under the pillars were strewn ancient coffins, from which the chilling air was emanating.

Gu Jiuqing's physical body felt the bone-chilling cold!

His physical body was terrifyingly powerful, yet he still felt the chilling air.

If other disciples of the sect stepped into this place, they would be frozen by the chilling air in an instant.

The ground beneath his feet had already been frozen over. As he went deeper into the cave abode, the surroundings became brighter, and in the distance on a high platform, Gu Jiuqing saw Senior Brother Liang Xiao busily at work.

Senior Brother Liang Xiao wielded the great Five Elements Sword Qi, separating five types of sword intent from his five fingers, extracting the Buzhou Mountains from the bodies of the demonic beings one by one.

The Buzhou Mountains piled up like a mountain in the air, with various divine body origins disappearing.

Liang Xiao was seen opening his mouth and devouring a share of the Ganfeng divine body origin.

As the divine body origin entered Liang Xiao's body, a dark mist assaulted him, but within a few breaths, the Ganfeng divine body origin was refined.

"The taste is quite refreshing, not bad."

Senior Brother Liang Xiao extracted the spine of another demonic being, with divine body origins scattered across its thirty-three vertebrae.

Liang Xiao was delighted, and his Five Elements Sword Qi breathed, slicing off the origins from the thirty-three vertebrae one by one.

This was an extremely horrifying technique, without causing any damage to the Buzhou Mountains, and the origins remained intact.

This required meticulous control, single-mindedness, and could not be disturbed by the slightest external interference.

But in this situation, another arm extended from Senior Brother, reaching out in the air to find a Buzhou Mountain!

On this Buzhou Mountain, the origins had been separated, and several divine ability seeds were planted on the thirty-third vertebra, which was the peak of the Buzhou Mountain.

"I remember this Buzhou Mountain contains the Phoenix divine body, and what I hold in my hand is the Phoenix divine body origin!"

Senior Brother grasped the Phoenix divine body origin in his left hand, and this pool of origin coiled around his hand, actually transforming into a phoenix being reborn from the fire.

The phoenix fell into the void, entering this Buzhou Mountain!

Transplanting the same origin of others onto other Buzhou Mountains!

This was a bold idea.

The Buzhou Mountain that had been drained of its origin was once again covered with thirty-three vertebrae of the Phoenix divine body origin.

This Buzhou Mountain emitted a divine radiance, with a phoenix image enveloping the mountain, and the divine ability seeds on it shone with dazzling light.

With the divine light scattered from the Buzhou Mountain, these withered divine ability seeds were slowly being restored.

"Indeed, my conjecture was not wrong. When cultivators were still alive, the divine ability seeds were nurtured within their bodies, allowing them to use divine abilities at will. Once they died, the divine ability seeds would first wither, and then dissipate."

"But with the nourishment of the origin, the divine ability seeds will be restored."

The result of this experiment was terrifying.

Even Gu Jiuqing could feel it!

Killing demonic beings was simple, but once they died, their origins would dissipate, and the divine ability seeds would quickly wither and scatter.

But now, Senior Brother only needed to infuse these demonic beings with the same origin, preventing their origins from dissipating prematurely, and the divine ability seeds could be preserved.

This would greatly benefit Senior Brother's research on divine abilities.

Gu Jiuqing stood on the frozen ground and waited for two hours!

"Whew, I almost forgot, Junior Brother, you're here!"

Senior Brother Liang Xiao's inner spiritual power surged, forming thousands of arms that scooped up the demonic corpses and Buzhou Mountain remains on the ground, stuffing them all into the icy coffins.

The arms moved nimbly in the air like threads, and in just a few breaths, the entire cave abode was cleaned up completely.

"This is the Thousand-Petal Buddha Hand from Buddhism, best used for cleaning. No other divine abilities are as useful as the Thousand-Petal Buddha Hand."

Senior Brother Liang Xiao looked at Gu Jiuqing standing in place, and his eyes lit up.

"Junior Brother, have you refined your flying sword into pellets?"

A waterfall gushed out from his Baihui point, transforming into rootless water coiling around Gu Jiuqing's fingertips.

"I've just completed the way of the flying sword pellets, so I came to find Senior Brother."

"Good, good! Faster than Senior Brother expected by a few days."

Senior Brother Liang Xiao's figure suddenly dispersed, turning into a myriad of tiny lights.

The tiny lights scattered, and in the next moment, reassembled into Liang Xiao's figure.

This was a divine ability that even Gu Jiuqing, with his level of insight, could not comprehend its principles!

Seeing Gu Jiuqing's curiosity, Liang Xiao explained further.

"This is the Converging Quicksand technique. It disperses into sand, then converges back into a body. It can evade lethal strikes and also allow for concealment. To cultivate this technique, one must divide oneself into 129,600 grains of sand, then further divide those 129,600 portions of oneself in half repeatedly, eventually obtaining a body of tiny lights."

Converging Quicksand!

So that's how it is.

"This divine ability leaves no trace coming or going, yet Senior Brother uses it as a body technique, which is a bit of a waste."

Gu Jiuqing sighed.

"Haha, I can see the envy in Junior Brother's eyes."

"No matter, Senior Brother is compiling the Divine Abilities Encyclopedia, having already excerpted 586 divine abilities. Once the Divine Abilities Encyclopedia is complete, Junior Brother will be the first to study it!"

Compiling the Divine Abilities Encyclopedia!!

Gu Jiuqing's heart shook.

What was Senior Brother Liang Xiao trying to do?

Was he trying to compile all the divine abilities and techniques in the world into one?
