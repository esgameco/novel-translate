Mingtingfeng, the only palace within.

One by one, the ice coffins were overturned, revealing the corpses of demons and monsters.

Without the suppression of the ice coffins, the demon corpses began to decay in a short while, and their demonic aura was dispersed within the palace.

Soon, the demonic aura leaked out from the palace and spread over Mingtingfeng.

However, Mingtingfeng had a sword furnace, and the sword intent exhaled by the furnace extinguished the demonic aura by itself.

Otherwise, just the demonic aura emanating from the palace would demonize the human race, causing them to become demons and evil spirits!

"The Xuanhuang Wheel! Forge the Xuanhuang Wheel with the Xuanhuang qi!"

"Where is the Xuanhuang divine body? I remember there was a demon who cultivated into the Xuanhuang divine body, why can't I find this demon's corpse?"

Senior Brother Liang Xiao's hair was disheveled, and his clothes were in disarray. He searched among the ice coffins, looking for the demon corpse with the Xuanhuang divine body.

On a high platform, five spirit communication fruits were dim and could extinguish at any moment.

Below these five spirit seeds lay several demon corpses sealed in icy sword intent.

There were three demon corpses in total.

The demon corpse with the Four Symbols divine body, the demon corpse with the Yin-Yang divine body, and the demon corpse with the Five Elements divine body.

"Found it!"

Senior Brother Liang Xiao held up a demon corpse, and the five fingers on his hand bloomed, emanating the great Five Elements sword aura. He separated the demon's spine on the spot.

On the thirty-four vertebrae of the spine, eighteen vertebrae were covered in Xuanhuang qi!

He revealed a manic expression and forcibly plucked those eighteen vertebrae, then struck them into his own Buzhou Mountain!

The next moment, a Xuanhuang-colored divine wheel gathered above his head.

"Indeed, I can condense the Xuanhuang Divine Wheel using the Xuanhuang divine body's origin."

Following the same method, he struck the remaining three demons' divine body origins into Buzhou Mountain, using them to activate the Four Symbols Divine Wheel, the Five Elements Divine Wheel, and the Yin-Yang Divine Wheel.

In just the time it takes to burn an incense stick, four great divine wheels appeared above Senior Brother Liang Xiao's head!

Only the final Taiyi Divine Wheel was missing to form the Demonic Emperor's Myriad Worlds Life and Death Wheel!

"The spirit communication seeds contain spiritual power. If I can directly transplant these spirit seeds and plant them on my Buzhou Mountain..."

This was a bold idea!

"Kaka kaka ——————"

"Kaka kaka ——————————"

The sound of shattering came from within the cave dwelling.

Senior Brother Liang Xiao was still studying the Demonic Prince's spirit communication seeds, head bowed.

"Kaka!"

A few more sounds came.

Only then did Senior Brother Liang Xiao turn around, slapping his forehead.

"I almost forgot, my junior brother is still in the cave dwelling."

Spiritual power flowed out from his body, forming arms that hid the rotten demon corpses into millennium ice coffins and wiped away the demonic aura within the cave dwelling. Only then did Senior Brother Liang Xiao descend from the high platform.

Divine light rippled around his body, and after a few breaths, Senior Brother Liang Xiao regained his handsome appearance, his face exceptionally ruddy.

He stood beneath the cocoon, quietly waiting for Gu Jiuqing's rebirth from the pupal stage.

Kaka kaka!

The cocoon cracked, and the shredded blood-red silk slowly dissipated.

Soon, the cocoon encasing Gu Jiuqing tore apart and vanished.

"Congratulations junior brother, blessings junior brother, you have successfully shed your mortal form!"

Senior Brother Liang Xiao raised his hands in celebration of Gu Jiuqing.

But his hands froze midair, unmoving.

Hm?

His junior brother's aura was off!

Why was it exactly the same as before?

"Junior brother, how many times have you shed your mortal form after refining the Demonic Emperor's true blood?"

He had just probed Gu Jiuqing's aura with his divine sense but received only blankness!

If Gu Jiuqing had not just joined the sect, he would have suspected that Gu Jiuqing had already cultivated a divine body!

Only cultivators at the Buzhou realm who had formed a divine body could suppress their anomalies and present an aura like an ordinary mortal.

Gu Jiuqing stretched his limbs. After staying on the Blue Star for three days and waiting for several more, the cocoon enveloping him had finally dissipated.

"Hm?"

Gu Jiuqing was startled, oh right, he had forgotten to manifest the aura of shedding his mortal form!

"I have just completed the eighth shedding of my mortal form!"

"This Demonic Emperor's true blood seed allowed junior brother to consecutively shed three times, truly being reborn anew."

Senior Brother Liang Xiao nodded, just as he had expected.

He looked around Gu Jiuqing, where was the shed body?

It seemed the shed body had disintegrated.

A shed body was just a large skin that would break apart under external force, so its disappearance did not make Liang Xiao suspicious.

"Good, good, eight sheddings of the mortal form, discarding the shed body, junior brother, with your current foundation, you can open the Heavenly Mechanism and enter the Realm of Spiritual Power!"

Senior Brother Liang Xiao stared at Gu Jiuqing with a solemn expression.

He really wanted Gu Jiuqing to grow quickly.

However, Gu Jiuqing felt troubled.

Only he knew his own situation.

His biggest problem now was that the draconic and elephantine power within him was too immense, and his sword intent too dazzling. If he added spiritual power on top of that?

His physical body could not bear it.

The physical body was the boat, the soul was the sail, and the sailboat drifted freely in the vast mortal world.

But to traverse this mortal world, one needed spiritual power to propel the sail!

This was the normal way to ferry sentient beings across.

For Gu Jiuqing, however, it was completely different.

His "boat" was at the Shedding Mortal Form realm. Although it had undergone ten sheddings and was extremely sturdy, able to easily bear the spiritual power of the Realm of Spiritual Power, or even the spiritual power of the Buzhou realm, dozens of draconic and elephantine powers, or one part sword intent or two parts.

But!

Gu Jiuqing's physical body bore too many things. Sailing on the bitter sea, it would capsize directly.

Five hundred draconic and elephantine powers, five parts sword intent!
