Gu Jiuqing transmitted the Moonlight Grand Formation, the Star Harvesting Formation, and the Star Refining Formation to the Blue Star one after another.

During these past few days, Gu Jiuqing had been staying on the moon, witnessing with his own eyes the outrageous scale of the Dragon Country's construction efforts.

With the acknowledgment of the First Martial Saint, the twenty-three provinces of the Dragon Country devoted all their efforts to forging the three great formations.

Slabs of divine gold and blocks of divine materials were melted down in furnaces with modern technology and manufacturing processes.

In just three days, a three-hundred-meter Moonlight Grand Formation was laid out on the moon's surface.

"Boom boom boom————"

On the vacuum environment of the moon, no sound could be heard. But when the Moonlight Grand Formation was set up, a thick moonlight essence was outlined, forming a vast fog.

Similar to the desolate moon, the thick moonlight essence condensed into white light, drifting like water around the three-hundred-meter formation.

Setting up the formation required the assistance of various divine gold materials and the catalysis of spiritual energy. Fortunately, Gu Jiuqing had brought many spirit stones, enough to activate the formation.

As the Moonlight Grand Formation officially activated, the three-hundred-meter formation was enveloped in a cloud of mist. Researchers gazed at the formation excitedly, arranging personnel to enter and construct the Star Harvesting Formation within.

"The foundation for the Star Harvesting Formation has been transported from all over the country. At most two days and it will arrive on the moon. After one more day of assembly, we can set up the Star Harvesting Formation under the first Moonlight Grand Formation!"

"According to the descriptions of the Star Harvesting Formation, it locks onto a great star, causing elemental magnetic forces to manifest between it and other celestial bodies, achieving a space-folding technique to traverse the starry void."

"The first star to be harvested is the Valley God Star, the second is the Creation God Star. The Valley God Star has a diameter of 950 kilometers, the Creation God Star over a thousand kilometers. Capturing these two great stars will not cause any substantial harm to the Blue Star, and also meets the requirements!"

"It's hard to imagine, but these are stars over a billion kilometers away that we can directly summon to the moon through the Star Harvesting Formation."

"However, after capturing the great stars, we need to quickly set up the Star Refining Formation to refine them into Starseed Pearls!"

"The Valley God Pearl, Creation God Pearl project is officially launching!"

With the efforts of an entire nation and twenty-three provinces, Gu Jiuqing witnessed formation after formation of Moonlight Grand Formations being laid out.

The second was nine hundred meters wide, the third three kilometers, the fourth ten kilometers!

A ten-kilometer Moonlight Grand Formation could capture planet-sized celestial bodies.

The Blue Star's scientists were going too far! After learning of the Star Harvesting Formation's unique space-folding technique, they aimed astronomical telescopes to capture the UY Scuti hypergiant star in the Scutum constellation!

This star's volume was fifty billion times that of the sun.

When Gu Jiuqing learned of this, he was stunned by these researchers' ambitions.

In the Blue Star's galaxy, the sun was one hundred thirty million times the size of Earth, while UY Scuti was fifty billion times larger than the sun - an astounding six hundred forty trillion times bigger!!

It was an outrageous number. Even Gu Jiuqing, who had attained the Dragon Elephant Celestial Emperor's primordial spirit by removing seal after seal of truth, was briefly stunned.

"If this great star is harvested and refined into a divine weapon...?"

He dared not imagine it, could not fathom it.

And in the galaxy, there were other stars comparable to UY Scuti. This was just within the Milky Way galaxy alone.

Gazing at the vast starry sky, Gu Jiuqing realized that compared to the Blue Star's cosmos, the Primordial Chaos' starry realms seemed pitiful in size.

Under the first Moonlight Grand Formation, the Star Harvesting Formation was laid out. After precise calculations of the Valley God Star's orbit, the researchers began harvesting procedures.

Ripples appeared in the Moonlight Grand Formation as the moonlight essence churned violently. Within the formation's Star Harvesting Formation, a brilliant beam of light erupted, shooting straight into the boundless starry void.

Faintly, this beam of light seemed to intersect the orbits of Jupiter and Mars, intertwining with the Valley God Star.

The Moonlight Grand Formation shuddered as moonlight essence within hundreds of kilometers surged inwards, concentrating into the Star Harvesting Formation.

The divine gold slabs and materials carved with intricate patterns in the Star Harvesting Formation transformed into the Way's principles, resonating with the heavens and earth, resonating with the Valley God Star.

A pale waterfall poured into the starry void. After a few breaths of time, the waterfall finally dissipated, and the formation stopped shuddering.

The harvesting of the Valley God Star was a success!

In a corner of the starry void, a celestial body orbiting its path slightly deviated from its original orbit, arcing towards the moon in a parabolic space-folding trajectory.

"It worked! It worked!"

"The Valley God Star has been harvested, quickly activate the Star Refining Formation with spirit stones!"

Soon after, Gu Jiuqing saw the third three-hundred-meter formation outside activate.

The Star Refining Formation began refining the Valley God Star in the starry void into a Starseed Pearl!

Gu Jiuqing said nothing, as behind him stood the Dragon Country's highest echelons.

The First Martial Saint!

The Dragon Country's seventy-two Sage Lords had all come to the moon to witness this moment.

The leaders of Linjiang City - the Martial Chief, the Mayor, the Cultural Secretary and others - were also present.

At this moment!

Gu Jiuqing's status in the Dragon Country was solidified, unshakable by anyone.

It was on this day that Gu Jiuqing transmitted the Three Zhang Golden Body technique to the Martial Saint for cultivation, as well as the portrait painting of the Buddha to Elder Wen.

After staying on the Blue Star for around ten days, Gu Jiuqing finally returned to the Sword Sect.

In the Sword Sect's Celestial Abilities Cultivation Chamber!

Gu Jiuqing opened his eyes. "I have transmitted all the laws to the Blue Star. Now, all I need to do is wait."

Wait for time to pass, and the Blue Star cultivators' comprehension would accumulate to an astounding degree.

The transmission to Linjiang City was the experimental phase. Linjiang's leaders sealed off the city for three months, and Gu Jiuqing could do nothing about it.

The transmission to the Dragon Country's twenty-three provinces was the verification phase. The Dragon Country sealed off the cultivation of three billion people across the ocean, and Gu Jiuqing said nothing.

Whether it was ten billion or forty billion cultivators, a four-fold difference was neither too much nor too little.

In this verification phase, Gu Jiuqing mainly waited for the forging of divine weapons.

Without divine weapons, he dared not absorb the Blue Star cultivators' cultivation and comprehension.

Once the divine weapons on the moon satisfied his cultivation requirements, he would open communications between the Dragon Country and lands across the ocean, allowing the Blue Star cultivators to enter the Demonic World and Yaoguai World for further transmissions.

He might even enter those two worlds himself, or seek out other worlds to spread the laws.

"The third phase requires no interference. I'll let each of these worlds develop freely, while I reap the benefits like a fisherman."

Spread the laws across the world, and calmly await the harvest.

Gu Jiuqing retracted his thoughts. At his feet lay numerous paper cranes.

These were messages delivered by Lu Yuan.

Infused with spiritual energy, the paper cranes materialized words in midair.

"Senior Martial Brother Gu! The sect has instructed me to notify you that the Inner Disciple Trials are about to commence. Please make preparations, Senior Brother."

The Inner Disciple Trials?

The outer disciples had outer disciple trials, the inner disciples had inner disciple trials, and naturally the true disciples also had trials of their own.

For the Sword Sect's trials, disciples typically traversed the Sword Gate to hunt yaoguai in the Primordial Chaos.

Gu Jiuqing was a newly accepted true disciple of the Sword Sect, and the true disciple trials occurred at fixed intervals. This upcoming trial would take place in a month.

That paper crane dispersed as another materialized words in the air.

"Senior Martial Brother Gu! The sect has instructed me to notify you that the Inner Disciple Trials have been delayed. There are reports that the demonic lair of the Demonic Lineage outside the Sword Gate is in turmoil. The Demon Lord's firstborn, the Demon Prince, has fallen. Yaoguai armies are besieging the area around the Sword Gate."

The Demon Prince had fallen?

Gu Jiuqing's mind flashed back to his final moments against Zhu Yue.

Back then, when he opened his left eye, an scorching heat radiated from it.

With a subtle shift of his eye, a double pupil manifested.

Gu Jiuqing's left eye had transformed into a double pupil!

"Was it my divine gaze that incinerated the Demon Prince?"

Gu Jiuqing was doubtful.

That gaze was powerful, but incinerating a Pure Yang Immortal who had crossed numerous tribulations seemed nearly impossible.

At most, it could destroy the Demon Prince's physical body, allowing his primordial spirit to escape.

"Perhaps the Demon Prince's death had nothing to do with me."

A third paper crane rippled, words coalescing in the air.

"Senior Martial Brother Gu! A butler claiming to be from the Gu Family has arrived in the ancient Heavenly Abyss City in the northern regions. He is waiting below the Sword Gate."

The final paper crane dispersed.

"Steward Xie has come to the Sword Sect looking for me?"

Since joining the Sword Sect, Gu Jiuqing had not returned to the Heavenly Abyss Ancient City's Gu Family Estate. Too many things had happened recently as he traveled between the Blue Star and Primordial Chaos. He had nearly forgotten about the Gu Family Estate.

A stream of light shot out from the Celestial Sword Palace as a sword cultivator emerged and headed outside.

The grand gate of the Celestial Sword Palace opened, allowing the sword cultivator to enter.

In the divine hall, Lu Yuan stood before Gu Jiuqing.

In just half a month, Lu Yuan's primal yuan aura had drastically changed. His Sun God Body's primal yuan had evolved into the Eight Trigrams Primal Yuan, restarting his cultivation from filling the first vertebra. Even his Wood Constitution had withered away, replaced by a new primal Wood Constitution sprouting new spirit fruits.

Lu Yuan was no longer his former self.

This cultivator had become a true prodigy, comparable to...
