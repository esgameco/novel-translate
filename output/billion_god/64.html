Jianmen Pass, dividing the vast wilderness.

The boundless scene reappeared before Gu Jiuqing's eyes.

His perception was now very subtle.

Under the state of wandering freely, his "thoughts" were infinitely extended. With a single thought, he could reach anywhere in the world.

"The vast world has no bounds, and my thoughts have no limits either."

Gu Jiuqing saw his physical body at Mingting Peak.

His golden vital energy formed a divine halo, resonating with the world to seek the celestial mechanism.

With a flicker of thought, Gu Jiuqing's consciousness extended for unknown distances across the universe.

Vast!

Boundless!

Profound!

Jianmen Pass became a speck of dust in his "eyes." The entire northern lands were dwarfed under his gaze.

Mountain ranges crisscrossed, peaks winding endlessly, with plains, plateaus, and even primeval ancient forests. Gu Jiuqing even saw cities dotting this vast land.

Those were the cities of the Great Zhou Dynasty.

"Qingzhou!"

So this was the Qingzhou mentioned in the books.

One of the nine states, Qingzhou connected to the northern lands. The farther away from the north, the more prosperous it became.

Near the Qingzhou prefecture, there was a county town every hundred li, bustling with people. Soldiers and merchants walked along the official roads, as dense as ants.

Gu Jiuqing lived in the northern lands, thousands of li away from the nearest divine city.

And in the entire northern lands, there were only thirteen ancient cities; aside from those, there were no other signs of human habitation.

"The Great Zhou is so prosperous, the heart of our human race!"

The four major sects guarded the four desolate regions, preventing demons from entering the Great Zhou. This was the original purpose for establishing the four sects.

"Let me verify Qingzhou's resemblance to an elephant."

Ancient books recorded that Qingzhou's terrain resembled an elephant, with the northern lands as the tail and the thirteen cities strung along it like a line.

With a thought, the topography of Qingzhou appeared before Gu Jiuqing's eyes, followed by the other eight states.

"Indeed, Qingzhou does resemble a great elephant!"

Gu Jiuqing felt his thoughts flowing smoothly, as if he had gained enlightenment.

He gazed down upon each of the great states, then penetrated the entire geography of the nine states with a single glance.

Jianmen Pass in the northern lands!

The Lesser Western Heaven in the Western Wilderness!

Wind and Thunder Valley in the Eastern Wilderness!

The Thirty Thousand Mountains in the Southern Ridge!

These four desolate regions separated the Great Zhou from the vast wilderness, where countless people lived and multiplied across the nine states.

"I can wander the cosmos with a thought, but where is the celestial mechanism?"

Gu Jiuqing gazed up at the vast sky. He was already far from the wilderness, and the moon and sun had grown much larger.

Gu Jiuqing forcibly suppressed the urge to venture to the moon and sun.

The last time he went to the moon during the wandering state, he almost lost his physical and spiritual form.

As for the sun, the ancient legends called it the King of Stars, revered as the Sun Star. Even the Celestial Emperor dared not follow when patrolling the great sun, for fear of falling into the Sun Mountain.

Gu Jiuqing did not know if his wandering state could withstand the burning of the sun's true fire.

"Where is the celestial mechanism?"

Gu Jiuqing gazed blankly at the wilderness.

He then looked up at the myriad stars twinkling in the sky.

"Could the celestial mechanism be among those stars?"

Gu Jiuqing's thoughts swept over them but felt no mystical resonance.

This meant the celestial mechanism he sought was not among the stars.

"Then where could it be?"

Gu Jiuqing's thoughts extended infinitely, his consciousness growing thinner and thinner.

"Heaven!"

His thoughts circulated.

"Mechanism...

Where?"

After a long while, he finally formed a complete thought.

His thoughts had scattered too far, enveloping the nine states and encompassing the four desolate regions.

But his wandering state clearly had not reached such a profound level. His consciousness continued merging with heaven and earth, completely dissipating into the vast sky. If Gu Jiuqing could not awaken, then his consciousness would completely fuse with heaven and earth!

Entering an even higher state than the unity of man and heaven, harmonizing with the Dao, becoming one with heaven and earth.

I am heaven and earth, heaven and earth are me.

In simple terms, it meant dissipating into nothingness, or more poetically, merging one's body with the Dao.

Whoosh—————

His consciousness flowed like water, drifting through the wilderness. Gu Jiuqing's spirit wandered the cosmos, his thoughts scattering across the vast sky.

His consciousness grew weaker and weaker, until all his thoughts completely fragmented.

And at that very moment!

From beyond the wilderness, a gentle breeze blew, stirring the white clouds, the ancient primeval forests, and Gu Jiuqing's scattered consciousness.

"Why not ride the wind and soar? Traversing ninety thousand li!"

His consciousness began contracting, blown together by the great wind into the illusory form of a peng bird!

The great peng soared through the nine heavens and ten earths, falling into the azure void to become the great kun fish, roaming the mortal realm.

All of this!

Occurred in such a profound and wondrous way that Gu Jiuqing only felt he had grasped something.

"Profound!"

"Wondrous!"

Profoundly profound, the gateway to all wonders.

"The celestial mechanism is not in the wilderness, nor among the myriad stars. Where could it be?"

Gu Jiuqing's thought made contact!

Heaven and earth contracted, his thoughts descended, and countless wills returned to his physical body.

"My celestial mechanism is within my own body!"

At that moment, Gu Jiuqing understood the true way to release the celestial mechanism.

He had been misguided by Senior Martial Brother Liangxiao all along.

He thought releasing the celestial mechanism meant leaking it from heaven and earth into the human body.

But that wasn't wrong - what was wrong was Gu Jiuqing searching for the celestial mechanism in heaven and earth.

"The gateway to all wonders is not in heaven and earth, but within my body. Using the gateway of all wonders as a medium, I can touch upon the celestial mechanism!"

In the chamber,

The golden vital energy halo entered his body from the baihui acupoint, the endless flow of energy washing over him.

Gu Jiuqing's physical body suddenly moved.

The body that was originally standing now sat directly on the ground, eyes half-open, palms facing the heavens.

This was the first time he used the wandering state to explore his own physical body.

A vast divine mountain appeared before his eyes, with thirty-three celestial steps like the stairway to heaven on Mount Buzhou.

An enormous peng bird soared over Mount Buzhou, descending from the peak, intertwining with the primal forms of dragons and elephants, the yin-yang primal body, and the golden raven primal body.
