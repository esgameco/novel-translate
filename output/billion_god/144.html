The Ancient City of Polaris.

A refreshing breeze, a cultivator walking alone.

Within the city, there was a great river winding through, connecting the north and south, circling around the lord's manor.

It was not yet night, so the night scenery had not yet appeared.

"It has been a long time since I last saw such a sight," the young master sighed.

"Wu Shixiong, how long have you been in the Sword Sect?"

He was a disciple of the Wu Family from the Ancient City of Polaris. Thirty-two years ago, he joined the Sword Sect. Aside from two times when he returned home, he had never set foot in the Wu Family compound again.

Thirty-two years ago, he was just a youth. Thirty-two years later, he had become a cultivator at the Unitary Realm, with his divine body fully formed. Looking over the Ancient City of Polaris, he was undoubtedly a powerful figure.

Only...

Things change, time passes.

The friends who joined the Sword Sect together with him back then had all died.

"It has indeed been quite a while," the boatman exclaimed in amazement as he rowed.

"So this young master is a cultivator. With your youthful appearance, I thought you were just a young man from the city."

Wu Shaojie smiled faintly.

Back then, he joined the Sword Sect together with Dugu Ming, Gao Shuli, Lu Yuan, and several others.

Lu Yuan followed the path of the sword's paramount master, rising to great heights!

Dugu Ming met a tragic death on the moon.

Gao Shuli and he managed to avoid a catastrophe.

However, the paths they chose were completely different.

He chose to leave the mountain and become an outer disciple of the Sword Sect.

To put it nicely, he was part of the outer sect, no longer cultivating on the Sword Sect's sacred mountain, returning to his homeland instead.

The Sword Sect generally approved such requests.

The Sword Sect guarded the Sword Gate, cultivating the human race, in order to resist demons and monsters.

After joining the Sword Sect and learning their sword arts, if one had no hope of receiving the true transmission, a large portion of disciples would leave the mountain.

And Wu Shaojie was one of them!

"After the incident on the moon, Senior Brother Gao's fighting spirit was not extinguished. It was I who saw him off."

As for Gao Shuli, he roamed the world, traveling through the nine states. After leaving the northern lands and entering the Green State, Wu Shaojie never heard from Gao Shuli again.

The woman beside Wu Shaojie let out a bitter laugh.

"Senior Brother Gao was indeed worthy of being the young master of the Gao Family, undaunted by hardship. Perhaps in the future, Senior Brother Gao will be able to catch up to those who received the true transmission."

The woman had one empty sleeve - this was Xia Qingzhou.

Not long after returning from the moon, the Sword Sect's trial began.

The two of them chose to leave the mountain, which was how they reconnected.

Xia Qingzhou had joined the sect for less than a year and was still only at the Qi Refining Realm, but she had already lost her desire to pursue the path.

"How many have received the true transmission in the Sword Sect? Although Senior Brother Gao has lofty aspirations, he is still far inferior to those who received the true transmission."

Wu Shaojie sighed deeply.

Even the elders in their families did not dare to intervene.

Follow Gu Jiuqing?

They were merely from great families, unworthy to follow Gu Jiuqing.

At the Sword Gate, there was a nine-day great examination.

Gu Jiuqing faced discrimination from the young disciples of the Ancient Heaven City.
