The world was in chaos. In recent days, the atmosphere at the Sword Sect had also become somewhat abnormal. One after another true disciple, even Sword Immortals, flew down from the divine Sword Mountain and walked out of the Sword Sect's gate, heading towards the northern lands.

There were also some inner door disciples who had not yet reached the Primordial realm, leaving the mountain under the pretext of entering the Great Zhou Dynasty.

At that time, there was news circulating within the Sword Sect that slaying the demonic witch could make one become the number one of the younger generation.

When this news spread out, more disciples left the mountain.

As the sun rose, among the layered mountain peaks, there were noticeably fewer disciples doing their morning practice on each mountaintop.

The disciples cultivating and circulating purple mist to refine their inner power had partly left the mountain, while the remaining were mostly those of weaker cultivation bases.

They lacked confidence in their own cultivation, which was why they did not participate in this trial of slaying the demonic witch.

As the sun rose higher, a wisp of purple mist circulated, making the entire divine mountain appear like a Purple Cloud Mountain under the mist's reflection. After a while, the Purple Cloud Mountain illusion disappeared, and the Sword Sect regained its tranquility.

In the Heavenly Sword Palace, Gu Jiuqing withdrew his gaze. In recent days, he had seen quite a few sword cultivators descending from the True Lineage Islands.

"Senior Brother! When will you leave the mountain?"

Lu Yuan asked urgently.

Most of the senior brothers among the true disciples of the Sword Sect had already left the mountain.

Even Senior Brother Liang Xiao had been sent down the mountain by the Supreme Elder. There was also the Sword Son of the Sword Sect, who was the first disciple to descend the mountain.

Of the nine great peaks of the Sword Sect, just the true disciples alone numbered in the hundreds. Now half of the true disciples were traveling the world, heading to the Great Zhou to search for the demonic Seventh Celestial Lord!

Seeing Gu Jiuqing's indifferent expression, Lu Yuan proceeded to recount the events of the past few days.

"Senior Brother, in investigating the matter of the great tomb of the Long Life Immortal Lord within the Great Zhou for you, I've encountered some difficulties."

Lu Yuan cultivated the Eight Phases Divine Eye, able to see through people's hearts and discern truth from falsehood. He could even sit within the Sword Sect and gaze thousands of miles into the distance.

Relying on this divine eye, coupled with the assistance of the Lu family, the information he obtained about the great tomb of the Long Life Immortal Lord would at most be delayed by a day.

"Within the borders of the Great Zhou, the people of the Nine Provinces Governor have already surrounded the great tomb of the Long Life Immortal Lord. There are also the military forces of the Great Zhou royal family advancing towards the great tomb! It is said that the one leading the troops to station at the Long Life Immortal Lord's great tomb is none other than Princess Chengxiao."

"The Dao Child of the Dao Sect has yet to appear, but there are quite a few disciples of the Buddha Sect who have entered the world, all of them students of the current Buddha Lord."

The Buddha Lord!

One of the three Transcendent Immortal realms, ranked second in the world.

He had eighteen disciples who entered the world, collectively known as the Eighteen Arhats!

"The Celestial Maiden of the Celestial Pavilion was already at the Immortal Lord's great tomb, but someone saw the Celestial Maiden appear at the Southern Ridge's Thirty Thousand Great Mountains!"

Lu Yuan spoke in detail, "The Crown Prince of the Great Zhou is still searching for the Great Celestial Lord, but that Great Celestial Lord seems to have disappeared without a trace, and has yet to show himself."

The Great Celestial Lord!

Disappeared?

Gu Jiuqing frowned. Ever since Gu Linglong gifted him the Coffin Armament, Gu Linglong seemed to have vanished!

In the letter, Gu Linglong said he would bring the Princess of the Great Zhou to Gu Jiuqing for a marriage proposal.

But Gu Jiuqing had been waiting for Gu Linglong's letter, and it never came.

Could something have happened?

Time flew by quickly, yet Gu Jiuqing remained in the Heavenly Sword Palace.

Another five days passed.

On that day, Monk Cunyin came to the Heavenly Sword Palace and personally inquired when Gu Jiuqing would descend the mountain.

For the true disciples' trial, almost all of the Sword Sect's true disciples had left the mountain to travel, regardless of whether they were contending to be the number one of the younger generation or tempering their mental state.

This mountain!

He would have to descend as well.

Gu Jiuqing excused himself by saying his cultivation was about to break through, delaying his departure.

Five days later, another five days passed.

Lu Yuan visited the Heavenly Sword Palace again, this time bringing major news.

"The person ranked twelfth on the Dragon and Tiger Ranking, Qu Lizhou, battled against my Sword Sect's true disciple, the Blazing Sun Sword Immortal, at the Great Enlightenment Mountain. Qu Lizhou was defeated by the Blazing Sun Sword Immortal's single sword strike. The twelfth rank on the Dragon and Tiger Ranking has now been taken by the Sword Sect's Blazing Sun Sword Immortal!"

This was the first time a disciple from the four great sects had made the ranking.

It shook the world, causing tremors in the cultivation realm.

Who was this Blazing Sun Sword Immortal?

To actually defeat Qu Lizhou?

Later, news spread that the Blazing Sun Sword Immortal was one of the three sword prodigies of the Sword Sect. A hundred years ago, he had already begun traveling the world, having long since reached the Sword Immortal realm and was only one step away from the Celestial Realm!

With this, the storm surrounding this battle finally settled down.

"Who is this Qu Lizhou?"

Why would Senior Brother Blazing Sun challenge him?

From what Gu Jiuqing knew of Senior Brother Blazing Sun, he did not seem like someone who pursued fame and fortune, and would not provoke Qu Lizhou without reason.

Lu Yuan had already investigated Qu Lizhou's background.

Qu Lizhou!

From the Scholastic Palace of the Great Zhou, his erudition was highly regarded, having been personally received by the Great Preceptor Banshan. In the Great Zhou, he was renowned as the "Young Libationer."

Gu Jiuqing smiled faintly.

If he had not obtained the Blue Star cultivation method, Qu Lizhou would have been the kind of talented genius he aspired to become.

He might even have sought an audience with Qu Lizhou when entering the Great Zhou, depending on Qu Lizhou's attitude!

But now, in his eyes, such a celebrated scholar from the Scholastic Palace was merely an ordinary person, not worth mentioning.

"There have also been sightings of the Seventh Celestial Lord of the Demonic Sect. She is within the great tomb of the Long Life Immortal Lord. It is said that even when Princess Chengxiao personally took action, she was unable to kill her, and instead allowed her to escape!"

Gu Jiuqing felt no interest.

Lu Yuan sensed this and wisely kept silent before leaving the Heavenly Sword Palace.

Six days later, news came from the Great Zhou that among the Buddha Lord's eighteen Arhat disciples, two of them had battled and defeated the person ranked eighth on the Dragon and Tiger Ranking by the Luoshui River in Jiangnan. The Buddha Sect had taken the top spot on the Dragon and Tiger Ranking that day.

Another three days passed, and a scholar from the Scholastic Palace manifested a dragon with his left hand and a phoenix with his right, exhaling the aura of the vast universe. He suppressed a sword cultivator from the Sword Sect, and his fame rose greatly, earning him the title of "Dragon and Phoenix Scholar."

A few more days went by, and of all the True Lineage Islands of the Sword Sect, only the Heavenly Sword Palace Island of the Naming Peak lineage still shone with divine light.

The other islands had all dimmed, as the true disciples had completely left the mountain. The sect elders had also descended, secretly protecting the true disciples in the shadows.

When the disciples left the mountain, the sect would naturally choose to protect them secretly, acting as guardians.

The Dao Sect and Buddha Sect did the same.

Therefore!

During this period, it was actually safest to travel the world.

Although they did not know each other, they generally would not go for the kill, only going as far as exchanging blows.

It was not easy for the great sects to nurture elite seedlings, so they would not simply kill each other. How else could the younger generation grow?

However!

If even the guardians were killed, then naturally no one would know about it.

And such incidents did occur during each great era, but not frequently.

In the Spirit Communication Cultivation Room, Gu Jiuqing felt vexed.

There was still no news from Gu Linglong, not even a letter.

Calculating the time, it had been over a month.

However, the news from the Blue Star temporarily dispelled Gu Jiuqing's vexation.

"The Valley God Star! The Creation God Star! They're about to fly into the moon!"

The Blue Star, the moon.

Two beams of light circulated in the silent starry sky, moving at a rapid speed as if about to collide with the moon. But when they were only a hundred kilometers away, the divine lights dimmed, and two palm-sized stars were captured by the researchers at the lunar base.

On the moon, a grand formation sensed this and emitted divine light, guiding the Valley God Star and Creation God Star.

Under the guidance of the formation, the palm-sized stars flew into the array, interweaving strands of starlight and gradually condensing into two marble-sized star pearls.

The two star pearls solidified, transforming into two divine armaments suspended in the void.

Strand after strand of markings flickered on the star pearls, a thousand markings engraved on the divine armaments.

This method of refining divine armaments used stars as the material, tracing the Dao and principles with the stars' origins. After returning, the stars became thousand-markings divine armaments.

Soon, these two grand formations on the moon began collecting traces of other large stars to capture and refine them into stars.

In other areas of the moon, there were more grand formations attracting and refining large stars.

Within the Spirit Communication Cultivation Room, Gu Jiuqing toyed with the two star pearls in his hand.

These two star pearls were refined from the Valley God Star and Creation God Star of the Blue Star.

Swoosh!

Another star pearl flew out from within Gu Jiuqing's body.

This was a star pearl refined by the Celestial Pavilion.

This star pearl was one circle smaller than the Valley God Star and two circles smaller than the Creation God Star. It was a star pearl casually carried by the previous Celestial Lord!

"It seems the Valley God Star and Creation God Star were among the slightly larger stars in the vast wilderness."

The two star pearls intertwined with markings. Gu Jiuqing casually absorbed them into his body, using his inner power to refine the restrictions within the star pearls, refining the divine armaments!

His vital energy surged as the Dragon and Elephant Realm churned. With his inner power refining like a furnace, coupled with the power of the Naming Peak's Second Cycle Fourth Sequence Sword Intent, it only took four hours to refine the two thousand-markings divine armaments.

Two more thousand-markings divine armaments appeared on Buzhou Mountain.

The thousand-markings divine armaments of the Valley God Star and Creation God Star!

Seemingly the coffin of the Long Life Immortal Lord!
