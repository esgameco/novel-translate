The Sword Sect was powerful, sure.

"How did Gu Linglong obtain the Three Zhang Golden Body Technique from the Small Thunder Sound Temple?"

Could Gu Linglong match someone of the Spirit Refining Realm?

And even win?

This made Gu Jiuqing feel utterly incredulous. When did his sister become so powerful?

Luoshen held a golden talisman and walked towards the main hall entrance, while Gu Jiuqing was still pondering.

From outside the mansion, strange sounds were heard.

"Dong dong dong ----"

Like the ringing of a huge bell, it entered the Heavenly Abyss Ancient City.

"Dong dong dong --------"

The sky over the ancient city had changed!

"The Celestial Palace!"

"It's the Qu family's Celestial Palace!"

The Qu family's Celestial Palace?

"Rumble rumble rumble ----"

"Rumble rumble rumble --------"

In the ancient city, at the Qu family mansion.

Men, women, old and young - any Qu family disciple who had cultivated spiritual power flew up into the heavens, shouldering the ancestral temple.

The ancestral temple was enormous, the site of the Qu family's incense offerings over thousands of years, enshrining the spirit tablets of the Qu family's ancestors.

At this moment, unfilial descendants were moving the ancestral temple, with hundreds, even thousands of Qu family disciples shouldering it.

The entire ancient city witnessed this scene, gazing in shock at the Celestial Palace, unaware of what had happened.

"That's the Qu family's ancestral temple!"

"What? The Qu family's ancestral temple? But that's a powerful artifact, why would the Qu family mobilize such a powerful artifact?"

"The Qu family is the closest to being a great clan in the Heavenly Abyss Ancient City. With this move, all the great clans in the ancient city will have to act."

All beings were like soldiers!

The wind carried omens!

Gaze after gaze watched the Celestial Palace, observing those figures shouldering the ancestral temple, filled with shock and confusion.

"That's the current Qu family patriarch! The Qu family patriarch is even shouldering the ancestral temple?"

"Qu Wushuang! What is he going to do? Shouldering the ancestral temple and ascending to the Celestial Palace? Could it be that the Qu family's ancestors are going to mobilize the Celestial Palace?"

The Qu family's most powerful divine ability was the Yin-Yang Life and Death Wheel!

But the Celestial Palace was even more renowned.

"All the living elders of the Qu family have appeared. So this is the depth of the Qu family? These elders have formed divine bodies, the Yin-Yang divine bodies of the Qu family!"

"Eighteen divine bodies! The Qu family's depth is still so formidable."

For an ordinary great clan, having even one divine body expert could ensure its prosperity for a century.

Yet the Qu family had manifested eighteen divine body experts all at once.

At this moment, everyone could see that the Qu family was targeting the Gu family!

Inside the Gu family courtyard.

Steward Xie was on high alert!

Plus eighteen at the imperfect realm!

Once deployed, it meant they would fight to the death!

Gu Jiuqing joined the true lineage of the Sword Sect?

Attained the true transmission position?

Up in the sky, the Qu family disciples carrying the ancestral temples knelt without rising.

At the main hall entrance.

Luoshen turned around, looking at Gu Jiuqing in surprise.

"Young Master, you joined the Sword Sect and attained the true transmission?"
