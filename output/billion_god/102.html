Madman!

In the Great Wilderness, there was a cultivator who was praised as a madman by demons and monsters.

This madman began nine hundred years ago, constantly entering and exiting the Great Wilderness, hunting and killing demons and monsters.

In this era, demons and monsters were a great calamity for the human race. There were countless demon caves with millions of demon armies stationed, and within each demon cave was a supreme demon monarch.

And this madman was truly formidable. He traveled while masked, entering and exiting the Great Wilderness, hunting and slaying demons and monsters.

Not only did he hunt and kill demons and monsters, but in the places the human madman passed through, decomposing demon remains could occasionally be seen.

Three to four hundred years ago, the decomposing demon remains turned into sliced demon corpses.

Their dantians, heavenly spirit apertures, celestial platforms, celestial palaces, primordial spirits were all sliced into pieces. Their origins did not disappear but were solidified together, simply discarded in the desolate mountains and valleys of the Great Wilderness.

It would be one thing if it was just a single demon corpse, but there were dozens or even hundreds of them!

Every time this madman entered the Great Wilderness, hundreds of demons would be sliced up, and some demons would be taken away.

The demons set up defenses to block the human madman, but all ended in failure.

Later, the demon patriarchs at the catastrophe realm took action.

The first time, the human madman was defeated and fled in disorder back to the Sword Gate Pass.

Decades later, when the human madman re-entered the Great Wilderness, that catastrophe realm demon patriarch was sliced up, shaking the demons of the Great Wilderness.

Over a hundred years later, four catastrophe realm demon patriarchs joined forces to surround and kill the human madman, but this madman still managed to escape. In that perilous moment, the human madman injured two catastrophe realm demon patriarchs, took away one at the catastrophe realm, and killed one catastrophe realm demon on the spot!

Such power shook the demon sovereigns, and demon sovereigns, true lords, and supreme lords all showed signs of alarm.

After this incident, the human madman remained quiet for over a hundred years.

The most recent time was when the seventh son of the demon sovereign was taken away by the human madman, who also killed the protector deities of the seventh demon prince on the spot.

The human madman!

His name struck more fear in low-level demons than even the four great patriarchs of the human race.

Demon after demon retreated, looking at the white-robed cultivator standing before them with constantly flickering gazes.

"I remember that madman, he favors white robes. Madman, white robe, that must be him!"

Some demons cried out in horror, their auras undulating unceasingly, disrupting the void.

"Great Prince, please open the heavenly realm in this madman's palm!"

A demon at the celestial palace realm stood beside the great demon prince, pointing at the heavenly realm in Gu Jiuqing's hand.

"That madman comes from the Sword Gate Pass, an inheritor of the true sword teachings. The sword techniques of his lineage from the Namingtingfeng Peak are the great Namingtingjian sword aura."

The 22 zhang tall celestial palace demon's true form wavered, his eyes unable to conceal his panicked look.

The white-robed youth glanced back at him, and the demon hastily averted his gaze.

The closest demon cave to the Sword Gate Pass belonged to the demon sovereign's clan.

They had fought the Sword Gate for thousands of years and were well-acquainted with it.

They had even glimpsed the profound intent of the great Namingtingjian sword aura, which condensed the meaning of the ancient heavenly realm.

"The white-robed youth's appearance, the madman, the heavenly realm - this person must be that human madman."

The 22 zhang celestial palace demon's true form shrank, standing at the feet of the great demon prince like a huge crouching meatball.

The great demon prince's gaze remained fixed on Gu Jiuqing.

This was also his first time dealing with this human madman.

The white-robed youth's aura was suppressed by the Incomplete Mountain, making it impossible to discern. But his physical body seemed quite formidable, able to move through the air without the slightest leakage of physical power, with excellent spiritual control.

And that heavenly realm!

Just a glance was enough for the great demon prince to be captivated by the mythical heavenly realm. This ancient heavenly realm was too exquisite.

The heavenly realm was illusory, the three realms illusory!

Thirty-three layered heavens pressed down above the heavenly realm, the Milky Way blocked the human realm and Great Wilderness below, with the stars of the celestial realm hanging upside down, and the terrifying six realms of reincarnation swirling beneath the Great Wilderness. The profound meaning of those six realms of reincarnation made the great demon prince's eyelids twitch.

Celestial palaces dotted the realm, inhabited by immortals. Below were the four great continents dividing the Great Wilderness.

"Is it the four great sects dividing the great zhou dynasty, or the meaning of dividing the Great Wilderness?"

The great demon prince stared at the white-robed figure, asking the eight zhang tall demon holding up the female cultivator.

"Who is that person?"

As the great demon prince's voice fell, the female cultivator became alarmed.

She had already recognized this white-robed cultivator's identity.

It was Senior Brother Gu!!

Her senior martial brother who joined the Sword Gate in the same period as her.

Xia Qingzhou looked flustered, completely unaware of what Gu Jiuqing was doing.

That was the demon army, with a demon prince stationed there. She had personally witnessed Qingyang Bai's head being flicked off by the great demon prince, still hanging on the spirit tree.

When Senior Brother Lieyang tried to take the Moon Ship away under the spirit banner, the great demon prince sealed off heaven and earth, even extinguishing a thunderbolt strike from the ancient thunder pool with a single palm!

For Senior Brother to so brazenly appear before the demon army, wasn't he seeking his own death?!

A madman!

Had Senior Brother gone mad?

Xia Qingzhou was a disciple of the Xia Family, considered quite intelligent. After a few contemplations, she realized Gu Jiuqing would not act so recklessly.

The first breath passed, but Xia Qingzhou did not respond.

"The Great Prince asked you a question, so answer!"

The eight zhang demon reached out and tore off Xia Qingzhou's arm.

With a ripping sound, fresh blood dripped down as her entire arm was severed.

The pain struck her heart, but Xia Qingzhou remained silent!

She looked at the white-robed figure, hearing the shocked cries of the demon army, and coldly snorted.

"Hmph!"

"Who else could it be?"

"He is that madman from Namingtingfeng Peak!"

Xia Qingzhou did not speak, but Gu Jiuqing was indeed from Namingtingfeng Peak, and his current actions were those of a madman.

The great demon prince withdrew his gaze. This female cultivator did not lie.

The white-robed youth before him was indeed the human madman.

The great demon prince's figure stirred, his aura swelling as demon qi billowed forth from his body like clouds, condensing into his true demon form below the waist.

It was an eight-armed, 28 zhang tall demon, far larger than the eight zhang true form of the eighth demon prince, emanating boundless demon qi.

"I have heard much of your exploits in the Great Wilderness. Today I finally meet you in person."

"Indeed, a hundred praises cannot match a single glimpse. You truly possess unmatched elegance, worthy of being my opponent."

The great demon prince!

He gave the white-robed youth sufficient respect.

The white-robed youth stepped forward, glancing back at the great demon prince!

This look was quite particular!

First he looked at the great demon prince's true form - the majestic 28 zhang eight-armed demon body emanating boundless demon qi.

Then he looked at his dantian! Observing it with the naked eye!

Next he looked at the great demon prince's Incomplete Mountain, followed by the celestial platform formed from his 34th vertebra, and finally at the great demon prince's head!

Only after examining this head did the white-robed youth meet the great demon prince's gaze.

This guy!

He's putting immense pressure on me.

This examination made the great demon prince feel like he was about to be sliced up by this madman.

The white-robed youth said nothing. After appraising the great demon prince, he carried the heavenly realm and step-by-step walked to the entrance of the Moon Ship.

On the Moon Ship, figures were covered by boundless lightning drawn from the ancient thunder pool!

Gu Jiuqing calmly looked at the closed entrance, then towards the sword immortal under the spirit banner.

"What's wrong? You don't dare face me now?"

Lieyang Sword Immortal!

A sword pill flew over his head, and in his hand was a nine-sectioned sword that had come apart, only the two sections of the hilt remaining!

This divine weapon had cultivated over three hundred sword traces, but was damaged in this great battle.

He looked at Gu Jiuqing in alarm, unsure of what to do.

"It's the one from Namingtingfeng Peak!"

On the deck, only seven or eight Sword Gate disciples remained alive. Two sword cultivators cried out in unison upon seeing the white-robed youth below the Moon Ship.

Wu Shaojie and Gao Shuli looked at the gracefully white-robed youth with mixed feelings.

Wasn't this the sword dao's paramount figure that Luryuan had spoken of?

Yet he ignored the demon army, ignored the great demon prince, and simply walked right up to the Moon Ship?

And that great demon prince?

He just watched??

It was all too bizarre!

Everything about this was inexplicably strange.

Lieyang Sword Immortal offered up an ancient spirit symbol, and the Moon Ship's entrance opened in the next moment.

The white-robed figure entered the Moon Ship.

It was only after the white-robed youth boarded that Gu Jiuqing realized his back was already drenched in sweat.

Lieyang was about to ask Gu Jiuqing something as he descended from the spirit banner.

"Let's go to the cabin first, this place is unsuitable for discussion."

Upon hearing this, the Sword Gate disciples all closed their mouths and followed Gu Jiuqing into the cabin.

Outside the Moon Ship, the great demon prince watched the departing Sword Gate disciples and chuckled.

"The appearance of the human madman has given these Sword Gate disciples a glimmer of hope."

The two celestial palace demons beside him remained silent.

The madman was inside, who dared make a sound?

He turned to the eight zhang great demon, "Where is the Eighth Prince? Why hasn't he come to see me yet?"

This...

The great demon was at a loss, when suddenly a demon flew in from the sky, crying out in alarm.

"Disaster!"

"The Eighth Prince has been killed by a human!"

"And the human even took the Eighth Prince's remains."

The great demon prince's figure wavered, the demon qi below his waist churning chaotically.

"Jiang Buyu! Well done Jiang Buyu, you really think being a descendant of Jiang Xingyu allows you to kill two princes of my demon clan?"

Jiang Xingyu! The name of the Sword Gate's patriarch.

Jiang Buyu was the personal name of the Sword Gate patriarch's son.

Xing the clouds and raining down - the Sword Gate's inherited legacy!

Inside the Moon Ship's cabin.

The last time he entered this cabin, the Three Sword Immortals were present. But today, only Lieyang Sword Immortal remained.
