One hundred and twenty li away from the county town was an ancient city called Dawn Moon City.

That Sword Sect inheritor was in Dawn Moon City.

With these Sword Sect disciples under his control, he believed that inheritor would eventually fall into the trap.

In Dawn Moon City!

He had long sensed the auras of those two catastrophe-level celestials.
