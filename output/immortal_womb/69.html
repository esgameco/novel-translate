Chapter 69

Here is the full translation of the Chinese novel excerpt:

Just as Qin was gathering experts, preparing to annihilate the Mohists.

Ying Zheng suddenly received important news from the palace.

Shu Fei's child was about to be born!

Upon hearing this news, Ying Zheng couldn't even be bothered about the Mohists and ran straight to Shu Fei's palace.

What Mohists? How could they be more important than this child?

Shu Fei was currently less than seven months pregnant, how could she be giving birth now?!

Could there be any problems?

Could it be the impact of the assassin incident last time?

Ying Zheng was extremely anxious, fearing that there would be problems with the child.

He rushed into Shu Fei's palace like a whirlwind.

As soon as he entered, Ying Zheng heard Ying Chen's inner voice.

[Is this prince finally going to be born? Although it's a bit early, I can't wait any longer.]

[Fortunately, the Imperial Extreme Dao Sutra has broken through to the Imperial Qi Foundation Establishment realm. In a moment, I can recast my flesh and grow rapidly. I hope Brother Zheng won't be shocked.]

Ying Zheng was stunned upon hearing the inner voice, standing still on the spot.

This brat has already reached this level while still in his mother's womb?

Not only did he anticipate his own birth.

Moreover, recasting flesh and growing rapidly?!

He can even do such things?

What does it mean? Is he preparing to grow up immediately after being born?

Ying Zheng couldn't help but imagine a baby growing into a child immediately after landing.

This... if someone saw this, wouldn't they be scared to death?

It's only him who can always hear his son's inner voice and know his great abilities.

If it were someone else, they would definitely not be able to understand.

No, this matter must be kept secret!

Ying Zheng began to ponder whether it was feasible to conceal the existence of this child.

First, this child already has his own intelligence.

He already knows that he is Shu Fei's child and may not be willing to leave Shu Fei's side.

The second is the key issue of identity.

He must bring this child to his side to raise him. When a child of unknown age suddenly appears by his side, how should he explain it to the people around him?

He is fearless of others' rumors, but doing so would not be good for the child's future, and many people would doubt his identity.

Moreover, in the future, this son of his will definitely be a stunning talent, and no one will be able to obscure his brilliance.

At that time, when he wants to hand over Qin to this son, the lack of a clear identity will be another trouble.

After much thought, hiding this child is not beneficial after all.

Ying Zheng was troubled by his thoughts.

In the end, he gave up on this idea.

It's better to just let nature take its course and directly expose the growth process of this son.

Doing so will certainly cause the world to be shocked and astonished,

But after a period of time, everyone will get used to it.

Just like when he first heard his son's inner voice.

And as the son of him, the First Emperor, of course it's better to show more extraordinary abilities.

Giving him an innately sacred title will make it even more apparent that our Great Qin is favored by heaven and has the Mandate of Heaven. Isn't this very good?

After a moment, Ying Zheng made a decision in his heart.

No more hiding! He wants to openly announce to the world that this is his son! The future of Great Qin!

Seeing Ying Zheng standing still for a long time, Zhang Han reminded: "Your Majesty, the other concubines in the harem have now all sent their spies to come observe. Should we drive them away?"

The corners of Ying Zheng's mouth revealed a hint of a cold smile.

Upon hearing the news, this group of people ran faster than him, the birth father.

The abacus in their hearts is moving too fast, they don't even have time to pretend on the surface anymore.

Ying Zheng waved his hand: "No need to bother, just let them watch! Let them have a good look! It's just that I hope they won't be scared later."

Hearing Ying Zheng's reply, Zhang Han was puzzled.

What does that mean? Why would they be scared?

Could it be that Concubine Shu's child will be very shocking?

Ying Zheng summoned the imperial physicians and instructed them to ensure that Concubine Shu's delivery goes smoothly this time.

The imperial physicians repeatedly agreed and then went to make preparations.

Just then, a series of exclamations suddenly sounded from outside.

"Quick, look at the sky, what is that?!"

Hearing the sound, Ying Zheng immediately strode out of the palace with Zhang Han following closely behind.

The two raised their heads and looked towards the sky.

Only to see in the azure sky, a large patch of golden spots converging from all directions.

Then they stopped above Concubine Shu's palace, circling, and finally formed a huge golden vortex.

The center of the vortex was right above Concubine Shu's bedchamber.

Such a stunning celestial phenomenon can be said to be unprecedented in so many years!

Everyone was shocked to the point of numbness by this sight. They stood still, unable to move their gaze away.

Zhang Han opened his eyes wide, his gaze chasing the golden spots. He could feel that the spots contained a powerful force.

"This is... a vortex of primordial qi? No, this is no ordinary force of heaven and earth!"

Clearly, the scene before him had already exceeded Zhang Han's knowledge.

Ying Zheng stared at the scene with burning eyes.

Suddenly inspired, he thought of the word that often appeared in his son's inner voice. He exclaimed excitedly: "It's not primordial qi, this is imperial qi!"

Zhang Han looked at Concubine Shu's bedchamber, then at Ying Zheng. He was shocked in his heart. Could this be what His Majesty meant just now?

He had long known that the birth of this child would cause a big commotion?
