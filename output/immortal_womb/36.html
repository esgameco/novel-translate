Chapter 36

In the area outside the Shi Ya Prison, Chi Lian and the others were still unaware of what had happened inside.

They were anxiously waiting, ready to support Wai Zhuang at any moment when he came out.

Before long, Bai Feng descended from a high place and said with a grim face, "Someone has come out of the Shi Ya Prison, but it's not Lord Wai Zhuang!"

Chi Lian's face greatly changed.

How could this be?

If the person coming out at this time wasn't Wai Zhuang, did that mean he had already encountered danger?

Seeing Chi Lian's unsightly expression, Bai Feng suggested to everyone, "Let's all hide first and see the specific situation before we make plans."

Although everyone was very worried about Wai Zhuang's situation, there was no better way at the moment, so they could only do this.

Chi Lian stood out and said, "No, Zhuang must have encountered danger. I'm going down to find him."

Chi Lian's idea was immediately opposed by Bai Feng.

"If you go down now, you'll run into those people of unknown origin. You're not going to find him, but courting death."

What Bai Feng said was unpleasant to hear, but it was indeed the truth.

The others also pulled Chi Lian back and tried to persuade him.

"We're just temporarily assessing the situation. Don't be impulsive."

"Yes, once we've ascertained the situation, we can go down together."

"You're not the only one who's worried. We're also concerned, so don't act rashly."

Everyone spoke one after another, and finally managed to dissuade Chi Lian.

Chi Lian finally agreed not to act impulsively, but to act together with everyone.

After reaching a consensus, they quickly left the original place they were staying.

Finding a high place, they waited for the people to come.

Soon, the people Bai Feng saw from afar arrived.

It was Zhang Heng, Yue Shen and the others.

After circling around the vicinity a few times, Zhang Heng and the Yin-Yang family discovered some unusual traces.

Those were the traces that Chi Lian and the others didn't have time to eliminate because they left in a hurry.

Xing Hun said, "It's obvious they haven't gone far. What should we do? Should we chase them?"

After having Shadow Secret Guards check the surroundings, Zhang Heng shook his head and said, "The people of Liu Sha are not weak. They can't be dealt with in a short time. Since we couldn't catch up, it will be difficult to catch up."

"Let's go. We need to report back to His Majesty as soon as possible. This matter is more important."

Zhang Heng wished he could fly back immediately to tell the emperor the news about the Luo Web that he learned.

Yue Shen and the others naturally didn't care.

In any case, the order they received was to deal with Wai Zhuang, who appeared in the Shi Ya Prison with the intention of breaking the jail.

Now that the mission was completed, there was no need to pursue relentlessly.

Zhang Heng and the Yin-Yang family people thus reached a consensus and left.
