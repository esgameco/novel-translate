Chapter 41

Upon hearing Zhao Gao's words, Hu Hai was startled.

Zhao Gao's intention was to directly eliminate Shu Fei?!

Indeed, this was an excellent method. Once a person dies, all the love and favor would be gone.

In fact, he had long wanted to do this!

This woman was too much of an eyesore to him!

However, if Shu Fei was directly eliminated, and if the Emperor found out that he was involved...

With the Emperor's temperament, he would definitely not let him off easily!

Thinking of this, Hu Hai couldn't help but shudder.

But if Shu Fei was not eliminated, what would happen if she continued to be promoted?

At the rate of Shu Fei's promotion, it wouldn't be long before her rank would surpass his mother's!

By then, if Shu Fei gave birth to another prince, wouldn't the status of this newborn prince surpass his own?

A newborn baby would surpass the status he had built up over so many years?

Just thinking about this possibility made Hu Hai gnash his teeth!

Whether it was Shu Fei or this child, they were both a big threat to him!

He would not allow anyone to step on his head!

It was better to eliminate them early, so he could have peace of mind!

Thinking of this, Hu Hai no longer cared about the terrible consequences of being discovered by his father.

He hardened his heart and asked, "Zhao Gao, tell me, what should we do?"

Zhao Gao opened his mouth, but before he could speak, he was suddenly interrupted by a commotion outside the door.

A group of eunuchs arrived outside the door, and the leading eunuch shouted, "Zhao Gao, the Prefect of the Central Secretariat, quickly receive the imperial decree!"

It was an imperial decree directly from the Emperor!

Zhao Gao and Hu Hai, not having time to be surprised, quickly knelt down to receive the decree.

The leading eunuch began to read the imperial decree.

After the long decree was read, there was only one core content.

The First Emperor ordered Zhao Gao to send experts from the Luo Wang to guard the Shi Ya Prison to prevent Gai Nie and Wei Zhuang from escaping.

This task must succeed and must not fail!

If it failed, there would be severe punishment!

Hearing the content of the decree, Zhao Gao was puzzled.

The Shi Ya Prison had always been the responsibility of the Ying Mi Guards, so why was the Luo Wang now responsible for guarding it?

Could it be that His Majesty felt that the strength of the Ying Mi Guards was insufficient to guard the Shi Ya Prison?

If that was the case, it was good news for the Luo Wang.

The Ying Mi Guards had developed rapidly in recent years and had even shown signs of being able to go head-to-head with the Luo Wang.

Zhao Gao had long noticed this and regarded the Ying Mi Guards as a thorn in the side of the Luo Wang.

Unfortunately, His Majesty had always trusted the Ying Mi Guards and had given them many tasks, leaving the Luo Wang with few opportunities to prove themselves.

Now this...

This task was perfect for his Luo Web organization.

Luo Web just lacked an opportunity like this to prove themselves and demonstrate their immense power.

He wanted to make His Majesty understand that the capabilities of his Luo Web far exceeded those of the Shadow Guards.

His Majesty should rely on and make use of Luo Web, not the Shadow Guards!

If this mission succeeded, Luo Web's prestige would certainly rise greatly.

Not only would it increase His Majesty's favor towards Luo Web,

It would also be an opportunity to suppress the arrogance of the Shadow Guards.

Let the Shadow Guards understand how vast the gap was between them and Luo Web.

Make it clear that Luo Web was not an opponent they could surpass!

The lead eunuch, seeing Zhao Gao in a daze, glared at him and said, "Lord of the Central Carriage Department, why have you not yet received the decree?"

Zhao Gao came back to his senses and hurriedly accepted the decree with respect.

The person beside Zhao Gao was very perceptive and immediately handed a thick pouch to the lead eunuch.

The lead eunuch squeezed the thickness of the pouch without any change in expression, a look of satisfaction appearing on his face.

The originally stern eunuch instantly had a smile, and after a few polite exchanges, he left with his men.

Quiet returned to Prince Huhai's residence.

Huhai had also heard the contents of the imperial decree and understood that his imperial father had a task for Luo Web to handle.

He didn't think much of it, only worrying about one thing.

"Zhao Gao, Imperial Father ordered Luo Web's men to guard Demon Fang Prison? Then will the matter you spoke of earlier still proceed?"

Although Lady Shu was just one person, to secretly harm a consort of the rear palace, especially to do it without leaving any traces, required some thought and planning.

Huhai was worried Zhao Gao would hand this matter over to someone else because of the imperial decree.

This matter concerned his own life. Besides Zhao Gao, he didn't feel at ease entrusting it to anyone else.

Zhao Gao understood his misgivings and said with narrowed eyes, "The matter from earlier can only be temporarily put on hold. His Majesty's decree must be prioritized and obeyed."

His Majesty had already stated in the decree that failure of the mission would incur heavy punishment, which sufficiently indicated the importance His Majesty placed on this matter.

Moreover, Zhao Gao would not miss this opportunity.

Huhai understood Zhao Gao's thoughts and nodded in agreement.

There was no other choice, it could only be like this.

In any case, Lady Shu was in the rear palace and had nowhere to go.

Waiting a while before taking action made no difference.

It was just that doing this let that woman Lady Shu off the hook and allowed her to live a while longer.

Thinking of this, Huhai said resentfully, "Let that woman be arrogant a while longer then."
