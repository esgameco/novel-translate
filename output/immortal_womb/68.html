Don't they always think they're so smart?

Don't they always think about using him, the emperor, to overthrow the Great Qin?

If that's the case, then let him make good use of them!

Let them work hard for him and strive to wipe out the remnants of the Mohists!

It's also a good opportunity to squeeze out the remaining value of these two organizations!

This way, when the Shadow Guards finally deal with them, it will save some effort.

Zhang Han understood Ying Zheng's intention.

This method worked very well last time.
