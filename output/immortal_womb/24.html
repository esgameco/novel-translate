Chapter 24

Yingchen didn't want to take action in front of his mother.

Shu Fei was just an ordinary person. If any unusual events occurred, it would scare her to death.

Moreover, there must be some forces supporting Moyu Qilin's visit this time.

If she died, someone would definitely notice the oddity.

Maybe they would even associate it with Shu Fei.

At that time, his existence might be exposed.

Even before he was born, he had such abilities. Not to mention whether it would cause panic in others.

Safety alone was a big problem.

Those malicious people would definitely try every means to deal with him.

Luo Wang, Yin-Yang family, Liu Sha...

By then, what Shu Fei would face would be endless enemies.

Even if he was confident that he could protect Shu Fei, there were only thieves for a thousand days, but no one could guard against thieves for a thousand days.

He still needed to cultivate and couldn't watch over Shu Fei's safety every day.

In short, exposure now would not benefit him at all.

Since that was the case, as long as Moyu Qilin didn't take action for the time being, he would wait and see.

See if there were other solutions.

Next, Yingchen stared at Moyu Qilin's every move.

Then Yingchen discovered that she didn't seem to be here to hurt anyone, but more like she was here to investigate something.

After Moyu Qilin finished lighting the incense and withdrew, she searched all over Shu Fei's palace.

While pretending to be a palace maid busy with chores and chatting with others,

She took the opportunity of doing chores to patrol everywhere and look for places where people might be hiding.

If there was someone behind the scenes giving Shu Fei advice, this person most likely lived here.

And as long as there was a place where people lived, there must be traces.

With her abilities, it was impossible not to find out.

Moyu Qilin believed so.

So she searched very seriously, not even missing a single floor tile in Shu Fei's palace.

But after such a carpet search, she still didn't get any clues.

Moyu Qilin couldn't believe it.

Because she found that Shu Fei's palace was really too normal.

From her perspective, there was nothing suspicious of any mechanism inside the palace.

As for the palace maids and eunuchs, they were even more ordinary than ordinary.

Not a single one of them had practiced martial arts, and even those who could be said to be strong and powerful were barely so.

In her conversations with them, she also didn't find any special person at all.

At least there was no one who was especially favored by Shu Fei or treated specially.

And there was also no one capable enough to play the role of a strategist.

Moyu Qilin was confused.

This was completely different from the intelligence!

So where exactly was this person?

She didn't understand, how could there not be a single clue to be found?

In all her years of carrying out missions, she had never encountered this kind of situation before.

The Shu Fei Palace, which seemed completely unguarded and had its doors wide open, actually didn't have a single weakness?!

Could it be that the person strategizing for Shu Fei was not in the Shu Fei Palace?

But resided somewhere else in the palace?

Perhaps he was in contact with Shu Fei through other means?

If that was really the case, then things would be a bit troublesome.

Mo Yu Qilin felt her head aching faintly.

The rear palace was very large, and there were many people in charge of each place.

If that possibly existing person was not in the Shu Fei Palace, then it would take a lot of time to find them.

She would need to investigate the palaces one by one, as well as the people in charge inside them.

This task was no less than having to turn the entire imperial palace inside out.

Just thinking about this workload made one's scalp go numb.

She didn't know how long it would take for her alone to get this task done.

Forget it, in any case this mission didn't have a set time limit.

She might as well calm down and do it bit by bit.

Mo Yu Qilin planned to wait until nighttime and investigate the surrounding palaces with the Shu Fei Palace at the center.

Gradually expanding the scope of her search.

As long as this person was in the palace, sooner or later there would be a day she would catch them.

Thinking this way, Mo Yu Qilin was not in such a hurry anymore.

After all, she was facing a mountain of work, and being anxious was of no use.

Ying Chen discovered that Mo Yu Qilin seemed to have temporarily settled down, and truly seemed to regard herself as a palace maid, beginning to work diligently.

There were no signs of rash movements.

It seemed that this Mo Yu Qilin was also a rather patient person.

Ying Chen was the same as Mo Yu Qilin, he was also waiting for nightfall.

Ying Chen believed that since Mo Yu Qilin had not left, it meant her goal had not yet been achieved.

He estimated that in the daytime when there were many people and prying eyes, it was inconvenient for her to take action.

So there was a high likelihood she was waiting for nightfall, waiting for when there were fewer people to continue taking action.

If that was the case, then it suited his intentions perfectly.

He also had to wait for nightfall when there were fewer people to take care of her.
