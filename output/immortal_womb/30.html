Chapter 30

At the same time, a person arrived near the Mohist Mechanism City.

It was Zhang Liang, who had left the Xiaoshengxian Village.

Since he only knew that the Mohist Mechanism City was nearby, but did not know its exact location.

He could only come to the vicinity and try his luck.

At this moment, Zhang Liang received a message via carrier pigeon.

He opened it and read, the message inside made him hold his breath.

The few words inside only mentioned one thing.

The Flowing Sand expert Mo Yu Qilin was captured outside the imperial harem.

Zhang Liang's heart trembled.

He had long heard of the reputation of the Flowing Sand expert Mo Yu Qilin.

He was not an ordinary assassin.

Whether it was the art of disguise or his skills, Mo Yu Qilin was not someone that ordinary experts could handle.

And now, this person was quietly defeated in the imperial palace of the Qin State.

Who on earth was the one who defeated Mo Yu Qilin?

And what kind of powerful figure was able to force Mo Yu Qilin to the point where he couldn't even escape?!

Thinking of his speculations, Zhang Liang became more certain that there must be a master behind all these recent events!

It was he who planned and led everything behind the scenes.

Whether it was Gai Nie, papermaking techniques, or this time with Mo Yu Qilin.

Although there was no evidence, he had a strong premonition that the Mo Yu Qilin incident was related to this master.

Either he took action himself, or he planned it.

In any case, if this person was not eliminated, he would be the biggest obstacle to the anti-Qin alliance in the future!

He must find a way to obtain intelligence about this person!

Just as Zhang Liang was pondering, he sensed that people around him were approaching.

And gradually converging towards him.

Although there were many people, he could not sense any malice or killing intent.

He immediately guessed the identity of the arrivals.

Zhang Liang said loudly, "Zhang Liang of the Confucian school, here to visit the Mohist family, please lead the way."

Several Mohist disciples appeared from various places, confirmed Zhang Liang's identity, and explained to him, "So it's Mr. Zhang Liang, we just wanted to confirm your identity, please forgive us."

"However, outsiders who want to enter the Mohist Mechanism City must have their eyes covered."

The Mohist Mechanism City was the most mysterious stronghold of the Mohist school, and also the safest hiding place for anti-Qin people.

This move was only to ensure the safety of the Mohist Mechanism City and avoid the infiltration of spies sent by the Qin King Ying Zheng.

This action made people feel even safer.

Zhang Liang expressed his understanding, he closed his eyes and said, "I understand the rules, do it your way."

Following the Mohist disciples, they entered the Mohist Mechanical City.

It was unknown how long they had walked.

By the time the restrictions on Zhang Liang were lifted and he could see things again, he was already inside the Mohist Mechanical City.

Standing before him was a group of renowned figures in the martial world.

The one wearing a bamboo hat with only the lower half of his face exposed was the Mohist leader.

He stood in the middle of the crowd.

Next to him were Snow Woman, Gao Jianli, Dao Zhi, Big Iron Hammer, Master Ban and other Mohist leaders.

Everyone's eyes were examining Zhang Liang.

In those eyes there was curiosity, but also wariness.

The Mohist leader was the first to ask: "Zhang Liang, what have you come to the Mohists for?"

Zhang Liang did not answer this question, but instead asked: "Have you all received the news of Gai Nie being captured?"

As soon as Gai Nie's name came out, the faces of the Mohist crowd changed.

"Gai Nie? The Sword Saint?"

"Wasn't he a close guard of the King of Qin in the past? How could he have been captured?"

"I heard he defected, so he was captured and brought back."

"Isn't that a great thing then?"

Gao Jianli said coldly: "We naturally know of Gai Nie's great reputation. If it weren't for him, Jing Ke would not have died. He deserves to be caught."

Zhang Liang could see that most of the Mohists felt Gai Nie's fate was his own doing and did not see the deeper situation behind it.

He shook his head and began to recount the strange aspects of Gai Nie's defection.

"On the day Gai Nie defected, I heard it was because Ying Zheng ordered those Yin-Yang masters to ambush him in advance."

"But how did Ying Zheng learn of this news? Even the Shadow Night Guards only discovered his defection after Gai Nie had left."

"Don't you all find it strange?"

For a moment, no one spoke. Clearly, they too had realized the oddity in it.

Gao Jianli looked at Zhang Liang, his tone unfriendly: "What exactly are you trying to say?"

Zhang Liang put forth his speculation: "I speculate that behind Ying Zheng, there is a highly skilled person guiding him, which is why Ying Zheng thought to ambush Gai Nie in advance at Cangyue Valley."

"This person is very mysterious, and currently there is no intelligence related to them. However, I believe Gai Nie, who has been by Ying Zheng's side for a long time, may know something."

The Mohist leader heard the implication in Zhang Liang's words and astutely pointed out his intention: "So, after saying so much, you've actually come to request that we rescue Gai Nie?"

Upon hearing this, Gao Jianli's face changed.

Rescue Gai Nie?!

Rescue the Gai Nie who caused Jing Ke's death?!

Impossible!
