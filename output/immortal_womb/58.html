Chapter 58

In the room next to Ying Chen, there were rustling sounds.

It could almost be confirmed that two people were there at that moment.

The Great Siming and the Lesser Siming had met up.

The two had already searched the periphery and found nothing.

There were no rooms of any special people outside, so it could be basically determined that the person they were looking for was no longer there.

The only place left to search was Concubine Shu's bedchamber.

Perhaps that mysterious person was living in Concubine Shu's bedchamber.

Having made up their minds, the two gestured to each other and were about to enter Concubine Shu's bedchamber.

At this moment, Ying Chen appeared above the two through the wall.

The Lesser Siming was the first to notice something was wrong, and she looked up.

She saw a baby emitting a golden glow all over its body, floating in mid-air.

In the darkness, this scene was so abrupt.

But there was not a hint of evil aura on the suddenly appearing baby.

On the contrary, the baby's body was flowing with golden light, like a god suddenly born into the world in mythology.

It had a kind of transcendent divinity.

It did not make people feel evil in their hearts, only heartfelt admiration and respect.

The baby slowly opened its eyes.

Those eyes, as black as ink, stared at the two people below.

That gaze contained a strong sense of inquiry and warning.

There was no pure innocence of a naive baby at all!

Such a scene had already exceeded the inherent cognition of the Lesser Siming for so many years.

She stared with wide eyes, her mouth slightly open, looking at the scene before her in a daze, not knowing what to do.

This baby was looking at them! He was intelligent!

Did he understand that the two of them were intruders?!

No, before that, who exactly was this baby?!

Why did he appear here?!

...

A series of questions surged into the Lesser Siming's mind.

Almost burning through her brain.

For a moment, it even caused her whole person to become like a wooden puppet.

She could only stand there, staring blankly at the baby in the air, unable to move.

The Great Siming did not notice the Lesser Siming's abnormality.

She was still pondering with her head down and her chin in her hand about how to proceed next.

This room also did not reveal any valuable clues.

Should they go directly to Concubine Shu's room to investigate next, or should they continue to search other rooms?

At this moment, the Great Siming, who was thinking about the problem, suddenly felt her sleeve move.

She looked at the Lesser Siming.

It was the Lesser Siming's hand tugging at her sleeve.

The Great Siming did not understand what she meant. She looked at the Lesser Siming and asked, "Lesser Siming, why are you pulling me?"

The Lesser Siming's gaze was fixed straight up at the sky, not knowing what she was looking at.

She stretched out her slender jade finger and pointed to the night sky.

The Great Siming looked in the direction she was pointing.

Da Siming stammered through this sentence, her tongue tied.

However, no one answered her question.

Shao Siming had no idea either.

She herself had just discovered it and had no clue when this baby had arrived.

As for what exactly this baby was, she was even more clueless.

The two fell into silence for a moment.

Da Siming was the first to lose patience and decided to make a move on the baby to probe the situation.

This baby had suddenly appeared and had already discovered them.

What if it let out a cry or shout, attracting others?!

It was better for them to take the initiative and test it out first.

After all, the other party was just a baby. Perhaps it could be easily dealt with.

In a brief instant, thoughts rapidly turned in Da Siming's mind. A burst of energy appeared in her hand and shot towards the baby floating in midair!

But even if she was going to make a move, Da Siming didn't forget her identity as an intruder.

She didn't use any Yin-Yang family techniques, but only an ordinary burst of energy.

Even such an ordinary energy, if an average person were to come into contact with it, would result in either death or disability.

But this move was completely insufficient in Ying Chen's eyes.

He used the water sword he had just created, appearing in front of him. With a slight wave, it directly slashed through the energy!

Da Siming and Shao Siming exchanged glances.

The next second, without a word, the two fled the room in tacit understanding!

From that previous strike, they had both discovered one thing.

This little fellow who looked like a baby had a strength that was definitely not weak!

It was not an opponent they could easily deal with.

Since their whereabouts had been discovered and they couldn't quickly resolve it,

there was no need to continue. Continuing would bring trouble if it attracted the Shadow Guards or Ying Zheng.

Seeing the two decisively choose to flee, Ying Chen was unhurried.

Now the movement of his divine sense was much more convenient than his physical body.

He wasn't afraid at all of the distance they ran.

He directly disappeared from the room.

When he reappeared in midair, he was already blocking the path of the two Yin-Yang family women.

Without any warning, he suddenly appeared in front of them like this!

The two Yin-Yang family women abruptly halted their steps.

They looked at the figure in midair in disbelief.

What movement technique was this? It was as if he appeared out of thin air?
