Chapter 26

Moyu Qilin didn't expect her true identity to be exposed.

Instantly, the hair all over her body stood on end.

She widened her eyes and scanned her surroundings, desperately sensing the aura around her.

But no matter how hard she tried, the result was the same.

She didn't detect anyone's presence nearby.

Moyu Qilin found it hard to believe.

She asked, "Who are you?! When exactly did you discover me?!"

Ying Chen's voice was steady and calm, "From the moment you appeared before me, you were already exposed."

Receiving such an answer, Moyu Qilin's first reaction was disbelief.

Her disguise technique was the best in the world, how could she be exposed as soon as she appeared?

In this world, so far, there was no one who could see through her disguise technique!

This person must be bluffing her! He was just putting on a show!

Having never encountered setbacks, Moyu Qilin was too confident in her disguise technique.

She couldn't believe that she had failed so easily.

Ying Chen could easily see what Moyu Qilin was thinking.

He chuckled, "Your disguise technique is indeed ingenious, but in front of me, it's full of illusions everywhere, so fragile that it breaks with just a light prick."

Prick?

Hearing this word, Moyu Qilin was stunned for a moment.

Then she suddenly remembered something.

During the day in Shu Fei's palace, when she was lighting incense, she felt something unusual at that time.

Although it was only for an instant, her disguise technique had indeed stagnated!

Could it be that this person made a move?

In an instant, her face turned pale.

Moyu Qilin said in realization, "When I was lighting the incense, it was you who made a move! It was you who affected my disguise technique?"

Ying Chen calmly affirmed, "Being able to remember, you're not too stupid. Tell me, who sent you?"

Although Ying Chen already had a guess in his heart, he still wanted to get the answer from Moyu Qilin's mouth.

Moyu Qilin remained silent on this question.

In just a few short seconds, she thought a lot.

If her disguise technique was broken, wouldn't she be completely exposed in front of this mysterious person?

Doesn't that mean all her actions so far have been seen by him?

And she herself was completely unaware of this person who had been quietly observing her?

If it weren't for this person speaking up, she wouldn't even know of his existence!

Too terrifying!

Such an opponent is too terrifying!

There was actually such a master hidden in the rear palace!

No wonder that Jing Ni didn't even have time to make a move before being discovered.

This person's...

Her cultivation was far above his own.

In an instant, Mo Yuqilin had assessed the other party's strength and had the intention to retreat.

She could not stay here, she must leave this place and immediately pass on this intelligence!

Thinking this in her heart, Mo Yuqilin suddenly began to move.

With a swift speed, she fled towards the outside of the palace!

However, soon after, she discovered to her horror that no matter where she fled to,

That voice always followed her closely like a parasite.

Just like a cat catching a mouse, it always maintained a fixed distance, teasing her.

As if telling her that everything she did was in vain!

She simply could not shake off this person!

Fear gradually gripped Mo Yuqilin's heart.

She tried her best to cast aside the distracting thoughts in her mind, and escape from the imperial palace first before thinking of anything else.

So Mo Yuqilin chose to ignore that voice and instead tried her best to run towards the outside of the imperial palace.

As long as she got outside, with someone supporting her, she would still have a glimmer of hope.

Unfortunately, the hope she held onto was all illusory, and had never existed from the beginning.

Just as Mo Yuqilin was about to escape from the rear palace,

Ying Chen opened his mouth and crushed her only remaining hope, saying, "It seems you don't intend to speak. Since that's the case, then stay here."

In any case, those who sent her were nothing more than Flowing Sand, Luo Wang, Yin Yang family, or at worst those anti-Qin forces.

Afterwards, he could investigate it himself.

Then, Ying Chen's voice turned cold as he spat out the word "kill".

That "kill" filled with murderous aura exploded in Mo Yuqilin's mind along with the transmitted sound!

It was as if a small bomb had suddenly been detonated in her brain!

Mo Yuqilin simply could not withstand such an impact.

In an instant, blood flowed out from her seven apertures!

Mo Yuqilin's vision went black, her head spun and she instantly lost consciousness, her breathing disordered as she rolled off the roof.

The Shadow Guards patrolling outside the rear palace heard such a commotion and all drew their weapons at their waists.

They shouted, "Who goes there?!"

Hearing no response, the Shadow Guards directly headed towards the direction of the sound.

Then at once they saw a suspicious woman, bleeding from her seven apertures, lying on the ground.

The Shadow Guards quickly stepped forward to subdue Mo Yuqilin.

Seeing that Mo Yuqilin had already been captured by the Shadow Guards, Ying Chen retracted his divine sense.

Leave the rest for the Shadow Guards to handle.
