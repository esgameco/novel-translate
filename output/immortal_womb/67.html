Here is the full translation of the Chinese excerpt into English:

Zhang Liang thought of the Shadow Guards and couldn't help but worry.

"The Shadow Guards are known for being as inseparable as a shadow, so they may have already infiltrated the Yujing Lake Medical Manor."

"When the time comes, will they take the opportunity to expose the location of the Mechanical City while we enter it?"

Hearing Zhang Liang's concerns, the Mohist leader smiled faintly: "There's no need to worry about this problem. The Mohists naturally have a solution."

The Mohist Mechanical City took more than 300 years to build and has always been the Mohists' most mysterious fortress.

Both the interior and exterior are filled with traps, making it easy to defend but difficult to attack.

He was confident that even if outsiders knew the location, they wouldn't be able to enter.

Upon hearing this, Zhang Liang said no more and arranged for everyone to pack their things and begin preparing for the retreat.

Everyone packed useful items, weapons, food...

Especially the medicinal materials from Yujing Lake Medical Manor, which could save lives at critical moments and must be taken.

Soon, under the leadership of the Mohists, Zhang Liang, Liu Sha and the others retreated in an orderly manner.

In the shadows, several Shadow Guards followed from afar.

It turned out that since the day the Mohists successfully broke the prison, the Shadow Guards had been following them according to orders.

Seeing that this group of people had been staying at Yujing Lake Medical Manor and showed signs of staying for a long time,

The Shadow Guards decisively decided to kill a Mohist disciple to cause panic among them and make them retreat to their true lair.

Sure enough, after the death of that disciple was discovered, the Mohists took action again.

The Shadow Guards believed that this time they would truly uncover the real Mohist lair.

Each of them was in high spirits and extremely excited.

Once this mission was completed, they would be the meritorious heroes who had made a great contribution!

However, as they followed behind the Mohists, they didn't dare to get too close.

They always maintained a distance from these people to avoid being discovered by the experts.

Soon, the few tracking Shadow Guards were dumbfounded.

Because in plain sight, the enemies they were tracking actually entered the belly of a mechanical beast by the water and sank into the water!

It was one of the Mohists' Four Sacred Beasts, the Mechanical Black Tortoise.

It was an important part of the Mohist Mechanical City.

The Shadow Guards looked at each other. With this large group of people entering the mechanical beast in the water, how could they continue tracking?

Even if they swam down, they would be discovered and then attacked.

With no other choice, the Shadow Guards could only return to report.

In the Xianyang Palace.

Ying Zheng looked tired after finishing the morning court session.

He hadn't slept all night, always feeling uneasy about not hearing the familiar heartbeat.

Although the imperial physician said the child was very healthy, Ying Zheng couldn't help but let his imagination run wild.

What if something happened to this child? What about his method for immortality?

What about the future of his Great Qin?

From now on...

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese novel excerpt:

There was no one who could answer his questions anymore.

Thinking of this, Ying Zheng felt anxious and uncomfortable, as if a hundred claws were scratching at his heart.

It was not until close to dawn, when he heard Ying Chen's inner voice again, that he finally let out a long sigh of relief and put his mind at ease.

With no more worries weighing on his mind, he finally felt in the mood to hold morning court.

Now that morning court had ended, just as Ying Zheng was thinking of going back to catch up on some sleep, the eunuch reported: "Your Majesty, Zhang Han, the commander of the Shadow Guards, requests an audience."

Ying Zheng wanted to refuse at first, thinking that whatever the matter was, it could wait until after he had caught up on his sleep.

But suddenly, a thought stirred in his mind, giving rise to another idea.

Zhang Han was a perceptive man.

Since last night, he had been busy dealing with the matter of the assassin in Consort Shu's palace.

Zhang Han was aware of this, and also knew that he had not slept all night.

Since that was the case, for Zhang Han to seek an audience now despite the pressure, it must be for an important matter.

Thinking of this, Ying Zheng sat back down.

"Let Zhang Han come in."

The eunuch went out to summon Zhang Han according to the order.

Before long, Zhang Han entered.

His face showed a rare look of relaxation.

He reported: "Your Majesty, the Shadow Guards have discovered the whereabouts of the Mohist rebels. After a brief stay at Mirror Lake Medical Manor, they have now gone to the Mohist Mechanical City."

"Unfortunately, the Mohist rebels have entered the mechanical bastion within the Mohist Mechanical City, and the Shadow Guards can no longer track them."

Upon hearing the news brought by Zhang Han, Ying Zheng was overjoyed.

"To think that the location of Mirror Lake Medical Manor has already been found, excellent, excellent! Those anti-Qin rebels have one less place to hide now."

Mirror Lake Medical Manor had always enjoyed a good reputation in the jianghu. It was said that the medical immortals inside possessed superb medical skills and could heal even the most severe injuries.

Ying Zheng had long wanted to find Mirror Lake Medical Manor.

Because the medical sect and the Mohist sect had always been close.

Many of the jianghu people saved by Mirror Lake Medical Manor would join the Mohists under their influence.

Even if they did not join the Mohists, they would provide them with a lot of intelligence out of gratitude for the medical immortals.

Therefore, Mirror Lake Medical Manor could be said to be an outpost of the Mohists.

It had won over many talents and information for the Mohists.

But Mirror Lake Medical Manor was too mysterious, and its location could not be found at all.

Being able to find Mirror Lake Medical Manor this time was a big gain.

As for the disappearance of the Mohists in the water, Ying Zheng did not care.

Since a huge mechanical beast had appeared, it meant that the location of the Mohist Mechanical City was not far away, and it was just a matter of finding it.

No matter what kind of place it was, as long as he knew where it was, he could send troops to level it.

Let these anti-Qin rebels have no more places to hide in the future!
