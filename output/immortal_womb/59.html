Chapter 59

Facing the astonished gazes of the two women, Ying Chen remained calm and composed.

He looked down at them from his elevated position.

Suddenly, he asked, "Who are you?"

Both women were wrapped in tight-fitting night clothes, their faces covered tightly except for their eyes.

Therefore, apart from being women, their other identities could not be discerned.

The two women from the Yin-Yang family had not yet recovered from the baby's strange movements.

Abruptly hearing the baby suddenly speak,

both of them were greatly shocked!

Their pupils constricted slightly, and the breath exhaled from their delicate noses became heavier!

Clearly, they had not expected this baby-like person to be able to speak.

They had originally thought that this baby was acting on instinct.

Now it seemed that was not the case at all!

This baby already possessed the wisdom of an adult!

This was troublesome!

For a moment, the two women kept their mouths shut, not uttering a word, afraid of revealing their true identities.

Ying Chen could see that the two had no intention of speaking, seemingly using silence as a form of resistance.

Ying Chen sneered, "Not talking? Then I'll beat you until you do."

Perhaps it was because they hadn't encountered such an arrogant opponent in so many years, but Da Si Ming was not the least bit concerned.

She didn't take his words to heart, instead coldly retorting, "Beat us then, you really think we're afraid of you?"

Their Yin-Yang family had developed for so many years, and they were no pushovers.

There was no reason to cower like turtles when challenged.

Hearing the reply, Ying Chen knew that these two had no intention of revealing their identities.

He waved his water sword, drawing a fierce arc in the air.

"Whether you're afraid or not, you'll know once you've tried."

With these words, the two from the Yin-Yang family knew that this battle was inevitable and unavoidable.

They had to make a move to determine who was superior.

This baby could not be dealt with using simple techniques.

At this point, Da Si Ming no longer had any intention of probing with simple moves.

She wanted to end it with one fatal strike!

Da Si Ming's hands moved apart, and a red and black light appeared in her palms.

Within that red and black light, a taiji-like pattern appeared, condensing into an ominous Yin-Yang Qi Merging Hand Seal.

Countless hand seals shot towards Ying Chen!

Ying Chen was not flustered in the slightest. With one hand, he reached to the side.

From the nearby pool, streams of water rapidly converged.

In an instant, a constantly revolving water sphere formed around Ying Chen.

The water sphere looked as if it would shatter with a single touch, but it was incredibly resilient, directly blocking all the hand seals!

Ying Chen recognized this technique and suddenly realized, "Yin-Yang Qi Merging Hand Seal? So you're from the Yin-Yang family."

"In that case, if I'm not mistaken, you two must be Da Si Ming and Shao Si Ming."

The two women from the Yin-Yang family held their breath, suddenly feeling a chill in their hearts.

This infant actually knew the techniques of the Yin-Yang family?

And he recognized them with just one move?!

Who on earth was he?

The Great Siming stared at the infant, her mind swirling with infinite murderous intent.

No matter what this infant was.

Now they had to silence the infant!

Otherwise, everything that happened tonight would be leaked out!

With the intention of killing to silence, the Great Siming used the secret technique of the Yin-Yang family - the Skull Blood Hand Seal.

Once activated, a huge blood skull handprint headed towards Ying Chen!

The speed was extraordinarily fast, almost too fast for the human eye to follow!

This move was immensely powerful. After hitting the human body, it could cause the opponent's body to become disordered, veins to flow in reverse, and die in extreme pain.

Very few people could withstand this blow.

Unfortunately, this move was still firmly blocked by that water ball!

It didn't even touch Ying Chen's skin!

Her own attacks had been blocked twice in a row. The Great Siming's expression had already changed.

This infant actually used the same move to block her two attacks!

This showed that no matter his defense or reaction speed, they were all top-notch!

The Great Siming realized that they had encountered a tricky opponent whose strength might be above hers.

But before she could organize the next attack.

Ying Chen had already made the first move.

He waved his water sword, gathering countless water balls of all sizes around him, and then headed towards the Great Siming.

Those water balls were fast, turning into countless ice spikes as they revolved in the air, enveloping the Great Siming.

Then, those razor-sharp ice spikes launched an attack on the Great Siming together!

It was an attack from all directions, continuous and unceasing.

In just a short moment, with so many attacks, the Great Siming couldn't defend at all.

The Great Siming was shocked in her heart, not understanding why a mere infant would have such superb strength.

Who on earth was this person?

Lost in thought, caught off guard, the Great Siming was hit by countless ice spikes.

She felt heat in her throat, spat out a mouthful of blood, collapsed to the ground and passed out.

The Lesser Siming, seeing the situation was dire, immediately used the Myriad Leaves Flying Flower Flow.

A large patch of leaves condensed, blocking between Ying Chen and the two of them.

She wanted to use this to block Ying Chen's line of sight and rescue the Great Siming.

Her plan was good, but unfortunately underestimated the opponent.

When she turned around, she found that Ying Chen, who was originally behind her, had reappeared in front of her eyes.

As if he had been there from the beginning!

The Lesser Siming was horrified in her heart.

Was this guy opposite really just an infant?

Why was he so strong?
