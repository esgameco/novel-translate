Chapter 20

Another side of the Yin-Yang family was still unaware of what happened after Lord Yunzhong left, and how Ying Zheng used ants to test the medicine for poison.

After presenting the elixir, Lord Yunzhong met Dong Huang Taiyi.

The Moon Goddess was also there.

Dong Huang Taiyi was still dressed in all black, looking mysterious and unfathomable.

He looked at Lord Yunzhong and asked, "The elixir, did you give it to Ying Zheng?"

Lord Yunzhong thought of the bizarre series of events that had just occurred and felt that there was no point in hiding anything, so he spoke directly.

With a bitter smile on his face, he said, "I have presented the elixir according to your instructions, and Ying Zheng accepted it at the time, but he did not consume it on the spot."

"I feel that he seemed... seemed to have become suspicious of the elixir."

Upon hearing Lord Yunzhong say this, Dong Huang Taiyi's voice instantly turned cold: "Why is this? Did you perhaps reveal something?"

Hearing Dong Huang Taiyi suspect him like this, Lord Yunzhong felt extremely wronged.

"How could that be? You know me, my every move is according to your instructions, Great Lord Dong Huang, I couldn't have revealed anything!"

If Lord Yunzhong did not reveal anything, how could Ying Zheng suddenly become suspicious for no reason?

In the past, he had never been suspicious and had always looked forward to the day the elixir would be completed.

Dong Huang Taiyi somewhat disbelieved Lord Yunzhong's words.

The Moon Goddess beside them pondered for a moment, thought of something, and joined the conversation.

"When Gai Nie defected before, Ying Zheng suddenly told us to ambush Gai Nie at Canyue Valley. Even we ourselves did not know that Gai Nie would pass through Canyue Valley."

"In fact, Ying Zheng was right, Gai Nie did indeed pass through Canyue Valley."

"Also, that papermaking technique of unknown origin was suddenly instructed by Ying Zheng for Meng Tian to create and promote to the people."

"Perhaps, there is someone we don't know about behind Ying Zheng, always planning for him."

Someone they don't know about?

Who else could there be here that they don't know about?

Although their Yin-Yang family's intelligence network was not as omnipresent as a seamless net, it was still very advanced.

So while this possibility seemed reasonable, upon closer examination, the likelihood was minuscule.

Because there was almost always someone from their Yin-Yang family by Ying Zheng's side.

They had never heard of him favoring any strategist recently.

Even if there really was such a person, there should be some traces.

How could there be no traces revealed even now?

As for the matter of the elixir, it was also a big problem.

It was an elixir they had specially prepared for Ying Zheng, and he must consume it.

Dong Huang Taiyi pondered deeply.

In Concubine Shu's palace, Ying Zheng was reviewing the memorials in his hands.

He marveled that the paper he invented was so easy to use, much more convenient than the previous bamboo slips.

"Concubine Shu's massage skills are not bad. Promote Concubine Shu's rank to Bazi."

The eunuchs and palace maids present envied Concubine Shu's rocket-like promotion speed.

Only Ying Chen, who sensed everything with his divine consciousness, was very puzzled.
