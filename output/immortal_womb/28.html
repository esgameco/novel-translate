Chapter 28

Chapter Han brought Mo Yu Qilin to the Shi Ya Prison.

The escorted Mo Yu Qilin walked unsteadily.

She recalled the intelligence she had received before coming.

She remembered that according to the intelligence, Gai Nie was imprisoned in Shi Ya Prison after being captured.

Could it be that Gai Nie was really imprisoned in this place?

At the thought of this possibility, Mo Yu Qilin began to inconspicuously observe the surrounding prison cells.

Inside, one by one, were different prisoners.

As she passed by a cell, she couldn't help but slow down her pace.

The person imprisoned in that cell was none other than the former Sword Saint, Gai Nie!

Although the currently imprisoned Gai Nie was covered in wounds from head to toe, and his former graceful appearance as the Sword Saint could hardly be seen.

But Mo Yu Qilin still recognized him at a glance!

Gai Nie's miserable state made Mo Yu Qilin look at him a few more times involuntarily.

It seemed that the intelligence from Luo Wang was indeed accurate.

Gai Nie was indeed in Shi Ya Prison.

However, with this, she was at ease.

Although Wei Zhuang kept shouting about killing Gai Nie every day, saying that he would kill him at every turn.

But Mo Yu Qilin knew that Lord Wei Zhuang would definitely come to save them.

He would never watch Gai Nie die at the hands of others.

Noticing Gai Nie's injuries, Chapter Han stopped in his tracks.

He asked the jailer beside him, "Has Gai Nie confessed the detailed reasons for his defection?"

At the mention of this, the jailer shook his head helplessly and sighed, "No, no matter what punishment was used, he just won't say."

Hearing the movement of the two people talking, Gai Nie managed to raise his head and looked towards the speakers.

When he saw Chapter Han and the escorted Mo Yu Qilin, his eyes widened.

The original haziness in his eyes dissipated, revealing a few traces of clarity.

"Mo Yu Qilin?! You were also captured? How is Xiao Zhuang? How is Xiao Zhuang doing?"

Gai Nie's tone was anxious.

He knew Mo Yu Qilin's strength, and if even she was captured, wouldn't Wei Zhuang also be in danger?

Who on earth could make a move to capture Mo Yu Qilin?!

Seeing that Gai Nie still had the strength to speak, Mo Yu Qilin knew that he could still endure those injuries.

She breathed a sigh of relief in her heart.

On the surface, she said indifferently, "Lord Wei Zhuang is doing very well. He even said that even if you die, you have to die by his hands."

These words were very much in line with Wei Zhuang's personality and sounded like something he would say.

But more importantly, Gai Nie understood the other meaning behind these words.

Mo Yu Qilin was telling him that Wei Zhuang already knew he was imprisoned here and would definitely take action to come and rescue him.

Gai Nie couldn't help but smile bitterly.

If possible, he really wanted to tell Wei Zhuang not to come, not to take the risk to come and save him.

However, with Zhang Han present, he could only remain silent to avoid exposing Wei Zhuang's intentions and implicating Wei Zhuang.

Gai Nie's conversation with Mo Yu Qilin fell into the ears of another person.

At this time, a voice came from the prison cell next door, mocking Gai Nie: "Even your junior brother wants to kill you, how much of a failure must you be as a person."

"You say you've devoted yourself to Ying Zheng, but what have you gotten in return?"

The sarcasm in those words almost broke through the sky.

Every word revealed dissatisfaction with Ying Zheng.

Zhang Han took a few steps to the front of that prison cell and kicked the cell bars.

His face was ice cold: "Sheng Qi, are you tired of living? How dare you directly call His Majesty's name?"

The person speaking was Sheng Qi.

He had been captured by the Seven States many times and thrown into death row, but he always managed to escape.

But in the end, he was captured by Gai Nie, who was Ying Zheng's close guard at the time, and thrown into the Shi Ya Prison.

It had been ten years.

Of course he had no good feelings towards Gai Nie, who had caused him to fall into this situation.

From time to time, he would say a few words to mock Gai Nie, who was already used to it.

But Zhang Han could not tolerate his disrespect towards His Majesty.

Sheng Qi's heart had been tempered through the long years without freedom.

Faced with Zhang Han's accusation, he sneered, completely unafraid: "If you have the ability, kill me now."

This fearless attitude immediately enraged Zhang Han!

The veins on Zhang Han's forehead bulged as he almost wanted to end Sheng Qi's life immediately!

But he held back.

Sheng Qi was still useful now, and it wasn't up to him to decide whether to kill him or not.

That required His Majesty's decision.

Sheng Qi must also know this, which is why he dared to provoke him without fear.

Zhang Han would not fall for this trick.

Zhang Han turned and walked away, directly grabbing Mo Yu Qilin and locking her in another cell.

Mo Yu Qilin still didn't seem to show any panic, as if coming here was just a short stay.

Zhang Han knew that she probably still thought she could escape from here.

So, Zhang Han sneered in warning: "Now that you're here, don't even think about escaping. Honestly confess and you'll suffer less. It's better for both of us this way."

Honestly confess, and they would receive less torture, allowing the Shadow Guards to complete their mission sooner.

Of course, Zhang Han also knew that these treacherous rebels would not listen to him.

So he didn't expect them to cooperate obediently.

But the routine warning still had to be said.

After speaking, Zhang Han turned and left.
