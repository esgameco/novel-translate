The other Six Sword Slaves nodded, fully agreeing.

Apparently, in their hearts, Yan Ri and Jing Ni's strength was beyond doubt.

They themselves represented victory and had no possibility of defeat.

The movements of the Six Sword Slaves were restrained.

Not allowing any of them the opportunity to report back.

Amidst the chaos, Zhang Liang, Bai Feng, and Dao Zhi successfully entered the Devouring Fang Prison.

Inside the Devouring Fang Prison, they were still unaware of the three uninvited guests.

The prison guards patrolled everywhere, checking for any abnormalities within the prison.

Zhang Liang glanced at the prison guards and said to the other two, "Time is limited. Our goal is to save people, so don't get entangled with them."

"Otherwise, attracting others will only be a burden for us."

Bai Feng and Dao Zhi nodded, understanding Zhang Liang's meaning.

The two gathered their qi, used their lightness skills, and directly leaped over the heads of the prison guards, rushing inside.

Zhang Liang followed closely behind, his speed not slower than the other two in the slightest.

Bai Feng and Dao Zhi had originally been worried that Zhang Liang wouldn't be able to keep up with their speed, but it turned out they were overthinking.

Dao Zhi exclaimed with great joy, "I didn't expect Master Zhang Liang's lightness skills to be so amazing as well."

Bai Feng also nodded slightly, expressing his affirmation of Zhang Liang.

Zhang Liang humbly said, "You two praise me too much. Compared to you both, I'm still somewhat lacking."

The three used their lightness skills and quickly hurried, advancing into the depths of the Devouring Fang Prison.

Just then, a change suddenly occurred in the Devouring Fang Prison!

The path and terrain ahead were rapidly changing!

In no time, it had become completely different from the previous path!

Bai Feng was greatly alarmed!

His lightness skill faltered for a moment and he nearly fell!

It was also Dao Zhi's first time seeing a path change like this. He was startled for a moment, not knowing which way he should go.

Because the path changed so quickly, the direction they had originally come from and the direction they were originally headed had completely changed.

Before long, the few of them couldn't tell which direction was which.

Zhang Liang steadied his mind and while working hard to discern their current direction, he also comforted the other two.

"You two, don't panic. The Devouring Fang Prison itself is a large mechanism. This is the Devouring Fang Prison's strange transformation. I will make calculations."

As he spoke, Zhang Liang calculated the strange transformation and instructed them on the direction they should head.

"Young Hero Dao Zhi, remember the current changes well. From now on, I will lead the way. You two must follow closely behind me."

Dao Zhi nodded, "No problem, rest assured, I will firmly remember these changes."

Bai Feng had no objections and followed behind Zhang Liang.

The three continued to infiltrate deeper into the Devouring Fang Prison.
