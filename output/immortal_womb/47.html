Soon, the group arrived at the deepest cell in the Shi Ya Prison.

This was the place mentioned in the intelligence, the exit of Shi Ya Prison.

But according to the information, they still needed to find the character "Wang" (望).

For a while, the few people had no clue about finding the character "Wang".

This was also part of the intelligence, something they had never encountered in reality.

How could they find it in a short time?

They searched anxiously, but still couldn't find it.

At this moment, the Jing Ni (惊鲵) that they had left behind had already caught up.

It was getting closer and closer to them.

Before long, it would completely catch up with them!

At the critical moment, Wei Zhuang decisively drew out his Shark Tooth (鲨齿): "You continue to search, I will go stop the Jing Ni."

In this trip into Shi Ya Prison, because of the big loss from Luo Wang (罗网), he had been holding a belly full of anger.

Just now, if Gai Nie hadn't stopped him, he would have stayed to fight the Jing Ni.

But what Wei Zhuang didn't expect was that as soon as he gathered his internal strength, he felt a sharp pain in his body!

Immediately followed by a heat in his throat, he directly spat out a mouthful of fresh blood!

Gai Nie immediately stepped forward to support Wei Zhuang. Upon checking his condition, his face greatly changed: "Not good, Xiao Zhuang has been poisoned!"

Zhang Liang's heart sank. He didn't expect that Shi Ya Prison would use such despicable means.

Poisoning is the most despised practice among the Jianghu people!

Was it because they were wary of the strength of Wei Zhuang and Gai Nie, so they made such a move?

The person who came up with this plan was extremely meticulous in their thinking!

No wonder there were no Yin Yang Masters or Zhang Heng and other Shadow Guards near their cell!

That's because it was unnecessary!

The three people in the cell were poisoned without their own knowledge.

It can be imagined that even if someone came to rescue the three of them and encountered pursuers along the way,

The three would not be able to resist using their internal strength to go and fight.

In that case, the three of them would be poisoned.

It would also implicate the group of people who came to rescue them.

At that time, not only would the prison break plan fail, their large group of people would instead be caught and imprisoned!

What a vicious poisoning scheme!

They almost fell for it!

Zhang Liang felt a lingering fear in his heart and immediately instructed Gai Nie: "You must not recklessly use your internal strength now. If my guess is correct, you and Mo Yu Qilin are the same, you should also be poisoned."

"You three focus on finding the 'Wang' character, leave the Jing Ni to me to stop!"

With that said, Zhang Liang headed towards the Jing Ni without waiting for the others to object.

Seeing that the person coming was Zhang Liang, Jing Ni was very surprised.

But she adapted quickly, countering the moves, and soon started fighting with Zhang Liang.

Would they be unable to withstand the attack of Jingni?

Everyone was overjoyed and immediately called out to Zhang Liang.

Zhang Liang made a fake move, successfully deceiving Jingni, and escaped from the Devouring Fang Prison with the others.

Outside the Devouring Fang Prison.

Chi Lian and the others were besieging Yan Ri.

Although Yan Ri was powerful, facing the siege of the Flowing Sand group alone, he inevitably struggled a bit.

In his plan, he and Jingni would have been enough to deal with these few people from Flowing Sand.

But midway through the fight, he noticed something was wrong.

Flowing Sand and the others kept trying to lure them away.

Whenever he and Jingni wanted to stop, they would deliberately reveal openings to entice their attacks.

Yan Ri decisively let Jingni go back to support, holding off Flowing Sand and creating an opportunity for Jingni.

After Jingni left, under the intensive attacks of Flowing Sand, Yan Ri was wounded.

Seeing their attacks were effective, Flowing Sand and the others launched even more frenzied attacks.

However, after fighting for so long, Chi Lian and the others were not unscathed either.

Almost everyone had light to moderate injuries.

One against many, yet still not falling behind, Yan Ri's strength was evident.

Just as they were locked in a stalemate, a firework signal exploded in the sky.

Yan Ri instantly felt something was wrong and looked at the expressions of Flowing Sand and the others.

Sure enough, upon seeing the fireworks, Chi Lian's eyes lit up and he said, "They succeeded! Let's retreat quickly!"

Hearing this, the faces of the Flowing Sand group all showed joy.

From this moment, the situation of attack and defense changed!

Flowing Sand, who was originally attacking frantically, began to withdraw their offensive, wanting to leave.

And Yan Ri tried to hold back Flowing Sand and the others.

But with him alone, it was impossible to keep so many people from Flowing Sand.

Soon, the Flowing Sand group disappeared without a trace.

Helpless, Yan Ri could only return for now.

However, when Yan Ri returned to the entrance of the Devouring Fang Prison, what met his eyes were the heavily wounded Six Sword Slaves.

Every one of the Six Sword Slaves was injured, with Luan Shen and Wang Liang directly killed by Mo Mei's sword.

This sight made Yan Ri so angry that the veins on his forehead throbbed erratically.

Unable to catch his breath, he directly spat out a mouthful of fresh blood!

This time, the Luo Web suffered a huge loss!
