After Zhang Liang, Liu Sha and the Mohists finalized their plan, they arrived near the Shi Ya Prison.

Worried about encountering an ambush by the Shadow Guards and the Yin-Yang Sect, Zhang Liang, the Mohists, and Liu Sha's group were very cautious and first scouted the surrounding area.

This investigation uncovered an alarming discovery - they actually found traces of the Luo Wang nearby!

Not only were there six Sword Slaves guarding the area, but Yan Ri and Jing Ni were there as well!

What was the Luo Wang doing here?

Could it be that they had already anticipated that Liu Sha would not abandon Wei Zhuang, so they were waiting here in advance to prevent them from coming to rescue Wei Zhuang?

Capturing Wei Zhuang alone was not enough, they really wanted to completely exterminate Liu Sha's group and catch them all in one net!

Thinking this way, Liu Sha's group was instantly furious!

This further confirmed the suspicions in the minds of Liu Sha's group!

The people of the Luo Wang had betrayed them!

They were after Liu Sha's life!

Compared to the enraged Liu Sha, the observer Zhang Liang was much calmer.

He felt that the Luo Wang's appearance here was not targeting Liu Sha, but may have just been following the Emperor's orders.

But this was only his guess, he had no evidence, and he definitely could not get an answer from the Luo Wang.

Therefore, he remained silent and did not say much.

Chi Lian and the others' dissatisfaction with the Luo Wang had reached its peak at this time.

Chi Lian said hatefully: "I must teach the people of the Luo Wang a lesson, especially that bastard Yan Ri!"

The others echoed: "Me too, that Yan Ri guy had evil intentions from the beginning, I won't let him off."

"The entire Luo Wang is dirty, we should annihilate them all!"

The Liu Sha group was impassioned, and angry emotions were spreading among them.

Seeing their situation, Zhang Liang knew that his words could no longer stop them.

After all, he was not a member of Liu Sha, just an outsider, and had no real standing to say anything.

So Zhang Liang could only warn everyone: "Pay attention to safety, don't act impulsively, safety first in everything."

Everyone responded in unison.

Next, everyone discussed and began to assign tasks.

Liu Sha and the Luo Wang had a great grudge, so they took the initiative to deal with Yan Ri and Jing Ni.

The other Mohists would take advantage of Yan Ri and Jing Ni being drawn away to deal with the six Sword Slaves.

Meanwhile, Zhang Liang, Bai Fenghuang, and Dao Zhi would sneak into the Shi Ya Prison while they were fighting.

After the tasks were assigned, everyone dispersed and began to carry out their respective tasks.

Chi Lian, Wu Shuang Gui, Cang Lang Wang, and Yin Fu took the initiative to appear in front of the Luo Wang.

They opened their mouths to provoke: "Yan Ri, you still have the face to appear here shamelessly?!"
