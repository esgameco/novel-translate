Chapter 19

Originally, Ying Zheng had already harbored an overwhelming murderous intent towards the Yin-Yang family.

A bunch of ungrateful things, not worthy of living!

He even wanted to give the order now to arrest all the people of the Yin-Yang family.

But after hearing Ying Chen's inner thoughts, he calmed down instead.

Now the enemy was in the dark, and he was in the light.

He still didn't know if Yun Zhong Jun disguising the poison as an elixir and giving it to him was Yun Zhong Jun's plan alone?

Or was it the plan of the entire Yin-Yang family?

If it was Yun Zhong Jun's plan alone, then killing him would suffice.

If the people within the Yin-Yang family were also involved, or even if this matter was carried out under the manipulation of the Yin-Yang family.

Then things would be complicated.

The Yin-Yang family were not ordinary people, each possessing strange martial arts skills.

If he were to take action against them, he must succeed in one strike, capture them all in one net, and eliminate them root and branch.

Otherwise, if one was left out, it would be an endless source of future trouble.

Especially since Yun Zhong Jun could also use the Ghost Control Pill to manipulate the living.

If Yun Zhong Jun was given the opportunity to control him or others, it would bring a devastating blow to the empire.

Moreover, he hadn't forgotten about the Luo Wang matter.

Luo Wang was also lurking in the shadows with ill intentions.

If he revealed his murderous intent too early, allowing Luo Wang and the Yin-Yang family to secretly join forces, it would be troublesome.

Things had to be done one at a time, meals had to be eaten one bite at a time.

He would deal with them one by one, which was safer.

At the very least, he now knew their true colors.

And those people of theirs still didn't know his thoughts.

The situation was still in his favor.

Especially since this brat said he was interested in the people of the Yin-Yang family.

This was the first time in such a long time that he had heard of him wanting something.

It seemed that this brat was already interested in beauties.

Beauties, as long as they didn't hinder the overall situation, he didn't mind giving them to this brat as a favor.

After all, this kid had already helped him a lot.

It was only right to give a little something in return.

He was not an inflexible person either.

Since this brat was interested in the Yin-Yang family, then for the sake of this kid's face, he would not move against the Yin-Yang family for now.

Let them live a little longer.

In just a short moment, many thoughts had already flashed through Ying Zheng's mind.

The murderous intent that originally couldn't be hidden gradually subsided, until finally, it completely disappeared.

The eunuchs and palace maids in attendance secretly breathed a sigh of relief.

Just now, the aura from His Majesty was too frightening, as if blood would be shed on the spot.

It scared them into thinking that their lives were no longer safe.

Only after Zhang Han noticed that the emperor's aura had stabilized did his face become solemn.

He opened his mouth.

"Your Majesty, do we need to arrest Yun Zhong Jun and interrogate him?"

Zhang Han was truly loyal to the First Emperor. He would never allow anyone in the court to secretly harm the Emperor.

Ying Zheng knew his loyalty, but he had already made other plans during his contemplation just now.

So Ying Zheng shook his head and said, "No need. Please keep this matter confidential. If any of you leak this information, I will have your life!"

Upon hearing this, the eunuchs and palace maids present all knelt down, their bodies shaking like autumn leaves, fearfully saying they wouldn't dare.

Concubine Shu looked at the Emperor with a worried face.

"Your Majesty, what about your headache? Without the medicine, your headache will continue to act up."

The Emperor's migraine had been around for a long time, and the royal doctors were unable to treat it.

Others might not know, but Concubine Shu knew.

Every time the Emperor's headache acted up, it was extremely painful.

The pain couldn't be relieved whether he was walking or sitting.

At those times, let alone handling government affairs, even if he simply did nothing, it was still terribly painful.

Ying Chen heard these words and remembered Ying Zheng's headache. He subconsciously thought of his own ability.

[It's just a migraine. If he learns my Huangji Dadao Jing, he doesn't need to go too deep. As long as he enters the Huangqi realm, it can be easily solved.]

Ying Zheng was overjoyed when he heard this.

His son is indeed reliable!

Without any elixir, his headache can be resolved.

Having such a son, the ancestors of Qin must be blessing him!

So they sent such a son, not only to help him see through the true faces of the surrounding jackals and tigers,

But also to solve his own stubborn illness!

And later even fulfill his wish for longevity!

This is his great fortune!

It is also the luck of the Qin state!

Ying Zheng had a bottom in his heart. His brows relaxed, and his whole person became much more open-minded. Instead, he comforted Concubine Shu.

"Don't worry, it's just a headache. I've persisted for so many years, it's not a big deal."

Concubine Shu was still very worried.

She heartily felt distressed for the Emperor.

So, she reached out her hand and said, "Your Majesty, let me massage your head to relieve the pain."

Ying Zheng nodded, enjoying Concubine Shu's massage while pondering the inner voice he had just heard.

Huangji Dadao Jing?

Could this be the method for longevity that kid mentioned?

After cultivating it, it could cure his headache and let him live long?

Such a good technique, when can he actually start cultivating it?

Ying Zheng suddenly became expectant.
