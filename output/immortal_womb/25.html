Chapter 25

The golden sun sets in the west, and the round moon rises as the night quietly descends.

Moyu Qilin finally waits for the moment to take action.

She immediately changes into clothes convenient for action, eager to take the initiative.

Moyu Qilin begins to scout the surroundings, with the Shu Fei Palace as the center.

Shu Fei's rank was only Changshi until recently, so naturally, the palace she resides in is not in a good location.

The concubines around her are of similar rank to Shu Fei, and none of them are favored.

The eunuchs and palace maids serving the unfavored concubines are very few.

Moreover, after Shu Fei gained favor recently, the palace maids and eunuchs generally go to ingratiate themselves with her.

So the presence of the surrounding concubines is even lower.

Basically, as soon as night falls, they turn off the lights and go to sleep, and no one will come out.

This is convenient for Moyu Qilin to act.

She can search the nearby palaces extensively.

With her skills and disguise ability, it can be said that it is like entering an uninhabited place.

She can freely explore every palace nearby.

Almost at the same time as Moyu Qilin's actions, Ying Chen's divine sense is released and locked onto her.

Closely watching her movements.

At first, the palaces Moyu Qilin explores are all nearby.

But soon, as the scope of Moyu Qilin's search expands, she is about to escape the coverage of Ying Chen's divine sense.

Ying Chen recalls the operation of condensing divine sense into needles during the day and condenses his divine sense into threads.

Extending the threads outward.

Instantly, Moyu Qilin's blurry figure becomes clear again.

His divine sense can continue to cover outward.

It is indeed feasible. In this way, the range he can see will be much larger.

He never thought that this experimental operation of his would actually succeed.

Ying Chen is delighted in his heart, feeling that he has found more ways to use his power.

Now, Moyu Qilin is under his divine sense, with nowhere to hide.

Ying Chen has nothing to do and begins to carefully observe and study Moyu Qilin's disguise ability.

Moyu Qilin's disguise ability is very useful in obtaining intelligence.

If it weren't for him seeing through Moyu Qilin's disguise in advance.

If it were someone else in the world of Qin, they would probably be deceived by Moyu Qilin's disguise.

Thinking like this, unknowingly, Ying Chen is deconstructing the technique Moyu Qilin uses to disguise herself as others.

In an instant, Ying Chen feels a sudden change in his mind.

He suddenly feels that he can also achieve this kind of disguise.

It is his extremely strong, heaven-defying comprehension ability that is playing its role.

Allowing him to comprehend the skill of "Thousand Transformations".

His "Thousand Transformations" is different from the superficial changes brought about by Moyu Qilin's disguise.

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese novel excerpt:

He could use his own power to completely change his appearance.

This change was a true transformation, including his voice, breath, and temperament.

It could allow a person to completely become someone else.

His ability in this regard was even more terrifying than Mo Yuqilin's ability!

At this time, Mo Yuqilin arrived at an uninhabited palace chamber.

The place was covered in dust, and the cobwebs had formed a thick layer.

It was clearly a long-abandoned place that no one had visited for years.

She took a rough look around and, finding no clues, was about to leave.

Ying Chen was not going to wait any longer. This was the perfect time and place.

He used the skill he had acquired, transmitting his voice in secret.

"You dare to trespass in the rear palace? You're very bold!"

In the quiet night, this voice exploded like a clap of thunder next to Mo Yuqilin's ear!

Startled, Mo Yuqilin said, "Who's there?"

Ying Chen ignored her question.

Instead, he continued to interrogate Mo Yuqilin: "Speak, who sent you?"

The instant she heard the voice, Mo Yuqilin began searching for the speaker's location.

Unfortunately, no matter how she searched, she could not find the slightest trace.

Apart from herself, there was no sign of anyone else's presence nearby!

How could this be?

Who on earth was the one speaking?

Unable to find Ying Chen's location, Mo Yuqilin began to panic.

Who exactly was the other party?

Despite clearly hearing a voice, why could she not sense anyone's presence at all?

There was actually such a master hidden in the rear palace, yet she had not detected them at all!

Was this possible?

Alarm bells rang in Mo Yuqilin's heart, but on the surface, she pretended to be an ordinary palace maid in a panic.

"Who is it? Who is speaking?"

"This servant is just a lowly palace maid from Consort Shu's palace. I took a wrong turn and can't find my way back."

"My lord, please don't frighten this servant. I will leave right away."

She acted very engrossed in the role, even adding a sob to her voice.

She vividly portrayed a timid little palace maid.

If one truly did not know her background, they might have been fooled by her current act.

Unfortunately, this was all a pointless act in front of Ying Chen's blind eyes.

Ying Chen sneered mockingly, "Is that so? The great Mo Yuqilin, number one assassin of Han, took a wrong turn?"

Mo Yuqilin was stunned.

Her pupils constricted, and her heart nearly stopped beating!

What was going on?

Who on earth was this person?

Why did they know her true identity?

Was her identity exposed?
