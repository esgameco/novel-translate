Chapter 61

Ying Chen came before Shao Siming and raised his hand, saying calmly, "Now, it's your turn."

Upon hearing these words, Shao Siming's face was filled with horror as she kept retreating.

Like a frightened, puffed-up kitten, she kept backing away while staring at him vigilantly, ready to seize any opportunity to fight back.

Seeing her in this state, Ying Chen said helplessly, "Why are you making me out to be the villain? You were the ones who barged into my mother's bedchamber."

Mother's bedchamber?

Shao Siming suddenly grasped this important keyword.

In an instant, a flash of lightning struck her mind.

Clearing away the thick fog, the puzzles that had long confounded everyone finally had an answer!

Shao Siming couldn't help but ask in a startled voice, "You are the child in Concubine Shu's womb?!"

Having his true identity exposed, Ying Chen wasn't flustered at all and just smiled faintly.

"Congratulations on guessing correctly, but the reward for guessing the right answer is: from now on, you will have to submit to me."

Shao Siming was shocked beyond belief upon receiving the affirmative answer from Ying Chen.

How could this be possible?

How could an unborn child possess such wisdom? And such martial prowess?

Could it be that the recent series of events were all caused by this child?

So there was never any expert behind Concubine Shu!

Everything was done by this unborn child before her eyes!

Who could have thought of this answer?!

The truth was too shocking for anyone to accept in this brief moment.

Shao Siming's body trembled slightly, wanting to stand up and leave this place to relay the truth back to the Yin-Yang family.

Unfortunately, she had no chance to send any message back.

Almost the instant she made a move, Ying Chen took action!

Without any hesitation, he directly extracted Shao Siming's soul.

With the previous opportunity of dealing with Da Siming as a foundation, Ying Chen's movements were incredibly skilled this time.

Shao Siming's body collapsed to the ground after her soul was extracted.

After extracting the soul, the next step for Ying Chen was to modify Shao Siming's cognition.

However, just as Ying Chen was about to modify her cognition, he discovered an amazing phenomenon.

Shao Siming's memories were actually sealed!

Ying Chen inwardly mused how interesting this was.

It seemed that the Yin-Yang family also had secret techniques that could affect the soul.

If given the chance, he wouldn't mind meeting Dong Huang Taiyi to see what this fellow was all about and what secrets he held.

As for this sealed portion of Shao Siming's memories, Ying Chen didn't rashly undo it.

Not knowing the contents inside, if he carelessly undid the seal, there was no telling what changes Shao Siming would undergo.

Moreover, on Dong Huang Taiyi's side...

Here is the full translation of the Chinese excerpt into English:

If it was discovered that the seal had been removed, they would certainly turn their attention to Shao Siming, which would startle the snake in the grass.

Therefore, Ying Chen did not remove the seal on Shao Siming.

Instead, he only modified Shao Siming's cognition.

Just like with Da Siming, he also modified Shao Siming's cognition to be his maid.

After the modification, he put the soul back into Shao Siming's body.

When Shao Siming opened her eyes and woke up, seeing Ying Chen, she also became obedient.

She slowly walked to Da Siming's side and knelt down side by side towards Ying Chen.

Ying Chen was very satisfied with his ability to modify cognition.

He began to order the two: "You will go back to the Yin-Yang family in a while, and then hide in the Yin-Yang family."

"As for the results of this investigation, just say that you encountered a mysterious person's attack in the harem and barely escaped after a difficult battle."

The excuse Ying Chen thought of for them was perfect.

You know, the highest state of telling a lie is seven parts truth and three parts falsehood.

After the previous battle, the two of them now had injuries on their bodies.

It can be said that except for his identity, everything else was true.

The group of people in the Yin-Yang family had no choice but to believe it.

Da Siming and Shao Siming replied respectfully, "We will obey the master's orders."

The two now completely regarded Ying Chen as their master.

No matter what Ying Chen said, they would do it.

Since everything had been arranged, and there was nothing else, Ying Chen ordered the two to leave.

The two women carefully avoided the guards along the way and left the harem.

After the two left, Ying Chen couldn't help yawning, feeling a wave of sleepiness coming over him.

He hadn't felt such a strong sleepiness in a long time.

This should be the side effect of his soul being out of his body for too long.

After being busy for so long, he really should have a good sleep.

Ying Chen controlled his soul to avoid the teams of guards.

He returned to Shu Fei's bedchamber.

The bedchamber was peaceful, and most people were still dreaming, just as it was when he left before.

Ying Chen put his mind at ease and returned his soul to his body.

Then, he fell into a deep sleep.

The sleeping Ying Chen was very relieved.

Because the palace was now tense.

The guards were almost straining every nerve in their bodies.

They were anxiously searching the area near where the sound had come from, hoping to find a clue to the assassin.

At this time, no matter which camp people in the harem belonged to, they dared not act rashly.

Unless they wanted to face Zheng Ge's storm of rage.
