Disguised as the palace maid Ruyi, it didn't take long before she saw the shadow of Shu Fei's palace.

She walked in with utmost naturalness, greeting the others.

Everyone responded to her while going about their own business.

The entire process was smooth as silk, and no one noticed that she was a fake Ruyi in disguise.

The palace maid Jixiang saw that Ruyi had finally returned and couldn't help but complain, "I sent you to get some incense, why did it take you so long?"

With Ruyi gone, many tasks fell on her shoulders, and she couldn't handle it all by herself.

So when she saw Ruyi, she couldn't help but say a few words.

The fake Ruyi explained in a gentle tone, "I should have been back earlier, but I encountered someone on my way back who said they needed help, so I was slightly delayed."

Her words were reasonable and left Jixiang with nothing to say.

The fake Ruyi was not afraid of talking to others at all.

Because her voice was exactly the same as the real Ruyi's, without the slightest difference!

No one could discern the difference between them.

As expected, Jixiang didn't suspect anything and urged, "Since you're back, hurry and light the incense. If His Majesty comes and there's no incense to calm his mind, it won't do."

Ever since His Majesty needed this incense, Shu Fei would light it in advance.

This way, when His Majesty arrived, he could directly smell the fragrance.

Of course, the ingredients in the incense posed no harm to Shu Fei's body or the child in her womb.

On the contrary, many of the medicinal materials were beneficial to her health.

So Shu Fei herself also enjoyed the scent of this incense.

Hearing Jixiang say this, Ruyi nodded and carried the incense towards the inner chamber.

Inside the inner chamber, Shu Fei was feeling drowsy.

Seeing the fake Ruyi enter with the incense, she knew her purpose.

The fake Ruyi respectfully bowed to Shu Fei.

Shu Fei nodded, exempting the fake Ruyi from formalities, and didn't notice any difference between her and the previous Ruyi.

Ruyi was one of her close attendants, and Shu Fei had always trusted those by her side without suspicion, so she wouldn't monitor their actions.

Thus, Shu Fei quickly shifted her gaze, no longer watching the fake Ruyi's movements.

Ying Chen, who was cultivating, sensed the changes in the outside world and took a glance with his spiritual consciousness.

He thought Ying Zheng had come again, but it seemed that wasn't the case.

It turned out that someone had just entered, and she appeared to be an ordinary palace maid.

However, just as Ying Chen was about to continue his cultivation, he suddenly felt something abnormal!

The palace maid who had just entered was suspicious!

The aura on her body was not something an ordinary person could possess!

There was something fishy about her!

His divine sense enveloped her body, and the area covered by his divine sense appeared slightly distorted.

That distorted area had a somewhat illusory color, as if a layer of skin was draped over the surface.

It made it impossible to clearly see what was inside.

This made it even more suspicious.

Ying Chen was more curious about the palace maid's true identity.

After some thought, Ying Chen condensed his divine sense into a thin needle.

Then he pierced it towards the palace maid's body!

In an instant, the palace maid's true face was fully exposed under his powerful divine sense!

The disguise on the surface faded away, revealing a different face underneath!

That face had absolutely no resemblance to the original palace maid!

She was someone else disguised as a palace maid!

Ying Chen was a bit surprised, not expecting that he would inadvertently discover a big fish!

Aside from himself, probably no one else could see through this kind of disguise ability.

Could this person be that mysterious Mo Yu Qilin?!

Ying Chen recalled the descriptions he had read about Mo Yu Qilin and the fragments of her appearances.

At this moment, Mo Yu Qilin, who was lighting incense, paused her movements and couldn't help but look around.

What's going on?

Just now for an instant, her disguise ability actually stagnated for a moment!

It was as if someone had lifted her disguise!

This was the first time she ever had this feeling!

Was this just her illusion?

Or was there a master here?

Mo Yu Qilin recalled those rumors about the Shu Fei Palace.

Even the current generation's Sword Saint Gai Nie had stumbled here, with someone seeing through his defection route.

Could it be that this master could also see through her disguise?

As soon as this thought arose, Mo Yu Qilin's actions became even more cautious.

Her every move was filled with a careful air.

It seemed this mission was not as simple as she had imagined!

Especially the Shu Fei Palace, it really was a bit unusual!

Before, she had thought that others exaggerated the rumors because of their own failures.

Now she unexpectedly discovered it was all true!

There really was a master here!

Someone who had not exposed their aura at all, but instead made her aura stagnate for a moment!

Such a master might have already seen through her disguise!

However, this also happened to arouse her competitive spirit.

In terms of fighting, she was not the strongest.

But in terms of infiltration and disguise, she had absolute confidence in her abilities!

In this area, she did not believe she would lose!

She would use her skills to investigate the other party's background, and then transmit the intelligence out!
