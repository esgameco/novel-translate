Chapter 27

In the Xianyang Palace, the lights were bright.

Ying Zheng rubbed his brow and leaned back in his chair, closing his eyes to rest.

He had just finished dealing with military affairs and was feeling tired.

The Northern Barbarians were harassing the border, and he planned to send out Meng Tian. He would lead the army north to guard the border in a few days.

At this moment, Zhang Han appeared and reported: "Your Majesty, the Shadow Guards have just captured a person who intruded into the rear palace. It seems to be Mo Yuqilin of the Flowing Sand Organization."

Upon hearing this, Ying Zheng opened his eyes and sat up straight.

How could Mo Yuqilin of the Flowing Sand Organization be in the palace?!

He knew of Mo Yuqilin's great reputation.

He was the number one assassin in the Han Kingdom.

This person had no form or appearance, yet could transform into all living beings. Until now, no one knew his true identity.

Ying Zheng's gaze fell on Zhang Han, and he asked, "Isn't this person said to be able to enter anywhere? How did you manage to catch him?"

In his words, disbelief was mixed with a little pleasant surprise.

When did his Shadow Guards become so capable?

They caught a big fish without making a sound!

Hearing the emperor's words, Zhang Han's expression was strange, but he still reported truthfully: "Actually, when we caught her, she was already unconscious."

Ying Zheng was stunned, not quite understanding the meaning.

"Already unconscious when caught? What's going on? It wasn't you who made a move? Then who did?"

Speaking of this, Zhang Han was extremely ashamed.

So many Shadow Guards, yet not a single one clearly saw who made the move.

He knelt down, lowered his head, and pleaded for forgiveness: "Please forgive me, Your Majesty, this... your subordinate does not know."

In the rear palace, someone had already made a move.

Especially to silently beat Mo Yuqilin, such a highly skilled person, into this state.

Yet he had no idea. As the commander of the Shadow Guards, this was indeed his undeniable dereliction of duty.

Ying Zheng's heart stirred, and a absurd idea suddenly popped into his mind.

This Mo Yuqilin was rendered unconscious by someone in the rear palace.

He was familiar with his rear palace; there shouldn't be such a highly skilled person.

Could this matter be related to that stinky boy?

Was it he who dealt with Mo Yuqilin?

After all, he already had wisdom and had acquired martial arts.

If that were the case, it would make sense that Zhang Han and his Shadow Guards didn't discover any traces.

Somehow, even though this guess was quite unreliable,

Ying Zheng just had an inexplicable certainty.

There was an eighty to ninety percent chance that this matter was related to his son.

Thinking of this, Ying Zheng pardoned Zhang Han's crime and said, "Go and bring Mo Yuqilin up."

He wanted to interrogate personally.

Seeing that the emperor had no intention of blaming him, Zhang Han immediately thanked him for his grace and withdrew.

The weakened Moyu Qilin was brought up, currently tied up and looking miserable all over.

Ying Zheng interrogated: "Moyu Qilin, who sent you here?"

Facing Ying Zheng's questioning, Moyu Qilin did not answer, but instead tried to sow discord: "Do you know there is an extremely powerful person hidden in your harem?"

Upon hearing this, Zhang Han's face greatly changed.

Moyu Qilin's implication was that the person who injured her did not come from outside the palace.

But had been hiding inside the palace all along.

Yet their Shadow Guards had no idea such a person existed!

This almost directly pointed at the Shadow Guards, implying they were all useless!

Zhang Han angrily rebuked Moyu Qilin: "Nonsense!"

Moyu Qilin laughed bitterly: "Then who do you think injured me?"

Zhang Han was stunned.

He had to admit, Moyu Qilin was an expert, not just anyone could heavily injure her.

Especially with hardly any traces left at the scene.

It strongly suggested this person was most likely someone who had long been concealed in the harem.

Familiar with the harem situation, so they could directly leave after injuring someone without leaving traces.

Realizing all this, Zhang Han turned pale and knelt down to apologize to the emperor.

But to Zhang Han and Moyu Qilin's surprise, Ying Zheng's expression remained normal.

When Moyu Qilin brought up that possibility, he showed no reaction of surprise.

As if he didn't care at all about this person possibly hiding in the harem.

Ying Zheng's gaze was calm as he glanced at Zhang Han, signaling him to stand up.

Zhang Han stood and retreated to the emperor's side.

Ying Zheng paced over to Moyu Qilin, his bearing as unperturbed as always.

"Even if you don't say it, I know, behind you is Flowing Sand, Wei Zhuang is getting bolder and bolder, daring to even intrude my harem."

"The only thing I'm curious about is, why? What does Wei Zhuang want to do?"

Mentioning the Flowing Sand organization behind her, as well as Wei Zhuang,

Moyu Qilin fell silent.

She would not reveal any intelligence from her own mouth.

Even if Ying Zheng threatened her with death, it would be the same.

Ying Zheng saw her tightly closed lips, and fearless appearance.

He knew it was hopeless to expect getting any intelligence directly from her mouth.

"Since you refuse to tell me, then we can only use torture to open your mouth."

"Zhang Han, lock Moyu Qilin in the Devouring Fang Prison! Severely interrogate her for me, find out her purpose for intruding the harem!"

"Yes! This subordinate obeys the order!"

Zhang Han accepted the command and took people to drag Moyu Qilin away.
