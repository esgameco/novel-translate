Chapter 34

In the Devouring Fang Prison.

Zhang Han, Moon Goddess, Star Soul, Great Director of Destiny, and Lesser Director of Destiny were standing guard outside Ge Nie's cell.

Star Soul marveled at the layout of the Devouring Fang Prison, saying, "Would that Weizhang really dare to come to such a terrifying place?"

It wasn't that he doubted the Emperor's decision.

He just felt that any sane person would not choose to break into such a place.

Zhang Han said expressionlessly, "Since His Majesty has arranged it this way, he must have his reasons. We just need to follow His Majesty's orders."

As the commander of the Shadow Guards, Zhang Han unconditionally obeyed the Emperor's orders.

Even if he himself believed that Weizhang would not come, an order was an order, and no mistakes were allowed.

He just had to play his role well.

Moon Goddess, recalling the recent abnormalities, had other thoughts in her mind.

Whether it was the ambush for Ge Nie, the recent papermaking techniques, or the matter of the elixirs, they all showed that His Majesty was very different from the past.

So it was not certain whether all of this was really arranged by His Majesty.

But she only dared to think this in her heart; she could never say it out loud.

Ge Nie noticed Zhang Han and those Yin-Yang masters who had captured him, and a bad premonition arose in his heart.

He frowned directly and asked Zhang Han, "What are you all gathering at the Devouring Fang Prison for?"

Facing Ge Nie, Zhang Han smiled slightly and replied, "Naturally, we are here waiting for Weizhang to take the bait."

They already knew that Weizhang was coming to the Devouring Fang Prison?!

They were gathering here now to ambush Weizhang!

Not good! If Weizhang really came here, he would be in danger!

Thinking of this, Ge Nie's face instantly turned grim.

Mo Yuqilin, who also heard Zhang Han's answer, was even more shocked.

She knew the inside story of the cooperation with Luowang, so she understood the gravity of the situation even more.

And she understood Weizhang.

With her captured, Weizhang would definitely have Luowang provide intelligence and come to the Devouring Fang Prison.

But what was going on?

Why were Zhang Han and the others so certain that Weizhang would come?

Was the plan of Flowing Sand exposed?!

But that shouldn't be!

She hadn't said a word, and Zhang Han and the others also didn't know that Flowing Sand had cooperated with Luowang.

It was even more impossible for them to know about Flowing Sand's subsequent plans.

Could it be Luowang?

Did Luowang betray them and leak Flowing Sand's plan?!

But what benefit would that bring them?

Mo Yuqilin suddenly thought of a terrifying possibility.

Perhaps Luowang had never intended to seek cooperation with Flowing Sand in the first place, but was setting a trap for them.

Otherwise, how could she have exposed her identity so easily?

Wei Zhuang continued to penetrate into the interior of Shi Ya Prison.

What made him feel a little strange was that this process seemed simpler than imagined.

Did he overthink things?

Perhaps Shi Ya Prison was not as terrible as the intelligence indicated.

Finally, according to the intelligence given by Yan Ri, Wei Zhuang successfully entered the depths of Shi Ya Prison.

If the intelligence was correct, ahead was the area where felons were imprisoned.

Xing Hun couldn't help but say, "You really came?"

What did that mean?

It sounded as if they had anticipated his actions long ago.

Could it be that they knew he would come?

And then they were waiting here for him on purpose?
