Li Si was overjoyed upon hearing that Ying Zheng would no longer seek the elixir of immortality.

"Your Majesty is wise! Making such a great sacrifice for the people of the world, truly a benevolent ruler!"

Li Si was genuinely moved this time. His Majesty was actually willing to let go of his long-cherished wish for immortality and put the people's affairs first!

His Majesty had really changed!

Zhang Heng also secretly breathed a sigh of relief.

In fact, they all felt that the matter of the immortality elixir was unreliable.

Throughout history, who had truly achieved immortality?

But the First Emperor was so enthusiastic and determined to find the elixir of immortality that they didn't have the courage to oppose him.

Ying Zheng was very pleased to hear Li Si's praise.

In any case, he already had his son's method of immortality, so he didn't care about mirages at all.

But he was a qualified emperor and would not become complacent after hearing a little praise from his ministers. There were still a lot of things waiting for him to arrange.

"Li Si, go and arrange the manpower for catching fish."

Li Si happily agreed.

The worrying issue of food shortage had a solution, and his mood had improved a lot.

Li Si withdrew with light steps, eager to go back and arrange the task the First Emperor had given him.

But after leaving the Xianyang Palace, his footsteps suddenly stopped.

He suddenly felt that something was wrong.

He sorted out today's events from the beginning and found them increasingly strange.

What exactly happened today?

Since His Majesty had a countermeasure to the food emergency from the beginning, why did he take him to the Shu Concubine's palace halfway through?!

Moreover, how could His Majesty know the method of making ice with saltpeter?

He didn't admit that he invented it himself, but he also didn't say who invented it.

Moreover, His Majesty could only come into contact with these ministers in the palace.

Li Si understood his colleagues and did not believe that they had presented the method of making ice with saltpeter.

Thinking of this, Li Si also thought of the recent events.

Gai Nie was inexplicably intercepted, papermaking appeared out of nowhere, and today the method of making ice with saltpeter suddenly appeared.

These events did not seem like His Majesty's style at all.

How did it feel like there was another person behind His Majesty?

Li Si was startled by his own guess.

Then he shook his head and denied his speculation.

Another person? How could that be possible?

Today he was by His Majesty's side without leaving a step. His Majesty clearly had not been in contact with anyone except these few people.

He was really overthinking.

Li Si laughed at himself for being oversensitive and left.
