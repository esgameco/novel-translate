Chapter 62

In the Shu Fei Palace, the Emperor's grand carriage arrived at this moment.

Just as Ying Chen had just fallen asleep, Ying Zheng arrived.

Upon hearing that the Emperor had arrived, Shu Fei immediately tidied herself up and stood up to pay her respects.

Ying Zheng immediately supported Shu Fei and did not let her perform the courtesy.

"From now on, as your body grows heavier, you can be exempted from these formalities in front of me."

After saying that, Ying Zheng began to show concern for Shu Fei's well-being.

"The weather has been changing quite quickly recently. Do you have any discomfort?"

Not only did he exempt her from the courtesy, but he also showed such concern for her physical condition. This was something that an emperor would do, and it was a gesture that he would not make to anyone else!

Shu Fei was instantly overwhelmed by the favor.

She said softly, "I eat well and sleep soundly, and I have no worries. It is Your Majesty who should pay attention to the tiring work of handling political affairs and take care to rest."

Hearing that Shu Fei was still using her free time to care about him, Ying Zheng felt a warmth in his heart.

However, he knew Shu Fei's considerate and gentle nature and was afraid that she might be enduring any discomfort.

Only after carefully confirming that she was indeed not injured or ill did he feel slightly relieved.

Then he took Shu Fei's hand and asked her to go back to sleep so that she would not be in poor spirits.

Now that it was almost dawn, how could Shu Fei still fall asleep?

Especially with an emperor sitting beside her, how could she possibly continue to sleep?

So she made an excuse that she was not sleepy and wanted to sit down and chat with His Majesty.

Ying Zheng could not force her, so he had to sit down and chat with Shu Fei for a while.

Slowly, Ying Zheng's expression became tense again.

He suddenly realized that he had been here for so long and had not heard a single word from his heart!

Could it be that something had happened to the stinky little boy inside?

At the thought of this possibility, Ying Zheng became anxious and uneasy.

"Could it be that the fetus has been affected? Let's call the imperial physician to take a look!"

Shu Fei herself did not feel anything, but Ying Zheng still insisted on summoning the imperial physician without hesitation.

The imperial physician suddenly received a summons from the emperor, and the eunuch who came kept urging him anxiously, thinking that something big had happened.

He hurried to Shu Fei's bedchamber, running all the way.

By the time he arrived, he was covered in sweat, soaking his clothes.

The imperial physician did not dare to be negligent. Regardless of his own condition, he immediately took Shu Fei's pulse and examined her, asking many questions about her condition.

After all the examinations, the final result was that Shu Fei was very healthy and had no problems at all.

When the imperial physician announced this conclusion, everyone breathed a sigh of relief, especially the eunuchs and palace maids who served her.

If something had happened to Shu Fei at this juncture, their heads would surely not be spared!

After hearing this conclusion, Ying Zheng still looked nervous and asked,

"What about the child? Is the child alright?"

The royal physician was slightly surprised by this question, but still patiently answered: "The child is naturally fine too, very healthy. At this time, the child should be sound asleep."

Only after confirming the child was fine did Ying Zheng put his mind at ease.

This child is related to the future of Great Qin! It's good that there's no problem!

With the weight off his mind, Ying Zheng finally had the mindset to deal with tonight's matter.

Ying Zheng let Shu Fei go rest in the inner chamber.

Then he angrily said in the main hall: "Where is Zhang Han? Tell him to come see me immediately!"

Before long, Zhang Han received the order and came.

He knelt down with a thud: "This subordinate failed to protect Shu Fei. Please forgive me, Your Majesty!"

Ying Zheng looked at Zhang Han coldly: "You are guilty, but now is not the time to pursue responsibility. Have the assassins been caught?"

Mentioning this matter, Zhang Han felt extremely ashamed.

He lowered his head and replied: "When this subordinate arrived, I only saw the assassins' retreating figures from afar. They should be two women, but their skills were very strong and the Shadow Guards were unable to stop them."

"However, besides this, the Shadow Guards discovered traces of the Luo Web near where they escaped."

Upon hearing that people from the Luo Web participated in this night's intrusion into Shu Fei's palace, Ying Zheng immediately erupted in towering rage!

"The Luo Web?! Zhao Gao is so bold! Does he really think I don't dare to move against the Luo Web?"

Ever since learning that the Luo Web was spying on the imperial harem with disloyal intentions, Ying Zheng had detested the Luo Web to the bone!

Previously, he just didn't have a free hand to deal with the Luo Web.

Later, thinking of using the Luo Web to strike at the Mohists, he held his nose and endured it.

Who would have thought the Luo Web would be so brazen!

They actually dared to reach their hands so far as to intrude into the imperial harem with the intent to assassinate Shu Fei!

Fortunately it was discovered early this time and they were not allowed to succeed!

If they had succeeded and this son was lost, his Great Qin would be ruined!

Thinking of the dire consequences, Ying Zheng's fury reached its peak!

The people of the Luo Web deserve to die! The one behind it pulling the strings deserves death even more!

Ying Zheng coldly ordered: "Immediately mobilize all Shadow Guards and the Imperial Guard to secretly investigate and grasp the whereabouts of all the Luo Web assassins. I want a list and information on all members of the Luo Web."

"If this matter is not handled well again, the two crimes will be punished together. Zhang Han, you see to it yourself."

Zhang Han's heart trembled, hearing the thick murderous intent in these two sentences.

It seems His Majesty is going to take action against the Luo Web!

He, Zhang Han, is to be one of the sharp swords in this!

If not put to good use, then in the future there will no longer be any use for him!

He must prove his worth and absolutely cannot fail again!

Zhang Han accepted the order and left.
