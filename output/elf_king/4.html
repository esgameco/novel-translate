Lin Yi took a look at the remaining energy crystals, which were just over 1,100.

He immediately went to the base of the ancient war tree again.

Whether they were sprite swordsmen or sprite archers, they were all adult great sprites.

The former wore exquisite metal armor and wielded long, sharp sprite swords.

Each sprite was fully armed and exuded a magical aura.

It is worth mentioning that...

All these sprites were female, without a single male.

They each had exquisite and pretty faces, as well as perfectly tall and slender figures.

Sprites are a rare intelligent race that can develop their clan without having to combine with the opposite sex. All sprites are nurtured from the Tree of Life.

They are born as sprite balls and grow into great sprites when they mature.

They have the innate appearance and physical structure of females.

In the entire sprite clan, the only male sprites are the most noble sun sprites.

The sun sprite king who founded the Sprite Empire in the past was a male sprite.

Now, many years later, Lin Yi has become the second one.

This is why the ancient tree said that Lin Yi is destined to become the Sprite King.

There can be many Sprite Queens, but there is only one Sprite King.

Lin Yi is born to be the material for the Sprite King!

Earlier that day, he had killed thousands of monsters, including vampire mosquitoes. But the mid-level monsters of level 5 were only in the double digits.

It was evident that killing 1,000 of these high-level monsters was no simple task.

The difficulty did not lie in the killing itself, but in finding them.

In the ruins he had explored so far, the vast majority of the monsters were low-level.

Mid-level monsters were extremely rare, and he had not yet encountered any high-level monsters.

"I wonder what the divine-granted palace is used for..."

Just as Lin Yi was about to inquire the system, he sensed a surge of malevolent magical power coming from the northeast of the plaza. Raising his gaze, he saw a large number of green flames flickering in the darkness, like a dense swarm of ghostly fires.

"Your Majesty the Elven King, those are undead creatures!" said an Elven Archer.

Lin Yi nodded.

The area where the undead creatures appeared was along the path he had taken to return to his domain.

Clearly, they had been attracted by his presence.

"Relay my orders, prepare for battle!"

"As you command, Your Majesty!"

The Elven troops quickly assembled.

Ten Elven Knights in the front, twelve Elven Archers in the rear.

Their formation was orderly, their gazes sharp, and a sense of ruthlessness could be seen on their delicate faces.

No wonder they were considered the elite!
