There was a desolate wilderness, overgrown with weeds. Through the moonlight, one could clearly see huge crisscrossing cracks on the ground.

The moonlight pierced through the darkness, revealing a youth of around fifteen years old, wearing a tight-fitting black long robe, with a silver-white hair crown on his head. His handsome face was streaked with tear stains.

And on the youth's chin, a crystal clear teardrop could be distinctly seen.

"Plop." The teardrop, having absorbed enough moisture, fell from the youth's chin to the ground, making a faint echo.

This youth of around fifteen years old was none other than Luo Zhongxian. Ever since slaying Luo Changtian and Luo Meng, the father and son, in his room, he had been here for several hours.

This place was called the "Fallen God's Land" by the people of the tribe. Ten years ago, the tribal leader Luo Shentong led a hundred elites here to fend off an invasion of a million ferocious beasts.

Relying on the Profound Sword Technique, Luo Shentong slew ten beasts of the same level, bathing himself in their blood.

However, there were several hundred beasts that had reached the Ninth Realm of Mortal Transcendence among the horde, and although the hundred elites fought desperately, they were ultimately decimated with heavy casualties.

With his eyes nearly popping out in fury, Luo Shentong forcibly broke through his realm, reaching the Divine Realm.

Powerful auras emanated from his body, and a strange aura in the sky triggered a heavenly tribulation!

Layers of thunderclouds covered hundreds of miles! The martial artists and ferocious beasts in the Mortal Transcendence Realm below all felt the overwhelming pressure!

Subsequently, a vast destructive thunderbolt descended, covering an area of hundreds of miles in radius. The ferocious beasts were struck dead by the heavenly tribulation, and the million-strong horde instantly scattered.

Only a few hundred beast giants managed to endure for a while, but they too were cleanly killed by the tremendous thunderbolt.

Luo Shentong watched as the million-strong horde was struck dead by the heavenly tribulation. Although he was bleeding all over, a smile appeared on his face.

The tribe was saved! But the smile quickly disappeared.

For the power of the heavenly tribulation was concentrated solely on him. He had not prepared for this tribulation but had forcibly broken through, and he knew what that meant.

Under the bombardment of the thunderbolt, his body disappeared inch by inch! He turned back and cast a warm gaze towards the tribe, where his child was.

Luo Shentong was originally the most likely person in the Luo Tribe to reach the Divine Realm. He had an exceptionally high aptitude, being identified as having a Nine-Star Constitution at the age of five, causing strange phenomena to appear within a hundred-mile radius.

He broke through to the Mortal Transcendence Realm at the age of ten, becoming the youngest Mortal Transcendence martial artist in the tribe. At eighteen, he became one of the nine great tribes of the Ten Thousand Savage Mountains, earning...

"Hurry up and go, the situation in the tribe has changed now, let's escape under the cover of night, if we wait for Luo Zhongxian and that Deadly Star to discover us, we won't be able to escape even if we want to..." a soft voice said.

"Good, let's stop here." A voice suddenly came from a large tree on the big mountain.

What, what person, come out!

"Eee eee eee", a strange bird cry sounded, dropping a few feathers, then bursting through the sky above the mountain.

"Rumble, rumble" Dozens of resting ferocious beasts sensed the danger, their huge bodies quickly leaving the mountain, charging into the vast wilderness...

The snakes, strange creatures, and ferocious beasts in the mountain were all scared away by the screams, disappearing without a trace.

"The Fallen God's Land" Luo Zhongxian's face suddenly turned solemn, his divine sense sweeping the area ten miles around, seeing Liu Fang and the few black-clothed people.

"As expected of the Sui Dynasty's Marquis of Pacifying Rebellion, acting so quickly and even capturing a few hateful fellows."

He had impressions of these fellows. If not for them, his uncle Kun would not have died in battle in the wilderness.

"I should also start making a move. Father, your son will shine like the sun in this sky. One day, the entire continent will sing praises of Xian'er's name. Your legendary story will also be passed down to every clan, town, dynasty and sect."

Luo Zhongxian took off, flying a hundred miles away to a wilderness where a group of ferocious beasts were searching for food. Luo Zhongxian needed a great amount of battle merits.
