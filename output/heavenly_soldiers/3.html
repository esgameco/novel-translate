Luo Zhongxian suddenly opened his eyes and saw a group of ferocious beasts with red eyes and drooling mouths crawling towards Kun Shu's corpse.

Enraged!

With a "clang" sound, he stood up and drew the Imperial Sword.

Why was the Imperial Sword black? Why did it feel just like an ordinary sword, only lighter? He had many doubts in his mind, but there was no time now!

Holding the black Imperial Sword, he looked coldly at the group of ferocious beasts in front of him.

These beasts looked strange, some looked like pigs from the previous world but had bony spikes on their backs; others looked like alligators but had two thick teeth that were half a meter long!

The ferocious beasts brought him gray memories, his father died because of them!

His right hand skillfully drew a sword flower and charged towards the group of beasts!

He had a conviction in his heart: "I absolutely cannot let the ferocious beasts defile Kun Shu's corpse!"

Although his martial arts cultivation had not progressed in these years, he did not give up in the slightest, practicing sword techniques and boxing in the young master's courtyard every morning.

He often practiced until his whole body trembled and he could no longer hold the sword, only then would he pick up the towel prepared by Kun Shu to wipe off his sweat!

The Imperial Sword slashed towards a ferocious beast's head.

"Boom!"

The beast's head fell to the ground, its blood spurting out a meter long! Even Luo Zhongxian was splattered all over with blood!

That's not right! How could I kill a ferocious beast with one sword strike! Although this was the lowest level body tempering beast!

Luo Zhongxian examined carefully and had an epiphany.

It was because my physical constitution had changed, no longer a defective body but a devouring body!

My body became lighter! My strength increased tenfold! And this sword...

He looked down at the still dripping Imperial Sword.

"Although it's still in a sealed state! But I can still sense its sharpness! Imperial Sword, you will accompany me to conquer the world from now on!"

The Imperial Sword shook, as if responding!

"Congratulations to the host for gaining 10 battle merits!"

Oh, killing ferocious beasts gives battle merits? Although I don't know what use they are, it's definitely...the more the better!

Kill! The Imperial Sword swept horizontally.

"Roar"

The ferocious beasts smelled the blood and their eyes shone with bloodlust as they charged madly towards Luo Zhongxian.

"Roar"

They rushed towards Luo Zhongxian, their thick paws stomping frantically.

Some beasts opened their huge bloody mouths, wanting to swallow Luo Zhongxian whole..

Other beasts lowered their heads to the ground, opening two huge teeth, wanting to skewer Luo Zhongxian to death...

"Hmph"

What a bunch of ferocious beasts with undeveloped intelligence! Such naive attacks! How adorable!
