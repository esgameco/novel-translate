The Luo clan; the Jiang clan; the Huang clan; the Jiang clan; the Lin clan; the Li clan; the Xing clan; the Yan clan; the Hua clan.

Among them, the Luo clan was the strongest (when Luo Shentong was around).

Next were the Jiang clan, Xing clan, and Hua clan.

The Hua clan was a clan composed entirely of women, with a relatively small number of only five hundred members. However, these women's martial arts cultivation was quite profound.

It was rumored among the eight major clans that these women came from the outside world and fled here in difficult times.

Originally there were 1,300 of them, but now only 500 remained. The Hua clan's ability to bear children was an enigma to the other clans.

Their main cultivation method: The "Seductive Heart Sutra"

It was said that once a licentious person, relying on his high level of cultivation, tried to enter the Hua clan to "seek flowers and inquire about willows."

As a result, the Hua clan's leader, Hua Rong, appeared. The women of the clan called her "Sister Hua."

She was a woman with lingering charm, a beautiful face, moving eyes, a jade-like nose, and willow-like eyebrows. She truly lived up to her name: like clouds adorning her clothes and flowers adorning her countenance.

The licentious person immediately fixed his eyes on Hua Rong, feeling his blood boiling within, wishing he could embrace her tightly and indulge in intimacy.

He did not sense any danger from Hua Rong and cheerfully walked towards her.

A hint of annoyance flashed across Hua Rong's face, but she quickly regained her composure and smiled at the licentious person before her.

In a place the licentious person did not notice, a mysterious pinkish aura emanated from Hua Rong's body and drifted towards him, entering his body through his nose.

He seemed completely oblivious, already almost within half a meter of Hua Rong, about to reach out and touch her body.

Hua Rong wore a thin veil over her face, her eyebrows slightly furrowed. She let out a light sigh, and another pinkish aura entered the man's body.

The man's body suddenly froze, his eyes showing confusion.

"Hang his head at the entrance of the clan, and throw his body into the sea of flowers to feed the flowers," Sister Hua said as she walked back to the clan.

"Yes, Sister Hua..." several women responded.

From then on, the people of the eight major clans never dared to underestimate the women of the Hua clan, especially the beautiful ones.

Because of the topic of the Sword Demon, they chatted noisily together.

In the courtyard of the Young Master of the Luo Clan, Luo Zhongxian was holding a pen and writing something fervently.

The Sword Demon that the few young ladies from the Flower Clan were discussing was precisely Luo Zhongxian.

These past two days, he had swept through the outskirts of the Hundred Thousand Savage Mountains, eliminating twenty large savage beast valleys and over a hundred medium-sized savage beast valleys.

He did not bother with the small savage beast valleys, as the battle merits gained were too little. It was better to leave them to grow, or for the young people of the clan to challenge them, lest they lose their edge.

In total, he slew one thousand beasts at the ninth stage of Shedding Mortality, tens of thousands of beasts at the Shedding Mortality stage, and innumerable beasts at the Tempering Body stage.

The frenzied slaughter of these two days brought in over eight hundred million battle merits.

Luo Zhongxian successively purchased:

The Reincarnation Fist; Remnants of the Nine Cycles of Body Refinement; The Enlightenment Formation; The Cloud Concealment Formation; The War God Formation;

Essence Consolidating Liquid: one hundred bottles

Spirit Fruit: ten

Shedding Mortality Pills: one thousand

Tendon Transforming Liquid: ten thousand bottles

Total cost: 133,200,000 battle merits, leaving 680,000,000 battle merits remaining.

Apart from this, within Luo Zhongxian's system space of ten li, savage beast corpses were piled up like mountains.

Luo Zhongxian had long since reached the perfect ninth stage of Shedding Mortality, but he did not want to advance too quickly. He wanted to lay a solid foundation, even re-practicing martial arts from the beginning to build an unwavering base.

Without a firm foundation, even mountains could shake. From a long-term perspective, advancing too quickly was not necessarily a good thing.

The piles of savage beast corpses in his system space were "provisions" prepared for re-practicing his martial arts.

"Young Master, I have notified everyone in the clan. Tomorrow morning at sunrise, they will gather at the practice grounds," Liu Fang came over and said to Luo Zhongxian.

"Good. You've worked hard these past few days. Take these three tea leaves from the Enlightenment Tea Tree," Luo Zhongxian looked at the resolute Liu Fang and handed him three Enlightenment Tea Tree leaves.

"Thank you, Young Master. I will definitely break through to the Spirit Realm," Liu Fang said emotionally. Having encountered such a master, what more could one ask for?

After Liu Fang left, Luo Zhongxian muttered to himself, "It's time for the Luo Clan to 'reboot'."
