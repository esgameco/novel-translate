"Reporting to Young Master, this subordinate has one million pounds of strength at the Molt Nine Layers, equivalent to the strength of an elephant. An ordinary Molt Nine Layers only has one hundred thousand pounds of strength."

In the Barbarian Wilderness Continent, there was a creature called the Barbarian Elephant, with incredibly powerful physical strength. A single stomp from an adult could exert one million pounds of force. Thus, in the Barbarian Wilderness, having strength reaching one million pounds was simply called "the strength of an elephant."

Liu Fang was ten times stronger than an ordinary human, befitting a great general of the Sui Dynasty.

"So apart from prodigies, I have greater strength than any ordinary Molt Realm cultivator."

Massive barbarian beasts rushed out, their crimson eyes, drooling foul saliva. Their huge paws stomped the ground, causing thunderous booms.

"Perfect timing." Luo Zhongxian let out a loud roar, then drew the Human Emperor Sword and charged into the barbarian beast horde, unleashing a massive sword energy that swept through the front ranks.
