On the outskirts of the Ten Thousand Savage Mountains, beside a waterfall.

Like the heavens were pouring down, the water flowed from the sky, making a "rumbling, rumbling" sound.

A mile away from the waterfall, there was a green mountain covered in lush trees.

Luo Zhongxian stood beside a graveyard, with Liu Fang behind him.

On a dirt mound stood a tombstone, with sword-carved characters three feet deep, clearly visible: The Grave of Uncle Kun!

Luo Zhongxian's expression was wooden: "Uncle Kun, thank you for your ten years of personal protection. Since my father left, Luo Changdian and Luo Meng father and son have done nothing but try to kill me quickly, and everyone in the tribe wants to eat my blood.

"Uncle Kun, you allowed me to feel the only warmth in this world besides my father.

"Uncle Kun, you were my father's best subordinate. I think when my father meets you in the underworld, he will be proud to have such a subordinate.

"Father, Uncle Kun.

"I am no longer a cripple. Now I possess the legendary physique - the Devouring Physique. Now my martial arts cultivation has broken through again, advancing five levels in a single day, an occurrence rarely seen in a thousand years!

"Before long, I will personally cut off the heads of Luo Changdian and Luo Meng father and son,

"To avenge my father and Uncle Kun!"

Beside the waterfall cliff.

Watching the rolling river water flow eastward below, Luo Zhongxian recalled in a daze the previous life when the Yellow River rose, like a dragon dancing in the air.

"Liu Fang, go excavate a cave and check if there are any dangerous beasts nearby."

"Yes, young master, this subordinate will go take care of it now."

It's good to have a subordinate to share the burden. No wonder everyone wanted to be a leader in the previous life.

This river flows so rapidly. There must be many fish surviving in the water, so food is taken care of.

Night fell, and the moon brought a touch of cold to the pitch-black night.
