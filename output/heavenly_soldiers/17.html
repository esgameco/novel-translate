Twenty-three young people, both men and women, stood in the main hall of the Lord's mansion, curiously looking around.

It was their first time at the Lord's mansion, and it didn't seem extraordinary at all - just a main seat and a few tables and chairs.

As they were whispering among themselves, Luo Zhongxian walked in, saying as he approached:

"You are all very young with great potential ahead of you. You are all descendants of heroes. Your elders fought against ferocious beasts to protect the tribe. For your future, they were willing to shed blood and sacrifice their lives. This hard-won freedom should be cherished. At the same time, you should learn from your elders' spirit of self-strengthening and dare to struggle against enemies.

Alright, tell me your names."

Hearing the Lord mention their elders, they all looked at Luo Zhongxian. When asked about their names, they said one after another:

"Greetings Lord, I'm Luo Susu; I'm Luo Qi; Luo Chuchu; Luo Mengran; Luo Jian; Luo Xiao; Luo Meirong."

Luo Zhongxian looked at these young faces full of hope and expectations, the best age of life.

"Good, all nice names. Your names carry your elders' wishes for you to be happy or strong. For example, Luo Jian means being steadfast and persevering; Luo Xiao hopes you'll be happy; Luo Meirong wishes you to be radiant and graceful; Luo Susu is simple and natural."

As Luo Zhongxian spoke, he took out twenty-three bottles of Moulting Spirit Pills from his ring and handed them out, instructing:

"These are Moulting Spirit Pills that can help you break through the Moulting Realm, and they are also effective for further breakthroughs within that realm. Take them and from now on, you'll all work at the Lord's mansion.

You can live at home or stay here - there are a few vacant rooms that can be cleaned up for living. Men and women must live separately. Men cannot enter the women's area, and women cannot enter the men's area either. This is the rule. Any violation will result in immediate expulsion from the Lord's mansion."

"Let me assign today's tasks. Luo Susu, Luo Qi, and Luo Chuchu, you three form a group. Carry the Lord's token and conduct a population survey of the tribe. Register the names, cultivation realms, and household sizes of all tribespeople. After completing this, you can return. If you have any questions, you can ask me or your teammates. Open your minds and communicate more."

Luo Susu, Luo Qi, and Luo Chuchu went to prepare and discuss how to complete the Lord's first task.

Today I will do the investigation myself, do the public visit, but now there are people for that. He only needs to allocate resources to motivate everyone's behavior, tell everyone the general direction of what to do, and then he doesn't need to do it himself.

In the evening, the young master's mansion was brightly lit. The aroma of grilled meat wafted from the rooms. Everyone sat together eating barbecue. Luo Zhongxian was also there. He watched everyone quietly eating meat without saying a word, thinking about something in his mind. He found an excuse and left.

Indeed, not long after Luo Zhongxian left, the young people on site began to chat. They had never done anything like this before - investigation, public imitation, gathering together to eat, and even having meat to eat. More importantly, the young master's attitude towards them made them feel warmth...

They had so much to say, but with the young master present, they felt shy to speak up. So when Luo Zhongxian left, they began whispering, their voices growing louder and more lively.

Luo Zhongxian smiled secretly, recalling some past events from his previous life, and then headed to the study.

On the desk were the materials collected by the young people.

"I hope I can eat my fill every day."

"I hope I can go to the Martial Arts Academy to study and protect everyone in the future."

"I hope for more equal trade."

"I hope the academy is built near the martial arts grounds..."

"I hope the tribe has more spiritual energy."

"I hope there are fewer diseases in the tribe."

"I want to learn to read and write."

Looking at the materials, Luo Zhongxian's eyes became moist. The tribe was more backward and impoverished than he had imagined, with all trades waiting to flourish.

The issues the masses cared about were the issues Luo Zhongxian needed to prioritize and address as a qualified tribal leader. He must worry about the worries of all under heaven before enjoying their joys. If he keeps the masses in mind, the masses will naturally keep him in their hearts. He cannot be careless about matters concerning the interests of the masses.

Thinking this, Luo Zhongxian blew out the lights in the room and entered a sleep state. He had no cultivation, so he still needed rest.

"Tomorrow I'll go to the outside world to exchange for food and necessary supplies," thought Luo Zhongxian as he lay on his side in bed, not falling asleep for a long time...
