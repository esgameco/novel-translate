"Your urine can actually help the spirit plant grow?"

Chu Ning transmitted his voice in astonishment to Ling Xiaobai using his spiritual sense, and Ling Xiaobai responded with a "woo" sound.

There was even a hint of pride in that sound.

"Want to eat!"

The next moment, Ling Xiaobai's spiritual transmission resounded in Chu Ning's mind.

It was evident that Ling Xiaobai could grasp more than just the three words "let me go."

However, communicating this way was actually more convenient.

"Okay, remember to help this iron orchid grow every day. If the iron orchid fruit really grows, I'll share a few with you to eat."

As soon as Chu Ning finished speaking, Ling Xiaobai let out a "woo" and went straight to the other iron orchid plants.

And urinated in a puddle again.

Chu Ning's reaction was no longer like yesterday's. His only thought at the moment was...

"If this little guy urinates like this every day, and I cultivate sitting in the middle of these iron orchids in the future, won't the smell be too strong?"

Thinking this, yet Chu Ning's hands did not stop, releasing stream after stream of Green Wood Spring Florescence Arts, covering the entire iron orchid ring.

It was only after thirty more Green Wood Spring Florescence Arts that Chu Ning ended his spellcasting on the iron orchids.

Chu Ning then turned towards the spirit fruits and released stream after stream of Green Wood Spring Florescence Arts.

After finishing all this, Chu Ning finally sat cross-legged in the most densely planted area of the spirit fruits, preparing to cultivate the Green Wood Longevity Art.

Since coming to the market town, his cultivation could not be considered normal.

Previously, it was mainly due to the occurrence of the beast calamity, which had distracted too much of his effort in talisman making.

Then the spirit plants he had planted had not grown well, so the Green Wood Spring Florescence Art he commonly used to aid his cultivation had not shown its effects yet.

A few days ago, he had caught up some progress with the help of spirit stones, but after cultivating the spirit fruits and iron orchids successively, his daily cultivation methods could be used again.

[Green Wood Longevity Art (Yellow Grade Lower Tier), Second Layer (528/900)]

[Nine Revolutions Body Refining Art, First Scroll, Deathless Bones 181/600]

[Spirit Refining Art, First Layer 171/1000]

After checking his current proficiency levels, Chu Ning closed his eyes and began cultivating in the next moment.

Immediately, streams of spiritual energy converged on Chu Ning's body from the spirit fruits.

Ling Xiaobai, who had never paid attention to Chu Ning's cultivation before, tilted its little head in surprise at this scene.

It then looked down at its own body, as if deep in thought.

However, it soon lay down and fell into a deep sleep again.

Chu Ning cultivated for about half a day, feeling very satisfied with the speed of cultivation aided by the Green Wood Spring Florescence Art.

The Green Wood Longevity Art successfully increased by 1 proficiency point.

Chu Ning did not stop there. In the next moment, he took out a jade vial from his storage bag and poured out a pill from it.

It was the Qi Gathering Pill he had just bought from Gu Xiaoxing.

All along, Chu Ning had not relied too much on pills for his cultivation.

The main reason was that Chu Ning had the enhancement of the Green Wood Spring Florescence Art, so his cultivation speed was not slow.

There was also another important reason - Chu Ning possessed a Tiger Wood Spirit Body, allowing him to absorb spiritual energy from spirit stones very quickly.

For ordinary cultivators unable to rapidly absorb spiritual energy from spirit stones, using spirit stones to buy pills to aid cultivation became the best method.

Whenever Chu Ning needed a major breakthrough, he would directly use spirit stones.

However, Chu Ning was now prepared to give it a try. He had reached the sixth layer of Qi Refinement, and this Qi Gathering Pill would have better effects in the middle stages of Qi Refinement.

In the later stages of Qi Refinement, its effects would be average.

Taking a Qi Gathering Pill, Chu Ning swallowed it directly.

Feeling the spiritual power transformed from the pill in his body, Chu Ning began operating the Green Wood Longevity Art to cultivate.

It was only after the effects of the entire Qi Gathering Pill had been converted that Chu Ning slowly opened his eyes.

The first thing he did was check on the Green Wood Longevity Art.

[Green Wood Longevity Art (Yellow Grade Lower Tier), Second Layer (532/900)]

"One Qi Gathering Pill increased the proficiency by 3 points."

This rate of increase was actually about the same as directly using 10 spirit stones, or even slightly less.

"However, directly refining 10 spirit stones would take me longer," Chu Ning began to ponder.

"But if I use these Qi Gathering Pills, I can save some time, so it can still help speed up my cultivation.

Considering that the effects of pills will diminish if taken too frequently, these Qi Gathering Pills can only be taken for a period of time."

Thinking this, Chu Ning made up his mind.

For the time being, he could use some Qi Gathering Pills for cultivation.

And he didn't need to hide it anymore, he could just directly buy them from Gu Xiaoxing.

This way, there would be a reason for the increase in his cultivation speed, as he couldn't remain outwardly at the "fourth layer of Qi Refinement" forever.

Over the next ten days, Chu Ning cultivated, farmed, and played with Ling Xiaobai.

He intentionally trained Ling Xiaobai to understand his normal speech. Sometimes he would say something and then transmit it again with his spiritual sense.

The results were good - Ling Xiaobai gradually began to understand simple words.

As for cultivation, without neglecting Body Refinement and Spirit Refinement, Chu Ning took one Qi Gathering Pill every day to cultivate the Green Wood Longevity Art.

At the same time, five days after planting the iron orchids, they had completely grown normally, and Chu Ning began using the iron orchids with the Green Wood Spring Florescence Art to aid his cultivation as well.

He even occasionally used spirit stones.

With these multiple enhancements, Chu Ning's Green Wood Longevity Art advanced at a rapid pace of nearly 5 proficiency points per day.

Within ten days, Chu Ning's proficiency in the Green Wood Longevity Art directly increased by 50 points.

And a whole bottle of Qi Gathering Pills was used up.

Having tasted the benefits, Chu Ning went straight to Gu Xiaoxing's shop early that day, intending to buy some more to continue using pills to aid his cultivation.

Ling Xiaobai didn't know what was going on, but it seemed to be sleeping less than before these past couple of days.

When Chu Ning went out, it also waddled along behind him.

"Senior Sister Gu."

When he arrived at Gu Xiaoxing's shop, she was holding a few jade vials and working on some pills.

"Junior Brother Chu has come."

Gu Xiaoxing greeted Chu Ning first, then her gaze fell on Ling Xiaobai beside him.

"Oh, what a cute spirit beast!

Junior Brother Chu, is this a spirit beast you cultivated? I've never seen it before."

As she said this, Gu Xiaoxing's eyes lit up.

It seemed this cute little pet still had quite an impact on women.

"No, I didn't cultivate it. I just picked it up on the road," Chu Ning casually replied, intending to ask Gu Xiaoxing about buying Qi Gathering Pills.

However, this woman was clearly attracted by the slightly adorable Ling Xiaobai and had her attention drawn to it.

She even walked over to Chu Ning's side at this moment.

Ling Xiaobai looked at the approaching Gu Xiaoxing with a somewhat wary gaze, appearing very guarded, as if ready to launch an attack at any moment.

Seeing this, Chu Ning quickly transmitted to Ling Xiaobai with his spiritual sense, indicating that this was his friend and there was no danger.

After hearing the spiritual transmission, Ling Xiaobai finally dropped its guarded look.

Only then did Chu Ning relax a little.

He hadn't forgotten that when he first met Ling Xiaobai, it could unleash offensive spells.

Although he had blocked it with a magic weapon back then, the Silver Crystal Bamboo Shield was a mid-grade magic weapon.

Ling Xiaobai's white spell seemed to be of the ice element, and its power didn't seem too weak either.

If Gu Xiaoxing wasn't careful and got hurt, that wouldn't be good.

Seeing Ling Xiaobai's reaction, Gu Xiaoxing's interest clearly increased.

She squatted down, looking at Ling Xiaobai curiously and asked, "You picked it up? What kind of spirit beast is this? I don't recognize it."

Chu Ning shook his head, "I don't know what kind of spirit beast it is either."

He did hope Gu Xiaoxing would recognize it, but it was evident that this fellow senior sister didn't know either.

Gu Xiaoxing just curiously examined Ling Xiaobai.

"This little spirit beast is just too adorable..."

As she spoke, Gu Xiaoxing reached out her hand, wanting to pet Ling Xiaobai.

However, the little guy wasn't willing, leaping away and jumping straight onto Chu Ning's shoulder.

This startled Gu Xiaoxing.

"This little one is still a bit afraid of strangers since I just picked it up," Chu Ning quickly explained upon seeing Ling Xiaobai's reaction.

"Senior Sister Gu, do you have any Qi Gathering Pills for sale? I want to buy some."

Seeing that Ling Xiaobai didn't want to interact with her, Gu Xiaoxing retracted her hand with some regret, glancing at the little beast a few more times before looking at Chu Ning and saying, "Qi Gathering Pills? Junior Brother Chu wants to take them himself."

Chu Ning nodded, "Yes, I'm nearing a breakthrough, so I want to buy some to improve my cultivation."

"So Junior Brother Chu is about to break through to the fifth layer of Qi Refinement," Gu Xiaoxing gave Chu Ning a gentle smile.

"How many do you want?"

Chu Ning thought for a moment, "30 pills."

30 would be enough for a month's consumption.

"30 pills?" Gu Xiaoxing was slightly taken aback upon hearing this.

Then she said seriously, "Junior Brother Chu, it's not that the more of these pills you take, the better.

Normally, it's best to take one Qi Gathering Pill every three days to fully absorb the medicinal effects."

She paused, then continued, "Moreover, pills generally carry some pill toxins. This three-day period is also just enough time to expel the accompanying pill toxins through cultivation.

If you take them too frequently, the pill toxins will accumulate in your body, and over a long period of time, they will cause some damage."

"Pill toxins?" Chu Ning was slightly stunned upon hearing this.

He had been taking them continuously for ten days now, but he hadn't felt any pill toxins.

Thinking of this, he asked Gu Xiaoxing, "Senior Sister Gu, can we sense these residual pill toxins in our bodies?"

"Of course!" Gu Xiaoxing nodded slightly.

"We cultivators have sensitive perceptions. Not to mention 30 pills, even with just 5 or 10 pills, if the pill toxins are not expelled in time, we would be able to sense them during cultivation."

Upon hearing this, Chu Ning blinked.

He had already continuously taken 10 pills, one per day, yet he hadn't felt any presence of pill toxins.

Was it because of his Tiger Wood Spirit Body? Or the Nine Revolutions Body Refining Art?

Chu Ning brushed off this thought and didn't dwell on it further.

At least from the current situation, taking pills at this frequency didn't seem to have any effect on him.

Since that was the case, it shouldn't be a problem to continue taking them more frequently for a while.

At most, if he sensed any pill toxins, he could just stop taking the pills first, then expel the toxins.

Thinking this, Chu Ning said to Gu Xiaoxing, "Senior Sister Gu, I'll pay attention. I'll buy some first and control the dosage myself."

Hearing Chu Ning say this, Gu Xiaoxing naturally didn't say anything more.

She took out three vials of Qi Gathering Pills and handed them to Chu Ning.

Then she took out another jade vial, poured out two pills from it, and smiled, "I won't give Junior Brother Chu a discount on the price. Let me gift you two extra pills. When your spirit fruits are ripe, don't forget to give me a price concession."

"Thank you, Senior Sister," Chu Ning replied as he took out 300 spirit stones and handed them to her, then accepted the Qi Gathering Pills.

With these pills, he could focus on cultivating and improving his progress for the time being.

Bringing Ling Xiaobai back to the courtyard he was staying in, Chu Ning went straight to the back garden.

The iron orchids had grown half a foot taller than when he had first planted them.

Apart from Chu Ning performing the Green Wood Spring Florescence Art every day, Ling Xiaobai had also made an indispensable contribution.

It was precisely because Ling Xiaobai had persisted in "irrigating" them every day that the iron orchids had grown so well.

What made Chu Ning even happier was that all 50 iron orchids had bloomed.

And these iron orchids would definitely bear fruit once they flowered.

Chu Ning didn't think this was due to his Green Wood Spring Florescence Art, it could only be Ling Xiaobai's ability.

"This little guy sure has quite a few abilities," Chu Ning praised Ling Xiaobai, then cast the Green Wood Spring Florescence Art on the iron orchids a full thirty times.
