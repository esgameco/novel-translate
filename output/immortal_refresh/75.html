Chapter 75

Here is a full translation of the excerpt from Chinese to English:

Chu Ning and the other person rode on the wind, walking at a good pace.

In just half an incense stick's time, they covered over ten li and arrived.

The twenty mu spiritual field was located between two small hills, relatively isolated.

At first glance, Chu Ning was very satisfied with it.

Such a relatively isolated place was perfect for him.

After all, in addition to regular farming, he still needed to cultivate using the Green Wood Spring Technique.

His only concern was safety.

So Chu Ning turned his head and asked Du Han:

"Senior Brother Du, this spiritual field is over ten li away from the town. I don't know if this area is safe."

Seeing Chu Ning's cautious look, Du Han slightly frowned.

But he still answered Chu Ning's question.

"Rest assured, the hills around this spiritual field are also spiritual fields belonging to a few cultivator families like the Sun family, who are subordinate to our Green Creek Sect."

"They have patrol teams patrolling the area every day."

Chu Ning nodded and confirmed with him again:

"So no one will try to steal my spiritual plants at night, right?"

Du Han frowned again, "I said there are people patrolling here."

"Moreover, this spiritual field belongs to our Green Creek Sect. Everyone in the Yan Ji Town area knows this.

No rogue cultivators would dare target our Green Creek Sect."

Saying this, Du Han glanced at Chu Ning,

"Besides, don't you have formation arrays set up in your Spiritual Plant Pavilion?"

Chu Ning listened and nodded slightly.

This spiritual field did indeed have simple formation arrays set up, just like the ones in the sect.

However, these arrays were extremely basic, which could be broken through with just a talisman.

They only prevented noble people, not malicious ones.

In other words, they only guarded against very low-level cultivators or mortals trespassing.

That's why Chu Ning asked so clearly about it.

Chu Ning took out the array plate from his body, about to directly enter the spiritual field.

Du Han spoke from the side: "Take a look if you want, I'll head back first."

"Senior Brother Du isn't going to take a look?" Chu Ning stopped and asked.

Du Han waved his hand dismissively.

"How you cultivate this spiritual field is up to your Celestial Arts Pavilion.

If you want to look, stay. If not, leave with me now."

Chu Ning thought for a moment and said, "Then Senior Brother Du can head back first, I still need to see what to plant in this spiritual field."

Hearing this, Du Han didn't say much more and turned to leave directly.

Only after Du Han left did Chu Ning slightly shake his head.

To be honest, he did miss interacting with Zhuang Yunde and He Changfeng from the same pavilion back at the sect.

After all, they were from the same pavilion and had some friendship.

But soon, Chu Ning dismissed this thought.

The Spiritual Plant Pavilion also had people like Cao Dongxin and Qi Chongmao - the world has all kinds of people.

Walking into the spiritual field, Chu Ning carefully sensed it again.

He had never seen the Grade B spiritual fields in the sect, but the spiritual qi quality here was indeed higher than the Grade C field where he took the advancement test before.

With twenty mu of land, what should he plant?

Chu Ning began contemplating. First, he definitely had to plant spirit bamboo for making talismans.

After all, he himself needed large quantities of talisman paper to make talismans.

With the quality of this spiritual field, he didn't need to plant ink spirit bamboo, but could plant purple crystal bamboo instead.

Purple crystal bamboo was also a premium material for making talisman paper, with far lower impurities than ink spirit bamboo.

The same bamboo stalk could produce up to fifty to seventy percent more pulp.

Of course, the difficulty of cultivation was correspondingly higher.

However, for Chu Ning at this time, there was not much difficulty in planting.

"Ten mu to grow purple crystal bamboo, first to meet my own talisman-making needs." Chu Ning immediately made up his mind.

Before coming, he had brought bamboo seeds of ink spirit bamboo and purple crystal bamboo, intending to see the situation of the spiritual field afterwards.

Now that he found the spiritual field level was sufficient, naturally there was no hesitation in growing the purple crystal bamboo.

"Another 10 mu..."

Chu Ning pondered for a moment, not making a direct decision.

Most of the disciples from the Spirit Plant Pavilion previously grew spirit-returning spiritual grass.

This spiritual grass is an excellent medicinal material for refining spirit-returning pills, which can be sold to the Pill Refining Pavilion.

But Chu Ning was not sure if Gu Xiaoqing was also refining those spirit-returning pills for sale.

"I'll go back and discuss with Senior Sister Gu. If she still needs to refine spirit-returning pills, then I'll grow spirit-returning spiritual grass. If not, I'll consider other options."

Chu Ning thought to himself, no longer dwelling on the matter of the 10 mu.

Instead, he took out the purple crystal bamboo seeds from his storage bag and began the catalytic planting process.

After all, even if he used the Green Wood Revitalization Technique, it would take him a few months for the purple crystal bamboo to mature enough for making talisman paper.

Chu Ning did not want to delay any time.

The purple crystal bamboo seeds were slightly more difficult to catalyze than the ink spirit bamboo, but still easier than the bloodwood tree.

So for Chu Ning, it was not a problem.

Speaking of the bloodwood tree, Chu Ning also had its seeds, but this spiritual plant was difficult to catalyze, and its absorption of spiritual energy was not fast, requiring a very long growth time.

It was not suitable for practicing the Green Wood Revitalization Technique, so it was not within Chu Ning's consideration.

Chu Ning took out the purple crystal bamboo seeds, first using the Object Driving Technique to evenly scatter the seeds, then casting several catalytic spells.

Soon, small purple bamboo shoots emerged from the ground in this field.

In the span of one hour, Chu Ning had completed planting 3 mu of the spiritual field.

This speed was much faster than when he previously planted the ink spirit bamboo.

Seeing the time of day, it was already noon, so Chu Ning left the spiritual field directly.

No matter how diligent one is, one still needs to eat.

After all, at the Qi Refining stage, he could not yet practice fasting cultivation.

He returned by riding the wind along the path he came.

Along the way, Chu Ning saw that there were indeed some spiritual fields nearby, which must be the lands of the Sun Family and other clans that Du Han mentioned.

Half an hour later, Chu Ning returned to the market street.

He came to his own shop, but before he could open the door, he heard two consecutive "creak" sounds as the doors of the neighboring shops opened.

Luo Yiping and Lu Miaoying from the Spirit Beast Pavilion next door, as well as Gu Xiaoqing across the street, all came out.

Moreover, their gazes were all directed towards Chu Ning, clearly indicating they had something to discuss with him.

The few of them exchanged glances, seeming surprised that the others also wanted to find Chu Ning.

Finally, the more impatient Luo Yiping spoke first.

"Chu Ning, you're back. We've been waiting for you for half a day."

Hearing Luo Yiping's address, Chu Ning frowned inwardly but calmly asked, "Senior Brother Luo, you were looking for me?"

Luo Yiping nodded, then said to Chu Ning, "Let's go inside to talk."

Chu Ning then opened the shop door and went straight in, followed by Luo Yiping, Lu Miaoying, and Gu Xiaoqing.

Before they could say anything, hearing the commotion, Shen Zhengquan also came in curiously.

For the time being, everyone had gathered.

Seeing this, Chu Ning was puzzled inwardly, but did not take the initiative to speak.

Since these people had come to find him, they would naturally state their business.

Sure enough, the next moment, Luo Yiping spoke first.

"Chu Ning, let me tell you something."

(End of chapter)
