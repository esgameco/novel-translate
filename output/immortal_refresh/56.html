After returning to his own residence, a slight smile appeared on Chun Ning's face.

Although he had felt that his performance today was a bit too high-profile, he was undoubtedly still happy to have been admitted into the third-level disciple rank.

Even though he didn't know exactly how the sect would arrange for them to learn various skills, as Mu Ling had mentioned earlier.

But at least he was now considered a third-level disciple, which gave him hope.

If he could truly learn talisman scripting, with his innate talent for talisman sources, the income he could earn from that would be far higher than just making talisman papers.

While cultivators' innate talents were certainly important, resources were no less crucial.

Just as Chun Ning was feeling a bit excited, he vaguely heard the sound of conversation coming from the courtyard next door.

His excitement faded a bit, and he frowned instead.

In fact, what truly made Chun Ning uneasy after this promotion was not that he had stood out too much.

After all, people might forget about that kind of thing after some time.

What really made Chun Ning uneasy was that Luo Hongping and Lu Yunfang had also both successfully been promoted.

If they were arranged residences or spirit fields in the third-level disciple area, he didn't know if they would end up too close to him.

Ever since he suspected that the two of them might have some connection to Qi Chongmao, Chun Ning had been somewhat wary of interacting with them.

"It's a pity that the third-level disciple area isn't overseen by Zhuang Yunde. Otherwise, I could have asked him to help arrange things before he left the sect."

Speaking of which, Chun Ning felt a bit of melancholy.

He didn't know if things would still be as convenient in the third-level disciple area without Zhuang Yunde there.

As melancholic as he felt, Chun Ning quickly adjusted his mindset.

He continued with his regular cultivation.

That night, after practicing the Qingmu Changchun Gong, he sat cross-legged on the bed and began cultivating the Spirit Refining Technique.

This Spirit Refining Technique was not only for strengthening his spiritual sense, but also contained methods for utilizing the spiritual sense.

In addition to higher-level spirit sense attack secrets like the Illusion Piercing Thorn, there were also some simpler applications, such as using the spiritual sense for detection and telepathic communication.

Chun Ning had actually learned the voice transmission technique before, but it was still different from this.

The voice transmission technique involved making an extremely subtle sound with the mouth and transmitting it to others through spiritual energy.

Whereas this spiritual sense technique allowed one to directly transmit messages into the recipient's mind without making any sound at all, which was more advanced.

After cultivating for a while, just as Chun Ning was about to end his session and rest, his mind stirred.

He sensed someone appearing in the courtyard and walking towards his bedroom.

He released his spiritual sense without a second thought!

After a period of cultivation, his spiritual sense had improved, and its external range had expanded from about 2 zhang to an additional half chi.

Soon, a familiar figure appeared within the range of his spiritual sense.

Cao Dongxin?

When he sensed the visitor's identity, Chun Ning let out a slight sigh.

"So he's come after all!"

Today, Chun Ning had deliberately ignored Cao Dongxin, with the intention of provoking or testing him.

Cao Dongxin had a reclusive personality, the kind of person most prone to taking extreme actions.

This meant that if he realized Chun Ning's future accomplishments would surpass his own, he might worry about retaliation.

As for whether he could swallow this grievance or would take an extreme path, Chun Ning wasn't sure.

He just wanted to probe a bit.

And now it seemed that Cao Dongxin had indeed not disappointed him in this probing.

Thinking this, Chun Ning continued sensing him with his spiritual sense while preparing to respond accordingly.

When Cao Dongxin arrived near Chun Ning's room door, he took something out from his body and used a Driving Force Technique to fling it towards Chun Ning's room.

Seeing this, Chun Ning's body flickered as he leapt off the bed.

Activating his Wind Riding Technique, he flashed out to the rear courtyard after a few movements.

However, Chu Ning did not dare to be careless.

After entering the backyard, Chu Ning continued to release his spiritual sense to probe the surroundings.

Then, he saw that the thing Cao Dongxin had thrown at the doorway, after silently falling to the floor, actually emitted a hazy gray smoke.

Cao Dongxin immediately used a simple spell to force that gray smoke directly into the room.

Seeing this scene, how could Chu Ning not understand the other party's intentions?

Although unclear what this gray smoke was, since the other party used it, it must be effective against cultivators.

Either to make one lose their powers, or to poison and knock out.

It was obvious that Cao Dongxin was also worried about being discovered, so he resorted to these despicable means.

After all, there were people living next door to this courtyard.

"Indeed, a pitiful person must have despicable traits!"

At this moment, Chu Ning felt a chill in his heart.

Before this, he really had not intended to do anything to the other party.

He just didn't want to show Cao Dongxin a good face.

But now that the other party had taken the initiative to provoke him, and had clearly revealed such hostility towards him,

Chu Ning naturally had nothing good to say.

In the backyard, Qi Chongmao was alone, and he didn't mind letting the other party accompany him.

The only trouble was that the couple next door might notice too much commotion and link Qi Chongmao's disappearance to him.

Thinking of this, Chu Ning remained in the backyard, calmly using his spiritual sense to monitor Cao Dongxin's every move.

He saw that after about the time it takes to drink a cup of tea, Cao Dongxin threw a pill into his mouth, then pushed open Chu Ning's room door and walked in.

"Why is no one here?"

Cao Dongxin entered the room and, seeing no sign of Chu Ning, his dark face immediately changed.

Then, he glanced at the backyard, his gaze flickering, holding a talisman in his hand as he walked towards the backyard.

Chu Ning had completely concealed his aura in his Yinmu Spiritual Body, using his spiritual sense to detect the other party's distance.

The moment Cao Dongxin stepped out of the room into the backyard, the well-prepared Chu Ning gripped a Spiritual Movement Talisman in his hand.

In the next moment, he flashed over, appearing right in front of Cao Dongxin.

Tiangang Fist!

Chu Ning didn't give the other party any time to react, striking out with a lethal move!

Cao Dongxin only then reacted, trying to activate the talisman in his hand, but it was too late.

"Bang!"

Chu Ning's fist struck Cao Dongxin directly.

"You..."

Cao Dongxin's eyes were wide, filled with disbelief.

Even until his death, he couldn't figure out

How Chu Ning had discovered him in advance. He had been extremely cautious, even using the Mind-Muddling Powder.

And how had Chu Ning, at the fourth level of Qi Refining, become a Body Cultivator who had cultivated such an extraordinary ability?

He was at the fifth level of Qi Refining, yet didn't even have the strength to fight back.

Chu Ning didn't care what Cao Dongxin was thinking before he died. He swiftly collected all the items on his body, then shot out a few fireballs, burning him to ashes.

Afterwards, Chu Ning released his spiritual sense again to probe the surroundings.

Finding that the couple next door was still resting with no abnormalities, he finally relaxed a bit.

His actions just now had been extremely decisive and swift, so the two neighbors should not have noticed anything.

However, even so, Chu Ning did not let his guard down in the slightest.

That night, Chu Ning simply sat in meditation to cultivate. It wasn't until dawn with still no anomalies that he truly set his mind at ease.

After tidying up a bit, Chu Ning left the courtyard to head to the Dao Cultivation Hall.
