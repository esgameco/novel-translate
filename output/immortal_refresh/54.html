The location chosen for the promotion assessment in District C was a medium-grade spirit field in District C.

District C did not have any low-grade spirit fields, but its medium-grade spirit fields were already somewhat better than the high-grade spirit fields in District D.

When Chu Ning and the others arrived, dozens of people had already gathered in the fields.

What surprised Chu Ning slightly was that Cao Dongxin and Shang Zhaoxiang were also present.

However, after a moment's thought, Chu Ning felt it was normal. The two of them had already been at the fifth level of Qi refining for quite some time.

It was evident that making greater breakthroughs in District D was not easy, so it was normal for them to try their luck and see if they could obtain better resources by coming to District C.

Chu Ning's appearance also surprised the two of them to some extent. Cao Dongxin continued to have an expression as if others owed him something.

As for Shang Zhaoxiang, he smiled at Chu Ning, but since they were far apart, he did not say much.

After Chu Ning and the others arrived, more people continued to come, eventually reaching nearly a hundred people.

Clearly, Chu Ning was not the only one who wanted to advance and obtain better resources.

After waiting for a while, He Changyou appeared, accompanied by several disciple enforcers from the Spirit Plant House, including Zhuang Yunde.

"You are all here to participate in the promotion assessment. There's no need for further explanation. The assessment method is very simple.

I will give each of you a blood teak seed. Within half a day, the top six people who grow the best quality plants will complete the promotion."

After He Changyou finished speaking, many people in the field already looked displeased, including Cao Dongxin and Shang Zhaoxiang.

The blood teak was notoriously difficult to cultivate, requiring several years of time before it could be harvested.

Yet now, He Changyou was only giving them half a day to cultivate it.

He Changyou did not provide any explanation and directly instructed the enforcers beside him to distribute the seeds to everyone.

After receiving the blood teak seeds, Chu Ning and the others were arranged to stand apart from each other, with a distance of five zhang between each person to avoid interfering with each other's spellcasting.

Once He Changyou saw that everyone was in position, he waved his hand.

"The assessment begins!"

Following He Changyou's words, nearly a hundred people immediately sprang into action.

During the past half month, Chu Ning had roughly understood the spirit plants suitable for cultivation in District C, including the blood teak.

He first placed the dark red blood teak seed in the soil of the spirit field, burying it about one finger-joint deep. Then he sat cross-legged on the ground.

Catalyzing Technique, instant cast!

Without any hand seals, he simply waved his hand, and a spell formula struck the spot where the blood teak seed was buried in the soil.

Apart from Chu Ning, some others capable of the Catalyzing Technique also began casting their spells.

However, not everyone in the field could instantly cast the Catalyzing Technique at this time.

For example, Cao Dongxin and others were still forming hand seals to gather spiritual power.

Chu Ning and those who could instantly cast had already gained some time advantage over the others.

Catalyzing the blood teak was indeed not a simple matter.

Chu Ning's instant cast of the Catalyzing Technique saved a bit of time, but in the overall catalyzing process, it was not too significant.

However, after repeated castings of the Catalyzing Technique, the time and effect would be different.

Moreover, for Chu Ning, the instant cast of the Catalyzing Technique was not just for saving time.

The instant cast Catalyzing Technique not only conserved spiritual power but also had a better catalyzing effect.

As the spiritual power from the first Catalyzing Technique fully merged into the blood teak seed, Chu Ning unhesitatingly cast the second one.

Then the third, the fourth...

Catalyzing the blood teak was very difficult. In terms of difficulty, it was the most difficult spirit plant Chu Ning had encountered so far in catalyzing.

Previously, when Chu Ning catalyzed the Seven-Star Spirit Fruit, he had only used ten Catalyzing Techniques.

At this moment, Chu Ning had used a total of twenty catalyzing spells before the blood teak seed finally reacted and began to crack its shell.

Chu Ning's spiritual sense perceived the changes in the blood teak seed, and he immediately felt delighted.

The catalyzing spell was cast again. This time, his spiritual power was released more precisely, directly penetrating through the cracked gap and striking into the interior of the seed.

This was completely an unconscious action by Chu Ning. After all, he had not catalyzed any spirit plants since cultivating the Refining Spirit Art.

However, this method produced an amazing effect.

As Chu Ning's spell struck, the cracked gap in the blood teak seed instantly widened, and a tender sprout directly grew out, about to emerge from the soil.

Seeing this scene, how could Chu Ning hesitate? Another catalyzing spell was released.

Immediately, that tender sprout grew as if aided by divine forces, directly breaking through the soil.

"Eh?"

Chu Ning's movements were first noticed by He Changyu.

His gaze swept over, and he discovered that Chu Ning was actually the first to complete the catalysis of the blood teak gift, immediately revealing an astonished expression.

Subsequently, Zhuang Yunde and the others also noticed and turned their gazes towards Chu Ning.

A flash of surprise appeared on Zhuang Yunde's face, but then his round face regained its usual smile.

"This Junior Brother Chu can really surprise himself."

At this time, Chu Ning had already stood up and begun performing the hand seals for the Sweet Rain Art.

Just as Chu Ning stood up and started the hand seals for the Sweet Rain Art, four more people also successively completed the catalysis.

Two were men in their late twenties, and the other two were surprisingly Luo Hongping and Lu Yunfang.

The five of them simultaneously performed the Sweet Rain Art, but Chu Ning, who had started first, fell behind.

The reason was simple - the other four could instantly cast the Sweet Rain Art.

But Chu Ning took some time to complete the hand seals before finishing the Sweet Rain Art.

Zhuang Yunde silently shook his head at this moment.

From his observation of the quality of the Ink Bamboo that Chu Ning cultivated, Chu Ning's mastery of the Green Wood Spring Bloom Art should definitely not be low.

Even if he couldn't cast it instantly, it shouldn't be much different.

Yet this even simpler Sweet Rain Art, how could Chu Ning not be able to cast it instantly?

However, Zhuang Yunde soon realized the reason.

He recalled that Chu Ning had previously told him that he was extremely compatible with wood-type techniques and spells like the Green Wood Spring Bloom Art.

At this point, the Sweet Rain Arts of the few people had all concluded.

Surprisingly, Luo Hongping and Lu Yunfang were the first to use the Green Wood Spring Bloom Art, and it seemed they could also cast it instantly.

Not just them, even the disciples with the surnames Lu and Chen could instantly cast the Green Wood Spring Bloom Art.

Fortunately, Chu Ning did not lag too far behind them and soon completed casting the Sweet Rain Art as well.

Instant cast! Green Wood Spring Bloom Art!

Although Chu Ning had reserved some spiritual power, he did not hold back on his spells.

His instantly cast Green Wood Spring Bloom Art was not inferior to the other four at all and was even better at gathering spiritual energy.

Chu Ning unleashed one Green Wood Spring Bloom Art after another, casting nearly twenty of them in total.

After a half hour's pause, he used the Green Wood Spring Bloom Art again.

The blood teak in front of Chu Ning grew at a pace no weaker than the other four disciples at the Refining Qi sixth level.

Until a few hours later, He Changyu's voice suddenly rang out.

"Time's up, everyone stop casting."

Upon hearing this, Chu Ning and the others immediately stopped casting.

The advancement assessment had officially concluded.
