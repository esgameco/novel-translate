The white little demonic beast tilted its head slightly at this moment, showing an expression of bewilderment similar to humans.

"Can you understand what I'm saying?"

This time, the white little beast finally stopped its other actions and let out a "wu" sound.

Chu Ning was greatly astonished and asked again with his divine sense transmission: "You want me to release you."

As soon as these words fell, the white little beast kept nodding its head and making "wu wu" sounds from its mouth.

Then, it seemed to have remembered something, looking towards Chu Ning.

"Release me!"

The next moment, this voice resounded in Chu Ning's ears again.

Chu Ning had finally confirmed at this point that this white little beast could indeed communicate using divine sense, which made him extremely surprised.

Even if he didn't understand much about spiritual beasts and demonic beasts, he could tell at this moment that this white little beast was not an ordinary demonic beast.

After all, from Chu Ning's divine sense perception, this white little beast was just a mid-level first-grade demonic beast.

Normally, demonic beasts of this level would absolutely not possess such intelligence.

Chu Ning felt that such a spiritually-aware little beast would be a bit of a pity to directly kill.

However, this little beast was very fast, and it also seemed to have some special abilities.

Even a beast trapping talisman could not trap it, and it was unknown how it had passed through the protective forbidden formation to appear in the spirit field.

If he directly released it, it would probably run away immediately.

Chu Ning communicated with the white little beast again using his divine sense.

"What if you run away after I release you?"

The white little beast kept shaking its head and waving its hooves, looking very anxious.

"Release me!"

At the same time, Chu Ning heard this voice again.

Chu Ning realized that although the white little beast could communicate using divine sense, it seemed to have just learned how to do so.

It could understand what he said, but it could not produce many sounds itself.

Thinking it over, Chu Ning could only say to it with his divine sense:

"I can release you, but you cannot run around randomly. Otherwise, I will trap you again, and moreover..."

As he spoke, Chu Ning's hand had already produced a few more beast trapping talismans and a few sword talismans made of solid metal.

Seeing the talismans in Chu Ning's hand, a hint of fear appeared in the white little beast's eyes.

Immediately after, Chu Ning saw a faint white halo appear on the white little beast's forehead.

At the same time, the white little beast's weak voice also sounded in his ear.

"Blood!"

Chu Ning hesitated slightly, understanding the white little beast's meaning.

It was asking him to drip his blood into that white halo, likely to establish some kind of contract relationship.

As a traveler from another world, Chu Ning roughly understood the intention behind the little beast's request.

However, Chu Ning was still somewhat hesitant.

He did not know much about spiritual beasts and demonic beasts, and was not sure whether establishing such a contract-like relationship would have any impact on himself.

After all, he knew that when cultivators formed contracts with higher-level spiritual beasts or demonic beasts, there were often certain conditions that had to be met.

In some cases, if the spiritual beast died after forming a contract, the cultivator would also suffer harm.

And this little beast was clearly not an ordinary being.

Thinking it over, Chu Ning shook his head, not daring to act rashly.

However, considering how intelligent this beast seemed, he immediately communicated with it using his divine sense:

"Never mind, there's no need to form a contract. I'll trust you."

The white little beast opened its eyes, revealing an expression of human-like delight, and the white halo on its forehead disappeared.

"Wu!"

The little beast then let out a happy sound.

Chu Ning smiled slightly after hearing it.

Following the method for using the beast trapping talisman, he made a hand seal and directed his spiritual power towards the white little beast.

Immediately, the restraints originally binding the white little beast vanished.

Thinking further, Chu Ning also took out a healing talisman.

The white little beast's gaze flickered, showing a somewhat fearful look.

Chu Ning then communicated to it with his divine sense:

"Don't be afraid, this is a spell to help heal your injuries."

After hearing this, the white little beast looked at Chu Ning and nodded, as if saying "I trust you too."

Chu Ning smiled again, activated the healing talisman, and a healing spell fell upon the white little beast.

The wounds on the white little beast's body disappeared, and it joyfully spun around on the spot.

"Wu!"

It called out happily towards Chu Ning again.

"This little beast is quite interesting," Chu Ning felt inwardly.

After entering Qingxi Sect, Chu Ning had always been cautious and on guard.

After this interaction with the little beast, his heart felt somewhat relaxed and joyful.

However, looking at the pitted ground of his spirit field beside him, he couldn't help but glare at the little beast again.

"Look at the mess you've made, ruining my spirit field."

Hearing this, the white little beast lay down on the ground, acting as if it was admitting its mistake and accepting punishment.

Chu Ning shook his head, not saying anything further.

Fortunately, these were plants he had just planted not long ago, and he hadn't come to tend to them since the beast disaster occurred after planting them.

So these two spirit plants hadn't grown very well over the past few days.

As long as he used the seeds to replant them with a catalyzing spell, and then applied the Green Wood Spring Blossom Technique, the impact shouldn't be too great.

Thinking this, Chu Ning started from a corner of the spirit field, first taking out the seeds of the Golden Origin Fruit to catalyze and replant them.

Seeing Chu Ning cultivating, the white little beast followed a few zhang behind.

At this moment, Chu Ning still could not fully trust this little beast, so he held the Silver Crystal Frost Bamboo Shield in one hand.

In any case, his catalyzing spell was instantaneous and did not require making hand seals.
