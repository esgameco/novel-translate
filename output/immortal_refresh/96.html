Here is my full translation of the excerpt from Chinese to English:

Initially, it was through performing the Qingmu Chunhua technique on this old man that Chu Ning came into contact with the Seven Star Spirit Fruit.

So with just one glance, Chu Ning recognized him.

However, he didn't know why this Old Man Sun had appeared here.

Moreover, at this moment, he clearly seemed to be being pursued by someone.

Though surprised, Chu Ning had no intention of lending a hand to rescue him.

Although this Old Man Sun had given him some spirit stones back then, after all, the two only had a transaction relationship.

And now looking with his spiritual sense at those pursuing from behind, two were at the sixth level of Qi refinement, and one at the seventh level.

If it weren't for Old Man Sun continuously triggering talismans, with his fifth level of Qi refinement, he would have been caught up to long ago.

It wasn't until their figures had gone far away for quite a while that Chu Ning finally left the Spirit Field forbidden area.

However, at this point Chu Ning had changed his appearance.

There was no special reason, purely a precautionary behavior after seeing someone.

After leaving the forbidden area, Chu Ning then remembered Ling Xiaobai, and was about to take out the spirit beast pouch to put it inside.

"Wuu!"

At this moment, Ling Xiaobai suddenly called out.

The next moment, Ling Xiaobai's voice transmission entered Chu Ning's mind.

"Follow me!"

Then it nodded its head towards the northeast direction.

"Where to?" Chu Ning was slightly puzzled.

Ling Xiaobai didn't answer, but instead dashed towards the northeast.

Seeing this, Chu Ning hurriedly caught up a few steps and called out, "Xiaobai!"

"Wuu!"

Ling Xiaobai turned to look at Chu Ning, then continued running forward.

"Follow me!"

Chu Ning also sensed the signal from its spiritual sense again.

Watching Ling Xiaobai run ahead, Chu Ning couldn't help but hesitate.

But after calling out again with no sign of it stopping, Chu Ning could only follow along reluctantly.

Heading northeast, Chu Ning soon arrived at the hill where he had practiced the Cangmu Sword Technique earlier.

But Ling Xiaobai showed no intention of stopping, turning to head into the mountains.

This caused Chu Ning's brows to furrow slightly.

Going further into the mountains risked encountering demonic beasts.

But with Ling Xiaobai continuously signaling to follow, Chu Ning could only comply.

After all, even if there were demonic beasts nearby, they would only be initial or mid-level first-grade ones.

This was the first time the spiritually connected Ling Xiaobai had reacted this way, making Chu Ning curious about where it was leading him.

After running for about half a shichen this way, Ling Xiaobai suddenly stopped in front of a small mountain stream.

"Wuu!"

Just as Chu Ning hesitated, Ling Xiaobai suddenly walked up along the stream.

Soon, the two arrived at the end of the mountain stream.

A not particularly large waterfall fell down, forming a pool of water at this spot.

The pool wasn't very deep, looking about one person's height.

Just as Chu Ning was puzzled by Ling Xiaobai's sudden stop,

"Wuu!"

Ling Xiaobai turned its head and called out to Chu Ning, directly jumping onto his shoulder, then nodding its head.

"You want me to fly over?"

Listening to Ling Xiaobai, Chu Ning carefully looked over.

Only then did he realize that having been attracted by the waterfall earlier, he hadn't noticed that about three zhang above the pool,

There was actually a stone crevice.

Having come this far, Chu Ning naturally had no hesitation.

Taking out his Zhuifeng Shuttle from his storage pouch, Chu Ning took to the air, flying over towards that stone crevice.

Upon reaching the stone crevice, Ling Xiaobai leapt down from Chu Ning's body, then walked along the crevice inwards.

Chu Ning also put away the Zhuifeng Shuttle and followed.

After advancing about ten zhang, a small cave entrance appeared before Chu Ning.

The two squeezed through this cave entrance, walking another five zhang, before it suddenly opened up.

A cave about ten zhang in size appeared before Chu Ning's eyes.

The cave wasn't sealed, with wind blowing in through the crevices, and light shining through as well.

This allowed Chu Ning to see the full view of the entire cave.

There were clustered rocks inside, with a pool about one zhang wide in the center, and some plants growing in the cave as well.

When Chu Ning's gaze swept over these plants, his eyes suddenly lit up.

"Bloodmist Grass!"

Most of the plants here were ordinary flowers and grasses, but Chu Ning discovered there were a few stalks of the spirit plant called Bloodmist Grass.

This Bloodmist Grass was an excellent spirit herb for refining pills.

Its value was even higher than the Golden Yuan Fruit and Iron Orchid Fruit that Chu Ning cultivated.

Only these Bloodmist Grass were still very young seedlings.

"The spirit qi here is so dense, no wonder these spirit plants can grow."

At this time, Chu Ning also had this perception, and couldn't help but mutter to himself. In the next moment, his gaze fell on the small pool in the center.

Because he sensed that the spirit qi was most concentrated at that spot.

And at this point, Ling Xiaobai had also directly walked over in front of the pool.

"Wuu!"

Chu Ning walked over, and the next moment, his face was full of surprise.

This was no small pool, it was clearly a Spirit Spring!

These Spirit Springs usually form within mountain ranges with spirit veins, not entirely congealed from spirit qi.

Rather, they originate as natural springs that absorb spirit qi over a long period to form by chance.

Directly drinking this Spirit Spring water doesn't have a very obvious effect for cultivators.

However, Spirit Springs are often accompanied by the appearance of spirit herbs and grasses, like the Bloodmist Grass.

If used in combination, it has an excellent body tempering effect.

So many body cultivators go to great lengths to seek them out.

This information was something Chu Ning had read about in the jade slip on body cultivation methods gifted by Zhuang Yunde.

What Chu Ning didn't expect was that there would actually be a Spirit Spring in this branch vein.

"Body tempering?"

Chu Ning looked at this Spirit Spring, his eyes flickering.

He was cultivating the Nine Evolution Body Refinement Art, which was naturally a body cultivation method.

However, because the Nine Evolution Body Refinement Art was such an ancient method, it didn't mention external body tempering.

So he had never tried it before.

But Chu Ning also felt that this body tempering might be equally effective for him.

He had even thought before that if the Iron Orchid Fruit really bore fruit, he would use it to try body tempering.

But what Chu Ning didn't expect was to now encounter a Spirit Spring instead.

Just as Chu Ning's heart stirred, his brows furrowed again as he slowly walked towards the few seedlings of pale red spirit grass.

This Spirit Spring looked to have formed quite some time ago, so why were these Bloodmist Grass still so tender?

Bloodmist Grass at this stage couldn't be used in combination with the Spirit Spring.

And when his gaze fell beside the Bloodmist Grass, his expression changed abruptly.

The soil beside these few Bloodmist Grass stalks was dug up, clearly something had been pulled out.

"Someone harvested this Bloodmist Grass, and it was very recently too."

Thinking of this, Chu Ning instinctively thought of the Old Man Sun from before, and those pursuing him.
