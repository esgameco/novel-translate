Chapter 43

Here is a full translation of the excerpt from Chinese to English:

As the spiritual fruit entered his mouth, Chu Ning immediately felt his mouth watering.

A rich spiritual energy enveloped his entire oral cavity.

As he slowly chewed, the fruit juice entered his stomach.

Suddenly, a surge of hot energy gushed up from his dantian, flowing towards his limbs and entire body.

The next moment, this surge of heat abruptly became extremely intense.

Chu Ning only felt his body becoming scorching hot, with his skin, muscles and bones all feeling a burning sensation, as if his blood was about to ignite.

In just an instant, Chu Ning felt his consciousness becoming somewhat blurred, as though he could be overwhelmed by the tidal wave of heat at any moment.

Chu Ning's expression changed slightly.

"The effect of this Seven-Star Spiritual Fruit on the body is so powerful!"

Feeling the surging energy within his body that seemed about to burst out, he dared not let this unrestrained energy run rampant in his body.

He immediately began practicing the Nine Revolutions Body Refining Technique in the relatively spacious backyard.

Although he had cultivated it countless times before, Chu Ning had never felt it as difficult as this time.

The searing heat made every inch of his body feel like it was being torn apart.

It was as if with every movement, his entire being would split open.

When Chu Ning finished one set of the Nine Revolutions Body Refining Technique, his body was already drenched in sweat.

Yet at this moment, the surging heat within his body was still extremely ferocious.

However, what made Chu Ning slightly gratified was that some of that heat had been expended.

Continue!

Chu Ning unhesitatingly continued practicing.

Set after set.

As time passed, Chu Ning felt the burning sensation gradually subsiding.

And his body became increasingly unobstructed.

He did not know how many sets he had practiced.

"Boom!"

Until at a certain moment, Chu Ning suddenly felt some kind of shackle within his body being broken open.

The burning sensation completely disappeared, and his body became exceptionally light and transparent.

"Phew!"

Chu Ning exhaled deeply, finally stopping his cultivation, his gaze also becoming exceptionally bright.

It could be seen that with a casual clench of his fist, his entire skin took on an ancient bronze hue.

When he lightly flicked it with one finger, it even seemed to produce a faint ringing sound.

And when he relaxed his hand, all his skin returned to normal.

Chu Ning could feel that his body still contained the medicinal effects of the Seven-Star Spiritual Fruit.

However, it would no longer affect his normal cultivation.

"When this Seven-Star Spiritual Fruit was auctioned off, they said it would be effective for Foundation Establishment cultivators refining their bodies, and it seems that was true.

It's a good thing I realized quickly, and that I was practicing the ancient Nine Revolutions Body Refining Technique, otherwise my body might not have been able to withstand the potency of the Seven-Star Spiritual Fruit."

Recalling this, Chu Ning felt a bit of lingering fear.

He had been somewhat reckless at the time.

However, the result was undoubtedly also something that made Chu Ning extremely gratified.

His consciousness swiftly descended into his sea of consciousness, and his cultivation progress immediately appeared before him...

The rotten skin realm was perfected, and what was refreshed was the innate divine ability of the Heavenly Pillars Fist.

According to the ancient texts, this was originally an innate divine ability that came with the Nine Revolutions Body Refining Technique.

"So the innate abilities I refreshed and the cultivation content I practiced are closely related."

Chu Ning vaguely felt that if he continued cultivating the qi refining method, what might be refreshed could be cultivation innate abilities similar to the spiritual body.

As for body refining methods, what would likely be refreshed were innate divine abilities like the Heavenly Pillars Fist.

Immediately after, Chu Ning checked the complete information about the Heavenly Pillars Fist in his mind.

This Heavenly Pillars Fist was a close-combat divine secret technique specifically for body cultivators. It could utilize one's own yuan qi to drive the spiritual forces of heaven and earth, unleashing powerful and fierce attacks.

It could break through ordinary spells with defensive power below the late qi refining stage with just one punch!

If an ordinary qi refining cultivator tried to block it with their body, they would almost certainly die.

Although he had never come into contact with this divine secret technique before, at this moment,

Chu Ning felt that it was like the catalyzing spell, the Green Wood Spring Blossom Art, and other spells he could already cast instantly.

It had completely transformed into his own innate divine ability!

It seemed that with just a thought, he could unleash a powerful and fierce punch at any time.

"I finally have another skill for self-protection!"

Thinking of this, Chu Ning felt slightly more at ease about the impending danger.

In the next half day, Chu Ning practiced the Heavenly Pillars Fist twice in the backyard.

This Heavenly Pillars Fist consumed a tremendous amount of spiritual power. Chu Ning promptly took a Recovery Pill to restore his spiritual power, keeping it at an abundant level.

Then, he reviewed all the spells and talisman arts he knew.

He was prepared for any possible danger that might arise.

Chu Ning had also considered whether he should go hide in the outer sect, or directly ask Zhang Yunde to come to his place.

But this thought only flashed briefly before Chu Ning dismissed it.

As the saying goes, you can avoid the first day, but not the fifteenth.

If the previous feeling of being watched came from Qi Chongmao, it also meant that he would naturally know if there was any movement in Chu Ning's courtyard.

If Zhang Yunde was here, the other party definitely would not show up.

And if Chu Ning went to the outer sect, he would have to return at night according to the rules.

Even if he handed over the Seven-Star Spirit Fruit to the sect, it didn't necessarily mean that Qi Chongmao would ignore him.

Unless he was just being overly paranoid, the confrontation between the two of them seemed unavoidable.

As night fell, although the Seven-Star Spirit Fruit had been picked, Chu Ning still stayed in the backyard to cultivate.

Because there were many iron vine ropes here.

Soon, that familiar and extremely uncomfortable feeling of being watched appeared again.

However, this feeling only appeared for an instant before disappearing immediately, leaving Chu Ning slightly puzzled.

Just as Chu Ning was puzzled, his expression changed slightly.

Because at that moment, the sound of someone riding the wind and landing came from the front courtyard.

Qi Chongmao had arrived!

The well-prepared Chu Ning immediately extended his hands, grasping a few talisman arts in each hand.

The spiritual power in his dantian surged, ready to act at any moment.

At the same time, a figure appeared in the backyard under the moonlight.

Thorny Entanglement Art!

Chu Ning was about to unleash this spell that could bind and restrain his opponent.

But then, he suddenly felt a "weng" sound by his ear and a stinging pain in his head.

"Ah!"

The sudden intense pain caused Chu Ning to let out a low groan of agony.

"Kid, you've already picked the matured Seven-Star Spirit Fruit, haven't you?

I never would have imagined that you would actually get lucky enough to obtain such a spirit fruit."

At the same time, Qi Chongmao's voice, which normally sounded kind, now sounded extremely sinister.

"For us in the Qingxi Sect, cultivating and planting this Seven-Star Spirit Fruit is not difficult, nor is preserving it.

But this fruit is very special. Not only is its maturation period extremely short, just a few months, but it will also automatically rot and leave only the seed if it touches the soil.

If the seed is not taken out and put into a jade box within a day, even the seed will rot.

So unless there's a fortuitous opportunity, it's extremely difficult to obtain these seeds."

In Qi Chongmao's sinister voice was a hint of excitement and greed.

"But your good luck has run out, kid. This Seven-Star Spirit Fruit is all mine."

Accompanying this voice, Chu Ning saw several ice blades flying towards him!
