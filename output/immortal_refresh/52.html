Chapter 52

After entering the outer gate, Chu Ning went familiarly towards the residence of Zhuang Yunde.

Unexpectedly, halfway there, he saw Mu Ling.

"Junior Martial Brother Chu, I heard that you've recently come to hand in your contributions."

Mu Ling slightly tilted her head, smiling at Chu Ning with a knowing look.

Chu Ning was naturally no longer bewildered and wary like before, and now directly took out talisman papers from his storage bag.

"I was just about to go to Senior Martial Brother Zhuang's place and then find you, Senior Martial Sister. These are the talisman papers I've contributed to the sect this season. I don't know if you need this many, Senior Martial Sister."

Mu Ling's eyes immediately lit up, "How many are there?"

"3,000 sheets," Chu Ning answered.

"So many, then I don't have to worry about wasting them..." Mu Ling said with a slight excitement, but immediately restrained herself.

Her expression became a bit more restrained, and then she reached out to brush her hair back.

"With this, I can cultivate talisman-making properly."

The two quickly made the transaction. Chu Ning gave her 3,000 talisman papers, and in exchange received 60 spirit stones.

As for things like giving a discount for buying in bulk, Chu Ning hadn't even considered it.

Who else would he earn this money for?

Seeing Mu Ling about to leave with the talisman papers, Chu Ning hurriedly spoke up:

"Senior Martial Sister Mu, I've become quite familiar with buying talisman papers and dealing with various merchants in the market recently.

I should be able to get more goods. Would you like to buy some more, Senior Martial Sister?"

"Buy! Buy!" Mu Ling immediately exclaimed upon hearing this.

"Buy as many as you can get."

After exclaiming, Mu Ling thought for a moment and said, "I have 3,000 sheets here, which should last me a month. Next month, you can buy some more then."

Chu Ning nodded and said to her, "Then I'll help you buy 1,500 sheets each month, Senior Martial Sister."

He currently had 7,900 talisman papers left in his storage bag.

Selling 1,500 sheets to her each month, for 4 months, would be 6,000 sheets.

He would still have 1,900 sheets left for himself.

Chu Ning planned to keep these talisman papers for his own use.

After all, he was preparing to participate in the advancement assessment.

If the information Mu Ling had revealed before was true, and he had a chance to obtain the talisman-making method after advancing, then these talisman papers could be used for him to practice talisman-making himself. According to Chu Ning's thoughts, it should be enough.

After all, he couldn't be as wasteful as Mu Ling.

Mu Ling nodded at this time, and took out 10 spirit stones from her storage bag to hand to Chu Ning.

Adding this to the amount she had planned to give Chu Ning each month, it came to 32 spirit stones.

"Then I'll buy an extra 500 sheets each month."

After saying this, she took out another 3 spirit stones and handed them to Chu Ning together with the others.

"You've worked hard this period of time."

Chu Ning accepted these spirit stones, and couldn't help but inwardly click his tongue as he watched Mu Ling's light-footed departure.

What a wasteful woman.

However, the feeling of earning spirit stones was truly great.

These 3 spirit stones, plus the errand fee for this month, amounted to a full 5 spirit stones.

To put it into perspective, this was already more than what Qiu Shunyi and the others could earn from an entire season's harvest after contributing to the sect.

But then he thought, with her having a Golden Dan stage great-aunt, this amount of spirit stones really wasn't much.

Chu Ning felt justified in accepting it.

He put all the spirit stones into his storage bag, and arrived at Zhuang Yunde's courtyard.

Seeing Chu Ning, Zhuang Yunde had his usual smile on his face.

"Oh, Chu Ning is here. I was just about to go over to your place this afternoon."

"Senior Martial Brother Zhuang, I've come to hand in my contributions."

Chu Ning said, taking out 3,500 talisman papers from his storage bag, as well as an additional 11...

"A chunk of spirit stone."

Zhuang Yunde immediately asked with a smile, "You met Mu Ling again?"

Chu Ning nodded, "Yeah, I sold some extra talisman papers to Senior Sister Mu."

Zhuang Yunde didn't say much more, skillfully putting the items into two separate storage bags.

At this time, Chu Ning straightforwardly stated his purpose for coming.

"Senior Brother Zhuang, I don't know if I need to register with you to participate in the assessment for promotion to the C-district?"

"You want to take the promotion assessment?" Zhuang Yunde was slightly surprised at first.

But after glancing at Chu Ning, there was an added look of surprise on his face.

"Oh, you've already reached the fourth level of Qi refining, so you do indeed meet the conditions for promotion."

Zhuang Yunde smiled and looked at Chu Ning.

"Junior Brother entered the sect one and a half years ago and has already reached the fourth level of Qi refining. This cultivation speed is not slow at all."

Chu Ning cupped his hands towards the other party.

"It's thanks to Senior Brother's guidance that I earned some spirit stones, which were basically all spent on improving my cultivation. I officially entered the middle stage of Qi refining last month."

It was only then that Zhuang Yunde slightly nodded and said:

"You are inherently very compatible with the Qingmu Changchun Skill. Plus, with the assistance of spirit stones for cultivation, it's natural that you progressed faster."

Then, he changed the subject:

"However, I cannot make the decision regarding this promotion assessment. You need to go register with Executive Officer He. Only with his approval can you participate."

"Executive Officer He Changyu?" Chu Ning asked to confirm.

Zhuang Yunde nodded again, "I didn't expect that you would be able to reach the fourth level of Qi refining in just one and a half years after joining the sect.

Plus, your grasp of spells is not bad either. Executive Officer He should agree.

However, the competition is also very fierce, so you need to be mentally prepared."

Chu Ning's expression turned serious as he said, "So in the next half month or so, I plan to use spirit stones to further improve my cultivation."

"Whether I can be promoted or not, I hope Senior Brother Zhuang can continue to provide guidance in the future." Chu Ning said this very sincerely.

To be honest, although he had a golden finger, his current cultivation speed and income were indeed closely related to Zhuang Yunde.

Although the two of them were essentially in a transactional, mutually beneficial relationship, he still needed to acknowledge this.

Upon hearing Chu Ning's words, Zhuang Yunde's round face showed a few more smiles. However, his expression soon turned somewhat sentimental as he said:

"I really wish I could provide more guidance to you newer junior brothers, but I'm afraid I won't have the opportunity. I'll be leaving soon too."

Hearing Zhuang Yunde's words, Chu Ning was slightly stunned and asked in surprise:

"Senior Brother is leaving?"

Zhuang Yunde nodded, hesitated for a moment, but ultimately spoke:

"Junior Brother, you've been in the sect for about one and a half years.

You should have heard that every one or two years, the sect organizes a group of Qi refining disciples to go out and carry out missions."

Chu Ning suddenly remembered what Shang Zhaoqiang had said before and blurted out:

"Is Senior Brother referring to the rumor that the sect discovered a treasure land elsewhere and needs to open it up, so many servant disciples were arranged to go there?

But I heard that it's not the servant disciples being sent. Could it be that Senior Brother is going there to oversee things?"

Zhuang Yunde nodded and then shook his head.

"I am indeed participating in this matter, but I'm not going there to oversee things.

And what you heard outside is not entirely accurate. From the very beginning, it was the inner sect disciples and outer sect disciples who were arranged to go.

It was only later, when there was a shortage of manpower, that the servant disciples were allowed to participate."

"Shortage of manpower?" Chu Ning murmured these words, then raised the doubt that had always lingered in his mind.

"Senior Brother Zhuang, the sect is arranging so many Qi refining disciples to go. Is it really for opening up a new territory?

I've never heard of any senior brothers returning."

Upon hearing Chu Ning's question, Zhuang Yunde's expression became slightly more complicated.

Seeing his expression, Chu Ning vaguely felt that he seemed to have asked about something secret.

(End of chapter)
