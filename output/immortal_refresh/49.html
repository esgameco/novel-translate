Here is a full translation of the excerpt from Chinese to English:

On the way back to the courtyard from the outer gate, Chu Ning was pondering the information he had heard from Mu Ling.

According to Mu Ling's account, she heard from an elder of the sect that Qingxi Sect would increase its efforts in training menial disciples.

Originally, basic skills like talisman making, pill refining, and weapon forging were only taught to inner and outer disciples.

However, after this season ends, menial disciples would have the opportunity to learn some basic refining techniques.

However, Mu Ling also mentioned that even for menial disciples, there would be limitations, as there were so many menial disciples in Qingxi Sect.

For example, for the menial disciples in the Spiritual Herbs Chamber, since they cultivated wood-attribute martial arts, they would naturally be more suited for talisman making.

But the menial disciples in Area D basically had no chance to compete, while those in Area C could have opportunities.

"It seems I still need to find a way to be promoted to an Area C disciple," Chu Ning carefully calculated in his mind.

In fact, Chu Ning met all the hard requirements for promotion to an Area C disciple, such as reaching the middle stage of Qi Refining and being able to instantly cast several cultivation techniques related to spiritual farming.

However, meeting these requirements did not guarantee promotion to an Area C disciple.

Qingxi Sect held a promotion exam once a year, and the number of promotion spots was very limited.

Only by outperforming others in the promotion assessment could one obtain a spot.

As for the timing of the promotion assessment, Chu Ning knew it would be in three months.

Three months after Chu Ning joined the sect, he completed the spiritual field assignment left behind by disciples who had just been promoted.

"I originally wanted to keep a low profile, but now it seems I need to properly compete for what should be competed for."

After entering his own courtyard, Chu Ning's expression became more serious.

This time, he would not be competing with new disciples like Yuan Guang.

Instead, he would be competing with all the menial disciples in Area D, including many senior martial brothers who had joined the sect years ago.

As a newly joined disciple, Chu Ning's participation in the promotion assessment would undoubtedly draw attention from others.

But at this moment, Chu Ning could not be bothered by such concerns.

For a menial disciple to break through to the fourth level of Qi Refining within a year or so was not earth-shattering.

As for his ability to cast martial arts techniques, he would use the excuse he had given before.

After truly entering Area C and lying low for some time, people would naturally forget about him.

And such an opportunity, if missed, would not come again.

Having made up his mind, Chu Ning barely hesitated before deciding to participate in the promotion assessment in three months.

Over the next few days, Chu Ning spent his time farming and cultivating, with nothing particularly special happening.

One evening, as Chu Ning was returning from the fields, he unexpectedly noticed that the courtyard gate of his neighbor Qi Chongmao was open.

Chu Ning's heart skipped a beat.

"Could it be that someone has come to investigate Qi Chongmao's disappearance again?"

Zhuang Yunde had said that the sect would not thoroughly investigate the disappearance of a menial disciple.

The Law Enforcement Hall had already investigated ten days ago, so why would someone else be investigating now?

With these thoughts in mind, Chu Ning unconsciously slowed his pace.

At that moment, he vaguely heard the sound of conversation coming from the courtyard, and it seemed like someone was about to leave.

Without further hesitation, Chu Ning maintained a calm demeanor, pushed open the gate, and prepared to enter his own courtyard.

"Please wait, Junior Martial Brother!" a voice suddenly called out from behind him as he had just stepped into the courtyard, causing him to stop.

Turning around, Chu Ning saw a man and a woman walking out of Qi Chongmao's courtyard next door.

The middle-aged man was of average build, around forty years old, with thick eyebrows and big eyes, looking a bit simple-minded.

The woman appeared slightly younger in age. Although not stunningly beautiful, she had a graceful demeanor.

Chu Ning's initial perception was that the fluctuations of spiritual power from the two of them were even stronger than his own, having reached the sixth level of Qi refinement.

"This junior brother, my wife and I have just been arranged by the sect to live in this courtyard, so we'll be neighbors from now on," said the middle-aged man. Hearing this, Chu Ning was slightly surprised.

He did not expect that within just a month, the sect had directly arranged for people to move into Qi Chongmao's courtyard, and it was even a married couple.

Chu Ning had occasionally seen some female disciples in Area D, but this was the first time he had seen a married couple.

Just as he was about to speak, the woman had already opened her mouth first:

"You haven't even introduced yourself, how would others know who you are?"

The middle-aged man smiled sheepishly, and after the woman finished speaking, she turned her head and smiled at Chu Ning.

"Junior brother, my name is Lu Yunfang, and this is my husband Lu Hongping. We just came back from outside and were arranged to live in this courtyard."

Upon hearing their introductions, Chu Ning cupped his hands in salutation, "Chu Ning greets Senior Brother and Senior Sister."

Then, with some surprise, he asked, "Just now, Senior Sister mentioned that you two came back from outside?"

"Yes, we usually stay at a cultivating family clan..."

As Lu Yunfang explained, Chu Ning gradually understood the situation.

It turned out that the two of them had previously been stationed at a spiritual field belonging to a cultivating family clan under the sect's jurisdiction.

That family clan had many spiritual fields, but previously they had suffered a decline in population, with few cultivators possessing spiritual roots, so gradually these spiritual fields could not be properly tended.

Therefore, the couple Lu Hongping had been arranged by the sect to be responsible for the fields outside.

In recent years, that family clan had slowly produced a few more cultivators, and the couple had been wanting to return to the sect for a long time, but never had a suitable opportunity.

This time, with Qi Chongmao's disappearance and no one tending to the spiritual field, they were called back.

Lu Yunfang then smiled and continued, "There's nothing else, just wanted to greet you."

After she finished speaking, Chu Ning did not say much in response, timely displaying an unsociable manner, and politely saw the couple off.

Although this married couple seemed very warm-hearted, given the previous example of Qi Chongmao, Chu Ning still felt a sense of vigilance in his heart. After all, not everyone who appeared kind-hearted was necessarily harmless.

He lit a fire to cook, boiling some spiritual rice, and Chu Ning ate a big bowl of it.

Feeling a slight inflow of spiritual power circulating within his body along with the fragrant taste, he began his cultivation.

As usual, Chu Ning first cultivated the Nine Revolutions Body Refining Technique.

Then he cultivated the Green Wood Evergreen Skill for a while, and finally, he cultivated the Spirit Refining Technique.

At this moment, Chu Ning's mud pill palace was still swirling in a mist-like state, without any condensation, meaning he was still unable to utilize his spiritual sense for perception.

Chu Ning did not dwell on it, only continuing to cultivate according to the incantations of the Spirit Refining Technique.

After completing all his cultivation for the day, it was already late at night. Chu Ning returned to his room and went to sleep.

Thinking for a moment, he took out a few items from his storage bag and placed them beside his pillow: an attack talisman, a defensive talisman, and his black wooden sword of unknown material.

These few items were placed within his immediate reach.

Ever since the incident with Qi Chongmao, even within the sect, Chu Ning had become increasingly cautious.

Especially today, with two new people moving into the neighboring courtyard.
