At first, upon hearing this coquettish voice, Chu Ning did not realize it was calling him.

Until the other party spoke again.

"Excuse me, are you Junior Brother Chu Ning from the Spirit Plant House? Could you stop for a moment please."

Chu Ning raised his head in astonishment.

He saw a petite and exquisite female disciple, wearing a blue sect robe, rapidly walking towards him from not far away on the right.

Although small in stature, her chest was full and ample in size.

The female disciple came up to Chu Ning, slightly tilted her head up and smiled, asking:

"Are you Junior Brother Chu Ning from the Spirit Plant House?"

Chu Ning found it strange how she recognized him, so he just nodded.

"I'm Mu Ling, also a disciple of the Hundred Arts Hall. Senior Brother Zhuang Yunde asked me to wait for you here."

Upon hearing the petite girl's words, Chu Ning finally realized and cupped his fist, slightly bowing his head to say:

"Greetings, Senior Sister Mu."

With this bow, he happened to see Mu Ling's bright shining eyes staring at him intently. Chu Ning couldn't help but silently wonder to himself:

Why does this senior sister seem so interested in me?

But with just my third level of Qi refinement, there's no reason for her interest.

After all, Chu Ning's sense of Qi could not discern her depth, which was clearly above the sixth level of Qi refinement.

As he pondered this, Mu Ling had already swiftly opened her mouth to say:

"Junior Brother Chu, did you come to submit talisman papers as the harvest from the Ink Bamboo?"

Chu Ning nodded again, now believing that Zhuang Yunde had indeed informed her about him.

"Then it's perfect, just hand the talisman papers to me. Senior Brother Zhuang is out right now, it's the same giving them to me."

As she spoke, Mu Ling stared at the cloth bag Chu Ning was holding, stretching out her little hand with an eager expression.

Upon hearing this, Chu Ning immediately became wary, his face showing difficulty as he said:

"This...Senior Sister, Senior Brother Zhuang specifically instructed that these talisman papers must be handed to him personally to offset against the harvest owed to the Hall."

"It's still offsetting the harvest if you give them to me," Mu Ling glared her pretty brows.

"You can't possibly think I would take advantage of a newly joined junior brother like you."

That's not necessarily true.

Chu Ning thought to himself, feeling that something seemed amiss about this.

However, he was absolutely unwilling to just hand over the papers to Mu Ling.

Leaving aside whether Mu Ling was really from the Hundred Arts Hall or not, which he wasn't clear about.

Even if she was, what if after getting the papers from him, she didn't submit them to the Hall, or submitted less than the full amount?

"Senior Sister would certainly not bully a newly joined junior brother like me. It's just that I've not long joined the sect, so I'm really not familiar with these rules.

I'll come back another day when Senior Brother Zhuang is here instead, and not trouble you, Senior Sister."

Hearing Chu Ning's words, Mu Ling's face immediately showed a pouty, inflated expression that admittedly looked quite cute.

Chu Ning naturally saw it too, but thinking about her designs on his talisman papers, he didn't find it cute at all.

At this moment, however, Mu Ling swiftly dropped the pouty look and instead showed a sweet smile.

"Junior Brother, how about this? I won't take the talisman papers you need to submit to offset the sect harvest. But you must have spare papers too, right?"

Just as Chu Ning was about to open his mouth, Mu Ling's black, bright eyes stared straight at him.

"Don't say you don't have any, or else I'll take them by force."

Chu Ning's expression immediately changed slightly, and his hand gripping the cloth bag tightened somewhat.

Mu Ling's cultivation level was clearly higher than his.

Moreover, she brazenly stated she would rob him, showing every intention of taking action.

Seeing that her threat was effective, a hint of smugness appeared on Muling's face as she continued:

"Don't worry, I won't ask for it for free. I'll pay you spirit stones. If you turn in the talisman papers to the sect for recycling, you'd probably only get one low-grade spirit stone for every 55 talisman papers. But I'll give you one low-grade spirit stone for just 50 talisman papers."

Hearing Muling say she would buy them with spirit stones, Chuning finally breathed a slight sigh of relief, but asked with some confusion:

"Senior Sister, buying one spirit stone for 50 talisman papers is the same price whether you buy from the sect or the market..."

"Don't worry about that, I just find it too troublesome," Muling said impatiently. "Are you selling or not?"

Chuning sighed inwardly. After you've said so much, how could I not sell?

If I don't sell, wouldn't you just take them by force?

So Chuning asked, "How many talisman papers does Senior Sister want to buy?"

"However many you have, I'll take them all!" Muling boastfully shook her storage bag.

"I have plenty of spirit stones here."

Chuning looked at the spirit stones, then picked up the smaller of the two cloth bags he was holding, preparing to open it.

He had indeed brought some extra talisman papers this time, originally planning to ask Zhuang Yunde if the sect would buy them back.

Of course, he couldn't claim he hadn't made any extras, but Chuning also didn't want to reveal too much.

This small cloth bag contained the 2,050 talisman papers intended for outside the sect. He planned to claim these were all the talisman papers he had.

At this moment, he prepared to separate out Zhuang Yunde's 550 papers and give the rest to Muling.

But before he could move, as soon as he opened the bag, Muling made a hand seal and instantly swept all the talisman papers into her storage bag.

Chuning's expression changed slightly, but then Muling took out a talisman paper, examined it carefully, and said with satisfaction:

"The quality of these talisman papers is not bad. I'll take them first in case Junior Brother changes his mind."

Pausing, Muling seemed to sense the contents of her storage bag with her spiritual power, then continued:

"There are 2,050 talisman papers in total, so 41 low-grade spirit stones."

She took out a small pile of spirit stones and handed them to Chuning.

Chuning hurriedly accepted them and put them in his pocket.

Seeing Chuning put the spirit stones directly in his pocket, Muling asked in surprise:

"Oh, doesn't Junior Brother have a storage bag?"

Chuning shook his head. "This is my first season's harvest. I don't have enough spirit stones to buy a storage bag."

Muling blinked, then quickly took out a small bag from her pocket and said to Chuning:

"Junior Brother Chu, I have an extra storage bag here. If Junior Brother is willing to do me a small favor, I can give this storage bag to you. How about it?"

Chuning didn't respond. He felt there was something not quite right about this windfall.

But Muling didn't press him, just smiling:

"Junior Brother Chu doesn't need to agree right away. You should go see Senior Brother Zhuang first. I just saw him in his room."

With that, Muling turned and left, leaving a bewildered Chuning behind.

After thinking it over, Chuning still headed towards Zhuang Yunde's residence.

Originally he had planned to go back to his lodgings first and get the 550 talisman papers for Zhuang Yunde before coming.

But hearing Zhuang Yunde was actually home, he decided to go find him first.

Although this forced sale by Muling wasn't a loss for him, her strange appearance left him feeling uneasy until he could clarify things with Zhuang Yunde.
