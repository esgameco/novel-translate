"To think that my first attempt at talisman making had a success rate of over 40%."

Chu Ning looked at the small stack of talisman papers before him, his face a mix of surprise and delight.

Within this period of time, Chu Ning had attempted to draw 40 talisman papers, and an astonishing 18 of them were successful.

He had not neglected to understand the relevant knowledge about talisman making during this period.

Normally, for a talisman maker to achieve a success rate of around 40% in producing talismans is already considered qualified.

A 50% success rate in talisman making is considered an excellent standard for a talisman maker.

"This innate talisman body constitution is indeed formidable!"

At this moment, Chu Ning could not help but exclaim in admiration.

Moreover, Chu Ning could clearly feel that as he continued attempting, his success rate in talisman making was still increasing.

This was because his success rate towards the end was clearly higher than at the beginning.

If it were not for his spiritual sense being unable to sustain itself any longer, he would have wanted to continue trying.

"The real drain in talisman making is not on spiritual power, but on spiritual sense."

At this moment, Chu Ning silently thought to himself.

He actually did not realize this initially.

But ever since cultivating this Spirit Refining Art, he became extremely sensitive to his spiritual sense.

After attempting talisman making around five times, Chu Ning felt changes in his spiritual sense.

However, thinking about it again, Chu Ning felt this was normal.

After all, the spiritual power consumed by infusing into the spirit pen was not much.

But focusing wholeheartedly on doing one thing undoubtedly consumed a lot of spiritual sense.

As for most disciples at the Qi Refining stage,

Because they had not cultivated the secret methods of spiritual sense, they could not even clearly sense changes in their spiritual sense.

Thinking of this, a flash of inspiration suddenly struck Chu Ning's mind.

Just now, he had not paid attention, but this spiritual sense was actually being consumed unconsciously.

If he adopted the methods of spiritual sense control from the Spirit Refining Art, concentrating his spiritual sense in the talisman making process,

Would his consumption of spiritual sense possibly be reduced?

The more Chu Ning thought about it, the more he felt this approach was worth trying.

However, feeling the fatigue in his mind, Chu Ning had to temporarily suppress this idea for now.

Chu Ning began cultivating the Spirit Refining Art again to recover his spiritual sense.

This time, the speed of recovering his spiritual sense was still not fast.

But what surprised Chu Ning was that although it took longer, the effect was good.

His proficiency in the Spirit Refining Art also increased by 1 point.

"So consuming spiritual sense, then cultivating the Spirit Refining Art, can strengthen spiritual sense?"

Chu Ning vaguely realized this possibility, which made him secretly delighted.

Because this meant that there was now a way to accelerate the cultivation of the Spirit Refining Art.

As for whether this was true or not, it needed to be verified later on.

By now, it had reached noon.

Following his usual schedule, he first cooked and ate spirit rice.

After resting for a while, he returned to the table in his room, preparing to continue practicing the art of talisman making.

At this moment, Chu Ning was full of energy, his spiritual sense having recovered to its peak state.

Pondering the idea of using spiritual sense for talisman making from the morning, as he grasped the spirit pen and prepared to make talismans,

He released his spiritual sense, consciously focusing it on the talisman paper and spirit pen.

As the spirit pen descended, instantly, a profound feeling surged into Chu Ning's heart.

That feeling was as if every line on the talisman paper, every drop of talisman ink on the spirit pen was completely under his control.

Almost without any obstruction, Chu Ning completed the making of a Wood Talisman in one go.

Chu Ning did not stop, immediately starting on the next talisman.

Five, ten, twenty...

Sixty!

This time, Chu Ning used a full sixty talisman papers before feeling some fatigue from his spiritual sense being consumed.

Although his expression showed some tiredness, Chu Ning's smile was even brighter.

"By controlling the range of his spiritual sense dispersion, he indeed consumed one-third less spiritual sense!"

When Chu Ning briefly checked the talismans he had successfully made, his mouth slightly opened wide.

32 talismans!

Chu Ning used 60 talisman papers, yet he actually made 32 wood-element talismans, with a success rate of over 50%!

Moreover, Chu Ning could even clearly feel that the quality of these 32 wood-element talismans was higher than the 18 he made in the morning!

This was no longer talisman making, he was simply printing talismans!

Chu Ning felt a little excited in his heart. Each of these talismans could sell for 2 low-grade spirit stones.

In other words, just from today's work, the talismans he made could sell for nearly a hundred spirit stones.

As for the slight loss of talisman paper and ink, in Chu Ning's view, it didn't matter at all.

At this moment, Chu Ning even had an unreal feeling in his heart.

Is talisman making really so difficult?

Could it be that Mu Ling's rapid destruction of talisman papers was intentional?

...

Outside, Mu Ling, who was making talismans with a brush, was feeling vexed looking at the ashes in front of her.

"Achoo!"

After sneezing, Mu Ling muttered doubtfully to herself,

"Who is talking about me?"

However, she soon picked up the obviously exquisite talisman brush again and lightly bit her lip.

"I don't believe that despite drawing these talismans every day recently, my success rate is still not even 40%. Let's go again!"

...

Over the next few days,

Chu Ning holed up in his room every day, repeating the process of cultivation and talisman making.

Of course, he also understood the importance of alternating labor and rest.

So he did not solely cultivate the Spirit Cultivation Technique, but also practiced the Green Wood Evergreen Skill and the Nine Revolutions Body Refining Technique without neglecting them.

In order to prevent the two next door from detecting anything,

Chu Ning cultivated the Green Wood Evergreen Skill and the Spirit Cultivation Technique in the courtyard.

After all, these two only required sitting meditation to cultivate.

As for cultivating the Nine Revolutions Body Refining Technique, Chu Ning went to the spirit field.

The only thing that left Chu Ning feeling a bit puzzled was that

the speed of his Spirit Cultivation Technique varied, sometimes fast and sometimes slow.

It was not always as fast as when he first released his spiritual sense.

Sometimes it was the same as before releasing his spiritual sense.

Sometimes it was even faster than his recent spiritual sense cultivation speed.

No matter how much Chu Ning pondered it, he could not figure it out, so he stopped thinking about it.

After all, over these past few days, as he continuously used and cultivated his spiritual sense,

the progress of this Spirit Cultivation Technique had sped up considerably compared to before.

Five days, and he had gained 8 proficiency points.

Of course, it was not only Chu Ning's Spirit Cultivation Technique that had improved, but also his talisman making success rate.

"Seventy percent!

If outsiders knew about this success rate, they would probably be so shocked their eyes would pop out."

Chu Ning could not help but inwardly sigh as he walked out of the courtyard.

Although over the past few days, his talisman making success rate did not increase as rapidly as that first day,

it still rose a little every day.

And after five days, Chu Ning, who had never dealt with wood-element talismans before,

could now stably achieve a 70% success rate in making them.

Because this talisman making success rate was too heaven-defying, for today's talisman assessment, Chu Ning did not plan to go all out.

Of course, he did not plan to underperform either.

Although he had a false spiritual root, having such an affinity for wood-element skills and spells meant he had a bit of talent in talisman making, which was not unreasonable.

As for controlling this success rate,

for Chu Ning now, it was undoubtedly an easy task.
