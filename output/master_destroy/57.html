Matters of the world must be handled with attention to details.

One must make a living from life itself.

Reality will not give you any special treatment just because of your lofty ambitions.

This is the real world.

It's just like working with concrete in civil engineering projects in a previous life.

You may claim to have been born with superior talents, aiming for the highest goals, but once on the construction site, under the supervision of the tough foreman, you still have to do the grunt work of mixing concrete if that's what you're assigned.

Many cannot accept such a gap between expectations and reality.

But Yi Chen didn't mind at all.

Making money is not something to be looked down upon. Face is worth so little - Yi Chen only cared about real benefits.

He was a man who had fought dogs for food ten years ago, though he never actually did fight them.

Setting up the spirit altar, burning paper money offerings.

Full one-stop funeral services.

That's professionalism.

And if the Wang family wants something more presentable, with some more money, he could even make custom paper figurines of people, horses, and mansions - an entire underground Forbidden City, a paradise for the afterlife, worth cherishing for a lifetime.

More money! More money! More money!

The level of service will match the price you pay at the Celestial Peak.

This is the level of professionalism approved even by the Golden Finger at the third level of presiding over funerals.

You should know, when the Golden Finger first awakened, Master Yi's minor Yang skill was only at the second level...

What? You say concrete work doesn't pay much? Low pay, lots of work, working far from home? Easy to get harassed by foreign troublemakers?

Oh, no problem then.

My apologies, I misspoke.

However, even as such a professional Master Yi has decided to further elevate his 'Yin-Yang Spirit Eye Technique' before going out tomorrow.

He has now accumulated 81 deep red points. Although he gained some insights from the recent battle, he still lacks 419 points to reach the 500 needed to break through to the next level of his pure Yang inner skill.

It would take forever to save up that many.

Rather than hoarding them, he might as well spend 40 deep red points to upgrade the 'Yin-Yang Spirit Eye Technique' one more level, especially since he's about to travel far.

The three attributes he gained from the third level of the 'Yin-Yang Spirit Eye Technique' - eagle eye vision, night vision, and yin energy perception - have all provided him tremendous help during the previous process.

None of them were duds.

This made him look forward even more to what surprises the fourth level of the 'Yin-Yang Spirit Eye Technique' might bring.

Spending a little to accomplish something big - it's the perfect time to upgrade the Yin-Yang Spirit Eye Technique.

"Deep red, breakthrough!"

With this inner cry, Yi Chen spent 40 deep red points in a flash, and unfamiliar memories suddenly surfaced in his mind.

Like watching a movie, Yi Chen browsed through the memories, seeing a young Taoist sitting cross-legged before a Yin-Yang diagram, observing the interplay of Yin and Yang as he practiced breathing exercises.

After a long while, he closed his eyes, and everything fell into stillness. His spiritual sense began to perceive the surrounding environment.

He felt the flow of the wind, the fluttering of mosquito wings, the fragrance of the earth...

Day after day of sitting meditation, night after night of spiritual practice, forty years passed, and his five senses eventually became extraordinarily acute. The once youthful Taoist now had streaks of gray in his hair.

At this point, he still had his eyes closed as he stood up.

He walked out of the courtyard gate, crossed a shallow stream, passed through a narrow path, walked over a wooden bridge. Although his eyes were shut, the surrounding scenery was vividly reflected in his mind.
