He really did want this knife, it wasn't just empty talk.

Not long after grabbing it, a trickle of cool energy entered his body, and his deep red value immediately increased by twenty points, which undoubtedly made him both surprised and delighted.

He put the chopping knife on his waistband, then stretched out his big hand in front of the old man's face and beckoned with his fingers, his expression vicious, looking just like a ruffian.

When the clouds covered the moon earlier, Yi Chen had sensed something amiss and perceived a burst of yin energy not far from the southern district, so he immediately rushed over.

After observing secretly for a second or two, he realized the yin energy from this demonic entity was not as dense as he had imagined, about the same as the old ghost who sold 'melons' in the Underworld.

Seeing this young daoist priest dressed in a robe but looking like a bandit, the old white-haired man finally reacted.

It seemed he had been robbed.

What an outrageous situation.

A surge of anger rushed to the top of his head.

He curled his lips into an eerie sinister smile, about to speak, when suddenly a fierce gust of wind attacked - Yi Chen had taken the initiative to launch a sneak attack.

With great force, he landed a slanting knee strike squarely on the old man's solar plexus.

His whole body was like a spring, directly bouncing up high. In an instant, his figure moved above the old man's body.

His elbow suffused with a light purple pure yang inner energy smashed viciously down on the area below the old man's neck.

The old white-haired man accelerated downwards even faster than his upward motion.

Bang!

A tremendous crash.

The old man's whole body was embedded into the street paved with long bluestone slabs, sinking completely into the ground.

With the point where he fell as the center, cracks radiated outwards.

Billowing dust rose everywhere.

At this moment, Master Lianhua and Daoist Qingxu had just rushed over, witnessing the scene of Yi Chen leaping high to deliver that elbow strike onto the old man.

The two opened their eyes wide in unison, looking like they had seen a ghost.

Qingxu in particular was even more astonished. This wasn't right.

The fighting style of this Yi Chengzi was too different from his master's.

He twitched his nose, focused intently on the light purple inner energy emanating from Yi Chen's body, and the doubts in his heart grew even more.

"It's the aura of pure yang true energy, but not as pristine as Bai Yunzi's, and the color is off."

"But it's not far from pure yang true energy, with a similarly strong restraining effect on demonic entities. And this inhuman, incomparable great force is simply unimaginable, not something a human physique should possess."
