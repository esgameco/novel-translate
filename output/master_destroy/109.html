Just as Yi Chen's anger was about to erupt, causing the hem of his Taoist robe to flutter wildly, a young attendant came running in holding a yellow paper crane.

"Reporting to Your Eminence, there is an urgent message crane from Mò Bǎihù, please read it yourself."

A minute later, Yi Chen's expression returned to calm, but he was as cold and indifferent as ancient glacial ice.

He summoned another young attendant and instructed him to notify Qian Yue Ru to go to the Chen Family Manor to find him. Then, his entire body transformed into a slender pale blue line, shooting straight through the roof, flying swiftly towards the Chen Family Manor.

Yi Chen had always been one to assume the worst intentions of others, but he never expected anyone could be so vile and cruel to this degree.

Corroborating the intelligence from Mò Bǎihù's message crane, he had now pieced together about 60-70% of the truth.

It was none other than an inside spiritual force colluding with demonic forces.

Humans fear ghosts for their terror, but ghosts understand the evil in human hearts!

Only the Chen Family had the power and influence to affect the entire Longjiang Prefecture to this extent.

The Huangquan Sect, the Red Chamber, and the Yin Mountain Manor must have made unspeakable deals with the Chen Family.

Yi Chen felt he had set his standards too high, which allowed the Chen Family to make a fool of him this time.

Night fell on the Chen Family Manor. In front of the 5-meter-tall vermilion gates, a towering Taoist figure, larger than a bear, descended slowly. He gazed quietly at the soft glow from the red lanterns in front of the manor, then clenched his huge fist, casting a massive shadowy fist over the gates.

"Some people don't value others' lives at all!"

"Some think they can control everything, have it all calculated!"

"Some look down from on high, refusing to give this poor Taoist any face!"

Boom!

The 5-meter vermilion gates suddenly exploded outwards like a cannonball firing backwards.

"What a pity, it's all an illusion!"

Inside the Chen Family Manor, Chen Bouyun's son Chen Daqiwas frowning worriedly as he asked his elderly father.

He felt his father had gone completely mad by agreeing to deal with those demonic heretic forces.

Chen Bouyun glanced at the streak of graying black hair by his ear, wiped the tea leaves off the cup lid, and said displeased, "Daqi, you need to stay calm for major events, otherwise how can I entrust the Chen Family to you in the future?"

"They're just commoners after all, their lives as insignificant as weeds - cut one batch and another will grow next year. We'll just collect less tax."

"Back in my day, I was known as the 'Master of Calculations'. Do you think I wouldn't have a backup plan?"

"The sacrifices chosen this time are either unregistered tenant farmers, servants, or helpless disadvantaged people our family has hidden over the years. Disposable people whose deaths won't be avenged."

"We haven't harmed anyone we shouldn't. The Chen Family has operated in Longjiang Prefecture for many years, with students and officials everywhere. Even if someone notices, they won't dare intervene against us."

"In Longjiang Prefecture now, the only one the Chen Family is wary of is that girl Qian Yue Ru, the True Lord's daughter with a prestigious identity."

"But even Qian Yue Ru cannot make a move here without my support. If I want, I can cripple her senses in an instant."

"As for that newly arrived brat, a drifter from some tiny town, heh.

He doesn't understand what real power is!

Just an imbecile.

Everyone in Longjiang Prefecture, from top to bottom, is my person.

Two days, and what has he discovered?

If he could find any clues, I'll drink from the chamber pot!

"Chen, you old dog, come out and face me!" A thunderous roar echoed endlessly over the Chen Family Manor.

The smug look on Chen Bouyun's face instantly froze, turning ghastly pale.

Yi Chen's body was shrouded in a pale blue aura, not bothering to conceal his murderous intent as he strode towards the heart of the manor grounds.

Any cultivated retainers who appeared to question or attack him were instantly slapped away by Yi Chen, their lives or deaths unclear.

"Fellow Taoist, for what reason do you barge into the Chen Family Manor in the night? Without a proper explanation, you'll have to answer to the Chen Family," Chen Bouyun said with a grim expression, deciding there would be no more chamber pots allowed in the Chen Family from this day forth.

"Hand over my subordinate Mò Bǎihù, or every last one of you will die," Yi Chen coldly refused to play word games with the snake.

There were few good people left in this world. He could not allow one who carried firewood for the masses to drown in the snow. Yi Chen still harbored a glimmer of hope to rescue Mò Bǎihù.

"What Mò Bǎihù nonsense?" Chen Bouyun's expression flickered with genuine surprise, not seeming like an act to Yi Chen.

But then, an ominous change suddenly occurred.

A light laughter echoed out.

Four massive black iron pillars abruptly emerged from the ground at the center of the Chen Family Manor, spanning a diameter of 1000 meters.

Each pillar was etched with bizarre inscribed patterns. Written on the pillars in ancient blood-red script were the four words: "Ghostly Qi Defying Heaven".

As the iron pillars rose, a powerful surge of yin energy rapidly expanded outwards, repelling everything around it.

A semi-transparent gray energy dome then appeared, enclosing the entire central grounds of the Chen Family Manor.

The Umbrella Maiden walked over from where the pillars emerged, lightly laughing as she said, "Manor Lord Chen, I did catch a glimpse of this 'Mò Bǎihù' the Taoist mentioned earlier. Please take a look."

With a wave of her hand, a black ghostly mirror formed in mid-air.

It showed an eerie blood pool bubbling beneath the grounds in front of the Chen Family Manor.
