The ten seconds passed quickly.

The demonic insect lay in the deep pit, its exoskeleton now tattered and broken, sticky green fluid clinging to its body.

Its last two intact compound eyes looked up in horror at the pale blue giant falling straight towards it from the sky, its mind reeling.

Now, the ten second period had arrived!

"Earth Escape!"

The demonic insect mustered its last energy, roaring towards the sky with a heart-wrenching cry.

But it was too late.

Like a star crashing from the wilderness, Yichen's two huge feet suddenly slammed down, crushing its body.

At the last moment, the demonic insect's earth escape spell activated, but only halfway successfully.

The rear half of its body vanished without a trace, but the front half had its head pinned firmly under Yichen's huge foot, unable to escape.

Now, half of the demonic insect's body lay in the pit, green fluid continuously flowing out from its severed end.

It had not yet perished, but its last two compound eyes had also been completely gouged out by Yichen's powerful strike.

The demonic insect struggled to raise its last intact insect leg, trembling as it tried to point it at Yichen. Its mouth opened and closed slowly, as if trying to say something.

Crack!

Another huge hand suddenly appeared, snatching the leg away.

Yichen had completely torn off the demonic insect's last giant leg and casually tossed it behind him onto the open ground.

The cultivators from the Mobai Sect soon discovered another giant leg lying on the ground.

This one was even more severely damaged, mottled scars bearing witness to the devastating injuries its owner had suffered.

"An invigorating battle today, though the predecessor was still slightly inferior to this pauper."

Yichen leapt out of the deep pit and stood at the edge, his back to the crowd, saying arrogantly.

Hearing Yichen's shameless words, the demonic insect finally could no longer endure. A huge green geyser erupted from its massive mouth, as if spraying the King of Kings, then its head lolled to the side as it miserably perished.

It was unknown whether it died from its severe injuries or from being enraged to death by Yichen.

Looking at its final death throes, no one knew the depths of its unwillingness in its last moments.

If it had been at its peak, it absolutely would not have ended up like this.

It still had tricks it had not used, ending before it could even begin.

[You have successfully killed the Demonic Insect Worm, gaining 220 deep red points.]

A line of information suddenly appeared on Yichen's retina.

A girl in black armor slowly walked out from a corner of the side courtyard, two huge bagua halberd axes crossed over her back.

The axe blades were caked with dried blood, surrounded by a dense aura of bloody killing intent - who knew how many unfortunate souls had died under these axes in recent days.

"Friend Qian, you're here?"

Yichen turned to face Qian Yueru.

He had sensed her aura midway through the latter half of the battle.

With such a major event occurring in the Southern District, she, as the garrison commander of Longjiang Prefecture, naturally had many people reporting to her, so she had kept some attention focused on the Southern District.

Yichen's actions had also been able to proceed smoothly with her support.

"Yeah, I'm here."

"It's a pity, after lying in wait for so long, even bringing some little toys, I didn't expect the big fish behind the Yellow Springs Sect, Yin Mountain Manor, and Red House to not show up for a rescue. Really disappointing."

Qian Yueru still had her hair tied in a high ponytail, but unlike their previous meeting, today she wore not cloud boots but a uniquely styled pair of high combat boots.

The black boots tightly gripped her slender, porcelain-white legs, making them appear even more fair and shapely.
