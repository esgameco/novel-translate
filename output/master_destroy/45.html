He looked at the transformed monster that the black robe man had become, shaken inexplicably in his heart.

What kind of technique was this?

A living person could still undergo corpse transformation?

Corpse qi permeated his body, yet he also had the aura of a living person. Truly strange.

Suddenly, a guess emerged in Yi Chen's mind.

Could this lunatic from the Corpse Deity Cult be cultivating using corpse refining secret techniques on his own body?

Refining one's own corpse, the people of the Corpse Deity Cult really had ideas.

The blood-colored light pillar above the blood pool became more concentrated, and the peculiar undulation in the air grew stronger.

"I'll tear you apart!"

"Follow the Longevity God, the vastly compassionate savior!"

The black robe man's corpse qi surged, transforming into a black streak that shot straight at Yi Chen, then flew away even faster.

Back when Yi Chen was struck back by the black robe man, he had secretly activated the Positive Polarity Shift.

This was the first time he used it after breaking through to the seventh level of his pure positive true cultivation. An incomparable sense of power emerged in Yi Chen's heart.

He felt like he could hammer the golden armored corpse skull that was battling Lin Baihu into its chest cavity.

Just imagining that scene made his heart tremble.

It had been a long time since he last hit someone's head. People had already forgotten how to write the "Yi" character in his Dharma name Yi Chengzi.

Now every mutt and tomcat dared to bare their fangs at him.

A neither human nor corpse freak, did it qualify?

Power surged through his body, a burning sensation.

His heart was pounding wildly, pumping energy throughout his body.

His figure suddenly grew nearly 30 centimeters taller, the muscles in his neck, hands, waist and legs swelling madly, expanding and thickening like donning a muscle armor.

The will to battle in his heart was fully ignited!

At this moment, Yi Chen abnormally sheathed his Dragon Slaying Sword.

He only needed his fists!

He was loyal to his fists!

Compared to the sword, he trusted the sense of faith and power conveyed by his fists more.

Of course, more importantly, he was afraid the violent clash between his Dragon Slaying Sword and the black robe's cudgel would create a few nicks...

After a sudden accelerated sneak attack sent the black robe flying, Yi Chen sped up, forcefully kicking off the ground with his left leg. Borrowing the powerful counterforce, he somehow caught up to the black robe he had struck away, delivering a vicious elbow strike to his chest.

This one was both fast and vicious, creating a dent in the black robe's chest after his corpse transformation.

Bang!

The black robe's body slammed heavily into an invisible air wall, then bounced off and fell to the ground.

Only then did Yi Chen have a chance to observe the entire battlefield.

To his left.

Master Lianhua, Master Shuiyue and Daoist Qingxu joined forces against the white-robed female ghost wielding red scissors. The three fought energetically with the ghost, barely holding the battlefield with their sect's magic weapons.

To his right.

Lin Baihu was enveloped in a deep green aura, using some unknown secret technique that boosted his aura by a level. He drew his battle blade, the flashing blade trapping the two golden armored corpses within.

Then...frantic scraping.

Lin Baihu's battle blade made clanging sounds like hammering iron, even striking sparks, yet those two golden armored corpses were still flailing and howling.

Watching this, Yi Chen felt like shouting: Master Lin, stop scraping!

It was obvious that Lin Baihu, having awakened the spiritual nature of wind and taking the agile path, was truly fast, but he lacked force.

For a man, being fast alone without force was a big problem.
