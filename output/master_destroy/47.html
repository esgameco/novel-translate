At this moment, this strange occurrence had already left Shitai Water Moon and the others dumbfounded and dumbstruck.

In the previous frame, Daoist Master Yi Chengzi was still displaying his mighty powers, suspending the mysterious black-robed person in the air and beating them. In the next frame, the situation suddenly changed drastically, with the mysterious 'black-robed person' pummeling Daoist Master Yi instead.

"Daoist Master Yi, are you alright?"

Shitai Water Moon and the others hurriedly went over with concern. An immense sense of despair involuntarily arose in everyone's hearts.

The strongest among them, Yi Chen, was completely powerless against this mysterious 'black-robed person' before their eyes. This feeling of helplessness caused a mix of emotions to well up in everyone's hearts.

"All of you, stay back. Don't come any closer."

Yi Chen used his gaze to stop the others from approaching further. He then turned his eyes towards the 'black robe', licking the trail of blood from the corner of his mouth, a long-forgotten taste of iron appearing on his tongue.

"No power, no power at all! Were you also taught by my master?"

"Hahaha."

A crazed expression appeared on his face.

He mustered all his inner energy and took the initiative to attack the 'black robe'.

Bang!

Once again, he was sent flying.

The 'black robe' looked at the fallen Yi Chen and couldn't help but shake his head. He was not affected by Yi Chen's taunting words.

After living for so many years, how could he be easily disturbed by someone's mere words? Wouldn't that mean his lifetime of cultivation had gone to the dogs?

In fact, an admiring look grew increasingly pronounced in the 'black robe's' eyes.

He liked this young man.

Quick-witted, principled, and strong-willed.

It was as if he saw his younger self.

Also a handsome fellow.

If Yi Chen had readily agreed after he extended the olive branch, he would have looked down on him instead. It would have just been toying with him, not truly nurturing him.

But now, he was truly moved.

He saw Yi Chen's determination to fight to the death rather than compromise.

That will, as sharp as a blade's edge, as weighty as a mountain, unyielding and brilliant.

After all, who in this world truly likes those driven by greed and desire?

If today I offer a high price, and you turn your back on your master's teachings to join me without a second thought.

Then tomorrow, if someone else offers an even higher price, wouldn't you also turn your back on me just as readily?

The 'black robe' now truly wanted to take Yi Chen under his wing.

He walked step by step, a black aura swirling around his hands.

"Edict: Body Bind."

He glanced towards Shitai Water Moon and the others, coldly saying.

A terrifying force of spatial stasis descended upon Shitai Water Moon and the rest, a feeling as if the entire space was opposing them.

They felt as though they were defying heaven and earth, an indescribably profound technique.

"Edict: Summon."

The 'black robe' continued walking, his hands constantly forming wondrous hand seals. As his words fell, the strange undulation in the air became even more pronounced.

Inside the Pavilion of Charming Scenery, the previously cracked crevice in the ground widened again. The black gas within the crevice churned violently, as if some object was about to gush out from underground.

No, there was indeed something stirring beneath the ground.

In an unnamed corner underground in Fengyun County, a jade box lay quietly in a subterranean river, its existence unknown for who knows how many years.

Three faded talisman papers made of some unknown demonic beast's hide, rather than regular paper, were affixed to the jade box, appearing unremarkable.

Inside the jade box, a tender, lotus root-like infant's left arm lay still, suppressed by a jade of emerald hue that flickered with arcing light, continuously draining the arm of its vitality.

The strange undulation in the air reached the underground river, as if answering some summons. The arm in the jade box began to violently tremble, speeding towards the source of the undulation.

The black gas emerging from the crevice grew thicker, occasionally seeping groundwater.

After the 'black robe' completed these actions, he turned his gaze back towards Yi Chen.
