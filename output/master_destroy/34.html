At this time, a tremendous change occurred once again on Yichen's character panel.

His maximum lifespan increased to 107 years, a full ten years more than before.

His strength attribute rose from the previously upgraded 33 to 45, topping the four dimensions and surpassing the others. Although not as high as strength, his stamina, spirit, and agility attributes all made significant progress as well.

Yichen read the small words on the light screen panel word by word, gaining some understanding of the active skill from the seventh layer of his Small Pure Yang Skill.

"With it having become this demonic form, it's no longer appropriate to call it the Small Pure Yang Skill. I might as well call it the Pure Yang True Skill."

Seeing the words "Pure Yang True Skill" for the seventh layer on the light screen, Yichen's thoughts lightly tapped on the plus sign behind it, and immediately a line of small words appeared at the very bottom of his retina's display.

[Current Deep Red value insufficient at less than 600, cannot be upgraded.]

Yichen couldn't help but gasp in shock at this sight.

Damn, the price has directly increased sixfold?

Accumulating just one hundred Deep Red value had already cost him dearly, even provoking some unknown powerful demonic force's master.

To accumulate six hundred, he would have to kill twenty corpse ghosts, mist ghosts, or mud-dragging ghosts of that level.

He couldn't afford to provoke that, no way.

"Dad, big dad, can't you invite some friends to lend a hand? This is too expensive," Yichen inwardly complained to the light screen display on his retina.

"Let me try out the quality of the seventh layer Pure Yang True Skill." Yichen's expression shifted, recalling that the seventh layer Pure Yang True Skill had gained the new qualities of Regaining Breath and Ignition, his curiosity instantly piqued.

He could no longer hold back, a streak of deep purple pure yang inner energy appearing at his fingertip. He lightly drew it across the rosewood table on the balcony, and the solid table surface was cut through as if by an oxyacetylene torch, a triangular slice falling onto the balcony floor.

The cut surface was pitch black, with faint blue flames still flickering, undoubtedly the ignition effect brought about by the deep purple pure yang inner energy.

Yichen was extremely satisfied by what he saw.

"The seventh layer Pure Yang True Skill is indeed impressive."

"This bone-rotting ignition effect is simply a demonic nightmare."

"Even if used for killing, arson, destroying corpses - it would be extremely effective."

"Scratch that, pretend I didn't say it."

Yichen flicked his finger, shooting a streak of deep purple pure yang inner energy directly at the triangular slice, instantly igniting and reducing it to ash.

He looked at the charred remains, ground them to pieces underfoot, and only then left the balcony to return inside.

One's courage stems from one's strength.

Compared to his previously fatigued appearance after the upgrade, now the weariness had completely vanished from his face, leaving only a faint smile.

He raised his head, gazing through the carved wooden window into the depths of the night sky, his eyes deep like a still lake, lost in thought.

After a while, he finally came to and shook his head with a smile.

"Excessive thinking is pointless."

"I should sleep instead."

"Striving to increase my own strength is the proper way."

With this thought, Yichen threw himself onto the bed and fell into a deep slumber.

Among the four, Monk Lianhua spoke first.

Monk Lianhua sighed and said helplessly, "It seems we can only take it one step at a time for now. After I return, I will send a letter to the Yuanlun Temple in the prefecture city to ask if they have any information. If they do, I will send someone to notify you all."
