Yi Chen was preparing for his grand century-long plan to have his spirit beast serve him, but it was aborted as soon as it began.

Looking at the little black kitten that had jumped off his shoulder and gnawed the eggshell until nothing was left, Yi Chen stared for a long time but couldn't see any aura of a celestial spirit seed.

It was quite skilled at being adorable, though.

But what use was being adorable?

Send a confidential letter to Master Shui Yue, saying there's a little black kitten in my Taoist monastery that can do backflips, does she want to come take a look?

...

At this moment, the little black kitten that had just finished gnawing on the eggshell walked over with graceful cat steps. It leapt onto Yi Chen's shoulder, nuzzled his face with its little face, and a purple ray shot up from the top of its head and entered his body at a speed too fast for Yi Chen to react.

"Meow meow ow." (Father)

A voice suddenly rang out from the depths of his heart.

It was still a child's voice.

Yi Chen instantly jumped up like he had seen a ghost. He looked around the room left and right, and only after confirming there were no living creatures did he turn his gaze back to the little creature in front of him.

"Was that you calling me?"

"Meow meow meow." (Yes, father)

"You're kidding, I haven't even touched a woman in this life, how could I have such a grown child? Don't call me that anymore, call me brother or master."

"Meow meow meow." (Okay, father)

Yi Chen: "......"

After a cross-species communication session, Yi Chen finally discovered the extraordinary aspect of the little black kitten - he could understand the kitten's meows thanks to an innate spell it awakened while eating the eggshell, the Equal Spirit Imprint.

This was an uncanny beast that was born with a bloodline spell.

Uncanny beasts with bloodline spells were the basic requirement to be considered a celestial spirit seed.

For a moment, Yi Chen didn't know how to evaluate the little black kitten in front of him.

If you say it's useless, it has a bloodline spell.

If you say it's extraordinary, apart from being smart, it really doesn't have any special skills.

At most it's fast.

Oh, and it can do backflips.

Looking at the kitten's wing-like appendages, Yi Chen's imagination ran wild.

Could this little black kitten be some kind of deviant offspring between different celestial species?

A combination of birds and beasts, depraved, shameful, forced?

What kind of twisted concept is this?

Is this a perversion of animal nature or a degradation of morality?

Only this could explain why there is no such spirit beast recorded in the spirit beast encyclopedia, yet it possesses a bloodline spell.

An interspecies hybrid, born by chance, ultimately rejected by its birth parents and cruelly abandoned in some ruin.

Good, it matches Yi Chen well.

In this life, Yi Chen himself is also an orphan, so the little black kitten is basically an orphan too.

An orphan meeting an orphan, so they are family.

All the fellow disciples of the Hidden Dragon Monastery were orphans taken in by Bai Yun Zi, now like one family.

Thinking of this, Yi Chen's gaze towards the little black kitten couldn't help but soften.

He decided to let the little black kitten regard him as an older brother and join the Hidden Dragon Monastery's orphan family.

"You're so black, why don't I just call you Little Black from now on?" Yi Chen stroked the little black kitten's head and casually gave it a name. "Simple and to the point."

"Meow meow meow..." (No no no)

The little black kitten shook its head like a lion's roar drum, clearly very resistant to this name.

After another encrypted communication session between human and cat, they finally reached an agreement.

The little black kitten would no longer call Yi Chen "father", but "older brother" instead.

And Yi Chen would not call it Little Black, changing its name to Little Meow, because it kept meowing and meowing in an annoying way.

Opening the door, the sun had already set and the moon risen. Yi Chen had reminded Qing Yun Zi and the others earlier to eat first and not wait for him. So Yi Chen ate dinner in his room with Little Meow.

He tore off a wild boar trotter and disdainfully placed it in the large bowl in front of Little Meow.

The bowl was nearly half the size of Little Meow's body.

"Little Meow, look at your scrawny self, you should learn more from your older brother. Look at these muscles of mine. After you get stronger, you can find those two irresponsible parents of yours and give them a beating, you know?"

"Eat more to build up strength."

"With those thin arms and legs, you'll never amount to anything."

Initially, it was terribly afraid.
