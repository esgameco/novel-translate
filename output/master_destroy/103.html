A strange, explosive force surged through the point of contact between it and Yi Chen.

It was the penetrating power unique to breaking through the Force Realm.

With a single strike, the demonic insect was forced to the ground, its giant foot retreating continuously to dispel the immense force Yi Chen had struck it with.

Witnessing this, Yi Chen couldn't help but feel enlightened.

There was indeed a problem!

How could a demonic insect capable of astonishing Mo Yu only possess this level of strength?

It was already in such a state after taking just his ordinary full-force punch.

In that case...

To not take advantage of its weakness and finish it off, would that be human?

One should pursue the remaining enemy relentlessly, not seek empty glory like a hegemon.

After contemplating for a moment, Yi Chen decided to increase his power against the demonic insect to prevent any future troubles.

"True Yang Tyrannical Force!"

Yi Chen slapped his chest and let out a fierce shout.

The light blue inner energy suddenly rippled outward with him as the center, then instantly converged back onto his body, forming a light blue glazed armor.

This was the unique transformation granted by the Eighth Layer of the Pure Yang True Technique.

The azure glazed armor, like a heavy exoskeleton, tightly encased his entire body, leaving only two eyes filled with a ferocious gaze exposed.

His figure began growing taller, his bones thickening and enlarging. Rings of muscles started wildly proliferating and hardening on his feet, thighs, abdomen, hands, arms, neck, as immense power began dancing freely within his body.

The muscles, like armor, covered his body, while the azure glazed armor formed from his inner energy adhered to the muscles like sugar coating on hawthorn candies.

His height increased rapidly, soon surpassing two and a half meters.

The light blue inner energy began thundering through his body as an imposing aura emerged.

Endless moonlight poured into Yi Chen's body like a whale taking in water, directly draining the surrounding area of light until it grew dim.

This made the azure-armored Yi Chen stand out even more against the night sky.

"As a junior, I request guidance from the senior. Let us have an all-out battle today."

"Demonic Insect Daoist..."

"Today, you and I will determine superiority and decide life or death!"

Yi Chen's voice boomed like thunder. Before the word "death" had fully left his lips, he was already diving toward the strange demonic insect again, trailing a light blue blazing tail behind him.

The commotion inside had also attracted some of the town security officers standing guard outside, including a few junior officers of decent cultivation.
