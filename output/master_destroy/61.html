The Venerable White Crane disdainfully glanced sideways at Yi Chen, who was gorging himself.

"This young man is still too young. The Wang family is so wealthy, yet instead of leaving a good impression on the main household, he only knows how to gorge himself. If you offend the host here, will you even get a decent red envelope afterwards?"

"You can always eat the food later. These little lamb chops won't run away. You must maintain some decorum."

The Venerable White Crane looked at the crispy-on-the-outside, tender-on-the-inside little lamb chops in front of him, licked his lips, but restrained himself.

He could pack them up to eat later at night, it would be the same.

Then, just as he was focused on putting on airs, the Venerable White Crane suddenly felt the surrounding light dim, and a large face leaned in close to him.

"Venerable White Crane, don't you like these little lamb chops?"

"What a pity~"

"Uh...this pauper monk does not eat meat."

"I love them. Since you don't want to eat them Venerable White Crane, why don't you give them to me?"

Without waiting for the Venerable White Crane to respond, Yi Chen directly took all the little lamb chops from in front of him, and even snatched a peach that was there too.

The Venerable White Crane's mouth trembled, his chest heaving violently. After a long while, he still hadn't said a word, as he knew his midnight snack was gone.

At this time, the Wang family master and his wife, seated in the main positions, had little appetite for the exquisite food and just ate a few bites before apologizing and withdrawing to attend to the old madame's final rites.

However, the sharp-eyed Yi Chen noticed a slight abnormal flush on the wife's face at this moment.

Although she had put on powder, that unusual blush was still caught by Yi Chen.

It was the afterglow of intimacy...

Drawing on his past life's experience with tea tasting, Yi Chen was certain he was not mistaken.

Tsk~

The old madame was about to pass away, yet these two still had the free time for amorous activities?

Or was it... on the grassy plains?

These city folks really know how to have fun.

Yi Chen's gossipy soul burned fiercely, yet his heart remained unruffled. He broke off a rib and gave it to the kitten, then turned and polished off the rest of the lamb chops in three bites.

He patted his belly, and under the lead of a maidservant, returned to the guest room.

The heavy rain had already stopped by the time Yi Chen arrived in Ping'an County Town. Tonight, the moon was bright and the stars were twinkling.

Yi Chen opened the window to let the evening breeze fill the room. He picked up a cup of brewed tea on the table and took small sips, his thoughts unknown to anyone.

The little kitten was doing sit-ups on the floor, hooking its hind legs under the horizontal bar beneath the table, going up and down repeatedly.

Everything was so harmonious.

Ping'an County Town, the Wang Manor, rear courtyard.

Xiuer was the wife's personal maidservant, naturally favored by the wife. However, five or six days ago, the wife had unexpectedly arranged for her to move to the rear courtyard to live with the other maidservants.

She was extremely unhappy, unaware of what mistake she had made.

The events of the past five or six days were enough for the newly 18-year-old girl to remember for life.

Previously, she had been the wife's personal head maidservant. The menservants adored her, the older servants doted on her, the younger maidservants envied her.

Everything had been so wonderful.

But all of that was completely shattered five or six days ago, like a soap bubble bursting.

The colorful world suddenly turned gray and gloomy.

The world changed overnight.

The older servants who had previously flattered her now oppressed her.

The menservants who had desperately pursued her previously hurled vicious insults at her when she rejected their advances again, cursing that she still thought she was the head maidservant, acting so high and mighty - a plucked phoenix was no better than a chicken, let alone a mere maidservant like her.

As for the other maidservants who had previously called her "elder sister" this and "elder sister" that, they now looked at her with disdain and marginalized her openly and secretly.

The harsh reality of people raising one up only to trample them down was vividly displayed before her.

Tears welled up in Xiuer's beautiful large eyes.

When she was little, she had heard a storyteller recount a tale. She had mostly forgotten the specific story, only remembering that at the end, the storyteller had said a line with a wistful expression:

"Life is like a monkey climbing a tree. Looking up, you only see asses. Looking down, you only see smiling faces."

She didn't understand the meaning of those words as a child, only remembering them because the storyteller looked so handsome, which captivated her young self.

Curious in the blossoming years of her youth, she had asked the storyteller scholar for his name.

He didn't give his surname, only saying his style name was Yunlong. Smiling, he told her about his past.

He said he wasn't from Ping'an County Town.

He said he used to be a renowned scholar of their region, skilled in the bamboo craft.

He said he used to have a lover named Xiuer, just like her.

But it wasn't her.

Afterwards, that storyteller dejectedly left with his book trunk, departing from Ping'an.
