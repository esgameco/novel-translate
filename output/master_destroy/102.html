Time passed second by second, one person after another was investigated. Although some inexplicable clues were found, more were actually just people who were mistakenly arrested due to all kinds of messy situations.

The various messy reasons made Yi Chen feel both amused and helpless.

Soon, another person was brought in.

It was none other than Li Xiucai.

Yi Chen massaged his brow and routinely questioned him, "Speak up, Li Xiucai. I heard you recently came into a bit of money. And when did you get your hands on a secret steamed bun recipe?"

"This...this...this..." Li Xiucai's forehead was now beaded with sweat, as if there was an internal struggle raging within him.

"I, the Taoist master, don't care about your petty stuff. Speak frankly, or do you want to end up like those people on the ground over there?"

"This...this...this...the money was something I found, the recipe was from an ancient book I saw..."

"Li Xiucai, you really are as brazen as a steel pipe poking a little frog. You dare deceive me with lies at a time like this?"

Upon hearing this, Yi Chen's pupils immediately constricted, and his expression became slightly more serious.

"I'll count to three!"

"Three"

"One"

"Stubborn fool."

Without further ado, a massive pale blue fist force rushed straight towards Li Xiucai.

The Taoist master was in a hurry.

Scoundrels who hid the truth had to be sent to the afterlife on the spot.

Suddenly, a light sound came from a corner of Li Xiucai's body.

Like the shattering of glass.

A ferocious, upright centipede-like giant insect appeared in front of Li Xiucai.

It seemed to harbor resentment towards Li Xiucai. One of its huge feet stepped forward, directly taking on the casual fist force from Yi Chen. Its pink tongue lashed out, instantly separating Li Xiucai into two pieces.

"Demonic insect, you've met a Taoist friend. Your skills are truly admirable."

"But do you not know the karmic consequences of killing people indiscriminately?"

"Killing arises from the heart, you reap what you sow. You should join my holy sect."

Under the moonlight, the giant insect's mouth opened and closed as it actually began to question Yi Chen's actions, making for a bizarre scene.

The giant insect was shrouded in ominous energy, its aura extremely intimidating.

Seeing this situation, Yi Chen suddenly stood up, unable to hide a sinister smile on his face.

After investigating all these small fries, he had finally hooked a big fish.

If he wasn't mistaken, this strange occurrence was most likely caused by this demonic insect in the southern city.

He just didn't know what methods the insect used to conceal its aura and form, allowing it to evade his detection until now.

The insect was meticulous in its actions, not placing any spells on Li Xiucai, but rather luring him with money and attacking his heart, thus hiding until this moment.

Dammit.

The old society turned people into ghosts.

Seeing the towering four-meter giant insect standing in the night sky, Mo Yu, who was standing behind Yi Chen, seemed to recall something. He quietly retreated two steps and exclaimed in shock: "Be careful, Taoist master! This demonic insect is the mount of Madam Butterfly from the Red Chamber. Its cultivation is astonishingly high."

Hearing this, the insect's abdomen swelled with pride at being Madam Butterfly's mount.

Its mouth opened as it loudly declared: "Devouring heaven and earth, devouring the sun and moon, the insect leaps to the ninth heaven to devour..."

However, before it could finish, Yi Chen had already transformed into a pale blue thread, fiercely attacking it, while rolling thunder sounded from behind him.

"So it's the little runt!"

"You vile wench."

"A mount with its own pseudonym?"
