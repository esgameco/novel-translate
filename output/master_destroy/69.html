Yi Chen clenched his fists tightly, explosive power surging through his body.

At this moment, he had a feeling that if he faced the Five Fiends Demon again, he would not need to use the Yang Polarity Shift to directly crush it head-on.

After the Strength Realm Breakthrough, he gained two special effects - one was penetration.

Yi Chen tested it out and found that unlike the internal force achieved through martial skill, the penetration effect from the Strength Realm Breakthrough was more like an innate rule, a realm beyond what skills could achieve.

To make an imperfect analogy, this penetration effect was essentially shockwaves and...explosions.

With strength solidified into one, if analyzed with technology from his previous life, it would be waves with extremely high vibration frequencies that could transmit force through obstacles by the high-frequency vibrations, and explode violently upon reaching the peak.

It was like the difference between a gun with and without rifling in the barrel.

The gun was the same, but with rifling added, the penetration power of the bullets increased dramatically. The penetration effect after the Strength Realm Breakthrough was like adding that rifling.

"It's practically a corpse-shattering divine technique."

Seeing the table leg of the long redwood table in front of him reduced to powder, Yi Chen's pupils suddenly contracted.

He had just controlled his strength to the level that had previously broken the table leg and struck the long table, but the table instantly exploded into dust, which might even affect the floor below.

What a terrifying shockwave penetration effect, greatly enhancing the destructive power of each unit of his strength.

The other effect after his first Strength Realm Breakthrough was easier to understand - Strength Aura.

It additionally increased his strength attribute by 20%. With his current base strength of 50, he could exert the effect of 60, a free bonus of 10 points. The higher the base, the more he would gain...

"What a pleasant surprise."

Yi Chen couldn't help but grin widely.

Just one Strength Realm Breakthrough had such an incredible effect. When his base strength reached 100 for the next breakthrough, wouldn't it be even more amazing?

What about the third, fourth, or fifth breakthroughs?

Wouldn't he be able to kill with a single punch and explode anything he touched?

Combined with the ferocious and violent pure yang inner qi, it would be perfect.

Just as Yi Chen was indulging in imagining the future, there was a sudden urgent knocking at the door, accompanied by cursing.

"Hey upstairs, not sleeping in the middle of the night, do you think I'm the Jade Prince to just eat..."

The door creaked open.

There were lanterns providing light in the hallway.

A huge shadow loomed over the person outside the door.

It was a young master dressed in brocade robes, with two large, muscular guards behind him, their temples bulging prominently, looking quite formidable.

"What's the matter?" Yi Chen opened the door and asked calmly.

"You bastard, disturbing my young master's..." One of the guards behind the young master cursed angrily, but before he could finish, the young master kicked him flying with a vicious kick.

"My lord, it's fine now."

"These few guards of mine drank too much horse piss and are acting crazy drunk. Please don't take them seriously."

"We'll leave right away, right away."

The so-called Jade Prince young master now had a big smile on his face, nodding and bowing obsequiously.

At his neck, a small fiery red squirrel was trembling in the folds of his robe.

However, a cold indifferent voice came, causing the Jade Prince to freeze in shock.

"Did I tell you to leave?"

The Jade Prince felt like crying now. He had been exploring the mysteries of the human body with a girl downstairs.
