The blood moon hung in the sky, meteors descended to the world.

Such a major event naturally drew attention from all under heaven.

Dragon Tiger Mountain was the ancestral court of the Celestial Masters sect, towering into the clouds, unrivaled under heaven.

At this time, on the golden peak of Dragon Tiger Mountain, an emaciated Taoist priest sitting cross-legged on the golden peak watched the blood moon in the sky, his face showing a sorrowful expression. With a wave of his hand, he unleashed nine lightning dragons to meet the largest of the blood-colored meteors streaking across the night sky.

In the end, the lightning dragons and meteors were both extinguished.

However, the number of lightning dragons was limited, while the blood-colored meteors, large and small, numbered over a thousand. It was like a drop in the bucket, utterly ineffective.

The emaciated Taoist priest's expression grew even more sorrowful. His body flashed, leaving behind a golden talisman before disappearing into the back mountains.

At Brightness Supreme Mountain, the Buddhist ancestral court, a white-browed elderly monk had been sitting motionless on a cliff for who knows how many years. He suddenly opened his eyes, looking at the meteors filling the sky. He lightly struck the wooden fish beside him, and nine golden lotus platforms rose into the air to meet nine large blood-colored meteors before also vanishing.

The old monk sighed and suddenly disappeared from the spot.

At Green Lotus Mountain, the abode of the number one female Perfected of the era, Lady Green Lotus, a crown talisman hundreds of zhang tall suddenly appeared at the mountain peak to face the meteors filling the sky. Lady Green Lotus's willow brows furrowed, and with a light rebuke, a sword formed from gathered starlight shot forth from her mouth.

The sword expanded in the wind, instantly swelling many times over. Countless stars were drawn to it like a magnet, increasing its might. After shattering twelve large blood-colored stars, it finally exploded thunderously.

Seeing this, Lady Green Lotus waved her wide sleeve, dissipating the talisman, and disappeared into the starry sky.

At this time, the top cultivators from the various major powers - the Three Mountains and Six Sects, the reigning dynasty, the Five Great Sword Sects, the Twelve Clans, the Nine Great Aristocratic Families - all took action, intercepting the blood-colored meteors raining down. Countless rays of magical light illuminated heaven and earth.

With their combined efforts, they managed to brush aside most of the blood-colored meteors, deflecting them beyond the celestial veil.

However, many smaller blood meteors still could not be stopped and fell to the earth. One small blood meteor happened to fall not far from Ping'an Town, at the Yang Family Village.

As the blood meteors fell and impacted, the blood moon in the sky also slowly faded, and the pure moonlight once again illuminated the earth, restoring everything to its original state.

Aside from the fallen blood meteors, it seemed as if heaven and earth were no different from before.

But was this realm of heaven and earth truly unchanged from before?

Yi Chen stood with his sleeves hanging, watching this scene before decidedly choosing to add points.

This was too exciting.

He had just felt that life was meaningless and wanted to pursue a more exciting world, and then this super boosted event occurred.

He wondered if he had been infected by Linbaihu's pessimism.

The abnormal celestial phenomenon that dyed the moon blood-red, the sudden manifestation and disappearance of that overwhelming malicious force filling heaven and earth - it all proved to Yi Chen that this realm would become exceedingly exciting from now on.

"Alas, why is it so hard to seize control of one's own destiny?"

With a thought, four origin points instantly went into his Strength attribute.

Yi Chen's heart couldn't help but roar.

"Come on, origin points, let me see your limits!"

"It's time to break through the Strength Realm!"

A tremendous warm current suddenly appeared within Yi Chen's limbs and body. Unlike before, this warm current was mixed with an indescribable, profound essence.

Unlike the comforting warmth from adding points before, like soaking in a bath, today's warm current grew increasingly scorching as it rapidly circulated.

This was no longer a bath - it was like soaking in lava.
