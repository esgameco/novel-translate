One thousand six hundred deep red points were instantly deducted, and at the same time, a magnificent memory suddenly appeared in Yi Chen's mind.

It was the familiar scene of reading memories again.

The memories unfolded rapidly like watching a panoramic view.

Beside a thousand-foot waterfall, a Taoist lived in a thatched hut.

He watched the rising sun in the morning and the setting sun in the evening. After more than a dozen years of this, he regrettably made little progress.

He then left and began traveling the world with a bamboo staff and straw sandals.

He had seen thousands of miles of frozen land, snow flurries spanning millions of miles. Inside and outside the strategic passes, there was nothing but vast desolation. Along the great rivers, the once mighty currents disappeared.

He had also witnessed the eruption of a long-dormant volcano that devastated the land. The powerful volcanic ash blocked out the sun, and all living things wailed in anguish.

He saw rivers bursting their banks, with tens of thousands of civilians joining hands to carry stones, like ants lining up one after another, jumping down perilous areas to block the breach, their united efforts astounding heaven and earth.

He had also witnessed plagues sweeping across thousands of miles, like the thrashing of an earth dragon.

He had seen the frivolous romantic atmosphere in wine cups and the fading beauty of women, generals growing old, and Buddhas with downcast eyes.

The Taoist traveled the world, seeing himself, the heavens and earth, and all living beings. Finally, after a hundred years, he returned to the waterfall where he had originally lived in a thatched hut.

By then, the impurities in his heart had long been washed away by years of experience, leaving a state of pure clarity.

He had realized the direction he should advance.

To further his practice of the Pure Yang Way to the current level, he would have to seek inwardly as well as outwardly.

During the day, the Taoist ventured deep into the mountains and marshes to gather rare herbs and slay demonic beasts, nourishing his body.

At night, he would sleep beneath the thousand-foot waterfall, tempering his spirit in pursuit of supernatural powers.

Day after day of replenishing himself, night after night of honing, his formidable spiritual willpower began to further integrate with his pale green inner energy, undergoing a metamorphosis.

When the first strand of spiritual energy permeated the inner energy, becoming inseparable, a profound and abstruse transformation began to occur, unstoppably.

After more than a hundred years of effort beneath the waterfall, a Taoist stood bare-chested, revealing exceptionally powerful muscles. He raised his sword-like brows to greet the rising sun and chanted softly:

"Pure Yang!"

Then he chanted again, louder:

"Pure Yang! Pure Yang!"

And again, even louder:

"Pure Yang! Pure Yang! Pure Yang!"

The Taoist's chanting grew louder, more intense, and more rapid with each repetition!

The entire mountain forest trembled at the sound of his "Pure Yang" chant.

Birds and beasts were startled!

In that moment, boars dashed and wolves fled. Countless wild fruits rained down from the mountains, some falling into the water, others into the mud.

A sound wave called Pure Yang swept out wave after wave for hundreds of miles into the distance.

Over two thousand meters away from the Taoist, a long-tongued goblin was suddenly struck by this Pure Yang sound wave, instantly shattering its gall bladder and liver, its soul dissipating into oblivion.

"Pure Yang! Pure Yang! Pure Yang! Pure Yang!"

The Taoist stood beneath the thousand-foot waterfall, eyes bulging with fury, muscles knotted all over his body. After chanting "Pure Yang" four more times, he fell silent, then suddenly opened his mouth wide, inhaling sharply toward the rising sun while forming an esoteric hand seal.

The Taoist's body seethed with circulating energy. Even the raging thousand-foot waterfall could not wet him with a single drop.

With this movement, countless streaks of purple energy in the sky seemed to be drawn by an inexplicable force, rushing into the Taoist's body like a whale gulping water.

As the purple energy merged with his inner energy and combined with his powerful spiritual will, the pale green inner energy continuously metamorphosed toward a deeper shade of green, until the transformation was complete.

However, this was not the end.

An even more intense transformation was about to occur.

With the influx of purple energy, the inner energy undulated, and the spiritual willpower thundered in the void. The combination of the three produced a profound, abstruse change.

Like 3D printing, a deep green aura that seemed both illusory and real gradually appeared, enveloping the Taoist's body.

It started with the soles of the feet, then the calves and thighs, followed by the waist, abdomen, and chest, and finally the neck.

However, the transformation seemed to falter when it reached the head.

Seeing this, the Taoist slapped his chest, threw his arms open, and let out a mighty roar facing the sky!

Two deep red lines suddenly shot up from his chest and abdomen, reaching his brow and forming an intricate, esoteric pattern that flickered mysteriously, as if something profound was gestating within.

Countless visible rays of light in the sky were also drawn like iron filings to a magnet, merging into the Taoist's deep green aura to complete the final transformation.

The illusory deep green aura finally formed a head.

At the last moment, the two-meter-tall Taoist stood beneath the thousand-foot waterfall, untouched by a single drop.

His muscular body was like a masterpiece sculpted by the finest artist, neither too bulky nor lacking in a sense of solidity and power.

At his brow was an intricate, flickering red pattern that seemed to contain some profound mystery.

Around the Taoist's body stood a three-meter-tall, imposing illusory deep green aura.

Surrounding the aura, deep green inner energy pulsed and flickered.

The Taoist raised his head and gazed at the sky.

His illusory aura also raised its head, following his consciousness.

As the transformation completed, a burst of exhilarated laughter erupted from the Taoist's mouth.

Sheer bliss.

He let out a long, ringing shout of laughter.

"The True Yang Celestial Body!"

With that, the Taoist stomped his foot beneath the waterfall and instantly shot upwards against the current, reaching the source of the waterfall in a flash.

The memory ended abruptly there.

It was like Yi Chen had just watched an accelerated stream-of-consciousness movie.
