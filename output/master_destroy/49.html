Yichen gripped the red scissors on the ground in his hand, and two streams of information suddenly appeared on his retina.

[You have successfully killed the White-Robed Female Ghost Red Scissors, gaining 40 deep red points.]

[Contact with a special item, deep red point quantity +20]

At this moment, he felt speechless in his heart.

The black robe and that separated spiritual body of the Yan Zhenren were not demonic spirits, so they did not "drop money". The only good news was that the red scissors in front of him, like the firewood knife of the corpse ghost before, also contributed 20 deep red points to him.

Yichen casually tore off a strip of cloth from his body and wrapped his left palm, which was almost exposed to the white bone, round and round.

Throughout the whole process, he remained silent, enduring the excruciating pain, very manly.

The motto of his previous life was:

In one's life, apart from life and death, everything else is just a scratch.

A pure man, like a lone wolf, licks his own wounds when hurt, and gets used to the pain after a while.

Showing your weakness not only makes you effeminate, but also gives others the right to hurt you.

So.

A man must be vigorous!

Daoist Yi had profound insights into this.

After dealing with his injuries, Yichen looked around.

At this moment, suddenly, a jade box caught his attention.

With the eagle-eye vision enhanced by the Yin-Yang Cave Divine Pupil Technique, he discovered that a jade box was quietly lying on its side among the piles of loaches in the crevice far away.

Yichen walked quickly to the crevice. By now, the crevice in the ground was no longer emitting black gas, and the area around the crevice was covered in mud and water stains. A baby's left arm, like a lotus root segment, lay in the muddy water. Nearby were an overturned jade box and a jade of emerald green color.

Yichen drew the Dragon Slaying Sword, intending to pick up the emerald green jade from the muddy water.

Then, he happily started playing with it.

"Damn, this broken thing is electrified."

He hurriedly cut off the convulsions of his body with his willpower and broke free.

The Dragon Slaying Sword was even thrown onto the ground.

Just as he was about to curse loudly, he suddenly stopped his action.

The decimal places after the decimal point of the source points on the virtual panel had directly increased by 0.001.

Wow, electrotherapy?

At this moment, a bold idea emerged from his mind.

Perhaps he didn't need to stew himself in an iron pot anymore.

If he directly electrotherapized himself, wouldn't that be a major event?

Touch the electric door and electrify yourself once, that's 0.001.

Touch the electric door and electrify yourself ten times, that's 0.01.

Touch the electric door and electrify yourself a hundred times, that's 0.1.

Touch the electric door and electrify yourself a thousand times, even the King of Hell would praise me for my good health.

At this moment, Yichen looked at the emerald green jade on the ground with shining eyes, as if he were looking at a rare treasure.

No, it was simply a source point treasury.

In an instant, Yichen's depression from not harvesting enough deep red points in this battle vanished.

Deep red is daddy, but isn't the source point also daddy?

Although any way of stimulating the body would eventually produce resistance, leading to diminishing returns later on, but for such an intense operation as electrocution, this resistance ceiling must be extremely high.

Based on his previous experience, Yichen conservatively estimated that if he made good use of it, he could probably gain forty or fifty source points in a short period of time.

Damn, what a loss on one side and gain on the other.

If I torture myself again, I won't be human anymore.

Thank you for nature's gift!

Source point, let me see your limit!

After experimenting, Yichen found that wrapping the jade in several layers of cloth could insulate the electric discharge from the emerald green jade. Without hesitation, he tore off a large piece from the hem of Qingxu Daoren's Daoist robe, wrapped the emerald green jade in three layers inside and three layers outside, and put it in his arms.

Since he did owe the three Qingxu Daoists a life debt, tearing off a few inches of their clothes was reasonable, right?

This way, he would also be cooler.

As for the baby's left arm lying on the ground, like a lotus root segment, Yichen had already put it into the jade box and sealed it again with animal skin talisman slips.

At this moment, he already had a rough idea of how to deal with this jade box.

After simply explaining to the County Master at the county office, Yichen hired four horse carriages to collectively transport the four lying people back to the Hidden Dragon Monastery.

After being violently shaken by Yichen's intense battle, they suffered slight cerebral concussions and needed some time to recover.

After all, the Great Protector's separated spiritual body was skilled in myriad secret spells, and without spiritual defenses, they were not much stronger than ordinary people.

At this moment, a figure appeared on the distant horizon, slowly approaching from afar.

This person was also wearing a fish robe, but it looked much more magnificent than the one worn by Lin Baihu, with different animal patterns on the chest.

Though his pace was not fast, he could cover more than ten meters in the blink of an eye. Soon, this person floated into the Hidden Dragon Monastery.
