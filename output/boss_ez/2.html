Pan-fried hairtail fish, stir-fried pork with green peppers, and scrambled eggs with tomatoes.

Tonight's dinner was especially bountiful.

All of this was for tonight's gaming session.

Fang Yu looked towards the corner of the room.

Boxes of instant noodles in flavors like braised beef, spicy beef, and seafood shrimp were stacked in the corner - this was his strategic food reserve.

In addition, there were also some canned emergency food items, truly prepared to the point where he wouldn't have to go out at all.

After this round of shopping, his bank account had finally dropped from five digits to four digits.

This was also the reason he could so quickly forgive that suit-wearing man for occupying his spot.

There was no help for it, he was just too poor. Getting a 10,000 yuan gaming helmet for free was like manna from heaven! Fang Yu hugged the gaming helmet to the bed, rubbing his hands excitedly as he unwrapped the package. A gaming helmet with a sleek, assembly line-inspired design appeared before his eyes.

The most important thing was that it even had high-end RGB lights! The sense of technology and atmosphere was immediately maxed out!

Okay, the design has my approval! It really does seem worth 10,000 yuan! He checked the time on his phone - the game was set to go live in just ten minutes.

The only thing that was clear was that this game was produced by the prestigious Blue Sea Group, and anything from Blue Sea Group was bound to be a masterpiece.

The last game they made that dominated the gaming world and was hailed by players as the "dawn of a new era" also came from Blue Sea Group.

And now this "Seek the Demon" was their second game, with information kept completely under wraps.

Everyone was incredibly eager, and Fang Yu was no exception.

"Last time I tried being a professional gamer, I ended up losing money, but that was because the game I chose was the problem, not me!"

"This time, 99% of players are all focused on this game. As long as it doesn't completely flop, I've got it in the bag! Not even Jesus can stop me!"
