Fortunately, at this moment, a graceful figure flashed by from the inner courtyard of the Yuanti Martial Arts Hall.

Fang Yu was immediately overjoyed.

"Senior Sister Mei!!"

Shouting excitedly, Fang Yu's feet smashed the ground, causing the bricks to crack slightly, and he took off like a rocket, rushing towards the Yuanti Martial Arts Hall.

This sudden burst of speed left the two who had been quickly approaching him visibly stunned.

After a female head poked out from the inner courtyard and greeted Fang Yu, the two exchanged a glance and then silently left like ordinary passersby.

"Why are you so late today? And just so you know, I've already eaten all the dumplings, not a single one left."

Shi Cang Mei thought Fang Yu was calling her because he had finally realized the importance of the Yuanxin Meat Dumplings for martial arts training.

But Fang Yu didn't care about the dumplings at all! He was panting heavily and looked back, seeing that there was no one at the hall's entrance anymore.

"Senior Sister, I owe you one."

Patting Shi Cang Mei's shoulder, Fang Yu quickly walked into the inner part of the hall, leaving the bewildered Shi Cang Mei behind.

Narrowly escaping with his life, that's the only way to describe it.

As Fang Yu walked quickly, he tried to calm his racing heart.

He never expected the demons to be so blatant!

This was broad daylight! There were people everywhere, except for the Yuanti Martial Arts Hall, which had just passed its recruitment period and had fewer visitors.

Otherwise, those demons wouldn't have had any opportunity to make a move.

What puzzled Fang Yu the most was how those three demonic beings had targeted him.

Could it be that the demonic bloodline I just awakened attracted them, like kindred spirits?

No, that can't be right! If it was a demonic bloodline, they should have recognized me as a demon, just like Yue Guang.

But those three demons were clearly after my life!

Could it be that there are also factional disputes among demons?

Wait a minute! Fang Yu suddenly stopped.

He had thought of something.

It was... the aura of slaughter! Although he could barely sense it now, there was no denying that remnants of the aura of slaughter still lingered on him.

It was likely this aura that had attracted the demons to him!

Realizing this, Fang Yu felt much more at ease.

At least he didn't have to worry about being targeted by demons for no reason.

But then his brow furrowed again.

Those three demons know my appearance and the remnants of the aura of slaughter on me.

Will they really give up so easily? If I wait until nightfall when the aura dissipates and then boldly walk out, will those three demons give up the hunt, or will they be waiting at the entrance like a tiger waiting for its prey? Fang Yu couldn't find the answer, and he felt trapped in a difficult situation.

Facing such a predicament, Fang Yu's evil tendencies surfaced, and he suddenly made a turn, striding towards the storage room. Kill the old monk, absorb the great blood, and slay the demons!

With each step, his aura grew stronger, and by the time he reached the storage room, his spirit was soaring, his resolve unwavering. But just as he entered the storage room, he collided with a figure.

Boom! The figure was knocked to the ground, but Fang Yu remained unmoved.

Looking down, he saw that it was Lin Wu.
