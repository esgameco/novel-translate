Second sister, our family has money now!

It's just that for the next attribute point, he'll definitely have to put it into physique.

Originally thinking he was just an ordinary junior brother.

Turns out, he was the trump card.

"...Nevermind, the master is looking for you, come with me."

It's finally here!

The 10 taels of silver table compensation!

"We have money!"

Frightened by a single command from Shi Chongmei, they all scattered like birds and beasts.

The awe that Master had accumulated over the years was not just talk - no one dared to defy him.

Following Shi Chongmei, Fang Yu was actually brought back to the hall where he had previously taken on an apprenticeship.

This familiar scene made Fang Yu momentarily feel like he had been rolled back on the server.

"Diao Deyi, come in."

Okay, this part didn't happen when he took on the apprenticeship before.

After paying respects, Fang Yu looked up to see Yuan Hongxin had put down the letter.

Glancing at the contents of the letter, he vaguely saw the name Liqian Xuan.

So this was really the same letter as before? Why was Yuan Hongxin reading it twice? Was he bored? "Diao Deyi, have you started practicing the martial arts?"

Yuan Hongxin asked, already knowing the answer.

"Yes. I have learned the [Yuan Body Stabilizing Technique]."

"Mm."

Yuan Hongxin stood up and walked around Fang Yu in a circle.

One circle, two circles, three circles...

His expression changed from initial doubt to surprise, confusion, and finally shock.

Suddenly, Yuan Hongxin grabbed Fang Yu's hands.

On the backs of Fang Yu's hands, there were white bony structures covering them.

The coverage area was not large, about half the back of the hand, but it was extremely hard, like an exoskeleton.

Fang Yu was startled.

What the hell is this?!

He had just felt his body weak and his hands numb, but he didn't expect these strange things to grow out.

But Yuan Hongxin was intoxicated by this, as if admiring a perfect creation of heaven and earth! "The bone solidification is so pure and white!"

Yuan Hongxin murmured to himself, then suddenly looked sharply at Fang Yu.

"Tell me, what level have you reached in the Yuan Body Stabilizing Technique?"

"Level? The fourth layer? Furnace-like purity?"

Fang Yu looked bewildered.

"Furnace-like purity! Indeed, furnace-like purity!"

Yuan Hongxin was in disbelief.

The Yuan Body Stabilizing Technique was the foundational and core martial art of the Yuan Body Sect.

But those who could reach the furnace-like purity level were few and far between.

Someone like Fang Yu, who had cultivated the purest white bone, was practically one in ten thousand.

No, there was none.

Even Yuan Hongxin himself only had a half-gray, half-red bone armor.

The reason was simple.

After reaching the [Gradual Improvement] stage in the Yuan Body Stabilizing Technique, there would be a bottleneck.

This bottleneck tested one's mental purity.

If the mental purity was not pure, the bones would be stained with various colors.

Once the white bones were stained, it would be extremely difficult to continue cultivating this technique.

And to break through to the furnace-like purity, it was even more difficult, almost impossible.

Even Yuan Hongxin had used many tricks to cultivate this technique to the furnace-like purity level and produce the most important white bone gauntlet of the bone armor.

The Yuan Body was a very complex martial art.

So complex that the previous generations had even divided the techniques, to complete the learning of the techniques.

Each technique corresponded to a part of the body, forming an external bone armor.

Finally, by operating multiple techniques together, they could complete the full-body coverage of the bone armor.

Only then would it be considered a success, and the subsequent step would be to just improve the strength of the bone armor.

But this early stage of cultivating the individual parts of the bone armor was the most difficult.

It was also the weakest and most vulnerable stage of cultivating this set of techniques.

Among them, the most difficult to cultivate were the heart armor, hand armor, and head armor.

They were closely related to mental purity, and one misstep would make this martial art unattainable, leaving flaws in the bone armor formation.

Logically speaking, such a difficult martial art, Diao Deyi, who had only started learning martial arts at the age of sixteen, should not have been able to break through to the furnace-like purity level.

But the fact was...

Diao Deyi had learned it.

And he had cultivated the purest white bone! The highest quality bone armor gauntlet! (End of chapter)
