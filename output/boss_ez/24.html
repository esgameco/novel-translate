He didn't even dare to eat this thing!

Moreover, he also realized a problem.

That is, when this [Drill Demon] is hidden inside the human body, before it triggers resonance, he cannot see the blood bar.

Only after triggering the resonance can it be detected and the blood bar displayed, its hiding ability is far beyond that of ordinary demons.

At the same time, Fang Yu imagined a terrifying scene.

After he joined the sect, one day when he went to the cafeteria to eat with his senior brothers, if all the senior brothers' bodies started resonating with the densely packed [Drill Demons], that scene would be absolutely horrifying! Fang Yu shuddered, but Shi Chuangmei was very happy and said, "If that's the case, I'll have someone bring the medicinal soup."

The medicinal soup arrived, but Fang Yu hesitated.

"What ingredients are in this medicinal soup?"

"This is the master's secret formula, how could I tell you?"

"...Does it contain demonic blood and flesh?"

Shi Chuangmei looked at Fang Yu suspiciously.

"You're not from the Li family trying to steal our sect's formula, are you?"

"No, that's not the case!"

Fang Yu stirred the medicinal soup with his finger and didn't see anything unusual.

It seems the medicinal soup is just ordinary soup.

But Fang Yu was still a bit uneasy.

After all, Shi Chuangmei's body covered in bugs was too terrifying.

He didn't want to become like that either.

After thinking it over, Fang Yu looked at Shi Chuangmei.

"Senior Sister Mei, do other sects also have medicinal soups and meat pills?"

Shi Chuangmei said,

"Medicinal soups are available in every sect, this is the basic medicinal supplement for martial artists. Without this to assist, it would be extremely difficult to enter the martial dao."

"In ancient times, the number of martial artists was small, precisely because there was no such systematic medicinal soup support, ordinary people found it hard to step into the martial dao."

"Now the martial dao is flourishing, and every sect has medicinal soups, at most the formulas have slightly different focuses."

It seems the Yuanxin meat pills are the specialty of your sect.

Fang Yu felt much more at ease.

Thinking back, after drinking the medicinal soup, he didn't see any blood bars popping up in his body, so it should be fine.

Everyone says my talent is poor.

Combined with my relatively advanced age, and the fact that I've already paid the tuition to the Yuanti Sect.

Although I'm not completely broke, but I don't have enough money to join another sect.

And once you've joined a sect and become a disciple, there's no just leaving as you please.

With all these conditions, the best option for Fang Yu in the short term is still here.

Either cultivate martial arts here, or waste time here waiting for the right opportunity to leave the sect.

But that means this period of time will be wasted, allowing other players to catch up.

Since I can see the blood bars, I'll just be extra careful in various aspects.

As for finding Li Qianhuan and exposing the Yuanti Sect, without finding a powerful backer first, that would be courting death.

The Yuanti Sect has been in Tianyuan Town for so long without incident, maybe they have someone backing them up.

Acting rashly would not be appropriate.

No matter what, the key is to strengthen myself, only then can I stand firm.

In this moment, Fang Yu thought through a lot.

After confirming the medicinal soup is basically fine, he drank it down in one gulp.

This is Fang Yu's third bowl of medicinal soup, aside from the free ones.

Worth... 4 days' worth of meat pills! [System Prompt: Player has consumed [Yuanfamily Medicinal Soup], Experience +1.]

Closing his eyes, he focused.

"..."

Failed.

But, no foreign body sensation, no bugs, no blood bars, can continue.

Fang Yu looked at Shi Chuangmei, or made an 'eight' gesture.

The fourth bowl, 8 days' worth of meat pills! Served, drank down.

Closed his eyes, savored it.

Failed, but felt the Qi.

Moreover, the medicinal effect is dissipating faster and faster.

[System Prompt: Player has consumed [Yuanfamily Medicinal Soup], Experience +0.]

No more experience? Fang Yu didn't pay much attention, he was focused on savoring it.

Fang Yu roughly understood why some people can never enter the martial dao.

The medicinal soup can stimulate the body to perceive the external Qi.

But the human body has drug tolerance.

The more times the medicinal soup is consumed, the shorter the perception time provided by the medicinal effect, the more slim the chance of stepping into the martial dao.

"Refill."

"16 taels, that is, 16 days' worth of meat pills."

"Mm."

The fifth bowl was served up.
