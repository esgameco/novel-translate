Chapter 50

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

At the top of the snow-capped mountain, there was an estate.

It was called the Wine and Chess Manor.

The wine was a fiery liquor.

The chess was a deadly game.

Drinking the wine, exhaling fire, tempering the chess pieces, forging weapons.

The people in the manor diligently practiced martial arts, all for one purpose.

The annual Wine and Chess Tournament.

Nearby heroes and champions would flock here, also vying for a share.

One carriage after another crossed the snowy path, leaving a chaotic and colorful trail.

"The disciples of the Horizontal Sword Gate have arrived!"

"The disciples of the Azure Bird Gate have arrived!"

"The disciples of the Interlaced Flower Gate have arrived!"

Ye Hunhong, the young master of the Wine and Chess Manor, was not skilled in martial arts, but enjoyed the lively atmosphere.

Sitting at the entrance, whenever someone entered the manor to participate in the tournament, he would shout to liven up the occasion.

"This year's turnout is more enthusiastic than previous years."

"It's no wonder, the sealing of the Wine and Chess Ancient Tomb is about to be lifted. Whoever can take the top prize will have the chance to enter the tomb."

Even as the young master of the Wine and Chess Manor, Ye Hunhong had never had the qualifications to step into the Wine and Chess Ancient Tomb.

It was a rule set by his ancestors that could not be transgressed.

Suddenly, a fast-moving carriage approached from the distance.

But there was no flag or insignia on the carriage.

"A wandering martial artist?"

Ye Hunhong curiously stretched his neck, and the carriage abruptly stopped, kicking up a large cloud of snow that sprayed all over him.

"Cough cough cough! What kind of driving skills does this carriage driver have... Ughâ€”"

Ye Hunhong suddenly drew in a sharp breath.

What a beautiful woman!

From the carriage stepped out a cold and elegant woman, who tossed ten taels of bloodstained silver to the carriage driver.

The driver humbly accepted the silver and turned the carriage around, driving back down the mountain.

Normally, when disciples from other sects arrived, they would leave their drivers waiting.

After all, going up and down the snowy mountain without a carriage would be very inconvenient.

On any other day, Ye Hunhong would have noticed this subtle irregularity.

But at the moment, he was captivated by the woman.

"Miss, are you also here to participate in the Wine and Chess Tournament?"

Ye Hunhong enthusiastically approached her.

Qi Xiaojin merely gave him a cold glance and continued walking into the manor.

Rejected, Ye Hunhong awkwardly smiled and stopped at the entrance.

Not until Qi Xiaojin had disappeared from sight inside the manor did Ye Hunhong avert his gaze.

He sat down on a rocking chair at the entrance, becoming somewhat distracted.

"A beautiful and virtuous lady, a worthy companion for a gentleman."

"That miss seems to have a cold temperament. Perhaps she would like some ice flower ornaments?"

"Our Wine and Chess Manor has a hot spring on the great snow peak. If I were to tell her about that place, maybe she would have a better impression of me?"

"The tournament lasts for three days. I must not be hasty. As the books say, advance gradually."

The commotion inside the manor seemed to have become more intense.

The sounds could be heard from the sparring grounds all the way to the entrance.

Looks like some formidable fighters have taken the stage today?

Ye Hunhong had little interest in martial arts, so naturally he was not invested in the tournament either, but he enjoyed the lively atmosphere.

In previous years, it was usually after the sparring stage of the tournament that he became most active.

"There's still some time before sunset. When the first day's tournament ends, I'll go look for that miss again..."

Ye Hunhong stood up, beginning to think about what opening lines he should use when meeting the woman.

"Miss, I'm attracted to your beauty... Ptui ptui ptui ptui!"

"Miss, your fingers have calluses, are you skilled in the sword?"

"This line is not bad. I can use it..."

Just as Ye Hunhong was thinking this, a loud shout rang out from inside.

"Kill!!!"

Ye Hunhong was unfazed.

Every year during the tournament, there would be a few sects that liked to issue challenges, shouting louder and louder.

Ye Hunhong was not worried, as someone inside would handle it.

Sure enough, the shouting soon died down.

Ye Hunhong continued to lounge at the entrance, pondering how to win the lady's heart.

Then he noticed another carriage arriving at the manor.

He identified the flag.

"The disciples of the Nine Flames Sect have arrived!"

Ye Hunhong called out perfunctorily.

After getting off the carriage, there were about five or six people. The one leading the group was someone that Ye Hunhong recognized.

"Zhuo Qi, you've come again this year."

"Haha! Last year I lost half a move to you at the Alcohol Chess Manor, this year I must reclaim my ground."

After the initial greetings, Zhuo Qi led his group into the manor.

Ye Hunhong didn't pay much attention to it at first.

But...

Soon after, a heart-wrenching scream suddenly came from inside.

Ye Hunhong was startled and quickly got up.

Looking into the manor, he saw that of the Nine Fire Gang, only three people remained, covered in wounds, frantically rushing out with looks of terror on their faces.

"Run... run quickly!! There's a female devil! Brother Zhuo he's already..."

Whoosh!!

Three azure rays of light flashed by.

The three people running this way suddenly had their backs burst open with blood, and they collapsed to the ground.

The blood slowly seeped out, staining the ground red.

Thud.

Ye Hunhong fell to the ground, looking towards the front.

He saw the woman he had briefly met earlier coldly retract her right hand and turn to walk back into the manor.

It wasn't a walk, but faster than running, her lightness skill clearly exceptional.

"Wait, wait!"

Not knowing where the courage came from, Ye Hunhong chased after her.

Entering the manor, Ye Hunhong was immediately horrified, his eyes wide open.

Inside the manor, there were bodies strewn everywhere! The bodies extended all the way to the arena of the martial arts competition, their numbers reaching a peak there.

Dozens of sects, countless experts, all lay dead at the edges of the arena.

The disciples of the Alcohol Chess Manor had also been wiped out, not a single survivor.

The scorched black marks all around told of the fierce battle that had taken place.

But the victor was only one person - that mysterious woman unstained by blood!

"Red'er..."

Suddenly, a weak voice came from directly in front.

Ye Hunhong focused his gaze and saw his father, the master of the Alcohol Chess Manor, severely wounded and kneeling while embracing a corpse.

His mother's corpse.

Boom! Ye Hunhong's mind went blank, as if he had fallen into a nightmare.

Then, the devil spoke.

"Ye Hunhong?"

Ye Hunhong stared blankly at the woman.

He saw the woman standing behind his father, grasping his father's head with her hand.

Without saying a word, she made a single motion.

Bang!!

In the next instant, his father's head was crushed and exploded open by her grip.

The red blood and white brain matter sprayed high up, yet did not touch even the edge of the woman's clothes.

"Ah..."

"Ahhh..."

"Ahhhhhhh!!"

His head splitting in pain, he knelt on the ground, clutching his head and wailing.

He felt the world spinning around him.

He felt a power about to surge out from within his body.

He recalled the memories from his childhood.

"Red'er, this is the key [Red Candle Heart] that our Alcohol Chess Manor has preserved for generations, which can open the true [Ancient Alcohol Chess Tomb]."

"The Red Candle Heart must be nourished by a human soul to endure."

"This is our honor, and also the blessing of the Spiritual Lord."

The Red Candle Heart, that's right, the power of the Red Candle Heart!

As if hearing Ye Hunhong's call, that power began to surge frantically, filling his entire body.

"Power, the power is surging..."

Bang! Ye Hunhong's head suddenly exploded open by itself.

What emerged was a bright red key. Standing upright on the headless corpse, it spoke in a mechanical voice.

"Charging 100% complete, Spiritual Servant No. 7531, ready to serve. Please enter the authorization password."

Qi Xiaojin, as if having anticipated this, calmly said,

"The Spiritual Lord is about to return, and the opportunity of others can be seized."

"Password correct, now opening the true Ancient Alcohol Chess Tomb."

Rumble, the entire manor shook, and after a while, it finally settled down.
