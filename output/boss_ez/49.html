Chapter 49

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Qi Xiaojin didn't expect this to be the result.

When Old Zhou died in the community, she vaguely sensed the traces of a spirit.

So when she learned that Old Wang had also suddenly died, she rushed over to investigate.

This time, she did find the spirit.

But she discovered that thing was too powerful for her to defeat! "The Deathly Stillness Spirit..."

"How could this demonic thing appear here?"

"In my past life memories, Fang Yu once told me that after the apocalypse came, the neighboring East Gate City was not destroyed by nuclear bombs."

"But rather, all the people in the entire city, at the same time, were suddenly turned into a bloody mist and died."

"The real reason was that the Deathly Stillness Spirit, which had grown to its full power, wiped out the entire city's population in an instant!"

"The entire city, nearly ten million people, were exterminated in a moment, turning it into a dead city."

"After receiving the news, they were so frightened that they bombed it with nuclear weapons, sealing and isolating it, forming the ruins of a dead city."

Qi Xiaojin gnawed on her fingers anxiously, a habit left over from her past life.

"How could a spirit of this level appear so early!"

"The game has just started, and the apocalypse is at least a full year away!"

"Did the Deathly Stillness Spirit already exist in the past life, or has the world line really changed because of my rebirth, triggering the butterfly effect?"

"Also, this should be the spirit that caused the neighboring East Gate City to be wiped out overnight, so why did it appear in our Jiangnan City?"

Qi Xiaojin didn't know the answers, she only knew the current situation was very tricky.

"Calm down, Qi Xiaojin!"

"Think about Fang Yu! Think about what he has sacrificed to save you!"

"A mere Deathly Stillness Spirit, how can it make you waver like this!"

"The days to come are still long, the apocalypse, the struggle between humans and monsters, the destruction of spirits and gods..."

Phew--

Qi Xiaojin suddenly stopped, took a deep breath, and exhaled heavily.

"Jie Jie?"

Fang Yu stopped, puzzled.

Just seeing Fang Yu's adorable appearance softened Qi Xiaojin's heart.

Yes.

There's no need to rush.

I still have time.

Fang Yu is by my side, he hasn't left at all.

Protecting an entire city may be difficult.

But guarding one person is much simpler.

"I'm fine," Qi Xiaojin shook her head slightly, her gaze gentle.

"Let's go faster, oh right, shall we have lunch at my place?"

Go to Jie Jie's house for lunch?

Fang Yu immediately shook his head vigorously like a rattle.

Fang Yu had been there before! On the first day he moved in, Fang Yu's initial impression of Jie Jie was that she was beautiful, sweet-voiced, so gentle and cute, practically the dream image of a girl-next-door.

He had fantasized about a beautiful scenario that he shouldn't have.

When Jie Jie proposed a celebratory meal for the new tenant, with the landlord providing a free meal, Fang Yu immediately took the bait.

Until he personally tasted Jie Jie's cooking, blushing and forcing himself to swallow the half-raw fish meat.

The charred black pork, the potatoes as hard as stones, the salty dancing eggs on the tongue.

Fang Yu could still vividly recall the dishes from that day.

That night, he went back and vomited three times, had diarrhea all night.

Ever since then, whenever Jie Jie said she was treating him to a meal, Fang Yu would pretend not to hear it.

This time would be no exception.

"That, Jie Jie, I'm in a career development period, I'm busy!"

Fang Yu showed an appeasing smile.

"Then next time."

Qi Xiaojin's eyes were smiling, not pressing Fang Yu.

"He doesn't know yet, how much hardship we've been through together in the apocalypse."

"I've also learned a lot of cooking skills because of that."

"Next time when you eat the food I make, you'll be in for a shock."

Fang Yu's presence was like a healing medicine, warming Qi Xiaojin's heart.

They had arrived at the community, Qi Xiaojin watched Fang Yu get on the elevator, and her gentle expression slowly receded, becoming calm and exuding a cold, aloof aura.

"Humans have seven souls and six corporeal souls."

"The soul of destiny is outside the cycle, the cycle soul is the beginning."

"The cycle, thought, emptiness, heart, pearl, wisdom."

"If I'm not mistaken, the power of the young lifeless spirit should be between the [thought level] and the [emptiness level]."

"In its complete form, according to the previous life's Fang Yu's conjecture, it has likely already opened the seven souls and is a [six corporeal souls] level monster."

"Growth, too fast!"

Qi Xiaojin felt a sense of urgency.

"The lifeless spirit will not dissipate even if the host dies."

"There are many types of spirits, and only believers and demons can destroy this kind of spirit."

"The lifeless spirit can initially only show the host a 24-hour countdown to death."

"As it grows, this countdown will gradually increase."

"The lifeless spirit that destroyed East Gate City, how long of a countdown could the hosts in the entire city see? Ten days? Or a month?"

Qi Xiaojin couldn't quite remember.

After the apocalypse, death was just a number, and the city's destruction was just another news story.

At the time, she had been struggling just to survive, let alone worry about other things.

"Who would have thought I could be reborn..."

Qi Xiaojin gnawed on her fingers, gradually forming an idea.

"My alcohol flower spirit is only at the cycle level."

"The cycle, that is the cycle soul.

"The cycle soul is like a grinding wheel, tempering the body, refining the spirit, laying the foundation to contain stronger spirits."

"Therefore, I am still far from being a match for the lifeless spirit."

"I need time to grow!"

Qi Xiaojin turned and walked down the hallway towards the security room.

"It has been confirmed that the two hosts of the lifeless spirit are both at Twin Babies Hospital."

"The dispute between Mr. Wang's family and the hospital over his death won't be resolved anytime soon."

"For now, the countdown should still be on the two hosts. Before it spreads to Mr. Wang's family members, I must drive out the entire Wang family and perform the separation."

"Additionally, I need to hire people to seal the entrances and exits, and anyone who has entered Twin Babies Hospital is not allowed to enter the community."

"The community's security is unreliable. I must hire a private security team to be responsible for the community's security and strictly enforce my orders."

The lifeless spirit cannot be killed now.

Once the lifeless spirit grows to a certain stage, anyone who has ever come into contact with its hosts will be infected.

That is, on the first night Mr. Zhou died, the entire community had already been infected and become backup hosts.

The only difference is when the lifeless spirit will mature enough to activate these backup hosts.

"Seize every moment, become stronger!"

After instructing the security guards and temporarily setting up a cordon, Qi Xiaojin immediately called a private security company, using her financial power to finalize an agreement.

Tomorrow, people will come to negotiate the contract.

Back home, lying on the bed and preparing to put on the game helmet to enter the game, Qi Xiaojin suddenly thought of something.

"This life, barring any accidents, Fang Yu should still be born in Tianyuan Town."

"Three months later, Tianyuan Town will be destroyed by demons, leaving no trace."

"Should I go there for a visit?"

Qi Xiaojin frowned, thinking, then shook her head slightly.

"Inappropriate, the flapping of a butterfly's wings will cause huge changes."

"The demon's massacre of the city is both a crisis and an opportunity."

"In the previous life, Fang Yu was able to fight his way out of that environment and become so powerful."

"This opportunity, I should not interfere with it."

Thinking of Fang Yu, Qi Xiaojin's somewhat excited mood calmed down.

"Without a doubt, Fang Yu will also become a powerful believer this time."

"And I, having entered the game earlier this life, have already gained this ability."

"Soon, we will fight side by side as in the previous life!"

Thinking of the previous life, people called her and Fang Yu, the two believers, "divine lovers".

Qi Xiaojin's ears started to flush slightly.

"Enter the game! Become stronger!"

Putting on the helmet.

The game begins!...
