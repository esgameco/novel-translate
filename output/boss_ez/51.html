In...

Still in! What is this thing? What will happen when the countdown ends? The nurse is very anxious and afraid.

An hour ago, she followed behind Dr. Zhang and confronted the medical protesters.

Then, she suddenly saw the countdown.

At the time, it was [24 hours 02 minutes 15 seconds].

She was stunned, thinking her eyes were playing tricks on her.

But after rubbing her eyes a few times, the countdown was still there, decreasing second by second.

She finally panicked and told Dr. Zhang, but Dr. Zhang was busy arguing with the protesting patient and had no time for her.

The nurse could only go find a psychiatrist for help.

After a series of tests, the final result was that due to the intense dispute between the doctor and patient, she had experienced hallucinations, and was preliminarily diagnosed with mild mental disorder.

Then, she got a slip, took leave early, and went home to rest.

"Hallucination... is it really a hallucination?"

The nurse was distraught.

If she was diagnosed with mental disorder, she might lose this job. Her husband only makes two or three thousand a month, how would they get by without her income from this big hospital?

"Honey?"

Suddenly, the mirror reflected the figure of a man.

That was her husband, pot-bellied, with an ordinary face that made one feel nauseous.

Her husband seemed to be chewing on something, and the anxious nurse didn't look closely.

The next moment, the nurse's pupils suddenly contracted, and her whole body instantly stiffened, as if falling into an icy pit.

She saw her husband chewing and swallowing, behaving like an animal, regurgitating a blood-stained human finger! The nurse's body trembled, her eyes wide open.

She couldn't be mistaken.

As a nurse, she had seen too many patients in the hospital, she knew what a human finger looked like even with her eyes closed.

Most importantly, there was a wedding ring on that finger, the same ring they had exchanged.

As if the reason her husband couldn't chew it down was because of this.

With a "ptui", he spat out the ring, and the "husband" looked up and saw the nurse's reaction in the mirror.

He first paused, then slowly revealed a smile.

"You... saw it?"

At these words, the nurse's whole body stiffened, unable to move, tears streaming down her face.

It was her husband's face and body, but if there was anything different, it was the finger that usually wore the ring, which had a strong sense of incongruity compared to the other fingers.

As if that finger had become thinner and more slender.

No! Wait!

It changed!

The nurse opened her mouth wide, covering her mouth in disbelief.

She saw that thin finger, as if filled with muscle, slowly swelling up and blending in with the surrounding fingers, becoming normal again.

The nurse looked up in terror at the mirror, at her husband.

Her husband had somehow teleported behind her, his mouth gaping open wide, like a bloody maw capable of swallowing the nurse's head in one bite.

Terrified, she turned around, only to see her husband's mouth split open, black and cavernous. The nurse let out a shrill scream, rolled her eyes back, and fainted to the floor.

【These humans are so weak.】

Black words appeared on the husband's belly.

All the people on the street, like a self-service buffet, could be eaten however they wanted.

These humans had no defense against demons! What a wonderful world! The parchment controlled the fat middle-aged man's body, lifting the nurse's face, ready to bite down.

Suddenly, it paused.

Using the human nose, it sniffed the woman closely.

In the next instant, it recoiled!
