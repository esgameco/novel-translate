Fang Yu opened his eyes.

Then, he found that a thin blanket had been placed over him.

The fabric of the blanket was new, but there were some footprints and dust on it.

It should be the new blanket that his second sister bought yesterday.

His second sister didn't even want to use it herself, but she covered him with it.

When the character logged out of the game, he wasn't covered with a blanket.

This means that his second sister has already woken up.

Getting up and leaving the room, a scent of cooked food wafted over.

"Second sister?"

"I'm here, the food will be ready soon, just wait a bit."

Fang Yu was a bit surprised.

After a few meals at home, it seemed they had only been eating porridge, but he didn't expect his second sister to be able to cook.

Thinking about it, Fang Yu understood.

Previously, they had only been eating porridge because they didn't have enough money.

That watery porridge was just rice water, just enough to fill their stomachs.

According to his second sister's past memories, they used to eat a bit better, but it was just adding some wilted vegetable leaves when cooking the porridge.

The eldest brother was a tea house worker, ensuring a basic living standard.

The second sister was a temporary worker at a cloth workshop, occasionally earning money to improve their quality of life, determining the upper limit of their living.

As for him, Jiao De Yi, he was definitely the one dragging them down.

Idle and without any skills, no one wanted to hire him, the one contributing the least to the family.

But now, everything has gotten better.

The house has been cleaned up by the second sister, becoming much cleaner.

This made Fang Yu feel a bit embarrassed.

At this time, the dishes were also ready, it was stir-fried pork and cabbage.

The abundance was comparable to luxury.

Hmm, compared to the character's previous experiences.

"Jiao De Yi, do you remember what I told you last night?"

The second sister said while eating.

You told me so many things last night, which one are you talking about?

But Fang Yu still nodded.

"I remember."

After all, he just remembered! You just tell me what it is.

Sure enough, the second sister sighed.

"Big brother has been missing for so many days, I'm really worried about his safety. That [Investigation Agency], their reputation is really good, I want to hire them to investigate big brother's whereabouts."

Saying this, the second sister looked at Fang Yu cautiously, even putting down her chopsticks, her expression tense.

Fang Yu was a bit puzzled by his second sister's behavior.

But he soon reacted.

"Is it expensive?"

"...Yes."

The second sister lowered her head, her voice as soft as a mosquito.

Having money can certainly allow the two of them to live a good life.

But without the most important family member by their side, can this money be spent with a clear conscience? The second sister didn't know, this kind of problem was too complicated.

She just hoped that the big brother could come back alive and reunite with them.

"I remember you said before that you had posted notices on the streets and set up a reward, but it didn't work, right?"

"A few people came while you were sleeping, but they were just false information." The second sister shook her head, her eyes red.

"How much?"

"What?"

"The price of the [Investigation Agency]."

The second sister first showed joy, then said cautiously, "One tael..."

Ah! Fang Yu waved his hand and placed one tael on the table.

Then he heard the second sister continue.

"...per day."

Fang Yu: ???"Are they made of gold or silver? Should I just join their [Investigation Agency]??"

The second sister hurriedly covered her head, weakly saying, "They, they have a very good reputation. Last time, when Aunt Zhang's cat went missing, they were the ones who helped find it."

Aunt Zhang was a wealthy lady that the second sister did temporary work for at the cloth workshop.

No wonder the second sister knew about this expensive place.

Fang Yu felt that this [Investigation Agency] was definitely problematic, being so expensive.

But seeing the second sister's eyes filled with tears, he couldn't help but sigh.

He took out another two taels.

"3 taels. Let them investigate for three days and see."

Three days, in three days, I don't even know if the character will still be alive.

Even the inspection at the Fool's Realm two days later could take his life at any moment.

"Jiao De Yi! I knew you were the best!"

It's best to invest in the [Elemental Body Two-Zero Sword] first.

Without the matching weapon, even if he mastered the [Elemental Body Two-Zero Sword], it wouldn't be of much use, he wouldn't be able to fully display it.

Physique and spirit didn't need to be increased either, so the remaining points could only be used on...

[Name: Diao Deyi.]

The Elemental Body Stabilizing Technique could still be upgraded after reaching the Pinnacle of Refinement?

[System Prompt: This upgrade will consume 2 Attribute Points.]

Fang Yu thought about it, the Elemental Body Stabilizing Technique had always been improving his overall physical qualities, with the hands being the focus of this improvement.

This thing could be invested in.

The main thing was that he wanted to see what effect the Elemental Body Stabilizing Technique would have after upgrading with 2 Attribute Points.

He allocated the points.

[Elemental Body Stabilizing Technique [Pinnacle of Refinement] → Breakthrough to Flower Realm Intermediate.]
