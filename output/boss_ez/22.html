Li Qianxuan's recommendation letter turned out to be more useful than Fang Yu had expected.

After entering the Yuanti Martial Arts Hall, he found that it was quite large.

From the archery dummies to the training wooden posts, the venue was spacious and well-organized, with a full range of equipment.

Some people were practicing against each other, their fists colliding fiercely.

Others were striking the wooden posts, their techniques flowing smoothly, inspiring admiration.

Following Shi Mei, Fang Yu finally arrived at a grand hall and saw the head of the Yuanti Martial Arts Hall, Yuan Hongxin.

He was a middle-aged man in his forties, wearing a gray cloth robe with the character "Yuan" embroidered in the center.

Sitting in the chair like the head of the household, he slowly sipped his tea and glanced at the contents of the letter.

"Disciple, the person is here."

"Mm."

Shi Mei was about to leave, but Yuan Hongxin called her back.

"Shi Mei, we're not accepting any new disciples today. Go and disperse the people at the entrance."

Shi Mei was taken aback.

It had just started, and they were already not accepting new disciples? Shi Mei glanced at Fang Yu, feeling that the matter was likely related to him.

Obeying the order, she left.

The hall was left with only Yuan Hongxin and Fang Yu.

Fang Yu took a look at Yuan Hongxin's health bar.

【Yuan Hongxin: 350/350】

Such a high health value! Even stronger than Li Qianxuan!

Fang Yu quickly adopted a humble posture.

"I've read the letter, and since you are a person recommended by the Li family, I will accept you."

"The rules remain the same: a tuition fee of 10 taels, and a monthly basic living expense of 2 taels afterward."

"The martial arts hall will provide a secret medicinal soup every month."

"If you want more, you'll need to pay extra."

"As for learning martial arts, the first manual is free, and each subsequent one costs 5 taels of silver."

"Any questions?"

Yes, this is really heartless! Fang Yu had thought he was quite wealthy, but coming to the martial arts hall had already peeled off a layer of his skin.

But on the surface, he immediately performed the master-disciple ritual.

"Disciple Diao Deyi is willing to enter the Yuanti Martial Arts Hall."

Paying the money, offering tea, the ceremony was complete.

Fang Yu had officially become a disciple.

Yuan Hongxin tossed a booklet to Fang Yu.

"Mm, this is the 'Yuanti Fundamental Technique,' the core martial art of our Yuanti Martial Arts Hall. It's the free martial arts manual included in the tuition fee. Study it first, and come talk to me when you've made progress."

To be honest, Yuan Hongxin didn't have high hopes for Fang Yu.

Starting martial arts at the age of 16 was too late.

Sure enough, Fang Yu took the 'Yuanti Fundamental Technique' and seemed to have trouble understanding it even after half a day.

Just then, Shi Mei came in with the secret medicinal soup.

"Shi Mei, you stay and teach him."

"Yes."

Yuan Hongxin gave the order and left, truly acting like a hands-off boss.
