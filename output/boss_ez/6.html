"Lord Qi Shi, please speak up for me, that Diao De Yi..."

The young man was about to continue, but Li Qian Xuan raised a finger to his lips, gesturing him to be quiet.

The young man immediately fell silent.

If Fang Yu couldn't see the health bars, he would have thought Li Qian Xuan was the real monster.

[Li Bai Gu: 785.2/1000]

Glancing at the young man's health, apart from the two hundred points lost when his arms were broken, the rest was just a drizzle.

Fortunately, these people could keep this guy in check, or else Fang Yu would have had to start over.

Li Qian Xuan rode his horse past the two of them and approached Tu Gou Dan, who was currently pinned to the ground, his leg pierced by an arrow.

[Tu Gou Dan: 2/8]

The arrow didn't kill Tu Gou Dan, but it had immobilized him, causing him to let out cries of pain, completely losing the crazed momentum he had earlier.

"Stop howling. I saw you just now, rampaging on the street and chasing that person. Do you have any defense?"

Li Qian Xuan sat on his horse, looking down at Tu Gou Dan with a sidelong glance.

"I was just punishing the evil..."

Swish!!

Before Tu Gou Dan could finish his sentence, a sword flashed across his neck, and his head rolled to the ground.

So fast!

Fang Yu didn't even blink, and the sword had already passed.

[Tu Gou Dan: 0/8]

Blood splattered everywhere, and the onlookers let out a chorus of startled cries. But the other people from the Foolish Underworld seemed unfazed, simply letting Tu Gou Dan's headless body fall to the ground. Dealing with the corpse was not their responsibility; someone else would handle the aftermath.

Li Qian Xuan then rode his horse to stand in front of Fang Yu and the young man in white.

"Now, it's your turn. You first, and remember, I don't like to hear nonsense, so be concise and clear."

Li Qian Xuan's gaze was fixed on Fang Yu.

Fang Yu felt his spirits lift. Although he didn't kneel, his backbone had been recognized by someone.

"Let me speak, Lord Qi!"

"I have two points to make. First, the owner and all the employees of the pharmacy, including Wang Er and the others, were all killed by Li Bai Gu!"

"Second, Li Bai Gu is pretending to be a harmless person, but his identity is definitely suspicious!"

Fang Yu took the initiative, directly stating the key points.

Li Qian Xuan only slightly furrowed his brow, then asked a question.

"You've said it well, but I have a question for you. How could a young man who just had his arms broken manage to kill more than a dozen people at the pharmacy?"

"So, he must have been hiding his true strength! Lord Qi, it's like this..."

"Enough, make him shut up."

Slap! Just as Fang Yu was about to continue, a hand reached out and struck him.
