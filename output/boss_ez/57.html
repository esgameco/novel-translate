Wasn't it just the Yuan Ti Liang Ling Sword? He learned it? He actually learned it! Yuan Hong-xin couldn't believe it, it was astonishing!

[Flower-level skill: Yuan Ti Liang Ling Sword [Initial Mastery (6/100)]]

[Experience value: 60/100.]

Boring, boring. With 11 attribute points in hand, Fang Yu had no patience for this slow-paced practice.

Of course, it's still necessary to...

Deep blue, add points! [Attribute points: 11 → 10.]

[Yuan Ti Liang Ling Sword [Initial Mastery] → Yuan Ti Liang Ling Sword [Slight Mastery].]

The understanding of the sword techniques instantly flooded his mind.

His body was as if it had practiced the sword techniques countless times, every move and form etched in his memory.

Slight Mastery!

At this moment, the master spoke.

"The sword technique you just practiced, was it the Yuan Ti Liang Ling Sword?"

"Yes."

"... Perform it from the beginning for me to see."

"Huh?"

"What 'huh'? Just do it."

"Okay..."

Just now, he had only seen the middle part of the sword technique demonstration.

Yuan Hong-xin couldn't believe his eyes. Based on his assessment of Diao De-yi's martial arts aptitude yesterday, Diao De-yi should have been a trash talent, only proficient in one specialized skill, the Yuan Ti Gu Ben Gong.

But after just one day, he could even pick up the Yuan Ti Liang Ling Sword? He had already shown no talent on the first day!

Yuan Hong-xin was very skeptical, very skeptical! Then, he saw Fang Yu start to perform the sword dance.

The sword movements were very fast, but after a complete set, Yuan Hong-xin was shocked again!

Because if the first time he saw Fang Yu's Yuan Ti Liang Ling Sword, it gave him the feeling of [Initial Mastery].

Then this time, the feeling was [Slight Mastery]!

The sword techniques don't lie.

That fluency, the understanding of the techniques, the natural body coordination, couldn't be faked.

This was the [Slight Mastery] of the Yuan Ti Liang Ling Sword!

From [Initial Mastery] to [Slight Mastery] in just one round.

This progress directly shocked Yuan Hong-xin.

"You, are you really Diao De-yi?"

He even suspected that Diao De-yi was possessed by a demon!

But this half-joking remark from Yuan Hong-xin made Fang Yu a bit nervous.

"Master, I am who I am, how could there be a fake?"

That was true.

After all, apart from practicing the sword techniques quickly, there was no other difference.

"Alright, perform the sword technique for me one more time."

"...Master, you're just teasing me."

"I told you to practice, so practice."

Fang Yu had no choice but to practice again.

This time, Fang Yu practiced while Yuan Hong-xin guided him.

Then, Yuan Hong-xin confirmed it.

Diao De-yi, was still Diao De-yi.

The [Slight Mastery] of the Yuan Ti Liang Ling Sword was real.

But the talent, was still that foolish talent.

The understanding of the entire sword technique, the entire entirety, was stuck at the [Slight Mastery] stage, without the slightest bit of expanded thinking.

Dull-witted!

Yuanni, to enhance physique.
