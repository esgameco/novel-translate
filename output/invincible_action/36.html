The internal force surged wildly. In an instant, it was as if hundreds of true dragons were roaring around, soaring out and shaking everything.

The remaining six Yingniang Kings bore the brunt of this palm strike and were all knocked back by it.

And this was the effect Ying Xiaowanted. Employing the Lingbo Micro-Steps in tandem with the Dragon-Shaped Internal Force, brief bloodstains appeared on his pristine Heavenly Questioning Sword as two more figures fell to the ground.

They were the Blade Yingniang King and the Withered Wood Yingniang King.

"How is this possible...how is this possible..." The remaining four Yingniang Kings were all terrified, howling incessantly in the darkness. This was the first time they had felt fear.

They could not believe that after going through such a battle, Ying Xiao still possessed such formidable power.

The three waves of arrow and spear attacks - that kind of assault could breach a city's defenses, could annihilate three thousand iron cavalry.

And after that, there were over a hundred Night Yingniang assassins.

At this moment, they could not accept it. They had fallen into the abyss of terrified despair.

The Yingniang Kings, the strongest assassins of the Night Yingniang - in the past, they were the ones who struck fear into their enemies, because the night belonged only to them. They were the kings of death in the darkness.

When the Night Yingniang crowed at midnight, who could survive until dawn?

But now, fear was sprouting in every part of their bodies.

Ying Xiao's combat power, his methods, his martial arts - everything made them think of one realm: the legendary Terrestrial Immortals.

What were Terrestrial Immortals? They looked down upon all mortals. No matter how powerful a mortal was, before a Terrestrial Immortal, they were like ants.

"You...just what realm have you reached?" The Death Soul Yingniang King asked tremblingly.

"What realm has this king reached? The sword strike coming next will tell you. Of course, it remains to be seen if you four can withstand it," Ying Xiao said coldly. At this moment, he was the true soul of death. His dispassionate voice seemed to come from the depths of hell itself, surrounded by boundless sword auras that seemed to manifest a world of blades.

"Myriad Swords Return to the Source!"

The Heavenly Questioning Sword led the way as countless transforming sword images followed behind.

"Ahhh!"

"Ahhh!"

"Ahhhhh!"

Wretched cries rang out as four more figures fell to the ground.

"The seven Yingniang Kings were indeed formidable, but still not enough before this king," Ying Xiao said coldly. "The Chancellor wanted to kill this king, yet still hoped to preserve me - that was indeed a bit naive."

Swish!

Suddenly, a sickle flew towards Chilian.

Ever vigilant, Chilian immediately swung his Red Viper Sword to deflect the flying sickle.

Ying Xiao also struck out at that moment.

"I underestimated you, Death Soul Yingniang King. To have survived under Myriad Swords Return to the Source."

Boom! Boom! Boom!

But then another change occurred. In the originally pitch-black palace, streaks of fire suddenly ignited, instantly illuminating the entire palace. The palace floor trembled as countless small mechanical azure dragons emerged.

Looking closely, they were miniature mechanical azure dragons. Some quickly merged to form larger mechanical azure dragons, spewing out a torrent of projectiles towards Ying Xiao. Three of them even coiled around him.

"The Mohist Azure Dragons!" Ying Xiao's voice was utterly cold as he was forced to deal with these mechanical dragons first.

Without Ying Xiao's help, Chilian was quickly overwhelmed. Although the Death Soul Yingniang King was severely injured, with the aid of the mechanical azure dragons, he swiftly suppressed Chilian.

A sickle slashed across Chilian's wrist, making him drop the Red Viper Sword. Chilian was then bound by one of the large mechanical azure dragons, immobilized.

No matter how potent Chilian's poison was, it was useless against the mechanical azure dragons.
