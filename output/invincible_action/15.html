What shocked and sobered Bai Feng was not that Ying Xiao had caught up with him.

Moreover, he didn't know how Ying Xiao had managed to catch up.

"How is this possible..." Bai Feng muttered to himself, immediately holding nothing back and advancing at full speed.

However, no matter how fast he went, every time he looked back, Ying Xiao was always by his side.

"Wind Feather!"

Bai Feng was already using his ultimate move.

"Cloud Ladder!" Ying Xiao spoke calmly, always staying ahead of Bai Feng with ease. If Ying Xiao wished, he could leave Bai Feng in the dust in the blink of an eye.

"It seems the Grand Commander was right. Even trying a thousand more times would yield the same result. This helpless gap in power... and it's in the speed I take pride in." Bai Feng gave up the contest and sighed helplessly.

"You are already very good," Ying Xiao said. "The so-called feeling of powerlessness is only because you are facing me, the King."

Amidst overlapping mountain ranges, a thick fog obscured the view.

A giant white bird descended from the sky, landing on a cliff.

"Your subordinate, Meng Yi!"

"Gong Shu Chou!"

"Greetings to Your Highness, the Martial King!"

As Ying Xiao dismounted, valiant and extraordinary generals, a half-man half-machine elder, and a group of elite Shadow Guards all knelt down.

Wei Zhuang and Chi Lian also showed their respect for Ying Xiao, nodding at him.

Wei Zhuang spoke, "Your Highness arrives at just the right time. A good show is about to unfold."

Gathering internal energy in his eyes, Ying Xiao looked in the direction of Wei Zhuang's sharp gaze. Through layers of thick fog, he saw frenzied lightning energy in the dense forest, like a storm.

"The Mohist Iron Hammer, Thunder God Hammer Second Layer - Power of the Thunder God," Ying Xiao said. "Besides that, there is another frenzied giant force, the Peerless Demon."

"However, the Peerless Demon is not the protagonist. The hidden bats of Reverse Flow Sand and that Black Qilin are the main characters. As night falls, it's their time to take the stage."

"Or rather, the Black Qilin is the sole protagonist."

"Dark moon, cold wind, invisible reaper, ever-changing and inexplicable, the Ink Jade Qilin."

Hearing Ying Xiao's words, Wei Zhuang's sharp eyes were filled with shock. He felt once again that he had no secrets before Ying Xiao. He said in a deep voice, "Your Highness is very familiar with the Flow Sand, as if you had foreseen this scene."

"When leaving Xianyang, I said that this jianghu is dominated by me," Ying Xiao smiled. It was a gentle smile, but in Wei Zhuang's eyes, it seemed like the most terrifying sword.

Then, Ying Xiao's gaze fell on Meng Yi.

"General Meng, why have you come?"

"Your Highness, your subordinate is now a member of Tian Xing," Meng Yi responded. "These Shadow Guards are no longer Shadow Guards, but people of Tian Xing."

"Very well. Tian Xing is newly established and indeed needs you." Ying Xiao nodded.

"Tian Xing..." Wei Zhuang murmured.

"After all, you were once the strongest weapon to stop punishment with punishment."

"However, even as the strongest weapon, it's still a waste of your talents."

"The place that can truly accommodate Sir Wei Zhuang is the vast battlefield," Ying Xiao continued. "The Xiongnu in the north, the Baiyue in the south, a battlefield of ten thousand miles with clashing armies. Doesn't Sir Wei Zhuang want to see it?"

"But I digress. Night is about to fall..."

At this moment, Wei Zhuang's expression had clearly changed.
