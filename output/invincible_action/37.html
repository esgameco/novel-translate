Here is my translation of the excerpt from the Chinese novel:

"Your Highness, don't worry about me," Chiliang said resolutely, though restrained. As a former assassin from the Flowing Sands, she had envisioned scenarios like this countless times in her mind.

The determination in her eyes signaled her acceptance of the impending death.

Chiliang did not think Prince Xingxiao would spare her life just because she was captured.

But this time, Chiliang was mistaken. The moment the Ghost Skylark King spoke, Xingxiao's heavenly sword dropped to the ground.

This scene shocked Chiliang.

"Your...Your Highness, you..." Chiliang's beautiful eyes trembled as she stuttered in disbelief.

Xingxiao put down his heavenly sword without any hesitation.

"Your Highness..." Chiliang murmured in a daze, still unwilling to believe it despite the reality before her eyes.

In her dazed state, she wondered if it were Weizhuang instead, he might care about her life, but surely he would not put down his shark-tooth sword.

This scene also shocked the Ghost Skylark King.

To be honest, the Ghost Skylark King never expected to truly threaten Xingxiao with Chiliang's life - it was just a dying counterattack, a last-ditch effort.

Xingxiao was the king, the ruler of the vast Qin Empire, and deeply trusted by the First Emperor. Compared to the chancellor Li Si, Xingxiao was truly peerless, able to have any woman he desired. How could he give up success and life itself for a mere Chiliang?

But now...

"Hahaha!"

"Hahaha!"

After regaining his composure, the Ghost Skylark King laughed uproariously. "I never imagined the mighty Martial Prince of Qin, the one most trusted by the First Emperor himself, would forsake his life for a woman."

"Your Highness, you have truly opened my eyes."

"Ghost Skylark King, enough talk. Release her, and I promise you won't die," Xingxiao said calmly. "Otherwise, I have ten thousand ways to make you wish you were dead."

"Your Highness, it seems I control the situation now. With that tone, this Red Lotus Princess might just..." The Ghost Skylark King sneered coldly.

Swish!

The iron chains in the Ghost Skylark King's hands contracted, and the sickle opened a bloody gash on Chiliang's jade-like neck.

"Very well, I'll do as you say," Xingxiao immediately replied.

"Hahaha, Your Highness, that's more like it. Only then will this peerless beauty remain unharmed," the arrogant Ghost Skylark King cackled, savoring the moment with a vicious hissing laugh. "Now, Your Highness, withdraw all your internal energy and let the mechanical Azure Dragons bind you."

"Your Highness, don't listen to him. Just take action - as an assassin, I've long known this day would come," Chiliang shouted, trying to stop Xingxiao.

But Xingxiao ignored Chiliang and followed the Ghost Skylark King's instructions, withdrawing all his internal energy.

Clank clank clank!

Without the protection of his internal energy, two mechanical Azure Dragons immediately bound Xingxiao - one coiling from above, the other from below, intertwining around his body. Numerous smaller mechanical Azure Dragons also covered Xingxiao's entire body, interconnecting like chains, locking his every joint.

Moreover, these small Azure Dragons had needles protruding from their mouths, sealing all of Xingxiao's accupressure points.

Thus, Xingxiao was rendered unable to circulate his internal energy...
