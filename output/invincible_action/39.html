Here is my translation of the excerpt from the Chinese novel:

The power erupted from the ruler's staff, engulfing Ying Xiao. It numbed and suppressed Ying Xiao, creating the perfect opportunity for the Tianzhijiao Master and Shang Tongjiao Master to land a fatal blow.

"Great Transposition of the Universe!"

Ying Xiao shouted loudly, employing the Great Transposition of the Universe technique to greatly shift the Tianzhijiao Master's internal energy. He also activated his Jingang Buxie ability again, allowing the remaining thunderous internal energy to strike his body, then borrowing the Tianzhijiao Master's thunderous internal energy to rapidly retreat backwards.

Step step step!

Ying Xiao heavily retreated dozens of meters before Chi Lian supported him to a stop.

"The strength of these three old fellows is truly astounding to this king," Ying Xiao said with narrowed eyes. "This king has underestimated them. Compared to them, Ye Juanjuan is indeed just an ant."

"Your Highness, you have indeed not yet reached the half-step Ludianshenxian realm. Why not surrender? We promise to give Your Highness a quick death," the Tianzhijiao Master said disdainfully. "Otherwise, Your Highness's precious body may not even be able to be brought back to Xianyang for burial."

"In that case, the August Emperor would only be able to build an empty grave mound for Your Highness. The supreme ruler who unified all under heaven would be so heartbroken!"

"The internal energy of the half-step Ludianshenxian realm has indeed suppressed this king, but it has only suppressed me," Ying Xiao calmly replied, twisting his neck. "If the internal energy cultivated over two hundred years cannot be fully unleashed, it is ultimately useless."

As he spoke, Ying Xiao began comprehending again.

[Ding! Host recalls the Star Soul Blade, comprehending the Six Paths Divine Sword!]

In the novel The Heaven Sword and Dragon Saber, Duan Yu was able to cripple Mu Rong Fu with just his half-baked Six Paths Divine Sword, and even Qiao Feng admitted he could not block the Six Paths Divine Sword.

The Six Paths Divine Sword that Ying Xiao comprehended far surpassed the original.

Therefore, Ying Xiao was confident he could defeat these three Mohist Masters.

"Fall back, do not interfere in the coming battle," Ying Xiao firmly instructed Chi Lian, tossing him the Heaven Questioning Sword.

"Your Highness, you don't need the Heaven Questioning Sword?" Chi Lian asked anxiously.

"The pinnacle of swordsmanship is often to be unarmed and defeat the armed," Ying Xiao said. "Rest assured, since I brought you here, I will take you away. This is my promise."

Hearing Ying Xiao's words, the three Mohist Masters could not help but look at each other, all three pairs of eyes narrowing.

Being unarmed and defeating the armed - they knew of and had experienced this realm of swordsmanship before, and had been defeated by it.

At this moment, the name "Guiguzi" echoed in their minds again. If Ying Xiao had truly reached this realm of swordsmanship, with his current strength, he absolutely had the ability to kill them.

"Your Highness, some words should not be spoken lightly. Being unarmed and defeating the armed - this master does not believe a martial artist of the Celestial Human realm could reach that," Shang Tong said harshly.

"The three of you, your tones have changed. It seems in your hearts, I am not an ordinary Celestial Human realm martial artist," Ying Xiao said coldly. "And from your expressions, I can see you have faced such swordsmanship before."

"Perhaps you were also defeated quite miserably..."

Hearing the words "quite miserably", the three Mohist Masters' expressions turned ferocious for the first time, their ferocious gazes wishing they could immediately tear Ying Xiao's body into shreds.
