The degree would increase exponentially.

"Your Highness, there is no need to manage your subordinates at this moment," Red Luan said again.

Two falcon kings stood on either side. One held a knife in his left hand and a sword in his right, known as the Sword and Blade Falcon King. The other held a bluish-green sword that resembled a wooden sword, known as the Withered Wood Falcon King.

"I never imagined that among the Night Falcons there would actually be such highly skilled masters, and eighteen of them no less," Xiao Xian said in shock. "Minister Li has hidden them deeply."

"It seems this Night Falcon organization has existed for a long time. After all, to forge a truly lethal sword requires at least ten years of grinding."

"Then where are the other fifteen?"

"If only you three want to kill me, it is far from enough."

"The Night Falcons are not a web. We never underestimate anyone, but Your Highness, for you to demand all eighteen Falcon Kings to assemble is too arrogant. Even the legendary celestial immortals would not have the qualification to summon all eighteen of us."

"However, you are correct that just the three of us Falcon Kings wanting to kill you is simply a fantasy, as our brief exchange has proven."

"So this time, seven Falcon Kings have assembled."

"Since the founding of the Night Falcons until now, this is the only time seven Falcon Kings have jointly carried out a mission. Never before has a mission required more than three Falcon Kings. Your Highness, though you may die today, on the Night Falcons' must-kill list, you will forever be the first."

"Because I do not think there will be anyone in the future who can mobilize eight Falcon Kings simultaneously."

"In that case, it is indeed an honor for me. However, it is an honor I will not accept, and you will not obtain the glory you desire," Xiao Xian said calmly.

"That is not for Your Highness to decide," another voice rang out. In the bloody light, first a crimson sword hilt appeared. With one step, the crimson blade reflected out. Two steps, and the blade suddenly disappeared. Three steps, and it gradually reappeared...

"This sword reminds me of the sixteenth ranked Luminous Sword in the Sword Manual," Xiao Xian said lightly.

"Crimson Mirror Falcon King, greeting Your Highness." The black-robed figure emerged, bowing with hands together.

Thud thud thud!

Clank clank!

Heavy footsteps accompanied the clanking of iron chains. In the bloody light, a massive figure approached heavily, body wrapped in silver-white iron chains, tightly gripping twin shields in his hands.

"Your Highness, the White Shield Falcon King pays his respects."

A golden light flashed in the bloody radiance as a black-robed figure holding a golden staff leapt down from above.

"The Bronze Heaven Falcon King, under Minister Li's orders, has come to take Your Highness's head."

"Then who is the seventh?" Xiao Xian asked.

Swish!

A sickle flashed through the bloody light, embedding itself in a pillar before Xiao Xian. Standing on the sickle, a figure folded his arms, licking his lips as he coldly laughed, "Prince Wu, I, the Death Soul Falcon King, very much want to taste the blood of the Great Qin royal family, the sole king of Great Qin."

"Is that so? I just want to ask one question - have you tasted your own blood?" Xiao Xian smiled.

"To answer Your Highness, no, because no one has ever been able to injure me, the Death Soul Falcon King. From the moment I became a Night Falcon assassin, this sickle of mine has only severed the heads of enemies," the Death Soul Falcon King said ominously.

"Moreover, this sickle is the signature weapon of the first Falcon King."

"Greetings to the First Falcon King!"
