The tail also broke through the palace floor, layer upon layer of exquisite dragon scales emerging, blocking the lethal strike aimed at the three great masters.

As the palace pillars shattered, a mechanical dragon head accompanied by a large amount of quicksand descended from above.

On top of the dragon head stood an old man, whose aura was no less formidable than the three great masters of the Moist Sect, clearly also a master himself.

"Old Master Jiezang, you've finally arrived," the Unyielding Sect Master said with a sense of relief, as if having survived a calamity.

"One of the ten great masters - Master Jiezang, greetings to Your Highness the Martial King," the old man on top cupped his fists towards Xingxiao, saying, "I couldn't have imagined that even the seven Nightingale Kings, the three great masters of the Moist Sect, plus the mechanical Azure Dragon, would still not be a match for Your Highness."

"Whether it was the great sage in the past or the current chancellor Li Si of the Qin state, they all underestimated Your Highness."

"Is the great Qin about to produce not only an unprecedented emperor, but also a supreme master who commands the martial arts world?" The Master Jiezang's tone turned resentful and indignant.

Xingxiao smiled faintly and asked, "Which Azure Dragon is this from the Moist Sect?"

"The number one Azure Dragon, the strongest," Master Jiezang replied. "Unfortunately, there seems to be no such thing as the strongest in front of Your Highness."

"Your Highness, we shall take our leave. If you can leave this place, we will meet again."

As he spoke, the Azure Dragon swiftly ascended.

At the same time, three mechanical hands emerged from the huge tail of the Azure Dragon, attempting to take away the three supreme weapons - Heavenly Will, Unyielding, and Likewise.

This gave Xingxiao and Chilian an opportunity.

Xingxiao immediately looked towards Chilian with a signal.

Understanding Xingxiao's intention, Chilian nodded in response.

As the collapsing mechanical Azure Dragon was reduced to just the tip of its tail, Chilian's Red Viper Sword instantly unfurled completely, and with a forceful swing, it coiled around the huge mechanical tail.

Xingxiao had already descended to Chilian's side via the cloud ladder, taking over the Red Viper Sword while simultaneously activating his three-foot energy barrier to envelop himself and Chilian. Chilian once again leaned into Xingxiao's embrace.

At this moment, with Xingxiao's upper body bare and not activating his Adamantine Indestructible Body skill, Chilian could truly feel the warmth of his masculine body, her face involuntarily blushing red.

Once again passing through the darkness of the quicksand, Xingxiao and Chilian returned to the surface.

On the desolate dunes, though the night was deep, it was overshadowed by the cold wind and the stench of blood. The bleached bones protruding from the sand added to the desolation of this place.

"What?" A cry shattered the night's silence, coming from Master Likewise, who was the first to sense Xingxiao and Chilian behind the Azure Dragon's tail.

"You...you actually came up?"

"Thank you, the four of you," Xingxiao said. "To repay this kindness, this king promises to leave your whole corpses."

"Your Highness, your tone is a bit too arrogant," Master Jiezang said coldly. "Perhaps the four of us are still not your match, but with this number one Azure Dragon, if we want to leave, Your Highness cannot stop us."

"You should know, this is no longer the palace beneath the sand. The number one Azure Dragon can fully unleash its power here."

"Then let's try!" Xingxiao said coldly, his body radiating a golden light, having transformed into his Adamantine Indestructible form. The Adamantine power gathered in his Adamantine hands, forming Adamantine Dragon Claw hands.

"I wonder if these hands of mine can destroy your vaunted number one Azure Dragon."

"Xingxiao, don't be so arrogant..." Master Heavenly Will roared angrily. "Old Master Jiezang, kill him for this master. With that kind of mouth, this master wants to tear it apart."
