Dust stirred up as the three fine horses disappeared from here.

......

In the maple forest, slightly yellowed maple leaves fluttered down like hands, adding a desolate air to this bleak place.

Hiss hiss hiss~!

Under the maple leaves, hissing sounds arose. At some point, dazzling scarlet poisonous snakes had surrounded Ying Xiao and the other two.

"Princess Hong Lian, greeting guests in such a way is not very polite." Ying Xiao said softly.

"Heh heh heh..."

A charming laughter rang out. Not far ahead in the maple trees, a giant scarlet python descended. A voluptuous woman wearing a fiery red dress and with a bewitching appearance alighted from the python's back. She sat alluringly on a tree branch before lightly leaping down.

"The Han state no longer exists. Prince Wu, you are clearer on this point than me. So here there is only Chi Lian of Flowing Sands, no princess." Chi Lian spoke charmingly.

"It's just a form of address, does Princess Hong Lian mind so much?" Ying Xiao said with a laugh.

In his heart he also sighed, "Indeed a peerless beauty."

Chi Lian slowly descended and spoke charmingly, "Prince Wu, you and the two great beauties beside you must follow closely, because the forest here devours people, even if these two great beauties are the two elders of the Yin-Yang Sect."

"Much thanks." Ying Xiao nodded.

Under Chi Lian's lead, Ying Xiao soon saw Wei Zhuang.

Wearing splendid robes, with white hair, eyes closed on a broken stone seat.

Swish!

The next moment, Wei Zhuang opened his eyes.

The Shark Tooth sword beside him trembled.

"Prince Wu of the Qin... this title is very interesting..." A heavy yet domineering voice rang out. A breeze came, causing a strand of hair on the side of Ying Xiao's face to flutter. Wei Zhuang had already appeared in front of Ying Xiao, the Shark Tooth sword heavily descending.

In Ying Xiao's hand, the Tianwen sword had long left its sheath, horizontally blocking in front.

"Mister Wei Zhuang, isn't this way of greeting a bit too rude." Ying Xiao said softly.

Boom!

At this moment, Wei Zhuang's inner force completely erupted, demonic sword light flashing from the Shark Tooth.

"It seems Mister Wei Zhuang really wants to find out if the Tianwen sword is qualified to rank first. So be it. Since I am the guest, I'll let the host have his way." Ying Xiao said.

As he spoke, his inner force also instantly surged, the sword light of Tianwen immediately replacing the demonic sword light.

"The Zonghen sword art, unparalleled in the world. Mister Wei Zhuang, I also really want to experience it."

In the midst of their battle, Ying Xiao and Wei Zhuang had already exchanged several dozen moves.

Wei Zhuang grew more and more excited as the fight went on.

"The title of Martial King is well-deserved indeed!"

"Crossing the Eight Directions!"

At the pinnacle of his sword technique, Crossing the Eight Directions was already in Ying Xiao's sight.

An extremely domineering sword move!

Perhaps even the most domineering sword move in the martial world.

"Tai Chi!"

Compared to Wei Zhuang's domineering style, Ying Xiao's sword technique instantly became gentle and subdued, swinging the Heavenly Question sword extremely slowly.

"It is an incomparably slow sword move, but also an inscrutable one." The Great Commander focused all his energy and spirit to observe, but unfortunately, even though he had cultivated the Sacred Fire Order Divine Skill, he was still unable to see through the subtleties of Tai Chi.

Because, under the enlightenment of Ying Xiao's heaven-defying comprehension system, this Tai Chi had already far surpassed Zhang Sanfeng's Tai Chi.

Under the Tai Chi sword move, Wei Zhuang's domineering Crossing the Eight Directions vanished without a trace. In Wei Zhuang's eyes, the Heavenly Question sword was incomparably slow, yet in an instant it arrived at Wei Zhuang's neck.

The two stopped their hands at this moment.

"Indeed, even someone as strong as Wei Zhuang is still no match for Your Highness the Martial King. Perhaps because this is not a life-or-death battle, Wei Zhuang did not continue to counterattack, but even if Wei Zhuang had continued to counterattack, the result would have been the same." The Great Commander said in his heart.

"Wei Zhuang also did not continue his sword attack precisely because he saw this point."

Chi Lian was already stunned.

With just one sword move, the man who was like a god in her heart had been defeated.

In her mind, it had always been Wei Zhuang killing people with a single sword move. She had never seen a scene like this before.

Wei Zhuang's expression remained unchanged, but his heart was already surging with shock. In the martial world, there was only one person he cared about in terms of swordsmanship. That person was known as the Sword Saint, and besides him, there were no other swords in Wei Zhuang's eyes.

Today, such a sword had appeared.

Moreover, it was a very powerful sword.

Furthermore, he considered his own swordsmanship to have reached the pinnacle, but at this moment, his sword was like a child's. That deep sense of powerlessness, he had only experienced it when facing that person when he first entered the Ghost Valley.

"Tai Chi..." Wei Zhuang slowly raised his eyes and repeated.

"Crossing the Eight Directions, unparalleled in its domineering power. Seeing it today, it lives up to its reputation." Ying Xiao said with a smile.

"With Your Highness the Martial King possessing such skill, why come to Flowing Sands?" Wei Zhuang slowly turned around, with shark teeth erected in front of him, and asked indifferently.

"A deal!" Ying Xiao said, "And also, to appoint generals and confer titles."
