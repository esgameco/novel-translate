At the forefront of the attack, the Lingyu Celestial Venerable is not particularly strong, but in terms of suppressing and containing enemies, among the dozens of supreme celestial venerables from the six great ancient nations, the Lingyu Celestial Venerable can definitely be ranked among the top three.

As long as it is not a peak supreme celestial venerable, once they fall into his Lingyu, they will be trapped for at least several days to tens of days.

Facing a peak supreme celestial venerable? The Lingyu Celestial Venerable will not easily bring such a powerful expert into his sight.

First, he cannot contain them for long.

Secondly, it will divert most of his mental focus to suppress them.

The benefits may not outweigh the costs.

This is not a problem, after all, among the dozens of supreme celestial venerables from the six great ancient nations, there are not many who can truly be called peak supreme celestial venerables, adding up to only around ten to twenty.

The vast majority are still normal supreme celestial venerables.

The Guanglie Celestial Venerable can split into thirty-three avatars, and each avatar is equivalent to a relatively powerful supreme celestial venerable, but they will still be suppressed and contained by the Lingyu Celestial Venerable for a period of time.

In fact, not only will the Lingyu Celestial Venerable not easily make a move against a certain peak supreme celestial venerable, the other peak supreme celestial venerables among themselves also cannot just meet and engage in battle, even for the sake of a core celestial pillar.

Now that many powerful experts have just entered the treasure land, there are still many unoccupied core celestial pillars, and even if the peak supreme celestial venerables encounter each other, in the end, one of them will retreat and seek a new core celestial pillar.

As for fighting over it? Unless it's in the later stage of the competition, when most of the core celestial pillars have already been claimed, the peak supreme celestial venerables will then unleash their full power and fight to determine the outcome.

"My eye?"

The Lingyu Celestial Venerable let out a miserable scream, his right eye exploded, which was equivalent to the disruption of the path of his evolved chaotic power, greatly affecting him.

Rustling sounds.

From the depths of the dense spatial fluctuations, Lin Yuan slowly walked out, glancing at the Lingyu Celestial Venerable who was covering his right eye.

In the Lingyu Celestial Venerable's right eye Lingyu, the space-time had almost solidified, and the moment Lin Yuan fell into it, he actively activated the divine ability 'World', with 33,333 blazing sun seeds in his body forming a great cycle, projecting outwards to the universe, transforming into 33,333 blazing suns.

This single move burst open the Lingyu Celestial Venerable's right eye Lingyu.

"This Lingyu Celestial Venerable is truly worthy of his reputation," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

If the blazing sun seeds in his body had not formed a great cycle, then at this moment, trapped inside the other's Lingyu, he would have been suppressed for at least several days or even ten days.

"Silver River Celestial Venerable, do you possess the strength of a peak supreme celestial venerable?"

The Lingyu Celestial Venerable's figure trembled slightly, but quickly calmed down. Although his right eye was destroyed, he still had his left eye, and his foundation remained, so he was able to stabilize himself quickly.

The one who could break through his Lingyu must be a peak supreme celestial venerable, and moreover, one who specializes in killing.

The Lingyu Celestial Venerable was reluctant to bring a peak supreme celestial venerable into his sight, and this was precisely the concern he had.

The path of chaotic evolution of the peak supreme celestial venerables encompasses everything, and if they encounter one with terrifying killing power, they may very well be able to shatter the Lingyu from within.

Lin Yuan was precisely such a case.

The surrounding other supreme celestial venerables from the great spiritual ancient nations also had subtle changes in their expressions.

A peak supreme celestial venerable? The Silver River Celestial Venerable has just stepped into the celestial venerable realm?

"Apologies." Lin Yuan calmly looked at the Lingyu Celestial Venerable.

Having been trapped in the other's right eye Lingyu, Lin Yuan could only use his full strength, otherwise, if he was trapped inside, the loss would be even greater.

"Even if you have crossed into the celestial venerable realm with the bloodline of an ancestor, I don't believe your strength has risen so quickly."

The Lingyu Celestial Venerable stared at Lin Yuan, and in the depths of his left eye, a vortex was slowly rotating.

In an instant, the laws of heaven and earth were changing, and a deep and dark prison was forming, seemingly like the Lingyu in the Lingyu Celestial Venerable's left eye, but transferred to the outside world.

When facing opponents of different levels, the Lingyu Celestial Venerable has different methods. Against ordinary supreme celestial venerables, he can simply trap them in his Lingyu.

But when facing peak supreme celestial venerables of the same level, he has to be much more cautious. He would first use the Lingyu in his eye to probe, and only when he confirms that the opponent has no means to disrupt the foundation of the Lingyu, would he then proceed to trap them.

"I see, you think I used external forces?"

Lin Yuan understood, his method of bursting open the Lingyu clearly reached the level of a peak supreme celestial venerable, but this did not mean that Lin Yuan was a peak supreme celestial venerable himself.

Perhaps he had used some external force?

With his right eye destroyed, the Lingyu Celestial Venerable can be considered to have suffered a devastating loss, so he definitely would not let Lin Yuan go easily.

"Very well, let me see the strength of a peak supreme celestial venerable."

Lin Yuan observed the changing heavens and earth around them. At the peak supreme celestial venerable level, one is already approaching the invincible great saints in the chaotic void. Such an opponent piqued Lin Yuan's interest as well.

The previous bursting of the Lingyu was completely due to the Lingyu Celestial Venerable's underestimation, and Lin Yuan had taken advantage of that.

Rumbling sounds.

The next moment, two completely different spatial-temporal forces began to violently collide, the Lingyu in the Lingyu Celestial Venerable's left eye intertwining with the cosmic power from Lin Yuan's exterior, their terrifying might causing the space-time to be erased in large swaths.

"The Lingyu Celestial Venerable has unleashed his true power."

The supreme celestial venerables from the great spiritual ancient nations who were about to refine the core celestial pillar immediately retreated far away.

Among the celestial venerables of the six great ancient nations, there are four levels:

From weak to strong, they are:

Ordinary celestial venerables,

Supreme celestial venerables,

Peak supreme celestial venerables,

And the strongest, the Profound Void Celestial Venerables.

The path of chaotic evolution that the peak supreme celestial venerables cultivate is much stronger than that of the ordinary celestial venerables and supreme celestial venerables, resulting in a huge gap in combat power.

As for the Profound Void Celestial Venerables, the strongest? They are essentially on the same level as the peak supreme celestial venerables, as their paths of chaotic evolution are basically at the same tier.

The reason the Profound Void Celestial Venerables are above the peak supreme celestial venerables is that they have mastered certain supreme inheritances passed down by the thirteen-tier ancestors.

The thirteen-tier ancestors of the six great ancient nations have all left related supreme inheritances in their own treasure vaults, and many celestial venerables have tried to cultivate them, but truly succeeding is extremely rare.

Cultivating and mastering are two different things.

"So this is the power of a peak supreme celestial venerable?"

The several supreme celestial venerables from the Great Spiritual Ancient Nation couldn't help but feel a sense of awe.

The collision between Lin Yuan and the Lingyu Celestial Venerable had released shockwaves that were almost equivalent to their full-power attacks as supreme celestial venerables.

"The Silver River Celestial Venerable should be a peak supreme celestial venerable."

The several supreme celestial venerables from the Great Spiritual Ancient Nation silently observed, coming to this conclusion.

The Lingyu Celestial Venerable suspected that Lin Yuan had relied on external forces to burst open his right eye Lingyu, and they naturally had similar speculations.

After all, Lin Yuan's rise was too fast, even with the bloodline of a celestial venerable ancestor, but his growth and development did not require time? But after this direct confrontation, although the Lingyu Celestial Venerable had lost his right eye, his peak supreme celestial venerable strength remained, and he still could not suppress the Silver River Celestial Venerable in the slightest.

This was a genuine, head-on clash.

This was one of his killing moves, specially prepared for other peak supreme celestial sovereigns. But now, he used it against Lin Yuan.

Rumbling.

The blazing suns hung high.

This was the first time Lin Yuan fully activated the divine ability "World" in front of others. Previously, when he was trapped in the Nether Eternal Sovereign's right eye, there were no other powerful experts observing.

The Nether Eternal Sovereign's expression turned ferocious. It was his right eye that had been burst open by the power of these countless blazing suns.

"Nether Eternal Sovereign, aren't you going to run?"

Blazing suns floated around Lin Yuan, and he himself had transformed into an even larger blazing sun, illuminating the four corners of the universe.

"Run?"

As for the few supreme celestial sovereigns of the Great Abyss Ancient Country at the beginning, they had long since retreated far away.

Lin Yuan did not continue to pursue and kill, but instead returned to the core celestial pillar below and began to refine it.

In the independent space of the Blazing Sun Capital.
