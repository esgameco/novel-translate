It was boundless, like a true 'source world'.

But Lin Yuan clearly felt that the giant painting frame had been constructed.

However, within the giant painting, there were still many blurred and blank areas that had not been painted.

If this giant painting was completed, it would be a true 'source world'.

At that time, the Lord of Wu Mountain, relying on this giant painting, would at least be able to enter the Thirteenth Realm Lord level.

As one's life force reaches a high level, what they pursue is all related to their own Dao. The Lord of Wu Mountain wanted to cross into the Thirteenth Realm through the Dao of Painting, and to advance further, he would need to paint a complete 'source world'.

"Remarkable."

Lin Yuan carefully observed the Eternal Painting.

How vast and boundless is a source world? It was no longer a matter of size, but of encompassing countless Dao principles.

To paint a source world was equivalent to creating a source world, and the difficulty would be far greater than Lin Yuan theoretically reaching the Seventh Layer of the 'Infinite Void'.

The ultimate goal of the 'Infinite Void' was the Chaotic Void, but although the source world was on the same level as the Chaotic Void in terms of hierarchy, the source world was more perfect than the Chaotic Void.

The Thirteenth Realm Temporal Existences in the Chaotic Void had left behind many special places to compensate for the hidden dangers of the Chaotic Void, but the source world did not need that, as it was inherently near-perfect.

"Although it's not finished, it's enough for me."

Lin Yuan observed the Eternal Painting. The painting itself was composed of endless runes.

The Lord of Wu Mountain painted the source world encompassing the universe using runes.

【Your comprehension is extraordinary. Observing the unknown giant painting has enhanced your understanding of the Dao of Runes.】

Lin Yuan's mind was immersed in the boundless Eternal Painting, and his entire being was being elevated in all aspects.

The Dao of Runes, at its core, was an auxiliary Dao that could be integrated into any cultivation system. With the help of runes, one could achieve many things that were originally impossible or difficult to do.

For example, the task Lin Yuan was currently undertaking - the opening of particle worlds. Normally, maintaining the existence of each particle world would require a small amount of mental effort. This was unacceptable for Lin Yuan, whose goal was to open an infinite number of particle worlds. The Dao of Runes solved this problem, as the runes could be used to sustain each particle world without any mental investment.

Time passed, and Lin Yuan slowly opened his eyes.

"How was it?" the golden figure asked.

"It had a profound impact." Lin Yuan nodded.

The Eternal Painting had now been imprinted deeply in his mind, and he could contemplate and comprehend it at any time.

"That's all it took to have such a profound impact?" The golden figure smiled.

"Huh?" Lin Yuan looked at the golden figure, sensing that there was more to it.

"Haha." The golden figure's face showed a faint smile. With his seniority, even the Eternal Scions would naturally address him as "Senior," but Lin Yuan?

Lin Yuan's strength was so abnormal that the golden figure did not even consider him as a life form below the Eternal Lords.

"What is the purpose of this test space?" the golden figure asked.

"To cultivate a new Eternal Lord." After some thought, Lin Yuan answered.

This was what the golden figure had told him at the beginning - the test space was the Lord of Wu Mountain's arrangement before the great changes in heaven and earth. The purpose was to cultivate an Eternal Lord for this realm, so that they could clear the 'Demonic' disaster after its arrival.

"Do you think the giant painting left behind by the Lord of Wu Mountain can cultivate an Eternal Lord?" the golden figure asked.

"Well..." Lin Yuan pondered.

The Eternal Painting left by the Lord of Wu Mountain was profoundly abstruse. Even for other Thirteenth Realm Temporal Existences, it would require gradual comprehension. For Lin Yuan, it was even more so. Even after reaching the Thirteenth Realm, he would continue to benefit from that giant painting.

However, the essence of the painting did not mention how to cross into the Thirteenth Realm. Even if one fully comprehended the Eternal Painting, it would not provide qualitative help for breaking through the time cycle.

If one could not cross into the Thirteenth Realm and enhance the possibility of breaking through the time cycle, this Eternal Painting would not be of great significance. It would be less valuable than the martial dao Lin Yuan had disseminated, which could at least help cultivate one more practitioner and strengthen the 'anchor' a bit.

"In fact, in addition to the great Lord of Wu Mountain's own painted inheritance, there is another inheritance - the inheritance of the Lord of Celestial Light," the golden figure said, looking at Lin Yuan.

"The Lord of Celestial Light?" Lin Yuan's mind shook.

Whether it was the records of the Qing Yun Sacred Mountain or the other Carefree Realm Experts' legacies he had explored, they all mentioned this Eternal Lord.

The Lord of Celestial Light was the earliest Eternal Lord to appear in this realm. He was also the most mysterious Eternal Lord. The reason he was called the 'earliest to appear' was that no one knew when he became an Eternal Lord - from the very beginning of heaven and earth, or even earlier? No one knew.

It was only known that when the Lord of Celestial Light first appeared before the masses, he was already an Eternal Lord. It was because of the Lord of Celestial Light that the cultivation system in this realm could flourish, and the Dao of Runes even originated from him.

"Although the great Lord of Wu Mountain also had some experience in how to become an Eternal Lord, it must be admitted that compared to the Lord of Celestial Light, he was still far behind," the golden figure continued. "If it weren't for the Lord of Celestial Light, the current number of Eternal Lords would definitely be less than half."

"Although the Lord of Celestial Light did not directly cultivate Eternal Lords, all the current Eternal Lords have inherited the karmic connections from the Lord of Celestial Light, and they have reached the pinnacle of cultivation on the foundation he established."

The golden figure's tone was full of reverence and admiration for the Lord of Celestial Light.

"I see." Lin Yuan nodded slightly.

Each Eternal Lord walked a different path and pursued different goals. The Lord of Celestial Light had cultivated a solid foundation of cultivation for this realm, and it was on this foundation that the various Eternal Lords had emerged.

"The Lord of Celestial Light, in order to show the weak how to become Eternal Lords, specially created an inheritance with thirty-three original texts, and the great Lord of Wu Mountain obtained one of them," the golden figure said.

These inheritance original texts were written by the creator of the inheritance themselves, and while they could be copied, the essence would be diminished. In terms of the impact on cultivators, the original inheritance texts were the most direct and authentic.

"This is that inheritance." The golden figure flipped his right hand, and a book appeared before them.

"An inheritance specifically created to help breakthrough to the Thirteenth Realm?" Lin Yuan was highly excited. So far, any inheritance he had obtained had not been directly related to reaching the Thirteenth Realm.
