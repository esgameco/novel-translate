This kind of technique is not a big deal in normal times, as even if trapped, Supreme Celestial Lords can still break out, only wasting some time.

The many Supreme Celestial Lords of the Great Spirit Ancient Country have also gathered here.

It would be extremely frustrating.

The Earth Yin Celestial Lord spoke.

Among the Twelve Supreme Celestial Lords of the Dark Yin Ancient Country, four can be considered top-tier, including the Secluded Yin Celestial Lord, the Earth Yin Celestial Lord, and two others.

"We still need to pay more attention."

The Secluded Yin Celestial Lord said. No matter what, the Nether Spirit Celestial Lord is definitely one of the most difficult to deal with among the many top-tier Supreme Celestial Lords.

In the Thunder Roar Ancient Country...

As one of the Six Great Ancient Countries, the experts of the Thunder Roar Ancient Country are essentially born from thunder and lightning. The ancestor of the Thunder Roar is said to be the first life in this source world that attained enlightenment through thunder.

Later, he was taken as a disciple by the Great Submersion Ancestor, eventually crossing into the Thirteen Tiers and establishing the Thunder Roar Ancient Country.

The Great Submersion Ancient Country and the Thunder Roar Ancient Country have a close relationship. Although the Thunder Roar Ancestor broke away from the Great Submersion Ancient Country to establish the Thunder Roar Ancient Country, the roots between them have not been severed.

"No problem."

The other Supreme Celestial Lords of the Thunder Roar Ancient Country nodded.

This can be seen as a way to mitigate the losses, at least the Great Submersion Ancient Country won't just take the precious land 'cores' for no reason.
