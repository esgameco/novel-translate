The secret laws are no different - the three great protective secret laws of the Xuanhuang lineage are each unimaginable.

"This kid, the aura of his true experienced years shouldn't exceed four thousand years. Yet he has opened up so many layers of space, and now this?"

The six-armed figure fell silent.

Based on Lin Yuan's current cultivation speed, at most five thousand years and he could cultivate the 'Xuanhuang Strike' to the third stage.

At that time, Lin Yuan's casual strikes would unleash four times his limit in power,

And he could maintain this state "permanently."

"In my Xuanhuang lineage, when our Sovereign was still present, the fastest anyone cultivated the 'Xuanhuang Strike' to the third stage was seven thousand three hundred and ninety-four years - and that person was an 11th stage life form."

The six-armed figure thought to himself, a 9th stage life form cultivating the secret law faster than an 11th stage? This was something he could never have imagined.

A 9th stage and 11th stage life form were almost different species altogether.

The gap between them was like a vast chasm - the fundamental principles an 11th stage could see through at a glance might take a 9th stage ten thousand years to perceive.

"But then again, maybe not."

"Smoothly cultivating the first stage doesn't guarantee the second and third stages will go as smoothly."

The six-armed figure let out a light sigh of relief, suddenly laughing, "What am I worried about? As the descendant of our Sovereign, the more outrageous Lin Yuan's talents and qualities, the better. If they're not outrageous, how can he keep up with our Sovereign's pace?"

At this moment, the innumerable layers of space surrounding the Xuanhuang Secret Realm suddenly began to ripple.

"Hmm?"

"Those 12th stage life forms are trying to lock onto the secret realm's location again?"

The six-armed figure glanced over and sneered, "Even if our Sovereign has been gone for dozens of universe cycles, you 12th stage beings who have never experienced the complete annihilation of a universe want to break through our Sovereign's methods? That's a dreamer's talk."

Deep in the vast layers of space,

Several enormously large powerful beings stood towering.

Their mere presence caused many space layers to retreat, forming a domain that belonged solely to them. Within this domain, space and time were playthings to be freely manipulated.

"Just where is this Xuanhuang Secret Realm? I'm getting more and more curious about its former master, that 'Sovereign.'"

An existence with twenty-four pairs of wings behind its back spoke in a resounding voice laced with curiosity.

"So what do we do now? We still haven't locked onto the secret realm's location." Another being, its body spanning millions of miles, opened its mouth. Its voice traveled through space-time, disrupting it in this region.

"What can we do? We came from somewhere, so we'll just go back!" The third towering figure turned and took a step, disappearing into the distance.

"Hmph!" The second towering figure's expression was calm. Every time the Xuanhuang Secret Realm opened, supreme beings would come and try to lock onto its coordinates, but failed without exception.

At the level of supreme beings, most treasures, weapons and secret laws in this world were of little use to them. But this Xuanhuang Secret Realm was different.

Not to mention anything else, just the concealment method alone made many supreme beings feel alarmed.

Moreover, all the supreme beings shared a common thought - that the secret realm must contain great secrets or treasures.

Otherwise, why go to such lengths to hide the Xuanhuang Secret Realm? What was the point?

Soon, the few supreme beings present disappeared, and this region of space-time returned to stability.

Inside the Xuanhuang Secret Realm,

Lin Yuan circulated the 'Xuanhuang Strike' and found that the speed of refining the secret realm's core had greatly increased, making his heart rejoice.

Among the three great protective secret laws of the Xuanhuang lineage, the 'Xuanhuang Strike' is listed as an offensive secret law, but that's not quite accurate. It's more like a "passive" secret law that can be applied to anything.

For example, now Lin Yuan was using the 'Xuanhuang Strike' to accelerate the refinement of the secret realm's core, which had nothing to do with attack.

As Lin Yuan's progress in refining the secret realm's core increased, so did his control over the secret realm.

Now, Lin Yuan could clearly sense every little thing happening in the outer region of the Xuanhuang Secret Realm - the 9th stage xenoraces hiding in various corners, their communications with each other, and so on.

Nothing could escape Lin Yuan's perception.

Of course, the "outer region" here referred to the area surrounding the continent where these outsiders had descended.

As for the other two continents, they would be completely under Lin Yuan's control as he continued refining the secret realm's core.

"These 9th stage xenoraces...if I wanted to kill them now, I could do it anytime." Lin Yuan's heart stirred.

Within the Xuanhuang Secret Realm, any outsider would be suppressed, unable to teleport being just one aspect. Their perception would also be greatly reduced.

So normally, as long as a 9th stage xenorace didn't make too much noise and just hid obediently in some corner, there wouldn't be any danger.

"Never mind, I should probably keep a low profile." Lin Yuan gave up on this tempting idea.

His taking over as the new master of the Xuanhuang Secret Realm must not leave any traces behind.

Precisely tracking the location of every 9th stage xenorace and hunting them down? This was no longer just a matter of strength.

The six-armed figure had already told Lin Yuan that whenever the Xuanhuang Secret Realm opened, it would attract the attention of supreme beings.

Under such circumstances, it was better to keep a low profile. If Lin Yuan could become a supreme being in the future, then he could openly declare himself the secret realm's master - so what?

"At my current refinement speed, it will take about five more years to completely refine it."

Lin Yuan continued gazing at the secret realm's core before him. Without the 'Xuanhuang Strike', refining it would take at least twenty years.

Using the 'Xuanhuang Strike' greatly reduced the time required.

Actually, how long it took to refine the core didn't really matter to Lin Yuan.

By becoming the "Sovereign" Xuanhuang's descendant and starting to refine the core, Lin Yuan had gained the "permission" to enter the Xuanhuang Secret Realm anytime.

He no longer had to wait for the secret realm to open.

As long as Lin Yuan willed it, he could enter the Xuanhuang Secret Realm using the Xuanhuang imprint.

"However, I can't take the initiative against those 9th stage xenoraces, but from now on, the 9th stage evolved humans from human civilization inside the secret realm will clear out and expel all hostile 9th stage xenoraces. At that time, it won't be a problem for me to take action."

Lin Yuan thought silently to himself.

With human civilization providing cover, as long as he was careful, he shouldn't expose anything.

As for displaying some power beyond normal - compared to the entire Xuanhuang Secret Realm, what did that amount to?

To the xenorace supreme beings, as long as Lin Yuan didn't cross into the 12th stage, in the long river of time he would just be a "pitiful creature" eventually facing the limit of his longevity and impending death from old age.

On the Galactic Main Star,

Lin Yuan was wholeheartedly comprehending the 'Xuanhuang Strike'.

Of the three great protective secret laws, the 'Xuanhuang Body' required a large amount of Xuanhuang crystals, so he couldn't cultivate it for now.

The 'Space-Time Step' was an escape secret law, but currently Lin Yuan was under no pressure. His true body was stationed on the Galactic Main Star, absolutely safe within human civilization's domain under the watchful eyes of supreme beings.

His Yin Yuan spirit was on Battlefield #B0001, absorbing the leaking primal forces from the permanent space rift. No one knew the true identity of Lin Yuan's Yin Yuan spirit.

And with the Yin Yuan spirit's strength, hiding on a mid-sized xenorace battlefield was as stable as could be.

As for the Yang Yuan spirit, it had already become the semi-master of the Xuanhuang Secret Realm. To a certain extent, not even supreme beings could do anything to it.

For now, there was no urgent need for Lin Yuan to cultivate the 'Space-Time Step' secret law.
