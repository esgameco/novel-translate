Chapter 318: Me, Hiding? (Please Subscribe)

"Continue expanding the space layers," Lin Yuan's thoughts focused.

Actually, as early as when he had expanded to millions of space layers, Lin Yuan could have attempted to break through to the tenth stage.

According to the experience of countless ninth stage cultivators in the main world breaking through to the tenth stage, millions of space layers satisfied the threshold for the breakthrough.

However, Lin Yuan was unwilling to give up this greatest opportunity from his world-traversing journey.

After breaking through to the tenth stage, the world within his body would continue to metamorphose and begin to "solidify" to a certain degree.

At that time, wanting to expand space layers like in the ninth stage would not be so easy.

Rather than doing that, it would be better to expand as many space layers as possible while in the ninth stage.

Why did that peak ninth stage life form from the Feather Race, Hunyi, expand over thirty million space layers yet still refuse to break through to the tenth stage?

Was he stupid?

Of course not.

Space layers represent the thickness and depth of a world, an even more profound essence beyond just the visible size.

The more space layers expanded in the ninth stage, the greater the help it would provide for stepping into the tenth stage, and even the eleventh stage after the world within transforms into a inner universe.

This was also the reason why Lin Yuan was currently frantically expanding space layers, although it couldn't be said that there wouldn't be other opportunities later.

But he had to seize this chance.

Additionally,

Another reason Lin Yuan did not attempt to break through to the tenth stage was the Yellow Yin Secret Realm.

The Yellow Yin Secret Realm did not allow cultivators of the tenth stage or above to enter - this was a rule since the opening of the Yellow Yin Secret Realm.

Even supreme experts could not forcibly enter. Lin Yuan was worried that if he broke through to the tenth stage while traversing worlds, the metamorphosis of his spiritual will's essence would be detected as an anomaly by the Yellow Yin Secret Realm, causing him to be "expelled."

Of course,

This possibility was not very high.

Lin Yuan's will descending here was separate from his main body, so even if he truly broke through to the tenth stage, it would have no relation.

For Lin Yuan, the descent of his will also served the purpose of "trial and error."

But to be safe, considering various factors, Lin Yuan would focus on expanding space layers for now.

With his current accumulation, whenever he wanted to break through to the tenth stage, he could do so. There was no need to rush.

Those peak ninth stage experts who had just expanded to millions of space layers were eager to attempt the tenth stage breakthrough because their longevity was nearing its limit.

They had no other choice but to do so.

Lin Yuan's longevity was still quite abundant, so there was no need to rush.

"The Yellow Yin Secret Realm..." Lin Yuan once again passed through the Myriad Realms Gate, sensing the situation over at the Sun Primordial Spirit's side.

"Xio Lan's aura has already weakened by nearly a quarter," Lin Yuan kept count in his heart, knowing Xio Lan could persist for a while longer.

Lin Yuan could foresee that by the end of this traversal, with his current strength, he should be able to sweep through all the ninth stage experts in the Yellow Yin Secret Realm.

At that time, the myriad resources within the secret realm would be his to take or leave.

In fact, Lin Yuan did not need to fully experience this traversal journey. If he went back now, he could also sweep through the entire Yellow Yin Secret Realm.

In the Ocean of Light,

Hundreds of True Gods sat in a spacious palace hall.

Lin Yuan's two primordial spirits had long kidnapped a large number of Demigods, thoroughly enraging the gods of the Ocean of Light. Coupled with the large number of divine crystals plundered three hundred years ago, old grievances and new resentments made the gods furious.

"My most devout believer, the seventh-level Demigod Yinmosi, has mysteriously disappeared for over two hundred years. I can sense he is still alive, but I don't know where he is," said a True God with a single horn, scanning the surrounding gods.

"My two Demigod believers are the same," said another True God with white wings.

"My believer too."

"Blocked from our perception, could they have fallen into other divine nations?"

"Was it done by those dark abyss gods?"

"No, the dark abyss also has primordial gods restraining them, they wouldn't do such a thing."

The hundreds of True Gods discussed, eventually concluding that their believers were likely in other divine nations. Otherwise, it would be impossible to block the gods' perception of their believers.

But these divine nations might not belong to living gods. In the distant past, the shattered divine nations of fallen gods also had similar abilities.

"So someone kidnapped our Demigod believers and hid them within a shattered divine nation?"

"What is their motive? And why not just kill them directly?"

The True Gods were puzzled. Although this conclusion was inconceivable, other than this possibility, there was no way to explain what they had experienced.

"There is a group of powerful Demigods in the mortal realm who luckily obtained divine destiny and gained a shattered divine nation. However, they began targeting our churches' Demigods, kidnapping them one by one for some purpose and placing them in that shattered divine nation."

Soon, a True God speculated on the cause and process of the events.

Although there were still many unexplained things in between, at least the origins of the matter were clarified.

"So what should we do now? Where exactly are our Demigod believers, and who is the culprit behind all this? Are there any clues?"

A True God voiced his doubts.

At this moment, the True God seated in the highest position spoke.

He was a Primordial God, the highest-ranking deity below the Primordial Gods, and surveyed the gods in the hall before saying slowly, "I have already sent the auras of your believers to the God of Divination over a hundred years ago, to have him deduce their final location."

Upon hearing this, the gods in the hall felt reassured.

The God of Divination was one of the twelve Primordial Gods of the Ocean of Light.

The God of Divination possessed a very special ability - that of foresight and divination.

The God of Divination could commune with the heavens and earth, observing the changes and evolution of all things in the world from a unique perspective.

Simply put, as long as the God of Divination was willing to pay the price, he could know anything in the world.

However, to request the God of Divination's assistance also required paying an immense price. If it were not for the severe loss of their Demigod-level believers, this Primordial God would not have sought out the God of Divination.

"The God of Divination told me he will provide the results during this period," the Primordial God seated in the highest position said slowly.

"Good."

"Then we shall wait."

"We will also bear some of the cost of requesting the God of Divination's assistance."

The gods looked at each other and spoke up one by one.

Hearing this, the Primordial God's expression improved slightly.

Time passed slowly, and several months went by in a blink.

Suddenly, the expression of the Primordial God seated in the highest position changed.

The other True Gods noticed and turned their gazes towards him.

After a moment, that Primordial God's expression gradually returned to normal.

Then, he looked towards the gods and said slowly, "The God of Divination has provided the results."

"As expected of the God of Divination."

"So where did our Demigod believers disappear to in the end?"

The gods looked at the Primordial God with anticipation.

"The God of Divination..."
