They could experience the long passage of years together.

But close relatives? How many of the close relatives of those tenth stage, eleventh stage evolved beings were still alive?

Even the close relatives of the most powerful had basically all died. The most powerful could go against the flow and revive past living beings.

But if they died due to reaching their life's limit, even if forcibly revived, they would die again when their lifespan ended.

To achieve eternity, one had to become a twelfth stage life form and transcend the river of time.

Who could have foreseen that Lin Yuan would cause such a huge commotion?

When the Crimson Leviathan Sovereign entered the Xuan Huang Secret Realm, he was extremely cautious, because the eighth stage was invincible, belonging to the lower levels of the secret realm.

But Lin Yuan's performance in the Xuan Huang Secret Realm was so overwhelmingly domineering. Those lofty special ninth stage experts had no choice but to flee with their tails between their legs. If they were too slow, they would die.

As for Lin Yuan advancing to the tenth stage, the Crimson Leviathan Sovereign's heart was complex, but he felt a sense of liberation.

When Lin Yuan crossed into the ninth stage, the Crimson Leviathan Sovereign was an eighth stage invincible, with hope of breaking into the ninth stage and becoming an evolved being at the same level as his junior disciple.

If not for Lin Yuan's provocation, the Crimson Leviathan Sovereign might have continued brewing for thousands of years more.

He would naturally reach the pinnacle and then consider crossing into the ninth stage when the time came.

After all, he still had a very long lifespan left. As one with a supreme bloodline of the Starry Sea Leviathans, the Crimson Leviathan Sovereign's lifespan far exceeded that of evolved beings at the same level.

But now?

Lin Yuan had advanced to the tenth stage.

The Crimson Leviathan Sovereign actually felt a sense of relief, because the tenth stage was not something he could achieve just by secluding himself.

Even with the Starry Sea Leviathan's great assistance in cultivation at the ninth stage, opening up countless layers of space, for such a favored child of the spatial origin, it would still be much easier.

But to fully complete the ninth stage and open up millions upon millions of spatial layers?

Without a hundred thousand years of time, don't even think about it.

And that was under ideal circumstances.

If things didn't go smoothly and he encountered a few bottlenecks, the time would be greatly extended.

The Crimson Leviathan Sovereign's thoughts rose and fell, and he felt a sense of emotion.

When the gap was not large, there was still the idea of catching up.

But when the gap was like a vast chasm, the Crimson Leviathan Sovereign completely lost such thoughts.

"Master, Junior Brother, Junior Brother has stepped into the tenth stage," the Tenth Peak Master immediately said, sharing this good news with the Crimson Leviathan Sovereign.

Upon these words, the ten Peak Masters looked at each other in confusion.

Learn from their junior brother? What could they learn from their junior brother?

"Back then when I had you return the Milky Way Sovereign's friend, look what happened. Not only did you fail to gain any advantage, you even offended the Milky Way Sovereign."

Si Zhong the Primordial God looked at Tuo Ba the Primordial God and shook his head slightly.

"So I offended him, so what?" Tuo Ba the Primordial God was completely unconcerned.

Given the talent and aptitude the Milky Way Sovereign displayed, his advancement to the tenth stage was only normal.

Tuo Ba the Primordial God did not worry about this at all. Even if the Milky Way Sovereign crossed into the eleventh stage in the future, so what?

For a Primordial God like himself standing at the pinnacle of the eleventh stage, unless the Milky Way Sovereign became a Paramount, he would be powerless against Tuo Ba.

But becoming a Paramount was no easy feat.

"You may not care about the Milky Way Sovereign, but what about that eleventh stage human evolved being who comprehended the laws of time from human civilization? You're saying he won't care either?"

"This human evolved being has great hope of becoming a Paramount. When that happens, human civilization will give birth to the tenth Paramount, which will have profound impacts on the state of affairs between the various major powers in the cosmos."

"Can you still not care then?"

Si Zhong the Primordial God spoke.

Afterwards, human civilization also publicly announced that a secluded tenth stage evolved being had broken through to the eleventh stage.

Matching the speculations of the alien Paramounts.

"A tenth stage who comprehended the laws of time..." Tuo Ba the Primordial God fell silent.

He could not care about the Milky Way Sovereign, because no matter how deviant his talent, it provided no direct help in assaulting the ultimate.

But a tenth stage who comprehended the laws of time? This provided no small assistance towards the ultimate, allowing one more chance than a normal eleventh stage peak expert to withstand the infusion of the chaotic primordial origin.

The world within would receive great benefits, making the future ultimate metamorphosis into an inner universe much easier.

"It has no impact on me." Tuo Ba the Primordial God looked at Si Zhong the Primordial God. "Even if human civilization produces another Paramount, it will only affect the state of affairs between the various peak powers. At most I'll defect to the Insectoid race."

Human civilization producing another Paramount would only increase their foundational strength slightly, far from allowing them to run rampant without restraint.

"You plan to assault the ultimate within the next million years, and in the meantime defect to the Insectoid race. Are you really saying it won't have any impact at all?" Si Zhong the Primordial God smiled.

He also felt a tinge of regret now. Who could have foreseen the Milky Way Sovereign being so unaffected by threats?

Moreover, the foundations of human civilization were too terrifying. It had only just given birth to nine Paramounts, yet there was already hope for the birth of the tenth so soon?

If he had known earlier, he definitely should have decisively returned the Milky Way Sovereign's friend. That would have been the best choice.

At least he wouldn't have offended the Milky Way Sovereign to the death, offended human civilization to the death.

This warmed the Crimson Leviathan Sovereign's heart.
