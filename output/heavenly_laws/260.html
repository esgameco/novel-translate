The dozens of figures on the scene spoke one after another.

The so-called large-scale war means that the Huai Dragon Palace and the Daode Immortal Sect will fight with all their strength. The True Immortals on both sides will take the field, and the two major powers will not hold back at all.

Such a large-scale battle often does not happen for hundreds of thousands or even millions of years, because the cost is too great.

No matter which side wins, it will be a tragic victory. The Profound King Hall will not stand by and watch the Daode Immortal Sect fall. It will definitely intervene.

"Profound King Hall?"

But now...

The Huai Dragon Palace Master has passed the physical body tribulation, and her combat power has greatly increased. She doesn't take ordinary half-step Immortal Kings seriously at all.

Before passing the physical body tribulation, she could be on par with half-step Immortal Kings by relying on her True Dragon bloodline.

Now that she has become a half-step Immortal King, it is estimated that three or four half-step Immortal Kings are needed to contend with her.

This time, the Huai Dragon Palace is determined to obtain that Heavenly Water Essence.

The Heavenly Water Essence has a significant effect on the dragon descendants' transformation into dragons.

If the Huai Dragon Palace can obtain that Heavenly Water Essence, their strength will rise to a higher level within ten thousand years at most.

It even has a certain beneficial effect for the Huai Dragon Palace Master to pass the mental tribulation.

For ordinary Dao Convergence True Immortals, the chance of passing the mental tribulation is greater than passing the physical body tribulation.

Cangqing Immortal felt a bit of a headache. Even with the support of the Profound King Hall, facing the Huai Dragon Palace's repeated decisive battles, the Daode Immortal Sect had begun to be unable to withstand it.

The Profound King Hall was the most powerful force in the Profound King Domain. The Daode Immortal Sect and another major power were known as the three giants of the Profound King Domain along with the Profound King Hall.

"The Heavenly Water Essence..."

Cangqing Immortal thought of the culprit that caused the endless war between the two major powers of the Daode Immortal Sect and the Huai Dragon Palace.

In fact, the Heavenly Water Essence was not of much use to human cultivators.

But even if it was useless, it absolutely could not be handed over to the Huai Dragon Palace.

Cangqing Immortal could imagine that once the Huai Dragon Palace obtained the Heavenly Water Essence, its strength would greatly increase within ten thousand years, directly surpassing the Profound King Domain and becoming the most powerful force in the surrounding dozens of large domains.

At that time, the Daode Immortal Sect would no longer have the ability to resist the Huai Dragon Palace.

"The Heavenly Water Essence is rooted in the earth veins and cannot be moved or destroyed..."

Cangqing Immortal sighed lightly. Even if he used treasures to forcibly shatter the Heavenly Water Essence, as long as the earth veins remained, a new Heavenly Water Essence would be born within a few thousand years.

Cangqing Immortal speculated.

The Daode Immortal Sect and the Huai Dragon Palace had been at war for hundreds of thousands of years and were very familiar with the latter. But Cangqing Immortal did not break his promise because of this. After all, he had made a Dao oath.

Back then, when he negotiated with Lin Yuan, he agreed that martial arts could be spread in the lower realm, and martial arts cultivators who reached the sixth level could use the ascension platform to ascend to the Spirit Realm and be provided with protection.
