The moment the Blazing Sun Divine Body was formed, a large amount of almost instinctive information surged into Lin Yuan's mind.

Lin Yuan's consciousness quickly absorbed a large amount of information, which originated from the innate abilities of the divine body, just like how a newborn baby knows how to breathe and open its eyes.

"So this is the Blazing Sun Divine Body?" Lin Yuan was inevitably shocked. He had already sensed the terrifying nature of this constitution during the transformation process.

In the many classics and inheritances in the Burning Heaven Institute, there was also a lot of information introduced about the Blazing Sun Divine Body.

But when his own physical body completely transformed into the Blazing Sun Divine Body, Lin Yuan realized that he had still underestimated this kind of divine body.

"It's too powerful." Lin Yuan raised his right hand. Although it was still flesh and blood, the most microscopic components were now Blazing Sun particles with profound and mysterious patterns on the surface.

"Burn everything, devour everything?" Lin Yuan carefully felt the details of the Blazing Sun Divine Body.

When Lin Yuan condensed the thirtieth thousand Blazing Sun Seed, his spiritual will once entered a scarlet red space, where he glimpsed what seemed to be the figure of the Blazing Sun Ancestor.

It had countless appearances, and the image seen by different beings was different.

And the Blazing Sun Divine Bodies awakened by different experts also had differences.

Lin Yuan's Blazing Sun Divine Body, while maintaining the endless burning power, also possessed the power of devouring.

Any living or dead thing burned to ashes by Lin Yuan's Blazing Sun Divine Body power would have its essence directly absorbed.

Even space and time could be burned and turned into the source to replenish Lin Yuan.

This devouring and absorption happened in an instant, without the need for any refining time.

In simple terms, for Lin Yuan at this moment, combat and slaughter were not consumption, but feeding.

"This ability..." Lin Yuan shook his head slightly, "It's too monstrous, equivalent to a 'perpetual motion machine' without the problem of consumption."

After all, even the Chaos Venerable would worry about excessive consumption, and the Chaos Saints were much better in this regard, able to refine Chaos Qi to replenish themselves at any time.

But what if there was no Chaos Qi around?

The ability of Lin Yuan's Blazing Sun Divine Body was to burn everything and devour everything. Chaos Qi could be burned and devoured, the enemy's killing moves could also be burned and devoured, the visible and invisible, the substantial and illusory, all could be burned and devoured.

A flame suddenly ignited on Lin Yuan's fingertips.

The flame burned quietly, and the surrounding space began to distort, with cracks appearing. Lin Yuan immediately felt a stream of warmth flowing into his physical body.

"So this is the feeling of burning and devouring space?"

Lin Yuan looked thoughtful, and then consciously extinguished the flame.

This ability should still be used cautiously. If not careful, it could lead to the collapse of a large area of space-time, and attract the attention of the upper echelons of the Blazing Sun Ancient Country.

After all, this matter was definitely not a good thing. If Lin Yuan was allowed to burn freely, the entire Blazing Sun Ancient Country might be burned and devoured.

Of course, the territory of the Blazing Sun Ancient Country was vast and boundless. It was unknown how much time it would take to burn and devour such a large area.

Moreover, the enfeoffed kings and dukes within the ancient country would not just watch Lin Yuan burn and devour, not to mention the Eternal Blazing Sun who reigned supreme in the sky.

The most critical point was that the source obtained from the devouring could only replenish the consumption and heal injuries, and had no other great use.

"My current strength..." Lin Yuan let out a light sigh.

The so-called burning and devouring was just one of the abilities accompanying the Blazing Sun Divine Body. After the Blazing Sun Divine Body transformation was complete, Lin Yuan's physical strength and body toughness had increased by a large margin.

"In terms of direct combat and killing, I should not be weaker than the true body of a Chaos Void Venerable, and even if it comes to a stalemate, I should be the one to win in the end."

Lin Yuan examined himself. The Blazing Sun Divine Body he had awakened, with its accompanying burning and devouring abilities, was absolutely invincible in a stalemate battle.

Want to compete in consumption? Even the Saints might not be stronger than Lin Yuan.

"Become the Blazing Sun! Become the Blazing Sun!" Lin Yuan felt the details of the divine body. The Blazing Sun Divine Body awakened by each expert had differences, but they all had one common ability.

That was - to become the Blazing Sun.

This was also the ultimate killing move of the Blazing Sun Divine Body. Once activated, the physical body would completely transform, becoming a miniature Blazing Sun.

At that time, they could borrow a tiny bit of the power of the Eternal Blazing Sun in the sky.

Of course, this ultimate killing move would impose an extremely heavy burden on the foundation of the Blazing Sun Divine Body. It should not be used unless it was the last resort.

Activating it once would require at least hundreds of thousands or millions of years to recover.

"The Blazing Sun Divine Body I have awakened has a much stronger enhancement to my physical body compared to other Blazing Sun Divine Bodies."

Lin Yuan thought to himself. As the strongest divine body in the Blazing Sun Ancient Country, there were naturally many introductions and descriptions about the Blazing Sun Divine Body.

Lin Yuan also knew about the self-enhancement of other experts after awakening the Blazing Sun Divine Body.

The improvement was far lower than his own.

"It's the influence of the Blazing Sun Seeds." Lin Yuan nodded slightly.

Even when the Blazing Sun Divine Body was still in the transformation stage, Lin Yuan had noticed that the number of Blazing Sun Seeds had a driving effect on the body's transformation.

Now it was confirmed.

In fact, the more Blazing Sun Seeds one condenses, the higher the power of the awakened Blazing Sun Divine Body. This was no secret among the great experts of the Blazing Sun Ancient Country.

But knowing it was one thing, being able to do it was another.

In the Chaos Void, Lin Yuan sat cross-legged.

"The Blazing Sun Divine Body..."

Lin Yuan carefully observed the details of the Blazing Sun Divine Body.

The source world's clone shared the same consciousness with him, so Lin Yuan could observe this divine body from the most comprehensive angles.

"It seems very difficult to replicate this constitution."

Lin Yuan reached a conclusion.

The Blazing Sun Divine Body was too complex. The most microscopic components, those flame particles with countless profound and mysterious patterns on the surface, gave Lin Yuan a feeling of facing a boundless universe.

The Blazing Sun Divine Body of the source world was a natural transformation.

According to the cycle formed by the three thousand three hundred and thirty-three Blazing Sun Seeds, the physical body was slowly transformed, and finally the Blazing Sun Divine Body was awakened.

But in the Chaos Void, the Blazing Sun Seeds were useless. So to replicate a Blazing Sun Divine Body here, he must comprehend the most microscopic details of every flame particle.

"Take it slowly."

Lin Yuan gathered his thoughts. Relying on his extraordinary perceptiveness, comprehending the essence of the Blazing Sun Divine Body was indeed difficult, but not impossible.

Lin Yuan's attempt to comprehend the Blazing Sun Divine Body was not to replicate a Blazing Sun Divine Body in the Chaos Void, but rather out of curiosity about the microscopic particle composition of the Blazing Sun Divine Body.

It might involve another great Dao, a Heavenly Dao no less than the Infinite Void Inheritance of the Thirteen Realms.

Once he could comprehend it, he could easily extend it to create many other constitutions not inferior to the Blazing Sun Divine Body.

He could reach unimaginable heights in the realm of physical body and constitution.

In the Blue Burning City, the powerful experts from the Purple Dream Domain gathered.

"We've finally infiltrated in."

A thin-looking man spoke with a solemn expression.

They were now inside the Blue Burning City. If discovered, they would be doomed without a doubt.

A single thought from the Blue Burning Marquis could completely erase them.

"We've used secret treasures to completely suppress our auras. As long as the Blue Burning Marquis doesn't specifically look at us, he won't notice us."

The purple-haired woman spoke calmly.

As the domain city, the Blue Burning City had countless experts coming and going. How could the Blue Burning Marquis possibly inspect every single being that entered the city?

"If the Blue Burning City hadn't set up a special array to detect the mass and weight of life forms, we wouldn't have had such a hard time infiltrating."

The purple-haired woman shook her head slightly.

The powerful experts from the Purple Dream Domain who had infiltrated the Blue Burning City this time were only twelve in number. Any more would easily trigger the alert of the array detecting the mass and weight of life forms, and then attract the attention of the Blue Burning Marquis.

Life forms had mass and weight, and while auras could be suppressed, the mass of life could not be hidden.

As a domain city, occasionally having a dozen or so Sovereign Realm life forms enter was not unusual.

But any more than that would be abnormal.

For example, sending fifty or a hundred Sovereign Realm experts? As long as the total mass of life forms in the Blue Burning City reached the alert level, the Blue Burning Marquis would thoroughly investigate the entire city, even if he hadn't detected any anomalies.

Once the investigation began, the Blue Burning City would enter a state of full defense. At that time, with the Blue Burning Marquis on guard, the Purple Dream Lord might not necessarily succeed.

"This mission is very important. We must use the Myriad Array Crystal to replicate all the formations in the Blue Burning City. We cannot fail."

The purple-haired woman spoke solemnly. If this plan failed, their future attempts might be impossible.
