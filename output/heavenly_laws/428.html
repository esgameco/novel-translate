Chapter 428: Refining the Strongest Weapon (Seeking Subscriptions)

In a daze, Lin Yuan opened his eyes.

"I'm back."

Sensing the immeasurable fluctuations of the rules in the main universe, a smile appeared on Lin Yuan's face.

In the last twenty-odd years in the Boundless Void, Lin Yuan's comprehension of the rules had progressed slowly.

One of the main reasons was that the ocean of rules in the Boundless Void was too shallow.

One could see the bottom with just a glance.

But the main universe?

Lin Yuan saw hope for a significant improvement in his comprehension of the rules.

"In the Boundless Void, the Six Realms of Space-Time was already the limit, theoretically it was impossible to achieve the Seven Realms of Space-Time," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

In fact, even the Six Realms of Space-Time was extremely difficult to attain.

The reason why the creators of the secret realms and the Children of the Void were able to cross into the tenth stage was due to their innate talents and aptitudes.

However, now, Lin Yuan sensed the even more vast and profound fluctuations of space-time beyond the Six Realms.

Of course, sensing it was one thing, being able to comprehend it was another.

It was like an ordinary person seeing a towering mountain in the distance - seeing it and reaching it were two different concepts.

After comprehending the Six Realms of Space-Time, Lin Yuan did not delve deeper.

Apart from the limitations of the Boundless Void, it was also because his own level was too low.

Comprehending the Six Realms at the tenth stage? At least in the main universe, he did not know if there were any predecessors, but he was definitely unprecedented.

As for comprehending the Seven Realms of Space-Time? Lin Yuan did not have that thought for the time being.

"The space-time of the cosmic starry sky is too vast."

At this moment, Lin Yuan's understanding of the cosmic starry sky had changed greatly compared to before the tenth traversal.

Before the tenth traversal, Lin Yuan's boundary of space-time was only at the Second Realm, but now he had reached the Six Realms, with every detail of space-time flooding into his eyes.

"It's too vast, too magnificent."

Lin Yuan observed carefully, marveling in his heart.

If the entire cosmic starry sky was a football field, even with the comprehension of the Six Realms of Space-Time, he could not achieve the "travel by thought" ability he had in the Boundless Void.

This was the difference in the vastness of space-time between the main universe and the Boundless Void.

In the cosmic starry sky, not to mention Lin Yuan who had merely reached the Six Realms of Space-Time comprehension, even a Paramount Realm expert at the twelfth stage could not "travel by thought."

Within the vast domains of the main universe, there were occasionally scattered space-time hazards.

There existed layer upon layer of space-time labyrinths.

Even with the comprehension of the Six Realms of Space-Time, one could not traverse them directly.

"However, with my current control over space-time, coupled with the 'Nine Steps of Space-Time'..."

"Unless a Paramount expert personally pursues me, I would be standing on undefeatable ground."

Lin Yuan was certain in his heart.

Although he could not achieve the "omnipresence" of the Boundless Void in the main universe, his ability to traverse space was far beyond what it was before the traversal.

Add to that the "Nine Steps of Space-Time"...

Not to mention those below the Paramount Realm, Lin Yuan had a feeling that even Paramount experts would have to wait until the fifth or sixth step to have a chance of catching up to him, or they might as well wait until he completed all nine steps.

The Xuanhuang Sect's life-escaping secret technique, the "Nine Steps of Space-Time," was unquestionably formidable in terms of escape speed.

This was a Dharma-protecting secret technique created by the Xuanhuang Emperor himself specifically for the disciples of the Xuanhuang Sect while traversing the Chaos Void.

Although Lin Yuan believed that Paramount experts could not necessarily catch up to him within the nine steps when it came to escaping, he would not be foolish enough to offend a Paramount expert.

If a Paramount expert wanted to kill a life form below the twelfth stage, did they need to personally catch up? A soul attack would be enough.

"My current strength..."

Lin Yuan began to evaluate himself.

But now, even a Legendary Realm expert at the perfected eleventh stage like the Four Celestial Kings, if they did not wield a Paramount weapon, Lin Yuan could easily suppress them.

The Six Realms of Space-Time was simply overwhelming compared to the Five Realms.

If the combat power of a perfected eleventh stage Legendary Realm expert was 10, and the combat power of the Evil Deities at the tenth stage in the Boundless Void was between 15 and 20, then Lin Yuan's combat power would be around 100.

As for the combat power of a twelfth stage Paramount expert, using this as a standard, it would be in the range of millions to hundreds of millions.

Although Lin Yuan was still far from matching a twelfth stage Paramount expert, he had now surpassed ordinary perfected eleventh stage Legendary Realm experts.

In the two-dimensional world, Lin Yuan was able to overwhelm those perfected eleventh stage Legendary Realm experts by taking advantage of the environmental limitations of the two-dimensional world.

In reality, however, he had merely taken a shortcut.

But now, Lin Yuan's true combat power had fully surpassed perfected eleventh stage Legendary Realm experts.

By "fully surpassed," he meant those Legendary Realm experts relying solely on their own abilities, not including external factors.

Compared to the Evil Deities at the tenth stage in the Boundless Void, the perfected eleventh stage Legendary Realm experts in the main universe had a huge advantage - weapons.

The Four Celestial Kings could integrate with a Paramount weapon, causing a significant increase in their combat power.

Other perfected eleventh stage Legendary Realm experts also had the opportunity to obtain a Paramount weapon.

Most perfected eleventh stage Legendary Realm experts in the main universe were backed by either a paramount clan or a Paramount expert.

In order to increase their chances of breaking through to the ultimate realm, the paramount clans or Paramount experts would bestow a Paramount weapon, allowing these Legendary Realm experts to comprehend the power of the twelfth stage in advance.

These Paramount weapons were only temporarily granted. If they successfully crossed into the twelfth stage, they would keep the weapon. If they failed to break through, the weapon would return to the clan.

The reason why the Four Celestial Kings had such a great reputation among the many perfected eleventh stage Legendary Realm experts in the main universe was because of the Paramount weapon they possessed, which was highly compatible with their own abilities.

A Paramount weapon that was compatible with oneself not only allowed the comprehension of more power from the twelfth stage but also enabled the profound exploitation of the weapon's capabilities.

Unfortunately, the Paramount weapon possessed by the Four Celestial Kings was lost in the two-dimensional world, along with the departure of that "blank sheet of paper," resulting in the loss of their fundamental spiritual will.

This caused the Four Celestial Kings to fall from the pinnacle of perfected eleventh stage Legendary Realm experts.

There was no need to elaborate on the enhancement of combat power provided by Paramount weapons.

When fully activated, they could unleash a combat power of hundreds, and this activation was solely through one's own power, without any backup from a Paramount expert within the Paramount weapon.

Based on the combat power of 10 for a perfected eleventh stage Legendary Realm expert, unleashing a combat power of hundreds meant that a Legendary expert wielding a Paramount weapon could easily overwhelm a group of Legendary experts without Paramount weapons.

Of course, a perfected eleventh stage Legendary Realm expert wielding a Paramount weapon could unleash power far beyond their own limits.

However, the cost of activating a Paramount weapon was also terrifyingly high. With the foundation of a perfected eleventh stage Legendary Realm expert, they could not activate a Paramount weapon for more than a few moves.

"Paramount weapons... I have many."

With a thought, Lin Yuan's Solar Celestial Spirit entered the Xuanhuang Secret Realm.

In the cosmic starry sky of the main universe, if one were to discuss the quantity of Paramount weapons, Lin Yuan ranked first among those below the twelfth stage, and no one dared claim second place.

During the journey in the two-dimensional world, Lin Yuan had acquired nearly a hundred wondrous items.

Even if half of these wondrous items were just twelfth-grade precious materials, the number of Paramount weapons still exceeded thirty.

Thirty Paramount weapons? Although not as many as the treasuries of paramount clans, it was enough to make any perfected eleventh stage Legendary Realm expert look up in awe.

In fact, they could not even look up.

Almost all perfected eleventh stage Legendary Realm experts had only temporarily "borrowed" the Paramount weapon they possessed from their clans.

Whoosh.

Lin Yuan's figure appeared in the deepest part of the Xuanhuang Secret Realm.

This was also where many wondrous items were stored.

"Paramount weapons..."

With a thought, thirty-two Paramount weapons appeared before him.

The thirty-two Paramount weapons were neatly arranged, including swords, guns, cauldrons, and towers, with various types.

"Now I can choose a few Paramount weapons."

Lin Yuan stroked his chin.

Previously, Lin Yuan had not chosen any Paramount weapons because he wanted to refine them.

Even for a preliminary refinement, it required the boundary of comprehension at the Five Realms of Space-Time.

Without reaching the Five Realms of Space-Time, it was impossible to activate the vast space-time within a Paramount weapon, let alone refine it.

This was the case even for a perfected eleventh stage Legendary Realm expert.
