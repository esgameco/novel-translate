"What realm of the Shaman Ancestor are you currently at?" The Xia Qinzhi Supremacy fell silent for a moment before asking.

The two-dimensional world within the white paper could block the perception of even a Supremacy.

Therefore, even the Xia Qinzhi Supremacy could not discern Lin Yuan's current realm.

"Truly the nineteenth realm of the Shaman Ancestor?" The Xia Qinzhi Supremacy looked at Lin Yuan, even though he had already anticipated this.

But when Lin Yuan said it himself, the Xia Qinzhi Supremacy was still somewhat shaken.

Yet Lin Yuan, by himself, had already crossed into the nineteenth realm of the Shaman Ancestor?

Admittedly, the 'two-dimensional world' that the nine human Supremacies had descended into was merely a forced recreation by the Virtual Realm, with only one-tenth or one-twentieth of the similarity.

Moreover, the cultivation methods deduced by the nine Supremacies were not limited to any particular life form but could be popularized to a certain extent.

In terms of difficulty, they were undoubtedly higher than the cultivation methods explored by the powerful beings within the two-dimensional world.

But they were Supremacies! Ultimate existences! Twelfth-rank life forms!

The vision and insight of Supremacies far surpassed those of eleventh-rank life forms, let alone Lin Yuan, who was only a tenth-rank being.

Under such circumstances, it was understandable for those legendary perfected eleventh-rank life forms to reach the eighteenth or nineteenth realms of the Shaman Ancestor through their own exploration. But Lin Yuan?

"You..." The Xia Qinzhi Supremacy sighed. "You really gave me a fright."

Originally, when one of Lin Yuan's avatars fell into the two-dimensional world, the Xia Qinzhi Supremacy was extremely worried, fearing it would affect Lin Yuan's hope of reaching the ultimate realm.

The nine human Supremacies had joined forces, mobilizing the Virtual Realm to recreate the two-dimensional world, at no small cost.

Yet the Xia Qinzhi Supremacy had insisted on doing so, concerned that Lin Yuan's avatar might be crippled in the two-dimensional world.

But now it seemed that the Xia Qinzhi Supremacy's worries were completely unnecessary.

If Lin Yuan truly possessed the strength of the nineteenth realm of the Shaman Ancestor, he must have benefited greatly from that two-dimensional world.

"I only just managed to cross into the nineteenth realm of the Shaman Ancestor not long ago," Lin Yuan immediately said.

"Not long ago?"

"Just barely managed?"

"If you could cultivate to the nineteenth realm of the Shaman Ancestor in such a short time, our human civilization wouldn't need to actively seek out the Stellar Alliance."

A smile appeared on the Xia Qinzhi Supremacy's face.

"Seek out the Stellar Alliance?" Lin Yuan was slightly puzzled.

The Stellar Alliance was a union of various special life forms across the cosmos, on par with the pinnacle race alliances like the human civilization.

"If we don't seek the Stellar Alliance's help, it will be difficult to bring out those wondrous objects from the two-dimensional world," the Xia Qinzhi Supremacy explained. "Currently, the four Celestial Kings of the Stellar Alliance are the life forms most likely to pass through the Celestial Gate and return to the cosmic universe."

"The wondrous objects obtained by the evolutionary beings of our human civilization in the two-dimensional world can only be brought out with the help of the four Celestial Kings."

The value of those wondrous objects could only be fully realized once they were taken out of the two-dimensional world.

However, the evolutionary beings of the human civilization did not perform impressively within the two-dimensional world. At least based on the current situation, they could not compete with powerful beings like the four Celestial Kings for a spot to enter the Celestial Gate.

But now that the Xia Qinzhi Supremacy learned of Lin Yuan's strength, a glimmer of hope arose within him.

Given Lin Yuan's current realm, even if he was still no match for the four Celestial Kings, he could set his sights on the next or the one after that opening of the Celestial Gate.

"I see," Lin Yuan nodded.

Seeking help from the Stellar Alliance, seeking help from the four Celestial Kings, would inevitably require paying a greater price, as it would be a request.

But the human civilization had no other choice. In the two-dimensional world, the advantages of special life forms were infinitely amplified.

It wasn't just the human civilization; even pinnacle races like the Insect Race and the Feathered Race were unable to compete with special life forms like the Stellar Alliance in the two-dimensional world.

"Of course, if you manage to pass through the Celestial Gate and leave the two-dimensional world in the future, bringing out those wondrous objects of our human civilization, you won't be doing it for free. For each wondrous object, based on its value, you'll be given fifty percent."

The Xia Qinzhi Supremacy immediately said.

Merely bringing out the wondrous objects would earn Lin Yuan fifty percent of their value, which seemed exaggerated.

But one had to understand that if the wondrous objects were not brought out, the human civilization would gain nothing at all.

Moreover, this fifty percent value was not being given to the Stellar Alliance but to one of their own. The Xia Qinzhi Supremacy was more than willing to do so.

"No problem," Lin Yuan immediately agreed.

It was like receiving a free gift.

"I just don't know if I'll be able to enter the Celestial Gate."

Lin Yuan expressed some hesitation.

"With your cultivation speed, as long as you avoid the four Celestial Kings and focus your efforts on the second or third opening of the Celestial Gate, there is still great hope."

The Xia Qinzhi Supremacy said.

"Avoid the four Celestial Kings?" Lin Yuan's heart stirred. The four Celestial Kings' reputation for might was immense, whether in the two-dimensional world or the main cosmos.

"Yes, that supreme weapon of the four Celestial Kings is perfectly suited for them. Even if your realm is higher and your strength greater, your overall combat power would still be inferior."

"With your potential and cultivation speed, there's no need to compete with the four Celestial Kings. The risk is too great."

The Xia Qinzhi Supremacy shook his head.

"Understood," Lin Yuan's thoughts aligned with the Xia Qinzhi Supremacy's.

Unless he had absolute confidence, Lin Yuan would not take the risk of competing for the first opening of the Celestial Gate.

Time was on Lin Yuan's side. The longer the time, the greater the advantage his extraordinary comprehension would bring.

In just over a hundred years, Lin Yuan was about to catch up to those legendary perfected eleventh-rank life forms. Give him another ten thousand years, and even Lin Yuan himself didn't know what level he might reach.

"By the way..."

"Since you've already reached the nineteenth realm of the Shaman Ancestor, the cultivation methods we deduced don't hold much significance for you. However, you could try comprehending the essence of the power captured by the 'Virtual Realm' in recreating the two-dimensional world."

The Xia Qinzhi Supremacy said. The essence of power captured through the 'Virtual Realm' was the foundation upon which the nine Supremacies had deduced the cultivation methods of the Shaman Ancestor.

Originally, even if that essence of power were placed before tenth or eleventh-rank life forms, they would not be able to comprehend much from it.

But seeing Lin Yuan's performance, the Xia Qinzhi Supremacy had a different perspective.

After all, it wouldn't cause any harm to let Lin Yuan try comprehending it.

"The essence of power?" Lin Yuan's eyes lit up.

While in the two-dimensional world, comprehending the essence of power from that realm was slow due to the world's inherent suppression.

But out here, without the limitations of the two-dimensional world, comprehending that essence of power would undoubtedly yield great gains.

"Yes, your true self is in the main galaxy, right? I'll have Feather guide a strand of the 'Virtual Realm's' essence of power for you to comprehend."

The Xia Qinzhi Supremacy said.

"Thank you, Supremacy," Lin Yuan expressed his gratitude.

For the peerless Feather Supremacy to run an errand like this, it was likely an honor that few in the history of the human civilization had received.

"Your asking price is too high," said the supreme being Xia Qin, aware of Lin Yuan's true strength, feeling very confident, and was just stalling the other party.

The Virtual Realm Heaven and Earth originated from the greatest opportunity in the history of the human civilization, before their long period of hibernation, when countless treasures and legacies rained down into the universe.

When Lin Yuan's solar spirit fell into that two-dimensional world, it was difficult for him to fully experience the entirety of it.

But now, Lin Yuan had a concrete concept of this two-dimensional world.

"So this is the essential nature of the power in the two-dimensional world?"

Compared to the supreme beings of the human civilization who could only sense the overall aura of the two-dimensional world, Lin Yuan had his spirit avatar present within it.

By comparing the two, the gains he received were far greater than those of the supreme beings.

"Two, there are even vaster hidden spaces within the two-dimensional world that the Virtual Realm Heaven and Earth cannot detect, and thus cannot replicate."

"Never mind."

"What secrets are hidden in that two-dimensional world, and how is that related to me?"

Then, when the heavenly gate opened, he would defeat the other competitors and leave the two-dimensional world to return to the main universe.

In the two-dimensional world, while sensing the essence of heaven and earth, Lin Yuan took out the silver mirror and gently caressed it.

Even in this two-dimensional world, the existence of spiritual power was still allowed.

However, like other supernatural powers, spiritual power was also greatly suppressed.

Lin Yuan, at the nineteenth level of the Shaman Ancestor realm, could condense a considerable amount of spiritual power, giving him the confidence to expend some on the mirror.

For other powerful beings, losing even a sliver of spiritual power might cause adverse effects like a decline in state and a significant decrease in strength.

Cha Ran Gu appeared outside, wanting to see Lin Yuan.

"The royal family of the Blood Rain Ancient Kingdom wishes to see you, Master," Cha Ran Gu quickly said.

If it was when he had just descended, Lin Yuan might have been wary of the Blood Rain Ancient Kingdom's royalty.

But now? Not just the Blood Rain Ancient Kingdom's royalty, even if the royalties of all twelve ancient kingdoms combined, Lin Yuan wouldn't frown.
