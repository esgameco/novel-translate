And the Ancestors also promised that if they discovered a particularly special future timeline, they would tell him in time.

He could feel that his spiritual will had already approached a certain limit, and that to achieve another exponential surge, he would probably have to wait until he crossed into the Thirteenth Realm and experienced a leap in his fundamental essence of life.

"However, three hundred million spiritual will is already enough for me to divert a portion of my mental power to try and explore the next source world."

Lin Yuan never forgot the coordinates of the thirty-three source worlds deep within the Multiverse Gate.

After crossing into the Twelfth Realm, the way Lin Yuan traveled and descended had changed. The traversal after the Twelfth Realm would not affect his true body, but in return, the burden on his spiritual will had increased significantly.

To the extent that Lin Yuan's spiritual will at the time could only bear the exploration of one source world. If he wanted to explore the second source world, he would have to abandon the first one.

But now, with the substantial increase in his spiritual will, Lin Yuan was confident that he could try to explore the second source world while maintaining the true body in the Chaotic Void and the first source world.

"The second source world."

Lin Yuan's expression became slightly solemn.

The reason he had not explored the second source world was not only due to the pressure on his spiritual will, but also because of the warning reminder from the Multiverse Gate:

The second source world contained unknown dangers, and reckless exploration and descent would encounter irreversible consequences! Lin Yuan believed the judgment of the Multiverse Gate, so he had not taken any action.

But now?

The substantial increase Lin Yuan had gained was not only in his spiritual will, but also in his own strength.

To be precise, the improvement in his spiritual will stemmed from the enhancement of his strength.

And the danger depends on the strength. With low strength, the danger is naturally greater; with high strength, the danger becomes lower.

"Let me take a look now."

Lin Yuan's mind immediately sank into the depths of his consciousness, looking towards the majestic and grand Multiverse Gate.

Each time Lin Yuan observed the Multiverse Gate, he had a different feeling, and this time was no exception. The Multiverse Gate gave Lin Yuan a sense that it was superior to the very source of the source worlds.

Lin Yuan carefully observed that the Multiverse Gate deep in his consciousness was a true projection of the real Multiverse Gate, because with his current essence of life, even though he had been recognized by the Multiverse Gate, he still could not bear the descent of the true Multiverse Gate.

Lin Yuan's expression was thoughtful. At this moment, he had already confirmed that the existence of the Multiverse Gate was definitely beyond the Thirteenth Realm, at least superior to the six ancestors of the six ancient countries, as his current feeling was still not as strong as the Multiverse Gate.

Lin Yuan's heart was certain that without a specific warning, it meant that this source world posed no real threat to him.

For Lin Yuan, as long as it did not involve the true body in other dimensions, even if the true body in this dimension perished, it would not be a big deal, at most he would just start over.

"This world."

Lin Yuan began to briefly observe the details of the second source world from the perspective of the Multiverse Gate.

"Something's not right?"

"Interesting."

"Since there is no real danger, I'll just descend directly."

In the depths of his mind, Lin Yuan looked at the majestic and grand Multiverse Gate, and after just a moment of contemplation, he immediately began to traverse.

The destination was the second source world.
