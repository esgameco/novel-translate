Qi Yuan glanced at the fifteen Yang Gods with disdain: "They're too useless, with so little Ominous Qi, they can be killed with just a stick of incense."

If these fifteen Yang Gods had enough Ominous Qi to continuously revive, this single knife strike could last for three thousand years, endlessly slaying and resurrecting the Yang Gods until they were utterly destroyed.

This place would become a forbidden land or a cosmic wonder, worthy of mankind's reverence.

Beside him, Shangguan Qingteng and Shangguan Qingmiao's pupils shrank dramatically.

What had they seen? What was this? He... was truly the Realm Lord.

How was that possible?

To prove one's status as a Realm Lord, one must sacrifice all the Mandate Bearers.

Could it be... from another universe? Even more impossible.

His mind was in turmoil, unable to comprehend.

"Why am I so strong?" Qi Yuan paused, then said, "Because I understand the Art of War, and I excel at using strength to overcome weakness, so I am undefeatable in battle."

Shangguan Qingteng and Shangguan Qingmiao felt the air suddenly grow colder, not just an illusion.

Because after telling his cold joke, Qi Yuan would deliberately make the air colder to match.

Without further addressing the two, Qi Yuan said indifferently, "Those who obstruct my mortal transformation, I shall kill without mercy!"

This occurred in countless realms in the Chaotic Stellar Realm.

Mighty hands descended, slaughtering all Ominous Spirits and Ominous Gods.

Even Yang Gods with infinite Ominous Qi revival were slain by Qi Yuan.

Countless people looked up at the sky, their faces filled with shock and amazement.

"What is that?"

They did not understand, but they knew that from this day on, the heavens above the Chaotic Stellar Realm had changed.

In less than a breath, the Ominous Spirits that had once terrorized the Qing Shan Realm were all slaughtered and exterminated.

Many looked up at the sky, their eyes filled with fear and horror.

"What kind of mighty existence is this?"

"A celestial being?"

"A god has descended?"

"Brother-in-law? Is that Brother-in-law's voice?" someone exclaimed joyfully, like a fool.

Others were amazed, for the overbearing and extraordinary voice in the sky was indeed too familiar.

At the same time, in the Imperial City, a beautiful and petite woman heard the voice in the sky, her eyes flashing with wild delight: "Cai Lao?"

As the one who had transformed into the Realm Lord, Qi Yuan could hear any voice related to him, even if he was countless miles away.

When he heard "Cai Lao", he couldn't help but make a face.

However, being a person of quality, he couldn't be bothered to pay attention.

His gaze seemed to transcend time and space, falling on Shen Lingfeng in Feng Shan Prefecture.

"Tell my mother-in-law that Ling Xuan is safe with me now."

Shen Lingfeng's eyes shone with joy: "It really is you, Brother-in-law!"

At the same time, in the Shen Residence, the Shen Family patriarch and his wife saw the giant hand in the sky, and like ordinary people, they were filled with fear of the unknown.

However, the Shen Family matriarch had a strange feeling: "Dear, do you feel like that voice sounds familiar?"

"Careful with your words, don't provoke this mighty existence!" the Shen patriarch hurriedly said anxiously.

Such a powerful existence could not be fathomed by common logic.

Just then, Shen Lingfeng rushed in.

"Father, Mother... Brother-in-law!"
