Instantly, the yellow and black prison trapping the two supreme great sages began to collapse, and the Great Sage of Chaotic Heavens and the Great Sage of Jade Unicorn inside started to disintegrate as well.

"Last time you survived, but this time, you won't."

After all, the two supreme great sages would also conceal their own causal threads.

But while he could not locate the positions of the two supreme great sages through their causal threads, he could still determine whether they were alive or not based on the existence or disappearance of their causal threads.

Now that the causal threads of Hunxiao the great sage and Yulian the great sage had disappeared, this disappearance was so complete, as if they had been erased from existence.

Just as Lin Yuan's thoughts were wandering,

The burly and mighty man appeared not far away, taking a step forward and standing before Lin Yuan.

"I know you have many questions."

The burly and mighty man looked at Lin Yuan, with a faint smile on his face: "Don't be in a hurry, I will explain everything to you."
