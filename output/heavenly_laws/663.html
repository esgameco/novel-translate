In less than a moment, the surroundings became completely clear, with only various weapons, treasures, and precious items floating about.

"Before entering the Chaos Realm, my strength was on par with the mightiest Sovereign Immortals like Fuxu Immortal Sovereign, at most slightly stronger. But after entering the Chaos Realm, the essence of my life has been transformed. So-called Sovereign Immortals? They are no match for me at all."

Lin Yuan thought to himself that if it weren't for the independent time-space of the treasure land, prolonged use of the 'World' divine ability would have conflicted with the treasure land's time-space, affecting the future cultivation of precious items.

Lin Yuan didn't even need to refine one core celestial pillar after another, he could simply envelop the entire treasure land with his external universe.

It was only because Fuxu Immortal Sovereign and many other peak Sovereign Immortals had scattered and fled that Lin Yuan was able to briefly use his external universe to annihilate them.

The use of the 'World' divine ability didn't last long, and with Lin Yuan's careful control, it didn't have any impact on the treasure land.

"Phew!"

Lin Yuan's thoughts moved, and he retracted his external universe, as well as the spoils left behind by the peak Sovereign Immortals.

For this display of strength that exceeded the understanding of the Six Ancient Kingdoms, Lin Yuan had already prepared an explanation, attributing it all to the Ancestor's bloodline at the Sovereign Immortal level.

As the first existence in this source world to have excavated the Ancestor's bloodline to the Sovereign Immortal level, aside from Lin Yuan, no other Sovereign Immortal knew the effects of the Ancestor's bloodline reaching that level.

Wasn't everything still under Lin Yuan's control? As for when the second Sovereign Immortal-level Ancestor's bloodline would be born, that would be tens of thousands of years later, by which time Lin Yuan would have already entered the Thirteen Realms and be deeply entrenched in the time loop, unaffected by the outside world.

In fact, even without the Sovereign Immortal-level Ancestor's bloodline as an excuse, no one would dare to question Lin Yuan. The Six Ancient Kingdoms revered strength, and with the combat power Lin Yuan had displayed, the Ancestor not intervening meant he was invincible. Those mightiest Sovereign Immortals didn't dare to appear before Lin Yuan.

As for the Ancestor? The Ancestor was lofty and aloof, and while Lin Yuan's strength surpassed the mightiest Sovereign Immortals, to the Thirteen Realms Ancestor, he was merely a slightly larger ant, not worthy of much attention.

Currently, aside from the Multiverse Gate, all of Lin Yuan's trump cards wouldn't be taken seriously by the Ancestors.

The Thirteen Realms inheritance? It originally came from the Ancestor.

Thirteen Realms weapons and treasures? The Ancestors had them as well.

Whoosh.

Lin Yuan briefly inspected the spoils left behind by the peak Sovereign Immortals, then took a step and appeared before Crimson Flame Sovereign.

"Silver River, Silver River Sovereign." Crimson Flame Sovereign was inwardly shaken, barely suppressing it, his expression reverent and his posture very low as he looked at Lin Yuan: "I never imagined that your strength, Silver River Sovereign, would be so powerful, single-handedly sweeping away Fuxu Immortal Sovereign and many other top peak Sovereign Immortals. When the Emperor mentioned you earlier, I had some doubts, but now I realize how foolish that was."

Crimson Flame Sovereign felt somewhat ashamed when he thought about his previous 'disdain' for Silver River Sovereign, and a sense of trepidation also arose.

"It's nothing."

Lin Yuan shook his head slightly.

Crimson Flame Sovereign didn't have any personal motives; his doubts about Lin Yuan's strength were for the sake of the Vermilion Sun Ancient Kingdom, after all, in the competition for a precious treasure land, the participating Sovereign Immortals' strength had to be up to par.

If a certain participating Sovereign Immortal had dragged them down, causing the failure to seize the treasure land, that would have been detrimental to the entire ancient kingdom.

After Lin Yuan sparred with Scarlet Lotus Sovereign and proved his strength, Crimson Flame Sovereign didn't say anything further.

"Hm?"

At this moment, Crimson Flame Sovereign looked up and saw more than ten figures suddenly appear in the distance, which were the twelve peak Sovereign Immortals of the Savage Wilderness Ancient Kingdom.

After sensing the battle here, or more accurately, feeling the one-sided crushing of Silver River Sovereign, the peak Sovereign Immortals of the Savage Wilderness Ancient Kingdom, although finding it hard to believe, could only muster the courage to come over.

If they didn't come, were they supposed to wait for Silver River Sovereign to come to them?

"Silver River Sovereign!"

Savage Kun Sovereign was inwardly shaken. Through sensing the aura, he clearly understood that Fuxu Immortal Sovereign and many other top peak Sovereign Immortals had been swept away by Silver River Sovereign.

It was an absolute sweeping; even Fuxu Immortal Sovereign, whose strength Savage Kun Sovereign disliked, was utterly crushed by Silver River Sovereign.

"Savage Kun."

Lin Yuan also looked towards Savage Kun Sovereign.

The core celestial pillars occupied by the Savage Wilderness Ancient Kingdom were mostly concentrated in the upper half of the treasure land.

When Lin Yuan started pushing from the corner, he didn't encounter the core celestial pillars occupied and refined by the Savage Wilderness Ancient Kingdom.

"Silver River Sovereign, our Savage Wilderness Ancient Kingdom is willing to hand over all the core celestial pillars," Savage Kun Sovereign immediately said.

Seeing the fate of the other ancient kingdoms' peak Sovereign Immortals, whose main combat bodies had fallen one after another, in this situation, the wisest choice was to honestly hand over the core celestial pillars.

At least they could preserve their main combat bodies.

As well as the harvests they had accumulated in the treasure land over the past one or two thousand years, the Savage Wilderness Ancient Kingdom's peak Sovereign Immortals had collected quite a few of the golden mists.

"Alright, you can leave now."

Lin Yuan nodded, and didn't make things difficult for Savage Kun Sovereign.

He had shown no mercy towards the peak Sovereign Immortals of the Yin Ancient Kingdom, the Great Spirit Ancient Kingdom, and others, because they were enemies of the Vermilion Sun Ancient Kingdom, and had even demanded dozens of core celestial pillars as an astronomical price.

"Thank you, Silver River Sovereign."

Savage Kun Sovereign felt a little relieved in his heart.

At least this time in the treasure land competition, the Savage Wilderness Ancient Kingdom's peak Sovereign Immortals had gained some harvests, not ending up with a net loss.

Compared to powerhouses like Fuxu Immortal Sovereign, Ancestral Yin Sovereign, and Nether Hell Sovereign, the Savage Wilderness Ancient Kingdom was already considered fortunate.

The other eleven peak Sovereign Immortals of the Savage Wilderness Ancient Kingdom looked at each other, not knowing why a sense of relief was welling up in their hearts.

In the past, when new treasure lands were born and fought over, the many peak Sovereign Immortals of the Savage Wilderness Ancient Kingdom who participated often ended up in failure, their hearts filled with resentment and unwillingness.

But now, including Savage Kun Sovereign, the twelve peak Sovereign Immortals actually felt relieved.

Fortunately, Savage Kun Sovereign came quickly and communicated well with Silver River Sovereign, so they were able to be spared.

Otherwise, they would have met the same fate as the peak Sovereign Immortals of the Great Abyss Ancient Kingdom and the others.

In the competition for treasure lands, death and annihilation were the most normal occurrences. Even if Silver River Sovereign hadn't spared them, there would have been nothing wrong with that.

With Silver River Sovereign's permission, the twelve peak Sovereign Immortals of the Savage Wilderness Ancient Kingdom quickly left the treasure land entrance.

In the distance, the ten peak Sovereign Immortals of the Vermilion Sun Ancient Kingdom looked at Lin Yuan, and for a moment, they didn't dare to approach.

The victory had come too easily and too unbelievably. Just a moment ago, they had felt desperate, believing that the Great Abyss Ancient Kingdom's alliance with the Yin Ancient Kingdom, the Great Spirit Ancient Kingdom, and the Thunder Roar Ancient Kingdom meant that even with the Savage Wilderness Ancient Kingdom on their side, the Vermilion Sun Ancient Kingdom's hope of seizing the treasure land was slim.

The Celestial Sovereign of the Milky Way is the first Unrivaled Celestial Sovereign since the opening of the Six Great Ancient Kingdoms, and probably also the last.
