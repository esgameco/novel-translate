Boom!

Accompanied by Lin Yuan's dragging, as if pulling the entire Origin World, the vast demonic energy permeating the four corners of heaven and earth boiled simultaneously.

Under the sky, the Demon God who was originally looking down suddenly stumbled. Its giant tail struggled mightily at the end, working with the surrounding demonic energy to forcibly break free from Lin Yuan's grip and return behind it.

"Haha, quite strong indeed."

Lin Yuan glanced at his right hand. The surface of his palm was now covered with dense black holes, all from the corrosion encountered while gripping the giant tail.

Was the Demon God's tail so easy to grab? The intense backlash produced during the process was enough to give even a 13th-level Eternal being a headache.

Lin Yuan dared to do this only after fully completing the creation of his Infinite Sand Particle Worlds, relying on each particle world to share the backlash.

"Lord Galaxy actually..."

In the Hidden World, the several sect leaders were completely shocked.

If earlier, Lin Yuan was just using all his strength to resist the Demon God's attacks.

Then just now, actively dragging the Demon God's tail, destabilizing its foundation and nearly pulling it along, meant that Lin Yuan already possessed the direct strength to shake the Demon God.

"Lord Galaxy is not an Eternal Lord, how could he possibly shake a Demon God?"

The minds of the many high-level members of the Eternal Sect went blank.

Barely blocking the Demon God's attacks versus turning around and shaking the Demon God were two completely different things.

The latter meant that the strength difference from the Demon God was not very large, and could even be said to be on the same level.

In the four corners of heaven and earth, the experts of various supreme holy sects and holy sects were also watching and observing.

After Lin Yuan revealed his true form that reached the heavens, even from far away, they could faintly see and perceive him resisting the Demon God's attacks.

"Lord Galaxy shook the Demon God?"

"Wasn't it said that Demon Gods are on par with Eternal Lords?"

Many experts were filled with disbelief. Although the struggle between Lin Yuan and the Demon God essentially involved countless mysteries, what was displayed was just punches and kicks.

It wasn't very difficult to distinguish.

The black holes on the surface of his right hand quickly healed.

Lin Yuan looked at the Demon God, his gaze exuding intense fighting spirit.

After refining the Eternal Source, the infinite sand particle worlds within his body had fully completed their creation. Each particle world could resonate with each other, erupting with power at least ten or a hundred times stronger than before when the creation was not yet complete. It was precisely because of this.

That Lin Yuan was able to go from barely resisting the Demon God's attacks to directly shaking the Demon God.

Because if he went all out now, he could indeed pose a threat to the Demon God. Not a life-or-death threat, but at least enough to make the opponent take him seriously.

"It's only polite to return the favor."

Lin Yuan smiled as he looked at the somewhat dazed Demon God.

His form directly merged into space-time, disappearing from the spot.

"Hmm?"

Seeing this, the Demon God's hollow eyes showed solemnity. This realm was filled with demonic energy, and the demonic energy was equivalent to the Demon God's domain. Wherever the demonic energy covered was within the Demon God's control.

But the moment Lin Yuan disappeared, the Demon God sensed that it had lost control over the surrounding realm.

This loss was forced, without any warning.

The next moment.

Lin Yuan's enormous body appeared beside the Demon God, raising his hand to deliver a slap.

The Demon God's body also began to rapidly grow larger, becoming as tall as Lin Yuan, its giant tail whipping out again.

Colliding with Lin Yuan's slap.

Crash!

Large amounts of space-time and the world were constantly being annihilated. This annihilation was fundamental, enough to cause irreversible effects on the Origin World's essence. But at this moment, neither Lin Yuan nor the Demon God cared about these things.

Lin Yuan had not yet crossed into the 13th level. Although his foundation had accumulated to the extreme, when truly facing a Demon God on par with Eternal Lords, he naturally dared not hold back in any way.

Boom! In this clash, the Demon God was clearly at a disadvantage. Lin Yuan's sudden disappearance and reappearance caught it off guard, only able to barely defend.

The result was that the Demon God retreated several steps backwards.

Before the Demon God could react.

Lin Yuan's form disappeared once again.

"Roar!!!"

Without any hesitation, the Demon God let out a roar! This roar was like the rage of heaven and earth, an apocalyptic roar.

The 'black ripples' formed by the roar swept out in all directions. The earth was torn apart by the roar, space-time also collapsed in the roar. Most of the central area of heaven and earth was affected by the roar, constantly being annihilated.

As for the nearby Hidden World, at the critical moment another defensive Eternal Treasure was activated. Combined with the fact that the Demon God's roar was not aimed at the Hidden World, it barely managed to withstand.

As the 'black ripples' spread, Lin Yuan's form was forced to appear, gazing at the Demon God from afar.

The black ripples were very powerful, but still could not overcome Lin Yuan at this moment. As the infinite sand particle worlds operated, the destructive power formed by the 'black ripples' was diluted in an instant.

No matter how strong an attack, when dispersed into infinite sand particles and averaged across each particle world, it no longer posed such a great threat.

"Demon God's Roar, this is the Demon God's Roar, the Demon God's trump card killing move."

Inside the Hidden World, the several sect leaders looked with shock at the 'black ripples' spreading in all directions.

In the various futures predicted by the Eternal Lords, after the Demon God completely suppressed the Origin World's consciousness with its own instinctive consciousness, it would use this move when focusing its efforts on attacking the Hidden World.

And according to the Eternal Lords' foresight, by the time the Demon God used this move, the situation in this Origin World would be considered extremely dire.

Because under the Demon God's Roar, even the Hidden World protected by multiple defensive Eternal Treasures could not hold out for too long.

"The killing move that the Demon God would only use after endless years, forced out early by Lord Galaxy..."

Because since the great change in heaven and earth, there had been no heavenly phenomena indicating someone crossing into the realm of Eternal Lord.

Lin Yuan also stopped, looking towards the Demon God that had stopped in the distance.

In the hidden world, countless living beings looked up, the Demon God's disappearance allowing them to more directly sense Lin Yuan's enormity. "Is it over?"

The expressions of several sect masters were excited.

Outside the source world.

In a distorted space-time.

More than ten towering figures stood here, their gazes occasionally looking towards the distant source world.

However, at this moment, the area around the source world was filled with infinite demonic energy, as if the entire source world was wrapped in endless darkness.

But just then.

The expression of one of the towering figures suddenly changed, its gaze turning back towards the distant source world.
