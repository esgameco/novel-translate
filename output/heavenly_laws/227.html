"An ordinary member of the Magic Jade Tower?"

"Magic Jade Tower?"

Lin Yuan was shocked in his heart. He had inexplicably joined the Magic Jade Tower?

Having comprehended the 3,333 paths of evolution, Lin Yuan felt it was incredible. He had only comprehended them and hadn't actually cultivated them. How did the other party know he had accomplished this?

"Magic Jade Tower"

Lin Yuan's thoughts surged. At the level he was at now, he would at least have heard of any slightly notable races or forces in the main world, even if he didn't fully understand them. However, before today, he had never heard of the three words "Magic Jade Tower".

"Is it a force related to the owner of this body? From outside the main world?" Lin Yuan thought to himself.

The prerequisite for becoming an ordinary member of the Magic Jade Tower was to comprehend the 3,333 paths of evolution within that body. It was easy to associate that body with the "Magic Jade Tower".

"This kind of means..." Lin Yuan marveled in his heart. Originally, he thought that the greatest opportunity from this corpse, which was worth several top powerhouses fighting over, was that supreme path of evolution.

But now it seemed that becoming a member of the Magic Jade Tower was the ultimate benefit.

"Magic Jade Tower? Why is it named after magic?"

The clear whispers Lin Yuan heard were not in any language he knew, yet he could inexplicably understand their meaning.

And the name Magic Jade Tower sounded like it might not be a positive force.

Moreover, if that top powerhouse's corpse that floated in from the permanent spatial rift was related to the Magic Jade Tower, then why did he die?

Many doubts flashed through Lin Yuan's mind.

"The Magic Jade Imprint..."

Lin Yuan began to carefully observe his soul.

After he comprehended the 3,333 paths of evolution, these paths underwent changes and condensed into the so-called "Magic Jade Imprint", which automatically made Lin Yuan an ordinary member of the Magic Jade Tower.

So that "Magic Jade Imprint" should be the key.

Lin Yuan remembered that this imprint ultimately merged into the depths of his own soul.

"Found it."

Lin Yuan quickly found an imprint of a "Nine-Layered Black Tower" deep in his soul.

"Hmm?"

As Lin Yuan's mind merged with this imprint, he immediately received a large amount of information.

"So that's how it is..."

There were two ways to become a member of the Magic Jade Tower.

The first way was to cultivate any one of the comprehended paths of evolution.

Each path of evolution contained within that body had huge flaws and hidden dangers.

Cultivating it, making up for its deficiencies, and overcoming many difficulties would automatically make one an ordinary member of the Magic Jade Tower.

The second way was like Lin Yuan's. Without cultivating, just by comprehending and seeing through the 3,333 paths of evolution, it would trigger a certain threshold, condense the Magic Jade Imprint, and automatically make one a member of the Magic Jade Tower.

These two methods mainly tested two aspects.

The former tested whether the evolver could overcome repeated difficulties when faced with a thorny path and make it to the end.

The latter tested the evolver's comprehension and mental willpower.

It should be known that without cultivating, just comprehending would continually face pressure on one's mental willpower.

To be able to comprehend 3,333 paths of evolution under such premises, as long as the time spent did not exceed 100,000 years, that comprehension ability could be called terrifying. The Magic Jade Tower very much welcomed such evolvers to join.

"The owner of this body should have been a top powerhouse from the Magic Jade Tower."

"It's just unknown for what reason he perished, and his body carried many of the Magic Jade Tower's 'wrong' paths of evolution."

"Perhaps it can't be said he carried them, but rather this top powerhouse himself cultivated them, which is why we could comprehend them..."

Lin Yuan speculated in his heart.

"And this 'Magic Jade Imprint'..."

With the help of this imprint, Lin Yuan vaguely sensed that he could perceive a mysterious space.

This mysterious space was not in the main world, and the "Magic Jade Imprint" was like a key. As long as Lin Yuan was willing, he could enter.

At the same time, Lin Yuan also received a warning.

"Entry into the Magic Jade Space is forbidden before reaching the Eighth Stage Limit!"

"Magic Jade Space?"

"One can only enter at the Eighth Stage Limit?"

Lin Yuan thought to himself that although his current inner world had reached over 100,000 miles in radius and could be said to not be much weaker than an Eighth Stage Limit evolver, who knew what aspect the Magic Jade Space tested Eighth Stage Limit evolvers on? What if it wasn't the inner world?

Although Lin Yuan was very curious about that Magic Jade Space, he also felt he should obediently wait until reaching the Eighth Stage Limit before considering exploring it.

In any case, with Lin Yuan's cultivation speed, reaching the Eighth Stage Limit wouldn't take too long.

"Condensing the Magic Jade Imprint didn't attract the attention of others?"

Lin Yuan opened his eyes and looked around, discovering that the other evolvers were still painstakingly comprehending that body. From time to time, an evolver's body would collapse and a new evolver would quickly fill in.

"I can continue comprehending?"

Lin Yuan looked at that body and found it still tested the comprehension of more paths of evolution.

"I can feel that the speed of comprehension has increased, and my mental willpower has also been enhanced to a certain degree. Is it because of that 'Magic Jade Imprint'?" Lin Yuan thought in his heart.

The Magic Jade Imprint not only served as a key, but also stabilized Lin Yuan's soul and mental willpower, like a calming divine needle, roughly equivalent to an Eighth Stage soul defense treasure standing guard.

"It's too incredible."

Lin Yuan shook his head slightly. Just one imprint could have the effect of an Eighth Stage soul defense treasure? The origins of this Magic Jade Tower were unimaginable.

In order to comprehend that body, Lin Yuan had spent most of his time on it, resulting in not seeing his family for almost one or two years.

"It's okay. Xiaoyuan, you are now the 13th Peak Master of the Vermilion Roc Star Domain. It's normal to be busy," his mother Lu Qiong said, shaking her head.

At most, she would just nag a few more times usually and naturally wouldn't delay Lin Yuan's affairs.
