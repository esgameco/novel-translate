In the future, the chances of surviving the great destruction of the universe would drop significantly.

But if they didn't hand over their treasures, they wouldn't even have a chance to survive the universe's destruction.

The many ultimate twelfth-level beings naturally knew how to choose.

In fact, during the time they were suppressed in the palace, many twelfth-level ultimate beings were already very dissatisfied.

If they had agreed to the Milky Way Star Lord's conditions earlier, handing over 90% of their twelfth-level weapons, treasures and rarities, and submitting to human civilization, nothing would have happened.

Some twelfth-level ultimate beings were unwilling and wanted to resist.

Resist?

What was the result? Therefore,

When faced with Lin Yuan's new conditions, not a single twelfth-level ultimate being dared to hesitate.

"Hand them over first," Lin Yuan said.

At this moment, the dozens of twelfth-level ultimate beings in the palace were like chicken ribs to Lin Yuan.

When the great destruction of the universe arrives, if Lin Yuan continues to carry the palace and the dozens of twelfth-level ultimate beings inside with him, he would face the backlash and suppression of most of the universe's origin.

It would be equivalent to Lin Yuan helping dozens of twelfth-level ultimate beings resist the universe's destruction.

"Hand them over first?"

Inside the palace, dozens of twelfth-level ultimate beings hesitated slightly.

If they handed over their treasures and weapons first, what if the Milky Way's supreme being went back on his word and didn't let them out?

"I trust the Milky Way's supreme being."

The twelfth-level ultimate being from the Starry Sky Alliance thought for a moment, then decisively took out all his treasures and weapons.

Now they had no leverage to negotiate with the Milky Way's supreme being. He even felt that the Milky Way's supreme being's willingness to give them a chance and let them out was a kind of grace.

"Go out," said Lin Yuan, putting away the treasures and weapons, and directly teleporting the ultimate being from the Starry Sky Alliance out.

"Thank you, Milky Way Supreme Being," the ultimate being from the Starry Sky Alliance said with wild joy in his voice. After repeatedly bowing in thanks, his form immediately disappeared.

Seeing this, the twelfth-level ultimate beings in the palace immediately offered up all their treasures and rarities, and were successively teleported out.

"Remember, the current universe is the territory of human civilization," Lin Yuan rarely reminded.

"Rest assured, Milky Way Supreme Being, we will absolutely not violate the rules of human civilization," the many twelfth-level ultimate beings hurriedly said.

Violate the rules?

If the Milky Way Star Lord could suppress them once, he could suppress them twice or thrice.

Looking at the twelfth-level ultimate beings successively disappearing, Lin Yuan's expression remained calm.

Having lost their weapons and treasures, these twelfth-level ultimate beings probably only had a 20-30% chance of surviving the universe's destruction.

And even if they could survive, if they dared to show any malice,

Lin Yuan still had dozens of avatars waiting outside the universe.

Compared to inside the universe, those twelfth-level ultimate beings would face a Lin Yuan whose various aspects were greatly suppressed by the universe's origin.

Outside the universe, even just one avatar could use methods far beyond those inside the universe. That would be true despair.

"These weapons, treasures and rarities..."

Lin Yuan glanced at the things offered by those dozens of twelfth-level ultimate beings and nodded slightly.

The total value of all the weapons, treasures and rarities added up to nearly 100,000 chaos stones, equivalent to the wealth of a normal Sovereign realm powerhouse.

At the Thousand Treasures Convention on the Ancient Mingchaos Continent, Lin Yuan spent 30,000 chaos stones to buy that golden box. Now he had recouped some of that.

"Chaos Void..."

Lin Yuan's gaze was deep.

After the universe's great destruction, what the human civilization needed to do was to quickly find a new home in the Chaos Void, then establish a foothold and develop.

As for continuing to live in the inner universe? The inner universe could not nurture truly powerful beings. In terms of origin, the inner universe was far inferior to the real universe and starry sky, let alone the Chaos Void. So far, any twelfth-level life in the universe, whether special life or racial life, was nurtured from within the universe. As for the inner universe? The upper limit was directly set.

"With human civilization's current foundation, it shouldn't be a problem to buy a continent fragment next to the Ancient Mingchaos Continent," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

The new home of human civilization would naturally prioritize safety, and the safest large area nearby would naturally be the Ancient Mingchaos Continent.

The Ancient Mingchaos Continent was equivalent in size to dozens of universes, and naturally had some continent fragments revolving around it.

As part of the Ancient Mingchaos Continent, even if the Ancient Mingchaos Continent moved and set sail, it would carry the surrounding revolving fragments with it.

In the virtual world.

Lin Shoucheng and Lu Qiong, husband and wife, were full of worry. Lin Yi also stood obediently beside them.

"Xiao Yuan, what's going on now? Why are many citizens of Canglan Star starting to migrate?" Lu Qiong asked in a low voice.

"Yes, migrate? Migrate to where?" Lin Shoucheng couldn't help asking.

According to the plan formulated by Supreme Being Xuanyuan and the other eight supreme beings, only about 1% of all citizens of human civilization could be migrated into the inner universe.

But because of Lin Yuan, all citizens of Canglan Star could obtain migration rights.

"Migrate to another universe," Lin Yuan said simply.

To prevent extreme reactions from citizens, Supreme Being Xuanyuan and the other eight supreme beings did not announce the reason for the migration.

After all, the universe's great destruction was really inconceivable.

"Why do we need to migrate to another universe?"

Lu Qiong still didn't understand.

"Don't tell others about this," Lin Yuan looked at his parents. Under his protection, his parents would definitely survive safely.

Not just his parents...

But she also knew this would delay the progress of comprehension. To comprehend 3,333 runes in one universe era was an extremely arduous task.

"Just endure it for a while."
