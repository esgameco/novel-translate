"This kind of Immortal King aura..."

The purple-robed elder gazed into the distance towards Xiaoyao City, or more precisely, he was looking at the vast expanse under the dome of the sky above Xiaoyao City.

In the eyes of true immortals, there was nothing there, and even ordinary tribulation-transcending Immortal Kings had no perception of it.

But in the sight of the purple-robed elder, there was a magnificent world evolving, vast and unparalleled, with a wisp of aura emanating from it no weaker than the aura of an ordinary Immortal King.

"The aura is actually a world..."

The purple-robed elder's mind trembled. The auras of the vast majority of Immortal Kings were the external forms of certain objects, such as a heavenly pillar or an ocean.

But the magnificent world aura before his eyes was more like an encompassment of countless other auras rather than a single aura.

"Martial Ancestor is definitely not a new Immortal King..."

The purple-robed elder composed himself. How could a new Immortal King have such a magnificent aura? Although an Immortal King's aura was only linked to their strength to a certain extent and was not absolute.

But could the boundless aura the purple-robed elder was observing now be displayed by an ordinary Immortal King? Within the infinite world, numerous wonders were contained within, something even those tribulation-transcending peak Immortal Kings who had cultivated for millions of years could not achieve.

"Supreme Immortal King, only a Supreme Immortal King who has mastered the complete power of creation can possibly condense an aura of this level."

The purple-robed elder took a deep breath. Tribulation-transcending Immortal Kings were divided into two types.

The first were normal Immortal Kings, including new, ordinary, veteran, and peak Immortal Kings.

These four levels of Immortal Kings had differences in strength, mainly determined by their mastery of their own power of creation, but the gap was not too big.

Even peak Immortal Kings would find it difficult to kill a new Immortal King.

Upon reaching the tribulation-transcending stage, having passed the physical tribulation and mental tribulation, Immortal Kings who mastered the power of creation had exceptionally powerful life-preserving abilities. Even if they couldn't win, escaping was not much of a problem.

The second type of tribulation-transcending Immortal Kings were Supreme Immortal Kings.

Supreme Immortal Kings had mastered the complete power of creation, and some even mastered more than one type of creative power.

The strength of Supreme Immortal Kings had almost no upper limit. Some ancient Supreme Immortal Kings could kill a peak Immortal King without too much trouble.

Therefore, even among tribulation-transcending Immortal Kings, Supreme Immortal Kings were existences that absolutely could not be provoked and required reverence.

Of course, the number of Supreme Immortal Kings was pitifully small, with only a handful of renowned ones like the Paramount Immortal King.

"I never thought this Martial Ancestor would actually be a Supreme Immortal King?"

The purple-robed elder felt his scalp go a bit numb, but he couldn't help feeling curious.

Everything in the world, any existence, would leave traces, let alone a Supreme Immortal King?

Becoming a Supreme Immortal King could not be achieved just by secluded cultivation. It required interaction with the outside world and adventuring through numerous secret realms. The growth trajectory of any Supreme Immortal King could not be completely kept secret.

But Martial Ancestor...

The purple-robed elder considered himself knowledgeable, yet he had never heard of any Supreme Immortal King related to the word "Martial".

"Could it be that those undying ones from the prehistoric era have emerged?" The purple-robed elder speculated inwardly.

Long ages ago, the Spirit Realm had experienced a historical fault. Before that fault was the prehistoric era.

The purple-robed elder's speculation about Lin Yuan could only lean towards the prehistoric era. Otherwise, there was no way to explain the sudden appearance of a Supreme Immortal King.

"I'm out of here. Regardless of whether he's a Supreme Immortal King from the prehistoric era, it's best to avoid getting close..." Before figuring out this Supreme Immortal King's temperament, the purple-robed elder immediately planned to stay far away.

If Lin Yuan was just a new Immortal King, or a veteran or peak Immortal King, he wouldn't mind interacting with him.

But a Supreme Immortal King suspected to be from the prehistoric era? The purple-robed elder didn't dare to casually make contact.

The stronger one was, the more they understood the terror of Supreme Immortal Kings, especially those from that glorious prehistoric era.

"I'm leaving." The purple-robed elder turned around, about to depart.

But the next moment.

He froze in place.

Because a figure had appeared not far away at some unknown time, with his back to him and hands clasped behind.

"It's Martial Ancestor..."

The purple-robed elder's scalp exploded. To be able to appear beside him without him detecting anything, even peak Immortal Kings couldn't do it, only that Martial Ancestor could.

"Greetings, Martial Ancestor."

In a flash, the purple-robed elder immediately greeted him with slight respect.

Since Martial Ancestor had appeared here, it indicated he had discovered him. Running away at this point was obviously too late.

"Mm." Lin Yuan glanced at the purple-robed elder, quite satisfied in his heart.

Besides promoting the martial dao cultivation system, he had revealed part of his strength to attract other tribulation-transcending Immortal Kings.

In order to obtain the true high-level information of the Spirit Realm.

The Spirit Realm was too vast, with the three thousand Dao Prefectures being just a small part. If Lin Yuan were to personally search for those tribulation-transcending Immortal Kings, it would likely consume a great deal of time.

Rather than that, it was better to lie in wait.

The moment the purple-robed elder approached Xiaoyao City, Lin Yuan had detected him.

While the purple-robed elder was observing Martial Ancestor's aura, Lin Yuan was also observing the purple-robed elder.

Although he didn't know what means the purple-robed elder used, just now Lin Yuan had a faint feeling of being spied upon.

This spying was only the most superficial kind and far from perceiving Lin Yuan's foundation, but it was still rare.

"I wonder if Martial Ancestor has any tasks that require my assistance. I guarantee to follow your orders..." The purple-robed elder took the initiative to say.

"I'll ask you a few questions." Lin Yuan said casually.

"Please speak, Martial Ancestor."

"As long as I know, I will certainly hold nothing back."

The purple-robed elder felt slightly relieved in his heart.

Let alone a few questions, even if it were a few hundred questions, he would not hesitate at all.

"Where do most tribulation-transcending Immortal Kings reside?" Lin Yuan asked directly.

So far, there were rarely any traces of tribulation-transcending Immortal Kings in the three thousand Dao Prefectures, not only in Dongyun Prefecture but even in those few core prefectures. At most, there existed lineages left behind by Immortal King level experts.

Other prefectures occasionally had rumors of Immortal Kings, but they were just rumors.

For example, Jiujiang Domain was rumored to be the "Dragon Transformation Land" of a certain tribulation-transcending Dragon King.

As for the Immortal King level experts themselves, they were still nowhere to be seen.

This made Lin Yuan a bit puzzled. Although tribulation-transcending Immortal Kings were rare, they had long lifespans. It was difficult for an era to give birth to one, but accumulated over eras, there should definitely be some.

"Where are all the Immortal Kings?"

Lin Yuan's question made the purple-robed elder's heart skip a beat.

This meant that the Martial Ancestor allowed him to make contact.

Being able to contact a supreme immortal king at any time, and a supreme immortal king from the prehistoric era at that, how great of an opportunity would this be?

He was worried that if he encountered those bloodthirsty supreme immortal kings, wouldn't he be jumping into a pit on his own?
