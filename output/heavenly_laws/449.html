Chapter 449 You Are All Surrounded by Me (Seeking Subscriptions)

Xuanyuan Yu's war plan was very comprehensive, but what Lin Yuan was mainly looking at was not the plan itself, but the powerful experts of the other peak race forces, who were the opponents of the Human Civilization Alliance.

"Does it take twenty of our human civilization's peers at the same level of evolution to hold back each Calamity Race expert?" Lin Yuan saw the battle plan targeting the Calamity Race.

Even though Lin Yuan had already heard about the terrifying nature of this race, he was still somewhat surprised.

From Xuanyuan Yu's war plan, Lin Yuan didn't see any intention to defeat the opponent at all. The entire purpose was just to hold back the Calamity Race experts.

"The Calamity Race is very terrifying," Xuanyuan Yu sighed. "If it weren't for the unimaginably high difficulty they face in breaking through to the Ultimate, this entire universe would have belonged to the Calamity Race."

Breakthrough to the 11th level Ultimate requires escaping the constraints of the River of Time. The normal difficulty for a 11th level cultivator to escape the River of Time constraints is 10, but for the Calamity Race, it's 100 or 1000 times harder.

When the Calamity Race breaks through to the Ultimate, the backlash from the entire universe will descend upon them, not giving them any chance to adapt.

This is also the reason why the Calamity Race currently only has two 12th level Ultimate experts. In addition to their small population, the success rate of becoming Ultimate is also pitifully low.

"The war plan has no problems," Lin Yuan looked at Xuanyuan Yu and said, "but you don't need to arrange anything for me."

Xuanyuan Yu's war plan had arrangements for Lin Yuan as well, such as dealing with the strongest 11th level experts of certain peak race forces.

"Doesn't the Galactic Overlord need to be arranged?" Xuanyuan Yu was a bit puzzled, but still did as Lin Yuan said, "Okay."

Although this would put a lot more pressure on the other 11th level experts of the Human Civilization, Xuanyuan Yu didn't have the slightest doubt about following Lin Yuan's orders.

"Alright. When the full-scale war starts, just contact me directly. Handle the other matters on your own." Lin Yuan waved his hand, and Xuanyuan Yu along with the other 11th level perfect evolution experts were all teleported out of the control room.

"The Chaos Rule." In addition to comprehending the space-time fusion rule and many other rules, Lin Yuan was also considering how to attain the Chaos Rule.

After breaking through to the 11th level, Lin Yuan's depth of rule comprehension and his innate awakening ability had both improved greatly. But for the Chaos Rule, he still felt a bit lost.

All things in the world originate from Chaos, but what exactly is Chaos, and how to attain the Chaos Rule, Lin Yuan felt a bit confused.

Emperor Xuanhuang and the Patriarch of the Demonic Jade Tower had both comprehended the Chaos Rule. Their explanation was that as long as one accumulates enough foundation, they would naturally comprehend it.

But what exactly counts as "enough" foundation, that's not clear. Emperor Xuanhuang and the Patriarch of the Demonic Jade Tower were both Chaos Realm experts. If the required foundation is at the Chaos Realm level, then Lin Yuan is destined to be unable to comprehend the Chaos Rule at the 11th level.

Even experts like Emperor Xuanhuang and the Patriarch of the Demonic Jade Tower didn't comprehend the Chaos Rule when they were at the 11th level.

"Take it slow. I'm not in a hurry. At worst, I'll just spend more time at the 11th level. Without being sure, I absolutely won't attempt the breakthrough to the Ultimate."

Lin Yuan gathered his thoughts. With his current mental willpower, he could easily live until the end of the universe. Just a few hundred years, even counting all the time he spent traversing worlds, would only be a few thousand years, which is nothing compared to the complete lifespan of a universe.

So Lin Yuan had plenty of time to slowly comprehend it.

"The 11th level of the Martial Dao?" Lin Yuan divided his attention and tried to establish a complete 11th level Martial Dao.

This was also a review and summary of his own path. Establishing the 11th level Martial Dao chapter was not just about upgrading the Martial Dao evolution path to the 11th level, but more importantly, to review the path he had walked.

Only by doing so could he grasp the present and better look towards the future.

Outside the super-large battlefield, many 12th level Ultimate experts were observing at will.

"Xia Qin, tell me the truth, is that Galactic Overlord the same as the Human Bloodbath?" A peak expert of the Star Alliance asked Xia Qin telepathically.

This guess was quite unbelievable. For example, when that blank paper descended into this universe, the Galactic Overlord was only at the 10th level. In the 2D world, all extraordinary powers were suppressed, theoretically making the 10th and 11th levels at the same starting point.

But that was just the starting point. As time passed, the growth speed of the higher life level side would far exceed the lower life level side.

The Galactic Overlord was only at the 10th level. Even if he possessed treasures bestowed by the Human Civilization to break free from the 2D world suppression, his realm and life level were still there. No matter how powerful the treasures were, he must have sufficient strength to activate them.

The Galactic Overlord was only at the 10th level back then, what could he use to crush the likes of the Four Divine Kings and other 11th level perfect experts? Moreover, the Four Divine Kings also had suitable supreme weapons for themselves.

However, according to the Galactic Overlord's current displayed strength, he had reached the level of the strongest 11th level experts of the various peak races upon just entering the 11th level.

Combined with the fact that the Human Bloodbath and the Galactic Overlord had never appeared in the same time and space, and that the blank paper escaped the universe, and the Galactic Overlord soon broke through to the 11th level, etc.

Logically speaking, after the root loss in the 2D world, the Galactic Overlord should have been affected even if the Human Civilization compensated him. How could he have crossed into the 11th level so easily?

The only explanation is that the Galactic Overlord was not affected at all by the 2D world. This is consistent with the Human Bloodbath leaving the 2D world alive.

All the clues point to one possibility - that the Human Bloodbath who massacred in the 2D world is the Galactic Overlord.

If it were just one similar clue, it could be a coincidence. But when all the clues are so similar, even without solid evidence, the peak experts of the Star Alliance have already affirmed it in their hearts.

"Hmm? And then?" Xia Qin, the peak expert, said indifferently.

When Lin Yuan entered the battlefield and revealed some of his strength, Xia Qin had already anticipated that the identity of the Human Bloodbath might be exposed.

But so what if it was exposed? Those powerful experts killed by the Human Bloodbath in the 2D world, what could they do about it?

"Very good." The peak expert of the Star Alliance fell silent for a while, a strong sense of shock welling up in his heart.

Even when he first saw the Human Bloodbath crushing the Four Divine Kings in the 2D world, he didn't feel that much shock, especially after speculating that the Human Bloodbath might possess treasures bestowed by the Human Civilization.

But when the Human Bloodbath and the Galactic Overlord overlapped, this Star Alliance peak expert couldn't sit still.

Prodigy. The prodigy level of the Galactic Overlord exceeded the expectations of all the peak experts.

"The Human Bloodbath is the Galactic Overlord?" "How is that possible? Wasn't the Galactic Overlord only at the 10th level back then?" "A 10th level could sweep through 11th level perfects?"

The thinking speed of the many peak experts was extremely fast, and they similarly deduced that the Galactic Overlord was the Human Bloodbath.
