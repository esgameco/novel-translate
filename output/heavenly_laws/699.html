Lin Yuan's mind flashed with the details of the first and second source worlds, especially their fundamental operations, and he began to slowly integrate them into his own cosmic universe.

Only by doing so could he lay the foundation before the root of his cosmic universe was established, so that he could continue to integrate more source world essences in the future.
