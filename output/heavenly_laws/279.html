The Magic Jade Tower is a mysterious force from outside the universe.

According to the will of the supreme being Xia Qin, the master of the Magic Jade Tower suddenly disappeared in the distant past.

Many members of the Magic Jade Tower also vanished without a trace.

The corpse of the supreme being that drifted in from outside the universe this time should be a member of the Magic Jade Tower, otherwise it would not contain evolutionary paths related to the Magic Jade Tower.

The information Lin Yuan obtained from the Magic Jade imprint shows that the connected Magic Jade Space was specially created by the master of the Magic Jade Tower back then to train its members.

Since it is for training, there must be risks involved. It's easy to have accidents if one's cultivation is not at the peak of the 8th stage.

Whoosh.

A wisp of Lin Yuan's mental will descended into a mysterious space along the Magic Jade imprint.

"Is this the Magic Jade Space?"

Lin Yuan carefully observed his surroundings.

"Am I here in mental will form? Is my physical body still on the main star of the galaxy?"

Lin Yuan immediately determined his own state.

Having experienced traveling through the Myriad Worlds Gate multiple times, Lin Yuan was no stranger to this feeling.

The difference is, when he descended to the traversed worlds through the Myriad Worlds Gate, he couldn't sense the existence of his physical body in the main world at all.

The eighth traversal allowed him to perceive the details around his physical body, that was by relying on the Myriad Worlds Gate as a "medium".

But now.

Lin Yuan could sense where his physical body was, although extremely far away, it felt real and not illusory.

"This Magic Jade Space is definitely not in the main world, but it should be in the same latitude and dimension as the main world."

Lin Yuan speculated in his mind.

If it was in a different latitude and dimension from the main world, he probably wouldn't be able to sense where his physical body was.

"If I want to go back now, I just need to disperse this wisp of mental will." Lin Yuan thought to himself.

The Magic Jade imprint only brought a wisp of mental will to descend here, the main body of the mental will was still in the physical body. The current Lin Yuan was similar to a consciousness projection.

"A wisp of consciousness projection... The power I can utilize now is only rule insights and cosmic energy?"

Lin Yuan's thoughts expanded as he looked around.

All he saw was white misty fog, Lin Yuan's mind was vaguely attracted to the front left.

"Is it telling me to go there?"

Lin Yuan thought for a moment, this attraction came from the Magic Jade imprint.

Without the Magic Jade imprint, even if he forcibly descended into this space, he wouldn't know which direction to go, as the white misty fog concealed and covered everything.

Lin Yuan had no intention of wandering around blindly, but followed the instructions of the Magic Jade imprint and walked towards the very front.

Although he didn't know what level of expert the master of the Magic Jade Tower was, they definitely wouldn't be weaker than a supreme being. Being in a space created by such an existence, it was safest to obediently follow their rules. As for taking a different path? That would be the most foolish thing to do.

"How many tens of thousands of years have passed, finally another Magic Jade Tower member has entered this training ground?"

The stone statue looked at Lin Yuan from afar, but felt nostalgic in its heart, "Once upon a time, our Magic Jade Tower was so prosperous, with members coming to train at every moment. Now, to wait for a trainee, it takes a very, very long time."

The humanoid stone statue sighed, "Master, where exactly did you go? With you gone, is there still a need for the Magic Jade Tower to exist?"

On the main star of the galaxy.

Lin Yuan's brows furrowed slightly, he could feel everything that the wisp of mental will sensed after entering the Magic Jade Space through the Magic Jade imprint.

Even as that wisp of mental will explored the Magic Jade Space, he could still cultivate and comprehend the Dao on this side.

As an 8th stage evolutioner, multitasking was perfectly normal.

Unless the events encountered by that wisp of incarnated mental will were too important, most of his mental power was still on his true body.

"I wonder if there are any human evolvers who have become ordinary members of the Magic Jade Tower so far." Lin Yuan thought silently.

Let alone comprehending the 3,333 evolutionary paths, in recent years, some human evolvers might have already perfected and made up for a certain evolutionary path.

This method of becoming an ordinary member of the Magic Jade Tower was much simpler than comprehending the 3,333 evolutionary paths.

"What about the alien races?"

Lin Yuan frowned.

Even the apex races didn't have the means of the human virtual world and couldn't isolate most of the powerful influence of that severed body.

So the apex races also faced great risks when comprehending that severed supreme being's body.

But similarly, the greater the risk, the greater the variables. Perhaps an alien who was extremely compatible with that severed body had their comprehension speed greatly increased?

"Forget it."

"I should focus on comprehending the 33,333 evolutionary paths as soon as possible."

Comprehending 3,333 evolutionary paths automatically makes one an ordinary member of the Magic Jade Tower.

Lin Yuan speculated that if he could comprehend 33,333 evolutionary paths, his ordinary member rank in the Magic Jade Tower would likely rise by a level.

Although Lin Yuan didn't know what he would encounter in the Magic Jade Space, having a higher member rank in the Magic Jade Tower definitely wouldn't be a bad thing.

Of course, Lin Yuan's main focus and effort would definitely be on merging rules.

Magic Jade Space.

The nine-story tower was majestic and enormous, completely black, and the aura it emitted vaguely penetrated layers of space.

"This tower?" Lin Yuan felt the oppression his mental will was under.

The shape of this nine-story tower was identical to the "Nine-Layered Black Tower" of the Magic Jade imprint.

However, the former was countless times larger than the latter, and the oppression it emitted was so strong that Lin Yuan had the illusion of facing the supreme being Xia Qin again.

"Consistent with the oppression of a supreme being, could it be that this tower is comparable to a supreme being?" Lin Yuan felt it was unbelievable.

In the main world, supreme beings were the epitome of absolute invincibility, dominating the vast starry seas.

Just as Lin Yuan was repeatedly examining the nine-story tower.

Although Lin Yuan had never heard the language it spoke, he inexplicably understood its meaning.
