A four-armed man stood there silently. He was only about 1.6 or 1.7 meters tall, yet he exuded an intangible and immeasurably vast aura, especially from his four arms, which seemed vaguely out of place with the surrounding world.

"The Four Celestial Kings."

"The Four Celestial Kings."

"The Four Celestial Kings."

Dozens of figures on the lakeshore murmured in a frenzied tone.

Two hundred years ago, the strength of the Four Celestial Kings had already far surpassed theirs.

Now, after another two hundred years, the gap would only be greater and wider.

"Is the Heavenly Gate about to open?"

The four-armed Celestial King slowly opened his profound eyes and calmly spoke.

"According to the time flow of this two-dimensional world, the Heavenly Gate will open in two more months," the leading figure on the shore immediately replied.

The Heavenly Gate opens once every ten thousand years, the greatest event in this two-dimensional world. Countless powerful beings will come to witness it.

Before the arrival of the powerful beings from the myriad races, only those at the Demon Emperor level are qualified to compete for entry into the Heavenly Gate, while Demon Ancestor level beings can guarantee reaching the gate.

But now, with the intervention of the powerful beings from the myriad races, the threshold has been raised infinitely higher.

"Understood."

"We shall depart for the Celestial Mountains in one day."

The Four Celestial Kings said slowly.

The Celestial Mountains are known as the most towering peaks in this two-dimensional world. Each time the Heavenly Gate opens, it appears atop the Celestial Mountains, hence their name over time.

"Yes."

The dozens of figures on the shore immediately nodded.

"Four Celestial Kings, we..." The leading figure suddenly hesitated.

"Rest assured, you have handled so many trivial matters for me in this two-dimensional world. Once I can leave, I will have the most powerful beings assist and compensate you as much as possible for your fundamental losses."

The Four Celestial Kings looked at the many figures on the shore. These figures were all powerful, special lifeforms that would be unmatched forces in the vast cosmos.

"Thank you, Four Celestial Kings."

"We trouble you then, Four Celestial Kings."

"It is our honor to handle trivial matters for the Four Celestial Kings."

The dozens of figures felt slightly relieved. Fundamental losses could not be recovered, but they could be compensated for. As long as the most powerful beings took action and paid the price, their losses could be minimized.

Of course, the reason the most powerful beings were willing to act was because the Four Celestial Kings would take all the wondrous treasures from this two-dimensional world, in which these figures had contributed.

"With Your Celestial Majesty's strength, who in this two-dimensional world can match you?" The leading figure spoke.

The rest agreed. Although they were all eleventh-stage special lifeforms, they knew they had no hope of leaving and could not compete with the Four Celestial Kings before them.

As for giving up this Heavenly Gate opening and waiting for the next one, that was a matter for the nine strongest beings. It had nothing to do with them, these relatively mediocre special lifeforms.

The current nine strongest beings in the two-dimensional world had similar standings and would not submit to any side, even if they were all special lifeforms.

"Alright."

"You may go prepare."

The Four Celestial Kings spoke, and the dozens of figures on the shore swiftly turned and left.

Soon, only the Four Celestial Kings remained on the vast lake surface.

"Over the past two hundred years, I have tried my best to excavate the potential of the supreme weapon integrated into my body. In terms of combat strength, I have probably reached the limit of this two-dimensional world," the Four Celestial Kings thought silently.

Unlike the wondrous treasures of this two-dimensional world, his supreme weapon was brought from the outside realm.

And this created an essential difference from the wondrous treasures wielded by the powerful beings of the myriad races.

The difference was that the Four Celestial Kings' supreme weapon had been refined.

The other powerful beings from the myriad races could obtain wondrous treasures, and even if their might was no less than the Four Celestial Kings' supreme weapon, they could not refine them, only unleashing their lowest power.

In this two-dimensional world, the various means of the powerful beings from the myriad races could not be fully utilized, as they could not refine the wondrous treasures.

But the Four Celestial Kings' supreme weapon had already been refined in the outside realm.

Although it fell into this two-dimensional world, with the supreme weapon's myriad abilities suppressed, as a refined supreme weapon, the Four Celestial Kings could still achieve unity of mind and body with it, and slowly unlock more of its power.

Moreover, this supreme weapon was perfectly suited for the Four Celestial Kings, allowing him to unleash his full combat strength.

"This time, when the Heavenly Gate opens, I will definitely be able to leave."

The Four Celestial Kings was full of confidence. As a perfected eleventh-stage legendary being, he was already among the strongest of the powerful beings descending from the myriad races. Coupled with possessing a supreme weapon, he had an immense advantage.

With such an edge, the Four Celestial Kings did not think it possible that he could fail to leave.

Inside a volcano, before the roiling magma, sat a scrawny old man in lotus position. His form resembled a raging blaze, with the surrounding temperature constantly soaring, melting the rock walls.

"That's about enough."

"I've reached an extreme limit."

The scrawny old man let out a light sigh, and the nearby temperature rapidly dropped. "Time to head for the Celestial Mountains."

The scrawny old man narrowed his eyes slightly. "This time the Heavenly Gate opens, the only ones who can rival me are those few old fellows. The Four Celestial Kings counts as one - he brought a true supreme weapon, so he won't be easy to deal with."

The scrawny old man stroked his chin, deep in thought.

Normally, eleventh-stage beings were not qualified to possess supreme weapons, even perfected eleventh-stage legendary beings.

Although supreme weapons were considered foundational treasures for peak races, an eleventh-stage being possessing one could very likely draw the attention of the most powerful beings to seize it by force.

The most powerful beings disdained bullying the weak, but if there was sufficient benefit, they would not hesitate to do so.

Unless this supreme weapon was bestowed by another of the most powerful beings.

The Four Celestial Kings was extremely valued in the Stellar Grand Union and was being groomed as the next ultimate being, so he was granted a supreme weapon.

"But in this two-dimensional world, even if he has refined a supreme weapon, how much of its power can he truly excavate? When the time comes, a few of us old fellows can join forces to deal with the Four Celestial Kings first."

The scrawny old man thought to himself.

In terms of pure combat strength, the Four Celestial Kings was definitely stronger than him, perhaps even the strongest among all the powerful beings descending this time.

But being the strongest did not mean invincible.

As long as he was not invincible, the Four Celestial Kings could be defeated and suppressed.

At least in the scrawny old man's view, the Four Celestial Kings posed the greatest threat and would definitely become his target of attack.

Without dealing with the Four Celestial Kings first, the scrawny old man, as a perfected eleventh-stage legendary being, would not dare go all out.

One could imagine that atop the Celestial Mountains, the Four Celestial Kings would become the target of a concentrated assault.

"Forget it."

"I'll take this chance to discuss with those old friends how to deal with the Four Celestial Kings."

A smile appeared on the scrawny old man's face. Apart from those few peers at the same level of perfected eleventh-stage legendary beings, he simply did not take any of the powerful beings from the myriad races seriously.

Outside the Twelve Ancient Kingdoms.

A secluded power.

Within this two-dimensional world, the Twelve Ancient Kingdoms were only the largest forces in scale.

Outside the Twelve Ancient Kingdoms, there were many secluded powers whose foundations were no less than the Twelve, all established by a Demon Ancestor.

These powers had always remained in seclusion, uninterested in outside information.

Rumble...

At this moment, a terrifying aura rose from the depths of one such secluded power, a vast force of vital energy shaking everything.

"Demon Ancestor!"

"I have finally become a Demon Ancestor!"

An excited middle-aged man walked out. His name was Yan Fu, the strongest expert of this secluded power. After arduous cultivation, he had finally crossed into the Demon Ancestor realm.

For the native lifeforms of this two-dimensional world, cultivating to Demon Ancestor was more difficult than ascending to heaven. In past Heavenly Gate openings, most competitors were Demon Emperors.

As for Demon Ancestors? If even one Demon Ancestor was born, it basically guaranteed an entry into the Heavenly Gate.

"Perfectly timed, crossing into the Demon Ancestor realm right before the Heavenly Gate opens."

"What my ancestors could not achieve, I shall accomplish."

Yan Fu's mind was stirred.

For the lifeforms of this two-dimensional world, entering the Heavenly Gate was the ultimate goal and dream.

But even as a Demon Ancestor, it was not guaranteed one could enter the Heavenly Gate.

The Heavenly Gate opened once every ten thousand years, but a Demon Ancestor's lifespan was at most two to three thousand years. So to enter the gate, even as powerful as a Demon Ancestor, one still needed luck.

"I don't know how my current strength compares to the Four Celestial Kings who possess supreme weapons."

Lin Yuan thought to himself. In terms of realm, he was certain he surpassed the Four Celestial Kings, but in terms of combat strength, he was unsure since they possessed a refined supreme weapon.

The closer one got to the limit, the greater the difficulty in making further progress.

As for the capital of the Bloodrain Ancient Nation, apart from taking a look in the first few years, Lin Yuan had basically never gone back.

Lin Yuan was about to reach the Celestial Mountains.

Among those two hundred thousand, there were only a little over two hundred eleventh stage powerful beings, a reduction of one-third from the original three hundred or so.

Lin Yuan finally arrived at the Celestial Mountains.

"So that's the Celestial Mountains?"

Lin Yuan stopped in his tracks and gazed at the majestic towering mountains in the distance, his expression becoming slightly solemn.
