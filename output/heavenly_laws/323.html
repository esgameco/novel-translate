Chapter 323: Twilight of the Gods (Please Subscribe)

Outside the Longshan Palace.

The numerous members of the Longshan family gazed at the heavens with trembling hearts.

There, massive stars fell into the mortal world like rain.

It wasn't just one or two stars falling, but entire regions and even all the stars in the entire sky were falling.

This was too unbelievable.

Everyone's minds were in a daze as they stared blankly at the shocking scene before them.

In this world, the stars in the sky were not merely stars; they were the abodes of the gods, symbolizing the immortality of the deities.

Even those without any faith would hold the utmost reverence for the stars in the sky, as they represented the limit of what life could reach.

Throughout the long years, in the eyes of almost everyone, the stars in the sky were eternally unchanging. From their birth to their death, and then to the birth and death of their descendants, the stars they saw were always the same.

Therefore, in the eyes of many, the stars in the sky were almost as eternally enduring as the heavens and earth.

But now, the cognitive views of the numerous Longshan family members began to shatter, because the stars across the sky had actually begun to fall simultaneously.

"What's going on?"

"The stars, they are the divine realms of the gods. How can they fall?"

"The fall of the divine realms implies the fall of the gods, but how is this possible?"

"The gods are immortal. How can they fall?"

The Longshan family members couldn't believe it. Some even thought they were dreaming. After slapping themselves a few times and still feeling the pain, the trepidation in their hearts nearly overflowed.

"Not good. The Patriarch just entered the Ocean of Light."

"The divine realms of the gods are rapidly falling, clearly something major has happened in the Ocean of Light and the Abyss of Darkness. Could something have happened to the Patriarch?"

Some of the Longshan family elders thought more. Lin Yuan was the pillar of the Longshan family, about to lead the family to the pinnacle of the Myriad Nations Plateau, but now this had happened?

"I hope nothing has happened to the Patriarch."

A few elderly members of the Longshan family silently thought.

In the sky above, countless stars that had hung in the sky for long years began to fall simultaneously. Naturally, it wasn't only the Longshan family members who could see this.

At this moment, in the vast mortal world, the numerous living beings gathered in places like the Myriad Nations Plateau also discovered the changes in the sky.

"The stars are falling?"

"Are the gods falling?"

"How is this possible? How can so many gods fall at once?"

In many secluded corners, the old half-god monsters who had survived for tens of thousands or even hundreds of thousands of years were also dumbfounded.

They had lived longer and knew the history of the past, clearly aware of the existence of the "God Wars".

Gods were not invulnerable to falling, it was just that they lived for such a long time that almost all living beings believed they were eternal.

During the "God Wars", the true gods of the Ocean of Light and the Abyss of Darkness would slaughter each other, resulting in the fall of gods.

But even in the most brutal god wars, the death of dozens or hundreds of gods was the limit. But now? The number of falling stars was no less than thousands.

The heartbeats of many half-god old monsters began to slow down, because the scene before their eyes was too shocking to their souls. Some half-god old monsters even suspected that the gods of the Ocean of Light and the Abyss of Darkness had all been kicked over in one go.

Otherwise, how could so many stars fall at the same time?

"What exactly is going on?"

"What happened in the Ocean of Light and the Abyss of Darkness?"

"Could it be that the twenty-four chief gods have joined hands to slaughter the gods?"

Many half-god old monsters couldn't help but speculate in this direction, and fear appeared on their faces.

To be able to cause the fall of so many true gods at once, only the chief gods could do it, and not just one chief god, but the twenty-four chief gods joining hands in a slaughter.

If even one chief god disagreed and resisted, the frequency of falling stars would not be so fast.

From the regions and scope of the falling stars, the half-god old monsters vaguely deduced that in the Ocean of Light or the Abyss of Darkness, a one-sided slaughter targeting the true gods was taking place.

The numerous true gods being slaughtered had no ability to resist.

This made all the half-god old monsters somewhat disheartened. They didn't know how much they had paid to ignite the divine fire, step into the seventh level, then hibernate and secretly gather faith, wanting to condense divine realms and raise them high.

But now they saw the true gods, whom they regarded as their ultimate goal, being slaughtered like pigs and dogs in the Ocean of Light and Abyss of Darkness.

So the gods they had always regarded as lofty could also be so weak? Unable to decide their own fate? Only able to obediently wait for death?

For a time, the state of mind of many half-god old monsters became unstable, and the divine fires they had ignited began to flicker.

What was the point of gathering faith and raising divine realms? Even if they became true gods and entered the Ocean of Light, what could they do?

It might not even be as safe or free as the mortal world there.

"Why do the chief gods want to slaughter the true gods?"

"For faith? It shouldn't be. The twenty-four chief gods already occupy the majority of faith in the mortal world. Even if they purge all the true gods, their own faith won't undergo a qualitative change."

"Are they worried about their positions being threatened? That's also impossible. Let's not even mention the power gap between chief gods and true gods. The number of chief gods is fixed. No matter how outstanding a true god is, without a chief god's position, they still can't become a chief god, let alone shake the chief gods' status."

The numerous half-god old monsters rapidly discussed.

"The mighty twenty-four high-level chief gods actually entered to slaughter ordinary true gods." A relatively young half-god spoke in an indignant tone, wanting to condemn the twenty-four chief gods.

Just at this moment.

And now, following the fall of stars one after another into the mortal world, the twenty-four massive stars in this region also began to fall, one by one.

One.

Two.

Five.

Ten.

In just a few breaths, more than ten chief gods' divine realms had fallen into the mortal world.

This made the half-gods in the mortal world who were condemning and criticizing the twenty-four chief gods fall completely silent, and then they fell into confusion.

It turned out that it wasn't only the true gods who were falling.

The chief gods were also falling?

This scene made an unprecedented terror rise in the hearts of all the half-gods.

The chief gods were falling.

And it was a large-scale fall.

Then who exactly was taking action to slaughter them?

If it was the twenty-four chief gods joining hands to slaughter the true gods, then how could the chief gods also be falling?

It should be known that the battles between chief gods were lengthy. Even if the strongest Light Chief God and Dark Chief God wanted to kill the weakest chief god, it was almost impossible.

Let alone crushing them like this.

Now that Lin Yuan himself had gone to the Light Ocean to negotiate with the gods, once the negotiations failed and he started to take action, naturally he would not waste those falling stars.

Even if he couldn't use them himself, he could use the "Primordial Force" to return all the Divine Kingdoms to their origins, transforming them into the purest energy and then letting the Primordial Essence Gold absorb it.

Bringing it back to the main world through "indirect" means.

Splash.
