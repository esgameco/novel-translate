The Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign and the Extreme Cold Celestial Sovereign were two ancient celestial sovereigns from the mysterious Yin ancient country.

Compared to other celestial sovereigns, their spiritual consciousness and awareness were somewhat lacking. This was why they so coveted the rare and precious black jade produced in the Black Jade Nest.

As the guardianship of the Black Jade Nest was about to change hands, they planned to secretly infiltrate it. It wasn't really much of a risk, as the Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign had made ample preparations for this operation.

The new guardian, the Grand Sovereign of the Milky Way, was much stronger than the other peak experts of the imperial clan, but had not yet reached the realm of celestial sovereignty. He was nowhere near as difficult to deal with as the previous guardian, the Blazing Sun Celestial Sovereign.

The Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign had also observed many recordings of the Milky Way Grand Sovereign's battles, and felt he had a good understanding of this new rising imperial clan from the Scorching Yang Ancient Country.

Even if they were discovered immediately upon infiltrating, they were confident they could easily retreat without exposing any information related to their identities. As long as the Scorching Yang Ancient Country couldn't gather solid evidence afterwards, the matter would simply be left unresolved.

"Ten breaths," the Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign instructed the Extreme Cold Celestial Sovereign as they successfully infiltrated the Black Jade Nest. "No matter how much black jade we obtain, we must retreat immediately after ten breaths."

Ten breaths was the time it would take for reinforcements from the Scorching Yang capital to arrive at the Black Jade Nest via teleportation.

The two celestial sovereigns were not overly concerned about the Milky Way Grand Sovereign, but they were wary of the reinforcements from the Scorching Yang capital.

"Understood," the Extreme Cold Celestial Sovereign nodded.

Since they dared to infiltrate this premier treasure trove, they had to plan for the worst-case scenario. With their strength, it was unlikely they would be discovered immediately upon entering. But as long as there was that possibility, they had to make their plans accordingly.

In just a fraction of a breath, the two celestial sovereigns had fully infiltrated the Black Jade Nest and were rapidly approaching the area where the black jade was being cultivated.

In the depths of the Black Jade Nest, Lin Yuan was refining the black jade, with strands of black jade energy slowly and steadily tempering his spiritual will.

Suddenly, Lin Yuan sensed two auras infiltrating the area. These auras had not been detected by the defensive formations of the Black Jade Nest, but were sensed purely through Lin Yuan's own perception and the semi-activated state of his "World" divine ability.

Ever since the Black Jade Nest was nearing maturity, Lin Yuan had been extremely cautious, keeping his "World" divine ability in a semi-activated state at all times. The Blazing Sun Celestial Sovereign, a powerful celestial sovereign, had not hesitated to completely offend several other celestial sovereigns in order to gain the opportunity to guard the Black Jade Nest. How could the celestial sovereigns of other ancient countries be any less covetous?

"It should be two celestial sovereigns from the Mysterious Yin Ancient Country," Lin Yuan surmised as he gazed down at the rapidly approaching auras.

The Extreme Cold Celestial Sovereign and the Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign had prepared thoroughly, confident they could evade detection by the Black Jade Nest's defensive formations. And indeed, the formations did not detect their presence.

However, while the defensive formations could not sense them, Lin Yuan's "World" divine ability had them in clear view.

"They've arrived, the area where the black jade is cultivated is just ahead," the Extreme Cold Celestial Sovereign and the Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign felt their spirits stirring, especially the Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign, as the Black Jade Nest's defensive formations had not reacted at all so far. It seemed the Milky Way Grand Sovereign hadn't even noticed their presence.

"Two breaths have already passed, we'll have three breaths to retreat, leaving us only five breaths to collect the black jade," the Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign glanced at the Extreme Cold Celestial Sovereign.

The Extreme Cold Celestial Sovereign immediately nodded. Cultivating to the realm of celestial sovereignty, they would not lose their composure in the face of treasures, and would act according to their pre-made plan.

The next moment, the two celestial sovereigns entered the area where the black jade was being cultivated. A vast expanse of profound, inky-black jade stones came into view, and the Extreme Cold Celestial Sovereign and Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign suppressed their excitement.

"Collect them all, even the unmatured black jade," the Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign said quickly.

The black jade cultivated in the Black Jade Nest was only extracted once it was fully matured. But the unmatured black jade still had effects, just not as potent in tempering spiritual will as the fully matured black jade.

As plunderers, the Extreme Cold Celestial Sovereign and Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign naturally wanted to collect everything in sight, regardless of maturity.

However, when they tried to envelop the black jade with their power, they discovered a layered spatial barrier surrounding each piece.

"What's going on?" the Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign frowned, able to sense that this spatial barrier did not originate from the Black Jade Nest's defensive formations.

"Northern Yin, what do we do now? I can't collect them," the Extreme Cold Celestial Sovereign quickly asked, finding the spatial barrier covering the black jade completely impenetrable.

As the Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign pondered their next move, his expression suddenly changed as he looked up to see a young man emerge from the void, calmly gazing down at the two celestial sovereigns from on high, like two ants before an ancient, powerful being.

"The Milky Way Grand Sovereign?" the Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign immediately recognized this young man as the current guardian of the Black Jade Nest.

"We've been discovered?" the Extreme Cold Celestial Sovereign said solemnly, with the black jade now fully protected and the Milky Way Grand Sovereign present, it was clear they had been detected.

"Has this Milky Way Grand Sovereign been prepared for us all along?" the Extreme Cold Celestial Sovereign thought, glancing at the Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign, waiting to see what they should do next.

"Let's go," the Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign said, though somewhat reluctantly. He knew that in this situation, the best choice was to retreat immediately. Staying would be pointless, as they had no way to collect the black jade for now.

The Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign's mind raced, and since they had already been discovered, he no longer needed to conceal his aura so thoroughly. As long as he didn't reveal his signature attack, it would be fine.

A spatial disturbance began to build as the Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign tried to forcefully break through the spatial channel connecting this place to the outside world, in order to retreat.

However, the power of the formations surged, suppressing the Northern Yin Celestial Sovereign's attempt to pierce the spatial fabric.
