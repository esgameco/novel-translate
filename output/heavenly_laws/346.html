"The guidance from the supreme expert has indeed allowed me to take a shorter path," Lin Yuan's face showed a smile.

Without the explanation from the supreme expert Xia Qin, even if he could comprehend the Three Obscure Time Indices, it would have taken him months or even a year or two.

"However, this effect of guidance is completely built upon the foundation I have accumulated over a long period," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

Before seeking advice from the supreme expert, Lin Yuan had already been stuck in this predicament for some time and had made various attempts from different angles.

It was precisely because of this that when Xia Qin gave a brief explanation, Lin Yuan immediately integrated the insights.

If he had not made all those previous attempts and instead sought guidance from the supreme expert as soon as he encountered the bottleneck, it was highly unlikely he would have achieved the current effect.

"The supreme expert Xia Qin told me to go see him again after comprehending all the time models," Lin Yuan recalled Xia Qin's advice before leaving.

At the main star of the galaxy...

Chun Bo, Jia Li, and the red-skinned, blue-eyed administrators among others paid a visit to Lin Yuan.

Normally, matters concerning the main star of the galaxy, or even the entire galaxy domain, would be discussed and decided by them, and then the results would be uniformly reported to Lin Yuan.

Only when they encountered issues they could not decide on, or matters beyond their authority, would they visit Lin Yuan.

"Star Master," Chun Bo, Jia Li and the others were very respectful, looking at the young man sitting cross-legged on the ground. Just looking at him made their minds tremble, unable to even harbor thoughts of defiance.

It was as if ordinary citizens or first-tier evolved beings were facing a celestial dragon larger than a planet - the innate pressure of a superior life form suppressed any notion of hostility.

"The Star Master has already restrained his aura as much as possible, yet we still feel immense pressure. Just how powerful is the Star Master's strength?" The few main star administrators could not help but wonder.

"In our human civilization now, the Star Master's reputation even surpasses that of many tenth-tier evolved beings. The incident in the Profound Yellow Secret Realm has made countless alien races both hate and fear the Star Master."

As eighth-tier evolved beings, those present naturally had access to the upper circles of the human civilization and had heard about the recent events in the Profound Yellow Secret Realm.

"The Star Master's current strength has probably far surpassed the old master," the red-skinned and blue-eyed alien races secretly marveled. "The old master used to worry that when the Star Master initially reached the eighth tier and took over the galaxy domain, he might be bullied. But look how quickly things have changed - who would dare bully the Star Master now?"

The red-skinned and blue-eyed felt deeply moved. They could be considered Lin Yuan's most trusted aides, having served from the time of the Crimson Dragon Star Master to the current Galaxy Star Master.

As alien races, the red-skinned and blue-eyed were destined to have limited development in human civilization. The only one they could rely on was the Galaxy Star Master.

"What is the matter?" Lin Yuan opened his eyes, most of his mind still focused on contemplation and cultivation, looking towards the few before him.

He had already left instructions that unless an issue arose that could not be decided on, or would affect the entire galaxy domain, they should avoid disturbing him as much as possible.

"Star Master," the red-skinned one respectfully said, "Not long ago, the Shadow Tribe contacted us, wanting to establish a trade route with the galaxy domain."

"The Shadow Tribe? A trade route?" Lin Yuan's mind stirred slightly, and he understood all the details of this matter.

The territory of the galaxy domain originally belonged to the Shadow Tribe.

It was just that on the alien battlefield, they lost to the human civilization alliance, so they were forced to withdraw.

So in reality, the galaxy domain borders the Shadow Tribe's territory.

"I see," Lin Yuan's expression became contemplative.

No wonder Chun Bo, Jia Li, the red-skinned, blue-eyed and the other administrators had specifically come to visit him.

They indeed could not decide on this matter themselves.

Establishing trade routes with alien races has always been a sensitive issue, especially when the alien race in question is an adversary of human civilization.

"Some of the Shadow Tribe's resources are indeed quite useful for our human civilization," Lin Yuan thought silently to himself.

For example, the Shadow Tribe's specialty "Darkness Stone" is a necessary material for constructing high-level warships and can enhance the stealth capabilities of warships.

"Alright," Lin Yuan agreed after some thought.

Engaging in moderate trade with alien races could accelerate the development of the galaxy domain.

If Lin Yuan was still at the eighth tier, he definitely would not have dared to get so involved with the Shadow Tribe. But now, as a ninth-tier being evolving towards the tenth tier, the threat from the Shadow Tribe was not worth considering.

As long as he controlled the extent of trade and implemented restrictions on certain trade resources on the human civilization side, there would be no problem.

Of course, the most important thing was that by establishing a trade route with the Shadow Tribe, the controlling power rested entirely with Lin Yuan's side, and he could terminate it at any time.

"Very well," the administrators felt that while trading with the Shadow Tribe was absolutely more beneficial than detrimental, no one but the Star Master dared make this decision.

"Have there been any major events in the galaxy domain recently?" Lin Yuan asked.

"Nothing major," the blue-eyed one organized his words and said, "Following the spread of the Star Master's reputation, more and more business consortiums are willing to establish branches on the main star. The development of the main star is much faster than expected."

For fringe galaxy domains in human civilization to develop rapidly, the first key consideration is stability and safety.

The fringe galaxy domains border alien races, so conflicts and clashes often occur. At such times, the strength of the Star Master becomes very important.

A powerful Star Master can provide protection for the galaxy domain, giving all citizens a great sense of security.

Under such circumstances, major corporations and conglomerates would naturally be happy to station themselves here.

After all, besides being on the frontier, fringe galaxy domains also mean lands yet to be developed - if one can seize the opportunity, it means an endless stream of wealth.

After further exchanging with Chun Bo, Jia Li, the red-skinned, blue-eyed and the other administrators to understand the current situation of the galaxy domain, Lin Yuan waved his hand to dismiss them.

"The galaxy domain has already started turning a profit now."

"Just the tax revenue from the past two years has reached eight thousand cosmic crystals."

Lin Yuan thought to himself that eight thousand cosmic crystals was basically equivalent to the entire fortune of an eighth-tier evolved being.

Of course, the galaxy domain's tax revenue was not unrelated to Lin Yuan's influence. An ordinary eighth-tier Star Master, no matter how well they managed their domain, could not achieve such income.

In the deepest part of the Profound Yellow Secret Realm...

Lin Yuan sat cross-legged, his inner aura fluctuating before finally converging and permeating every inch of his flesh and blood.

"The 'Profound Yellow Physical Body' is finally somewhat accomplished," Lin Yuan showed a smile on his face.

At the same time, an invisible field expanded ten zhang (about 30 meters) in all directions with his physical body as the center.

The Profound Yellow Protective Light.

Only after somewhat accomplishing the 'Profound Yellow Physical Body' could one manifest this ability to block all attacks.

Physical attacks, soul attacks, law attacks and so on - all were within the defensive range of the Profound Yellow Protective Light.

As long as the Profound Yellow Protective Light remained unbroken, Lin Yuan's body would be indestructible for eternity.

This represented the formidable defensive aspect of the three great secret techniques of the Profound Yellow lineage.

The Profound Yellow Crystal worth at least one billion cosmic crystals was only used to somewhat accomplish this secret technique, not even counting the weapons and treasures Lin Yuan had devoured.

"With my current defensive capabilities, even special ninth-tiers cannot break through the Profound Yellow Protective Light alone," Lin Yuan's expression improved slightly. Although he had paid a great price, the returns were equally gratifying.

"There is also the 'Profound Yellow Strike'," Lin Yuan raised his right hand, threads of aura converging.

Besides refining the 'Profound Yellow Physical Body', Lin Yuan had also cultivated the 'Profound Yellow Strike' to the second stage.

With a casual movement, he could maintain a 'sustained' state surpassing his own strength by three times.

Besides consuming more energy, this explosive secret technique had no flaws and could maintain a 'sustained' state with no cooldown period after activation.

Simply put, it meant he could activate it continuously without any downtime.

"I shall continue cultivating," Lin Yuan briefly examined himself before immersing in cultivation once more.

With his current strength, although he could be considered formidable at the ninth and tenth tiers, he was still far from touching the pinnacle of the cosmos.

Even with the protection of the human civilization's supreme experts, Lin Yuan still felt that power grasped in his own hands was the safest.

Protection from supreme experts? It was better to become a supreme expert himself.

Time flowed slowly...

The cosmic starry night saw the undercurrents of myriad races stirring.

Lin Yuan too was continuously improving his strength through cultivation.

In the blink of an eye, twenty years passed.
