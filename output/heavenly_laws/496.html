The peak Sovereign Realm expert 'Cang Weiyang' left behind many precious weapons and treasures, which greatly benefited Lin Yuan.

At least under the control of the owner, even if dozens of Twelve Ultimate experts in the universe attacked together, they would not be able to shake it.

The Chaotic Void is full of dangers, and the avatar Lin Yuan sent out had long been prepared to dissipate at any time.

Therefore, he wanted to quickly send the harvests back.

If he didn't send them back, and encountered any danger, and the avatar perished, wouldn't all these treasures be lost?

The treasures left behind by the peak Sovereign Realm expert 'Cang Weiyang' were enough to make ordinary Sovereign Realm experts envious. For Lin Yuan, who had just begun to explore the Chaotic Void, they were his first "pot of gold".

With a "whoosh", the avatar quickly disappeared under Lin Yuan's gaze, returning to his home universe at high speed.

"Next, continue to explore."

Lin Yuan was full of vigor, and for him at the moment, any harvest in the Chaotic Void could satisfy him, after all, it was his first exploration.

"Senior Cang Weiyang left me a map of the Chaotic Void, but now without any specific reference points, the map is meaningless."

Lin Yuan thought to himself, "First, find the nearest Chaotic Continent, with this Chaotic Continent as a reference point, I can also know what's around it."

The maps of the Chaotic Void are divided into two types, one is the public type, and the other is the private type.

The latter is the map personally explored by each Twelve Ultimate expert, which is not publicly disclosed, and it marks many places of opportunity.

The map left by Cang Weiyang was a private map.

The Chaotic Continent is the place where many lives gather in the Chaotic Void, and most of the Chaotic Continents are formed afterwards, created by Chaotic Realm experts as their own territories.

The order in the Chaotic Continent is strict, because only with order can it continuously attract powerful experts to gather, without order, what's the difference from the Chaotic Void?

"As long as I can determine which Chaotic Continent is the closest, Senior Cang Weiyang's map will be useful."

Lin Yuan randomly chose a direction and quickly traversed the void.

In fact, whether it was the Demon Jade Tower or the Xuanhuang Lineage, they both left Lin Yuan with maps of the Chaotic Void.

But these two existed too long ago, even if the maps they left were much more detailed than Cang Weiyang's by many times, they were still of little reference value.

As the universes flowed by, even if the Chaotic Continents were dominated by Chaotic Realm experts, they would eventually collapse, and the areas marked on the maps might not even exist anymore.

Moreover, the Chaotic Void is too vast, even a Chaotic Sage cannot explore it completely. Whether the maps left by the Xuanhuang Emperor and the Demon Jade Tower Master are still effective, it is difficult to say whether they have marked the nearby areas.

Lin Yuan had seen the images of the 33,333 Xuanhuang Secret Realms opened by the Xuanhuang Emperor, which were completely aimless openings, and the Xuanhuang Emperor himself might not even know the location of every Xuanhuang Secret Realm.

The Demon Jade Tower Master is probably similar.

Lin Yuan became a member of the Demon Jade Tower sequence, and could vaguely feel the general location of the Demon Jade Space in the Chaotic Void, but it was only vague, except for the direction, the distance and other details were not clear.

Compared to these two Chaotic Sages, Cang Weiyang's map is the most useful, because Senior Cang Weiyang was the weakest, even if he perished, his cave mansion could not have flown too far.

And based on the traces of the passage of time on the cave mansion, Lin Yuan judged that the loss of control would not be too long, probably around two to three universal periods.

Two to three universal periods is not too long for Twelve Ultimate experts in the Chaotic Void.

In the starry sky, Lin Yuan sat cross-legged, and the details of the 98 secret techniques left by Cang Weiyang emerged in his mind.

Now Lin Yuan has reached the ultimate in the Eleventh Realm, he is truly at the pinnacle. If it weren't for the fact that his inner world is still undergoing transformation, he might have already been preparing to break through to the Ultimate.

So Lin Yuan has been quite idle recently, even though the vast rules of the main universe and the Chaotic Void can still bring him help, his life realm limitation has restricted Lin Yuan's advancement.

With just the Eleventh Realm life realm, even with his extraordinary comprehension, he cannot elevate his Spatial-Temporal Comprehension to the Eighth Realm, nor can he elevate the Chaotic Rules to the Second Realm.

But the newly acquired 98 Sovereign Realm secret techniques have made Lin Yuan quite pleasantly surprised. The secret techniques are not a path of evolution, and their cultivation requirements are not harsh.

"There are six secret techniques among the 98 Sovereign Realm secret techniques that are suitable for me."

Lin Yuan thought to himself, most of the secret techniques require reaching the Dao Splitting Realm to cultivate, and there are only six that can be cultivated at the Eleventh Realm.

These six secret techniques, three are offensive techniques, one is a defensive technique, and the remaining two are auxiliary techniques.

They can all play a very important role for the current Lin Yuan.

"They are much simpler than the three Protective Dao Secret Techniques of the Xuanhuang Lineage."

Lin Yuan thought as he began to comprehend them.

【Your extraordinary comprehension has allowed you to comprehend the Withering and Flourishing Seal, reaching the pinnacle of the Eleventh Realm.】

【Your extraordinary comprehension has allowed you to comprehend the Falcon Divine Blade, reaching the pinnacle of the Eleventh Realm.】

【Your extraordinary comprehension has allowed you to comprehend the Nine Tempering Feather Kill, reaching the pinnacle of the Eleventh Realm.】

In less than a month, Lin Yuan had fully cultivated all the secret techniques he could currently practice to the extreme.

"Now, among my many techniques, the 'Rend the Heavens' physical technique is still the strongest." Lin Yuan examined himself.

The 'Rend the Heavens' technique is an extremely offensive technique, with no superfluous effects, it just tears open everything in front of it.

However, there are some limitations in activating this physical technique, so many other secret techniques are needed to complement it, and 'Rend the Heavens' is Lin Yuan's ultimate trump card, which he will definitely save for the last moment.

With Lin Yuan's current foundation, a single 'Rend the Heavens' strike can unleash a power of hundreds of thousands or even millions of battle power, and this is the pure 'Rend the Heavens' without any powerful weapons.

"The avatar of a Twelve Ultimate in front of me can be easily torn apart, even if it's a Twelve Ultimate, if it tries to withstand it head-on, it will probably be injured."

Lin Yuan had a rough understanding of his own abilities.

Of course, this is the 'Rend the Heavens' technique used by his true body, if it's his two major divine souls, the power might be a bit weaker.
