The few 8th-stage invincibles sent out by the Tianyu race were undoubtedly the most elite, and they even wielded 9th-stage secret treasures.

Yet, they were killed one by one by the Galaxy Master?

Under such circumstances, White Cloud Master dared to guarantee that they would at least not lose this battle.

"Seize the moment and launch a counterattack immediately."

White Cloud Master was about to command the numerous 8th-stage invincible evolutionaries.

But then he witnessed an unimaginable scene.

In the distance, the 8th-stage invincibles of the Tianyu race seemed to be frightened by something and began to retreat.

"What?"

White Cloud Master was a little dumbfounded.

He couldn't understand why the Tianyu race would fully retreat.

This meant that the other party had completely abandoned this battlefield and this war.

Why was this?

Although the Galaxy Master had shown a strong performance, it wasn't too outrageous. It took him more than half an hour, and the Tianyu race could have sent out more 8th-stage invincible experts to keep him in check.

The next moment.

Lin Yuan's figure appeared above the heads of the fleeing 8th-stage invincibles of the Tianyu race.

"Die!"

Lin Yuan's mind moved slightly, and the power that fused the laws of space and various cosmic forces swept out.

This power was not fully formed and only half-fused, but it could still be used barely.

Rumble.

The invisible fused power swept in all directions, and the 8th-stage invincibles of the Tianyu race were as fragile as thin paper, all turning into powder.

"No..."

The few Tianyu race 8th-stage invincibles who had escaped further away showed a horrified expression. Then, the invisible fused power swept over and they also turned into powder.

In just a few breaths, the dozens of Tianyu race 8th-stage invincibles who had scattered and were fleeing towards the rear all perished.

Although these 8th-stage invincibles still had their true bodies and were not completely dead, refining a clone with strength close to the true body was not an easy task.

It could be expected that for a long time to come, these 8th-stage invincibles of the Tianyu race would not be able to support other battlefields.

"Space bracelet."

Lin Yuan raised his right hand, and dozens of spatial storage treasures from those Tianyu race 8th-stage invincibles all fell into his hand.

Although these 8th-stage invincibles would not carry overly precious treasures on their bodies, to ensure the combat power of their clones, they would at least carry 8th-stage weapons and secret treasures.

The value of these weapons and secret treasures was not high individually, but added together, it was still a considerable fortune, not less than 200,000 cosmic crystals.

"This, this..."

In the Tianyu race's territory, the true bodies of the 8th-stage invincibles opened their eyes. Their clones had just perished, and at this moment, their expressions were all horrified.

"How could this be?"

"That Galaxy Master, his strength is too terrifying."

"9th-stage, this must be 9th-stage. The human civilization has violated the rules and mobilized 9th-stage evolutionaries."

"It shouldn't be 9th-stage. Otherwise, our race's 9th-stage experts would have taken action long ago. However, even if the Galaxy Master is not 9th-stage, his combat power definitely exceeds the 9th-stage threshold. Otherwise, it would be impossible for him to crush us like this."

A 9th-stage expert who had just entered the 9th-stage and had not yet comprehended the complete laws of space would have difficulty killing an 8th-stage invincible.

Yet the Galaxy Master could sweep through a group of 8th-stage invincibles and easily kill them. His combat power was not something ordinary 9th-stage experts could compare to.

"That Galaxy Master had been hiding his strength all along."

"I finally understand now why our race's 9th-stage expert issued the order to retreat."

"The human civilization is truly favored by the universe to give birth to such a monster like the Galaxy Master."

The 8th-stage invincibles of the Tianyu race spoke with complex tones, feeling fear, panic, awe, and a hint of admiration for Lin Yuan.

With an 8th-stage body, he could unleash 9th-stage combat power. Although they didn't know how the Galaxy Master achieved this, from the perspective of evolution, he was undoubtedly ahead of them.

Not just ahead, but far surpassing all the 8th-stage lives of this generation.

Unprecedented in ancient and modern times, among the myriad races of the cosmic starry sky, including those few peak races, all the 8th-stage lives of this generation appeared exceptionally insignificant before the Galaxy Master.

On the human side.

All the evolutionaries watched with their own eyes as those 8th-stage invincibles of the Tianyu race turned into powder under Lin Yuan's feet.

They subconsciously fell into silence.

The scene before their eyes was too shocking. Originally, Lin Yuan spent more than half an hour killing those Tianyu race geniuses wielding 9th-stage secret treasures one by one.

This had already made the numerous human evolutionaries excited, but now, the Galaxy Master killing those 8th-stage invincibles was as simple as killing chickens.

No, it was even easier than killing chickens. Chickens would at least struggle a few times, but those 8th-stage invincibles of the Tianyu race couldn't even struggle and directly disappeared.

The evolutionaries on the human side had thought that under the leadership of the Galaxy Master, they would win the war against the Tianyu race, but that would inevitably involve arduous battles and even require the fall of some human 8th-stage invincibles to achieve it.

As for now?

The Galaxy Master had solved everything?

From beginning to end, except for White Cloud Master taking action, they, as 8th-stage invincible evolutionaries, were like ornaments watching from behind.

"Invincible Galaxy Master!"

All the 8th-stage invincibles were completely fanatical. They had never seen an 8th-stage life so strong that it could completely rebel against the 9th-stage. This was too rare for the evolutionaries of the human civilization.
