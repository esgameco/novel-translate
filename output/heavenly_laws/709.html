The sect leaders and many high-level sect members looked at Lin Yuan with slight surprise.

He had a way to deal with the Demon God?

The Demon God possessed the power of a 13th-rank Eternal Lord.

Only an Eternal Lord of the same level could contend against the Demon God.

Even though the hidden world had withstood the Demon God's frenzied attacks so far,

At its core, it relied on arrangements left behind by Eternal Lords.

Essentially, it was still Eternal Lords battling against the Demon God.

And this resistance was not true opposition.

Merely relying on arrangements left by Eternal Lords could only delay temporarily.

Once the resources powering the many Eternal Treasures were exhausted,

The consequences would be unthinkable.

But Lin Yuan said there were other ways to deal with the Demon God?

"Please tell us, Lord Galaxy, what should we do?"

The sect leaders hesitated only for an instant before choosing to trust Lin Yuan.

Over the past ten thousand years, Lin Yuan's many actions had earned their trust.

"Give me all the foundational resources powering the Eternal Treasures," Lin Yuan said.

The infinite sand particle world he was creating was now 99% complete.

The remaining 1% progress would be very difficult to achieve in a short time.

After all, Lin Yuan had hunted down all the somewhat powerful 'demons' in this source world.

This was also a key reason for the Demon God's fury upon descending.

As for absorbing and devouring demonic energy?

The efficiency was too slow.

And now that the Demon God had descended,

Controlling vast demonic energy with a thought, Lin Yuan couldn't absorb or devour it even if he wanted to.

Now Lin Yuan was trapped in the hidden world, with the Demon God right outside.

Even in the worst case scenario of taking hundreds or thousands of years to complete the final 1%, it would likely be very difficult to achieve.

After all, with Lin Yuan's strength, if he dared to step out of the hidden world,

He would inevitably become the Demon God's first target of attack.

There simply wouldn't be time to continue hunting other 'demons'.

After much consideration,

Lin Yuan's hope of fully creating his infinite sand particle world

Could only be placed on the bottom-line resources of the major Eternal Sects.

Rather than consuming these resources in constant defense against the Demon God,

It would be better to give them all to Lin Yuan, to let him complete the creation of the infinite sand particle world.

Once the particle world was fully formed, Lin Yuan's strength would surge, giving him a real chance of directly contending against the Demon God.

This could also prevent the hidden world from being breached.

This was currently the most beneficial method Lin Yuan could think of, for both himself and the major Eternal Sects.

Based on the Demon God's current methods of attack outside,

Lin Yuan was very confident in his particle world's completion allowing him to directly contend against the Demon God.

"Lord Galaxy wants all the foundational resources?" The Eternal Infinite Sect Leader was shocked.

The other sect leaders also looked solemn.

These foundational resources were currently their hope for resisting the Demon God.

Although they would be exhausted in at most a million or ten million years, being able to delay for so long

Would at least buy enough time for the elite members of the major Eternal Sects to evacuate.

But giving everything to Lin Yuan? If Lin Yuan couldn't stop the Demon God,

The hidden world would be breached in the blink of an eye.

"That's right,"

Lin Yuan nodded.

Actually, to complete the final 1% progress, besides the current foundational resources of the Eternal Sects,

There were other methods.

For example, using the Gate of Ten Thousand Worlds to transport resources over.

Using the Gate of Ten Thousand Worlds to supply Lin Yuan from start to finish in creating the infinite sand particle world.

The cost would be too great, Lin Yuan couldn't afford it.

But for just the final 1% progress,

Lin Yuan could grit his teeth and accept it.

However, since it involved realm-breaking source power, he would definitely save it if he could.

"Lord Galaxy... are you confident?"

The Eternal Infinite Sect Leader asked cautiously.

"I don't think there will be any problems,"

Lin Yuan glanced at the Demon God outside.

If the infinite sand particle world was fully created, with that level of strength, stopping the Demon God likely wouldn't be an issue at all.

The other sect leaders looked at each other.

This hidden world was arranged by several Eternal Lords.

Even with the methods of several Eternal Lords, it couldn't withstand the Demon God's attacks for millions or tens of millions of years.

Yet Lin Yuan said there wouldn't be any problems?

"In that case, we'll entrust these six Eternal Sources to Lord Galaxy."

The Eternal Infinite Sect Leader's expression changed repeatedly before he spoke solemnly.

The sect leaders behind him wanted to speak but held back, ultimately saying nothing.

For the Eternal Sects, lasting millions of years versus lasting ten or eight years made no significant difference.

Rather than watching helplessly as the hidden world was breached by the Demon God, it was better to place all their hopes on Lin Yuan.

After all, the current situation had already exceeded the predictions of the many Eternal Lords before the great changes in heaven and earth.

Not a single Eternal Lord's predicted future timeline encompassed the current situation.

So the sect leaders were also very lost, not knowing how to respond.

Whoosh! Strange fluctuations surged through the hidden world.

Then six sun-like Eternal Sources emerged from the deepest part of the hidden world.

It was these six Eternal Sources that had powered the operation of multiple defensive Eternal Treasures, blocking the Demon God's repeated attacks from outside.

Lin Yuan looked at the six Eternal Sources emanating vast waves of energy.

Eternal Sources were condensed by Eternal Lords using their own power, able to fully activate the might of Eternal Treasures.

"I only need five, that's enough."

Lin Yuan didn't hold back. Outside, with each of the Demon God's attacks, the energy within the six Eternal Sources decreased a bit.

By Lin Yuan's estimate, four Eternal Sources would be enough for him to complete the final 1% progress.

The reason he asked for one more was to be absolutely certain.

"Mm."

The Eternal Infinite Sect Leader nodded.

Since they had decided to fully support Lin Yuan, even if he took all six Eternal Sources, the Eternal Infinite Sect Leader wouldn't frown.

Hum~ Lin Yuan flipped his right hand, and five of the six floating Eternal Sources silently disappeared.

Taken into Lin Yuan's body.

In an instant,

The five Eternal Sources provided vast ocean-like energy, allowing the remaining particles in Lin Yuan's body to rapidly create world after world.

Rumble rumble.

And with the disappearance of the five Eternal Sources,

Outside the hidden world,

Several Eternal Treasures that had been resisting the Demon God's attacks suddenly plummeted in power.

Even with one Eternal Source still supplying energy, the instantaneous energy supply rate was certainly no match for six Eternal Sources.

"Not good."

The Eternal Infinite Sect Leader's expression changed drastically.

He hadn't anticipated this.

Originally he thought six Eternal Sources could sustain the hidden world for millions or tens of millions of years.

One Eternal Source should be more than enough to last hundreds of thousands of years.

But without the simultaneous supply of six Eternal Sources, relying on just one Eternal Source made it very difficult to maintain so many Eternal Treasures at once.

"Hm?"

Lin Yuan also noticed the change in the Eternal Treasures outside.

"No need to worry."

Lin Yuan glanced at the sect leaders.

Since he had taken five of their Eternal Sources, Lin Yuan naturally wouldn't take them for nothing.

Even though the remaining 1% of particles were rapidly being created, it didn't affect Lin Yuan taking action.

"Leave this to me."

Lin Yuan took a step forward and vanished from his spot.

Before the sect leaders and many high-level sect members could react,

A towering figure appeared in the world outside.

Lin Yuan stood calmly next to the hidden world created by the Eternal Lords, which only reached his ankles.

An invisible domain descended and spread out, forming layer upon layer of barriers, actively intercepting the Demon God's attacks.

"What?"

"Lord Galaxy went outside?"
