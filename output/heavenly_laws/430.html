"Why do I have a feeling that I'm facing another ultimate existence?"

The Summer Qin Supreme was startled. This was the perception unique to supreme beings, only arising when facing existences of the same level.

The Summer Qin Supreme would experience this perception when interacting with the other eight supreme beings.

He would also sense this when dealing with the ultimates of other races.

But now?

Lin Yuan?

An extremely young cultivator who had just entered the tenth stage?

Actually gave the Summer Qin Supreme a similar feeling?

If the Summer Qin Supreme wasn't personally experiencing this, he would never believe it.

It was too absurd.

"What's the reason?"

"Spiritual will? The Galactic Sovereign's spiritual will exceeds the 18 million spiritual will index threshold, no, not just exceeds it, but exceeds it by a large margin."

The Summer Qin Supreme's thought process was extremely fast, immediately deducing the most likely reason.

First, this was a virtual world, whether it was Lin Yuan or the Summer Qin Supreme himself, they were both condensed from a strand of spiritual will.

Secondly, this was the Summer Qin Supreme's personal world. Without his permission, even other supreme beings could not exert any power here.

In other words, the feeling Lin Yuan gave the Summer Qin Supreme completely stemmed from the strength of his spiritual will.

Only by having a spiritual will that crossed into the supreme being realm could one give the Summer Qin Supreme such a feeling.

But Lin Yuan?

Having the spiritual will strength of a supreme being?

The Summer Qin Supreme didn't know how to describe it.

A tenth stage life form? Possessing a supreme being's spiritual will strength?

Could this still be called a tenth stage life form?

The Summer Qin Supreme fell silent.

"Supreme Being?" Lin Yuan also sensed the Summer Qin Supreme's repeated gazes of scrutiny.

Normally, when he came to visit the Summer Qin Supreme, the latter would have already started asking questions.

"It seems he has sensed the strength of my spiritual will," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

This was unavoidable. Although Lin Yuan's spiritual will had reached the twelfth stage, his realm was still the tenth stage, so he couldn't perfectly conceal it in front of a supreme being.

Even if he didn't come to visit the Summer Qin Supreme, when he was about to break through to the eleventh stage, the supreme being avatars would descend and discover it as well.

After being silent for a while, the Summer Qin Supreme directly started asking.

When the spiritual will index exceeded one million points, it became difficult to quantify precisely.

The Summer Qin Supreme only felt that Lin Yuan's spiritual will index had definitely reached the twelfth stage realm, and it wasn't just at the initial level.

"More or less," Lin Yuan nodded.

In fact, his spiritual will index had reached 33.4 million points, but it was indeed over 20 million.

"You..."

After getting Lin Yuan's confirmation, the Summer Qin Supreme didn't know what to say.

A prodigy.

Too much of a prodigy.

Just at the tenth stage, yet in terms of spiritual will he had crossed into the twelfth stage realm. Could a human cultivator really achieve this?

Compared to other talents and aptitudes, spiritual will was the most impossible to take shortcuts with. In the cosmos, even those uniquely special lifeforms with noble bloodlines, while their physical bodies and souls were extraordinarily superior, their spiritual will still had to start from the beginning. The stronger physical body and soul would nurture a more powerful spiritual will, but it absolutely did not have the overwhelming advantage that it did in the physical and soul aspects.

"I had an epiphany regarding spiritual will during a period of seclusion not long ago," Lin Yuan stated the reason he had prepared beforehand.

Besides slowly increasing over an extremely long period of time through experiences, spiritual will could also be rapidly enhanced through "epiphanies."

This "epiphany" referred to an epiphany about the operation of the cosmos and the myriad things in nature.

However, such occurrences were rare and had not happened many times since the beginning of the cosmos. Even when they did occur, it was mostly among lower-stage lifeforms.

For example, a fourth or fifth stage lifeform occasionally having an epiphany, causing their spiritual will index to directly soar from a few hundred to a few thousand.

Or some indigenous lifeforms on primitive planets, without undergoing any cultivation, having a spiritual will index approaching 10,000.

But for someone like Lin Yuan to have his spiritual will index directly exceed 20 million points after an "epiphany," it was estimated that had never happened before.

"Epiphany..." The Summer Qin Supreme fell silent again. He vaguely guessed that Lin Yuan's spiritual will increasing so drastically was probably not as simple as an epiphany.

However, this was Lin Yuan's own secret, and the Summer Qin Supreme would not pry.

Ever since Lin Yuan entered the tenth stage, triggering the infusion of primal chaos from the void and tearing apart cosmic rifts spanning light-years, the Summer Qin Supreme knew that Lin Yuan possessed his own extraordinary opportunity.

Regarding such matters, the Summer Qin Supreme wouldn't even ask.

"Spiritual will... The stronger, the better, naturally," the Summer Qin Supreme said slowly after pondering for a while.

The higher the level of a cultivator, the more they could feel the importance of spiritual will.

First to sixth stage cultivators probably didn't even know what spiritual will was.

But by the seventh stage, especially when breaking through from the seventh to the eighth stage, one of the prerequisites was having a spiritual will index of 10,000 points, only then could one safely pass the demonic tribulation.

From the eighth stage onward, the influence of spiritual will became greater and greater. When charging towards the ultimate at the eleventh stage, one needed a spiritual will index of millions of points.

In fact, the importance of spiritual will was far greater than that.

Whether it was the eleventh stage human cultivators or the eleventh stage experts of other races, their physical bodies and souls would no longer decay.

But why did they still need to slow down the flow of time to extend their longevity?

In theory, eleventh stage lifeforms' physical bodies and souls should have no longevity limit.

However, the longer they lived, even if their physical bodies and souls had no issues, their own spiritual will would still become distorted.

Once the spiritual will became distorted and insane, true death would not be far away.

At this point, the importance of having a powerful spiritual will became apparent.

The stronger one's spiritual will, the better it could resist this distortion and insanity.

With Lin Yuan's current spiritual will strength, he could easily live through an entire cosmic cycle.

If not for the eventual destruction of the cosmos, the drying up and collapse of the river of time that would cause all lifeforms in the long river to inevitably die, Lin Yuan could even live through multiple cosmic cycles.

Furthermore, a powerful spiritual will had far-reaching effects in various aspects.

"Supreme Being, I came this time to prepare for my breakthrough to the eleventh stage," Lin Yuan brought up the main purpose for visiting the Summer Qin Supreme.

"Breaking through to the eleventh stage?" The Summer Qin Supreme immediately reacted.

He had already guessed that Lin Yuan was here for this matter.

However, as soon as they met, he was stunned by Lin Yuan's terrifying spiritual will, which was why he still hadn't recovered his composure until now.

"When are you planning to break through?" The Summer Qin Supreme asked solemnly.

"Within these few months," Lin Yuan replied.

For such a breakthrough, he naturally had to choose the time when his state was at its peak.

At Lin Yuan's current adjustment speed, it would take a few more months.

"No problem," the Summer Qin Supreme nodded.

"At that time, I will call upon them to jointly protect the Dharma for you."

The Summer Qin Supreme's expression became slightly solemn.

The "them" he referred to were naturally the other nine supreme beings of the human civilization.

A couple of hundred years ago, when Lin Yuan broke through to the tenth stage, the commotion was so great that the Summer Qin Supreme had not anticipated it at all.

If it weren't for forcibly borrowing the power of a supreme being, aided by the Butterfly Supreme Being's assistance, with the combined power of the Summer Qin Supreme's avatar at that time, the other racial supreme beings might have detected the anomaly.

This time, the Summer Qin Supreme had learned his lesson and would directly call upon the other eight supreme beings of the human civilization.

With the power of nine supreme beings, even protecting the Dharma for Lin Yuan's breakthrough to the eleventh stage and charging towards the ultimate would be more than enough.

So the Summer Qin Supreme felt he was well-prepared this time.

"Rest assured, my true self will keep watch over the Galactic Sovereign Star. You can break through without worry," the Summer Qin Supreme added.

The true selves of the nine supreme beings of the human civilization were all stationed at key nodes of their domains, deterring the supreme beings of other races.

But for Lin Yuan's breakthrough this time, the Summer Qin Supreme had already prepared to mobilize his true self at any moment.

Ensuring Lin Yuan's successful entry into the eleventh stage was currently the most important matter for the human civilization, and any price paid for this would be acceptable.

Moreover, even if the Summer Qin Supreme mobilized his true self, the other races would most likely not...
