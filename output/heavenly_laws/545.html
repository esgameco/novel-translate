Lin Yuan quietly sensed the terrifying waves surging like a tide.

The grudge between two Chaos Saints caused this universe to enter the Great Destruction prematurely.

If not for Lin Yuan, perhaps only one in a million human civilization citizens would have survived.

In fact, it was far more than just Lin Yuan's universe. As the Nine Phoenix Saint and Shadow Demon Saint battled their way here, countless universes had entered premature Great Destruction.

For the living beings within the universe, including 12th level Ultimates, this was absolutely a catastrophe! A catastrophe that could not be stopped, and could only be endured! This was the power of Chaos Saints, the absolute peak existences of the Chaos Void. Even their casual actions could cause the fates of countless lives to change.

"Get ready."

Lin Yuan immediately contacted the Xuanyuan Ultimate and the other eight Ultimates.

Whoosh whoosh whoosh! Nine figures appeared in succession. Over 200 years ago, human civilization had basically completed its migration, and the nine Ultimates had been waiting for the catastrophe to descend at any moment.

"Galaxy, you're telling us to prepare?"

The Xuanyuan Ultimate looked at Lin Yuan and asked in a low voice.

Their comprehension of space-time fusion laws was still at the 6th level boundary. Lin Yuan could vaguely sense the terrifying energy waves approaching like a tide, but the nine human civilization Ultimates could not perceive it.

"It's almost here."

Lin Yuan nodded.

These words filled the nine Ultimates with complex emotions.

Although they had long heard from Lin Yuan that their home universe would be affected by the battle between two Chaos Saints, causing premature Great Destruction, even Lin Yuan couldn't be certain about this. After all, the trajectory of the two Chaos Saints' battle could only be roughly predicted, not 100% guaranteed. What if the two Saints suddenly changed direction as they fought?

So the nine Ultimates had always harbored a glimmer of hope, as this was their home universe and they had deep feelings for it.

But now Lin Yuan was actively telling them to prepare, meaning the aftershocks had already arrived, equivalent to the Great Destruction becoming inevitable.

As time passed.

Two extremely terrifying auras rapidly approached the home universe. Not only could Lin Yuan sense it, but 12th level powerhouses within the universe could also perceive it.

"Is the universe about to undergo Great Destruction?"

Various 12th level alien races became nervous. Except for freaks like Lin Yuan, for any 12th level being, universal Great Destruction was like walking through the gates of hell.

Especially since this Great Destruction was arriving half a universal era early, these 12th level alien races had lost half a universal era to improve their strength, lowering their chances of surviving the Great Destruction.

"The battle between two Saints..."

In the Catastrophe Race's territory, two Ultimates hid in a wooden house, carefully sensing the vast waves of energy.

"Even Chaos Saints can at most cause the universe to enter premature Great Destruction, but cannot completely erase the universe. We'll be safe hiding in our ancestral house."

The two Catastrophe Race Ultimates thought to themselves.

Whether it was the Chaos Saint-level power of the Catastrophe Race's mother clan, or Lord Xuanhuang, they all chose to arrange their backup plans within the universe.

This was because the universe itself was protected by Chaos laws. Even supreme Chaos Saints could not interfere or influence it too greatly.

Lin Yuan sat cross-legged under the starry sky. At this moment, he could clearly sense the destructive aftershocks enveloping and covering everything.

These destructive aftershocks were divided into two forces, constantly intertwining and colliding, shattering and tearing apart everything in the Chaos Void.

Even something as vast as the universe and starry sky would suffer irreversible damage to its foundation after these forces swept through, beginning to enter Great Destruction.

Lin Yuan observed carefully. Within the two constantly intertwining and colliding forces, one was fiery red, using infinite Chaos as fuel to burn Chaos flames.

These Chaos flames were completely different from the Chaos flames mastered by Duke Qingfen in the Origin World.

Duke Qingfen's Chaos flames were endless destruction and incineration, while these Chaos flames were filled with infinite vitality, containing the power of rebirth.

"Nine Phoenix Saint?"

Lin Yuan thought to himself. Although he hadn't heard the sounds of the two Chaos Saints fighting, he was certain this power came from the Nine Phoenix Saint.

The Nine Phoenix Saint had reached the Saint level through the Dao of Chaos flames.

The other force intertwined with the Nine Phoenix Saint's Chaos flames appeared as large patches of dark shadows. Everywhere this force passed, everything fell into darkness and silence, devoid of any vitality.

"Shadow Demon Saint?"

Lin Yuan quickly thought. The only force that could constantly intertwine with the Nine Phoenix Saint's power was that of the other Chaos Saint - the Shadow Demon Saint.

Boom boom boom.

The two drastically different, almost opposite forces intertwined and collided, with the aftershocks spreading and covering in all directions.

Large areas of void were affected and torn apart.

Lin Yuan's perception rose infinitely. His peak 7th level boundary comprehension of space-time allowed him to vaguely sense the nearest universe to their home universe.

That universe was enveloped and covered by these two forces before their home universe.

Crash crash crash.

The universe began to collapse from its foundations. The river of time rapidly dried up, and countless lives within the universe fell into extreme panic.

"Ahhhhhh!"

"Universal Great Destruction? Our universe was still far from Great Destruction, how could it happen so soon?"
