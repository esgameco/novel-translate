The Nether Realm Ultimate spoke slowly.

Truthfully, if their race produced a peak Paragon Realm expert, the first thing they would do is sweep across the major forces in the universe.

However, reaching the peak of the Paragon Realm was extremely difficult, and it was rare for even one to appear in dozens or hundreds of universes.

The two Paragon Realm Ultimates of the Calamity Tribe were also at the peak, but they were suppressed by the will of the universe and dared not act with full force, so they could not be considered normal peak Paragon Realm experts.

"The Paragon Realm? The human civilization?" The Insect Empress' expression darkened further.

At that time, the Insect Tribe would certainly be subjected to the full force of the human civilization. The thought of a peak Paragon Realm expert wielding a tribal treasure, in their prime, filled her with dread.

"The Galactic Overlord has already reached the pinnacle of the Eleven Tribulation Realm. His true self and main body are surely under the protection of the human civilization. How should we proceed?" the Void Tribe Ultimate said.

Curse-killing? Even if a Twelve Tribulation Realm Ultimate personally acted, they could only curse and kill Nine and Ten Tribulation Realm experts' clones and main bodies, but the Galactic Overlord had already reached the Eleven Tribulation Realm, so even if the Twelve Tribulation Realm Ultimates paid a heavy price, they could not kill him.

In the early days of the universe, a mysterious being fell into the universe, nearly causing a great extinction of all life. In the end, many Twelve Tribulation Realm Ultimates joined forces to barely drive it out.

That mysterious being was not a normal life form, possessing only instincts and no consciousness. Ordinary Twelve Tribulation Realm living beings could not enter the universe.

The mysterious being had a terrifying polluting ability, and its fresh blood was something even they did not dare touch. As long as they could target one of the Galactic Overlord's clones or avatars, even if it did not kill him, the Mysterious Blood could severely injure him and affect his potential to break through to the Twelve Tribulation Realm!

The Nether Realm Ultimate looked towards the Insect Empress.

The other Twelve Tribulation Realm Ultimates were slightly shaken upon hearing this. They did not know about this matter, but since the Nether Realm Ultimate was so certain, it must be true.

"Mysterious Blood?" The Insect Empress furrowed her brows. "I need to discuss this with Wanhua."

Among the Insect Empress's group, Wanhua was the strongest and most ancient, with the highest status.

"I hope Wanhua can consider the bigger picture," the Nether Realm Ultimate nodded and spoke.

After a moment.

The Insect Empress looked at the other Twelve Tribulation Realm Ultimates around her. "Wanhua is willing to provide that drop of blood."

"That's excellent." The Nether Realm Ultimate let out a sigh of relief.

That mysterious being had fallen into the universe before the human civilization was born, so the strongest experts of the human civilization did not know about this.

The Nether Realm Ultimate was able to glimpse Wanhua's actions due to his own abilities, which is why he brought it up today.

"But that drop of Mysterious Blood is the source of endless chaos and pollution. Wanhua considers it a precious treasure, so she is willing to provide it, but not without conditions."

The Insect Empress suddenly said.

"Whatever Wanhua requires, we will provide it," the Nether Realm Ultimate nodded.

"Rest assured, whatever Wanhua needs, our tribe will offer it," the other Twelve Tribulation Realm Ultimates one by one guaranteed.

After a moment.

The Twelve Tribulation Realm Ultimates reached an agreement on the conditions.

"From now on, we only need to locate one of the Galactic Overlord's clones or avatars, then unleash our full force to suppress him, and then use that drop of Mysterious Blood," the Insect Empress spoke.

"That is natural," the other Twelve Tribulation Realm Ultimates nodded.

Just then, the silent Calamity Tribe Ultimate said, "My Calamity Tribe has no interest in the Galactic Overlord of the human civilization. We will withdraw from this operation."

"The Calamity Tribe is withdrawing?"

The other Twelve Tribulation Realm Ultimates were somewhat surprised.

If the human civilization produced a Paragon Realm Ultimate, the Calamity Tribe would certainly be affected as well.

The human civilization would not specifically spare the Calamity Tribe, after all, a Paragon Realm peak expert in the universe meant invincibility, and the other Twelve Tribulation Realm Ultimates could only avoid them.

"No need to say more."

The Calamity Tribe Ultimate's figure directly disappeared.

"Those two old fellows, with their Paragon Realm peak cultivation, yet their courage is so small," the Nether Realm Ultimate frowned.
