However, this possibility is very low, as the Ten Thousand Dao Pavilion has always adhered to fair trade since the beginning of the universe, otherwise it would not have such a great influence.

Relying solely on the strength of the experts? At most, it can only make people intimidated and fearful.

But the Ten Thousand Dao Pavilion is adored by countless lives, and even the Mysterious Emperor and the Lord of the Demon Jade Pavilion have not found any 'dirt' on it, so it should be trustworthy.

"Don't worry."

"The Lord of the Ten Thousand Dao Pavilion?"

Lin Yuan recognized the identity of this old man, who was one of the strongest figures that could not be provoked on the Ancient Chaotic Continent.

"What's that?"

Lin Yuan looked at the old man's side, where there was a vague vortex, and a huge eye was also examining him.

The pupil of the huge eye was golden.

"The Eye of the Ten Thousand Laws!"

Lin Yuan was startled, the outside world knows little about this actual controller of the Ten Thousand Dao Pavilion, but the Mysterious Emperor and the Lord of the Demon Jade Pavilion are very clear, and have even left a reminder.

"It seems you know me?"

The Eye of the Ten Thousand Laws looked at Lin Yuan and said calmly.

"I've met the Eye of the Ten Thousand Laws."

Lin Yuan bowed slightly.

Even the Mysterious Emperor and the Lord of the Demon Jade Pavilion had to greet the Eye of the Ten Thousand Laws in this way, so Lin Yuan naturally would not be arrogant.

"Well, the evolutionary path that can develop branch paths must have a remarkable origin, so it's normal for you to know me."

The Eye of the Ten Thousand Laws continued to look at Lin Yuan, talking to itself.

Its reputation, to Transcendent Realm and Sovereign Realm experts, is slightly unfamiliar, but to Chaotic Realm Lords and Great Saints, the stronger the power and the higher the realm, the more awe-inspiring it is.

Lin Yuan's thoughts surged, quickly accepting it. According to the speculation of the Mysterious Emperor, the Eye of the Ten Thousand Laws is related to the Thirteenth Realm of Time Life, and has the ability to see through the Ten Thousand Laws and Ten Thousand Ways, so it is not surprising that it now knows the true essence of the martial arts of life and death.

"Yes."

Lin Yuan nodded in acknowledgment.

In the eyes of such an ancient existence as the Eye of the Ten Thousand Laws, there was no need to hide anything.

"Over the course of countless universe cycles and Chaotic Eras, there have indeed been many lives, just like you, who want to use the power of my Ten Thousand Dao Pavilion to spread their own evolutionary paths, and many of them have now cultivated to the Chaotic Realm Perfection."

Chaotic Realm Perfection is Great Saints, and as for Peerless Great Saints, they are also Chaotic Realm Perfection, only their combat power is much stronger than Great Saints.

So far, the Chaotic Realm Perfection that Lin Yuan has encountered are only two, one is the Lord of the Demon Jade Pavilion, and the other is the Mysterious Emperor.

These two are not only Chaotic Realm Perfection, but are above Great Saints, reaching the level of Peerless Great Saints.

Even Chaotic Lords can only flee in panic before Chaotic Realm Perfection.

In addition, the Demon Sage Great Saint and the Nine Phoenix Great Saint are also Chaotic Realm Perfection.

Lin Yuan's expression became grave.

This was the first time he had heard of the information about breaking through from the Twelfth Realm to the Thirteenth Realm.

Time loop?

Break free?

To enter the Thirteenth Realm, one must break free from the time loop? "What if they can't break free?"

"If they can't break free, then they can't break free, they will be trapped in the time loop forever." The Eye of the Ten Thousand Laws said.

No wonder the Xuanhuang lineage and the Moyu Tower had not left any information about crossing from the 12th to the 13th realm.

The Xuanhuang evolution path has not yet produced a single 12th realm cultivator so far, but Emperor Xuanhuang has disappeared without a trace, which is very similar to being trapped in a time loop.

According to the description of the Mirror of All Laws, after being trapped in a time loop, one neither lives nor dies, not considered dead, nor alive.

"Does spreading one's own evolutionary path help to break free from the time loop?" Lin Yuan asked.

"Since you've come here, why are you still asking?" The Eyes of All Laws re-examined Lin Yuan and explained, "The hope of breaking free from the time loop by relying solely on oneself is too slim. Relying on external 'path markers' or 'anchors' can increase the possibility of breaking free."

"So many Chaos Realm beings have laid the groundwork in advance, hoping to leverage the influence of my Wanfa Pavilion to spread their own evolutionary paths and increase their chances of breaking through to the 13th realm."

Even if they have a powerful background and their elders are Chaos Realm perfected, they still wouldn't do that. Otherwise, if they spread it halfway and then find that this evolutionary path is not suitable for them and "scatter the Dao" to switch to other evolutionary paths, wouldn't all the previous spreading be in vain?

Since Lin Yuan dares to come and spread his own evolutionary path, there can only be one possibility.

That is, he is absolutely certain that the evolutionary path he is walking is suitable for him, and this is usually the case when the evolutionary path is one that he has opened up himself.

One can only be sure whether it is suitable or not after reaching the Chaos Realm, as the uncertainty is too great at the Pili Dao Realm or Mastery Realm.

And if Lin Yuan has created his own evolutionary path, to have opened up a branching path capable of being explored at just the Pili Dao Realm?

The Eyes of All Laws has a very high opinion of Lin Yuan's potential, so it actively engaged in conversation and even "enlightened" Lin Yuan.

"It was just luck," Lin Yuan said, hearing the tone in the Eyes of All Laws' voice, indicating that it had seen through something.

"Opening up an evolutionary path is not just luck, especially the evolutionary path you have opened up, whose branches all have Chaos Realm potential," the Eyes of All Laws said.

Branches with Chaos Realm potential? Then the potential of Lin Yuan's main path can be imagined.

Lin Yuan listened quietly.

In the face of an existence like the Eyes of All Laws, there's no need to be too humble. If it can recognize and praise you, it means you are worthy of the praise.

"Alright, let's get to the main point," the Eyes of All Laws looked at Lin Yuan. "You want to use my Wanfa Pavilion to spread your own evolutionary path, no problem, but there's a condition."

During the human civilization era in the universe, relying on the power of human civilization to spread one's own evolutionary path was because Lin Yuan himself was a human citizen.

But outside of human civilization, those ancient gods in the depths of the starry sky, if they want to spread their own evolutionary path within human civilization, they will undoubtedly be suppressed and constrained.

Lin Yuan has no connection with the Wanfa Pavilion, and if he wants to use its power to spread the branches of his own evolutionary path to prepare for the future breakthrough to the 13th realm, he must certainly pay a price.

"It's very simple," the Eyes of All Laws said. "You don't need to consider this now. Wait until you cross into the Chaos Realm perfection, and every ten universal cycles, do one thing for my Wanfa Pavilion."

"Every ten universal cycles, do one thing?" Lin Yuan also looked at the Eyes of All Laws.

The premise of this condition is that Lin Yuan reaches Chaos Realm perfection.

If it were other Pili Dao Realm beings, or even Mastery Realm or Chaos Venerable, they would definitely agree without even thinking.

After all, even for a Chaos Venerable, there is no guarantee that they can cultivate to Chaos Realm perfection.

The condition proposed by the Eyes of All Laws is actually not very restrictive.

But for Lin Yuan?

Relying on his extraordinary perceptiveness and the Gate of All Worlds, Lin Yuan dares not guarantee that he can break through to the 13th realm, but he has a certain degree of confidence in reaching Chaos Realm perfection, it's just a matter of time.

In short, Lin Yuan can basically see the path from the Pili Dao Realm to Chaos Realm perfection.

Therefore, the condition proposed by the Eyes of All Laws is not actually very beneficial to Lin Yuan.

"Every ten universal cycles, do one thing for the Wanfa Pavilion? What kind of thing?" Lin Yuan asked. "What if they want me to go to my death or actively enter dangerous places, and I have to obey?"

"Don't worry, I won't let you do anything that you have no chance of succeeding in, and there will be corresponding rewards," the Eyes of All Laws said. "And even if you fail the task, I won't force you to continue doing it."

If Lin Yuan really grows to Chaos Realm perfection, the Eyes of All Laws also doesn't want to be too coercive.

Hearing this, Lin Yuan began to weigh the pros and cons.

First of all, there will be rewards for the tasks, which makes Lin Yuan somewhat satisfied, as he is not afraid of putting in the effort, but just doesn't want to work in vain.

And once a task fails, if it is determined that he cannot complete it, he will not be forced, which also puts Lin Yuan at ease.

The worst-case scenario of a task failure is just the destruction of one divine soul, which is a cost Lin Yuan can completely accept.

And most importantly, the requirement from the Eyes of All Laws is to do one thing every ten universal cycles.

From his birth to now, even if all the time he spent traversing worlds is added together, it's not even a million years. Ten universal cycles are too long for Lin Yuan.

"Okay," Lin Yuan thought it over and nodded.

"That's good," the Eyes of All Laws nodded slightly.

Just helping Lin Yuan spread the evolutionary path can gain the promise of his future Chaos Realm perfection, and this transaction is not a losing one for the Wanfa Pavilion no matter what.

"Let's begin," the Eyes of All Laws said, and a golden scroll materialized in front of them.

Characters appeared on the scroll, and although Lin Yuan didn't recognize them, a glance revealed the meaning contained within - the requirements the Eyes of All Laws had just proposed.

The Wanfa Pavilion will help Lin Yuan spread his evolutionary path, but in return, after Lin Yuan reaches Chaos Realm perfection, he must do one thing for the Wanfa Pavilion every ten universal cycles.

The scroll contained various restrictions, mentioning that he cannot be forced and that rewards must be paid, etc.

Lin Yuan carefully read through it several times.

"If you have no objections, just release a wisp of your soul aura to imprint it on the scroll," the Eyes of All Laws said.

The requirements on the golden scroll cannot be violated. Even if one reaches Chaos Realm perfection, daring to violate it will result in severe consequences, directly severing the possibility of breaking through to the 13th realm.

So as long as Lin Yuan is willing to imprint a wisp of his soul aura on it, the Eyes of All Laws will naturally feel at ease.

"Wait," Lin Yuan thought for a moment. "What is the limitation on the Wanfa Pavilion helping me spread my evolutionary path branches? Is it just one type?"

If they only help Lin Yuan spread the Life and Death Martial Dao evolutionary path, then the requirements on the scroll would be somewhat unfavorable.

One martial dao branch can at most produce a single 12th realm ultimate, and the effect of fixing his own Dao marker and anchor point wouldn't be very significant.

"Of course not just one type," the Eyes of All Laws shook its head. If it were just one, it would have already marked it on the scroll.

"If you open up other evolutionary path branches in your future cultivation, the Wanfa Pavilion can also help spread them," the Eyes of All Laws said.

"That's good," Lin Yuan's heart settled. "No need to wait for the future, I already have some other evolutionary path branches."
