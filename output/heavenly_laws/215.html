On the main planet of the Vermilion Roc, within the Thirteen Mountain Peaks.

Deep inside the grand hall, Lin Yuan slowly opened his eyes.

"Six hundred years have passed in the blink of an eye."

Lin Yuan's gaze rippled as scenes from his experiences in the Tianqing World flashed through his mind, eventually returning to tranquility.

Six hundred years, for an old monster like the Vermilion Roc Star Lord who had lived for hundreds of thousands of years, might have just been a nap.

Even in the eyes of his obviously much younger senior brothers, it might have just been the time to close up once, to cultivate a secret technique?

But for Lin Yuan, it could already be considered a long time. Even adding the previous few times of traversing, the six hundred years definitely accounted for a significant portion of the total time.

"Phew."

Lin Yuan looked around.

"The world has changed."

Lin Yuan's eyes were calm.

After comprehending over a thousand core spatial models,

Lin Yuan could now see, wherever his gaze reached, the countless "ripples" caused by the fluctuations of spatial power.

If the Tianqing World was a small pond,

Lin Yuan could trace the source and see the end of every "ripple",

Everything was under his control.

Then the universe and starry sky of the main world was like a vast ocean in comparison,

Any spatial "ripple" could spread and affect extremely distant places,

Even if Lin Yuan tried his best to observe, he could only see a small segment of the fluctuations.

"Different world scales lead to different perceptions and feelings."

Lin Yuan looked pensive.

He clearly felt that after returning to the main world,

The bottlenecks and shackles faced in comprehending core spatial models,

Were now, if not completely swept away, at least mostly dissipated.

"Let's put those aside for now."

"Let's check the connection between the Myriad Worlds Gate and the Tianqing World."

Lin Yuan gathered his thoughts and immersed his mind, looking towards that majestic and grand Myriad Worlds Gate.

In the last two hundred years in the Tianqing World, Lin Yuan gave up everything and devoted all his energy to establishing a method to replace the world consciousness.

If it was just about controlling the Tianqing World, Lin Yuan didn't need to go through so much trouble. With the strength of the Primordial Demon Tree back then, wherever its will reached, who dared to disobey?

Lin Yuan's true goal was to transform the Tianqing World into his backyard, and then have some ideas about the boundless void outside the Tianqing World.

Buzz.

Lin Yuan carefully observed the area below the Myriad Worlds Gate.

He saw that after the previous six world coordinate imprints, there was a faint seventh world coordinate imprint taking shape.

It was precisely the world coordinates of the Tianqing World.

Lin Yuan stared at the Tianqing World coordinates as bits of information began to pour into his mind.

"As expected."

"Now, choosing to descend to the Tianqing World only consumes one-third of a strand of world-breaking power?"

Lin Yuan was delighted.

For precisely descending to a certain world using world coordinates like this, the consumption of world-breaking power was ten strands.

But now, it was only one-third of a strand, clearly the Tianqing World no longer resisted Lin Yuan's consciousness and even actively accepted it, greatly saving the consumption of world-breaking power.

"One-third of a strand of world-breaking power is equivalent to just over ninety days in the main world."

A smile appeared on Lin Yuan's face.

The time consumed for the Myriad Worlds Gate to normally accumulate one strand of world-breaking power was three hundred and thirty-three days.

With the boost from the permanent spatial rift, one strand only required two hundred and ninety days.

"Also, through the world coordinates on the Myriad Worlds Gate, I seem to be able to receive a lot of information from the Primordial Demon Tree?"

Lin Yuan suddenly noticed something.

The current Primordial Demon Tree had become the new world consciousness of the Tianqing World, that is, the "Heavenly Dao".

And the Primordial Demon Tree was just Lin Yuan's vessel, similar to an avatar. In principle, the two should share the same consciousness and memories.

However.

Due to the vast distance between the main world and the Tianqing World in the boundless void,

They were not even in the same dimension or the same space-time, and there was a huge difference in the flow of time.

That's why the perceptual connection between Lin Yuan and the Primordial Demon Tree was completely severed.

"I didn't expect the Myriad Worlds Gate to have this use as well."

A smile appeared on Lin Yuan's face. Through this faint connection, Lin Yuan had a general understanding of the situation in the Tianqing World, so the timing of choosing to descend would also be more precise.

Even though the cost of descending to the Tianqing World now was only one-third of a strand of world-breaking power, even one-tenth of a strand was still world-breaking power and could not be consumed casually.

After confirming the connection with the Tianqing World.

Lin Yuan turned his attention to himself.

"Let's take a look at the inner world."

With a slight movement of his mind, Lin Yuan's consciousness descended into his inner world.

Before the sixth traversal, Lin Yuan's inner world was only ten miles in diameter.

But now.

As Lin Yuan's consciousness descended.

Many insights into the laws of space merged in.

Rumble.

The inner world directly underwent a huge change.

At the edge of the world, countless spatial powers gathered, space trembled, and the world's limit rapidly increased.

This led to.

Lin Yuan once again fell into a kind of crazy hunger.

When he stepped into the seventh stage and opened up his inner world, Lin Yuan had this feeling.

But at that time, he had prepared ample Scarlet Spirit Liquid, so the opening of the inner world was exceptionally smooth.

But now...

"Wait a moment, wait a moment."

Lin Yuan hurriedly suppressed the expansion of his inner world. With his current comprehension of the laws of space, the final limit of his inner world was at least over ten thousand miles in diameter.

Ten miles in diameter versus over ten thousand miles in diameter, it was definitely not as simple as a thousand-fold or tens of thousands-fold increase. The energy requirement was terrifying.

If Lin Yuan didn't suppress it, he would probably be sucked dry.

"Actively suppressing the expansion of my inner world, this kind of behavior of mine can be considered rare in the history of human civilization, right?"

Generally speaking, the expansion of an eighth-stage evolutioner's inner world was gradual. It mainly depended on the comprehension of the laws of space.

And naturally, comprehending the laws of space, especially the core spatial models, would not see a sudden leap in a short period of time, but a slow improvement.

In this way, the expansion of the inner world could be achieved by naturally inhaling and exhaling external energy.

As for Lin Yuan's current situation, instantly comprehending over a thousand core spatial models.

Such a span, such a magnitude, if spoken out loud, probably not many eighth-stage evolutioners would believe it.

"Requesting Scarlet Spirit Liquid."

Lin Yuan immediately connected his consciousness to the virtual network and began to request Scarlet Spirit Liquid in large quantities.

"It will probably take a few days."

After completing the request, Lin Yuan thought to himself.

If it was just a normal quota of Scarlet Spirit Liquid, if Lin Yuan requested it now, it would probably be delivered within half an hour.

After all, he was currently on the main planet of the Vermilion Roc.

But the amount of Scarlet Spirit Liquid Lin Yuan requested was simply too huge, tens of thousands of times the amount he requested when he broke through to the seventh stage back then.

"The Martial Dao Evolution Path, Seventh Stage Chapter."

What Lin Yuan needed to focus on next was improving his willpower and mind, in order to face the Demon Heart Tribulation.

However, there was no specific way to pass the Demon Heart Tribulation. The current common method used by the Human Alliance to pass it was simply to live long enough.

The Human Civilization Alliance's condition for becoming an Evolution Grand Scholar was to create a complete, stable, and brand-new Eighth Stage evolution path.

Although it was just one evolution path, the many evolution theories involved in it were vast like smoke on the sea. A Grand Scholar who could create an Eighth Stage evolution path undoubtedly had an extremely profound understanding of many things in the field of evolution.

Becoming an Eighth Stage evolved human might involve some luck, such as obtaining a rare treasure that could assist in opening up an inner world.

But becoming an Evolution Grand Scholar had no luck at all, it was purely one's own strength.

Since the opening of the Chikun Star Domain, in over a million years, only three Evolution Grand Scholars had been born.

In comparison, the number of Eighth Stage evolved humans was hundreds to thousands of times more.
