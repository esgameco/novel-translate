"Silver River, what is your strength?"

The Crimson Flame Patriarch looked at Lin Yuan, his heart stirring with waves.

Before he even opened his mouth to inquire, he had already surmised that it was Lin Yuan who had clashed with the Nether Realm Patriarch.

After all, that scorching sun-like aura could not be faked, it was absolutely the power of a strong cultivator from the Ancient Blazing Sun Realm. And at that time in that area, only Lin Yuan came from the Ancient Blazing Sun Realm.

Although this possibility was extremely incredible - the Silver River Patriarch was a top-tier supreme Patriarch? But it was the only possibility.

"Not long ago, I had a breakthrough," Lin Yuan said.

This was also the truth. If he had not condensed the key Blazing Sun Seed and formed the Blazing Sun Great Circulation within his body, his strength would not have undergone a qualitative leap.

At most, Lin Yuan was at the same level as the Crimson Lotus Patriarch.

After all, Lin Yuan's current true life realm was only at the Sovereign Realm.

Without crossing a great realm, it was difficult to suddenly jump from a Supreme Patriarch to a top-tier Supreme Patriarch.

"Hahaha, good, good, good!"

The Vast Flame Patriarch burst into laughter, looking at Lin Yuan with great appreciation: "With you, the Silver River Patriarch, here, the Ancient Blazing Sun Realm now has four top-tier Supreme Patriarchs, which means there will be more methods to contend for and refine the core celestial pillar."

The top-tier Supreme Patriarchs of the major ancient realms were certainly not just four in number, but most of the top-tier Supreme Patriarchs, even the strongest Supreme Patriarchs, were in seclusion, attempting to break through to the Thirteenth Realm. As for competing for the newly born sacred land? It was difficult to attract these Supreme Patriarchs.

And the top-tier Supreme Patriarchs that the Ancient Blazing Sun Realm could mobilize at any time were only the Crimson Flame, Vast Flame, and Eternal Sun Patriarchs.

Other ancient realms were about the same, with only the Ancient Profound Realm having a few more.

The Ancient Blazing Sun Realm having one more top-tier Supreme Patriarch combat strength would definitely boost its foundation. It might not be apparent in the early stage, but when it came to the later stage of competing and fighting with other ancient realms, the usefulness would be great.

"The Silver River Patriarch was able to shatter the Nether Realm Patriarch's right Nether Eye, which in terms of killing power, is already quite strong among the top-tier Supreme Patriarchs," the Eternal Sun Patriarch said thoughtfully as he looked at Lin Yuan.

He had fought against the Nether Realm Patriarch before and was clear about the latter's methods. Even if the Nether Realm Patriarch had underestimated Lin Yuan and trapped him in the right Nether Eye, shattering it was still very difficult.

Unless it was a power that instantly exceeded the Nether Eye's limit of tolerance, otherwise, with even the slightest delay, the Nether Realm Patriarch would have time to react and there would be a chance of expelling Lin Yuan from within.

"It was just luck," Lin Yuan said.

"Hahaha, the stronger the Silver River Patriarch is, the more advantageous it will be for us to compete for the sacred land," the Crimson Flame Patriarch said with a smile on his face.

He did not expect the Lin Yuan who was stuffed in by the Emperor to bring him such a pleasant surprise.

The other numerous Supreme Patriarchs looked at Lin Yuan with somewhat complex gazes. Originally, Lin Yuan was at the same level as them, but in the blink of an eye, he had become a top-tier Supreme Patriarch? "The Silver River Patriarch, I was still on par with him not long ago?"

The Crimson Lotus Patriarch looked at Lin Yuan, his thoughts in turmoil.

Not long ago, he had sparred with Lin Yuan, and at that time, he was still amazed by the power of this Silver River Patriarch.

But it had only been a short while, and now, not only could he not spar with the Silver River Patriarch, it would be considered a rare feat if he could even defend himself against the other party.

"That sacred land's core celestial pillar is just about to be fully refined," Lin Yuan looked at the Crimson Flame Patriarch, Eternal Sun Patriarch, and Vast Flame Patriarch, the three top-tier Supreme Patriarchs, and spoke.

According to the plan they had initially formulated, all the discovered sacred land core celestial pillars should be refined by these three top-tier Supreme Patriarchs.

As for the other Supreme Patriarchs, their main task was to guard the sacred land core celestial pillars.

"Since you are also a top-tier Supreme Patriarch, Silver River, you can refine the sacred land core celestial pillars that are discovered yourself," the Crimson Flame Patriarch nodded.

"Alright."

Lin Yuan's thoughts stirred.

The true body's main avatar in front of that core celestial pillar immediately completed the refinement of the last remaining area of the celestial pillar.

Hum.

In the moment of refinement,

Lin Yuan vaguely sensed a connection with this core celestial pillar.

"Now, as long as I'm within the range of this sacred land, I can instantly appear beside this core celestial pillar?" Lin Yuan thought to himself.

The reason why Crimson Flame Patriarch and the others had formulated the plan for the top-tier Supreme Patriarchs to refine the core celestial pillars was based on this ability.

The top-tier Supreme Patriarchs had the strongest strength, and if they refined the core celestial pillars, they could instantly appear beside any core celestial pillar, which could best protect the celestial pillars.

Of course, this instant appearance had to be based on the premise of stable space-time. If they were in the midst of a fierce battle with other powerful cultivators, and the surrounding space-time was disintegrating and collapsing, they would not be able to escape and appear through the core celestial pillar.

Previously, the Nether Realm Patriarch had brought the main combat bodies of many Supreme Patriarchs to search for the core celestial pillars, allowing these Supreme Patriarchs to refine the outer areas of the celestial pillars, while leaving the final innermost areas for himself to refine.

This was the most efficient method.

"At the same time, I can feel that the suppression of this sacred land on me has decreased a bit," Lin Yuan looked around.

Completely refining this core celestial pillar had allowed him to sense many changes. Previously, his divine ability 'World' could at most carefully examine the area within a hundred billion li around him.

But now, the exploration range had probably increased by about one-tenth.

This exploration was a careful one, able to detect the core celestial pillars.

It could be inferred that the more core celestial pillars he refined, the larger the exploration range would become.

In the Capital of the Great Spirit Realm,

A manifestation of the Nether Realm Patriarch sat there.

The other eleven avatar figures were also quite extraordinary.

The precious treasures nurtured by each first-class treasure land had different effects, such as the Black Jade from the Black Jade's Nest, which could enhance the strength of the mind and will, or the Infinite Crystal from the Infinite Eye, which could grant the Infinite Divine Body.

"This mist..."

Lin Yuan observed it carefully, his expression gradually changing.
