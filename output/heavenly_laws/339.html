From a certain perspective, the 'Xuanhuang Strike' is more like a "passive" secret technique.

Of course, activating the 'Xuanhuang Strike' consumes a lot, but what Lin Yuan fears least now is consumption.

That vast inner world, whether judged by its intuitive size or spatial depth, is unknown multiples larger than an ordinary ninth-level cultivator's.

"If I combine the 'Xuanhuang Strike' with the divine ability 'Augmentation', my instantaneous explosive attack power..." Lin Yuan's heart raced.

So far, Lin Yuan's defensive means far exceeded his offensive means, after all, the divine ability 'Augmentation' cannot be used at will.

But with the 'Xuanhuang Strike', it can greatly make up for Lin Yuan's offensive capabilities.

As for the defensive secret technique 'Xuanhuang Body', it is a body refining secret technique.

The body becomes like the mystic yellow, indestructible and imperishable.

This secret technique is also divided into four stages.

Once cultivated to perfection,

The flesh and blood permeates trillions of light years, even if facing an ultimate existence, it would be extremely difficult to destroy.

Lin Yuan remembered that among the secret techniques left by the master of the Demon Jade Pavilion, there was one called the 'Undying Demon Jade Body', which was also an extremely formidable body refining method.

It's just unknown whether it is stronger or weaker compared to the 'Xuanhuang Body'.

"Good, good, good."

Lin Yuan rejoiced in his heart.

Although he had two powerful physical divine abilities protecting his body, as well as the Origin Bell guarding his physical body, soul and spirit,

The ability to revive from a drop of blood only enhances his revival power, and does not increase his physical strength. The elemental body only has an immunity effect, and also does not increase physical strength.

The liquid in the mysterious gourd does increase his physical strength, but that is not a secret technique.

Most importantly, secret techniques can be universally cultivated, even his primordial spirit can cultivate them.

Like the Origin Bell, it can only be used as a trump card for his main body. Lin Yuan is unwilling to give the Origin Bell to his primordial spirit.

If his primordial spirit dies, so be it, Lin Yuan doesn't care. But if he loses the Origin Bell, he would definitely feel heartbroken.

"Remember, there are no taboos for cultivating the 'Xuanhuang Strike' and 'Xuanhuang Body'. Cultivate them according to your own situation. But for the 'Spacetime Steps', even if you cultivate it, you must use it cautiously."

The six-armed figure suddenly said.

"'Spacetime Steps'?"

Lin Yuan's expression turned solemn, and information about this secret technique surfaced in his mind.

This is an escape secret technique. The most precious aspect is that this escape technique combines the rules of time and space.

The rules of time and space are the two major pillars of rules. Especially the rule of time, which is abstruse and difficult to understand, let alone creating a secret technique based on the combination of these two major rules.

"The 'Spacetime Steps' can allow you to instantly traverse vast distances in space. But you must be clear, you can only take nine steps at most, even the strongest can only take nine steps."

The six-armed figure said solemnly.

Lin Yuan understood.

If the 'Xuanhuang Strike' consumes his power by "quantity", then the 'Spacetime Steps' consumes by "proportion".

Regardless of what realm a cultivator is in, activating this secret technique and taking one step will consume one-ninth of all their power - blood, soul, spirit and everything.

Taking nine consecutive steps will deplete all of one's power completely.

If another step is taken at this point, it will cause irreversible damage.

Of course, the price is huge. But the effect is also astonishing.

At Lin Yuan's current realm and strength, activating the 'Spacetime Steps' would allow him to traverse hundreds of millions of light-years with each step.

Hundreds of millions of light-years, in just one step?

One should know that with an ordinary great translocation technique, even if Lin Yuan exhausts all his power, he can only traverse thousands of light-years in one step.

Hundreds of millions of light-years? Lin Yuan would need to take a thousand consecutive steps without pause.

Even if it takes one breath per step, that's still a thousand breaths for a thousand steps.

With this secret technique, even facing an eleventh-level expert, as long as Lin Yuan has not taken all nine steps, the opponent cannot catch up.

By the time Lin Yuan finishes the nine steps, he would have likely reached a safe place. One step is hundreds of millions of light-years, nine steps is equivalent to billions of light-years.

Even in the vast cosmic starry sky, billions of light-years is no short distance.

"If an ultimate expert uses this secret technique, they can descend upon any location in the universe with just one casual step." Lin Yuan thought to himself.

The stronger one's strength and realm, the greater the distance traversed with each step of this secret technique. Lin Yuan can travel hundreds of millions of light-years per step, while an ultimate expert can likely traverse half the universe in a single step.

No matter how far, they could reach it in just two steps.

However, for an ultimate expert, the consumption of one-ninth or two-ninths of their total power is no small amount either.

"You have fully obtained the inheritance of the Sovereign." The six-armed figure looked at Lin Yuan. "Next, there is one more thing you need to do."

"What is it?" Lin Yuan immediately asked.

If the three secret techniques of offense, defense and escape were known to many, they would inevitably target their weaknesses.

No secret technique is perfect. For example, the 'Spacetime Steps' seems invincible, but its flaw is also obvious - it can only take nine steps.

After nine steps, one would fall into absolute weakness.

If the enemy doesn't know about this, it's fine. But once they find out, they could specifically wait until after the ninth step to pursue and attack when one is vulnerable. What then?

The 'Xuanhuang Strike' and 'Xuanhuang Body' also have weaknesses and flaws, though not as glaring as the 'Spacetime Steps'. But they absolutely cannot be spread externally.

This is not just about Lin Yuan, the Xuanhuang inheritor, but about the other Xuanhuang inheritors across the universe.

"No problem." Lin Yuan agreed without hesitation.

Just don't spread them externally.

Lin Yuan never intended to spread them in the first place.

Spreading such extremely personal life-protecting secret techniques is like actively revealing one's weaknesses, isn't it?

"Good." The six-armed figure nodded. "Repeat after me."

"I, a disciple of the Sovereign Xuanhuang, swear by the Xuanhuang's mark that I will absolutely not spread the protective secret techniques of the Xuanhuang lineage. If I violate this oath, my soul will be extinguished."

The six-armed figure looked at Lin Yuan.

"I, a disciple of the Sovereign Xuanhuang, swear by the Xuanhuang's mark that I will absolutely not spread the protective secret techniques of the Xuanhuang lineage. If I violate this oath, my soul will be extinguished."

Lin Yuan pondered for a moment, then repeated the oath.

The instant he finished reciting it, a towering yellow mountain-like mark emerged from the depths of his soul, then quickly dissipated.

"The Xuanhuang's mark?" Lin Yuan's heart stirred slightly.

Apart from the Xuanhuang's mark, he also had the Demon Jade's mark deep in his soul.

"Alright." The six-armed figure seemed to relax a bit.

"You naturally formed the Xuanhuang's mark by cultivating the Xuanhuang's evolutionary path. I know you are a life form from within the universe that can be revived by ultimate existences. But this oath is rooted in the depths of your memory. If you die because of this oath, even if revived, as long as the memory remains, you will continue dying endlessly."

"Rest assured." Lin Yuan nodded.

"But you can be at ease, as long as you don't spread the secret techniques, nothing will happen." The six-armed figure added.

"Mm." Lin Yuan also understood clearly.

After taking that oath, a wealth of information surged into his mind, every single word was definite and without any doubt.

"Refine the secret realm's core."

Lin Yuan exhaled lightly.

He then sat cross-legged, took out the yellow crystal, and began infusing it with the power of the world, slowly refining it.

"Hm?"

"So this is what the entire Xuanhuang secret realm looks like?" Through the secret realm's core, Lin Yuan saw the true full appearance of the Xuanhuang secret realm.

The full appearance of the Xuanhuang secret realm consisted of three vast continents. The many ninth-level experts from the cosmic starry sky that entered only accessed one of those continents.

As for the other two continents, they remained unopened, shrouded in dense mists. Apart from Lin Yuan and the six-armed figure, not a single expert had detected them.

"This Xuanhuang secret realm is too inconceivable, no wonder it evaded the lockdown of all the ultimate experts." As he continued refining the secret realm's core, Lin Yuan understood more about the Xuanhuang secret realm.

The Xuanhuang secret realm did not exist in any particular spatial layer, but rather in the gaps between spatial layers, changing positions as the countless spatial layers of the universe flowed and shifted.

In other words, the Xuanhuang secret realm's location changed every moment, which is why the ultimate experts could not accurately lock onto it.

Lin Yuan sat cross-legged.

The primordial spirit at the Xuanhuang secret realm's sun was furiously refining the secret realm's core, striving to master the entire Xuanhuang secret realm as soon as possible.

As for the rules of time, that could wait - breaking through from the ninth to tenth level did not require much understanding of the time rules.
