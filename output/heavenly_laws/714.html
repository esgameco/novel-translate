Perhaps paying a huge price to go back would be very disadvantageous in the short term.

But over a longer timeline, as long as they could befriend Lin Yuan and befriend the future Time Lord, what did this small cost matter? Moreover, Lin Yuan was definitely not an ordinary Time Lord.

Possessing this kind of almost certain future, his background was so great that even the thirteen Eternal Lords dared not think too deeply about it.

Otherwise, if twelve Eternal Lords went back and became familiar with young friend 'Galaxy', leaving just one Eternal Lord who didn't go,

Many future opportunities in the timelines would likely have nothing to do with that one.
