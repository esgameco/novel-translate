Chapter 232 - Entering the Eighth Stage (Please Subscribe)

At this moment, a vast mental will condensed within Lin Yuan's body. It made him feel that everything he currently saw was like an illusion, easily punctured with a casual hand.

Splash!

The instant Lin Yuan became sober, his consciousness began to sink again.

When Lin Yuan opened his eyes once more, he had already arrived in the cosmic starry sky. A powerful alien race's aura came from behind - Lin Yuan was apparently being hunted by the alien race.

"Hahaha, even if you are the ultimate genius of human civilization, so what? You still have to die." The alien pursuer behind him laughed maniacally.

"I'm being hunted?" Lin Yuan frowned, instinctively wanting to continue fleeing for his life.

The next moment, the vast mental will condensed in his body again, making Lin Yuan instantly understand his situation.

"Mere heart demons." Lin Yuan stopped fleeing as the surrounding illusions began collapsing again.

The essence of the Heart Demon Tribulation was heart demons. When facing the Heart Demon Tribulation, the Evolver would experience the scenes they were most unwilling to experience. Or the scenes they most wanted to experience.

For example, what Lin Yuan feared most was being prematurely killed by alien races before he could grow up. Originally, if one's own mental will did not meet the standard, the Evolver could easily get lost in the layers of illusions before them, which meant failing the Heart Demon Tribulation.

And a powerful mental will could make the Evolver quickly sober up. Or let the Evolver realize that everything around them was an illusion, all fictitious. Naturally, the probability of passing the Heart Demon Tribulation would greatly increase.

In the blink of an eye, Lin Yuan continuously broke through hundreds of heart demon illusions.

The scene changed. An insect race mother nest comparable to a star system appeared in the distance. Seventh stage, eighth stage, and even ninth stage insect race warriors manufactured by the mother nest rushed out one after another.

"Is this an Insect Queen?" Lin Yuan was startled in his heart. An Insect Queen could continuously produce a large number of seventh, eighth and ninth stage insect warriors. These insect warriors, in a one-on-one fight, were definitely not a match for human Evolvers of the same stage.

But there was no way to match the sheer numbers - endless, millions of ordinary eighth stage insect warriors would make even the Vermilion Roc Star Lord's scalp go numb upon seeing them. It was precisely because of this that in the war between human civilization and the insect race, the human side did not have much of an advantage in the low and mid-level battlefields, but rather a disadvantage.

Only in the struggle at the supreme level were the Insect Queens suppressed by the supreme powerhouses on the human side. Because the Insect Queens could not produce insect warriors at the supreme level.

"An Insect Queen personally making a move against me is too fake." Lin Yuan shook his head slightly. Even without a powerful mental will, he felt that the scene before him was somewhat absurd. The Queens were such existences that they could forcibly contend against supreme powerhouses.

Even the ultimate genius Evolver of the human race, unmatched in an era, was not worthy of a Queen's personal action. Their identity and status were there.

Rumble!

As the vast mental will gathered, Lin Yuan crushed this layer of heart demon illusion again.

On the top of a majestic and grand Evolution Tower, Lin Yuan sat cross-legged. At this moment, Lin Yuan's aura was as deep and mysterious as the cosmic starry sky, and wherever his gaze reached was the scope of his own territory.

"Great supreme powerhouse, under your leadership, human civilization has become an unprecedented force in the cosmic starry sky. The insect race and other top races have surrendered one after another." An old man below said loudly, his tone containing rich worship.

"Becoming a supreme powerhouse and leading human civilization to the peak is something every human Evolver yearns to achieve." At this moment, Lin Yuan was being worshipped by countless humans, lofty and looking down, like a deity.

"Unfortunately, it's fake, all fake." Lin Yuan sighed, and the scenes in all directions began to collapse, along with the invincible power in Lin Yuan's body that was also dissipating.

On the high mountain, Lin Yuan opened his eyes without a sound. "The Heart Demon Tribulation has ended." Lin Yuan's eyes were deep and distant, as if he had broken through some shackles, and his entire mental state suddenly began to sublimate.

There were three prerequisites to becoming an eighth stage Evolver. One was the Heart Demon Tribulation, the second was the inner world, and the third was the ultimate perfection of the physical body and soul.

Unable to pass the Heart Demon Tribulation, one would forever be only at the peak of the seventh stage. Even if the inner world and the physical body and soul all met the requirements, it would be useless. Because the realm could not be promoted.

Only after passing the Heart Demon Tribulation could one smoothly advance to the eighth stage in terms of realm. Next, if the inner world was opened up and the physical body and soul reached perfection, one could officially step into the eighth stage.

"Let's end the traversal." Lin Yuan glanced at the remaining time. There were still five years left.

The countless layers of heart demon illusions Lin Yuan experienced in the Heart Demon Tribulation were just an instant in reality. As for the remaining five years in the traversal world, there was no need to waste them here.

The most important thing for Lin Yuan now was to completely step into the eighth stage.

Vermilion Roc Main Star.

Lin Yuan opened his eyes.

Splash!

A large amount of Vermilion Spirit Liquid appeared in front of him. Early on, several years ago, Lin Yuan had already begun to prepare for stepping into the eighth stage, naturally not forgetting about the Vermilion Spirit Liquid.

"Breakthrough." Lin Yuan closed his eyes.

Rumble!

First, the inner world began to expand crazily again. The original inner world of only 130,000 li in circumference instantly surged to 250,000 li in circumference.

The surge of the inner world did not cause much of a disturbance. Previously, when Lin Yuan expanded his inner world, it surged from a dozen or so li in circumference to 120,000 li in circumference.

But now, going from 130,000 li to 250,000 li in circumference, although the increase was greater than before, the base number was much stronger. Lin Yuan had the complete ability to restrain it himself.

Vermilion Palace.

The Vermilion Roc Star Lord was instructing the Great Peak Lord. With the Great Peak Lord's breakthrough to the eighth stage and imminent departure to take charge of the star domain, as his teacher, the Vermilion Roc Star Lord would naturally bestow some trump cards and backup plans.

"Rest assured, Teacher, I will be cautious and careful." The Great Peak Lord nodded, also knowing that the Vermilion Roc Star Lord was concerned about him.

"I wonder, among my disciples, who will be the next to break through to the eighth stage?" The Vermilion Roc Star Lord sighed, saying with some emotion.

"Second Junior Brother? Third Junior Brother? Or Little Junior Brother?" The Great Peak Lord tried to guess.

Among the Vermilion Roc Star Lord's disciples now, only these three were at the seventh stage.

"Who do you think it will be?" The Vermilion Roc Star Lord asked with interest.

"In terms of talent, Little Junior Brother is definitely the strongest, but to step into the eighth stage, mental will is very important. This requires accumulation over a long period of time. Second Junior Brother has almost accumulated enough in this aspect. As long as he breaks through to the King realm, he should be able to try to open up his inner world and then charge into the eighth stage." The Great Peak Lord analyzed calmly.

It was not that he looked down on Little Junior Brother, but the increase in the mental will index indeed required a lot of time. Little Junior Brother was not even a hundred years old now.

"What you said is not bad." Upon hearing this, the Vermilion Roc Star Lord pondered carefully for a moment and nodded in agreement.

"It's just..." Before the Vermilion Roc Star Lord could finish, it was as if he sensed something.
