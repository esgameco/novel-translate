Lin Yuan was surprised.

In a sense, the imperial capital was also a treasure trove.

Here was the largest yang vein of the ancient Blazing Sun Kingdom, the sacred land for the cultivation of all powerful practitioners on the yang path.

"It is to be selected from the currently unassigned resource treasures."

The Emperor looked at Lin Yuan and shook his head, saying, "Take a closer look."

"Okay."

Lin Yuan nodded and looked at the territorial map of the ancient Blazing Sun Kingdom.

The territory of the ancient Blazing Sun Kingdom was vast and boundless, not limited to a certain spatial dimension, as long as it was within the temporal and spatial scope, it belonged to the ancient Blazing Sun Kingdom.

"Any three unclaimed treasure lands can be selected?"

The Crimson Treasure King was slightly surprised.

The resource treasure lands of the ancient Blazing Sun Kingdom were divided from top to bottom into first-class to ninth-class.

The first-class treasure lands had the highest value, and the ninth-class treasure lands had the lowest value.

Normally, when a royal family member is enfeoffed as a king, they would be granted one or two treasure lands, but they would usually be fourth-class or fifth-class treasure lands.

To occupy more precious resource treasure lands? It's possible, but one needs to exchange it with meritorious deeds.

But Lin Yuan?

The Emperor's intention was to allow him to freely select any three treasure lands from the currently unclaimed ones? Wouldn't that mean he could directly select three first-class treasure lands? Occupying first-class treasure lands, in addition to the meritorious deeds granted by the ancient kingdom, the resources produced by the treasure lands themselves were also extremely precious.

Even if nine-tenths were to be handed over to the ancient kingdom, the remaining one-tenth would be enough to make Celestial Venerable-level experts envious.

The resources produced by the treasure lands were not one-time, but continuous.

Some special resources were even priceless, as soon as they appeared, they would be bought up, and they could not be purchased at ordinary times.

"Consider it carefully, the more precious the treasure land, the more risks it entails."

The Emperor reminded.

"Understood."

Lin Yuan nodded.

Why do treasure lands require powerful practitioners to occupy them? It is because they need to withstand the risks.

These risks come from many reasons, some treasure lands are located at the border between two ancient kingdoms, and may face invasions from the opposing ancient kingdom.

And some treasure lands are connected to certain special dangerous areas, such as the first-class treasure land called "Star Sand River" in the ancient Blazing Sun Kingdom.

It produces the precious resource star sand, which is a rare cultivation treasure, and even for Celestial Venerables, it has a significant effect, making it sought after by the powerful practitioners of various ancient kingdoms.

However, the bottom of the Star Sand River is connected to a mysterious world, and occasionally, the powerful practitioners from that mysterious world will invade along the Star Sand River.

The one occupying the Star Sand River must ensure its safety and the continuous output of star sand to be handed over to the ancient kingdom.

In general, the more precious the occupied resource treasure land, the greater the benefits, but as for the hidden dangers? If a treasure land is too dangerous, even if it is extremely precious, no powerful practitioner would choose to occupy it.

Once the resource requirements set by the ancient kingdom are not met while occupying a certain resource treasure land, one will be punished, so the Emperor reminded Lin Yuan to consider it carefully.

Lin Yuan had already familiarized himself with many of the resource treasure lands of the ancient Blazing Sun Kingdom before coming here.

After all, any core royal family member would be granted one or two treasure lands, and Lin Yuan estimated that he would be no exception, he just didn't expect the Emperor to let him freely choose the treasure lands.

"My ancient Blazing Sun Kingdom is constantly at war with other ancient kingdoms, which is essentially a struggle for more precious resource treasure lands."

The Emperor smiled and asked, "Silver River, have you decided which three treasure lands to choose?"

Lin Yuan looked at the many flickering lights on the territorial map in front of him, which were all the treasure lands he could choose from, and after a brief contemplation, he circled the three resource treasure lands he had selected.

"The Black Jade's Nest?" The Emperor and the Crimson Treasure King glanced at it.

The three treasure lands Lin Yuan chose were two first-class and one second-class.

Among them, the Black Jade's Nest was one of the two first-class treasure lands.

The Black Jade's Nest produced a special resource called black jade, which could refine the spiritual will of living beings.

For powerful practitioners, spiritual will is extremely important, and its importance to the enhancement of one's strength is not as obvious as the physical body and soul, but it is also exceptionally crucial.

It is precisely because of this that the Black Jade's Nest can be listed as a first-class treasure land.

Lin Yuan's choice of the Black Jade's Nest was also based on his own considerations.

The strength of spiritual will is more akin to the comprehension of the Dao, and it can synchronize with the chaotic void and this source world.

By occupying the Black Jade's Nest, he can obtain one-tenth of the black jade from this first-class treasure land, which Lin Yuan can use to refine his own spiritual will.

The enhancement of the spiritual will in the source world body and mind will naturally also lead to the improvement of the spiritual will of his true body and spirit in the chaotic void.

"The Black Jade's Nest is located at the border between the ancient Blazing Sun Kingdom and the ancient Dark Yin Kingdom, which is not a safe place." The Emperor looked thoughtfully at Lin Yuan.

The black jade from the Black Jade's Nest is also useful for the powerful practitioners of the ancient Dark Yin Kingdom, so occupying this treasure land would require constantly facing the invasion of the ancient Dark Yin Kingdom's powerful practitioners.

"I know."

Lin Yuan nodded.

He had already planned to deploy a spirit avatar to permanently occupy the Black Jade's Nest.

Generally speaking, the conflicts between the two ancient kingdoms, unless it is a large-scale or even full-scale war, the Celestial Venerable-level combat forces will not be mobilized.

Although the Black Jade's Nest is a precious first-class treasure land, only by long-term occupation and stable harvesting of the black jade can its benefits be fully realized.

Deploying a Celestial Venerable to occupy the Black Jade's Nest for a short period of time, and then being taken over by the enemy's Celestial Venerable, would not bring much benefit.

The strength of Lin Yuan's spirit avatar, although not as strong as his true body and spirit, is still far beyond the normal limit of core royal family members, and it is more than enough to occupy the Black Jade's Nest.

In addition to the Black Jade's Nest, the other first-class treasure land he chose was also quite dangerous, and Lin Yuan planned to deploy another spirit avatar to occupy it.
