Only able to reveal an insignificant trace of spirituality.

The bronze compass that Lin Yuan possessed was like this, as was Tuobagu Shen's inner armor, and Pei Shen's jet-black iron staff.

However, after carefully sensing this silver mirror, Lin Yuan discovered the vast fluctuations of space contained within.

"Space?"

Lin Yuan's expression turned solemn. To contain such a degree of spatial fluctuations in this two-dimensional world, what would it be like in the main universe?

"This mirror is quite extraordinary."

Lin Yuan thought to himself.

"But what is the use of this mirror?"

Lin Yuan tried for a while, using methods like blood and others, but still made no discoveries.

"Tuobagu Shen must have only recently obtained this mirror," Lin Yuan speculated in his heart.

He had pursued Tuobagu Shen for so long, during which the other party desperately fled, never using this mirror.

From this, it can be deduced that Tuobagu Shen also did not know the abilities of this mirror. Otherwise, in a life-or-death situation, what means would he not employ to stay alive?

"Slowly."

"Even if I can't figure it out here."

"Once I take it out, I'll eventually understand."

The silver mirror was suppressed by the two-dimensional world, unable to exhibit its many wondrous uses.

As long as Lin Yuan could take it out, all problems would naturally be solved.

Even if Lin Yuan still couldn't figure it out, he could inquire with the spirits of the Xuanhuang Secret Realm and the humanoid stone statue Shen Ji in the Devil Jade Space.

Thinking of this, Lin Yuan solemnly put away the silver mirror and took out Tuobagu Shen's inner armor.

This inner armor was very light. Lin Yuan put it on and vaguely felt his limbs and torso being protected by some force.

Wearing the inner armor, any strikes on Lin Yuan's body would be blocked by the armor itself. Even the residual force that seeped through would be dispersed and diluted.

"No wonder Tuobagu Shen was so hard to kill," Lin Yuan smiled.

Wearing this inner armor practically maxed out his defensive abilities.

"If I take this inner armor to the outside world, who knows what kind of power it would have," Lin Yuan's spirit soared.

Among all types of weapons, defensive weapons are far more precious than offensive ones. After all, survival is always the top priority, no matter what.

"The only downside is that the head is not protected," Lin Yuan shook his head slightly.

The inner armor only covered the limbs and torso, but not the head. Unless there was a corresponding helmet, the head would be exposed.

And in this two-dimensional world, there was no concept of reviving after decapitation. Once the head was blown apart, no matter how strong, one would die.

Of course, as a fatal weakness, every powerful being from the myriad races would pay attention. When facing Lin Yuan's pursuit, Tuobagu Shen had carefully protected his head.

Moreover, although the head was a fatal weakness, the skull was also the hardest bone in the body. When tempering their flesh, powerful beings from the myriad races placed great importance on tempering their bones.

To directly blast apart the skull and head, even beings of the same level would find it very difficult.

"There's also no avoiding penetration," Lin Yuan thought. He had killed Tuobagu Shen by penetrating his flesh before the final explosion.

"This silver mirror can serve as a heart protector."

Lin Yuan took out the mirror and placed it on his chest.

Although he didn't understand the use of the silver mirror, the mirror itself was extremely hard and indestructible, a common trait of all 'wondrous items' in this two-dimensional world.

With the silver mirror as a second layer of defense, at least Lin Yuan's heart would be better protected than other areas.

And with Lin Yuan's attainment in the 'Evergreen' cultivation method, as long as it wasn't his heart, head, or other vital organs, any other damaged organs could recover.

"Iron staff."

After donning the inner armor and heart mirror protector, Lin Yuan looked towards the jet-black iron staff.

The ability of the jet-black iron staff was simple - it was strength. With the same strike enhanced by the jet-black iron staff, the power would surge two to three times.

Don't underestimate this increase. In this two-dimensional world, improving strength was extremely difficult. A two to three-fold increase would allow a powerful being of the myriad races to raise their level by several ranks.

"It's just too big and conspicuous."

Lin Yuan stroked the jet-black iron staff, thinking to himself.

Compared to the compact inner armor and silver mirror, the jet-black iron staff could not be concealed at all. Nearly two meters long, it would stand out no matter where it was placed.

This was also the reason why wondrous items in the two-dimensional world could not be refined. Otherwise, with the nature of these items, adjusting size at will would be the most basic ability.

"Being big doesn't matter."

Lin Yuan thought for a moment. After all, this iron staff provided the most direct boost to his strength.

As for the inner armor? Unless in dire straits, Lin Yuan didn't think he would need to use it.

The inner armor enhanced Lin Yuan's survival ability, but in this world, there were extremely few existences that could force Lin Yuan to retreat for his life.

Moreover, as time passed, Lin Yuan's strength would increase at an ever-faster pace, and such powerful beings would quickly disappear.

"This operation was a complete success."

Lin Yuan summed up the gains from this endeavor.

First, he had avenged his grievances, causing Tuobagu Shen to suffer a fundamental loss of part of his spiritual will.

Tuobagu Shen was originally a special life form at the fifth rank of the eleventh stage. His chances of breaking through to the ultimate concept were already slim. Now that he had lost part of his spiritual will, it could be said that it was not unexpected.

Tuobagu Shen had cut off any hope of reaching the ultimate realm. Not that he couldn't try to break through, but any attempt would lead to death, with almost no chance of success.

This act of severing Tuobagu Shen's future path and hope was Lin Yuan's retaliation for being deceived before.

His good friend Fang Qing had practically died at Tuobagu Shen's hands as well. Although revived by a supreme being, the grievance had been sown.

If Lin Yuan had the ability, he naturally would not let the other party off.

Next were the three wondrous items he obtained, which comprehensively enhanced Lin Yuan's strength.

Defense, offense, plus the 'foresight' from the bronze compass - Lin Yuan had become an all-rounded warrior without any shortcomings.

If he could unlock the abilities of the silver mirror and wield spatial arts, then Lin Yuan could roam freely in this two-dimensional world.

Moreover, these gains were not limited to the two-dimensional world. As long as Lin Yuan could successfully return to the main universe, the gains would multiply tremendously.

The three wondrous items were equivalent to three twelve-star divine treasures and weapons. Just thinking about it filled Lin Yuan with excitement.

"Continue cultivating."

"I can feel that the nineteenth stage of the Shaman Ancestor realm is approaching."

Lin Yuan thought inwardly. He had already lingered at the eighteenth stage of the Shaman Ancestor realm for some time and had many insights into the nineteenth stage ahead.

In the virtual world.

Deputy Tower Master Nalan contacted Lin Yuan in time to inform him of the major events occurring in the two-dimensional world.

This was something Lin Yuan had requested, as he had been hiding in the Luochuan gathering place for a long time, far from the core circles of the two-dimensional world, and was not well-informed about much of the intelligence.

So he had asked Deputy Tower Master Nalan to do this.

Although confident that with his incredible comprehension he could surpass even those legendary lifeforms who had perfected the eleventh stage, Lin Yuan was not so arrogant as to ignore everything.

"A major event, a major event."

Deputy Tower Master Nalan's expression was grave. Upon seeing Lin Yuan, he quickly spoke, "A mysterious expert not weaker than the nine great experts has emerged in the Bloodrain Ancient Kingdom."

The Bloodrain Ancient Kingdom was rather inconspicuous among the twelve ancient kingdoms, with many powerful beings of the myriad races scattered there but never producing any extraordinary experts.

This was also why Tuobagu Shen and the other special lifeforms had targeted the Bloodrain Ancient Kingdom.

They wanted to pick the softest persimmon.

And among the twelve ancient kingdoms, the Bloodrain Ancient Kingdom was undoubtedly the soft persimmon.

However, they never expected that this time, Tuobagu Shen and the other special lifeforms would suffer such a major setback.

"That mysterious expert from the Bloodrain Ancient Kingdom is terrifyingly powerful. With bare hands, he killed Tuobagu Shen and Pei Shen, who both wielded wondrous items!"

Deputy Tower Master Nalan's tone was full of amazement. Although at the same eleventh stage, there was a vast gap between him and Tuobagu Shen and Pei Shen. If he encountered them in the two-dimensional world, he might not even be able to escape.

Lin Yuan listened quietly on the side. He was the mysterious expert Deputy Tower Master Nalan spoke of, but he did not intend to reveal this for now.

There was no benefit, and it would only attract unwanted attention.
