Rule comprehension is one thing, but the composition of strength is not entirely determined by rule comprehension. An ordinary person, even if they comprehend the rules of space and time, what can they do? With a low base of personal power, it is difficult to pose a threat to highly evolved beings.

The Void God Supreme's tone was filled with astonishment. Tianyu Chen's heavy injuries were largely due to the River of Time.

If both Divine Spirits were like this, if the true body were to be deployed, Tianyu Chen would likely be facing a formidable foe.

The Featherfly Supreme muttered softly.

The other Supremes heard this and deeply agreed.

If those alien races had produced a prodigy like the Galactic Overlord, the Nine Supremes would likely have been restless.

But now, it is those alien Twelfth Realm Ultimates who are restless because of the Galactic Overlord's existence.

Although Tianyu Chen's heavy injuries stemmed from the River of Time, if the Galactic Overlord had not dragged him into it, it would not have been so.

And to forcibly drag a Twelfth Realm Ultimate into the River of Time, was that so easy to accomplish?

"If I had been the one to attack then..." Fan Hou's heart trembled.

After performing the secret technique 'Worship the Nine Suns', his own foundation had been weakened.

If he had not been cautious and withdrawn from the attack on the Galactic Overlord under the pretext of injury...

Perhaps Tianyu Chen was able to break free from the constraints of the River of Time because he was originally in a flourishing state.

But him, Fan Hou? With his foundation weakened, and then being dragged into the River of Time, even if he ultimately broke free, the injuries he sustained would likely exceed Tianyu Chen's, and his chances of weathering the Universal Cataclysm would be minuscule.

"This has become troublesome."

Fan Hou's thoughts scattered, and he soon thought of even more distant and broader implications.

The Galactic Overlord had not yet entered the Twelfth Realm, yet had such strength. When he does enter the Twelfth Realm, what then?
