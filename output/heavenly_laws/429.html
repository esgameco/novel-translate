Chapter 429: The Perplexed Supreme Being (Seeking Subscriptions)

The task of refining the supreme martial weapon was naturally assigned to the Solar Primordial Spirit.

Lin Yuan's true self and the two major primordial spirit wills shared consciousness. No matter which aspect did the refining, the other two could enjoy the results.

Essentially speaking, the true self and the primordial spirits belonged to the same person.

The main star of the galaxy.

Lin Yuan sat cross-legged.

"After a few more months of adjustment, I can advance to the eleventh stage."

Lin Yuan thought to himself.

Now, he had almost reached the pinnacle in all aspects.

The only choice left was to cross into the eleventh stage.

In fact, with Lin Yuan's current accumulation, not to mention the tenth stage, even in the eleventh stage, it was unprecedented.

Since the beginning of this universe, no eleventh stage life had comprehended the sixfold realm of space-time.

"However, now I can try to become a sequence disciple of the Devil Jade Pavilion?"

A thought arose in Lin Yuan's mind.

The disciples of the Devil Jade Pavilion, or rather the members of the Devil Jade Pavilion, were divided into four types:

Ordinary disciples/members.

Core disciples/members.

Sequence disciples/members.

Personally transmitted disciples/members.

According to the way Lin Yuan had chosen to join the Devil Jade Pavilion, to become a sequence disciple, the first condition was to comprehend all the demonic evolutionary paths within the body of the supreme being.

The second condition was to be below the twelfth stage of life.

To become an ordinary disciple of the Devil Jade Pavilion, one needed to comprehend three thousand three hundred and thirty-three demonic evolutionary paths.

To become a core disciple of the Devil Jade Pavilion, one needed to comprehend thirty-three thousand three hundred and thirty-three demonic evolutionary paths.

To become a sequence disciple, one needed to comprehend all the demonic evolutionary paths.

These were the conditions for Lin Yuan to become a sequence disciple.

The sequence disciples of the Devil Jade Pavilion were considered the elite foundation of the Devil Jade Pavilion. According to the human-shaped stone statue Shen Ji, if Lin Yuan could become a sequence disciple, he would have the authority to take over that Devil Jade space.

But to become a sequence disciple was too difficult. Even Shen Ji, who had witnessed the creation of many miracles, did not think Lin Yuan had this hope.

To comprehend all the demonic evolutionary paths? And be limited to life below the twelfth stage?

For a life below the twelfth stage to comprehend the demonic evolutionary paths originating from the body of the supreme being? Even if this supreme being had perished, it was almost impossible to achieve.

Most of the lives that became sequence disciples during the heyday of the Devil Jade Pavilion followed the path of cultivating a certain demonic evolutionary path and perfecting it.

To directly comprehend the demonic evolutionary paths from the supreme being's body itself? It was almost impossible to do.

The interference originating from the supreme being's body itself was too great. The ubiquitous will pressure was greatly increasing the difficulty of comprehending the demonic evolutionary paths.

But now Lin Yuan was planning to try.

The main reason was that the rewards for becoming a sequence disciple of the Devil Jade Pavilion greatly attracted Lin Yuan.

Moreover, taking over the Devil Jade space would allow Lin Yuan to enjoy the benefits of being a sequence disciple to the greatest extent.

Finally, the tenth traversal gave Lin Yuan the confidence to comprehend all the demonic evolutionary paths.

Virtual world.

Space for comprehending the demonic evolutionary paths.

The illusory body of the supreme being still hovered in the sky above.

Countless human civilizational evolutionaries came and went.

So far, among the many human civilizational evolutionaries, only the one named "Yuan Qing" had become an ordinary member of the Devil Jade Pavilion by cultivating and supplementing the demonic evolutionary paths.

"The more I cultivate, the more I can sense the unfathomable depths of the supreme being's realm."

Lin Yuan sat cross-legged on the mountain peak, looking up at that body fragment.

Although Lin Yuan had reached the twelfth stage realm in certain aspects, there was still a vast gap compared to a true supreme being.

Not to mention other things, the size of the internal universe within a supreme being's body reached tens of thousands to hundreds of thousands of light-years. The physical and spiritual bodies nurtured in such a vast internal universe, how immense must they be?

Lin Yuan's inner world had already broken through the constraints of the main universe, but it was only two to three billion miles in diameter. Compared to a supreme being's internal universe measured in light-years, it was not even on the same level.

"Let me first comprehend the demonic evolutionary paths."

Lin Yuan gathered his thoughts and carefully observed the body fragment in the sky.

Before the tenth traversal, the biggest obstacle for Lin Yuan in comprehending this body fragment was the ubiquitous mental will pressure originating from within the body.

These mental will pressures constantly interfered with Lin Yuan's comprehension of the demonic evolutionary paths.

Most crucially, compared to when his mental will was below ten thousand, using these mental will pressures to temper his own mental will had almost no effect when his mental will index exceeded ten thousand.

Having lost the use of tempering his mental will, coupled with the constant interference from the mental will pressures, even with Lin Yuan's extraordinary comprehension ability, he saw no hope of comprehending all the demonic evolutionary paths in a short time.

The mental will pressure originated from the supreme being. Any life below the supreme being would be disturbed. Comprehending the demonic evolutionary paths...
