The sacred Blue Cloud Mountain stood tall, as the supreme overlord power before the great changes of heaven and earth. It was the home of the Blue Cloud Sect.

Within the Blue Cloud Mountain, numerous small worlds had been opened up.

They were used to cultivate the fresh blood of the Blue Cloud Sect.

This allowed the sect to persist until the present day, even as most of the outside regions were occupied by the 'Demons'.

In the Blue Cloud Courtyard, Elder Lin Yuan had not changed much compared to a thousand years ago, except that his aura had become more restrained.

"Steward Chen, it is said that the 'Demons' are indestructible. Is there really no way to completely eliminate them?" asked a young disciple, just after exchanging the necessary jade slips.

Over the past thousand years, Elder Lin Yuan had lived in seclusion in the Blue Cloud Courtyard, occasionally guiding the doubts of the newly promoted disciples. Over time, he had gained quite a reputation, and many disciples were willing to ask him questions.

"The Demons originate from the demonic qi of heaven and earth. As long as the demonic qi exists, the Demons will not be extinguished," Lin Yuan said calmly. "To completely eliminate the Demons in the world, one must erase the source of the demonic qi."

The so-called source of the demonic qi was the legendary Demon God.

Having lived in this source world for a thousand years, Lin Yuan had also learned many hidden secrets.

For example, the demonic qi? If the demonic qi were to be compared to sunlight, then the Demon God would be the sun.

As long as the sun exists, the demonic qi will flow endlessly, and the terrifying 'Demons', even if completely annihilated, will be reborn under the endless demonic qi.

"How can one erase the source of the demonic qi?" the young disciple continued to ask.

"I don't know," Lin Yuan shook his head, although he had sensed that all the demonic qi originated from the Demon God.

But where the Demon God was, Lin Yuan did not know.

In fact, even if he knew, he would not try to erase it, as the Demon God's strength was at least at the Thirteenth Realm, capable of brewing a 'Demonic' disaster that could engulf the entire source world.

"Alright, you don't need to think too much about it," Lin Yuan said casually to the worried young disciple.

"Mm," the young disciple nodded and left the Blue Cloud Courtyard.

As soon as he reached the entrance, a few other young disciples gathered around him.

"Ouyang, what did you discuss with Steward Chen?" the young disciples were quite curious.

"I asked how to completely eliminate the 'Demons' in the outside world," the young disciple named Ouyang said truthfully.

"What did Steward Chen say?"

"Steward Chen said that to destroy the Demons, one must erase the source of the demonic qi."

Ouyang explained.

"Erase the source of the demonic qi?" the few young disciples pondered for a while.

They did not doubt Lin Yuan's response. Over the past thousand years, the Steward Chen of the Blue Cloud Courtyard, although he had never revealed any powerful abilities, was well-versed and experienced, and even the elders of the sect would occasionally come to consult him.

"Ah, with Steward Chen's understanding of the 'Demons', why is he still staying in the Blue Cloud Courtyard? Wouldn't it be better for him to go out and eliminate the demonic qi gathering places?"

"I heard that Steward Chen was once infected by the demonic qi during an operation to eliminate a demonic qi gathering place, leaving him with some hidden dangers."

"I see, Steward Chen has made such a great contribution to our sect!" the young disciples said with reverence.

Being infected by demonic qi had severe consequences, and those who survived were very few.

Deep within the Blue Cloud Courtyard, Lin Yuan sat in meditation.

"The waters of this source world are deep. The few eternal sects in the center of heaven and earth must have time immortals guarding them, but they have allowed the demonic qi to spread. What is the reason behind this?" Lin Yuan's expression became slightly solemn.

This was also the reason why, eight hundred years ago, after he had regained his peak strength, he had still hidden himself in the Blue Cloud Mountain - he could not fathom the situation in the outside world.

But hiding in the Blue Cloud Mountain did not hinder Lin Yuan's cultivation and enlightenment.

The symbolic scriptures and techniques recorded in the Blue Cloud Sect were enough for Lin Yuan to ponder and comprehend for hundreds or even thousands of years.

Moreover, Lin Yuan also needed a lot of time to slowly open up his own particle worlds. With the demonic qi permeating the outside world and 'Demons' everywhere, if he ever lacked resources, he could simply go out and return fully loaded, as the so-called 'Demons' were nothing but chicks in his eyes, and even the great Demons could be easily subdued and refined by him.

If not for his caution towards the Demon God, Lin Yuan would have long ago gone to the center of heaven and earth to hunt down those source Demons.

"After a thousand years of cultivation, I have reached the peak of the first realm of the Chaotic Yin-Yang Grand Dao. In a few hundred to a few thousand years, I can cross into the second realm," Lin Yuan's face broke into a smile.

The Chaotic Yin-Yang Grand Dao was his foundation, and even the terrifying 'Demon' refining techniques he had now were based on the power of the Chaotic Yin-Yang Grand Dao.

The stronger the Chaotic Yin-Yang Grand Dao, the greater the help it would provide to Lin Yuan, a fundamental help that nourished his body, spirit, mind, and will.

"And there is also the Thirteenth Realm inheritance, the 'Infinite Void'."

After a thousand years of cultivation and refinement, he had also completed half the progress of the fourth layer of this inheritance.

In the past thousand years, Lin Yuan had made sufficient advancements in all aspects, especially after comprehending the symbolic scriptures of the second source world, which had greatly helped him in understanding many great Dao and rules.

But the greatest harvest was the opening of his particle worlds.

By stabilizing each particle world with the Dao of Symbols, and with the 'inexhaustible' Demons of this source world, the number of particle worlds Lin Yuan had opened had already reached a hundred billion.

A hundred billion particle worlds.

Although there was still a distance to reach his ultimate goal of Endless Particle Worlds, these hundred billion particle worlds had already bestowed Lin Yuan with terrifying power.

Currently, Lin Yuan had three true body avatars, respectively in the Chaotic Void, the first source world, and the second source world.

But in terms of strength, the strongest true body avatar was the one in the second source world, without a doubt.

Even considering the advantages of the universe within the Chaotic Void body and the Blazing Sun Divine Body in the first source world, they were still far inferior to the current true body avatar in this source world.

"I am different from Yuanlong."

"The particle worlds Yuanlong opened were just ordinary particle worlds."

"But the particle worlds I have opened have all been reconstructed through the inheritance of the 'Infinite Void'."

Lin Yuan's expression was radiant. The Thirteenth Realm inheritance, the 'Infinite Void', was a legacy that could be cultivated on any world, including particle worlds.

It was precisely because of this that the mere hundred billion particle worlds were almost comparable to Yuanlong's entire Endless Particle Worlds.

"So far, the opening of the particle worlds has been proceeding smoothly, but I will still wait until I have fully mastered it before making a move," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

The true body avatar in the second source world was a pioneer, exploring and opening up the particle worlds.
