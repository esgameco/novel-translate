Of course, altering the past is something that even Time Lords don't dare to do.

They can, but they dare not.

Firstly, the backlash is too terrifying.

In the infinite dimensions and planes, each time point spawns countless future timelines. If the past of a certain time point were altered, the resulting butterfly effect would be enormous. Even Time Lords couldn't withstand such backlash.

Changing the past is fundamentally different from the resurrection of 12th level beings.

The latter's resurrection targets the 'life imprint' and has no impact on the past.

But changing the past affects the entire span of time from the past to the present.

But they also can't prove that the perfect time being doesn't exist.

Since neither can be proven,

It's best not to change the past.

If the perfect time being really exists, and the past, present, and future are all under its watch, your unauthorized intervention to change things would be equivalent to defying the perfect time being.

In that case, even the strongest Time Lord would perish.

Their potential also began to decline significantly at the same time.

Returning to the level before the great change in heaven and earth.

This caused countless cultivators to feel uneasy and fearful.

If it weren't for the fact that the current 'demon' calamity had been mostly suppressed by Lin Yuan,

And that great demons, let alone demon spirits, were extremely rare everywhere,

It wouldn't have caused any losses.

"When the Lord status shattered initially, it merged into the depths of the bloodlines of the beings in this world, causing a significant boost in bloodline potential."

"Now that I've recondensed the Lord status, it's equivalent to taking back this power from the depths of everyone's bloodlines, naturally everything will return to its original state."

Lin Yuan understood this point.

However, now that he had basically suppressed the demon calamity, and after breaking through to the 13th level, he would thoroughly eradicate the demon calamity.

So they hadn't really gained any advantage.

In fact, without Lin Yuan, even if their physical bodies and potential had increased significantly, what good would it do? The end result would most likely still be perishing under the endless demon calamity.
