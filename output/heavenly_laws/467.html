Chapter 467 - Transaction (Seeking Subscriptions)

"What reward will the Sect Master have me refine a batch of Nine Revolutions Elixir for?" Lin Yuan asked directly, without beating around the bush.

"Senior Daoist Cloud Seer, shouldn't you first ask how many Nine Revolutions Elixir pills you will be refining?" The Sect Master of the Azure Dragon Sect was slightly taken aback.

"I prefer to ask about the reward first, that way I'll have the motivation to refine the elixir," Lin Yuan replied with a smile.

In fact, as long as the Azure Dragon Sect could provide the materials for the Nine Revolutions Elixir, Lin Yuan could refine as many as they wanted.

"I see, our sect plans to request that Senior Daoist Cloud Seer refine twenty Nine Revolutions Elixir pills, and as for the reward, how about one million top-grade immortal crystals?" The Sect Master pondered for a while before speaking.

One million top-grade immortal crystals was an enormous sum, equivalent to ten times the wealth of an ordinary Jade Immortal.

Five hundred years ago, the resources Lin Yuan had obtained from Cloud Seer to 'revive' himself amounted to at most a hundred thousand top-grade immortal crystals.

"One million top-grade immortal crystals?" Lin Yuan looked at the Sect Master again, "It seems the Sect Master is not very sincere."

If this had been tens of thousands or hundreds of thousands of years ago, a reward of one million top-grade immortal crystals for refining twenty Nine Revolutions Elixir pills would have been a reasonable price.

But now?

The Immortal-Demon Great War was about to begin, and the value of all kinds of immortal elixirs and resources was skyrocketing, especially for strategic immortal elixirs like the Nine Revolutions Elixir.

"Senior Daoist Cloud Seer, how much do you want?" The Sect Master asked.

"Ten million top-grade immortal crystals," Lin Yuan said directly.

"Ten million?" The Sect Master's expression turned unsightly.

The Nine Dragons Commerce Association was a powerful organization, and they could afford to provide ten million top-grade immortal crystals, but for the reward of refining the Nine Revolutions Elixir?

"Or you could give me a piece of 'Immortal Source' from the Nine Dragons Commerce Association, that could also offset the reward for refining the Nine Revolutions Elixir," Lin Yuan suddenly said.

Immortal Source was said to come from the source of the Immortal Realm and had great benefits for comprehending the Dao.

However, only the Patriarchs of the Dao and their direct disciples could enter the source of the Immortal Realm, and Immortal Source was extremely rare in its outflow.

For the past five hundred years, Lin Yuan had tried both openly and secretly, but he had never obtained a piece of 'Immortal Source'.

Now, while helping the Nine Dragons Commerce Association refine the Nine Revolutions Elixir, he had the chance to see if he could acquire one.

The Nine Dragons Commerce Association had a significant influence in the Thirty-Three Immortal Realms, so they should have the means to obtain 'Immortal Source'.

"A piece of 'Immortal Source'? Senior Daoist Cloud Seer should know the value of 'Immortal Source'," the Sect Master said.

'Immortal Source' was too rare to be measured by immortal crystals.

"The Nine Revolutions Elixir I refine should be worth a piece of 'Immortal Source'," Lin Yuan said.

"I cannot give you 'Immortal Source'," the Sect Master stood up, "It seems we cannot cooperate with Senior Daoist Cloud Seer."

The Nine Dragons Commerce Association could provide 'Immortal Source', but the Sect Master felt that the reward for refining a batch of Nine Revolutions Elixir was not worth that price.

"No matter."

"We can cooperate in the future."

Lin Yuan watched as the Sect Master left.

In another immortal dwelling in Cangwu Immortal City, the Sect Master's figure appeared.

"How did the transaction with that Senior Daoist Cloud Seer go?" An old man with white hair, also a Jade Immortal, who had introduced the Cloud Seer to the Sect Master, asked.

"It didn't work out," the Sect Master frowned, "That Cloud Seer made an outrageous demand, asking me for ten million immortal crystals or a piece of 'Immortal Source'."

"Ten million immortal crystals?" The old man thought for a moment, "It is indeed a bit high, but you also understand the current situation, the Immortal-Demon Great War is approaching, and the reward for refining the Nine Revolutions Elixir has naturally increased."

"But ten million immortal crystals is still too high, with that kind of money, I could easily hire the top-tier elixir refiners," the Sect Master said.

"Before the last Immortal-Demon Great War, Cloud Seer's elixir refinement skills were just average, and it's only in the past few hundred years that he's been able to refine some decent immortal elixirs. But that's just a recent development, how can he compare to those top-tier elixir refiners?"

The quality of elixirs refined by elixir refiners had an element of randomness, depending on their state and luck.

It's possible that Cloud Seer's state has been good recently, allowing him to refine decent immortal elixirs, but those top-tier elixir refiners could maintain that level of quality consistently.

In the Sect Master's view, Cloud Seer's good state might not last, and he might even fail to succeed in refining the Nine Revolutions Elixir.

If an elixir refiner fails, the Nine Dragons Commerce Association would have to bear the loss of the materials, so the Sect Master naturally didn't want to pay too much for Cloud Seer.

"I originally thought this Cloud Seer, as a newcomer to the ranks of elixir refiners, would be more humble, but it turns out he's also very greedy. These elixir refiners, taking advantage of the impending Immortal-Demon Great War to arbitrarily raise their rewards, they all deserve to die," the Sect Master's gaze turned icy.

The old man with white hair couldn't help but find it a bit strange.

Wasn't the Nine Dragons Commerce Association currently doing the same thing, shamelessly profiting from the impending war?

Lin Yuan didn't mind too much that the Sect Master didn't accept the conditions.

As his reputation as an elixir refiner spread, there were often powers or powerful Jade Immortals who would request him to refine immortal elixirs, and he didn't really need to cooperate with the Nine Dragons Commerce Association.

"It seems 'Immortal Source' is really hard to obtain."

Lin Yuan sighed.

From the Sect Master's attitude, Lin Yuan could see that the value of a piece of 'Immortal Source' was far beyond even ten million immortal crystals.

At least when faced with a reward of ten million immortal crystals, the Sect Master showed some contemplation, but for 'Immortal Source'? Lin Yuan could tell the other party didn't even consider it.
