In the capital of the Lieyang Kingdom, the true body of Lin Yuanzhen went to the newly born sacred land, while the Taiyin Primordial Spirit and the Taiyang Primordial Spirit respectively guarded the other two top-tier sacred lands.

Therefore, the one left in the royal palace of the kingdom was just an incarnation, but even this incarnation was on par with the Peerless Supreme Celestial Venerable, given Lin Yuan's strength.

"Silver River, is it true that you swept through the Void Celestial Venerables and other top-tier Celestial Venerables in the sacred land?" A message came, from the Emperor of the Ancient Lieyang Kingdom.

Although the Emperor of the Ancient Lieyang Kingdom had already received accurate intelligence, and had even seen the relevant video footage, he still found it hard to believe, so he came to ask in person.

"My strength has just had a breakthrough," Lin Yuan did not hide it.

As a member of the core royal clan of the Ancient Lieyang Kingdom, his growing strength would only strengthen the foundation of the ancient kingdom.

As for causing any jealousy? With his current strength, Lin Yuan could completely ignore such things.

"Good, good, good." The Emperor of the Ancient Lieyang Kingdom was overjoyed. The Silver River Celestial Venerable's strength, which could crush the mightiest Celestial Venerables, was not just about the contention for a sacred land.

It would also have a profound impact on the situation of the Six Ancient Kingdoms in the long run.

With the existence of Lin Yuan, who far surpassed the mightiest Celestial Venerables, which of the other five ancient kingdoms, including the Great Fu Ancient Kingdom, would dare to engage in a high-intensity war with the Ancient Lieyang Kingdom? Without daring to start a war, they could only yield and retreat.

"Silver River Celestial Venerable, you have surprised me too much." The Emperor of the Ancient Lieyang Kingdom looked at Lin Yuan with deep emotion.

He did not ask Lin Yuan why he had such strong strength, as every powerful expert had their own secrets, and the ancient kingdoms had always not interfered with this.

Lin Yuan smiled without saying anything.

"Do you know why the group of the mightiest Celestial Venerables of the Great Fu Ancient Kingdom call you?" The Emperor of the Ancient Lieyang Kingdom couldn't help but say.

"What?" Lin Yuan was quite curious.

In the sacred land, he had not held back at all against the group of Peerless Celestial Venerables of the Great Fu Ancient Kingdom, and had even subdued the Void Celestial Venerable, the mightiest of them all.

"The Undefeatable Celestial Venerable." The Emperor of the Ancient Lieyang Kingdom couldn't help but be stunned.

The Undefeatable Celestial Venerable, what did that mean? That was absolute crushing and sweeping.

"The Undefeatable Celestial Venerable?" Lin Yuan understood.

The Chaotic Void had the concept of the Undefeatable Great Saint, where a certain peerless Great Saint swept away all other peerless Great Saints, and made them submit wholeheartedly, so he could claim the title of the Undefeatable Great Saint.

But in the Six Ancient Kingdoms? The so-called Undefeatable Great Saint was equivalent to the mightiest Celestial Venerables of the Six Ancient Kingdoms.

The Undefeatable Great Saint was unstoppable in the Chaotic Void, but in the Six Ancient Kingdoms, it was not a matter of being undefeatable, as there were still opponents, with the various ancestral founders nurturing a group of Celestial Venerables that were on par with the Undefeatable Great Saint.

And now, Lin Yuan's strength had made the group of the mightiest Celestial Venerables of the Six Ancient Kingdoms despair, so the title of the Undefeatable Celestial Venerable was well-deserved.

"Silver River, you have redefined the limit of Celestial Venerables." The Emperor of the Ancient Lieyang Kingdom said, not only the Great Fu Ancient Kingdom, but the other four ancient kingdoms had also engaged in heated discussions about the Silver River Celestial Venerable.

"The limit of Celestial Venerables?" Lin Yuan thought to himself: "In fact, I am still far from reaching the limit."

Currently, Lin Yuan had reached the perfect Chaotic Realm in terms of cultivation, but in other aspects, he still had a lot of room for improvement.

For example, the "Infinite Void" inheritance, Lin Yuan was still at the third layer, and he did not dare to expect the theoretical seventh layer, but he was quite confident in the sixth layer.

And the distance between the third layer and the sixth layer was the fourth layer and the fifth layer.

In addition, there was the evolving Chaotic Taiji Dao, which was currently at the first level, and according to the understanding of the many Celestial Venerables of the Six Ancient Kingdoms on the path of Chaotic evolution, the upper limit should be the third level.

Finally, there were the many thirteenth-tier inheritances in the treasure trove of the Ancient Lieyang Kingdom, which Lin Yuan could also cultivate to enhance his strength, etc.

Cultivating the thirteenth-tier inheritances required a lot of merit, but after this contention for the sacred land, Lin Yuan estimated that he would gain a huge amount of ancient kingdom merit.

"Whether it's cultivating the thirteenth-tier inheritances or improving the Chaotic evolution Dao, it's all for the sake of breaking through to the thirteenth tier." Lin Yuan was very clear about his goal.

Secret techniques and methods were just branches.

Cultivation realm was the root.

Cultivating the thirteenth-tier inheritances was also to experience the power of the thirteenth-tier realm.

In order to better break through the time loop.

"Silver River, what's the situation in the sacred land?" The Emperor of the Ancient Lieyang Kingdom asked.

"It's all under control." Lin Yuan nodded.

The many Peerless Celestial Venerables of the Great Fu Ancient Kingdom and the other four ancient kingdoms had either died or fled, and dared not stay in the sacred land any longer.

What Lin Yuan needed to do next was to refine the core celestial pillars one by one, and when the two-thousand-year contention for the sacred land ended, the owner of the sacred land would be officially determined.

"Blazing Flame Celestial Venerable told me about the situation in the sacred land. If it weren't for you, Silver River, the chances of my Ancient Lieyang Kingdom seizing this sacred land would not be great. Even if we could seize it, we would have been severely punished by Xuanyin and Daling."

The Emperor of the Ancient Lieyang Kingdom sighed deeply: "In this contention for the sacred land, Silver River, you have made great contributions. It can be said that without you, the situation would not be as it is now."

Without the Blazing Sun Ancient Country, those supreme Transcendent Saints from the other ancient countries would not be able to fight him on equal terms, and at most they would go seek help from their own ancestral founders.

Lin Yuan looked around, his pupils slightly contracting.
