Lin Yuan once again unleashed his physical divine abilities - the 'World'. There were no longer any restrictions.

There was no concept of duration.

As long as the source was sufficient, it could be maintained indefinitely.

To a certain extent, Lin Yuan's divine ability 'World' has now become more than just a divine ability.

It has integrated with the 'Infinite Void' and the 'Scorching Sun Parting the Heavens', forming a brand-new technique.

"There is actually a Great Sage? And one who is not weaker than us?"

The Black Obsidian Great Sage, the Dual Source Great Sage, and the Fierce Barbarian Great Sage immediately stopped their conflict and turned their targets towards Lin Yuan.

The three Great Sages attacked each other, while also keeping an eye on the surrounding situation. At their level of cultivation, they naturally did not want to be taken advantage of by other 'fishermen'.

Boom.

The Dual Source Great Sage struck first, with his gray-blue energy spreading and forcibly penetrating the scope of Lin Yuan's outer universe, disrupting the space-time.

"Hmm?"

Lin Yuan had already torn open a space-time passage leading into the Mysterious Yellow Universe, with one foot already stepping in, but he then withdrew it.

The Black Obsidian Great Sage did not want to take the risk of forcibly passing through, as that might result in him being the last to enter the Mysterious Yellow Universe. Lin Yuan was also unwilling to do so at the moment.

He held a map of the interior of the Mysterious Yellow Universe. As long as he could be among the first to enter, the harvest would far surpass the other Great Sages.

"You took the opportunity while we were fighting to make your move, that was a good plan."

The Black Obsidian Great Sage also activated his Black Obsidian Domain. Previously, 99% of the power of the Black Obsidian Domain was concentrated on the Dual Source Great Sage and the Fierce Barbarian Great Sage, which was why it was easily expelled by Lin Yuan's outer universe.

Now that the Black Obsidian Great Sage had regained his composure, the Black Obsidian Domain surged back, constantly colliding with Lin Yuan's outer universe, turning the edge areas into powder.

"Your Excellencies' strength is indeed formidable, I do not consider myself your match." Lin Yuan turned to look at the three Great Sages and smiled: "That's why I did this, please forgive me."

"Not your match?" The Fierce Barbarian Great Sage's eyes faintly revealed a murderous aura. Apart from other things, the fact that Lin Yuan easily passed through their territory and reached the position closest to the Mysterious Yellow Universe alone showed that his strength was not inferior to theirs.

Not their match? If it were not for the Dual Source Great Sage's timely reaction, Lin Yuan might have already slipped into the Mysterious Yellow Universe first.

Even so, the fact that Lin Yuan could withstand the gray-blue energy of the Dual Source Great Sage and the Black Obsidian Domain of the Black Obsidian Great Sage was proof of his own strength.

How could he not be their match? "Who are you, exactly? Why have I never heard of you before?"

The Dual Source Great Sage's eyes narrowed slightly.

From the time Lin Yuan erupted until now, although it was brief, they had already collided tens of thousands of times.

But what puzzled the Dual Source Great Sage was that he was actually so unfamiliar with Lin Yuan? It should be known that among the Chaos Lords, there might be cases where they had not heard of each other due to being too far apart in their void domains.

But for a Chaos Great Sage? The appearance of a Chaos Great Sage would quickly spread throughout the Chaotic Void, and the many Lords in various void domains would know of them.

For example, the Nine Phoenix Great Sage and the Shadow Demon Great Sage, although they were newly ascended Great Sages, the Dual Source Great Sage had already heard of them.

Moreover, Lin Yuan's strength surpassed the Nine Phoenix Great Sage and the Shadow Demon Great Sage by a large margin, and this powerful Great Sage, his reputation and cultivation path should have long since spread throughout the Chaotic Void, shouldn't it?

"The Tenth Great Sage?"

"There is actually another Great Sage hidden away?"

"Who is this Great Sage that dares to intervene in the battle between the Dual Source Great Sage and the others, and yet does not fall behind?"

The Chaos Lords hiding in the distance were trembling in their hearts. The collision between the four Great Sages, even a tiny backlash could severely injure or even annihilate a large group of Lords.

"The Dual Source Great Sage, the Black Obsidian Great Sage, they all have far-reaching reputations, but this newly appeared Great Sage, why has he never shown up before?"

"Could it be a Great Sage from the ancient, distant eras?"

The many Chaos Lords speculated. The reputation of a Great Sage would not be limited by space, but it could gradually be forgotten over the long passage of time.

Even a peerless Great Sage, if they disappeared for too long, ten thousand universe cycles? A hundred thousand universe cycles? A million universe cycles? They would still be forgotten, and the traces of their existence would be gradually erased.

For example, the former Emperor Xuan, who was undefeatable in his era, and even other supreme Great Sages had to avoid his might, but now, those who know of Emperor Xuan are very few, mostly beings like the All-Seeing Eye and the Altar Spirit.

"A Great Sage from the distant past eras? It seems this post-Heavenly Universe is quite remarkable, to have attracted such a powerful being."

Those Chaos Lords gazed at the revealed corner of the post-Heavenly Universe. At the Great Sage level, their lifespans were infinite, and as long as they did not court death, it would be very difficult for them to die.

But most Chaos Great Sages would eventually choose to break through to the Thirteenth Realm, for Great Sages, living too long did not have much meaning, only by reaching the Thirteenth Realm could they attain the ultimate freedom.

"The Dual Source Great Sage's Chaotic Evolution Dao Path energy can destroy the foundation of space-time, yet this newly appeared Great Sage can actually withstand such destructive power. So strong."

"Not only the Dual Source Great Sage, the Black Obsidian Great Sage's Black Obsidian Domain is also suppressing the opponent. Facing the combined might of the Dual Source Great Sage and the Black Obsidian Great Sage, yet still able to hold on without showing any signs of decline. Incredible."

The Celestial Prosperity Great Sage and a few other Great Sages were also secretly observing.

They had long since given up the idea of rushing into the Mysterious Yellow Universe first, as the Dual Source Great Sage and the others were not easy opponents to deal with. However, Lin Yuan forcibly inserted himself, revealing a strength that truly shocked them.

"How can this newly appeared Great Sage have a slight karmic connection with me?" The Shadow Demon Great Sage and the Nine Phoenix Great Sage were puzzled.

As Chaos Great Sages, even if they did nothing, their influence would still permeate through numerous void domains, and the newly ascended Chaos Realm Perfected Great Sages like the Shadow Demon and the Nine Phoenix were even more so.

The beings with karmic connections to them were countless.

In contrast, the ancient, peerless Great Sages who had attained Chaos Realm Perfection long ago, their karmic connections would be much simpler.

But even for a newly ascended Chaos Great Sage, for their karmic connections to be related to a Great Sage of Lin Yuan's strength, it still left the Shadow Demon Great Sage and the Nine Phoenix Great Sage feeling uneasy and uncertain.

"That person... is he a Great Sage? Or a Great Sage that can match the Dual Source Great Sage and the others?" Compared to the surrounding Chaos Lords who were in awe, the Spiritual Wind Lord and a few other Lords were filled with panic.

The other Lords, when facing a Great Sage of Lin Yuan's strength, at most would only feel fear. But they had previously interacted with Lin Yuan.

Even when Lin Yuan rejected their invitation, the Spiritual Wind Lord had subtly threatened him.

"Spiritual Wind, what should we do now?"

The two Chaos Lords following behind the Spiritual Wind Lord immediately transmitted a message. Chaos Lords could indeed survive in the presence of a Great Sage, but unless they had no other choice, Chaos Lords would absolutely not dare to offend a Chaos Great Sage.

Especially one as powerful as Lin Yuan, who could contend with the Dual Source Great Sage.

"We must join hands to ensure he doesn't enter the universe first." The Twin Source Sage spoke.

The Black Brilliance Sage securing the second spot was obviously more in line with his own interests than the third.

The Black Brilliance's domain rapidly expanded, covering Lin Yuan, but was blocked by his outer universe.

"The Twin Source Sage..."

Lin Yuan, who was at the forefront, seemed to sense something and glanced back.

He could feel that the Twin Source Sage had given up on attacking him, but this Chaos Sage's surrounding space-time was in chaos, and Lin Yuan couldn't discern anything for the moment.

Just as Lin Yuan was extremely close to the entrance of the Xuanhuang Universe.

"Didn't the Twin Source say he could hold him back?"

The Black Brilliance Sage's face looked unsightly. The Twin Source Sage had indeed held back Lin Yuan, but for how long? In such a short time, the Twin Source Sage had already rushed into the Xuanhuang Universe, and what about him?

Rumble.

"This?"

But the universe-suppressing force and the radiant power of the blazing sun were even stronger, and the Black Brilliance Sage clearly felt a bit strained.

"He didn't use his full strength just now?" The Black Brilliance Sage frowned, and when facing Lin Yuan alone, he immediately felt the pressure.

The Savage Sage at the back saw this and was delighted. Under Lin Yuan's slap, the Black Brilliance Sage had fallen behind him.
