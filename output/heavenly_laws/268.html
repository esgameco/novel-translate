"The power of the world?"

The supreme immortal kings like Kaitianxian Wang and Taishangxian Wang looked at each other. They had never heard of such a creative power before.

However, since the Martial Ancestor said so, this power must exist.

"Let's go."

"Let's take a look at the Eighth Heaven."

After saying that, Lin Yuan headed towards the entrance of the Eighth Heaven.

At this time, there were no supreme immortal kings from the prehistoric era guarding the entrance of the Eighth Heaven. It was empty, with wisps of illusory power floating around.

This was the tribulation from the Seventh Heaven to the Eighth Heaven, a natural phenomenon within the Nine Heavens. Even if destroyed, it would recover.

Just like the barrier between the heavens torn apart by Lin Yuan, it was now quickly recovering.

"Let's go directly from the entrance."

Lin Yuan sized it up.

Previously, when the Taiji Primordial Spirit was ambushed by Lingxu Xianwang, Lin Yuan's true body was in a hurry, so he charged through recklessly. But now there was no need.

The entrance was right in front, he could go straight through.

The tribulation from the Seventh Heaven to the Eighth Heaven was similar to a spiritual illusion, similar to the Heart Demon Tribulation and Mind Tribulation, but many times stronger than the latter.

However, the many supreme immortal kings present had experienced long years, and their mental strength, even without deliberate cultivation, was not weak. They could easily pass through.

Although Lin Yuan had experienced a relatively short time, his mental willpower index had been tempered by the supreme physique, plus the nourishment of the vast inner world.

He was only stronger, not weaker, than the supreme immortal kings present.

Whoosh.

Lin Yuan easily arrived at the Eighth Heaven.

The environment here was similar to the Seventh Heaven.

The only difference was a faint mysterious aura emanating from the Ninth Heaven above.

Beyond man there is man, beyond heaven there is heaven, and above the Nine Heavens there are celestial beings.

This had been the legend about the Ninth Heaven in the Spirit Realm since ancient times.

The so-called celestial beings, according to the speculation of many supreme immortal kings, might be an invincible powerhouse sitting in the Ninth Heaven overlooking the vast Spirit Realm.

The supreme immortal kings of the prehistoric era and the Three Thousand Dao States era longed to enter the Ninth Heaven in order to gain guidance from that invincible powerhouse.

"So this is the Eighth Heaven?"

"It is indeed extraordinary, different from the Seventh Heaven."

The supreme immortal kings like Kaitianxian Wang and Taishangxian Wang also followed.

This was their first time ascending to the Eighth Heaven. In previous battles to ascend to heaven, they were all beaten back by Lingxu Xianwang.

"The Ninth Heaven?"

Lin Yuan looked up towards the sky.

Compared to the barriers of the previous eight heavens, the barrier of the Ninth Heaven faintly emitted a pale golden color. Even Lin Yuan was not confident in breaking it.

Or rather, he was confident in tearing it apart, but it would take a lot of time, because the barriers between the Nine Heavens would quickly recover once damaged.

Lin Yuan's tearing speed had to be much faster than the recovery speed of the barrier.

"Let's go to the entrance to the Ninth Heaven."

Lin Yuan made up his mind. After searching the soul of Lingxu Xianwang, he was very familiar with the Eighth Heaven and directly flew towards the entrance leading to the Ninth Heaven.

The entrance to the Ninth Heaven.

Dozens of figures stood there.

These dozens of figures were the most powerful supreme immortal kings of the prehistoric era. Each of them had cultivated at least one kind of high-level creative power to the third level.

Most of them had mastered more than two kinds of high-level creative powers at the third level. Lingxu Xianwang had suppressed the supreme immortal kings of the Three Thousand Dao States era like Kaitianxian Wang to the point of suffocation.

But among this group of supreme immortal kings, they basically belonged to the bottom.

The prehistoric era had experienced a complete era, plus the long years experienced by the current Three Thousand Dao States. The number of supreme immortal kings was far more than the immature Three Thousand Dao States era, and they were also much stronger.

Whoosh whoosh whoosh.

Lin Yuan's figure appeared not far away.

The supreme immortal kings like Kaitianxian Wang and Taishangxian Wang also followed behind Lin Yuan.

"So many powerhouses?"

Kaitianxian Wang felt a bit numb. Only now did they realize how ridiculous their battle to ascend to heaven was.

The many supreme immortal kings standing at the entrance of the Ninth Heaven, if a few of them came out, they could suppress their more than twenty supreme immortal kings.

Taishangxian Wang was also nervous at this moment. If Lin Yuan wasn't standing in front of them, they might have had the idea to flee.

The supreme immortal king leading the many prehistoric supreme immortal kings had a tall figure, his aura was chaotically mixed, and he stood at the forefront of all the immortal kings, clearly the leader among the strong supreme immortal kings of the prehistoric era.

"Chaos Immortal King."

Lin Yuan's gaze was calm.

Having searched the soul of Lingxu Xianwang, Lin Yuan naturally knew that Chaos Immortal King was the strongest immortal king of the prehistoric era.

Chaos Immortal King turned around and looked at Lin Yuan solemnly, "Lingxu has already told me."

"Martial Ancestor, your strength far surpasses mine."

"Whatever Martial Ancestor wants, just say it directly, I will agree to everything."

Chaos Immortal King said frankly, as if taking the initiative to yield was not an embarrassing thing.

In fact, it was indeed so. Lingxu Xianwang had already shown the scene that happened in the Seventh Heaven to Chaos Immortal King and other supreme immortal kings.

Including Chaos Immortal King, all the supreme immortal kings of the prehistoric era admitted that they could not do what Martial Ancestor did.

Even in Chaos Immortal King's many deductions, even if all their supreme immortal kings joined forces, facing Martial Ancestor, they could only struggle to survive for a while before being defeated in the end.

Since he already knew the outcome, Chaos Immortal King naturally would not resist and took the initiative to admit defeat.

"Oh?"

Lin Yuan took an extra look at Chaos Immortal King.

This was good, saving him some trouble.

"Split out your soul and let me search it." Lin Yuan said directly.

He didn't choose to search directly, which could be considered a response to Chaos Immortal King's active cooperation.

There was no significant difference between splitting out the soul to be searched by Lin Yuan and being directly suppressed by Lin Yuan before searching the soul, but it was much better in terms of face.

"No problem."

Chaos Immortal King split out a part of his soul without hesitation.

This part of the soul contained all his memories. Lin Yuan only needed to search this soul to know everything he wanted to know.

Buzz.

Lin Yuan took the partial soul of Chaos Immortal King.

The soul search was completed in a few breaths.

"So that's how it is."

Lin Yuan felt a bit enlightened.

In Chaos Immortal King's memory, there was the reason for the end of the prehistoric era.

At the end of the prehistoric era, a treasure fell from the Ninth Heaven.

That great battle caused the Spirit Realm to seemingly fall into doomsday, with immortal kings at the Tribulation Transcendence stage falling in large numbers.

In the end, Chaos Immortal King, as the victor, obtained that treasure and entered the Eighth Heaven with his many subordinates.

After the prehistoric era.

The Spirit Realm recovered through long years and gradually entered the next era.
