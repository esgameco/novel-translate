In the far distance of space-time, three figures stood silently.

Existences capable of reaching the space-time nine-layer realm of comprehension at the chaotic great saint level were extremely rare and precious, even more so than peerless great saints.

Such chaotic great saints, even if they were still not the match of peerless great saints, could easily retreat unharmed.

"Yes," the last peerless great saint nodded.

Originally, the three peerless great saints had planned to find the Galactic Sage and inquire about the events that had occurred in the Xuanhuang Universe.

But now, it seemed unnecessary.

Since they could not harm the other party, even if they went to inquire, they would certainly not get the truth.

"Forget it," the leading peerless great saint carefully thought it over and made a decision in his heart.

If the Galactic Sage was just an ordinary chaotic great saint, they could naturally manipulate him.

But now? The Galactic Sage had the confidence to resist them, and the three peerless great saints did not want to make an enemy out of him blindly before they knew what opportunities the Xuan Emperor had left him.

These three peerless great saints only knew that Lin Yuan's space-time comprehension had reached the nine-layer realm, but they were unaware of Lin Yuan's true combat power, which was considered strong even among peerless great saints.

Different starting points of the internal universe would naturally result in different levels of improvement after cultivating the third layer of the 'Infinite Void'.

The difference in the foundation and profundity of the internal worlds would lead to different levels of improvement after breaking through to the chaotic realm. It was clear that the true body and avatar of the chaotic void would benefit more in this aspect compared to the Source World's true body and avatar.

Not only the 'Infinite Void' and breaking through to the chaotic realm, but also the evolution of the Chaotic Taiji Force, could all bring Lin Yuan significant improvements.
