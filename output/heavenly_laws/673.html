"This Milky Way."

At the same time, his perception of the timelines was more acute, vaguely aware that the future of the Milky Way Sovereign was extremely terrifying, beyond what he could observe and peek into.

"This timeline"

The Patriarch of the Great Fu gathered his thoughts, and a single timeline did not represent anything.

Perhaps it was just his bad luck that he happened to see the timeline with the highest potential of the Milky Way Sovereign.

A single timeline cannot replace true potential. Since the opening of the Six Ancient Nations, there have been so many Sovereigns who have broken through to the 13th Realm, and theoretically each Sovereign has the potential to become a Patriarch.

But after such a long period of time, were there only the six of them who became Patriarchs? The future is full of too many variables, becoming a Patriarch in just a single timeline? The probability is too low, too low.

Soon,

The Patriarch of the Great Fu quickly went through hundreds, thousands, and tens of thousands of timelines.

"How is this possible?"

The Patriarch of the Great Fu found it unbelievable that in hundreds, thousands, and tens of thousands of timelines, the Milky Way Sovereign had all successfully crossed into the 13th Realm, which was very unreasonable.

In the future, at any given moment, countless timelines will be born, and each timeline will branch out and continue to extend into countless more timelines. Even in the chess game between the lives of time, they cannot guarantee that a certain event will happen 100%.

There will be various factors affecting the outcome, as well as the influence of other time lives.

But this Milky Way Sovereign? At least in the many timelines the Patriarch of the Great Fu saw, the other party smoothly crossed into the 13th Realm, it was just a matter of time, in simple terms, the Milky Way Sovereign crossing into the 13th Realm was unstoppable.

At least in the timelines the Patriarch of the Great Fu saw, it could not be prevented.

"Is it his innate talent? Or external forces?" The Patriarch of the Great Fu's thoughts expanded, leaning more towards external forces.

How could one rely solely on innate talent to achieve the certainty of crossing into the 13th Realm? The most powerful talent the Patriarch of the Great Fu had seen was that of the 'Abyss Dragon' born at the beginning of the Source World.

This 'Abyss Dragon' indeed had the possibility of crossing into the 13th Realm, but after the Patriarch of the Great Fu crossed into the 13th Realm, the timelines of the 'Abyss Dragon' crossing into the 13th Realm were basically cut off.

Because the Patriarch of the Great Fu would definitely act against the 'Abyss Dragon', the enmity between them could not be reconciled, it was determined since the birth of heaven and earth. If the 'Abyss Dragon' crossed into the 13th Realm earlier, the Patriarch of the Great Fu would certainly die.

From this, it can be seen that even with the strongest talent, it cannot be guaranteed to cross into the 13th Realm, because it will be interfered and affected by external forces, especially the suppression of a time life, which is a disaster for beings below the 13th Realm.

A time life that sees through countless timelines can block all your paths, and your every choice is basically seen through by the time life.

It's just that when facing Lin Yuan,

The Patriarch of the Great Fu felt powerless.

Because he could not stop him.

Forcibly obstructing would only delay the other party's crossing into the 13th Realm.

And the price of doing so would be that after the Milky Way Sovereign crossed into the 13th Realm, he would retaliate against him. At that time, the Patriarch of the Great Fu would suffer greatly, even if he escaped this dimension, he could not avoid the other party's pursuit.

"Perhaps it's because my strength is not strong enough, I haven't seen enough timelines, and I can't observe the timelines that can deal with this Milky Way. But it doesn't matter anymore, this Milky Way must not be provoked."

The Patriarch of the Great Fu thought to himself, with the many timelines of the Milky Way Sovereign's development, he absolutely cannot be an enemy.

Just as the Patriarch of the Great Fu was pondering,

The top peak Sovereigns and the strongest Sovereigns of the Great Fu Ancient Nation below were extremely uncomfortable, the Patriarch's silent and unspoken manner clearly showed that he was extremely disappointed with them, his descendants, and did not even want to say more.

Well, the Patriarch had painstakingly cultivated them, but in the end they were still swept away and crushed by the Milky Way Sovereign of the Radiant Sun Ancient Nation.

After all, the Patriarch of the Radiant Sun basically let his descendants develop on their own without much interference, yet they were still like this, they were ashamed in front of the Patriarch.

"Patriarch."

The Sovereign of the Great Void spoke with a firm gaze and a solemn tone: "I will cultivate the Forbidden Supreme Inheritance, that Milky Way Sovereign, I am not a match for him now, but after cultivating the Forbidden Supreme Inheritance, my strength will greatly increase, and I will go challenge the Milky Way Sovereign again, so that our Ancient Nation will not suffer humiliation again."

The so-called Forbidden Supreme Inheritance is similar to the "Heart of the Radiant Sun" inheritance of the Radiant Sun Ancient Nation, which allows a certain part of oneself to leap towards the 13th Realm, thereby achieving a rapid increase in strength.

But the reason why this kind of inheritance is called the "Forbidden Supreme Inheritance" is that the hidden dangers of cultivation are too great. The "Heart of the Radiant Sun" requires the sacrifice of a large part of one's own rationality, and the Forbidden Supreme Inheritances of other Ancient Nations are the same, or even the price is greater.

It is precisely because of this that almost no peak Sovereigns are willing to cultivate the Forbidden Inheritances, even though the enhancement of strength by the Forbidden Supreme Inheritance far exceeds the Supreme Inheritance, no peak Sovereign is willing to pay such a huge price to enhance their strength.

By the time they reach the peak Sovereign realm, they have basically stood at the top of their respective Ancient Nations, with the hope of breaking through to the Patriarch realm, and they absolutely do not need to pay such a huge price to enhance their strength.

Especially some Forbidden Supreme Inheritances, once they start cultivating, the hidden dangers cannot be removed, and it will affect the breakthrough to that next step.

But now, faced with the Patriarch's disappointment and the shame of the Ancient Nation, Sovereign of the Great Void felt that he should do something.

Although this time he was crushed by the Milky Way Sovereign in the treasure land, it was not his problem at all, and if the other strongest Sovereigns of the Ancient Nation were in this position, they would have the same fate and the same outcome.

But the Sovereign of the Great Void still felt uneasy, even if he had to pay a price, he wanted to make the Patriarch feel better.

"Patriarch, I will also cultivate the Forbidden Supreme Inheritance, I just don't believe that with the foundation of our Great Fu Ancient Nation, we still can't match a mere Milky Way Sovereign."

The strongest Sovereigns all had doubts in their hearts about whether they could withstand the Milky Way Sovereign after cultivating the Forbidden Supreme Inheritance, but at least the strength gap would be narrowed, if one couldn't do it, then two, if two couldn't do it, then three.

The Great Fu Ancient Nation had a solid foundation, even if they had to pile up, they would bury the Milky Way Sovereign.

The heroic spirit of the other strongest Sovereigns was also ignited, the Great Fu Ancient Nation was the earliest Ancient Nation, and the Patriarch was the first time life, so the starting point and beginning were the highest, there was no reason they would lose to the Milky Way Sovereign.

The Patriarch of the Great Fu looked down at the indignant Sovereigns who were eager to challenge Lin Yuan again, the corners of his mouth twitching slightly.

"Actually, there's no need." The Patriarch of the Great Fu said: "That Milky Way Sovereign's strength is indeed far beyond you, it's better to just let him be, it's not a disgrace."

In the timelines the Patriarch of the Great Fu observed, even he himself found it difficult to resist the Milky Way Sovereign after crossing into the 13th Realm.
