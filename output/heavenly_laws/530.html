"Space-time."

"The use of Sun Seeds is not as simple as just enhancing rule comprehension," Lin Yuan thought to himself, his expression turning slightly serious.

When condensing 3,333 Sun Seeds, all the Sun Seeds in his body seemed to form a cycle, causing Lin Yuan's physical body to undergo a certain transformation.

This transformation was not simply a strengthening or enhancement, but leaned more towards a transformation in terms of rules.

"3,333 Sun Seeds is some kind of major threshold!"

Lin Yuan concluded. The vast majority of life forms in the Qingfen Domain knew about the thresholds for Sun Seeds - one seed, ten seeds, a hundred seeds... But whether it was ten seeds or a hundred seeds, or even a thousand seeds, it only increased the degree of comprehension of rules.

But with 3,333 Sun Seeds, Lin Yuan's physical body was faintly transforming towards the direction of ultimate rules.

Lin Yuan did not know what the end point of this transformation was, but it was certainly not a bad thing.

"Setting aside the physical transformation for now, my current comprehension of space-time fusion rules has entered the peak of the seventh level. It will take at most one year to cross over."

Lin Yuan thought to himself. Originally, without the aid of Sun Seeds, it would have taken tens of thousands of years to have a chance of reaching the peak of the seventh level.

After condensing ten seeds, it could be achieved in a thousand years.

As for now? With the boost of 3,700 Sun Seeds, Lin Yuan was very certain he could reach the peak of the seventh level of space-time in at most one year.

He even had hope of touching the eighth level of space-time in a short time.

"It's just that condensing new Sun Seeds now is clearly much more difficult than at the beginning," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

Starting from the 3,334th Sun Seed, the difficulty increased sharply with each new seed Lin Yuan condensed.

Before, Lin Yuan could condense a Sun Seed with each breath, but now it took ten breaths, or even a hundred breaths to do so.

"The next major threshold for Sun Seeds should be 33,333 seeds. I wonder what ability that will bring."

Lin Yuan speculated in his mind. From the current situation, 3,333 Sun Seeds could form a small cycle.

And 33,333 Sun Seeds would be able to form a large cycle.

The condensation of Sun Seeds mainly tested one's comprehension ability. Each Sun Seed was extremely complex, and to condense one required first fully comprehending it.

"However, the 'Sun Essence Cultivation Method' can only be practiced in this source world."

Lin Yuan thought to himself.

His avatar in the chaos void had also tried to condense Sun Seeds.

But the result was failure without suspense.

Actually it wasn't really a failure, as Sun Seeds were eventually condensed, but they were very different from the Sun Seeds he was condensing now, and did not have the effect of enhancing rule comprehension.

"Let's condense more Sun Seeds first before thinking further."

Lin Yuan reined in his thoughts. Over these past few months, he had been re-cultivating his martial arts while condensing Sun Seeds, and also comprehending the unique rule fluctuations of this source world.

The rule fluctuations of this source world were different from those of the chaos void. Re-integrating martial arts required some thought, but it wasn't a big problem.

It was equivalent to an alternative form of longevity and immortality.

Simply put, the time and space in Lin Yuan's current inner universe were extremely rigorous, even somewhat harsh.

The difference between an ordinary 10 million light year inner universe and Lin Yuan's inner universe was like the difference between tofu and diamond - one would crumble at a touch.

That was the inner universe of a Chaos Lord! Vast and mighty, on par with the innate universes born from the chaos void.
