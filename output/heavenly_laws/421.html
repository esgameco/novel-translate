The Son of the Void stood towering, silently gazing towards the direction of the Azure World.

Although his own power had now largely recovered, and he could mobilize the entire Void Secret Realm, with just a thought he could suppress everything in the boundless Void if he wished.

Yet the Son of the Void remained focused, observing intently the source of that spatiotemporal fluctuation, his mind filled with shock and uncertainty.

Behind him, the many perfected Demon Sovereigns were similarly transfixed. Among them, the most ancient ones had existed for eons, even predating the establishment of the Void Secret Realm itself.

This spatiotemporal fluctuation seemed to trigger some recollection in the most ancient Demon Sovereign, a trace of dread appearing on his face.

Over the countless eons since the birth of the boundless Void, innumerable Demon Sovereigns had dominated their domains. But even a perfected Ninth Stage Demon Sovereign could not fundamentally shake the boundless Void.

In all of history, there had only been three occurrences that impacted the space-time of the entire boundless Void to this degree.

The first was when the boundless Void gave birth to its first Tenth Stage Demon, whose existence merged with the rules of the boundless Void in a majestic manner.

The second was when that Tenth Stage Demon established the Void Secret Realm - the birth of the Void Secret Realm fundamentally altered the boundless Void in a profound way.

The third was when the Son of the Void became the new master of the Void Secret Realm. The explosive release of the Void Secret Realm's power similarly triggered a spatiotemporal fluctuation enveloping the boundless Void.

And now, this was the fourth time.

The spatiotemporal fluctuation originating from the direction of the Azure World seemed poised to sweep across the entire boundless Void.

These four occurrences that caused spatiotemporal fluctuations covering the whole boundless Void shared one commonality - they were all related to the Tenth Stage.

In the third case, although the Son of the Void himself had not yet reached the Tenth Stage, the Void Secret Realm he now mastered was painstakingly established by a Tenth Stage existence.

"This spatiotemporal fluctuation..."

"Is it the Tenth Stage? Has my boundless Void given birth to another Tenth Stage existence?"

"Indeed, it feels just like when the founding master of the Void Secret Realm crossed into the Tenth Stage, the impact on space-time was similar."

The perfected Demon Sovereigns were stunned. Originally they thought the second existence in the boundless Void to reach the Tenth Stage would be the Son of the Void. After all, as master of the Void Secret Realm painstakingly established by the previous Tenth Stage Demon, the Son of the Void was best positioned to comprehend its deepest undulations and cross into the Tenth Stage.

But now? The Son of the Void stood right before them.

So the one who crossed into the Tenth Stage was someone else?

"The Azure World...could it be the Martial Ancestor?"

The Heaven's Wisdom Sovereign had visited the Azure World before and could estimate that the source of the spatiotemporal fluctuation was somewhere around there. And the Azure World was where the Martial Ancestor resided. For such an event impacting the entire boundless Void to originate from the Martial Ancestor's abode, the Heaven's Wisdom Sovereign could hardly think of any other possibility.

"The Martial Ancestor? The Tenth Stage?"

The Heaven's Wisdom Sovereign was apprehensive and uneasy. Not long ago, he had rallied fellow Demons to besiege the Lord of Yin, even brazenly probing into the Azure World to spy on the Martial Ancestor's lair. Now it seemed that was simply courting death.

"Great Son of the Void, what...what should we do now?"

The Eye of Divinity Sovereign mustered his courage to ask tentatively.

He had almost devoured an entire world earlier, only stopping due to this spatiotemporal fluctuation. One could imagine his shock.

His words drew the gazes of all the other perfected Demon Sovereigns towards the Son of the Void.

"What to do?"

The Son of the Void fell silent. Compared to the other Demons who could only sense the source of the spatiotemporal fluctuation, as master of the Void Secret Realm, the Son of the Void's perception was far more acute.

Aided by the power of the Void Secret Realm, the Son of the Void's gaze had already fallen upon the Azure World. Were it not for the dense spatiotemporal force permeating the Azure World's interior that gave him pause, the Son of the Void might have forcibly probed it already.

"Let's go take a look."

The Son of the Void said slowly. Regardless of whether the Martial Ancestor had crossed into the Tenth Stage, he would pay a visit.

If the Martial Ancestor truly became a Tenth Stage existence, the Son of the Void would have to lead the Demon Sovereigns to show their submission.

As master of the Void Secret Realm, the Son of the Void understood all too well the terror of a Tenth Stage existence. Although aided by the Void Secret Realm he could manifest the combat prowess of the Tenth Stage, the Son of the Void had no confidence in being able to contend against a true Tenth Stage existence.

Since he could not defy it, he would submit willingly.

And if the Martial Ancestor had not crossed into the Tenth Stage, the Son of the Void had no intention of antagonizing him either. This visit was to ease relations with the Martial Ancestor.

Even if this spatiotemporal fluctuation was not a sign of breaking into the Tenth Stage, it at least signified the Martial Ancestor commanding a treasure comparable to the Void Secret Realm, a Tenth Stage legacy.

Much like how the Son of the Void mastered the Void Secret Realm.

In such a scenario, even if the Son of the Void went all-out, he would be powerless against the Martial Ancestor. It was better to turn swords into jade and silk.

At the Son of the Void's level, all he truly sought in the vast boundless Void was to cross into the Tenth Stage - everything else was an illusion.

"Let's go."

The Son of the Void glanced at the Demon Sovereigns, directly tapping into the Void Secret Realm's power to teleport over vast distances towards the Azure World.

In the depths of the Azure World's Martial Palace, dense spatiotemporal fluctuations permeated the air as Lin Yuan sat cross-legged, the raging spatial forces and abstruse temporal forces obediently swirling before him like docile kittens.

"So this is the Six Profundities Realm of the Space-Time Fusion Rules?"

A smile appeared on Lin Yuan's face. In his comprehension of the Space-Time Fusion Rules, he had now thoroughly entered the Six Profundities Realm, the paramount realm.

Of course, this was only his comprehension of space-time, barely reaching the paramount realm. As for his actual strength, the gap between him and a paramount expert was still as vast as the gulf between heaven and earth.

If one viewed the comprehension of the Space-Time Fusion Rules as a skill, then in addition to skill level, one's innate power was also a factor impacting strength.

At the same skill level, if one side's power was a hundred pounds while the other's was a hundred tons, in battle the outcome would be a foregone conclusion of utter domination. The Six Profundities Realm of the Space-Time Fusion Rules was merely the entry point for paramount experts in this domain.

Beyond this fusion of rules, paramount experts grasped myriad other fused rules, even transforming certain fundamental rules into their own personal Great Daos.

"The Six Profundities Realm of the Space-Time Fusion Rules signifies a qualitative change, involving transcending the shackles of the temporal river's constraints."

Lin Yuan carefully observed the vast space-time, numerous insights welling up.

Why was the Six Profundities Realm of Space-Time Fusion hailed as the paramount realm? It was because at this profound level of comprehension, one could begin to condense a miniature temporal river.

Lin Yuan had no doubt that if he wished, he now possessed the ability to assault the Terminus.

The reason the myriad realms publicly acknowledged that only at the Eleventh Stage's apex could one assault the Terminus was simply because at that level, a lifeform's various aspects barely met the threshold to make such an attempt.

But for Lin Yuan?

He could indeed assault the Terminus now, but whether he could succeed was another matter. At the very least, even currently if Lin Yuan directly assaulted the Terminus, his chances of eventual success would be greater than legendary-tier lifeforms like the Four Divine Kings. His comprehension of the Six Profundities of the Space-Time Fusion Rules carried immense weight in spiritual willpower.
