Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Hazy and indistinct.

Dazzling and intertwined.

Lin Yuan carefully observed and felt.

In a flash, he found himself in a world engulfed in endless darkness.

There was not a single ray of light here, only cold and deathly silence.

At this moment.

Somewhere in the world.

A flame suddenly ignited.

The appearance of the flame broke the ancient silence.

It gave this world the concept of light for the first time.

The concept of heat.

The flame burned, and the light and heat spread.

A brand-new world was opened up.

Finally, the flame transformed into a blazing sun, eternally suspended in the vast sky.

Wherever the light shone, it became a domain.

Lin Yuan sat cross-legged in silence, but his heart could not calm down for a long time.

Even though he had stopped contemplating the divine imprint, the scenes he had just witnessed kept flashing in his mind.

Who would have thought that the physical divine technique he had awakened this time had such a far-reaching intent? On the surface, it was just the appearance of a blazing sun in the endless darkness.

But in reality, with the light of this blazing sun spreading and covering, a complete world was opened up.

Opening up a world? For the current Lin Yuan, it was not a difficult task, but the realm of the world opened up by that blazing sun was so high that it was probably based on the 'template' of the source world he was in.

Opening up the source world? The source world is so vast and mysterious, even more so than the chaotic void. To open up such a world is almost an impossible task.

"The 13th-tier time life is truly terrifying," Lin Yuan thought, his mind focused.

The creator of 'Infinite Void', that 13th-tier time life, wanted to use this heritage to nurture a universe within its body that could rival the chaotic void.

And the physical divine technique that Lin Yuan had awakened this time originated from the Progenitor of the Blazing Sun, who was also a 13th-tier time life, and its ultimate goal was to open up a vast world like the source world.

"The 13th-tier time life has basically occupied a dimensional space, and their perspective is no longer within the dimension they are in, but rather they are eager to explore and occupy more dimensions," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

Dimension is a general concept, and it encompasses more than the chaotic void or the source world.

No matter whether it's the chaotic void or the source world, they are all part of a certain dimensional space.

"Now, I am still too far from the 13th-tier time life," Lin Yuan shook his head slightly, for the difference between the 12th-tier and the 13th-tier is like the difference between a person in the painting and a person outside the painting.

"This physical divine technique..." Lin Yuan continued to observe the divine imprint and pondered for a moment, "let's call it 'Blazing Sun Opening the Heavens'."

In fact, whether it was the first physical divine technique or the current seventh one, they didn't have specific names. Lin Yuan named them based on the effects and functions of the divine techniques.

The seventh physical divine technique, in essence, is a blazing sun hanging high, and wherever the light shines, it becomes a domain, that is, the world opened up by the blazing sun.

So Lin Yuan named it 'Blazing Sun Opening the Heavens'.

"Blazing Sun," Lin Yuan's mind moved, and a flame appeared in front of him.

This flame emanated a mysterious aura, and the surrounding space began to change and assimilate rapidly under its reflection.

If Lin Yuan were to continuously infuse energy into this flame to sustain its burning and expansion, theoretically, a second eternal blazing sun would be formed.

Illuminating the vast ancient kingdom.

Of course, this is just a theory. Firstly, Lin Yuan doesn't have enough energy to support it. Secondly, even if he did, letting this flame evolve would inevitably attract the attention of the true eternal blazing sun in the sky.

"Opening the Heavens?" Lin Yuan looked at the slowly burning flame, "With my current realm and strength, it's impossible for me to open up a world similar to the source world, but now, by relying on the awakened physical divine technique, I've indirectly achieved it?"

To open up the heavens and the earth, one needs to replace the rules and Dao of the heavens and the earth with one's own.

Whether it's the rules and Dao of the chaotic void or the rules and Dao of this source world, they are boundless and endless. With Lin Yuan's current understanding of the rules and Dao, opening up such a heaven and earth would be nothing but a pipe dream.

"This is the power of divine techniques," Lin Yuan said with a pensive expression. He had not yet mastered or fully comprehended it, but he could still use and unleash it.

In simple terms, it's similar to the Lin Yuan at the first tier, who could use techniques that required a complete understanding of the spatial rules.

Of course, these forcibly unleashed techniques, compared to the true understanding of the spatial rules, are extremely rigid and inflexible, only able to use the same move over and over again.

But there's no doubt that they can still overwhelm opponents at the same level.

"With my current strength, if I fully activate this divine technique, I can probably maintain the 'Blazing Sun' for about three seconds," Lin Yuan thought.

"But within these three seconds, even a Supremacy-level expert would not be able to withstand it, and even a Supremacy peak expert would die. Probably only a Celestial Venerable could survive."

The divine technique 'Blazing Sun Opening the Heavens' is clearly equivalent to a 13th-tier secret art.

However, due to the limitations of Lin Yuan's strength and foundation, he cannot fully unleash the power of this secret art.

Even so, he can still kill Supremacy-level peak life forms with his Mastery-level life force.

From Mastery to Supremacy, it is a world-shaking difference. Even the core imperial families of the great ancient kingdoms can only reach the Mastery realm and contend with the Supremacy.

As for killing a Supremacy, or even a Supremacy peak?

Even for Lin Yuan, with his boundless foundation, he only dared to think he had the strength to kill a Supremacy peak after reaching the pinnacle of the Mastery realm and cultivating many techniques like the 'Infinite Void' to the limit.

But now? Lin Yuan's actual combat strength is only approaching the Supremacy peak.

"With the power of this physical divine technique, as long as I step into the Chaotic realm, basically no Supremacy, Celestial Venerable can be my opponent," Lin Yuan thought.

If he were in the chaotic void, even a Great Saint would be easily swept away by Lin Yuan.

The Supremacy in this source world is equivalent to the Venerable in the chaotic void, and the Celestial Venerable is equivalent to the Great Saint.

However, this divine technique also has its flaws. The consumption is too great, and Lin Yuan cannot control it perfectly. In a one-on-one or one-against-a-few situation, Lin Yuan would have no worries, but if they come at him one after another, taking advantage of the interval when Lin Yuan activates this divine technique, he would be a bit troubled.

"Regardless, this divine technique is definitely the most powerful trump card I currently possess. Even the so-called pure killing power divine technique 'Tear the Heavens' cannot compare to it," Lin Yuan said with a spirited expression.

The divine technique 'Tear the Heavens' only has that one 'tear', while 'Blazing Sun Opening the Heavens' can be sustained for a period of time, essentially a greatly enhanced version of the divine technique 'World'.

Of course, the profundity involved in the 'Blazing Sun Opening the Heavens' divine technique far exceeds that of the 'World' divine technique.

So far, Lin Yuan has only been thinking about the power of the 'Blazing Sun Opening the Heavens' divine technique, but in fact, this divine technique is more inclined towards 'creation' and 'opening up'.

As for destruction? It's just one aspect of it.

[Name: Yu Yan (Lin Yuan)]

[Identity: Gatekeeper of the Myriad Realms]

[Bound Talent: Inverted Heavenly Comprehension]

[Divine Technique: Bloodline Rebirth]

[Divine Technique: Move Mountains]

[Divine Technique: Strengthening]

[Divine Technique: Elemental Body]

[Divine Technique: World]

[Divine Technique: Tear the Heavens]

[Divine Technique: Blazing Sun Opening the Heavens]

[Current State: Consciousness Descended]

[Remaining Dwelling Time: Unlimited]

Lin Yuan glanced at the newly 'updated' seventh divine technique in the lower left of his vision.

"The current priority is to comprehend this divine imprint," Lin Yuan calmed his mind. Only by comprehending this divine imprint could he bring it back to the chaotic void, for it to be used by his various true bodies, divine souls, and incarnations.

Of course, by 'comprehending' here, Lin Yuan did not mean comprehending the essence of the 'Blazing Sun Opening the Heavens', which was too difficult for him to achieve.

Rather, it was simply to comprehend the divine imprint containing this divine technique.

It's like a high-tech product, as long as you can perfectly replicate each component inside, you can reassemble it.

As for the function and effect of each component, you can research it slowly later.

"Before I comprehend it, I can't die," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

The current physical body is the carrier of the 'Blazing Sun Opening the Heavens' divine technique. If this body were to die, the divine technique imprint would dissipate, and before Lin Yuan comprehended it, it would be equivalent to permanently losing this divine technique.

This is a cost that Lin Yuan cannot accept.

As for choosing to transmigrate to a life that is not completely the same and then choosing to transmigrate again, Lin Yuan is not sure if he can continue to awaken the 'Blazing Sun Opening the Heavens' divine technique.

Moreover, choosing to transmigrate and descend again means that Lin Yuan has to start over.

All the time and effort he had previously invested would be wasted.

"As long as I am cautious with my current strength..."

Some should also be very difficult to kill." Lin Yuan was quite confident. Becoming the emperor certainly brought many powers, but it would also occupy them with numerous trivial matters.

In the Azure Burning City.

Lin Yuan's true body was cultivating and contemplating in the Burning Heaven Pavilion, while his incarnation was outside, interacting with other Sages.

Each Sage had already evolved their own unique power path from the Chaos, and this held significant enlightenment value for Lin Yuan.

Normally, the Sages would be guarding their own territories, and even if Lin Yuan wanted to exchange insights with them, he wouldn't have the opportunity.

But now? With the imminent invasion of the Purple Dream Domain, nearly twenty Sages had gathered in the Azure Burning City, allowing Lin Yuan to freely exchange insights with them at any time.

It wasn't really Lin Yuan exchanging insights with these Sages, but rather the Sages themselves engaging in mutual exchanges, with Lin Yuan silently listening.

Like a Chaotic Flame Axe, cleaving through everything.
