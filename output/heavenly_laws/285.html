The rules of space are one of the pillars that support the operation of the universe.

What are the pillar rules? It means that without the rules of space, the universe would become imperfect or even collapse.

Everything that exists, everything that is, must rely on space. It is the foundation of all things.

Lin Yuan has completely mastered the rules of space. Even if it's just one aspect, his perception of the outside world is completely different now.

Before comprehending the rules of space, Lin Yuan was like a fish in water, seeing and feeling only what was around him. But after comprehending the rules of space, it's as if he has leaped out of the water surface and can see more and look further.

"The rules of space may not be as profound as the rules of time, unable to go against the flow and resurrect lives that have died in the past. But its penetration into all things is deeper," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

At this moment, he has only reached the ninth level in all aspects. Without mastering the rules of space, even if Lin Yuan's combat power and destructive power exceed the ninth level threshold, if he were to fight against a ninth level powerhouse, the other party could completely come and go as they please. By utilizing space, they could "play with" Lin Yuan at a higher level.

It's like a whale living at the bottom of the ocean. No matter how terrifyingly powerful it is, it still cannot threaten lives on land.

"Mastering space is just the beginning. Next, I still need to integrate the Taiji rules and many cosmic forces with the complete rules of space to walk my own path."

Lin Yuan's heart surged. The ninth level path was right before his eyes. He only needed to complete the final integration of rules to step into the ninth level.

In the myriad races of the cosmic starry sky, the ninth level can be considered a foundational existence. For many weak races and ordinary races, even their most powerful ancestors are only at this level.

"My inner world?"

Lin Yuan's consciousness descended into his inner world and discovered that his inner world, which was originally just over a billion miles, had once again begun to expand.

Fortunately, Lin Yuan had anticipated this and took out the Taiwei clear crystals he had prepared in advance, fusing them into his inner world.

In no time, his inner world expanded to a size of 1.31 billion miles in diameter.

The thick world force circulated. An ordinary invincible eighth level would only be crushed in the face of such a vast world force, unable to even resist.

"With my current inner world as support, there should be no difficulty in completely absorbing the mysterious gourd's green liquid," Lin Yuan thought. In recent years, he had tried to absorb the green liquid but always got stuck at the last bit. Now, he estimated that he could easily pass through it.

"First, absorb the green liquid."

Lin Yuan's consciousness returned to the outside world. He took out the gray-green gourd and immediately began absorbing with the gourd mouth facing down.

Thousands of light-years away from the No. B0001 alien battlefield.

A black cosmic spaceship emerged after ending its wormhole jump.

"So that's the No. B0001 alien battlefield?" Lin Yuan was inside the spaceship, looking at the behemoth thousands of light-years away.

The No. B0001 alien battlefield was the first medium-sized alien battlefield opened up by human civilization, about twelve light-years in size.

"Forget it, I'll just use Great Teleportation to cover the remaining distance."

Lin Yuan exited the cosmic spaceship. After comprehending the complete rules of space, any casual Great Teleportation could span hundreds of light-years without a "cooldown time". It was much more efficient than the spaceship's wormhole jump.

Of course, using Great Teleportation to travel consumed a lot of mental energy. If not in a hurry, it was still better to use the spaceship for wormhole jumps.

Lin Yuan also saw that there were only a few thousand light-years left, so he decided to use Great Teleportation to travel, just wanting to try out the feeling of using it to get around.

Whoosh.

At the edge of Battlefield No. 0001.

Lin Yuan appeared and looked at the battlefield before him.

"The spatial fluctuations are obvious, far more concentrated than the medium-sized alien battlefield where Taiyin Yuanshen is," Lin Yuan thought.

The spatial fluctuations on the alien battlefields originated from the constant battles between countless powerhouses, and whether there were permanent spatial rifts.

As the oldest alien battlefield, Battlefield No. 0001 often experienced escalations in the intensity of wars in the distant past.

Tenth level evolvers personally wielding the strongest weapons would tear open permanent spatial rifts with widths and lengths much greater than other medium-sized alien battlefields.

The spatial rifts of ordinary small alien battlefields were about ten to several tens of meters, while the spatial rifts of medium-sized alien battlefields mostly exceeded ten thousand meters, between ten thousand and twenty thousand meters.

And the largest permanent spatial rift on Battlefield No. 0001 reached thirty thousand meters.

These were not ordinary spatial rifts, but permanent spatial rifts, "wounds" that even the instinctive operation of the universe could not heal.

"I'll go take a look at that thirty-thousand-meter-long permanent spatial rift first."

Lin Yuan concealed his aura and headed towards the location of his target. Within the alien battlefield, the spatial fluctuations were obvious. Even after mastering the complete rules of space, it was best not to use the Great Teleportation technique.

Because it was very easy to be affected by the fluctuating space, leading to deviating from the destination, or even getting briefly lost in the depths of the layered space.

Milky Way Main Star.

Lin Yuan suddenly opened his eyes. The green flames inside his body burned frantically and finally returned to calm.

"I've finally completely absorbed it."

Lin Yuan's consciousness once again descended into his inner world, looking at the vast world that had silently reached 1.332 billion miles in diameter, a satisfied look appearing on his face.

For a normal human evolver who has mastered the complete rules of space, the limit of their inner world is a billion miles in diameter. For those special lifeforms with peak bloodlines, such as Starry Sea Rocs or other spatially unique lifeforms, the upper limit of their inner worlds is probably also just hundreds of millions of miles in diameter.

The universe is fair. In giving birth to all living things, the stronger the innate talent and bloodline of a lifeform, the lower and more difficult its reproductive ability.

The human civilization has a terrifying reproductive ability, but the vast majority have very mediocre talent.

Starry Sea Rocs have powerful innate talent and bloodlines, but reproduction is extremely difficult for them. But no matter how difficult, Starry Sea Rocs can still barely form a race.

And some special lifeforms are so few in number that they cannot even form a race. The terrifying innate talent of these special lifeforms cannot be discussed with common sense.

In the depths of the cosmic starry sky, many "ancient gods" who dominate a region are born in this way.

"Next is... the cyan liquid?"

Lin Yuan flipped his right hand and the mysterious gourd appeared in front of him.

At this moment, the color on the surface of the mysterious gourd began to slowly change, from the previous gray-green to gray-cyan.

"Absorb."

Lin Yuan inverted the gourd and a drop of profound cyan liquid dripped out.

Rumble.

The cyan liquid entered his throat and turned into pure cyan flames, beginning to burn Lin Yuan's internal organs.

Ordinary eighth-stage evolved beings, when facing Absolute Space, let alone approaching, all attacks would be trapped in layers of space.

Lin Yuan pretty much used the second floor of the Black Tower as a standard to test his own strength.

Although the various means of ninth-stage lives were not only about mastering various rules and cosmic powers, it was also a very important aspect.

If Lin Yuan could pass through the second floor of the Black Tower, even if he couldn't be considered a powerhouse among the ninth stage, he would still be stronger than more than half of the ninth stage.

Every time Lin Yuan came to challenge the second floor of the Black Tower, it would pay close attention. It could pass the boring time.
