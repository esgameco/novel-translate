The Butterfly Paramount was surprised. These kinds of inheritance stars were divided into three types.

The 9th tier space inheritance stars, the 10th tier fusion rule inheritance stars, and the 11th tier fusion rule inheritance stars.

This black planet belonged to the 10th tier fusion rule inheritance stars, only allowing 10th tier lifeforms to enter.

Adding that the inheritance space inside the inheritance star suppressed fluctuations of other rules, only allowing the corresponding fusion rule to operate, external forces, treasures and such means were all useless -

It was as if the originally 'normally' playing scene suddenly began 'rewinding'.

Time regression. Any lifeform that comprehended the time rules could unleash this secret technique.

However, even for Lin Yuan, the area he could regress time in at most was just dozens or hundreds of miles. Any more would be too much, yet the Butterfly Paramount's time regression area was directly millions of square miles.

Moreover, this was just an insignificant avatar of the Butterfly Paramount. As long as within the domain of human civilization, under the virtual network's shroud, the Butterfly Paramount could have as many such avatars as needed.

"There was indeed a slight tremor."

The Butterfly Paramount nodded slightly. Through time regression, she also discovered the black planet had experienced a slight tremor.

"What caused this?"

The Summer Qin Paramount was somewhat surprised.

Actually, there were only two possibilities.

One was that the black planet's interior itself had some issue or accident during operation.

Two was that inside the black planet's inheritance space, a cultivator unleashed an 11th tier realm attack, exceeding the black planet's interior limit, thus leading to that scene just now.

Not discussing the first possibility for now.

As for the second possibility? Currently in the black planet's interior inheritance space, there was only Lin Yuan alone.

Which was to say, if this was the possibility, then the one who unleashed the 11th tier realm attack was Lin Yuan.

But how was that possible?

If the gap between the 8th and 9th tiers was one, then the gap between the 9th and 10th tiers was a hundred, and the gap between the 10th and 11th tiers was ten thousand or a hundred thousand.

Crossing realms to fight?

At the 3rd, 4th, 5th tiers, perhaps it could be achieved by relying on one's bloodline, constitution, mastered secret techniques and so on.

But the higher the realm, the lower the probability of such an occurrence, until it became impossible.

The two Paramounts knew when Lin Yuan broke through to the 10th tier, he endured the immense infusion of primal chaos void origins, gaining immense benefits.

But no matter how great the benefits, it could not change the fact that Lin Yuan was a 10th tier lifeform.

Perhaps with Lin Yuan's talents and aptitude, plus enduring so much primal chaos void origin infusion, by cultivating to the 10th tier peak, he might be able to raise his attack limit to the 11th tier threshold.

But it had only been so long?

For a normal 10th tier newcomer, they still hadn't fully familiarized themselves with their explosively increased power, yet Lin Yuan's attack limit had already reached the 11th tier threshold?

"This little guy." The Butterfly Paramount shook her head slightly.

Since the two of them began paying attention to Lin Yuan, the frequency they were surprised was almost catching up to the total of the past millions of years.

The human civilization's star calendar only spanned over two million years, but before that were the long dormant years.

The Summer Qin and Butterfly Paramounts were born long before even those dormant years.

Inside the black planet's vast world,

The black-robed man stared blankly at the terrifying scene caused by Lin Yuan's strike.

Strands of terrifying aura from the life-and-death cycle force kept diffusing outwards through the hole, even causing space to twist and annihilate under this strand of power.

"Is this the life-and-death cycle rules you just comprehended?"

The black-robed man was silent for a while. "At least comparable to the second or even third level of the life-and-death cycle rules."

The black-robed man's gaze shifted to Lin Yuan who unleashed that strike, replaying many scenes in his mind.

As the altar's spirit, he had absolute control over this space.

The life-and-death cycle rules Lin Yuan operated were indeed just comprehended, only at the initial first level.

Whether ordinary rules fused into new rules, or pillar-level rules fused into new rules, based on the depth of comprehension, they were divided into nine levels from the first to ninth.

For a 10th tier cultivator to break through to the 11th tier, the threshold was to at least comprehend a pillar-level fusion rule and cultivate it to the second level.

Comprehending rules themselves was extremely difficult, let alone fused rules?

If comprehending the universe's original rules, one only needed to quietly sense the rule fluctuations.

It was like having the direction ahead, though still difficult but at least the end could be seen.

But fused rules? Fused rules were formed by experts based on their comprehensions, fusing different rules' characteristics together.

In cultivating fused rules, each level was worlds apart.

"Why would a first level life-and-death cycle rule have such great power?" The black-robed man carefully observed Lin Yuan.

"Without borrowing any external forces, nor any fluctuations from treasures or weapons, is it his own strength? His flesh and blood far exceeds a normal 10th tier lifeform."

The black-robed man vaguely discovered something.

This world's trial did not allow using any external forces.

But the external forces referred to weapons, treasures, or higher level trump cards and so on.

As for one's own strength, like the flesh, soul, mind, will - did those count as external forces?

In the black planet's initial rules, those did not count as external forces.

The life-and-death cycle rules Lin Yuan operated, though only at the initial first level he just comprehended,

His foundation was simply too powerful.

It was like the same knife, being swung with a hundred pounds of force,

Versus being swung with a hundred tons of force.

The explosive power would obviously be different.

And here the 'knife' referred to the life-and-death cycle rules.

The depth of comprehending the life-and-death cycle rules did indeed affect the power of these fused rules.

But it was also related to the strength of the cultivator mastering these rules.

"Senior, have I passed the trial?" Lin Yuan unleashed his full power strike, then looked towards the black-robed man not far away and asked.

The black-robed man told him not to hold back, so Lin Yuan naturally did not hold back at all. That strike was made with his full effort, pouring in the vast power of his inner world, flesh, blood, soul, as well as the third stage 'Profound Yellow Strike' he had cultivated - all infused into it.

As for the divine ability 'Strengthen'? Lin Yuan did not activate it, as catalyzing this ability would make it not a full power strike, but a desperate strike.

"According to the rules, you naturally pass the trial," the black-robed man said impassively.

The trial the black planet originally set was that as long as Lin Yuan had the power of the first level peak life-and-death cycle rules, he could pass.

This was basically also the peak a 10th tier lifeform could reach.

As for the second level life-and-death cycle rules? Cultivating the pillar-level fusion rules to the second level would swiftly cross into the 11th tier.

Although Lin Yuan's life-and-death cycle rules were only at the initial first level in terms of comprehension, the power had even reached the threshold of the third level.

"That's good then," Lin Yuan smiled faintly.

The trial inside the black planet was not too troublesome, just needing to unleash one strike.

"Pass through that door, and that is where the ultimate reward of this path lies," the black-robed man looked at Lin Yuan for a moment before speaking.

Not far away, a black door had appeared at some point.

Compared to the twelve great doors outside, this black door was only five meters tall, quite small.

"But let me remind you."

"Any lifeform only has one chance to enter this door. If you come out midway, even if you pass the trial again, you cannot enter."

The black-robed man reminded.

"I don't know if there will be any dangers inside the door?" Lin Yuan asked upon hearing this.

The end of life was death, the end of death was life. Life and death cycled endlessly.

"The fusion of rules points the way?" Ripples rose in Lin Yuan's heart.

The fused rules did not have a general direction like the innate rules of the universe. The comprehension of the fused rules mainly relied on oneself.

But nothing was absolute. If a being who had reached the seventh realm of comprehending the fused rules was willing to 'separate' their understanding, they could create a guiding path like this.

If it was the seventh realm of a pillar-level fused rule, that was a domain only the most powerful could enter.

The cycle of life and death before him was precisely a pillar-level rule. In other words, the guiding path to the cycle of life and death was derived from a supreme being's comprehension of this fused rule.

This separation was permanent. Even for a supreme being, it came at no small cost.

"The guiding path to the cycle of life and death rules has reached the fourth realm." After a moment of comprehension, Lin Yuan concluded inwardly.

A fourth realm guiding path meant that when Lin Yuan studied the cycle of life and death rules, from the first to the fourth realm, he could obtain concrete guidance.

And depending on how much of their comprehension a supreme being separated, the resulting guiding path would differ.

Just a second realm guiding path would not greatly impact a supreme being, who could re-cultivate and recover in a few thousand years.

A third realm guiding path would have a significant impact, requiring at least tens of thousands of years to re-comprehend and make up for the loss.

A fourth realm guiding path was already the limit a supreme being could recover from. If it was a fifth realm guiding path, the separated supreme being would essentially have to abandon that fused rule.

"What is the origin of this black planet that made a supreme being pay such a price?" Lin Yuan pondered silently.

No, not one supreme being. Perhaps twelve supreme beings.

Because there were twelve black gates on the black planet.

Each black gate corresponded to a different pillar-level fused rule, like the fourth realm guiding path to the cycle of life and death that Lin Yuan was currently observing.

The final rewards behind the other eleven black gates were likely similar.

Lin Yuan gathered his thoughts and immediately began observing the essence of the intermingling life and death rules.

[Your comprehension is heaven-defying. By observing the fusion of the life and death rules, your understanding of the life and death rules has greatly increased.]

Fifty years? To a tenth-stage evolver, fifty years passed in the blink of an eye.

The deputy tower master Nalan was stuck here, as were veteran tenth-stage experts like the Black Star Master and the Liuli Star Master.

Even with Lin Yuan's heaven-defying comprehension, it would probably take him hundreds of years to cultivate the cycle of life and death rules to the second realm.

But now, it would only take fifty years?

That meant, if Lin Yuan was willing, he could attempt to break through to the eleventh stage in fifty years.

The formation of such a fused rule guiding path involved beings at the supreme level. At least up until now, Lin Yuan had only heard of such things but never seen them before.

The human civilization might have similar opportunities, but they would definitely have to queue up.

Taking the black planet as an example, only one life could be inside each black gate at a time.

At the main star of the galaxy, a smile appeared on Lin Yuan's face.

Lin Yuan was in a good mood. Even with a complete guiding path, it would still take considerable time to elevate the cycle of life and death rules to the fourth realm.

If his true spirit had entered that gate, Lin Yuan estimated he would be unable to use that spirit's power for a very long time in the future.

After all, once you left during the comprehension of a fused rule guiding path, you could not re-enter. This was a cost Lin Yuan could not accept.

Ever since stepping into the tenth stage and receiving a massive infusion of primal chaotic void essence, Lin Yuan's physical body, inner world, and soul had all explosively grown by several levels.

Because after arriving at this thousand-mile wide permanent spatial rift, Lin Yuan could feel his speed of accumulating disruptive origin power increase to an almost exaggerated degree.
