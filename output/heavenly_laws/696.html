"This sword strike?"

The man in white robes felt a chill run down his spine. His strength was superior to the powerful beings depicted in the previous two giant paintings.

Therefore, when faced with the sword strike unleashed by Lin Yuan, he was not caught completely off guard and instantly annihilated, returning to the painting.

The man in white robes reacted in time, and in an instant, arrays of talismans permeated the void.

The talismans the man in white robes had condensed were exceptionally substantial and emanated an aura of boundless radiance.

But they were useless.

In the face of the black and white sword light that split the yin and yang of heaven and earth, all the talismans were destroyed.

In an instant,

The black and white sword light arrived before the man in white robes.

The man in white robes let out a light sigh and closed his eyes.

Rustling sounds.

Immediately after,

The man in white robes, like the powerful beings in the previous two giant paintings, had his form disintegrate.

However, unlike the golden-armored youth and the alluring woman, the disintegrated form of the man in white robes did not return to the painting.

Instead, it re-condensed.

"Hmm?"

Lin Yuan looked at the man in white robes.

His expression couldn't help but be surprised.

Although that strike just now couldn't be considered his strongest killing move, it was still a full-power strike.

A full-power strike in his normal state.

According to Lin Yuan's estimation, even if it were ten or a hundred abyssal dragons, they would not be able to withstand this strike.

Moreover, this strike contained the Dao of the Chaos Yin-Yang Taiji, possessing boundless destructive power. With a single strike, the physical body, spirit, and soul would all be annihilated, and at the very least, this true body could not be restored.

In Lin Yuan's comprehension, if his Dao of the Chaos Yin-Yang Taiji cultivation reached the third layer, it would encompass causality, and the enemies he slew with this Dao would have their true bodies and main bodies affected by the pull of causality.

In simple terms, any experts below the Thirteenth Realm were like helpless ants before Lin Yuan, having no means of saving their lives.

"Don't think too much about it."

"You have passed the test."

The man in white robes looked at Lin Yuan, as if he had perceived something, and said, "I was able to re-condense my form by relying on the power of the giant paintings. If I didn't have the giant paintings, I would have been completely annihilated by your sword strike."

The man in white robes carefully examined Lin Yuan.

As the most perfect and powerful life created by the Lord of the Wu Mountain, he had never been directly defeated by any existence since his birth.

Not even the Eternal Scions were an exception.

The Lord of the Wu Mountain had opened up numerous test realms, and many powerful beings had come to undergo the tests since the great changes in heaven and earth, including some Eternal Scions and Heaven-Endowed Scions born under the threat of the Calamity.

But even among those who had passed the test, they had only managed to forcibly overcome the man in white robes through desperate means and methods to enter the entrance and pass the test.

Someone like Lin Yuan, who directly annihilated the man in white robes with a single sword strike, had never appeared before.

The man in white robes himself had never imagined that he would be so easily defeated by a being under the Eternal Lord, even those Primal Demons would not be able to achieve this level.

"You're too kind."

Lin Yuan immediately understood.

He looked at the man in white robes and nodded slightly.

It had to be said that the man in white robes was the strongest life form Lin Yuan had encountered so far below the Thirteenth Realm.

The first abyssal dragon in the first source world was somewhat inferior to the man in white robes.

But unfortunately, the man in white robes had encountered Lin Yuan.

A thousand years ago, Lin Yuan's strength had already surpassed the abyssal dragon and was not inferior to the current man in white robes.

Not to mention the tremendous increase in his rule comprehension and methods after a thousand years of cultivation, and the opening of the hundred billion particle world.

The hundred billion particle world, which had been reshaped by the "Infinite Void", brought terrifying blessings to Lin Yuan.

After descending into this source world, Lin Yuan had gathered all resources and abandoned the cultivation of the two great divine souls, focusing on opening the particle world, which could also demonstrate the importance of this method to his advancement.

"It's not a matter of being too kind."

The man in white robes shook his head, "I am indeed not your match, far from it."

The man in white robes frankly admitted.

His gap with Lin Yuan was too large.

The sword strike that Lin Yuan had unleashed, after annihilating the golden-armored youth in the first giant painting and the alluring woman in the second giant painting, was still able to annihilate him.

It meant that he couldn't even block the residual power of that sword strike.

"You are the strongest among the six who have passed the test, and are likely the most powerful being under the Eternal Lord. I hope you can fulfill the expectations of the Lord of the Wu Mountain and completely eradicate the 'Calamity'."

The man in white robes looked at Lin Yuan, not knowing what he was thinking, and his form disintegrated, returning to the painting.

The reason he relied on the power of the giant paintings to re-condense his form was simply to see what the existence that had one-shot him was like.

Nothing more.

"Completely eradicate the Calamity?"

Lin Yuan did not respond.

According to the golden figure's implication,

The purpose of the Lord of the Wu Mountain in opening up numerous test realms

Was to cultivate a new Eternal Lord.

In other words, to eradicate the 'Calamity', one must become the new Eternal Lord.

Although in the future timeline seen by the six progenitors of the first source world, Lin Yuan was almost certain to reach the Thirteenth Realm.

But he didn't have complete confidence, and Lin Yuan always felt uneasy.

This was also why Lin Yuan had been striving to break through to the Thirteenth Realm.
