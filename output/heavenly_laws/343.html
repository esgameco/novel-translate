Lin Yuan sat cross-legged, with a slowly floating Xuanhuang-colored crystal in front of him.

At this moment, Lin Yuan was just one small step away from fully refining the core of this secret realm.

It could take as short as half an hour or as long as several days to complete the final refinement.

Once done, Lin Yuan would take command of the Xuanhuang Secret Realm, achieving what even the strongest beings in the universe yearned for but could not obtain.

The paramount experts of the major factions in the main universe, the solitary paramount experts, and all other twelfth-stage lifeforms coveted the Xuanhuang Secret Realm.

Every time the secret realm opened, several paramount experts would try to lock onto its coordinates.

The other paramount experts who did not take action would also observe secretly.

If any variable occurred, they could rush over in an instant.

The importance paramount experts attached to the Xuanhuang Secret Realm was self-evident.

This included the nine paramount experts of human civilization.

This was also why Lin Yuan was unwilling to reveal that he was the new master of the Xuanhuang Secret Realm.

He did not want to say too much, even to human civilization.

It was not out of vigilance against human civilization, but because some things were better left unsaid to avoid more undesirable events from happening.

Once Lin Yuan reached the twelfth stage, he could publicly claim this matter without any risk.

"This Xuanhuang Secret Realm is not an independent, complete world," Lin Yuan could sense the true nature of the secret realm as he was about to fully refine its core.

The Xuanhuang Secret Realm existed by relying on the main universe and shared the same temporal rules.

"The methods of the Xuanhuang Emperor are truly inconceivable."

After carefully perceiving it for a while, Lin Yuan could not help but feel amazed.

The Xuanhuang Secret Realm was hidden deep within the innumerable spatial layers of the main universe, where even paramount experts could not lock onto its coordinates.

What allowed this?

It was the secret realm's near-fusion with the vast main universe.

By borrowing the immense main universe, it caused those paramount experts to fail in locating it.

With the Xuanhuang Emperor's abilities, he could certainly have created a complete independent world.

However, that would prevent it from fusing with the main universe, and paramount experts would eventually lock onto its coordinates.

It was like a black dot suddenly appearing on a white paper - it would quickly be discovered no matter how small, due to the stark contrast with its surroundings.

But if a white dot appeared on the white paper, blending into the same color, it would be much harder to notice.

Before twelfth-stage lifeforms like paramount experts, whether the Xuanhuang Secret Realm was an independent world or not made little difference once its coordinates were locked.

"Hm?"

There was a certain resonance between the various Xuanhuang Secret Realms.

But it was only a resonance. Each secret realm existed in different universes, separated by vast distances.

Unless one was a paramount expert, even eleventh-stage lifeforms would be unable to traverse such distances.

And at the paramount expert level, after taking command of one Xuanhuang Secret Realm, the others held little use for them.

What attracted paramount experts most about the Xuanhuang Secret Realms was the heritage left behind by the Xuanhuang Emperor, the three great Dharma protector secret arts.

As for other things like Xuanhuang crystals or ninth or tenth-stage secret treasures? They were not worth paramount experts crossing numerous universes.

"Since the Xuanhuang Emperor's disappearance until now, no ultimate being has been born through the Xuanhuang evolutionary path," Lin Yuan stroked his chin, his mind wavering.

The threshold for the Xuanhuang evolutionary path was too high, preventing its propagation. To make the ultimate leap, one could only rely on oneself.

In the era the Xuanhuang Emperor lived, there was no concept of propagating one's evolutionary path.

Borrowing the power of living beings to aid the ultimate leap was an idea that emerged after the Xuanhuang Emperor vanished.

Whether the Xuanhuang evolutionary path had an ultimate being or not did not affect Lin Yuan much.

He never intended to become a paramount expert through that path.

To Lin Yuan, the Xuanhuang evolutionary path at most served as a reference to draw insights from.

He would extract its essence and integrate it into his own path.

In Lin Yuan's view, the Xuanhuang evolutionary path had uncertainties.

For instance, what if the Xuanhuang Emperor "returned" after one became a paramount expert through this path?

Would there be two ultimate beings on the same evolutionary path?

What would happen then?

Would the two ultimate beings wage a war over the Dao?

Would one ultimate being become nourishment for the other?

Of course, this possibility was extremely slim, almost negligible.

There was basically no hope for the Xuanhuang Emperor's "return."

But a risk was still a risk.

Lin Yuan had his own martial path that he created from nothing. Naturally, he would not choose the former.

He aimed even higher.

"Continue cultivating the Xuanhuang Body," Lin Yuan flipped his right hand, producing a fist-sized Xuanhuang crystal that dissolved into powder and fused into his body.

Cultivating the Xuanhuang Body secret art did not consume much heart force.

Lin Yuan only needed to operate the secret art continuously while absorbing Xuanhuang crystals and various secret weapons and treasures.

Whoosh.

In a moment, the fist-sized Xuanhuang crystal was fully absorbed.

Lin Yuan took out another one.

"This Xuanhuang Body consumes an astonishing amount," Lin Yuan sighed inwardly.

In these five years, the Xuanhuang crystals Lin Yuan consumed cultivating this secret art alone were equivalent to over a million universal crystals.

A million universal crystals was the entire fortune of a ninth-stage expert at the million-fold realm.

Yet in front of Lin Yuan, they only lasted five years.

Of course, the results were extremely gratifying after such consumption.

Currently, Lin Yuan's Xuanhuang Body had not reached a small success stage.

But his physical body's strength had already directly increased twofold.

It must be known that after the amplification from the mysterious gourd, Lin Yuan's physical body was already nearly twenty times stronger than normal experts at the same level.

Increasing that twentyfold base by another two times, Lin Yuan felt that with just the power of his physical body and inexhaustible blood essence, he could pierce through numerous spatial layers to suppress special ninth-stage experts capable of opening millions of realms.

The martial path naturally excelled in physical body and physique compared to other paths at the same level, not to mention Lin Yuan's amplification from the mysterious gourd.

Moreover, the increase in physical strength was just one aspect.

After cultivating the Xuanhuang Body, Lin Yuan's physical body would naturally emanate an aura called the "Xuanhuang Emperor's Protective Light."

Although this aura could not directly render him immune to ninety percent of attacks from the same level like the Element Body, as long as the Xuanhuang Emperor's Protective Light remained unbroken, Lin Yuan would not suffer any damage - be it physical, law, or even more mystical attacks.

The Xuanhuang Emperor's Protective Light, Revive from a Drop of Blood, Element Body, and Primordial Bell.

These were currently Lin Yuan's four main defensive measures.

Of course, the Primordial Bell was with his true original body, but relying on just the first three, Lin Yuan's defenses had almost reached the pinnacle of the ninth stage.

He estimated that ordinary tenth-stage experts could not break through his defenses.

In the virtual world, Lin Yuan was chatting with his parents.

"By the way, little Yuan, Yiyi is getting married," his mother Lu Qiong immediately shared this good news.

"Lin Yi is getting married?" Lin Yuan was slightly taken aback. "So soon?"

He had indeed heard before that his younger sister was dating someone and they were very compatible. But he did not expect it to progress so quickly to the topic of marriage.

"What do you mean so soon?" His mother rolled her eyes. "She's over ninety years old already. And you're over a hundred yourself."

"Over a hundred..." Lin Yuan came to a realization.

Evolutionary beings had long lifespans. Even first or second-stage evolutionary beings could easily live for hundreds or thousands of years with biotechnology.

But his parents were originally just ordinary people, common citizens of human civilization who generally lived up to two hundred years. Getting married at over ninety was considered "late" for them.

"Is Yi's boyfriend that kid from the Xia family?" Lin Yuan asked.

"Yes, little Xia is honest, loyal and reliable. Yi will definitely not live a difficult life with him in the future," his mother said with delight.

"Honest? Loyal and reliable?"

Lin Yuan fell silent.

The Xia family was one of the great families of human civilization, officially called the "Human Xia Family." They had produced eighth-stage or higher citizens throughout history, and this generation even had an eleventh-stage expert presiding over them.

Someone born into such a family could hardly be described as honest and loyal.

The fact that he could marry Lin Yi might even be a "political marriage" aimed at her brother - Lin Yuan himself.

The Human Xia Family had a decent reputation. If Lin Yi married into it, then even if something happened to Lin Yuan in the future, his parents would at least...
