The Savage Great Saint, the Black Jade Great Saint, and several other Chaotic Great Saints quickly came to see Lin Yuan.

At this moment, Lin Yuan was facing away from them, adjusting the many great dao rules of the Chaotic Continent.

When the Chaotic Continent was first opened, although the space-time had stabilized, many other great dao rules still needed to be adjusted.

Only by doing so could this Chaotic Continent become more perfect and free of hidden dangers.

"Silver River Great Saint," the Savage Great Saint, the Black Jade Great Saint, and the other Chaotic Great Saints saw that Lin Yuan did not respond to them, and did not dare to continue asking, so they could only obediently stand in place.

The vast rule fluctuations occasionally transmitted from the Chaotic Continent below shocked the minds of several Chaotic Great Saints.

"The Silver River Great Saint's understanding of the rules is too terrifying." The Chaotic Great Saints trembled inwardly, and at such close range, they could clearly feel Lin Yuan's influence on the Chaotic Continent below.

"Even if our true bodies come here now and play all our trump cards, we probably won't be able to last long against the Silver River Great Saint," the Black Jade Great Saint thought to himself, able to clearly feel the difference in strength between the current Silver River Great Saint and the one in the Mysterious Yellow Universe.

"You've come." Lin Yuan glanced at the Black Jade Great Saint and the Savage Great Saint and said casually, "Did you bring the Chaotic Stones?"

"We have brought them," the Black Jade Great Saint said respectfully, completely treating Lin Yuan as an unparalleled Great Saint, and then took out a world bracelet.

"We have also brought them," the Savage Great Saint, the Tian Lu Great Saint, and the other Chaotic Great Saints said, immediately taking out the Chaotic Stones they had prepared earlier.

"In that case..." Lin Yuan finished counting, and then released the main combat bodies of the several Chaotic Great Saints.

Of course, all the weapons, treasures, and other precious items on these main combat bodies were confiscated by Lin Yuan.

The Savage Great Saint, the Black Jade Great Saint, and the other Great Saints had long expected this, and they had only redeemed their main combat bodies. As for the weapons and treasures they carried, they didn't have any thoughts about them.

"Then, Silver River Great Saint, we'll take our leave first," the Black Jade Great Saint said tentatively.

After getting back their main combat bodies, the Black Jade Great Saint felt relieved in his heart. Although he was well aware of Lin Yuan's strength and had no intention of going back on his word, he still felt much more at ease.

"Mm." Lin Yuan nodded and looked at the several Chaotic Great Saints, then suddenly said, "Are you interested in having your forces stationed in the Chaotic Continent I've opened?"

Whether it was the Black Jade Great Saint, the Savage Great Saint, or the Tian Lu Great Saint and the other two newly promoted Great Saints, they all had forces backing them.

Unlike Lin Yuan's sudden rise, the other Chaotic Great Saints all had their own growth trajectories over the long years. Even if they didn't intentionally establish forces, as their strength grew, they would naturally form their own forces.

"Silver River Great Saint, you're not holding a grudge against us for our previous actions?" the Black Jade Great Saint asked cautiously, his heart filled with joy.

Being able to station their forces in a Chaotic Continent almost on par with the Peerless Great Saints was naturally a great opportunity. But they felt that they had enmity with Lin Yuan, so they didn't dare to make such a request.

"Since you've given the Chaotic Stones, there's nothing else," Lin Yuan said. The enmity between him and the Black Jade Great Saint and the others could be resolved, and since they had already sent the Chaotic Stones, Lin Yuan had no intention of pursuing it further.

Now that the Chaotic Continent had just been opened, it needed the stationing of many forces to develop quickly.

"Good. I'll go back and prepare." The Chaotic Great Saints were delighted and immediately withdrew.

"Two hundred and thirty billion Chaotic Stones." After the Chaotic Great Saints left, Lin Yuan looked at the harvest again.

"Next, I'll continue cultivating, striving to break through to the Chaotic Realm as soon as possible," Lin Yuan thought to himself, and then began to meditate in seclusion.

As for the various affairs on the Chaotic Continent, Lin Yuan only needed to spare a thread of his divine sense to hold the overall situation, and the trivial matters could be completely handed over to the three Goddesses.

In the Source World, in the Blazing Sun Ancient Country, in the Black Jade Nest, Lin Yuan's Yin Primordial Spirit sat in meditation, refining his mind and will with the precious Black Jade.

As a first-class treasure land of the Blazing Sun Ancient Country, the Black Jade produced in the Black Jade Nest could greatly help in the cultivation of one's mind and will.

"My mind and will index should be approaching 90 million points now," Lin Yuan thought happily. As the guardian of the Black Jade Nest, as long as he wished, he could completely monopolize the Black Jade there, but Lin Yuan had no intention of doing so. At the very least, he would not lack Black Jade.

"There are still 8,000 years before the Black Jade in the Black Jade Nest matures. Before that, I need to be careful of the Mysterious Yin Ancient Country side."

Lin Yuan's gaze looked far towards the direction of the Mysterious Yin Ancient Country.

The Black Jade Nest was located on the border between the Blazing Sun Ancient Country and the Mysterious Yin Ancient Country. Although it was currently occupied by the Blazing Sun Ancient Country, it would attract strong experts from the Mysterious Yin Ancient Country every time it approached maturity.

The strong experts from the Mysterious Yin Ancient Country did not have the intention to completely occupy the Black Jade Nest. But as long as they could attack the Black Jade Nest during the maturity period and plunder some of the Black Jade produced there, it would still be a huge gain for them.

"The information and intelligence provided by the Blazing Sun Celestial Emperor has mentioned that the Mysterious Yin Ancient Country side has been quite restless before and after each maturity period," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

The precious items that could refine and enhance one's mind and will? Not only did the strong experts of the Blazing Sun Ancient Country have a demand for them, but the Mysterious Yin Ancient Country...
