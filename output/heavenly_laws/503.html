"Can you do a little bit of everything?"

Hearing the Ancestor of the Martial Dao speak these words in an incredibly calm tone, Zhang Tianshi fell silent for a moment.

Even the most powerful evolutionary paths have their own areas of focus, and the strongest cultivators are no exception. Yet, the Ancestor of the Martial Dao can do a little bit of everything? Although he said "a little bit", Zhang Tianshi dared not truly believe it was just "a little bit".

Doesn't this mean that the Ancestor of the Martial Dao is stronger than the nine supreme cultivators of human civilization in certain aspects?

If it were any other cultivator, no matter how old the information, Zhang Tianshi would have considered this a joke.

Stronger than the supreme cultivators? The nine supreme cultivators of human civilization not only stand at the pinnacle of human civilization, but also at the pinnacle of all the tribes in the starry sky.

To be stronger than the supreme cultivators, isn't that just nonsense? But the Ancestor of the Martial Dao?

Zhang Tianshi had a vague feeling that the Ancestor of the Martial Dao was not lying.

The fact that the Martial Dao evolutionary path has not yet reached the twelfth-stage supreme cultivation path, but is already ranked fifth among human civilization, has already revealed many things.

Next.

Lin Yuan casually answered a few of Zhang Tianshi's doubts, and then ended the "call" with him.

Lin Yuan personally met with Zhang Tianshi, solely because the latter was the first to break through to the eighth stage of the Martial Dao evolutionary path.

It was completely out of pity for future cultivators.

On the savage planet.

Zhang Tianshi disconnected from the virtual world, his expression wistful.

In the brief exchange with the Ancestor of the Martial Dao, Zhang Tianshi felt that his understanding of the Martial Dao and many other evolutionary paths had undergone a tremendous change.

This change was a transformation that came from a bird's-eye view, a significant leap in Zhang Tianshi's comprehension of the essence of cultivation.

"So this is the Ancestor of the Martial Dao," Zhang Tianshi calmed himself, and in his heart, he wanted to say one more thing.

That is -

The supreme cultivators are not much after all.

"I've heard the rumor that the Ancestor of the Martial Dao's true identity is the Galactic Lord," Zhang Tianshi thought about the rumors that had been circulating within human civilization.

The matter of the Galactic Lord being the founder of the Martial Dao has been widely spread.

The human civilization authorities have not stopped this rumor, and the Ancestor of the Martial Dao himself has not come out to refute it.

This has caused the credibility of this rumor to grow higher and higher.

Given the Ancestor of the Martial Dao's status in human civilization, if there were any false rumors, the human civilization authorities would certainly have stepped in to suppress them.

But that did not happen.

Although the human civilization authorities have not confirmed it either.

But sometimes silence is a form of acknowledgment, and the Ancestor of the Martial Dao is no exception.

"The Galactic Lord." Zhang Tianshi swallowed, the growth trajectory of the Galactic Lord from birth to the present can be clearly traced.

This cultivator genius, not even a thousand years old, has no connection to the founder of the Martial Dao evolutionary path, yet the two are the same person.

"The eighth stage of the Martial Dao."

The details that the Ancestor of the Martial Dao had just mentioned kept flashing in Zhang Tianshi's mind.

At this moment, Zhang Tianshi felt that the path from the eighth stage of the Martial Dao to the ninth stage was clearly visible.

This is due to the high-level guidance of the Ancestor of the Martial Dao, as well as Zhang Tianshi's own accumulation over the past thousands of years, which has now been ignited under the Ancestor's prompting.

"I'm going to start cultivating in seclusion right now."

Zhang Tianshi immediately closed his eyes, intending to continue his seclusion cultivation for hundreds or thousands of years, fully digesting the guidance of the Ancestor of the Martial Dao.

In front of the naturally formed cosmic black hole.

Lin Yuan's solar divine soul was sitting in meditation.

The black hole is a special celestial body within the universe, and the space-time black hole is formed by the collision of countless black holes, leading to the collapse of space-time, forming a vast cosmic phenomenon.

Even an eleventh-stage perfected cultivator would appear extremely insignificant in the face of the massive space-time black hole, and the slightest mistake could result in being sucked into it.

Many eleventh-stage perfected ancient gods have established various space-time black hole secret techniques based on the naturally formed space-time black holes in the universe.

Even the strongest among them can only possess one-millionth or one-ten-millionth of the power of a true space-time black hole.

"Space-time black hole?" Lin Yuan carefully observed the space-time black hole in front of him. Time and space are inherently one, but they can also repel each other.

The space-time black hole is an example of this, where the ultimate repulsion and collision of time and space leads to the formation of the space-time black hole, and the resulting shockwaves are close to the twelfth-stage level.

"If I can truly comprehend the space-time black hole in front of me, even if I don't reach the peak of the seventh realm, I won't be too far off."

Lin Yuan thought to himself.

The unconscious, unguided collision of time and space alone can unleash power close to the twelfth-stage level. Once under human control, the effects that can be produced are unimaginable.

"It's time to leave."

After a long while, Lin Yuan gradually stopped his contemplation.

Now that his true body and main spirit have reached the pinnacle in all aspects, the assault on the ultimate is imminent. He can come back to contemplate the space-time black hole after breaking through to the twelfth-stage.

In the gloomy palace.

Nine days have passed in the blink of an eye.

More than a dozen twelfth-stage ultimate cultivators descended once again.

"Nine days have passed."

"Lord Fan, have you locked onto the location of the Galactic Lord?"

"Yes, have you locked onto it? Time is of the essence, the Galactic Lord can attempt the breakthrough to the ultimate at any moment."

The twelfth-stage ultimate cultivators spoke with a sense of urgency in their voices.

Once the Galactic Lord decides to attempt the breakthrough to the ultimate, they will no longer have the opportunity to intervene.

If the Galactic Lord breaks through to the ultimate, the nine supreme cultivators of human civilization will undoubtedly set up a complex space-time labyrinth.
