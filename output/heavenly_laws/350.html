Here is my translation of the Chinese excerpt to English:

At the wedding of his younger sister Lin Yi, Lin Yuan met a group of Stage 10 evolved beings.

As the greatest genius in the history of human civilization, there were still many powerful beings willing to give Lin Yuan face.

"What?"

"The Galactic Lord wants to contact the ancient god Tuoba?"

"This ancient god is said to have a bad temper."

"I don't know Tuoba the ancient god, but I know other ancient gods. The ancient gods should have connections with each other."

As the pinnacle civilization in the universe, humanity had great influence. Most of the Stage 10 evolved beings had some connections, and they quickly brought results for Lin Yuan.

In his personal space, a plump figure appeared.

"Galactic Lord."

This plump figure spoke in a rather complicated tone.

"Is that you?"

Lin Yuan was a bit stunned. This plump figure was none other than the Mado cultivator from the Xuanhuang Secret Realm.

The Mado cultivator was also a special life form, the descendant of an ancient god from the depths of the cosmic starry sky.

The virtual world network of human civilization was in principle not open to other races.

But as a high-level evolved being, one could temporarily apply for access rights to build a temporary virtual network to allow other races to connect.

"My ancient ancestor knows Tuoba the ancient god and said he can help make an introduction," the Mado cultivator said directly.

Regarding the Xuanhuang Secret Realm incident, the Mado cultivator did not act in time, causing his ancient god ancestor to regret it deeply.

What a great opportunity that was, wasted just like that.

And now, hearing that the Galactic Lord wanted to contact Tuoba the ancient god, the Mado cultivator's ancient god ancestor immediately knew the opportunity had come again.

Although he didn't know what matter Lin Yuan wanted to contact Tuoba about, such an open request must be quite important.

If he could help the Galactic Lord at this time, it may not build deep friendship, but at least the Galactic Lord would look upon him favorably.

As the saying goes, friendship doesn't come overnight, but accumulates bit by bit.

"I see," Lin Yuan nodded. "Then thank you to the Sizhong Ancient God."

The Mado cultivator's ancient god ancestor was named Sizhong Ancient God, known for his extensive connections, so it was not surprising that he knew Tuoba the Ancient God.

"However, my ancestor said that Tuoba has a bad temper, so before meeting Tuoba, my ancestor wants to meet you, the Galactic Lord, first."

"No problem," Lin Yuan nodded.

The meeting would definitely take place in a temporarily constructed virtual world, so Lin Yuan's safety was not a concern.

Soon, another plump figure appeared.

Compared to the Mado cultivator, this plump figure gave Lin Yuan a much stronger sense of oppression.

Among all the beings Lin Yuan had met so far, this was second only to the extremely powerful Xia Qin.

"Sizhong Ancient God."

"Galactic Lord."

The Sizhong Ancient God also smiled and said without any airs of being an 11th stage ancient god.

After some pleasantries, they got to the main topic.

"May I ask what matter the Galactic Lord wishes to see Tuoba about?" the Sizhong Ancient God asked.

"I have a good friend who cultivated Tuoba's evolutionary path, but recently his spiritual will disappeared. Through causal resonance, I confirmed it happened in Tuoba's domain," Lin Yuan said directly without concealing anything.

"I understand," the Sizhong Ancient God nodded, then frowned slightly. "That old fellow Tuoba's lifespan shouldn't have much left, probably within a billion years he'll forcefully attempt to break through to the ultimate stage."

Stage 11 powerhouses could slow their personal time flow to achieve a kind of "eternity", but it was only a different kind in the end. In reality, a Stage 11's lifespan was still slowly depleting. Tuoba was born at the beginning of the universe's creation and had experienced an untold number of years.

Even by slowing the time flow on his body's surface, his remaining lifespan couldn't be much. To continue living, he had to break through to Stage 12 before his great limit arrived.

"Forcefully attempt the ultimate breakthrough?" Lin Yuan was a bit surprised.

"But why did Tuoba take away my friend's spiritual will? He's only Stage 6," Lin Yuan voiced his inner doubt.

"Hahaha, Galactic Lord, you wouldn't know this," the Sizhong Ancient God laughed. "We ancient gods were innately extremely powerful, which led to spiritual will being our weakness."

"I guess Tuoba took your friend's spiritual will to immerse himself in your friend's memories, experiencing a weak life, as a way to temper his spiritual will."

"But usually this kind of immersion selects life forms very similar to oneself. Your friend was probably chosen for this reason."

"I see, that's how it is," Lin Yuan nodded.

To make the ultimate leap, spiritual will absolutely could not be a shortcoming. For ancient Stage 11 life forms, occasionally inhabiting an extremely weak life not in control of its destiny was a unique experience beneficial for spiritual will transformation.

"This matter shouldn't be difficult. With me mediating in between, there shouldn't be a big problem," the Sizhong Ancient God said nonchalantly. This one's gone, just get another, no big deal.

"Thank you, Sizhong Ancient God," Lin Yuan said solemnly.

"You're welcome." The Sizhong Ancient God was pleased - this was what he was waiting for from Lin Yuan.

"I'll contact Tuoba right away."

Soon, a scrawny figure appeared in this temporary virtual network space.

This scrawny figure emanated an indistinct aura, his face blurred and features indistinguishable.

"Tuoba, you're here," the Sizhong Ancient God turned to the scrawny figure. "This is the Galactic Lord."

"I've told you about the matter. The spiritual will you took belongs to the Galactic Lord's good friend. In my view, it doesn't have to be him, does it?"

"Galactic Lord," Tuoba the Ancient God ignored the Sizhong Ancient God and instead looked at Lin Yuan with a smile. "Even an old fellow like me has heard of the Galactic Lord's reputation. Quite impressive."

"It's just a reputation," Lin Yuan shook his head. If he couldn't become an ultimate powerhouse and transcend the river of time, even astonishing genius had no meaning.

"Not at all. Galactic Lord, you have the support of human civilization and are favored by nine ultimate powerhouses. Your future hope of breaking through to the ultimate stage is far greater than us old fellows," Tuoba said.

"With your talent and aptitude, Galactic Lord, you'll definitely receive the highest priority and resources from human civilization that we without civilizations can only envy."

"Breaking through to the ultimate stage? That's too far away for me," Lin Yuan frowned slightly.

Currently he was only Stage 9, with Stage 10 and then 11 still ahead - two major realms to cross before attempting the ultimate breakthrough.

"Tuoba Ancient God, let me get straight to the point," Lin Yuan thought for a moment before saying directly, "The spiritual will you took belongs to a good friend of mine. As long as you return it to me, we can discuss conditions."

Lin Yuan had never thought to ask Tuoba to unconditionally return Fang Qing's spiritual will. But the price probably wouldn't be too high - just the spiritual will of a Stage 6 life form, with the Sizhong Ancient God mediating as well.

Add in the intimidation of human civilization - not only was Lin Yuan an unmatched genius, he had humanity backing him.

"Conditions, good, let's discuss conditions then," Tuoba looked at Lin Yuan. "You've probably heard from Sizhong, my lifespan doesn't have much left."

"If I keep waiting, I'll probably have to forcefully attempt the ultimate breakthrough," Tuoba said slowly.

"But for breaking through to the ultimate, I don't have much confidence."

Tuoba shook his head. "So my condition is, Galactic Lord, as long as you can provide one treasure that can increase my chances of successfully breaking through, I'll hand over that spiritual will."

"A treasure to increase ultimate breakthrough chances?" Lin Yuan was slightly stunned.

This Tuoba really dared to open his mouth wide - to make such an outrageous demand?

Breaking through to the ultimate stage was the highest goal for all evolved life. A treasure to increase those chances was infinitely precious.

"Tuoba, have you gone mad?" The Sizhong Ancient God was dumbfounded, never imagining Tuoba would actually say such things out loud.

"Anyway, those are the conditions. Galactic Lord, if you can produce it, we'll make the trade," Tuoba smiled. "I believe in your abilities, Galactic Lord, you'll definitely satisfy me."
