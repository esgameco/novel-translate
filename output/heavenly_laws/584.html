Humming! All the forces from every part of the Taiji Universe were suppressing that Eternal Primal Gold.

The Eternal Primal Gold began to visibly deform and twist, and terrifying traces appeared on its surface, spreading to the depths, forming deep pits one after another.

The entire Eternal Primal Gold had become uneven and slightly "miserable" in appearance.

Although he was not sure how strong the Taiji Primordial Spirit's internal universe was now.

But this Eternal Primal Gold? It only became a little uneven on the surface? "The deepest trace is over a meter, close to two meters."

"..."

The red-robed girl child fell silent.

She had realized something was wrong as soon as she entered this universe, and immediately felt the terrifying power unleashed by Lin Yuan's full operation of the internal universe.

Finally, the Eternal Primal Gold quickly deformed and twisted.

"Master, why is the power of your Primordial Spirit's internal universe so strong?"

The red-robed girl child took a deep breath, then looked at Lin Yuan with disbelief.

If she wasn't sure that this internal universe came from Lin Yuan, and the aura and imprint were connected, the red-robed girl child would even suspect that this was the internal universe of another powerful expert.

It was too exaggerated.

Too abnormal.

"How is it possible? This universe has not cultivated the second layer of the 'Infinite Void', it is still at the first layer, why is it so strong?"

The red-robed girl child was full of confusion, the scene in front of her was too unbelievable.

"I made some improvements to the internal universe." Lin Yuan thought for a moment and did not tell the red-robed girl child about the 'Sun Opening the Heavens'.

After all, this involved another dimensional space.

In fact, Lin Yuan had already concealed the existence of the scorching sun in the center of the universe before bringing the red-robed girl child over, and when he was fully operating the internal universe, he also deliberately suppressed the power of the sun.

The internal universe was Lin Yuan's home field, as long as Lin Yuan was unwilling, even the red-robed girl child could not see through much.

Previously, the red-robed girl child was able to perceive whether Lin Yuan was cultivating the second layer of the 'Infinite Void' because Lin Yuan actively showed it to her.

"Improved?"
