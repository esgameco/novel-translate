Realizing this, the auras of the two peerless great saints began to fluctuate slightly.

Lin Yuan stepped forward directly.

The surrounding space-time began to distort, this was the Mysterious Yellow lineage's three great protective secret techniques - the "Nine Steps of Space-Time".
