"This is indeed a path of martial arts evolution," said the powerful Xuanyuan cultivator next to him, after reading it over a few times.

An evolutionary path means a cultivation path, and the existence of the 12th stage can be sensed with careful perception.

This allows one to judge whether this evolutionary path has the potential to produce 12th stage cultivators.

At the moment, this thunder and lightning martial arts evolutionary path is extremely similar to the martial arts evolutionary path that Lin Yuan walked, yet it does not seem to have any 12th stage cultivators?

"Correct," Lin Yuan said.

"This evolutionary path is a branch of the martial arts that I have opened up and perfected. It can definitely give rise to new 12th stage cultivators."

"Unbelievable," the ancient powerful cultivator exclaimed.

They had heard that some extremely broad evolutionary paths could accommodate multiple parallel branches, all of which could give rise to new 12th stage cultivators.

But such evolutionary paths were extremely rare, requiring perfection in all aspects, with the foundation and profundity reaching the limit. Otherwise, if the path itself was not broad enough, how could it accommodate other evolutionary branches? Furthermore, it needed to be in perfect harmony with the Chaotic Void and its Dao.

However, even if everything was perfect, if it did not align with the Dao of the Chaotic Void, it could not extend into branches.

"It seems we have underestimated the martial arts before," the Xia Qin powerful cultivator said, equally shocked after reading it.

He could feel that this thunder and lightning martial arts, even if just a branch of the true martial arts, had greater potential than the 12th stage evolutionary path he cultivated.

If the branch was like this, how about the main path?

"Yes," the Xuanyuan powerful cultivator nodded.

Evolutionary paths that could extend branches were all those with the potential to become 13th stage Dao paths.

"The ability to open up branches is not apparent below the 12th stage," Lin Yuan shook his head slightly.

This was not the fault of the nine human powerful cultivators. Even for Chaotic Sovereign cultivators, merely observing the martial arts evolutionary path from the 1st to the 11th stage would make it difficult to judge whether the martial arts could extend branches.

"Silver, are you planning to publicly reveal this thunder and lightning martial arts in the clan?" the Feather Butterfly powerful cultivator asked tentatively.

"Yes," Lin Yuan nodded, as he also longed for the thunder and lightning martial arts to produce a 12th stage cultivator.

The foundation of a great tree does not lie in its thick and tall trunk, but in the countless roots that spread underground. The more powerful cultivators of the martial arts branches, the more they will nourish the martial arts.

"Wonderful."

"In that case, our human civilization will be equivalent to having an additional 12th stage evolutionary path."

The Feather Butterfly powerful cultivator nodded slightly. Even if the Chaotic Void could obtain 12th stage evolutionary paths, where would they have one as reliable as this thunder and lightning martial arts?

"Unfortunately, I did not walk the path of thunder and lightning, otherwise I might have directly transitioned to this evolutionary path," the ancient powerful cultivator sighed.

Within the universe, any evolving cultivator who has chosen an evolutionary path would find it very difficult to switch to another path after reaching the 6th stage.

But that was for cultivators below the 12th stage. For a 12th stage cultivator, to switch to another evolutionary path, they only needed to "scatter the Dao."

To disperse their cultivated Dao.

Of course, no 12th stage cultivator would casually "scatter the Dao," as the risk was too great.

A 12th stage life whose "Dao" has been scattered would have their qi, blood, and soul at the 12th stage level, but without a path to stand on, their combat power would not be even one-tenth or one-thousandth of their full potential.

And to cultivate another evolutionary path to the 12th stage? That would also require a tremendous amount of time.

Therefore, unless they encountered an evolutionary path that was extremely suitable for them, no 12th stage life would be willing to "scatter the Dao."

Even if they encountered a highly suitable evolutionary path, the matter of "scattering the Dao" would be approached with utmost caution. If they were attacked by enemies during the "scattering" process, the consequences would be unimaginable.

"In fact, this branch is more suitable for you, Ancient," Lin Yuan smiled slightly, and transmitted the "Strength Martial Arts" he had opened up and perfected.

Lin Yuan had awakened the physical divine ability "Moving Mountains" long ago, and had a deep understanding of the Dao of Strength. Naturally, the martial arts had also extended a Strength Martial Arts branch related to the Dao of Strength.

"This branch?"

The Ancient cultivator was momentarily confused. In addition to the thunder and lightning martial arts, there was another branch? But the next moment, the Ancient cultivator was stunned, as all the details about the Strength Martial Arts flooded into his mind.

"This, this, this..." the Ancient cultivator was dumbfounded, muttering, "I never imagined the path of Strength could be cultivated in this way, in this manner."

The Ancient cultivator's eyes shone brightly, "The Dao of Strength is not just about power, but also involves space-time. If I had taken this cultivation path earlier, I might have already approached the peak of the Dao-Severing Realm."

The Ancient cultivator was the second powerful cultivator to emerge in human civilization, on par with the Xuanyuan powerful cultivator.

"Different paths lead to the same Dao. To cross into the Chaotic Realm, one must encompass myriad rules and give birth to Chaotic rules," Lin Yuan said.

When Lin Yuan was in seclusion, extending and perfecting the various martial arts branches, he naturally prioritized the paths walked by the nine powerful cultivators of human civilization.

Such as the Strength Dao of the Ancient cultivator, the Dream Dao of the Feather Butterfly cultivator, the Profound Dao of the Xia Qin cultivator - the paths of the nine powerful cultivators were all included in the martial arts branches.

"This Strength Martial Arts is indeed more promising than the evolutionary path the Ancient cultivated, and more suitable for him," the Xuanyuan powerful cultivator said after reviewing it.

"Indeed," Lin Yuan nodded.

How could it not be suitable?

This Strength Martial Arts was specifically perfected by Lin Yuan for the Ancient cultivator.

With Lin Yuan's realm, he had long since seen the strengths and weaknesses of the Ancient cultivator.

Based on the martial arts as the foundation, perfecting a Strength Martial Arts suitable for the Ancient cultivator's cultivation was as simple as could be.

After all, it was not created out of thin air.

"Ancient, I suggest you transition to this Strength Martial Arts," the Xia Qin powerful cultivator said beside him.

"I think so too," the Ancient cultivator said excitedly, then tentatively asked Lin Yuan, "Silver, can I transition to this Strength Martial Arts?"

Since this Strength Martial Arts was brought out by Lin Yuan, and was a branch of the path Lin Yuan walked, he naturally needed to ask Lin Yuan's opinion before transitioning to it.

"No problem," Lin Yuan smiled.

The Ancient cultivator transitioning to the Strength Martial Arts would also greatly benefit Lin Yuan.

Normally, even if the Strength Martial Arts was opened up to all the citizens of human civilization, it was unknown how long it would take to produce a 12th stage cultivator in this path.

Just having an evolutionary path does not mean it will definitely produce a 12th stage cultivator.

But the Ancient cultivator? He was already at the 12th stage realm. If he chose to "scatter the Dao" and cultivate the Strength Martial Arts, he would likely have no bottleneck in re-entering the 12th stage realm.

"If the Ancient transitions to the Strength Martial Arts, he might become the second Dao-Severing Realm peak expert in our human civilization," the Virtual God powerful cultivator sighed, "The future of our human civilization will become even more prosperous."

"Don't be hasty," Lin Yuan said beside them, "I've also prepared some branches for you all."

The Xuanyuan, Xia Qin, Feather Butterfly and the other six powerful cultivators were slightly taken aback.

Lin Yuan then transmitted the "Profound Martial Arts," "Dream Martial Arts" and other eight martial arts branches to them.

"This?"

The eight powerful cultivators took a deep breath, and then quickly began to read and review them.

After a long while,

The Xia Qin powerful cultivator suddenly opened his eyes, his gaze deep and dark, but then quickly recovered.

"Silver, this evolutionary path..." Xia Qin's voice trembled slightly as he looked at Lin Yuan.

"The Dream Martial Arts, blurring the line between illusion and reality. This evolutionary path should have no problem reaching the Mastery Realm," the Feather Butterfly powerful cultivator's eyes shone with unprecedented brilliance, "Cultivated to the extreme, I will become an illusory life form. Even Chaotic Sovereigns would have to put in considerable effort to deal with me."

"Indeed," the other powerful cultivators also nodded in agreement, equally shocked by the martial arts branches Lin Yuan had transmitted.

"Silver, these..." the Xuanyuan powerful cultivator looked at Lin Yuan, his gaze filled with excitement.

"You can all cultivate them," Lin Yuan said directly.

Extending and perfecting specialized martial arts branches for the nine powerful cultivators of human civilization was mutually beneficial.

Lin Yuan's martial arts foundation was deeper, and the nine powerful cultivators of human civilization also had a higher and more distant future.

"There are dozens more martial arts branches that can serve as entirely new 12th stage evolutionary paths," Lin Yuan brought out all the martial arts branches he had opened up and perfected - a total of seventy-three.

The nine powerful cultivators looked at each other, and their shock was slightly diminished.

At least they had the previous thunder and lightning martial arts, as well as the martial arts branches perfectly suited for the nine of them, as a foundation. With dozens more 12th stage martial arts branches added, it was not an overwhelming amount.
