Chapter 286: No matter, the Lord of the Silver Galaxy is watching over us, just fight with all your might (please subscribe)

The humanoid stone statue had already greatly underestimated Lin Yuan.

Over two thousand years old--

Cultivated to the 8th stage.

Comprehended dozens of cosmic powers.

As well as more than ten advanced cosmic powers.

And one primordial-level cosmic power.

Such a monstrous genius, no matter which universe he was in.

Could be described as earth-shattering and unparalleled.

But no matter what, it shouldn't be possible to comprehend the laws of space so quickly, right?

Those aren't ordinary laws, but pillar laws!

The humanoid stone statue found it hard to believe. It knew that the "Void Interpretation" left behind by the owner of the Magic Jade Tower would definitely bring great help to Lin Yuan.

But at most it was just help, not the owner of Magic Jade Tower personally comprehending the laws of space. It mainly depended on Lin Yuan himself.

The "Void Interpretation" was abstruse and hard to understand. If one couldn't comprehend it, let alone helping, it might even be counterproductive.

"In this world, how could there possibly be such a..."

The humanoid stone statue Shengji muttered to itself, seemingly unable to find the words to describe Lin Yuan.

On the second floor of the Black Tower.

Lin Yuan used absolute space to isolate the inside from outside, dissipating and eliminating all the pressure bearing down.

He quickly walked through one-third of the way.

As he continued deeper, the pressure around him became increasingly heavy and terrifying.

Vaguely, illusory creatures condensed and pounced towards Lin Yuan as if baring fangs and brandishing claws.

"I can't hold back anymore."

Lin Yuan's heart sank and he immediately began to activate cosmic powers.

The seemingly endless pressure kept surging. Even relying on absolute space, it was difficult to completely isolate it. Stronger methods had to be used.

Phew...

Various cosmic powers continuously combined, with more than ten advanced cosmic powers as the main body and the primordial-level original power as the core. All the cosmic powers perfectly fused together.

Over the years, Lin Yuan often used the pressure from the second floor of the Black Tower to temper himself and his use of cosmic powers. Naturally, it wasn't without gains.

The current method of fusing all cosmic powers was Lin Yuan's greatest achievement. It could make the power of each cosmic power nearly double in its eruption.

Rumble rumble...

Lin Yuan, withstanding the attacks of the illusory creatures, strode towards the center.

The humanoid stone statue Shengji closely watched the situation on the second floor of the Black Tower.

"If relying only on these, it will be very difficult to pass the second floor..."

The humanoid stone statue Shengji muttered to itself. It used "very difficult" to express its opinion.

Very difficult, but not impossible.

After Lin Yuan comprehended the laws of space in just a few short years, the humanoid stone statue Shengji didn't dare to be too certain about anything, lest it gets slapped in the face afterwards.

No choice, Lin Yuan had already created too many miracles.

So now it left room in any of its opinions.

"The pressure will get greater and greater after the second floor..."

The humanoid stone statue Shengji thought inwardly. Many 9th stage lifeforms could often walk through the first two-thirds of the way.

But in the last one-third of the way, they would be crushed, leading to the failure of the trial.

With Lin Yuan's current state, there was a high possibility he would be similar to these 9th stage lifeforms that failed the trial.

After all, the 9th stage lifeforms that could pass the second floor of the Black Tower all passed through the first two-thirds very easily.

On the second floor of the Black Tower.

Lin Yuan's steps gradually slowed. When he reached the last one-third of the way, the terrifying pressure was like substance, filling every inch of the void.

"Without using the 'Strengthening' divine ability, it will be very difficult for me to pass the second floor by relying on my own power."

Lin Yuan inwardly sighed. This second floor of the Black Tower was indeed specially prepared for 9th stage lifeforms. He had comprehended the complete laws of space and grasped so many cosmic powers.

Yet still had no hope of passing through.

"Let's see."

"Walk a few more steps."

"If the timing is right, I'll use the 'Strengthening' divine ability."

Lin Yuan gathered his thoughts. This kind of divine ability could only be maintained for ten breaths of time, so he had to get as close as possible. Otherwise, if the duration of the divine ability ended and he was still a bit short of passing through, wouldn't that be a terrible loss.

The 'Strengthening' divine ability was not like absolute space, cosmic powers and other methods. It required Lin Yuan to pay a price for a short period of time.

It would be bad if he didn't pass the second floor and fell into a weakened state himself.

Boom boom boom.

After nearly half an hour, he reached a position about fifty meters from the central area.

At this moment, Lin Yuan could almost be said to be struggling with each step. The extremely heavy pressure locked Lin Yuan in place.

If it weren't for absolute space and the many fused cosmic powers isolating him, Lin Yuan estimated he would be crushed in an instant.

"Still fifty meters..."

"It's enough."

Lin Yuan composed himself and slowly closed his eyes.

At the same time.

On the main planet of the Silver Galaxy.

Lin Yuan's real body opened its eyes.

Buzz.

Strands of blood-colored aura intertwined and collided, forming a complex divine ability imprint.

As soon as this divine ability imprint appeared, it quickly merged into Lin Yuan's flesh body. In an instant, the strength of Lin Yuan's flesh and blood began to surge, increasing tenfold.

The flesh led the soul, the soul led the mind and will. In a flash, all of Lin Yuan surged tenfold.

Magic Jade Space.

On the second floor of the Black Tower.

The instant he actively activated the 'Strengthening' divine ability.

This thread of Lin Yuan's mind and will also surged tenfold.

As a result, the power of absolute space and the power of dozens of cosmic powers soared together.

The 'Strengthening' divine ability only acted on Lin Yuan's flesh, soul, mind, will and other aspects. It did not have an amplifying effect on the comprehension of laws and the power of cosmic forces.

But the surge of the mind and will would also affect these aspects.

The mind and will were fundamental. For example, with the same level of comprehension of the laws of space, when wielded by an 8th stage lifeform versus a 9th stage lifeform, the power displayed would be completely different.

Rumble rumble.

After the mind and will were amplified tenfold, Lin Yuan immediately felt that the originally suffocating pressure had vanished.

As if breaking free from shackles, his whole being became extremely unrestrained.

Lin Yuan immediately strode forward.

At this moment, the pressure bearing down from the second floor of the Black Tower no longer posed any obstruction to Lin Yuan.

Only in the last five meters did he pause for a moment. In the end, with only four breaths of time remaining, he passed the second floor of the Black Tower.

"He really passed through?"

Outside the nine-story Black Tower, the humanoid stone statue Shengji fell silent again.

Even though it had predicted this possibility early on... After all, based on Lin Yuan's performance of comprehending the laws of space, it seemed that passing the second floor of the Black Tower as an 8th stage wasn't entirely impossible.

But when this scene truly appeared before it, the humanoid stone statue Shengji's heart still surged with shock.

"His aura soared at the end. Did he use some kind of eruption secret art?"

The humanoid stone statue Shengji guessed inwardly. The owner of the Magic Jade Tower left behind a rule that during the tower challenge, the use of any 'external force' was prohibited.

But eruption-type secret arts completely originated from oneself and did not count as 'external forces'.

So in the end, Lin Yuan passing through...
