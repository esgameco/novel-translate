Lin Yuan's consciousness projection was a black ancient tree, while the other three consciousness projections were a giant snake, a pale green lake, and a huge eyeball respectively.

The vast and boundless outer realm, of course, was not only home to the Ancestral Demon Tree.

These three consciousness projections came from three other outer realm evil gods.

The outer realm evil gods did not necessarily have to fight each other when they met. There was also the possibility of cooperation.

For example, Lin Yuan and these three outer realm evil gods usually got along well, occasionally exchanging or trading information with each other.

In the past three hundred years, besides cultivating, Lin Yuan had been trying to befriend those outer realm evil gods.

According to what he had learned over the years, Lin Yuan found out that in the depths of the outer realm, there existed a large number of "gathering places" where outer realm evil gods resided.

The ruler there was also an outer realm evil god, said to have reached the ninth stage.

The growth of outer realm evil gods was divided into stages. For example, Lin Yuan was currently at the seventh stage, and the previous generation Ancestral Demon Tree was also at the seventh stage in a strict sense.

As for the ninth stage, although it was said that the growth stage of outer realm evil gods had nothing to do with strength, how weak could an outer realm evil god who reached this stage be?

You should know that for outer realm evil gods like the Ancestral Demon Tree, the growth limit was the eighth stage.

Even with Lin Yuan's heaven-defying comprehension, he could not deduce any useful information about the ninth stage.

According to Lin Yuan's speculation, the ninth-stage outer realm evil gods, even if not as strong as the supreme beings of the main world, probably were not too far off.

"The gathering place of outer realm evil gods."

Lin Yuan sighed. If possible, he really wanted to go there.

However, according to the information obtained from other outer realm evil gods,

To go from the Tianqing World to that gathering place,

The time spent would be calculated in thousands or even tens of thousands of years.

Of course, for outer realm evil gods with almost endless lifespans,

Thousands or tens of thousands of years were just a few naps, but in Lin Yuan's view, there was no need for that at all.

Because the duration of stay was not enough.

"I wonder what a ninth-stage outer realm evil god looks like? Can it be observed?"

Lin Yuan was a little curious.

Outer realm evil gods were very special lifeforms. Their growth stages were completely different from the normal cultivation system.

For example, if Lin Yuan were to grow the Ancestral Demon Tree to the eighth stage, perhaps the meaning of his own existence would be "conceptualized"?

"Demon Brother, we envy you for being able to occupy such a world as a place for cultivation."

Just as Lin Yuan was lost in thought, the giant snake projection sent out a mental fluctuation.

However, they could only envy and desire. Now that the Tianqing World had Lin Yuan, this Ancestral Demon Tree, guarding it, the defense had almost reached the extreme.

Other outer realm evil gods, even if more than a dozen came, might not necessarily be able to break through the world barrier.

And wanting more than a dozen outer realm evil gods to join hands was harder than ascending to the heavens.

Most importantly, even if they broke through the Tianqing World, a mere world was far from satisfying the appetite of more than a dozen outer realm evil gods.

"Alright."

"Let's talk less and start the transaction."

The huge eyeball evil god projection sent out a mental fluctuation to remind them.

The reason why the three of them let their consciousnesses descend here was to conduct a transaction with Lin Yuan.

Most of the time, outer realm evil gods wandered in the vast void. Occasionally, they could still obtain some good things.

"The usual rules."

"I only want Sin Water and secret techniques related to the world consciousness."

Lin Yuan nodded, and with a slight movement of his mind, pure cyan-colored demon seeds appeared in front of him.

The demon seeds condensed by the Ancestral Demon Tree, besides being used as a means to deal with enemies, could also be a treasure that was quite beneficial to other evil gods by recalling the life imprint inside.

Over the years, Lin Yuan had indeed earned a lot of wealth by relying on the demon seeds.

Of course, the demon seeds here referred to the ones after Lin Yuan perfected the "parasitism" method.

Each time such a demon seed was condensed, Lin Yuan had to spend some time and energy.

And the demon seeds after recalling his own life imprint did not occupy Lin Yuan's "parasitism" quota.

That's why Lin Yuan was willing to make such an exchange.

"No problem."

"Here are ten portions of Sin Water."

The lake consciousness projection took out the collected Sin Water.

And exchanged it for twenty portions of demon seeds from Lin Yuan.

Sin Water was a treasure produced in some secret realms of the outer void.

It contained boundless sins and was most suitable to serve as a supplement for certain outer realm evil gods.

It was precisely because of this.

That Lin Yuan would take the initiative to exchange for Sin Water.

Although the strength of the Ancestral Demon Tree in this world had basically no impact on Lin Yuan's true body in the main world,

That was based on the premise of needing to use the origin of the Tianqing World to carry the path of martial arts evolution. If the foundation and strength of the Ancestral Demon Tree could be improved, that would naturally be even better.

"I have two secret techniques related to the world consciousness. Please take a look, Demon Brother." The giant snake consciousness projection sent out a mental fluctuation.

It transmitted the beginning of two secret techniques related to the world consciousness to Lin Yuan.

"Mm, not bad."

"These two secret techniques have their merits."

Lin Yuan nodded and took out ten portions of demon seeds for the other party.

The secret techniques related to the world consciousness were related to one of Lin Yuan's schemes, or rather, goals.

The world consciousness was illusory and intangible. For the creatures within the world, it was basically untouchable.

But among the outer realm evil gods, the world consciousness was not a secret. Many outer realm evil gods who wanted to break through a world would be hindered by the world consciousness.

When the previous generation Ancestral Demon Tree wanted to descend into the Tianqing World,

Under the threat of subversion, the world consciousness did not hesitate to let the world sword appear,

Then it organized the Tianqing Alliance and concentrated all its power to resist the invasion of the Ancestral Demon Tree.

Therefore,

For countless tens of thousands of years,

The outer realm evil gods had a deep understanding of the world consciousness and even formed various secret techniques specifically targeting it.

"I have also collected a few secret techniques."

The huge eyeball projection spoke last.

The four outer realm evil gods completed the transaction with this, and they all looked very satisfied.

"Demon Brother, you have already occupied this world, so why do you collect so many secret techniques related to the world consciousness?"

The giant snake consciousness projection asked curiously.

The other two evil god consciousness projections, upon hearing this, also looked at Lin Yuan.

With the means of outer realm evil gods, as long as they descended into the world, it basically declared victory. Once merged into the vast origin of the world, it didn't matter what kind of world it was.
