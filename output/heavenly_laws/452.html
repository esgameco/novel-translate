Chapter 452: Shift (Seeking Subscriptions)

"Perhaps the Galactic Overlord has other opportunities?"

The Xia Qin Grandmaster responded.

It was truly the divine ability 'World' activated by Lin Yuan that possessed an inconceivable power, completely exceeding the understanding of the Grandmasters towards the Eleventh Realm existences.

So in the eyes of the numerous Twelfth Realm ultimate existences, Lin Yuan must have relied on external means, impossible to accomplish that feat through his own power alone.

Even the nine Grandmasters of human civilization instinctively thought so, for they had indeed witnessed the earth-shattering phenomenon when Lin Yuan stepped into the Eleventh Realm.

But without reaching the Twelfth Realm, no matter how profound one's foundation is, they are still lives within the river of time.

Relying solely on oneself, one cannot exert the terrifying power that far surpasses the Eleventh Realm.

Actually, the Grandmasters' thoughts were not entirely wrong.

Without the divine ability 'World', even if Lin Yuan's strength could sweep through many Eleventh Realm experts, it would still require a tremendous amount of time.

If tens of thousands of Eleventh Realm experts scattered, Lin Yuan truly had no way to deal with them, after all, he was not a Grandmaster.

The divine ability 'World' allowed Lin Yuan to fully unleash his greatest advantage. With his inner world enhanced over 20 times by the mysterious gourd, and having gone through the scrutiny of the Chaotic Void Origin twice, these various factors led to the divine ability 'World' exceeding its standard power.

Of course, the terrifying power of the divine ability 'World' was entirely due to Lin Yuan.

If it were other experts, even Eleventh Realm Legendary existences, possessing the exact same 'World' divine ability as Lin Yuan, they would not be able to cause such destructive power.

An Eleventh Realm Legendary existence's inner world at most spans only a few million miles, even if they mastered the 'World' divine ability, what difference would it make?

"Probably."

The Xuanyuan Grandmaster nodded slightly.

He also agreed with Xia Qin Grandmaster's view that the Galactic Overlord must have other opportunities.

"The Galactic Overlord has truly brought us a great surprise."

The Xuanyuan Grandmaster glanced at the distant group of Eleventh Realm peak forces' Grandmasters, a smile appearing on his face.

No matter on what scale the battlefield, when the Human Civilization Alliance clashed with other peak force factions, it usually required a brutal fight, consuming a huge amount of time for both sides to gain victory or ultimately be defeated.

Like just now, the confrontation had just begun, yet it already had an air of conclusion, which was the first time the Human Civilization had experienced.

"Xuanyuan, those fellows seem to be starting to join hands."

The Xushen Grandmaster suddenly spoke, his voice sounding in the ears of the other eight Grandmasters of human civilization.

At this time, apart from the Human Civilization, the Insect Clan, Tianyu Clan, Star Alliance and other peak force factions' Twelfth Realm ultimate existences were quickly exchanging information.

Even the usually arrogant Juexing Clan's Grandmasters had a slight change in their expressions, apparently also joining the exchange.

Although the Xushen Grandmaster did not know the specific content they were discussing, he was certain it was related to the Human Civilization.

"Normal."

The Xuanyuan Grandmaster's expression remained calm.

Just like in past wars, towards the later stages, other force factions would often join hands to confront the Juexing Clan's experts.

At this moment, due to the Galactic Overlord's display of extraordinary combat power, he had naturally replaced the former 'Juexing Clan' and become the target of all the peak force factions' joint efforts.

The Xuanyuan Grandmaster was not surprised by this. If it were other peak force factions, and a figure similar to the Galactic Overlord appeared, the Human Civilization Alliance would also choose to join hands, not fight alone.

On the edge of the core battlefield.

Lin Yuan's figure suddenly appeared, carefully observing the surroundings.

"I remember the Insect Clan's Matriarch, carrying the Clan Pacifier Weapon 'Nightmare Realm', fled in this direction in the end."

Lin Yuan scanned every inch of space, his eyes penetrating the layers of space, continuously searching for the traces of that strongest Eleventh Realm Insect Clan expert.

In the central battlefield, after annihilating tens of thousands of foreign Eleventh Realm experts,

Lin Yuan had given a few instructions to Xuanyuan, Yu and the other human civilization Evolveds.

He then personally pursued and hunted down those over 20 strongest Eleventh Realm experts who had escaped the range of the 'World' divine ability using the Clan Pacifier Weapon.

Although Lin Yuan was quite wary of the Clan Pacifier Weapons, which contained Twelfth Realm ultimate methods within.

But even without the Clan Pacifier Weapons, killing those strongest Eleventh Realm experts would still be a loss for the foreign factions.

Even if he only killed their projections, it could still weaken their foundations, as creating projections was not an easy task.

"I can sense the last remaining causal traces of the Insect Clan's strongest Eleventh Realm expert in this area, but I'm not sure exactly where."

Lin Yuan shook his head slightly, the ability to evade his perception must be due to that Clan Pacifier Weapon.

"Forget it, let's try the next one."

After sensing for nearly half a day without any results, Lin Yuan directly turned and left.

There were over 20 strongest Eleventh Realm experts who had escaped, and since he couldn't find the Insect Clan one for now, he might as well switch to the next target.

Long after Lin Yuan's departure.

Hiding in a certain spatial layer with the aid of the Clan Pacifier Weapon 'Nightmare Realm', the Insect Clan's strongest Eleventh Realm expert heaved a huge sigh of relief.

"How can this Galactic Overlord be so strong?"

The Insect Clan's strongest Eleventh Realm expert was still trembling inwardly. The greatest effect of the Nightmare Realm was to suppress all spiritual power, even in the hands of Grandmasters it was the same.

But now, she had to use the Nightmare Realm to conceal her aura and hide herself, which was something the Eleventh Realm Insect Clan experts who had possessed the Nightmare Realm had probably never tried before.

As a Clan Pacifier Weapon, at the Eleventh Realm level, it was basically unstoppable, even when facing the Juexing Clan's strongest Eleventh Realm experts, relying on the Nightmare Realm, they could defend and resist.

But now, to become a stray dog, this was the first time.

"I wonder what the Matriarchs are thinking now, how should we deal with the Galactic Overlord." Even though the Galactic Overlord had left, the Insect Clan's strongest Eleventh Realm expert did not dare to move a muscle, quickly pondering in her heart.

On another part of the battlefield.

Lin Yuan stretched out his right hand, and the space rippled like a 'Milky Way', a stunningly beautiful woman with twenty pairs of wings squeezed out from the spatial crevice.

"I've found you."

Lin Yuan's mind flickered, and space converged from all directions.

"Not good."

The Tianyu's expression changed drastically, the holy angel crown on her head emitting an extremely sacred light, piercing through the many layers of space, gathering Twelfth Realm power from all directions, and instantly breaking free from the spatial confinement, fleeing into the distance.

"Escaped again?"

Lin Yuan sighed inwardly.

Those strongest Eleventh Realm experts wielding Clan Pacifier Weapons could escape from him multiple times, relying not on their own strength, but on the Twelfth Realm methods left behind in the Clan Pacifier Weapons.

"With my current strength, dealing with the Clan Pacifier Weapons of the various peak force factions is indeed a bit challenging."

Lin Yuan silently thought to himself.

This time when he was pursuing and killing those Eleventh Realm experts who had fled, it was also just a casual attempt.
