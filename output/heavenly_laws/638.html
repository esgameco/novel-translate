"I'm definitely interested," said Lin Yuan.

For a Celestial Sovereign, exploring and developing a newly born precious land, especially a top-tier one, is an opportunity that is hard to come by.

Currently, the six great ancient nations control only a few hundred top-tier precious lands in total.

And these hundreds of top-tier precious lands are the result of accumulation over a long period of time.

Ordinary Celestial Sovereigns may not have even experienced the birth of a top-tier precious land from the time they were born to when they broke through to the Thirteenth Realm.

"Haha," the Emperor of the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation showed a smile on his face.

As an emperor, he needs to weigh the forces on all sides. But when it comes to real benefits, he will definitely prioritize his own people.

That's why he was the first to meet Lin Yuan, suppressing the other Celestial Sovereigns waiting outside.

"However, although I have the right to decide who can represent the ancient nation to participate in the competition, you still need to display some strength later on."

The Emperor of the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation looked at Lin Yuan, "Otherwise, the other Celestial Sovereigns may not be satisfied."

"That's understandable," Lin Yuan nodded.

Indeed, in the competition for a newly born precious land, any participating Celestial Sovereign can gain rewards. But the one who seizes the precious land will gain the most.

If the inclusion of a quota leads to the failure in the final competition for the precious land, losing to other ancient nations, even the emperor may not be able to suppress the backlash.

Factional struggles must be placed behind the interests of the ancient nation - this is the rule of the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation.

"Hmm?" The Emperor of the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation looked at Lin Yuan, his expression somewhat surprised.

He could feel Lin Yuan's confidence.

"Normally, the strength of Celestial Sovereigns participating in the competition for a precious land must at least reach the threshold of Peerless Celestial Sovereigns. Do you have the confidence?" The Emperor's tone was slightly solemn.

Lin Yuan had excavated his ancestral bloodline to an unprecedented Celestial Sovereign level. According to the potential of the ancestral bloodline, his growth to the limit would be slightly stronger than a Peerless Celestial Sovereign.

But the prerequisite is reaching the limit.

Just like the core imperial clan at the Venerable Sovereign realm, if they haven't grown to the limit, they can't be called unrivaled Venerable Sovereigns.

"I will do my best," Lin Yuan said.

The Peerless Celestial Sovereigns of the six great ancient nations are equivalent to the supreme great saints of the Chaotic Void.

And before Lin Yuan's comprehension of the spatial-temporal rules breakthrough to the Ninth Realm, he could already contend slightly with the supreme great saints.

After his spatial-temporal comprehension reached the Ninth Realm, and he continued to ponder the Thirteen Realms Celestial Void Cauldron, as well as the comprehension of the Scorching Sun and Dark Yin in this source world, Lin Yuan's true strength would be far stronger than when he fought against the Chaotic Celestial Sovereign.

"Just do your best," the Emperor of the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation nodded. "I'll let those Celestial Sovereigns come in, and announce to the outside that I'm adding you to the competition quota for the precious land."

"If the other Celestial Sovereigns have major objections, I'll select one Peerless Celestial Sovereign to spar with you."

"You just need to hold on for a while, and prove that you have the strength at the Peerless Celestial Sovereign level, and there won't be any problems."

The Emperor of the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation looked at Lin Yuan.

"Understood," Lin Yuan acknowledged.

In the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation, the imperial clan can certainly obtain resource inclination, but the prerequisite is that they cannot be too weak, otherwise it would be a waste of resources.

"Then I'll let them come in," the Emperor of the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation glanced outside the hall.

Soon, powerful auras entered, all of them were the Celestial Sovereigns of the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation. Many of them had been in seclusion for many years, but now they emerged due to the birth of a new top-tier precious land.

"Emperor," the Celestial Sovereigns slightly bowed, showing respect, their gazes intentionally or unintentionally falling on Lin Yuan.

"Silver River?"

"Is he the imperial clan member who has excavated his bloodline to the Celestial Sovereign level?"

"The Emperor met him first, so the twelve quotas are probably already allocated, with one for him."

Many Celestial Sovereigns stared at Lin Yuan, their thoughts fluctuating. There were only twelve quotas to participate in the competition for the precious land, and Lin Yuan occupied one, which meant they had one less.

"Let me introduce," the Emperor of the Scorching Sun smiled, "This is Silver River, the first core imperial clan member who has reached the Celestial Sovereign level in our Scorching Sun Ancient Nation."

After the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation introduced Lin Yuan, they began to introduce the other Celestial Sovereigns.

In fact, there was no need to introduce them, as with the reputation of Celestial Sovereigns, Lin Yuan could at least sense their auras and know which Celestial Sovereign they were.

At this moment, there were more than thirty Celestial Sovereigns standing in the hall, of which twenty had the strength of Peerless Celestial Sovereigns, and the remaining more than ten also had strength close to that of Peerless Celestial Sovereigns.

Of course, this doesn't mean that the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation has more Peerless Celestial Sovereigns than ordinary Celestial Sovereigns, but rather that the Celestial Sovereigns present here are only a part, the most elite part.

Lin Yuan nodded at each of the Celestial Sovereigns.

Among the Celestial Sovereigns present, the three most powerful ones were recognized as Scarlet Flame Celestial Sovereign, Eternal Sun Celestial Sovereign, and Vast Scorching Celestial Sovereign. These three Celestial Sovereigns were all Peerless Celestial Sovereigns, and the paths of their evolved Chaotic force were extremely powerful, shaking the six great ancient nations.

"Alright, I won't beat around the bush," the Emperor of the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation looked at the many Celestial Sovereigns, "This time the appearance of a top-tier precious land, our Scorching Sun Ancient Nation will have twelve quotas to participate in the competition. As for the other eleven quotas, I don't care, but Silver River must occupy one."

"Do any of you have any objections?"

The Emperor of the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation said directly.

At these words, the hall fell silent.

The Celestial Sovereigns looked at each other, inevitably looking towards Scarlet Flame Celestial Sovereign, Eternal Sun Celestial Sovereign, and Vast Scorching Celestial Sovereign.

The Emperor's request was not unreasonable, just one out of the twelve quotas. They had even thought that more imperial clan members would be arranged to participate.

Among the imperial clan, there were also Celestial Sovereign-level powerhouses, but the Celestial Sovereigns here referred to those who had comprehended the path of Chaotic force evolution.

"Inappropriate," Scarlet Flame Celestial Sovereign suddenly spoke.

"Silver River Celestial Sovereign's potential is indeed worth cultivating," Scarlet Flame Celestial Sovereign looked at Lin Yuan, "but the competition for a top-tier precious land relies on strength. Every participating Celestial Sovereign must not be a drag."

"I can promise that if we seize this top-tier precious land, we can give Silver River Celestial Sovereign more of the precious land and its rare treasures. But for the competition itself, he still needs to prove his strength."

Scarlet Flame Celestial Sovereign finally looked at the Emperor of the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation, "I hope the Emperor will prioritize the interests of the ancient nation."

"Mm," the Emperor of the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation nodded, then looked at Lin Yuan, "Silver River, what do you think?"

If Lin Yuan had just expressed uncertainty about his strength, the Emperor of the Scorching Sun Ancient Nation would have definitely refused Scarlet Flame Celestial Sovereign's request with various reasons.

"Then let's go with what Scarlet Flame Celestial Sovereign said," Lin Yuan said.

At these words, the Celestial Sovereigns in the hall were stunned.

He agreed so easily? Scarlet Flame Celestial Sovereign also looked at Lin Yuan a little more.

He had originally thought the Emperor would find some excuse to fend him off.

But this Silver River Celestial Sovereign was so confident.

Although Silver River Celestial Sovereign had previously left a mark against Extreme Cold Celestial Sovereign and North Yin Celestial Sovereign, that was in the Black Jade Nest, with the spatial-temporal transmission array connecting the capital just nearby.

Whether Lin Yuan's true strength was stronger than when he fought against the Chaotic Celestial Sovereign, remained to be seen.

Three hundred and thirty-three spatial swords composed entirely of spatial power but containing overwhelming power, slashed towards the distant Red Lotus Celestial Sovereign who was suppressing the remaining spatial power in his right hand.
