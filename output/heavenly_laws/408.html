"What's going on?"

Lin Yuan stared at the towering and magnificent Door of the Myriad Realms.

He immediately realized that the fluctuation did not come from the Door of the Myriad Realms itself.

Rather, it was transmitted from the many worlds connected to the Door of the Myriad Realms.

The Door of the Myriad Realms was merely a 'transit' point, merely a 'medium'.

"Which world did it come from?"

Lin Yuan looked towards the bottom of the Door of the Myriad Realms, where the coordinates of ten worlds were engraved in succession.

They were the nine worlds Lin Yuan had traversed before, and the Immortal Realm he had yet to descend upon.

"The Azure World?"

Lin Yuan's gaze fell upon the world imprint of the Azure World.

The source of the fluctuation was indeed the Azure World.

Before Lin Yuan descended upon the Azure World, that realm was facing an invasion from the Outer Realm Demon Gods.

At that time, Lin Yuan took control of a branch of the Primordial Demon Tree, which was the progenitor of the Outer Realm Demon Gods, eventually replacing the Primordial Demon Tree and becoming the Heavenly Dao of the Azure World.

He became the absolute sovereign of that world.

Lin Yuan had a deep impression of the Azure World.

Because the so-called Azure World was merely a small world in the boundless void.

Outside the Azure World was the paradise of the Demon Gods.

Unfortunately, at that time, Lin Yuan did not have enough time. After replacing the Heavenly Dao of the Azure World, the time he could stay was almost up.

He could not continue to explore the boundless void outside that world.

However, due to becoming the Heavenly Dao of the Azure World, and with the help of the remaining body of the Primordial Demon Tree,

Lin Yuan could barely communicate between the two realms through the 'transit' of the Door of the Myriad Realms.

Even without descending upon the Azure World, he could receive information and get a general idea of the situation there through the Primordial Demon Tree.

When he had just returned from the Azure World, Lin Yuan was still quite interested and often inquired about its development.

But as time went on, he let it go, except for receiving regular 'reports' from the Primordial Demon Tree at intervals, he did not pay much attention to it.

But now?

The main body of the Primordial Demon Tree in the Azure World was actively transmitting information to Lin Yuan.

According to the contingency plan Lin Yuan had left behind, it would only do so in an extremely urgent situation.

"Hmm?"

Lin Yuan's consciousness immediately began to receive the message from the Primordial Demon Tree along the coordinates of the Azure World.

"The Outer Realm Demon Gods are invading again?"

Lin Yuan's expression became contemplative. The time flow ratio between the Azure World and the main cosmos was six hundred to one.

Six hundred years had passed in the Azure World, while only one year had passed in the main cosmos.

And it had been about three hundred years since Lin Yuan ended his traversal of the Azure World.

In other words, nearly twenty million years had passed in the Azure World.

"Are those two Outer Realm Demon Gods invading the Azure World?"

Lin Yuan thought silently. After replacing the Primordial Demon Tree, he had also received its 'relationship network'.

At that time, apart from the Primordial Demon Tree, there were two ancient Outer Realm Demon Gods lurking outside the Azure World.

Behind these two Outer Realm Demon Gods, there were even more powerful Outer Realm Demon Gods.

After Lin Yuan became the Heavenly Dao of the Azure World, he had some communication with these two Outer Realm Demon Gods and vaguely sensed that they seemed to have ill intentions towards him.

Because they had invited him more than once to explore a secret realm in the boundless void.

However, Lin Yuan had directly refused, as he did not have much time left to stay and only wanted to guard the Azure World steadily.

As for the opportunity to explore the secret realms of the boundless void, the risk was too great, which did not align with Lin Yuan's set goals.

Moreover, Lin Yuan was wary of those two Outer Realm Demon Gods. The 'relationship' between Demon Gods was almost non-existent.

If their strength allowed it, those two Outer Realm Demon Gods would unhesitatingly devour Lin Yuan.

"It has been tens of millions of years, why did they suddenly think of invading the Azure World?" Lin Yuan was slightly curious.

Demon Gods invading worlds also depended on the circumstances.

The Azure World had the Primordial Demon Tree stationed there, as well as many contingency plans left behind by Lin Yuan.

For those two Outer Realm Demon Gods to conquer the Azure World, they would inevitably have to pay a heavy price, which would be completely disproportionate to their gains.

Moreover, relying solely on those two Outer Realm Demon Gods, they would probably fail to conquer the Azure World. For the Primordial Demon Tree to contact Lin Yuan, they must have employed even higher-level means.

And such means were extremely precious, using them to invade the Azure World was simply not worth it.

"There must have been some changes in the Azure World during these tens of millions of years."

"Or some changes occurred in the boundless void, forcing those two Outer Realm Demon Gods to invade."

Lin Yuan thought to himself.

"What should I do now?"

Lin Yuan stroked his chin.

The Primordial Demon Tree had sent a distress signal, clearly stating that the situation in the Azure World had become extremely dire.

Far worse than when Lin Yuan had descended upon it and the Primordial Demon Tree was invading, the power of those two Outer Realm Demon Gods had already penetrated into the Azure World itself.

Under such circumstances, if Lin Yuan turned a blind eye, the entire Azure World would change hands.

"Forget it."

"I'll descend once more."

Lin Yuan made up his mind.

To Lin Yuan, there were still many unknown secrets in the Azure World, and many powerful and terrifying Demon Gods that devoured entire worlds.

Although the Azure World's upper limit was not high, the mysterious boundless void outside it had a much higher limit.

According to Lin Yuan's speculation, even if the entire boundless void was not on par with the Immortal Realm, it was not far off. The strongest Demon Gods there might reach the peak of the eleventh stage.

This time, Lin Yuan's decision to descend upon the Azure World again had three main considerations.

First, the Azure World was his backyard, where the path of martial cultivation was supreme, being the world with the most martial cultivators among all the worlds Lin Yuan had traversed.

After tens of millions of years of development, the martial cultivation system in the Azure World had become the only one, something other worlds could never achieve.

In other traversed worlds, Lin Yuan still had to consider the influence of the world consciousness, but in the Azure World? Lin Yuan himself was the world consciousness!

And within the Azure World, there were an incalculable number of martial cultivators, which would become a driving force for Lin Yuan's ultimate breakthrough in the future. He could not easily give up on them.

Second, he could take the opportunity to explore the secrets of the mysterious boundless void outside the Azure World.

When Lin Yuan had left the Azure World before, he was only at the seventh stage and lacked the strength to explore the vast and boundless void.

But now, Lin Yuan had reached the tenth stage, a transcendent tenth stage on par with the peak of the eleventh stage.

From the fourth level of the eleventh stage onwards, including the fifth level, they could all be considered the peak of the eleventh stage.

As for the sixth level? That was the perfection of the eleventh stage.

With Lin Yuan's current strength, he might not be the strongest in the entire boundless void, but he would not be far off. It was a good opportunity to descend and take a look, while also taking advantage of the different time flow rates between the two realms to quickly comprehend the fifth boundary of the fusion of space-time rules.

The final consideration was that the cost-effectiveness was too high.

With the status of the Heavenly Dao of the Azure World, Lin Yuan's connection between the two realms meant that if he chose to descend again, the Realm-Breaking Primal Essence he would expend would only be one-third of a strand.

That's right.

Not three strands.

Not even one strand.

But one-third of a strand.

One must know that if Lin Yuan chose to traverse again now, except for the Immortal Realm, he could only 'customize' his traversal.

Lin Yuan's strength was too high, and in the myriad heavens and realms, the countless dimensions, there were few worlds that could offer him any help.

If he chose to traverse randomly, he would probably gain nothing even after hundreds or thousands of attempts.

But the Azure World was just right. Although not on par with the Immortal Realm, it was cheap – only one-third of a strand of Realm-Breaking Primal Essence, what more could one ask for?

Moreover, those Outer Realm Demon Gods possessed many innate abilities and authorities involving various profound rules. Last time in the Azure World, Lin Yuan could only study the Primordial Demon Tree.

Due to the lack of quantity, this time he could capture more Outer Realm Demon Gods to study, which might help his cultivation.

That was the boundless void, a dimension different from the main cosmos and even the void of chaos.

The difference between dimensions was something even those great existences approaching the perfection of time and life could hardly comprehend.

That blank space within the low-dimensional space was a product of the exploration of low-dimensional worlds by existences approaching the perfection of time and life.

With the help of the Door of the Myriad Realms, Lin Yuan could study them freely, so he had to seize this opportunity.

Since he had decided to descend upon the Azure World again, Lin Yuan made some preparations.

When traversing worlds, Lin Yuan's consciousness would leave through the Door of the Myriad Realms, and his physical body would become an empty shell.

Of course, with the Door of the Myriad Realms maintaining it, even if Lin Yuan's consciousness was in other dimensional worlds, he could still sense the situation around his true body and primordial spirit. If necessary, he could completely terminate the traversal and forcibly return.
