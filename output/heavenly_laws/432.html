After all, from a core member to a sequence member, it wasn't like storming floors where the higher the displayed potential, the richer the rewards.

One only needed to meet the set conditions.

It's just that now, this goal was completed before his impact on the Eleventh Floor.

In the depths of the main hall of the 13th key territory of the human civilization.

Deep in his soul's memories, the various demonic evolutionary paths seemed to undergo a qualitative change, converging into that core member imprint of the Demon Jade Pavilion.

After his spiritual will reached the point where it could easily withstand the spiritual pressure within his body, Lin Yuan's comprehension of the demonic evolutionary paths was like making a thousand miles of progress in a day.
