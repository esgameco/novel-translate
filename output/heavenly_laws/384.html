Nalan Vice Chief curiously asked, "Galaxy Chief, your cultivation in the two-dimensional world should have crossed into the Wuzhu realm, right?"

Lin Yuan was the strongest genius in the history of human civilization. His talent and comprehension were unquestionable, so Nalan Vice Chief was almost certain that Lin Yuan had already become a Wuzhu.

It's worth knowing that in the two-dimensional world for over a hundred years, a considerable portion of the tenth-level lifeforms had just entered the Wuzhu realm.

Before descending into the early two-dimensional world, eighth-level, ninth-level, tenth-level, and eleventh-level lifeforms were all at the same starting point.

But as time passed, the gap between them would widen, and the gap would become larger and larger.

"Yes." Lin Yuan nodded. The eighteenth level of the Wuzhu realm was also a Wuzhu.

"In that case, our human eleventh-level evolutionary cultivators have compiled seventeen cultivation methods after the Wuzhu realm. I'll send them over for you to take a look."

Nalan Vice Chief immediately sent them to Lin Yuan.

In the two-dimensional world, special lifeforms were rampant, and human civilization was relatively weak, so they had to stick together.

Sharing cultivation methods with each other was common. As for the competition when the Heavenly Gate opened, as long as most of those who left the two-dimensional world were human evolutionary cultivators, human civilization would be considered the winner.

However, based on the current situation, this hope was not very high.

Not to mention those mysterious experts hidden in the dark, like the one from the Ancient Blood Rain Nation. Just the Four Celestial Kings alone had almost secured the spots to leave when the Heavenly Gate opened for the first time.

"Thank you."

Lin Yuan glanced at the cultivation methods Nalan Vice Chief sent over, his expression pensive.

The evolutionary cultivators who pioneered these post-Wuzhu cultivation methods were basically eleventh-level existences of human civilization. They themselves were the most astonishingly talented evolutionary geniuses of their era. The cultivation methods they explored were quite thought-provoking for Lin Yuan.

At least they were more thought-provoking than the native Wuzhu cultivation methods of the two-dimensional world.

"It's just that my cultivation method may not be suitable for you," Lin Yuan said hesitantly after briefly looking through them, gazing at Nalan Vice Chief.

The Wuzhu cultivation method Lin Yuan practiced was created based on his extraordinary comprehension and his own conditions.

The advantage was that it could excavate all the potential of his physical body at every stage, but the disadvantage was that it was only tailored for Lin Yuan himself. Other experts, even if they wanted to learn from it, couldn't.

In fact, the more than ten post-Wuzhu cultivation methods compiled by Nalan Vice Chief were also explored by many eleventh-level human evolutionary cultivators based on their own conditions.

Similarly, they lacked universality, but Lin Yuan possessed extraordinary comprehension and could observe the direction of cultivation methods from the most fundamental perspective.

"It doesn't matter," Nalan Vice Chief shook his head. Although Lin Yuan was the strongest genius in the history of human civilization, he was ultimately only a tenth-level lifeform. Compared to eleventh-level evolutionary cultivators, his perspective was narrower and closer.

"After the Heavenly Gate opens this time, I will specially compile some cultivation methods for you all," Lin Yuan pondered for a moment and said.

For now, his most important task was to enhance his strength as much as possible before the Heavenly Gate opened in two hundred years.

As for spending time specifically to create suitable cultivation methods for other evolutionary cultivators of human civilization, that would be deviating from his goal.

Such matters could wait until two hundred years later.

By then, Lin Yuan should have more free time.

Deep underground.

Lin Yuan sat cross-legged, his aura simmering like an abyss.

At a certain moment.

Lin Yuan was like an unyielding rock, not emitting the slightest aura, and then immediately, like a tsunami, a terrifying surge of blood energy radiated outward in all directions.

If he weren't deep underground, the impact would probably have alarmed all the powerful experts within thousands of miles.

"The nineteenth level of the Wuzhu realm."

Lin Yuan slowly opened his eyes, a faint smile appearing on his face.

After observing the Wuzhu cultivation paths explored by other eleventh-level evolutionary cultivators of human civilization, Lin Yuan's own accumulation finally reached a qualitative change, crossing into the nineteenth level of the Wuzhu realm.

"My current strength is about sixty percent stronger than the eighteenth level of the Wuzhu realm. To hunt down the ancient god Tuoba again, it would take at most two hours to penetrate the inner armor of the mystical artifact and kill him through shockwaves."

Lin Yuan felt the explosive surge of power within his body, his expression satisfied.

"I wonder what level the Four Celestial Kings are at now?"

Lin Yuan thought to himself. The Four Celestial Kings possessed extremely powerful weapons that were perfectly suited for them.

Under such circumstances, by using these supreme weapons to comprehend the power of this realm, they would probably far surpass other powerful experts.

"Time to go back."

Lin Yuan stood up, leaving the underground depths and returning to the Luochuan gathering place.

The Blood Rain Tavern knew nothing about this super expert, but Cha Ran Gu vaguely realized that this might be related to Lin Yuan.

After all, the time of this great battle coincided with when Lin Yuan disappeared.

Moreover, this incident happened after he mentioned it to Lin Yuan.

Just thinking about the horrific traces on the battlefield described in the intelligence from the Blood Rain Tavern made Cha Ran Gu's heart tremble.

Was that really the strength a human could possess?

"I need to continue my seclusion."

"Unless it's an important matter, don't disturb me."

Lin Yuan glanced at Cha Ran Gu and instructed.

Cha Ran Gu's emotional fluctuations were like an open book to Lin Yuan, clear as day.
