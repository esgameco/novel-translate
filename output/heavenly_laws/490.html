Yet Grandmaster Xia Qin discovered a large number of completely new rule models within the Martial Dao Eleventh Tier chapter.

Completely new rule models? A large number of them?

Grandmaster Xia Qin was filled with doubt and uncertainty, and immediately began to delve deeper into the Martial Dao Eleventh Tier chapter, studying each of the rule models it contained.

The other eight human grandmasters also did the same, focusing most of their mental energy on the rule models.

"This is the rule model of the Boundless Space Rule? This pillar-level fusion rule, our human civilization has only a few evolvers who have mastered it, and yet there is a rule model for it?"

"And there is this rule model, the Destructive Thunder Rule Model? The fusion of the Great Destruction Rule and the Thunder Rule? The Great Destruction Rule is already a pillar-level fusion rule, and it has been fused with the Thunder Rule as well? There is also a rule model for this?"

"The Time Shadow Rule Model? The model is not a problem, but how did the Galactic Overlord create these rule models?"

As they continued to understand, a tremendous wave of shock swept through the minds of the nine human grandmasters.

The establishment and perfection of a rule model first requires the attainment of the ultimate level of insight into the corresponding rule.

Otherwise, not only would they be unable to establish a rule model, they would not even be able to comprehend such a rule.

Secondly, the established and perfected rule model must possess "universality".

A evolution scholar who has just attained insight into the Time Rule may, through great effort, be able to establish a complete Time Rule model. But this Time Rule model would only be applicable to that evolution scholar, and would not be of much help to the insight of the Time Rule for other evolvers.

No matter what kind of rule, it is boundless.

Even for the most basic Gold Rule, the Gold Rule grasped by different evolvers who have attained insight into it is not necessarily the same. They are merely different aspects of the Gold Rule.

Rule models are derived from the summarization of the corresponding rules, and the more aspects they encompass, the greater their universality.

But while it sounds easy, it is extremely difficult to achieve.

However.

The nine human grandmasters continuously observed the rule models within the Martial Dao Eleventh Tier chapter, and were astounded to find that these rule models had an unbelievably high level of universality, completely reaching the standard of being fully disseminated among all the evolvers in human civilization.

But how could this be possible?

The rules corresponding to the rule models they saw numbered in the thousands.

The Galactic Overlord would have to elevate his understanding of these rules to the ultimate level in order to establish the corresponding rule models.

Thousands of rules? And at least all of them are pillar-level fusion rules? Attaining ultimate insight?

The nine grandmasters were momentarily dumbfounded, as they had not reached such a level of rule comprehension.

"The rule model of the Space-Time Fusion Rule."

At this moment, Grandmaster Xia Qin's mind shook, for he had seen the core part of the Martial Dao Eleventh Tier chapter.

That was the rule model corresponding to the Space-Time Fusion Rule.

The most important rule for any evolutionary path, including the Grandmaster evolutionary path, is the Space-Time Fusion Rule.

Other pillar-level fusion rules, you can choose to comprehend them or not.

For example, to break through from the Tenth Tier to the Eleventh Tier, you only need to elevate one pillar-level fusion rule to the Second Realm.

There is no requirement for which specific pillar-level fusion rule.

But the Space-Time Fusion Rule? To advance to the Eleventh Tier, the rule that needs to be comprehended is the Space-Time Fusion Rule.

Time and space are the two fundamental pillars of all rules, and the Space-Time Fusion Rule is even more so.

In simple terms, the Space-Time Fusion Rule is the "must-have" for every evolver who reaches the Eleventh Tier.

"The rule model from the First Realm to the Fifth Realm of the Space-Time Fusion Rule?"

"Three thousand three hundred and thirty-three rule models?"

"These rule models..."

It took the nine grandmasters more than an hour to fully examine the over thirty thousand rule models corresponding to the Space-Time Fusion Rule.

It was not that the nine grandmasters could not understand them, as their comprehension of space-time had reached the Sixth Realm upon entering the Twelfth Tier.

It was just that to determine whether a certain rule model had reached the standard for widespread dissemination, they not only needed to understand the essence of the rule involved, but also needed to examine the other aspects.

And the rule models of the Space-Time Fusion Rule were of utmost importance, so as the strongest in human civilization, they naturally wanted to review them several times before being satisfied.

"The rule models of the Space-Time Fusion Rule have a very high level of universality, reaching the standard for widespread dissemination," Guli Grandmaster said after a long while, his tone tinged with disbelief.

Before seeing the Space-Time Fusion Rule models, they had been both expectant and apprehensive.

But after confirming it, they were almost unable to believe it.

If the Space-Time Fusion Rule models came from the hands of Grandmaster Xuanyuan, the eight human grandmasters could have reluctantly accepted it.

After all, Grandmaster Xuanyuan's realm was there for all to see.

He was the strongest grandmaster in human civilization, with strength approaching the peak of the Paragon Realm.

But Lin Yuan?

A newcomer to the Eleventh Tier?

"The Galactic Overlord's rule comprehension must have surpassed ours," Xushen Grandmaster said after a moment of silence.

At least the nine grandmasters were unable to create and perfect so many rule models. They had been trying to establish the Space-Time Fusion Rule models, but were still far from perfecting them.

Yet now, in the Martial Dao Eleventh Tier chapter, they saw the incredibly perfected Space-Time Fusion Rule models, going all the way from the First Realm to the Fifth Realm.

"Surpassed us?"

Grandmaster Xia Qin and the other grandmasters looked at each other, somewhat dazed.

Although they had long recognized the potential of the Galactic Overlord and believed Lin Yuan to be the greatest evolutionary genius in the history of human civilization, surpassing them nine grandmasters who had not yet entered the Twelfth Tier, this was something they had not anticipated.

If Lin Yuan could break through to the ultimate and enter the Twelfth Tier, he might quickly become the second strongest after Grandmaster Xuanyuan in human civilization.

Or even beyond that, directly surpass Grandmaster Xuanyuan and become the strongest grandmaster in human civilization.

But the prerequisite for all this was Lin Yuan entering the Twelfth Tier.

Yet now, Lin Yuan was only at the Eleventh Tier, and his rule comprehension had already surpassed these grandmasters.
