"This is my Chaos Continent."

Looking down, Lin Yuan realized that the location he had chosen to establish the Chaos Continent was not within any void domain.

Rather, it was in the desolate void between void domains, the chaotic void, where 90% of the area was barren void.

To a certain extent, the barren void was the main body of the chaotic void, while the void domains were like small islands on the vast expanse.

The reason why cultivators mostly resided in a certain void domain was that the chaotic qi within the void domains was more concentrated, providing a better living environment.

"The spatial-temporal rules have already stabilized, and the other complex rules will operate and evolve on their own."

Lin Yuan nodded slightly. With his strength, it had taken him several years to establish this Chaos Continent, which was already considered a painstaking effort on his part.

Although the Chaos Continent was established in the barren void, the spatial-temporal rules that Lin Yuan had invoked had directly devoured the vast amounts of chaotic qi in the surrounding areas.

The notion that the barren void had sparse chaotic qi was based on the premise that the area of the barren void was too immense, and the total amount of chaotic qi was too low. On average, it would appear sparse.

However, if the chaotic qi from the surrounding vast areas could be concentrated in a small region, the chaotic qi would no longer be considered sparse.

Of course, most Chaos Supremes would not be able to achieve this, as the area involved would be too extensive.

Only Lin Yuan, whose spatial-temporal rules had reached the Ninth Realm, had the deep enough understanding of space-time to dare attempt this.

Normally, Chaos Supremes who wanted to establish a Chaos Continent would do so within an existing void domain, then occupy everything within that void domain.

In the distance, a large group of Chaos Venerable had long been observing.

Over the years, the news of the Silver River Sage establishing the Chaos Continent had spread from one to ten, and then to hundreds.

Chaos Venerables from thousands, even tens of thousands, of void domains nearby were rushing over, and even though most had not yet arrived, a large number had already gathered.

The number of Chaos Venerables was far greater than the scene when the Mysterious Yellow Universe was born.

When the Mysterious Yellow Universe was born, many Chaos Venerables had the self-awareness not to join the commotion.

But for the Silver River Sage establishing the Chaos Continent? Almost all the Venerables came, and if the main body did not come, their projections would still come, just to witness the subtle spatial-temporal attainments displayed by the Silver River Sage during the establishment.

"My goodness, the Chaos Continent established by the Silver River Sage must be about one-tenth the size of a void domain!"

"Pulling in the sparse chaotic qi from the vast surrounding barren void, making the chaotic qi in the Chaos Continent extremely dense, such a method, such spatial-temporal attainments, no wonder he is the Silver River Sage."

"The Silver River Sage is indeed benevolent, not establishing the Chaos Continent within a void domain, which would have squeezed the living space of the weaker tribes."

The Chaos Venerables exclaimed in awe. Over these years, they had witnessed the establishment of an unprecedented vast expanse of land, from nothing to something, and they had also gained a lot themselves.

The stronger the cultivator, the more awe-inspiring they found the methods displayed by the Silver River Sage. Perhaps the newly promoted Chaos Venerables only saw the establishment of a boundless continent.

But the slightly more experienced Chaos Venerables could see the interweaving of spatial-temporal rules and the operation of myriad great Dao on this continent.

Some of the peak Venerables could even see the radiation and influence of this Chaos Continent on the surrounding infinite barren void, like the radiant glow of a blazing sun.

"You all can come in and have a look."

Lin Yuan looked at the gathered Chaos Venerables and smiled.

The Chaos Continent not only served as a home base, but could also be developed into a trade and exchange center for the surrounding void domains.

Over time, it could accumulate a vast amount of wealth, which was in line with Lin Yuan's usual style.

He would absolutely not risk exploring secret realms and ruins if he could obtain resources through normal trade.

And allowing these Chaos Venerables to enter and observe would have a significant effect on establishing this Chaos Continent.

"Okay."

"Thank you, Silver River Sage."

"Then we'll go in."

The Chaos Venerables were excited and immediately began to enter the Chaos Continent in an orderly manner.

Although all the spatial-temporal aspects of the Chaos Continent were under Lin Yuan's control, the Chaos Venerables were not worried, nor did they feel that the Silver River Sage would do anything harmful.

Given the strength the Silver River Sage had displayed, if he wanted to attack, he could have done so outside without inviting them in.

"Congratulations to the Silver River Sage on establishing the Chaos Continent."

"This Chaos Continent is so vast, I wonder if we can take up residence here."

The Chaos Venerables who entered offered their congratulations and inquired about the possibility of commercial forces taking up residence.

With the reputation of the Silver River Sage and the scale of this Chaos Continent, it was inevitable that it would attract a large number of powerful cultivators over time, and the commercial settlement would also be a natural occurrence.

"I didn't expect to collect billions of Chaos Stones as a welcoming gift this time."

Lin Yuan sat at the center of the Chaos Continent, thinking to himself.

Billions of Chaos Stones, even for a Chaos Saint, was a considerable fortune.

"Since the Chaos Continent has been established, let's move the human civilization here."

Lin Yuan looked at the ancient Chaos Continent, the 'Shard World' of Star Tears, whose environment was not bad, but naturally could not compare to the current Chaos Continent he had established.

Hum.

Lin Yuan's thoughts stirred.

The entire Star Tears Shard World was then moved from the ancient Chaos Continent to the front of him.

Immediately, human civilization, under the guidance of the Nine Supremes and Three Goddesses, began the great migration anew.

"What's going on? I thought our original homeland was pretty good, why are we migrating again?"

Many of the evolved humans were still unclear about the situation.

But they soon received a detailed explanation:

"Our human civilization's Silver Galaxy Supremes have now established a brand-new Chaos Continent, and we are to migrate there."

Silver Galaxy Supremes? Establishing a Chaos Continent? A small number of evolved humans took a deep breath, as their strength was sufficient, and they had enough understanding of the information they had accessed, naturally aware of what establishing a Chaos Continent meant.

At least one would need the strength of a Chaos Venerable to accomplish this.

"Has the Silver Galaxy Supremes advanced into the Chaos Realm?"

"Not just the Chaos Realm, but the Chaos Realm has been perfected, and the current Silver Galaxy Supremes are now known as the Silver River Sage."

Within the human civilization, information and news about Lin Yuan began to spread.

This information had previously been sealed by the Three Goddesses, as it involved the pillars of human civilization, but now, with Lin Yuan's strength, there was no need to keep it hidden anymore.

"My junior brother has become a Chaos Sage?"

Among the migrants to the Chaos Continent, the Star Sovereign of the Crimson Flood and many of his peak disciples gathered together, discussing this matter with some disbelief.

"It's been such a short time?"

The Star Sovereign of the Crimson Flood was also shocked in his heart. He had long known that Lin Yuan would inevitably lead the human civilization to the peak, but he didn't expect this day to come so quickly.
