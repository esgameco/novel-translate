"This is actually a kind of cosmic power?"

Lin Yuan repeatedly observed and finally came to a conclusion.

The innate purple qi mastered by the old man in purple robes, if placed in the main world, would be a cosmic power that only an invincible Eighth Stage could master.

"So that's how it is."

Lin Yuan fell silent for a moment, suddenly realizing in his heart.

No wonder the Crossing Tribulation Immortal Kings of the Spirit Realm, without the support of an inner world, could rival the Eighth Stage evolvers of the main world.

The world power extended from the inner worlds of Eighth Stage evolvers allowed human evolvers to catch up to those innately powerful special lifeforms.

Very few powers of the same level could compare to the amplification effect of world power on evolvers.

But cosmic power was definitely one of them.

The combat power boost caused by cosmic power was even above that of world power.

The innate purple qi mastered by the old man in purple robes only grasped a portion of its power. Once fully mastered, even without world power, one could be on par with peak Eighth Stage evolvers.

"Those supreme Immortal Kings, without inner worlds, can rival invincible Eighth Stage. The Dao-forming power they grasp definitely belongs to high-grade cosmic power."

Lin Yuan thought to himself.

The invincible Eighth Stage of the main world also had varying strengths. Although the strongest could not defeat the weakest, they could still suppress them. The difference lay in the different cosmic powers.

Some cosmic powers were simply stronger than others, and the former were called high-grade cosmic powers.

"Opportunity."

"This is the biggest opportunity brought by my eighth crossing!"

Lin Yuan's eyes shone brightly. What was the greatest help brought to him by the eighth crossing?

Establishing the Eighth Stage of the Martial Dao?

No.

The Eighth Stage could be established and perfected anywhere.

Even without experiencing the crossing, given time, Lin Yuan could still establish it in the main world.

Comprehending the spatial rule model?

It was not much different from establishing the Eighth Stage of the Martial Dao.

It did not require the Spirit Realm.

Before knowing that the Dao-forming power grasped by the Immortal Kings was equivalent to cosmic power,

the greatest help the crossing to the Spirit Realm brought Lin Yuan was saving time, as well as learning from the immortal cultivation system and other cultivation systems.

But now,

after determining the essence of Dao-forming power,

it was an irreplaceable gain.

In the cosmic starry sky of the main world, each kind of cosmic power was unique and could not be replicated or simulated.

As long as a powerhouse comprehended a certain cosmic power, subsequent lifeforms would be unable to comprehend it.

This came from the rules of the main world.

The "rules" here did not refer to the rules of metal, wood, water, fire, earth, thunder, light, etc.

But the "restrictions" from the main world.

For example, under the Supremes, all lifeforms had a lifespan limit.

This was also a rule of the main world.

Another example was that in the cosmic starry sky, the fastest speed was the speed of light. Once exceeding the speed of light, one would enter a wormhole.

This was also a rule of the main world.

"The 'restrictions' of the main world do not apply in the Spirit Realm. At least for cosmic powers, the Spirit Realm is much more lenient. There is no saying that a certain Dao-forming power can only be mastered by one Crossing Tribulation Immortal King."

Lin Yuan's mind was slightly excited. This meant that in the Spirit Realm, he could fully comprehend a large number of Dao-forming powers.

Then, upon returning to the main world, these Dao-forming powers would collectively transform into cosmic powers.

In the long history of the myriad races in the cosmic starry sky of the main world, many invincible Eighth Stage powerhouses had emerged, but the vast majority of them only grasped one kind of cosmic power.

An extremely small number, due to various accidents, grasped two kinds of cosmic power, which was already the upper limit.

For normal Eighth Stage powerhouses, there were two goals: one was to step into the Ninth Stage, and the other was to grasp a certain cosmic power.

But for the invincible Eighth Stage, there was only one goal, which was to break through to the Ninth Stage.

Only by advancing to the Ninth Stage could their lifespan be extended. As for continuing to comprehend other cosmic powers, let's not talk about whether it was foolish or not. The key was, could they even comprehend it?

The comprehension of each cosmic power involved luck, without a fixed direction. How could you determine that the direction you were comprehending would definitely lead to the comprehension of a cosmic power?

This was also the key to Lin Yuan's excitement.

If he had not discovered the essence of Dao-forming power, even if he knew that the Spirit Realm did not have such great restrictions on cosmic powers,

he would not have had any other thoughts.

Comprehending more cosmic powers? You should know that before fully comprehending the rules of the Supreme Yang and Supreme Yin, Lin Yuan himself did not know that the power of Taiji would be a cosmic power.

Even with a heaven-defying comprehension ability, wanting to comprehend the next cosmic power would still waste a lot of time, causing Lin Yuan's comprehension in other aspects to slow down or even come to a halt.

With a clear comprehension of Dao-forming power, Lin Yuan only needed to spend time comprehending and perceiving, just like comprehending evolution paths.

Fully comprehending a Dao-forming power in the Spirit Realm and returning to the main world would result in a unique cosmic power.

"Innate Purple Qi... In the records of human civilization on the many invincible Eighth Stage powerhouses in history, no invincible Eighth Stage has grasped this kind of cosmic power."

Lin Yuan thought to himself. Of course, there might be unrecorded invincible Eighth Stage powerhouses who had grasped the cosmic power of Innate Purple Qi.

But the probability was very low, and what Lin Yuan planned to comprehend was definitely not limited to the cosmic power of Innate Purple Qi. He intended to go through all the Dao-forming powers grasped by the other Crossing Tribulation Immortal Kings.

"The explanation of cosmic powers by human civilization is of great help for stepping into the Ninth Stage in the future. Theoretically, the more cosmic powers one grasps, the greater this help will be. But it is not mentioned much because it is ultimately just a theory."

Lin Yuan thought silently in his heart.

Normal Eighth Stage evolvers, no matter how extraordinarily talented, could at most grasp one kind of cosmic power, or two at most. As for more?

It had never appeared before.

Because it was unrealistic.

"Fully comprehending a certain Dao-forming power is very difficult for other Immortal Kings. With that time, it is better to deepen the comprehension of the already mastered Dao-forming power. But I have a heaven-defying comprehension ability, it is only a matter of time."

A smile appeared on Lin Yuan's face.

He had already imagined that when he returned to the main world, grasping dozens of cosmic powers, coupled with the amplification of a vast inner world far surpassing the shackles of human evolvers,

he might even have the hope of arm wrestling with Ninth Stage powerhouses.

Historically, no matter how powerful an invincible Eighth Stage was, at most they could only preserve their lives in the hands of Ninth Stage powerhouses. As for arm wrestling?

That was seeking death.

Because the method for invincible Eighth Stage to preserve their lives in front of Ninth Stage powerhouses was to persist for a while without dying and then wait for the rescue of Ninth Stage powerhouses of the same race.

Under normal circumstances, invincible Eighth Stage had no ability to resist in front of the Ninth Stage.

"Let's fully comprehend this strand of Innate Purple Qi first." Lin Yuan's thoughts converged, and he began to seriously observe the strand of purple qi in front of him.
