The moment the five invincible eighth-level alien races emerged from the depths of the spatial layers, the space around them began to solidify, as if a flowing river had suddenly frozen. The creatures in the river seemed to be deeply trapped in amber.

To capture Lin Yuan alive, the first thing to do was to prevent him from escaping. In this regard, sealing the space was the most suitable method. Once the surrounding void was sealed, all means of movement would be rendered useless.

With the capabilities of the five invincible eighth-level beings and their comprehension of spatial laws, there were only two possibilities for Lin Yuan to break through their spatial seal. One was to fully comprehend the complete spatial laws and leave with absolute control over them. The other was to possess power far surpassing the combined strength of the five, proving that breaking through with force was not just empty talk. Even space could be shattered by absolute power.

"It's done," Sala of the Dark Rhino Clan felt relieved. Before they made their move, Lin Yuan's only chance of survival was to escape before the space was sealed. As a disciple of the Scarlet Roc Star Lord, who had a profound understanding of spatial laws, it was possible that Lin Yuan had been granted some means of instant teleportation and sent away in advance.

But now? The spatial seal had been formed, and Lin Yuan had not left. Sala of the Dark Rhino Clan immediately felt that everything was under control. Under the joint sealing of the five invincible eighth-level beings, even the Scarlet Roc Star Lord would be suppressed if he didn't leave beforehand.

"Quicksand," Sala's eyes turned cold. Misty yellow sand particles gathered and swept in from all directions, rapidly accumulating and increasing in power.

The power of quicksand was a cosmic force mastered by Sala of the Dark Rhino Clan. This kind of cosmic force was neither offensive nor defensive, but rather a domain-like cosmic force with endless suppressive and controlling abilities. This time, with him leading the five invincible eighth-level beings, it was precisely because of this cosmic force.

The Insectoid Clan wanted to use the poison of memories to thoroughly kill the Galaxy Star Lord, and the prerequisite was to suppress and control the opponent. The cosmic force mastered by Sala of the Dark Rhino Clan was very suitable. With the power of quicksand dominating the battlefield, the other four invincible eighth-level beings could basically lock down any resistance from the Galaxy Star Lord.

Of course, the resistance mentioned here referred to the combat power previously displayed by the Galaxy Star Lord.

The moment the quicksand power swept across, the other four invincible eighth-level beings also made their move. The cosmic forces wielded by the invincible eighth-level beings shook while reserving strength. Their goal was to control the Galaxy Star Lord, not to directly kill him.

Outside the Galaxy Star...

Suddenly, space solidified. Before Captain Nolan could react, a thick layer of quicksand power enveloped him. Captain Nolan was also an eighth-level evolved being, close to the peak of the eighth level, but at this moment, he couldn't even move. He could only sense that four unmatched auras had descended.

"It's four invincible eighth-level beings. No, it's five invincible eighth-level beings." Captain Nolan's scalp tingled. Usually, it was rare to see even one invincible eighth-level being. Now there were five directly.

Moreover, the killing intent emanating from these five invincible eighth-level beings was undisguised. Even though it wasn't directed at him, Captain Nolan felt his soul trembling.

"Galaxy Star Lord!" Captain Nolan looked at Lin Yuan, realizing that the target of these five invincible eighth-level beings was the Galaxy Star Lord. Apart from the Galaxy Star Lord, who else could be worthy of the joint efforts of five invincible eighth-level beings?

"So there were five more bugs hiding outside?" Lin Yuan glanced at Sala and the other invincible eighth-level beings. Before Lin Yuan stepped out of the Galaxy Star, these five invincible eighth-level beings were hidden in layers of deep spatial layers, extremely secretive. Unless deliberately checked, they were difficult to discover.

After all, Lin Yuan was now at the eighth level, considered to be at the same realm as the invincible eighth-level beings.

Buzz!

The next moment, with Lin Yuan at the center, a black and white yin-yang force expanded in all directions. Wherever the black and white yin-yang force expanded, the solidified space returned to normal. The five cosmic forces, including the quicksand power, were all shattered. Even the four invincible eighth-level beings who charged forward began to retreat, their eyes revealing a hint of fear as they looked at Lin Yuan.

"This power..." Sala's expression turned solemn. "Cosmic force, it's definitely a cosmic force. Galaxy Star Lord, you have comprehended a cosmic force?"

Sala found it hard to believe. Just over twenty years ago, Lin Yuan had just entered the eighth level, and now he had comprehended a certain cosmic force and become an invincible eighth-level being?

What... what kind of monster was he?

Although Lin Yuan had just entered the eighth level not long ago, he had cleansed the Galaxy Star Domain and displayed the combat power of the peak eighth level. However, the peak eighth level and the invincible eighth level were completely different concepts.

Moreover, Sala could sense that the cosmic force comprehended by the Galaxy Star Lord was definitely not simple. It belonged to the level of high-grade cosmic forces and was even much stronger than normal high-grade cosmic forces.

Just as the five invincible eighth-level alien races appeared, billions of miles away from the Galaxy Star, a bald man in white robes appeared, looking in the direction of the Galaxy Star. "Those alien races have indeed made their move."

This bald man in white robes was none other than the White Light Star Lord, a good friend of the Scarlet Roc Star Lord. Not long ago, he had even traded the cosmic treasure "Heart of Thunder" with Lin Yuan.

The White Light Star Lord was a senior at the peak of the eighth level, and his comprehension of spatial laws was not inferior to the Scarlet Roc Star Lord.

Therefore, the White Light Star Lord had a strong ability to save his life. The deeper the comprehension of space, the farther one could cross billions of miles in a single step and hide in layers of space, equivalent to being invincible from the start.

As a result, although the White Light Star Lord's combat power was not as strong as the invincible eighth-level beings, his means of escape were superior to them.

The most powerful aspect of the invincible eighth-level beings lay in their cosmic forces, but cosmic forces were just a kind of power. Unless they were cosmic forces specialized in escaping, they were indeed not as good as the White Light Star Lord.

After trading the Heart of Thunder with Lin Yuan, the White Light Star Lord mentioned it to the Scarlet Roc Star Lord, who then requested the White Light Star Lord to stay outside the Galaxy Star for a while to protect Lin Yuan's safety.

"Five invincible eighth-level beings?" The White Light Star Lord's expression changed slightly. If it were only one or two invincible eighth-level beings assassinating Lin Yuan, he could intervene and save him as long as Lin Yuan held on for a moment.

But five invincible eighth-level beings...

Even if these five invincible eighth-level beings' comprehension of spatial laws was not as good as his, quantitative changes led to qualitative changes. What he needed to do was not just escape but rescue Lin Yuan and then escape.

"It seems I can only use that treasure." The White Light Star Lord raised his right hand, and a cluster of white flames began to burn.

As the pinnacle race, human civilization had countless treasures. The White Light Star Lord, as a senior at the peak of the eighth level, naturally possessed many hidden techniques.

The white flames burning on his hand were a one-time treasure. Once activated, it would unleash a basic power close to the ninth level...

Galaxy's Star Lord.

Therefore, Eighth Stage invincibles placed great importance on methods of preserving their lives, and most of them had backgrounds, able to receive bestowals from even stronger existences.
