The longer the time drags on, the lower the possibility of breakthrough.

The era when the Master of the Demon Jade Tower was from was separated from now by long universe eras, basically there was no possibility of breakthrough.

It's just that there is a price to pay, and no time life form is willing to do so.

But Lin Yuan had karmic ties with the Master of the Demon Jade Tower, and had received quite a few benefits left by him.

If the price of breaking the time loop was not too great, Lin Yuan was willing to give it a try.

Of course, after the time loop is broken by external force, even if the life inside regains freedom, it loses the possibility of becoming a 13th level life form.
