So many stars hung there, the divine kingdoms of the gods of the Ocean of Light and Abyss of Darkness.

The number of gods is limited, so the divine kingdoms are limited, and the stars above the sky are limited.

In this world, some demigods carefully and stealthily collect faith, and may be able to escape the perception of the gods.

But if they take that step and become a true god, raising a divine kingdom, then it cannot be concealed.

Because the divine kingdoms are too conspicuous, transforming into stars, hanging high above the heavens. There are only so many, so the appearance of any new one is bound to attract the attention of the gods.

It's just that in the past few hundred years, or even a thousand years, no new stars have appeared in the sky.

In other words, no new gods have been born.

From this, it can be inferred that the Longshan Patriarch has not yet become a god, at least he has not raised a divine kingdom.

But without raising a divine kingdom and borrowing its power, why would the Longshan Patriarch be so strong?

The Pope of Order felt his understanding shattering. The Longshan Patriarch is not a god, so how could he kill gods like ants?

The Pope of Order calmed himself, collected his thoughts, and looked at Longshan Ling and the other core members of the Longshan Clan beside him, who were all puzzled and confused.

Danger?

Longshan Ling and the others breathed a sigh of relief, and at the same time became more and more curious.

"Those incarnations of the true gods were all dealt with by the Longshan Patriarch. The 'mediator' figures in the Longshan Patriarch's palm just now were those incarnations of the true gods."

The Pope of Order was silent for a while, then patiently explained.

He had a premonition that after today, the prestige of the Longshan Patriarch would resound through the Ocean of Light and Abyss of Darkness.

"Those things were incarnations of true gods?"

Longshan Ling and the others were a bit dumbfounded. "The incarnations of true gods are so weak?"

As soon as they said the last sentence, they reacted.

It's not that the incarnations of true gods are weak, but that their patriarch is too strong. Compared to the patriarch, the mighty and invincible incarnations of true gods appear feeble and fragile.

"I'm leaving first."

The Pope of Order took one last deep look inside the Longshan Hall, then turned and left.

What he had witnessed was too incredible and needed to be reported to the God of Order as soon as possible.

Actually, there was no need for him to report it. This matter couldn't be kept under wraps at all. The true gods whose divine incarnations were swallowed by Lin Yuan in one gulp had already blown their tops.

This matter would spread among the gods at a terrifying speed, and would not be limited to the Ocean of Light.

Moreover, their divine incarnations were on their way to Longshan Ridge to call that patriarch of the Longshan Clan to account when they were moved into a space cage.

With all the clues converging, it pointed to one possibility.

That patriarch of the Longshan Clan, whom they regarded as an ant and wanted to burn his soul in the divine kingdom for ten thousand years, was just a mere demigod.

To be honest, the gods had never taken the Longshan Patriarch seriously, even though he was the mastermind behind the disappearance of demigods in recent years.

But a demigod is a demigod.

No matter how powerful a demigod is, as long as he has not gathered faith and raised a divine kingdom, he is an ant.

But now.

The ant in the eyes of the gods had suddenly transformed into a towering dragon?

"Impossible."

"How can he be so strong without becoming a god?"

"Right, that Longshan Patriarch isn't even a true god, so why is he so powerful?"

Even with so much evidence laid out in front of them, there were still true gods who found it hard to believe.

They had gone through countless hardships and finally raised divine kingdoms and entered the Ocean of Light, becoming gods high above the masses.

And now this happens?

"I want to see the main god and find out the reason for this. Why exactly is that Longshan Patriarch so powerful?"

"Yes, but which main god should we go see?"

"Let's go see the God of Divination. There's nothing in the world he doesn't know. The God of Divination must know the origins of that Longshan Patriarch."

The true gods immediately got up.

Among the twelve main gods of the Ocean of Light, the God of Divination was the most unique existence, and also the one with the best temper.

The other main gods, such as the God of Order, the Goddess of the Magic Web, the God of the Seven-Colored Dragon, and so on, were all nearly absolutely rational, and when not sleeping, they were in seclusion.

Only the God of Divination would sometimes enlighten them.

Previously, the gods had sought the God of Divination to deduce the final location of those disappeared demigods.

Now it seems that this deduction was obviously correct. With the strength of the Longshan Patriarch, it would be too easy to secretly abduct some demigods.

"Could that Longshan Patriarch be a god who nearly fell and whose divine kingdom fell to the mortal world in the long ages past?"

Some true gods speculated.

In the distant past ages, there were several divine wars, and many gods fell, their divine kingdoms falling to the mortal world.

Of course, the probability of this possibility was pitifully low, and it was only a theoretical possibility that had not actually happened.

But the Longshan Patriarch was too powerful, so some true gods couldn't help but think in this direction.

"Whether he is a fallen god from the past or not, everything will be clear once we see the God of Divination." A true god replied.

They had absolute trust in the God of Divination.
