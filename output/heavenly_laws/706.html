The second Origin World.

In the center of heaven and earth, four or five majestic Origin Demons were fleeing, with incomparably dark and profound demonic energy sweeping around.

As Origin Demons second only to Demon Gods, since the descent of the Demon Calamity, such a sorry sight had never appeared before, let alone four or five Origin Demons together? Lin Yuan looked down at the four or five Origin Demons.

Space and time were scooped up.

The four or five Origin Demons appeared in Lin Yuan's palm like four or five ants.

Then they were instantly ground into billions of pieces by the Yin-Yang Taiji Great Millstone, turning into the purest energy, surging into the most microscopic particle worlds within Lin Yuan's body.

The pure energy transformed from the four or five Origin Demons was vast, instantly opening up over a trillion particle worlds. Lin Yuan's energy imperceptibly increased by a notch.

"Now the Origin Demons in the center of heaven and earth have all hidden away, making them hard to catch."

After successfully refining the four or five Origin Demons, Lin Yuan sighed inwardly.

Ten thousand years ago, Lin Yuan had made a deal with the Eternal Sect forces, promising to help them deal with the Origin Demons.

After that, Lin Yuan began hunting those Origin Demons from the edge of heaven and earth.

In this Origin World, most Origin Demons gathered in the center of heaven and earth, but there were still a small number of Origin Demons in the four directions of heaven and earth.

Lin Yuan's initial target was these Origin Demons located in the four directions.

Nine thousand years ago.

The Origin Demons in the four directions were hunted to extinction by Lin Yuan.

Lin Yuan's target then shifted to the center of heaven and earth on its own.

There, over 90% of the Origin Demons were gathered.

Compared to the scattered Origin Demons in the four directions, the Origin Demons in the center of heaven and earth, if not discovered in groups, would at least be encountered much more frequently.

Eight thousand years ago, 30% of the Origin Demons in the center of heaven and earth were hunted by Lin Yuan, and these Origin Demons also realized something was wrong.

Although Origin Demons had no thoughts or consciousness, they had instincts. After perceiving a large decrease in the number of their companions, some Origin Demons began to hide.

Until now.

In the center of heaven and earth, apart from endless demonic energy, the shadows of terrifyingly powerful Origin Demons could hardly be seen, as they had all hidden away.

Origin Demons originated from demonic energy, and demonic energy was everywhere in heaven and earth. Once Origin Demons sincerely hid within the demonic energy, even Lin Yuan had no way to locate and discover them on a large scale.

He could only search bit by bit.

"After ten thousand years of hunting, the particle worlds I've opened up already occupy 99% of my body."

Lin Yuan felt the vast energy within his body and nodded with satisfaction.

The physical body contains infinite sand-like particles, and to open up a world within each particle requires opening up infinite sand-like particle worlds.

What is meant by "sand-like"? It means endless.

Infinite sand-like particle worlds means endless particle worlds.

These particle worlds are maintained by runes, inheriting the cultivation of "Infinite Void".

It can be said that each particle world is comparable to the internal universe of a supreme great saint.

"Among the three true body avatars of Chaotic Void, the First Origin World, and the Second Origin World, this one is definitely the strongest."

Lin Yuan thought silently to himself.

In terms of pure strength, even if the true body avatars of Chaotic Void and the First Origin World were combined and enhanced dozens of times, they still couldn't compare to the true body avatar of the Second Origin World.

Lin Yuan, who was close to completely opening up infinite sand-like particle worlds, felt that he even had the ability to directly confront a 13th-level time lifeform.

"So far, opening up particle worlds hasn't encountered any major problems. I just need to wait until it's completely opened up, then I can apply it to the other two true body avatars."

Lin Yuan thought to himself.

Opening up one or two worlds from particles is very simple, but opening up infinite sand-like particle worlds? If there's a mistake, the consequence would be the collapse of the true body avatar.

So Lin Yuan is currently only trying this on his third major true body avatar.

Only after this true body avatar has completely walked this path and confirmed that there are no hidden dangers or questions, will he apply it to the other two major true body avatars.

As for whether it would waste time? For Lin Yuan, who has already opened up infinite sand-like particle worlds once, opening them up again wouldn't take much time.

Currently, what's limiting Lin Yuan's speed of opening particle worlds is resources.

And now, the two true body avatars in Chaotic Void and the First Origin World are already collecting large amounts of resources. As long as the third true body avatar succeeds, he will be able to complete the opening of infinite sand-like particle worlds on the other two true body avatars in a short time.

"In ten thousand years, besides constantly hunting Origin Demons, I've comprehended all the rune inheritances of the Eternal Sects and absorbed and digested the great paths walked by those Eternal Lords. Otherwise, it wouldn't have been possible to open up particle worlds so quickly."

Lin Yuan nodded slightly. The operation of infinite sand-like particle worlds within his body requires the maintenance of rune paths.

The more particle worlds opened, the higher the comprehension of rune paths needed. Otherwise, based on Lin Yuan's rune comprehension from ten thousand years ago, it would have been impossible to maintain the current 99% of infinite sand-like particle worlds.

Lin Yuan thought of the inheritance created by the Lord of Heavenly Light.
