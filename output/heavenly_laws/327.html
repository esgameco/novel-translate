Chapter 327 - Cultivation Results (Please Subscribe)

In the vast Martial Realm, since the first Martial God ascended here a hundred years ago, the originally silent and lifeless Martial Realm has gradually become lively.

The Martial Realm was created by Lin Yuan, and its scale far exceeds the Ocean of Light and the Dark Abyss. Even Martial Gods have not explored it to the end.

Over the past hundred years, with the ascension of Martial Gods one after another, the Martial Realm has gradually formed three regions.

One region is the gathering place of the Martial Gods of the Longshan family, centered around Longshan City.

Another region is the gathering place of Martial Gods outside the Longshan family, centered around Wanxiang City.

As for the third region, it is the cultivation place of the great Longshan Patriarch, occupying the center of the Martial Realm, where the Origin Qi is most abundant.

The spread of martial cultivation methods and the creation of the Martial Realm all originated from the Longshan Patriarch. Any Martial God who ascended from the mortal world has boundless respect for the Longshan Patriarch.

He is the peerless powerhouse who ended the era of gods and opened the martial era.

Nowadays, although the Ocean of Light and the Dark Abyss still exist, and new gods continue to be born, they are simply incomparable to the power of the Martial Realm.

[Name: Longshan Yuan (Lin Yuan)]

[Identity: Wielder of the Gate of Myriad Realms]

[Innate Talent: Defying Heaven's Perception]

[Divine Ability: Rebirth from a Drop of Blood]

[Divine Ability: Moving Mountains]

[Divine Ability: Strengthening]

[Divine Ability: Elemental Body]

[Current State: Descended Consciousness]

[Remaining Time: Three Years]

Lin Yuan slowly opened his eyes and glanced at the illusory text below his line of sight.

"The time limit for my stay is approaching."

Lin Yuan looked ahead, and everything that happened in the vast Martial Realm was reflected in his eyes.

As the creator of this world, nothing that happens in the Martial Realm can be hidden from him. It is almost equivalent to a second inner world.

"Back then, when I killed the gods, it caused a large amount of faith to overflow in the mortal world. In addition, after several times of popularizing martial cultivation methods, the first Martial God was born in just five hundred years."

Lin Yuan thought, Martial Gods correspond to the eighth stage of martial arts, but of course, there are also significant differences between the two.

The advantage of Martial Gods lies in the power of bloodline, so the cultivation speed is extremely fast before the seventh level. As long as the upper limit of the bloodline is high enough, there will be almost no bottlenecks.

After the seventh level, igniting the divine fire, in theory one should hibernate for a long time, secretly gathering faith, but with the fall of the gods, there is no need to be so secretive.

Moreover, with Lin Yuan's existence, martial cultivators are full of confidence. After the first Martial God entered the Martial Realm, a large number of other Martial Gods were quickly born.

In just a hundred years, the number of Martial Gods in the Martial Realm has exceeded one thousand.

"Among the one thousand Martial Gods, the number of Martial Gods belonging to the Longshan family is less than one hundred?"

But unfortunately, the geniuses of the Longshan family are not motivated enough and cannot compare to the geniuses from other places in the mortal world.

In fact, this is also very normal. Even with Lin Yuan's presence, the Longshan family is still just one family after all, and the number of family members is limited, naturally far less than the masses of the mortal world in terms of population base.

However, even though the Longshan family has only a few dozen Martial Gods, they still occupy an absolute dominant position in the Martial Realm. The Martial Gods outside the Longshan family simply do not dare to offend the Martial Gods of the Longshan family.

And all this, of course, is because of Lin Yuan, the Longshan Patriarch.

"Hundreds of years ago, some youngsters from the Longshan family begged me to suppress the Martial Gods outside the family and suppress the martial arts geniuses in the mortal world from the source of faith."

Lin Yuan's eyes were calm, and he naturally did not agree to this request.

Lin Yuan's goal has always been to better spread martial arts. As for the rise and fall of the Longshan family itself? He doesn't care much, as long as the martial cultivation method is still spreading in this world, it meets expectations.

As for limiting the development of martial cultivation methods for the sake of the Longshan family's dominance, Lin Yuan naturally would not do it.

Even for the Longshan family, Lin Yuan is gradually letting go. In the past few decades, he has not let his avatar return to Longshan Ridge in the mortal world.

"Their lifespan should be reaching the limit..." Lin Yuan looked down, and everything in the mortal world was in his sight.

With Lin Yuan's help, the couple Longshan Fang reached the most perfect state allowed by this world in terms of physique.

That is, the sixth-level Legendary Knight level, directly possessing a thousand-year lifespan.

However, after a thousand years, if the divine fire is not ignited, one will still face the fate of dying of old age.

The old age here does not refer to the decay of the physical body, but the decay of the soul and even the will of the mind.

With Lin Yuan's strength, as long as he maintains the nourishment of the inner world, the Longshan Fang couple could probably live for several thousand more years.

But the prerequisite is not to stop the nourishment. However, Lin Yuan will eventually return to the main world. Once he leaves, the Longshan Fang couple will lose the nourishment, and their souls and minds will quickly wither.

"Igniting the divine fire..."

Lin Yuan's eyes were deep. In this world, igniting the divine fire is of utmost importance, the dividing line between mortals and demigods.

To ignite the divine fire, one must rely on one's own unparalleled will and mind. External forces cannot play any role in this aspect, similar to the "Heart Demon Tribulation" from the seventh to the eighth stage in the main world.

Although in the past six hundred years, the number of martial demigods in the mortal world has exceeded one hundred thousand, it depends on what kind of population base it is built upon.

On average, there may not even be one martial demigod out of tens of billions of people.

Longshan Yuan's parents are just ordinary people and have not experienced any setbacks or hardships.

Since Lin Yuan's rise, their days have been very comfortable every day, and the possibility of igniting the divine fire is almost zero.

"Those who are not the strongest will eventually have to face the limit of their lifespan." Lin Yuan had an epiphany in his heart.

In the face of eternal time, the minds of weak creatures will gradually become distorted and decayed. Their cognition and understanding of all things will be affected by time.

For the same thing, creatures that have lived for ten thousand years and creatures that have lived for a hundred years may have completely different views.

The Supreme Immortal Kings and the gods have only lived for not long enough. If they live for another hundred million years, billion years, or ten billion years, they may go crazy and even have thoughts of suicide.

The mind and will cannot withstand the baptism of time.

Only the strongest, the ultimate existence of a certain path of evolution, a twelfth-level life, has an eternal and indestructible mind and will. They are the truly eternal ones.

Of course, even the strongest will die, but they will definitely not die due to lifespan.

"I possess the Gate of Myriad Realms and numerous life-saving means. It can be said that I will not die unless I seek death, but as long as I do not become the strongest, there will still be a day when I die, and no one will be able to save me then."

Lin Yuan thought in his heart about the strongest in the main world.
