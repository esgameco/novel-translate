Here is my full translation of the excerpt from Chinese to English:

However.

With a swipe of his card.

Lin Yuan's figure disappeared again.

When he reappeared, he was already standing next to Old Ten, casually stretching out his hand to take the box of purple spirit crystals, and then twisting Old Ten's neck to break it.

"By killing you all, don't all these things become mine?"

Lin Yuan closed the box and solemnly put it away.

"Let's go."

Seeing this, the rough-looking man didn't hesitate to dash backwards.

The remaining fifteen people also fled in different directions.

"Want to escape?"

Lin Yuan opened his mouth and gently inhaled.

Rumble rumble.

Terrifying airflows converged from all directions, gushing into Lin Yuan's mouth like the eye of a storm. Ancient trees within miles were uprooted and flew towards Lin Yuan.

The sixteen fleeing people were also restricted in place, unable to move.

"This...?"

The sixteen Cloud Mountain Bandits who had stopped saw this scene and their scalps went numb.

Is this still a human?

Is this still a human?

Just by inhaling, he could cause such terrifying chaos?

At this moment, these peak Shaman King level experts felt their understanding of the world was being shattered.

"Die."

Lin Yuan's figure disappeared, and in the blink of an eye, he twisted the necks of six Cloud Mountain Bandits.

"Explode for me!"

The rough-looking man, who was the farthest away, didn't hesitate to start burning his essence blood, causing his aura to surge violently as he crossed into the Shaman Emperor realm.

As the leader of the Cloud Mountain Eighteen Bandits, this rough man had many hidden tricks, and this explosive power increase was one of them.

His strength skyrocketed for a short time, entering the Shaman Emperor realm. Afterwards, he would have to pay a price, but it was worth it to stay alive.

"Go."

After becoming a Shaman Emperor, the rough man had no intention of staying behind to cover his brothers' retreat or stall Lin Yuan. He fled without hesitation.

Although he didn't know Lin Yuan's true strength, after forcibly entering the Shaman Emperor realm, the rough man still felt a terrifying pressure facing Lin Yuan.

This pressure was no different from when he was at the peak of the Shaman King realm.

"Escape, escape, escape."

At this moment, the rough man had only one thought in his mind.

After an unknown period of time, the rough man finally stopped.

Because not far ahead, Lin Yuan was standing there, quietly watching him.

"Sir."

The rough man's heart trembled. He had burned his essence blood recklessly to enter the Shaman Emperor realm, yet he still couldn't escape far from Lin Yuan?

Without needing to think, the rough man knew his other Cloud Mountain Bandit brothers were all dead.

At this moment, many thoughts flashed through his mind. The scene of the bronze compass spinning endlessly resurfaced.

"So...the compass wasn't malfunctioning." The rough man was struck with fear.

The reason the bronze compass's needle kept spinning without stopping for so long...

Was because for the eighteen of them, there was no path to survival.

No matter which direction they went, it led to death, unable to escape Lin Yuan's pursuit.

Thinking of this, the rough man couldn't help but kneel down and beg, "Sir, we shouldn't have made that deal with you. As long as you spare my life, I'll listen to you from now on and serve you for the rest of my days."

The rough man wanted to live.

If he could survive, even if it meant becoming a slave, he would gladly accept it.

"Serve me?"

Lin Yuan shook his head slightly, "No need."

Lin Yuan had killed seventeen of the Cloud Mountain Eighteen Bandits, who were said to be as close as family with an extremely good relationship.

Did the remaining rough man really have no thoughts about it?

Even if he was cowed by Lin Yuan's strength and dared not harbor any ill intentions now, what about in the future?

Lin Yuan naturally didn't want to leave such a hidden threat by his side, so he simply killed him to avoid future troubles.

As for subordinates and followers? There were so many natives in this world, did he really need to worry about not having enough people under him?

"You eighteen can go together." Lin Yuan raised his right hand and easily twisted the rough man's neck to break it.

"I..."

The rough man's eyes bulged, and his final breath quickly faded away.

"The Cloud Mountain Eighteen Bandits have all perished here," said Lin Yuan as he retracted his hand. "Six hundred million spirit crystals, plus that box of purple spirit crystals worth nine hundred million, one and a half billion spirit crystals in total, as well as these eighteen people's accumulated resources for breaking through to the Shaman Emperor realm - I've gathered enough to reach the Shaman Emperor boundary."

A smile appeared on Lin Yuan's face.

This trip had completely met his expectations. Originally he thought he would need to take a few more bounties, but unexpectedly it was accomplished in one go.

"Hm?"

Lin Yuan happened to search the rough man's body and possessions.

Suddenly.

A bronze compass fell out.

"Hm?"

Lin Yuan's gaze fell on the bronze compass, vaguely sensing an unusual aura emanating from it.

(End of chapter)
