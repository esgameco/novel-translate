Chapter 484 Surpassing the Nine Great Supremes (Seeking Subscriptions)

In the Qingyou Immortal City, there was the Zuimeng Pavilion.

As the most renowned place in the Qingyou Immortal City and even the entire Qingyou Immortal Realm, the Zuimeng Pavilion had its unique Zuimeng Immortal Wine, which could allow Golden Immortals and Jade Immortals to briefly immerse themselves in a well-crafted dream.

This immersion was not just a simple immersion, as it had great benefits for nourishing the spirit and will, and one could break free from it at any time.

It was precisely because of this that every day, Immortals from the Thirty-Three Immortal Realms would specifically come to the Zuimeng Pavilion to savor the renowned Zuimeng Immortal Wine.

"The cultivation system of the Martial Dao is indeed not simple, opening up an inner celestial world and absorbing the essence of the outer celestial world, using the physical body as the foundation. "That's right, I have an heir who is naturally endowed with divine strength and a robust physique, but his spiritual root is not good, so I had him cultivate the Martial Dao, and now he has already entered the sixth stage of the Martial Dao."

"I wonder what kind of person the Ancestor of the Martial Dao, who established the Martial Dao cultivation system, is."

"Unfortunately, more than a thousand years ago, the Ancestor of the Martial Dao had publicly preached several times, but I was in seclusion and did not go, so I never met the Ancestor of the Martial Dao."

In the Zuimeng Pavilion, a large number of Immortals from all over the Immortal Realm had gathered, and while savoring the Zuimeng Immortal Wine, they were discussing the major events happening in the Immortal Realm.

Among the many major events, the one that could most arouse the heated discussion of the Immortals was the Martial Dao cultivation system and the Ancestor of the Martial Dao who had suddenly emerged.

As for the Martial Dao cultivation system, it needs no further elaboration. After it was fully disseminated, it received the affirmation of many Jade Immortals, and its evaluation was no less than the Immortal Dao cultivation system.

In fact, the evaluation here completely underestimated the Martial Dao cultivation system, after all, the Jade Immortals who made the evaluation were Immortal Dao powerhouses, and they must have a "filter" towards their own Immortal Dao cultivation system.

As for the Ancestor of the Martial Dao? After more than a thousand years of dissemination, the Ancestor of the Martial Dao has already been recognized as the third Dao Ancestor-level powerhouse in the Immortal Abyss of the Immortal Realm.

By spreading the Martial Dao cultivation system and single-handedly ending the Immortal-Demon War, he has silenced the many disciples of the Immortal Dao Ancestor lineage.

Although the Ancestor of the Martial Dao has not made a move since the Immortal-Demon War more than a thousand years ago, one can still extract a lot of information from the reactions of the disciples of the Immortal Dao Ancestor lineage.

"I heard that in the Xuantian Immortal Realm, there is a Martial Dao powerhouse who has specially opened up an Immortal City called the Kaiyuan Martial City, where many Martial Dao powerhouses gather, and the disciples of the Ancestor of the Martial Dao all reside there."

"I've also heard that when the Kaiyuan Martial City was first opened, there were celestial anomalies, and the core disciples of the Immortal Dao Ancestor lineage all went to congratulate it."

"I wonder where the Ancestor of the Martial Dao is now."

The tone of the Immortals was filled with a sense of awe. Many ancient Immortals who had lived for billions of years had witnessed countless extraordinary powerhouses.

But the Ancestor of the Martial Dao was beyond the imagination of all Immortals. He was a Dao Ancestor.

Since the establishment of the Immortal Realm, even including the one in the Immortal Abyss, there were only two Dao Ancestors, and many Immortals even felt that a third Dao Ancestor-level powerhouse would never be born.

In the VIP Palace of the Zuimeng Pavilion.

This was a place specially reserved for distinguished guests of the Zuimeng Pavilion, and only the top-tier Jade Immortals were allowed to enter.

Lin Yuan was sitting here, and the discussions and debates of the many Immortals in the Zuimeng Pavilion naturally did not escape his notice.

Over the past thousand years, Lin Yuan's true body and two major divine souls had naturally been cultivating and enlightening the Dao, and he had also manifested a projection to roam the Immortal Realm.

The Lin Yuan currently sitting in the VIP Palace of the Zuimeng Pavilion was one such projection, who had come here casually after hearing the fame of the Zuimeng Immortal Wine.

"The evolution of the Martial Dao is progressing well."

Lin Yuan sat cross-legged, silently thinking to himself.

This could be seen from the discussions of the many Immortals in the Zuimeng Pavilion.

In the past thousand years, not many Martial Dao powerhouses had emerged, apart from Lin Yuan himself, the strongest one was still Liqing.

But the number of cultivators of the Martial Dao evolution path and the breadth of its dissemination had almost encompassed the Thirty-Three Immortal Realms.

Lin Yuan was already very satisfied with this, and with the current foundation of the Martial Dao evolution path, as long as there is enough time, be it hundreds of thousands of years or millions of years, it will eventually not be inferior to the Immortal Dao cultivation system and give birth to many Martial Dao Eleventh Stage powerhouses.

Lin Yuan gently closed his eyes, and his field of vision suddenly turned pitch-black, with vast swaths of starry lights scattered everywhere, and each point of starlight represented a Martial Dao cultivator.

At this moment, the starry lights that Lin Yuan could sense were vast and boundless, even extending beyond the Immortal Realm.

Lin Yuan was not only spreading the Martial Dao in the Immortal Realm, but also in the subordinate worlds of the Immortal Realm, such as the Spirit Realm, which had three thousand three hundred and thirty-three worlds.

Although the Spirit Realm is far inferior to the Immortal Realm, and even the three thousand three hundred and thirty-three subordinate worlds combined are not as large as a single Immortal Realm, as the saying goes, "Even a mosquito has meat," Lin Yuan will not miss any opportunity to enhance his ultimate breakthrough.

In fact, if there is enough time, Lin Yuan even plans to spread the Martial Dao in the Middle Celestial Continent, which is a subordinate world of the Spirit Realm. Apart from the Middle Celestial Continent, the Spirit Realm has hundreds of thousands of subordinate worlds.

The other three thousand three hundred and thirty-two Immortal Realm subordinate worlds are similar.

"Now, the number of beings cultivating the Martial Dao evolution path is probably more than the total number of cultivators of the Nine Great Supremes evolution paths in human civilization."

Lin Yuan thought to himself.

Just the Immortal Realm alone is comparable to one percent of the main universe in size.

Human civilization is one of the Twelve Peak Forces of the main universe, occupying an area roughly equivalent to one-twentieth to one-thirtieth of the main universe.

In terms of area, the territory of human civilization is three to four times that of the Immortal Realm.

But human civilization has Nine Great Supremes evolution paths, while the Immortal Realm only has the Immortal Dao cultivation system and the Martial Dao cultivation system, as well as the evolution paths Lin Yuan had spread during his past ten crossovers.

And the humans cultivating the Martial Dao evolution path in the main universe.

The total number of cultivators of the Nine Great Supremes evolution paths in human civilization is probably still not as many as the Martial Dao evolution path.

After all, the Martial Dao evolution path is not limited to the main universe, but extends to the boundless dimensions and realms of the Myriad Worlds.

Lin Yuan's cultivation time is still short, and the number of crossovers he has experienced is not many, but in the future, as the worlds he crosses over to become more advanced, the spread of the Martial Dao evolution path will become even more extensive.

"I can feel that the more beings cultivating the Martial Dao evolution path, the lighter the suppression of the river of time on me."

Lin Yuan silently thought to himself.

This feeling was not so obvious before his journey to the Immortal Realm, but became clearer after the Martial Dao evolution path was fully disseminated in the Immortal Realm.

The endless Martial Dao cultivators were collectively supporting Lin Yuan, and this support was not the kind of support from divine faith, but a support under Lin Yuan's control.

Just as Lin Yuan was examining himself,

A graceful Jade Immortal woman slowly walked into the palace.
