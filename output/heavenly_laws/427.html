In order to reduce the possibility of any accidents occurring as much as possible, Lin Yuan naturally had to make preparations by having all the evil gods swear the corresponding oath.

The so-called oath was a term from the Spiritual Realm, and in the Boundless Void it was called a heart oath. By swearing it, one would resonate with the rules of the Great Dao, thereby achieving the purpose of constraining oneself.

Lin Yuan had these foreign evil gods swear the oath instead of directly killing them to eliminate future troubles. This was in order to give martial cultivators sufficient pressure.

With pressure comes motivation, allowing martial arts to develop more vigorously.

As for why the constraint was for a hundred billion years - if after a hundred billion years, martial arts still could not produce a single cultivator on par with a ninth stage evil god, then Lin Yuan really should reflect on whether there was a problem with the martial arts system he created.

After a hundred billion years, even with the six-hundred-fold difference in the flow of space-time between the Boundless Void and the main universe, several million years would have passed in the main universe.

If Lin Yuan had not successfully assaulted the ultimate by then, he would likely have failed and his path would be extinguished upon death.

In such a situation, the outcome of the martial cultivation system would no longer be something Lin Yuan could be concerned about.

"Okay," the Child of the Void nodded without hesitation.

The other perfected evil god sovereigns below also showed no reluctance.

Are you kidding? Even without the constraining oath, with the Martial Ancestor Lin Yuan watching over them, these evil gods would not dare lay a hand on any martial cultivator no matter how daring they were.

Soon, all the evil gods swore their oaths before Lin Yuan, triggering a resonance in the Boundless Void.

After giving the many evil gods one last look, Lin Yuan's form disappeared without a sound.

A long time after Lin Yuan left, the Child of the Void finally dared to straighten his back and look around.

"The Martial Ancestor has left?"

The Child of the Void let out a huge sigh of relief in his heart.

"Just what level of power does the Martial Ancestor possess?" The Child of the Void gulped.

Before he broke through to the tenth stage, facing the Martial Ancestor required enduring tremendous pressure.

But now that he had broken through to the tenth stage, facing the Martial Ancestor brought even greater pressure than before his breakthrough.

"Could it be...the eleventh stage? The twelfth stage?"

The Child of the Void quickly thought to himself. He could only speculate in this direction, because the power Lin Yuan displayed seemed like divine punishment compared to a normal tenth stage existence.

Not only had the Child of the Void crossed into the tenth stage himself, but the information left behind by the secret realm's founder also contained descriptions of the tenth stage.

After crossing into the tenth stage, the Child of the Void felt he matched the founder's descriptions quite well. But Lin Yuan?

The Child of the Void could not understand.

"The great Child of the Void."

Just as the Child of the Void's thoughts were in disarray, one of the perfected evil god sovereigns below could not help but speak up tentatively.

This time they had come to congratulate the Child of the Void on crossing into the tenth stage, allowing them to hold their heads high before that Martial Ancestor.

"You all can withdraw."

The Child of the Void felt indifferent, feeling it might have been better if he had not broken through to the tenth stage.
