The virtual world.

Xia Qinzhizhe, the supreme being, sat solemnly on the throne, his expression grave. "You mean to say that the 'blank paper' already has an owner?"

In the long years of the main universe's past, objects from outside the universe would often squeeze through the permanent spatial cracks.

The vast majority of these extra-universal objects were 'inanimate'.

For example, the extra-universal aurora that appeared not long ago.

But a small portion were 'living beings'.

During the dormant years of human civilization, on the large battlefield opened up by the Insect Race and the Feathered Race, an anomalous life form squeezed through one of those permanent spatial cracks.

This anomalous life form nearly brought an irreversible disaster to the universe's starry realm.

In the end, more than a dozen supreme beings joined forces to barely banish that anomalous life form back outside the universe.

Compared to 'inanimate objects', 'living beings' had greater unpredictability, enough to put even ultimate existences on guard.

If what Lin Yuan said was correct, that the 'blank paper' already had an owner, and that owner was inside the 'blank paper' itself, then they, as ultimate existences, would have to re-evaluate how to deal with it.

"Can't say it has an owner, at least it has given birth to a 'spirit' with an independent will," Lin Yuan stated his conjecture.

According to the half-meter-tall old man, the two-dimensional world within this 'blank paper' was created by a great existence close to a perfect temporal life.

But the half-meter-tall old man himself did not seem to be close to a perfect temporal life, at most he was similar to an existence like a 'spirit', or a 'butler' casually created by the near-perfect temporal life.

"As long as it has an independent will, it counts as a 'living being'," Xia Qinzhizhe, the supreme being, frowned. "No wonder for these past hundred years, our repeated attempts to refine it have had no effect whatsoever."

With the means of an ultimate existence, if it were truly an ownerless object, not to mention being completely refined, there should have at least been some hope.

But in reality, their attempts to refine the 'blank paper' had received absolutely no response.

"There's one more thing," Lin Yuan hesitated for a moment before continuing, "The evolutionary trailblazers of my human civilization within the two-dimensional world probably can't come out."

Lin Yuan then repeated what the half-meter-tall old man had said.

"Of all the lifeforms that fall into the two-dimensional world, only one can eventually leave."

Lin Yuan stated truthfully.

"I see, so that's how it is," Xia Qinzhizhe fell silent for a moment before quickly accepting it.

If not for Lin Yuan, none of the evolutionary trailblazers of human civilization would be able to leave following normal development.

If Lin Yuan had never fallen into the two-dimensional world, then it would have been a stage for special lifeforms, and the eventual victor would only be legendary special lifeforms like the Four Divine Kings.

That Lin Yuan became the final victor of the two-dimensional world already satisfied Xia Qinzhizhe immensely - at least they didn't have to face a fundamental loss.

Moreover, Lin Yuan had mentioned that the final victor would receive a corresponding reward.

Of course, Xia Qinzhizhe didn't ask about the specifics of the reward, as that was Lin Yuan's personal opportunity.

"Not bad at all."

"Don't feel burdened, that they can't come out is not because of you."

Xia Qinzhizhe consoled Lin Yuan with a few words.

It was indeed true that even with Lin Yuan's appearance, he had recovered some of the losses.

After all, he had brought out many wondrous objects. Even if Lin Yuan took away half of them, and the human civilization took away a portion of the remaining half, what was left in the hands of each human evolutionary trailblazer would still be considered immensely precious.

Although they could not restore the fundamental losses suffered by the many human evolutionary trailblazers, they could make up for most of it in other ways.

"No one else knows about this, right?" Xia Qinzhizhe suddenly asked.

"It seems not," Lin Yuan pondered for a moment before nodding. "Even for me, it was only from the increasing difficulty when the Heavenly Gate opened twice that I vaguely deduced something like this was happening. "As for the other myriad races? They never even had a chance to come into contact with the Heavenly Gate, let alone think in this direction or know about this."

The two-dimensional world.

Lin Yuan obediently sat in the corner, secretly observing his surroundings.

"Are you wondering where those lifeforms that entered the Heavenly Gate in the past are now?" The half-meter-tall old man glanced at Lin Yuan in front of the huge painting and casually said.

"I'm just a bit curious," Lin Yuan did have such thoughts.

How long had the history of the two-dimensional world lasted? It was probably calculated in cosmic eras.

According to the Heavenly Gate opening once every ten thousand years, there should have been many lifeforms leaving the two-dimensional world.

"The creator of this low-dimensional space, that great existence close to a perfect temporal life, was benevolent. He believed that even tiny low-dimensional lifeforms should have the freedom to break free from everything."

"That's why the Heavenly Gate opens once every ten thousand years," the half-meter-tall old man explained.

"Any lifeform that enters the Heavenly Gate can shed the nature of a low-dimensional lifeform. I usually toss them into a nearby universe."

"Oh, I've tossed quite a few into your universe too. Most of them are already dead now," the half-meter-tall old man casually said.

The initial intention behind the creation of this low-dimensional space was to explore even lower-dimensional worlds. Secondly, it was discovered that by projecting a sliver of spiritual will into it, it could conduct a battle of insights from the same starting point.

The latter gradually became the main purpose of this low-dimensional space. Over the long years of constant refinement, it gave birth to a complete low-dimensional world within, where countless low-dimensional lifeforms multiplied.

Later, after a great calamity, this low-dimensional space fell dormant within the Taiyan Ruins. Eon after eon passed, until it was eventually ejected from the Taiyan Ruins by the various forces within, automatically beginning to absorb all nearby lifeforms into the two-dimensional world.

As long as there were enough lifeforms that fell into the two-dimensional world, this low-dimensional space would slowly come to a halt and begin a new 'game'.

These were like fixed 'programs' engraved in the deepest part of the 'blank paper', which even the half-meter-tall old man had to follow.

Hearing this, Lin Yuan fell into a brief silence.

In fact, escaping the low-dimensional space was not necessarily a good thing for those lifeforms within the two-dimensional world.

The Demon Emperors and Demon Patriarchs who dominated in the two-dimensional world might become extremely weak in the main universe. Their upper limits would be raised, but how many could actually reach those limits?

Just as the half-meter-tall old man said, the vast majority of low-dimensional lifeforms tossed into nearby universes would die in a short time, and the few who persisted for a while would also eventually die.

"Hm?"

Lin Yuan gazed towards the distant huge painting and vaguely sensed a change in it.

"The flow of time?"

Lin Yuan's expression turned solemn as he felt the fluctuations in the flow of time within the painting, beginning to surge rapidly.

"To take away all the 'insights into the rules' from the game participants, they naturally need to die completely," the half-meter-tall old man rarely explained.

"A hundredfold increase in the flow of time is really too slow. Let me speed it up."

Just as the half-meter-tall old man finished speaking, Lin Yuan saw that the flow of time within the painting had already reached a million to one, ten million to one ratio.

In the two-dimensional world.

The Four Divine Kings and many other powerful beings of the myriad races witnessed Lin Yuan enter the Heavenly Gate, which then quickly dissipated.

They each left Heavenly Mountain, waiting for the next opening of the Heavenly Gate.

As eleventh-step lifeforms, their insights were naturally among the best. After expending a great deal of time and effort, they had also comprehended some longevity techniques.

Combined with the longevity of the Demon Patriarch's Thirtieth and Fortieth Realms, enduring tens of thousands or hundreds of thousands of years was not difficult.

"Next time the Heavenly Gate opens, the one entering will definitely be me," the Four Divine Kings were full of confidence.

At this moment, the Four Divine Kings' expressions changed slightly.

Relying on the 'coordinates' of their true bodies outside, they clearly felt the change in the flow of time within the two-dimensional world.

From the original hundredfold, it began to surge infinitely.

In the span of a dozen minutes in the main universe, ten thousand years had passed in the two-dimensional world, heralding the third opening of the Heavenly Gate.

This time, when the Heavenly Gate opened, the Four Divine Kings swept across all directions and stood before the gate.

However, what awaited them was an immovable Heavenly Gate.

Ten thousand years passed.

Twenty thousand years.

Fifty thousand years.

A hundred thousand years.

The Four Divine Kings stood before the Heavenly Gate time and again, only to find themselves unable to open it. In the end, they could only grow old and die in the two-dimensional world with immense resentment.

"What's going on?"

"Why did this 'blank paper' undergo such changes?"

"Why is there such a vast difference in the flow of time?"
