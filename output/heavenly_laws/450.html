Chapter 450 The Might of the 'World' (Seeking Subscriptions)

The first large-scale war after the opening of the super-large battlefield has also attracted the attention of the Twelve Ultimates.

"The Nightmare Realm of the Insect Tribe was forged using the precious Nightmare Heart of the Twelve Ultimate Realm."

"The Nightmare Heart originally drifted from the Chaotic Void into the universe and fell into the hands of the Insect Tribe's Wanhua Empress."

"With the Nightmare Heart as the main material, the Nightmare Realm was forged, possessing an incredible suppressive effect on the soul."

The Twelve Ultimates observed the core battlefield and exchanged views.

The Nightmare Heart was not a treasure that could be nurtured by the universe; it came from the even more vast Chaotic Void, innately suppressing any soul power.

The Insect Tribe's Wanhua Empress spent tremendous effort and many auxiliary materials to create the Nightmare Realm.

This tribal weapon in the hands of the Insect Tribe Empress could unleash power no less than the 'Virtual World' of human civilization.

"The Nightmare Realm?"

The nine human civilization Supremes, including Xia Qin, glanced at the Insect Tribe Alliance.

"No problem," said the silent Xuanyuan Supreme. "The Eleven Realms cannot fully unleash the power of the Nightmare Realm."

The more powerful the supreme weapon, the higher the requirements for the user.

For weapons like the Nightmare Realm and the Angel's Holy Crown, when grasped by Eleven Realm beings through 'tricks', the power they can unleash is only slightly stronger than ordinary supreme weapons.

The Xuanyuan Supreme had given Xuanyuan Yu many backup treasures. Not only could they shatter the Nightmare Realm, they could at least block it without any issues.

Even before the opening of the super-large battlefield, human civilization had already conducted many simulations and predictions of the means of the other eleven peak tribal forces.

The Insect Tribe's Nightmare Realm was completely within their expectations.

"But... the Galactic Lord," the Xuanyuan Supreme muttered with slight doubt.

However, the next moment, a figure plummeted into the center of the battlefield between human civilization and the Insect Tribe.

This figure was the Galactic Lord. With a light stomp of his foot, the terrifying spatiotemporal fluctuations formed a grinding millstone, tearing and crushing countless Insect Tribe warriors.

The Eleven Realm Insect Tribe Hives seemed like small boats floating on the sea, about to capsize.

Deep within the Insect Tribe Alliance, the Hive that was activating the 'Nightmare Realm' retreated greatly.

The 'Palace' floating above, constantly emitting void fluctuations, also began to spin rapidly, as if receiving a violent impact.

"This..."

For a moment, the gaze of all the Twelve Ultimates converged on the battlefield between human civilization and the Insect Tribe.

Previously, most of the Twelve Ultimates had been focused on the battlefields of the Calamity Tribe, the Void, and the Chaos, as the fighting there was the most intense, especially the strongest Eleven Realm of the Calamity Tribe, who was basically suppressing the other peak tribal forces single-handedly.

Many Twelve Ultimates planned to glimpse the methods of the Calamity Tribe's two Twelve Ultimates from the strongest Eleven Realm.

But now?

"The Galactic Lord? Just a stomp of his foot, and he almost swept away the Insect Tribe Alliance?"

"Even the Nightmare Realm was impacted and its soul suppression was interrupted?"

"What treasure did the Galactic Lord use? Could it be that he brought the true foundation of human civilization?"

The Twelve Ultimates looked at each other in disbelief. At least the battle power the Galactic Lord displayed now was at the level of the strongest Eleven Realm of the Calamity Tribe.

The strongest Eleven Realms of the major tribal groups, each wielding tribal weapons, had to barely hold back the strongest Eleven Realm of the Calamity Tribe.

In the center of the battlefield between human civilization and the Insect Tribe, Lin Yuan looked around. The reason he waited until now to appear was that in the initial large-scale battle, there were still many Eleven Realms who had not yet entered Lin Yuan's killing range.

This killing range was a circle with Lin Yuan as the center and a radius of 700 billion li. This was the limit of the world within Lin Yuan's body, and after activating the 'World' divine ability, everything within this 700 billion li became Lin Yuan's world.

This did not mean that Lin Yuan's attack range was limited to 700 billion li. Beyond this range, he did not have the absolute certainty to kill.

After all, among the Eleven Realm experts who had entered the battlefield, a considerable number possessed supreme weapons to protect them.

Lin Yuan faced thousands of enemies, his power dispersed, and with the 700 billion li distance, he did not have the confidence to kill a crazily fleeing Eleven Realm expert with a supreme weapon.

But within the 700 billion li, it would be different.

Within the range of the 'World' divine ability, Lin Yuan's strength would be fully unleashed.

"It's about time," Lin Yuan said calmly, also sensing the gaze of tens of thousands of Eleven Realm experts.

Just now, when he descended, a mere stomp of his foot had caused the entire Insect Tribe Alliance to be thrown into disarray, a scene that was also witnessed by the other peak tribal groups.

Such extraordinary strength left all the Eleven Realm experts dumbfounded.

"Divine Ability: World!"

Lin Yuan's thoughts stirred, and the imprint of the 'World' divine ability deep within his body suddenly began to vibrate and expand. Threads of world power instantly permeated the 700 billion li range, centered on Lin Yuan.

Since entering the super-large battlefield, Lin Yuan had been suppressing his true strength, waiting for this moment.

If he had revealed his true power earlier, how could these Eleven Realm experts from the various peak tribal groups have gathered here obediently, allowing Lin Yuan to slaughter them?

Although with Lin Yuan here, he would ultimately be able to take control of the super-large battlefield war, if he could not kill those Eleven Realm experts from other tribes, where would he get the resources to cultivate the 'Mysterious Yellow Body'?

Boom!

A heavy, oppressive aura instantly enveloped the 700 billion li area, and the tens of thousands of Eleven Realm experts immediately felt that their physical bodies and souls were being completely suppressed.

This was not ordinary suppression, but total suppression, with their entire strength reduced to almost nothing.

The 'World' divine ability, vast and mighty, with Lin Yuan's current strength, could instantly crush those ordinary Eleven Realm experts under its domain.

Even Eleven Realm Perfected experts with supreme weapons could only withstand it for a few breaths.

"What's going on?"

"My strength has been suppressed by 99%?"

"It's the Galactic Lord, the source of this suppression is the Galactic Lord!"

The thought processes of the Eleven Realm experts were extremely fast. They immediately realized that something was amiss, from the Galactic Lord's descent to the battlefield to the current moment, it had only been an instant.

In the previous moment, they were still shocked by the Galactic Lord's stomp, and in the next moment, they had all become trapped fish in the 'World' divine ability.

"Retreat, immediately retreat from the Galactic Lord."

"Retreat from the Galactic Lord, see if we can escape this suppression."

The many Eleven Realm experts almost simultaneously made the decision to flee.
