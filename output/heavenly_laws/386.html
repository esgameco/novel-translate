The miraculous nature of the Bloodrain Tea Tree was no secret to the upper echelons of the Bloodrain Ancient Kingdom.

After realizing the value of the wondrous objects in the two-dimensional world, the Bloodrain Tea Tree would definitely not be ignored and had long been targeted by powerful experts.

It was something the Bloodrain Ancient Kingdom could not keep.

"Could it be that the Bloodrain Ancient Kingdom is hiding other wondrous objects?" Lin Yuan became interested.

The Bloodrain Ancient Kingdom's royalty had stated they had treasures to offer, so Lin Yuan naturally associated it with this direction.

"Never mind."

"I'll just meet them."

Lin Yuan made up his mind, since it involved a wondrous object, it was appropriate to meet them.

Without Lin Yuan's permission, even the Bloodrain Ancient Kingdom's royalty would not dare enter the Luochuan gathering grounds on their own.

Cha Ranggu went to notify the Bloodrain Ancient Kingdom's royalty, and there was still some time before they could have an audience with Lin Yuan.

Lin Yuan continued to take out the silver mirror and channel his spiritual power into it.

Hum hum hum.

Accompanied by Lin Yuan expending a great deal of spiritual power,

The silver mirror in front of him gradually revealed its extraordinary nature.

Faint traces of silver radiance began to appear.

At the same time,

Lin Yuan sensed an extremely familiar fluctuation.

A spatial fluctuation.

It was purely a spatial fluctuation, not involving any spatial rules.

"Interesting."

Seeing this, Lin Yuan continued to maintain the infusion of his spiritual will.

Only experts at the nineteenth level of the Shaman Ancestor realm could possess such abundant spiritual power.

Those at the fifteenth or sixteenth level could not even meet the prerequisite to activate this silver mirror.

Time passed.

Half a day went by in the blink of an eye.

Lin Yuan's brow furrowed slightly.

Continuously maintaining the infusion of his spiritual will like this, even Lin Yuan found it somewhat unbearable.

If this continued, it would probably affect his own strength.

Just as Lin Yuan was considering whether to keep going,

Swish swish.

The silver mirror seemed to undergo some kind of qualitative change.

It actually began to slowly levitate.

The mirror surface even produced ripples like mercury.

"This?"

Lin Yuan gazed at the levitating silver mirror in front of him, his face showing astonishment.

The mirror levitating was not surprising to Lin Yuan.

With his current strength, by erupting with full force, he could also briefly levitate for a short time.

It was simply a matter of making his blood essence boil, erupting with a powerful life magnetic field to counteract the earth's gravitational pull.

What truly astonished Lin Yuan was that he sensed an independent space contained within the mirror surface!

The space was not large, only ten square meters.

But it must be known that in this two-dimensional world, there was no concept of spatial depth, everything was two-dimensional, everything was a plane.

Yet now, within the mirror surface, there was an independent three-dimensional space maintained, which was an inconceivable ability in this two-dimensional world!

"The direct visible size of the world within my body reaches 2.331 billion li in circumference, with over 30 billion layers of nested spaces. Even so, after falling into this two-dimensional world, the world within my body was instantly suppressed into a 'paper', ceasing to exist."

Lin Yuan marveled inwardly.

Yet now, the silver mirror actually contained an independent space.

If this independent space returned to the main universe and fully unfolded, how vast would it become?

"With the space inside the mirror, it will be much more convenient." Lin Yuan repeatedly stroked the silver mirror, silently thinking to himself.

Although the space inside the mirror was not large, it was more than enough to store Lin Yuan's daily necessities, especially that jet-black iron rod, which was too conspicuous to carry on his back.

Now he could put the iron rod inside.

And when he wanted to use it, he could instantly take it out.

Moreover, for Lin Yuan, the silver mirror also had the crucial life-saving ability.

If he encountered an attack he could not withstand, he could completely hide inside the silver mirror.

As for the exposed silver mirror body itself, as one of the twelve stellar wondrous treasures and weapons, it was equivalent to an indestructible existence in this two-dimensional world.

Even if the most powerful experts fell into this place and were suppressed by the two-dimensional world, unable to unleash their full power, they still could not shake the silver mirror.

"Not bad, not bad."

Lin Yuan rejoiced inwardly, with this silver mirror, his means had been enriched once again.

"First, I'll put the iron rod inside." With a thought from Lin Yuan, the nearly two-meter-long jet-black iron rod disappeared from its original spot, entering the space within the silver mirror.

He then also put the bronze compass inside. This wondrous compass was of great use to Lin Yuan, so he had to always pay attention to it when carrying it on his person.

But by sending it into the space within the silver mirror, he could be much more at ease.

It did not take Lin Yuan long to understand the abilities of the silver mirror.

Now, meeting the Prince of Zhenbei in this way was also a manifestation of cause and effect.

That exquisite jade axe should be an extremely powerful weapon, similar to the jet-black iron staff.

As for that piece of wood, it was probably some kind of rare material from the Twelve Stars, which would likely provoke the pursuit of peerless experts even in the main universe.

"Yes."

The Bloodrain Empire indeed became much more peaceful, and those unruly powerful experts from all races immediately became much more obedient.

The numerous powerful experts from all races unanimously believed that Lin Yuan must be a legendary life form at the eleventh perfect stage from the main universe.

Facing such an existence, who would dare cause trouble within the area under his protection?

There were only two months left until the Heavenly Gate opened, which happened once every ten thousand years.
