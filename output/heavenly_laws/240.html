The two leading Evolved Ones, one wearing a purple robe and the other with a head of golden hair, respectfully straightened their waists.

After confirming his position as the new Galaxy Star Lord, Lin Yuan had received a lot of information, including details about Chunbo and Jiali.

"Alright, let them disperse." Lin Yuan looked at the densely packed army of Evolved Ones behind.

"Okay."

Chunbo nodded, turned around to face the army behind and said, "The Star Lord commands, you may all disperse."

His voice quickly reached the ears of every Evolved One.

"Yes."

"We obey the Star Lord's order."

The large army of Evolved Ones immediately dispersed in an orderly manner.

"Star Lord, allow me to show you around the main star." Chunbo immediately said.

The Galaxy main star was very large. Although Lin Yuan had already obtained relevant information, for such a huge star, it was impossible for everything to be recorded in the data.

After roughly touring the main star, Chunbo and Jiali brought Lin Yuan to a magnificent palace.

Lin Yuan asked.

The transformation of the Galaxy main star had begun when Lin Yuan paid 100,000 cubic universe crystals. Relevant Evolved Ones from nearby star alliances rushed over to carry out the transformation.

"Yes."

"The star transformation is now in its final stages and is about to be completed."

The stronger the planetary defense system on the main star, the safer it would be. This gave him a sense of confidence.

Excluding travel time, the star transformation took at most three to four years.

In such a short time, transforming this planet into a "forbidden land" that could easily suppress most 8th-stage Evolved Ones was no easy feat.

"Who dares not work hard when serving the Star Lord?"

Jiali, who was beside them, spoke up.

The Galaxy Star Lord was no ordinary Star Lord. Not only was he the disciple of the Chiyan Star Lord, he was also a top human genius with unlimited potential.

Those working on transforming his main star must have given their all, not daring to show any negligence.

Of course, the main reason was that Lin Yuan had paid generously. Some of the 100,000 cubic universe crystals could be distributed to the workers.

"I have two subordinates who will manage the main star together with you." Lin Yuan looked at Jiali and Chunbo.

The two 8th-stage alien races gifted to him by the Chiyan Star Lord, Red Skin and Blue Eyes, not only had strength reaching the 8th-stage, but also had rich experience in star management.

To properly manage a star, especially a main star that served as the economic and political center of a star domain, required a high level of leadership ability.

"Alright."

Chunbo and Jiali had no objections.

When taking over an unfamiliar star domain for the first time, it was only natural for Lin Yuan to assign his trusted subordinates.

If Lin Yuan didn't make such arrangements and completely trusted them instead, Chunbo and Jiali would actually feel uneasy.

Inside the inner world.

The two 8th-stage alien races, Red Skin and Blue Eyes, had already fallen into a state of numbness.

Five years.

For 8th-stage alien races like them, it wasn't even enough time for a nap.

But under the watchful eyes of the two 8th-stage alien races, Lin Yuan's inner world had exploded from nearly 300,000 li in diameter before departure to 700,000 li now.

It had expanded by a whopping 400,000 li in diameter.

Is this guy even human?

Red Skin and Blue Eyes composed themselves and managed to recover.

Originally, they speculated that when Lin Yuan first entered the 8th-stage, his inner world had reached nearly 300,000 li. Perhaps he had used some supreme treasure or rare item related to space?

Some rare items found in the depths of the cosmic starry sky were very useful for opening up inner worlds, although there were many thresholds to using them.

But at least it could barely explain why Lin Yuan's inner world could reach a diameter of nearly 300,000 li upon first entering the 8th-stage.

However, what happened over the past five years undoubtedly shattered the perceptions of the two 8th-stage alien races.

They watched bit by bit as Lin Yuan's inner world expanded every day, every month, every year, until it reached 700,000 li in diameter now.

When first opening up an inner world, some supreme space treasures or rare items could be used to increase its size.

But after that, if one wanted to continue expanding their inner world, they could only rely on their level of comprehension of the laws of space.

In other words, over the past five years, Lin Yuan's expansion of his inner world was entirely due to his own understanding of spatial laws.

An inner world with a diameter of 700,000 li...

Red Skin and Blue Eyes tried their best to calm themselves.

They had seen inner worlds with diameters of 700,000 li before. The Chiyan Star Lord's inner world far exceeded 700,000 li.

But in just five short years, expanding from nearly 300,000 li to nearly 700,000 li...

The two of them had never heard of such a thing.

Even for Star Roaming Kun, a "favored child" with a peak bloodline and innate inner world related to the origin of space, it shouldn't be possible to have such monstrous comprehension of the core spatial model, right?

What the two 8th-stage alien races didn't know was that Lin Yuan had already dispersed most of his time and energy on perfecting the 8th-stage martial dao and comprehending the path of evolution.

If he had focused all his efforts on comprehending the core model of spatial laws, the explosive growth of his inner world would have increased by an even greater margin.

"Hmm?"

"Is the Peak Lord going to let us out?"

"We've already arrived at the Galaxy main star."

In an instant.

The two of them disappeared from the inner world.

Galaxy main star, central palace.

Lin Yuan sat on the throne and looked at Chunbo and Jiali below.

The next moment.

Red Skin and Blue Eyes appeared in the great hall.

"Are they the Star Lord's subordinates?"

Chunbo and Jiali's hearts surged with shock upon seeing Red Skin and Blue Eyes.

8th-stage.

These two were both at the 8th-stage.

Chunbo and Jiali's eyelids twitched.

Having two 8th-stage subordinates right after entering the 8th-stage himself, what an impressive feat...

Do you really think 8th-stage powerhouses are like cabbages by the roadside? Available in any quantity you want?

Chunbo and Jiali once again realized how terrifying Lin Yuan's background was. Just how much did the Chiyan Star Lord dote on him?

"You four will be responsible for various matters within the Galaxy Star Domain from now on." Lin Yuan said.

"Yes."

Chunbo and Jiali immediately replied.

"Yes."

"By the way, I need detailed information on the special lifeforms and lone alien races in the current star domain, as well as their movements in recent years..."

Lin Yuan assessed himself.

The invincible eighth stage masters grasped a kind of cosmic power, and their combat power surpassed the eighth stage. But with Lin Yuan's current life-saving means, at least he wouldn't be like Ou Yin, unable to resist even a single move.

In terms of spiritual defense, Lin Yuan had an eighth-stage spiritual defense treasure guarding him, the "Demon Jade Imprint" protecting him, and a mental strength that had nearly reached 14,000 points.

Even if it was an invincible eighth-stage soul-type evolutionary, Lin Yuan believed he could withstand dozens of moves without dying.

Chunbo and Jiali sent the intelligence information about those special lifeforms and lone wanderers of different races currently in the Galaxy Star Domain.

"Hmm?"

Lin Yuan carefully browsed through it.

"These special lifeforms are really too arrogant."

For special lifeforms born from the universe, they instinctively regarded everything in the universe as their own.

Especially that special lifeform named "Aodu," who needed to devour planets every once in a while. Resource planets? Life planets?

They were all dishes on Aodu's plate.

Its actions were the most arrogant after the Shadow Race retreated and human civilization was about to take over. It even continuously devoured dozens of life planets.

"This Aodu can't be kept."

Lin Yuan had already made a decision in his heart.

The Shadow Race was wary of Aodu's strength and background, but Lin Yuan didn't care.

Human civilization had never been afraid of anyone while conquering in all directions.

In the current Galaxy Star Domain, only Lin Yuan had the strength to deal with Aodu.
