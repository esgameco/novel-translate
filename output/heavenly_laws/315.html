Longshan Ridge.

Although he had deceived Xiran about being her "senior", as long as she could persevere until Lin Yuan finished his dimension travel, he would bring the treasures back to her.

All beings existed by the faith of mortals, so they would not do anything to undermine their own foundations.

As for descending divine incarnations? Maintaining them required a huge expenditure of divine crystals. Investigating such extremely time-consuming matters could not be done by divine incarnations indefinitely.

Time passed.

With no hidden dangers, and since one cultivation method could achieve the effects of most knight cultivation methods, why not propagate it? The later it was propagated, the more knights would be produced by other empires that had already adopted it, falling behind step-by-step and eventually impacting national strength.

That was the full extent of the enhancements brought about after completely absorbing the blue liquid.

Secondly, it could enhance the physical divine ability "Revive from a Drop of Blood".

This was almost equivalent to developing a million spatial layers per year, surely crushing countless ninth-level evolved beings in the main universe.

The true gods spoke in icy tones. Ever since raising their divine realms and ascending to the Ocean of Light, their majesty could not be violated. When had they suffered such humiliation?

Lin Yuan pondered in his heart.

This was also the downside of the path of faith.

At that time, who knows how many mortals would fall and perish.

Underground cultivation room.

With the power of faith, seventh-level demigods could more easily raise divine realms. How much effort would those seventh-level peak evolved beings in the main world need to open inner worlds within their bodies?

But in this realm, one only needed to accumulate enough faith from mortals.

This was quite inconceivable. When any object was magnified billions of times, it would exhibit different microscopic forms, revealing even more minute components.

Of course, waging war against the twenty-four supreme gods was an extreme situation that Lin Yuan would not undertake unless absolutely necessary.

"Forget it, as long as it benefits me, that's enough. I'll understand the principles later." After some thought, Lin Yuan began absorbing the blue liquid.

A greyish-blue gourd slowly emerged from the Gate of Myriad Realms, crossing boundless dimensions to descend into this world.

The ripples caused by this affected nearly half the mortal realm.

In this world, Lin Yuan could fully absorb the blue liquid within a short period of half a month to a few months.

After all, bringing these two treasures into the dimension travel would only cost two strands of Dimension Crossing Origin Power. With Lin Yuan's current wealth of over a hundred strands, it was easily affordable.

How would they act?

One by one, the true gods gathered here.

The true gods assembled here included eighteen lesser gods and one middle god.

The territory of the Duke of Longshan encompassed the surrounding subordinate duchies and vacant lands, reaching an area of five billion square miles - nearly ten times the original six million square miles.

Lin Yuan pondered silently.

Lin Yuan's consciousness descended into his inner world, which now spanned nearly two billion square miles, vast and boundless.

But he was still somewhat puzzled about the true nature of the blue liquid. Even when magnified billions of times, the blue liquid remained a blue liquid.

Lin Yuan was more inclined to believe that the twenty-four supreme gods would not know of his existence before he grew to his limit.

"As long as I can reach the ninth level of ten million realms, Hunyi will be no threat." Lin Yuan was very confident. This dimension travel had also awakened his "Elemental Body" physical divine ability.

Lin Yuan had been the patriarch of the Longshan clan for a hundred years.

The two were equivalent and progressed in sync.

Similarly, if Lin Yuan fully absorbed the sixth blue liquid from the mysterious gourd in this world, there would be no change.

The prosperity of the martial arts cultivation method made countless experts in the Highlands curious about its source, the Duke of Longshan at Longshan Ridge.

"Finally..."

The flaws of the path of faith were also very obvious - just as mortals raised them up, mortals could also drag them down.

Unknowingly...
