"Patriarch."

The old man's name was Longshan Zhu, who had already been a fourth-level knight for many years and was part of the foundation of the Longshan family.

How many fourth-level knights were there in the entire Longshan Ridge?

If it weren't for the personal request of Viscount Longshan this time, given Longshan Zhu's status, it would be impossible for him to give special treatment to a junior member of the Longshan family.

However, Longshan Zhu never expected that the matter he originally planned to casually deal with would turn out to be so dreamy and unbelievable, to the point that he still felt a bit dazed and needed to constantly check if he was dreaming.

"What's going on?"

Viscount Longshan noticed Longshan Zhu's silence and vaguely sensed that something was wrong. "Could it be that Ayuan is really a piece of rotten wood?"

Viscount Longshan's tone was a bit disappointed. The Longshan family had an extraordinary bloodline and practiced the orthodox Longshan Cultivation Method.

With the guidance of a senior like Longshan Zhu, even if Longshan Yuan was incompetent, it shouldn't be too difficult for him to sense the Qihai within a month.

"Rotten wood?"

Hearing Viscount Longshan say this, Longshan Zhu was slightly stunned and quickly explained, "Patriarch, Young Master Yuan is not a rotten wood. He, he has already sensed the Qihai."

"Not a rotten wood."

Viscount Longshan had just breathed a sigh of relief, but immediately widened his eyes. "What did you say? He has already sensed the Qihai?"

Whether it was the Longshan Cultivation Method or other knight cultivation methods, the first step was to sense the Qihai.

This was also the prerequisite for becoming a first-level knight. Any creature that sensed the Qihai could use its power to transform themselves and then choose a suitable knight cultivation method.

But sensing the Qihai was very difficult, and it was also a test of a knight's aptitude.

The time the Longshan family prepared for its members to sense the Qihai was one month.

As long as one sensed the Qihai within this month, they would have the hope of becoming a formal knight and receive the nurturing of the Longshan family.

But now?

How long had it been since Longshan Zhu went to guide Longshan Yuan?

One hour?

Or two hours?

"You mean, Ayuan sensed the Qihai within two hours?" Viscount Longshan's voice trembled slightly.

In the history of the Longshan family, since the first generation viscount took over Longshan Ridge, only three people were able to sense the Qihai within two hours.

Among these three, two eventually became fifth-level great knights, leaving a deep mark in the history of the Longshan family.

The remaining one unfortunately fell midway.

The Longshan family did not have a fifth-level great knight presiding over every generation. For example, the strongest in the current generation of Longshan Ridge was only at the fourth level.

Even the empire would bestow rewards again to congratulate the Longshan family for giving birth to another great knight.

So many honors and benefits were all due to a fifth-level great knight.

"Sensing the Qihai within two hours." Longshan Zhu fell into silence again.

If it were really like this, he would undoubtedly be overjoyed at this moment, instead of being at a loss for words and feeling a hint of trepidation like he was now.

"Patriarch, the time it took for Young Master Yuan to sense the Qihai... was two breaths." Longshan Zhu composed himself and said with some difficulty.

In fact, Longshan Zhu wasn't sure if it was exactly two breaths. He only remembered that Longshan Yuan took a glance at the Longshan Cultivation Method and then said he sensed the Qihai.

If Longshan Zhu hadn't truly and honestly sensed the Qihai fluctuations in Longshan Yuan's body, he probably would have planned to reprimand him.

"What did you say... two breaths?" Viscount Longshan was dumbfounded.

If it was said that sensing the Qihai within two hours, he could still barely understand it, but two breaths?

Two breaths weren't even enough time to calm down, yet he sensed the Qihai?

"Are you sure?"

Viscount Longshan took a deep breath and stood up, asking in a solemn voice.

Although based on his understanding of Longshan Zhu, he knew that this old man of the Longshan family never liked to joke around, especially when it came to important family matters, and would never conceal or deceive.

But sensing the Qihai in two breaths was too unbelievable.

"I'm sure."

Longshan Zhu said with utmost certainty.

He was certain that Longshan Yuan's Qihai was sensed after seeing him and reading that Longshan Cultivation Method book.

"Sensing the Qihai in two breaths... is my Longshan family really going to soar?" After the shock, Viscount Longshan was ecstatic.

Sensing the Qihai in two hours meant the hope of becoming a fifth-level great knight.

Then what about sensing it in two breaths? A sixth-level legendary knight? Or a seventh-level demigod?

It should be known that on the Wanguo Plateau, as long as a seventh-level demigod didn't secretly spread their faith, they could join any empire and be endlessly glorified. Even the deities residing in the Sea of Light would cast approving gazes.

A seventh-level demigod, as long as they were willing, could make a deal with the deities of the Sea of Light and completely open up another empire.

"Who else knows about this?"

Viscount Longshan's eyes suddenly became deep.

Longshan Zhu also understood the seriousness of the matter and said earnestly.

"That's good."

Viscount Longshan nodded.

The surrounding viscounty territories and even more distant forces would allow the Longshan family to give birth to a future quasi-great knight who sensed the Qihai in two hours.

But sensing it within two breaths? Once this matter was leaked, it would not be a good thing.

Viscount Longshan could be certain that the empire would definitely intervene. By then, Longshan Yuan might be forcibly conscripted to the imperial capital.

"From now on, the speed at which Ayuan sensed the Qihai is not two breaths, but five hours."

After thinking for a while, Viscount Longshan solemnly said to Longshan Zhu.

Sensing the Qihai in two hours, this was the aptitude of a future quasi-great knight. Viscount Longshan still felt it was a bit too eye-catching.

Five hours, just right. It meant having the aptitude to become a fourth-level knight and wouldn't attract too much attention from other forces.

"Understood."

Longshan Zhu immediately responded.

He understood that this was an unprecedented opportunity for the Longshan family. As long as they patiently waited for Longshan Yuan to grow up, it would be the moment for the Longshan family to soar.

"Ayuan is the hope of my Longshan family. Absolutely no one is allowed to threaten Ayuan," Viscount Longshan muttered to himself.
