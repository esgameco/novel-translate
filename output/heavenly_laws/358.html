Chapter 358: Preparation for the Breakthrough (Please Subscribe)

"Good, good, good!" The Supreme Being Xia Qinzhi spoke with an excited tone, becoming more and more satisfied the more he looked at Lin Yuan.

Over the long years, he had also cultivated several disciples, devoting his heart and effort to them. Those disciples possessed the bloodline of the twelfth stage of life, plus the Supreme Being's own cultivation. They indeed achieved quite remarkable accomplishments.

But compared to Lin Yuan? Xia Qinzhi felt a bit embarrassed.

So far, the only cultivation the Supreme Being had provided to the Galactic Stellar Master was taking him to visit the shore of the River of Time once.

As for anything more? There was nothing else.

That pearl that could withstand the ultimate attacks of the same realm - the Divine Subduing Pearl - was basically given to every human civilization's peerless genius, as long as they were summoned by the Supreme Being.

The only difference in how Xia Qinzhi treated Lin Yuan compared to other peerless human geniuses was allowing the Galactic Stellar Master to glimpse the full view of the River of Time when he was at the ninth stage.

This opportunity could be considered great, or it could be considered not that great.

For a mediocre person, seeing it or not seeing it made no difference.

Even for a peerless evolutionary genius, at most it would just make comprehending the laws of time a bit smoother.

But Lin Yuan? He only looked at it for a short while, and in less than thirty years, he thoroughly comprehended the laws of time?

Even Xia Qinzhi was a bit dumbfounded by such a harvest.

He did have the expectation for Lin Yuan to comprehend the laws of time when he reached the ninth stage. But he didn't expect it to happen so quickly!

"Good, good, good?" Lin Yuan looked towards Xia Qinzhi sitting on the throne.

The first time he was summoned by the Supreme Being in the past, even though he was told Lin Yuan was the pioneer of the martial path of evolution, the Supreme Being only revealed a few approving glances. But now, to be so excited as to say "good" three times?

Before today, Lin Yuan had never imagined the Supreme Being would reveal such emotional fluctuations.

"It seems the harvest from breaking through to the tenth stage after comprehending the laws of time at the ninth stage will exceed even my imagination?" Lin Yuan thought to himself.

"When will the metamorphosis be complete?" Xia Qinzhi observed Lin Yuan for a moment and asked.

He was naturally very familiar with the various details of breaking through from the ninth to the tenth stage.

One's physical body, soul, mind, will, and even the inner world within the body would all undergo a slow metamorphosis.

Once the metamorphosis reached a critical point, the final breakthrough could be made to fully cross into the tenth stage.

After completing the ninth layer of dimension travel, the reason Lin Yuan waited until after the Xuanhuang Secret Realm to make the breakthrough was out of concern that some accident might occur during the metamorphosis period.

When breaking through from the ninth to the tenth stage, not only would Lin Yuan's main body undergo metamorphosis, but his two primordial spirits would also undergo metamorphosis.

"Originally it would take sixty years."

"Now at most twenty years, and I can reach the pinnacle of metamorphosis, then I can break through."

Lin Yuan immediately said.

Why do ninth-stage cultivators need to open at least a million layers of spatial realms to reach the threshold of breaking through to the tenth stage?

Because only that number of spatial realm layers can barely undergo constant metamorphosis to eventually cross into the tenth stage.

But here it is "barely" - the more spatial realm layers opened, the easier the metamorphosis process becomes.

"You comprehended the laws of time, mastered space-time, and your inner world is complete and independent, so naturally the speed of your metamorphosis will soar," Xia Qinzhi nodded.

It's like how the speed of two wheels definitely cannot compare to four wheels or eight wheels.

And with the addition of the laws of time fused into the inner world supported only by the laws of space, it was not just a matter of adding a few more wheels - it was a qualitative leap, a great transcendence.

"When breaking through from the ninth to the tenth stage, even for ordinary cultivators, they need to withstand the infusion of the cosmic origin and cannot be disturbed during that period," Xia Qinzhi looked at Lin Yuan.

Ordinary cultivators need to withstand the infusion of the cosmic origin.

As for Lin Yuan, he would need to withstand the infusion of the chaotic void origin.

He could not be disturbed even more so.

"Where does the Supreme Being plan to have me break through?" Lin Yuan took the initiative to ask.

The Supreme Being would definitely be most clear about these matters.

"Here's what we'll do, you can break through within the Galactic Stellar Domain."

"Additionally, try to recall your outer avatars as much as possible during the breakthrough."

After pondering for a moment, Xia Qinzhi spoke.

When breaking through from the ninth to the tenth stage, it was definitely necessary to break through in as safe a place as possible.

The safest areas for the human civilization were those few locations.

Even if supreme beings of other races went there, they would gain nothing.

Secondly were the great evolutionary towers.

The evolutionary towers, or the places where supreme beings resided, were equally safe places once inside, enjoying the protection of the supreme beings.

However...

The speed of Lin Yuan's inner world metamorphosis was simply too fast.

Less than twenty years until the pinnacle of metamorphosis.

No matter the nearest evolutionary tower or the Central Stellar Domain, they were separated from the Galactic Stellar Domain by countless millions of light-years.

If Lin Yuan wanted to go there, he would inevitably have to spend a lot of time just traveling.

This made Xia Qinzhi feel it was inappropriate.

Lin Yuan was currently the only existence in human civilization at the ninth stage who had comprehended the laws of time.

He might even be the only one in this entire universe cycle from its birth to destruction.

Naturally, there was no need to waste the precious time before his breakthrough just on traveling.

"I will send an avatar to the Galactic Stellar Domain. You can focus solely on your breakthrough," Xia Qinzhi said.

Although the Galactic Stellar Domain was covered by the virtual world network where the Supreme Being Butterfly could manifest an avatar at any time, Lin Yuan was ultimately the one Xia Qinzhi had taken a liking to.

It was fine to have the Supreme Being Butterfly watch over him normally, but now even at this critical juncture of breaking through from the ninth to tenth stage, to still rely on the Supreme Being Butterfly?

Xia Qinzhi felt it was a bit inappropriate.

In any case, the time for Lin Yuan's breakthrough was not that long, so dispatching an avatar would not take much effort.

"Thank you, Supreme Being," Lin Yuan expressed his gratitude.

To have the Supreme Being personally protect him during his breakthrough? In the human civilization, it was estimated that only those breaking through from the eleventh to twelfth stage would receive such treatment.

However, based on the potential Lin Yuan had revealed so far, in terms of importance, he was not at all weaker than the peak eleventh-stage cultivators of the human civilization.

In fact, in the Supreme Being's heart, he was far superior.

To the Supreme Being, there was not much difference between the ninth and eleventh stages.

The key was the possibility and potential to cross into the twelfth stage.

Currently, Lin Yuan's performance in this aspect surpassed even those peak eleventh-stage cultivators.

Breaking through from the ninth to tenth stage, then from tenth to eleventh stage, enjoying the infusion of the chaotic void origin twice in a row, which was treatment usually only received when breaking through from the eleventh to twelfth stage?

It meant that when Lin Yuan crossed into the eleventh stage, his physical body, soul, mind, will, inner world, etc. would be able to crush most eleventh-stage experts.

Even if he was inferior to those peak eleventh-stage experts in comprehending laws, he could still preserve his life and even contend with them relying on his own foundation.

And once Lin Yuan reached the eleventh-stage peak, his chances of ultimately assaulting the Supreme realm would be far higher than other peak eleventh-stage experts assaulting the Supreme realm.

The increase in probability here came entirely from Lin Yuan himself, not external factors.

If further supported by the human civilization deploying those few key supreme treasures, then at the very least, Lin Yuan was the cultivator with the highest possibility of crossing into the Supreme realm in the past two million years of human civilization.

For such an astonishing genius, how could they not value and cherish him?

"During the breakthrough, try to enter deeper layers of spatial realms as much as possible," Xia Qinzhi finally reminded.

"Understood," Lin Yuan nodded.

When breaking through from the eighth to ninth stage, he had not even opened any spatial realm layers.

So he chose to break through in a desolate stellar domain to minimize the impact.

The Supreme Being telling him to break through in deeper spatial realm layers was for a similar reason.

When Lin Yuan broke through from the eighth to ninth stage, the impact only spanned dozens or hundreds of light-years.

But breaking through from the ninth to tenth stage, not to mention other factors, just the foundation of his inner world alone would increase by over a million-fold.

If he did not break through in the deeper spatial realm layers, who knows how great the impact would be?

Suppress it? Suppress the infusion of the chaotic void origin?

The Supreme Being Xia Qinzhi would naturally not do something so foolish.

Shua!

Lin Yuan's figure disappeared.

Seated on the throne, Xia Qinzhi's mind stirred slightly.

An avatar immediately appeared outside the evolutionary tower.
