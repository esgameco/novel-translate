A figure appeared silently - it was Lin Yuan.

"Canglan Star." Lin Yuan looked towards the unremarkable planet in the distance, feeling quite emotional.

Canglan Star was only a medium-level life-bearing planet. Even though it had been transformed into a high-level life-bearing planet due to his influence, to Lin Yuan it was still like a speck of dust.

But this was ultimately where Lin Yuan grew up, and his parents still lived on Canglan Star.

"It's been a long time since I've been back," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

Since leaving Canglan Star for the Chikun main star, Lin Yuan hadn't returned. He had only met with his parents in the virtual world.

On Canglan Star, in Estate No. 1 on the Central Continent.

Lin Shoucheng and Lu Qiong were planting flowers. With human civilization's technology, any rare flower could be rapidly propagated, but Lu Qiong enjoyed the feeling of flowers growing slowly.

"Old Lin, I see many people saying Little Yuan has become a Supreme Being?" Lu Qiong said with some surprise after tending to the flowers that needed care.

"Little Yuan became a Supreme Being?"

Lin Shoucheng was a bit dazed. Not long ago he was still stunned that his son had overpowered a Supreme Being, and now he had directly become a Supreme Being himself?

"It must be fake, right?" Lin Shoucheng said instinctively.

"It's true, the Central Star Region has issued an announcement," Lu Qiong immediately said, as virtual screens continuously appeared before her.

Just then.

A figure appeared in the distance, smiling as he looked at Lin Shoucheng and Lu Qiong.

"Little Yuan."

Lin Shoucheng called out in surprise when he saw the figure.

Compared to when he left Canglan Star, the current Lin Yuan had almost no changes, even wearing the same clothes. Only his gaze and aura seemed more profound.

"Little Yuan, how did you come back too?"

Lu Qiong's face was full of joy. Although they often met Lin Yuan in the virtual world, the virtual world was ultimately virtual - how could it compare to the realness of reality?

"Dad, Mom." Lin Yuan walked over casually with a smile.

"Little Yuan, everyone online is saying you've become a Supreme Being," Lu Qiong mentioned after chatting briefly.

Lin Shoucheng beside her immediately looked at Lin Yuan.

"I suppose so,"

Lin Yuan nodded with a smile.

"You really became a Supreme Being?" Lu Qiong and Lin Shoucheng looked at each other.

Their son had become a Supreme Being?

"Little Yuan, I heard that Supreme Beings' life essence transcends the river of time. Do we look like ants to you now?"

Lu Qiong couldn't help but ask.

"Hahaha." Lin Yuan laughed. "No, you're different."

As long as there is life, there will be emotions. Lin Yuan was no exception. No matter how weak his parents were, they were still his parents.

Two figures walked down from it - Lin Yi and Xia Keran.

"Dad, Mom, there's big news." Lin Yi strode towards the estate, calling out as she walked. Beside her, Xia Keran also looked excited.

When they entered the estate and saw Lin Yuan standing to the side, they were momentarily stunned.

"Big brother." Lin Yi called out instinctively.

Xia Keran stood behind Lin Yi, staring blankly at Lin Yuan. Then he reacted, immediately bowing deeply and saying: "Xia Keran pays respects to the Supreme Being."

As one of the great evolutionary families of human civilization, the Xia family naturally learned first about the Milky Way Star Lord reaching the twelfth level, and even received confirmed information.

The reason Xia Keran and Lin Yi rushed to Canglan Star so quickly was because the Milky Way Star Lord had reached the twelfth level. They wanted to tell this joyous news to the Milky Way Star Lord's parents.

Although in the internet age, by the time they reached Canglan Star, Lin Shoucheng and his wife would likely already know about this, coming in person was also a show of attitude.

Now Lin Shoucheng and Lu Qiong were the parents of a Supreme Being.

But Xia Keran hadn't expected Lin Yuan to be here as well.

"Rise," Lin Yuan said casually with a slight smile.

"Yes." Xia Keran still felt very nervous. He recalled the instructions from the Xia family's eleventh-level ancestor before he came - absolutely do not have any improper behavior or actions in front of the Milky Way Star Lord.

Xia Keran agreed with this. After all, the Milky Way Star Lord was now a Supreme Being, the tenth Supreme Being of human civilization. How noble was his status?

"Big brother, how did you come here?" Lin Yi's tone was also much more careful. The pressure brought by the three words "Supreme Being" was too great.

Especially having just received this news, when Lin Yi thought about how her brother was now a Supreme Being of human civilization, transcending everything, she felt a bit shaky inside.

"I had some free time, so I came over," Lin Yuan said casually.

Before breaking through to the ultimate level, Lin Yuan had put almost all his energy and focus into cultivation. After all, if he didn't reach the twelfth level, he would still be a life within the river of time, and would die when the river of time collapsed.

Under this pressure, Lin Yuan naturally gave it his all.

But after becoming a Supreme Being, Lin Yuan became more relaxed.

Supreme Beings had infinite lifespans. The only issue was facing the universe's great destruction, but with Lin Yuan's strength, he could easily withstand it.

All underwent huge changes because Lin Yuan had entered the twelfth level.

An ordinary planet.

"I'm going back first."
