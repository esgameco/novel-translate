Lin Yuan looked down at the gradually dissipating Chaos Fire, his thoughts rippling.

To get a Chaos Sage to actively extinguish and disappear was too difficult. To cultivate to the perfect Chaos Realm, how steadfast must one's Dao heart be? Such powerful experts could be killed, could be suppressed, but for them to actively and completely vanish and extinguish themselves was almost impossible.

When the Mysterious Emperor perished, it was due to the backlash from forcibly breaking free of the time loop, but the Ninth Phoenix Sage?

He had willingly walked towards annihilation and destruction right before Lin Yuan's eyes.

Of course, this was due to Lin Yuan's strength, but even if they were not opponents, no Chaos Sage would willingly walk towards their own annihilation and destruction.

For example, Lin Yuan, even if faced with the lofty Time Life, if he truly had no retreat, he would not surrender meekly, but would definitely resist and struggle.

In the time line images seen by the Heavenly Void Cauldron, after Lin Yuan completely destroyed that special place, he actively extinguished his physical body and soul, because he still had the refuge of the Source World.

It was not true death.

However, Lin Yuan could clearly feel that the burning of the Ninth Phoenix Sage was a complete and utter burning, leaving behind not a single item related to his own cause and effect to preserve his life.

As for "reincarnation"? Lin Yuan did not sense even the slightest ripple of his spiritual will merging into the Chaos Void Dao rules to undergo reincarnation.

In other words, the Ninth Phoenix Sage was heading towards complete death and extinction.

"The cause and effect has been resolved."

Lin Yuan looked at the rapidly dissipating Chaos Fire, feeling that there was not a trace of cause and effect left of the two Chaos Sages.

From the premature great destruction of his hometown universe, to the arduous integration of human civilization into the Chaos Void, it was all due to these two Chaos Sages.

Now that they had completely perished, the enmity and cause and effect between them had been resolved.

Phew~ The Chaos Fire dissipated.

A crystal-clear, fiery red crystal appeared there.

Lin Yuan glanced at it, and his right hand reached out to take the fiery red crystalline stone.

The stone contained threads of Chaos Fire within it, though not much, but it was extremely pure.

"Is this the Dao fruit cultivated by the Ninth Phoenix Sage's entire life?"

Lin Yuan looked at the stone. No matter what being, be it below the Twelfth Rank or Dao Sovereign Mastering Chaos, as long as they fused with this stone, they could quickly embark on the evolutionary path of the Chaos power that the Ninth Phoenix Sage had walked.

In simple terms, this stone could rapidly create a Chaos Sage.

Such a treasure, even Lin Yuan had only heard of, but had never seen before.

Even the Mysterious Emperor, the Peerless Sage of the Demon Jade Tower, had never seen it.

Because it was too difficult.

It required the peak state of the perfect Chaos Realm, willingly abandoning everything, giving up on breaking through to the Thirteenth Rank, giving up on reincarnation, and so on, in order to have a chance of condensing it.

And the most crucial point was that even if a Chaos Sage was willing to do so, in the end, they might not be able to condense such a Dao fruit.

"If my Dao can continue, it would not be considered a complete demise."

Lin Yuan looked at the fiery red crystal stone in his hand, storing it within his personal universe, and then turned to return to the Milky Way Chaos Continent.

The Source World.

Within the Treasure Land.

The true body and master of the Chaos Void had entered the Chaos Realm, and the true body within this Source World had naturally also entered the Chaos Realm.

"This space-time?"

Lin Yuan sat cross-legged, feeling the surging power within him.

After entering the Chaos Realm, the essence of life had undergone a metamorphosis and leap.

Lin Yuan once again felt that the suppression of the space-time of the Treasure Land on him had become almost negligible.

"The Source World's Origin Site"

Lin Yuan's gaze was profound, vaguely seeing through the space-time of this Treasure Land, looking deeper into the space-time.

The essence of the Treasure Land was that it had broken off from the Source World. If one were to compare the Source World to a towering tree, then the Treasure Land would be the fruit growing on the tree.

The fallen fruit, having already severed its connection with the towering tree, were places like the Black Jade's Nest and other Treasure Lands.

And this Treasure Land here had just emerged, its relationship with the Source World being both severed and not severed.

And with Lin Yuan's current cultivation, he could perceive the essence of the Source World itself, this towering tree, through this half-severed connection.

Hum~ At this moment, a manifestation of the Venerable Yin of the Mysterious Yin Ancient Kingdom descended.

"Silver River Celestial, how have you decided?" The Venerable Yin was somewhat impatient.

Ever since six hundred years ago, when he planned to occupy the core celestial pillar of his ancient kingdom and trade with the Silver River Celestial to gain a large amount of benefits.

This Silver River Celestial had been constantly considering, saying that such a big matter, he could not decide.

At first, the Venerable Yin was still quite understanding, after all, the request he had proposed was indeed excessive, completely worthy of being called a "lion's mouth opening".

The Venerable Yin knew that he was opening his lion's mouth, and the Silver River Celestial and the Blazing Sun Ancient Kingdom also knew that he was opening his lion's mouth, but now, in the situation within the Treasure Land, even if the Silver River Celestial and the Blazing Sun Ancient Kingdom were unwilling, as long as they wanted to obtain this Treasure Land, they could only let him open his lion's mouth.

However.

Six hundred years had passed.

The Silver River Celestial had been saying he was considering it.

Originally a simple transaction, but it had been dragged on by the Silver River Celestial for six hundred years?

This had completely eroded the Venerable Yin's patience.

If it were not for the fact that he had proposed conditions to the Blazing Sun Ancient Kingdom that were far superior to the Great Fu Ancient Kingdom, from the perspective of benefits, the trade with the Silver River Celestial would have gained him more.

The Venerable Yin would have long since turned to the Great Fu Ancient Kingdom to complete the transaction.
