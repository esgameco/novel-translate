Chiyuan Heavenly Venerable's expression was solemn. As an observer, although he did not directly face the power of Lin Yuan's Sword of Spacetime, he could still sense it to some degree.

Just a single strike of the Sword of Spacetime had already inflicted some injuries on the Crimson Lotus Heavenly Venerable. Now that 333 Swords of Spacetime were unleashed simultaneously, the Crimson Lotus Heavenly Venerable would need to resort to his true techniques to block them.

"The Silvery River Heavenly Venerable is also not weak in his comprehension of the Dao rules."

Hengyang Heavenly Venerable reached this conclusion. The comprehension of the Nine Layers of Spacetime Realm was considered ordinary among the Pinnacle Heavenly Venerables, but among the imperial clan who only knew how to excavate their bloodlines, it was already extremely extraordinary.

It was essentially the strongest level under the comprehension of the Chaotic Evolutionary Power rules.

"Haha, good, good, good!"

The Emperor of the Scorching Sun Ancient Kingdom's face broke into a smile when he saw this.

Originally, he was worried about how Lin Yuan could withstand the Crimson Lotus Heavenly Venerable for even a hundred breaths.

After all, the Crimson Lotus Heavenly Venerable's techniques were focused on swift battles and decisive victories. The Crimson Lotus Hellfire disregarded defenses, and ordinary Heavenly Venerables would find it difficult to endure under such raging flames for a hundred breaths.

But now?

The situation had evolved beyond the question of whether Lin Yuan could withstand for a hundred breaths, but rather, which of these two Heavenly Venerables would emerge victorious in the end.

"Crimson Lotuses Filling the Skies!"

Facing the 333 Swords of Spacetime, the Crimson Lotus Heavenly Venerable was on high alert. His physical body dispersed once again, transforming into numerous Crimson Lotus Hellfires that drifted in the spacetime.

Whoosh! Whoosh! Whoosh!!! The 333 Swords of Spacetime slashed through, causing large swaths of spacetime to shatter. The drifting Crimson Lotus Hellfires were also affected, but relying on their own Void attributes, they managed to barely withstand the 333 Swords of Spacetime.

After the 333 Swords of Spacetime passed, the Crimson Lotus Heavenly Venerable's form coalesced once again.

"Hmm?"

Lin Yuan immediately prepared to strike again.

"Silvery River Heavenly Venerable, there's no need to continue fighting," the Crimson Lotus Heavenly Venerable said immediately.

With the strength Lin Yuan had just displayed, it was only natural for him to be able to withstand for a hundred breaths.

And based on the current level of their exchange, if they continued to fight, the Crimson Lotus Heavenly Venerable would have to resort to his true lethal techniques.

However, those techniques were meant for dealing with enemies, and the Crimson Lotus Heavenly Venerable naturally did not wish to use them against his own Heavenly Venerable.

"Chiyuan, what do you think?" the Crimson Lotus Heavenly Venerable looked towards the distant Chiyuan Heavenly Venerable.

Having Chiyuan Heavenly Venerable assess Lin Yuan's strength was Crimson Lotus Heavenly Venerable's original intention.

"Indeed, there's no need to continue fighting," Chiyuan Heavenly Venerable nodded. The reason he had the Crimson Lotus Heavenly Venerable take action was to increase the chances of securing that first-grade Blessed Land in the upcoming competition.

As long as Lin Yuan's strength reached the Pinnacle Heavenly Venerable threshold and did not drag them down, Chiyuan Heavenly Venerable would turn a blind eye.

And now, the strength Lin Yuan had displayed was not just avoiding being a drag, but a pleasant surprise.

Although Chiyuan Heavenly Venerable also desired that first-grade Blessed Land, he hoped more that it would be occupied by the Scorching Sun Ancient Kingdom, and everything he had done was based on the interests of the Scorching Sun Ancient Kingdom.

"Alright," Lin Yuan nodded and retracted his divine ability 'World'.

This technique had fused and devoured the Scorching Yang Source, so when he unleashed it, the Scorching Yang aura was extremely dense. Even if he said he had not excavated his Ancestor's Bloodline to the Heavenly Venerable level, no one would believe him.

"The Silvery River Heavenly Venerable's strength?"

"It's too terrifying, it seems to be no weaker than the Crimson Lotus Heavenly Venerable at all!"

"Is this the Ancestor's Bloodline at the Heavenly Venerable level? Unprecedented?"

"Even for an Ancestor's Bloodline at the Heavenly Venerable level, it still requires a vast amount of time to excavate. How long has the Silvery River Heavenly Venerable's bloodline been refined to the Heavenly Venerable level?"

"Monstrous, truly monstrous."

The other ancient Heavenly Venerables observing were filled with shock. They could also feel the terrifying power of Lin Yuan.

Originally, the Heavenly Venerables had been quite dissatisfied with the Emperor's decision, thinking that forcibly inserting the Silvery River Heavenly Venerable into the Heavenly Venerable roster was the Emperor simply using 'backdoor' methods.

But now?

Even if Lin Yuan did not have a core imperial clan identity, he could completely compete for one of the twelve Heavenly Venerable positions based solely on his own strength.

The Emperor's intervention to place Lin Yuan on the Heavenly Venerable list was simply the icing on the cake.

"Silvery River Heavenly Venerable."

Among the many Heavenly Venerables, the Scorching Sun Heavenly Venerable was astonished as he looked at Lin Yuan.

He had previously had some interactions with Lin Yuan when the latter took over as the guardian of the Black Jade Nest.

At the time, the Scorching Sun Heavenly Venerable did not give Lin Yuan any trouble, but instead decisively stepped aside, unwilling to offend this core imperial clan member.

In the Scorching Sun Heavenly Venerable's view, Lin Yuan's future potential was immense.

There was no need for him to risk offending Lin Yuan over such a small matter as the Black Jade Nest.

Now, looking at it...

The Scorching Sun Heavenly Venerable was extremely grateful for his decision back then.

It had been such a short time, yet the Silvery River Heavenly Venerable had already attained such strength?

At this moment, Lin Yuan and the Crimson Lotus Heavenly Venerable flew towards the gathered Heavenly Venerables.

"Silvery River Heavenly Venerable," the Crimson Lotus Heavenly Venerable looked at Lin Yuan and sincerely admired him, "I have always believed that the Dao rules are the foundation, but now I see that the pinnacle of bloodline power is no less than the power of the Dao rules."

Throughout their exchange, the other party, Lin Yuan, had not even utilized his full strength.

Just by observing the surroundings, Lin Yuan sensed something was off.

Compared to his previous visits to this flame world, the location where he had appeared this time was different.

"It's much higher up," Lin Yuan concluded after careful observation.

"The Scorching Sun Ancestor."

Lin Yuan looked up at the towering figure standing with hands behind his back at the highest point of the world. Due to the elevated position, the majestic figure was much clearer, and Lin Yuan could sense many subtle auras.
