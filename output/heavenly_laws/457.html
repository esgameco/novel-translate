Chapter 457: Entering the Twelfth Realm (Seeking Subscriptions)

After activating the 'World' divine ability, it could only be maintained for six seconds.

Within these six seconds, Lin Yuan decided to try to kill the strongest Eleventh Realm Insect Race expert.

The main reason was that the hatred between human civilization and the Insect Race was too great.

In the past, when Lin Yuan had not yet grown strong, he had experienced several assassination attempts, most of which were plotted by the Insect Race.

In such a situation, naturally the priority target to eliminate would be from the Insect Race.

"Silver Galaxy Overlord!"

The strongest Eleventh Realm Insect Race expert let out a shrill cry, and invisible soul fluctuations swept out in all directions.

However, all these soul fluctuations seemed to sink into mud when they reached Lin Yuan.

In the eyes of the strongest Eleventh Realm Insect Race expert, Lin Yuan's right hand pressing down on her grew larger and larger, and the terrifying pressure made her feel like she was facing an ultimate expert.

Hum hum hum.

At this moment, a hazy glow suddenly emanated from the top of the strongest Eleventh Realm Insect Race expert's head, where the Nightmare Realm was located.

This hazy glow was completely different from the light emitted when the strongest Eleventh Realm Insect Race expert activated her abilities. It was filled with an illusion that all things were illusory and dreamlike.

Even Lin Yuan's mind was briefly captivated by this momentary illusion, but the next moment, relying on his powerful mental will, he broke free.

"Hmm?"

"Such a powerful mental will?"

A slightly surprised voice came, and Lin Yuan looked up to see a vague figure slowly emerging from the Nightmare Realm.

This figure was extremely illusory, but it gave Lin Yuan a sense of absolute reality, similar to that of an ultimate expert.

"Is this the Twelfth Realm Apex?"

Lin Yuan's figure disappeared and reappeared in the air above the altar.

Although he had long known that killing the strongest Eleventh Realm expert would trigger the backup left behind by the Twelfth Realm Apex in the Tribal Weapon, Lin Yuan didn't expect it to be a Twelfth Realm Apex avatar.

Even though this avatar was just an incarnation of the Apex, it was not something Lin Yuan could currently deal with.

Moreover, the Tribal Weapon was still in the hands of that Insect Race Apex, and if the Apex avatar activated the Tribal Weapon, the destructive power it could unleash, even if it didn't reach a million points, would likely be extremely close.

"Hahaha, Wanhua, as the mighty Apex, you dare to bully a little guy? That's unacceptable," from within the altar, a ring on Xuanyuan Yu's right hand also began to glow.

A male figure coalesced, gazing towards the Apex that had emerged from the Nightmare Realm.

"It's the Xuanyuan Strongest Expert," Lin Yuan thought, looking at the male figure.

Among the nine Strongest Experts of human civilization, Lin Yuan had the most contact with the Xia Qin Strongest Expert, as he was affiliated with Xia Yu's Evolution Tower.

As for the Xuanyuan Strongest Expert, although Lin Yuan hadn't interacted with him much, he knew that Xuanyuan was the first Strongest Expert born in human civilization.

He was also the Strongest Expert who had borne the greatest pressure and paid the highest price among the nine.

After all, human civilization had obtained that ultimate opportunity from the heavens, with countless inherited treasures descending from the cosmic universe, at a time when human civilization had not yet risen.

But when the first Strongest Expert of human civilization emerged, those Twelfth Realm Apexes naturally turned their attention to human civilization, leading to the exposure of that ultimate opportunity.

The human civilization and the then newly ascended Twelfth Realm Xuanyuan Strongest Expert became the focus of the cosmic universe.

One Twelfth Realm Apex after another lay in wait, ready to attack human civilization at any time and plunder that ultimate opportunity.

Xuanyuan Strongest Expert paid an unimaginable price to lead human civilization through that period, and it was only after the second, third, and fourth Strongest Experts were born that the situation for human civilization improved significantly.

"Wanhua Empress Mother."

Lin Yuan's gaze turned to the figure that had emerged from the Nightmare Realm.

Among the Empress Mothers of the Insect Race, Wanhua Empress Mother was also the oldest and most powerful. It was she who had personally refined the Tribal Weapon, the Nightmare Realm.

"Empress Mother."

The strongest Eleventh Realm Insect Race expert bowed to Wanhua Empress Mother. Normally, the backup within the Nightmare Realm would only be triggered when she was truly facing a life-or-death crisis.

Now that Wanhua Empress Mother had appeared, it meant that she had just been on the verge of being killed by the Silver Galaxy Overlord.

"Could the Silver Galaxy Overlord really have killed me across the Tribal Weapon?" The strongest Eleventh Realm Insect Race expert found it hard to believe, but Wanhua Empress Mother's appearance could only mean that.

"Xuanyuan, you've also made a move?" Wanhua Empress Mother smiled slightly, and her invisible soul fluctuations affected all living beings. All the Eleventh Realm experts of the human civilization alliance in the altar began to involuntarily fall into a trance.

"Hmph."

The Xuanyuan Strongest Expert snorted coldly, his voice resonating in the minds of all the Eleventh Realm experts.

"And besides, you say I'm bullying the Silver Galaxy Overlord, that's fine, but calling him a little guy? You're underestimating him way too much, aren't you?"

Wanhua Empress Mother looked at the Silver Galaxy Overlord with a meaningful expression and said, "The Silver Galaxy Overlord's mental will has probably already reached the Twelfth Realm level, hasn't it?"

"Still a little guy?"

Lin Yuan's expression did not change upon hearing this.

Just now, he had quickly shaken off Wanhua Empress Mother's soul influence relying on his powerful mental will.

Wanhua Empress Mother seemed to have also noticed this clue.

"What? The Silver Galaxy Overlord's mental will has already reached the Twelfth Realm level?" The strongest Eleventh Realm Insect Race expert looked at Lin Yuan in astonishment.

Compared to the advancement of the physical body and soul, the advancement of mental will was the most difficult, especially after reaching hundreds of millions of points, where each increment required an enormous price.

As for those below the 12th stage? Given the strength the Galactic Overlord has now displayed, the strongest 11th stage beings wielding tribal weapons or even ultimate essences are clearly not his match.

If he really went berserk and let the Galactic Overlord unleash his ultimate essence, wouldn't the mightiest 11th stage beings of the various peak tribal powers be crushed into the ground?
