Facing that casual glance from Lin Yuan, the Python Sovereign truly felt the threat of complete destruction.

The Python Sovereign said solemnly.

But a Sovereign who had grown to the perfect ninth stage was extremely rare.

At this level, a Sovereign possessed the strength to kill ordinary Sovereigns.

For them, becoming the legendary tenth stage Demon was the ultimate goal.

In the vast boundless void, the number of Sovereigns that could be considered perfect ninth stage could be counted on one's fingers, not exceeding a hundred.

"Yes, stay far away."

Among demons, the weak were prey for the strong. Even at the ninth stage demon level, devouring their kind no longer held much significance.
