But with dozens of 12th-level ultimate beings present, the consumption spread among each ultimate being became much more manageable.

The Yu Clan ultimate being dared to bargain with Lin Yuan because he was relying on this peak Path Breaking realm defensive treasure.

Even if the Milky Way Star Lord was at the peak of the Path Breaking realm, so what? Even if he was stronger than ordinary peak Path Breaking realm experts, so what? As long as he was still within the scope of the Path Breaking realm, he could not overcome this palace.
