"This time, the physical divine ability I've awakened..."

Just the blink of an eye.

Lin Yuan thought to himself.

Conversely, this descent to the Origin World had no time limit for staying, so Lin Yuan didn't plan to take this risk. He would just wait slowly for the physical divine ability to finish nurturing.

"There's also the Blazing Sun Divine Body."

Lin Yuan shifted his attention to observe the changes in his physical body.

After condensing 3,333 Blazing Sun Seeds, all the seeds would form a cycle, influencing and transforming the physical body.

This was what Duke Qing Fen referred to as the 'Blazing Sun Divine Body'.

"Blazing Sun Divine Body?"

Lin Yuan was quite curious. From reading countless classics and inheritances of the Burning Sky Academy, he knew how powerful the Blazing Sun Divine Body was. The physical body would be like a blazing sun, illuminating and burning everything.

Especially under the eternal blazing sun of the Blazing Sun Ancient Country, the power it unleashed was even more terrifying.

As the number of condensed Blazing Sun Seeds increased, the speed of transformation into the Blazing Sun Divine Body would become faster and faster.

"The Blazing Sun Seeds can't be replicated in the Chaos Void, probably due to the different dimensions. But what about the Blazing Sun Divine Body?"

Lin Yuan didn't plan to have his true self transform into a Blazing Sun Divine Body, but he could experiment on his avatar.

After all, this constitution was so powerful that it was no weaker than the miraculous lifeforms nurtured in the Chaos Void. It could definitely take his avatar's strength to the next level.

His experience in the Blazing Sun Ancient Country of the Origin World had brought Lin Yuan's understanding of rules to a whole new level.

This was understanding, not comprehension.

"Yin and yang. My dao is yin and yang. I want to evolve Chaos Yin and Yang."

Lin Yuan's eyes burst with intense color. The martial dao he walked was rooted in yin and yang. The rotation of yin and yang forms taiji, the Chaos Taiji.

Duke Qing Fen's path was the dao of fire, so the power he ultimately evolved was Chaos Fire. To be precise, it was Duke Qing Fen's unique Chaos Azure Fire.

Even for the same dao of fire, due to different beings' different comprehensions, what is manifested can be very different.

Duke Qing Fen's dao of fire lay in 'azure' and 'burning', so it was called Chaos Azure Fire.

Currently, Lin Yuan's comprehension of chaos rules was at the first level. To evolve Chaos Yin Yang Taiji, he would probably need to reach the second level.

The levels of comprehension for other rules ranged from the first to the ninth level, and it was the same for space-time fusion rules.

But for chaos rules, there were only three levels.

The first level was the lowest, and the third level was the highest.

"I still need to improve my comprehension of other rules." Lin Yuan had a clear idea.

"Space-time eighth level." Lin Yuan turned to comprehend space-time fusion rules.

Now his attainment in comprehending space-time fusion rules had reached the peak of the seventh level, just a step away from the eighth level.

As long as he reached the eighth level of space-time comprehension, using space-time to drive the comprehension of myriad rules, and then advancing his comprehension of chaos rules, he should be able to enter the second level of chaos.

Lin Yuan's thoughts shifted, looking towards his internal universe.

Since mastering the first layer of 'Infinite Void', Lin Yuan's internal universe had reached the level of a Chaos Venerable, and he had begun to adjust it.

It wasn't that there were imperfections in his internal universe, but rather that compared to the starry sky of the main universe, the pressure in Lin Yuan's internal universe was too great.

Lin Yuan's internal universe was only twenty million light-years across, but the starry sky of the main universe far exceeded this size.

Under these circumstances, why could Lin Yuan's internal universe rival the starry sky of the main universe? It was because the space-time in Lin Yuan's internal universe was extremely stable. The so-called rivaling of the main universe's starry sky was not in terms of size, but in overall aspects.

This caused normal first and second order lifeforms to have great difficulty living in Lin Yuan's internal universe, as if they had come to a place where gravity was thousands or tens of thousands of times that of the main universe's starry sky.
