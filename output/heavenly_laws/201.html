The Progenitor Demon Tree paid a huge price.

And since then, the Tianqing World's precautions against the Progenitor Demon Tree had reached a higher level.

So the Progenitor Demon Tree's perception of Lin Yuan was actually very limited. Coupled with the various aura-concealing techniques Lin Yuan had comprehended himself, it could be said to be foolproof.

"On the battlefield outside the world, the Tianqing Alliance has been victorious recently."

Lin Yuan was not surprised that the Tianqing Alliance had gained an advantage on the battlefield.

In the past ten years or so, in order to "subsidize" Lin Yuan, the Progenitor Demon Tree had sent over who knows how much of its own Origin power.

As a result, its strength had declined, and the number of evil god puppets it could create had decreased.

Strictly speaking, although Lin Yuan was the offspring of the Progenitor Demon Tree, he still contributed to the Tianqing World by lowering the Progenitor Demon Tree's strength.

On the battlefield outside the world, the Tianqing Alliance gained the upper hand, and Lin Yuan deserved the most credit.

Outside the Tianqing World...

The Progenitor Demon Tree stood tall and vast in the void, countless vines swaying, and a terrifying demon domain spreading in all directions.

And opposite the Progenitor Demon Tree...

Thirteen figures stood shoulder to shoulder.

They were none other than the Tianqing Alliance's leader and twelve deputy leaders.

The leader of the Tianqing Alliance was the strongest in the Tianqing World. Wielding the World Sword, he could almost exert eighth-stage combat power.

As for the twelve deputy leaders, they were all seventh-stage existences, not to be underestimated.

"Evil God, die!"

The Tianqing Alliance leader grasped the stone longsword and slashed at the Progenitor Demon Tree.

Rip!

The Progenitor Demon Tree's figure remained huge, countless black vines swaying, barely blocking this sword.

"If it weren't for this world... You group of ants, how would you have the qualifications to stand before me?"

The Progenitor Demon Tree emitted vast mental fluctuations.

What it feared the most was the stone longsword in the hands of the Tianqing Alliance leader.

It was just that this time, the Progenitor Demon Tree's invasion made the Tianqing World exceptionally vigilant. Without hesitation, it allowed the stone longsword to emerge and join forces with the Tianqing World's powerhouses to resist the evil god's invasion.

The Progenitor Demon Tree retreated on its own.

The stone longsword in the hands of the Tianqing Alliance leader, the closer it was to the Tianqing World, the greater its power.

Similarly, the farther away it was from the Tianqing World, the weaker its power, and in the end, it would be no different from an ordinary longsword.
