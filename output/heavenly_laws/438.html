"The strong one." An indistinct and majestic figure emerged in Lin Yuan's mind.

Among the nine strongest experts of human civilization he had encountered several times, the Xuanxuan expert was undoubtedly the most taciturn.

He basically never spoke.

But in terms of strength, the Xuanxuan expert was unquestionable.

If the Xia Qin expert and Gu Li expert were considered top-tier in killing power among the nine strongest experts of human civilization,

And the Xushen expert was deemed the strongest in mastering space,

While the Yudie expert was unmatched in the realm of illusions,

Then the Xuanxuan expert was the overall strongest without any shortcomings.

It was the Xuanxuan expert who supported human civilization through its most difficult period.

"It's said that the Xuanxuan expert has almost reached the peak of the 'Path Opening Realm,'" the nearby Vice Patriarch Nalan couldn't help but say.

"Peak of the Path Opening Realm..." Lin Yuan's thoughts wandered.

The ninth level was divided into the Ten Thousand Realm, Million Realm, Ten Million Realm, Hundred Million Realm, and Billion Realm.

The tenth level also had many subdivided ranks, with the highest being the Grade A Tenth Level.

The eleventh level was similar, divided into six tiers, with the sixth tier of the perfected Eleventh Level able to break through to the ultimate at any time.

As for the twelfth level, the ultimate realm of the strongest experts, it could also be divided into three ranks:

The Path Opening Realm, the Dominance Realm, and the Chaos Realm.

In fact, before the Path Opening Realm, there was a transitional stage that newly promoted twelfth level experts would go through.

However, this stage would pass quickly, and twelfth level experts wouldn't take long to open up their own great path.

This was the origin of the Path Opening Realm.

Opening up one's own great path was the Path Opening Realm.

After the Path Opening Realm came the Dominance Realm and the Chaos Realm.

Experts like the Lord of the Magic Jewel Tower and the Xuanhuang Emperor were at the peak of the Chaos Realm, traversing the boundless chaos and establishing their own supreme forces.

"The peak of the Path Opening Realm is not that easy to reach."

Xuanxuan Du shook his head slightly. At the eleventh level realm, one could access much information about the twelfth level domain.

This included the catastrophe of the end of the cosmic cycle when all things would collapse.

To survive the destruction of the cosmos, more than half of the twelfth level ultimates would have to die. To safely pass through, one would need at least the strength of the peak Path Opening Realm.

Without reaching the peak Path Opening Realm, it would just depend on luck. During the period of cosmic destruction, the pressure each strongest expert had to endure was different. With good luck, one might be able to persevere.

As for the Dominance Realm after the Path Opening Realm, twelfth level experts of the Dominance Realm, even in the boundless chaos, were considered strong and ruled over everything.

"In a normal and complete cosmic cycle, it's extremely rare for a peak Path Opening Realm twelfth level expert to be born," Xuanxuan Du added.

If the Xuanxuan Patriarch could cultivate to the peak of the Path Opening Realm, the impact on human civilization would be unprecedented.

The explosive growth in combat strength went without saying, but at least when facing the catastrophe at the end of the cosmos, he could preserve a large part of the elite human civilization and smoothly enter the boundless chaos.

Lin Yuan was already well-versed in the specific divisions of the twelfth level realm, whether it was the Magic Jewel Space or the Xuanhuang Secret Realm, both had records on this.

"For now, the cosmos I'm in is still extremely young, having only experienced one-tenth of a cosmic cycle. As long as there are no accidents, it's still early before the destructive end period."

Lin Yuan thought to himself. A complete cosmic cycle, if measured in "years", would be an unimaginable number of billions.

For experts like the Lord of the Magic Jewel Tower and the Xuanhuang Emperor at the peak of the Chaos Realm, they had long abandoned using the time "units" within the cosmos to measure the years, instead preferring to use cosmic cycles.

Beings of different realms perceived the scale of time differently.

Lin Yuan chatted with Xuanxuan Du and Vice Patriarch Nalan for a while about the information of the twelfth level realm.

Soon, they shifted the topic to the upcoming super-large alien battlefield.

"Galactic Star Master, will you also enter this super-large battlefield?" Xuanxuan Du casually asked.

"I should go," Lin Yuan nodded. This was something he had discussed with the strongest experts.

Lin Yuan would participate in the form of the Dinghai Divine Needle, helping human civilization seize the first initiative in the super-large alien battlefield.

Of course, this matter was top secret. If the aliens found out, they might come up with countermeasures.

"Indeed."

Xuanxuan Du nodded, then seemed to think of something. He glanced at the other eleventh level cultivators around, shielded their surroundings, and said in a low voice:

"But rest assured, for this super-large alien battlefield, our human civilization is not unprepared, we have a key trump card in reserve."
