They had thought through all kinds of possible scenarios.

But the scene of the Milky Way Saint reaching out and grabbing the entire Source Fruit tree was still done by an avatar.

This left all the Supreme Saints watching in silence.

The scene was too inconceivable.

Could an avatar be this strong? Or rather, how powerful must the true self be, to casually condense an avatar this strong?

The Source Fruit tree itself belonged to this category. The actions of Supreme Saints like Wind Candle were like child's play in its eyes.

Let alone the fact that the rift connecting to the Chaos Void would close in three days. Even if it didn't close, Wind Candle and the other Supreme Saints definitely wouldn't be able to pluck the Source Fruit in the end.

Originally, the Source Fruit tree had planned to wait until Wind Candle and the other Supreme Saints went deeper, then make a move to devour them.

Unexpectedly, Lin Yuan arrived instead.

"Too strong."

The Source Fruit tree trembled in fear.

But compared to Lin Yuan, they were far too inferior.

Lin Yuan refreshed the Source Fruit tree's understanding of life below the 13th level.

With just one avatar having such strength, who in the Chaos Void could force the Milky Way Saint's true self to go all out?

The spirits of those special places?

Let's not mention that the spirits of special places cannot take action.

Even if they could act, they probably wouldn't be a match for this current avatar of the Milky Way Saint.

Not to mention the Milky Way Saint's true self.

In the Eye of All Laws' view, only the lofty 13th level beings could force the Milky Way Saint's true self to go all out.

But 13th level time beings were the least likely to take action.

The 13th level time beings wouldn't even pay attention to what was happening in the Chaos Void.

The gaze and mental energy of 13th level time beings were basically focused on the distant future. As for the past and present? Unless something happened that affected the foundation of the Chaos Void.

For example, if a special place was completely destroyed.

Otherwise, it was very difficult to attract the attention of 13th level time beings.

"I wonder if that one knows that the legacy left behind has cultivated an existence like the Milky Way Saint"

The Eye of All Laws thought to himself.

The so-called 'that one' was the founder of the 'Infinite Void', and also one of the time beings of this Chaos Void.

Over the long cosmic era, the Eye of All Laws had witnessed a succession of cultivators inheriting the 'Infinite Void'.

But Lin Yuan? All the 'Infinite Void' legacy cultivators in history combined probably wouldn't be enough for the Milky Way Saint to slap with one hand.

To be precise, they wouldn't be enough for one avatar of the Milky Way Saint to slap with one hand.

"Very smooth."

Lin Yuan reached out and retrieved the entire Source Fruit tree, without encountering any fierce resistance.

At the moment his avatar made a move, his true self had already turned attention to this side, ready to descend and take action at any time if something unexpected happened.

He had always held a sense of awe towards the dimensional gap.

The Great Dao rules operated within dimensional space, an environment very suitable for cultivation, equivalent to a cradle of life.

Some dimensional spaces even had great gifts for life.

For example, the Chaos Void's origin infusion was a 'benefit' that every life in this dimensional space could enjoy.

But the dimensional gap? The Great Dao rules did not exist, and it had to bear the pressure emitted by huge dimensional spaces.

Life that could survive in the dimensional gap was all very strong, because the weak simply couldn't survive.

In fact, the dimensional gap was also the battlefield of the 13th level time beings.

13th level time beings rarely fought and killed each other within dimensional spaces, as that would cause too much damage to the dimensional space itself.

Therefore, the battlefield of the 13th level was in the dimensional gap. Only this vast space could withstand the all-out battles between time beings.

"Thank you so much, Galaxy Great Saint," the Eye of All Laws said to Lin Yuan. The acquisition of this Law Source Fruit had been incredibly smooth.

The Eye of All Laws saying this was also a way of currying favor with Lin Yuan.
