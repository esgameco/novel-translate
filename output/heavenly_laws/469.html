Chapter 469 Immortal Source (Seeking Subscriptions)

It is not entirely accurate to say that it took five hundred years for Lin Yuan to comprehend this divine imprint.

It should be four hundred and ninety years, and Lin Yuan did not devote his full effort to the comprehension.

Apart from comprehending the rules of time and space, perfecting the Eleven Stages of the Martial Dao, and many other aspects, the true mental and spiritual energy he devoted to comprehending this divine imprint was only about one-fiftieth.

But even so, it still shows the extraordinary nature of this divine imprint. Given Lin Yuan's transcendent comprehension abilities, even with only one-fiftieth of his effort, it still took him close to five hundred years to fully comprehend it.

"Divine Technique: Rend the Heavens!"

Lin Yuan gazed at the swirling energy between his fingers.

The sixth Flesh Body Divine Technique, named "Rend the Heavens", is a pure and extreme offensive divine technique.

Lin Yuan's fifth Flesh Body Divine Technique, named "The World", is also an offensive divine technique, but it also has a suppressive effect. Within the area covered by the external world, all enemies will be suppressed.

But the "Rend the Heavens" divine technique? It has only one effect - to rend and shatter everything.

In terms of pure individual killing power, the might of the "Rend the Heavens" divine technique surpasses even the "The World".

After all, the offensive power of the "The World" divine technique, even its ultimate self-detonation strike, is still targeted at a group area.

While the "Rend the Heavens" divine technique, it is aimed at a single point or line, with the ultimate destructive power, which even makes Lin Yuan feel a sense of trepidation.

Since Lin Yuan awakened this Flesh Body Divine Technique, he has not fully utilized it, fearing that the scale of destruction it could cause might attract the attention of the Immortal Dao Ancestor.

"If measured by the standard of the main world's combat power, the limit of this divine technique's power should be between four hundred thousand to five hundred thousand."

Lin Yuan estimated in his heart.

It should be known that the measurement standard here, the Perfected Eleventh Stage, is the basic unit, which is one.

After Lin Yuan stepped into the Eleventh Stage, his combat power limit is also ten thousand. Subsequent growth in strength can reach tens of thousands at most.

The self-detonation of the external world can unleash a momentary power of around three hundred thousand.

And the "Rend the Heavens" divine technique, directly unleashing four to five hundred thousand combat power? The ultimate threshold of the Twelfth Stage is a hundred million.

"Too extreme."

Lin Yuan continued to observe the essence of the "Rend the Heavens" divine technique. The previous five Flesh Body Divine Techniques all empowered various aspects, but "Rend the Heavens" has only destruction.

"Unfortunately, in the short term, I can only unleash one strike of 'Rend the Heavens'." Lin Yuan thought to himself.

This is also very normal. The "Rend the Heavens" divine technique grants Lin Yuan a strike far exceeding his own strength, if he could use it freely, Lin Yuan would be afraid.

The three great protective secret techniques of the Xuanhuang lineage, the perfected level of the "Xuanhuang Single Strike", can only maintain Lin Yuan's combat power at six times his own, and it is based on a huge consumption.

And the "Rend the Heavens" divine technique? Not just sixty times or six hundred times the combat power explosion.

From three hundred thousand combat power to four to five hundred thousand, it may seem like less than a doubling, but the gap in between is unimaginable.

"Pity, the 'Rend the Heavens' divine technique cannot be combined with the 'The World' divine technique," Lin Yuan sighed.

These two divine techniques both belong to the offensive type and cannot be fused together.

However, Lin Yuan is already very satisfied. After all, whether it's the "The World" divine technique or the "Rend the Heavens" divine technique, their power is not fixed, but depends on Lin Yuan's own strength.

The "The World" divine technique depends on Lin Yuan's internal world, while the "Rend the Heavens" divine technique depends on Lin Yuan's life essence.

They will increase in power as Lin Yuan grows stronger.

Half a year later.

The Pavilion Master of the Immortal Transcendence Pavilion visited again.

"Haha, Master Yunzhen, have you summoned me because the Nine Revolutions Elixir has been successfully refined?" The Pavilion Master laughed heartily.

Whenever Lin Yuan successfully refines the Nine Revolutions Elixir, it will cause celestial anomalies, with dense storm clouds gathering. The Pavilion Master can thus infer the quality of the refined Nine Revolutions Elixir.

"I have not failed the task."

Lin Yuan extended his right hand, and a blue gourd appeared in front of the Pavilion Master.

The Pavilion Master carefully opened the gourd and poured out a golden immortal pill, examining it closely for a while.

"Not bad, not bad."

"This Nine Revolutions Elixir is definitely the highest quality I have ever seen among all the Nine Revolutions Elixirs." The Pavilion Master was extremely satisfied.

He had intentionally offered a half-sale, half-gift arrangement, using three types of immortal sources as remuneration, just to assess Master Yunzhen's alchemy skills, not as simple as it appeared on the surface.

Now it seems the Pavilion Master's gamble paid off, as this highest quality Nine Revolutions Elixir alone is enough to demonstrate Lin Yuan's alchemy mastery, surpassing even the great alchemists.

"The fifty Nine Revolutions Elixirs are all inside, I hope the Pavilion Master can count them."

Lin Yuan said.

The blue gourd contained the fifty Nine Revolutions Elixirs that were part of the transaction with the Pavilion Master.

In this batch of refinement, Lin Yuan had successfully refined a total of one hundred and thirty-two elixirs. Apart from these fifty, Lin Yuan had secretly taken eighty-two Nine Revolutions Elixirs for himself.

"Haha, Master Yunzhen directly handed over all the Nine Revolutions Elixirs to me, isn't he afraid I'll just leave?" The Pavilion Master's face broke into a smile.

Fifty Nine Revolutions Elixirs would be enough to drive ordinary Jade Immortals mad. Even the Sect Leader of the Azure Dragon Sect...
