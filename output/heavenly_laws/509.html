"I'm not sure either," Wanhua's mother queen shook her head.

She truly couldn't understand why, if the Jie Clan wanted to seize the opportunity to make demands, they didn't even give her a chance to speak.

Not only did the Jie Clan not participate in the plan to deal with the Galactic Overlord, they had directly notified the Galactic Overlord of their plans.

In this case, when the Galactic Overlord eventually reached the Twelfth-Level, the Jie Clan wouldn't have too much trouble.

After a long silence, one of the Twelfth-Level Ultimates spoke up.

"So what do we do now?"

The outbreak of war would require a huge amount of time, especially for an all-out war. The various peak clan forces would likely have to spend hundreds or thousands of years preparing.

By the time that much time had passed, the Galactic Overlord would probably have already broken through to the Ultimate level, rendering it meaningless.

And dozens of the strongest experts taking action and attacking the human civilization domain was also not very realistic.

The nine Strongest Experts of the human civilization didn't even need to engage in direct combat, they could just hide within their Clan Weapons.

Moreover, with the existence of these Twelfth-Level Ultimates, they were also unwilling to do this. If they really pushed the nine Strongest Experts of the human civilization to the brink, and they fought to the death, could they on this side emerge unscathed? The only use of this would be to interfere with the Galactic Overlord's breakthrough to the Ultimate level, but how much could they really interfere?

"Actually, there is another way,"

At this moment, a Zhozu Clan Ultimate spoke up.

The surrounding Twelfth-Level Ultimates immediately perked up and turned their gaze towards him.

"The Galactic Overlord is indeed very strong, but even if he's strong, there's still a chance he could fail in his breakthrough to the Ultimate level," this Zhozu Clan Ultimate slowly said, "We just need to wait for the Galactic Overlord's breakthrough to the Ultimate level to fail."

As long as the Galactic Overlord's breakthrough to the Ultimate level failed and he was completely destroyed, the balance of the Twelve Great Peak Forces in the universe would be restored.

"Wait for the Galactic Overlord's breakthrough to the Ultimate level to fail?" Wanhua's mother queen fell silent again.

Wasn't this just leaving everything to the whims of fate?

After a long silence, Wanhua's mother queen spoke up again, "But what if the Galactic Overlord succeeds in breaking through to the Twelfth-Level?"

"Succeeds in breaking through to the Twelfth-Level?" The Zhozu Clan Ultimate shook his head, "Then I'll be out of options."

Although the "solution" proposed by this Zhozu Clan Ultimate was not really a solution at all, in a certain sense, it was the only hope left for all the Twelfth-Level Ultimates.

When breaking through to the Ultimate level, one would face the suppression and backlash of the long river of time and the entire universe.

No matter how powerful one's life essence was, there was still a possibility of complete destruction.

And this was the only way for the Galactic Overlord to be completely destroyed at the moment.

In the Xuanhuang Secret Realm, Lin Yuan sat in meditation.

At this moment, all aspects of him had reached the pinnacle, reaching perfection, and he was completely ready to break through to the Ultimate level.

"The Gate of the Myriad Realms."

Lin Yuan's mind sank into his sea of consciousness, gazing at the majestic and grand Gate of the Myriad Realms.

At this moment, the coordinates of the first Source World were vaguely visible, a boundless and vast world, no less than the Chaotic Void, with a surging aura.

"Once I've stabilized after breaking through to the Twelfth-Level, I'll carry out the Twelfth Traversal."

Lin Yuan thought to himself.

Why did he have to wait until everything was stable before carrying out the next traversal? The time flow in the Source World was the same as the Chaotic Void, where one year there was equivalent to about three hundred years in the main universe.

And as long as Lin Yuan stayed in that Source World for a few years, thousands of years would have passed in the main universe.

Therefore, Lin Yuan had to wait until there were no more hidden dangers on his side before carrying out the Twelfth Traversal.

Otherwise, if he was in the middle of a traversal and something happened in the universe, wouldn't he have to abort the traversal?

In the previous eleven traversals, the time flow in the universe was slower than the time flow in those traversal worlds, so no matter how long Lin Yuan stayed in the traversal worlds, not much time would have passed in the universe.

"I can feel that after breaking through to the Twelfth-Level, the Gate of the Myriad Realms will undergo some kind of change," Lin Yuan thought with anticipation, as his comprehension of space-time had reached the Seventh Realm.
