"Can I take away all the treasures in the human civilization's treasure trove?"

It's just that if there's a need, you can take it away.

But the Xuanyuan Supremacy Powerhouse didn't know this.

Since the Xuanyuan Supremacy Powerhouse said so, he must have prepared for a massive reduction in the rare treasures of the human civilization's treasure trove.

"As long as you can successfully cross into the twelfth level, any price that human civilization pays will be worth it," the Xuanyuan Supremacy Powerhouse said.

"Before the final assault, remember to use those auxiliary treasures in the treasure trove," the Xuanyuan Supremacy Powerhouse reminded.

In the treasure trove of human civilization, there are some rare treasures that can help with the final assault, such as the Cosmic Source that Lin Yuan obtained earlier, which can increase the time foundation in his inner world.

In addition, there are some other rare treasures similar to the Cosmic Source, and the ancient gods who have reached the eleventh level of perfection in the depths of the cosmic starry sky, don't know how much they crave these rare treasures.

"Understood," Lin Yuan nodded.

After being promoted to an eleventh-level citizen, Lin Yuan's personal authority has reached the highest level of human civilization, and he can view the information of any treasure.

"Also, when you're preparing for the final assault, be sure to say that we're doing this to guard against those alien twelfth-level beings," the Xuanyuan Supremacy Powerhouse said.

The leap from the eleventh level to the twelfth level, after the final assault, the essence of life and everything about oneself will transcend the long river of time, and cannot be hindered or influenced, even the Supremacy Powerhouse cannot interfere.

But before that, bearing the infusion of the chaotic void origin, if the twelfth-level final assault is discovered in time, there is still a chance to intervene.

Of course, it's just a possibility.

But Lin Yuan is of utmost importance to human civilization, so the Xuanyuan Supremacy Powerhouse naturally wants to eliminate all possibilities.

"Okay."

Lin Yuan nodded.

When he broke through to the tenth and eleventh levels, there were Supremacy Powerhouses guarding him, and the assault on the twelfth level would be no exception.

"Then you keep cultivating, and if you have any requests, just tell me or Xia Qin directly," the Xuanyuan Supremacy Powerhouse said, then cut off the video call.

Lin Yuan sat cross-legged and began to browse through the many treasures in the human civilization's treasure trove.

Ordinary rare treasures, even at the twelfth-star level, have little effect on Lin Yuan.

Lin Yuan's main focus is on the rare treasures that the Xuanyuan Supremacy Powerhouse mentioned, which can assist in the final assault.

"These rare treasures."

Lin Yuan gave them a brief glance and shook his head slightly.

The rare treasures in the human civilization's treasure trove that can assist in the final assault are absolutely a godsend for ordinary eleventh-level perfected experts.

But for Lin Yuan, they basically have no effect.

The rare treasures that can assist in the final assault are also graded, with the Time-Space Heart left by the Demon Jade Pavilion Master being the highest grade, while the Cosmic Source and the like belong to the middle grade.

The probability of an ordinary eleventh-level expert breaking through to the final level is about one in ten thousand to one in a hundred thousand.

In such a case, even the lowest-grade treasure to assist in the final assault can have a great effect.

But for Lin Yuan? The probability of breaking through to the final level is estimated to be 95% or even 97% or 98%.

With Lin Yuan's accumulated foundation, even a high-grade treasure like the Time-Space Heart would have a very limited effect on him, and the auxiliary rare treasures in the human civilization's treasure trove would basically be of no help.

Raising the probability from one in ten thousand to two in ten thousand is not that difficult, but raising it from 98% to 99% is as difficult as climbing to the sky.

In fact, even those eleventh-level lives with towering backgrounds in the chaotic void, the probability of breaking through to the final level would not exceed 20%.

Lin Yuan's ability to reach 95% is entirely due to his mastery of the chaotic rules and the seven-layer realm of time and space.

"These auxiliary rare treasures can be left for the future evolution practitioners who will break through to the twelfth level," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

So in the Chaotic Void, the destructive power of experts from the main universe would drop significantly.

Of course, the Chaotic Void with its more complete and vast rules would have a higher ceiling for any expert. At least in the Chaotic Void, the difficulty of entering the Mastery Realm would be slightly easier than in the universe.

Just by casually sending out an incarnation to explore, he had encountered the opportunity to obtain heavenly treasures falling from the sky?
