"Give it to him?"

The Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies maintained a calm smile on the surface, but his heart was churning with shock.

Give it to him? That "Fragment World" worth 1.2 million chaos stones, just give it directly to Lin Yuan?

But the one who sent the message was his master Elder Gu Ming, the ruler of this Chaos Continent. If Elder Gu Ming said to give it to Lin Yuan, then it must be given.

"Master, how should I give it?"

The Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies hurriedly sent a mental message to inquire.

As Elder Gu Ming's disciple, the Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies had ways to directly contact his master.

"Just find some excuse to give it to him. Remember, don't make it seem too deliberate," that voice continued to sound in the Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies' ear.

This left the Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies lost in thought.

He discreetly sized up Lin Yuan.

From his master's tone of voice, the Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies could tell he wanted to befriend, or even curry favor with, Lin Yuan.

A "Fragment World" worth 1.2 million, given away just like that, and they even had to find a suitable excuse to give it, for fear of making Lin Yuan suspicious and wary.

If this wasn't befriending and currying favor, what was it? The Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies was very familiar with this tone - as a disciple of an Elder, he was often on the receiving end of other powerful beings trying to curry favor with him.

But now, it was Elder Gu Ming taking the initiative to curry favor with Lin Yuan.

Elder Gu Ming was a Chaos Elder, and quite powerful even among Chaos Elders. What kind of being would even Elder Gu Ming need to curry favor with?

The Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies' mind trembled, not daring to think further.

"Could it be that Brother Yinhe has some incredible background?"

The Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies' understanding led him to only consider whether Lin Yuan had some great background.

"These 'Fragment Worlds'?"

Lin Yuan carefully examined the other dozens of "Fragment Worlds".

In terms of quality, they all met Lin Yuan's requirements. Although far inferior to the Star Tear Stone "Fragment World", the prices were there, at least within Lin Yuan's budget.

"I'll take this one."

After much comparison, Lin Yuan finally selected a "Fragment World".

This "Fragment World" was only about one hundred-thousandth the size of a normal universe, but its advantage was that it had no radiation and would not negatively impact weak and frail human citizens.

"Yinhe."

The Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies waved his hand, and all the miniature "Fragment World" projections in front of him disappeared, leaving only that Star Tear "Fragment World".

"Brother Ten Thousand Melodies?"

Lin Yuan looked at the Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies with a puzzled expression.

"I feel like I've known Brother Yinhe for years. If Brother Yinhe wants this Star Tear 'Fragment World', I can make the decision to trade it to you first."

"As for the chaos stones? You can give them whenever you have them in the future."

The Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies said.

"Trade it to me first?"

Lin Yuan was shocked after hearing this.

Give the chaos stones whenever he has them in the future, but trade the "Fragment World" to him first?

No matter where, money and goods changing hands simultaneously is the most basic principle of transactions. Some places even require paying a "deposit" in advance.

As for taking the goods first and paying whenever you have the money in the future, how was this any different from giving it away for free?

"There's no other way. This Star Tear 'Fragment World' is too expensive, probably won't have any buyers. Rather than letting it sit empty, might as well give it to Brother Yinhe first."

The Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies said with a smile: "Don't worry, Brother Yinhe. This is a normal transaction. You don't need to bear any karmic burden or feel any pressure."

"A normal transaction?"

Lin Yuan looked at him.

Feeling quite strange inside.

He also understood the meaning behind the Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies' words.

The more powerful a being, the more they care about karma, and the more unwilling they are to owe favors.

The Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies was practically giving away this "Fragment World" for free. Without that sentence, Lin Yuan would probably have considered whether he would owe a karmic debt.

First giving away the "Fragment World" for free.

Then promising no karmic burden.

For a moment, Lin Yuan didn't know what the Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies' purpose was.

"Is it Elder Gu Ming?"

Lin Yuan pondered to himself.

As an Elder's disciple, the Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies had great authority.

But no matter how great, there were still limits. Giving away a "Fragment World" worth 1.2 million chaos stones for free? If Elder Gu Ming hadn't nodded and agreed behind the scenes,

This simply wouldn't be possible.

"What do you say? If Brother Yinhe agrees, we can settle this matter now. The ownership of the Star Tear 'Fragment World' can be transferred over immediately."

The Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies said.

"Thank you."

Lin Yuan didn't hesitate at all.

A "Fragment World" worth 1.2 million - even with karmic restrictions, Lin Yuan would consider it, let alone without any karmic burden?

It was equivalent to picking it up for free, so why not take it? After all, the human civilization had just left their universe. The better the environment of their new home, the more beneficial it would be for the future development of human civilization. Lin Yuan wouldn't have any reservations about this.

"Good."

"This is the core control formation for the Star Tear 'Fragment World'."

The Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies produced a crystal with a flip of his hand. Earlier, when the Chaos Dragon Beast's roar shattered countless massive meteorites that collapsed into many "Fragment Worlds", he had taken the initiative to organize them and integrate them into the grand formation of the Gu Ming Chaos Continent.

This crystal represented the control rights to that Star Tear "Fragment World".

Lin Yuan took the crystal and briefly examined it, his heart slightly settling.

Next, the Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies chatted with Lin Yuan for a while longer, mainly introducing some uses of the "Fragment World".

After parting with the Lord of Ten Thousand Melodies, Lin Yuan got up and left the hall.

"Why did Elder Gu Ming give me such a 'Fragment World'?" Lin Yuan walked in the trading area, pondering in his heart.

First of all, Elder Gu Ming certainly had no hostility towards him.

With Elder Gu Ming's status as a Chaos Elder, if he had any killing intent, this avatar of Lin Yuan would have been destroyed long ago, with no chance of survival.

There was also no hatred between Lin Yuan and Elder Gu Ming, and it was even more impossible for there to be any grudges.

Lin Yuan could sense Elder Gu Ming's goodwill towards him. If he had even slightly ulterior motives, he wouldn't have absolved Lin Yuan of any karmic debt.

"At least the clan's new home is settled for now."

Lin Yuan looked up at that Star Tear "Fragment World", thinking to himself.

He had originally thought he would have to settle for second best, choosing those slightly inferior "Fragment Worlds". He didn't expect the most satisfying Star Tear "Fragment World" would still end up in his hands, and in an almost "free" way.

In the deepest part of the Chaos Continent.

Elder Gu Ming and the black-robed youth were watching the scenes in the hall.

"That 'Yinhe' accepted the Fragment World." Elder Gu Ming let out a slight sigh of relief, a smile appearing on his face.

According to the black-robed youth's "foresight" talent, he predicted that Lin Yuan would very likely become a Chaos Great Saint in the future. Faced with such a being of limitless potential, not offending him was the most basic approach - to do otherwise would be seeking death.

The next step was to consider how to befriend him.

This befriending required great finesse. First, they couldn't make the other party feel any pressure or discomfort. For example, if Elder Gu Ming appeared directly and offered various treasures, it would instead make Lin Yuan wary.
