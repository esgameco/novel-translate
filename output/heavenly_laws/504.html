Facing the more than a dozen towering figures that had descended in complete silence, even the vast spatial black hole spanning thousands and thousands of light-years seemed somewhat insignificant.

"Twelve-tier alien existences?"

Lin Yuan looked at the numerous twelve-tier ultimate beings who had sealed the space-time, and there was no fear in his heart, but rather a hint of surprise.

How had these twelve-tier alien ultimate beings locked onto his precise spatial coordinates?

"Has the karmic connection between myself and a certain twelve-tier life form increased exponentially by tens of thousands of times in a short period of time?"

After a brief examination, Lin Yuan found the reason.

He knew of some secret techniques that could temporarily increase karmic connections, but they all required a tremendous price to be paid.

Especially given Lin Yuan's profound understanding of the karmic rules, for these twelve-tier ultimates to try and lock onto his location through karmic connections?

Even if they were twelve-tier existences, they would still suffer severe injuries, and it might take them millions of years to recover.

Boom!! At this moment, the ancestor of the Tianyu Clan slammed down a palm, intending to directly suppress Lin Yuan.

The other twelve-tier ultimate beings did not intervene, but instead watched coldly. In their eyes, the Tianyu Clan ancestor alone was enough.

No matter how brilliant and extraordinary the Galactic Overlord was, he was only an Eleventh-tier existence, and there was no way he could withstand the personal strike of a Twelve-tier ultimate.

The Tianyu Clan ancestor thought the same.

From the very beginning of the universe to the present, even with the occasional information they obtained from the chaotic void outside the universe, they had never heard of an Eleventh-tier existence being able to contend with a Twelve-tier existence.

"Hmm?"

The expressions of the Strongest of the Human Civilization, Xuanyuan and Xia Qin, suddenly changed.

The disturbance caused by the Twelve-tier ultimate's strike was so great that it could not be concealed. The moment the auras of the more than a dozen Twelve-tier aliens descended upon the spatial black hole, the nine Strongest of the Human Civilization sensed it.

"Those old fellows."

Xuanyuan's thought processes were lightning-fast, and he immediately deduced that the more than a dozen alien Twelve-tiers were likely going to attack Lin Yuan.

Lin Yuan had shrouded his own karmic connections, so not only were the alien Twelve-tiers unable to detect anything, but the Strongest of the Human Civilization were also unable to sense much through karmic perception.

Whoosh. Xuanyuan's true body took a step forward, traversing billions of light-years in an instant, rushing towards the location of the spatial black hole.

The other few Strongest of the Human Civilization did the same.

"Lin Yuan." Xia Qin's heart was filled with worry.

More than a hundred years ago, he had planned to send Lin Yuan's true body into the Central Star Realm, which was the safest place in the entire Human Civilization.

"With the Xuanhuang Secret Realm shielding karmic connections, those old fellows won't be able to affect Lin Yuan's true body at all. And as for memory contamination? Xia Qin's thoughts raced, and he felt slightly relieved.

In his view, those alien Twelve-tiers would be completely incapable of harming the Galactic Overlord, at most they could only kill one of his projections or avatars.

However, even so, Xia Qin and the other nine Strongest of the Human Civilization did not slow down in the slightest.

Being unable to harm Lin Yuan was one thing, but attacking him was another.

Even if they couldn't cause any impact, as Twelve-tier existences, striking an Eleventh-tier life form was a violation of the unspoken rules between Twelve-tier ultimates!

The spatial pressure became increasingly heavy.

The Tianyu Clan ancestor's gaze was cold as he looked down on Lin Yuan, but his descending palm did not pause in the slightest.

From the moment of their arrival to the present, everything had happened in the blink of an eye. Even if the Galactic Overlord barely reacted in time and wanted to self-detonate this projection, he wouldn't have the time.

Self-detonating a projection required time.

By the time the Galactic Overlord's self-detonation order was transmitted, the Tianyu Clan ancestor's palm would have already suppressed him.

However.

The next moment.

Under the incredulous gaze of the surrounding more than a dozen Twelve-tier ultimates, the seemingly stunned Galactic Overlord took a single step backwards.

Just a single step away, as if a universe separated them, the Tianyu Clan ancestor's palm struck empty air.

The spatial fluctuations were violent enough to annihilate an Eleventh-tier being in body and spirit, but when they approached within a few meters of Lin Yuan, they quickly subsided.

"This?" The Tianyu Clan ancestor was somewhat dazed. His Twelve-tier ultimate-level strike had missed.

Prior to this, the Tianyu Clan ancestor had anticipated any possible scenario, including the possibility that Lin Yuan might withstand the strike using the Human Civilization's foundational weapons.

Of course, this possibility was slim. How could such precious foundational weapons be carried by a projection or avatar? Even the mightiest Eleventh-tier beings in the grand battlefields only carried clan-protecting weapons under the watchful eyes of the Twelve-tier ultimates.

But to miss? The Tianyu Clan ancestor could not understand it. He could sense that this was a pure confrontation at the rules level.

To forcibly block his strike might have involved external aid, but a rules-level confrontation? It meant that the Galactic Overlord's understanding of the rules was not weaker than his own.

The Twelve-tier ultimates surrounding them had grave expressions. At this moment, they were certain that Lin Yuan's understanding of the rules had reached the Twelve-tier level in all aspects.

Not only had he reached their level, but he had surpassed them.

After all, for an Eleventh-tier existence to casually let the Tianyu Clan ancestor's Twelve-tier strike miss, this could not be explained by just being on the same rules level.

In this situation, their collective Twelve-tier ultimate assault would be nothing but a joke.

Lin Yuan's rules mastery had reached such a height that even though he was still an Eleventh-tier existence, he was far from being their match.

But it also ruled out the possibility of him being suppressed, because Lin Yuan was fully capable of self-detonating this projection before being suppressed.

And the prerequisite for using that strange blood to contaminate and influence Lin Yuan was to completely suppress him, to the point where he could not even have a single thought.

But now, this possibility was approaching zero.

Lin Yuan was not a fool, he would surely self-detonate before realizing he was about to be suppressed.
