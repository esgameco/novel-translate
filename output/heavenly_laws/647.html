The golden mist appeared like clouds, able to be captured and contained, but Lin Yuan could not touch it.

"Unable to touch it?"

Lin Yuan's gaze was strange, the divine ability 'World' constantly operating, with the countless dimensions all under his control, yet he could not truly make contact with the golden mist.

"Interesting."

Lin Yuan stretched out his right hand, prepared to collect all the golden mist in the valley.

To ensure the golden mist would not be damaged, Lin Yuan moved the entire valley into his cosmic universe.

"It should be related to time."

Lin Yuan looked at the golden mist, thinking to himself.

He could faintly sense temporal fluctuations from the golden mist, combined with the time reversal region outside the blessed land, he could basically confirm this newly born first-grade blessed land was inclined towards the attribute of time.

In fact, the many conjectures of the ancient nations regarding this blessed land also believed the rare treasures it nurtured would be related to time.

"Should I try a spiritual soul inquiry?"

Lin Yuan pondered.

Many rare treasures nurtured in blessed lands must be absorbed and refined using special methods.

For example, the black jade nurtured in the Black Jade Nest could only be refined by the will of the spirit and soul. As for other methods? They would have no effect at all.

"Forget it, it's too risky."

After brief consideration, Lin Yuan abandoned this idea.

The spirit and soul were too important. What if this golden rare treasure had some side effect or harm to the spirit and soul? Wouldn't that be courting disaster?

Lin Yuan dared to refine the black jade with the will of his spirit and soul because the Black Jade Nest had existed for countless billions of years, and the refinement method of the black jade nurtured within had been verified by countless Supremes, without any hidden dangers.

But this golden mist? Who knows what effects it might have.

Lin Yuan had no need to be too impatient.

He could obtain the golden mist in this valley, and the Supremes of the other ancient nations could certainly also obtain it.

Compared to the experienced Supremes of the six ancient nations, Lin Yuan felt he should wait a bit longer, until the proper usage method of the golden mist was explored, before attempting it himself.

"Continue searching for the core celestial pillar."

Lin Yuan did not linger too long in the valley, and after collecting all the golden mist, he continued to search in the direction.

The Crimson Flame Supreme frowned and quickly asked, "Which Supreme?"

If it was a top Supreme, the Crimson Flame Supreme would suggest the Scarlet Lotus Supreme yield that core celestial pillar.

It was not out of fear of the Great Submersion Ancient Nation, but because with the Scarlet Lotus Supreme's strength, he would not be able to hold out for long against a top Supreme from the Great Submersion Ancient Nation.

Unless the Scarlet Lotus Supreme had hidden his true strength like Lin Yuan, but the possibility of that was too low.

When it came to the ownership of this first-grade blessed land, as long as there was a chance to win, they must fight for it.

Although the Submergence Sway Supreme was stronger than him, it was not a matter of winning or losing against the Submergence Sway Supreme, only to hold him off, and the Scarlet Lotus Supreme was confident in that.

As one of the four top Supremes of the Blazing Sun Ancient Nation, Lin Yuan also had the task of refining the core celestial pillars.

If the core celestial pillars discovered by the other Supremes of the Blazing Sun Ancient Nation were closest to him, Lin Yuan would need to go there.

Normally, searching for the core celestial pillars would require slowly flying and searching.

But to quickly reach a certain coordinate, he could directly teleport through the void.

The majestic celestial pillar seemed illusory.

Below the Submergence Void Supreme were four top Supremes, and the Submergence Sway Supreme was the third tier of Supremes after them.
