Linyuan's thoughts wandered as he gazed at the increasing number of Longshan Clan members.

What those fourth-rank peak knights from the major duchies did not know was that the Longshan Duke they fought against was actually an existence comparable to a true god.

After the Longshan Clan publicly spread the martial path cultivation method and it rapidly transmitted, it also reached the Imperial Court.

Fabian immediately realized the importance of the martial path cultivation method, as it may trigger a revolution in all knights' cultivation methods. He promptly brought everyone to Longshan City to secretly observe that Longshan Duke.

Heavens!
