Chapter 475 The Ancestor of the Abyss of Demons' Confusion (Seeking Subscriptions)

Lin Yuan crossed into the Seventh Realm of Time and Space without any obstruction.

When a large amount of spiritual enlightenment converged, Lin Yuan comprehended the essence of the Seventh Realm of Time and Space.

Lin Yuan's accumulation was too formidable, coupled with the special cultivation environment of the Immortal Realm, as well as the scene of the Immortal Source where the Immortal Realm's origin was condensed, and so on.

The result of the accumulation of various foundations.

Of course, the most important thing among all the foundations was Lin Yuan's extraordinary perceptive ability.

Without the extraordinary perceptive ability to integrate everything, even if the above-mentioned environmental conditions were strengthened tenfold, it would be meaningless.

In the Immortal Realm, which of the direct disciples of the Ancestors of the Immortal Dao had a cultivation environment and resources not as good as Lin Yuan's? They could even directly enter the origin of the Immortal Realm to cultivate.

But so far, none of the Ancestor's disciples have crossed into the Seventh Realm of Time and Space in terms of spiritual enlightenment.

Not to mention the Ancestor's disciples, even the Ancestors themselves have never touched the Seventh Realm in terms of spiritual enlightenment.

"I see."

Lin Yuan's expression was enlightened. After reaching the Seventh Realm of Time and Space, the time and space he felt seemed to have come alive.

It was not that time and space had come alive, but that time and space was originally alive and constantly changing, it was just that Lin Yuan could not perceive or observe it before.

"This is time and space!"

Lin Yuan's gaze was distant, the time and space of the entire Immortal Realm was transparent to him, and even the time and space of the Abyss of Demons was vividly presented before his eyes.

"It turns out that the Immortal Realm and the Abyss of Demons are actually the same world, it's just that due to the difference in the nature of the rules, there is a clear distinction." Lin Yuan suddenly realized.

Previously, he thought that the Abyss of Demons was a certain spatial layer of the Immortal Realm, but in fact, the Abyss of Demons also belongs to the main space of the Immortal Realm, and the Immortal Realm may not even occupy a dominant position for the Abyss of Demons.

The Immortal Realm and the Abyss of Demons are equal, without any distinction of primary and secondary.

"Hmm?"

"The subordinate worlds of the Immortal Realm?"

Lin Yuan's gaze shifted, and he looked beyond the boundless and endless power of time and space towards the outside of the Immortal Realm.

The level where the Immortal Realm is located is the supreme, the pinnacle of this dimension.

But below the Immortal Realm, there are three thousand three hundred and thirty-three worlds floating.

Among these three thousand three hundred and thirty-three worlds, the Spiritual Realm that Lin Yuan had descended upon was among them.

And below these three thousand three hundred and thirty-three worlds, each world has more than a million lower-level worlds, and the Middle Earth Divine Continent is among them.

"Three thousand three hundred and thirty-three? This number again?"

Lin Yuan pondered for a moment and withdrew his gaze.

With his current mastery of time and space, as long as he wished, he could easily descend to any world at this latitude.

But it would be meaningless.

The lower-level worlds are likely to have difficulty even accommodating Lin Yuan, like the Middle Earth Divine Continent before, which was probably not as large as the world within Lin Yuan. Descending there would not achieve anything.

"My current strength..."

Lin Yuan began to examine himself.

According to the information left by Emperor Xuanhuang and the Demon Jade Pavilion Master, the Seventh Realm of Time and Space is normally only attainable by Twelve Tribulation Sovereign Realm cultivators.

A few of the top Twelve Tribulation Dao Splitting cultivators may also have the chance to touch it.

In other words, now, relying solely on his time and space enlightenment, Lin Yuan has surpassed the vast majority of Twelve Tribulation Dao Splitting cultivators.

Relying on his time and space enlightenment, in a direct power confrontation, Lin Yuan is not the opponent of the ultimate Twelve Tribulation Sovereign Realm, but he can completely use his time and space enlightenment to "play" with those Twelve Tribulation Sovereign Realm cultivators.

Just like the Mysterious Yellow Forbidden Realm, which is deep within the main universe's time and space, but opens only once every few tens of thousands of years, and all Twelve Tribulation Sovereign Realm cultivators are unable to lock onto its coordinates.

This is the suppression from the level of rules.

"This is the Seventh Realm of Time and Space."

Lin Yuan thought quickly.

For almost all life forms, the difficulty of awakening the Seventh Realm of Time and Space is greater than breaking through to the ultimate.

After all, the vast majority of Twelve Tribulation Dao Splitting Realm experts have not reached the Seventh Realm of Time and Space in their spiritual enlightenment.

"Now I..."

Lin Yuan's thoughts shifted, and after crossing into the Seventh Realm of Time and Space, with a single thought, his true body, spiritual sense, and various incarnations all merged into the depths of time and space.

Even Twelve Tribulation Sovereign Realm cultivators would not be able to detect him at all, unless Lin Yuan actively revealed himself.

In simple terms, the current Lin Yuan is like a high-latitude observer, and even if it's a Twelve Tribulation Sovereign Realm cultivator, as long as their rule enlightenment has not reached a certain level, they don't even have the ability to sense Lin Yuan.

"So time and space can be used in this way?"

Lin Yuan continued to feel the changes in himself.

After reaching the Seventh Realm of Time and Space, the boundless time and space seemed to have changed in Lin Yuan's eyes, and many time and space mysteries that he could not understand before were now completely resolved.

Just as Lin Yuan continued to seclude himself and familiarize himself with the Seventh Realm of Time and Space,

Outside the dwelling, the City Lord of the Immortal City and a group of high-level bigwigs gathered.

They had an anxious and slightly uneasy expression. After sensing that Lin Yuan's dwelling was out of sync with the surrounding environment, they speculated that Lin Yuan might be related to the powerful existence that attacked the outside.

After a brief exchange and communication, the City Lord of the Immortal City and the other high-level bigwigs put down the nearly ruined Cangwu Immortal City, and came to visit Lin Yuan as the first priority.

There was no other choice.

If Lin Yuan was indeed related to the powerful existence that had struck, the first thing they needed to do was to find out the powerful existence's intentions and what it wanted, rather than to restore the Cangwu Immortal City.

After all, the powerful existence's attack was definitely not without reason.

If they didn't figure out these things, the City Lord of the Immortal City and the other high-level bigwigs wouldn't even be able to sleep soundly, let alone stabilize the situation in the Cangwu Immortal City.
