Chapter 207: Spreading Martial Arts, Planning Ahead (Happy New Year)

Lin Yuan gazed expressionlessly at the many powerful figures below him, their postures respectful. These people could be said to be the most elite forces in the entire Tianqing World.

Or rather, they were the core group that held the reins of power in the Tianqing Alliance.

Indeed, within the Tianqing Alliance, the Alliance Leader was the strongest. But to command a force, relying solely on the strongest was useless.

It still required subordinates. Power did not flow from top to bottom, but from bottom to top.

The Tianqing Alliance Leader was usually either in secluded cultivation or battling the Ancestral Demon Tree.

Where would he find the time to manage the Tianqing Alliance?

Simply put, these people were the actual controllers of the entire Tianqing Alliance, and thus the Tianqing World.

With Lin Yuan controlling these people, saying he controlled the Tianqing World was not an exaggeration in the slightest.

As for the Tianqing Alliance Leader?

His most trusted subordinates, cherished lovers, valued offspring, etc., had all become Lin Yuan's people now, and were currently completely unaware.

To Lin Yuan, the Tianqing Alliance Leader was nothing more than meat on a chopping block, to be dealt with whenever he pleased.

"Too smooth."

Lin Yuan swept his gaze over the crowd, his heart inevitably feeling a tinge of emotion.

When he first started, he merely wanted to find a few powerful figures from the Tianqing Alliance to parasitize, then infiltrate the Tianqing Alliance.

And slowly develop from there.

Who would have thought development would be so fast, he simply couldn't stop the momentum. Before he knew it, he had accidentally become the behind-the-scenes controller of the Tianqing World.

Lin Yuan began to review in his heart.

Firstly, for things to go so smoothly,

It was greatly related to the Tianqing Alliance Leader's decision to launch a counterattack against the foreign evil gods, dispatching all twelve sub-leaders and many guardian elders into the Tianqing World, mobilizing their foundation.

If these powerful figures of the Tianqing Alliance were still on the evil god battlefield, it would have been very difficult for Lin Yuan to make a move.

Because in order to prevent "desertion", the Tianqing Alliance Leader had long ago decreed that cultivators on the evil god battlefield were forbidden from returning to the Tianqing World without his orders.

This would have made Lin Yuan's "parasitism" not go very smoothly.

Because the parasitic ability of the perfected Demon Seed required Lin Yuan to be present to personally utilize it.

If those powerful figures did not come, Lin Yuan definitely could not go to the evil god battlefield alone.

But under the Tianqing Alliance Leader's orders, the various sub-leaders and guardian elders all returned, providing Lin Yuan with an excellent external environment.

Secondly, emotions.

Generally speaking, the stronger one was, the more lonely they were, and the fewer people they could call good friends.

Without deep feelings, if you said some place had a peerless treasure, basically no powerful figure would believe it.

You say there's a treasure, so there's a treasure?

Perhaps only with a life-and-death level of trust.

The foundation of trust was very high. Moreover, being in the Tianqing World, everyone's mood was good and their guard was lowered. Thus, they were attracted over one by one, allowing Lin Yuan to "parasitize" them individually.

Furthermore, it was Lin Yuan's perfected "parasitism" ability.

This reason could be said to be the most important point. The Tianqing Alliance was very familiar with the Ancestral Demon Tree's parasitic ability.

They never imagined that a parasitized powerful figure would perfectly replace the original, causing no one to suspect in that direction.

This was also why these powerful figures of the Tianqing World were inadequate in dealing with sudden situations and had no countermeasures.

Lastly,

It was because Lin Yuan's strength was sufficiently powerful.

Even if he lured them in one by one, those twelve sub-leaders and four sacred beast kings all had seventh level battle power.

Although far inferior to Lin Yuan, they still had a chance to transmit information out.

However, under the Space Prison Lin Yuan had comprehended, isolating heaven and earth, those sub-leaders and sacred beast kings were successfully parasitized without sending out any information.

Most importantly, the parasitized people did not die, but lived on in another way. Thus, life fire and other methods could not detect any abnormalities.

Because the people did not die, life fire and many other life-or-death divination methods were naturally useless.

"Master, this is everything I know."

An extremely beautiful woman respectfully said. This woman was the Tianqing Alliance Leader's mistress.

With her account, Lin Yuan's understanding of the Tianqing Alliance Leader had reached an unprecedented level.

He even knew the color of the other party's underwear.

"Master, should we lure the Alliance Leader over now?"

Another sub-leader respectfully inquired. This sub-leader had made great contributions to Lin Yuan smoothly taking control of the Tianqing World this time.

He had brought over most of the Tianqing Alliance's sub-leaders.

Because this sub-leader was the current Tianqing Alliance Leader's master, and had extremely high prestige in the Tianqing Alliance.

With him taking action, how would the other sub-leaders dare refuse?

As for how this sub-leader was lured over, it was entirely because Lin Yuan controlled one of his offspring who held the position of guardian elder.

This sub-leader doted on this offspring immensely, leading to Lin Yuan's successful move.

Lin Yuan shook his head, not planning to immediately take action against the Tianqing Alliance Leader.

His greatest advantage now was being hidden in the dark. That Tianqing Alliance Leader had no idea that everything he had painstakingly built had already fallen into Lin Yuan's hands.

Therefore,

The timing of when Lin Yuan made a move against the Tianqing Alliance Leader was extremely important.

If he lured the Tianqing Alliance Leader over now, there was a chance it would arouse the other party's suspicions.

After all, with the foreign evil gods still unresolved, what was the meaning of the Tianqing Alliance's strongest, the only one capable of blocking the Ancestral Demon Tree, returning to the Tianqing World instead of guarding outside?

Moreover,

Even if the Tianqing Alliance Leader was lured over, there was a high possibility he would bring that World Sword.

The World Sword was a world weapon nurtured by the Tianqing World for countless years. Outside the world, it could exchange a few moves with the Ancestral Demon Tree at its peak.

As for inside the world, even the current Lin Yuan was not very willing to directly confront the World Sword.

But Lin Yuan had long schemed on how to deal with the World Sword.

Fundamentally speaking, the Tianqing Alliance Leader was not the owner of the World Sword.

He was just a temporary user. If not for the threat of the foreign evil gods, the Tianqing World's will would never have let the World Sword appear.

The root cause for the World Sword's appearance was the foreign evil gods.

As long as the foreign evil gods were dealt with, the World Sword would not continue to remain in the Tianqing Alliance Leader's hands.

Therefore, Lin Yuan had no intention of immediately moving against the Tianqing Alliance Leader.

"I have two tasks for you."

Lin Yuan looked towards the many powerful figures below and slowly spoke.

"Please speak, Master."

"Rest assured, Master. Let alone two tasks, even if it's twenty or two hundred, we will complete them."

"Indeed, Master, please tell us."

Nearly three hundred powerful figures listened attentively, eyes blazing, bowing their bodies.

As parasitic hosts of the Demon Tree's seeds, they had an innate xxx towards Lin Yuan.
