"What's going on?"

"Why won't the compass stop spinning?"

The Eighteen Thieves of Yunshan looked at each other, somewhat perplexed.

According to habit, the needle on the compass should have stopped quickly.

Even when being pursued by the Prince of Zhenbei's men, it would stop spinning after at most ten breaths.

But now?

It had been spinning for over half an hour already.

If they hadn't witnessed the miraculous nature of the bronze compass before, the Eighteen Thieves would have lost patience long ago.

"Let's wait a bit longer."

The Eighteen Thieves restrained themselves and continued waiting.

Another half hour passed.

The bronze needle suspended in the center of the bronze compass in front of the eighteen men was still rapidly spinning.

"Is the compass malfunctioning?"

The rugged leading man stood up and carefully examined the bronze compass.

"Oh compass, compass."

"Which direction should we brothers head next to be safe?"

The rugged man asked an alternative question instead.

Hummmmmm.

Upon saying this, the bronze needle in the center of the compass began spinning even faster.

"What does that mean?"

This scene left everyone dumbfounded.

What was the compass trying to express?

The rugged man's brow furrowed tightly. He vaguely conceived of one possibility.

But that possibility was too inconceivable?

Even trapped in the killing grounds set by the Prince of Zhenbei, they could escape unscathed. But in this remote area?

"We'll disperse and evacuate immediately."

The rugged man put away the compass and said solemnly to the other seventeen.

"Yes."

"Okay, boss."

Although the seventeen didn't know why the rugged man gave such an order, over the many years they had developed absolute trust in him.

It was precisely because of this trust that they had turned dangers into safety time and again.

Whoosh.

In the next moment, the eighteenth of the thieves took off running in a chosen direction first.

The remaining seventeen were also about to choose directions to evacuate.

Suddenly,

Swish swish.

A figure appeared soundlessly behind the fleeing eighteenth thief.

And then easily twisted his neck, breaking it.
