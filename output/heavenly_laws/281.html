"Over two thousand years old?" Lin Yuan was also carefully observing the black mirror.

After passing through the Gate of Myriad Realms eight times, each time staying for twenty years, eighty years, one hundred and sixty years, three hundred years, five hundred years, six hundred years, thirty years, and nine hundred and twenty years respectively.

Adding up all the time spent in those realms, it totaled two thousand six hundred and ten years. If the time in the main world was included, Lin Yuan's precise age was two thousand six hundred and thirty years old.

Here, the precise age refers to the age of the mind and will, which is the most fundamental way to determine the age of a living being.

As for the age of the physical body? The age of the soul? They cannot fundamentally measure the true years a life has experienced.

After all, the physical body can be replaced, and the soul can be abandoned. Only the true spirit of the mind and will is the core of life.

"It can actually calculate my true age so precisely?" Lin Yuan was a bit surprised.

When he met the supreme expert Xia Qin earlier, the other party also sensed Lin Yuan's true age, but far less accurately than this mirror.

Xia Qin, who had mastered the complete rules of time, could sense the true years Lin Yuan had experienced, which was not surprising.

But this mirror?

"It must be a treasure related to the rules of time." Lin Yuan speculated in his heart.

Time-related treasures contain the power of time, and only those who have fully mastered the rules of time have the ability to refine them.

"How exactly did you cultivate? In just over two thousand years, not only did you cultivate to the eighth stage, but you also comprehended so many universal powers?" The stone statue Shen Ji couldn't understand. "Could it be that you have never encountered any bottlenecks or shackles?"

The stone statue Shen Ji felt it was a bit absurd.

The talent Lin Yuan had displayed so far, even if he were four or five million years old, would be considered incredible, let alone just over two thousand years old. What could one achieve in just over two thousand years?

Enough to comprehend a universal power?

At this moment, the stone statue had many guesses in its mind.

For example, was there something wrong with the black mirror?

Or was Lin Yuan a legendary time being?

The former was almost impossible. The black mirror was refined by the owner of the Magic Jade Tower and contained complete time rules. Even if there was a problem with this trial space, it would not malfunction.

As for the latter, time beings are born in time, living in the river of time, existing in the past, present, and future. The passage of time cannot leave any traces on them, so they escaped the probing of the black mirror?

But even for a great existence like the owner of the Magic Jade Tower, they had only heard of time beings and had never seen them with their own eyes.

The more the stone statue Shen Ji thought about it, the more difficult it was to understand.

Even if one was an abnormally monstrous genius, they should at least conform to basic logic, right? Lin Yuan, however, had completely shattered its understanding.

What the stone statue Shen Ji didn't know was that the eighth stage cultivation and dozens of universal powers were just part of Lin Yuan's achievements in the past two thousand years.

Lin Yuan's greatest gain was opening up the path of martial arts evolution, which was his true focus and effort.

The path of martial arts evolution was Lin Yuan's strongest foundation for becoming a supreme expert in the future. Whether it was spatial rules and other rules, or various universal powers, they all served the path of martial arts evolution.

"Perhaps my comprehension is a bit higher than others?" Lin Yuan thought for a moment and said.

"Alright, I don't care why you can do these things. The talent and potential you have shown can allow you to freely choose three treasures from all the rewards on the first and second floors of the Black Tower."

The stone statue Shen Ji spoke.

Normally, those who pass the first floor of the Magic Jade Tower will randomly obtain a certain treasure from the first floor. Among all the treasure rewards on the first floor, these treasures are basically in the lower-middle tier.

Lin Yuan utilized dozens of universal powers, including source-grade universal powers. His potential was so high that it exceeded the stone statue's imagination, so it decided to let Lin Yuan freely choose three items from the numerous treasures on the first floor.

Freely choosing, and three items at that.

Who knows how much stronger it is compared to being randomly bestowed.

But now, upon learning that Lin Yuan was only a little over two thousand years old, the stone statue changed the scope of selection from the first floor rewards to both the first and second floor rewards.

It should be known that when the owner of the Magic Jade Tower first created this trial space, they established separate reward vaults for each floor. The higher the floor of the Black Tower, the richer and rarer the corresponding reward vault.

The reward vault of the second floor was naturally much higher than the reward vault of the first floor.

Lin Yuan had only passed the first floor, yet the scope of his reward selection included the many reward treasures in the second floor reward vault of the Black Tower. One could imagine the shock in the stone statue's heart.

If it weren't for the fact that Lin Yuan basically had no use for most of the reward treasures in the third floor reward vault of the Black Tower, it might have even considered opening up the third floor vault.

After the stone statue Shen Ji finished speaking, it raised its right hand, and multiple layers of light screens appeared in front of Lin Yuan.

They recorded numerous treasures and rarities.

"So many treasures?"

Lin Yuan's heart raced. The vast majority of the treasures recorded on the reward list were ones he had never even heard of, but through the explanatory notes for these reward treasures, he was certain of their incomparable preciousness.

If placed in the main world, they would be enough to make those ninth-stage and tenth-stage experts break their skulls.

For example, the source essence gold that the Vermilion Roc Star Lord obtained at a huge cost could only rank at the bottom of this reward list.

"Too many, too many."

Lin Yuan looked through it several times and found it a bit difficult to choose. The main reason was that he had never heard of the vast majority of the reward treasures. Although the list had basic explanatory notes for each reward, it was difficult to thoroughly understand the reward treasure based on these notes alone, and to determine whether it was suitable for him.

"Senior Shen Ji." Lin Yuan's gaze left the reward list and looked at the stone statue standing beside him. "I don't know which three reward treasures I should choose."

Lin Yuan found it difficult to choose because he was unfamiliar with these reward treasures.

But the stone statue Shen Ji, as the guardian of this trial space, was in charge of bestowing the rewards for each floor and certainly knew the value of each reward treasure.

Normally, it would be too lazy to bother with these things. But after so many years, it had been too lonely for too long. Moreover, Lin Yuan had displayed talent that was utterly unreasonable and incomprehensible.

The stone statue Shen Ji was still very willing to offer its own suggestions.

"Are you planning to choose the most valuable treasure rewards, or are you planning to choose the treasure rewards most suitable for you?"

The stone statue Shen Ji asked. It needed to understand Lin Yuan's needs before it could make specific suggestions.

"Suitable for me." Lin Yuan said without hesitation.

Most of the treasures on the reward list were ones that Lin Yuan had never even heard of, which meant that they might be very difficult to find even in the main world.

No wonder he recently felt it was difficult when comprehending the integration of rules. It turned out that the spatial rules he had comprehended so far were not enough.

"Interpretation of the Void": It is the interpretation and understanding of space by the owner of the Magic Jade Tower.

The introduction is very short. The only thing that caught Lin Yuan's attention was that this "Interpretation of the Void" came from the owner of the Magic Jade Tower.

"The spatial rules are vast and endless. The so-called complete mastery of spatial rules is only mastering one aspect of it. This 'Interpretation of the Void' written by the owner interprets and understands the spatial rules from another perspective."

What kind of existence is the owner of the Magic Jade Tower? Even a few words he wrote are priceless, let alone a complete book of "Interpretation of the Void"?
