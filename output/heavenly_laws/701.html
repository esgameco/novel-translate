Here is my full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Vast, desolate void.

Lin Yuan drifted along with the thin primordial chaos energy.

Except for entities like the Eye of All Laws that had deep karmic connections with Lin Yuan, other powerful beings wouldn't be able to detect even a trace of Lin Yuan's aura even if they passed by nearby.

Lin Yuan's spirit and form seemed to have merged with the infinite chaos energy and vast void.

"That dimensional rift?"

Lin Yuan casually glanced in a certain direction. He had already sent out an avatar to go there.

Lin Yuan was still quite cautious about dimensional rifts.

The 13th-tier weapon Heavenly Void Cauldron that Emperor Xuan obtained came from a dimensional rift.

In that dimensional rift, even Emperor Xuan with his invincible Great Saint realm strength almost suffered severe injuries. This showed how dangerous dimensional rifts could be.

Although Lin Yuan's current strength far surpassed Emperor Xuan back then, he still wouldn't be careless.

Sending only an avatar showed confidence in himself, but was also mainly for probing first.

Even if there were hidden dangers in that dimensional rift that the Eye of All Laws hadn't detected,

Lin Yuan would at most lose one avatar.

He could reform it with a single thought.

There would be no impact on himself.

"Hope everything goes smoothly," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

Even though the Eye of All Laws guaranteed that as long as Lin Yuan made an attempt, their karmic debt would be cleared even if he didn't retrieve that Law Source Fruit in the end.

But Lin Yuan naturally hoped things would go smoothly.

After all, the Eye of All Laws did save him a lot of trouble back then.

Without the Eye of All Laws, even with Lin Yuan's current strength, if he wanted to spread the branch path of martial arts evolution,

He would need to invest a great deal of effort and time, and might not even do as good a job as the Eye of All Laws in the end.

Even an invincible Great Saint could only oversee a certain era, and would ultimately still need to break through to the 13th tier.

But special existences like the Eye of All Laws were nearly eternal. As long as the primordial chaos void didn't collapse, it could continue to exist.

"Hm?"

"The fourth level of 'Infinite Void' is about to be completed?"

Lin Yuan's thoughts stirred slightly. He sent out a consciousness avatar to descend into his inner universe.

The vast inner universe was boundless and endless. At this moment, it was about to complete a certain transformation.

"Stop."

Lin Yuan immediately halted the final transformation of 'Infinite Void'.

"The 'Infinite Void' inheritance I'm cultivating was created by a time lifeform of the primordial chaos void, with the primordial chaos void as the ultimate goal. Theoretically, when cultivated to the seventh level, the inner universe can transform into an existence equivalent to the scale of the primordial chaos void."

Lin Yuan thought to himself.

Of course, even the time lifeform who created this inheritance hadn't cultivated to the so-called seventh level.

If one could truly cultivate the seventh level, they would be considered formidable even among 13th tier beings.

Even if they hadn't become a Time Lord, they probably wouldn't be far off.

In the second Source World, Master Wu Shan's pursuit was to paint a 'Source World'.

Once he achieved that, he would be able to join the ranks of Time Lords.

The 'Infinite Void' inheritance, on the other hand, was about nurturing a primordial chaos void.

Though the processes were different, their ultimate goals were similar.

"If I hadn't descended to the first and second Source Worlds, I probably would have thought this inheritance was flawless. After all, it came from the hand of a great time lifeform. How could their insights and perspectives be wrong?"

"But..."

Lin Yuan shook his head slightly.

After experiencing two Source Worlds, Lin Yuan realized that even time lifeforms might not always be right.

For example, this 'Infinite Void' inheritance wasn't necessarily wrong, but was the inner universe it nurtured, comparable to the primordial chaos void, really perfect?

Even the primordial chaos void itself wasn't perfect and needed various time lifeforms to open up special places to make up for its deficiencies.

How could an inner universe nurtured based on the primordial chaos void be perfect?

It's just that.

The time lifeform who created this inheritance could only access the primordial chaos void dimension.

Without other more perfect dimensional spaces for reference, they couldn't realize this.

Even though time lifeforms could roam dimensional gaps, it was very difficult for them to contact and explore other dimensional spaces.

Firstly, they couldn't find them.

Secondly, even if they found them, they would face strong rejection from the dimensional space itself.

As well as obstruction from the 13th tier time lifeforms born in that dimensional space.

For example, if time lifeforms from other dimensions tried to approach the primordial chaos void, they would naturally be stopped by the time lifeforms born from the primordial chaos void.

But Lin Yuan was different.

Using the Gate of All Worlds, he could silently integrate into worlds within various dimensional spaces.

He even had two Source Worlds more perfect than the primordial chaos void.

In the first Source World, Lin Yuan observed the operation of the Source World's origin, as well as the miniature Source World nurtured within the Abyss Dragon's Heart.

His understanding and insights into that Source World even surpassed those of the primordial chaos void.

In the second Source World, Lin Yuan comprehended the half-finished Eternal Painting drawn by Master Wu Shan. This Eternal Painting depicted that Source World.

In addition, there was Lord Tianguang's understanding of the rune 'Omnipotence'.

Lin Yuan also gained deep insights from this. While he may not have fully grasped the basic operation and essence of the second Source World, he certainly surpassed any Carefree Realm expert in that world.

"The ultimate transformation direction my inner universe pursues cannot be the primordial chaos void."

Lin Yuan thought silently to himself. With the primordial chaos void as the ultimate pursuit, it was uncertain whether one could even become a Time Lord.

"Using all heavens and worlds, infinite dimensions and planes as the foundation - this should be the direction my inner universe will transform towards."

Flashes of the first and second Source Worlds appeared in Lin Yuan's mind, especially their essential operations. He began to slowly integrate them into his own inner universe.

These things had to be completed before finishing the fourth level of 'Infinite Void'.

Only in this way could he lay the foundation before his inner universe's base was set, in order to continuously integrate the essence of more Source Worlds in the future.

"The reason Source Worlds are called Source Worlds is because they are more perfect than other worlds of the same level, closer to true perfection. My inner universe absorbing the essential operations of Source Worlds will also be closer to perfection, and ultimately may have hope of becoming perfect."

"A perfect world? That's a world that only 14th tier perfect time lifeforms can create. Once my inner universe transforms to true perfection, I probably won't be far from becoming a 14th tier perfect time lifeform."

Of course, this was just Lin Yuan's speculation. As for truly becoming a 14th tier perfect time lifeform? Lin Yuan didn't have much of a clue.

Perfect time lifeforms could freely roam timelines, existing in the past, present and future. They could even create the future they wanted. Such level of lifeforms were simply unimaginable to ordinary beings.

Master Wu Shan's ultimate pursuit was only to paint a Source World and join the ranks of Time Lords.

As for becoming a perfect time lifeform? The only thing Lin Yuan knew was that the prerequisite for a perfect time lifeform was perfection.

If his inner universe truly reached perfection, then perhaps he would be very close to becoming a perfect time lifeform?

"Take it slow."

Lin Yuan continued to integrate the operational essence of the two Source Worlds into his inner universe.

For now, perfect time lifeforms were still too distant for him. He wasn't even a time lifeform yet.
