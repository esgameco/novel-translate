"I can't continue watching any longer."

The Progenitor of the Scorching Sun forcibly maintained his blurred consciousness and quickly stopped observing the future timeline, and the turbulent state of his mind gradually recovered.

However, the backlash caused by this forceful peeking into the future would take him at least a few years to dispel.

"This?"

After the Progenitor of the Scorching Sun had calmed down, he was exceptionally shocked in his heart. Time Beings, while able to glimpse into future timelines, have certain limitations.

For example, there are countless future timelines, and even though he is as powerful as a Thirteenth Realm cultivator, he can only observe a portion of the timelines, unable to grasp all the futures.

Furthermore, once the observed future timeline involves a Time Being of the same realm, it becomes exceptionally difficult, and may even suffer backlash.

And the further the observed future, the more variables there are, and the higher the difficulty of the observation.

"Did I receive a huge backlash?"

The Progenitor of the Scorching Sun's expression was solemn. He tried to peek into the distant future of Lin Yuan, but was directly met with such a backlash, which could only be attributed to a few rare situations.

One is that in the distant future, Lin Yuan has established a causal relationship with an extremely powerful Time Being, and enjoys the protection of that powerful Time Being, so the Progenitor naturally cannot glimpse more details.

The second is that Lin Yuan is in a special environment, where there are some time origin places between dimensions, which can interfere with the future lines that a Thirteenth Realm cultivator can observe.

The third is that in the distant future, Lin Yuan has already become an extremely powerful Time Being himself, able to shield against all observation, even from the past.

Apart from these three situations, there are some other possibilities, but the probability is not high.

The Progenitor of the Scorching Sun's expression was solemn, and he was more inclined towards the third situation.

Because just from the known timeline, he could judge that Lin Yuan's growth speed was unbelievably fast. Even after entering the Thirteenth Realm, he was still growing at a terrifying pace, at least the Great Progenitor and the Great Progenitor of Submission would have no strength to contend with Lin Yuan in the future.

Of course, the future the Progenitor of the Scorching Sun saw was just one possibility, and in the actual future, the Great Progenitor and the Great Progenitor of Submission might not necessarily be in opposition to Lin Yuan.

"I can now glimpse into the futures of the Galaxy because his life realm is still low, at the Sovereign Realm."

The Progenitor of the Scorching Sun thought to himself, and for him, whether it was the Pinnacle Sovereign Realm or the Top Pinnacle Sovereign Realm, the Strongest Sovereign, the Invincible Sovereign, they were all at the Sovereign Realm.

"When he crosses into the Thirteenth Realm, it will be very difficult for me to glimpse into his future again."

The Progenitor of the Scorching Sun thought to himself.

Directly observing the future of a Time Being of the same realm is not only difficult, but also very vague, with much information incomplete.

The confrontation and layout between Thirteenth Realm Time Beings is determined by observing a large number of possible timelines, and then judging the plans or layouts of other Time Beings, and then responding accordingly.

But whether one can accurately judge the plans and layouts of other Time Beings depends on how many future timelines one has observed.

If unable to directly observe the future of a certain Time Being, one can only make indirect inferences and guesses based on observing the futures in one's own countless timelines.

"It seems I need to start observing the timelines again recently, with the existence of the Galaxy, there will probably be a lot of changes in the future."

The Progenitor of the Scorching Sun thought to himself, apart from cultivation, Time Beings spend their time observing future timelines, but they usually observe the future timelines by following a certain timeline, which leads to them spending a lot of time.

Before meeting Lin Yuan, the timelines the Progenitor of the Scorching Sun had observed had lasted for hundreds of thousands to millions of years, starting from hundreds of thousands to millions of years ago.

At that time, Lin Yuan had not yet descended to this Source World, so the many timelines the Progenitor of the Scorching Sun had observed were timelines without Lin Yuan's existence, so he had never noticed the monstrous prodigy that had appeared in the Scorching Sun Ancient Kingdom.

Now that Lin Yuan's existence has been confirmed, the Progenitor of the Scorching Sun naturally decided to abandon the many timelines he had previously observed, and start observing anew from this moment on.

With the introduction of Lin Yuan, who is destined to reach the Thirteenth Realm, the future of this Source World will surely take a different direction, and the timelines he sees will be completely different from the ones he saw before.

"Unbelievable." The Progenitor of the Scorching Sun sighed deeply in his heart, through observing Lin Yuan's future, he had gained new insights, and realized that the infinitely variable future actually has a certain degree of certainty? "It is said that the Time Lords who are about to break through to the Perfect Time Being are beginning to converge their own timelines, making the past, present and future unified, but the Galaxy is not even a Thirteenth Realm cultivator yet, how can he achieve such a definite future?"

The Progenitor of the Scorching Sun pondered for a while, and then continued to observe the future timelines again.

In the Silver River Palace.

Lin Yuan was sitting cross-legged, at this moment most of his mental energy was focused on comprehending the operation of the Source World's origin, and the remaining energy was used to process the guidance of various supreme inheritances, as well as to perfect the path of the Martial Dao Evolution.

The path of the Martial Dao Evolution had long been mature, what Lin Yuan needed to do now was to perfectly integrate the path of the Martial Dao Evolution into this Source World.

Each dimension has its own unique rules, for example, in the Chaotic Void, life will receive the baptism of the Chaotic Void origin at key nodes, which is why the Cosmos can thrive within the body.

If there is no such baptism of the Chaotic Void origin, the Cosmos would be difficult to spread in the Chaotic Void, because most experts cannot afford the consumption of the Cosmos' operation.

But in this Source World, there is no such origin baptism, but there are the six Progenitors providing nourishment, and almost every life form has the bloodline of the Progenitors flowing within them.

The Progenitor bloodline is a Thirteenth Realm bloodline, no matter how diluted it is, once excavated, the potential it can unleash is extremely terrifying.

So the entry point of the Martial Dao Evolution path in this Source World is to fully excavate the Progenitor bloodline.

"Bloodline."

Lin Yuan looked at his palm, and was also observing a drop of Dragon Abyss Blood, comprehending the most fundamental power hidden within the bloodline.

Suddenly, the Dragon Abyss Blood floating in front of Lin Yuan began to boil, and in an instant transformed into a palm-sized black dragon.

Every scale on the black dragon's body seemed to be an endless abyss, and the intangible aura it emanated began to silently influence the surrounding space-time.

"Is this the 'Abyss Dragon', the very first living being?" Lin Yuan carefully observed, through the excavation of the fundamental power of the bloodline, he had reconstructed the true form of the 'Abyss Dragon' from a single drop of blood.

Of course, it was only the form, and the consciousness and soul had long been erased, but even so, Lin Yuan could still sense the terrifying power of the 'Abyss Dragon'.

"Just the form of the Abyss Dragon created from a single drop of blood has such might, the full power of this first living being in its prime was indeed not weaker than my current self." Lin Yuan thought to himself.
