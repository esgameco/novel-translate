Chapter 464 The Conflict Between Immortals and Demons (Seeking Subscriptions)

The spiritual world is boundless and infinite.

This is the realm of the spiritual will of life.

It can be extremely small or extremely vast, depending on the strength of one's spiritual will.

The spiritual world of ordinary mortals may only be as narrow as a thatched hut.

The stronger the spiritual will, the more boundless the corresponding spiritual world.

But at this moment, compared to the massive entity in the distance, there was no comparison at all.

The jade immortal consciousness had interacted with many Immortals at the same level, and their spiritual wills were on the same level, even if there were differences, they would not be too great.

To be able to cultivate to the Jade Immortal realm, the spiritual will would basically not be too weak.

"Am I trying to possess the Dao Ancestor?"

The jade immortal consciousness couldn't help but have this thought. Such a terrifying spiritual will could not possibly belong to a Jade Immortal.

Only the supreme Dao Ancestor of the Thirty-Three Immortal Realms of the Immortal World could possess such a level of spiritual will.

"Hmm?"

Lin Yuan's spiritual will opened his eyes and looked down at the insignificant jade immortal consciousness.

In the spiritual world, one can only rely on the strength of one's own will, similar to the reflection of the essence of life in the long river of time.

Lin Yuan's spiritual will index reached over 3,330,000 points, which would be considered powerful even at the Twelfth-Rank ultimate level.

It was naturally incomparable to a mere Eleventh-Rank Jade Immortal.

In fact, the moment Lin Yuan touched that purple jade slip, he had already sensed the problem. The jade immortal will surged up and spread along the spiritual sense that reached out.

Lin Yuan could have stopped it at that time.

But when he realized that this jade immortal consciousness was targeting his spiritual consciousness, or more precisely, his spiritual will, Lin Yuan had no intention of stopping it.

The spiritual world was Lin Yuan's home field, and with his spiritual will strength, even if a Twelfth-Rank Dao Ancestor's spiritual will descended, it would likely be in vain.

This jade immortal consciousness invading Lin Yuan's spiritual world was like a piece of meat jumping onto the chopping board, and Lin Yuan naturally would not refuse.

After all, the jade immortal consciousness came from a Jade Immortal, and Lin Yuan's current strength had not recovered much. He still needed to use some methods to deal with such a consciousness.

While in the Immortal World, Lin Yuan did not intend to reveal his true spiritual will strength, lest it be detected by the Dao Ancestor.

But once the other party fell into his spiritual world, there would be no such hidden danger.

"No, you are not the Dao Ancestor."

The jade immortal consciousness gradually reacted after a brief moment of confusion.

He had heard the Dao Ancestor lecture before and could distinguish the Dao Ancestor's aura, but the aura emanating from Lin Yuan's spiritual will was completely different.

Moreover, if Lin Yuan were the Dao Ancestor, would he allow him to enter the spiritual world? With the Dao Ancestor's formidable power, a single thought could have reduced him to ashes.

However, before the jade immortal consciousness could think further, everything began to fade into darkness.

"It's good that I'm not very familiar with the Immortal World," Lin Yuan smiled and casually suppressed the jade immortal consciousness, then began to browse through its memories.

Although he had just received the memories of the original body 'Gao Wou' and 'Wu Xiang Yan', these two were only Tribulation Crossing experts. Their understanding of the deeper and higher-level information and intelligence of the Immortal World was limited.

But this jade immortal consciousness was different. As an Eleventh-Rank Jade Immortal, he was already considered a top expert in this Immortal World.

Examining this jade immortal consciousness's memories would be of great help to Lin Yuan.

After dozens of breaths, Lin Yuan had thoroughly reviewed the memories of the jade immortal consciousness.

"The Immortal World? The Demon Abyss?"

Lin Yuan's expression became thoughtful.

The memories of the original body 'Gao Wou' already contained a lot of information about the Demon Abyss.

The demonic beasts of the Demon Abyss were the mortal enemies of all Immortals. Any Immortal who encountered demonic beasts would have to exert their full strength to annihilate them.

And in the memories of the jade immortal consciousness, he had a more concrete understanding of the Demon Abyss.

The Demon Abyss was the other side of the Immortal World, the resting place of the Demon Abyss Ancestor.

The endless demonic beasts were nothing more than the condensation of the demonic qi emanated by the slumbering Demon Abyss Ancestor.

The Immortal World and the Demon Abyss were in constant conflict. Every few million to tens of millions of years, the Demon Abyss Ancestor would awaken a portion of its consciousness.

At that time, the demonic beasts from the depths of the Demon Abyss would frantically invade the Immortal World, and many Golden Immortals and Jade Immortals would exert their full strength to fight against the demonic beasts.

The jade immortal consciousness was named 'Yunzhen Zi', and his true body perished in the Immortal-Demon Great War nine million years ago.

However, for an expert at the Jade Immortal level, even when he was alive, he had prepared many life-saving and resurrection methods.

The revival consciousness in the purple jade slip was one of them. Once touched, the inner consciousness would be activated and take over the life form that touched the jade slip.

Yunzhen Zi had deliberately prepared various resources from low to high, hoping that after successfully occupying a body, he could quickly recover to his peak period.

Unfortunately, he chose the wrong person.

"The most terrifying aspect of demonic beasts is not their strength, but the pollution."

Lin Yuan's expression became slightly grave. Even Jade Immortals, when fighting against demonic beasts at the same level, would still face the risk of being polluted.

This pollution referred to the pollution of memories and spiritual consciousness.

In fact, Yunzhen Zi had not only set up the purple jade slip as a resurrection method. He had also refined many life ponds and life stones, so that his consciousness would revive on them the moment his true body perished.

But the revived consciousness was all entangled by the pollution carried by the demonic beasts. Only this purple jade slip, isolated from the inside and outside, isolated from memories and causality, and could not be actively revived, barely escaped the pollution.

"The Demon Abyss Ancestor?"

Lin Yuan thought to himself. With his level of perception, he had long since sensed the Twelfth-Rank existence deep within the Demon Abyss, he just didn't expect it to be so terrifying.

"As for the pollution of demonic beasts, it shouldn't pose any threat to me."

Lin Yuan thought to himself. The so-called pollution was not something he could not resist, but a matter of whether his spiritual will was strong enough.

Lin Yuan's spiritual consciousness was obviously strong enough. Not only the pollution of demonic beasts, even the pollution of the Demon Abyss Ancestor, Lin Yuan could easily withstand it.
