Qi Yuan's expression was somewhat fanatical, even... ferocious.

Just like a cat seeing a mouse.

This reaction left Red Mei and Zhao Yan dumbfounded.

After all, just from hearing the description of the Xueong Master, they knew how powerful, terrifying, and even desperate the enemy they were facing was.

Extremely powerful, yet unkillable.

How do you fight and kill such an enemy?

The Xueong Master also had a hesitant look, with a confused expression in his eyes.

In the past, many geniuses had asked him about the existence of the Black Heavens and Black Lands, and all who heard his narration were filled with despair and gloom.

But Qi Yuan, with his fanatical expression, was an exception.

He seemed not to be a person of the Divine Wood Universe, but rather one of the Black Heavens and Black Lands.

"This kind of White Moon is truly exceptional," Qi Yuan was even about to swallow his saliva.

This expression puzzled even the usually calm Xueong Master.

However, in the next moment, the Xueong Master's expression changed slightly.

"The Devilish Gods... have arrived."

He was not anxious, only regretful, as if he was also reminding Qi Yuan to flee quickly.

"Devilish Gods?" Qi Yuan squinted his eyes, "What Devilish Gods? This is my beloved White Moon!"

Qi Yuan rose from the recliner and looked up at the sky.

At that moment, the sky and earth suddenly darkened.

A huge shadow replaced the vast expanse of the land.

In the Cloud Blood City, many civilians subconsciously looked up at the sky.

When they saw those massive beings, many people were stunned, abruptly sitting on the ground.

Some kept kowtowing, with snot and tears flowing.

"The Devilish Gods have descended!"

"I told you, those guys shouldn't have rebelled and provoked the wrath of the Devilish Gods!"

"It's over!"

In the sky, ninety-nine Devilish Gods gathered.

The smaller ones were only a few hundred zhang in size, while the larger ones spanned dozens of li.

The descent of the Devilish Gods blocked out the sun, giving a sense of the arrival of a vast celestial army.

However, to describe them as a celestial army would be an overstatement.

No one knew, not even the strongest in the Divine Wood Universe, the Primal One, that in the core of the Black Heavens, the Yang Gods... were only ranked as celestial soldiers.

Qi Yuan looked at these Devilish Gods, his gaze falling on the familiar Dark-Eyed Devilish God: "So you're still alive!"

The Dark-Eyed Devilish God looked at Qi Yuan with some trepidation, but thinking about the other Devilish Gods behind him, he became confident.

"Mere human, how do you know the greatness of Lord Kaihuang? You killed me, but with Lord Kaihuang here, I can be revived at any time!"

The Dark-Eyed Devilish God said arrogantly.

The other Devilish Gods also eyed Qi Yuan with malicious intent.

As for the Xueong Master...

In their sight, the Xueong Master seemed to not exist at all.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan's joy only increased.

This was an experience package.

He couldn't scare them away.

He pondered deeply, trying to find a way to lure all these Devilish Gods down.

But after much thought, he couldn't come up with a good plan.

Even if his acting skills were excellent, he would still give the impression that they could easily kill him.

However, they had not cast any debuff spells, so he could just die a few times and then flee.

"Unexpectedly, you don't run upon seeing us. It seems that even a withered mythical legend has such confidence," the white-bearded Kaihuang spoke, his face as confident as ever.

"Why should I run?" Qi Yuan said indifferently.

In fact, he was overjoyed in his heart.

Especially his left eyebrow, it was jumping frantically!

It was jumping so fast that it seemed to have stopped.

Qi Yuan knew that this was a sign of great fortune and blessing.

"Indeed, with mythical power and the Mandate of Heaven favoring you, you have the qualifications and confidence to not fear us," Kaihuang said calmly.

It was true that in the fallen regions of other worlds, even if the Devilish Gods gathered together, they might not be able to subdue Qi Yuan.

"But unfortunately... you have encountered me!"

Kaihuang's face flashed with a sharp gleam.

Qi Yuan suddenly felt a powerful, massive force pulling him in.

This force was extremely powerful.

His left eyebrow twitched violently again.

Without any hesitation, Qi Yuan shouted, "What is this? Don't pull me in!"

Although he shouted this, Qi Yuan did not resist at all, but rather seemed to be actively diving into that precious object.

The Feathered Devilish God saw this and flattered, "As expected of a Devilish God from the Black Heavens and Black Lands, Lord Kaihuang's treasure can easily draw in even mythical experts."

The other Devilish Gods echoed, "Even the mythical ones are powerless against it."

Kaihuang was filled with pride.

The effect of this treasure was even better than he had expected.

"Alright, let's all enter the treasure, don't keep the human waiting."

After saying this, the nearly hundred Devilish Gods swiftly entered the treasure.

At this moment, Qi Yuan stood quietly in the treasure.

The treasure was ethereal, like a true vacuum, occasionally revealing some meteorite fragments or stellar debris.

When Kaihuang and the other ninety-nine Devilish Gods entered, a look of apprehension appeared on Qi Yuan's face.

"Can't get out of here?"

His voice trembled, as if he had a little fear.

Before Kaihuang could speak, the Feathered Devilish God said, "This treasure was bestowed by Lord Kaihuang's uncle. Even Yang Gods cannot escape from it."

Kaihuang nodded in satisfaction, and added, "Not even at the first level."

Seemingly, he was dissatisfied with the Feathered Devilish God for underestimating the treasure.

"So strong? Doesn't that mean... we all have to stay here?" Qi Yuan looked very apprehensive.

In reality, he was just trying to gather more information.

"Hmph, this treasure has its own sentience... As long as you die, or we all perish, we can then leave," Kaihuang did not mind telling a dead man more.

This behavior was like pushing the enemy's minions into their own crystal, not attacking the tower, and taunting in front of the enemy's fountain while waiting for them to revive, occasionally showing off a famous sword to block the fountain's attack.

"I don't die, and you can't get out?" Qi Yuan's eyes widened, looking very... fearful and worried.

His nose did not grow longer, which meant the other side was not lying.

"Hmph, now you're afraid, aren't you?" the Dark-Eyed Devilish God said.

"Look at him, being a mythical legend, his body trembling, not even better than a commoner!"

"But don't underestimate him, he's still a mythical expert!"

"Yes, although Lord Kaihuang has many Devilish Origins, we can't waste them."

"No matter, wasting a little won't matter, I... have many Devilish Origins, and my uncle has many too," Kaihuang said confidently.

Obtaining this information, Qi Yuan suddenly burst into laughter: "Hahaha haha haha..."

This behavior startled the Devilish Gods.

"Has he gone mad?"

"Is he crazy?"

Qi Yuan's gaze turned to Kaihuang, his eyes burning with fanaticism: "So you're called Kaihuang, eh? After you die, I'll erect a tomb for you in the Underworld!"

Qi Yuan was always deeply devoted to his White Moons.

To ensure their inheritance was not completely devoured by others, he would personally take care of the White Moons' legacy.

As for Kaihuang, he was even more considerate, wanting to erect a tomb and offer sacrifices to him.

Although Kaihuang did not have an elderly aura.

But Qi Yuan's eyebrow was twitching, comparable to refining the Heavenly Emperor's Banner, even stronger by a fraction.

"You're courting death!" Kaihuang raged.

The other dozens of Devilish Gods were also furious.
