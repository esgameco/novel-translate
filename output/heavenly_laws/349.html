Chapter 349: The River of Time (5,000 words, please subscribe)

"The full view of the River of Time?"

Lin Yuan looked at the Supreme Being Xia Qinzhi sitting on the throne.

In the chaotic void, sections of the universe, the vast power of time swept over everything like a great river.

All things and beings were within the River of Time. Except for Supreme Beings who could transcend the River of Time, any life form had to experience the "scouring" of the River of Time.

Here, "scouring" referred to the limit of one's lifespan. Even existences that comprehended the rules of time could only temporarily leap out of the River of Time, but would eventually fall back in and still age and die.

Lin Yuan had always thought the River of Time was just a conceptual metaphor. But now the Supreme Being Xia Qinzhi said he would take him to see the full view of the River of Time?

This left Lin Yuan somewhat astonished.

The River of Time? It has a full view?

"Everyone's understanding of the rules of time is different. My comprehension of the time rules may not apply to you."

"Since that's the case, I'll take you to see the River of Time with your own eyes. What insights you gain will depend on yourself."

The Supreme Being Xia Qinzhi smiled faintly.

In the next moment.

Before Lin Yuan could respond,

He felt a strand of his spiritual will instantly break free from some shackle and arrive at a mysterious space.

"This is not the main universe anymore?"

Ultimately, Lin Yuan had attained a high level of comprehension of the time rules. He was absolutely certain that this mysterious space he had arrived in was distinctly different from the main universe, like a little fish leaving the river. This strand of spiritual will could no longer sense any fluctuations of the time rules.

Lin Yuan looked around with difficulty and saw countless "currents" surging below.

These "currents" were condensed from extremely pure temporal forces, vast and mighty, flowing from the past towards the future, as if nothing could stop them.

"Is this the River of Time?"

Lin Yuan was shaken to the core. Vaguely, he could see different "fish" swimming in the depths of the river.

Some of the "fish" were huge, almost spanning half the width of the river, while others were tiny, barely perceptible.

But all of the "fish" were within Lin Yuan's observation, yet they were completely oblivious to his gaze.

It was like a two-dimensional painting world unable to detect the observation of life outside the painting. The "fish" in the river were the same.

"The River of Time."

"This is the River of Time."

Lin Yuan was immensely excited as he feverishly observed the scenes before him.

[Your innate aptitude is heaven-defying. Observing the River of Time causes your comprehension of the time rules to increase at an astonishing speed.]

Compared to the time rules in the miniature universe, this River of Time was inconceivably more vast, encompassing countless lives.

"Hm?"

Lin Yuan tried to look further into the River of Time and saw that occasionally a few "big fish" would leap out of the surging surface.

Those "big fish" only maintained their position above the surface for a few fleeting moments before falling back down, far from matching Lin Yuan's vantage point for observing this River of Time.

"Those 'big fish' should be existences that have comprehended the time rules. They can temporarily leap out of the water, but only temporarily."

Lin Yuan thought silently to himself.

After observing the past of the River of Time for a while, Lin Yuan turned his gaze towards the direction the river flowed, which was the future.

"Can't see clearly." Lin Yuan did not feel any surprise.

The past is set, the future is uncertain.

Every moment could branch into different futures.

Even Supreme Beings who could swim upstream to the past could not flow downstream into the distant future.

If a Supreme Being dared to do so, they would most likely get lost in the endless timelines, never able to return to the present space-time.

Lin Yuan continued observing the River of Time.

"Hm?"

Lin Yuan saw the towering figure standing beside him.

At this moment, he was standing on the bank of the River of Time, sheltered by this towering figure.

And this towering figure was none other than the Supreme Being Xia Qinzhi.

Without reaching the level of a Supreme Being, one could not transcend the River of Time to reach the "riverbank" - this was an iron law, just like how lives that had perished in the past would be utterly annihilated.

To break this iron law, even Supreme Beings had to pay a price.

For a Supreme Being to resurrect a life that had died in the past, they had to withstand the backlash from the universe.

Now, for Xia Qinzhi to bring him to the "riverbank", he would certainly have to pay a similar price.

Lin Yuan had a feeling that the price Xia Qinzhi needed to pay might be far more severe than the backlash from resurrecting a few eleventh-tier experts.

Otherwise, why had he never heard of Supreme Beings being able to bring others to transcend the River of Time? He knew that Supreme Beings could resurrect the dead, and at least cultivators of some ability had heard of this.

But bringing others to transcend the River of Time? This was the first time Lin Yuan had witnessed it.

This only showed that for even a Supreme Being to do this, they needed to carefully consider it over and over again.

Lin Yuan continued observing everything about the River of Time, his gaze turning towards other positions on the "riverbank".

Soon, Lin Yuan saw another towering figure. This one was burly and powerful, its mere presence exuding boundless strength that could crush all opposition - the pinnacle of power.

"The Supreme Being Gu Li?" Although Lin Yuan had never seen this Supreme Being of human civilization in person, he had a strong feeling that this towering, muscular figure was indeed the Supreme Being Gu Li.

Moreover, this imposing figure seemed to have noticed Lin Yuan's gaze, lowering its head to meet his eyes, revealing a hint of benevolence.

Lin Yuan's gaze continued into the distance.

One by one, the towering figures of the Supreme Beings of human civilization appeared.

The Supreme Beings Yu Die, Xu Shen, and so on.

All the Supreme Beings' figures on the banks of the River of Time were immensely imposing.

Soon, Lin Yuan saw the tenth Supreme Being.

This Supreme Being was not from human civilization, shrouded in a dark, hazy mist. Lin Yuan had barely observed it for an instant before it noticed him.

"Hmph!"

A faint, cold snort rang out. Lin Yuan's expression changed slightly, but at that moment, the Supreme Being Xia Qinzhi who was sheltering Lin Yuan raised his right hand, and the cold snort completely disappeared.

"That Supreme Being harbors great malice towards me."

Lin Yuan did not dare continue observing and could only turn his gaze towards other positions as much as possible.

The towering figure shrouded in dark mist stood on the bank of the River of Time, glaring coldly into the distance at the Supreme Being Xia Qinzhi.

"Willing to pay such a price to forcibly bring an insect that has not even comprehended the time rules out of the River of Time - utterly foolish." This towering figure thought silently.

Such an act required a horrific price to pay.

Almost every moment was comparable to the backlash from resurrecting dozens or hundreds of eleventh-tier experts.

For Xia Qinzhi to do this was to risk being unable to maintain his peak combat strength for a long time in the future.

"Human civilization." The towering figure's thoughts wavered.

The rise of human civilization could be considered the fastest among all peak races.

The main reason was the unity among its people. Even Supreme Beings would unhesitatingly pay certain prices to help gifted descendants of their race.

Was it worth it?

For most Supreme Beings, their race was secondary - only their own self mattered.

Lin Yuan's strand of spiritual will continued gazing into the distance.

Naturally, he also saw one towering figure after another symbolizing Supreme Beings.

Some of these imposing figures paid no heed to Lin Yuan's gaze, some revealed a benevolent intent, but most were displeased, though all were blocked by the Supreme Being Xia Qinzhi.

In the process of constant observation, Lin Yuan's comprehension of the time rules was increasing at a terrifying speed. This was the River of Time, the most direct manifestation of the time rules.

Normally, any life qualified to overlook the River of Time would have already comprehended the time rules. Those unqualified to transcend the River of Time could not comprehend the time rules either.

Yet here he was...
