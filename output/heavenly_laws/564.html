The irritable big-bearded man also hurriedly said, "Realm Lord, you must remain calm."

Qi Yuán looked at the ominous battleship, his gaze profound: "I want to confront them, but they have to agree to it first."

Hearing this, Yuán's expression suddenly changed.

Could it be... there is an unexpected situation?

The other Ancestral Deities' thoughts were also rapidly turning, seemingly realizing something.

As Qi Yuán's voice fell.

Eternal burning flames, crackled and popped, and the colorful specks of light in the Void Sea were all blown away.

The terrifying flame giant roared, "Qi Yuán, die!"

This kind of supremely powerful being's attack was overbearing and unreasonable.

Reaching the Realm Lord realm, one could already influence the environment and rules of heaven and earth.

In fact, if the Flame Realm Lord were to sneeze, it could turn a cultivation realm the size of the Qinglan Realm into a Flame Realm, with the great fire lasting for billions of years without extinguishing.

A strong sense of scorching heat assailed him, and even with Qi Yuán's strength, he could feel the intense heat.

"Eternal Flames, capable of burning through heaven and earth, Qi Yuán... where will you run, just let my flames consume you!"

Absolute Adaptation!

In an instant, he transformed into a being of fire.

He did not use the Enlightened Ones, but only Absolute Adaptation, fighting fire with fire.

The Flame Realm Lord was stunned, his expression furious: "Die!"

The flames grew even more intense, their power ten or even a hundredfold stronger than before! Qi Yuán's rule was something the Flame Realm Lord had seen similar versions of before.

However, most were crude imitations.

Qi Yuán shrugged, his gaze shifting: "You're a bit hard to kill, aren't you?"

His eyes could see many things.

Reaching the Realm Lord realm, they were not so easily killed.

Unless the power gap between them was too great.

This was far too slow for Qi Yuán.

"Hmph, you're hard to kill as well," the Flame Realm Lord said seriously.

What he understood as "hard to kill" was different from Qi Yuán's understanding.

What he understood was that even standing at the edge of the universe, it would be difficult for him to defeat the other.

"But... even if you're hard to kill, I, the Sovereign, will kill you!" The flames in the Flame Realm Lord's eyes burned even more intensely, and a sinister expression appeared in his eyes, "I have the Ominous Source, I do not fear death, what do you have?"

At this time, outside the Void Sea, Yuán, Sheng Nü, and the other Ancestral Deities' expressions suddenly changed.

"This is bad, the Flame Realm Lord has gone mad, he's going to trade his life for yours!"

"How could this be? Is the one revived by the Ominous Source him? How could he be so unwise?"

"Realm Lord, retreat quickly, do not engage in prolonged battle, stay away from the Flame Realm Lord!"

Yuán urgently warned.

The Ancestral Deities present were on edge.

Between Realm Lords, no one could truly harm the other, but if one side was willing to sacrifice themselves, consuming their own source rules in a self-destructive manner, then a single such act could severely injure an opponent of equal strength, and a second time... would surely result in mutual destruction.

In the past, the Black Sky Black Earth had not used such methods, because no one knew whether the one revived by the Ominous Source was still themselves.

But the Flame Realm Lord did not care about that.

"Wuxkong, lend me the Ominous Source to block this person's retreat!" The Flame Realm Lord roared.

The Wuxkong Realm Lord's gaze was indifferent, and the Ominous Source flew into the Flame Realm Lord's hand. His towering figure just happened to block Qi Yuán's path.

"With me here, he won't be able to escape," the Wuxkong Realm Lord said indifferently.

"What are you going to do?" Qi Yuán had a terrifying guess in his heart.

"Hmph, don't you know?" The Flame Realm Lord's expression was cold.

"This is too unwise of you," Qi Yuán pretended to be alarmed.

But inwardly, he was about to burst out laughing.

Not bad, Uncle Qihuang, or rather, the main body of Qihuang, just as foolish as Qihuang.

To actually self-destruct, not knowing... that I have a Blood Lock Hanging?

The more severe the injury, the more terrifying the Absolute Adaptation empowered by the Death Radiance.

"The seven Enlightened Ones can help you block my rule dissolution seven times, but what about after the seventh time?"

Rule dissolution, that is, the Realm Lord's self-destruction.

The Realm Lord's self-destruction was not an explosion of the body, but the dissolution of the rules.

The power released by the dissolution of the rules was no less than a great earthquake on the dimensional scale of the universe.

"To kill you, I specifically borrowed the Wuxkong Realm Lord's Ominous Source, enough to sustain my rule dissolution thirty times... how will you survive?"

The Flame Realm Lord roared, his eyes bloodshot.

Qi Yuán's expression became serious: "No, don't!"

He originally wanted to act more convincingly, saying something like "Is the one revived by the Ominous Source still you?" But he was afraid of being too harsh and scaring the Flame Realm Lord.

So he could only keep saying "No, don't!" Inwardly, he was thinking, "Just self-destruct already! After all, it would take me a day to kill you, but self-destruction only takes an instant!"

"Die!" The Flame Realm Lord let out a thunderous roar.

Endless flames of rule dissolution surged forth at this moment.

The Void Sea seemed to tremble.

Even the majestic battleship appeared to be shaken by this.

Qi Yuán saw this and held the Enlightened Ones.

"Block it for me!"

In reality, all the rule dissolution was being blocked by his own body.

Death.
