Chapter 236: Choosing a Star Domain (End of Volume, Please Subscribe)

Lin Yuan's consciousness returned to reality. Scenes of his interactions with the Supreme Being Xia Qin kept flashing through his mind.

"Supreme Beings are too terrifying," Lin Yuan marveled in his heart. Just based on the virtual world's conscious avatar, Xia Qin was able to see the traces of time passing in his mind and will?

"But the Gate of Myriad Realms..." Lin Yuan was quite certain that the Supreme Being Xia Qin had not noticed the Gate of Myriad Realms at all.

Although he had only experienced seven crossings, Lin Yuan was truly clear about how much value the Gate of Myriad Realms held for human civilization.

If human civilization could master the Gate of Myriad Realms, there would be no need to continue waging wars with the surrounding peak races.

Why did the Human Civilization Alliance keep launching wars?

For resources.

It was all for resources.

In the vast universe, resources were limited.

If human civilization wanted to keep developing, it could only continuously plunder resources.

And this inevitably led to conflicts with other peak races, triggering wars.

But with the Gate of Myriad Realms, human civilization could completely invade other dimensional planes and obtain vast resources.

Lin Yuan was very clear about this point, but he absolutely could not actively reveal the existence of the Gate of Myriad Realms.

The human heart was unpredictable.

In the face of absolute benefits, no one could guarantee what they might do.

Perhaps in the future, when Lin Yuan became a Supreme Being, or surpassed the Supreme Beings, he would use the Gate of Myriad Realms to open up passages to other dimensional worlds, allowing human civilization's evolved beings to expand their territory.

As for now, Lin Yuan absolutely could not reveal the existence of the Gate of Myriad Realms to anyone.

This was Lin Yuan's biggest trump card.

The thirteen Peak Masters of the Chiyu Lineage stepping into the Eighth Stage was a major event that shook the Chiyu Star Domain and the Annan Star Alliance.

Especially since the Great Peak Master had also stepped into the Eighth Stage not long before. In a short period of time, the Chiyu Lineage gained two Eighth Stage experts, so it was no exaggeration to say their foundation had greatly increased.

Therefore, the Chiyu Lineage decided to hold a grand ceremony to celebrate the birth of the two Eighth Stage experts.

The ceremony was held in the virtual world. The Annan Star Alliance and dozens of surrounding star alliances, as well as any somewhat renowned forces or independent evolved beings, all rushed over. It was much grander than the ceremony held when Lin Yuan stepped into the Sixth Stage.

The Chiyu Lineage gaining another Peak Master was a big deal for the Chiyu Star Domain, but outside the Chiyu Star Domain, it wasn't as significant.

However, the birth of Eighth Stage evolved beings, especially one under a hundred years old like Lin Yuan, signified Lin Yuan's terrifying future potential to any force.

If they didn't come to congratulate him now, they might not have the chance to do so in the future.

"Is that the Hongfeng Star Lord? And the Wanyang Star Lord? Even the Jinlong Star Lord is here?"

"With this scale, it's comparable to a Star Alliance level ceremony..."

The guests whispered among themselves. In their respective star domains, they were all big shots presiding over their territories. But here, they were just ordinary.

At the Thirteenth Peak Master's ceremony, only Eighth Stage Star Lords could make people take a second glance. As for Seventh Stage and Sixth Stage evolved beings, there were too many of them to count.

"That should be the Thirteenth Peak Master. So young."

"Indeed. Who knows how he cultivated to reach the Eighth Stage at such a young age."

Naturally, Lin Yuan was the focus of countless guests. As for the Great Peak Master, although his advancement to the Eighth Stage was dazzling, he paled in comparison to the Thirteenth Peak Master.

Lin Yuan sat at a table with his parents and family. The surrounding tables were all for the Star Lords.

"There are so many big shots..." Lin Yizuo looked around in amazement. Fortunately, they were in the virtual world where the auras of high-level evolved beings were restrained. If this was reality, a low-level evolved being like her might tremble in fear just from a glance.

This was the oppression from the essence of life.

"Xiaoyuan, you..." Father Lin Shoucheng sighed. His son was truly outstanding. Originally, he thought Lin Yuan becoming the Thirteenth Peak Master of the Chiyu Lineage was already an ancestral grave-shaking result.

But now, Lin Yuan had stepped into the Eighth Stage, and under a hundred years old at that.

During this period, countless massive forces from other star domains had contacted him, either actively or passively.

Their goal was to curry favor with Lin Yuan.

"Xiaoyuan, I heard Eighth Stage evolved beings all need to be stationed at the border star domains. Isn't it very dangerous there?" Mother Lu Qiong asked in a low voice, her tone revealing concern.

"It is somewhat dangerous, but I can handle it," Lin Yuan said with a smile.

The one stationed at the border star domain was Lin Yuan's true body, while his Yin and Yang Spirits were in the alien battlefield and the Chiyu main star respectively.

They were quite safe.

For other evolved beings' cloning techniques, the clones could only be revived if the true body still existed.

If the true body perished, all the clones would perish together.

But Lin Yuan's Martial Dao Primordial Spirits were not clones in the strict sense.

Even if the true body perished, the Yin and Yang Spirits could use the Taiji Creation technique to recreate a physical body.

Of course, with the physical body divine ability "Rebirth from a Drop of Blood", Lin Yuan didn't need to go through such trouble.

"Disciple, come over for a moment." Just then, the voice of the Chiyu Star Lord sounded by Lin Yuan's ear.

"I'll be away for a bit." Lin Yuan stood up and gestured to his parents.

"These few are all my good friends who wanted to meet you." The Chiyu Star Lord was chatting and laughing with a few other Star Lords.

Seeing Lin Yuan approach, he immediately introduced them.

"So this is your little disciple."

"His talent is indeed terrifying. An Eighth Stage under a hundred years old. Old Chiyu, your luck is too good!"

The Star Lords spoke one after another.

When Lin Yuan stepped into the Sixth Stage under a hundred years old, most of these Star Lords had praised the Chiyu Star Lord's excellent methods in guiding disciples.

But now, with Lin Yuan stepping into the Eighth Stage under a hundred years old, the tone of all the Star Lords changed. They felt the Chiyu Star Lord was just lucky.

Several Star Lords' eyes turned red when they saw Lin Yuan, feeling that if they had taken in such a disciple, they would have done no worse than the Chiyu Star Lord.

In fact, that was indeed the case.

The Chiyu Star Lord knew his own affairs well.

His guidance to Lin Yuan was pitifully little.

Apart from material resource support, the Chiyu Star Lord had instructed Lin Yuan less than five times.

"Hahaha, what's wrong? Luck is also a kind of strength. Not convinced? Go find a disciple like this yourself."

The Chiyu Star Lord was in a good mood. Not only did he not refute them, he took pride in it.

The ceremony lasted for several days.

Lin Yuan quickly began cultivating again.

Lin Yuan's mind raced as a flood of insights surged in his mind. He comprehended the gains from that torso fragment, all of which were now transformed into his foundation, pushing him to continuously open up the Eighth Stage chapters of his Martial Dao.

"Rule fusion must have a pillar rule. I will fuse the Yin-Yang Rules with the Spatial Rule..."

The foundation of Lin Yuan's cultivated Martial Dao was the Yin-Yang and Taiji.

Therefore, at the Eighth Stage, the rule fusion...
