"Has a life form comprehended the rules of time?" The thoughts of the Supreme Lord Xia Qinzhi expanded.

If it was in the past, he naturally would not have thought too much about it. The long river of time is vast and endless, encompassing not only the many life forms of the present, but also the countless past lives as numerous as the smoke over the sea.

It would be perfectly normal for a life form that comprehends the rules of time to occasionally appear.

Just looking at how the other Supreme Lords paid no heed to this at all, one could tell.

But the Supreme Lord Xia Qinzhi had a vague guess in his heart.

Could it be that the one who comprehended the rules of time this time is that little fellow?

The Supreme Lord Xia Qinzhi began to look forward to it inwardly.

The main star of the galaxy.

Lin Yuan sat cross-legged, separating a strand of will to descend into the inner world within his body.

Hum.

Accompanying the complete comprehension of the rules of time, the rules of time were added as a pillar to the inner world, along with the rules of space and many other rules, beginning to uphold the inner world.

Rumble rumble.

A special kind of change occurred in the inner world.

Lin Yuan felt intuitively that the inner world did not become larger, nor did it gain an additional spatial layer.

But with the integration of the rules of time, Lin Yuan vaguely felt that the inner world became "complete".

It was as if the previous inner world was "deficient", and now it was finally "made whole" and became "complete".

"Is this the 'independence' of the inner world?" Lin Yuan's expression was pensive.

With the rules of time and space, the space-time within the inner world no longer relied on the main universe. Strictly speaking, the inner world and the main universe were on the same level.

They were both worlds with space-time.

As for the difference, it was simply that the main universe was countless times larger than the inner world, but no matter how great the difference, they both belonged to worlds.

"Too wonderful, is this an independent inner world?" Lin Yuan murmured to himself. At this moment, his inner world could already block out the perception of the main universe.

It should be known that the main universe could sense everything in the inner world.

Previously, when Lin Yuan ended his journey through the Spirit Realm and comprehended dozens of Universal Forces, it was perceived by the main universe, and the power from the Universal Origin lingered outside Lin Yuan's inner world.

It was as if waiting for Lin Yuan to open his inner world, allowing the dozens of Universal Forces to adapt to the main universe.

But now, Lin Yuan had a feeling that even if there were treasures in the inner world that the main universe craved, they would not be perceived.

Because the inner world was completely isolated from the main universe, the two were "independent" and complete.

"Although there is no change in combat strength, there is more 'freedom'." A smile appeared on Lin Yuan's face.

In fact, it was inaccurate to say there was no change in combat strength. After comprehending the rules of time, Lin Yuan's attainments in the aspect of time were unknown as to how much stronger they had become compared to the past.

Even if his frontal combat strength was inferior to the Tenth Grade Alpha-level, he could still toy with those Tenth Grade Alpha-levels by relying on his comprehension of the rules of time.

For example, now Lin Yuan could use the rules of time to deploy the Time Prison. Even if those Tenth Grade Alpha-levels struggled with all their might, they would need to spend at least half a time period before they could break free.

Half a time period? The vegetables would have gone cold by then.

"Hm?"

The breakthrough from the Ninth Grade to the Tenth Grade was a slow process of metamorphosis. According to Lin Yuan's own estimation, it would take about a hundred years to complete the metamorphosis.

But now, Lin Yuan estimated that he would only need sixty or seventy years.

"Comprehending the rules of time actually has this kind of benefit?" Lin Yuan's mood was good.

Although with his foundation, breaking through to the Tenth Grade would not have any variables, the earlier he crossed into the Tenth Grade, the better.

The Primordial Essence infusion from the Chaos Void? Theoretically, this was a treatment that Eleventh Grade life forms would receive when they impacted the Ultimate.
