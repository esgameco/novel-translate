But for Lin Yuan, he is naturally not included in this category.

With Lin Yuan's extraordinary comprehension ability, if he truly devotes himself to the cultivation, not only can he definitely cultivate to the fifth level, he may even have a good chance of reaching the realm of the Eternal Lord's comprehension.

It is just a matter of how much effort and time he is willing to invest.

On the edge of the Chaotic Void, there is a rift connecting the dimensions outside.

"This dimensional rift..." the All-Seeing Eye carefully examined it.

The Chaotic Void is not perfect, even with the presence of numerous time lives to maintain it, there are still dimensional rifts appearing at the edges.

The dimensional rifts connect to the dimensional interstices, which have already departed from the dimension where the Chaotic Void resides.

That place is full of dangers, but also contains many opportunities.

In order to obtain this resource, the All-Seeing Eye has mobilized the accumulated favors to send more than ten Supremely Saintly Experts.

Swoosh! At this moment, a figure condensed, forming a silver-robed elder.

"Senior All-Seeing."

"How is it?" the All-Seeing Eye looked at Wind Candle Saintly Expert and asked.

"The native creatures on the small continent outside the dimensional rift are too troublesome to deal with. I'm afraid it will be difficult to obtain that Law Source Fruit," Wind Candle Saintly Expert said truthfully.

The Law Source Fruit is a dimensional treasure, floating in the dimensional interstices. The closer it gets to a dimension, the more "Dao Principles" of that dimension it can absorb.

Therefore, the Law Source Fruit itself contains the fundamental Dao Principles of different dimensions.

This rare treasure would greatly benefit the All-Seeing Eye.

Of course, the area around the Law Source Fruit often nurtures some dimensional creatures.

Dimensional creatures are born with great innate strength in the dimensional interstices.

And outside the dimension, the Supremely Saintly Experts of the Chaotic Void can only display limited power, unable to rely on the power of the Chaotic Void.

So even though the All-Seeing Eye has mobilized more than ten Supremely Saintly Experts to pluck that Law Source Fruit, they are still facing strong resistance.

"As long as you can obtain the Law Source Fruit, I will have a great reward for you," the All-Seeing Eye said to Wind Candle Saintly Expert.

"Since Senior All-Seeing has said so, I will definitely give it my all," Wind Candle Saintly Expert nodded.

The rewards the All-Seeing Eye can offer, even for Supremely Saintly Experts, are enough to make them excited.

Just then - a slight fluctuation came from outside the dimensional rift.

The dimensional rift that had been fixed in place actually began to slowly contract.

"Not good."

"It's the dimensional tide."

The gaze of the All-Seeing Eye slightly changed.
