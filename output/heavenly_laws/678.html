"Great Celestial Monarch of the Galaxy, come out and show yourself at once!"

The terrifying sound waves reverberated through the space-time, and at this moment, every living being on the Chaotic Galactic Continent heard this voice.

In fact, it was not just the Chaotic Galactic Continent, but a vast expanse of the surrounding void that was covered by these sound waves. As a supreme Celestial Ascendant, the range of the booming voice was incredibly broad.

The bald man behind him had a calm gaze, with no objections.

Although the Celestial Monarch of the Galaxy was not an absolute Celestial Ascendant, given his combat prowess within the Mysterious Yellow Universe, he was likely close to the level of an absolute Celestial Ascendant.

While the two absolute Celestial Ascendants were not afraid, they would not easily venture deep into the Chaotic Galactic Continent. The Chaotic Galactic Continent was the domain of the Celestial Monarch of the Galaxy, where he could unleash his full strength. For these outsiders, they would be suppressed there.

Given this ebb and flow of power, the bald man naturally would not take the initiative to enter the Chaotic Galactic Continent.

In the eyes of the two absolute Celestial Ascendants, the Celestial Monarch of the Galaxy was ultimately just a Chaotic Celestial Ascendant, while they were absolute Celestial Ascendants who followed the 'Great Celestial Ascendant Rahu'. Rahu, the Great Celestial Ascendant, was about to sweep through the Chaotic Void and establish his reputation as the 'Invincible'. By following Rahu, their status and position were naturally not comparable to ordinary Celestial Ascendants, so they did not need to be too kind-hearted when facing the Celestial Monarch of the Galaxy.

In the Chaotic Void, an Invincible Celestial Ascendant would appear only in rare eras, and once they emerged, they would be absolute in their power and domination.

Whether it was the Mysterious Emperor or the Patriarch of the Demonic Jade Tower, after acquiring the power of an Invincible Celestial Ascendant, they would not pay much attention to the thoughts of other Celestial Ascendants.

"Who is summoning the Celestial Monarch of the Galaxy to come out?"

"Only an absolute Celestial Ascendant can exert this kind of spatial-temporal suppression."

"Do the absolute Celestial Ascendants wish to see the Celestial Monarch of the Galaxy?"

On the Chaotic Galactic Continent, countless powerful beings looked up towards the source of the sound waves, among them many Venerable Ones at the peak of the Chaotic Realm, who clearly sensed the spatial-temporal suppression and deduced that the visitors were absolute Celestial Ascendants.

Even without detecting the spatial-temporal suppression, many Venerable Ones would have guessed that it was the absolute Celestial Ascendants speaking. After all, who else would dare to address the Celestial Monarch of the Galaxy in such a manner? It was not too difficult for the Celestial Monarch of the Galaxy to subdue other Chaotic Celestial Ascendants, as evidenced by the examples of the Dual-Source Celestial Ascendant and the Black Jade Celestial Ascendant.

"Are those two little fellows?"

Even further away, the awareness of several special existences descended, observing the slender figure and the bald man, the two absolute Celestial Ascendants.

Among the many special existences, Lin Yuan was likely one of the top five, or even top three, Invincible Celestial Ascendants in the history of the Chaotic Void, so they naturally paid close attention.

Especially since not long ago, when Lin Yuan had cultivated the path of Chaotic Power and imprinted the Chaotic Void, it was estimated that he had already approached the level of an Invincible Celestial Ascendant, or even become one.

"These two little fellows should have had some contact with Rahu."

"Rahu, huh? Unexpectedly, he should have already mastered that lineage, possessing the power of an Invincible Celestial Ascendant. Unfortunately, the Celestial Monarch of the Galaxy has appeared in this era."

One of the special existences shook their head slightly.

Normally, in the Chaotic Void, with an absolute Celestial Ascendant and the mastery of a certain Thirteenth-Tier lineage, one would possess the power of an Invincible Celestial Ascendant.

However, having the power of an Invincible Celestial Ascendant and actually becoming an Invincible Celestial Ascendant are two different things. Only one Invincible Celestial Ascendant can exist in an era. Even if there were ten Celestial Ascendants with the power of an Invincible Celestial Ascendant coexisting in the same era, the most likely outcome would be that no Invincible Celestial Ascendant would emerge.

After all, the prerequisite for becoming an Invincible Celestial Ascendant is to have all the other absolute Celestial Ascendants wholeheartedly acknowledge and submit to you. If there are ten Celestial Ascendants at the same level, and the gap in their combat prowess is not too large, it would be impossible for them to truly accept one as the Invincible Celestial Ascendant.

In such a situation, even with the power of an Invincible Celestial Ascendant, one would not be able to become the Invincible Celestial Ascendant recognized by all the absolute Celestial Ascendants, as true invincibility could not be achieved.

"It's also possible that Rahu, that fellow, has too solid a foundation. The Celestial Monarch of the Galaxy has terrifying potential, but with Rahu in this era, the Celestial Monarch of the Galaxy may not have an easy time becoming the Invincible Celestial Ascendant."

Another special existence's awareness evaluated, recognizing the Celestial Monarch of the Galaxy's potential, but also acknowledging that Rahu was no simple matter.

Both Celestial Ascendants certainly possessed the power of an Invincible Celestial Ascendant, but whether the Celestial Monarch of the Galaxy could overwhelm Rahu was another question entirely.

"Let's watch and see."

"These two little fellows probably don't know the true power of the Celestial Monarch of the Galaxy, which is quite interesting. Perhaps the Celestial Monarch and Rahu will make contact sooner than expected."

The various special existences were intrigued. The appearance of a Celestial Ascendant with the power of an Invincible Celestial Ascendant was extremely rare in the long history of the universe, and now two had emerged in this era.

For these special existences, the joys of the long ages were not many, so watching the struggle between these two powerful Celestial Ascendants would certainly be one of their interests.

Deep within the Chaotic Galactic Continent, Lin Yuan was in seclusion, cultivating and comprehending the various supreme lineages bestowed upon him by the Ancestral Progenitors of the Source World.

"Hmm?"

Lin Yuan heard the voice from the outside world, and his brow furrowed slightly.
