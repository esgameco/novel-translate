Chapter 447 - Me Against the Galactic Overlord? (Seeking Subscriptions)

A few days later.

Lin Yuan was about to arrive at the war base of the Human Civilization Alliance.

In fact, if it weren't for his unconscious searching along the way, trying to discover the traces of other Rank 11 alien races and obtain a few more supreme weapons, Lin Yuan would have reached the war base much earlier.

"Those Rank 11 alien races have become quite smart as well," Lin Yuan shook his head slightly.

Ever since he had killed nine Rank 11 members from the Prison Tribe and the Bug Tribe, three of whom were carrying supreme weapons, the peak experts of the various alien races in the core battlefield area had become much more low-key. Not only did they stay away from the regions where Lin Yuan had last appeared, but they also tried their best to avoid fighting each other.

Without the spatial fluctuations caused by such battles, Lin Yuan could only detect the traces of alien races within a radius of tens of millions of miles, which was but a drop in the ocean compared to the vast core battlefield area.

"Next, I'll have to find an opportunity to wait until all those Rank 11 alien races gather together, and then make my move," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

Only by doing so could he maximize his harvests. If Lin Yuan were to reveal his true strength prematurely, those Rank 11 alien races would probably not dare to gather together, and would instead scatter across the corners of the core battlefield, engaging in guerrilla warfare.

After all, Lin Yuan's external strength was not something they could easily contend with. At least the few strongest Rank 11 experts at the peak had the confidence to deal with him. This was the illusion that Lin Yuan had created through his previous actions.

Outside the war base of the Human Civilization Alliance, dozens of figures were already waiting.

They were all Rank 11 evolutions from the nine major Evolution Towers.

"A few days ago, the Galactic Overlord struck again, killing nine Rank 11 members from the Bug Tribe and the Prison Tribe," one of them said in a low voice.

"That's terrifying. How long has the Galactic Overlord been cultivating?" another exclaimed.

"Is the Galactic Overlord the greatest genius in the history of the Human Civilization? I bet there's no one in the history of the entire universe who can match the Galactic Overlord's talent," a third one commented.

The dozens of Rank 11 evolution experts, who were basically all Rank 11 Perfected Evolutions, were discussing in hushed tones. Despite their arrogance, they could only admire the Galactic Overlord's formidable battle achievements.

Killing Rank 11 members wielding supreme weapons was a completely different concept from defeating Rank 11 members wielding supreme weapons.

While there were a few Rank 11 Perfected Evolution experts on the scene who could forcibly kill Rank 11 members wielding supreme weapons with the help of treasures bestowed by the Supremes, the Galactic Overlord had accomplished this feat after cultivating for such a short time.

When they were the Galactic Overlord's age, they were still playing at the Rank 5 or Rank 6 level.

But the Galactic Overlord had already been massacring on this vast core battlefield.

Just as the many Rank 11 Perfected Evolution experts were quickly exchanging information, a figure silently materialized before them.

"Galactic Overlord."

"Greetings, Galactic Overlord."

The Rank 11 Perfected Evolution experts immediately looked at Lin Yuan with burning gazes.

"Has the war base been set up?" Lin Yuan glanced down at the war base, which had not been arranged by him, but rather entrusted to the Rank 11 Perfected Evolution experts from the various Evolution Towers.

Of course, as the supreme commander of the Human Civilization Alliance on this battlefield, Lin Yuan had the final control over the super-large battlefield.

"Yes."

"It's almost done," the leading Rank 11 Perfected Evolution expert, named Xuanyuan You from the Xuanyuan Evolution Tower, replied with a smile. If it weren't for Lin Yuan, he should have been the supreme commander of this battlefield.

Lin Yuan looked at the Rank 11 Perfected Evolution experts and said, "I haven't participated in many alien wars. The main problems on the battlefield still rely on you."

"The Galactic Overlord is being too humble," Xuanyuan You couldn't help but say. "With the Galactic Overlord's strength, you have the ability to influence the outcome of the entire war. We are the ones who should rely on the Galactic Overlord more."

"How's the current situation?" Lin Yuan asked.

"Currently, there are rarely any local conflicts on the super-large battlefield. They are all preparing for the full-scale war," Xuanyuan You's face showed a hint of strangeness. "This should be the deterrence of the Galactic Overlord."

Normally, the strongest Rank 11 experts of the peak factions, while having the ability to suppress ordinary Rank 11 members wielding supreme weapons, generally would not take action.

But because of Lin Yuan, those strongest Rank 11 experts had also started to take action one after another, causing most Rank 11 members to not dare to fight openly on the battlefield, and instead hide in the war base, waiting for the full-scale war to begin.

The full-scale war was the decisive battle where the forces of the twelve peak factions gathered the majority of their strength.

In the full-scale war, the strongest Rank 11 experts would basically have their peers of the same level to contend with. Ordinary Rank 11 members would also face ordinary Rank 11 members as opponents, without the scene of the strongest Rank 11 experts bullying the ordinary Rank 11 members.

"I see," Lin Yuan nodded. No wonder he had barely sensed any fighting fluctuations along the way. It turned out that not only was he taking action, but the strongest Rank 11 experts of the other peak factions were also taking action.

"Alright," Lin Yuan spoke. "I won't interfere with the specific deployment of the war. When the full-scale war is about to begin, just call me."
