In the distorted space-time center.

After crossing into the eleventh level, the first thing Lin Yuan did was come to see the paramount experts.

The tenth evolutionary tower meant the birth of the tenth paramount expert in human civilization.

To receive such an evaluation from the ancient paramount expert upon just entering the eleventh level showed how valued Lin Yuan was.

To a certain extent, the ancient paramount expert had already regarded Lin Yuan as a future paramount expert.

"Compared to the paramount experts, I am still far behind," Lin Yuan immediately said.

The more he cultivated, the more Lin Yuan felt the gap between the twelfth and eleventh levels.

The two were simply not on the same level of existence. Even the strongest eleventh level life could only live within the river of time. Although they could temporarily leap out, they would eventually fall back in.

But the twelfth level? They had completely broken free from the shackles of the river of time, standing on the shore and looking down at the countless struggling lives in the river.

Not only was there an immense gap in realm and strength, but their mentalities were also completely different.

"Do not belittle yourself. If even you cannot cross into the twelfth level, then our human civilization, or even the myriad races of the universe, will never give birth to a twelfth level existence."

The ancient paramount expert said, "Walking the path of evolution requires appropriate humility, but you must also have confidence."

Upon hearing this, the other eight paramount experts slightly nodded. From the fluctuations of Lin Yuan's breakthrough to the eleventh level, they vaguely sensed the profound foundation of this Galactic Lord.

Tearing open the spatial rift in the universe to welcome the descent of the primordial chaos was entirely based on one's own circumstances.

No one could fake that.

The spatial rift torn open by Lin Yuan at the tenth level was not at all smaller than those torn by eleventh level perfected experts impacting the ultimate realm.

Under circumstances of a lower realm, yet ultimately tearing open an equal spatial rift, meant that in other aspects, Lin Yuan surpassed eleventh level perfection.

This was what most shocked the nine paramount experts of human civilization.

"The paramount expert is right," Lin Yuan immediately said.

But in his heart, he couldn't help but mutter.

What he meant by being far behind the paramount experts was only in terms of combat strength.

Now, Lin Yuan's combat strength was far beyond eleventh level perfection. Even eleventh level perfected experts wielding paramount weapons could at most barely survive before him.

But compared to true twelfth level experts, Lin Yuan had no confidence at all.

Eleventh level perfected experts impacting the ultimate realm, as long as they succeeded and landed on the shore, experiencing the "shoreline" trigger, would basically comprehend the six-fold space-time realm.

This was also the reason why the six-fold space-time realm was called the threshold of the twelfth level.

In terms of comprehending the fusion of space-time, Lin Yuan had no advantage, and was even at a disadvantage. In physical body and soul, he was also inferior to paramount experts. As for his inner world?

A paramount expert's inner world would metamorphose into an inner universe spanning tens of thousands to a hundred thousand light-years across - something Lin Yuan could not compare to.

The only place where Lin Yuan could surpass ordinary twelfth level experts was in his spiritual will.

With a spiritual will index as high as over 3,330,000 points, it was estimated that most paramount experts in the universe had never reached that level.

Spiritual will was certainly important and could play a huge role when combined with any foundation. But if one only had spiritual will, the effect would be very limited.

What Lin Yuan meant was simply that at the current stage, he could not defeat paramount experts.

But the ancient paramount expert mistakenly thought he lacked confidence in crossing into the twelfth level.

In fact, Lin Yuan was quite confident in impacting the ultimate realm. He had been tempering and elevating all the fusion rules to the twelfth level realm during the eleventh level.

Even if he did not comprehend the primordial rules in the end, Lin Yuan impacting the ultimate realm had a relatively high chance of success.

This chance of success came not only from his own profound foundation, but also from many external factors, such as that "Heart of Space-Time" artifact.

Being able to extend the time spent in the river of time, this kind of reverse...
