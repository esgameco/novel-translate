Crimson Flame Sovereign followed behind Lin Yuan, also overlooking the numerous supreme sovereign experts below.

However, Crimson Flame Sovereign's gaze was slightly dazed. Along the way, only he had witnessed how the Milky Way Sovereign had 'swept aside' numerous peak sovereign experts.

That irresistible crushing force made Crimson Flame Sovereign almost feel like he was dreaming.

Especially for many peak sovereign experts, they directly activated their foundation-shattering treasures, unleashing their terrifying power, but in front of Lin Yuan, they were still easily pierced like thin paper.

This made Crimson Flame Sovereign's heart tremble.

"Milky Way."

Crimson Flame Sovereign looked at the young man standing in front of him, his expression somewhat dazed, finally understanding the meaning of that 'sweeping aside'.

"Milky Way Sovereign?"

In front of the third giant core celestial pillar, including Profound Void Sovereign, all the peak sovereign experts looked up in awe, like tiny mayflies facing the vast sky.

In just three or four breaths, one hundred and three core celestial pillars were seized, and at least one main combat body of the peak sovereign experts on the scene had fallen at the hands of the Milky Way Sovereign.

They knew the despair and powerlessness of facing the Milky Way Sovereign.

Especially for the peak sovereign experts who fell later, they did not have the lack of preparedness of those who fell earlier.

These peak sovereign experts were extremely cautious, directly activating their foundation-shattering treasures, but they were still crushed and shattered.

"Milky Way Sovereign, what do you want to do?"

Profound Void Sovereign took a deep breath, steadying his mind and asking.

Even though it was inconceivable to him at the moment, he could not reveal it, otherwise the morale of the many peak sovereign experts on his side might collapse directly.

"What do I want to do?"

Lin Yuan looked at Profound Void Sovereign and shook his head, saying, "Surrender all the core celestial pillars."

Compared to other peak sovereign experts, Profound Void Sovereign was a bit more difficult to deal with, and Lin Yuan was currently locking onto the location of the other's true body.

Although Profound Void Sovereign was standing there, his true body was actually merged into the layers of space-time, and he was using the power of the foundation-shattering treasure of the Great Profound Ancient Nation to conceal it. Lin Yuan had to put in some effort to lock onto it.

The foundation-shattering treasures made by the ancestors, when in the hands of peak sovereign experts, exhibited vastly different powers compared to when they were in the hands of the strongest sovereign experts like Profound Void Sovereign.

Especially the foundation-shattering treasure that Profound Void Sovereign activated and operated, it was not an ordinary foundation-shattering treasure.

"Surrender all the core celestial pillars?"

Profound Void Sovereign's expression turned grim: "That's impossible."

Surrendering all the core celestial pillars would be equivalent to giving up the fight for this precious land.

This was an unacceptable result for Profound Void Sovereign.

"Impossible? Then go die."

Lin Yuan said calmly.

As soon as the words left his mouth, he stretched out his right hand and grabbed towards Profound Void Sovereign.

Rumbling.

Formless and majestic spatial forces swept over.

Profound Void Sovereign's expression changed drastically, immediately realizing that his true body hidden in the depths of space-time should have been discovered and locked onto by the Milky Way Sovereign.

"This Milky Way Sovereign actually locked onto my true body so easily?"

Profound Void Sovereign was incredulous. He had used the power of the foundation-shattering treasure of the ancient nation to conceal the location of his true body, and even the strongest sovereign experts of the same level could not lock onto it, equivalent to standing undefeated from the start.

The power the Milky Way Sovereign displayed was clearly superior to the strongest sovereign experts. Profound Void Sovereign did not plan to use this trick to resist the Milky Way Sovereign, but at least he could hold out for a while.

But how long had it been? Less than five breaths, and his true body's location had already been found?

"Block!"

Profound Void Sovereign's aura suddenly erupted, and numerous mysterious auras rose up, all coming from the foundation-shattering treasures of the Great Profound Ancient Nation, trying to resist Lin Yuan's grab.

But unfortunately.

The various barriers formed by the mysterious auras were easily shattered by Lin Yuan's grab.

But at least it had bought Profound Void Sovereign some breathing room.

As the strongest sovereign expert, he was ultimately far superior to the peak sovereign experts, and did not immediately collapse in front of Lin Yuan.

"If you all don't act soon, this precious land will fall into the Milky Way Sovereign's hands."

Profound Void Sovereign immediately transmitted a message to sovereign experts like Yin Darkness Sovereign and Spirit Prison Sovereign.

Just before this, the several ancient nations had completed a transaction and were completely bound together.

The Great Profound Ancient Nation obtained this precious land, and the Mysterious Yin Ancient Nation and Great Spirit Ancient Nation could still have a sip of soup.

But if it was the Blazing Sun Ancient Nation that obtained this precious land, the Mysterious Yin Ancient Nation and Great Spirit Ancient Nation would not only be unable to have a sip of soup, but they might not even be able to leave alive.

"Profound Void, don't be hasty, our true body masters have already rushed over."

Sovereign experts like Spirit Prison Sovereign and Yin Darkness Sovereign said in a deep voice.

Although they were quite regretful at the moment, if they had known the Milky Way Sovereign was so strong, they definitely would not have made such an outrageous demand, but since the matter had already happened, they had no choice.

They could only fight a life-or-death battle with Profound Void Sovereign.

Uniting the four major ancient nations of Great Profound, Mysterious Yin, Great Spirit, and Thunder Roar, and their leading peak sovereign experts, wielding foundation-shattering treasures, to contend with the Milky Way Sovereign.

Rumbling.

In the distance.

Sovereign experts like Spirit Prison Sovereign and Yin Darkness Sovereign activated their foundation-shattering treasures, attacking Lin Yuan from afar, unleashing their terrifying power, and vast space was annihilated.

All the leading sovereign experts held nothing back, attacking with full force.

"Hmm?"

The space around Lin Yuan twisted, and all the attacks were like pebbles falling into the sea. He glanced at the distant leading peak sovereign experts.

"They're not useful at all?"
