But that was based on the premise that Fuxu Venerable would be the first to reach the gigantic core celestial pillar and refine it.

Forcibly snatching the gigantic core celestial pillar from Fuxu Venerable's hands would be too costly, and whether he could ultimately seize it was another matter.

So, after weighing the pros and cons, Blazing Sun Venerable had chosen to give up, which was the most rational decision.

But now?

The gigantic core celestial pillar had appeared right next to Lin Yuan.

That meant Lin Yuan was the first powerful cultivator to come into contact with the gigantic core celestial pillar. In such a situation, Blazing Sun Venerable had almost no hesitation and chose to compete for this gigantic core celestial pillar.

There was no other choice.

Lin Yuan nodded.

Fuxu Venerable could reach the site in one breath, while Blazing Sun Venerable could reach it in three breaths.

That meant Lin Yuan needed to hold out against Fuxu Venerable for two breaths.

It couldn't really be called "holding out"; as long as he delayed Fuxu Venerable for two breaths, preventing him from refining the gigantic core celestial pillar, that would be enough.

In front of the gigantic core celestial pillar.

Lin Yuan only used one-tenth of a breath to successfully refine it.

Suddenly, Lin Yuan felt the spatial suppression of this treasure land had decreased significantly.

At the same time, many of his incarnations could now instantly manifest here.

"Hm?"

Lin Yuan's expression changed slightly as he noticed ripples spreading in the distance, and a man in a dark robe emerged.

"Fuxu Venerable?"

Lin Yuan's expression became solemn. This man in the dark robe was Fuxu Venerable, a being on par with the Demonic Jade Pavilion Master.

"How could he be so fast?"

Lin Yuan felt the pressure. It had only been one-tenth of a breath, yet Fuxu Venerable had already arrived?

There were two possible reasons for this:

One, Fuxu Venerable had hidden his true strength, and his actual abilities were far beyond what he had shown over the past millennium.

Two, Fuxu Venerable just happened to be in the vicinity, so his speed of arrival exceeded Lin Yuan's expectations.

"Your luck is quite good."

Fuxu Venerable glanced at Lin Yuan and said with a smile.

Given his speed, by the time he arrived at the gigantic core celestial pillar, not only had Lin Yuan already reached it, but he had also refined it.

There was only one explanation for this.

The gigantic core celestial pillar had appeared right next to Lin Yuan.

"But unfortunately, you've encountered me."

Fuxu Venerable's words had barely left his mouth when the surrounding space began to darken.

A sense of all-pervasive illusory energy descended.

From the moment he arrived at this place to the time he spoke his second sentence, not even a fraction of a breath had passed, yet Fuxu Venerable had already taken action, without the slightest delay.

"This...?"

Lin Yuan's brow furrowed as the illusory power caused his "World" divine ability to start collapsing and dissipating.

"Begone."

Fuxu Venerable slapped his right hand, and Lin Yuan's expression changed drastically as his external universe immediately crumbled, and he was directly knocked away.

Whoosh.

Fuxu Venerable took a step forward, standing next to the gigantic core celestial pillar, and immediately began refining it. In an instant, he successfully removed Lin Yuan's imprint and smoothly refined the gigantic core celestial pillar.

"Fuxu Venerable?"

Lin Yuan's expression was grim. From Fuxu Venerable's appearance to the present, only half a breath had passed.

"Is this the power of one who stands shoulder-to-shoulder with the invincible Great Saints?"

Lin Yuan took a deep breath. Although he had not sustained any injuries, under Fuxu Venerable's attack, he was completely unable to resist, and could only be passively knocked away.

"Fuxu Venerable's cultivation of that 13th-tier inheritance must be related to time."

Through the brief exchange just now, Lin Yuan had also deduced a lot. When facing Fuxu Venerable's attack, his sense of time was completely disrupted, and he had no intention of actively resisting.
