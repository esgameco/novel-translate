Passing the Heart Demon Tribulation could be said to be not very difficult.

However, to be on the safe side and prevent extreme situations from happening, Lin Yuan still decided to face the Heart Demon Tribulation in the Traversing World.

Over the past 2 million years of the Star Sea Calendar, the various evolutionary scholars of human civilization have conducted detailed research on the Heart Demon Tribulation.

Some scholars believe that the "Heart Demon" faced when breaking through the 8th stage comes from the outside world, condensed from the negative emotions of countless living beings.

There are also some scholars who believe that the so-called "Heart Demon" comes from the obsessions of the evolvers themselves.

Which one specifically it is, there is no conclusion to this day.

And the reason Lin Yuan chose to face the Heart Demon in the Traversing World was to target the first possibility.

If the Heart Demon really comes from the outside world, and Lin Yuan senses something amiss and feels he can't pass the tribulation, he can completely rely on the Gate of Myriad Worlds to escape back to the main world.

No matter how strong the Heart Demon is, it's impossible for it to cross dimensional planes and chase him to the main world.

Of course, with Lin Yuan's current mental willpower index exceeding 11,000 points, he doesn't think this possibility will occur, but one should always leave a backup plan for everything.

"Begin."

Lin Yuan's mind sank deep into his sea of consciousness. He looked towards that majestic and magnificent Gate of Myriad Worlds.

"The Realm-Breaking Source Power has now accumulated to 7 strands."

Lin Yuan checked the amount of Realm-Breaking Source Power. With the blessing of the Yin Spirit constantly at the side of that permanent spatial rift in the alien battlefield, it only takes 290 days to accumulate one strand of Realm-Breaking Source Power.

"Spending one strand of Realm-Breaking Source Power for peace of mind is not a loss."

Lin Yuan thought to himself. The main goal of this traversal was to pass the Heart Demon Tribulation.

As for continuing to cultivate? Comprehending laws? Perfecting the martial dao evolution path? Push all that back.

"Traverse."

Lin Yuan directly consumed one strand of Realm-Breaking Source Power, and his will surged into the Gate of Myriad Worlds.

"This old man, added together there's not much meat on him."

"Having some is better than none, at least we can boil some soup."

"Sigh, that's how it is. But that girl I saw today, she looked so pretty and delicate."

"Pretty and delicate, so what? In times like these, no matter how pretty, it's not as good as a pound of grain."

His consciousness had just awakened not long ago and hadn't fully adapted yet, so he could only quietly listen to the conversation outside.

After a dozen breaths or so, Lin Yuan's consciousness finally stabilized.

"Hmm?"

"Great Yan Empire? End of the dynasty, warlords fighting? People can barely survive?"

Lin Yuan quickly accepted the original body's memories and understood his own situation.

The level of this world is not very high. At least in the original body's memories, even those rare powerhouses who could fight a hundred were extremely rare.

The Great Yan Empire has ruled the world for nearly 500 years, and is now completely corrupt. With various "righteous armies" rising up in rebellion, the entire empire is already on the verge of collapse.

The reason it has come to this is because the common people simply can't survive. Continuous disasters, coupled with increasingly heavy taxes imposed by the court year after year.

It has now reached the point of cannibalism.

Just like Lin Yuan now, being treated as food, and even complained about for having little meat.

"My current body..."

Lin Yuan sensed his body and frowned slightly. First was the age - the original body was already in his 60s, with severely degraded bodily functions. Plus being in a prolonged state of starvation.

At this moment, even standing up was a bit difficult.

Lin Yuan pondered for a while. In the memories, the original body was a small landlord, but with the world in chaos and warlords attacking, he was forced to relocate with his family servants.

However, not long after escaping, he encountered a group of refugees. All his possessions were robbed, and he barely escaped, soon ending up here.

Lin Yuan rested for a moment. Relying on his powerful mental willpower, he mobilized his qi and blood circulation, and managed to recover a little.

"This world?"

Lin Yuan looked around. From the moment his consciousness awakened until now, he hadn't felt the slightest bit of world spiritual qi or primal qi.

"The era of decline?"

Lin Yuan judged in his heart.

"Only 30 years of stay time?"

"So the duration of stay in the traversed world depends on the world's level, not one's own realm?"

Lin Yuan looked pensive.

In the previous six traversals, Lin Yuan's stay time got longer each time, making him think that the stay time in the traversed world was related to his own realm.

The higher the realm, the longer the stay.

But now it seems it should depend on the level of the traversed world.

After all, the current world seems to be only a low-level martial world or even a non-martial world, probably even weaker than the world Lin Yuan traversed to the first time, so there's only a 30-year stay.

"The traversal performed by consuming one strand of Realm-Breaking Source Power is completely random. The previous six times, I was lucky, so each traversed world was helpful to me. But this time, I don't have the same luck as before."

Lin Yuan thought silently.

However, the level of this traversed world doesn't really impact Lin Yuan's main goal.

In fact, the weaker the traversed world, the happier Lin Yuan is. Because according to the analysis of those evolutionary scholars of human civilization:

The Heart Demons of the Heart Demon Tribulation come from the outside, condensed from the negative emotions of countless living beings.

Then the Heart Demons condensed from the negative emotions of the countless beings in the main world,

Compared to the Heart Demons condensed from the beings in this low-level martial world, they must be worlds apart.

Completely incomparable.

Lin Yuan undergoing the Heart Demon Tribulation here is equivalent to lowering the difficulty.

"Facing the Heart Demon Tribulation has nothing to do with the physical body and soul. The Heart Demon doesn't target the physical body and soul, but the mental willpower."

"As long as the realm is reached, the Heart Demon can be attracted to descend at any time."

"There's no need for the physical body and soul to simultaneously reach the 7th stage limit."

Lin Yuan silently thought.

Stepping into the 8th stage requires the physical body and soul to reach the ultimate perfection.

In fact, Lin Yuan at this moment could already attract the Heart Demon to descend.

"Although the stay this time is not long, it's still 30 years. There's no need to rush."

Lin Yuan swept his eyes around. Most importantly, his current situation is not very good. He can't have his physical body disturbed by others while he's in the middle of the Heart Demon Tribulation.

"Don't tell me you're trying to escape? Where can you escape to?"
