"Unfortunately, this world's suppression on lifespan is too harsh. Wanting to normally comprehend the essence of external laws and then break through to the Seventh Level within a thousand years is more difficult than ascending to the heavens," Lin Yuan thought to himself.

The Martial Dao's evolutionary path, after Lin Yuan's localization, strengthened the excavation of one's own bloodline.

Of course, even with the world laws suppressing to this extent, Lin Yuan still left behind a way in the Martial Dao cultivation method to comprehend the external laws and then open up an inner world without relying on faith.

Even if this method would never be usable for the future martial cultivators of this world, Lin Yuan still left it behind.

"However, the advantages of the bloodline cultivation path are also very obvious. That is, by borrowing the power of the bloodline, there are almost no bottlenecks in the early stages of cultivation," Lin Yuan silently thought.

In that case, if it could not be popularized and adapted, even if Lin Yuan forcibly created the Ninth Level Chapter of the Martial Dao, there would be no outstanding aspects to it.

But now, through comprehending the numerous Divine Kingdoms of the gods and main gods, Lin Yuan keenly grasped the key to creating the Ninth Level Chapter of the Martial Dao.

Regardless of the Eighth Level, Ninth Level, or even Tenth and Eleventh Levels, the inner world was the foundation and the source of one's own power.

The structure of the inner world, after countless evolutionary scholars of the main world's human civilization continuously perfected it, had already reached an extreme limit.

It was very difficult to improve even a little on things like the racial shackles of a myriad li square.

Under such circumstances, Lin Yuan, relying on his heaven-defying comprehension, realized part of the concepts of the Divine Kingdoms and then integrated them into the Ninth Level Chapter of the Martial Dao.

"If it goes smoothly, the inner worlds of the future Ninth Level Martial Dao evolutionary experts will be more stable and perfect than the inner worlds of Ninth Level experts of the same level, making it easier for them to transform into inner universes in the future."

Lin Yuan's spirit was invigorated.

Simply put, this was equivalent to future Ninth Level Martial Dao evolutionary experts each possessing a World Tree.

The World Tree, as an Eleventh Star top-grade cosmic treasure, had the effect of stabilizing the inner world. After Lin Yuan perfected the Ninth Level Chapter of the Martial Dao, the stability of the inner world would not be much inferior to the World Tree.

The most crucial point was that this kind of stability originated from the inner world itself and was not reliant on external forces, so it could make the final transformation of the inner world a bit easier.

Of course, the World Tree not only had the effect of stabilizing the inner world, but even so, compared to other experts of the same level, the Ninth Level Martial Dao evolutionary experts possessed an innate advantage.

It was similar to those special lifeforms compared to normal experts of the same level.

There was no way around it. With the same attack, your inner world was on the verge of collapse, teetering on the brink.

But the inner world of a Ninth Level Martial Dao evolutionary expert merely shook for a moment before returning to normal.

A large portion of the countless citizens in the main world had too low comprehension and were very sluggish to the fluctuations of external laws, making them very suitable for the bloodline evolutionary path.

Of course, the prerequisite was having a bloodline of sufficient strength within the body.

But in any case, it was still a path, much better than being unable to become an evolutionary expert for one's entire life.
