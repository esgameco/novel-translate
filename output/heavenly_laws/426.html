Chapter 426: As if Falling into an Ice Cellar (Seeking Subscriptions)

[Remaining Residence Time: One Year]

In the hidden space of the Void Secret Realm, Lin Yuan sat cross-legged on a huge rock, opening his eyes and glancing at the illusory text in the lower left of his vision.

"Time to go back."

Lin Yuan thought to himself. This time descending into the Boundless Void for six hundred years was about to end.

Unless Lin Yuan spent another one-third of a strand of Realm-Crossing Primal Force, otherwise when the residence time was up, he would automatically return to the main world.

One-third of a strand of Realm-Crossing Primal Force was basically nothing to Lin Yuan. In exchange for six hundred years of peaceful cultivation time, it was worth it.

In the recent twenty years, Lin Yuan had felt this Boundless Universe's limitations on him.

Simply put, the efficiency of his comprehension of rules was slowly decreasing.

To put it in more understandable terms, the Boundless Void had been emptied out by Lin Yuan.

Worlds of different sizes contain different profundities of innate rules.

Take the rules of time for example. Compared to the time rules of the Central Earth World and Spirit Realm that Lin Yuan had traversed before, the time rules of the main universe were vastly more vast, by who knows how many times.

So the upper limit of the main universe was far beyond that of the Central Earth World and Spirit Realm.

Because the living beings in those latter worlds were innately unable to sense the more complex and profound essence of rules.

Now, to Lin Yuan, the Boundless Void was more or less the same reasoning.

Continuing to cultivate in the Boundless Void, unless he completely gave up comprehending rules, was basically meaningless, just a waste of time.

Even if Lin Yuan could cross into the eleventh or twelfth stages, the profundities of rules he comprehended would probably surpass the Boundless Void.

At that time, if Lin Yuan descended into the Boundless Void again, it would be the Boundless Void trying to leech off Lin Yuan's comprehension of rules.

"Perhaps the reason that the opener of this secret realm failed to reach the ultimate in the long years before was not just due to insufficient foundation or bad luck, but because of the inherent limitations of the Boundless Void itself?"

Lin Yuan speculated inwardly. At least from what he could see currently, there had never appeared a twelfth stage supreme being in the Boundless Void.

The only one who had tried to break through to the twelfth stage, the opener of this secret realm, had ultimately failed as well, with all traces of his existence wiped out.

"But it doesn't really affect me."

Lin Yuan's expression was calm. If the Boundless Void could no longer allow him to comprehend rules, he would just return to the main universe to comprehend them.

And if one day the main universe also could not provide him comprehension of rules, then he would use the Myriad Realms Gate to go to even higher dimensional spaces to comprehend them.

For almost all living beings, their limits were predetermined by the world. But Lin Yuan was different - he had no such shackles of limits.

"A hundred years of bitter cultivation, although the speed has slowed down in the recent twenty years, the overall result is still not bad."

A smile appeared on Lin Yuan's face.

Relying on the entire resources of the Boundless Void, Lin Yuan had cultivated the 'Profound Yellow Body' of the Martial Ancestor's Body to the perfect tenth stage level.

The reason he invested such a huge cost into the Martial Ancestor's Body was because Lin Yuan had long treated the Martial Ancestor's Body as his permanent physical body when descending into this Boundless Void.

Although the Boundless Void could not provide much help for Lin Yuan's comprehension of rules, cultivation was not just about comprehending rules.

If Lin Yuan planned to rest and not comprehend any rules in the future, the Boundless Void was actually not a bad choice. With the Martial Ancestor's Body, once he descended he could directly take it over, saving a lot of time.

After all, cultivating the 'Profound Yellow Body' of the Martial Ancestor's Body to the perfect stage used the resources of the Boundless Void, without Lin Yuan having to spend any of his own.

"The Primal Bell." With a thought from Lin Yuan, a palm-sized golden bell hovered in front of him.

After taking over the Martial Ancestor's Body when descending into this Boundless Void, the first thing Lin Yuan did was summon this weapon that he had been nurturing along the way until now.

Six hundred years of uninterrupted nurturing, plus constantly providing abundant resources, the might of the Primal Bell was no weaker than any eleventh stage secret treasure.

If used by Lin Yuan, the power it could unleash was incalculable.

After all, the Primal Bell was nurtured from scratch by Lin Yuan himself, perfectly compatible with him. Lin Yuan could unleash its full might with just a thought.

For the same strike, other secret treasures would need full power to activate, but the Primal Bell only needed a fraction of Lin Yuan's power.

This was also the reason why Lin Yuan planned to shatter all those miraculous objects he obtained from the two-dimensional world into primal forces to feed back into the Primal Bell.

As long as the Primal Bell could reach the twelfth stage, the help it provided Lin Yuan would be greater than ten twelfth stage weapons, or even dozens of twelfth stage weapons combined.

A weapon suited for oneself was already extremely rare, not to mention one as perfectly compatible as the Primal Bell.

"Myriad rules..."

Lin Yuan's gaze deepened.

This journey's biggest gain for Lin Yuan was his spiritual will.

Before descending, Lin Yuan's spiritual will index was only a few hundred million. But now, his spiritual will index had reached over three billion three hundred and thirty million.

The improvement of spiritual will was not as simple as multiplying by a number - the higher it went, the more difficult it became.

The spiritual will index threshold for a Supreme Being was 1.8 billion. The spiritual will index of eleventh stage perfected legendary beings was generally over 1 billion.

It's hard to believe that a Supreme Being's spiritual will is only about 1.8 times stronger than those eleventh stage perfected legendary beings, right?

Besides spiritual will, Lin Yuan's biggest improvement was in his comprehension of rules.

Especially after comprehending the sixth level of the Space-Time Fusion Rules, in the past hundred years of contemplating other fusion rules, Lin Yuan had made meteoric progress.

"Namely the Reincarnation Rules, Great Destruction Rules, Causality Fluctuation Rules, Space-Time Dissolution Rules, Endless Celestial Rules, Space-Time River Rules, Void Tidal Rules, Cosmic Crevice Rules, Five Phases Cycle Rules, Spatial Annihilation Rules..."

The sixth level of the Space-Time Fusion Rules belonged to the Supreme Being realm.

For the supporting pillar-level rules below this, one needed to reach the seventh level to be considered the ultimate stage.

Among the eighty-three pillar-level fusion rules, although none had reached the Supreme Being realm and were all at the peak of the sixth level, the sheer number was immense.

Even for a Supreme Being, it was probably very difficult to simultaneously comprehend so many pillar-level fusion rules and raise all of them to the sixth level.

After all, Supreme Beings also had limited time, and needed to allocate more mental energy to comprehending their own paths.

In fact, with Lin Yuan's comprehension ability, if he focused on a single pillar-level fusion rule, he could likely raise one or two of them to the seventh level within a hundred years.

But it wouldn't mean much. Without first comprehending the sixth level of Space-Time Rules, raising a pillar-level fusion rule to the seventh level, while giving Lin Yuan a significant power increase, would not be that remarkable.

Compared to that, comprehending more sixth level peak pillar-level fusion rules would be more helpful for Lin Yuan.

It would also prepare him for eventually comprehending the Chaos Rules.

Although he didn't know the specifics of how to comprehend the Chaos Rules, based on the information Lin Yuan currently understood, the more rules he comprehended, the better.

Without a sufficient number of comprehended rules, he would be unable to ultimately integrate everything and return to the origin, becoming Chaos.

Apart from the pillar-level fusion rules, Lin Yuan had also comprehended a great number of ordinary fusion rules, numbering in the hundreds or even thousands.

These ordinary fusion rules were basically not ones Lin Yuan intentionally comprehended, but ones he touched upon as side paths while comprehending other pillar-level fusion rules, and comprehended them along the way.

"Thinking about it now, my tenth stage compared to the tenth stages of the myriad races of the cosmic universe..." Lin Yuan shook his head slightly.

For the tenth stage evolved beings of the human civilization in the main universe, as well as the tenth stage experts of other races, comprehending a single pillar-level fusion rule made them a top-grade tenth stage. Comprehending that pillar-level fusion rule to the second level allowed them to attempt the eleventh stage.

But Lin Yuan?

The second level? Not to mention the sixth level Space-Time Fusion Rules, just the pillar-level fusion rules, he had eighty-three at the sixth level.

In terms of pure comprehension of rules, even those eleventh stage perfected legendary beings couldn't catch up to Lin Yuan no matter how hard they tried.

"My current strength, compared to a hundred years ago when I had just comprehended the sixth level of Space-Time Rules, has improved greatly." Lin Yuan felt quite pleased.

Having reached the sixth level in so many rules...
