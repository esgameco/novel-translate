To be precise, he had not even had time to react before appearing here.

This left the King of Thousand Teeth's mind completely blank. He was a Saint! Even if he was not the Peak Saint or the Top Peak Saint.

But who could so easily ignore his resistance and move him here? Even the mightiest Saints would not possess such power.

While the gap between Saints and the mightiest Saints is vast, they all belong to the Saint realm, and such a scene of overwhelming power at the rule level should not occur.

The Galactic Saint.

The Saint that the Six Great Ancient Kingdoms dare not provoke the most, the Galactic Saint! Having seen the portrait of the Galactic Saint by the order of the Great Spiritual Ancestor, the King of Thousand Teeth naturally could not mistake his identity.

And the ability to silently move him here was likely only possessed by the legendary invincible Saint, the Galactic Saint.

"King of Thousand Teeth?"

His escape had actually attracted the lofty King of Thousand Teeth, and the young Meng Shi was somewhat hopeless.

But the young Meng Shi soon realized something was wrong, as the King of Thousand Teeth also seemed fearful, not knowing how he ended up here. "Is it the senior?"

The young Meng Shi was not stupid, and immediately realized the appearance of the King of Thousand Teeth was related to the senior who had blocked him. Especially when Lin Yuan finally said to the King of Thousand Teeth, "What do you think?", it seemed to imply that the senior already knew the King of Thousand Teeth would come.

As for refuting?

The King of Thousand Teeth was well aware of the unspoken rule among the Six Great Ancient Kingdoms that the Saints were prohibited from interfering. For the Galactic Saint, this rule was ineffective.

The Great Spiritual Ancestor had personally ordered them not to provoke the Galactic Saint for any reason, and the King of Thousand Teeth naturally knew what he should do.

It was just not to offend him.

The King of Thousand Teeth had even prepared to cede the Thousand Teeth Domain. With the Galactic Saint's personal descent, the matter he sought must not be small, perhaps he had set his sights on the Thousand Teeth Domain.

There was a second-grade treasure land within the Thousand Teeth Domain, of great value.

"Did you hear that?"

Lin Yuan smiled at the young Meng Shi.

He was quite satisfied with the other's performance.

Although Lin Yuan did not care much about the character of martial cultivators, as long as they cultivated the Dao, they were Lin Yuan's "anchors" and "guideposts".

But if the character was good, Lin Yuan would also feel at ease.

"Ah?"

The young Meng Shi finally reacted.

He looked at the respectful King of Thousand Teeth, and then at the smiling Lin Yuan.

For a moment, he was at a loss.

In his heart, the lofty King of Thousand Teeth, faced with the "senior" who had blocked him, had an attitude so low that he almost kowtowed to the ground.

"Then I'll take this person away."

Lin Yuan looked at the King of Thousand Teeth and said in a flat tone.

"Yes, yes, yes, Saint, not only this one person, even if you want to take away all the living beings in the Thousand Teeth Domain, it's no problem."

Lin Yuan waved his hand and left with the still stunned young Meng Shi.

The King of Thousand Teeth bowed for a while, tentatively raised his head to look around, and felt a sense of relief in his heart.

"So that was the Galactic Saint? The invincible Saint recognized by the Six Great Ancient Kingdoms?"

The King of Thousand Teeth's thoughts scattered.

With the Galactic Saint's strength, even without the Great Spiritual Ancestor's order, he would not dare to provoke him.

In the newly born treasure land, the Galactic Saint had slaughtered indiscriminately, even crushing the mightiest Saint of the Great Buried Ancient Kingdom, known as the number one Saint since the establishment of the Six Great Ancient Kingdoms.

Such a terrifying power, even the mightiest Saints trembled before him, let alone the ordinary Saint like the King of Thousand Teeth.

"The Galactic Saint wants to take that person away? I don't know why he did that?"

The King of Thousand Teeth was puzzled, but dared not think too much about it, and immediately reported the matter to the Emperor of the Great Spiritual Ancient Kingdom.

Soon, the Emperor's reply came, saying that he had done well, and that he should agree to any request the Galactic Saint made.

"Agree to any request?"

The King of Thousand Teeth once again realized the terrifying nature of the Galactic Saint. The Emperor dared to say that, it must have been with the Ancestor's tacit approval.

And the Ancestor actually valued the Galactic Saint so much, saying that any request would be agreed to? At this moment, dozens of figures came chasing after, the same strong ones who had previously pursued the young Meng Shi.

These strong ones stopped when they saw the King of Thousand Teeth, and bowed in reverence.

"The one you were just chasing, his name is Meng Shi, right?" The King of Thousand Teeth asked one more question.

He did not know Meng Shi, as there were too many such insignificant ants in the Thousand Teeth Domain.

"Yes."

The dozens of pursuers looked at the King of Thousand Teeth cautiously.

"Don't chase him anymore."

The King of Thousand Teeth pondered for a while and ordered, "In the future, if you encounter Meng Shi again, immediately notify me, and do not dare to lay a hand on him."

Although he did not know the purpose of the Galactic Saint in taking Meng Shi away, since it was related to the Galactic Saint, the King of Thousand Teeth, even if it was just an ant, dared not be negligent.

Lin Yuan brought the young Meng Shi into the Galactic King's Mansion.

"Galactic."

The young Meng Shi looked at the majestic mansion, vaguely guessing Lin Yuan's true identity.

In fact, the young Meng Shi, after reacting, also finally understood why the lofty King of Thousand Teeth had behaved in that way when he saw Lin Yuan.

Because the senior who had blocked him was the Galactic Saint.

The Galactic Saint who shook the Six Great Ancient Kingdoms.

The martial dao inheritance that the young Meng Shi had recently tried to cultivate was established by the Galactic Saint himself.
