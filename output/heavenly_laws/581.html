King Chisonge finally agreed, which surprised Lin Yuan a bit. That previous sentence "You're quite good at asking" implied that this request was indeed a bit difficult to handle.

Lin Yuan had already prepared to be rejected and then come up with another request.

Aside from comprehending the Grade-A war machines, Lin Yuan wanted too many precious treasures.

"Thank you, Your Highness," Lin Yuan said immediately, without any modesty.

The opportunity to comprehend a Grade-A war machine was rare and hard to come by.

If it wasn't a time of war, Lin Yuan wouldn't even have the chance to see a war machine, let alone comprehend it.

"Are you trying to comprehend the chaotic secret patterns embedded in the war machine?" King Chisonge asked curiously.

"Yes," Lin Yuan nodded.

There was no need to hide this.

The most precious aspect of war machines lies in the chaotic secret patterns engraved on them. It is relying on these chaotic secret patterns that the boundless power rules of the Sovereign Realm can be perfectly integrated and then greatly amplified. Without the chaotic secret patterns, the value of war machines would be no more than some precious materials.

"The chaotic secret patterns were bestowed by the Ancestors, and then replicated onto the war machines by the few strongest Celestial Venerables of our ancient nation," King Chisonge said with some emotion. "It's a good thing that you can comprehend the chaotic secret patterns."

"I'm just taking a look," Lin Yuan said.

He also learned the true origin of the chaotic secret patterns - the Ancestors of the Brilliant Yang Ancient Nation. To be able to easily condense the essence of the chaotic rules into secret patterns, their attainment in the Chaotic Dao must have surpassed the three-layer limit.

Only the lofty Ancestors who overlook the ancient nation and even the source world can easily condense such secret patterns.

"But you can only comprehend the war machine during this war period. If the war ends, whether in victory or defeat, all the war machines will be returned to the capital," King Chisonge reminded.

Allowing Lin Yuan to comprehend the war machine was somewhat irregular, but King Chisonge turned a blind eye to it.

However, when the war ends, the war machines would have to be recalled, and King Chisonge definitely wouldn't dare to forcibly keep them for Lin Yuan's comprehension.

"That's understandable," Lin Yuan nodded.

After that, King Chisonge and Lin Yuan chatted a bit, mainly about Lin Yuan's subsequent arrangements. Since Lin Yuan had revealed the strength of an old-generation Venerable, he naturally needed to be stationed in a more important defensive line area, as the Ding Seven defensive line was too peripheral.

Lin Yuan had no objections to this, as the strength he had displayed was here, and King Chisonge wouldn't send him to resist a peak Venerable.

After a while, King Chisonge had finished chatting, and the Marquis of Qingfen then led Lin Yuan to leave.

The study was left with only King Chisonge sitting there.

"Father," a young man emerged from the void, bearing a strong resemblance to King Chisonge. He looked at the direction where Lin Yuan and the Marquis of Qingfen had disappeared.

"The war machines are our ancient nation's precious treasures," the young man said, somewhat puzzled. "Even the lowest-grade Type-C war machines have important chaotic secret patterns engraved on them. How could you let Silver River comprehend them so easily, just because he defeated the Venerable Leigo?"

Defeating Venerable Leigo and holding the Ding Seven defensive line was indeed meritorious, but it didn't warrant such a reward.

King Chisonge looked at the young man, who was his most beloved offspring, otherwise he wouldn't have brought him along during the war.

"What do you think of Silver River's strength?" King Chisonge asked thoughtfully.

"Silver River Venerable's strength? Old-generation Venerable? Veteran Venerable?" the young man asked, slightly taken aback.

"Wrong," King Chisonge shook his head. "I see that Silver River's attainment in Chaotic Evolution is not strong. When he fought against Venerable Leigo, he basically didn't use that aspect of power."

"That's true," the young man said, deep in thought.

"Silver River Venerable is only at the initial Venerable realm. That he can unleash such powerful combat strength is relying on the Brilliant Yang Divine Body," King Chisonge said.

"At the initial Venerable realm, relying on the Brilliant Yang Divine Body to unleash combat strength close to a peak Venerable, this Silver River Venerable must have a high concentration of Brilliant Yang bloodline in his body," the young man realized.

"I see," the young man understood.

"Just relying on the Brilliant Yang Divine Body, he has such combat strength. If he breaks through in Chaotic Evolution and reaches the Veteran Venerable level, wouldn't that be equivalent to a peak Venerable?" King Chisonge's face showed a smile. Mobilizing a peak Venerable was not an easy task, as many peak Venerables were unwilling to get involved in the conflict between the two ancient nations and directly refused the summons.

Now, as long as King Chisonge turned a blind eye, he could gain another peak Venerable to serve his side. This was an extremely profitable deal.

"Father is indeed thoughtful," the young man sighed inwardly. If they just let Silver River comprehend the war machines, they could gain an additional peak Venerable combat strength on their side. It was truly worth it.

"As for the risk?" King Chisonge smiled. "The chaotic secret patterns on the war machines are the ancient nation's top secrets, but the chaotic secret patterns cannot be leaked just by wanting to leak them."

"The prerequisite for leaking the chaotic secret patterns is to comprehend them thoroughly. But Silver River is only a newly ascended Venerable, what can he use to comprehend the chaotic secret patterns on the war machines?"

Each war machine has a "self-destruct" secret pattern to prevent it from falling into the enemy's hands and being comprehended and replicated.

But here, the "enemy" refers to at least Venerable-level experts. For a war machine to fall into the enemy's hands and then be sent to a Venerable-level expert for comprehension and replication, that would be the complete process of leaking the chaotic secrets.

As for Lin Yuan? At the initial Venerable realm, his attainment in Chaotic Evolution is not high, so he just wants to comprehend more chaotic secret patterns, but he is still far from thoroughly comprehending the complete chaotic secret patterns of a war machine.

Moreover, Lin Yuan will be comprehending the war machine under King Chisonge's watchful eye, so there is no chance of any leaks.

Most importantly, Lin Yuan has awakened the Brilliant Yang Divine Body, equivalent to being one of their own people in the Brilliant Yang Ancient Nation, so he has no reason to betray the ancient nation.

"Father's insight is remarkable," the young man was deeply convinced. On the surface, it appears that King Chisonge agreed to let Silver River comprehend the Grade-A war machine as a reward for defeating Venerable Leigo. But in reality, it was a multi-layered plan that almost completely eliminated any risks.

"With Father's strategic planning, the Spiritual Might King is bound to be defeated in this war," the young man immediately said.

"It's still too early to talk about victory or defeat," King Chisonge shook his head, a hint of worry in his tone. "I always have a lingering feeling that the Spiritual Might King has many hidden trump cards."

After leaving King Chisonge's small city dwelling, the Marquis of Qingfen looked at Lin Yuan, his tone slightly solemn, "Disciple, the fact that the Chisonge Highness agreed so readily makes me suspect that he has seen you are no ordinary person."
