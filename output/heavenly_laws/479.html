"No more sparring?" Lin Yuan asked.

"No need for more sparring," the Ancestor of the Abyss of Demons replied.

In reality, the Ancestor of the Abyss of Demons still had even stronger trump cards that he had not used.

Even the Ancestor of the Immortal Dao in the origin realm of the Immortal Realm had not made a move yet.

But through these two probing attacks, the Ancestor of the Abyss of Demons had already confirmed that not only could he not lock onto the spatial-temporal location where Lin Yuan was, the attacks he unleashed also could not touch or affect Lin Yuan.

So continuing the sparring would be completely meaningless. It would just be the Ancestor of the Abyss of Demons unilaterally outputting attacks.

"Then may I enter the origin realm of the Immortal Realm?" Lin Yuan continued to ask.

"Of course." The corners of the Ancestor of the Abyss of Demons' mouth twitched slightly. Based on his conclusions after sparring with Lin Yuan, even if he disagreed, it would be useless.

Lin Yuan could simply ignore him and enter the origin realm.

Lin Yuan was able to silently enter the Abyss of Demons and appear before the Ancestor of the Abyss of Demons, and the latter was unable to do anything about it.

So he would similarly be able to silently enter the origin realm of the Immortal Realm, and even if he was in front of the Ancestor of the Immortal Dao, he would be powerless.

"Then thank you," Lin Yuan nodded.

Although he could directly enter the origin realm of the Immortal Realm, he should still greet the master of the place.

"I'll be going first. If there's anything, just contact me directly."

After saying this, Lin Yuan's figure directly dissipated, as the incarnation that had descended here had dispersed its spiritual will, so the incarnation naturally vanished.

"Martial Dao Ancestor."

After Lin Yuan left, the Ancestor of the Abyss of Demons stood for a long time, then sighed and disappeared as well.

The clash and sparring between the Ancestor of the Abyss of Demons and Lin Yuan had caused profound ripples in the Abyss of Demons. Countless demonic beings and demonic sovereigns were uneasy and apprehensive.

Since their birth, they had never felt their divine father wield such power, the spatial-temporal fluctuations of which affected the entire Abyss of Demons.

Xuantian Immortal Realm.

Lin Yuan sat cross-legged.

"Twelfth-tier strength..." Lin Yuan carefully examined the two attacks the Ancestor of the Abyss of Demons had just unleashed.

"I still have a considerable gap in direct combat strength compared to the Twelfth-tier ultimate." Lin Yuan assessed himself and reached this conclusion.

He could only survive in the face of the Twelfth-tier ultimate, it did not mean he could contend with the Twelfth-tier ultimate.

"But the Ancestor of the Abyss of Demons' strength should be relatively weaker compared to the Twelfth-tier experts of the main universe?" Lin Yuan thought to himself.

Even among the Twelfth-tier, there were differences in strength, not to mention the three great realms of Paragon, Sovereign, and Chaos.

Even within the Paragon Realm alone, the gap was huge. Twelfth-tier Paragon Realm experts at the peak could safely weather the great calamity of the universe's destruction, while ordinary Twelfth-tier Paragon Realm experts would die in large numbers facing the great calamity of the universe's destruction.

It was understandable that the Ancestor of the Abyss of Demons was weaker than the powerful Twelfth-tier experts of the main universe.

After all, those Twelfth-tier experts of the main universe occasionally clashed with each other, competed, and even obtained higher inheritances from outside the universe.

But the Ancestor of the Abyss of Demons? United with the Ancestor of the Immortal Dao as two sides of the same coin, before Lin Yuan's descent into the Immortal Realm, he had already been unrivaled for countless years, losing the driving force of having enemies, so his strength had naturally stagnated.

However, even the weakest Twelfth-tier is the ultimate, and Lin Yuan, who had thoroughly mastered the Seventh Realm of Spatial-Temporal, could only survive in their presence.

"The spatial-temporal channel is completely formed now."

Lin Yuan's gaze shifted, looking at the spatial-temporal channel in front of him.

This was a channel that Lin Yuan had forcibly established between the Immortal Realm and the Spirit Realm, based on his own comprehension of the Seventh Realm of Spatial-Temporal.

This kind of method could only be done by Lin Yuan. Even the Ancestor of the Immortal Dao and the Ancestor of the Abyss of Demons could at most bring a small number of lives into the Immortal Realm.

As for opening a stable spatial-temporal channel? They could hardly do it.

Before long, faint figures began to appear within the spatial-temporal channel in front of Lin Yuan.

"This, this is the Immortal Realm?"

Crossing the long spatial-temporal channel, upon entering the Immortal Realm, Qi Qing felt the dense immortal qi and the extremely complete Dao rules, and for a moment was lost in reverie.

She, Qi Qing, actually had the day to enter the Immortal Realm in this life?

Behind Qi Qing, Shan Yang, Nie Yun, and the other Eighth Realm Martial Dao cultivators steadied themselves and, seeing the Lin Yuan standing in the distance with his hands behind his back, immediately knelt down in fervent worship.

The news that the Martial Ancestor had descended into the Spirit Realm again and opened a channel to the Immortal Realm had long since spread throughout the Three Thousand Dao States of the Spirit Realm, causing countless Martial Dao cultivators to go crazy.

Not to mention the prospect of entering the legendary Immortal Realm, just seeing the Martial Ancestor, the progenitor of Martial Dao, was something they would die for.

"Rise."

"First get used to the environment of the Immortal Realm."

Lin Yuan nodded slightly. In the Eighth Crossing, Martial Dao was weak in the Spirit Realm, relying solely on the support of Qi Qing, Shan Yang, Nie Yun, the three Seventh Realm Martial Dao cultivators.
