Even the vast Chaotic Void was not truly perfect, and still had certain defects.

Every moment spawns countless timelines, and the Thirteen Realm Beings can only observe a portion of them. Perhaps the Timeless Being behind that special realm did not witness the timeline where Lin Yuan destroyed it.

Lin Yuan's thoughts expanded. The Red-Robed Girl was right in advising him to fuse the root sources.
