Here is my translation of the excerpt from Chinese to English:

"Alright, according to the rules left by the master, by passing the third and fourth levels, you can obtain one treasure each from the third-level treasury and the fourth-level treasury."

The stone statue figure Shen Ji looked at Lin Yuan and pondered for a moment before saying: "However, I can give you another choice, which is to directly select from the fifth-level treasury. But you can only choose one item."

"Of course, you can also refuse and pick two treasures from the third and fourth level treasuries instead."

The stone statue figure said.

The so-called third, fourth, and fifth level treasuries corresponded to the levels of the nine-story black tower.

The higher the level, the more precious the reward treasures in the treasury would be.

"I choose to select from the fifth-level treasury," Lin Yuan said without hesitation.

At least a Level 11 expert who had comprehended the complete time rules could break through the fifth level of the black tower. The corresponding treasury likely started with Level 11 treasures, and there were probably many Level 11 premium treasures like the World Tree.

One Level 11 premium treasure was invaluable, incomparable to several ordinary Level 11 treasures.

"Good." The stone statue Shen Ji nodded and waved his hand.

Three illusions appeared in front of him.

"This jade stone records a broken path of evolution," the stone statue Shen Ji smiled slightly. "A broken path of the strongest evolution."

"This strongest evolutionary path was already lost in the distant past when the ultimate existences perished."

"The strongest evolutionary path?" Lin Yuan's mind shook. This was a priceless treasure that could not be measured by value. Without the strongest evolutionary path of the ultimate existences? Even if it was broken, it would drive many Level 11 lifeforms crazy.

After all, even if the evolutionary path was broken, since it was originally at the strongest level, the path had already been set based on the remaining unbroken parts. The rest could be slowly repaired.

Lin Yuan had a feeling that if the peak tribes in the cosmic starry sky learned of this broken strongest evolutionary path, it might trigger a full-scale war among the major peak tribes.

"The second treasure is a special secret treasure, slightly stronger than an ordinary Level 11 secret treasure," the stone statue Shen Ji looked at the second illusion, which was a gray long spear. Its surface was somewhat mottled, but the vague killing intent it emanated chilled Lin Yuan's heart.

"Stronger than a Level 11 secret treasure?" Lin Yuan's heart burned. This could directly boost his combat strength.

How were the permanent spatial rifts formed on the small and medium-sized alien battlefields?

Were they created by the strongest experts personally taking action? Basically, the strongest experts would not directly intervene in battlefields below the large-scale alien battlefield. The permanent spatial rifts on the small and medium-sized battlefields were created by the strongest strikes from certain Level 10 and 11 experts wielding strongest weapons.

During certain special periods when human civilization waged all-out war against other alien races, even on small alien battlefields, there were traces of Level 10 experts.

So in theory, Lin Yuan could also use this gray long spear that surpassed Level 11 secret treasures, just not unleash its full might.

"The third treasure," the stone statue Shen Ji looked at the last illusion.

"This is a miniature universe, a completely independent and intact miniature universe that the former master spent hundreds of millions of years creating during his seclusion."

The third illusion in front of the stone statue Shen Ji was a grayish spherical object that gave Lin Yuan a feeling similar to the Chaos Crystal, but even more profound.

"A miniature universe?"

"A completely independent miniature universe?"

Lin Yuan's eyes lit up. What did completely independent mean? Within the main universe, the inner worlds of Level 8, 9, 10, and even 11 experts were not strictly independent worlds.

They needed to rely on the main world and share the time flow rate of the main universe.

To be truly independent, one had to fuse space-time, transforming the inner world into an inner universe.

A completely independent world had its own independent time flow rate, unaffected by other factors.

"Once you refine this miniature universe, you can deeply sense the fluctuations of various rules within," the stone statue Shen Ji explained.

In a sense, this miniature universe had already surpassed the realm of Level 11 treasures. Only the strongest experts had the ability to refine it, and not just any strongest expert.

"You can choose one of the three treasures."

"Choose one?"

Lin Yuan's thoughts raced. First was the broken path of the strongest evolution.

If Lin Yuan didn't have his heavenly comprehension, he would definitely choose this first, because any Level 11 lifeform could not resist the temptation of an evolutionary path without an ultimate existence at the end.

But Lin Yuan had heavenly comprehension and planned to deduce the martial path to the strongest level. In this case, the strongest evolutionary path didn't mean much to him.

Because Lin Yuan would not abandon the martial path to walk a completely unfamiliar evolutionary path.

As for learning from it? The human civilization already had nine strongest evolutionary paths, so Lin Yuan didn't lack references.

The most precious aspect of this broken strongest evolutionary path was not the path itself, but the fact that there was no ultimate existence at its end.

As for the second treasure, the gray long spear stronger than a Level 11 secret treasure, Lin Yuan also rejected it. He could not let his two spirit avatars carry such a precious secret treasure.

But his main spirit was generally in an absolutely safe place and had little use for a treasure of this level.

So it seemed somewhat redundant.

"I choose that miniature universe," Lin Yuan looked at the third illusion.

"If so, take it," the stone statue Shen Ji smiled and waved his hand.

Immediately, the miniature universe emanating a grayish mist flew towards Lin Yuan.

Lin Yuan reached out with his right hand and found that the space inside was only a few hundred light-years in size, but it had complete time and space fluctuations.

"Hurry back and refine it," the stone statue Shen Ji smiled. "You'll find it very beneficial."

"Okay," Lin Yuan nodded.

Before leaving the magic jade space, Lin Yuan seemed to think of something and looked at the stone statue Shen Ji, asking, "Senior Shen Ji, have you heard of the 'Xuanhuang' Sovereign?"

The Xuanhuang Secret Realm in the main universe was unknown to the strongest experts of the major peak tribes, who could only confirm it was not a product of the current cosmic era.

Although the stone statue Shen Ji was not a strongest expert, he came from the Magic Jade Tower and followed its mysterious master.

With his knowledge, he might have heard something about the origin of the "Xuanhuang" Secret Realm.

"The 'Xuanhuang' Sovereign?" The stone statue was slightly stunned. After pondering for a moment, he spoke in an odd tone, "You must mean the 'Xuanhuang' Sovereign, right?"
