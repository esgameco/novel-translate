I wolfed it down, eating and eating.

Liang Qu held the chopsticks, frantically scooping up the crab roe, waiting for it to cook while not forgetting to dig out the meat from the crab legs and toss it to the two beasts.

The two beasts had no hands, and the crab monster's shell was extremely hard. If they couldn't move, it would be fine, but they could only barely bite the outer shell. The fat catfish was powerless, finding it very difficult to eat, relying entirely on their master Liang Qu.

Eating and digging at the same time, not neglecting either.

After a quarter of an hour.

"Burp~~~~"

Liang Qu let out a resounding belch, he had already reached his limit, wanting to eat more but unable to. And just eating the crab roe was really too greasy, making him feel nauseous. He had been forcing himself to eat it halfway through.

The two beasts' stomachs were bigger than his, able to eat a lot, crunching and munching away.

Being able to eat is a blessing, Liang Qu hoped the two beasts would eat more, accumulate more essence, so they could evolve into a Horned Alligator and a Bullhead Catfish sooner.

The names after evolution sounded very impressive, must be extremely powerful. Then he could easily slaughter a crab monster, unlike the difficulty he faced today.

For the moment, Liang Qu became a free laborer, frantically scooping out the meat to be dried on the boat's bow, killing the bacteria through high heat to extend the preservation time. Whatever couldn't be cooked, he just tossed to the two beasts to eat.

The crab monster was large enough that he didn't need to break the shell, just reaching in to scoop out the snow-white crab meat.

After scooping out the crab meat, Liang Qu didn't even spare the crab gills, washing them clean before cooking them too, as the part that filtered the crab's impurities, easily causing diarrhea in humans, but the two beasts didn't mind, still crunching away.

Before gaining intelligence, they used to just swallow the crabs raw, not even washing them, let alone now after washing them once.

After eating, Liang Qu got into a boxing stance, trying to increase his digestion speed. Half an hour later, he continued eating.

The essence absorbed was continuously consumed, used to enhance the fusion of the Water Monkey's spiritual power.

Almost every minute, the fusion degree on the panel would jump up a few points.

Countless bright spots scattered out from the Spiritual Cauldron, gradually merging into the Monkey Spirit's illusory form.

Not only in his mind, but also in the unseen angle outside, a faint white-furred Monkey Spirit illusion appeared behind him.

On the sides of the boat, the immobile and fat catfish frantically swam, trying to consume their internal energy. But when this illusory form appeared, an inexplicable awe welled up in their hearts, making them stop moving.

The two beasts looked around, finding nothing, but when they saw Liang Qu crouching at the bow, they immediately understood the source.

The sky god had become even more terrifying.

At the same time, even deeper changes were happening to Liang Qu's body.

The enhancement of the spiritual power fusion not only strengthened his innate abilities, but also improved his physical qualities.

It was thanks to this that he didn't directly exhaust himself the first time he submerged and commanded the Boar Dragon.

Until around 4 o'clock in the afternoon, cooking a meal every hour, Liang Qu sat shirtless at the bow, his whole body flushed red like blood.

His body temperature was rising sharply, yet strangely without any discomfort.

The sweat flowed out and evaporated, leaving only salt grains and oil.

Liang Qu had to desperately punch like a monkey to consume it, the breath in his chest almost appearing the moment he started moving, rapidly circulating, growing stronger with every second.

Accompanying his breathing, white mist like sharp arrows gushed out from his nose and mouth.

Contracting and expanding,

Surging ceaselessly!

At some point, Liang Qu could even feel scorching liquid flowing in his mouth and nose, almost about to spurt out, but under his uninterrupted punching, the heat flow remained at the verge of eruption.

It was so uncomfortable!

Liang Qu suppressed it firmly, daring not to spit blood.

The endless flow of blood and qi gushed out, the initial half-chopstick-thick chest qi had now flourished to the thickness of a little finger, and was still growing.

Little finger thick! According to Hu Qi's words, it had reached the level of being able to refine the skin, but Liang Qu had no idea what would happen next.
