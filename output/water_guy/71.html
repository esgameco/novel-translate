"The master is not here?"

Liang Qu was kneeling on the ground, his collar being grasped tightly by Lady Xu, which was quite uncomfortable, but he didn't dare to struggle.

Lady Xu was quite old, and it seemed she had not practiced any martial arts, so it would be unwise to hurt her.

"Mm, he's not here. He went to Chaojiang County and won't be back for ten days or half a month. Not only your master, but your second senior brother and fifth senior sister and sixth senior brother all went with him."

Liang Qu lowered his head, his brows furrowed.

At this time, they were not around, and would be gone for such a long time.

It felt like "there must be a conspiracy, right?"

"Yes, mm?" Liang Qu nodded unconsciously, then quickly realized something was off and looked up at Lady Xu. "How does the Shimu know?"

"Luring the tiger away from the mountain." Lady Xu said calmly, "A few days ago, the Chaojiang County Magistrate sent his eldest son over, saying they had discovered a heretic of the Shuishu Sect, and the constables had been investigating for half a month, finally confirming the location. To prevent any accidents, they requested your master to go and stand guard. With one going ahead and the others following, how could there be so many coincidences?"

"What is the Shuishu Sect?"

"An old dog hiding in the vast marshes, mostly remnants from the previous dynasty, pitiful creatures plotting to restore the country."

"Did the master agree?"

"Mm, the County Magistrate offered many benefits, and at the time we didn't realize anything was amiss."

"If it is indeed luring the tiger away from the mountain, then the County Magistrate must be involved as well?"

"It's hard to say. If he is involved, informing us would be like startling the snake in the grass. If not, perhaps it is the Shuishu Sect's plot, wanting to make us doubt each other - both are possible."

Nan Di came over from the side, carrying a tray with a small medicine bottle.

Lady Xu took it, pried open the red cap, and poured out three pills the size of fish eyes.

"Open your mouth."

"Shimu really doesn't need to."

"Open your mouth when I tell you to."

Liang Qu had no choice but to open his mouth, and one pill was placed in.

"Good boy."

Then Lady Xu directly pulled open the bandages on his shoulders and chest, crushing a pill in each hand and sprinkling the powder onto the wounds.

After taking the pill and applying the medicine, Liang Qu immediately felt a warm sensation in the wounds, and they started to heal at an astonishing speed, much faster than lotus roots. Extravagant, too extravagant - these were just minor injuries.

Liang Qu felt pained, as he was poor and felt like he was wasting precious things.

"This bottle has a total of ten pills. Three have been used, the rest are all for you. Don't be stingy with the medicine when you're injured. No one knows when an accident might happen, you shouldn't let yourself be in a weakened state."

Liang Qu lowered his head: "Disciple has received the instruction."

Lady Xu leaned back in the armchair: "If it is indeed the Shuishu Sect, then I have some clues about the mountain ghosts. Do you know that mountain ghosts can gestate?"

"I know."

"Mm, it seems you have been diligently cultivating these days. The gestation of mountain ghosts is extremely fast, they can quickly drain the vital energy of their host. The stronger the host, the more blood energy they can absorb, and the stronger the mountain ghosts that hatch will be. If you can dig out the gestating fetus before it hatches, that would be an excellent medicinal ingredient.

If there is a skilled alchemist who knows how to refine it, they can fuse several gestating pills together and refine them into a Taizhuan Dan (Gestating Pearl Elixir).

The Shuishu Sect has done this kind of thing before, with remarkable effects. A hundred-person portion of Taizhuan Dan could instantly refine a normal person into a martial arts master on the level of a galloping horse, and the upper limit is unknown. At that time, they wiped out thousands of people from several villages.

The Taizu (Emperor) was furious and sent the veteran general Xu Youguang to lead the troops to exterminate them. I was just over ten years old then, but I remember it clearly. After that incident, the Shuishu Sect didn't appear for decades.

I suspect that the mountain ghosts in Yixing City may not be a coincidence, but rather hidden agents left behind by the Shuishu Sect. They were accidentally awakened by the commotion of your ritual to the River God."

Liang Qu was deeply worried. His little village still had to deal with such a powerful boss.
