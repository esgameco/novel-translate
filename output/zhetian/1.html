And just after this Ancestor of the Phoenix had completed the establishment of this [Underworld] and made the announcement, a straight, wide, illusory, and colorless dark river appeared within the spiritual realm.

Just by looking at it, one could associate it with "death", "eternal sleep", "the end", "the abyss", and other symbolic things, as if this river was the embodiment of these concepts.
