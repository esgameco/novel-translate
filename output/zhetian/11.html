This was a sight that shook the world.

A cataclysmic divine punishment, a great annihilation, boundless and immense. Seeing how a single bolt of lightning could destroy a heavenly prodigy, it seemed no one could withstand it. But Spiritual Treasure strolled through it as if in a leisurely garden, comprehending the great Daos and principles within - truly worthy of being the future Spiritual Treasure Venerable!
