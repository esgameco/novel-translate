"Ah, good junior brother." Lingbao truly felt what it was like to have a junior brother at this moment.

After all, Lingbao's master only took him as a disciple. Now that Lingbao was accepting disciples on behalf of his master, he had a new and special feeling with the addition of a junior brother.

"This is just outwardly fulfilling the responsibilities of an elder brother, while inwardly doing the master's work." Lingbao couldn't help but sigh.

He also thought about the chaos caused by Xiong Mo's spiritual awareness wandering around the sect, which had alarmed the elders who were monitoring the sect.

"Maybe I shouldn't take on any more disciples in the future, and just leave behind some legacy teachings in the sect or elsewhere. Taking on disciples is really tiring, after all." Lingbao kept muttering to himself.

Xiong Mo, seeing his elder brother Lingbao staring at him without saying anything, also felt a bit anxious.

"Elder brother, I'm sorry." Xiong Mo quickly drew on his past experience and immediately began apologizing.

"It's alright, no need to be so nervous. After all, you are my junior brother, the sect's second Saint. The issue of your spiritual awareness wandering around the sect has been resolved." Lingbao's expression softened as he responded to Xiong Mo.

"Thank you, Elder Brother Lingbao, for handling the trouble I caused." Xiong Mo knew the matter had been resolved, so he quickly expressed his gratitude to Lingbao.

"I've been in the stone cocoon for over ten thousand years, and suddenly being able to go out and wander around, I just couldn't resist it." Xiong Mo explained the reason to Lingbao.

"Your personality really doesn't seem like that of a Saint." Lingbao sighed.

"You don't need to apologize to me, you should apologize to those elders who are monitoring the sect's situation. Your sudden spiritual awareness appearance really startled them, they almost thought there was a dead body secretly infiltrating the sect."

Lingbao then pointed at Hu Yuan and said, "And him, he was also greatly angered by the trouble you caused."

"Elder Brother Lingbao, please convey my apologies to those elders." Xiong Mo felt embarrassed as he apologized to the elders. "I'm sorry, Elder Hu Yuan."

Hu Yuan calmly accepted Xiong Mo's apology, saying, "Since you are the junior brother of the Saint and the Saint Lingbao, the matter of you wandering around the sect has been downplayed."

Hmm, although he heard Hu Yuan say this, Xiong Mo could tell from the look in his eyes that he wouldn't be able to leave someone's sight for the next period of time.

Lingbao spoke in a serious tone, "I will convey your apologies to those elders. Now that your matter has been mostly resolved, it's time for me to deal with my own business."

Hearing this, Hu Yuan was a bit stunned. He didn't know what matter the two Saints needed to discuss together. Recently the frontlines had been relatively calm, with the highest-level undead only reaching the half-Saint realm, which Lingbao's Saint-grade artifacts and formations had been able to handle.

Xiong Mo also became serious: "Elder Brother Lingbao is leaving?"

Lingbao responded, "Yes, I need to leave this star."

Hu Yuan immediately became anxious: "Why does Saint Lingbao need to leave? The only usable Saint-level combat power right now is him, and the mid-to-high-level forces in the sect haven't fully recovered yet."

"Elder brother can't leave, the danger of the undead calamity is only getting greater." Xiong Mo sighed.

He also didn't want Lingbao to leave, after all, Lingbao was his elder brother.
