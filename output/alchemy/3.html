Chapter 3

Here is a full translation of the excerpt from Chinese to English:

Three male cultivators wearing tight Daoist robes surrounded him, bending down to look at the things on the bluestone slab.

"Hey, a Qi Concealing Talisman, good stuff! How much for one?"

"Five spirit stones per talisman. An essential item for home, travel, mountain hunting. At a critical moment, activate this talisman, and even a prickly wolf won't be able to smell you!"

"So expensive, why don't you just rob people?"

"Just say if you want it or not!"

"Give us a discount, we need to go into the mountains and buy many kinds of talismans. We can buy a bunch from you."

"Don't you guys want to ask around? I'm Old Daoist Chen at the Great River Market, known for my talisman crafting skills! Besides, can the talisman give you a discount when the demon beast attacks? Will it bite you one less time?"

"Three spirit stones for this Qi Concealing Talisman, plus three Earth Thorn Talismans and six Fireball Talismans."

"Four stones! Can't go any lower, this Qi Concealing Talisman is my unique product, I only make one per month!"

The three male cultivators discussed and decided to buy this Qi Concealing Talisman.

After settling the bill, they turned their attention to the jade bottle in the corner.

"Fasting Pills?"

Luo Chen perked up, "One pill can fill you up. Ten pills will keep you from getting hungry for three days and three nights. How many bottles do you want?"

"How much?"

"Let me tell you, these Fasting Pills use premium first-grade yellow cattle meat as the main ingredient, supplemented with ginseng and angelica, and finally refined with the Hundred Flower Honey, a specialty of the Medicine King Sect. They are high quality at a reasonable price!"

"Really that good?"

"Here, smell this fragrance, isn't it alluring? Look at the color, absolutely no adulteration with flour. I dare say no one in this area has better quality than mine!"

"Big brother, it does seem more fragrant than the Fasting Pills we bought before."

"Yeah, this Daoist friend is clearly an expert. The Fasting Pills you bought before must have been fake beef!"

"How much?"

"Four bottles for just one spirit stone!"

"Why don't you just rob people?"

"Just say if you want them or not! After this stall, there's no such good stuff. Let me tell you, I have many regular customers. Wait a bit and I'll sell out and go home."

"Give us a discount?"

"Since you three Daoists seem sincere about buying, how about this, still one spirit stone, but five bottles! How's that? Consider it making new friends, and I'll give you a discount when I have new pills in the future too!"

"Alright, ten bottles then. We still have some stock left from before, let's try yours this time."

After the transaction, Luo Chen and Old Daoist Chen cupped their hands in blessing, "Safe travels into the mountains, may your harvest be bountiful."

After the people left, the two men smiled at each other.

Old Daoist Chen even secretly put another Qi Concealing Talisman on the stall.

Didn't he say he only made one per month?

As the sun rose higher, the scattered cultivator area in the southwest also became more lively.

There were vendors selling pills, cultivation methods, demon beast meat, you name it.

Buyers browsing goods came in an endless stream, and occasionally one or two disciples in the standard Daoist robe of the Jade Tripod Sword Sect would appear.

When the latter showed up, the scattered cultivators became even more energized, raising their prices by dozens of percent.

Sect disciples, those were the real fat sheep!

But that had nothing to do with Luo Chen.

Fasting Pills were just the most common goods on the road, every sect had them.

What's more, the ones he refined were the lowest grade Fasting Pills, they couldn't even be called pills, just Fasting Powder kneaded into pellet form.

No matter how well he refined this kind of stuff, it had no competitive advantage.

By noon, Luo Chen had sold most of his goods.

Just twenty bottles in total, not much at all. Luo Chen had only accumulated this amount over six or seven days.

It could only be said that life was harsh for a low-level pill refiner!

If they were not supported by a sect or family, the life of a wandering low-level pill refining Taoist would be even more miserably inhumane!

Without needing to tidy up anything, Chen the old Taoist gave Luo Chen some beef jerky made from scraps, and Luo Chen was ready to leave.

Before leaving, while chewing on the beef jerky with plain water, Chen the old Taoist called out to stop him.

"Are you going to refine a new pill recipe? It can't be that incomplete pill formula you stumbled upon last year, can it?"

"Kid, don't aim too high. I've seen too many wandering pill refiners research new pill formulas and end up penniless with nothing to show for it."

"It wasn't easy for you to save up those spirit stones. You'd be better off going to the Spirit Medicine Hall to buy a couple bottles of qi-replenishing pills to raise your cultivation a bit."

This counted as rare sincere advice from this stingy old man who loved taking advantage of others.

Luo Chen could sense the other's sincerity.

In fact, the original host had paid an even greater price than what Chen described for that incomplete pill formula.

However, Luo Chen had other plans, so he naturally wouldn't do something as overambitious as that.

"You're getting on in years, be careful with those teeth. This beef jerky could make you lose your teeth."

Chen the old Taoist viciously bit into the beef jerky, with shreds getting stuck between his teeth quite uncomfortably, but he still forcibly swallowed it down.

He glared at Luo Chen.

"Scram!"

Luo Chen snickered, then quickly squeezed into the crowd and disappeared from sight.

He was still in the southwestern wandering cultivator area, but he had arrived at the herb trading district.

Having frequented this area for a long time, he was quite familiar with the buying and selling of all kinds of goods here.

"Sister Wang, did your husband just come back from the mountains with this fresh batch? Feels like it hasn't even dried yet, I'm afraid discerning buyers won't want it."

"Little brother, let me do you a favor today and take ten pounds off your hands, albeit reluctantly!"

"This piece of Polyporusterrootstock, looks like it's not even ten years old! Discount it, and I'll take it."

"Diascorea opposita, yeah, I'll take fifty pounds. I used to procure twenty pounds for one spirit stone, but now the price has doubled. At least give me a bit of a deal!"

"Nevermind, I won't bargain anymore. Just throw in a Taxus fruit, this big one will do."

Over the course of the afternoon, Luo Chen wandered around the herb district, making large-scale purchases of all the medicinal herbs he needed.

Since they were just wandering cultivators selling at stalls, the quality was judged by the eye, and the prices were negotiated.

After wearing his lips dry from all the bargaining, Luo Chen had finally procured this and that.

Next, he still had to make a trip to the city center.

The Spirit Medicine Hall, one of the six top-tier shops in the Daxing Market District, stood in the most prominent location of the city center.

Five stories tall, with intricate carved beams and painted rafters, it cut a breathtakingly beautiful figure.

However, they only sold finished pill products, which was still out of Luo Chen's budget for now.

His destination was the Hundred Herb Hall next to the Spirit Medicine Hall.

Rumor had it that both the Spirit Medicine Hall and Hundred Herb Hall originated from the Pharmaceutical Sect, but no one knew why they had split into two separate establishments.

Compared to the grand Spirit Medicine Hall, the Hundred Herb Hall was much more ordinary.

A row of flat houses extended to the back, appearing plain and unremarkable.

But as one approached, the rich aroma of medicinal herbs wafted over.

Luo Chen's spirit trembled as he gently set down the large sack on his back at the entrance.

The stout shopkeeper eyed that large sack, narrowing his eyes slightly and twitching his nose, before shaking his head.

Luo Chen wasn't bothered as he approached the counter.

"Sir, do you have any Dioscorea persimilis or Cynanchum stauntonii in stock?"

Upon hearing the names of these two medicinal ingredients, the stout shopkeeper gave Luo Chen an intrigued look.
