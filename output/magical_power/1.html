Chapter 1

Here is a full translation of the excerpt from Chinese to English:

Snake Twisting Mountain, Flying Horse Gathering.

Leaning against the wall, Gao Xian's leg twitched slightly as he was abruptly awakened from a nightmare.

The first thing that caught his eye was a strangely shaped yellow brass pill furnace, about one meter tall, shaped like a kettle without a spout. It had three toad-leg-like supports and a brick base underneath.

Below the base was a passage for making fires, connected to a chimney. More accurately, it was a rather complex wood-burning stove.

Gao Xian stared blankly for a moment before sighing. This oddly shaped pill furnace was his means of making a living.

It was already the third day since he had transmigrated. He had adapted to his new body and new name, but could not get used to his new identity: a pill refiner.

Gao Xian stretched out his left hand and silently chanted: "Summon."

A bronze mirror of ancient elegance appeared in his hand, with the four words "Wind and Moon Precious Mirror" engraved on the handle.

This Wind and Moon Precious Mirror was a peripheral item from the novel Dream of the Red Chamber, a birthday gift from a friend. Somehow it had transmigrated with him.

Currently, the smooth mirror surface reflected a handsome, youthful face.

Gao Xian usually just read some Water Margin fan fiction and watched some low-budget movies, so he couldn't find suitable words to describe his current appearance.

Phrases like "sword-like brows and starry eyes" or "jade-like countenance" seemed too clich√©d and hollow.

He could only say he looked a bit like a certain young male movie star - handsome, upright, and vigorous. Especially his black eyes, sparkling and bright as if they could shine...

It felt like there was a professional lighting crew shining lights right at him!

The only flaw was his pale complexion and dark undereye circles, making him look rather unhealthy.

"If I had this face, I could stay single for over thirty years!"

Gao Xian stroked his own face with some vanity, lamenting the laborious days ahead relying solely on his own hands.

This was the only aspect of his transmigration he was satisfied with - a youthful body full of vitality and a handsome, hopeful face!

Words began appearing on the mirror's surface:

Gao Xian: Lifespan 24/66

Cultivation: Qi Refining Second Level (2/200)

Five Phase Skill Second Level Proficient (15/200)

Fire Control Beginner (88/100)

Water Control Beginner (80/100)

Cleaning Beginner (95/100)

Featherweight Proficient (99/200)

Pure Wind Sword Beginner (33/100)

First Level Pill Refining Proficient (29/1000)

Qi Restoring Pill Proficient (55/200)

Foundation Establishing Pill Proficient (41/200)

White Dew Pill Proficient (11/200)

Gao Xian had studied for two days and basically understood the uses of the Wind and Moon Precious Mirror.

This mirror could display his personal status, quantifying his various abilities. The problem was that his Pill Refining skill was grayed out, unable to be used.

No matter what methods Gao Xian tried, he could not activate Pill Refining or change the data shown in the mirror.

According to the original host's memories, he was the pill refiner for a pharmacy!

The pharmacy provided the pill ingredients, and he provided the pills.

He currently owed the pharmacy five hundred each of the Qi Restoring Pill, Foundation Establishing Pill, and White Dew Pill.

He also had a huge problem: the original host had secretly exchanged a batch of refined pills for Blood Revitalizing Pills.

The Blood Revitalizing Pill was an extremely potent qi-cultivating pill. After taking it, the original host had broken through to the second level of qi refining, but then died somehow, leaving his body to Gao Xian.

The large quantity of ingredients used for refining had become a huge loss. How could he explain this to the pharmacy owner?

An even more serious issue was that the original host's memories were incomplete, including those related to pill refining.

Even if he had enough ingredients, he currently could not refine any pills.

Calculating the time, he should have delivered the first batch of pills five days ago.

He didn't know what consequences there would be for not delivering the pills on time, but he imagined there would be nothing good!

As Gao Xian was pondering solutions, he heard the courtyard gate creak open. He startled and hurried to peek out the window.

Through the cracks in the window paper, Gao Xian saw the short, fat pig-like Proprietor Zhu, followed by the tall, large-bosomed Madam Zhu.

The two stood together, almost two feet difference in their heights.

There was one fat and one tall, this combination left an exceptionally deep impression. It also allowed Gao Xian to recognize their identities at a glance.

Gao Xian sighed and tugged at his wrinkled Daoist robe. After taking a deep breath, he pushed open the door to welcome them in.

"Brother Zhu, Sister-in-law, please come inside..."

Gao Xian put on the professional smile he had cultivated over 20 years as a corporate drone, politely and warmly greeting the other party.

"Little Gao, how's it going?" The fat and big-eared Boss Zhu asked with a smile.

Gao Xian said with an embarrassed look: "Brother Zhu, I'm in a hurry to break through to the second level of qi cultivation, but I haven't refined the spirit pills yet. As soon as I'm done, I'll send them right over to you."

Boss Zhu smiled and said, "Little Gao, cultivation must proceed step-by-step. You absolutely cannot be impatient. Going off the rails could cost lives."

The madam standing beside him had a cold, hard expression. She didn't say a word, but just standing there with a solemn face was oppressive.

Gao Xian felt a twinge of guilt. His own stature was actually not short - he reckoned he was at least 1.85 meters tall. Yet standing before the madam, he was shorter by half a head.

In his memory, this madam had reached the ninth level of qi cultivation and excelled at breaking bones with overwhelming martial power.

However, this madam had deep eyes and brows, well-defined facial features, and a wheat-colored skin that was tight and lustrous. Her loose blue Daoist robe could not conceal her ample bosom and long legs.

Her facial features were profound, bearing an uncanny resemblance to the movie version of Wonder Woman, just more exquisite.

Up close, Gao Xian realized the madam was very beautiful. In this dusty place, there was actually such a beauty!

Out of a man's instinct, out of over thirty years of being a shy introvert, he couldn't help but take an extra look.

The madam's brows furrowed slightly, her beautiful green eyes shifting to give Gao Xian a meaningful glance.

"What does the madam mean by that look?"

Gao Xian didn't understand the meaning behind her gaze, yet it didn't stop him from being humble and obedient. With a modest expression, he explained, "Brother Zhu, I'm in a hurry to break through my cultivation level so I can better refine spirit pills.

"The higher the cultivation, the better control I'll have over the fire temperature, and the higher quality the spirit pills will be."

Gao Xian smiled apologetically, "Brother Zhu, Sister-in-law, I didn't mean to delay things."

Boss Zhu waved his hand and said, "Even if I don't consider your master's face, a few days' delay is no big deal."

The smile on his fat face was amiable, as if this wasn't an issue at all.

"Will giving you another ten days be enough?"

Since Boss Zhu was so reasonable, it put Gao Xian at ease too.

He smiled and nodded, "Yes, ten days will be enough. Once they're refined, I'll send them right over."

"Refine the pills well, don't let down your master's expectations of you," Boss Zhu encouraged with a pat on Gao Xian's shoulder, taking on the air of an elder's concern for his junior.

"If you have any issues with your life, you can tell me...Alright, I have something else to attend to, so I'll get going. You don't need to see me out."

Gao Xian hurriedly went to see them off. Boss Zhu smiled and hummed as he went out the door, acting very polite towards Gao Xian's conduct.

Zhu Qiniang followed behind Boss Zhu, but as she was leaving, she gave Gao Xian a deep look.

That gaze seemed to contain rather complex undertones...

Gao Xian was baffled - does the sister-in-law... have a thing for him?

As the saying goes, nothing beats dumplings, but I'm not that kind of person!

No matter what he thought inwardly, outwardly Gao Xian still behaved very respectfully, bowing to see Boss Zhu and his wife off.
