The one leading them was surprisingly Chen Sisi. Ever since the banquet after Su Zhu successfully formed her Natal Spiritual Soul, Su Yu had not seen her again.

Su Qingyi had defected from the Su Family and joined the demonic path, and Chen Sisi had also sent word to the Su Family, severing her Dao partner relationship with Su Qingyi.

Following Chen Sisi were two people. One of them was the white-haired Chen Family's Second Elder.

The other had jet-black hair and appeared to be in middle age, at the ninth level of Qi Refining.

Chen Sisi's cultivation had also reached the ninth level of Qi Refining.

Upon seeing Su Yu, Chen Sisi exchanged a smile with him and nodded slightly, saying, "Brother Yu."

"You know the Second Elder." Chen Sisi pointed to the other robust, nearly nine-foot-tall middle-aged man and said, "This is our Chen Family's Fourth Elder, Chen Kaiyon."

"Greetings, fellow Daoists," Su Yu said respectfully. "May I ask why you are looking for me?"

Chen Sisi said, "Brother Yu, my Chen Family would like two of those puppet beasts that Senior Sister Su Zhu previously had."

Su Yu's brows furrowed slightly. After pondering for a moment, he said, "Alright, two thousand low-grade spirit stones each."

This price was not cheap, on par with high-grade magic treasures.

However, the elderly Chen Family's Second Elder showed a slight smile and said, "Very well, thank you for your kindness, Young Friend Su."

Top-tier puppet beasts at the Natal Spiritual Soul stage were not something one could obtain simply by wanting them, let alone two of them. Even at auctions in Bai Yue Immortal City, one would likely need at least three thousand low-grade spirit stones to purchase a single one.

These were peak combat forces for the Qi Refining Realm that could serve a family for generations, merely requiring the consumption of spirit stones.

The Chen Family's Second Elder understood this, so he thanked Su Yu for his kindness, implying that the Chen Family remembered his favor and was not ungrateful.

The Chen Family immediately handed over four thousand low-grade spirit stones to Su Yu, displaying their wealth and affluence, which slightly moved Su Yu's heart.

It seemed that over the years, the Chen Family had managed to secure that Purple Gold Sand Spirit Vein and had made a small fortune.

Regarding the puppet beasts, the Chen Family agreed to collect them in a year.

Afterward, Chen Sisi and the others were about to take their leave, but Su Yu kept Chen Sisi behind for a casual chat.

Su Yu said, "Congratulations on reaching the ninth level of Qi Refining. Have you considered when you might form your Natal Spiritual Soul?"

Chen Sisi looked into Su Yu's clear, pure eyes devoid of any impurities and sighed, "If all goes well, perhaps in a year or two."

Su Yu smiled and said, "If you need any help, feel free to ask."

"Of course, I'm referring to things like spirit pills, talismans, and the like."

If he could invest a few spirit pills, talismans, and other external objects in someone forming their Natal Spiritual Soul, as long as Chen Sisi succeeded, he would profit.

Just like now, when he sometimes needed second-grade materials, whether for pill refining, talisman crafting, puppet beast materials, or second-grade demonic beast blood essences for body cultivation techniques, he would ask Su Zhu for help collecting them.

Apart from the familial bond, this was also due to the favors Su Yu had done for Su Zhu in the past.

Without Su Zhu's assistance, it would not be easy for him to gather those materials.

Yun Shan Market Town was too small.

When he first came here over a decade ago, he thought it was quite large.

However, after staying here for a long time and gaining a thorough understanding of the surrounding cultivation world, Su Yu realized that Yun Shan Market Town was indeed small in scale.

The largest market town nearest to Cloud Sword Sect was Bai Yue Immortal City.

The scale of Bai Yue Immortal City was several times, perhaps even ten times, larger than Yun Shan Market Town.

It was said that beneath Bai Yue Immortal City ran a third-grade spirit vein, and deep within the city, there was even a third-grade spirit vein blessed land.
