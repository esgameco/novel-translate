Just this alone made it extraordinary.

"What is this?" Suyu was surprised and took it out.

When Suyu's spiritual sense fell on the paper, a loud boom shook his mind, and a vast inheritance of knowledge surged in.

At the very top were three big words:

[Golden Cicada Art]!

After Suyu regained his senses and calmed down, able to gradually recall the inheritance that had entered his mind, it took Suyu more than three hours to go through the inheritance. The more he looked at this thing, the more his heart pounded with shock.

This was a soul cultivation art.

Using one's soul to transform into a golden cicada, strengthening the power of the soul, and ultimately achieving the level of influencing one's spiritual power and physical body with the power of the soul.

For orthodox cultivation methods, this art had an unimaginable empowering effect.

Perhaps it could even allow an originally mediocre genius to metamorphose into a prodigy.

As for the demonic path—

This Golden Cicada Art was simply a divine skill.

Compared to orthodox cultivation methods, the demonic path's cultivation speed was ten times faster, or even a hundred times faster.

The early convenience of fast cultivation had to be paid back doubly later on!

However, the Golden Cicada Art could use the soul to influence one's spiritual power and physical body, gradually tempering one's spiritual power and physical body, making them as pure as a golden cicada's body.

One could say that once the Golden Cicada Art was cultivated, combined with demonic cultivation methods—

He could never cultivate the demonic path!

Otherwise, those demonic cultivators would go crazy.

Although in his view, this Golden Cicada Art was not something ordinary people could cultivate, and the difficulty did not lie in the foundation materials, but in the art itself.

But no matter how difficult, this art was indeed beyond imagination, not an ordinary object.

Suyu even felt that after cultivating the Golden Cicada Art, reaching the Nascent Soul stage would be a breeze.

"I'll try cultivating the introductory level first and see how much it can help my cultivation."

Suyu was extremely eager.

Time passed in a flash, and another half a year went by.

As Suyu was cultivating the Golden Cicada Art, silently and without a sound, he truly entered the art. A golden radiance flashed in all directions in his mind, and his originally illusory soul transformed into a golden cicada.

The radiance of the Golden Cicada Art was ethereal and intangible to the naked eye, permeating Suyu's body.
