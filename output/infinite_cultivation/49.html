"Such a wastrel who breeds like a pig, how dare he take Sister Zhi as a concubine?"

"Even I could kill him with one hand now! I am a hundred times better than him!"

But now that he was in the Su family, he had the qualifications to say so.

One should cultivate when it is time to cultivate, and be firm when one can. If one cannot win, then endure and continue cultivating for a hundred or a thousand years. If one can win, then dare to provoke me?

This was Su Yu's character.

Excluding Su Zhi, the Su family only had one person, Su Bin, at the Foundation Establishment realm.

Even with the Su family's full support, Su Bin had only managed to break through to the second level of the Foundation Establishment realm.

But there was still a vast gulf between the Su family and major cultivating clans like the Jiang family in terms of strength, influence, and foundation.

Apart from the disparity in power and influence, there was also a difference in resources.

The Jiang family could periodically gather materials to refine Foundation Establishment Pills, accumulating resources over many years that allowed them to break through the blockade imposed by major sects like Yunjianzong.

The Su family was different - they were still beneath the blockade line at the bottom.

Apart from the one flawed Foundation Establishment Pill obtained years ago by spending over five thousand low-grade spirit stones, they had not been able to get another pill in over ten years.

Not even a flawed quality one.

Su Yu had only managed to gather three of the core ingredients needed to refine Foundation Establishment Pills.

After a while, Su Peng looked at Su Yu and said, "You mentioned before that you wanted to leave Yunshan Town and go to Baiyue Immortal City. Now that you're back, do you still plan to go?"

Su Yu nodded, "Yunshan Town can no longer help me much. Baiyue Immortal City may be better."

Su Peng started to speak but stopped, eventually shaking his head, "Baiyue Immortal City is quite far from the clan. If you want to settle there, you'll most likely have to rely on yourself in most cases."

He had originally considered whether the clan should follow Su Yu to Baiyue Immortal City and open a pill pavilion branch there.

But after thinking it over, Baiyue was too far away for the clan to properly manage affairs there.

Instead of that, it would be better to let Su Yu decide for himself.

Su Peng looked at Su Yu, "Of course, you may encounter troubles if you go alone. Would you like to take a few people with you?"

After some thought, Su Yu politely declined, wanting to go take a look first by himself.

Su Peng asked Su Yu to stay at the clan residence for a while longer.

Night fell.

Su Yu sat cross-legged in the courtyard, looking up at the starry night sky, suddenly reminded of his former maidservant Lindong.

After inquiring, he learned that over ten years ago, Lindong had already married and now had three children - the oldest was twelve, and the youngest a three-year-old girl with a low-grade spiritual root of the water attribute.

"A water spiritual root?" Su Yu thought of the water attribute cultivation method and spells he had obtained from someone in the Lin family long ago.

Under the cover of night, Su Yu sent those along with ten low-grade spirit stones to Lindong.

Calculating her age, Lindong must be almost forty now.

The next time he saw her, she was no longer the cute and charming young girl from before, but a middle-aged woman.

"There is a divide between immortals and mortals," Su Yu thought to himself after returning. "Perhaps I should find a maidservant with a spiritual root and decent cultivating talent."

If his maidservant could cultivate, even if her talent was just decent, she could at least accompany him for decades or centuries.

However, this was just a thought for now.

Why would a female cultivator with decent talent agree to be his maidservant?

And he didn't look favorably on ordinary female cultivators either.

Su Yu stayed at the clan for a month, taking the opportunity between his cultivation to visit Cangshan Mountain, Jiangcheng City, and tour Lingyue Lake.

Lingyue Lake was considered a spiritual place, capable of producing spirit creatures like moonlight fish.

However, after circling the lake, Su Yu didn't see any difference between it and an ordinary lake.

Perhaps only a geomancer could discern its special qualities.

After a month, Su Yu bid farewell to his master, the clan leader, and the elders before setting off for Baiyue Immortal City.
