Ten miles away from the Su Family's Herbal Garden.

Originally, the geomancer Guo Chudong, invited by the Su Family at great expense, had come here with a few apprentices and joined a group of people.

Seeing Guo Chudong and others arrive, the two Lin Family elders who had been waiting for a long time were overjoyed.

The fourth elder, Lin Hai, hurriedly went up to ask, "Daoist Guo, what's the situation?"

Guo Chudong was neither tall nor thin, with white hair and a face full of wrinkles. He looked older than Lin Hai, but in fact, he was ten years younger than Lin Hai, only in his fifties.

As a geomancer, he had far more cultivation resources than ordinary cultivators.

His cultivation had already reached the ninth level of the Qi Refining Realm.

This trip to the Su Family was for Guo Chudong to accumulate resources to break through to the Foundation Establishment Realm.

Guo Chudong said indifferently, "The guardian beasts of the spiritual vein have gone into a frenzy. Although their number is less than I estimated, there are still two to three hundred of them."

"There are also three late-stage demonic beasts. When in a frenzy, the Rock Rats will become extremely bloodthirsty, and their strength will increase."

"With the cultivators' strength in the Su Family's Herbal Garden, even if they don't all die, they will definitely suffer heavy casualties."

"Wait another hour, and when the frenzied Rock Rats and demonic beasts retreat, we can enter."

Upon hearing this, Lin Hai and the Lin Family's eldest elder, Lin Kui, were overjoyed.

Lin Kui hurriedly stepped forward and said, "With Daoist Guo's help, even if the Su Family doesn't die this time, they will have to roll out of Jiangcheng."

"Daoist Guo, rest assured, the Lin Family's reward will be delivered with both hands when the time comes."

A hint of a smile appeared on Guo Chudong's face as he said, "Just wait."

The Su Family had only paid him a deposit for seeking the dragon vein and constructing a blessed land.

But after the Su Family found him, the Lin Family also found him.

For Guo Chudong, neither the Su Family nor the Lin Family mattered.

What mattered was that the Lin Family said that if he helped deal with the Su Family and take over that first-grade spiritual vein, the Lin Family would double the reward after success.

Thinking of this, Guo Chudong felt anticipation and thought to himself, 'With that money, I can try to collect the materials for the Foundation Establishment Pill and have someone refine it.'

Foundation Establishment!

Guo Chudong clenched his teeth silently. For a geomancer to establish the foundation was at least ten times more difficult than for ordinary people.

Previously, he had failed twice.

Years ago, when he opened the ancient jade box and obtained the incomplete geomancer's inheritance, he was overjoyed and unhesitatingly chose to cultivate.

Little did he know that this geomancer's inheritance would now be like a death talisman, not only locking his path to establishing the foundation but also devouring countless years of his lifespan.

Everyone waited here for an hour.

According to Guo Chudong's estimation, at this time, the Rock Rats would recover from their frenzy and most of them would return underground, greatly reducing the danger.

At this point, entering the Su Family's Herbal Garden and cleaning up the aftermath would allow them to easily seize the Herbal Garden.

"Let's go."

Following Guo Chudong's low shout, along with over a hundred cultivators from the Lin Family, everyone charged towards the Su Family's Herbal Garden.

Upon arriving outside the Su Family's Herbal Garden, Guo Chudong said with some pride, "During this period, under the guise of seeking the dragon vein, I have already grasped the great formation of the Su Family's Herbal Garden and even tampered with a few nodes."

"So we can go in directly."

As he spoke, he took out several formation flags and integrated them into the several layers of the great formation around the Su Family's Herbal Garden, controlling the formation.

After a moment, Guo Chudong said, "Done."

Hum!

With a thought, an entrance immediately opened in the great formation ahead.

When the group of cultivators entered together, the originally open layers of formations suddenly closed, sealing the entrance.

Lin Kui was stunned for a moment: "What's going on?"

"Daoist Guo, Daoist Guo."

Lin Kui called out a few times, but there was no response.

As he was puzzled, suddenly the front few layers of formations erupted with intense aura. This abnormal situation immediately caused Lin Kui's expression to change drastically.

"Not good."

Inside the formation enclosure.

Guo Chudong, Lin Hai and others who had just entered the medicine garden were also stunned, as the formation barrier closed behind them.

Guo Chudong even sensed that the few formation flags he had integrated into the great formation had been destroyed by someone.

He lost control over the medicine garden's great formation.

Guo Chudong's heart skipped a beat, and his face changed slightly: "Not good."

The next moment, the cold and murderous voice of the Third Elder Su Chang rang in their ears: "Guo Chudong, my Su family has treated you well. The price you asked for lending a hand is worth a Foundation Establishment Pill."

"But you colluded with the Lin family, trying to seize my Su family's medicine garden. You even instigated the rock rat demons to rebel, wanting my Su family to die."

"You have such audacity!"

Su Chang roared angrily, then continued towards Lin Hai: "Lin Hai, your Lin family has provoked my Su family again and again. Do you really think my Su family is easy to bully?"

"Today you came in here, so don't think about leaving again!"

"Boom!"

As the words fell, Su Chang activated all the layers of the great formation in the medicine garden. These were top-tier first-grade formations that the Su family spent a huge sum to set up.

Besides the defensive formation, there were also killing and trapping formations.

Perhaps they were difficult to deal with for Foundation Establishment cultivators, but they were easy to handle for Qi Condensation cultivators.

Sensing the aura of the great formation, Guo Chudong's face changed again. With a wave of his hand, formation flags appeared one by one.

But before he could break the formation, a raging wind swept in from the heavens and earth.

Sensing the sharp aura of that raging wind, even Guo Chudong, a ninth layer Qi Condensation cultivator, couldn't help but feel his scalp go numb.

In desperation, he could only set up a defensive formation around himself.

"Boom!!!"

The raging wind struck. The few disciples around him, including Lin Hai, could only resist for a moment before turning into a bloody mist, killed by the great formation.

Shrill, miserable screams echoed in Guo Chudong's ears.

In the courtyard.

Su Chang sensed Guo Chudong setting up a formation to defend against the raging wind attack, and couldn't help revealing a hint of graveness in his eyes, thinking to himself: "This person is indeed not simple."

The two sides deadlocked for a moment, then Guo Chudong's voice came from the formation he set up: "Daoist Su, shall we talk about how to resolve this?"

"Your formation can't do anything to me, and I've also set up some tricks in the spirit vein."

"If you really push me too far, although I can't destroy the spirit vein, letting it suffer serious damage is no problem at all."

Upon hearing this, Su Chang's face instantly changed.

He hadn't put all of the Lin family's people inside earlier, because he was afraid this person might have other tricks up his sleeve.

Plus, the Lin family's Elder Lin Kui was at the ninth layer of Qi Condensation. If they joined forces, who knew if there might be an accident.

So he didn't put everyone inside.

But he didn't expect that even just Guo Chudong alone would be so difficult to deal with.

At this time, Su Yu said in a low voice: "Third Granduncle, you can trap him first. When the Patriarch and others arrive, we can discuss further."

"As long as we subdue him, he'll just be a lamb awaiting slaughter. Whether he lives or dies will be up to our Su family to decide."

Hearing this, Su Chang's eyes immediately lit up.

Subduing a Geomancer - that would bring great benefits to the Su family.
