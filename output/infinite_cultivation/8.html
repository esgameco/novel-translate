Seeing the elder, Su Yu respectfully went forward and bowed, saying: "Third Elder, I've come to redeem the bounty."

He handed over the sack.

The old man was Su Chang, the third elder of the Su family, in charge of guarding the medicinal garden and overseeing the planting and operation of the spirit herbs.

This medicinal hall was the center of the medicinal garden.

Su Chang was slightly surprised to see one mid-grade first-level and two early first-level rock rats in the sack. He looked at Su Yu and asked, "Did you hunt these?"

Su Chang praised him: "Not bad, being able to hunt a mid-grade first-level rock rat has eliminated a major threat to the medicinal garden."

The rock rat demonic beast group was quite numerous, but most were only early first-level demonic beasts. Mid-grade first-level rock rats were not common.

He had not seen any late first-level rock rats.

After praising him, Su Chang took out the low-grade spirit stones for the bounty and the material value of the three demonic beasts. Since the mid-grade first-level rock rat did not have any essence blood material, the total, including the bounty reward, was only forty-two low-grade spirit stones.

The bounty reward alone was thirty low-grade spirit stones.

Early first-level demonic beast materials were not worth much.

After receiving the spirit stones, Su Yu respectfully took his leave and left the medicinal hall.

Its only two specialties were sharpness and armor penetration.

Priced at sixty low-grade spirit stones, it was not expensive.

After acquiring the talisman, Su Yu still had twenty-four low-grade spirit stones left.

In the time to come, Su Yu did not plan to hunt earth rock rats so frequently, at most once every half a month or month.

At the pharmacy, the Third Elder Su Chang looked at the bounty records with slight surprise. Within half a month, that junior named Su Yu had actually hunted as many as nine earth rock rats.

This number was quite unusual.

Looking further, not long ago Su Yu had spent sixty low-grade spirit stones to exchange for a low-grade Crimson Luo Blade talisman.

Su Yu refined the low-grade Crimson Luo Blade talisman, storing it within his dantian to nurture it with his spiritual power.

This talisman was not for long-range use, but rather to take advantage of his own Body Refining technique's strengths in close combat.

After all, compared to his mere second level of Qi Refining spiritual power, his fourth level Body Refining technique was his strongest ability at present.

"Next, I'll focus on cultivating the Willow Green Wood Mantra." Su Yu looked at the Crimson Luo Blade talisman in his dantian and secretly breathed a sigh of relief.

With the fourth level of Body Refining technique and a low-grade talisman by his side, he at least had some assurance of safety.

He was no longer a helpless novice cultivator to be slaughtered at will.

Nurturing the talisman, cultivating spiritual power, and using the essence blood of first level mid-stage demonic beasts to cultivate the Body Refining technique.

Su Yu's life in the medicine garden was very regular, from morning till night, each day seeing his mastery of the techniques steadily increase, giving him a sense of fulfillment.

It was only after the Body Refining technique reached the fourth level that the essence blood of first level mid-stage demonic beasts no longer had much effect on it.

The rate of increase in mastery of the Body Refining technique became as slow as that of spiritual power from the Willow Green Wood Mantra.

This peaceful daily life suddenly changed after half a year.

Su Qingyi and a few cultivators from the medicine garden clan had gone out on leave but were ambushed by the Lin family, with only Su Qingyi managing to escape back to Jiangcheng, but severely injured.

The other few were killed in the ambush by the Lin family.

Immediately, the relationship between the two major cultivator families in Jiangcheng became extremely tense, with swords drawn and bows bent.
