Inside the secret room.

Su Yu sat cross-legged and used the Dao Body Puppet Technique to extract the strand of soul he had previously cut from the first-grade upper-rank puppet beast.

He then fused it into the Dragon Alligator puppet beast's body.

At the moment of fusion, Su Yu felt a ferocious demonic aura assaulting him.

Kill! Kill! Kill!

That ferocious, violent, and wild aura madly invaded Su Yu's strand of soul.

This was one of the dangers of practicing the Dao Body Puppet Technique.

The material of the puppet beast would affect the fused strand of soul, and over time, it could potentially change the host's state of mind.

However, at the moment the Dragon Alligator puppet beast's ferocious aura attacked, Su Yu employed the Golden Cicada Technique.

Hum!

The brilliant golden light illuminated the entire Dragon Alligator puppet beast.

With the Dao Body Puppet Technique in effect, the Dragon Alligator puppet beast, accompanied by a flash of light, took the form of a Dao Body puppet that looked exactly like Su Yu.

Su Yu admired it for a moment. If the Dao Body puppet's body was not damaged, even Suzhi and the others might not be able to tell that it was just a puppet.

However, if he opened it himself, the risk would be a bit high. Being at the Qi Refining realm was too eye-catching and would arouse jealousy from others.

The next day.

Su Yu invited Yu Ke'er over as a guest. It had been some time since they last met, and Yu Ke'er's face showed a hint of weariness, with traces of wrinkles on her forehead, though not obvious.

"Miss Yu has been very busy lately?" Su Yu asked, slightly surprised.

Yu Ke'er gently shook her head, took a sip of the spirit tea Su Yu handed her, but didn't respond. She just quietly drank her tea, seeming to enjoy the tranquility of the courtyard that allowed her heart to calm down.

After a while.

Yu Ke'er finally looked at Su Yu and said, "You're not the type to casually invite people over for idle chatter. Out with it, what's the matter?"

Upon hearing this, Su Yu just smiled, for he had a thick enough face.

Su Yu said, "Are you interested in partnering to open an elixir pavilion? Right here in Bai Yue Immortal City."

"I'm not interested," Yu Ke'er shook her head at first, but then paused. She gazed silently at Su Yu for a moment, her mind racing with many thoughts, even guessing Su Yu's intention behind those words.

If it were anyone else, Yu Ke'er might have turned and left without a second thought.

But as she looked at Su Yu's handsome face that hadn't changed in dozens of years, she fell silent for a while.

Yu Ke'er said, "How did you plan to open it?"

Su Yu tried proposing a 70/30 partnership split, already prepared for Yu Ke'er to counter-offer, with a 50/50 split being his bottom line.

However, Yu Ke'er shook her head and said, "A 90/10 split, with you taking 90 and me 10. It can be opened under my name."

Su Yu stared at Yu Ke'er in a daze.

At this moment, Yu Ke'er smiled charmingly and said, "It's rare to find an opportunity to make you owe me a favor, so let's do it this way."

Su Yu remained silent for a long while, but eventually shook his head and said, "Let's do 70/30 instead, otherwise I won't feel at ease."

Yu Ke'er looked at Su Yu for a moment, then nodded lightly without arguing further. Instead, she brought up matters like the Spiritual Void Fruit and so on.

After chatting idly for a while, Yu Ke'er finally left.

As she was leaving, Yu Ke'er took it upon herself to handle matters like choosing the elixir pavilion's location and so on. The premises would count as her investment into the partnership.

After Yu Ke'er left, Su Yu gently frowned, unsure whether opening this elixir pavilion was a good or bad thing.

He roughly understood Yu Ke'er's intentions, but he didn't have those kinds of thoughts.

"Let's see how it goes. As long as there are no issues, the elixir pavilion can keep operating. But if..." Su Yu sighed lightly, wondering if continuing that line of thought would make him a scoundrel.

Pah, he was completely pure and upright.

Although this Miss Yu Ke'er of the Yu Family's main lineage was quite exceptional, at least a nine out of ten.

Shaking his head to dispel the jumbled thoughts, a smile appeared on Su Yu's face: "With a stable income secured, I can cultivate steadily from now on."
