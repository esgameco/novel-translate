The Yuanyang Wilderness Ridge was a demonic beast wilderness within the territory of Baiyue Immortal City, filled with countless demonic beasts. It was rumored that even demon tribes were hiding there, making it extremely dangerous.

Under Wu Yugang's lead, it took the four of them nearly two days of traveling before they arrived outside the Yuanyang Wilderness Ridge and saw the deep mountains and ancient forests ahead.

Without the slightest hesitation, the four of them charged straight in.

A moment later.

A stealthy figure arrived silently. The spirit puppet transformed into a shadow panther and followed them from a distance.

The undergrowth in the forest was dense, with demonic beasts and venomous insects hiding everywhere.

Wu Yugang's group moved very slowly, with each step potentially bringing unknown dangers.

So after entering the Yuanyang Wilderness Ridge, the four of them used their spiritual power to create a barrier around their bodies. This way, even if there were venomous insects or demonic beasts, they could temporarily withstand them.

The downside was that they had to stop and rest every so often to recover their spiritual power.

The spirit puppet hid in the dense undergrowth, watching those four prey from afar, unhurried.

"It's convenient for the beast form to move in this demonic beast wilderness, while the human form can walk outside." Su Yu felt the experience of the spirit puppet's shadow panther form moving through the dense forest and found it very comfortable.

Moreover, his mind was unprecedentedly relaxed, as if he had returned to his 'own' den.

The spirit puppet entered the Yuanyang Wilderness Ridge, and he wasn't worried at all.

It was completely different from the experience when his main body entered the dense forest while hunting the Zuo Mountain Eagle with Su Zhizhi.

Two days later.

When Su Yu saw Wu Yugang's group stop in front of a mountain valley, he made a mental note and took out a bottle of Confusion Liquid from his cosmic bag, preparing to apply it to the shadow panther's claws before taking action.

"Confusion Liquid, a second-grade low-rank Freezing Talisman, a Fire Dragon Talisman, and two second-grade early-stage Beast Subjugation Talismans... should be stable?"

In front of the mountain valley, Wu Yugang's group carefully explored the surroundings before returning here.

Wu Yugang frowned slightly and transmitted, "I think the Cold Heart Lotus will mature in a day or half a day at most. By then, those demonic beasts will definitely fight for it."
