Sealing the letter.

Su Yu took out the letter and opened it to see a line of elegant small characters entering his eyes.

It described some of her experiences and unexpected opportunities she encountered at Bayaoxiling, and in there, she discovered that extraordinary bead.

After pondering for a long time, she still decided to hand over this object to Su Yu.

At the end of the letter, she entrusted him to take care of the Chen family.

Su Yu read it several times, remaining silent for a long while: "......"

The Heavenly Dao is unpredictable.

The path of immortality is equally difficult to tread, no one dares to guarantee eternal youthfulness.

Putting away the letter, Su Yu's gaze fell on the ink jade-like round bead, his pupils contracting slightly as he sensed the spiritual power emanating from the round bead, he couldn't help but exclaim in shock: "Is this really a Kuanghun?"

Just as there are treasured artifacts existing on magical weapons, there are also treasured talismans existing on ordinary talismans.

In the realm of puppets, ancient cultivators similarly crafted a special type of treasure called Kuanghun, meaning the soul of a puppet, or a soul treasure.

This round bead should be the embryonic form of a Kuanghun.

According to the inheritance of the 'Forging Method of the Golden Jade Celestial Puppet', Kuanghun is the 'treasured artifact' or 'treasured talisman' of puppets.

It can be integrated into any puppet, forming a puppet akin to a 'treasured artifact'. It can be absorbed into the dantian to be nurtured, and can devour special materials to gradually elevate the rank of the puppet, cultivating the Kuanghun.

Just like the fabled flying swords cultivated by sword cultivators as their life-bound treasures.

Although this embryonic Kuanghun bead is still just an embryo, the spiritual aura emanating from it is extraordinary, causing even Su Yu to feel a chill upon sensing it.

It's possible that this embryonic Kuanghun has already reached the peak of the Foundation Establishment realm, or even the pseudo-Pill realm.

According to the inheritance, the Celestial Puppets of the Golden Jade Immortal Sect require a Kuanghun to be forged.

However, there is no information left regarding just how powerful these Celestial Puppets are.

Su Yu's brows furrowed slightly, feeling both delighted and troubled: "Can the Dragon Crocodile Puppet Beast withstand the integration of this embryonic Kuanghun bead?"

Let's give it a try!

Su Yu gritted his teeth and summoned his Dao Body Puppet, and to his surprise, the fusion between the Dao Body Puppet and the embryonic Kuanghun bead went far more smoothly than expected.

He severed a strand of his soul and infused it into the Dao Body Puppet to take control of the Kuanghun, then fused the Kuanghun with the puppet.

The Golden Cicada Art, Dao Body Puppet Technique, Kuanghun, and puppets - these were simply a perfect match.

However, after integrating the Kuanghun, Su Yu realized that while it was fine when not utilizing the Kuanghun, once he did use it, the power of the Kuanghun would need to be re-nurtured and accumulated.

Additionally, at the current rank of the Kuanghun, if its full power was unleashed, the Dragon Crocodile Puppet Beast might be severely injured in a single battle, unable to withstand it.

Of course.

These two issues were not major problems.

The first issue could be resolved by the Golden Cicada Art, which would greatly accelerate the recovery speed of the Kuanghun.

As for the second issue -

If the Dragon Crocodile Puppet Beast could absorb materials to elevate itself to a second-rank superior puppet, then there would be no issues at all.

And if it couldn't be elevated in a short period of time, with the Kuanghun present, as long as it devoured materials, the Dragon Crocodile Puppet Beast would still be able to recover to its original state and would not be directly destroyed. So, it wasn't a big problem.

Ignoring these issues.

The appearance of the embryonic Kuanghun bead had already given Su Yu an unprecedented means of self-preservation.

Chen Sisi had delivered to him an opportunity and great gift that he could not refuse.

As he pondered, Su Yu gazed silently towards the direction of the Yunjian Sect's domain. He had never thought of getting involved in the remnants of the Golden Jade Immortal Sect.

But who could have imagined that he, a person far away in Baiyue Immortal City, would still obtain a great opportunity from the remnants of the Golden Jade Immortal Sect?

However, the karmic tie that Chen Sisi had formed, he still had to repay.
