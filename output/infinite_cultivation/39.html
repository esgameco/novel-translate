Su Jingbang shook his head, not optimistic about the Chen family's ability to defend the Purple Golden Sand Spirit Vein.

Su Yu was not surprised by this.

In the cultivation world, every resource site was a huge trouble. Resources were limited, so if you wanted to occupy a resource area, you had to be prepared for major troubles.

The Chen family's Purple Golden Sand Spirit Vein was one such case.

However, what was going on with the Huang family of Guanlan Mountain? How did they get involved? Su Yu had heard of this cultivation family skilled in formation arts.

If the cultivation families under Cloud Sword Sect were ranked, the Yu family of Black Rock Mountain would be second only to the Guan family. Another low-profile but powerful family was the Jiang family, skilled in puppetry, whose strength was no less than the Yu family's.

The Huang family of Guanlan Mountain might rank third or in the top five.

With the Huang family's power and strength, they should not have looked down upon a mere Purple Golden Sand Spirit Vein, nor committed such an outrageous act?

Su Yu voiced his doubts. As Su Jingbang was about to speak, Su Yundao said, "Recently all kinds of resources are in short supply. Purple golden sand is an important material for forging formation flags, so it's not surprising the Huang family intervened."

Su Jingbang said, "The patriarch now intends to withdraw from the contest for the Purple Golden Sand Spirit Vein, but...it may still take some time."

Hearing this, Su Yu felt relieved, as he had felt they should not get involved with that spirit vein.

It was good they were withdrawing now!

However, events outpaced plans. Four months later, there was a sudden reversal regarding the Chen family's Purple Golden Sand Spirit Vein. An elder of the Bai family at the Foundation Establishment realm, along with some Foundation Establishment cultivators from the Huang family, encountered the Blood Saber Cultivators and were all slaughtered by them.

The Bai family was severely damaged! They retreated back to Maple City, trembling in fear of being targeted by the Blood Saber Cultivators.

As a result, the Chen family's Purple Golden Sand Spirit Vein was temporarily free of risk and threats. The Su family patriarch Su Peng also suspended his plan to withdraw from it.

Su Yu was stunned for a long time upon hearing this news. Who could have imagined the Bai and Huang families would suffer such misfortune? This matter involved the Chen family and Yu Ke'er of the Yu family's third branch. Since Chen Sisi had married into the Su family, it was inappropriate for her to speak up at this point.
