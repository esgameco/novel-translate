"Only Bai Yue Immortal City has more of what we need."

Hearing Su Yu discuss Bai Yue Immortal City, a look of longing appeared in the eyes of Su Gutong, Su Ruian, and the other members of the Su Family.

Without chatting idly with Su Gutong and the others for long, after making arrangements, Su Yu chose to leave.

He came as a youth, and though Su Yu was now forty years old when he left, he still felt like a youth.

The appearance of a youth, the mindset of a youth.

After staying in Cloud Mountain Town for twenty years, Su Yu stopped his spirit horse and looked back at the town one last time.

"Let's go!"

"Hahaha!"

Su Yu laughed out loud a few times, filled with relaxation and delight, and swiftly rode away on his spirit horse.

In the town.

At the Heavenly Book Pavilion, an old man sitting behind the counter who had been dozing off suddenly woke up with a start. His divine sense probed out and saw Su Yu's figure leaving the town.

"Huh? This kid is leaving?" The old man muttered, his expression a bit conflicted.

"Forget it, I'll give him a kind blessing."

In the end, the old man let out a slight sigh.

Over these years, he had found Su Yu a bit likable.

As he stretched out his hand, a jade slip appeared, but in the next moment, the old man waved his hand and the jade slip instantly vanished without a trace.

Su Yu, who had already left Cloud Mountain Town over ten li away, was riding his spirit horse toward the Su Family.

Suddenly, a black dot descended from the sky and struck him on the head without any warning, almost knocking Su Yu off and causing him to fall.

"???"

Su Yu picked up the jade slip that had fallen to the ground and his pupils constricted as his divine sense scanned in all directions, but he didn't detect the presence of anyone.

He had no idea where this jade slip had come from.

Damn it.

Run quickly!

Su Yu was jolted, and immediately spurred his horse to gallop madly. He ran for hundreds of li before finally stopping in a desolate forest.

Three days later.

At the blessed land of Cang Mountain in Jiang City.

After many years, Su Yu returned and saw a scene of prosperity in Cang Mountain. Slightly closer to the blessed land, the Su family had opened up plot after plot of spiritual fields to grow spiritual rice.

As for the original fields, they continued to expand outwards, nourished by the spiritual qi of a first-grade spiritual vein, allowing the planting of spiritual herbs even in the areas further out.

Apart from nearly twenty new cultivators from their own family, there were over thirty outside cultivators who had pledged allegiance to the Su family.

However, when Su Yu entered the manor, he could sense an oppressive atmosphere in the household.

The family master Su Peng, Elder Su Yun, Second Elder Su Chang, Third Elder Su Ruosu, and Fourth Elder Su Jingbang had all arrived.

He was over seventy years old. Although he had cultivated the family master's Willow Green Wood Mantra, extending his lifespan beyond that of an ordinary ninth level Qi Condensation cultivator, at this age, he had reached his final peak period.

"As for Xiaozhi, she is considered part of the elder lineage of the Cloud Sword Sect."

Sect infighting!

Su Yu's pupils constricted as he inwardly raged. This was truly a case of celestials fighting while mortals suffer. The Jiang family must see the Su family as easy to bully.

But after serious consideration, compared to the Jiang family's strength and influence, the Su family did seem rather easy to bully.
