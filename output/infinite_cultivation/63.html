Su Zhi was born into the Cloud Sword Sect and knew how rare it was to obtain a high-quality foundation pill. Even the second-grade senior pill refiners of the Cloud Sword Sect had only managed to refine two or three such pills over the years.

It was possible that after refining five or six batches of foundation pills, one high-quality pill might be produced by chance.

And within the Cloud Sword Sect, only the direct disciples had the opportunity to request high-quality foundation pills; others did not even have a chance to exchange for them.

Originally, Su Zhi did not expect Su Yu to be able to refine many foundation pills, as long as there was one of ordinary quality.

But now, looking at the two high-quality and two ordinary foundation pills before her, she deeply realized just how strong her younger brother's pill refining skills were.

Su Zhi was dazed for a long time before finally taking a few deep breaths to suppress her joy and excitement. Looking at Su Yu, she said, "If your pill refining skills were to spread, there would definitely be many people coming to ask you to refine pills for them."

Upon hearing this, Su Yu hurriedly said, "No, this is just a fluke for me."

"Moreover, I'm still only at the Qi Refining realm. If this gets out, it will bring trouble rather than blessings."

When Su Zhi heard this, her expression changed slightly as she remembered that Su Yu was still only at the eighth level of the Qi Refining realm.

With such cultivation, yet able to refine high-quality foundation pills—

"Whew."

Su Zhi took another deep breath and looked at Su Yu with a complicated expression, saying, "Your pill refining talent really is as monstrous as Sister Chen and Sister Yu said."

"If only your spiritual root was a bit better, or if I could give you mine, that would be great."

Su Yu smiled and said, "Sister, I don't want your spiritual root."

"Spiritual roots are destined by heaven, but one's achievements are not predetermined. Just like the legendary Real Person Under the Moon, didn't he also achieve the realm of Pill Immortal despite having an inferior spiritual root?"

"My current pace of cultivation is actually quite good, steady and progressive."

After their casual chat, Su Yu and Su Zhi discussed how to handle these foundation pills.

"The First Elder, Second Elder, and Third Elder are all quite old. Although they are at the ninth level of the Qi Refining realm, the possibility of them breaking through to the Foundation Establishment realm is almost non-existent. Even with foundation pills, they probably wouldn't choose to take them."

"So, in our family, there are currently two candidates left: my father, who is seventy-three years old, and Su Jing Bang, the Fourth Elder, who is seventy-five years old."

"They are both on the verge of being able to attempt to break through to the Foundation Establishment realm, but the probability of failure is quite high for both of them."

Su Zhi explained the situation of the Su family.

"For those younger, in their fifties and sixties at the eighth level of the Qi Refining realm, our family currently has four."

"We don't need to consider them for now; we'll see about them later."

Su Yu listened and said, "Since that's the case, let's invite the family leader and Elder Jing Bang to come to Bai Yue Immortal City."

"There are two high-quality foundation pills, just enough for the two of them."

Upon hearing this, Su Zhi immediately objected, "No, you must keep one high-quality foundation pill for yourself."

Su Yu opened his mouth but didn't know how to explain.

He didn't actually have any bottlenecks; as long as his proficiency reached the required level, he could break through.

Even the Foundation Establishment realm was the same for him.

But this was obviously something he couldn't reveal or explain.

Su Zhi looked at Su Yu and said, "You have to listen to me on this. You must keep one high-quality foundation pill for yourself."

"I'll write a letter to have Father and the Fourth Elder come to Bai Yue Immortal City."

This speed of advancement was unimaginably fast.

Another month passed, and the 'Profound Nether Thick Earth Scripture' had reached the Qi Refining third level.

At the same time, Su Yu had mastered the two Earth-attribute spells he was practicing to the perfect realm.

On the other hand, after receiving Su Zhi's letter, the Su Family was greatly shaken. Although the letter did not mention Condensing Pellets, Su Zhi asking Su Peng and Su Jingbang to go to Worship Moon Immortal City to attempt breakthroughs was almost an outright statement.

"What's going on? Didn't Zhi go to acquire core materials like the Void Spirit Fruit? How did she end up asking me and the Fourth Elder to go to Worship Moon Immortal City to attempt breakthroughs?"

"Could it be that she and Yu obtained Condensing Pellets? But that doesn't seem right, she's asking both of us to go, this—"

They were both astonished and puzzled.

After discussing with the other family members, especially the decision of the Condensing Foundation cultivator Su Bin, Su Peng and Su Jingbang set off for Worship Moon Immortal City.

As for the First Elder Su Yun, Second Elder Su Chang, and Third Elder Su Ruosu, they did not dwell on it much.

They were already old and had long given up on the idea of breaking through to the Condensing Foundation realm.

Instead,

Seeing Su Peng and Su Jingbang set off for Worship Moon Immortal City, the three elders were filled with immense anticipation and hope.

So, while Su Peng, Su Jingbang and the others were attempting to break through to the Condensing Foundation realm in that cave abode in the core area, half a month after returning to his courtyard residence, Su Yu succeeded!

The second-grade lower-rank dragon alligator puppet beast was refined!

Inside the secret room, a monster resembling a ferocious dragon three zhang tall yet with the head, limbs, and tail of a monstrous alligator appeared in this world.

The appearance of this dragon alligator puppet beast was slightly different from the puppet master inheritance Su Yu had obtained.

And this slightly modified puppet beast undoubtedly emanated an even stronger aura.
