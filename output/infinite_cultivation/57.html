This top-grade dark panther puppet beast was the initial top-grade first-level puppet product, with a strength of only the eighth level of the Qi Refining realm.

Once the second-grade lower-level dragon alligator puppet beast was ready, he could use that puppet beast to transform into a body puppet.

This puppet was just a transitional tool.

"Interesting."

Su Yu controlled the 'old man' body puppet to leave the basement room and wander the streets of Bai Yue Xian City. At the same time, he could also practice the Golden Cicada Technique to recover the damage to his soul.

In the following period, Su Yu familiarized himself with the body puppet while methodically cultivating the Cangmu Suixuan Technique, the Alligator Demon's One Hundred and Eight Body Refining Techniques, and the Golden Cicada Technique.

Note on the tenth level Body Refining Techniques 'shattering Foundation Establishment realm bodies with one punch': Cultivators without body refining have very fragile physical bodies. The tenth level of body refining techniques is equivalent to the power of the first level Foundation Establishment realm. The passage means that in close combat after breaking through defenses, one has the ability to shatter the bodies of Foundation Establishment cultivators who have not cultivated body refining techniques. It is no longer powerless against the Foundation Establishment realm, and can threaten or even kill them. It does not mean literally being able to shatter them with a single strike.

In an actual fight, a first level Foundation Establishment cultivator's power is greater than that of body refining at the same realm, with higher odds of winning.
