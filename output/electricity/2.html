Jixing Fire let out a groan and then regained consciousness: "I'm not dead!"

He jolted up and found himself sitting on the floor, in the familiar bedroom. There was a patch of coagulated blood where he had been lying.

Apart from a bit of dizziness, he had no other discomfort.

Jixing Fire felt a sense of relief.

Before losing consciousness, he had truly thought he was going to die. He was still young, with so much of life left to enjoy - the vast galaxy and starry skies, countless magnificent landscapes he had yet to appreciate, thrilling adventures he had not yet embarked on. It would have been too tragic to die so soon.

"It's good to be alive!"

Filled with the joy of surviving, Jixing Fire tried to find the culprit behind it all, scanning the room, but he couldn't find the gemstone.

"Was it a successful fusion with an alien species?" He was both surprised and delighted, even though he didn't understand why the gemstone fused with his eyes.

"What about the special ability?"

Getting up from the floor, Jixing Fire found that he didn't seem to have gained any supernatural powers.

He stood up unsteadily, clutching his head, feeling increasingly dizzy.

His vision was also flickering. Everything he saw was trembling, as if the signal reception was unstable, blacking out for a moment every few seconds, his vision becoming blurry before quickly regaining clarity, repeating endlessly and making him feel very uncomfortable.

"There's something wrong with my eyes!"

Jixing Fire realized that this must be the special ability he had gained from the fusion.

He staggered into the bathroom, stood in front of the sink, and was startled by the sight of himself in the mirror. His face was covered in blood, with blood flowing from his eyes, nose, and ears, as well as some unknown white and red fluids that had congealed, covering half his face, making it look like his brain had exploded - a terrifying sight.

Jixing Fire quickly washed off the dirt on his face, and found no wounds on his head, nor any strange growths, finally letting out a sigh of relief.

"Luckily, my face is still intact." He felt his face.

Although he wasn't the kind of handsome guy that everyone loved, he was still quite good-looking, with distinct features and a sunny, resolute demeanor. He had quite a few female admirers at school, and could be considered a little heartthrob. If he didn't do well in the future, he could still consider relying on his looks.

Jixing Fire checked the time - he had been unconscious for about half an hour.

The dizziness in his head had greatly subsided, and his vision was gradually no longer flickering or blurry, seemingly returning to normal.

No, not normal at all!

Jixing Fire noticed something strange - his vision was not just stabilizing, but actually enhancing. The world around him became increasingly clear, and he could see more and more details.

He could see the countless tiny water droplets floating in the air of the bathroom; the floor, which seemed spotless, was actually lightly coated with a thin layer of dust, some of which were his own skin flakes; when he raised his hand, the skin on the back looked like a cracked, dried-up earth, with deep crevices, and he could even see the fine hairs and the dust particles clinging to them.

The entire world had changed in Jixing Fire's eyes.

"Eagle Eye?"

Jixing Fire immediately thought of "Eagle Eye" - after the fusion, he had gained the ability of far-sighted vision and extremely keen dynamic vision, comparable to the eyes of an eagle, hence the name.

Eagle Eye was an excellent ability, not too rare.

Jixing Fire felt a bit disappointed.

If it was just Eagle Eye, then he had really gotten the short end of the stick. The gap between an excellent ability and a superior ability was huge, not even comparable.

"It shouldn't be Eagle Eye."

Jixing Fire quickly realized the difference.

The most prominent feature of Eagle Eye was long-distance vision, but what he was seeing now was close-up, like the effect of a magnifying glass.

"Is this... Microscopic Vision?" Jixing Fire was a bit puzzled.

Microscopic Vision, like Eagle Eye, was also an excellent ability, one that enhanced near-vision instead of far-vision. It was even more valuable than Eagle Eye, as it not only strengthened visual abilities, but also provided powerful assistance in close-quarters combat.

Two completely opposite abilities, how could they both manifest? Moreover, how could a single alien species have two abilities?

Jixing Fire returned to the bedroom.

The apartment he rented had a simple, minimalist design, with no living room - the entrance led directly into the spacious 60-square-meter bedroom. The remaining 40 square meters were divided into a study and a training room, which were also connected.

The apartment's furnishings were sparse - there were two rows of sofas and a coffee table in the middle, and a simple bar counter/dining table with a kettle and a few other food preparation tools.

In the corner, there was a virtual platform login capsule.

Apart from that, there were no other decorations, making the entire apartment feel empty and cold.

A large bed was placed near the balcony, with an embedded adjustable glass wall behind the headboard.

"Open the window."

Jixing Fire spoke softly.

Instantly, the glass wall became transparent, revealing the outside scenery. Brilliant neon lights streamed into the apartment, and looking out, Jixing Fire saw the towering skyscrapers, like a range of mountains in the night, their lights illuminating the sky.

The hundreds of floors of residential lights were all lit up, and the giant screens on the buildings were playing advertisements, the colorful lights intertwining in a dreamlike manner.

Looking down, he could see the multi-layered superconducting highways between the buildings.

Magnetic levitation vehicles were speeding along the highways, their taillights forming long, endless streams of light, like the blood vessels of this super-metropolis, criss-crossing and flowing ceaselessly.

Jixing Fire was already accustomed to this sight.

He stood in front of the glass window, his gaze quickly locking onto a building three kilometers away.

The night was clear, with no clouds obscuring the view.

Jixing Fire could clearly see the four glowing characters on the top of the building: "Super Ascent Group".

In the past, he could only vaguely make out that there were four characters there, but couldn't read the content. Now, not only could he see the large characters, but also the smaller slogan below: "Super Ascent Group, wholeheartedly serving you, helping you break through bottlenecks, and achieve an extraordinary life!"

"Haha..."

Jixing Fire couldn't help but chuckle.

The Super Ascent Group was a renowned company, one of the top firms that helped people with special abilities to cultivate and break through. It had been publicly listed for decades, with a market value already exceeding a trillion.

However, the group's name, which sounded like "Super Birth", contrasted sharply with the country's population situation and birth policies, often becoming the butt of jokes.

"It's definitely Eagle Eye, but not just that."

Jixing Fire shifted his gaze to his own hands, feeling an exquisitely fine sense of touch, and a slight increase in the speed of his thoughts.

"This is clearly the effect of Microscopic Vision."

Eagle Eye and Microscopic Vision, two abilities combined into one.

But there was more! After a moment of focus, Jixing Fire realized that when he concentrated on a target, he would experience a sensation similar to mental locking. He had an intuition that if he had something to throw, as long as the target was within range, he would be able to hit it with certainty.

"It's Precision!"

Jixing Fire was quite delighted.

The Precision ability greatly enhanced his accuracy in shooting and throwing objects. With training, he could achieve a 100% hit rate on fixed targets, and precisely control the force of his movements.

Although Precision was only a quality ability, below the excellent tier, combined with Eagle Eye and Microscopic Vision, the three visual abilities would create a formidable synergy.
