Jixinghuò was assigned to the third group in the draw.

Lǔ Yúnhǎi happened to be the referee in this group, and as he passed by, he greeted Jixinghuò with a smile.

"Coach Lǔ, do you know him?"

Fāng Yínpíng was also assigned to this group, and seeing the two of them talking, she curiously asked a question.

"Sister Fāng, we were university classmates." Lǔ Yúnhǎi immediately responded, and he spoke respectfully towards Fāng Yínpíng, explaining, "Xīnghuǒ just started learning archery three weeks ago, and it was me who signed him up for the course."

"Really?" Fāng Yínpíng was very surprised.

She couldn't help but take another look at Jixinghuò.

For a beginner to have reached such an archery level, it was clear that he had exceptional archery talent, far surpassing most archers. If she hadn't fused the Thousand Nimble Hands and Precision, and practiced archery for many years, she wouldn't dare say she could easily defeat Jixinghuò.

"Nice to meet you, Mr. Jī." Fāng Yínpíng smiled, "About the small misunderstanding this morning, I hope Mr. Jī won't take it to heart."

Jixinghuò hadn't really thought much of it. "Sister Fāng is overthinking, it's just a trivial matter."

Fāng Yínpíng nodded and went back to prepare for the competition.

After she left, Jixinghuò asked, "Who is this Sister Fāng? She seems quite famous."

"She is the daughter of one of the shareholders in our archery club," Lǔ Yúnhǎi replied in a low voice. "Sister Fāng used to come to the archery club to practice frequently, but she has been competing in tournaments outside lately, so you haven't seen her. Her archery level is very high, the club director has given her pointers many times, and she has won the Archery Cup championship twice in the past five competitions."

"No wonder." Jixinghuò suddenly understood.

"Sister Fāng is an open and friendly person, and she's easy to get along with," Lǔ Yúnhǎi said with a smile. "Many people in the archery circle in Changan like her."

"What does that have to do with me?" Jixinghuò felt a bit puzzled.

"Haha, I was just making a casual remark."

As they were talking, they had arrived at the venue for the fourth round of competition, and Lǔ Yúnhǎi returned to the referee team to preside over the competition.

This round required shooting at a 50-meter moving target, with only one person on the field at a time.

Jixinghuò looked around.

The field was very open, and the weather was good today. Although it was the scorching summer, it wasn't as hot up in Mount Qinling as in the city, and the competitors were all hiding in the shade of the trees, watching as the first contestant was called to the field.

They also had one minute of preparation time.

Beep!

The buzzer sounded, and a drone took off 50 meters away, flying at a speed not much faster than a person's running pace, with an 80-centimeter archery target suspended below it, following a fixed trajectory.

This was the moving target, and the shooting window was only two seconds long, with any shots after that time invalid.

Bang! An arrow hit the target, but not the bullseye.

The contestant on the field shot the first arrow, not stopping, and quickly nocked the second arrow. Five seconds later, the second drone target flew by, and he quickly aimed and shot.

Five drone targets flew by in succession, five seconds apart, and the speed kept increasing.

All five arrows were shot in less than half a minute.

"39 points!"

The score immediately displayed on the screen at the far end of the field, not a very high score. The contestant shook his head and stepped down.

The second contestant quickly took the field, and each person's time was very short.

Consecutive five-arrow shots, each one more difficult than the last, with the scores naturally decreasing, averaging between 35 and 40 points.

Soon, it was Jixinghuò's turn.

Bang, bang, bang, bang, bang! Five crisp, decisive shots, all hitting the bullseye.

"50 points!"

The spectators watching from under the trees exclaimed in surprise - this was the first perfect 50-point score in the fourth round. Many people inquired about Jixinghuò's background, but no one recognized him.

However, no one was particularly concerned, because Fāng Yínpíng took the field and also shot a perfect 50.

The fourth round ended.

In Jixinghuò's group, four people had achieved perfect scores.

Due to the tight schedule, the referees immediately led this group of contestants to the next field for the fifth round. The fifth round was also shooting at a moving target, but the distance had increased to 70 meters, and it was a downward angle shot from a higher position.

The field was located on a hillside, with the contestants gathered at the top of the slope and the targets halfway down the hill, occasionally with a light breeze blowing.

The shooting difficulty had increased again.

The contestants who went before had scores that just kept getting worse, with half of them scoring below 30 points after the five shots.

Jixinghuò's luck wasn't great.

When he took the field, there happened to be a strong gust of wind blowing across the slope, causing two of his arrows to hit the 9-ring, and he ended up with a score of 48 points.

In the sixth round, the target distance was increased to 100 meters, and it was an upward angle shot, with the field moved to the bottom of the slope and the targets on the hill above.

The contestants were mostly defeated, and those who managed to score above 30 points were few.

Jixinghuò's performance was also not ideal.

He shot five arrows, with only one hitting the 10-ring, giving him a score of 41 points. However, this score was still among the top, as the others had even lower scores.

After three rounds of competition, the contestants waited for the results to see who would advance.

The total scores from these three rounds would be calculated, and the top 32 would advance to the seventh round.

The results came out quickly.

The screens set up around the field displayed the rankings, and Jixinghuò easily found his name, in third place with a total score of 139 points.

"I'm actually only in third place?" Jixinghuò was a bit surprised. The shooting difficulty in these three rounds had already been very high, and yet there were still people who had scored 143 points, ranking first.

The second-place score was also 2 points higher than his.

That Fāng Yínpíng had also advanced, with a score of 138 points, tied for fifth place.

Jixinghuò looked up at the weather, knowing that outdoor shooting was easily affected by climate, especially wind, and even a slight mistake could cause a miss.

With these uncertain factors, no one could say they would definitely win the championship, it would all come down to the performance on the day.

The top 10 all had a chance.

At this time, a broadcast echoed through the valley: "Will the 32 advancing contestants please gather at Target Field 9, the seventh round will begin in 15 minutes."

Jixinghuò hurried over.

Not only the advancing contestants, but even those who had been eliminated had not left, becoming spectators wanting to see the final results.

Target Field 9 was set up on a 100-meter-high cliff, but the slope was gentle, and safety railings had been installed, so there was no real danger.

A flat area and spectator stands had been built along the cliff edge, enough to accommodate over a thousand people.

The remaining four rounds would all take place here.

When Jixinghuò arrived, most of the spectator stands were already filled, and Yangtianqing and a few other club directors were sitting and chatting.

"Please come up, everyone," the chief referee stood on a platform jutting out from the cliff, with shooting position lines drawn on the ground, and the 32 contestants stood a few meters back.

The referee quickly explained the rules.

In the open space in front of the cliff, numerous drones with archery targets were flying at different speeds and trajectories. When the contestants were in position, one of the drones would light up with a prominent red light for one second, and the contestant had to complete the shot within that one second.

Jixinghuò had seen the videos beforehand, but the actual experience was quite different from the videos.

With so many spectators, it was inevitable to feel some nervousness.

"Contestant 76, Jī Xīnghuò!"

In this round, the order of appearance was determined by the registration numbers, and Jixinghuò's number was 76.
