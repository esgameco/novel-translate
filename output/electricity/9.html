Today is Jixinghuos 22nd birthday. As Lu Yunhai said, this may be the most important birthday of his life.

Because on this day, Jixinghuos will meet his biological parents.

If they are still alive.

In the afternoon, after Jixinghuos finished his cultivation, when his physical strength and star power had recovered, he went out as usual to the Qishan Wutong Archery Hall to practice archery.

He had already registered for the "Shoot Leaf Cup" competition, which was in three days.

These days, following the training plan formulated by Lu Yunhai, Jixinghuos had been practicing shooting moving targets and random targets, and his progress was very fast, with his hit rate increasing significantly.

When the training was over, Lu Yunhai came over and said, "Happy birthday!"

"Thank you." Jixinghuos was packing up his bow case and asked, "Are you really not coming to the birthday party tonight?"

Lu Yunhai shook his head, "No, after all, it's your family gathering, I won't participate in your family gathering."

"Okay. I'll invite you separately tomorrow."

Jixinghuos didn't insist, packed up his things and left the archery hall.

Back home, he washed up and changed into new clothes, looking fresh and clean.

However, he did not leave immediately, but went to the wall, opened the glass window, and quietly looked at the prosperous city scenery outside, with a complex expression.

As the sky gradually darkened, Jixinghuos' figure was lengthened by the setting sun, casting a shadow on the wall behind him.

The skyscrapers outside were lit up with neon lights, and the maglev cars on the superconducting highways also turned on their lights, forming a dazzling and endless stream of light.

Jixinghuos came to his senses, glanced at the time, and then left.

It was 7 o'clock in the evening.

Jixinghuos arrived at the "Weiyang Building" in the center of Chang'an City, a skyscraper reaching a height of 1,800 meters, one of the tallest buildings in the capital, with an architectural design resembling a majestic palace reaching into the clouds, known as the "Palace in the Sky."

He took the elevator to the 228th floor, where there was a high-end restaurant called "Guxiang."

"Welcome back to Guxiang!"

Jixinghuos followed the slender female server, walking through the luxurious corridor, and the well-dressed and upper-class customers passed by.

He rarely came to such an upscale place, but his mind was not on that at the moment.

Entering the private room.

One side of the private room was a full-length transparent glass wall, overlooking most of Chang'an, taking in the prosperity and hustle and bustle of the capital of the Allied Sphere.

Standing here, it was as if standing above the clouds, overlooking the masses.

However, Jixinghuos did not look around much, sat down and ordered a table of dishes, then quietly waited.

After a moment.

"Xinghuos." A tall young man pushed the door open and came in.

He had a handsome appearance, with a sunny smile on his face, and an aura of vigor and confidence as he walked, and a warmth that was different from ordinary people could be felt when he approached, making people feel very comfortable.

"Second Brother."

Jixinghuos stood up to greet him.

The two hugged, and Zhongming patted him firmly on the back, then let go and handed him a wrapped box, "This is your birthday gift, the latest 'Zhuodian' power shoes, you can use them for transportation, I think you'll like them."

"Thank you, Second Brother." Jixinghuos took it without hesitation.

Zhongming's income was very high, and a pair of power shoes worth tens of thousands of yuan was just a few days' salary for him.

He was the most accomplished among the five siblings, becoming an Anomaly at the age of nineteen, with the professional template of "Blazing Fury."

Last year, Zhongming was promoted to an intermediate Anomaly at the age of twenty-two, and after graduation, he signed a contract with a club and is now playing in the minor league.

The team believes he has the potential to enter the super league in the future and has already gained some fame.

"Hm?"

Zhongming was about to sit down when his movement paused for a moment, carefully examining Jixinghuos' eyes and asked, "Xinghuos, have you become an Anomaly?"

Before Jixinghuos could answer, a cry came from outside the door.

"Third Brother! Have you become an Anomaly too?"

A young man and woman quickly stepped in, both in their early twenties, and the one speaking was a beautiful young woman.

She was wearing a tight-fitting crop top that exposed her midriff, with a colorful pleated skirt underneath, paired with thigh-high stockings, her face lightly made up, and her dyed sea-blue hair styled neatly, exuding a youthful and vibrant aura.

In comparison, the young man beside her looked very ordinary, thin in build, unkempt, with his hands in his pockets, in a lazy posture, intentionally keeping his distance from her.

In fact, he was keeping his distance from the hovering camera next to the girl.

"Feifei, turn off the live stream, will you?" Zhongming reminded.

"Second Brother, I'm not live streaming, I'm just filming my own life, and if I become famous in the future, it'll be good material for a documentary!" Liang Qiu Feifei walked in with a bright smile, maneuvering the camera to fly around the room, pointing the lens at Zhongming and Jixinghuos.

Zhongming glanced at it and confirmed it was just filming, shaking his head helplessly.

"Third Brother, you don't mind, do you?" Liang Qiu Feifei asked again.

"No problem."

Jixinghuos was indifferent to this, already used to it.

When Liang Qiu Feifei turned 18 and came of age, she had set her sights on becoming a globally famous live streamer, singing, dancing, gaming, performing, she could do it all, but not excelling at any. Relying on her relatively outstanding appearance, she has been live streaming for three years and is now a small streamer with a few hundred regular viewers.

Except for sleeping and using the bathroom, she is almost always live streaming.

There are over a million people like her who live stream their personal lives across the country, and you can encounter seven or eight of them just by walking a few hundred meters on the streets of Chang'an.

Jixinghuos never wanted to appear on camera.

In his "Personal Network Privacy and Security" settings, he had enabled the "Portrait Protection" mode.

This way, any images of himself that appear in live streams, videos, or photos publicly posted online, no matter who took them, unless he authorizes it, his face will be automatically processed and replaced with a random generic face.

Of course, this is only for public portrait protection. If it involves legal issues or cases, government agencies have the right to access the data and obtain the real information.
