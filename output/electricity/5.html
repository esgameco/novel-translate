Although Jixinghuо could confirm the fusion success himself, seeing the words "Nascent Anomalous" on the interface still filled his heart with uncontrollable joy.

The 23rd century was the era of the Anomalous.

The chasm between ordinary people and the Anomalous was immense, and the disparity in individual power profoundly impacted human society in every aspect.

From culture, economy, and politics, to science, sports, and entertainment, the advantages of the Anomalous were ubiquitous, almost dominating all human activities.

Even if countries tried to downplay the differences between the Anomalous and ordinary people, emphasizing equality before the law, and even restricting the rights of the Anomalous in certain places to tilt towards ordinary people, in reality, ordinary people were always the underdogs.

The Anomalous not only possessed supernatural abilities, but were also more powerful, more intelligent, and even had longer lifespans. Ordinary people naturally lacked the confidence to face the Anomalous.

In fact, those Anomalous who had advanced far along the path of evolution were already a different species from ordinary people.

Just as the feudal era had the saying "All else is low, only scholarship is high," now it was "All else is low, only the Anomalous are high." While somewhat exaggerated, this statement was not far from the truth.

Regardless of which country they were citizens of, or what political system they lived under, the first life goal that all people set for themselves from a young age was to become an Anomalous! Jixinghuо was certainly no exception.

Now, he had finally achieved this, joining the ranks of the Anomalous!

"Doctor!"

Suddenly, someone in the observation room called out. A young man with a pale face shouted loudly, "My head hurts, what's going on?" Blood was flowing from his nostrils, and his expression was both agonized and panicked as he cried out, "Didn't my fusion fail?"

A doctor, accompanied by two nurses, immediately rushed over. After a brief examination, he shook his head, "Take him to the medical room immediately, his conflict is quite severe, and he may need emergency treatment."

"Did my fusion fail?" the young man heard the implication in the doctor's words.

"Yes," the doctor comforted him. "There's no danger to your life, it will take at most a year to recover, and you can try fusing with another species next time."

The nurses helped the young man up, and he looked dejected and mournful as he said, "I fused with the 'Beast Whisperer,' and next time I'll have to buy the species myself, over 2 million Aeonian credits. How many years will it take me to save up that much money?"

Jixinghuо and the remaining people in the observation room watched him being taken to the medical room, all with sympathetic expressions.

For citizens of the Asia-Pacific Community, everyone only had one free opportunity to obtain an Anomalous species.

If the fusion failed, they would have to pay for the species themselves the next time they wanted to try.

And almost all the core abilities of professional templates were at least Exceptional Abilities, worth no less than 1 million Aeonian credits. Without family support, young people simply couldn't afford it, even with low-interest government loans, it was still a heavy burden.

A single fusion failure was a huge blow for the vast majority of ordinary people, a double loss of both money and time, causing many to give up on becoming Anomalous.

Jixinghuо felt relieved.

He continued to look at his interface, and in addition to his [Rank] changing to Nascent Anomalous, his [Ability] was no longer "None," but displayed "Thousand Nimble Hands," with the additional information: Level 1/47.

"Level 1."

Jixinghuо stroked his chin, this was easy to understand - Anomalous Abilities could be upgraded through cultivation, and his just-fused Thousand Nimble Hands was at Level 1.

But what did the "1/47" mean? "Ability upgrade progress?"

Jixinghuо couldn't help but speculate. Was it like in virtual games, where as proficiency increased, reaching 47/47 would allow Thousand Nimble Hands to advance to Level 2? But in games, there were skill points used for upgrading, so what would he use here?

His gaze fell on [Spiritual Source Energy], which was still just a duck egg, unchanged.

He had been pondering this term "Spiritual Source Energy" for a long time last night. Jixinghuо had never heard of it before, and searches online didn't yield any relevant answers. The results related to the Anomalous were either about spiritual powers, mental powers, or something similar, but nothing called Spiritual Source Energy.

However, Jixinghuо didn't dwell on it for too long.

Even if he couldn't directly increase the level, just having a progress display was already extremely helpful.

There were corresponding methods for Ability upgrades, with various countries having conducted extensive research on different Abilities, and there were dozens of cultivation methods for Thousand Nimble Hands that could be downloaded from the Stellar Bureau's official website.

But Ability cultivation was long, tedious, and difficult, often taking years of practice without any level-up, truly a painful process.

So many Anomalous couldn't persist, and their Abilities remained at very low levels, unable to fully unleash their potential and power.

But now that he knew the upgrade progress, he had a clear goal.

Each Ability cultivation would provide positive feedback, and he could quickly correct any ineffective or incorrect methods, knowing that he would definitely level up. He could even calculate the exact date of the upgrade and make a cultivation plan - his Ability would definitely level up much faster than other Anomalous! Jixinghuо was full of confidence about this.

Next to the information on the interface, his virtual body avatar had also changed.

The most obvious was a network of fine lines appearing within his arms, running from his neck, along the shoulders and arms, all the way to the palms and fingertips, like a dense neural network.

When he focused on the virtual arms, the neural network would light up, becoming very prominent.

At the same time, it was labeled: Thousand Nimble Hands, Level 1.

Jixinghuо was a bit puzzled, as the Ability information was already displayed next to it, so why was it repeated on the virtual body? Was there another purpose?

After carefully observing for a moment, he soon made a discovery.

The lines where Thousand Nimble Hands was located were emitting faint points of light, like wandering energy, gradually spreading throughout the entire virtual body. Over time, the points of light covered every corner of the body, although still very weak, they could no longer be ignored.

"Stellar Force!"

Jixinghuо suddenly realized, these points of light represented Stellar Force.

At the same time, a new line of text had appeared on the left side of the interface, below [Rank].

[Stellar Force: 1]

"It's indeed Stellar Force!" Jixinghuо was very happy that he had guessed correctly.

All Anomalous had to cultivate Stellar Force, its importance and priority were even higher than Abilities, because Stellar Force was the primal driving force of life evolution!

Stellar Force was derived from ether energy, with the human body absorbing ether and converting it into Stellar Force, strengthening the body's strength, speed, and endurance, and promoting life evolution, extending lifespan.

It could be said that Stellar Force opened the door to cultivation, and only by possessing Stellar Force could one truly be on the path of evolution!

An Anomalous's Ability could be mediocre or even useless, but their Stellar Force could never be weak, because Stellar Force was the source of an Anomalous's power.

Moreover, the use of Abilities also consumed Stellar Force.

Using a virtual game analogy, Abilities were the skills, while Stellar Force was the character attributes, serving as both life force and mana.

Obtaining Stellar Force was not difficult, at least for Anomalous it was very simple.

As long as one successfully fused with any Anomalous species, they would gain Stellar Force, as Anomalous species were born from the Stellar Realm, and must contain ether energy, which would be converted into Stellar Force upon successful fusion.

Cultivating Stellar Force was a mandatory course for Anomalous.

There was no special technique or shortcut, the only way was to constantly exercise the body, consuming the Stellar Force within, and then waiting for it to recover.

Through repeated consumption and recovery, the Stellar Force would slowly increase.

From the Nascent Anomalous stage to the Advanced Anomalous stage, Stellar Force existed in the body in an undetectable energy form, relatively weak.

Outwardly, Stellar Force could not be seen, only the Anomalous themselves could sense it.

Yet Stellar Force was a real existence, and when it accumulated to a certain degree, it would allow the Anomalous to evolve and advance, enabling them to fuse with another Ability.

With Earth's current technology, it was impossible to detect the existence of Stellar Force, let alone measure its precise values.

But the Myriad Phenomena Astral Eyes could display it directly.

However, the [Stellar Force] on the interface was just a numerical value, without any unit, so Jixinghuо could not compare it to other Anomalous' Stellar Force, he could only treat it as a reference for now.

A few minutes later, the Stellar Force value changed to 2.

Jixinghuо also quickly felt the effects of the Stellar Force.
