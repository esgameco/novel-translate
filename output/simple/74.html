It made his face twitch.

You don't even know why we came, yet you attacked us so viciously?

Are you insane?

Master Liu opened his mouth wide, his throat moving, but he couldn't utter a word.

Seeing this, Zhou You frowned and checked Master Liu's injuries, realizing they were life-threatening - he had reached the end stage.

Zhou You's current pill refinement skills could only produce first-grade superior pills, none that could heal Master Liu.
