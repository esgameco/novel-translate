So Zhou You took out Elder Brother Fang's jade identity token and dropped it on his body. He then collected all the magical weapons, especially the golden talisman in Elder Brother Fang's hand - this item must be extremely valuable, otherwise Elder Brother Fang would not have used it at the most critical moment.
