"It seems Junior Brother Zhou is not too welcoming to us."

Mu Jihan looked around vigilantly, but spoke in a very casual tone.

At the same time, he had already taken out a talisman, holding it between his fingers. He murmured a few incantations, then gave a cold snort and flung out the talisman: "Break!"

Obviously, he had just been stalling for time in order to cast this talisman to break the illusion.

This talisman for breaking illusions was of the second-grade premium level, quite precious, able to dispel most illusion formations.

He reckoned that Zhou You's illusion formation couldn't be that powerful, so as long as he broke the formation, Zhou You would be at their mercy.

The talisman for breaking illusions spontaneously combusted, then erupted with a burst of spiritual power that spread out in all directions.

As that spiritual power spread outwards, the surrounding environment also gradually changed. The wooden hut that had been destroyed by "Meng Xun" gradually reappeared, and everything returned to normal.

They could even see the candlelight inside the wooden hut.

It seemed that Mu Jihan had truly broken through the illusion formation.

"Junior Brother Zhou, if you don't come out, I'll have to go in."

Mu Jihan looked at the wooden hut and said indifferently.

He did not act rashly, but remained standing outside.

Although he had broken through the illusion formation, the minor Five Phases Formation was still surrounding them.

This minor Five Phases Formation, though a Qi Refining stage formation, was quite profound and could harm cultivators at the early Foundation Establishment stage.

Therefore, even Mu Jihan had to be careful.

However...

There was no movement from inside the wooden hut, and Zhou You still did not respond to them.

The injured Senior Brother Liu, who had temporarily suppressed his wounds, gave a cold snort and said icily, "The illusion is gone, yet you still want to play tricks? Courting death!"

After speaking, Senior Brother Liu waved his hand towards the wooden hut.

His powerful spiritual energy surged into the ground, and the earth near the hut rumbled loudly.

Then, a huge muddy hand burst forth from the ground and slammed down hard on the wooden hut.

"Boom--"

The wooden hut had no resistance against the muddy hand and immediately collapsed into ruins!

Next, the muddy hand scooped up the debris and swiftly brought it before Senior Brother Liu.

The muddy hand opened, scattering the remnants of the hut all over, but there was no sign of Zhou You!

"He's not there?"

Senior Brother Liu frowned, immediately getting a bad feeling.

As soon as this feeling arose, Mu Jihan beside him suddenly changed expression and waved his hand towards Senior Brother Liu.

A flying sword shot out, instantly piercing through Senior Brother Liu's chest.

Senior Brother Liu's eyes widened in disbelief as he stared at "Mu Jihan", utterly dazed.

"It's still an illusion!"

Senior Brother Liu quickly realized what was happening. He stared fixedly at Mu Jihan, a chill running through his heart.

This illusion realm was too terrifying!

He suddenly recalled that after retreating frantically, Mu Jihan had disappeared, only reappearing later to mention the "illusion formation combined with the minor Five Phases Formation."

But the minor Five Phases Formation was an ancient formation recorded in the Tao Te Ching of the Taibai Book Institute - an ordinary person could never come into contact with it, let alone know about the minor Five Phases Formation.

Although Mu Jihan was the young master of Linan Prefecture, he should not have heard of the minor Five Phases Formation either.

Earlier, Senior Brother Liu had recognized it as the minor Five Phases Formation, but it was just an unconscious reaction, and he had not said it out loud.

So how could Mu Jihan possibly know that this formation was the minor Five Phases Formation?

It was clear that Mu Jihan was also an illusion - an illusion from Senior Brother Liu's own mind!

Senior Brother Liu's scalp went numb as he thought of another ancient formation from the Tao Te Ching!

The minor Mind-Startling Formation!

Just who was this Zhou You? Was he really just a servant of the Xiao family?

How could he possibly possess both the minor Five Phases Formation and the minor Mind-Startling Formation at the same time!

He dared not think further. Holding his chest wound, he could feel his right lung had been pierced. Combined with his previous injuries, if he remained here any longer, he might really perish.
