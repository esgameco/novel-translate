"I don't know if this Light and Shadow Sword technique can harm cultivators at the Qi Refining 2nd Level?"

Zhou You muttered to himself as he gently stroked the iron sword.

He had some understanding of the strength of the Qi Refining 1st Level, after all he himself was also at the 1st Level.

But as for the Qi Refining 2nd Level, Zhou You was not familiar with it. Combined with the fact that the Light and Shadow Sword technique itself was just a mortal sword manual, whether it could pose a threat to 2nd Level Qi Refining cultivators was an unknown.

Based on Zhou You's guess, it should pose a threat.

Although the Light and Shadow Sword technique was a mortal sword manual, and its usage requirements were extremely stringent, needing extremely strong reflexes and spiritual power to capture the changes in light and shadows, borrowing the instantaneous changes to unleash the moves, achieving the effects of using the false to disrupt the true and striking by surprise.

But its might was far greater and more practical than ordinary sword techniques.

Success or failure hung on an instant.

If used well, perhaps it could truly threaten 2nd Level Qi Refining cultivators!

......

In the blink of an eye, another three days passed.

This was already the 50th day of Zhou You's cultivation.

On this morning, he finished his morning cultivation as usual.

He was just about to get up and eat something, then start his day's busy work.

However, the next moment, the golden seal in his consciousness flickered incessantly.

On Zhou You's right arm, a golden meridian channel appeared, and a large amount of spiritual qi surged into the meridians of his right arm.

Gradually, that golden meridian channel condensed into form, another meridian channel was formed!

"Qi Refining 2nd Level!"

Zhou You's eyes flashed with some excitement.

He inwardly observed the line of words on the golden seal.

[Opening the Meridians Scripture - First Part (50/100): A basic cultivation technique, exhaling and inhaling daily can increase 1 progress, completing it in 100 days!]

20 progress for Qi Refining 1st Level.

50 progress for Qi Refining 2nd Level!

Then 100 progress should be Qi Refining 3rd Level.

It turned out to be about the same as Zhou You's guess.

The Opening the Meridians Scripture he obtained was only the first part, at most allowing him to cultivate to the Qi Refining 3rd Level. So cultivating to the perfect Realized realm should be the Qi Refining 3rd Level.

Now it seemed his guess was not wrong.

Zhou You could feel the spiritual power roaming within his body was at least three times stronger than before!

For each level of the Qi Refining realm, the increase was a qualitative leap.

Now that Zhou You's cultivation had reached the Qi Refining 2nd Level, he had gained another means of self-defense, and was one step closer to the Foundation Establishment realm.

Zhou You also knew that at this speed, he was considered extremely fast for the entire Qi Refining period.

Take Huang Li for example, it took him three years just to reach the Qi Refining 1st Level.

"Once I reach Foundation Establishment, I can remove the soul imprint left on me by the Xiao Family. After that, I'll be free as a bird soaring in the sky."

Zhou You smiled, feeling the spiritual power in his meridian channels, his mood quite good.

Servant disciples of cultivator clans all had soul imprints left by the main family within their bodies.

These soul imprints would not harm the servant disciples, only serving as a mark. One only needed to scan it with their spiritual sense to know which clan's servant they were.

Similar to the brands burned onto the faces of mortal slaves.

However, once their cultivation reached the Foundation Establishment realm, the soul imprint would automatically dissipate, and the servant disciple would gain freedom.

As long as Zhou You cultivated steadily, reaching Foundation Establishment was only a matter of time.

......

The newly planted vegetable seeds had already sprouted. Since it had only been three days since planting, he couldn't yet see which seedlings were growing faster. He could only wait a while longer.
