Chapter 4

Here is a full translation of the excerpt from Chinese to English:

No matter what, with the assistance of the golden seal [Great Tao of Simplicity], Zhou You was also qualified to embark on the path of cultivation!

In this world, cultivation was revered above all else.

As long as one embarked on the path of cultivation, it meant that anything was possible.

Although he was currently still affiliated with the Xiao family and held the status of a servant, he at least had the possibility of changing his fate.

In the past, a mere servant would never have had any chance of leaving the Xiao family.

The Xiao family was a great cultivating clan, lacking neither wealth nor money. Even if Zhou You saved up countless silver taels, he would never be able to buy his freedom.

He would have remained the Xiao family's servant for life.

This mission to fish for spiritual fish carried a certain degree of danger, otherwise Zhao Bin would not have disappeared without a trace. However, if Zhou You did not seize this opportunity, he might never be able to change his destiny!

Fortunately, Zhou You grasped it, and performed admirably himself.

If he did not have spiritual roots, even if Xiao Qing helped him, he would not have been able to obtain this mission, and thus acquire the [Scripture of Meridian Opening]!

With the path of cultivation open to him, as long as his cultivation level rose, he would definitely be able to shed his servitude.

Thinking of this, Zhou You left the thatched hut and looked up at the full moon in the sky.

He quickly estimated the approximate time from the position of the moon.

The golden seal [Great Tao of Simplicity] had a limited rate of progress each day.

For example, with the [Scripture of Meridian Opening], cultivating for two hours each day would grant 1 point of progress.

After obtaining the progress, one could continue cultivating, but it seemed the speed would revert to that of an inferior spiritual root, becoming excruciatingly slow.

Zhou You had already obtained today's progress, and thinking he had no other matters, he decided to continue cultivating.

Just as he had guessed, the cultivation speed was excruciatingly slow, perhaps this was the true speed of cultivating with an inferior spiritual root.

After half an hour, it should have become the next day, and the cultivation speed increased again, visibly faster.

"It should have returned to the initial cultivation speed."

Zhou You murmured to himself and continued the breathing exercises according to the [Scripture of Meridian Opening].

In the blink of an eye, another two hours passed.

Another line of text appeared in the golden seal in his spiritual ocean.

[Scripture of Meridian Opening: Introductory Volume (2/100): A foundational cultivation technique, each day of breathing exercises can increase progress by 1 point, complete in 100 days!]

The progress increased by another point, just as Zhou You had guessed.

However, Zhou You did not give up, and continued cultivating.

Before he knew it, another two hours had passed.

The golden seal remained motionless, but his spiritual power had increased slightly.

Zhou You gave up directly, no longer wasting time.

"Indeed, after increasing the progress each day, continuing to cultivate is excruciatingly slow, with a poor cost-effectiveness ratio. It's better to do other things and develop the abilities of the golden seal."

Zhou You laughed and couldn't help but say, "I wonder when I'll be able to reach the first level of Qi Condensation?"

He felt a slight sense of anticipation.

...

In the blink of an eye, the sky began to brighten with the purple qi of dawn.

The rising sun shone upon Erhai Lake, its surface rippling with dazzling waves, like an exquisite painting.

Zhou You woke up very early, perhaps due to his cultivation, he did not feel tired despite missing two hours of sleep last night, but rather full of energy.

After eating a simple meal of dry rations, Zhou You took a hoe and went to the vegetable patch.

The vegetables had completely withered and died, no longer usable, so he had to till the soil again and plant new seeds.

The earlier the seeds were planted, the earlier the harvest would come.

He could not rely solely on dry rations to sustain himself every day.

The round trip to the nearest town took nearly a full day.

Naturally, being self-sufficient was ideal.

Fortunately, Zhou You had spent years here after being transported to this world, serving as a servant of the Xiao family, so he knew how to till the land.

If he had just been transported here as a modern shut-in who had never gotten his hands dirty, he might not have known how to till the fields.

He could have starved himself to death.

After the entire morning, several plots of land had been tilled and planted with seeds, then watered with water from Erhai Lake. Only then did he feel at ease.

Erhai...

The sea was not a real sea, but only a lake, with fresh water inside.

[Farming (33/100: A very simple subject, can be mastered in three days!]

Another line of words appeared on the golden seal again.

Zhou You glanced at it without paying much attention.

So in the following three days, except for two hours of cultivation in the morning, Zhou You spent the rest of his time tinkering with the vegetable field.

After finishing today's work, another line of words appeared on the golden seal.

[Farming (100/100): A very simple subject, already mastered!]

Seeing this line, Zhou You was not surprised either, and prepared to go back with the hoe.

Next, he should be busy with fishing for spiritual fish.

But after taking just two steps, Zhou You found something was amiss.

"Eh, it seems my strength has increased a lot?"

Zhou You said in astonishment.

Thinking this, he hurried back to the house, took two wooden barrels, drew two barrels of water from Erhai Lake, and quickly carried them back to the vegetable field.

Indeed, he found that his strength seemed to have increased a lot, and even the muscles all over his body had become sturdier.

The strength of a normal person is probably around 100 jin, but Zhou You felt he should be able to have 150 jin!

What was going on?

He could confirm that his strength had only increased just now.

It was after [Farming] was mastered that it increased!

"Could it be that these seemingly ordinary abilities have some kind of bonus after being mastered?"

Zhou You said in astonishment.

He put down the wooden barrels and carefully recalled what changes he had experienced after mastering [Sweeping].

"Spirit! Yes, it was in the spiritual aspect! After mastering [Sweeping], my attention became more focused than before!"

Zhou You's eyes lit up, and he said excitedly, "The spiritual power aspect is quite abstract, so it's not noticeable in daily life, which is why I didn't notice it! Now it seems that this 'The Way of Simplicity' golden seal also has enhancements in various aspects!"

If this were true, then it would be another channel for gaining strength.

As long as he strived to learn those seemingly ordinary abilities, he could gain certain enhancements!

This made Zhou You look forward to it!

Those skills in life were not limited to just sweeping and farming.

There were also chopping firewood, fetching water, tailoring, cooking, and so on, fishing included.

It seemed he had to try more.

...

The next day.

This was already the fifth day since Zhou You came to Erhai Lake.

The progress of the 'Opening the Meridians Scripture: First Volume' in the 'The Way of Simplicity' golden seal had reached 5.

He finally began to prepare for fishing.

Previously, when he was cultivating in the small courtyard of Xiao Yueying, he had heard Zhao Bin say that fishing for spiritual fish was not something just anyone could do.

Not only did it require spiritual power, but also some skills.

If one didn't have the skills, even a cultivator with extremely powerful spiritual power would find it difficult to catch spiritual fish.

In his previous life, Zhou You was also a semi-professional angler who rarely missed an opportunity.

However, he didn't know whether the skills from his previous life would be useful for spiritual fish, so he could only try first.

Zhou You brought his equipment to the shore of Erhai Lake.

Since Erhai Lake was vast, with a calm surface and flat terrain along the shore, there were basically no prime fishing spots, and it was more or less the same everywhere, so Zhou You randomly chose a spot and sat down.

After all the preparations were done, Zhou You took out the bait and started chumming, then calmed his mind and began angling.

Since he didn't understand the preferences of spiritual fish, for now he could only explore.

See whether they preferred deep or shallow water.

Time passed slowly, and after half an hour, there was still no movement on the lake surface.

Zhou You quickly adjusted his strategy, controlling the depth of the bait.

(End of this chapter)
