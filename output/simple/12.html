Naturally, he wouldn't spread the news of Huang Li's plan to escape.

This matter wasn't that simple; the existence of the Righteous Sect seemed too abrupt.

In this world, were there really such good things?

Zhou You shook his head, feeling that only things he struggled for himself would be dependable. Receiving undue favors from others wasn't a good thing.

Even Xiao Yueying's abnormal kindness made Zhou You feel unsettled.

Although Huang Li said there wouldn't be any danger, joining the Righteous Sect would inevitably lead to contact with other cultivators, which would naturally add unforeseen dangers.

Before his strength was strong enough, Zhou You didn't want to put himself in a dangerous situation.

For the sake of caution, Zhou You rejected Huang Li's invitation.

An extremely comfortable feeling welled up in his heart.

Zhou You couldn't help but let out a cry, then a large amount of black impurities gushed out from the pores all over his body.

There were far more impurities than when he broke through levels of cultivation!

As the impurities surged out, Zhou You clearly felt his overall physical strength greatly increase!

His four limbs and hundreds of bones were filled with power!

"This is..."

Zhou You's eyes flickered, utterly astonished: "The first level of Body Tempering?"

The Body Tempering realm is actually the Qi Refining stage.

It is a type of physical cultivation realm, also with nine levels, and the first level of Body Tempering corresponds to the first level of Qi Refining.

Physical cultivation is another way of practicing in the cultivation world, not cultivating spiritual power, but focusing on tempering the body. The difficulty is many times greater than spiritual cultivation!

Not only does it take a huge amount of time, but it also requires a large quantity of medicinal materials to temper the body.

Usually it is the clansmen of major cultivation families who have no spiritual roots and cannot practice spiritual cultivation who are forced to take the path of physical cultivation out of necessity.

For ordinary people to step onto the path of physical cultivation is impossible.

Zhou You heard Huang Li mention that the realms of physical cultivation are different from spiritual cultivation only at the first stage, after which they are the same.

He never expected that with the extremely difficult physical cultivation, he would successfully enter the first level of Body Tempering just from perfecting the practice of the Light and Shadow Sword Technique!

In other words, now Zhou You could smash cultivators at the first level of Qi Refining with just his physical strength alone!

At the same level, if a physical cultivator can get in close, they can often crush spiritual cultivators!

"It seems that cultivating martial arts manuals can increase my physical strength, and this is also a path of cultivation!"

Zhou You thought to himself.

Martial arts manuals are simplified versions of physical cultivation methods, also evolved from physical cultivation methods.

For ordinary people to cultivate these mortal martial arts, it's difficult to make any progress even after decades, but Zhou You has the golden seal, allowing him to gain such enhancements.

If he can obtain physical cultivation methods in the future, the enhancements will likely be even more terrifying.

Of course, that's a topic for later. With Zhou You's current financial resources, it's unrealistic to obtain physical cultivation methods.
