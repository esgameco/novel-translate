Here is a full translation of the Chinese excerpt to English:

When Zhou You saw Xiao Qing riding the white jade dragon horse and coming towards him, he was dumbfounded.

He had just gone to hand in the spirit fish three days ago and had seen Xiao Qing then. Why was Xiao Qing looking for him again now?

Moreover, she was riding the white jade dragon horse.

The white jade dragon horse was the spiritual beast of the young lady Xiao Yueyingying, with some demonic beast bloodline, capable of traveling five thousand li in a day.

From Xiao Yueyingying's cultivation courtyard to here, if riding the white jade dragon horse, it would take less than the time to burn an incense stick to arrive.

Zhou You thought about it briefly, there must be some urgent matter, so Xiao Qing was sent to notify him.

"Xiao Qing, why have you come?"

Zhou You put down his fishing rod and said with some surprise.

When Xiao Qing saw Zhou You, she visibly relaxed, then jumped off the white jade dragon horse, looking around in amazement, and couldn't help but say: "Brother You, I didn't expect the scenery of Erhai Lake to be so beautiful, you're really living a carefree life here!"

This place had been put in order by Zhou You, with a vegetable garden, a kitchen, and his own little world.

It made Xiao Qing envious.

Zhou You smiled, and because of Xiao Qing, he didn't feel any estrangement from her despite his cultivation of immortality.

If it weren't for Xiao Qing, he would have had no chance to step onto the path of immortal cultivation.

Zhou You said, "If you like it, you can request to reside here for a period of time when the young lady is in seclusion."

Of course, this was just a polite offer.

Xiao Qing waved her hands repeatedly, saying, "No need, I'm afraid if I get used to it, I won't want to leave, hehe."

Next, Xiao Qing finally revealed the reason for her visit.

She looked at Zhou You and said, "Brother You, seeing that you're still here, I'm relieved."

Zhou You frowned and said, "What do you mean by that? I just went back three days ago, why would you say such a thing?"

Xiao Qing's pretty face became somewhat solemn as she said, "Brother You, actually the young lady asked me to come check on you, and told you to be very careful here, and never believe anyone's words."

Hearing this, Zhou You immediately felt a sense of foreboding.

Sure enough, Xiao Qing's next words made Zhou You's sense of crisis rise even more.

"Zhao Bin's corpse has been found."

Xiao Qing said.

"Corpse?"

Zhou You knitted his brows tightly, not understanding, "I heard that he..."

"Ran away, right?"

Before Zhou You could finish, Xiao Qing interrupted him.

Zhou You nodded.

Xiao Qing said, "We thought he had just run away at first, but later we found out that he didn't run away, but was deceived into joining the so-called Righteous Alliance. After that, no one saw him again, until yesterday, his remains were found near a mine five hundred li outside the city."

Almost instantly, Zhou You thought of Huang Li.

A month ago, the last time Zhou You saw Huang Li, Huang Li had repeatedly tried to persuade him to join the Righteous Alliance, promising all sorts of benefits and a monthly allocation of spirit stones.

Now it seemed that this Righteous Alliance was likely problematic!

If he had truly followed Huang Li, his fate might have been the same as Zhao Bin's.

Zhou You said, "This Righteous Alliance, isn't it just an organization for wandering cultivators? How could it..."

Xiao Qing let out a cold laugh, "What kind of wandering cultivator organization, I heard from the young lady that this Righteous Alliance is just a cult organized by a few cultivators. They wave the banner of helping wandering cultivators to lure in those with shallow cultivation bases. Then they either force them to mine or sell them to demonic cultivators!"

"Especially people like you fish servants, you're their key development targets. They often exploit your resentment to attract you over, then control and evaluate you. If you can mine, they keep you, if not, they sell you to demonic cultivators."

"Recently, quite a few fish servants of clan disciples have gone missing without a trace. The young lady was worried something might happen to you, so she asked me to come check on you."

After hearing Xiao Qing's words, Zhou You felt a surge of fear at the narrow escape.
