Chapter 647

A faintly mocking smile appeared on the blurred face as if it was pulsating.

Then, his body instantly transformed from a mixture of black and red to all black!

It was as if ink dripped into a pool, and the surrounding space was dyed black in an instant!

Li Fan's mind was greatly shaken.

Even the surviving cultivators who had greedy desires while watching Chi Yan, now had their hearts enveloped by fear.

Because the spiritual essence of the world they sensed from Chi Yan had quietly transformed into the spiritual essence of the world: [Ink Kill]!

"What's going on?!"

Not only were they panicked, but even Li Fan was also stunned.

"Spiritual essences of the world can be exchanged with each other? Ink Kill can instantly replace Chi Yan and descend into the world without his death?"

Li Fan's heart raced, his thoughts spinning. The scenes he witnessed today seemed to have opened a new door for him.

The first to bear the brunt of this sudden change was Hongxi, who sacrificed himself in a desperate strike.

He transformed from being resolute and determined to terrified and hopeless.

But once the arrow is released from the bow, he could no longer restrain himself and collided into the pitch-black domain of Ink Kill.

There was no earth-shattering collision, no dazzling colors.

It was like snow melting into water, his whole body seemingly dissolved in an instant and disappeared silently.

Ink Kill's head tilted slightly, a strange smile appearing on his lips.

The sudden change caused a vision to emerge above his head.

"God-transforming cultivator Hongxi, cultivated for seven hundred and sixty-three years, achieved the foundation of Dao with the [Five Elements Mountain], and formed the Golden Core with the five methods of metal, wood, water, fire, and earth."

"Swallowed [Bai Ling Dong Tian] to form the Nascent Soul, extracted [Tai Hua Jiang Sheng Qian Shan] to transform his spirit."

"Fortunately, heaven chastised, Dao disappeared and returned to the heavens!"

In the sky, the collapse of the continuous mountain range symbolized the fall of the exalted god-transforming cultivator, Xianjun Hongxi, from the Cloud Sea region.

At the moment Ink Kill appeared, Tianyang already sensed the danger.

Though he vowed not to be weaker than others in his lifetime, it did not mean Tianyang would just sacrifice himself.

The Earth Fire Returning to Origin Formation no longer had any room for maneuvering. At the cost of damaging the formation, it completely exploded.

As the earth fire lava raged, Tianyang tried to escape by breaking his hand.

While the giant hand still trapped Ink Kill, Tianyang had already quietly fled.

But no matter how fast his movements were, he was still not as fast as Ink Kill.

Before the fiery red magma could reach Ink Kill, it was contaminated and transformed into tiny black particles.

As if a drop of ink fell from the sky and swiftly spread.

Before Tianyang could escape far enough, he was caught up by the expanding circular black ink area.

Unlike Hongxi's silent dissolution, there were constant sounds of flames and crackling coming from the pitch-black area.

Even as the vast black area continued to expand towards the surroundings, signs of Tianyang's struggling could still be seen at the center.

But it seemed to only add to his agony.

Shortly after, all the commotion disappeared completely, and calm returned to the dark.

Li Fan's eyelids jumped incessantly.

He knew that the material he had used to reinforce Tianyang included the supposedly indestructible "Eternal Crisis Essence Metal."

Originally thinking he could hold his ground against Ink Kill for a few rounds, but the result...

"The gap is too big. Ink Kill..."

"Truly terrifying." Even after witnessing battles involving Heavenly Healers and Grandmasters, Li Fan still felt a chilling sensation in his heart seeing Ink Kill in action once again.

"Moreover, this Ink Kill in this life seems to be more 'vivid' than before."

Whether it was Li Fan's misconception or not, as he gazed at the expanding black spot in the sky, he suddenly had this inexplicable realization.

The remaining cultivators inside the Cloud City were not just slightly chilled like Li Fan.

The situation changed suddenly, and in an instant, the two god-transforming cultivators who seemed to have the victory in their hands had all fallen.

This plunged the remaining people into despair.

In the Cloud City, many cultivators who were in seclusion in the Tianxuan Mirror were suddenly thrown out.

Looking confused, they didn't understand the situation.

"Tianxuan Mirror has also withdrawn its empowerment. It seems to have abandoned the Cloud Sea."

"What should come will come."

After Tianyang fell, the spread of Ink Kill gradually slowed down.

Reverting to a humanoid form, Ink Kill's gaze swept over the people in the Cloud City.

All the cultivators he looked at were frozen, unable to move, and filled with extreme fear.

And then, perhaps in a playful mood like catching mice, Ink Kill didn't immediately attack these cultivators but disappeared in a flash.

The cultivators' faces were full of disbelief, thinking that Ink Kill had spared them.

A glimmer of hope ignited in their hearts.

But obviously, this was impossible.

Black rain began to fall from the sky.

As the black droplets descended, everything they touched vaporized.

Then more ink-colored substances emerged from nowhere and devoured everything around them.

Under the gaze of all, the Cloud Sea was fading away.

Ironically, all the black rain avoided the Cloud City in the sky.

It was like a punishment, making the cultivators in the city watch as the Cloud Sea was slowly erased.

Ink Kill orchestrated all these events.

"This Ink Kill has a somewhat sinister sense of humor."

Li Fan stared at the increasingly dark space around him, feeling the weight in his heart gradually intensify as he thought so.

Such a horrifying scene completely drove the cultivators inside the Cloud City into madness.

Knowing they had no chance of survival, some gave up on themselves, ending their lives.

Some, with red eyes, launched suicidal attacks towards the dark area outside the Cloud City.

And some just sat on the ground, crying uncontrollably.

He Zhenghao was one of them.

Trembling uncontrollably, the advice from his daughter kept echoing in his mind.

"Terrifying calamity, life-threatening worries."

...

With a bewildered and regretful expression on his face, He Zhenghao once again visualized the faces of his beloved wife, Heng Ruoshui, and his daughter He Xinxin.

"What a fool I am!"

The rapidly changing scenes of this life flashed through his mind, causing He Zhenghao to sob uncontrollably.

But suddenly, he stopped abruptly.

Suddenly, he turned and rushed towards Li Fan.

"Master Assistant! Master Assistant, you must think of a way!"

"You surely have a solution, right?"

Crawling and scrambling, he made his way to Li Fan, hugging onto the last shred of hope as he asked.

Li Fan did not reply, he just silently flew up into the sky.

With a provocative gesture towards the pitch-black region outside the Cloud City, the ink color of the world seemed to freeze suddenly.

Then, a figure appeared not far from Li Fan, watching him with great interest.
