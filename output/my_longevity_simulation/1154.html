Chapter 1152: Reshaping the Realm with One Hand

Although Shang Shaojun was the commander of the Qingxuan Army, he had never directly confronted a Changsheng realm existence before.

During the anxious waiting process, he felt restless and uneasy, as if a day felt like a year.

After an unknown amount of time, the constantly rotating sword light around them finally stopped.

Li Fan, who had been waiting with his eyes closed, finally opened them.

As the sword light dissipated, Shang Shaojun subconsciously looked around.

The scene he saw instantly made him feel like he had fallen into the depths of cold, his whole body freezing.

Just now, those few shattered worlds that Li Fan had stimulated and destroyed with the sword light had inexplicably reappeared!

Hanging upside down overhead, the eerie red threads intertwined with each other, becoming even thicker.

Densely packed, they continuously extended towards the Chita Abyss.

"Not only did they not disappear, they even..." Shang Shaojun's heart trembled as he looked at Li Fan.

He even began to doubt whether the scene of the worlds being destroyed that he had witnessed before was just an illusion.

"He has been here," Li Fan said in a deep voice.

"Who is he?" Shang Shaojun couldn't help but ask.

The Holy Fetus Li Fan shook his head.

"However, it's obvious at a glance."

Li Fan flicked his fingers, pointing towards the void in front.

The thousands of sword lights that formed the sword array reappeared in front of the two.

They then began to rapidly revolve and rotate.

As the speed of the sword light became faster and faster, some blurry scenes also appeared on the light screen formed by the sword light!

"The one who can do all this must be an extremely powerful existence. If we had secretly peeked just now, even with my supreme formation concealing us, even with the impact of the worlds being destroyed as a natural cover..."

"I'm afraid we would still have been noticed by Him."

At this moment, Li Fan finally explained the reason why he had used the formation to shield Shang Shaojun's prying gaze earlier.

Shang Shaojun's heart trembled, but he heard Li Fan continue, "Even the sword light I used to record was not concentrated in one place. Each sword light only recorded a fragment of information. Only when fully combined together does it show the true nature of what was recorded."

"Only in this way can we minimize the possibility of being discovered."

As Li Fan whispered, the images on the sword screen also began to change.

The remnants of the shattered worlds were destroyed, and the world-destroying fluctuations erupted and rampaged outwards.

Just as this violent scene was slowly subsiding.

A figure suddenly appeared in the center of the storm.

Or perhaps, he had never revealed his true appearance in the first place?

Shang Shaojun only knew that the moment this mastermind appeared, the originally turbulent energy fluctuations instantly calmed down.

That figure observed for a moment, seeming a bit puzzled.

Then he glanced in the direction of the Chita Abyss.

Even though it was just a simple recorded image, even though the other party had no clear appearance, even though they were separated in different times and spaces.

Being swept by this glance, Shang Shaojun felt as if his body and even his soul were about to be frozen.

His whole body was stiff, unable to think.

Fortunately, that figure only did a brief inspection and didn't investigate deeply.

He withdrew his gaze.

Shang Shaojun felt as if he had experienced a great battle, his whole body exhausted, panting heavily.

But he still stared intently at the constantly changing rotating sword screen.

That figure pressed his hands in front of his body, and the spatial storm here instantly stopped.

With a wave of his hand, the remaining embers of the worlds were taken away by him.

Then...
