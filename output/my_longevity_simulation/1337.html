The principle of the Ends of the Earth Formation was different from ordinary teleportation formations, but the effect was essentially the same. Baixua habitually called it a teleportation formation, and Li Fan did not correct her.

Having these great figures who had survived since the ancient calamity explore the path was Li Fan's plan to begin with. This sudden eruption of the rebounding starry force had only accelerated this process.

Li Fan decided not to worry about them for now. He would ask them in detail when they returned.

"The mark should have been left by Xu Kexin, but I searched around it and couldn't find any trace of him. "This caused them to form a peculiar stable state. Now, this stability has been broken."

"The true immortal power is pouring down like a raging flood, slowly flowing into the lowest part of the Dark Starry Sea, raising the entire sea..."

A glimmer of enlightenment flashed through Li Fan's mind.

In the previous life, the rapid recovery of the Starry Sea may have been driven by this event.

"What I need to do now is to find a way to intercept and temporarily preserve this flood."

"In the hope of using it when the Xuanhuang Realm ascends in the future."

This was undoubtedly a huge project.

It was impossible to do it perfectly in just half a month. Li Fan could only do his best.

The position of the Xuanhuang Realm was very special - outside the Immortal Ruins. Due to the existence of enlightened true immortals within the Immortal Ruins, from the overall height distribution of the Dark Starry Sea, the Xuanhuang Realm was absolutely at the highest peak.

In fact, because it was so close, the Xuanhuang Realm itself was slightly elevated relative to its surroundings, like the raised earth around an emerging mountain peak.

"Therefore, it's as if there is an invisible barrier, so the impact the Xuanhuang Realm naturally faces will be much less."

"What I need to do is create a basin, and then guide and accumulate the true immortal power from a wide area as much as possible."

Li Fan nodded slightly.

He had personally experienced the journey of ascension. To ascend and approach immortality, one only needed to continuously stimulate one's energy and elevate one's realm.

But as for how to create a basin...

Li Fan was still completely ignorant for now.

However, when he had overlooked the Dark Starry Sea from the Remnant Wall and the main control core, he had indeed seen areas that were lower than the ordinary regions within the sea.

Li Fan swiftly went to Oushan Tian and generated some true immortal flesh and blood.

Seeing Oushan Tian's face already pale as paper, Li Fan frowned slightly.

"It seems finding true immortal remains has become urgent."

Li Fan temporarily suppressed this thought, condensing his source power into a protective golden armor. The illusory wooden sword unsheathed, and in an instant he had left the Xuanhuang Realm, flying rapidly through the Dark Starry Sea.

As he sped towards the target area from his memory, he comprehended the changes in the differences of height.

After the wooden sword left one area of the starry sea, Li Fan seemed to sense something.

He returned again, hovering in the void, carefully observing.

"This is indeed a natural basin."

"But if I hadn't witnessed the heights and lows and viewed it from a higher dimensional perspective, I probably wouldn't have been able to discover the special nature of this place."

"It doesn't seem any different from the other areas of the starry sea."

Li Fan frowned and sent out strand after strand of divine thoughts to explore various parts of the area.

The result was the same as his perception.

This basin seemed to show no difference from normal areas despite its lower position.

After standing there for a long while still at a loss, Li Fan suddenly realized something.

Controlling his physical and spiritual power, he flicked his finger and released a sword energy strike.

It was just simulating the level of a spirit formation cultivator, but as the sword energy whistled through, it tore a crack in the space along its path.

Although the crack quickly closed up, Li Fan had seen it.

"I see."

"This basin has become fragile," Li Fan realized.

It wasn't just the space itself.

All the conditions maintaining the existence of this space had greatly diminished compared to normal starry sea areas.

"The Xuanhuang Realm is to this place as the Immortal Realm is to the Xuanhuang Realm."

"Cultivators of the Xuanhuang Realm would be like true immortals in this area. They could destroy the world with a glance, without even needing to exert effort."

Li Fan sensed the fragility of this basin.

"Its height compared to the Xuanhuang Realm doesn't actually differ that much. But the manifestation of the phenomenon is so obvious," Li Fan pondered silently.

"If it were even lower, to the extreme, what would happen?"

A question couldn't help but arise.

Li Fan's thoughts drifted.

However, this place was already nearly the lowest part of the Dark Starry Sea.

His question had no ready answer to refer to for now.

As the date of the rebounding starry force grew closer, Li Fan's thoughts returned to the present.

"With my current understanding, I still can't figure out the specific reasons for the formation of these basins."

"But it doesn't prevent me from utilizing them first."

When Li Fan had been an official in the Great Xuanhuang in the past, he had personally conducted on-site inspections, designed blueprints, and constructed water conservancy projects to benefit the region.

Now in his eyes, this project of guiding the true immortal power to converge in the basin was essentially no different from water conservancy construction.

"This basin is a natural reservoir."

"What I need to do is channel the true immortal power to flow here!"

"First, I can't let the basin absorb the true immortal power I guide here. Otherwise it will only lead to the basin rising and disappearing..."

"Source power essence is insoluble in true immortal power, yet it is an excellent material."

A ray of brilliance flashed in Li Fan's eyes.

Then an endless, cloth-like stream of source power essence erupted from his body.

Just a thin layer, to separate and isolate.

Under Li Fan's control, it quickly covered the entire basin.

Golden light radiated out as the source power essence dissipated into the darkness, seeming no different from before.

But Li Fan knew that relative to before, there was now an invisible barrier separating the basin from the outside.
