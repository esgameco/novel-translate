Chapter 275

Finally, a little boy with slightly yellowish hair stepped out and said timidly, "Doggy was turned into this by bad people."

"In the wealthy families in the city, they all keep these 'human dogs'."

"Human dogs are more obedient, smarter, and stronger than regular dogs."

"So they are very popular."

"Turning into a human dog requires undergoing immortal magic transformation, which is an extremely painful process."

"If one can't endure it, they will lose their mind and become crazy."

"Then they will be abandoned and left to fend for themselves."

Although the little boy was young, he spoke clearly and logically.

"Immortal magic..." Li Fan sneered in his heart.

He couldn't see any traces of spiritual power at work on this dog at all.

It was just the dog's limbs and human body crudely sewn together.

The so-called "immortal magic" was nothing more than a deceptive term used to fool mortals.

If Senior Brother Zhang saw such methods, he would surely angrily denounce it as "evil sorcery that everyone should execute".

"Doggy is just an abandoned defective product. If it weren't for Little Bai..."

The yellow-haired little boy was speaking when he was fiercely kicked from behind.

Patting his bottom, he looked a bit embarrassed.

The little boy quickly corrected himself, "If it weren't for Mr. Bai's intervention to treat him, I'm afraid Doggy would have been dead long ago."

Li Fan's expression changed slightly.

Mr. Bai?

It seemed he had heard of him before in this Fallen Immortal Realm?

The blue crystal in his mind sea flickered with brilliance. In an instant, it retrieved the relevant information from the depths of his memory.

In the experience of obtaining the "Purple Empyrean Soul Searching Art", after the two cultivators Zhang Qianmo and Song Hesong had a great battle that turned Ningyuan City into ruins, some beggar children around ten years old suddenly appeared amidst everyone's despair and cried out.

Igniting people's hope for survival, they were even able to organize the rescue efforts in an orderly manner.

And the one they obeyed was none other than this "Mr. Bai".

Li Fan couldn't help but become interested. He immediately asked, "Who is Mr. Bai?"

As soon as Mr. Bai was mentioned, the yellow-haired little boy became a bit excited.

He opened up and chattered on endlessly, "Mr. Bai is the best person in the world!"

"He's also the most skilled doctor in the world!"

"And the best-looking person in the world too!"

The few children behind him also chimed in one after another:

"Mr. Bai is the gentlest speaker in the world!"

"He's also the smartest person in the world!"

...

Listening to the children's words overflowing with praise, Li Fan couldn't help but find it a bit amusing.

In the eyes of these children, perhaps this small Ningyuan City was their entire world.

However, one could also see that this Mr. Bai indeed seemed to be an extraordinary person, and treated the children extremely well.

Could such a character be the owner of this remnant space?

Li Fan became interested.

"This Mr. Bai is really amazing! Could you introduce me to him?" Li Fan asked with a smile.

In an instant, all five children quieted down.

They subconsciously took a step back and looked at Li Fan with vigilant eyes.

As if Li Fan was some bad guy with ulterior motives.

Li Fan chuckled, "Don't be afraid of me. I'm a good person just like your Mr. Bai."

"I just want to meet him, that's all."

The children exchanged glances again. In the end, it was still the yellow-haired little boy who spoke up.

"Actually, we don't know where Mr. Bai lives either."

"We live in the small temple in the east of the city. Mr. Bai often comes to see us."

"If you want to meet Mr. Bai, you can wait in the small temple."

Li Fan narrowed his eyes slightly and made a judgment.

The yellow-haired little boy wasn't lying.

He tossed the still sleeping Doggy to the few of them. The yellow-haired little boy took a look and saw that Doggy had indeed just fallen asleep. He let out a sigh of relief.

Confirming that Li Fan wasn't some evil person, he finally felt at ease.

So, under their lead, Li Fan arrived at a small temple in the east part of Ningyuan City.

It seemed to be an abandoned temple.

The stone statue being worshipped had long been broken, with only a small remnant left.

There were a dozen more children in the small temple, dressed in similarly tattered clothes, both boys and girls.

Although they were all rather thin and weak, each of them was quite spirited, with a glimmer in their eyes.

Seeing the yellow-haired little boy and the other four return, they all gathered around.

Chattering, "Luya, who is this uncle?"

"How come you came back empty-handed, didn't get anything to eat this time!"

"Doggy! What happened to Doggy!"

So Luya explained to everyone.

Li Fan also understood why Luya and the other four would inexplicably follow him.

It was nothing more than seeing that he was an unfamiliar outsider and wanting to get some silver coins from him to buy something to eat.

There were other groups like Luya's that hadn't returned yet.

The children in the small temple all looked at Li Fan with vigilant and curious eyes.

But none came forward to chat with him.

Li Fan was too lazy to bicker with a bunch of kids anyway. He just waited on the side for this Mr. Bai.

If things went as expected, when Senior Brother Zhang started making his move and turned Ningyuan City into ruins under the hands of the two cultivators,

Mr. Bai would appear.

After waiting for about half a day, seeing that it was about time, Li Fan sent a message to Senior Brother Zhang.

"Be careful of Steward Song..."

Sure enough, before long, Senior Brother Zhang's thunderous angry shout rang out in the sky above Ningyuan City.

"Evil demons! Seeking death!"

Purple lightning, representing the majestic heavenly authority, flashed in the sky.

"Fellow Daoists, quickly take action!" This was Chu Liang's voice.

The battle broke out again.

Knowing Senior Brother Zhang's strength, Li Fan didn't pay too much attention this time.

Instead, he watched the performance of the children in the small temple with interest.

"Quick! Quick! Hide in the cellar!"

Seeing the strange scene in the sky, Luya's face turned a bit pale as he repeatedly directed.

The children seemed to be very skilled at taking shelter.

Pushing aside the small remnant of the stone statue revealed a hole.

They crawled in one after another in a hurry but orderly manner.

"Uncle, you should also come and take shelter!"

When only Luya was left in the temple, he turned his head and said to Li Fan, "Immortals are fighting outside, it's too dangerous!"

Li Fan smiled slightly, "Don't worry about me, you go down and take shelter first."

Luya hesitated for a moment, then nodded. His small figure then disappeared into the hole.

The stone statue slowly returned to its original position, covering the hole.

Only then did Li Fan slowly walk to the front of the stone statue, revealing a strange expression.

Just now, when this group of children pushed aside the stone statue, revealing the secret chamber below, Li Fan was also a bit surprised.

"Interesting, this seemingly ordinary stone statue can actually block my divine sense?"

Slowly stroking this aged stone statue, his internal spiritual power surged out.
