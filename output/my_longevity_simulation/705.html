Ordinary cultivators wanting to see the Tian Ling Flower didn't know how long they would have to wait.

The Flower Invitation was a qualification to enter early without queuing.

The "Flower Cave" was the location prepared by Tian Ling City for the Flower Appreciation Festival, opened by his uncle Sun Liancheng and a cultivator named Zhao Chong jointly.

Zhao Chong was known for his love of flowers.

Upon hearing about the Flower Appreciation Festival being held in Tian Ling Prefecture, he approached Sun Liancheng through an intermediary, expressing his willingness to help for free with setting up the venue. He did so in hopes of getting a chance to see the real demeanor of the Tian Ling Flower.

Although Zhao Chong was only a Nascent Divinity cultivator, his network of friends was extensive, and he had connections with several esteemed Harmony Dao cultivators in the Ten Thousand Immortal Alliance.

Sun Liancheng couldn't refuse and agreed to his proposal.

The entrance to the Flower Cave was located above the City's wide square. Jiao Xiuyuan, with a dark complexion, had set up a table and stood smiling at the entrance.

After showing the Flower Invitation, Sun Qi was finally able to enter the Flower Cave.

Before he could clearly see anything, the enchanting scent of flowers already wafted towards him.

On the open ground laid with various flowers, numerous cultivators had already taken their seats on chairs woven from green vines.

The usually reclusive Sun Qi scanned around but didn't recognize anyone. Thus, he directly proceeded to the seat corresponding to his invitation and patiently waited.

After some time, the atmosphere inside the cave suddenly became lively.

The first lucky ones had begun to enter gradually.

Once all the cultivators were inside, Jiao Xiuyuan suddenly appeared on a high platform constructed with various beautiful flowers. He held a red wooden box in his hand, attracting the gaze of all present.

"Friends, the Flower Appreciation Festival has officially begun!"

Without many words, Jiao Xiuyuan declared after a brief opening speech.
