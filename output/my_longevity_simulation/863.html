Chapter 863

Li Fan, however, was not angry.

A group of pure and extreme seven-colored light emerged in his hand.

It wrapped the one-eyed little man inside.

This seven-colored divine light was continuously and deliberately compressed by Li Fan.

The seven colors of light almost merged into one.

It turned into a dazzling white light.

It was as if there was an extra sun in his hand, illuminating the surrounding void to an incomparably white-hot state.

The yellow paper man in the white light seemed to become a little distorted due to the huge pressure of exclusion.

But Li Fan was still increasing the output of the seven-colored divine light.

With the Five Elements Grotto Heaven as a supplement, even the damaged Seven-Colored Jade Void Flowing Light could exert its original power to the greatest extent.

The seven-colored divine light did not belong to the Xuanhuang Realm.

When it began to condense, it continuously rejected all external objects.

The yellow one-eyed paper man finally seemed to sense the crisis as well.

Its eye rotated rapidly and looked at Li Fan.

It seemed to have the intention of communicating.

But Li Fan completely ignored it.

He had no intention of stopping at all and once again increased the output power of the seven-colored divine light.

Finally, when a bit of white light was refined to the extreme.

Like the violent explosion that occurred when the world was created, in the blazing fire, the yellow talisman paper was completely burned away.

The stone pressing on his heart finally fell to the ground, and Li Fan let out a long sigh of relief, slowly retracting the seven-colored divine light back into his body.

He had just advanced to the Nascent Soul stage and had not yet completely refined the Seven-Colored Jade Void Flowing Light.

What he just did was actually a bit of an overload for Li Fan.

However, it was worth it to finally destroy the means of this Crossing Calamity Sect.

"[Eternal Calamity World], seeking the person of calamity."

"It seems that the yellow one-eyed paper man is their method of finding the person of calamity."

"As expected of the legendary sect that uses [Crossing Calamity] as cultivation resources. Ordinary magic techniques simply cannot touch these paper men, let alone destroy them."

"..."

"I wonder where these old fellows are hiding."

Li Fan frowned slightly.

After thinking for a long time, he still had no clue.

The Five Elements Great Grotto Heaven had already been devoured into Li Fan's body, so naturally the passage with the changed docking coordinates also became invalid.

At this moment, Li Fan was actually in the void of Tianling Prefecture.

When he concealed his aura and broke through the void to return to the Xuanhuang Realm.

A bone-chilling feeling suddenly welled up from his heart.

Li Fan raised his head and looked at the world.

He didn't know if it was his illusion, but Li Fan seemed to see one-eyed little men made of countless yellow talisman papers.

They were distributed in every corner of the Xuanhuang Realm.

Unnoticed by anyone, they quietly widened their single eyes and observed the world.

The moment Li Fan broke through the void and returned to the Xuanhuang Realm, they seemed to sense the culprit who had destroyed one of their members.

They all rotated their eyes and looked at Li Fan!

Locked onto by thousands of gazes, Li Fan's body instantly stiffened.

However, before he could even breathe out in his mind, those gazes naturally disappeared.

From beginning to end, it was as if it was just Li Fan's illusion.

His stiffened body gradually returned to normal, but Li Fan's face could not relax.

He knew that what had happened in an instant just now was not an illusion at all.

It really happened.

In this Xuanhuang Realm, it was actually filled with eyes by the people of this Crossing Calamity Sect.

Wow! And it was done with almost no one knowing!

Li Fan couldn't help but think of himself still being a mortal, on the edge of the Great Profound Abyss.

He was almost bewitched by the illusions in the abyss and about to fall off the cliff.

But he was suddenly awakened by the same illusion.

Escaping a disaster.

Does this mean...

Even the Profound Realm has been spread and fixed with a yellow single-eyed paper man?

A hint of coolness arose in Li Fan's heart.

But after all, he had experienced too much and did not lose his composure.

After some analysis, he gradually calmed down.

First of all, it can be determined that the range that each single-eyed paper man can monitor will not be too large.

Otherwise, there would be no need for so many talismans to complete the coverage of the entire Profound Yellow Realm.

Secondly, apart from monitoring, these Crossing Calamity Sect talismans should not have any other functions.

The Myriad Immortals Alliance and the Five Elders Association definitely also know about the existence of these yellow talismans.

But it's not an easy thing to destroy them.

So most likely they will choose to turn a blind eye.

"No wonder the headquarters of the Myriad Immortals Alliance is built inside the Profound Mirror, not in the Profound Yellow Realm."

"It must be with the intention of avoiding these yellow paper men that are like psoriasis."

"And Senior Sister Zhao's One Heart Immortal Sect is also in a separate space."

Li Fan recalled his experience of almost being led to the One Heart Immortal Sect.

"The quality of this Crossing Calamity Sect is so damn low."

Through a hundred reincarnations, Li Fan had seen figures like Mo Sha, Tian Yi, Ruo Mu, Mr. White, and True Immortals.

Although each time was fraught with danger, Li Fan had reverence for these truly powerful people.

And indeed, their actions did not disappoint the demeanor of top experts.

Even if Li Fan died at their hands due to a mistake.

He would not have any complaints in his heart.

Just felt that he was not careful enough and his skills were inferior, that's all.

But only this time, Li Fan was really disgusted.

Anyone who knows that such a good Profound Yellow Realm is covered with talismans everywhere.

I'm afraid their mood will not be too good.

Even in the 114th lifetime, when the Profound Net descended, it was done openly and aboveboard.

But it is definitely much better than these densely packed, space-filling dog skin plasters!

Recalling the startling glimpse just now, Li Fan couldn't help but have the idea of thoroughly sweeping them clean.

But...

It's just a thought.

Since the Myriad Immortals Alliance has allowed the existence of these paper men for so many years.

It shows that it is not an easy thing to thoroughly eliminate them.

After all, who would be willing to live under the watchful eyes of others every day?

"Crossing Calamity Sect, Crossing Calamity Sect..."

A hint of fierceness flashed in Li Fan's eyes.

For the first time, he had the idea of actively going to kill them.

"It seems that other things will have to be put aside for the time being."

"At the very least, we have to solve the problem of being constantly monitored first."

Li Fan secretly said.

At the same time, a hint of doubt welled up in his heart.

What exactly do these people from the Crossing Calamity Sect want to do?

He had experienced quite a few endings of the Profound Yellow Realm.

Even the destruction of the world had happened.

But no matter which direction the future of the Profound Yellow Realm went, he had never seen a true Crossing Calamity Sect cultivator appear.

"Are they always waiting for the person of the tribulation to appear and for the calamity to thoroughly pass?"

Feeling the faint gaze coming from the heaven and earth.

Li Fan's thoughts surged.

After a long time, he flew towards the Nine Mountains Prefecture.
