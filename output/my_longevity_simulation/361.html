The devouring white mist continued to swallow Li Fan's lifespan, making him feel even more turbulent than in the enchantment realm.

Amidst the vast white mist, Li Fan sensed the location of Broken Palm Immortal City again.

The next moment, he fully displayed the Purple Sky Escape Technique and quickly flew towards it.

As Li Fan gradually distanced himself from the enchantment realm where the chaotic stone was located, an inexplicable wave, similar to the one emitted by the fragmented stone earlier, faintly reached him from behind.

Even the endless vast white mist seemed to ripple slightly.

"Hmm?"

Without looking back, Li Fan could still sense that the realm of chaotic stones seemed to be gradually disappearing.

Or rather, it seemed to be hiding.

However, Li Fan couldn't focus on it for the time being.

There was still some distance to Eternal Immortal Stronghold.

And as the devouring white mist continued to erode, Li Fan's age was increasing rapidly.

As he headed towards the Chizha Abyss, the speed at which his lifespan was being consumed also accelerated.

156, 178, 193...

Li Fan watched the numbers on the True Panel change continuously, feeling a long-lost sense of weakness surging in his heart.

The sudden aging made him seem like the old Great Xuan Master again.

Although the Eternal Immortal Stronghold was within reach, Li Fan calculated that with his remaining lifespan, it would be extremely difficult to cross the remaining distance.

A decisive thought flashed through his mind, and without any hesitation, Li Fan took out the Life-Extending Fruit from his storage ring and swallowed it in one gulp.
