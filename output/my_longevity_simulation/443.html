Chapter 443

The woman in blue slowly spoke.

When Li Chenfeng, Zhu Shaotang, and others heard this, they looked at each other in astonishment.

They knew very well that the two holy masters of the empire, Qing and Hong, had been powerful beings since ancient times.

The two holy masters had witnessed the empire's evolution from a mere human community to the vast empire it was today.

The guidance of the holy masters was indispensable in the eyes of the people of the empire.

In the eyes of the people of the empire, the two holy masters represented the embodiment of power and wisdom.

But now, the Qing Holy Master actually said that he couldn't see through the messenger just now?

Thinking back to his previous reckless behavior in front of the messenger, Li Chenfeng couldn't help but feel a chill.

"Perhaps I haven't been back here for too long. I'm a bit out of place after all."

At this moment, Qing's somewhat melancholic sigh made Li Chenfeng feel a little relieved.

Hundreds of miles away.

Feeling the intermittent and unclear scene of the invisible killing intent coming, Li Fan was also secretly surprised.

"It seems that this holy master is not simple either, to be able to faintly sense and counteract the invisible killing intent."

"It's a good thing my skills are better."

In exchange for the technology held by the empire, such as the "Beast God Armor," Li Fan was more than willing.

Moreover, with five million contribution points, it was a bit tight to handle.

"It seems it's time to move forward with my original plan."

Thinking of this, Li Fan sensed the position where Han Zhenghao was.

Heading towards Liuli Island, he transformed back into the appearance of an ancient Tianji sect cultivator.

...

Han Zhenghao had been feeling a bit troubled recently.

Watching the incessant inquiries coming through the communication talisman, he simply sealed it completely in his storage ring, finally calming down a bit.

Now, he didn't even dare to step half a foot outside of Liuli Island.

With the spread of the myth of his sudden wealth overnight, more and more people were trying to pry into news about Li Fan from him.

Some approached with skepticism, wanting to personally verify the master's worth.

Some purely wanted to latch onto him and replicate Han Zhenghao's experience of ascending to the heavens.

There were even some speaking inexplicable words, wanting to make a deal with him.

This made him feel overwhelmed, with his head feeling like it was going to explode, and he was exhausted.

He could only pretend to be in seclusion, hiding inside the stellar formation of Liuli Island's mountains and rivers, not daring to go out and meet people.

Han Zhenghao was in deep trouble.

It wasn't that he was trying to shift the blame; ever since he joined the Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance, the senior from the Tianji sect had disappeared and he couldn't see him again.

It was really beyond his control!

Just as he was frowning and thinking about how to explain to his friends, a somewhat familiar aura was rapidly approaching.

Breaking through the defense of the stellar formation, a figure landed in front of him with great ease.

"Haha, Han kid, long time no see!"

Li Fan laughed heartily, full of confidence and contentment.

Han Zhenghao was first stunned, then overjoyed.

It seemed like when you think about it, it happens.

"Senior, you finally came!" he almost wanted to hug Li Fan's legs and cry.

"Huh? What's wrong with you, kid?" With a light touch that pushed Han Zhenghao back several meters, Li Fan asked knowingly.

After Han Zhenghao poured out his grievances, Li Fan immediately suppressed his smile and scolded, "Serve you right!"

"You're already at the Foundation Establishment stage; how can you not understand the principle of silently making a fortune?"

"You've obtained the Yuanying technique, should focus on cultivation, why did you show off to others?"

"It's fine if only close friends know, but why did you have to make it known to everyone?"

"If you reap the consequences, you're not the only one who will suffer, but it will also implicate me!"

Shaking and trembling, Han Zhenghao didn't dare to speak.

Li Fan shook his head, looking at Han Zhenghao with disappointment.

As if about to fly away.

Han Zhenghao immediately panicked, "Senior, please wait! Senior, save me!"

With a tearful face, he explained, "I didn't mean to show off. Perhaps it was the result of years of longing, and I was so excited that I couldn't control myself that day."

"By the time I came to my senses, it had already spread, and it was too late to regret."

In fact, Han Zhenghao's will was not so dismal.

While emotional motivation and impulsiveness were factors, the main cause was the subtle temptations and guidance from Li Fan on that day.

Seeing Han Zhenghao's abject remorse, Li Fan chuckled inwardly but maintained a stern face.

He coldly said, "Save you? How can I save you? One who acts recklessly brings misfortune upon himself; how can I help you?"

Han Zhenghao's face looked even more sorrowful, but seeing that Li Fan didn't fly away immediately, he knew there was still room to maneuver.

So, he hurriedly said, "All these friends who are pestering me are just hoping to be like me, to receive your guidance and profit from it."

"Senior, with your ability to perceive hidden secrets, you can..."

Li Fan's face turned cold, "A joke. If they're neither relatives nor close, why should I help them?"

"Moreover, using such abilities also comes with risks. How does it benefit me?"

With a wave of his sleeve, Li Fan, displeased, was about to leave.

Han Zhenghao hastened to stop him. He had a sudden inspiration and said, "Sitting on the Mountain Secret! Sitting on the Mountain Secret!"

"Previously, when I broke through to the later stages of Foundation Establishment, did you sense the feedback of my cultivation?"

Li Fan's face slightly twitched, and after a while, he said slowly, "It seems there was indeed something like that."

Han Zhenghao slapped his thigh, "Exactly! Senior, think about it. Nowadays, in the cultivation world, techniques are exchanged through contribution points."

"By helping us earn contribution points, we can buy cultivation techniques with them."

"Doesn't this mean that indirectly you are passing on your teachings?"

"You should know that in the current cultivation world, passing on one's teachings is the greatest form of gratitude!"

"By practicing the Sitting on the Mountain Secret, by doing so, you will surely receive a strong feedback in cultivation."

"Not to mention, there are many friends who have come after hearing about it."

"If you take action and help them all..."

"Gradual accumulation will result in a tremendous increase in overall cultivation!"

"In this way, Senior, you should soon be able to restore the cultivation you had before going into seclusion, right?"

Han Zhenghao seemed to have an epiphany and became more and more excited.

Li Fan nodded slowly, "It makes some sense!"

"However... with so many people, my use of such abilities is limited. How can I help them one by one?"

After thinking for a while, Li Fan made a firm decision.

At that moment, Han Zhenghao had a sudden realization.

Without thinking, he said, "We can collect the contribution points from those who want you to intervene and manage them collectively."

"When you use your abilities to find suitable investment methods to earn contribution points, we can return them."

Li Fan was very satisfied and thought to himself, "Well done, Han Zhenghao, you've learned how to answer quickly."
