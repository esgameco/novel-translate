Chapter 261

Li Fan remained motionless within the Tianxuan Mirror, quietly awaiting the outcome.

Three months later, the exploration of the ruins had yet to make any significant progress.

However, He Zhenghao eagerly sent a message in advance.

"Fellow Daoist Li Fan, I have good news."

"After some maneuvering on my part, I've secured three potential positions, each located in a different province."

"Fellow Daoist, please advise me on this matter."

Li Fan immediately replied, "Oh? Do tell."

"The first one is the leader of the Martial Arts Hall in Taihua Province."

"Taihua Province is not far from the Congyun Sea. A mountain range called Taihua spans the entire province, earning it the nickname 'the province on the back of the mountains.'"

"Countless peaks stand tall. If I go to this province, my cultivation speed will be much faster compared to the Congyun Sea."

"The second one is a consultant position at the Strategy Formation Hall in Qingwei Province."

"Qingwei Province is located south of Tianyu Province. Although it's still quite far, it's much closer compared to the Congyun Sea."

"The third one is city guardian in Tianyun Province," He Zhenghao concluded.

"Tianyun Province is not far west of Tianyu Province. Therefore, the competition for this position is extremely fierce."

"The transfer would only be to an equivalent city guardian position."

"The advantage is that it's closer to the Wanxian Alliance headquarters, making promotions a bit easier compared to other places."

He Zhenghao slowly revealed the results of his efforts during this period.

"Tianyun Province..."

Li Fan pondered for a moment before replying, "Let's not rush to make a decision. I have an acquainted cultivator in Tianyun Province. I'll inquire on your behalf and see if we can take it a step further."

He Zhenghao was quite surprised upon hearing this. "Fellow Daoist, you actually have connections in Tianyun Province?"

"Hehe, I can't guarantee it. Let's give it a try. Fellow Daoist, wait for my news," Li Fan said calmly.

He Zhenghao repeatedly agreed.

So, Li Fan contacted Shenyu Zi and explained the situation.

"Senior, do you have any means to help?"

Shenyu Zi simply replied, "I'll ask around for you."

And then there was no further response.

Li Fan wasn't in a hurry and patiently waited.

Half a day later, a cultivator named Jingxuan Daoren requested to add Li Fan as a friend.

He was also from Tianyun Province.

Li Fan's heart stirred, and he quickly accepted.

Jingxuan's hearty voice came through.

"Hahaha, you must be Junior Li Fan? I heard from Shenyu that you have a good friend who wants to take up a position in our Tianyun Province?"

"Fourth-stage Golden Core, tsk tsk, the younger generation is truly formidable."

"How about this? Ask him if he's interested in joining our Tianyun Province Garrison Institute."

"Hehe, due to that great war a few years ago, there are still some vacant positions that haven't been filled."

Li Fan was slightly startled and couldn't help but ask, "Senior Jingxuan, may I ask about this Garrison Institute..."

Jingxuan was also taken aback.

He didn't expect Li Fan to be unaware of what the Garrison Institute was.

However, he soon realized that Li Fan came from a remote area and understood, explaining, "The Garrison Institute is an organization unique to the heartland of the Wanxian Alliance."

"It reports directly to the Wanxian Alliance headquarters, and its main responsibility is to suppress evil and maintain stability in the heartland."

"When necessary, it will also follow emergency orders to engage in battles in other provinces."

With Jingxuan's explanation, Li Fan understood.

In essence, it was similar to the garrison camp stationed outside Xuanjing City in Daxuan, which only listened to Li Fan's orders and guarded the safety of the capital.

"It should be considered an important position."

"However, it seems to involve frequent battles. I don't know if He Zhenghao, with his current temperament, would be willing to agree."

After pondering for a moment, Li Fan bid farewell to Jingxuan and contacted He Zhenghao, explaining the situation.

"Garrison Institute?" This time, He Zhenghao was truly shocked.

In his youth, he had spent some time in Tianyu Province, so he naturally understood what the Garrison Institute was.

Like the Dharma Protector Hall, they were both institutions that directly took orders from the Wanxian Alliance headquarters.

Only below the Dharma Protector Hall.

"Fellow Daoist Li Fan is truly unfathomable, actually being able to secure this position."

"Although it's a bit dangerous, but..."

A graceful figure suddenly flashed through He Zhenghao's mind.

He immediately gritted his teeth and agreed.

After learning of He Zhenghao's determination, Li Fan also relayed the information to Jingxuan Daoren.

"Great!" Jingxuan was very pleased.

"Have your friend come to our Tianyun Province first and serve as a city guardian."

"After three years of accumulating experience, I will use my connections to transfer him into the Garrison Institute."

Next, Jingxuan carefully explained some precautions before ending the communication.

Li Fan, now in a quiet state, thought for a moment. Although the matter had not been fully resolved, he still transferred 100,000 contribution points each to Shenyu Zi and Jingxuan.

As a gesture of gratitude.

Of course, this expense was recorded under He Zhenghao's account.

...

Tianyun Province.

Upon receiving the contribution points, Shenyu Zi was slightly startled and laughed in silence.

Jingxuan immediately sent a message inquiring, "Old Shenyu, did you receive the contribution point transfer from Junior Li? 100,000 contribution points! I remember he's only at the Foundation Establishment stage, right? Such a generous gesture!"

Shenyu Zi replied with a smile, "In any case, it's much more generous than when we were at the Foundation Establishment stage."

"I merely passed on a message and received the same reward as you. Jingxuan, you won't be angry, right?"

Jingxuan laughed heartily, "Hahaha, treating us equally, this little kid is quite interesting."

"Not bad, not bad. I find him more pleasing to the eye than that old man Guangwu."

"Mess it up? Who are you looking down on?" Jingxuan snorted coldly and fell silent.

...

Time passed.

Three months later, He Zhenghao's appointment order arrived.

There were no major issues.

City guardian of Guang'an City, Tianyun Province.

For this, a total of 80,000 contribution points were spent from start to finish.

Naturally, it was borrowed from Li Fan.

Anyway, He Zhenghao's debts didn't weigh him down, and he had no mental burden.

Li Fan was also generous, lending as much as needed.

Before departing, He Zhenghao made a solemn vow.

Once he had a stable footing in Tianyun Province, he would try to arrange for Li Fan to be transferred there as well.

Li Fan didn't say much and simply patted He Zhenghao's shoulder.

After He Zhenghao left, Li Fan hid in the Tianxuan Mirror all day, not going out at all, and diligently cultivated.

Soon, time arrived at the 23rd year of the Maoding Era.

The ruins that had risen into the sky were finally discovered.

They had fallen into a thousand-mile swamp in the center of Jiushan Province.

As cultivators began exploring the ruins, the origins of these ruins were also unearthed.

The ancient immortal sect, Chaoyuan Sect.

Although not as renowned as Taishang or Dadao Sect, whose prestige remained well-known even thousands of years later, it was still a famous and prominent sect before the great calamity descended.

Based on previous experiences of exploring sect ruins, there were definitely abundant treasures stored here.

When the news spread, it immediately attracted many cultivators to venture to Jiushan Province for exploration.

Jiushan Province, which was already in a state of chaos, became even more turbulent.

The extent of their arrogance was evident.

The weakness of the Wanxian Alliance's control over Jiushan Province could also be seen.

Originally, it was just a normal ruins exploration, and the Wanxian Alliance had no intention of interfering too much.

But at this time, a piece of news arrived, causing the Wanxian Alliance to be furious.

A Nascent Soul cultivator named Luo Xi from the Five Elders Council came to explore the ruins, which was fine.

However, not only did he not conceal his identity as a cultivator from the Five Elders Council, he even relied on his profound cultivation to injure and kill many exploration members of the Wanxian Alliance.

After entering the ruins and obtaining unknown treasures, he left with his head held high.

On my turf, and still being so arrogant?

It was simply a slap in the face.

However, suspicions also arose.

Such behavior carried a clear provocative intent.

Could there be some hidden scheme?

Could it be that the Five Elders Council wanted to start another war?

Regardless, face could not be lost.

The Wanxian Alliance swiftly dispatched several Nascent Soul cultivators to Jiushan Province to maintain order.

And they issued a stern warning.

All cultivators from the Five Elders Council were prohibited from coming to Jiushan Province.

Those who dared to come would face death!
