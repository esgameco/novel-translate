"I have also seen the cultivator in Duan Xian Lou who is said to have entered the Dao using a new method. It is said that he only took three years to attain the Dao. Moreover, his ability to control the power of true immortals is not inferior to mine," Yu Shuo said with an inexplicable tone.

"If that's the case, why do you still seem to have many doubts in your heart, Old Yu?"

"Because in the entire Duan Xian Lou, the cultivators who are currently claimed to have 'entered the Dao' and have publicly shown their faces in public occasions, the true immortal power they control is all of the same kind," Yu Shuo said in a deep voice.

"What does this indicate?" Zhao Xian was a little puzzled.

Yu Shuo glanced at Zhao Xian with an inexplicable expression. After a long silence, he finally spoke very calmly, "Zhao Xian, you must remember. When dealing with the power of true immortals, never be careless."

"The deeper one enters the Dao, the more dangerous one becomes to oneself."

Yu Shuo did not directly explain but said this sentence for no reason.

"Today's lesson ends here. Next time, it will be Nan Gong's turn." Yu Shuo's figure disappeared along with his voice.

Zhao Xian still stood in place, pondering Yu Shuo's last words.

A flash of inspiration in his mind made him suddenly realize the implication behind it.

"The power of true immortals is like a net. By relying on the true immortal net, although one can invoke the power of true immortals, the result can only be getting more and more entangled..."

Zhao Xian, who suddenly realized this principle, felt a chill all over as if a basin of cold water had been poured on him in winter.

"If this is true, wouldn't my Zheng Dao Academy and even the entire Xuan Xian Boat be..."

But soon, Zhao Xian laughed bitterly.

"In order to survive, we have no choice but to do so. After all, survival is the first priority that the immortal boat needs to maintain. Moreover, we have been wandering in the starry sea for so many years, only seeing the remnants of true immortals, not their shadows. There are indeed risks, but they can be temporarily excluded from consideration."

At the same time, Zhao Xian also understood what Yu Shuo said about the strangeness of Duan Xian Lou.

In the darkest starry sea, various true immortal remnant powers are mixed.

If one really mastered the method of absorbing the remnants of the destroyed realm harmlessly and perfectly, then the performance of those cultivators in Duan Xian Lou who entered the Dao should also simultaneously have various different states.

After all, the true immortal remnant power that each person can adapt to is not the same.

In the darkest starry sea, there are no less than five known types of true immortal powers. The three chiefs of Zheng Dao Academy each have different directions in entering the Dao.

However, the six cultivators who have currently appeared in Duan Xian Lou and entered the Dao all exhibit the same power of entering the Dao.

This probability exists but is very small.

"Either, the technique they have mastered is only effective for a single true immortal power."

"Or..."

Zhao Xian felt a chill in his heart and dared not think too much.

Now the task given to him by the academy is to temporarily consolidate his Nascent Soul cultivation, and then challenge the Transformation Deity realm again.

Once he successfully transforms into a deity, he will slowly start trying to "enter the Dao".

"Entering the Dao, it can't be too early. Nor can it be too late."

"If one comes into contact with the power of true immortals too early, the weak soul and will are almost impossible to resist the vast and unmatched power of true immortals. It is very likely to directly mutate into a crazy, deformed monster."

"And if it's too late..."

The teaching voice of Nan Gong Lie, the head of Zheng Dao Academy, echoed in Zhao Xian's mind.

"The academy will not overly force it for now. The most urgent task is still to advance as soon as possible..."
