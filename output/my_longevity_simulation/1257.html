The Holy Emperor's words were not empty promises.

Regardless of whether it was the Great Master of the Heavenly Capital or the existence secretly monitoring Xuanhuang, they certainly would not transcend the realm of [Immortals].

Although Li Ping was currently only at the Longevity Realm of the Xuanhuang World, as the incarnation of the Heavenly Dao on this land where too many strange things had happened, he grasped the means to achieve mutual destruction and restraint.

Li Ping needed some time to sort out the gains he obtained from the stone tablet and the Crossing Calamity Sect.

He put away the Crossing Calamity Book and stored it in the treasure vault for suppression; he gave the stone tablet a rare leisure time to relax and disguise itself as a mortal to travel in the Xuanhuang World.

Li Ping himself first arrived in the Xuanhuang World.

Fuguang State, Great Rift Valley.

Deep underground.

Ignoring the superficial protective formations around, Li Ping arrived at the depths of the relic ruins.

Here, he felt two extremely terrifying forces that even made him feel a little uneasy.

One came from the two statues or two remains in the ruins.

The other came from this [First Sect] ruin itself.

"The worrisome hidden dangers can all find solutions here."

Li Ping stared at the ruins that had been desolate for a long time but seemed to have just been destroyed, and thought to himself.

Without using the power of the golden origin force essence, he silently chanted in his heart: "Cat Treasure."

At this time, Cat Treasure was in the treasure vault under the Holy Emperor's seat, using its paws to tease the trembling Crossing Calamity Book. After sensing Li Ping's summons, the figure pressing on the Crossing Calamity Book did not disappear, but another small cat body appeared on Li Ping's shoulder.

"Meow~"

Cat Treasure called out in response.

"Take everything here away." Li Ping patted its head.

Cat Treasure glanced at the [First Sect] ruins, a trace of disdain flashing in its eyes. However, it did not disobey Li Ping's order.

Its figure instantly became ethereal like data blue light, and Cat Treasure slightly opened its mouth.

The blue dots expanded, enveloping the entire underground ruins.

After Cat Treasure returned to its original form, the original location of the [First Sect] ruins, including the buildings, formations, space, and everything inside.

All disappeared without a trace.

Only pure nothingness remained.

Cat Treasure yawned and became a little listless.

The First Sect ruins were not directly destroyed but were completely stored in Cat Treasure's "belly". With a single thought from Li Ping, he could release it again.

In fact, with Li Ping's current strength alone, he could easily achieve this point with simple space cutting and transfer.

The reason why Li Ping let Cat Treasure take action was to prevent his own origin force essence from disturbing the remnants of the Eroding Wind Calamity in the ruins.

It was also to test the power nature of Cat Treasure.

"Not only are the remains of the Great Master of the Heavenly Capital unaffected, but even the Eroding Wind Calamity, which is extremely sensitive to the power of [Immortals], is also unmoved."

"Although Cat Treasure is not an immortal artifact, it seems to be taking a completely different path."

The scenes of disciple Sun Erlang's experiences in the True Nation flashed before Li Ping's eyes one by one.

Just as the Holy Dynasty was greatly opened, and under the operation of the great faceless Holy Emperor, its strength became more and more powerful.

Far across the starry sea, the Holy Fetus Li Fan also ushered in a huge harvest.

On this day, the reverberating star power originating from the invisible high wall erupted again.

Hidden in the Remnant Wall, the Holy Fetus Li Fan could vaguely feel the slight tremor of the surrounding worlds.

The tremors of different remnant pieces merged and harmonized with each other, condensing into a strange power, resisting the vast immortal power that could easily destroy the starry sea.

Affected by the reverberating star power, although the Holy Fetus Li Fan's body was involuntarily attracted towards the high wall, there was no bewilderment or panic in his eyes like the first encounter.

Instead, there was immersion and the joy of enlightenment.

"The reverberating star power is the remnant of true immortal power."

"And the Remnant Wall is the corpse of the mortal cultivation world. Although the cultivation world was vulnerable to the immortal power when it was alive, after its death, it can unite to resist it together."

The Holy Fetus Li Fan's eyes became brighter and brighter: "This is the Dao of the entire Dark Star Sea, evolving spontaneously."

"It is no longer the existence called the lower realm when the immortal realm still existed, but a new Dao system reborn from destruction."

"The destruction of the immortal realm, regardless of the reason, indicates that the immortal realm has already gone against the most fundamental Dao of the world. And the Dark Star Sea that still exists today is instinctively and slowly transforming towards this most fundamental Dao."

"As one rises, the other falls. This is also the reason why the Remnant Wall can actually resist the power of the true immortal seal characters. The Dark Star Sea is still far from completing this sublimation, but if given enough time..."

"Perhaps the Dark Star Sea within this high wall can develop into an existence comparable to the former immortal realm."

The Holy Fetus Li Fan had a realization, and in his perception, the overall rhythm of the Remnant Wall also became clearer and brighter, like a melodious song.

His speed of floating towards the invisible high wall also gradually slowed down.

"One day of enlightenment here is equivalent to a hundred years in the Xuanhuang World."

"In this sense, it is not wrong to call this place the [Immortal Realm]."

The body of the Holy Fetus Li Fan finally came to a complete stop, floating within the remnant pieces of various realms.

Strangely, the sword light he slashed out, after flying straight for a distance, changed its original direction.

It flew towards the nearest world remnant piece.

After locking onto the target, it accelerated. Like a silver-white spider silk, it fiercely embedded into it.

The Holy Fetus Li Fan continuously moved in the opposite direction of the high wall, stepping out of the Remnant Wall bit by bit.

What was left behind were thousands of sword light threads that slowly concealed into the darkness.

"With the Remnant Wall as an anchor, not only do I not have to worry about being attracted by the high wall again, but I can also shuttle through the starry sea at a faster speed."

The Holy Fetus Li Fan turned into an invisible light and quickly returned to the Profound Immortal Boat.

The divine sense he left behind was no longer sufficient to deal with the increasingly complex situation. It was time for him to make an appearance.

The avatar and the Holy Fetus both accumulated strength for future use.
