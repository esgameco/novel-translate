Chapter 866

Here is the full English translation of the excerpt from the Chinese novel:

With the stone tablet scholar's words, Li Fan's heart had in fact already become extremely tense.

However, the current Li Taishi already possessed the battle power of Uniting the Dao. Moreover, he had the Seven-Colored Jade Void Flowing Light and many other great divine powers at his side.

He would no longer be easily frightened by someone's few words and "truly believe it".

Even if the other party was the Eternal Life Venerated One!

Li Fan decided...

To further probe the other party's background!

So, Li Fan pretended to be somewhat doubtful and a bit surprised: "Beyond the Primordial Yellow Realm? What does that have to do with me?"

The stone tablet scholar smiled slightly: "Since you refuse to admit it, then let's take a look at the records about you on the stone tablet."

Behind the stone tablet, a surge of cyan light welled up.

An aura of the vicissitudes of time flowed from the scholar's body. Various images began to continuously revolve and appear around the two of them.

It was like falling into the long river of history, with scenes of past events in the Primordial Yellow Realm unfolding like a scroll painting.

The scholar even let out a muffled groan as his body began to expand.

His facial features and the clothes on his body, for some reason, rapidly changed in succession.

One moment he was a handsome scholar, the next a robust woodcutter.

Then he became a charming beauty.

Sometimes he wasn't even human.

But a tree, a flower.

Birds flying in the sky, beasts walking on the ground.

...

Only the stone tablet behind, with its eternal cyan light, remained unchanged.

In the end, the images revolving on the surrounding screens gradually began to stabilize.

Glowing brightly, what appeared on it was precisely Li Fan!

However, there was no scene of him flying out from the Great Mystery.

There wasn't even the scene in the beginning of him coming to the Primordial Yellow Realm through a spatial passage and heading straight for Tianyang Cave Mansion to subdue Tianyang.

It only showed him bringing Tianyang and entering Shilin Prefecture.

Finding the entrance to the Spirit Wood Realm.

Entering and exiting again, then capturing Shang Tianci in Jiushan Prefecture.

Finding Mr. Bai's secret room underground in Taihua Prefecture.

Returning to the Spirit Wood Realm, then bringing out Lin Ling who had the power of Uniting the Dao.

First completing a transaction with the Five Elders Society, then heading straight for the Five Elements Great Grotto-Heaven in Tianling Prefecture.

The scenes kept changing.

However, the vigilance in Li Fan's heart instantly relaxed quite a bit.

"It's just as I thought."

"The Creation Stone Tablet automatically records all things that occur in the Primordial Yellow Realm in great detail. But it's limited to just the original Primordial Yellow Realm!"

"You should know, the current Primordial Yellow Realm was formed from the fusion of the original realm with countless grotto-heavens, small worlds, and even fragments of many other immortal cultivation realms!"

"The Creation Stone Tablet may not necessarily have records of these outsiders!"

To use a house as an analogy:

The original Primordial Yellow Realm was just a single small room.

But after so many years of expansion by countless cultivators, the Primordial Yellow Realm has become much larger than before.

The Creation Stone Tablet born together with heaven and earth in that initial small room has complete recording authority.

But for these lands that came from outside, the Creation Stone Tablet has lost its ability to record!

...

The scenes continued to change.

Just as Li Fan anticipated, it was intermittent.

Most critically, Congyun Sea, Spirit Wood Realm, Five Elements Great Grotto-Heaven... where he often appeared were not within the scope of the records.

So it was basically all scenes of him hurrying on the road, constantly hurrying.

"Then when this scholar said he came for me..."

"Where did the flaw appear?" Li Fan's mind raced.

The scenes shown on the stone tablet were already approaching the current point in time.

Suddenly, Ji Shaoli's avatar appeared in the scene.

The moment he entered Yong Yun State, the scene abruptly ended.

After Ji Shaoli left Yong Yun State, he headed straight for the Lingmu Realm.

"Was it the seven-colored divine light that caught his attention?" Li Fan couldn't help but think.

The words spoken by the stone tablet scholar next also confirmed his guess.

"I don't actually care about the truth or falsehood of that so-called Myriad Realms Alliance."

"What I'm more interested in is where you obtained this seven-colored divine light from in Yong Yun State." The scholar looked at Li Fan, his eyes burning with intensity.

"The level of this object is not something that can be nurtured in the remnant states."

"Can't it be a divine ability that I comprehended myself?" Li Fan retorted.

The scholar laughed in spite of himself.

"I possess the Creation Stone Tablet, so I am naturally very familiar with the aura emanating from your seven-colored radiance. It is a power that has nearly condensed the Dao of a world. If you had such abilities, then the one asking questions here today would not be me."

"But you." The scholar said calmly.

Although there was no mocking intent, coupled with his expression and tone, it still made Li Fan snort coldly.

Seeing Li Fan not answering, the stone tablet scholar continued, "Rest assured, I won't snatch your belongings. This seven-colored divine light, while powerful, is just a burden to me."

"What I truly want to know is the place that nurtured the seven-colored divine light." The stone tablet scholar stated frankly.

Li Fan narrowed his eyes, "What does Senior mean by this? How do you know that the seven-colored divine light was produced by something? Can't it be that there's just a single treasure hidden in Yong Yun State?"

The stone tablet scholar sighed.

The surrounding scenery on the light screens suddenly turned pitch black.

Then, a point of seven-colored light abruptly appeared.

In the darkness, it was incomparably dazzling and eye-catching.

The scholar looked at Li Fan, "Do you understand now?"

"As long as such a treasure exists in the world, regardless of whether it has been refined by someone or not. It is always like this."

"In the past..."

"Some people thoroughly searched the Xuanhuang Realm. Apart from that Tianxuan Mirror, there are no longer any treasures above the realm-grade in the world."

"Therefore, your seven-colored divine light must have appeared not long ago..."

Hearing this, Li Fan's expression turned somewhat unsightly.

He didn't expect that while this seven-colored divine light was powerful, in the eyes of true powerhouses, it was actually so eye-catching?

Faced with the stone tablet scholar's inquiry, Li Fan's mind also raced, thinking about how to answer.

After a moment, he had a plan.

Putting on a very troubled appearance, full of struggle, he finally let out a long sigh, "Forget it, I knew this matter couldn't be kept hidden after all."

"Senior, follow me, and you'll know at a glance."

With that, he awakened Lin Ling, who had been in a dazed state and watching from the side, not daring to interject.

Then, he dove underground, heading straight for Yong Yun State.

In the earth veins, the stone tablet scholar followed closely behind, as if taking a leisurely stroll, unhurried.

Seeing this scene, Li Fan inwardly clicked his tongue.

But then he thought, "No wonder in the previous life, even though he had a treasure like the Fishing Pond, Zhong Shentong didn't always guard it later on. Instead, he wandered around everywhere."

"It seems that after he left Yong Yun State and revealed the seven-colored divine light, it was discovered by this stone tablet scholar. Causing the other party to come knocking on his door."

"He was forced to lose ownership of his ultimate treasure."
