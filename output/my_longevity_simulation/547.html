As Li Fan kept talking incessantly, he noticed the lack of response from the other party. Perhaps feeling uninterested himself, he also stopped midway.

Xiaoqing's gaze towards Li Fan turned slightly unkind.

However, after this incident, she no longer doubted Li Fan's identity.

"You also know, the period before the outbreak of war is the most tense for both sides."

"Perhaps we can make some gains."

Watching Li Fan's self-satisfied appearance, Xiaoqing was skeptical about his mention of the 'impending war.'

The empire also had its own sources of information.

In recent years, although the number of lawbreakers was small, they were scattered all over the Yellow Realm.

During her return to the cultivation world, Xiaoqing had already established contact with the majority of them.

Because of their presence, Xiaoqing actually had some understanding of the current situation in the Yellow Realm.

As the saying goes, a great wind arises from a slight breeze.

There must be reasons for the outbreak of war, right?

Then there should be some signs of the impending war now.

"Maybe they missed some details..."

Whether there were any signs of an impending war.
