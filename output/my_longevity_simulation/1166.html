Chapter 1164: Overturning Heaven and Earth with One Hand

Baihua Daoren turned his head away, trembling and not daring to respond.

Li Ping waited for a long time, but still did not see Baihua change his mind. He then sighed lightly and put away the three True Immortal seal characters beside him.

"Forget it, if Fellow Daoist is unwilling, I won't force it."

"After all, not everyone has the courage to leverage the power of True Immortals..."

"As for the matter of transporting the Five Elements Great Grotto-Heaven, does Fellow Daoist have any difficulties?" Li Ping returned to the main topic and asked.

Feeling the terrifying power and aura around him disappear, Baihua Daoren finally recovered from his shock for a brief moment.

The tone of Sacred Emperor Li Ping's question was already subtly different from before. Baihua knew that if he refused again, he would probably truly anger this madman. He could only agree with difficulty.

"Fellow Daoist, please set off as soon as possible. By the way, since you are unfamiliar with the destination, to be on the safe side, I will send a disciple to accompany you." Li Ping said intentionally or unintentionally.

When Baihua Daoren saw the so-called disciple of Li Ping, he couldn't help but feel his scalp go numb and his body tremble again.

"This is..."

Even though Baihua had extraordinary knowledge and had personally witnessed the destruction of various worlds, at this moment he still found it a bit hard to accept: a body that looked completely the same as an ordinary human, but was actually composed of countless constantly wriggling unknown insects inside. Each of these insects was an individual with its own independent thoughts, yet they gathered to form a collective "human" entity.

Baihua Daoren's scalp went numb, not because he was scared by the endless ugly insects. But because it was his body's instinctive reaction.

Those insects seemed to have some kind of natural restraint on bodies purely made of energy.

Even though she was at the Eternal Life realm, even though there was still some distance between her and these insects. Baihua still felt the sudden birth of anxiety and unease within her body. Constantly warning her to stay away from this insect person.

"You can call him [Dao Yi]." Li Ping answered irrelevantly.

"Don't worry, although Dao Yi's strength is a bit lacking, he has some peculiar abilities. With his help, it should be much easier for Fellow Daoist to transport the Five Elements Great Grotto-Heaven."

Seeing this, Baihua Daoren could only steel himself and nod slightly. Led by Dao Yi, he headed straight for Tianling Prefecture.

Watching the two figures disappear into the distance, Li Ping actually did not feel much joy in intimidating an Eternal Life realm expert with a few words.

"My own strength is still not enough. I can only use external objects to threaten. If I could use the essence of Source Power, there would be no need for such trouble."

Dao Yi was a special variant of the Dao Yi Insect preserved in the Myriad Beasts Crystal.

No matter how much the group population expanded, it could always maintain a unified tribal consciousness. More crucially, this Dao Yi Insect not only simply produced spiritual energy.

It seemed to be able to reside within spiritual energy, using it to multiply itself, and then gradually transform itself into an existence like spiritual energy.

With sufficient external breeding conditions, [Dao Yi] only needed about a year to convert all the spiritual energy of an entire small world into "itself".

The whole process was silent and would not even affect the various creatures in the world that relied on spiritual energy to survive.

Even weaker cultivators were unable to detect it.

Only after reaching the Nascent Soul stage and forming an independent grotto-heaven in the body could one sense this mutated spiritual energy.

Li Fan sent [Dao Yi], cultivated with a wisp of his own consciousness, to the Five Elements Great Grotto-Heaven.
