The Youzu people.

What surprised Li Fan a little was that in this small world, there was no garrison of the Wanxian Alliance.

The number of Youzu people was also very scarce, with less than a hundred people.

The tribe was not as strong and healthy as Li Fan had seen before.

Each of them was as thin as a rake, as if they were at risk of sudden death at any time.

After some investigation, Li Fan vaguely understood.

The Youzu tribe before his eyes had fled for their lives from their original homeland.

The Wanxian Alliance captured the Youzu, and they escaped at the risk of their lives and found such a place to hide.

"This is even better."

Li Fan no longer hesitated. With his full power erupting and cooperating with Tianyang, he was about to suppress this group of Youzu people into his own Dongtian.

However, something that Li Fan didn't quite expect happened.

Faced with Li Fan's attack, these Youzu people, who seemed to have no extraordinary aura, burst out with a very strange power.

The elders in the tribe, though startled, remained calm.

Chanting loudly, a strange fluctuation instantly enveloped the whole group.

It was not some kind of illusion, but the attack really missed.

"Hmm? Interesting."

At that moment, Li Fan clearly felt the fluctuation of the power of darkness.

"You can run away from the monk, but you can't run away from the temple."

When entering this small world, Li Fan had already used a formation to completely block the entrance and exit.

Without his permission, no one could leave this world!

His divine sense swept around, searching the entire small world, but still could not find the hidden Youzu people.

Li Fan simply launched indiscriminate attacks.

The black light full of destructive power fell from the sky and bombarded every inch of the earth.

Countless lives were affected.

But Li Fan remained unmoved and cleared all places in this small world.

Under this large-scale, dead-angle cleansing, the group of Youzu people finally revealed flaws.

Sensing that something seemed to have blocked his Great Five Elements Annihilation Sword, a flash of brilliance appeared in Li Fan's eyes.

He instantly used formations to seal off that area layer by layer.

Under coercion, the Youzu people finally showed their figures.

They huddled together, looking up at Li Fan in the sky.

There was fear and hatred in their eyes.

The leading elder finally couldn't help but speak at this time: "Why does the Immortal have to kill to the end?"

"Otherwise, with your meager strength, could you survive under my hands?"

With that said, Li Fan immediately operated the Soul Burning Method at full power.

The five great Dongtian, under the illumination of blood color, were extremely weird.

With the blessing of the Tianyang Immortal Puppet, the strength Li Fan displayed at this time was already comparable to that of an Immortal of Eternal Life realm.

The small world he was in couldn't contain it, and black spatial cracks appeared one after another.

The heaven and earth were shaking and gradually disintegrating.

Just the aura released had already led to an apocalyptic scene.

This made the Youzu people below extremely terrified.

"The Wanxian Alliance is currently exploring your sacred land, and I want to take the opportunity to secretly assassinate them."

"Are you willing to lend me a helping hand?"

There was no second option. Coupled with the hatred for the Wanxian Alliance, the Youzu people below quickly chose to submit.
