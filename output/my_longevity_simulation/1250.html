Chapter 1250

Upon hearing Sun Erlang's words, the white-haired Taoist priest smiled slightly.

"Indeed, every person has their own independent personality and thoughts. It is the instinct of all living beings to flee when faced with great calamity. It is quite difficult to make everyone unite with one heart and mind. The various events that have occurred in the history of the Xuanhuang Realm have proven this point."

The white-haired Taoist priest lightly waved his feather duster, and in an instant, countless scenes appeared in the pure white space before Sun Erlang's eyes.

The black shadow representing the end of the world floated above the Xuanhuang Realm, ready to descend at any moment. However, regarding how to deal with this catastrophe, the various sects and factions in the Xuanhuang Realm still held different attitudes.

Some were determined to abandon the realm and flee, some chose to be ostriches burying their heads in the sand, and some tried their best to mobilize and call on everyone to unite.

There were also those who took advantage of the chaos to satisfy their own desires.

The beings in the scenes had no specific appearances and were all represented by faceless little people.

But the absurd farces being played out were incredibly real.

"But this is the Xuanhuang Realm of the past," the white-haired Taoist priest said, waving his feather duster again, and the scenes suddenly changed.

"The masses united as one, their will firm enough to defy the heavenly fate, boldly combining their divine powers, without worries and thus without fear, a single word could settle the Nine Tripods..."

Accompanied by the words of the True Celestial Venerable, what appeared before Sun Erlang were detailed scenes of human life in the various territories of the Five Elders Council.

"What do you think, how does this compare to the Myriad Immortals Alliance?" the True Celestial Venerable asked.

"It is naturally much stronger than the Myriad Immortals Alliance," Sun Erlang said without hesitation.

He then added, "It's just a pity that all of this is built upon the great divine powers of the five Celestial Venerables. Everything is as illusory as a flower in a mirror or the moon in the water. If one day, any of these five Celestial Venerables suddenly meets with misfortune, this seemingly beautiful scene will collapse in an instant."

To openly discuss the possibility of Celestial Venerables falling on the territory of the Five Elders Council - such words could be considered utterly treasonous. However, the white-haired Taoist priest, who seemed to be the True Celestial Venerable, did not become angry.

Instead, he nodded in agreement, "What you said indeed makes some sense, but also no sense at all."

Sun Erlang was startled and respectfully said with his hands clasped, "Please enlighten me, Celestial Venerable."

"The reason I say you make sense is because from the perspective of an ordinary cultivator from outside, it indeed seems so. The order here exists because of the five Celestial Venerables. If the Celestial Venerables were to pass away, the order would also cease to exist."

"As for why I say you make no sense, it's because you don't understand the true meaning of Immortality in this realm."

The True Celestial Venerable stroked his beard and said slowly, "Defying the principles of heaven and earth to attain Immortality."

"After cultivators defy the principles, they naturally become a part of the principles of the world. Just like sunlight, water droplets, and air, they together constitute the [World] itself."

"Humans are not isolated individuals. It is meaningless to deny the existence of Immortals and then discuss cultivators under the influence of Immortality."

Sun Erlang didn't quite understand what the True Celestial Venerable was saying.

In his view, it sounded more like some kind of sophistry.

But as an Immortal Celestial Venerable standing at the pinnacle of this realm, he obviously wouldn't engage in a battle of wits with a junior like him.

So Sun Erlang frowned and pondered carefully.

After a long while, he said thoughtfully, "We are discussing the cultivators of the Xuanhuang Realm. This is the major premise. And Immortal Celestial Venerables have already become an inseparable part of the Xuanhuang Realm, so..."

"No," Sun Erlang suddenly became alert, "The key is that your power doesn't seem to cover the entire Xuanhuang Realm? It's limited to just this area."

The True Celestial Venerable asked, "Have you ever sensed the existence of Celestial Venerable Wuyou's divine power?"

"As fellow Immortals, Wuyou's strength is not much greater than the rest of us. What he can do..."

"We can naturally do as well," the True Celestial Venerable said slowly.

Upon hearing this, Sun Erlang instinctively found it a bit unbelievable at first.

But as he thought about it, his expression gradually became somewhat solemn.

"Then why?" he couldn't help but ask.

"Naturally, the time has not yet come." The white-haired Taoist priest smiled lightly and waved his feather duster again.

The white space of the True Fruit Tree slowly faded away.

And the figure of the True Celestial Venerable also gradually became blurry.

"Celestial Venerable!"

It was only at this moment that Sun Erlang suddenly remembered his master's instructions and hurriedly called out, "Does the Celestial Venerable know about the Eastern Extremity Celestial Venerable?"

"You will know when you see."

The white space exploded with a bang.

Countless lights and shadows finally burst forth together, blooming in Sun Erlang's mind.

Returning to the real world, Sun Erlang was still immersed in the images transmitted by the True Celestial Venerable.

"Defy the principles of heaven and earth again? Are you crazy? You've already come this far, just one step away from defying the principles. As long as you proceed normally step by step, it will be enough to attain the Dao of Immortality. Why do you have to do this?" The young Taoist priest's expression was full of incomprehension, his words intense.

"Immortality? It's nothing more than transforming the body into the Dao." The figure opposite him had an indiscernible face, but his tone was quite disdainful of [Immortality].

He continued, "If it weren't for that existence, it wouldn't matter if we transformed and attained Immortality. But the entire cultivation system is built upon the framework he created..."

"Xinghan, do you know what the consequences will be?"

The young Taoist priest remained silent.

"The Xuanhuang Realm is being reborn amidst destruction. This is the only thing he cares about at the moment. Someone attaining the Dao of Immortality is utterly irrelevant to him. In fact, he would even hope for the emergence of Immortals that meet his expectations. Do you know why?"

The young Taoist priest continued to keep his head down and remain silent.

"Your talents are not inferior to mine. How could you not understand? You're just harboring a fluke."

"Let me put it bluntly. One day, when the Xuanhuang Realm welcomes its rebirth and completes its transformation, the so-called Immortal Celestial Venerables will completely become a part of the Dao of the new world... a fate worse than death, or rather, life and death will be meaningless."

"They're nothing more than tools for remaking the world."

"It's just your imagination and speculation..." After a long time, the young Taoist priest retorted weakly.

The blurry figure, however, paid no heed and continued talking to himself, "But I refuse to believe it! I will fight against him!"

"Defying the principles of heaven and earth to attain Immortality. This is the foundation of his attainment of the Dao, but it is also his greatest flaw. If one can defy his principles again, one can instantly steal all his creations and replace him..."

"Eastern Extremity! You..." The young Taoist priest hurriedly looked around, afraid that these treasonous words would be heard by others.

That blurry figure, however, was filled with ambition, his eyes brimming with wild aspirations.

"Xinghan, don't be nervous. I am not alone on this path." At this moment, Eastern Extremity suddenly spoke up to reassure him.

"Within that Immortal-Mortal Miasma, there are hidden mysteries of defying the principles of heaven and earth again. It originated from the previous Xuanhuang Great Celestial Venerable who has already passed away..."
