There was only darkness surrounding Wu Ninglu, and she couldn't find the source of the voice.

And that voice kept laughing strangely, constantly stimulating her.

"Come out!" Wu Ninglu roared frantically.

The other party didn't respond, but instead, one picture after another illuminated the darkness.

When Wu Ninglu looked, the resentment in her heart immediately rose to another level.

It was the lively scene of Sun Tianzi, the Qilin son of the Sun family, being born in accordance with the Dao, shaking the world, and all the major families of the Wanxian Alliance sending people to congratulate him.

This even included her own family, the Chen family of the Medicine Hall! Despite having already torn their faces with the Sun family before, they still sent an envoy to congratulate them. As the saying goes, don't hit a smiling person with an outstretched hand. For the sake of the newly born Sun Tianzi, they also accepted the congratulatory gifts with great magnanimity.

As for Wu Ninglu, the inexplicably missing biological mother of Sun Tianzi, no one cared about her at all.

...

"Originally, all this glory and praise should have been mine!"

The strong contrast in circumstances between mother and son made Wu Ninglu, who was in the darkness, even more insane.

Crying and shouting, like ghosts wailing and wolves howling.

At this time, that voice finally sounded again.

"You..."

"Want to take revenge on them?"

Instinct drove Wu Ninglu to want to curse loudly, but her remaining bit of reason made her slowly calm down.

She answered without hesitation: "I want to! Senior, save me!"

"Hahaha!"

A wild laugh came from the darkness: "Your body and soul have already disappeared from this world, leaving only a few remnants of spiritual thoughts. Even if you can be resurrected, how can you fight against the five Dao-achieving members of the Sun family?"

Upon hearing this, Wu Ninglu's thoughts instantly went blank.

That's right, let alone that she was already in this state. Even if she successfully devoured the essence of heaven and earth and achieved the Dao herself, how could she be an opponent to the Sun family, a rising new family in full swing, with just the strength of one person?

Wu Ninglu was in utter despair, but suddenly thought that this senior, who could save her in front of the three Dao-achievers, definitely wouldn't just be teasing her for fun.

The reason for still saying this...

Wu Ninglu suddenly realized.

"I still hope that the senior will teach me! Whether it succeeds or not, Ninglu is willing to be a cow, a horse, a slave, a maid, and serve by the senior's side!"

Wu Ninglu said through gritted teeth.

"Humph, you're just a remnant thought struggling to survive, do I need your willingness to control you?"

The powerful pressure enveloped Wu Ninglu, and in the terrible suffocation, Wu Ninglu only felt that her cautious thoughts could be completely destroyed at any time.

Her thoughts became weaker and weaker, and Wu Ninglu's heart was filled with despair.

At this moment, the sense of urgency around her inexplicably disappeared.

That voice, like heavenly music, sounded in her ears again.

At the same time, an extremely profound text also appeared in her mind.

The Three Disasters and Hundred Tribulations Divine Method.

One thought gives birth to ten thousand demons, this is a supreme divine skill that can make up for the gap in the number of cultivators.

Wu Ninglu read every word in it as if she was starving and thirsty.

"Sun family, Sun family..."

Under the support of the terrible obsession of wanting revenge in her mind, the first murderous intent demon god slowly gestated.

Hiding in the darkness, Li Fan, who saw the whole process of this change, nodded slightly with his divine sense.

Although the Sun family's saint could be said to have been personally cultivated by him, it did not prevent him from creating a natural nemesis for the other party.

As for when this future supreme demon god, who only had the desire for revenge left, would be released, or even whether she would be released at all.

It was still an unknown.

Everything was just a casual backup he left.

"In this world, many preparations have already been made. Next, we just need to wait for the good show to start."

"Judging from the personality of the Holy Emperor and the current comparison of the situation between the two sides, the first battle between the two sides won't be too far away."

Three months later, the first confrontation between the Holy Dynasty and the Wanxian Alliance took the stage in a way that no one had anticipated.

The origin of the conflict, however, was due to a sword light that came from outside the sky.

That day, golden light was like a rainbow, shining in the sky.

On the other side of the starry sea, under the high wall, the Xuanshang Immortal Boat, floating and crossing tribulations.

In the seven-colored rainbow light that could be seen throughout the Xuanhuang Realm, sixteen characters shone brightly.

It seemed to be a peculiar form of information dissemination. Even if someone from the Wanxian Alliance tried to remove it, it had no effect at all.

They could only let this rainbow light float quietly in the sky for seven days before it slowly faded away.

However, the shock brought by these sixteen characters was far from subsiding.

Instead, it showed signs of intensifying.

Shang Shaojun recalled these sixteen characters and was in a daze.

"I knew he wasn't dead. On the other side of the starry sea, under the high wall..."

"Has he gone this far?"

The carefree and unrestrained appearance of the Holy Fetus Li Fan appeared in Shang Shaojun's mind again, making him feel a little nostalgia and a hint of loss.

"Outside the Xuanhuang Realm."

"If I had abandoned everything and gone with him back then, what kind of situation would it be now?"

After being in a daze for a while, Shang Shaojun finally came back to his senses and started doing proper things.

He began to send messages to people like Di Yijinglun and Ji Hongdao.

"Have you seen the rainbow in the sky these past few days? That is exactly the white-clothed sword immortal I introduced to you before. You said that I exaggerated a lot in my words back then, how about now?"

After showing off with satisfaction, Shang Shaojun also inquired about the amount of Kongming Flowing Crystal in his private treasury.

Although the Wanxian Alliance had long listed it as a prohibited item for circulation, in fact, all the major families of the Immortal Alliance had more or less secretly hidden it.

Previously, this item had been gradually forgotten, but with the appearance of the seven-day rainbow light, Kongming Flowing Crystal once again returned to the public eye.

The world didn't know how exactly the Holy Fetus Li Fan left the Xuanhuang Realm.

So they instinctively guessed that the method he used was the same as the sixteen Dao-achievers who had left the Wanxian Alliance before.

The sixteen Dao-achievers went into the depths of the starry sea for some unknown reason, but the information conveyed by the rainbow light made it clear.

On the other side of the starry sea, in a place called the High Wall, there is an immortal boat called Xuanshang.

This Xuanshang Immortal Boat has supreme dharma, and can float and cross tribulations!

Just sixteen short words, but it made countless cultivators' minds sway and their imaginations run wild, unable to control themselves.

The cleverness of this message from the Holy Fetus Li Fan was in its vagueness.

It fully exerted the subjective initiative of the Xuanhuang cultivators, making the Xuanxian boat in their imagination more and more mysterious.

Some cultivators even searched through ancient books and found records about the Xuanshang Immortal Boat from ancient times.

"Being able to travel and shuttle between various realms for trade, and after so many years, it must be much more developed than our Xuanhuang Realm that is full of hardships!"

Sun Family.

The high-level of the Myriad Immortal Alliance has already agreed to the plan of recreating the Kongming Crystals. This time, the number of cultivators who can leave Xuanhuang and enter the starry space may be even more than before.

Completely disregarding the feelings of those grassroots Harmonious Dao cultivators who worked hard to suppress the various states.

Now that these people have united, even the Dharma Transmitters have some headaches.
