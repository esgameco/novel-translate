Chapter 397

Exploring opportunities is not without risks.

If they were to go together, the whole army would eventually be wiped out.

If the inheritance of 【Fu Xian】 ceases here.

This is the biggest concern in Xiao Heng's heart.

However, Su Changyu also noticed that, except for Xiao Heng, the others present did not attach much importance to the so-called 【Fu Xian】 inheritance, at least in comparison to their own cultivation levels.

In Xiao Heng's heart, he might also understand this.

However, he has his principles in his heart, but does not impose them on others.

So, he couldn't say it directly.

Although Su Changyu's eyes were closed all along, he could see through everyone's thoughts in the room.

So he took the initiative to resolve the situation: "How about this, Xiao Heng, Little Sister, and Zhang Haobo, you all go together to Cangwu Abyss."

"As for me, I'll stay here."

Before Su Xiaomei could retort, he continued explaining, "The cultivation technique I practice is special. In fact, opportunities are not of much use to me."

"So you don't need to worry about me."

Upon hearing this, Xiao Heng cast a grateful look at Su Changyu.

Su Xiaomei, however, turned her head stubbornly, not looking at Su Changyu.

But she still believed in Su Changyu's words.

Now that he had made a decision, the matter was almost settled.

"So we'll have one more Small Medicine King Cauldron. Could it be that, as Senior said, we should sell it?" Zhang Haobo furrowed his brows and asked.

"No." Su Changyu shook his head.

"Holding the Small Medicine King Cauldron is already a crime. If we expose our knowledge of this medicine king's true cauldron for a small gain, it would be unwise."

"It's better to keep it and throw it out at a critical moment as a distraction to divert the enemy's attention."

Xiao Heng also agreed with Su Changyu's words.

"Although it's a bit of a pity, this is the safest method."

"Regardless! Since you have the final say!" Su Xiaomei snorted and stopped objecting.

She grabbed Han Wuyu and went outside to play.

"To go to Cangwu Abyss, the journey ahead is long. We are not cultivators of the Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance, so we cannot rely on transmission arrays."

"If we fly with our physical bodies, it will take too long, and there might be unexpected incidents along the way."

"We need to come up with a good solution..."

Xiao Heng stroked his chin, furrowing his brows.

"Um..." Zhang Haobo hesitated.

"What?" Xiao Heng looked at Zhang Haobo with some surprise.

"Perhaps we can also use a transmission array," Zhang Haobo explained.

"You know, during our adventure in the Jiushan Province before, we met Senior Shaofeng."

"During the trip to the Chao Yuan Sect, we fought our way out of the sea of corpses and blood together."

Our relationship is deep."

"When parting, Senior Shaofeng invited us to join the Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance, although we politely declined."

"But he understood and left a jade card for Little Sister."

"With that jade card, we should be able to use the transmission array without any problems."

Upon hearing this, Xiao Heng nodded, saying, "That's just perfect."

He thought that Zhang Haobo's hesitation earlier was because Shaofeng was a cultivator of the Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance.

Xiao Heng said seriously, "I am also a flexible person. Our enemies are the Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance, not every member of the alliance."

"If we can make friends with cultivators of the Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance and unravel them from within, it would benefit us without harm."

"Why would I object?"

"I'll go talk to Little Sister about it."

With that, Xiao Heng left to find Su Xiaomei.

Zhang Haobo watched Xiao Heng's back and shook his head helplessly.

"Why, is there something between Little Sister and Shaofeng?" Su Changyu noticed Zhang Haobo's intentions and asked with interest.

"Who knows, but it seems there's a spark between them. Although Shaofeng has returned to the realm of Nascent Divinity, he doesn't mind Little Sister's cultivation level."

Su Changyu chuckled, "You worry too much. In my opinion, there may not be any romantic feelings in Little Sister's heart."

Zhang Haobo shook his head in silence.

...

After many years, those few kids from the beginning seemed to have grown up.

But there were still some things that hadn't changed.

Li Fan couldn't help but feel a bit sentimental, suddenly recalling his children from his past lives.

"I've really gotten old," he thought to himself.

Soon after, Li Fan quickly suppressed these emotions.

...

Meanwhile, Xiao Heng spent a lot of effort to convince Su Xiaomei to agree and hand over Shaofeng's jade card.

After a few days of preparation, Xiao Heng, Su Xiaomei, and Zhang Haobo set out on the road to Cangwu Province.

Han Wuyu, with his big eyes full of envy, waved his hands to bid them farewell.

Su Changyu's eyelids twitched violently, as if he wanted to open his eyes.

But his face remained calm and composed.

Until the end, he never opened his heavy eyes.

Shaofeng's jade card worked wonders.

Even though they were not cultivators of the Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance, Xiao Heng and the others were able to enter the Stone Immortal City smoothly.

Xiao Heng held onto Su Xiaomei, who was very curious and wanted to explore everything.

Feeling a bit nervous, he activated the transmission array.

They were teleported to the border of the Stone Immortal Province, crossed the White Mist Barrier, and arrived at the Source Dao Province.

Continuously using transmission arrays, they rushed to the Cangwu Abyss in the southwest corner of the Xuanhuang Realm.

And so, after nearly half a month.

As Li Fan began inspecting the Tianxuan Locking Spirit Formation in Lanlin Province, Xiao Heng and his group finally arrived at their destination.

With everyone's eyes, Li Fan also finally got to see the long-awaited Cangwu Abyss.

Unlike the completely vanished Abyss of Clouds from before.

This Cangwu Abyss more resembled a vast sinkhole of the Great Mystery.

Beneath the towering cliffs was an endless deep valley.

Above the deep valley, red-colored clouds swirled, with no end in sight.

Many flying birds and strange beasts soared through the clouds.

On the cliff walls, at intervals heading downward, there were many buildings built into the mountains and deep into the mountain's interior.

"Cangwu Abyss was formed when all the mountains and waters in the Cangwu Province disappeared many years ago."

"Hence, the Abyss has its boundaries, shaped like a foot."

"It has been explored to a depth of about three hundred thousand zhang, yet the bottom is still not visible."

"The Cangwu Abyss is not empty, but each layer has its own features."

"The first layer, from the surface to thirty thousand zhang below, is where many strange beasts inhabit the Cangwu clouds, the Abyss at the boundary, and the cliff walls."

"These beasts can swallow gold and spit out lightning, and their strength is formidable. They are fierce and unafraid of death, so beware."

"The second layer, from thirty thousand zhang to eighty thousand zhang underground."

Su Xiaomei read aloud from the jade slip given by the cultivators outside Cangwu Abyss, her eyes widened.

"So big, so deep."

"Even if the Medicine King True Cauldron is really inside this Cangwu Abyss, we won't be able to find it, right?" she transmitted.

But Xiao Heng felt a stir in his heart.

He manipulated the Small Medicine King Cauldron in his storage ring to sense.
