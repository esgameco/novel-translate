Chapter 1239: The Calamity of Sealing Immortals with Source Power

Back in the 119th lifetime, Li Fan had fabricated rumors about the so-called "True Immortal's Remnant Body". He used it as bait to lure the cultivators of the Myriad Immortal Alliance into the small world of the Life and Death Reversal Grand Formation, exposing the "Rebirth Venerable" prematurely to the world.

However, he never expected that there was indeed a true immortal's remnant body hidden within the Xuanhuang Realm.

Moreover, it was not an ordinary immortal.

"The immortal body of the first generation Grand Venerable?"

"Interesting."

Just this one sentence hinted at many secrets.

The Xuanhuang Grand Venerable did not refer to a specific cultivator, but represented a certain status and title.

Li Fan had already learned about this in the deepest part of the Fallen Immortal Realm.

"My guess back then was correct. As long as one passes the trial of the Fallen Immortal Realm, they should be able to succeed as the new Xuanhuang Grand Venerable. And it's very likely that they will also obtain the few immortal artifacts left by Mr. White at the same time."

"Then, where would Mr. White have hidden the immortal body of the first generation Grand Venerable? Would it still be in the Fallen Immortal Realm?"

Li Fan's thoughts raced.

He was particularly intrigued by this first generation Grand Venerable.

If the remnant body could be called an immortal body, there was no doubt that this Grand Venerable had at least reached the True Immortal realm.

Why did this True Immortal die? Before his fall, why did he choose to leave his remnant body in the Xuanhuang Realm?

Would the birth of the Fallen Immortal Realm have anything to do with this True Immortal Grand Venerable?

Now, the avatar Holy Emperor had already stood at the pinnacle of the Xuanhuang Realm.

Taking into account the inherent expansion of the Xuanhuang Realm over these years, the Holy Emperor's strength could be said to be only a hair's breadth away from Mr. White's back then.

With a concrete reference, Li Fan was now certain that Mr. White alone did not have the power to create the vast and illusory Fallen Immortal Realm.

But during the era of the Ten Immortal Sects, the world was unaware of the existence of the Fallen Immortal Realm.

So it could be confirmed that even if it was not created by Mr. White himself, the Fallen Immortal Realm must have had a great connection to Mr. White.

The several layers of illusory trials undoubtedly proved this point.

"Although Mr. White 'betrayed' the Xuanhuang Dao, he did not break his word. The immortal artifacts and immortal body were all returned to the Xuanhuang Dao. Even his cultivation was returned to the heavens along with his demise in the process of reversing the mortal and immortal principles."

Li Fan pondered silently, but did not have much intention to go searching for the Grand Venerable's immortal body.

Although the Grand Venerable's immortal body could be considered a supreme treasure, it was of little use to Li Fan at present. Even if he could rely on the protection of the immortal body to cross the high wall, Li Fan would never rashly leave without knowing anything about what lay beyond the high wall.

Not to mention, Li Fan's current realm was still far from being able to utilize immortal spiritual energy.

Just how to detonate the immortal artifacts and use their explosive power to cross the high wall without harming himself was not something Li Fan knew.

"I still need to take it step by step. Anyway, as long as it's within the Xuanhuang Realm or the scope of the Darkest Starry Sea, it will all be mine sooner or later."

Li Fan had a very clear understanding of his advantages.

"The Holy Emperor avatar of this lifetime already has sufficient capital to contend with the Myriad Immortal Alliance. Now, with the boost in strength from various enhancements, he has even surpassed me. Let's see how he stirs up the wind and rain."

"However, my avatar left in the Sun family can cultivate a future Grand Venerable."

His attention shifted in an instant.

The avatar had now completely taken over control of Wu Ninglu's body.

But it did not let her notice.

It just silently influenced her every decision and action.

Most importantly, it did not hesitate to sacrifice its own cultivation to nourish the fetus in her womb.

If it were the previous Wu Ninglu, she definitely would not have done this.

She only regarded this unborn child as a bargaining chip to control Sun Ang.

But now with Li Fan's intervention, things were naturally different.

Since the avatar had previously sensed the extraordinary nature of this fetus, it had begun meticulous prenatal education.

Having experienced the cultivation stage before birth on the body of the little black heavenly bird in the Fallen Immortal Realm, Li Fan had quite rich experience.

Under his personal guidance, the fetus absorbed the essence of the mother's cultivation, gradually making up for the innate deficiency caused by the previous seal.

Its physique grew stronger day by day, and its consciousness slowly stabilized from being weak.

In a state of complete ignorance, not knowing anything, it was led by Li Fan's avatar into one carefully crafted illusory realm after another.

"It is said that a sage is born. But in fact, it is not impossible to create a sage through acquired means."

"The so-called judging by actions, not by heart. No matter what one thinks in their heart, as long as they always have strict moral requirements and codes of conduct for themselves, and will not break them even unto death..."

"Then in the eyes of others, this is a true sage."

Cultivating from a state of innate ignorance was definitely much easier than acquired education.

What Li Fan was doing now was more like shaping the personality of a living being.

He did not have any special experience and could only explore through practice. If this proved feasible, then forget about sages, mass-producing gentlemen would definitely not be a problem.

Li Fan had already discovered that although the Xuanhuang Dao had been deceived by Mr. White once before, it still trusted people like Mr. White more easily.

Just like the faceless Holy Emperor of this lifetime.

"And the fundamental reason probably traces back to Xuantian King Xuanyuan Hong, and even the first Xuanhuang Grand Venerable." Li Fan mused.

The birth of the fetus in Wu Ninglu's body had already been planned by Li Fan to coincide with her devouring the Worldly Souls and advancing to the Harmonized Dao realm.

"Perhaps, there is even a possibility of giving birth to the first cultivator in the Xuanhuang Realm who is born in the Harmonized Dao realm..."

"With such an extraordinary phenomenon, who would dare say he is not a sage?"

Thinking like this, Li Fan couldn't help but have some expectations for the future of this fetus.

He had even composed the script of its destiny in advance.

"Such a heaven's pride will definitely be seen as the future hope of the Myriad Immortal Alliance. And it is such a figure who, when the Great Sheng Dynasty and the Myriad Immortal Alliance are at war in full swing, resolutely chooses to turn his weapon around."

"And firmly claims to be standing on the side of justice."

"The scene of all the people in the Xuanhuang Realm being forced to pass on the teachings will surely be very interesting."

Vivid images flashed through Li Fan's mind.

Without the aid of the Dao-Transforming Stone to deduce, he could already roughly predict the trajectory of this lifetime.

However, this was the ideal state.
