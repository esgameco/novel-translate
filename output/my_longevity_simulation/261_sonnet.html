Chapter 261

Here is my translation of the Chinese excerpt to English:

Li Fan remained motionless in the Heavenly Mystery Mirror, calmly awaiting the result.

Three months later, there was still no particular progress in the exploration of the ruins.

However, He Zhenghao excitedly sent a message first.

"Daoist friend Li Fan, good news."

"After some effort on my part, I have secured three potential positions in different states."

"Daoist friend, please advise me."

Li Fan immediately replied: "Oh? Let me hear it."

"The first is the leader of the Martial Arts Hall in Taihua State."

"Taihua State is not far from the Cong Yun Sea. There is the Taihua Mountain Range running across the entire state, also known as the 'state on the mountain ridge'."

"With towering peaks too numerous to count, my cultivation speed there would be much faster compared to the Cong Yun Sea."

"The second is a staff officer in the Strategy Formation Hall of Qingwei State."

"Although Qingwei State is still quite far south of Tianyu State, it's much closer compared to the Cong Yun Sea."

"The third is the garrison commander of Tian Yun State."

"This Tian Yun State is not far west of Tianyu State. Therefore, the competition for this position is extremely fierce."

"Being transferred there would just be a lateral move to a city garrison commander position."

"The advantage is being closer to the headquarters of the Myriad Immortal Alliance, so promotions would be easier than other places."

He Zhenghao slowly laid out the results of his efforts during this period.

"Tian Yun State..."

Li Fan pondered for a moment, then replied: "Don't make a decision hastily. I have an acquainted cultivator in Tian Yun State, let me inquire further to see if we can advance this."

Hearing this, He Zhenghao was very surprised: "Daoist friend, you even have connections in Tian Yun State?"

"Hehe, I can't guarantee it. Let me try first, Daoist friend wait for my message." Li Fan said calmly.

He Zhenghao repeatedly agreed.

So Li Fan contacted Shen Yuzi and explained the situation.

"Senior, do you have a way?"

Shen Yuzi only replied: "I'll ask around for you."

And there was no further response.

Li Fan was not in a hurry either, patiently waiting.

Half a day later, a cultivator named Jing Xuan Daoren requested to add Li Fan as a friend.

He was also from Tian Yun State.

Li Fan's heart stirred and he quickly accepted.

Jing Xuan's hearty voice came through.

"Hahaha, is this young friend Li Fan? I heard from Shen Yu that you have a friend who wants to take up a position in our Tian Yun State?"

"The Four Dings Golden Dan, tsk tsk, a formidable junior!"

"So, ask him if he's interested in joining our Tian Yun State's Garrison Institute?"

"Hehe, because of that great battle a few years ago, there are still some vacant positions that haven't been filled."

Li Fan was slightly taken aback and couldn't help asking: "Senior Jing Xuan, I don't know what this Garrison Institute is..."

Jing Xuan was also stunned.

He didn't expect Li Fan to be completely unaware of what the Garrison Institute was.

But he soon realized that Li Fan came from a remote place, and understood, explaining: "The Garrison Institute is a unique organization in the heartland of the Myriad Immortal Alliance."

"It reports directly to the headquarters of the Myriad Immortal Alliance. Its main responsibility is to suppress demonic cultivators and guard the stability of the heartland."

"When necessary, it will also heed emergency orders to go to other states for battle."

With Jing Xuan's explanation, Li Fan understood.

It was essentially the same as the garrison troops stationed outside Xuanjing City under Da Xuan's command before, only taking orders from Li Fan to guard the safety of the city.

"It should be considered an important position."

"However, it seems to require frequent combat. I don't know if He Zhenghao's current temperament would be willing to accept it."

Li Fan pondered for a moment, bid farewell to Jing Xuan, and then contacted He Zhenghao again to explain the situation.

"The Garrison Institute?" This time, He Zhenghao was truly shocked.

In his youth, he had stayed in Tianyu State for a while, so he naturally understood what the Garrison Institute was.

Just like the Law Enforcement Hall, it was an organization that took orders directly from the headquarters of the Myriad Immortal Alliance.

Only below the Law Enforcement Hall.

"Daoist friend Li Fan is truly unfathomable, actually able to secure this position."

"Although it's a bit dangerous, but..."

An elegant figure flashed through He Zhenghao's mind.

He immediately gritted his teeth and agreed.

After learning of He Zhenghao's determination, Li Fan also replied to Daoren Jing Xuan.

"Good!"Jing Xuan was very pleased.

"Have your friend come to our Tian Yun State first as a garrison commander."

"After three years of service and accumulating experience, I will pull some strings to have him transferred into the Garrison Institute."

Next, Jing Xuan carefully gave some additional reminders before ending the communication.

Li Fan relayed everything to He Zhenghao word for word.

After carefully listening, He Zhenghao hurriedly made preparations for his future.

The tranquil Li Fan thought for a moment. Although the matter was not settled yet, he still transferred 100,000 contribution points to Shen Yuzi and Jing Xuan each as a token of gratitude.

Of course, he recorded this expense under He Zhenghao's name.

......

In Tian Yun State.

Upon receiving the contribution points, Shen Yuzi was slightly taken aback and laughed wryly.

Jing Xuan immediately sent a message inquiring: "Old Shen Yu, did you receive Li's transfer of contribution points? 100,000 contribution points, I remember he's only at the Foundation Establishment realm. What a generous hand!"

Shen Yuzi smiled and replied: "At least he's more generous than us when we were at the Foundation Establishment realm."

"I only passed on a message, yet received the same reward as you, Jing Xuan. Aren't you upset?"

Jing Xuan laughed heartily: "Hahaha, sharing the water evenly, this little kid is quite interesting."

"Not bad, not bad. I find him more likable than old Guangwu."

"Messing it up? Looking down on who?" Jing Xuan snorted coldly, then fell silent.

......

Time passed.

Three months later, He Zhenghao's transfer order came through.

There were no issues.

Garrison Commander of Guangan Town, Tian Yun State.

For this, a total of 80,000 contribution points were spent, front and back.

Naturally, it was borrowed from Li Fan.

Of course, He Zhenghao had no qualms about being in debt, feeling no mental burden.

Li Fan was also very generous, lending as much as needed.

Before departing, He Zhenghao vowed solemnly.

After establishing himself in Tian Yun State, he would try to arrange for Li Fan to be transferred there as well.

Li Fan didn't say much, only patting He Zhenghao's shoulder.

After He Zhenghao left, Li Fan remained in the Heavenly Mystery Mirror every day, never going out, diligently cultivating.

Soon, time came to the anchored 23rd year.

That soaring ruin was finally discovered.

However, it had landed in a vast marshland in the center of Jiu Shan State.

When cultivators began exploring it, the origins of this ruin were also uncovered.

The ancient Immortal Sect, Zhaoyuan Sect.

Although not as renowned as sects like Taoist or Dadao, whose fame was still known to all even thousands of years later.

Before the great catastrophe, it was still a famous major sect.

Based on past experience exploring sect ruins, this place must contain abundant treasures.

When the news spread, it immediately attracted many cultivators to venture to Jiu Shan State for exploration.

The already chaotic Jiu Shan State became even more tumultuous.

The extent of arrogance was quite evident.

It also showed how weak the Myriad Immortal Alliance's control over Jiu Shan State was.

Originally, it was just a normal ruin exploration, which the Myriad Immortal Alliance didn't intend to interfere with much.

But at this time, a piece of news came that enraged the Myriad Immortal Alliance.

One of the Five Elders Council's Celestial Immortals named Luo Xi came to explore the ruins.

Not only did he make no attempt to conceal his identity as a Five Elders cultivator, he even took advantage of his profound cultivation to injure and kill many Myriad Immortal Alliance explorers.

After obtaining some unknown treasures deep in the ruins, he left arrogantly.

Doing this so brazenly on our turf?

It was simply a slap in the face.

However, doubts also arose.

Such conduct clearly had provocative intentions.

Could the Five Elders Council be trying to start another war?

Regardless, they could not lose face.

The Myriad Immortal Alliance swiftly dispatched several Celestial Immortals to Jiu Shan State to take command.

And issued a stern warning.

Prohibiting any Five Elders Council cultivators from entering Jiu Shan State.

If they dared to come, it would mean death!
