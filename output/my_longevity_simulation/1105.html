Chapter 1103: The Reincarnation Rivers of Xuanhuang

Changji, disregarding the strange looks from the crowd, respectfully bowed towards the sky.

Then, he transformed into a beam of light and flew away.

The words he spoke before leaving immediately caught the attention of the cultivators who were interested.

After some investigation, people finally discovered that this Changji was supposed to have died completely a year ago.

During the exploration of a wild sect's ruins, he unfortunately triggered a protective formation and died on the spot, leaving no intact corpse.

More than a dozen people who were with him at the time could testify to this, and it could not be faked!

And now, this Changji had actually been resurrected!

The place of resurrection happened to be the nearest large river to the place where he had fallen.

This somewhat horrifying discovery quickly spread throughout the entire Wanxian Alliance in an instant.

Soon, Changji's friends from his previous life came to find him one after another, wanting to confirm the truth of his disappearance.

Changji himself did not deny it, but openly admitted that he had been resurrected.

"Thanks to the divine power of the Celestial Venerable!" He did not forget to say very devoutly in front of everyone.

In the past, when cultivators in the Wanxian Alliance spoke of the Celestial Venerable, they must have been referring to the Dharma-transmitting Celestial Venerable.

But at this moment, everyone knew that the one Changji spoke of was the Longevity Celestial Venerable who had reversed life and death quietly.

Seeing that his friends and relatives were still somewhat skeptical, Changji directly invited everyone to enter his Tianxuan Mirror space.

As everyone knows, the Tianxuan Mirror space is bound to the cultivator, and only the cultivator himself can open it.

With the Tianxuan Mirror as proof, everyone finally completely believed in Changji's true identity.

To die and be reborn, and it was definitely not an isolated case.

This shocking news soon became known to everyone.

And in the following days, the appearance of more and more cultivators who had died and been reborn was also a verification of its authenticity.

After summarizing, on the side of the Wanxian Alliance, the cultivators who were resurrected invariably reappeared from the nearest large river to their place of death.

In the territory of the Immortal Alliance, there were fifty-four reincarnation rivers.

As for the Five Elders Council's side, there seemed to be a corresponding fifty-four as well.

These one hundred and eight rivers in total constituted the reincarnation rivers of the Xuanhuang Realm.

The length of time it took for cultivators to be resurrected seemed to be related to their strength during their previous lives.

The stronger the realm, the longer it took to be resurrected.

The first batch of resurrected cultivators were all, without exception, only at the Foundation Establishment stage and below.

As for how long it would take for cultivators of higher realms to be resurrected, it would need to be slowly verified in the future.

After resurrection, they always appeared naked. The items carried on the body before death, even those hidden in the soul, would not appear together. However, it was already a great blessing to be able to get back a life. No one paid too much attention to these external possessions.

However, after detailed measurements, it was found that the resurrected body seemed to be one year younger than before the fall.

Not only cultivators but all living beings within the Xuanhuang Realm would be reborn from the river after death.

Therefore, people could always see the wonder of creatures constantly coming ashore from the water in the reincarnation rivers.

There seemed to be a miraculous power in the river water that protected the newly resurrected creatures from drowning in the river water as soon as they were resurrected.

But this power disappeared quickly after leaving the reincarnation rivers. Even if one re-entered the river water, one could not find its traces again.

...

The various wonders of death and rebirth became the hottest topic of discussion among the cultivators of the Wanxian Alliance.

Some people were grateful that there would be no more worries about falling in the future.
