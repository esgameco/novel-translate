Chapter 924

"Bastard!"

Huo Jingtong was extremely annoyed.

Before she left, she had repeatedly warned him to behave himself.

She never thought that this unfilial disciple would not only disobey her, but also become even more unscrupulous and bring disaster to all the disciples on Liuyue Peak!

If this were to spread...

Thinking about the consequences of this matter being leaked, Huo Jingtong felt faint.

"I can't indulge him anymore, I must teach him a harsh lesson!"

With this thought, Huo Jingtong gathered one-tenth of her full power in her right hand and struck towards Li Qing.

The moment she made her move, Huo Jingtong felt a little regretful.

After all, she was a Hua Shen cultivator, and even one-tenth of her power was not something that Li Qing, who had just entered the Yuan Ying stage, could withstand.

If he was injured...

Just as Huo Jingtong was thinking about holding back some of her power, something shocking happened.

A crescent moon rose from behind Li Qing, illuminating his already extraordinary appearance, making him look even more like a heavenly being.

And the moonlight that Huo Jingtong had struck out was actually easily blocked by Li Qing!

"How is this possible! The moon is half visible, this is a sign of mastering the 'Moonlight Transformation'! Only those in the late Yuan Ying stage can achieve this! How long have I been away?" Huo Jingtong's heart was filled with surprise and joy, her blood surging.

This triggered the internal injuries in her body, causing her face to turn pale and she spat out a mouthful of fresh blood.

Seeing this, Li Qing flashed to Huo Jingtong's side and supported her shoulders. His face showed anxiety and concern: "Master, how did you get injured?"

"Let me heal you!"

"Hmph, I, a mighty Hua Shen cultivator, still need..." Before Huo Jingtong could finish her words, she let out a moan and was forced to stop.

Feeling the pure Yin energy coming from Li Qing's hot palms, Huo Jingtong felt as if her whole body was instantly covered in ants, numb and itchy.

However, she was after all a Hua Shen cultivator, and quickly suppressed the strangeness in her heart. More than that, she was shocked by the pure energy that her disciple was continuously transmitting into her body.

After entering, this pure Yin energy seemed to act as a catalyst, stirring up waves in her cultivated moonlight power. After intertwining like overturning rivers and seas, the pure Yin energy completely merged into the vast moonlight power.

And the moonlight power, which Huo Jingtong had thought was already close to perfection, actually became even purer after this fusion. It even faintly carried a hint of profound Dao aura!

This was a sign of breaking through to the He Dao realm!

Words could not describe the shock and confusion in Huo Jingtong's heart at this moment.

A thought even arose in her mind, that it would be great if Li Qing's transmission of pure Yin energy would never stop.

Amidst the interweaving of the two powers, accompanied by the gradual rise of her cultivation that had been stagnant for a long time, Huo Jingtong's mind slowly became lost.

Who knows how long had passed...

Li Qing released his palms pressed against Huo Jingtong's back.

"Master..."

Huo Jingtong came back to her senses, and a blush instantly surged on her cheeks.

"My injury is healed, you go out first."

Feeling a little flustered, Huo Jingtong said repeatedly.

"Then I'm relieved." Li Qing breathed a sigh of relief and left with his hands clasped.

Huo Jingtong watched Li Qing quickly retreat, and couldn't help but feel a trace of annoyance in her heart.

......

From this day onwards, there was a different affection spreading between the master and disciple.

But Li Qing did not directly point it out, and always maintained the appearance of a well-behaved disciple who respected his master in front of Huo Jingtong.

And Li Qing seemed to have no thoughts about his own master at all, every move was extremely proper.

In this way, two years passed in a flash.

At the top of Liuyue Peak, in the closed-door room.

Huo Jingtong looked nervously at Li Qing in front of her, surrounded by a vast spiritual power, her heart filled with complex feelings.

"In just over two years, he's going to break through to the Demigod realm."

"This kind of cultivation speed is simply unheard of."

"If he successfully breaks through, won't his cultivation be equal to mine? If he forces himself on me..."

Huo Jingtong's mind was in a mess.

Then suddenly it disappeared.

"He really succeeded?!" Huo Jingtong looked at Li Qing, her mind in a daze.

"Thank you Master for protecting me during my cultivation!" Li Qing bowed with his hands clasped, genteel and refined.

But Huo Jingtong turned away, avoiding Li Qing's bow.

According to the rules of the Great Dao Sect, as long as the disciple's cultivation catches up to or surpasses the master's, they can henceforth address each other as peers.

Of course, if one respects the teacher and values the Dao, and does not distinguish their status based on strength, the master-disciple relationship can still be maintained.

Li Qing smiled slightly: "Even if one day I attain immortality, in my heart, Master, you will always be my master. My only master."

Hearing this, Huo Jingtong's body trembled slightly.

Looking at Li Qing's sincere face, she couldn't help but feel touched.

"Let's go. Liuyue Peak now has another Demigod cultivator, such a joyous occasion should be shared with others soon." Li Qing walked up and gently held Huo Jingtong's flawless white hand.

Huo Jingtong struggled symbolically, but did not break free.

So she stopped resisting.

On Liuyue Peak, there was a burst of indulgence.

And when passion ran deep, even Huo Jingtong couldn't escape the mundane in the end.

At night, after everyone else had fallen into deep sleep.

Li Qing, feeling the vast Yin energy absorbed from Huo Jingtong's body, which he had never experienced before, nodded with satisfaction.

"The feedback from a Demigod cultivator is indeed extraordinary."

"This vast Yin essence is enough to support me in rapidly cultivating in the Demigod realm for a while."

"However, it's still not enough!"

With his soaring strength, Li Qing's ambition also swelled.

He began to set his sights on the other female cultivators of the Moonlight Lineage.

"Only the Immortal realm is my ultimate pursuit."

Under the shroud of moonlight, Li Qing's shadow was not revealed.

Only his face was hidden in the night, unclear to see.

The next day, after Huo Jingtong woke up, she pulled Li Qing to go into seclusion together.

The bottleneck that had trapped and troubled her for many years finally loosened a bit after last night's impact.

Huo Jingtong had a feeling that her advancement to the Harmonious Dao realm was not far off.
