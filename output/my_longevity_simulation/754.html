The old man seemed extremely aged, as if he could die at any moment.

However, his movements were anything but vague - they were ruthless and cruel.

His beard and hair were like living creatures, suddenly lashing out to injure people. They were like silver dragons that instantly pierced through the bodies of the Immortal Alliance cultivators.

In an instant, countless cultivators rapidly aged, and some couldn't even let out a mournful cry before turning into mere skeletons. Meanwhile, the old man's face became a bit more ruddy.

The strength of this white-haired old man was at least at the Dao Realm level. Moreover, he seemed to have a special advantage in his small world, as ordinary Heavenly Immortal cultivators had absolutely no ability to resist him before their life force was completely drained.

This scene immediately aroused the great indignation of the Immortal Alliance cultivators.

"He can actually absorb the life span of cultivators? Such behavior is no different from that of an evil demon! Everyone must go and behead him!"

"Well said, fellow Daoist. I support you! Go quickly and cut off his head!"

"Tsk, I didn't expect there to be such a ruthless person in this small world. I don't think just a few Dao Realm cultivators can kill him."

"You fools, haven't you realized yet? Lu Zhixuan was the one who deliberately let him out, to act as a beacon and lure more cultivators to their deaths!"

"That makes sense. But if that's the case, why doesn't he just leave that small world and wreak havoc in the Xuanhuang Realm?"

"Because he's not as stupid as you."

Although this sparked intense discussion, the Immortal Alliance cultivators were not fools. Since there was such a monster in that small world, they would simply go around it. There were countless undiscovered small worlds, so why bother fighting to the death with him?

And so, a dramatic scene unfolded. This small world was marked with a red skull on the map by the cultivators, but not a single one was willing to explore it further.

"In the end, the Immortal Alliance will definitely send people to clean it up."

The cultivators all held this mentality and simply ignored the small world.

The Immortal Alliance's official stance also seemed to tacitly approve of this behavior, as they did not issue any bounty or pursuit order for that small world.

"Interesting," Xu Bai murmured after investigating further. It seemed there was a dispute within the Immortal Alliance about how to handle this small world.

One faction believed the scoundrel should be swiftly eliminated to demonstrate the Immortal Alliance's supreme authority.

The other faction argued that in future conquests of worlds, they would surely encounter opponents of similar strength to this white-haired old man. If they had to eliminate every such threat, there would inevitably be casualties.

Dao Realm cultivators were the pillars of the Immortal Alliance. Losing even one was a huge loss. Even with the existence of the Reincarnation Pavilion, where they could be revived, the Dao Realm cultivators seemed to have a certain fear of "resurrection" and were not eager to be the first to sacrifice themselves.

After much effort, Xu Bai finally understood the reason behind this.

Apparently, even though there were various methods to revive cultivators and even preserve their previous cultivation and memories, there was a fatal flaw that all Dao Realm cultivators could not ignore.

That was, once they entered the Reincarnation Pavilion, they would forever lose the chance to reach the Eternal Life Realm.

The reason for this was unknown, but judging from the behavior of the Dao Realm experts, it must be a real and serious issue.

After all, which Dao Realm powerhouse didn't have the ambition to cultivate eternal life? Resurrection was merely their last resort.
