Chapter 961

Living in a remote mountain in Tongyan Prefecture, Li Fan spent quite some effort to find this place.

Outside the cave dwelling, Li Fan could faintly sense the bursts of gloomy aura emanating from within.

There were also many faint whispers circling around his ears.

Just like the rumored vengeful spirits.

But Li Fan was very clear that in the Xuanhuang Realm, there was no such thing as ghost cultivators.

In the vast majority of cases, when a cultivator perished, they would directly return to the Dao of Heaven.

Only in extremely rare situations could some remnant souls be preserved.

It was already difficult to retain complete memories and clear consciousness from when they were alive, let alone cultivate with a remnant soul body.

Of course, the bizarre beings of Yunshui Heavenly Palace who transformed their bodies were an exception.

They were not within the scope of ordinary discussion.

Li Fan snorted coldly, and the whispers by his ears instantly disappeared.

Striding into Luo Suzhen's cave dwelling, he saw that the ground was littered with human skeletons lying in all directions.

They were all arranged in different poses, giving the feeling that they were still alive.

A dwarf cultivator, whose height only reached Li Fan's knees, was solemnly conversing with one of the skeletons: "Third Brother, don't worry, I know you're not satisfied with the new wife I found for you."

"It's okay, I have already selected a new one for you again. You should also persuade Fourth Brother. It's already embarrassing enough that he didn't find a Dao companion when he was alive. Could it be that he still wants to be lonely for eternity after death..."

He rambled on and on, with a serious expression.

As if the dry bones in front of him were still alive.

Li Fan watched expressionlessly and lightly coughed, awakening the immersed dwarf cultivator.

"Huh? Who are you?" The dwarf cultivator looked up, seeming to try hard to see Li Fan's appearance clearly.

But Li Fan had already changed his appearance while also concealing himself.

The dwarf cultivator couldn't see clearly at all.

"Are you the cultivator who can reconstruct the appearance of the deceased based on their skull?" Li Fan explained his purpose.

The dwarf cultivator finally came to his senses and nodded repeatedly: "Yes, yes, that's me."

He rubbed his hands: "I've been waiting for you for a long time. You said that as long as I complete the reconstruction task, the reward will be at least 10,000 contribution points?"

Li Fan smiled slightly and nodded: "Don't worry, you won't be short-changed."

"However, are you really confident?" Li Fan lowered his head to size up the other party and asked again with a heavier tone.

Where Li Fan's gaze reached, the invisible pressure made the dwarf cultivator involuntarily shiver.

But he quickly patted his chest with a thump, guaranteeing: "I, Luo Suzhen, have been interacting with the dead since I was young. In my eyes, they are not just a bunch of skeletons at all! They are all living people! If I can't do it, I'm afraid there's no one else in this world who can!"

Only then did Li Fan slowly say: "Then let's begin."

He set up a formation around to seal off Luo Suzhen's cave dwelling.

At first, Luo Suzhen was still a bit absent-minded, still glancing from time to time at the skeletons placed in his cave dwelling.

But as the skull gradually took shape, Luo Suzhen's attention was slowly drawn in.

When the skull was completely formed, Luo Suzhen was completely stunned. He involuntarily approached the projection, with a dazed expression.

"Too perfect..."

"How can there be such a perfect skull structure in this world?" Luo Suzhen muttered to himself.

"Perfect?" Li Fan frowned slightly.

Thinking that Li Fan was questioning him, Luo Suzhen immediately explained excitedly: "Isn't this considered perfect?"

"Look at this frontal bone, this parietal bone, and this occipital bone..."

"Exquisite beyond words..."

The proportions and combinations of each other were so perfect."

"Simply, simply..."

After holding back for a long time, Luo Suzhen finally uttered eight words: "A natural and flawless perfection."

Li Fan slowly narrowed his eyes.

Without waiting for Li Fan's urging, Luo Suzhen eagerly took the initiative to restore the skull's appearance before death.

His method was also very bizarre, not through any secret technique.

Instead, he nervously attempted to start a conversation with the skull.

It was only a projection, naturally unable to respond to Luo Suzhen.

But Luo Suzhen seemed to have truly received a response, becoming more and more excited.

Just as Li Fan was having doubts about this, he saw Luo Suzhen waving his right hand in the air, beginning to depict the skull's appearance before death.

So Li Fan waited patiently again.

As the appearance was gradually restored, the expression on Li Fan's face also slowly turned solemn.

When the portrait finally appeared before Li Fan...

He tried hard to control the expression on his face, attempting to conceal the shock in his heart.

He stared at the image in the air for a long time.

Li Fan asked in a low voice, "Are you... sure you haven't made a mistake?"

As expected, Luo Suzhen's reaction was very agitated: "A mistake? You can question my..."

Before the other party could finish speaking, Li Fan took a deep breath, waved his sleeve, and sealed Luo Suzhen along with everything in the cave into the Five Elements Grotto-Heaven.

He then completely erased all traces of this place.

"How could it be him?"

Standing in the air for a long time, Li Fan still couldn't figure it out.

Unable to face this fact.

"The first cultivator infected with the Immortal-Mortal Miasma. The first cultivator infected with the Immortal-Mortal Miasma..."

Li Fan pondered for a long time, then flew off towards the Congyu Sea.

The target was naturally Yin Shangren, who was also conducting research on the Immortal-Mortal Miasma.

In Li Fan's perception, Yin Shangren's research still hadn't reached a final conclusion.

And Yin Shangren seemed to have sensed something, becoming a bit hesitant and apprehensive.

The frequency of experiments had clearly decreased.

Even...

There was a hint of reluctance to proceed with the final step of verification.

Speeding up to the fastest, Li Fan quickly arrived before Yin Shangren.

Yin Shangren was very surprised by Li Fan's sudden appearance.

Just as he was about to speak, Li Fan took the initiative to wave his hand, stopping him.

"Daoist Yin, regarding the first cultivator infected with the Immortal-Mortal Miasma..."

"I've made a shocking discovery."

Yin Shangren's whole body trembled violently as he looked at Li Fan in disbelief.

"This result is too incredible. It simply can't be explained. You should have had some premonition too, right?"

Yin Shangren was silent for a while, then nodded his head bitterly.

Li Fan took a deep breath and said in a deep voice, "Then, please begin the final verification."

"I also want to ask you to confirm it for me."

Motionless, after a long time, Yin Shangren sighed in defeat and led Li Fan to the front of the laboratory.

Through the transparent outer wall, Li Fan could vaguely see the white rib that Yin Shangren possessed placed inside.

As thick white miasma continuously sprayed out from the surroundings, filling the entire area.

Yin Shangren continuously made hand seals, his expression changing.

Under his manipulation, the fog slowly thinned.

Slowly, it seemed as if something in the air flowed into that white rib.

When the transparent area was completely emptied, Yin Shangren, who had confirmed his guess, turned pale and collapsed to the ground.

"So it is like this, so it is like this."

He muttered.

Li Fan then slowly stated the answer.
