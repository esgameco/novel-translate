"...and what other surprises will it bring me..."

Li Fan couldn't help but secretly look forward to it in his heart.

His mind was immersed in fantasy, not knowing how long had passed.

However, there was a movement from a demon god thought that came from the "Three Disasters and Hundred Tribulations Divine Method".

"Hmm?"

Li Fan quickly located that demon god.

It occupied a Yuanying stage cultivator named Feng Qiran.

This person was cautious in his actions. Since obtaining this "Divine Possession" secret technique, he had never revealed it in front of others.

He only practiced in seclusion.

But some things cannot be avoided just by being careful.

On this day, Feng Qiran brushed shoulders with a young cultivator in green robes.

A moment later, he was horrified to find that he had lost control of his body.

He couldn't even release his demon god avatar.

That young cultivator in green robes carefully observed Feng Qiran, and actually revealed a look of being torn between laughter and tears.

Then, without saying a word, a thread that was like an abyss, and also like a bright mirror, appeared between Feng Qiran and the young cultivator.

Under the illumination of the obscure light, it didn't take long for that young cultivator to become exactly the same as Feng Qiran!

Reaching out his hands towards the inexplicably shocked Feng Qiran, the thread exploded with a bang.

At the same time, through the demon god left in his body by Li Fan, Feng Qiran realized a reverse connection with Li Fan.

There were no formal words, only a sinister and mysterious power that invaded Li Fan's sea of consciousness along the invisible connection in the void.

Li Fan had long sensed the abnormality, how could he not take precautions?

In an instant, thousands of demon gods appeared.

Gloomy ghosts and demons descended into the sea of consciousness, and five strange blood moons appeared in the sky. Li Fan revealed a ferocious face, staring at Feng Qiran who was surrounded by countless demon gods below.

"How dare you invade my sea of consciousness..."

"You're really seeking death!"

"Hehehe..."

Li Fan laughed wildly, and immediately wanted to launch an attack.

He had already seen that the Feng Qiran in front of him was actually Moru Bin, the Dharma King of the Xuantian Sect.

He didn't know why he had changed his identity to a young Dharma transmitter and went out alone.

However, since he hadn't revealed his identity at this time, it wouldn't hurt to test the Dharma King of the Xuantian Sect first.

Without mercy, thousands of ghosts and gods roared and sprayed black rays of light towards Moru Bin.

Those were all physical manifestations of consciousness attacks.

It was not only Li Fan's power alone, but also the drawn and borrowed power of consciousness from countless cultivators who practiced the "Divine Possession" technique.

Coupled with Li Fan's own extraordinary strength of consciousness...

Li Fan believed that even if it was the Eternal Venerable on the opposite side, he would be severely injured!

"Wait a moment..."

As expected, before Moru Bin could finish speaking, his consciousness avatar was devoured by the terrifying destructive power.

Unable to even resist for a moment, it was immediately annihilated and expelled from Li Fan's sea of consciousness.

Very satisfied with his battle results, although there was a bit of a sneak attack flavor, after all, he had directly repelled the invasion of an Eternal realm expert.

Feeling the fatigue from the thousands of demon gods inhabiting the cultivators, Li Fan couldn't help but feel a little regretful: "It's a pity that there is only the power of one strike and it cannot last."

Without withdrawing his attention, Li Fan still waited for Moru Bin's return.

Just as Li Fan thought, it was only a consciousness that was dispelled.

Moru Bin did not give up on establishing contact with Li Fan.
