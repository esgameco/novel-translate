Before luring Yan Fa Jue into his hands, Li Fan had already thought of a temporary alternative solution.

That was the [Cangshen Lingyuan Technique] of the former Lingyuan Sect.

The more people cultivating simultaneously, the faster the cultivation speed of this technique. It was enough to offset the negative effects brought by the incompatibility of practicing multiple laws.

Because the number of cultivators practicing the Lingyuan Technique had sharply decreased, the amplifying effect of the technique could not offset the negative impact of practicing incompatible laws. Therefore, these people had all changed their cultivation techniques.

Previously, Li Qing's avatar had quietly gone to bring this person back to the Lingmu Realm for interrogation.

"Of course, if I want them to risk their lives for me, simple oppression definitely won't work. I still need to entice them with benefits..."

Thinking this way, Li Fan formed hand seals and reconstructed the protective formation covering this mountain temple.

Previously, because the supreme treasure Fishing Pool couldn't be moved at all, Li Fan had only set up a simple formation here.

Although the Book of Genesis similarly hid itself spontaneously, its status was ultimately one level lower than the Fishing Pool.

Moreover, it couldn't be ruled out that if discovered, it might be forcibly relocated by some old monsters in the Xuanhuang Realm.

Li Fan, who already considered it his own, naturally wouldn't allow such a thing to happen.

Moreover, Li Fan had not displayed his true strength in front of her. In her eyes, Li Fan was just a slightly stronger Yuan Ying realm cultivator.

Having some selfish thoughts was quite normal.

Li Fan had long noticed this, which was why he didn't immediately go to her when he received her message before.

However, Li Fan didn't care what Lin Ling thought.
