Chapter 1079: The Truth of the Misunderstanding

"Is this how the Beast Taming Sect treats its guests?" Mr. White's pleasant voice resounded through the heavens and earth. There was no trace of anger, but it made the sect's guardian, the Azure Dragon, feel greatly threatened as it glared at him.

Out of its own defensive instinct, just as the Azure Dragon was about to make a move, at this critical moment, a figure suddenly rose from the ground.

"Ahhhh!" Accompanied by a miserable cry, it flew straight towards Mr. White.

The tense atmosphere was instantly shattered.

Countless gazes in the world instantly focused on that small figure.

"It's Xu Ke..." Mr. White smiled.

Xu Ke rubbed the back of his head, feeling uneasy.

But he still opened his mouth shyly as Li Fan had taught him, "Mr. White, why are you here? Did you come to see me?"

Before Mr. White could answer, a blue light suddenly arrived from the horizon in an instant, blocking between him and Xu Ke.

"Big Brother Lu Ya!" Seeing old acquaintances one after another, Xu Ke was very excited.

And Mr. White, seeing the vigilance on Lu Ya's face, flashed a hint of helplessness in his eyes, "Lu Ya, long time no see."

Lu Ya said, "What business do you have?"

His tone was icy cold, full of wariness.

Mr. White didn't mind, and slowly said, "This trip is not with ill intent. I'm just here to find the Beast Taming Sect, as there is a matter I need their help with."

Hearing this, Lu Ya fell into contemplation.

Just then, an elderly voice rang out, "Guests from afar are still guests, it is our Beast Taming Sect that has been inhospitable."

An old man with white hair and eyebrows appeared in an instant.

"Grand Elder?" Lu Ya was startled.

Emperor Sanmao nodded in response. Then he looked at Mr. White, "Honored guest, please follow me. If there is anything the Beast Taming Sect can assist with, we will certainly cooperate."

This very polite attitude made Lu Ya somewhat puzzled and hard to accept.

However, he still respected his teachers after all, and did not openly refute it.

"Then I will thank you in advance." As the saying goes, don't hit a smiling person. Mr. White also retracted the aura he was emitting.

Following Emperor Sanmao, he disappeared from sight.

"Huh? Why did he leave?" Xu Ke, who originally thought Mr. White would take him along, felt a little frustrated for a moment.

"I told you not to get too close to Mr. White! And you foolishly rushed up to him as soon as you saw him!" Lu Ya lightly patted Xu Ke's head and admonished.

"He is not at all like how he appears in front of us usually." Lu Ya looked into the distance of the Beast Taming Sect, his face gloomy.

"Big Brother Lu Ya, why do you say that?! Mr. White is really a great person!" Xu Ke was a bit puzzled, but said very seriously.

"After Ningyuan City was razed to the ground, we were under Mr. White's..."

"Under his command, calling on the people to save themselves. And helping to transfer all the wounded to the small broken temple."

"But, that day..."

Lu Ya fell into recollection, his expression a bit dazed.

"Among the wounded, someone's condition suddenly worsened, and it looked like he wouldn't last. Mr. White happened to be away, and our skill level simply couldn't save him..."

"So we could only split up and search the city for Mr. White."

At this point, Xu Ke also suddenly said, "Right, I remember now, there was indeed such an incident at the time. Not long after, Big Brother Lu Ya, your attitude towards Mr. White completely changed. Up to..."
