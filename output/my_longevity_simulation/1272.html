Chapter 1269: The Celestial Folding Formation

After a search, traces suspected to be remnants of the realm-protecting grand formation were indeed discovered.

However, compared to the Ashen Minor World, the formation lines of the Xuan-Huang Realm were more ancient and profound.

Li Fan continued to investigate while activating the Limitless Mirror. At the same time, he input the probed formation data into the formation model previously derived from the Doomsday Separation Disc.

"The vast majority match. But at certain critical nodes..." Li Fan's eyes flickered as he grabbed the Limitless Mirror, transforming into a streamline shuttling through the Nine Lands, arriving at the deepest part of the underground, still beneath the Xuan-Huang Leylines.

The surroundings were a chaotic expanse where matter no longer existed under unimaginable pressure. Only dark red aura flowed, stirring up mysterious whispers.

Under this extreme environment, Li Fan's divine sense had no choice but to hide inside the Limitless Mirror, relying on the artifact's main body to maintain survival.

The Limitless Mirror was not having it easy either. Actively releasing faint light to protect the main body, Sun Luyao was extremely vigilant as he surveyed the surroundings.

"So that's how it is."

An impulse suddenly surged in Li Fan's heart.

While the Limitless Mirror didn't feel much, Li Fan's divine sense was visibly aging at an accelerated rate.

Just as Li Fan's divine sense was about to completely dissipate due to aging, a mysterious vitality suddenly welled up from its core.

To his surprise, it was exactly the same as the method Chuan Fa had used to conceal and seal the Calamity Transcendence Scripture!

Namely: the superposition of space in multiple directions. To put it simply, a line is hidden in different spatial dimensions. Naturally, nothing can be discovered from a certain angle. Only by constantly changing perspectives and "rotating" all the distributed individuals into one, will the hidden formation finally be revealed.

"And I also sensed it here, the aura of those Celestial Seal Characters. Many of them..."

"Hmm?"

Such a turn of events left the Limitless Mirror spirit Sun Luyao staring in shock.

"If the Celestial Realm still existed, this Regression Technique would be equivalent to forcibly opening a passage to the Celestial Realm. Although it only opens to specific targets."

"This inexplicable twist in the formation node, unexpected yet seeming as it should be, my previous understanding was still too superficial."

Although it seemed like a casual question, Li Fan was actually staring intently at Sun Luyao, observing his changes.

"Wonderful, wonderful."

"The passage still exists, but the destination is no longer there. However, according to the Limitless Mirror, this experiencer in the flesh, it's also possible that the true Celestial Ruins did not disappear. "No." There were no abnormal fluctuations in Sun Luyao's emotions as he honestly confessed, "After being annihilated by the power of the Celestial Seal Characters, my consciousness was in a blank state. And those vanished parts, I completely lost sense of their existence."

Sun Luyao's words suddenly stopped, as if he had fallen into some kind of contemplation and doubt.

After the Limitless Mirror shattered, the information related to the Celestial Realm also disappeared along with it. Even though Li Fan helped it complete a rough reconstruction, the lost records did not return.

And this was merely because the action of "seeing" brought about proximity.

Using the remaining essence of the source power, see if it can disguise as a celestial artifact and use the power of the Regression Technique to explore the true Celestial Ruins.

"After coming into contact with the Celestial Seal Characters, have any memories been restored?" Li Fan asked while observing the surroundings.

Li Fan exclaimed in surprise again and again, immersed in the traces and ruins of a true celestial-grade grand formation. He seemed to ignore the crisis of his own aging and disappearance.

This was the first time Li Fan had seen a real celestial-grade formation, not just a theoretical one.

"Uh, I don't know how to express it to Senior. In any case, it was a very magical experience. Although it indeed no longer exists, it felt like instantly flying to another extremely distant world. It felt too profound, I really don't know if it was my true realization or an illusion amidst the confusion of life and death." Sun Luyao said slowly, his tone quite dreamy.

"Senior, the formation ruins I just scanned above are clearly here. But when I get close, I can't see them anymore..."

"Much stronger than before." Sun Luyao emphasized.

The intention of death completely vanished, like a withered tree meeting spring. Even the lingering aura of the Xuan-Huang Realm's protective grand formation could not shake those few miserable souls that were previously like candles in the wind.

But after "returning" and opposing the Celestial Realm, perhaps there were some changes, which is why he asked.

"However..."

Once the thought arose, it was like a surging flood, difficult to suppress. It was actually the Xuan-Huang Celestial Heart Mantra that automatically circulated to suppress the desire.

It was like the merciless trampling of a terrifying behemoth, leaving marks that even space itself was branded with a sense of vicissitude.

"However..."

After hearing his words, Li Fan's thoughts paused slightly.

It was clearer than what was seen on the surface.

In his contemplation, Li Fan's divine sense also found a way to break the concealment of the original protective grand formation of the Xuan-Huang Realm.

Li Fan was already able to move and talk to himself within the aura of the grand formation. He glanced at Sun Luyao and smiled slightly, "What, you want to learn?"

Under Li Fan's guidance, traces of the formation lines gradually appeared on the ancient mirror surface of the Limitless Mirror.

"For me, relying on my divine sense to try it out while my true body makes full preparations to return at any time, the risk is not very high. Hmm... It just so happens that waiting for the final act to arrive in this world will take a long time. I can plan carefully..."

As for the Limitless Mirror Sun Luyao, he seemed to be too shocked to react, just staring blankly and allowing Li Fan's divine sense to rapidly weaken. Without any reminder.

Although there were only a few strokes, it allowed Li Fan, who claimed to be at the peak of formations in the mortal realm, to gain a lot.

"This is called the Profound Art of Life and Death. Reversing life into death, no life no death."

After holding back for a while, he slowly said, "Senior's divine might is really beyond my imagination..."

If the discovered details were proportionally enlarged into the model derived from the Doomsday Separation Disc, then theoretically speaking, the scope of the original protective grand formation was actually more than several dozen times larger than the initial Xuan-Huang Realm!
