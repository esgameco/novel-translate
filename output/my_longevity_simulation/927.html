The path ahead seemed somewhat hazy and confusing.

Li Qing wiped the blood clean. While her body still had strength, she quickened her pace to hurry along the path.

But the demonic sounds in her ears not only did not cease, but instead grew more and more intense.

"Cough, cough..."

Li Qing coughed violently, even coughing up fragments of her internal organs.

After walking several more dozen steps, this body ultimately could not hold on.

But very quickly, as the flesh in the void squirmed, a new body was about to take shape again.

However, those ubiquitous demonic sounds seemed to have no intention of letting Li Qing go.

Before the body had fully manifested, it was crushed again.

And so on this long staircase, an extremely strange scene appeared.

A lump of rotten flesh that no longer resembled human form struggled to crawl and climb upwards.

With each crushing, it reappeared anew.

The total mass of flesh seemed to diminish a little each time.

Although Li Qing never gave up, in the end, only a small misshapen meatball remained, struggling and clumsily rolling.

Even though things had already become this miserable, the surrounding sounds showed no signs of weakening in the slightest.

Instead, they grew more excited. The sounds turned shrill, no longer sounding human, but rather like all kinds of meaningless noises between heaven and earth.

Just as Li Qing's last lump of flesh was about to completely disappear,

The phantom image of a black sword suddenly appeared.

Emitting a soft sword cry,

In an instant, it temporarily suppressed the surrounding demonic cacophony!

Seizing this fleeting moment, the speed of the black sword phantom suddenly exploded. Transforming into a line, it charged straight into the sect's grand hall.

Although the doors of the grand hall were open, gazing in from outside, the interior was pitch-black.

Like an abyss, nothing could be seen.

After that moment of silence, countless sounds suddenly erupted.

Like mountains roaring and seas howling, ghosts wailing and wolves crying!

"I said he could do it!"

"Heh heh heh, interesting, interesting!"

"Why deliberately let him go, why? Why not let me crush him inch by inch into a pulp!"

"Stop pretending. Is it meaningful to deceive yourself?"

...

Inside the grand hall of the Dadao Sect, it was unusually quiet.

In the center of the spacious hall, it was empty except for the sect leader's seat at the highest point.

Zhao Shijie sat quietly on the seat.

Like a child, sitting properly with her back straight.

Hands placed on her thighs.

The expression on her face was also frozen, unmoving.

Li Qing quietly glanced at Zhao Shijie.

Her appearance seemed to be frozen at around 10 years old.

Exactly the same as what was seen in the illusion.

"Greetings to the One Heart Celestial Venerate."

Although the atmosphere was a bit strange, Li Qing still respectfully bowed in salute.

"Why have you come here?"

Zhao Shijie's pleasant voice sounded out.

But it did not come from the figure sitting on the seat, but rather emanated from all directions of the grand hall.

Li Qing did not conceal anything, directly saying: "I have come for the former secret manuals of the Xuantian Sect."

"The Treatise on Heaven and Earth's Mutual Conquest, the Eternal I Alone Secret, the Soul-Severing Divine Illumination Technique, the Scripture of Heaven's Sorrow and Earth's Grief..."

"These texts originally belonged to my Xuantian Sect. Today they should be returned to their rightful owner."

Li Qing said solemnly.

The grand hall was silent for a moment.

Then Zhao Shijie laughed, as if hearing an extremely funny joke.

"The people of Xuantian Sect are not all dead yet?"

"And how did you know that I once obtained these few secret techniques?"
