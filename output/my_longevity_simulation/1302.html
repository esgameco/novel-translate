"Lord Heavenly Venerate has returned."

Upon hearing these words, Lu Xichan and the array masters present were momentarily stunned.

Then, inevitably, a strong fear surged in their hearts, and they lowered their heads.

Even their bodies couldn't help but tremble slightly.

This was the overwhelming oppressive power of the Longevity Heavenly Venerate who had dominated the Xuanhuang realm for thousands of years and single-handedly created the Wanxian Alliance.

With Dharma Transmitter Ren's words, in each state of the Wanxian Alliance, the Sacred Emperor Talismans attached to the giant Dharma Transmission Stone Statues suddenly moved on their own without wind.

They fluttered incessantly, as if they could be blown open at any moment.

The stone statues suppressed by the talismans also seemed to come alive, emitting subtle shaking.

The Sacred Emperor Talismans emitted a purple-golden starlight, while the Dharma Transmission Stone Statues emitted a white glow.

The two forces constantly opposed each other.

The sky and earth of the Xuanhuang realm changed colors.

The Sacred Emperor also seemed to sense something, raising his head to look at the sky while lecturing, but without stopping the principles of the Great Dao he was expounding.

It's just that his speaking speed had unintentionally quickened a little.

Amidst the anomalies in the Xuanhuang realm, some Alliance cultivators had already guessed what was happening.

Their faces turned pale, cold sweat pouring down their foreheads. They no longer had the heart to listen to what the Sacred Emperor was saying, their whole bodies shivering from fear.

While some cultivators had flickering eyes, gathering to listen to the Sacred Emperor's teachings. At the same time, they prayed in their hearts for the Dharma Transmitter to return a little later.

"The changes of ancient and modern times, the principles of the Great Dao."

"The Dharma Transmission Method cannot be contained by Xuanhuang."

"I have now attained the Dao, and will try to change it..."

Finally, the Sacred Emperor's Dharma lecture came to an end.

The Sacred Emperor's gaze also completely shifted from the masses below to the figure in the starry sky outside the Xuanhuang realm.

Nodding slightly, he suddenly disappeared into the horizon.

Without the Sacred Emperor's protection, all kinds of anomalies suddenly descended between the heaven and earth of the Xuanhuang realm.

Rolling dark clouds emerged out of nowhere. Continuous and unbroken, they slowly obscured the bright moon transformed by the Great Enlightenment of the Sacred Dynasty.

On the earth, the birds and beasts that had originally gone out to listen to the Sacred Emperor's Dharma lecture were startled, fleeing in panic and rushing back to their nests to take shelter.

Even the flowers, plants and trees were broken and scattered in the increasingly violent winds.

Thunderbolts exploded from the dark clouds one after another.

Then heavy rain pounded the ground.

With the wind, rain, and thunder combined, the entire Xuanhuang realm instantly became like a doomsday.

Those Alliance cultivators whose hearts were shaken by listening to the Sacred Emperor's Dharma lecture instantly felt like a basin of cold water had been poured over their heads, going from feverish to sober.

"What was I thinking! That's the Dharma Transmitting Heavenly Venerate! The Dharma Transmitting Heavenly Venerate who single-handedly suppressed the Xuanhuang realm for thousands of years! I actually fantasized that the Sacred... no, this group of bandits would win?"

"Although the Sacred Emperor is strong, he definitely won't be a match for the Dharma Transmitting Heavenly Venerate. Damn, I need to hurry and forget this new Dharma too."

"Heavenly Venerate above, please forgive this humble one's momentary delusion..."

"With the Dharma Transmitting Heavenly Venerate taking action, everything will get better!"

The Wanxian Alliance cultivators were awed by the Dharma Transmitter's might, their hearts and galls trembling.

The Sacred Dynasty soldiers, due to the innate huge gap in realms, were also momentarily terrified.

But very quickly, their eyes became firm again.

Some of them recalled the words the Sacred Emperor had said to them before this expedition.

So someone shouted to the sky: "The Sacred Dynasty will surely win, the Sacred Emperor will surely win!"

At first it was just sporadic shouts.

But it quickly formed a wildfire momentum. Without anyone deliberately directing, all the Sacred Dynasty soldiers shouted in unison, as if their hearts were connected.

The voices also grew louder and louder, from shouting to roaring, from roaring to howling.

The sound of the Enlightened soldiers, like rolling waves, spread throughout the entire Xuanhuang realm with the momentum of collapsing mountains and overturning seas.

As if responding to their call, the bright moon in the sky burst out with purple-golden light.

It drilled out from the boundless dark clouds again.

The swaying wind and rain of Xuanhuang actually showed a trend of gradually subsiding amidst this earth-shattering roar.

Hearing the sound coming from afar, Dharma Transmitter Ren sneered: "Just a dying struggle."

As if echoing his words, transmission gates appeared one after another in the four central states of the Alliance.

Huge flying ship arrays constantly flew out from them, instantly filling the sky of the four states.

A Dharma Transmitter stood at the bow of each of these flying ships.

The Dharma Transmitters looked up at the sky and bowed slightly towards the Dharma Transmitting Heavenly Venerate in outer space.

Lu Xichan secretly peeked at this scene, inwardly surprised: "These should be the cultivator forces from the small worlds of the various Dharma Transmitters. Since Lord Heavenly Venerate has already appeared, naturally they will no longer hide behind the scenes."

"Judging from the aura emanating from those flying ships, there are quite a few Harmonized Dao cultivators among them."

Hearing her master's name and the ridicule directed at her, Lu Xichan couldn't help but feel a surge of anger in her heart. She just lowered her head and said, "This subordinate is incompetent."

"Forget it, tell your people to step aside." With a lazy sentence from Dharma Transmitter Dou, a team of lads floated down from the flying ship in the sky.

They first bowed slightly to the Mirror Holders guarding the Detached Discs. After receiving the array data analyzed by the Detached Discs so far, they began to break the array.

These lads numbered thirty-six in total.

Their faces were all devoid of any expression. Even their eyes had no emotional fluctuations at all.

One of them stretched his hand into the light beam of the Bright Moon Starlight Grand Array.

Then his body also instantly became ethereal.

From his hand to his body, he gradually became the same as the starlight of the array.

The lad testing the array with his body was about to be completely devoured by the starlight of the array when his figure suddenly disappeared, turning into a stream of light and flying to another lad.

This second lad also touched with his hand and then tested the array with his body.

This repeated back and forth.

Until the last thirty-sixth lad, just before his body turned into starlight.

His body shook, shaking out the original thirty companions. He himself also returned to normal.

The thirty-six lads whispered to each other.

Then, as if reaching a consensus, they all nodded in unison.

They split into two groups, one starting to break the array, the other repeating the process of testing the array with their bodies.

The Alliance array masters observing on the side almost immediately felt the extraordinary nature of this group of lads.

According to their speed of deciphering, this Bright Moon Starlight Grand Array would definitely have a hole torn open in less than half a day at most.

As the chief of the Strategy Array Hall, Lu Xichan's shock was far beyond ordinary people.

Looking at these thirty-six array-breaking lads, the four words "Array Spirit Sacred Body" came to her mind.

This was an extremely special physique, as if the body was composed of natural arrays. Not only did they have a natural affinity and resonance with various arrays, they could also merge their body with the array, greatly increasing the strength of the array.

Just like spirit tools to magic treasures, they could transform into array spirits, causing the array to sublimate and evolve.

Lu Xichan's master Lu Xia had wanted to find cultivators with this physique to inherit his mantle when he was alive. Unfortunately, he never got his wish until his death, and had no choice but to pass on all his lifelong learnings to Lu Xichan.

But she never expected that today, more than thirty of them appeared at once!

Lu Xichan looked up at the sky again, at the figures standing on the densely packed floating ships.

Her heart turned slightly cold: "Just how many things are these Dharma Transmitters hiding?"

After Dharma Transmitter Ren learned about the progress of breaking the array, a hint of killing intent flashed in his eyes.

This time the Wanxian Alliance had been made to look so miserable, Lord Heavenly Venerate's punishment was definitely unavoidable.

Since that's the case, before receiving the punishment, he would vent his anger on these troublemakers.

With a wave of his hand, the group of black-robed cultivators who had been gathering their moves and were forced to stop because of the Sacred Emperor's Dharma lecture began their actions again.

Several more cultivators in blue Daoist robes, holding whisks and talismans, quietly descended from the flying ships in the sky.

The whisks and talismans waved, shaping the power in the accumulated light orbs into one hideous-looking, killing intent-filled, god-like figure after another.

As soon as these energy gods descended, the might emanating from them made even Lu Xichan, a Harmonized Dao realm cultivator, feel genuine fear.

The Wanxian Alliance's counterattack was about to be unleashed.

Outside the Bright Moon Starlight Grand Array, the Sacred Dynasty soldiers had also prepared for close combat with the Wanxian Alliance.

The battlefield commander-in-chief, Sun Erlang, was methodically issuing one order after another through the Sacred Emperor's Star Network.

"Those below the Nascent Soul stage, guard your positions and do not act recklessly. Continue to supervise the construction progress of the suppression grand array. Remember, being able to complete the grand array on time is a great merit!"

"For cultivators at the Nascent Soul stage and above, I'm making the following deployment adjustments."

The brighter stars in the sea of stars dispersed and regrouped under Sun Erlang's mental manipulation.

"Don't clash head-on with the Wanxian Alliance, extend the battle lines to every part of the Wanxian Alliance."

"We don't need to attack the Wanxian Alliance headquarters, what we need is to wait for the Sacred Emperor to return victorious from the sea of stars!"

Sun Erlang said with great determination.

The Enlightened soldiers responded with a thunderous cry.

After everyone's emotions were stirred up, Sun Erlang observed the scene inside the Bright Moon Starlight Grand Array and made final adjustments to the soldiers' assignments.

At the same time, the remaining mental power had already connected to the silently standing dark golden Sacred Punishment Puppet.

"Finally, I can stretch my muscles and bones a bit."

Three hours later.

The Bright Moon Starlight Grand Array that had tightly sealed the four states of the Alliance for a full seven days was finally torn open with a small hole.

In an instant, the powerful attacks filled with the anger of the Alliance cultivators poured out from this gap.

Solidified spell techniques, furious-eyed manifested deities, and countless magic treasure lights...

Shining upon each other, gushing out.

The terrifying energy sufficient to completely flatten several states of the Alliance was impatiently seeking targets to strike.

However, what awaited this long-accumulated counterattack from the Wanxian Alliance was just an ordinary black door.

As the black door opened, all the frenzied attacks were devoured by it.

Like a deep abyss monster opening its bloody huge mouth.

Not a bit of energy ripple was left for the Wanxian Alliance.

After devouring all the accumulated explosive energy, the black door disappeared again as silently as it appeared.

Leaving the somewhat stunned Wanxian Alliance crowd.

"The Dark Sea?" Dharma Transmitter Ren's face changed slightly, as he sensed a hint of familiarity from this door.

And just as he expected, this "vast" energy devoured into the Dark Sea only formed another accumulating cloud deep in the Dark Sea. Other than that, it didn't stir up any other ripples.

"So the recent disturbance in the Dark Sea was also related to this group of bandits."

With new and old grudges combined, Dharma Transmitter Ren's heart had already reached the peak of anger.

The thirty-six lads continued to break the array, intending to completely shatter the Bright Moon Starlight Grand Array.

Cultivators under the other Dharma Transmitters also poured out from the gap in the grand array, intending to sweep and slaughter the bandits in each state.
