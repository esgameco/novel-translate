After giving instructions, Li Fan felt somewhat bored for a while.

At this time, all the plans had officially entered the accumulation stage, just waiting for the next step to make a big move.

Li Fan, who had nothing to do for a while, flashed to the outside of the hall where the faceless stone statue was placed.

With a light point towards the sky, he released Yan Fangzhou, who had been trapped for a long time.

Being trapped in a place with nothing and constantly disturbed by the formation, it felt like years had passed.

According to Li Fan's observation, this former prodigy of the heavens should have really gone mad by now.

He was completely unaware that he had been released and gained freedom. Even when Li Fan waved his hand in front of his eyes, there was no reaction at all.

Li Fan separated a thread of divine sense and entered Yan Fangzhou's sea of consciousness.

There was no instinctive defense mechanism against the intrusion of external objects.

Yan Fangzhou's past memories were completely fragmented, as if cut by a knife.

Only occasional storms of fear, helplessness, and pleading thoughts swept through the sea of consciousness.

"Poor child, let me save you." Li Fan smiled slightly.

The divine sense swept across, crushing all of Yan Fangzhou's remaining thoughts.

Then, as if painting on a blank canvas, he freely fabricated a whole new life for Yan Fangzhou.

Born in the Spirit Wood Realm, he was sent to infiltrate the Yan family of the Myriad Immortals Alliance from a young age.

Later, while communicating with the Spirit Wood Realm, he was accidentally discovered by someone. In order to prevent his identity from being exposed, he fought while retreating, planning to lure the other party into the Spirit Wood Realm to silence them.

Although he succeeded, he himself was also seriously injured and fell unconscious.
