Chapter 1174

As the commander of the Qingxuan Army, Shang Shaojun had suppressed the Chituo Abyss for a hundred years. Even in the face of such a terrifying world-destroying demonic sound wave, he could remain calm and composed.

But now, he truly felt a deep-rooted fear that he hadn't experienced in a long time.

There were simply too many dead cultivators in this frozen sea.

Shang Shaojun couldn't even imagine where these cultivators came from.

The number of cultivators buried here was probably more than the entire Xuanhuang Realm!

The Holy Fetus Li Fan also pretended to be frightened by the scene before him, his body trembling slightly and his face showing a look of horror.

"Shaojun, don't act rashly and be careful," he said, as if coming back to his senses after a while, reassuring Shang Shaojun.

Shang Shaojun's face still looked grim, and he kept looking around from time to time, as if there were some unknown enemies hidden around.

"There's something wrong here. Did you hear any sounds?" Shang Shaojun asked telepathically.

"Sounds? What sounds?" The Holy Fetus Li Fan was somewhat surprised, and this was not an act.

Shang Shaojun said with a gloomy face, "Perhaps it's because I've been in the Chituo Abyss for a long time, I'm more sensitive to any sounds. Ever since I came to this maze, I've been hearing some kind of whisper from time to time. You didn't hear it?"

The Holy Fetus Li Fan narrowed his eyes.

This was not his first time coming here.

He had even guided various cultivators to come here.

But no one was like Shang Shaojun, who could actually hear mysterious sounds in the dead silence of the frozen sea.

With a different thought in mind, the Holy Fetus Li Fan said solemnly, "What does Shaojun think? There must be a great secret involved here. But similarly, if we want to investigate, we must also take great risks. Should we leave before being discovered, or should we explore it?"

Shang Shaojun didn't answer Li Fan immediately. Instead, his ears moved slightly, as if trying to discern the sound he heard.

After a while, he explained telepathically, "Brother Li, follow me. I have a feeling that tracing the source of this sound may unravel some of the mysteries of this place."

The Holy Fetus Li Fan nodded slightly, his sword shadow unsheathed and guarding the two of them.

"Be careful."

The two shuttled through the endless frozen sea.

In fact, Li Fan had long been clear about the overall structure of the "Reverse Life and Death Grand Formation".

However, since he pretended to be here for the first time, even though he saw Shang Shaojun leading him around in circles within the formation, he didn't expose it.

He just followed behind in silence.

But as Shang Shaojun stubbornly kept advancing in a certain direction, the Holy Fetus Li Fan also noticed the subtle changes in the surroundings.

It had even escaped the analysis of the Doomsday Separation Disc, which he had overlooked before!

"There's actually this hidden change in the grand formation? No wonder it can prove the Dao of immortality." Li Fan was secretly shocked.

They continued to advance.

It seemed like they were walking in place, but the frozen coffins in the surrounding dead sea kept rotating around them.

"Brother Li, be careful. It seems that the source of the sound is right ahead!" Shang Shaojun's voice was also a bit nervous.

It was like passing through a long and narrow space-time tunnel, with the surroundings turning into layers of overlapping lines due to the rapid speed.

When they broke through the end of the tunnel, countless circles converged into a point.

Then it exploded in their vision with a bang.

It was suddenly clear.

But the surrounding air became even colder.

It even made the Holy Fetus Li Fan and Shang Shaojun feel...

At this moment, Saint Embryo Li Fan realized that some invisible existence seemed to have descended into this space.

After a few short sentences, the two fell into an extremely oppressive silence.

"The sound I heard just now should be the echoes of that unknown existence's footsteps here," Shao Jun speculated. "Even though it has left, the lingering sound still persists."

"The countless frozen corpses in the yin-yang spheres seem to be the handiwork of that fellow. But I still can't figure out where so many corpses came from. Also, what would be outside this silver-white space?"

These questions, Saint Embryo Li Fan also could not answer.

Originally, they thought this was only the Immortal Venerable's Daochang. But unexpectedly, in this life, by a fluke, they actually discovered a deeper secret hidden behind it.
