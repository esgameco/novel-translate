Chapter 432

"Unlike you, in Dalí, there stands a towering volcano at the center."

"Every once in a while, the volcano erupts, taking away countless lives."

"Moreover, in recent years, its activities have become increasingly frequent."

"It is clear that the ultimate fate of Dalí is to be completely engulfed by the lava of the volcano."

"Centuries ago, I came to the Xuánhuáng Realm alone, trying to find a way to save my homeland."

Li Fan looked back with nostalgia as he spoke to Li Chenfeng and the others.

"Unfortunately, although I found a way to temporarily suppress the volcano, with my current cultivation level, I am unable to completely eradicate the hidden dangers."

"However, that is not the main point."

Suddenly changing the topic, Li Fan continued, "During my time in the Xuánhuáng Realm, I encountered many ordinary people who, like me, crossed over from small worlds."

"They often lead extremely difficult lives. Touched by their plight, I often try my best to lend a helping hand."

"It is because of this that I was able to join the organization I am with today. This is also the reason why I have come to find you all today."

Li Chenfeng and the others were stunned, "What organization?"

Li Fan said extremely seriously, "An organization that aims to unite all the lands outside the realms and launch a counterattack against the immortal cultivation world."

"A counterattack against the immortal cultivation world?" The three of them present were all shocked.

Li Fan stood with his hands behind his back, nodding slightly.

"Yes!"

"A thousand years ago, we were exiled from our homeland with pain and resentment. Over the long years, the memories of most people about their homeland have faded."

"But there is still a group of people who always keep these memories in their hearts."

"They vowed that one day, they will return here. They will reclaim everything that originally belonged to them."

Li Fan looked into the distance with determination and said in a voice filled with seduction, "Compared to the small worlds we are in, the Xuánhuáng Realm is vast."

"It is teeming with life and abundance."

"Yet it is occupied by those cursed immortal cultivators. And we can only live in barren, cramped territories, struggling for survival."

"How unfair!"

Li Fan said indignantly.

Li Chenfeng and the others compared the world of the empire with the Xuánhuáng Realm, nodding in agreement.

"We understand very well that our own strength is too weak compared to the vast alliance of immortal cultivators."

"Only by uniting all our strengths, do we stand a chance of competing with them."

"In fact, five hundred years ago, we made an attempt."

"Unfortunately, at that time, we did not have enough unity and ultimately failed."

"However, we did not give up."

Li Fan's eyes were piercing as he looked at Li Chenfeng and the others.

"Over these years, we have been searching for new allies, rebuilding our strength."

"There are countless small worlds outside the realms, and no one knows exactly how many are yet to be united."

"But just like you, those who have crossed over are always cautious in their actions, hence the progress is slow."

"It was only recently that I noticed you, and after some probing, indeed..."

Zhù Shǎo Táng seemed to have remembered something and said, "The battle against the immortal cultivation world five hundred years ago?"

"In the history of the Xuánhuáng Realm that the empire has collected, indeed, there is such an event."

Li Chenfeng’s eyes flickered with some regret, but he quickly concealed it.

After a moment of contemplation, he carefully asked, "So, Senior, are you here this time to invite our empire to join the organization?"

"Yes!" Li Fan nodded.

When Zhù Shǎo Táng was about to speak, he was stopped by Li Chenfeng.

This young man seemed to be in a leading position among the three.

He looked at Li Fan and answered somewhat implicitly, "The grand goal of the organization, this junior admires greatly."

"But our empire is too weak. Even if we join the organization, we may not be of much help. It might even become a burden..."

Li Fan interrupted Li Chenfeng's words with a raised hand.

"Though not very strong, among the numerous worlds that have joined the organization, you are already ranked slightly above average."

"You don't have to belittle yourselves."

Li Fan looked at Li Chenfeng with a half-smile, saying, "Moreover, even those small worlds that entirely lack extraordinary powers, the organization has not rejected their applications to join."

"After all, every bit of strength is beneficial. Even without power, you can still contribute."

"The organization will train qualified fighters free of charge."

Li Fan's tone left no room for refusal, and Li Chenfeng's smile gradually became forced.

He wanted to resist, but seeing Li Fan's expression turning cold, Li Chenfeng's heart sank gradually.

Tension began to spread in the secret room.

However, inexplicably, after a while, Li Fan seemed to relax again.

With a cold snort, he said, "I didn't want to tell you this originally, but it seems like you still cling to that ridiculous escape plan."

"You are just a bunch of weak fleeing cowards..."

Li Fan said coldly, "To tell you the truth, your plan to use one hundred thousand Split-Realm Whales to escape from Xuánhuáng Realm is simply impossible."

"The moment you leave Xuánhuáng Realm will be the day of your empire's destruction!"

Li Chenfeng and his companions were completely stunned.

"What do you mean by this, Senior?"

"See for yourselves!"

Li Fan pointed towards the foreheads of the three.

Suddenly, Li Chenfeng felt the surrounding scenery disappear.

As if placed in an endless void.

In the distance, a huge sphere of light shimmered.

A stream of light flew out from the sphere of light, trying to escape.

Strangely, when it had moved a certain distance away from the sphere of light.

Its flight trajectory changed, irreversible and rose upwards.

...

Li Chenfeng and his companions broke out in cold sweats, falling to the ground in confusion and helplessness.

"What was that?"

He stared intently at Li Fan, his eyes full of shock.

"Why do you think we are fighting desperately to counterattack the immortal cultivation world?"

"Because we have nowhere else to go."

Li Fan said mockingly.

"That is the 'Immortal Ruins,' a terrifying unknown entity that casts greedy eyes on the entire Xuánhuáng Realm."

"The Xuánhuáng Realm is vast and huge, perhaps it could endure for a while against the Immortal Ruins."

"But for the small worlds we are in, it's uncertain."

"Not to mention, the ridiculous and laughable plan your empire came up with."

Li Fan continued to hit Li Chenfeng with his words.

The three from the empire also realized the kind of fate awaiting them if they escaped from Xuánhuáng Realm.

Their faces turned extremely pale.

"So, joining us is the only way out."

Li Fan said calmly.
