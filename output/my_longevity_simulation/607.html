When understanding that he was reincarnated, in the ancient immortal world where the ten sects of the Immortal Dao were ruling the world with their immense power and the new laws were yet to be born, a bold idea emerged in Qiao Sidao's mind.

He wanted to follow the Heavenly Transmitter, and become one of the first few to transmit the laws!

Don't be fooled by the current flourishing of the ten sects of the Immortal Dao.

Qiao Sidao, who was well aware of the future trends, knew very well that this seemingly eternal rule would be completely vulnerable before the surging tide of the new laws!

Before long, the powerful army of new law cultivators, capable of seizing heaven and earth and cultivating at a frightening speed, would, under the leadership of the Heavenly Transmitter, crush the Immortal Dao ten sects, and establish the great Ten Thousand Immortal Alliance, ruling for countless years in the future.

And there would be no greater achievement than following the trend.

If he pledged allegiance before the Heavenly Transmitter rose to prominence, he would proactively surrender.

Later, when the Heavenly Transmitter roamed beyond the sky, the highest ruling body of the Ten Thousand Immortal Alliance, the Transmitter Council, would surely have a place for him!
