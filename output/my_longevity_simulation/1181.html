There was also no way to distinguish the existence of these voids.

He could only adopt a desperate measure and continue to let the remaining stone puppets lead the way, while he himself avoided the routes where the inexplicably disappeared puppets had passed through.

As the number of stone puppets continued to decrease, Li Fan's heart slowly sank.

At this rate of attrition, it seemed absolutely impossible to reach the depths of the starry sea in this lifetime.

However, what Li Fan was worried about did not happen.

When there were only thirty-three puppets left, after flying for a long time in the void, there were no more sudden disappearances of puppets.

"It seems that these voids do not widely exist in the Dark Starry Sea. Instead, they are densely gathered in a certain area."

"Outside the Xuanhuang Realm?" The Holy Fetus Li Fan pondered.

The thirty-three puppets were in front, and the sword light transformed by Li Fan followed behind.

The speed kept increasing as they headed towards the unknown darkness ahead.

"It's a bit smoother than I expected."

"Now we are already a considerable distance away from the Xuanhuang Realm, but this Xuanhuang Realm sword dao incarnation of mine doesn't feel any weakening of power."

"This is quite interesting."

According to common sense, without the support of the world's power, the various laws that wander outside the world will inevitably be weakened to a certain extent.

But now this reduction did not appear.

Even...

The sword dao, which had already reached its limit in the Xuanhuang Realm and could not be further comprehended no matter what, suddenly loosened its bottleneck after staying in the void for a while.

Even though the talent of this Holy Fetus was not particularly outstanding, he felt that all kinds of thoughts and insights came rushing uncontrollably.

It was as if he had directly transformed into some unparalleled genius.

The Holy Fetus Li Fan even had the urge to ignore everything and concentrate all his energy on comprehending the Dao.

But this was forcibly suppressed by Li Fan operating the Immortal Heart Mantra.

This abnormal phenomenon made Li Fan's imagination run wild.

"The entire Dark Starry Sea actually shares a common system of laws?"

"The Xuanhuang Realm's sword dao is a part of the common sword dao of the entire starry sea."

"Outside the world, in the void, one can see a broader world. The upper limit is invisibly expanded..."

"It is indeed a place where danger and opportunity coexist."

"No wonder the Heavenly Doctor and the Dharma Transmitter often run to the Dark Starry Sea without hesitation. There must be this factor involved as well."

Thinking of the Heavenly Doctor and the Dharma Transmitter, Li Fan's heart became even more solemn.

It was hard to imagine what realm these two, who had lived in the starry sea for thousands of years, had already reached.

The Heavenly Doctor's advice was indeed words of wisdom.

Even the "disease" in the void that he treated very cautiously still descended upon the Holy Fetus Li Fan.

Without any warning.

Just when Li Fan was immersed in the joy of his impending breakthrough in sword dao comprehension, an inexplicable sense of weakness suddenly surged in his heart.

Upon introspection, a black spot indeed appeared in his soul.

That black spot corroded his soul, as if it directly occupied his original soul.

No matter how many treasures Li Fan had previously swallowed to protect his soul, they could not stop its invasion.

And the prepared means to nourish the soul had no effect either.

"The upper limit of soul strength has decreased, not the soul itself being damaged."

The Holy Fetus Li Fan was a bit solemn.

Only then could he support himself in cutting his own soul so wantonly without becoming an idiot on the spot.

It was an ending more desperate and terrifying than dying of old age.

What made him frown was not the invisible void or the black spot plague in the Starry Sea.

But the time difference between the true body and the Holy Fetus.

The Holy Fetus had turned into a sword, borrowing the power of the Starry Sea, and had already left the Xuanhuang Realm for a long distance.

Therefore, Li Fan was able to discover that between the Xuanhuang Realm and the depths of the Abyssal Starry Sea...

The time flow was not synchronized.

"It seems that it is still affected by the Immortal Ruins."

"Although it's not as terrifying as the edge of the Immortal Ruins, where a moment passes like a thousand years. But the time flow is still slower than other parts of the Abyssal Starry Sea."

"Now, it's about a one-to-two ratio."

"Moreover, as the Holy Fetus continues to move away, this ratio is still expanding."

Li Fan couldn't help but think more.

"The Abyssal Starry Sea within the high walls is inevitably all affected by the Immortal Ruins. It's just a matter of degree."

"Then, does that mean that the time flow inside and outside the high walls is also not exactly the same?"

"Is it possible that tens of thousands of years have passed since the catastrophe that cut off the ancient immortal path in the Xuanhuang Realm."

"And outside the high walls, in fact..."

"Has even more time passed?"

Thinking of this, Li Fan's eyes closed involuntarily, concealing the faint light within them.
