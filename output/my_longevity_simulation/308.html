Chapter 308

In an instant, it had already enveloped the entire sea of clouds.

"Chiyan Immortal Zhang Zhiliang, cultivated for 683 years. Achieved the foundation with the rare treasure 'Sleepless Eyes', and formed the golden core with the 'Infinite Formation'."

"Seized the 'One-Eyed Grotto Heaven' to form the Nascent Soul."

"Sacrificed the 'Chiyan Soul' to unite with the Dao."

"Fortunately, heavenly punishment descended, and the Dao was extinguished and returned to the heavens!"

...

Against the backdrop of the fallen Immortal's phenomenon filling the sky, a jet-black figure quietly appeared.

Zhang Zhiliang's corpse turned into a drop of thick ink, wriggling back into His hand.

Then, His gaze turned to the sea of clouds below.

At this moment, all the cultivators in the sea of clouds who witnessed this scene first fell into a daze.

Then, an unstoppable fear was born in their hearts.

Making their bodies tremble and souls shudder.

"The soul of heaven and earth, Mosha!"

For some reason, another soul of heaven and earth suddenly descended.

He not only wiped out Zhang Zhiliang, who had successfully united with the Dao, with a casual strike.

He seemed unsatisfied and set his sights on the myriad beings in the sea of clouds!

Mosha lightly pointed to a nearby space.

"Tianyang, you..."

Before he could utter the last sentence of his life, Tianyang, who had narrowly escaped with his life under Zhang Qianmo's Purple Thunder, was devoured by thick ink.

Another phenomenon of a cultivator's demise appeared in the sky.

However, countless black particles surged out from Mosha's body.

These black particles jumped and danced, surging into the sky, obscuring the entire firmament.

True darkness had descended.

The black particles continued to multiply, like a huge black curtain, enveloping the entire sea of clouds.

Then it pressed down, devouring everything.

This scene was too horrifying. The cultivators on the Myriad Immortals Island revealed their ugly sides under extreme fear.

Just like when Mosha destroyed the world before, some completely lost the courage to resist, collapsed on the ground, and waited for death in despair.

Some were filled with resentment, throwing themselves at the ink like moths to a flame, constantly attacking it in an attempt to find a glimmer of hope for survival.

Some were crying and laughing, seemingly driven insane by this sight.

"What's meant to come will come."

Li Fan once again became the one among the cultivators who was completely different.

He observed the scene of Mosha corroding everything with a hungry and greedy gaze, unwilling to miss a single detail.

No matter what magical techniques or divine powers, in the face of those black particles, they all lost their effect and could only be devoured and assimilated.

Life, spiritual energy, matter...

They all became nourishment for its multiplication.

"It is truly terrifying and formidable..."

Seeing Mosha's actions again, Li Fan was still shocked by His methods.

"If I could achieve the golden core using Mosha's method, my killing power would not be inferior to anyone here," Li Fan thought to himself.

Staring intently at the black particles in the sky pressing down, Li Fan's expression was obsessed.

Just as Li Fan was immersed in comprehending the Dao, another figure was also flying excitedly on the Myriad Immortals Island, using a seven-colored imprint stone to record the scene before the world's destruction.

"Wonderful! Wonderful! I originally thought the immortals punishing Chiyan was the disaster, but I didn't expect there to be an even greater one!"

"Mosha descends to the world, exterminating everything!"

"This exclusive limited edition footage will allow me to make a small fortune again!"

...

Jiao Xiuyuan muttered to himself, his face beaming with excitement.

Suddenly, his flying body came to an abrupt halt.

Looking in the direction of Li Fan, he exclaimed in surprise, "Daoist Lin Fan, why are you still here?"

Li Fan woke up from his comprehension of the Dao, looked at Jiao Xiuyuan, and smiled slightly, cupping his hands in greeting.

Then he focused his attention on the black particles in the sky again.

Jiao Xiuyuan turned back to look at Li Fan, carefully observing his expression.

Suddenly realizing something, he said with some shock, "You're also a puppet?"

"No, not a puppet. A clone?"

His eyes narrowed as he recalled the various events that had occurred since interacting with this Lin Fan, and a strange expression appeared on his face.

"Interesting, I never thought there would be a day when I, Jiao Xiuyuan, would misjudge someone."

"It seems this person's true identity is indeed not simple..."

Just then, a strange sound interrupted his thoughts.

He looked in the direction the sound came from, and a hint of surprise flashed in his eyes.

"What is that?"

Li Fan was also attracted by this strange sound.

Looking up, he saw huge rumbling sounds constantly coming from the sky that was already surrounded by endless black particles.

It seemed as if something was constantly stirring in the ink-colored ocean, causing these black particles to become even more active and boiling.

"That direction is..."

Li Fan's eyes narrowed as he pondered for a moment, somewhat unsure.

"Yunshui Palace?"

The anomaly continued.

And the rumbling grew louder.

It seemed that under this black curtain, something was rapidly approaching.

A thin line tore through the black ocean, accompanied by a whistling sound, and suddenly arrived.

In an instant, it appeared above the Myriad Immortals Island.

The surrounding black particles were restless, wanting to devour this presumptuous foreign object.

But the distorted blackness around that thin line formed an extremely sturdy defense line, making the black particles that could devour everything unable to advance an inch.

"It can actually resist Mosha's corrosion?! This is?" Li Fan was shocked and looked towards the thin line that had suddenly appeared.

"The Tiansha Sword?"

The sword tip pointed straight ahead, and the killing intent gushed out as if it were tangible, forcing back all the black particles around it.

In the direction the Tiansha Sword was pointing, the black particles surged, and Mosha's figure reappeared.

He stared at the Tiansha Sword in front of him, seemingly with a solemn expression.

"What treasure is this? It can actually contend with Mosha?" Jiao Xiuyuan exclaimed in surprise.

Li Fan's heart was even more shaken, and at the same time, he felt an inexplicable excitement.

"Between Mosha and Tiansha, who will prevail?"

He carefully observed these two.

Extremely similar, yet seemingly fundamentally different.

Mosha was pure ink-black, an all-devouring profundity.

While Tiansha was a distorted black, the epitome of madness and destruction.

The two confronted each other, and the battle was about to begin.

However, Mosha suddenly glanced at the Myriad Immortals Island below.

Countless black particles instantly vibrated, and another figure, identical to Mosha, momentarily appeared.

It turned into a thin line, easily shattering the protective Seven-Colored Falling Immortal Formation.

It also led the ink-colored ocean to completely corrode everything on the island.

"This is..."

Li Fan was somewhat stunned.

He didn't expect that in the face of a great enemy, Mosha would still not forget to simultaneously carry out his own duties.

The sky gradually pressed down, and the figures of Mosha and Tiansha gradually disappeared into the black ocean.

Only the black particles that undulated like waves from time to time told of the terrifying battle above.

Li Fan's heart was filled with regret.

"It's a pity that I can't witness the battle between these two."
