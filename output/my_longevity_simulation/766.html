Li Fan felt like his head was about to explode from the unimaginable pain, and he couldn't help but let out a muffled groan.

So to the outsiders, Li Fan's line figure seemed to be violently shaking and swaying, as if receiving a tremendous impact.

Consequently, Li Fan felt a mysterious weakening within himself.

Alarmed, Li Fan focused his mind and tried his best to absorb and process the data that the Heavenly Mirror had infused into his consciousness.

But the complexity of this divine technique exceeded his imagination.

Despite his efforts for a long time, there was no sign of the redundant data decreasing.

"The data volume contained in this divine technique is too massive. If this continues..." Li Fan's heart sank.

With just this little effort, his line figure had already dissolved by nearly half.

"Should I abandon this divine technique and try again next time?"

In a flash, Li Fan suppressed this spontaneous thought.

Though shaken, he contemplated and found a better way to handle this.

Under Li Fan's control, the crystal of the Dao Stone at the bottom of the Primal Disc suddenly erupted with a powerful azure glow.

Similar to the white light that had previously infused the divine technique, it instantly swept across Li Fan's entire sea of consciousness.

Within the azure light, the data of the divine technique was completely absorbed.

Just like the endless waves continuously washing away the messy footprints on the shore.

As the azure light flickered on and off, the divine technique data filling Li Fan's sea of consciousness was gradually absorbed by the Dao Stone.

"With the Dao Stone, I don't need to rush. I can store the data first and study it slowly later."

The crisis gradually subsided, and Li Fan's thoughts returned to normal as he let out a silent sigh of relief.

Observing the Dao Stone, which seemed to have reached a certain limit in its load, Li Fan was startled.

Faint cracks were also vaguely visible on the surface of the crystal.

"This... Isn't it said that the divine techniques are at most at the Dao Divinity Realm? How could the data volume be so terrifying?"

Li Fan was momentarily filled with doubt and uncertainty.

Although he was not sure about the divine techniques of other Dao Divinity cultivators, he could confirm that his [Infinite Yin-Yang Art] was definitely not something ordinary Dao Divinity divine techniques could compare to.

"Could it be that the Heavenly Mirror has discovered something and left a hidden door?"

Li Fan was alarmed by this thought, but it was just his speculation. It was also possible that he was simply scaring himself.

After some contemplation, Li Fan decided to remain inactive for the time being.

He neither started learning the [Infinite Yin-Yang Art] nor deleted the stored divine technique data.
