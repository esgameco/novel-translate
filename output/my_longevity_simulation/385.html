Time continued to pass in this tense atmosphere.

In the blink of an eye, two years had passed since entering the Five Elements Daoist Realm.

During this time, there were indeed cultivators who came to support gradually.

But it was obvious that it was far from the large-scale reinforcements promised by Qi Buyi.

With more supporting cultivators arriving, through them, the others also learned why reinforcements had been delayed.

In the external world over these two years, many significant events had occurred.

Firstly, there were external threats.

The Wulao Association had stationed troops at the border again, repeatedly showing signs of probing and provocation.

Unlike before, the other side did not seem to have the intention of starting a large-scale conflict.

Instead, they concentrated all their forces on the Wind and Thunder Island separated from the Heavenly Spirit State.

Apart from the pressure brought by the Wulao Association.

There was also unrest within the Wanxian Alliance territory.

During this period, there emerged many voices opposing the widespread promotion of the Heavenly Profound Lock Spirit Formation.

Moreover, the voices of opposition did not come from unknown entities.

Most were cultivators with Deity Transformation or higher cultivation bases who held important positions in various states.

It was unknown what had happened, but these cultivators who had unanimously declared their staunch support for the formation building plan not long ago suddenly changed their tune, strongly expressing their opposition.

And their opposition was not baseless; they had ample arguments and reasons.

It was well known that no matter how exquisite a formation was, there would always be a day when it could be deciphered and broken.

Because the "Thousand-Faced Demon Lord," who had always been haunting the Wanxian Alliance's territory, had recently become even more rampant.

Many high-ranking cultivators within the states had not been able to escape his clutches.

Even in states where the Heavenly Profound Lock Spirit Formation had been constructed, it only slightly slowed down his killing spree.

Ultimately, they still failed to contain him.

Moreover, even if he was captured, so what?

Since the Wulao Association could cultivate one Thousand-Faced Demon Lord, then they could undoubtedly cultivate two or three.

At the beginning of the promotion, the greatest significance of the Heavenly Profound Lock Spirit Formation was to expose all the Wulao Association cultivators within the territory.

And now?

He openly ravaged your territory, and you were powerless to stop him.

What use was this Heavenly Profound Lock Spirit Formation?

It had to be admitted that these people's viewpoints were very reasonable.

Thus, when they raised their doubts to the Wanxian Alliance headquarters, it resonated with quite a few more people.

As the opposition grew stronger, the Wanxian Alliance headquarters, contrary to their usual practice, did not punish these dissenters but fell into a strange silence.

In this background, the Thousand-Faced Demon Lord's whereabouts became even more elusive, and his rampage grew increasingly ferocious.

Besides internal and external problems, Li Fan also heard an interesting story.

As the situation became increasingly tense, the Nine Mountain State at the border between the Wanxian Alliance and the Wulao Association, an organization called the Spirit Yuan Sect, was unexpectedly thriving.

It certainly attracted the attention of many cultivators.

All because the Spirit Yuan Sect had a unique cultivation technique.
