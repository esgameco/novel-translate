Chapter 878

Under the gaze of the Dharma Transmission Heavenly Venerate, the layers of defenses set up by the Five Elders Society in Jiushan Prefecture collapsed in an instant.

In the face of the absolute power gap, all struggles and resistance were futile!

All living beings within the prefecture, including the cultivators stationed by the Five Elders Society, the Lingyuan Sect followers, and even countless mortals.

They all perished in the moment the Dharma Transmission Heavenly Venerate opened her eyes!

Just as the Xuanhuang Realm could not withstand the direct gaze of a True Immortal.

The living beings of Jiushan Prefecture also could not face the might of Dharma Transmission directly!

In an instant, countless cultivators died.

Originally, there should have been endless falling phenomena in the sky.

But perhaps because the figure of Dharma Transmission did not dissipate.

Under the oppressive might, those falling phenomena also failed to condense for a long time.

This scene was as if even the Heavenly Dao of the Xuanhuang Realm was restrained in front of the Dharma Transmission Heavenly Venerate, not daring to wantonly issue ridicule and celebration for the death of cultivators.

......

The footage provided by the Five Elders Society abruptly ended.

Although it was indeed somewhat shocking.

But for Li Fan, who had already witnessed the scene of a True Immortal destroying the world...

It was nothing special.

After repeatedly savoring it, Li Fan still immediately noticed something amiss.

"Dharma Transmission? One of the thirty-three Immortal Chosen?"

During the 115th era, the Dharma Transmission Heavenly Venerate had once revealed her true appearance.

The entire Xuanhuang Realm trembled under her might.

As if the end of the world was about to come, and it could be completely torn apart by her power at any time.

In comparison, the Dharma Transmission Heavenly Venerate who appeared in Jiushan Prefecture this time was not only one step weaker.

"What is this, a projection? An avatar?"

A glimmer flashed in Li Fan's eyes.

In his perception, Wei Qizheng, one of the Immortal Chosen, was still alive and well.

And just now, the cultivators of the Five Elders Society had also mentioned that she, that is, the Dharma Transmission Phantom, might appear more frequently in the future.

This proved that it was not a one-time consumable means, but an operation that could be repeatedly reproduced.

"Although the strength is far inferior to the Dharma Transmission's true body, it is definitely the combat power of the Eternal Life Realm."

"More crucially, it is still unclear how the Dharma Transmission Projection appeared in the sky of Jiushan Prefecture. From beginning to end, those Immortal Chosen never appeared. If the location restriction can be ignored and the Dharma Transmission Projection can be summoned to descend at will..."

"Then the deterrence is too great. Before finding a countermeasure, the Five Elders Society simply does not dare to act rashly."

Who knows how many living beings were buried here not long ago. But it was very peculiar that not a bit of bloody smell could be smelled.

After all, under the oppressive might of Dharma Transmission, even blood was crushed out of existence.

"As expected of a means in the form of Dharma Transmission. Rather than killing, it's more like annihilation."

Li Fan sighed in his heart.

Having figured out what happened in Jiushan Prefecture, Li Fan did not linger here.

He quickly returned through the power of the earth veins.

"If I'm not mistaken, if the Five Elders Society wants to counterattack next, they are very likely to use the [Samantabhadra True Boat] to launch a surprise attack in the white fog."

"As long as the battle starts in the belly of the Immortal Alliance, the Wanxian Alliance will have to consider carefully if they want to use that Dharma Transmission Projection move again."

"But this kind of special move has the greatest effect only the first time. The Five Elders Society should be thinking of using it at the decisive battle moment to settle the matter once and for all..."

"Interesting. Let's see how the Five Elders Society will choose."
