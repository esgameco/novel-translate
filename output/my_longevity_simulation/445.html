Chapter 445

The sky darkened, and a piece of paper covered with golden characters suddenly appeared in the sky above the island.

It covered the sky, as if the next moment would bring down the oppression on all the people in the empire.

Qing's expression changed, and two green lights soared into the sky like sharp blades.

They collided with the huge piece of paper.

The golden paper was torn apart, revealing the sky once again.

However, the golden characters on the paper did not disappear.

Instead, they fluttered down towards the ground.

From Qing's body, a series of light blades emerged, dissolving the characters one by one.

It was evident that the two were fighting very restrainedly.

The anomalies they caused were limited to the surroundings of the island, and even observers like Li Fan and Li Chenfeng were unaffected.

After a while, when Qing completely suppressed the golden symbol of the Divine Oracle, she suppressed her anger and was about to question the other party's intentions.

However, she found that the mysterious person who had used the golden symbol said something to Li Fan before leaving on his own.

Qing narrowed her eyes but did not stop him.

Watching Li Fan approach boldly.

"What does the Wan Jie Alliance mean?"

At this moment, Qing asked with a cold tone.

Li Fan shrugged, "We mean no harm. However, since we are to cooperate, naturally we need to see your strength and whether it is worth it."

"We will help the empire accelerate its plan to break free from the Mysterious Yellow Realm, and you will need to allocate a portion of your strength to integrate into the Alliance."

"However..."

Li Fan paused slightly, "Considering the current situation of both parties, the organization believes that the cooperation plan can be gradually implemented."

"What do you mean by gradually?" Qing's tone softened.

Li Fan threw a storage ring over, Qing took the ring and checked it, surprised by its contents.

Inside were various materials needed by the empire.

Qingjing Crystal, Wanhua Jade Silk, and Tongbi Xuanshen...

Although the quantity was a bit less, it was rare to have every type included.

Having left the Mysterious Yellow Realm for thousands of years, Qing did not know the current prices of these items.

But she also knew that the value of the materials in the storage ring was definitely not low.

Thus, Qing's expression gradually softened.

"These are the first batch of materials."

"The quantity you need is too much. To avoid attracting the attention of the factions in the Mysterious Yellow Realm, we are gradually purchasing them in batches."

Li Fan explained.

"To eliminate your doubts and show the sincerity of the organization. These materials are provided free of charge to the empire."

"After about a year, when the next batch of materials is purchased, I will come again."

"However, at that time, the Wan Jie Alliance hopes to see the attitude of the empire."

"Then we can discuss the next steps of cooperation."

Li Fan bowed, didn't wait for Qing's response, and left directly.

"Saint..."

Li Chenfeng looked at Qing, hesitant to speak.

Qing furrowed her brows, carefully examined the storage ring, found nothing suspicious, and carefully put it away.

"What he said makes sense; we can gradually start the cooperation. Exchange those less important techniques with them first."

"Once anything goes wrong, we can cut losses in time."

It seemed far more complicated than it appeared on the surface.

Wan Xian Island.

After expressing gratitude to the Divine Oracle once again, Li Fan followed his usual routine and searched for information in the Tianxuan Mirror.

The results of the entrance examination were being gradually announced, causing joy and sorrow among the candidates.

There were still controversies about the admission status for some positions, but they quickly disappeared.

In the southwest sea area of Congyunhai, a storm devastated over ten mortal islands.

The price of the special product, Bixuan Lu, experienced a slight fluctuation. Li Fan bought low and sold high, making a small profit.

The Taixu Mountain Range, one of the ten veins of the Taihua Mountain in Taihua State, suddenly collapsed for unknown reasons.

When Li Fan swept over this piece of information, his gaze slightly narrowed.

"Hmm? Ten veins of the Taihua Mountain? I recall, in my past lives, I only saw the nine veins of Taihua."

"It seems that this time the casualties are quite severe."

Li Fan pondered for a moment, but did not delve deeper.

He recorded the key points and continued to browse other news.

Afterward, Li Fan separately searched for the recent situation of "Yufulaoren," "Wuchi'er," "Zhongshentong," "Shuofeng," and others.

Yufulaoren was still wandering around the Central Four States near the Wanxian League.

Wuchi'er and Zhongshentong had not gained much fame.

Shuofeng had not started rebuilding yet and remained a renowned Godly Immortal.

Based on his experience from the previous life, Li Fan speculated on the possible time of their adventures.

A month later, Li Fan woke up from seclusion.

Feeling that the time was about right, he directly transferred half a million contribution points to He Zhenghao.

"This time, luck was good; I quickly sensed an opportunity and achieved the goal early."

Li Fan said lazily.

A moment later, came the ecstatic voice of He Zhenghao.

"Senior's divination is truly incredible!"

"Hehe, the backlash of the sect's secret technique is still inevitable. The next recruitment will still be at the time previously set. Go and explain it to them."

After instructing him, Li Fan ignored He Zhenghao and swallowed another Enlightenment Pill before entering seclusion again.

Time flew by, and in the blink of an eye, it was the ninth year.

During this time, Li Fan's initial layout began to show results.

More than twenty subtle increases in cultivation emerged in his Dantian, due to various feedbacks.

Combined with nearly a year of inspiration and cultivation under the influence of the Enlightenment and Dao Pills.

After reaching the peak of the Golden Pill Intermediate Stage, Li Fan was only troubled by the bottleneck of the "Heaven's Calamity" for just over ten days.

He successfully advanced to the later stage of the Golden Pill.

Li Fan exhaled slowly, easing his cultivation for now.

"Next, whether to intimidate the people of the empire or explore the void of the Tianlingzhou, acting alone may be inadequate."

"Perhaps I should seek some assistance."

Li Fan pondered for a moment and quietly left Wan Xian Island, heading straight for the Tianyang Cave.
