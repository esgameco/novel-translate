Chapter 749

"It's really amazing."

The girl's tone became somewhat dreamy: "It's like seeing another version of myself in the mirror, and I can't help but feel close to it."

"Master Qing, what exactly is going on here?"

Upon hearing the girl's affirmative answer, a look of disbelief also appeared in Xiao Qing's eyes.

"Could it be..."

...

Xu Bai was unaware of what was happening on the other side of the wall.

At this moment, his mind was completely immersed in the enlightenment of the Red Rope Sword Intent.

As his understanding deepened, the red rope that suppressed the black flesh seemed to come alive. It gently floated to Xu Bai's side, affectionately circling him.

After an unknown period of time, Xu Bai suddenly opened his eyes and flicked his fingers lightly.

An almost imperceptible red light flashed from his hand, then flew towards the twisted flesh ahead.

The wailing voices echoing in the temple fell silent for a moment.

Though it immediately resumed after a moment.

But this strange phenomenon still greatly shocked the woman in a gauze skirt waiting behind Xu Bai.

"Sir..." She couldn't help but ask.

Xu Bai, however, stopped her question with a gesture, only looking at the faint bloodstain that appeared on his wrist at some point.

With the physique of a newly-born Yuan Infant cultivator, even if he suffered cuts and wounds, they could instantly heal. However, this slight bloodstain seemed inherent and couldn't be dispelled.

"Such serious side effects." Xu Bai couldn't help but furrow his brow slightly.

"It seems that Xu Ke's disappearance years ago may also be related to his repeated use of the Red Rope Sword Art."

"Although the Wooden Sword contains all the sword principles in the world, it cannot strike first."

"Although the Rope Sword's killing intent is unparalleled, it requires one's own life in exchange."

A glint shimmered in Xu Bai's eyes. "They all have certain limitations. Interesting..."

Next, Xu Bai successively visited the remaining eight temples.

Aside from deeper understanding of the Red Rope Sword Art, he also witnessed the unyielding gaze of the three unburied heads of the Crimson Nine-Headed Beast.

The hair, flesh, and eyeballs of one head had been hollowed out by the Imperial people, leaving only the mottled skull.

However, the other two heads remained alive and spirited as if freshly dead.

One could sense the boundless malevolence and resentment emanating from them even from a distance.

"A truly heinous beast!"

This was Xu Bai's first encounter with a true ancient monstrous beast, causing an inexplicable chill in his heart.

"A thousand years after death, the vicious nature remains. I wonder how fierce it was in its prime. And Mr. Bai, who managed to slay it with just a red rope..."

Thoughts raced through Xu Bai's mind.

After finishing the tour, Xu Bai returned to the towering palace.

Looking at the woman sitting next to Xiao Qing, her face veiled, Xu Bai felt a strange connection in his heart.

He tried to raise his right hand.

Something that surprised him happened.

The woman in front, despite no prior agreement, also raised her right hand almost synchronously.

Without prior coordination, they were almost perfectly synchronized.

It felt like there was a mirror between them.

Except for some differences in appearance, the veiled woman was almost like himself.

"The Sacred Blood Embryo Transformation Technique." In an instant, Xu Bai knew the reason for this phenomenon.

He also knew the identity of the woman in front of him.

Empress.

The descendant of Xu Ke, the nominal ruler of the Empire.

But he clearly could not reveal it.

Feigning extreme surprise, he suddenly stood up, pointing at the Empress, and exclaimed, "What is going on here?"

Xiao Qing observed Xu Bai's every expression carefully, trying to find some flaws. But she was disappointed.

Xu Bai's behavior was almost identical to the Empress.

"It seems that he is also unaware of his true identity," Xiao Qing thought to herself.

At this moment, Xu Bai seemed to have calmed down from his initial shock. He stared at the Empress, his eyes filled with deep perplexity.

"Who is this? Although we are meeting for the first time, I feel a strong sense of familiarity with her."

"It's not an illusion. I feel the same way," the Empress softly responded.

Now, Xu Bai seemed even more shocked.

Turning back to Xiao Qing, he asked, "Friend Qing, what is going on here?"

Xiao Qing stared at him for a long time before speaking. Instead of answering, she asked, "Xu friend, how much do you know about your origins?"

"My origins?" Xu Bai looked puzzled.

"I am just an ordinary human, originally living in a small world. Later, by chance, I joined the Multiverse Union, embarking on the path of immortality. My closest blood relatives have long passed away..."

"What's wrong? Is there something unusual about my origins?" Xu Bai asked.

Xiao Qing looked into Xu Bai's eyes, trying to discern the truth in his words.

After a long silence, she finally gave up.

Then Xiao Qing took out a portrait, with the figure on it almost identical to Xu Bai.

"What is this..." Xu Bai continued to play dumb.

"This is the founder of the Empire, also my master," Xiao Qing said in a deep tone. "His name is Xu Ke."

"Many years ago, he bid farewell to everyone and disappeared. Since then, his whereabouts have been unknown."

Xu Bai stared at the portrait, seemingly struck by the heavy information, standing still in shock.

After a long time, he finally spoke slowly, "He also surnamed Xu. Could it be that I am also a descendant of Xu Ke?"

"From the current situation, this seems to be the only reasonable explanation," Xiao Qing's tone also sounded uncertain.

"Friend Xu, where is your hometown, in which small world? Based on what I know about the master, he doesn't seem like someone who would marry and have children..." Xiao Qing asked inexplicably.

"My hometown is..." Xu Bai began but suddenly stopped.

Coming back to his senses, he immediately accused, looking at Xiao Qing and the Empress with suspicion, "Friend Qing, are you playing tricks on me like a child? How could this be so coincidental?"

"Enough talk; what is your purpose?" Xu Bai looked coldly at Xiao Qing as his attitude suddenly changed.

Faced with Xu Bai's sudden change in attitude, Xiao Qing couldn't help but be stunned.

After a slight smile, she said, "Honestly, even I find it hard to believe. But everything is just so coincidental. It's like it's meant to be, that I would meet you thousands of years later when I had long given up."

"As for whether what I said is true, there is a way to find out."

After saying this, she signaled to the Empress.

The Empress took out a scepter and lightly tapped the ground.

Suddenly, a hole appeared on the floor, swallowing the three of them instantly.

Xu Bai did not resist and found himself in a vast space without visible end.

Rows of different beast carcasses filled this place.

In the distance, various mechanical devices seemed to be stored.

Xu Bai wanted to move forward but found an invisible barrier blocking his way.

"This is the Imperial Treasury, where the wealth accumulated by the Empire over thousands of years is stored."

(End of this chapter)
