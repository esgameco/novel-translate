Chapter 916

Not a single bit was accepted.

But this was within Li Qing's expectations.

The wonder of the wooden sword was the pure principle of swordsmanship.

How could it easily accommodate other principles?

Li Qing was not discouraged, trying different methods.

Time passed slowly like this.

The black sword became more solid, its aura becoming stronger.

One day, the black sword, which had always strongly rejected foreign objects, suddenly absorbed a bit of Li Qing's insight!

Li Qing's eyes brightened as she quickly identified the presence that seemed to be able to merge with the black sword!

It turned out to be "clumsiness"!

"Hidden sharpness in clumsiness, the advantage of collecting the sword. By not being aggressive, one can achieve ultimate victory..."

In Li Qing's mind, an endless stream of inspiration burst forth.

Some images seemed familiar, but she didn't know where they came from.

However, Li Qing did not mind. Instead, she used the almost gushing inspiration to continuously infuse the black sword.

The previously aloof black sword, which was only interested in swordsmanship and nothing else, was now exceptionally cooperative.

It even seemed eager, actively accepting Li Qing's infusion.

As the way of clumsiness slowly merged in, the black sword, which was originally gradually taking shape and beginning to reveal its sharpness, slowly lost its edge.

It became dark and inconspicuous again.

But Li Qing knew that this was the black sword breaking free from the usual swordsmanship constraints, symbolizing a further breakthrough.

In the sword mound, the countless unclaimed swords remained unaware.

Only the central wonder of the wooden sword emitted a faint, almost invisible sword hum.

As if expressing its joy.

Because of this sword hum, the myriad swords bowed their heads once more. Only the black sword by Li Qing's side seemed to be bowing as well.

But stood motionless.

"It's done!"

Li Qing knew that the next step to completely control the black sword, the wonder of the wooden sword, was not far away.

She closed her eyes, injecting the last essence of swordsmanship insight into the black sword.

After the wonder of the wooden sword hum, the sword mound fell into an increasingly silent state.

The silence was terrifying, and even in Li Qing's ears, there were various hallucinations.

As if the myriad unclaimed swords were whispering, tempting Li Qing to abandon the black sword and take other swords.

Scenes of swords destroying the heavens and earth, with their magnificent power, constantly appeared in Li Qing's mind.

Attempting to disturb Li Qing's determination.

However, Li Qing, who he was, could not be easily swayed.

He knew this might be a covert measure by the wonder of the wooden sword.

The reason may be it was reluctant to let go of the black sword and thus spontaneously obstructing it.

But, regardless, these tricks could not affect Li Qing in the end.

Facing the myriad illusions, Li Qing remained unmoved.

Her mind and the black sword merged, with nothing else interfering.

Until three months later.

One day, Li Qing suddenly opened her eyes.

The next moment, she reached out her right hand and naturally gripped the hilt of the black sword.

Then she gently drew it.

There was no earth-shattering noise like a divine object emerging.

To be precise, there was not even a slight sound.

In the tranquil atmosphere, the black sword fell into Li Qing's hand like this.

The myriad unclaimed swords in the sword mound seemed completely unaware of this.

Even the central wonder of the wooden sword showed no sign of objection.

The silence in the sword mound indicated Li Qing's success.

"Creating a false wonder of heaven and earth to establish the foundation of the path."

Li Qing silently chanted in her heart, and then the essence of the black sword instantly turned into a faint light, merging into her body.

It flew into her elixir field and settled down.

Moments later, the spiritual power in Li Qing's body, at the perfection stage of the golden elixir realm, instantly boiled.

Like ten thousand streams rushing into the sea, all surged towards the black sword.

Under the surging spiritual power wash, the black sword gradually became ethereal.

Completely merging into Li Qing's elixir field, becoming her path foundation.

"To peek into the ways of heaven and earth to refine the golden elixir."

Li Qing chanted in her heart again.

The overwhelming spiritual power continued to rush towards the foundation formed by the black sword.

In the vortex of spiritual power, the shadow of the black sword became more and more obscure.

Eventually, dissolving completely but seemed to form an abyss, engulfing all the spiritual power.

Boom!

A bright light erupted, like the radiance when the world was first created.

Illuminating Li Qing's body completely.

The light flickered, lasting for a few breaths of time.

When the dazzling light finally faded away, a completely black, exuding an ancient and simple aura, the unique golden elixir appeared in Li Qing's elixir field.

If one looked closely, they could faintly see countless sword shadows on the black golden elixir.

But as if facing darkness, the illusion disappeared upon closer inspection.

A golden elixir was formed in just a few breaths of time.

Li Qing, who was originally just an empty shell in the perfect stage of the golden elixir realm, had now become a genuine golden elixir cultivator who had formed a foundation and achieved elixir using the wonder of the black sword.

However, Li Qing was not satisfied.

In her mind, Li Qing continued to chant.

Ordinary cultivators needed a blessed cave to reach the Nascent Soul realm, but those who established the foundation with the wonder of the heavens did not need it.

Because the wonder of the heavens is a part of the heavenly path.

It could completely evolve oneself into a blessed cave!

Li Qing's mind moved, and the black golden elixir that had just formed exploded instantly.

The aura inside diffused and spread throughout Li Qing's body.

The numerous black golden elixir fragments immediately flew to various parts of her body in an apparently random distribution but concealing profound reasoning!

The distribution of the golden elixir fragments could vaguely reveal the original appearance of the black sword!

With these remnants as a foundation, Li Qing's spiritual energy, flesh and blood, tendons, bones, everything in her body began to melt like liquid, absorbed by these remnants.

Externally, Li Qing instantly appeared like a puppet torn asunder, becoming malformed and distorted.

Even with the terrifying sound of flesh and blood being forcibly extracted, along with the hideous appearance caused by the squeezing of flesh and muscles.

All of this seemed sinister and magical.

Facing the surging pain, Li Qing didnâ€™t care.

She only accelerated the process of the golden elixir remnants devouring her body.

When all the remains of her flesh and blood in the air were completely absorbed.

The scattered golden elixir fragments seemed to form a network of meridians.

Interconnected with threads of dark red tissues, eerie and mesmerizing.

Suddenly, a beam of light simultaneously emerged in the golden elixir remnants.

Then, the body transformed in an instant.
