Chapter 895

Causing everyone present to lose track of the Xuantian Master.

"Indeed, besides the Heavenly Lord, the only ones who can influence and control the Tianxuan Mirror are their Xuantian Sect!"

This thought flashed through the young man's mind.

After scanning around and not finding any trace of the other party, the young man's heart sank slightly, about to give the order to pursue.

However, he suddenly noticed something was wrong.

"Hmm?"

"I have already set up the Nine Heavens and Ten Earths formation in advance here. If the formation is not broken, he absolutely has no possibility of escaping."

"Meaning, he is still here?!"

The young man then commanded the group of black-robed cultivators to scan the space again.

To the young man's surprise, they could not find any trace of the disappeared Xuantian Master of the Xuantian Sect at all!

The young man's face as a transmitter of the law turned somewhat grim.

"The Xuantian Sect..."

"Indeed, they are not easy to deal with. No wonder they survived from the joint annihilation of the Ten Sects of the Immortal Dao."

The young man's gaze continued to scan back and forth in the space.

"Where exactly are you hiding?"

Suddenly, his gaze focused.

Landing on the group of black-robed cultivators under his command.

The young man faintly felt that something was amiss.

"How many people did I bring along this time?"

A question involuntarily surfaced in his mind.

However, what shocked him was that with such a simple question...

He couldn't remember at all!

Having lived in seclusion for who knows how many years, this kind of thing happening to him was simply unimaginable!

"So, this is the cunning aspect of Xuantian Sect's techniques..."

The young man narrowed his eyes, various rumors about the ancient Xuantian Sect emerged in his mind.

Realizing that the opponent was hiding among this group of black-robed cultivators under his command, the young man then ordered them to verify each other's identities.

Although the young man was not very familiar with the vast majority of black-robed cultivators.

But these cultivators definitely knew each other well.

However, what the young man never expected was that after verifying each other.

They couldn't find anyone with a mismatched identity.

Each person's information was flawless.

Even in their minds, they had memories of their past interactions with each other.

...

At this point, the young man's complexion became extremely unsightly.

At this point in time, even if he was unwilling, he had to admit.

He might have overstepped this time.

As one of the earliest to follow the Transmitter Heavenly Lord, and one of those who helped overturn the Ten Sects of the Immortal Dao.

The young man actually didn't pay much attention to this so-called Xuantian Master of the Xuantian Sect.

After all, the Transmitter Heavenly Lord and his followers were the defeated who overthrew the Xuantian Sect.

No matter how strong the Xuantian Sect was, what could they do?

So, for the sake of monopolizing unique skills, he had come this time without informing other Transmitters.

Unexpectedly, he had now fallen into such a dilemma.

The young man's expression kept changing.

And now he had two choices, one was to inform the headquarters of the Immortal Alliance and provide the list of black-robed cultivators who participated in this operation.

With a simple comparison, the hidden Xuantian Master could be found.

But doing so would definitely alert other Transmitters.

And could even invite ridicule.

After some consideration, the young man made another choice.

He pointed at one of the black-robed cultivators at random and coldly said, "You, take your own life."

With a swift and forceful pat on their heads, their breath vanished in an instant.

Their body also turned into dust, as if dissolving into the air.

Only a green light wrapped around something, breaking through the air.

The young man's face showed no emotion as he looked at the next black-robed cultivator.

In the gaze of the young man's deadly stare, one by one, the black-robed cultivators took their own lives.

What was eerie was that the phenomenon of falling cultivators, which should have occurred in the sky, never appeared from beginning to end.

A total of thirty-five black-robed cultivators came with him.

Now, twenty-six had taken their own lives, but the hidden Xuantian Master was still not found.

"Could it be that my luck is so bad?"

"Or did I judge wrongly?"

The young man's face became extremely grim.

But at this point, there was no room for regret.

Determined, he continued to give the command for suicide like a gambler on a losing streak.

Until...

Only one black-robed cultivator was left in the field.

Just like the ones before, he raised his hand and was about to strike his own head forcefully.

But just before his head burst, he stopped.

Lowering his hand slowly, he looked somewhat mockingly at the young transmitter in front of him. "Is this the current ruling cultivation organization?"

"Treating subordinates as mere grass..."

"You say our Xuantian Sect is ruthless and unjust, but in my eyes, you are far worse."

The young man was not provoked by Li Chen's words.

In his eyes, those black-robed cultivators were merely resources that could be regenerated at any time.

Not to mention the death of dozens.

Even if hundreds or thousands died, he wouldn't feel a thing.

The young man would never believe that it was his luck that caused the last person he randomly pointed at to be the Xuantian Master.

This should be due to the inducement of the opponent's cunning technique.

Indeed, Li Chen did not deny it.

"Dark magic?"

Li Chen snorted, "These are the supreme lineages of the Dao, the authentic Immortal Sects! How can people like you see through them!"

The young man's identity was extremely noble.

For thousands of years, no one had dared to speak so arrogantly in front of him!

Especially with the deep, genuine disdain in the opponent's eyes, it made the young man unable to contain his anger.

"Now, it's just the two of us here. Let's see what other tricks you can play!"

There was a strong killing intent in the young man's tone, his clothes moving without wind.

The space sealed by the Nine Heavens and Ten Earths formation suddenly lit up with twinkling starlight.

Then, like the arrival of nightfall, stars covered the sky.

Countless beams of starlight shone, like sharp arrows, aiming to pierce through the Xuantian Master!

Faced with the young man's sudden attack, Li Chen seemed to be stunned, motionless, standing still.

Just as countless starlight was about to hit, ready to pierce through, the young man faintly felt that something was amiss.

But it was already too late.

In the next moment, numerous small holes appeared on the young man's body.

Blood gushed out, and he was pierced through his heart by his own starlight!

Under the thousands of starlights, the young man's body turned into a puddle of mud.

After a short moment, with a slight wriggling.

A whole body emerged from the void.

"You..."

The young man looked at the black-robed cultivator in front of him, finally feeling a sense of fear rising in his heart.

It was an emotion he hadn't tasted in a long time.

"Me?"

"Am I not you?"

Looking at the young man, the black-robed cultivator showed a slightly eerie smile.

A sense of confusion arose in the young man's eyes.
