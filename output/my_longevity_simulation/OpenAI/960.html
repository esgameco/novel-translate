Chapter 960

According to the shape of Li Fan's soul, a tightly wrapped protective layer formed on the periphery.

"No wonder it can be controlled effortlessly without refinement, it is simply tailored like a treasure, perfectly fitting the soul," Li Fan remarked.

"And..."

Squinting his eyes, Li Fan used the power of his soul to impact the layer of golden needles from the inside out.

As the soul impact collided with the protective layer, Li Fan felt a soft force dispersing him, followed by ripples spreading outwards towards the impact point.

The impact was evenly distributed, easily resolved.

If the protective layer had such a strong protective force internally, it was logical that its external toughness was even more formidable.

After continuous experimentation, Li Fan gave the evaluation of "as firm as golden soup".

"However, the protection of the golden needles is limited to the soul aspect. If it is pure physical harm, its protective function is actually inferior to ordinary armor treasures."

"Needles unbroken, person unharmed..."

"The words of the Heavenly Physician are not exaggerated. As long as the soul is intact, with his strength, helping to reshape the flesh is but a simple matter."

"And to destroy the soul, the first step is to break through the protection of the golden needles."

"Even..."

Suddenly, a scene flashed in Li Fan's mind.

At the moment of a fatal blow, just before the golden needles were about to shatter, a golden light rose into the sky, carrying Li Fan's soul and flew back into the hands of the Heavenly Physician.

Li Fan could be sure that the Heavenly Physician still had certain control over the sent golden needles.

While this was undoubtedly due to the care for the "sole current inheritor of the Heavenly Law Realm," it was not a good thing for Li Fan.

How could one rest peacefully beside their enemy?

With Li Fan's character, he couldn't possibly allow something he couldn't fully control to wrap around his soul.

As for others, it was fine, but if his soul was frozen and he couldn't return, it would truly be a disaster!

"Is there a way to enjoy the protection of the golden needles while ensuring absolute safety..."

In front of the faceless statue, Li Fan began to ponder.

In fact, at present, he had few trump cards that could restrain the power of the Heavenly Physician, and they were all uncontrollable variables.

Such as chaos, hunger, gray mold, etc.

One slip could lead to catastrophic consequences.

After much consideration, Li Fan ultimately focused on the golden wish power generated by the faceless statue.

The golden wish power had been stored in Li Fan's lifeblood for some time, and whenever it was almost full, it could compress on its own without Li Fan's manipulation.

Therefore, despite the tremendous accumulation of golden wish power during this time, it still couldn't fill even one lifeblood vessel.

However, this lifeblood vessel with Li Fan's appearance looked dazzling and sacred.

The golden wish power was a existence beyond the levels of power systems Li Fan currently accessed, and he had not yet found a way to use it.

The only thing he could do was guide it.

"Wish power, wish power..."

"Could invoking it according to what is in the heart unleash its power?"

Li Fan had a thought suddenly and began to try.

The result was obvious - it seemed like they were completely different species, and the golden wish power was indifferent to Li Fan's call.

After unsuccessful attempts, Li Fan had to change his approach.

He created another lifeblood vessel and slowly guided the golden wish power into it. At the same time, he triggered the transformation of the lifeblood vessel into a long rope-like form.

Like a pipeline, carrying the slowly moving golden wish power.

As this pipeline wound around the golden needle protection enveloping Li Fan's soul, it slowly weaved into an outer garment.

Throughout the process, Li Fan was extremely cautious.

Whether it was the Heavenly Physician's golden needles or the golden wish power, they were powers he currently could not control.

Only by guiding them, forming a mutual constraint between the two, could he feel a bit at ease.

Indeed, doing so outside his soul was extremely dangerous.

The slightest mistake could lead to the complete destruction of his soul.

But since the means were available, Li Fan still had the qualification to boldly try!

"If things really change, these two must be the first to spot each other's colossal presence. Subconsciously ignoring me as a mere ant. It's human nature."

"It's safer for me to face either of them alone than both together."

"This is the way of balance."

If the Heavenly Physician's golden needles erupted, they would definitely first trigger the golden wish power.

And vice versa.

If Li Fan were to be struck by an external fatal blow, the assailant would face the dual counterattacks of the Heavenly Physician's golden needles and the golden wish power.

Inappropriately put, Li Fan was currently akin to having two linked bombs attached to him.

Any act that could harm Li Fan would completely set off the bombs.

Li Fan believed that even the Longevity Heavenly Venerable would not fare well under the simultaneous attacks of these two forces!

And in times of necessity, Li Fan could even voluntarily detonate these two bombs, achieving unexpected results!

"Utilizing circumstances in the situation and navigating through it. Revealing my true nature..."

When the balance between the two forces was finally achieved, Li Fan quietly admired his masterpiece, feeling somewhat proud.

However, he soon came back to reality.

"This is just a temporary expedient, if I can research thoroughly the Heavenly Physician's golden needles and form a soul protection of my own..."

"Since the Heavenly Physician can achieve this, there is no reason I can't."

Li Fan carefully felt the energy attributes of the Heavenly Physician's golden needles outside his soul, combined with the content of the "Heavenly Physician's Scripture" and the "Sky-Repairing Record", silently pondering.

As such, it was uncertain for how long he pondered.

Li Fan was startled awake by an image in his mind.

It turned out that the task he had given the followers of Red Dust Compassionate Immortal to find someone who could restore a cultivator's appearance based on their skull finally had some progress.

Originally, Li Fan thought it would be a simple matter, but in reality, in the vast Xuanhuang Realm, besides himself, there was truly no one else with such a need.

Since there was no practical value in usage, cultivators did not delve into this technique.

Only after Li Fan issued the mission and offered rewards did cultivators attempt to do so.

"Tong Yanzhou, Luo Sizhen."

After grasping the information of the targeted cultivator, Li Fan thought for a moment and ultimately decided to personally take action.

As it concerned something Luo Sizhen highly desired, it had to be taken seriously.

His figure flew swiftly, ethereal.

Soon, Li Fan arrived at Luo Sizhen's cave mansion.
