Chapter 910

Just in that year when Li Fan was patiently waiting for changes in the world of Xuanyellow, and for his clone to successfully take away the Black Sword.

Unexpectedly, something happened that he had never experienced in his previous cycles of reincarnation.

On that day, Tianyang, who was lying idle in the Spirit Wood Realm, suddenly got up.

He looked towards a certain direction in the Xuanyellow Realm, his expression serious.

Tianyang's unusual behavior immediately raised Li Fan's alertness.

He knew the temperament of this junior brother, who was indifferent to almost everything except himself and his senior brother.

For him to look so serious now, things must not be simple.

"Junior brother, what's wrong?" Li Fan flashed over to Tianyang and casually asked.

"I felt a very strong spatial fluctuation descending upon the Xuanyellow Realm. It was fleeting, and I couldn't pinpoint the location."

"But it definitely won't be wrong," Tianyang spoke more fluently as he continued, getting straight to the point.

Upon hearing this, Li Fan's heart skipped a beat, "A very strong spatial fluctuation? What does that mean?"

"If it was simply a spatial transmission within the Xuanyellow Realm, it wouldn't emit such a strong wave. This means that the Xuanyellow Realm..."

"Has an outsider arrived?" Li Fan continued.

Tianyang nodded slightly.

Li Fan's expression also turned extremely solemn.

"To think that there are still other cultivators alive in this deepest star sea?"

"Or could it be that this person comes from beyond the High Wall?"

Numerous thoughts flashed through Li Fan's mind.

His emotions surged, and even with his composure, he couldn’t calm down for a long time.

Apart from the arrival of an outsider being too shocking, the more crucial reason was that this event seemed to have never occurred in his past cycles of reincarnation.

What caused the unexpected appearance of an outsider?

"There might be another possibility."

A sudden idea popped in Li Fan's mind, "This matter occurred in previous lives, but with my power back then, I couldn’t sense it."

After pondering for a while, Li Fan finally calmed down.

With such a huge commotion, the Wanxian Alliance and the Five Elders must have also noticed.

Let's see how they react.

"Junior brother, you've been working hard recently. See if you can try to track that spatial fluctuation," Li Fan instructed.

Tianyang nodded in agreement.

As it turned out, the arrival of the outsider was not known to the general public.

But for the apparent rulers of the Xuanyellow Realm, it indeed had a significant impact.

The most obvious change was that the previously tense situation suddenly eased without any explanation.

For over thirty days, the Wanxian Alliance’s Heavenly Venerable and the Five Elders did not appear again.

The situation at the Five Elders' side was unusually quiet as well.

This scenario seemed like both sides had stopped their activities and were focusing on something else.

Li Fan also sent a message to the Five Elders, inquiring about the strong spatial fluctuation.

Upon receiving a confirmed response from the Five Elders, they informed him that they were investigating the cause of the spatial fluctuation.

They also mentioned that if the Wanjie Cooperative discovered anything, they should notify them as soon as possible.

...

"This seriousness is too rare." Li Fan stroked his chin, his brows slightly furrowed.

"Most crucially, the Wanxian Alliance and the Five Elders have been searching together for so many days. Yet they still haven't found this outsider?"

Li Fan felt uneasy.

After pacing back and forth for a while, he finally made up his mind.

He headed to the old dilapidated temple in Yongyun Prefecture and stood before Senior Shi Ban.

"Are you talking about the recent fluctuation?" Senior Shi Ban, seeming to have truly comprehended the Dao of fishing, had a mysterious aura about him. Like the tide, his aura rose and fell, emanating a daunting effect on those around him.

With Li Fan's current strength, whenever he tried to approach, he would be forcefully pushed back by this unintentionally emitted power. Li Fan couldn’t help but feel shocked.

"Yes. The intensity of the spatial fluctuation clearly exceeded the specifications of internal spatial transmission in the Xuanyellow Realm. If it's an outsider, I fear..." Li Fan spoke with concern.

"The Wanxian Alliance and the Five Elders have been searching for so long without any results. So, for the safety of the Xuanyellow Realm, I can only seek help from Senior Shi Ban," Li Fan said.

Senior Shi Ban remained silent for a long time.

Yet it wasn’t that he didn’t want to answer, but more as if he had fallen asleep.

"In fact, this is a misunderstanding. There is no outsider..."

"Anyway, if you truly want to know the ins and outs of this matter, go find this person." Senior Shi Ban pointed towards Li Fan lightly.

Subsequently, corresponding information emerged in Li Fan's mind.

Senior Shi Ban returned to his contemplation of the Dao of fishing. Li Fan bowed respectfully before leaving.

"The Qinghuang Prefecture, Han Zhong?"

Reading the information sent by Senior Shi Ban, Li Fan frowned involuntarily.

He had visited the Qinghuang Prefecture before.

Located in the southwest corner of the Wanxian Alliance, it was desolate and remote, sparsely populated.

Compared to the Cloud Cluster Sea, it was even more backward, being the most remote area in the entire Wanxian Alliance.

"Could it be that precisely because of this, it's more suitable for hiding?"

With such thoughts in mind, Li Fan transformed into a streak of light and headed towards the Qinghuang Prefecture.

After some effort, when he arrived at his destination and stood in front of Han Zhong's residence.

Before he could enter, Li Fan caught a whiff of alcohol.

"Why doesn't anyone believe me?"

"Good wine!"

"Haha, none of it's real, it's all just a dream! *Burp*"

Li Fan's brow furrowed even more.

Upon entering the room, he saw a cultivator with unkempt beard and ragged clothes, lying on the floor with no dignity.

He tightly gripped a wine jug in his hand, his expression dazed.

Li Fan coldly asked, "Are you Han Zhong?"

Han Zhong glanced at Li Fan somewhat confusedly, remained silent for a while, and then as if he hadn't seen Li Fan, went back to drinking from his jug.

With a snort, Li Fan was about to use the Soul Search Technique to extract the secrets from this man's drunken mind.

However, just as Li Fan's right hand was about to reach Han Zhong's head.

As if burned by fire, Li Fan instantly retracted his hands.

"Hmm?"

Li Fan stared at his palm, somewhat puzzled.

Although his outward physical body seemed unaffected, Li Fan keenly sensed that the moment his soul touched the other person, it decayed!

Not injured, but as if his soul was depleted, akin to a cultivator on the verge of death, with a withered spirit!

Li Fan couldn’t help but be shaken.

He was aware that his soul's strength at this moment, even among the Integrated Dao cultivators, was almost unparalleled.
