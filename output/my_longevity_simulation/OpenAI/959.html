Chapter 959

As he had expected, halfway through, the voice of the Heavenly Doctor once again sounded in his mind.

"You did well."

Obviously, the Heavenly Doctor had sensed that the seal of the Cloud Water Celestial Palace had been reinforced once more.

"With your help, it really saved me some trouble."

"If I had gone..."

The Heavenly Doctor chuckled lightly, then fell silent.

"In the process of reinforcing the seal, I encountered some things." Before the Heavenly Doctor could speak again, Li Fan took the initiative to report everything that had happened in the Cloud Water Celestial Palace.

After a long silence, Li Fan couldn't help but ask, "Elder, what really happened back then..."

It seemed that Li Fan's use of "Elder" had stirred up memories deep in the Heavenly Doctor's mind.

His tone unexpectedly fluctuated slightly, "To discover the hidden truth in Hanhai's consciousness, it's quite unexpected, considering your aptitude. No wonder with your talent, Tianjue would regard you as a successor..."

Li Fan's expression slightly stiffened.

"The events of the past..."

"Leave it for me to bear. Knowing too much won't do you any good."

"You are still too weak now. When your dao reaches its peak, perhaps then you will be qualified to know."

The Heavenly Doctor rarely explained a few more sentences.

Li Fan wanted to speak but hesitated, ultimately nodding slightly.

"Oh, how's your study of the 'Records of Mending the Sky'? Are there any parts that are difficult to understand?"

After a moment, the Heavenly Doctor asked again. It seemed that he was going to personally help Li Fan with any doubts.

Li Fan couldn't miss this great opportunity, immediately presenting the doubts he had encountered during his self-study process for clarification.

The Heavenly Doctor patiently answered each of them.

Indeed, being the Heavenly Doctor, his understanding of the path of "medicine" had already reached an extraordinary level.

With just a few words, Li Fan often had sudden realizations.

Moreover, in the Heavenly Doctor's casual remarks, new viewpoints not recorded in the "Records of Mending the Sky" were brought up.

Li Fan eagerly absorbed the Heavenly Doctor's teachings.

Good times are always short-lived.

Soon after, it seemed that the Heavenly Doctor encountered something and interrupted the teaching.

"You should have been hiding in a small world before, that's why I spent some effort but still hadn't found you."

Li Fan's heart skipped a beat, about to respond.

But then he heard the Heavenly Doctor continue, "Hmm, a good choice. Until you succeed in cultivation, it's safer for you to stay in the small world as much as possible."

"Keep practicing. Remember, techniques are secondary. What truly matters is contemplating the operation of the heavenly path and the essence of the world."

"No matter how powerful a technique is, it will ultimately belong to others. Only what truly suits oneself is the most perfect technique."

Upon hearing these words, Li Fan felt as if struck by a lightning bolt, his mind shaken.

"A technique truly suited to oneself?"

His eyes seemed perplexed, sinking into endless contemplation.

"A gentleman hides his skills until the right time. The current Xuanhuang Realm is not your true stage. Wait a little longer...” The Heavenly Doctor's voice was deep, hinting at something.

After that, a golden light descended from the sky.

Swiftly flying and arriving in front of Li Fan in an instant.

It wasn't until Li Fan snapped out of his contemplation that he saw a floating golden needle in front of him.

"Hehe, back then it was renowned as 'No one comes back from beneath the needle.'"

"If I can leave a few behind, perhaps I can preserve some embers."

The Heavenly Doctor's calm voice spoke.

"This needle is given to you for protection. The needle does not break, and the person does not perish."

Li Fan's face lit up with joy, "Thank you, Elder!"

However, the Heavenly Doctor did not respond further.

Without missing the courtesy, Li Fan continued to bow towards the sky.

Then, looking at the golden needle in front of him, he felt inexplicably puzzled.

In the end, he reached out his hand, trying to touch it.

It felt different from a physical needle, with a cool sensation.

To Li Fan's surprise, a warm flow traveled into his body through his palm.

Li Fan felt as if his soul was immersed in a warm spring, comfortable and soothing.

A sense of security he had never experienced before rose in his heart.

"What a treasure!"

This thought floated in Li Fan's mind, as he hastened towards the Spirit Wood Realm.

Upon returning to the Spirit Wood Realm, Li Fan keenly sensed a slight increase in the spiritual energy density here.

It seemed that the tree people's "tree planting and forestation" project had begun to show initial results.

Without disturbing them, Li Fan went straight to where the faceless stone statue was located.

When his mind connected with the faceless stone statue, memories of the World Union Council and the followers of the Red Dust Compassionate Immortal appeared in his mind.

Li Fan finally breathed a sigh of relief.

Both his body and mind truly felt a sense of safety.

Although he had preliminarily confirmed that, in the eyes of Tianfajie's cultivators, the Heavenly Doctor was indeed "kind."

However, Li Fan did not relax his vigilance because of this.

He always remained cautious of the Heavenly Doctor's mind-reading ability.

Even if not face to face, just with a single golden needle from the other side, it was the same.

Unwilling to accept the possibility that the Heavenly Doctor might sense his thoughts through this needle, Li Fan, who had suffered a major setback before, how could he simply accept the golden needle without any grievances?

And at present, the only trump card Li Fan could think of to balance against the Heavenly Doctor was the faceless stone statue.

Apparently, the true strength of the Faceless Immortal was above that of the Heavenly Doctor.

With the interference of her stone statue, it should be able to shield the Heavenly Doctor’s perception.

Even though he understood that this was somewhat of a temporary solution to a bigger problem, Li Fan still did it anyway.

After all, apart from King Xuantian, no one had truly seen the Faceless Immortal.

And the Heavenly Doctor was now a real opponent.

"Although there are some risks, undoubtedly, the rewards are enormous."

"Leaving aside this golden needle, just the guidance from the Heavenly Doctor alone is worth ten years of hard cultivation."

"And also, his final reminder."

"The operation of the heavenly path, the essence of the world..."

"The technique truly suited to oneself..."

With narrowed eyes, Li Fan gradually began to form a preliminary idea.

However, for now, he set it aside and started to study the golden needle bestowed upon him by the Heavenly Doctor.

Although given by someone else, it felt as if it had always been a part of Li Fan's body.

Without the need for any refining process, he could control it as he pleased.

This method was something Li Fan had never heard of before.

With a single thought, the golden needle reappeared in front of Li Fan.

"It's not made of any metal material..."

After observing repeatedly with his spiritual sense, Li Fan first came to this conclusion.

"And..."

After comparing the golden needle from his memory with the one in front of him using the records of the Dao transformation stone, Li Fan made a new discovery.
