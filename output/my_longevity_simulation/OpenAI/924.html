Chapter 924

Huo Jingtong was extremely upset.

Before leaving, she had reminded and instructed him a thousand times to behave himself.

She never expected this culprit not only did not listen to her, but instead became even more violent, causing harm to all the disciples on Liuyue Peak!

If this were to get out...

Thinking about the consequences of this matter being revealed, Huo Jingtong's vision darkened.

"I can no longer tolerate him; I must give him a harsh lesson!"

With this thought in mind, Huo Jingtong condensed one-tenth of her full power into a moonlight beam in her right hand and aimed it at Li Qing.

The moment she acted, Huo Jingtong already regretted it.

After all, she was a cultivated deity, and even with only one-tenth of her power, it was not something Li Qing, who had just entered the Yuan Ying stage, could resist.

If she were to injure him...

Huo Jingtong was already contemplating reducing the force when something that shook her immensely happened.

A crescent moon rose behind Li Qing, turning his already extraordinary appearance into that resembling a celestial being.

And the moonlight beam sent out by Huo Jingtong was effortlessly blocked by Li Qing!

"How is this possible! With half of the moon revealed, this is the sign of mastering 【Transforming Moonlight】, only achievable in the later stages of Yuan Ying! How long was I gone for?" With a mixture of astonishment and joy in her heart, Huo Jingtong's blood surged.

Stimulating her internal injuries, her face suddenly turned pale, and a mouthful of blood spurted out.

Seeing this, Li Qing quickly dashed to Huo Jingtong's side and held her shoulder, showing worry and concern, saying, "Master, how did you get injured? Let me heal you!"

"Hmph, I am a deity cultivator; I don't need..." Huo Jingtong's words were cut off with a groan as she was forced to stop.

Feeling the pure Yin energy coming from Li Qing's warm palm, Huo Jingtong suddenly felt as if her body was crawling with ants, but as a deity cultivator, she quickly suppressed the strange feeling in her heart. Instead, she was more amazed by the pure energy continuously flowing from her disciple into her body.

This influx of pure Yin energy seemed to be a catalyst, stirring up waves of her moonlight power. After a tumultuous entanglement, the pure Yin energy completely merged into her majestic moonlight power.

The moonlight power that Huo Jingtong had thought was close to perfection, after this fusion, became even purer and even carried a subtle aura of profound mysteriousness!

This was a sign of breaking through to the Harmonious Path realm!

Words could not describe the shock and confusion in Huo Jingtong's heart at this moment.

Even a thought emerged in her mind that it would be great if Li Qing's supply of pure Yin energy never stopped.

Amidst the interplay of these two forces and the gradual rise of her stagnant cultivation, Huo Jingtong's mind slowly became lost.

She had lost track of time when Li Qing released his palms from supporting her back.

"Master..."

Seeing Huo Jingtong a bit dazed, he called out softly.

Coming back to her senses, a blush quickly rose to Huo Jingtong's cheeks.

"I'm fine now, you should leave first," she said in a fluster.

"That makes me feel relieved." Letting out a sigh of relief, Li Qing bowed and left.

Watching Li Qing quickly retreat, a hint of annoyance surfaced in Huo Jingtong's heart.

...

From that day onwards, there was a different kind of emotion between the master and disciple.

But Li Qing did not directly confront it, and he always maintained a respectful and obedient image in front of Huo Jingtong.

However, behind the scenes, his actions causing chaos on Liuyue Peak never stopped.

Huo Jingtong's spiritual awareness enveloped the entire mountain, capturing everything that was happening within.

Although still absurd in principle, it was too late to change the situation. Should she really have to exterminate all the disciples under her sect?

Moreover, Li Qing did not lie. Under the influence of the miraculous pure Yin energy, the progress of the disciples' strength was obvious to all.

Therefore, Huo Jingtong had to back down and let Li Qing do as he pleased.

And in order to prevent other practitioners from the peaks discovering, she even actively set up arrays to conceal it.

Under the nourishment of the reverse Yin energy, the disciples of Liuyue Peak enjoyed happiness and significant progress in their cultivation.

Only Huo Jingtong herself did not benefit from this.

And Li Qing seemed to have no thoughts regarding his master at all, behaving extremely upright and proper.

Two years passed in the blink of an eye.

On the peak of Liuyue, inside a secluded room.

"Over two years, it's time to break through to the Harmonious Path."

"Such a speed of cultivation is unheard of."

"If he successfully breaks through, won't his cultivation be on par with mine? If he insists on..."

Huo Jingtong's mind was in chaos.

At that moment, a strong wind blew, and a majestic aura emanated from Li Qing before suddenly disappearing.

"He really succeeded?!" Huo Jingtong watched Li Qing, her mind bewildered.

"Thank you for protecting me, Master!" Li Qing bowed and expressed his gratitude, maintaining a polite demeanor.

Huo Jingtong, however, turned away, avoiding Li Qing's bow.

According to the rules of Daoist sects, if a disciple's cultivation matches or surpasses their master's, they can be considered equals.

Of course, if respect for the master is upheld, it doesn't matter who is stronger. The master-disciple relationship can still be maintained.

Li Qing smiled faintly, saying, "Even if I achieve eternal life one day, in my heart, you will always be my master. The only master."

At his words, Huo Jingtong's body trembled slightly.

Looking at Li Qing's sincere expression, she couldn't help but feel touched.

"Let's go. With another deity cultivator on Liuyue Peak, such a big event should be shared with others as soon as possible." Li Qing approached and gently held Huo Jingtong's flawless hand.

Huo Jingtong symbolically resisted for a moment before giving in.

On Liuyue Peak, a night of joy ensued.

As emotions deepened, Huo Jingtong eventually couldn't resist.

At night, after everyone had fallen asleep deeply, Li Qing, feeling a massive Yin energy never experienced before from Huo Jingtong's body, nodded in satisfaction.

"The feedback from a deity cultivator is truly extraordinary."

"This immense Yin element is sufficient to support my rapid cultivation in the deity realm for a period of time."

"However, it's not enough!"

With his strength skyrocketing, Li Qing's ambitions swelled even further.

He began to focus his attention on other female cultivators from the Moonlight lineage.

"Only the Distinguished Immortal realm is my ultimate pursuit."

Under the moonlight, Li Qing's shadow was nowhere to be seen.

Only his face remained concealed in the night, leaving his true intentions a mystery.

The next day, Huo Jingtong woke up and immediately brought Li Qing into seclusion.

The bottleneck that had plagued and troubled her for many years had loosened slightly under last night's impact.

Huo Jingtong sensed that her advancement to the Harmonious Path was not far off.
