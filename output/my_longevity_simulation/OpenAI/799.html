Chapter 799

Many scenes of Modan Lake emerged, accompanied by a radiant light.

When Gu Xinghan was drawn to the scenes in this light, the jade disc seemed to be like a living being, emitting waves of eager feelings.

As Gu Xinghan came to his senses, he was somewhat puzzled and surprised.

However, he had heard of treasures possessing spirits before.

After quickly accepting this concept, Gu Xinghan started to feel the jade disc's desire.

But it was just an instinctual reaction to Modan Lake.

The jade disc could not speak, relying entirely on him to guess…

Gu Xinghan tried various methods to try to satisfy the jade disc.

Spirit stones, mineral jades, herbs…

The jade disc seemed to show no interest in any of these.

It continued to emit a persistent desire towards Gu Xinghan.

And with other little companions successfully building their foundations, he was at a loss on how to explain.

The combination of these two reasons made Gu Xinghan feel restless.

It wasn't until a fortuitous coincidence, seeing Elder Fang Zaiji preparing the "Endtimes Disc" for his newly recruited core disciples, that Gu Xinghan suddenly understood its goal.

"This…"

Gu Xinghan hesitated.

After all, the "Endtimes Disc" was something Elder Fang repeatedly emphasized as a precious item to pay attention to.

If there was a mistake…

But in the end, he couldn't resist the jade disc.

A flash of light passed, and the jade disc appeared in his sea of consciousness.

Under Gu Xinghan's cautious observation, the jade disc slowly descended onto the "Endtimes Disc."

Then from underneath, it extended dense and tiny light beams, resembling tentacles.

Adhering to the "Endtimes Disc."

Initially, Gu Xinghan thought the jade disc would damage the "Endtimes Disc.

But after observing for a while, he realized that was not the case.

Instead, the two seemed to be merging into one through the sticky light beams.

This put Gu Xinghan's mind at ease.

At the same time, he felt a sense of novelty.

"Could it be that these two jade discs and the disc are actually the same thing?" Gu Xinghan couldn't help but think.

However, because of his childish nature, Gu Xinghan quickly stopped researching after finding no results and instead focused on studying the scenes he obtained from the jade disc.

"Building my foundation? What is this? It seems to match my internal situation?"

Gu Xinghan was overjoyed.

He no longer paid attention to the jade disc and devoted himself to studying the method of building his foundation.

When he regained consciousness from the meditation, the jade disc had been completely integrated with the "Endtimes Disc."

However, this integration process seemed as if the jade disc was pressed onto him, almost forced.

And the final product wasn't as aesthetically pleasing.

However, Gu Xinghan didn't care about this.

He knew that the jade disc might indeed be a remarkable treasure.

Although young, Gu Xinghan was not naive. On the contrary, he was very aware of the dangers of the world.

Knowing the principle that a man without fault carries the burden of everyone's faults, he decided to keep silent, whether it was regarding the jade disc or the method of building his foundation.

"Perhaps I can use this new jade disc as a cover-up. Claim it to be my miraculous foundation-building item…" the young Gu Xinghan pondered in this way.

Thus, in secrecy, the only cultivator of the "Building Foundation with Me" technique in the world began his cultivation.

At the beginning, his performance surprised the members of the Medicine King Sect, including Liusan.

He quickly grasped the essentials, and breakthroughs came effortlessly.

Among his peers, he became unbeatable, and even refining Golden Core Dan was a piece of cake.

For a while, Gu Xinghan became the most outstanding junior disciple of the Medicine King Sect.

But…

His peak period, like a shooting star, passed in the blink of an eye.

Not long after, his peers made breakthroughs to the Golden Core and Original Infant realms.

Meanwhile, Gu Xinghan remained stagnant in his progress.

This highly unusual situation puzzled Liusan and others in the Medicine King Sect.

Thinking there might be something wrong with Gu Xinghan's body, they took turns examining him but failed to discover any problems.

When asked, Gu Xinghan simply stated that he had done his best but couldn't advance further.

Thirty years passed in the blink of an eye.

The Medicine King Sect had no choice but to accept this fact.

They officially gave up on nurturing Gu Xinghan.

However, they did not overly criticize him, and he still had access to various resources but was no longer the center of attention.

Facing peers whose cultivation surpassed his own, Gu Xinghan became increasingly silent.

He lived in seclusion, avoiding worldly affairs and rarely interacting with others.

He was almost forgotten by everyone.

And so, 263 years passed.

The white-haired Gu Xinghan finally reached the end of his life.

"Golden Core Dan, Golden Core Dan…"

"What does it mean to reach Unity?"

He laughed bitterly, his face filled with sorrow.

"To create something out of nothing, to carve a new path. It's too hard, incredibly hard…"

"To succeed in 'Building Foundation with Me' without even the chance to regret or change the cultivation method."

In his hands suddenly appeared a smooth jade disc.

It was as inconspicuous as a stone found by the roadside, not attracting anyone's attention.

Gu Xinghan gazed at the jade disc that had accompanied him for almost a lifetime with a complicated expression.

"Mistake me for a lifetime, mistake me for a lifetime…"

He murmured softly.

He wanted to throw the jade disc on the ground fiercely, but he felt a reluctance.

In the end, he firmly held onto the jade disc. Amidst a sense of reluctance, he passed away.

Gu Xinghan's death did not cause any ripples.

It wasn't until three years later, due to the lack of any signs after his death, that people discovered his body.

Over two centuries had passed, and Longevity Valley had already changed beyond recognition.

Hardly anyone remembered the once hopeful figure in the Medicine King Sect.

No one paid attention.

The funeral was simple.

Soon, Gu Xinghan's body was buried in the disciples' cemetery of the Medicine King Sect within the Beast Clan Realm.

His few remaining possessions, including the jade disc in Gu Xinghan's hands, went unnoticed.

Buried alongside him, disappearing into the endless darkness underground.

...

Beyond the Xuanhuang Realm.

Inside the Medicine King True Cauldron.

A figure extremely similar to Li Fan but extremely weak, groaned softly.

He wiped the fresh blood constantly flowing from his mouth and nose, as if he might die from excessive blood loss at any moment.

But his expression remained unchanged.

"In the cauldron for a moment, a thousand years pass in the Xuanhuang Realm."

He coughed softly, eyes filled with infinite emotions.

Inside the Medicine King True Cauldron was the "Sacred Embryo Li Fan."

As part of a disguise, Li Fan used his own blood to create a secondary incarnation through the "Sacred Blood as Embryo Technique" before the arrival of Mr. Bai.

Due to the rushed timeline and having the same aptitude as the original, he was just a complete ordinary mortal without any cultivation.

Not long after leaving the Xuanhuang Realm, he felt that the Medicine King Cauldron had deviated from its original trajectory.

Additionally, the scenes transmitted from his body avatar, the "Disassociation Disc," were increasing rapidly.

The massive amount of information almost overwhelmed his brain but eventually subsided, easing the pressure.

"Although I'm an incarnation, because my Sacred Embryo still exists, I can remotely control the body avatar with some simple manipulation."

"It's limited to this, though."

The information recorded in the Disassociation Disc happened within the Xuanhuang Realm, but being outside, Li Fan did not witness the battle between the transmission master and Mr. Bai.

However, being able to witness the Transmission Master's actions for the first time was a significant gain for Li Fan.

Temporarily setting aside thoughts about the Transmission, the Sacred Embryo Li Fan was now becoming somewhat excited.

He was about to see the legendary "Immortal Ruins."

Creaking sounds echoed from various parts of the Medicine King True Cauldron, indicating the immense pressure it was enduring.

Li Fan used the already-prepared Far-Seeing Mirror in the cauldron to look towards the Immortal Ruins.

Ahead, darkness enveloped everything.

The Medicine King Cauldron's speed continued to increase.

Thunderous roars reverberated from all directions.

As if there were imminent risks of disintegration.

Nevertheless, in Li Fan's view, he still didn't see anything.

"Is it going to end like this?"

Boom!

The Medicine King Cauldron started to disintegrate.

The violent shaking made it nearly impossible for him to stand stably.

Struggling, Li Fan carried the Far-Seeing Mirror and hid in the most central part of the Medicine King True Cauldron.

It was a narrow refuge made entirely of Eternal-Jade Essence Gold.

Under the increasingly strong, almost unimaginable enormous pressure, the Medicine King Cauldron finally couldn't hold on and shattered into countless pieces.

And they all plummeted towards the Immortal Ruins.

Just moments later, Li Fan felt that even the Eternal-Jade Essence Gold couldn't withstand the pressure.

But due to his innate temperament, he did not give up.

Raising the Far-Seeing Mirror once more, he looked ahead.

In an instant, he was slightly startled.

In the originally pitch-black vision, he found that there was a continuous wasteland.

The wasteland was littered with numerous ruins.

A tall corpse sat cross-legged on the ground.

Most of the flesh and blood had almost vanished, leaving only a layer of skin hanging on the bones.

Dozens of blood-red chains were deeply embedded in the ground.

Binding this skeleton on the wasteland.

And around the corpse were deep trenches crisscrossing each other.

On the vast wasteland, they outlined an extremely intricate symbol.

Although he didn't recognize the symbol, upon seeing it, Li Fan naturally understood its meaning.

It was a word.

A word, "Hunger"!

A sense of endless hunger surged into Li Fan's mind.

At the same time...
