"But there's no need to rush within a year."

He looked in the direction of the central area of the Ten Thousand Immortal Alliance.

Just now, he inexplicably sensed a connection in his Sea of Consciousness with the Endless Disintegration Discs, indicating the birth of nine new Disintegration Discs in this world.

Perhaps originally formed by the fusion of nine Disintegration Discs, the Ultimate Creation could now clearly sense the presence of the newly born Disintegration Discs, even if separated by thousands of miles.

Moreover...

Therefore, the path ahead in this world was already very clear.

That was to figure out a way to contact Morubin, gain his trust, and then, using his identity as a Law Transmitter, obtain various secrets that Li Fansi needed.

With these thoughts in mind, Li Fansi returned to the Spirit Wood Realm.

Before long, Shang Tianci came looking for him.

"Senior..." He hesitated, as if wanting to say something but hesitating.

"What's the matter?"

"Yan Fangzhou's situation seems a bit unfavorable. Can we let him out?" Shang Tianci asked cautiously.

Li Fansi's expression remained unchanged, revealing no joy or anger. "I will handle this matter myself. You may go."

Shang Tianci didn't dare to argue and could only bow and leave.

Li Fansi's divine sense instantly focused on Yan Fangzhou.

Trapped in an enhanced version of the Taiji Dust Array, no matter how he struggled, he faced only endless repetition and boring scenes. He couldn't escape.

With the inner and outer separation of spiritual energy in the formation, he couldn't cultivate.

Even when he tried to calm his mind, he couldn't. Every critical moment, the formation would precisely disturb him, causing him to feel restless and entangled.

Under this torment, Yan Fangzhou was on the verge of going insane.

He launched indiscriminate attacks on his surroundings, venting his frustration.

But his anger was impotent.

Not only could he not change the situation, but he also made his state worse.

By the time Yan Fangzhou came back to his senses, he had exhausted all his strength.

Like an ordinary person, he was now helplessly imprisoned in the array.

Helpless and awaiting death.

After experiencing stages of raging, crying, looking lifeless, Yan Fangzhou, the "arrogant talent," now seemed completely devoid of spirit, sitting limp and motionless on the ground.

However, Li Fansi didn't appear to mock him but carefully observed Yan Fangzhou.

Then, a slight smile appeared on his lips.

Although Yan Fangzhou's act was good, fooling the cunning Li Fansi completely was still a bit lacking.

Apparently, Yan Fangzhou had never seen what a real breakdown looked like.

Deep within his hollow eyes, there was still a trace of intelligence.

"This is pretending to be crazy; trying to deceive me and attacking me again when I lower my guard."

"Unfortunately, he may fool the naive Shang Tianci, but not me."

Li Fansi shook his head slightly. "He still has these little tricks, showing that he has not truly reached the point of breaking down."

"Let's wait until he truly goes crazy."

With a light touch on Yan Fangzhou, who stood motionless in front of the black gate, Li Fansi reinforced the formation once again.

Only then did he leave satisfied.

Unaware of the fact that he had cleverly undone his own cleverness, Yan Fangzhou continued to play the fool in the array, waiting for someone to come.

Since he intended to win the trust of Morubin, the Law King of the Xuantian School, Li Fansi naturally had to play his role well.

The action of recruiting soldiers and buying horses could not be halted.
