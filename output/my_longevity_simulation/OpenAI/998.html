Chapter 998

Originally, Li Fan thought that using the Real Fruit of Temptation would go as smoothly as before. However, he was somewhat surprised when the silver-white color was about to invade the minds of the two, but suddenly stopped.

It was as if they had encountered some sort of obstacle, as the power of the Real Fruit of Temptation was completely blocked from their souls.

The Real Fruit of Temptation disappeared in the air, but it did not change the perception of Xuansu Chang and Lu Xichan.

Instead, the silver light tightly enveloped their souls.

"What is this..."

Li Fan's expression changed, and upon careful observation, he found that it was not the failure of the power of the True Celestial.

Their souls had a special defense layer that resisted the influence of distorted perception.

"It seems that the high-level souls of the Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance have a special defense mechanism."

"It makes sense; otherwise, with just mass-producing Real Fruits of Temptation, the Five Elders could have conquered the Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance."

After the initial unsuccessful induction attempt, Li Fan did not give up.

At the same time, he did not plan to inquire with Mo Rubin about the soul defense mechanism.

"Xuansu Chang is involved in the secrets of the Tianxuan Mirror's avatar, it seems I will have to use you for experimentation." Li Fan's gaze turned to the sleeping Lu Xichan.

In addition to Mo Rubin's shadow seal, Li Fan also consecutively laid formations around the two.

Ensuring that in case of any mishap during the experiment, they would not wake up inexplicably and be unable to communicate with the outside world or escape.

He then sent a strand of divine sense into Lu Xichan's soul.

It had to be said that Lu Xichan's soul strength was somewhat inadequate compared to her position as the chief of the Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance Strategy Hall.

In the Yunsui Tiangong, Li Fan had once treated Hanhai, coming into close contact with his soul.

As fellow Daoists, Lu Xichan was significantly weaker than the former head of Yunsui Tiangong.

Li Fan did not feel threatened at all and observed calmly.

Outside the soul, there seemed to be dark golden lines engraved with patterns.

Appearing and disappearing from time to time, they seemed like chains, not only binding the soul but also forming a protective layer similar to armor.

But during his observation, Li Fan keenly discovered that apart from protection, these dark golden patterns probably had a self-destruct function.

"If forcefully breaking through the protection of the dark golden patterns, these chains, which have almost merged with the soul, would instantly shatter into pieces..."

"The only outcome would be soul scattering!"

Understanding the role of the dark golden patterns, Li Fan immediately felt the situation was challenging.

But fortunately, after a brief moment of contemplation, Li Fan found a similar case in the Getian Records.

It spoke of two cultivators who sought medical advice from the heavens with their son.

These two cultivators had been Dao companions since the Qi Refining period. Supporting each other along the way, they progressed together, finally reaching the Nascent Soul realm.

They were blessed with a son, which was considered a double blessing.

However, what caught these two cultivators off guard was that even when their son was over ten years old, he still could not speak.

With a dull and ignorant appearance, he showed very weak responses to the outside world. Only when his parents called him, he would respond accordingly.

After examining him, the heavenly physician explained the cause to them.

Their son's soul was not damaged or incomplete; it was even stronger than ordinary cultivators.

But the vast soul and the weak body were not compatible, leading to the overflowing of soul power that formed a cage-like cocoon of distortion.

After a long period of abnormality, it eventually tightly wrapped up his original soul, making it difficult to communicate with the outside world.

To solve this, it was actually straightforward.

All that was needed was to trim these young branches of the soul.

But to truly resolve the issue was not as easy as it sounded.

These branches were also part of the original soul.

To clean them without harming the original soul and without provoking intense resistance from the entire soul...

The heavenly physician provided the best solution.

The immediate family's souls would touch and cleanse these soul branches.

Using their own souls as scissors, they would clean them all.

During the process, the souls of both the savior and the practitioner would merge.

But precisely because they were close family members, there would be no conflict from start to finish.

It was somewhat akin to living together for an extended period.

The only cost was that the practitioner's soul would suffer severe damage.

A drop in several realms was inevitable.

The heavenly physician explained the solution to the two, and the expressions of the couple clearly differed.

After a heated argument, the couple ultimately chose not to treat their child.

To spare him from further suffering, the couple themselves killed their child.

Subsequently, this pair of divine companions also gradually grew apart.

Later, during pursuit by enemies, they each fled for their lives, only to ultimately perish.

...

"This kind of spiritual restriction seems very similar to the soul branches in that case."

"Intertwined with the soul, extremely sensitive to external forces, and if the body is damaged, it will take the original soul down with it."

"However, because it is intertwined with the soul, it does not have that much defense against the soul power itself."

"So, to remove the soul restriction, the method is indeed similar."

Li Fan had already made up his mind.

After a moment, a continuous stream of soul power quietly poured into Lu Xichan's soul.

Facing the grand and dam-like dark golden protection, like a surging tide, it forcefully collided.

As the restriction itself had to deal with the fluctuations of soul power, a similar soul impact was commonplace and did not trigger a counterattack.

However, during the continuous cleansing process, the soul power output by Li Fan was quickly depleted.

But soon enough, Li Fan, operating the Infinite Yin-Yang Technique, redirected the soul power again.

Like rocks on the shore eroded by the sea over years, the dark golden pattern restriction similarly faded away gradually.

Li Fan closed his eyes, outputting soul power, and before long, a dark golden pattern slowly faded and disappeared.

The original Real Fruit of Temptation that had been circling outside Lu Xichan's soul, mixed with Li Fan's soul power, used this opening to pour in mightily.

Colliding with Lu Xichan's soul!

If it was only Li Fan's soul alone, a stranger's intrusion would undoubtedly alert and trigger Lu Xichan's defenses, even if she was in a state of sleep.

But at this moment, within the Real Fruit of Temptation, there contained carefully crafted lies by Li Fan.
