Chapter 932

Among them, "Heaven and Earth Taxation" and "Only I Know the Eternal Secret" and a few other techniques were almost completely consistent with the knowledge Li Fan gained in the illusion realm.

Even the depiction in the illusion realm was somewhat crude.

Take "Heaven and Earth Taxation," for example. In the illusion realm, it was simply a dual cultivation technique. However, after the calamity of incompatible practices arrived, the effect of rapidly advancing cultivation offered by this technique was nullified.

But in reality, this technique had another crucial and mysterious use apart from dual cultivation.

That is, locking onto a target and forcibly equalizing the target's luck, cultivation speed, and even personal characteristics like comprehension.

This is known as the harmony of heaven and earth.

For true peerless geniuses, this technique naturally seemed quite insignificant. But for someone like Li Fan, it was undoubtedly a profound technique.

This was only one of the abilities the initial level of "Heaven and Earth Taxation" could possess.

As one progressed in cultivation, just like the name of the technique, the locked-on target could even extend from people to heaven and earth.

"With a mortal body, equate the power of heaven and earth..."

"It truly is a true immortal inheritance."

The upper limit of "Heaven and Earth Taxation" was extremely high, hinting that it could still be used even after ascending to the immortal realm.

The only fatal flaw was...

The locked-on target would sense the practitioner of the technique.

Indeed, this single point greatly diminished the importance of this technique in Li Fan's mind.

For someone like Li Fan, who preferred to strategize before acting and often calculated in the shadows, exposing oneself constantly to another person was not a wise move.

The only potential major use he could think of now was to capture a peerless genius and keep them in check.

Then forcibly equalize with them to enhance his own understanding, aptitude, and luck.

Having skimmed through "Heaven and Earth Taxation," Li Fan then glanced at "Only I Know the Eternal Secret."

"Zhao Senior Sister actually trusted me with this path to enlightenment..."

After a moment of thought, Li Fan suddenly came to a realization.

It could be said that among all these techniques, this "Only I Know the Eternal Secret" was the most worthless.

Because based on the principle of "Unity in Diversity," it had already been monopolized by Zhao Ruoxi.

With no way out, and possibly being completely monitored by Senior Sister Zhao throughout the practice process...

Li Fan decisively sealed it away and set it aside.

Glancing over a few techniques, Li Fan then focused his attention on the two techniques he had seen first.

"The Forgetful Canon" and "Record of Three Perils of Ghosts and Gods in the Dark Abyss."

"The earlier techniques were from a giant man named Sikong Lin among the Twelve King's Laws."

"And these techniques..."

The appearances of the twelve distinctive cultivators of the Profound Sky Sect flashed through Li Fan's mind.

Quickly, Li Fan identified the possible sources based on the styles of the techniques.

"Sun Xingwang."

Because the aura exhibited by these two techniques was truly unique.

Take "The Forgetful Canon," for instance. This technique bore some resemblance to the abilities of the former Profound Sky King.

Both possessed a similar divine power of "words shape reality."

The distinction lay in the fact that the words of the Profound Sky King Xuan Yuanhong manifested on himself.

Such as "Giant Body Force" and "Heavenly Way of Maneuver," among others.

Meanwhile, "The Forgetful Canon" influenced all external things.

Upon reaching a certain level of cultivation, one could even reshape the entire world according to one's thoughts through the murmurs of the technique.

For example, within the technique, there was an example.

The hallmark of mastering The Forgetful Canon was to create a fictional character out of thin air through whispered words.

As the details of this character became more profound, the character would actually come into existence.

And act according to the preset rules.

Furthermore, there would be no sensitivity towards the creator behind the scenes.

The astonishing nature of "The Forgetful Canon" left Li Fan amazed repeatedly.

However, similar to "Heaven and Earth Taxation," "The Forgetful Canon" also had an extremely potent side effect.

That was, every hundred years of cultivation in the technique, the practitioner would completely lose all memories except for the content of "The Forgetful Canon."

And any original written records or hints would be actively ignored by the practitioner himself.

Leaving the practitioner unaware of this fact.

Initiating a new chapter of life every hundred years.

...

"Whoever could invent 'The Forgetful Canon' is undoubtedly a stellar talent."

Li Fan sighed lightly, then turned his attention to the last technique.

"Record of Three Perils of Ghosts and Gods in the Dark Abyss."

This technique was the most unique.

It was a summoning technique.

Not summoning something from another realm, but summoning the "ghosts" and "gods" from within one's own body.

According to the technique's explanation, everyone was born with endless thoughts.

These thoughts were like waves on the sea.

Most of them were fleeting.

But these waves of thoughts made up the vast sea of a person.

If every fleeting thought was preserved, nourished day and night, and allowed to grow and develop.

Then countless refined thought essences would be born in one's mind.

These thoughts would mold themselves into "ghosts" and "gods" according to the nature and content of the thoughts, forming a triumvirate of human, ghost, and god.

Due to the fact that these ghosts and gods originated from oneself, all three were interrelated.

And as the strength of the person grew, the power of the "ghosts" and "gods" would also expand by a thousandfold.

A single thought released would seem like the depths of the netherworld reappearing in the mortal realm.

Enveloped in darkness, with souls roaming freely.

Under deliberate indulgence, destroying the world with a single thought and turning all other beings into their own ghosts and gods would be a simple task.

This "Record of Three Perils of Ghosts and Gods in the Dark Abyss" did not have any particularly strong side effects.

If it needed to be stated, the innumerable whispers of the ghosts and gods that did not exist in one's body served as the only adverse factor.

If one's mind was not strong, they might genuinely drive themselves insane.

And even with unwavering will, facing the incessant murmurs of countless ghosts and gods day and night would eventually become unbearable.

Eventually, the practitioner of this technique would go berserk.

Releasing the multitudes of ghosts and gods to obliterate the world.

However, when the "Record of Three Perils of Ghosts and Gods in the Dark Abyss" was combined with "The Forgetful Canon," an unexpected effect surfaced.

The memory deletion every hundred years would sweep away the negative emotions accumulated by the multitude of ghosts and gods.

But these ghosts and gods themselves would not disappear.

Even if one forgot the "Record of Three Perils of Ghosts and Gods in the Dark Abyss" technique, cultivating the thought essences of the ghosts and gods had become a bodily instinct.

With time passing, one's own strength would constantly increase.

"However, I remember Sun Xingwang mentioned that practicing both of these techniques together might have other unforeseen points of rejection."
