In the gentle introduction of the woman, they soon arrived at one of the broken heads of the Nine-Headed Crimson.

"This is one of the nine temples."

Following the woman, Xu Bai walked into the quiet building. Upon stepping in, he faintly perceived the howls of resentment and sorrow.

The ground and walls were covered with dark slate, creating a solemn and dark atmosphere in the temple.

Without the woman guiding him, Xu Bai followed the direction of the sound and walked towards the depths of the temple.

The further he went in, the louder the cries of the exotic beasts became.

Through the introduction from the veiled woman, he realized that these were not just the sounds from the Nine-Headed Crimson before its death.

Instead, it was the collective screams of the countless exotic beasts that had been slaughtered as sacrifices over the years to suppress the ferocity of the divine bird.

Each temple had witnessed the tragic deaths of countless exotic beasts.

With numerous resentful souls lingering, the place had turned into a hellish underworld.

However, inside this temple, even though there was some solemnity, there were no signs of haunting by resentful spirits.

Because there was an unparalleled sharp sword qi that firmly suppressed them.

"Swoosh..."

A strand of hair suddenly fell, causing Xu Bai to halt his steps.

In front of him, the black fleshy mass, like entangled vines, coiled into a ball.

Occasionally, it would wriggle, making it hard to believe that it was the corpse of a monster that had died thousands of years ago.

Above the black mass of flesh, there was a red sword qi.

In fact, ordinary cultivators couldn't see this red sword qi.

Let alone the ordinary people in the Empire.

They could only deduce the residual "immortal sword qi" from the continuously suppressed black fleshy mass and the sporadic outbursts of sword qi.

But Xu Bai could see everything clearly.

Even...

Xu Bai closed his eyes slightly.

A scene suddenly appeared in his mind.

It was a red light that illuminated the heaven and earth, seemingly faster than lightning.

Contained within was a sharp intent that could cleave the heavens and earth apart.

"Seems like a rope, in fact, it is..."

"A sword."

Gazing at the red line in front of him, Xu Bai, lost in thought, said, "The red rope sword given by Mr. Bai... Compared to the 'Wooden Sword' he wielded in the Meteorite Immortal Realm, this red rope sword carries a stronger killing intent. It's more like it carries a trace of punishment."

Numerous insights continuously emerged from Xu Bai's heart.

He seemed to see the figure of Mr. Bai gently flicking a strand of thread.

And the once unrivaled and arrogant Nine-Headed Crimson, died a miserable death on the spot.

...

In this way, Xu Bai immersed himself in contemplation, unaware of the passing time.

The veiled girl did not dare to disturb him and stood silently behind Xu Bai.

And not far from Xu Bai, on the other side of the wall of the temple.

Xiao Qing and a graceful girl were staring intently at Xu Bai through the transparent wall.

"How is it, can you confirm?" Xiao Qing asked, with a rare hint of anxiety flickering in her eyes.

The girl kept her eyes closed the whole time.

Her eyelashes trembled, and sweat beads fell from her face.

After a long time, she suddenly opened her eyes.

"How could this be?"

The girl was filled with shock.

"When I saw him, it felt like I was looking at myself!"
