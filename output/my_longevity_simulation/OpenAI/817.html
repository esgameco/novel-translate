Chapter 817

Ji Shaoli's clone's eyes flickered as he spent half a million the consecutive times according to the experiences from the previous lives.

He exchanged all his contributions for the materials that were about to appreciate rapidly in the upcoming period.

"Well, it seems that the downfall of Jiushan Prefecture has indeed had some impact."

"The trends of some commodities' prices have already changed. Some have surged ahead, and some have experienced devaluation that was not seen before."

The clone operated on the Tianxuan Small Mirror.

And there was a ray of light that isolated the Tianxuan Small Mirror.

Preventing it from sensing anything from the outside.

Then, he spent a million more contributions to purchase some commonly used items.

After storing them away, Li Fan quietly left Haixuan Island.

The clone remained motionless but contacted He Zhenghao.

Yes, this Ji Shaoli was also acquainted with He Zhenghao.

After all, He was well-connected, and everyone was within the Congyun Sea, so it was not surprising for them to know each other.

However, their acquaintance was superficial, and they were not close.

"He Daoist friend, how have you been recently?"

"Are you willing to gather on Haixuan Island?"

Not only to He Zhenghao, but the clone also sent banquet invitations to other cultivators he had some acquaintance with from his memories.

...

"While promoting the merger between the Ten Immortal Alliance and the Five Elders' Association, we must not neglect the research on 'Establishing Foundation with Myself.'"

"After all, this is the matter that can truly shake the foundation of the legacy."

After leaving Haixuan Island, Li Fan's eyes flickered as he detoured and headed straight for Yinyin Island.

Upon arrival, he did not act recklessly but carefully observed his surroundings.

Faintly, the cries of mortals on the island could be heard.

Apparently, he was still studying the mysteries of the Immortal-Mortal Miasma.

The small dog with eyes on its abdomen, holding a white rib in its mouth, was running wildly on the island.

Appearing very happy.

"Senior Yin, succeeded in establishing the foundation using oneself in just three years."

"It wasn't long ago."

Li Fan pondered, not daring to be presumptuous, and transmitted, "Senior Yin, are you there?"

The scene of battling with Senior Yin in the past life was still vivid in his mind.

Even with his Nascent Soul cultivation base and numerous magical techniques, he was helpless against Senior Yin.

It was only by ultimately resorting to the chaotic spell that he managed to kill Senior Yin.

In this life, in order not to disturb the legacy, Li Fan would not easily use the chaotic spell.

Moreover, he hoped that the other party would continue to derive the 'Establishing Foundation with Myself' technique for him...

It would be best to persuade with words.

"I'm here. Daoist friend, please come in!"

The stone gate on the island opened with a bang, and Li Fan landed.

The man and the small dog suddenly turned their heads, staring at Li Fan.

Emitting a threatening growl, their eyes were full of vigilance.

As soon as it growled, the white rib fell to the ground with a clatter.

Li Fan's eyes flickered as he picked it up.

This rib came from a cultivator surnamed Su who was the first to be infected by the Immortal-Mortal Miasma.

In the 115th life, Li Fan threw it out, attracting the attention of a huge monster made up of countless corpses in the white mist, escaping a disaster.

The man and the small dog became fierce when their beloved item was snatched away, but as soon as Li Fan glanced at them.

It seemed to be greatly frightened and instantly restrained.

With its tail drooping, it timidly fled into a building on the island.

Li Fan chuckled and followed it in.

Just as in the previous lives when he saw him, he was still staring at the mortals being tortured in the transparent cage, muttering something in his mouth.

Li Fan threw the white rib in his hand, and Senior Yin instinctively caught it.

"What is this..." He was first stunned but then held onto it tightly.

"Just now at the door, I found this little thing in the dogâ€™s mouth. I vaguely felt that this bone was extraordinary. I thought the little guy was mischievous and stole it to play with," Li Fan explained with a smile.

Senior Yin suddenly realized, and he kicked the small dog hard.

Taking the ribbone and putting it away.

His expression softened a lot as he looked at Li Fan and asked, "I wonder what brings you here?"

Li Fan looked around and said, "This is not a place to talk..."

Senior Yin fell silent immediately.

However, he was not entangled and led Li Fan to the underground secret room.

Passing through Senior Yin's gallery of collection, he also introduced him to Li Fan.

To his surprise, Li Fan seemed to have some knowledge of these things too.

He could provide some input from time to time.

This delighted Senior Yin, who had found someone who understood him.

His attitude towards Li Fan became even friendlier.

After kicking the small dog again, he served two cups of dark, swirling Spiritual Mushroom Tea.

Before Senior Yin could speak, Li Fan boldly picked up the cup and drank it all in one go.

Without changing his expression.

"Good tea!" He even seemed a bit reluctant to finish.

This further surprised Senior Yin.

"This secret room is quite secretive. Daoist friend, feel free to speak your mind," he took a sip and said slowly.

"Senior Yin, do you know 'Establishing Foundation with Myself'?"

Li Fan's words made him freeze on the spot the next moment.

"Are you here for 'Establishing Foundation with Myself'?"

Indeed, he had mentioned this to some others, but most of them thought it was absurd.

They just laughed it off.

Li Fan, who came to visit him so earnestly, was the first.

In mutual understanding, Senior Yin couldn't help but advise, "But I see you have already achieved the Golden Elixir realm. 'Establishing Foundation with Myself' is not perfect..."

Unexpectedly, Li Fan interrupted him.

Next sentence, was earth-shattering.

"When I first heard about your 'Establishing Foundation with Myself' technique, I was amazed. But after careful consideration, I found significant flaws."

"So, after much research and effort, I finally perfected it."

"Overjoyed, I came here specifically to share it with you."

"I hope Senior Yin can point out any shortcomings."

Li Fan said gently.

Senior Yin, upon hearing this, suddenly stood up.

He looked at Li Fan, full of disbelief.

He even laughed, "You perfected the 'Establishing Foundation with Myself' technique? Daoist friend, don't joke with me..."

However, his smile disappeared as Li Fan spoke word by word.

Soon, his expression turned from surprise to solemn.

Then to shock.

Eventually, as Li Fan explained the entire 'Establishing Foundation with Myself' technique, Senior Yin's body began to tremble uncontrollably.

"How is this possible..."

"This...How is this possible..."

His eyes were full of confusion, as if he was verifying the feasibility of the 'Establishing Foundation with Myself' technique.

But no matter how he calculated, he could only reach one conclusion.

Flawless, perfect.

Senior Yin's legs weakened, and he stumbled to the ground.

Pointing at Li Fan, he trembled uncontrollably.

For a long time, he couldn't say a word.
