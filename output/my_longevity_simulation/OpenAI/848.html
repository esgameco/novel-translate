Chapter 848

At the next moment, the scenery changed drastically.

The connection with the original body was severed.

The strand of consciousness separated was then injected into a body.

Simultaneously, a myriad of information flooded in like a tide.

The possessed body belonged to an ordinary mortal.

It took several days for all the information to be absorbed amidst the throbbing pain in the mind.

Li Fan's face showed a strange expression.

Within this radiant sphere was a fictional time and space.

It was the end of a dynasty, with corrupt officials and people living in misery.

Disasters were rampant, and starvation was widespread.

The common people only sought food and fullness, but it was merely a luxury.

Thus, in despair, they rose up to fight back.

Now, smoke signals were rising everywhere, and Li Fan was part of one of the rebel forces, an inconspicuous figure among them.

Due to digesting the knowledge, he looked dazed and was considered to have contracted a strange illness.

He was sent to the military doctor for treatment.

This military doctor was quite knowledgeable and upon mere observation, prescribed a sedative.

As Li Fan regained his senses and was about to leave, a prompt suddenly appeared before him: "Identified Wang Zuo's talent, White Mo Rest."

"Current Favorability: 1."

Li Fan's expression couldn't help but be strange.

"What kind of ghost is the Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance playing?"

Instead of having too much contact with the military doctor White Mo Rest, Li Fan took a stroll in the rebel camp.

In just three or four days, he discovered a "Peerless General," and two "Ancient Divine Weapons."

There were also numerous other materials, too many to count.

Some only had rough favorability prompts, but some even had strategy reminders.

Their preferences, personality, weaknesses - everything was known clearly.

Li Fan became interested.

Following the prompts, he approached and catered to their interests one by one.

In no time, from being an unknown in the rebel forces, he started gaining some fame.

More than ten days later, he even received a reminder of the imminent night attack by the official troops.

However, instead of warning anyone, he only gathered a group of trusted followers and made preparations in secret.

The rebel forces were instantly defeated in the night attack.

Li Fan, along with his group, managed to escape by a stroke of luck.

As the leader who led everyone to survive, Li Fan gradually established his prestige.

Later, following another prompt that appeared before him, solely with a hundred or so people, after a long-distance raid, they occupied a small city left vacant by the army that went out to suppress bandits.

He found his place.

From there, he gradually absorbed surrounding powers.

In just five years, he cleared the realm and overthrew the decadent rule of the dynasty, becoming a founding king of a new era.

The view shattered layer by layer.

Everything returned to nothingness.

Just as Li Fan pondered the purpose of this illusionary scene, the scenery changed once again.

It was another illusory time and space, this time set in a fictional cultivation world.

He possessed the body of a young mortal man who was rejected by the Tai Chu Sect because he had no spiritual roots.

The young man begged bitterly and only then obtained the identity of a miscellaneous disciple.

Originally, he thought he could only barely get through life.

But he never expected that, by chance, he found a stone bead in the mountains behind.

This stone bead had the incredible effect of devouring surrounding objects, nourishing with spiritual fluid, and improving aptitude.

He gradually became a Foundation Establishment cultivator, Nascent Soul cultivator, and Yuanying cultivator, step by step.

During this journey, he not only gained three beautiful Dao companions and an ancient divine beast as a mount but also encountered one stroke of luck after another, discovered immortal tombs, and obtained unprecedented immortal tools in the cultivation world.

After wandering the cultivation world for a century, he eventually became the strongest of his time.

He left in all his beauty.

The picture shattered once again.

When he returned from this illusion, Li Fan faintly felt that his mindset had undergone some changes.

Nevertheless, before he could analyze it, he fell into the cycle of illusion once more.

Next, Li Fan went through another ninety-seven cycles of reincarnation.

The encounters were mostly similar.

Generally, they began with a humble background, followed by accidental opportunities. Subsequently, they would rise through adversity and achieve the typical story of becoming the world's strongest.

After all the cycles of reincarnation ended, the radiant sphere in Li Fan's hands faded completely and disappeared.

"..."

Although the experiences of the ninety-nine cycles of reincarnation were illusory, they felt too real.

They were eerily similar to what he had experienced with Zhao Shijie, extremely vivid.

Having gone through so many cycles of reincarnation, even with Li Fan's current mindset, he couldn't help but be influenced to some extent.

Slowly activating the Mysterious Yellow Immortal Heart Mantra, he gradually diluted these experiences.

"The Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance's move is quite interesting," Li Fan said mysteriously.

The radiant sphere that had descended earlier like a torrential rain had coincidentally fallen into some territories belonging to the Five Elders Council.

Countless cultivators and mortals were attracted to the sphere out of curiosity and fell into the cycle of ninety-nine reincarnations.

Upon waking up, although their bodies were not affected in any way, something had changedâ€¦

It was the impact of the illusion. Originally ordinary individuals, after waking up, inexplicably saw themselves as the "Chosen One."

If it had just been a one-sided belief, it wouldn't have been a big deal.

But they all realized that they lived in a world forcibly reconstructed by the Five Elders Council.

Thus, they all made grand vows to overthrow the Five Elders Council's rule and rescue the cultivators from their miserable lives.

After going through the ninety-nine cycles of reincarnation, these people had indeed become somewhat different.

Instead of confronting head-on, they would lurk, cultivate first, and then cause destruction.

Although not specifically expressed, judging from the tone of the Five Elders Council, the influence of the radiant white sphere had caused quite a headache.

Li Fan pondered, "Apart from awakening the cultivators, there seems to be some kind of spiritual imprint-like existence."

"How can the sons of heaven bow beneath others?"

"To be able to instill the idea of overturning the rule of the Five Elders Council under the influence of the Five Venerable Beings."

"Good stuff!"

Naturally, Li Fan was not affected by the radiant sphere.

Even if he had been affected...

It wouldn't have been a problem.

After all, he was different from the others.

With "The Real Deal" in his hands, he could be considered a true Chosen One!

Looking at the storage ring sent by the Five Elders Council, filled densely with at least a thousand radiant spheres...

Li Fan rubbed his chin.

"It seems the cost of this item isn't high. Otherwise, it wouldn't have been scattered on such a large scale."

He took out another sphere and started studying it.

A faint light radiated from the sphere, and he was decrypting it in his Knowledge Sea.

"There are traces of an illusion formation."

"But more importantly..."

Li Fan furrowed his brows slightly, sensing a familiar odor.

"The Monkey Mind Trap?"
