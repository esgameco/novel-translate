Chapter 942

"Looking at the whole Xuanhuang world, besides me, who is willing to accompany you for a thousand rounds of chess? Most likely, there is no one else, right?"

With a smile, Shengtai dropped a piece on the board and asked back.

This first move was directly placed on the position of "Tianyuan."

The messy old man's face changed, he glanced at Shengtai, and his expression became extremely serious.

"You, kid, clearly only have the cultivation of Yuanying. Your calculating ability is surprisingly strong. Moreover, you don't even bother to disguise it, sitting directly next to me."

"It's obvious you came prepared!"

"I was somewhat delighted at first, thinking that after all these years, I could finally have some play. It wasn't until halfway through that I realized I had fallen into your trap!"

The old man sighed slightly.

Shengtai just smiled without replying.

The messy old man's name is Qianxun Daoren. He is an old-school Dao master who fell in cultivation due to injuries.

It is unknown what contributions he made to the Fairy Alliance in the past, as he surprisingly carried a "Qinfa Jade Pendant.”

Of course, at this point in time, few people in the world knew about this.

In the eyes of the public, this Qianxun Daoren was just an ordinary cultivator with a bit of insanity.

Only more than ten years later, a mortal named Xiaoshengke met Qianxun Daoren.

After accompanying Qianxun Daoren in begging for a thousand days, he was gifted the Qinfa Jade Pendant.

With an ordinary body, he met the Yansuo Emerald as a commoner.

Acquiring the Dao method from the Dao master, he embarked on the path of immortality.

This event was often talked about by later generations.

Shengtai found Qianxun Daoren naturally for his Qinfa Jade Pendant.

However, begging for a thousand days is too long. So, deliberately provoking the other person to play chess with him using words.

Qianxun Daoren, his name is closely related to his Dao methods.

Everything must be done in one thousand steps, and one needs companionship.

While playing chess with Shengtai, he was actually practicing.

The only limitation was that he could not actively mention the original intention to others. After completing a segment of "Qianxun" cultivation, his appearance and aura would change as if he were a different person.

So, everything depends on fate.

But Shengtai, Li Fan, learned about this in advance.

When the last piece fell, Shengtai Li Fan was just one step away, but ultimately lost to Qianxun Daoren.

"You brat, at least you know to let me win a game."

Although he won, Qianxun Daoren didn't feel happy no matter what.

He overturned the chessboard again, but this time the black and white pieces dispersed into the air on their own.

A surge of energy emanated from the old man's body.

"Congratulations, senior, you are not far from merging Dao again!" Shengtai Li Fan said with a smile.

"What do you want?" Qianxun Daoren didn’t beat around the bush and asked directly.

"The Qinfa Jade Pendant."

"Get lost!" Qianxun Daoren threw the jade pendant directly at him.

Before Li Fan could respond, the old man's figure disappeared.

Shengtai put away the jade pendant, seemingly unconcerned. Anyone who lost nine hundred and ninety-nine games in a row, always with the situation where they were just one step away from victory, would surely be a bit irritable.

Not retaliating physically was already considered a sign of Qianxun Daoren's high quality.

"With the Divine Dao Stone's guidance, there are rare few in the world who can defeat me in a game of chess."

Shengtai shook his head slightly, arriving at a secluded place with no one around.

He directly used the Qinfa Jade Pendant.

Before this, he had already sorted out his identity within the Ten Thousand Immortal Alliance.

So the whole process went smoothly without any surprises.

As usual, a cultivator arrived to guide him, and a door appeared out of thin air.

However, after a moment of dizziness, he found himself back at the headquarters of the Ten Thousand Immortal Alliance...

"Hmm?"

Looking around, it was not the scene of the Ten Thousand Immortal Alliance's headquarters.

Several figures stood in a light sphere by a fictional lake.

Intermittent voices could be heard coming from them.

"Do your best and leave the rest to fate."

"That doesn't sound like something you'd say."

"Our strengths are too disparate, we can only give it our all."

"Fine, go then. We don't know if it will be good or bad."

…

The voice of one of the figures sounded somewhat familiar to Shengtai Li Fan.

But it was also entirely different from anyone else.

He knew this was an illusion induced by the Patriarch's influence upon entering the alliance's headquarters for the first time.

However, for some reason, it was different from what he had seen regarding the original body and the clone before.

"Is one of the figures here the Patriarch?"

"What does this signify?"

Before Li Fan could contemplate further, he suddenly felt the world quaking, and the images shattered layer by layer.

He exited the illusion and appeared back at the Immortal Alliance headquarters.

Used to such occurrences, the guiding cultivator briefly explained before leading Li Fan to where Yanfa Jue was.

With the same familiarity as in previous lives, the encounters were identical.

Until entering the inner sanctum of the protective hall, the notification that reached his ears left Li Fan in astonishment.

"User of Yanfa Jue request, Van Li."

"Identification number: 005. Authorization level: Merging Dao."

"Please wait patiently here. The estimated remaining wait time is three thousand six hundred and twenty-three days and six hours."

Li Fan initially thought he misheard, but after listening again, he was shocked.

"Why do I have to wait for so long?"

Doubting his mind, he made a corresponding expression, couldn't help but ask the empty space.

"If unwilling to wait, feel free to leave."

"Warning, leaving means voluntarily forfeiting the right to use the Qinfa Jade Pendant."

"I am number 005, yet I need to wait for almost ten years. How does that make sense?"

"I haven't used Yanfa Jue before? How long does it take to deduce a method? There must be someone abusing public resources!"

Despite not showing any fear, Li Fan ramped up the protest.

One figure manifested slowly before his eyes in response to Li Fan's uproar.

Expressionless, the figure stared at Li Fan as if he were a dead man.

Li Fan instantly straightened up, "Let me take a look, perhaps I can help."
