Chapter 767

At least, Wang Wan accepted the data calmly.

The black lines that formed his lower body showed no signs of movement at all.

Not to mention detaching and leaving.

"Could it be that I was lucky to draw the grand prize?" Li Fan's eyes flashed with a hint of gloom.

"It seems that Wang Wan is not here for the first time using the Tianxuan Mirror to deduce divine abilities. Maybe I can ask him some questions."

Having completed the deduction of divine abilities, Li Fan's connection with the black lines connected to the platform below had been severed.

However, the countdown was still ongoing, so he had not been expelled from this space yet.

Li Fan did not rush to leave, instead observing the two people beside him and patiently waiting.

As time passed slowly, there was less than an hour left.

Deng Mingsi, who had not made a decision yet, seemed to be getting anxious.

She looked somewhat helpless, and in the end, seemed to make a choice that she was not satisfied with.

Meanwhile, Wang Wan, like Li Fan, had completed the comprehension of divine abilities.

The connection with the lines under his feet was cut, and he opened his eyes.

Sensing Li Fan's gaze, Wang Wan seemed somewhat surprised.

However, communication was not possible here, so after giving a look, he turned his gaze back to Deng Mingsi.

After observing for a while, he shook his head slightly.

The countdown reached zero.

A colorful light shone down from above.

The black and white lines gradually faded, and the surrounding space became lively.

In a moment, the scene before their eyes changed.

In the next moment, Li Fan and the others unconsciously found themselves back on the Lotus Stone Mountain outside Tianquan Prefecture.

Deng Mingsi released a burst of energy, fiercely hitting a nearby mountain.

However, this mountain was different from the ordinary ones, extremely hard.

Deng Mingsi couldn't even create a crack in it.

Tears welled up in her eyes involuntarily.

"Young lady, if you are greedy, you will fail!" Wang Wan sighed upon seeing this situation.

Through Li Fan's inquiries, he slowly learned about the peculiarities of this Endless Spirit Void Technique.

It turned out that the divine tree generated in the minds of cultivators by the Tianxuan Mirror could not be completely comprehended by all cultivators.

Because it contained too many branches, ordinary cultivators could not comprehend it completely.

They could only select by luck.

Like Deng Mingsi, she was delayed in comprehending because she faced too many choices.

"It's okay, don't feel too discouraged, you are still young. Return in the next century. When I first came here, I was just like you, always wanting to choose the most suitable and powerful divine ability on that divine tree. In the end, I wasted a precious opportunity," Wang Wan continued to console Deng Mingsi.

Deng Mingsi was somewhat regretful, saying, "It's all because when I came, my brother secretly told me that the Tianxuan Mirror evaluates potential based on the overall view of the divine tree for cultivators."

"The more comprehensive the view, the greater the potential. When instilling divine abilities, the maximum power limit of the divine ability is also larger."

"I just thought that a little more viewing wouldn't hurt..."

As Deng Mingsi spoke, she seemed increasingly aggrieved, almost on the verge of tears.

"This brother of yours might not have good intentions after all," Li Fan thought to himself.

"What did you say!" Deng Mingsi suddenly froze, then looked at Li Fan in an instant.

"Hmm?" Li Fan's expression changed.

"What did you mean by what you just thought?" Deng Mingsi stared at Li Fan intently, with an aggressive tone.

Li Fan restrained all his thoughts, squinting his eyes. "Friend Deng, I don't understand what you mean."

"Don't pretend, I can sense what others are thinking, especially when it concerns me!" Deng Mingsi said angrily, feeling even more enraged as Li Fan played dumb.

"What you just thought was that my brother had ill intentions. What does that mean?" Deng Mingsi continued to challenge Li Fan.

"I can clearly sense what you're thinking. Brother Yingyu was really thinking for my good when he secretly told me this secret," Deng Mingsi said righteously.

"This little girl really knows nothing about the ways of the world," Li Fan thought to himself again.

Sure enough, Deng Mingsi, who noticed Li Fan's thoughts, blushed deeply.

"To actually sense what others are thinking. What a surprise," Li Fan was amazed in his heart.

Once again tightening his thoughts and no longer teasing the young girl, he began to explain to her.

"If he really cared about you, he wouldn't have told you about this. He would just let you choose a divine ability that suits you. But inadvertently, he revealed it to you."

"He did this on purpose to stimulate your desire, causing you to return empty-handed this time!"

"As for the thoughts you sensed from the other party..."

"If he didn't know in advance about your ability to read minds, then perhaps what you sensed could have been true."

"But obviously, he is aware of your mind-reading ability. So what you sensed cannot be trusted."

Upon hearing this, Deng Mingsi wanted to argue.

But she suddenly froze because she sensed from Li Fan's mind a strong sense of protectiveness and pity.

Such intense and straightforward emotions were rare in her experience.

Deng Mingsi instantly blushed.

"You..."

She stomped her foot in extreme embarrassment, not daring to look at Li Fan again, and flew away.

"Hmph." Li Fan chuckled.

"If possible, friend, you should temporarily leave Tianquan Prefecture to avoid any trouble," Wang Wan, who had witnessed the whole process, couldn't help but remind them.

"Although it was an unintended slip, that little girl heard your thoughts. But after all, you did reveal Deng Qingyu's actions to her."

"This person has a narrow mind. Always seeking revenge for the slightest grievance," Wang Wan warned out of concern.

Li Fan nodded politely. "Oh? I wonder how the Deng family compares to the Xuan family?"

"Xuan..." Wang Wan was stunned at first, then his face changed drastically. "You are..."

"I am a mid-level guest in the Xuan family," Li Fan said calmly.

"In that case, I have said too much. The younger generation of the Deng family..." Wang Wan was somewhat speechless.

He quickly expressed his apologies to Li Fan.

"No need to worry. Friend, you were just reminding me with good intentions." Li Fan dismissed it and left his contact information before flying away.

As he flew, Li Fan felt somewhat lucky.

"I didn't expect that after so many days, I would encounter another cultivator who can read minds, just like the Heavenly Healer."
