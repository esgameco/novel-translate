Chapter 862

In the center of Li Fan's lower abdomen, as the primordial power of the world's source was continuously extracted, the colorful round bead seemed to have dimmed a bit.

But in contrast, the sky-covering roots of the earth penetrated deep into every corner of the Five Elements' Grand Immortal Cave.

The roots of the earth nourished by the source power were somewhat different from those derived from the spiritual power of ordinary cultivators.

They became more ferocious, and...

more greedy.

As if savoring the taste, they did not need Li Fan to manipulate them too much.

With just a thought, the densely packed black tentacles eagerly expanded towards untouched virgin areas.

The vibrant and colorful world of the cave gradually darkened.

It fell into a haunting darkness.

Li Fan watched this scene with an indescribable feeling, even a premonition arose in his heart.

Even without deliberate manipulation, the strengthened roots of the earth could complete the devouring process in a short time.

"The merging of worlds..."

"When Primordial Master created a new law, he must have witnessed scenes of world fusion." Li Fan realized.

He didn't care about the loss of the colorful round bead.

He accelerated the speed of devouring to the limit.

Three months later.

"It's time."

Li Fan suddenly opened his eyes.

The black roots had completely entwined the Five Elements' Grand Immortal Cave.

Looking from outside the void, all that could be seen was a weird ball covered with countless tentacles.

There was no longer the magnificent, grand, vibrant Immortal Cave.

With a thought, numerous roots immediately sprang to life from below.

The Five Elements' Grand Immortal Cave trembled slightly.

The pure and abundant vitality was transmitted to Li Fan's body through the roots.

The originally over twenty-year-old body seemed to regress back to its teenage years in an instant.

And every part of his body - tissues, bones, and flesh - underwent earth-shattering changes under the pure and vital force.

Taking a deep breath, Li Fan adapted to the overwhelming vitality.

Squinting his eyes, he calmly drew a deep bloodline on his arm, almost revealing the bone inside.

Despite the severity of the injury, the wound healed at a visible speed after just a few breaths.

It was noteworthy that Li Fan had not formally devoured the Grand Immortal Cave of the Primordial Infant yet.

Just relying on the feedback of vitality from the Immortal Cave, he had achieved such a level!

"Compared to the initial Immortal Cave, this is much stronger." Li Fan couldn't help but sigh.

As his body slowly dissolved, transforming into flowing ink, it surged towards the Five Elements' Grand Immortal Cave along the countless tentacles.

The Grand Immortal Cave wrapped in black had also sensed the crisis.

A loud rumbling echoed in the Immortal Cave world, the earth split, and the ocean surged with giant waves...

Countless creatures within it involuntarily cried out in agony.

But they couldn't affect Li Fan's devouring speed at all.

With the constant infusion of blackness, a part of the Five Elements' Grand Immortal Cave began to become transparent and disappear.

The struggle of the Immortal Cave grew weaker.

In the end, it fell completely silent.

Although the Five Elements' Grand Immortal Cave disappeared.

The void here still remained.

Empty and void.

After an unknown period of time, a figure suddenly appeared.

It was Li Fan, who had successfully ascended to the Primordial Infant stage.

Surging with immense power all over his body, Li Fan had never felt so strong since he began cultivation.

"Ordinary Harmonizing, the power covers the lands of the Yellow Realm."

"The Five Elements' Grand Immortal Cave I devoured is equivalent to the merging of five provinces."

"Quantitative change leads to qualitative change. With absolute strength alone, I can easily crush all weak Harmonizing cultivators."

"Moreover, I have many divine abilities in me."

Li Fan's eyes sparkled, finally feeling a sense of security.

As long as he didn't encounter a Longevity Realm expert, no matter how reckless he behaved in this world, he should be able to escape calmly!

"With such a significant expansion of power, planning in the future will be much more convenient." He thought to himself.

Having successfully ascended, Li Fan did not rush to leave.

Instead, his spiritual sense continuously swept through the void here, seemingly searching for something.

One time, two times.

After several attempts, nothing abnormal was found.

Li Fan didn't give up.

After his strength greatly increased, he keenly perceived that there seemed to be something else hidden in the Five Elements' void here.

Deeply concealed.

But when Li Fan successfully broke through and thoroughly devoured the Five Elements' Grand Immortal Cave, due to the drastic change in the environment, a bit of a flaw was revealed.

Detected by Li Fan.

This hidden presence did not belong to the Yellow Realm's [Roots of the Earth].

It was the lingering and ever-present...

Du'e Sect!

Li Fan snorted coldly.

A rainbow aura enveloped him, then the rainbow light suddenly brightened countless times in an instant.

Expanding into the surrounding vacuum, the rainbow divine light appeared tangible.

Completely filling and occupying the empty space.

The effects were excellent.

Li Fan's goal had ultimately been achieved.

Under the pure rainbow light, a foreign object finally revealed itself.

Li Fan's eyes shimmered as he reached out to capture it.

Upon careful observation, it was a small figure made of yellow paper.

As if existing for too long, the yellow paper doll had faded somewhat.

And this little figure was quite peculiar.

It had no facial features on its head, only a single eye occupying the entire face.

What made Li Fan feel uneasy was that the one-eyed paper doll seemed to be alive.

As Li Fan grabbed it, the eye slightly moved, staring fixedly at him.

Li Fan felt a hint of heat in his hand, as if being scorched by a blazing fire.

"Playing tricks!"

A burst of flames appeared in his hand, enveloping the one-eyed paper doll.

Li Fan attempted to burn it to ashes.

However, despite the flames dancing for a while, the paper doll remained completely unaffected.

It was as if it had no physical existence at all; even if Li Fan's flames were scorching hot, they couldn't damage it in the slightest!

Next, Li Fan tried various methods.

Five Elements spells, Annihilation Sword, and even formations.

None of them could destroy it.

Seeing this scene, a phrase popped into Li Fan's mind.

"Jump out of the Three Realms and not in the Five Elements."

The yellow paper doll's eye turned slightly.
