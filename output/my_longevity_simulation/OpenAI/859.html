Chapter 859

Although he was very eager to see what would happen after Mr. Bai, Zhenxian, Luan, Ji, and even "Real" formed their ideas into fish bait, the fishing pool was ultimately a first-grade immortal tool, and its interior was even more mysterious and unpredictable.

If he insisted on doing so, no one could guarantee whether he would catch some unimaginable monsters.

Then, like True Immortal in the 115th world, he would rudely destroy the Xuanhuang Realm.

This world had just begun, and the layout was just starting to take effect.

Li Fan currently did not have a plan to catch Real.

"Maybe I'll try it when this world ends," he said.

Referring back to the first idea of fish bait, Ji Shaoli was actually contemplating the rainbow divine light caught by Zhong Shentong from the last world!

When it came to like attracting like, what could better fit the bill than the items in the brilliant starry sky themselves?

At the same time, this was also an experiment.

If it really worked as expected, known items would be easier to catch...

With the help of "Real," the difficulty of fishing would undoubtedly be greatly reduced.

As long as there were enough cycles and attempts, establishing a complete list of items within the fishing pool's light orbs.

Perhaps one day, even this endless fishing pool could be completely emptied!

The key point was that the resource output of this fishing pool was completely independent and unaffected by any other events in the Xuanhuang Realm.

Each of Li Fan's different plans in each world's reincarnation, leading to different historical paths, would not prevent the fishing pool from becoming the most stable source of treasure supply.

It was of great significance.

Of course...

The premise was that Li Fan's speculation was correct.

In the fishing pool of the brilliant starry sky, the clone Ji Shaoli's mind floated with various characteristics of the rainbow divine light displayed by Zhong Shentong.

The rainbow divine light was endless.

Not only was it extremely powerful, but its speed was also terrifying.

It could rival the Yuanying stage while Li Fan was only at the Foundation Building stage.

Moreover, it seemed to have a special protective function as well.

In the 114th world, Zhong Shentong could freely shuttle through the white mist using the rainbow divine light, unaffected by the devouring white mist.

It was truly an all-around offense and defense tactic, with endless clever uses. Once acquired, there was no need for other treasures.

Even with Li Fan's experience, having witnessed all the scenes in the various worlds of reincarnation, the mysterious nature of this rainbow divine light was undoubtedly at the forefront.

To have such performance at the Foundation Building stage was remarkable.

When he cultivated his Dao, reached the Harmony stage, or even the Longevity stage, would the rainbow divine light reveal even more incredible features?

As Ji Shaoli continued to contemplate, a colorful light shadow gradually formed at the end of the fishing line that hung down.
