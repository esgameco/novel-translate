Chapter 774

He contemplated on countermeasures.

Now that he had calmed down, he was not extremely anxious.

After all, the current situation was far from being at the end of the road for him.

In the vast world of Xuanhuang, without reaching the realm of truth, life only grants one chance.

He had merely returned to the same starting point as all sentient beings.

Moreover, he still possessed the significant advantage of insights accumulated over countless reincarnations from previous lives.

"All things stem from an occasional thought in the beginning."

"While creating a clone using my refined blood, I integrated a trace of Mister Bai's charm."

Li Fan pondered quickly.

Soon he identified the various anomalies within himself.

"Indeed, there's no free lunch in the world."

"By resembling Mister Bai, I gained unparalleled talent and grasped some divine abilities."

"In return, my clone, and even my true self, were subtly tainted by it."

"Although, at the first sign of something amiss, I had been on guard. Once I noticed the impact on my true self, I had to return to my true form immediately."

"But what I didn't anticipate was that by the time my true self realized this, it was already too late."

In Li Fan's mind, the stunning and flawless face appeared once again for an instant.

"Mister Bai..."

"Interesting."

"Wishing for my rebirth? Then, let me fulfill your wish!"

A glint flashed in Li Fan's eyes.

The Philosophizing Stone in his mind, which had been overloaded to the extreme, suddenly erupted in a full blue brilliance.

Countless scenes flickered within it.

These were the different possibilities that Li Fan had deduced about the future.

"If you want to obstruct my path..."

"It won't be that simple!"

In an instant, the radiance of the Philosophizing Stone extinguished completely.

Yet, Li Fan had already thought of a strategy to deal with it.

With a hint of a cold smile on his lips, Li Fan sat down slowly, leisurely decrypting the data of the 'Boundless Spirit Illusion Law' once again.

The scene shifts back to Xu Bai's side.

Glancing at the Sacred Embryo, both of them revealed eerie smiles simultaneously.

Xu Bai gently pointed at the red rope in front of him, saying, "Go!"

The red rope, as if coming alive, flew straight towards the Sacred Embryo.

At the same time, Xu Bai took out stacks of top-grade spirit stones from his storage ring.

With a single slap, he shattered them.

The Sacred Blood Embryo Technique, which created offspring or clones, did not possess cultivation.

However, for Xu Bai, the Sacred Embryo, this was not an issue at all.

His aptitude was similar to Xu Bai's, having already reached an unimaginable level and having accumulated years of cultivation experience due to Li Fan's reincarnations.

"Absorb the essence of heaven and earth to control its energy!"

Sacred Embryo Xu Bai intoned slowly.

Within the secret chamber, an invisible whirlwind seemed to stir.

The substantial spiritual energy emitted a howling sound, rushing towards Sacred Embryo Xu Bai in torrents.

In just a few breaths, he broke through the barrier between mortals and cultivators, successfully stepping into the Qi Refinement Initial Stage.

The speed of absorbing spiritual energy did not slow down; it only accelerated.

The clone Xu Bai remained indifferent, repeating the process of taking out top-grade spirit stones, shattering them, then taking out more of them.

If it were an ordinary cultivator absorbing so much spiritual energy in a short time, their body would likely have burst due to the pressure.

However, the Sacred Embryo Xu Bai seemed to be a bottomless pit, absorbing all the spiritual energy into his dantian.

His realm too was visibly ascending rapidly.

Mid Qi Refinement, Late Qi Refinement, Consummation of Qi Refinement!

The Heavenly Calamity that troubled numerous cultivators seemed to be non-existent on Sacred Embryo Xu Bai!

The clone Xu Bai witnessed firsthand the terrifying cultivation speed of a truly top-tier new law cultivator without any bottleneck hindrances.

"Use the wonders of the False Heavens and Earth to lay the foundation!"

Sacred Embryo Xu Bai intoned again.

Within his dantian, the red rope emitted a faint glow.

Without any obstruction, everything fell into place effortlessly.

In just a breath's time, Sacred Embryo Xu Bai had successfully built his foundation.

The faint red rope dissolved into his dantian, becoming his foundation.

The true name of it was revealed as well.

"The Will of Heaven Sword."

"If there is the Will of Heaven, it shall be with me."

"Wonderful."

Sacred Embryo Xu Bai and the clone Xu Bai echoed in unison.

Despite stepping into the Foundation Establishment Stage, Sacred Embryo Xu Bai did not stop his cultivation.

He continued to absorb the dense and pure spiritual energy within the secret chamber endlessly.

His cultivation realm also swiftly and steadily ascended.

Middle Foundation Establishment, Late Foundation Establishment, Consummation of Foundation Establishment...

"Peer into the laws of Heaven and Earth, and refine the Golden Dan."

Sacred Embryo Xu Bai stood with his hands behind his back.

With a slight inhalation, the dense spiritual energy, which was on the verge of condensation into liquid in the secret chamber, vanished instantly.

He simply took over a storage ring filled with spirit stones handed to him by the clone Xu Bai.

Grasping it in his hand, a red light slowly materialized in his dantian.

"I will be the Will of Heaven, and my heart will be the Heart of Heaven."

Sacred Embryo Xu Bai naturally spoke these words slowly.

As if a ground-shaking explosion occurred.

The red light spread throughout his dantian.

Eventually, it condensed to its utmost limits.

After a moment, a red light spot appeared in Sacred Embryo Xu Bai's dantian.

Sacred Embryo Xu Bai entered the Golden Dan Stage!

In such a short time, he achieved what ordinary cultivators would take decades, or even centuries, to accomplish.

He did not halt his cultivation pace.

The supply of spiritual energy continued unceasingly, propelling Sacred Embryo Xu Bai towards the Consummation of the Golden Dan stage.

As expected, no Heavenly Calamity emerged.

This duality in the world of Xuanhuang showcased the way of heaven.

And when Sacred Embryo Xu Bai ultimately ascended to the Consummation of the Golden Dan stage.

Barely half a day had passed since his creation.

"Is this the cultivation speed of Mister Bai?"

"No, Mister Bai back then was even faster. Changing from mortal to deity in an instant."

Within the secret chamber, Sacred Embryo Xu Bai and the clone Xu Bai engaged in a dialogue.

After a long silence, the clone Xu Bai shook his head slightly.

"In the current world of Xuanhuang, reaching the Golden Dan stage within half a day is already the limit."

Sacred Embryo Xu Bai nodded in agreement, "Absorb the spirit of heaven and earth to nurture the Yuan Ying."

"Without proper preparation, even with high talent, it's impossible to advance further."

"As the saying goes..."

Sacred Embryo Xu Bai bowed slightly towards the clone Xu Bai and gracefully exited, saying, "Before the immortal path, one cannot step; the ancestral founder is above!"

His figure disappeared.

However, his voice continued to resonate in the secret chamber for a long time.

The clone Xu Bai knew that Sacred Embryo Xu Bai had gone out to seek opportunities to form the Yuan Ying.

For other cultivators, this process might be extremely arduous.

But for Sacred Embryo Xu Bai, it was a simple matter.

He didn't even need to worry about Sacred Embryo Xu Bai's safety.

Although he had only reached the Consummation of the Golden Dan stage for now, even Nascent Soul cultivators might not be his match.

Because...
