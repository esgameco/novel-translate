Chapter 773

In every life, one must create a clone of oneself. He had long been accustomed to splitting his mind in two.

However, at this moment, with the birth of this other entity, almost equivalent to a third clone, a completely different feeling surged in Xu Bai's heart.

The miraculous and mystical transformation was based on the "Stealing Heaven and Changing Day" technique, further derived from it.

It could be said to be the most orthodox technique of creating clones in the immortal cultivation world.

Whether the main body or the clone, both were completely controlled by his consciousness.

In contrast to the "Holy Blood Incarnation" technique.

Before the Holy Fetus separated from the mother's body, it did indeed feel like a part of his own body. He could control it as he pleased.

However, after he separated from it behind his back, it seemed to undergo a mysterious change.

Indeed, another consciousness split from himself, inside the Holy Fetus.

And he could still maintain full control of the Holy Fetus.

But...

There was always a feeling that something was not right.

The Holy Fetus turned around, locking eyes with Xu Bai.

It was as if there was a mirror between the two, one clothed and the other completely naked.

From the outside, there was no apparent difference between the two.

But after staring at Xu Bai for a long time, there was a constant feeling in his heart that something was off between them.

He suddenly remembered a horror movie scene he had seen before crossing over.

Late at night, in the bathroom.

Having finished washing up, he inadvertently caught sight of his other self in the mirror.

To his horror, the reflection of himself in the mirror suddenly revealed a strange smile towards him.

Thoughts flashed by in an instant.

In reality, there was no event where the Holy Fetus lost control and smiled mysteriously at him.

But as he looked at the Holy Fetus before him, a thought suddenly occurred to Xu Bai.

If his original self, Li Fan, were to fall, there was no doubt that his clone, Xu Bai, would also perish.

But what about this Holy Fetus?

Would it also die with them?

The purpose of the "Holy Blood Incarnation" technique was to ensure the eternal lineage of the imperial family.

So the answer was quite obvious. It would not.

"This is the fundamental difference between the Holy Fetus and the clone," Xu Bai realized.

"What Xiao Qing said is almost the same as having a clone..."

"It's just almost."

Understanding this, Li Fan, who was in Tianquan Province comprehending the "Boundless Illusory Law," couldn't help but pause his actions.

Recalling the journey of his clone in the empire, a strange expression appeared on his face.

"It's interesting. Indeed, it seems inevitable that it has been influenced to some extent."

Turning back, looking from the perspective of his original self.

The choices of his clone Xu Bai seemed a bit hasty.

Of course, these decisions were still made autonomously by Li Fan.

But in different situations and environments, even the same person might make completely opposite choices.

That's what happened with Xu Bai.

If the original self personally experienced this, faced with the Red String and the "Holy Blood Incarnation," he would undoubtedly first study the Red String.

Rather than immediately going for the "Holy Blood Incarnation."

Although he did indeed have a plan to use the "Holy Blood Incarnation" to create another clone.

But he wouldn't be so eager.

He would be more cautious.

Li Fan, who usually thought everything over in an instant, although he still didn't feel any sense of crisis in his heart.

He still felt some unease.

After weighing for a long time, he couldn't help but have the direct thought of "returning to the truth."

"Whether to virtualize the current scene and return to the original anchor point."

The surroundings gradually became void as he received the hint to return to the truth.

He thought of the yet-to-be-deciphered "Boundless Illusory Law."

He thought of the unresolved Disintegration Disc that had not yet been integrated.

He thought of not having found a way to completely shield the Tianxuan Treasure Mirror's perception using the End Stone Plate.

He thought of not having used the Medicine King Ding, breaking out of the Xuanhuang Realm and exploring the Immortal Ruins.

He thought of...

Leaving was something he truly hesitated over.

After a long hesitation, Li Fan, like a withered stone, completely stopped moving.

The fierce mental struggle in his mind continued, and Li Fan's body began to tremble slightly.

"Whether to virtualize the current scene and return to the original anchor point."

The hint to return to the truth echoed again.

But to Li Fan, it was no longer pleasant to hear.

Humans rely on desires to drive their actions.

And at this moment, as Li Fan reopened the truth panel for the second time, the desire to "return to the truth" was slightly weaker than before...

This time, there was less struggle.

In the end, Li Fan still closed the truth panel.

Exhaling heavily...

It was as if in an instant, he lost all his strength.

Li Fan slumped directly to the ground, panting heavily.

After a long time, barely supporting his body with both hands, Li Fan's eyes showed emotions he couldn't quite grasp.

He began his third attempt.

The result was evident.

This time, he made decisions even faster.

Within just a few breaths, he closed the truth panel.

"The Profound Yellow Immortal Heart Mantra..."

For the fourth time, Li Fan silently activated the supreme immortal method that had saved him from danger multiple times.

But as he activated the Immortal Heart Mantra, at the moment he opened the truth panel...

Even before the many things that bound him had a chance to interfere, he voluntarily withdrew.

The Immortal Heart Mantra was formed by reversing the cultivation way of the heart.

And now, as Li Fan desired to return to the truth to avoid disaster.

Under the operation of the Immortal Heart Mantra, the thought of returning to the truth instantaneously turned into nourishment for the expanded divine consciousness within him.

After another failure, Li Fan stood still in the secret chamber, silent for a long time.

This time, Li Fan didn't struggle.

Instead, he closed his eyes and started to think calmly.

At this moment, Li Fan encountered the most dangerous situation since crossing over.

Because the layout of this world had been established for a long time and had not yet come to fruition. This led to many emotions of "reluctance."

Under the influence of a certain power, these emotions were amplified to the extreme, causing him to autonomously refuse to return to the truth.

Even at this moment, Li Fan still believed there was nothing wrong with himself.

It was just that he had been a bit too greedy.

But Li Fan was ultimately mindful of his life.

Having a firm belief in longevity, he also realized.

If he couldn't return to the truth, in this perilous and mysterious Xuanhuang Realm, there would indeed be a risk of falling at any time.
