"The more unattainable something is, the more we desire it."

Upon hearing Li Fan's words, Little Lolita's eyes immediately lit up.

She couldn't help but say, "How can anyone feel so bored! If I didn't have to deduce the martial arts techniques, I would definitely sleep for decades first!"

Seemingly yearning for that beautiful holiday life, Little Lolita's face was full of longing.

However, she soon felt very discouraged, saying, "Ah, don't even talk about resting for decades. Even resting for ten days or half a month is very difficult."

"It's so good to not have to work!"

Recalling the happy times of slacking off earlier, Little Lolita couldn't help but sigh.

Then she glanced at Li Fan and complained, "It's all because of you, otherwise I could pretend for a bit longer."

"Avoiding the issue won't solve anything," Li Fan chuckled, "Solving the root cause of the problem is the correct path."

Little Lolita snorted, "It's easy for you to say. I made an agreement with the Supreme Transcendant back then that I need to deduce all the martial arts techniques in the world to completion before I can rest completely."

Little Lolita gave Li Fan a disdainful look, "I am unique in this world, where else can you find another me!"

However, Little Lolita then paused, "If the me in thousands of years could directly hand over the deduced martial arts techniques to me, then I could pretend to be working while actually resting, right?"

Little Lolita's eyes were full of excitement, lost in fantasy.

Through the conversation with Deducter Jué, Saint Embryo Li Fan had gathered enough information.

At this point, he decisively spoke to wake the other person up, "I have explained your condition. I need the martial arts, right?"

Deducer Jué had no reaction at all, just staring blankly and chuckling foolishly.

Li Fan had to raise his voice and repeated himself.

It was then that Little Lolita woke up as if from a dream.

Looking at Li Fan with resentment, she complained, "Give me a bit more time to rest, really..."

"It will probably take some time before those guys come to urge us."

"And your martial arts are indeed more complicated, maybe I can take the opportunity to rest a bit more."

Waving his hand, let Li Fan wait a little longer.

Little Lolita zoned out again.

Unlike when Deductor Jué pretended to sleep earlier, this time he could only see the names of the martial arts written on the book covers.

The martial arts themselves were bound by chains of characters, making them unreadable.

Li Fan gave up his idea of prying. He watched his breath, his nose, and his heart, patiently waiting for Little Lolita to rest to her fill.

A whole day passed, seemingly under the urging inquiries from the Wanjie, Deductor Jué finally woke up with a hint of regret.

"Ah..."

She reached out her small hand, and a book titled "Three Calamities and a Hundred Tribulations Bright Divine Law" appeared out of thin air.

Obviously, she had deduced it long ago.

"Take it, you should leave soon too."

"I hope the next person who deduces the martial arts will make it more difficult, so I can save some more free time."

In her words, Saint Embryo Li Fan returned to the character light ball space.

Once back, Deductor Jué became as listless and drowsy as before.

Seeing the faint warning in the other party's eyes, Li Fan suddenly understood.

In reality, Deductor Jué's speed of deducing martial arts was far faster than her outward performance suggested.

Every martial art deduction could be quickly completed. Then she would have extra time to rest.

Perhaps this was one of the reasons she could persist in deducing for thousands of years.

The Guarding Hall of the Ten Thousand Immortal League was probably aware of this.

But pressing too hard might have a reverse effect. So they chose to turn a blind eye.

With the martial arts in hand, the purpose of this trip had been achieved.

Li Fan bid farewell to the white-haired elder.

Before leaving the headquarters of the Ten Thousand Immortal League, he expressed his desire to visit the "Museum of Divine Treasures.”

The white-haired elder was taken aback.

For him, visiting the treasure trove could be done at any time.

However, the token he gave represented a favor, which had many uses.

If left outside, countless cultivators would fight for it desperately.

Was it worth being wasted so easily?

"Young man, reconsider?" Even the white-haired elder himself felt it was a bit extravagant and couldn't help but advise.

"No need. I have made up my mind," Li Fan replied calmly.

The white-haired elder shook his head slightly and stopped trying to dissuade him.

After retrieving the token and seeing Li Fan off from the Guarding Hall, he instructed the escorting cultivator before returning to the Guarding Hall.

Meanwhile, Li Fan followed behind the escorting cultivator and headed towards the Museum of Divine Treasures.

He had changed his mind this time and wanted to visit the Museum of Divine Treasures on a whim.

It was actually the intention of the original self.

Firstly, he wanted to take a look at the skull displayed in the center of the treasure trove.

Secondly, during the time spent in the museum, he planned to memorize the "Three Calamities and a Hundred Tribulations Bright Divine Law."

It should be safe inside the treasure trove.

But once he left the headquarters of the Immortal League, that might not be the case.

Moreover, his performance in the Guarding Hall had been somewhat out of line, so he might be targeted.

Therefore, Li Fan had already decided that once he left the Immortal League headquarters, he would discard the Sacred Embryo to prevent being traced.

Upon entering the Museum of Divine Treasures, memories about the collections surfaced.

It seemed that the original self had been here before.

But Li Fan still pretended as if it was his first visit.

Amazed, he secretly browsed through the "Three Calamities and a Hundred Tribulations Bright Divine Law" deduced by Deductor Jué.

The complexity of this martial art was not inferior to the Xuantian School’s Immortal Techniques.

However, its power was weaker, suitable for performance at the Harmonious Way Realm level.

But it should be enough to deceive and manipulate Mòrúbīn.

After memorizing the entire martial art, Li Fan nodded satisfactorily.

Immersed in the comprehension of the martial art, he finally came to the central area of the Museum of Divine Treasures and returned to his senses.

He gazed at the skull in front of him, lost in thought.

It was no different from an ordinary human skull.
