Chapter 851

Ji Shaoli patted Wei Qizheng's shoulder, his eyes showing an encouraging look.

After leaving the secret room, everyone saw Wei Qizheng completely transformed, and couldn't help but secretly admire.

They didn't know what secret technique Ji Shaoli had used on him, as all the previous apprehension and tension had completely disappeared.

In its place was full of confidence.

Ji Shaoli and Wei Qizheng did not explain.

"Everyone, City Lord is still waiting, I will leave first," Wei Qizheng said as he bowed.

"Then, early congratulations on Brother Wei's success!"

"Wei little brother, if you become wealthy, don't forget us!"

At this moment, everyone's attitude towards Wei Qizheng had completely changed, and they quickly returned the courtesy.

Wei Qizheng nodded slightly and left the Hai Xuan Island through the teleportation array.

Watching his gradually disappearing figure, He Zhenghao sighed with a bit of pride, "I have long seen that Brother Wei is extraordinary, destined for success. But I didn't expect it to come so fast! It's not in vain that I've taken care of him before."

"Comparisons are odious. We prepared for the exam more than a year in advance, but still couldn't match his last-minute brilliance! Sigh..."

"Sigh. If Wei Dao You really gets selected as one of the Thirty-Three Immortal candidates, and directly ascends to Unity Path in the future. Wonâ€™t I be able to be considered a brother or disciple of the Unity Path Immortal Lord? He wouldn't turn his back on me then, right?"

...

People's reactions were varied.

But overall, they couldn't hide their envy and jealousy.

Only Ji Shaoli's eyes were flickering.

The Wan Xian Alliance attached great importance to hosting the selection plan for the Thirty-Three Immortal candidates.

To be safe, he did not try to harm Wei Qizheng in any way.

Even the fragment that separated called the "End Disintegration Disk" was just an individual entity. It had no connection to the mother body in his sea of consciousness.

It only silently recorded all the surrounding sensations, and it would only transmit the collected information when it was completely destroyed.

Such caution was to prevent any clues about Wei Qizheng from being discovered.

For some reason, Ji Shaoli had a vague premonition in his heart.

Wei Qizheng was likely to be successfully selected as one of the Thirty-Three Immortal candidates. But similarly, wanting to see him again might be a long time away.

Wei Qizheng's summons to the Immortal Alliance Headquarters was not known to many cultivators on the Hai Xuan Island.

He left quietly.

On the third day after his departure, which was the tenth day after the end of the exam, the results were announced.

Some were happy, some were worried.

But on Hai Xuan Island, it was full of joy and laughter.

"Six candidates registered, all six passed! If this news spreads, it will definitely cause a sensation!"

"Haha, those who didn't join our mutual aid association before will definitely regret it!"

Amidst the excitement, He Zhenghao suddenly frowned, worrying and said, "What if jealous people report us? What should we do?"

As soon as this was said, the room fell silent.

"It shouldn't...right?"

A hint of worry flashed in everyone's eyes.

"Hehe, what are you afraid of? A clear conscience fears no accusation!" At this moment, Ji Shaoli said solemnly.

"It's just hitting the right questions! The whole Wan Xian Alliance has countless organizations studying the exam and focusing on the questions. How does one more make a difference?" Ji Shaoli justified himself.

He Zhenghao and the others were slightly stunned.

But then Ji Shaoli coldly added, "Furthermore, who can report us? Leaking exam questions is a serious offense, without solid evidence, who dares to speak nonsense!"

"If there are really some shortsighted people..."

"Who knows who will die first!"

These words were tough and made everyone start to think, slowly dispelling their worries.

"Alright, let's not talk about these unlucky things. Since you have all passed the exam, what are your thoughts on where you want to work in the future?"

Upon registering for the exam, there was a preliminary indication of the region and position preferences.

However, the specific assignment of positions would still be determined by the Immortal Alliance Headquarters.

It was based on various factors such as the cultivator's origin, cultivation level, and potential.

The general principle was to assign most people locally and transfer elite cultivators to the Immortal Alliance Headquarters.

But occasionally, there were special cases.

However, they were all assigned to this remote place in the Cloud Sea because of the pressure from the Heng Family.

Most cultivators could only resign themselves to the assigned positions.

But for those with backgrounds and connections, it meant maximizing their own benefits.

Most of them were already satisfied with passing the exam.

But after hearing Ji Shaoli's words, it seemed like there was still room for maneuvering.

Therefore, everyone became a little tempted.

They quietly communicated with Ji Shaoli.

He Zhenghao, however, hesitated.

"He Dao You, what do you think?" Ji Shaoli asked with a smile.

After hesitating for a while, He Zhenghao ultimately responded, "If possible, I would like to go to Tianyunzhou. Tianquan Prefecture is also fine."

Neither were Tianyu Prefecture but both were very close.

Ji Shaoli instantly understood He Zhenghao's thoughts.

After pondering for a moment, he looked at everyone and said, "This exam is special. I can't guarantee the final outcome, so I can only do my best."

"Success or failure, it all depends on your own fortunes!"

Everyone understood this well and thanked Ji Shaoli once again.

"Before the final results are out, make good use of this time to practice. If you can advance to a higher realm, then things will change again."

He Zhenghao and the others all nodded in agreement.

With hope, they began to practice more earnestly than ever before.

Meanwhile, Ji Shaoli started leveraging his financial power, trying to insert as many influences as possible into the Immortal Alliance's interior.

The Cloud Sea was destined for destruction, and there was no future in staying there.

The activities related to Jing Xuan, Shen Yu Zi, Huang Fu Song, and others to smooth out loose ends were temporarily not mentioned.

As the exam came to an end, the attention of the Wan Xian Alliance cultivators shifted to the biggest winners of this exam - the three hundred plus lucky individuals who had passed the initial screening and were heading to the Immortal Alliance Headquarters to participate in the final selection for the Thirty-Three Immortal candidates.

After all, reaching the position of Unity Path Immortal Lord...

Such influence was beyond comparison with ordinary Immortal Alliance positions.

Those who were well-informed quickly compiled a list of these lucky three hundred plus candidates.

Detailed information about them also appeared before the eyes of others.

After some research, it was found that there was no commonality among them.

The cultivation levels of the three hundred plus selected cultivators varied. Ranging from Foundation Building to Nascent Soul, all realms were represented.

From lone cultivators without background to core disciples of prominent families, everyone was included.

The selection criteria of the Wan Xian Alliance really confused people.

At this moment, Li Fan was also holding this list and studying it.

"Hmm, there's no Shuo Feng."

"Nor the names of those true high-level heirs of the Immortal Alliance seen in previous eras."

"..."
