Chapter 863

In his hand surged a pure and extremely colorful brilliance.

Encasing the one-eyed little man inside.

This colorful divine light, after being repeatedly and deliberately compressed by Li Fan.

The seven colors of light were almost merging into one.

Turning into a dazzling white light.

Like holding a sun in his hand, illuminating the entire surrounding void with intense brightness.

The yellow paper figure in the white light seemed to become somewhat distorted due to immense pressure.

But Li Fan continued to increase the output of the colorful divine light.

With the Five Elements Heavenly Realm as a backup, even the damaged colorful jade light could maximize its original power.

The colorful divine light does not belong to the realm of Xuanyellow.

As it began to condense, it began to repel all external objects.

The yellow one-eyed paper man seemed to finally sense the danger.

Its eyes turned rapidly, looking at Li Fan.

It seemed to have an intention to communicate.

But Li Fan completely ignored it.

Without any intention of stopping, he increased the output power of the colorful divine light once again.

Finally, when a point of white brilliance reached its limit.

Like a violent explosion that occurred when the world was created, in the dazzling fire, the yellow paper figure was completely burned away.

The weight on his heart finally lifted, Li Fan let out a sigh of relief and slowly retracted the colorful divine light back into his body.

He had just ascended to the Yuan Infant stage and had not completely refined the colorful jade light.

The recent actions were actually somewhat overwhelming for Li Fan.

But finally destroying this method of the Dutuo Sect was worth it.

"Eternal Calamity World, seeking the one to endure calamity."

"It seems that the yellow one-eyed paper man is their way of finding the one to endure calamity."

"No wonder they are a sect that uses 'enduring calamity' as a cultivation resource."

"Ordinary spells simply cannot touch these paper figures, let alone destroy them."

"..."

"I wonder where these old folks are hiding."

Li Fan frowned slightly.

After much consideration, he still had no clue.

Deciding to put this matter aside for now.

The Five Elements Heavenly Realm had already been swallowed into Li Fan's body, and the passage that had changed the coordinates naturally became ineffective.

At this moment, Li Fan was actually in the void of Tianlingzhou.

When he concealed his breath, tore through the void, and returned to the Xuanyellow Realm.

A chilling feeling suddenly emerged from his heart.

Li Fan raised his head and looked towards the sky.

Whether it was his illusion or not, Li Fan seemed to see countless yellow one-eyed paper figures made of paper distributed in every corner of the Xuanyellow Realm.

Unnoticed by anyone, they silently stared with their single eyes, observing the world.

When Li Fan broke through the void back to the Xuanyellow Realm, they seemed to sense the perpetrator who had destroyed one of their kind.

All their eyes turned towards Li Fan!

The moment his body stiffened as all eyes locked onto him, Li Fan's body instantly froze.

However, before he could even breathe, those gazes naturally disappeared.

From start to finish, it was as if it were just Li Fan's illusion.

His frozen body gradually returned to normal, but Li Fan's expression didn't relax.

He knew that the scene that just happened was not an illusion.

In the Xuanyellow Realm, it had been infested by these Dutuo Sect members and their surveillance eyes!

And almost no one knew about it!

Li Fan couldn't help but think that even he, a mortal, near the edge of the Great Abyss, was also suddenly awakened by this illusion.

Escaping a disaster.

Does this mean...

That even the Xuanyellow Realm is dotted with fixed yellow one-eyed paper figures?

Thinking about his actions and realizing that they may have been scrutinized by these Dutuo Sect cultivators behind the scenes, a chill ran through Li Fan's heart.

But having experienced so much, he didn't lose his composure.

After some analysis, he gradually calmed down.

First of all, it could be confirmed that each one-eyed paper figure's surveillance range was not too large.

Otherwise, it wouldn't require so many paper talismans to cover the entire Xuanyellow Realm.

Secondly, besides monitoring, these Dutuo Sect talismans probably didn't have any other functions.

The Ten Thousand Immortals Union and the Five Elders Council must also be aware of the existence of these yellow paper talismans.

But destroying them wouldn't be an easy task.

So, more probably, they chose to turn a blind eye.

"No wonder the headquarters of the Ten Thousand Immortals Union is built within the Tianxuan Mirror, not in the Xuanyellow Realm."

"It's probably to avoid being watched by these yellow paper figures."

"And Zhao Senior Sister's One Mind Immortal Sect is also in a separate space."

Li Fan recalled his encounter when he was almost led to the One Mind Immortal Sect.

"The Dutuo Sect is really low-quality."

In his life journey, Li Fan had encountered figures like Ink Kill, Divine Healer, Ruomu, Mr. Bai, True Immortals, and more.

Although every encounter was fraught with danger, Li Fan held deep respect for these true powerhouses.

Even if he died at their hands due to a mistake, he wouldn't hold any resentment.

But this time, Li Fan was genuinely disgusted.

Anyone who discovered the Xuanyellow Realm filled with these paper talismans wouldn't feel good at all.

Even during the 114th era when the Abyssal Net descended, it was openly done.

The green sky, though somewhat eerie, was definitely much better than being plastered with these paper patches that obscured heaven and earth.

Recalling the brief glimpse just now, a thought emerged in Li Fan's mind to completely clear them out.

But...

It was just wishful thinking.

After so many years, since the Ten Thousand Immortals Union had tolerated the existence of these paper figures, it seemed clear that eradicating them completely was no easy task.

After all, who would be willing to live under the watchful eyes of others every day?

"It seems I have to put other matters aside for now."

"At the very least, I must first address this constant surveillance issue."

Li Fan thought to himself.

At the same time, a hint of doubt arose in his heart.

What did these Dutuo Sect members really want to achieve?

Li Fan had experienced many endings in the Xuanyellow Realm.

He had even witnessed the destruction of worlds.

Yet he had never seen a true Dutuo Sect cultivator present.

"Are they waiting for the appearance of the one to endure calamity and for the calamity to be over completely?"

Feeling the faint, elusive gazes transmitted from the surroundings, Li Fan's thoughts fluctuated.

After a long time, he flew towards Jiu Mountain Prefecture.
