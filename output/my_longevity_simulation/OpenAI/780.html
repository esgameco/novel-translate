Chapter 780

Together with it, the rapid expansion of the cave's land was happening once again.

With abundant river water nourishing it, the vegetation inside the cave became even more lush. Trees sprung up densely, flourishing.

"Tian Yu will give birth to the river..."

Li Fan looked at the Sacred Embryo Xu Bai with some satisfaction nodding.

Then leaving the cave and once again embarking on a journey in the mysterious yellow realm.

Like an ordinary person, measuring this land with his own two feet.

Whenever encountering a suitable transformation of Tian Yu, it was like harvesting herbs and incorporating it into his own cave.

After the initial shock, as he saw more, he became somewhat numb and accustomed to it.

Li Fan had read many insights from Core Formation cultivators turning into deities.

It was said that the transformation of Tian Yu in the world, like the meridians in a mortal's body, was extremely difficult to find without special means.

And once it was extracted, it would cause a huge sensation, creating a great disturbance.

But all these experiences were ineffective on Sacred Embryo Xu Bai.

By just squinting and observing for a moment, one could determine the presence or absence of the "transformation of the heavens and the earth".

Then, with a slow wave of his hand, as if summoning a light breeze from the heavens and earth. Quietly and imperceptibly, he would make it his own.

As more and more changes and innovations were integrated into the cave, driven by the power of transformation, Sacred Embryo Xu Bai's own cave was renewed daily.

Mountains, rivers, valleys, plains, rivers, forests...

The cave world was full of vitality, constantly changing, resembling a small world that was continuously growing.

However, Sacred Embryo Xu Bai wrinkled his brow in a rare show of concern.

"Something seems to be missing."

"This vitality is just a fa√ßade and cannot support a perfect cycle on its own."

"If there is no external injection, this artificially thriving cave will soon return to desolation."

Li Fan sensed the thoughts of the other party.

Sacred Embryo Xu Bai, on the other hand, flew in mid-air, scanning the surroundings and then looking into the distance.

This went on for a whole day.

Then, a slight smile appeared on his face.

Calm and composed, he took a step forward towards his destination.

From Li Fan's perspective, Sacred Embryo Xu Bai's speed was not fast at all.

He didn't even use his spiritual power, just relying solely on his physical body to move forward.

But to an outsider, it seemed as though he was covering vast distances in a single step, disappearing in an instant.

"It's not me moving, but rather the heavens and earth cooperating actively to shorten the distance."

"Condensing space to move swiftly."

As Li Fan carefully pondered his steps, the changes in the surrounding heavens and earth brought a sudden enlightenment.

"Although this time, Mr. Bai used my body for rebirth, it's also a great opportunity."

"Every untapped transformation of Tian Yu represents the supreme wealth that can create powerful deities."

"And, every move of Sacred Embryo Xu Bai is in harmony with the heavens and earth. Each can be seen as divine abilities."

"Such as the greenery that nurtures the cave, or plucking the essence of the heavens effortlessly. And even this space-shrinking technique now."

"With my level of comprehension, realizing one insight each lifetime is already fortunate. But they are coming one after another now."

"Even more valuable is that all these abilities are being exerted through my body, senses, just like I would do it myself, seamlessly. With a little more contemplation in the future, it might be possible to perfectly recreate them."

"The prerequisite being, if there is a next life."

Countless thoughts raced through Li Fan's mind as Sacred Embryo Xu Bai finally arrived at his destination after a long journey.

"Is this... the Cloudy Mist Sea?"

A surprised expression flashed in Li Fan's mind.

It had been almost ten years since the world-destroying event at Mo Sha.

Although it still looked like the post-apocalyptic scenery here, the previously raging lava had ceased spewing. It had gradually cooled down, forming hard black rocks.

The spaces and voids that seemed to have been gnawed by insects had all healed unknowingly.

At a glance, the ruins of the Cloudy Mist Sea appeared desolate.

But with a closer inspection, there seemed to be a different kind of power incubating.

If other cultivators were to come here, they would probably sense a deadly crisis and be wary of it.

Sacred Embryo Xu Bai gazed at the ruins for a long time before entering the Cloudy Mist Sea. However, before stepping in, he glanced in the direction of Yuandao Province.

An intense feeling of resentment and fear surged in his heart.

For the first time, a negative emotion was revealed on Sacred Embryo Xu Bai's face.

With a cold snort, he turned around, stepping calmly into the Cloudy Mist Sea.

Unlike the emotions that arose when glimpsing Yuandao Province, as he walked through the ruins here, Sacred Embryo Xu Bai's emotions were unexpectedly complex.

It was a blend of multiple emotions that made it difficult for Li Fan to discern the exact nature of the feeling.

However, his attention quickly shifted to the movements of Sacred Embryo Xu Bai.

Clearly, his purpose for this journey was to perfect the "transformation of Tian Yu" in his own cave.

He didn't reach out like before but showed unusual solemnity this time.

Using his hand as a pen, he drew lines of reality and illusion in the sky as he moved between heaven and earth.

The continuous lines stretched from the west where Yuandao Province and Shi Linzhou were near the coast, to the east where the endless ocean met.

After nearly half a day's effort, the approximate area of the original Cloudy Mist Sea was enclosed within those lines.

However, the contours were peculiar and it was much smaller than the original Cloudy Mist Sea area.

Upon closing the lines of reality and illusion, they suddenly disappeared into the heavens and earth.

Then, Sacred Embryo Xu Bai took a deep breath.

Reaching out to grasp the invisible circle, he slowly exerted force.

As the circle was gradually collected in his hand, something seemed to be pulled out from the heavens and earth in this Cloudy Mist Sea area.

Li Fan concentrated intensely, not wanting to miss any detail.

The crystal in his mind emitted a radiant blue light, recording every insight and comprehension originating from Sacred Embryo Xu Bai, without missing any detail.

Initially, the process of gathering the lines seemed relatively easy for Sacred Embryo Xu Bai. But as he progressed, the counterforce from the void seemed to grow stronger.

Each tightening required almost his entire strength. The fine lines gathered in his palm became tangible upon contact, feeling warm like a gentle spring breeze yet cold like ice threads.

With a resolute eye, Sacred Embryo Xu Bai continued to collect the lines.

As more and more of the circle was pulled out, a sudden change occurred in the once calm Cloudy Mist Sea ruins!

Boom!

The previously quelled black and red molten lava erupted violently once again.

Numerous black-colored wriggling voids emerged like countless small insects, densely covering the area.

The terrifying aura of the end times descended suddenly.

"This is..." Li Fan's pupils shrunk in shock.

"The Cloudy Mist Sea that was about to recover has turned into a dead land again!"

"Those pulled out are the forces that drive the world's revival!"

The surroundings turned back into a veritable earthly purgatory, yet Sacred Embryo Xu Bai's actions continued without the slightest hesitation.
