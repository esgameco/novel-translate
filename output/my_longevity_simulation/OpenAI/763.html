Chapter 763

"My judgment is far better than that old Willow. I never make mistakes. I bet that these little ones are all rare talents in cultivation."

"I've traveled through more than a dozen states and only found these few. I know how difficult this journey has been."

Fang Zaiji sighed.

"You didn't even say hello and just kidnapped these kids, didn't you?" Staring at the crying group of children, San Liu's expression turned unfriendly.

"Joke, walking the immortal path, cultivating the path to immortality. Many mortals can't achieve it even if they beg. I kindly guide them. Do I still need their parents' permission?" Fang Zaiji glanced at San Liu and said matter-of-factly.

"Besides, our Medicine King Sect is currently in seclusion. We need to be more discreet in our actions."

San Liu was speechless after hearing these words.

"Since Mr. Fang found them, it means they have a connection with our Medicine King Sect. I believe in Mr. Fang's judgment," Xu Bai cleared his throat and confirmed.

"Nowadays, it's not like ancient times when countless sects roamed the world selecting disciples. The Ten Thousand Immortal Alliance rules by non-interference, and whoever embarks on the path of cultivation depends on their own fate. Therefore, there must be a lot of exceptional talents buried in obscurity."

"This may be an opportunity for our Medicine King Sect."

Upon hearing this, San Liu's eyes also lit up. "The Sect Master is right."

"Then, taking care of these disciples will need your help, Mr. Liu. Teach them the methods of cultivation first, and then let them build their foundation..."

While Xu Bai was speaking, he suddenly had a flash of inspiration.

After pondering for a moment, he changed his mind: "When initially informing them of the cultivation method, don't mention the part about 'using the wonders of heaven and earth to build the foundation'."

"Instead, just tell them to 'melt their own bodies to build the foundation'!"

Upon hearing this, both San Liu and Fang Zaiji were slightly stunned.

Both of them were not dull-witted and instantly understood Xu Bai's intention.

"Wonderful, this way, if one of them succeeds in building the foundation, it will definitely be 'melt their own bodies to build the foundation'!" Fang Zaiji exclaimed in admiration.

San Liu, however, had a trace of hesitation on his face. "Sect Master, isn't this a bit..."

Xu Bai smiled faintly, "If they truly cannot build the foundation and have completely given up, there is no harm in telling them the truth then."

Seeing Xu Bai's reasoning, San Liu had to nod in agreement.

After explaining in detail the details of 'melting their own bodies to build the foundation' that he knew, Xu Bai suddenly asked, "By the way, why didn't I see Elder Jiang?"

"I clearly sensed her aura returning."

Fang Zaiji immediately teased, "She got into a fight with someone and ended up losing miserably. She got hurt, so she's currently in seclusion trying to recover."

"Haha, I heard the other party was a woman. If she's prettier than Elder Jiang, it must have been quite an insult. It would be strange if Yushan didn't lose her temper."

"We can finally have some peace without hearing her annoying voice." Fang Zaiji looked extremely pleased.

San Liu's expression changed slightly, "What, such an important matter, why didn't you mention it earlier, Fang? Is Yushan hurt?"

Saying this, San Liu seemed anxious. He put aside the tasks entrusted to him by Xu Bai and quickly flew out to visit Jiang Yushan.

Fang Zaiji shrugged, "I don't understand what San Liu sees in that woman."

"Since Elder Jiang is in seclusion, I'll leave the task of creating the End-Times Disc to you, Mr. Fang," Xu Bai interrupted at this point.

"With your abilities, there shouldn't be any problem, right?" Xu Bai looked at Fang Zaiji with some expectation.

Rather than being angry at being assigned a laborious task, Fang Zaiji was delighted and said, "No problem at all! The End-Times Disc is something almost as valuable as an immortal tool. I've always found Elder Jiang's methods a little rough."

"Sect Master, rest assured, I will treat it very gently."

Fang Zaiji looked expectantly at Xu Bai.

After smiling, Xu Bai tossed the End-Times Disc to Fang Zaiji, entrusting it to him.

Without waiting for further instructions, Fang Zaiji, with the treasure in hand, joyfully left on his own.

At this moment, Xu Bai shifted his gaze to the group of children on the square.

If it were the original body, he would have found their crying very noisy.

But as a clone, Xu Bai didn't feel impatient with the noisy children. Instead, he felt a sense of pity and protection for them.

Quietly realizing the source of his feelings, a faint smile appeared on Xu Bai's face as he gently approached the children.

With a divine-like appearance and an extremely gentle aura, he instantly caught the attention of all the children present. They all quieted down and looked at Xu Bai together.

Xu Bai interacted very kindly with the children.

In just a short while, he already knew all their names and their respective talents in cultivation.

"It's no wonder that during ancient times, being hunted by all major sects could still escape every time. This Fang Zaiji indeed has some skills."

"The talents of these children are almost on par with Zhang Haobo's."

Just like the previous life, the practitioner who first released a long-winded document revealing the true face of the 'Sky Xuan Locking Spirit Array' to the world bore the same name.

That long-winded document, tens of thousands of words long and full of passion, appeared in Xu Bai's mind instantaneously.

Looking at the five-year-old child in front of him, Xu Bai's and another figure slowly overlapped.

"It really is him. Interesting."

As a leader of the Overt Heaven Assembly in the previous life, Gu Xinghan led numerous cultivators in a struggle against the Mysterious Web but was never captured by the Heavenly Alliance.

His capabilities were beyond doubt.

"It seems that this boy might bring me some surprises."

Xu Bai lightly patted Gu Xinghan's shoulder and his smile became more pronounced.

After a while, San Liu returned somewhat dejected.

Obviously, he hadn't even seen Jiang Yushan.

He had to pull himself together and deal seriously with the group of children.

After Xu Bai comforted them earlier, the children behaved much better. When Xu Bai left, they even waved goodbye to him.

San Liu was amazed at how well-behaved they had become.

Instead of returning to Tianling Prefecture, Xu Bai went to the secret chamber in Changsheng Valley.

He was uncertain about a sudden idea that had emerged in his mind and needed time to think it through carefully.

"Should I use this body to perform the ritual and enter the Immortal Descent Realm?"

This was a thought that popped up in his mind while interacting with the children.

Seeing how kind Mr. Bai was to the children, it seemed like he truly was a "good person," just as Xu Ke had said.
