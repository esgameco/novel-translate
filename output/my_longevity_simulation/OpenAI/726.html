Chapter 726

When Li Fansan and the others explored the entire maze but still couldn't find the exit, they had no choice but to return to the black sphere.

"It seems that if we want to get out, we still have to figure it out from this thing." Huang Fu Song's expression was grim.

"Could the exit be within this thing?" Dongfang Yao shivered.

"What exactly is this black sphere? You two have broad knowledge, any clues?" Li Fan stared ahead for a long time before asking.

"I do know some things that cannot be sensed by divine sense, but it clearly doesn't match with this thing!" Dongfang and Huang Fu were at a loss.

After a moment of silence, the black symbol above Li Fan's head gradually weakened, indicating the depletion of energy.

"Staying here is not a solution, we should try to figure out its reality." Taking out a new symbol, Li Fan first flew to the tentacles protruding from the black sphere and lightly touched them.

Like an illusion, Li Fan's hand ended up in emptiness.

"It's as if it doesn't exist. But why can it stick to Puxian Zhenzhou?" The three looked at each other, unable to figure it out.

After hesitating for a moment, Li Fan shielded himself, then flew directly towards the huge black sphere.

Huang Fu and Dongfang hesitated for a moment but followed immediately.

The constantly wriggling black sticky substance gathered together, filling the whole field of vision and creating a tremendous pressure.

Suppressing the uneasiness in their hearts, the three resisted the discomfort and "drilled" into the interior of the black orb.

Although they couldn't touch the black substances with their divine sense and physical bodies, once their bodies were completely enveloped by them, they felt a strange sensation.

It was like being covered all over with cold reptiles, feeling damp and nauseous.

Fortunately, apart from the "feeling" in their hearts, these viscous black substances did not seem to pose any other harm.

The three moved cautiously, like walking in a muddy swamp, slowly advancing towards the central area.

After an unknown amount of time, surrounded by a black ocean, some changes finally appeared.

Ahead, something seemed to be emitting a faint light.

"Quick! The exit should be ahead!" Huang Fu Song's divine sense transmitted excitedly.

Li Fan and Dongfang Yao were just about to respond when the previously calm black sea outside the ball suddenly boiled.

The black sticky substances, which were supposed to be intangible and impossible to touch, began rushing madly towards Huang Fu Song.

And now, inexplicably, these substances found a way to penetrate into him through his orifices.

"Wu wu..." His face showed an expression of extreme horror, and his body gradually began to swell like a balloon.

"Help me!" He couldn't make a sound, but Huang Fu Song transmitted his message through divine sense to his two companions for help.

However, this had the opposite effect, as the infusion of black substances into him became faster.

Huang Fu Song's face showed a ferocious and painful expression.

One artifact after another emerged from him, and he even took out a disintegration disk to set up a formation around them in an instant.

But...

All in vain!

In the blink of an eye, his body had swollen more than ten times.

With a bang!

In the end, to the horror of Li Fan and Dongfang Yao, he burst open with a bang!

There was no splattering of blood and flesh, only a large amount of black sticky substances dispersed around.

According to common sense, cultivators above the Nascent Soul stage, even if their physical bodies are destroyed, as long as their spiritual realm remains intact, they can instantly resurrect.

But at this moment, something terrifying happened that left the two shocked.

After a while, there was still no sign of Huang Fu Song's physical reincarnation.

Instead, they seemed to sense a faint ray of light breaking through the air and flying away, its destination unknown.

"Is he... dead?" Li Fan suddenly froze in shock.

"Huang Fu!" Dongfang Yao's eyes widened with rage, rushing forward frantically to try to find traces of Huang Fu Song.

But all they found in the original spot were Huang Fu Song's personal belongings like the Quantity Heaven Mirror and disintegration disk. Nothing remained, not even a hair.

As they replayed the sudden events in their minds, a guess suddenly struck Li Fan's mind.

Perhaps these black sticky substances are...

Shocked, Li Fan shouted, "Brother Dongfang, remember, don't let your divine sense leave your body! Communication through words is enough!"

Dongfang Yao instantly understood, his face turning pale.

Li Fan flew to Dongfang Yao's side and collected Huang Fu Song's belongings.

Then he said in a low voice, "We shouldn't stay here for long, let's go!"

Grabbing Dongfang Yao, they flew towards the center of the divine sense black sphere.

Outside the black sphere, the aggregation of divine sense was relatively dispersed. So even when they used their own divine sense to probe, it didn't trigger any special reaction.

But inside this black sphere, the divine sense had condensed to a startling degree.

The impulsive transmission of divine sense by Huang Fu Song had immediately stirred these concentrated divine senses, providing them with an outlet.

Before Huang Fu Song could resist, they poured into his sea of consciousness.

Even with the resilience of a Nascent Soul cultivator's soul, it couldn't withstand such a massive influx of divine sense.

He burst on the spot, soul scattered!

As Eastfang Yao felt sorrowful and frightened, he also broke out in a cold sweat.

If he had transmitted his divine sense first just now...

Taking Eastfang Yao with him, Li Fan passed through the most central glowing layer of space and entered a relatively empty inner space.

Although there were no tangible black divine senses here, there were floating transparent faces.

Expressions of joy, anger, sorrow, and fear.

Countless faces had different expressions, but they all lacked eyes.

Like a shoal of fish, they wandered around here constantly.

Dongfang Yao was shocked and tried to avoid these transparent faces floating past.

However, Li Fan casually took one of the faces and absorbed it.

After a moment, a blurry image appeared in his mind.

Sounds of wailing and crying came from all directions.

Under the crimson sky, the shadow of death rapidly engulfed all living creatures.

But like being blindfolded, these scenes could only provide a rough overview.

When the shadows reached before their eyes, everything went dark, and the images in their minds shattered suddenly.

Li Fan carefully pondered for a while, uncertain if his spiritual strength had increased.

But the sudden disappearance of the transparent faces caught the attention of the other existences in the area.

They seemed to become somewhat restless, and their wandering speed increased.

After a moment of contemplation, Li Fan decided not to act rashly.

"Let's go!"

Taking Dongfang Yao, Li Fan flew towards the top of the space, where the light was most dazzling.

They found the exit of the maze.

A moment of haze, and they returned outside the maze.

"Huang Fu Brother!"
