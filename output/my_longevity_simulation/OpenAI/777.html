After a long period of data processing, Li Fan had fully analyzed the entire content of the Wuji Yingxu Method.

After trying to use his supernatural powers, Li Fan pondered whether these could eliminate Mr. Bai's influence on him.

Unfortunately, perhaps due to the limits of his powers or because he had already been tainted and irreversibly influenced by Mr. Bai.

When Li Fan uttered the "True Return," various emotions of reluctance once again swept over him.

As if bursting out at the same time, he quickly closed the True Return interface.

"It seems I was thinking too much."

Having failed multiple times, Li Fan was not discouraged.

Instead, he set it aside for the time being.

He took out the Separation Disc.

"Nine discs vie for supremacy, now only two are left."
