Chapter 886

Li Fan looked at the faceless statue in front of him, his expression changing constantly. Regardless of the method used to test it, the results were the same: this stone statue seemed plain and ordinary, without any hint of extraordinary power.

But even this seemingly ordinary existence could shield spiritual perception and could only be seen with "eyes."

It was clear that the Faceless Mysterious Sky God Statue was definitely not as simple as it appeared.

And...

In this world, Li Fan called it "Merciful True Immortal of the Mortal World," and used it as a relay station for feedback in the meditation techniques. All the members of the mutual aid society who swore in front of the statue all felt an inexplicable sense of "restraint."

Through the side attacks of the alternate identity Ji Shao Li, it was basically confirmed that this was not a singular accidental phenomenon but a universal one.

One must know that the oath designed by Li Fan for membership was simply a piece of text, without any additional mystical power.

"And it's not a psychological effect either."

"In the face of the cultivators' oaths, the Mysterious Sky God Statue really produces a special response."

Li Fan squinted his eyes, his gaze falling on the empty face of the statue.

Placing the statue in the main hall at the entrance of the All Realms Union for countless visitors to admire in the future.

Li Fan was nearly certain that unexpected changes would definitely occur!

"Is the statue developing self-awareness? Or will it attract remnants of the Mysterious Sky Religion?"

Thinking this, Li Fan decisively put down the Mysterious Sky God Statue.

With a bang, the faceless stone statue safely landed on the ground.

Then countless white threads appeared around, which were part of the core of the Spiritual Wood Realm's array, the Disintegration Disc No. 2, protecting the statue rapidly with the array.

"With my current strength, I can easily suppress ordinary Dao masters. And with this array spread throughout the small world..."

"Not entering the small world of eternal life, I am invincible in this Spiritual Wood Realm."

A glint of determination flashed in Li Fan's eyes. "If they don't come, it's fine."

"If they really dare to come here, let them try my methods!"

Li Fan had no doubt that there were still members of the former Mysterious Sky Religion in the Mysterious Yellow Realm.

After all, from what he knew, there was a Mysterious Sky Religion law king slumbering in the remains of the Purple Cloud Sect in Linlang Prefecture.

Certainly, there would be remnants in other places as well.

However, at this point, Li Fan didn't have much fear.

"According to various information I've obtained, except for the Mysterious Sky King at the half-immortal realm, the other twelve law kings of the Mysterious Sky Religion are only fighting forces of the old Dao Longevity."

"..."

Li Fan sneered inwardly. In the next moment, his figure gradually faded and appeared in the space directly under the Spiritual Tree.

This was the core of the entire Spiritual Wood Realm, more important than the Spiritual Wood itself.

A separate space, able to be cut off from the Spiritual Wood Realm at any time for survival.

Outside the space, layers of the Spiritual Wood Array enveloped. The body of the Disintegration Disc No. 2 was placed here.

This was also where Li Fan planned to seclude himself and practice.

"Given enough preparation, even against the old Dao Longevity, I may have a chance at fighting."

Thinking this, Li Fan took out a rainbow light cocooned sphere from his own pocket dimension.

As soon as the cocooned sphere appeared, the Spiritual Wood above seemed to sense some deadly threat.

The branches stirred without wind, and the upright body trembled incessantly.

This attracted the spirits within the Spiritual Wood Realm to look.

Unintentionally witnessing the destructive power of the mysterious gray mold inside the three-eyed skull, Li Fan couldn't help but feel secretly shocked.

"In theory, this three-eyed skull also belongs to me. So when I restart this time, can I choose to keep it?"

"If I can, I should be able to figure out its details."

Thinking this, Li Fan took out an ice-sealed exotic beast he had plundered from the Imperial Treasury.

As if sensing the breath of life, the gray mold residing inside the three-eyed skull suddenly became restless.

When Li Fan removed the rainbow light surrounding the exotic beast, he clearly observed the squirming of the gray mold as if it were alive.

However, for it, the presence of the skull seemed like a shackle.

As the mold was about to spread beyond the skull, it suddenly retracted.

Li Fan carefully observed the changes in the dark golden patterns on the skull's surface during this process.

But to his disappointment, the enigmatic patterns remained unchanged.

"Could my guess be wrong? Is this three-eyed skull really dead?"

Being extremely cautious, Li Fan manipulated the ice-sealed exotic beast to approach the skull.

The gray mold became more irritable.

It floated within the skull, seemingly ready to pounce viciously at any moment.

But as if reluctant, it always stopped at the most critical moment.

Watching this scene, Li Fan pondered.

Under his control, the ice-sealed beast gradually regained vitality.

The breath of life suddenly became strong.

Ultimately, unable to resist the temptation, a large section of the gray mold suddenly disappeared.

There were no traces of movement.

Instead, as if the space had been directly corrupted, a hole similar to a yellow spot appeared.

In an instant, the once vibrant exotic beast completely faded like a desaturated photo, then slowly descended and dissipated.

Within those gray fragments, Li Fan keenly saw a new small piece of gray mold!

Having waited for so long, he naturally did not miss such a good opportunity.

A rainbow light burst out like a galloping horse, instantly sealing and enveloping the newly appeared gray mold layer by layer.

The gray mold, which had just regained its freedom, saw itself about to be sealed again.

Enraged to the extreme, it struck towards the rainbow light as if wanting to erode it.

But how could the keen-eyed Li Fan be deceived by its tricks?

While not neglecting the main body, he released endless radiance to seal off even the tiny gray spot in the nearby space corner.

"Playing these little tricks in front of me is truly laughable."
