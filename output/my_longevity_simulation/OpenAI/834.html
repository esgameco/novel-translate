Chapter 834

A dazzling yellow light enveloped an area of tens of thousands of meters in diameter.

The affected cultivators all reverted back to the state before surrounding Oushangtian.

By the time they realized it, Oushangtian and his son had already disappeared without a trace.

In their haste, they continued to chase but found only Oushangtian remaining in flight.

After resisting for a while, it was ultimately a lucky individual named Xie Xiantao who captured him.

Facing the greedy gazes of the other cultivators, Xie Xiantao made a wise decision promptly.

He handed Oushangtian over to the Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance in exchange for a reward.

In fact, during his escape, Oushangtian had killed no less than a hundred cultivators from the Immortals Alliance.

Although it was in self-defense, it still violated the laws of the Immortals Alliance.

Oushangtian had long been a repeat offender within the Immortals Alliance.

Officially there had been no consequences before, but that didn't mean the matter was not being monitored.

Sure enough, when Xie Xiantao openly declared his choice, in just a moment, a group of black-clad cultivators arrived at the scene and took him and the barely alive Oushangtian away.

The uproar of the grand pursuit event came to a close.

As for another protagonist of the event, Oushangtian's father, Oudaozi, no one cared about him anymore.

Except for Li Fan.

In a dark forest in Luo Yanzhou.

Oudaozi hid under layers of dried leaves, severely wounded.

But the thought of his son sacrificing himself to protect him, exhausting his own abilities and diverting the enemies, tore at him, causing immense pain.

"The Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance..."

Oudaozi's eyes were bloodshot, and the faces of those who had participated in the pursuit flashed in his mind repeatedly.

His heart was filled with resentment.

But at this moment, he dared not move, afraid of being discovered.

He silently rested and recovered from his injuries.

"Sigh..."

Suddenly, a sigh came from somewhere.

Oudaozi was startled, wanting to escape, but it was already too late.

Three familiar figures, who knows when, had already appeared by his side.

"It's you!" Oudaozi glared at Li Fan, the mastermind of all the troubles, his eyes filled with fury.

He was about to explode on the spot and face the end together with Li Fan, but was suppressed by Lin Ling and Tian Yang joining hands.

Already at a disadvantage in terms of strength, now extremely injured and having lost most of his power, it didn't take long for him to be completely restrained.

"Weird, why are you looking at me with those eyes?"

"I just wanted to ask for your help in designing buildings. Before you and your son even met us, you fled in a hurry and even schemed to take my life. If it wasn't for my good fortune, I might have perished right then and there."

"I haven't settled the score with you yet."

"Moreover, all those who pursued and captured your son were members of the Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance. What does that have to do with me?" Li Fan said innocently.

"This is outrageous!" Oudaozi, already furious at Li Fan's nonsensical talk, was at his breaking point.

Veins bulged on his forehead, pulsating non-stop.

Seemingly on the verge of a life-and-death struggle with Li Fan, but he was overwhelmed by Lin Ling and Tian Yang.

He had been weak from the start, and now, severely injured and weakened, lost most of his combat capabilities.

Before long, he was completely under control.

"It's a misunderstanding that has led to my son and me being unable to meet again..."

"There will always be a chance. Friend, look how do you like my Lingmu Realm?" Li Fan changed the subject, pointing to the small world that was being constructed fervently.

"Tsk tsk...," Oudaozi observed for a while, shook his head repeatedly, a hint of disdain in his eyes.

"It doesn't quite match the prestige of the Ten Thousand Realms Union."

Li Fan, rather than being angry, politely said, "Then I'll trouble you, friend, to make some changes."

With a narrowed eye, Oudaozi nodded.

Already deeply immersed in this path, Oudaozi quickly became absorbed in his own plans and visions.

"Friend, please carry on. If you need anything, just tell the leader of the tree people in this realm."

With Li Fan's approval, Oudaozi temporarily forgot about his earlier sorrow.

He threw himself wholeheartedly into the construction of the Lingmu Realm.

"After some setbacks, the main goal has been achieved."

"The Kunqian Bone Hand, the Ten Thousand Death Book..."

Li Fan furrowed his brows slightly.

According to Oudaozi, Oushangtian had not fully mastered the abilities of the Kunqian Bone Hand yet.

During the escape, he seemed to sense that there were still powerful abilities hidden within, waiting to be explored.

If given some more time, he might really be able to achieve some degree of time reversal.

However, this was only Oushangtian's speculation.

The specifics were still uncertain.
