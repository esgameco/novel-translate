Chapter 883

The ocean below churns up towering waves.

The city built on the Nine-Headed Crimson Body continues to tremble. It seems like it could fall from the sky at any moment.

What shocked Xiaoqing and Xiaohong the most was that under this pressure, not to mention the exotic beasts driven by the Imperial Guards.

Even they themselves were overwhelmed with fear, unable to muster any thoughts of rebellion!

"The power gap is too vast. I'm afraid that even releasing that Fusion Beast wouldn't be this person's match!"

"If we forcefully resist, with just a thought, the empire could be easily destroyed."

Xiaoqing and Xiaohong looked at each other, helplessness and unwillingness evident in their eyes.

"We can only flatter and bide our time for now."

They thought so.

"Senior, we are willing to surrender." Xiaoqing bit her silver teeth lightly and bowed.

"Hmph. Don't think I don't know what you are thinking." Seeing this, Li Fan put away his holographic view of the cave.

His gaze swept back and forth between Qing and Hong: "It's better to abandon your little schemes. The Union of Myriad Realms has been observing you for a long time now."

"I am well aware of all your so-called trump cards."

"Even if it's [Imperial One] in person, I am not afraid. Not to mention its poor imitation."

Li Fan's words were arrogant to the extreme, but to Qing and Hong, who had no idea of Li Fan's true strength, they were shocked and raised their heads suddenly.

Li Fan's profound gaze made their hearts turn cold.

The Fusion Beast of the myriad realms is an absolute secret of the empire.

Only the emperors of the past and these two knew about it.

However, this powerful figure from the Union of Myriad Realms, whom they had never heard of, actually knew as well?

Wouldn't that mean they had no secrets in the eyes of the other party?

Even more crucially, they didn't know when the leak happened.

A sense of helplessness and fear surged in their hearts.

Even when they were at the Beast Taming Sect long ago, facing experts of the Longevity Realm, they had never felt such powerlessness.

"Let's go, take me to the Empire's treasury." Seeing that he had probed enough, Li Fan ordered casually.

With no will to resist left, Xiaoqing and Xiaohong had no choice but to obey Li Fan's command.

Soon, Li Fan arrived at the empire's treasury, containing countless treasures and information.

It was no different from what he had seen in the previous life.

After a brief tour, and another check on the condition of the Fusion Beast, Li Fan seemed to hit a dead end.

"Bring out the insights that Xu Ke researched on [Beast Wheel] back then."

Li Fan turned around, staring at Xiaoqing and Xiaohong, and spoke in a deep voice.

At this revelation, the two birds were utterly unable to remain calm.

"Who are you exactly?" Xiaoqing's beautiful eyes were full of shock, and her body couldn't help but tremble slightly.

Knowing the name of their master, Xu Ke, and even knowing in detail about his research on the Beast Wheel, together with the words "Union of Myriad Realms," a possibility couldn't help but surface in Xiaoqing's mind.

"Beast Taming Sect?"

Seeing the inexplicable shock on the other party's face, Li Fan smiled grimly.

"What, are you planning to resist?"

"Well, I shall grant your wish."

"Tsk tsk, Xu Ke, that boy, if he knew of your deaths, he must be feeling terrible." Li Fan sneered.

Xiaoqing and Xiaohong quickly said, "Senior, you've misunderstood..."

"Wait, what do you mean, senior?"

They looked at Li Fan with confusion.

"You guessed right, that boy Xu Ke is still alive." Li Fan snorted.

This news was too shocking, Xiaoqing and Xiaohong were almost unable to control their emotions and almost stumbled.

"Enough, stop dilly-dallying. Deathâ€¦"

"Or submission!"

Seeing Li Fan's impatience, Xiaoqing and Xiaohong felt a pang of fear.

"We..."

After hesitating for a moment, Xiaoqing still handed over a jade pendant engraved with a blue phoenix.

This jade pendant belonged to Xu Ke originally, and she had been carrying it with her all these years.

Handing it over now, her eyes were full of reluctance.

At the same time, the figure of Xu Ke vaguely appeared in her mind.

While she was lost in thought, Li Fan had already read through the contents of the jade pendant.

"Beast Wheel..."

"Indeed worthy of being the treasure of the Beast Taming Sect, it is truly extraordinary."

After the Beast Taming Sect relocated to the small world to seek refuge, they pulled out their elite disciples to research the Beast Wheel.

They attempted to find a way to control the demonic beasts without practicing the true art of Beast Taming.

Whether they succeeded or not, Li Fan didn't know.

But later, the fugitive Xu Ke managed to research the [Harmony Mantra] on his own, using it to control the exotic beasts in the empire's small world.

Li Fan was already well aware of the Harmony Mantra.

What truly interested him was the intricacies of the Beast Wheel.

And Xu Ke, recognized by Mr. Bai, was truly a talent who established the imperial lineage.

The detailed information on the Beast Wheel in the jade pendant exceeded Li Fan's expectations.

"With such detailed information, when the Medicine King Cauldron returns, it might be possible to forge a weakened version of the Beast Wheel."

Though there was still a while before the return of the Medicine King Sect, Li Fan had already made arrangements for their tasks.

Seeing Xiaoqing's dazed expression, Li Fan smiled and tossed the jade pendant back to her.

"I know about your plans to escape from the Profound Yellow Realm..."

"But I can tell you clearly, that's a suicidal path."

"Beyond the Endless Void, the danger is overwhelming. It surpasses the Profound Yellow Realm by far..."

Li Fan sighed softly, then his expression turned serious: "Join us, the Union of Myriad Realms, in counterattacking the world of cultivation, reclaiming the Profound Yellow Realm, that's the only way to survive!"

Upon hearing Li Fan's words, Xiaoqing became even more convinced of her judgment.

This Union of Myriad Realms, as expected, was formed by the ancient sects fleeing to various small worlds.

"This place of yours is a bit weak. But it has some unique aspects."

Li Fan commented briefly, then assigned tasks to them: "For example, the spiritual energy synthesis technique is very useful for many small worlds lacking spiritual energy."

"However, the efficiency is still too low."

"Even if all of you use the power of the whole world to cultivate exotic beasts, focusing solely on slaughtering them, it won't meet the needs of many small worlds."

"So..."

Feeling Li Fan's gaze, Xiaoqing and Xiaohong shivered inwardly.

"At all costs, upgrade the spiritual energy synthesis technique."

"I will give you three years. After three years, I will come to inspect."

"If the results satisfy me, there will be rewards."

"Otherwise..."

"Wasting resources on useless people is better to die early."
