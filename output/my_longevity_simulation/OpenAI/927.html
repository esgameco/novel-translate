Chapter 927

As Li Qing walked, he observed his surroundings.

"Kid, are you having fun in the illusion world?"

At that moment, a voice suddenly caught his attention.

Looking over, Li Qing saw a shabby old man lying on the ground, seemingly abandoned by the world.

Uncertain of the man's intentions, Li Qing cautiously chose not to respond.

"Hehe, no need to be so nervous. Being too restrained is not good!"

"Just act in the illusion world the way you want! We lack someone like you among us!"

The old man praised Li Qing generously.

However, as the old man spoke, Li Qing faintly sensed that unfriendly gazes were constantly fixated on him.

"By the way, let me tell you a little secret."

"In order to have fun, not only are your original memories sealed in the illusion world, even mine is too."

"Only when the illusion is about to shatter, the consciousness of our true selves will awaken."

"At that moment, memories of everyone in the illusion world will suddenly burst forth together!"

A hint of intoxication appeared on the old man's face, as if he had just tasted a vintage wine!

"Two hundred thirty-six thousand eight hundred times…"

"Two hundred thirty-six thousand eight hundred times!"

The old man repeated this number.

"Tsk tsk tsk…"

"The joy you experience doesn't even compare to one-thousandth of mine!"

The old man shook his head. **(Note: the URL provided in the text has been removed for security reasons.)**

Upon hearing the number "two hundred thirty-six thousand eight hundred," Li Qing was briefly stunned before not fully grasping its significance.

However, he quickly understood, and his expression became somewhat strange.

When he looked back, the shabby old man had already disappeared.

"This body seems to have belonged to a senior elder of the Great Dao Sect many years ago."

Li Qing pondered over the memories in the illusion.

Continuing towards the top of the sect's main hall, Li Qing still heard many arguments along the way.

"Why did you castrate him? Isn't a normal start better?"

"Why the opposition now? Weren't you in favor in the beginning?"

"I don't see any problem with a little punishment. The key issue is, why give him the 'Reverse Morality Scripture'?"

"It's a newly researched technique, worth trying out for its effects. Who would've thought this kid was so adept at it? Tsk tsk…"

"Hehe, I think it was intentional on your part."

"In that case, we can't use the 'Reverse Morality Scripture' again."

"No, such a good thing, how can we just ban it like that?"

"I object!"

"I object too!"

…

Though no one was in sight, the voices reverberated around Li Qing as if ghosts, constantly whispering in his ears.

As if countless figures were staring at Li Qing, murmuring secretly.

Though Li Qing remained composed on the surface, he started to feel a growing chill in his heart.

These voices interweaved, much like countless thoughts in his mind.

His mind became divided into numerous fragments.

As Li Qing approached the sect's main hall, the discordant voices became more and more prevalent.

The content of the discussions grew increasingly frantic.

They became akin to the ravings of demons, piercing into Li Qing's mind like needles.

Suddenly, Li Qing halted his steps.

He noticed a slight dampness on his face and gently touched it, only to find fresh blood flowing from his nostrils and eyes.

The surroundings seemed to have become somewhat hazy at some point.

But the demonic voices in his ears not only didn't cease but intensified.

"Cough…"

Li Qing coughed abruptly, even spitting out fragments of his internal organs.

After taking a few more steps, this body ultimately couldn't hold up.

Under the vibration of the myriad cacophonous voices, it turned into a puddle of mud.

However, as the blood and flesh undulated in the void, a new body was beginning to take shape again.

But the omnipresent demonic voices seemed to have no intention of sparing Li Qing at all.

Before the body could fully manifest, it was crushed again.

Thus, on this long staircase, a bizarre scene unfolded.

A lump of shapeless decomposing flesh struggled and rolled upwards laboriously.

After each reappearance following being crushed, the total amount of fleshy substance seemed to decrease.

Although Li Qing never gave up, in the end, only a small, deformed cluster of flesh remained, struggling and rolling clumsily.

Even in such a wretched state, the surrounding voices showed no sign of abating.

On the contrary, they grew more excited. The voices turned sharper, no longer resembling human sounds but rather all sorts of meaningless noise from the heavens and earth.

Just as the last remnant of Li Qing's flesh was about to disappear completely, a black sword shadow suddenly appeared.

Issuing a faint sword hum.

In an instant, it momentarily suppressed the chaotic cacophony of demon voices around!

Seizing this fleeting moment, the black sword shadow burst forth with immense speed. It transformed into a virtual line, piercing directly into the grand hall of the sect.

Although the doors of the hall were open, the interior appeared pitch-black from the outside.

Like an abyss where nothing could be seen.

After that moment of silence, countless voices erupted suddenly.

Like thunderous roars, cries of ghosts, and howls of wolves!

"Hehehe, interesting, interesting!"

"Why did you purposely let him go, why? Why didn't I get to crush him inch by inch into minced meat!"

"Stop pretending. Is deceiving yourself fun?"

…

Contrary to the chaos and horror outside the hall, the Great Dao Sect's main hall was unusually quiet.

In the vast hall, aside from the highest seat of the sect master, there was nothing else.

Senior Sister Zhao sat quietly in the seat.

Sitting upright like a child, hands placed on her thighs.

Her expression frozen, unmoving.

Li Qing sneakily glanced at Senior Sister Zhao.

Her appearance seemed frozen in her teenage years.

Identical to the one seen in the illusion.

"I have met the One-Minded Celestial Master."

Although the atmosphere in the hall felt strange, Li Qing respectfully bowed and saluted.

"What brings you here?"

The melodious voice of Senior Sister Zhao rang out.

Not from the body in the seat but seemingly from all directions within the sect's main hall.

Li Qing didn't hold back and directly said, "I have come for the lost techniques of the Ancient Heaven Sect."

"The 'Heaven and Earth Tribute,' 'Eternal Martial Art,' 'Soul-Cleaving Divine Technique,' 'Heavenly Grief, Earthly Desolation Scripture'…"

"These scriptures were originally possessions of our Ancient Heaven Sect. Today, they should return to their rightful owner."

Li Qing spoke earnestly.

There was a moment of silence in the hall.

Then, Senior Sister Zhao chuckled as if she had heard an extremely amusing joke, laughing softly.

"Aren't all the Ancient Heaven Sect people dead yet?"

"How do you know that I once acquired these techniques?"
