"It's time!"

With a thought, the chaotic word puppets buried all over the Yellow Realm were detonated by Li Fan, one-third of them.

An unknown and terrifying aura burst forth shortly thereafter from various places.

At this moment, all the cultivators in the Yellow Realm felt a sense of impending disaster.

Panic and inexplicable fear, accelerated heartbeats.

But they couldn't explain the reason.

More and more vast divine senses swept through the Yellow Realm.

Attempting to find the source of these disturbances.

The sky also changed its color.

As if something was getting closer to spy.

Now, as the Yellow Realm was influenced by the chaotic word technique, it fell into turmoil for a moment.

Mr. Bai's actions also experienced a brief moment of stiffness.

Testing it, although he could open the interface of "Return to Reality," he still couldn't confirm the truth.

So Li Fan began his next actions.

With a step, he left the secret chamber for cultivation in an instant.

The power of "chaos" instantly enveloped the Valley of Longevity.

The sky was obscured by a hazy force.

Chaos descended.

Like bamboo shoots after the rain, suddenly, hundreds of yellow withered branches sprouted from the ground in the Valley of Longevity.

Their faces, surprisingly, were all identical to Li Fan!

They all grinned eerily at the people present, and then quickly receded back into the ground, disappearing.

Including everyone, even Mr. Bai, all were attracted by this scene.

The remaining two-thirds of the chaotic puppet words were detonated in unison, causing a commotion.

Mr. Bai's body once again stiffened.

A jet-black small cauldron shot into the sky.

It was about to break through the secret realm and fly towards the sky of the Yellow Realm.

No matter how hidden the actions were, with many eyes around, someone still noticed the departure of the Medicine King True Cauldron.

A cry of surprise drew everyone's attention.

But soon, their faces turned even more grim.

Li San's body trembled violently, his color suddenly changing.

With no time to spare, let alone rescue.

Li San's eyes were bloodshot, and even with all his efforts, he only managed to save a dozen or so.

Just as he began to feel somewhat desperate.

Mr. Bai, who had already returned to normal from his stiffness, reached out his hand.

He caught all the fallen cultivators, gently placing them on the ground.

While the influence of the chaotic words had not yet dissipated, Mr. Bai, who had done all of this, strangely twisted his right elbow, as if it had been broken.

Although he quickly returned to normal, it still made him furrow his brows slightly.

"Thank you for saving us, Senior!" After confirming that none of the fellow disciples of the sect were in mortal danger, Li San looked around and then flew forward, expressing his gratitude with clasped hands.

Mr. Bai did not speak, only looking at the Medicine King True Cauldron, which had disappeared into the sky, and the hundreds of puppets that had already fled to distant lands, as well as the body of Li Fan lying lifeless on the ground, having lost his life force.
