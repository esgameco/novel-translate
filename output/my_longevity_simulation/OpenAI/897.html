Chapter 897

Countless cultivators inquired about it, but he ignored them all.

After all, this matter was too shocking. If it were to spread, the cultivation world on the brink of despair would seem like the end times in an instant.

Alone, he hid away, pondering over the gains and contemplating the meaning of the words from the faceless true immortal.

After a flurry of commotion, the Xuánhuáng Realm gradually returned to its calm state.

With the collapse and disappearance of the ascension passages, the impact of the catastrophe on the mortal realm seemed to gradually dissipate.

After about a year passed, the king of the realm suddenly realized that there was a slight deviation in the token position of the Xuánhuáng Realm.

The change was minimal.

If he hadn't been paying close attention and experiencing a sudden surge in strength due to the baptism of celestial energy, he might have overlooked it.

But how could the prepared king ignore this detail?

Three more years passed.

By monitoring the changes in the token values, the king of the realm finally confirmed that the entire Xuánhuáng Realm was deviating towards a direction in the boundless void.

What shocked the king even more was that it wasn't just the Xuánhuáng Realm.

All the other cultivation worlds he could observe and connect to were the same!

The conclusion was obvious.

Something existed in the boundless void, drawing all the cultivation worlds towards it!

Thinking back to the words of the faceless true immortal.

"Quickly escape, if you delay, it will be too late."

The king of the realm instantly felt a chill down his spine.

At that moment, the king might have had the immediate thought of escaping alone.

As the cultivator with the highest talent in the Xuánhuáng Realm at the time, the king of the realm could not bear to watch his homeland slowly march towards destruction.

Therefore, the king ultimately made a noble decision.

That was to escape with the Xuánhuáng Realm.

Even though the king of the realm already had the body of a demigod at that time, pushing the entire world was just wishful thinking.

Not to mention overcoming that terrifying suction force…

But the king of the realm did not give up.

After studying the scriptures scraped from the immortals and integrating his own knowledge, the scholar king deduced a grand and extraordinary formation.

He named it the "Floating Crossing Starry Sky Formation."

According to the king's calculations, relying solely on the resources of the Xuánhuáng Realm was simply not enough to resist the unknown suction force in the boundless void.

Even if the Xuánhuáng Realm were emptied, it would not be able to escape that terrifying and unimaginable force.

So, one of the functions of the Floating Crossing Starry Sky Formation was to lock onto the caves and small worlds in the surrounding void, capture them, and initiate interrealm warfare.

While plundering resources and replenishing one's own strength, it also formed a chain-like effect, reducing the speed at which the Xuánhuáng Realm was being attracted.

It was indeed killing two birds with one stone.

However, building such a grand formation with his own strength alone was beyond his capabilities.

It was necessary to gather and coordinate the vast majority of the Xuánhuáng Realm's forces to complete the construction of the Floating Crossing Starry Sky Formation.

As for revealing the true situation and rallying everyone together…

The king of the realm had also considered this.

After inspecting the actual conduct of the Xuánhuáng Realm cultivators, he decisively gave up.

If at that time, those cultivators knew about the crisis facing the Xuánhuáng Realm, the vast majority of them would choose to escape immediately.

Choosing to stay and rescue the Xuánhuáng Realm with the king of the realm would be an extremely rare occurrence.

Building the Floating Crossing Starry Sky Formation required the strength of these cultivators.

Therefore, the king of the realm decided to first unify the Xuánhuáng Realm and better coordinate the forces.

He was a once-in-a-thousand-year genius, and now the only body of a demigod in the Xuánhuáng Realm.

With the many immortal techniques inherited from the ascension passages, achieving this was not very difficult.

And everything went as he anticipated.

After persuading the twelve top cultivators with astonishing talents to assist him, the king of the realm eradicated all resistance within a few years.

Led by him, the twelve Immortal Kings, they established the overpowering Xuántiān Sect in the Xuánhuáng Realm.

The Floating Crossing Starry Sky Formation was constructed with unprecedented efficiency.

And it successfully captured and conquered other worlds.

The speed of the token deviation gradually slowed down.

Everything seemed to be developing in a favorable direction.

The king of the realm seemed to have seen the bright future of the Xuánhuáng Realm escaping calamity.

However, the arrival of an unexpected guest completely disrupted his plans.

The moment the purple aura appeared in the Xuánhuáng Realm, the king of the realm already faintly sensed the opponent's background.

An incomplete demigod body that had not been completely cleansed, a fragmented and incomplete aura of immortality.

It should be like him, a cultivator who had escaped with his life from the collapse of the ascension passages.

Naturally, the king of the realm was vigilant towards this equally formidable opponent.

However, the opponent claimed to be just passing through. They would stay for a short while and leave soon.

If two demigods were to fight, it would undoubtedly cause irreparable trauma to the Xuánhuáng Realm.

After much consideration, the king of the realm chose not to act.

He had never expected that this decision would sow the seeds of future disaster.

When the Floating Crossing Starry Sky Formation captured another new world and the king of the realm personally went out to battle during the gap.

The existence calling himself the Grand Mage of Heaven seduced many local cultivators from the Xuánhuáng Realm into his sect.

And passed on numerous techniques, secrets, and mystical powers.

Upon returning victorious, the king of the realm immediately understood the opponent's intentions.

He wanted to shake the rule of the Xuántiān Sect by using those cultivators.

To incite a rebellion, then reap the benefits when both sides were weakened.

And the ultimate goal was the aura of immortality on the king of the realm.

How could the king of the realm comply with the opponent's wishes?

So, he rushed into the Heavenly City and directly engaged in a battle with the Grand Mage of Heaven.

Although he suffered minor injuries, the opponent's injuries were far more severe than his. From then on, the opponent behaved.

However, the opponent refused to leave, and the king of the realm had no good solution.

Fearing the opponent would act out of desperation, the king of the realm simply ignored him.

In this battle, he felt he had gained something.

By extracting some of the aura of immortality from the opponent, he might be able to directly reshape his demigod body in the mortal realm.

At that time, infusing the power of immortality, he could significantly reduce the resources needed to activate the Floating Crossing Starry Sky Formation.

The Xuánhuáng Realm could then quickly escape the crisis.

But the king of the realm underestimated the despicableness of some cultivators.

Or underestimated the Grand Mage of Heaven's ability to deceive.

When those cultivators who had entered the Heavenly City united at a crucial moment when everything seemed to be heading in the best direction.

They launched a surprise attack on the Xuántiān Sect.

From then on, the history of the Xuánhuáng Realm took a completely different path.

...

Thinking back to the past, the young man's expression was absent-minded.

He looked up, as if he could see the terrifying presence that attracted and devoured everything.
