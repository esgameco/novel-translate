Chapter 746

When Li Chenfeng returned to the Empire, the deity had already passed through the surveillance of the formless killing mechanism and knew the entrance to the Empire.

However, he hadn't had a chance to go there before, so he took advantage of this opportunity to make a trip.

In his sea of consciousness, the rhombus-shaped crystal once again emitted colorful light, enveloping the Tianxuan Treasure Mirror below.

Controlled by Xu Bai, virtual images appeared one after another in the vibrant light.

Distorting the perception of the Tianxuan Treasure Mirror.

Then swiftly flew to the entrance location.

After a careful search, he indeed discovered a hidden spatial passage concealed by some special means.

Xu Bai looked at the Tianxuan Treasure Mirror, which did not issue any alerts.

"Indeed useful."

Xu Bai nodded to himself, not hurrying to enter.

After scanning with his divine sense and making sure there were no cultivators nearby, he took out a terminal disk and laid down a formation to cover the entrance.

Then he entered the spatial passage.

In sight was an endless darkness.

This scene was not unfamiliar to him; it was extremely similar to the void scenes opened up by countless Riftwhales when he crossed the Immortal Array.

However, this place seemed even more profound.

After a moment of contemplation, the Clear Light Lustrous Robe crafted by Fang Zai emerged on his body. With an extra layer of protection, Xu Bai then flew forward.

He had traversed the Immortal Array countless times. He was already very familiar with it.

Without any surprises along the way, he smoothly passed through the grand array, but did not see any trace of the small world.

What greeted him was still the boundless dark void.

"It seems that I have already passed the Riftwhales and deviated from the original location," Xu Bai pondered.

Standing in the void, Xu Bai closed his eyes and focused on sensing.

Just a moment later, he faintly sensed a very familiar aura fluctuation in the distance in the darkness.

Following the aura induction, Xu Bai flew rapidly.

As he was about to approach, a loud roaring sound of a whale suddenly rang out.

Xu Bai abruptly stopped his figure.

As if triggering some kind of chain reaction, one after another whale cries echoed near and far, without stopping all the way to the far distance.

It was impossible to distinguish how many Riftwhales existed in the dark void. They echoed each other, resonating back and forth as if playing a grand musical composition.

Surrounded by these countless Riftwhales, Xu Bai did not feel the slightest sense of danger.

Because he could hear that these Riftwhales were just resonating to express their joy, as if welcoming Xu Bai's arrival.

"Interesting." Xu Bai's eyes flickered, and he continued to fly forward.

As the group of Riftwhales sensed his aura on the way, they voluntarily opened up a path for him.

Not long after, Xu Bai had already arrived outside the Empire.

Looking from the dark void, it was an oval-shaped world. One could vaguely see large water bodies and...

The body of the 'Scarlet Nine-Headed' floating in the air.

While Xu Bai was observing intently, a strange fluctuation suddenly occurred in the space ahead.

Seven cultivators wearing beast-god armor, riding on an odd beast resembling an eel, suddenly appeared in front of him.

"Who are you!"

These cultivators coordinated extremely well. As soon as they noticed Xu Bai, their expressions changed slightly. They quickly spread out, forming a formation to surround Xu Bai in the middle.

They were all wearing beast-god armor at the golden core realm. How could they be Xu Bai's opponents?

Xu Bai smiled slightly, waved the folding fan in his hand, and before they could react, he subdued all seven of them in an instant.

"Oooh..."

They showed a look of horror but could not speak.

What happened next made their faces change dramatically.

Xu Bai beckoned, and the 'Void Wanderer' dragon, which they had worked hard to subdue using the 'Heart Unity Technique,' moved very obediently towards the other party.

It even seemed to be ingratiating itself, actively sticking its head out and rubbing against the others.

This groveling appearance almost made the seven people think they were hallucinating! When did the ruthless 'Void Wanderer' become so docile?

Feeling the creature's affectionate intent, Xu Bai placed his hand on its head and gently stroked it.

Thus, Xu Bai also learned its name.

"It does have some dominance. However, possessing the ability to shuttle through space at short distances is quite remarkable even in the Profound Yellow Realm."

Xu Bai nodded slightly.

"Lead the way." He commanded.

The Void Wanderer let out a cheerful cry, swayed its body, and took the initiative to support Xu Bai.

Xu Bai did not forget to grab the seven people who had been subdued on the side.

After a wave of fluctuations in space, in the next moment, Xu Bai arrived in the midst of the Empire.

A human kingdom built on the body of the Scarlet Nine-Head.

Stepping on the Void Wanderer, with seven beast-god warriors still in tow.

Even his appearance was extraordinary.

As soon as Xu Bai appeared, he almost captured the attention of everyone nearby.

After a brief trance, the surrounding area immediately fell into chaos.

"Enemy attack!"

"It's a cultivator!"

"Where is the guard team!"

"Quick, go get the 'Saint Qing Master'!"

...

Amidst the successive exclamations, hundreds of figures swooped over from various parts of the city.

Some were draped in beast-god armor, some were simply seated on various odd creatures.

Some even flew with their physical bodies. Judging from their aura, they were clearly cultivators similar to Xu Bai.

Although their numbers were numerous, the strongest were only at the Nascent Soul realm.

With Xu Bai protected by the Clear Light Lustrous Robe, he remained calm and fearless.

"Who are you, sir?"

Sensing that Xu Bai was different, they were very cautious and did not choose to attack directly.

Xu Bai did not answer, but just scanned around.

"What is your purpose in coming here?"

A cultivator from the Empire Guard team asked loudly.

At this moment, a green light flew out from the central part of the Empire's capital and arrived in front of Xu Bai.

"Friend..."

Xiao Qing stood still, the words barely out of her mouth.

Upon seeing Xu Bai's appearance clearly, she was thunderstruck and froze on the spot.

"Master..."

Before the word "person" could come out, Xiao Qing felt that something was off.

Suppressing the shock in her heart, she stared at Xu Bai for a long time before asking, "You are..."

Her tone seemed to contain a thick sense of doubt.

"Friend Xiao, do you know that your Empire is about to face great calamity?" Xu Bai smiled and asked.

"Bold bandit! How dare you speak arrogantly!"

"How dare you act so insolently in front of Saint Qing Master!"

As soon as this was said, it instantly caused everyone to be furious, and they began to curse loudly.

However, in Xiao's eyes, there was already a great surprise, "Do you know me?"

Xu Bai nodded slightly and asked, "Do you still remember the Multiverse Alliance?"

(End of this chapter)
