Chapter 961

Outside the cave residence, Li Fan faintly felt the eerie atmosphere emanating from within.

There were also many whispers that seemed to be circling around his ears.

It was like the rumored lost souls.

But Li Fan was very clear that there were no ghost cultivators in the Xuanhuang realm.

After a cultivator fell, in the vast majority of cases, they would return directly to the heavens.

Only in very few cases could some remnants of the soul be preserved.

It was already difficult to retain complete memories from their lifetime, let alone cultivate with a remnant soul.

Of course, the strange beings in the Yunsui Tiangong were exceptions.

They were beyond the usual scope of discussion.

Li Fan snorted, and the whispers in his ears disappeared instantly.

He walked boldly into the Luo Shuzhen's cave residence, only to see human skeletons scattered all over the place, arranged in various poses, giving the impression that they were still alive.

A dwarf cultivator whose height only reached Li Fan's knees was solemnly conversing with one of the skulls, "Third brother, rest assured, I know you are not satisfied with the new wife I found for you."

"It's okay, I have already found someone else for you. Also, persuade fourth brother, it's already embarrassing not finding a partner in life, do you want to be forever lonely even after death?"

Chattering away, with a serious expression.

As if the skeleton in front of him was still alive.

Li Fan watched expressionlessly, cleared his throat, and awakened the absorbed dwarf cultivator.

"Hey? Who are you?" The dwarf cultivator tilted his head, apparently trying hard to see Li Fan's face.

But by the time the dwarf cultivator tried to discern Li Fan's appearance, he had already changed his appearance and veiled himself.

The dwarf cultivator couldn't see clearly at all.

"You are the cultivator who can reconstruct the appearance of the deceased based on the skull?" Li Fan explained his purpose.

The dwarf cultivator then came to his senses and nodded repeatedly, "Yes, that's me."

He rubbed his hands, "I've been waiting for you for half a day. You said finishing the restoration task would reward at least ten thousand contribution points?"

Li Fan smiled slightly, nodding, "Rest assured, you will not be shortchanged."

"But, are you really confident?" Li Fan lowered his head and scrutinized the other party, emphasizing his tone and asking again.

Under Li Fan's gaze, the dwarf cultivator couldn't help but shiver involuntarily.

But soon he patted his chest loudly, guaranteeing, "I, Luo Shuzhen, have been dealing with the dead since I was a child. In my eyes, they are not just a bunch of skeletons! They are all living people! If I can't do it, perhaps no one else in this world can!"

Only then did Li Fan say slowly, "Then let's begin."

He set up a formation around them, sealing off Luo Shuzhen's cave residence.

Then Li Fan projected the skull shape at the center of the Grand Museum.

Initially, Luo Shuzhen seemed distracted, still occasionally glancing at the skeletons placed in his cave residence.

However, as the skull gradually took shape, Luo Shuzhen's attention was completely drawn. The appearance of the skull left him completely dazed, drifting closer to the projection involuntarily, looking dazed.

"It's so perfect..."

"How can there be such a perfect skull structure in the world?" Luo Shuzhen muttered to himself.

"Perfect?" Li Fan furrowed his brows slightly.

Thinking Li Fan was questioning, Luo Shuzhen immediately explained excitedly, "Isn't this perfection?"

"Look at this frontal bone, this occipital bone, and this sphenoid bone..."

"It's unparalleled. The proportions are just right."

"Absolutely... absolutely..."

After pausing for a while, Luo Shuzhen finally uttered eight words, "Perfect to the point of being flawless."

Li Fan narrowed his eyes slowly.

Before Li Fan could prompt him, Luo Shuzhen eagerly began to reconstruct the appearance of the skull's previous life.

His method was also very peculiar, not through any secret technique.

But by nervously attempting to converse with the skull.

Although it was just a projection, there was naturally no response from Luo Shuzhen.

But Luo Shuzhen seemed to have received a response, growing more and more excited.

Just as Li Fan was beginning to doubt, he saw Luo Shuzhen waving his right hand in the air, starting to depict the skull's appearance from its previous life.

So Li Fan patiently waited.

When the final portrait appeared in front of Li Fan...

He struggled to control his facial expression, trying to conceal his astonishment.

Gazing at the image in the air for a long time.

Li Fan asked in a low voice, "Are you... sure you're not mistaken?"

Even though he knew it was a redundant question, Li Fan still asked.

Sure enough, Luo Shuzhen was extremely excited, "Mistaken? You can question my..."

Before the other finished speaking, Li Fan took a deep breath, waved his sleeves, and suppressed Luo Shuzhen along with everything in his cave residence into the Wuxing Cave Dayan, erasing all traces of their presence.

"How could it be him?"

Standing in the air for a long time, Li Fan remained puzzled.

Unable to face this reality.

"The first cultivator infected by the Immortal Mortal Pestilence. The first cultivator infected by the Immortal Mortal Pestilence..."

Li Fan contemplated for a long while, then soared towards the Yunzun Sea, his target being Yinshan, who was also conducting research on the Immortal Mortal Pestilence.

In Li Fan's sensing, Yinshan's research had not yet reached its final conclusion.

Yinshan seemed to have sensed something, becoming somewhat hesitant and indecisive.

The frequency of experiments had noticeably decreased.

Even...

He seemed reluctant to carry out the final verification.

With his speed at its peak, Li Fan soon arrived in front of Yinshan.

Yinshan was taken aback by Li Fan's sudden appearance.

As he was about to speak, Li Fan raised his hand to stop him.

"Friend Yin, regarding the first cultivator infected with the Immortal Mortal Pestilence..."

"I have made a startling discovery."

"This result is too unbelievable. It's incomprehensible. Perhaps you have a premonition as well?"

After a moment of silence, Yinshan nodded with bitterness.

Li Fan took a deep breath and said in a deep voice, "Well, then, please start the final verification."

"I also ask you to confirm it for me."

Standing still, after a long while, Yinshan sighed and led Li Fan to the laboratory.

Through the transparent outer wall, Li Fan could vaguely see the white rib Yinshan possessed displayed inside.

As the dense white pestilence continued to spew out, filling the entire area, Yinshan kept reciting incantations, his expression changing.

Under his control, the mist gradually thinned.

Slowly, it seemed as if something was flowing into the white rib.

Once the transparent area was completely emptied, Yinshan confirmed his speculations, his face pale as he fell to the ground.

"It's just as I suspected, just as I suspected."

He murmured softly.

Li Fan then slowly uttered his response.
