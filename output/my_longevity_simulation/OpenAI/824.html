Chapter 824

Just by ensuring control over the life and death authority of the spirit tree through the Heart of the Spirit Wood, he finally relaxed slightly.

The pressure of the tree spirit continued to rise.

Yuan Infant, Divine Transformation...

Until it reached a level comparable to Harmonization, then it gradually stopped.

"The spirit of the giant tree is equivalent to the Tao of the Spirit Wood Realm."

"If the Tao indeed has a spirit, the Heavenly Cavern and Yuan Infant realms are equivalent. The miniature world varies based on its own scale, ranging from Divine Transformation to Harmonization."

"Originally, with the foundation of the Spirit Wood Realm, it was almost impossible for a tree spirit to be born with the power of Harmonization. However, due to the continuous influx of miraculous scenes by Shangtian, it has somewhat achieved a certain upgrade."

"Similar to Mr. Bai previously creating a miniature Golden Huang Realm out of thin air. However, Shangtian accomplished this through pure replication."

As Li Fan pondered, the green light spots converging from all directions had stopped gathering.

A body almost identical to a human's appeared in front of him.

Graceful curves, like the elder of the tree people, externally showing female characteristics.

Li Fan glanced at her, his thoughts moved slightly, and through the Heart of the Spirit Wood, he dressed her in clothes.

The tree spirit did not resist.

Instead, she curiously inspected the clothes that suddenly appeared on her body.

Although her eyes seemed innocent, they were certainly not ignorant.

Inheriting all the memories of the spirit tree, she was born with innate intelligence, with the only thing lacking compared to ordinary cultivators being experiences of human interaction. (From a 69 book)

"Although her strength surpasses mine, due to me having long since assimilated the Heart of the Spirit Wood, she still must be under my control."

With this in mind, Li Fan made the tree spirit kneel before him at his mere thought.

Kneeling to a Golden Dan at the Harmonization stage, regardless of the situation, was a great insult.

However, the tree spirit did not show any signs of anger.

"Master," she naturally addressed Li Fan in this way.

Creatures of the heavens and earth have simple nature, without the cunning of humans.

Especially when just born, they would not hide their emotions.

After confirming that the tree spirit's submission was her true intention, Li Fan nodded silently.

"Since you have taken human form, you should have your own name from now on."

"From now on, you shall be called Lin Ling."

Saying this, Li Fan pointed a finger at Lin Ling's forehead.

After a moment, a red mark appeared on her forehead.

"Thank you, Master." Lin Ling, now with her own name, was very happy, thanking Li Fan repeatedly.

"She still lacks the concept of power hierarchy, so even though she calls me, whose strength is weaker, Master, there is no sense of dissatisfaction or rebellion."

"In the future, as she grows and experiences more, it may not be the same." (From 69 book)

"However, breaking free from the constraints of the Heart of the Spirit Wood is not an easy task."

Li Fan carefully pondered the subtle connection between himself and Lin Ling, lost in thought.

Over the next period, Lin Ling showcased her strength as a being born from the Tao within the Spirit Wood Realm to Li Fan.

Capable of freely manipulating all the powers in the miniature world and always in the most efficient form.

With her help, the "transportation" process by Shangtian suddenly became much easier.

And Lin Ling did not feel like it was a tedious task; she played happily instead.

Her innocent and carefree expression deeply attracted the young man Shangtian.

As a being born from the Tao, Lin Ling's beauty naturally needs no explanation.

Perfect facial features with no flaws, her smiles exuded a unique charm.

Shangtian couldn't take his eyes off her, reluctant to look away.

"After... Senior," after Lin Ling finished her work and Li Fan let her play elsewhere, Shangtian found Li Fan, stuttering with a flushed face.

"Hehe, very good." Li Fan patted Shangtian's shoulder firmly, revealing a meaningful smile.

He didn't promise anything but made Shangtian instantly feel as if he had been energized.

"Senior, the miraculous scenes stored in my Heavenly Views Gourd have been mostly replicated. So, I want to go record other things in the Golden Huang Realm."

"Good, try to convince some others in Shanhe Xuan to come along," he volunteered.

Seeing his sense of initiative, Li Fan had no reason to refuse.

However, for safety reasons, he also gave Shangtian a jade pendant as a medium to perform the "Killing without Killing" technique.

Only then did he let him leave.

Li Fan also slowly left the Spirit Wood Realm.

To his surprise, Lin Ling insisted on following him outside.

"You can leave here?" Li Fan couldn't help but be astonished.

"Master, don't worry, it's fine," Lin Ling reassured with a firm nod.

"However, it may not be as free outside as it is at home," she added.

As a free Helper at the Harmonization stage, Li Fan naturally agreed.

When the two arrived in the Golden Huang Realm, Li Fan immediately observed her changes.

"After all, there has been some influence, and in terms of actual combat power, she is somewhat inferior to a true Harmonization being," he silently nodded.

"However, she is still stronger than an average Divine Transformation." Li Fan concluded.

With Lin Ling in tow, they flew towards the direction of Jiushan Prefecture.

After flying for a while, Lin Ling suddenly asked, "Master, why don't we travel underground? Flying in the sky is so slow."

Li Fan was slightly taken aback.

"Underground? What do you mean?"

Lin Ling widened her eyes, looking at Li Fan. She opened her mouth, trying to explain, but couldn't find the right words for a while.

After a while, she suddenly tapped her head and transmitted a scene to Li Fan's mind.

Almost instantly, Li Fan understood what Lin Ling meant.

"Power of the earth veins?"

"You can shuttle through the Earth Vein's power?" he couldn't help but ask.

"Master, can't you?" Lin Ling was surprised.

Seeing Li Fan fall silent, Lin Ling pondered for a moment, then comforted him, "It's okay, Master."

"I seem to be able to take you with me."

Feeling it out, Lin Ling seemed to be telling the truth.

Li Fan hesitated for a moment and then nodded slowly, "Let's give it a try."

"Alright, Master! Hold on to me tightly!" Lin Ling, excited at Li Fan's agreement, displayed great enthusiasm.

A green light shield appeared around them, and they plunged from the sky into the underground.

Li Fan's vision went dark at first.

Then, it felt as if he was falling into a rushing river, surrounded by surging yellow energy.

The immense pressure even made Li Fan feel stifled.

But Lin Ling seemed to be as comfortable as a fish in water, not only able to move freely but also seemingly able to draw from the endless Earth Vein's power to strengthen herself.

Yelling along the way, they moved through the Earth Vein, flowing swiftly.
