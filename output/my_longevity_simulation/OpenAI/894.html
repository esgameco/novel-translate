Chapter 894

A look of fear and shock froze on his face.

Looking at the yellow withered branch held by the other party, a hint of a mocking smile couldn't help but appear on the young man's mouth.

"The ancient tree of Dìmài, as well as this mysterious rainbow brilliance..."

"But to think a Golden Core cultivator could possess such treasures."

"When did such an impressive organization appear under our Wanxian Alliance?"

"Let me take a good look at it."

Under the gaze of the young man, Jì Shào Lí's soul was slowly pulled out of his body.

In that instant, his body also aged a thousand times over.

In just a moment, it turned into a skeleton and collapsed on the ground.

What the young man was displaying at this moment was evidently an extremely malicious soul-searching technique!

Jì Shào Lí's soul, without any resistance, fell into the hands of the young man.

Instantly, as the soul-searching technique operated, a large amount of information surged into the young man's mind.

The young man's body trembled slightly, a trace of horror flashing in his eyes.

...

"The Mysterious Heaven Sect?"

At that moment, the soul-searching technique seemed to trigger a prohibition within the other party's soul.

Before the young man could fully read all the information contained in the soul, it disappeared like a withered flower.

The young man was unfazed by this scene.

If the contents seen during the soul-searching were true, the existence of such prohibitions in the soul was reasonable.

"I didn't expect that after thousands of years, the Mysterious Heaven Sect is still lingering?"

The young man's eyebrows furrowed slightly.

From the search results just now, it seemed that Jì Shào Lí, a cultivator, had been recruited by a mysterious Mysterious Heaven Sect Law King.

Cultivating influence secretly in the Clouds Sea and various places under the Wanxian Alliance, attempting to make a comeback.

"In what era are we, and they still haven't given up. Truly ignorant."

The young man snorted coldly, but there was no trace of negligence in his heart.

"The remnants of the Purple Cloud Sect? Actually hiding in such a place..."

The young man's eyes revealed a murderous intent, and his figure slowly disappeared.

...

At the same time.

Lǐ Fán, who was not far from the remnants of the Purple Cloud Sect, felt the disappearance of his clone.

His figure paused slightly.

A few images of the ink painting appeared in his mind, making him thoughtful: "The Palm Heaven Bureau?"

"To think such an organization still exists."

"But thinking about it, it's normal."

"Before, my idea of cultivating Joint Dao cultivators at will once I knew about the existence of the essence of Heaven and Earth was a bit naive."

"The Wanxian Alliance has existed for thousands of years, and if they didn't have this level of control, they would have been overthrown long ago."

Suppressing his thoughts for the time being, Lǐ Fán looked at the remnants of the Purple Cloud Sect not far away.

"Now that the fish has taken the bait, I'll just sit back and watch the show."

With narrowed eyes, he scanned with his divine sense.

Soon, based on the information he had learned from his past life and a faint, almost imperceptible strand of vitality.

Lǐ Fán located the dormant Mysterious Heaven Sect Law King in a sealed chamber deep underground!

Seeing his face, it was indeed exactly the same as the one Lǐ Fán had once possessed, Lǐ Chén of the Purple Cloud Sect!

Swinging a wave of Silence-Joy Sword Energy, heading straight underground.

Carrying a powerful aura full of destruction, as if to engulf everything in the chamber.

Just as the black light was about to touch the chamber, golden threads suddenly appeared in the void.

The chamber disintegrated, but the protective barrier formed by the rings of golden threads consistently kept Lǐ Chén in the center.

Seeing this, Lǐ Fán continued to relentlessly unleash several waves of destruction sword energy.

The golden threads were struck by this tremendous force and began to collapse inward.

Seemingly on the verge of breaking apart.

Lǐ Fán smiled, his figure flickered, and he disappeared without a trace.

After a moment, when Lǐ Chén awoke, he stood still in place for a while.

Having slept for too long, it would take some time to rearrange the memories in his mind.

However, before he could fully grasp the current situation.

Lǐ Chén's expression changed slightly.

Because he suddenly sensed that the surrounding space seemed to have been suddenly sealed off.

Thinking of the recent attack he seemed to have suffered, Lǐ Chén instantly understood that his hiding place had somehow been discovered.

However, being the Law King of the Mysterious Heaven Sect, he had escaped from the pursuit of the ten immortal sects in the past with ease.

Though surprised, he acted calmly and first took out a bottle from his storage ring.

He took a sip of the rainbow-colored liqueur inside it.

The aura on his body, which had grown somewhat weak due to long dormancy, instantly solidified.

Just as Lǐ Chén was regaining his strength.

A door in the sky opened, and a group of dozens of black-robed cultivators walked out in unison.

Their expressions were solemn, and the killing intent around them seemed almost tangible.

And leading them was the young cultivator who had recently probed Jì Shào Lí's soul in the Clouds Sea.

His eyes narrowed as he scanned the ruins below.

"Three chimes have rung, it is indeed the Law King of the Mysterious Heaven Sect!"

Feeling somewhat excited, the young man waved his hand, and the black-robed cultivators instantly flew to various corners in the sky, each holding a small mirror.

After taking their positions, infinite green lights radiated from many small mirrors.

In an instant, the light swept through the space here.

Under the cyan light, the underground space became as if transparent.

Consequently, Lǐ Chén, who was hiding within, appeared in front of the Wanxian Alliance group.

Surrounded by everyone, Lǐ Chén's expression remained unfazed.

He just stared at the small mirrors held by the black-robed cultivators, a tinge of reminiscence and resentment flashing in his eyes.

With a thought, the black-robed cultivators around him, at his command, raised the small mirrors high.

Countless cyan beams were aimed at the Law King of the Mysterious Heaven Sect beneath them.

The cyan light seemed like solid shackles, binding Lǐ Chén.

Lǐ Chén's face showed a mocking expression. "Using something from our Mysterious Heaven Sect to capture the Law King of the Mysterious Heaven Sect?"

"Are today's cultivators stupid enough to stoop this low?"

A crisp bell sound seemed to ring with Lǐ Chén's words, and the blue light that shone from the small mirrors began to shake violently.

Then, as if losing control, it erupted fiercely.

The cyan light instantly took over the entire remnants of the Purple Cloud Sect.

Under the dazzling light, Lǐ Chén's figure disappeared instantly in front of all eyes!
