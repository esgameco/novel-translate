Chapter 997

Xuansushang appeared in front of Shuofeng, with a serious expression.

However, at the very first moment, Luxi-chan tried to contact the outside world but found it impossible to connect.

"The insignificant ones, kneel in the presence of the Law Transmission Celestial Being!"

Four voices thundered in their ears.

In the dim sky, it seemed as if the Law Transmission Celestial Being had really appeared.

Staring down without anger.

Boom!

A huge invisible pressure suddenly descended, and the bamboo building instantly turned to dust.

The entire ground trembled and sank several feet in a roaring sound.

Xuansushang and the other two were momentarily locked in place by this pressure, unable to move!

Before they could even struggle, they saw the sky, shrouded in shadows, reveal five bloody moons.

"Draw threads!"

A cold voice without any emotion sounded from the sky.

Thousands of threads descended from the blood moon.

Like countless tentacles, they silently and swiftly flew towards them, entwining them.

The threads seemed invisible, but the moment they touched their bodies, a sudden sense of weakness arose from the depths of their hearts.

"Xichan!" Xuansushang and Shuofeng both had armor suddenly appearing on their bodies.

But it couldn't stop the invisible threads.

Luxi-chan's situation was even worse. Around her, silver symbols representing formations appeared one after another, only to quickly disappear without a trace.

Sweat beaded on Luxi-chan's forehead, her eyes first filled with shock and then became hard to conceal her fear.

The chaotic battle may have been incomprehensible to others who were not well-versed in formations, but Luxi-chan clearly understood. Every time she set up a formation, a mysterious force would instantly break it when it was formed!

Even before the formation took shape, it was tragically broken!

As if the other party had known in advance what formations she wanted to set up, the speed at which the formations were broken was simply unthinkable!

At first, Luxi-chan thought there was a traitor in her Formation Hall leaking all the formations she had mastered.

But soon, as she brought out the formations she had kept in reserve, she faced the same outcome: the formations were instantly broken!

As a result, Luxi-chan's fear gradually spread uncontrollably.

She felt as though she had returned to the first time she saw her Master.

The powerful formations displayed by the Master had amazed, intoxicated, and fascinated her. Her once proud talent was simply not worth mentioning in front of him.

And now, the presence of formations breaking around her was even surpassing that of her Master!

It was a realm she could not even imagine!

"Who could it be?"

As the proud Chief of the Formation Hall of the Ten Thousand Immortal Alliance, Luxi-chan could feel herself shattered, seeing countless faces of her Master in the shadows around her.

"Xichan, you really disappoint me!"

"From now on, don't tell anyone you're my disciple, I can't afford to lose face because of you!"

"Luxi-chan, I won't rest in peace!"

...

"No, Master...” Trembling with fear after the hallucination, Luxi-chan completely lost her resistance for a while.

Thick shadows completely enveloped her, and she disappeared into the bamboo forest.

Seeing this scene, Xuansushang couldn't help but pale. But facing the Law Transmission Celestial Being above and the encirclement of numerous threads, she was already at her wits’ end.

"Fellow Xingwang, I have temporarily sealed Luxi-chan. However, just now..." Mo Rubin's voice came with some doubt.

"Hehe, I just happened to break through her mental weakness. Who would have thought that the renowned Chief of the Formation Hall in the world is actually a coward who fears the Master so much..." Li Fan responded with a sinister smirk.

"I see." As the battle was not yet over, Mo Rubin did not dwell on this matter.

"My remaining power is rapidly fading. I'm afraid it's not enough to seal Xuansushang and Shuofeng..."

"No matter, they are already doomed."

While the two briefly communicated, the offensive remained deadly.

The three members of Tianyang enforced the formation, and even with the protective armor, Xuansushang was as vulnerable as a drifting leaf under the combined spiritual pressure of the four Unification Realm experts.

The crimson light protected her mind, but the gaze of the Law Transmission Celestial Being in the sky kept pressing down.

At this moment, Xuansushang also realized that the illusion above her head was not the Law Transmission Celestial Being itself.

It was just the best representation to intimidate her mentally in the first place.

After all, assisting the Tianxuan Mirror to divide human avatars had violated the system established by the Law Transmission Celestial Being.

It was impossible for Xuansushang not to feel fear.

Although not the actual Law Transmission Celestial Being, the pressure it brought to Xuansushang remained unaltered.

And the constant eerie laughter from the shadows around her made Xuansushang surrounded by hallucinations, making it difficult to resist.

Since the battle started, the gray threads entwining her had been increasing.

Her thoughts and movements became slow as a result.

"Who are you, really?"

Before losing consciousness, Xuansushang's mind was flooded with countless unresolved thoughts.

The two Unification Realm experts were already subdued, and the subsequent battle was without suspense.

Shuofeng's transformation into a Divine Being was already out of reach.

Under the spiritual coercion of the four Unification Realm experts, she had long passed out.

The remaining cultivators in the bamboo sea cave were all sealed in the Heavenly Realm, and Li Fan watched the two Unification Realm female cultivators who were now unconscious.

Both had unparalleled beauty, Luxi-chan with an elegant temperament and Xuansushang resembling a young girl, still retaining a hint of innocence.

"Fellow Xingwang, my power is dissipating, so I leave this to you for now."

The boundless shadows that enveloped the bamboo sea converged into a figure that eventually turned into a phantom.

He looked at Li Fan and said slowly, "If you have any news about the Tianxuan Mirror Avatar, contact me as soon as possible."

"Occupying a body has been completely destroyed, remember to bring it along next time we meet."

"Or in the hidden Heavenly Realm from last time."

The voice gradually dissipated with the shadow.

"This battle went so smoothly, Mo Rubin's shadow technique was indispensable."

"Even though they are not adversaries, I felt pressured. Even in a moment of confusion, I saw the appearance of the Heavenly Physician and the Immortal of Xianxu in the darkness..."

"Tianxuan Dao... truly extraordinary."

With a passing thought, Li Fan's attention once again focused on Xuansushang and the others.

"Senior, should we evacuate now?" Baihuadaoren asked cautiously.

After pondering for a moment, Li Fan shook his head, "You all go to the bamboo sea here and guard the entrance."

"I will interrogate about the Tianxuan Mirror Avatar on the spot."

The three left to carry out their orders.

Li Fan then took out two "Real Bait Fruits."

"Only these two are left, it seems it's time to request more from the Five Elders' Guild."

Narrowing his eyes, Li Fan began weaving intricate lies slowly.
