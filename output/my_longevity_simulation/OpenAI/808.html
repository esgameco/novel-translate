Chapter 808

The draft box has stored a halfway written side story for a long time. While the inspiration is still there, I quickly wrote it out.

Don't delay the normal update at night. There are still two normal chapters tonight.

"Ding dong!"

I was suddenly startled by the sound of the phone notification, waking me up from my sleep.

After half a minute of recovery, my pounding heart finally calmed down slowly. I picked up my phone from the table and checked the time.

2:30 in the morning.

"Damn it, sending messages at this time, it must be that old guy Zhang Yaobin!"

Cursing in my mind, I opened the messaging app and sure enough.

"Li Yang, check your email. Write a research report based on the information in it, around 30,000 words. Before noon tomorrow."

Accompanied by Zhang Yaobin's sly smile in the profile picture, instantly made my eyelids twitch and blood pressure rise.

In my mind, I had already greeted his ancestors for numerous generations, but my fingers involuntarily started tapping on the phone keyboard.

"Okay, boss!"

I sighed.

"No choice, I got stuck with such a supervisor. Hang in there, once this paper gets successfully published, I can graduate."

"Six reviewers, four of them are Zhang Yaobin's friends. Whether the paper gets approved or not, it's all up to him. Before the review period is over, he will definitely treat me as a workhorse. Really pushing it this time."

Complaining in my mind, I opened the email, downloaded the attachment, unzipped it, and started looking at its contents.

"Inverting the principles of heaven and earth, proving eternal life..."

"What on earth is this?"

My drowsiness disappeared instantly, and I couldn't help but straighten my posture, staring closely at the image on the computer.

A strange event occurred in the Baixi Boundary area of Xiangnan Province half a month ago. A mountain peak of a kilometer high split open revealing a smooth as a mirror cliff. On the cliff, nine large characters were inscribed from top to bottom in ancient seal script: "Inverting the principles of heaven and earth, proving eternal life."

The local government acted promptly. The site was quickly sealed off after the incident. This matter is not yet known to the public.

Because the ancient seal script that wrote these nine characters dated back 7,000 years to the Xian Dynasty period, the investigation team invited Chief Expert Zhang Yaobin, who studies Xian Dynasty history domestically, to assist in the investigation.

To see if they could find some clues in ancient books.

...

I checked the time in the lower right corner of the screen.

"It's not April Fool's Day today, why play this joke on me."

As soon as I saw this information, my initial reaction was disbelief.

"Lingyun Peak, over 1,300 meters. The first peak in the Baixi Boundary area..."

I quickly searched for the mountain mentioned in the event.

"Nine characters, over a thousand meters. From the spacing of the characters in the picture, each character is at least a hundred meters in size."

"Even now, it would be a major project to engrave them on the mountain cliff."

"A 7,000-year-old Xian Dynasty? How is that possible?"

"Although these characters are really well-written."

My thoughts stalled for a moment.

However, the old guy Zhang Yaobin, ugly as he may be with his capitalist aura, was very reliable when it came to work. He wouldn't be messing around with fictional things just to tease me.

I quickly searched for related news about the Baixi Boundary area online.

Sure enough, I found something suspicious.

"Family, I'm just speechless. Finally took off for the year, wanted to tour Baixi Boundary. Arrived at the entrance of the scenic area, but suddenly couldn't get in! Who understands this feeling!"

"Emergency at Baixi Boundary, the scenic area is temporarily closed. For self-safety considerations, please cooperate, dear tourists!"

"I might not believe it when I say it, but what did I really see in Baixi Boundary..."

Before I could click on it, this content had already been deleted.

"Looks like there's something fishy going on."

"The Xian Dynasty..."

Rubbing my chin, memories about it started to resurface in my mind.

The ancient Xian Dynasty was the first dynasty with documented records.

And there was a huge cultural gap between it and the second dynasty, Yu, spanning almost eight hundred years. To this day, no one knows what happened during those lost eight hundred years. How the flourishing ancient Xian Dynasty met its end still remains a mystery.

Although my research direction was not the Xian Dynasty, due to the scarcity of the remaining relics from that era, they had all been thoroughly researched. I had no choice but to change directions.

However, bowing to the old thief Zhang Yaobin, though he looked a bit ugly and had a strong capitalist aura, he was very reliable when it came to work. Perhaps I might have to endure being ordered around like a cow and horse until this paper gets successfully published.

But what caught my attention was the inscription of these nine characters at Lingyun Peak.

Having a cup of coffee, staying up late and finishing the material the old thief sent me, it was already nearly dawn.

But my doubt grew even stronger.

The "Inverting the principles of heaven and earth, proving eternal life" inscription in those nine big characters, were still engraved in my mind.

The second I woke up the next day, the first thing I did was check my phone.

"Li Yang, don't forget, by tomorrow noon."

The old thief had sent another message.

"I..."

Suppressing the urge to curse, I checked the time.

"I only slept for four hours..."

"Why does it feel like I've slept for three days and nights?"

I moved my sore body a bit and quickly fixed myself some breakfast.

Then I started writing the research report.

"There are still 28,000 words left, and 27 hours remaining. With my speed, it should be easy to manage!"
