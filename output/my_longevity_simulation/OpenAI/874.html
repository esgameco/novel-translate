Chapter 874

Despite the threefold restrictions, the faint sense of crisis that had always lingered in the heart gradually receded.

"…"

"It seems that it's not only the Xuanhuang realm that has encountered calamities."

"After the Divine Path was cut off, great disasters became widespread."

Easily sealing away this dangerous seal, Li Fan placed it within his own cave world to suppress it.

Li Fan then turned to Tianyang and asked, "How do you feel now?"

Tianyang flew over to Li Fan, sensing for a moment, then nodded, "That ant-like sensation has indeed weakened. However, it seems like it hasn't completely disappeared."

Li Fan then bent down and touched the soil below with his hand.

The burning sting still came through, but his palm turned charred black in a moment.

Before the black spots spread further, Li Fan skillfully cut off his palm.

It fell to the ground, turning into ashes.

Even though the main culprit had been suppressed by Li Fan, the bizarre situation in the Yin-Yang Realm remained unchanged.

"This is…"

"The cornerstone of the world's laws has been completely altered?"

After pondering for a moment, Li Fan felt a chill.

"If the Immortal Mortal Miasma is specifically aimed at cultivators, then this gray mold seems to be meant to infect the world."

"The current state of the Yin-Yang Realm seems to be a corpse of the world."

"What kind of person would be so insane to develop such a thing deliberately?"

A tinge of interest flashed in Li Fan's eyes.

"In the 115th year, from the time at which the Immortal Alliance Exploration Team arrived, only a little over twenty years have passed. What could have happened for such ghostly soul bodies to be born in this dead place?"

"Well, no, that's not right."

Li Fan suddenly froze.

The conclusion drawn by the investigation team before was that the nourishment of countless soul bodies in the Yin-Yang Realm created the vast and immense group of Boundary-Crossing Whales in the Outer Realms.

But now, with no soul bodies appearing, where did those numerous Boundary-Crossing Whales they had seen before come from?

Clarifying this issue was not difficult.

Li Fan and Tianyang stayed in the Yin-Yang Realm for another three months.

Remaining hidden and patiently waiting.

It was all to determine whether the Boundary-Crossing Whales had a habit of "hunting" in this Yin-Yang Realm.

Their efforts paid off.

One day, just when Li Fan was getting a bit impatient, a few distant cries suddenly sounded.

Around ten Boundary-Crossing Whales arrived in the sky above the Yin-Yang Realm.

Compared to regular Boundary-Crossing Whales, they were generally larger in size.

Seemingly aware of the danger here, the Boundary-Crossing Whales did not act as recklessly in the Outer Realms, instead cautiously following some kind of pattern as they floated in the air. Heading towards a certain location in the Yin-Yang Realm.

Li Fan and Tianyang quietly followed.

At the edge of the Yin-Yang Realm, where the two had previously explored and found nothing unusual, the Boundary-Crossing Whales stopped.

With one experienced-looking Boundary-Crossing Whale leading the way, they aimed directly at a certain spot on the ground and rammed into it head-on.

The leading Boundary-Crossing Whale disappeared suddenly, and the others followed suit.

However, the area they could pass through seemed not particularly wide, and three out of the ten directly collided with the soil due to misjudgment of their positions.

There was no loud collision sound.

Only black spots quickly engulfed them, turning them into fragments of soil scattered on the ground.

Li Fan hesitated as he reached the spot where the Boundary-Crossing Whales vanished.

"Senior brother, let me go first," Tianyang volunteered.

Li Fan nodded, and Tianyang flew in without hesitation.

Instead of rushing in, Li Fan patiently waited outside the entrance.

Soon after, Tianyang returned.

Even his originally taciturn face showed a rare look of astonishment.

Through the images transmitted by Tianyang, Li Fan finally understood what was hidden inside.

Numerous soul bodies floated on the yellow river, old and young.

They were dressed in different clothing, but all had their eyes tightly shut, seemingly asleep.

Having soaked in the yellow river for who knows how long, these soul bodies had undergone eerie changes.

Their lower halves were no longer human-like, lacking legs and instead sprouting numerous roots that deeply embedded into the yellow river.

It seemed that this was a way to avoid being washed away by the river currents.

The Boundary-Crossing Whales that had sneaked in here acted as if they had entered a place of indulgence.

Excited, they feasted heartily.

With a single inhale, they swallowed thousands of soul bodies.

After consuming these soul bodies soaked in the yellow river, the transformation in the appearance of the Boundary-Crossing Whales was striking.

They almost instantly expanded, and it seemed like something was gestating within their bellies.

In the yellow river, countless soul bodies floated densely.

All seven Boundary-Crossing Whales were satiated, and the swallowed soul bodies were just a tiny fraction of the vast number.

Once they left satisfied, Li Fan used his colorful divine light to shield himself and entered the yellow river.

Even though he had seen numerous floating frozen corpses in the reversal of life and death array before.

Facing the tens of millions of human-like soul bodies floating in the river, Li Fan still felt somewhat horrified.

Especially the "roots" of these soul bodies' lower halves, some layered and entangled together.

Resulting in a large group of human faces, all closed-eyed, squeezed together at a place.

Like a round ball made up of numerous frightening faces.

"Senior brother, this place…"

Tianyang's transmission was also somewhat uneasy.

Li Fan swam through the yellow river, brushing past numerous soul bodies. Under the protection of the colorful divine light, apart from a little disgust, he felt no discomfort reaching a remarkable site.

This yellow river was much larger than the entire Yin-Yang Realm.

After searching it thoroughly, Li Fan vaguely understood the reason behind the strange occurrences in this place.

"The Heavenly Dao of the Yin-Yang Realm has mutated due to the erosion of the gray mold."

"Fire as cold as ice, life as dead ashes."

"However, life seems to emerge here. It's just because this place was originally the 'death' location of the Yin-Yang Realm."

Li Fan observed that as the yellow river flowed around, some inexplicable factors were washed together, gradually forming human-shaped molds.

As the water continued to rush, the soul bodies became clearer and clearer.

Forming complete and distinct human forms.

"If the normal world's Great Dao were in operation, this newly born soul might have been reincarnated into the mortal realm."

"But the Yin-Yang Realm itself is already dead, so the cycle of life and death no longer exists."

"For many years, these newly born souls have been continuously produced and accumulated here."
