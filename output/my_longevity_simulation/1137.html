"Safely returned."

"Proving my method is feasible."

Li Fan breathed a sigh of relief, ignoring the prompt about returning to reality for now.

The influence of the faceless True Immortal's power had indeed declined to a certain extent after returning to reality once again.

Li Fan's face was no longer a smooth blank slate.

Instead, the contours of some facial features slightly protruded.

However, this appearance looked even more eerie compared to being purely faceless.

But Li Fan didn't mind.

"Next, I just need to follow the same method."

He flew towards Daxuan Capital with practiced ease.

Next, the events that occurred in Daxuan and the other few small worlds were almost exactly the same as in the previous life, that is, the 120th world.

The only possible difference was that Li Fan was more skilled and omitted many probing steps.

To be on the safe side, Li Fan did not go off track, and even stopped researching the use of the Youzu people's power of faith.

Minimizing all variables.

In the 121st world, the progress of charging for returning to reality was a bit faster.

Completing the charge three months in advance.

Hiding in the dark sea, inheriting cultivation, returning to reality.

All in one go.

Next, the 122nd world, 123rd world...

All the way to the 125th world.

At the beginning of the 8th year, Li Fan once again came to the dark sea.

He had a premonition that after returning to reality again this time, he would completely escape the influence of the faceless True Immortal.

Now his face was no different from a normal person's.

It's just that he still sensed some defects that the naked eye and divine sense could not discern.

The influence of the True Immortal Net still existed.

But it would shatter with a single touch.

Li Fan suppressed the urge to court death in his heart.

"Return to reality!"

The scene instantly changed between illusion and reality.

The mist gathered and dispersed in an instant.

126th world, anchored for 1 year, detached from the mountain!

"Phew!"

A feeling completely different from the previous few worlds arose in Li Fan's mind.

It was the true freedom of being unrestrained.
