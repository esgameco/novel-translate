Chapter 314

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

As Zhang Zhiliang said, after studying for a long time in the Huanyu Villa, Li Fan already understood roughly seventy to eighty percent of how to construct the Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation.

However, as for how powerful this constructed Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation actually was, Li Fan wasn't completely clear and lacked a quantified understanding.

It's just like how workers on a weapons production line may not necessarily be very clear about the lethality of what they are manufacturing.

Initially, Li Fan also thought that the main purpose of constructing this Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation was to mark the Five Elders Society cultivators who infiltrated the Wanxian Alliance's territory.

But after Zhang Zhiliang's reminder, Li Fan realized that the real purpose may be far more than this.

The Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation is indeed outstanding in distinguishing between enemy and ally cultivators.

But if it was only for this purpose, it would be a bit of overkill.

It must be known that according to the current construction plan, a Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation will be built in every state of the Wanxian Alliance.

The amount of materials required is incalculable.

Even if the number of Five Elders Society spies doubled, the total losses they caused may not be worth more than these materials.

Not to mention those remote places in the Wanxian Alliance that are hardly worth infiltrating.

Yet without exception, Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formations are to be built for saturated prevention.

Sweeping out and preventing spies is one aspect, but more importantly, it's to achieve a high degree of control over one's own territory.

Under the coverage of the Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation, as long as a Five Elders Society cultivator enters, they will be marked.

This actually means that everything within the formation is under constant surveillance.

The first time he saw the Tianxuan Mirror, Li Fan had done a test.

After learning the news that the liuli fish on Liuli Island had gone extinct, he first bought a batch of liuli pearls at the original price, and only later sold this news to the Tianxuan Mirror.

Afterwards, the price of liuli pearls skyrocketed, and he made a small fortune from it.

He also confirmed that the Tianxuan Mirror is not an omniscient existence. It cannot know everything that happens after leaving Wanxian Island.

With the "Real Truth" on him, in order to ensure his own secrets were not noticed, Li Fan had deliberately avoided the Tianxuan Mirror's detection many times.

But once the Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation is established... the Tianxuan Mirror will make up for this shortcoming.

I'm afraid all of Li Fan's little tricks will not escape its prying eyes.

Thinking that his every move in the future will be under the constant surveillance of the Tianxuan Mirror, Li Fan couldn't help but feel a chill in his heart.

He maliciously speculated whether the Wanxian Alliance would establish a special institution.

Once a cultivator's behavior is found to be a bit abnormal, the institution's members would be dispatched to deal with it.

In this way, I'm afraid all unstable factors will be nipped in the bud.

"It seems I need to reconsider whether to rely on Spirit Mist Grass for profit in the next life."

"Although I can get rich overnight, the impact and changes brought by a war are too great. Especially once this Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation is completed, I'm afraid my every step will be difficult and it will be hard to keep the secrets on me."

Thinking of this, for the first time in this life, Li Fan finally felt like he had lifted a rock only to drop it on his own feet.

"No wonder the Wanxian Alliance's determination to construct the Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation this time is so strong. In order to ensure the number of formation laying personnel, even an eliminated person like me was pulled back..."

"But the popularization of the Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation in this life is already an unstoppable trend. In the future, I can only act more cautiously and never expose my abnormality."
