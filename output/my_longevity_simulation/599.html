Chapter 599

天絕 trembled all over.

"Yes, I killed them all!"

"Qin Tang, little bug, Fu Yun…"

"I killed them all!"

Even Confucius found it difficult to accept this answer for a moment.

"Why…" he asked in a daze.

At that moment, Tian Jue suddenly changed his expression.

"No…"

"It's not true!"

"It wasn't me!"

Tian Jue raised his head abruptly and stared fiercely at Tian Yi: "It's all your doing!"

"All the memories in my mind, they're all fake!"

"Ever since the day I met you, things have become twisted."

"Everything is out of control..."

Tian Kill Sword pointed directly at Tian Yi: "Was it you after all!"

Facing Tian Jue's question, Tian Yi's face remained unchanged.

"I thought this time, you guys would make some progress."

"Turns out, it's still the same."

He shook his head somewhat regretfully.

"What do you mean by that?" Tian Yu sensed a different meaning in Tian Yi's words and asked suddenly.

Tian Yi did not answer, but continued, saying to himself: "I've explained it to you many times. Sometimes, even what you see with your own eyes might not be true."

As he spoke, he inexplicably took out a set of nine silver needles from somewhere and placed them in the air one by one.

"They went crazy, trying to kill me."

"I had no choice but to deal with the problem in my own way."

Tian Yu watched as Tian Yi took out the nine silver needles, his expression suddenly darkening.

Without listening to Tian Yi's explanation anymore, he immediately took action.

"No breaking of laws!"

"Bind!"

As if something was entwining Tian Yi's body, his movements in manipulating the silver needles stuttered slightly.

Weak! Hurt! Stagnant! Decayed! Damaged!...

Several different words continuously came out of Tian Yu's mouth.

With each word, Tian Yi's movements slowed down a bit more.

With eyes turning red, the old fisherman who had been watching Tian Yi's actions closely at this moment also grabbed the Tian Kill Sword.

His body turned into a black aura, merging with the Tian Kill Sword.

Like a sharp arrow, it aimed straight at Tian Yi.

And Tian Yi, controlled by Tian Yu, seemed to have no strength to resist, unable to make a move.

In an instant, the black sword aura passed straight through his chest.

The black aura surged angrily, engulfing Tian Yi like a tide, devouring him.

In a blink of an eye, Tian Yi, who had a calm expression, disappeared within the Cloud Water Temple.

"Tian Yi is dead?"

Hidden in a corner, watching the battle between the three, Zhou Qing'ang was suddenly stunned.

The battle between Tian Yi, Tian Yu, and Tian Jue did not produce any aftermath.

So, the clone watching secretly was temporarily safe.

But the scene in front of him was obviously beyond his expectations.

He had thought that Tian Yi, with his strong abilities, could not have fallen so easily under the joint attack of Tian Yu and Tian Jue...

"With his strength, it's absolutely impossible to be so helpless, right?"

"Wait a moment..."

In an instant, Li Fan seemed to realize something.

He looked up and gazed at the sky.

Slowly, two dark clouds appeared in the sky.

As he recognized what they were, Li Fan's breath caught in his throat.

Those were two giant hands, crossing through the sky, coming from the outside and landing inside the Cloud Water Temple.

Confucius and the fisherman both looked up in astonishment at the two hands above.

Wanting to resist, they found themselves already firmly restrained.

A horrified expression crept onto their faces.

"It's okay, this surgery will soon be over."

A thunderous sound came from the sky, the voice of Tian Yi!

Li Fan's heart thumped violently, almost stopping his thoughts.

Yes, from start to finish, the confrontation between the fisherman, Confucius, and Tian Yi was just his image.

And now, these two hands were the true form of Tian Yi!

Vaguely sensing Tian Jue's strength, and Confucius.

At this moment, in front of these two giant hands, they were like bugs on an operating table, utterly powerless to resist.

They could only let the hands catch them and hold them in the air.

Then Tian Yi's two giant hands beckoned the nine silver needles taken out by the clone earlier and began to "treat" both of them.

Looking at this scene from afar, Li Fan shivered uncontrollably.

"Tian Yi, what is he..."

At this moment, a single thought completely occupied Li Fan's mind.

Crisis! Great danger!

Tian Yi's true strength far exceeded his estimates.

Even if he was not in the realm of immortals, he was not far off.

He immediately abandoned this clone, not wanting Tian Yi to follow the clues and find his main body!

Quickly!

However, he soon awoke.

No!

Tian Yi had obviously discovered that someone was scheming behind his back.

But his first thought would probably be the group of people with strength similar to his own, not to search among the ants watching the battle beside him.

This was a fixed way of thinking.

But if at this crucial moment, a random ant inexplicably died...

It would be too obvious!

Under Tian Yi's terrifying power, not only would all leads not be destroyed, there was a high possibility that it would attract Tian Yi's attention!

What to do?

In an instant, many thoughts flooded Li Fan's mind.

But soon, he made a decision.

Wait and see!

He wanted to gamble on whether Tian Yi still retained a "human" way of thinking.

Or if he had elevated himself and could no longer be reasoned with.

If he guessed right, then even though he was defeated miserably, at least it wouldn't be entirely without gain.

If he guessed wrong...

Following the signal from the clone, it would take some time to reach the main body.

And at this moment, his main body was not in the Cloud Water Sea, but deep within the Wuxian League.

He believed that even if Tian Yi discovered some clues and wanted to find him, it wouldn't happen in an instant.

Enough for him, Li Fan, to escape!

This was based on his experience from the last encounter with Tian Yi.

Li Fan bet!

With immense fear in his heart, Li Fan resisted the urge to kill the clone directly.

Eyes widened, all other thoughts cleared from his mind, he watched Tian Yi's surgery intently.

Even with Li Fan's current vision, he still couldn't make sense of it.

Just silver lights flying around, the angry shouts of Tian Yu and Tian Jue becoming weaker and weaker.

Finally, they seemed to fall into a deep sleep, barely audible.

After the surgery was completed, the fisherman reverted to his original forgetful appearance.

After scanning around, his face was filled with confusion.

Ignoring the two hands in the sky, he simply walked out of the Cloud Water Temple.

As for Confucius, he loaded his cart again and disappeared amidst the tranquil sound of reading.

Having done all this, the two giant hands put away the silver needles.

Gently pressing down.

The Cloud Water Temple, which had been filled with screams moments ago, fell quiet.
