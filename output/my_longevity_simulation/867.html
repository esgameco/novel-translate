"I'm afraid that Zhong Shentong, a small Foundation Establishment cultivator, was able to take possession of the Seven-Colored Jade Void Flowing Light treasure without it being snatched away by others. It must be because he handed over the Fishing Pond and obtained its protection."

Li Fan felt a little emotional in his heart.

Originally, he shouldn't have overlooked this point.

But first he was delighted to obtain the treasure, and then he successfully broke through. This caused his state of mind to become a bit complacent.

So he neglected this strange point.

"However, it's not a bad thing either. At the very least, I know that this Seven-Colored Divine Light cannot be used lightly. And it also drew out this elder."

Li Fan looked at the stone tablet scholar behind him without revealing anything.

In the surging earth veins, the scholar walked like an ordinary person, neither fast nor slow.

And the ancient earth vein trees that would normally attack cultivators who broke into them, did not show any abnormal movements at this moment.

It was very calm.

Even when Lin Ling called out, they refused to respond at all.

Li Fan silently cursed and urged Lin Ling to continue hurrying along the way.

At the same time, in Jiushan State.

At the previous meeting place.

Not long after Li Fan and the scholar left together.

A cultivator dressed as a member of the Five Elders Association arrived belatedly.

After waiting for a long time without seeing anyone appear.

He couldn't help but be confused.

...

Yongyun State.

In a small mountain temple, outside the Fishing Pond.

"Senior, please look." Li Fan respectfully pointed to the small pond in front and said.

Because Jishao Li had informed him in advance before coming, Zhong Shentong already knew that someone would be coming.

He looked at the stone tablet scholar and Li Fan, not daring to breathe.

He protected Liuli behind him, firmly pressing down on her head as she wanted to stick it out and see what was going on.

The stone tablet scholar observed with great interest for a long time.

"It's just an ordinary small pond..."

"Could there be some profound mystery?"

The scholar became interested.

Li Fan signaled with his eyes for Zhong Shentong to step forward and demonstrate.

Seeing this, Li Fan had no choice but to personally take action.

He sat by the pond, solemn and dignified.

He cast out his fishing rod.

The next moment, a strange light erupted in the stone tablet scholar's eyes.

When Li Fan retracted the rod and returned, allowing him to examine the white glowing object in his hands, only then did he realize.

"So that's how it is."

He couldn't wait to step forward, wanting to also give it a try.

Li Fan was about to speak up to remind him of the correct way to use the Fishing Pond.

But he saw the stone tablet scholar ponder for a moment, and then his figure blurred as he directly transformed his appearance!

The owner of that appearance was also very familiar to Li Fan.

It was none other than the crazy fisherman!

It's just that the fish basket on his back was replaced with a heavy blue stone tablet!

"Oh right, I forgot to tell you. This scholar is not my original appearance."

"But rather one I imitated from that member of the Five Elders Association."

The "fisherman" smiled, humming something, and cast out his rod.

Looking at his expression and posture, there was almost no difference from the real fisherman.

Li Fan's heart sank.

Previously, he had inevitably looked down a bit on this Longevity Realm expert due to their encounters.

After all, the Creation Stone Tablet's monitoring function only worked in certain areas.

And this person was unable to discern his background, and was overjoyed upon seeing the treasure Fishing Pond like an ordinary cultivator.

Imperceptibly, this lowered his "aura" quite a bit in Li Fan's mind.

But now it seems...

This person is also hiding his depths very well.

Although he did not attain the Dao through Heaven-Defying Principles, he definitely cannot be underestimated.

Zhong Shentong was completely frozen like a revered statue, with his facial expression and body stiffened.

Among those present, only Lin Ling and Liuli, the two brave ones, were secretly staring at the Stone Plate Fisherman.

The fisherman's first fishing process was exceptionally long.

And he seemed to be deeply immersed in this fishing process.

Even after most of the day had passed, he still sat motionless. This made Li Fan suspect that he might have already failed but was still pretending.

However, with a dazzling golden light appearing once again in the mountain temple.

Li Fan's doubts quickly vanished.

Looking at the golden light sphere in the Stone Plate Fisherman's hand, Li Fan couldn't help but feel a bit skeptical about life.

Zhong Shentong was also dumbfounded, not daring to believe his own eyes.

The Stone Plate Fisherman stared at the light sphere for a while: "The Heavenly Emperor's Qi Canon?"

"Achieving the status of an emperor in the world, gathering the qi fortune of all living beings and dynasties. Without cultivation, one can become as powerful as a long-lived realm expert..."

"Always thinking about fighting and killing, how boring."

The Stone Plate Fisherman was bored: "You can have it."

With that said, he casually threw the golden light sphere towards Li Fan and the others.

Although Zhong Shentong also wanted to reach out and catch it, under Li Fan's threatening gaze, he didn't dare to be presumptuous.

After catching the golden light sphere, Li Fan quickly examined it.

The contents inside were consistent with what the Stone Plate Fisherman had said.

It was a unique cultivation method circulating in a world without spiritual energy.

"It's indeed ingeniously unique."

"Unfortunately, to condense the qi fortune to the scale described in the canon, it would require the joint efforts of hundreds of years and more than ten generations of people."

"A chicken rib."

Li Fan commented with some regret, but still solemnly stored it in his storage ring.

Looking at the Stone Plate Fisherman, seeing that he was still in the posture of fishing, Li Fan couldn't help but say: "Senior, this fishing pool only has one opportunity per day."

The Stone Plate Fisherman yawned: "I know."

Li Fan was stunned.

The Stone Plate Fisherman seemed to truly enjoy the process of fishing.

And he didn't care whether he could fish up a treasure light sphere.

Compared to Zhong Shentong and Li Fan, he was like a true fisherman.

"From now on, I'll stay here."

"You don't need to be nervous, just pretend I don't exist."

"The things I fish up, I don't want them. They're all yours."

The Stone Plate Fisherman suddenly spoke.

Li Fan had already anticipated this in his heart, but even if the other party said so, how could he possibly ignore such a long-lived realm expert?

"Senior, could it be that you want to find something useful from this fishing pool?"

The Stone Plate Fisherman didn't seem to have any malicious intent.

Li Fan mustered up the courage to ask.

The Stone Plate Fisherman's answer was also very straightforward: "Yes. In the Xuanhuang Realm, there's nothing that can remove the stone plate from my back."

"Perhaps it might exist in this fishing pool."

This answer caught Li Fan off guard, and he fell into silence.

As for Zhong Shentong and Liuli, they were full of curiosity as they looked at the ancient stone plate on his back.

"Too tiring. Too painful."

The Stone Plate Fisherman sighed while fishing.

"The things happening in the Xuanhuang Realm, every moment."

"Even if you don't want to know, they still uncontrollably appear in your mind."

"You think it's a good thing?"

The Stone Plate Fisherman shook his head repeatedly.

"In the beginning, I tried to directly split open my own head."

"But the stone plate is indestructible, so I can't die."
