The standing one had an unclear appearance.

However, facing the golden needles of the Heavenly Doctor and the furious Holy Emperor in front of him, he was extremely calm.

"Again, this familiar feeling..."

The Holy Emperor felt like falling into an abyss, his heart icy cold.

The Heavenly Doctor's nine golden needles had long been blown away to unknown places in the violent gusts.

Everything was beyond redemption.

This place where myriad realms converged, the only paradise of the Darkest Star Sea at present, was now being rapidly devoured by this world-ending storm.

Sun Tiansi, who was still trying to awaken his mother at the center of the formation, could no longer care about his mother's curses at this moment.

In his sea of consciousness, his mother, who had been viciously cursing at him regardless of what was happening in the outside world, gradually seemed to forget about her own existence.

"Tiansi, Tiansi..."

A tear inexplicably fell from the corner of her eye.

Wu Ninglu and Sun Tiansi perished together in the heart-burning fire of the Xuanhuang Great Heavenly Venerated One.

Instead, he focused his attention on the few figures still struggling in the immortal formation.
