Extremely tense.

One of them said, "I've heard that on the Wuliang Wall of Mount Wuliang, just like the [Mortal to Deity Wall], there are occasional strange phenomena that appear. So we discussed coming here to observe it. Little did we know that as soon as we arrived and were trying to locate the exact position of the Wuliang Wall, Brother Guan suddenly disappeared."

Another person continued, "At first, we thought he had to leave for some urgent matter and went back first. We didn't think much of it. But when we finished admiring the wonders of the Wuliang Wall and returned to Tianrang City, we still couldn't get in touch with Brother Guan, that's when we knew something was wrong."

"Not only could we not contact him through spiritual symbols, I also tried to infer from the items he left with me, but still got no results," the third cultivator named Fu Xingchi supplemented.

Xu Bai circled above Mount Wuliang but did not detect any abnormalities.

Given Xu Bai's level of attunement with the Dao, if there was a problem with Mount Wuliang, he would have sensed it.

It seems the issue is not with the location...

Wait.

Xu Bai suddenly thought of something and asked, "Where is this Wuliang Wall you mentioned?"

Fu Xingchi hesitated for a moment, then pointed to a valley below, "It's at the foot of the mountain."

"Take me there," Xu Bai said solemnly.

The three men, not understanding the reason, but knowing it was a serious matter, did not dare to object.

They led Xu Bai to the Wuliang Wall.

The originally rocky and vegetation-covered mountainside suddenly became as smooth as a mirror as they approached the base.

Even the streams in the mountains were reflected in it.

Xu Bai gazed at this three-hundred-zhang long and fifty-zhang high mirror-like mountainside and saw his own reflection.

Reaching out to touch it, it was as cold as jade.

"Did you see the so-called strange phenomena when you were here?" Xu Bai asked.

Fu Xingchi nodded, "The strange occurrences on the Wuliang Wall do not have a fixed schedule. Sometimes it appears multiple times a day. Sometimes it won't appear for years. It all depends on individual fortune."

"Can you re-depict what you saw at the time?"

"I can."

Fu Xingchi took out a brush and started drawing in the air.

Moments later, just like a water mirror technique directly recording, a vivid scene of cultivators wielding swords appeared before them.

"Brother Fu's marvelous painting skills are truly extraordinary, it's exactly the same as my memory," the other two cultivators couldn't help but exclaim in admiration.

Xu Bai stared at the sword-wielding scene for a long time, then suddenly asked, "Does Brother Guan know sword techniques?"

Fu Xingchi was stunned on the spot.

But he quickly reacted, "Are you suspecting that the figure appearing in the Wuliang Wall was Brother Guan?"

"No, that's impossible. Brother Guan cultivates the Dao of music, not sword techniques," Fu Xingchi shook his head in denial.

"I heard that Brother Changge greatly admires our senior Brother Guan. He often laments that he cannot master the earth-shaking, ghost-crying sword techniques like him," at this moment, another cultivator suddenly recalled something.

At these words, the few people present all fell silent.

They unanimously looked towards the Wuliang Wall in front of them, a chill rising in their hearts.

Xu Bai was the first to break the silence, "For now, it's just a guess, there's no solid evidence. You all help me seal off Mount Wuliang first, don't let anyone else get close. I'll go back and check if there have been similar incidents in the past."

Fu Xingchi and the others had solemn expressions, nodding to indicate they understood.

Xu Bai hurriedly returned to Tianrang City and applied to access the historical record files.

Sure enough, he found some anomalies.

In the past hundred years, there have been occasional reports of cultivators mysteriously disappearing.

But they were mostly obscure cultivators without a strong foundation.

After their disappearance, it was only fellow cultivators with closer relationships who would report it to the Immortal Alliance.

The path of cultivation is already arduous, with life and death being unpredictable.

After investigating, it was confirmed that it was not the work of spies from the Five Elders' Council. Yet, no useful clues could be found, so the cases were often left unresolved.

In this way, the Wuliang Wall has silently consumed countless nameless cultivators, yet no one has ever noticed.

Until now, this disappearance case has occurred.
