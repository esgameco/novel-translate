Chapter 1113: Hiding Alone in the Dark Prison

Despite the possibility of being discovered by the Dharma Transmission Heavenly Venerate and the Wangsheng Sect, Li Fan was still very confident that he would not be found.

Not to mention the countless concealment formations on the True Immortal Puppet, which were extremely complex and even surpassed the Life and Death Reversal Grand Formation.

In this world, no one had ever been to the Frozen Dead Sea before.

Yet the True Immortal's remnant body was able to accurately locate its position.

Just this point alone was enough to make Wangsheng suspicious.

"According to common sense, he should focus his target on those old acquaintances that may have existed in recent years."

"Even if he wants to investigate me, there's no way to start."

"The only flaw is that the True Immortal Puppet's transfer method uses the power of darkness."

"And apart from the Myriad Immortals Alliance, only I from the Myriad Realms United Association have been to the Dark Realm before..."

Thinking of this, Li Fan couldn't help but feel a chill in his heart.

At the level of the Dharma Transmission Heavenly Venerate, there was no longer a need for evidence to "speculate" when doing things.

As long as there was a shred of possibility, it was enough for him to make a decision.

"Since I just reviewed that in the entire Mysterious Yellow Realm, there are not many forces that can accomplish this matter. Then putting myself in his shoes, as long as I eliminate them all, I can prevent future troubles..."

"Although it seems a bit unreasonable, it is the way of the dominator."

As his thoughts turned, Li Fan's heart suddenly jumped.

He almost immediately made a decision.

In the Spirit Wood Realm, all defensive formations were activated at full power.

The cultivators of the Myriad Realms United Association were all prepared for battle at any time.

He summoned the main figures from the Heavenly Machine Realm, Medicine King Sect, Imperial Small World and others, explaining the critical situation.

Qiaogong, Liu Ruchen and others all slightly changed color.

"This is too unreasonable? Just because we have the strength to kill the Dharma Transmitters and they can't find the murderer, they want to destroy us all together?"

"Is the Envoy being a little too pessimistic? I don't think the Myriad Immortals Alliance would act so recklessly, right?"

Everyone discussed, but seeing Li Fan's gradually darkening face, they all instantly chose to shut up.

"By begging for the blessing of the Red Dust Compassionate Immortal, I have almost confirmed this matter."

"There is no need to doubt, just prepare in advance. In this battle, there is no room for reconciliation."

Li Fan said coldly.

Hearing this, everyone's faces turned pale.

"The only chance of survival is if our resistance is strong enough."

"They are going to conquer us in order to avenge the three Dharma Transmitters. But if we can bring them not just the demise of the three Dharma Transmitters..."

"Although the Myriad Immortals Alliance is powerful, it is impossible for them to attack us with the full force of the alliance."

"Plus, we have the geographical advantage. The Spirit Wood Realm is impregnable, covered by countless formations, and has the fortifications of the Heavenly Machine Realm."

"Finally, we can also mobilize the power of the Dark Realm to launch a surprise attack on the enemy with the dark tide..."

Li Fan said confidently: "As long as we can withstand the first few waves of the fiercest attacks, we should be able to get through this crisis."

Although Li Fan said so, the others still couldn't see optimism on their faces.

They all left with a heavy heart to mobilize before the battle.

The Spirit Wood Realm was completely sealed off, even if they had thoughts of escaping, they couldn't escape.

The only option was to fight to the death.

As for Li Fan's side...

In the underground secret chamber.

A sacred fetus that looked exactly like Li Fan was rapidly taking shape.

Using the divine power of the Red Dust Compassionate Immortal,

After forcibly raising the Holy Fetus to the Nascent Soul realm, it left the secret chamber and put on the Tianyang War Puppet.

As for Li Fan himself...

He didn't bring anything and quietly left the Spirit Wood Realm without anyone noticing.

Knowing that the Myriad Immortal Alliance would come to attack the Spirit Wood Realm, how could Li Fan stay behind with his true body to fight them to the death?

For Li Fan, the Myriad Realm Alliance, the Spirit Wood Realm, the Heavenly Medicine King...

They were all just external things that could be abandoned at any time.

Only his own safety was the most important.

As long as he could preserve his life and survive this storm,

After the Dharma Transmission and reincarnation, when they relaxed their guard, he would activate the Destruction Layout.

Once everything returned to its original state, everything he had lost would eventually return to his hands.

With Li Fan's current concealment abilities, after leaving the Spirit Wood Realm, he traveled swiftly without anyone discovering his whereabouts from beginning to end.

As for where he would hide in the coming period, Li Fan had already thought of it.

Before long, he had already reached his destination.

It was none other than the Myriad Immortal Alliance's Secluded Prison, where he had previously taken out the Dharma Transmitter Wei Congzhong!

"Hehe, does this count as turning myself in?"

Li Fan laughed at himself in his heart.

"However, this so-called prison net cannot stop me."

After carefully observing for a while, Li Fan had already cracked the formation covering the entrance of the Secluded Prison.

Transforming into a dark shadow, he quietly flew inside.

The scene before his eyes suddenly became dim.

It was somewhat similar to the scene in the Nether Realm.

There was no sky or earth, and all that could be seen was endless darkness.

But compared to the complete darkness of the Nether Realm, there was still a little light here.

At least divine sense could barely distinguish the direction to proceed without getting completely lost.

In the endless dark space, towering prison pillars stood.

Each pillar seemed to be suppressing a prisoner.

And on the surface of the pillars, various horrifying torture scenes kept changing.

As for the jailers of this Secluded Prison, they were monstrous creatures made of unknown black mist-like substances floating here.

There were thousands of them, even outnumbering the pillars in the space.

From time to time, they let out bursts of roars and flew into the pillars where prisoners were held.

Before long, faint screams of agony echoed in the prisons.

After torturing for a long time, these black mist monsters would only then leave the pillars with satisfaction.

Similar scenes kept playing out in the Secluded Prison, as if there was no end...

...

Seeing this scene that was like a purgatory, Li Fan's expression did not change in the slightest.

After observing the composition of these prison pillars for a while, he determined they were formed by some kind of formation.

His eyes flashed, and he cracked it almost instantly.

Then he chose an empty one and took the initiative to enter it himself.

"Not bad."

After entering the black pillar, Li Fan looked around and found that compared to the outside, it was actually brighter inside the prison.

It's just that formation attacks would fall at any time as a means of punishment.

Naturally, they could not hurt Li Fan.

"I'm afraid even if they turn the Profound Yellow Realm inside out, they would never imagine that I'm hiding here."

"And when they forget about me, that will be the time for me to go out."

Li Fan settled down comfortably in the Secluded Prison as if he had returned home.

For him, the Spirit Wood Realm and the Secluded Prison were essentially no different.
