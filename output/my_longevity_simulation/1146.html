It was most suitable for running errands with certain risks.

With this thought, Wang Xuanba's shadow body transformed into a dark swimming dragon and broke through the void.

More than ten days later, he finally found the location of Yin Yin Island.

The faint wailing sound coming from the island made Wang Xuanba feel a little uneasy.

He called out a few times, but no one answered. Wang Xuanba walked into the building, and the strange and bizarre creatures he saw made his scalp numb.

"These things are even more disgusting than the exotic beasts I've merged with."

Going deeper, he finally saw the person in question in front of a transparent cage.

"Sir, my master wants to invite you to a gathering." Wang Xuanba said neither humbly nor arrogantly.

Senior Yin had no reaction at all.

Wang Xuanba said it several times, but it was the same.

Seeing this situation, Wang Xuanba simply followed the Holy Emperor's instructions and said directly, "Sir, do you want to know who created the Immortal Miasma?"

Senior Yin's body trembled, and he slowly moved his gaze from the experimental subject to Wang Xuanba.

"What did you say...?"

The double overlapping voice made Wang Xuanba swallow his saliva.

But he still plucked up the courage to repeat it.

Then, without waiting for Senior Yin to speak, he said again, "Sir, do you want to know the perfect method of [Building Foundation with Me]?"

Senior Yin's originally stooped body slowly straightened.

He stared at Wang Xuanba, a dangerous light flashing in his eyes: "Who exactly are you?"

Wang Xuanba chuckled, "I'm just a errand runner. If you want to know the answers to the questions, just come with me."

"Lead the way!"

What surprised Wang Xuanba was that this time Senior Yin agreed so readily.

Thick fog sprayed out from the cage behind, and several mortals screamed and disappeared.

Wang Xuanba's whole body tightened, and he quickly nodded.

Senior Yin's eyes turned cold, and the murderous intent in his eyes could no longer be concealed.

But looking at the fearless Wang Xuanba, he still forcibly suppressed his killing intent.

After searching on his body, he didn't find the rib.

In the end, the rib was found in the nest of that human-handed puppy.

Kicking the dog away with one foot, Senior Yin indicated that they could go.

Wang Xuanba then flew ahead to lead the way, passing through the entrance to the small world concealed by the formation, and returned to the Great Qi Dynasty.

Slightly surprised by the rich concentration of spiritual energy here, Senior Yin still focused his attention on the tallest tower-like building in the Holy Capital.

He could sense that there seemed to be an extremely powerful existence there.

Soon, Senior Yin stood in front of Li Ping, the faceless Holy Emperor.

"Fellow Daoist, what's the intention of going to great lengths to bring me here?"

The expression on Senior Yin's face was not very pleasant.

"I've just long admired your talent, sir." Li Ping sat on the high Holy Emperor's seat.
