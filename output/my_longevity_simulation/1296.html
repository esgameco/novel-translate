Just as Sun Tianci was somewhat stunned by the scene before him, Li Fan's reprimanding voice rang in his ears, "What are you still dazed for? Do you really want to wait for the spirit of heaven and earth to descend?"

And because the "erroneous descent of the spirit of heaven and earth" was terminated just before it was completed, the alertness from the Profound Yellow world was also minuscule.

Li Fan narrowed his eyes to sense it and once again unleashed the Distorting Heaven and Earth Principles.

This time, Sun Tianci reacted quickly. As soon as the blurry human figure was about to take shape, it was sent back again.

This repeated over a hundred times until it was about to arouse the notice of the Profound Yellow world, and only then did Li Fan stop.

Sun Tianci let out a sigh of relief and wiped the sweat from his forehead with a pale face.

Obviously, continuously utilizing the Profound Underworld Art to "decompose" over a hundred spirits of heaven and earth, even if they were only half-formed, was still a significant burden for him.

In a cave surrounded by mountains and cliffs in Tong Yan Prefecture.

Scenes from the 119th world flashed before Li Fan's eyes, the concealed grotto heaven he encountered after infiltrating the headquarters of the Myriad Immortal Alliance and meeting Mo Rubin.

It was exactly the same as this place.

"Because of the natural characteristics here, the stationed cultivators will certainly not have high vigilance. But we still need to be careful..." Li Fan's divine sense laid out a series of formations around Sun Tianci, and then the two of them flew into the cave together.

After passing through a narrow, downward passage, they arrived in front of a deep hole only as wide as a person's waist.

Pitch-black, straight down, and bottomless.

"Don't probe with divine sense. It will trigger a pre-warning."

But with a single glance, Li Fan saw through the mechanism of this pit.

It took the time to brew a pot of tea, as if penetrating through the Profound Yellow earth, reaching the bottomless depths of the Nine Underworlds.

The cramped cave vein gradually became more open.

Passing through a horizontal passage, a huge cave shaped like an inverted cone, located extremely deep underground, suddenly appeared before Sun Tianci.

The cliff walls of the cave were densely packed with square openings.

Upon closer inspection, the objects placed in these openings were all amber-colored crystals.

And without exception, a cultivator was sleeping inside each of these crystals.

From time to time, patrolling Immortal Alliance guards could be seen in mid-air. Sun Tianci didn't dare to spread out his divine sense to probe, but he roughly estimated that this underground cave imprisoned and sealed at least 100,000 cultivators.

Their faces were rosy and even had smiles on their faces. They were completely unaware that they were eternally falling into slumber.

"According to Master, this is only a part of the Myriad Immortal Alliance's [Eternal Immortal Wall]..." A chill rose in Sun Tianci's heart, and he subconsciously clenched his fists.

"Go. The rest is up to you." Li Fan seemed to see the suppressed anger in Sun Tianci's heart and said indifferently.

At the same time, he synchronized the method to break the formation sealing the cultivators here.

"Master, what do you think will happen if we awaken all the cultivators here?" Sun Tianci suddenly asked.

"I advise you to abandon that thought. Since the Myriad Immortal Alliance dares to do this so blatantly, they must have taken precautions. Believe me, your speed of awakening them will definitely not keep up with the Myriad Immortal Alliance's speed of dealing with them. You should know that the Myriad Immortal Alliance possesses the means to extract and preserve a cultivator's Dao foundation..." Li Fan said coldly.

Sun Tianci's eyes flickered, but in the end, he had no choice but to give up.

Avoiding the patrolling cultivators, he swiftly flew past these pitiful bugs in slumber. A barely noticeable breeze was stirred up, dissolving all the Dao foundations within their bodies.

"Without cultivation, they won't live long in their slumber... It's equivalent to me personally killing them..." Sun Tianci's flying figure trembled slightly.

"Kill? Or save? Look at their current state. Isn't it a fate worse than death? If they could clearly perceive their surroundings, they would surely beg you to take action and help them find release." Li Fan spoke to console him.

After another round of persuasion, Sun Tianci's somewhat hesitant movements became resolute again.

For more than five years, Li Fan cultivated Sun Tianci according to his ideal image of the Profound Yellow Heavenly Venerate.

Making him benevolent but not pedantic. And once he made a decision, he would become incomparably decisive.

The Profound Yellow world power formed by dissolving the Dao foundations of thousands of cultivators was gathered by Li Fan and condensed into a bright orb.

When one-fifth of the act of bestowing liberation was completed, Li Fan spoke, "That's about enough. If we continue, even I won't be able to conceal this intense energy fluctuation."

Sun Tianci looked at the remaining ones, vaguely showing a hint of regret.

But he still followed his Master's orders and quietly flew towards the exit.

"No matter. Just leave."

After a brief silence, Li Fan finally responded.

Sun Tianci didn't inquire further and rushed upward all the way. After leaving this horrifying dungeon, Sun Tianci took the orb containing the Dao foundations of many cultivators and lightly crushed it.

Countless rays of light instantly burst forth. In a daze, Sun Tianci seemed to see the wondrous sights of the world, the Profound Yellow grotto heavens, and the changing scenery of mountains and rivers.

In the sky, the wind and clouds changed color. A creaking sound, as if a giant millstone was grinding, reverberated above the cloud layer.

And the entire Profound Yellow realm trembled in an instant.

Although the magnitude was minuscule, Sun Tianci still sensed it.

"The Myriad Immortal Alliance should be sending people to investigate. Let's go!" Li Fan spoke.

Without needing a reminder, Sun Tianci had already flown away, distancing himself from the scene the moment he crushed the orb.

At this moment, he was immersed in the rapidly increasing Profound Yellow Celestial Favor.

As for Li Fan, after ensuring no one was tracking them, he recalled the scene he had just witnessed in that great underground cave.

Several faces flashed before his eyes. Although he didn't know their names, Li Fan was certain that he had seen them before.

However, it was not in the Myriad Immortal Alliance, but in...

The territory of the Five Elders Council.

In the space of the Dharma Orb, within the sea of consciousness of his true body, the light of the Dao Stone flickered, bringing up the scenes he had seen before.

It was from the 119th world. After the Myriad Immortal Alliance used the Immortal Selectees to unleash the power contained in the Dharma Transmission Statues, restraining the five Eternal Life Venerates, they launched a fierce attack on the Five Elders Council.

The Five Elders Council seemed to be forced into a desperate situation, but their hidden means were finally revealed.

Ancient sects that had already gone extinct reappeared in the world. There were also cultivators from the [Carefree Paradise] who fearlessly wielded various strange magic tools in a desperate counterattack.

Li Fan compared the few faces that had appeared in this war of subjugation with the slumbering cultivators he had just seen in the great underground cave.

They matched perfectly.
