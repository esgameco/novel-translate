Chapter 1161: Sword Pointing at the Fishing Pond

After suffering such a big loss at the hands of Mo Rubin and Tian Xuanjing, it was absolutely impossible for Li Fan not to have any thoughts of revenge.

However, as one of the Dharma Kings of the Xuantian Sect, Mo Rubin's abilities were too mysterious. He could even infiltrate the headquarters of the Wanxian Alliance as if entering an uninhabited area.

Although he later died inexplicably in the Youjie realm, Li Fan currently didn't have a hundred percent confidence in subduing him.

Without a fundamental conflict of interest, Li Fan had no intention of provoking him in this lifetime for the time being.

"Perhaps I can secretly learn how the Wanxian Alliance dealt with this Dharma King of the Xuantian Sect."

According to the normal historical trajectory, Mo Rubin would be dug out by the Wanxian Alliance without awakening.

Perhaps within the Wanxian Alliance, there might be some special means to deal with members of the Xuantian Sect. Under the preemptive strike, Mo Rubin may have indeed lost the power to resist.

At that time, Li Fan could learn from it and deal with Mo Rubin like this in every future life.

But if Mo Rubin was just pretending to be subdued like in the 119th world, and actually took the opportunity to infiltrate...

Then before finding an exact way to deal with him, Li Fan would not actively become enemies with Mo Rubin.

As for Tian Xuanjing.

Carrying a Tian Xuanjing origin, he was already in an invincible position.

Now that he had mastered the method to peek into its interior...

Rejecting heaven's gift would only bring misfortune upon oneself!

Li Fan's mind quickly pondered.

"The Wanxian Alliance searching for Mo Rubin in the ruins of major sects is to promote finding the ultimate method to restrain Tian Xuanjing. There should be a quiet push from the Zhangjing lineage behind this."

"Cang, Fu, hehe. These two are not easy to deal with."

"But it's also where my opportunity lies."

With surging thoughts, Li Fan once again turned into a virtual light and entered the Wuliang Mirror.

He continued to construct formations inside to consolidate control.

While waiting for Sun Luyuan to return to the secret room, he also took the opportunity to study the traces of the immortal-level grand formation remaining in the Wuliang Mirror.

During this period, with Wang Xuanba sparing no effort to travel all over the Xuanhuang Realm.

In addition to Yin Shangren, Ou Daozi and his son, Xi Menyu and other "Xuanhuang Stars" selected by Li Ping were all persuaded by Wang Xuanba with the rhetoric of the Holy Emperor's teaching and came to Shengjing of the Holy Dynasty in Daqi.

Of course, not everyone on this list was convinced. Not only did they refuse the invitation, some even killed Wang Xuanba's avatar.

Wang Xuanba was not angry, he just wrote down these people's names, reported to the Holy Emperor.

And then skipped them and went to invite the next person.

After the Xuanhuang stars arrived in Daqi, they all settled down in the Holy Dynasty like Yin Shangren under Li Ping's personal reception.

For example, Xi Menyu, Li Ping promised to help him deepen his research on the power of earth veins. And he also revealed some important news about the ancient earth vein trees.

For example, Ou Daozi would be responsible for the construction of the new Shengjing in Daqi. And in the future, every small world conquered by the Holy Dynasty would be comprehensively transformed by Ou Daozi as the chief planning designer.

More crucially, Li Ping saw at a glance that Ou Shangtian possessed two strange objects, the Kunqian Bone Hand and the Book of Ten Thousand Deaths. Subsequently, he accepted Ou Shangtian as his personal disciple.

Ou Shangtian did not feel the slightest malice from Li Ping.

Moreover, the various interesting secrets of the cultivation world and many profound principles of the great Dao mentioned by the Holy Emperor in his conversations indeed deeply attracted the young Ou Shangtian.

So he willingly became his disciple.

A fishing competition.

Personally ordered by the Holy Emperor, anyone who is interested can participate, regardless of whether they have any previous fishing experience.

Each region will first decide on the winners, and then they will go to the Holy Capital to participate in the final competition.

At that time, the Holy Emperor will also make an appearance and enjoy the event with the people.

In addition to the common people, the Holy Emperor's disciples will also participate in the competition.

This undoubtedly further stimulates people's enthusiasm.

The Holy Emperor has no heirs.

His disciples are undoubtedly the successors of the Holy Dynasty.

If one can be noticed by the Holy Emperor in the fishing competition and be accepted as a true disciple...

It is even more important than studying and cultivating.

The reason for allowing it and even helping to push the wave is naturally for the treasure of the Fishing Pool.

This immortal artifact is even beyond the existence of the Xuanhuang Realm.

There are countless opportunities within it.

Perhaps hidden within it is his method of breaking the situation.

He certainly won't miss the opportunity to acquire a large number of divine objects at no cost.
