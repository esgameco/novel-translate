As for the Underworld Prison, with such a large-scale escape incident, it had already become a mere shell of its former self.

After completing the transfer of the remaining prisoners, even the entrance to the Underworld Prison was no longer concealed.

It was thus abandoned.

Time slowly passed, and no one came here anymore. It seemed as if the world had forgotten about it.

After an unknown amount of time, Li Fan's figure slowly appeared.

"Suppressed in the deepest part of the Underworld Prison, even I couldn't see their face clearly."

"The transfer required three Dharma transmitters to personally come..."

"Interesting."

Instead of immediately escaping with the group of escaped cultivators, Li Fan directly hid at the entrance. The purpose was to explore the prisoner confined in the deepest part of the Underworld Prison, which he had been unable to approach for the past three years.

It wasn't that the formations in the deepest part were too ingenious for Li Fan to crack, but rather that the jailers in charge of guarding there were actually three Hedao cultivators working together.

It seemed that they were only there to suppress that one prisoner. Even when the Underworld Prison above was breached and countless felons escaped, they remained unmoved and did not come out to stop it.

When the reception team from the Myriad Immortals Alliance arrived, during their transfer process, Li Fan was able to see that prisoner.

There was a vague sense of familiarity to Li Fan.

However, being concealed by secret techniques, it couldn't be fully confirmed.

"Before the Myriad Spirits Grand Formation is completed, it's still best not to stir up more trouble."

"Let's wait until the next life to investigate thoroughly."

"The only thing that needs to be done now is to quietly wait for the opportunity and watch the world turn upside down!"

Li Fan looked towards the distant horizon, as if seeing the Myriad Spirits Grand Formation continuously lighting up in the thousands of mortal worlds above.

The Heaven and Earth Myriad Spirits Grand Formation shown to the crowd of felons was actually not the complete name of the formation.

This formation was derived from the Final Separation Disk's deductions within three years.

It represented the highest standard of formations that the Separation Disk could deduce in its current state.

Using the Heaven and Earth Myriad Spirits Grand Formation that originally covered the Spirit Wood Realm, the Empire, and the Beast Realm as a prototype, after further deduction, a world-shocking formation was created.

The full name of the formation was "Heaven and Earth Myriad Spirits, All Living Beings Refining Divinity".

The main function of this formation, and the purpose Li Fan went to great lengths to release this group of prisoners from the Underworld Prison and have them work for him, was encapsulated in the four words "All Living Beings Refining Divinity".

It had a similar ingenuity to the All Living Beings Refining Divinity Formation that Song Hesong from Ningyuan City in the Fallen Immortal Illusion Realm used when fighting against Senior Brother Zhang.

The killing power of the formation also used the thoughts of the myriad spirits and countless living beings of heaven and earth.

However, after Li Fan's modifications...

It was hundreds of billions of times stronger.

Because the materials needed to be consumed increased by hundreds of billions of times.

And Li Fan used the thousands of small worlds and trillions of living beings in the Primordial Realm, just to be able to block the Faceless True Immortal for a moment.

During the three years hiding in the Underworld Prison after the Spirit Wood Realm was annihilated by the Myriad Immortals Alliance, Li Fan had been constantly thinking and reviewing the final world-ending plan.

After all, it was the first time confronting the power of True Immortals. Even borrowing force to fight force and watching immortals battle from a distance meant endless risks.

After all, these two were truly transcendent immortals.

Not single-minded Foundation Establishment cultivators.

If they were really that easy to scheme against, how could they be worthy of the name True Immortal?

Once it failed, it would be an irreversible situation.
