"To truly activate the reverse flow of time, predetermined anchors are needed. Let's take the three current anchor points on 69shu8.com, all set before Mr. Bai's resurrection."

"Even if he truly manages to wrest away the 'Reversal of Truth' from me, he cannot return to an era when he did not exist."

"Any new anchors he can use must be set after obtaining the 'Reversal of Truth'."

"So I only need to spread word of the existence of the 'Reversal of Truth' across the world before my death. Even if he succeeds in taking revenge."

Xu Bai made a "death note" while imagining the scene after his own death. He hadn't felt this way for a long time.

Ever since awakening the "Reversal of Truth" at the brink of death in his first life, he hadn't contemplated facing death himself for a very long time.

Perhaps because it had not truly descended yet, he did not feel much fear in his heart. Rather, it was a jumble of emotions, even containing a fantasy of having narrowly escaped disaster.

It was the unknown future that caused his unsettled mood.

In the past, no matter what perilous situation he faced, he never felt lost. Even if things were in utter chaos, he thought "no matter what, I can just use Reversal of Truth!"

Whether it was the study he lived in as a mortal, or the summit of Departure Mountain, they were the safest harbors in his heart.

But now, a stretch of sea riddled with reefs and undercurrents suddenly lay outside the harbor.

The slightest carelessness could lead to being smashed to pieces.

...

While his thoughts wandered, the first "death note" was completed. On 69shu8.com

The so-called "death note" was actually an instantaneous, large-scale image created by the aberration after a cultivator falls, embedded with a wisp of his consciousness to act as a detonation or destruction switch.

"I need to bury them in corners of the Primordial Realm that even I don't know about beforehand."

"That way, even if the resurrected Mr. Bai wants to stop it, he won't be able to."

"Hmm, I still need to find someone trustworthy to do this."

Face after face flashed rapidly through his mind. Soon, he had an idea.

After making 108 "death imprints", Xu Bai left Longevity Valley and returned to Tianliang State to find his former superior, Nan Gong Shi Rong.

Nan Gong Shi Rong initially thought Xu Bai had changed his mind and wanted to take up an official post again.

But Xu Bai's request left him taken aback.

"The Soul Reversal Tally? What do you need that for?" Nan Gong Shi Rong studied Xu Bai.

The Soul Reversal Tally was a prop from the Underworld Department that could trace a cultivator's reincarnation based on objects carrying their former aura, to prevent some people from "maliciously killing themselves" to escape due punishment through rebirth.

"The Underworld is relatively independent. Even if I govern a state, I can't really talk to them." Nan Gong Shi Rong looked hesitant.

Xu Bai didn't speak and passed him a storage ring instead. Nan Gong Shi Rong suspiciously took and checked it, almost blinded by the massive pile of top-grade spirit stones crammed inside.

A spiritual scan revealed that exchanging these spirit stones for merit would amount to no less than three million!

Nan Gong Shi Rong couldn't help but gasp coldly. Although merit had inflated a lot due to the White Mist pioneering era and conquest of realms, with large amounts issued, three million merit was still an astronomical sum - a colossal fortune even for someone of his status.
