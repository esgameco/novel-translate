Chapter 1129: The Meaning of the Anchor Point

A dark black long robe, embroidered with nine golden dragons intertwined.

The crown's tassels hung down, concealing the face, making it impossible to see the features clearly.

"As expected of the Heavenly Mandate Holy Emperor, ordinary people can hardly hope to gaze upon the dragon's countenance."

Sun Erlang felt Li Fan's gaze upon him, his body trembling slightly, instinctively lowering his head.

At the same time, he spoke in a hoarse voice, "Reporting to Teacher, this disciple is incompetent. Just yesterday, I broke through to the first layer of the Qi Refining stage."

"Relying solely on the little bit of spiritual energy gathered within the imperial palace, it's not easy for you to complete the process of drawing qi into your body in such a short time." Li Fan did not reprimand him, but instead nodded and comforted him.

Hearing this, Sun Erlang revealed an even more guilty expression, "If it weren't for the [Xuanhuang Immortal Heart Chant] taught by Teacher, I'm afraid at this moment I would still be affected by the miasma within my body, making it difficult to ascend the immortal path."

After saying this, Sun Erlang kowtowed several times with great gratitude.

Li Fan did not stop him.

When Sun Erlang stood up again, he asked indifferently, "What is the overall progress of the Imperial Guards in learning the Immortal Heart Chant?"

"As of yesterday, a total of one hundred and sixty officers and soldiers have successfully entered the door and shed their mortal bodies. The rest who failed to pass the test have all been thoroughly dealt with." A trace of reluctance flashed in Sun Erlang's eyes, but it was quickly concealed.

"From now on, you will be the main force of my army to conquer the myriad realms. You must diligently cultivate day and night, and must not slack off."

"As for the issue of spiritual energy, you don't need to worry. In three days, you will personally take this immortal boat and head to the Eastern Desolate Abyss."

With a casual point of his finger, Li Fan made a wooden boat about thirty meters in size appear in front of Sun Erlang.

The boat was covered in silver patterns, emitting bursts of profound energy.

"It is truly a supreme treasure of the immortals. Teacher is actually so readily entrusting such a precious item to me." Sun Erlang's heart trembled as he quickly nodded in agreement.

The immortal boat then flew into his embrace.

A feeling of being able to control it at will suddenly surfaced in Sun Erlang's mind.

"You may go." Just as he was experiencing the wonders of the immortal boat, Li Fan's voice rang out at the right moment.

Sun Erlang bowed respectfully and took his leave.

In the entire imperial palace's rear garden, only Li Fan remained.

Under the tassels, the faceless visage in the shadows looked strange and sinister.

"Hehe, if it weren't for the fact that this world is destined not to last long, and I simply tried bestowing the technique upon others, I'm afraid it would take me a long time to discover that the [Xuanhuang Immortal Heart Chant] is not restricted by the rule that techniques cannot be cultivated together."

"Is it because the Immortal Heart Chant does not belong to the category of cultivation methods, or is it the preferential treatment left by the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao for the Xuanhuang Great Celestial Lord?"

"In any case, the plan to select suitable people to succeed the Xuanhuang Great Celestial Lord in the future will be much easier to carry out than imagined."

Li Fan's thoughts slowly turned as he flashed and arrived atop the towering [Holy Emperor Throne] in Xuanjing City.

Wisps of faint spiritual energy nourished Li Fan's body.

Making his current late Foundation Establishment realm not regress due to the lack of spiritual energy.

Having gone through more than a hundred reincarnations, with his highest cultivation reaching the Deity Transformation realm, able to rival Harmonized Dao cultivators, although Li Fan had now returned to the Foundation Establishment realm, the world in his eyes was already different from the past.

It's just that due to the special structure of the small world itself, coupled with the sealing of the Immortal Severing Grand Formation, the spiritual energy was extremely thin, almost equivalent to none.

Ordinary methods of cultivators simply could not utilize it. Only those animals with long lifespans could accumulate and seize the spiritual energy bit by bit over time.

Just like that hundreds of zhang long snake on Mount Jieli.

"Extracting the spiritual energy of an entire world can only barely sustain a Foundation Establishment cultivator's survival..."

"But it doesn't hurt to borrow some from the Xuanhuang Realm."

Li Fan added some new formations to the Taiyan Boat he gave to Sun Erlang. The purpose was to automatically fly to the Xuanhuang Realm, absorb and store spiritual energy, and then return.

"The Myriad Immortals Alliance launched a surprise attack and the headquarters of the Alliance was destroyed?" Xiao Qing's face turned deathly pale after hearing what Li Fan said.

The Imperial Princess and Xiao Hong looked at each other, both filled with lingering fear in their hearts.

"But you don't need to worry too much. Although the Spirit Wood Realm was destroyed, the vast majority of the headquarters members escaped safely and are temporarily lying low. Once the storm passes, the Alliance will be able to rise again in an instant." Li Fan deceived them with a straight face.
