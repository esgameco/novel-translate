While the Yao Wang Sect and others were pondering their future path, more details about the invasion of the minor realms were gradually being revealed within the Wan Xian Alliance.

Once a cultivator possessing the Xuan Tian Treasure Mirror discovered an entrance to a minor realm, they had two choices. One was to explore it alone first. If able to single-handedly sweep through an entire realm, then they could monopolize all the merits of discovering that realm. The rewards would be unimaginably bountiful. Of course, if their personal strength was insufficient to overcome all obstacles encountered, they could choose to share the entrance to the minor realm. At that point, anyone holding the Xuan Tian Treasure Mirror would become aware of this entrance's existence and swarm over.

In addition to the "discovery merit," the initial cultivator who found it could also obtain a portion of the subsequent cultivators' merits and contributions. Although this percentage was minuscule, when added up across numerous cultivators, it would absolutely amount to a considerable fortune.

So whether choosing to monopolize or share, the discoverer of a minor realm entrance would become fabulously wealthy overnight. This greatly encouraged cultivators to thoroughly explore.

After the battle to conquer the minor realms concluded, the distribution of spoils would be even more exciting.

Any celestial realms discovered during this campaign would be publicized in the Xuan Tian Treasure Mirror afterward. As long as one had sufficient merits, they could directly exchange for ownership of a celestial realm! Moreover, the original discoverers of celestial realms or minor realms would have priority purchasing rights!

Once this news spread, cultivators who were originally just a bit restless immediately went into a frenzy.

It's important to know that in the current Xuan Huang Realm, the Yuanying realm was a very obvious dividing line. One could still rely on their own enlightenment to reach the Golden Dan realm. But to achieve a Yuanying celestial realm required either incredible luck in finding one or powerful connections to obtain one. Among a hundred Golden Dan cultivators, perhaps not even one would be able to reach Yuanying - illustrating the immense difficulty.

Now, the Wan Xian Alliance aimed to unify the Xuan Huang Realm. At that time, all existing celestial realms in the Xuan Huang Realm would be laid bare. Luck and connections would no longer be deciding factors. As long as you worked hard enough, you had a chance to "seize the essence of heaven and earth to become Yuanying!"

The era where anyone could become Yuanying was dawning!

Amid the immense excitement, cultivators also felt a sense of urgency. After all, everyone knew the total number of celestial realms in the Xuan Huang Realm was limited - first come, first served. If you were too slow, you might miss your chance at Yuanying in this lifetime. To pursue further breakthroughs, you'd have to wait until Yuanying cultivators fell and new celestial realms regenerated!

So within the Wan Xian Alliance's territory, an incredible scene unfolded. Cultivators swarmed like locusts, meticulously inspecting every inch of land - deep mountains, great lakes, jungles...
