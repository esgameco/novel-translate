Chapter 420

The only "Heavenly Covenant" that rules over the world of Xuanhuang, integrating all resources.

And perhaps, only with the extraordinary strategic planning and calculation ability of the Tianxuan Mirror, it might be possible.

In this way, the Heavenly Venerables can turn a blind eye to the actions of the Tianxuan Mirror, and even actively facilitate the merger of the Wanxian Alliance and the Five Elders' Association, thus can be explained.

Because once successful, they will completely rid themselves of the threat from the Immortal Realm.

The vast void, from now on, they can roam freely.

No longer confined to the little corner of the world of Xuanhuang.

The life and death of cultivators, what does it have to do with them?

Even if it is unsuccessful, it is not a loss for the Heavenly Venerables.

After all, the Heavenly Covenant is not just about slaughtering cultivators.

It is about suppressing all the cultivators who maintain the "weight" of Xuanhuang within the "Eternal Immortal Wall."

No need to worry about controlling the number of cultivators, perhaps the Heavenly Venerables find it more reassuring this way!

As for the final plan for Xuanwang to leave the Immortal Realm, whether it was successful or not...

Li Fan is inclined to believe it was successful.

Because in the previous life, before he truly existed, after nearly a thousand years of silence, he clearly felt the revival of vitality in the world of Xuanhuang.

The long-lost sunlight reappeared.

Although most living beings in the world were extinct, the world itself was rejuvenated.

Li Fan did not believe that after falling into the Immortal Realm, the Xuanhuang world could simply revive after a thousand years of silence.

If that were the case, the so-called Immortal Realm would not have made the Heavenly Venerables so fearful.

"For cultivators, the Xuan Network is naturally unforgivable."

"But in a sense, it saved the Xuanhuang world."

"It even cleaned up all the cultivators."

"Perhaps because of this, when the Xuan Network took over, the Heavenly Dao of this realm did not offer much resistance."

After pondering for a long time, Li Fan slowly suppressed those thoughts.

"In addition to these inevitable events, there are also many developments influenced by the establishment of the Xuan Network."

"All the causes stem from my killing the Giant Luan Fish and then personally orchestrating the Heavenly Spirit Grass incident."

"The large-scale war of the Heavenly Spirit Province, which should not have happened, had a profound impact on both the Wanxian Alliance and the Five Elders' Association."

"The Five Elders' Association subtly sensed the disappearance of the Law-Transmitting Venerable and continued to probe in the following days."

"Faced with pressure from the Five Elders' Association, the Wanxian Alliance, for self-preservation, created monstrous formations like the Tianxuan Soul-Locking Array."

"Perhaps even without the Heavenly Spirit Province War, there would still be the Heavenly Judgment Province and the Nine Mountain Provinces War."

"But the timing of the wars could lead to completely different future outcomes."

"Countless futures, infinite possibilities."

"The birth of the Xuan Network is just one of them."

"The world after the widespread use of the Xuan Network is too dangerous for cultivators."

"A little carelessness and they will be suppressed in the 'Eternal Immortal Wall,' unsuitable for cultivation."

"It seems that in this world, efforts must be made to prevent its birth."

Li Fan thought to himself.

"However, whether it's the Heavenly Spirit Grass incident or the widespread deployment of the soul-locking array, I, who have foresight, can benefit from it."

"If I give up, it won't be beautiful either."

"Then..."

After a brief contemplation, Li Fan immediately had an idea.

"Balancing and pulling each other, the overall situation can be stable."

"The Xuanhuang world should not be too chaotic!"

After reviewing the previous life, the next step is to plan for this life.

The goal is clear, to achieve the Yuan Ying Realm.

Before the Great Battle of the Heavenly Spirit Province, cultivate to the perfection of the Golden Dan Realm.

After the relocation of the Five Elements Great Cave Heaven, explore the void that can continuously evolve into the Five Elements Great Cave Heaven.

If possible, break through based on that.

If not, then find a way to obtain the Five Elements Great Cave Heaven from the Wanxian Alliance.

"However, before that, there is one thing to do."

Without any distractions, he activated the "Purple Heaven Sublime Intent," a secret technique passed down from the sect of Purple Heaven, which he obtained in the previous life in the Immortal Realm.

Li Fan hadn't forgotten that there were traces left by "It" from the Source Dao Realm on his body.

Although Li Fan vaguely felt that with his reincarnations and transformations, these traces might have already disappeared.

But having an extra layer of security wouldn't hurt.

As the technique operated, Li Fan felt his consciousness soaring continuously.

Ascending, breaking through the firmament, he arrived at a sacred and inexplicable place.

There was nothing else.

Only the profound and great meaning of heaven and earth, overflowing within.

This immense and pure aura washed over Li Fan's soul like flowing water.

Li Fan gradually immersed himself in it.

Due to the influence of "It" from the Source Dao Realm and the shadow caused by the Heavenly Healer, the feelings dissipated.

Even the vicissitudes brought about by the three thousand incarnations were slowly washed away.

...

Anchor for 3 years.

Li Fan put away the supernatural ability, feeling his mind clear and body relaxed.

"The secret technique of the Purple Heaven Sect is truly extraordinary."

Li Fan nodded in satisfaction.

Casually checking the interface of "It," he was pleasantly surprised.

He suddenly discovered that the charging speed of "It" was much faster than before.

"Originally needed nearly nine years, but now, it will probably only take a little over seven years to complete the charge."

"It seems that the Xuanhuang Refining Heart Chant's reverse training of the Dao Heart to strengthen the soul is effective."

"Faster charging means I will be able to leave Da Xuan and go to the Xuanhuang world by the beginning of the 8th year of anchoring."

"Just in time for the Wanxian Alliance's once-in-ten-years entrance exam..."

Following the routine, he descended the mountain and visited the tomb of Yi Yin.

He retrieved the Transformative Stone, slowly absorbed it, and waited for "It's" charging to be completed.

Soon, the eighth year of anchoring arrived.

Before setting off, Li Fan opened the "It" interface.

Supernatural Abilities: Chaos, Great Five Elements Annihilation Sword, Life and Death Connection, Formless Killing Intent, Binding Insects, Sword of the Congregated Clouds, Heaven Overturning Palm

...

"Three thousand incarnations, only a little over a hundred years added to the mental age. For now, there should be no need to worry."

"As for the 'Chaos' supernatural ability..."

Li Fan recalled the dreadful side effects, even with the refined puppet body, it completely disintegrates after being used.

Although powerful, it was currently too strenuous to use.

Closing the interface of "It", in this life, he decided not to cultivate the power of the "Zuo Shan Jue" or plunder the finances of the Great Xuan court anymore.
