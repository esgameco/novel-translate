Chapter 1119: Battle of Two Celestial Venerables

The instant he saw this black-haired, bare-bodied man, Li Fan had almost confirmed his identity.

There was no doubt that he should be the Celestial Venerable of the Eastern Extremity who had mysteriously disappeared.

But a dignified Celestial Venerable of Longevity had actually been turned into a puppet-like existence controlled by others.

How could this not make one feel terrified?

"No wonder almost no information could be found about the Eastern Extremity. Even through possessing the Dharma transmitters and inheriting all their memories, whether it was Mo Rubin or the Tianxuan Mirror, it was the same."

"No wonder when the Celestial Venerable took action, the cultivators of the Myriad Immortals Alliance all withdrew. Such a secret is too horrifying. If they didn't leave, they would probably only end up being silenced."

Although he was being controlled, the Eastern Extremity's instincts seemed to still be there.

Facing the crimson flames erupted by Tianyang who was also at the Longevity Realm in battle power, the long hair behind him suddenly lengthened. It transformed into giant dark beasts, extinguishing the flames.

Tianyang did not hesitate at all. With a fluctuation in space, he instantly appeared beside the Eastern Extremity.

In front of the Primordial Giant's figure, the Eastern Extremity looked like an ant.

The scarlet flames burning on Tianyang's body could almost scorch the surrounding space into distortion. However, the Eastern Extremity was not affected at all.

As if he existed independently from this space itself.

His lips moved slightly, as if saying something. A large chunk of Tianyang's body suddenly collapsed without any sign. The puppet body shook violently inevitably.

Tianyang let out an angry roar, his body restoring to its original state.

Then with a bang, he turned into countless starfires, scattering and surging towards the black-haired Eastern Extremity.

The crimson fires were like dragons, burning everything.

The countless black hair monsters differentiated from the Eastern Extremity's body also caught on fire. The flames spread towards the main body.

At this moment, the Eastern Extremity's pitch-black eyes moved slightly.

Those black hair monsters that had already turned into flying ashes actually reappeared from the flames.

Even if Tianyang's crimson fires burned them several times, they could always revive again.

If even the transformed black hairs could not be thoroughly injured, let alone the main body of the Eastern Extremity.

Although Tianyang was roaring, unwilling in his heart.

But this fact could not be changed.

"Tianyang only has the power of the Longevity Realm, but it was forcibly transformed through the essence of Source Power. He does not possess the true mystical powers of the Longevity Realm. When encountering Dao-harmonized cultivators, bullying them naturally has no pressure. But when facing a true Celestial Venerable of Longevity..."

"He's not a match at all." Li Fan shook his head slightly in secret.

"Moreover, this Celestial Venerable of the Eastern Extremity doesn't seem to be a weakling either. The oppressive feeling he gives me is even stronger than Senior Sister Zhao."

In the battle between the two Longevity Realm experts just now, Li Fan vaguely sensed the traces of the Dao of Heaven and Earth in operation.

Especially when those black hair monsters were reborn from the flames, it was particularly strong.

"Is this the Dao that the Eastern Extremity goes against?"

"Reversing the Dao of the development and change of things?"

"Is this the reason why the Dharma Sect took such a ruthless action against the Eastern Extremity?"

"If the Eastern Extremity had fully attained the Dao, he might directly overturn the rules set by the Dharma Sect in the Xuanhuang Realm..."

With Li Fan's experience of repeatedly peeping into the Heavenly Dao, his keen perception of the Dao of Heaven and Earth was almost unmatched by any Dao-harmonized cultivator.

Such a detailed observation at close range, the inferences obtained should not have any problems.
