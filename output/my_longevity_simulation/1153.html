Chapter 1151: The True Culprit Behind the Destruction of the Realm

"Could it be..."

"Eternal Life?"

This thought suddenly arose in Shang Shaojun's mind.

As far as he knew, among the survivors from other immortal realms, there were indeed some who had reached the Eternal Life realm.

Although they were not as invincible as the Eternal Life Celestial Venerables of the Xuanhuang Realm, they were indeed not opponents that ordinary Harmonious Dao cultivators could deal with.

"It should be the case." Shang Shaojun's heart trembled, and he became even more cautious towards Li Fan.

The two continued to delve deeper into the Chita Abyss, as the degree of fusion between different world laws reached its peak.

In the darkness, new strange phenomena began to appear.

A bloody glow illuminated the way ahead.

Shattered continents were suspended and floating above their heads.

From a distance, one could vaguely see the ruins that covered the land, and the dense white bones within the ruins.

All the floating world fragments were deathly silent.

Thick, root-like red threads grew out from these fragmented continents, extending into the Chita Abyss and hiding in the darkness.

From time to time, hair-raising sounds would echo in the space.

It was like a metal blade scraping across the surface of bones, or the heavy breathing of a dying person.

The eerie atmosphere of this place caused Li Fan and his companion, who had come to explore the secrets, to stop in their tracks simultaneously.

Seeing that Li Fan seemed to be carefully observing the strange scene ahead, Shang Shaojun pondered for a moment and then also transmitted his thoughts: "These shattered worlds seem to be still alive?" He was a bit uncertain.

The Holy Fetus Li Fan did not respond.

He simply waved his hand, sending out a sword light that flew straight towards one of the shattered worlds.

Seeing this, Shang Shaojun's heart tightened, and he immediately became alert.

Although he had witnessed how extraordinary Li Fan's swordsmanship was, the backlash from a world, even a dying one, was not to be underestimated.

The silvery-white sword light instantly crossed the space and arrived above that shattered continent.

What surprised Shang Shaojun a little was that the sword strike Li Fan unleashed did not seem to be intended for destruction.

After piercing through the barrier and descending, it remained motionless.

And the trail it left in the air as it came did not fade away either.

The sword light condensed, seemingly forming a silver thread.

It connected Li Fan with that shattered world.

Li Fan's figure floated, standing on the silver thread, eyes closed as if comprehending something.

"This is..."

But as he watched, for some reason, the scene of a mortal physician taking a pulse to treat an illness flashed in his mind.

"Using the sword as a guide to discern life and death."

"Could it be that this person not only has superb swordsmanship but also has profound attainments in the Dao of [Medicine]?"

Shang Shaojun was shocked.

And Li Fan's next action made his heart pound even more.

He saw Li Fan lightly stomp on the silver thread, and a ripple spread along the thread into the shattered world.

At first, it was an inconspicuous slight tremor.

Then, as if triggering some resonance, the tremors became more and more intense. The land, which had been completely silent, suddenly awakened.

Rumbling sounds came from underground, and countless lava erupted with the world's roar.

...

At this moment, Li Fan lightly snapped his fingers, severing the sword light under his feet.

Then he retreated far back in the direction they had come from.

"Get farther away."

Before Li Fan could finish his words, Shang Shaojun felt himself being hit by a huge force, his figure out of control...

The figure flew backwards from the impact.

His internal qi and blood were boiling, as if he had already suffered minor injuries.

Shang Shaojun turned his head in shock, only to discover that the world "diagnosed" by Li Fan had already entered a destructive end amidst intense self-collision.

It was originally just a fragment, and the world was extremely fragile.

It simply could not withstand such violent energy fluctuations within itself.

The earth completely disintegrated, turning into endless crimson flames.

While flashing its final light in the darkness, it also represented the passing of a world.

Shang Shaojun watched this scene, lost in thought for a moment.

Li Fan, by his side, explained: "If this world was destroyed by an external force, not only would it cause the world itself to retaliate, but it might also alert the existence behind all of this."

"But if the world's remaining power is forcibly stimulated, causing it to die naturally..."

As Li Fan spoke, thousands of sword phantoms appeared behind him once again.

"Enter the formation."

Hearing Li Fan's transmitted voice, Shang Shaojun's heart jumped again.

However, he had already been shocked to the point of numbness, and just subconsciously followed.

Upon entering the formation space, Shang Shaojun saw the sword shadows flying and circling around rapidly.

A thousand swords flew together, creating a very concealed hiding spot.

Before hiding in this sword formation, Shang Shaojun also saw Li Fan destroying several broken worlds ahead in the same manner.

The terrifying impact released by the destruction of the worlds made this deepest part of the Abyss instantly resemble purgatory.

Countless dark red ashes, carried by the raging energy, spewed out towards the outside of the abyss.

Like ten thousand ghosts marching at night, like a beast stampede.

The formation of a thousand swords seemed to be struggling a bit.

Constantly shrinking.

The countless sword lights were also rotating faster and faster, almost forming rings of lines.

But Li Fan didn't seem worried about the formation not holding up.

Eyes closed, standing straight, not knowing what he was doing.

"Wait."

As if seeing Shang Shaojun's confusion, Li Fan simply transmitted a sentence, then spoke no more.

Shang Shaojun took a deep breath, and quickly became tranquil under the boost of the cool air.

The surrounding sword lights were dancing, not only blocking the outside scenery.

It even blocked his divine sense, unable to clearly see what was happening outside.

But judging from the booming sounds of the sword formation being hit by huge objects from time to time, the destruction of several world fragments in a row must have had an extremely terrifying impact on the outside.

"If it weren't for this person, just this impact alone would have forced me to retreat in a sorry state."

"Swordsmanship, medical skills, formations... how can there still be someone in this world who is proficient in all methods?"

Shang Shaojun's thoughts were surging. Even with the boost of the Immortal Heart Mantra, he could not completely suppress his speculations about Li Fan's identity.

I don't know how long it took.

The storm outside the sword formation finally slowly subsided.

But Li Fan still didn't seem to have any intention of undoing the sword formation.

And judging from his increasingly solemn expression, it seemed that there was still some existence outside that was even more terrifying than the destruction of the worlds.

"The mastermind behind the scenes?"

Shang Shaojun's mind recalled the horrifying scene of countless world fragments hanging overhead.

This was obviously not something that could form naturally.

Thinking of the overbearing existence that could treat worlds as playthings, and perhaps was now appearing not far away, Shang Shaojun couldn't help but feel his scalp go numb.

"I don't know if this sword formation can hide us completely?"

"If we're discovered by It, it will definitely be a dead end!"
