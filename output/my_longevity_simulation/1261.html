The handwriting was extremely sloppy, with large sections of scribbles and deletion marks.

However, Sun Erlang could still grasp the general idea.

The Celestial Dynasty ruled by the Taoist Emperor Tai Xuan was growing increasingly powerful, with talented individuals emerging one after another. In the struggles against the other two nations, they emerged victorious time and again.

The Master of Heaven and Earth and the Sage of the Human Realm had no choice but to join hands to resist the might of Tai Xuan...

This plot was clearly not what the writer truly wanted to write about.

Not only was the writing style and wording much weaker compared to before, even the attitude towards writing became perfunctory. Perhaps unable to bear it any longer, the writer was unwilling to continue writing before reaching the ending. He spent his days drinking and living in a daze.

Sun Erlang was not surprised by this outcome.

In the Great Awakening of the Holy Dynasty, the symbols on Taoist Emperor Tai Xuan and the Faceless Sacred Emperor were too similar. If he suffered even a little setback in the plot, the writer would be criticized by thousands. Under such real-life pressure, Taoist Emperor Tai Xuan naturally had to enjoy smooth sailing in the plot. As this continued, his strength surpassed the other two sages by a large margin.

However, Sun Erlang was a bit curious about what kind of plot development the writer originally had in mind.

With a thought, Sun Erlang sent out a thread of divine sense, probing into the writer's sea of consciousness.

Before long, amidst the chaotic thoughts, he found the content related to "The Romance of the Three Realms of Profound and Yellow".

It had to be said that the writer, after all, was a native of the Holy Dynasty. Having basked in the grace of the Sacred Emperor for many years, his subconscious reverence for the Sacred Emperor made the ending he envisioned still one where Taoist Emperor Tai Xuan overcame various difficulties and successively suppressed the other two victors, unifying the Profound Yellow Realm.

However, there were failures, setbacks, and even moments when he was on the verge of doom, his life hanging by a thread.

Sun Erlang had to admit that compared to the revised version he saw in reality, the original plot arrangement in the writer's mind was more ups and downs and gripping.

"As they say, writing is like viewing mountains - one does not enjoy flatness. In comparison, the outside version is indeed as bland as chewing on wax."

Although this was what Sun Erlang thought, the content of this book involved his master after all, so he dared not make decisions on his own.

In a flash, he returned to the Sacred Emperor's seat and reported the matter to Li Ping.

It was just a small matter, but for some reason, it made the Sacred Emperor silent for a long time.

"Taoist Emperor Tai Xuan is me, the Sage of the Human Realm is me, and the Master of Heaven and Earth is also me."

In the end, the Sacred Emperor slowly uttered a sentence that echoed in the cold Sacred Emperor's seat.

Sun Erlang was stunned at first, but then he suddenly understood.

He left with a delighted expression.

However, Li Ping's state of mind, which was usually difficult to shake by ordinary things, rarely rippled with waves that could not be calmed because of this matter.

He might have understood a little why Chuan Fa had stopped concerning himself with worldly affairs and disappeared from the public eye after establishing the League of Ten Thousand Immortals.

Hongze Realm.

It seemed that some uninvited guests from the League of Ten Thousand Immortals had intruded into this small world.

"Three Nascent Souls, seven Golden Cores."

Zhang Ming's brows furrowed tightly.

"This Hongze Realm is not big, and the aura carried by the Kongming Flowing Crystals is very unique. Even though I have formation talismans to conceal it slightly, I'm afraid it will be difficult to escape the investigation of these people."

"It seems that this battle is inevitable."

Zhang Ming quickly calculated the forces he could mobilize at present.

Including himself, there were only five Golden Cores at most.

It could be said that the disparity in strength was huge, and defeat was certain.

If he chose to retreat, the Holy Dynasty would not impose a very severe punishment.

However, the pride of being a soldier of the Great Awakening made Zhang Ming determined not to directly withdraw without even having the courage to fight.

Zhang Ming was very decisive and directly unleashed the only [Concealing Heaven Talisman].

As the name suggests, the Concealing Heaven Talisman could conceal the Heavenly Dao.

It was well known that when a cultivator in the Profound Yellow Realm fell, there would be anomalies descending in the sky. The fallen cultivator's life, and even the specific manner of death, would be vividly displayed in the sky.

Under normal circumstances, it was fine, but if it was a life-and-death battle like today, any casualty on either side would instantly spread. It greatly increased the difficulty of combat.

However, with the [Concealing Heaven Talisman], the situation could be reversed and even turned into favorable combat conditions.

The Concealing Heaven Talisman could not only conceal the anomalies of cultivators' deaths but also accurately identify friend and foe and treat them differently.

If our side killed the enemy cultivators, no anomalies would occur. But if our cultivators were killed, anomalies would still descend and alert us.

Although the specific principle of how this talisman worked was unknown, there was no doubt that this single Concealing Heaven Talisman alone could turn the tide of victory.

"Relying on this talisman alone is not enough."

Zhang Ming looked at a white jade arrow in his bosom.

"Unfortunately, with our team's qualifications, we can only apply for one Annihilating Army Arrow each time we go out."

"If we could get more, let alone these Nascent Soul cultivators, I wouldn't be afraid even if they were Transformed Spirit."

The Annihilating Army Arrow was a weakened version of the Holy Dynasty's Patrolling Heaven Arrow.

Although it was weakened, it could still cause devastating damage to Nascent Soul realm cultivators and those below.

Moreover, it had built-in tracking and aura locking functions. Once targeted, there was no chance of survival.

"With only this one arrow, it needs to be utilized to the fullest."

Soon, Zhang Ming had an idea in mind.

"You use the [Rushing Flow Talisman] to transport these Kongming Flowing Crystals to the underground here." Zhang Ming marked the specific location on the map.

Fu Ge did not hesitate at all and collected the storage ring handed over by Zhang Ming.

With a flash of yellow light on his body, he disappeared from the ground.

The storage ring contained all the crystals Zhang Ming and the others had mined, which was only a small part of the giant ore vein here.

However, if it were to be detonated and specially guided, the diffused aura would be enough to attract the attention of the League of Ten Thousand Immortals cultivators on the ground.

A middle-aged man with an earth-yellow complexion responded and left.

However, even though these few people all possessed unique skills, they still felt a bit stretched in the face of the absolute power gap.

The plan encountered an unexpected situation in the first step.

Those few League of Ten Thousand Immortals cultivators were not ordinary either. The person responsible for luring them to the ambush location was named Qiu Di.
