Chapter 589

During this time, Ruò Mù did consider fighting back.

The trees trembled, giving birth to numerous spirits and monsters that fiercely attacked the cultivators.

Even Ruò Mù itself threw out branches like swords to pierce towards the cultivators.

However, the cultivators who came to this place were truly terrifying in their cultivation level.

All resistance was futile and completely suppressed in an instant.

Even Ruò Mù's final counterattack, which left it wounded as well, caused all the purple-gold fruits on the tree's crown to wither completely.

They were all saved by this group of people.

Powerless to resist, Ruò Mù could only watch as its life essence was ruthlessly drained.

The leaves slowly turned yellow and fell like rain.

Amidst thunderous sounds, many tree trunks and branches cracked and broke.

Cracks began to appear on Ruò Mù's trunk.

Yet, this group of people showed no intention of stopping.

They seemed determined not to stop until Ruò Mù was completely drained.

Fortunately, at a critical moment, it seemed that another group of cultivators arrived.

A melee broke out between the two sides, and the draining actions were forced to cease.

They successfully retrieved the green light clusters condensed in the air and, as they retreated, did not forget to launch a joint attack on the already heavily damaged Ruò Mù.

The damage from this attack seemed even greater than the forced draining of its life force moments ago.

Large fissures extended from the base of the tree trunk upward, and masses of branches fell, their destination unknown.

Seeing this, the rescuing cultivators also gave up the chase and hurried to heal Ruò Mù.

Through their efforts, they managed to prevent Ruò Mù from meeting a complete demise.

But the damage it suffered was too great to reverse.

From then on, withered and aging became its constant companions.

Until several years later, a humanoid cultivator emerged from within Ruò Mù.

...

The scenes seen in Jìan Rén Shòu were only fragmented images, but they contained a vast amount of information.

Li Fan watched and replayed them repeatedly through the Transcendental Stone.

Combined with the information he had gathered before, he slowly formed a conjecture.

"The group of cultivators who harmed Ruò Mù is probably those who advocated abandoning the Xuán Huáng border and fleeing during the great catastrophe that occurred back then."

"Judging by the power they displayed, there are undoubtedly experts in the Eternal Life Realm among them."

"Five hundred years ago, for some reason, this group of people sought to return to the Xuán Huáng border, leading to a great war."

"Sī Mǎ Cháng Kōng once said that both heaven and earth exhibited extreme hatred towards these cultivators, surpassing even us, who can plunder the heavens and earth."

"Traitors, what did they steal..."

"Could it be this Ruò Mù that can produce the Longevity Fruit?"

"Hmm... judging by the methods of these people, there might be some other things as well."

"Thousands of years have passed and they've returned. Did they ultimately fail to escape the Immortal Ruins? Or is there another reason?"

Li Fan furrowed his brow.

The existence of the Immortal Ruins was not a secret for the Ten Sects of Immortality.

The ruthlessness with which this group acted, leaving no room for escape, clearly showed their capability to successfully flee the Xuán Huáng border.

But it was unclear what incident forced them to return.

The known information was too scant; for now, he chose not to dwell on it.

Regardless, Li Fan finally understood why Ruò Mù harbored such hatred towards cultivators.

Whether it was being plundered recklessly by humans or the near-death encounter later on, the animosity between it and the cultivators could be described as unending.

If Li Fan were treated in such a manner, his retaliation methods would probably be far more ruthless.

"After the Supreme Transcendent disseminated new doctrines, the Longevity Fruit lost its power to grant longevity."

"For Ruò Mù, this might be a good thing."

"In a sense, the dissemination of the doctrines could be considered its savior."

"Judging by its attempt to reverse the 'hurt the same kind' principle, it seems that it hasn't attained the Eternal Life Realm yet. However, for a creature of its kind, with an indefinite lifespan and unimaginable abilities, power cannot be simply measured by the Unity Realm."

"Is it unable to reach the Eternal Life Realm beyond the Unity Realm..."

"The gap between Unity and Eternal Life is indeed quite vast."

Li Fan saw the Celestial Physician, the Fisherman, and this Decaying Wood in this realm.

Although it was just a coincidence, it also illustrated the difficulty of defying the laws of nature and attaining eternal life.

Though there were many heroes and talents in the world, the new doctrines of Eternal Life had only a few proponents.

Li Fan's eyes narrowed as he fell into contemplation.

Having concluded his harvest, Li Fan began to reflect.

"Being seen by Decaying Wood this time and nearly losing my life, it was truly bad luck overall."

"If I boast after breaking through a realm, I might become complacent... But that's not the case."

"This is Heaven Spirit Zhou City; it's a branch leased by the Doctrine Tower. Safety is guaranteed."

"Who would have thought that a cultivator engaging in illicit activities, selling Golden Pills, would turn out to be a powerful being with ill intentions towards humans?"

Thinking of this, Li Fan felt somewhat helpless.

The real him couldn't always hide. Relying solely on his avatars for action had limitations, especially because avatars were just avatars and couldn't fully utilize various supernatural abilities.

With a cultivation level one minor realm lower than his main self, pursuing Eternal Life through him was like daydreaming.

"It's like being struck by lightning while walking on the street. A completely improbable event; one shouldn't be paralyzed by fear."

"Many things can be done only by my real self without leaving a trace."

"However, the ability of Decaying Wood Daoist to distort and alter one's thoughts needs to be countered."

"Perhaps it felt my hatred towards cultivators and foresaw my future actions, intending to utilize me for revenge against cultivators before setting me free."

"I might have been lost in the treetops for who knows how long if I hadn't been awakened."

"I believe my resolve is firm enough, yet I still seem defenseless..."

"But in the world of cultivation, there are countless miraculous items and techniques, so I will surely find a way to counter it."

"I don't seek complete resistance; just keeping a trace of clarity will make up for my shortcomings."

Li Fan firmly remembered this matter and placed it at the forefront of his thoughts.

He intended to focus on exploring further information in this aspect in the days to come.

After a long time had passed and his mind had settled, Li Fan finally picked up the withered branches of Ruò Mù that had been placed on the table all along.

As he gently touched them, Li Fan suddenly felt a hint of familiarity.

Not because he had come into close contact with the body of Ruò Mù before, but because...
