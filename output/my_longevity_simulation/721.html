Time passed hurriedly in this process.

Huangfu and Dongfang had already exchanged for the Dissolving Disc and the Devouring Element Sacred Body, redirecting their goals towards other treasures.

And the accumulation of Azure Profound Points was getting closer to a hundred thousand.

Considering all the realms they had explored, including the initial Life-and-Death Realm, they had reached the number thirteen.

As they became more skillful in exploration, Li Fan also summarized some rules about the existence of the "realms."

Based on the conclusion that "the current Xuanhuang Realm is a fusion of many ancient Xuanhuang Realms with other worlds," Li Fan discovered that the so-called realms might be "fragments" that fell off from the original worlds as they merged and collided during the evolution of the worlds.

The realms were extremely mysterious, often possessing their own unique rules, which was very similar to the "strangeness" in the Xuanhuang Realm.

Comparing the attire and architectural styles of the dead cultivators in the realms, Li Fan found that these realms evidently had different origins.

"The world-level evolution is happening in the collision, and the White Mist is its buffer zone..."

After exploring another realm, Li Fan had a realization.

This might also explain why there was such vast space in the White Mist, as it originally represented the distance between worlds.

"D*mn it, it's getting harder to find undiscovered realms now."

For several days, Puxian Zhenzhou had been flying in the White Mist, passing through several realms, but the Tianjian Mirror had already recorded tips that were on file. Dongfang Yao couldn't help cursing.

Huangfu Song absentmindedly played with the Dissolving Disc in his hand and said, "It's normal. It's been almost a year since the Immortal Alliance issued the exploration order. With the number of Immortal Alliance cultivators, the surface part of the White Mist should have been explored quite thoroughly by now. If we want to make more discoveries, we must continue to delve deeper..."

"Boom!" Before Huangfu Song finished speaking, a loud bang was suddenly heard.

As if something had fallen from above and violently shook Puxian Zhenzhou for a moment.

Subconsciously putting away the Dissolving Disc in his hand, Huangfu Song scanned with his divine sense and showed a strange expression.

Because what collided with Puxian Zhenzhou in flight was the corpse of a cultivator!

Moreover, judging from the clothes the body was wearing, it didn't come from a realm but from the Ten Thousand Immortals Alliance!

The three on the ship instantly looked serious, thinking that perhaps there had been an accident resulting in the death of an Immortal Alliance cultivator. They immediately became vigilant.

But what happened next made Li Fan and the others couldn't help but show shocked expressions.

"Boom!"

"Boom!"

First it was one or two sounds, then it became more and more frequent.

The continuous collision sounds were heard, and Puxian Zhenzhou was constantly shaking, and its protective light barrier seemed to become precarious in an instant.

While Huangfu Song reinforced it with spells, he looked at the scene outside with a pale face.

Overhead, the sky was densely covered with countless dead cultivation bodies falling like raindrops.

Some bodies were relatively intact, while others were already fragmented, with only limbs remaining.

However, without exception, there seemed to be a twisted, squirming black thread trace around the edges of their bodies.

Occasionally glimpsing their faces, they were not as hideous and terrifying as those seen in the realms, but instead had closed eyes and calm expressions.

The rain of bodies continued endlessly, as if it would never stop.

If they forcefully rushed out of the range covered by the rain of bodies, Puxian Zhenzhou might be damaged.

The three stopped the flying boat and used their hands to intercept the falling bodies attacking from above.

Although they were lifeless beings and not very threatening, this eerie scene still sent a shiver down their spines.
