Here is the full English translation of the excerpt:

However, she did not directly make a move.

Her big eyes turned slightly as she said "Please!", and then stood still on the spot.

For some reason, Shuo Feng also did not choose to act.

Instead, he also stood motionless in place, not moving for a long time.

Zhang Haobo looked at the two suspiciously, not knowing what they were up to.

After a long while, a trace of embarrassment appeared on Shuo Feng's face: "Little sister, didn't you say you wanted to fight me? Why aren't you making a move?"

Su Xiaomei replied seriously: "My elder brother often teaches me that when walking outside, one must respect the elderly and love the young. You are so much older than me, so I must definitely let you make the first move before I counterattack!"

Shuo Feng seemed to be stunned by Su Xiaomei's words.

Then he said solemnly: "I already know your intention. However, I can barely be considered half your elder, so of course I have to yield a bit to the younger generation."

"You should still make the first move!"

"No no no, you go first..."

"You first!"

...

Zhang Haobo watched this bizarre scene of the two mutually yielding in front of him, and his expression couldn't help but turn strange.

"This person called Shuo Feng..."

Thinking of how he had repeatedly provoked Shen Yuruo before, and the trace of disappointment that appeared on his face after seeing that she still did not make a move.

Zhang Haobo couldn't help but realize something.

"Alright, it seems little sister you figured it out. The cultivation technique I practice is a bit special." After a while, seeing that the two were still at an impasse, Shuo Feng did not hide it.

He stated frankly: "If I don't receive an attack from someone else, I have no way to make the first move. So, please little sister, you go first!"

Su Xiaomei had a look of "as expected", appearing a bit smug.

But immediately after, she became solemn.

A layer of faint red flames appeared on her body, actually using her foundational stage magic item [Spirit Fury Fire] right from the start.

The air distorted for an instant, and Su Xiaomei disappeared from her original spot.

And beside Shuo Feng, at the same time, six identical figures appeared.

They all had Su Xiaomei's appearance, but the expressions on their faces were each different.

Joy, anger, sorrow, fear, pensiveness, worry.

Along with that, the flames on their bodies were all different colors.

The six avatars surrounded Shuo Feng, using fists, palms, fingers, legs, elbows, shoulders, each unleashing close combat techniques.

Accompanied by colorful flames, they were about to strike Shuo Feng's body.

"Flashy and fancy!"

Shuo Feng's voice rang out at this moment.

A flash of white light.

Drawing several folded lines in the air.

"Bang!"

It was unknown what happened, but in an instant, all six of Su Xiaomei's avatars were struck.

They paused in midair for a moment, then exploded with bursts of air, sent flying out at a horrifying speed.

"Just like this..."

Before Shuo Feng could finish speaking, those six flying bodies suddenly disappeared.

Reappearing beside Shuo Feng.

Moreover, their speed was unchanged.

Only the direction changed to charging at Shuo Feng.

Another flash of white light.

The six flying bodies were sent flying again.

Even faster than before.

But it was useless.

Just moments later, Su Xiaomei's flying bodies, carrying boiling flames, returned to approach Shuo Feng at an even greater speed.

What greeted them was still a dazzling white light.

Sent flying again.

...

The speed at which Su Xiaomei's avatars attacked became faster and faster.

And the flashing of that white light from Shuo Feng also became more and more frequent.

At this time, Zhang Haobo, who had been observing the battle for a long time, finally saw through it.

That white light was a sword.

A wooden sword.

Every time the white light flashed, the wooden sword would appear.

Then Shuo Feng would use the flat of the blade to lightly pat Su Xiaomei's body.

"This kind of speed..." Zhang Haobo was extremely shocked in his heart.

Seeing Shuo Feng's relaxed appearance, it was clear he was not using his full strength.

Even so, the strength he displayed was extremely terrifying.

Zhang Haobo admitted to himself that if he switched places with Su Xiaomei at this moment, he would likely be defeated within these few short exchanges.

"However, little sister also doesn't seem to be using her full power..."

In the center of the battlefield, Shuo Feng seemed to have tired of this back-and-forth game.

"Not bad. But it ends here!"

His expression turned solemn, and this time when he made a move, he used the edge of his sword.

The white light flashed, instantly sweeping across the six avatars in succession.

Countless silver lines exploded from within the avatars' bodies.

In an instant, the avatars were cut into countless pieces.

Scattered all over the ground.

However, the expressions of both Zhang Haobo and Shuo Feng did not change at all.

"Hehe!"

"Wuwu!"

"Ah!"

...

As if each had its own consciousness, they squirmed on the ground, moving closer to each other.

Moments later, they reassembled into six avatars again.

After "reviving", their individual expressions seemed to become even richer.

"Interesting." Surrounded and attacked again, the expression on Shuo Feng's face finally turned a bit serious.

Six identical wooden swords instantly appeared around him.

Each facing one of Su Xiaomei's six avatars.

While blocking the avatars, Shuo Feng swept his gaze around, as if searching for something.

A moment later, a smile appeared on his face.

The next instant, Shuo Feng appeared six zhang to the left, in a seemingly empty spot.

He gripped another wooden sword in his hand, lightly waving it forward.

A hidden figure was suddenly struck out by Shuo Feng.

However, in the next second, the smile on Shuo Feng's face disappeared.

Because the expression on this hidden figure's face was full of worry.

It was not the real body, but the avatar of worry!

With one sword shattering the avatar, Shuo Feng looked back at where the six avatars were before.

He saw Su Xiaomei's real body mischievously winking at him.

Shuo Feng's face was expressionless as six wooden swords slashed, instantly cutting the six bodies in half.

"Where else can you escape to?"

Shuo Feng looked at the fragments of the total seven figures, his eyes narrowing slightly.

"Escape? From the beginning, I've been right here."

As if responding to his words, Su Xiaomei's voice rang out behind Shuo Feng.

Her eyes turned crimson red, and the fragments all over the battlefield suddenly disappeared, returning to her real body.

Six different expressions rapidly changed on her face.

Finally settling on anger.

The redness in her eyes spread, dyeing her hair red.

Eight times the power suddenly erupted.

Su Xiaomei fiercely punched Shuo Feng's body!

However...

A light flashed across the blue cloth robes on Shuo Feng's body.

He himself did not move an inch.

Only the corner of his robe fluttered.

Shuo Feng's expression did not change in the slightest.

Only, in his eyes, a sword shadow suddenly appeared.

Right at this moment, Su Xiaomei sighed repeatedly: "No more fighting! No fun!"

Shuo Feng's movement paused slightly.

Instead, he turned his head to look in the direction of Stone Details Mountain where they came from.

The world seemed to brighten a bit in an instant.

A sword light flew into the distance.

"Boom!"

As if colliding with something.

The sound of rumbling and muffled groans faintly transmitted.

Looking at the few peach blossom petals slowly falling in the distance, Shuo Feng snorted coldly and closed his eyes to retract his sword.

...

In the Heavenly Mystery Mirror, Li Fan, who had taken in this entire battle, couldn't help but praise.

"Good swordsmanship!"
