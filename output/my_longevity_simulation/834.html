Chapter 834

At the critical moment, Ou Shangtian erupted once again. A brilliant yellow light enveloped an area of tens of thousands of meters.

The cultivators affected by it were all returned to the state before they surrounded Ou Shangtian.

By the time they reacted, Ou Shangtian and his son had already disappeared without a trace.

Furious, they continued the pursuit, only to find that only Ou Shangtian was left fleeing.

After resisting for a while, he was finally captured by a lucky fellow named Xie Xiantao.

Faced with the covetous gazes of other cultivators, Xie Xiantao made a wise move without hesitation.

That was to hand over Ou Shangtian to the Wanxian Alliance in exchange for a reward.

In fact, during the escape, Ou Shangtian had killed no less than a hundred cultivators from the Immortal Alliance.

Although it was forced self-defense, he had violated the laws of the Immortal Alliance after all.

Ou Shangtian was already a major criminal in the eyes of the Immortal Alliance.

Previously, the authorities had not intervened, but that didn't mean they weren't paying attention to the matter.

Sure enough, shortly after Xie Xiantao publicly announced his choice,

a team of black-clothed cultivators arrived at the scene and took him and the dying Ou Shangtian away.

The sensational nationwide manhunt event was also declared to have come to an end.

As for the other protagonist of the event, Ou Shangtian's father, Ou Daozi,

no one cared about him anymore.

Except for Li Fan.

In a gloomy jungle in Luoyan Province,

Ou Daozi hid under layers of dead leaves, seriously injured.

But when he thought of his son exhausting his own abilities to protect him and lure away the enemies,

his heart ached and he was in agony.

"Wanxian Alliance..."

Ou Daozi's eyes were red, and the faces of those who participated in the pursuit kept flashing in his mind.

His heart was filled with resentment.

But at this moment, he dared not move, for fear of being discovered.

Quietly nursing his wounds.

"Sigh..."

Just then, a sigh suddenly came.

Ou Daozi was startled and wanted to escape, but it was too late.

"It's you!" Seeing Li Fan, the culprit, Ou Daozi was furious.

He was about to go berserk and perish together with Li Fan.

But he was suppressed by the joint efforts of Lin Ling and Tian Yang.

He was already weaker in strength, and now he was severely injured and had lost most of his combat power.

Before long, he was completely subdued.

"Strange, why are you looking at me like that?"

"I originally just wanted to ask you to help design a building, but before I even met you and your son, you hurriedly ran away. You even used tricks to take my life. If I weren't lucky, I would have died on the spot."

"I haven't even settled the score with you yet."

"Besides, it was all the people from the Wanxian Alliance who hunted down and captured your son alive. What does it have to do with me?"

Li Fan said innocently.

"Preposterous!" Seeing Li Fan talking nonsense in broad daylight, Ou Daozi was already furious to the extreme.

The blood vessels on his forehead bulged and kept throbbing.

It seemed that he was going to fight Li Fan to the death on the spot.

"Your thinking is wrong." Li Fan shook his head slightly.

He took out a crystal clear fruit and showed a kind smile.

"Come on, open your mouth. Eat this and you'll understand."

Ou Daozi's heart was suddenly filled with an ominous premonition: "What is this thing?"

Li Fan didn't answer either.

He handed the Zhenshi Temptation Fruit to Lin Ling and forced-

Xing fed the fruit to Ou Daozi.

As the fruit entered his stomach, Ou Daozi's struggles gradually weakened.

The look in his eyes towards Li Fan also began to change.

"Don't you want to save your son?"

"Although companion wonders usually cannot be transferred, the Wanxian Alliance has many methods. Who knows what suffering your son might endure." Li Fan whispered.

"Wanxian Alliance!" Ou Daozi's eyes instantly turned bloodshot with agitation.

"Our Wanjie United Association exists precisely for this purpose!"

...

Under the influence of the Fruit of True Temptation, Ou Daozi's mind was quickly rewritten.

He joined the Wanjie United Association with the goal of destroying the Wanxian Alliance.

He began to treat like-minded individuals such as Li Fan as his own kin.

From Ou Daozi's mouth, Li Fan also learned the secret of Ou Shangtian's companion wonders.

"My son has two kinds of companion wonders."

"One I knew about since he was young - the [Book of Ten Thousand Deaths] that can predict the manner of one's future death."

"As for the [Kunqian Bone Hands] that can reverse heaven and earth, that was awakened two years ago when Guiyuan Mountain collapsed."

"At that time, he had just entered the Qi Refining stage, but suddenly encountered a natural disaster like a mountain range collapsing. His life hung by a thread."

"At the critical juncture between life and death, his potential erupted. Only then did he activate the previously hidden ability of the [Kunqian Bone Hands]."

"The collapsed mountain reappeared. This was truly too shocking. So this kid actually kept it hidden even from me."

"Until recently, he hurriedly found me and urged me to leave. He said he foresaw an extremely terrifying group of people who would soon come knocking..."

Along the way to the Lingmu Realm, Ou Daozi recounted the whole story to Li Fan.

Li Fan's expression remained normal: "Countless futures, countless possibilities. What he foresaw may not necessarily be true."

"If I really wanted to take action, just relying on the two beside me, you would be powerless to resist."

Ou Daozi's eyes showed a trace of confusion, and then he let out a long sigh: "A misunderstanding actually led to me and my son being unable to meet again..."

"There will always be opportunities. Fellow Daoist, what do you think of my Lingmu Realm?" Li Fan changed the subject, pointing to the small world under intense construction.

"Tsk tsk tsk..." Ou Daozi observed for a while, shaking his head repeatedly, a hint of disdain in his eyes.

"The location is good. But the design and construction plans are really subpar..."

"It doesn't quite match the reputation of the Wanjie United Association."

Hearing this, Li Fan didn't show any anger.

Instead, he cupped his hands and said, "Then I'll have to trouble Fellow Daoist to work hard and transform it."

Ou Daozi narrowed his eyes and nodded in agreement.

Already quite obsessed with this path, very quickly, Ou Daozi immersed himself in his own plans and visions.

"Fellow Daoist, feel free to do as you wish. If you need anything, just tell the leader of the treemen here directly."

With Li Fan's approval, Ou Daozi temporarily forgot his earlier grief.

He threw himself wholeheartedly into the construction of the Lingmu Realm.

"Although it took some twists and turns, fortunately the initial goal has been achieved."

"Kunqian Bone Hands, Book of Ten Thousand Deaths..."

Li Fan furrowed his brows slightly.

According to Ou Daozi, Ou Shangtian had not actually fully mastered the abilities of the Kunqian Bone Hands.

During his escape, he seemed to sense that there were still powerful abilities hidden within that had not been tapped.

If given a bit more time, perhaps he could really achieve time reversal to a certain degree.

However, this was just Ou Shangtian's guess.

The specifics were still unconfirmed.
