Chapter 1077

"Since the creation of the new law by the Heavenly Venerate, no new Xuan Huang Heavenly Venerate has been born. So what exactly will happen..."

"No one knows."

The Dharma Transmitter Zhou said with a solemn expression.

Li Fan did not answer.

In his mind, the words of Mr. Bai "There is no immortal path ahead, the Dharma Transmitting Heavenly Venerate is at the top" surfaced again.

If his guess was correct, the reason why Mr. Bai, with a mortal body, ascended to heaven in one step was because he attained the [Xuan Huang Heavenly Venerate].

However, he used his own life as the price to reverse the principle of "mortals cannot kill immortals".

Creating the mortal-immortal miasma.

This indirectly shows that even the Xuan Huang Heavenly Venerate does not have a good solution to the reversed laws of heaven and earth.

"If we consider the Xuan Huang Realm as some kind of exquisite creation, originally the Xuan Huang Heavenly Venerate had all the permissions of this creation."

"But because the Dharma Transmitter found a flaw in the existence of this creation. Reversing the laws of heaven and earth, he created a new permission out of thin air. Moreover, it can continuously erode the existing permissions of the Xuan Huang Realm."

"This has almost led to the current situation of the Xuan Huang Realm changing hands."

It's still the metaphor of comparing the Xuan Huang Realm to a prison house.

The existence that originally created the Xuan Huang Realm left a master control room in the prison.

Xuan Huang Heavenly Venerate is the code name for the person who can enter the master control room.

Inside the master control room, one can have a clear understanding of everything in the Xuan Huang Realm.

Even creating a Tian Zhi Qi representing a certain law with bare hands is an easy task.

However, one day an uninvited guest with superb skills suddenly arrived at the prison.

He actually cut off the control of the master control room over a certain part of the prison through some means. And subsequently took over the control.

Moreover, he also developed a large number of believers in the prison.

By directly changing the walls, the prison is being transformed. Gradually eroding it.

Naturally, the master control room also lost control over the transformed and newly built parts.

...

"At present, it seems that the current Xuan Huang Heavenly Venerate's power is also greatly diminished compared to before."

"At least it may not be a match for the Dharma Transmitter."

"But..."

Li Fan's eyes flashed.

He was clear about his own situation, and should not be able to meet the conditions to become the Xuan Huang Heavenly Venerate.

However, he could find someone who meets the conditions, like Xuan Tian Wang and Mr. Bai, who are extremely sincere and virtuous.

After the other party becomes the Xuan Huang Heavenly Venerate of the era, he will then promote them to fight against the Dharma Transmitter.

While thinking this way, Li Fan frowned and asked, "So the situation is so urgent, shouldn't we do something now?"

"If Mo Rubin really becomes the Xuan Huang Heavenly Venerate, things will be difficult to handle."

The Dharma Transmitter Zhou also showed a helpless expression, "It's not that we don't want to do anything, but there really isn't a good solution."

"Many of us have already tried, but we can't even pass the first hurdle to become the Xuan Huang Heavenly Venerate."

"Of course, if you are interested, you can try it yourself."

Li Fan narrowed his eyes slightly, "Oh?"

"It's still the ritual of entering the Fallen Immortal Realm. Just do it in reverse." said the Dharma Transmitter Zhou.

"Hmm?" Li Fan was a little surprised.

"Okay, I will give it a try." Having gathered all the latest information about Mo Rubin, the Immortal Puppet Li Fan took his leave.

In the secret room of the Lingmu Realm.

Li Fan took out the four essential items and placed them in the four corners.

Then he walked four steps in reverse, muttering something.

Dharma Transmission

Zhou said that the Immortal Alliance had already sent out nearly ten thousand cultivators to attempt it, but not a single person had succeeded.

Originally, Li Fan didn't have much hope for himself either.

However, to his surprise, after completing the reversal ritual, a familiar dizziness suddenly swept over him.

"I actually succeeded?"

Li Fan was greatly surprised.

Just as this thought appeared in his mind, his vision went black and he slowly lost consciousness.

"Junior Brother, Junior Brother?"

A somewhat familiar voice came.

"What are you thinking about? How come you've been in a daze for so long?"

Li Fan slowly regained his senses and looked around.

What he saw left him somewhat shocked.

A young cultivator wearing a purple and white Daoist robe, with a purple lightning mark engraved between his eyebrows, was looking this way.

He was extremely familiar to him!

Wasn't this the "Ning Yuan Picking the Longevity Fruit" that he encountered every time he entered the Fallen Immortal Realm?

"Am I back in the Fallen Immortal Realm again? No difference?"

"No, that's not right!"

Li Fan instantly understood.

"This is the test to become the Xuanhuang Great Celestial Venerate?"

"It's actually exactly the same as what I encountered when I entered the ordinary Fallen Immortal Realm?"

"What exactly..."

Even with Li Fan's current composure, after encountering this somewhat incredible event, he was still shocked in his heart and filled with disbelief.

If becoming the Xuanhuang Great Celestial Venerate was a once-in-a-lifetime, extremely important test.

Then the content of this test question was actually exactly the same as the mock exams he usually took!

How could this astonishing discovery not make him feel shocked?

From the first time he entered the Fallen Immortal Realm, Li Fan knew that what he experienced was different from all other cultivators.

But he didn't know why this bizarre phenomenon occurred.

However, now Li Fan already had a vague answer in his heart.

"Xuanhuang Great Celestial Venerate..."

"Xuanhuang Heart Cleansing Chant, Xuanhuang Heart Tempering Chant, Xuanhuang Immortal Heart Chant..."

"The Xuanhuang Heart Cleansing Chant is a method circulated among the people to remove the mortal-immortal miasma from the bodies of mortals."

"And the mortal-immortal miasma was left behind by Mr. Bai who went against the laws of heaven and earth!"

Li Fan's thoughts turned rapidly, and the image of Mr. Bai's face with a smile in his eyes and a kind expression suddenly appeared before him.

"That's right. Mr. Bai is, after all, compassionate."

"Although he reversed the laws of heaven and earth and created the mortal-immortal miasma, from a big picture perspective, it was to protect more mortals from being slaughtered by cultivators in the future. It might even be part of some world-saving plan."

"But it also cut off the path for mortals who wanted to cultivate immortality."

"So while creating the mortal-immortal miasma, he also left behind a way to break it for people."

"If one is determined to seek immortality, it's not impossible."

"Even someone like me, with extremely firm willpower..."

Li Fan frowned.

"Someone like me..."

"With utmost sincerity in seeking the Dao, to the point of disregarding everything else. In Mr. Bai's eyes, I'm probably not a good person at all."

"So..."

Li Fan thought of the "Reverse Tempering of the Dao Heart" required for the Immortal Heart Tempering Chant.

He also thought of the various scenes he encountered in the Fallen Immortal Realm after cultivating the Xuanhuang Heart Tempering Chant.

It seemed more like some kind of guidance.

"Ning Yuan Picking the Longevity Fruit. To achieve the perfect condition, one has to find a way to save all the mortals in the city."

"As for the second scene, the changes in the Beast Taming Sect, and the third scene, the sword tempering of the Heavenly Sword Sect..."

"To perfectly accomplish them, one probably also has to conform to the way the 'Xuanhuang Great Celestial Venerate' does things."

"Guiding me towards goodness? Or even if that's not possible, making me appear good through the reverse tempering of the Dao heart."
