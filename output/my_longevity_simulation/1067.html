Chapter 1065: The World-Destroying Black Tide Rises

In the end, it was the Myriad Immortal Alliance's misjudgment of the possible number of Dao-One Insects that might exist underground in Shilin Prefecture.

The underground caves, with their twists and turns, not only occupied an area almost equivalent to an entire Shilin Prefecture.

They also kept expanding deeper underground.

Even Li Fan, who now controlled the entire insect swarm, had not yet figured out how big the Hundred Stone Heavenly Cave in Shilin Prefecture really was.

Thanks to such a vast breeding ground, the Dao-One Insects were able to multiply so rapidly.

In less than a month, they could devour the spiritual energy of an entire prefecture.

"Although paper is thin and can be broken with a snap of the fingers, if stacked in large quantities, it can withstand thousands of troops and horses!"

"How can you kill them all by yourself?"

Feeling the number of Dao-One Insects that continued to increase rather than decrease even under the slaughter of the Golden Sword Immortal Venerable, Li Fan showed a strange smile.

...

"Huff huff..."

Looking at another emptied underground cave, the Golden Sword Immortal Venerable finally felt a hint of fatigue.

During this period, he had not rested for a moment. Thousands of golden swords crisscrossed, illuminating one dark underground cave after another, leaving behind a ground full of black insect corpses.

How many black insects had he slaughtered?

One billion? Ten billion?

Even with his Dao-Unifying Immortal Venerable's spiritual power reserves, they were now almost drained.

But it seemed that due to the depth underground, there were too many black insects here.

The spiritual energy was very thin and could not be absorbed to nourish himself.

Helplessly, the Golden Sword Immortal Venerable could only slowly recover by absorbing the spirit stones stored in his body.

"It's been many years since I've been in such a sorry state."

The Golden Sword Immortal Venerable, sitting on the ground, couldn't help but sigh.

In his mind, memories surfaced of exploring the sect's ruins with his companions back when he was still a Foundation Establishment cultivator.

Suddenly, he inexplicably stood up, his expression becoming solemn.

Because he finally realized what the inexplicable feeling in his heart meant since he set foot in this Hundred Stone Heavenly Cave.

It was the warning from his keen spiritual perception that had not appeared for a long time.

Three hundred years ago, it was precisely by relying on this keen intuition that he became the only survivor of that exploration team.

However, after surviving the great calamity, everything had been going smoothly and he had not encountered any misfortune. He had almost forgotten this sense of crisis.

Until today, after many years, this spiritual warning appeared again!

"This..."

His thoughts turned rapidly, and he made a decision.

He immediately disregarded rest, his figure turning into a golden light, speeding towards the depths of the underground caves.

He wanted to see first, just how big the scale of this Hundred Stone Heavenly Cave was!

Three days later.

The Golden Sword Immortal Venerable's face was deathly pale.

With his flying speed, three days was enough to circle the territory of the Myriad Immortal Alliance several times.

But he still had not finished exploring this Hundred Stone Heavenly Cave.

Like an abyss, like the sea, its depths could not be seen. It seemed to have no end at all.

The Golden Sword Immortal Venerable shuddered, and a terrifying image suddenly surged in his mind of the black insects densely packed in the caves, overflowing towards the outside world like surging seawater.

"Disaster, disaster."

"No, I must hurry and report the situation to the alliance headquarters."

The Golden Sword Immortal Venerable hastily took out a communication talisman, wanting to establish contact with the alliance headquarters.

But it seemed that due to being too deep underground, the communication could not be established.

The Golden Sword Immortal Venerable's heart was uneasy.

The premonition became stronger and stronger.

He sped up to the extreme, wanting to leave this underground cave.

But for some reason, those black bugs that were originally just quietly lying down and eating spiritual energy suddenly rushed towards him as if they had gone mad.

Trying to stop him from moving forward.

At this moment, Golden Sword Immortal finally understood what it meant to be difficult to take a single step.

Without any reservations, all the golden swords refined by his body were unsheathed and surrounded him on both sides.

All the black bugs blocking his way were crushed into powder.

Golden Sword Immortal, with an unstoppable momentum, quickly returned from underground.

The difference between the black bugs in front and behind made Golden Sword Immortal increasingly certain that there were traces of a conspiracy behind this.

If it was not stopped quickly, it might not only affect Shilin Prefecture. Perhaps the entire Wanxian Alliance and even the Xuanhuang Realm would suffer heavily!

"Who exactly is plotting this unrighteous thing!"

When he finally broke through the layers of obstruction from the black bugs and saw the light of day again.

Just as he was about to urgently report the situation to the Immortal Alliance headquarters, he suddenly felt something in his heart and stood still.

Golden Sword Immortal looked up at the sky and stretched out his left hand.

"The wind... is picking up?"

Between heaven and earth, a gust of wind inexplicably blew.

Then the breeze gradually grew stronger, becoming more and more intense.

The howling sound rose like thunder, rumbling.

In the blink of an eye, the blazing sun and clear sky were obscured by layers of dark clouds.

Under the shadow, at this moment, all the cultivators in Shilin Prefecture felt a sense of impending doom.

"Whoosh!"

A gust of demonic wind blew, so fierce that it made it a little difficult for Golden Sword Immortal to maintain his posture.

Although this demonic wind disappeared in an instant, the meaning behind it made this Immortal of the Harmonious Dao feel chilled to the bone.

There seemed to be a faint and dense sound of wind in his ears.

But Golden Sword did not sense any signs of wind.

Just as he was puzzled and confused, countless flying insects like a black tidal wave suddenly appeared from below.

Riding the wind, they surged out from the hundreds of stone caves.

Soaring to the ninth heaven, like a doomsday dark cloud, they spread unstoppably to various prefectures of the Xuanhuang Realm.

In the face of the immeasurable sea of black bugs, Golden Sword's protective spiritual power was also like a small tree in a storm, in imminent danger.

Feeling horrified, Golden Sword Immortal no longer had the thought of stopping the disaster.

He only tried his best to ensure his own safety.

The black bugs in the underground caves seemed endless. As far as Golden Sword could see, he could no longer see any other scenes.

Falling into the nightmare of the black tide, moving and breathing became a pipe dream.

Even if he was not the direct target of the black bugs' attack, just being crushed by them on their migration path.

Under the sweeping of the boundless sea of black bugs, it still made this Immortal of the Harmonious Dao gradually lose consciousness.

"It's over..."

In the moment before he fainted, the first thing that came to Golden Sword Immortal's mind was not the safety of his own life.

But the ensuing accountability from the Wanxian Alliance after resolving the insect disaster in the future.

"Damn it, if I had known, I would never have agreed to this crappy task no matter what."

His figure slowly faded, eventually turning into a golden long sword that seemed to be composed of countless small swords, clanging as it fell to the ground.

Indeed, with the strength of the black bugs, at most they could make an Immortal of the Harmonious Dao exhausted and briefly faint.

But to kill them, it was a bit insufficient.

However...

Li Fan's figure emerged from the black tide.

He picked up this golden long sword, the corner of his mouth revealing a hint of a smile.

Then he casually placed tens of thousands of formation restrictions on it.
