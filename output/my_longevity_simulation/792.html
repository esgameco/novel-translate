"Although I have lost the same-body sensing with the holy fetus Xu Bai, this kind of 'loss' may only be one-way and simple shielding."

"It cannot be ruled out that Mr. Bai can still sense the situation on my side through the avatar Xu Bai or even the pollution of the main body."

"So the first step is to send away the avatar Xu Bai and try to sever and weaken the connection between him and me."

"Otherwise, any plan would be transparent in front of him."

But it seemed that Mr. Bai's speed of rushing over was much slower than expected.

"Then where did he go?"

A hint of doubt flashed in his mind.

But soon, he vaguely guessed a possibility.

He believed that with Mr. Bai's current strength, he could easily teleport through the spatial restrictions and arrive in Changsheng Valley.

But don't forget, the Dharma Heavenly Venerable was watching and observing the Xuanhuang Realm from the dome above.

"In any case, it should be soon." Li Fan sat cross-legged in the center of the cultivation secret room.

The avatar Xu Bai quietly arrived at the entrance of Changsheng Valley.

The Yao Wang Sect disciples on duty were stunned.

Each strike was enough to severely injure a Dao cultivator!
