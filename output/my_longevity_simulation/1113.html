Chapter 1111: Quietly Setting Up the Prisoner's Net

In the Xuanhuang Realm, at the Myriad Immortals Alliance.

All the floating celestial cities simultaneously rang out with urgent bell sounds at the same moment.

The immortal alliance cultivators stopped their actions and stood stunned in place.

After a brief moment of being dumbfounded, they finally recalled the meaning represented by these bell sounds.

This was the highest level of warning from the Myriad Immortals Alliance.

It would only be activated when the immortal alliance faced a life-or-death crisis.

After a brief silence, a chaotic uproar suddenly erupted.

People were panic-stricken and at a loss.

Naturally, Li Fan, who was in the Lingmu Realm, also heard the bell sounds the instant they rang out.

At first, he thought his world-destroying arrangements had been discovered.

However, Li Fan quickly realized that these abruptly ringing bells were likely related to what happened inside the Life and Death Grand Formation.

This was confirmed when the recorded images from the formation left behind by the True Immortal's Remnant Shell arrived.

The images finally stopped at the scene of the Dharma Transmitters disappearing into the white mist.

Afterwards, the recording formation was similarly destroyed by that surging, endless white mist.

Li Fan repeatedly reviewed the images, a hint of solemnity flashing through his eyes: "The Myriad Immortals Alliance's reaction is so intense, could it be..."

"Those three Dharma Transmitters died?"

Li Fan's heart was instantly filled with startled doubts.

He knew that each Dharma Transmitter was protected by the "Dharma Transmission Celestial Venerate".

As long as this protective power did not disappear, they could be said to be immortal and indestructible existences in this Xuanhuang Realm.

Although they were restricted because of this and unable to advance to the Eternal Life realm,

They were almost no different from having eternal life.

Yet unexpectedly, in front of this newly ascended Eternal Life Celestial Venerate, Wangsheng, they were so vulnerable.

"Actually able to instantly kill Dharma Transmitters?"

"If these three Dharma Transmitters really died just like that, then Wangsheng's strength..."

Li Fan's eyes couldn't help but narrow slightly.

Then, a hint of doubt flashed through his mind.

"If he's really this powerful, what reason would he have for killing the three Dharma Transmitters?"

"It was clearly just an accidental intrusion, and there were no destructive or disrespectful actions during this time."

Li Fan once again slowly reviewed the images recorded by the formation.

"It seems that right before their departure, Wangsheng had no intention of appearing."

"But Dharma Transmitter Wei seemed to have discovered something, which led to Wangsheng's appearance."

"So, it was to silence them?"

Li Fan's expression couldn't help but become strange.

Killing to silence someone, such a thing appearing on an Eternal Life Celestial Venerate seemed a bit beneath his status.

But he actually did just that.

There was only one explanation. If what Dharma Transmitter Wei discovered was leaked, it would have an immeasurable impact on him.

To avoid the occurrence of variables, Wangsheng chose to annihilate them.

"Before his death, Dharma Transmitter Wei should have been pondering reversing the Life and Death Grand Formation."

Following this train of thought, Li Fan put himself in that position. With his current attainments in formations, he naturally quickly understood the key points in this.

"As I previously intuited, reversing life and death, the ultimate goal is not to reverse the life and death of cultivators."

"The resurrection of cultivators is merely an incidental change in the laws of heaven and earth brought about during this process."

"The true target this Celestial Venerate wants to reverse life and death for is the [world]."

Li Fan's eyes suddenly erupted with a brilliant light.

"To cover up this goal, the group from the Myriad Immortals Alliance had to be annihilated."

"This shows that the target he wants to resurrect is not the [Xuanhuang Realm], but in these years, it has already been..."

Here is the full translation of the Chinese excerpt into English:

"One of the many cultivation worlds devoured by the Xuanhuang Realm."

"Interesting. Is this a case of never forgetting one's homeland?"

After sorting through the context again, Li Fan nodded slightly: "No wonder this person has been keeping a low profile and never exposing himself to the public eye over the years. This move can be called borrowing a corpse to resurrect the soul. If successful, his homeland world may indeed have a chance to come back to life."

"But the fate of the Xuanhuang Realm itself is uncertain."

"If this leaks out, it will surely face fierce resistance from the native cultivators of the Xuanhuang Realm."

"It turns out he's doing shady things, just like me."

A smile appeared at the corner of Li Fan's mouth.

"But all the plans are bound to come to nothing."

The smile disappeared in an instant.

At this moment, it had already returned to the Xuanhuang Realm.

As the direct culprit behind the fall of the three Dharma Transmitters, it was immediately pursued by the Wanxian Alliance upon appearing.

This time, the Wanxian Alliance was serious.

After all, in the previous Moruo Bin's possession incident, no Dharma Transmitters had perished.

Now, with three of them falling at once, the shock to the Dharma Transmitter Council was unimaginable.

More than ten Dharma Transmitters appeared together, locking onto the True Immortal Remnant Body in the center.

Among the crowd, Li Fan even discovered the figure of the curator of the Divine Tibetan Museum, the beast clan emperor Disanmo.

Compared to his appearance in the Fallen Immortal Realm, he looked even older.

However, he didn't seem to care much about the pursuit of the Wanxian Alliance.

Expressionless, with his eyes slightly closed.

Obviously, his attendance this time was just going through the motions.

In addition, Li Fan finally saw the Zhangjing Ren lineage.

They all wore ink-green Daoist robes, with mirror-like marks engraved on their foreheads.

The three forces that truly controlled the Wanxian Alliance gathered here, showing the great impact of the three Dharma Transmitters' demise.

Under the influence of various forces, Li Fan felt his control over the True Immortal Remnant Body weakening.

He was about to detonate the remnant body, opening up the passage between the Xuanhuang Realm and the Netherworld, creating a wave of the Netherworld Sea.

Something that surprised him a little happened.

The core of the Wanxian Alliance, originally poised to strike, seemed to suddenly receive some message.

At first, they were a bit puzzled and in disbelief.

But soon, without hesitation, they took action in unison.

Their figures became blurred, ignoring the culprit and preparing to leave this place!

Li Fan even saw that Emperor Disanmo, who originally had his eyes closed, suddenly opened them, as if something had arrived and attracted his attention.

Far away in the Lingmu Realm, Li Fan immediately felt a sense of unease in his heart.

Years of experience made him make a decision immediately.

He severed the connection with the True Immortal Remnant Body and detonated it at the same time.

Just as the True Immortal Remnant Body shattered inch by inch, a white mist figure quietly appeared beside him.

It appeared without any sign and was so swift.

But in the end, it was a step too late.

Endless darkness surged out from the cracks of the True Immortal Remnant Body.

It collided with the white mist surging from the white mist figure.

The dark tide that Li Fan had expected did not descend.

The white mist was like bags of waterproof sandbags, or the dam that always stood firm by the sea.

It firmly blocked the dark tide that could have caused a catastrophe.

...

In the Lingmu Realm, a trace of coldness flashed across Li Fan's face.

"What does this mean?"

"The two sides have cooperated?"

"In order to find me, even the fall of the Dharma Transmitters can be ignored?"
