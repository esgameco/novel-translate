Chapter 649

Five statues with indiscernible faces stood in a circle.

The statues bowed their heads, overlooking the center.

There, a large glowing cloud floated in the sky.

Within the cloud, miniature scenes of land, mountains, rivers, and streams could faintly be seen.

The cloud was divided into various isolated areas, each area suspended a small glowing spot.

At the edge of this glowing cloud, in a small blue area, there was a black dot moving irregularly.

Suddenly, in an instant, this area erupted in several different colors.

Then, as if an intense explosion occurred, a slight vibration spread from this area towards the surrounding cloud.

Like clouds swayed by the wind, the cloud fluttered gently.

Then, a large black mass suddenly surged out from the turbulent area on the edge of the glowing cloud.

Swiftly engulfing it all.

As if something suddenly bit down and swallowed everything, the area blinked out and disappeared into the light.

At the same time.

Except for the central statue with closed eyes and bowed head, the other four statues all opened their eyes in an instant.

They all gazed towards the now-vanished area.

An eerie atmosphere circulated within this space.

"Friend Yixin, what do you see?"

"Hehe, my eyes are dazzled, I can't see clearly."

"There's a force disturbing our perception. This seems to be..."

"That's right."

"How is that possible?"

The scene fell silent in an instant.

As if sensing something.

After a long time, voices resonated once more.

"In addition to ['Chaos'], there are also ['Mystic Yellow Sky Palm'], ['Violet Firmament Body']..."

"It's been a while since Xuanhuang Realm has been this lively, I really look forward to more in the future," Zhao Senior Sister's lazy voice rang out.

The other three chose to ignore her.

"Each of us has triggered a divine ability, most likely due to the deal with the 'Sky Overturning Alliance.'"

"Zhou Qing'ang, Li Fan?"

"All dead?"

"Hehe, how interesting."

"These are just trivial matters, possibly a deliberate attempt to divert our attention with illusions. The key is why 'Chaos' has reappeared in Xuanhuang Realm."

"I will order my subordinates to investigate. However, it's best not to have too high hopes. This matter carries a hint of unusual significance."

"That ancient one transmitting the law seems to have disappeared again lately."

"I seem to recall something interesting, hehe."

...

Continuously communicating, the five statues eventually closed their eyes once again.

The space fell silent again. (Translation continues in next message)
