Chapter 261

Li Fan remained dormant in the Tian Xuan Mirror, quietly awaiting the outcome.

Three months later, the exploration of the ruins had not made any significant progress.

However, He Zhenghao eagerly sent a message first.

"Fellow Daoist Li Fan, good news."

"After some efforts, I have secured three potential positions, each located in a different state."

"Fellow Daoist, please advise me."

Li Fan immediately replied, "Oh? Tell me about them."

"The first one is the leader of the Martial Arts Hall in Taihua State."

"Taihua State is not far from the Congyun Sea. A mountain range called Taihua Mountains runs through the entire state, which is also known as the state on the back of the mountains."

"There are countless mountain peaks. If I go to this state, my cultivation speed will be much faster than in the Congyun Sea."

"The second one is a staff member of the Strategy Formation Hall in Qingwei State."

"Qingwei State is south of Tianyu State. Although it is still quite far, it is much closer compared to the Congyun Sea."

"The third one is a city guardian position in Tianyun State."

"Tianyun State is not far west of Tianyu State. Therefore, the competition for this position is very fierce."

"The transfer is only to an equivalent city guardian position."

"The advantage is that it is closer to the headquarters of the Wanxian Alliance, and promotion is a bit easier than in other places."

He Zhenghao slowly revealed the results of his efforts during this period.

"Tianyun State..."

Li Fan pondered for a moment and replied, "No need to rush to make a decision. I have an acquainted cultivator in Tianyun State. Let me inquire for you and see if we can take it a step further."

He Zhenghao was very surprised upon hearing this, "Fellow Daoist, you even have connections in Tianyun State?"

"Hehe, I can't guarantee it. Let's give it a try. Fellow Daoist, wait for my news," Li Fan said calmly.

He Zhenghao repeatedly agreed.

So Li Fan contacted Shenyu Zi and explained the situation.

"Senior, do you have any way?"

Shenyu Zi only replied, "I'll ask for you."

And there was no further response.

Li Fan was not in a hurry and waited patiently.

Half a day later, a cultivator named Jing Xuan Daoist requested to add Li Fan as a friend.

Also from Tianyun State.

Li Fan's heart stirred, and he quickly accepted.

Jing Xuan's hearty voice came through.

"Hahaha, you must be Junior Li Fan? I heard from Shenyu that you have a good friend who wants to take a position in our Tianyun State?"

"Fourth-level Golden Core, tsk tsk, the younger generation is really impressive."

"How about this, ask him if he is interested in joining our Tianyun State Garrison Academy?"

"Hehe, due to the great war a few years ago, there are still some vacancies that have not been filled."

Li Fan was slightly stunned and couldn't help but ask, "Senior Jing Xuan, what is this Garrison Academy..."

Jing Xuan was also stunned.

He didn't expect that Li Fan didn't even know what the Garrison Academy was.

However, he immediately realized that Li Fan came from a remote area and explained, "The Garrison Academy is a unique organization in the hinterland of the Wanxian Alliance."

"It is directly responsible to the headquarters of the Wanxian Alliance. Its main duty is to suppress the wicked and maintain the stability of the hinterland."

"When necessary, it will also follow emergency orders to go to other states for combat."

With Jing Xuan's explanation, Li Fan understood.

In essence, it was the same as the garrison camp stationed outside Xuanjing City in Daxuan, which only listened to Li Fan's orders and guarded the safety of the capital.

"It should be considered an important position."

"But it seems to require frequent battles. I don't know if He Zhenghao, with his current temperament, would agree to it."

Li Fan pondered for a moment, exchanged a few words with Jing Xuan, and then contacted He Zhenghao to explain the situation.

"Garrison Academy?" This time He Zhenghao was really a bit shocked.

When he was young, he had stayed in Tianyu State for a while, so he naturally knew what the Garrison Academy was.

Like the Dharma Protector Hall, they were both institutions directly under the command of the Wanxian Alliance headquarters.

Only below the Dharma Protector Hall.

"Fellow Daoist Li Fan is truly unfathomable, actually being able to secure this position."

"Although it's a bit dangerous, but..."

A graceful figure flashed through He Zhenghao's mind.

He immediately gritted his teeth and agreed.

After learning of He Zhenghao's determination, Li Fan also replied to Daoist Jing Xuan.

"Great!" Jing Xuan was very pleased.

"Tell your friend to come to our Tianyun State first and serve as a city guardian."

"After three years of accumulating experience, I will use my connections to transfer him into the Garrison Academy."

Next, Jing Xuan carefully explained some precautions before ending the communication.

Li Fan thought for a moment. Although the matter had not been settled yet, he still transferred 100,000 contribution points each to Shenyu Zi and Jing Xuan.

As a token of gratitude.

Of course, this expense was recorded under He Zhenghao's account.

...

In Tianyun State.

Shenyu Zi, who received the contribution points, was slightly stunned and laughed in spite of himself.

Jing Xuan also immediately sent a message to inquire, "Old Shenyu, did you receive the contribution point transfer from Junior Li? 100,000 contribution points! I remember he is only at the Foundation Establishment stage, right? What a generous gesture!"

Shenyu Zi replied with a smile, "Anyway, it's much more generous than when we were at the Foundation Establishment stage."

"I only passed on a message and received the same reward as you. Jing Xuan, you won't be angry, right?"

Jing Xuan laughed loudly, "Hahaha, treating us equally, this little kid is quite interesting."

"Not bad, not bad. I find him more pleasing to the eye than that old man Guangwu."

"Screw up? Who are you looking down on?" Jing Xuan snorted coldly and fell silent.

...

Time passed.

Three months later, He Zhenghao's appointment order came.

There were no major issues.

Guangan City Guardian of Tianyun State.

For this, a total of 80,000 contribution points were spent back and forth.

Naturally, it was borrowed from Li Fan.

Anyway, He Zhenghao was already in debt, so he had no mental burden.

Li Fan was also very generous, lending as much as needed.

Before leaving, He Zhenghao made a solemn vow.

Once he had a stable footing in Tianyun State, he would try to arrange for Li Fan to be transferred there as well.

Li Fan didn't say much, only patting He Zhenghao's shoulder.

After He Zhenghao left, Li Fan hid in the Tian Xuan Mirror all day, not going out at all, and diligently cultivated.

Soon, time arrived at the 23rd year of the Anchor Era.

The ruins that had risen into the sky were finally found.

It had fallen into a thousand-mile swamp in the center of Jiushan State.

And when the cultivators began to explore it, the origins of these ruins were also unearthed.

The ancient immortal sect, Chaoyuan Sect.

Although not as renowned as Taishang and Dadao Sect, whose fame was still known to everyone even thousands of years later,

It was still a famous and prominent sect before the great calamity descended.

According to past experiences of exploring sect ruins, there must be abundant treasures stored here.

When the news spread, it immediately attracted many cultivators to venture to Jiushan State.

Jiushan State, which was already in chaos, became even more turbulent.

The level of arrogance could be seen at a glance.

The weakness of the Wanxian Alliance's control over Jiushan State could also be glimpsed.

Originally, it was just a normal ruin exploration, and the Wanxian Alliance had no intention of interfering too much.

But at this time, a piece of news arrived, making the Wanxian Alliance furious.

A Nascent Soul cultivator named Luo Xi from the Five Elders Council came to explore the ruins, which was fine.

Not only did he not conceal his identity as a cultivator of the Five Elders Council, but he also relied on his profound cultivation to injure and kill many exploration members of the Wanxian Alliance.

After entering the ruins and obtaining some unknown treasures, he left with his head held high.

On my turf, and still being so arrogant?

It was simply a slap in the face.

However, doubts also followed.

Such behavior carried a clear provocative meaning.

Could there be some conspiracy?

Could it be that the Five Elders Council wanted to start another war?

Regardless, face could not be lost.

The Wanxian Alliance swiftly dispatched several Nascent Soul experts to Jiushan State to take control of the situation.

And issued a stern warning.

All cultivators from the Five Elders Council were prohibited from coming to Jiushan State.

Those who dared to come would die!
