"Now that I have the help of millions of cultivators, how could I fear him?"

Li Fan thought for a moment, and his mind instantly connected to that towering ancient tree in the Spirit Wood Realm.

Tempering day and night without stopping, Li Fan's control over the Spirit Wood Realm was approaching completeness.

At this moment, his mind merged with the ancient tree, as if he was residing in heaven and earth.

His mind intertwined with the heaven and earth of the Spirit Wood Realm.

At this moment, Li Fan was the Spirit Wood Realm, and the Spirit Wood Realm was Li Fan!

Li Fan, who had always been cautious in his actions, still did not make a move, allowing the young man to observe the scenes in his sea of consciousness.

"This feeling..." A strange and indescribable feeling welled up in Li Fan's heart.

Looking at the young man in front of him, it seemed as if he was looking at himself.

His head felt dizzy, and countless memories suddenly surged forth.

Originally a child from an ordinary mortal family, he was fortunate to meet the Dharma Heavenly Venerable. Receiving his teachings, he later voluntarily spread the new dharma...

These were the memories he had lost due to his injuries...

"So that's how it is. I am Jiang, the spreader of the dharma."

"Could there be any secrets between oneself and oneself?"

Thoughts like these suddenly welled up in Li Fan's mind.

Li Fan's guard slowly lowered, and the fierce appearances of the myriad demon gods also softened.

The young man strolled leisurely in Li Fan's sea of consciousness, as if taking a casual walk in his own home.

"Interesting. Each divine demon is formed from a thread of consciousness," the young man remarked with a nod of amazement.

"They can occupy the bodies of other cultivators and even devour their divine consciousness to strengthen their own power."

The young man was slightly startled, and then his eyes flashed as he observed Li Fan more closely.

"Not bad, the True Immortal cultivation technique is truly formidable!" the young man exclaimed, clapping his hands after a moment.

"It's just a pity that to achieve the goal, it's still lacking a little bit," the young man said with some regret.

Li Fan also nodded slightly, "Indeed. I don't know what happened with Mo Rubin. We had previously agreed that as long as he represented the Myriad Immortal Alliance to resolve this matter, I would cooperate and have all these divine demons self-sacrifice, allowing him to establish great merit..."

"But this fellow has been slow to come, there must have been some changes."

"The Myriad Immortal Alliance truly has unfathomable foundations."

Li Fan narrowed his eyes, nodding and pondering.

Hearing Li Fan's words, the young man's expression finally changed slightly, a hint of solemnity flashing in his eyes. He continued, "What should we do then?"

"If the millions of divine demons continue to rampage, there might be a risk of losing control if this goes on. It's better to contact him first and clarify the reason properly."

Li Fan waved his hand, revealing a grim smile, "Hehe, it doesn't matter. Even if he is exposed and dies, it won't have much impact on me."

"You see, the power we can control now is so immense! Losing control? What's that! It's not even worth mentioning!"

Li Fan's face was full of confidence, "If things truly become unmanageable and the Myriad Immortal Alliance can't resolve it, all the better."

"With the importance the Dharma Heavenly Venerable places on the Xuanhuang Realm, it might even force him to show himself."
