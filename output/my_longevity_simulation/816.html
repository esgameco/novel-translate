Compared to the tense atmosphere between the land and the states, the Congyu Sea had not changed much.

The clouds of war had not yet spread here, and from time to time, one could still see the figures of cultivators hurrying back and forth between the various islands.

Li Fan concealed his form and quietly flew to the middle west of the Congyu Sea, near an island called Haixuan.

Looking from afar, a majestic grand formation enveloped the island.

It faintly exuded an intent to kill that warned strangers not to approach.

"[Tahuang Eight Extremes Dragon Slaying Formation], it is said to have been jointly created by two Hua Shen formation masters invited at a high price. Cultivators below the Hedao realm who enter by mistake will either die or be injured."

The Terminal Dissociation Disk in the sea of consciousness emitted a glowing white light, and in an instant, it had already cracked the seemingly impenetrable formation before his eyes.

Transforming into an imperceptible black line, Li Fan flew into the formation without triggering any signs of the formation's counterattack.

"Senior Brother Ji, come on..."

From the cave abode on Haixuan Island, the coquettish moans and teasing voices of several women could be faintly heard.

"Such fragrant wine! And such fragrant beauties!"

As well as the complacent laughter of a middle-aged man indulging himself.

Li Fan quietly arrived above the cave abode on the island and looked down.

He saw the owner of Haixuan Island, a Golden Core cultivator named Ji Shaoli, embracing two identical twin female cultivators, one on each side.

He was watching seven women below, all lovely and charming, plump and slender, twisting their graceful bodies...
