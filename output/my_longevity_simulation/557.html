Chapter 557

Gazing into the distance, the sight seemed to cross over thousands of miles, reaching a deep underwater trench.

That was the location where Han Yi appeared after escaping from the small world since childhood.

"There aren't any ordinary people here, only a world full of 'Beast-kind'..." Li Fan furrowed his brows slightly.

Based on Han Yi's memories, Li Fan believed that the creatures in that small world were somewhat similar to the 'Beast-kind' he had encountered several times before, which were beings that were half-human and half-beast.

However, there were subtle differences as well.

Although they were a mix of humans and demon beasts, in the 'Beast-kind,' the part that belonged to "humans" was slightly more dominant.

As for the creatures in the small world Han Li experienced, they leaned more towards being "demon beasts."

This could be deduced from whether their heads were that of a human or a beast, revealing some clues.

What was even more interesting was the legend that Han Yi had heard about that world.

In the beginning, most of the residents above the wilderness had the appearance of being half-beast and half-human. There were even quite a few who were purely "human."

However, as time passed, there were occasional descendants among the residents who had a higher degree of beastification.

Initially, these mutants were considered heretics, abandoned, or even hunted down.

But when some of these mutants stubbornly survived and demonstrated great power, things began to change.

They slaughtered the original villages on the wilderness and migrated to the depths of the wilderness, establishing a new order.

There, the higher the degree of beastification, the more esteemed the status.

The stark difference in ideologies inevitably led to wars.

On the wilderness, the strong reigned supreme. The individual strength of the mutants who had devolved from their ancestors was evidently stronger than the common beast-kind.

Although initially, they were outnumbered, causing the battles to be evenly matched.

However, as time passed, their numbers increased, and they began to display overwhelming combat prowess in the war.

After bloody massacres, the opposition naturally disappeared.

Thus, on the wilderness, it became what Han Yi saw, where "beasts were respected, and humans were considered heretics."

"Perhaps it's related to a route prepared by 'Emperor One' for the demon beast tribe. But it seems like His plan has failed," Li Fan contemplated.

"When the bloodline of the demon beasts is triggered, they are much stronger than ordinary humans," Li Fan thought of the deities in that world.

The celestial destiny Xuan Bird.

Although there were other beings in the Xuan Bird tribe, when Li Fan saw the statue of the deity, he suddenly felt a strange sensation in his heart.

That celestial destiny Xuan Bird was the same one that Li Fan had possessed in the Meteor Immortal Realm and had once accompanied Xu Ke as "Xiao Hei."

There was no concrete evidence for this feeling; perhaps it was just due to Li Fan having possessed it, resulting in a mysterious connection.

"It actually fell?"

"Weird."

"Even a mere Green Phoenix bird is doing fine. It doesn't make sense that the Xuan Bird fused with a small world's celestial destiny would fall first," Li Fan furrowed his brows, puzzled.

After pondering for a while, he came to terms with it.

Among humans, strength often meant longevity. But in the demon beast community, this principle did not necessarily apply.

...

Even though he was very intrigued by that small world, Li Fan knew it wasn't the time to explore it yet.

Although Han Yi was only at the Qi-Refining stage and couldn't use spiritual power, managing to stir up storms there easily made it seem like there was no danger in that small world.

However, knowing that Han Yi manipulated the free celestial destinies with the 'Destiny Scriptures,' he was essentially half a child of destiny and would naturally turn unfavorable situations into favorable ones.

If Li Fan rashly entered without using the method of controlling the destiny, he might encounter some misfortunes.

He might even accidentally run into the millennium great demon in the depths of the wilderness.

After all, when Li Fan attempted to follow Han Yi into the deep-sea cave back then, the sudden feeling of danger that arose in his heart was not a false alarm.

Before mastering the method of controlling celestial destinies, Li Fan would not venture into unknown risks.

"No matter, whatever happened back then, I will soon know once I reach the Meteor Immortal Realm," Li Fan withdrew his gaze and headed towards the nearest guarding island.

Before long, he arrived at the Chongwu Island.

As a nominal subordinate, Li Fan knew a bit about this person. He was meticulous in trivial matters and extremely muddled in significant matters, constantly making small mistakes and being excessively greedy.

He was the perfect candidate.

Li Fan concealed his true cultivation level and only showed the aura of Foundation Establishment perfection when visiting.

"How should I address you, Daoist friend?" Huang Ranqi handed him a cup of tea, smiling amiably and asked.

At this moment, Li Fan appeared as his original self, not in the appearance of the "Ancient Celestial Master." Therefore, Huang Ranqi did not recognize him.

Li Fan cupped his hands: "I am Li Fan!"

"Cough..." Huang Ranqi, who was leisurely sipping tea, almost spurted out the water in his mouth upon hearing Li Fan's words.

After coughing a few times to relieve his embarrassment, Huang Ranqi lost his voice and asked, "You are also named Li Fan?"

Li Fan, upon hearing this, looked strange and curiously asked, "Why does Daoist friend say so? What do you mean by 'also'?"

"Could it be that Daoist friend also knows another cultivator named Li Fan?" He quickly seemed to react, looking extremely surprised.

"Hehe, does Daoist friend lock himself up and not come out? Not even aware of the title of a senior envoy?" Huang Ranqi squinted his eyes, carefully examining Li Fan.

"Exactly as Daoist friend guessed! I was originally a wandering cultivator in the Congyun Sea. In the past few years, I went out to seek opportunities to break through. Who knew I accidentally entered and got lost in a dangerous small world until I recently managed to escape back," Li Fan shook his head, looking deeply emotional.

He vividly described his experiences in the small world to Huang Ranqi.

"No spiritual energy, but beasts everywhere?" Huang Ranqi was indeed attracted by the strange scenes depicted by Li Fan.

"To survive in such a ferocious place and return alive, Daoist friend must have exceptional luck," he also sighed.

Li Fan nodded in agreement, "What Daoist friend said is true. Between life and death, it's the most likely to stir up emotions. That's why, having just returned, I successfully broke through the bottleneck and reached Foundation Establishment perfection."

"As I just said, I was just a wandering cultivator originally. For the sake of 'freedom,' I was unwilling to join the Ten Thousand Immortal Alliance and be exploited. But after this ordeal, I have come to understand. As long as one can live, what freedom, it's simply not worth mentioning!"

Li Fan earnestly said, "So, I would like to ask Daoist friend for a recommendation!"

After pondering for a moment and observing Li Fan up and down for a while, Huang Ranqi did not immediately agree.

"I heard that recently there have been tensions between the Five Elders Council and our Immortal Alliance, with frequent frictions. Could it be that Daoist friend is a spy sent by the Five Elders Council?" Huang Ranqi suddenly asked.
