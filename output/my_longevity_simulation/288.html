Chapter 288

It seems that the bodies were all his own corpses!

Even though Su Changyu prided himself on his firm will and being different from ordinary people, at this moment, he couldn't help but break out in a cold sweat from this bizarre scene.

"Hehe, don't be so nervous. They're just dead people, what's there to be afraid of."

"You should know, the living are much more frightening than the dead."

The white-haired monk said gloomily.

With a light point of his finger, a set of table and chairs rose from the ground.

"Sit, and enjoy the view."

"No one has come here for many years."

After the initial horror towards the strange double voice and the frightening surroundings, Su Changyu gradually adapted.

Calming his mind, he sat down steadily.

"May I ask how to address Senior?"

After sitting down, seeing the white-haired monk only blankly observing the surroundings, Su Changyu couldn't help but ask.

"My name is Yin Shangren."

"Hehe, others call me Yin Yin Shangren."

Yin Shangren said with a hint of self-mockery.

"Greetings Senior Yin. I am Su Changyu." Su Changyu introduced himself neither humbly nor arrogantly.

At this time, Yin Shangren suddenly turned his head back and carefully looked over Su Changyu.

After a long while, he finally moved his gaze away.

"Tsk, there really is fate amidst the mysteries of the heavens..." Yin Shangren muttered under his breath.

Su Changyu heard it in his heart but didn't understand the meaning.

After a long time, Yin Shangren spoke again, "You also encountered a bottleneck in Establishing Foundation, and were guided by the opportunity in your heart to come here, right?"

"Establishing Foundation?" Su Changyu was startled. He had just reached late Qi Refining realm.

Just as he was about to deny it, he heard Yin Shangren say to himself, "Hehe, I'm used to it. Anyway, every few years, there will be cultivators guided here."

"Each time one comes, I have to repeat it. I'm sick of saying the same things."

"When immortals fight, the little ghosts suffer. Damn, it's so annoying I could die..."

Su Changyu frowned slightly. This Yin Shangren was a bit crazy, saying difficult to understand things. He didn't know the deeper meaning.

"Forget it, I'll say it one more time then."

Yin Shangren also sat down and said, "The so-called Establishing Foundation is to build the foundation of the Dao. Then do you know what this foundation of the Dao is?"

...

"I used myself as a marvel to build the foundation of the Dao!"

The body split in two, revealing another face amidst the internal organs and blood.

The two faces inside and out spoke in unison.

Finally ending the elaboration with two eerily smiling faces.

Su Changyu first stood up startled by the horrifying scene before him, then slowly sat back down seeing Yin Shangren restore to normal.

"Using myself to build the foundation..." Strange light flashed in Su Changyu's eyes.

"What? Tempted?" Yin Shangren asked with a chuckle.

"The Dao foundation perfectly overlaps with oneself, all divine powers and techniques belong to one's own body."

"From now on, no matter what secret arts or techniques, you'll master them as soon as you learn them."

"Punch unrivaled geniuses, step on hidden powerhouses..."

"Why not take action quickly?"

Yin Shangren looked at Su Changyu with a somewhat crazed gaze, saying full of temptation.

Su Changyu rarely maintained his reason and calm, "Then, may I ask Senior, what is the price?"

"If everyone could easily 'use themselves to build the foundation', wouldn't geniuses be everywhere?"

"If everyone was a genius, then there would be no distinction between the mediocre and geniuses."

Yin Shangren laughed foolishly and pointed at the hundreds of corpses on the ground, "These are the price!"

Su Changyu didn't understand and could only remain silent.

"After dissecting hundreds of 'me's and having sufficient confidence,"

"I began to 'use myself to build the foundation' anew."

"Even so, it still fell short of perfect success by a tiny margin."

Yin Shangren explained with a cold laugh.

"The human body is so exquisite and complex."

"How can ordinary people fully know themselves to build their foundation."

"To be able to do this completely by oneself without external help is a rare genius in the world."

Hearing this, Su Changyu immediately understood.

He couldn't help feeling a bit dejected in his heart.

Those who know others are wise, those who know themselves are enlightened.

Su Changyu was very clear about his own cultivation talent.

Definitely couldn't be called a genius at all.

Not even ordinary.

Even using "mediocre" to evaluate would be no exaggeration.

Wanting to "use himself to build the foundation" was even more impossible.

Yin Shangren saw the changes in Su Changyu's expression and chuckled, "Little Su, don't be discouraged."

"At this moment, you came before me, indicating everything is destined."

With that said, he extended his index finger and lightly tapped the center of Su Changyu's brows.

In an instant, countless scenes and knowledge frantically surged into Su Changyu's mind.

Those were the scenes and insights of Yin Shangren dissecting hundreds of Establishing Foundation corpses.

And even more precious was the process and half-successful experience of "using oneself to build the foundation".

...

Suddenly receiving such a vast amount of information, Su Changyu felt a bit overwhelmed for a time.

His temples kept throbbing, as if his head would split open.

But he knew in his heart that this fortuitous encounter was likely his only chance in this life to change his fate.

So he gritted his teeth and persevered.

After a while, he collapsed into the chair, his whole body drenched in sweat.

After resting for a bit, Su Changyu mustered his spirit and deeply bowed to Yin Shangren.

"Many thanks Senior!"

"Hehe, no need to thank me."

Yin Shangren revealed an ill-intentioned smile, "Originally you had given up the idea of 'using yourself to build the foundation'."

"Now that I've transmitted my experiences to your mind, it has given you the intention to give it a shot."

"You should know each person is unique. My situation definitely may not all apply to you."

"Perhaps because of this, you will fail and perish in body and Dao."

"In that sense, it would be me who harmed you!"

Su Changyu said solemnly, "Life and death are a matter of fate, wealth and rank are up to Heaven."

Yin Shangren's smile disappeared, "Good ambition indeed."

He said bluntly without courtesy, "If your business here is done, then scram!"

Not expecting the other party to suddenly give him the boot, Su Changyu was slightly stunned.

But since Yin Shangren had already said so, it wasn't good for him to overstay.

Bowing again, he was about to leave.

Before departing, looking at Yin Shangren's corpses covering the ground, Su Changyu couldn't help but ask, "Senior Yin, may I ask where these corpses came from?"

Yin Shangren swept his gaze around, his eyes becoming a bit dazed.

"Yeah, where did these corpses come from?"

"Ah! I remember!"

"There was a huge rain! Countless corpses fell from the sky like rainwater, so I took the chance to collect some..."

Yin Shangren muttered a bit crazily.

Getting the answer, Su Changyu once again felt a chill in his heart.

Not daring to stay any longer on this bizarre little island, he hurriedly left.

Watching Su Changyu's retreating figure, Yin Shangren slowly returned to normal.

He touched his chest, seeming to hide some hard to part with treasure there.

...

A long time later, the extinguished flames reignited.

Completely engulfing Yin Yin Island.
