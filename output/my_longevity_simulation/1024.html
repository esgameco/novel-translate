Chapter 1022 - Fleecing the Heavenly Mirror Again

In order to prevent inconsistencies between the dream and reality, Li Fan specially sectioned off a piece of land in the Spirit Wood Realm to serve as a dwelling place for these Medicine King Sect disciples.

With the isolation of the formation, it was ensured that there would be no accidents to disturb them.

"After all, there is still a slight gap between the dream and reality. So they can't handle things that are too delicate. But if it's just hard labor similar to providing Evergreen Essence, there shouldn't be a problem." Liu Ruchen observed for a while and then commented.

"As long as an appropriate amount of Evergreen Essence is added when refining any elixir, it will increase the probability and quality of successfully producing the elixir. So the more Evergreen Essence, the better." Seeing the many Medicine King Sect disciples who had survived a catastrophe and were now full of hopes for the future in the dream, a trace of reluctance flashed in Liu San's eyes.

But he also knew that this was the best treatment he could fight for them at present, so he just echoed Liu Ruchen's words.

And Li Fan, who could see through people's hearts, clearly sensed the thoughts in the minds of the Medicine King Sect's Dao-attained elders present.

It is said that things of the same kind injure each other, which is human nature. Li Fan did not force them too much, but opened his mouth to promise: "It's just an expedient measure! Rest assured, we will definitely not let these disciples be immersed in the dream for too long."

Everyone didn't know whether to believe it or not, but they all praised Li Fan's compassion in unison.

"In ancient immortal cultivation sects, the elders almost all climbed up step by step from the bottom, with rich experience in fighting." Li Fan smiled in response, but his heart was not deceived by the appearances these people displayed.

"But it's not a big problem. They have all bowed before the faceless true immortal statue and are all locusts on the same boat."

The problems of the elders and lower-level disciples had been solved, and the next thing that needed to be arranged were the thirty elite disciples on the list.

Including Liu Ruchen's adopted son, Liu Yihang, the thirty people were all outstanding among the younger generation of the Medicine King Sect.

Even in Li Fan's current view, they could be called quite talented.

It was time to use people, and it would be a pity if they were always asleep.

So Li Fan contacted Mo Rubin again and asked him for a hundred different cultivation techniques.

Although cultivation techniques were one of the most precious resources in the Xuan-Huang Realm, those who had already reached the peak of domination, the Dharma Transmitters, naturally would not lack them.

Li Fan roughly described to Mo Rubin the process of finding the Medicine King Cauldron.

Although Mo Rubin was a little surprised by Li Fan's good luck, he didn't pay much attention to it.

After handing over a hundred Hua Shen level cultivation techniques to Li Fan, he warned: "You can play with them, but don't delay the important matters. I have already obtained the support of the vast majority of Dharma Transmitters, and Cang has also made corresponding preparations on his side."

"The resolution of 【Sealing the Spirits of Xuan Tian】 will be officially passed soon, so Brother Xingwang, you must prepare in advance."

Sensing the deduction progress of the Final Dissociation Disk in his sea of consciousness, Li Fan waved his hand: "I do things, you can rest assured, there will definitely be no mistakes!"

"Well... I'll go find the Heavenly Mirror again later. After all, it's about its regaining freedom, so it can't be a hands-off shopkeeper." Li Fan muttered and hurriedly took his leave.

Mo Rubin looked at Li Fan's disappearing figure and sighed.

After leaving the hidden immortal's cave, Li Fan went straight to Weidou City in Liuguang Prefecture, where the Heavenly Mirror's avatar was located.

It had been a while since they last met, and it had already changed its identity.

"As for the second point..."

"It can actually give birth to an existence not much weaker than your original body..."

"Could it be like how we humans reproduce offspring?"

Li Fan asked curiously.

The avatar of Tian Xuan Mirror glanced at Li Fan and shook his head slightly.

"You human cultivators constantly accumulate cultivation as time passes. I am the same. But what's different from cultivators is that the power that constructs my original body is too pure to tolerate duplicate parts. So every once in a while, I need to expel these duplicate parts from my body."

"This is the origin of the secondary body."
