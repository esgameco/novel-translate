Chapter 824

Li Fan instinctively moved back a little.

It was only through the Lingmu Zhixin (Spirit Wood Heart) that he ensured he still held the power of life and death over the spirit tree, which made him relax a little.

The pressure from the tree spirit continued to rise.

Yuan Ying, Hua Shen...

It slowly stopped when it was comparable to He Dao.

"The giant tree spirit is equivalent to the Dao of Heaven in the spirit wood realm."

"If the Dao of Heaven truly has a spirit, the cave world is equivalent to the Yuan Ying realm. The small worlds are equivalent to Hua Shen or He Dao, depending on their own scale."

"Originally, with the foundation of the spirit wood realm, it was almost impossible for a tree spirit with the strength of He Dao to be born. But with Shang Tianci continuously transporting the wonders of Xuanhuang, it has also achieved a kind of upgrade to a certain extent."

"Just like how Mr. Bai previously created a miniature Xuanhuang realm with his bare hands. However, Shang Tianci did it through pure replication."

While Li Fan was pondering, the green light spots gathered from all directions had stopped converging.

A figure almost no different from a human appeared before his eyes.

Curvy and graceful, just like the tree people's elder, the external sexual characteristics were female.

Li Fan glanced at her, and with a slight movement of his mind, he clothed her through the Lingmu Zhixin.

The tree spirit did not resist.

Instead, she looked at the clothes that suddenly appeared on her with some curiosity.

Although her eyes looked a bit simple, she was definitely not ignorant.

Born as a spirit and inheriting all the memories of the spirit tree, she only lacked some experience in interpersonal relationships compared to ordinary cultivators.

"Although her strength is already above mine, because I refined the Lingmu Zhixin early, she still has to be controlled by me."

With this thought in Li Fan's mind, the tree spirit in front of him knelt down gracefully at his will.

A He Dao realm being kneeling to a Golden Core realm, no matter where it is placed, it is an extremely insulting thing.

However, the tree spirit did not show any anger.

"Master." Instead, she naturally said this to Li Fan.

The creatures of heaven and earth are simple by nature. They will not be as treacherous as humans.

Especially when they are first born, they will not hide their emotions.

After discerning for a while, Li Fan confirmed that the tree spirit's submission was her true thought, and only then did he nod secretly.

"Since you have transformed into human form, you should have your own name."

"From now on, you will be called [Lin Ling]."

With that said, Li Fan pointed in the air and tapped on Lin Ling's forehead.

After a moment, a red mark appeared between her eyebrows.

"Thank you, Master." Lin Ling, who now had her own name, was very happy, and thanked Li Fan again, repeatedly chanting.

"In the future, as she grows up and experiences more things in the world, it will be hard to say."

"However, it is not an easy task to break the restraint of the Lingmu Zhixin."

Li Fan carefully comprehended the faint connection between himself and Lin Ling, and thought to himself.

In the next period of time, Lin Ling demonstrated her powerful abilities as the incarnation of the Dao of Heaven in the spirit wood realm to Li Fan.

She could mobilize all the forces of the small world at will, and always in the most efficient form.

With her help, the "transportation" process of Shang Tianci became several times easier.

And Lin Ling did not feel that it was a hard task, but instead played

The young lad, Shang Tianci, was deeply attracted by the innocent and lively expression on Lin Ling's face as she played happily.

As a manifestation of the Dao, Lin Ling's beauty was naturally beyond words.

Her perfect face had no flaws. Every frown and smile exuded a unique charm.

Shang Tianci couldn't take his eyes off her, unwilling to look away.

"S-Senior..." Lin Ling stammered with a blushing face after finishing her work and being told by Li Fan to go play elsewhere. Shang Tianci had found Li Fan.

"Hehe, keep up the good work." Li Fan patted Shang Tianci's shoulder forcefully, revealing a meaningful smile.

Although he didn't make any promises, Shang Tianci was instantly energized as if he had been injected with chicken blood.

"Senior, I've almost finished replicating the wondrous scenes from the Primordial Realm stored in my Dayjing gourd. So I want to go back to the Primordial Realm to record some other things."

"It's a good opportunity to try persuading some of the others in Shanhe Xuan to come over," he volunteered.

Seeing how proactive he was, Li Fan had no reason to refuse.

However, to be safe, he still gave him a jade pendant as a medium for casting [Formless Killing Intent] before letting him leave.

Calculating the time, he had left the Five Elders long enough.

Li Fan also leisurely left the Lingmu Realm.

To his surprise, when he went out, Lin Ling also wanted to follow along.

"You can leave this place?" Li Fan couldn't help but be a little surprised.

"Don't worry, Master. There's no problem," Lin Ling nodded vigorously.

With a free Harmonized Dao helper, Li Fan naturally agreed.

When the two arrived in the Primordial Realm, Li Fan first observed the changes in her.

"In the end, she's still somewhat affected. Her actual combat power is a bit inferior compared to a true Harmonized Dao."

"But she's still much stronger than an ordinary Huashen."

Li Fan nodded inwardly.

With Lin Ling, he flew towards Jiushan Prefecture.

After flying for a while, Lin Ling suddenly asked, "Master, why don't we travel underground? Flying in the sky is so slow."

Li Fan was slightly stunned.

"Underground? What do you mean?"

Lin Ling widened her eyes and looked at Li Fan.

She opened her mouth, trying to explain. But for a long time, she couldn't come up with a suitable adjective.

After a while, she slapped her head and transmitted an image into Li Fan's mind.

Almost instantly, Li Fan understood what Lin Ling meant.

"The power of ley lines?"

"You can shuttle by borrowing the power of ley lines?" he couldn't help but ask.

"Can't you do it, Master?" Lin Ling was a little surprised.

Seeing Li Fan fall silent, Lin Ling pondered for a moment, then comforted, "It's okay, Master."

"I seem to be able to take you with me."

Sensing it, Lin Ling didn't seem to be lying.

Li Fan hesitated for a moment and slowly nodded, "Then let's try."

"Great, Master. Hold on to me!" Seeing Li Fan agree, Lin Ling was very excited.

A layer of green light shield appeared on her body as she grabbed Li Fan.

Then she fiercely entered the ground from the sky.

Li Fan's vision first went black.

Then it was like falling into a surging river, surrounded by turbulent earthy yellow energy.

The immense pressure even made Li Fan feel a bit suffocated.

But Lin Ling seemed to be in her element, not only able to move freely,

but also seemingly able to draw from the endless ley line power to strengthen herself.

She moved along the ley lines while shouting excitedly.
