The scene shifted to another unknown location.

It was clearly a top-secret place with numerous defensive measures. Even the sensing images of the faceless stone statues were slightly blurred.

But after all, her power level was higher than that of the Xuanhuang Realm.

So the Wanxian Alliance couldn't completely block Li Fan's prying eyes.

Knowing and understanding the formation diagram, and being able to independently construct the formation, were two completely different concepts.

He Zhenghao, who theoretically boasted, encountered unimaginable difficulties when actually putting it into practice.

However, the Wanxian Alliance also knew the difficulty of creating this formation and dispatched six equally talented formation masters to assist.

They explored and discussed together.

Even Lu Xichan, the chief of the Strategy Formation Hall, would often come to inquire about the recent progress of the formation.

This naturally made He Zhenghao feel immense pressure, but he was also excited with a sense of passion surging in his heart.

Excavating his own talent in formations to the extreme, he successfully created the preliminary Xuantian Sealing Spirit Formation without asking the Red Dust Compassionate Immortal for help.

A beam of faint light shot out, hitting a quaint small mirror in front.

Ripples seemed to emanate from the small mirror's body, as if to resist the erosion of the faint light.

The two confronted each other for a moment, but in the end, it ended with the victory of the quaint small mirror.

He Zhenghao couldn't help but show disappointment on his face.

This caused the cultivators present to be in a flurry.

Although the first test failed, the Immortal Alliance did not blame him.

Instead, they comforted him with kind words.

They even made a promise that if the Tianxuan Sealing Spirit Formation was successfully developed, the Immortal Alliance headquarters would personally arrange for He Zhenghao to marry Heng Ruoshui.

Hearing this, He Zhenghao, who had been on the verge of death a moment ago, immediately jumped up from the ground and wanted to proceed with the next test right away.

He Zhenghao knew that although the Heng family's power was vast, it was not worth mentioning in front of the entire Wanxian Alliance.

If the Immortal Alliance headquarters intervened, the Heng family elders would have no reason to obstruct their marriage.

Imagining the scene of returning to the Heng family in glory not long after, He Zhenghao was instantly full of energy.

All the fatigue and hardship were forgotten.

This scene made Li Fan shake his head repeatedly.

"Since even He Zhenghao's previous information has been thoroughly investigated, I'm sure his joining the Congyun Mutual Aid Society and rising to prominence since then can't be hidden either."

"Let's see when Mo Rubin will come knocking."

A faint light flashed in Li Fan's eyes.

However, passively waiting was never Li Fan's style of doing things.

He decided to actively stir up some trouble to attract Mo Rubin's attention.

In a flash, Li Fan left the Lingmu Realm.

After flying rapidly for half a day, he arrived at the border region of Jiushan Prefecture.

At this moment, Jiushan Prefecture was no longer a desolate plain with no one in sight.

Since the Five Elders voluntarily apologized, Jiushan Prefecture had returned to the rule of the Wanxian Alliance.

Moreover, this was the place where the Heavenly Venerate Dharma Image first displayed its might, attracting many cultivators from the Immortal Alliance to come and observe.

Some cultivators even tried to comprehend and deduce the killing divine powers of the Heavenly Venerate Dharma Image from here.

In addition, the Wanxian Alliance had gained an absolute strategic advantage, and there was no trouble of the Five Elders' invasion for a while.

Therefore, this originally border wilderness had become quite lively.

The new floating sky city had not been completed yet, and the vast majority of cultivators were resting in a ground defensive fortress.
