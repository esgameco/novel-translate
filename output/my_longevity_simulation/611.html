Chapter 611

It was named because the valley was abundant in the raw materials needed to refine the "Elixir of Longevity".

As the name suggests, the Elixir of Longevity is a type of elixir that can prolong the life of cultivators.

It is the ultimate secret of the Medicine King Sect, and is also the key to the Medicine King Sect's prosperity in a region.

Depending on the quality of the elixir, the increase in lifespan varies. The finest Elixir of Longevity can even surpass the "Heavenly Feather Elixir," extending life by a thousand years.

In the refining process of the Elixir of Longevity, besides the high quality requirements for medicinal materials, it also requires the Medicine King True Cauldron as the core, and ninety-nine auxiliary mini cauldrons to form the "World-Healing Elixir of Longevity Formation." During the alchemy process, the "Green Life of Longevity" needs to be continuously infused.

Although the process is a bit complex, it is definitely worth it.

Compared to other life-extending wonders, the greatest feature of the Elixir of Longevity is that it can be repeatedly consumed.

Although each pill will be much less effective than the previous one, eventually losing all effectiveness.

But compared to other life-extending wonders that become completely ineffective after one consumption, the Elixir of Longevity is much better.

The vast Void Sea requires great courage to cross.

The Elixir of Longevity, along with the "Dream of Spring and Autumn Array" that can put all sect cultivators into a dormant state, are the two mainstays of the Medicine King Sect.

"The Elixir of Longevity is indeed a great treasure. Unfortunately, before the departure of the Medicine King Sect, most of them were sold and exchanged for reserve resources."

"The remaining ones are also in the hands of those elders."

Li Fan checked the inventory list of the Medicine King Cauldron and did not find any Elixir of Longevity, feeling somewhat regretful.

However, considering that most of the Medicine King Sect's cultivators are now in his hands, he has a way to make them refine elixirs for himself.

Li Fan felt somewhat relieved.

"Let's go see the current situation in the Valley of Longevity."

"If no one has discovered it all this time, that would be ideal. After thousands of years, it is really hard to imagine what kind of herbs could have grown inside."

"If it has been discovered and developed by the Ten Thousand Immortal Alliance..."

"It wouldn't matter."

Li Fan checked the materials needed to refine the Elixir of Longevity and found that they could all be purchased in the current Tianxuan Mirror.

Although the prices were a bit high, buying them back to refine into the Elixir of Longevity and then selling them would definitely bring profits.

Furthermore, Li Fan currently does not lack contribution points.

"Jiarong Border in South Fuzhou..."

"What kind of place is this?"

However, when Li Fan wanted to follow the memories of Linghu Chang to go to the Valley of Longevity.

He encountered a dilemma.

Because there was no such place within the territory of the Ten Thousand Immortal Alliance now.

"Could it be within the territory of the Five Elders Association?" Li Fan suddenly had a bad premonition.

"It's not certain. After so many years and lineage interruptions, maybe it just changed its name."

Li Fan thought again.

Narrowing his eyes, he compared the terrain around the Valley of Longevity with the current territory of the Ten Thousand Immortal Alliance.

In an attempt to finally determine the exact location of the Valley of Longevity.

After some hard work, he finally...

Helplessly gave up.

"While I know that the changes in the Xuanhuang Realm are unpredictable, I didn't expect it to be to this extent."

Li Fan took a deep breath.

Near the Valley of Longevity in Linghu Chang's memory, there is a Wormtail Mountain Range.

But now, there are many Wormtail Mountain Ranges within the Ten Thousand Immortal Alliance's territory.

Compared to thousands of years ago, the Wormtail Mountain Range seemed to have been cut into several pieces, and then dispersed in a disorderly and chaotic manner.

This was just in terms of names. Not to mention those parts that had lost their names.

Trying to find the exact location of the Valley of Longevity with his own power was like finding a needle in a haystack.

If he tried each place one by one, it would indeed take a lot of time to eventually find it.

But...

It was unnecessary.

Li Fan's time was very precious.

After much contemplation, he decided to leave it to the professionals.

Subsequently, through research in the Tianxuan Mirror, he contacted a cultivator who specialized in studying ancient and current geographical changes, Ximen Yu.

His work, "Historical Examination of Mountains and Rivers," was widely circulated.

Calculating based on the remains of several ancient sect sites, then estimating the changes in position.

The results he calculated in the book had been verified by many cultivators with very small errors.

Of course, the Xuanhuang Realm was vast. The examples in the historical examination were just to prove Ximen Yu's ability to do this.

To determine the correct current location of the locations recorded in ancient texts?

Spend some contribution points and he'll solve it for you right away!

Ximen Yu's intention was clear â€“ to earn contribution points through his unique skill.

Li Fan also appreciated people who did things for money.

In order to conceal his true purpose, he directly listed dozens of places from the ancient times in his memory for Ximen Yu to calculate.

It was the first time Ximen Yu had accepted such a large order, and he was pleasantly surprised. He even offered Li Fan a discount.

Li Fan just waved his hand, rejecting it. He only asked him to speed up the process.

Once Ximen Yu received the advance payment, he readily accepted it. He promptly ended the communication and went to calculate.

The extra contribution points were completely overlooked by Li Fan.

For him, it was just a drop in the bucket. As long as it could avoid potential trouble, it was worth it.

Reassessing over fifty locations was also a massive project.

It would take some time to get the results.

During this time, Li Fan quietly arrived on Shenhua Island.

It had been seven years since he had brought the people from the Ling Realm to this place.

Yi Feipeng, Xiao Heng, Sun Erlang, and others had grown into young men.

However, due to Li Fan's rule that they had to defeat the Sun Puppet to leave the island, they had been stuck on Shenhua Island for so long, never taking a step off the island.

Being youthful and full of blood, they were almost going crazy from being cooped up for so long.

However, they didn't dare to provoke the Sun Puppet. They only competed with each other every day to vent their energy.

Li Fan smiled and glanced at everyone.

Without attracting their attention, he then entered the secret room on the island.

Here, there were formations set up by Li Fan for protection, and only Sun Yang had the authority to enter.

Inside were jars of bright red essence blood, from which faint angry roars could be heard.

These were the essences extracted from Yi Feipeng at intervals under Sun Yang's command.

After a long time without Sun Yang's visits, a considerable amount had accumulated.

Even with the Kunpeng bloodline, how could Yi Feipeng fatten up with such frequent blood extraction?

So, in this life, Yi Feipeng was not chubby like in the previous life.

Rather, he appeared a bit lean.
