Chapter 832

After about half a cup of tea's time, Li Fan and his two companions found a hiding place in a mountain cave located at the bottom of a deep valley.

There was a formation concealing the entrance, and a secret method to hide their presence.

Ordinary cultivators, even if they deliberately searched, would find it very difficult to discover this entrance.

However, in front of Li Fan, who possessed the "Final Separation Disc", and Lin Ling, to whom all the trees in the mountains were eyes and ears, everything was full of flaws.

Yet, what surprised Li Fan once again was that there was still no one inside the cave!

Just like before, they had just escaped not long ago, and their presence had not yet dissipated!

Li Fan couldn't help but narrow his eyes.

"This is..."

"Hiding from me?"

"Able to know in advance that I'm coming to find them?"

"Is it a prophecy, or..."

Li Fan suddenly thought of the collapse and reappearance of Fuxing Mountain.

"Time reversal?"

The moment the guess appeared, Li Fan's heart couldn't help but beat violently.

However, after all, he had experienced a lot, and quickly regained his composure.

"It's just a guess, it doesn't count as the truth."

"As to what exactly it is, once we catch the father and son, we'll have the answer."

Driven by the long-lost excitement, Li Fan looked at Lin Ling again.

However, Lin Ling looked around for a while and shook her head: "They didn't leave this cave."

Hearing this, Li Fan carefully examined again: "There are also no signs of spatial jumping. Could it be..."

Li Fan and Lin Ling looked at the ground at the same time.

With a hint of a smile on the corner of his mouth, Li Fan lazily ordered, "We'll pursue."

Lin Ling nodded, enveloped in a green light, and drilled into the ground.

In theory, it should be dark underground with no light, making it difficult to distinguish east, west, south and north.

It's very easy to evade pursuit.

But coincidentally, among Li Fan's group, there was Lin Ling who could establish a relationship with the ancient trees of the earth veins!

Although the underground space was vast, it was all the territory of the ancient trees of the earth veins.

Every move was hard to escape from their eyes!

However, at the same time, Li Fan also discovered that they only walked underground for a short while before hurriedly returning to the surface.

"Did they discover that we were chasing them again?"

"They're really hard to deal with."

Thinking this way, he returned to the surface once more.

This time, the perception Lin Ling got was that Ou Daozi and his son had flown into the air.

Unfortunately, the art of flying must leave behind fluctuations of spiritual energy.

Very quickly, Lin Ling found the direction of their escape.

Taking Li Fan with her, she closely followed.

No matter how Ou Daozi and the others changed their direction of travel or laid out mazes, it was difficult to escape Lin Ling's tracking.

Finally, Ou Daozi and the others seemed to give up struggling and no longer fled.

They stood still in the clouds ahead, waiting.

"This little Lin Ling is really useful." Finally catching up, looking at the figures of an adult and a child in front, Li Fan couldn't help but sigh inwardly.

"I wonder what the senior has been painstakingly searching for?" Facing Li Fan and his three companions who surrounded him, Ou Daozi cupped his hands and asked, neither servile nor overbearing.

"Originally, it was just that the construction at home had started, and I wanted to ask you for a favor. Unexpectedly, there was an unexpected gain." Li Fan narrowed his eyes slightly, his gaze sweeping back and forth between the two.

Ou Daozi's son was only eight or nine years old. His appearance could not be considered handsome, but facing Li Fan, who was vaguely releasing a murderous aura, he was unafraid and at ease. He had a unique bearing.

Especially his eyes, which stared fiercely at Li Fan. As if...

It seemed to further validate Li Fan's guess.

Ou Daozi also noticed his son's abnormality and his face changed. He blocked his son behind him: "I don't understand what you mean!"

Li Fan snorted coldly: "The collapse of Fuxing Mountain has reappeared. It must be your son's doing, right?"

"What exactly is his ability? Could it be the legendary time reversal technique?"

Although Li Fan appeared to be only a Golden Core cultivator, both Lin Ling, a Harmonious Dao cultivator, and Tian Yang, a Hua Shen cultivator, regarded him as their leader.

Ou Daozi couldn't fathom Li Fan's true cultivation level and only said: "Senior, please don't joke around. How can there be such a thing as time reversal in this world?"

As Ou Daozi spoke, he lowered his voice on one hand, while deliberately leaving things unsaid on the other.

It was as if he was waiting for something!

Li Fan's heart was instantly on high alert: "Binding Insect!"

The target was not Ou Daozi, but his son!

The child instantly lost control, but there was no panic in his eyes.

It was as if everything was within his expectations.

Instead, he looked at Li Fan with a slight hint of mockery.

At the same time, Tian Yang's body suddenly expanded, forming a black and red spherical ball, surrounding Li Fan in the center.

"Boom!"

A huge roaring sound suddenly rang out, as if colliding with something.

Li Fan instantly felt dizzy, and cracks appeared on Tian Yang's puppet body.

Fortunately, Lin Ling took action in time to save them, otherwise they would have been shattered into pieces.

"What the hell was that!"

Li Fan cursed inwardly and glanced at the status panel. The charge level of his protective shield had not decreased.

His heart instantly settled.

The blue light of the Hua Dao Stone in his mind flashed, slowing down the recorded images countless times.

Only then did he see clearly the culprit that caused the sudden change just now.

As well as everything that happened in a flash.

The attacker that was too fast for Lin Ling and Tian Yang to react to was actually an arrow.

Ou Daozi and his son seemed to have anticipated its flight trajectory.

They deliberately lingered in front to attract Li Fan and the others to the trajectory's position.

While stalling for time, waiting for the moment when the arrow collided with Li Fan.

A yellow light emanated from Ou Daozi's son.

Then, in an instant, this arrow, without changing its forward direction, flew back to where it came from in a bizarre manner!

The yellow light enveloped Ou Daozi and his son, and with the unimaginable speed of the arrow's loss, they escaped from Li Fan and the others' encirclement in an instant!

"Master, they're gone. They've run too far away, and I can't sense their aura anymore." Lin Ling said with some self-blame.

Tian Yang also shook his head, feeling a bit guilty.

Li Fan waved his hand, not minding it.

Reflecting on what had just happened again, Li Fan vaguely knew what the other party's divine ability actually was.

It wasn't time reversal as imagined...

Or rather, it had a certain relationship.

Reversing the state of an object to a certain point in time!

For it to be bestowed upon a seven or eight-year-old child, Li Fan was not surprised but rather delighted.

Because this was the only cultivator he had seen since his reincarnation that could be related to the Dao of Time.

"Run? Can you run?"

"You may escape for a moment, but not for a lifetime!"

"Once I've set my sights on you, there's no possibility of escape!"
