It was as if they were really outside the Xuanhuang Realm in the void, surrounded by darkness and coldness.

Nascent Soul, Void Refinement, and even Unity Realm. No matter what cultivation level, once they entered, none returned.

They still had to ask if he, the Destined Sacred Emperor, agreed or not.

Luoyan State, after the Luoyan Celestial City was broken through, most of the Sacred Dynasty soldiers transferred to other battlefields.
