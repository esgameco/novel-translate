In the distant reaches of the starry sea.

Deep within the continuous sea of relics, in the Xuanshang Boat.

Relying on the Lingsheng Yuanrong technique, Zhao Xian's cultivation speed soared at an astonishing rate, breaking through the shackles of the Foundation Establishment stage.

Successfully condensing the Golden Core.

The tremors caused by the breakthrough to the Golden Core realm finally disturbed Zhao Xian's master, Zhao Wuji.

After discovering Zhao Xian's terrifyingly rapid progress, Zhao Wuji was not pleased but instead alarmed.

"Master, why?" Zhao Xian asked, full of discontent.

Zhao Wuji did not report the news of Zhao Xian's successful breakthrough to the academy, and even actively suppressed and concealed it.

This naturally made Zhao Xian very dissatisfied, and in a moment of impulse, he even wanted to fully disclose everything he had seen in his dreams.

However, after experiencing many things in his dreams, he knew that his master was trustworthy.

"Too fast, Xian'er, your cultivation is simply too fast. If it were only the late Foundation Establishment stage, it would be fine, but directly jumping to the Golden Core realm..." Zhao Wuji's face was full of worry.

Only after Zhao Xian's repeated questioning did Zhao Wuji finally explain the matter.

"Those like you, true geniuses, although extremely rare in our academy in the past, have existed before. When your master was young, I had a senior sister."

"She was also truly exceptional in talent. Even faster than your cultivation, without the process of accumulating over time, in just three years, she went from Qi Refinement directly to the perfected Golden Core realm."

"Naturally, it caused a sensation in the academy, and then..."

Upon hearing this, Zhao Xian's expression also became somewhat solemn.

Because he had never heard of such a person existing within the academy.

"Senior Sister was then conscripted. From then on, there was no news, not knowing if she was alive or dead," Zhao Wuji sighed deeply.

"Conscripted? Conscripted to where?" Zhao Xian was slightly startled.

The immortal boat had not experienced real warfare for a very long time.

He could not understand the term 'conscription' mentioned by his master.

Zhao Wuji hesitated to speak.

"Because our academy provided the most important theoretical support for the survival of the immortal boat after the great tribulation descended," Zhao Wuji said solemnly.
