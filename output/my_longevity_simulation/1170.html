After pondering for a moment, Li Fan couldn't help but shake his head, awakening from his fantasy.

Even if he had the ability to analyze the fishing pool, Li Fan would not easily choose to take action. The terrifying scene of Senior Shi Ban being mysteriously fished away was still vivid in his mind, so how could Li Fan repeat the same mistake for a moment's small gain?

With the lesson from his 119th life, before he was confident in directly confronting an existence at the True Immortal level, he would never actively provoke Them again.

In any case, the treasures inside the fishing pool could be obtained by slowly and continuously fishing. At most, it would just be a bit slower, and time was what he lacked the least.

"If the [Immeasurable, Vast and Hazy Fang] Great Formation is derived, perhaps the Derivation Jade can be used for an initial test of effectiveness," the avatar secretly pondered after briefly checking on the situation of the true body.

In the space of the Derivation Jade, it seemed as if time had lost its meaning of existence.

Every day, what could be seen was the unchanging scene of the Derivation Light Sphere rotating non-stop. And the little Derivation Loli had slowly gotten used to Li Fan's intimidation, actually gradually becoming fearless. If it weren't for the other two Harmonized Dao guardians stationed there, Li Fan would definitely let her have a taste of his methods.

However, at present, it was not advisable to be too high-profile, so he had to endure a little.

"Fellow Daoist Sun, calculate the time. In the next few days, a Dharma Transmitter should come to inspect. At that time, we still need to pay attention," Hua Yueye, who had been silent, suddenly remembered something and transmitted a reminder.

Previously, when taking office, he was indeed informed of the precautions regarding the regular inspections by the Dharma Transmitters. So Li Fan was not surprised and nodded to indicate his understanding.

"After the Dharma Transmitter completes the inspection, the use of the Dharma Audience Jade Tablet should be restarted. By then, it should become livelier here."

"Those who can come here and request the help of the Derivation Jade to derive techniques either have an illustrious family background or heaven-defying luck. In the end, their future achievements will definitely be extraordinary. While doing our job well, it doesn't hurt to leave a good connection. Maybe in a hundred years when we go out, we can make use of this relationship," Hua Yueye kindly pointed out.

At this time, Elder Zhang Xu also echoed, "That's the principle. In the hundreds of years, although the Derivation Jade space remains unchanged, the outside world may have already undergone drastic changes. Having one more path is always good."

Although they were already Harmonized Dao powerhouses, those who would come here to guard for five hundred years basically would not be the most core members of their families. Making preparations for the future was also reasonable.

Half a month later, the Dharma Transmitter came to inspect as scheduled.

Dharma Transmitter Lang, Li Fan had met this person once at the Dharma Transmitter Council.

Ignoring the three guardians, Dharma Transmitter Lang came to the little Derivation Loli with a smile.

The little loli rolled her eyes, then covered her ears with both hands, looking as if she couldn't hear anything no matter what he said.

Dharma Transmitter Lang seemed to be accustomed to this and was not angered.

A row of silver needles appeared by his hand.

Dharma Transmitter Lang picked up one of them and then pierced it towards the Derivation Jade's head.

The little loli's body trembled slightly, but it seemed as if some force was restraining her. Unable to resist, she could only let the silver needles fall one by one on different acupuncture points of her body.
