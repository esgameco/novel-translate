Chapter 1027: The Bloody Secret of Chaoyuan

After realizing how powerful the top-grade Dao Transformation Stone was, Li Fan wasted no time in capturing the other two people.

Unfortunately, they didn't hit the jackpot. The Dao Transformation Stones they carried were still only of top-grade quality.

Li Fan repeated the process.

After absorbing a total of three top-grade Dao Transformation Stones, Li Fan once again felt a long-lost qualitative change, as if he had broken through a major realm.

However, he didn't become complacent. He re-analyzed his experiences in this world once more.

With his enhanced planning abilities, he made general projections on how to successfully break free from the "True Immortal's Net" in the future, return to the true world smoothly, and even reap benefits from it.

"But I still haven't reached the level of the Dao Transformation Stone's source, which can project the levels of the real world. I still need to adapt to the circumstances."

Li Fan remained silent, with numerous thoughts swirling in his mind.

After secluding himself for three full days, he first summoned his avatar, Li Qing.

"You can go to the Congyun Sea first and scare away the Chaoyuan Sect in advance. If there are no accidents, the ruins of the Chaoyuan Sect will still fly towards the Jiushan State."

"After the cultivators from various small worlds forcibly advance to the Golden Core realm by taking the elixirs from the Medicine King Sect, you can lead them there."

"With your protection, they should be able to break out of the mountain of corpses and river of blood left by the Chaoyuan Sect. When they reappear, they will be able to collectively advance to the Nascent Soul realm. At that time, it will be much easier to explore the secrets from the bloody river."

The avatar's mind also instantly recalled everything about the Chaoyuan Sect.

The Chaoyuan Sect was an ancient cave mansion hidden in the Congyun Sea, beneath the three fault lines on the seabed.

Before the Congyun Sea was about to suffer a calamity, it fled far away and landed in the Jiushan State.

Shuo Feng, Zhang Haobo, and Su Xiaomei once explored together in search of a breakthrough opportunity.

From their experiences, Li Fan also learned the secrets of the Chaoyuan Sect.

The Chaoyuan Sect was a native sect of the Xuanhuang Realm.

Since the great calamity descended, they had disappeared without a trace, as if they had vanished from the world.

In fact, the entire sect had been transformed into a strange state, existing between the real and the illusory, isolated from the outside world.

Time seemed to have stagnated within it, in an attempt to avoid the disaster.

This was the Chaoyuan Sect's original plan.

However, unexpected changes always occur.

First, the area where they were located was slapped into the Congyun Sea by the Dharma Transmission Heavenly Venerate, causing the Chaoyuan Sect to be buried deep underground.

Later, perhaps due to the influence of the merging forces of the two realms, various bizarre changes gradually occurred within the Chaoyuan Sect.

When Su Xiaomei and the others entered the ruins of the Chaoyuan Sect, although the cultivators inside seemed to be living normally,

they had actually all turned into faceless blood faces, losing their own appearances.

And they themselves had no awareness of it whatsoever.

They would suddenly launch attacks as if they had gone mad.

As if they had lost their minds, regardless of their cultivation level, they only attacked like beasts, using their physical bodies and claws.

Although they wouldn't use divine powers, these faceless blood faces were truly difficult to deal with.

They were extremely fast and would recover quickly from injuries.

Only by cutting off their entire blood face could they finally be killed.

After the faceless blood faces were eliminated, they would transform into a red light that attached to the cultivator who killed them.

These red lights were actually the source power of the Chaoyuan Sect's world.

After leaving the Chaoyuan Sect, these seized red lights would also follow...
