Here is the English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Despite the need for caution and effort, the Termination Separation Disc had already considered the various environmental factors that could affect the formation.

Its database contained numerous variations, allowing it to quickly find the solution by comparison and retrieval.

Even if the formation was quite strange, the Termination Separation Disc's terrifying computing power could crack it in an instant.

In fact, unknown formations could further strengthen the Termination Separation Disc.

Witnessing the entire process of the Termination Separation Disc breaking the formation, Li Fan even felt a sense of sorrow as a formation master.

He felt that all the formations he had painstakingly studied over the generations had become mere jokes in its presence.

"With this thing, no formation master in the world can be proud of themselves anymore."

"Fortunately, it is in my hands now."

Li Fan's gaze flickered as he passed through the Returning Element Formation and approached Tian Yang.

At this time, Tian Yang's puppet was not as gigantic as it would be in a few years, only the size of a child, seemingly Tian Yang's original form.

The raging flames were guided by the Returning Element Formation, turning into strands of scorching red energy that were absorbed by the puppet's body.

"Little junior brother!"

During the journey, Li Fan had already activated the Myriad Furnace technique, transforming into the appearance of Tian Yang's senior brother.

However, Tian Yang had been born too early, appearing somewhat underdeveloped, with a weak spirit and cultivation only reaching the late Nascent Soul stage.

But Li Fan didn't mind. Rather than wasting time slowly absorbing the earth fire at the bottom of the sea, it would be better for Tian Yang to follow behind him and prepare for an early Dao Unification.

Li Fan knew of at least five hidden sources of the Primal Energies of Heaven and Earth from the memories of the previous self. If given enough power, it would not be difficult to instantly create five or six Dao Unification cultivators.

However, the emergence of Dao Unification cultivators would inevitably attract the attention of the powerful figures in the Mysterious Yellow Realm. Especially at this time, when the transmission of the Dao had not yet left the Mysterious Yellow Realm, caution was required.

Sitting on Tian Yang's shoulder, Li Fan headed towards the entrance of another small world he remembered, as a safe and hidden base was essential for establishing a true Interworld Alliance.

The location had to be suitable, with abundant spiritual energy but not too strong local resistance forces. Fortunately, Li Fan knew of such a place.

The entrance to the small world was located in the Stone Forest within Stone Linden City, where the remnants of the Heavenly Mechanism Sect had once been discovered. Li Fan had been there in the previous life but had not noticed the spatial passage, testament to its concealment.

In fact, this small world had only revealed itself after the surrender of the Taiyan Sect, as even the cultivators of the Myriad Immortal Alliance might not have been able to easily find it without the help of the Heavenly Jade Mirror.

After much searching in the winding underground cave with its countless stalactites, Li Fan finally located the precise position of the entrance.

"It seems the unique terrain of this place has a certain effect on the concealment of the spatial passage. This suits my needs well."

Li Fan lightly pointed at one of the stone pillars, causing a faint spatial fluctuation to appear. Then, with the Tian Yang puppet, he flew into the passage.
