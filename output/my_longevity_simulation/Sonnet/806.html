Chapter 806

Here is my translation of the Chinese excerpt to English:

Under the bated breath of the crowd, that immortal master shouted angrily and deflected the huge meteor with just one hand!

Two enormous forces collided in an instant.

Countless small fragments were blasted off from the giant meteor.

As they were about to rain down below, that immortal master acted again!

One hand slowly raised the giant meteor, while the other hand instantly emitted rays of white light,

Disintegrating the showering fragments below.

In just a few breaths of time, a world-ending catastrophe was averted.

After a brief moment of shock, the mortals burst into grateful tears.

They all knelt down, kowtowing towards the figure in the sky, repeatedly crying out: "Thank you, immortal master, for saving our lives!"

The existence of cultivators was no secret in the capital of Dali.

After all, immortals would descend every so often.

Many years ago, there was even an incident of an "enraged immortal causing rivers of blood."

So when this immortal master saved the entire city, the mortals felt both gratitude and trepidation.

After all, it was said that to immortals, mortals were no different from ants.

Yet now, an immortal had acted to save the lives of ants.

How could one not be astonished?

The mortals knelt on the ground, not daring to rise.

After dissipating the meteor's impact force, the immortal master casually tossed it into the uninhabited wilderness outside the city.

Amidst the great tremors on the ground, the immortal swiftly flew towards the central volcano.

Soon after, the billowing black smoke from the volcano gradually disappeared.

The raging volcano calmed down once more.

Everyone in the world of Dali who witnessed this scene couldn't help but cheer in relief at having survived the calamity.

The emperor of Dali led the people to line up in the city, awaiting the immortal master's arrival.

Unfortunately, they waited three days and three nights, but the immortal never came.

The emperor was anxious, unsure if he had done something wrong.

He tossed and turned for several nights, unable to sleep well.

Until seven days later, someone reported that a small hill had risen from flat ground outside the city.

And it seemed someone was living on that hill.

Upon hearing this, the emperor's heart shook violently.

Knowing this was the work of an immortal, he hurriedly led his officials there.

"Those with affinity may see me."

"Those without affinity, please leave."

A young girl of over ten years old timidly said to the emperor.

Among the line of officials, Scholar Su immediately raised his head upon hearing this voice.

Seeing that it was indeed his younger sister Su Yuqing speaking, he nearly went weak in the knees and collapsed to the ground.

Fortunately, his quick-witted friend supported him in time.

"Scholar Su, could this immortal girl be someone you know?"

Scholar Su's mouth went dry, and despite his vast knowledge, he could not utter a single word at that moment.

After bravely dismissing the court officials, Su Yuqing finally relaxed with a sigh of relief.

She patted her chest, signaling to her departing brother, then turned to look at the thatched hut on the hill, feeling rather vexed.

Not long ago, this immortal master had suddenly appeared at the Su residence.

Claiming that Yuqing had immortal affinity, she wasted no time and brought her to this hill.

The master then passed down an immortal cultivation method, instructing her to calmly cultivate.

But now her mind was in turmoil, how could she possibly cultivate?

Here is the English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The thoughts in her heart!

After much deliberation, Su Yuqing still mustered up her courage and came to the mountain hut, knocking on the door.

The door automatically opened, and the immortal elder was sitting cross-legged in the air, eyes closed in meditation.

"What is it?"

"Greetings, Immortal Master." Su Yuqing first kowtowed in respect, then gathered her courage to say, "Please allow me to return home. I have no desire to seek immortality or the Dao. I only wish to stay with my family and live out my life peacefully."

These words, coming from the mouth of a girl in her teens, seemed so out of place.

But when set against Su Yuqing's own demeanor, they did not seem so incongruous.

After saying this, Su Yuqing's heart suddenly began to pound.

She had seen with her own eyes how the Immortal Master had easily dealt with the threat of the falling meteor. If she had angered him...

No, the Immortal Master had used his powers to save so many people in the city. He must be a good person, right?

Su Yuqing thought to herself.

The mountain hut fell into an eerie silence.

After a long while, he slowly spoke: "Immortal affinity cannot be forced."

"If you truly do not wish to cultivate, I will not force you."

"However, when you one day witness the tragic deaths of your loved ones, do not regret it then."

Su Yuqing was slightly taken aback, her face paling: "The tragic deaths of my loved ones?"

The Immortal Master did not explain further, but closed his eyes again: "You may go now."

Su Yuqing felt a gust of wind, and in an instant, she found herself outside the hut.

The door slammed shut, leaving Su Yuqing feeling bewildered.

She walked down the mountain path in a daze, her mind filled with the Immortal Master's words.

As she was about to leave the immortal mountain, she hesitated.

"The Immortal Master surely would not lie to me."

"The tragic deaths of my loved ones..."

Unbidden, the terrifying scene of the volcanic eruption, with the dark clouds obscuring the sky, resurfaced in Su Yuqing's mind.

She shuddered, understanding dawning on her.

Hurriedly, she changed direction and ran back towards the mountain hut.

"Immortal Master, will the Central Volcano erupt again?"

She called out to the door, but there was no response.

Su Yuqing tried to push the door open, but it would not budge.

Growing increasingly anxious, yet not daring to act rashly before the immortal, Su Yuqing knelt down and kowtowed.

"Immortal Master, this humble girl is willing to follow and cultivate diligently!"

Still, there was no reply.

Gritting her teeth, Su Yuqing maintained the position, unmoving.

As the only daughter of an esteemed scholar, Su Yuqing had been doted on since birth.

But now, she seemed to see the scene of the Great Rift being destroyed in the volcanic eruption.

Her gaze grew resolute, and she stubbornly persisted.

One hour, two hours...

Until after four or five hours, her consciousness began to fade.

Just before she lost consciousness, she thought she heard the sound of the hut door opening.

Su Yuqing had a dream.

In the dream, the Great Rift faced its end.

The volcanic plume shot up to the heavens. Lava flowed recklessly, and there were no living beings left on the land.

It was like a living hell.

This horrific vision jolted her awake from the nightmare.

"Do you see now? That is the future of the Great Rift," the Immortal Master said calmly.

"Please, Immortal Master, save the Great Rift!" Su Yuqing's eyes were now moist with tears as she kowtowed and pleaded.
