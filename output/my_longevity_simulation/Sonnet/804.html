After a while, a strange look appeared in Li Fan's eyes. Perhaps it was because he had previously ascended to the Yuan Ying realm and had an inner celestial realm within his body. Or maybe it was because he had once, in Xu Bai's form, merged with the Heavenly Dao of a small world.

Li Fan's perception of "inner realms" and "small worlds" had become much more acute. With this observation, he suddenly realized the source of the odd feeling he got whenever he saw this volcano in the Separated Realm.

"This volcano does not actually belong to the Separated Realm. It comes from another small world!"

"No wonder it always felt out of place and discordant!"

In fact, the surrounding terrain already explained the issue. On the boundless plain, a ferocious volcano jutted out. It was clearly an abnormal phenomenon. Ordinary people would simply attribute it to the profound workings of Heaven and Earth.

Li Fan became interested. "What's going on? Why has a volcano from another small world suddenly appeared in the Separated Realm?"

Li Fan casually set up protective barriers around himself, then flew into the volcanic crater. The scorching, churning magma could not harm him in the slightest.

Li Fan delved deep into the volcano, trying to find the source of the anomaly from within. It was not until the very bottom, where the volcano met the earthly veins, that he finally made a discovery.

"This is..."

Even with his Golden Core late stage's defenses, he could still feel the heat.

"It's not spiritual energy, but it is a transcendent power."

"The Separated Realm is a purely mortal world without the conditions to produce this earth fire."

Li Fan put the earth fire back and carefully examined this space. Compared to the crater, the magma flow here was relatively calm and tranquil, only occasionally bubbling up.

And at the edge of the magma sea, there was a very faint boundary line hidden in the orange-red lava, utterly inconspicuous. If one did not deliberately search for it, it was easy to overlook its existence.

From this boundary line emanated a power of the same origin as the earth fire.

"Another spatial channel?"

Li Fan pondered thoughtfully. After a moment of contemplation and setting up protective formations, he leapt into it.

Boom!

In a flash, as soon as he entered, a fireball came smashing towards Li Fan. But its force was not very strong, deflected by the protective formations.

Li Fan had thought it was someone attacking at first and was on guard. But looking closely, he relaxed as he was captivated by the scenery of this world.

This was a world devoid of life, purely belonging to the element of "fire."

A churning sea of scorching magma, the hellish fires never ceasing. There were occasional thunderous explosion sounds as massive gouts of lava erupted skyward, bursting apart in the air and leaving countless streaks of crimson.
