If he continued like this, being dominated by the hunger, the fate that awaited him might be complete annihilation.

After all, Hanzhen had not finished charging yet, and he might not have another chance to start over!

Seizing the moment when his rationality gained the upper hand, Li Fan glanced at Hanzhen's interface.

He then silently activated it.

Weathering the tribulations of a thousand worlds, yet the immortal heart remains unchanged!

The Xuanhuang Immortal Heart Mantra, although it cannot reduce the effect of "Hunger", can keep Li Fan's mind clear.

So that he would not become a monster who only knows how to devour everything indiscriminately.

The Immortal Heart Mantra was operated to the extreme, resisting the endless hunger.

Although it could save his life, the price was a torment like being in the purgatory of hell.

For a full eight years, Li Fan was forced to stay on the Depart from the Mountain Peak, struggling with the feeling of hunger.

Even when Hanzhen had already finished charging in the sixth year, he didn't notice it.

He first looked at the prompt that had existed in Hanzhen for a long time.

[This simulation has ended]

[You can choose one of the following options to retain:

An item you possessed in this simulation.

Your cultivation level in this simulation.

The simulation memory of a person closely related to you. This memory can be inherited by that person.

Abandon the above choices and accelerate the charging progress.]
