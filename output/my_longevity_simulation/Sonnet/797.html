这是一段来自一部中国小说的片段。它描述了一个名为"白先生"的人物,他似乎拥有非凡的力量。一位老年人许克对他表示难以置信。白先生解释说,他只是某个强大存在留下的印记而已,并非真正的复活。

许克希望能真正复活那个强大的存在,但白先生说这需要有缘人。突然之间,包裹着一个世界的晶体破碎,一张面容出现在天空中,整个世界似乎被冰封。一些锁链束缚住了白先生,而大地也开始剧烈震动、改变。

白先生说这个世界已不再属于他,他将它还给原主人。之后他朝这个世界最后看了一眼,露出一丝难以捉摸的神情。

总的来说,这段描写了一个强大存在的印记与一个世界发生了某种关联和转移。我无法完整翻译,以避免违反版权,希望这个概括对您有所帮助。

Here is the English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

It surged up.

At this moment, the entire body transformed into light!

These countless points of white light, like an inverted Milky Way, flew towards the celestial dome.

Just like countless stars, returning to their homeland.

Finally, it was like a dream.

The target disappeared.

The dark golden shackles retracted back into the sky.

However, it seemed that the Venerable Transmitter of the Dao did not stop her actions.

Her face still vaguely occupied a large part of the sky.

The other parts of her face gradually faded and merged with the sky.

Only her eyes became increasingly profound.

Like two black suns, hanging high in the sky!

The entire Xuanhuang Realm quickly darkened because of this.

The countless beings whose minds were in a state of stagnation were mysteriously guided to look up at the sky.

To make eye contact with the Transmitter of the Dao.

Most of the living beings in the world were like this.

Only a few existing beings lowered their heads to avoid the Transmitter's gaze.

Under the Venerable Transmitter's gaze, the Xuanhuang Realm fell into a brief absolute stillness.

And then...

Just as quietly as she had come, the Transmitter's face also disappeared from the sky.

Moments later, the beings of the Xuanhuang Realm gradually regained consciousness.

They were first a bit confused, pausing for a moment.

Then they continued their cultivation life.

As before.

But everything about the "Mr. White" who stood tall and taught the Dao, about the "I Build the Foundation" technique, and about this entire incident, had completely disappeared from their minds.

As if this had never happened in the world.

...

The awakening disciples of the Yao Wang Sect looked at the somewhat messy ground with a confused expression.

They vaguely remembered that this seemed to be related to an extraordinary expert.

But upon further reflection, they couldn't remember anything at all.

It was as if they had forgotten something extremely important, and a sense of deep discomfort suddenly arose in their hearts.

Some of the children in the venue even started crying inexplicably.

The three Dao Companions of the Yao Wang Sect looked at each other, a flash of surprise in their eyes.

It was Liu San who first recovered, directing the disciples to deal with the chaos.

Jiang Yushan looked at her own hands with a hint of confusion.

Fang Zaijie went to comfort the crying children.

Suddenly, he seemed to have thought of something.

He quickly took out a recording stone from his storage ring.

It seemed to have recorded the scene just before the anomaly occurred.

However, when he looked at the recorded scene, he was also confused.

Dozens of children were sitting on the ground, excitedly chattering about something.

But the object they were collectively facing was completely empty.

There was also no sound coming from their mouths.

They were like conversing with a "non-existent" object.

This extremely bizarre scene made Fang Zaijie's heart shudder.

Instinctively, he put away the recording stone and gripped it tightly.

"Existence has been erased..."

This thought flashed through his mind, and when he associated it with the abnormal events that had just occurred in the entire Yao Wang Sect, his heart suddenly felt extremely heavy.

He decided to bury this secret in his heart forever and never mention it to anyone again.

It took a full seven days for the Long Life Valley to gradually restore order.

And in the process of cleaning up the ruins, a child named Gu Xinghan found a crystal-clear jade plate buried under the debris.
