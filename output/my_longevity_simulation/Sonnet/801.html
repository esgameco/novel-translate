"The fundamental reason," said Li Fan.

While relying on his pure willpower, Li Fan suppressed the feeling of hunger. He pondered slowly.

The dispersion of attention is also remarkably effective in overcoming the sensation of hunger. Recalling the experiences of his previous lives, Li Fan considered the plan for his future actions.

He had witnessed the manifestation of the Dharma transmission, and also seen the power of the true immortals. As his horizons expanded, Li Fan's course of action had to be correspondingly adjusted.

Soon, another four years had passed. He absorbed another low-grade Dao stone, causing the Dao stone crystals in his mind to become even more brilliant and dazzling. Li Fan activated the Returning to Truth technique once more.

After returning to the peak of the Separation Mountain, his body and spirit felt no abnormalities. Unwilling to let his guard down, Li Fan carefully examined himself from top to bottom. He then circulated the Vast Violet Qi to cleanse his spiritual soul, eliminating any potential hidden dangers. This process took an entire year.

Standing on the mountain top, by the cliff's edge, Li Fan mused, "The Returning to Truth technique has been performed only a dozen or so times, yet it feels as if an eternity has passed, far longer than the century I spent building my foundation."

"Hunger..." The complex symbol of "Hunger" suddenly appeared in Li Fan's mind, recalling the desolate wilderness of the Immortal Ruins.

He dared not rashly attempt to experiment with it, only contemplating it in his mind. "I once saw the character 'Chaos' on a broken stone in the center of the Mist Maze. And above the Immortal Ruins, there exists a character of 'Hunger'."

"This 'Hunger' character may very well be the ultimate reason behind the Immortal Ruins' constant attraction of external objects."

"Immortal techniques? But that would be too terrifying."

Li Fan's eyelids twitched slightly. Calming his mind, he felt something was amiss.

"The 'Hunger' character in the Immortal Ruins is a complex symbol. Although I don't understand its meaning, I instinctively grasped its implication the moment I saw it. However, the 'Chaos' character in the Mist Maze is the same as the common script used in the cultivation world."

"The 'Hunger' character could completely plunge countless cultivation worlds into ruin. While the 'Chaos' I encountered was merely a reflection monster that couldn't even reach the Dao Unification realm."

Li Fan suddenly realized, "It's not the original 'Chaos', but rather an imitation of the purposeful Immortal technique 'Chaos'."

Falling silent, Li Fan contemplated the implications. Even his own crude imitation of the technique had caused considerable upheaval in the Mysterious Yellow Realm. Then what kind of terrifying power would the true 'Chaos' character, comparable to the 'Hunger' in the Immortal Ruins, possess?

Immortals were already suppressed and their fates unknown by a single 'Hunger' character. The severing of the Immortal Dao, the mysterious disconnection of other cultivation realms - could all this be related to 'Chaos'?

In an instant, Li Fan was lost in endless contemplation. This harvest was too bountiful, even exceeding his limits of comprehension. It was not necessarily a good thing, as even the Dharma Transmission Venerable did not know how to confront this.

"I must not be overly ambitious. I need to step by step, carefully make my way out of the life-and-death crisis in the Mysterious Yellow Realm first," Li Fan thought, feeling somewhat melancholic.
