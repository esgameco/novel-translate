Chapter 360

Li Fan paused for a moment, then held his breath and slowly approached.

Until he reached the broken stone, he did not feel any changes in himself.

However, Li Fan's vigilance in his heart still did not diminish.

This enchanting area was filled with various strange phenomena, so he could not be careless.

Without touching the broken stone, he observed it carefully from a distance.

The broken stone was partially buried in the soil, roughly forming a square shape about an arm's length wide.

Its edges showed clear signs of being broken.

It seemed as if it was deliberately cut from a complete stone.

The word "chaos" seemed to have some other characters carved in front and behind it.

Unfortunately, precisely the standalone character "chaos" was separated, and no trace of the strokes of the preceding and following characters could be seen.

"Looking at the appearance of this broken stone, it doesn't seem like a natural break. Instead, it looks like it was deliberately cut apart."

"Chaos."

"Is the area centered around this circular giant pit, filled with distorted directions and even bizarre phenomena like human body transformation, related to this word?"

"If one word can trigger so many strange phenomena..."

"What relationship does this 'chaos character stone' have with the Tianyi?"

After pondering for a moment, Li Fan decided to temporarily ignore it and check if there were any signs of an "exit" around.

Unfortunately, at the bottom of the circular giant pit, besides the sticky, unknown substances everywhere, there was only this chaotic character stone.

"The reverse-folded monster now acts solely on instinct; it shouldn't be deceiving me."

After a moment of thought, Li Fan returned to the side of the broken stone, activated his spiritual power, and lifted it from the ground.

It was not like any celestial treasure; it was as heavy as an ordinary stone.

And after it was pulled out, there was still no expected exit revealed beneath the broken stone.

No abnormal situation occurred.

"It's strange."

Li Fan set down the chaotic character stone and rubbed his chin.

Time was running out, and Li Fan didn't waste time searching for other possibilities.

Instead, he focused intently on the stone in front of him, pondering where the exit might be.

With little time to waste, after a moment, Li Fan extended his finger and shot a black light towards it.

As he retreated, he observed the changes that occurred when the Extinction Sword and the chaotic character stone collided.

The black sword light flew in an instant to the front of the broken stone.

Right before the collision, the black light suddenly distorted.

Under Li Fan's gaze, three extremely familiar energies were resolved from the black light.

Madness, darkness, swirling...

It was the three forces that made up the Grand Extinction Sword: Sky Devil, Ink Kill, and the power of the Five Elements.

Under the influence of the chaotic character stone, the black light gradually faded away.

Then, these energies that were supposed to be under Li Fan's control started to struggle against each other, each fighting on its own.

At the same time, an inexplicable ripple burst forth from the chaotic character stone.

Sweeping towards the outside of the circular giant pit.

Caught off guard, Li Fan was unable to avoid it and was struck through.

His expression changed suddenly, thinking he had been ambushed.

However, to his surprise, the ripple was more bark than bite.

He only felt a tingling sensation as if ants were crawling all over his body, but after a quick circulation of spiritual energy, it was soon suppressed.

Li Fan paused, staring at the chaotic character stone thoughtfully.

"The chaos character has dissipated, only the form remains."

"But even so, it can still affect my Extinction Sword."

Li Fan glanced towards where the three forces had been tangled together.

In the recent disturbance, they had already destroyed each other in the struggle.

The last piece of the Black Death talisman he wore had dimmed to the point of almost disappearing.

For anyone else experiencing this situation, they would have been overwhelmed by the chaos and unable to think clearly.

However, with "Huan Zhen" as his underlying foundation, Li Fan remained unfazed.

Without having to consider his own crisis, Li Fan's thoughts raced as he continued to ponder.

After trying various methods, when the Black Death talisman completely shattered.

The white mist that had been omnipresent around him, with no constraints, began to consume Li Fan's lifespan.

In just a few breaths, Li Fan's physical age had jumped from 51 to 52.

Although half the reason for the passage of 51 years had already passed, the terrifying speed at which the white mist devoured his lifespan was evident.

His life was rapidly slipping away before his eyes, yet it didn't make Li Fan panic. Instead, it made his mind even calmer.

When Li Fan's lifespan had reached a hundred years, suddenly, his eyes lit up.

He leaped towards the broken stone, using his hand as a pen to trace the character "chaos" on the stone's surface.

Each stroke quickly formed.

A faint golden light flashed and disappeared.

The chaotic character on the broken stone seemed to regain some luster.

What astonished Li Fan was that the moment the character chaos reappeared, his spiritual energy was instantly depleted.

If not for the Nine Laws Golden Pill sensing the danger and quickly replenishing his body with spiritual energy from the Five Elements, he would have been drained instantly.

Even so, Li Fan felt a blackout and almost fell to the ground.

By absorbing his entire spiritual energy, the chaotic character stone also underwent an inexplicable change.

The characters seemed to come alive, exuding a terrifying aura naturally.

The entire stone became ethereal.

Emitting a dream-like light.

Seeing this, Li Fan quickly hid inside to avoid further loss of lifespan.

Perhaps because the chaotic character stone this time was absorbing the spiritual energy within Li Fan, he did not sense its influence when he approached the broken stone.

However, he continuously heard creaking sounds from the surroundings. The sticky substances underground, as if imbued with life, started to twist and move mysteriously.

Li Fan keenly noticed that the motionless mutated corpses standing in the circular stone pit were trembling slightly, as if they would come back to life at any moment.

As the instigator of all these happenings, Li Fan couldn't help but feel his hair stand on end.

"This is not a place to linger."

Observing the flashing light and the chaotic character stone between reality and illusion, Li Fan had a vague feeling in his heart.

It felt like a door, and he could enter it at any time.

There was no sense of danger ahead, only the sole chance of survival inside.

Without hesitation, Li Fan flew towards it.

As he expected, the stone felt as if it didn't exist.

Li Fan passed straight through it.

His vision blurred, and an endless white mist filled his sight.

Finally, Li Fan left that eerie realm and returned to the normal white mist barrier.
