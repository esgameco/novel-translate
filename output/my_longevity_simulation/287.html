Chapter 287

"This time, after thoroughly resolving his obsession, I'm afraid the next time I enter the Fallen Immortal Realm, it will be in a different location."

"I wonder what encounters I will have at that time."

Now it seems that being trapped in the same time and space within the Fallen Immortal Realm is actually a rare opportunity.

If there was only one chance to try, and one couldn't repeatedly strategize and uncover the various hidden clues within the time and space,

It would be extremely difficult to gain so many rewards.

Of course, the premise is being able to escape from the trap of endless reincarnation.

As for how exactly, I'll know once I enter again next time.

Sensing something in his heart, Li Fan frowned.

Perhaps because there were two harvests this time, the time limit for entering nearly doubled.

It's estimated that the restriction will disappear after about a year.

"Good things are hard to come by, so I can only wait patiently." Li Fan sighed inwardly.

...

Desolate Island.

Ever since Su Xiaomei and Zhang Haobo left, Xiao Heng seemed to have received some kind of stimulation, and his cultivation became even more diligent.

The bottleneck that had been troubling him for a long time finally dissipated, and he could advance to the Golden Core stage at any time.

Feeling something in his heart, he went to the central waters of the Congyun Sea to seek his own opportunity.

At the same time, the Yin sisters also reached the perfection of Qi Refining, and obtained the top-grade human wonders "Treasure-Gathering Gauze" and "Divine Escape Needle" from the White Bone Pioneer Realm, and began their own Closed Door Foundation Establishment journey.

On the desolate island, the only one left who had not established a foundation was Su Changyu.

However, he had just broken through to the late stage of Qi Refining.

Foundation establishment was still a long way off.

For a time, even with Su Changyu's calm temperament, he couldn't help but feel at a loss.

"When Xiaomei and Xiao Heng come back, they should all be in the Golden Core stage, right?"

"I'm still lingering in the Qi Refining stage."

"And as time goes on, the gap between me and them will only grow wider."

Su Changyu stood on the beach, watching the tides rise and fall, feeling a bit bitter in his heart.

He was not a saint.

The people around him were all exceptionally talented. Staying with them all day, how could he not feel pressure?

It's just that in the past, he always suppressed these feelings of depression in his heart and never told anyone.

Now that the familiar people around him had suddenly disappeared,

Even the two little sisters who joined the desolate island later were going to establish their foundation a step ahead of him.

Su Changyu finally broke down a little.

After all, he was just an ordinary young man.

Feeling troubled, he couldn't even muster the energy to cultivate.

For the first time, Su Changyu flew away from the desolate island and wandered aimlessly.

Along the way, when he encountered other cultivators, he would instinctively avoid them.

Like this, he didn't know how long had passed. When he came back to his senses, he had unknowingly arrived near a desolate island.

The island was not big, and there was not much vegetation on it.

Only a square black stone building stood on it.

Seeing that this island seemed to be inhabited, in order to avoid causing misunderstandings, Su Changyu was about to go around it from afar.

Just as he was about to leave, flames suddenly burst out from the black stone building on the island.

And there seemed to be faint cries for help accompanying it.

After hesitating for a moment, Su Changyu finally landed and rushed into the square building.

Inside the building, there were transparent cages distributed.

Inside were all kinds of grotesque and horrifying creatures.

Two-headed giant snakes, sheep-headed sea turtles, giant coiled maggots, strange species composed of six heads and hands, etc.

For some reason, these creatures had all lost their vitality.

Only their corpses lay quietly in the cages.

Su Changyu's expression turned solemn as he remained vigilant and continued to go deeper.

All the way inside, he arrived in front of a huge rectangular transparent cage.

In these four prison cells, there were several ragged mortals.

Some had already died, while some were still struggling to survive.

The raging flames were starting to spread from the central giant cage to the surroundings.

The cries for help that Su Changyu heard just now came from these mortals.

Seeing this, Su Changyu made a water-controlling hand seal, wanting to extinguish the flames.

Who knew that these flames were not ordinary flames.

After encountering water, not only did they not go out, but they burned even more vigorously.

Su Changyu's face changed. While manipulating the water, he also used the spiritual energy in his body.

Only then did he slightly block the raging flames.

But he was still unable to extinguish them.

Before long, Su Changyu was having a hard time persisting.

The output of spiritual energy became weaker and weaker, and the flames became more and more intense.

Seeing an immortal master coming to save them, the mortals in the prison cells rekindled their hope for life and kowtowed to Su Changyu one after another.

Su Changyu gritted his teeth and persisted.

Just at the moment before his spiritual energy was exhausted, two strange voices sounded at the same time.

"Why? Why would you, a cultivator, go and save these mortals?"

"Why? Why would you, a cultivator, go and save these mortals?"

...

Su Changyu was suddenly startled. Looking in the direction of the voice, he saw a cultivator with white hair who looked a bit old, not knowing when he had arrived behind him.

He stared at Su Changyu in a somewhat crazed manner and muttered.

Su Changyu didn't know how to answer for a moment.

He had left Dali and the life of mortals for just over ten years.

During this time, he had been staying on the desolate island, living with his relatives and friends.

He didn't have any thoughts about the distinction between immortals and mortals.

Seeing Su Changyu's somewhat embarrassed appearance, that person sneered.

With a casual wave of his hand, the raging flames instantly dissipated.

Su Changyu immediately breathed a sigh of relief.

"Follow me."

"Follow me."

Again, two voices sounded at the same time.

A door on the side suddenly opened, and Su Changyu followed and walked in.

The chaotic scene inside shocked him.

It seemed to have been deliberately destroyed by someone, with broken corpse remains all over the floor.

Some belonged to humans, and some belonged to strange beasts.

Even in the middle of the passage, there was a horrifying little dog with human hands for limbs and eyes all over its belly.

That white-haired cultivator didn't seem to care much about this.

He casually swept these things to the side, clearing a path.

Su Changyu carefully followed.

At the end of the passage was a staircase extending downward.

That person looked back at Su Changyu, smiled inexplicably as if signaling him to follow,

Then slowly walked down.

There was an eerie atmosphere everywhere. Su Changyu took a deep breath, thought for a moment, and still chose to follow the stairs down.

As his eyes adapted to the darkness of the space below, a chill immediately surged in Su Changyu's heart when he saw the surroundings clearly.

In the not-so-spacious place, hundreds of corpses were densely packed.

Most of these corpses were already shattered, like torn rag dolls.

The various parts of the bodies were scattered on the ground without any pattern, and the situation was as terrifying as hell.
