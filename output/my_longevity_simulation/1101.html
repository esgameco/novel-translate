Chapter 1099: Seeking Opportunities in the Dark

"The more frequently one begs for the power of darkness, the more cracks it will create. The possibility of darkness invading will also increase," Li Fan calmly analyzed the characteristics of the power of darkness.

It was conceivable that if the mysterious power that fixed the dark ocean did not exist, the Xuanhuang realm would have long been eroded and riddled with holes by the dark tides.

"The existence of the Immortal Ruins created the dark ocean. Then what is it that conceals this crisis in an invisible manner without the world's awareness?"

Thinking about Mo Rubin's desperate attempt to enter the dark realm, Li Fan could almost be certain that there must be something related to the Mysterious Heavenly King.

Perhaps it was his residual power.

Or...

It was him in person.

Li Fan selected three young disciples from the Dark Tribe and had them respectively teach the ritual of communicating with the power of darkness.

He then took the Dark Tribe leader and more than a dozen strong tribesmen out of the Lingmu realm.

The existence of the dark tides would lead to the invasion of the power of darkness. Naturally, Li Fan would not perform the ritual in his own home.

Fortunately, he knew of quite a few ownerless small worlds.

After half a day, Li Fan selected a suitable one and infiltrated it.

This small world had no ground.

There were only continuous underground caves. The only luminous objects were the light moss that occasionally grew on the cave walls.

It was quite suitable for the Dark Tribe to live in.

Under Fang Zaiji's urging, the Dark Tribe leader began the ritual to open the passage to the dark realm.

Afterwards, Li Fan ordered him to lead the way.

Li Fan himself, along with Tian Yang, Qiao Gong Jushi, Liu Ruchen, and Fang Zaiji, the five of them held hands tightly and followed closely behind.

Entering the boundless dark world again.

Li Fan had adapted a lot.

Qiao Gong and the others, however, were clearly feeling tense and fearful emotions from them.

Even though they had heard about it before and had sufficient mental preparation.

But when they were truly experiencing it, it was still difficult to get rid of these emotions.

The group, connected as one, communicated with their divine sense.

"Can you sense the presence of the people from the Wanxian Alliance here?" Li Fan asked the Dark Tribe leader.

However, he gave a negative answer.

"The power of darkness is too profound. Even we, the long-term residents, find it difficult to see things around us. Moreover, I haven't been home for a long time. Your Excellency, please let me adapt a little more..."

Fortunately, he was just overthinking.

After a while, the Dark Tribe leader finally roughly distinguished the direction.

"Follow me. Not far away is where our original residence was located. If the people from the Wanxian Alliance are stationed here, they will definitely choose that place," he explained to Li Fan and the others, his tone filled with hatred.

At this moment, Li Fan noticed.

The inexplicable rhythm he felt from Mo Rubin's corpse when he entered last time seemed to have disappeared.

And on top of the darkness, it was impossible to distinguish the location.

"It seems that we can only find clues from this Wanxian Alliance's stronghold."

Li Fan thought to himself.

"Can you figure out how many people are inside?"

The Dark Tribe leader sensed for a moment.

"There should be around five people. As for their strength, it's similar to this person..."

He was referring to Liu Ruchen among the group.
