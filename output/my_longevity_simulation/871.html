Chapter 871

The scene of that downfall was indeed somewhat similar to when Blue Feather faked his death.

Heaven and earth mourned together.

It wasn't just about helping heaven and earth.

Helping these cultivators who were regarded as allies by heaven and earth would still increase the favorability of the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao.

From a rational perspective, in the Xuanhuang Realm, being a running dog of the Heavenly Dao would absolutely not lead to a good outcome. One would only be suppressed to death by the Myriad Immortals Alliance and the Five Elders Society.

Cultivators with normal thinking would not make such a choice.

But with Huan Zhen's help, Li Fan could be without any worries.

Because Li Fan wasn't really going to be the hatchet man of the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao.

He just needed to gain her trust.

If I do things for you, you should at least lend me some power.

After all, the Myriad Immortals Alliance and Five Elders Society are not easy to deal with either.

As long as he could successfully trick the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao into giving him some of her remaining power, Li Fan's goal would be achieved.

Wanting him to really fight the Myriad Immortals Alliance and Five Elders Society...

That was naturally impossible.

Once Huan Zhen was there, Li Fan wouldn't owe anyone anything!

"It's just a matter of how to gain the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao's trust step by step, which still needs long-term planning."

"This is absolutely forbidden content, there are no records to refer to. I can only carefully try it myself."

"By the way, me pushing the Myriad Immortals Alliance and Five Elders Society to fight each other should also count, right?"

Li Fan suddenly raised his head, his gaze seeming to pierce through the Lingmu Realm and straight to the sky of the Xuanhuang Realm.

"No. Keeping a low profile and working hard without anyone knowing won't gain the recognition of the higher-ups."

"Accomplishments that the higher-ups don't know about are the same as not having any."

"Perhaps it can be conveyed to the heavens through Senior Stone Tablet."

Li Fan suddenly had an idea in his heart.

Many of his schemes were completed while hiding in small worlds, Congyun Sea and other places.

The Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao might not know.

In the future, he could go to Senior Stone Tablet more often and tell him about any achievements in detail, no matter how trivial.

This way he could not only get familiar with Senior Stone Tablet, but also gain the favorability of heaven and earth.

"No, if I do this myself, it might be a bit too deliberate."

"Mm, I can leave it to Lin Ling."

Thinking of Lin Ling's naturally silly appearance, Li Fan couldn't help nodding to himself.

Li Fan called Tian Yang to him.

Recently, he had been staying in the Lingmu Realm, helping Shang Tianci and Ou Daozi build the headquarters of the joint association.

Even though he never had a moment of rest, Li Fan still found that Tian Yang's strength had improved quite a bit compared to when they had just formed their Dao together.

"Junior Brother, let's spar a little."

Li Fan said with a kind expression.

Tian Yang looked at Li Fan with some suspicion, then looked at his own fists.

Then he slowly shook his head.

He seemed to be afraid of hurting Li Fan.

Li Fan chuckled, revealing a hint of the aura belonging to the Five Elements Grand Heavenly Cave.

Tian Yang's eyes immediately lit up.

He grinned, seeming delighted by Li Fan's greatly improved strength.

"Don't worry Junior Brother. My combat power now is not inferior to yours."

These words, however, stirred up Tian Yang's competitive spirit. He was immediately eager to try.

Li Fan looked around the Lingmu Realm, at the joint association and headquarters buildings that had finally taken some shape after much difficulty.

"Although the Lingmu Realm is not small, it can't withstand the impact of the two of us fighting."

With a thought, Li Fan immediately thought of a suitable place.

"Follow me."

The two, one in front and one behind, left the Lingmu Realm and...

After crossing through the lands of four states, they arrived at the present-day territory of Langhuan State.

Hidden within this state was a small world known as "Hunyin".

During the 115th era, among the many small worlds explored by the Wanxian Alliance, this Hunyin realm was an extremely unique one.

For unknown reasons, all the mortals who had migrated into this realm had become extinct.

Only countless ghost-like soul shadows were left, drifting between heaven and earth.

These soul entities seemed to be related to the power of space.

They were actually a great nourishment for the Rift Whales.

After these soul entities disappeared, they would be naturally reborn by heaven and earth.

They became the Rift Whales' natural and best food source.

Therefore, among all the small worlds where Rift Whales existed, the Hunyin realm was where the Rift Whale population thrived the most.

The Rift Whales opened up spatial passages day and night, and the Hunyin realm was also the farthest from the Xuanhuang realm.

"During the 115th era, the exploration team from the Immortal Alliance that went there directly lost contact. Originally, it was thought that they had met with a tragic end, but unexpectedly, it was because the distance was too far, and even ordinary means of communication had lost their effectiveness."

"The vast and boundless void space is just right for me and Tianyang to go all out."

Thinking this way, Li Fan found the entrance to the Hunyin realm.

After setting up a concealment formation around it, Li Fan took the lead and flew inside.

A sense of darkness and profoundness instantly surged forth.

It was a completely different feeling from flying in the spatial passages leading to Dali and Daxuan.

A strange and oppressive atmosphere welled up in Li Fan's mind.

Even the usually somewhat dull-minded Tianyang rarely revealed a solemn expression.

"This feeling..."

Li Fan narrowed his eyes, carefully comprehending it.

After a long time, he had a sudden realization: "It's like mortals being in the deep sea..."

"Is it because the distance from the Xuanhuang realm is too far?"

"In the previously collected information, this point was not mentioned. No wonder the members of that exploration team were never seen again after returning."

"It seems that crossing the endless void is not as easy as I imagined."

Li Fan pondered for a long time and temporarily suppressed these thoughts.

He continued flying along the spatial passage for about half a day.

Feeling that it was far enough, he finally stopped.

"Junior Brother, please make your move," Li Fan said in a deep voice.

Tianyang nodded, and without saying anything further, an imposing aura appeared.

He revealed his red and black world-soul body.

His limbs also seemed to be shackled with heavy fetters, unable to move.

"So this is the power of space."

Li Fan knew that his junior brother Tianyang was holding back.

Otherwise, if the power of space directly acted on his body, it could instantly tear Li Fan into pieces!

"Junior Brother, I said, use your full strength!"

Without trying to break free from the spatial shackles, Li Fan's face turned solemn as he said seriously.

Tianyang was stunned for a moment.

Afterwards, Li Fan only felt that the space around him became even more profound.

It was as if an invisible monster had taken a bite.

Li Fan's head, hands, and feet instantly disappeared into the darkness.

Only his bare trunk was left, floating helplessly in the air.

If it were an ancient cultivator, in this instant, they would have already perished.

But for the new cultivators who had devoured the heavenly caves, it was just a trivial injury.

Bang!

Li Fan's trunk instantly exploded into a cloud of blood mist.

However, his voice was like rolling thunder, echoing in the void space around Tianyang.
