Chapter 946

Here is the English translation of the excerpt from the Chinese novel:

The voice of the Holy Fetus Li Fan echoed in this endless sea of cultivation methods.

"The more you can't get something, the more you want it."

"So to completely quell the greed in your heart, you just need to satisfy all the desires you have for 'rest' in one go."

"Give yourself a long vacation. Long enough that you have nothing to do and are bored out of your mind..."

Hearing Li Fan's words, the little loli's eyes immediately lit up.

She couldn't help but say, "How could anyone possibly feel bored from having nothing to do?! If I didn't have to derive cultivation methods anymore, I would definitely sleep for a few decades first!"

As if longing for that wonderful holiday life, the face of the little loli who derived the methods was full of yearning.

However, very quickly, she said very dejectedly, "Sigh, let alone resting for a few decades. Even taking a break for ten days or half a month would be very difficult."

"It's so nice to not have to work!"

Recalling the happy times of slacking off before, the little loli couldn't help but sigh.

Then she glanced at Li Fan and complained, "It's all your fault, otherwise I could have pretended for a while longer."

"Escaping is not the solution." Li Fan smiled wryly, "Thoroughly resolving the root cause is the right way."

The little loli snorted, "It's easy for you to say. I had an agreement with the Venerable One of Imparting Methods back then, that I could only rest after deriving all the cultivation methods in the world."

"Sigh, at the current pace, it will probably take a few thousand more years. It's really worrying me to death..."

The little loli pouted and sighed.

"If only we could find another Derivation Orb to help you derive, then you could rest." Just then, the Holy Fetus Li Fan suddenly spoke up and lamented.

The little loli gave Li Fan a look, "This noble lady is unique in the world, where can you find another me?!"

However, the little loli paused after a moment, "If my future self a few thousand years later could directly give me the completed derived methods, wouldn't I be able to work on the surface but actually rest as I wish?"

The little loli's eyes became bright and lively as she fell into a fantasy.

Through the conversation with the Derivation Orb, the Holy Fetus Li Fan had already obtained enough information.

At this time, he decisively spoke up to wake up the other party, "I have already stated the cause of your illness. Where are the cultivation methods I need?"

The Derivation Orb didn't react at all, only staring blankly with empty eyes and giggling foolishly.

Li Fan had to raise his voice and repeat it again.

Only then did the little loli wake up as if from a dream.

She gave Li Fan a resentful look and complained, "Let me rest a little longer, geez..."

"Those guys probably won't come to urge me for a while."

"Besides, your cultivation method is indeed quite complex, I can take the opportunity to rest a bit more."

She waved her hand, signaling Li Fan to wait a bit longer.

The little loli started spacing out again.

Li Fan also did not stop her, but turned his gaze to the countless cultivation methods floating around.

However, the cultivation methods themselves were bound by chains made of characters and could not be examined.

Li Fan gave up the idea of peeking. Eyes on the nose, nose on the heart, he patiently waited for the little loli to rest enough.

A full day passed. It seemed that under the urging and inquiries from the myriad realms, the Derivation Orb finally woke up slowly with a bit of reluctance.

"Sigh..."

She stretched out her little hand, and a book titled "Three Calamities Hundred Tribulations Illuminated God Art" immediately appeared.

The books appeared out of thin air.

It was obvious that she had already deduced them long ago.

"Here, take them. It's time for you to go."

"I hope the next person who comes to deduce the techniques will be just as difficult, so I can save some free time again."

Amidst the little girl's words, the Holy Fetus Li Fan returned to the space of the character light sphere.

As soon as he returned, Yan Fa Jue became listless and sleepy as before.

Seeing the faint warning in her eyes, Li Fan immediately understood.

Yan Fa Jue's actual speed of deducing techniques was far faster than what she outwardly showed.

Each time she deduced a technique, it could be completed very quickly. So the remaining time could be used for free rest.

This was perhaps one of the reasons why she could persist in deducing for thousands of years.

The Protector Hall of the Myriad Immortals Alliance was probably also clear about this matter.

But if they pushed too hard, it might backfire. So they simply turned a blind eye.

With the techniques in hand, the purpose of this trip had been achieved.

Li Fan cupped his hands in farewell to the white-haired Harmonious Dao elder.

Before leaving, he also explained in detail the source of Yan Fa Jue's illness.

Just as he was about to leave, Li Fan remembered something and took out the token that the white-haired elder had given him earlier.

"Before leaving the headquarters of the Myriad Immortals Alliance, I'd like to visit the [Museum of Divine Treasures], is that possible?"

The white-haired elder was stunned.

Visiting the Museum of Divine Treasures was something he could do anytime.

But the token he had given represented a personal favor from him, which had many uses.

To waste it so easily?

"Young man, why don't you reconsider?" Even the white-haired elder himself felt it was a bit of a waste and couldn't help but advise.

"No need. My mind is made up." Li Fan replied calmly.

The white-haired elder shook his head slightly and no longer dissuaded him.

He took back the token and sent Li Fan out of the Protector Hall, then instructed the guiding cultivator.

Afterwards, he returned to the Protector Hall.

As for Li Fan, he followed behind the guiding cultivator towards the Museum of Divine Treasures.

His change of mind to suddenly visit the Museum of Divine Treasures was actually the idea of his true self.

One reason was to take a look at the skull displayed in the center of the Museum of Divine Treasures.

The second was to use the time while touring the museum to fully memorize the [Three Calamities Hundred Tribulations Bright God Technique].

It should be safe inside the Museum of Divine Treasures.

But once he left the headquarters of the Immortal Alliance, it was uncertain.

Moreover, his unconventional performance in the Protector Hall would inevitably attract attention.

So Li Fan had already decided that as soon as he left the Immortal Alliance headquarters, he would directly abandon this Holy Fetus to prevent being tracked.

After entering the Museum of Divine Treasures, memories related to the collection also surged forth.

It seemed that his true self had been here before.

However, Li Fan still pretended to be visiting for the first time.

He exclaimed in amazement.

In reality, he was secretly browsing the [Three Calamities Hundred Tribulations Bright God Technique] deduced by Yan Fa Jue.

The complexity of this technique was no less than the Immortal Techniques of the Mysterious Heaven Sect.

It was just that its power was more than a notch weaker, only at the level and combat strength of the Harmonious Dao realm.

However, it should be enough for Li Fan to use to deceive Mo Rubin with tricks.

Having fully memorized the entire technique, Li Fan nodded in satisfaction.

Immersed in the comprehension of the technique, he only came back to his senses when he finally arrived at the very center of the Museum of Divine Treasures.

He stared at the skull in front of him, lost in thought.

It was no different from a human skull.
