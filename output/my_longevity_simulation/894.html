Chapter 894

At this moment, Ji Shaoli stood still like a lamb waiting to be slaughtered. The expression of shock and horror froze on his face.

Seeing the yellow withered branch in the other person's hand, a mocking smile couldn't help but appear on the corner of the young man's mouth.

"The ancient tree of the earth veins, and this mysterious seven-colored brilliance..."

"But a Golden Core stage cultivator actually possesses such a treasure."

"Since when did such a remarkable organization appear under the jurisdiction of our Wanxian Alliance?"

"Let me take a good look."

Under the young man's gaze, Ji Shaoli's soul was slowly extracted from his body.

In this instant, his body aged a thousand times over.

In just a moment, it turned into a skeleton, scattering all over the ground.

The technique the young man used was obviously an extremely vicious soul searching technique!

Ji Shaoli's soul fell into the young man's hands without any resistance.

In an instant, with the operation of the soul searching technique, a large amount of information surged in the young man's mind.

The young man's body trembled slightly, and a trace of horror flashed in his eyes.

...

"Xuantian Sect?"

At this moment, it seemed as if the soul searching technique triggered a certain restriction in the other party's soul.

Before the young man could completely read all the information contained in the soul in his hand, the soul disappeared like a withered flower.

The young man didn't take this scene to heart.

If the content he saw from the previous soul search was true, then the existence of this kind of restriction in the soul was reasonable.

"I didn't expect that after thousands of years, the Xuantian Sect still lingers like a ghost?"

The young man couldn't help but frown slightly.

From the search results just now, this cultivator named Ji Shaoli was taken as a subordinate by a mysterious Dharma King of the Xuantian Sect.

They secretly cultivated forces in Congyun Sea and various places of the Wanxian Alliance, intending to rise again.

"What era is it, and they still don't give up. They really don't know their place."

The young man snorted coldly, but there was no negligence in his heart.

"The ruins of Purple Mist Sect? They actually hid in that kind of place..."

The young man's eyes revealed a murderous aura, and his figure slowly disappeared.

...

At the same time.

His figure paused slightly.

Some blurry images appeared in his mind, making him ponder: "The Heavenly Control Department?"

"There is actually such an organization."

"But thinking about it, it's normal."

"My previous naive thought of being able to freely cultivate Dao-matching cultivators just by knowing the existence of the Earth's Spiritual Essence was a bit childish."

"The Wanxian Alliance has existed for thousands of years. If it didn't even have this level of control, it would have been overthrown long ago."

Suppressing the thoughts in his mind for now, Li Fan looked at the ruins of the Purple Mist Sect not far away.

"Since the fish has taken the bait, I'll just sit and watch the show."

Eyes narrowed slightly, his divine sense swept over.

Soon, according to the intelligence he mastered in his previous life, and that faint trace of vitality that would almost be ignored by ordinary people,

Li Fan found that Dharma King of the Xuantian Sect who was in a deep slumber in a sealed secret room deep underground!

Looking at his appearance, it was indeed exactly the same as Li Chen of the Purple Mist Sect whom Li Fan had previously possessed!

He shot out a ray of annihilation sword qi, heading straight underground.

Carrying the destructive power filled with annihilating aura, it seemed to want to devour everything in the secret room.

Just as the black light was about to touch the secret room, golden threads suddenly appeared in the void.

The golden threads forming the protective circle around Li Chen began to disintegrate, but they still managed to keep him shielded at the center.

Seeing this, Li Fan relentlessly unleashed several destructive sword energies.

The golden threads, under the impact of this immense force, caved inward.

It seemed as if they would snap at any moment.

At this critical juncture of life and death, the Xuantian Sect's Dharma King finally slowly opened his eyes.

Li Fan smiled, his figure flickering and vanishing without a trace.

Moments later, when Li Chen awoke, he first stood there, dazed for some time.

Having slumbered for too long, it would take a while to sort through the memories in his mind.

However, before he could fully grasp his current situation,

He suddenly sensed that the surrounding space seemed to have been sealed off by someone.

Recalling that he had just been attacked, Li Chen instantly realized that his hiding place must have been discovered somehow.

Nevertheless, as the Xuantian Sect's Dharma King, a being who could calmly escape pursuit from the Ten Sects of the Immortal Dao back then,

He remained composed and first took out a porcelain vial from his storage ring.

He drank a mouthful of the seven-colored jade nectar inside.

His somewhat withered aura due to the prolonged slumber instantly solidified.

Just as Li Chen was restoring his strength,

A door opened in the sky, and a team of dozens of black-robed cultivators walked out together.

Their expressions were solemn, the killing intent surrounding them was almost tangible.

The one leading them was none other than the young cultivator who had just searched Ji Shaoli's soul in Congyun Sea not long ago.

His eyes narrowed slightly as he swept his gaze over the ruins below.

"Three chimes of the bell, it is indeed the Xuantian Sect's Dharma King!"

His heart was somewhat excited. With a wave of his hand, the black-robed cultivators instantly flew to various corners in the air, each holding a small mirror.

Once in position, boundless cyan light radiated from the numerous small mirrors.

In an instant, the light swept through the space.

Under the illumination of the cyan light, the underground space became transparent.

Thus, Li Chen, who was hidden within, had his figure revealed before the eyes of the Wanxian Alliance crowd.

Surrounded by the crowd, Li Chen's expression was not panicked.

He only stared at the small mirrors in the hands of the black-robed cultivators, his eyes flashing with a bit of reminiscence and a bit of resentment.

The young preacher, seeing the look in Li Chen's eyes, snorted coldly in his heart.

With a thought, the surrounding black-robed cultivators, under his direction, raised the small mirrors high.

Countless cyan light beams were focused on the Xuantian Sect's Dharma King below.

The cyan light was like solid shackles, binding Li Chen.

Li Chen showed a mocking expression: "Using our Xuantian Sect's items to try to capture the Xuantian Sect's Dharma King?"

"Have the cultivators nowadays become this foolish?"

A crisp ringing sound seemed to resonate beside Li Chen along with his words.

In an instant, the cyan light shackling his body actually exhibited a bizarre bend.

The restraints loosened a bit.

Different expressions instantly appeared on the faces of Li Chen and the young man.

Li Chen's was one of surprise and solemnity.

The young man's was one of wild joy.

"This Xuantian Sect friend, you'd better come with me!" He took a deep breath, suppressing the excitement in his heart, and said loudly.

But Li Chen completely ignored him.

The crisp ringing sounded again, and the cyan light emitted by the small mirrors began to shake violently.

Then, as if losing control, it erupted fiercely.

The cyan light instantly occupied the entire Purple Empyrean Sect ruins.

Under the cover of the dazzling light, right before everyone's eyes, Li Chen's figure instantly disappeared!
