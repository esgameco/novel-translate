Indeed, this was the name that Xu Bai had created for the "True Temptation".

Fang Zaiji's eyes lit up, and he quickly said, "Master, leave it to me. In fact, when you instructed me before about how to strengthen the Yao Wang True Ding, I already had a plan in mind. However, due to time and material constraints, I haven't been able to act on it..."

Xu Bai smiled and said, "Now, Elder Fang, you can rest assured. Just let me know what materials you need, and I'll provide them."

He extended his palm, and the Yao Wang True Ding began to rotate continuously on it.

"After all, our Yao Wang Sect disciples are still within it, so we must consider their safety," Xu Bai said meaningfully.

Liu San's gaze lingered on the Yao Wang True Ding for a long time.

It was not until Xu Bai followed Fang Zaiji to the forging room that he reluctantly withdrew his gaze.

His expression was indecipherable, and it was unclear what he was thinking about.

In the Tianquan State, after extensive data processing, Li Fan had managed to fully analyze the content of the "Wuji Yingxu Fa" (Boundless Yin-Yang Technique).

After attempting to activate the spiritual technique, Li Fan still hoped that he could use it to eliminate the influence that Mr. Bai had on him.

Unfortunately, either due to the limitations of his spiritual abilities or because he had already been tainted by Mr. Bai, the attempt was futile and irreversible.

When Li Fan exhaled the "Huan Zhen" (Returning to the True), the emotions of reluctance and unwillingness surged back again, causing him to quickly close the Huan Zhen panel.

"It seems I was overthinking it," Li Fan, who had failed numerous times, said without much concern.

After firmly memorizing the content of the "Wuji Yingxu Fa", he did not continue to cultivate it. Instead, he temporarily set it aside and took out the Separation Disc.

Compared to before, the Separation Disc had become even more translucent and pristine.

"The Nine Discs Contending for Supremacy, seven have already gone," Li Fan mused as he played with the smooth and jade-like Separation Disc.

During his previous free time, he had already infused all the formations he knew, except for the "Nizhuan Shisheng Da Zhen" (Reverse Life and Death Formation) and the "Tianxuan Suoling Zhen" (Heavenly Mysterious Sealing Formation), into this Separation Disc.

He also felt that his Separation Disc was much more powerful than the remaining one.

Even if he no longer fed it with formation data and let it evolve naturally, it would still emerge victorious in the end.

"It seems the other side has resigned and given up the struggle," Li Fan sighed.

"Unfortunately, I don't have enough time."

Li Fan shook his head slightly, deciding not to infuse the last formation data into the Separation Disc.

"If the Tianxuan Offspring is still within the sensing range of the Tianxuan Mirror..."

"Then this will be my trump card."

"I wonder, after the Tianxuan Mirror's upgrade, how does it compare in strength to the revived Mr. Bai?"

A cold glint flashed in Li Fan's eyes.

In order to seek life from death, he naturally had to create more chaos and confusion.

Just like when the Crimson Flame Scorched the Sea and the Ink Slaughter descended, to prevent the ability to trace the true body through clones, the more chaotic, the better!

There were too many hidden existences in the Xuanhuang Realm, and only by letting them fight each other could he slightly obscure their vision.

"In fact, there is another way to escape Mr. Bai's influence."

"That is to directly remove the cause of 'attachment'."

"For example, if I'm greedy for the complete Separation Disc, then I should unlock its full potential."

"Or if I'm reluctant to part with the Endgame Circular Plate that can completely shield the Tianxuan Treasure Mirror, then I should research the possible results as soon as possible."

"Unfortunately..."

"It's only theoretically possible."
