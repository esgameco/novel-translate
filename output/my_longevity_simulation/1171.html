The abundant spiritual energy within the Five Elements Grand Cavern Heaven was the perfect breeding ground for the unlimited reproduction of the Dao-One Insects.

A catastrophic change was quietly unfolding within the five cavern heavens.

Ordinary creatures may not be able to perceive the fundamental changes in the spiritual energy.

However, the top-level life forms within the cavern heavens reacted immediately.

The most conspicuous among them was the towering giant tree in the wood-attribute cavern heaven.

Its branches and leaves moved without wind, as if trying to resist an invisible enemy.

A hazy green aura surged from within the giant tree, forming a barrier in an attempt to prevent the infection of the mutated spiritual energy.

Unfortunately, this giant tree had obviously not reached the point of being able to form its own cycle.

The larger the body, the more nourishment it needs to absorb from the outside world at every moment.

Unable to persist for long, the green barrier collapsed on its own.

The invisible mutated spiritual energy instantly swarmed and devoured the giant tree.

Far outside the cavern heaven, Li Ping could clearly sense the process of the giant tree being completely altered and consumed by the Dao-One Insects.

Upon closer inspection, one could see countless traces of small insects crawling over the leaves of the giant tree in the wood-attribute cavern heaven.

Moreover, on the tree trunk, a human face was formed under the squirming of numerous Dao-One Insects.

Hideous and terrifying, its gaze met Li Ping's across the void!

"Not bad."

Li Ping nodded slightly.

After struggling for a moment, the human face on the tree trunk was slowly pulled into the tree and disappeared. However, if viewed from a microscopic perspective, at this moment, this giant tree had completely transformed into a combination of countless Dao-One Insects!

It was not just this giant tree.

All existences within the Five Elements Grand Cavern Heaven were inevitably undergoing the same kind of transformation.

Li Ping called it...

"Insectification"!

If all the phenomena and changes brought about by the mutated Dao-One Insects so far were within the expectations of the Holy Emperor Li Ping,

then the next scene that unfolded caught him somewhat off guard.

When the insectification degree of the entire Five Elements Grand Cavern Heaven exceeded one-third,

it seemed that a certain existence suddenly awakened.

Unlike the pure consciousness of the cavern heaven or small world's Dao, it was hatched from the collective consciousness of billions of Dao-One Insects, using the body of the small world's Dao as a shell!

Compared to the world's Dao under normal circumstances, the newly born world consciousness of the Five Elements Grand Cavern Heaven was similar to the mutated Dao-One Insects.

It was filled with aggressiveness and endless, insatiable desire to devour!

Nine pale purple dragons suddenly appeared, guarding around Li Ping.

"A newborn calf dares to devour a tiger."

Li Ping snorted coldly, without the slightest panic.

Although the overall insectification of the Five Elements Grand Cavern Heaven exceeded his expectations,

since he dared to let Baihua Daoren transport the Five Elements Grand Cavern Heaven here, it meant that he had sufficient confidence to deal with various problems.

"The fortune of the Holy Dynasty transforms into dragons. Billions of subjects are at my disposal!"

Li Ping chanted, and behind him, the virtual image of the prosperous scene of countless subjects living and working in peace in the Great Qi of the Holy Dynasty appeared.

Even though the scale of the Great Qi small world was far inferior to the Five Elements Grand Cavern Heaven, it was precisely because of the existence of countless beings with firm beliefs within it that it did not lose out even when facing a huge creature a hundred times its size!

The scene of the Holy Dynasty was permeated with a faint purple aura, as if it were substantial.

Bearing the beliefs and fortune of the billions of beings in the Great Qi, Li Ping's figure flickered, and he stepped onto the insectified Five Elements Grand Cavern Heaven.

He stomped down fiercely!
