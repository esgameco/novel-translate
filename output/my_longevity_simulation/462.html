Chapter 462

So, please prove it yourself, friend!

He looked at Li Fan, his voice stern.

Now the pressure was on Li Fan.

More and more gazes came from all directions, converging on him.

Since the friend claimed to be able to deduce future events, then please perform again on the spot.

Since the friend can accurately predict the rise and fall of specific items' prices, it must be easy to deduce something else as well. Gongsun Qixuan said in a low voice, showing no room for Li Fan to refuse.

After contemplating for a moment, Li Fan had to agree.

In that case, I will offer a demonstration! With a polite gesture, he took out the long-prepared Heavenly Dice, closed his eyes and concentrated, standing still in place.

The place fell silent instantly, everyone staring intently at Li Fan, waiting for his next move.

In the jade slips obtained in the ruins of the Tianji sect, there were indeed some spells for deducing the future.

However, they were all minor spells, hitting the mark only one out of a hundred times, requiring a lot of luck.

After carefully studying them, Li Fan roughly grasped the method of operation, enough to show off.

However, it was absolutely impossible to truly deduce anything.

Seeing all the countless eyes on him, Li Fan did not dare to be too arrogant.

He prepared himself to run away immediately once exposed.

At the same time, he activated the "Sky-covering" secret technique, enveloping his whole body in a mysterious aura.

The Heavenly Dice flew to Li Fan, spinning rapidly. The surfaces of the twenty faces flashed scenes of shattered images.

Li Fan knew that even though these images were fleeting, they couldn't escape the scrutiny of the hidden cultivators present.

So the images shown by the Heavenly Dice were not manipulated by him at will.

They were truly events that Li Fan would experience in the future in the previous life.

True within falsehood, seemingly real.

Only in this way could he deceive the people in the Martial Hall.

However, no matter how realistic it seemed, Li Fan knew that this fabrication could not withstand careful scrutiny.

So, as the Heavenly Dice spun faster and faster, gradually revealing a dark scene, Li Fan's body began to tremble constantly, his face growing paler.

The look of horror on his face grew stronger.
