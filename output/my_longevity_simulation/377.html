Chapter 377

They communicated using their divine senses.

"I knew a big shot was coming, but I never expected it to be Chief Lu!"

"Brother, please forgive my ignorance. May I ask who Chief Lu is?"

"Seriously? Brother, which state are you from to not even know the title of our Chief Formation Master of the Formation Hall?"

"With Chief Lu's abilities in merging with Dao, she can easily scan through the entire Hua Ling Gorge with just a thought. Why does she need to inspect every place personally?"

"You don't understand. She definitely needs to do that. If she just comes and goes without seriously examining anywhere, wouldn't it seem..."

"Brother, be careful what you say!"

...

Li Fan did not participate in the lively discussion.

He calmly focused on conducting formation tests as if nothing was happening.

Because he vaguely sensed a huge divine sense continuously sweeping back and forth above the Hua Ling Gorge.

"Everyone knows we have to put on an act, yet some are using divine senses to gossip right under others' noses. They really think divine voice transmission is absolutely secure."

Li Fan shook his head inwardly.

He maintained his composure, refusing to engage in such self-destructive behavior.

After the initial excitement passed, an order was issued, causing everyone to groan in distress.

The Chief Adult would leave in two days.

Before that, everyone needed to work harder.

Thoroughly complete the construction of the entire Heaven Dark Locking Spirit Array.

Although the original progress was almost complete, now the deadline was forcibly compressed to two days.

The sudden increase in workload was almost quadrupled.

But this order was directly issued by Dong Fangyao, and the wording was extremely severe.

Everyone also knew that Chief Lu was present, so they dared not complain.

They had to work desperately.

Fortunately for Li Fan, it was just a matter of continuously working without a break, which was ordinary for him.

His expression remained unchanged, showing no signs of abnormality.

To prevent mistakes in their busyness, Huo Fu Song and Dong Fangyao would sometimes appear to check on progress.

As for Lu Xichan herself, apart from showing herself that day, she had not appeared in front of everyone again.

No one knew what she was busy with.

But the huge divine sense enveloping the Hua Ling Gorge never left, so Li Fan worked diligently without displaying any anomalies.

Time flew by rapidly amidst everyone's intense work.

In the blink of an eye, two days had passed.

Just as they were about to completely form the main pivot and lock-spirit array of the Heaven Dark Array, Chief Lu finally appeared again.

"I will install this Heaven Dark Mirror myself."

She made a somewhat surprising request that left everyone astonished.

Although it was unexpected, no one dared to disobey Chief Lu's order.

Dong Fangyao's face tensed slightly as he handed over the red phantom formed from the pivot of the Heaven Dark Array to Lu Xichan.

Amidst the gaze of the crowd, Lu Xichan placed the Heaven Dark Mirror in the central core area of the formation.

The mirror seemed to be extremely familiar with her.

It cleverly found its position without much manipulation and began to connect with the outer formations.

The scene was similar to what Li Fan had seen outside the Abyss.

Blue rays of light continuously expanded outward from the central pivot, forming a spectacular display.

"Hmm?"

However, Li Fan keenly sensed that the Heaven Dark Locking Spirit Array here seemed to have a slight difference from the one he had constructed.

He did not show any change in his expression.

His mind was just rapidly turning.

"What's different here?"

Simply looking at it from the perspective of formation knowledge, Chief Lu's operation could be called flawless and showed no flaws.

Quietly observing the formation taking shape, he once again felt that familiar feeling of being watched.

Li Fan's heart skipped a beat, realizing the source of his feeling.

Right at the moment of completion of the Heaven Dark Locking Spirit Array, the previously unnamed formation that had enveloped the Hua Ling Gorge and the transparent patterns vanished simultaneously.

The huge divine sense he had perceived during this time also disappeared without a trace.

Everything seemed to follow a logical sequence.

Even if someone noticed it, they would just assume Chief Lu had completed the formation and withdrew her divine abilities.

But Li Fan did not think so.

A daring thought suddenly emerged in his mind.

Regardless of the unknown formation that unfolded in sync with the Heaven Dark Locking Spirit Array or the strikingly similar surveillance effect, Li Fan couldn't help but speculate.

"It didn't disappear."

"Did they merge?"

"Are they integrated into one entity? Or just lurking?"

Li Fan's gaze remained calm, but his mind was filled with tumultuous thoughts.

"Is she leaving a backdoor for herself in the Heaven Dark Locking Spirit Array?"

As the first formation master to propose the Heaven Dark Locking Spirit Array project, Lu Xichan undoubtedly had the ability to do so.

But her intentions puzzled Li Fan.

"Is it to prevent the potential loss of control in the future with the Heaven Dark Locking Spirit Array?"

"Or is there another purpose?"

"After the widespread use of the Heaven Dark Locking Spirit Array, controlling it also means holding immense power."

"The Chief Formation Master of the Formation Hall is just a formation master after all."

"There is still quite a distance to reach the peak of real power."

"Even achieving immortality is not impossible."

As Li Fan's thoughts continuously diverged,

The Heaven Dark Locking Spirit Array, which had been thoroughly formed by Chief Lu's personal operation, stirred the applause of the cultivators inside the Hua Ling Gorge.

Lu Xichan also rarely revealed a smile.

Her expression mild, she looked down at the cultivators.

As her gaze swept over the face of an elderly man with white hair, her eyes suddenly narrowed.

Where had she seen this person before?

Lu Xichan felt puzzled.

But he was only a Foundation Building cultivator and shouldn't have caught her attention.

Her thoughts turned rapidly, memories flooding her mind.

Suddenly, her memory stopped at a few years ago when her daughter returned after being missing for a while.

Her daughter, Lu Xuejing, had been taken by an old fisherman due to her arrogance.

Fortunately, a cultivator had exchanged his belongings to bring her back.

As Lu Xuejing was about to thank her savior, he hurriedly left, seeming to be in a hurry.

She knew nothing of her savior, only remembered his face.

Using the water mirror technique, her daughter had described the man's appearance to her and she had remembered it.

It should be the same person.

But why had he aged so much in just a few years?

"Who is that person?"

Lu Xichan asked Dong Fangyao through voice transmission.

"He indeed came!"

Seeing the gaze of Chief Lu and Dong Fangyao simultaneously shift towards Li Fan, Huo Fusong inwardly remarked.
