"Go all out..." Yin Shangren fell silent upon receiving the answer.

"When the time comes for the Dharma to manifest, it will be the moment when the Holy Dynasty's army launches an all-out attack on the headquarters of the Myriad Immortal Alliance. You two will take advantage of the chaos to sneak into the [Museum of Divine Treasures] and bring out Mr. Bai's skull. At this point, victory or defeat is a struggle of the overall situation. Although both of you are not weak, you cannot influence the overall situation. Moreover, restraining the Huanzhen Sage lineage that controls the Divine Treasure Pavilion is also a great achievement."

A hint of gloominess flashed in Yin Shangren's eyes: "It's just the undying ghostly souls of ancient beasts from the Xuanhuang Realm. If [Emperor One] was still here, I might be wary. But with only Emperor Three Mao..."

"Nothing to fear!" Yin Shangren snorted coldly.

"The Huanzhen Sage wields the powerful artifact [Era]. Even the most mediocre existence, after hundreds of thousands of simulations and deductions, can give birth to miracles. Do not underestimate the enemy!" Li Ping said solemnly.

The Holy Emperor's words still carried some weight in Yin Shangren's heart.

He no longer refuted, but instead narrowed his eyes in thought.

As he was thinking, Yin Shangren's scalp once again transmitted a rustling sound like silkworms crawling. His scalp undulated, as if something would drill out from the cracks on the top of his head at any time.

Yin Shangren instantly became somewhat irritated, while Li Ping flashed to his side.

With a light touch of his palm and the infiltration of starlight, the abnormality gradually subsided.

"This is the reaction of your true self. Forcibly suppressing it is not a good strategy," the Holy Emperor saw through it at a glance and advised.

Yin Shangren showed a helpless expression: "Starting halfway, after all, is not the same as cultivating from the beginning. It is destined to leave various sequelae. At first, I thought that as long as I devoured my original imperfect Dao foundation and re-established [Establishing Foundation with Myself], everything would be fine. But some time ago, when I heard your sermon in the Starry Sea, I had some new insights. I realized that my original cognition, my complete self, was not entirely complete. The foundation is shaken and indeed needs to be re-adjusted."

"Don't worry, I will handle it well before the decisive battle arrives," Yin Shangren promised.

"As long as you have it under control, it's fine."

The Holy Emperor's voice lingered, but his figure had already disappeared.

Yin Shangren looked around, but was surprised to find that the incomplete [New New Law] that had been virtualized into reality and taken away by the Holy Emperor had suddenly reappeared on the long table where the Holy Emperor had been sitting.

Knowing that the Holy Emperor had deliberately left it for him, Yin Shangren was not polite and immediately picked it up to study.

"Wonderful..."

Even though it was only compiled to deceive the world, in Yin Shangren's eyes, this half of the New New Law was still profound and boundless, almost no different from true divine techniques.

He quickly became completely obsessed.

Holy Dynasty, Five Elements Grand Grotto-Heaven, Fire Attribute Grotto-Heaven.

Endless sea of fire, raging flames like towering waves, bursting out from time to time, rushing straight to the sky.

The scorching flames, like wild fiery horses, gnawed at everything inside the grotto-heaven.

But in the deepest part of this hellish scene, a golden glaze flower was blooming. No matter how rampant the surrounding flames were, the flower remained as clear as ever, unaffected in the slightest.

In the center of the glaze flower, the image of a world was faintly visible.

From time to time, a wisp of golden light would float out from the glaze world within the flower. When the surrounding flames came into contact with this golden light, they would instantly ignite and burn even more vigorously.

Compared to when it was first captured here, not only the fire grotto-heaven, but the entire Five Elements Grand Grotto-Heaven had almost expanded by a fold. The Hundred Flowers Daoist had made indispensable contributions.

The figure of Holy Emperor Li Ping flashed beside the golden glaze light.

The raging flames instantly withered. They didn't even dare to approach, only swaying from afar in a fawning manner.

"You've done well. The Five Elements Grotto-Heaven has been managed very well."

"The other grotto-heavens as well," Li Ping said indifferently.

His gaze penetrated the sky of the Five Elements Grand Grotto-Heaven and reached the void outside the realm.

The Five Elements Grand Grotto-Heaven, like a strong helmsman, led the Holy Dynasty's Great Vessel swiftly through the void.

And around it, it simultaneously enveloped hundreds of different small worlds of various sizes.

With the Holy Dynasty's Great Vessel as the core, they were vaguely connected into a formation. Like a giant battleship, it was awe-inspiring.

"It's just part of my duty. If it weren't for the Holy Emperor bestowing the Myriad Spirit Jade Nectar, I wouldn't be able to achieve this level," Hundred Flowers came to Li Ping and bowed.

Hundred Flowers also looked in the direction of Li Ping's gaze.

After a brief silence, she couldn't help but sigh: "I can't believe that such a magnificent sight was actually done by me."

"Most of them are no smaller than my hometown."

"Next, I'll have to trouble you again. Send this Five Elements Grand Grotto-Heaven back to the Xuanhuang Realm," Li Ping's voice came at the right time.

Hundred Flowers was slightly stunned.

The Holy Emperor smiled and explained: "The time for evasion has passed. Now is the time for close combat. Let it take the lead in this first battle!"

With these words from the Holy Emperor, the surrounding flames seemed to be inspired and surged violently.

And Hundred Flowers also saw that in the entire fire grotto-heaven with raging flames, besides the flames themselves, there was also a deep shadow flowing inside.

It was a terrifying existence that could threaten her.

The Holy Emperor stretched out his palm, and the shadow flowed, quickly coming to his palm, transforming into a cute little bug with a tiger's head.

It looked chubby and harmless, but Hundred Flowers was very clear about the horror of this little thing.

The fire grotto-heaven they were in, and even the entire Five Elements Grand Grotto-Heaven, were nothing more than the incarnations of this black bug.

In these years, Hundred Flowers had been taking care of the grotto-heaven, or rather, raising this bug named [Dao One].

"With [Dao One] helping you, dragging the grotto-heaven back won't be as difficult as when you came."

While speaking, Li Ping's palm emitted starlight.

The Dao One bug seemed a little proud, and its fat body affectionately rubbed against Li Ping's palm. Then it greedily devoured the starlight.

Its black body seemed to become even more condensed.

"Go."

Li Ping lightly patted, and although the Dao One bug was a little reluctant, it obediently dispersed its shadow and returned to the surrounding grotto-heaven.

The surrounding flames instantly became even more prosperous.

And Hundred Flowers also sensed that the entire grotto-heaven seemed to come alive in an instant. Being inside it was like being in the belly of a giant beast.

The constantly rising sense of crisis made Hundred Flowers restless.

Li Ping also noticed Hundred Flowers' abnormality and brought her to the void outside the Five Elements Grotto-Heaven.

The Holy Emperor lightly tapped his finger, and the Five Elements Grotto-Heaven detached from the Holy Dynasty's Great Vessel and the void fleet formation.

"Don't worry. Dao One will only loyally execute my orders."

Since the Holy Emperor said so, Hundred Flowers could only nod. Suppressing the fear in her heart, she transformed into a golden light and flew towards the Five Elements Grotto-Heaven.

The Five Rings Grotto-Heaven instantly seemed to be dyed with a golden edge.

Boom boom!

After the initial rumbling sound, the movement of the grotto-heaven was absolutely silent.

Under the concealment of the Holy Emperor's formation, even if one was beside the grotto-heaven it passed by, they might not necessarily be able to detect this moving behemoth.

Looking at the Five Elements Grand Grotto-Heaven that was returning home, Li Ping secretly pondered in his heart: "With [Dao One]'s current state, it is enough to change the spiritual energy of the entire Xuanhuang Realm silently."

"Not only is it aimed at the Myriad Immortal Alliance, but it can also take the opportunity to stimulate the Xuanhuang Realm and further trigger its revival."

Since the last time he comprehended the Dao in the center of the Starry Sea, the power of revival had briefly erupted within the Xuanhuang Realm. However, after all, it was rootless water, and as time passed, the signs of revival had gradually stagnated.

The tender buds that had newly opened on the branches were turning yellow again.

The Five Elements Grand Grotto-Heaven, transformed by the Dao One bug, was like a potent medicine.

The spiritual energy of heaven and earth was an extremely important component of the Xuanhuang Realm. If it was eroded, it would inevitably trigger the Xuanhuang Realm's own instinctive and intense reaction.

"The mission of the Dao One bug, in addition to creating a battlefield environment suitable for the Great Vessel's soldiers to fight, is to try to infest and devour the foreign immortal realms that have been stitched together over the past several thousand years."

"After the great battle ends, the Dao One bug will die. The spiritual energy will return to normal, and the corpse of the Dao One bug, which is those foreign realms, can be used as nourishment for the Xuanhuang Realm."

In the Holy Emperor's mind, he kept deducing the scenes of the great battle in the near future.

His eyes looked into the distance, penetrating layers of time and space barriers, and saw Hundred Flowers Daoist successfully dragging the infested Five Elements Grotto-Heaven back into position.

In the silent roar of heaven and earth, the Five Elements Grand Grotto-Heaven that had returned to Tianling Prefecture began to emit an abnormal spiritual energy.

The concentration of spiritual energy in the Xuanhuang Realm would slightly increase in a short period of time.

Cultivators would find it difficult to detect the abnormality of this newly born spiritual energy. Only when the [Infestation] was activated would they be caught off guard and lose their "right to invoke" the spiritual energy of heaven and earth.

Seeing the plan being implemented smoothly, Holy Emperor Li Ping finally withdrew his gaze.

With a step, he returned to the Holy Dynasty's Great Vessel.

The deepest part of the Holy Emperor's seat.

The core of the Myriad Spirit Grand Formation.

In addition, there were many other formations stacked, such as the Sentient Beings Refining God Formation. Even the consciousness of the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao, which was closely related to the Holy Emperor, would have its connection cut off here.

Ensuring that all external probing could be severed.

The Holy Emperor's disciple, Lin Ling, the former incarnation of the Spirit Wood Realm, now revealed her true form.

However, it looked quite strange. The tree was inverted, with the crown at the bottom.

The dense roots were at the top.

The buildings scattered throughout the Holy Dynasty's Great Vessel, such as the Hero Seat, the Holy Physician Institute, the Holy Academy, the Holy Heart Palace, and the Holy Supervision Court, were all connected to the roots of the inverted spirit tree.

In the heart of the tree, all the scenes happening within the Holy Dynasty's Great Vessel were simultaneously reflected.

After playing through a scene, the light and shadow would transform into green light and be absorbed by the inverted spirit tree.

Holy Emperor Li Ping looked at the inverted spirit tree in front of him, and a rare hint of hesitation flashed in his eyes.

"The so-called 'before seeking victory, first seek defeat.' Only with great wisdom can the Holy Emperor make such a move!"

Beside Li Ping, a yellow paper man with a worried face was praising without reservation.

It was none other than One Absolute Daoist from the Crossing Calamity Sect.

He was seen sitting on the Book of Crossing Calamity, his eyes tightly fixed on the inverted spirit tree in front.

"Moreover, the Holy Emperor's plan this time is for the sake of the Xuanhuang Realm. It is not a way out left for himself, so it cannot be said to dampen his sharp spirit and determination," One Absolute Daoist continued.

The Holy Emperor remained silent.

And at the heart of the tree, the speed at which those Great Vessel scenes were absorbed seemed to have increased a bit.

After a long time, the Holy Emperor finally stopped his actions.

He suddenly spoke: "In this decisive battle with the Myriad Immortal Alliance, your Crossing Calamity Sect does not need to take action."

"Still follow what I said before, hide in the dark and see..."

"Whether there are other prying eyes," the Holy Emperor said in a mysterious tone.

"The battle between the Holy Dynasty and the Immortal Alliance is a change in the Xuanhuang Realm unseen for ten thousand years. If that force secretly monitoring the Xuanhuang Realm is still operating, it will inevitably supervise this and then transmit the information. I don't require you to find the final destination of the information. Just determine whether the event has occurred or not," the Holy Emperor instructed word by word.
