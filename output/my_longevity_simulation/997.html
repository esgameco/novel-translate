Chapter 997

"Feng'er, be careful!"

Xuan Sushang flashed to Shuo Feng's side, her expression solemn.

Luo Xichan's first thought was to contact the outside world, but she found it impossible to connect.

"You scoundrels, in the presence of the Heavenly Venerable, why do you not kneel!"

A thunderous voice resounded in their ears.

In the dim sky, it seemed as if the Heavenly Venerable's divine form truly appeared.

Glaring down at them with an awe-inspiring gaze.

Boom!

An immense, intangible pressure suddenly descended, instantly reducing the bamboo building to dust.

The entire ground sank several feet amidst the rumbling.

Xuan Sushang and the other two were momentarily locked in place by this pressure, unable to move!

Before they could even struggle, they saw five blood moons appear in the shadow-shrouded sky.

"Silk threads!"

An emotionless, icy voice rang out from the heavens.

Thousands of threads descended from the blood moons.

Like countless tentacles, they silently and swiftly enveloped them.

The threads seemed intangible, but the instant they touched their bodies, a sense of weakness rose from their hearts.

"Xichan!" Xuan Sushang and Shuo Feng's battle armor suddenly appeared around them.

But it could not block these formless silk threads.

Luo Xichan's situation was even more dire. Around her, silver symbols representing formations continuously appeared but quickly vanished without a trace.

Beads of cold sweat formed on Luo Xichan's forehead, shock in her eyes followed by undisguised terror.

Amidst the chaotic battle, perhaps others not well-versed in formations did not know what was happening to her.

But Luo Xichan was very clear - every time she laid down a formation, a mysterious force would instantly shatter it!

The formations crumbled before even fully taking shape!

It was as if the other party already knew what formations she wanted to arrange. The speed of breaking them was simply inconceivable!

At first, Luo Xichan thought there was a traitor in her Strategy Hall who had leaked all the formations she mastered.

But soon, as she took out her most secret formations, she faced the same outcome: instant destruction!

Thus, the dread in Luo Xichan's heart spread uncontrollably.

She seemed to be transported back to when she first met her master.

The powerful mastery of formations her master displayed left her in awe, captivated, obsessed. The talent she previously prided herself on was utterly insignificant before him.

It was a realm she could not even imagine!

"Who on earth is it?"

The pride of being the chief of the Wanxian Alliance's Strategy Hall shattered. Luo Xichan only felt countless faces of her master appear in the surrounding shadows.

"Xichan ah, you have disappointed your master so much!"

"From now on, don't tell people you are my disciple. Your master can't bear to lose face like this!"

"Luo Xichan, I cannot die in peace!"

...

"No, it's not like that, Master..." Trapped in the illusion, Luo Xichan trembled.

She had completely lost the will to resist.

The thick shadows engulfed her entirely, and she vanished into the bamboo sea.

Seeing this, Xuan Sushang's face turned pale. But facing the Heavenly Venerable's divine form overhead and the countless attacking threads, she herself was barely holding on.

"Xingwang brothers, I already..."

After temporarily sealing Lu Xichan, Mu Rubin's slightly puzzled voice came through.

"Hehe, it just so happened to strike at her mental weakness. Who would have thought that the renowned chief of the Strategy Formation Hall is actually a coward who's terrified of her master..." Li Fan also responded with a strange laugh.

"So that's how it is." The battle wasn't over yet, so Mu Rubin didn't dwell on the matter.

"My remaining strength is rapidly dissipating. I'm afraid it won't be enough to seal Xuan Sushang and Shuo Feng..."

"It doesn't matter, they're already doomed."

While the two briefly communicated, the offensive remained lethal.

The three from Tianyang presided over the formation, and even with her battle armor protecting her, Xuan Sushang was like duckweed in the wind, in imminent danger.

The crimson light protected her mind, but was constantly oppressed by the gaze cast by the Heavenly Venerate's dharma image in the sky.

She had already reached the limit of what she could endure.

At this point, Xuan Sushang also realized that the phantom image above her head was not actually some Dharma Transmission Heavenly Venerate.

It was just the best concrete manifestation to intimidate her mind at the first opportunity.

After all, helping the Heavenly Profound Mirror split into human avatars violated the system established by the Dharma Transmission Heavenly Venerate.

It was impossible for Xuan Sushang to say she wasn't afraid in her heart.

And the strange laughter constantly coming from the surrounding shadows made Xuan Sushang have one illusion after another, making it difficult for her to resist.

The gray threads that had been entangling her body since the beginning of the battle were also accumulating more and more.

Her thoughts and movements also slowed down because of it.

"Who on earth is it?"

Before losing consciousness, countless puzzled thoughts flashed through Xuan Sushang's mind.

The two Dao-attained cultivators were already restrained, and there was no suspense in the subsequent battle.

After imprisoning the remaining cultivators in the Zhuhai Cave Abode in the Dongtian to suppress them, Li Fan looked at the two unconscious Dao-attained female cultivators in front of him.

Both had world-shaking beauty, Lu Xichan had a noble temperament, while Xuan Sushang was like a young girl, as if she hadn't lost her childishness.

"Brother Xingwang, my strength is about to dissipate, I'll leave things here to you."

The boundless shadows enveloping the bamboo sea converged, finally turning into a blurry figure.

He looked at Li Fan and said slowly, "If there's news of the Heavenly Profound Mirror's avatar, contact me as soon as possible."

"The body it occupied has been completely destroyed, remember to bring another one next time we meet."

"Still in the hidden cave abode from last time."

The voice gradually dissipated along with the shadows.

"This battle went so smoothly, Mu Rubin's world-covering shadows played an indispensable role."

"Even if we're not adversaries, I still vaguely felt the pressure. I even fleetingly saw the figures of the Heavenly Physician and the Xianxu Immortal in the darkness..."

"The Xuantian Sect... is indeed extraordinary."

The thought flashed by, and Li Fan's attention focused on Xuan Sushang and the other again.

"Senior, should we withdraw now?" Baihua Daoist asked cautiously.

Li Fan pondered for a moment and shook his head, "You go guard the entrance to the bamboo sea here."

"I'm going to interrogate them on the spot for information about the Heavenly Profound Mirror's avatar."

The three received their orders and left.

Li Fan took out two [True Temptation Fruits].

"Only these two left, looks like it's time to ask the Five Elders Association for some more."

Li Fan narrowed his eyes slightly, slowly weaving an intricate web of lies.
