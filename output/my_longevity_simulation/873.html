Chapter 873

"Devouring your ability?"

Upon hearing this, Li Fan couldn't help but be shocked.

The ability that Tianyang spoke of naturally originated from the spatial power of the "Ink Stone" derived from the essence of heaven and earth.

It was transformed by the laws of Xuanhuang, and once absorbed by cultivators through new methods,

even the current Xuanhuang Realm had no way to strip it away.

And in this bizarre and remote small world, it was actually being devoured?

"Senior Brother, don't worry. To me, those unknown existences are like ants crawling all over my body."

"It might be painful or itchy, but they can't threaten my life."

Tianyang transmitted an image, briefly expressing his meaning.

However, Li Fan didn't let his guard down.

This Hunyin Realm was obviously different from the situation detected by the 115th Immortal Alliance expedition team when they arrived.

On alert, Li Fan and Tianyang began exploring this Hunyin Realm.

This small world was not vast, and its area was not even as large as the Lingmu Realm.

But strangely, Li Fan didn't find the spiritual bodies described by the Immortal Alliance expedition team.

The vast earth was dim and gloomy, without any undulations in the terrain.

It was all composed of gray soil.

Not to mention animals and plants, there wasn't even a complete stone.

When his divine sense swept across the Hunyin Realm, apart from the two outsiders, Li Fan and Tianyang, there were surprisingly no other existences.

Li Fan's expression was solemn as he took some of the gray soil from the ground and carefully observed it in his hand.

It seemed to be ordinary soil.

But looking at his own hand, with just a moment of contact, it appeared as if it was burned, and Li Fan knew that this was definitely not something ordinary.

"No pain. The corrosion is silent." Li Fan's eyes flashed.

A surge of vitality welled up from within his body, trying to restore the injuries on his hand.

But to Li Fan's surprise, this infused vitality seemed to have become nourishment for that scorched area!

The black spots on his palm quickly expanded, and it looked like they were about to spread to his entire body.

Li Fan decisively cut off his palm.

And what happened next made Li Fan's pupils contract in shock.

The severed flesh and blood palm, after falling on the gray soil,

seemed to fade and turn to ash like the remnants after being burned by flames.

Then it merged with the soil.

Looking at the world full of gray soil, Li Fan was horrified.

The bizarreness of this place somewhat exceeded Li Fan's imagination.

Just as he was considering leaving, Tianyang suddenly transmitted a message.

"Senior Brother, the strength of the devouring force around here seems to be different in every place."

"Perhaps we can identify where the anomaly is by the magnitude of the force."

Li Fan hesitated for a moment and nodded in agreement.

With Li Fan's approval, Tianyang quickly flew over the Hunyin Realm while sensing.

Soon, he locked onto a location.

The "devouring force" there seemed to be extremely strong.

Li Fan could even vaguely feel a strange fluctuation from Tianyang.

It was as if Tianyang was engaged in an intense battle with an equally matched opponent.

After staying for just a moment, Tianyang had no choice but to leave.

"It's like being devoured by tens of thousands of ants. Senior Brother, can't you feel it?" Tianyang asked through a transmitted message.

Li Fan shook his head and simply wielded the seven-colored divine light, flying towards that location.

Indeed, he didn't feel anything unusual.

But Li Fan had a vague premonition in his heart. If he were to use his fire attribute spiritual power like before,

The consequences would probably not be as simple as his physical body being frozen into a withered sculpture.

Therefore, Li Fan suppressed all the spiritual energy within his body.

He acted only by relying on his physical body and the power of the seven-colored divine light.

Arriving above the target location, Li Fan carefully observed, trying to find useful clues.

He indeed noticed something amiss.

The entire Hunyin Realm that he had previously explored was an absolutely flat place.

But that spot...

seemed to be slightly raised.

This time, with experience, Li Fan protected his right hand with the seven-colored divine light and dug open the soil.

He kept digging downwards, and suddenly, Li Fan touched a hard object.

With a solemn expression, he took it out from deep within the gray soil.

The gray soil accumulated on the surface disappeared soon after leaving the ground.

The hard object thus revealed its true appearance.

"This is?"

Li Fan was a bit stunned.

The thing in his hand was actually a human skull.

But unlike ordinary people, this skull seemed to have a third eye socket at the glabella, with a hole the size of an eye socket.

Moreover, there were naturally existing gold and black patterns on the skull.

Li Fan turned the skull over to examine it, but he was somewhat horrified to find that the inside of the skull was like moldy.

One after another, the gray spots were shocking.

Moreover, these gray spots seemed to have their own consciousness.

Having been dormant for who knows how many years, as soon as they sensed the presence of a living person around, they immediately wriggled strangely.

As if exploring the surroundings.

A sense of crisis that was difficult to describe in words suddenly welled up in Li Fan's heart.

He shot out streaks of seven-colored divine light, forming a perfect seal.

Isolating Li Fan's aura.

Only then did those gray mold spots calm down again.

Looking at the strange skull inside the seven-colored light ball, Li Fan slightly frowned.

"Having three eyes on the head, it looks human, but is also different. Obviously not someone from the Xuanhuang Realm."

"It seems that the gray mold spots were the cause of the catastrophe in the Hunyin Realm."

"Could it be that it was precisely because this outsider brought the gray mold spots here that caused this place to turn into a gray realm?"

"And the culprit was also eroded by the gray mold spots, not even leaving a complete corpse."

...

A possible answer appeared in Li Fan's mind.

"This place is the farthest from the Xuanhuang Realm and may have already reached the scope that other immortal realms can touch."

"This three-eyed person is from this immortal realm."

"Coming here to escape for his life?"

"But brought this terrifying gray mold spot over."

Scene after scene appeared in Li Fan's mind.

Li Fan discovered that the seven-colored divine light was not enough to completely resist the power of this gray mold spot.

Even just sealing it, Li Fan still felt the seven-colored divine light being constantly consumed.

If it weren't for the continuous replenishment of power from the Five Elements Grotto-Heaven, the seal would have probably been broken long ago.

After pondering for a moment, Li Fan didn't put the skull back in its original place.

Instead, he tried to store it in his storage ring.

Somewhat unexpectedly, it went extremely smoothly.

Without any obstruction, it was collected just like that.
