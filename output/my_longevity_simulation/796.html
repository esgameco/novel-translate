Chapter 796

Here is the full translation of the Chinese novel excerpt into English:

Ravines and gullies extended from the earth's surface. The ground of the Luoyan and Lueying prefectures was pressed down several zhang by a living force.

The power of just two fingers of the Dharma Transmission was already so terrifying!

Facing the giant fingers that were about to crush down on his head, Mr. Bai's expression remained unchanged.

Instead, he showed a hint of a long-awaited, gratified smile.

The motion of his sleeves drawing out the white fog barrier slightly stagnated, and the originally surging white fog suddenly stopped.

And then...

Swish!

It was as if countless rays of light illuminated the dark and abnormally purple world.

In an instant, under the two fingers of the Dharma Transmission, a densely packed giant net appeared!

Compressed in an instant from the devouring primordial white fog!

Even high in the sky, it still emitted an aura that made cultivators feel unsettled.

The bright white light met the descending giant fingers.

A huge roaring sound, as if the sky was collapsing, resounded between heaven and earth.

The sound was too immense. When it reached the cultivators' ears, it was instead a silent stillness.

But not long after, cultivators in the two prefectures discovered that gurgling blood was gushing out from their ears.

Covering their ears with both hands, they were all horrified.

Ka... cha...

Blocked by the giant net of devouring primordial energy, the momentum of the Dharma Transmission's two fingers did not stop.

It only paused for a moment before continuing to press down.

Although the white fog was being consumed at an extremely fast speed,

Mr. Bai was constantly replenishing it by drawing from the barrier.

For a time, the battle between the two sides fell into a strange stalemate.

Cultivators with some cultivation base could see at this time,

that the Dharma Transmission was not actually using its full strength.

It was just that for some reason, it had some misgivings.

The white fog from various places in the Xuanhuang Realm was drawn by Mr. Bai to confront the Dharma Transmission.

Gradually, cultivators in each prefecture discovered a strange scene.

The thick white fog that had not dissipated for tens of thousands of years was slowly thinning.

The originally somewhat oppressive field of vision gradually became clear.

Vaguely, they could see all kinds of strange phenomena from the Luoyan and Lueying prefectures.

Standing on the earth, gazing into the distance at the full view of Xuanhuang.

Realizing that the white fog was dissipating, Mr. Bai revealed a faint smile that was not easily noticeable.

Then he raised his head and looked at the Dharma Transmission's two fingers that were still pressing down, his expression solemn.

The next moment.

Everywhere in the Xuanhuang Realm, clouds moved.

The spiritual energy of the entire world seemed to erupt in an instant.

At an incomprehensible speed to humans, in a flash, ignoring the distance of dozens or even hundreds of prefectures, it gathered on Mr. Bai.

So Mr. Bai's originally towering figure expanded several times again.

Almost occupying most of the sky, making him visible no matter where one was in the Xuanhuang Realm.

"Sorry."

"But during this time, Xuanhuang is my world."

Mr. Bai said calmly to the Dharma Transmission in the sky above.

Then, he grasped the surging white fog flow coming from the entire world and slashed upward.

The vast white fog instantly solidified.

Transforming into a blade, splitting open the sky and earth!

As if countless suns rose in the sky at the same time, the white fog sky-opening blade illuminated the entire Xuanhuang Realm!

All living beings in the world instinctively closed their eyes in an instant.

Without earth-shattering sounds, when they opened their eyes again,

they discovered that the Dharma Transmission's two fingers had disappeared.

The entire sky of Xuanhuang was covered with a layer of amber-like yellow crystal at some unknown time.

Fragile, yet hard.

Outside the yellow crystal, there seemed to be huge shadows moving.

This fantastic and magnificent scene was being staged in the world of Xuanhuang for the first time.

"Alright. We can finally have some peace and quiet for a while."

"Now, let's begin the lesson."

"Behold, the myriad beings of the Xuanhuang Realm below," Mr. Bai said with a smile as he gazed down at the countless creatures.

"In the past, the Heavenly Venerate had a method called 'Borrowing the Wonders of Heaven and Earth to Establish the Foundation of the Dao'."

"Now, I too have a method."

"Without relying on external things, one can see the mind's clarity and discern one's own body."

"It is called: 'Establishing the Foundation with Oneself'."

"Although humans are mortal, they are born of Heaven and Earth. They truly possess the marvels of Heaven and Earth, and the transformations of Yin and Yang."

...

Mr. Bai spoke eloquently, word by word.

The beings of the Xuanhuang Realm listened as if intoxicated.

However, his actions seemed to have thoroughly provoked the wrath of the Dharma Transmission.

The yellow crystal enveloping the entire Xuanhuang Realm was suddenly subjected to violent impacts.

It was as if countless thunderbolts were rising in the sky.

The incomparably hard amber crystal actually revealed a crack.

Yet, Mr. Bai didn't care at all and continued teaching without stopping.

His voice seemed to possess a special magic that could soothe people's minds.

Even though rumbling sounds were continuously coming from the Ninth Heaven above,

it still could not affect the people who were attentively listening to Mr. Bai's teachings.

After thoroughly imparting the "Establishing the Foundation with Oneself" method to the beings of Xuanhuang, Mr. Bai didn't stop.

Instead, he went on to expound on the path of cultivation after achieving self-establishment.

Namely: "Primordial Golden Elixir", "Physical Infant", and "Mind Transforming into Spirit".

Mr. Bai also frankly admitted that this was only his hasty inference.

Perhaps it was not perfect and had many loopholes.

As for the higher-level cultivation methods that do not rely on external things under the new law and principles,

cultivators still needed to explore them on their own.

Faced with Mr. Bai's selfless teaching of a cultivation path completely contrary to that of the Dharma Transmitting Heavenly Venerate,

the attitudes of the cultivators in the world varied.

Some were skeptical, some were inexplicably excited, some were pensive, and some were ambitious...

The saying goes, all beings have different dispositions.

But Mr. Bai didn't mind.

After fully imparting everything he had learned,

he fell silent.

His figure returned to its original size as he looked up at the amber crystal that was about to completely shatter.

Facing the possible retaliation from the Dharma Transmitting Heavenly Venerate,

Mr. Bai's face showed no fear.

He was just calm and composed, quietly waiting.

At this moment...

He suddenly noticed a figure hurrying towards him at a rapid speed.

Somewhat old and trembling.

But the excitement in his heart was beyond words.

"Mr. Bai..."

He flew to Mr. Bai's side and called out, unable to contain himself.

The appearance of the newcomer was very similar to the current Mr. Bai, only his face was full of wrinkles, not knowing how many vicissitudes he had experienced.

He carried a black coffin on his back, which looked a bit strange.

If Li Fan or Xiao Qing and the others were present,

they should be able to recognize at a glance that this old man was none other than Xu Ke from back then.

Many years later, seeing the resurrected Mr. Bai again,

he should have been overjoyed.

But oddly enough...

The moment he truly saw the other party, the expression on his face was somewhat startled.

Then it turned extremely crestfallen: "You are not Mr. Bai..."

Xu Ke said in a somewhat dazed manner.

"Mr. Bai" looked at the old man in front of him who was almost identical to himself, and gently patted his shoulder.

"Indeed, I am not him."

"The deceased is gone; how could someone who has completely perished be so easily resurrected?"

"I am merely the imprint, the trace he left in this world."

"In the words of you cultivators,"

"It is also called..."

"'The Soul of Heaven and Earth'."
