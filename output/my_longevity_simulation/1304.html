"I have also tried to follow the mysterious feeling to search for this person's whereabouts. However, it always ended in failure..." The Holy Emperor said while feeling the rising wind in the Star Sea.

The revival of the Star Sea and the resistance against the remnant power of the True Immortals was a rather lengthy process.

Converted to the human time scale, it would last for more than a decade.

Before this Star Sea storm reached its peak, he could go to war with the Dharma Transmission at any time.

Temporary cooperation did not mean eternal reconciliation.

In addition to the fact that their strengths were indeed evenly matched and it was difficult to determine the winner, there were also some considerations of buying time in choosing to cease fire and pursue the mastermind behind the scenes first.

The Holy Emperor's gaze inadvertently swept over the distant Xuanhuang Realm.

The increasing number of Foundation Establishment cultivators from my side was weakening the foundation of the Dharma Transmission.

The plan of the Stone Tablet and Ruomu to establish the Xuanhuang orthodoxy was also progressing more rapidly as the Holy Dynasty took over a large number of territories originally belonging to the Myriad Immortals Alliance.

As the Star Sea storm gradually intensified until it reached its peak, any delay was advantageous to the Holy Emperor.

Since the Dharma Transmission took the initiative to propose it, the Holy Emperor naturally jumped at the chance.

Moreover, the action of fishing for treasures in the fishing pool had never stopped for a moment.

Perhaps during this period, some supreme treasure that could turn the tide of the battle would be fished up again.

In the midst of his rapidly turning thoughts, he used the Star Constellation Giant Net to complete the instructions to his subordinates. During the conversation with the Dharma Transmission, the Holy Emperor forced out a drop of blood from his glabella.

The Dharma Transmission lightly coughed and first restored his high crown that had been cut off in the previous battle.

Then, surging light concealed the small wounds all over his body.

Seeing this scene, the Holy Emperor pondered: "Could it be that the Dharma Transmission is buying time to take the opportunity to comprehend the principle of 'the false is also true'?"

Although he had such a guess in his heart, the Holy Emperor was not worried that the Dharma Transmission would be able to comprehend it during this time.

Because the Holy Emperor had a vague feeling that this method of "the true becomes false and the false becomes true" had its own peculiarities.

It was not something that could be understood simply by relying on a heaven-defying comprehension ability.

While the Holy Emperor was pondering, the Dharma Transmission Heavenly Venerable, who had already restored his original appearance of a compassionate old man with a high crown, made a series of hand seals to envelop the Holy Emperor's blood in mid-air.

The color of the light kept changing and finally settled on a pale yellow color that was extremely similar to the appearance of the Xuanhuang world.

Seeing this scene, the Dharma Transmission's expression changed slightly.

However, his hands did not stop and he continued to cast spells.

In the pale yellow light sphere, the blood struggled violently as if it had come to life.

Then it split into two protrusions.

One of them pointed in the direction of the Xuanhuang Realm.

And the other...

It actually pointed outside the Star Sea!

Moreover, it was still spinning violently!

"How could this..." Seeing the Dharma Transmission's increasingly solemn expression, the Holy Emperor also became grave. He had just started to ask, but before he could finish his sentence.

The space around them suddenly became turbulent.

Unlike the storm caused by the revival power of the Star Sea, it was more like something was about to break out of the ground and drill out from the bottom.

The Holy Emperor and the Dharma Transmission both flashed away and retreated far to avoid it.

They watched as the protrusion that was spinning in the blood wrapped in the pale yellow light sphere gradually stopped.

Quietly pointing forward.

After a moment...

Ripples that were barely perceptible came from the front.

As if a drop of water had just fallen.

Neither divine sense nor sight found the target, but both the Holy Emperor and the Dharma Transmission knew that something had just descended in front of them.

The Dharma Transmission narrowed his eyes slightly, seeming to show a trace of reminiscence.

But when he looked at the Holy Emperor's blood drop again, a hint of puzzlement flashed in his eyes.

"Interesting. That mastermind behind the scenes actually took the initiative to show up..."

In the pale yellow light sphere, the Holy Emperor's blood drop slowly burned out.

However, the protruding part still firmly pointed to the void in front.

"The Immortal Boat..." The Dharma Transmission murmured in a low voice.

The Holy Emperor also narrowed his eyes.

He was naturally clear about the sword light message that had appeared outside the Xuanhuang Realm before.

In an instant, his mind was filled with thoughts.

"Could it be that there is really a conspiracy involving this Immortal Boat behind the scenes?" The Holy Emperor's heart trembled slightly.

"But..."

"The timing of this flying boat's appearance is too coincidental." The Holy Emperor stared ahead and did not take action first.

"The Xuanxian Boat is a remnant of the ancient Xuanshang Immortal Boat. It has been hiding in the endless ruins under the high wall..." The Dharma Transmission briefly introduced the background of the Xuanxian Boat in front of them to the Holy Emperor.

Seeing the magnificent scene at the edge of the Star Sea and under the high wall, the Holy Emperor was momentarily stunned.

But precisely because of this, it highlighted the terrifying nature of the high wall that firmly sealed off this part of the Star Sea.

"As you and I fought fiercely, they appeared here..."

"Let this old man see how much progress the Xuanxian Boat has made over these years." A cold light flashed in the Dharma Transmission's eyes.

His figure remained motionless, only giving a light glance.

The space in front that had been rippling due to the passage of the Xuanxian Boat slowly became calm.

Then, as if an icy seal had descended, the space gradually fell into absolute stillness.

No fluctuations emanated from that dead zone, as if it were the end of the Star Sea world.

"The divine powers of the Dharma Transmission are indeed extraordinary."

Seeing this scene, the Holy Emperor's pupils contracted slightly. He thought in his mind about how he should deal with this move if it were him.

The absolute ice seal could not last.

Just before it was completely finished, a crude force suddenly surged.

It completely shattered the ice-sealed realm.

The image of the Xuanxian Boat flashed by.

The power released by the Xuanxian Boat not only broke the Dharma Transmission's ice-sealed realm, but also resonated with many remnant immortal powers in this part of the Star Sea.

Like a hundred rivers converging, it rushed over in an instant.

If the trap that the Holy Emperor had set up before by diverting the flow was a stream of remnant True Immortal power.

Then what was stirred up at this moment was a mighty river.

Because they were resisting the revival power of the Star Sea, those remnant True Immortal powers were already far more active than usual.

At this moment, being attracted and sensing the aura of the Dharma Transmission and the Holy Emperor's living beings, they chased after them like beasts that had caught the scent of prey.

Both of them were forced to evade.

Although the two had already reached the Star Sea limit known as the Supreme.

Facing the remnant power of the True Immortals that could contend with the Star Sea itself, they were still very wary.

Especially since the two were still in a state of cooperation and hostility, if one side suffered heavy injuries, it would undoubtedly fall into a disadvantage in the decisive battle later.

After all, the remnant power of the True Immortals was different from ordinary moves and could not be easily expelled.

Relying on his connection with the Star Sea, the Holy Emperor could see the remnant power of the True Immortals surging in like a tide and deftly evaded them.

Occasionally, when it was really unavoidable, the flashing of the starlight robe and the prayers coming from the Holy Dynasty's great enlightenment could quickly expel the remnant power of the True Immortals with the essence of the source power that emerged.

As for the Dharma Transmission's methods, they were quite peculiar.

He took out that long arm composed of countless destroyed worlds again.

Like two sponges, it absorbed all the raging remnant power of the True Immortals.

Some of the remnant worlds were completely turned to ashes under the baptism of the remnant power of the True Immortals.

However, other remnant worlds quickly supplemented the inside of the long arm.

One really didn't know how many world fragments like this the Dharma Transmission had collected over the years.

Seeing the Dharma Transmission and the Holy Emperor resolve the crisis so easily, the Xuanxian Boat, which had failed to divert the trouble, made another move.

An earth-yellow paper talisman slowly fell from the void.

It seemed slow and leisurely.

In fact, it was extremely swift.

The Holy Emperor and the Dharma Transmission had just sensed the existence of this talisman in the previous moment, and in the next instant, this talisman had already fallen on their heads.

This was the peculiarity of this paper talisman.

When it first floated down, there was clearly only one. But because there were two targets, it fell on the heads of both at the same time.

"True Immortal Seal Script..."

This thought flashed through the minds of the Holy Emperor and the Dharma Transmission at the same time.

Soon, they felt the power contained in this True Immortal Seal Script.

First, the Holy Emperor noticed that his connection with the Abyssal Star Sea was severed.

It was not directly cut off by the True Immortal Seal Script, but the Star Sea's origin had actively severed the connection.

The Holy Emperor could even vaguely feel the hostility coming from the Star Sea before the loss of contact.

Originally, he could consider it as half of his home field, with the power of the Star Sea assisting his every move. But now, it was as if he was in a quagmire, and every action was met with great resistance.

The Dharma Transmission, who was originally regarded as a temporary cooperation partner, the Holy Emperor also felt a strong malice from him.

In an instant, looking around, there were only enemies!

The Holy Emperor, who had never experienced such an encounter before, looked around and his thoughts turned rapidly.

"Is this the power of the True Immortal Seal Script?"

"It's not the remnant power that can be seen everywhere in the Star Sea, but the complete power of a True Immortal..."

In his mind, the Holy Emperor tried to recall the pattern on the paper talisman he had just seen.

He actually couldn't remember a single detail.

While trying to find a way to deal with the increasingly dense hostile forces around him, the Holy Emperor looked at the Dharma Transmission not far away.

Due to the thickening hostility, the distance between them had unknowingly widened a lot compared to before.

The Dharma Transmission's high crown emitted a jade-like silver light.

It reflected the statues of the Dharma Transmission Heavenly Venerable standing in the Xuanhuang Realm.

One of the statues walked out from the high crown.

In a flash of light, it became a real entity.

As soon as the statue condensed, it was attacked by all the surrounding forces. The power of the Star Sea, the remnant power of the True Immortals, and even the Dharma Transmission himself...

The statue shattered with a bang. The violent explosion caused by the collision of multiple forces swept past and reached the Holy Emperor's position.

It actually changed targets again and surged towards the Holy Emperor.

As if it had truly seen some irreconcilable enemy.

The Holy Emperor solemnly resolved this impact. At the same time, he continued to observe the Dharma Transmission's actions.

As another statue walked out from the high crown and was jointly smashed to pieces by multiple forces.

The Holy Emperor sensed that the power of the True Immortal Seal Script condensed on the Dharma Transmission Heavenly Venerable's body had weakened a little.

"Is this using the stone statues to fake death and substitute himself to reduce the hostility?"

With his outstanding comprehension ability, the Holy Emperor quickly understood the Dharma Transmission's intention.

With a thought, a vague phantom of the Holy Emperor also walked out from the starlight holy robe.

As expected, the moment the phantom was differentiated, it was suddenly attacked by all the surrounding forces.

Including the Holy Emperor himself.

Even when the Holy Emperor struck his own differentiated phantom, he felt a slight pleasure of revenge being completed in his heart.

He and the Dharma Transmission had both reached the limit of the Star Sea.

But after being affected by the True Immortal Seal Script, they were helpless to fight back.

It was only by using this kind of "dead method" that they could slowly resolve the influence of the True Immortal Seal Script.

They simply couldn't contend with it using power of the same level.

"Perhaps, after manifesting the Eternal Xuanhuang Realm and relying on the comprehension of 'the false is also true', it can be resisted."

"As for the other means I currently possess..."

The Holy Emperor quickly went through them in his mind, but couldn't find any that could be compared with it.

In an instant, a rather ridiculous scene appeared in the Star Sea.

The two Supreme experts were pinned in place by a True Immortal Seal Script out of nowhere.

And the Xuanxian Boat, which had been observing in the dark for a while, finally made another move.

The somewhat disheveled Holy Emperor heard a message transmission in his ear.

"The Xuanxian Boat only seeks the materials to survive in the Star Sea."

"Perhaps we can cooperate with each other."

The Holy Emperor was startled in his heart.

Seeing that the Holy Emperor did not respond for a long time, the Xuanxian Boat sent another message.
