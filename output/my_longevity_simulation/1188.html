The three of them looked at each other and saw the ambition in each other's hearts.

However, the whispers stopped in the empty and uninhabited realm.

When they returned to the original valley, they became silent and looked resentful.

After reporting back to Elder Gou, they came to live in the wooden house in the valley.

There were a total of one hundred cultivators living here.

Every once in a while, some of them would go out to the Immortal Ark to collect relics of world destruction.

The people of the Immortal Ark seemed extremely afraid of the dark starry sea outside, and every time before carrying out a mission, they looked uneasy.

And indeed, the casualty rate for collecting relics was very high.

It was not that they suffered any substantial damage in the starry sea outside the Immortal Ark, but every time they went out, the lifespan of the Immortal Ark people seemed to be greatly reduced.

This cultivator surnamed Zhao, Zhao Tianjiang, after going out twice, the wrinkles on his face were obviously deeper.

"No wonder I haven't seen any young cultivators here, they are all middle-aged and elderly people." Li Fan suddenly realized in his heart.

The hundred or so people in the valley did not cultivate on weekdays.

In addition to undertaking the task of going out to collect relics, they did not have to do anything else.

From their daily chats, Li Fan also collected a lot of information about the Immortal Ark.

This valley was called 【Guilt Valley】 by them, a place of exile for cultivators who had violated the laws of the Immortal Ark but whose crimes were not punishable by death.

Collecting relics of world destruction was to eliminate the sins on their bodies.

When they had made amends for their crimes, they could leave this place and live a normal life again.

In their hopeful words, Li Fan glimpsed the daily life of the people of the Immortal Ark.

All matters, big and small, in the Immortal Ark were under the jurisdiction of the 【Celestial Pivot Court】.

Ordinary people of the Immortal Ark did not have to worry about survival, as the Celestial Pivot Court provided a fixed amount of living materials every year.

But it was just barely enough to satisfy survival.

In the Immortal Ark, there was strict control over the total population and the number of cultivators.

Not only did procreation of the next generation require approval, but even cultivation had only a fixed number of places.

In addition to obtaining the qualification for cultivation by drawing lots, it could also be obtained by making outstanding contributions to the Immortal Ark.

For example, going out to salvage relics of world destruction.

In this Guilt Valley, the vast majority of people had no cultivation.

That's why they had such great fear of going out.

In addition to ensuring their own safety, they could also identify which relics were qualified and could be exchanged for merit.

As for what the Celestial Pivot Court wanted these relics of world destruction for...

Naturally, these bottom-level people of the Immortal Ark would not be clear about it.
