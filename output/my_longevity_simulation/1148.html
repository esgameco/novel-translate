The First Emperor Beast had long led the demon beast tribes to flee the Xuan Huang Realm, while the Third Emperor Mo was deeply hidden within the Immortal Alliance headquarters, rarely seen by ordinary people.

However, they both appeared in the Fallen Immortal Realm's "Beast Taming Sect Incident", so one could go find them and ask for clarification.

"Fellow Daoist Sun?"

Elder Zhang Xu's voice transmission interrupted Li Fan's thoughts.

Attention returned to the Derivation Orb's guardian space, where the little loli was handling an urgent derivation task.

For no reason, she had an extra task, and she looked angry with a face full of resentment.

The task only required a joint Dao technique, but it had already taken three days and three nights and still hadn't been derived.

There was a high possibility that this little miss was using the opportunity to slack off.

In the derivation space, besides the three joint Dao guardians, there was also a young man dressed in blue.

Although his body was straight and motionless, one could still see the anxiety in his demeanor.

Considering that the level of this derivation task was quite high, if it was truly delayed it might cause some trouble. Guardian Zhang Xu then secretly voice transmitted, telling Li Fan to be the bad guy.

In any case, during this period of time, Li Fan's image in Derivation Orb's heart had already been classified as one of the most despicable people in the world.

There was no need to transform into a gong to remind her.

When that hourglass silently appeared above Derivation Orb's head, the little loli's face greatly changed, and the speed of the character light orbs' rotation instantly multiplied several times.

Before long, she derived the technique needed by the blue-clothed youth.

The youth expressed his endless gratitude and then hurriedly left.

"In such a hurry, it seems the Qing Xuan Army has encountered trouble." Zhang Xu sighed.

After repeatedly taking action to stop Derivation Orb's slacking off behavior many times, Li Fan also gradually became familiar with the other two joint Dao guardians.

Zhang Xu had entered over a hundred years earlier than Li Fan.

As for the other one, named Hua Yue Ye, his name sounded a bit feminine, but he was an undoubtedly manly man.

Hating evil like an enemy, because he couldn't stand a Dharma transmitter's conduct, he actually fought with them on the spot.

The consequences were imaginable.

Although with the help of relatives and friends, he ultimately avoided death, he was still punished to guard Derivation Orb here for five hundred years.

Now not even half that time had passed.

Originally when Li Fan first came, Hua Yue Ye was too lazy to even greet him. Now that they were familiar, he actually talked more than Zhang Xu.

"Although I really dislike Young Master Shang's bearing, guarding the demon abyss for hundreds of years without leaving a step, one can't help but admire him..."

"The Qing Xuan Army..."

In the two's casual chat, Li Fan also recalled the identity of that blue-clothed cultivator just now.

They had met once before, his name was Yan Huan.

"The Young Master's temper is even more stubborn than mine. Once he decides on something, I'm afraid even a Celestial Venerate couldn't pull him back. The abolished case that was rejected by both the Dharma transmitters and mirror holders, even after so many years he still shows no intention of giving up."
