Li Fan looked behind Xu Ke.

Only now upon closer inspection did he discover that this pale white coffin seemed to be vaguely connected to the towering stone mountain on his body.

Or to put it another way...

Li Fan narrowed his eyes and observed for a long time, then suddenly realized.

The scene of the entire small world in his eyes instantly flipped.

The earth was above, the sky below.

The towering stone mountain on the coffin seemed to be supporting the entire sky.

Or, the weight of the entire world was pressing down on the coffin.

Moreover, Li Fan felt an extremely familiar aura emanating from this coffin.

It was an aura that no Xuanhuang cultivator would find unfamiliar.

He also recalled Xu Ke's self-introduction during their previous meeting, mentioning "the one who is against the world".

"This... could it be?" Li Fan was shocked and terrified. He abruptly raised his head and stared at Xu Ke.

Once this was done, upon returning to the Xuanhuang Realm, he would surely receive a reward a thousand times over.

If it were an ordinary cultivator, perhaps in this instant they would have been convinced by its words, falling into becoming its tool for breaking free and fighting against Xu Ke.

But who was Li Fan?

Not to mention the firmness of his own will, just that thread of wariness he always maintained towards Mr. Bai meant that he was destined not to be bewitched by this somewhat creepy-looking Mr. Bai.

With a cold snort, the scene before his eyes completely shattered.

Li Fan awoke from the illusion, only to find Xu Ke observing him coldly, seeming like he would take action at any disagreeable word.

However, after seeing that Li Fan was not affected by the illusory realm, the killing intent in his eyes also faded away.

"The sealed heavenly favor? What exactly is that thing?" Li Fan put on a lingering fear.

"The self-awareness of the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao attached to Mr. Bai was stripped away by him. After so many years of sealing and suppression, it has gradually become somewhat extreme. If it were released and allowed to return to the Xuanhuang Realm..." Xu Ke's expression was very solemn.

"I'm afraid a calamity ten times more terrifying than the 'Dharma Cannot Be Jointly Cultivated' would descend in an instant. This matter is of great importance and absolutely cannot be neglected."

"An extreme Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao?" In the depths of Li Fan's eyes, a trace of unusual brilliance flashed for an instant.

"Yes. I can sense that in these sealed years, the heavenly dao consciousness inside has become more and more bizarre. I really worry that in the future, there will also come a day when I can no longer persist." A deep concern flashed through Xu Ke's eyes.

"Actually, in this recent period of time, I have also been secretly looking for a suitable successor. However, I have never found someone I can feel at ease with. Today, Fellow Daoist, by a coincidence of fate, you came knocking..."

Xu Ke suddenly changed the topic: "Could it be that there is a predestined arrangement in the mysteries?"

"I observe that Fellow Daoist's character and integrity are absolutely top-notch." He looked at Li Fan sincerely, speaking very earnestly.

Li Fan pondered without responding.

"Of course, the mission of suppressing the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao is too lonely and perilous. If Fellow Daoist is unwilling, I can also understand. But I have a request..." Xu Ke said.

"Please speak, Fellow Daoist." Li Fan's expression turned solemn.

"If one day, I am controlled by the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao in this coffin and lose my self-awareness, I ask that Fellow Daoist take action and slay me." Xu Ke said very solemnly.

Looking at Xu Ke's aged gaze, Li Fan nodded, "Alright, I promise you."

"Also, you don't need to worry about finding a successor."

Xu Ke's expression slightly froze.
