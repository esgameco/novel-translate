Chapter 302

Ji Hongdao's tone was very serious.

All the students understood that such an opportunity might only come once in a lifetime.

No matter how good their mentality was, they couldn't help but become nervous and expectant.

In front of everyone, Ji Hongdao took out a communication talisman, seeming to be contacting someone.

After a moment, he nodded and ended the communication.

He took out a Tianxuan Mirror from his storage ring.

Tossing it up, it hovered high above everyone's heads.

He then took out a silver jade slip and gently crushed it.

In an instant, countless chaotic symbols surged out from the jade slip.

Ji Hongdao made a hand seal, and those symbols flew towards the Tianxuan Mirror in the sky according to a specific order.

Li Fan squinted his eyes and saw that on the surface of the Tianxuan Mirror, lines of text flashed in succession.

The symbols kept changing, and after absorbing the text from this jade slip, the originally somewhat ordinary Tianxuan Mirror underwent a strange transformation.

The outline of the mirror emitted a faint glow and began to dissolve and disappear.

Without the constraint of the mirror's edge, the mirror's surface began to expand rapidly in all directions.

In the blink of an eye, a palm-sized mirror turned into a huge door, a hundred zhang high and thirty zhang wide.

On the towering and wide mirror surface, the surrounding scenery was not reflected, only streams of silver light flowing.

As everyone was marveling at this scene of change before their eyes, Ji Hongdao flew to the front of the mirror surface and made a hand seal.

A beam of silver light shot out from the mirror surface, enveloping Ji Hongdao.

A line of symbols on the mirror surface appeared and disappeared in an instant.

It seemed to have confirmed Ji Hongdao's identity. The light beam dimmed, and then a touch of black appeared from the center of the mirror surface.

In a flash, it expanded to the entire mirror surface.

A rectangular spatial passage appeared before everyone.

"This is..."

"Using the mirror image of any Tianxuan Mirror as a node, spatial transmission can be opened?"

"It seems that it also requires dual verification of password and personnel." Li Fan silently guessed based on the previous signs.

"Follow behind me. Only after passing the identity verification of the Tianxuan Mirror can you enter the passage."

With that said, Ji Hongdao took the lead and flew in.

With excited hearts, the students couldn't wait to fly over as well.

Whenever someone was about to approach the passage, a silver light beam would fall from above and lock onto them.

If no abnormality appeared and the light beam suddenly disappeared again, it meant the verification was passed.

"This seems to have a similar ingenuity to the Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation." Thinking this, Li Fan also followed the crowd and squeezed into the spatial passage.

A feeling of dizziness hit him fiercely.

A sensation extremely similar to long-distance transmission, as if even space-time was stretched and perception was distorted.

The next moment, it was like tearing out of a thin membrane that wrapped around the entire body.

Li Fan fell into a strange space.

All colors instantly faded away, and everything in sight was gray and white.

Black lines whistled from the distant other side, brushing past everyone's bodies, some even passing through, and rushing towards the endless distance.

White lines also shot out from the abyss above or below, heading in the opposite direction.

Occasionally, black and white lines collided, the two colors constantly entangled with each other, eventually forming a void point.

The duration of the void points varied.

Some lasted for a moment, some persisted for a few breaths.

But in the end, they would all completely vanish.

Just as everyone was stunned looking at this somewhat surreal and fantastic scene before their eyes.

Everyone's heads suddenly swam, and their perspective abruptly rose to an infinite height.

They saw that in this vast world that seemed to have no boundaries.

Black and white lines seemed to be moving rapidly without any pattern, yet also seemed to follow certain rules.

Colliding, blending with each other.

Void points were born and perished in constant flashes.

...

"It's said that all the secrets of the world are hidden in this black and white. Unfortunately, I am of mediocre aptitude and unable to comprehend it." Ji Hongdao's voice of regretful sigh rang in everyone's ears.

"Let's go, the newborn mirror surface is just ahead."

Ji Hongdao casually grabbed a passing black line, and in an instant, his figure disappeared from everyone's sight along with the black line.

Seeing this, the students also imitated it, grabbing the passing black lines beside them.

As if fusing with the black line, the originally high-speed moving gray world also became extremely slow in Li Fan's eyes.

Constantly moving towards the distant target, when encountering white lines moving up and down along the way.

These white lines seemed to have self-awareness, and would actively avoid them.

Li Fan, riding the "express delivery", marveled at the sights along the way.

After an unknown amount of time, they arrived at the edge of this gray and white world.

The black line faded and disappeared, and everyone recovered their original appearance one after another.

Ahead was a colorful halo that clashed with the gray and white world.

Perhaps it was because they had stayed here for a long time and hadn't seen colors for a while.

When Li Fan saw that churning, distorted mass of colors not far away, he inexplicably felt a sense of nausea and disgust in his heart.

Gray and white collided and rubbed against the colors.

Although it didn't make any sound, Li Fan could feel two powerful forces competing and shaking the space against each other.

In the confrontation, a part of the colorful mass seemed to be cut off by the gray.

Losing its original colors, it became dull and lifeless, quietly floating at the edge of the space.

After a long time, a faint glimmer seemed to light up on them at the same time.

Originally gray and white, they slowly started to become a bit crystal-clear and translucent.

Ji Hongdao didn't speak, but the students already understood that what was before their eyes was the birth process of the newborn mirror surfaces of Tianxuan Mirrors.

They all quieted their hearts and minds to carefully comprehend it.

However, Li Fan was slightly stunned for a moment, first looking around, then sensing his own body.

He couldn't help but fall into deep thought.

Time seemed to have accelerated.

One after another, newborn mirror surfaces were born.

Some stayed in place after birth, while some suddenly disappeared to unknown destinations.

But overall, the vast majority floated at the edge of the gray world.

The number of mirror surfaces accumulated more and more.

Finally, at a certain moment.

Endless black and white fine lines surged from the gray world towards the newborn mirror surfaces.

Black and white intertwined, like the most exquisite loom, quickly weaving clothes for these newborns.

When the mirror surfaces were completely shrouded by the woven fabric of black and white lines.

Black and white instantly fused into one.

A sphere with an extremely smooth surface and black and white colors flowing in a chaotic manner suddenly appeared.

"This is the pivot of the [Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation]." Ji Hongdao's voice rang out just at the right moment.

"Did you all see it clearly?"

The voice instantly faded away.

All the surrounding scenes fell into stillness.

And shattered completely.
