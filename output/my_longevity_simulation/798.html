As the second small world power to actively surrender, and with its overall strength still quite intact, the Medicine King Sect was highly valued by the Myriad Immortals Alliance.

As for the fact that many cultivators from the Medicine King Sect had already infiltrated the Immortal Alliance, Qing Yun did not investigate deeply.
