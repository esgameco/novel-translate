Chapter 942

"What are you talking about, senior? What's wrong with me?"

"In the entire Xuanhuang Realm, besides me, there's probably no one else willing to play a thousand games of chess with you, right?"

Shengtai smiled and asked in return while placing a piece. This first move was directly placed in the "Tianyuan" position.

The disheveled old man's expression changed. He glanced at Shengtai, and his demeanor became extremely serious.

"You little fellow, you clearly only have Yuan Ying cultivation. But your deduction ability is frighteningly strong. And you don't even try to conceal it, directly sitting beside me."

"You came fully prepared!"

"Originally, I was a bit happy. After so many years, I could finally satisfy my chess craving. Halfway through the game, I realized that I had fallen into your trap!"

The old man sighed slightly.

Shengtai just smiled without answering.

The disheveled old man was named "Thousand Seeking Daoist". He was a veteran Harmonious Dao cultivator who had fallen in cultivation due to injuries.

No one knew what contributions he had made to the Immortal Alliance, but he actually carried a "Jing Fa Jade Tablet" on his body.

Of course, at this point in time, few people in the world knew about this.

In the eyes of the crowd, this Thousand Seeking Daoist was just an ordinary cultivator who was a bit crazy.

Only more than ten years later, a mortal named Xiao Shengke encountered Thousand Seeking Daoist by chance.

After accompanying Thousand Seeking Daoist to complete a thousand days of begging together, he was gifted the Jing Fa Jade Tablet.

With a mortal body, he met Yan Fa Jue face to face.

He obtained the Harmonious Dao cultivation method and from then on strode on the immortal path.

This matter was often talked about with relish by later generations.

Shengtai naturally also sought out Thousand Seeking Daoist for his Harmonious Dao Jing Fa Jade Tablet.

However, a thousand days of begging was too long. So he deliberately used words to provoke the other party to play chess with him.

Thousand Seeking Daoist, his name was closely related to his cultivation method.

Everything needed to be completed in a thousand increments and required someone to accompany him.

When he played chess with Shengtai, he was actually also cultivating.

The only limitation was that he could not take the initiative to mention the inside story to others. And when he completed a segment of "Thousand Seeking" cultivation, his appearance and aura would change as if he was a different person.

So everything could only depend on fate.

But Shengtai Li Fan was able to know about this matter in advance.

When the last piece was placed, Shengtai Li Fan lost by one move and ultimately lost to Thousand Seeking Daoist.

"You little brat, you at least knew to let me win one game."

Thousand Seeking Daoist cursed angrily. Although he won, he couldn't be happy no matter what.

But a surging aura emanated from the old man's body.

"Congratulations senior, you are not far from re-attaining Harmonious Dao!" Shengtai Li Fan said with a smile.

"What do you want?" Thousand Seeking Daoist didn't waste words and directly asked.

"The Jing Fa Jade Tablet."

"Get lost!" Thousand Seeking Daoist directly threw over the jade tablet.

Without waiting for Li Fan to respond, his figure disappeared.

Shengtai put away the jade tablet, not minding. Anyone who lost 999 games in a row, and all seemed to be "narrow defeats" just one move short, would definitely be a bit irritated.

Not making a move to teach a lesson could already be considered Thousand Seeking Daoist having high character.

"With the Hua Dao Stone's deductions, there are truly few in the world who can beat me in the Dao of chess."

Shengtai shook his head slightly and arrived at a remote place with no one around.

He directly used the Jing Fa Jade Tablet.

Before this, he had already settled his identity within the Myriad Immortal Alliance.

So...

The entire process went without any unexpected incidents.

As usual, a disciple came to guide the way, and a doorway appeared out of thin air.

However, after a moment of dizziness, he once again arrived at the headquarters of the Myriad Immortals Alliance...

"Hmm?"

Looking around, the scene was not that of the Myriad Immortals Alliance headquarters.

In a hazy sphere of light, several silhouettes stood.

Fragments of conversation could be heard coming from within.

"This time, how confident are you?"

"I can only do my best and leave the rest to fate."

"That doesn't sound like something you would say."

"The difference in strength is too great, I can only risk my life and fight."

"Forget it, you should go. Who knows if it will be a blessing or a curse."

The voice of one of the silhouettes sounded somewhat familiar to the Holy Fetus Li Fan.

Yet it wasn't exactly the same as anyone he knew.

He knew this was an illusion caused by the influence of the Dharma Transmission Venerable when he first entered the Alliance headquarters.

But for some reason, this time it was different from what his original self and his clones had seen before.

"Is one of these silhouettes the Dharma Transmission Venerable?"

"And what does this signify?"

Before Li Fan could ponder further, he felt the world shake and the scene shatter into layers.

He left the illusory realm and appeared in the headquarters of the Immortal Alliance.

The guiding disciple was used to this, gave a simple explanation, and continued leading Li Fan towards the Dharma Derivation Orb.

The route was familiar, exactly the same as his experiences in previous lives.

Until he entered the inner area of the Dharma Protector Hall's headquarters, the announcement he heard greatly surprised Li Fan.

"Applicant for the use of the Dharma Derivation Orb, Fan Li."

"Number: 005. Authority level: Harmonious Dao."

"Please wait patiently here. Estimated remaining waiting time, three thousand six hundred and twenty-three days and six hours."

At first, the Holy Fetus Li Fan thought he had misheard. After hearing it again, he was startled.

"How can the wait be so long?"

Shocked and doubtful, he made a corresponding expression and couldn't help but ask the empty space.

"If you are unwilling to wait, you may leave on your own."

"Warning, leaving means voluntarily giving up the right to use the Dharma Derivation Jade Tablet."

After a long time, a voice finally responded in the space.

"I'm number 005, yet I need to wait for nearly ten years. How does that make sense?"

"It's not like I've never used the Dharma Derivation Orb before. How long can it take to derive a cultivation method? Someone must be misusing this public artifact for private purposes!"

The Holy Fetus Li Fan was not intimidated at all, instead he made a big fuss.

He seemed determined to get an explanation.

"Due to uncontrollable factors, the derivation ability of the Dharma Derivation Orb has been severely disrupted."

"Therefore, the waiting time has also been greatly extended."

"Please understand and cooperate, fellow cultivator."

"Warning, this matter is a top secret of the Immortal Alliance, please be sure not to leak it."

That voice warned.

The Holy Fetus Li Fan's expression changed slightly, but he did not restrain himself at all.

Instead, he shouted exaggeratedly, "What? The foundation of the Immortal Alliance, a wonder of the heavens, the Dharma Derivation Orb is actually malfunctioning?"

"What the hell are you Dharma Protectors doing!"

"Such a serious matter has occurred, not only have you not thought about how to solve it, you even deliberately try to conceal it?!"

"There must be a traitor among you!"

"No, I must make this matter known to all!"

...

Amidst the Holy Fetus Li Fan's shouting, a figure slowly appeared in front of him.

Expressionless, looking at the Holy Fetus Li Fan as if looking at a dead man.

But Li Fan instantly became serious: "Why don't you let me take a look, perhaps I have a solution."
