Chapter 350

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Assisted Congyun Sea Martial Arts Hall in clearing the red-haired beasts on Wanlai Island.

Those beasts feed on sound and reproduce extremely quickly.

They can also merge together as a group, forming monsters far exceeding the average strength of individuals.

After a fierce battle, the martial arts team successfully eliminated a certain number of red-haired beasts and smoothly completed the mission.

Li Fan asked Jiang Zhengji in surprise why they didn't completely exterminate the beasts on Wanlai Island.

Jiang Zhengji told him that the Wanxian Alliance Martial Arts Hall headquarters has a mission that requires collecting and submitting a certain number of sound-absorbing exotic treasures from various places each year.

And the required quantity is increasing.

At that time, Li Fan just felt this mission was quite strange.

Now it seems it was to deal with this Chizha Abyss?

Sure enough, just as this thought popped up in Li Fan's mind, he heard Huangfu Song say with some emotion: "The demonic sound coming from the bottom of the Chizha Abyss is extremely terrifying. The Alliance has tried various methods but none can block it."

"Silencing spells and soundproof materials have no effect at all."

"It was only later when a cultivator used a clam shell magic treasure that can swallow and spit out sound that it was discovered these types of exotic treasures have a remarkable effect."

"Perhaps it's because these exotic treasures have no intelligence or complex thoughts, the demonic sound that has a fatal effect on cultivators has a greatly reduced effect on them."

"One thing subdues another indeed!"

"The Alliance collected many such exotic treasures and finally held the defense line of the Chizha Abyss."

"And for safety's sake, the Eternal Immortal Fortress was moved back to its original position, suppressing the Chizha Abyss."

"So our trip there this time, is it because something happened to the Eternal Immortal Fortress?" Li Fan asked.

Huangfu Song shook his head: "With that person guarding the Eternal Immortal Fortress, and the accumulation of many exotic treasures, the defense line is almost unshakable."

"In fact, the change this time occurred outside the Chizha Abyss..."

Huangfu Song was about to say more but his expression slightly changed.

Glancing out the window, he instructed: "Get ready, it's time to set off."

"Kid Xue, drink the tea. Passing through the white fog will consume quite a bit of lifespan. This is free compensation for you. Miss it and there won't be any more."

With that said, he shifted his buttocks and sat solemnly on a round seat beneath him.

Seeing this, Li Fan also sat up straight in a similar manner.

Sensing slightly, it was as if entering another space in an instant, separated from the cabin.

At this time, Xue Mu had just finished drinking the spirit tea. As the flying boat suddenly shook violently, he staggered and almost fell.

But this sudden acceleration of the flying boat had no effect on Li Fan and Huangfu Song.

The two looked at Xue Mu at the same time, their faces full of smiles.

The flying boat seemed to be accelerating constantly. Xue Mu spent quite some effort before managing to sit on the round seat in a somewhat disheveled manner.

Only after Xue Mu sat down did Huangfu Song put away his smile and continue the previous topic: "Not long ago, the Alliance discovered signs of another realm about to be born not far from the Chizha Abyss in this foggy domain."

"Dealing with the Chizha Abyss alone is not easy. If these two realms merge, who knows what else will happen."

"So the Alliance gave the order to relocate this newly appeared realm to another empty area."

"By the way, build the Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation outside these two realms for future monitoring and observation."

"This is our mission for this trip."

Huangfu Song said lightly.

This information was a bit overwhelming.

"Realms can be relocated?"

"Realms can merge?"

Xue Mu and Li Fan asked at the same time.

Huangfu Song glanced at the two and explained: "A realm is just the spatial structure changing after entering a specific area."

"Outside this area, everything is normal."

"This is the boundary of a realm. Naturally, if one grasps the Dao of space, one can cut and move this entire area."

"Although it's a bit troublesome, it's not a difficult matter."

"Of course, this kind of thing is for us old guys to handle. Can't expect you youngsters to do it."

"Your mission is to cooperate in building the Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation and seal off the realm."

Li Fan nodded slightly.

But Xue Mu was still a bit puzzled: "Senior Huangfu, if realms can be relocated, why not move that Chizha Abyss away instead of spending a lot of effort to suppress it?"

Huangfu Song smiled grimly: "Yeah, why?"

Xue Mu was immediately at a loss for words, not knowing how to answer.

He looked at Xue Mu deeply and said slowly: "Who knows what more terrifying things will pop up after moving it? It's better to honestly suppress it. At least now it's still at a stage that can be dealt with."

Just at the moment when Huangfu Song's words abruptly stopped, a spine-chilling feeling suddenly appeared in Li Fan's heart.

Feeling chilly all over, as if something was crawling on him.

His heart felt empty, as if something had quietly slipped away unnoticed.

Xue Mu's face turned pale, and although Huangfu Song's expression was normal, the fleeting fear in his eyes did not escape Li Fan's notice.

Li Fan opened the Huanzhen panel and saw that his physiological age of 49 had turned to 50 in a short period of time.

Fortunately, his psychological youth remained unmoved, making him fearless.

But this couldn't be shown. Li Fan also pretended to be very nervous and asked, "Have we already entered the Life-Devouring White Fog?"

Then he looked out the window.

Sure enough, it was a white blur and nothing could be seen.

Huangfu Song nodded: "The location of the Chizha Abyss is not far from the boundary of the white fog. We'll be there soon."

"The closer to the realm, the stronger the life-devouring ability of the white fog."

"It's still okay inside this [Crossing Dust Boat]. Without protection, infiltrating the realm alone, even a thousand years of lifespan is not enough."

"Remember, you must carry the [Black Death Talisman] with you at all times in this realm."

"Otherwise, with your mere Golden Core cultivation, I'm afraid you'll turn into bare white bones in just the time it takes to burn an incense stick."

Huangfu Song solemnly instructed, then thought of something and added: "Hmm, this thing will be distributed to you uniformly before disembarking later."

"It also needs to be collected before leaving."

I didn't expect the location of this mission to be so dangerous.

Xue Mu, who was already not good with words, became even more silent now.

Although he tried to appear calm, he couldn't hide the panic in his heart.

Li Fan also continued to show a mix of fear and composure.

Huangfu Song had finished saying what needed to be said. The cabin fell into silence for a while.

But the quiet didn't last long.

Soon after, rustling sounds suddenly appeared in Li Fan's ears.

"Doc..."

"Heav..."

Li Fan's heart jumped and he looked at the two beside him.

"Did you hear something?"

He asked.
