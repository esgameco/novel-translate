Chapter 429

After pondering for a moment, the person spoke cautiously, "This matter still needs to be carefully considered. Let's meet again in seven days."

After giving a coordinate location to Li Fan, the person's face disappeared quietly.

However, a moment later, he reappeared.

"Right, no matter what, He Zhenghao, this person, has a high value of attraction."

"You must form a good relationship with him. Even if this exam is fake, based on his relationship with the Tianyu State's Heng family alone, it's worth us investing heavily."

"I heard from your report before that this person is most fascinated by formations?"

"Perhaps we can start from this aspect."

After giving a few more instructions, the person finally left.

After emitting a yellow light, the smooth broken stone began to crack inch by inch and fell to the ground.

Li Fan went forward to inspect it and found that it had been completely destroyed.

Standing still for a moment, Li Fan still sported the appearance of Zhou Qing'ang and quietly flew away.

After flying far enough and confirming that no one was following or monitoring him along the way, Li Fan transformed back into the middle-aged man he originally looked like in the shadows.

"The bait has been set, now it depends on whether the Five Elders will take the hook."

As he flew, Li Fan recollected memories related to the Five Elders.

"The Realm of No Worries..."

"I suppose the one controlling this realm is the Longevity Tianzun who can make people forget disasters."

"But what is the true country about. The Realm of No Worries Tianzun's rebellion against the principles of heaven and earth should cover almost the entire Xuanhuang Realm."

"However, 'Cannot Lie' obviously does not affect other regions, only in specific areas."

"Zhao Shijie's 'Unity of All' seems to be the same."

"The Realm of No Worries and the Transmitter of Laws should be at a higher level than these two..."

"No wonder the Five Elders have five Longevity Tianzuns guarding them, and they still fear the Transmitter of Laws."

"Even if they make a move to test, they are careful."

Li Fan pondered.

This connection with the Five Elders was not a spur of the moment decision for Li Fan.

Apart from maximizing profits using the entrance exam questions of the Immortal Alliance.

In the future, to prevent the Tianxuan Locking Spirit Formation from purifying towards the 'Xuan Network,' balancing with the Five Elders is also crucial.

Having a doppelganger acting as a spy for the Five Elders will make things much easier in the future.

Next, Li Fan did not return to Wuxian Island but flew towards the southeastern sea area of the Congyun Sea.

The remote small island located in the center of the sea area was Li Fan's destination for this trip.

Half a day later, Li Fan saw from afar the compound courtyard on the island.

In the yard, five cultivators were sitting cross-legged.

An old man with a red face and white hair, looking very spirited, was pacing around the cultivators.

He continued to speak some profound and obscure words that were difficult to understand.

This cultivator at the late Foundation Building stage was the 'Foundation Building Master' that Li Fan had met years ago.

Including Li Chenfeng who stood guard, and the man surnamed Xiao who worked as an actor.

In fact, these three people were all lawbreakers who had sneaked into the small world since childhood.

Their hometown, a small world called the 'Empire,' was trying to break free from the constraints of the Xuanhuang Realm by using the power of a group of whales composed of ten thousand split boundary whales.

And they were tasked with stealing the cultivation texts from the Xuanhuang Realm.

In order to set up a new cultivation tradition once the 'Empire' breaks free from the Xuanhuang Realm.

Using ordinary methods, it was like a foolish dream for a Foundation Building cultivator to accumulate enough contribution points to exchange for joint path techniques.

So they took risks, accumulating wealth through these fraudulent methods.

Li Fan came to find them not because of the minimal contribution points they possessed but to leverage the Foundation Building Master's reputation among the numerous Qi Refining cultivators.

Li Fan smiled faintly and changed his appearance again to appear as a stranger.

Boldly landing in front of the Foundation Building Master.

"Calm your mind, focus attentively..." The Foundation Building Master was shaking his head and reciting these words like chanting scriptures.

Seeing Li Fan descending from the sky, his words suddenly stopped.

"A Gold Core cultivator?"

Instantly, various thoughts surged in the Foundation Building Master's mind.

But Li Fan just waved his hand gently, knocking out the other four Qi Refining cultivators present.

Only the actor surnamed Xiao and the Foundation Building Master remained, and then he said calmly, "It's you, who sneaked into this world from a small world since childhood?"

This sentence made the faces of the two men change drastically.

The Foundation Building Master was about to explain but felt as if he was tightly entangled by a net all of a sudden.

At the same time he lost control of his body, he couldn't even speak.

He couldn't even commit suicide.

A hint of despair flashed in the Foundation Building Master's eyes.

Li Fan was about to speak but suddenly felt something rapidly approaching behind him.

He teleported his body, dodging in place.

Looking back, Li Fan's gaze slightly condensed.

He saw a figure about two zhang (around 6 meters) tall, and upon seeing the appearance, it turned out to be the Qi Refining cultivator Li Chenfeng.

But at this moment, he had undergone a huge transformation in his appearance.

His body had expanded several times, and horn-like carapaces grew on the surface of his body.

Two pairs of transparent wings flapped rapidly behind him, keeping him suspended in mid-air.

The parts of his hands were replaced by a pair of sharp dark-gold giant claws.

His face also seemed to have a mask covered with the appearance of a monstrous beast, with only his eyes exposed.

The whole person seemed to be a combination of a human and a beast. Under the sunlight, it exuded a strange beauty.

"Interesting. What is this thing?"

The changes in front of him were beyond Li Fan's expectations.

Staring at Li Chenfeng, he found that his transformed body seemed to be capable of shielding to a certain extent.

The two zhang giant, when sensed with divine sense, appeared to be the size of a fly.

"Senior, spare us. Pretend you didn't see anything, can you?" Li Chenfeng's mixed voice sounded.

However, before Li Fan could respond, his massive body disappeared from the spot.

With his giant claws raised high, he was about to tear Li Fan's head apart.

Li Fan's expression changed slightly.

Because he found that his Insect Sealing technique had lost its effect on Li Chenfeng.

This was the first time since he learned it from his Insect Master brother.

Although surprised, he quickly set up several formations by his side in an instant.

Raising his hand, he faced the attack head-on.

The cyan giant palm collided with the dark-gold giant claw, erupting with a thunderous noise.

Using this opportunity, Li Fan distanced himself while illuminating the world with a blue water sword.

The Cloud Cluster Sword pierced through Li Chenfeng's body, splitting him in two.

Countless blood sprayed, but Li Fan did not relax his vigilance.

Because the severed body of Li Chenfeng quickly healed the wound in an instant.

Afterwards, his two halves came at Li Fan from left and right.

At this moment, the formations set up by Li Fan earlier quietly activated.

After flying forward for a while, Li Chenfeng seemed to be frozen in mid-air, unable to move another step forward!
