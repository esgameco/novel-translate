Li Fan didn't forget his goal, and after searching the ruins again without finding any useful information,

He marked the ruins, chose a direction, stuck close to the ground, and flew away again.

Two days later, Li Fan found another new ruin in the sea of mist.

The scale of this ruin was several times larger than the first ruin.

Correspondingly, the scene of countless bodies piled up was even more tragic.

The vivid expressions on their faces made Li Fan feel as if he was almost at the scene of the tragedy.

Suppressing the emotions in his heart, Li Fan searched around as usual.

Although he didn't find any valuable information, the bodies in the sea of corpses caught his attention.

The vast majority of cultivators in the first and second ruins died in an instant.

Apart from the expressions of fear and despair on their faces, they left no other information.

Among so many people, there were still one or two cultivators who were relatively strong in their cultivation base.

Before their deaths, they stared in a resentful and unwilling manner toward a certain direction.

It seemed that the entity that took their lives was there.

Even after several years of death, they still maintained this posture.

This inadvertently pointed the direction for Li Fan, an outsider, in the white mist.

So, following the guidance of the bodies, Li Fan flew rapidly for a day and found the third ruin.

The fourth, the fifth, the sixth...

Until there was only enough supplies left to last for three more days.

Li Fan had encountered eleven ruins filled with corpses front and back.

While he was rushing towards the twelfth ruin on the way,

An eerie thing happened.

After flying forward quickly, not long after, he unexpectedly returned to the eleventh ruin.

After finding the mark he had made before, Li Fan confirmed it wasn't his illusion.

So, he set off again.

Yet, the result was the same.

After making a circle, he returned to the starting point.

After pondering for a moment, Li Fan didn't panic.

On his third attempt.

This time, he slowed down, continued flying while constantly sensing the direction of the eleventh ruin.

Finally understanding what was happening.

At a certain position on the way, he encountered something.

That made the direction he was heading, in a moment, and without any trace, turn backward.

During the first two times, Li Fan was unaware and thought he was still flying forward.

In reality, he had turned around.

Naturally, not long after, he returned to the starting point once again.

At this moment, with a serious expression on his face, Li Fan stood in front of the area that caused the change.

Flying in, his body position didn't change. (*Note: The text includes a website link that can be omitted.)

But the eleventh ruin that was sensed behind him had inexplicably appeared in front of him.

Frowning slightly, Li Fan turned back and tried to move away from the eleventh ruin.

But not long after flying, the direction became disoriented again.

However, Li Fan had gained experience and always kept the eleventh ruin behind him.

Adjusting the direction continuously, he moved forward.

So, after many twists and turns, and spending two more days, he finally arrived at the twelfth ruin.

This ruin was the largest in scale among all the ruins.

But strangely, there were no traces of bodies on the periphery.

When things are abnormal, they are bound to be demonic.

Li Fan braced himself and explored towards the center of the ruin.

In this realm that stood alone, even the demonic sounds from the depths could not reach.

Besides the sounds he caused, Li Fan hadn't heard any other noises for a long time.

However, when Li Fan approached the center of the twelfth ruin, the sudden sound of rustling made him break out in a cold sweat.

Through the inexplicably thinning white mist, Li Fan saw on the ground not far away.

A cultivator lying face up, but with limbs strangely bent downwards.

The hands and feet were irregularly broken.

Fingers and toes twisted together like twisted dough.

Using these rather creepy limbs, the cultivator crawled in circles laboriously.

Unrelenting.

For an unknown duration, it seemed like it would never stop.

Looking at the creature that had survived for who knows how long in the white mist, Li Fan's scalp tingled, and his heart almost stopped.

Holding his breath, he slowly retreated.

However, the perception of this twisted creature seemed extremely sharp.

It immediately sensed the arrival of a foreign visitor.

With a snap of its drooping head, it turned over onto its belly.

Then it turned directly 180 degrees, and with its face, it stared closely at Li Fan.

The facial features of the twisted creature in Li Fan's eyes immediately made his heart skip a beat.

The creature's facial features were completely inverted and disordered.

Two pairs of eyes were located where the mouth should be, constantly moving strangely on the face.

It had a nose embedded inside the skull.

The mouth, following the continuous positional changes of the eyes, kept opening and closing.

Inside were not tongues and teeth, but other faces covered in blood.

Although the twisted creature's body had become grotesque, its movements were exceptionally fast.

Its hands and feet continuously paddling, it crawled in front of Li Fan in no time.

Li Fan angrily shouted, "Die!"

The Great Five Elements Annihilation Sword kept coming out in succession.

As his figure retreated rapidly, the Annihilation Sword remained relentless.

However, to Li Fan's astonishment, the black sword light that fell on the creature surprisingly lost its effect of annihilation that it had always possessed.

Then, it rushed towards Li Fan even faster.

Even though Li Fan continuously unleashed hundreds of sword lights, the creature took them all head-on without showing any signs of injury.

"What the hell!"

Seeing the twisted creature about to pounce on him, Li Fan cursed inwardly and was about to launch another attack.

The next second, something unexpected happened.

The twisted creature approached Li Fan, but instead of attacking, it bowed down in front of him.

Continuously kowtowing.

While kowtowing, it emitted a weeping wail.

Tears of blood flowed from its constantly wandering eyes.

Its mouth also seemed eager to say something.

But all that came out were constantly changing, crying blood-stained faces, unable to express its inner thoughts.

"What's going on?"

Li Fan looked at the creature still wailing and bowing in front of him, his mind unsettled.

Having temporarily escaped danger, Li Fan halted the progress of his attack.
