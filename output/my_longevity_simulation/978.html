However, it would be a true pity to just watch it disappear right before his eyes.

Might as well give it a try!

What surprised Li Fan a little was that when Sima Changkong's transformed threads were about to approach, without deliberate manipulation, the stone tablet seemed to exert a pulling force.

Just like a ball of thread being unraveled in an instant, Sima Changkong's body completely dissipated.

And the flickering stone tablet also seemed to be tainted with a bit of dust.

Li Fan retreated slightly to a safe position with vigilant mind, carefully observing.

Fallen leaves return to their roots, ripe fruits fall to the ground. When the stone tablet was recreated by heaven and earth once again, there were no unusual phenomena appearing in the surroundings. The rustic, grayish-green stone tablet showed no traces of being an extraordinary treasure. It floated quietly in the air.

Perhaps it was the guidance of Sima Changkong's transformed threads, the Creation Stone Tablet did not go to other places.

It still remained here in the small mountain temple, seemingly indicating that Li Fan's attempt had succeeded.

But what Li Fan found a bit strange was that Sima Changkong did not appear.

He just disappeared like that, or perhaps...

"Merged with the Creation Stone Tablet?" Li Fan speculated.

Just as Li Fan was still secretly observing, new changes occurred on the Creation Stone Tablet.

Densely packed blood-red symbols suddenly emerged on the stone tablet, and then these symbols flowed like fresh blood, turning into thin streams.

Afterwards, the blood characters increased, and the thin streams gradually turned into surging rivers.

Covering the entire stone tablet.

A dazzling red light burst out in an instant. When Li Fan looked again, he found that the whole stone tablet was actually flipped up like a book page, hovering outside the stone tablet!

"This is..." Li Fan's pupils constricted.

The changes on the stone tablet continued.

Page after page of stone tablets filled with blood-colored symbols flipped like paper, faster and faster. The stone tablet itself also became thinner and thinner. Finally, when the complete transformation was finished.

A stone book emitting a faint blood-golden light appeared before Li Fan!

Compared to the previous Creation Stone Tablet, this stone book had a bit more of a sacred and ancient aura.

The anomalies gradually subsided, and the light emitted by the stone book slowly internalized.

Calmly suspended in the air.

"I'm afraid that from now on, there will no longer be the existence of the Sima family's 'Blood Lineage Book' in this world."

"Instead, it is the 'Creation History Book' before our eyes."

After observing for a long time, Li Fan had a subtle feeling in his heart.

"It seems that with Sima Changkong's strength, it is not enough to become a qualified vessel like Senior Stone Tablet. But by a twist of fate, the Blood Lineage Book within his body had a quite peculiar impact on the Creation Stone Tablet."

"Hmm, perhaps, this is still intentionally facilitated by the Dao of Heaven in the Xuanhuang Realm."
