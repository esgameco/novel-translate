Tianjian Sect, Sword Tomb.

Li Fan, who was concentrating on comprehending, suddenly woke up with a start.

It wasn't because of the crisis his avatar might face, but because he inexplicably couldn't comprehend anything from the [Sword] at all.

Li Fan looked around.

He found that it wasn't just him.

The thousands of remnant swords in the Sword Tomb also seemed to have lost their connection to the [Sword] at this moment.

The slightly bent sword bodies returned to being straight, looking utterly confused.

Li Fan stared intently at the wooden sword in front of him: "Is it its own change? Loss of spiritual nature?"

"No, this is temporary."

Very quickly, Li Fan noticed the clue.

He didn't act rashly, just carefully observed the wooden sword and patiently waited.

Sure enough, just a quarter of an hour later, the wooden sword once again regained its magnificence as a Heavenly Wonder.

Filled with the profound mystery of the sword, causing thousands of swords to bow their heads.

The question was...

What happened during that quarter of an hour?

Li Fan immediately became interested.

While comprehending the Dao of the Sword, he waited for the next "failure" event to occur.

After several consecutive days of observation, Li Fan also discovered the pattern in it.

Every period of time, the swords would regularly lose their spirituality for a quarter of an hour.

During this time, the thousands of remnant swords in the Sword Tomb also simultaneously lost their connection to the [Sword].

There was only one exception!

Thanks to the existence of the Dao Stone in his mind, Li Fan was able to completely record the reactions of each sword before and after, and then compare them one by one.

Thus discovering this peculiarity!

Locking onto the target, when the next "failure" event occurred, Li Fan leaped to its side.

It was an inconspicuous black sword covered in dust.

Plain and simple.

It had neither a sharp blade nor an intricate design.

It was like the first practice work of a blacksmith apprentice, looking so ordinary and even a bit ugly.

But it was this very sword that gained the favor of the wooden sword, the Heavenly Wonder.

If this scene of thousands of swords in the Sword Tomb paying homage was said to be the wooden sword giving a unified lesson.
