Chapter 1294

The atmosphere of the meeting reached its peak with the announcement of Gan Jingda's resolution.

A cultivation world that was still intact and rich in resources - something that originally only existed in the imagination of those on the celestial ship - now seemed to be within reach. How could this not make people excited?

Before the meeting, the vast majority of cultivators on the Xuan celestial ship only knew about the Xuanhuang world from the captured flying ship that came from there. After the meeting, they began frantically studying information related to the Xuanhuang world.

The Xuan celestial ship had a long heritage. Although it had experienced the tragedy of splitting into two, it had not been severed after all.

On the tenth day after the end of the council meeting, a detailed report on the Xuanhuang world was produced through the joint efforts of the断仙楼 (Duanxian Tower), 仙枢院 (Immortal Pivot Institute), 证道学宫 (Dao Verification Academy), and the major families of the celestial ship.

The content provided by the Duanxian Tower accounted for the vast majority of the report, sourced from the confessions of the captured Lan Yu of the Hedao Sect.

The content provided by the Immortal Pivot Institute and other organizations originated from the records left behind by the Xuan Merchant Celestial Ship's several visits to the Xuanhuang world before the Star Sea Tribulation struck.

At a small-scale council meeting among the high-level members of the Xuan Celestial Ship, this Xuanhuang report was distributed to everyone.

Although Li Fan's status was not high enough, he attended as Ai Tianjue's disciple.

"At present, we still cannot determine the extent to which the Star Sea anomaly will develop after three years. So whether we can actually launch an expedition to Xuanhuang still needs further observation. But that doesn't prevent us from making preparations first."

"Please take a look, everyone..."

Lu Yuzhi, one of the five [Rudao] cultivators of the Duanxian Tower, summarized for everyone.

"Our Xuan Merchant Celestial Ship visited the Xuanhuang world three times in ancient times. One of them was an official visit and was warmly received by Xuanhuang. The other two were secret visits to collect resources. At that time, Xuanhuang had two specialties that were priceless even in the entire Star Sea. One was the [Ruomu Fruit], and the other was [Immortal Realm Bestowed Artifacts]."

"This Ruomu Fruit contains the most primordial laws of life in Xuanhuang. After a cultivator consumes it, it can greatly increase their lifespan."

"As for these Immortal Realm Bestowed Artifacts..."

Lu Yuzhi paused for a moment, and Li Fan could hear a slight fluctuation in his tone.

"According to records, Xuanhuang was one of the few cultivation worlds that had the most frequent contact with the Immortal Realm in ancient times. Therefore, it had a considerable number of bestowed immortal artifacts remaining. Some of these artifacts gradually became ownerless as time passed. There were cultivators in the Star Sea who specialized in going to Xuanhuang to secretly collect information about these lost immortal artifacts, hoping to reap huge profits."

"Although the probability of success was extremely low, there were indeed a few successes. The two secret visits by our Xuan Celestial Ship were actually to rendezvous with those who succeeded."

Lu Yuzhi's words immediately caused a commotion among those present.

Even as high-level members of the celestial ship, some of them had never heard of such secrets.

"Of the two artifacts we collected from Xuanhuang, one was sold by us before the Star Sea Tribulation descended. The other fell into the hands of the [Shang Celestial Ship] during the celestial ship split that year." Lu Yuzhi said with great regret.

Everyone held their breath, their faces showing indignant expressions.

Gan Jingda, the second elder of Duanxian Tower, lightly coughed to suppress the whispers and gestured for Lu Yuzhi to continue.

"Although the immortal artifacts that flowed out of Xuanhuang are no longer in our hands, our celestial ship still has [Ruomu Fruits] remaining."

After seeking permission, the representative from the Immortal Pivot Institute made several hand seals.

Amidst the dazzling light, a crystal clear, jade-like round fruit appeared before everyone's eyes.

Even under layers of restrictive seals, the cultivators present could still feel the surging vitality emanating from this Ruomu Fruit.

For the celestial ship cultivators who were very short on resources, this was undoubtedly a huge temptation. Even though they were all high-level members, none of them had enjoyed such a treasure.

The vast majority stared intently at the Ruomu Fruit, swallowing their saliva.

Li Fan also carefully observed.

"It indeed comes from a Ruomu tree. But the vigorous vitality permeating the fruit is not something the current Ruomu tree can produce. It seems the injuries the Ruomu tree suffered back then were really severe."

"Also..."

Li Fan's heart stirred. He noticed that strands of vitality seemed to emanate from the Ruomu Fruit and disappear into thin air. Or rather, flow into the void.

Lu Yuzhi said solemnly, "You should all understand that the vitality of this Ruomu Fruit has been dissipating for tens of thousands of years. Although our celestial ship has used many secret techniques over the generations to greatly slow down the rate of vitality dissipation, we ultimately cannot stop this process."

Someone in the audience exclaimed in amazement, "The vitality it contains is so powerful even after dissipating for tens of thousands of years. It's hard to imagine what the original Ruomu Fruit was like."

"No wonder it's said to be a priceless treasure in the entire Star Sea."

"Hmm, strange. How could those old fellows in the Immortal Pivot Institute restrain themselves from directly swallowing such a treasure?"

Amidst the discussions, someone grasped the key point of Lu Yuzhi's introduction of this Ruomu Fruit.

"Brother Lu means that the vitality dissipating from this Ruomu Fruit is returning to the Xuanhuang world?"

Upon hearing this, everyone quieted down. In addition to greed and desire, their eyes looking at the Ruomu Fruit contained a different meaning.

Lu Yuzhi nodded, "Correct!"

"The Star Sea is vast and boundless. Even that Lan Yu of Xuanhuang we captured can't remember the way back. But through this Ruomu Fruit, we can accurately locate the position of the Xuanhuang world in the vast Star Sea!"

After a moment of silence, intense discussions erupted again.

"Does the Xuanhuang world still have Ruomu Fruits now? If we each had one, that feeling would be unimaginable..."

"No wonder those fellows in the Immortal Pivot Institute didn't consume it. Although the treasure is good, it's inextricably linked to the fundamental power of another cultivation world. It's fine for other cultivators, but as the rulers of the celestial ship, they absolutely cannot do so. Unless they are willing to give up their positions."

"Wait, are there such coincidences in the world? Also, the way the fruit's vitality drifts towards its original world seems a bit too deliberate. Could it be that this fruit is actually bait to lure beings in the Star Sea over to be hunted?"

...

The crowd argued endlessly. Lu Yuzhi, the host, did not stop them.

Instead, he closed his eyes in contemplation.

It was only after the venue became quiet again that Lu Yuzhi continued to summarize, "From Lan Yu's memories, we have ruled out the possibility of this being a trap. Even if the Ruomu Fruit conspiracy really existed in ancient times, there is no need to worry about the current Xuanhuang world. Although Xuanhuang survived the Star Sea Tribulation, it also experienced unimaginable changes."

"The current Xuanhuang world is under the joint rule of the Wanxian Alliance and the Five Elders Council..."

Lu Yuzhi introduced the relevant information about the Xuanhuang world to everyone in detail.

"That's not right. How come the scale of this Xuanhuang world is much larger than what was recorded in our Xuan Celestial Ship?"

"The initial judgment is that it should be the joint efforts of Star Sea survivors to resist the attraction of the Immortal Ruins. The so-called Xuanhuang world would be more appropriately called the [Land of Survivors from Various Worlds]."

"Defying the laws of heaven and earth to achieve growth and life... it's indeed a bit interesting. Binding with the world can indeed obtain a lifespan that ordinary cultivators can hardly reach. One can even synchronously amplify their own strength as the world prospers. It's just a pity that once outside the world, the strength is greatly weakened. And when the world is destroyed, one can only perish along with it."

Everyone solemnly reviewed the information about the Xuanhuang world.

"Let's not forget that there are many immortal artifacts bestowed by the Immortal Realm in the Xuanhuang world. This Tianxuan Mirror of the Wanxian Alliance is obviously one of them. Are there other immortal artifacts in the Wanxian Alliance? If we go on an expedition, we must take this factor into consideration. I believe everyone understands how powerful the strength of true immortals is. Can we really win that easily?"

"In my opinion, there's no need to fight. From Lan Yu's memories, it can be seen that the resources in the current Xuanhuang world are still quite abundant. Our celestial ship, at most, is equivalent to less than half a state of the Wanxian Alliance. We can very well use the method of trade to exchange for what we need."

"That's right. Even if we can't occupy the Xuanhuang world, exploring the so-called Immortal Ruins will also greatly benefit us. Such surging true immortal power... maybe there will be complete [True Immortal Seal Characters] inside!"

"Sigh, I advise everyone to be more cautious. We covet the various resources of the Xuanhuang world, but the Xuanhuang world is also very eager to get rid of the shackles of the Immortal Ruins, right? Our Xuan Celestial Ship, in their eyes, is also a piece of fat meat, isn't it?"

...

Whether to go to the distant Xuanhuang world was definitely something that could completely change the fate of the celestial ship. It was really difficult to make a decision in a short time. And because of historical reasons, everyone present tacitly did not propose to allocate a portion of their forces as a probe.

The Xuan Celestial Ship, no matter what, must advance and retreat together. It could no longer withstand a second split.

The council decided to discuss again in thirty days to make a final decision.

After everyone left one after another, only nine cultivators remained.

Four from Duanxian Tower, three from Immortal Pivot Institute, one from Dao Verification Academy, and one from the Celestial Ship Forging Star Workshop.

Li Fan, as a personal disciple, did not have the qualifications to be present and waited outside.

Gan Jingda lightly pointed his finger, and a scene immediately appeared in front of everyone.

A sword light split the sky.

Sixteen characters glowed brightly in the sky.

On the other side of the Star Sea, below the high walls, the Xuan Merchant Celestial Ship, ferrying through the tribulation.

Gan Jingda slowly said, "What do you all think?"

"This transmission method... [Boundless]?" Nan Gong Lie from the Dao Verification Academy had a solemn expression.

"There are traitors on the celestial ship?"

"Not necessarily. It could also be that group of people who defected back then. Wanting to bring calamity from afar? And then reap the benefits of the fisherman?"

"Anyway, in the face of absolute strength, any conspiracies and schemes are just illusions. The only thing to consider is whether it's worth it or not."

...

The few of them discussed the future of the Xuan Celestial Ship, each expressing their opinions.

He looked at the buildings located in various parts of the celestial ship's divine tree below, but his thoughts were focused on the Ruomu Fruit that had appeared briefly at the council meeting.

"The vitality of the Ruomu Fruit is indeed dissipating and drifting towards the Xuanhuang world. But it's not through ordinary means, but involves the level of the Star Sea itself."

"Wanting to use its vitality to lock onto the location of the Xuanhuang world... is simply impossible."

"They are lying. They should be relying on other methods."

Several possibilities flashed through Li Fan's mind, but at present, he had not yet touched the true core of the celestial ship's high level.

He didn't have the answer to lock onto the correct one.

Just as he was pondering, a transmission interrupted his thoughts.

"Brother Zhao, come quickly!"

It was a voice transmission from Wang Hai, a fellow disciple under Ai Tianjue.

Under normal circumstances, Wang Hai would almost never take the initiative to find Li Fan. This time it was so urgent, so it must be something important.

Li Fan also stopped waiting for Ai Tianjue and directly returned to Wang Hai's residence.

"Brother Zhao, take a look at this."

"How could the celestial ship's report have such a big oversight?"

Although Wang Hai did not attend this meeting, he had already obtained the celestial ship's numbers.
