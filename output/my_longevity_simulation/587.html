Li Fan clearly saw the fleeting twisted and agonizing faces on the leaves' surfaces.

According to common sense, anyone seeing such a bizarre scene would inevitably feel horrified.

But at this moment, in Li Fan's eyes, this place called "Life-Burning Land" was indescribably beautiful.

Taking a deep breath, the foul-smelling air entered his lungs, strangely feeling somewhat sweet.

So he greedily took a few more breaths.

Carefully observing the monks on the trees, he found that they all had pitch-black eyes, indiscernible facial features, and wore eerie smiles.

How about it, fellow Daoist, wasn't what I said true? The Old Decaying Wood seemed to be getting weaker, coughing continuously for a long time before finally recovering to ask.

Indeed, fellow Daoist's words are true! Li Fan nodded in agreement.

Truly a great creation! I wonder, what should this tree be called? He gazed at the withered giant tree with a hint of fascination in his eyes.

Originally called "Ruo Wood", but now… The Old Decaying Wood also raised its head, with an inexplicable expression on its face.

It's called "Genhuan Huanheng Yuan."

It was an extremely tongue-twisting term, but the moment Old Decaying Wood mentioned it, Li Fan understood its significance.

From ancient times, it had already appeared and would continue to exist eternally. Standing above the River of Life like a wall supporting the sky.

No, I still prefer its original name. Li Fan shook his head.

The Old Decaying Wood let out a dark chuckle, indifferent: A name is just a code. As time passes, successive generations of beings change, and the names they are called by also keep changing.

But it won't have any impact on its existence.

For some reason, as he spoke this sentence, a hint of cloudiness seemed to pass through the Old Decaying Wood's eyes.

Thus, Li Fan naturally knew a lot of relevant information.

He showed a hint of nostalgia and recited to himself: "Born beings of the heavens and earth, Ruo Wood shortens their lives. Absorb life, produce the elixir of life, those who take it can live long."

In the Old Decaying Wood's cloudy eyes, upon hearing the verse from Li Fan's mouth, they also faintly glimmered.

After remaining silent for a long time, the two men resumed their conversation.

Fellow Daoist, you are indeed a person of great wisdom! The Old Decaying Wood sighed heavily.

There have been thousands of practitioners who visited here in the past, yet it's very rare to see someone like you with such rapid changes in thought.

Li Fan squinted his eyes and spoke solemnly:

It's not that I have wisdom, but they are inherently ignorant.

What essential difference is there between the practitioners here and other beings?

Trees bear fruit, flowers produce nectar. Beasts give birth, birds lay eggs.

People take from these, thinking it's ordinary. Why is it hard to accept when one is taken by others?

Do they truly think they are the spirits of heaven and earth, the masters of all things?

Li Fan snorted.

The Old Decaying Wood was caught off guard as Li Fan seemed to speak out what he had prepared to say, feeling somewhat dumbfounded for a moment.

I see it as nothing but "survival of the fittest."

Now, swallowing, one feels comfortable. One day being eaten by others, there should be no complaints. Heaven's justice is good, and retribution is unshakable!

Li Fan shook his head and immersed himself.

The Old Decaying Wood stared at Li Fan for a while, suddenly understanding something.

What a pity, what a pity!

The Old Decaying Wood shook his head slightly.

But Li Fan ignored him as if he hadn't heard the old man's words.

Still, his gaze was fixed on the withered Ruo Wood before him, as if there was something deeply attracting him.

But to unexpectedly have such anomalies among the cultivators… The Old Decaying Wood frowned slightly, as if pondering something.

Cough…

He suddenly burst into a violent cough, faintly showing green blood flowing from the corners of his mouth.

The Ruo Wood above his head began to move without any wind, and numerous dry leaves fell along with it.

So good to die, so good to die!

Seeing something, the Old Decaying Wood's face suddenly revealed a fierce look of madness.

He then burst into wild laughter: Indeed, heaven's justice is good, and evildoers have trials within trials!

This kid staying with me is pointless. It's better to send him back to cause trouble!

Retribution, everything is retribution!

…

After being frenzied for a long time, the Old Decaying Wood gradually regained calm.

Returning to being just like when he met Li Fan outside.

Fellow Daoist, now that you have seen it in person, you must have some idea of our product's quality.

Can we continue the previous transaction? He smirked and asked.

Transaction? Li Fan turned his head, feeling a moment of confusion.

Oh, that thing! No rush! He waved his hand, surprisingly rejecting the proposal.

Hehe… The Old Decaying Wood let out an unpleasant laugh: Although this place is good, it's not where you should stay.

The Xuanhuang Realm outside seems more suitable for you.

Li Fan was about to retort but was grabbed by the Old Decaying Wood's shoulder.

Rolling gray mist surged from the ground, about to envelop him once again.

Why are you so eager to make me leave, fellow Daoist? Li Fan asked anxiously.

The Old Decaying Wood smiled sardonically, not answering.

You want the Golden Elixir, right? Well, since you've been a good person, I'll just give you some for free this time!

The vision was completely obscured by the gray mist, and Li Fan vaguely heard the Old Decaying Wood saying such things in his ear.

With a sudden flash before his eyes, Li Fan stumbled and found himself back in the secondary courtyard of the Tianlingzhou Discussion Hall.

The Old Decaying Wood, the "Genhuan Huanheng Yuan," and the countless hanging monks had all disappeared in an instant.

Everything seemed as if it was just a hallucination for Li Fan.

But the dry yellow tree branch quietly placed on the table was proof of something.

Without the influence of the Ruo Wood, Li Fan had regained his normal thoughts.

Recalling his recent experience, he felt a chill.

Just fearing that the Old Decaying Wood might still be spying from the side, he forcibly suppressed his fear, pretending to be calm.
