Chapter 1311

"When truth becomes falsehood, falsehood becomes truth."

"The transformations between truth and falsehood are endlessly profound."

Li Fan once again recited in his heart, unable to contain his joy.

Although it only lasted for a moment, he had indeed "transformed" his Hua Shen cultivation from his previous life out of thin air.

However, it was after all his first attempt at this great divine ability.

After a fleeting moment of success, this transformed cultivation disappeared like a dream or bubble, vanishing in an instant.

Li Fan was not discouraged by this, still overjoyed.

Creating something out of the void, even legendary true immortals may not be capable of it.

But he achieved it preliminarily by relying on the insights brought by multiple Huan Zhen simulations.

As the saying goes, all things are difficult before they are easy. With this first success, he would surely become more and more familiar with the use of this divine ability in the future.

"Not only my own cultivation. The things I experienced and possessed, and even..."

"Different individuals, the Xuan Huang realm, the Abyssal Starry Sea, and everything beyond the high walls. One day, I can recreate them all with a single thought!"

Li Fan stood atop the sea of clouds, savoring the feeling when he used the True-False Transformation, his heart surging with excitement.

After a long while, he finally came back to his senses from the epiphany.

Looking at the inheritance options on the Huan Zhen interface, he first chose to inherit the cultivation.

The same scene played out again, with the Great Xuan Heavens rumbling incessantly.

This was the disturbance caused by Li Fan's Hua Shen cultivation being invisibly infused into the small world.

However, compared to the scene of him inheriting cultivation in his previous life, the Great Xuan small world in this life, due to the world transformation triggered by the Zao Hua True Immortal seal script earlier, did not show signs of being overwhelmed.

There were only dark clouds gathering and strange phenomena occurring.

"Phew..." When the familiar power of cultivation returned to his body, Li Fan let out a long sigh.

Next, his gaze locked onto the second inheritance choice.

The inherit cultivation option had already dimmed, leaving only options 1, 3, and 4.

Without hesitation, Li Fan chose to inherit items.

The items he possessed in his previous life's experiences, including his divine clones and avatars, all instantly appeared in Li Fan's mind.

Waiting for him to quietly select.

His gaze swept over slightly, pondering for a moment, and finally landed on an ordinary-looking white ring.

This white ring, before being fished up, the Holy Emperor had not completely severed from Li Fan's true body, so it was also considered an item he once possessed.

After confirming the selection, light flashed in his hand, finally condensing into a plain white ring.

It felt slightly cool to the touch. Sweeping over it with divine sense, no spiritual power fluctuations could be detected.

It seemed that because it was transformed from Huan Zhen, there was no need for refining to acknowledge its master. The moment Li Fan held the white ring, he already had the feeling of being able to freely use it.

Li Fan looked at the name displayed for this white ring on the Huan Zhen interface.

"Luo Fan Chen." (Falling into the Mortal Dust)

Hand caressing the white ring, Li Fan muttered these three words, while pondering the correct way to use the white ring in his heart.

"Go!"

Li Fan tossed the white ring towards Mount Jie Li below.

With a thought, the white ring grew in the wind, instantly enveloping the entire mountain peak.

Then it fell downwards.

As the white ring landed, the mountain that it encircled along the way disintegrated and disappeared inch by inch before Li Fan's eyes.

Transforming into spiritual energy, dissipating into the Great Xuan world.

The several thousand zhang tall Mount Jie Li disappeared just like that. The countless creatures residing on the mountain, due to the sudden disappearance of the mountain, fell uncontrollably from the sky. If it weren't for the spiritual energy rising from the white ring's disintegration of Mount Jie Li holding them in place, perhaps in an instant Mount Jie Li would have already become a hell.

Now this area could perhaps be called the Jie Li Ring.

The earth looked as if it had been sliced by a knife, smooth as a mirror.

The creatures of Mount Jie Li who lost their habitat frantically fled and left due to instinctive fear.

Before long, the originally lively Mount Jie Li had already become a forbidden zone for life.

Li Fan took a deep breath, absorbing all the disintegrated spiritual energy into his body.

This bit of spiritual energy, for him now, was not even a drop in the bucket. It was mainly to analyze the spiritual energy of the Luo Fan Chen white ring to see what differences it had compared to ordinary spiritual energy between heaven and earth.

After carefully savoring it for a while, Li Fan came to a conclusion.

There was no difference at all.

This spiritual energy even gave Li Fan an extremely familiar feeling, as if it was originally meant to be born in the Great Xuan small world.

"Clearly it's the energy from matter disintegration, but on the Huan Zhen interface, it has a name like Luo Fan Chen (Falling into the Mortal Dust)."

"Luo Fan Chen..."

"Perhaps, this should be a punitive treasure specifically for immortal weapons." Li Fan suddenly had an epiphany in his heart.

"In my previous life, the Holy Emperor's avatar didn't have a chance to test it more. In this life, perhaps I can use the few near-immortal weapons of the Xuan Huang realm to verify my guess."

Putting away the white ring, Li Fan looked downwards.

For ordinary mortals, this was definitely an unimaginably terrifying event.

Believing it to be a punishment sent down from the heavens, the mortals below Mount Jie Li had already spontaneously begun to organize sacrifices in trepidation.

Not daring to approach that huge ring on the ground, people only dared to pray outside the ring.

Unsurprisingly, Li Fan saw Sun Er Lang and Wang Xuan Ba, his former disciples, among this group of people.

With a flick of his finger, a figure floated down from the sky, descending to the mortal world.

Its appearance was 70% similar to Li Fan's. However, with a long beard and white hair, its attire was somewhat similar to the Dharma Heavenly Venerate's.

"Quick, look, an immortal!"

The commoners all knelt on the ground.

The figure waved his hand, and the kneeling mortals stood up again.

The figure sat cross-legged in the air and began to preach to the surrounding mortals.

The wisps of spiritual energy born from the world transformation in the Great Xuan small world were all attracted here by the preaching figure.

Forming a visible vortex above the Jie Li Ring.

Li Fan used this spiritual energy to casually set up an array. Before long, a miniature dharma array for expelling miasma from the bodies of mortals was formed.

Under the dual effects of the figure's preaching and the array.

In just the time to drink a cup of tea, among the mortals listening to the teachings below, there were already those who completed the expulsion of miasma.

Sun Er Lang and Wang Xuan Ba were naturally among them.

Inside the Jie Li Ring, the earth rumbled, and the positions of these creatures who completed the first step of cultivation flowed like water.

After gathering them in one place, it slowly rose and bulged.

Thus, these people were a bit closer to the immortal master preaching above.

The figure preached for a full seven days.

Within the seven days, the positions of those who successfully guided qi into their bodies and reached the Qi Refining stage continued to rise.

Forming distinct stair-like platforms.

After seven days, the figure transformed into a soaring light and flew away. At the same time, a thought flashed in the minds of all the cultivators present.

The immortal master's preaching would continue in a month.
