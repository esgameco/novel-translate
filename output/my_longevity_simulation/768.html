"From the mirror space of the Heavenly Mystery, the Deng Mingxi that was seen had the appearance of only 13 or 14 years old. This should be her actual age."

"Though young in years, she must have been well protected by her clan members. Thus, despite being able to read minds, she currently lacks much guile," Li Fan pondered inwardly.

"She only has the Golden Dan cultivation level, yet can sense my thoughts..."

"It should not be due to her cultivation method. Perhaps it has to do with the strange object she used to establish her foundation. The ability to read minds is already rare in the Xuanhuang Realm. To be able to transcend cultivation realm disparities to this degree, it may already be considered a semi-heavenly wonder." A strange light flashed in Li Fan's eyes.

In his cycles through the worlds, apart from the heavenly wonders of the "Cultivation Bead" and the "Sword", Li Fan had also encountered many cultivators possessing semi-heavenly wonders.

There was He Zhengdaughter's "Dao Compass", the Celestial Maiden of the Void's "Yin-Yang Jade", and Sun Qi's "Unity of Qian and Kun".

But none had stirred Li Fan's heart like Deng Mingxi's mind-reading strange object.

As the saying goes, the human heart is inscrutable. This was even more so in the cultivation world where methods could not be practiced together. No one could guarantee that a so-called cultivation brother walking beside you was not actually harboring malicious intentions.

If one could master the ability to read minds, even if only for a few critical moments, it could greatly reduce the various risks encountered in the cultivation journey.

Not to mention, it could also serve as a way to gauge one's strength to some degree. If one suddenly lost the ability to read minds, it meant the other party was someone not to be provoked.

"Perhaps I could even deduce a way to prevent my thoughts from being read by others."

With his strong determination, Li Fan immediately returned to the Heavenly Mystery Mirror in Heavenly Rights City to inquire about information related to Deng Mingxi.

Unexpectedly, he could not find any valuable information about her.

It was as if Deng Mingxi had appeared out of nowhere.

Li Fan changed his approach and instead searched for the "Elder Brother Qingyu" that Deng Mingxi had mentioned, along with the arrogant and vengeful personality described by Wang Yuan.

He almost immediately got a match.

However, Deng Qingyu's backing was not from the Deng family, but rather the Heavenly Rights Royal Family.

"So he's an adopted son. But the Royal Family treats him as their own, even better than their direct descendants."

"It's probably because of the grace of his sister," Li Fan nodded to himself.

Although the Heavenly Rights Royal Family could not be considered top-tier nobility in the immortal sects, ordinary people still could not afford to offend them. Many of the family's cultivators served in the mysterious Guardian Hall.

It was said that the head of the Royal Family was even entrusted with safeguarding the "Cultivation Bead"...

The moment he saw this information, Li Fan had already made up his mind.

He vowed to become friends with this Royal Family for generations to come.

However, after searching for more information, Li Fan's brow furrowed slightly.

It turned out that not only Deng Qingyu had a bad reputation, but the entire younger generation of the Royal Family, including the elders, had no good reputation.

In the entire Immortal Alliance, there was not a single cultivator willing to speak up for them. Whenever the Heavenly Rights Royal Family was mentioned, people would spit in disdain.

"Is it to avoid suspicion?"

Li Fan, who had experienced the official circles before, was not unfamiliar with such tactics.

"Although there are no reckless actions, all my movements and plans seem to still be under my control. But in this world, I inexplicably feel a sense of difficulty in taking every step."

"Let's take it step by step, first completely master the 'Wuji Yingxu Technique'."

Li Fan closed his eyes, and the blue crystal in his mind, as if overstuffed, struggled to emit a faint glow, slowly analyzing and processing the data of the divine abilities.

Xu Bai suddenly received a message from Liu San.

"What?" Xu Bai was stunned for a moment.

What is Liu San...

After a while, Liu San's next words slowly sounded.

"But it's not the 'Establish Foundation with Me' technique, but a young man named Jie Lin, who felt that this foundation establishment method was too difficult. He unexpectedly self-taught a method to establish his foundation using other things in heaven and earth."

"At first, I didn't pay much attention. In the blink of an eye, he successfully established his foundation using a thousand-year-old Fulong Grass from the Longevity Valley."

"Hehe, in that case, this Jie Lin can indeed be considered an extraordinary talent. Since he has already successfully established his foundation, there is no need to force him further. Just take him out separately and prevent the true foundation establishment method from spreading among the disciples."
