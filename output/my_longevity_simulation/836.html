Title: The Capture of the Mostone

With the construction of the Multiverse Alliance headquarters under the direction of Oudao Zi, things were gradually getting on track. The continuous feedback of cultivation from the Likai and Congyun Sea Mutual Aid Association had also allowed Li Fan to successfully break through his realm.

For some reason, he did not encounter the restraint of the Heavenly Tribulation this time. He quickly returned to the late Golden Core stage. Based on the current accelerating trend, it was believed that the distance to perfection was not far away.

"It's time to consider the matter of merging with Tianyang," Li Fan thought.

"However..."

Li Fan took out a [Fruit of True Temptation] and pondered for a moment. In the end, he still summoned Tianyang and let him eat the fruit. Tianyang didn't ask why and swallowed the crystal clear Fruit of True Temptation without any hesitation.

"Good, there's no time to waste. Let's set off now. This time, I have prepared a big gift for you!" Only then did Li Fan put his mind at ease. Looking at Tianyang, he said with some gratification.

Tianyang tilted his head, seeming not to quite understand the meaning behind Li Fan's words. Li Fan didn't mind. He still commanded Lin Ling, left the Lingmu Realm, and headed straight for the destination.

This time, Li Fan's target was the Worldsoul [Mostone]. Being able to share the same prefix as Mosha, Mostone's strength was beyond doubt. The current Mostone had just been born not long ago. Hidden underground in Qiling State, it was not yet known to the world.

It was thanks to Mr. Bai that Li Fan was able to know about the existence of Mostone at this point in time. If Mosha's heavenly duty was to exterminate everything, then Mostone's mission was to fill the void and cavities brought about by the destruction.

Deep underground in Qiling State, some terrible catastrophe had occurred, causing large areas of void cavities to appear. It was as if the depths of the earth had been continuously gnawed by insects, already riddled with holes. If there was no external intervention, it would not be long before Qiling State would be destroyed in a shocking collapse.

The birth of Mostone was to prevent this disaster from happening. Its ability was to generate black, stone-like hard substances to fill the void cavities and repair the gnawed and destroyed space.

But if Mostone's ability was limited to this, Li Fan would not have set his sights on it. In addition to "repairing", Mostone had another absolutely powerful ability - "space shattering".

The so-called "Mostone" did not refer to stones, but a kind of power that could affect and transform space. This was only a part of the information synchronized from Mr. Bai's memory. It was believed that when Tianyang truly refined and integrated with Mostone, he would be able to awaken more abilities.

As for how Li Fan would deal with such a powerful Worldsoul... Before the group traveled through the earth veins and quickly arrived underground in Qiling State, Li Fan had already conducted a simulated deduction in his mind.

Although the Dao-Transforming Stone in his mind was only of medium grade, Li Fan believed that as long as there were no more variables involved, this hunt should be under his control from start to finish.

When the three emerged from the surging earth veins, they also saw the shocking endless cavities ahead. Intermittently, it looked like the traces left by a twisted long worm drilling and destroying.
