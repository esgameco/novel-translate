Chapter 1278: Seeking Survival Amidst Apocalyptic Destruction

"The fear is that the remains of that true immortal will come back to life."

As Mu Rubin uttered these words, Li Ping wasn't sure if it was just his illusion, but the depths of this dark sea seemed to have turned even colder.

It was as if some unknown existence had suddenly set its sights on him, causing a chill to rise in his heart.

The Holy Emperor solemnly surveyed his surroundings but did not detect any lurking presences.

Looking again at the husk of Fang Dingge from the Great Dao Sect, Li Ping asked, "Having already turned into this state, can it still come back to life?"

Li Ping was naturally not asking about Fang Dingge, but rather that true immortal whose body had transformed into the dark sea and whose consciousness had become the Fallen Immortal Realm.

Mu Rubin also examined the nearby corpse and said softly, "Let me tell you a story."

"Our Nine Refinements Sect once enshrined a statue of our patriarch. It's said that it was personally crafted by the patriarch of the Nine Refinements, the Wondrous Dharma Monk, for himself."

"This statue was worshipped for thousands of years. Whenever disciples of the sect had any doubts on their cultivation path, as long as they prayed to it sincerely, the vast majority would receive a response from the mysterious void. Therefore, incense offerings never ceased over the many years."

"Later, one day, tribulation clouds suddenly enveloped the sky above the Nine Refinements Sect, and bolts of divine lightning struck down, targeting that patriarch statue! A human figure broke out of the statue and shot straight into the ninth heaven! This person's strength was so terrifyingly powerful that the ninth heaven god lightning capable of instantly annihilating Harmonized Dao cultivators was completely ineffective against him. He laughed incessantly amidst the lightning, looking like he would overcome the tribulation and ascend at any moment..."

Mu Rubin paused again at the critical point.

After waiting for a long time without the Holy Emperor urging him on, he could only hold his breath and continue, "The patriarch statue fragments that were originally scattered on the ground actually quietly gathered together again. They approached the tribulation transcender. Taking advantage of his lack of vigilance, it swallowed him back into the statue!"

"Now, no matter how much that figure struggled, he could no longer escape from the statue's encasement. The statue also broke through the void and ascended together with that figure!"

After hearing this somewhat mysterious story, Li Ping couldn't help frowning slightly.

Mu Rubin asked, "Guess which of the two who ascended together is our Nine Refinements Sect's patriarch?"

Li Ping pondered for a moment before answering, "The figure inside the statue needs to face the ascension tribulation. It should be him, right? Unable to ascend in his lifetime, he took the form of a stone statue and was worshipped by disciples, accumulating power for thousands of years in an attempt to ascend..."

Mu Rubin smiled slightly, "Wrong!"

"The patriarch of our Nine Refinements Sect, the Wondrous Dharma Monk, has clear records of ascending to immortality in front of countless eyes. However, what only the successive sect masters of the Nine Refinements know is that the Wondrous Dharma Monk fell for unknown reasons in the second year after entering the immortal realm!"

Li Ping was stunned upon hearing this.

Mu Rubin continued with satisfaction, "However, that statue he personally crafted contained his contingency. Even if his divine soul was completely destroyed in the upper realm, he could still be reborn using this statue!"

Li Ping frowned again, "Then who was the figure inside the statue?"

Mu Rubin chuckled, "It was the third generation sect master of our Nine Refinements Sect. This person did not follow the patriarch's instructions and wanted to establish his own sect rules. After a round of internal strife, he was seriously injured in defeat and disappeared without a trace. Who would have thought he had been hiding inside the patriarch statue and actually managed to cultivate all the way to the ascension realm in one go."

"But in the end, he still ended up as someone else's stepping stone."

"The Wondrous Dharma Monk had only ascended for a year and didn't have much foundation, yet he could still be reborn using the stone statue. Not to mention that true immortal."

"Just look at this dark sea and you can imagine how powerful He was in life. To put it bluntly, don't use the perspective of us cultivators to speculate about the means of an immortal. They are truly worlds apart!"

Perhaps because they had communicated for some time, Mu Rubin's emotions had stabilized a lot. He spoke with deep feeling.

Li Ping remained silent.

He felt there were still some inconsistencies in what the other party said about the Wondrous Dharma Monk being reborn using the statue and the entanglements with the third generation Nine Refinements sect master.

But after all, these were ancient secrets. At this moment, having ventured deep into danger, it was not the time to investigate thoroughly.

"Let's go in and take a look first. If the true immortal has already resurrected, there's no way He would still remain in the original spot after so many years." As Mu Rubin spoke, he circled around Fang Dingge's remains and continued diving into the darkness.

They passed through three formation barriers, some originally part of the Spirit Gathering Ascension Formation and others set up by later arrivals.

With the combined efforts of Li Ping and Mu Rubin, they finally reached the control center of the Spirit Gathering Ascension Formation after some effort.

Along the way, they did not find the bones of the other four fallen Eternal Life realm experts.

The control center, clearly located in the deepest part of the entire dark expanse, was like a transparent observation deck.

Through the formation barrier entities surrounding this place on all sides, resembling panoramic glass, Li Ping and Mu Rubin could clearly see the full picture of the dark sea.

Even though it had been magnified countless times, they could still clearly see the human-like outline of the dark sea.

Mu Rubin had obviously been here before and was not distracted by the magnificent sight before him. Instead, he came to the center of the observation deck.

His expression changed slightly.

"One piece of good news and one piece of bad news. Which do you want to hear first?" Mu Rubin asked.

After a moment of silence, Li Ping still responded, "Let's start with the good news."

"The good news is that the fallen true immortal did not resurrect using the remains," Mu Rubin said in a dark tone.

"Then what's the bad news?"

Mu Rubin sneered, "You'll know when you come and look."

Li Ping walked slowly to where Mu Rubin was standing.

The scene before his eyes suddenly changed, as if the lofty observation deck was rapidly falling.

The view of the dark sea below quickly approached and continuously enlarged.

It abruptly filled his vision, reaching deep into his divine soul.

Boom!

The surrounding scenery changed, as if they had already left the dark sea and arrived in a completely unknown new place.

"Don't move."

Mu Rubin's voice resounded in his mind. Feeling slightly reassured, Li Ping surveyed his surroundings.

He saw the other four fallen Eternal Life realm experts.

They maintained the postures of their final moments in life, but unlike Fang Dingge, their faces were covered in a layer of gray, making it impossible to see their specific features.

Li Ping tried to search for the culprit responsible for the demise of these five Eternal Life realm experts.

The gray vine-like substances extending from beneath the four Eternal Life realm corpses, intertwining and entangling together, caught Li Ping's attention.

In Li Ping's observation, these gray vines absolutely had no life aura and were purely inanimate objects formed from energy condensation.

But for some reason, when facing them, Li Ping felt a sense of being targeted by a venomous snake.

"What is this?" Li Ping had no psychological barriers about asking when he didn't understand.

"This is the 'will to survive.' The will to survive of the true immortal's remains."

Mu Rubin took a good while before slowly answering Li Ping's question.

"Will to survive?" Li Ping carefully pondered the meaning of these four words.

"True immortals cannot be judged by common sense. When Brother Xuanyuan was modifying the true immortal's remains back then, he had concerns in this regard. In order to prevent Him from resurrecting, he made many additional arrangements. But who would have thought..."

Although Li Ping couldn't see Mu Rubin's figure, he could sense him staring intently at the gray "will to survive" spread throughout this place.

"Logically speaking, the true immortal's remains that have already turned into a dark sea shouldn't be able to generate such a strong will to survive."

Hearing Mu Rubin's words, Li Ping also became pensive, "Unless someone deliberately triggered it."

"Indeed. This person is no ordinary figure." Mu Rubin evaluated as such.

"Firstly, being able to break Brother Xuanyuan's restrictions is no simple feat. Secondly, being able to trigger the will of the true immortal's remains is also remarkable. Thirdly, being able to escape unscathed after triggering the will to survive... impressive." Mu Rubin praised without any hint of exaggeration in his tone.

"Don't underestimate these things. Even if it were the two of us, if we're not careful and get targeted by them, I'm afraid it would be difficult to escape death. We would end up like these five empty shells."

Li Ping frowned slightly. He still had some confidence in his own strength.

Mu Rubin didn't argue with Li Ping but continued to explain in a low voice, "When the will to survive of someone who has already died is reignited, it's like grasping the last lifeline, and the power that erupts..."

"Instinctively devours all the life it can come into contact with, converting it into nourishment for its own resurrection. Only those with strength like Brother Xuanyuan's can barely resist. Although you and I may appear strong, we still fall far short."

Li Ping fell silent.

Mu Rubin continued, "Fortunately, the person who triggered this back then didn't destroy Brother Xuanyuan's arrangements. After this 'will to survive' drained these four Eternal Life realm experts, it could no longer find any targets to absorb and thus fell into silence again."

"If it were to escape the dark sea..."

"I'm afraid the entire Xuanhuang Realm would become its prey."

Li Ping instantly became extremely solemn.

"But then again, Brother Xuanyuan's methods are not so easily broken. This triggering person, who you call the Dharma Transmitter, also didn't directly destroy the dark sea's restrictions. He didn't even change them and added his own techniques on top."

"Let's get to work first." Just when Li Ping was worried that Mu Rubin would become frenzied and out of control, Mu Rubin suddenly became calm again.

He transmitted a large amount of information into Li Ping's mind.

"This is... the Spirit Gathering Ascension Formation?" Li Ping immediately saw through the profound mysteries within.

"Strictly speaking, it's the Spirit Gathering Ascension Formation modified by Brother Xuanyuan. The original version of the Ascension Formation was created by a great demonic cultivator. Heh, this person was a true peerless fiend. He was of humble birth and his cultivation aptitude was also insufficient, reaching his limit at the Golden Core stage. But he stubbornly created this fiendish formation. As long as the formation enveloped an area, everything inside would be refined into the purest energy."

"He started with small mortal worlds, gradually increasing in strength. Who knows how many worlds and living beings he exterminated before finally ascending to the immortal realm. The first thing he did after arriving in the immortal realm was to improve the formation, intending to repeat his old tricks there. Hmph, even such a character died as easily as a fly during the immortal realm's tribulation. All the treasures he accumulated in his lifetime conveniently fell into Brother Xuanyuan's hands. However, Brother Xuanyuan was very disdainful of his legacy..." Mu Rubin said with a cold laugh.

"Someone so violent and cruel can also attain the Dao and become an immortal? Is the immortal tribulation in his world different from the Xuanhuang Realm's?" Li Ping asked with a frown.

Because in Li Ping's memory, in the Xuanhuang Realm, the difficulty of the heavenly tribulation faced by such villains during their tribulation was dozens of times greater than usual. There was no possibility of successfully transcending the tribulation. Hence, he asked this question.

"No matter how powerful the heavenly tribulation is, there is a limit in the lower realms. As long as you're stronger than it, that's enough. Moreover, good and evil... actually have no definitive conclusion..."

Li Ping could clearly feel that as Mu Rubin spoke, he once again fell into a state of confusion.

"What do you need me to do?" Li Ping timely shifted Mu Rubin's attention.

"Your attainments in formations shouldn't be much inferior to Brother Xuanyuan's. The control center of the Spirit Gathering Ascension Formation here cannot be recklessly disturbed. If possible, add another layer of formations to compress this place further into the dark sea. Then, sever the connection between the formation-controlling magic treasure in the hands of the Myriad Immortal Alliance and the grand formation." The clear-headed Mu Rubin said as such.
