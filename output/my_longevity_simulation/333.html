Chapter 333

Here is the full English translation of the excerpt:

Li Fan was slightly stunned.

"He didn't die? Not only did he escape the great calamity of Mo Sha's world destruction, but it seems he had another opportunity in Yuan Dao State?"

"This kind of luck..."

Li Fan suddenly became interested in this Han Yi.

Looking carefully, he was completely different from his previous image of tattered clothes and messy hair.

Now, Han Yi was wearing a purple-gold robe, with a faint glow flowing on it, clearly not an ordinary item at first glance.

On his left hand, he wore a green jade thumb ring, which not only had storage functions but also traces of formation fluctuations.

At first glance, he gave the impression of being extraordinary and either wealthy or noble.

His own cultivation was also remarkable.

Thirteen years ago, he had not yet broken through to the Foundation Establishment realm.

Now, however, he was already at the Golden Core stage, just like Li Fan.

"Interesting." Li Fan's eyes moved slightly, and a hint of a smile appeared at the corner of his mouth.

Under the gaze of the crowd, a silver light was emitted from the surface of the Tian Xuan Mirror in the center of the hall, shining on the purple wooden box in Han Yi's hand.

After a moment, the purple wooden box disappeared.

And the list on the surface of the Tian Xuan Mirror changed slightly.

At the top of the list, it clearly changed to "Han Yi, Tian Du Hua Yu Dan. Can prolong life by 806 years."

The cultivators in the hall immediately erupted in a commotion.

"It's actually true? This kid wasn't boasting?"

"A mere Golden Core cultivator, his luck is too good, isn't it? To actually find a treasure that can prolong life by 800 years? I'm really envious!"

"Tian Du Hua Yu... I seem to have heard this name somewhere before."

"The previous Blue Feather Immortal Master in Yuan Dao State who has already fallen, wasn't the cultivation technique he practiced called 'Tian Du Hua Yu Technique'? I heard that in order to fight for this Dao-harmonizing true technique, Yuan Dao State is in chaos, and many cultivators have fallen. I don't know what relationship this Tian Du Hua Yu Dan has with the Blue Feather Immortal Master."

Everyone was discussing, staring at Han Yi with gazes full of various meanings.

But Han Yi, facing the gazes of many cultivators far higher in cultivation than him, was able to remain calm and composed, his expression unchanged.

He even gracefully saluted the cultivators and said with a smile, "Please forgive me, everyone! If nothing unexpected happens, the first place in this bounty hunt will be mine."

As soon as these words came out, the expressions of the cultivators present turned unpleasant.

But Han Yi didn't care. He took out a folding fan from somewhere and opened it with a snap.

Fanning himself leisurely, he found a place to sit down and waited for the final result to come out.

Han Yi's sudden appearance made the atmosphere in the Tian Ji Academy's hall a lot more depressing for a while.

It was not as enthusiastically discussed as before.

The first and second places were almost set.

Although the ownership of the third place might change, the degree of attention was obviously not as high as before.

The technique Yan Fa Jue could deduce was extraordinary, but the third place could only deduce it to the Nascent Soul realm.

It was completely incomparable to the Primordial Spirit and even Dao-harmonizing techniques.

After about half a day, two or three more cultivators arrived at the hall one after another.

After looking at the list on the surface of the Tian Xuan Mirror, they knew there was no hope of competing for the top three.

But these cultivators still offered their treasures one by one for the double reward promised by Immortal Master Ji Cheng.

Seeing that it was about time, Li Fan took out a Wangshou Fruit from his storage ring and placed it in front of the Tian Xuan Mirror.

A flash of light passed, and the list on the Tian Xuan Mirror was updated again.

"Xiao Fan, Wangshou Fruit. Can prolong life by 300 years."

Li Fan's sudden rise caused a ripple in the somewhat depressed hall.

There was even a cultivator who suddenly stood up, his eyes red, staring at Li Fan.

As if Li Fan had an irreconcilable feud with him.

"Brother Wan! Calm down, calm down!"

It turned out that this person was the original second place, Wan Yu.

The appearance of Han Yi and Li Fan made him fall out of the top three in one fell swoop, losing the opportunity to use Yan Fa Jue.

However, Li Fan's gaze only lingered on him for a moment before completely ignoring him.

Under the gaze of the crowd of cultivators, Li Fan slowly walked to a seat near Han Yi and sat down.

Han Yi looked at Li Fan, a glint flashing in his eyes.

But he didn't say anything at this time, only cupping his hands towards Li Fan with a smile on his face.

After another half a cup of tea's time, a countdown began to appear on the surface of the Tian Xuan Mirror.

The deadline for the bounty was not far away.

Just at this last moment, a figure dressed as a scholar rushed in at the last minute.

His figure stopped under the Tian Xuan Mirror, and he looked up, squinting.

His reaction next was also very interesting. First, he let out a long sigh of relief.

Then he shook his head, looking very regretful.

His gaze swept around and directly locked onto Han Yi.

He also found a seat near Han Yi, just like Li Fan.

Han Yi couldn't help but sneer, "Interesting!"

But the movement of his fan became a bit hurried.

The countdown on the Tian Xuan Mirror slowly returned to zero.

And the ranking list on the mirror surface finally only left the top three lines.

A blue-robed cultivator suddenly appeared in the hall at this moment.

He said in a loud voice, "Han Yi, Xi Men Yue, Xiao Fan, please follow me."

"The remaining cultivators stay here, and someone will be responsible for verifying and distributing the rewards later."

Hearing this, Han Yi took the lead and stood up, walking at the very front.

After Li Fan stood up, he made a gesture of invitation to Xi Men Yue, who was in second place.

Xi Men Yue hesitated for a moment, then thanked him in a low voice and followed Han Yi out.

Li Fan walked at the back of the three.

Under the envious gazes of the crowd of cultivators, the three of them followed the blue-robed cultivator out of the hall, turned right, and went all the way inside.

After passing through more than a dozen doors, they finally arrived at a small courtyard with a big tree in the center.

This big tree seemed to have grown very luxuriantly before, with branches extending and almost covering the entire courtyard.

But now, it was unknown what kind of changes it had suffered. The leaves had all fallen off.

Even on the main trunk of the tree, yellowish spots appeared.

His figure was a bit hunched, stroking the equally dying big tree in front of him.

His back looked a bit desolate.

"Greetings, Immortal Master!"

Seeing this, how could Li Fan and the other two not understand the identity of this old man?

They hurriedly saluted at the same time.

Han Yi also put away the arrogance on his face and became more restrained.

Immortal Master Ji Cheng slowly turned around, looking at the three young people, a trace of nostalgia and sadness flashing in his eyes.

"You... are very good."

Immortal Master Ji Cheng said word by word, slowly.

"To be honest, the effects of the life-prolonging treasures you submitted this time far exceeded my original expectations."

"Thanks to you, I can finally stand up again and move around a bit."

"To make some arrangements for my affairs after death."

Immortal Master Ji Cheng spoke very slowly, and a hint of death qi faintly appeared on his face.

"Immortal Master, do you mean you have already consumed all these life-prolonging treasures?"

"But why?"

At this time, Xi Men Yue couldn't help but ask.
