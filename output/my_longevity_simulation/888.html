Chapter 888

Here is the full translation of the Chinese excerpt into English:

In the following period of time, the Dharma Ancestor's Dharma Image appeared quite frequently.

Within a month, it appeared a total of five times.

On one occasion, there was even only a two-day interval between appearances.

Originally, Li Fan thought that using such a big move, the thirty-three Immortal Chosen Ones would have to pay a considerable price at the very least.

But from this frequency, it seemed that it was not as he thought.

Each time, the Dharma Transmission Image appeared in a different state of the Five Elders Council, forcing several Celestial Venerables of the Council to take action, before quietly disappearing again.

Moreover, what was even more frightening was that each time this Image flashed by, it seemed to become more and more "vivid".

From the beginning, the technique was just opening both eyes.

Later on, it slowly tried to strike with its palms.

The Dharma Image was improving at a speed visible to the naked eye.

It was hard to imagine, if it tried enough times, whether it would really become the same as the real Dharma Ancestor.

"No need to reach Dharma Ancestor's level, even with just one-tenth of his power, it would be enough for the Five Elders Council to drink a pot."

"No wonder the Council is so anxious. They sent several messages in a row, wanting me to help probe the background of the thirty-three Immortal Chosen Ones."

Li Fan sneered: "Unfortunately, this time I'm really powerless."

Ignoring the Five Elders Council, Li Fan focused his attention on the new guest who had arrived at the Spirit Wood Realm.

At this moment, he was being led by Shang Tianci, touring various places in the Spirit Wood Realm.

Even younger than Shang Tianci, but on his somewhat immature face, there would occasionally flash a hint of evil aura.

"I say, Tianci, this small world has nice scenery. But to forcibly call it the 'Myriad Realms United Conference', isn't that a bit of a misnomer?"

The visitor was none other than Yan Fangzhou, whom Shang Tianci had gone through great pains to bring here.

Yan Fangzhou said somewhat disdainfully: "The so-called myriad realms united, just this few people? It really made me excited for nothing, I thought I could finally do something big."

"I'm afraid let alone subverting the Myriad Immortals Alliance. Just the Martial Arts and Dharma Protector Hall of this Shilin State alone, you guys probably can't handle it, right?"

Shang Tianci's face reddened, and he was about to retort.

But suddenly, he heard an icy voice abruptly ring out: "Young in age, big in words. Let them come and try?"

This voice was like the wrath of heaven and earth, echoing endlessly in this small world.

Yan Fangzhou's expression changed slightly, wanting to find the owner of the voice.

But he found that he had unknowingly been isolated, and Shang Tianci beside him had disappeared.

The entire world seemed to be infinitely expanding, and he felt like an ant between heaven and earth, feeling the sky was high and the earth was vast, endless.

And because of what he had just said, he seemed to have angered the heaven and earth here.

Amidst the changing winds and clouds, unimaginable killing intent enveloped him.

"Putting on an act!" Yan Fangzhou was after all a generation's prodigy, how could he be so easily frightened.

A black flame phantom suddenly appeared behind him.

The instant the black fire appeared, the originally turbulent sky immediately came to a standstill.

An aura of annihilation emanated from this black flame.

The killing intent of heaven and earth all froze, and then fell into annihilation.

Bang!

The surrounding scene shattered like broken glass, exploding in layers.

A hint of triumph couldn't help but appear on Yan Fangzhou's face.

But soon, this bit of complacency disappeared.

Because what appeared before him immediately after was still the vast world filled with endless killing intent just now!

It seemed that what he had shattered was merely an illusion!

"No matter how many layers you have, I will break them all!"

Yan Fangzhou snorted coldly, and the black flames shot into the sky like a meteor flying in reverse.

Wherever it passed, the space all seemed to be frozen and fell into a state of stillness.

And then it was crudely crushed by the black trajectory.

The surrounding scene suddenly changed again, and the original scene appeared once more.

The black long line did not lose momentum and charged straight ahead.

In the blink of an eye, who knows how many layers of heaven and earth it had shattered.

But...

No matter how many layers he shattered, the scene that appeared outside and surrounded him remained the same as before.

It was like an endless maze. Even if he ignored the walls of the maze and directly broke through them,

He simply could not see the end, so he could only be trapped in this maze forever.

After an unknown amount of time, the confidence on Yan Fangzhou's face slowly began to disappear.

Instead, it turned from disbelief to panic.

When he finally determined that he could not break through the blockade of this space-time, his face suddenly changed several times.

"You think you can subdue people with such little tricks?"

Yan Fangzhou said loudly to the world that had trapped him.

Unfortunately, to his disappointment, the other party seemed to not have heard his words at all.

There was no reaction at all.

A trace of sharpness flashed in Yan Fangzhou's eyes, and he took out a golden talisman shrouded in wisps of lightning.

He threw it into the air.

But to his surprise, this [Space-Breaking Talisman], which had always been effective in the past, had now lost its use.

Not only did it fail to break open the space and create an escape route,

Instead, it seemed to be lost, spinning aimlessly in the air.

Next, Yan Fangzhou tried all his life-saving methods one by one.

None of them worked!

At this point, Yan Fangzhou was truly panicked.

"I admit defeat!"

"I shouldn't have boasted! The Myriad Realms Alliance is indeed worthy of its reputation!"

Yan Fangzhou suppressed the viciousness in his eyes and shouted to the sky.

"Please allow me to join the Alliance, senior!"

The sky was silent, still without any response.

Yan Fangzhou was stunned for a moment, thinking that the other party had not heard him, and shouted a few more times.

The result was still the same.

Yan Fangzhou was a little dazed now.

...

Lingmu Realm.

Shang Tianci looked at Yan Fangzhou, who had suddenly stood still, and felt a little strange.

He called out a few times, but the other party seemed to not have heard him at all.

At this point, he finally realized that something was wrong.

Around Yan Fangzhou, there seemed to be an invisible barrier surrounding him.

Not only could outsiders not touch him, he also seemed unable to sense the outside world.

"Senior..." Shang Tianci said weakly, looking at Li Fan who had suddenly appeared.

"The young man is not bad, he just needs some tempering. Let him hang for a year or two first." Li Fan said calmly.

In his hand, a wisp of black flame flickered and jumped incessantly.

It seemed to want to break free from Li Fan's hand.

But it was tightly controlled.

Shang Tianci did not dare to refute and could only lower his head and agree.

Li Fan looked at Yan Fangzhou, who was trapped by his Infinite Law, and stroked his chin.

With a thought, Yan Fangzhou was immediately transferred outside the Black Jade Gate Tower of the Alliance.

Like a figurine, he was posed in a suitable posture.

"When he breaks down, let him out."

"It will also be convenient for training."
