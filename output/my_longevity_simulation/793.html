At this moment, all of his prior preparations erupted simultaneously.

First, his avatar Xu Bai appeared in front of Mr. Bai. The two stared at each other, looking at the extremely similar faces across from them. Yet, they didn't exchange many words.

Avatar Xu Bai had no hesitation. In an instant, his own grotto-heaven collapsed with a bang. Xu Bai had committed suicide and perished!

Although after the holy blood incarnation gives birth to an avatar, the death of the original body will not implicate the brand new avatar. But after all, they come from the same source and have a mysterious connection between them.

The demise of the original body will inevitably bring some slight, indelible influence to the avatar. This is a natural limitation of the holy blood incarnation technique. Even with Mr. Bai's current cultivation, it is still unavoidable.

Therefore, Mr. Bai's paralysis was prolonged for another moment. After avatar Xu Bai disappeared, Li Fan instantly felt that the mysterious connection with Mr. Bai was cut off quite a bit.

He tried, and although he could open the Huanzhen panel, he still couldn't confirm Huanzhen. So Li Fan began his next move.

With a step, he left the cultivation secret room in an instant. After his departure, the hidden ninety-nine Chaos Character Puppets in the secret room exploded with a bang.

The power of [Chaos] instantly enveloped Changsheng Valley. The sky was obscured by a hazy force. A chaos descended.

Like bamboo shoots after a spring rain, hundreds of yellow withered branches suddenly grew out of the ground in Changsheng Valley. Looking at their faces, they were surprisingly exactly the same as Li Fan's!

They all showed strange smiles to the people present, and then quickly retracted back into the ground and disappeared. Everyone, including Mr. Bai, had their attention drawn by this scene.

The next moment, a greater upheaval struck. It was the ripples set off by the simultaneous detonation of the remaining two-thirds of the Chaos Character Puppets. Mr. Bai's body was once again paralyzed.

At the same time, when no one was paying attention, a small black cauldron soared into the sky. It was about to break through the secret realm and fly towards the sky of the Xuanhuang Realm.

Even if he acted in the most secretive manner, there were still many people present after all, and someone still discovered the departing Medicine King's True Cauldron. A cry of alarm drew everyone's eyes.

But soon, their faces changed even more. Below the Medicine King's True Cauldron, countless human figures were suddenly thrown out forcefully in different directions like garbage.

Liu San's body shook violently, and his face suddenly changed. Because he had already seen that these were all the Medicine King Sect disciples who were slumbering in the Medicine King's True Cauldron!

They were all old-method cultivators with weak bodies. And they were still in a state of slumber. Being thrown at such a fast speed, if they crashed to the ground like this, perhaps except for a few exceptionally powerful ones, the rest would either die or be crippled!

"Save them!" Liu San shouted repeatedly.

The disciples of the Medicine King Sect reacted one after another. But there were too many cultivators being thrown down, and they all flew in different directions.

Inside Changsheng Valley, it was even more affected by the Chaos Characters, making it a bit difficult for the cultivators themselves to fly, and there was a risk of falling. They could barely take care of themselves, let alone save others.

Liu San's eyes were bloodshot, and even using all his strength, he only managed to save a dozen or so people. Just as he was feeling a bit desperate, Mr. Bai, who had already recovered from his paralysis, stretched out his hand.

He embraced all the falling cultivators and then gently placed them on the ground. The influence of the Chaos Characters had not yet disappeared. After doing all this, Mr. Bai's right elbow strangely rotated a circle, as if it had been broken.

Although it quickly returned to normal, it still made him slightly frown. "Thank you, Senior, for the rescue!" After checking that none of his fellow sect members were in mortal danger, Liu San flew forward, cupped his hands and said very gratefully.

Mr. Bai did not speak, only looking at the Medicine King's True Cauldron that had already disappeared in the sky, as well as the hundreds of puppets that had already fled to a place several states away.

And at Li Fan's corpse lying on the ground, which had already lost its vitality.
