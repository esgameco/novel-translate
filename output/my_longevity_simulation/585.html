Chapter 585

"After all, peeking into the secrets of heaven and earth, there is no quick way to directly plunder ready-made golden elixirs."

"But if it can be considered a big business..."

A trace of doubt flashed through Li Fan's mind: "What are they planning to do by purchasing a large number of cultivator corpses?"

Li Fan did not intend to dig deep into the other party's motives.

However, their actions were so mysterious, and Zhang Debiao had not returned for a long time. It was feared that the situation was already dire.

Although it might be difficult to silently kill a cultivator in the Xuanhuang Realm due to the existence of omens of falling, making them vanish into thin air with no trace was quite doable.

For example, Zhou Qing'ao was a living example, he was still lying at the bottom of the Cloud Sea!

Just when Li Fan was hesitating whether to spend some contributions to continue investigating the news about Zhang Debiao through someone else...

A stranger cultivator came uninvited and took the initiative to come to him.

During his actions in Tianlingzhou these days, Li Fan used a new identity to avoid drawing attention, going by the pseudonym Yu Ji.

So at this moment, Li Fan immediately knew the purpose of the other party approaching him must be related to the missing Zhang Debiao.

"Interesting, I will meet him."

In the alternate courtyard of the Lun Dao Tower, Li Fan met the visitor.

He was a cultivator in a gray robe.

Short and frail, with deep wrinkles on his face, walking with a limp, he looked extremely weak, as if a gust of wind could blow him over.

As the other person approached, Li Fan even faintly smelled a scent of decay and foulness emanating from him.

"Jie jie jie..."

"Daoist Yu, I heard you are looking for Zhang Debiao? Does he have something you need in his possession?"

The gray-robed cultivator cut to the chase, asking in a strange tone.

Li Fan narrowed his eyes, assessing the other party.

Although the other party seemed to only have Golden Core cultivation, Li Fan could almost certainly tell that it was just a disguise, a camouflage.

The intermittent sense of danger in his mind reminded Li Fan that this gray-robed cultivator before him was not simple.

"What is your name, Daoist?" Li Fan didn't answer directly but instead arched his hand and inquired about the other party's name.

"Xiumu Daoist." The gray-robed cultivator was also very straightforward, with a concise reply.

After scanning his mind, Li Fan had not heard of this person before.

Li Fan pondered for a moment, lowered his voice, and admitted, "Yes, I need some cultivator's golden elixirs."

"The more intact, the better. The higher the stage of the hidden laws within the Golden Core, the better."

"Price is not an issue." After saying that, Li Fan added.

A gleam flashed in Xiumu Daoist's eyes as he reached out and produced a green and red crisscrossed golden elixir from somewhere and handed it over.

"Daoist Yu, please have a look at the quality of this elixir."

Li Fan's heart moved as he took the golden elixir and carefully observed it.

It seemed as though it had just been excavated from a cultivator's body, and he could still see faint bloodstains on it.

Upon perceiving it, he found that the elixir contained insights related to wood and fire laws.

Using the skill 'Identify,' he received a prompt that absorption was possible.

"What's the price?" Li Fan then asked.

"Thirty thousand contribution points." Xiumu said in a deep voice.

Perhaps afraid that Li Fan would find it expensive, he continued, "I also have some single law Golden Cores over there, at a cheaper price of fifteen thousand contribution points. Of course, if you need a larger quantity, we can discuss the price further."

"Also, I only accept equivalent spirit stone transactions. I do not accept direct transfer of contribution points." While Li Fan was contemplating and not answering, Xiumu added.

Li Fan's concern was not the price, but rather the suspicious appearance of Xiumu Daoist.

Moreover, selling cultivator's golden elixirs, which was a taboo, without any cover-up, was quite unorthodox.

It seemed as though they were trading not prohibited items but rather common goods.

More importantly, Li Fan was currently using an identity of an unknown origin, a cultivator without any background.

He had no idea of Li Fan's background.

Yet Xiumu Daoist remained so brazen...

Was he not afraid that Li Fan came to investigate under the guise of fishing for law enforcement personnel related to the Wanxian Alliance?

"Twenty-eight thousand is also acceptable." Seeing Li Fan hesitating, Xiumu Daoist proactively offered a discount.

Li Fan then pretended to hesitate and nodded, "Deal."

On the 'Identify' interface, the progress of the unlocking of the anchor increased by 5%.

"When calculated this way, I need two hundred intact Golden Cores. This was after considering that the one I just absorbed was a dual law Golden Core. If it were just ordinary single law Golden Cores, I would probably need a larger quantity."

"Conventional means are no longer feasible for collection."

"Unless I dare to brazenly defy the world, transform into a demon, and rampageously slaughter cultivators for their Golden Cores..."

This thought only flashed through his mind for a moment before Li Fan discarded it.

"Daoist Xiumu." Li Fan cleared his throat and did not directly request another transaction but instead asked him about his doubts.

Unexpectedly, Xiumu Daoist didn't seem to mind: "Daoist, rest assured, we are not like that insignificant kid Zhang Debiao."

"The Golden Cores we provide have a safe and guaranteed source, absolutely no worries."

"Even if the Immortal Alliance investigates, it won't be a problem. Please trust me!"

He assured emphatically, to the extent that Li Fan suspected he might directly disperse himself with his force.

"Impossible, right?"

However, the answer he received was beyond Li Fan's expectations. He immediately showed an expression of disbelief: "This Golden Core obtained just now was clearly dug out from a cultivator recently..."

"Such inhumane behavior, not to mention our Wanxian Alliance, even the Five Elders' Council wouldn't turn a blind eye to it, right?"

"If it weren't for my special reasons and urgent needs, I wouldn't have done this morally questionable thing!"

Li Fan spoke firmly.

Xiumu Daoist sneered but did not argue with Li Fan's gibberish.

Instead, he suddenly asked, "If these Golden Core cultivators were all willing?"

"Even willing..." Li Fan suddenly froze.

"Willing? How is that possible?"

The words spoken by Xiumu Daoist indeed shocked Li Fan. He put on a look of amazement, staring at the other party with widened eyes.

"Where there's demand, there will naturally be a market."

"It's not something new that since the reconstruction of order in the Xuanhuang Realm all these years, people like us have emerged. It's not surprising."

Xiumu Daoist shook his head, explaining to Li Fan.

"Like Zhang Debiao, extracting Golden Cores from cultivator corpses is just too inefficient."
