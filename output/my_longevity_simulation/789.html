Chapter 789

Here is my translation of the excerpt from the Chinese novel:

Li Fan sensed the departing figure of Dongfang Yao after avoiding the Formless Killing Technique, and thought to himself.

"This person Dongfang..."

"Indeed."

After slowly withdrawing his gaze, Li Fan quietly came to a rather inexpensive stone forest area.

This place was located in Zhonghuaizhou, with scarce resources. And it had already been explored, with no small worlds or celestial realms entrances. So now very few cultivators came here.

It was perfect for Li Fan.

Just like Dongfang Yao, in the time to come, he too would set up contingencies in various places throughout the Xuanhuang Realm.

Arriving three thousand zhang underground, after opening up an empty space,

Li Fan took out a pile of broken puppet components.

They were very similar to those guardian puppets from the Heavenly Sun Cave Dwelling. Made of shattered porcelain, with black and red patterns on the exterior.

Upon closer inspection, those red threads on each puppet component seemed to be part of the strokes of certain characters.

This was the method Li Fan conceived to disassemble the Chaotic Words Talisman!

Setting up a formation array, he placed the puppet core in the center.

Then randomly arranged the puppet components within the array.

"When a specific condition is triggered, the array will activate the puppet core to instantly reassemble."

"The Chaotic Words Talisman is complete."

"Afterwards, the puppet self-destructs, serving as the energy for manifesting divine abilities."

Li Fan thought this in his mind as he finished the scattered placement of the puppets.

The silvery rays in the formation pierced through the puppet components as nodes, integrating the entire array.

Li Fan lightly tapped it, and the formation fell into quiescence, without leaking the slightest aura.

"The setup here is now complete."

"Although a bit crude, the cruder it is, the less likely it will be discovered."

Li Fan nodded with some satisfaction, then directly left stealthily without even setting up a concealing array.

After all, he had forged no less than a thousand of these chaotic word puppets.

Even if some were occasionally discovered, it was no great loss and would not affect the overall plan.

After drilling out from underground, Li Fan surveyed his surroundings to ensure no one was tracking him.

He then buried more chaotic word puppets at two other locations in Zhonghuaizhou.

With the setup complete, he headed towards the neighboring Shouyizhou.

He planned to bury three or four puppets in every prefecture of the Myriad Immortal Alliance.

"When the chaotic words are unleashed together, even a Longevity Celestial Emperor would have their vision attracted and obscured."

However, after methodically completing the setup in the thirty-fifth prefecture, he suddenly paused his movements.

"Is it finally time to ascend to the Immortal Realm?"

Li Fan looked up into the distance.

The footsteps of the Holy Embryo Xu Bai wandering the Xuanhuang Realm had also reached their destination at this moment.

The heavenly transformation he was about to undergo to enter his inner celestial realm was the "Celestial Spirit Birth Blossom".

"I had thought the Celestial Spirit Blossom, once tainted by demonic qi and its beauty lost forever, would never again appear in this world."

"It seems the self-restorative ability of this expanse of the Xuanhuang heavens and earth is truly remarkable," Li Fan pondered to himself.

As the Holy Embryo Xu Bai absorbed this transformation of heaven and earth, within his celestial realm, it was like a spring breeze blowing through.

Clusters of lush blossoms bloomed one after another, breathtakingly beautiful.

Even Li Fan, millions of miles away, seemed to catch a whiff of the floral fragrance from Xu Bai's celestial realm.

It was as if the final piece of the puzzle had been completed.

The entire celestial realm was finally elevated to the level of a small world.

An extraordinary energy began circulating within the celestial realm world.

From the celestial canopy, to all creatures, to the deep earth.

Then repeating the cycle endlessly.

Here is the English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

From the depths of the earth, a rumbling sound was faintly transmitted.

Accompanied by the sounds of rain, wind, insects, and birds in the Heavenly Cave World, they collectively formed a grand and vibrant symphony.

Li Fan seemed to see the Heavenly Cave World continuously expanding and sublimating in the future.

The Holy Fetus Xu Bai stood in the void of the Tianliang State.

Although no other cultivators had discovered his existence,

the subtle changes in the Tianliang State caused by him were noticed by some cultivators.

For example, Sun Qi, who was reporting his progress in cultivating the "Reverse the Heavens and Earth Record" to Sun Liancheng, suddenly stopped.

He raised his head and looked up at the sky outside.

Sun Qi's brow furrowed, and after a long time, he shook his head slightly.

"Perhaps it was just my illusion."

"Just now, I seemed to feel that the heavens and earth were reviving."

"But it was only for a moment."

"That feeling has already disappeared now."

Sun Qi spoke slowly, word by word. From the lingering uncertainty in his eyes, it was clear that it was not just a simple illusion.

"The revival of the heavens and earth..." Sun Liancheng narrowed his eyes, lost in thought.

Through the Formless Killing Intent, Li Fan sensed the scene transmitted from Sun Qi's side and became deeply pensive.

Only he knew that Sun Qi's intuition was correct.

The revival changes in the Tianliang State were extracted by the Holy Fetus Xu Bai and infused into his own Heavenly Cave World.

Without this change force, it would take the Tianliang State a long time to accumulate the power to return to its original prosperous state.

The revival force of the Tianliang State was slightly different from that of the Cong Yun Sea.

The Cong Yun Sea was a creative change from life to death, from destruction to rebirth.

While the Tianliang State was a normal life force transformation from desolation to prosperity.

With this change, the Heavenly Cave within the Holy Fetus Xu Bai had also completely evolved into a small world.

Li Fan, as part of the same body, could now fully appreciate the scenery of this new small world.

His divine sense quickly swept across the vast and vibrant land below.

A sense of familiarity suddenly surged in his heart.

"This geographical distribution..."

"It seems I've seen it somewhere before."

To confirm his thoughts, he quickly located several characteristic mountain ranges and water systems.

"Indeed, exactly the same."

Li Fan was greatly shocked.

"This is..."

Through the writings of Xi Men Yu, Li Fan understood the differences in the geography between the ancient and the current Xuanhuang Realm.

By removing the White Mist Barrier and reassembling the disrupted state domains, the original appearance of the ancient Xuanhuang Realm was restored!

This was what the Holy Fetus Xu Bai had accomplished during this period!

"Ordinary Transcendent Immortal cultivators rely on the changes of a single state or a single type."

"But the Holy Fetus Xu Bai used..."

Li Fan trembled.

"The Rebirth of the Xuanhuang Realm!"

"Using his Heavenly Cave World to rehearse the changes that the Xuanhuang Realm would undergo!"

"Without the interference of cultivators or the Immortal Emperors, this is the result of the Xuanhuang Realm's self-evolution!"

"This..."

The more Li Fan thought about it, the more astonished he felt.

"If he is not the Embodiment of the Heavenly Dao, obtaining the support of the world itself, he would never be able to accomplish this!"

"Even if I kept trying, it would be impossible!"

Li Fan understood this clearly.

Although he had advanced to the Transcendent Immortal realm, the aura of the Holy Fetus Xu Bai had not changed in the slightest.

He stood quietly in the void, like an ordinary person.
