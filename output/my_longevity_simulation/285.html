Chapter 285

"I never thought that the once greatest sect in the world would have fallen to the point of associating with demonic beasts."

A trace of disdain flashed across Zhang Qianmo's face: "It seems that the efforts of the immortal sects over these years have not been in vain."

Li Fan's expression remained unchanged, his tone faint: "Senior Brother Zhang, some things in this world are not as simple as they appear on the surface."

"If I said that I have nothing to do with the Xuantian Sect, would you believe me?"

Zhang Qianmo looked at Li Fan as if he were an idiot, with a look of 'do you think I would believe that?', and snorted without speaking.

Li Fan smiled and didn't dwell on this question any further.

Sizing up Zhang Qianmo and flipping through the memories of Li Chen's possessed body, he discovered some interesting details that he hadn't noticed before.

After pondering in silence for a moment, he spoke probingly: "The Xuantian Sect, that was something from many years ago. Your immortal sects regard it as taboo. Since I joined the Zixiao Sect, for so many years, I have never heard any news about the Xuantian Sect."

"Senior Brother, you're not much older than me. How do you know all these secrets so clearly?"

Zhang Qianmo's expression changed slightly, but he still didn't answer.

Seeing that his guess was confirmed, Li Fan continued to raise the stakes: "I always felt that Master and the elders had a strange attitude towards you."

"Before, I thought it was because Senior Brother's talent was too good."

"Now that I think about it..."

Li Fan smiled.

Zhang Qianmo's face slowly darkened.

"Moreover, although we are fellow disciples, Senior Brother's techniques and divine powers are far superior to mine!"

"Especially your [Purple Elixir Nirvana Technique]..."

When Li Fan said this, Zhang Qianmo's expression finally completely changed.

He looked deeply at Li Fan, as if to remember him forever.

In an instant, his aura, which had already weakened to that of an ordinary person, was rapidly disappearing.

Li Fan was a little surprised and hurriedly went to check, but it was too late.

By the time he walked to Zhang Qianmo's side, Senior Brother Zhang had already completely lost his vital signs.

"Feigning death?"

"Nirvana?"

Li Fan didn't let his guard down and started to examine.

After a long time, Li Fan got up and stroked his chin.

The result was somewhat unexpected.

Zhang Qianmo was actually really dead.

Moreover, he didn't undergo nirvana rebirth from the purple elixir after death like before.

Instead, he had completely fallen.

If it were in the real world, there should be an omen descending above his head at this time.

"A secret technique that can end one's own life even with all the acupoints in the body sealed..."

"Knowing ancient secrets, his identity and status in the sect are implicitly different."

"Although only at the Foundation Establishment stage, he has a heaven-defying ambition. His perspective is frighteningly high..."

Li Fan pondered and sighed: "It seems this Senior Brother Zhang has another identity."

"What a pity..."

To be cautious, he didn't remove the Immortal Severing Stone Needles, but directly searched Zhang Qianmo's corpse.

Apart from a storage ring, there was nothing else of value.

He tried to probe into the ring with his spiritual sense to see what was inside.

But he was blocked by a flash of purple lightning.

"It seems there are restrictions that can't be broken." After more than a dozen attempts without success, Li Fan felt a little regretful.

"Forget it, I can't bring it back to reality anyway."

Li Fan simply gave up.

"Zhang Qianmo is dealt with, next is Steward Song."

After some thought, Li Fan dug a pit in the secret room and put Zhang Qianmo's corpse in it.

Then he covered it with that Immortal Severing Stone slab.

Afterwards, he headed straight for the Life-Extending Tree in the center of the city.

As he approached, he released his own aura.

"Which Senior Brother has come?!" Song Hesong's slightly surprised voice came.

"So it's Senior Brother Li!"

Li Fan didn't exchange pleasantries with him, but directly said in a cold voice: "Steward Song, you've managed this Ningyuan City well!"

Song Hesong was startled by Li Fan's attitude of immediately questioning him, thinking that his group's ambush had been exposed.

He subconsciously wanted to take action.

But he still held back and asked probingly: "Senior Brother, what do you mean by this?"

Li Fan snorted: "Senior Brother Zhang and I discovered some taboo objects in the city. If these things are exposed in the territory of our Zixiao Sect, let alone a small steward like you, I'm afraid even the sect will be implicated."

"Taboo objects?" Song Hesong couldn't help but feel a little confused.

Putting on a look of fearful ignorance, Song Hesong asked: "What exactly are they that violate such a great taboo? Please enlighten me, Senior Brother!"

Li Fan turned and flew towards Mr. Bai's courtyard.

"Follow me!"

Song Hesong looked at Li Fan's receding back, hesitating for a moment.

With a sweep of his spiritual sense, Chu Liang and the others were still lying in ambush at the planned location without any issues.

Relying on his Golden Core cultivation, Song Hesong still followed fearlessly.

Soon, he arrived at the entrance to the underground secret room.

Li Fan's somewhat surprised voice came from below: "Huh? Where's Senior Brother Zhang?"

Song Hesong scanned the area with his spiritual sense, not detecting any ambush, and also squeezed in.

Upon seeing the dilapidated faceless stone statue standing in the secret room, he was slightly stunned.

Because this stone statue was completely outside the range of his spiritual sense.

"Could it be that the taboo object Senior Brother spoke of is this stone statue?" Song Hesong couldn't help but ask.

Li Fan nodded and approached: "That's right, it's this [Xuantian Divine Statue]!"

"Xuantian Divine Statue..." Steward Song frowned slightly, seeming to have heard this name a long time ago.

A moment later, he seemed to remember something, his body trembling and pupils contracting.

He looked at that faceless stone statue again.

At this moment...

A stone needle pierced through his dantian from behind.

Extremely horrified, he was about to counterattack when the circulation of his internal energy stagnated.

What awaited him were the next thirty-five Immortal Severing Stone Needles.

With all his acupoints sealed, Song Hesong collapsed to the ground.

A wave of remorse surged in his mind, but he still had some luck, struggling to speak: "Senior Brother, what is the meaning of this? I really didn't know about this Xuantian Divine Statue hidden in the city."

"Although I am guilty of dereliction of duty, the crime doesn't warrant this..."

Li Fan's words ruthlessly interrupted him, causing his heart to sink to the bottom.

"What if we add colluding with outsiders and harboring ill intentions? A Golden Core cultivator pretending to be a Qi Refining cultivator, you must feel so aggrieved!"

Song Hesong's face instantly turned deathly pale.

He still wanted to struggle one last time, but it was in vain.

Li Fan approached step by step: "Let me see what exactly you know."

Having seen Song Hesong's subtle changes in expression just now, Li Fan revealed a somewhat chilling smile on his face.

Song Hesong watched as the other party took out tools like hammers, knives, and nails one by one from the wooden box and methodically placed them on the table.

Cold sweat couldn't help but drip from his forehead.
