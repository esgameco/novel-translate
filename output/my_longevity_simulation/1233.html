Here is the full translation of the Chinese novel excerpt:

Li Ping stood quietly, feeling the unyielding will of the vast sea beneath his feet.

Faced with a life-and-death crisis, any existence would struggle with all its might.

Li Ping also felt the fierce desire for survival of this ocean.

But its struggle for survival was a naked "evil thought" for the Xuanhuang realm.

Li Ping, who claimed to be the incarnation of the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao, naturally could not stand on the side of the Congyun Sea.

"Since you've come, you should obediently submit."

"There's no need to make any more futile struggles."

In an instant, his divine sense enveloped the entire Congyun Ocean.

The scenes on the thousands of islands scattered within, as well as the countless creatures living in the seawater, were all instantly reflected in Li Ping's mind.

Li Ping was silent for a moment.

"This is a necessary sacrifice."

A flash of determination passed through his eyes, and threads of golden light surged from his body, then scattered to various parts of the Congyun Sea.

The power close to the essence of the original force could not be detected by anyone in the Congyun Sea, where the highest level was only Huashen cultivators.

In just a few breaths, the golden light had spread to all regions of the Congyun Sea. It penetrated deep into the ocean depths, reaching the bottom seabed.

At the bottom of the Congyun Sea, unknown violent tremors began to occur.

If one looked from the sky, one could see that the entire surface of the ocean seemed to form a huge net.

Enveloping this vibrant blue sea of life.

Suddenly, dark clouds gathered in the sky.

The originally calm sea surface suddenly stirred up terrifying waves.

Even more violent than the waves during past wind disasters.

Some islands capsized without warning, and even the spiritual islands with cultivators stationed on them were in a precarious situation amidst the fierce wind and rain.

The cultivators of the Congyun Sea all looked at the changes in the world with doubt and alarm.

Some cultivators with keen spiritual awareness had a feeling that a great calamity was imminent. Their faces changed color, and without further ado, they directly escaped through the transmission array on the Wanxian Island.

Their fleeing actions also attracted the attention of a considerable number of people. A small number of cultivators chose to leave with them.

But the vast majority of people sneered at these "easily frightened, timid as mice" escapees.

They hid on Wanxian Island with peace of mind, watching the changing scenery of wind and clouds between the heaven and earth of the Congyun Sea.

Li Ping did not pay attention to those fleeing cultivators, nor did he care about these onlookers.

His target, from beginning to end, was only the remnant will of the Congyun Sea.

The golden giant net, invisible to ordinary cultivators, had already sealed off the Congyun Sea.

The energy of the original force essence was accumulating and evolving within it, and a great formation was about to reveal its lofty appearance.

But in Li Ping's view, it was just impotent rage.

"How to erase the world consciousness?"

Li Ping pondered this question while constructing the formation.

"I refine all gods with all living beings."

He quickly thought of this unparalleled fierce formation.

"A formation that can even block true immortals, I think it's not a big problem to deal with the remnant world will."

"Countless meaningless miscellaneous thoughts to impact..."

As Li Ping set up the formation, strange scenes began to appear in the sky above the Congyun Sea for no reason.

They were distorted, desperate faces.

They howled and waved their arms.

As if begging for help, or perhaps just subconsciously screaming miserably.

On the sky curtain, appeared...

The strange faces in the sky became more and more numerous.

Densely packed, they almost filled every corner of the field of vision.

In the ears of the creatures within the Congyun Sea, slowly, besides the wailing of the human faces, no other sounds could be heard.

"Ah!"

Those with weak willpower were quickly influenced by these demonic faces, becoming equally desperate and mad.

And those who barely managed to hold on soon fell into a boundless and terrifying nightmare.

In a trance, they seemed to see a black sun rising in the sky, and the entire world on the brink of destruction - a scene of great terror.

Just like the despairing demon faces in the sky, they collectively experienced this unimaginable calamity.

The teleportation array on Wanxian Island remained open.

However, all the cultivators who saw the myriad faces in the sky lost the ability to control their own thoughts.

Regardless of their cultivation level, from Qi Refining junior cultivators to Nascent Soul island masters.

All of them were like this.

It should be known that the array formation laid down by Li Ping was not targeting these cultivators.

Just being affected by it had already brought them to such a state. One could imagine how cruel the situation faced by the consciousness of the Congyun Sea was at this moment.

With the remnant world consciousness of a single state, it was simply impossible to resist.

Amidst the incessant miserable howls of the countless demon faces, Li Ping felt that the will of the Congyun Sea was dissipating.

The towering giant waves were slowly subsiding.

It seemed that everything would pass, and the sky would clear after the rain.

But Li Fan knew that from now on, the Congyun Sea would never be the same as before.

The process of erasing the consciousness of the Congyun Sea lasted for an entire day.

During this period, all cultivators who entered the scope of the Congyun Sea were affected and fell into terrifying illusions.

But there was no incident of a large number of creatures perishing at the same time, so the Wanxian Alliance had not yet had time to react.

The dark clouds in the sky dispersed, and the Congyun Sea became crystal clear.

It was so quiet that it was a bit frightening.

Li Ping sensed the scattered will of the Congyun Sea between heaven and earth.

The golden giant net slowly retracted, incidentally bringing back the remnant thoughts of this world that had already been dismembered into myriad pieces.

"Ugh..."

As Li Ping absorbed the consciousness of the Congyun Sea, countless scenes began to flash incessantly in his mind.

Even though he was already used to constantly monitoring everything that happened within the Great Qi of the Holy Dynasty.

But the total amount of information between the two was not on the same level at all.

In the vast ocean, there were not only humans.

Below the tranquil sea surface, there were also countless living beings multiplying and thriving.

If it were from Li Ping's subjective perspective, he definitely would not pay much attention to these creatures that did not even have intelligence.

But at this moment, it was a passive absorption.

In the concept of the world's will, these fish were essentially no different from human cultivators.

Li Ping endured the intense pain in his sea of consciousness and accepted all this information.

As if he had taken a great tonic, his spiritual strength also began to expand like an inflating balloon.

Li Ping's spiritual strength was already strong enough among cultivators.

But in the midst of this rapid growth, it was inevitable for cracks to appear.

Ordinary cultivators encountering this situation would only have the option of despairingly waiting for their soul to scatter.

But Li Ping was different.

Possessing the Heavenly Emperor's Qi Scripture, he not only used the billions of citizens of the Great Qi of the Holy Dynasty as anchors.

Within his body, there was also the quintessence of transcendent power.

Golden energy surged, repairing the wounds of his spiritual soul.

At the same time, it served as a supplement for the expansion of his spiritual soul.
