Chapter 1033: A Righteous Showdown

Tears streamed down his face as his round body struggled to kneel on the ground.

He begged the immortal master to spare his life.

At this moment, Li Fan finally revealed himself.

"It seems that if an outsider intervenes, the rules become invalid. The challenge can only be won by relying entirely on one's own abilities."

Li Fan thought to himself.

Without wasting words on this round ball, he stared at his opponent, and an invisible force suddenly descended.

While crushing the more than 100,000 wailing thoughts in the other's mind into dust and turning them into nourishment for consciousness,

he also performed a liposuction surgery on his opponent.

After half a day, the survivor, who had regained his normal physique and whose consciousness had become so powerful that it could almost rival an ordinary Yuan Ying stage cultivator, slowly woke up.

"Jiang Nian'er, you residents of Fan Yong Island occupy a place favored by the heavens, yet you do not strive to improve yourselves and neglect the heavenly blessings. Therefore, the heavens have sent down a calamity to bring you this tribulation."

"Considering the heavens' virtue of cherishing life, and seeing that you are truly pitiful, I have lent a helping hand to save you."

Li Fan's voice was like thunder, and his divine sense poured directly into the other's mind.

He deeply engraved his words like a brand.

"Thank you for your grace, Immortal Master, thank you for your grace..."

Soon, Jiang Nian'er, who had returned to normal, wept bitterly and kept kowtowing on the ground.

"I only wish to follow by the Immortal Master's side from now on, serving as an ox or horse, to repay the kindness of giving me a second life..."

Jiang Nian'er, whose mind had been easily brainwashed by Li Fan despite being a combination of more than 100,000 mortal consciousnesses, said sincerely.

Li Fan did not respond, but only said solemnly, "This is a tribulation, but also a great opportunity."

"You want to become my disciple..."

"You are not qualified yet. For now, just follow by my side and be an acolyte. If your future performance satisfies me..."

Li Fan said indifferently.

Jiang Nian'er kowtowed to Li Fan again, not daring to raise his head.

"He is one of your tests!"

Li Fan pointed to the immobilized Foundation Establishment cultivator beside him.

"The cultivators of the world are divided into Qi Refinement, Foundation Establishment..."

After briefly introducing the cultivation system of the Xuan Huang Realm, Li Fan continued, "This person is a late-stage Foundation Establishment cultivator."

"You must defeat him with your own strength!"

"My patience is limited. I will only give you fifteen days."

Jiang Nian'er's body instantly stiffened.

How could he, a mere mortal, triumph over the power to fly, traverse the earth, and summon the wind and rain?

What could be accomplished in just fifteen days?

Li Fan saw Jiang Nian'er's trepidation and smiled, "Don't worry, I won't make you do something meaningless that will send you to your death."

Lightly tapping Jiang Nian'er's forehead, Li Fan transmitted a Golden Core cultivation technique and a set of basic formation techniques.

"With what I've taught you, it's enough for you to deal with him."

"Of course, if you can't digest these contents to some extent within fifteen days..."

"Then he will become the lucky one." Li Fan looked at the unknown Foundation Establishment cultivator and said with a smile.

Jiang Nian'er's heart tightened. He immediately sat cross-legged and began to comprehend the contents Li Fan had imparted.

"Absorbing the spiritual energy of heaven and earth to control qi..."

Starting from the most basic cultivation knowledge, a brand-new world of immortal cultivation gradually unfolded before Jiang Nian'er's eyes.

Although the strength of his divine sense was already almost on par with a Yuan Ying stage cultivator, Jiang Nian'er obviously still...

"No, no. I need to be faster, even faster."

It should be said that Jiang Nian'er was indeed the grand winner who emerged victorious among over a hundred thousand people.

He quickly learned not only to read ten lines at a glance but also to multitask.

While studying the introductory immortal cultivation technique, he also began to ponder formations.

Thus, three days later.

Li Fan sensed a fluctuation of spiritual energy from Jiang Nian'er.

"Refining qi in three days, it seems his aptitude is average."

Because the dark and mysterious power had already invaded, the mortal miasma on Jiang Nian'er's body had almost ceased to exist.

Although it saved the step of expelling the miasma from his body, completing the introduction of qi into the body in three days was by no means considered poor aptitude.

The so-called aptitude was not something Li Fan cared about in Jiang Nian'er in the first place. He didn't take it to heart.

Instead, he continued to observe his choices.

Not disappointing Li Fan, after successfully stepping into the Qi Refining stage and trying to cultivate for a period of time, he discovered that in the remaining twelve days, he absolutely could not complete the advancement.

Therefore, Jiang Nian'er used almost all his energy on researching the introductory formation bestowed by Li Fan.

The introductory formation taught by Li Fan, in terms of power alone, was more than enough to trap and kill a Foundation Establishment cultivator to death.

Moreover, it had undergone his special simplification, allowing Qi Refining cultivators to use it.

"As long as you're not too stupid and forget to accumulate the spiritual power needed to maintain the formation."

Li Fan revealed a trace of an inexplicable smile.

In fact, who won or lost between the two was not important to him at all.

Anyway, the winner would inherit the mysterious power and could not escape his palm, being used by him.

What Li Fan really wanted to observe were the specific changes that occurred when the mysterious rules took effect and the identity was devoured.

"The mysterious rules can actually also be regarded as the principles of heaven and earth."

"It's just that during the process of world fusion, due to the transition, the principles of heaven and earth that are not recognized by the Xuanhuang Realm's Heavenly Dao are born."

"We cultivators who possess the mysterious power will inevitably suffer the wrath of heaven."

"But this thing called the wrath of heaven..."

Li Fan shook his head slightly in his heart, he really wasn't afraid of it!

The fifteen days passed in a flash.

"The deadline has arrived, let's begin."

On this day, Li Fan suddenly spoke, breaking the tranquility on the island.

At the same time, he instantly released the restrictions on that Foundation Establishment cultivator.

The Foundation Establishment cultivator had always maintained his clarity, listening to the conversation between Li Fan and the other.

"Just a little Qi Refining cultivator who has only cultivated for a dozen days also wants to defeat me?"

"Such a disgrace, it must be washed away with your blood..."

His eyes turned red, and the moment he gained freedom, he summoned his Foundation Establishment magic item.

A straight wooden stick appeared in the sky, instantly locking onto Jiang Nian'er.

It was about to launch an attack and crush him.

However...

He suddenly found his vision blur, and the opponent's figure disappeared for no reason.

"What?" The Foundation Establishment cultivator was shocked.

He looked around and discovered that he had unknowingly appeared in a sea of bamboo.

The previous encounter on the desolate island seemed like his own illusion.

Outside the formation, Jiang Nian'er breathed a sigh of relief while struggling to maintain this [Bamboo Sea Dragon Trapping Formation].
