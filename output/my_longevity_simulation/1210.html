Chapter 1210

After surviving the catastrophe, Li Fan began to recall the terrifying power within the Infinite Mirror.

"Is this the reason for the damage to the Infinite Mirror?"

"The feeling it gives me even surpasses those few True Immortal seal characters."

"Although what I've seen so far are all imitations of True Immortal seal characters, what's in the Infinite Mirror are just remnants of the aftershocks."

"Could the tribulation in the Immortal Realm be related to this?"

Despite his myriad curiosities, Li Fan didn't want to delve into it for now.

The existence capable of destroying the Immortal Realm was definitely not something he could deal with at present.

"It's merely some remnants within the Infinite Mirror. Even if induced and released, it would at most envelop a city."

"It may have some use only at critical moments."

Li Fan pondered.

"No wonder such an esteemed Immortal Artifact ended up being occupied by the remnant soul of a Foundation Establishment cultivator. That annihilating tempest was truly too horrifying."

"An Immortal Artifact originally belonging to the Infinite Sect, located in the Xuanhuang Realm, actually underwent such changes..."

"Doesn't that indicate that this tribulation occurred in the Xuanhuang Realm?"

"If the aftermath alone could damage an Immortal Artifact, if it truly happened, it's impossible for there to be no traces left behind."

Li Fan's train of thought paused. He suddenly thought of two events that changed the historical trajectory of the Xuanhuang Realm, which might be related.

One was the mysterious disappearance of Xuantian King Xuanyuan Hong.

The second was the disappearance of the six top-level Eternal Life Realm cultivators who besieged Chuanfa, who at that time only had the cultivation of Harmonious Dao.

"The cultivation of these people had already stood at the pinnacle of their era."

"Within the Xuanhuang Realm, ordinary things couldn't harm them at all. Unless..."

Li Fan's heart stirred slightly.

"A power that transcends the mundane."

Li Fan recalled the Tiandu Great Master who came to the Xuanhuang Realm after the Great Tribulation and had been occupying the Dao Source State without moving ever since he was injured.

"Was his arrival back then really a coincidence?"

At this moment, Li Fan couldn't help but be filled with deep doubts.

This matter also served as a wake-up call for Li Fan.

Even if his perspective could already extend out of the Xuanhuang Realm and reach the other side of the starry sea, there were still existences within the Xuanhuang Realm that he couldn't see through and had yet to reach.

No matter what he did, he had to be extremely careful.

Amidst his surging thoughts, Sun Luyuan had already hurriedly returned.

He refined a large mass of essence blood and several materials, adding them into the essence blood pool.

The originally somewhat dim pool water once again became dazzling with flowing light.

It almost reverted back to the state before absorbing the fragments.

However, since it was more complete after all, Li Fan believed that after waiting for Sun Luyao to wake up, the situation would improve to a certain extent.

Moreover, Li Fan had also briefly glimpsed the more complete Hongmeng Jianfang Grand Formation. Putting aside perfect restoration, doing some repairs and touch-ups shouldn't be a problem.

Three days later, this Infinite Mirror Spirit Sun Luyao finally briefly woke up from his coma.

"Brother, I need some things."

After informing him of the materials needed to repair the formation, he fell unconscious again.

Sun Luyuan looked at the list of items his younger brother sent over and hurried off to gather them.

And during this time, fearing Sun Luyuan's retaliation if pushed too far, the Chen family of the Medicine Hall had been closely monitoring Sun Luyuan's movements.

However, Sun Luyuan seemed to have forgotten about this matter, coming and going as if busy with some urgent matter.

This left the Chen family somewhat puzzled.

Moreover, their backer, the Chuanfa Sect's Changzhe at the Immortal Alliance headquarters, had been out of contact.

So the Chen family also chose to hold their troops and not act.
