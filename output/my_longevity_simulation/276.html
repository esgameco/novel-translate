Chapter 276

Here is the full translation of the Chinese novel excerpt:

As if it were an insulator of spiritual energy and divine consciousness, Li Fan's various conventional methods were unable to probe this stone statue.

"Interesting, I wonder what kind of material it is."

Li Fan summoned a water blade and controlled it to swiftly slice towards the stone statue.

Let alone ordinary stones, even refined steel would be split in half by this water blade.

However, the water blade was unable to harm this incomplete stone statue in the slightest.

The moment it touched the statue, the spiritual energy maintaining the water blade's power scattered and dissipated.

Apart from washing the statue, it failed to cause any damage.

"What a treasure! It's a pity there's too little of it and it can't be brought back to reality."

"Otherwise, it could be used to create an absolutely safe secret chamber."

Li Fan imagined the many wonderful uses of this unknown stone material, but couldn't help feeling a bit regretful.

Suddenly, Li Fan found a stone on the ground nearby.

Using it as a tool, he forcefully chipped off another fragment from the stone statue.

He then fiercely stabbed this stone fragment into his own arm.

Blood immediately flowed.

Li Fan slightly frowned.

When spiritual energy circulated to the wound caused by the fragment, it could no longer flow freely.

It had to take a detour, greatly reducing efficiency.

Moreover, with the fragment blocking the wound, it could not be healed with spiritual energy unless removed.

After pondering for a moment, Li Fan endured the pain and dug out the fragment.

He played with it in his hand.

"Perhaps, there's a way to defeat Senior Brother Zhang without 'consuming' him."

He secretly thought.

While Li Fan was studying this miraculous stone statue, the battle in the sky had reached a white-hot stage.

Song Hesong revealed his true colors and joined Chu Liang and others in surrounding Zhang Qianmo.

The shockwaves from their clashes turned the city of Ningyuan below into ruins.

Countless houses collapsed, and who knows how many mortals died without knowing why.

Here at the small temple, with Li Fan's intervention, no danger occurred.

"Even without me, with the existence of this dilapidated stone statue, these children would probably be fine."

"No wonder they were still lively enough to organize rescue efforts after the great battle ended before."

Li Fan thought to himself.

The battle continued according to the established script.

Song Hesong was at a disadvantage. He slapped his hands towards Ningyuan City.

In an instant, countless mortals were killed or injured.

Their blood qi soared into the sky, forming the [Sentient Beings Refining God Formation], trapping Zhang Qianmo.

Song Hesong pressed his advantage, delivering several more palm strikes.

Each strike from the Golden Core cultivator took away the lives of tens of thousands of mortals.

One of the strikes happened to slap towards the small temple.

Li Fan's eyes flashed. He removed his protection and hid behind the remaining stone statue.

The palm force encountered the statue and dissipated without any effect.

Apart from where the statue blocked, everything else was reduced to ruins.

It was as if a vacuum of spiritual power had appeared, without arousing any notice from Song Hesong.

Li Fan stood up from behind the statue and casually cleared the surrounding debris.

He continued watching the battle in the sky.

After Zhang Qianmo broke through his predicament, entered the Purple Core state, and began slaughtering...

Li Fan thought for a bit, then pushed open the statue and entered the underground chamber.

The children inside were initially nervous seeing someone enter.

Realizing it was Li Fan, the "good person", they breathed a sigh of relief.

"Better to come in and hide. It's too terrifying outside." Li Fan sighed and shook his head.

Lu Ya grinned and didn't say much, just inviting Li Fan to sit down.

There was some stored water and food inside, evidently used as a frequent refuge.

Li Fan took this opportunity to chat with the blond-haired Lu Ya.

He learned that this temple was originally called "Xuantian Temple".

They had discovered this underground chamber by chance, it had existed long before.

As for who the temple enshrined, no one knew as it had been abandoned for many years.

Regarding where the missing half of the stone statue went, Lu Ya was even more clueless.

Just then, a little girl nearby interjected: "I know, I know! It's at Mr. Bai's house! Last time when Mr. Bai took me back to treat my illness, I saw it."

"Half a body stuck there, no face, really scary!"

Lu Ya was stunned: "You know where Mr. Bai's house is? Then why did you say you didn't know when I asked last time?"

The little girl blushed and glared at Lu Ya: "I went there, but I was sick and dazed, who remembers how to get there!"

"Mr. Bai..."

Hearing the conversation between the two children, Li Fan was deep in thought.

...

Above Ningyuan City, Zhang Qianmo, who had slain all the villains, was anxiously searching for his junior brother who had sent him a warning.

However, even after sweeping Ningyuan City several times with his divine sense, he couldn't find him.

Thinking his junior brother had met a tragic end, Zhang Qianmo was filled with remorse.

Unwilling to give up, he searched a few more times, wanting to at least find his junior brother's corpse.

Still no results.

Zhang Qianmo could only take the Longevity Fruit alone and left Ningyuan City in grief.

Not long after he left, at the ruins of the small temple, a petite figure peeked out.

After cautiously observing for a long time, the figure boldly shouted: "It's safe now, come out!"

Following that, Li Fan and a group of children crawled out from the underground chamber.

The children looked at Ningyuan City, now a ruins filled with corpses, and couldn't help feeling at a loss.

However, as orphans surviving alone, their mental resilience was very strong.

They were just momentarily at a loss, not knowing what to do.

"This Mr. Bai should be coming soon, right?"

With no other cultivators left in the city, Li Fan had no more worries.

He used his divine sense to search for traces of "Mr. Bai" among the survivors.

Based on the children's descriptions, it should be easy to find.

A young, gentle, and extremely good-looking scholarly image.

But...

Li Fan's expression changed slightly.

He didn't find any matching targets.

Li Fan searched a few more times, but the result remained the same.

"Could it be that the 'extremely good-looking' part was the children's own beautification in their minds?"

Li Fan couldn't help guessing so.

With no other choice, he could only wait at the small temple for Mr. Bai to arrive.

However, to Li Fan's surprise...

He waited until the duration of this Fallen Immortal Realm ended, but still didn't see this "Mr. Bai".

The surroundings became hazy. Just as Li Fan was about to leave the Fallen Immortal Realm...

He vaguely heard the children excitedly shouting: "Xiao Bai! Xiao Bai is here!"

"Mr. Bai! Mr. Bai!"
