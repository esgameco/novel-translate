Chapter 474

"Come in, come in." Han Yi, who was muttering to himself, was startled and reluctantly handed over a storage ring.

"There are two thousand low-grade spirit stones inside," Han Yi said somewhat painfully.

"Alright, follow me." Li Chenfeng, who had seen millions of contributions, had his horizons broadened and was already looking down on these eighteen hundred contribution points.

However, every bit counts, Li Chenfeng frowned and put away the storage ring.

Leading Han Yi all the way to the front of the Master of Foundation Establishment.

Seeing the elegant and imposing Foundation Establishment Master who was teaching, Han Yi was full of excitement.

Being cautious not to disturb, he sat down carefully and listened attentively to the Master's teachings.

Although it was a bit cloudy to him, he listened very seriously and did not miss a word.

After a lengthy discussion, Han Yi, like the other students, closed his eyes and began trying to establish his foundation.

The people on the island naturally did not know that the moment Han Yi set foot on the island, there was a pair of eyes that went through half of the misty clouds and landed on him.

"Han fellow Daoist, I have been waiting for you for a long time."

With the heavenly perspective locked on Li Chenfeng, Li Fan looked at Han Yi, who was meditating, with interest.

"Waiting for so long?"

"After breaking through the Foundation Establishment realm, does my fate depend on me, not the heavens?"

Li Fan carefully pondered the meaning behind these two sentences uttered by Han Yi.

Thinking back to his previous life, he had strange moments of both extraordinary luck and ordinary fortune.

An audacious guess emerged in Li Fan's mind.

"Could it be that Han Yi can actually control his own luck? And, after he establishes his foundation, the control over his luck will undergo a qualitative change?"

In an instant, Li Fan thought of the incarnation object in his immortal realm, the miraculous power of the celestial bird of destiny.

"So similar? Interesting!"

Staring intently at Han Yi. From the heavenly perspective, Li Fan saw a dark feather slowly forming in Han Yi's dantian.

"This is..."

No matter how he looked at it, this feather was so similar to the one that had once grown on his body.

"Even the Foundation Establishment miraculous object is like this. It seems there is no mistake, he is really connected to the celestial bird of destiny."

A thought stirred in Li Fan's mind.

If his previous conjecture was correct, the founder of the empire was Xu Ke. The revered master was the green phoenix bird from back then.

But what always puzzled Li Fan was that, according to Li Chenfeng's memories, there was never any trace of a mysterious bird in the empire.

Theoretically, the connection between the celestial bird of destiny and Xu Ke was much closer.

It didn't make sense for the green phoenix bird he picked up along the way always stayed by his side, while the celestial bird, his companion creature, disappeared.

If he could figure out the source of Han Yi's peculiar ability, perhaps he could find out where the celestial bird went back then.

...

As time passed slowly, just as the "Feather of the Celestial Bird" in Han Yi's dantian was about to completely form.

"Hahaha! I did it!"

Suddenly, a cultivator beside Han Yi stood up and exclaimed loudly.

Being influenced by it, Han Yi, who was concentrating, was somewhat affected, and the image of the black feather became diffuse again.

He furrowed his brows.

After calming down again, he attempted to condense his own Dao Foundation once more.

Another person successfully established their foundation in the courtyard, their aura surged, and they let out a long roar.

"Today I finally know who I am! Thank you, Master!"

That person laughed heartily, bowed to Zhu Shaotang, and then left in high spirits.

Han Yi's brows furrowed, and his Dao Foundation dispersed again.

"Ten years of hardship, success finally begins today. From now on, even though the sky and earth are vast, I, Nangong Qian, shall walk upon it." A scholar-looking cultivator shook his head, recited a poem, and then left with an ethereal demeanor.

In Han Yi's dantian, the imagery of the black feather of the celestial bird completely shattered.

"Is this the end?"

Vigorously spitting out a mouthful of blood, Han Yi angrily stood up and looked around.

But to his dismay, he found that he was the only one left in the courtyard.

With a look of pity, the Foundation Establishment Master looked at him and consoled, "Young man, don't be discouraged. Although you failed this time, I can see that success is not far off for you."

"I am sure you will succeed next time."

Upon hearing this, Han Yi, in a state of excitement, once again spat out a mouthful of blood.

"Next time you come, I'll only charge you one thousand contribution points!"

Han Yi opened his mouth as if he wanted to speak but hesitated.

Although he was filled with reluctance, faced with the Foundation Establishment Master who was far more powerful than him, there was nothing else he could do.

Bitterly swallowing the pill, Han Yi staggered as he flew away from the Foundation Establishment Island.

"Things couldn't get any worse for Han Yi."

"It seems that it's not yet time for his fortune to reverse."

Li Fan's eyes flickered slightly, quietly leaving the Ten Immortals Island, heading straight for Han Yi, who was locked on by an indeterminate killing intent.

Li Fan decided to directly use the soul-searching spell to see what secrets were hidden on Han Yi.

After coming out of the Foundation Establishment Island, Han Yi seemed to have suffered a great blow.

Lost without a goal or direction, he flew aimlessly.

When he suddenly came to his senses, he found himself in an unfamiliar sea area.

"Damn it, just a tiny bit short."

"This darn fate!"

With no one around, Han Yi pointed angrily at the sky, venting his frustration.

At this moment, the previously calm sea suddenly churned up huge waves.

As if something was emerging from the depths of the sea.

Han Yi stared at the surface of the water, his expression inexplicable.

After looking around, he immersed himself in the water.

Continuing downwards, deep into the sea bed.

He eventually discovered the source of the disturbance in the sea surface.

It turned out that within the ruins under the sea, a large hole had inexplicably appeared.

Countless seawater was sucked into the hole, forming a giant vortex.

Han Yi stared at the vortex, his expression constantly changing.

Finally, he gritted his teeth and was about to dive into the vortex.

At that moment...

"Fettering insect!"

He seemed to hear a faint sound in his ears.

Subsequently, Han Yi was horrified to find that he had completely lost control of his body and was unable to move at all!

An indistinct figure emerged behind him.

An extremely dangerous aura emanated from them, chilling Han Yi's heart.

As the person's hand was about to cover Han Yi's head...

Several golden lights suddenly shot out from above the sea surface.

The target was not Han Yi, but the shadow in front of him!

The golden lights were powerful, and the shadow had to dodge.

"Master Li, we've been looking for you so hard!"

Four black-clad cultivators, similarly concealing their figures, suddenly appeared, surrounding the two.
