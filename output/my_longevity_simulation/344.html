Chapter 344

"The Fallen Immortal Realm can be entered again. While there's still some time, we can explore it once more."

"However, before that, let's first take a look at the major events that have occurred in the past six months."

Knowing things in advance was his advantage. Every piece of valuable information could mean a great opportunity in the next life.

Li Fan naturally wouldn't neglect this. He immediately started searching in the Tianxuan Mirror.

Numerous strange events and stories flashed before his eyes.

However, two things caught his attention the most.

First, the old fisherman who hadn't appeared for a long time showed up again. This time, he stayed in Tianchen Province. But unlike in the past when he was alone, there was actually a seven or eight-year-old girl beside the old fisherman. Dressed like the old fisherman, wearing a straw raincoat, she carried the fish basket that the old fisherman usually carried on her back, which was almost as big as her. It looked quite comical.

Some bold cultivators tried to get close to this little fisherwoman, but they were scolded by her in an old-fashioned manner.

Although she appeared to be only at the Foundation Establishment stage, she used a tone of teaching juniors even when facing Nascent Soul cultivators.

It's worth noting that the old fisherman had wandered for a long time, appearing and disappearing mysteriously, and no one had ever been able to follow him.

This little girl immediately aroused the curiosity of many cultivators.

The cultivators speculated about the identity of this little fisherwoman, but there were various opinions and no conclusion was reached.

When Li Fan saw this news, for some reason, he suddenly thought of the Stealing the Heavens and Swapping the Sun Token he had traded to the old fisherman.

"Si Kong Yue?" Li Fan narrowed his eyes, guessing in his heart.

When he first obtained the Stealing the Heavens and Swapping the Sun Token and learned about the origin of that token, he had a vague guess.

The forger of this token, Si Kong Yue, the 38th generation inheritor of the Stealing the Heavens and Swapping the Sun Sect.

Perhaps through some secret method, she transformed herself into a token in order to seek survival in the extremely bloody era of the Great Darkness.

When later generations of cultivators tried to cultivate the Stealing the Heavens and Swapping the Sun Jue using the token, with both their body and soul merging with the token.

She could secretly absorb power and eventually achieve "stealing the heavens and swapping the sun" to resurrect.

So all along, Li Fan had been very wary of this token.

After obtaining it, he always left it in the corner of his storage ring and didn't provoke it.

When the old fisherman asked for this item, he handed it over without hesitation.

Now it seems that his premonition might have been correct.

"I just don't know what method the old fisherman used to awaken her. But after sleeping for thousands of years and finally being resurrected, only to be forced to act as a follower and little fisherwoman, I guess the feeling won't be pleasant." Li Fan thought to himself, and then pondered the second thing that he was very concerned about.

In the past year, there have been rumors spreading throughout the Myriad Immortal Alliance.

It is said that the Five Elders Association, which had been silent for a long time, made a move again.

A cultivator who specializes in the Dao of souls has reportedly quietly infiltrated the Myriad Immortal Alliance.

This cultivator's cultivation is unfathomable, and it is said that he was already at the Harmonious Dao realm a thousand years ago.

Moreover, he has abandoned his physical body and only exists as a soul.

His soul is so powerful that it is no different from an ordinary entity.

He can even freely enter the bodies of other cultivators, perform possession, and replace them.

Not long ago, a tragic case occurred in Lietu Province.

The garrison of Zhupi City suddenly had a drastic change in personality and slaughtered all the mortals under his rule.

He even falsely reported that "there was an infiltration by spies who committed a bloody case" and used the excuse of "investigating the scene details" to lure dozens of cultivators from the Martial Hall of Lietu Province into Zhupi City.

He then erupted with strength completely inconsistent with his original self, annihilating the Martial Hall team led by Nascent Soul cultivators in an instant.

Before the cultivators of Lietu Province could react, he left with his head held high.

Leaving only a city of blood, shocking and angering the cultivators who rushed there later.

After this, more than a dozen incidents occurred where cultivators suddenly had drastic changes in personality and then committed wanton slaughter as if they didn't recognize their relatives.

But the culprit was never found.

People connected these tragic cases with the previous news of a Harmonious Dao Immortal from the Five Elders Association infiltrating.

Almost everyone believed that the reason those cultivators had drastic changes in personality was that they were possessed by the Harmonious Dao Immortal from the Five Elders Association.

Moreover, as the slaughter incidents continued to occur, the cultivators of the Myriad Immortal Alliance also developed a sense of fear.

After all, no one could guarantee that they wouldn't become the next target of possession.

They also had to worry about their close friends suddenly stabbing them in the back.

As negative emotions accumulated, the result was that the voices criticizing the Martial Hall for being incompetent grew louder and louder.

And the calls for the Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation to be popularized as soon as possible also became stronger and stronger.

The effect of the Soul-Locking Formation has been clearly demonstrated in Jiushan Province.

Although the Myriad Immortal Alliance rarely announced an explanation that the light beam lock-on of the Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation is only applicable to cultivators below the Harmonious Dao realm.

But in the eyes of the cultivators, having an effect is always better than having no countermeasures at all and just passively waiting to be slaughtered.

Besides, formations can be improved and strengthened.

If it can't deal with Harmonious Dao cultivators now, it should be possible after continuous improvements, right?

In short, due to the existence of this Five Elders Association spy called the "Thousand-Faced Demon Venerable" by the cultivators of the Myriad Immortal Alliance, the public's attitude towards the popularization of the Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation.

It changed from initial opposition to later acquiescence, and then to the current hope and support.

The popularization of the Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation has now become an unstoppable trend.

Not only is the official attitude resolute, but the masses are also eagerly welcoming it.

Although there were some dissonant opposing voices in the face of this overwhelming wave.

But they were resolutely suppressed before attracting attention.

At the same time, the incompetence of the Martial Hall in the face of the invasion by the Thousand-Faced Demon Venerable also aroused considerable dissatisfaction among many cultivators.

Some cultivators believed that the current Martial Hall could no longer adapt to the increasingly fierce struggle with the Five Elders Association.

It would be better to follow the example of the existence of the Central Hinterland Guard Academy and re-establish a new military organization to protect the safety within the Myriad Immortal Alliance.

This idea also received a very strong call for support.

The Myriad Immortal Alliance officials also issued a statement saying that they would fully consider and respect the opinions of the vast number of cultivators.

Relevant matters are under negotiation, and specific measures will be announced soon.

The Myriad Immortal Alliance's rare active response made many cultivators excited.

They believed that this change might be a sign of good days to come.

But cultivators who truly glimpsed some clues, such as Li Fan at this moment, could only remain silent in the cold of their hearts.

"It's really a wonderful move."

"The appearance of the Thousand-Faced Demon Venerable not only greatly accelerated the popularization of the Tianxuan Soul-Locking Formation."

"Even after the formation is fully established, it will have wonderful uses."

"There will always be a day when the spies are all caught."
