Chapter 1072: How Easily Vows Change

"Back then, I was a homeless orphan, with clothes that couldn't cover my body and not enough food to fill my stomach. Every day I struggled between life and death, living in constant fear and uncertainty," said Transmitter Feng, his voice echoing in the circular conference hall, his face full of reminiscence.

"Fortunately, after joining the ranks of the Transmitters, my fate changed from then on, and I truly understood the meaning of life."

Feng then vividly described the brutal battles with the Ten Immortal Sects back in the day, and the careful hiding during the Great Calamity. He spoke of the excitement when the Transmitting Celestial Emperor reappeared thousands of years later and rebuilt the Myriad Immortal Alliance on the ruins of the cultivation world.

His words seemed to transport the Transmitters present back to that turbulent era.

Finally, Transmitter Feng declared resolutely, "I, Feng Rui, will forever be loyal to the Transmitting Celestial Emperor and always maintain loyalty to the Myriad Immortal Alliance."

"In life after life, I will never have a second heart!"

"Just like back then!"

Feng didn't play any ambiguous word games, but stated straightforwardly.

Above the circular conference hall, the countless phantoms of the martyrs all emitted a faint glow, shining upon Feng Rui.

Feng Rui held his head high, proudly accepting the scrutiny of everyone present. As if this was a supreme honor.

All the Transmitters present, including Jiang, were inexplicably moved.

They knew that Feng Rui had already passed the test of the Transmitter's Oath.

Zhou, Wei, and Huang glanced imperceptibly at Li Fan, whose expression was solemn.

Feng Rui returned from the center of the hall, and the Transmitters applauded thunderously to show their respect and congratulations.

Next, the other Transmitters who were detected to have the Profound Heaven Sect's techniques took turns to speak on stage.

Without exception, they all perfectly passed the review of the Transmitter's Oath.

"Fellow Daoist, what is going on?"

"Could it be that from beginning to end, you were fabricating lies to deceive us?" Wei asked loudly in front of the Transmitters, his face somewhat unsightly.

This sentence seemed to be an angry outburst after being deceived. In fact, it was to shift the contradiction and draw a clear line.

The Immortal Puppet Li Fan's expression remained unchanged.

At this moment, the situation in the circular conference hall was somewhat unexpected. It had already exceeded the scope of what he could handle. He was waiting for the decision of his true self.

Li Fan, who was remotely controlling from the Lingmu Realm, thought for a moment, but in the end, he still did not take out the footage recorded by the formation at that time - Moru Bin possessing Feng Rui's body.

Because the other party could directly use a sentence "the recorded footage is forged" to turn the tables.

After all, now that Feng Rui had passed the test of the Transmitter's Oath, it had already legally verified his identity as a Transmitter.

"This Feng Rui is actually truly loyal to the Transmitting Sect from the bottom of his heart..."

"There are two possibilities for this situation to occur."

"Either the Transmitting Celestial Emperor is actually the Profound Heaven King. The Myriad Immortal Alliance and the former Profound Heaven Sect are two sides of the same coin. Naturally, Moru Bin can openly say all this."

"Or..."

"What exactly is the truth?" Li Fan's true self pondered rapidly.

Scenes of past interactions with Moru Bin surfaced in his mind once again.

Although it was playacting, some meanings between the words were still extremely difficult to conceal and change.

Li Fan suddenly made a decision.

"If I guessed wrong, it would just be wasting an Immortal Puppet."
