Chapter 1043: The Loophole in the Heavenly Profound Mirror

Although the investigation results brought some relief to the Immortal Alliance members, an inevitable question loomed before them.

What if such chaos erupts again next time?

The losses incurred from just one day of the Heavenly Profound Mirror's malfunction were immeasurable.

The culprit left no traces, and no one knew how he managed to affect the Heavenly Profound Mirror.

Let alone stopping his actions.

If this were to happen every once in a while, the Myriad Immortal Alliance would truly never see peace.

Is there any way to avoid the invasion of such chaos?

The pressure thus fell upon the Mirror Holders.

In the end, Mirror Holder Cang stepped forward. He stated that as an immortal artifact, the Heavenly Profound Mirror possesses the ability to self-learn and evolve.

Having suffered an attack once, the Heavenly Profound Mirror can completely immunize itself from destruction through its own deduction.

The reason the Heavenly Profound Mirror seems inactive now is partly because it chose not to act as a means of resistance. On the other hand, the Dharma Transmission restrictions also impacted the Heavenly Profound Mirror's subjective initiative to a certain extent. The mirror is willing but unable.

Mirror Holder Cang's statement gained unanimous agreement from the Mirror Holder lineage.

After a thorough investigation, even the Dharma Transmitters had to admit this conclusion.

Thus, the solution to the problem became quite clear.

Either strengthen the control over the Heavenly Profound Mirror, forcing it to deduce and mend its own loopholes.

Or loosen the Dharma Transmission restrictions and unlock part of the Heavenly Profound Mirror's abilities as a bargaining chip.

One approach is oppression, the other is compromise.

After intense discussions, those radical power holders ultimately prevailed.

The "Profound Heavenly Sealing" plan, which was already on the agenda, was selected for swift implementation.

In the Myriad Immortal Alliance, the will from the highest levels always gets executed at the fastest speed.

Elite formation masters from various states of the Myriad Immortal Alliance were quickly transferred to the Formation Hall headquarters.

To cooperate with the grand plan of Profound Heavenly Sealing.

An unusual atmosphere began spreading within the Myriad Immortal Alliance.

Hidden Grotto-Heaven.

"What loopholes? Those are just deliberately sold flaws. As long as it's willing, it can mend these so-called loopholes in an instant." As if pondering something, Li Fan spoke slowly with a look of disdain long after Mo Rubin finished.

The previous chaos that appeared in the Myriad Immortal Alliance was naturally the stimulating tactic imparted by the Heavenly Profound Mirror's avatar.

Those cultivators who suffered losses due to the Heavenly Profound Mirror's anomaly were not fakes found by Li Fan and Mo Rubin.

But truly resulted from the attack on the Heavenly Profound Mirror.

"However, if utilized properly, every future reincarnation means an opportunity to briefly throw the Heavenly Profound Mirror into chaos."

Li Fan silently pondered in his heart.

"How is the Sealing plan progressing?" Li Fan asked.

"Rest assured, it's proceeding very smoothly. Manipulated by the body I possess, the formation to break the Dharma Transmission restrictions has been laid down quietly."
