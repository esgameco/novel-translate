Chapter 856

"Fish bite the bait because they want to eat. What do these orbs of light in the endless starry sky seek?"

Sitting atop the boundless starry sky, Ji Shaoli pondered this question.

"From the fleeting glimpses, it can be seen that within these orbs of light, there should be items, cultivation techniques, divine abilities, combat experiences, and the like."

"..."

"This fishing pool may be a celestial tool with a certain threshold, specifically designed to distribute rewards."

As his gaze swept over the white, cyan, blue, purple, and orange orbs of light, Ji Shaoli was deep in thought.

"The more valuable the things are, the higher the level they belong to. Therefore, the more difficult it is to attract them."

"But now, I only have my consciousness remaining. Even if I want to fish up those white orbs, I can only rely on luck. Let alone the higher-level orbs."

Despite trying various methods, Ji Shaoli still couldn't add "bait" to the dangling fishing line.

And his luck was really bad.

After a long time, not even a single white orb was attracted by him.

And in this starry sky, there seemed to be a time limit as well.

The surrounding starlight grew dimmer, and after layers of ripples dissipated, Ji Shaoli returned to the real world.

This time, the fishing ended with an empty rod!

Feeling dissatisfied, Ji Shaoli immediately prepared to cast another line. However, he recalled that there was only one chance per day to trigger the power of the fishing starry sky.

He could only take a deep breath and wait for tomorrow to come.

In the next ten days, Ji Shaoli participated in fishing once a day.

He only fished up a total of six orbs.

Five white and one blue.

The white orbs contained only things like ores and spiritual herbs.

Nothing of great value.

However, the blue orb opened to reveal a jade slip.

"'Taikang Geng Jin Sword Qi'?"

Ji Shaoli carefully read the contents of the jade slip.

"As expected, it's not a product of the Xuanhuang Realm."

This "Taikang Geng Jin Sword Qi" came from the Sword God Sect of the Taiyi Realm.

The situation in the Taiyi Realm was roughly the same as in the Xuanhuang Realm.

However, there were five great cultivators who had cultivated to the pinnacle but did not ascend to the Immortal Realm. Instead, they transformed their bodies into the Dao.

They merged with the Heavenly Dao of the Taiyi Realm and were called the "Five Taiyi Emperors" by the cultivators of the Taiyi Realm.

Taikang was one of these five emperors.

"How is this only blue quality?"

After reading through the contents of the jade slip, Ji Shaoli couldn't help but be a little surprised.

When he tried to cultivate this "Taikang Geng Jin Sword Qi," he finally realized.

It's all about adapting to local conditions.

This sword art was a cultivation technique specifically created according to the specific conditions of the Taiyi Realm. The necessary condition for cultivation was the power of Emperor Taikang.

However, there was no such thing as "Taikang" in the Xuanhuang Realm.

So naturally, this cultivation technique couldn't be practiced.

If it weren't for its quality of being in line with the Dao, it would probably be classified as white.

"This fishing platform even re-evaluates the value of items based on the environment?" Ji Shaoli couldn't help but be amazed.

Although he had almost no gains, Ji Shaoli was not angry.

He had observed Zhong Shentong's fishing process over the past ten days.

After all, Zhong Shentong had been in contact with this fishing platform earlier and seemed to have grasped some tricks.

Out of ten chances, not a single cast was empty.

Although it was only eight white and two blue, it was still much better than Ji Shaoli!

After some contemplation, Ji Shaoli decided not to waste any more time.

On this day, after Zhong Shentong had eaten and drunk his fill, he was about to go for his afternoon nap.

But suddenly, his face changed.

A golden light shield enveloped the entire small temple, including the fishing pond.

Countless golden snake-like thunderbolts were forming in the sky above.

As if they were about to strike down at any moment.

"Master!" The young disciple washing the dishes was shocked and lost color.

"Liuli, quickly come behind me!" Zhong Shentong grabbed the young disciple and protected him behind his back.

At the same time, he took out a command flag and inserted it into the ground.

Earthy yellow energy formed a spherical shield, enveloping the two of them.

"There may be some misunderstanding in this!"

Zhong Shentong said loudly.

But Ji Shaoli saw clearly that although he said this, he had already quietly taken out a small wooden sword.

Even though his realm was higher than the other party's, Ji Shaoli still felt a trace of fatal crisis from this small sword.

Ji Shaoli did not show himself, but only said coldly: "Daoist Zhong, if you don't want the matter of the fishing pond to be known to the world."

"I advise you not to resist!"

"Actually, I don't have any malicious intent either."

"It's just that as an angler, I'm equally curious about it!"

Upon hearing this, Zhong Shentong completely lost his composure.

His biggest secret had been exposed, and Zhong Shentong's heart was in a panic.

His face kept changing expressions, but in the end, he still put away the wooden sword.

"Since that's the case, please come out and meet me! What's the point of hiding?" He said in a deep voice.

"Hehe, Daoist Zhong, you don't know how many treasures you've gotten from that fishing pond. You have to be careful." Ji Shaoli was not moved by his words.

Instead, he asked about the method to fish up higher quality light spheres.

"Don't even think about deceiving me, I know everything about your gains in these ten days." Ji Shaoli said in a playful tone.

Zhong Shentong's heart completely sank.

The other party had been watching him for so many days.

And he was completely unaware of it.

It was enough to say that the other party's strength must far exceed his own.

His strength was already overwhelming, yet he was still so cautious...

Zhong Shentong couldn't help but curse inwardly.

Left with no choice, he could only tell the insights he had summarized himself.

"On the fishing platform, the more times you cast the rod, the stronger the connection with it."

"Perhaps there were other ways to directly increase the connection in the past. But now, we can only rely on the number of times and time to grind it out."

"The light spheres seem to have spirituality and can sense the contents of the bait in the intention."

...

Zhong Shentong honestly confessed.

Ji Shaoli listened carefully while discerning the truth and falsehood in his words.

"You don't have to worry about me deceiving you, you'll know when you try it yourself."

"Since the day I discovered the secret of this fishing pond, I knew that I couldn't monopolize it alone forever."

Zhong Shentong sighed deeply.

"It's just a pity that this fishing pond seems to be integrated with heaven and earth, fixed in this place. It can't be moved or taken away at all."

"Otherwise..."

Zhong Shentong said helplessly.

"I only hope that you will spare Liuli's life. If you want to kill, just kill me."

At this time, Ji Shaoli finally showed his figure.

"Hehe, Daoist Zhong, you should put away your magic weapon."

"I don't intend to monopolize the secret here either."

Zhong Shentong heard this and showed a suspicious look on his face.
