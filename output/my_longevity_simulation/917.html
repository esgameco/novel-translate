Chapter 917

The Sword Heavenly Cave is different from ordinary evolved heavenly caves.

Inside, there are no mountains, rivers, or lakes, only a scene extremely similar to the inside of this sword tomb.

Thousands of swords are stuck in the ground in a chaotic and disorderly manner.

Sword energy crisscrosses and flows, forming gusts of howling sword wind.

The entire heavenly cave is a world that completely belongs to swords. It cannot tolerate any other living things.

In the sword tomb, outside the Sword Heavenly Cave, Li Qing's avatar, which had originally been smashed to pieces, slowly reappeared with a squirming of flesh and blood.

"Early Yuan Ying stage." He sensed his own cultivation level and nodded slightly.

His realm was slightly weaker than his true body. Although his actual combat power was also incomparable, after all, he had built his foundation with the Heaven's Wonder, so crushing ordinary Hua Shen cultivators was not a problem.

However, after entering the Yuan Ying stage, Li Qing felt a subtle resistance to moving forward.

When the avatar bid farewell to the wooden sword Heaven's Wonder and returned to the Xuanhuang Realm,

That hidden malice became more obvious.

A passage of words appeared unbidden in Li Qing's mind.

"The Heavenly Venerate's new method is truly going against the heavens. The more one receives the grace of heaven and earth, the more difficult it will be to go against the principles of heaven and earth in the future."

"Even when sacrificing the souls of heaven and earth, one will suffer multiple times the backlash from the will of heaven and earth. No matter how high one's talent is, they will basically be limited to the Hua Shen stage."

Perhaps it was because the birth of this black sword also had a little contribution from Li Qing. When he devoured it and turned it into a heavenly cave, he did not sense too much resistance.

But he had a premonition that this was already the limit of what heaven and earth could tolerate.

When he wanted to go a step further, to advance to Hua Shen and even merge with the Dao, he would need to completely face the malice of heaven and earth.

However, Li Qing was not very concerned.

After all, as an avatar, his mission was not to cultivate, but to go to the Five Elders Council, to the place of [One Heart Heavenly Venerate], to retrieve the lost Xuan Tian Sect techniques.

After checking the information transmitted from his true body, due to the previous inexplicable spatial fluctuation, the Myriad Immortals Alliance and the Five Elders Council were now in a rare truce period.

Li Qing slightly discerned the direction and then transformed into a black light, quickly speeding towards his destination.

As he flew, Li Qing immediately felt that it was different from before.

When his true body moved through the air, he needed to overcome the resistance between heaven and earth, as well as the head-on sword winds.

But Li Qing's body transformed into sword light, like a fish in water, perfectly blending with the surrounding environment.

Not only could the resistance be almost ignored, the consumption of flying was also astonishingly small!

"The Heaven's Wonder originally belonged to the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao. Therefore, it naturally has preferential treatment."

Flying was no longer a laborious task, but instead became a kind of enjoyment.

Li Qing felt extremely refreshed in his heart. If he wasn't afraid of attracting the attention of the Myriad Immortals Alliance, he would have to let out a long whistle to vent.

The speed of the black sword light became faster and faster, flashing across the sky.

Even if someone caught a glimpse of it inadvertently, they would only think it was their own illusion.

In front of the white fog barrier spanning heaven and earth, a squad of Myriad Immortals Alliance's Martial Arts Hall cultivators were stationed.

Li Qing did not alarm them. Relying on the information transmitted from his true body, he easily avoided the detection of the Immortal Alliance's protective formation and disappeared into the white fog.

As the buffer state between the Five Elders Council and the Myriad Immortals Alliance, the Qiwei State did not have the power of any Heavenly Venerate other than the Carefree Heavenly Venerate involved.
