"As far as I know, in fact, the Lord of the Secret Envoys was forced to accept the role of acting island lord of the Wanxian Island. Tasked with rebuilding the Immortal City, it was like a duck being forced onto a perch, a necessary but reluctant matter," Gao Yuan suddenly revealed.

He Zhenghao was surprised, asking, "Is that so?"

Gao Yuan nodded, "Although I don't know all the details, fortunately, the Lord of the Secret Envoys eventually completed this mission."

"By the way, he also changed the fate of many of us."

"Now, although he holds the position of the Secret Envoy and oversees the political affairs of the entire region of Congyunhai, I speculate that he may not actually be interested in managing every little thing personally."

"I also believe that with the Lord's extraordinary talents, wasting time on such mundane matters would be a true waste."

"He needs a bigger stage. As for these trivial matters, there are naturally people below to handle them."

"What he needs are individuals who can handle everything efficiently in his absence, without him needing to worry too much."

"Perhaps there won't be any change in our positions, but in his heart, we will definitely be labeled as incompetent."

Gao Yuan spoke solemnly.

He Zhenghao was startled, "Really?"

Gao Yuan coldly hummed, "I don't know if you noticed the way the Lord looks at us."

"The look in his eyes, what about it?" He Zhenghao was somewhat puzzled and quickly asked for clarification.

"Although the Lord may appear friendly on the surface and sometimes even shows respects to his subordinates, in reality..."

A trace of horror flashed in Gao Yuan's eyes.

"When he looks at all of us, the gaze from deep within his eyes is the same."

"It's as if we, all of us cultivators, are like ants in his eyes..."

"No, that's not it..." Gao Yuan shook his head, frowning, seemingly searching for the right description.

After a moment, he suddenly pointed at the tables and chairs in the council chamber, saying in a deep voice, "That's right. In his eyes, all of us are like these, merely tools."

"It's as though we're not in the same realm of existence. To him, we are all just simple tools."

"When I was still standing guard at the entrance of the Ministry of Government Affairs, I was fortunate enough to see a True Dao Cultivator."

"But even if it was a True Dao Immortal, the look in his eyes towards me was not as indifferent as the Lord's..."

"It is said that the Lord is a cultivator from the ancient Tianji Sect. Due to some reason, he has been in deep sleep until now, and his cultivation has fallen to the Golden Core realm."

"There were rumors before of the Lord killing several Nascent Soul cultivators with his Golden Core cultivation."

"So, what was his cultivation level during the ancient peak period?"

Gao Yuan's voice grew quieter, and a hint of fear flashed in his eyes.

He Zhenghao couldn't help but shudder.

The implications in Gao Yuan's words were becoming obvious.

He suspected that in the ancient times, Li Fan was actually a Longevity Realm cultivator.

Because it was widely known that once they entered the Longevity Realm, they truly achieved transcendence.

The nature of life also changed, making them entirely different from cultivators in realms like Harmonization, Nascent Soul, or Divine Transformation.

Perhaps only a Longevity Supreme could view the world's beings indifferently and equally.

Once accepting this viewpoint and combining it with Li Fan's shocking abilities in the past, everything suddenly seemed logical.

There are countless cultivators proficient in divination arts in the world, but it has never been heard of anyone like Li Fan, effortlessly using it for profit and doubling assets.

If the divination art were truly so magical, then all the cultivators in the world would be fighting to learn it.

For thousands of years, the divination arts have been lukewarm for a reason.

With the greedy nature of cultivators who seize everything they can, there is no reason for them not to take advantage.

Perhaps only a Longevity Supreme who had perfected the divination arts could easily foresee the future.

In the underground rumor mill of Congyunhai, there were stories of Wanxian Island suddenly being sealed off, and then the Five Elders Association raiding and seizing the entire island.

All of this was due to a prophecy.

Although it was said that the renowned Gong Sun Qixuan derived the prophecy.

Furthermore, if Li Fan was once a Longevity Supreme, then his slaying of Nascent Soul cultivators with a Golden Core cultivation, and using a puppet with Divine Transformation cultivation as a mount...

All of this would not matter.

If they couldn't do these things, would they still be called Longevity Supremes?

...

In the council hall of the Ministry of Government Affairs, there was an absolute silence for a while.

A strange atmosphere was brewing between He Zhenghao and Gao Yuan.

The more they thought about it, the more they felt that these speculations were reasonable and well-founded.

Thus, their bodies began to tremble uncontrollably.

One was due to the inherent fear of powerful beings of the Longevity Realm.

The other was because...

Being taken as a confidant by a Longevity Supreme, even if temporarily, even just outwardly...

It was an absolute honor.

With their thoughts fluctuating, the two men imagined a lot and could not calm down for a long time.

Eventually, He Zhenghao broke the silence.

He cleared his throat and said softly, "Brother Gao, this matter is all your speculation."

"It's best not to easily speak of it to outsiders."

"Only you and I know."

Gao Yuan glanced at He Zhenghao and nodded faintly, "I am well aware of this matter."

The two men exchanged a smile, all understood without words.

There was still one superficial phrase they didn't say.

If Li Fan really was a Longevity Supreme, then serving him now.

Once his injuries are fully healed and his cultivation is restored...

By that time, their stage would no longer be this small corner of Congyunhai.

He Zhenghao also thought of his wife who had long extinguished the flame of ambition in her eyes, feeling it faintly reignite.

Deep within the Ministry of Government Affairs.

Li Fan withdrew his attention, summoning a water mirror.

He touched his chin and gazed at his reflection in the mirror.

Eyes that indifferent look at everything...

He stared blankly at his own eyes.
