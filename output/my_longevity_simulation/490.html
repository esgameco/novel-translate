Chapter 490

The wailing from outside grew louder, and Jiao Xiuyuan, who had received contributions, said to Li Fan in a deep voice. [Website address]

With that said, a new branch suddenly extended from his palm.

A single green leaf, standing tall and proud on the branch.

Upon hearing this, Li Fan's consciousness split in an instant and flew towards the branch in Jiao Xiuyuan's hand.

As he got closer, he sensed an inexplicable attraction coming from the green leaf on the branch.

Unable to control himself, he plunged into it.

His vision gradually became clear, and in a moment, Li Fan felt that besides his main body and clone, there was another body.

Fortunately, he had the secret art of transformation, accustomed to multitasking. Otherwise, it might have been difficult to adjust.

Controlling the puppet body wasn't much different from his clone technique in terms of sensation.

He could clearly perceive the gap between consciousness and physical body, unlike his clone which felt more unified.

It was more like "driving" rather than inhabiting a living body.

"The transaction is complete. This body is now yours to dispose of, Daoist friend."

"As long as you don't intentionally do anything disgusting, even if you destroy it, it's fine."

"Oh, when the puppet body is damaged, to prevent the attached consciousness from being captured, it will activate a forced destruction measure. You might experience a bit of soul shock then..."

Jiao Xiuyuan's voice resounded in Li Fan's mind. [Website address]

Before Li Fan could respond, the voice faded, becoming barely audible.

As Li Fan familiarized himself with the puppet's body, his main body hurried towards the remembered location of Qingfeng Hall.

At this moment, the entire Wanxian Island had been transported to the sky in front of a purple gate.

Being continuously swallowed by the teleportation array, it entered the territory of the Five Elders' association.

The cultivators who had attempted to break through the blockade due to their numbers had all been wiped out.

Under the lesson of bloodshed, the remaining cultivators sat despondently on the ground, waiting for the unknown fate.

Li Fan strode into the inconspicuous shop of Qingfeng Hall.

The place was now empty, devoid of the messy old man.

Calling out a few times yielded no response.

After a moment of contemplation, Li Fan took out a pile of high-grade spirit stones from his storage ring and placed them on the floor of Qingfeng Hall.

"Will these be enough to buy a way out?," Li Fan tentatively asked aloud.

After a long silence, there was still no response.

Li Fan added another pile of valuable spirit stones.

Altogether, the value was close to one million contributions.

This time, the seemingly deserted Qingfeng Hall finally reacted.

A green ripple floated in the air, forming a familiar green gate. [Website address]

Feeling a stir in his heart, Li Fan stepped into it.

In the blink of an eye, he appeared in the same space he had visited before.

The ancient tree mentioned by Jiao Xiuyuan stood right in front of Li Fan.

However, the human face on the tree had disappeared, making it look like an ordinary tree.

"Daoist friend, you are quite generous."

Just as Li Fan was about to inquire, a crisp female voice suddenly sounded.

"Awu, since you have received benefits from the other party, help him leave here."

"Alright, Miss." A human face suddenly appeared on the ancient tree, glanced at Li Fan, and said indifferently.

"Guest, please come in." The ancient tree opened a passage with its huge mouth.

"Thank you, miss!" Without hesitation, Li Fan bowed towards the direction of the voice, and then walked into the entrance on the ancient tree.

As Li Fan's figure disappeared, the ancient tree closed its mouth and revealed its appearance.

"Miss..."

"Hehe, ancient cultivators speak in an amusing way."

In the air, a laughter as clear as silver bells rang out. The leaves of the ancient tree trembled slightly in response.

...

Feeling similar to using a long-distance teleportation array, the surroundings shifted from darkness to colorful splendor, then into full brightness.

After adjusting to the discomfort of his body, Li Fan looked around.

There were a few cultivators still in Qingfeng Hall, and the bustling sounds of the market in the distance were calm.

All these signs indicated that Li Fan was no longer in danger, no longer in the midst of the chaotic clouds and seas.

Casually exploring, Li Fan finally understood his current location.

Surprisingly, he had arrived in the heartland of the Ten Thousand Immortals League, near the Xuanwei Prefecture of the Tianyun Prefecture.

Apparently, news of Wanxian Island's fall had not yet reached here. For now, Li Fan shifted his attention back to the puppet body on Wanxian Island.

Amidst the despairing cries of Wanxian League cultivators, the island was completely engulfed by the purple teleportation array.

After a long period of darkness, when the sky lit up again, what appeared before everyone was a peculiar scene.

It had just been daytime, but after passing through the purple gate and arriving at the Five Elders' territory, it was already nighttime.

There was no moon in the sky, but countless shimmering stars twinkled, reflecting each other's brilliance.

Streams of starry rivers flowed, creating a dazzling and enchanting display.

For most present, it was their first time witnessing such a magnificent starry sky, with nine out of ten being dazzled and entranced by the sight.

The previously wailing Wanxian Island fell into a sudden silence.

"Welcome, Wanxian League members, to the Heavenly Realm of Human Dao."

A middle-aged cultivator dressed in white Daoist robes flew to the top of the group and spoke loudly.

"I understand that you may have deep misunderstandings about our Five Elders' association. However, it's okay. Soon, you will completely dispel these misunderstandings."

"By that time, I believe you will make the right choice to join our Five Elders' association."

The white-robed cultivator spoke with a smile.

Seeing that the Five Elders' association didn't seem as ferocious as rumored, everyone heaved a sigh of relief.

However, they were entirely skeptical of the "misunderstanding" mentioned by the white-robed cultivator.

"First of all, please leave this island," said the white-robed cultivator, and a group of burly figures flew out.

They wielded blades and wore heavy armor.

Instead of cultivators, they looked more like soldiers.

Sensing their aura, it was apparent that each of them had the cultivation of Yuanying.

The Wanxian League cultivators present all changed their expressions, unable to resist, they were escorted by them, leaving Wanxian Island.

"It seems they didn't come for me specifically but simply for Wanxian Island."

"It's odd, what's so special about Wanxian Island?"

Blending in the crowd, Li Fan watched the distant white-robed cultivator fly into Wanxian Island with a solemn expression.

Following that, dozens of powerful cultivators appeared one after another, entering the island.

Explosive roars were heard, and buildings on the island collapsed.

They seemed to be searching for something.
