Li Fan was one of them.

A black sun appeared in the sky of the Profound Yellow Realm, devouring everything.

Countless living beings wailed, but had no time to react at all.

They all disappeared into that endless darkness.

The Profound Yellow Realm shattered, and living beings were in misery.

This terrifying doomsday scene rushed into Wei Ziguan's mind in a torrent when he was caught off guard.

It was not an illusion.

The expressions on the faces of every cultivator facing imminent catastrophe were extremely real.

"Ahhhh!"

Wei Ziguan went mad.

He only had the cultivation of Golden Core realm and had cultivated for less than a hundred years. Having had a smooth journey all the way, when had he ever witnessed such a hellish scene?

Severing the probe and returning to reality, Wei Ziguan screamed uncontrollably.

He appeared to be in a state of madness, constantly mumbling about the impending end of the world.

Li Fan held an arrow in his hand and quietly studied it.

I don't know what material it was made of, it was extremely hard.
