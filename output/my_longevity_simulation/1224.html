Sun Luyuan fell silent for a moment, then whispered, "I plan to personally make contact with that so-called Holy Dynasty's Great Awakening."

"Huh?" Sun Luyao was stunned upon hearing this.

He knew his older brother had plans to make contact with the Holy Dynasty's Great Awakening, but previously he intended to send his trusted subordinates to scout out the situation first.

"Why the sudden change of mind?"

"Times have changed," Sun Luyuan said with a serious expression, "Who knows how long that old fool Fu will be in seclusion for. I want to take advantage of this rare free time to make plans for our future as brothers and for the Sun family."

Sun Luyuan's eyes turned cold and fierce: "Originally, I actually had some hesitation. But that seven-colored rainbow light that left the realm made me completely give up on the Alliance. In the Myriad Immortal Alliance, we will always just be tools to be used. If one day the Xuanhuang Realm faces a great calamity, those mirror holders and law transmitters will definitely not take us with them when they flee."

"The situation with the Great Awakening is different. As they say, there is no greater merit than following a dragon before it rises. If we pledge allegiance while they have yet to make a name for themselves, then once they succeed in the future, we will become the law transmitters of the Great Awakening."

"Of course, in the end it still depends on the actual strength of this Holy Dynasty's Great Awakening."

Sun Luyao nodded slightly.

"However, this Great Awakening's small world is extremely mysterious, with few clues even in the Heavenly Profound Mirror. Brother, how will you find them?"

Sun Luyuan smiled mysteriously: "I asked a friend to help divine their location. Although we couldn't pinpoint the exact spot, we did find the entrance to the small world. We'll know once we take a look in person."

"Brother, it won't be dangerous, right?" Sun Luyao suddenly felt a surge of unease in his heart.

Sun Luyuan was full of confidence: "Don't worry, with my cultivation level, even if that Great Awakening is a dragon's pool and tiger's den, I can still escape unscathed."

After reassuring his younger brother, Sun Luyuan continued: "Oh right, there's more good news. Ning Lu has made a breakthrough. She's at the late Nascent Soul stage, just one step away from Dao Unification."

"When she broke through, her Qi was in disorder, so I sensed that she still has another life force hidden inside her body."

"She was already pregnant with Lu'er's child before, but had some reservations and had been using a secret technique to seal it." Sun Luyuan said with a smile that wasn't quite a smile.

Seeing his younger brother's somewhat strange gaze, Sun Luyuan said solemnly: "I've already checked, it undoubtedly carries the Sun family bloodline. Regardless of Ning Lu's identity, as long as she can bear children for our Sun family, nothing else matters."

"I plan to have Ning Lu temporarily seal the life force of the fetus in her womb. After she reaches Dao Unification, she can give birth then." Sun Luyuan suddenly said.

"Lu'er's talent is extraordinary to begin with, and Ning Lu's aptitude is also quite good. The offspring of these two is definitely the most anticipated of the Sun family's younger generation."

"In the process of devouring the world's essence, cultivators gain the laws of heaven and earth. If the fetus in the mother's body can synchronize with it..."

There was some excitement in Sun Luyuan's tone: "I'm afraid once this little baby is born, its talent won't be inferior to any of the geniuses in the Immortal Alliance. Only then can our Sun family be considered to truly have a successor."

"The key is, now we have the ability to plan for obtaining a world essence for him."

When discussing the future of the Sun family, Sun Luyuan always seemed to have endless things to say.

His younger brother Sun Luyao also rarely calmed down to listen with a smile.
