Chapter 1006: The Fate of the Mirror Slave

Hearing these words, the two women immediately looked at Li Fan, their eyes filled with surprise and joy.

After all, after the transformation, Li Fan now occupied an extremely important position in their consciousness.

After a brief contemplation, Xuan Sushang immediately agreed. In front of Li Fan, she began to contact her father, the Mirror Holder [Cang].

She did not use any communication talisman, but took out a small Tianxuan mirror that she carried with her and chanted some incantations.

After a while, a black figure with an indistinguishable face was projected from the small mirror into mid-air.

"Nian'er, what do you need from me?"

Cang's voice sounded like a kind middle-aged uncle, and his tone was very gentle. As for [Nian Nian], it was Xuan Sushang's nickname.

Cang had always called Xuan Sushang this way, which was enough to show his doting love for his daughter.

Xuan Sushang was a little apprehensive as she recounted the whole story and Li Fan's request.

At this time, Cang finally noticed Li Fan, who had been standing silently beside his daughter.

Their gazes collided and intertwined in space.

Possessing the essence of the Tianxuan Mirror, Li Fan now understood what the Tianxuan Mirror meant by [mirror slave].

In order to verify his guess, Li Fan visualized in his mind the image of grabbing the Mirror Holder Cang with his hand.

At the same time, Cang's blurry figure shook violently.

But under Li Fan's intentional control, Cang could not even make half a sound of pain.

"Father, what's wrong with you?" Xuan Sushang quickly noticed Cang's abnormality and was a little puzzled.

Feeling the desperate plea for mercy from Cang, Li Fan then released the imaginary hand.

Only then was Cang freed from the boundless pain.

His acting was also very competent, and his tone did not waver in the slightest: "It's nothing. It's just some protective measures to block the prying eyes of the wicked. You and Lu Xichan retreat for now, your father has important matters to discuss with..."

"This lord." Cang paused and said very solemnly.

Xuan Sushang and Lu Xichan subconsciously looked at Li Fan, and after receiving his nod, Xuan Sushang supported Lu Xichan and slowly left the bamboo building.

Li Fan casually set up a formation to isolate the inside from the outside.

"This old slave pays respect to the master." Cang now revealed his true appearance and knelt down slowly.

His face was full of trepidation, and he did not look at all like a Mirror Holder standing at the pinnacle of the Wanxian Alliance's power.

Cang and Xuan Sushang, the father and daughter, had some similarities in their appearance.

They both had the characteristic of "youth" that did not match their voices.

Cang's voice sounded like a middle-aged man, but in fact he looked like a handsome young man in his early twenties.

Li Fan observed Cang in silence.

And Cang never dared to raise his head, always maintaining a kneeling posture.

With the help of the Tianxuan Mirror's essence, in Li Fan's perception, Cang was a completely dependent existence attached to the Tianxuan Mirror.

His soul, body, thoughts, everything he had, the cornerstone of his existence was the Tianxuan Mirror.

Even if it was not the real Tianxuan Mirror, but only contained a trace of the mirror's essence.

He could only let Li Fan knead and mold him as he pleased, without the slightest room for resistance.

Li Fan could even completely erase Cang from the world with a single thought.

...

"What a poisonous and desperate hand..."

"Becoming a mirror slave, although having to be under the control of the Tianxuan Mirror for a lifetime, one also gains considerable benefits." Li Fan stared at the mirror surface where Cang disappeared and thought as such.

For example, deduction, observation, vast knowledge, and so on.
