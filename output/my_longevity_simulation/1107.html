Chapter 1105: The Profound Yellow Life and Death Transformation

Despite their doubts, they still had great faith in Zhang Haobo, who had repeatedly led them through perilous storms and brought them back from the brink of death.

No one chose to turn back; they all obediently followed along and embarked on an unknown journey.

Observing this group of people slowly disappearing into the distance, Li Fan also fell into deep thought.

"Even among a group of ordinary people, there are those like Zhang Haobo who can sense the impending disaster before it arrives."

"Not to mention the countless cultivators in the Xuanhuang Realm."

"It seems that perhaps I was a bit too optimistic. Although everything has been arranged discreetly enough, there's no guarantee that someone with a destined fate won't discover something unusual due to various coincidences."

"If you want to cover up something, the best method is to use another matter to attract people's attention."

Li Fan's eyes flashed, and he instantly had an idea in his mind.

However, for now, he would quietly watch the grand spectacle of the Blazing Flame Scorching Sea unfold.

Not long after Zhang Haobo and the others left.

Chi Yan, who had been sitting cross-legged, finally made his move.

The flame dragons coiled around his body revealed excited gazes and instantly transformed into countless strands of crimson flames, frantically rushing towards every part of the Congyun Sea.

The seawater's temperature rose at a terrifying speed, and fear spread among the creatures that had not yet left the sea of clouds.

Faced with this dire situation, some cultivators rose up to fight back.

But the highest among them was only at the Hua Shen realm, so how could they be a match for the enhanced Chi Yan?

Li Fan's attention was not on the rapidly vanishing lives.

Instead, he silently observed the changes in heaven and earth itself.

"Rebirth amidst destruction."

"Revival amidst ruin."

The Blazing Flame Scorching Sea lasted for three whole months.

The ocean dried up, the earth was scorched for a thousand miles, and the vast sea turned into deep valleys.

But Li Fan, who had witnessed this scene from beginning to end, vaguely sensed the hidden vitality within it.

"This is the Profound Yellow Life and Death Transformation."

"By comprehending this, I can now advance to the Hua Shen realm at any time. Moreover, just like the Five Elements Grand Hollow Heaven at the Yuan Ying realm, once I advance, I will definitely be the strongest among Hua Shen cultivators."

"What a pity..."

"My ambition does not end here."

Li Fan took a deep breath and forcibly suppressed the spiritual power stirring within his body.

"How can the life and death transformation of a local area in the Xuanhuang Realm compare to the instinctive response of the entire world amidst the crisis of apocalypse?"

Li Fan recalled the various insights he had gained from studying the "Patching Heaven Record" over the years.

When faced with a life-or-death crisis, people often unleash power that surpasses their limits.

The world is the same.

The moment the apocalyptic crisis descends, the Dao of Heaven in the Xuanhuang Realm will undergo various profound self-transformations in that instant, seeking a glimmer of hope.

The transformations born at that time will far exceed those during periods of stable operation.

"If successful, my strength after advancing to Hua Shen will be even greater than estimated."

"Therefore..."

"No one can stop me!"

Li Fan's eyes were filled with indifference as he turned and left.

...

Lingmu Realm.

Ou Daozi carefully examined the corpse before him.

"Incredible, incredible."

"This was really created by you, Senior Shangshi? If you hadn't personally told me, I would have almost believed that this was truly an immortal's remnant body."

"After all, the feeling it gives me is almost identical to when Shang Tian was by my side."

A body

The corpse emitting a faint golden light lay flat in mid-air.

Under the glow of the golden light, the facial features were indiscernible.

However, the sacred aura it exuded made Ou Daozi feel both reverence and greed.

After hearing Ou Daozi's conclusion, Li Fan nodded with satisfaction.

This forged immortal's remnant body was his leverage to stir up the Xuanhuang Realm.

The corpse was purchased at a high price, the extremely well-preserved remains of a Hedao cultivator from thousands of years ago.

It was then meticulously crafted with golden primordial force essence, imbuing it with a power that transcended the mundane.

Moreover, to make the disguise even more realistic,

Li Fan extracted and placed within the corpse some of the abilities of the exotic treasures he had previously obtained from the fishing pool.

For example, once one approached the remains, all kinds of emotions in the cultivator's heart would be infinitely amplified, almost to the point of losing reason.

When someone touched the remains, the pure and mutated spiritual energy hidden within would madly pour into their body, instantly elevating their cultivation.

...

All of this was done to make it as authentic as possible.

"Even Changsheng Tian Zun would probably be interested in the legendary true immortal's remains, right?"

"Then let's make a big fuss about it."

In addition to these tangible benefits, Li Fan had also secretly made some arrangements on this so-called true immortal's remains.

He guaranteed that the person who finally got their hands on it would receive a big surprise.

With the power of the Faceless True Immortal's concealment, it prevented those cultivators skilled in deduction from calculating the origin of the corpse.

After the final perfecting work was done, two pitch-black puppets walked in and carried away the "true immortal's remains".

They went to carry out Li Fan's mission.

From appearance to disappearance, they were silent and soundless.

Even if Li Fan were not their commander, it would be difficult to detect their movements.

These two puppets were the gains of the ingenious scholars of the Tianji Realm in recent years.

As expected of the most talented group in the Tianji Realm, after unremitting research, although they still hadn't been able to figure out the essence of the power of darkness,

there was no obstacle to achieving a certain degree of application.

The black puppets perfectly combined the properties of the power of darkness.

They could disappear and appear from the "cracks" of the world, just like the darkness.

Divine sense could not lock onto or perceive them.

And they had a strong suppressive effect on spiritual power.

Ordinary means of attack could hardly cause them any damage.

The only flaw was that the puppets had limited destructive power and could only carry out some covert lurking missions.

Moreover, the nature of darkness being contrary to the immortal ruins also meant that these puppets could not go out to fight for long periods of time.

Every once in a while, they had to return to the dark realm to replenish the power of darkness.

In any case, Li Fan was quite satisfied with these black puppets.

Entrusting them with missions would hardly reveal any flaws.

After sending Ou Daozi away, Li Fan came alone to a sealed space.

Flashing on the light screen were one young face after another.

Appearing along with the images below was a long string of descriptions about their personalities.

"There are countless mortals in the small worlds, but it's still a bit difficult to find someone with a pure heart and utmost sincerity like Mr. Bai."

"Especially since the power of Wangsheng Tian Zun has already impacted many small worlds."

"In recent years, the number of newborns has clearly decreased a lot."

"No choice. We can only take a step back and settle for the next best thing."

"If innate qualities won't do, then we'll influence them through acquired education."
