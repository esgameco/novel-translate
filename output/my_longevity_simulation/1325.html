They joined hands within the "illusion formation" set up by Li Fan, and laid down another layer of formation. Only after ensuring there were no flaws did the official exchange begin.

"This old man seeks guidance on the Dao, please bestow your teachings!" Without further pleasantries, Jiandaosheng, the chief of the Immortal Boat's Xianzhou Institute, solemnly wrote out a true immortal sigil.

Perhaps to suppress Li Fan's aura and seize the initiative, the true immortal sigil he chose this time was indeed no simple matter. Even in his previous life when Li Fan was learning in the Immortal Boat, he never had the chance to encounter it. Even after becoming a disciple of Ai Tianjue and nearly becoming a core member of the Immortal Boat, this was still the case.

The dark lines slowly moved in the void. The moment the character took shape, it was like a great sun, illuminating the dark star sea where Li Fan and the people of the Immortal Boat were located.

This true immortal sigil did not just bring about a change in light and darkness. Under the radiant light, it actually brought a hint of life to the previously deathly still darkest star sea!

"It's actually a true immortal sigil with positive effects?" Li Fan's heart stirred, and his expression immediately became serious.

Ignoring Jiandaosheng's rather smug look, he focused all his mind and soul on that true immortal sigil.

With Li Fan's current level of insight, especially after glimpsing the laws of creation and the mysterious characters that formed the bloodline of the Xuanhuang Dazun, his ability to analyze true immortal sigils was almost unparalleled in the darkest star sea.

Carefully perceiving the character that moved and changed from time to time yet always hung high like the great sun, in just the time it took to drink a cup of tea, the original meaning represented by this true immortal sigil appeared in Li Fan's mind.

Phenomena such as the rising sun, the circulation of vital energy, and the revival of all things...

These and many other wonders together formed this special true immortal sigil.

"So that's how it is."

After completing the analysis of the character, Li Fan also resolved a long-standing doubt in his mind.

Just what had allowed the Immortal Boat to survive the destruction of the star sea until now?

Initially, Li Fan thought it was due to the Immortal Boat's divine tree formed from the seeds of creation. But after staying on the Immortal Boat for a few years, he gradually realized this was not the case.

The Immortal Boat's divine tree needed to constantly absorb great beings who had attained the Dao as well as wondrous creatures like the human-shaped rabbits to provide sufficient energy for the Immortal Boat. This showed that relying solely on the divine tree itself was insufficient to offset the consumption of the Immortal Boat's normal operations.

During the blank period of nearly ten thousand years, the divine tree was not constantly absorbing external energy. So what had ensured the divine tree's lush growth during that time?

Moreover, the environment within the Immortal Boat, which was as vibrant and warm as a normal cultivation world, did not seem like something a mere extraterrestrial divine tree could provide. Not to mention that even the original body of the Immortal Boat's divine tree - the extraterrestrial divine tree of the Xuanhuang Realm's Divine Wood Sect - did not possess such an extraordinary function back then.

Today, with the revelation of this character, the mystery was finally solved.

It was this true immortal sigil representing the rising sun phenomenon that had long provided the Immortal Boat with a habitable environment.

"So this is the foundation the Immortal Boat relies on to survive, a top-notch treasure. The people of the Immortal Boat actually took it out first..."

"It seems they don't intend to let me leave?" In his previous life mingling in the Immortal Boat, Li Fan was very familiar with the temperaments of these elders. They viewed the true immortal sigils and the future of the Immortal Boat as more important than their own lives. Yet now they were revealing these utmost secrets related to the Immortal Boat's destiny...

Even if it was "exchanging one character for another", it was still extremely suspicious.

"Unfortunately, your plan is doomed to fail. You may be able to stir up trouble, but if you think you can keep me here... Hmph!"

It was like a child's game, full of loopholes. Moreover, he now had the supreme escape technique, so even if true immortals descended, he had an 80% chance of calmly escaping. He naturally did not fear being surrounded by the Immortal Boat elders.

Pretending to be unaware of their scheme, Li Fan continued to focus on analyzing the character.

However, as his research deepened, he discovered some issues.

The character did indeed represent the phenomenon of the rising yang. The morning sun was gentle, but that was from the perspective of the immortal realm.

For the beings of the lower realms, the gentle sunlight from the morning sun of the immortal realm was not something they could withstand. The descent of this character would not only fail to bring life, but would instantly turn the world into a blazing purgatory!

"So this is not a true immortal sigil with enhancing effects either. It still represents destruction and chaos. It's just that the people of the Immortal Boat have weakened the character's degree of similarity and reduced its power, turning harm into benefit."

"Ingenious, it's no wonder they are a force that survived the great catastrophe. They indeed have some tricks." Li Fan inwardly praised them. But his expression changed abruptly as he coldly scanned the Immortal Boat crowd: "But that's not right?"

"This character's degree of similarity is definitely not ninety percent! Were you trying to fool me into not noticing?"

Li Fan looked furious, then disappointed: "Forget it, I didn't expect you to be a group that doesn't keep your word. The exchange is canceled!"

The Immortal Boat crowd did not expect Li Fan to see through the true nature of the character so quickly. Shocked, Jiandaosheng hurriedly explained to Li Fan: "Friend, you are mistaken! It is not that this old man intentionally hid anything, but for us in the lower realms, a character with thirty percent similarity is actually more useful than one with higher completeness!"

"Of course, if friend insists, revealing the complete character is also fine."

Saying that, Jiandaosheng wrote out the character again.

Even though there were various great formations greatly reducing the true immortal sigil's power, Li Fan could still feel that the moment the character took shape, the previous warmth around them instantly turned into a suffocating heat wave. Life vanished in an instant, replaced by a solemn killing intent.
