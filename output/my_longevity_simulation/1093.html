After pondering for a moment, Li Fan decided not to continue being mysterious and nodded, "Indeed. How did Fellow Daoist calculate it out?"

Xu Ke chuckled lightly with his slightly deep voice, "For someone who is against the world, where else would my information exist besides the Fallen Immortal Realm and the Xuanhuang Realm?"

Li Fan was slightly startled, "Against the world? What does that mean?"

This time it was Xu Ke's turn to be a bit puzzled, "Don't you know what I'm sitting on?"

Li Fan shook his head frankly.

"Then why did you come looking for me?" Xu Ke stared intently at Li Fan, his expression unfathomable.

"I sought out Fellow Daoist for two reasons. First, to help the younger generation of the Empire fulfill their lifelong wishes."

"Second, to understand the various arrangements left behind by Mr. Bai back then. After learning about his deeds, I was deeply convinced by his righteousness. But I was troubled by the incomplete information obtained in the Fallen Immortal Realm, which were just fragments of memories from different periods. So I spent quite some effort to find Fellow Daoist's hiding place, hoping you could help clear my doubts." Li Fan cupped his hands and said sincerely.

Upon hearing Li Fan's attitude towards Mr. Bai, Xu Ke's expression immediately softened a lot.

"The Empire?" He narrowed his eyes, seeming to fall into recollection.

"It's the lineage Fellow Daoist left behind in the fallen world of Chi Jiutou back then," Li Fan spoke up to remind him.

"Ah..." Only then did Xu Ke remember.

"Little Qing, Little Hong..." He showed a reminiscent look.

"The two are still well. It's just that the Divine Bird of Heavenly Fate has already fallen in order to search for Fellow Daoist's whereabouts." As Li Fan spoke, he passed over an object.

It was the last Divine Bird feather Little Hei left in the Beast Realm.

Xu Ke took the jet-black feather, his hands trembling slightly. A trace of grief flashed in his eyes. After a long while, he slowly said, "I let it down."

"Can... can I have this?" Xu Ke asked.

"It's just returning the item to its original owner. If Little Hei knew that the Divine Bird feather he left behind could finally return to Fellow Daoist, he could also rest in peace." Li Fan sighed.

"Thank you." Xu Ke solemnly put away the Divine Bird feather.

Atop the towering stone mountain, absolute silence fell once again in an instant.

At the peak of this desolate world, it seemed even the wind did not exist.

There was only the eternal stillness that had been passed down since ancient times.

Li Fan was not in a hurry either. He just accompanied Xu Ke in experiencing this heavenly desolation, waiting patiently.

After a long time, Xu Ke finally spoke again.

Due to the effect of the previous combo, his attitude towards Li Fan was now vastly different.

"How much do you already know?" Xu Ke asked.

"Back then, you and Little Hei went far to Nanming Sacred Beast Mountain, which was actually a task the Beast Taming Sect was instructed with by Mr. Bai. The series of actions that followed were all in order to ultimately cultivate the Void Rending Whales that could infinitely reproduce and open up the void. This was so the mortals of the various small worlds that were forced to migrate due to the birth of the Immortal Miasma could stay as far away from Xuanhuang as possible."

"As for the so-called Immortal Miasma, it was also a product of Mr. Bai reversing the principles. He, as the Great Celestial Venerate of Xuanhuang, ultimately ended his own life because of it."
