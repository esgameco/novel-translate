Chapter 399

"The giant eye can peer into the deep depths below, effectively monitoring the changes in the Abyssal Chasm."

The three of them slowly descended from beside the City of the Giant Eye, feeling shaken in their hearts.

"Just a single eyeball, yet it is of such a massive scale."

"It's truly unimaginable how gigantic the monster that emerged from the Abyssal Chasm must have been."

At the moment when their bodies crossed the limit of eighty thousand zhang,

Xiao Heng once again felt his strength weaken slightly.

"Even at the Qi Refining later stage, it's impossible to stand firm here."

"Only with Foundation Building strength can one barely continue descending."

The pressure from the Abyss began to manifest.

"The third layer, not as vast as the Forest of Lingua, just between eighty thousand and one hundred thousand zhang deep."

"But the level of danger is more than several times higher."

Continuing to dive deeper, in the distance, Xiao Heng and the others heard rumbling sounds faintly coming from below.

As Xiao Heng looked into the far distance, in the vast white mist, there seemed to be two colossal figures hovering in mid-air, wielding sharp blades and fighting against each other.

"The third layer, the Marionette Gorge."

"There are two giant stone puppets from ancient times, fighting in this region."

The two puppets were in a strange state between reality and illusion. Approaching them, it was like touching a phantom.

But their impact on the surrounding area was real.

The puppets were nearly twenty thousand zhang tall, rumored to be close to the Harmonious Path level.

Even from a distance, there was a possibility of being injured by the shockwaves of their battle.

Bang! Bang!

In the misty white fog, the figures of the two stone puppets appeared and disappeared intermittently.

The three of them stared in astonishment at this fantastical scene.

After some time, Xiao Heng spoke up, "Alright, let's continue downwards."

"Caution is key."

During the descent, they had to be vigilant of the movements of the two giant stone puppets, avoiding potential energy impacts.

The appearance of different creatures in the mist and clouds became increasingly powerful.

Thus, the pace of the three became slower.

It took them two whole days to reach the lower boundary of the Marionette Gorge.

"Up ahead is the fourth layer."

"The good news is that it seems the Medicine King's True Cauldron is not far below."

Xiao Heng once again controlled the little Medicine King's Cauldron, sensed for a moment, and let out a sigh of relief.

"Nevertheless, we must not be careless."

With a slightly furrowed brow, Zhang Haobo transmitted through voice.

"Let's go, quick. I can't wait to see that treasure." Su Xiaomei raised an eyebrow and entered the fourth layer first.

There were no resting points provided for cultivators between the third and fourth layers.

Only a long crimson line carved on the cliff face defined the boundary.

"The fourth layer, the Lost Maze."

"Between one hundred thousand zhang and one hundred thirty thousand zhang underground."

Sensing his own condition, Xiao Heng frowned slightly.

Having shed the equivalent of a Foundation Establishment early stage cultivator's cultivation base out of thin air, he could already feel the effects to some extent.

Changing the flight direction, they no longer descended vertically.

Instead, they flew diagonally towards the center of the Lost Maze.

Numerous ruins buildings were scattered in disarray floating in mid-air.

It was as if a catastrophic disaster had occurred, with broken remnants everywhere.

No corpses, no belongings left behind.

Only the dead bodies of buildings lying quietly in this space, recounting their past.

The silence around was terrifying.

The repetitive scene of ruins brought a sense of oppression.

Xiao Heng and the others couldn't help but feel disheartened.

Furthermore, there were unscrupulous cultivators hiding in the vicinity, ready to launch surprise attacks.

The presence of the Town Celestial Power, along with the chaotic ruins, provided them with the perfect cover.

Enduring until after the Stone Beasts besieged each other, when both were exhausted, the chances of success would greatly increase.

Moreover, it was difficult for the anomaly of fallen cultivators beneath the Abyss to manifest.

After succeeding and fleeing, one could vanish without a trace in this Lost Maze where many cultivators inexplicably disappeared.

As Xiao Heng and his group delved deeper into the ruins, on their way to the location of the Medicine King's True Cauldron,

As expected, they were attacked by two waves of cultivators.

However, those cultivators were after all just small fries.

Su Xiaomei and Zhang Haobo had successfully faced tens of thousands of faceless blood-faced cultivators besieging them in the remnants of the Morning Yuan Sect before and had managed to retreat unscathed.

They wouldn't take these individuals seriously.

With little effort, these people were easily dealt with.

After eliminating the two groups and displaying their own strengths, the area quieted down.

"It seems like there's a tail following us."

Zhang Haobo reminded.

"I'll go take care of them." Su Xiaomei raised her eyebrows, a red light flashing in her eyes as she was about to turn and act.

"Stop! We're here to find the Medicine King's Cauldron, not to kill people." Xiao Heng quickly stopped her.

"Drawing attention will only make things worse."

"Shake them off."

After speaking, the three of them suddenly picked up their pace.

Navigating left and right through the ruins.

After spending most of the day, they finally managed to shake off the followers.

"Let's go!"

Zhang Haobo made a hand seal, concealing their auras.

After flying like this for half a day, they finally arrived at the sensed location.

"Huh?!"

Xiao Heng looked around in confusion, not seeing the presence of the Medicine King's True Cauldron.

"It should be here."

"Let's split up and search around."

Su Xiaomei and Zhang Haobo nodded, and then went their separate ways.

Not long after.

"Nothing?"

Xiao Heng furrowed his eyebrows deeply.

"This shouldn't be."

Unwilling to give up, Xiao Heng searched again.

But he still came up empty-handed.

Su Xiaomei took out the small Medicine King's Cauldron from her storage ring, held it in her hand, and tried to sense it again.

But there was no change in the result.

"What do we do?"

After a fruitless attempt, the three of them gathered again to discuss.

"It's strange."

"It feels like the Medicine King's True Cauldron is here, but it's mysteriously vanished."

Xiao Heng looked around, puzzled.

"Perhaps it has hidden itself."

Zhang Haobo squinted his eyes and suddenly said.

Saying so, he stimulated his spiritual power and sent out a spirit blade towards the location where he sensed the Medicine King's Cauldron.

As if hitting something, the air distorted for a moment.

The spirit blade disappeared soundlessly.

Following that motion, the scene returned to normal.

"Wow! That's exactly it!"

Su Xiaomei clapped excitedly.

She flew towards that abnormal point.

However, it seemed like an illusion, as she passed through effortlessly.

Somewhat skeptical, Su Xiaomei tried several times back and forth, with the same result each time.

"This is frustrating! What's going on!"

Xiao Heng remained calm, carefully touching the non-existent barrier with his hand.

"The small Medicine King's Cauldron is the key to entering the Medicine King's True Cauldron."

Xiao Heng pondered.

"This small Medicine King's Cauldron, is it capable of containing a person?"
