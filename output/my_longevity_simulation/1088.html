Looking at the boy's statue again, he actually felt a slight stinging in his eyes.

"Humph." Li Fan snorted coldly, suppressing the urge to smash the statue to pieces. He searched left and right for the hidden treasure vault.

Without much effort, he discovered a secret formation behind the two statues.

After breaking the formation, Li Fan was able to enter the treasure vault of the Tianshu Sect.

Different from the imagined scene filled with dazzling treasures, the Tianshu Sect's treasure vault could even be said to be a bit crude. So much so that Li Fan almost doubted he had gone to the wrong place.

On a purple-red long table, a stone seal, a wooden box, and a scroll were placed in order.

Apart from this, there was nothing else.

That stone seal was naturally Li Fan's target this time - the immortal artifact Huang Shen Seal.

Even though it was still in a sealed state at this time, Li Fan still vaguely felt the ancient and vast aura from it that made one's heart palpitate.

If it were Mr. Bai, he would naturally only take the Huang Shen Seal and quickly leave.

But how could Li Fan ever return empty-handed from a treasure mountain?

Even if this place was the Fallen Immortal Realm's illusory world and treasures could not be brought back to the real world.

It was still good to broaden his knowledge and feast his eyes.

Li Fan first carefully observed the wooden box. He keenly sensed that the wooden box seemed to be imprisoning some living creature. It was trembling slightly at an extremely small frequency and amplitude. With each tremor, the aura unintentionally emanating outward made Li Fan, now the Xuanhuang Great Celestial Venerate, feel a bit dangerous.

What was even more strange was that the creature in the wooden box also seemed to sense Li Fan's presence outside.

The trembling became a bit more urgent.

As if tempting and urging Li Fan to open the wooden box.

Faint rustling sounds emerged in his ears, and many illusions also appeared before his eyes.

Li Fan pondered for a long time and finally suppressed the impulse in his mind with great willpower.

It was at this moment that Li Fan was startled to realize that the [Xuanhuang Immortal Heart Mantra], which would automatically circulate in the past as long as he awakened his self-awareness, even in the Fallen Immortal Realm, had somehow become ineffective without him knowing!

Without the boost of the Immortal Heart Mantra, resisting temptation relied entirely on Li Fan's own willpower.

Fortunately, he had reincarnated through many lifetimes, had broad knowledge and experience, and had extraordinary determination.

Otherwise, he probably would have been unable to control himself on the spot and opened the wooden box.

Li Fan forcibly shifted his gaze to the scroll.

The auditory and visual hallucinations brought by the wooden box instantly diminished.

"This wooden box is a bit sinister. I don't know if it still exists in the Xuanhuang Realm now."

In stark contrast to the strangeness of the wooden box, the scroll emanated an absolutely sacred and orthodox Immortal Dao aura.
