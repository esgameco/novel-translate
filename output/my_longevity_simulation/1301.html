Chapter 1301

The Sage Emperor pondered, sensing the surging energy from the remnants of Tianyang's bones.

After a long while, he let out a deep breath.

"The Dao of Striving... can prove immortality, but it does not align with me."

There was not much regret in the Sage Emperor's tone as he withdrew from the state of Tianyang's armor.

Returning the remains, Li Ping instructed Tianyang, "Since the Dao of Striving is empty, these resonating remains are an invaluable treasure for you. Contemplate them well. Although the immortal dao is not manifest now, the existence of resonance indicates the dao still exists."

"Given time, you may very well be able to achieve true immortality with this!"

The Dueyue Sect's Yijue Daoist looked on eagerly as Tianyang put away the remains, then stuffed his long eyeballs back into the passage.

"What a pity, what a pity..." Yijue Daoist sighed.

"What lofty opinions does the Dueyue Sect Master have?" Yijue's attitude aroused Tianyang's displeasure.

Yijue Daoist shook his head and chuckled, "Just lamenting that the Dueyue Sect missed a good opportunity. As they say, when one attains the Dao, even the dogs ascend to heaven. If the great Sage Emperor had comprehended the remnant thoughts, perhaps one day he could have truly grasped the Great Dao. We could have all benefited."

"As for you..."

"Maybe there will be such a day. But our Dueyue Sect probably won't be around to see it."

His words made no attempt to hide his pessimistic view of Tianyang's chances at immortality.

This time, Tianyang was not angered by the Dueyue Sect's provocation. He just sensed the unyielding thoughts from his own remains, as if returning to when he still had a human body.

But perhaps because he had become a puppet, even after trying for a long time, Tianyang still could not rouse his passion.

The eight words "Tianyang's life, yields to no one" sounded a bit different when shouted compared to before.

The Sage Emperor's hall fell into silence for a time.

However, before long, the changes in the Wanxian Alliance below drew their attention.

In a flash of light, a group of black-clad, ruthless cultivators appeared at the edge of the moonlight in Tianquan Prefecture.

They first gave a slight bow to the mirror-wielding figure high above, then with solemn expressions, prepared spells and techniques towards the unbroken Bright Moon Starlight Formation.

It seemed they were about to launch an all-out attack on the formation at any moment.

Various spells were indeed unleashed, but the corresponding techniques appeared to be frozen in midair, as if a pause button had been pressed.

Purple long spears like heavenly thunder, distorted spatial rifts, endless yellow talismans...

All were activated and gathered in one place.

Just waiting for the Bright Moon Starlight Formation to be torn open before erupting together.

The black-robed cultivators of the Alliance took turns, condensing the power within their bodies into techniques.

Several hours later, the black-robed cultivators had rotated many times.

The accumulated techniques at the formation's edge had reached an almost startling degree.

A hint of solemnity flashed across the Sage Emperor's face.

Undoubtedly, if this suspended, converged sphere of light exploded on the surface of the Xuanhuang Realm, it could easily reduce several prefectures to ruins.

"Wait a bit longer, I have a way to handle it," Li Ping said in a deep voice.

Tianyang nodded slightly, not being stubborn.

Another half day passed, with a total of over a thousand all-out strikes being infused.

The condensed small sphere of light was now even a bit more dazzling than the bright moon above.

Even the mirror-wielding figure seemed to feel some apprehension.

The mark on his forehead released a small ancient bronze mirror, enveloping himself, the nine detached discs, and the other secondary phantoms.

Within the detached discs, the light and shadows from the formation analysis rapidly changed and finally produced feedback.

From time to time, a wisp of colorful thread flew out, floating towards Lu Xichuan not far away.

After Lu Xichuan comprehended the contents of these threads, her body trembled and she revealed an enraptured expression.

Passing the insights to the array masters present also elicited exclamations of surprise.

At this moment, when they looked at the Bright Moon Starlight Formation before them, the previous despair in their eyes was gone.

Under Lu Xichuan's lead, the array masters began to unravel the great formation.

The originally substantial moonlight, like silver silk threads, began to turn hazy under the influence of external forces.

The Sage Emperor sensed that this group of Alliance array masters were like little insects, constantly gnawing at the foundations of the Bright Moon Starlight Formation.

Indeed, Li Ping could make further adjustments to the great formation.

And he was confident that the speed at which the twenty-eight detached discs jointly deciphered could not keep up with his speed of changing the formation. If the Alliance did not bring out any more special means, he could trap them for a lifetime with just the formation.

His goal had never been as simple as just trapping them.

The arrangements left outside the Xuanhuang Realm told him that the winds had started blowing in the Starry Sea.

The struggle between the Starry Sea's revival power and the remnant immortal power was about to officially begin.

The Sage Emperor, who had been sitting all along, slowly stood up.

In a single thought, his majestic figure was projected onto the Xuanhuang sky.

As if cooperating, the holy radiance of the Bright Moon dimmed slightly, handing the stage to the Great Awakening Sage Emperor.

The instant the Sage Emperor's image appeared, the soldiers fighting for the Alliance in various prefectures were instantly filled with boiling blood and high morale.

They shouted in unison, "Greetings to the Sage Emperor!"

The captured and besieged cultivators of the Wanxian Alliance looked with some trepidation at that magnificent figure in the sky, but involuntarily thought of the various legends about the Alliance's Dharma-Transmitting Celestial Venerable.

"Where is the Celestial Venerable at this moment?"

And those Wanxian Alliance cultivators still fighting like trapped beasts were also stunned upon seeing this figure filling their entire field of vision.

"In the past, the Dharma Transmitter attained the Dao and widely spread it to all beings, creating a glorious thousand-year era of cultivation."

"However, the Dharma Transmission goes against the Heavens. The Heavens and Earth nurture cultivators. Devouring the Heavens and Earth is like killing one's own parents."

"It is not the righteous path."

The Sage Emperor's voice resounded throughout the Xuanhuang Realm.

Blessed by the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao, it clearly entered the ears of every living sage soul.

At first, the cultivators of the Wanxian Alliance did not react to what this Great Awakening Sage Emperor was going to do.

But when they heard the words "not the righteous path", their faces changed drastically.

The mirror-wielding figure guarding the detached discs had a gloomy expression.

The four central prefectures flashed with light even more, as the Alliance's high-level figures who had been holding down the headquarters were finally provoked.

Slandering the Dharma-Transmitting Celestial Venerable was equivalent to slandering the entire Wanxian Alliance.

It was shaking the legitimacy and foundation of the Wanxian Alliance's rule.

For the Alliance's upper echelons, the consequences of this matter were even more severe than losing all the other prefectural territories.

After all, even if they lost land, they could still take it back.

But once people's hearts changed...

"Break the formation! Break the formation for me immediately!"

Lu Xichuan heard the furious roar of the Dharma Transmitter Ren in her ear.

The Alliance's array masters were as still as cicadas in winter, desperately trying.

However, while breaking the formation, they involuntarily listened to the voice coming from high in the sky.

Accompanied by the constant appearance of many sacred phenomena in the sky, it spread to every corner of the Xuanhuang Realm.

"I also have a method that does not borrow the power of Heaven and Earth, and can also tread the immortal path."

"Namely, with my Foundation Establishment, Golden Core Refinement, Nascent Soul Formation, and Divine Transformation..."

"Resounding the Dao of Longevity!"

The Sage Emperor's words were like a bolt from the blue, shaking the Xuanhuang Realm and attracting the attention of all cultivators.

Just from the name alone, the Sage Emperor's method sounded much stronger than the Dharma Transmission.

Moreover, with auspicious clouds and flashing golden light appearing one after another between Heaven and Earth, it made the cultivators feel dazed.

The last time a supreme expert lectured on the Dao in the Xuanhuang Realm was the Dharma-Transmitting Immortal Venerable thousands of years ago.

After the Dharma Transmission was verified by the world, the Dharma-Transmitting Immortal Venerable, who was at the Harmonious Dao realm at that time, became the Dharma-Transmitting Celestial Venerable of the Longevity realm.

If this new method was feasible, wouldn't it mean that another peerless expert capable of contending with the Dharma Transmitter would emerge in the Xuanhuang Realm?

The bandits of unknown origin who surrendered might face a purge by the Wanxian Alliance in the future.

But if they turned to this Sage Emperor...

A strange atmosphere began to circulate among the Alliance cultivators.

The Sage Emperor's Dharma transmission continued.

"The first step on the immortal path is Foundation Establishment with the self."

"Borrowing the wonders of Heaven and Earth can establish the foundation. Why? The wonders of the world are manifestations of the laws."

"But the most wondrous thing in the world is not an object, but the spirit."

"Foundation Establishment with the self should be based on the myriad principles of the world!"

The originally profound method of Foundation Establishment with the self became easy to understand when the Sage Emperor explained it in detail.

At this moment, even mortals who had not yet set foot on the path of cultivation in the Xuanhuang Realm could vaguely understand the general idea.

Not to mention those Wanxian Alliance cultivators who had already successfully walked the path of Dharma Transmission!

The Sage Emperor's new method was actually built on the foundation of the Dharma Transmission, but broke free from its shackles and was reborn.

Cultivators who practiced the Dharma Transmission naturally felt extremely familiar with it.

Just by listening, thoughts of agreement such as "So that's how it is", "It's indeed feasible", and "Wonderful" had already welled up in their hearts.

Some Alliance cultivators who had just completed Qi Refinement and were trying to break through to the Foundation Establishment stage, but were struggling to find a foundation-establishing wonder, suddenly had a great epiphany.

As if enlightened, they were overjoyed: "Why bother searching for some wonder? The Sage Emperor is right, I myself am the wonder!"

"Foundation Establishment! Foundation Establishment!"

Although the method of Foundation Establishment with the self was not easy to actually cultivate, the vast Xuanhuang Realm had the largest number of cultivators at the Qi Refinement stage.

With such a huge base, even a small probability multiplied would result in thousands or tens of thousands in the end!

The Sage Emperor had only lectured up to the middle part of Golden Core Refinement, but new cultivators of Foundation Establishment with the self had already begun to descend on the Xuanhuang Realm!

The treatment for breakthroughs was completely different from that of Dharma Transmission cultivators.

After their breakthroughs, the spiritual energy of Heaven and Earth surged into their bodies as if crazy, as if they were truly the protagonists of destiny favored by the Heavenly Dao.

There were even a few who had rosy clouds descending beside them during their breakthroughs.

Within the rosy clouds were various heavenly materials and earthly treasures, as if gifts from Heaven and Earth.

The attitude of Heaven and Earth was so vastly different that other cultivators couldn't help but feel envious.

Cultivators at the Golden Core stage and above might still have some reservations.

But for cultivators at the Qi Refinement and Foundation Establishment stages, there was no burden in dispersing their cultivation and starting over.

After all, even if the Sage Emperor's new method could not be cultivated, as long as the foundation-establishing wonder remained, they could always start over at any time.

After dispersing their cultivation, they temporarily stored away the wonder.

They wholeheartedly cultivated the method of Foundation Establishment with the self.

The number of Qi Refinement and Foundation Establishment cultivators in the Xuanhuang Realm far exceeded the total of cultivators at other stages combined.

Even if one out of ten people in this group chose the Sage Emperor's new method, it would be a huge blow to the Dharma-Transmitting Celestial Venerable and the Wanxian Alliance.

Except for Dharma Transmitter Ren, the other Dharma Transmitters finally could not sit still.

Because inside the Alliance headquarters, the true feasibility of the Foundation Establishment with the self method had already been proven through experiments.

If they let this Sage Emperor continue lecturing...

The high-level cultivators of the Wanxian Alliance couldn't help but shudder.

In the Wanxian Alliance's prison.

In the deepest part of the space imprisoning thousands of extremely vicious bandits.

A figure that seemed to be reduced to just bones had its four limbs spread out, forming a big character, nailed in the void.

Looking closely, on each of the figure's bones, there seemed to be...
