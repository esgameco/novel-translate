The excerpt describes a scene where a character named Li Ping is confronting a mysterious entity called Qingfeng, which takes the form of a willow tree. As Li Ping pulls on Qingfeng with silver chains, Qingfeng's true form is slowly revealed.

Qingfeng seems confused, wondering what has happened over the years and recalling that its true name is Tianyin, not the name Qingfeng given by someone called Xuyuan.

Suddenly, a silver needle shoots out from within Qingfeng/Tianyin, aiming straight for Li Ping's mind. Li Ping barely manages to block it with a golden protective shield. Li Ping realizes the needle is connected to someone from the Tianfa Realm and vows to destroy them for "disturbing the Xuanhuang world."

However, Li Ping knows he is not yet a match for his opponent, the "Heavenly Doctor." With a heavy heart, Li Ping releases the silver chains binding Qingfeng/Tianyin and prepares to flee with his stunned companion Sun Luyuan. As he vanishes, Li Ping hears a lamenting sigh: "Where will Tianyin's fading voice fall..."

After they disappear, the chaos in the world only intensifies, with lightning and thunder raging as if heralding the apocalypse. A figure as dark as ink descends upon Luoyan Prefecture - someone Li Ping would surely recognize if he were present.

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese novel excerpt:

This familiar existence.

It was the soul of heaven and earth, Ink Killer!

As the ultimate world-ending means of the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao, it decisively descended after Li Ping left.

Ink Killer first glanced at the place where Li Ping had left.

Without any hesitation, it skillfully transformed into endless ink.

To thoroughly eliminate Luo Yan Prefecture.

Ink Killer descended, and this time the speed of its spread was much faster than the Ink Killer's destruction of the sea that Li Fan had experienced before.

The cultivators of Luo Yan Prefecture had no time to resist or escape at all.

In just the time it takes to drink a cup of tea, Luo Yan Prefecture, which was originally a bit desolate but still had vitality, had completely turned into a void.

The sudden appearance of Ink Killer naturally alarmed many hidden powerful figures within the Xuanhuang Realm.

But at this moment, Luo Yan Prefecture no longer existed.

Even if they wanted to find some clues, they were powerless.

The impact of the Ink Killer's abrupt annihilation of a prefecture had just begun.

In the Holy Dynasty's grand opening, on the Holy Emperor's seat.

Li Ping sat upright, the remnant power of the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao on his body had already let him know what had happened in Luo Yan Prefecture.

He knew that this was the action taken by the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao to prevent him from being probed and tracked by the Heavenly Doctor.

Moreover, Luo Yan Prefecture was originally a remnant of the Heavenly Law Realm, so the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao was very decisive in clearing it.

"That woman, named Tian Yin?"

"Like the Heavenly Doctor, she has the ability to peek into people's hearts."

"She is formless and hidden within the Xuanhuang Realm. In addition to her own abilities, it may also be the enhancement of the Heavenly Doctor's silver needle."

"Although the purpose is unknown, it must not be a good thing for the Xuanhuang Realm itself."

Although this contact with the Heavenly Doctor also ended in defeat.

But it also let Li Ping know the gap between him and the Heavenly Doctor.

"Even stronger than the Dharma Heavenly Venerate."

"It seems that the road I have to walk is still very long."

"However, the power of the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao I borrowed this time was limited. After all, I originally only thought of subduing that woman. If it was really a matter of life and death, the blessing of the Xuanhuang Heavenly Dao could be dozens of times more."

"My Holy Dynasty has also just begun, and there are still unlimited prospects."

"Moreover, the Fishing Pond is a supreme treasure, and it may bring me some surprises from outside the high walls."

After a brief failure, he immediately began to summarize and seek solutions.

"But just these are still not enough."

Li Ping naturally thought of the sealed evil thoughts of the Xuanhuang Realm.

After devouring the remnant thoughts of the Cong Yun Sea this time, he had a clearer understanding of the power of the world's will.

"If I could devour the Xuanhuang evil thoughts..."

"With the Xuanhuang power restored to completeness, I will have a qualitative change again."

"It seems that this matter must be put on the agenda."

...

While Li Ping was thinking, Sun Luyuan was trembling and hiding under the Holy Emperor's seat, not daring to move.

Just now, whether it was the Holy Emperor in front of him, or Tian Yin, or the performance of that silver needle, they all greatly shocked Sun Luyuan.

Even though he was also a cultivator who had attained the Dao, in front of them, he was simply like a child, with no power to resist at all.

"What do you think of that woman named Tian Yin?"

After a long time, the voice of the Heavenly Mandate Holy Emperor entered Sun Luyuan's ears.

Sun Luyuan shuddered and showed a bitter expression.

(End of chapter)
