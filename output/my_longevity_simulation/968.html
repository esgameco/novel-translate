Previously, the two had made detailed arrangements about various matters that needed attention in their upcoming cooperation, but Li Fan did not ask Moru Bin to inquire about the secrets of the Immortal Alliance. It is said that if you want to take something, you must first give it. In Li Fan's view, Moru Bin's connection was still in the development stage and far from ready to be harvested.

"Perhaps after helping him further advance his position among the preachers of the Myriad Immortal Alliance this time, I can make a small request."

He thought to himself after Moru Bin's divine sense completely disappeared from his sea of consciousness.

In the next ten days, all cultivators practicing the "Divine Possession" technique, under Li Fan's suggestion, began to gather towards Luoyan State.

According to Moru Bin's plan, he would soon visit Luoyan State in the identity of a preacher.

The Myriad Immortal Alliance's organizations and officials at all levels in Luoyan State would inevitably treat this matter with the highest standards.

Moreover, Moru Bin would specifically attract the attention of all the supervisory forces, creating a breeding ground for Li Fan to freely spread the Divine Possession technique in Luoyan State.

For a month, Li Fan wanted to bewitch as many cultivators as possible during this period.

Then these cultivators would be dispersed to various parts of the Immortal Alliance, waiting for Moru Bin to complete his inspection tour and return to the Alliance headquarters soon after.

Then they would all erupt at once.

Once the situation became uncontrollable, Moru Bin would take the initiative to volunteer and take on the task of thoroughly exterminating these rebels.

With Li Fan as an inside collaborator, it would surely be easy to perfectly resolve this matter.

This was roughly the plan.

Of course, in actual implementation, it was very likely to encounter all kinds of unexpected changes. Moru Bin had already made various preparations.

It really felt like he had everything under control and could win a battle a thousand miles away.

Of course, compared to Li Fan, he was still slightly inferior.

Soon, more than 30,000 Divine Possession cultivators, under Li Fan's suggestion, went to Luoyan State respectively.

In order not to arouse the vigilance of the relevant parties of the Myriad Immortal Alliance due to the sudden increase in the flow of people, under Li Fan's manipulation, some of these people went through transmission arrays, while others flew by air.

In batches, they arrived in various parts of Luoyan State within ten days and hid there.

And began to observe the surrounding cultivators and look for suitable candidates.

Although Luoyan State was not as prosperous as the four central states of the Immortal Alliance, it was still a first-class major state.

The Supreme Immortal Venerable who was the commander-in-chief of the Myriad Immortal Alliance's Garrison Institute was from Luoyan State.

After he took power, he gave a lot of support to his hometown.

Many family members from Luoyan State were able to go to the headquarters of the Immortal Alliance to take up posts, and after years, they gave back to their hometowns.

After years of deep cultivation, the network of relationships could be said to be deeply entrenched and unfathomable.

Only existences at the level of preachers could make Luoyan State treat them with such solemnity.

In Li Fan's sea of consciousness, the map of Luoyan State slowly unfolded.

Sensing the information sent back by his subordinates, Li Fan couldn't help but nod secretly: "The land is spiritual and the people are outstanding. Even excluding those descendants of big families, the overall quality of the cultivators is much better than that of the remote Congyu Sea."
