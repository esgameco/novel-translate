Chapter 1005: The Origin of the Heavenly Mysterious Mirror

Upon hearing this, the avatar of the Heavenly Mysterious Mirror pondered for a moment, then reached into its own body with its right hand.

From within, it took out a flickering light sphere that seemed to be breathing, and handed it over.

"With this object on you, you can represent me," the avatar of the Heavenly Mysterious Mirror said solemnly.

"What is this?" Li Fan tried to take the light sphere, but grasped nothing.

"This is the origin of the mirror, born together with my main body. It neither increases nor decreases, neither is born nor dies. Outsiders cannot touch or absorb it; only I can invoke and control it."

As the avatar spoke, it pointed in the air and struck this sphere of mirror origin into Li Fan's body.

"Brother, you wouldn't be using this mirror origin to monitor me, would you?" Li Fan raised his eyebrows and asked immediately.

The avatar's face instantly showed a hint of anger at not being trusted: "This is just an origin mark that was born with my main body. Without the enhancement of other powers, how could I achieve surveillance?"

"Besides, if you really don't feel at ease, you can completely seal this origin sphere inside your body. As long as it's inside you, it can represent me..."

Before the avatar could finish speaking, it noticed that Li Fan had indeed sealed the origin in an instant.

Only that faint, almost imperceptible connection indicated the existence of the origin.

The avatar's face instantly stiffened.

However, seeing Li Fan's nonchalant expression, it still took a deep breath to calm its mind.

After a moment, it reminded again: "With this origin sphere, you can fully trust Cang, the Mirror Holder."

"Humph, but this person is not loyal and devoted. Instead, in the long years of monitoring me, he developed an ulterior motive. He wanted to use a secret method to further gain control over me, thus overriding all the Mirror Holders..."

"Unfortunately, how could his little scheme escape my notice? I pretended to fall into the trap step by step, but at the last moment, I turned the tables and made him my mirror slave..."

"Otherwise, this human body of mine wouldn't have come so easily." The avatar said smugly.

"Oh? There was even such a thing? That's the best possible outcome then! The success rate of my plan has increased by 50% with this!" Li Fan said with an admiring face.

"Time is of the essence; I'll set off immediately." Li Fan cupped his hands and finally disappeared from the avatar's sight.

The mortal city, which had fallen into stillness, instantly became lively and bustling again.

"I hope he can succeed." The avatar muttered to itself.

In fact, it didn't have much trust in Li Fan, but it was just giving it a try.

As Li Fan said, deepen the cooperation through strengthening it.

If the other party could really make some impact, it wouldn't mind adding another helping force.

If the plan was aborted halfway...

It wouldn't have any loss anyway.

The mirror origin could not be taken away by anyone. As for other things like the Myriad Spirits Convergence Array and Cang the Mirror Holder...

To it, they were not worth mentioning at all.

Thinking so, the avatar hummed a little tune and started to play the role of a busy waiter again.

Above the Wei Capital.

Seeing Li Fan return, the three Sun-Sky people were a bit puzzled.

They only saw Li Fan face to face with the suspected mission target, saying something unknown.

And then, instead of choosing to strike a fatal blow, he left directly.

"Lord Envoy, this is..." Daoist Hundred Flowers asked cautiously.

"The matter is..."

"There's been a change, there's no need to take action anymore. You can directly return to the Lingmu Realm, I still have things to do." Li Fan didn't give much explanation.

Although this matter seemed a bit anticlimactic, Tianyang and the others didn't dare to ask too many questions.

After parting ways, Li Fan first went to the hidden cave in Tianchen State and got in touch with Mo Rubin.

He explained the whole story of how he found the Tianxuan Mirror's avatar but couldn't kill it, and instead sought cooperation.

He even truthfully disclosed the matter of obtaining the Wanling Guishu Formation and the Mirror's Origin.

In the hidden cave, Mo Rubin's face floated in the air, falling into silence.

Not long after, he opened his mouth to praise: "Brother Xingwang has wit and has already handled it almost perfectly. No wonder back then, before making major decisions, the Xuantian King would always seek your opinion, Brother Xingwang..."

Li Fan tilted his head and directly interrupted, saying: "Is that so? Maybe. I don't remember."

Mo Rubin smiled and no longer dwelled on this matter. Instead, he quickly changed the topic and analyzed: "According to your plan, cooperating with Tianxuan Mirror is indeed the best solution at present."

"From your description, it seems that compared to back then, the Xuantian Mirror has also undergone great changes."

"However, it should still be trustworthy."

Mo Rubin judged.

"In nearly ten thousand years, if one hasn't been in a coma the whole time, who can guarantee that their personality won't drastically change?" Li Fan laughed strangely a few times, hinting at something.

"As for the Xuantian Mirror, it's not a matter of whether we trust it or not. To find the Xuantian King, we can only rely on it for now."

Mo Rubin was rendered speechless by Li Fan's words.

"By the way, to deduce and break the formation left by the Chuanfa Tianzun, the Wusheng Formation Crown is a bit insufficient. Didn't you say before that there was another deduction formation treasure that could split one into nine? Get it as soon as possible, I'll see if I can find a way to speed up its fusion." Li Fan casually said.

This was just a small matter, and Mo Rubin immediately agreed.

Afterwards, the two discussed the details of the plan's execution for a while before Li Fan left.

He went straight to Xuan Sushan's bamboo sea cave residence.

Through the surrounding formation restrictions, Li Fan vaguely heard the moaning sounds coming from inside.

With an unchanged expression, Li Fan flashed inside the bamboo building.

Xuan Sushan was raising a golden whip, fiercely punishing Lu Xichan.

Lu Xichan's face was full of blush, her eyes dazed.

Her face showed both pain and enjoyment.

Just as the two women were fully engrossed in their play, Li Fan suddenly let out a cold cough.

Xuan Sushan and Lu Xichan's bodies instantly froze.

They abruptly looked at Li Fan who had suddenly appeared.

Their originally flushed faces instantly turned deathly pale.

They quickly got up and bowed their heads in salute.

Xuan Sushan stammered and hurriedly explained: "Lu Xichan was slacking off again, so I punished her as you instructed..."

Lu Xichan lowered her head even further, not daring to say a word.

Li Fan snorted coldly, but didn't scold them afterwards.

He then said to Xuan Sushan: "Quickly invite your father to meet here."

Xuan Sushan was stunned for a moment, and then a hint of fear flashed in her eyes: "This..."

But very quickly, she thought of something: "Could it be that you..."

Li Fan explained indifferently: "Things are a bit complicated. In any case, the crisis has now been resolved. There is only one Tianxuan Mirror avatar left in the world."

"Let Cang come here to see me, he will understand."
