"Through the unremitting efforts of Xianmeng cultivators, the complete exploration of the White Mist Barrier in the Xuanhuang Realm has been accomplished. This is a great milestone worthy of being remembered forever."

"But the path of cultivation is difficult, and the journey ahead is not over. Next, an even more arduous and great task awaits everyone."

"As is well known, in addition to the divided states and the towering White Mist Barrier, there are also innumerable small pocket worlds in the Xuanhuang Realm."

"Now that the White Mist Barrier has been fully explored, it is time to advance into these small worlds!"

"Bring them all under the rule of the Xianmeng Alliance and achieve the unification of the Xuanhuang Realm!"

"Any cultivator who contributes to this effort will gain immense merit and contribution points."

"When cultivators carrying the Xuantian Treasure Manual encounter portals leading to pocket worlds, the manual will send out a reminder."

"It is important to note that exploring pocket worlds is different from the White Mist Barrier, with dangers far exceeding it. In the White Mist, at most there were strange, deceased existences. But in the small worlds, there may be cultivators and species with different supernatural powers who will fight to the death in resistance."

"But do not be afraid!"

"Even if you unfortunately die during exploration, the merit and contribution points gained will not disappear, but will be inherited into your next reincarnation along with your true spirit."

"The unification of the Xuanhuang Realm is destined to be an extremely long process. But I believe that with the fearless spirit of our Xianmeng cultivators, this goal will eventually be achieved!"

Other cultivators reacted with surging hearts or doubts upon seeing this.

As for Xu Bai, he inwardly cursed, immediately realizing the source of his Lord's unease.

"Fang Zaiji, is the Terminal Disk Formation ready?" Li Fan flew to the entrance of Changsheng Valley and asked solemnly.

Li Fan shook his head slightly: "There is no time left. Tell me the current progress of the formation."

Fang Zaiji was a bit surprised but still promptly followed orders.

As Li Fan listened, he took out the Separation Disk and input the rudimentary formation into it.

A faint glow emanated from the Separation Disk as countless scenes flashed rapidly before Li Fan's eyes.

As the glow faded, Li Fan pointed into the air, transmitting the formation to Fang Zaiji, Liu San and others as they jointly set up the formation.

Fortunately, they were lucky that the entrance to Changsheng Valley was quite hidden within the mist.

In this world, the Myriad Immortal Alliance's plans were intricately connected. Every step they took had a deeper meaning.

So the question arises, why did the Immortal Alliance go to such great lengths, not hesitating to open the Heavenly Mirror to the public, to launch a war against the small worlds?

What special significance does the unification of the Xuanhuang Realm hold for the Myriad Immortal Alliance?

After the unification of the Xuanhuang Realm in the previous world, the Immortal Alliance seemed to want to use the thousands of obelisks to push the Xuanhuang Realm away from the pull of the Immortal Realm.

It was not due to a lack of ability, but perhaps...
