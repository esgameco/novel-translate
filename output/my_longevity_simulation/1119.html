Chapter 1117: Revenge is a Must

"Our branch established in the Blood River has not been discovered yet."

"If we are lucky enough to escape alive, we can regroup there."

"The Blood River's power is special in nature and should effectively block the Myriad Immortal Alliance's tracking," Li Fan, who had already thought of countermeasures after escaping, said to the core members of the alliance such as Liu Ruchen and Baihua Daoist.

As for how the specific location of the Spirit Wood Realm was exposed, Li Fan actually already had a vague answer in his heart.

However, now was not the time for settling scores.

Li Fan also did not utter the cold words that would make them rather die than surrender.

With the power of the Faceless Immortal binding them, even if they wanted to surrender, they probably wouldn't be able to do so.

Just as the people of the Spirit Wood Realm were preparing to flee for their lives, the Holy Fetus Li Fan quietly came before the Faceless Immortal's stone statue.

The statue was just a vessel.

The Immortal's power had long been revived through the growing network of alliance members.

"But there is no doubt that, compared to other ordinary members, He should value his own statue a bit more."

"Actually, I could choose to throw out the statue and let the Myriad Immortal Alliance directly face the Immortal's power. However..."

"This method can only be used once. I'm still counting on Him to contend with the Immortals of the Immortal Ruins later."

Under the gaze of Holy Fetus Li Fan's faint eyes, the Faceless statue actually slowly became transparent.

And then disappeared.

Underworld Prison.

Li Fan looked at the statue that slowly materialized out of thin air in front of him, his face expressionless.

Where the Immortal's web covered, the Immortal's power could naturally flow freely.

His previous bestowing of cultivation and knowledge to alliance members was actually also done through this method.

It's just that this was the first time he tried to transfer physical objects.

"If I really chose to completely side with the Faceless Immortal and unreservedly dedicate myself to become a true Immortal Envoy, perhaps by borrowing the vast Immortal power, the entire Spirit Wood Realm could be transferred in a single thought." Li Fan thought as he stared at the statue.

The Underworld Prison did not undergo any changes with the arrival of the Immortal statue.

After putting away the statue, Li Fan, who was hiding inside, focused his attention back on the Spirit Wood Realm.

The alliance members were hurriedly preparing to withdraw.

The Holy Fetus Li Fan was unhurriedly arranging formations.

Noticing the figure that appeared behind him, Li Fan's movements slightly paused.

"Envoy... do we really have to do this?"

The one who came was the spirit of the giant tree in the Spirit Wood Realm, Lin Ling.

"Once this formation is activated, I'm afraid everything in the Spirit Wood Realm will be reduced to ashes." Lin Ling said with a pale face as she looked at the faintly discernible silver lines on the ground.

"I haven't paid attention for a while, your formation skills have improved quite a bit. You can actually see the purpose of the formation I'm setting up?" Li Fan's gaze swept over Lin Ling.

Lin Ling pursed her lips, "I don't have that ability. It's just that being of one heart with the spirit wood, facing a life and death crisis, I can sense it."

"We will return here someday, right? Even if we temporarily let the Myriad Immortal Alliance have this place, so what? I believe that under the Envoy's leadership, that day will not be too far."

Who would have thought that the once ignorant tree spirit would actually say such persuasive words.

The Holy Fetus's movements in setting up the formation couldn't help but pause slightly.

After all, the entrance to the Spirit Wood Realm was extremely secret, known only to a few. After eliminating them one by one, the target could be quickly locked.

As for the possibility that Lin Ling was the mole and her reasoning was all fabricated to clear herself of suspicion...

Li Fan trusted his own judgment.

He also trusted his ability to read people.

"Ancient Tree of Earth Veins..."

"Relying on being one with the earth veins, he dares to be so presumptuous. Hmph."

In an instant, Li Fan had already thought of how to deal with him.

"The Wood of Ruo has a great appetite for these descendants left on the earth's surface back then. If I can send him a big gift, it might improve our relationship."

However, punishing the Ancient Tree of Earth Veins would have to wait until the next life.

Li Fan temporarily recorded this grudge and waited for the people of the Spirit Wood Realm to finish their preparations.

He gathered Liu Ruchen and the others again.

"After rushing out, follow behind Tian Yang."
