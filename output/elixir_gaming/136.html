"Kill!"

"Kill!"

The strength of this formation far surpassed that used by the Supreme Ones who had besieged Ning Tao before.

Vast and mighty, they advanced directly.

Qi Yuan stood in mid-air, his red robe more vivid than blood.

His face wore a lazy expression: "Now this is... playing a game."

Now he finally had the thrill of a pay-to-win player.

A group of minions, constantly advancing.

He didn't need to lift a finger himself.

Compared to the brutal battles in Di Jue, this was extremely relaxing.

Now he truly seemed like a great villain, constantly bombarding the righteous side, overwhelming them with sheer force.

Faced with enemies ten times their number, how could the Supreme Ones of the Myriad Beasts Realm dare to truly fight? They had long since scattered and fled.

Unfortunately, they were already surrounded on all sides by the hundred thousand red-robed Supreme Ones.

Over a hundred Supreme One camps constantly closed the distance, continuously unleashing primordial divine abilities in long-range attacks.

They only needed to cast divine abilities in specific areas, without targeting any particular Supreme One.

Using endless divine abilities to forcibly kill those beast race Supreme Ones.

The situation was completely one-sided. The Supreme Ones of the Myriad Beasts Realm, viewed as true great enemies by the Liufeng Realm, had absolutely no way to fight back under Qi Yuan's troop surge.

"No!"

"How is this possible!"

"Why is this happening?"

The once lofty and spirited Supreme Ones of the Myriad Beasts Realm felt small and helpless for the first time, utterly confused and bewildered.

Their cries were drowned out by the primordial divine abilities.

These Supreme Ones had no idea how to break through the defenses, nor which divine ability they should try to block.

There were too many.

Too many.

Far too many.

On this day, a hundred thousand Supreme Ones steamrolled the Supreme Ones of the Myriad Beasts Realm. Countless died.

The Ten Thousand Zhang Plain was blasted into an abyss.

Here, the primordial energy was incomparably dense.

Ordinary Supreme Ones would likely perish just by entering.

Like a battlefield after a nuclear weapon leak, no living being could enter.

Qi Yuan stood in mid-air in his long robe.

Chen Huan's face showed intense guilt: "Blood Lord, the Venerable One... has disappeared."

At this time, he did not call Qi Yuan "big brother", but "Blood Lord".

Now that almost all great enemies had been crushed and killed, only the Venerable One's whereabouts were unknown.

It was too strange that the Venerable One had inexplicably vanished under the advance and encirclement of a hundred thousand Supreme Ones.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan was not surprised. His eyes held a smile: "It seems the final boss has some skills to be the final boss after all.

As for her... she should probably be... there!"

He already roughly knew Shang Yanran's identity.

Indeed... she was a tricky boss.

But that was only for others.

Qi Yuan's gaze pierced an extremely long distance, seeming to pass through time itself, landing on the Liufeng Realm's final stronghold.

"Go!"

The army of a hundred thousand blood-robed Supreme Ones charged towards the final stronghold together.

This scene was incomparably magnificent, even... terrifying.

The Supreme Ones in the stronghold had lived most of their lives. What scenes had they not witnessed?

But this scene, they truly had never seen before!

Originally, they thought the final battle would be the most horrifying battle.

They might have to fight to the last drop of blood for the Liufeng Realm.

But who knew, in the final battle, they would have no part to play at all.

A hundred thousand Supreme Ones charged towards the Myriad Beasts Gate, directly overwhelming the Supreme Ones of the Myriad Beasts Realm.

It was truly... inconceivable.

And now, the blood-robed army, like a cloud of blood, was sweeping towards them.

Their hearts couldn't help but tremble.

In an instant, the vast and orderly military formation had arrived before the city.

The army parted neatly, and a blood-red figure emerged from within. All the Supreme Ones looked at that blood-colored figure, their eyes filled with intense reverence.

Qi Yuan's gaze fell on the black-dressed young woman in the distance.

His gaze softened considerably: "I've come."

This time, unlike in the first world, no accidents occurred.

He seemed to be speaking to the young woman, and also to the others in the stronghold.

The young woman Ning Tao looked at Qi Yuan. For the first time, her steps faltered. A thousand words in her heart finally became one sentence: "I've been waiting for you."

Today she wore light makeup and had specially changed into black stockings.

Under the black lace dress, the young woman's figure looked especially petite, tiny. The jade bowls on her chest, not big or small, were exquisite. Her slender white legs, wrapped in black stockings, were like a yandere girl who had walked out of an anime.

Her skin was very white, cold white, seeming to match her personality.

But she seemed out of place in this world.

In her, Qi Yuan saw the shadow of a canary.

At this moment, a coquettish voice came from within the stronghold.

"As expected of the Great Demon, actually making me lose one life."

The speaker was none other than the Venerable One who had vanished earlier.

She had appeared inside the stronghold!

"Venerable One!"

"How is she here!"

The hundreds of Supreme Ones in the stronghold were all shocked, and also extremely wary of the Venerable One.

The Venerable One hiding in the stronghold was using them as shields.

The Venerable One's strength was incredibly powerful, only slightly inferior to the White Emperor.

But of course she could not withstand the siege of a hundred thousand Supreme Ones alone.

Now, she was mixed among the Supreme Ones, making the Blood Lord hesitate to attack.

Qi Yuan looked at Shang Yanran: "Actually, I really want you to die now, but... I'm somewhat reluctant."

If Shang Yanran died, the game would end.

Every time a game ended, Qi Yuan felt somewhat melancholy.

It was like finishing a manga he had been following, or a TV show ending.

Having stayed in the Liufeng Realm for so many years, even longer than in the Canglan Realm, he had grown somewhat accustomed to it.

Shang Yanran did not understand Qi Yuan's words, but she also said: "I feel the same way about my teacher. I hope teacher could perish, yet also hope... teacher could remain in this world."

As she spoke, she seemed especially wistful.

Hearing this, Ling Yayi's eyes flashed with anger: "Shang Yanran, how can you be so shameless? Now that the situation is decided, what more do you have to say?"

Shang Yanran's expression was calm as she looked at Qi Yuan: "I have always believed that justice will triumph over evil!"

"Hah, you're justice? That's utterly ridiculous!" Ling Yayi cursed.

Though called demons, the Thirteen Northern Demons had not committed every evil deed, despite killing many pursuers.

"Everything I've done has been for the Liufeng Realm. I have no regrets!" Shang Yanran's expression was frank. "Ask Baize, which of my actions was not for the Liufeng Realm, for all living beings!"

Her voice became passionate and stirring.

A hint of a mocking smile appeared in Qi Yuan's eyes: "Is the final boss trying to whitewash herself before death, to perfect her character?"

Shang Yanran ignored Qi Yuan's words and looked towards Baize, questioning: "Wasn't the North-South decision to eliminate demonic qi? Wasn't luring the Supreme Ones of the Myriad Beasts Realm to suppress demonic qi?"

Baize was silent for a long while before saying: "Yanran, your methods were too extreme."

"If I wasn't extreme, if I didn't play the villain, the Liufeng Realm would have been finished long ago! Ten thousand years ago, I sacrificed one life to send out nearly three thousand elderly Supreme Ones!

Now, it's me again, actively becoming the sinner to build your reputation, Baize!" Shang Yanran's voice was hysterical.

"Once the amount of demonic qi in this world reaches a certain threshold, the Liufeng Realm... will truly collapse. Even the Heavenly Dao will be truly polluted! This realm will become a true paradise for demonic qi."

Baize remained silent.

A pained expression appeared on Shang Yanran's face: "You, Baize, were unwilling to kill, unwilling to kill Supreme Ones, so I did the killing.

But what's the result... Baize, look, who is truly the sinner of this world.

A hundred thousand Supreme Ones... what a grand gesture, an enormous gesture!

Do you think these hundred thousand Supreme Ones will peacefully pass away? If even one-tenth... are unwilling to pass away peacefully.

This realm is already abandoned by the gods. The Heavenly Dao will be polluted in an instant.

Everyone, all Supreme Ones, including you and me, will turn into demonic qi."

Shang Yanran looked at everyone, her expression agitated.

"So tell me, who is the true Great Demon, who is the true great villain bringing calamity to all living beings!"

As she spoke, she stared straight at Qi Yuan.

As if saying he was the true sinner.

While she was the pitiful, misunderstood kind-hearted flower.
