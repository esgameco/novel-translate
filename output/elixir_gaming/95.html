It can be said that the Black Chicken Old Demon has always been like a sharp sword, hanging over Qi Yuan's head.

In the middle stage of the Divine Infant, the Black Chicken Old Demon, who was suspected to be about to break through to the late stage of the Divine Infant, put tremendous pressure on him.

From the slaughter of Chu Tian Xiong to the destruction of the Black Mountain Sect, Qi Yuan always had a thorn in his heart, and that was the Black Chicken Old Demon.

Now, judging from the intentions of the nine Divine Infants in the room, Qi Yuan's heart was filled with a surprising guess.

Then he heard the old man leaning on the crutch say, "The Black Chicken Old Demon is a practitioner of the Demonic Dao, and has been wreaking havoc in our Great Shang cultivation world, indiscriminately slaughtering our fellow Dao companions.

The Black Mountain Sect has also been rampant, committing rape and plunder, and committing all kinds of evil.

Now, we have found the location of the Black Chicken Old Demon and must use thunderous methods to kill him!"

The Black Chicken Old Demon was one of the few Divine Infant-level cultivators in the Great Shang cultivation world, and was only one step away from the late stage of the Divine Infant.

In addition, the Black Chicken Old Demon had no sect, was adept at escape techniques, and had an uncertain whereabouts, so the major sects in the Great Shang cultivation world were mostly unwilling to offend the Black Chicken Old Demon.

However, now that the Black Chicken Old Demon's cave dwelling has been discovered, and he is in seclusion there to break through, they have organized a group to collectively kill the Black Chicken Old Demon.

Of course, Qi Yuan felt that the main reason was that the Black Chicken Old Demon was about to break through to the late stage of the Divine Infant.

Once he broke through, the pattern of the Great Shang cultivation world would truly change.

These great cultivators did not want another powerful figure wreaking havoc.

"Killing the Black Chicken Old Demon is doing justice, and is the wish of us cultivators!"

"We must kill the Black Chicken Old Demon!"

The nine Divine Infants spoke up one after another.

The Elixir Cultivators standing aside also had excited expressions in their eyes.

Qi Yuan was the same.

With so many Divine Infants here, the Black Chicken Old Demon was doomed.

A voice transmission entered Qi Yuan's ear.

"The Black Chicken Old Demon has many disciples and grandchildren, both Foundation Establishment and Elixir Cultivators.

No matter what method you use, as long as you kill one Elixir Cultivator, you will have passed my test.

This time the killing of the Black Chicken Old Demon is extremely dangerous, even Elixir Cultivators may perish, you can still withdraw now."

"No need." Qi Yuan would not withdraw.

In the Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm, he had already killed so many Elixir Cultivators.

To him now, Elixir Cultivators were no better than dogs.

And with the nine Divine Infants backing the Black Chicken Old Demon, Qi Yuan was not afraid at all.

"Good, as long as you kill an Elixir Cultivator, you will have passed the test.

At that time, I will discuss a matter with you, and if you agree, I will give you an extremely great opportunity; if you do not agree, I will also give you another opportunity." said the old man Gu Mu Zhen Jun.

Qi Yuan responded through voice transmission, without any objections.

After about the time of an incense stick, the nine Divine Infants had finished discussing the plan to kill the Black Chicken Old Demon.

The group signed an agreement.

Cultivators like Qi Yuan and other Elixir Cultivators did not sign.

"Let's go." The old man with the crutch spoke, waving his hand.

The more than twenty cultivators present were all cast with a technique to conceal their auras, and flew towards the depths of the Myriad Demon Ridge.

It wasn't long before the old man with the crutch stopped.

"This place is called the Six Demon Cave, with six entrances.

We will enter from the middle, and go straight to the Black Chicken Old Demon!"

The nine Divine Infants had no objections, and with disguises on, they entered through the middle entrance.

At this time, an Elixir Cultivator said, "There are five other entrances here, we have thirteen people, we can form five teams and go in separately."

With the Elixir Cultivator's words, five teams quickly formed.

Qi Yuan, a Foundation Establishment cultivator, was alone, as no one was willing to team up with him.

After all, entering the Six Demon Cave this time was extremely dangerous, and no Elixir Cultivators were willing to team up with a Foundation Establishment cultivator, afraid of being dragged down.

And a Foundation Establishment cultivator participating in such a major event must be a talented genius valued by the sect's upper echelons.

If such a genius accidentally died by their side, they were afraid of being targeted by the Divine Infant-level monsters.

It's better to do less.

Qi Yuan didn't feel anything about it.

Being alone was fine.

Just then, an aged voice came.

"This old man is willing to team up with this young man." An old man in a black robe emerged from the crowd and walked towards Qi Yuan.

The others present looked at this old man, but said nothing.

"Since that's the case, the five teams are formed, let's enter the cave."

The five teams of Elixir Cultivators entered their respective cave entrances.

Qi Yuan and the old man also entered the cave.

The vision became dim.

The cave was huge, with a diameter of about twenty meters. The terrain in the cave was peculiar, not ordinary stones, but extremely solid.

The old man faced Qi Yuan, polite and courteous: "This old man's surname is Liu, an Elixir Cultivator in the middle stage. If we encounter danger, we two should help each other."

Elder Liu's words were extremely courteous.

An Elixir Cultivator in the middle stage and a Foundation Establishment cultivator helping each other? No, it was completely the Elixir Cultivator in the middle stage taking care of the Foundation Establishment cultivator.

Elder Liu spoke this way, completely to befriend Qi Yuan.

Qi Yuan nodded: "Okay."

Elder Liu's face had a respectful expression.

He was a wandering cultivator who had arduously cultivated to the Elixir Cultivator realm, and was considered a strong figure in the Great Shang.

But compared to the three major sects and the medium-sized sects with Divine Infants, his Elixir Cultivator was nothing.

Now that he was old, with many children and grandchildren, the one who had lived a life of mediocrity, also wanted to make friends with some geniuses.

So he dared to enter the cave with Qi Yuan.

He had already planned that no matter what, he would protect this genius's safety.

Of course, his gamble was completely a gamble.

If this genius turned out to be ungrateful and cold-hearted, or even evil-minded, his gamble would be a complete failure.

The two were flying in the cave.

"Who dares to trespass the Six Demon Cave?"

Just then, a cold shout came from the front.

Three Foundation Establishment cultivators holding spirit tools came forward, blocking Qi Yuan and Elder Liu.

The leader had a fierce expression, with a strong aura of bloodshed, obviously a ruthless killer.

At this moment, a look of doubt flashed in his eyes.

Elder Liu saw this, but did not take action, and instead focused on Qi Yuan's reaction.

Foundation Establishment cultivators were nothing to this genius cultivator.

He couldn't steal the limelight from this genius.

Qi Yuan took a step forward and let out a loud roar: "Your grandpa!"

He pushed out a punch, bare-handed, and with one strike, the three Foundation Establishment cultivators were crushed to pieces before they could even react.

Qi Yuan's actions were quick, ruthless and decisive, not wasting any words with them.

Elder Liu stared with wide eyes.

Three Foundation Establishment cultivators?

Crushed with one punch?

Even he as an Elixir Cultivator in the middle stage could not do it so easily, right?

This was not a genius, but a prodigy!

Probably none of the Top Ten of the Great Shang could match this.

Elder Liu's heart was pounding.

He had bet correctly, this was truly a prodigy.

"Your Dao companion's strength is truly terrifying, even this old man is inferior!" Elder Liu stepped forward and flattered.

He said this to be modest. The strongest among Elixir Cultivators were the Gold Elixir.

Unleashing a Gold Elixir, ordinary Foundation Establishment cultivators would have no resistance at all.

Qi Yuan sighed: "Actually, it's not much. I know a sister, she's in the Foundation Establishment stage, and is much stronger than me."

Qi Yuan was talking about the Icy Mountain Tyrant Girl.

That was a true big shot.

Hearing Qi Yuan's words, Elder Liu's mood was indescribable.

If this was not considered much, what about all the Foundation Establishment cultivators in the Great Shang, including the Top Ten? He was at a loss, not knowing how to describe Qi Yuan.
