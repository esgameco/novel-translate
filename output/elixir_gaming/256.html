After all, Great Venerable Jasmine had spread rumors about liking him.

Presumably, Great Venerable Jasmine would be captivated by him.

With one sentence, he stunned everyone present.

These cultivators' minds couldn't quite keep up.

How did it become Great Venerable Jasmine proposing to the Blood-Robed Sword God? The smile on Great Venerable Jasmine's face also became strained: "Is the young master joking with this servant?"

If you love me so much, but aren't even willing to confess, isn't that fake?

Great Venerable Jasmine smiled without speaking.

Qi Yuan continued to morally bind her: "But, you only have some looks. In our village, you'd be considered the type who can't find a husband.

If you confess to me, you need to show some sincerity. For example, give me some mythical secret treasure as a gift, free of charge. Then maybe... I'll accept your confession."

Qi Yuan's words had too many points to criticize.

This caused all the onlooking cultivators to hold their breath.

They wanted to say, Blood-Robed Sword God, are you blind? Great Venerable Jasmine is the number one beauty in Phoenix Heaven Realm.

Yet he said she couldn't find... a husband.

And... using a mythical secret treasure as a gift, this Blood-Robed Sword God really dared to imagine, his calculations could be heard throughout Qinghong City.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan sighed deeply: "It seems you still don't love me enough.

Alas, I didn't expect that I, the great Blood-Robed Sword God, can't even compare to those delinquents riding ghost fire motorcycles.

Those delinquents can bewitch other people's daughters to the point of losing their minds.

But you... won't even give me the old man's inheritance."

"The young master jests." Jasmine's eyes held laughter, her voice becoming somewhat weaker, "Although this servant has admired the young master for a long time, unfortunately... this servant's marriage is not her own to decide.

Before my father passed away, he said that only someone willing to die for me could marry me and jointly control the mythical secret treasure with me."

"Your dad is an old stubborn fool, don't listen to him," Qi Yuan shrugged.

What era is this now, even the Qing Dynasty has fallen.

To date someone, still demanding life and death?

"It is my parents' command, I dare not disobey. It seems the young master and I are destined to be star-crossed lovers," Great Venerable Jasmine said pitifully, like the girl next door.

"It shows you still don't love me enough," Qi Yuan continued to use this tactic, saying coldly, "In that case, you may leave."

Hearing this, Great Venerable Jasmine's face broke into a smile: "When the young master has time, come play with this servant at Phoenix Palace.

I cannot give you the mythical secret treasure, but other things... I can give."

After speaking, Great Venerable Jasmine's figure disappeared.

The cultivators present, witnessing today's events, were extremely shocked.

Especially since they got to watch the drama unfold, they were very excited.

At this moment, Qi Yuan glanced at the corpses on the ground, lightly kicking them out of the room.

He looked at Ye He and said softly: "This brother spoke up for justice, many thanks."

Seeing this, Ye He was shaken.

The legendary Blood-Robed Sword God was actually speaking to him, he felt somewhat overwhelmed by the favor.
