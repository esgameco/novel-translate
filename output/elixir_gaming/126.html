Qi Yuan sat atop the city wall, dressed in a blood-red robe.

His gaze was sacred, his voice ethereal like celestial music.

As soon as he began preaching, everyone present was entranced.

Even the malicious Supreme Milo felt as if he was alternating between paradise and purgatory.

The crowd was spellbound, time slipping away unnoticed.

Outside No Return City, three terrifying auras lay in wait.

The leader was Milo Supreme's father, the Great Supreme Mikong.

An odd expression crossed his face: "Even with the aid of the Destiny Tablet, the Blood Lord's talent is frighteningly impressive."

"The Way of Human Demons is the way of human nature. One must either deeply understand human nature or be obsessed with a single thought to comprehend its essence. To preach it requires at least... worldly wisdom, seeing through the true nature of all things. Unfortunately, this also means... confusion," another Great Supreme sighed. "The Blood Lord's madness is not an act. Even a Great Supreme cannot withstand the extreme power of Human Demons. His insanity is normal."

All three present were Great Supremes.

They had inscribed their Origin Divine Abilities onto their bodies and souls.

They had a deeper understanding of the Way of Human Demons.

This path was truly chaotic and frenzied. The deeper one's realm, the greater the influence.

Not a single supreme powerhouse like the White Emperor or the Sovereign remained in the mortal world.

Otherwise, who knows if they might suddenly go mad.

With the help of the Destiny Tablet, the Blood Lord could clearly explain the chaotic Way of Human Demons, allowing Earthly Legends to directly contact the Origin. It could be said that the Blood Lord had delved too deep into the Human Demon path and could collapse into madness at any moment.

Even Great Supremes could not withstand it, let alone... the Blood Lord.

"Once we obtain the Destiny Tablet, we cannot emulate the Blood Lord and become too entangled with it. Each of us will wield it for seven years, taking turns," proposed a yellow-browed Great Supreme from the North.

This proposal had been agreed upon by them beforehand.

"No objections," said the blue-haired woman coldly. "I enjoy studying strange things. Two-thirds of the Blood Lord's body should be left for me to thoroughly examine."

Great Supreme Mikong chuckled: "When we make our move later, we can't be too fierce. If we accidentally smash the Blood Lord to pieces, Great Supreme Ice Snow won't be able to study him."

The yellow-browed Great Supreme smiled, his eyes filled with worry: "My eyebrow keeps twitching. I don't know if it's a good or bad omen."

"What else are you worried about?" asked Great Supreme Mikong.

"The Purple Daoist's disappearance in Blood Fall Thousand Cities is very strange," the yellow-browed Great Supreme said uneasily.

Hearing this, Great Supreme Mikong felt utter contempt for the yellow-browed Great Supreme.

They had discussed this matter many times, yet this old yellow-brow was still worried.

"Even if the Blood Lord used the Destiny Tablet to kill the Purple Daoist, we have... over a thousand Supremes at our command. Even the White Emperor would have to be cautious, let alone the Blood Lord!" Great Supreme Mikong reassured helplessly.

His lifespan was nearly exhausted. He had to succeed.

Over the years, the three Great Supremes had gradually sent peak Earthly Legends into No Return City.

Including those attending this sermon, there were a total of 1,500.

It could be said that No Return City's prosperity was inseparable from their contributions.

Great Supreme Ice Snow spoke coolly: "If we delay any longer, we won't be the only ones coveting the Blood Lord."

The yellow-browed Great Supreme pressed his twitching eyebrow: "Alright!"

Hearing this, Great Supreme Mikong smiled: "Let's discuss again how to divide the spoils."

Discussing these things was always exciting and joyful.

After all, this opportunity was even greater than when they became Great Supremes.

...

On the city wall, the sermon ended.

The audience sat, some frowning, some pondering.

Qi Yuan stood on the wall, murmuring: "I was much more serious in this sermon than in previous ones. Don't disappoint me."

As Qi Yuan's voice fell,

A man in the barbican suddenly stood up, the power of Origin bursting forth from his body.

His eyes were tightly closed, but his irrepressible joy was still visible.

A Supreme! Some Supremes awoke from their enlightenment and looked at the newly ascended Supreme with complex expressions.

As one Earthly Legend ascended to Supreme, more and more Supremes appeared like bamboo shoots after rain.

In just a few hours, the number of new Supremes in the barbican exceeded a thousand.

This was comparable to over a thousand years of accumulation in the Flowing Wind Realm.

Milo Supreme opened his eyes, filled with amazement.

The information he had received was nothing compared to witnessing it firsthand.

He glanced at the Blood Lord on No Return City's wall, an extremely bold idea flashing through his mind.

Soon... he could take his place.

Wait... wait... wait for all the sleeper agents to ascend to the Supreme realm.

Half a day passed. For working people, it was another day of torment. For those on vacation, it was far too short - the day ended just as they woke up and barely started to enjoy themselves.

For those who had broken through to Supreme, time was short, almost unreal.

But for Milo Supreme, this afternoon felt exceptionally long.

Finally, after the last Earthly Legend ascended to Supreme,

All 2,000+ Earthly Legends present had entered the Supreme realm.

As usual, these 2,000+ Earthly Legends bowed to Qi Yuan.

"We thank the Blood Lord for imparting the Dao!"

Over a thousand Supremes shouted in unison, their voices echoing for tens of miles throughout No Return City.

At least this shout was somewhat sincere.

Qi Yuan stood on the city wall, a lazy expression on his face: "It's finally over."

An ending is also a kind of beginning.

As he spoke, he seemed about to leave.

At this moment, a voice suddenly came from the crowd.

"It's not over yet." The speaker was Milo Supreme, who had been biding his time.

His voice was light, but it reached everyone's ears, including Qi Yuan's.

As soon as he spoke, the gazes of thousands of Supremes fell on him.

Hundreds of Supremes looked at Milo Supreme with complex expressions.

Some looked surprised, some terrified, some confused and uneasy.

Li Yunzhi stood in the crowd, cursing at Milo Supreme: "This guy is finally making his move."

There was also worry in his eyes.

Anyone who wasn't a fool could see that many Supremes were walking towards Milo Supreme, seemingly taking sides.

Among them were many veteran Supremes.

Qi Yuan looked at Milo Supreme: "If it's not over, hurry up. I'm busy."

Milo Supreme looked at the calm Qi Yuan, feeling nervous, but he knew that at this moment, the arrow was already on the string and had to be fired.

He shouted: "Today's matter is a grudge between myself and the Blood Lord. Everyone else... just watch. If you interfere and die here, don't blame me!"

Milo Supreme laid down his threat.

As his voice rang out, more and more Supremes flocked to him.

Even the "veteran" Supremes of No Return City rushed to Milo Supreme's side.

In just a few dozen breaths, over a thousand Supremes had gathered around Milo Supreme.

Over a thousand Supremes gathered together, their aura enough to shock everyone.

The remaining thousand or so Supremes were like scattered sand, their faces uncertain.

A tall and burly Supreme stepped forward, his expression agitated: "What are you doing? The Blood Lord granted us Origin enlightenment, helping us become Supremes. You've just become Supremes, and you're already betraying the Blood Lord?"

He cursed at Milo Supreme and those Supremes behind him.

After cursing, he shouted at those behind him: "Everyone, follow me to protect the Blood Lord! They have numbers, but the Blood Lord needs us!"

Of the thousand Supremes behind him, only a few dozen stepped forward.

The rest had various expressions - some hesitant, some uneasy, some even wanting to join Milo Supreme's side.

Milo Supreme looked at the tall and burly Supreme, contempt in his eyes.

Human nature... is always selfish.

The occasional flashes of brilliance are like the stars in the ancient legends of the sky.

When the stars are gone, where is the brilliance?

However, Milo Supreme still spoke with a smile in his eyes: "Don't be fooled by the Blood Lord. The Blood Lord was originally a young prodigy, quite admired by my father. My father, Great Supreme Mikong, even wanted to betroth my younger sister to him. But who knew this person had evil intentions? He seduced my sister, bewitched her, and stole a supreme treasure from my father, Great Supreme Mikong. It's by relying on that treasure that he achieved his current success. I have had a long-standing grudge with the Blood Lord. Please don't get involved!"

No matter when, it's necessary to have some righteousness.

Sure enough, when this news came out, some Supremes who were originally afraid and unwilling to protect the Blood Lord said:

"No wonder the Blood Lord could impart the Dao. So he stole Great Supreme Mikong's treasure. I'm unwilling to protect such a despicable person!" Some Supremes found a reason not to protect him, feeling more at ease.

As they spoke, some opportunists even asked: "Milo Supreme, can we join you?"
