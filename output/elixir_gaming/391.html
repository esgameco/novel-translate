Including his junior sister Jiang Lingsu. Even he would not be able to protect them.

"Sometimes living in a muddle-headed state without thinking about heavenly matters is also a good thing."

"You know about the mark?" Qi Yuan looked at the strange painter. "Did the Jade Maiden tell you?"

This mark could not be discovered without reaching the Yang Spirit realm. The strange painter couldn't possibly have discovered this mark. So it must have been that Jade Maiden.

"The Jade Maiden's identity is mysterious, suspected to be from the upper realms," said the strange painter.

He had been examining the marks on all cultivators every day, trying to find a way to break them. So far, he hadn't found any proper method to break them, though he did have some unorthodox ideas.

The strange painter's body trembled slightly: "This is related to a supreme treasure. The Jade Maiden once said that even Yang Spirit powerhouses in the upper realms deeply covet supreme treasures. It's even said that if a Yang Spirit from the lower three heavens obtains a supreme treasure, they can bring their entire sect directly up to the middle three heavens. If our Cang Lan realm had a supreme treasure, we might truly be able to escape the great calamity!"

The strange painter suppressed her voice, but one could still sense the fervor and anticipation in her words. This was the only solution she had found to resolve the great calamity after "giving up" for so many years.

Qi Yuan frowned in thought, then looked at the strange painter and said seriously: "Do I look like a supreme treasure to you?"

The strange painter: "..."

"Your expression makes it hard for me to keep a straight face. You're not going to draw a watermelon head on yourself later, record a video, and say 'Fellow Daoists, today I encountered a major speechless event...' are you?"

Qi Yuan maliciously speculated.

This strange painter had no face, but her expressions were too lifelike.

"I don't quite understand your meaning, but it's simple for powerful beings to have some quirks," said the strange painter.

"Speaking of which, if the mark is related to a supreme treasure, doesn't that mean... if I had another supreme treasure, I could solve the mark issue?" Qi Yuan pondered aloud.

The strange painter shook her head: "I'm not clear on these matters either. Supreme treasures can bring fatal disaster even to Yang Spirits. How would a small fry like me know about them? Of course, the greatest use of supreme treasures is far beyond just blocking marks..."

The strange painter didn't know much about supreme treasures, not even their specific names. But she had heard the Jade Maiden say that the fate of the Cang Lan realm and the other 3,000 realms was all due to one supreme treasure.

"Alright, we've learned about the game settings, now let's move on to the mission plot," Qi Yuan said. Then he added, "Sorry, I've been playing too many games and it's gotten to my head. The strange painter glanced at Qi Yuan: "I rarely paint for others, and I have some requirements for painting. I don't paint unless it's a difficult subject."

"Perfect, what I want painted is quite difficult," Qi Yuan said happily. "Don't worry, I won't shortchange you. If you paint it well, hmm, I'll leave you a... one square meter space in the painting!"

Qi Yuan said seriously, being quite generous this time by offering one square meter of space.

"Oh, paint what?" The strange painter directly ignored the latter half of Qi Yuan's statement.

"Paint... the underworld!" Qi Yuan said seriously.

The strange painter's face showed a surprised expression: "The netherworld? That's just a legend, there's nothing much to paint about it."

In the Cang Lan realm, there were also legends of the netherworld, and even rumors of reincarnation. But most cultivators didn't take them seriously.

When cultivators kill people, they destroy even their souls!

Where would there be a netherworld? How could one fall into reincarnation?

"The underworld I want painted is different from what you imagine. Moreover, I'll provide you with a brush. You can use this brush to paint," Qi Yuan said calmly.

"I'm usually only accustomed to using my own brushes for painting. The brushes in this world, including yours, are all too ordin-"

However, halfway through her sentence, her body froze.

Two laser-like beams flashed from her eyes, piercing through the paper covering her face.

She stared blankly at the Mountains and Rivers Brush that Qi Yuan took out, breathing rapidly.

"This brush... this brush... how could there be such a brush in this world? What kind of brush is this? Is it a magical treasure? No! A supreme treasure? Not that either!"

As soon as the Mountains and Rivers Brush appeared, her attention was completely drawn to it. Her eyes saw nothing but that brush.

She couldn't imagine how such a brush could exist in this world.

Qi Yuan held the Mountains and Rivers Brush and asked, "My brush is too ordin- what?"

The strange painter was stunned for a moment, her face heating up, wishing she could take back her words: "Too ordin... ordinarily amazing?"

After thinking for a long time, she finally came up with a word related to "ordin-" that could barely pass as praise.

Ordinarily amazing?

"It's a bit impolite to praise a brush like that," Qi Yuan smiled, not minding.

The strange painter's eyes remained fixed on the brush, her gaze constantly shifting: "What exactly is this? Could it be a supreme treasure? No, supreme treasures are just legends, even Yang Spirits rarely encounter them, how could one appear here?"

The strange painter's heart was unsettled.

Compared to this Mountains and Rivers Brush, the brush in her hand seemed extremely shabby.

"Supreme treasure?" Qi Yuan was stunned for a moment. "I don't know, but this brush is quite powerful."

In the past, the East Lord had used this brush to turn the virtual into reality, creating the Mountains and Seas Scroll.

Based on the description, it could indeed be called a supreme treasure.

But Qi Yuan wasn't clear about what level of treasure the one in the Supreme Radiance Palace, or the supreme treasure behind the mark, were.

"The brush is ready, now it's your turn to tell me about the underworld."
