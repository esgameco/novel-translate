But the enemy was the Moluo race.

After the great changes in heaven and earth, and the rise of martial arts, the Wangyue Continent produced many powerful experts.

But compared to the Moluo race, the gap was still too large.

The entire Fengui Kingdom had only a few third-realm cultivators, as well as the fourth-realm Luo Xi who had returned from the Moon Dynasty.

According to the classification of the Canglanjie, the third realm was equivalent to the Yuan Dan stage.

Among the soldiers, only one in ten was likely to be a martial artist.

"I wonder if Luo Xi has managed to bring reinforcements," the young queen said with a desolate expression.

General Qi fell silent upon hearing this.

The conditions Luo Xi offered were unlikely to persuade people from other worlds.

"I am willing to die for Fengui, willing to die for Wangyue!" General Qi said in a deep voice.

The war before them was not just a war of national annihilation, but a war to destroy their world.

The people of the Wangyue Continent had become delicacies in the mouths of foreign races.

Only by fighting with all their might did they have a glimmer of hope for survival.

Just then, an ethereal voice drifted over.

"I don't need you to die for me."

The killing intent emanating from this person from another realm made heaven and earth tremble.

Could such a god of killing really be Fengui's savior?

All the people of Fengui, seeing that white radiance, inexplicably felt warmth and a sense of closeness in their hearts.
