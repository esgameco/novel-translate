In the ancient mirror.

A man in a blood-red robe wielding a blood-colored long sword walked through the vast desert.

The blood-red robe fluttered in the wind. From afar, the solitary figure looked like a streak of bloody sunset.

The wounded young woman, clutching her bloody wound, resolutely chased after that blood-colored figure.

Everyone present was somewhat moved.

"Such a cruel and merciless sword strike on your body, yet you still pursue him for thousands of miles. Tianxin Palace Master, you really are... infatuated," Ling Yayi sighed.

Ning Tao's expression remained unchanged, only staring at the mirror surface: "She is her, I am me."

She naturally did not want to accept that the young woman in the ancient mirror was her reality.

The scene changed again.

The delicate young woman with willow-leaf eyebrows found the man in the blood-red robe again. She stared at him blankly, softly murmuring: "Blood Pearl..."

It seemed she wanted to say something more, but could not get the words out, only able to softly call his name.

The man in the blood-red robe looked up, his gaze cold and emotionless. There was no young woman in his line of sight.

What he saw was only air, a vague figure with an indistinct face.

"Everything is illusory, everything is forgotten."

He drew his sword again, a flash of blood-red sword light.

This time, his sword seemed to strike with more force. The young woman was cut in half at the waist.

The young woman's face bore an expression of disbelief, tears already in her eyes: "Blood Pearl..."

She could not say anything else, only softly calling out.

Seeing this scene, the expressions of those present were somewhat complex. Feelings of anger arose in their hearts towards that blood-colored figure.

"This person is too heartless!"

"Without feeling or loyalty, he must be a demon."

"I don't know what happened either, let's keep watching."

A trace of melancholy flashed through the eyes of the White Emperor's avatar.

Is this the suffering experienced in the Gate of Ten Thousand Demons?

The scene continued. The blood-red robed figure wandered aimlessly through the mortal world.

It seemed that without the young woman's attachment, he walked faster and killed more viciously.

When he encountered a town, he drew his sword and killed.

When he encountered a city, he drew his sword and slaughtered.

All living beings that entered his sight, he killed, killed, killed.

Those present were shocked: "This is a god of slaughter!"

"He must have fallen into demonic ways!"

"Tianxin Palace Master, how could you love such a great demon?"

Ning Tao watched coldly, biting her lip, not speaking.

The blood-colored figure was like the strongest demon in the world, slaughtering all enemies that entered his sight.

Wind and snow raged, wild gales howled.

On the city walls, a general in heavy armor led an army of tens of thousands out of the city to face that blood-colored figure.

Tens of thousands of troops marched bravely to their deaths.

The great battle lasted seven days and seven nights.

The entire nation's army gathered there.

That blood-colored figure was like the god of death, constantly reaping the lives of the world.

Finally, one night, the blood-red robed man was exhausted. He tightly gripped his broken sword, confusion in his eyes: "Am I... this weak?"

The blood-red robe did not understand why he was so weak.

He clearly remembered that he had once slaughtered three thousand cultivators of the same level without changing expression.

Why could such an army take him down?

The blood-colored figure was captured, heavy chains locked onto his body.

His entire person was wrapped in layer after layer of iron chains.

The people of the entire capital came out, spreading the news. They watched the blood-colored figure in the prisoner cart, dancing and laughing with joy on their faces.

Cursing and insulting.

Rotten eggs, stones, rotten cabbage were all thrown at the blood-colored figure.

The great general of the nation had captured the blood demon. The blood demon was paraded for seven days, to be executed by fire at the palace gates on the seventh day.

For seven days, the capital was extraordinarily lively.

All the gloom was vented upon that blood-colored robed figure.

After seven days passed, the blood-red robe was tied to an iron pillar.

Below was a high pile of firewood.

Women cursed at him viciously: "Demon, burn him to death, burn him!"

"Such a demon should die, fall into hell forever, never to reincarnate!"

Hearing the insults, confusion appeared in the eyes of the blood-red robed figure: "Am I... a demon?"

He felt he had forgotten something.

The roaring fire was lit, the smoke making it hard for him to open his eyes.

A somewhat familiar voice came: "Blood Pearl..."

A petite figure flew over the crowd below, tightly embracing the man tied in chains. She tried hard to break the chains, but to no avail.

"Blood Pearl..." The young woman was anxious. As the fire spread, she hugged the blood-red robe tightly, constantly pushing his body, as if trying to wake him up.

"You..." The blood-red robed figure had a moment of clarity, "Who are you?"

He opened his eyes, but could not see anything.

He clearly felt someone embracing him, but he could not see them.

"I..." The young woman wanted to say something, but could not speak. She could only keep calling the name "Blood Pearl".

The fire continued to spread. Under the watch of thousands upon thousands of people, their faces showed joy and happiness.

The young woman grasped the blood-red robe, unwilling to let go even as the fire burned her body.

The delicate young woman tightly hugged that blood-red robe, consumed by the great fire in the icy snow.

Snow continued to fall, the fire burned more and more fiercely, until... complete nothingness.

Ning Tao remained silent.

Ling Yayi continued to sigh: "Truly infatuated."

As he spoke, he glanced at Ning Tao, his expression tragic.

"That demon deserved to die, but it's a pity about the young woman who jumped into the flames with him," He Wu said.

"Truly unwise," a woman sighed. "Although we are the Thirteen Demons of the North, we should not sympathize with or pity a demon who kills innocents indiscriminately, much less fall in love with one."

"Tianxin Palace Master, I didn't expect you to have such a past," someone said from the side.

The young woman in the scene appeared foolish to everyone present.

At this time, the scene in the ancient mirror changed again.

Everyone was startled: "It's not over yet?"

The White Emperor's avatar said softly: "There is reincarnation in the world, let us all observe."

Everyone looked.

It was still in an icy, snowy land.

A young woman tightly hugged an infant, walking barefoot through the snow.

The sky was pitch black, only the snow was still white.

Countless torches spread, shouts came from behind.

"Put down the demon child!"

"This infant is considered inauspicious, it must be killed!"

The young woman hugged the infant tightly, evading the pursuit of hundreds of people.

The two fled into the mountains, entering a cave.

The young woman looked at the infant, a gentle smile in her eyes: "Blood Pearl..."

Her finger unconsciously moved to his mouth.

But unexpectedly, the infant suddenly opened its mouth wide. Rows of tiny teeth bit down hard, fresh blood staining his mouth red.

The young woman's heart jolted, but she hugged the infant tightly.

Those watching had stern expressions: "This is truly a demon... it should be killed!"

The scene changed. The demon infant of the past became the blood-red robed man from before.

He was very antisocial, seeming to rarely interact with others.

He sat by a stream, staring blankly at the water every day.

When fish swam by in the water, a flash of blood-red appeared in his eyes.

With a rip, the fish's body was pierced through.

Like red ink dripping in, spreading.

The delicate young woman appeared, her eyes filled with heartache.

The blood-red robe suddenly turned: "I'm not Blood Pearl, I... am a demon, I am the Great Demon!"

After entering the Gate of Ten Thousand Demons, the Great Demon's consciousness began to assimilate him.

The path he walked was the path the Great Demon had once walked.

Otherwise, if he were Qi Yuan, how could he have been captured by an army?

He was merely experiencing the Great Demon's cycle of reincarnation.

He glanced at where the young woman was, then his figure vanished.

The young woman anxiously chased after him.

Hearing this, those present were shocked.

"He is the Great Demon...!"

"The Great Demon!"

The supreme beings of the South may not know about the Great Demon, but those of the North knew very well.

Legend had it that all demonic creatures in the world were created by the Great Demon.

The current battle between North and South, the current tragic state of the Liufeng Realm, were all caused by the Great Demon.

This blood-red robed man was actually the legendary Great Demon.

Wouldn't that make Ning Tao... a demoness! Everyone looked at Ning Tao, deep wariness in their eyes, unconsciously moving away from her.

Surprise also flashed through Baize's eyes. He thought of something and said solemnly: "This is an illusion, not reality. Rather, he... has fallen into the Great Demon's reincarnation cycle.

Rather than saying these things happened to him, it's better to say this is the Great Demon's past!"

These events all happened to the Great Demon, none of it was real.

Only that out-of-place golden oriole, constantly trying to awaken the blood-red robe, everything was real for her.

In the scene, that blood-red robe left the mountains, like a demon escaping Pandora's box.

He wielded a long sword, constantly killing.

On a rainy night, he killed an entire household.

That young woman appeared again, anxiously calling out: "Blood Pearl..."

Killing intent flashed in the eyes of the blood-red robed youth. He said to the air: "I... am not Blood Pearl, I am the Great Demon.

All demons should slaughter all things.

All demons are hated by heaven and man."

But the young woman hugged the youth tightly, unwilling to let go.

"Die!" A merciless sword strike burst forth. The young woman's clothes were stained red, blood at the corner of her mouth.

Time passed. The self-proclaimed Great Demon in the blood-red robe wielded a long sword, slaughtering all living beings. It was as if that was the only meaning in his life.
