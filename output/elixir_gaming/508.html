He was somewhat worried.

As the words fell, its body suddenly plunged, the sound of the wind cutting through the air.

Zhan Lei's steel ball shot up into the sky.

Unfortunately, she was nearly two realms lower than the Winged Snake Elder.

The outcome was decided in a single exchange.

The Winged Snake Elder, if ranked in Ninguo, would be among the top thirty on the Grandmaster list, worthy of the title of a Grandmaster.

Zhan Lei coughed up blood, her face ashen.

Seeing this, Tu Shu was anxious, but he was helpless.

"Little Tu, if you hadn't plotted against me, perhaps only you would have died today, but now... there are two more." the Black Crow Elder said ominously.

The young master Tu was now very anxious, filled with fear.

At the Peak Grandmaster level, the Tus only had one such powerhouse, and he had been in seclusion for years, unaware of the situation here.

"I..." Tu gritted his teeth, feeling the path ahead was bleak.

At this moment, perhaps only the one from the Shen family could save him.

But the one from the Shen family, like their family patriarch, had been in seclusion, where would he appear from?

Suddenly, he thought of something, throwing caution to the wind, a sword of light appeared in his hand.

The light gathered, crystalline and sparkling with a cold glint.

"An interesting little toy." the Black Crow Elder glanced at it, giving his evaluation, then sneered, "Unexpectedly, a mere Profound Armored Soldier Cultivator dares to wield a sword in resistance?"

Tu was now in a desperate state, recalling Qi Yuan's words.

His heart was anxious.

"Senior, please don't deceive me, don't deceive me!"

"I want to walk tall in Ninguo!"

But ten thousand taels of silver could not possibly hire a Peak Grandmaster Armored Soldier.

Thinking of this, he still gritted his teeth and shouted.

"Summon! The Deity!"

The special ability activated by spending ten thousand taels of silver, if it was truly real! This scene made the Winged Snake unable to help but turn his gaze sideways: "The ant... has unleashed its ultimate move?"

The words were full of mockery.

The Black Crow Elder also laughed continuously.

Only Tu Shu had an expression of admiration: "As expected of our Tu family's son, keep your spirit up, don't lose face, use the sword to hack it, let them see that the bones of the Tu family men are not soft!"

Tu Shu's words almost made the young master Tu draw his sword and charge, before being slapped to death by a snake tail.

At this moment, the young master Tu looked at the sword of light, filled with expectation.

Alas, the light remained calm, without any special occurrence.

Could it be... that person had deceived him?

But thinking about it, ten thousand taels of silver, to summon the avatar of a deity, and three times at that, such a thing... no fool would believe it.

He was utterly despairing.

But just then, suddenly, the heavens above parted, and a stream of light descended from the sky.

In front of the young master Tu, a ball of radiance appeared, shining brightly.

A sense of majesty, sacredness, and holiness permeated the air.

The light orb was not large, just about the size of a person.

Completely white, the specific form could not be discerned, but the sacred aura emanated from within.

The Winged Snake Elder, the Black Crow Elder, and the others could not help but have their gazes drawn to that ball of light.

"What is this?" the Winged Snake was somewhat wary.

"This light descending from the heavens..." the Black Crow hesitated.

Just then, a majestic voice suddenly resounded.

"Where there is no light, the Deity shall descend!"

Boom! The light shattered in an instant! A tall figure emerged, bathed in light, stepping firmly and striding powerfully.

He was entirely forged of light, wearing armor of light, shimmering and resplendent, like a divine being descended from the heavens.

Dragon scales and phoenix feathers intertwined, totems covering his body.

The radiance was at times fierce, at times gentle, a combination of tyranny and tenderness throughout.

"It's true, it's true!" the young master Tu murmured, his gaze burning with fervor.

"What is this exactly?" the Black Crow was extremely wary.

"Pretentious nonsense!" the Winged Snake snorted coldly, for he could not see through the suddenly appearing tall figure.

Tu Shu and Zhan Lei looked at the young master Tu, utterly puzzled.

The young master Tu stood up and shouted towards the figure of light: "Deity, descend and slay these two evil spirits!"

As his voice fell, the gaze of the light figure fell upon the Winged Snake and the Black Crow.

In that gaze, the Winged Snake saw many things - sacredness, absolute indifference, and the order of justice.

"What... are you exactly?" the Winged Snake felt a chill down his spine.

After all, the Moso Kingdom was rather isolated, and even Heavenly Armored Soldiers were extremely rare, this scene was beyond his comprehension.

However, considering the young master Tu's words, he had a terrible guess.

"Deity, are you the incarnation of a god?" thinking of this, the Winged Snake's back shivered.

Gods existed only in legends.

It was rumored that in the Middle Continent Luosha Kingdom, above the Heavenly Armored Soldiers, there was an existence known as the Deity!

But the Deity was far removed from them.

How could this be the incarnation of a god? Yet at this moment, it had already developed a desire to retreat.

The item was important.

What if this was truly the incarnation of a god? It glanced at the Black Crow, and their eyes met.

The two old monsters had the same thought.

"Flee!"

Without any hesitation, they frantically headed towards the Kunling Ford.

The Kunling Ford was difficult to enter, even Peak Grandmaster Armored Soldiers could not enter unharmed.

But this did not apply to those with the Thorny Blood.

Their idea was simple, first enter the Kunling Ford to hide, and then escape.

The divine light figure stood quietly, silent.

It did not pursue, merely watching as the two fled.

Seeing this, the young master Tu was puzzled.

"Could this be a substandard product, summoning a mere illusion... scaring them away?"

He momentarily thought of this.

But even if it was fake, being able to scare away the Black Crow Elder and the Winged Snake Elder was still a great profit.

At this time, Tu Shu came over, a look of amazement on his face: "This thing..."

However, just then, a sudden change occurred.

"As I, the Deity, descend!"

The light figure's voice rang out with majesty.

As if countless believers were calling out.

The figure of the light incarnation began to grow taller.

Three meters, six meters, twenty feet, thirty feet... until it towered at a staggering one hundred feet!
