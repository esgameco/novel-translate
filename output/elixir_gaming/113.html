In the vast sky, the whispers were like sweet dew, falling onto his body.

His cracked body became smooth, his parched soul was filled for the first time.

After an unknown amount of time, he suddenly opened his eyes, with a terrifying light bursting forth.

"I... have enlightened!"

Chen Huan looked at Qi Yuan in his blood-red robe, his heart now filled with mixed emotions he couldn't describe.

Especially the way of teaching.

Even the White Emperor and the Supreme Lord couldn't teach in a way that allowed cultivators to know and comprehend the origin.

Could it be... that the Guardian of No Return City was a great being reincarnated? But even the greatest being shouldn't be able to teach the origin, right?

"How is it?" Qi Yuan's voice came, pulling Chen Huan out of his thoughts.

Chen Huan looked at Qi Yuan. The blood robe had faded, and he had returned to the appearance Chen Huan was familiar with.

That sense of awe and reverence diminished somewhat.

Chen Huan suddenly fell to his knees with a thud: "Father, father, I am your long-lost son!"

As he spoke, he hugged Qi Yuan's leg.

"Get lost!" Qi Yuan kicked Chen Huan away. "Hurry up and ascend to supreme, complete my plan!"

After speaking, Qi Yuan's figure disappeared.

Leaving only Chen Huan alone on the city wall, dejected.

"What a pity... I missed the chance to acknowledge him as my father." Chen Huan was disappointed, his expression like someone who went to the bathroom last night without paper, made do with a two-yuan lottery ticket, only to find out the next day that the ticket had won several million.

"However..." Chen Huan's spirits lifted again, "If I can't be the big shot's son, being his henchman isn't bad either!"

"Who would have thought that at just 1,100 years old, I could ascend to the supreme realm, and not just an ordinary supreme at that!"

Thinking of this, Chen Huan was extremely excited.

The origin taught by Qi Yuan was different from what ordinary supreme beings comprehended.

Although he didn't know what it was like for other supreme beings to comprehend the origin, he knew there was a difference.

Because those who barely comprehended the origin could only create the lowest level of origin techniques without pursuit.

But now Chen Huan felt that there were many paths available for him to choose.

Each one was a broad and grand path!

The supreme realm he was entering wasn't the lowest kind, but the kind with extreme potential!

The supreme beings guarding the endless sea of blood naturally heard the Supreme Lord's voice.

Their eyes revealed puzzled and shocked expressions.

Especially that sentence: Treat them... even better than me.

Who had appeared in the Ten Thousand Eight Thousand Cities? From the Supreme Lord's tone, that person seemed... quite extraordinary.

At the same time, in the White Emperor's Palace, a flash of light appeared.

The inner hall that had been closed for a thousand years opened, and a white crane flew out from inside, heading towards the Ten Thousand Eight Thousand Cities.

All the supreme beings who saw this were somewhat surprised.

What exactly had happened for the White Emperor's crane to fly out?

It should be known that this white crane had not appeared for thousands of years.

Even when the North-South decision was made, this white crane had never shown itself.

Countless messages spread from near the White Emperor's Palace to various parts of the Wind Realm.

The gazes of countless supreme beings all turned towards the Ten Thousand Eight Thousand Cities.

The entire Wind Realm was in turmoil beneath the surface.

"Big brother, I've ascended to supreme!" Chen Huan followed behind Qi Yuan, his tone of speech still the same as before, but with a bit more respect.

Actually, at first, he didn't know how to face Qi Yuan.

After all, he was clearly a big shot.

But after thinking for a long time, he decided to act the same as before.

Of course, he couldn't mention becoming sworn brothers again.

Perhaps there would be a chance to acknowledge him as an adoptive father in the future.

"Barely passable." Qi Yuan's spirits were also good. "Seven days to achieve supreme, it seems my meticulous plan is completely feasible."

"Big brother's plan is flawless, unprecedented and unparalleled!" Chen Huan immediately started flattering.

"Since that's the case, let's go hunt supreme beings." Qi Yuan said with anticipation.

He couldn't kill supreme beings himself, but he could have Chen Huan beat them to near death, and then he would deliver the final blow.

This way, he could gain massive amounts of experience points.

Currently, his leveling up was too slow.

Ordinary demonic creatures gave too little experience.

He needed to slay supreme beings to gain more experience points and level up to 120.

Now, with the appearance of the great supreme Purple Dao, Qi Yuan was once again restless.

He had to quickly reach level 120, comprehend the origin, and awaken origin techniques to have the power to fight.

"Hunt supreme beings?" Chen Huan was still somewhat worried. "Big brother, can you really do it?"

"What are you afraid of with you here?" Qi Yuan said confidently.

"That's not what I wanted to hear." Chen Huan didn't believe in himself.

"With me assisting on the side, supreme beings can be subdued with a wave of the hand.

Don't underestimate the value of the Milky Way's number one support."

Hearing this, Chen Huan felt much more at ease.

With the big shot as the main damage dealer, he just needed to provide some support.

"Big brother, how should we go about finding supreme beings?" Chen Huan asked.

"Just follow me, you can't go wrong."

Qi Yuan often went out to hunt demonic creatures, and occasionally had some "affairs", so he knew some places where supreme beings often appeared.

He had some understanding of the supreme beings in the Thousand Cities region.

"Let's go!" Qi Yuan said.

"Alright." Chen Huan hurriedly followed, his heart beating fast.

Hunting supreme beings!

That's hunting supreme beings!

What qualifications did he have to hunt supreme beings!

Liuchuan Demon Cave.

Neither demon controllers nor demon slayers were willing to come to this place.

Because every so often, endless streams of demonic creatures would emerge from here to participate in the great battle of the Ten Thousand Eight Thousand Cities.

At this moment, a woman with a gloomy expression appeared in the demon cave.

Below, over a dozen national guardians and even terrestrial myths trembled in fear, prostrating themselves on the ground, clearly terrified of this woman.
