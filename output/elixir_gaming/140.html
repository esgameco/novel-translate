True Lord Wukong glanced at the Duke of East Prosperity and said in a buzzing voice: "This matter is not your fault. It's that Blood-Robed Sword God who is stirring up trouble!"

Back then, True Lord Wukong had come to Chen Kingdom for leisure and took a liking to the Marquis of Earth Sword's mansion.

Beneath the mansion was a spirit eye, which produced the treasure Xingluo Essence.

The Duke of East Prosperity then drove out all the people from the Marquis of Earth Sword's mansion.

The Marquis of Earth Sword's over 90-year-old, decrepit daughter was forced to live in a dog kennel.

Later, the Duke of East Prosperity even secretly took action, massacring the entire household of the Marquis of Earth Sword.

After all, since they had already offended people, they might as well cut the weeds and eliminate the roots, leaving no future troubles! The Duke of East Prosperity came here now to curry favor with the Palace of Light.

The Duke of East Prosperity was just saying these words.

He actually hoped the Blood-Robed Sword God would clash with the Palace of Light.

Using the Palace of Light's hand to eliminate the Blood-Robed Sword God.

"Apologize? Hmph!" True Lord Wukong was angry.

This incident had blown up, causing quite a stir even in Cuiyun Continent.

He had been forced to come out of seclusion from his cultivation and come to this godforsaken place to apologize. How could he not be full of pent-up anger? Apologize?

Let's see if that Blood-Robed Sword God dares to accept an apology from the Palace of Light.

At this time, many cultivators from Chen Kingdom were standing on the side of the Palace of Light.

They were even more angry and disgusted with the Blood-Robed Sword God, wishing to get rid of him as soon as possible.

They feared that because of the Blood-Robed Sword God's matter, it would affect their status in the Palace of Light.

"There's no need for the True Lords of the Palace of Light to take action. If he comes, we will capture him and make him apologize to the True Lords of the Palace of Light!"

Hearing this, True Lord Wukong drank wine, his dissatisfaction at coming to this godforsaken place finally dissipating somewhat.

At this time, outside the treasure ship, on a small boat stood a young woman in blue robes, accompanied by an old woman.

The old woman looked at the huge treasure ship and said softly, "The Palace of Light is here. Would Miss Ya like to board the ship and speak with them?"

This was something that even the top three on the Eastern Lands Genius Rankings dreamed of.

When she entered the Purple Mansion realm and understood the essence of this world's spiritual energy, she would be able to comprehend her own origin divine ability from her path.

Jiang Ya stood at the front of the boat, her consciousness immersed in a jade slip.

Vast waves, moonlight flowing over misty islets.

The old woman also raised her head, a hint of solemnity in her eyes.

A full moon hung in the sky, its cold light shining on him, reflecting a red glow.

"Such a strong bloody aura!"

"How many people has he killed?"

Their eyes were filled with doubt and a hint of fear.

Such a great demon should be slain directly.

"Such a demon, your ancestors for eighteen generations should go to hell!" the Duke of East Prosperity also seized the opportunity to say.

At this time, a hint of confusion appeared in Qi Yuan's eyes: "Didn't you from the Palace of Light come to apologize? Is this your attitude?"

He had come here precisely because the Palace of Light said they would apologize.

He was prepared to extort something.

"If you were a righteous cultivator, I would apologize for my past minor mistakes, but... you, a demonic cultivator, are worthy of my apology?" True Lord Wukong once again stood on the moral high ground, feeling refreshed.

The path of immortality is vast, yet they are willing to subdue demons and eliminate evil, truly worthy of being called immortal heroes.

Alas, alas, I was thinking that if you compensated me with some spirit stones, I would not pursue the matter further."

As Qi Yuan spoke, he looked truly disappointed, as if in a daze.

Did this person really admire their Palace of Light?
