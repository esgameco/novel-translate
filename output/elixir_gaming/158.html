"But the Moluo clan has experts at the second step of Sky Treading!" The elderly voice said again.

They were all at the first step of Sky Treading, with only a few scattered divine apertures opened.

Facing Yin Gods at the second step of Sky Treading, they were no match at all.

At this point, the charming female voice said: "I heard that some time ago, a Blood-Robed Sword God destroyed the Palace of Light. The battle products that fell out of the virtual realm are not simple. This indicates that a battle at the level of the second step of Sky Treading took place in the virtual realm. "You really are daydreaming, would the Moluo clan give up a Yin God artifact?"

"This Venerable One did not calculate against him, it was his own wish to enter the Moon-Gazing Continent. This Venerable One merely fulfilled his small wish. As for dying on the Moon-Gazing Continent, that can't be blamed on me." The childish voice said righteously.

Undercurrents surged in the Eastern Land.

Even the small Great Shang was not peaceful at this time.

The sect master of the Divine Light Sect, True Lord Withered Wood, had been worried for days.

With Wood Void dead in the Divine Light Sect, the Red Sword Gate would have more reason to make trouble for the Divine Light Sect.

Although the elder of the Red Sword Gate was still severely injured, he was after all at the Purple Palace realm.

Purple Palace and Divine Infant realms were not on the same level at all.

Just as Yin Gods and Purple Palace cultivators were not on the same level.

The sect's grand formation had already been activated, just waiting for the Red Sword Gate's elder to come and fight.

At this moment, a terrifying aura swept across the sky.

True Lord Withered Wood's expression changed slightly: "Purple Palace?"

The elders and stewards of the Divine Light Sect also became solemn.

"They really came!"

"It's over, the Divine Light Sect is finished!"

"No, not just one, but two Purple Palace experts!" True Lord Withered Wood sensed two powerful auras, and his heart sank into complete despair.

One Purple Palace expert could easily destroy the Divine Light Sect, let alone two.

That undying old Purple Palace expert from the Red Sword Gate even found help?

How shameless!

"Sect Master, let's fight them to the death!" A Golden Core elder said angrily.

"We'll defend the sect to our last breath!"

The sect was united in purpose.

At this time, outside the sect, Mei Shan and Xu Yeli looked at each other in bewilderment.

"Did we come to the wrong place?"

"We shouldn't have."

"Why do I feel like they see us as great enemies?"

The two were confused.

Finally, they spoke.

"Mei Shan of the Blood Robe Alliance has come to visit the sect master of the Divine Light Sect!"

"Xu Yeli of the Blood Robe Alliance has come to visit the sect master of the Divine Light Sect!"

Their voices rolled through the mountain-protecting formation, directly entering the ears of all the Divine Light Sect disciples and elders.

These elders and disciples looked at each other in confusion.

"Blood Robe Alliance, what's that?"

"What's going on, isn't it supposed to be the Red Sword Gate?"

True Lord Withered Wood looked puzzled, and Elder Xuan Chong was also bewildered.

The elders flew to the front gate of the sect and looked out.

True Lord Withered Wood's eyes showed a surprised expression.

Because the two old men before him both had auras as deep as the sea.

Especially the one in front, who didn't seem to be an ordinary Purple Palace expert.

What were such powerful experts doing at the Divine Light Sect?

"Are you the sect master of the Divine Light Sect?" Mei Shan looked at True Lord Withered Wood, his attitude exceptionally amiable towards a Divine Infant realm cultivator.

After all, that was the elder of the Blood-Robed Sword God.

"This junior is Withered Wood, sect master of the Divine Light Sect. May I ask who you two are?" True Lord Withered Wood was puzzled inside.

"Our Blood Robe Alliance's leader has a gift to give to you." Mei Shan spoke.

A purple-gold gourd appeared in his hand.

Facing the mountain-protecting formation of the Divine Light Sect, the gourd seemed to pass through without hindrance, directly entering the Divine Light Sect and landing in True Lord Withered Wood's hand.

"The purple-gold gourd?" Looking at this familiar gourd, True Lord Withered Wood's eyes showed a shocked expression.

It was the lost treasure of his Wood clan!

For a thousand years, he hadn't seen this clan treasure!

Now, it suddenly appeared!

Xuan Chong looked at the purple-gold gourd, his eyes also fixed on it.

The other members of the sect were confused, not knowing why the sect master and Elder Xuan Chong had such a big reaction.

True Lord Withered Wood was excited inside, holding the treasure, with various memories flashing through his mind.

He looked at the treasure, but a puzzled expression flashed across his face.

The purple-gold gourd was listed as a reward for the Eastern Land Prodigy Ranking.

How did it appear here?

Suddenly, he thought of Qi Yuan and Kang Fulu.

He had only revealed the matter of his identity and the treasure to these two people.

Now that the treasure had returned, it must be related to these two!

Who was it?

True Lord Withered Wood first ruled out Kang Fulu.

After all, Kang Fulu was his son-in-law, and he knew his capabilities.

Only Ruan Yixi's disciple Qi Yuan was truly unfathomable to him.

Many thoughts flashed through his mind.

Could it be that Qi Yuan, that pretty boy, had caught the eye of the Blood Robe Alliance's leader?

It must be said, True Lord Withered Wood really thought this was possible.

Although Qi Yuan was not very proper in his behavior, he was very handsome, privately ranked as the tenth beauty of Great Shang.

If not for his outstanding personality, he might have been ranked first.

It was quite possible for him to be taken as a Dao companion by a senior.

Qi Yuan had sacrificed a lot for him, and True Lord Withered Wood felt moved.

Were these the two subordinates of Qi Yuan that Jiang Lingsu had mentioned?

If so, it would be easy to explain.

True Lord Withered Wood asked: "May I ask, senior, is the leader of the Blood Robe Alliance male or female?"

"Of course it's a man."

True Lord Withered Wood's expression froze, his gratitude increasing immeasurably.

Qi Yuan's sacrifice... was too great.

"The leader also said that you are his elder, having taken care of him a lot, and he has another gift for you." Mei Shan spoke.

True Lord Withered Wood was stunned.

Elder?

Taken care of?

What he had thought earlier was all wrong?

He knew the leader of the Blood Robe Alliance?

Could it be...

As for the gift, he didn't think much about it.

At this time, Mei Shan's eyes held a smile: "Please look, who is this?"

Xu Yeli pushed with his hand, and a figure fell into the Divine Light Sect.

"Purple Palace!"

"This is a Purple Palace expert?"

The figure's aura was weakened, bound by immortal-binding ropes, but any Divine Infant realm cultivator could see at a glance that it was a Purple Palace expert!

A Purple Palace expert, bound by an artifact, thrown before them like a dead dog, how could they not be shocked!

After all, this was the first time in their lives they had seen a Purple Palace expert up close.

Elder Xuan Chong's eyes saw the face of that Purple Palace expert, and his heart skipped a beat, countless emotions surging up.

"Hong Lian... that old bastard!"

The person before them was none other than Hong Lian, that old bastard!

Back then, he had forced the Holy Consort to her death, destroyed the Wood clan, and killed his entire family and clan!

How could he forget that face?

He saw that arrogant face in his dreams day and night!

True Lord Withered Wood's excitement was no less than Xuan Chong's.

He stared intently at Hong Lian, his heart shocked, elated, and puzzled.

"The leader said, this person is for you to dispose of as you see fit. We must return to report to the Blood Robe Alliance immediately. To avoid any accidents, you might as well behead Hong Lian and display his head publicly." Mei Shan suggested.

The so-called public beheading meant cutting off his head and displaying it.
