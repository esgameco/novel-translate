The Second Day.

In the imperial court, dark clouds gathered.

Minster of Rites Kong Qi, tall and handsome in his deep robes, was filled with anger: "Prime Minister, for three consecutive days, the Dragon Battle Army, the Black Flag Army, and the Liaodong Army have brazenly entered the imperial capital. Are you... planning a rebellion?"

A hush fell over the court.

It could be said that, except for a few officials, the vast majority of officials were completely obedient to Sima Ting.

Now, the arrival of the three armies in the capital was exactly what they had been hoping for.

It was time for the Southern Qin Dynasty to change.

Of course, they did not know that the entry of these armies was not a rebellion, but rather... just some sacrificial lambs.

And they themselves were also sacrificial lambs, no different from those soldiers and common people.

Sima Ting strode forward majestically and said to the Minister of Rites: "The three armies have entered the capital according to the orders of the Ministry of War, in compliance with the laws of Southern Qin."

The other officials all burst into laughter.

The esteemed Kong Qi, known for his gentle nature and upright character, was now enraged by Sima Ting.

"Sima Ting's intentions are known to all!" Kong Qi said, suddenly bursting into tears, "Among all the civil and military officials, not a single one... Southern Qin... is doomed!"

The other officials looked at Kong Qi with mocking expressions.

Kong Qi was a qualified esteemed scholar, but certainly not a mature official.

At this moment, the previously silent Duke of Yu spoke up: "Prime Minister, the purpose of mobilizing the three armies into the city, does His Majesty know of it?"

Sima Ting glanced at the Duke of Yu, surprised that this old turtle would also speak up for Jinli.

But none of this mattered anymore.

"Isn't His Majesty already aware of it now?" Sima Ting looked at Jinli, seated on the throne, with a smile, showing no respect at all.

The late emperor was a puppet in his hands.

Jinli was also placed on the throne by him.

It could be said that all the civil and military officials were his people.

He continued, "Recently, there have been demonic forces troubling the imperial capital. I have mobilized the three armies to ensure His Majesty's safety.

During this period, an edict will be issued prohibiting anyone from leaving the capital. After half a month, when the demonic forces are eliminated, the three armies will withdraw. There is no need for you all to be anxious!"

Sima Ting then turned and left abruptly, without even a glance.

The other officials also quickly departed one by one.

Some even secretly paid their respects to Jinli before hurriedly leaving.

Soon, the vast palace was left with only a few officials and the troubled Jinli.

When Jinli wearily returned to her sleeping quarters, she looked at the Linglong Jade Pendant in her hand.

If the country fell and she died, could the promise made back then still be fulfilled?

She looked up at the towering ancient Qi tree, lost in thought.

At this moment, Qin Aunt entered, her face heavy with fatigue and a hint of anger: "Your Majesty, the three armies have all entered, and the city gates are sealed.

If Sima Ting resorts to desperate measures, this old servant will risk her life to take Your Majesty away!"

The commander of the imperial guards, Hua Xian, also knelt on one knee: "I am willing to die for Your Majesty!"

Jinli looked at these loyal subjects and slowly said, "I'm sorry... for all of you."

She had never been a capable empress.

Even her cultivation had been secretly sabotaged by Sima Ting's people, progressing slowly.

"Sima Ting is extremely ambitious and will likely make his move within half a month.

I... will not be able to leave the imperial city.

As for you all, there is still a chance.

Leave the capital and go to the XuanYuan Forbidden Land, you may escape this calamity."

Jinli arranged a way out for Qin Aunt and her other trusted aides.

Aunt Qin's gaze became profound: "Your Majesty, that old scoundrel Sima Ting may not have simply usurped the throne. In the Prime Minister's mansion, I sensed several extremely obscure and powerful auras, including those of the Demon Clan!"

"The Demon Clan?" Jinli was stunned.

"And the auras of the Demon Clan outside the city seem even stronger, with Imperial-level powerhouses... no less than three of them!"

"What?" Jinli was completely shocked.

She didn't know what Sima Ting was planning to do...

Under the moonlight, Qi Yuan was clad in a blood-red battle armor, unleashing his combat prowess to the fullest.

He charged forward like a giant, rampaging dinosaur, slaying numerous Robe Monsters with each impact.

His experience bar surged upwards! Suddenly, Qi Yuan let out a loud roar.

"I'm level 90 now!"

He had finally reached level 90! At level 90, according to the division of the Wangyue Continent, he had reached the realm above the Celestial Domain, the God Realm.

This was a qualitative difference from level 89!

Qi Yuan could feel his strength, speed, and physique all surging across the board.

If previously he had been afraid of the Robe Monsters' attacks and only dared to venture out at night, now he was fearless.

He could grind monsters during the day, no matter how many Robe Monsters came, he would slay them all!

"Two new skills!"

Upon breaking through to level 90, he had gained two additional skills.

Thinking about this, he felt that Little Marry was truly the best Robe Monster in the world.

Wearing her not only increased his level by 5, but also allowed him to freeload two new skills.

He began to examine his skills.

"Spirit Stabilization?"

The first minor skill, called Spirit Stabilization, could stabilize the soul, allowing him to remain steadfast in the face of illusions and phantasms.

"Death Radiance?"

The major skill was called Death Radiance.

Looking at this major skill, Qi Yuan couldn't help but be amazed.

This skill was about dancing on the tightrope of death.

Only when life was on the brink, when treading the critical line of death, could one unleash the most dazzling brilliance and the mightiest combat power.

Moreover, this was not only a powerful offensive skill, but also a self-healing skill.

Damaging the enemy would allow him to absorb their life force to heal his own injuries.

In simple terms, the lower his health, the stronger he would become, and it also had the ability to drain the enemy's health and mana.

"Why didn't they give me the legendary sword Siming? Then I would be truly invincible!"

Qi Yuan felt that this skill was a bit dangerous and would need to be combined with the legendary sword Siming.

That way, he might even be able to steal the enemy's crystal.

"But it's still very good. When at full health, using Death Radiance can double my combat power."

Gripping the blood-red greatsword, Qi Yuan's gaze, through the Robe Prohibition, looked towards the deepest part.

"The Ugly Monster..."

"No, the Ugly Robe Monster King, I'm coming for you."

In the depths of the Robe Prohibition, the Ugly Robe Monster King, who had been staring at Qi Yuan all along, also swiftly approached Qi Yuan.

The two rushed towards each other in an instant, and then the most intense collision occurred!

"Ah!"

The blood-red aura on Qi Yuan's armor seemed to be about to overflow. He raised his sword, and the sword, which was originally only a few meters long, had now grown to over a thousand meters in length, fueled by the red blood aura.

"Eat my sword!"

With Death Radiance, Qi Yuan's fighting style had completely changed.

He pursued the strongest attack power and the widest range of destruction.

Although the aftershock couldn't harm the Ugly Robe Monster King, it could slay the other Robe Monsters.

This provided experience points and enemy loot for blood absorption.

More importantly, he no longer had to worry about running out of mana, as he could unleash his skills without restraint!

"Today, I will slay you!"
