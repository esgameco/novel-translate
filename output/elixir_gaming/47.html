He stood up, patted the wooden cat puppet's head, and looked at the beautiful morning light outside: "The day's plan lies in the morning!"

He shook off his drowsiness, and his gaze swept into the storage bag.

When he saw the towering piles of books and cultivation technique jade slips, his face fell.

"Arriving in another world, and still have to 996 (work overtime)!"

At the Black Mountain Sect, Qi Yuan had painstakingly emptied the library's books and cultivation technique jade slips.

For Qi Yuan, cultivation techniques were the most important thing.

After all, most of the cultivation techniques in this world were not suitable for him.

He needed to read other books, then use the information he saw with his own eyes to accumulate knowledge, and finally perfect his own cultivation technique.

Last time, using his junior sister Jiang Lingsu's cultivation technique jade slips, over 600 of them, Qi Yuan had just skimmed through them, and it had taken him two days.

But now, the number of cultivation techniques was not just 600, and the books were much harder to comprehend than the jade slips.

Moreover, last time it was only Foundation Establishment stage cultivation techniques, which he could skim through easily. Now, he had to carefully study the Foundation Establishment stage cultivation techniques.

With so many books, he definitely couldn't finish them in a month.

Qi Yuan buried himself in the books, reading them diligently.

He read them extremely carefully.

Because this was the foundation for him to create a Foundation Establishment cultivation technique.

The dry cultivation techniques, he read them more carefully than advanced mathematics.

Unfortunately, he read too slowly, each cultivation technique took a long time to read, far from the speed of quantum reading.

"It would be better to start cultivating the Heavenly Dao Foundation Establishment as soon as possible, so that I can release my spiritual sense outwards. That way, reading books wouldn't be so tiring." Qi Yuan flipped through the books, feeling that spiritual sense was a good thing.

Unfortunately, he could only release his spiritual sense outwards after reaching the Foundation Establishment stage.

For several consecutive days, Qi Yuan read books during the day and went online to play the game at night.

With the blessing of his "wife", Qi Yuan was already able to swagger around in the Ceremonial Prohibition.

Although he still couldn't slay the Ugly Robe Demon Emperor, as his strength grew, he could now injure the Ugly Robe Demon Emperor.

The terrifying Ugly Robe Demon Emperor was also powerless against Qi Yuan.

It could only let Qi Yuan rampage freely in the Ceremonial Prohibition.

Sword Qi Shatters the Skull!

"I don't have a head, how can you shatter my skull?"

As always, Qi Yuan provided the voiceovers for these Ceremonial Prohibition monsters, entertaining himself and finding some amusement.

Otherwise, the monotonous monster grinding time would be too boring.

The experience bar was soaring.

He, at level 89, was not far from level 90.

Qi Yuan looked at the approaching Ugly Robe Demon Emperor from afar: "Time to scram."

He didn't bother fighting the Ugly Robe Demon Emperor, he would wait until he reached level 90, when he would have a better chance.

Qi Yuan immediately logged out quickly.

He had already killed enough for today, couldn't stay online any longer, or his brain would not be able to take it.

After separating from his "wife", Qi Yuan dug a hole as usual.

Suddenly, he noticed a crooked-necked tree on the grave mound, bearing several purple-hued fruits.

They looked like a combination of bayberry and apple.

He picked some and took a bite, the sour taste of bayberry exploding on his taste buds: "Not bad, do you want to try some?"

He offered it to his "wife".

His "wife" did not respond.

Qi Yuan sighed: "No mouth, can't eat, can't... Guess we'll have to get a divorce!"

His "wife" was very aggrieved, and slid out a wooden board with a "snuggle" message.

Qi Yuan smiled, embraced his "wife" and crawled into the hole.

He changed to a comfortable position, just like a salted fish lying on the bed, embracing the cold "wife", then sent a message to Jinli.

But he lowered his head and looked at the obedient and cute, silent "wife".

He paused for a moment.

Now that he had a "wife", and was still chatting with a female online friend, would that be considered cheating?

It seems that after the divorce, the court would definitely order him to compensate her.

He had nothing except for a big treasure sword.

Without much thought, Qi Yuan resumed his usual chat with Jinli.

Jinli was like the evening breeze by the river, cool and fine, blowing on one's body, like a drizzle.
