"Elder... how did you win?"

After all, he was currently only a Stellar Golden Core cultivator, not even comparable to a perfect Heavenly Dao Foundation Building. He had to hide his true abilities and conceal his trump cards.

Everyone paying attention to this post was stunned.

They didn't expect to get such an answer.

"Of course it's true. When the Divine Flower Society big shots appear, I can only be considered a small shrimp. If it wasn't for the big shots lending me Stellar Golden Pills to use, I might have died," Qi Yuan replied seriously.

Using your own things can't be called borrowing?

Meanwhile, in the Eastern Lands, inside a cave dwelling.

A man in golden robes looked at the contents of the jade slip in silence.

In our Cang Lan Realm, besides the legendary one, who else can hold up a divine position in the myriad realms and illuminate all worlds?"

He didn't even dare to mention that person's name.

Because that person... could not be spoken of.

If it was Lord Wind, it would be... normal.

Why would Lord Wind take action?"

"Rather than saying that miserly old man Lord Wind took action, I'd rather believe the Blood-Robed Sword God summoned the sun." The old man was an elder of the Dao Seeking Palace, of the same generation as Lord Wind. The two had known each other in their youth.

Unfortunately, one became a Yin God, while the other was still seeking at the late stage of the Purple Mansion realm.

He was most familiar with Lord Wind's personality.

"Sigh, you came back too quickly. A powerhouse like the Blood-Robed Sword God should be recruited into our Dao Seeking Palace.

His strength is even greater than Old Man Wutian." The white-haired old man lamented.

Old Man Wutian was among the top tier of late stage Purple Mansion cultivators in the Dao Seeking Palace.

"He survived this time, but he has thoroughly offended Venerable Ye Qiong. I estimate he's not far from death!" The golden-robed man couldn't help saying.

"If he could destroy the Palace of Light, why couldn't he kill Venerable Ye Qiong?" The white-haired old man smiled.

The golden-robed man glared at the white-haired old man: "How can the Palace of Light with just one incomplete Yin God artifact be compared to the real Venerable Ye Qiong?"

"How do you know that someone who could easily destroy the Palace of Light can't kill Venerable Ye Qiong?" The white-haired old man still smiled. "It's a pity I can't get away recently, otherwise this old man would definitely go meet this Blood-Robed Sword God."

"Blood-Robed Sword God, I heard you killed Venerable Ye Qiong's envoy on your way to the Palace of Light?"

"You even dare to kill a Yin God's envoy, big shot... you're truly strong!

Elder, you should leave the Eastern Lands quickly!" That person replied.

The cultivators following this thread were all shocked.

Fox Den Little Li looked at the content, her expression dazed.

She had just commented on the thread that she was with the Blood-Robed Sword God, defeating all enemies who came.

But no one told her the enemy was a Yin God? She went crazy, pulling her hair: "Wuwu, I'll never reply to you again!"

But then thinking about how she was getting close to the Blood-Robed Sword God, and others called her Sister Li, she couldn't resist.

Qi Yuan looked at the comments below and replied.

"His envoy threatened me, claiming he would kill me, so I had no choice but to strike first.

If Venerable Ye Qiong is also so blind, I'll have to let him know why the sun is so red!"

The Divine Flower Society cultivators below, seeing the Blood-Robed Sword God's reply, were completely shocked.

If killing Venerable Ye Qiong's envoy had offended Venerable Ye Qiong,

These words now were completely provocative!

"Elder is too fierce!"

Qi Yuan casually glanced at the comments, then switched accounts and logged into the Divine Flower Four Emperors chat group.

"I have good news for everyone, the 'Hundred Days to Destroy the Palace of Light Plan' has been completed ahead of schedule!"

"Big shot 666!"

"This queen said it, the Origin Heavenly Venerable is no ordinary person."

Rogue Immortal Venerable and Ice Mountain Domineering Woman praised enthusiastically.

"He was here a few hours ago, but later he said his old man was looking for him, so he didn't show up. Probably had some urgent matter.

"It's right not to meet. If those flirty hooves see you, they'll probably strip your clothes off." Ice Mountain Domineering Woman spoke as domineeringly as ever.

Rogue Immortal Venerable looked at this and swallowed.

He also wanted to be stripped by immortal sisters.

"One sister's family runs the Seven Sounds Pavilion. She said from now on when you go to her place to have fun, it's all free."

"Seven Sounds Pavilion?" Rogue Immortal Venerable drooled enviously.

That was even more high-end than the Cloud Palace.

Doesn't that mean the Origin Heavenly Venerable can freeload in the future?

"I don't lack any of these things. What I lack most now are cultivation techniques - the more the better, and the higher level the better," Qi Yuan said.

He wasn't interested in freeloading.

Only cultivation techniques were what he needed most.

Probably by tomorrow, Wind Moon Immortal would have a new nickname, Nose-Picking Immortal.

So now, inside my body, I have both a Golden Core and a Divine Infant!"

A false core is when a Foundation Building cultivator, with no hope of breaking through to the Golden Core stage, uses external objects to form a false core.

Qi Yuan was shocked by Great Wisdom True Lord's train of thought.

This is what it means to be a prodigy, this is what it means to have great wisdom.

The prodigies beside him looked at Great Wisdom True Lord speechlessly.

Not only do your Golden Core and Divine Infant fight, but you yourself often fight with yourself.

They knew that Great Wisdom True Lord had a split personality, and two personalities had already appeared in his body.

"Such small problems are not problems," Qi Yuan said carelessly, "Excellent techniques will always have some issues. The technique I created also has flaws. After cultivation, it causes mental confusion and poor memory.

But aren't I in good spirits now? I can eat eight steamed buns in one sitting!"

The people present looked at Qi Yuan and instantly understood.

This is called being in good spirits?

At the same time, they felt even more sorry for Wind Moon Immortal.

Why was she targeted by two mentally ill people? Too pitiful.

"Unfortunately, I have already entered the Elemental Core realm and cannot cultivate the Two Core method," Qi Yuan sighed.

Great Wisdom True Lord's path was not suitable for him.
