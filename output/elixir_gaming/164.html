At this moment, he chose to rely on someone else's power! The spiritual energy in his body was not enough to summon the projection of a fixed star.

However, summoning Ning Tao's projection was possible.

For his first time relying on someone else's power, Qi Yuan was very excited and full of anticipation.

Cheerful suona music played a light melody.

The sun in the sky seemed to flash with light.

Space seemed to freeze, time seemed to stop.

Under everyone's gaze, a woman in a black dress appeared out of thin air.

She had a face that could enchant all living beings, with skin as white as snow, so fair it was excessive, like the most exquisite porcelain in the world.

Her petite figure and long black hair cascading like a waterfall at night, her left eye pure as a light rain, like a lively spring maiden, her right eye deep and alluring, like a yandere goddess of the night.

A profound and powerful aura emanated from her, like that of a deity.

Is this the Blood Robed Sword God's trump card?

Jiang Ya instantly understood - the Blood Robed Sword God and that Yin Goddess must have a close relationship, she may even be the Yin Goddess's most valued descendant.

"Second Step of Treading the Heavens!" Elder Mo Hui's pupils contracted, sensing a threat.

He had only reached the First Step of Treading the Heavens, with just three divine apertures.

But the other party was a terrifying existence at the Second Step of Treading the Heavens.

Her strength was at least ten times his own.

Lower level cultivators may be able to fight above their level.

But which of those who have cultivated to the realm of Yin God is not a true genius?

With one less divine aperture opened than the opponent, it's very difficult to make up the difference in combat power through other means.

This gap of a full small realm makes it impossible to fight above one's level.

Even young Great Venerable Ones have no record of fighting above their level.

He wanted to escape.

At this moment, Qi Yuan's voice came: "Ning Tao, quickly help me kill him!"

"Husband, please wait a moment!" Ning Tao spoke, waving her delicate hand.

Elder Mo Hui, who had originally wanted to escape, was instantly imprisoned in mid-air.

Seeing this scene, Jiang Ya was completely stunned.

What did this goddess... call the Blood Robed Sword God? Husband???

Wasn't she supposed to be the Blood Robed Sword God's ancestor? How did they become partners?

The Blood Robed Sword God, a mere Purple Palace cultivator, actually snagged a Second Step Yin God as a wife?

This is too...

She was a bit confused as she watched the battle unfold before her.

"No!" At this moment, Elder Mo Hui roared, the divine spiritual power in all three of his divine apertures constantly circulating, trying to break through this imprisonment.

However, his divine spiritual power, compared to Ning Tao's projection, was not even one-tenth as strong.

The gap between the two was greater than that between a late-stage Nascent Soul and an early-stage Golden Core.

A difference of a major realm.

His divine spiritual power was as weak as a mountain spring to Ning Tao.

Ning Tao merely pointed a finger, and he was locked in place.

"You can't kill me, my elder brother is also at the Second Step of Treading the Heavens!" Seeing that he couldn't break free, Elder Mo Hui quickly calmed down.

Ning Tao didn't speak, but looked at Qi Yuan, leaning against his chest like a little bird: "Husband... how should we deal with him?"

Jiang Ya, watching this scene from afar, had a look of disbelief in her eyes.

The lofty Yin God was like a girl in love.

Qi Yuan helplessly played with Ning Tao's hair.

The last time she appeared, when no one was around, she called him "little blood pearl".

This time, with many people present, she called him "husband".

How could he not understand a young girl's thoughts? Looking at Ning Tao's beautiful snow-white face, he muttered: "So this is what it feels like to rely on someone else's power... it really feels... great."

After muttering, he looked at Elder Mo Hui and said arrogantly: "Your elder brother is a Yin God, my... wife is also a Yin God!"

Perhaps to show off, he called Ning Tao his wife for the first time.

Ning Tao's left eye was full of tender affection, with a maiden's shyness, while her right eye was coquettish and alluring, her face tinged with a rosy hue.

Elder Mo Hui was confused by Qi Yuan's words.

"Our Mo Luo clan has five Yin Gods coming this time..."

"My wife is a Yin God!"

"Two at the Second Step of Treading the Heavens, three..."

"My wife is a Yin God!" Qi Yuan said proudly.

No matter what Mo Hui said, he blocked it with this sentence, appearing extremely smug.

"You are not from the Wangyue Continent, why must you go to war with our Mo Luo clan for the sake of the Wangyue Continent?

The three Venerable Ones from your Canglan Realm have already reached an agreement with us to divide the Wangyue Continent.

If you spare me, our Mo Luo clan can give you one-third of the territory of the Wangyue Continent!" Elder Mo Hui was still trying.

Qi Yuan's expression turned cold: "How ridiculous, the Wangyue Continent was mine to begin with, how can you give it to me!"

Seeing that he couldn't persuade Qi Yuan, Elder Mo Hui turned to Ning Tao: "The dispute between the Mo Luo clan and the Wangyue Continent involves... two Yang Gods.

Senior, cultivating to the Second Step of Treading the Heavens is extremely difficult.

One day, senior will become a Great Venerable One and ascend to the upper realm.

Why offend our Mo Luo clan and the... Yang God behind us over these small matters?"

At this moment, Elder Mo Hui brought out his trump card.

The dispute between the Mo Luo clan and the Wangyue Continent was merely a continuation of the conflict between two Yang Gods.

Of course, the Mo Luo clan actually had no connection to that Yang God, not even counting as direct subordinates. He was just using it to intimidate Ning Tao.

Ning Tao didn't even look at Elder Mo Hui, her eyes were fixed on Qi Yuan the whole time.

Her projection had limited time, every minute and second was precious.

"Using a Yang God to scare me? If you have the ability, tell him to come down and chop me!" If Qi Yuan could be scared by such threats, he wouldn't be Qi Yuan.

Besides, it's not so easy for a Yang God to descend to the lower realm.

"Kill him!" Qi Yuan embraced Ning Tao and said softly.

Ning Tao waved her hand.

Elder Mo Hui, who was already imprisoned, underwent a terrifying transformation.

His bloated fat turned withered, all the scales on his body became dry and cracked.

His appearance changed from youthful to elderly in an instant.

His turbid eyes revealed an expression of disbelief: "Divine realm seed..."

His aura completely extinguished at this moment.

A mighty Yin God, capable of destroying an entire nation's continent, just like that... perished.

He died simply and cleanly.

As if it wasn't a deity who died, but a mortal.

Everyone present felt shocked.

This was the first time they had seen a battle between Yin Gods.

They had thought it would be a battle that darkened the sun and moon, shattered mountains and rivers, but the result was... too simple.

The Divine Envoy from before also widened his eyes.

What happened today was beyond his expectations.

At this moment, Ning Tao's figure also became more ethereal.

"Husband... call me next time you encounter a strong enemy!" Her eyes were full of attachment. After saying this, her projection completely dissipated, and she returned to the fixed star's Golden Core.

She came like wind and rain, and left like dust.

Qi Yuan felt a faint emptiness in his heart.

Silence fell around them, even the suona stopped playing. They were all shocked by the battle just now.

After a long while, Great Wisdom True Lord couldn't help but say: "Senior is truly talented, to actually have a divine wife."

If his own partner hadn't passed away early, he would probably be unable to resist asking the Blood Robed Sword God how to find a divine wife.

Jiang Ya was silent, then said worriedly: "Senior still needs to be careful, the Mo Luo clan has more than one Yin God, and there are two at the Second Step of Treading the Heavens."

Jiang Ya's concern was not unfounded.

The Blood Robed Sword God's partner was just one Yin God.

As the saying goes, two fists are hard-pressed against four hands. The Mo Luo clan lost one Yin God, but still had four Yin Gods, with strong power.

"I have a plan that can solve senior's predicament," Great Wisdom True Lord said seriously. "As long as senior finds a few more divine partners, and calls them all over when encountering enemies, what is there to fear from the Mo Luo clan?"

Hearing this, Qi Yuan felt it made a lot of sense.

"It's fine."

Although he was relying on someone else's power now, it doesn't mean he'll do so for his whole life.

Before long, he would completely take control of the Wangyue Continent.

At that time, he would be the true ruler of the Wangyue Continent.

"Rest for half an hour, then we'll continue to exterminate the Mo Luo!" Qi Yuan looked at Elder Mo Hui's corpse, and also glanced at the five Purple Palace cultivators who had long been shattered by the aftershocks.

A strange light flashed in his eyes.

Just now, when Ning Tao killed Elder Mo Hui, he noticed that his game jade slip underwent a slight change.

While everyone was cleaning up the battlefield, Qi Yuan came alone to a secluded place.

His consciousness sank into the game jade slip.
