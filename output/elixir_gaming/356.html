This mission issued by the base was quite special.

It was somewhat similar to the story of Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai, but really only the character relationships were alike.

The goal of these mission participants was to protect "Zhu Yingtai's" safety and prevent him from being killed by "Ma Wencai".

At the same time, they needed to find Ma Wencai.

The reason Qi Yuan used Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai as an analogy for this "love triangle" story was because in this story, Ma Wencai was a horse demon.

After entering the game world, the first thing Qi Yuan did was arrest "Zhu Yingtai".

To avoid trouble, Qi Yuan directly knocked the person unconscious, then put them in a sack.

Of course, Qi Yuan was still very kind and considerate of the captive's feelings, and did not force them to do anything unwilling.

So before knocking out "Zhu Yingtai" Wang Yuanyu, he thoughtfully took out several sacks and asked Wang Yuanyu which color she preferred.

It could be said he was very democratic and considerate.

In the end, after Wang Yuanyu chose the black sack, Qi Yuan carried the unconscious Wang Yuanyu and traveled thousands of miles.

He killed and killed, nearly exterminating all the demons and monsters in this world.

Only after the demons and monsters were exterminated did Qi Yuan go to find the final horse demon's lair.

The horse demon's lair was naturally no match for him, and was completely slaughtered by him.

As for "Ma Wencai" Ma Longhun's demon family, they were wiped out entirely by Qi Yuan.

The only regret was that Ma Longhun had disappeared without a trace.

In order to find Ma Longhun, Qi Yuan took action on two fronts.

First, he had someone impersonate him to create a Demon Suppression Court to search for Ma Longhun.

On the other side, Qi Yuan secretly found people to bury Ma Longhun's family in the name of the "Holy Reason Church".

The Holy Reason Church was a small religious organization.

This church advocated for the coexistence of humans and demons, and natural harmony.

Hmm... most of the members of this church were humans.

During the war between demons and humans when humanity was in decline, they advocated for humans to give up resistance in order to coexist peacefully with demons.

After the Holy Reason Church buried Ma Longhun's family, Qi Yuan applied through special channels to become the gravekeeper of this cemetery.

He sat here waiting for Ma Longhun to come pay respects to his demon clan and parents.

Unfortunately, three years passed without any sign of Ma Longhun.

The Demon Suppression Court also failed to find Ma Longhun's whereabouts.

"Wiping out an entire family is really a technical job. I made such a good plan before coming, yet still can't find one person," Qi Yuan lamented.

At this moment, light footsteps could be heard in the distance.

"Sir, it's time to eat."

Wang Yuanyu was wearing a wide-sleeved cyan gauze robe, with a white jade belt around her waist. On the belt was embroidered a special bird.

Looking up, her head was covered with a black sack, only revealing her eyes and mouth. From a distance, one could not make out her facial features.

This black sack was also Qi Yuan being very generous and considerate to the beauty, specially asking what color she liked before covering her.

The reason Qi Yuan did this was to protect Wang Yuanyu's safety, and also for fear of scaring away Ma Longhun.

Wang Yuanyu placed three plates of food in front of Qi Yuan.

A faint fragrance wafted through the air, with various strange foods on the plates.

They were stir-fried cicadas, deep-fried dung beetles, and steamed crawling insects.

Looking at these dishes, Qi Yuan's eyes revealed a helpless expression: "Are you a bird? Making these insect feasts every day?"

Wang Yuanyu said reluctantly: "It's more fitting for your identity."

"Sigh, I'm confining you for your own good," Qi Yuan began to morally blackmail Wang Yuanyu.

As a person from the Blue Star, Qi Yuan was skilled in moral blackmail techniques.

I'm doing this for your own good.

You're so rich, why don't you donate more money.

Wang Yuanyu's eyes were complex: "Why did you knock me out and bring me here?"

She was also very confused.

A few years ago, she went out for a spring outing with her girlfriends, but encountered a strange man.

That strange man was Qi Yuan.

At the time, Qi Yuan was holding over a dozen sacks in his hand, asking her which one she liked.

She felt it was bizarre, but still chose one.

Then she fainted.

When she woke up again, she was in this cemetery.

"I was entrusted by an old friend to come protect you," Qi Yuan said softly. "The outside world is too dangerous. After ensuring your safety, I'll let you go."

Wang Yuanyu glanced at Qi Yuan, her expression changing several times. In the end she didn't say anything and slowly left.

About a quarter of an hour later, Wang Yuanyu arrived at her bamboo house.

This house was located deep in the cemetery.

As Wang Yuanyu entered the bamboo house, suddenly a flash of light appeared and a black shadow emerged.

Seeing this black shadow, Wang Yuanyu's expression remained unchanged.

"Madam Wang, have you investigated clearly?"

This black shadow was actually an old woman, with a somewhat pointed mouth resembling a bird's beak.

"Miss, the identity of this gravekeeper should be... a bloodline descendant from the Death Qi World?"

"Death Qi World?" Wang Yuanyu was surprised, hearing this name for the first time.

"To avoid the Dawn's Call, the Martial Lords of the Origin Realm joined forces to bring fated people from the End Realm into the Death Qi World to carry out missions.

Their mission should be to protect Miss.

That's why this person brought Miss to his side.

Now, there are undercurrents in this Mountain and Sea Scroll, and the Origin Realm is also fraught with danger. Miss might as well stay here. There's no rush to return until the master regains his strength."

Madam Wang explained quickly.

On the surface, Wang Yuanyu was just an ordinary member of the Mountain and Sea Scroll, but in reality, she was a bloodline descendant of a powerful Martial Lord from the Origin Realm.

Madam Wang came from the Origin Realm to protect Wang Yuanyu.

"Mm." Wang Yuanyu nodded.

With Madam Wang present, her memories were also gradually awakening.

"Miss, this servant has found Tu Kongming's whereabouts. He is very worried about Miss's safety. Would you like me to bring him to see Miss?" Madam Wang mentioned.

This Tu Kongming was the Liang Shanbo in Qi Yuan's eyes.

He was Wang Yuanyu's classmate and good friend, and had always been pursuing Wang Yuanyu.

Wang Yuanyu paused when she heard this name.

Seeing the young miss hesitate, Madam Wang continued: "Tu Kongming is like Miss, one of the only two people in this realm with direct bloodline of a Martial Lord, able to enter the Origin Realm.

This person can be befriended, while the others are all false."

In the Mountain and Sea Scroll, everything was false.

Only Wang Yuanyu and Tu Kongming were real, able to enter the Origin Realm.

Wang Yuanyu frowned: "I didn't expect he was also a direct bloodline descendant of a Martial Lord.

By the way, what about Qi Yuan? He's a bloodline descendant, can he enter the Origin Realm?"

Hearing this, Madam Wang's eyes flashed with wariness, then she said: "Among bloodline descendants, there are also some direct bloodlines that can see the Origin Realm.

Most are ordinary people. Qi Yuan doesn't have the mark of a Martial Lord, so he can't enter the Origin Realm.

Even if taken as a servant, it probably wouldn't work."

Hearing this, disappointment flashed in Wang Yuanyu's eyes. She also understood that apart from those with direct bloodlines of Martial Lords like themselves, other people, unless they became Martial Lords themselves, even Martial Kings couldn't enter the Origin Realm.

Wang Yuanyu sat in the pavilion, staring blankly at Qi Yuan outside.

Strange sounds came, but Wang Yuanyu was already used to them.

"You consumed 100 drops of demon blood and gained 100 days of cultivation."

"You consumed 1000 drops of demon blood and gained 1000 days of cultivation, greatly increasing your power."

Thinking of something, Wang Yuanyu approached and asked Qi Yuan: "Mr. Qi, may I ask you a question?"

"I'm in a good mood today, so I'll answer one question from you, NPC."

Indeed, he was in a good mood. According to the date, today was his wedding anniversary with Little Jia and Goldfinch.

"If one day you suddenly discovered that this world was all false, carefully designed and constructed by someone... what would you do?" Wang Yuanyu asked her doubt.

"Ah, you NPC have finally awakened and realized this world... is false. Not bad, not bad, you've made progress!" Qi Yuan looked at Wang Yuanyu with new respect.

He had played so many games, and those NPCs never realized it was just a game.

Hearing this, Wang Yuanyu didn't know whether to laugh or cry.

She was of the Martial Lord's direct bloodline and was real. Those of Qi Yuan's bloodline and others in the Mountain and Sea Scroll were the false ones.

"The world is inherently false. In this world, only I am real, and... whoever I consider real becomes real," Qi Yuan looked at Wang Yuanyu calmly. In her past memories, she and Tu Kongming were a divine couple in their previous life, mutually in love, but... they encountered a horse demon hunting them down. That horse demon forcibly separated and killed them.

How could he possibly have a previous life?

Like Ning Tao, who awakened memories of her previous life and immediately loved like a tidal wave.

However, Qi Yuan felt such feelings were extremely rare and unrealistic.
