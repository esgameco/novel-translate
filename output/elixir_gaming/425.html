Hearing Longyue Tianzun's words, Jinli instinctively glanced at Qi Yuan.

Her gaze became resolute.

"I wish to relinquish my status as a Moon Maiden!"

Becoming a Moon Maiden of the Moon God Palace meant one could not marry, and even after reaching the Yang God realm, one had to sever all worldly ties.

The implications of this were crystal clear.

Hearing this, Longyue Tianzun let out a soft sigh.

The status of Moon Maiden was not something just anyone could obtain.

The treatment they enjoyed was comparable to Yang Gods.

Their families also enjoyed many resources and preferential treatment.

Without the Moon Maiden status and support from the Moon God Palace's resources,

Not only would her family lose those resources, but she herself would find it extremely difficult to reach the Yang God realm.

The Yang God realm... talent alone was far from enough.

In the Six Heavens, there were countless worlds, too many to number.

Geniuses were as numerous as fireflies.

But in reality, a single world might not even produce one Yang God.

For Jinli to give up her Moon Maiden status would be an enormous loss for her.

In fact, such a thing had only happened once or twice in the history of the Moon God Palace.

"I cannot make this decision. We need to consult the Moon God Sovereign," Longyue Tianzun said calmly. "However, the Moon God Sovereign is currently in seclusion and likely cannot see you. This matter can only be put on hold for now.

But since you have fulfilled your meritorious service, I'm sure the esteemed Moon God Sovereign will not make things difficult for you.

Before the Moon God Sovereign emerges from seclusion, during this period of time... just be mindful of your actions."

Currently, the Moon God Sovereign was severely injured and in seclusion, with no knowing when they would reappear.

Longyue Tianzun's meaning was clear - relinquishing the Moon Maiden status was possible, but would have to wait until the Moon God Sovereign emerged from seclusion.

During this time, she should keep a low profile and save some face for the Moon God Palace.

Any ostentatious marriage plans would be unwise.

"Thank you, Tianzun." Jinli gracefully bowed, unable to suppress the joy in her heart.

At this moment, Qi Yuan looked at Longyue Tianzun. Something occurred to him, and he took out a token.

"May I ask, friend, is this Moon Token from the Moon God Palace?" Qi Yuan produced the Moon Token.

Previously, when he climbed to the peak of the Seven-Colored Mountain to seek an audience with his master,

He did not see his master, but instead found this Moon Token.

The information his eyes received was that this was a token of the Moon God Sovereign.

"This token was bestowed by my master Ruan Yixi. It seems to have some connection to the Moon God Palace. I wonder if you know if there is such a person as my master in the Moon God Palace," Qi Yuan asked earnestly.

Jinli frowned, pondering something.

She also hadn't expected Qi Yuan to have such a connection to the Moon God Palace.

Longyue Tianzun looked at the Moon Token, her eyes narrowing slightly, her expression solemn: "This is... the token of the previous generation's Moon God Sovereign."

"Previous generation?" Qi Yuan was also stunned, not expecting such a result.

"Regarding the previous Moon God Sovereign, I don't know much.

As for Ruan Yixi... I've never heard of this person," said Longyue Tianzun.

Information about Great Principle realm powerhouses, especially secrets related to the Moon God Sovereign, was not something a mere Yang God of the small Principle realm like her could know.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan felt somewhat disappointed: "Is it possible for me to meet the senior Moon God Sovereign?"

He wanted to clarify the identity of his master Ruan Yixi, and her current whereabouts.

Longyue Tianzun shook her head: "That's likely impossible in the short term, but... if it's related to the previous Moon God Sovereign, you could try asking... Moonlight Tianzun."

Moonlight Tianzun was one of only two Great Principle realm experts in the Moon God Palace.

With the Moon God Sovereign absent, Moonlight Tianzun had been managing affairs.

"When can I contact Moonlight Tianzun?" Qi Yuan asked curiously.

"In some time, the Principle Dao Discussion will begin. Moonlight Tianzun will represent the Moon God Palace in attendance. At that time, you can naturally pay your respects," Longyue Tianzun said, her eyes showing reverence when mentioning Moonlight Tianzun.

The gap between Yang Gods could be as vast as that between Yang Gods and Yin Gods.

Especially between the Great Principle and Small Principle realms.

The stronger one became, the harder it was to advance even a small level, like climbing to heaven.

"The Principle Dao Discussion, huh?" Qi Yuan murmured.

It seemed he must attend this Principle Dao Discussion.

Leaving the palace with Jinli, Qi Yuan held her hand as they strolled leisurely through the Moon God Palace area.

Jinli was like a delicate beauty from a wealthy family, having lost her former sharp coldness, now gentle and affectionate.

Along the way, many Moon God Palace cultivators who saw this scene were surprised, whispering among themselves.

"Am I seeing things?"

"What did I just see?"

"The Moon Maiden is actually holding a man's arm!"

Everyone in the Moon God Palace knew that Moon Maiden Jinli was an "ice queen", keeping people at a thousand-mile distance, always focused on killing.

They had never seen her with such a girlish demeanor before.

Some cultivators revealed envious and jealous expressions.

"So it turns out the Moon Maiden isn't naturally cold, she was just cold towards us."

"This man's appearance does indeed seem a perfect match for our Moon Maiden, but I wonder what his status is?"

"Is he worthy of our Moon Maiden?"

"To have won the Moon Maiden's heart, he must be exceptionally outstanding!"

These people looked at Jinli and Qi Yuan with curiosity and surprise.

After some time, an elegant and noble-looking woman approached along the path.

This woman was Yu Tiao, the host of the Moon Banquet.

Seeing Moon Maiden Jinli, she blinked her eyes curiously: "Greetings, Moon Maiden."

As she spoke, she couldn't help but look at Qi Yuan.

"And this is...?"

She was a Myth that illuminated all heavens, with a status in this battle zone only slightly lower than the Moon Maiden's.

But in terms of connections, she had far more.

Jinli looked at Qi Yuan, memories of the past flooding back. She grew bolder: "He is... my beloved."

"Beloved" meant dao partner.

At this moment, Jinli no longer wanted to retreat, but to be brave.

Upon hearing this, Yu Tiao and those around them changed expressions slightly.

Because Moon Maidens were not allowed to have beloveds.

"My lady, what you say..." Yu Tiao hesitated, still very shocked.

The ice queen Moon Maiden Jinli actually had someone in her heart.

"My meritorious service is already sufficient. As long as I can meet the Moon God Sovereign, I will no longer be a Moon Maiden of the Moon God Palace," Jinli said firmly.

The gazes of everyone present changed from slight surprise to shock, drawing in sharp breaths.

It was well known that the meaning of being a Moon Maiden was to progress step by step, with the support of the Moon God Sovereign, to certainly become a Yang God! Now, someone was actually giving up the qualifications of a Moon Maiden. How could they not be shocked, and even... sour.

Some looked at the Moon Maiden as if looking at someone blinded by love.

Giving up the status of Moon Maiden for a man, how foolish could one be?

Although this man was quite handsome, could good looks be eaten?

Moon Maiden Jinli understood the meaning behind these expressions, but she paid them no mind.

She had always been clear-headed, knowing what she wanted.

After a moment of shock, Yu Tiao's gaze scrutinized Qi Yuan.

She was very surprised and puzzled about what charm this man possessed to make Jinli give up her Moon Maiden status.

Qi Yuan looked at Jinli, his expression focused: "Whatever you lose, I will make up for."
