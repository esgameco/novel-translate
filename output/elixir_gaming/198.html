Everyone outside the city immediately looked over.

He couldn't figure it out, and his worst fears finally came true.

A giant auspicious beast pulled a divine carriage, following closely behind the fairies.

This procession finally met the funeral procession in the air.

Wang Chunfeng's heart sank, filled with trepidation.

At this moment, the golden-eyed man in the divine carriage had his eyes half-closed, with a very attractive young woman sitting in his arms.

Just then, a soft feminine voice came from outside the carriage: "Great Deity, there is a funeral procession blocking the way ahead."

The golden-eyed man opened his eyes, looking impatient: "How big is their coffin? Put all the others inside it."

The woman outside heard this and paused: "They... don't have a coffin."

She had just noticed that the procession in the distance looked like a funeral, but upon closer inspection, it wasn't quite right.

At that moment, a lazy voice rang out.

"Might that be Lord of Romantic Charms ahead?"

Hearing this, the golden-eyed man instantly sensed something unusual.

However, as a new god in conquered territory, he was very bold: "Speak."

"Our funeral procession is missing a key figure. Would you be willing to condescend to play the role of a corpse?" The voice was light and airy, but carried an undeniable tone.

These words echoed outside the city.

The officials waiting outside were stunned.

This wasn't a funeral procession, but troublemakers! Ji Buran was also puzzled - who would dare... such boldness!

In the current conquered territories, the Ancient Gods were all in fear for their lives. What Ancient God would come to manage Feiwu City! He looked towards the funeral procession, a strange light flashing in his eyes.

"How dare you!" The divine carriage of the Lord of Romantic Charms exploded along with his furious shout. The attractive woman in his arms also exploded into a bloody mess.

He glared angrily at the crowd ahead, killing intent flashing in his eyes: "State your name! I don't kill nameless nobodies!"
