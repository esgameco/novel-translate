As the bell rang, the final battle began.

The disciples of the Divine Light Sect looked at the arena with a hint of worry on their faces.

Qi Yuan also moved at this time.

Since the opponent was wielding a Foundation Establishment Artifact, then... let's not allow the opponent to fully unleash the power of the Foundation Establishment Artifact.

Although, he was not afraid either.

But if he could easily resolve it, why bother?

Stealth Technique!

In an instant, Qi Yuan's figure disappeared from the arena.

Chu Yun Dong was stunned for a moment.

Where's the person?

At this moment, a huge fist landed on the back of his head.

Boom! Chu Yun Dong didn't even have time to react, and was directly knocked down by a punch! Qi Yuan had played games for so long, with rich monster-clearing experience, what about sniping, positioning, and combos? It was easy-peasy for him! Dealing with Chu Yun Dong was a piece of cake.

Qi Yuan's single strike knocked down Chu Yun Dong, and then he cast a Dust Cleansing Technique on his fist, wondering if Chu Yun Dong had any dandruff on his head.

He extended his fist, just like a boxing champion: "I won."

Unfortunately, there were no sexy ring girls here to hold up the cards for him, nor were there any bold ones to even offer a passionate kiss.

Only the old bearded man, the Prince of Chang Shan, stood up and enthusiastically waved at Qi Yuan without any grace.

"Divine Light Sect, Qi Yuan wins!"

As this voice rang out, the others finally noticed what had happened on the arena.

While Chu Tian Xiong had a gloomy expression, looking at the fallen Chu Yun Dong: "Useless!"

Even the Patriarch Bai Chicken was momentarily stunned, but ultimately said nothing.

The other sect disciples were not surprised by this.

After all, Qi Yuan was the top disciple of the Seven Colors Peak.

Even if there were no behind-the-scenes dealings in this Foundation Establishment Conference, he would probably have a chance to win the championship.

Of course, if the Peak Master of the Seven Colors Peak had not made prior arrangements, this Foundation Establishment Conference might not have been so harmonious, and the outcome could have been unknown.

"As expected of the top disciple of the Seven Colors Peak!"

"It's clear that he's not just a cultivator of spiritual power, his mastery of the Dao of Combat is also so profound!"

"Senior brother, how did you win?" asked the Divine Light Sect disciples.

In their eyes, Qi Yuan was a cultivator of the Dao of Obsession, with decent talent, but in terms of the Dao of Combat?

Everyone knew that the eldest disciple of the Seven Colors Peak had never even gone to the Dao of Combat Hall to spar.

He was always obsessed with games, or just strolling around with a kitchen knife.

"By playing a lot of games," Qi Yuan answered honestly.

Hearing this, the disciples all laughed.

Qi Yuan stepped down from the arena, feeling light and carefree.

This Foundation Establishment Conference was held and concluded in a sloppy manner.

As he stepped down, he saw a white-haired sect elder with a kind expression, holding a brown wooden box with patterns of birds, fish, and insects on it.

"Qi Yuan, this is the reward for this Foundation Establishment Conference, a Grade One Foundation Establishment Spiritual Item, the Golden Flower Fruit!"

The sect elder handed the box to Qi Yuan.

Surrounding them, the Divine Light Sect disciples and other disciples all eyed the box with envy.

Even some elders also had a covetous gaze.

Qi Yuan's eyes also sparkled.

Over ten thousand spirit stones!

This was worth over ten thousand spirit stones! The sect elder encouraged, "I hope you can use this Foundation Establishment Spiritual Item to quickly establish your Foundation, and become an even greater combat force for our sect!"

Establish the Foundation?

Qi Yuan had no intention of using the Golden Flower Fruit to establish his Foundation. After all, he had a better choice.

To establish the Foundation, he would choose the best, the Heavenly Dao Foundation Establishment.

The Heavenly Dao Foundation Establishment that Executor Xu Ren Shan had spoken of last time had already entered his mind.

Of course, he didn't say these words.

Under the envious gaze of everyone, Qi Yuan bid farewell to the various sect elders and prepared to return to the Seven Colors Peak.

"You, this kid, with time, you will surely ascend to the peak of the cultivation world!"

"Senior Brother Qi Yuan, thank you." At this moment, Jiang Ran approached, her eyes still slightly red.

The old demon Black Chicken was like a thick, gloomy cloud, weighing heavily on her heart.

And the arrogant, cruel, and tyrannical Chu Tianxiong was like a thorn, hated by all the disciples of the Divine Light Sect, including Jiang Ran.

After all, the crimes Chu Tianxiong had committed in Rongcheng recently had not been punished at all.

And when she learned that the Black Mountain Sect's group would be resting in Rongcheng for a night, Jiang Ran felt even more furious and powerless.
