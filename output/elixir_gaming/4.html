Chapter 4

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Jiang Lingsu stood in place, she couldn't quite believe her ears.

She... the mighty...

Was building her own house? A thatched hut??

Her magical powers, used for this?

She really wanted to say something to Qi Yuan.

Unfortunately, Qi Yuan seemed not to have noticed her expression.

He looked up at the clouds in the sky and said, "You'd better hurry up, it might rain at night."

Jiang Lingsu paused, blinking her eyes: "Senior brother, what if I don't finish in time?"

She really wanted to send her senior brother a voice message: Help me.

"There's a bench in my room," Qi Yuan said, "If you're afraid of getting rained on, you can sleep on that bench."

After saying that, Qi Yuan went into the thatched hut.

Jiang Lingsu clenched her fists, looking at her senior brother's back.

Resigned to her fate.

After all, she wasn't the kind of woman who would force others to coddle her.

Her own matters, she would do them herself.

She followed Qi Yuan into the courtyard, then started carrying bundles of thatch and some wood to build her own... nest?

Troublesome as it was, Jiang Lingsu still remembered the purpose of her coming here.

The first thing to do was to find that eminent person.

Although the peak lord of the Seven-Color Peak, Ruan Yixi, had the highest probability of being that eminent person, there might also be someone else.

Who knows, maybe under the Divine Light Sect, there is an ancient seal, with a supremely powerful being slumbering within.

As for Qi Yuan, she would just treat him as an ordinary senior brother, not getting too close, nor deliberately keeping her distance, just going with the flow.

The clouds gathered and dispersed, Jiang Lingsu was busy building her thatched hut.

The originally empty space now had a thatched hut.

Although she had never built a house before, nor lived in a thatched hut, she had cultivated magical arts after all.

And she had quite a few treasures hidden on her person.

The house wasn't very stable, but just placing a "Paralysis Talisman" would prevent it from collapsing, right?

After finishing the house, it was still early.

Jiang Lingsu clapped her hands, looking at the house, feeling extremely satisfied within.

"Senior brother."

She went outside Qi Yuan's hut and called out.

No matter what, she had joined the Seven-Color Peak, so she needed to go through the motions.

She needed to cultivate the magical arts of the Seven-Color Peak.

"Come in," Qi Yuan's voice came.

Jiang Lingsu hurriedly went in.

As soon as she entered the room, she saw Qi Yuan holding a square jade slip in his hand.

She had seen this type of jade slip before.

"Senior brother, are you playing a game?" Jiang Lingsu asked.

The cultivation world of today was no longer the gloomy one of the past.

Entertainment was also extremely rich.

Alchemy gambling.

Visiting brothels and listening to music.

Sword immortal dance.

Even games were very abundant.

The square jade slip in Qi Yuan's hand, judging from its appearance, was a game jade slip.

These jade slips usually store various types of games.

Such as combat, exploration, farming... all kinds.

However, these game jade slips were not cheap.

Especially the more complex large-scale games, which even had formations engraved on them, and the character backgrounds were lifelike, as if they were real.

It was precisely because of this that jade slip games were particularly popular.

Of course, there were quite a few titles that were too obscene and could only be circulated in secret.

And those who could afford to play jade slip games were not too short on money.

Qi Yuan played the game, not even looking up: "Mm-hmm."

"Senior brother, have you been playing games all afternoon?" Jiang Lingsu asked.

Thinking about how she had been working hard the whole time, while her senior brother was here playing games, she felt a bit imbalanced.

"Senior brother hasn't..."

"Cultivation? If I'm not mistaken, Senior Brother should have already reached the peak of Qi Condensation, just one step away from Foundation Establishment." Jiang Lingsu was also at the peak of Qi Condensation.

"I'm cultivating right now," Qi Yuan said.

Playing games is cultivation?

Although some game jade slips do have benefits for cultivation, those kinds of game jade slips are extremely rare and precious.

"Playing games? Cultivation?" Jiang Lingsu paused, then suddenly realized something. "Senior Brother, you belong to the Infatuated Sect, what is the object of your infatuation?"

The cultivation of the Infatuated Sect is quite different from other sects.

For other sects, when cultivating, one needs to find the cultivation method that suits their spiritual root and cultivate on their own.

The Infatuated Sect, however, needs to find their own object of infatuation.

Infatuated with an object.

It could be jade, flowers, or even swords.

According to the elders of Jiang Lingsu's clan, the cultivation of the Infatuated Sect emphasizes the power of the mind.

Wholeheartedly devoted to the object of infatuation.

When the infatuation reaches its peak, it becomes madness.

Towards the outside world, they seem a bit out of place.

Therefore, cultivators of the Infatuated Sect are extremely difficult to communicate with.

Jiang Lingsu has seen cultivators of the Infatuated Sect before, but never one who is infatuated with games.

Generally speaking, those who are infatuated with games will be sent by their clan to receive treatment, with lightning therapy being the most popular.

After all, the traditional cultivation world does not really recognize game infatuation as part of the Infatuated Sect.

"Is this also considered infatuation?" Qi Yuan said without looking up. "If so, then I'm infatuated with it."

Jiang Lingsu didn't know what to say.

Should she apply for lightning therapy for her Senior Brother? Does game infatuation have any prospects?

"Senior Brother, isn't this a bit unreliable? I've never heard of any great cultivator who is infatuated with games."

The objects of infatuation in the Infatuated Sect can vary in potential.

For example, infatuation with a tree - a thousand-year-old dragon blood tree is better than an ordinary tree, and a Heaven-Blessed Tree is even better than a thousand-year-old dragon blood tree.

Games... what prospects could they have? "In the new era, we must accept new things," Qi Yuan said calmly, looking at Jiang Lingsu.

He had obtained this game jade slip a year before he transmigrated to this world.

In his eyes, this jade slip is like a game console.

But this game console is a bit special, it seems... to be able to connect to a network.

After he transmigrated to this world, this game jade slip has become closely related to him.

Even his eyes' ability to see hidden information might be related to this game jade slip.

In this world, he has also seen many other game jade slips, some similar to his, but completely different in reality.

Jiang Lingsu was speechless.

However, she remembered the purpose of her visit.

She continued, "Senior Brother, can you give me the cultivation method of the Seven-Colored Peak?"

Qi Yuan looked at Jiang Lingsu seriously and said, "The cultivation method of the Seven-Colored Peak cannot be cultivated."

"Why?" Jiang Lingsu asked.

Could it be that her Senior Brother knows some hidden secrets? Is it related to the Peak Master?

"The cultivation method is poisonous."

"Pfft..." If Jiang Lingsu had water in her mouth, she would have surely spat it out.

In her eyes, the current Qi Yuan is just a strange and eccentric madman.

However, considering the information she had investigated before coming here, this Senior Brother of the Seven-Colored Peak was indeed a bit strange.

So it's not that surprising.

"Senior Brother, I don't mind, please give me the cultivation method." Jiang Lingsu said.

Qi Yuan slowly took out a jade slip and handed it to Jiang Lingsu: "This is the cultivation method of the Seven-Colored Peak.

However, it's only half complete.

The first part is the original cultivation method, and the second part is what I've modified, but not fully completed."

He handed the cultivation method jade slip to Jiang Lingsu.
