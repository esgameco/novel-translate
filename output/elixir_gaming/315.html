Qi Yuan fell into brief contemplation beside the martial cloud carving.

He carefully recorded all the traces above.

"Are you saying Luo Bufan really broke through the limits of martial arts and shattered the void?" Han Xianyun asked blankly, looking at the traces left by Luo Bufan.

Luo Bufan was recorded in history as the most recent person to break through the limits of martial arts.

As a child, Han Xianyun admired Luo Bufan the most and saw him as an idol.

It could be said that most martial artists in martial arts circles greatly admired Luo Bufan.

"Perhaps he shattered the void, or perhaps... he died," Qi Yuan answered casually.

He recalled seeing Yu Xiu in that torture space last time.

Yu Xiu... was already a dead person.

So in this world... there was an underworld.

Did Yu Xiu come from the underworld? Qi Yuan thought of many possibilities.

Regarding breaking through the limits of martial arts, Qi Yuan already had some ideas.

"It seems I should also prepare to break through the limits of martial arts," Qi Yuan said seriously.

"?? Wait, Mr. Qi, you want to break through the limits of martial arts?" Han Xianyun was a bit confused.

He had seen Qi Yuan in action before.

This kind of cultivator was like a deity.

But now he was going to break through martial arts.

Was this... a hobby?

He suddenly thought of Zhang Yang from South Lion. Compared to Qi Yuan, Zhang Yang's couldn't even be called a hobby.

"Could it be that from looking at this martial cloud carving, you've already gained some insights... Did you figure out something from the traces left by Luo Bufan? Are you going to emulate Luo Bufan and break through the limits of martial arts?"

Qi Yuan shook his head: "I won't emulate Luo Bufan. First, I have no relatives. Second, his path is not suitable for me.

The little treasures in the sea are more suitable for me."

Han Xianyun was shocked inwardly.

A cultivator was indeed a cultivator, already discovering how to break through the limits of martial arts.

"By the way, since your family is so rich, can you lend me a yacht? I'm planning to go out to sea to find an opportunity to break through the limits of martial arts," Qi Yuan reached out to borrow something.

He had thick skin and would borrow whatever he lacked without any psychological burden.

"Ahem... I don't have a yacht," Han Xianyun replied awkwardly.

Although he was a second generation rich kid, he wasn't the kind that lived very well.

His father died early, and power had long been in his older brother's hands.

Compared to ordinary people, his life was heavenly, but compared to other second generation rich kids, it was quite tight.

"But Princess Huansha definitely has one!" Han Xianyun quickly said, "You can borrow from her!"

"The paramecium princess has one?" Qi Yuan's eyes lit up. He pondered, "I've protected her so well, borrowing a yacht from her... she shouldn't refuse, right?"

Qi Yuan felt this was a done deal.

After all, his protection of Princess Huansha was so thoughtful and thorough, he had almost paused her cell division.

This greatly extended her lifespan by over ten minutes.

Han Xianyun beside him was speechless hearing this.

This was called protecting well?

Of course, he didn't dare to refute Qi Yuan.

"Let's go find the princess and borrow a yacht," Qi Yuan waved his hand and headed towards where they came from.

At this moment, Han Xianyun was a bit anxious.

He was afraid the princess had turned into a paramecium and died.

If he remembered correctly, paramecia don't have very long lifespans.

One day later.

A luxury yacht sailed slowly.

A man stood on the yacht, his expression calm.

Princess Huansha stood behind the man, her eyes filled with awe.

Yesterday's ten-plus minutes were definitely the most wondrous experience of her life.

Facing this amazing man before her, she had none of a princess's arrogance, but was instead like a servant.

"Hmm, let's head southwest for a bit," Qi Yuan said at this time.

Princess Huansha quickly ordered the AI robot to steer the yacht according to Qi Yuan's instructions.

Immediately, the yacht sped off towards the southwest.

Qi Yuan stood on the yacht, his eyes constantly scanning the sea surface.

He was searching for the mutant sea monster he needed.

However, in the past half day, he had found almost nothing.

Mutant sea monsters were not so easy to encounter.

Moreover, it had to be a mutant sea monster of the same bloodline as Qi Yuan, which was even rarer.

At this moment, Qi Yuan had transformed into a radar, constantly patrolling.

Princess Huansha looked on curiously, with many questions in her heart, but obediently didn't ask.

Suddenly, Qi Yuan spoke: "Stop."

Princess Huansha, like a driver, had the AI robot stop the yacht.

She looked at Qi Yuan curiously.

At this moment, a long sword appeared in Qi Yuan's hand.

"Go!"

With Qi Yuan's voice, that blood-colored long sword immediately shot into the sea.

Princess Huansha saw this scene and widened her eyes.

She finally saw Qi Yuan take action again... and this blood-colored long sword... wasn't it? Her gaze towards Qi Yuan instantly changed again, as if looking at a deity.

After the sword left its sheath, Qi Yuan sat cross-legged on the yacht.

"It's actually over ten thousand meters deep, looks like we'll have to wait a while."

He discovered that the mutant sea monster was actually over ten thousand meters deep on the seafloor.

This depth was extremely terrifying.

On Earth, the enormous pressure would be enough to tear apart any human submarine.

Even though Arch Star's technology was a notch above Earth's, diving over ten thousand meters still posed huge difficulties.

To give an example, Princess Huansha's country of Liulis, even mobilizing the entire nation's resources, could not build a submarine capable of diving over ten thousand meters.

At this moment, Qi Yuan's target was below ten thousand meters on the seafloor.

Now, over ten thousand meters deep on the seafloor, broken pieces of wood were eerily still stained with fresh blood.

Various strange rocks were covered in all kinds of blood stains, as if even the passage of time could not wash away the blood.

The corpse of a bloated mutant sea monster lay quietly in the deep sea, encased in a transparent crystal.

It was as if it had been sealed inside.

At this time, the blood-red long sword flickered, and the transparent crystal containing the mutant sea monster's corpse rose up from the deep seafloor.

On the yacht, Qi Yuan's expression was surprised: "It seems my luck stat is very high in this game, encountering such an extraordinary sea monster right away."

"Game?" Princess Huansha beside him showed a curious expression, "What game?"

"I mean, your world is a game I like, and I'm a game player."

Princess Huansha was stunned briefly, then smiled and said, "Mr. Qi sure knows how to joke."

Even if Mr. Qi was a cultivator, it didn't mean everything he said was true.

"I never joke," Qi Yuan smiled, not continuing on this topic.

The people of this world were nothing but NPCs after all.

Soon, the sea surface in front churned.

A flash of blood-red light, and the blood-colored long sword brought a transparent crystal onto the yacht.

The transparent crystal was about one meter tall, one meter long, and one meter wide, a perfect cube.

Inside the transparent crystal was the corpse of a strange fish with three legs, two fins, and three eyes.

The strange fish's eyes were still open, and human emotions could be seen in them - anger and unwillingness.

[King of Ink Sha Fish, a fish of the Martial Hero level, possessing a terrifying bloodline. Once swallowed two thousand people from a small town whole. Sealed here.]

Qi Yuan's eyes also instantly saw various information.

His throat bobbed, feeling a thirst, very much wanting to break open the transparent crystal
