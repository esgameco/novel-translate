Chen Huan was extremely anxious.

The auras of several supreme beings were drawing closer, including one that belonged to a great supreme being.

"It's over, we can't escape!"

Chen Huan looked at Qi Yuan, and for some reason, he sensed that Qi Yuan was momentarily stunned.

"The ones who can't escape are them. I... have become a supreme being," Qi Yuan's eyes cleared, as if he had experienced countless years in an instant.

His enlightenment ended, and he returned to the present moment in the game world.

"Big brother... you've become a supreme being?" Chen Huan was shocked, then overjoyed.

He looked at Qi Yuan, feeling that he had become even more mysterious and unfathomable.

Wasn't he just a terrestrial legend a moment ago? How did he suddenly become a supreme being?

Had he comprehended the origin? Awakened origin divine abilities?

"Now, it's time to complete the third step of my plan - kill the incoming enemies!" Qi Yuan was about to implement his meticulous plan.

"You just broke through to become a supreme being, it won't work. They have a great supreme being whose origin divine abilities have reached the engraving realm!" Mentioning engraving, Chen Huan's eyes showed a trace of fear. "Origin divine abilities at the engraving level cannot be defeated even if flaws are found!"

Qi Yuan also knew what Chen Huan was talking about.

While fleeing with Ning Tao, and mastering the human demon path, he had gained more understanding of supreme beings.

Upon first entering the supreme being realm, the origin divine abilities mastered were at the time-exhausting level.

At the time-exhausting level, mastery of origin divine abilities was not very stable, and... consumption was very high, with a time limit.

In the middle stage of supreme being, origin divine abilities reached the subtle level.

With subtle control over origin divine abilities, one could use them very well without wasting the slightest bit.

As for the late stage supreme beings, the great supreme beings, they reached the legendary engraving level.

The origin divine abilities were engraved on their bodies and souls.

Without even moving, origin divine abilities would form on their own.

The gap between a great supreme being and an early stage supreme being was too large.

They could exhaust an early stage supreme being's origin divine abilities through attrition alone.

"Engraving... is indeed very powerful." Qi Yuan grinned, "But... I haven't killed anyone for tens of thousands of years, I've been itching to do so for a long time. The killing intent I've accumulated will give them a fright!"

In the Gate of Ten Thousand Differences, Qi Yuan had sat in meditation for who knows how many years.

He had forgotten time, and forgotten himself.

He really hadn't killed anyone for many years.

"Big brother, did you forget that we just killed a supreme being, and quite a few terrestrial legends too!" Chen Huan increasingly felt that his big brother was really unreliable.

Sometimes he seemed profound and unfathomable, other times he was very scatterbrained.

The scatterbrained appearance was very similar to himself, but at this moment, Chen Huan didn't want Qi Yuan to be scatterbrained.

Although their dialogue seemed to contain a lot of content, it was completed in less than two breaths.

The terrifying auras also fully descended at this moment.

Six supreme beings could be seen standing in mid-air, surrounding Qi Yuan and Chen Huan from all directions.

Purple Daoist's eyes were deep as he looked at the two, with surprise in his gaze: "How did you two manage to kill Supreme Being Yu Bai?"

As Purple Daoist spoke, his gaze fell completely on Qi Yuan.

As for Chen Huan, he was completely ignored.

The others also looked at Chen Huan and Qi Yuan with surprised expressions.

After all, Supreme Being Yu Bai's strength was well-known. Even if two supreme beings attacked together, it wouldn't be so quick to kill him.

Yet these two in front of them had done it. Did they have some secret?

The six people had varied expressions, each with their own thoughts.

Qi Yuan's gaze also fell on Purple Daoist, his eyes sharp as knives. He said slowly: "So it was you who sent Wu Tong to ambush me? Who gave you the confidence and courage... to ambush me?"

Before a fight, one's momentum couldn't be weak!

Even village dogs bark non-stop before fighting, right?

This Purple Daoist had ambushed him for no reason, there must be some inside story.

Hearing Qi Yuan's words, the others also looked at Purple Daoist.

Purple Daoist had actually ambushed the guardian of Wugui City before?

His coming to the Thousand Cities region this time... could there really be some inside story?

Purple Daoist didn't explain, he waved his sleeve: "Capture the guardian of Wugui City alive, as for the other one... kill him."

Hearing this, Chen Huan became furious: "I am a disciple of the White Emperor, wouldn't it be better to capture me alive like my big brother?"

The other five supreme beings quickly moved to attack Chen Huan.

Five terrifying origin divine abilities fell directly towards Chen Huan.

If these five divine abilities hit, even if Chen Huan didn't die, he would be severely injured.

"Big brother save me!" Chen Huan turned into a streak of light to escape, with the five divine abilities closely pursuing.

At this moment, Qi Yuan's voice entered Chen Huan's ears.

Now that Qi Yuan was stronger, it was also easier for him to spot the flaws in those five origin divine abilities.

Hearing this, Chen Huan's face lit up with joy: "Big brother, you're truly my father!"

As if divinely aided, Chen Huan directly escaped from the five divine abilities. However, he was still grazed by one, half his arm directly sliced off, causing him to grimace in pain.

This scene shocked all six people present.

They hadn't expected Chen Huan to be able to block their origin divine abilities.

Their gazes fell on Qi Yuan, filled with surprise, astonishment, fear, and delight.

Qi Yuan's voice also rang out at this moment.

"Now... it's my turn!"

Although this world was a game, it was very realistic, not turn-based combat.

Qi Yuan had been holding back all along, wanting to see through their origin divine abilities.

For a cultivator, their origin divine abilities were their great dao, directly reaching their true heart.

Attacking from the true heart was also the easiest way to induce heart demons.

With Qi Yuan's voice, for some reason, the five supreme beings present all felt a strong sense of fear arise in their hearts.

Purple Daoist stood in mid-air, narrowing his eyes, his expression fearless.

He had a premonition that when the guardian of Wugui City made his move, he could kill all the supreme beings present except for himself.

And this... happened to suit his intentions.

The secrets and treasures of Wugui City should all belong to him.

Qi Yuan took a step forward, his robes rising without wind.

At this moment, his robes also turned blood red.

He stood in place, his gaze deep and hollow, as if coming from the abyss.

Virtual Realm Overlord's Heart Demon Inducement!

At this moment, Qi Yuan immediately unleashed his origin divine ability.

He looked towards a female supreme being.

Lust - Red Lotus Karmic Fire!

Instantly, flames of fiery red ignited on that female supreme being's body, her clothes incinerated in an instant.

"No!" The female supreme being's left eye was filled with fear, while her right eye was filled with desire.

An aura belonging to demonic evil was spreading over her body.

Her body seemed about to burst.

Qi Yuan's gaze fell on a man wearing white robes.

Pride - Eye of Azure Heaven.

Qi Yuan spoke softly: "I hate show-offs the most. If anyone's going to show off, it should be me!"

With his words, a demonic evil inexplicably crawled out on the white-robed man's body.

The demonic evil was extremely hideous, with eyes that looked down on everything, crouching on the white-robed man's body, eerily embracing his neck.

Like being pricked by needles, like dry tree bark scraping across the back, like centipedes crawling on the neck, goosebumps rose, and the white-robed man felt fear arise.

"What is this thing!"

An origin divine ability struck his own neck, blood spurting from the corner of his mouth.

As the origin divine ability manifested, an eerie aura spread.

The five supreme beings present had expressions of fear, greed, or lust.

Their minds had all come under attack.

The scene suddenly became bizarre.

Watching this scene from not far away, Chen Huan was both shocked and fearful. He secretly glanced at Qi Yuan.

In his heart he wanted to say:

What the hell are you?

But he didn't dare to ask or speak.

The scene before him was too horrifying.

This guardian of Wugui City, his big brother, was acting just like a big villain.

Divine abilities manifested, and demonic evil was born.

Where in the world were there origin divine abilities like this? What cultivator in the world didn't fear demonic evil like scorpions?

But his big brother...

Chen Huan thought of something, a look of horror flashing across his face.

Qi Yuan looked at those five supreme beings, an extremely eerie smile on his face: "You've lived for so long, wasted so much food, it's time for you... to die."

With Qi Yuan's words, the auras on those five supreme beings instantly vanished.

Their heart demons broke out of their bodies, and they directly perished.

And since they didn't die of natural causes... no demonic evil formed either.

Five extremely powerful supreme beings died in frustration.

A trace of delight flashed in Purple Daoist's eyes: "Your origin divine ability is very interesting. One thought manifests and heart demons are born. Your origin divine ability... can be said to be unrivaled among those of the same level."

The origin divine ability of heart demon inducement was formed from the source of evil.

This kind of divine ability could easily induce people's heart demons.

The cultivators of the Liufeng Realm originally kept their heart demons confined within. The "Demon Confinement Technique" allowed them not to worry about heart demons suddenly breaking out of their bodies, but the heart demons always existed.
