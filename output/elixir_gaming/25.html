Here is the full English translation of the excerpt from the Chinese novel:

Jiang Lingsu wore a long blue dress, her scarf fluttering like a green snake woman.

Her lively eyes still carried a hint of weariness. She brushed the hair from her temples and took a deep breath: "It seems I lost a piece of Junyuan jade yesterday.

Alas, the Shenguang Sect can't buy it.

I'll just have to make do with something else."

Junyuan jade was a spiritual jade that could emit a mysterious fragrance and clear the mind when ignited.

Yesterday, the puppet had cleaned the room and thrown the remaining piece into the trash.

Normally, she wouldn't have minded.

But this was the Shenguang Sect, where replacements couldn't be found.

She decided to go to the trash heap and retrieve that piece of Junyuan jade.

She came to the trash heap, covering her mouth and nose with her sleeve, ready to summon the puppet to find the piece of Junyuan jade.

But... the trash heap.

"Who's been through it?"

She stared at the trash heap, stunned.

The trash heap usually contained her daily garbage and the remnants of some spiritual items.

Like the Junyuan jade.

She never expected the outdoor trash heap to have been rummaged through.

She thought of something, and a talisman appeared in her hand.

This talisman, called the Perception Talisman, was one of the many talismans Jiang Lingsu possessed, created by a Deity Infant Realm cultivator.

She usually kept it by her bedside to serve as an early warning.

Since the trash heap had been disturbed, she immediately took out the Perception Talisman and looked for clues on it.

In the center of the talisman, there was a faint pattern.

"Around the hour of the Ox last night? Was there a living being lingering outside my room for about an incense stick's time?"

Through the residual information on the Perception Talisman, Jiang Lingsu knew that there had been a living being lingering outside her room for a significant amount of time the previous night.

However, there was no malicious intent, as the Perception Talisman had not sounded an alarm, otherwise she wouldn't have been able to sleep.

Jiang Lingsu frowned as she looked at the trash heap: "On the Seven-Colored Peak, there are only the Sect Master, myself, and the Senior Disciple Brother.

Other ordinary animals wouldn't trigger the Perception Talisman's response.

At least it had to be a being with spiritual power."

"Could it be..."

Suddenly, Jiang Lingsu looked towards the neighboring thatched hut.

She recalled the appearance of her Senior Disciple Brother when he had eaten spiritual food, and a strange emotion arose within her.

Sigh...

She thought about it for a while, then returned to her own room.

Half an hour later, Jiang Lingsu came to the courtyard outside Qi Yuan's place.

"Senior Disciple Brother, are you awake?" Jiang Lingsu called softly.

"What is it, Junior Sister?" Qi Yuan had changed into a white robe, looking quite handsome and elegant. Today was the Foundation Establishment Assembly, so he had gotten up earlier than usual.

Seeing Qi Yuan's appearance, Jiang Lingsu felt her heart at ease.

Indeed, the sight of beauty always lifts one's mood.

"Senior Disciple Brother, may I trouble you with something?" Jiang Lingsu said.

"Oh, what is it?" Qi Yuan was very enthusiastic. The cultivation manual jade slips and spiritual food his Junior Sister had given him previously had made him quite grateful.

"Senior Disciple Brother, do you know if there are any special or mysterious people in our sect?"

Jiang Lingsu had initially focused on Sect Master Alun Yishe on the Seven-Colored Peak as the one who could withstand calamities.

However, Jiang Lingsu felt she could identify some other potential candidates as well.

"Special people?" Qi Yuan pondered, then his eyes lit up. "I know one person who is quite special."

"Who is it?" Jiang Lingsu's heart raced.

"Me!" Qi Yuan pointed to himself.

Jiang Lingsu was stunned upon hearing this.

Her Senior Disciple Brother was indeed quite special, after all... cultivators of the Foolish Path often had somewhat abnormal minds.

Jiang Lingsu felt a bit deflated, feeling that seeking help from her Senior Disciple Brother was not very reliable. But recalling the morning's events,

she took out a storage bag: "Senior Disciple Brother, here are a thousand spirit stones as your reward. Please help me look into some special people, there will be more rewards to come."

Jiang Lingsu left Qi Yuan's courtyard after saying this.

Qi Yuan held the storage bag and looked at the thousand spirit stones inside.

It must be said that this was the first time he had seen such a large sum of money.

However, his heart was not very disturbed.

Instead, he murmured, "Why did my sister disciple send me spirit stones?"

It seems that the sister disciple really wants him to help.

But these spirit stones, they look like a gift to him.

"Could it be that the sister disciple has seen through my inner stubbornness and wants to tear off my disguise? To keep me?"

Qi Yuan had a sudden inspiration.

But in the end, he did not dwell on it, but turned his gaze to the kitchen knife tied in the courtyard.

"Sister disciple, you have seen through my stubbornness, have you also seen it, knife?"

The kitchen knife lay on the ground, motionless.

"What should I name you?"

The kitchen knife still did not move.

"Knife?"

"Vegetable knife?"

"Cai Cai?"

"Knife Knife?"

"Demon Sword?"

"Sky Sword?"

The kitchen knife finally had some reaction, looking like it was shivering.

Qi Yuan quickly grabbed the kitchen knife, with a puzzled look in his eyes: "Is this having spiritual intelligence? How come it's not as smart as my little classmate Ai."

Little classmate Ai, even if you let her fart, she at least knows to call her classmate Xiao Du to fart.

This kitchen knife, it looks like it has spiritual intelligence, but it's no different from not having it.

But thinking about it, it's normal.

However, Qi Yuan holding the knife, he felt that his combat power had increased a layer.

"Eight slashes in the summer, can it kill a few Foundation Establishment cultivators?"

Unfortunately, this is the Seven-Color Peak, not suitable for practicing the sword.

And he still needs to represent the Shenguang Sect to attend the Foundation Establishment Conference, he has no time to practice the sword.

Inside the Shenguang Sect.

The Foundation Establishment Conference finally began.

And this Foundation Establishment Conference, because of the Grade One Foundation Establishment treasure, the Yellow Flower Fruit, the sects from the Great Merchant Kingdom that came were not weak.

"Elder Feihua from the Moyu Gate has arrived!"

"Sect Master Fenguang from the Suspended Mountain has arrived!"

"Prince of Changshan has arrived!"

"Patriarch Bai from the Heishan Sect has arrived!"

With the announcement of the child, outside the gate of the Shenguang Sect, multicolored clouds gathered.

One by one, spiritual boats descended from the sky and landed in front of the gate of the Shenguang Sect.

Qi Yuan, in a white robe, the wind blowing through his clothes, making a rustling sound, he looked at the guests from the various sects arriving, with a scholarly smile in his eyes: "Welcome, everyone, to my Shenguang Sect to attend the Foundation Establishment Conference!"

At this moment, he was quite reliable.
