The agent's voice droned on and on, endlessly.

Wu Qi listened with a headache.

The Hun Sha Sect was a small sect, with only one Yuan Dan elder in the inner sect.

It recruited disciples in various places in Qin Yuan, and had gained some reputation.

Many with talent who couldn't join major sects like the Tian Xuan Sect would choose to join medium and small sects like the Hun Sha Sect.

But even these medium and small sects weren't open to just anyone who wanted to join.

Wu Qi's talent wasn't high, but she had some, just barely making the cut.

Plus, since the agent's cousin had joined the Hun Sha Sect as a formal disciple, the agent wanted Wu Qi to join and become her cousin's follower.

"Sister Liu, my situation is complicated. Let's not push this matter," Wu Qi said firmly.

"Little Qi, I know you're still thinking about that Mr. Qi.

You want to climb into his bed and become a noble lady.

But times have changed. You should be an independent and strong woman. Join the Hun Sha Sect and you'll be nobility! That Qi Yuan, at most he's an earl. In the eyes of cultivators, how is he any different from a slightly larger ant?" the agent persuaded earnestly.

"Back then, you were a small celebrity, I could understand your desire for his power.

But now... times have changed! He's become just one of the masses. Don't waste your youth on him anymore!"

Wu Qi was speechless. She couldn't reveal or expose Qi Yuan's true identity.

At this moment, she thought of a famous saying and said, "In the past, I sat in a well looking at the sky. Seeing Mr. Qi was like a frog in a well seeing the sun and moon. Now that the immortal way has revived, seeing Qi Yuan is like a mayfly seeing the blue sky."

"You... what kind of love potion did he give you?" The agent's voice was disappointed.

Just then, the doorbell rang.

"I have a visitor, I have to go!" Wu Qi quickly hung up the phone, also wondering who would come to her place in the middle of the night.

Could it be one of her close girlfriends? Thinking this, she walked to the door and looked out through the peephole-like device.

When she saw that face, an expression of intense joy flashed across Wu Qi's face.

"Mr. Qi!"

Standing outside was none other than Mr. Qi.

He had actually come back.

And... what did he want with her? Without any hesitation, Wu Qi opened the door, her face beaming with joy: "Mr. Qi, why are you here?"

When the cool breeze from outside blew in, she felt a chill on her bare shoulders and only then realized she was wearing just a bathrobe. Her face immediately turned red.

"My ears are quite good. Hmm, just now the wind carried some sounds, saying you wanted to get into my bed..."

"Ah? She was talking nonsense, it's not true!" Wu Qi hurriedly explained.

At this moment, she was extremely nervous, her heart pounding, feeling as if the world was a bit hazy.

"Hmm, good that it's a misunderstanding. I'm a married man." Qi Yuan sat down in the room and finally stated his purpose, "I came here for a drop of your blood."

"One blood?" Wu Qi exclaimed in surprise, not hearing Qi Yuan's words clearly.

If this had happened when they first met on the cruise ship, Wu Qi might have hesitated a bit before willingly giving herself to Qi Yuan in order to get close to him.

But now, she didn't dare.

"Clean your ears and listen more carefully, it's one drop of blood.

Remember when I first rode in your car? We agreed then that you'd give me your blood."

Qi Yuan needed a drop of Wu Qi's blood as a medium to store his divine infant.

Wu Qi finally heard clearly, the misunderstanding cleared up, feeling even more embarrassed as she curled her toes.

"Mr. Qi, please take it." Wu Qi extended her finger.

In fact, she was also curious what Qi Yuan would do with this drop of her blood.

But since she had already bet on Qi Yuan, she would do as he asked no matter what.

As her slender white finger extended, a flash of red light passed through Qi Yuan's eyes.

A fine line appeared on Wu Qi's finger, and a drop of blood rolled down, falling into Qi Yuan's palm.

Qi Yuan's palm seemed to have a hole in it.

That drop of blood directly seeped in.

At this point, Qi Yuan closed his eyes.

He was sensing which divine infant had an affinity with Wu Qi.

About ten breaths later, Qi Yuan opened his eyes.

Wu Qi stood quietly to the side, puzzled.

"Last time, didn't I say I'd give you a grandmother?

Well, this time I'll give you... five grandmothers directly."

"Huh?" Wu Qi blinked, a bit dazed.

"They are the Four Heavenly Kings - Dhṛtarāṣṭra, Virūḍhaka, Virūpākṣa, and Vaiśravaṇa.

And another one is Yuè Yào. I didn't expect this pair of underdeveloped twins to be separated."

Qi Yuan muttered.

The Four Heavenly Kings were his earliest fighting force in Qingshui Village, with Dhṛtarāṣṭra being Wang Wenqi, the granddaughter of Venerable Tianbao.

Yuè Yào was one of the twin goddess sisters Qi Yuan encountered in the ancestral grounds of the Ancient Clan.

The elder sister had a cold personality, while the younger was more carefree.

This time, the younger sister didn't come with her elder sister, which was quite unexpected for Qi Yuan.

However, he was also somewhat surprised.

This Wu Qi looked to be only about 1.7 meters tall, but her blood was quite strong to be able to accommodate five grandmothers at once.

"They are..." Wu Qi was puzzled and confused.

"They are my old acquaintances, and can be considered my subjects..." Qi Yuan explained.

Wu Qi's heart tightened.

They were actually Mr. Qi's old acquaintances? She was a bit bewildered. Could it be something like remnant souls from the cultivation world?

"They are also my divine infants. They died in battle before, and now lie dormant. They will reside in your body.

They... can also guide you in cultivation and help you become stronger," Qi Yuan said softly.

There was no need to hide this.

"Ah..." Wu Qi's heart suddenly skipped a beat.

Because she heard the words "divine infant".

Could it be that Mr. Qi's old acquaintances were all divine infants!

Those were divine infant cultivators! Wu Qi was no longer ignorant now.

With the descent of the three realms to Gong Xing and the revival of the immortal way, she had some understanding of cultivation realms.

Divine infant cultivators were called deities.

Like that demonic cultivator who held the Banner of Ten Thousand Souls, easily slaughtering a city alone.

If not for the activation of the Eye in the Sky, who could have stopped him?

Such terrifying powerhouses, as majestic as gods, were the patriarchs of sects.

Even the heads of the Seven Martial Star families had to treat divine infant elders with courtesy.

If Mr. Qi's acquaintances were divine infants, then he... wouldn't he be a divine infant as well, or even higher?

"Don't worry, apart from guiding you, they won't harm you. As for the price you pay, it's just that drop of blood."

"Mr. Qi? A cultivator?"

Seeing Qi Yuan, he was like seeing a deity.

He admired Qi Yuan even more in his heart.

Someone like Ning Tao, a terrifying powerhouse who could easily destroy the Eye in the Sky, was willing to serve at Qi Yuan's side.
