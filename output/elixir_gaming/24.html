Here is my translation of the excerpt from the Chinese novel:

"In another ten days or so, I should be able to reach level 90.

I wonder what my awakened special skill will be."

Qi Yuan was very excited.

"If I reach level 90, I'll be able to quickly clear the Forbidden Attire dungeon.

If I can fully explore the map of this world, will I awaken a new special ability?"

Qi Yuan's thoughts wandered.

After entering the game jade slip, it wasn't long before his eyes underwent a mutation, allowing him to see hidden information.

If he could fully conquer the game, would he undergo other mutations as well?

"Will I get a supernatural sense of hearing? Will I be able to hear indescribable whispers?"

"Or maybe I'll grow a dog's nose?"

Qi Yuan thought about this as he lay in the pit, still unable to see the moon.

"Qi Yuan, thank you! Today Aunt Qin defeated the National Teacher of Nanfeng Country!

The gloomy expression on Sima Ting's face when Aunt Qin displayed her imperial-level strength was so interesting!"

A message came from Jinli. She seemed really happy, even using an interjection.

"Great, you've completed a lot of your tasks too. Once you finish up, we can finally meet in person.

By the way, should we decide on a meeting place? Where do you want to meet?" Qi Yuan said whatever came to mind.

It took a long time for a reply. Finally, one came.

"How about under the ancient Qi Chun tree? What do you think?" Jinli asked.

The ancient Qi Chun tree was the most legendary tree in the Wangyue Continent.

The towering ancient tree, whose height was unknown, was said that if one stood atop it, they could grasp the stars.

It was rumored that this tree was planted by a divine deity who had vanquished a celestial demon.

The deity told his wife that when the Qi Chun tree grew to 30 feet tall, he would return.

His wife waited under the tree for him, year after year, but he never came back.

The tree continued growing, reaching 300 feet, then 30,000 feet, even higher.

But the deity's wife never saw her husband return.

The Qi Chun tree had become the most legendary tree in the Wangyue Continent.

"The Qi Chun tree?" Qi Yuan paused, he knew of that tree, as there was one on the peak of the Seven Color Ridge.

But to find such a tree would be no easy task.

"Where is the Qi Chun tree?"

Jinli hesitated, then stepped outside and gazed into the distance.

The towering silhouette of a massive tree came into view.

Anywhere in the Wangyue Continent, if one looked far into the distance, they could see that ancient Qi Chun tree.

The ancient tree reached up to the heavens, its leaves dotted with stars.

"Look to the southwest, there is a huge tree - that's the Qi Chun tree."

Qi Yuan paused.

He climbed out of the pit and looked southwest.

There, in the southwest, stood a colossal ancient tree, its top reaching up to the stars, seemingly within reach.

"So that's the Qi Chun tree you mentioned? It's massive, if it grows any taller it'll touch the stars in the sky."

"When we meet, I'll do some barbecuing, you bring some good wine, and we can camp under the Qi Chun tree - it'll be quite an experience." Qi Yuan climbed back into the pit.

"Okay!" Jinli's expression was indecipherable.

Today, Aunt Qin had achieved a great victory. She wanted to seize the momentum and eliminate Sima Ting's forces.

But she also understood that she had not yet won.

Dangers lurked in all directions.

...

The Divine Light Sect.

Qi Yuan strolled with a knife in hand, or more accurately...

The Vegetable Knife.

At this time, a disciple from Mount Wanfa greeted Qi Yuan: "Senior brother, the great assembly for foundation building is tomorrow. Are you still walking your pet?"

Qi Yuan did not stop his steps: "My pet is about to awaken its spiritual intelligence, so I cannot be careless about it. I must take it out for a walk."

Hearing Qi Yuan's response, the person smiled and said, "Senior brother is truly a remarkable person."

Qi Yuan led his knife, walking it very diligently.

Behind him, several disciples from the Divine Light Sect were discussing something in low voices.

"This one from the Seven-Color Peak is really calm. Tomorrow is the great foundation building assembly, yet he still has the leisure to walk his pet, and it's just a vegetable knife!"

"The emperor is not anxious, but the eunuch is. If you have no time to spare, how come you are not representing the Divine Light Sect to compete?"

"Cough, cough, it's not that my cultivation is insufficient."

"The rumor is that the reward for winning the foundation building assembly this time is a Grade One Foundation Building Spiritual Treasure, the Golden Flower Fruit!"

"That is a Grade One Foundation Building Spiritual Treasure. Whoever wins it will surely be able to form their Embryonic Breath, and in the future, they will be a big shot in the Great Xia Kingdom, and perhaps even a hegemon in other countries!"

"A Grade One Foundation Building Spiritual Treasure is already so powerful. The ones above it, the Vein Foundation and the Heavenly Dao Foundation, must be even more extraordinary!"

"Don't even think about it. We have no chance of encountering such a person."

The crowd sighed, feeling that the universe was too vast and their own selves too insignificant.

At this moment, a male disciple gritted his teeth and said, "I heard that Chu Tianxiong from the Black Mountain Sect is also coming to our place!"

"He dares to come!"

"Damn it, a while ago, the elders of the Black Mountain Sect injured Zheng Jianghe, and they still dare to come to our place!"

"Our female disciples must be careful, or else... or else..." Some female disciples turned pale, obviously very afraid of Chu Tianxiong.

"Hmph, this is the Divine Light Sect, one of the three major sects of the Great Xia. How dare the Black Mountain Sect be so arrogant!" A disciple declared loudly.

Towards the Black Mountain Sect, the disciples of the Divine Light Sect were united in their hatred and anger, but they were also helpless.

After all, the Black Chicken Old Demon was like a towering mountain, pressing down on the three major sects.

Qi Yuan naturally did not hear the words of these people.

He walked his knife back to the Seven-Color Peak.

"Alas, why haven't you awakened your spiritual intelligence yet."

Qi Yuan really wanted to slap the knife, but he was afraid of cutting his own hand.

What he feared the most was that if he slapped the knife and made it stupid, what if it awakened its spiritual intelligence and became a fool?

"But I guess it'll happen in the next few days, or it might even be tonight." Qi Yuan thought to himself.

He returned to the courtyard and tied the knife to a wooden stake.

"Tonight, I need to quickly finish my game, then go to bed early. Tomorrow, I still have to attend that damn foundation building assembly, so troublesome!"

Qi Yuan felt that he was very socially anxious.

He preferred to communicate with the mute beasts in the game, and even give them voice-overs. It made him happier.

Tomorrow's foundation building assembly, he would be the face of the Divine Light Sect, welcoming guests from other sects, exchanging with them. Just thinking about it made him feel annoyed.

"If only my true self was a star in the vast universe, just hanging in the sky, not needing to say a word." Qi Yuan thought to himself as he entered the game.

What he didn't know was that at night, the vegetable knife suddenly jumped up and cut the rope that was tying it.

It frantically searched for half a day in the thatched hut, and only found a few straws to eat.

It seemed to be extremely hungry, and its gaze fell on a thatched hut not far away.

Under the moonlight, it sneaked out.

Then, it greedily eyed the thatched hut, and if it had eyes, they would have been shining. But in the end, it hesitated for a moment, and began... to rummage through the trash pile.

About half an hour later, it stealthily returned and used the rope to tie itself up again.

(End of this chapter)
