"Today's special: Buy 3 drinks, get 3 free."

Not long ago, as he was heading towards Cai Kingdom, he saw the Great Venerable's battle in the distance.

This made him feel that fate was on his side.

This time he had come to assassinate Zhang Feng, leaving his storage bag with Little Snow and bringing nothing of value.

The Thunder God Great Venerable very much wanted to say that after he assassinated Zhang Feng, it would be a double celebration.

Most of the people in the Mortal Heart Realm despised the evil gods.

As for traitors like Zhang Feng, they were even more universally condemned in private.

Hearing this, the innkeeper let out a sigh of relief.

This person even called the new gods sons of bitches, he definitely wasn't from the new god faction.

"The heavens of Cai Kingdom... have changed."

"Zhang Feng is dead."

He was preparing to go assassinate Zhang Feng, but now he's being told Zhang Feng is dead?
