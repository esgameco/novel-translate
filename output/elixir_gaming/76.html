The heavy snow continued for several days.

Qi Yuan's nose twitched: "This time it's not fish soup, you've changed the dish?"

The Moon Girl opened the bowl, and a sweet fragrance wafted over.

One Holy Maiden had left with the Seventh Month Sacrificial Priestess, her whereabouts unknown.

This Holy Maiden, the Moon Girl, was by his side.

Was she... abandoned?

The Moon Girl's gaze was resolute.

Qi Yuan said, "If you don't want to die, you have to become stronger yourself, protect yourself.

During this time, I'll occasionally give you some guidance, and whatever you can comprehend will be your fortune."

The Moon Girl heard this, her small face filled with excitement, and she nodded vigorously, looking very happy.

Qi Yuan saw this and smiled: "Respecting the elderly but not loving the young, that is not acceptable.

External demonic forces, you are wrong."

The snow did not diminish as expected, but continued to fall heavily.

Even the ancient Qi Cypress trees had turned into a white expanse.

On the Seventh Month Lake, a thick layer of ice had formed.

Qi Yuan sat alone by the lakeside.

Like the heavy drumbeats, pounding on one's heart.

The Moon Girl ran out of the wooden hut, wearing a thin long dress, her small face a little anxious.

The Moon Girl's thin figure slowly walked towards the sword a hundred meters away.

She walked alone against the wind and snow, leaving a line of neat, even footprints on the ground.
