After merging with Zitong, Qi Yuan, empowered by Xiaojia, stood nearly a hundred zhang tall.

His voice became even more demonic as he rambled nonsensically: "Many people can't see the future, but have actually already seen it."

Virtual Realm Ruler's Heart Demon Summoning.

Something terrifying happened.

Only to see a horrifying flesh tumor suddenly bulge out from his back, bursting through his robes.

Suddenly, the tumor seemed to be pierced by fingernails, yellow liquid spraying out continuously as a grotesque demon struggled free.

The eyes of that demon were full of greed and the desire to feed.

It crouched on Blue Charm Supreme's shoulder, constantly gnawing at the tumor.

This scene didn't just happen to Blue Charm Supreme.

The other supreme beings were the same.

Demons burst out of their bodies, like parasites living inside, devouring their hosts.

Some supreme beings were grimacing, twisting like beasts rolling on the ground.

Some supreme beings kept tearing at themselves, trying to peel off their own skin, as if that would make them feel better.

The scene was bizarre, horrifying, and utterly miserable!

"Devil, you're a devil!"

The mid-stage supreme cultivators could still barely speak, looking at Qi Yuan's terrifying figure with confusion and fear in their eyes.

"Spare me... I'm willing to be a dog!" Some supreme beings said with difficulty.

The tall blood-colored giant shadow looked at these frenzied supreme beings on the scene.

He calmly said: "This is the fate of those who oppose me."

The hundred supreme beings behind him all had shocked expressions. After hearing Qi Yuan's words, their hearts trembled even more.

The scene before them completely exceeded their understanding.

They only saw that Blood Lord they deeply respected and found so mysterious transform into a blood-red giant.

He drew his sword against over twenty supreme beings, and with one slash, bloody rain fell.

Terrifying things happened - the heart demons of those supreme beings were completely awakened. The extremely powerful supreme beings were helpless like children under the demons' devouring.

Such an innate divine ability easily slaughtered over twenty supreme beings. Those supreme beings had no ability to resist at all.

How could this scene not terrify and shock them?

How could the Blood Lord be a born divine being? He must be a god of heaven and hell! Everyone swallowed and remained silent.

Among the crowd, Yang Hun Supreme's body trembled.

Since becoming a supreme being, this was the first time he had felt this way.

His spine tingled, and his face was full of wild joy.

He stepped forward and shouted: "The Blood Lord is mighty, ruling over all!"

Now he looked nothing like a supreme being, more like the most fawning little follower.

Qi Yuan didn't look at Yang Hun Supreme, but focused on those twenty-some supreme beings.

At this time, those supreme beings were constantly dying painfully.

The terrifying supreme beings who could destroy a city died in agony.

Qi Yuan's experience bar was also growing frantically.

He couldn't help but sigh.

Supreme beings were worthy of the name.

The surging experience was far more enticing than those demons from earthly myths.

After a dozen breaths of time, all the supreme beings on the scene had completely perished.

The horrifying hellish scene disappeared, and Qi Yuan's level was fixed at 148.

As one cultivated to higher levels, the gaps between minor realms became larger, requiring endless time to grind through.

He was only 2 levels away from mid-stage supreme.

But Qi Yuan didn't need that. He just needed to kill, kill, kill, and his experience would grow.

Of course, this was just about levels.

Mastering innate divine abilities, from proficiency to subtlety, required one's own efforts.

Qi Yuan grinned, sweeping his gaze over everyone present: "Do you understand now how people should be killed?"

The previous battles had been wishy-washy, dragging on endlessly.

Half a day had passed without accomplishing a damn thing.

How could that compare to Qi Yuan's clean and decisive single sword strike kill?

"We understand." The supreme beings behind quickly answered.

Wang Gai was also among the crowd, his heart shocked.

Yang Hun Supreme had a face full of smiles, extremely fawning: "Compared to the Blood Lord slaying twenty-seven supreme beings with one sword, our supreme being battles are as laughable as children's squabbles."

Qi Yuan accepted the flattery. His gaze finally landed on the solitary Ning Tao, revealing a playful smile: "You're not leaving?"

Ning Tao's hand tightly gripped the chain. She looked at that blood-red figure, the trace of shock in her eyes dissipating: "If you don't speak, how dare I leave?"

"If you had left earlier, I probably couldn't have stopped you," Qi Yuan said calmly.

Ning Tao's strength was a notch above Purple Daoist's.

Of course, if Qi Yuan really wanted to stop her, he could.

But there was no need for him to expend so much effort.

He had rather complex emotions towards this NPC Ning Tao.

Ning Tao looked at Qi Yuan, her hand still tightly gripping the chain. Her expression was a bit unnatural, suppressing something - perhaps fear, perhaps something else.

"By slaying these Great Supremes, you have saved my life. This humble woman has no way to repay..."

Qi Yuan interrupted Ning Tao's words and slowly asked: "Do you know about the Blood Palace?"

Ning Tao shook her head in confusion.

"Do you know about the Blood Lord?"

Ning Tao still shook her head.

"Do you know about the Golden Canary? She's a very tsundere, very vain girl. I've been looking for her." Qi Yuan looked at Ning Tao, a trace of tenderness flashing in his blood-red eyes.

[Ning Tao, Level 197, Tianxin Palace Master, one of the cursed Thirteen Northern Demon Beauties.]

Ning Tao shook her head: "I don't know. She... must be someone very important to you?"

"Mm, I've been looking for her." Qi Yuan said, "I saved your life in passing, you don't need to repay me. If you ever encounter her in the future, tell her I'm waiting for her... in No Return City."

Qi Yuan's gaze was profound.

Ning Tao looked at Qi Yuan and replied: "Great kindness needs no thanks. I will help you look out for this person!"

After a long while, she suddenly asked: "Have we met somewhere before? Why do I keep feeling a sense of familiarity?"

"Perhaps we met in a dream." Qi Yuan laughed loudly.

After laughing, he swept his gaze over all the supreme beings present.

"Swear a heart demon oath not to reveal today's events. Understand?"

The other supreme beings hurriedly swore heart demon oaths without hesitation upon hearing this.

Ning Tao did the same.

"The heart demon oath is sworn. You may leave." Qi Yuan said generously to Ning Tao.

Originally, Qi Yuan had thought of the "humiliation" he suffered in the underground palace, almost being forced to call her "mother".

He had also prepared to force this Great Supreme Ning Tao to call him "daddy" before letting her go.

But in the end he thought playing too perversely didn't fit his image as the cold and domineering Blood Lord.

Ning Tao glanced at Qi Yuan, her hand tightly gripping the chain, her form turning into a streak of light and disappearing.

After an unknown amount of time, Ning Tao landed in the great desert. She looked at the flesh in the chain behind her and murmured: "Husband, Golden Canary must be a very lovely woman."

As she spoke, she covered her left cheek with her hand.

"She must be much prettier than my ugly appearance."

She took a deep look behind her, her eyes no longer lingering. She transformed into a streak of light and vanished.
