Qi Yuan slowly walked through the Divine Light Sect, holding a kitchen knife.

Along the way, laughter and sweet nothings could occasionally be heard.

This immortal cultivation world was truly harmonious.

He led his pet and looked at the sky: "It's time to return to the mountain."

It was still early, so he could open the game jade slip and play a round.

When he returned to the Seven-Colored Peak, the night had already fallen.

At the top of the peak, above the white jade palace, a crescent moon hung, and the cool moonlight cast a faint, graceful silhouette.

However, the silhouette only glanced at Qi Yuan briefly before disappearing.

Qi Yuan averted his gaze and looked at Jiang Lingshu's thatched cottage, murmuring to himself: "If only I had those paralysis talismans, then even if an earthquake happened on the Blue Star, those houses wouldn't collapse."

After sighing, he led the kitchen knife back to his own courtyard.

The courtyard was empty, only waiting for Qi Yuan's footsteps.

He untied the rope from the knife's handle: "Now that you're home, you don't need to be tied up. As long as you don't destroy the house, no one will hit you."

Qi Yuan said as he caressed the small nicks on the knife, then entered the world of the game jade slip.

However, when he was about to enter the game, Qi Yuan was stunned.

"She's there?"

When he obtained the game jade slip, Qi Yuan had treated it as a gaming device.

At the time, the game map had not yet been unlocked.

But Qi Yuan had inexplicably added a friend, and entered the friend list.

That was the only friend he made after falling ill.

Since his illness, he had lived alone, with no one to communicate with.

The friend in the game had become his companion.

Qi Yuan also treated that person as a fellow gamer.

They often chatted about all sorts of topics, with the other person doing most of the talking.

However, the other person's speech was quite strange, occasionally mentioning words like "Imperial Guards" and "Prime Minister."

Qi Yuan guessed that the other person must be a wealthy young lady inheriting the family business, but not yet fully in control of the company.

They had talked a lot, and Qi Yuan had even said, "Let's team up and play together."

Unfortunately, after the game map was refreshed, the friend list had disappeared, and he never saw the other person again, let alone team up.

Now, seeing the friend list, he pondered for a moment and sent a message: "Are you there?"

Since he had transmigrated to the immortal cultivation world, the other person might not be there.

Mist shrouded the area, and the ink painting on the screen seemed to be infected, with long cracks appearing.

"Leave."

A cold and majestic voice came, and the palace maids in attendance quickly bowed and left.

After the maids left, the beautiful face of the Empress of the Southern Qin Kingdom showed a weary expression.

Ten years ago, the Demon Tribe invaded. Her father, the Emperor, was killed in battle. She became the Empress of the Southern Qin Kingdom at the age of 7.

The vast empire now rested entirely on her shoulders.

The Demon Tribe's invasion, the coveting of neighboring kingdoms, and the disloyalty of court officials - she had struggled for ten years.

Now, she could barely maintain the balance of the empire. The burden on her shoulders was growing heavier and heavier.

She was even in constant danger of losing her life.

Especially recently, the Prime Minister Sima Ting had almost openly colluded with the Northern Huan Kingdom, making her situation even more precarious.

Three days ago, her most trusted Aunt Qin, a King-level expert, was attacked by a group of black-clad assassins and severely injured.

Although the assassins' origins were well hidden, everyone in the court knew who had sent them.

Knowing the enemy, yet being powerless to do anything - this feeling was unpleasant.

She stood in front of the screen, her azure gown simple yet elegant, the hem embroidered with golden phoenixes. Her slender waist was wrapped in a white jade belt.

As the belt was untied and the garment fell away, her flawless, jade-like skin was revealed.

The empress's age was not great, but her figure was very proud, forming a sharp contrast with her slender waist that could be grasped by one hand.

As she entered the bathtub, her snow-white and exquisite shoulders floated on the surface of the water. Through the water that was like white broth, one could almost see her pearly white skin.

At this moment, a flash of red light suddenly appeared on the table.

A gleam of joy flashed in Jinli's eyes.

"Qi Yuan?"

What was placed on the table was the rare and precious Linglong Jade Tide.

It was rumored that this treasure was a pair. Whoever obtained this treasure could communicate with each other through it.

In ancient times, this kind of treasure was very famous. Unfortunately, as the times changed, treasures that could transmit messages over long distances were no longer rare.

The Linglong Jade Tide had greatly depreciated in value and was placed in the imperial treasury.

A year ago, Jinli went to the imperial treasury and saw the Linglong Jade Tide, so she took it out casually.

Unexpectedly, she got to know an interesting person through the Linglong Jade Tide.

That person was named Qi Yuan, who spoke freely and always said things she didn't understand.

Since the two were not acquainted and seemed to be separated by mountains and rivers, she could freely express her heart.

After all, as the empress, she could not let others see her weakness.

Unfortunately, half a year ago, for some unknown reason, Qi Yuan disappeared without a trace. She was then somewhat disappointed and anxious.

Unexpectedly, the other party was still there.

The stone in her heart fell to the ground.

She picked up the Linglong Jade Tide and held it tightly, looking at the message on it, and quickly replied, "Here."

After sending the message, Jinli thought for a moment and asked, "Where have you been these days? You've been gone for a while."

"Last time, I was going to invite you to go grind monsters, but unexpectedly, I suddenly entered the game map and couldn't contact the outside world."

"That should be a very dangerous place, right?" Jinli asked.

In her eyes, Qi Yuan was a free and easy monster hunter. What he said about grinding monsters and the game map was about hunting demonic beasts.

On the other side, Qi Yuan shrugged, "We players are the fourth calamity, what's so dangerous about it? Those monsters are just our experience packages."

Seeing the familiar tone and response, Jinli's heart calmed down.

Every time she talked with Qi Yuan about heaven and earth, even though Qi Yuan said nonsensical things, she still felt a sense of peace in her heart.

"This time you were gone, I grinded monsters alone for half a year to finish. Do you want to team up with me? A male-female duo, the work won't be tiring. If it's the two of us, we'll definitely level up to 100 quickly."

Hearing this, Jinli replied, "I have some things to do at home and can't leave."

"Are you that busy? Don't even have time to play games. Sigh, you rich and beautiful ladies are so hardworking, how can we poor people cope?"

Seeing this, Jinli couldn't help but smile.

"How about you come and grind monsters with me now? I just changed the map, and there are a lot of monsters here. I need the support of the beautiful Lady Jinli," Qi Yuan said.

Seeing the other party's relaxed tone, Jinli knew that the other party was not in danger.

She curved her lips into a smile: "I'm not very convenient right now."

"What are you doing? Not convenient, you're not working overtime, are you?"

"I'm... bathing," Jinli hesitated for a moment before sending the message.

On the other side, Qi Yuan saw this and paused.

His gaze remained calm: "Then how about we make an appointment after you finish bathing?"

On the other side, Jinli gave a wry smile: "I can't come."

But thinking of something, she asked, "Where are you?"

"I was previously in Xuanyuan Forbidden, and now I'm on my way to Yiguan Forbidden," Qi Yuan did not hide it.

He was in the game's teleportation array, preparing to teleport to the next map.

"Xuanyuan Forbidden? Yiguan Forbidden?" Jinli was stunned.

Weren't those the four great forbidden lands?

The rumors said that even king-level experts would be bloodied to death if they entered, and even emperor-level experts might perish.

No, Xuanyuan Forbidden, even an emperor-level expert could not enter.
