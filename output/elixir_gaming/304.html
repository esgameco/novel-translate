Chapter 304

Here is my full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The glass on the buildings looked as if it hadn't been cleaned in over a decade, appearing gray and hazy.

Inside the building, the handrails on the stairs were greasy and blackened.

A man wearing some kind of military uniform looked at the civilian Wen Min and Qi Yuan, and belched: "Old Chen asked me to bring you guys over. It's no problem, but... you'll have to behave yourselves along the way, understand?"

As he spoke, the uniformed man lifted his coat, revealing a gun holstered at his waist.

"No problem!" the burly civilian Wen Min quickly agreed.

Qi Yuan didn't say anything, which was taken as agreement.

"Also, according to the rules... you need to hand over your weapons," the uniformed man said, looking at the two of them.

Wen Min didn't hesitate at all, pulling two guns from his waist and handing them to the uniformed man.

The uniformed man glanced at Qi Yuan: "What about you?"

The uniformed man looked at the neat and clean Qi Yuan with some confusion in his eyes.

Qi Yuan clearly wasn't a local, but rather looked like a pampered young master.

Why would he come here... to smuggle?

"Where are your weapons?"

At this point, Qi Yuan thought for a moment, then suddenly grabbed a handful of air and gave it to the uniformed man.

"Does this count?"

Wen Min was bewildered seeing this.

The uniformed man was stunned for a moment, then looked at Qi Yuan: "You're not carrying any weapons?"

"As a martial artist, my fists and legs are my weapons, but these... I can't give you," Qi Yuan said very sincerely.

When asked to hand over weapons, he directly grabbed a handful of air.

As a cultivator, even the air here could be used as a weapon.

But he had too many weapons, afraid the big guy couldn't handle them all, so he only gave him a handful of air.

"You're actually a martial arts practitioner?" The uniformed man smiled, clearly believing Qi Yuan's words. "When I was young, I was also crazy about martial arts. Later I studied for half a year, got an injection, and never practiced again."

The injection he mentioned was of course a gene drug.

Speaking as someone with experience, he said: "There's no future in practicing martial arts."

Before Qi Yuan could respond, a man behind the uniformed man said: "Who says martial artists are useless? Not long ago, a priest from Wugui country was caught off guard and killed by a martial artist."

Mentioning this, the uniformed man laughed loudly: "The same thing won't happen twice.

I may not look like much, but when facing those martial artists at their limits, it's no problem for me to easily take them on one-on-one.

If I can't win, I'll just blow their brains out with a gun."

The uniformed man spoke casually.

His words also largely represented the mainstream view of martial arts.

Can't compare to gene drugs, let alone guns.

About half an hour later, Qi Yuan was sitting in Wen Min's car.

His car had been searched, and all weapons had been taken away.

"When we get to Wugui country, I'll go find an arms dealer to buy some weapons," the burly Wen Min said to Qi Yuan.

Without weapons, how could they rescue Kuanghuan, they couldn't even protect themselves.

"No need to buy weapons, too flashy, and I can't use them anyway.

When we get to Wugui country, you just need to take me to a place where I can see the Wugui military camp, I'll handle the rest," Qi Yuan naturally didn't need weapons.

Moreover, there were many types of weapons on Gongxing, and he probably wouldn't know how to use many of them.

Wen Min was stunned, puzzled in his heart. He hesitated for a moment, but finally followed Qi Yuan's instructions: "Everything will be done as Mr. Qi says."

Many thoughts flashed through his mind.

Could it be that Mr. Qi had someone inside the Wugui military camp who could rescue the boss? Besides this, he couldn't think of any other possibilities.

Of course, he wouldn't imagine that Qi Yuan actually planned to storm into the Wugui military camp single-handedly to rescue the person.

The convoy began to move.

This smuggling convoy, led by the uniformed man, slowly drove towards Wugui country.

There were over twenty vehicles in total, quite an impressive sight, kicking up clouds of dust.

Outside the car window was a murky river, as well as various scorched earth. Occasionally, collapsed buildings and shell craters could be seen.

From time to time, drones could be seen wandering in the sky.

The burly Wen Min glanced at the drones in the sky, his eyes full of dread: "If we weren't following this convoy and encountered these drones, we would certainly die.

Even if we're not soldiers from Yuelang country, once killed by the drones, we could still be counted as military achievements by Wugui soldiers."

"You're exaggerating. With just these drones, I could take care of them all with one Kamehameha, where's the danger?" Qi Yuan looked at these drones, not concerned at all.

Now, he already had the ability to protect himself.

The only things that could threaten him were missiles and some weapons above that level.

Wen Min was already used to Qi Yuan's way of speaking. He smiled: "Then Mr. Qi, your Kamehameha must be stronger than bullets.

Ordinary bullets can't destroy this type of drone."

The car drove quickly, and the sound of artillery fire could be heard from time to time.

Wen Min explained that this might be border artillery exchanges between Yuelang and Wugui.

About two hours later, the convoy arrived at a checkpoint in Wugui country.

Here, there was a squad of Wugui soldiers stationed.

These soldiers were well-armed, wearing bulletproof vests, and conducted an inspection of the convoy.

The inspection lasted for half an hour before they were allowed to pass.

After passing through, Wen Min breathed a sigh of relief, but also felt uneasy: "Everything depends on Mr. Qi now."

Having entered Wugui country, getting out again wouldn't be so simple.

Those smugglers only brought them in, they wouldn't help them get out.

"Hmm..." Qi Yuan took a deep breath. He looked out the car window and asked curiously, "What are those red scarves hanging on the trees for? Are these trees... all Young Pioneers?"

Wen Min was stunned for a moment, then said: "That's a ritual of the Guiran Holy Religion.

Red scarves hanging on trees represent that this area is under the protection of the Wu God."

Wugui country was a religious state, where religious power even surpassed royal authority.

The holy religion of Wugui country was called Wu Religion, and they worshipped a deity called Wu God.

It could be said that over 90% of people in Wugui country believed in Wu God.

There was a saying in Wu God's teachings that all heretics should be hanged on trees.

Of course, in civilized society, the Wu Religion still wanted to save face and wouldn't blatantly persecute others.

Only some fanatics would do such things to persecute others.

They would kill heretics, drain their blood completely, dye cloths red with it, and hang them on trees.

So, among the red scarves on the trees, some were ordinary red scarves, while others might be the blood of heretics.

After entering Wugui territory, the probability of seeing red scarves on trees was quite high.

About three hours later, the car finally stopped in a small town.

The uniformed man came to Qi Yuan's car and knocked on the window: "I'm only responsible for bringing you here. If you want to leave, contact me, but the price... well... it's a bit expensive, same as those people."

As he spoke, he pointed to a group of disorderly people not far away.

After finishing, he left directly.

Qi Yuan looked towards the disorderly group of people in the distance. Among them were old people, young people, and children.

They were dressed in rags.

Some were dressed shabbily, while others wore bright clothes.

Wenmin explained: "Most of these people are not Guiran natives. They want to leave Wugui country.

Now that there's war in Wugui, these people want to leave here.

They don't have many options, smuggling convoys are their only way out.

However, most people can't afford the fare, so they can only exchange other things. Most people don't even have anything to offer."

Qi Yuan swept his gaze over those people, his eyes showing curiosity.

"Let's go take a closer look."

Wenmin was surprised, but without any hesitation, he drove the car towards that group of people.

Sure enough, along the way, those people reached out their hands, as if trying to pry open the car doors. From outside the car, their voices could also be heard.

"Take me with you, please!"

"My daughter is very beautiful, she can serve you along the way. Take us away from here!"

"I have money!"

"Antiques, thousand-year-old relics!"

These people shouted, their eyes revealing a desperate look.

Most of these people were not Guiran natives, but had come to Wugui country for work, or had entered illegally.

Some were in an even more miserable state, having been abducted here.

Wugui country was very xenophobic, and these people lived very poorly here.

Especially now that Wugui country was at war with Yuelang country.

Sometimes, to make their statistics look better, Wugui country would even kill a batch of them.

So, staying in Wugui country was like being lambs waiting for slaughter.

Seeing this scene, Wenmin couldn't help but sigh: "You can't see scenes like this online. Our people in Qinyuan country... can't see the miserable state of these people either."

For these people, their fate may have been sealed from birth, perhaps destined for misfortune.

Although Wugui was a religious country, it didn't mean you were one of them just because you believed in their religion.

Without Guiran bloodline, you were considered inferior.

Who knows when your blood might be drained by fanatics, to dye cloths red and tie them to trees.

The car drove slowly.

Qi Yuan's heart was calm, without much emotion.

"Stop the car."

The car stopped.

Chaotic voices came through.

Some unruly people crowded over: "Boss!"

"Get back!" Wenmin spoke, his voice gruff.

Immediately, those unruly people dared not make noise anymore.

Because they recognized that Qi Yuan's car had come in with the smugglers.

Such people had great power.

Even if they killed them, no one would care.

Qi Yuan scanned the crowd, his gaze falling on a dark-skinned youth.

This youth looked only eleven or twelve years old.

He was holding the hand of a little girl, who looked dirty, her hair seemingly unwashed for several months.

This girl appeared to be only seven or eight years old.

He looked at the two, his eyes revealing an interested expression.

"Do you two want to leave Wugui?"

He said to the youth and the little girl.

Hearing Qi Yuan's question, the youth's eyes burst with excitement, but then quickly showed fear and caution.

"Yes... sir, I... I have no money, but I'm strong and very obedient, I can do hard labor," the youth Yaji said nervously. As he spoke, he shielded the little girl behind him, afraid that Qi Yuan might be someone with perverse intentions.

"I can take you out, but... I'll need some of your blood,"

Qi Yuan stated his intention.

He had discovered that in this world, quite a few people had special blood.

For example, Wuqi.

And now this youth and little girl.

Of course, their specialness was different.

Qi Yuan hadn't yet researched what was special about Wuqi's blood.

But the specialness of the youth and little girl's blood was obvious - it was of the same origin as Qi Yuan's "bloodline".

For Qi Yuan to break through in his martial arts and improve his martial aptitude, the blood of these two... could be researched and utilized.

Qi Yuan's words were not only incomprehensible to the siblings, even Wenmin didn't understand.

The youth hesitated for a moment, but finally nodded vigorously and said with a hunched back: "Alright!"

No matter what, for them, leaving this place was the safest option now.

After all, just a while ago, their parents had already been taken away to serve as "military achievements".

Who knows when it would be their turn.

Now, their only chance of survival was to follow this car and leave Wugui country first.

Looking at Qi Yuan, the dark-skinned youth's face was full of gratitude.

The dirty little girl seemed scared and dared not look at Qi Yuan.

"Do you have anything to bring with you?" Qi Yuan asked.

The dark-skinned youth had a large bundle on his back, holding his sister's hand. That large bundle was all their family's possessions: "No."

"Alright, get in the car," Qi Yuan said.

The dark-skinned youth didn't hesitate at all, leading his sister into the car.

The car Wenmin was driving had three rows of seats.

The dark-skinned youth and his sister sat in the last row. The youth's face showed a respectful expression, using the phrases that Wugui people loved to say.

"May Wu God bless you!"

"You are the most generous person!"

Yaji wasn't a believer of the Wu religion, but to live in Wugui country, saying "May Wu God bless you" was a must.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan smiled: "If I wanted to kill that Yang God in Taihuang Palace, could Wu God bless me?"

That Yang God in Taihuang Palace was the person behind the great calamity, and also Qi Yuan's strongest enemy in the Canglanjie realm at this stage.

Yaji was stunned by Qi Yuan's words, not knowing how to respond.

The dirty little girl, however, said: "I heard them say Wu God is very powerful, can run while carrying a big mountain, so... he should be able to bless you!"

Living in Wugui country, they had naturally been influenced by the propaganda about Wu God's power.

Qi Yuan smiled: "It's all made up. They couldn't even make up a more powerful deity.

Running while carrying a big mountain... that's quite weak."

Qi Yuan found that people in this world were very practical.

Even their made-up stories weren't too exaggerated.

For a deity, they didn't dare to make up claims of omnipotence.

He wasn't sure if this was due to being too practical, or if there were deeper reasons.

The youth Yaji didn't quite understand Qi Yuan's words. He looked out the car window, his mind settling a bit. He secretly asked, "Sir... when will we leave Wugui?"

Leaving Wugui country was his greatest wish.

In this foreign land, he had no other relatives left except his sister.

As for revenge, it was unthinkable.

For small figures like them, struggling to survive was already difficult, let alone seeking revenge.

"After I go pick up a friend, then we'll leave," Qi Yuan answered casually.

Wenmin, who was driving, remained silent.

He didn't know why Qi Yuan wanted to bring these two children into the car.

He also didn't understand what leverage Qi Yuan had, daring to go to the military camp to bring the boss back.

The car drove quickly. Wenmin refueled once along the way, pushing the speed to the limit.

Following the navigation, the car sped towards the location of the military camp.

The number of drones in the sky also increased.

But at this time, obviously these drones weren't paying attention to this domestic car.
