He picked up the fish, his gaze complex.

"After so many years, I hope it hasn't expired?"

"This guy, he still left me some fish."

He used chopsticks to pick up a piece of fish, put it in his mouth, and chewed slowly, savoring the taste.

At this moment, a childish voice sounded in Qi Yuan's ear.

"Hehe, actually my name isn't Wu Shi, I don't have a name."

"When I first saw you, I already knew we would meet three times."

"Why? Because I not only heard your voice, but also heard your inner thoughts."

The girl's voice brought Qi Yuan back to not long ago, to the scene when he saw the girl Wu Shi, which was also the last time he saw the Mother of No Food.

The voice was childish, as if it was what she wanted to say when she first met him.

Qi Yuan had a moment of realization.

No wonder... she looked so strange, because she heard his inner thoughts.

So, she must have known that they would meet again in the future, and she also knew that her future self would die at the hands of Yang Shen.

Qi Yuan ate the fish as the girl's chattering voice continued.

"When I first saw you, I was very wary, thinking you might eat me. I misjudged you."

"But hehe, I wanted to eat you, but I was too timid and didn't dare."

The girl spoke about her past, as if writing in a diary, complaining about her lonely life.

As Qi Yuan ate the fish, savoring it slowly, the voice in his ear gradually lost its childishness and became more mature.

"The second time I saw you, I was a bit excited, because I could finally eat delicious fish."

"I was really, really hungry."

"Compared to eating fish, nothing else mattered."

"Your scent was even more tempting then, I... I really wanted to eat you at that time."

Qi Yuan couldn't help but recall the second time he saw the Mother of No Food, when she was curled up on the ground in a red dress, her face pale, looking at him with a hint of greed in her eyes.

But later, after she finished eating the fish, her expression turned cold, keeping Qi Yuan at a distance.

"So, her change in attitude then was because she wanted to eat me?"

Qi Yuan's gaze was calm.

From the Mother of No Food's words, he already knew she had both the Innate Original Body and the Devouring Original Body.

These two terrifying constitutions conflicted with each other.

No wonder whenever he saw the Mother of No Food, she always seemed strange, as if suppressing something.

What she was suppressing was her desire to devour his heart.

"A chance encounter, you give me fish to eat, I help you guard the Pillar of Heaven, we're even."

"If you harbor ill intentions, I devour you... that's not too much, is it?"

The Mother of No Food's tone was somewhat cheerful.

But Qi Yuan knew the suppression in her heart.

The desire to devour from the Devouring Original Body was very, very strong.

Back then, the mythical level Mother of No Food ate ordinary fish and ended up writhing in agony, trembling on the ground.

That was a mythical being, who didn't even cry out in pain when facing Yang Shen with her body riddled with wounds.

Yet just eating a piece of fish caused her unbearable pain.

"Fortunately, later I realized that what I had been eating all along was your... blood."

The Mother of No Food's voice carried a sense of detachment.

She seemed to have convinced herself not to follow her instinct to devour Qi Yuan.

Qi Yuan's gaze was complex: "So, at that time she... could really have eaten me?"

At that time, when he was near the Mother of No Food, even though separated by time and space, he sometimes felt a faint threat.

He recalled the first time he met the Mother of No Food.

She was quietly eating fish in front of him.

At that time, the fish had not been purified by his blood, yet the Mother of No Food still ate it with relish.

In the past, he didn't know why this was. He had thought that the Mother of No Food he saw each time was not the same person.

Now he understood, the Mother of No Food ate unpurified fish, using that extreme pain to suppress her desire to devour.

Qi Yuan's emotions were complex.

He never imagined that in their three simple meetings, where nothing earth-shattering happened, just eating, drinking, and chatting, so much was hidden behind it all.

"I wandered around the Mortal Heart Realm for ten thousand years, this world is much more fun than my hometown, and smells much better too..."

The Mother of No Food's voice was somewhat cheerful.

Qi Yuan remained silent.

The Mother of No Food's childhood was indeed miserable, raised at home like a canary, or rather like a commodity.

Coming to the Mortal Heart Realm, where people like Qi Yuan had limited knowledge and didn't know about the two terrifying constitutions she possessed, everyone looked at her normally.

It was natural that she lived more happily in the Mortal Heart Realm than in her hometown.

The ten thousand years in the Mortal Heart Realm were her happiest times.

As he ate the last piece of fish, a voice sounded in Qi Yuan's ear.

"Since we've met, how about we make a deal?"

"I'll help you guard the Pillar of Heaven, and you help me... protect the Mortal Heart Realm."

Qi Yuan ate the last of the fish.

He remembered that he had exchanged a fish for the Mother of No Food's help in guarding the Pillar of Heaven.

Now, the Mother of No Food had returned all the fish to him.

After their second meeting, when the Mother of No Food realized that the fish she ate contained Qi Yuan's blood, she stopped eating them and left the fish for Qi Yuan.

These fish contained flawless blood.

After eating these fish, the blood Qi Yuan had lost was replenished.

The aura around him also became more profound.

"Mother of No Food, you've lost out," Qi Yuan sighed.

Because, even without making a deal with the Mother of No Food, due to the game, he would have to protect the Mortal Heart Realm, or rather protect Qingshui Village.

In this deal, the Mother of No Food had lost out.

Qi Yuan's heart was calm as he thought about many things.

Just then, suddenly, his gaze changed drastically.

For some reason, he felt the solar true fire on the Star Golden Pill in the sky suddenly flare up.

"What's going on?"

He was somewhat surprised, looking around himself.

The Star Golden Pill in the sky had always been quiet, methodically devouring other stars according to Qi Yuan's wishes.

Ning Tao sat within it, time seemingly at a standstill.

Due to the Great Forgetting Heart Sutra, the divine domain seed awakened by Ning Tao was related to the passage of time.

Now, after eating the fish, Qi Yuan's Star Golden Pill suddenly underwent a change, as if it had abruptly gained the ability to devour.

"Devouring Original Body!"

Qi Yuan was suddenly startled.

He looked at his empty storage bag, many thoughts flashing through his mind.

Back then, he had used crystal-clear flawless blood to purify the fish for the Mother of No Food to eat.

Now... the Mother of No Food, not wanting to owe him, had also added her blood to the fish? Now, his body had gained a trace of the essence of devouring.

This essence of devouring was something the Mother of No Food had separated and placed in the fish as a gift to him.

This essence was different from divine duties, and different from divine domains.

A helpless smile appeared in Qi Yuan's eyes: "Such a great favor, are you asking me to protect this world... for a lifetime?"
