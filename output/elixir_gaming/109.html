"Guardian! Guardian!"

At the foot of the city wall, the cultivator ran forward excitedly.

Dong Long gazed out at the vast desert. The news from afar barely caused a ripple in his heart.

He was pondering how to deal with Wu Tong using Galan City's military forces.

Wu Tong had nearly a hundred Nation Protectors under his command.

Such terrifying strength would be difficult for Galan City to counter.

If only the Guardian of Wugui City had killed some Nation Protectors before leaving, that would have been helpful.

After about ten breaths, seemingly noticing the cultivator sent from Wugui City had climbed the city wall, he slowly asked: "Did the Guardian of Wugui City kill any Nation Protectors?"

The cultivator who originally wanted to report had to swallow the words he had prepared. He excitedly said: "Yes, he did!"

Hearing this, Dong Long was not surprised.

Given the Guardian of Wugui City's strength, killing a few Nation Protectors was not a problem.

"He killed..." Dong Long was about to ask how many were killed, but thinking that would make him seem too utilitarian, he instead asked, "Did the Guardian of Wugui City escape from Wugui City?"

Hearing this, the cultivator blinked.

He felt the Guardian was being too fussy today, always asking strange questions. Why couldn't he just let him report properly? But the other was his superior, so he couldn't be rude.

"He didn't escape!" the cultivator answered truthfully.

How could the Guardian of Wugui City have escaped? He was still sitting comfortably inside the city walls!

"What?" Hearing this, Dong Long was shocked, tears about to fall from his eyes. "The Guardian of Wugui City... actually fought to the death without fleeing?"

Hearing this, the soldiers present all felt a sense of tragic heroism. Some let out long cries to the heavens, some clenched their fists.

"Wugui City has fallen, but the Guardian of Wugui City will live on in our hearts!"

"No matter how strong Wu Tong is, he'll have to step over my corpse!"

Damn it! Hearing all this, the messenger cultivator really wanted to slap Dong Long, but he didn't dare.

However, he still mustered up the courage and said loudly: "Everyone, please listen to me. The Guardian of Wugui City is not dead!"

With these words, everyone present was stunned.

The gazes of over a dozen Nation Protectors fell on this cultivator, making him feel immense pressure.

"The Guardian of Wugui City isn't dead. Did he surrender?"

"How despicable!"

Looking at these bigwigs, the cultivator felt extremely speechless: "Um... could you all not speak and let me talk?"

Silence.

Silence.

A moment of silence fell over the scene.

Seeing this, Dong Long said calmly: "Everyone calm down and let him speak."

Hearing this, the cultivator let out a sigh of relief.

He hurriedly said:

"Not only is the Guardian of Wugui City not dead, he single-handedly fought against Wu Tong's demonic army of over ten thousand! With just one man and one sword, he annihilated Wu Tong's demonic army. Wu Tong was killed in battle. No, none of the demonic army or demon controllers who came are still alive!" The cultivator held his breath and blurted it all out in one go.

After saying it, he felt very satisfied. After feeling satisfied, he then felt a bit nervous.

At this moment, everyone present fell completely silent. They could hardly believe their ears.

Dong Long looked at the cultivator, his gaze piercing: "Is everything you said true?"

"Reporting to Guardian, this lowly one's words contain no falsehood. If I dare to deceive, I am your father!" The cultivator had been stifled earlier, so now he spoke boldly.

He even accidentally said something wrong at the end.

But at this moment, no one in Galan City paid attention to his last sentence.

Everyone's faces showed expressions of disbelief.

The Guardian of Wugui City single-handedly wiped out Wu Tong's army? Wu Tong was killed in battle?

This was simply like a myth! Even a Terrestrial Deity would only face death when surrounded by Wu Tong's army.

But not only did the Guardian of Wugui City not die, he killed all the enemies!

It was unimaginable, unthinkable, like a dream.

"Haha, Wu Tong is dead!" Dong Long couldn't help but laugh loudly. "To think the Guardian of Wugui City was so terrifyingly strong!"

Dong Long laughed heartily, dancing about. He pushed so hard that half the bricks on the city wall fell down, and he himself tumbled down from the wall.

A Nation Protector, laughing with joy, crashed into the city wall and fell straight down.

Seeing this, the other Nation Protectors present all burst out laughing too.

Only the messenger cultivator looked a bit worried, but seeing everyone laughing, he also started laughing along.

...

In Wugui City, Qi Yuan sat atop the city wall.

"A strike with the power of a Supreme Being..."

A thoughtful look flashed in Qi Yuan's eyes.

"Is a Supreme Being targeting me... or something else?"

He swept his gaze over Wugui City.

The empty Wugui City had only Yuan Qi sweeping fallen leaves, with no one else around.

"Right now, I'm still a bit weak against a Supreme Being." Qi Yuan sighed.

Although the opponent had used a strike with Supreme power this time, Qi Yuan had fought many fallen Supreme Beings in the Absolute Realm. He had mastered some techniques to evade their power, enough to kill the opponent.

But this wasn't omnipotent.

Against a true, undamaged Supreme Being, his attacks would not be able to harm them.

"Unless I have my own Supreme divine ability."

That would require reaching level 120.

Right now, he was only level 94.

He was still very far from level 120.

"Wu Tong is dead. The Yu Province region will be vacant for some time. I should take this opportunity to sweep through and clear out the demons in Yu Province."

Qi Yuan made a decision in his heart.

With his current strength, he could already protect himself. Even if he encountered a large-scale demonic army attack, he would not be afraid.

It was also time for him to consider clearing out the demons around Wugui City to increase his strength.

Quickly reach level 120, then go for the third enlightenment of the Source.

Qi Yuan didn't know how many years had passed for the canary between the second and third Source enlightenments.

He also didn't know if he would be able to see the canary again, to see Mount Jiupan.

"If she's still around, where would she be?" Qi Yuan raised his head to look at the vast desert.

...

Seven years passed in the blink of an eye.

Tremendous changes occurred in the Yu Province region.

The Guardian of Wugui City, like an elusive deity, frequently struck into the midst of demonic armies.

Every few days, thousands or tens of thousands of demon soldiers would fall to the Guardian of Wugui City.

Cultivators in the other cities of Yu Province would occasionally claim to have seen a terrifying blood-colored giant figure slaughtering demonic armies in the desert.

Even from afar, it inspired awe.

That Guardian of Wugui City was like an undying demon god, tireless and invulnerable, forcibly killing off all the demons in Yu Province.

Galan City had not seen a demon attack for a year.

The last time demons attacked the city, before the Galan City defenders could even warm up, a sword flew in from the sky and slaughtered those demons.

Now, outside Wugui City, the terrifying blood-red figure had grown to twelve zhang tall.

Qi Yuan stood in the yellow sand, his whole body emanating a thick murderous aura.

If people from the Palace of Light saw Qi Yuan now, they would definitely mistake him for a demon cultivator and kill him.

At this moment, Qi Yuan pulled out his broken sword and stuck it into the yellow sand.

He looked down at Yuan Qi and slowly said: "I will be leaving Wugui City, for a few months at shortest, a hundred years at longest."

Qi Yuan had made all preparations and was about to log off to take a look.

Hearing this, Yuan Qi's heart tightened. He said through gritted teeth: "I will help the lord watch over Wugui City!"

The closer he got to Qi Yuan, the greater his fear grew.

Especially after Qi Yuan slaughtered demons, he felt like he was facing not a cultivator, but a demon.

Over these years, Yuan Qi had watched Qi Yuan grow stronger step by step.

He guessed in his heart that Qi Yuan must be cultivating some secret technique to increase his strength, becoming like this in order to kill demons.

The Guardian had sacrificed too much for Wugui City. How could he fear when others feared him?

All these years, he had been in Wugui City and had seen what Qi Yuan faced.

One man guarding a city - very dashing? But also very, very tiring.

"This sword can kill demons. If too many demons attack, I will sense it.

If NPCs from other cities come, don't let them approach, to avoid accidental injury." Qi Yuan gave another instruction.

Now that the surrounding demons had been cleared out, Wugui City would not face a demonic army attack for some time.

It was time for him to log off.

After all, there was still mental consumption in the game.

"Understood!" Yuan Qi was like a loyal servant.

This once spirited young general had now entered middle age, with the traces of time etched on his face.

After speaking, Qi Yuan's figure disappeared.

He hugged Little Jia, found a secluded spot, dug a hole, and buried himself with Little Jia as he had done in the past.

"How unlucky!"

"So many bones, must be from Wu Tong's men?"

"No good, in the future I must grind the bones to dust after killing. Otherwise digging holes and hitting their bones would be too awkward!"

"Karmic ties!"

...

At Shenguang Sect, Qi Yuan woke up from the game.

One day in the real world, ten years in the game.
