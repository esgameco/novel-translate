Blood Robe spoke harshly, using the ice egg to "lure" the geniuses of Ghost Domination Heaven, seeking a battle.

As a result, when they came, Blood Robe's figure had disappeared without a trace.

They searched for a long time but couldn't find him.

"We thought he had some skills, but now that we've come, is he just hiding and not coming out?"

These geniuses spoke up, shouting everywhere in the Flower Moon Wonderland, wanting to overturn it to find Blood Robe.

They came aggressively, but stayed in groups of three to five, not daring to separate.

They were afraid Blood Robe was lurking in the shadows, waiting for them to be alone to suddenly attack.

So at this moment, they were all cursing Blood Robe, trying to force him to appear.

"Is this all you've got? And you call yourself the second genius of the Black Magic Abyss? You're far inferior to Black Sword."

Unfortunately, their angry curses weren't heard by Qi Yuan at all.

With his absolute adaptation, he was sleeping soundly.

The voices cursing him fell on his ears but were "deleted as bad comments" by his absolute adaptation.

So right now, he had no idea anyone was cursing him.

Of course, after waking up, if he carefully sensed things, he would know someone had cursed him.

Mo Zhong stood by with a look of dissatisfaction on his face: "When did these guys become so... refined?"

He was dissatisfied with these fellow cultivators from Ghost Domination Heaven.

Their curses at Blood Robe were too gentle.

Saying things like he's a coward or afraid of death didn't really cut to the soul.

Of course, Mo Zhong understood that these geniuses were being cautious, not wanting to be the first to die.

After all, Blood Robe had killed Mo Na.

If he was hiding in the shadows, whoever cursed too viciously might be suddenly attacked by Blood Robe and end up dead.

So they were all cursing, but not too harshly.

This made Mo Zhong dissatisfied.

"Fang Xue, you go curse Blood Robe. Be steady but bold. I'll protect you from the side," Mo Zhong said.

These geniuses still cared about face.

Even if they cursed boldly, they wouldn't be too vicious.

At most, they would use sarcastic language that was very easy to provoke people.

"Alright." Fang Xue held the Ten Thousand Souls Banner, his expression calm. He scanned the surroundings and finally shouted loudly towards the southwest, like a thunderclap.

"Blood Robe, I f**k your mother!"

The sound rolled out, spreading in all directions.

All the geniuses in Flower Moon Wonderland heard this voice like a dust storm at that moment, and involuntarily looked towards Fang Xue.

This guy... he really dared to curse!

Even Mo Zhong hadn't expected Fang Xue to be so direct and simple.

It should be known that they were cultivators. Although killing was commonplace for them, when it came to cursing... they still had to maintain an immortal bearing.

Fang Xue's way of cursing was particularly rare.

"Ahem, it's fine. I'm protecting you. Let's see if Blood Robe can endure this!"

Mo Zhong waved his gold-threaded robe, standing majestic as a giant statue next to Fang Xue.

On the other side, inside the formation.

Qi Yuan, who was originally drowsy, suddenly opened his eyes.

"Damn, I just took a nap and lost my mother?"

"No, I never had a mother to begin with!"

"Don't hit the face when fighting, don't curse the mother when insulting. This is too much!"

Qi Yuan was very angry.

He hadn't been this angry for a long time.

He had always been very moral.

When others cursed one of his family members, he would return the favor and curse their entire family.

Isn't that generous, loyal, and kind enough? But the result was that kindness invites bullying, just as a gentle horse gets ridden.

"It seems I can't sleep in anymore..."

Without any hesitation, the lazy aura around Qi Yuan dissipated.

"Great plan, small plan, proceed simultaneously!"

Meanwhile, after Fang Xue finished cursing, over a dozen geniuses gathered around him, surrounding and protecting him.

In the distance, many more geniuses were watching this area.

They were sensing their surroundings while remaining wary.

"This Blood Robe is indeed a coward. He's been cursed like this and still doesn't dare to come out!"

"Hmph, with so many of us guarding Fang Xue, if he dares to appear, he's just seeking death!"

"Even the Supreme Venerable in his youth probably couldn't defeat us!" one genius said proudly.

In their view, although the Supreme Venerable was stronger than them and had gone further, he might not have been able to defeat them in his youth.

"This Blood Robe can really keep his cool. He's a tough one!" Mo Zhong said solemnly.

Being able to stay calm is necessary to achieve great things.

True geniuses are those who can remain unperturbed even when mountains crumble before them.

However, just as he finished speaking, an extremely sharp sword light that pierced people's hearts appeared at that moment.

"Shrimp-head boy, you don't have a mother?"

"Could it be that your mother is just your biological mother?"

"Can't you show some respect?"

Three consecutive voices came faster than the sword light.

All the geniuses who saw this scene were shocked.

"Blood Robe has appeared!"

"What a powerful sword!"

"Kill!"

Without any hesitation, over thirty geniuses rushed madly towards Blood Robe to kill him.

"Where do you think you're going, Blood Robe!"

"Leave the egg behind!"

Countless Dao techniques and immortal spells exploded, endless and boundless.

The sound of great Dao lingered, and immortal fire fell.

With all these attacks launched simultaneously, even Mo Zhong could only turn and run.

However, Qi Yuan stood still without moving.

"Mere pseudo-gods dare to be so arrogant?"

Unable to even create worlds, yet calling themselves Yin gods! It was enough to make Qi Yuan laugh his teeth off!

Without any hesitation, his powerful sword light suddenly accelerated at this moment.

One could see a cold glint in the clouds of the nine heavens, making all evil retreat.

"Kill!"

Qi Yuan said softly.

With his strength, to kill a Fang Xue, there was no need to use supreme principles.

An ordinary sword thrust forward, and these geniuses couldn't understand this sword strike at all, nor could they resist it.

The powerful sword easily passed through dozens of geniuses and pierced straight into Fang Xue's throat.

This scene made Mo Zhong's face change drastically, and he piled on defenses to the extreme.

"It's not fair for all of you to gang up on me!"

"Leave the eggs behind!"

A grand chase scene unfolded within the Flower Moon Wonderland.

Qi Yuan was simply treating it like a game.

Otherwise, he could have easily slaughtered all these prodigies.

But he didn't, because his small plan was paving the way for the grand plan.

His target was never these prodigies, but rather... the Yang Gods!

Zi Yuanfeng stood outside the celestial curtain, his eyes fixed intently on the Flower Moon Wonderland.

"It's been so long, but finally the scent of blood is spreading. Blood Robe has finally made his move."

Although he didn't know exactly what was happening inside, he could judge based on the auras.

For a long time before, there had been no bloody aura inside, only various buzzing sounds.

This indicated that Blood Robe had been lying low.

This was normal - he didn't think Blood Robe could face dozens of prodigies head-on simultaneously.

Ambushing after lying low was a wise move.

Now, the bloody aura was finally spreading, and he could detect countless intense spiritual energy fluctuations.

Evidently, Blood Robe had made his move, killing one person and fleeing.

"I wonder how long it will take for this contest to end," Zi Yuanfeng sighed.

At the same time, his gaze also looked far into the distance.

A Yang God from Ghost Dominion was lurking there.

The two maintained a safe distance, with neither showing any intention of making a move.

When Yang Gods from both sides were mobilized, it would set off a chain reaction, so Zi Yuanfeng wasn't afraid the other side would send a Principle Yang God to deal with him.

If that happened, Yang Gods from Ghost Barrier would naturally come to his aid.

Just then, a sudden message transmission arrived, and Zi Yuanfeng's face lit up with joy.

"Ice Sword has emerged from seclusion?"

"A revised version of the 'Ancestral Blood Scripture' has appeared?"

"And it actually came from... Blood Robe's hand."

With the revised 'Ancestral Blood Scripture' in hand, Zi Yuanfeng examined it carefully.

The more he looked, the more he appreciated its profound mysteries.

"This Blood Robe is truly a genius. No wonder the ancestors value him so highly. If I had cultivated this Ancestral Blood Scripture earlier, I might have already reached the eleventh level by now!"

"This... is marvelous!"

"The only flaw is that cultivating it requires... divine molting."

It was easy to test whether there were any problems with the 'Ancestral Blood Scripture' - just see if their bloodline would resist or obstruct it.

If the bloodline didn't obstruct it, it meant this 'Ancestral Blood Scripture' had no issues.

The revised version of the 'Ancestral Blood Scripture' increased both power and cultivation speed by nearly twofold.

How could he not be tempted?

"I'm not doing anything else right now anyway... why not cultivate it a bit?"

"There are so many divine moltings, who would care about me?" Zi Yuanfeng pondered.

Suddenly, something occurred to him and his expression changed slightly.

"The ancestors value Blood Robe so highly - could it be that... they already knew Blood Robe had this 'Ancestral Blood Scripture'?"

Zi Yuanfeng suddenly felt he might have discovered the truth of the matter.

"Could it be... the ancestors have already... undergone divine molting?"

At this thought, his eyes lit up.

If even the ancestors could undergo divine molting, what was wrong with him doing it once?

Meanwhile, in Zi Yuan Heaven, Ancestor Zi Yuan looked at the technique in his hand, his expression changing repeatedly: "This guy... he had such an excellent technique and didn't offer it to me in advance!"

He felt somewhat displeased.

This Blood Robe was hiding it from him! He only found out about this benefit from others.

When others asked if he knew about this, he had to put on a brave face and say he'd known long ago and had already been cultivating it.

At the time, that Yang God Celestial Lord had given him a strange look, which had puzzled Zi Yuan Ancestor.

Now, with the complete technique in hand, he understood why the other had looked at him so oddly.

"Sigh, with Lin Ya's molting imminent, I must also increase my strength somewhat. Otherwise... I fear it will be difficult to protect Blood Robe as he grows."

"Laying an egg... is not such a big deal."

After some hesitation, Zi Yuan Ancestor also began cultivating this revised version of the 'Ancestral Blood Scripture'.

He didn't dare cultivate normally anymore, as it would trigger the descent of the Root Demon Tribulation.

But the improvements from cultivating this revised 'Ancestral Blood Scripture' were not just in realm, but also in bloodline.

Bloodline improvements would not give rise to the Root Demon Tribulation.

"What a pity. If I were a Supreme Lord, how would Ghost Dominion dare to assassinate Blood Robe?"

"How could I have needed to seek help from that old fellow? I would have attacked Ghost Dominion right then!"

Zi Yuan Ancestor sighed, then began his "humiliating" cultivation.
