He and the Fasting Goddess came to the nearby area, where all the shops were tightly closed and not open for business.

Faced with this situation, the Fasting Goddess directly entered and took the pots and pans.

Very domineering.

Once the Fasting Goddess's body was better, he could continue his visualization.

Why didn't she eat normally?

Now he could see what was really going on.

Qi Yuan sat to the side, enthusiastically directing: "The kitchen knife was bought for nothing, probably just going to collect dust."

"Hmm, scrape more carefully, there's still half a scale left."

"Cooking requires color, aroma and taste. Your fish pieces are cut unevenly, your knife skills are lacking."

Qi Yuan transformed into a commander, frantically giving instructions.

The Fasting Goddess was now like an ordinary mortal woman, struggling to complete a plate of braised fish under Qi Yuan's command.

"Not bad, for a first attempt this is already very good," Qi Yuan couldn't help praising as he looked at the overcooked fish.

After all, he had criticized before, now it was time to praise.

Criticism and praise combined is the kingly way.

"Hurry and eat some, see if your body improves," Qi Yuan said.

He was also very curious.

The Heavenly Pillar would recover when the Fasting Goddess ate food - what principle was this based on?

The Fasting Goddess frowned, hesitating.

"Don't judge by appearances, it tastes quite good," Qi Yuan continued to persuade.

For cultivators, no matter how bad the fish tasted, they could still eat it.

This fish was made under his guidance after all, so it couldn't be that bad.

The Fasting Goddess looked at the plate of fish. Her slender fingers grasped the chopsticks as she picked up a piece of fish and brought it near her lips. Her brows were tightly furrowed, her eyes full of resistance.

"Don't worry, it's not poisonous. Eating it won't make you sick," Qi Yuan encouraged.

The Fasting Goddess gave Qi Yuan a deep look, then forcefully stuffed the fish into her mouth.

Her face suddenly turned pale, as if she had been gravely injured.

Qi Yuan was slightly stunned.

Her reaction was so strong? "Is it that bad? You don't have to eat it. It must be the pot. Let's change pots and cook another batch," Qi Yuan said anxiously, wanting to reach out and pat the Fasting Goddess on the back.

But his hand passed through her, unable to touch the Fasting Goddess.

After all, they were separated by time and space, unable to touch each other.

"It's fine." The Fasting Goddess's body trembled slightly, her brows furrowed frighteningly. She parted her red lips and forcefully swallowed the fish meat.

Her actions were as if she was swallowing poison, even more difficult than swallowing poison.

Qi Yuan's expression changed slightly.

He believed it now - the Fasting Goddess had an eating disorder, and not just an ordinary one.

Just eating some fish... and she became like this?

He was very puzzled, staring at the Fasting Goddess, asking with concern: "Are you alright?"

At this moment, the Fasting Goddess's form was swaying unsteadily. She in her red dress lay down on the bamboo mat, eyes tightly shut, beads of sweat forming on her forehead.

But she seemed very cold, her body occasionally trembling slightly.

The sudden change left Qi Yuan unable to understand.

This was a mythical being.

Just from eating a piece of fish?

She ended up like this? Back when the Fasting Goddess fought the avatar of the Yang God, she was covered in wounds but didn't tremble.

When the Great Flood battle began, he had seen the Fasting Goddess eat fish before.

That time, the Fasting Goddess was very calm.

What was going on?

"Are you alright... Use your divine arts to spit out the fish!" Qi Yuan looked at the Fasting Goddess lying on the ground, but unfortunately his eyes could not obtain any useful information about her condition.

Separated by time and space, he also could not help the Fasting Goddess.

At this moment, the Fasting Goddess was like she had fallen asleep, not responding to Qi Yuan.

About a hundred breaths later, the Fasting Goddess's voice came, as faint as a mosquito: "It's fine. Perhaps if I sleep for a while, everything will be alright, and the Heavenly Pillar will be fully repaired."

Qi Yuan looked at the Fasting Goddess, his heart anxious but helpless.

"What's really going on? Did you react like this before when eating? Why did you still eat this time?" Qi Yuan asked, utterly confused.

About ten breaths passed before the Fasting Goddess's weak voice came: "I don't know. Wait until tomorrow."

Hearing this, Qi Yuan didn't speak further, so the Fasting Goddess wouldn't have to respond.

He stood quietly by her side, scenes constantly replaying in his mind as he pondered something.

The Fasting Goddess's Way was that all things were impure, harmful if eaten.

Could it be that her constitution was special, unable to eat anything?

But no matter how special, one still needed to eat something.

Could it be... innate? Suddenly, Qi Yuan recalled Qi Qi once saying the Fasting Goddess was suspected to have an innately pure constitution.

This type of constitution was pure and flawless, like crystal. Eating mortal food would taint such a constitution.

The Fasting Goddess's reaction was even stronger than imagined.
