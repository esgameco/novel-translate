He could accept this situation easily.

Isn't this just like normally playing a game?

The Thorn Blood staff member fell silent.

Cai Dao's heart is just too big, isn't it? If someone told him that everything he's experienced... is actually predetermined, he would have a hard time accepting that result.

"Dingbo Prefecture."

He originally came to this world to complete a task, and he didn't have any real malice towards Cai Dao and the other woman.

"What happened in the end with the An'ze People's Uprising?" Ji Wuyun asked.

Compared to the mutiny, she was more concerned about the people's uprising.

She felt that the fault lay with those minor officials beforehand, and as long as the court punished those minor officials, this would be a good opportunity to win over the people's hearts.

If she were the Prefect, she would definitely seize this chance to rally the people.

"The result? Of course, the ringleaders of the uprising were beheaded and displayed, while the minor officials who carried out the bloody massacre at the docks went unscathed." The Thorn Blood staff member chuckled lightly.

"How could that be?" Ji Wuyun was perplexed, "It was the common people who were in the right!"

This was clearly a good opportunity to win over the people's hearts.

"If their hearts were satisfied today, wouldn't that only increase their mentality of 'resisting from below'? Using this to coerce the officials?

Today punishing the minor officials, tomorrow punishing the County Magistrate, the day after punishing the Prefect, the day after that... Could it be that they would even punish the Emperor?"

The Thorn Blood staff member's eyes held a mocking look.

He had been to many worlds to complete tasks, and was most familiar with those wearing the black official hats.

"So, the things I did, changing those special events, is that why... my Mandate has been raised?" Qi Yuan said.

It seems that secretly throwing that merchant's steward into the water to drown was considered an accident.

The authorities also didn't send anyone to trouble Wang An, so naturally there was no bloody massacre at the docks, and no An'ze People's Uprising.

"This world has many special events, if you originally bore the Mandate of Heaven, and changed the course of these special events, then your Mandate would become increasingly more profound." The Thorn Blood staff member reminded.

Having more Mandate doesn't necessarily mean it's a good thing.

"So... our fate is predetermined... following the original trajectory, even if I bite myself now, or... even commit suicide, it's all fated?" Ji Wuyun felt on the verge of collapse.

It's as if there's a big hand manipulating her fate, and she's just a marionette.

Even her current reaction might be predestined.

"Am I in the Blue Mountain Book?" She was now very eager to open the Blue Mountain Book.

She must not follow what's written in the Blue Mountain Book.

"Everything is already determined, except for some people with special mandates who can slightly change the established situation, the rest of you... are no different from puppets.

Oh, and the Blue Mountain Book doesn't record just any old person, probably... you don't have the qualifications to be in it, haha..."

The Thorn Blood staff member couldn't help but laugh.

Ji Wuyun fell silent.

"If you want to leave a trace of your existence... why not come to the Divine Wood Abyss? Although I can't record your name in the Blue Mountain Book, I'm confident I can add your names to another book." The Thorn Blood staff member tempted.

Ji Wuyun remained silent.

"What's the use of being recorded in a book? Hey, if you give me a hundred thousand taels of silver, I'll remember you for the rest of my life." Qi Yuan spoke up at this point, "That's much more useful than writing in a book."

The Thorn Blood staff member laughed: "Even after countless ages and eras, the Blue Mountain Book will still exist. Brother Cai Dao, even if you are Cai Dao, you'll probably have long rusted and decayed."

Ji Wuyun also felt that Qi Yuan was just talking nonsense.

Being remembered by someone... what's the use of that?

"So... the background setting of this game, even the ending, has already been written?"

Suddenly, he remembered something.

"The Six Heavens of the Immortal Realm have no future, it was severed by a single sword.

Then... if the Immortal Realm hasn't had its future severed?"

A terrifying idea and speculation suddenly surged in Qi Yuan's mind.

A future that can be seen at a glance... is it still the future?

"Stop overthinking, or your brain will grow bigger."

"Nine-tenths of the troubles in this world stem from overthinking."

"I'm just playing a game, that's all."

"This is just a game."

Before his eyes went blind, he had to quickly improve his Armament Master level.

After going blind, it would be very difficult to make progress.

After an unknown amount of time, Qi Yuan suddenly opened his eyes, a hint of joy in his deep gaze: "I finally know the exact location of the Blood Cloud Bandits' headquarters."

"Looks like I'll have to go take a look."
