The remaining Purple Mansion cultivators of the Palace of Light would definitely contract and return to the Palace of Light headquarters.

This was Qi Yuan's opportunity.

If they weren't gathered together, to be honest... it would be quite difficult to kill them.

After all, if a Purple Mansion cultivator wanted to hide, it wouldn't be easy for Qi Yuan to find them.

Hearing Qi Yuan's words, Xu Yeli coughed: "I'll await senior's good news then."

A plan to destroy the Palace of Light? Xu Yeli naturally didn't believe it.

Without Yin Spirits appearing, who could destroy the Palace of Light? He was worried and felt the future was bleak.

Qi Yuan of course didn't know Xu Yeli's thoughts.

Even if he knew, it wouldn't matter to him.

After killing five old and charming moonlight beauties, Qi Yuan had reaped a full harvest this time.

The only regret was that he couldn't tag them.

Not taking credit, but deepening the theme, going from small to big, it was the ending of a perfect score high school exam essay.

After posting, Qi Yuan was very pleased with himself.

The big shot killed five more Purple Mansion cultivators? Why did the big shot mention my name?

Destroying the Palace of Light, you all played a part!"

He had to protect his die-hard fans.

Fox Den Little Li was completely at a loss, she wanted to play dead.

Time flew by, and more and more people replied.

"Senior... is truly strong, your strength is probably in the top 20 of the Eastern Lands."

"No wonder senior is so carefree, you don't take the Palace of Light seriously at all."

"Senior, as long as you don't directly confront the Palace of Light, they can't do anything to you!"

Especially that excellent plan of having children and usurping the nest.

And it also sounded very exciting.

But then he remembered that Ice Mountain Tyrant Lady and junior sister were still borrowing high-interest loans from the Palace of Light.

He had to ignore these brilliant strategies.

The Palace of Light must be destroyed within 100 days!

He wasn't a boss, nor was he a client.

Spirit Girl had sent him a message.
