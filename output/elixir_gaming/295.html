It was as if being stared at by a terrifying creature.

Goosebumps appeared on her delicate, snow-white skin.

She looked at Qi Yuan, breathing slightly rapidly: "What's so special?"

"Your... blood." Qi Yuan looked at Wu Qi, her skin was snow-white, and he could even see the blue veins, with blood flowing slowly through the blood vessels.

Wu Qi was stunned.

"My blood?"

She didn't quite understand, and even thought Qi Yuan was a nobleman with strange fetishes.

"To me, the most special thing about you is your blood." Qi Yuan shrugged, "Why don't you give me some blood, and I'll grant you some small wishes.

Oh... menstrual blood won't do!"

Qi Yuan added at the end of his speech.

If Wu Qi tried to cut corners and directly brought menstrual blood, that would be too much.

Qi Yuan never underestimated the "base nature" of humans.

Hearing Qi Yuan's words, Wu Qi, who had always thought herself open-minded and able to accept anything, couldn't help but blush to her ears.

She lowered her head, not daring to look directly into Qi Yuan's eyes.

Qi Yuan's words had too great an impact on her.

Now, her mind was in chaos, imagining various scenarios.

Could it be... that Mr. Qi was a... monster who loved to drink young girls' fresh blood?

"How's your consideration going?" Qi Yuan smiled lightly.

Wu Qi's heart was in turmoil, but finally she gritted her teeth, stretched her head towards Qi Yuan, and pulled down her clothing a bit, revealing her snow-white neck.

"Mr. Qi... take my blood!"

She faced death unflinchingly, as if resigned to her fate.

The path she had chosen, she had to walk it to the end.

Compared to losing some blood, she wanted more to become someone above others.

The opportunity was right in front of her, she didn't want to let it go.

"Although I'm omnivorous, I don't drink human blood. Drinking human blood... is illegal.

I never do anything illegal or against regulations.

Take your neck back, I'll find you when I need blood in the future."

His strength was still too weak now.

Although he had discovered that Wu Qi's blood was somewhat special, even if she gave it to him now, he would need to consume considerable energy to research why it was special.

For him now, this was not a key matter.

So, it was merely leaving a foreshadowing.

For him now, improving his strength was the most crucial thing.

When his strength was strong enough, he could see at a glance why it was special.

Hearing Qi Yuan's words, Wu Qi felt relieved.

"My blood will always be reserved for you, sir!" Wu Qi said seriously.

"I live in the Jun'an Residential Area, you drive."

"Jun'an Residential Area?"

She set Jun'an Residential Area as the destination.

When she saw the general location of Jun'an Residential Area, Wu Qi's heart skipped a beat.

In high school, she had heard from classmates that Qi Yuan was an orphan of a martyr.

However, after seeing Qi Yuan on the cruise ship, she had guessed that it was Qi Yuan deliberately hiding his identity to attend school.

But now, hearing that Qi Yuan lived in Jun'an Residential Area, many thoughts came to her mind.

How could such a big shot, someone even hereditary viscounts tried hard to please, live in such an ordinary neighborhood?

Could it be that there was some hidden story?

"Yes, Jun'an Residential Area." Qi Yuan closed his eyes slightly, not saying anything more.

Seeing this, Wu Qi felt confused and puzzled in her heart.

She really wanted to ask why Qi Yuan would live in such a place.

But even though she was confused, she didn't dare to ask.

After all, if she accidentally offended Qi Yuan, she wouldn't even know how she would die.

In Qinyuan Country, it wasn't a big deal for a nobleman to kill a celebrity.

Qinyuan Country, this ancient empire, had clear distinctions between upper and lower classes.

Wu Qi's heart was in turmoil as she drove her car towards Jun'an Residential Area.

As night was about to fall, and various colorful lights illuminated this modern city, the car finally arrived.

Unlike the bright daylight on the road, as night approached, not all the street lights near Jun'an Residential Area were lit.

It seemed like a completely different place compared to where Wu Qi lived.

Looking at this ordinary neighborhood, her confusion grew even stronger. This neighborhood had nothing to do with rich areas.

It was even inferior to her own neighborhood.

At this moment, Qi Yuan stretched lazily, looking at the familiar neighborhood, he smiled.

"We're here."

Qi Yuan got out of the car, and Wu Qi hurriedly followed, seeming to want to say something but hesitating.

"Thank you for driving me, it must have been troublesome," Qi Yuan said politely.

Wu Qi quickly replied, "It's no trouble."

"Here's... 27 yuan for the fare."

Qi Yuan said, taking out twenty-seven yuan from his pocket, in both bills and coins.

Wu Qi was a bit stunned.

Fare?

"Take it quickly, I'm not a freeloader," Qi Yuan always abided by the law, "And don't post about me online, saying I'm stingy. When I went to the martial arts gym, the fare was 26, and I added an extra yuan!"

Qi Yuan held out the money to Wu Qi.

He gave cash, not a transfer.

After all, he was afraid the police uncles might misunderstand.

Wu Qi took the money with a dazed expression, unable to understand Qi Yuan's actions.

Is this the eccentricity of a big shot?

"I don't have a cat that can cook at home, so I won't invite you in. Bye-bye."

Qi Yuan was very polite, but also very direct in leaving, leaving only a bewildered Wu Qi in the wind.

She looked at Qi Yuan, her inner emotions extremely complex.

"Does he really live here?"

She felt uneasy in her heart.

After all, her manager had told her this morning that maybe that Qi Yuan was just... an ordinary person who had helped Viscount Kuanghuan with something, which was why Viscount Kuanghuan valued him.

...

"Mr. Qi, Zhao Shiqi... asked me to... say he hopes to meet you and apologize."

On the phone, Kuanghuan's voice was a bit nervous and joyful.

Yesterday, he and Qi Yuan had been attacked by a sniper.

When he learned it was the work of Earl Heijing, his only thought was to take Qi Yuan and leave, to avoid this storm.

But unexpectedly... Earl Heijing Zhao Renji died suddenly that very day.

If this wasn't Qi Yuan's doing, Kuanghuan wouldn't believe it.

This also made Kuanghuan understand that Qi Yuan was truly a cultivator, able to kill invisibly.

Earl Heijing Zhao Renji had so many guards around him, but was still killed by Qi Yuan.

"Who is he? I don't know him." Qi Yuan had never even heard this name before.

"He is Zhao Renji's younger brother, and now... the head of the Zhao family."

After Zhao Renji's death, the power shifted.

Zhao Shiqi wasn't useless either. Although he had his own persistence and beliefs, the power of the Zhao family naturally wouldn't fall into others' hands.

In just one day, he had completely taken control of the Zhao family.

"Oh, him. I'm too lazy to meet. I've already killed the people I wanted to kill, what's the point of meeting him? Does he want to avenge his brother?

Tell him I have a certificate of mental illness, I'm not afraid of him." Qi Yuan replied.

Hearing these words, Kuanghuan felt both delighted and shocked.

Indeed, Zhao Renji was killed by Qi Yuan.

Fortunately, he hadn't been reluctant to pay that 100 million and double-cross Qi Yuan back then.

If Qi Yuan could easily kill Zhao Renji, killing him would be just as easy.

"I will convey Mr. Qi's words to Zhao Shiqi exactly.

By the way, Mr. Qi, I'm afraid Zhao Shiqi doesn't know yet... that Zhao Renji was killed by you. Should I... conceal this fact?" Kuanghuan paused on the phone.

"I'm not pretending to be weak to catch the strong, why should I conceal it?

Moreover, I have a certificate of mental illness, what am I afraid of?"

Qi Yuan said casually.

With no parents, no children, and a certificate of mental illness, Qi Yuan was already an invincible person in this era.

"Alright." Kuanghuan agreed on the phone.

Qi Yuan hung up the phone and continued to cultivate.
