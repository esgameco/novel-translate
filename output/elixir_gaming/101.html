"You can talk... Wow, you're a monster!"

The canary flapped its wings, flying three meters high, its small body trembling, looking extremely frightened.

Qi Yuan was speechless when he heard this: "I'm human, a genuine human.

"This young lady can speak bird language!"

"Biubiubiubiu... Jijijiji..."

The canary's voice sounded like a machine gun, or like an electric bike alarm, crisp and clear yet piercing.

"This young lady used bird language, so this young lady is not a monster. Your turn now, you're a drop of blood, say something in blood language, otherwise you're the monster?" the canary questioned.

Her conversation with Qi Yuan now lacked any logic, just like a foolish internet commenter.

How could blood talk?

Qi Yuan was dumbfounded.

But he couldn't let himself be outdone by a bird.

"Dinosaur... carry wolf... blood blood blood..."

Qi Yuan instantly made something up.

The canary's little eyes widened, eyeballs rolling around: "You're lying to me, where have you heard blood talk like that?"

"You're not blood, how would you know how blood talks?" Qi Yuan felt his intelligence had gained the upper hand against the canary, directly applying the allusion of 'You are not a fish, how do you know the joy of fish?'

Sure enough, the canary was stunned by Qi Yuan.

"Neither of us are monsters?" It cautiously flew over, landing near Qi Yuan.

Qi Yuan was stunned for a moment, realizing he had been drawn in.

Whether one could speak bird language or blood language had nothing to do with being a monster or not.

"You're a monster, I'm not." Qi Yuan emphasized.

The canary landed beside Qi Yuan: "Even if this young lady is a monster, she's a very beautiful monster, while you're an ugly freak!"

Hearing this, Qi Yuan couldn't tolerate it: "If I were to transform, I would also be handsome and dignified."

The two argued like children for a while, gradually becoming familiar with each other.

Qi Yuan discovered that this canary's intelligence wasn't very high, it was rather silly, and it was especially vain, or rather, loved beauty.

Qi Yuan felt that this canary had dragged him down to its level of intelligence.

He stopped arguing about meaningless things and asked: "Where is this place?"

"This is Mount Jiupan."

"Where is Mount Jiupan?"

"It's in Mount Jiupan, of course?" The canary gave him a look as if he were an idiot.

Qi Yuan was speechless, thinking "Why don't you just say it's on Second Immortal Bridge or Chenghua Avenue?"

But he understood that he wouldn't get any useful information from this canary.

"Do you know about the South-North Confrontation?" Qi Yuan asked again.

Anyone from the Liufeng Realm would generally know about the South-North Confrontation.

The canary shook its head.

"Then... do you know about the Demon Scourge?" Perhaps this wasn't the Liufeng Realm after all.

The canary's little eyes widened, showing a fearful expression: "You mean those ugly things? There are many, many of them at the foot of the mountain!"

Qi Yuan heard this.

He looked at the starless sky above and roughly understood that this should still be the Liufeng Realm.

"Those ugly things are crazy and deranged. When they see us, they come to attack us."

"Do you know what that door in the sky is?" Qi Yuan asked again.

The door in the sky was mysterious and ancient.

When he was in the Liufeng Realm before, he had never seen such a door in the sky.

This also meant that the place he was currently in...

"I don't know," the canary shook its head.

Qi Yuan didn't want to talk anymore, as he couldn't get any useful information out of this silly canary anyway.

He looked at the Door of Myriad Distinctions in the sky, slowly contemplating.

The canary beside him glanced at Qi Yuan, then returned to its own nest.

The small nest, hanging on a big tree, looked quite delicate and exquisite.

Back in its nest, the canary groomed its smooth feathers.

It spent half a day just grooming.

After some time, it seemed to sense something and poked its little head out: "Blood bead, what are you doing? What's so interesting about that door?"

"I'm comprehending the origin," Qi Yuan answered the canary.

His comprehension of the origin wasn't like in TV shows where one needs to be fully immersed and can't be disturbed.

He just needed to look at it with his eyes.

Anyway, fragments would fall.

[You have comprehended the Door of Myriad Distinctions, Back Scratching Ability +1.]

[You have comprehended the Door of Myriad Distinctions, Mutation Ability +1.]

[You have comprehended the Door of Myriad Distinctions, Mental Disorder +1.]

Anyway, all sorts of strange comprehensions.

The more Qi Yuan comprehended, the better his mental state became.

"Comprehending the origin, what's that?" The canary looked up at the Door of Myriad Distinctions in the sky, its eyes showing a hint of instinctive fear, "Can it make one more beautiful?"

"Probably... it's possible," Qi Yuan answered honestly.

After all, the things comprehended were all sorts of strange abilities, so becoming more beautiful was quite normal.

Hearing this, the canary became excited: "I want to comprehend too!"

It imitated Qi Yuan, staring blankly at the Door of Myriad Distinctions in the sky.

As he comprehended, Qi Yuan's form gradually grew larger.

Over the past month, what he had comprehended the most was the Mutation Ability.

He had a full ten of them now.

As for other bizarre abilities, they were just fleeting glimpses, not forming any system.

A month later, a sudden downpour swept over Mount Jiupan. The wind blew through the trees, the whole world was drenched, but Qi Yuan still stared at the Door of Myriad Distinctions in the sky through the rain.

Amidst the thunder and lightning.

[You have comprehended the Door of Myriad Distinctions, slight increase in combat power during thunderstorms.]

"This is possible too?" This was the first time in a month that Qi Yuan had comprehended an increase in combat power.

However, the cold rain hitting his face made him quite uncomfortable.

At this moment, a banana leaf covered his head, shielding him from the rain.

The canary's voice came: "Blood bead, it's raining heavily."

Qi Yuan looked at it, its feathers were half wet, and it was shivering: "I'm not afraid of rain, you should go back to your nest. Your feathers are getting wet, they won't be beautiful anymore."

"Hmph!" The canary held the banana leaf, "We're neighbors, this young lady saw you had no hands or feet and felt sorry for you, so I'm covering you... Achoo!"

"Can canaries sneeze too?" This was the first time Qi Yuan had seen this.

The canary was wet and a bit cold, it turned its head away, not wanting to look at Qi Yuan.

Qi Yuan said softly: "Fire."

Instantly, a weak flame rose up beside them.

Seeing this, the canary's eyes rolled around: "You know magic?"

"It's a small ability gained from comprehending the Door of Myriad Distinctions, but it's unstable."

This kind of ability was different from Qi Yuan's fireball technique in the Canglan Realm.

The fireball technique could be used in the Canglan Realm, but when operated in the game, it would have no effect.

Qi Yuan had a feeling that the abilities gained through comprehending the Door of Myriad Distinctions were interconnected, not only usable in the Liufeng Realm but also in the Canglan Realm.

This was somewhat similar to his game skills.

Although these abilities seemed useless, Qi Yuan felt their potential was extremely great.

"Wow, blood bead, you're so powerful. Why is it that when I comprehend the Door of Myriad Distinctions, I don't comprehend anything, and my eyes even become a bit blurry?" The canary moved closer to the flame, feeling lazy, some of the cold in its body dispersing.

"You're not becoming nearsighted, are you? How can you become nearsighted looking at the sky? Maybe you should stop comprehending."

"No, I want to become beautiful!" The canary said stubbornly. Suddenly, its eyes rolled, "Do you smell something strange, like something burning?"

Qi Yuan glanced over, the canary's tail had been set on fire by his flame: "...Your tail is on fire."

"Ah... uh... My feathers are on fire!"

"Blood bead, this young lady isn't done with you!"

The banana leaf was lifted, rain poured onto the canary's feathers, and the fire finally went out.

There were still scorch marks on its tail.

How could it, who loved beauty the most, accept this result? It glared angrily at Qi Yuan.

"This young lady will never speak to you again for the rest of her life."

It was like an angry child, running back to its own house.

It looked at its own tail, its expression pitiful and aggrieved.

It muttered: "Blood bead, if you don't take the initiative to talk to me, this young lady won't pay attention to you."

The canary looked at the heavy rain outside and decided to sleep in its nest.

It seemed that only sleep could alleviate the pain in its heart at this moment.

At night, a banana leaf fell above Qi Yuan, blocking most of the rain.

Qi Yuan glanced at that specially shaped bird's nest that could block wind and rain.

He saw the flustered canary, who seemed to realize it had been discovered and glared angrily at Qi Yuan.

"What are you looking at? If you don't apologize, this young lady won't forgive you."

In the following days, the canary indeed didn't chat with Qi Yuan.

It seemed that having its feathers burned had truly angered the naturally beauty-loving bird.

Qi Yuan continued to comprehend the Door of Myriad Distinctions, and his Mutation Ability kept increasing.

A full year passed, and Qi Yuan's Mutation Ability had nearly reached one hundred.

As for other bizarre abilities, there was a whole basket of them, all fleeting.

"Hey, blood bead, when are you going to take the initiative to apologize to this young lady!"
