In his mind, sword intent crisscrossed, slaughtering the myriad Dao, or perhaps... forgetting the myriad Dao.

Qi Yuan stepped on the black sea and arrived at the place of origin.

At this time, the shore of the black sea was teeming with people.

Nearly ten thousand Martial Lords stood there, as if they had long known Qi Yuan would come.

"We pay our respects to the Heavenly Venerable!"

These Martial Lords bowed, their expressions respectful.

Seeing this scene, Qi Yuan's expression remained calm: "What's this? Have you all come to your senses now?"

As the words fell, Qi Yuan thought to himself.

How should these people come to their senses?

Blossoming... perhaps they could only blossom with blood.

"Heavenly Venerable, everything before was a misunderstanding." The Black Deer Martial Lord stepped forward, his expression respectful. "Now, the Eastern Lord is waiting for you at the place of calamity.

The Eastern Lord said he will explain all the misunderstandings to you!"

Hearing this, Qi Yuan's expression did not change.

His expression was either calm or with a smile at the corner of his mouth, making it impossible to tell what he was thinking.

"This guy, he's finally willing to see me."

Qi Yuan stretched lazily.

"Since he's willing to see me, then I'll go meet him."

The realm of Myriad Dao Martial God is the realm of Yang Spirit.

However, this Myriad Dao Martial God is somewhat different from Yang Spirit.

In the Yang Spirit realm, going to other worlds or even universes would result in a downgrade.

The Myriad Dao Martial God seems to be unaffected by this limitation.

After breaking through to the Myriad Dao Martial God realm, Qi Yuan's strength had indeed increased significantly.

At this time, Elder God Thunder appeared: "This old man will go with you."

The other divine infants also spoke up one after another: "We shall go together."

They were also extremely wary of the Eastern Lord.

They were not at ease letting Qi Yuan face danger alone.

Qi Yuan looked at the place of origin, his expression calm: "Perhaps the Dawn's Call is about to begin, and this place of origin seems unable to sustain Yang Spirits. If you stay here... you'll be forced to leave anyway.

Since fate has arranged for you to leave... well, although I don't particularly like fate, today we'll follow what fate says. You and I will go together to meet the Eastern Lord."

In an instant, countless shadowy figures appeared behind Qi Yuan.

"We obey your command!"

Each shadowy figure was noble beyond words, mysterious and powerful.

Like chaos demon gods, they stood behind Qi Yuan. Even though they were suppressing their auras with all their might, people still dared not look at them directly.

Those Martial Lords all felt an urge to prostrate themselves in worship.

Some of them even gave the feeling of being more majestic and terrifying than the Eastern Lord.

At this moment, those who were originally unwavering began to feel worried in their hearts.

But soon, their gazes became firm again.

The Eastern Lord was the ultimate truth of this world.

Numbers... were useless.

"Farewell everyone, I hope we'll have the chance to meet again."

Qi Yuan's figure disappeared.

Those terrifying, majestic demon god-like shadows also vanished at this moment.

The Black Deer Martial Lord gritted his teeth, cold sweat breaking out on his forehead. Only after those figures had completely disappeared did he let out a sigh of relief.

The other Martial Lords present were the same.

The pressure Qi Yuan and those terrifying figures gave them was too great.

Especially since those terrifying figures were far more numerous than they had imagined.

They had thought there would only be a few, but the result was... thousands?

"The Eastern Lord should be able to deal with them, right?" a female Martial Lord asked anxiously.

Just now, those Heavenly Venerables had given them too much pressure.

"What do you mean 'deal with'? The Eastern Lord will tell them the truth, and they will trust the Eastern Lord, just like us!"

"Right, with the support of so many strong experts over the years, the Eastern Lord will surely resolve the calamity and successfully save the world."

"We can't just sit and wait either. Let's go resurrect his dao companion as the Eastern Lord entrusted us to do!"

"Right!"

"Now that the worlds are converging unstoppably, we will also descend to the place of ending.

We should choose a protagonist and have her kill all the girls of destiny, merge all the memories, and then fuse in... She will be the Eastern Lord's dao companion! If she is resurrected and goes to the place of calamity, everything will be fine.

We too will welcome true rebirth!"

"Haha, they're gone, no one can stop us now!"

Several titled Martial Lords were excited, their expressions twisted.

After all, they had restarted several eras and failed each time.

This time, they finally saw the dawn of success, how could they not be excited?

At this moment, suddenly, a different voice spoke up: "Seniors, if the Scroll of Mountains and Seas converges, won't everyone kill each other, leaving only the One True?"

The speaker was a male.

This man was a peak Martial King who had come from a stronghold.

In the Scroll of Mountains and Seas, he had pledged his love to a prime minister's daughter and even taught her martial arts.

In the past, he had looked forward to reuniting with his lover.

But now it seemed that the merging of worlds... would be a massacre?

"Mm, in each Scroll of Mountains and Seas, there is an avatar of the Eastern Lord's dao companion.

Each avatar is experiencing past events.

The Scroll of Mountains and Seas was originally illusory. The Eastern Lord used the Brush of Mountains, Rivers and Land to draw out the illusion and turn it into the creation of one truth.

We will support one avatar, letting her kill and devour the other avatars.

Finally, she will devour destiny and become the Eastern Lord's dao companion.

The Scroll of Mountains and Seas she is in will represent the truth, the future of our place of origin.

The other Scrolls of Mountains and Seas will become illusory, and the place of ending will become the past.

Without an ending, the Scroll of Mountains and Seas created by the Eastern Lord will be the true future, an infinite future!"

The Black Deer Martial Lord's face showed a fanatical expression.

After so many years, after the world had been restarted time and time again, they were finally about to succeed.

"But this way... won't everyone in the other Scrolls of Mountains and Seas die?" The man's face turned bitter. His lover was still in one of the Scrolls of Mountains and Seas.

If his lover was in one of the Scrolls to be sacrificed, he couldn't accept it.

"For the sake of the world, some sacrifices are necessary!" the Black Deer Martial Lord replied.

A titled Martial Lord also spoke up: "They were created by the Eastern Lord in the first place. Their purpose from birth was to be sacrificed.

Now, it's time for them to sacrifice themselves!"

"That's right, the Eastern Lord created them, a tremendous kindness they could never repay in a thousand lifetimes!"

The gazes of these Martial Lords were firm.

After all, having been firm for so many years, even if they truly realized their mistake, they wouldn't admit it.

"The Scroll of Mountains and Seas was indeed created by the Eastern Lord, so there is gratitude owed, but the place of ending... how innocent are they?" At this point, Mu Yan finally couldn't help but speak up.

"They were doomed to perish anyway, we're just helping them along!" At this moment, Mu Yan's bloodline ancestor spoke up. He looked at Mu Yan and said softly, "You newly ascended Martial Lords have experienced too few years and haven't seen the Eastern Lord's true face, so it's normal to have such misunderstandings.

For this task of the Eastern Lord, you should just stay and guard the place of origin."

As this Martial Lord spoke, he immediately took action.

Many Martial Lords present also took action, restraining those newly ascended Martial Lords.

"No, ancestor, what you're doing is too insane! Perhaps there's no need to destroy these worlds at all. The Heavenly Venerable Qi Yuan might be able to stop the great calamity!" Mu Yan shouted hoarsely, his voice filled with anguish.

Although he considered himself selfish and self-interested, he was unwilling to see the death of those he knew or was close to.

In the Scroll of Mountains and Seas, he had lived for tens of thousands of years.

He had also made many friends.

He really didn't want to see them die.

Most importantly, he had never seen the Eastern Lord and didn't trust the Eastern Lord's plan at all.
