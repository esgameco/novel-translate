"Will we... meet again in the future?" Wu Shi curled up, her suppressed desire bursting forth.

Every moment she spent with that man was difficult for her. The urge to devour him was hard to control.

She had to restrain her true nature. She didn't dare show it, because... she was too weak.

Fortunately... he left.

If he had stayed one more day, she didn't know what she might have done.

"When we meet again... can I eat him?" The young girl thought to herself.

"What a boring, tedious journey."

Qi Yuan swept his gaze across the vast land and couldn't help but sigh.

This time, he had seen the Fasting Saint Mother again, but as a young child.

He had originally thought he might discover some hidden historical secrets or shocking ancient legends.

But it was all so ordinary - just watching a little kid catch and eat fish. Nothing particularly special seemed to happen.

He and the Fasting Saint Mother didn't have any deep grudges or entanglements either.

It was like watching a movie.

"Visualize!"

At this point, Qi Yuan stopped thinking and directly entered the realm of visualization.

This time, his evil god spiritual power was very abundant.

This time, he wanted to visualize many people.

A group of big shots, all visualized at once.

"Of the Six Honored Ones, I've only visualized one so far. The other five need to appear too.

Li Tianwang who holds the pagoda, Yang Jian, the Great Sage Equaling Heaven, the Fire God Zhurong, the Ancestral Shamans, Yi... even the Three Pure Ones, and even Pangu!"

Qi Yuan's memory contained many, many deities.

Many of the deities Qi Yuan had seen in his previous life on the Blue Star were mistaken records.

However, even so, the deities he visualized were identical to those in his memory.

This actually surprised Qi Yuan quite a bit, and he couldn't explain it.

If forced to explain, there were two possible explanations.

The first was that these deities really existed, and even if the visualization was inaccurate, it wouldn't affect the deity visualization technique.

The second was that the primordial world could create infinite possibilities, and these deities existed because of Qi Yuan's visualization.

Regardless of the explanation, it didn't affect Qi Yuan's plans.

So he couldn't be bothered to dwell on it.

"Often the things that seem most ridiculous and made up are actually reality.

Because reality doesn't follow logic, but making up stories requires logic."

Qi Yuan's gaze grew solemn.

The first one he wanted to visualize was the King of the Underworld, the Earth Deity who inherited Heaven's ways and emulated profound virtue!

He had already planned out Qi Qi's path - first attaining true divinity through the Phoenix, then reaching Great Veneration through Houtu.

After all, his goddaughter deserved some good things, didn't she?

As for the Thunder Deity, Qi Yuan also planned to visualize the South Pole Longevity Emperor, one of the Six Honored Ones.

The Heavenly Worthy of Universal Transformation of Thunder of the Nine Heavens was originally an incarnation of the South Pole Longevity Emperor.

So he needed to give the Thunder Deity another boost.

Qi Yuan also had his own selfish motives.

For most of the followers on his mission, he would naturally bestow deity visualization techniques suitable for them.

This way he could maximize benefits.

But for those he was close and familiar with, he would naturally give a bit more.

If a person had no selfish motives, would they still be human? With a mix of selfishness, Qi Yuan began to visualize.

"Master of yin and yang, ruler of all things, governing the nine regions, mother of the great earth!"

In Qi Yuan's mind appeared that divine woman.

She was mythical, seated in the netherworld. In all myths and legends, she had a mysterious aura.

She was even more noble than the Heavenly Worthy of Universal Transformation of Thunder of the Nine Heavens.

A deity of this level could perhaps crush a Yang spirit from the heavens back to the underworld with just a wisp of her aura.

This was Qi Yuan's first time visualizing a deity of this level.

"I hope... it's possible."

In his mind, countless memories and images rotated.

Just like the previous visualization, nothing appeared around Qi Yuan's body

Visualizing these indeed made Qi Yuan feel somewhat excited.

Even more outrageous than visualizing the Calabash Brothers last time.

Because he had visualized... the Gatling Gun Bodhisattva.
