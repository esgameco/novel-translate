"The Three Pure Ones are worthy of their name. Have I now reached the pinnacle of mythology?"

Qi Yuan pondered.

After visualizing the Three Pure Ones, his power had skyrocketed.

He no longer needed even a sword to kill the former military lords - a mere glance would suffice.

Even the Starving Holy Mother at her peak from his memories would be no match for him now.

Moreover, Qi Yuan had a feeling he could even destroy Blood Sun Saint's avatar in the mortal realm!

His power had increased not just tenfold or hundredfold.

He even felt he could be considered the number one mythical being he had ever encountered!

However... his cultivation had also stopped at the seventh step to heaven.

After seven steps, one could become a Yang Spirit.

But these seven steps seemed vast, with no path forward.

As others had said, he had stopped at mythology, unable to advance further.

A thoughtful look flashed through his calm eyes.

"Godfather, the emissary of the Evil God has arrived."

The speaker was Qi Qi. She now wore a brown dress, her aura becoming more profound and increasingly cold.

She was somewhat different from her previously mischievous self.

The deity Qi Qi visualized was Houtu, one of the Six Honored Ones, supremely noble.

"Evil God's emissary?" Qi Yuan was surprised.

He had not expected the Evil God to send an emissary to see him.

Now, with war imminent, he would meet with them.

About a hundred breaths of time passed.

For some reason, upon seeing Qi Yuan, he suddenly felt an urge to prostrate himself in worship.

This shocked him greatly.

Though both were at the seventh step to heaven, why was the gap so large?

Especially in this remote Feiwu City, his divine power kept trembling involuntarily, as if terrifying divine demons were lurking in this small city.

At this moment, Qi Yuan, seated in the high position, glanced at Military Lord Wu Kui with a rueful expression: "It's a pity you all escaped last time, but it's fine, we were fated to meet again."

The last time Qi Yuan had swept away the Evil God from Qingheng Continent, he had prepared to wipe out the remaining military lords in one fell swoop.

But before he could act, those Evil Gods had fled, which had greatly disappointed Qi Yuan at the time.

Military Lord Wu Kui's heart inexplicably tightened, but remembering his master's instructions, he said solemnly: "Greetings to Origin Venerable. I come on my lord's orders to... negotiate peace with the Venerable!"
