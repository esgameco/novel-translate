"His name is Qi Yuan."

"Never heard of him," the old man shook his head. "But he's very bold, daring to scheme for even that strange land."

"He seems to have some connection to the Moon God Palace," the voice said vaguely.

"There is a member of the Holy Light Clan in that sacred land."

"What... the legendary Holy Light Clan!" As an Immortal sitting in the Nine Heavens, there were few things in this world that could disturb his emotions.

Because... the Holy Light Clan was a taboo, a taboo among Immortals.

"So it's the Holy Light Clan. It seems that Immortal's schemes will ultimately come to nothing."

The Holy Light Clan had an extraordinary origin, often appearing in strange lands.

This clan had an extremely powerful innate divine ability.

This ability was called the Words of Holy Light.

Whatever the Holy Light Clan said, those who heard it would believe it to be true.

Once, an Immortal of the Lesser Principle Realm entered a strange land and encountered a member of the Holy Light Clan.

That Holy Light Clan member said, "I am your father."

And that Lesser Principle Realm Immortal actually believed it to be true.

The Words of Holy Light were just that domineering.

Therefore, generally speaking, Immortals would avoid strange lands with Holy Light Clan members.

Otherwise, they were very likely to perish and become nourishment for the Holy Light Clan.

"The Holy Light Clan is too unbeatable. Unless one's realm is much higher, one will definitely be deceived.

Even if one's realm is much higher, there's still a chance of being deceived," the fishing old man sighed.

"The Words of Holy Light are comparable to the Principles of the Nine Heavens, simply irresistible," said another Immortal.

"I have some karmic connection with the Moon God Palace. I should pass on this information about the Holy Light Clan to them," the fishing old man said after some thought.

Perhaps the Moon God Palace didn't know this information, which was why they sent an Immortal to the lower realm to scheme for that strange land.

"It's a pity, even if we tell them now it's too late.

That strange land is in the lower realm. When an Immortal descends to the lower realm, if they don't capture the strange land and turn it into their own star, they can't return to the upper realm."

"Alas, the Moon God Palace will lose another Immortal. It's difficult, difficult, difficult. Are we about to witness the fall of a sacred land?"

In the great hall, the murmurs were filled with endless sighs.

The robot Zero One held Xie Xinsu, sitting on the riverbank.

The wide river was covered with a thick layer of ice.

Qi Yuan wore a white robe, the same color as the snow, blending together so that the boundaries were indistinguishable.

Snow fell on their shoulders as Zero One, Xie Xinsu, and Qi Yuan were all bathed in this snowfall.

In a moment, everything was a vast expanse of white.

"The myriad ways of the world are too numerous..."

Qi Yuan couldn't help but sigh at this time.

Snow had its own way.

Great Wisdom True Lord had once said that snowflakes don't talk because they don't have mouths.

Qi Yuan wanted to comprehend the way of snow. The most effective method would be to communicate with snowflakes that had mouths.

"You snowflake, why don't you grow a mouth? If you don't grow a mouth to speak your way, how am I supposed to know what your way is? Always making people guess!" Qi Yuan couldn't help but say.

Don't try to guess the thoughts of snowflakes.

All the myriad ways had to be guessed.

Hearing this, the robot Zero One couldn't help but laugh: "Master, anything is possible in this world.

Who knows, in the next moment, you might have an epiphany and understand all the myriad ways?"

If others heard this, they definitely wouldn't believe it.

But Qi Yuan was different. He nodded seriously: "You're right, anything is possible.

Who knows, in the next moment, all the myriad ways of the world might take a liking to me, grow mouths, and frantically introduce themselves."
