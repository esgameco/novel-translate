"Now in the Canglan Realm, two suns hang in the sky."

Old Man Wutian sat in his wheelchair, his gaze profound.

"It's a pity the second sun is too small. Otherwise, with the two suns competing, our Canglan Realm might have had a chance to break free from the great calamity."

Qi Yuan was speechless. He looked at Old Man Wutian and said, "You're the one who's small!"

He had always disliked being called small.

Old Man Wutian seemed not to have heard Qi Yuan's words, and instead murmured, "Born in the Canglan Realm, we are too constrained by the great calamity."

"If we shatter that sun in the sky, wouldn't the great calamity be gone?" Qi Yuan asked seriously.

Old Man Wutian chuckled and said, "The great calamity itself is already invincible in this world. Even if all the great masters of this realm joined forces, they would be no match for the great calamity.

And... to kill that sun in the sky, one would need to be at least at the Yang Spirit realm.

It's impossible to break through to the Yang Spirit realm without ascending to the upper realm.

But once we ascend to the upper realm, we people of the Canglan Realm would be like meat on their plates."

Generally speaking, cultivators at the Yin Spirit realm have the opportunity to ascend to the upper realm.

The laws of heaven and earth are more complete in the upper realm, and the world's origin is also more abundant.

To advance to the Yang Spirit realm, one must be in the upper realm.

It's impossible to advance to the Yang Spirit realm in the lower realm.

"This old man has sat idle for countless years, and finally found a way out," Old Man Wutian's wheelchair rolled, and he stopped in front of a jade slip, picking it up.

"What way out?"

"We... become fodder for the great calamity, marked by that sun, all because our cultivation methods... have problems," Old Man Wutian said heavily.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan revealed a surprised expression.

Qi Yuan had long known there were problems with the cultivation methods.

This was the first time he had heard this from a native of the Canglan Realm.

The Yin Spirit cultivators he had met before had never mentioned this, and Qi Yuan had also discovered that the cultivation methods they practiced all had problems.

For a Purple Palace cultivator to mention this was indeed interesting.

"If we create new methods, cultivate to the Yin Spirit realm with them, and ascend to the upper realm, we can... avoid the great calamity," Old Man Wutian spoke, his voice hoarse, "But new methods are too difficult, too difficult."

Qi Yuan suddenly had an idea: "If we use cultivation methods left behind in the Canglan Realm from the upper realm, wouldn't there be no problem cultivating them?"

"Not so," Old Man Wutian said, "According to ancient records, that supreme expert back then obtained a cultivation method taught in a dream by a Yang Spirit cultivator, and cultivated all the way to the Great Venerable realm.

However, he still... became fodder for that sun.

The great calamity has even influenced the will of the Dao in the Canglan Realm. Even cultivation methods obtained from the upper realm will inadvertently turn practitioners into fodder when cultivated."

Hearing this, Qi Yuan was inwardly surprised.

This great calamity sounded truly unsolvable.

Fortunately, although his "Qi Yuan Scripture" had some minor issues when cultivated, it wouldn't turn him into real fodder.

"Alas, I have wasted all this time and still haven't been able to create a new method that truly escapes the great calamity," Old Man Wutian sighed deeply, his voice infinitely desolate.

The purpose of his establishing the Ascension Pavilion was to create new methods to avoid the great calamity.

Qi Yuan then said, "Even if new methods are created, at most only one or two cultivators could avoid the great calamity.

If the new methods spread and become known to the great calamity and that sun above, wouldn't the Canglan Realm still... sink? So I believe the great calamity cannot be avoided, and escaping won't solve the problem.

We need to face the difficulty head-on, take the initiative to attack, and resolve the great calamity!"

Qi Yuan spoke passionately.

With his increased strength, he had swept through a series of Yuan Dan and Divine Infant cultivators in the Eastern Lands.

At the Divine Flower Meeting, countless people had constantly flattered him, and he had become somewhat complacent.

He felt like he could command the world, and no hero dared disobey.

Old Man Wutian looked at Qi Yuan and pointed in a direction.

"The great calamity should be in that direction. It would take three months to reach it. Does Great Venerable Wangyue really want to go see the great calamity?"

Qi Yuan immediately looked in that direction.

Suddenly, his right eyebrow began twitching uncontrollably.

A twitching left eye means wealth, a twitching right eye means disaster.

If his right eye was twitching normally, it could still mean wealth.

But it was twitching too violently!

Qi Yuan couldn't deceive himself.

"Never mind, I'll leave the great calamity alone for now," Qi Yuan decided not to confront the great calamity until he broke through to the Divine Infant realm.

"Can I make copies of all the cultivation methods here?" Qi Yuan asked.

Once he emptied out the Ascension Pavilion's techniques, he could return to Seven-Colored Peak with peace of mind and play games contentedly.

The game icon was almost fully loaded, and he only needed a few days of preparation before he could enter.

Qi Yuan now planned to memorize all the cultivation methods, so he could continue to deduce the "Qi Yuan Scripture" in his spare time while in the game.

Otherwise, if he didn't obtain enough methods to advance the "Qi Yuan Scripture", his strength would increase in the game, but without sufficient techniques, he wouldn't be able to rapidly increase his cultivation in the real world.

"Great Venerable Wangyue, feel free," Old Man Wutian said, and left pushing his wheelchair.

Qi Yuan then immersed himself in the Ascension Pavilion, collecting all the cultivation methods.

Seven days later.

On a spirit boat, Kang Fulu had a smug look on his face: "In the Eastern Lands Genius Competition, your senior brother here stood against many opponents, not letting down the prestige of the Five Light Sacred Technique!

Even with several geniuses from major sects standing before me, I didn't show the slightest fear and defeated them fair and square.

Now, I'm also ranked 498th on the Eastern Lands Genius Ranking.

It's a pity you didn't participate in the Eastern Lands Genius Competition, otherwise you could have seen your senior brother's heroic bearing."

Qi Yuan saw the few extra strands of white hair on Kang Fulu's head, knowing that he had worked hard to achieve this in the Eastern Lands Genius Competition.

After leaving the Ascension Pavilion, Qi Yuan briefly instructed the Blood Robe Alliance, announced he was going into seclusion, then changed back to his Qi Yuan identity and returned to the Divine Light Sect with Qi Jianjun.

On the way back, he unexpectedly encountered Kang Fulu and traveled together with him.

"It's a pity I couldn't enter the top 100 of the Eastern Lands Genius Ranking and bring back the treasure the Sect Master needed.

During this time, I've been secretly investigating, trying to find out who took that treasure, but to no avail," Kang Fulu said with disappointment.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan smiled.

If Kang Fulu could actually find out, that would be a big problem.

"Sigh, you little rascal, you disappear for a while and come back with such a grown daughter?" Kang Fulu looked at Qi Jianjun beside Qi Yuan with curiosity.

Qi Jianjun was playing chess with another little girl on the spirit boat, and seemed to hear Kang Fulu's voice, turning her head to look.

"The past is in the past," Qi Yuan couldn't be bothered to explain.

Qi Yuan saw the approaching sect gate and said to Kang Fulu, "Senior Brother, I won't go with you to see the Sect Master. I need to return to my peak."

He waved his hand, and Qi Jianjun ran over.

Hearing this, Kang Fulu said, "I understand, I understand."

He patted Qi Yuan's shoulder and said earnestly, "Junior Brother, don't blame yourself. There are differences between people. Given some more time, Junior Brother will surely be able to enter the Eastern Lands Genius Ranking."

Kang Fulu was very excited.

Being able to enter the Eastern Lands Genius Ranking this time was already an exceptional performance.

Returning to the sect would surely dazzle the eyes of his master and the Sect Master, right? After all, a true dragon had finally emerged from this poor place like the Great Shang Country.

On Seven-Colored Peak.

Qi Jianjun held Qi Yuan's hand, her eyes filled with curiosity: "Is this Father's home?"

"Mm," Qi Yuan felt a sense of peace returning to Seven-Colored Peak again.

Stirring up trouble in the outside world was certainly carefree, but home was the harbor for the soul.

"It looks quite beautiful, suitable for seclusion," Qi Jianjun said.

Qi Yuan gave his daughter a sidelong glance. Wasn't she just saying it was remote?

Qi Yuan and Qi Jianjun landed in front of the brick house.

He looked towards the wooden cabin next door, his expression strange: "Where's Junior Sister?"

Suddenly, his expression changed.

Because he sensed a thick frosty aura emanating from Junior Sister's wooden cabin.

He walked in, and several talismans immediately burst into flames, forming an array to block Qi Yuan.

When cultivating, Junior Sister often used some talismans to guard the surroundings.

The power exerted by these talismans approached that of a Divine Infant cultivator.

In the Great Shang Country, they were considered top-tier protective talismans.

Qi Yuan's gaze swept over, and those top-tier protective talismans shattered instantly.

His figure turned, and he immediately saw Jiang Lingsu lying on the bed, wearing thin clothes.

She was covered with a thin gauze, exposing her fair arms and snow-white neck, which were covered with a thin layer of frost.

She was frowning, seeming to be enduring some pain.

"Junior Sister?" Qi Yuan's heart tightened.

He had always known there was a problem with Junior Sister's body that could only be solved by a Purple Palace cultivator with a water-attribute Celestial Root.

Unfortunately, even after establishing the Blood Robe Alliance and practically dominating the Eastern Lands, he hadn't found a Purple Palace cultivator with a water-attribute Celestial Root.

He reached out and grasped Jiang Lingsu's arm.

A scorching sensation swept over at this moment.

Qi Yuan cultivated with the Five Elements Qi, and although he couldn't eradicate the root cause of Jiang Lingsu's illness, he could alleviate the pain.

His junior sister, this wealthy lady, had helped him so much. He needed to take good care of her to repay her kindness.

A rosy blush spread across Jiang Lingsu's snow-white face as she turned her head away, unwilling to look at Qi Yuan.

Qi Yuan looked puzzled.

What was wrong with Junior Sister? "Father, this big sister probably wants you to help pull up her clothes," Qi Jianjun said teasingly from the side.

Lying on the bed, Jiang Lingsu's skin was faintly visible through the pink gauze. Even lying down, her graceful figure with slender branches and plump fruits was hard to conceal. At this moment, one plump fruit had half escaped, dazzlingly white.

Seeing this, Qi Yuan gently blew, and Jiang Lingsu's clothes immediately moved up to cover it.

Feeling Qi Yuan's warm breath, the blush on Jiang Lingsu's face deepened.

Who helps pull up clothes like that? But Qi Yuan felt quite pleased with himself, thinking he had shown high emotional intelligence once again.

As the saying goes, men and women should not touch when passing objects. He didn't use his hands to avoid touching anything inappropriate, but chose to gently blow instead.

"Junior Sister, no need to thank me." Seeing Jiang Lingsu's complexion improving, Qi Yuan let out a sigh of relief.

When he had just dispelled the cold in his junior sister's body, he sensed that the cold that had just appeared had accumulated over half a month.

For half a month, she had been lying in bed alone enduring the pain, with no one to talk to.

"It's fine, I'm used to it," Jiang Lingsu seemed quite relaxed about it, her expression carefree. "Senior Brother, how did it go with the Eastern Lands Genius Rankings this time?"

Mortal Heart Realm.

Qingshui Village.

The thin and sickly villagers trudged back in droves, their faces bearing not insignificant injuries.

Village Chief Yu Lu was a small old man with a hunched back. His pants were rolled up to his knees, revealing legs as thin as hemp stalks.

"We lost the village battle, what a disgrace!"

A fat man wailed, looking especially grief-stricken.

"Stone Family Village has no honor, they actually worship a new god!" A scholarly-looking man spoke up, expressing disdain for Stone Family Village's worship of a new god.

At the mention of new gods, everyone's expressions darkened.

For these villages to have good weather and harvests, they had to worship deities.

Qingshui Village's Foxtail Grass God had shown no response for decades.

According to the priest Shen Tu, the Foxtail Grass God had probably starved to death.

It sounded ridiculous, but it was also normal.

After all, Qingshui Village had always been dirt poor and had nothing to offer the Foxtail Grass God.

"Without a deity's blessing, we simply can't defeat Stone Family Village!" a man said.

Qingshui Village's overall strength was actually greater than Stone Family Village's.

However, Stone Family Village had a new god who could bless the villagers, which led to their strength surpassing Qingshui Village by quite a bit.

In this battle for water resources, Qingshui Village suffered heavy losses.

"Even if the Foxtail Grass God blessed us, we still couldn't beat Stone Family Village. Their new god is too powerful!"

At the mention of the new god, fear appeared in the eyes of the villagers present.

"Why don't we also worship a new god?" a villager tentatively asked at this point.

"No!" Village Chief Yu Lu, who had been silent until now, spoke up. "No matter what, our Qingshui Village cannot worship a new god, absolutely not!"

Everyone fell silent.

They all knew why the village chief had such a strong reaction.

The difference between new gods and old gods was that new gods required human sacrifices!

The new god of Stone Family Village ate a child every month.

This was something the people of Qingshui Village simply could not accept.

"Moreover, we are descendants of an ancient clan. How could we worship new gods?" Yu Lu said.

Hearing "ancient clan," pride appeared in the eyes of the villagers present.

The ancient clans were known for their unyielding nature.

In the past, when Heaven was unjust, the great deities of the ancient clans dared to challenge the Heavens.

Now, in this chaotic era of new gods, how could they submit to new gods just to survive?

"Our ancient clan dared to challenge the gods. Even though we have declined, we should not bend our backs!"

"We dared to battle the Heavens and right the wrongs of the world. The ancient clans cannot be disgraced!"

"Waaah, I'm so hungry. Goudan, give me a steamed bun to eat!"

"You have pure ancient clan bloodline and you're asking me for a steamed bun? Have you no shame?"
