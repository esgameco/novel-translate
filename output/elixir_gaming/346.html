In this world, he was just a passerby, a game player.

Suddenly, his hand flicked out.

A brilliant smile appeared in Qi Yuan's eyes: "It would be interesting if a monkey picked you up."

With most of the demons in this world exterminated, it was time for him to complete his mission and leave.

It could be said that in this world, he had reaped a bountiful harvest.

These white moons really spoiled him.

These days, she had nightmares every night.

She dreamed that a crack appeared between heaven and earth, and a woman who looked exactly like her appeared and stabbed her to death with a knife.

The woman's cold expression was still vivid in her memory.

"I'm not really suffering from kidney deficiency, am I, having such strange dreams?"

They seemed to come from other worlds, wearing various ancient costumes, or riding technologically advanced warships.
