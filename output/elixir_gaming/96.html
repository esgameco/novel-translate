Chapter 96

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

A look of surprise flashed across his eyes.

Where is the golden elixir? It's gone.

He glanced over and saw what seemed to be a Foundation Establishment cultivator looking at him.

When he heard the words of the Foundation Establishment cultivator, a trace of suspicion flashed in his eyes.

"My final spiritual attack, yet a mere Foundation Establishment cultivator is unharmed. It seems you possess a precious spiritual treasure!"

Thinking of this, the eyes of the old demonic chicken demon were filled with ecstatic joy.

"The heavens have been merciful, I have survived great calamity, there must be good fortune to come!"

Surrounded and attacked by nine Infant Immortals, he had feigned death and escaped, his spiritual power already greatly depleted.

But he never expected to encounter a Foundation Establishment cultivator immune to his spiritual attacks.

Before his death, his indiscriminate spiritual attacks had dealt the greatest damage to those nine Infant Immortals, and the next greatest to the cultivators near Patriarch Zu Yuan.

The result was that the Mid-Nascent Soul cultivator was unconscious, while the Foundation Establishment cultivator was lively and energetic.

This clearly showed that the other party possessed a precious treasure that could defend against spiritual attacks. Judging from the level, it was of a very high grade.

If he could seize it, find a secluded place, and cultivate step-by-step, the chances of him weathering the tribulation of the Purple Palace would increase greatly.

How could he not be excited? Looking at Qi Yuan, he was like looking at a delicacy.

Qi Yuan was also looking at him, his eyes filled with puzzlement: "The one who was woken up by a fart really does have a problem with his head."

He held a kitchen knife, ready to strike.

"Hmph, even in my injured state, I am not someone a mere Foundation Establishment cultivator can humiliate." The old demonic chicken demon's eyes were filled with disdain as he instantly unleashed his Dao techniques, intending to trap Qi Yuan.

But Qi Yuan, holding the kitchen knife, directly struck forward.

Crack.

The kitchen knife, as if able to cleave spiritual bodies, fell upon the old demonic chicken demon's spiritual body.

"Impossible!" Excruciating pain like tearing spread, the old demonic chicken demon's smugness and excitement completely vanishing.

This was no delicacy, this was a demon! Qi Yuan's kitchen knife directly shattered his remaining soul.

Patriarch Zu Yuan's physical body fell to the ground again.

"I wonder if someone will fart and wake him up again?" Qi Yuan looked at the scene with suspicion.

At this moment, his gaze fell on the area near Patriarch Zu Yuan's body.

There, suspended in the air, was a blood-red drop of blood.

He immediately looked at it.

[Spiritual Blood, originating from the Great Void, accidentally fell in the Great Zhao, and was obtained by the old demonic chicken demon.

This blood, fused with the spiritual body, can cultivate a seed, which will eventually transform into a spiritual treasure.

The stronger the seed, the more powerful the spiritual treasure.

Even after death, one can be revived on the seed.]

"So, he was the old demonic chicken demon's seed, and the Patriarch Zu Yuan I just killed was the old demonic chicken demon?"

Qi Yuan looked at the drop of blood, his eyes filled with greed.

"This drop of blood is truly a good thing!"

"Old demon, you are truly my money-spending fairy! My pure white moon, even whiter than the broken wolf!"

"This is Spiritual Blood from the Great Void, truly a precious treasure!"

Spiritual treasures were extremely rare.

In the entire Great Zhao, there might not even be a single spiritual treasure.

And the spiritual treasure formed from the condensation of Spiritual Blood seemed to be a growth-type, which was even more terrifying.

Qi Yuan immediately swallowed this drop of Spiritual Blood.

In an instant, he felt a drop of fresh blood appear on his spiritual body.

This drop of blood could be parasitized at any time.

"If I were to parasitize on the body of a Yang God, wouldn't that Yang God become my spiritual treasure?"

Qi Yuan had wild thoughts, but also felt this idea was not very feasible.

"I need to find a suitable object to parasitize and cultivate my spiritual treasure!"

Qi Yuan thought this way.

The matter was over, it was time to leave this place.

He looked at the old man, who had not yet regained consciousness.

"Since you previously let me leave first, I'll take you out of here."

Qi Yuan waved his hand, and the old man's entire body floated behind him, as if being controlled like a corpse.

At this time, a message came from Ku Mu Zhen Jun in Qi Yuan's jade slip.

"The old demonic chicken demon has been slain, we nine will be recuperating in the Myriad Demon Cave for four days.

How are things on your end?"

Qi Yuan quickly replied: "It's done, I killed an Elixir Nascent Soul."

There was a moment of silence from Ku Mu Zhen Jun's end, before he slowly replied: "In four days, I will come to the Seven-Colored Peak to find you."

"Mm."

Carrying the old man, Qi Yuan left the cave.

At this time, outside the cave, the thirteen Elixir Nascent Soul cultivators who had entered earlier had come out.

But this time, only eleven people came out, the other two were corpses.

The leader unexpectedly glanced at Qi Yuan, seeing the unconscious old man, he was even more surprised.

"The matter here is concluded, we shall all return to our respective homes.

However, during this time, the news of the old demonic chicken demon's demise must not be leaked."

The leader spoke, then disappeared like a flowing light.

The cultivators present one by one left.

Qi Yuan looked at the old man behind him.

"Sigh, it seems I still have to send you back home, to Wuxue City... Lao Guan, right?"

He still remembered going to Wuxue City before.

Back then, when he went to destroy the Black Mountain Sect, the spiritual boat he rode on had landed in Wuxue City.

At that time, there was also a beautiful and talkative young woman sitting next to him on the spiritual boat.

...

Half a day later.

Sitting in the courtyard, Qi Yuan's eyes flashed with a trace of surprise.

He didn't expect to see the beautiful young woman from the spiritual boat again.

That beautiful young woman was actually the daughter of the old man Lao Guan.

"I've already returned the person, I should be going now." Qi Yuan's voice was a bit hoarse, changing his voice tone was the simplest thing for him.

"Thank you, senior, for saving my father's life." Liu Piao was deeply grateful.

Her father was injured and unconscious outside.

If any malicious cultivators had found him, her father would not have survived.

She never expected someone would bring her father back.

How could she not be moved?

Just then, hurried footsteps approached: "Grandfather, Grandfather."

Duan Qianrou, in a white dress, her forehead beaded with fine sweat, looked very anxious.

"Your grandfather has not yet regained consciousness." Liu Piao saw her daughter, her heart relieved. "It was this senior who brought your grandfather back."

Duan Qianrou then carefully examined Qi Yuan.

But unfortunately, Qi Yuan's face was covered by a mask, so she could not see his features clearly.

"Senior, for saving my grandfather from danger, Qianrou has prepared a small gift, please don't disdain it." Duan Qianrou said, extending a storage bag.

The bag was filled to the brim with blood crystals.

Qi Yuan did not refuse, he took the blood crystals, and suddenly he felt the tattoo of Xiao Jia on his chest become slightly warm.

He pondered.

Could it be that these blood crystals are useful for Xiao Jia?

"No problem, a small favor, not worth mentioning, the person has been delivered, and I should leave now."

Qi Yuan was now eager to leave, to see what use these blood crystals could be for Xiao Jia.

Why did the Xiao Jia tattoo on his chest become warm?

Without waiting for Duan Qianrou to speak, Qi Yuan disappeared like a flash of light.

It wasn't long before, in a place with no one around, Qi Yuan summoned Xiao Jia out.

He took out the blood crystals.

The blood crystals were not very large, crystal clear, somewhat resembling pomegranate seeds, but even more blood-red.

Seeing the blood crystals, a wooden plaque fell out of Xiao Jia's sleeve.

A joyful expression.

"Do you like them? I'll give them to you."

Xiao Jia's sleeve picked up one of the blood crystals.

After some time, that blood crystal disappeared.

Xiao Jia looked very excited.

Seeing Xiao Jia happy, Qi Yuan's mood also improved greatly.

"Did you eat it?" Qi Yuan thought Xiao Jia didn't eat things.

Unexpectedly, Xiao Jia actually ate things.

"I have a lot more here, enough for you to eat." Qi Yuan took out some more of the blood crystals for Xiao Jia.

Watching Xiao Jia slowly digest them.

He looked at the blood crystals, and some information flashed in his eyes.

[Blood crystals, effects comparable to low-grade spirit stones.]

The information he saw was random, and he might not be able to see the key information immediately.

"Since you like them, when I see the Sect Master, I'll find an excuse to take over the blood crystal mine in Wuxue City, these spirit stones will be enough for you to eat."

Qi Yuan couldn't be bothered to spend money to buy blood crystals, it would be much better to directly take over the blood crystal mine in Wuxue City.

Let the people of Wuxue City work for him.

After all, the current Emperor of the Great Xia is his junior brother.
