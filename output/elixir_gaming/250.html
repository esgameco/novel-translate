The Upper Realm.

Within the city, immortal light permeated everywhere.

In the sky above, streaks of light occasionally flashed by, falling into Dream Return City.

Shen Wushen, dressed in a black robe, flew through the air with fluctuating spiritual power.

Cultivators he encountered on the way, whether at the Yuandan or Shenying stage, or even Purple Palace realm, all unconsciously made way for Shen Wushen.

Because Shen Wushen was flying while riding on a small mountain.

This small mountain was the mark of a true disciple of Mount Futu.

Mount Futu was the strongest sect in this area, said to be a holy land with a Yang Immortal presiding over it.

The status of true disciples was even higher than ordinary Yin Immortals.

Shen Wushen landed in Dream Return City. Before him stood a building called the Eight Treasures Tower.

This tower was a branch of the Tianzi Tower.

Its specialized business was transmitting information.

The advertising that Qi Yuan mentioned, Tianzi Tower also did.

"An honored guest has arrived. My apologies for not welcoming you from afar. Please forgive me."

A cheerful voice rang out as the manager of this floor of the Eight Treasures Tower walked out.

He was an elderly man with white hair, wearing a neat Daoist robe embroidered with a crane about to take flight from the clouds.

The manager's strength was at the peak of Purple Palace realm, just one step away from Yin Immortal. But he was exceptionally respectful when facing Shen Wushen.

"I need to use your Eight Treasures Tower to transmit a message," Shen Wushen said, getting straight to the point.

He was never good at socializing, nor did he need to be, since everyone he met treated him quite well.

The manager's eyes held a kindly smile. "Where does this message need to be sent?"

"Moon Goddess Palace, to Moon Lady Jin Li," Shen Wushen replied.

The message to be transmitted included not only Qi Jianjun's verbal message, but also the cultivation technique composed by Qi Yuan.

"Seeing You."

These items were placed in a storage bag and given simple processing.

Of course, the Tianzi Tower behind the Eight Treasures Tower would not allow these things to leak.

After all, Tianzi Tower's business was so large that it even did business for Yang Immortals, continuing for a million years.

If there had been any problems, Tianzi Tower would have collapsed long ago.

Even in a world where strength reigns supreme, credibility is extremely important.

Once it crumbles, it could lead to the collapse of etiquette and music.

"Moon Goddess Palace?" The manager hesitated, taking out a jade slip resembling a map and consulting it.

Evidently, the manager had heard of this holy land, but did not know the specific location of Moon Goddess Palace.

"It's quite far..." After learning the approximate location of Moon Goddess Palace, the manager sighed, "It will take about three years to transmit it there."

The Upper Realm was extremely vast, or rather, it was the collective name for many worlds.

The Upper Realm was boundless. Even ordinary Yang Immortals could not set foot in all areas in their lifetime.

How many individual worlds were there in total in the Upper Realm?

Even Yang Immortals did not know.

However, the worlds of the Upper Realm were divided into nine heavens.

Mount Futu was in the second heaven of the lower three heavens.

Moon Goddess Palace was located in the sixth heaven of the middle three heavens.

"Mm, thank you for your trouble," Shen Wushen said, taking out two storage bags. One contained the items to be transmitted, the other was the payment.

The manager hurriedly accepted them, then took out an ancient parchment to sign a contract with Shen Wushen.

The agreement was thus reached.

Shen Wushen breathed a sigh of relief. One of the tasks Qi Yuan had asked him to do was now completed.

There was still one more...

Thinking of this, he hesitated a bit.

That one was much more troublesome than transmitting a message.

Moon Goddess Palace was huge, but there were only a few Moon Ladies, so sending a message to a Moon Lady was still feasible.

But "confessing to Ruan Yixi" was obviously more complicated.

Moon Goddess Palace was too big. To send a message to someone named Ruan Yixi would require broadcasting it widely, as Qi Yuan had said.

The cost... would also be much higher.

He still needed to spend a lot of money to exchange for some cultivation techniques for Qi Yuan.

The Lower Realm also needed money, which was another huge expense.

So now, he was penniless.

The only way he could make money... was by doing the tasks Qi Yuan mentioned.

He didn't have high hopes for what Qi Yuan said.

Perhaps if he took on dozens of tasks, Qi Yuan might be able to complete two or three, which would be a big profit.

So he wouldn't lose too much.

Thinking of this, he was about to leave.

Suddenly, he saw a beautiful fair-skinned woman with an extremely exaggerated waist-to-hip ratio, who couldn't take another step.

Large breasts, willow waist, plump buttocks, plus that face as charming as autumn waters, like a seductive fox spirit - as soon as the woman appeared, she attracted the attention of almost everyone present.

"I wonder who could become the Dao partner of such a celestial maiden."

"I heard the Holy Son of Mount Futu sought Lady Lian Mei as his Dao partner but was unsuccessful."

Lady Lian Mei was a true disciple of Mount Futu, with strength at the peak of Shenying realm.

Among the true disciples of Mount Futu, Lady Lian Mei was also in the top ten.

At this moment, Lady Lian Mei's gaze fell on Shen Wushen. She saw his incredibly intense, naked gaze, as if he wanted to strip off her clothes.

She had seen many such gazes, but they were always furtive looks. She had almost never encountered anyone as direct as Shen Wushen.

"Senior Sister Lian Mei, may I challenge you to a battle!" At this moment, Shen Wushen spoke up, a strong fighting spirit surging from his body.

His gaze was blazing, as if he had seen a peerless beauty.

Whether in the Upper or Lower Realm, whenever he saw an opponent, he would challenge them to a fight, seeking defeat to hone his own Dao and innate divine abilities.

Lady Lian Mei smiled charmingly. "Why not?"

Shen Wushen's reputation as a battle maniac was quite well-known in Mount Futu.

The last time Shen Wushen had challenged her, she had easily defeated him.

This time he came to challenge her again, and Lady Lian Mei naturally agreed.

The cultivators nearby all showed envious expressions.

"I also want to have a fair and square battle with Lady Lian Mei!"

"You rascal, you want to duel with Lady Lian Mei? You're clearly lusting after her body!"

"That guy really doesn't understand romance. He could invite Lady Lian Mei to do anything, why insist on a duel?"

"Shen Wushen is strong, but he's still quite inferior to Lady Lian Mei."

Even so, these cultivators watched with great interest.

A hundred breaths of time passed.

Lady Lian Mei looked at Shen Wushen, a smile appearing in her eyes. "Junior Brother Shen has come to challenge this senior sister. It seems this journey through the myriad worlds has been quite fruitful."

"Mm, I befriended a genius who gave me some pointers, greatly increasing my strength," Shen Wushen answered honestly.

Lady Lian Mei's smile grew wider. "I can see that. You're even confident enough to challenge me."

Among the true disciples, Lian Mei belonged to the first tier, while Shen Wushen belonged to the second tier.

"Compared to that genius, I may not be as good, but compared to senior sister, I'm confident I can win!" Shen Wushen said.

Lady Lian Mei smiled coyly. "Junior brother is still as direct as ever in his speech."

In other words, he didn't think before he spoke.

The battle also erupted at this moment.

All the onlookers widened their eyes and held their breath to watch this exciting duel.

However, contrary to their expectations, the duel began quickly and ended just as quickly.

In less than ten breaths of time, Lady Lian Mei's body fell from the air.

On her snow-white chest appeared a bright red footprint. Blood trickled from the corner of her mouth, and her face showed a complex expression.

A flash of snow-white gauze covered her chest.

She held her chest with her hand, looking at Shen Wushen with a complicated expression. "I lost."

If there weren't so many people around, she would definitely want to go back.
