Not existing in the present space-time, endlessly devouring everything.

Unless he reaches the same level as the Black Sky.

How could he stop it then? "The so-called Black Sky is the fourth layer of the Sun God!"

Qi Yuan fell silent.

The fourth layer of the Sun God.

Indeed.

The Black Sky is the fourth layer of the Sun God.

It does not exist in the present, only in the past or the future, a terrifying existence.

Like the Root Demon, it has become the Root Demon Calamity in the hearts of countless Sun Gods.

But the Black Sky is different, the Black Sky is chaotic and disorderly, devouring is its only purpose, its ultimate goal.

For the first time, Qi Yuan felt immense pressure and a sense of being undefeatable.

Without entering the fourth layer of the Sun God, he could not see the Black Sky, nor could he stop it.

If he stepped into the fourth layer of the Sun God, he would become the chaotic Black Sky.

Consciousness dissolved, true spirit vanished...

They all lacked wisdom and consciousness, merely being a program, a projection.

This was truly terrifying. The gap between each layer of the Sun God was like an abyss.

The third layer of the Sun God could never witness the fourth layer in this lifetime.

But the third layer would be unconsciously devoured by the fourth layer.

This game was too dangerous?

Return?

It was impossible to return! Not only was this his own game, but if he returned, the Black Sky would also spread to the Immortal Realm.

The next to be devoured would be the Immortal Realm.

So, unlike other games, Qi Yuan had no retreat this time, not even the ability to return to reality and end the game.

"I insist on seeing if I can stop it!"

At this moment, a profound expression flashed in Qi Yuan's eyes.

The Great Oblivion Heart Sutra!

This time, he would forget his own self.

"I am not in the Heavenly Realm!"

"I am not the fifth layer of the Sun God!"

"I am the fourth layer of the Sun God!"

The Great Oblivion Heart Sutra was continuously activated.
