After all, condensing one Divine Infant was the common understanding.

It was like how one plus one equals two.

Could someone really condense two Divine Infants?

Only Shen Wushen, upon hearing Qi Yuan's words, furrowed his brows and kept pondering, muttering, "Too few Divine Infants?"

In his understanding, one could naturally only form one Divine Infant.

A Divine Infant was condensed from a shattered Golden Core.

One Golden Core could naturally only condense into one Divine Infant.

Even if one forcibly condensed two, wouldn't it still be the amount of one Golden Core?

Wouldn't separating it actually make it weaker? Shen Wushen looked at Qi Yuan with a puzzled expression and said, "Divine Infants originate from Golden Cores. Even if one had the ability to condense two, there wouldn't be any fundamental difference."

Shen Wushen asked Qi Yuan earnestly, seriously discussing the matter.

This made the other Divine Infant cultivators feel strange.

Was this... even worth discussing?

Divine Zhi Feng's expression changed several times. She wanted to remind Shen Wushen.

But after some thought, she suppressed the idea in her heart.

"What you say is correct. That's how ordinary people think. Only true geniuses would take a different approach. To solve this problem is simple - don't shatter the Golden Core to form a Divine Infant! Let the Golden Core follow the path of the Golden Core, and the Divine Infant follow the path of the Divine Infant. Two broad roads, both leading to Rome." Qi Yuan's words were too shocking, and the term "Rome" was incomprehensible to them, making his words seem profound and unfathomable.

The other Divine Infant cultivators all frowned, seemingly pondering the true meaning behind Qi Yuan's words.

Could the Golden Core really follow its own path?

Could the Divine Infant follow its own path?

Shen Wushen also furrowed his brows tightly, seriously contemplating.

She naturally felt that Qi Yuan was talking nonsense, deliberately teasing these cultivators.

Many Divine Infant cultivators shared this thought.

What Qi Yuan said was too incredible, or rather, incomprehensible.

After all, it's very difficult to change people's perceptions.

It's like if someone on Earth told a passerby that the Earth was flat, that passerby probably wouldn't believe it either.

"Unless you show us two Divine Infants," Divine Zhi Feng said.

Qi Yuan shrugged, "Why should I take out my Divine Infants to show you?"

Although he had organized this Divine Infant conference to show off, there was a difference between actively showing off and being forced to do so.

Divine Zhi Feng was stunned for a moment, then said, "Ten jade-level secret techniques!"

Hearing this, Qi Yuan felt no ripples in his heart.

This was more like it.

However, remembering his bet with Shen Wushen, he said, "What if I do have two Divine Infants?"

He wanted to extract more.

"If you have them, I'm willing to give 100,000 spirit stones!" Divine Zhi Feng said, "If you don't, you'll be Shen Gongzi's sparring partner for ten years."

A crazy fan is still a crazy fan, still thinking about Shen Wushen.

"No good, I'm not interested in spirit stones," said Qi Yuan, who indeed no longer cared for spirit stones at his current level.

He could now say, like Jack Ma, that he wasn't interested in money.

Only cultivation techniques could pique his interest now.

Divine Zhi Feng gritted her teeth. As the leader of a sect, she could naturally offer more.

But she couldn't bet with sect resources either.

She thought for a moment, then said through gritted teeth, "If you have two Divine Infants, I'll eat Wu Dian Monk's boots. How about that?"

Hearing this, Qi Yuan's eyes lit up.

There was such an entertaining option?

Usually, when dealing with his first loves, Qi Yuan always acted to benefit himself at others' expense.

Now, if Divine Zhi Feng were to eat Wu Dian Monk's boots, it would be harming others without benefiting himself.

Qi Yuan quite liked this kind of thing too.

"Alright!"

Qi Yuan naturally agreed.

The Divine Infant cultivators on the scene all looked at each other, some trying to hold back their laughter.

Wu Dian Monk hadn't expected the matter to involve him. He blinked his eyes and looked at Qi Yuan, "True Lord, quickly show us your two Divine Infants!"

He especially hoped that Qi Yuan really had two Divine Infants.

This way, his boots would have a destination.

Wu Dian Monk had always gone barefoot, and there was actually a reason for this.

Wu Dian Monk had a pair of magical boots, extremely precious, that could increase his flying speed considerably.

Those boots were also one of Wu Dian Monk's favorite treasures.

He had once practiced a foot technique. This dao technique related to footwork was very powerful, but Wu Dian Monk encountered a mishap during cultivation and went astray.

He ended up with terribly smelly, rotten feet.

This wasn't ordinary mortal stink, but cultivator-level stink.

His magical boots were also contaminated by this stench, emitting a foul odor like a dead rat in a drain or a rotting snake by the roadside.

Because of that foot technique, Wu Dian Monk could no longer wear shoes, and the stench on his feet only dissipated when barefoot.

However, he still kept those magical boots, always wanting to sell them.

Unfortunately, even at a price of 20,000 spirit stones, no Divine Infant cultivator was willing to buy them.

After all, the stench on the boots remained.

Wu Dian Monk excitedly said, "Sect Leader Shen, if you want to eat my boots later, you'll have to pay me 20,000 spirit stones!"

This was why Wu Dian Monk hoped Qi Yuan would win.

This way, the boots he had been unable to sell would finally find a good home.

As he spoke, he took out two boots from a separate storage bag.

Instantly, a terribly foul odor spread.

"Wu Dian Monk, put those away quickly!"

"Ugh..."

Even Divine Infant cultivators couldn't stand this stench.

Nearby, Qi Yuan smiled and said, "The cultivation world is better. When betting to eat boots, they really eat them."

After all, there were so many scammers on Earth.

If Qi Yuan encountered them, he would have to make them honest people.

"Watch closely, my two Divine Infants."

Qi Yuan had many Divine Infants, so taking out two was easy.

Moreover, unlike when treating his junior sister Jiang Lingsu's illness, he didn't need to activate the Divine Infants or put them in a semi-awakened state.

Instantly, two streaks of light flashed.

Around Qi Yuan floated two crystal-clear Divine Infants, like infants.

Each Divine Infant emitted an ancient, restrained aura, as if hiding immense energy.

All the Divine Infant cultivators present sensed an extremely dangerous aura.
