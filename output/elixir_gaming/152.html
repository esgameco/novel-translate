"Why can't I mute them?"

"Big shot, I just came to cheer you on, don't drag me into this!"

Because Blood Robe Sword God had declared he would raze the Palace of Light in seven days, which was beyond many people's expectations.

These people didn't understand why Blood Robe Sword God dared to do this. Even the number one expert below yin spirit level in the Eastern Lands, Ascension Pavilion Master Old Man Wutian, wouldn't dare say he could destroy the Palace of Light.

How did Blood Robe Sword God dare?

After all, even if Blood Robe Sword God killed nine Purple Mansion experts of the Palace of Light, they still had over fifty Purple Mansion cultivators, including three late-stage Purple Mansion experts.

Plus their foundation built over tens of thousands of years, how could Blood Robe Sword God fight against that?

At this time, Qi Yuan came out of his cave dwelling, intending to bask in the sun.

A voice immediately reached his ears.

"This Blood Robe Sword God and junior brother Qi Yuan must both be cultivators of the Path of Folly, otherwise how can you explain a Purple Mansion powerhouse declaring he'll personally go to the Palace of Light and raze it to the ground!" The speaker was Kang Fulu.

Jiang Ya stood nearby, her brows furrowed as if also pondering something.

Hearing Kang Fulu's words, Qi Yuan was immediately displeased: "Senior brother, is it not possible that Blood Robe Sword God could display great divine might and destroy the Palace of Light?"

"Impossible!" Kang Fulu shook his head.

Jiang Ya then spoke up: "Master once said the Palace of Light has yin spirit level foundations... even he couldn't do anything to the Palace of Light.

For Blood Robe Sword God... it would be difficult.

The waters here run deep, I don't know what the final outcome will be either.

But the result of this matter is irrelevant to us, we should focus on preparing for the Eastern Lands Genius Rankings."

Hearing this, Kang Fulu's expression also turned serious.

The Eastern Lands Genius Rankings opening in half a month was of utmost importance.

He still needed to work hard on the task entrusted by the Divine Light Sect Leader, his father-in-law.

Qi Yuan continued basking in the sun, muttering: "No wonder the Palace of Light has been tyrannical for so many years and remains evergreen, because everyone in the world fears yin spirits.

But I'm not afraid. I have Heavenly Dao Foundation Establishment, Star Gold Core, and Lord Feng. What's a mere yin spirit to worry about?"

After sunbathing for a while, Qi Yuan felt bored. It was time to go back and continue revising techniques.

The progress of the "Qi Yuan Scripture" needed to be pushed forward.

Looking at Little Jia obediently eating blood crystals beside him, Qi Yuan couldn't help asking: "Do you think I can destroy the Palace of Light?"

Little Jia paused, her sleeves tugging Qi Yuan's hand as she nodded vigorously.

A smile immediately appeared on Qi Yuan's face as he embraced Little Jia's slender waist.

"As expected, you have the keenest eye, Little Jia!"

Qi Yuan's previous slight irritation disappeared without a trace.

Still thinking about music at a time like this, this Blood Robe Sword God was truly otherworldly!

"Senior, when do we depart?" Xu Yeli's heart was conflicted at this moment.

To be honest, he felt some trepidation.

This time was different from before, they were heading to the Palace of Light headquarters.

"He really dared to go!"
