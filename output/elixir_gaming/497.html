Just one step away from becoming a mysterious-grade military commander.

"As the Descending Deity" is a skill, or something akin to a divine ability.

When there are faithful worshippers kneeling in reverence or praying, Qi Yuan can sense it and manifest a divine form to display divine miracles.

This divine form possesses a portion of the power of the Descending Deity divine weapon.

Of course, it is not without limitations, the further the distance, the weaker the power.

The only flaw is that it can only be summoned by others, he cannot initiate it himself.

This skill is too passive.

"I'm actually the Descending Deity, quickly summon me!" Qi Yuan wanted to conduct an experiment.

Old man Chen Kangbao closed his eyes, his face filled with reverent expression: "Oh God, please open your eyes and look at me, this pale and thin man!"

The voice resounded in his ears and in his heart.

Qi Yuan had three choices:

1. Respond to the summons as the Descending Deity.

2. Activate his superpower to communicate with him.

3. Ignore it or block it.

"Okay, as long as he can summon me himself."

Qi Yuan did not choose the first option.

He was too weak now, too lazy to respond.

So he chose the second option.

"Since that's the case, I'll fulfill your wish."

After the Purple Palace, Qi Yuan had gained the superpower to communicate with "heard" voices, and unexpected things would happen.

This skill was particularly suitable for the identity of the Descending Deity.

Sure enough, Chen Kangbao suddenly opened his eyes, and on the ground in front of him, a fragrant aroma wafted up.
