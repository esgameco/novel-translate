Chapter 23

Here is the full English translation of the excerpt from the Chinese novel:

Aunt Qin first tried doing push-ups.

She carried a ladder on her back and bent down.

Her palms touched the dirt, and she felt there was nothing mysterious about it.

"Perhaps... it's just nonsense?"

With this thought, Aunt Qin began doing push-ups.

It must be said that a person wearing brocade and silk clothes doing push-ups felt quite incongruous.

In less than a single breath, Aunt Qin had already completed a push-up.

Aunt Qin originally had a resigned smile on her face, because during the process of doing the push-ups, she did not feel the circulation of her inner energy and true essence.

Without these, how could she become stronger?

But in the next moment, as soon as she finished a push-up, a special change occurred instantly.

Jinli hurriedly asked, "How is it, Aunt Qin?"

At this time, Aunt Qin was directly stunned.

"This old body... this old body feels... as if it has become a little stronger."

"What?" The people present exclaimed in surprise.

Could it really be that doing exercise can make one stronger?

"How much stronger is it?" Huaxian quickly asked.

She was afraid that Aunt Qin, out of consideration for the Empress Jinli's face, would give an ambiguous answer.

Aunt Qin then seriously said, "Just one push-up is equivalent to half an hour of my cultivation."

"What!" The people present were all shocked.

One push-up is equivalent to half an hour of cultivation.

Isn't this too exaggerated? "Doesn't this mean that without a thousand push-ups, it can be compared to a year of cultivation?" Huaxian calculated and was suddenly greatly surprised.

"If this is true, then by cultivating here for a day, it can be equivalent to ten years of cultivation outside?"

The crowd was extremely shocked.

Doing push-ups can improve one's cultivation?

What is the principle behind this? Where has such a thing happened before?

It is simply incomprehensible! It's as if saying that by eating a meal, drinking some water, or passing gas, one can break through their cultivation level.

Although the crowd was shocked and puzzled, they still began to do exercises one after another.

So these thirty trusted aides all started doing exercises, carrying ladders on their backs in the XuanYuan Forbidden Area.

They were all cultivators with strong abilities, and they did push-ups at an extremely fast pace, "push-ups" like flying.

This scene was extremely shocking.

If the people from Qi Yuan's previous world were to see this, they would definitely exclaim "Awesome!"

Of course, if it were a video, the comments would be "Nothing special," "I can do it too!" "I have a friend..."

After all, that is the comment section, where all kinds of extraordinary talents gather.

"I... broke through!"

"The bottleneck that has confined me for three years has finally been broken!"

"Doing push-ups is very beneficial for breaking through bottlenecks!"

The people present were all extremely surprised, quickly doing push-ups and sharing their feelings.

In just half an hour, more than thirty people present had three consecutive breakthroughs.

Two of them were breakthroughs in minor realms, and one was a breakthrough in a major realm.

"Go try other exercises as well."

These people naturally did not just do push-ups, but also tried other exercises.

Pull-ups, carrying ladders on their backs, were a bit difficult, but they could still do it.

And doing pull-ups could also improve their cultivation, with different focuses.

Some people chose pull-ups, while others chose push-ups.

"But what about sit-ups? How do we do them with the ladder on our backs?"

"Carrying the ladder on your back? You don't necessarily have to do it that way, you can also use your feet!"

"Stand the ladder up and hook your feet around it to do sit-ups!"

The crowd carefully tried it out.

The result was that it really worked, and they could also quickly increase their cultivation.

Some people even placed the ladder horizontally in the air and hooked their feet around it to do sit-ups.

After all, they were all cultivators, and even poses that would be difficult for dance students, they could easily perform.

On the scene, the crowd kept doing exercises, push-ups, sit-ups, pull-ups... with great enthusiasm.

Jinli looked at Qin Aunt and asked, "Qin Aunt, how are you doing?"

At this moment, Qin Aunt's complexion was ruddy, and her cultivation had become even more profound.

She continued her exercises: "This old body feels... extraordinary! Perhaps even the supreme imperial masters may not have such techniques. His Majesty's good friend is truly an extraordinary person from the outside world."

Thinking of Qi Yuan, Jinli's expression became somewhat complex, and a strong sense of inferiority overwhelmed her.

However, considering her current predicament, she had no time to think about other things.

"Qin Aunt, about how long do you think it will take for you to break through to the Imperial Realm here?" Jinli asked.

Only by reaching the Imperial Realm could they decisively win the upcoming battle in a few days.

"In just two days, I will be able to step into the Imperial Realm!" Qin Aunt said confidently.

Originally, she was already in the late King Realm.

In the Xuanyuan Forbidden Land, the cultivation advancement was as easy as drinking water.

Breaking through to the Imperial Realm was no big deal! That's the Imperial Realm, after all!

She was inwardly extremely excited.

Hearing this, Jinli felt relieved.

She looked at the group and said softly, "There's something I forgot to tell you all.

While doing these exercises to improve your cultivation in the Xuanyuan Forbidden Land, there are certain side effects."

Upon hearing these words, the initial joy of the people present was somewhat dampened.

Ah, that makes sense.

If there were no side effects, being able to increase their cultivation so much would be unreasonable.

"We are willing to die for His Majesty!" they exclaimed in unison, as if they were ready to face death without regret.

In their eyes, such a miraculous way of increasing cultivation must have terrifying side effects.

Qin Aunt also said solemnly, "To die for His Majesty, without any regrets!"

Jinli, seeing this, was both touched and somewhat amused: "The side effects are not that terrible, it won't make you all die."

The people present felt relieved.

The Forbidden Army Commander, Hua Xian, looked at Jinli and slowly asked, "May I ask what the side effect is?"

"Becoming stronger... may result in hair loss."

"Hair loss?"

"Just hair loss?"

"Is that it?"

The people were shocked.

What shocked them was... that's it? With such a massive increase in cultivation, the only side effect was just hair loss.

Not to mention hair loss, for the sake of improving their strength, some people in the Southern Qian Kingdom even self-castrated to cultivate evil techniques.

Compared to that, losing some hair was nothing! At this time, a woman who had previously broken through a realm barrier said with a smile, "When I broke through the realm barrier before, I lost a dozen or so hairs, and I didn't know the reason behind it. I never expected it to be like this."

Not just a dozen hairs, even if they lost all their body hair during a major realm breakthrough, they would still be willing to do it.

And at this moment, a female Forbidden Army soldier who was bald suddenly said, "I don't have any hair... what am I going to lose?"

The others looked at each other, not knowing how to respond.

...

Time flew by quickly.

Qi Yuan continued to hunt down the Yiguan Demons.

After his recent efforts, he had now reached level 88.

He slew a Yiguan Demon with a single sword strike.

Unfortunately, these Yiguan Demons could not speak or scream in agony.

Suddenly, Qi Yuan's expression changed drastically.

"It hurts!" he cried out in anguish, his face contorted in pain as he clutched his abdomen. "My body is... about to be cut apart."

However, soon after, seeing the Yiguan Demon completely shattered, he revealed a smug smile.

"I'm such a good person."

"Seeing that you're mute and can't speak, I specially provided the voice-over for you."

"Today, I'll be a voice actor for a day."

He said, then raised his sword and charged towards the Yiguan Demons again.

"Sword Shattering Skull!"

"No!"

"Xiang River Sword Rain!"

"It hurts!"

"Divine Dragon Spin!"

"Ah!"
