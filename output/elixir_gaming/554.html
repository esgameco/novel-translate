In the outer city area, most of the pale and thin people are now hiding in their rooms, shivering in fear, not daring to go out.

"Was that rumbling sound just now the rebels breaking in?"

A group of people whispered and exchanged thoughts, their minds complex, each with their own views.

On the street, a man in black clothes walked quickly, his expression hurried.

Just then, a special bird call sounded.

The man in black's expression changed slightly, and he immediately walked towards the direction of the bird call.

About a dozen breaths later, his figure appeared in an abandoned courtyard.

In the courtyard, there were two people - one was the familiar Wu Xiang, the other was a fat man dressed in brocade and silk.

"Secretary Li, this is a big deal!" the fat man spoke, his fleshy cheeks quivering.

This fat man was a rebel, who had been hiding in Cloud Blood City.

His business was quite successful, gaining the favor of the aristocrats, and he also had some connections in the inner city.

So when something happened in the inner city, he could get the news first.

"What exactly happened?" asked the black-clothed Secretary Li.

First there was the rumbling, then the military strategist of the Blood Tiger Gang said someone was massacring in the inner city.

"They're dead, all dead!" the fat man said anxiously, his mind in a panic, unable to speak clearly.

"Calm down." Secretary Li's voice was crisp, subduing the fat man's mind.

The fat man finally settled down and organized his words: "A powerful expert entered the inner city and killed... the Devilish God's daughter, a princess!"

"What... killed the princess?" Secretary Li's breathing quickened.

The so-called princess was the offspring of the Devilish God! That was the Devilish God, a god-level expert, the existence that suppressed the Kuijin Realm.

Yet, he was actually killed by someone! This was like tearing open the heavens!

"Is the Thorn Blood chasing him?" Secretary Li asked.

Now he was a bit uneasy, not knowing whether he should receive this person to join the rebels.

After all, this might bring disaster upon them.

"No!" the fat man shook his head, "He's the one killing those aristocrats!"

"What?" Secretary Li was confused.

After slaying the Devilish God's princess, instead of fleeing, he continued to kill those unnatural aristocrats in the city, seeking death in vain.

The city had dozens of heaven-level experts guarding it.

This mysterious expert was so formidable.

"If he had left immediately, he might have had a chance to survive. But now, after slaying the aristocrats, he's probably... already surrounded by the Thorn Blood elders!" Secretary Li speculated, inwardly anxious.

"No!" the fat man said, "He's already killed all the Thorn Blood elders!"

"What?" Secretary Li was completely shocked.

He found it hard to believe the fat man's words.

Although Cloud Blood City was not a big city, there were still dozens of heaven-level experts.

All of them killed? This was some ruthless person, so powerful? After all, their rebel army was already considered one of the strongest in the area, with only three heaven-level experts.

Now, a ruthless person had come and slaughtered all the Thorn Blood elders in Cloud Blood City? "Is this true?" Secretary Li still couldn't quite believe it.

"It's absolutely true, I've already passed this news to the leader!" the fat man said seriously.

If he wasn't sure the information was correct, he wouldn't dare to pass such news to the leader. The leader was a thorn in the side of the Thorn Blood, if the leader came to Cloud Blood City because of this and was ambushed by the Thorn Blood, he would have nowhere to be buried.

Secretary Li eyed the fat man, his gaze deep.

The fat man's scalp tingled.

Wu Xiang then said, "I believe Brother Chu."

Secretary Li's gaze kept changing, and finally he slowly said:

"He has slain the Thorn Blood elders and is rampaging in the city, he will definitely draw the Devilish God's attention.

We must hurry to the inner city and warn him to leave!

Secretary Li's idea was simple - go take a look at the truth. If it was true, then quickly help that person leave.

After all, that was the Devilish God. No matter how powerful a mortal, how could they contend with a god? In this Kuijin Realm, there were ninety-nine Devilish Gods stationed!

Mortals rashly offending the divine might would surely die without a doubt.

As for helping Wu Xiang receive Qi Yuan, it had already been pushed to the back of his mind.

That Qi Yuan, though he had some strength, having slain dozens of inner hall disciples of the Blood Tiger Gang, how could he compare to the terrifying expert rampaging in the inner city?

"Let's go!" Secretary Li said.

The fat man gritted his teeth, "I'll go too!"

Wu Xiang didn't speak, his silence indicating he had no objections.

The group of a few people hurried towards the inner city, their minds filled with many thoughts.

In the outer city area now, silence and clamor coexisted.

Some humans hid in their rooms, afraid to come out, while others dragged their families and ran out of the city.

To these, Secretary Li was already used to and unfazed.

"I hope the Devilish God hasn't acted yet, otherwise... that senior is in grave danger!"

The concept of the Devilish God being undefeatable was imprinted in their minds since birth.

After all, those terrifying Devilish Gods, not to mention their divine powers, just their towering hundreds-of-zhang tall bodies were not something the heaven-level Armament Masters who only wielded some weak earth and heaven forces could contend with.

"Secretary Li, don't worry, the Devilish Gods generally stay in seclusion cultivating, rarely descending," the fat man said.

The Devilish Gods were high and mighty, even heaven-level experts might never see one in their entire lives.

"If the Devilish God has descended, I'm afraid... he has already begun suppressing that senior.

That senior was able to slay the Thorn Blood elders, facing the Devilish God he might be able to hold out for a dozen breaths.

In those dozen breaths, the battle aftershocks will definitely be transmitted here," Wu Xiang made a reasonable inference.

If the Devilish God had descended, the aftershocks of the battle to slay that senior would be enough to be heard here.

This inference was quite reasonable, the only loophole being that the Devilish God was stronger than imagined, instantly killing that senior without even transmitting the sound of battle.

Of course, there was another possibility that Wu Xiang didn't even consider.

The three hurried towards the inner city, when suddenly, Secretary Li widened his eyes, gazing ahead, his face filled with disbelief.

"What is this?"

He looked utterly horrified.

Wu Xiang and the fat man also widened their eyes, their faces filled with disbelief.

The fat man's fleshy cheeks trembled uncontrollably.

For in front of them, at the entrance to the inner city, stood a towering colossus! A massive giant hundreds of zhang tall, dwarfing the imposing city walls before it like toys.

Darkish gray scales, a powerful physique, blood-red demonic horns standing upright, all manifesting the terrifying existence's great strength.

"The Devilish God!"

"This is the Devilish God Ominous Eyes!"

Glowing crimson pupils, like high-hanging lanterns, cold and merciless.

Secretary Li's gaze met those hundreds-of-zhang tall, horrifying eyes, his body trembling involuntarily.

This was the instinct of living beings! The Devilish God had truly descended! What were they to do? His mind was in turmoil, but suddenly, his spirit focused.

"No, this Devilish God is somehow different..."

Red Plum was different from ordinary women, as she had always wanted to seek out the legendary "Academy."

As for the "Academy," Zhao Yan only knew about it from what Red Plum had told him.

The master of the "Academy" was a mysterious existence, wandering between realms, recruiting disciples and compiling ancient texts.

Now, the master of the "Academy" was in the Kuijin Realm.

If they could meet the Academy master and join the Academy to serve the master, then they might be able to... obtain the master's protection, and even leave this realm.

Joining the mysterious Academy meant that she could escape this cage.

Offending the Blood Tiger Gang would be considered a major event for them, but in the grand scheme of history, it was just an insignificant little thing.

Reuniting with an old friend was like a long-awaited sweet rain after a drought.
