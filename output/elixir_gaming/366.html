Now he found this bank card number and password quite interesting.

It was a new kind of system.

You could call it technology, but it wasn't entirely technology.

"This spaceship only has this coffin girl, so she is nominally the owner of the ship.

The things on this ship belong to her property.

By senior, didn't he mean everything in this relic belonged to him?

Seeing this, the Purple Palace cultivator also took out his Sky Eyes.

"Fair," Qi Yuan said calmly.

He advocated peace and fairness.

He and Ning Tao had already taken the coffin.

The other things would naturally be divided among the other four people.

These four cultivators' minds worked quickly, rapidly understanding what Qi Yuan meant by fairness in his words, and divided up the Sky Eyes.

They divided them very quickly and evenly.

There was even one Sky Eye that couldn't be divided, so they split it into four parts.

They didn't want to be unfair and end up like those dead cultivators.

A few days later, on Dazzling Star.

A disheveled man sat high in the clouds, his hair flying, dense black eyeballs hidden within his hair.

Suddenly, countless rays of light flashed and burst out of the clouds.

The disheveled man let out a strange roar.

This sound was like the wailing of ghosts and howling of wolves.

The officials and nobles gathered in the great hall saw this scene and all had solemn expressions.

The terrifying expert in the clouds was clearly a great power from the Myriad Demon Realm.

Now nine-tenths of the power on Dazzling Star was controlled by the Myriad Demon Realm.

The king was merely a puppet.

The princess stood in a corner, looking at that demon in the sky with deep wariness in her eyes.

However, unlike the Demon Court demons occupying Dazzling Star, they were the original inhabitants of the Myriad Demon Realm, enslaved demons.

He naturally hated the Demon Court demons intensely.

"Damn it, if war breaks out, we citizens of Dazzling Star can only become cannon fodder!" The tall and slender princess gritted her teeth, her eyes filled with strong dissatisfaction.

Although Dazzling Star had been at war with Arching Star before.

But after so many years, the new king advocated recuperation and development.

But if the demons of the Myriad Demon Realm started a war, it would definitely be innocent Dazzling Star citizens who died.

Back then, when the Ancestor Demon disappeared, these demons from the upper realm descended, established the Demon Court, and enslaved myriad demons.

Now on Dazzling Star, they were just repeating old tricks.

"Fang Hun, can your grandfather help us?!" The princess looked at Fang Hun, her eyes pleading.

Fang Hun's grandfather was a powerful cultivator with superhuman abilities.

"My... grandfather probably can't." Fang Hun's face darkened.

If his grandfather could stop the Demon Court demons, they as ancient demons wouldn't have to live in secret realms, barely surviving.

"Even your grandfather can't?" The princess was dejected.

At this moment, a strange sound rang out.

The terrifying expert in the clouds suddenly shouted.

"Blood flows from Arching Star, heaven and earth dance together, in a hundred days, war begins!"

"War begins!"

"War begins!"

Those warmongers on Dazzling Star also frantically shouted.

"War!"

"War!"

"War!"

The sound spread like a tide, rising and spreading in the city.

Seeing this scene, the princess seemed to see broken spaceships, and land baptized by artillery fire and blood.

War... was finally about to begin.

And some intelligence agents hidden on Dazzling Star, upon learning this news, felt as if the sky was about to collapse.

Arching Star... was finished. The news was subsequently transmitted back to Arching Star.

The Seven Martial Star families and the royal family all became anxious at this moment.

Although Dazzling Star had supported the Ancient Witches before, the support was limited.

Now with war about to break out, could Arching Star alone resist? Moreover, there were rumors that behind Dazzling Star was an incredibly powerful Myriad Demon Realm.

The Myriad Demon Realm was far, far stronger than the cultivators of the Three Realms.

All strongholds had ships heading to the Origin Land, divided into twelve routes in total.

At this moment, Qi Yuan was lying on a lounge chair, looking extremely relaxed and comfortable.

He had a vague premonition that this game was coming to an end soon.

When they first entered the world of death energy, they carried out missions day and night, struggling on the edge of life and death.

Now, entering the Origin Land, such things would come to an end.

Once they became Martial Lords, they would change from chess pieces to chess players.

At that time, dozens of Martial Kings were trapped there.

Eventually, someone discovered that ancient martial artists were only weakened by half in that place.

Later, an army was formed by ancient martial artists to go and rescue them.

The other type was like Qi Yuan, without the Martial Lord imprint. Even if these peak Martial Kings were strong, their future prospects couldn't compare to those with the Martial Lord imprint.

These Martial Kings entering the Origin Land would undergo a hundred-day ancestral blood battle.

At that time, Martial Lords would personally act as examiners, observing their performance.

Those who performed exceptionally well would be granted more ancestral blood.

Different levels and amounts of ancestral blood meant different potentials for them.

Spirit Realm Martial Lord... he had never heard of this term before.

"I've never heard of this," Mu Yan said, "So I can't guarantee I can help you succeed."

He was a person who valued honesty.

He still had no clue about Spirit Realm Martial Lords.

Neither the Thunder Grand Venerable nor the other subjects in the Origin Land had discovered any information about Spirit Realm Martial Lords.

It was like a mystery.

She was about to say something when Mu Yan pulled her away.

The two came to the lower deck, and the monkey-faced woman couldn't help but say: "This Qi Yuan seems a bit ungrateful. Brother Mu Yan, with your talent, you'll definitely become a Great Martial Lord in the future! If he misses this chance, he might not even have the opportunity to see you again later."

Mu Yan's talent was indeed very strong.

Among all the Martial Kings heading to the Origin Land, his talent could enter the top thirty.

This ancestral blood battle would have thirty-six examiners.

Among them, three were chief examiners.

Mu Yan's blood ancestor was a Great Martial Lord and one of the examiners.

If they could get help from an examiner, even if their performance was a bit lacking, they could still be deemed "excellent".

"I can't guarantee this either. It all depends on one's own performance."

Mu Yan refused.

He didn't want to get entangled with the monkey-faced woman.

On the other side, Qi Yuan lay on the lounge chair, his eyes looking at the sky, occasionally glancing at the Black Sea, countless streams of information flowing.

"This place is not bad. There are traces of time everywhere.

Our ship seems to be sailing against time, from the present to the ancient, along the other shore of the river of time. It's very suitable for the Golden Canary."

The Golden Canary's divine realm was the same as time.

Bringing the Golden Canary here to comprehend time might rapidly increase its strength.
