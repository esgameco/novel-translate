"I won't sleep in the bridal chamber, I don't like lying on a hard board," Qi Yuan said seriously to the little bride behind him.

The little bride followed silently.

Qi Yuan jumped into the pit.

Qi Yuan immediately climbed out of the pit and saw the little bride knocked down by a rock.

He helped the little bride up and brushed the dirt off her.

"Sure enough, without eyes, she just can't help but fall down!"

During this time of being with the little bride, Qi Yuan had already discovered many of her attributes.

For example, she was easy to fall, and also easy to bump and knock things over in the house.

"In the future when I get married, I must find someone with eyes," Qi Yuan sighed.

He thought for a moment, then embraced the little bride like gathering clothes in the rain, and then crawled back into the pit.

The Yin-Yang Forbidden Land.

In a certain valley, dozens of coffins were arranged.

Crack.

The coffin lids flipped open.

Dozens of humanoid beings sat up.

They were both male and female, emanating a vast aura.

If Qi Yuan saw these people, he would definitely be dazzled.

They were all level 90 NPCs!

Even some were level 95 and above!

At this moment, the strongest of this group of humanoid beings, a man with a vertical pupil on his forehead, his gaze pierced through the Yin-Yang Forbidden Land, as if he had seen the Clothing Taboo.

He slowly spoke, his voice neither male nor female: "Another fellow traveler will soon arrive in the mortal world."

"Congratulations, unexpectedly, the evil demons from the outside world have penetrated the heavenly realm, and even someone has been able to step into the divine domain."

Since thousands of years ago, when the celestial gods disappeared, the Wuyue Continent had undergone a terrifying change.

Martial artists cultivated, from the Posterior Heaven (levels 0-9), Prior Heaven (levels 10-19), Master (levels 20-29), King (levels 30-49), Emperor (levels 50-69), Divine Realm (levels 70-89), to the God Realm.

Whenever a martial artist broke through the Emperor level and entered the Divine Realm, a fishing line would fall from the sky above, enter that martial artist's body, and suck out their brain and flesh.

In the end, the fishing line would leave, leaving only an empty human skin.

So, on the Wuyue Continent, for thousands of years, there have been almost no new powerful figures in the Divine Realm.

"Do you know his origins?" said a lady in white robes, who was a 97-level bigshot. In the distant past, she had a fleeting romance with the celestial gods.

"Elder Zang Hua, he is from the Clothing Taboo, and most likely relied on the resources of the Empress of the Bridal Robe to reach this step," an old man said, stealing a glance at the lady.

"Hmph, it seems that fellow Jinye has left a lot of good things for that stinky woman," Elder Zang Hua said with a hint of mockery.

Jinye was the name of the celestial god.

The others heard this and did not respond, not wanting to provoke Elder Zang Hua's wrath.

The man with the vertical pupil, with a towering figure, spoke, his voice like rumbling thunder: "Since he is about to enter the God Realm and is the new husband of the Empress of the Bridal Robe, he should also be our fellow traveler.

Three hundred years from now, when the celestial dog swallows the moon, that will be the weakest time for the evil demons from the outside world. That will be the time for us to attack the Earthly Forbidden Land."

The evil demons from the outside world occupied the Earthly Forbidden Land, causing chaos in the mortal world.

These former Divine Realm, now God Realm experts, could only hide in the Yin-Yang Forbidden Land and the Five Elements Forbidden Land.

They have been cultivating in secret for thousands of years, accumulating strength, just waiting for the time when the evil demons from the outside world are at their weakest, to hold a great demonic subjugation and attack the Earthly Forbidden Land together.

Otherwise, if the evil demons from the outside world completely occupy the Earthly Forbidden Land and can appear in the Wuyue Continent in their full form, even with the Yin-Yang Forbidden Land and the Five Elements Forbidden Land, they would be unable to stop the evil demons, and would only perish.
