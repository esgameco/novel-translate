Qi Yuan looked over.

In the scene, a burly man reeking of alcohol was punching and kicking an old man. Finally, the burly man snatched some broken silver coins from the old man's embrace and kicked the old man before leaving.

Qi Yuan watched, unmoved.

"Young friend, look again." The elder waved his hand, and the scene changed.

The same burly man who had beaten the old man was now extremely filial towards him, carefully feeding him, pouring tea, helping him urinate, and wiping his body.

"It is because we have promulgated the law of filial piety that people in the world have begun to become filial. So we have come here to save the world."

Hearing this, Qi Yuan calmly smiled: "This is just a sham filial piety."

The elder, not hurried, said, "Young friend, do you know that if you had abolished the law of filial piety, the man in the scene would have killed his father that day. The responsibility for all this would fall on you."

Qi Yuan looked at the elder with suspicion: "Is it your head or my head that has a problem? A demonic entity from another realm is actually starting to morally blackmail me?"

"Sigh." Hearing this, the elder showed a helpless expression. "Young friend, we do not wish to be your enemy. But everything I said is true. We really are here to save this world."

Qi Yuan did not speak, he was on alert.

Seeing this, the elder continued:

"We fish and devour the flesh and blood of the masses, really out of necessity. Because... once we return to our proper rest, we will turn into terrifying demonic scourges.

Demonic scourges have no rationality and will only slaughter any living being they see. The strength of the demonic scourges will reach the fifth realm and wreak havoc on the world.

The appearance of any demonic scourge will plunge this world into ruin, and no one can stop it.

So we had no choice but to fish in the human world, consume flesh and blood, in order to extend our lifespan a little, so that the world would suffer less disaster."

The elder's face showed a sorrowful and compassionate expression, seeming truly concerned for the world.

"After we came to this realm, we also feared bringing disaster to this world.

So we specifically came to the Realm of Extremes.

Here, one day is ten years in the outside world.

The talents and martial artists that appear in the outside world in ten years are enough for us to replenish our lifespan.

The demonic scourges will not truly appear.

I do this with a heavy heart.

Otherwise, why would we stay in this Realm of Extremes instead of going directly to the human world?"

The elder pleaded earnestly.

What he said seemed to make sense.

Once their lifespan was exhausted, they would turn into demonic scourges.

And the strength of the demonic scourges, from what he said, would be even stronger than them.

If there were one or two of them in the world, the human world would truly fall into calamity.

Qi Yuan stared into the old man's eyes and suddenly said, directly pointing to the key issue: "Since you will turn into demonic scourges after returning to your proper rest, how did you deal with the demonic scourges in the world you came from? Or how did you prevent the demonic scourges from appearing?"

Sure enough, the old man's gaze froze upon hearing this, and a painful expression appeared in his eyes.

Recalling the law of filial piety and the old man's words, Qi Yuan immediately understood. He looked at the old man and said word by word: "If returning to your proper rest leads to the appearance of demonic scourges, doesn't that mean..."

"You are not returning to your proper rest, so demonic scourges will not appear!"

Qi Yuan's words brought painful memories to the old man.

After a long while, he raised his head and looked at Qi Yuan, his expression agitated.

"Do you know? I have been fighting for my family since I was seventeen years old. I have shed blood for my family, I have mined seventeen secret silver mines for my family, and I have even faced attacks from peers of the same level, with more than seven hundred large and small injuries on my body.

But why, why, when I am old, with only a few years left before death, are my family members discussing how to kill me?

They have even invited people from the Exorcism Association."

The old man was hysterical.

"They are the children I love the most, the ones I raised with my own hands.

And what do they want to do? I just want to return to my proper rest, is that wrong?

If it were you, would you accept such a fate?"

He looked at Qi Yuan, extremely agitated.

"What a boring setting." Qi Yuan looked at the old man and slowly said, "You are just a villain, the big boss I should slaughter. I'm not interested in how much suffering you've endured or how much injustice you've faced.

Even if you turn into a pitiful, weeping beauty lamenting your miserable and forced life, I won't be overwhelmed by empathy, nor will I be particularly interested.

What I'm most interested in is using my great sword to chop off your dog's head!"

After all, he's just a big boss, so just kill him directly.

The old man's face turned gloomy: "Are you sure you want to be our enemy? Have you prepared for the consequences of doing so?"

The old man did not want to fight Qi Yuan.

Because their condition was not good.

If they fought, their lifespan would be greatly reduced.

Qi Yuan's strength was far beyond the group of heavenly deities.

"It's not that I want to be your enemy, but it's destined that I will defeat you bastards!

I am the young man who slays evil dragons!"

Qi Yuan said and immediately attacked! "You're courting death!" The old man was furious.

"Hmph, you're all old fogeys, while I'm young and vigorous!" Qi Yuan had already found the weakness of these demonic entities from another realm.

Their lifespan was not much.

And now, all the black-robed guards had been slain.

As long as Qi Yuan blocked the only entrance to the Realm of Extremes, these old fogeys would not be able to replenish their lifespan.

And Qi Yuan would continue to fight them.

These demonic entities from another realm would then be unable to return to their proper rest.

Of course, this method is easy to think of, but that doesn't mean anyone can do it.

Qi Yuan finished speaking, holding his great sword and standing at the bronze gate, the only entrance to the Realm of Extremes.

He lazily looked at the crowd: "Those from the heavens return to the heavens, those from the human world return to the human world, I'll come and act as the South Heaven Gate Guardian!"

Qi Yuan stood with his sword, blocking the gate.

But at this moment, several cold voices came from the depths.

"Overestimating your abilities!"

"Hmph, I want one-tenth of his flesh and blood."

"Haha, how dare he block us in the divine domain!"

As the voices fell, five powerful figures suddenly appeared before Qi Yuan.

These five figures had terrifying auras, similar to the scholarly and refined old man.

They gathered with the scholarly and refined old man, pressing towards Qi Yuan.

Qi Yuan looked at these six people, holding his treasure sword, his eyes filled with puzzlement: "Is this all the demonic entities from another realm? This boss is too easy to defeat, isn't it?"

"Hmph." The scholarly and refined old man let out a cold laugh.

At this moment, the sky of the Realm of Extremes suddenly darkened.

Qi Yuan looked up and saw countless glowing eyes in the sky, silently staring at him.

However, the owners of these eyes seemed to be in a special state and unable to act rashly.

In Qi Yuan's view, they were likely in a dormant state.

Once the dormant state was broken, their lifespan would be depleted much faster.

This terrifying sight made Qi Yuan shudder involuntarily: "So you've brought a team, haven't you? A team of big bosses?"

A team of big bosses?

This was too much! Qi Yuan immediately felt discouraged.

"It's not too late to beg for mercy now!" The old man spoke, looking at Qi Yuan coldly.

Qi Yuan didn't care, though he felt discouraged, he still spoke firmly: "It seems that today I will do a little 'old man killing'!"

Qi Yuan held his sword, blocking the gate of the Realm of Extremes.

The six 119-level experts simultaneously attacked Qi Yuan.

Tyrannical power!

In an instant, Qi Yuan activated his own great skill.

He strengthened his body, doubling his own qualities.

Slashing Heaven Sword Technique! He swung his sword, and the six old experts felt a great threat.

They retreated in succession.

"So strong!"

"I have to use my origin power!"

These six experts suddenly displayed the same ability as the scholarly and refined old man before.

"The more origin power we use, the less lifespan we have left. We can no longer hold back, kill him!"

The six experts attacked again, this time all using their strongest techniques.

Facing the attacks of these six experts far surpassing his own strength, Qi Yuan was unafraid.

In the Ascension Platform, Qi Yuan had experienced countless battles, some of which involved simultaneously facing three thousand experts of the same level.

His combat experience had reached a superb level.

He held his sword, attacking while defending!

"Kill!"

A sword strike, and the air rippled with blood energy.

The six experts' blood and qi surged.

However, the white-haired old man suddenly laughed loudly: "Meat, the meat is so delicious! It was worth it to forcibly use my origin power, it was worth it!"

Somehow, he now had a bloody piece of meat in his hand.

Qi Yuan looked at his chest, and there was a bloody wound that he didn't even know when it appeared.

He was injured.

The blood was dripping.

On the first day he arrived, he was already injured.

The old man held the piece of meat, his eyes filled with greed.

He stuck out his snake-like tongue and swallowed Qi Yuan's piece of flesh.

Suddenly, he widened his eyes, looking at Qi Yuan: "No taste!"

The other five demonic entities from another realm also froze, not understanding.

"His flesh and blood are not flesh and blood at all, they cannot replenish our lifespan!" The white-haired old man was furious, "What are you?"

He had just used so much origin power, and his lifespan had been greatly reduced again.

He originally wanted to seize a piece of Qi Yuan's flesh to supplement himself.

But unexpectedly, Qi Yuan's flesh had no taste.

Qi Yuan looked at the six demonic entities: "My flesh and blood have no taste, that's great."

This made Qi Yuan feel surprised, but also normal.

He raised his sword, looking at the six demonic entities: "I'm young and vigorous, I'll outlive you old fogeys!"

The only regret was that he couldn't log off and secretly eat a braised pork rice, or else he would have even more energy.

"No, ordinary methods can't hurt him.

I don't have much time left, I have to kill him and replenish my flesh and blood.

Everyone, don't hold back any longer, use your origin power to kill him!"

She wanted to become stronger.

If she were stronger, the pain the master endured would be lessened.
