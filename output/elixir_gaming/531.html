Several bandits rushed forward, surrounding Qi Yuan, Liu Chuchun, and the others.

Qi Yuan's eyes were not filled with fear, but rather a carefree contentment.

"If I had an abnormal thought process, I would definitely say, why only kill the men and not the women?"

"Nonsense!" the one-armed bandit roared angrily.

The surrounding bandits wielding large knives suddenly started hacking at the horses of Qi Yuan and the others.

These bandits were all Mysterious-rank Armored Soldiers, considered elite members within the Blood Cloud Bandits, serving as squad leaders.

Dealing with ordinary passing merchants would be easy for them.

Liu Chuchun's face showed a look of disgust.

She had always detested these evil and sinful bandits.

With her signal, the two Judgment Enforcers in black robes behind her acted in unison.

A sword beam flashed, and the four attacking bandits were immediately torn apart, their bodies and heads separated.

Between Earth-rank and Mysterious-rank Armored Soldiers, there was an insurmountable chasm.

Qi Yuan saw this scene and a trace of regret flashed in his eyes.

"It's a pity the people weren't killed by me. It's a bit of a waste."

Although these few were weak, their experience was still experience, right? Liu Chuchun kept her eyes on Qi Yuan, her gaze gentle: "Big Brother Qi, your heart is too kind. These people deserved to die, you don't need to be saddened by their deaths."

She mistook Qi Yuan's regret for sadness.

On the other side, the one-armed bandit's gaze suddenly changed: "Two Earth-rank Armored Soldiers?"

His mind was quick, rapidly turning.

"How about we call it a day, gentlemen?" the one-armed bandit said fearlessly.

Everyone knew they were from the Blood Cloud Bandits.

Out on the road, those Armored Soldiers would usually show them some face, fearing them.

Now, their people would have a hard time slaying those two Earth-rank Armored Soldiers, so it was better to call a truce.

This kind of thing happened often.

Out on the road, although they should seek vengeance, it's better to avoid injury if possible.

Hearing this, the young Miss Zhao's eyes were filled with panic, and she immediately broke down in tears, the tears falling like rain. Her expression was lonely and helpless, eliciting pity: "Please save us, they are the Blood Cloud Bandits, and also people of Lord Liu!"

Liu Chuchun's eyes flashed with a trace of compassion, and she said in a lofty manner to Qi Yuan: "You see, these helpless victims are the ones worthy of our mercy, these bandits are not worth regretting their deaths."

After "educating" Qi Yuan, Liu Chuchun felt a sense of accomplishment.

She looked at the one-armed bandit, her voice cold: "All Blood Cloud Bandits must die!"

The two Judgment Enforcers beside her immediately left their horses, their long swords in hand, charging into the Blood Cloud Bandits.

Swords flashed, blood sprayed.

The two Judgment Enforcers entered a realm of no one, giving these bandits no chance to survive.

One sword strike severed a bandit's knife and body, another beheaded another bandit.

In just dozens of breaths, these bandits were cleared away like weeds.

The one-armed bandit, seeing the situation turn grim, wielded his knife and fled hastily.

The two Judgment Enforcers were entangled by the remaining bandits, and with the one-armed bandit also being an Earth-rank Armored Soldier, it was difficult to catch up to him.

In the end, all the bandits were slain, only the one-armed bandit escaped.

The old master and young Miss Zhao had an expression of having survived a calamity.

Especially the young Miss Zhao, her snow-white face still had two tear tracks, her eyes brimming with water, extremely delicate, able to evoke a strong protective instinct, of course, for the "lowly men", it would be a frenzy of ravaging.

"Thank you, heroes, this old man has a small gift, please accept it!" The old master Zhao bowed deeply, his posture very low.

He produced three thousand taels of silver.

Not much, but not little either.

"Upholding justice without seeking reward is our principle." Liu Chuchun spoke, directly refusing.

She didn't lack money.

The Judgment organization also didn't lack funds.

So she directly refused.

What she admired the most was the God of Judgment from a century ago.

Hearing this, the old master Zhao was somewhat stunned.

But soon, his smile grew even brighter: "Miss, your lofty character, unbound by worldly things, is admirable!"

Since she didn't like gold and silver, she must like to hear pretty words.

Liu Chuchun nodded, looking in the direction the one-armed bandit had fled: "It's a pity... one got away. Big Brother Qi, this matter has been satisfactorily resolved, we should be going now."

The old master Zhao and the young Miss Zhao both heaved a sigh of relief.

But at this moment, Qi Yuan spoke: "The matter is not yet over."

"Huh?" Liu Chuchun was taken aback.

"There are still evil people who haven't been killed." Qi Yuan said calmly.

"That person is an Earth-rank Armored Soldier, he ran too fast, we couldn't catch up." Liu Chuchun said, somewhat disappointed.

"No, I'm referring to these two." Qi Yuan's gaze fell on the old master Zhao and the young Miss Zhao.

Liu Chuchun was taken aback.

The young Miss Zhao's delicate face turned deathly pale, about to cry again.

At this time, one of the silent Judgment Enforcers spoke up.

"Just now, before we arrived, they were already loudly calling for help, and also... pointed out the connection between the bandits and the bloodstained ones, they were afraid the bandits would spare us."

The other Judgment Enforcer also spoke: "During our battle with the bandits, they were preparing to flee, and only when they saw us gaining the absolute upper hand did they choose to stay."

"No, the noble ones are wronging us... the young miss only cried out in fear." The young Miss Zhao was on the verge of tears.

The old master Zhao hurriedly said, "Xingyu was at a loss for words, causing trouble for the noble ones, it is our..."

They quickly apologized and admitted their mistakes, their attitude extremely humble.

Liu Chuchun narrowed her eyes, thoughtfully considering: "Being frightened and losing one's wits is also normal."

"Chuchun, if we were just ordinary people, then... it would have been us who died." Qi Yuan said calmly.

One of the Judgment Enforcers also said softly, "We came here without revealing our auras, indistinguishable from ordinary people, yet they chose to call for help... what intentions do they have, do you need to wonder?"

The young Miss Zhao and the old master Zhao turned deathly pale, loudly claiming their innocence.

Liu Chuchun was somewhat hesitant.

"If only the God of Judgment was still here, then we would know their true intentions." Liu Chuchun sighed.

Now, all these words were just subjective judgments.

Even if reasonable, they were still just conjectures.

She had already believed Qi Yuan and the Judgment Enforcers, but without proof, she couldn't get over that mental hurdle.

However, as soon as she finished speaking, a flash of light appeared.

The heads of the old master Zhao and the young Miss Zhao fell to the ground.

Their eyes still held expressions of extreme terror and disbelief.

Liu Chuchun's gaze suddenly fell on Qi Yuan, her eyes filled with disbelief: "Did you kill them?"

She was a bit dazed. Qi Yuan, who didn't even want to kill ants, had just killed these two? "Towards kind ants, I am very gentle. Towards evil people, I never hold back my hand." Qi Yuan said righteously.

This demeanor had a touch of the feeling that stray animals are the real family.

Liu Chuchun looked deeply at Qi Yuan, a slight knot forming in her heart, but she took a deep breath and said, "Big Brother Qi, you acted rashly, you should have clarified the facts before killing."

But the people were already dead, so she didn't dwell on it further.

It's just that other thoughts began to emerge in her heart.
