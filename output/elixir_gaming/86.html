About tens of breaths passed.

Qi Yuan stopped in his tracks and held his breath.

"This is Lü Fei's room, right?"

"White Moon, I'm here!"

Qi Yuan stood outside the room, his body hidden in the darkness.

He listened carefully.

Unfortunately, only the sound of water came from the room.

Qi Yuan listened for a long time, but gained nothing.

However, Qi Yuan did not give up, he was very patient.

That was a profound-level cultivation technique, and the more profound-level techniques he could find, the more complete his "Qi Yuan Classic" would be.

And the more cultivation techniques he mastered, the more it would benefit him.

For example, with his eyes, he could see different levels of cultivation techniques and obtain different information, and see different loopholes.

For instance, before obtaining the profound-level techniques, when he looked at a jade-level technique, he could see seven loopholes and improve the cultivation.

After obtaining the profound-level techniques, when he looked at that jade-level technique again, he could see ten loopholes.

Qi Yuan even felt that if a heaven-level technique was placed in front of him now, he would not be able to see its loopholes.

The more cultivation techniques he understood, the more information he could see.

The night grew deeper, and Qi Yuan kept his ears open, listening intently.

He stood in the darkness, with people passing by from time to time, but no one noticed his presence.

After a while, Qi Yuan's eyes lit up.

"Has it begun?"

He finally heard Lü Fei's inner voice during her cultivation.

"Chaotic Boundless, qi flowing through the cinnabar field, the emperor's way and..."

Qi Yuan widened his eyes, listening carefully, afraid of missing anything.

After some time, a satisfied expression appeared in Qi Yuan's eyes.

"The Yin Maiden's Profound Cultivation? How malicious!"

Qi Yuan obtained the profound-level technique "The Yin Maiden's Profound Cultivation". This technique was worthy of the name of the Demonic Desire Sect.

To cultivate this technique, one needs to cultivate with seven men who are deeply in love with oneself, and kill them at the most passionate moment.

"The people on the Blue Star aren't even this miserable, this Lü Fei is simply too much.

Not only does she need to lick, but she'll also lose her life while licking! Such a malicious person is not worthy of being my White Moon, the wild wolf is better, he's a rich and handsome man with many cultivation techniques!"

After stealing the cultivation technique, Qi Yuan's feelings for Lü Fei instantly disappeared, feeling a sense of ruthlessness.

Just as he was about to leave.

Suddenly, a man in a black robe appeared in Qi Yuan's sight.

"A thief?"

"An adulterer?"

The man in the black robe entered Lü Fei's room.

Qi Yuan crouched outside the room, his thoughts racing.

For some reason, the man in the black robe gave Qi Yuan a heart-pounding feeling.

"Could it be the Demonic Immortal Elder?"

Qi Yuan thought, feeling that it would be better to quickly flee.

With his current strength, he didn't know if he could defeat the Demonic Immortal Elder.

If the Demonic Immortal Elder discovered him, he might be in danger, so it's better to flee.

After thinking about it, he listened for a while outside the room, found that there seemed to be a technique to isolate sound, and then quietly slipped away.

...

Two days passed quickly.

During these two days, many things happened.

The first thing was that this Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm was somewhat different from the first Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm.

In the first Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm, only cultivators under the age of thirty could enter.

But in this Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm, it was discovered in these two days that cultivators over a hundred years old could also enter.

This caused a stir.

Many princes without the backing of the three major sects united to change the previous rules of the Dragon Seizing Competition, allowing Yuandan cultivators to participate as well.

This caused a lot of waves in the palace.

Later, a Yuandan Venerable from the Divine Light Sect killed a Yuandan cultivator, and this storm was finally calmed.

This proposal was also rejected by the three major sects.

The Dragon Seizing Competition would still follow the previous rules.

Many princes were dissatisfied, but faced with the three major sects, they were powerless.

At this time, countless Foundation Establishment cultivators gathered at the entrance of the Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm.

Various princes gathered here, some joyful, some worried.

Suddenly, a divine light flashed above the Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm.

All the cultivators present felt a suppressive and powerful aura.

They saw an old white-haired man appear in the sky.

"Greetings, Master!" Kong Fulou quickly paid his respects.

"Greetings, Uncle!" Although Qi Yuan was a killer of old men, he was actually very respectful and caring towards the elderly.

"Greetings, True Lord Taigang!" All the cultivators on the ground paid their respects, looking at the old man in the sky with awe.

Because that old man was the peak master of the Divine Light Sect's Five Lights Peak, the True Lord Taigang! A Demonic Immortal-level expert.

In the entire Great Chu, he was considered an absolute top expert.

"The Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm has encountered some twists and turns, and cultivators under the age of a hundred can also enter.

To prevent some cultivators from sneaking in secretly, this time I will be stationed at the entrance of the Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm," said True Lord Taigang.

Immediately, the faces of many princes turned pale, while the three princes supported by the three major sects, especially Dong Xian, showed excited expressions.

Originally, his foundation was very shallow, and if people were allowed to sneak in, he would be even further from the throne.

Kong Fulou stood next to Qi Yuan, with a smug expression: "With Master here, there will surely be no accidents in this Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm!"

Qi Yuan didn't have too much feeling, he was thinking about the mysterious expert who seemed to be a Demonic Immortal that he saw last night, wondering who it was.

After about the time of an incense stick, the entrance of the Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm was completely opened.

A huge amount of spiritual energy gushed out, and the originally mundane city outside immediately became filled with a majestic spiritual energy.

This spiritual energy permeated the area, and for the next few decades, the spiritual energy here would be quite abundant.

Ordinary people who live here long-term will have their illnesses disappear and their lifespan extended.

Some wandering cultivators will also choose to come here and build their dwellings to cultivate.

"Smells nice." Qi Yuan was not impressed by the spiritual energy here, as it was not as abundant as on the Seven-Color Peak.

As the Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm opened, a rich floral fragrance permeated the air.

Qi Yuan smelled the thick flower scent.

At this time, Dong Xian explained: "The Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm is said to be an external secret realm.

For some reason, it drifted to our Great Chu nation.

In this secret realm, there are bird songs and flower fragrances, with all kinds of rare flowers and grasses.

Centuries ago, when our Great Chu nation's ancestors entered the first secret realm, they once saw a crystal coffin, in which a special being was sleeping.

Our ancestors suspected that this special being was the master of the secret realm.

Unfortunately, our ancestors exhausted countless efforts, but could not open the crystal coffin.

Even, it seemed to have triggered the formation of the crystal coffin, and that crystal coffin was teleported away.

I wonder if in this Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm, we can see that crystal coffin again.

That should be the most precious treasure in the Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm. Whoever obtains it will immediately become the emperor of Great Chu."

This information, Dong Xian partly knew from the imperial archives, and partly from what Elder Qi had told him, such as the Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm coming from the external world.

After all, Great Chu obtained the Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm...
