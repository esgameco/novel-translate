An indiscriminate punch directly fell into the mortal realm.

The speed of this punch was not very fast, but its power was infinite.

Many of the Yuan Dan elders of the Fighting God Sect were greatly shocked.

"Demon! It's definitely a demon!"

"He's come to destroy our sect!"

"Sect Master, kill this scoundrel!"

The expression of Saint Fighter True Lord was even more shocked.

"Ahhh!"

The spiritual power on his body poured out as if it cost nothing, and the divine technique Heavenly Lotus surged towards the blood-red sky.

For a moment, the sky was a mix of red and white.

"Those who trespass into the Fighting God Sect shall die!"

"Those who slander this god shall be killed without mercy!"

The Ten Kills Tyrant Fist didn't follow any logic.

This punch had traces of immortal techniques and divine powers, as well as traces of martial arts and skills.

The killing intent hidden and concealed by Ning Tao erupted at this moment, condensing into a single punch.

This punch was like the queen of an insect colony searching for its kind.

Crack!

Saint Fighter True Lord's divine technique shattered directly.

The powerful killing intent of the Ten Kills Tyrant Fist rushed straight towards his eyes and ears.

In an instant, he felt the wailing of ghosts and howling of wolves, seeming to see countless piles of white bones and corpses.

Saint Fighter True Lord was known for being decisive in killing, but he had never seen such mountains of corpses before.

Blood gushed uncontrollably from his ears, mouth, and nostrils.

Now he no longer looked like an immortal cultivator, but more like a small mortal old man.

His face was full of disbelief and terror.

"What kind of monster are you?!"

"I am a god, a great god!"

Qi Yuan threw out the red certificate again.

Saint Fighter True Lord was confused and powerless in his heart.

He didn't understand why such a powerful and mysterious person would tease him with a mortal world certificate. Before he could think more, the terrifying Ten Kills Tyrant Fist directly pierced through his body and rushed towards the ground.

Boom! As the punch landed, a blood shadow appeared, and killing intent boiled.

Those elders and stewards in the Fighting God Tower all felt a sense of doomsday approaching.

"Those who abuse and kill ten mortals shall die!"

Qi Yuan's voice came at this time, like the voice of a heavenly god.

In an instant, killing intent surged on more than 99% of the elders, stewards, and disciples of the Fighting God Sect.

Behind them was a small blood curtain.

This blood curtain was like a coordinate, attracting the Ten Kills Tyrant Fist.

The elders and disciples in the tower had their bodies continuously shattered, like firecrackers.

Even if they exhausted all their magical power, they couldn't resist, and could only let this punch annihilate them.

Even among the mortals who had not yet entered the sect and came to seek teachers, eight were killed by the Ten Kills Tyrant Fist.

The crowd was shaken and in chaos again.

In just a few breaths, the Fighting God Tower became like purgatory, with a layer of dense blood mist rising.

Of the disciples and elders of the Fighting God Sect, less than one in a hundred survived.

Qi Yuan stood under the blood-colored sky curtain, his expression calm, murmuring: "The sects in the game are still a bit weak.

Not like the Palace of Light, where I still had to write essays and carefully plan.

But the feeling of not liking beef is really not bad."

At this time, Qi Yuan's eyes scanned the remaining survivors through his mask.

Ordinary people and even immortal cultivators who were examined by this gaze couldn't help but be startled.

The body of the Nascent Soul stage wandering cultivator outside the blood curtain also trembled violently.

"What realm is this exactly? Is it Purple Palace? But it doesn't seem like it, this isn't an origin divine technique.

But if it's not an origin divine technique, how can it have such power?

Saint Fighter True Lord was comparable to late-stage Nascent Soul, yet he couldn't even withstand one punch."

His mind was in chaos, shocked, confused, and terrified.

The Fighting God Sect, which had been passed down for ten thousand years, was destroyed in this moment.

Then the man in the blood robe spoke, his voice calm but with legal authority.

"I am a certified god!"

"If any more immortal cultivation sects dare to challenge the dignity of gods like the Fighting God Sect, they shall be killed without mercy!"

The voice rolled out, entering the ears of tens of thousands of ordinary people present, as well as those wandering cultivators.

Hearing this, the Nascent Soul wandering cultivator's expression froze, then he muttered bitterly: "Such a domineering god, is he destroying the Fighting God Sect to warn us outsiders?"

This Nascent Soul wandering cultivator thought a lot.

This "god" should be a native of Gong Xing.

After coming out of seclusion, he found that there were many more immortal cultivators on Gong Xing.

To ensure his authority and control,

He acted boldly, destroying the Fighting God Sect to establish his might.

As for the sect's name being inappropriate or "mental illness", those were just excuses.

When such a powerful being acts, there must be deep meaning behind it.

This was destroying the Fighting God Sect to warn other sects.

"It seems we immortal cultivators also need to keep a low profile."

The Nascent Soul wandering cultivator took a deep look at this place, then his figure dispersed and disappeared.

Half a day later.

In the main hall, several figures flickered, emitting subtle auras.

The leader, completely shrouded in a black robe, made it impossible to see his face.

"What do you all think about the destruction of the Fighting God Sect?"

The black-robed man spoke, his voice hoarse, as he looked around at the men and women hiding their identities.

These cultivators were Nascent Soul cultivators from the Three Realms.

The destruction of the Fighting God Sect alarmed all directions, and they also woke up from their seclusion.

"This person is not a cultivator from the Three Realms, he should be a native, and his actions should be to establish authority," said a female Nascent Soul cultivator, her voice calm and unperturbed.

"To establish authority by destroying a sect, how domineering!"

"His strength should be comparable to the Purple Palace realm!"

At this, the cultivators present all changed their expressions slightly.

The Purple Palace realm was almost the peak of the lower realms.

In general cultivation worlds, reaching Purple Palace meant being able to dominate a realm.

Above that was the Yin Spirit realm, which could ascend to the upper realms.

The Three Realms were adjacent to each other and interconnected.

In recent thousands of years, spiritual energy had been depleted, and Purple Palace cultivators had become legends.

Nascent Soul was already the peak.

Now, encountering a Purple Palace cultivator, how could they not be shocked?

"It's a pity that Saint Fighter True Lord died, if he had brought the sect's supreme treasure to this realm, how could he have been killed by a mere Purple Palace cultivator?" A man in green robes spoke, his voice cold and stern.

The founders of their sects were all Yin Spirits who had ascended to the upper realms.

These founders left behind supreme treasures to protect their sects.

So, even facing a Purple Palace cultivator, they still had the power to fight.

Facing the fierce person who destroyed the Fighting God Sect, they were afraid, but not too afraid.

Of course, what he didn't know was that Saint Fighter True Lord actually had a supreme treasure, but didn't have time to use it.

Even if he had used it, the outcome couldn't have been changed.

"He is after all a living Purple Palace cultivator, we need to be careful and cautious," a female cultivator spoke.

The other cultivators nodded.

"With him here, our plans... may have become more difficult," the leading black-robed man spoke, his voice hoarse.

The other cultivators fell silent, seeming to be pondering something.

The cultivators from the Three Realms coming to Gong Xing were certainly not here to do good deeds and teach immortal techniques.

For example, these dozen or so Nascent Soul cultivators present represented ten major sects, and the ancestors of these sects had long been in collusion.

"The Sky-Overturning Plan cannot fail, and the new world must appear!" the woman said coldly, her voice carrying killing intent.

The spiritual energy in the Three Realms was becoming more and more depleted.
