"Indeed, without playing games, going to bed early and getting up early, I can enjoy this beautiful morning."

At this time, Jiang Lingsu's voice came: "Elder Brother, we have all finished our morning cultivation."

Jiang Lingsu continued, "But compared to the past few days, Elder Brother, you have made progress.

"It's not bad for you to take care of me." Qi Yuan said, "The sentence I like to say the most is 'If you are rich and noble, don't forget me'."

Jiang Lingsu gave Qi Yuan a disdainful look.

And Qi Yuan thought of something, took out the cultivation technique jade slip: "Sister, your jade slip, I'm returning it to you, thank you."

Jiang Lingsu took the jade slip, with a smile at the corners of her mouth: "Elder Brother, have you created your own cultivation technique?"

"Of course." Qi Yuan said confidently, "It's just not perfect yet, I can only cultivate it to the Qi Refinement Realm, I need to improve it to the Foundation Establishment Realm."

Jiang Lingsu heard this, paused for a moment, and then laughed even more happily: "In just two days, Elder Brother has created his own cultivation technique, truly a heaven-born genius!"

She said this insincerely.

Because this is an impossible thing.

Unless, rather than saying he created the cultivation technique, it would be more accurate to say he modified someone else's cultivation technique and claimed it as his own.

Of course, this is also very impressive.

"Do you want to modify the cultivation technique I created?" Qi Yuan said seriously, "The cultivation techniques you all cultivate have too many problems."

But it may become the resource of a certain existence.

Qi Yuan naturally would not cultivate such a cultivation technique.

"Thank you for your kindness, Elder Brother, but I don't need to." Jiang Lingsu refused.

The Fa Decision she cultivated was a Heaven-level Fa Decision, and there was no way she would modify it.

"Elder Brother, from your attire, it seems you are preparing to go down the mountain?"

"En." Qi Yuan nodded, "I'm going to create a Foundation Establishment cultivation technique, but unfortunately I don't understand much about Foundation Establishment, especially the grade of Foundation Establishment, so I'm going to listen to a big lecture about Foundation Establishment down the mountain."

Jiang Lingsu heard this and was even more convinced that what the Elder Brother had said before was all just a joke.

To create a cultivation technique without understanding much about Foundation Establishment.

Would such a cultivation technique really be able to be cultivated?

Before leaving, Jiang Lingsu kindly reminded, "Elder Brother, a cultivation technique is the foundation of a cultivator, you must never casually modify it."

Half an hour later.

Qi Yuan was sitting in the cultivation hall of the big lecture.

He came early and found a good seat.

Later, many disciples came in one after another, and when they saw Qi Yuan, they all had puzzled expressions.

They whispered in low voices, "He's wearing a purple robe, he's a true disciple, why is he coming to attend this big lecture?"

"This is not an ordinary purple robe, look at the nine golden lines on his collar, he's not an ordinary true disciple, but a peak sect leader disciple!"

"What?"

The people here looked at Qi Yuan with awe and puzzlement.

After all, this kind of big lecture is usually only attended by outer sect disciples and servant disciples, inner sect disciples are relatively few, let alone true disciples.

In the Divine Light Sect, disciples are divided into true disciples and formal disciples, as well as servant disciples.

Servant disciples have the lowest status, they can cultivate but have to do servant duties.

Formal disciples are further divided into inner sect disciples and outer sect disciples, both of whom have formally entered a peak.

And once an inner sect disciple takes a elder as their master, they will become a true disciple.

There are also differences among true disciples, this difference mainly reflects the strength of the master.

And like the peak sect leader disciple, that is the true disciple among true disciples, even ordinary elders don't dare to offend them. Such a peak sect leader disciple, with the peak master's guidance, also has extraordinary talents.

About half an incense stick's worth of time passed, and the teaching steward who was supposed to give the lecture finally arrived.

He was an old man whose body was half sunken into the ground.

When he saw Qi Yuan, he was slightly taken aback, but then a look of smugness flashed across his mind.

He felt that it was because his lectures were so good that even this chief disciple had come to listen to his class.

He decided that this time he would exert two hundred percent of his strength in the lecture, to make it grand and impressive, definitely to shock Qi Yuan.

This was what is commonly known as "empty grandeur".

"I am called Xu Renshan, and today it will be I who will teach you all the key points about foundation establishment."

After Xu Renshan said this, the disciples below all became serious.

Although their cultivation was still far from foundation establishment, getting advance knowledge about foundation establishment was very important to them.

"Above the Qi Refining stage, there is the Foundation Establishment stage. Becoming a Foundation Establishment cultivator means you can become a steward of our Divine Light Sect and enjoy the offerings."

Xu Renshan said, with a nostalgic look in his eyes.

In the past, he had also almost failed to establish his foundation.

"Foundation Establishment also has different levels.

You all should know that Foundation Establishment requires a Foundation Establishment Treasure, which is the foundation for establishing one's foundation."

Xu Renshan spoke eloquently.

Immediately, a disciple responded, "I know, Foundation Establishment Treasures are divided into upper, middle, and lower grades, with the upper grade being the best and the lower grade being the worst."

Xu Renshan nodded in satisfaction, "The higher the grade of the Foundation Establishment Treasure, the better the effect of the Foundation Establishment.

You know about the grades of Foundation Establishment Treasures, but I'm afraid not many of you know that Foundation Establishment itself also has grades."

At these words, many people in the audience focused their attention even more.

This was indeed something they didn't know.

Xu Renshan glanced at Qi Yuan's position and saw that Qi Yuan was also listening attentively. He felt very proud.

"Foundation Establishment is divided into Mortal-Grade Foundation Establishment, Spirit-Grade Foundation Establishment, Vein-Grade Foundation Establishment, and Immortal-Grade Foundation Establishment.

Generally, lower-grade Foundation Establishment Treasures can only achieve Mortal-Grade Foundation Establishment, like myself, wasting my life without any hope of reaching the Elixir Dao, how regrettable.

Middle-grade Foundation Establishment Treasures can achieve Spirit-Grade Foundation Establishment, and there's also a chance of achieving Vein-Grade Foundation Establishment.

As for upper-grade Foundation Establishment Treasures, they correspond to Vein-Grade Foundation Establishment.

It is rumored that above the upper-grade Foundation Establishment Treasures, there are even higher-level Foundation Establishment Treasures that can achieve Immortal-Grade Foundation Establishment!

Immortal-Grade Foundation Establishment represents the hope of becoming a true Immortal."

The people in the audience were all breathing heavily, who wouldn't want to become a true Immortal? "Of course, within each level of Foundation Establishment, there are also further subdivisions."

Qi Yuan frowned and suddenly asked, "May I ask, teacher, is Immortal-Grade Foundation Establishment the best?"

Hearing the chief disciple of the Seven-Colored Peak ask him a question, Xu Renshan's beard almost stood on end.

"No, above Immortal-Grade Foundation Establishment, there is the rumored Heavenly-Dao Foundation Establishment, that is the strongest Foundation Establishment."

"Then may I ask, teacher, what is Heavenly-Dao Foundation Establishment?" Qi Yuan asked seriously.

Xu Renshan was stunned.

He was just a small Foundation Establishment cultivator, how would he know anything about Heavenly-Dao Foundation Establishment?

However, he still forced himself to say, "The so-called Heavenly-Dao Foundation Establishment is profound and unfathomable, I can only make a few guesses, you can just listen, don't take it as the truth."

After saying this, Xu Renshan let out a sigh of relief.

He started to make things up.

After all, he had already said that this was just his guess, it couldn't be taken as the truth.

And this kind of thing, the disciples here would never use in their entire lives.

He made up nonsense, and no one would expose him.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan immediately focused all his attention.

He wanted to know what Heavenly-Dao Foundation Establishment was.
