The four people present had their eyes shining.

This was the Flowing Sand of the Five Elements, carried on one's person, which could increase the chance of perceiving the Qi of the Five Essences.

In the Tempering Grounds of the Five Elements, this was a precious treasure! How could the Patriarch of the Clothing Sect have it?

He was so fierce, and with the Flowing Sand of the Five Elements...

It should be of some use to you, so I'll give it to you."

The other four heard this, and their suspicions dissipated.

Useless... Useful.

Indeed, the Patriarch of the Clothing Sect's chance of perceiving the Qi of the Five Essences was 100%, so it was truly useless to him.

Thinking about this, they held the Flowing Sand of the Five Essences, and their excitement had diminished.

At this time, Tan Yan thought of something and suddenly said, "Patriarch of the Clothing Sect, didn't you complain that the Five Elements Perfection was too slow? Seeing this Flowing Sand of the Five Elements, I have a method, I don't know if it can be used or not."

Qi Yuan's eyes lit up, it seems this Flowing Sand of the Five Elements was given to the right person!

Indeed, by doing more things in the game, perhaps some new plot would be triggered.

"What method?" Qi Yuan asked.

"The formation conditions of the Flowing Sand of the Five Elements are very harsh. If it is formed, it means that there must be something in the vicinity that makes the Five Souls fear.

The Patriarch of the Clothing Sect can go to the place where the Flowing Sand of the Five Elements is formed and find that thing.

Relying on that thing, the Patriarch of the Clothing Sect can slay the Five Souls and perceive the Qi of the Five Essences faster."Tan Yan truthfully said what he knew.

Qi Yuan heard this and felt it made sense.

The reason for the formation of the Flowing Sand of the Five Elements was that it was scorched by the Great Sun.

This means that the Five Souls fear the Great Sun!

The Great Sun is the nemesis of the Five Souls!

If he had the Great Sun, wouldn't he be able to quickly sweep away and kill all the Five Souls in the Forbidden Grounds of the Five Elements? Uproot them!

After all, they didn't call him to the party!

Qi Yuan's desire for revenge was strong.

It's just that the Great Sun... Qi Yuan immediately thought of the Daydream Incantation.

"Thank you all, I'll take my leave!"

Qi Yuan ran off in a flash, he had to find a safe place and dig a hole to bury himself.

The four people on the scene, looking at Qi Yuan's retreating figure, had different expressions on their faces.

...

On the Seven-Colored Peak, Qi Yuan recorded the fragmented Daydream Incantation.

He looked at the content on it, with a hesitant expression: "Should I really practice it? Won't there be any problems?"

He looked at his own "Qi Yuan Sutra".

"Actually, the problem is not that big. Cultivating the 'Qi Yuan Sutra' occasionally causes mental confusion and memory loss.

But I also feel that I'm doing well now, my spirit is very good... It's not bad either?"

"Moreover, I don't need to fully cultivate it, I just need to simulate the aura of the Great Sun.

Then use the Great Treasure Sword to unleash it, to startle those Five Souls!"

After convincing himself, Qi Yuan immediately focused his attention on the Daydream Incantation.

He did not fully cultivate it, but focused on simulating the aura of the Great Sun within it.

This kind of cultivation is very simple, and it is not a complete mastery of the technique, but it is very effective.

In just half a day, Qi Yuan successfully simulated it.

He was delighted.

"Why do I feel a bit cold?"

After coming out of the focused state of cultivation, Qi Yuan noticed a cold feeling, like a knife cutting his skin.

"Am I too empty?"

Qi Yuan couldn't help but be puzzled, and then he was stunned: "Am I too empty?"

Qi Yuan felt that he had accidentally discovered the essence of the world.

The legendary Emptiness might just be an extremely empty universe that needs to drink Kidney Treasure.

He added some more clothes to himself, but the coldness on his body had not completely dissipated.

He lit a fire in the courtyard.

After the firewood was lit, he got up and went out, only to see the also pale-faced Jiang Lingsu.

"Shidi, are you alright?"

"No... No problem." Jiang Lingsu looked a little dazed when she saw her Shixiong.

She didn't know why, but recently the ice had been entering her body more and more frequently.

She now felt very cold.

Seeing that her Shixiong also seemed very cold, she couldn't help but speculate, could it be that her ice had already affected the surrounding environment? It seems that the problem with her body is quite serious.

"Shidi, if you're cold, why don't you come to my courtyard? I just lit a fire, you can warm up a bit." Qi Yuan said warmly.

"No need." Jiang Lingsu curled up her body.

She was afraid of making her Shixiong cold.

She turned and went back to her room, crawling into the quilt.

Qi Yuan looked at Jiang Lingsu's retreating figure and couldn't help but say, "On the Seven-Colored Peak, there are two cold ones, one is Shidi, and the other is Shifu."

"No, there's also that moon!" Qi Yuan looked at the cold moon on the peak palace and blurted out.

"My enthusiasm is like a fire, it must be able to burn the ice cold in the world!" Especially after simulating the aura of the Great Sun, Qi Yuan felt even more confident.

After entering the game.

Qi Yuan, as usual, merged with Xiaojia, and then broke into the Core Region.

The Gold Soul, Wood Soul, and the other Five Souls, seeing Qi Yuan, were enemies meeting, and were extremely resentful.

They roared and charged towards Qi Yuan.

Qi Yuan, seeing this, was not flustered.

"Great Sun!"

He shouted loudly! On the blood-red treasure sword, a layer of white light appeared.

This light did not have too strong an attack power.

It really didn't increase the power of Qi Yuan's sword strike.

However, with the light appearing, the originally fierce Five Souls looked as if they had seen their natural enemy.

The organ similar to their eyes revealed a fearful expression.

The Five Souls not far from Qi Yuan, their bodies became strange.

The closer they were to Qi Yuan, the greater the effect on them, their bodies became soft and flabby, like flowing sand.

And at this moment, they were like lambs awaiting slaughter!

"It really works!"

Qi Yuan was overjoyed.
