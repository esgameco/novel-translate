An Qiao also remained silent, finally asking: "Mr. Qi wants to conduct guerrilla warfare here to obtain demon blood?"

Of course, he wasn't planning guerrilla warfare, but to sweep through.

"This..." An Qiao hesitated before saying, "Why don't we leave first, otherwise staying here would only... hold you back, Mr. Qi!"

An Qiao was telling the truth.

With her late Blood Qi Realm strength, she would be finished if she encountered a Jade Blood Realm great demon.

If Qi Yuan was conducting guerrilla warfare, her staying here would likely affect his efficiency.

"What you say makes sense," Qi Yuan nodded, then swept his gaze over the three people present, looking at Yang Guang, "Can you kill another hundred Jade Blood Realm great demons?"

O-one hundred?

The expression on Yang Guang's face was worse than crying.

A hundred Jade Blood Realm great demons would be enough to stuff him to death.

"I... can't kill that many."

"It seems your kidney deficiency is still too severe. Go back with An Qiao and the others," Qi Yuan said with a look of disdain.

Folks, who can understand? Hired a bodyguard who looks strong and sturdy, but turns out to have kidney deficiency.

"Ahem..." Yang Guang was at a loss for words.

An Qiao looked at Qi Yuan and said seriously: "Mr. Qi, please prioritize your own safety. If it's not feasible, please leave as soon as possible!"

However, at this moment, an unexpectedly childish voice spoke up: "Mr. Qi, you're slaying demons in Shu Demon Mountain and also lack someone to take care of you. I, Huo Mushi, am willing to stay here and look after your meals and daily needs!"

Huo Mushi's childish face was full of determination, as if he had also struggled with these words for a long time.

Beside him, An Qiao and Yang Guang looked at Huo Mushi with surprise, seemingly amazed by his courage.

Qi Yuan, however, appeared very calm: "I have high standards for meals, three dishes and one soup. Can you manage that?"

"..." Yang Guang.

"I can!" Huo Mushi's face showed excitement.

Seeing this, An Qiao didn't say anything more.

Originally, she wanted to remind Huo Mushi that staying in Shu Demon Mountain could very likely lead to death.

At the same time, she also didn't understand why Qi Yuan would bring along a burden.

Of course, she really couldn't comprehend Qi Yuan's thought process.

Sometimes, even Qi Yuan himself couldn't understand his own thoughts.

A quarter hour later.

An Qiao and Yang Guang's figures dissipated.

In the cave, only Qi Yuan and Huo Mushi remained.

Looking at the pitch-black night, if Huo Mushi said he wasn't afraid, that would be a lie.

However, he still tightly hugged his knife, guarding at the entrance of the cave.

Since the southern migration incident, the surrounding cities were in a miserable state.

Almost all the young and strong in Zhenyang City had died in battle.

Now, among those guarding the city walls, one-third were child soldiers, one-third were white-haired soldiers.

The elderly could no longer enjoy their twilight years, and children could no longer grow up.

This was the current situation in Zhenyang City.

However, compared to other cities that had fallen to demon territories, Zhenyang City was still considered lucky.

Unlike other places where groups of demons would swarm upon seeing a city, massacring and exterminating families, using human corpses to make dried rations.

And... the leader in Shu Demon Mountain seemed to have more "vision".

Rumors said that he listened to his military advisor's suggestion to besiege Zhenyang City, treating the people inside like livestock.

This way, when they wanted to eat meat, they would send demon soldiers to snatch people.

For them, the human meat rations in Zhenyang City were endless.

Unlike other demon territories that exhausted their resources.

When other demon territories lacked human population and human meat snacks, they would still capture people from Zhenyang City to sell to other demon territories, obtaining resources.

It could be said that Zhenyang City was both a livestock farm and a treasury for Shu Demon Mountain.

Huo Mushi hugged his knife, occasionally glancing into the cave.

From inside the cave came Qi Yuan's strange voice pretending to be a robot.

Many thoughts crossed Huo Mushi's mind.

"If only I could also continuously consume demon blood... that would be great."

Of course, he was just thinking about it.

At this moment, suddenly there was a sound from the cave, and Qi Yuan got up and walked out. He looked at Huo Mushi with a regretful expression in his eyes: "It's a pity you don't know how to play the suona, otherwise..."

Qi Yuan recalled the times in Canglang Realm and Fanxin Realm.

Whenever he went on an expedition, there would always be a suona army following.

When the suona sounded, nothing would be left, not even bones.

"Huh?" Huo Mushi was stunned for a moment, then said, "The suona you mentioned, sir, I can play it! My grandfather was a funeral musician."

Qi Yuan's eyes lit up.

"It seems fate has led you to stay here."

With a wave of Qi Yuan's hand, a suona appeared.

This suona, of course, wasn't carried by Qi Yuan, but was conjured by his magic.

"I'll give you a task. Whenever I'm killing demons, you stand beside me and play the suona... understand?"

"Ah... alright!" Huo Mushi was stunned, then quickly nodded in agreement.

Three days later.

In Shu Demon Mountain, within the demon camp.

A purple-robed peacock woman's eyes carried a hint of anger: "Are they all useless? A single human... couldn't be captured in three days, and nearly a hundred demon soldiers died?"

The peacock's eyes were full of rage.

The death of nearly a hundred demon soldiers.

For the demon camp, this was considered an extremely significant loss.

Each time they attacked Zhenyang City, the casualties were only a few demon soldiers, sometimes even zero.

As a result, a single human caused the death of nearly a hundred demon soldiers. This was definitely the biggest loss Shu Demon Mountain had ever suffered.

"That fellow's strength is formidable... probably only slightly weaker than the Twelve Generals!" A demon lowered his head, his eyes showing a hint of fear.

In Shu Demon Mountain, there was one leader, three great generals, and twelve generals.

The Twelve Generals were all late Jade Blood Realm, and not ordinary late Jade Blood Realm at that.

"Hmph." The peacock woman snorted coldly.

She was a great general, a terrifying demon of the Demon Elite level.

"Within three days, I want to see his corpse!" The peacock woman spoke, her voice stern.

At this moment, a tall man approached, dressed as a white-robed scholar, his voice cheerful.

"Why should the Great General be angry? Shu Demon Mountain's terrain is complex, it's indeed difficult to find a late Jade Blood Realm human cultivator."

This white-robed scholar was the military advisor of Shu Demon Mountain.

Seeing him approach, a hint of affection appeared in the peacock's eyes.

"But these demon soldiers are just lazy and afraid of death. Otherwise, wouldn't it be easy to capture him by sending a few demon generals?"

"That person's strength is ordinary. The difficulty lies in finding him. It's unwise to dispatch a large number of demon soldiers to search for one person." The military advisor spoke.

The peacock nodded.

Currently, the leader was refining a demon artifact.

Most of the demon soldiers needed to guard the demon camp to prevent any unexpected events.

"So, to resolve this matter... I have a method, but I need the Great General's agreement." The military advisor spoke, looking at the peacock's face with a gentle expression.

"The military advisor is so clever, please tell me!" The peacock quickly said.
