The starry sky was brilliant, with the Milky Way stretching across the heavens.

The universe was vast and profound, desolate yet boundless.

As far as the eye could see, countless stars filled the view.

A single star burned with endless fire, radiating infinite heat, seemingly within arm's reach of Qi Yuan, close enough to touch.

The scorching heat caused fine beads of sweat to form on Ning Tao's delicate white face. Her black long dress clung tightly to her petite figure, accentuating her graceful curves. Even as a supreme being, she felt the heat at this moment.

She gazed at this universe with a look of wonder, tinged with a hint of fear of the unknown.

The stars were all said to be connected to legendary deities.

And this universe contained so many stars...

Qi Yuan held Ning Tao in his arms. His body was burning hot, even hotter than Ning Tao's delicate form. His voice trembled as he spoke: "This is a brand new universe..."

In terms of cosmic concepts, the Cang Lan Realm, Wangyue Continent, and Liu Feng Realm were all actually in the same universe.

Above the sky, they shared the same stars, the same divine projections, or reflections.

Of course, the situation in the Liu Feng Realm may have been somewhat special.

Qi Yuan had come to a brand new universe through the Gate of Ten Thousand Differences.

This universe was completely silent.

Qi Yuan did not sense any living beings.

Or perhaps there were living beings, but he could not detect them.

In any case, the sun, moon and stars here were real celestial bodies.

Not reflections of deities, not false projections.

Qi Yuan held Ning Tao as the Great Sun Golden Lotus burned everything within him. His consciousness began to grow hazy.

After leaving the Liu Feng Realm, he seemed to have exited the game, and did not need to immediately return to the Cang Lan Realm.

But his current physical condition needed to be addressed.

The Great Sun Golden Lotus continued to burn.

He looked at Ning Tao, a flicker of emotion crossing his face: "I should go now."

Otherwise, the Great Sun Golden Lotus would harm Ning Tao.

Ning Tao stood in the vast emptiness of space, gazing at the familiar figure before her. Her eyes were determined as she tightly grasped Qi Yuan's sleeve. Her long, silk-stockinged legs also wrapped firmly around his waist, as if terrified he would suddenly disappear again like he had before.

Looking at the timid Ning Tao, a faint smile appeared in Qi Yuan's eyes: "Don't worry, I'll be fine."

Qi Yuan spoke as he looked at the nearby star that appeared small despite being so close.

He said: "You see that little sun? It's about to become my golden core."

A look of confusion flashed in Ning Tao's eyes.

She didn't understand, but listened quietly.

"I'm going there to swallow it," Qi Yuan said, his eyes showing a resolute expression.

"Don't worry about me. I... with my Heavenly Dao Foundation, swallowing a star is nothing! I once heard of a powerful being who swallowed the entire universe in one gulp.

Swallowing a star is no big deal for me."

"Blood Pearl... you're not allowed to lie to me!" Tears welled up in Ning Tao's starry eyes.

She was afraid Qi Yuan was deceiving her.

That he planned to leave her here alone while he went to face the all-consuming Great Sun Golden Lotus by himself.

"When have I ever lied to you?" Qi Yuan's thoughts were complex as he held Ning Tao's delicate hand, his expression gentle.

The bond between them was very deep.

At this moment, the burning of the Great Sun Golden Lotus grew even more terrifying, instantly incinerating Ning Tao's clothes. She seemed to deliberately not resist this burning.

Qi Yuan's hand rested on Ning Tao's smooth pink back, her jade-like body fully exposed to his view.

Her small frame twisted slightly, her soft curves brushing against his arm. Qi Yuan suppressed the greedy desire in his heart.

Ning Tao leaned close to Qi Yuan's ear, her voice soft but tone imperious: "Blood Pearl, you've seen this young lady's naked body. No matter how ugly I am, you can't dislike me!

If you leave without saying goodbye, I... will lock you by my side for all lifetimes to come!"

In this moment, Qi Yuan couldn't tell if the alluring young woman in his arms was the Golden Canary or Ning Tao.

He only knew that her eyes were exceptionally captivating.

"I won't," Qi Yuan looked at the young woman, his gaze firm but voice tinged with concern, "It's just... after I refine the star into my golden core, I may... I may also leave this place. I'm afraid only the golden core will remain here.

If a golden core forms, don't keep staring at it, or you may lose your memory."

Part of him would remain here.

Ning Tao seemed to half-understand. She tilted her head, her moist red lips approaching Qi Yuan's. Her teeth lightly bit down as a smug smile appeared on her face: "I've left my mark. Husband, from now on you belong to me."

But her smile quickly faded, replaced by a timid expression: "Blood Pearl, you should bite this young lady too. This young lady... wants to be yours!"

The bold and alluring one was Ning Tao, the shy and proud one was the Golden Canary.

Qi Yuan lowered his head and leaned in. A faint fragrance wafted over as he said: "Our combined mental age is still smaller than someone else's shoe size.

Little Ning Tao, I have to go now."

This childish, simple, and pure interaction dispersed the worries in Qi Yuan's heart.

Ning Tao hugged Qi Yuan's head, her snow-white face flushed. A black dress once again appeared on her body, with black stockings wrapping her slender white legs.

At their level of cultivation, condensing a piece of clothing from cosmic dust was trivial. Of course, they could also use spiritual energy to form clothes.

She looked at Qi Yuan, her voice carrying a hint of willfulness: "You know."

Qi Yuan nodded. He gazed deeply at Ning Tao one last time, then looked out at the countless stars in the vast universe.

Finally, his gaze settled on the closest and smallest sun.

He charged towards it without hesitation.

Ning Tao watched from afar, separated by the immense expanse of the universe.

Time seemed to freeze in that moment.

After an unknown period, a brilliant flash of light suddenly appeared on that star.

The light flared, then faded again.

At this moment, countless thoughts flashed through Qi Yuan's eyes.

He had already entered the interior of this star.

Infinite pressure and heat crushed and burned his body.

Within his body, the Great Sun Golden Lotus also incinerated everything.

The two forces burned continuously, reaching a delicate balance.

Qi Yuan's body endured pain far beyond anything he had experienced before.

Especially the Great Sun Golden Lotus - its terrifying ability to burn away everything made his body nearly unbearable.

Death's Radiant Light! The great skill was continuously activated, causing Qi Yuan's power to rapidly increase.

Resisting the Great Sun Golden Lotus.

The Great Sun Golden Lotus was the relic left after the Great Sun Radiant Buddha's nirvana, incredibly powerful.

After being tainted, it sought to burn away everything in the world.

"Forget!"

At this moment, Qi Yuan activated his innate divine ability, the Great Forgetting Heart Sutra.

Now, only the Great Forgetting Heart Sutra could change the situation.

In this moment, he unleashed the Great Forgetting Heart Sutra to its fullest extent.

This innate divine ability, powerful enough to leave a mark on the Gate of Ten Thousand Differences, now displayed its true might.

In the Liu Feng Realm, Qi Yuan had rarely used the Great Forgetting Heart Sutra.

The previous Qi Yuan seldom activated the Great Forgetting Heart Sutra, because using it too much would affect himself as well.

So even when he did use it, he exercised great restraint.

The innate divine ability was his own Dao.

As a player, how could he allow his own path to affect himself? Even if he forgot, it should be because he wanted to forget, not because he was influenced by the innate divine ability.

When condensing the Great Forgetting Heart Sutra to break through the predicament of the Gate of Ten Thousand Demons transforming into a great demon, he had already made plans.

Just as he had inscribed the innate divine ability of the Void Realm Lord's Heart Demon Seal onto the divine soul tool formed from a drop of his blood.

His plan was to inscribe the Great Forgetting Heart Sutra onto his golden core.

That's why he now used the Great Forgetting Heart Sutra without restraint.

"Forgetting is the truth."

"Forgetting is the Dao."

"You are the Great Sun Golden Lotus, you have not been tainted."

"I am the Great Sun Radiant Buddha, you are part of my body!"

The Great Forgetting Heart Sutra influenced everything.

Qi Yuan's body was scorching hot and blood-red, with flames constantly burning.

Yet his body also emanated a pervasive Buddhist nature.

The Great Sun Golden Lotus seemed to possess sentience, truly slowing its burning. However, when it sensed the auras of greed, arrogance, lust and other emotions from the blood-colored sword, it began to burn again, even more fiercely than before.

Qi Yuan took the long sword and immediately threw it away in disgust.

"It is my three corpses, already severed!"

"I am the Great Sun Radiant Buddha!"

"You are the relic in my belly!"

"You have not been tainted!"

Qi Yuan continuously activated his innate divine ability, soothing the Great Sun Golden Lotus.

Lotus flowers spilled from his mouth as he spoke in an enchanting voice.

Inside the star, the burning of the Great Sun Golden Lotus finally subsided.

Qi Yuan let out a sigh of relief: "Two great treasures, I've finally placated one. Still have one more to go."

The Great Sun Golden Lotus had been soothed, but this star had not yet been appeased.

"Thanks to Bai Ze, otherwise it wouldn't have been so easy," Qi Yuan mused.

Before leaving the Liu Feng Realm, Bai Ze had helped Qi Yuan absorb all the heart demons in the world into the Heart Demon Seal sword.

Bai Ze had also bestowed countless Buddhist teachings and some gifts from the Great Sun Radiant Buddha upon Qi Yuan.

That's why Qi Yuan was better able to use the Great Forgetting Heart Sutra to soothe the Great Sun Golden Lotus.

"Great Sun Golden Lotus, forge my supreme flawless golden body of glazed crystal!"

Qi Yuan let out a mighty shout.
