Twenty outstanding talents gathered, each with their own thoughts.

Except for Ling Jiao, the other talents at least showed outward respect towards Qi Yuan, and were also very curious about which Yin God's descendant this strange man was.

In the entire Eastern Land, there were only a handful of Yin Gods.

At this moment, the powerful Wind Orchid appeared, with the Wind Moon Fairy following behind him. Her figure was graceful, with white gloves covering her nails and fingers.

The gathered talents all looked towards Wind Orchid with respect in their eyes.

"Greetings, Senior Wind Orchid!"

These talents naturally showed respect to a Purple Palace powerhouse.

Wind Orchid nodded, glancing at Qi Yuan who was carrying a turtle shell on his back, with a strange look in his eyes. He cleared his throat and said: "You are all outstanding talents of our Eastern Land, carrying important responsibilities for your sects. Let me briefly introduce the situation on the Wangyue Continent to you all."

As he spoke, twenty jade slips fell into everyone's hands.

The jade slips contained detailed information about the Wangyue Continent.

"On the Wangyue Continent, including us, there are now three forces. They are the natives of the Wangyue Continent, the Moluo clan, and us. The natives of the continent are not worth mentioning. As for the strength of the Moluo clan... it's very strong. However, we of the Eastern Land have reached an agreement with the Moluo clan that forces of the fifth rank and above will not take action," Wind Orchid explained.

The fifth rank referred to the Purple Palace realm.

"Who are our opponents on the Wangyue Continent?" asked Gu Xiaoyao.

Qi Yuan also glanced at Gu Xiaoyao at this point.

This was a perfect Heavenly Dao Foundation Building, someone even he was wary of.

"Among the three forces, the Moluo clan is the strongest. We will... temporarily form an alliance with the natives of the Wangyue Continent. Of course, in reality, both are our opponents."

The people present nodded.

Qi Yuan also looked at the jade slip, gaining a better understanding of the complex situation on the Wangyue Continent.

The Canglan Continent and the Moluo clan were gradually swallowing up the territory of the Wangyue Continent.

"On the Wangyue Continent, three Venerable Ones have already established strongholds, with many Divine Infant cultivators guarding them," Wind Orchid continued. "Remember, you must not make enemies of them."

On the surface, the Eastern Land only had those three Venerable Ones.

The Wangyue Continent was discovered and occupied by those three.

The Eastern Land's outstanding talent competition offered twenty spots to distribute some benefits to other major forces in the Eastern Land.

These twenty people entering the Wangyue Continent would, firstly, gain experience; secondly, clarify the state of the Wangyue Continent; and thirdly, gain more say based on their contributions on the Wangyue Continent.

Simply put, they were working for those three Venerable Ones.

Those who performed well would receive more rewards, and their sects could send more people to occupy some territory, used to avoid the great calamity.

"Understood."

The twenty talents responded in unison.

"In that case, everyone, enter the Wangyue Continent!" Wind Orchid finished.

Suddenly, a door appeared in front of everyone.

The gazes of those present fell on that door, including Qi Yuan's.

There was a look of surprise in his eyes.

To be honest, this was his first time... reaching another world through such means.

"Let's go!" Gu Xiaoyao spoke.

The gathered people all entered that door one after another.

What met their eyes was a burst of white light.

Everyone felt their vision become a sea of white, unable to see anything clearly.

Suddenly, a feeling of the world spinning came over them.

This sensation lasted for a full dozen breaths.

Qi Yuan's eyes were full of anticipation, with a hint of nervousness.

He didn't know if this Wangyue Continent was the world he had once stayed in.

He also didn't know if he could see his old acquaintances here.

On the Wangyue Continent, what year was it now?

The white light dissipated, replaced by a dry, intense heat.

It felt like a wave of hot air sweeping over them.

Everyone present felt the extreme heat.

The place they had appeared was like a desert.

"Is this the Wangyue Continent?"

"It's quite hot!"

Everyone had different expressions, full of curiosity about this unknown world.

At this moment, Ling Jiao glanced at Qi Yuan and couldn't help saying: "Some people are wearing such thick clothes in this heat, do they really think this is still the Canglan Realm?"

In the Canglan Realm, spiritual energy filled the world.

As long as one's spiritual power was abundant, spells could theoretically be used endlessly.

But the Wangyue Continent was different.

This was not the same world as the Canglan Realm; there was no spiritual energy in this world.

The spiritual power in their dantians was limited, and once used up, it was difficult to replenish.

Even with spirit stones, absorption was more troublesome than in the Canglan Realm.

So they were very economical with the spiritual power in their dantians, trying not to use it if possible.

At this moment, they were being very frugal, not using spells to make the place cooler.

Qi Yuan, however, didn't respond to Ling Jiao.

Or rather, he hadn't heard Ling Jiao's words at all.

He crouched down, looking at a withered small plant on the ground.

He reached out his finger and touched that small plant.

As he touched it, something amazing happened.

The withered plant on the ground seemed to have been nourished by spring rain.

The originally withered yellow branches and leaves became tender and full of life at this moment.

In the desert, one, two, three small plants appeared continuously, growing wildly.

At Qi Yuan's feet, the dry desert instantly transformed into a patch of green.

These small plants stretched out their leaf buds, as if saying "hello" to Qi Yuan like the garden plants in Wang Duoyu's manor.

Such a scene, in the world of mortals, would be enough for those mortals to view it as a miracle.

However, for the gathered talents, it felt strange.

Especially that Ling Jiao, whose eyes held a cold smile: "Wasting spiritual power on such trivial matters, how interesting."

The Great Wisdom True Lord then said: "This fellow Daoist is willing to consume his own spiritual power just to save a withered plant. This shows his character and is worth befriending."

Qi Yuan finally stood up and looked at Ling Jiao: "If not used for this, should spiritual power be used on you?"

Ling Jiao gritted her teeth, about to retort.

Just then, an elderly voice came, interrupting the argument between Qi Yuan and Ling Jiao.

"Everyone has arrived. This old man is True Lord Falling Wood, and I have been waiting here for some time. Greetings to you all!"

A Divine Infant cultivator appeared, with a neutral expression on his face.

"Greetings, True Lord Falling Wood!" The gathered talents all showed great respect to this Divine Infant cultivator.

After all, those who had come to the Wangyue Continent now were all subordinates of those three Venerable Ones.

"Come with me, everyone. As it happens, representatives from seven forces on the Wangyue Continent have come to our stronghold seeking an alliance. You can choose to ally with these forces on your own to jointly deal with the Moluo clan," the Divine Infant cultivator True Lord Falling Wood said, also explaining the current situation to the gathered talents.

Currently, there were seven kingdoms near this stronghold.

These seven kingdoms were resisting the Moluo clan.

With their strength, they naturally couldn't face the Moluo clan, so they were seeking cooperation with the cultivators from the Canglan Realm.

"You call it an alliance?" Ling Jiao's eyes showed a light smile. "It's nothing more than ceding territory to seek help. These natives of the Wangyue Continent are truly weak."

The bargaining chips offered by these natives were to cede some territories to them in exchange for their help.

Hearing this, the gathered talents all sighed inexplicably.

In this world, weakness was a sin.

Because of their weakness, the Wangyue Continent could only struggle to survive between two terrifying enemies.

However, their struggle, their humiliating concessions, seemed particularly amusing in the eyes of others.

Qi Yuan's mood at this moment was exceptionally calm.

The words of others seemed not to have reached his ears.

He looked at the azure sky, whispering in his heart: "Twenty-one days, is it?"

When he stepped onto the Wangyue Continent.

He knew that this was his Wangyue Continent, his home.

His body, his divine soul, was regaining control of this world.

And to fully take control, to become the Heavenly Dao of this world, the time needed was twenty-one days.

...

In the stronghold, representatives from the seven kingdoms gathered together.

Their expressions were not very good.

After all, coming here, on the surface, was to seek help and achieve cooperation.

But in reality, they would be signing treaties that surrendered rights and humiliated their countries.

If their countries perished, that would be another matter.

If their countries survived, they would become sinners for all time.

Luo Xi wore a monk's white robe, his face handsome, with the only regret being that he was bald, without a single hair on his forehead or eyebrows.

At this moment, his face also carried deep worry.

"I heard that twenty young talents from major forces in the Canglan Realm have come to ally with us. Do you have any information about them?"
