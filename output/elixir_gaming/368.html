Qi Yuan had a thoughtful expression.

Qi Yuan declared passionately.

The subjects beside him looked at each other in confusion.

They were just looking for Eastern Lord, how did it turn into "my fate is in my own hands"?

But they had grown accustomed to Qi Yuan.

The divine lord was just like this, occasionally saying inexplicable things, with peculiar thought processes.

"Dad, with our strength, we wouldn't be supporting characters in any world, right?" Qi Qi said softly.

Hearing this, the other subjects also had strange expressions.

Don't be fooled by how common Yang Spirits were here, as plentiful as cabbages in a field.

But...even in the Innate Realm, if Qi Yuan hadn't come.

Even given hundreds of millions or billions of years, it might not have produced a single Yang Spirit.

Except for the Holy Mother of No Food, even other mythical powerhouses had almost no chance of reaching the Yang Spirit realm.

As for these subjects...well, forget about Yang Spirit, most of them couldn't even reach the Great Venerable realm.

Meeting Qi Yuan was the greatest opportunity of their lives.

If Qi Yuan wasn't here holding them back.

If they were let loose in the Upper Realm, they would likely cause a commotion in the Immortal World.

After all, even in the Upper Realm, a Yang Spirit was the foundation of a holy land.

With a Yang Spirit present, a holy land could last for tens of millions of years.

"But Dad has a point, Eastern Lord's strength is unknown, we should keep a low profile," Qi Qi mentioned again.

Qi Yuan nodded: "Right, it's better to be cautious.

Qi Qi couldn't help whispering: "Dad, even I haven't done that in a long time, how could the Dao Ancestor?"

"Maybe we should drop this topic, it might not be good to speculate about the Dao Ancestor?" Old Thunder God said.

When he was weak, Old Thunder God wouldn't have been afraid to curse the Dao Ancestor every day.

But now it was different, he was at the Yang Spirit realm.

He had even visualized the Heavenly Worthy of Universal Transformation of the Nine Heavens, and the South Pole Longevity Emperor, one of the Six Emperors.

The status of these two was extremely high, unimaginable.

So he was quite wary of supreme beings like the Dao Ancestor.

Qi Yuan nodded.

"Let's go, take me to where the purple qi was born, I want to try to learn from it, see if I can do some bulk purchasing."

In the Origin Land.

In a shattered palace, Fat Egg Martial Lord suddenly opened his eyes.

"My father's... aura?"

Just a short while ago, as he was resting with his eyes closed, he suddenly caught a whiff of a familiar feeling, along with a stirring of his bloodline.

This feeling clearly meant his "father" had come out of seclusion.

Over these years, he had been extremely happy.

As expected of his never-met "father", Fat Egg Martial Lord's strength had been rising steadily.

This also led to a leap in his bloodline.

His cultivation had actually reached the Great Martial Lord realm of Martial Lord Stage 4.

This kind of improvement was extremely terrifying.

Of course, this was related to his good foundation, which not only the beauties knew about, but all the other Martial Lords knew as well.

Before the Great Calamity, he had actually been a Titled Martial Lord.

Now with the leap in his bloodline, his strength frequently increased.

Because of this, his status in the Eastern Alliance rose again, and his position was upgraded.

After all, he had a hidden powerful father behind him.

By the looks of it, possibly at the Titled Martial Lord level.

"Fat Egg Martial Lord, your father has appeared?"

At this time, a voice came from the shattered palace, belonging to a thin old man.

This old man had also been a Titled Martial Lord before the Great Calamity.

However, he was now a Great Martial Lord of Martial Lord Stage 6, one of the higher-ups in the Eastern Alliance.

"Mm, I seem to have caught his scent, he should have come out of seclusion," Fat Egg Martial Lord said thoughtfully.

"Where is he? Take me to meet this elder.

Our Eastern Alliance is not doing well these days, we need to absorb some fresh blood." The thin old man seemed expectant and urgent.

The tasks assigned by Eastern Lord had been progressing smoothly at first.

But over the past thousand years, suddenly many tasks had failed.

If the task completion rate couldn't reach 60%, when the Dawn's Call came, everyone would die.

The Origin Land, the End Land, the Mountain and Sea Scroll, would all be destroyed.

Now, having an extra bit of strength was always good.

"Let's go, I'll take you to meet my... father!" Fat Egg Martial Lord gritted his teeth and said.

"Good." The thin old man nodded.

"What if my father doesn't recognize me?" Suddenly, Fat Egg Martial Lord hesitated.

"Fat Egg Martial Lord is overthinking. Your bloodline is very strong, you should have inherited perfectly from him. He looks just like you, how could he not recognize you?" the thin old man said.

Fat Egg looked at his own round belly, and finally slowly nodded: "You have a point, let's go!"

...

In the Origin Land, the Sea of Ten Thousand Souls.

This was where the ends of the earth converged in the Origin Land.

Exuberant vitality flourished.

Qi Yuan sat alone, his clothes fluttering in the wind, his expression calm.

"All things in this world emerged from the Sea of Ten Thousand Souls.

Where did all things in the world come from?

Born of coincidence, or the One that escaped from the great Dao?"

Looking at the Sea of Ten Thousand Souls, he thought of many things.

"No good, I can't think anymore, I'm going to grow a brain."

Qi Yuan's divine realm was a mess, with all sorts of strange things.

Planets turned into footballs lying on a football field, dung beetles rolling huge spheres.

Qi Yuan's thoughts were also a bit chaotic.

If he grew more brain or pondered too much, he feared his true spirit couldn't withstand it.

This was the first time Qi Yuan felt that his body couldn't match up to his noble and clever mind.

"What a pity, even someone as smart as me can't replicate the purple qi."

Qi Yuan looked ahead, countless bits of information pouring in.

But replicating the purple qi was simply impossible.

Or rather, if he sat here for ten thousand years, a hundred thousand years, or even five million years, seeing different information every day, he might be able to do it.

But that would take too much time.

Just then, suddenly there was a ripple in space behind him.

One fat and one thin figure appeared.

Fat Egg Martial Lord craned his neck, looking around, searching for something.

When his gaze fell on Qi Yuan, he was stunned for a moment.

A feeling of shame spread.

He wanted to tear his own flesh to pieces.

This was too... perfect, wasn't it? "Is this my father?" Fat Egg Martial Lord took a deep breath, sucked in his stomach, trying to look slimmer.

The thin old man also looked doubtful, looking at Qi Yuan, then examining Fat Egg Martial Lord: "They don't really look alike."

However, the two didn't say anything more and flew over.

"Eastern Alliance Elder pays respects to the senior!"

"Eastern Alliance Fat Egg pays respects to the senior!"

The two bowed respectfully, standing to the side.

One fat and one thin, they were quite eye-catching.

Although Fat Egg Martial Lord was trying his best to suck in his stomach, his figure was still much rounder than the Elder Martial Lord.
