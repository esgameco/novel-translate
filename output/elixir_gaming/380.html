Moreover, someone who could lie to themselves so convincingly was clearly not a good person.

Fortunately, his nose could grow longer.

The dragon-slaying youth had been activated, ready to solo the boss.

The martial lords discussed among themselves, placing their hopes on the East Monarch.

Hearing this, Mu Yan felt even more resistant.

The ancestor's kind face broke into a smile, "Sometimes, isn't choosing to believe... better than despair? Among all the martial lords present, nine out of ten actively chose to believe in the East Monarch!"

Hearing this, Mu Yan was shocked.

Actively choosing to believe in the East Monarch?

What did that mean?

"But... what if the East Monarch makes a mistake?" Mu Yan said with difficulty.

"When a person's words are revered as truth by everyone, he cannot be wrong! Everything he says is correct!" The bloodline ancestor spoke, his eyes filled with fanatical fervor, "At this point, actively believing and offering one's loyalty is far more appropriate than passively believing!"

The ancestor's expression remained kind.

"First, the East Monarch cannot be wrong, he is saving the world. Second, all living beings in the world who see the East Monarch will become his followers. Such an East Monarch... whatever he does is right. It's impossible for there to be a will in the world that opposes the East Monarch."

However, as he spoke, a tide of killing intent surged in his eyes.

"Those who dare to resist the East Monarch have long been buried in the Black Sea by us! We are the East Monarch's first and most loyal followers! The Black Sea is filled with bones everywhere!" The ancestor's kind face twisted into a fierce expression.

"I..." Mu Yan became somewhat afraid.

He felt that his once-familiar ancestor had become extremely unfamiliar.

"Are you afraid? You won't be afraid once you become one of us. You've contributed to saving the world this time, the East Monarch will remember your merits."

"At this rate, how long will it take to comprehend the myriad ways?"

Just charge straight through.

And it was particularly boring.

When he sees Jinli and his beautiful master later, he can just hand over the marriage certificate.

Perfect.
