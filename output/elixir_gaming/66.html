Qi Yuan's innate golden energy was already abundant, and he possessed a vigorous golden qi within his body.

The golden qi and spiritual power could be interchanged.

With a single finger gesture, golden radiance appeared.

A golden sword suddenly materialized in his hand! The golden sword was formed from the qi of the five elements of metal.

Its edge was incredibly sharp.

Beside him, Dong Xian saw this scene and widened his eyes.

What was this? Did Senior Brother Qi Yuan truly transform his qi into a sword? How did he do it?

The Vessel Elder in the depths of his soul seemed to ponder something.

"This seems to be akin to a martial dao divine technique."

Qi Yuan, wielding the qi-transformed sword, slowly said, "Watch as I cleave the flying bird in the sky with a single stroke!"

As he spoke, he raised the golden sword towards the heavens.

However, at that moment, a sudden bolt of lightning descended from the clear sky, directly striking towards Qi Yuan.

On a sunny day, how could there suddenly be lightning? And it was striking at him?

This startled Qi Yuan: "Oh no, by raising it high, I've attracted the lightning down."

He hastily waved his hand, throwing the golden sword away, letting it fall into the distance.

The lightning bolt then struck the golden sword.

In an instant, the sword shattered, transforming back into golden qi that dissipated into the air.

The heavenly lightning also vanished without a trace.

Beside him, Dong Xian was completely dumbfounded by this sight.

What was going on? At this moment, the voice of the Vessel Elder echoed in his mind.

"It seems Qi Yuan has cultivated some strange forbidden technique, which is why he could transform his qi into a sword, but he was struck by heavenly retribution in the form of lightning.

This technique of transforming qi into a sword should never be learned." The Vessel Elder warned.

Dong Xian immediately understood. He had once read records in the imperial archives about heavenly lightning.

Nowadays, heavenly lightning rarely manifested in the mortal realm.

But if one cultivated some strange forbidden techniques, it would draw out the appearance of heavenly lightning.

Thinking of this, Dong Xian quickly said, "Senior Brother Qi Yuan, you must not continue practicing this qi-transformation sword technique, for it is a forbidden method that the heavens do not permit."

To be honest, Dong Xian was still trembling in fear, worried that Qi Yuan might be struck dead by the lightning.

The Peak Lord of the Seven-Colored Peak, would he still tolerate him? And the Vessel Elder in his mind felt extremely strange.

Logically, the heavenly retribution should have struck the one who cultivated the forbidden technique.

Yet the lightning only struck the sword that Qi Yuan had thrown away, and not Qi Yuan himself.

Hearing Dong Xian's words, Qi Yuan had a peculiar expression: "Shouldn't it be because I raised the sword high, and the sword being a conductor, that it attracted the heavenly lightning down? Could it be that this is truly a forbidden technique?"

Beside him, Dong Xian did not understand the first half of Qi Yuan's words, but he understood the latter half, and he emphasized, "Senior Brother Qi Yuan, you should refrain from practicing this technique in the future!"

Dong Xian felt relieved that he had made Qi Yuan demonstrate it first. If Qi Yuan had taught him the cultivation method of this technique, and he had cultivated it blindly, he might have been struck by lightning before even transforming the qi into a sword!

The "qi-transformation horse" that Qi Yuan mentioned was probably... the idea of being struck by lightning while riding a horse and not being able to escape easily. "Senior, I shall take my leave now." Dong Xian bid farewell to Qi Yuan and quickly flew away.

Indeed, madmen were not only dangerous to provoke, but also unsafe to be around for too long.

Meanwhile, on the Seven-Colored Peak, Qi Yuan sat on the ground, deep in thought.

Transforming qi into a sword!

Suddenly, he stood up, transforming qi into a sword!

The heavenly lightning descended once again without any warning.

Qi Yuan hastily threw the sword away, and the lightning instantly shattered the sword.

Qi Yuan witnessed this scene: "It seems this is truly a forbidden technique!"

However, there was no sense of disappointment in his eyes, but rather an intense excitement.

"I am truly a genius, for I have created a divine technique!"

"This technique shall be called, 'I am the Father of Heavenly Lightning, I strike wherever I point!'"

He condensed another golden sword, and the heavenly lightning descended from the sky once more.

"Strike there!"

He threw the sword, and the lightning swiftly struck, destroying the indestructible golden sword.

"No, I need to change the name. This heavenly lightning is like a dog, I can't call myself its father."

His swords were like bones, and the heavenly lightning was like a dog.

He would throw the sword, and the lightning would pounce on it.

How intriguing.

"I am truly a genius, for I have created a secret technique!"

The power of the heavenly lightning was far greater than the power of his qi-transformation sword.

The only pity was that this secret technique could not be used in the game.

Otherwise, he would have given the primordial humans and the Extradimensional Heavenly Demons a little shock.

...

Yin-Yang Forbidden.

Ying looked at Qi Yuan and slowly said, "The Yin-Yang Boundary Stone is now in your possession.

With the Yin-Yang Boundary Stone, and by comprehending life and death, seeing the great terror between life and death, you can truly master the Yin-Yang Forbidden."

Qi Yuan held a common-looking stone in his hand, with a puzzled expression.

[The Yin-Yang Boundary Stone, a peculiar stone within the Yin-Yang Forbidden.]

"How do I comprehend life and death, and see through life and death?" Qi Yuan asked.

"I do not know." Ying shook her head. "That depends on your own comprehension."

Ying finished speaking and retreated into the coffin.

Qi Yuan held the Yin-Yang Boundary Stone, led by Little Bride, and left the place.

"Comprehending life and death, could this mean that I have to die?"

He held the Yin-Yang Boundary Stone and thought for a moment, then picked up the great treasure sword and stabbed himself, creating a hole in his body.

Blood flowed.

Beside him, Little Bride immediately revealed an expression of apparent anxiety.

She instantly covered Qi Yuan's body with her red armor.

Qi Yuan's wound also disappeared in an instant.

Little Bride had actively merged with him, entering a defensive stance.

Seeing this, Qi Yuan felt a pain in his heart.

His wound had been healed by the armor...
