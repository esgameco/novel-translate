As soon as Zero One finished the video, it came out and sat upright on the living room sofa, seemingly dozing off.

Not far away, Xie Xinsu secretly glanced at Zero One, her mind relaxed.

In front of the robot, she didn't hide her peculiarities at all, such as sitting in meditation to cultivate.

The stupid robots of this world didn't understand what this was at all.

Even if they saw it, they wouldn't record it or make associations.

"The spiritual energy here is so rich, there must be an ancient magical treasure present!"

Her pink nose twitched, wanting to find the source of the spiritual energy.

"When they're asleep, I need to go look for where the treasure is! Such an unclaimed magical item is surely a heaven-sent opportunity!"

Xie Xinsu was very excited.

Could this be considered the end of her suffering and the start of sweetness? After enduring so much hardship, it was time for her to enjoy herself a bit.

She continued to sit in meditation and cultivate.

However, although the spiritual energy was abundant, her cultivation progress was worryingly slow.

She quickly discovered this problem.

"A cat's body is ultimately not suitable for the immortal cultivation methods I learned before..."

The immortal cultivation method she had learned was a grand and righteous path.

However, it was suitable for human cultivators to practice.

She had never mastered immortal methods suitable for beast cultivators before.

Now as she tried to cultivate, it felt strange.

"Don't worry about it, just keep cultivating. As long as there are no problems, keep going!"

All immortal methods lead to the great Dao.

Even if some small issues arose, it wasn't a big deal.

At this time, the night grew darker.

The entire Military Security neighborhood, except for a few street lamps emitting an orange glow, appeared especially gloomy and quiet.

The entire Arching Star, viewed from space, looked like a large ball shrouded in mist.

Above this mist, two distinctly different types of fog were merging... devouring each other.

In the living room, the dozing Zero One suddenly opened its eyes.

It couldn't sleep.

Especially after posting the video.

Every so often, it had to go check if anyone had commented on the video it posted.

It opened the video and looked at the red notification dots, unable to resist clicking in.

A cat cooking and meditating to cultivate immortality - such an interesting video easily gained traffic support.

Sure enough, just a few hours after posting, there were already hundreds of comments.

"Can I come to your house to watch the cat cook?"

"I stole the video and sent it to my crush, inviting her to come to my place to watch a cat cook."

"It's 4 AM now, only three more hours until I have to get up for work."

"Can an expert tell me if this video is real or synthesized? How can a cat cook, and what's with the meditation?"

"As we all know, videos can't be faked, so this must be real."

"Cats are very smart, sometimes smarter than people. It's normal for them to be able to cook!"

"???"

The video comments were varied.

Most were saying how cute and clever the cat was.

Of course, some people also said the video was synthesized.

After all, a cat cooking... was too unbelievable.

In this world, cats were cats. It was already amazing if they could understand human commands, let alone cook.

How could an ordinary cat possibly cook?

Zero One looked at these comments, and ripples stirred in its calm electronic heart.

It posted a comment in the comment section to clarify:

"Video is real footage, not synthesized."

Seeing that it had gained over a hundred new followers, Zero One felt an inexplicable sense of joy arise in its heart.

It glanced at the now sleeping Xie Xinsu, thinking about what to film tomorrow.

A few days later.

Xie Xinsu lay limp on the sofa, feeling very aggrieved in her cat heart.

This robot wasn't human at all! It had been tormenting her these past few days.

At first, just performing cooking was fine, but later it made her perform with nunchucks.

Unfortunately, her delicate body couldn't even grip the nunchucks steadily, so the performance was... quite miserable.

This robot really wasn't human, taking such endless delight in this.

Xie Xinsu felt like a performing animal.

Especially when she saw Zero One showing off the video's view count in front of her, she really wanted to leave a comment:

"Involved party here, refuse animal performances!"

But she had no phone.

She lay on the sofa, feeling that cat life was bleak.

But thinking that she could cultivate, she perked up again.

When her cultivation succeeded, she would definitely force this robot to cook, perform with nunchucks, take baths, and then film videos.

With this thought, she rallied her spirits to cultivate.

"Hmm... the spiritual energy has become even richer, that magical treasure... smells so good."

She had a intoxicated expression, looking just like a white cat eating corn, bewitched.

Regarding that treasure, Xie Xinsu's original plan was to sneak in and steal it when Qi Yuan wasn't around.

But that hateful robot kept watching her and making her film videos, so she had no chance to get close, and hadn't even been able to enter Qi Yuan's room.

At this moment, Qi Yuan in the room opened his eyes, a smile appearing in them.

"I've finally reached Foundation Establishment... No need to fear flying on planes anymore."

After more than a month in this world, he had finally reached Foundation Establishment.

Of course, unlike the previous Heavenly Dao Foundation Establishment, this time it was very simple. Qi Yuan casually had Wild Revelry find some mutated sea monsters, refined these mutated sea monsters into Foundation Establishment spirit objects, and used them to establish his foundation.

After Foundation Establishment, Qi Yuan's strength increased greatly. Even if problems occurred while flying, he could fall from the plane without worrying about the risk of perishing.

"Unfortunately... this curse is still too powerful.

Even with Foundation Establishment, I've only increased my lifespan by a few years, unable to resolve the curse."

The curse on his body was somewhat terrifying.

Qi Yuan had a premonition that he likely wouldn't be able to resolve it until reaching the Yin Spirit realm.

This was quite extreme.

In his mind, the image of that featherless bird he had seen before kept appearing.

That bird's status must have been very high.

Otherwise, how could he, as a mortal back then, become cursed with death in a year just from seeing that bird in his mind?

"However... if I participate in the martial arts tournament now... I should be able to dominate, right?"

Qi Yuan thought to himself.

The curse had been suppressing his strength all along.

But now he was a genuine Foundation Establishment cultivator.

Moreover, his martial arts cultivation had progressed rapidly.

The "Bright Martial Resonance Fist" had already reached the tenth level.

Before the martial arts tournament began, he was confident he could reach the twelfth level.

And then... break through the limits of martial arts.

He looked forward to seeing the world after breaking through the martial arts limit.

Just then, a message notification on his phone interrupted Qi Yuan's contemplation.

He opened the message and found it was a video.

In the video, a cat was using nunchucks, performing quite impressively and interestingly.

The video was sent by his old classmate Li Yan.

Below the video were a few messages.

"Is this your account?"

"I came across this video and it looked familiar."

"Isn't this the decor of your home?"

"You got a new cat? It's pretty funny. How did you film this video?"

"So envious, 100,000 followers. Remember to help me out if you get famous!"

Li Yan seemed quite excited in her messages.
