Maple Grove Town was an ordinary mortal town. Because many maple trees grew in this area, with leaves as red as fire, wealthy families often brought their children here to enjoy the scenery when the season was right.

At this moment, Chen Xiyu lifted the curtain of the carriage, a curious expression on her face.

"There's a strange man in a red robe playing chess in North Wild Forest?"

"Yes, miss. A passing woodcutter saw him, but his clothing and appearance were too strange, so no one dared approach," the maid replied.

Chen Xiyu's eyes lit up: "Xiao Xi, do you think that person... could he be one of the legendary immortals?"

In the mortal world, there had long been rumors of immortals within a thousand miles.

But Chen Xiyu had never seen an immortal before.

She had loved strange tales since childhood and was especially interested in such things.

"No! Miss, what if he's a bad person!

There have been many skin-flaying incidents in town recently. Miss, it's best not to go deep into the mountains and forests!" The maid Xiao Xi hurriedly tried to dissuade her.

Maple Grove Town had never been very peaceful.

A few days ago, another skin-flaying incident occurred. In a village near Maple Grove Town, two hundred households woke up from their sleep one night to suddenly find that over half the villagers had been flayed in their sleep, covered in blood, terrifyingly gruesome.

Maple Grove Town and the surrounding cities were in a state of panic.

After all, in the Kingdom of Huan, skin-flaying incidents occurred periodically.

In the past, the king had even spent enormous resources to invite immortal masters from ten thousand miles away, but to no avail.

The skin-flaying incidents continued to occur.

Chen Xiyu shook her head, her attitude very determined.

"I want to go take a look."

Two hours later, Chen Xiyu headed to North Wild Forest accompanied by several guards.

The forest was full of shrubs and thorny vines. Even with several guards clearing the way, Chen Xiyu's delicate white arms were inevitably scratched several times, leaving trails of blood.

But she remained excited and in high spirits.

"Miss, look, over there!"

At this moment, the maid Xiao Xi pointed into the distance, her face showing delight.

Chen Xiyu looked over, curiosity in her eyes.

In the depths of the forest ahead, a man in a red robe sat upright on a stone bench. His eyes were tightly closed, but even from his outline, one could see his extraordinary handsomeness.

"I never knew there could be such a handsome man in this world." Chen Xiyu was dumbstruck.

Her originally fair arms now looked quite rough in comparison.

"He must be no ordinary person!" Chen Xiyu's heart leapt with excitement.

She wanted to seek an immortal destiny and find the true culprit behind the skin-flaying incidents.

"Miss, should we go over and ask?" Xiao Xi inquired.

Chen Xiyu quickly shook her head: "His eyes are closed, he may be busy. We shouldn't disturb him."

Chen Xiyu hesitated, only daring to steal a glance at the man in the blood-red robe from afar.

About an hour passed, and the man still sat motionless, occasionally reaching out to place a black stone on the chessboard.

This demeanor further convinced Chen Xiyu that the man in the blood-red robe before her was no mere mortal.

"Let's go back!" Chen Xiyu made her decision. She had to seize this opportunity.

How could an immortal casually bestow an opportunity on her?

She was just an ordinary person, nothing special, not the protagonist of the world.

She would go home and bring out all the gold and silver treasures she had saved over the years, as well as various rare and strange objects she had collected, to offer to the immortal, all for the chance at an immortal destiny.

She quickly returned home and brought out all her savings.

However, her actions also drew questioning from her family members.

Her cousin sister came upon hearing the news, her tone not very polite: "With Second Uncle not at home, you're just giving away the clan's wealth as you please?

Little sister, the world is chaotic now, don't be fooled by swindlers.

You believe just any country bumpkin putting on an act?"

Faced with her cousin sister, Chen Xiyu didn't explain much, but said: "This is my money!"

With that, she turned and left.

Her cousin sister watched Chen Xiyu's retreating figure, pity in her eyes: "Little sister is still too naive. How could there be so many immortals in the world?

Even if they were immortals, how could they bestow immortal destiny so easily? More often than not, they just take everything and leave. People's hearts are treacherous, and immortals even more so."

In this country, such things happened often. Some master would encounter an "immortal" and offer half his family fortune seeking an immortal destiny.

Unfortunately, those "immortals" would often take the fortune and run, the "immortal destiny"... nothing but empty promises.

"Hopefully little sister will be more settled after being deceived this time, and stop daydreaming all day."

Meanwhile, Chen Xiyu headed towards North Wild Forest carrying gold, silver, and various strange treasures she had collected.

Her heart was full of trepidation, as well as uncertainty about the future.

She understood what her cousin sister had said.

But she didn't want to give up, nor did she want to just muddle through life aimlessly.

As night fell, Chen Xiyu once again arrived about fifty meters from the chessboard.

She looked at that figure, her emotions in turmoil.

Seeing that man still in silent meditation, she didn't go disturb him.

Time passed day by day.

Chen Xiyu had been coming here for two days.

The man on the stone bench still sat there, unmoving.

If not for him occasionally placing a stone, Chen Xiyu would have thought he was a corpse.

Finally, on the fourth day, Qi Yuan opened his eyes.

Chen Xiyu, who had been sitting nearby, showed excitement in her eyes.

The immortal had finally awakened! She hurriedly tidied herself up a bit, and had the guards bring over the various treasures as she walked forward.

Qi Yuan's gaze fell on Chen Xiyu, his expression enigmatic.

Over these past days, he had of course been aware of Chen Xiyu's arrival.

However, he had paid her no mind.

Chen Xiyu approached, a respectful expression on her face. She was about to speak when she heard the immortal say:

His voice was like a pool of water in a dark pond, clear and emerald green.

"You wish to seek an immortal destiny?"

The immortal's words were simple and direct.

Chen Xiyu's heart tightened, and she hurriedly said respectfully: "This humble girl is willing to offer everything, only asking the master to bestow an immortal destiny."

Chen Xiyu lowered her head, her attitude extremely devout.

The guards behind her opened the treasure chests they were carrying, and dazzling jewels and precious objects came into view.

All sorts of gold ornaments, silver ingots, pearl hairpins, and jewelry.

The chest wasn't large, but it was packed full of various items.

Another chest contained various ancient texts Chen Xiyu had collected, as well as some strange objects.

To be honest, these things were enough to move some Qi Condensation stage cultivators to bestow a crude "immortal destiny".

Qi Yuan glanced at these objects, curiosity in his expression.

Most of the strange things Chen Xiyu had collected in the other chest were useless, but there was one item that emitted a faint spiritual energy fluctuation. For a mid Qi Condensation cultivator, it could be considered an unexpected delight.

Thinking of this, Qi Yuan smiled faintly: "If you had come half a year earlier, it would have been interesting."

If she had come half a year earlier, these things would have had some use for Qi Yuan.
