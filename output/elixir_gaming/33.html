Qi Yuan carefully looked at the books.

The worry on his brow gradually eased.

"Is this all there is to the Black Mountain Sect?"

Originally, the Black Mountain Sect was quite prominent in the Great Merchant Kingdom.

Qi Yuan had thought that the Black Mountain Sect's strength was absolutely formidable.

But now, it seems that the main sect's strength in the Great Merchant Kingdom is only in the third tier.

The first tier is the three major Immortal Cultivation sects.

The second tier is at least those with the strength of a Nascent Soul, or have the means to withstand the attacks of a Nascent Soul expert.

But the Black Mountain Sect has neither. In the Black Mountain Sect, there are only seven Elixir Dao Perfected.

Among the seven Elixir Dao Perfected, the strongest is the sect master, who is only at the middle Elixir Dao stage.

The rest are only at the initial Elixir Dao stage.

This level of strength in the Great Merchant Kingdom only belongs to the third tier.

"In other words, without the Black Chicken Demonic Ancestor, the Black Mountain Sect is just this?"

The more Qi Yuan thought about it, the more at ease he felt.

The knife had already awakened its spiritual intelligence. If it reached the late Elixir Dao stage and its protective elixir light was fully cultivated, he would still have some trepidation.

But just a middle Elixir Dao and a few initial Elixir Dao, with their Golden Core still not of high grade, he had no need to worry.

His eyes gleamed as he sneered, "With this strength, they still dare to come and cause me trouble? They have already invited their own deaths!"

"Strike first, and the one who strikes later will suffer!"

"They must not have expected that I would transcend time and space to slaughter them in advance, nipping this cause and effect in the bud."

Qi Yuan planned his strategy.

The Black Chicken Demonic Ancestor was not within the Black Mountain Sect, so as long as he was fast enough, he could completely take his revenge and then flee.

"It's a pity, I'm still too weak. Otherwise, would I still need to avoid the Black Chicken Demonic Ancestor?

I could just directly behead him!"

"Black Chicken Demonic Ancestor, I will remember you!"

"If only I could truly travel through time and space, I would definitely slaughter you when you were still young and drink your chicken soup, or dance on your grave after your lifespan ends."

Qi Yuan couldn't help but think of a female hero in the Go world on the Blue Star.

That female hero was a powerful expert who had mastered the rules of time.

She had defeated the current number one female Go player in the present, and the former number one female Go player in the past.

She was revered as "Punching the Southern Mountain Nursing Home" and "Kicking the Northern Sea Kindergarten".

"Senior Brother Qi Yuan, what are you doing here?" At this moment, a familiar voice came.

Zheng Jiang He was staring at Qi Yuan with a puzzled expression.

When Zheng Jiang He came to the third floor of the library, he noticed Qi Yuan.

Qi Yuan's attire was strange, and his behavior was sneaky, not like a good person.

Hiding in the corner, trying not to attract attention, how could that not be suspicious?

Zheng Jiang He had been observing for a while before confirming that this was indeed the senior disciple of the Seven-Colored Peak, Senior Brother Qi Yuan.

Seeing Zheng Jiang He, Qi Yuan was also startled.

It was like a guilty couple being discovered in the library, but he quickly calmed down.

"Mm, I came to look at some information."

"Senior Brother is also looking at information about the Black Mountain Sect?

If you're done, could you please give me the materials?

I need to take these materials out of the library and give them to the elders."

Zheng Jiang He said seriously.

"No problem." Qi Yuan put down the books, then suddenly asked, "What's written here is true, right? The Black Chicken Demonic Ancestor has always been outside the Black Mountain Sect?"

Zheng Jiang He felt Qi Yuan's question was strange, but still answered truthfully, "The Black Chicken Demonic Ancestor's cultivation base is in the Myriad Demon Ridge, thousands of miles away from the Black Mountain Sect.

The rumor is that the Black Chicken Demonic Ancestor is recently seeking a breakthrough to the late Nascent Soul stage, and should be in closed-door cultivation for the time being."

"That's good." Qi Yuan's heart was greatly relieved.

Zheng Jiang He looked at him strangely, "I wonder what Senior Brother is investigating the Black Mountain Sect for?"

Quick, go buy that cleaver!" Elder Qi's voice was urgent.

Dong Xian was stunned.

If it was just an ordinary cleaver and Qi Yuan was just an ordinary disciple of the Divine Light Sect, he might still have a chance to buy it.

But... that's not the case.

Despite Elder Qi's instructions, he still mustered the courage to go.
