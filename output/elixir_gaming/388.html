Qiong Yunting finally left, satisfied.

At this moment, Qi Yuan's hand pressed on Jiang Lingsu's delicate hand, stopping her from pulling back the pink storage bag: "Junior sister, I don't want to owe money. The feeling of being in debt is really not good - I want to run away whenever I see the creditor.

Qi Yuan was saying things that were difficult to understand.

Jiang Lingsu seemed to be seriously considering it.

At this time, Jiang Qian, the manager of the Jiang family business, stopped in her tracks. Hearing Qi Yuan's words, she couldn't help but show a helpless expression in her eyes.

"Young miss, investing in housing and land contracts in Cloud Sky City is not very wise," Jiang Qian couldn't help but remind her.

In her heart, her evaluation of Qi Yuan dropped a few more points.

Although she couldn't discern Qi Yuan's cultivation level, just based on his face alone, it would be enough for him to marry into the Jiang family.

Jiang Lingsu glanced back at Jiang Qian: "I'm investing in my senior brother, not in Cloud Sky City. I trust my senior brother!"

"Save me a good spot!"

She could still be of help to her senior brother.

Jiang Qian was quite envious of Jiang Lingsu.

The young miss was from the main branch of the Jiang family, born with a silver spoon in her mouth, enjoying abundant cultivation resources.

Moreover, the young miss had a high aptitude for cultivation and was qualified to see the Jiang family's greatest treasure, enhancing her talent with it.

As for herself, she was from a branch family. Without exceptional talent or outstanding beauty, it was almost impossible to make significant progress on the path of immortal cultivation.

Generally, people like her would go out to manage some businesses, earning spirit stones and resources for the main branch of the Jiang family to cultivate.

So naturally, she was extremely envious of the young miss, who excelled in both appearance and talent.

If she could have any one of the young miss's looks, talent, or background, her life would be completely different.

After reporting the situation, Jiang Qian walked out of the pavilion, her eyes carrying a hint of envy.

If only she were born in the main branch, she could choose what she liked, even keeping a trophy husband wouldn't be bad.

Soon, she pushed these thoughts aside, hurrying back to deal with the Jiang family's business in this area.

A man with shifty eyes appeared before Jiang Qian. He was wearing a blood-red robe and holding a fan, looking extremely flamboyant.

Seeing the newcomer, Jiang Qian frowned slightly: "Young Master Feng."

The person before her was named Feng Ruohan.

His name was nice, but his reputation was not good. He was a well-known playboy in Cloud Sky City.

However, the Feng family was a major family in Cloud Sky City and even in the entire Helan Province, with much stronger power than the Jiang family.

Feng Ruohan's red robe was clearly imitating the legendary Blood Robe Sword Saint.

He closed the folding fan in his hand and said, "I heard that the young miss of the Jiang family's main branch is as beautiful as a heavenly fairy. I wonder if I might have the fortune to meet her?"

"That would need to be asked of the young miss herself."

"Manager Jiang, you're just someone from a branch family in the Jiang clan. You probably don't have much hope for promotion in this lifetime," Feng Ruohan suddenly sighed.

"What do you mean, Young Master Feng?" Jiang Qian frowned, feeling wary in her heart.

"It's simple. I want to make a deal with you," Feng Ruohan said with a smile on his face. "I've long admired the Jiang young miss and wish to form a marriage alliance with the Jiang clan. But I'm not very good-looking, and the young miss has a handsome senior brother by her side.

So, I hope to get your help. Pour this vial of potion into the bowl the young miss drinks from.

Once she drinks it, she'll fall deeply in love with me, and the Jiang family won't notice anything.

Don't you want to cooperate with our Feng family? As long as you help me this time, the Feng family will immediately assist you, helping you resolve your crisis.

I will also strongly support you behind the scenes, helping you get promoted. In this lifetime, you won't be limited to being a mere manager, and might even have a chance at reaching the Divine Embryo realm.

How about it? Is my sincerity enough?" Feng Ruohan stared into Jiang Qian's eyes.

Jiang Qian's face turned pale, and her hand was already preparing to activate a teleportation talisman: "I'm sorry, Young Master Feng, but this kind of joke is not funny."

The expression on Feng Ruohan's face froze: "You mean... you want to refuse?"

Killing intent surged in his eyes.

Before coming here, he had already set up a formation in this area.

Since Jiang Qian had heard his plan and didn't agree, there was only one path left for her - death.

After Jiang Qian dies, he would then properly meet that Jiang Lingsu.

He grinned, revealing a cruel and lewd smile in his eyes.

"Don't worry, I'll only play with your corpse for one day."

Jiang Qian's face turned deathly pale as she realized the teleportation talisman had failed at this moment.

However, just then, suddenly, a flash of red light appeared in the sky.

A thin line appeared on Feng Ruohan's neck, and in an instant, his head separated from his neck, revealing a smooth cut.

His body collapsed to the ground with a thud.

A cold voice filled with killing intent came:

"Indeed, it's better to stay at home. You don't encounter so many disgusting things.

Sigh, the atmosphere in the immortal cultivation world is still too poor."

A handsome man in a blood-colored robe appeared, his words carrying a hint of helplessness.

Seeing the newcomer, Jiang Qian's eyes changed slightly: "Young Master Qi!"

She hadn't expected that the one who saved her would be Qi Yuan.

This level of strength... Divine Embryo realm?

Her heart suddenly tensed.

Young Master Qi had killed Feng Ruohan. The sky over Cloud Sky City... was about to change.
