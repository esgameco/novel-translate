Xu Renshan cleared his throat.

"What do you all think is the most precious foundation-building spiritual object in the world?"

He looked down at the disciples below, exuding an air of a highly accomplished cultivator.

"Is it the Nine Heavens Divine Thunder Fruit?" a female disciple tentatively asked.

Seeing this, Xu Renshan said, "The Nine Heavens Divine Thunder Fruit is rumored to only be born in the Nine Heavens Thunder Realm, an upper-grade foundation-building spiritual object that can be considered rare in the world, but it is still a bit distant from being the most precious."

"Star Tear?"

"Primal Chaos Origin Qi?"

"Sovereign Bone?"

The many disciples on the scene all mentioned some extremely precious foundation-building spiritual objects.

These foundation-building spiritual objects were all just legendary precious ones.

Xu Renshan looked at the constantly pondering Qi Yuan and asked, "Qi Yuan, what do you think it is?"

Qi Yuan replied, "Since it is the Heavenly Dao foundation-building, shouldn't it be related to the Heavenly Dao?"

Xu Renshan praised, "You're right, Heavenly Dao foundation-building, the required foundation-building spiritual object should be..."

At this moment, Qi Yuan suddenly felt a great enlightenment, as if his mind had been opened.

He felt like his brain had grown!

He was about to comprehend it! His expression became excited and somewhat frenzied.

"I've comprehended it, the Heavenly Dao foundation-building, it's actually like this."

Qi Yuan solemnly paid his respects to Xu Renshan as a disciple, then took his leave.

The inspiration was fleeting, he had to hurry back to record it.

Xu Renshan saw this scene and revealed a smile.

He continued to explain to the rest of the Shenguang Sect disciples, "There is a rare object in the world called the Heavenly Vein Qi, rumored to be a wisp of qi that has overflowed from the descent of the Heavenly Dao, obtaining it can forge the Heavenly Dao foundation."

These were things Xu Renshan had seen in ancient texts.

"The Heavenly Vein Qi is the most precious foundation-building spiritual object."

...

On the Seven-Colored Peak, Qi Yuan sat blankly, his brain unusually rapidly processing.

"Heavenly Dao foundation-building..."

"So-called Heavenly Dao foundation-building is actually using the Heavenly Dao as the foundation-building spiritual object."

Qi Yuan had comprehended it, his brain had grown. If his goal wasn't so grand, perhaps the Heavenly Dao of this world would directly bestow blessings, allowing him to gather the Five Flowers.

The Heavenly Dao loved him too much, not only the Three Flowers but also an additional two.

Qi Yuan raised his head, looking at the sky, with a puzzled expression: "But... how do I refine the Heavenly Dao into my foundation-building spiritual object?"

"I don't know what the Heavenly Dao tastes like, is it delicious or not."

Unfortunately, no one responded to him.

"Speaking of the Heavenly Dao, I seemed to have obtained a fragment of the Heavenly Dao in the XuanYuan Forbidden Land, can that be used as a foundation-building spiritual object?"

Qi Yuan's spirit was stirred, discovering something extraordinary.

That game jade slip was not just a game to Qi Yuan.

But he was lazy, too lazy to think deeply about it.

"It's been a while since I played games, activate!"

Thinking this, Qi Yuan entered the game again.

This time, as usual, Qi Yuan was buried in the ground.

Opening the chat interface, he saw a message from Jin Li.

He revealed a smile at the corners of his mouth: "Didn't I tell you, the XuanYuan Forbidden Land is now in my shape, you can take whatever you want inside."

He was quite generous.

The most precious thing in the XuanYuan Forbidden Land, he had already taken.

As for those so-called spirit herbs, they were just snacks to him.

The ones that didn't taste good, he didn't want.

Moreover, those spirit herbs were not as effective as killing a monster.

He replied again: "Those spirit herbs are of average value, the most valuable things are the fierce beasts, killing one can give a lot of experience, leveling up quickly."

However, after sending the message, Qi Yuan felt a bit regretful.

That Jin Li was probably at a relatively low level, unable to defeat those monsters.

"If you have time, come to the Yiguan Forbidden Land, I'll take you to grind monsters, I have to say, there are really a lot of monsters here."

After sending the message, Qi Yuan left the pit.
