Moon Dynasty.

The woman in white looked at the young girl in front of her, with a helpless expression in her eyes.

"High Priestess, do you really want to go with him... to the Azure Waves Realm?"

"You're going to the Upper Realm alone, I have to stay here." Qi Jianjun's face bore a sweet smile.

This was different from the High Priestess the woman in white had seen before.

All of this was because of that man.

"High Priestess, do you think his... current state is a side effect of swallowing this realm's Heavenly Dao?" the woman in white asked.

She was actually reminding Qi Jianjun to be careful.

After all, a deranged person might hurt others at any time.

"In such a dreamlike world, isn't it nice to be a crazy person?"

Qi Jianjun answered irrelevantly.

The woman in white fell silent.

Qi Jianjun continued: "Alright, the transmission formation has been activated. You should go to the Upper Realm now.

If you can reach the Moon God Palace, tell Mother that I've found Father."

The transmission formation for ascending to the Upper Realm was random, and one couldn't be certain which area it would transport to.

It might take the woman in white hundreds or thousands of years to find the Moon God Palace.

The High Priestess glanced at the woman in white, then walked outside.

When she saw that figure in blood-red robes, her eyes lit up with an excited smile.

"Father, are we going to... the Azure Waves Realm?"

"Mm." Qi Yuan nodded.

In the past few days, he had planned to visit the world of the Moluo clan.

Unfortunately, the gate had already closed.

He threw a punch from afar at the Moluo Realm, but nothing happened.

Later, in one day, he traveled all over the Wangyue Continent, wanting to find traces of the past.

Unfortunately, whether it was Xuanyuan Forbidden Land, Yiguan Forbidden Land, Five Elements Forbidden Land, Yin-Yang Forbidden Land or Tianjue, they were all empty.

He didn't see any of his old acquaintances, nor did he see any familiar traces.

Without the power of reincarnation, without becoming a Yin Spirit, there was almost no possibility of reincarnation.

Death meant complete annihilation, dissolving into the world.

No one in the world remembered them anymore.

Except for Qi Yuan.

So Qi Yuan could only prepare to return, back to the Azure Waves Realm, and also back to Seven-Color Peak.

Of course, before going back, he wanted to visit the Ascending Heaven Pavilion.

Jiang Ya still owed him quite a few cultivation techniques.

"Everyone's here, let's go." Qi Yuan looked up and gazed deeply at the ancient Qichun tree.

The towering giant tree was covered in silver, piled with snowflakes, a beautiful sight.

"Let's go, time to return." Qi Yuan glanced at Great Wisdom True Lord, Xu Tongchen and Jiang Ya.

Four people came, five were returning, with the addition of a child.

"Sigh, it's time to leave." Great Wisdom True Lord sighed, "Am I leaving this world, or is the world leaving me."

The group of five instantly arrived at the desert from before, and quickly entered the world passage.

Qi Yuan also felt that the terrifying power he wielded over the Wangyue Continent was slowly dissipating.

However, he remained calm and unhurried.

When he stepped into the Azure Waves Realm again, Qi Yuan narrowed his eyes.

"There are quite a few people, could they be Ye Qiong's helpers?" Qi Yuan held Qi Jianjun's hand, sensing four terrifying presences.

These four terrifying presences were all Yin Spirits.

"Greetings to Elder Blood Robe Sword Immortal!" Cloud Gathering Venerable spoke.

The others also bowed in greeting.

"Elder, we are not acquainted with Venerable Ye Qiong."

"This junior Leichen pays respects to Elder."

The four Yin Spirits present all looked at Qi Yuan with respect.

The lofty Yin Spirits faced Qi Yuan as if they were Purple Mansion cultivators facing Yin Spirits.

Many people also noticed the young girl Qi Yuan was holding hands with, their eyes filled with strange expressions.

"Are you all Yin Spirits from the Eastern Lands?" Qi Yuan asked.

He only knew that there were three publicly known Yin Spirits in the Eastern Lands.

"Yes."

"No, this junior has come to apologize." The elderly Tiger Roar Venerable was like a child before Qi Yuan.

Qi Yuan glanced at Tiger Roar Venerable, who hurriedly explained the circumstances.

Finally, Tiger Roar Venerable added: "I knew nothing of Venerable Ye Qiong's schemes."

"Those who don't know are not guilty." Qi Yuan's eyes held a gentle smile.

Indeed, strength was everything.

Back then, when the Palace of Light initiated the Hundred Nations Blood War, wanting to incite war in the Hundred Nations, not a single Yin Spirit was willing to speak up for the Hundred Nations.

Now, just because Ling Jiao had spoken a few harsh words to him, Cloud Gathering Venerable had stopped the Water Cloud Palace from sending troops.

Before Qi Yuan could even express an opinion, the master of Water Cloud Palace had been restrained and brought here, to be dealt with as he saw fit.

Hearing Qi Yuan's words, Tiger Roar Venerable breathed a sigh of relief.

He was truly afraid of the Blood Robe Sword Immortal.

Through the descriptions of Gu Xiaoyao and other Divine Infants, they had confirmed that the Blood Robe Sword Immortal had swallowed the Heavenly Dao of the Wangyue Continent.

And to swallow the Heavenly Dao, one needed at least Great Venerable level strength.

In other words, the Blood Robe Sword Immortal was at least a Great Venerable.

The power he possessed in the Wangyue Continent was even higher than that of an ordinary Great Venerable.

"You've come to find me, not just to welcome me back, right?" Qi Yuan looked at these four Yin Spirits.

For some reason, he had a feeling similar to when those Purple Mansion cultivators sought him out after he destroyed the Palace of Light.

Cloud Gathering Venerable stepped forward and said: "Regarding Elder's affairs, we have already sealed off the information. Currently, besides us four, only Ice Soul Venerable knows."

"I've noted your and Ice Soul's kindness." Qi Yuan said, as Tiger Roar Venerable had just explained the circumstances, informing him of Cloud Gathering Venerable's assistance to the Blood Robe Alliance.

Cloud Gathering Venerable's face lit up with joy.

At the same time, he sighed inwardly that sometimes it was better to be a bit timid.

Otherwise, if he had gone with Venerable Ye Qiong and Venerable You Qing to deal with the Blood Robe Sword Immortal this time, it would have been like sheep entering a tiger's den.

"Elder, we have come with a request," Cloud Gathering Venerable spoke up at this time.

Because the other Yin Spirits present were all looking at him.

"What is it?" Qi Yuan knew there were no free lunches in this world.

"When the great calamity approaches, we hope Elder will open the way for us to enter the Wangyue Continent for shelter!" Cloud Gathering Venerable looked at him expectantly.

Even a Great Venerable would be powerless against the great calamity.

If it were an ordinary Wangyue Continent, entering it would not protect them from the calamity.

But if it were a Great Venerable who had swallowed the Heavenly Dao, that would be different.

If they took shelter there, there was a high chance of avoiding the calamity.

Qi Yuan looked at these Yin Spirits: "If you want my help, it's simple. I need cultivation techniques, the more the better!"

After all, there were still hundreds of years until the great calamity.

It was still early.

Getting the benefits in hand first was the most real.

"Does Elder have any other conditions?" Leichen Venerable asked.

Because what the Blood Robe Sword Immortal said was really too simple.

"That's all for now, I'll think about others later," Qi Yuan said.

The other Yin Spirits were all somewhat apprehensive.

They very much wanted to tell Qi Yuan, why don't you make more demands?

With too few demands, they couldn't feel at ease.

On the Divine Flower Society forum.

Little Li from Fox Den was engaged in a war of words.

"Elder Blood Robe Sword Immortal is a righteous person. He opposed the Palace of Light to stop the Hundred Nations Blood War, for the greater good."

"What bullshit greater good, cultivators like that only care about benefits. Arrogant and domineering cultivators like Blood Robe Sword Immortal deserve to be killed by Venerable Ye Qiong.

I bet those Yin Spirits gathering at the Blood Robe Alliance are there to divide up Blood Robe Sword Immortal's treasures.

You can see how many ordinary cultivators Blood Robe Sword Immortal must have killed to obtain those treasures."

"You're slandering him!" Seeing these comments, Little Li from Fox Den was furious and quickly recorded them with her image stone.

"If Blood Robe Sword Immortal thinks I'm slandering him, he's welcome to come confront me.

If he doesn't dare answer me, isn't that admitting it?

No, actually he's just not capable. He's probably long dead."

"You!" Seeing this, Little Li from Fox Den was enraged.

She could only record these posts.

After arguing for too long, she felt mentally exhausted.

Just as she was about to exit the forum, a post suddenly caught her eye.

Her pupils constricted.

《Thank you all for your concern. I, Blood Robe Sword Immortal, was indeed ambushed by Yin Spirits, but unfortunately they were too weak.》

"After entering the Wangyue Continent, one day I heard people talking bad about me behind my back.

I looked and saw it was Ye Qiong and You Qing.

They were badmouthing me together with people from the Moluo clan.

I couldn't stand it and executed them on the spot."

"Elder Blood Robe Sword Immortal is indeed still alive!" Little Li from Fox Den was excited.

She quickly replied: "I knew the big shot would survive. Big shot, you're awesome! Can I have a friend spot?"

"Sure, I'll add you." At this moment, Blood Robe Sword Immortal replied to her.

Little Li in the Fox Den widened her eyes.

"The big shot actually added me as a friend!" She was elated and overjoyed in her heart. She wanted to tell the elders of her clan about this.

But on second thought, she decided against it.

"Big shot, after you left, many people in the Divine Flower Society were slandering you behind your back!" After adding him as a friend, Little Li from Fox Den quickly recounted what had happened on the Divine Flower Society forum.

"What? People are badmouthing me behind my back!" Qi Yuan took a look and immediately became agitated. "The internet is not lawless. These people must pay the price!"

He hadn't even cursed anyone online yet, and someone was already cursing him.

"Big shot, I've recorded it all. Do you want me to send it to you?" Little Li from Fox Den sent the message, then worried, "How should I send the image stone to the big shot?"

Liqi City was a rather remote cultivation city.

The strongest cultivation sect nearby was only at the late Yuan Dan stage.

At this time, Wang Shenghai was seeking pleasure in Red Lip House, but his brows were furrowed, unable to relax.

A group of cultivators were also drinking and making merry.

"Brother Wang, why so gloomy?" a Yuan Dan cultivator asked, feeling curious.

"Sigh, didn't I join that Divine Flower Society? Originally, I thought Venerable Yiqiong would successfully kill that great demon Blood Robe Sword God.

Who knew that useless Venerable Yiqiong would be killed by Blood Robe Sword God instead, what a waste!" Wang Shenghai spoke crudely.

After all, how could such a big shot have the free time to pay attention to a small fry like him?

The few Yuan Dan cultivators in Red Lip House were all dumbfounded.

The aura of each cultivator was unfathomable, they couldn't discern their cultivation level at all.

At least Divine Embryo stage!

Yin Spirit... this was actually a Yin Spirit bigshot! Such a bigshot, coming to their small place!

Qi Jianjun then took out a jade slip and read coldly: "Wang Shenghai, seventy years ago, in order to obtain a first-grade Foundation Establishment spiritual item, plotted to kill his junior brother Li Xiangyang's entire family; twenty years ago, forcibly abducted women...

Six days ago, spouted nonsense on the Divine Flower Society forum, slandering Blood Robe Sword God.

His crimes are heinous, punishable by multiple offenses, to be executed by... firing squad."

Qi Yuan stretched out his hand, making a gun-firing gesture: "Don't badmouth people behind their backs in the future.

Also, after you die, you'll become the biggest celebrity in this city, no need to thank me."

Qi Yuan fired.

Bang! Wang Shenghai died instantly.

Until death, he never imagined he would die at the hands of a legendary Yin Spirit.

The cultivators present felt both terrified and shocked.

A Yin Spirit had actually come to Liqi City.

This Wang Shenghai would definitely become the biggest celebrity in Liqi City from now on.

After all, a Yin Spirit Venerable brought a group of subordinates, traveled tens of thousands of miles, to personally execute a mere Yuan Dan cultivator.

Qi Yuan swept his gaze over everyone present and said softly: "Rectifying the atmosphere of the cultivation world starts with me.

As for you all, do not commit evil, no matter how small."

After speaking, Qi Yuan left directly with this group of Purple Palace musicians.

Only those cultivators who could remain dazed for a day were left in Red Lip House.

What happened today was too outrageous!
