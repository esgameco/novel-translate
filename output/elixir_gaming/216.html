He wished he could find Qi Yuan and send him to the North Sea God's Eye.

The Twilight Elder stood to the side, his eyes filled with emotion: "The Origin Heavenly Venerable has always done things differently. Nothing he does would be surprising."

At the Hundred Flowers Gathering, the Twilight Elder had witnessed Qi Yuan's uniqueness firsthand.

This uniqueness - daring to take action against evil gods, daring to be the first in the world - filled the Twilight Elder with admiration.

The Fire Lotus Elder also looked curious: "I'd like to meet this extraordinary person too."

Qi Yuan, who could visualize twenty-one legends and eight myths - just what kind of divine being was he?

Such a legendary ancient god would likely be on par with the Mortal Realm's number one, the Holy Mother of No Food.

The Fire Lotus Elder was also transformed from an ancient heroic spirit.

Her previous incarnation had never met the Holy Mother of No Food, but had heard tales of her deeds.

The Fire Lotus Elder held the utmost reverence for the Holy Mother of No Food.

In some ways, she was also half a follower of the Holy Mother of No Food's teachings.

"Oh, Old Thunder God, you're back?" Just then, a voice came from the sky.

A flash of light stopped, and two figures appeared before everyone present.

All eyes turned to look at Qi Yuan, with surprise and awe in their gazes.

So this was that peerless genius?

"You little brat, you know to come back?" The Great Thunder Venerable looked at Qi Yuan and said angrily, "Hurry up and get ready, we're going to the North Sea God's Eye!"

"The North Sea God's Eye? What for?" Qi Yuan was confused. "Old Thunder God, stop being so flustered. Just tell me what's going on!"

"What else could it be for? To escape with our lives, look at the mess you've made!" The Great Thunder Venerable seemed to explode with rage. "You sent your subordinates to kill the evil gods of the Maple and Forest countries. Sure, it felt good, but what about the consequences?"

The Great Thunder Venerable pointed at the Twilight Elder and the others, saying: "These people left the Ancient God Alliance to come support you in Green Water Village, risking their lives!"

Only then did Qi Yuan look towards the Twilight Elder and the others.

Qi Qi was also introducing these people in his ear.

Hearing Qi Qi's introduction, Qi Yuan's gaze softened.

Leaving the Ancient God Alliance and traveling thousands of miles to Green Water Village - this kind of favor was something Qi Yuan would remember.

When it came to friends, Qi Yuan was never stingy.

"Do you want to become my subjects?" he asked the Twilight Elder and the others.

At these words, the ancient gods of the Ancient God Alliance were all a bit stunned.

They had come to support Green Water Village prepared to die.

But now the Origin Heavenly Venerable was asking them to be his subjects? They were confused.

The Great Thunder Venerable glared at Qi Yuan: "Now you're still thinking about your damn subjects! Hurry to the North Sea God's Eye, otherwise when the evil god army camps attack, you'll die here!"

The other ancient gods felt the same way.

The urgent matter at hand was to leave here and go to the North Sea God's Eye.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan shook his head: "The people from the evil god army camps won't be coming."

"What?" The Great Thunder Venerable didn't understand.

"Just now, Zhu Zhuangshi and I took a casual stroll and blew up four nearby evil god army camps."

Qi Yuan said casually, taking out a bottle of fish-blasting thunderbolt bombs.

"The quality of these firecrackers is really good, and they can even limit the blast range.

It's a pity, there were only eight large ones in total, and four have already been used.

In the future we can only blast some small fish and shrimp." Qi Yuan lamented.

"Yin Thunder Pearls!" The Great Thunder Venerable's eyes widened.

As one who wielded thunder techniques, the Great Thunder Venerable knew these objects better than anyone.

This pile of small beads were actually all Yin Thunder Pearls.

Moreover, the four that were the size of fists - even the Great Thunder Venerable could sense the intense threat they posed.

The other ancient gods also looked at the Yin Thunder Pearls in Qi Yuan's hand with trepidation. The divine power in their spirit apertures involuntarily circulated to protect their bodies.

"Used four of these big ones to blow up four evil god army camps." Qi Yuan put away the Yin Thunder Pearls. "So for now, I estimate no evil god army camps will be coming.

Everyone can eat in peace."

Qi Yuan's voice was lazy.

Since killing the evil gods of the Maple and Forest countries would bring the evil god army camps to encircle and kill them,

To avoid this situation, it was simple - just destroy the evil god army camps in advance.

Without evil god army camps, wouldn't Green Water Village be worry-free?

The Great Thunder Venerable fell silent.

The ancient gods of the Ancient God Alliance present also couldn't speak.

Qi Yuan was always breaking through their understanding.

The Great Thunder Venerable took a deep breath and cursed: "In the future, whoever calls me reckless, I'll fight them!"

Reckless?

How could he compare? The Origin Heavenly Venerable was truly reckless!

Compared to the Origin Heavenly Venerable, he was just a cowardly turtle.

"So why go to the North Sea God's Eye?" Qi Yuan looked at everyone again, extending an invitation.

"Do you want to become my subjects? I'll have some small benefits to give you."

The Twilight Elder was helpless. Qi Yuan was really fixated on having subjects.

The first time they met, he had heard Qi Yuan's invitation.

The Fire Lotus Elder looked surprised, but didn't speak.

The other ancient gods were also a bit confused, not understanding Qi Yuan's meaning.

But the ancient gods who had followed Qi Yuan from the ancestral land looked at him expectantly: "My lord, do we also get small benefits?"

Looking at these people, the Pine Gazing Ancient God couldn't help saying: "The lord has already given you all divine realm nectar, what more do you want?"

At this point, the Pine Gazing Ancient God had osteoporosis and was always considering things for Qi Yuan.

Hearing this, the Twilight Elder and other ancient gods were surprised.

This benefit was divine realm nectar?

Divine realm nectar could condense the embryonic form of a divine realm.

For Venerables, it was a priceless treasure.

Many ancient gods' breathing quickened.

"We came here prepared to die anyway. If becoming subjects means becoming subjects, getting divine realm nectar isn't a bad deal." The Twilight Elder said with a smile.

In other circumstances, if someone tried to tempt him with divine realm nectar to become a subject, he definitely wouldn't choose it.

But now, everyone was in the same boat, sharing life and death.

There was no need to quibble over these things.

They were all going to die anyway.

And the Origin Heavenly Venerable's actions had earned their admiration.

"Then we shall serve the Origin Heavenly Venerable as our lord!" the Fire Lotus Elder said gently.

The other ancient gods also spoke up. Some hesitated, but were still willing to serve the Origin Heavenly Venerable as their lord.

In the Mortal Realm, this kind of acknowledgment of a lord, even if just verbal, would take effect.

The Great Thunder Venerable looked at Qi Yuan and snorted coldly: "I'm not interested in your divine realm nectar. I won't be your subject."

He was already a Great Venerable, and wouldn't bow down just for divine realm nectar.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan seemed a bit disappointed: "What a pity. I had originally prepared a great gift for you, Old Thunder God. Since you're unwilling to be my subject, this great gift... I'll have to reconsider."

"What great gift? Do you think I care?" The Great Thunder Venerable sneered disdainfully. But looking at Qi Yuan, he then said, "Fine, you've begged me three or four times to become a villager of Green Water Village.

Since you've lowered yourself so much, I'm kindhearted and will reluctantly agree to be your subject.

Don't think it's because I'm interested in that damn great gift of yours!"

The Great Thunder Venerable looked quite tsundere.

In the end, he still chose to become Qi Yuan's subject.

"There is much to celebrate today." Qi Yuan was also very excited.
