After all, he didn't even have money to buy Gongxing.

Most importantly, how could there be such a good thing in the world as giving both grandparents and money?

"The royal family is willing to provide some funds to assist Mr. Qi." The Seventh Prince gritted his teeth and said seriously, "The national treasury will also set aside some funds to help Mr. Qi."

If we're going to do this, we might as well do it right.

If Mr. Qi offers to send grandmothers but no one signs up, wouldn't that be very awkward?

The Seventh Prince had already thought about using money to motivate people.

Anyone who sends their blood through official channels would receive a 500 subsidy.

500 isn't much for rich people.

But for ordinary people and the poor, it's still very attractive.

So this move would definitely attract many poor people to participate.

As for the wealthy, they might not care about this 500 subsidy and not bother with it at all.

"Doesn't that mean... mostly those who lack money will participate?

By the way, are there more rich people or poor people in the Qinyuan Kingdom?" Qi Yuan asked casually.

He wasn't too concerned about the specific process, as long as the result was right.

"According to statistics, those with fewer assets are more numerous."

"As long as there are many people, proceed according to your plan.

Sigh, I'm such a good person, not only giving people grandfathers, but also giving money." Qi Yuan said smugly, feeling he was a great person.

As if whatever he did made her happy.

This made him quite surprised.

How could a mere local, even the champion of the Martial Arts Tournament, be given such importance by the royal family?

Such a punch could be said to transcend the mortal realm.

"Martial arts indeed has its merits, but at this level, it doesn't even reach Foundation Establishment..."

Wang Kaixiang found it very strange.

If the celestial way had not descended, perhaps such a powerful martial artist as Qi Yuan would attract the royal family's attention.

But now, with celestial cultivators as common as fish in a river, what did Qi Yuan have to offer?

"However, judging by the Seventh Prince's attitude towards him, it might be worth making contact. Perhaps he can be used to persuade the royal family about the Heaven Reversing Formation!"

Unable to convince the royal family for now, he decided to try other channels.

Meanwhile, in the main hall, Qi Yuan and the Seventh Prince were discussing the details of the plan.

"The plan is perfect, now we just need to implement it." Qi Yuan looked at the Seventh Prince, his eyes filled with approval.

The Seventh Prince was talented, supplementing his plan and filling in the gaps.

"The royal family will support it with all our strength."

"Alright, I'll wait for your good news. I have to go now, bye."

After speaking, Qi Yuan glanced at Ning Tao, who immediately understood and embraced Qi Yuan. Their figures disappeared in that moment.
