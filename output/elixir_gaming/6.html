"Fragments of the Heavenly Dao?"

"This game is quite generous, the things it gives are not bad."

As a person from the Blue Star, the Fragments of the Heavenly Dao were the most familiar thing to him.

"It seems that the Xuanyuan Prohibition has become mine?"

Qi Yuan accepted the Fragments of the Heavenly Dao.

He suddenly felt that the Xuanyuan Prohibition had become his own domain.

"Interesting." Qi Yuan murmured.

The Fragments of the Heavenly Dao seemed to control the authority over the Xuanyuan Prohibition.

Qi Yuan obtaining the Fragments of the Heavenly Dao was like becoming the GM of the game.

"This Xuanyuan Prohibition... is it mine?"

Qi Yuan was not pleased.

"Is this all the Fragments of the Heavenly Dao?"

"Or is it only when all the fragments are collected that it becomes useful?"

Qi Yuan shifted his attention from the Fragments of the Heavenly Dao to the Divine Brush.

"The Divine Brush is condensed from the power of the rules, can it inscribe rules on the Xuanyuan Prohibition?"

"For example, how can one enter the Xuanyuan Prohibition?"

"Then one must carry a ladder to enter."

Qi Yuan recalled some interesting things he had seen on the Blue Star, where a person could go anywhere as long as they carried a ladder on their back.

Movie theaters, amusement parks, zoos, as long as you had a ladder on your back, the staff wouldn't stop you.

Thinking about it, Qi Yuan inscribed a rule on the Xuanyuan Prohibition.

Carrying a ladder can enter, with unobstructed passage.

"It's getting late, I should go for a walk with my pet."

Qi Yuan rubbed his tired eyes.

Playing this game too much had taken a bit of a mental toll on him.

So in the Divine Light Sect, he sometimes appeared dazed and disorganized.

Of course, it wasn't entirely due to the game.

His personality was also like that.

He seemed... a bit eccentric.

Qi Yuan tidied up and prepared to take a stroll with his pet, and also to see Zhuge Miu.

At the same time, Jiang Lingsu also came out of the thatched hut.

She had been busy all day, finally finishing up the house.

Now she was thinking of going down the mountain to buy some daily necessities.

But then, footsteps approached, and Jiang Lingsu immediately looked over.

It was the senior brother going out? But when she saw the senior brother, Jiang Lingsu was stunned.

"Senior brother, what are you doing?" Jiang Lingsu's face was filled with confusion.

In her line of sight, Qi Yuan was still wearing the white robe from earlier, but unlike the initial encounter, he was holding a rope in his hand.

It looked like he was walking a pet.

But that pet... was it really a pet?

That was... a cleaver!

Yes, the end of the rope was tied to a cleaver.

And Qi Yuan was leading the cleaver, this scene was too absurd.

"I'm walking my pet." Qi Yuan said calmly, "It's been cooped up in the house for too long, its legs are about to rust. Taking it out for a walk to breathe some fresh air."

A cleaver has legs?

Walking to prevent rusting? You're treating it like it's made of spiritual iron!

This is clearly an ordinary iron cleaver.

She was now certain that this senior brother's head was a bit problematic.

"Senior brother's taste is quite peculiar." Jiang Lingsu forced out these words.

"Are you also going down the mountain? Want to come along?" Qi Yuan invited.

"No!" Jiang Lingsu quickly refused, "Senior brother, you go first, I'll go later."

If she followed the senior brother walking a cleaver, it would definitely attract too much attention.

That would be too embarrassing! Qi Yuan glanced at Jiang Lingsu and then turned his gaze back to the cleaver on the rope, focusing as he said, "Be good, listen obediently, don't struggle."

She had carefully selected this spiritual beast.
