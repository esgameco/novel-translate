A commonplace sword strike once again pierced through the shattered body of Dao Buer.

If the first strike had extinguished Dao Buer's physical form, then this second strike had shattered his divine soul.

This strike from Qi Yuan contained the full force of a Great Dao Sovereign.

Although he was incapable of injuring an ordinary Great Dao Sovereign, effortlessly slaying one was no problem.

In Dao Buer's shattered pupils, an expression of incomprehension and disbelief was etched.

"You are not... a Great Dao Sovereign, so why...?"

Over the years of verbal battles with Qi Yuan, Dao Buer had secretly probed the depths of Qi Yuan's abilities. How could Qi Yuan so easily and swiftly slay him?

"No wonder you lost, your memory seems a bit lacking." Qi Yuan said.

"I was never a Great Dao Sovereign to begin with, I am... the Purple Palace!" As the words left his mouth, Qi Yuan's hand trembled.

In that moment, Dao Buer's towering and powerful physical form and divine soul were utterly crushed.

Until his dying breath, Dao Buer's face remained etched with an expression of incomprehension.

He could not fathom how Qi Yuan's words, which he felt were sincere, could be true. Could his opponent really be from the Purple Palace? How was that possible?

"I... have been slain by one from the Purple Palace!"

With his last vestiges of life, he let out a furious roar before instantly fading into nothingness.

At the same time, the cultivators within the Heavenly Profound Bizarre Land stared with eyes wide in disbelief, no less than Dao Buer himself.

"Is that it? He's dead?"

Originally, they had thought this would be an earth-shattering battle that would even affect them. After all, Dao Buer had directly absorbed all the spiritual energy within the Heavenly Profound Bizarre Land.

This had left them in a weakened state.

They had expected a cataclysmic battle that would tear the heavens and earth asunder.

Yet, it had ended in a mere instant kill!

And the one slain was the Saint Light Clan's esteemed figure.

Some cultivators then turned their gaze towards Liu Xi, their expressions tinged with regret.

"It's a pity, Fellow Daoist Liu Xi, if you had served that esteemed figure in the past, your future prospects would have been boundless."

"Could this terrifying power be that of the legendary Great Dao Sovereign?"

Since they had chosen to enter the Heavenly Profound Bizarre Land, they had naturally conducted thorough investigations on the Saint Light Clan.

Otherwise, they would not have dared to venture in.

That this figure could slay the Saint Light Clan's Solar Deity was simply... horrifying!

Hearing this, Liu Xi's expression remained calm as he serenely replied, "There are countless choices in this world, who dares to claim that every choice they make is the right one? At least I'm still alive, and that's a gain."

Liu Xi remained carefree and readily accepted the situation.

At this moment, high in the heavens, Qi Yuan looked at the tattered robe of the departed soul, his gaze fluctuating.

He was actually somewhat curious about what scheme Dao Buer had been planning with that tattered robe.

"Old man, why don't you come to my place and stop wandering outside, I'll give you a... home!" Qi Yuan called out to the tattered robe.

This tattered robe seemed to be no ordinary thing.

He was not interested in what Dao Buer had been plotting, but he was very interested in the essence of this tattered robe.

If he could incorporate it into his Imperial Scepter Banner, it would undoubtedly propel the refinement of his Imperial Scepter Banner to new heights.

The tattered robe, aimlessly drifting in the void, once again paused.

Seeing this, Qi Yuan's eyes lit up as he quickly said, "Don't worry, I won't charge you any rent, and I'll provide you with food and lodging!"

Qi Yuan gritted his teeth, unwilling to let this opportunity slip away.

The tattered robe had no physical form, but Qi Yuan felt as if a pair of eyes were staring at him.

Seeing the tattered robe make no other move, Qi Yuan gritted his teeth again, "I'll even provide you with social insurance and a double pension!"

This time, he was really going all out.

After all, facing this tattered robe, Qi Yuan even sensed a hint of threat.

The tattered robe looked at Qi Yuan, and a sense of desolate grandeur suddenly permeated the air.

Qi Yuan seemed to hear a mournful voice from the depths of the underworld, the ancient past.

"Without forging the light, there is no seeing the light."

As the vast voice faded, the tattered robe's figure had disappeared from Qi Yuan's sight.

The blood-red hue in the sky also vanished at that moment.

The tattered robe's figure had disappeared, and Qi Yuan did not know where it had gone.

"What is this...?"

Qi Yuan was puzzled, for in his hand, a small, pearl-like round granule had appeared.

On this shimmering little pearl, there was a single drop of blood stain.

[The embryo of light, stained with ominous blood.]

Seeing this, Qi Yuan was slightly taken aback.

With a wave of his hand, the Ancient Mirror of the Heavenly Profound appeared in his grasp.

Previously, when he had observed it with his eyes, it also seemed to be stained with ominous blood.

"This ominous blood... what kind of blood is it?"

"And... why did this tattered robe suddenly give me the embryo of light? Could it be that my offer of social insurance and double pension really moved him... but why didn't he come with me, could it be... he doesn't need the lodging?"

Qi Yuan pondered for a long time but could not figure it out.

He shouted into the void, "Hey, old man, what do you mean by this?"

Alas, there was no response from the void.

"'Without forging the light, there is no seeing the light,' what could this mean?"

Qi Yuan contemplated.

At this moment, the embryo of light in his hand suddenly burrowed into the depths of Qi Yuan's divine soul.

Qi Yuan was stunned: "It's burrowing into my divine soul?"

He shook it out, his face filled with a curious expression.

"This thing can even burrow into the divine soul?"

Qi Yuan was surprised.

After all, most treasures are simply stored in a spatial pouch or placed in the dantian or underworld, rarely entering the divine soul directly.

"It seems this is a powerful treasure, but... I don't know what its use is yet?"

For the time being, Qi Yuan could not discern the purpose of the embryo of light.

"Alright, the trip to the Heavenly Profound Bizarre Land... is temporarily over, I should head back."

Qi Yuan said calmly.

In the Ghost Pass, there were still a bunch of moonlight beauties who didn't like to bathe waiting for him.

"I don't know why, but my right eyebrow has been twitching lately... could it be that I'm about to encounter danger?"

Qi Yuan pondered slightly.

"Could it be that the Ghost Monarch has a method to target me?"

"No, with the Ghost Monarch's strength, he's not qualified!"

"So the most likely possibility is the Celestial Palace, could it be that my superb acting skills are useless, and the Celestial Palace has... discovered me?"

Qi Yuan contemplated.

Although he had been hiding since coming to the Upper Realm, if one were to carefully investigate, they would surely find some clues.

"It's fine, I'm not afraid. Even if the Celestial Palace comes after me, I'm in the right!"

"Their sun's light shining into my dwelling, infringing on my sacred private property!"

"This is a grave crime!"

Qi Yuan was not worried.

He had many retreat options.

For example, the Heavenly Profound Bizarre Land was actually a qualified retreat for him.

Not to mention Dao Buer, even that tattered robe would be able to intimidate the Great Dao Sovereigns, preventing them from daring to venture into the Heavenly Profound Bizarre Land.

Qi Yuan was different, his conscience was clear, so he boldly entered.

Luan Shi sat next to Tian Yun: "Don't think about it too much, how he is has nothing to do with us."

"Excuse me, may I... come in?"

Outside the dwelling, a black-robed figure appeared.

Hearing this, Tian Yun's expression changed drastically.

Luan Shi also became extremely vigilant, her eyes filled with trepidation.

"It's... it's that esteemed senior." Tian Yun stammered.

Then, she and Luan Shi exchanged a glance and said, "Esteemed one, please come in!"

As the words fell, the black-robed man appeared.

This black-robed man was naturally Qi Yuan.

After delivering the stone tablet, Qi Yuan had left the Heavenly Profound Bizarre Land.

He did not immediately rush to the Ghost Pass, but instead came to visit Tian Yun.

After seeing the stone tablet and the others, Qi Yuan had discovered that Tian Yun and the others shared the same bloodline, with Tian Yun's bloodline being even more potent.

And this bloodline seemed to have a deep connection with that tattered robe.

In order to unravel the mystery of the embryo of light, Qi Yuan had come to visit Tian Yun.

"Esteemed senior... have you come from the Heavenly Profound Bizarre Land? What business do you have with this humble one?" Tian Yun sat upright, her expression anxious.

Luan Shi also stood by the side like a scolded junior.

Her heart was in turmoil.

How could this person have come from the Heavenly Profound Bizarre Land?

According to the timeline, he should have been captured by that Saint Light Clan long ago, so why was he still alive?

"On the purest yin ghost night, I encountered a tattered robe, and he seems to have a deep connection with you all." Qi Yuan said calmly.

Hearing this, Tian Yun and Luan Shi's breathing quickened.

Finally, Luan Shi spoke, her voice desolate: "According to the ancient records of our clan, that tattered robe is... the Human King of our Heavenly Clan."

"Oh, the Human King?" Qi Yuan paused slightly.

To have the title of Human King, he must have been a great hero of the people, or the sovereign of a realm.

No wonder that tattered robe did not want to accept his lodging offer, for he was merely a Human King, and staying in the Imperial Scepter Banner would be overstepping his bounds.

"He once spoke a sentence to me that I did not understand, so I've come to ask you about it." Qi Yuan stated his purpose.

"The Human King... spoke?" Luan Shi looked at Qi Yuan with shock, seemingly finding it hard to believe.

The Human King's remnant soul had existed for countless millennia, yet in all these years, had he ever spoken a single word?

"He told me, 'Without forging the light, there is no seeing the light.' What does this mean?" Qi Yuan asked.

Hearing this, Luan Shi's expression changed again.

She believed that this mysterious figure had truly made the Human King's remnant soul speak.

She lowered her head and said in a desolate voice, "In our Heavenly Clan, there is a record that in the ancient past, under the Human King's leadership, our Heavenly Clan... was extremely prosperous and flourishing, even not inferior to the current Six Heavens of the Immortal Realm."

Mentioning this, Luan Shi had an expression of great pride.

The Heavenly Clan had once had a brilliant civilization, a world without equal.
