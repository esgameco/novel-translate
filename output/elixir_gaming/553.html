As he spoke, his figure suddenly accelerated.

"Watch my supersonic attack!"

Powerful fists, a strong physique, and lightning-fast speed. This single punch, relying solely on the force of his body's impact, was enough to kill a Heavenly Realm Armored Soldier.

Boom! The terrifying figure collided, and the Elder's eyes widened in disbelief. The power of Heaven and Earth... actually could not stop him!

Clearly, the white-haired man in front of them was such a special existence.

As Qi Yuan's words fell, his unparalleled speed unleashed another punch.

Instantly, another Thorn Blood Elder was slain.

Qi Yuan was getting impatient.

"You're still stammering and living in this world, taking up memory space? Let me help the Divine Wood Universe clear some memory!"

Qi Yuan still had an aggressive attitude, instantly slaying the stammering Thorn Blood Elder.

In the blink of an eye, three Thorn Blood Elders had fallen.

Jin Dan Crane, seeing this, was furious: "Who are you? This is the domain of the Enigmatic Deity, offending the Enigmatic Deity will surely lead to your death!"

Jin Dan Crane could not understand how someone dared to run amok and indiscriminately kill in Cloud Blood City. This was the territory controlled by the Enigmatic Deity, the domain of the Black Heavens and Black Earth.

Even if they were from the Human Awakening Society, coming here, they could only tuck their tails and behave. But this bold and unrestrained behavior was unprecedented!

Moreover, although Qi Yuan had white hair, his spirit and vitality seemed very good. The Human Awakening Society would not have placed such a genius in an enemy-occupied area. Wasn't this just sending him to his death? In the Exquisite Gold Realm, there was no way out once you entered!

"What Enigmatic Deity? If you don't let me become mortal, you all have to die?" Qi Yuan said nonchalantly.

Now, his biggest goal was to become mortal. Anything that hindered his mortal transformation was a potential enemy.

"Think you can run? Have you asked my wings?" Qi Yuan's speed was extremely fast, killing gods and Buddhas alike.

These lofty Heavenly Realm experts were no match for Qi Yuan at all. Annihilation! Completely annihilated! The one-sided battle had no suspense whatsoever.

In just a dozen breaths, all the Heavenly Realm experts had been slain. As for Jin Dan Crane, although he fled quickly, he simply couldn't match Qi Yuan's speed without wings.

"They're all dead, but... the battle is not over yet." Qi Yuan's gaze turned to the Blood Tiger below, his expression grave.

That was the true great enemy, the others were just appetizers.

But when he saw that person, Qi Yuan's gaze suddenly shrank.

How could it be...?

At this time, the Blood Tiger's appearance was utterly miserable. The aftershock of a battle with a Heavenly Realm expert had struck his body, shattering it and causing his blood to flow. Only a sliver of life remained.

That last bit of life was used to send a message through a communication artifact: "Fuck your mother!"

After sending this message, the Blood Tiger's pupils had long since dispersed. He was so hateful! Extremely hateful towards that group of scum from the Blood Tiger Gang, actually sending him to deal with such a terrifying existence.

Didn't he see how those Heavenly Realm Elders were slaughtered like vegetables in his hands? The Blood Tiger's neck twisted in a characteristic manner, his breath exhausted, dying without peace.

Qi Yuan's figure descended, his face showing an expression of disbelief.

After a long while, he sighed, "A true gentleman, he should have realized he was no match for me and taken his own life."

As for being hit by the aftershock? That was definitely just an illusion.

"The enigmatic spirits in this inner city... are quite numerous, it seems I need to help the Exquisite Gold Realm clear some memory!"

Qi Yuan's gaze turned fierce.

All these enigmatic spirits in the inner city were potential criminals hindering his mortal transformation, they all had to be killed.

And by slaying these enigmatic spirits, he could also gain a considerable amount of experience points, which was a win-win situation.

If he could kill all the enigmatic spirits in this world, the amount of experience points he would gain would probably be massive.

With a few more such incidents, he might not even need to undergo the mortal transformation to become an Ancestral God.

"Kill!" Qi Yuan's figure vanished without a trace.

The slaughter... had only just begun!

In an unknown space, the Immortal Elegant Handsome frowned, his expression complex. The elder smiled cheerfully, immortal and celestial: "What's wrong, did this mission fail and greatly disappoint you?"

The Immortal Elegant Handsome shook his head, his mind once again recalling Qi Yuan's words and his various extraordinary performances.

"Elder, could it be... that the Divine Wood Universe is hiding... a Realm Lord who has undergone mortal transformation?"

The elder before him was the Grand Elder of the Immortal Elegant Clan.
