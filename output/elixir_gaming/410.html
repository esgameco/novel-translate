Holding Little Bride in his arms, Qi Yuan personally fed her quite a few snacks.

Little Bride's wedding dress became increasingly solid, but the aura around her remained unchanged.

Many complex emotions flashed through Qi Yuan's mind.

Little Bride was of the royal bloodline of the Wedding Dress clan.

Qi Yuan had seen many others from the Wedding Dress clan before.

Those Wedding Dress clan members could speak and transform into human form.

Little Bride was different, seeming to only exist in the form of a wedding dress.

Even with Qi Yuan's current Yang Spirit level of combat power, he was unable to change Little Bride.

The strength of a Yang Spirit was already terrifying.

Though unable to make a stone truly awaken consciousness on its own, it was effortless to awaken consciousness in an insect or fish.

"There are many talented people in the Upper Realm, perhaps someone could make Little Bride speak," Qi Yuan gazed at Little Bride eating snacks with a doting expression.

Yang Spirits of the Great Principle level existed in the Upper Realm, perhaps they would understand the issue with Little Bride.

After about half an hour, Little Bride stopped absorbing blood crystals, apparently full.

"Let's go take a look at... the Strange Land. We should also prepare to go to the Upper Realm."

The Chaotic Catastrophe had fallen, transforming into a Strange Land within the Netherworld.

Qi Yuan was quite curious about that Strange Land.

However, since awakening, he had been busy devouring the Three Thousand Realms and hadn't gone to see the Strange Land yet.

After a few breaths of time, Qi Yuan appeared in the Strange Land holding Little Bride.

Qi Qi also appeared at this moment, her face somewhat pale, clearly her injuries had not yet healed.

She had also been severely injured in the battle against the Three Thousand Catastrophes.

Although she and the other Netherworld deities had worked together to separate the Canglan Realm from the great battle, the fight between the Three Thousand Catastrophes and Four Thousand Yang Spirits took place within the Purple Palace, and was still too intense.

If it had been outside the Purple Palace, so much wouldn't have happened.

"The fragments from the fallen Catastrophe transformed into this Strange Land.

From my daughter's investigation, this Strange Land... is somewhat similar to chaos, it's a kind of energy.

This energy... could perhaps be utilized to support the operation of the Netherworld." Qi Qi told Qi Yuan of her discoveries.

During this time, she had been staying in the Strange Land, researching it.

After all, Father had entrusted the management of the Netherworld to her, so she needed to investigate thoroughly when an unknown area appeared.

"It can be utilized?" Qi Yuan's expression was calm.

"Yes." Qi Qi nodded vigorously, her eyes flashing with joy, "When Father revived the shattered mountains and rivers of the Canglan Realm, as well as the dead living beings, it wasn't actually creating them out of nothing, it consumed a lot of the Netherworld's energy.

This energy mostly came from the Mountains and Rivers Brush, as well as the relocated Wuguicheng, Jiupan Mountain and others, and some came from you, Father.

The Strange Land formed from the Catastrophe is actually quite precious energy, able to revive even more."

As Qi Qi spoke, she waved her hand and grabbed a handful of the special energy from the Strange Land.

"In the battle against the Catastrophe, Father had 327 divine embryos shattered.

If left to heal on their own in the Netherworld, it might take tens of thousands of years, and the Netherworld's energy wouldn't be enough to revive them all.

But using this energy is different! With just a third of the energy here, Father's shattered divine embryos could walk out of the Netherworld again, and it would only take a year.

In fact, if more energy was consumed, they could awaken in an instant!"

Qi Qi's face was excited.

She knew very well what this meant.

If such a Netherworld existed in the Mortal Heart Realm,

What would the Ancient Gods fear in facing the New Gods? What would they fear in battle? They could even grow stronger through battle, fighting more and more fiercely.

This was simply the strongest logistical base.

At this point, Qi Yuan also rarely used his brain: "So you're saying, three Catastrophes are enough to revive one divine embryo, a ratio of about 3 to 1?"

Qi Qi nodded: "That's right."

Qi Yuan's eyes lit up, then immediately became extremely pained: "Aiya, I was too extravagant and wasteful before. All those past loves of mine, died for nothing, alas... if they had died in my Netherworld and turned into energy, they would have continued to contribute even after death. That would truly be 'some people die, but they still live on'!"

Qi Yuan was full of regret.

Back then with so many past loves, why did he just kill them and not care?

He should have taken their corpses too.

After all, they were Qi Yuan's past loves in life, they should be Qi Yuan's past loves in death too.

Otherwise, wouldn't it fail to live up to Qi Yuan's reputation for deep affection.

Qi Qi wanted to laugh, but she was professional and didn't actually laugh: "What Father says makes sense, the corpses of slain enemies all have great use.

However, the Catastrophes are special, three Catastrophes can gather enough energy to revive one Yang Spirit.

For ordinary Yang Spirits, it may take more."

Alas, I was so foolish before, why did I let those gentle and lovely past loves, who were just a bit old and didn't like to bathe, be exposed in the wilderness.

Their bodies were eaten by wild dogs and pecked by eagles, how painful and wasteful that must have been!"

"This time, when we go to the Upper Realm, I must carry forward the traditional virtues of frugality and thrift, and let every past love fulfill their value even after death!"

Qi Qi didn't speak, she was all too familiar with her godfather's personality.

After all, in the Mortal Heart Realm, what Ancient God had ever thought of going to eat the New Gods' banquet?

Godfather had thought of it, and even done it.

Led by Qi Qi, after patrolling the Netherworld with Little Bride, Qi Yuan's form arrived before the Door of Ten Thousand Differences.

The Door of Ten Thousand Differences could be any door in the world.

Surrounded by purple qi, the Door of Ten Thousand Differences transformed into the Door of Great Principle, enough to drive any Yang Spirit mad.

If opened, it could lure Yang Spirits into the Netherworld, and then surround and attack them.

If they died, dying in the Netherworld wouldn't be a waste.

"What if we lure in a Great Principle level being?" Qi Yuan hesitated.

He still had no concept of how powerful Great Principle level beings were.

He dared to take on one of those Principle level true Buddhas.

If too many came, he felt he would be finished.

As for Great Principle level... they were likely even more powerful.

"So, I need to optimize this trap. Going through the Door of Ten Thousand Differences shouldn't just enter the Netherworld, otherwise my Netherworld could be wrecked."

Qi Yuan pondered, again rarely using his brain.

"Could Father move the other end of this door to the void between spaces? That way if we encounter an unbeatable enemy, we could put them in the void between spaces." Qi Qi made an appropriate suggestion at this time.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan had a flash of inspiration. He got a bit excited: "Goddaughter, you truly have inherited my genetic talent, your intelligence is almost comparable to Great Wisdom True Lord, you've given me an idea!"

Qi Qi's words gave Qi Yuan many hints.

Back when he was in the Flowing Wind Realm, he had used the Key of Ten Thousand Differences to open the Door of Ten Thousand Differences and arrived in a dead universe.

So, could he also use the Door of Ten Thousand Differences to return to the universe of his Solar Golden Core?

Could the other end of the Door of Ten Thousand Differences be set in that universe?

Then...

A crazed look flashed in Qi Yuan's eyes.

If he created a Black Hole Golden Core and aimed the other end of the Door of Ten Thousand Differences at the black hole.

Could a Great Principle level Yang Spirit who entered... come out? If not, they would be hit by hundreds or even thousands of Solar Golden Cores as soon as they entered.

These Solar Golden Cores were real stars, not the illusory reflections that illuminated the myriad realms.

The power would likely... be even greater.

"If they're too strong, just banish them to the other end of the universe. Yang Spirits are very fast, but it would probably still take a long time to traverse the entire universe!"
