Memories of the past flooded into his mind.

All the fragmented scenes flashed through his mind.

"Are you... Wooden Hairpin?" Qi Yuan looked at the young girl.

Back then, when he emerged from Tianjue, he had carefully prepared a gift for Jin Li.

Unfortunately, by the lake in July, he did not see Jin Li.

The two of them were on opposite ends of time and space.

The one who accompanied Qi Yuan as snow fell on his shoulders was Moon Lady.

Later, he went alone to Dijue. Moon Lady left the mark of the moon on his arm, and he also left the hairpin for Moon Lady, hoping she could give it to Jin Li who existed in the future.

"I'm not some wooden hairpin, I'm daddy's daughter." The High Priestess raised her head stubbornly.

Qi Yuan looked at her, feeling deeply emotional inside.

Everything connected in this moment.

He and Jin Li had only communicated through the Linglong Jade.

They had never met in person, but Qi Yuan could sense that she was just an ordinary young girl.

He recalled fragments he had heard in the Five Elements Forbidden Land.

Back then, Dari encountered a powerful enemy and was ultimately defeated. He nearly exhausted all his methods just to injure that enemy's leg.

He had also heard Jin Li talk about an embarrassing incident from her youth - she fell on flat ground during her coronation. Her leg had been injured since childhood and never fully healed.

So the powerful enemy who fought Dari back then was Jin Li's previous incarnation.

But later, why did she become Moon Lady, and then Jin Li? The High Priestess seemed to see the confusion in Qi Yuan's eyes. She said in a crisp voice: "Does daddy want to know about mommy's background?"

"...Mm." To be honest, Qi Yuan didn't really want to acknowledge this daughter.

After all, suddenly having an extra daughter, and one who hadn't yet ruled over all of history, made his previous boasts seem very awkward.

But thinking that having an extra daughter was better than mysteriously losing a daughter, he accepted it in his heart.

In a sense, she really was his daughter.

"Mommy came from the Upper Realm," the High Priestess said, looking up at the starry sky.

Qi Yuan also couldn't help but look at the stars dotting the ancient Qi Chun trees.

He thought of the Moon God Sovereign, and the Moon Token his master had left for her.

"Back then, mommy came to the lower realm on orders from the Moon Palace to destroy Dari, the master of this realm.

She easily killed Dari, but fell victim to a scheme by the Black Magic Abyss.

The Black Magic Abyss is an even more powerful force than the Moon Palace in the Upper Realm, and has long been at odds with the Moon Palace.

After slaying her enemy, mommy also fell into the cycle of reincarnation.

The arrangements made by that Yin God of the Black Magic Abyss before his demise also began to take effect - that was the Heavenly Dog Devouring the Moon formation.

Once completed, mommy would perish completely and be unable to return to the Upper Realm.

Later, mommy lost her past memories and became Moon Lady, meeting daddy.

Daddy went alone to Dijue, destroyed the otherworldly demons and Dari, broke the Heavenly Dog Devouring the Moon formation, and also saved mommy." The High Priestess's cherry-like small mouth had thin lips, her teeth like shattered jade.

Qi Yuan was silent for a long time before saying: "Later, was it she who awakened me?"

Jin Li's first reincarnation in this realm was named Moon Lady.

She left the crescent moon mark on Qi Yuan's hand.

Back then, Qi Yuan fought the otherworldly demons alone, killed Dari, and established the foundation of the Heavenly Way.

In reality, his injuries were extremely severe.

It was Moon Lady who consumed her remaining divine power to heal his wounds.

So when Qi Yuan awoke again, Moon Lady no longer existed in the world.

After sleeping for who knows how many years, Jin Li appeared, having almost completely become mortal.

Without divine power, she could not even suppress the old wound on her leg left by Dari.

The High Priestess looked at Qi Yuan and raised her head: "Mommy said daddy is very handsome."

"Where is she now?" Qi Yuan couldn't help but ask.

"The battle between the Moon Palace and Black Magic Abyss has reached a white-hot stage.

After mommy recovered her powers, she ascended to the Upper Realm through a transmission formation.

She left me here because mommy believed daddy definitely hadn't died.

One day, mommy will see daddy again!" The High Priestess's starry eyes were full of smiles.

To see you, to see you.

How could they have missed each other? They had met before, just in the wrong time and space, not recognizing each other.

Qi Yuan raised his head to look at the sky, at the myriad stars, his mind full of thoughts.

Why... were they all in the heavens?

"Can I go to the Upper Realm through the transmission formation?" Qi Yuan asked.

The High Priestess focused her gaze: "One must reach the Yin God realm to ascend to the Upper Realm through the transmission formation."

In the Moon Dynasty there was a transmission formation that could send some people to ascend to other lower realms under the Moon God Sovereign's other Daos.

The formations in those Daos could allow Yin Gods to ascend to the Upper Realm.

"Yin God... that's so far away." Qi Yuan sighed.

He could kill Yin Gods, but his current cultivation was only at the Yuan Dan stage.

He was still two major realms - Divine Infant and Purple Palace - away from Yin God!

"What's daddy's current cultivation level?" The High Priestess stared at Qi Yuan's face.

"I'm very weak, just a small Yuan Dan cultivator."

"Yuan Dan?" The High Priestess was surprised. "That must not be an ordinary Yuan Dan."

Qi Yuan looked at this daughter he had never met before: "What... is your name?"

The High Priestess's starry eyes smiled as she kept staring at Qi Yuan: "Qi Jianjun."

In the inn.

Great Wisdom True Lord yawned.

Jiang Ya sat to the side, carefully studying the new techniques she had obtained.

Xu Tongchen was seriously looking at the new "Suona Score" he had gotten, learning the techniques.

The inn door opened and a cold wind blew in, mixed with white foam.

Two figures, one large and one small, entered the inn.

Great Wisdom True Lord glanced at Qi Yuan, wisdom flashing in his eyes.

Jiang Ya stared at the little girl, sensing a threatening aura.

Yin God?

How could a Yin God level expert be born on the Wangyue Continent? Wasn't the Heavenly Way incomplete here? Moreover, this Yin God was holding the Blood Robed Sword Saint's hand... what for?

At this moment, Zhao Ci's eyes widened. He looked at the girl holding the Blood Robed Sword Saint's hand, confusion in his eyes which then turned to reverence.

"Greetings to the High Priestess!" Zhao Ci hurriedly came forward, his eyes full of respect.

Six or seven people from the suona troupe also came forward to pay respects to Qi Jianjun.

In fact, they all had deep doubts in their hearts.

Although the High Priestess was short, she had a cold personality and rarely interacted with others, always maintaining an air of dignity.

When had she ever been this lively and adorable, like a young girl frolicking in a elder's arms?

Qi Jianjun's eyes rolled and her face showed a smug expression: "Daddy, see, your daughter is still very strong."

Hearing this, Qi Yuan didn't want to speak.

After so many years, she still hadn't ruled over all of history - how was that strong?

He had no reaction.

But Jiang Ya's eyes widened.

If the Blood Robed Sword Saint had a Yin God level partner before, she could barely accept it.

But now... in less than half an hour, he gained a Yin God daughter? What was happening to this world?

"She's your daughter... how does Senior have a Yin God... daughter?" Jiang Ya's voice trembled.

"It's understandable for Senior to have a Yin God wife and a Yin God daughter," Great Wisdom True Lord said.

At this point, Qi Jianjun's small hand pinched Qi Yuan: "Daddy, did you find me another stepmother? Should I tell mommy about this after I ascend to the Upper Realm?"

Hearing this, Jiang Ya was completely confused!

Wasn't this High Priestess's mother that Yin God who had taken two steps to heaven?

Was it another Yin God from the Upper Realm? How could this Blood Robed Sword Saint... merely at the Purple Palace realm, have two Yin God partners and a Yin God daughter?

It felt very strange.

Great Wisdom True Lord revealed a wise look: "Senior Blood Robed Sword Saint is truly marvelous! Originally I only proposed a plan for the Blood Robed Sword Saint to find more Yin God partners to become invincible in the world. I didn't expect Senior to have already started implementing the plan!"

"Ahem..." Qi Yuan coughed.

"The only pity is that there's too little time left for the Blood Robed Sword Saint," Great Wisdom True Lord lamented again.

After all, the threat of the Moluo clan was imminent.

Where would the Blood Robed Sword Saint find time to look for more wives?

Xu Tongchen, Jiang Ya and the others also woke from their earlier shock, their expressions worried.

The Moluo clan had two Yin Gods who had taken two steps to heaven, and two who had taken one step.

The Blood Robed Sword Saint had killed Venerable Mohui, so the Moluo clan would surely come for revenge.

Their situation was actually still very precarious.

"There is a transmission formation in the Moon Dynasty. If we can't resist, we can take the formation to go to other worlds," Qi Jianjun said in a clear voice like bells.

The Moluo clan belonged to the Black Magic Abyss Dao lineage.

The Black Magic Abyss and Moon Palace had long been in conflict.

The Wangyue Continent, surrounded by the Black Magic Abyss Dao lineage's worlds, stood out as an anomaly.

"Since I've come, I won't leave. I will deal with the Moluo clan myself," Qi Yuan said.

In a few more days, he would fully regain control of the Heavenly Way.

His power would reach a terrifying level.
