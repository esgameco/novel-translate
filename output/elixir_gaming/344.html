However, compared to Mount Shuyao, the demons on Mount Xianlong were not organized and seemed very scattered.

"Alright!" Huo Mushi gripped the rope tightly, looking excited.

Who had come?

What did they want? Why had they tied her up?

She desperately wanted to speak, but couldn't utter a word.

However, the conversation between the two that followed shocked her even more.

"My hand is sore from gripping the staff all night. I really want to call my wife over to massage it."

"My lord... you're so mighty!"

"After we slaughter the thousands of demons on Mount Xianlong this time, I'll keep two beautiful female demons to give me a massage before killing them."

"My lord... you're so formidable!"

In any case, Huo Mushi was like a parrot, only able to offer praise.

The flower demon, covered by the black bag, heard Qi Yuan's words and felt contempt in her heart.

This guy was really boasting too much! Slaughter all the demons on Mount Xianlong?

If you're so amazing, why don't you pluck the stars from the sky? But before she could think much more, she heard that detestable person bragging again.

"Mushi, do you have any good ways to make money?"

"If my lord returns to Zhenyang, the county magistrate will surely gather the resources of the whole city to raise funds for you!"

Qi Yuan had slaughtered all the demons on Mount Shuyao, which was a life-saving grace for the people of Zhenyang City.

Even if Qi Yuan raised a flag in rebellion,

The county magistrate and sheriff would likely approve with both hands raised.

What's a bit of silver compared to that?

"It's not enough money. I want to buy a planet, like Gongxing."

"Huh?" Huo Mushi fell silent.

Sure enough, he still couldn't keep up with Qi Yuan's train of thought.

In ancient times of this world, the concept of planets already existed.

The common people also knew they lived on the surface of a planet.

So when he heard Qi Yuan say he wanted to buy a planet, Huo Mushi was speechless.

The flower demon in the black bag wanted to laugh coldly.

"It's a pity you can't find a buyer in this world of yours. Otherwise if I sold it, I could definitely make a lot of money!"

Hearing this, the flower demon felt Qi Yuan was even more arrogant and ignorant.

However, Qi Yuan's power was too strong. He had restrained her and prevented her from speaking.

Unable to escape or argue back, she could only become a prisoner.

The flower demon's heart was filled with confusion.

There must be a reason these two had captured her.

Were they after money or lust, or had they come for Tong Tianyou?

They should at least interrogate her.

But these two seemed to have forgotten about her and Tong Tianyou.

They just kept traveling.

If it weren't for the childlike follower feeding Tong Tianyou once along the way, she would have thought they had completely forgotten about the two of them.

She just wanted to say: "I may be a demon, but I need to eat too!"

Unfortunately, she couldn't speak, and Huo Mushi had never fed her.

Now she only hoped they would reach Mount Xianlong quickly, and these two arrogant and conceited people would die at the hands of other demons.

That way, she could be rescued, and also... pee!...

The people from the Demon Suppression Department in Zhenyang City were gradually making their way to Mount Shuyao.

In recent days, they had figured out most of the mission and learned that Tong Tianyou was in the hands of the flower demon Manlo.

As long as they went to Wind Flower Cave and found the flower demon Manlo,

Everything would come to light.

The only two dangers of this mission were possibly encountering other powerful demons on Mount Shuyao, and the threat posed by the flower demon Manlo herself.

So these people from the Demon Suppression Department were all carefully making their way through Mount Shuyao, just to find Wind Flower Cave and locate the flower demon Manlo and Tong Tianyou.

At this moment on Mount Shuyao, Yang Guang was wearing a wild boar skin, disguised as a pig demon.

He sniffed the air and revealed a strange expression: "Strange, why do I smell such a strong scent of blood?"

"You're not a dog. Leave this to the professionals." An Qiao shook the rope.

The tracking dog reluctantly came over.

"Actually I think he's quite dog-like and professional too."

The tracking dog spoke venomously as usual.

Yang Guang glared angrily at the tracking dog and said irritably: "Be careful or I'll catch a male dog demon to mate with you!"

Hearing this, the tracking dog immediately tucked its tail between its legs and didn't dare talk back.

"Also smell for Mr. Qi's scent and see if you can find where he is so we can rescue him!" An Qiao said worriedly.

Now, Mr. Qi was also trapped somewhere on Mount Shuyao.

Who knows, he might be surrounded by demon soldiers.

If they could find and rescue Mr. Qi, that would be good.

"Right, we need to find Brother Qi. I promised to protect him!" Now that Yang Guang's blood energy had recovered, he regained his confidence.

"Mm, got it."

The tracking dog spoke as it put its nose to the ground and started sniffing hard.

Yang Guang waited quietly. The strong smell of blood made him uneasy.

Suddenly, the tracking dog's tail stood straight up and its eyes showed human-like terror: "Blood... so much blood. I smell mountains of corpses and seas of blood!"

The tracking dog spoke as it raised its head to look north with a terrified expression.

"What?" An Qiao quickly asked.

"Over there... there's blood everywhere. It's hell for demons!" The tracking dog looked north, its eyes full of horror.

"That's the demon camp!" Yang Guang looked in the direction the dog was facing with a grave expression.

Could it be that a major incident had occurred on Mount Shuyao?

"What about Mr. Qi?"

"Not good, Mr. Qi's scent is in there too!" The tracking dog shouted.

"What? No, I promised to protect Mr. Qi!" Yang Guang's heart tightened, fearing Qi Yuan had already met with misfortune.

After all, connecting what the tracking dog said, it was very possible that Qi Yuan had been surrounded in the demon camp and engaged in a fierce battle.

That would explain the strong smell of blood.

"I must go take a look!" Yang Guang was still very true to his word.
