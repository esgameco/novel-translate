If this happened on the Blue Star, he would be called a "living Bodhisattva" for providing so many job opportunities.

Outside the wind cave, the roars of the yin ghost army had long since dissipated, leaving a sense of tranquility, but faint strange sounds could still be heard.

He was very anxious in his heart, wanting to go see what was happening outside.

How was the great person who had been protecting them doing now?

In the strange land of Tian Kun, he had been here for over a hundred years, what had he not seen?

Encountering yin ghosts in broad daylight, he had truly never encountered this before.

But this... was uncountable, truly uncountable.
