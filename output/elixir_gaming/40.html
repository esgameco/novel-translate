Through Linglongyu Xishi, Jinli sent these words to Qi Yuan.

Jinli quickly read it.

"What's the point of being an emperor? The task is done, just leave directly!

Why not come hunt monsters with me, that would be much more fun!"

When she saw the last sentence, a faint smile appeared on Jinli's lips.

"Okay, I'll go with you."

This unqualified female emperor would have another sleepless night, tossing and turning.

As for the instigator Qi Yuan, he was now holding his greatsword, ready to begin his hunt.

However, as he entered the Yiguan Forbidden Depths, Qi Yuan narrowed his eyes.

"Something's not right."

He noticed that the Yiguan Forbidden Depths today were a bit different from usual.

On a crooked-necked tree, the branches were mostly broken, revealing jagged fractures, and a section of gray pants leg was hanging from it.

"This is not... what I injured."

"Is there someone else?"

The monsters Qi Yuan killed would always turn to ash, becoming his experience.

They would never be left in such a broken state.

His gaze became grave, and he became more cautious.

After all, this world still had figures like the treacherous Prime Minister Sima Ting, and there were likely other human NPCs as well.

Perhaps there were such NPCs lurking in the Yiguan Forbidden Depths.

Qi Yuan was extremely careful and observant.

However, he only found a few broken clothing monsters.

The rest of the clothing monsters were the same as usual.

Qi Yuan hesitated: "Should I continue killing?"

If he couldn't figure out the situation, he would feel uneasy.

Unfortunately, after a night of stealth, he still couldn't find anything special.

The clothing monsters in the Yiguan Forbidden Depths were just as they had always been, hanging silently on the trees like ghosts.

"Could it be that I'm just scaring myself?"

Qi Yuan exited the depths of the Yiguan Forbidden Depths and decided to dig a hole.

After all, when feeling afraid, hiding in a blanket would make the fear dissipate.

According to the first principle of ghosts, ghosts cannot harm those who are hiding under a blanket.

Qi Yuan decided to first hide in the hole.

The space in the hole was narrow, with no ventilation on all sides, just like some special spots in the battle royale games that could give a false sense of security.

The cold moonlight shone on the trees, and not far away, the half-length blue robe hung there, the torn clothes looking as if they had been ripped apart by animal claws. With the wind blowing, the half-length blue robe fluttered.

Qi Yuan quickly dug a hole with his greatsword.

He immediately crawled into the hole.

Looking at the surrounding walls of earth, with no other space, Qi Yuan's heart calmed down: "Indeed, I feel much better now, I just need to block the entrance."

He stood up, ready to block the entrance and bury himself underground.

However, as he looked up, he immediately met a pair of deep, hollow eyes!

At the entrance of the hole, there was a head, silently staring at Qi Yuan.

This head seemed to be wearing a mask, black and white in color, half black and half white. The white part looked like a cold corpse that had been carefully made up by an inexperienced mortician, still giving off an eerie feeling.

He heaved a sigh of relief.

He ran for a few more hundred miles.

"Is this a graveyard?"

"Doesn't matter, if I don't dig, I'll have to log out forcibly!"

Qi Yuan found a spot a bit further from the graves and dug a large hole.

He was relieved he didn't dig into his neighbor's home.

He quickly buried the hole and lay down in a comfortable position.

Just as he closed his eyes, he felt like he touched something slimy.

"I didn't touch an old person's skull, did I?"

And on the Seven-Colored Peak, he succumbed to slumber.

In the graveyard, half an hour later.

The sound of firecrackers and gongs suddenly rang out.

As if in a funeral procession, or a wedding.
