Chapter 30

Here is the full English translation of the excerpt from the Chinese novel:

"Heavenly Bear Attendant, when I went into the city today, I seemed to hear the sound of firecrackers and gongs, as if someone's family was celebrating a marriage.

I deliberately released a spirit insect to follow and recorded the address.

Heavenly Bear Attendant, would you like your disciples to accompany you tonight to practice the Dao techniques?"

A disciple of the Black Mountain Sect said in a flattering tone.

Chu Tianxiong heard this and revealed a satisfied smile.

"Is that you, Liu Wei? You're capable, I'll remember you.

Tonight we'll cultivate the Fa, and I'll let you be the first to taste the flavor of the new bride."

The crowd burst into laughter.

However, as if thinking of something, Chu Tianxiong's face took on a fierce expression.

"That Seven-Colored Peak disciple Qi Yuan has truly gone too far!

If it weren't for him having a good master, I would make him understand what a blood calamity and hell on earth really are!"

"Heavenly Bear Attendant, I've heard that Qi Yuan has a problem with his head. Why should the senior bother with a fool? When we unify the Immortal Cultivation World of the Great Chu, you can do whatever you want to that fool."

The night gradually grew later.

At this moment, a seemingly very rhythmic knocking sound suddenly came from the main gate.

The Black Mountain Sect members in the courtyard stopped their conversation.

Chu Tianxiong looked towards the gate and said, "Liu Wei, go take a look."

Liu Wei stood up and walked towards the gate.

The door opened, and a masked man appeared in the sight of the crowd.

The mask was very simple, without even the outline of features, completely flat.

Seeing the newcomer, Liu Wei couldn't help but reveal a mocking expression: "What are you here for?"

The masked man took out a kitchen knife and slowly spoke in a hoarse voice, "I'm a bit hungry, preparing to kill some fish to eat."

"Is he a fool?" Liu Wei paused.

Chu Tianxiong noticed the events at the gate and his voice came, "There are fish in the pond, let him come in."

Liu Wei smiled, "Heavenly Bear Attendant is kind, rewarding you with a fish to eat."

But inside the courtyard, someone asked, "Heavenly Bear Attendant, why let him in?"

Chu Tianxiong replied, "It's boring here, let's find some entertainment.

That person is crazy, but his skin... quite white."

They were very bold and powerful, not worried about encountering any unsolvable troubles.

Moreover, they did not detect any dangerous aura from the masked man.

The masked man, holding the kitchen knife, entered the courtyard.

Elder Bai was cultivating in the inner courtyard, and the rest looked at the masked man with mocking expressions.

When the masked man approached, Chu Tianxiong suddenly called out, "Take off the mask, let me see what you look like."

The masked man paused for a moment, then spoke after a long time, seeming very hesitant, "Do you really want to see?"

"Haha, what, you won't show it? Could it be that you're ugly beyond belief?"

"If you're ugly, I'll give you a new face!"

The Black Mountain Sect burst into laughter, the moonlight shining on their faces, making them appear extremely ferocious.

"Sigh, before I put on this mask today, I've already said, those who see my face won't have a good end."

"What kind of bad end? Tell me about it!" Chu Tianxiong said.

However, at this moment, something unexpected happened that no one could have anticipated.

Suddenly, the masked man raised the kitchen knife.

A flash of white light passed, and the crowd couldn't even react in time.

Chu Tianxiong's huge head directly fell to the ground.

His face still bore the previous smile, which was frozen in that moment.

The people present were all shocked.

The event happened too fast, too suddenly.

The masked man looked at Chu Tianxiong's severed head and said thoughtfully, "It turns out, your blood calamity was meant to be upon me.

Encountering me is your tribulation.

If you have a next life, be reborn as an ant, I'm kind-hearted and have never used hot water to scald ant nests."

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The rest were dumbfounded, furious, and shocked.

"Heavenly Bear Attendant!"

"How dare you!"

"Treacherous person!"

These disciples, although inwardly terrified, reacted one after another, rushing to attack the masked man.

The masked man saw this and a carefree smile appeared in his eyes: "The monsters in the game don't have health bars, and you don't have health bars either. What's the difference between you and the monsters?"

"Oh, there is still some difference."

"You will scream in agony, no need for me to dub the voice myself."

"Of course, if I have the talent, it must be useful. You will all cry in pain!"

"I rhymed, I'm truly a genius!"

At the moment when everyone was perplexed, puzzled, terrified, and furious.

He held a cleaver, like the most graceful dancer.

The knife rose and fell.

Eight slashes in the summer.

"Ah!"

"Run!"

"Who are you?"

In an instant, most of the Black Mountain Sect disciples present had already died.

Only four or five people were left, trembling in fear.

At this moment, an anxious and angry voice came from the inner courtyard.

"Scoundrel! Coward!"

The originally cultivating Patriarch Bai Ji finally woke up and took action.

The remaining Black Mountain Sect disciples saw this and all showed expressions of relief at having survived.

Although the masked man in front of them was extremely terrifying, he was definitely not an Elixir Cultivator! With Patriarch Bai Ji's furious roar, a beam of elixir light shot towards the masked man.

The masked man saw this, held the cleaver, and let out a loud roar: "Heavenly Sword Slashing Technique!"

This was the first time he had used this powerful skill in reality! The idea was solid, so he had to unleash his trump card.

He leaped up, and the moonlight poured down on his white robe.

The parts outlining his body emitted a faint white glow.

His entire figure appeared extremely illusory, with only the outlined parts being extremely solid.

In Patriarch Bai Ji's line of sight, it was as if a cleaver was slashing out from the moon.

An overwhelmingly mighty and unimaginable blade light directly charged towards Patriarch Bai Ji.

For some reason, Patriarch Bai Ji suddenly showed an expression of fear at this moment.

After all, he was an Elixir Cultivator.

The other party... was definitely not an Elixir Cultivator.

The blade light swept through everything, instantly submerging Patriarch Bai Ji's elixir light.

And in the next moment, that blade light directly sliced through, shattering Patriarch Bai Ji's protective elixir light as well.

The mighty Patriarch Bai Ji was directly slain by a single blade.

The round golden elixir core also shattered into a small broken ball at this moment, covered in countless holes.

The masked man paused: "That's it?"

He knew he would win, but he didn't expect it to be this easy.

Perhaps, he was just too powerful.

Perhaps, his knife was also very good.

Or perhaps...

He looked at the small golden elixir core on the ground and commented: "His elixir core is too small, and his elixir light is also not strong enough, unable to withstand even a single one of my blows.

It seems that in the future, if I were to form an elixir core, I should form a larger one, and the elixir light should be at least as bright as the moonlight."

The masked man said this as he looked up at the moon in the sky.

"At the very least, the elixir light should be as bright as the moonlight, right?"

At this time, the remaining few small cats were trembling uncontrollably, with some even fleeing.

The masked man saw this and chanted: "I am a patriotic person, I love killing entire families the most!"

With that, he swung his blade, and those few small cats died an even more miserable death.

The masked man looked at the corpses on the ground, then slowly walked towards the pond.

"Time to catch a fish to eat."

He said, rolling up his pants, and by the pond, he cleaned the cleaver, then caught a black fish.
