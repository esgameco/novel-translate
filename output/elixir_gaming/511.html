This question left Shen Lingfeng bewildered again.

"Hmph, Qi Yuan's mischief can be forgiven, since he's been in a mental asylum before. Do you want to go to the asylum too?"

Thinking about the bathwater made Mrs. Shen angry. Because she suddenly realized that Shen Lingfeng's previous request for a little sister was intentional.

"Pouring out the bathwater is one thing, but what was the meaning of putting onions, ginger, and garlic in it?"

Mrs. Shen brandished the whip, cracking it in the air.

Shen Lingfeng screamed in terror, wailing like a ghost.

The servants and maids kept their distance, not daring to approach.

"Today is a very lucky day."

"Qi Yuan's performance as the son-in-law has reached 70%."

"That's more than expected. Could it be that not only the mother-in-law bathed, but the father-in-law bathed as well?"

Qi Yuan speculated in secret.

According to his calculations, pouring out the bathwater for the mother-in-law would have raised the performance rating to around 60%. But it ended up being 10 points higher.

Clearly, the bathwater he poured out was not just for the mother-in-law...

"The reward for this performance level is... Yin-Yang White Fire."

When his performance reached 30%, Qi Yuan had gained the Heart of Forging, improving his success rate in forging.

Now, he has obtained the Yin-Yang White Fire.

The Yin-Yang White Fire is a powerful spiritual fire.

The strength of a craftsman not only depends on their forging techniques, but also on their spiritual fire.

There are many materials in the world that are extremely hard and difficult to melt.

Some craftsmen, whose spiritual fire is not strong enough, cannot even melt certain rare and precious materials.

But the Yin-Yang White Fire is different - it is a powerful and domineering flame that can melt even divine-level materials.

"It seems this son-in-law Qi Yuan actually has very high innate talent as a craftsman, he just hasn't awakened it yet."

Qi Yuan evaluated.

"Yin-Yang White Fire!"

With Qi Yuan's movement, a fierce white flame appeared.

In this flame, Qi Yuan could sense the ultimate coldness and scorching heat - two completely different essences coexisting in one flame, very contradictory.

Qi Yuan took out a special material he had purchased - Wind Brilliance Gold, a heavenly-grade material.

The unranked spirit tools he had forged before, if infused with a bit of Wind Brilliance Gold, could be elevated to the yellow-grade.

And if they were yellow-grade, they could be promoted one small rank higher.

Previously, Qi Yuan was unable to melt this material.

But now, with the Yin-Yang White Fire surging, the Wind Brilliance Gold quickly softened and liquefied.

"The Yin-Yang White Fire truly lives up to its name, now even I can forge earth-grade spirit tools."

Up to this point, the strongest spirit tools Qi Yuan had forged were only at the mysterious-grade.

After awakening the Yin-Yang White Fire, he could try forging earth-grade spirit tools.

An earth-grade craftsman would be considered a regional overlord in the Ji Ning Prefecture.

The next day, Shen Lingfeng did not come, and Chen Shenwei approached.

"In half a month, I will be following the Shen family to Mount Ao Lake to attend the Forging Conference." Chen Shenwei mentioned this with a worried expression on his face. "I wonder if there will be any young talents who can repair the Mighty Dragon Xuan Yuan."

The Forging Conference, also known as the Alchemy Convention, was held with the purpose of gathering the young elites from the three prefectures, to discuss the repair of the Mighty Dragon Xuan Yuan.

"In the past, an event of this caliber would not have allowed someone like you, who has just entered the field, to participate.

But now, with the imminent threat of the Water Lord of Mount Ao Lake, every craftsman's unique ideas are needed." Chen Shenwei continued.

"I may not be good at forging, but I excel at unique ideas." Qi Yuan said casually. "If I can repair the Mighty Dragon Xuan Yuan, what kind of reward can I get?"

"You're thinking too much." Chen Shenwei chuckled. "Probably around one million taels."

"Only one million taels, that's too little?" Qi Yuan couldn't help but say.

If this had been a few days ago, he would have thought one million taels was a lot.

But now, with two million taels in his possession, he felt it was not enough.

"Sigh, this is just a small goal, worth me making an effort."

"You fool, a regular earth-grade spirit tool is only worth a few to tens of thousands of taels.

The Mighty Dragon Xuan Yuan is a near-heavenly tool, and it's related to the safety of the Ji Ning Prefecture's common people, so it's worth one million taels! You actually think the amount is too little, the Shen Mansion probably can't even produce one million taels in cash." Chen Shenwei panted as he spoke.

The Shen Mansion's assets exceeded one million taels, but most of it was in fixed assets, with little cash or silver notes.

Chen Shenwei was considered relatively wealthy, but the silver notes he had on him were barely worth ten thousand taels.

"Enough about that, here are the materials for forging the Mysterious Element Spear, forge it in the next few days and you can go to Mount Ao Lake." Chen Shenwei handed a pile of materials to Qi Yuan.

The Mysterious Element Spear was a common unranked spirit tool.

In a regular military formation, there would be a powerful spear unit, and their weapons were the Mysterious Element Spears.

These spearmen were the elite of the army.

A thousand-man spear unit could suppress an earth-grade spirit cultivator.

"Okay, I'll start forging it now."

"Isn't it a bit too early? Aren't you going to..."

Chen Shenwei's words got stuck at this point.

He saw Qi Yuan's arm emanating a cold and scorching flame, and a large pile of materials appeared within it.

In an instant, those materials had all melted into molten iron.

"This is Mysterious Jade Iron, even I would need to melt it for a quarter of an hour... You, you, you..." Chen Shenwei was shocked.

He saw Qi Yuan melt the Mysterious Jade Iron in just a few breaths, and looked at Qi Yuan's spiritual fire, full of amazement.

Qi Yuan's spiritual fire had grown stronger than what Chen Shenwei had seen before? Could his spiritual fire continue to evolve? What kind of spiritual fire was this? Countless question marks arose in his mind.

He had never seen this kind of spiritual fire before.

However, his shock did not last long, as a brand new, straight Mysterious Element Spear appeared before him.

The spearhead gleamed with a frigid light, as if a cold beam was flowing through it.

That cold light shone brilliantly, seeming able to pierce through any obstacle in the world.

"Teacher Chen, do I now qualify to go to Mount Ao Lake?" Qi Yuan asked calmly, holding the Mysterious Element Spear.

"Yes, yes, yes!" Chen Shenwei felt like he was still dreaming, hurriedly nodding. "Cough, cough, Qi Yuan, if you manage to get that one million taels, could you give me a little bit... cough, cough."

Qi Yuan gave Chen Shenwei a surprised look.

Who in this world would dare ask him for money? He was the Debt Collection Hall's master.

"Considering how you've been telling good stories these past few days, I'll give you a share." Qi Yuan was very generous.
