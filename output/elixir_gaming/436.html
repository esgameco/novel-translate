Seeing this scene, Qi Yuan was stunned.

This Purple Origin Xiao Lu... seemed to be his fanatic.

There were good people among "fanatics" too.

The two girls argued endlessly.

That was Yang Spirits discoursing, comprehending principles!

For geniuses like them, the benefits were immense, laying the foundation for their future paths.

It could be said, if they had some insights, it could save tens of thousands of years of cultivation.

Even ordinary Yang Spirits, without working for free for the Principle Assembly, had no qualifications to participate.

Only top holy lands like Black Demon Abyss had more spots, even allowing the younger generation to participate.

"Damn, I haven't done missions in so many years!"

"There's less than a year until the Principle Discourse Meeting, how many contributions can we amass?"

The geniuses present had solemn expressions, some happy, some worried.

There were two ways to obtain contribution points: one was doing missions, the other was breaking through in the 'Ancestor Blood Scripture'.

The higher the level breakthrough in the 'Ancestor Blood Scripture', the more contribution points obtained.

For example, Ice Sword had already reached the eighth level of the 'Ancestor Blood Scripture'.

Just the contribution points he could exchange from this were enough to support him getting a spot for the Principle Discourse Meeting.

As the Heavenly Venerable's lesson ended, everyone slowly left.

Purple Origin Xiao Lu's face was a bit red now, obviously she rarely argued with people and was not skilled at it.

She frowned at times, pondered at times, seeming to be replaying her argument in her mind, thinking about where she could further optimize.

At this time, An Xun Lu suddenly attacked her from nearby, speaking sarcastically: "Senior Brother Blood Robe just came to Purple Origin Heaven, just started cultivating the 'Ancestor Blood Scripture', and hasn't done any missions. He'll probably miss this Principle Discourse Meeting, what a pity!"

Qi Yuan felt nothing hearing these words.

Even if he didn't qualify, he still had Mo Chi's invitation.

When the time came, he could just pretend to be Mo Chi and go.

However, it angered Purple Origin Xiao Lu greatly, she directly turned her head and refused to look at An Xun Lu.

She wouldn't listen to bad comments.

An Xun Lu was smug, seeming to declare victory. She didn't pay attention to Qi Yuan anymore, but went towards the mountain peak to see Black Sword.

Purple Origin Xiao Lu's face bore a worried expression.

She waved her hand, revealing many mission lists inside.

She looked at the mission lists with a worried expression.

"Senior Brother Blood Robe, the contribution points for these missions are too few, and they take too much time, you..."

She was very worried for Qi Yuan.

After all, Blood Robe's talent was absolutely terrifying, but if he didn't go to the Principle Discourse Meeting, it would be a huge loss.

The gap with Black Sword would only grow larger.

"It's fine." Qi Yuan's expression was lazy. He looked at the mission list below and suddenly asked, "These contribution points... can they be exchanged for Black Demon Ancestor's blood?"

Because he found that many missions on it were related to the Mo Luo clan.

Recently, the Mo Luo clan stopped paying tribute, causing quite a stir in Black Demon Abyss.

Many new missions were related to the Mo Luo clan.

If he wanted to complete them, he could do so with a wave of his hand.

For example, one of the missions was to go to the Fifth Heaven, communicate with the Mo Luo clan, and get them to pay at least 100 portions of Mi Luo Light as tribute.

Qi Yuan was good at these missions.

"No." Purple Origin Xiao Lu shook her head, her expression troubled.

The preciousness of Black Demon Ancestor's blood meant that apart from Great Principles, there were almost no other ways to obtain it.

She saw Qi Yuan's gaze fixed on the Mo Luo clan section, and thought of something: "Could it be that Senior Brother Blood Robe can solve these Mo Luo clan missions?"
