On the Divine Flower Association forum.

A post was bumped to the top.

"Major news: Water Cloud Palace intends to make a move against the Blood Robe Alliance. Calling on Elder Blood Robe Sword Saint!"

"What's going on? How dare Water Cloud Palace do this? Didn't Elder Blood Robe Sword Saint just destroy Light Palace?"

However, in the next moment, someone commented on her post:

"If Water Cloud Palace dares to make a move, they must be confident and have made thorough preparations.

When Fox Den Xiaoli heard this, she was shocked internally and felt it made sense. She became worried for Blood Robe Sword Saint.

After all, he was the only great cultivator who had chatted with her.

"It can't be true, right? Is Blood Robe Sword Saint okay?!"

At this time, more and more people were replying to the post.

"Alas, it seems Venerable Yiqiong has indeed taken action!"

"This old man long felt Blood Robe Sword Saint would meet this evil fate. He was simply too arrogant and didn't understand how to be humble in dealing with others. He deserves this."

"What a pity, a great elder of a generation!"

"Hey, the outcome hasn't been decided yet. How do you know Blood Robe Sword Saint will be defeated?" Fox Den Xiaoli felt indignant and strongly rebuked those who were singing his doom.

"Since Venerable Yiqiong has chosen to take action, he must be absolutely certain of victory and will defeat him with thunderous methods. Blood Robe Sword Saint is nothing but a clown who suddenly obtained powerful cultivation."

"You speak of Blood Robe Sword Saint like this. If he sees it, aren't you afraid of retaliation?"

"Blood Robe Sword Saint can barely protect himself now. This old man is not afraid of retaliation. Let him come if he has the ability!"

"Blood Robe Sword Saint, this troll, is finally going to die. This is heavenly retribution. This young master is happy and will have an extra meal today!"

On the forum, some people came forward to criticize Blood Robe Sword Saint.

During Blood Robe Sword Saint's peak, they were jealous and envious but didn't dare to speak.

As soon as Blood Robe Sword Saint was "supposedly" in trouble, they all jumped out to offer their opinions.

Moreover, since they weren't openly provoking Blood Robe Sword Saint like he did, they didn't need to fear retaliation.

After all, others didn't know their identities.

Fox Den Xiaoli saw this scene and her eyes turned red: "You're all finished. I'm going to record what you said and send it to Blood Robe Sword Saint!"

She took out a recording stone and recorded the slanderous remarks about Blood Robe Sword Saint on the forum, including the IDs.

At this moment, she was like a devoted fan of Blood Robe Sword Saint, patrolling the entire forum and recording anyone who slandered Blood Robe Sword Saint.

Unfortunately, she was just one person. Otherwise, if there were more of them, they could control the comments like those celebrity fans on the Blue Star.

Majestic mountains and rivers.

Venerable Yunju wore a white robe, looking like an immortal.

Suddenly, he opened his eyes with a curious expression.

"How does my old friend have time to visit me?"

In front of Venerable Yunju, an old man with white hair appeared.

The old man's body exuded a strong sense of twilight years.

Venerable Yunju looked at the old man with complex thoughts.

This old friend had known him for a long time.

His talent was even higher than his own.

However, a thousand years ago, in order to capture a strand of heavenly veins to help his granddaughter build her foundation with the Heavenly Dao, he accidentally came into contact with a trace of the Great Calamity.

His cultivation could no longer advance, and his lifespan was constantly decreasing.

In just a thousand years, he had already entered his twilight years, like a candle in the wind.

Probably in less than a thousand years, a Yin God would fall because of this.

All Yin Gods were even more fearful of the Great Calamity.

Only a few old friends knew about this white-haired Yin God's injury. Others didn't know.

Even his granddaughter who built her foundation with the Heavenly Dao didn't know.

"It's still my granddaughter, insisting that I come over to ask about Blood Robe Sword Saint's situation.

So, did Venerable Yiqiong really take action against Blood Robe Sword Saint?" the white-haired old man asked.

Venerable Yunju sighed: "I can't disclose this matter."

He had just taken an oath in the Heavenly Earth Cave and couldn't violate it.

He looked at the white-haired old man and added: "Even if you went, you couldn't change the outcome."

"Is that so? Sigh... Ever since I was injured by the Great Calamity, I've become useless, unable to fulfill even a small request from my own granddaughter." The old man lamented, probably also understanding that Venerable Yiqiong had indeed taken action against Blood Robe Sword Saint, and was likely already in the process of doing so. "Yunju, please help this old man one more time.

I can't help Blood Robe Sword Saint, but his Blood Robe Alliance needs to be taken care of."

Hearing this, Venerable Yunju smiled lightly: "You really dote on that granddaughter of yours.

This is a small matter. I'll have someone write a letter to send to Water Cloud Palace to stop the conflict."

"Thank you, old friend." The white-haired old man sighed.

"Binhun, your injury..." Venerable Yunju looked at the old man full of twilight years, his thoughts turning.

"I've already sought an audience with a Great Venerable, but even that Great Venerable was powerless to help." Venerable Binhun's face was full of desolation. "Now I'm at death's door. I only hope that my granddaughter can enter the realm of Yin God before I pass."

"The realm of Yin God, how difficult it is." Venerable Yunju lamented. "Especially with the Great Calamity approaching, breaking through is even more difficult."

Venerable Binhun remained silent.

Now in the Cang Lan Realm, to become a Yin God, one must go to the Outer Heavens to transform their spiritual power into divine spiritual power.

In the Outer Heavens, there are endless seas of lightning. Without someone to protect the way, his granddaughter's foundation was insufficient and she simply couldn't break through to become a Yin God.

But with the Great Calamity approaching, all Yin Gods could barely protect themselves. Who would be willing to waste their own divine spiritual power to go to the Outer Heavens to protect the way?

That kind of consumption was even more than fighting with a Venerable of the same level. Without a hundred years, don't even think about recovering.

"Binhun, you once saved my life. If you... pass on, I will look after your granddaughter to some extent." Venerable Yunju spoke.

A gentle smile appeared on Venerable Binhun's face: "Thank you, old friend."

Snow was falling.

Qi Yuan reached out his hand and caught a snowflake.

Great Wisdom True Lord stood nearby, looking at the snow falling from the sky: "I've heard someone say that no two snowflakes in the world are exactly the same.

Snowflakes are like people... there are no two completely identical people in the world."

"Then why don't snowflakes speak human language?" Qi Yuan asked.

Great Wisdom True Lord looked at the snowflakes: "Maybe because they don't have mouths."

"Really?"

"Probably." Great Wisdom True Lord said uncertainly.

At this time, the snow was falling heavier and heavier.

The aura on Qi Yuan's body was quietly changing.

But this transformation couldn't be seen by anyone, not even that Yin God woman in white.

Only Qi Jianjun, looking at Qi Yuan, had a strange expression.

Getting closer to Qi Yuan, she felt the divine spiritual power in her body constantly increasing, and the speed of opening divine apertures also became faster.

After an unknown amount of time, Qi Yuan held a snowflake.

He looked at the snowflake and said softly: "Grow a mouth."

Only to see that on that petal of snow, a tiny, barely visible little mouth had indeed grown.

"Now that the snowflake has a mouth, it should be able to speak human language, right?"

"Little Snow, speak!"

Jiang Ya, who was not far away, saw this scene and wanted to hold her stomach and laugh.

This Blood Robe Sword Saint made a snowflake grow a mouth, and the snowflake could speak? She naturally didn't know that Qi Yuan now was not using magic to make this snowflake grow a mouth.

But using the ability of the Heavenly Dao.

Just now, he had completely mastered the Heavenly Dao of the Wangyue Continent.

Now he was terrifyingly powerful.

He looked at the snowflake with a curious expression.

Even though he used the power of the Heavenly Dao to make it grow a mouth and ordered it to speak, the snowflake was still as quiet as before.

Great Wisdom True Lord spoke: "Maybe it doesn't have a brain and doesn't know how to speak."

"Brain?" Qi Yuan pondered.

A miniature pig brain appeared inside the snowflake, looking particularly strange.

Jiang Ya saw this and was completely helpless: "Creating life out of thin air is something even a Great Venerable can't do."

"Is that so?" Qi Yuan was a bit disappointed.

He originally thought that after becoming the Heavenly Dao of this world, the world would completely belong to him.

He was comparable to a game creator, able to delete files at will, create game characters, and restore backups.

But... it didn't work.

He could only change the Wangyue Continent within certain rules.

It was as if he, as the Heavenly Dao, also had a heaven above him that managed him.

Just then, the woman in white walked over hurriedly: "Great Priest, the Moluohe army is gathering more and more. I fear in three months, they will be at the city gates.

The Yin Gods of the Moluohe race will also take action at that time! Please, Great Priest, make early preparations and open the teleportation formation!"

Upon hearing this, the expressions of those present changed slightly.

The army of the Moluohe race was finally coming.

Those few Yin Gods were also finally arriving.

They didn't have much time left, only three months.

"Three months?" Qi Yuan was surprised.

The woman in white glanced at Qi Yuan. She knew the Great Priest listened to Blood Robe Sword Saint very much: "Yes, we only have three months left."

"Three months is too long, isn't it? These people are too slow, no wonder they can't accomplish great things." Qi Yuan sighed.

This Moluohe race clearly wasn't capable of doing great things.

Qi Yuan looked back at the crowd behind him and said earnestly: "Never talk bad about others behind their backs, or you'll be executed by firing squad. I once received such a text message and it scared me to death."

Everyone present was completely dumbfounded.
