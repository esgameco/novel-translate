Xianling Yingjun smiled wryly: "Fengti is different from the opponents he has faced before."

The Divine Arrival is strong, unrivaled in the Qingshan Realm.

It can be considered the strongest in the Qingshan Realm.

This scope is limited to one world.

But Fengti, that is on the scale of the universe.

Comparing the Divine Arrival and Fengti is like comparing a village school and a galactic university.

"I hope he doesn't come here... if Brother Qiyuan is really in the Shen Wood Abyss... perhaps he can avoid Fengti." Xianling Yingjun said.

Now this ladder has been refined for a large part.

The blind woman shook her head, she looked up at the tiny specks of light in the sky, and her eyes saw only emptiness.

"This exit cannot accommodate me." The blind woman shook her head.

She wanted to go out, but this exit would not work.

But based on her breathing and heartbeat, Qiyuan felt that although the blind woman had a tall figure, she was not exactly a giant.

The blind woman did not respond, and then asked, "Has the refinement of the artifact arts been perfected? Has the heart's eye awakened? Has the divine artifact been refined?"

Three consecutive questions.

Qiyuan smiled awkwardly: "No."

He didn't want to talk to the blind woman either.

Where did she attack people like this.

"Ah, the Eight Doors Artifact Refinement is too difficult, even now I've only mastered it."

"...A bit slow." The blind woman hesitated for a while before making the comment.

She put down the fruits and went to the place where she often swung on the swing.

Qiyuan set up the repaired ladder, towering high, nearly tens of zhang tall.

He carefully climbed up, finally reaching the top of the ladder, panting heavily.

He began to look at the tiny specks of light again.

Seemingly closer, he could see them more clearly.

"Why... do I hear the sound of water?"

"Could it be that this exit leads to a river?"

"Ah, the heart's eye is so difficult to comprehend."

"If I had a progress bar, it would be better."

"You took a look at the front, proficiency increased by one."

Qiyuan talked nonsense.

"The ladder is indeed useful, it feels closer... and I understand more."

The wind blew, Qiyuan stood on the ladder, blindly surveying that tiny speck of light.

Time passed, Qiyuan had been in the Shen Wood Abyss for almost a year.

The ladder has finally reached the top.

After installing the ladder, Qiyuan climbed up eagerly.

"I see this exit is large and round, yet I still can't comprehend it?"

After focusing on one thing for so long, Qiyuan felt... his awakening of the heart's eye might just be in an instant.

Perhaps, when he touches that speck of light, he will awaken the heart's eye.

With the heart's eye to assist him, the speed of his artifact refinement will probably increase.

Perhaps, the blind artifact refiner's wish can be realized, playing the dual roles of the Divine Arrival and the blind artifact refiner.

With these thoughts, Qiyuan had already climbed to the top of the ladder.

The wind was a bit strong, and the ladder was swaying.

He stared at the speck of light, holding his breath, looking at it intently.

The wind stopped, his mind calmed.

"So-called heart's eye, is just another way of observing things."

"The eyes can deceive, but the heart cannot."

"No, my eyes do not deceive me, nor does my heart."

In that instant, Qiyuan felt a sense of enlightenment.

"The heart is the continuation of the eyes..."

A profound feeling permeated Qiyuan's eyes.

Suddenly, he felt the world become clear, no longer just black and red.

"Still a blind person... but my perception... has increased greatly."

This enhancement of perception, for Qiyuan, could be considered a small golden finger.

His perception was already very strong, and now it has been further enhanced.
