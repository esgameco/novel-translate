Qi Yuan also remembered these two people, a brother and sister.

This morning when they came to visit him, they also brought some spiritual soup.

The taste was good, just a bit salty.

"This fight for the dragon must be handled with great care," Kong Fulu's face was gloomy. "Daring to harm our Shenguang Sect's allies, they are courting death!"

"This person's Regret Dragon Hand has been practiced to great perfection, three people were swiftly killed. Among the imperial princes, who is hiding so deeply?" Dong Xian was puzzled.

The imperial princes of the Great Liang Kingdom were mediocre in talent.

Otherwise, they would not have sought external aid to help with their foundation building.

The result was that one prince and two foundation building cultivators were killed in one strike, indicating the murderer's strength was extraordinary.

"There's no need to think about who the murderer is, we will eventually encounter the murderer and kill him!" Qi Yuan said on the side.

In the cultivation world, Qi Yuan did not want to use his brain.

If he could use his hands, why bother using his brain?

"Mm, let's continue deeper into the Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm," the white-haired Kong Fulu said.

...

Two days passed, Qi Yuan, Kong Fulu, and Dong Xian had walked a considerable distance deeper into the secret realm.

This time, they discovered six more corpses in the depths of the secret realm.

These corpses, just like the ones Dong Xian had seen before, were all killed by the Regret Dragon Hand.

Kong Fulu's face looked extremely grim: "Again the Regret Dragon Hand, one strike killed six foundation building cultivators, does the Great Liang Kingdom's imperial clan have such a powerful prince?"

Even Kong Fulu himself could not instantly kill six foundation building cultivators, two of whom were in the late foundation building stage.

This was at least at the Yuandan level of strength.

Qi Yuan looked at the six corpses, his eyes revealing a solemn expression for the first time: "This killer is quite formidable."

His eyes could see more.

[He was an ordinary foundation building cultivator, killed by a unique Infant Immortal cultivator.]

The other five corpses did not reveal any useful information.

An Infant Immortal, how could they enter the Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm? At the entrance of the Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm, the Venerable True Lord Taigang was stationed.

And to reach the Infant Immortal realm in just a hundred years?

This talent was too exaggerated.

Even for a unique Infant Immortal, it was still exaggerated.

"Which of your Great Liang Kingdom's royalty has cultivated to the Infant Immortal realm?" Qi Yuan asked.

Dong Xian was taken aback, not knowing why Qi Yuan asked this, but he still answered seriously.

"In our Great Liang Kingdom's royal clan, there are only two Infant Immortal ancestors, but the two of them... passed away several years ago," Dong Xian said dejectedly.

The decline of the royal clan was also greatly related to the passing of those two Infant Immortal ancestors.

Nowadays, the Infant Immortal veneration in the royal clan was not from the royal bloodline.

"Is that so?" Qi Yuan did not ask further.

An Infant Immortal monster somehow infiltrated and hunted the foundation building cultivators entering the Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm.

Qi Yuan couldn't help but smell a whiff of conspiracy.

At this moment, Kong Fulu suddenly said, "There are fluctuations of battle ahead, it seems to be people from the Floating Mountain, let's go take a look."

"Okay." Qi Yuan was somewhat expectant.

Was his white moonlight there?

The three hurried towards the direction of the battle.

Even before arriving, Qi Yuan saw a sky full of sword qi and constantly severed trees.

Evidently, the battle ahead was extremely fierce.

"Jingold Sword Technique!" Kong Fulu's eyes revealed a solemn expression, "One of the seven major sword techniques of the Floating Mountain, it is indeed powerful."

The three hurried over, only to see in front of them, Lei Wen and Miao Li were battling a robe-wearing expert.

That robe-wearing expert, alone faced the two foundation building disciples of the Floating Mountain, and did not fall behind.

This was extremely terrifying.

Dong Xian saw the robe-wearing expert and his eyes revealed a trace of shock: "Second Prince!"

That robe-wearing expert was actually the Second Prince.

The Second Prince, who was also his second elder brother, when did he become so formidable, able to contend with the two true transmission disciples of the Floating Mountain.

At this time, Lei Wen's voice came: "Elder Brother Kong, quickly come and help me, this person in the Heavenly Dragon Immortal Realm is indiscriminately killing innocents with the Regret Dragon Hand, at least three princes have already died by his hand.

The fellow disciples of the Hatred Gate and the Great Complete Association have died by his hand!"

"So it was him?" Kong Fulu was furious, "Deliberately targeting us, let him experience the might of the Shenguang Sect's Sect Master's son-in-law, the Peak Master of the Five Light Peak's personal disciple!"

He also saw that three princes died at the hands of an unknown expert.

It seems that it was this Second Prince recklessly killing innocents here.

And this Second Prince, just happened to target the sects related to the Shenguang Sect and the Floating Mountain.

Kong Fulu raised his duster, quickly joining the battlefield.

Five Light Saint Art!

As soon as Kong Fulu acted, it was his most satisfactory Five Light Saint Art.

Qi Yuan did not take action, because that Lei Wen did not call him.

And he felt that with Kong Fulu's addition, that Second Prince would certainly not be a match.

He was a Dao Foundation Building cultivator, how could he casually act?

He had to maintain his style.

Sure enough, with Kong Fulu's intervention, the Five Light Saint Art combined with the Jingold Sword Technique.

The trees in front were flying, and multicolored lights constantly appeared.

It was like a messy special effects animation video.

Qi Yuan watching on the side felt quite bored.

Dong Xian watched with a trembling heart, with sweat constantly flowing out.

"Sigh, it's really better with music in the battle, it looks more passionate, how boring." Qi Yuan couldn't help but sigh.

Watching animations or browsing short videos before, the content might be ordinary, but with the music playing, the aura would be completely different.

It could even give people a surge of fighting spirit, making them feel deeply moved.

Dong Xian stood beside Qi Yuan, completely bewildered.

At this time, Qi Yuan took out a jade slip from his storage bag: "Fortunately, I have some music stored."

Since the battle in front of him was not worthy of music, he would provide his own.

Sure enough, with the music playing, Qi Yuan watched the battle with great interest.

"I am a Dao Foundation Building cultivator, in the future when I fight, I must also have a BGM for myself, this way it will be more in line with my style." Qi Yuan thought seriously.

BGM could give him an invincible mentality.

When he faced the nearly three thousand foreign evil cultivators in the Realm Extermination, if he had a BGM, perhaps he would not have suffered such heavy injuries.

The battle was now nearing its end, the battling Miao Li, seeing Qi Yuan playing some tunes, glared at him angrily.

Others were fighting, while he was just playing music.

"Good tune!" Kong Fulu felt spirited, feeling that his junior brother really understood him.

His figure flashed, the Five Light Saint Art landed on the Second Prince's chest.

"Die!"

The powerful energy of the Five Light Saint Art drilled into the Second Prince's chest.

Blood flowed from the Second Prince's seven orifices, his aura rapidly plummeting, but he had a crazed smile on his face: "Insulting my royal clan, you will all die."

As he spoke, a blood-red flower appeared in his hand, and he crushed it.

His aura completely disappeared at that moment.

Qi Yuan at this time widened his eyes: "What a loss!"

Because just now, his eyes had glimpsed that flower.

His eyes had received a prompt.

[Soul Remembrance Flower, when crushed, it can completely transmit the scenes witnessed by the deceased to the person holding the main flower.

If not crushed, it can also transmit only fragments of words.

The Soul Remembrance Flower is also the main medicinal herb for refining the Divided Soul Pill, consuming it can enhance one's spiritual will.]

Qi Yuan's figure flashed, appearing in front of the Second Prince's corpse, picking up the fragments of the Soul Remembrance Flower, his eyes filled with a look of regret.

The flat-chested Miao Li glanced at Qi Yuan, her eyes revealing a resentful expression.

"Junior brother, what have you discovered?" Kong Fulu asked.

Lei Wen also stood nearby, full of curiosity, wondering what Qi Yuan had discovered.

"This is called the Soul Remembrance Flower, after being crushed, the scenes that occurred here will be transmitted to the person holding the main flower.

So, the Second Prince must have had an accomplice." There was no need to hide this information.

Kong Fulu's face turned gloomy upon hearing this: "It seems this matter is not simple."

Miao Li couldn't help but say, "Even if there are accomplices, could their strength surpass the Second Prince? A minor problem."

Lei Wen also agreed to some extent.

The Venerable True Lord Taigang was stationed at the entrance, even if someone was trying to fish in troubled waters and enter, it could not be in large numbers.

At this time, Qi Yuan said, "Elder Brother, I may have to separate from you all."

Since there was a Soul Remembrance Flower, the Second Prince's accomplice must also have a Soul Remembrance Flower.

Now, what Qi Yuan lacked the most was the strength of his spiritual will.

With a weak spiritual will, he couldn't even enter the game.

Currently, Qi Yuan had been constantly seeking methods to enhance his spiritual will.

Consuming the Soul Remembrance Flower could increase his spiritual will.

And it could also be used to refine the Divided Soul Pill.

He urgently needed it.

With an enhanced spiritual will, perhaps he could even enter the second game.

That mischievous game had been tempting him every day, but wouldn't let him in.
