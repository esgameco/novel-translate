The people of Stone Family Village all had terrified expressions on their faces.

Qi Yuan looked at the red-skirted wolf dog, which was the god of Stone Family Village, with a strange look in his eyes.

"A god-infant at the peak stage who has grasped a trace of primordial divine power?"

He could tell at a glance that the so-called deity before him actually had limited strength, not much more powerful than the villagers.

Merely a god-infant at the peak stage, yet under the worship of the villagers, it had condensed a trace of primordial divine power.

This was already quite frightening.

In the Azure Waves Realm, if god-infant cultivators could master primordial divine powers through such means, without a doubt, the entire Azure Waves Realm would be thrown into chaos, with "deities" everywhere.

It's worth noting that the Great Shang Nation had dozens of god-infant cultivators, while the Hundred Nations had thousands, yet not a single Purple Palace cultivator among them.

This shows just how vast the gap is between Purple Palace and god-infant stages.

The red-skirted wolf dog struck an alluring pose and extended its thick tongue: "A newly born ancient god must taste delicious."

New gods devouring ancient gods can increase their cultivation.

The red-skirted wolf dog's eyes gleamed as it looked at Qi Yuan.

Qi Yuan, maintaining his high status, casually said: "Hmph, you little monster, you should be able to give me quite a bit of experience points, right?"

After coming to this world, Qi Yuan discovered that his character level was different from the previous 1 to 100 levels.

Now, he was actually a Divine Being Level 1.

While before him, the red-skirted wolf dog was a Divine Being Level 55.

The gap between them... was extremely large.

It was comparable to the difference between early Yuan Dan stage and late God-infant stage.

Moreover, the opponent was the deity of this territory, a god-infant wielding primordial divine powers.

Generally speaking, it could indeed instantly kill Qi Yuan.

Currently, unless Qi Yuan summoned his Star Golden Core, it would indeed be difficult to overcome such a large level gap with just his in-game strength.

"My people, offer yourselves to me!"

"Follow me to slay the enemy!"

At this moment, the red-skirted wolf dog let out a coquettish shout.

The villagers of Stone Family Village couldn't help but frown, their faces turning pale.

Evidently, faced with Qi Yuan, this suddenly appearing ancient god, the red-skirted wolf dog appeared contemptuous on the surface, but went all out as soon as it acted.

Countless red light dots flew out from the villagers' bodies, falling into the red-skirted wolf dog's hands.

Finally, they formed a huge wolf tooth club.

Shen Tu looked at the wolf tooth club, his gums trembling: "This is a sacrificial weapon!"

A sacrificial weapon is formed by the offerings of countless people, containing the hearts of the masses, representing the primordial essence of the world.

Of course, according to Qi Yuan's understanding, this was simply a magical tool that could unleash primordial divine powers.

"Interesting," Qi Yuan was surprised.

This was the first time he had seen someone like him inscribe primordial divine powers onto a magical tool.

At this time, Village Chief Yu Lu had arrived with nearly a hundred villagers.

Seeing the red-skirted wolf dog absorbing the villagers' offerings, fear appeared in their eyes.

"We're doomed!"

"Evil god... ah!"

"Go, quickly help our deity!" Village Chief Yu Lu spoke.

All the villagers didn't hesitate, forming a united front at this moment to go help Qi Yuan.

But Qi Yuan said: "More people means a livelier feast."

Qi Yuan showed no fear towards this Level 55 Divine Being.

"Sword, come!"

He called out lightly.

The long sword behind him suddenly unsheathed.

The sword was blood-red all over, engraved with complex patterns. On the hilt, seven figures with ferocious faces sat in a circle, each with a different expression.

An overwhelming blood aura spread at this moment, turning the entire sky of Stone Family Village blood-red.

The sky was covered with a layer of blood color, as if a giant demon had descended.

The villagers from Qingshui Village who had come to provide support couldn't help but stop in their tracks.

That terrifying blood aura... was like an evil weapon that had slaughtered countless lives.

Seeing this, the villagers of Stone Family Village couldn't help but look frightened: "Evil god!"

"It's actually an evil god!"

Just now, the villagers of Qingshui Village were saying the deity of Stone Family Village was an evil god.

Now, the villagers of Stone Family Village were turning around and calling the deity of Qingshui Village an evil god!

Qi Yuan looked at the red-skirted wolf dog: "You have the support of your people and the backing of your temple, but I have one sword to cut off your dog head!"

This time when saying "cut off your dog head", Qi Yuan said it with a clear conscience.

Because the opponent really did have a dog's head.

"Sword, go."

Qi Yuan didn't even move himself.

His body worth thousands of gold, he wouldn't stand beneath a dangerous wall.

At only Level 1, if he got touched, it would be a big disaster.

The long sword inscribed with primordial divine powers flew towards the red-skirted wolf dog.

The red-skirted wolf dog's expression was terrified.

"Where did you get a sacrificial weapon!"

It didn't understand.

The opponent had clearly left the temple and territory, so how could he still have a sacrificial weapon, and such a terrifying one at that.

The wolf tooth club in its hand was just a small fry compared to that terrifying long sword.

"Chop off the dog head!" Qi Yuan didn't bother responding, directly commanding.

The long sword inscribed with primordial divine powers charged towards the red-skirted wolf dog.

The primordial divine power of the Heart Demon Attractor of the Void Realm was unleashed.

How could a mere god-infant at the peak stage withstand an inscribed primordial divine power?

In just half a breath's time, the guardian deity of Stone Family Village fell into madness.

"Eat, I want to eat!"

The sword body flew past, and the red-skirted wolf dog's head fell to the ground.

The mighty guardian deity was easily slain.

A vast amount of experience entered Qi Yuan's body at this moment.

His level also rose from 1 to 2, 3, finally stopping at 11.

The villagers of Stone Family Village were all stunned.

Village Chief Yu Lu was also stunned.

The villagers behind him were all dumbfounded.

Shouldn't a deity who had left the temple be very weak?

How... could he slay Stone Family Village's new god?

Especially the fat man who was previously prepared to laugh at Qi Yuan, his eyes were now wide open.

"Our god's mind seems a bit off, but his strength... is so powerful!"

How could such a powerful god appear in their village, with such a dilapidated temple? It seemed unreasonable.

Village Chief Yu Lu's eyes fell on that blood-colored long sword.

"It's not the god who is strong, it's that sword!"

He could see that the primordial divine power aura contained in that sword was particularly dense.

One step further and it could become a divine artifact!

That would be a powerful divine artifact capable of suppressing an entire city!

"Our ancient god was born holding this sword, he may be... the offspring of some true deity!" Village Chief Yu Lu judged.

Otherwise, how could an ordinary guardian deity be born with such a powerful sacrificial weapon?

Hearing this, the other villagers also felt excited.

Their broken village's deity had quite a background!

"With such a powerful deity, can we eat two steamed buns per meal now?"

These villagers boldly fantasized.

At this time, the villagers of Stone Family Village were even more terrified.

Especially the most arrogant family in Stone Family Village.

They had invited the new god, and their fates were tightly bound to the new god.

With the new god's fall, they...

"No!"

A terrifying wolf head struggled out from one villager's body.

Then, wolf heads successively emerged from over a dozen other villagers' bodies.

"They have already been assimilated by the new god. With the new god's fall, they will also transform into... divine abominations!" Shen Tu quickly said.

The more loyal one was to the new god, the more benefits they received, and the more the new god would assimilate them, turning them into temple guardians.

And if the new god fell, they would become divine abominations with no consciousness, only knowing how to kill.

The previously most arrogant family in Stone Family Village instantly had dozens of divine abominations appear.

"The little monster died and turned into littler monsters?"

Qi Yuan glanced over.

The blood-colored long sword turned into a streak of light.

"All things ward off evil!"

"Chop off all their heads!"

"Hold a great feast!"

"Feast for thirty days straight!"

A feast held by one deity would be rather meager, lasting only one day.

But if an entire family was killed, wouldn't that allow for several days of feasting?

The blood-colored light streaked through Stone Family Village.

With each villager-turned-wolf-person slain, a trace of satisfaction appeared on Qi Yuan's face.

The days of feasting could be extended by another day.

In a dozen breaths' time, all those terrifying divine abominations were slain by Qi Yuan.

His experience increased slightly again.

Compared to the guardian deity, the experience gained from those divine abominations was hardly worth mentioning.

After slaying all the divine abominations, Qi Yuan and Shen Tu landed in Stone Family Village.

Qi Yuan's expression was majestic, his body emanating a powerful aura.

The blood-colored long sword returned to its scabbard.

Seeing this, the remaining villagers of Stone Family Village quickly knelt down: "Great deity, please spare our lives!"

There was no escape, they could only beg for mercy now.

"We didn't worship the new god, it was all forced upon us by that dog-son Stone Fugui!"

The burly man who was previously held down also solemnly knelt: "Thank you deity for saving my child. I, Wang, pledge my life to you, great deity!"

How could such a god kill Stone Family Village's evil god?
