He was no longer the novice cultivator who had to sneak around at night to destroy the Black Mountain Sect; nor was he the small Core Formation cultivator who had to use various aliases to post and provoke conflicts when destroying the Palace of Light.

He was now ranked among the Purple Mansion great cultivators, able to claim a mountain as his own and establish his own sect in the Azure Waves Realm!

This time, he directly declared war, telling the Great Calamity that he, Qi Yuan, was coming to eliminate it.

However, he would personally go to destroy the Great Calamity.

It was that simple, that casual!

"Blood Robe Sword God has such great confidence, his aura is so strong, he looks like an extraordinary and imposing person at first glance. Being so powerful, he will surely have a different fate than others who resist the Great Calamity," Mixed Poverty Great Venerable said with a grin at this moment.

He had only sent an avatar, his true body not revealed, so he was used to acting more freely.

His voice, if changed to a female voice, and if he cooperated by turning into a woman, with stars in his eyes, clenching his little fists, and shouting "Big brother is amazing," that flavor would be absolutely perfect.

Qi Yuan's gaze fell on Mixed Poverty Great Venerable: "Why do you speak in such a sarcastic tone? Could it be... you have an affinity with my Yin-Yang Forbidden Realm, destined to be a pile of dog shit in the Yin-Yang Forbidden Realm!"

"Hey, please, can't villains just cut off their own heads directly? Why do you have to talk so much? Are you paid by the line?"

As Qi Yuan spoke, without any hesitation, a blood-colored giant hand reached out to grab Mixed Poverty Great Venerable.

Mixed Poverty Great Venerable's expression remained unchanged, calm and composed: "Are you already agitated? You don't have the demeanor of a strong person at all."

Mixed Poverty Great Venerable waved his sleeve, seemingly unafraid of Qi Yuan's attack.

After all, this was merely an avatar projection, not worth mentioning.

As long as he extinguished his consciousness, he could return to his main body.

The Azure Waves Realm was so vast, he could hide in a corner, and even if Blood Robe Sword God turned the heavens upside down, he wouldn't be able to find him.

However, when that blood-red hand grasped his entire being, his expression suddenly changed.

A strong warning surged forth, he felt like an infant, seemingly held in the palm of a mighty deity.

Everything about him seemed to be within the grasp of this blood-robed man before him.

"In the Azure Waves Realm, as long as I lay eyes on any evildoer, they cannot escape my grasp. Do you know what the gold content of a Purple Mansion great cultivator means? Die for me!"

Within the blood-colored giant hand, blood energy boiled and churned.

With a crisp cracking sound, Mixed Poverty Great Venerable's body began to break apart piece by piece.

His eyes revealed an expression of disbelief.

Because his consciousness sensed that it wasn't just this avatar breaking apart, but also his main body! His main body was tens of thousands of miles away, in an unfathomable place.

There, arrays were laid out, such that even if a mythical being came, they wouldn't be able to penetrate the arrays in a short time.

But now... how was this possible!

He looked at the blood-robed man with a shocked face, his eyes revealing a pleading expression, along with intense confusion.

"How... how did you do this?"

As his body shattered, his consciousness also began to crack, blood and soul turning into fragments at this moment.

Qi Yuan narrowed his eyes, his expression cold: "Did you think I was just talking when I said I could hit people through the internet?"

After destroying the Palace of Light, Qi Yuan had indeed hit people through the internet.

Now, he was merely walking the same path as before.

What use was distance?

Back then, there was even a disciple of the Supreme Radiance Palace who talked tough in the Upper Realm, wasn't he still destroyed by a sun shadow Qi Yuan threw down? As for Mixed Poverty Great Venerable, there was no need for a sun shadow, he was directly crushed.

With Qi Yuan's current strength as a Purple Mansion great cultivator, to put it bluntly, besides the Great Calamity, who in this Azure Waves Realm could stop him?

Mixed Poverty Great Venerable perished, turning into a mist of blood.

This was annihilation at the level of the soul, even if a Great Venerable could be reborn from a drop of blood, he could no longer reappear in the mortal world.

The other Great Venerables who saw this scene also revealed surprised expressions in their eyes.

Yellow Ox Great Venerable trembled and said: "Mixed Poverty Great Venerable... is dead?"

This death wasn't just the death of an avatar, but the extinction of the main body as well.

The other Great Venerables all sensed this point, while the Yin Spirit Venerables didn't notice, thinking only Mixed Poverty Great Venerable's avatar had died.

Qi Yuan clapped his hands, killing Mixed Poverty Great Venerable was just like squashing an ant.

"Everyone, I'll trouble you with the promotion," Qi Yuan's tone became casual and relaxed again.

The Yin Spirit Venerables present all breathed a sigh of relief.

"We will vigorously promote 'Qi Yuan Eliminates the Great Calamity'."

"Within one day, half the people in the Azure Waves Realm should know of this plan!"

"Senior Blood Robe Sword God, please be careful, the Great Calamity... is not so easy to deal with. Otherwise... the Azure Waves Realm wouldn't still be like a pigsty after so many years!" an old man sighed, his eyes filled with intense longing.

For cultivators like them, ascending to the Upper Realm was almost a lifelong desire.

In the Lower Realm, they were restricted, unable to obtain sufficient celestial essence to nourish their divine bodies, like living in a sewer.

"Hmm, no more nonsense, I've already personally written the promotional copy, you just need to copy and paste it to send out. You promote the plan, I'll implement it, bye! Oh right, rectifying the cultivation atmosphere of the Azure Waves Realm is everyone's responsibility, starting with me!"

After Qi Yuan finished speaking, his figure also disappeared at this moment.

Left in the room were Great Venerables and Venerables with bewildered, confused, and complex emotions on their faces.

"Can Blood Robe Sword God really do it?"

"That's the Great Calamity... the Great Calamity that even Yang Spirits couldn't destroy!"
