Chapter 232

Here is my full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Wu Shi kept looking ahead, wanting to see the man's appearance.

But no matter what, she couldn't see him.

She stopped looking and shifted her gaze to the charred fish meat.

Disregarding the scalding heat, she grabbed it with her hands and ate the fish meat greedily, savoring it like a delicacy.

As a primordial being, she rejected all the filth of the world. Even specially made nutrient solutions were difficult for her to swallow.

Yet the fish before her was like the most delicious food in the world.

It was a taste she had never experienced before.

"Do you... want some?" Wu Shi paused, holding out the half-eaten fish in her hand towards where the man was.

Then, she heard the man's thoughts.

"Across time and space, I can't touch anything, how could I eat fish?"

Wu Shi was a bit confused by the thoughts she heard.

However, she also believed in her heart that the man before her had come from the future.

"I'm not hungry, I won't eat. You eat more," the man said.

He naturally hoped the No Food Holy Mother would eat more and grow stronger.

After all, he still needed to go to the Celestial Pillar to practice visualization techniques.

Wu Shi felt a bit disappointed. The first time she ate something so delicious, she had to eat alone.

"I said earlier, you give me a fish, I'll grant you a wish. What do you want?" Wu Shi looked up.

She didn't like owing others.

So even when her parents sold her off like merchandise, she never harbored any resentment in her heart.

"A wish?" The man looked at the No Food Holy Mother with a hint of surprise in his eyes. "Do you see that mountain that reaches into the clouds?"

Wu Shi turned her head and seemed to see a celestial pillar.

"That's the Celestial Pillar. Help me keep an eye on it, don't let it collapse," the man said casually.

For some reason, after saying this, he had a sense of destiny.

Wu Shi seemed a bit dazed, but nodded: "Okay."

The man smiled: "Go pick up some more fish, don't starve to death."

Wu Shi was stunned for a moment, then her eyes lit up: "I can eat them all?"

"If I leave and you starve to death early, that wouldn't be interesting," the man said.

Seeing the No Food Holy Mother's appearance, if she hadn't met him, she really would have starved to death.

Then in the future... what would happen with the Celestial Pillar? Moreover, he was originally very kind and law-abiding.

Seeing such a situation, casually showing some kindness would have no impact.

Furthermore, he and the No Food Holy Mother had met before, this being their third and final meeting.

Hearing the man's words, Wu Shi looked very excited. She sat on a wooden stick, flying back and forth here, picking up fish from the ground.

Soon, like a hamster with a hoarding habit, she had collected a large pile of fish.

The man looked at all these fish with a pained expression: "You really don't hold back."

However, he always kept his word. What he had promised, he would do even with tears in his eyes.

So he stepped forward, his flawless glassy blood dripping.

His face gradually turned pale, and his form became somewhat weak, though not significantly.

Wu Shi couldn't see the man's appearance. She looked at all the fish with delight.

"I will help you keep the Celestial Pillar safe," Wu Shi said seriously.

The man thought of something and then said: "Do you have any cultivation techniques now?"

The little girl before him was still too weak, and might perish at any time.

"No," Wu Shi hesitated, "If I cultivate, I won't be worth anything."

"How can you not cultivate? You need to learn to protect yourself," the man said this way.

Protecting the No Food Holy Mother was also protecting the Celestial Pillar.

"I'll teach you to cultivate," the man thought, "First cook the fish, wait for me for a day, I'll create a 'Wu Shi Scripture' for you!"

The visualization technique was special, and he, formed from the Celestial Pillar, couldn't show it to Wu Shi.

So he could only create his own technique.

The man looked at the little Wu Shi and couldn't help but think:

If the No Food Holy Mother cultivated the "Wu Shi Scripture" and became powerful enough to destroy the evil gods, wouldn't that mean...

If he came to this world, would he just need to lie flat and cling to someone powerful?

He looked at Wu Shi with anticipation. He turned around and began seriously writing the cultivation technique.

Creating the "Wu Shi Scripture" was naturally not as complex as the "Qi Yuan Scripture".

On the other side, Wu Shi watched the silent man as she happily started roasting all the fish.

"So hungry."

As night fell, the firelight reflected on Wu Shi's face. Having just washed up simply, she was no longer disheveled, her snow-white skin delicate, appearing otherworldly like an immortal.

She stared blankly at where the man was, pondering something.

"Why... is it so delicious?"

"How... did he do it?"

A seed of doubt was planted in the little girl's heart.

At daybreak, the man opened his eyes and looked towards the little girl not far away. He couldn't help but laugh.

The little girl was hugging a huge cooked fish, seemingly fast asleep.

Suddenly, the little girl opened her eyes. Her eyes were still a bit hazy, as if she had just woken up, but she said expectantly: "Are you ready to teach me to cultivate?"

A look of surprise flashed in the man's eyes: "How did you know I finished writing the technique?"

A sly look flashed in the little girl's eyes: "A woman's third sense."

Of course, she wouldn't say that she had been hearing his inner thoughts all along.

His inner voice had woken her up.

"Tsk tsk, now even a woman's third sense has come out." Qi Yuan didn't pursue the matter further. "The technique I created for you is called the 'Wu Shi Scripture'. Try cultivating with it and see."

As he spoke, he recited the contents of the "Wu Shi Scripture" for Wu Shi to hear.

There was a lot of content. It took him a full three to four hours to finish explaining it once.

However, Wu Shi also demonstrated an ability to memorize things after seeing them once, which saved him from having to repeat himself.

Having obtained the "Wu Shi Scripture", Wu Shi quickly continued cultivating.

Her talent was very good, even to the point of being described as exaggerated.

The man asked himself, if placed in the Cang Lan Realm, Wu Shi's talent would surpass all the geniuses on the Genius Ranking.

Even he himself couldn't compare.

A day later, the man looked at Wu Shi's thin frame and slowly said: "Since... you now barely have the ability to protect yourself, I should be going."

He really should go. He needed to enter the Mountain of Contemplation to contemplate.

Then, bring back his improved subordinates and return for the final battle.

Looking at Wu Shi, he couldn't help but sigh softly.

He recalled his later meeting with the No Food Holy Mother, and also saw her blood spilling on the heavenly pillar.

She... was someone who had already died long ago, a person from the past buried in the dust of history.

Wu Shi's heart tightened, her eyes seeming to show some disappointment. After a moment of silence, she slowly asked: "Will we have a chance to meet again?"

Hearing this, the man also fell silent.

The three chances to use the Lost Fluorescent Stick had all been used up, so he naturally couldn't return to the past again.

As for the No Food Holy Mother...

Thinking of the No Food Holy Mother he met later, she didn't seem to recognize him.

Could it be that the three No Food Holy Mothers he met... were not the same person?

"If fate allows, we'll meet again."

After the man finished speaking, his figure disappeared.

By the dilapidated pond, a pile of burnt fish lay beside the young girl.

At this moment, many emotions flickered in her eyes.

Her hands were tightly clenched, her nails digging into her flesh, drawing blood.

Her eyes revealed a strong look of desire: "I really want to... eat him."

This desire was a bodily instinct.

As one who could devour the original body... she felt that if she wanted to, she could fiercely bite into the void and perhaps tear off a piece of flesh.

But... she didn't dare. She was too timid.

That person was too mysterious.

He could even make food pure, so that even she could eat it.

This was...

She didn't understand why he would do this.

(End of chapter)
