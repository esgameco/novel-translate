She looked at Tusan Gongzi, one-third like looking at a fool, seven-thirds like looking at a swindler.

Tusan Gongzi was silent for a moment, glanced at the ice-cold Xiu Simo standing beside him, and ultimately did not refute.

He looked at Qi Yuan and cautiously asked, "Senior, is his special ability real?"

Facing the elder of the Thorn Blood, he needed some self-defense measures.

"Of course it's real. With this lightsaber, you can walk through Ninguo arrogantly.

As for the other two, do you want to buy them? They're very cheap, and if you don't buy them now, they'll probably be out of stock in a few days." Qi Yuan tempted.

"Without this lightsaber, even I, the Miss, can walk through Ninguo arrogantly." An untimely voice came, and the petite woman smiled brightly.

As she said this, she turned around and walked sideways, like a crab.

Seeing this scene, Qi Yuan's eyes narrowed.

This game still has ruthless people!

What he just said about walking arrogantly was actually a pun.

The ability to "summon the gods" was indeed enough to protect Tusan Gongzi to walk arrogantly, and he had not deceived.

Tusan Gongzi smiled awkwardly: "I feel relieved."

As for Qi Yuan's words, he only believed a third of them.

After all, if Qi Yuan could walk arrogantly himself, he wouldn't be selling this lightsaber?

He could just rob the money directly.

He thought this way, judging the gentleman by his own mean mind.

"Sigh, it seems like another day without any business." Qi Yuan said disappointedly.

"Hey, isn't my 990 taels considered business?" The petite woman couldn't help but say.

"Small business doesn't count as business."

"You..." The petite woman was speechless, "This, this, this... wrap a few of them up for me, I want to buy them."

She arrogantly pointed to a few small items, all of which were non-grade spiritual artifacts.

After all, that kind of grade spiritual artifact lightsaber cost 10,000 taels, and she didn't want to encounter any spiritual artifact assassins.

Qi Yuan immediately beamed, handing the few small items to the petite woman: "A total of 572 taels."

He gave a figure with both zeros and integers, making himself look like a serious businessman.

The petite woman furrowed her brows.

If it were elsewhere, these non-grade spiritual artifacts would be so expensive, she definitely wouldn't bother buying them.

But thinking about the 10,000 taels lightsaber, she felt she was the one being cheated.

Most importantly, these inferior spiritual artifacts had interesting designs.

"Is this considered business now?" The petite woman repeated.

"Medium business doesn't count as business." Qi Yuan answered seriously.

"Greedy!" The petite woman cursed silently, then said, "You're quite creative, how about joining the Heavenly Crafting Hall with me?"

She finally revealed her true purpose.

Her grandfather was a member of the Heavenly Crafting Hall.

At this statement, the expressions of the stall owners and passersby in the area suddenly changed.

"It's the Heavenly Crafting Hall!"

"The Heavenly Crafting Hall is on par with the Artifact Craftsman Association?"

In the Blue Mountain Realm, all artifact craftsmen had an organization called the Artifact Craftsman Association.

All artifact certification and issuance were done by the Artifact Craftsman Association.

This was a huge organization that could influence some regimes.

The world knew the Artifact Craftsman Association was powerful, but in fact, there was a similarly large organization that was not inferior to the Artifact Craftsman Association - that was the Heavenly Crafting Hall.

The Artifact Craftsman Association had many members, and any artifact craftsman could join, with a low threshold.

The Heavenly Crafting Hall was different, it was an elite version of the Artifact Craftsman Association, only allowing some genius artifact craftsmen to join.

Although the Heavenly Crafting Hall had fewer members, its prestige was not much inferior to the Artifact Craftsman Association.

Many artifact craftsmen were proud to join the Heavenly Crafting Hall.

However, the threshold for joining the Heavenly Crafting Hall was too high, and many artifact craftsmen did not qualify, only high-grade artifact craftsmen could directly join.

"Can the Heavenly Crafting Hall give me 100,000 taels of silver per month? Can I use the materials for free?" Qi Yuan asked.

He was currently very weak, so he had only set his salary at 100,000 taels.

"...You, you're really crazy!" The petite woman was exasperated.

Remembering what her senior sister had said, this person was a lunatic, it seems it was true.

Join the Heavenly Crafting Hall and want 100,000 taels per month? Probably even Heavenly-grade artifact craftsmen don't have such treatment.

"Looks like there's no such benefits, the salary is too low, I'm not interested in joining." Qi Yuan refused.

The surrounding stall owners looked at him with amazement.

Those passersby who knew the reputation of the Heavenly Crafting Hall also felt it was unbelievable.

Which artifact craftsman wouldn't want to join the Heavenly Crafting Hall?

"Don't you know what the Heavenly Crafting Hall represents?" The petite woman was stunned that Qi Yuan had rejected her.

The spiritual artifacts Qi Yuan forged were full of creativity.

The Heavenly Crafting Hall was often introducing new things, and they needed people like Qi Yuan.

"No silver, no materials, and even..." Qi Yuan glanced at the ice-cold Xiu Simo, "No mount, I won't go."

"You..." The petite woman gritted her teeth in anger, her chest heaving.

She was so furious she could almost burst.

"This is the token of the Heavenly Crafting Hall. If you want to join, you can join the Heavenly Crafting Hall anytime with this token.

The token cannot be given to others."

The petite woman handed a token to Qi Yuan, then left with the spiritual artifacts she had purchased.

The surrounding people looked at Qi Yuan with envious gazes.

In the following time, Qi Yuan continued to run his stall, and when the night became pitch-black, Qi Yuan couldn't help but sigh.

"Indeed, heaven does not favor the diligent. I worked overtime until 12 o'clock, worked so hard, but in the end, I didn't sell a single spiritual artifact."

"Old man, is it because I'm not working hard enough?"

"Young master has worked very hard!" Chen Kangbao hurriedly said.

After packing up the stall, Qi Yuan took Chen Kangbao back.

The night was deep, and the moonlight seemed cold.

Qi Yuan returned to the Shen Mansion, and found that the Shen Mansion today looked cleaner and more festive than usual.

"Brother-in-law, you're finally back!"

Outside the gate, a sleepy-eyed Shen Lingfeng with big eye bags poked out his head.

"Brother-in-law, my sister came back today!" Shen Lingfeng quickly said, "Unfortunately, she only stayed at home for a little over an hour, and seeing that you hadn't returned, she had an urgent matter and went to Aoyuan Lake again."

Shen Lingfeng looked regretful.

After all, his sister and brother-in-law, according to the actual situation, had not yet met.

Qi Yuan smiled: "It's okay."
