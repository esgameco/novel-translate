Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

Qi Yuan had once again entered the game.

This time, he woke up embracing his little wife.

A prompt appeared.

【The Five Elements Forbidden Realm has been cleared, and rewards have been obtained.】

【Reward 1: 10 Fragments of the Heavenly Dao.】

【Reward 2: The Heart of the Five Elements.】

The Heavenly Dao Fragments this time were not contaminated.

10 fragments, which was within his expectations.

The Heart of the Five Elements... a gift from the Five Ancestors? According to the description, this Heart of the Five Elements could better assist Qi Yuan in the transformation of his Qi cultivation.

The Qi of the Five Elements was extraordinary.

They were both mutually destructive and mutually generative.

Without the Heart of the Five Elements, Qi Yuan's transformation of his Qi into the Qi of the Five Elements would have been quite troublesome.

The first type might be quick, but the second, third, and so on would be very slow.

The Heart of the Five Elements shortened the time and also avoided a lot of trouble.

"Is there only one forbidden land and two absolute domains left?" Qi Yuan sighed.

Through Tang Yan, Qi Yuan had already learned that the so-called Demonic Beings from the Outer Realms were in the Absolute Domain.

So, Qi Yuan only needed to conquer the Yin-Yang Forbidden Realm and the Heavenly Absolute Domain, and then subdue the Demonic Beings from the Outer Realms, and he would be able to obtain all the Heavenly Dao Fragments.

But before that, he needed to go to the Coffin Gathering Place.

The powerful cultivators of the Five Elements Forbidden Realm still had a grand gift for him.

...

Half an hour later.

Qi Yuan stood beside Tang Yan, somewhat surprised.

He didn't expect that these powerful cultivators of the Five Elements Forbidden Realm would perform a Divine Impartation on him.

This meant that they would transfer all their insights into cultivation techniques, as well as all their life experiences, to him.

This feeling was quite extraordinary.

These NPCs in front of him made Qi Yuan develop a sense of admiration in his heart.

He himself could not do something like this, to completely give away all his possessions to someone else.

"Lord of the Clothing Faction, we shall perform a Divine Impartation on you.

Our cultivation insights and experiences will all be passed on to you.

This will accelerate your cultivation progress.

Do you agree?" Feather's voice was gentle.

Dozens of eyes in the coffin stared at Qi Yuan.

To be honest, if it was at night, this would be a bit creepy.

But he already had his Bride, so why would he be afraid of this?

"Okay." Qi Yuan did not refuse.

This Divine Impartation from these NPCs would be of great help to him.

The combat experience, spell casting, and other aspects should also be beneficial to his actual cultivation.

"Lord of the Clothing Faction, before you accept the Divine Impartation, you must take the Demonic Heart Oath and swear to slay the Demonic Beings from the Outer Realms!" Dragon Judge, a man with a full beard, spoke up, his voice somewhat softened as he looked at Qi Yuan.

"No problem." He had already made the same promise to the Five Ancestors of the Five Elements, so agreeing to it with these people was also very normal.

Qi Yuan said, and followed the instructions of these powerful cultivators of the Divine Realm to take the Demonic Heart Oath.

With the oath completed, it meant that Qi Yuan must go and slay the Demonic Beings from the Outer Realms.

"Since the oath is made, then the Divine Impartation... shall begin!" Feather spoke.

The fifty-three people present all looked towards Qi Yuan.

Most of these fifty-three were half-reclining in the coffins.

There were also Tang Yan, the red-robed man, and Little Green, who were the young geniuses of the new generation.

Being stared at by so many people, Qi Yuan remained calm.

"Divine Impartation!"

With Feather's loud shout, all the powerful cultivators of the Divine Realm chanted the words "Divine Impartation".

In an instant, Qi Yuan felt the images in his mind constantly flowing.

The memories, cultivation insights, and overwhelming experiences of the fifty-three top experts poured into Qi Yuan's mind.

"Calm your mind, empty your heart, do not try to comprehend, just be an observer!" Feather reminded from the side.

At the beginning of the Divine Impartation, the recipient would only see some deeply imprinted memories.

It was only after the Divine Impartation was completed that they would slowly accept and digest all the memories and insights.

"Mm." Qi Yuan closed his eyes, and many scenes appeared in his mind.

He saw a young man blushing, secretly glancing at the enthroned Bride Princess.

This handsome young man had extraordinary talent, but... was easily flustered, so he deliberately grew a full beard.

This young man was later the Dragon Judge.

He saw Feather's entire family being killed, and the man howling in the rainy night, completely different from his current calm and gentle demeanor.

He saw the burly man, who was carefree and liked to fight crickets in his youth, suddenly become diligent after reading a random book one day.

He saw a fishing line hanging down, the red-robed man's father perishing, the red-robed man hugging the human skin, his eyes bloodshot.

He also saw Gold Ancestor taking Little Green back to the Five Elements Forbidden Realm, and saw Little Green feeding the golden chicken alone in the empty courtyard just yesterday.

He saw many things.

He also received the insights into many cultivation techniques and experiences.

After an unknown period of time, Feather suddenly spoke.

"The Divine Impartation is complete."

The connection was severed, the images abruptly stopped.

But Qi Yuan understood that those memories and insights were now hidden deep in his mind.

"It's over."

"Phew, I've been holding it in for so long, I can finally spit it out."

"It seems I'll have to go into seclusion for ten years."

After the Divine Impartation, they all had a certain degree of weakness.

Qi Yuan looked at the fifty-three people present, and slowly bowed deeply: "All of you who have passed on your techniques to me are my teachers! I will remember this kindness in my heart."

The fifty-three people present either smiled and responded, "We are all allies, as long as we slay the Demonic Beings from the Outer Realms."

More exciting than that.

The Yin-Yang Forbidden, one of the four great forbidden lands.

The most distinctive feature of this forbidden land was that half of it was night, and half of it was day, alternating.

This made Qi Yuan think of the Blue Star.

Unfortunately, the Yin-Yang Forbidden was not a sphere.

Even if Qi Yuan flew up to look, it was not a sphere.

"Defeating Zang Hua and obtaining the divine imprint, that's not a problem."

"But how should I control the Yin-Yang Forbidden?"

Qi Yuan did not want to be a killer of old men again.

And the Yin-Yang Forbidden did not have beings like the Golden Ancestor or the Water Ancestor.

Controlling the Yin-Yang Forbidden became more complicated.

At this moment, a voice sounded from beside Qi Yuan.

"Master of the Clothing Department, you've arrived!"

The speaker was a woman with three thousand strands of blue hair. Her beauty was exquisite, her expression cold, with a kind of broken beauty.

"Zang Hua?" Qi Yuan realized that this would be his opponent in the Yin-Yang Forbidden.

"Defeat me, and the Yin-Yang Forbidden will be under your control!" Zang Hua said coldly.

She finished speaking, without any hesitation, and immediately attacked Qi Yuan.
