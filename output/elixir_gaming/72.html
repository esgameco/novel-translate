The blood-red armor covered his entire body, and Qi Yuan's height had already grown to 137 meters.

On his shoulders, the blood-red thorns were scarlet red, transparent and enchanting under the sunlight, as if they were about to drip blood.

He held a huge longsword, like a demon who had emerged from hell, taking big steps.

Qi Yuan held the sword, his voice hoarse and heavy.

"Kill!"

At this moment, there was only one word - kill! Kill! Kill!

Three thousand mountain peaks flew towards Qi Yuan, and peak-level experts of the Divine Realm attacked from all directions.

Qi Yuan opened his blood-red eyes, and he saw countless afterimages and countless martial arts techniques.

"Kill!"

He raised the longsword with one hand and jumped up violently.

The huge impact force cracked the ground beneath his feet.

The massive blood-colored armor leaped up.

At this moment, all techniques seemed useless.

The 137-meter-tall massive body and the downward impact force were enough to terrify anyone.

Not to mention this huge entity also unleashed an extremely powerful sword strike.

"Die, all of you!"

Everything he saw, he killed!

Qi Yuan did not know how many people he had killed.

But in the game world, on the Ascension Platform, all the people he saw were slaughtered by him one by one!

Seen, then dead!

One sword strike, and the huge mountain peaks were shattered into pieces.

Rocks and soil flew in all directions, breaking into fragments.

The owners of the mountain peaks were directly killed by the sword strike.

"Ah!"

Qi Yuan was like the God of Slaughter, no, the Demon of Slaughter! The huge blood-red treasure sword swung, killing one after another.

Three thousand peak experts of the Divine Realm united and attacked him continuously.

It was as if they were using a spider web to entangle a huge entity.

Qi Yuan wielded the sword, brutally sweeping, breaking through all laws.

One sword strike, no matter what martial arts techniques or forbidden arts, they were all cut off like spider webs and turned into nothingness.

The mountain peaks were broken.

The river water flowed backwards!

The Divine Realm was drenched in blood.

Facing three thousand peak experts of the Divine Realm, Qi Yuan was like a wolf among sheep! The powerful beast, stepping on the ground, looking down from the clouds, slaughtering the masses with the huge treasure sword.

His combat skills, his will, had been forged to perfection in these 129,600 battles.

His battle with the peak experts of the Divine Realm was filled with the aesthetics of violence, brutal, impolite, and domineering; yet also filled with the ultimate in skill, natural, and artistic.

"Kill, kill, kill!"

"Kill, kill, kill!"

Kill him until the heavens and earth are overturned, kill him until the sun and moon lose their light, kill him until the human world is in chaos!

Together with his wife, Qi Yuan slaughtered the three thousand Divine Realm!

In the Five Elements Forbidden Land.

Dragon Judge's eyes had a trace of worry: "Have you felt that the sealing of the Five Elements Forbidden Land is slowly dissipating? Perhaps in a thousand years, it will become an ordinary forbidden land, and even the Supreme Sovereign can step into it."

"You're right." Dragon Judge ended the topic, "How is the Clothing Sect Master faring in the human world now, and how much has our divine projection... consumed?"

"He's only been out for a month? You're thinking too much. There are still three hundred years, I hope he can reach the peak of the Divine Realm, so that we will have a better chance against the evil entities from the Outer Realm."

Dragon Judge thought of something, and his expression became grave: "When the Heavenly Deity went to resist the evil entities from the Outer Realm, he once sent you a message. Apart from what you've already told us, is there really nothing else, such as the strength and specific number of the evil entities from the Outer Realm?"

Feather fell silent for a moment: "No, I've already told you everything the Heavenly Deity said."

"Why bother hiding it?" Dragon Judge sighed upon hearing this.

"There's nothing."

...

"Die!"

The huge treasure sword descended from the sky, shattering the last mountain peak.

The sword tip also ground down the last peak-level expert of the Three Thousand Divine Realm.

Yes, not stabbed to death, because his body could not accommodate such a huge hole, so he was directly ground down.

Qi Yuan stood in place, his entire body filled with a dense bloody aura, as if it was about to solidify into a tangible form.

Half a day in the outside world, one year in the Ascension Platform.

Qi Yuan fought without rest, continuously.

Finally, he had completely killed all three thousand experts of the Divine Realm.

The blood-red armor on his body had dried up, with some black spots.

He stuck the sword diagonally into the mountain peak and sat down, panting heavily.

The exhaled air from his nostrils and mouth blew away the surrounding debris and mud.

He grabbed the water in the lake and splashed it on his face repeatedly, causing the water level to drop significantly, exposing the silt.

Qi Yuan felt much cooler now.

After defeating the Three Thousand Divine Realm, he had now climbed 129,600 steps.

Going up further, there would be the true Ascension Platform.

After defeating the Golden Armor Divine General, he would completely control Tian Jue.

On the last steps, there were still the Three Thousand Divine Realm.

The final Golden Armor Divine General, what a terrifying existence would he be?

Qi Yuan did not dare to imagine.

"Fight!"

Qi Yuan had no fear.

Now he was the weakest, but also the strongest.

If he retreated, his martial will would dissipate.

He could only forge ahead, unstoppable.

He pulled the sword out from the crevice of the mountain peak, and the mountain peak shattered into pieces.

He held the sword and entered the final Ascension Platform.

His body size was also rapidly shrinking, becoming 12 meters tall.

At this time, a majestic voice came: "You've come."

Qi Yuan looked over and saw the one-armed Golden Armor Divine General.

He stood on the Ascension Platform, his expression mechanical and cold, holding a black spear in his right hand.

The spear was stained with blood and emanated a cold aura.

He was slightly different from the Golden Armor Divine General Qi Yuan first saw.

"After all these years, I never thought someone would still be able to ascend the Ascension Platform," the Golden Armor Divine General spoke, his mechanical voice seeming a bit melancholic.

Qi Yuan asked, "Where can one go after ascending the Ascension Platform?"

The Golden Armor Divine General's expressionless face revealed a strange smile for the first time: "They say it's the starry sky, but..."

He did not continue.

Qi Yuan asked, "But what?"

"But in reality, it's to become the disciple of our Master, and ultimately enter our Master's stomach." The Golden Armor Divine General pointed the black spear at Qi Yuan.

Qi Yuan felt a chill down his spine upon hearing this.

Who is the Master of Tian Jue? Who created the arena? He did not know.

However, the Master of Di Jue is now an evil entity from the Outer Realm.

"The world is now in chaos, with the sun and moon reversed, so a weak one like you has the opportunity to ascend the true Ascension Platform, to stand before me." The Golden Armor Divine General spoke, his voice mechanical and cold, "Come, end this boring battle."

The spear was sharp, emanating a chilling aura.

The one-armed Golden Armor Divine General stood in place, the pressure permeating the air, surpassing that of a myriad armies.

Qi Yuan narrowed his eyes, and information appeared.

【Defeat the Broken Golden Armor Divine General's Remnant Soul, Level 119.】

Level 119?! This was the first time he had seen such a powerful expert in this world.

Previously, he had not seen anyone able to break through level 100.

Even he himself could not break through level 100.

Now, he faced a 119-level expert.

An unknown, mysterious, and powerful expert.

Qi Yuan's hackles rose, his body trembled, every muscle tense.

All his focus, all his mental energy, were concentrated on the Golden Armor Divine General.

He held the sword, he was alert, he attacked.

All his desire to kill, all his fighting will, reached their peak at this moment.

"Kill!"

He understood that this battle was almost certain death.

So, when Qi Yuan struck, it was with his strongest attack.

"Ah!"

The huge treasure sword, empowered by numerous martial arts techniques and skills, unleashed the mightiest strike in the world.

The sword strike pierced directly into the body of the Golden Armor Divine General.

"Ah?!"

The Golden Armor Divine General shattered into fragments.

"Where is he?"

With one sword strike, Qi Yuan saw the Golden Armor Divine General's look of astonishment, saw him turn into fragments.

But how could a 119-level expert be killed by his sword strike?

He naturally knew that was just an illusion!

"Come out!"

"Come out!"

"I see you!"

Qi Yuan held the giant sword and unleashed his signature sword technique again.

The Eight Summer Slashes.

This sword art, wildly swinging, full of loopholes, yet could give him the greatest sense of security.

As if whoever came, would be injured by his Eight Summer Slashes.

Alas, no matter how Qi Yuan shouted, no one responded.

At this moment, a prompt appeared in Qi Yuan's mind.

【Slaying the Broken Golden Armor Divine General's Remnant Soul has granted you the opportunity to reach level 100. Do you wish to ascend?】

Qi Yuan was stunned.

"Did I kill him?"

Lone smoke from the desert, the river's sunset round.
