Soon, the set meal they ordered was served. Qi Yuan and the woman began to share the food.

They chatted as they ate.

"Where's Cui Fan? Where is he? I need to find him urgently," Qi Yuan asked while eating a chicken leg.

Finding Cui Fan was of course to borrow high-interest loans. He might be able to borrow hundreds of thousands.

The woman in the tight outfit smiled coyly, "He's been arrested and is in prison."

Such bad luck! He was chatting with Cui Fan just two days ago, planning to borrow some high-interest loans to spend, and now he's in prison?

"High-interest lenders have backgrounds that are not to be trifled with. They're either involved with organized crime or... If you're short on money, it's best not to borrow from high-interest lenders," the woman named Xiao Mei said softly.

In Tiangyue City, the relationships of high-interest lenders were complex. Those arrested were merely sacrificial pawns.

"But... I need money," Qi Yuan said helplessly. "These police are working too efficiently. Couldn't they wait until after I borrowed the money to make arrests? That way, I wouldn't even have to pay it back."

The woman in the tight outfit was speechless.

She had been looking at it from too high a perspective.

Indeed, for Qi Yuan, practicing martial arts was indeed a... way out.

"If you need money, I can lend you some. How about 100,000?" the woman in the tight outfit asked.

Qi Yuan felt disappointed upon hearing this.

He wanted to borrow money that he wouldn't have to pay back.

With someone like Xiao Mei, it was obvious he would have to repay it.

"I'll borrow, but can I borrow more?" Qi Yuan urgently needed money.

Besides what he needed for martial arts training, he also needed to buy various materials for cultivating immortality.

So, he needed to get more money.

"How about 90 million?" Qi Yuan looked at Xiao Mei expectantly. She seemed very capable.

So he might as well ask for more. What if it worked?

Xiao Mei was speechless. She looked at Qi Yuan and finally said, "At most 300,000."

She had calculated the price well.

300,000 was enough for Qi Yuan to dabble in martial arts.

"Okay, 300,000 it is," Qi Yuan was easily satisfied.

As he spoke, he gave his bank account number to Xiao Mei.

Xiao Mei squinted at Qi Yuan, "It's our first time meeting, and it's a blind date. I'm willing to lend you 300,000. Aren't you afraid... there might be some scheme?"

Indeed, if it were anyone else, today's events would not have happened.

"I have a mental illness certificate. I'm not afraid of schemes," Qi Yuan said matter-of-factly.

"Now I'm really suspecting you're just pretending," the woman in the tight outfit said.

"I've been pretending all along."

"Alright, check your account. I've transferred the money to you. Goodbye," the woman said decisively as she stood up. Her black tight-fitting outfit clung to her long legs, making them appear more fleshy.

"Wait, don't go. We haven't paid for the meal yet!" Qi Yuan called out to the woman in the tight outfit.

The woman was stunned, "I'm paying?"

"Have you ever seen a mentally ill person pay for their own meal?"

The woman's face froze. She gritted her teeth and looked at Qi Yuan, "Fine, I'll pay!"

Qi Yuan lowered his head and continued eating his Qin Deji.

After paying, the woman in the tight outfit left the restaurant and got into a black car parked on the street.

There were two people already in the car.

A male driver and a muscular woman.

Both were sitting in the front, while the woman in the tight outfit sat in the back.

"Captain, is it done?" the muscular woman spoke, her voice gruff.

"Mm..." The woman in the tight outfit had a deep look in her eyes. "I've seen Master's son... Judging by his condition, his body seems fine. It looks like the information we received before was incorrect."

A few days ago, the woman in the tight outfit had received news that Master's son was seriously ill. She hurried back, intending to see Qi Yuan.

But upon meeting him today, although Qi Yuan looked a bit weak, he could still eat. Moreover, her military-grade detection device didn't find any major problems with Qi Yuan's body.

So, the weight on her mind was lifted.

"That's good news," the muscular woman squeezed out a smile. "It's a blessing that he could grow up safe and sound."

"He wants to practice martial arts," the woman in the tight outfit said pensively, fingering a small knife in her slender hands, seemingly lost in thought.

"Martial arts?" The muscular woman's pupils constricted. "Could it be... he knows about Master's death... and wants revenge? But... how could martial arts be enough..."

The woman in the tight outfit smiled gracefully, "You're overthinking it. From what I saw, his interest in martial arts is merely for physical fitness. As for Master's affairs, we'll handle that ourselves."

"You're right," the muscular woman nodded. "Sigh, I don't know what's going on lately, but we've been getting more and more missions. It seems like something strange is happening."

"Mm, it seems like there are more missions to explore ancient tombs and relics," the woman in the tight outfit frowned.

Such missions used to be occasional, but rare.

Recently, however, they've been popping up like bamboo shoots after a spring rain.

"I have a feeling something big is about to happen," the muscular woman murmured.

The driver in front laughed, "No matter how big it is, could it possibly affect small fry like us?"
