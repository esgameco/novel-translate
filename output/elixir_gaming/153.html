Chapter 153

Here is my full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The slaughter began in an instant.

The Grand Palace Master took a step forward, his expression calm. He let out a light shout:

"Nine-Layered Radiant Formation of Huatian!"

Above the nine heavens, radiance descended.

The Grand Palace Master was enveloped in light, his entire being becoming incomparably divine.

The Second and Third Palace Masters flew towards the sky, their deep eyes filled with reverence.

"I summon the Glorious Sword!"

Following their voices, the over fifty Purple Mansion cultivators below suddenly became solemn, shouting in unison:

"We summon the Glorious Sword!"

The forms of the fifty-plus Purple Mansion cultivators became transparent.

Countless rays of light spread over their bodies. As their sleeves waved, all was white light. Each person was like a saint.

Over a thousand Spirit Infant cultivators flew towards the sky like fireflies, emitting their own radiance.

"Glorious Sword, protect our light!"

"Unbreakable Shield, dispel all demons!"

As over a thousand cultivators shouted, thousands of light swords appeared in the sky above the Palace of Light, as if ten thousand swords were being launched simultaneously.

An aura of light, holiness, devotion and sacrifice permeated the air.

Every cultivator had a solemn expression, showing no fear of death, as if fighting for the light.

Seeing this scene, the smile froze on Qi Yuan's face.

He looked at the holy Palace of Light, then at his own blood-soaked self.

How did it feel like he was the big villain? "I'm the one who represents the light, you shameless thieves of light!"

"I, the Blood-Robed Sword Immortal, represent justice and light, to eliminate the likes of you!"

"The true lineage of the Glorious Sword is with me!"

His blood-colored robe fluttered in the wind as Qi Yuan let out a great shout.

In the sky above, endless blood-colored energy boiled and churned. An overwhelming killing intent swept out. Anyone who caught a glimpse of the sky could not help but feel fear.

The blood sword behind him unsheathed, its blood-red light eerie, like the temptation of an evil god.

Virtual Realm Ruler's Heart Demon Summoning!

In that moment, the blood-colored soul sword flew towards the sky.

As if responding to a summons, over a thousand blood-red longswords rose up from the endless sea of blood.

Arrogance, evil, greed, gluttony...

Ghostly winds howled, demons grimaced.

Endless auras spread over the thousand blood-red longswords.

"This is what you call a Glorious Sword!" Qi Yuan glanced back at the Glorious Swords behind him, feeling a bit guilty.

If he had only summoned one sword, he could have still disguised it as white, radiant and holy.

But with over a thousand, it was too difficult to disguise.

So he simply stopped pretending.

Who says villains can't be good guys? "Justice will triumph over evil!" Qi Yuan roared.

The Palace of Light cultivators also shouted: "Glorious Sword, slay all evil!"

In that moment, the red blood swords and the holy light swords collided!

The entire sky was split in two.

One half was a sea of blood, the other half was holy as rain.

Blood color and white collided.

The holy radiance, the purest thing in the world, was instantly defiled by the blood color.

Even the white light on the Grand Palace Master's body became unstable at this moment, as if about to fall off.

The faces of the thousand Spirit Infants and fifty-plus Purple Mansion cultivators all turned deathly pale.

Among them, several dozen Spirit Infants saw their light swords completely turn blood-colored in that instant.

Qi Yuan's face also turned pale.

He had forcibly received several dozen Origin Divine Abilities.

At that moment, he quickly used Death's Radiant Light to recover to normal.

"Not bad, you have some skill. Your fake Glorious Swords can actually contend with my real ones.

It seems killing you will take a day and a night."

In the first clash, both sides suffered losses.

Over a dozen Spirit Infants in the Palace of Light camp directly perished.

While Qi Yuan was somewhat depleted, he was instantly replenished by Death's Radiant Light, as if nothing had happened.

Hearing Qi Yuan's words, all the Purple Mansion cultivators of the Palace of Light were shaken.

Originally, they thought this strike would at least severely injure the Blood-Robed Sword Immortal, if not kill him outright!

But how did the Blood-Robed Sword Immortal look like he was affected at all?

He didn't even have a minor injury.

"Again!" Qi Yuan grew more vigorous as he fought.

In the past, he had challenged the 2,700-year-old elder above his level.

Now, with Origin Divine Abilities, what was wrong with him at the Yuan Dan realm killing some Purple Mansion cultivators?

This time, he summoned a full three thousand Glorious Blood Swords.

Overwhelming with numbers!

"We'll die defending the Palace of Light!" Those Spirit Infants and Purple Mansion cultivators roared, intense light flashing on their bodies.

Boom! The collision happened again.

This time, Qi Yuan simultaneously used Tyrannical Power and Death's Radiant Light, fusing them into his Origin Divine Ability.

Light and blood color intermingled.

Above the light, an additional eerie blood color appeared.

Qi Yuan laughed loudly: "Haha, continue! Your false light will eventually be consumed by me!"

In the distance, all the Purple Mansion cultivators watching the battle were shocked.

"The Blood-Robed Sword Immortal... is terrifyingly strong. Perhaps even Old Man Wutian is no match for him!"

"Give him ten thousand more years, and he might truly become a Yin Immortal!"

"Where did this monster come from? His blood energy reaches the heavens. If he really defeats the Palace of Light, what will become of our Cuiyun Continent in the future?"

Origin Divine Abilities best reveal a cultivator's future nature.

The Blood-Robed Sword Immortal's Origin Divine Ability was a sea of blood, with all kinds of negative emotions gushing forth.

Anyone who looked at him could sense that he was definitely an enormous demon.

"Could it be he's not a cultivator of this realm?" a Purple Mansion cultivator wondered in surprise.

Watching the battle up close, they felt that Qi Yuan's Origin Divine Ability seemed somewhat different from their own.

"A demon that crossed realms? How did the Palace of Light offend him?"

"With such overwhelming blood energy, he must have slaughtered at least several million living beings!"

At this time, space rippled and a man in golden robes murmured: "A body overflowing with blood energy, he must certainly be a great demon.

Originally, I thought to save his life and bring him into our Dao-Seeking Palace, but it seems... we'd better not."

The nearby Purple Mansion cultivators were all shocked to hear this.

"The Dao-Seeking Palace?"

The Dao-Seeking Palace was a mysterious power in the Cang Lan Realm.

This power was founded by a Yin Immortal.

The minimum requirement for entry was late Purple Mansion stage.

These Purple Mansion cultivators gathered together to seek a path to break through to the Yin Immortal realm.

Now, even people from the Dao-Seeking Palace were paying attention to the Blood-Robed Sword Immortal! How could they not be surprised?

"Senior, who do you think will win this battle?" a Purple Mansion cultivator couldn't help but ask the golden-robed man from the Dao-Seeking Palace.

The golden-robed man looked at the great battle ahead: "The Blood-Robed Sword Immortal's Origin Divine Ability is strange, and he has cultivated secret techniques. His overall strength surpasses Old Man Wutian by a notch. If the Palace of Light doesn't change tactics, the Blood-Robed Sword Immortal will inevitably win if this continues.

However..."

The golden-robed man changed his tone.

"The Palace of Light won't sit idly by!

As long as... they bring out a Yin Immortal artifact, the sun and moon will lose their light, and the Blood-Robed Sword Immortal will certainly perish.

This battle... the outcome is already decided."

After speaking, the golden-robed man's form vanished.

At this time, the Palace of Light and Qi Yuan had already clashed over ten rounds.

Each exchange ended with Qi Yuan gaining a slight advantage.

With repeated collisions, over a hundred Spirit Infant cultivators of the Palace of Light were contaminated, giving rise to heart demons.

Light swords turned to blood swords.

Even several Purple Mansion cultivators were affected.

"Continue!" Qi Yuan's divine abilities seemed endless.

The Origin Divine Ability inscribed on his soul sword had absorbed the heart demons of an entire realm in the Liufeng Realm.

What Qi Yuan had no shortage of was Origin Divine Abilities.

The Grand Palace Master's expression was cold as he looked at Qi Yuan, his eyes showing deep wariness: "You have indeed exceeded my expectations. Even Old Man Wutian may not be your match."

Qi Yuan stood in mid-air, his blood-colored robe increasingly bewitching.

"What's the point of saying so much? Today, the Palace of Light will be destroyed!"

Qi Yuan was very relaxed.

To be honest, battling the Palace of Light was not a difficult fight for him at all.

It was just a waste of time, having to grind them down slowly.

"Brother, why waste words with him? Bring out the Yin Immortal artifact and kill this scoundrel in one strike!" the Third Palace Master Zi Cui shouted loudly.

She didn't want to continue this frustrating battle.

If they delayed another incense stick's time, allowing the Blood-Robed Sword Immortal to contaminate more elders and stewards of the Palace of Light, they would certainly suffer severe losses.

The usually taciturn Second Palace Master also spoke up: "Brother, it's time to use the Yin Immortal artifact."

They knew the Yin Immortal artifact couldn't be used lightly, because it was already damaged and would take even longer to repair after use.

With the great calamity approaching, they didn't have much time left to prepare.

But the Blood-Robed Sword Immortal's strength exceeded their expectations, so they had to use this trump card.

The Grand Palace Master then looked at Qi Yuan and sighed softly: "Why must you push us so hard, friend? We great cultivators, our cultivation is hard-won.

Those mortals, killing them is no great matter. Give it a few hundred years, or even a thousand, and they'll appear again like locusts.

They can be said to be inexhaustible, endless.

But for us, it takes over a thousand years of time for even one to appear."

He still held onto the idea of conserving resources if possible.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan's expression was calm: "Is your life so precious?

No, actually it is precious. My moonlight, she is indeed superior, her life is indeed precious!"

His tone held no compromise at all.

Seeing this, the Grand Palace Master's expression turned cold. At this moment he finally made up his mind.

The Imperial Shocking Dragon Crown! This damaged Yin Immortal artifact finally flew out from his sleeve and landed on his head.

The Imperial Shocking Dragon Crown was entirely golden, with a drop of blood on it - Yin Immortal blood.

If that drop of blood fell, even a late Purple Mansion cultivator could not catch it.

A terrifying, chilling, overwhelmingly domineering aura continuously spread out.

The Grand Palace Master's face showed a fierce and pained expression.

Dense scales continuously emerged all over his body.

His eyes turned golden, and in that instant his head transformed into a dragon's head.

An ancient, mysterious, invincibly powerful aura emanated forth.

Qi Yuan felt that he was no longer facing a Purple Mansion cultivator, but a heavenly god.

"Those who offend my divine might shall die!"

The voice was deep, thick, full of authority.

All the Purple Mansion cultivators' hearts pounded. They felt that if this divine being wished it, their bodies would involuntarily shatter.

At this time, the spiritual energy for thousands of miles around continuously churned, forming a spiritual storm.

"A Yin Immortal!"

"A Yin Immortal has appeared!"

"So this is the Yin Immortal realm. Just one glance and my eyes!" A Spirit Infant cultivator's eyes bled after looking.

"The Blood-Robed Sword Immortal... is finished."

When a Yin Immortal appears, who can oppose them?

All beings are equal - whether Foundation Establishment, Yuan Dan, Spirit Infant or Purple Mansion, all are as fragile as mortals.

"So this is the power of a god?"

For the first time, Qi Yuan's gaze became solemn.

As the Grand Palace Master put on the Imperial Shocking Dragon Crown,

The entire Palace of Light, all the Spirit Infants and Purple Mansion cultivators, including Qi Yuan, were all pulled into the virtual realm.

Qi Yuan saw that the originally majestic Grand Palace Master now had surging, turbulent energy, with an ancient deity lurking within.

"Die!"

The god extended a finger.

This finger was overbearing and unreasonable.

One finger from a god falling could erase an area of nearly 100,000 square kilometers of the Great Shang Nation from the map.

Such a finger was simply not something a human could resist!

Even if dozens, hundreds, or even over a thousand late Purple Mansion great cultivators faced such a finger, they still could not withstand it.

"Using our foundation to kill you, Blood-Robed Sword Immortal, you die with honor."
