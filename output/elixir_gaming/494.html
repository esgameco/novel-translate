Tiger Zhou left the Huang family, his face still clouded with gloom.

"You said the death of the young master of the Huang family, is it related to Qi Yuan?" Zhou Tiger asked the nearby constable, deep in thought.

The death of the director of the asylum, and Huang Yanhou being killed, these two events were easily connected.

At this time, a middle-aged, short and sturdy constable hurriedly came over: "Boss, Qi Yuan has disappeared from the asylum."

"It seems he is indeed involved." Zhou Tiger furrowed his brow, then shook his head.

Even if he was involved, it had nothing to do with him.

"I wonder what abilities the person the Shen family sent to Ninghe County possesses?"

Zhou Tiger was eager for the person to be a Profound Rank Armored Soldier.

But thinking about it, that was impossible.

Although the Shen family was a powerful clan, Profound Rank Armored Soldiers were extremely rare, so how could they dispatch one to handle such a minor matter in Ninghe County?

Profound Rank Armored Soldiers were overwhelmingly powerful, fierce as tigers, with the momentum of elephants, and the spirit of dragons.

One person could be a formidable war machine!

They could easily defeat hundreds of armored elite soldiers in formation.

How could such a terrifying war machine be sent here? Qi Yuan... was not worthy.

As for Earth Rank Armored Soldiers, they were even more terrifying, one person could influence the outcome of a small-scale war.

...

"I'm level 30 now."

In the inn, Qi Yuan stretched and yawned.

In these past days, he and Chen Kangbao had wandered all over Ninghe County, hunting for Ominous Spirits to slay.

He had finally reached level 30.

"There's a strange feeling... as if my realm has reached Foundation Establishment, but my combat power is not as strong as the Foundation Establishment in the Immortal Realm.

Could it be because the worlds are different?"

Qi Yuan pondered.

But thinking about it, Qi Yuan felt his judgment was wrong.

Because he had no reference point.

For example, if his punch had the force of one jin.

What if, in this universe, a one jin rock was heavier than a star in the Immortal Realm?

Or, he may now appear to be less than two meters tall, but if he went to another universe, he might suddenly find himself bigger than a sun.

These were all possibilities.

"If compared to the extraordinary system of this universe, I should now be... a Yellow Rank Armored Soldier?"

Qi Yuan speculated.

Ninghe County also had Armored Soldiers, although they were weak, Qi Yuan had secretly observed them.

Not to mention combat experience, just in terms of performance, from level 1 to 29, they should be Mortal Rank Armored Soldiers.

From level 30 to 59, Qi Yuan estimated they would be Yellow Rank Armored Soldiers.

Every 30 levels corresponded to a major realm, and every 10 levels corresponded to early, middle, and late stages.

"Unexpectedly, this time I also awakened a major skill."

On the Wangyue Continent, every 10 levels Qi Yuan would awaken a minor skill, and every 30 levels a major skill.

Such as Tyrannical Strength, Heavenly Sword Art, and Deathly Radiance.

In this world, those minor skills did not awaken.

But at level 30, the major skill Tyrannical Strength returned.

In addition, Qi Yuan awakened an extra skill.

Thousand Transformations!

As the name suggests, this is a skill of transformation.

When activating the skill, Qi Yuan can disguise himself as others, or even objects, like a dog's tail.

This skill, when viewed in the Immortal Cultivation world, seems quite useless.

After all, great cultivators can all transform.

But in this world, this skill is quite extraordinary.

"According to the memories of the Qi Yuan the son-in-law, even those legendary Earth Rank Armored Soldiers can at most change their own appearance, or even change their bones, but cannot transform like Thousand Transformations... even saying that as long as my level is high enough, I can transform into anything."

If one could say that Profound Rank Armored Soldiers were the elite of a force, the upper echelon power.

Then Earth Rank Armored Soldiers would be the hegemon of a region.

The weak Ning Kingdom controlled only three states.

Each state had about seven prefectures.

Jining Prefecture was located in Jiang State, and only had three prefectures left, the rest had been ceded to the Moshan Kingdom.

The imperial power of the Ning Kingdom had declined, in recent years the imperial court did not even have a Heaven Rank Armored Soldier.

This also led to the imperial court being unable to suppress the powerful aristocratic families.

Aristocratic families with Earth Rank Armored Soldiers were already considered colossal forces in the Ning Kingdom, daring to defy the imperial court.

Of course, it was just defiance.

Similar to a certain manga, where a Great Sage Bodily Cultivator defied the Emperor.

The nine major families of Jining Prefecture included the Shen family, and the Shen family had an Earth Rank Armored Soldier stationed there.

"Thousand Transformations!"

At this moment, Qi Yuan activated his skill, transforming into a cleaver.

Suddenly, a large cleaver appeared in the room.

This scene, if seen by an Earth Rank Armored Soldier, would be astounding and unbelievable.

While Chen Kangbao was eating a bun, his face full of puzzlement and confusion: "Young Master, why did you turn into a cleaver?"

As the young master was a divine being descended from the heavens, Chen Kangbao found it unsurprising that he could turn into a cleaver.

"Because I'm pretending to be a cleaver." The cleaver spoke with a mouth.

The mouth on this cleaver looked like cheap special effects in a micro-film.

"It seems I'm still too weak, and can only transform into things not much larger than myself."

Now Qi Yuan's strength was weak, so his Thousand Transformations was limited.

Otherwise, when facing a powerful enemy, he could directly transform into that enemy's cell nucleus, and the enemy probably wouldn't be able to find him.

However, the good news was that he could now transform into a cleaver.

Wouldn't that make playing the role of a cleaver more convenient in the future?

"I can transform into a cleaver, then... can I transform into a god?"

Qi Yuan suddenly had a strange idea.

He didn't know what a god was.

But with Thousand Transformations, transforming into himself... shouldn't be a problem, right?

Although he didn't know what a god was, what if it succeeded?

Thinking of doing it, he did it.

Thousand Transformations!

Qi Yuan activated this newly mastered skill.

In an instant, a deep, vast aura swept over.

Qi Yuan's thoughts became clear at this moment.

Countless murmurs and whispers sounded in his ears, as if tens of thousands of monks were chanting sutras in his ears.

These voices were too distant for him to hear clearly.

However, a faint young voice suddenly rang out clearly in his ears.

"More and more people have been dying in Red Cloud Stronghold lately, god... please bless me!"

Qi Yuan raised his gaze and looked towards the direction of the voice.

In the simple house in Red Cloud Stronghold, Li Xiaodanwas kneeling in front of the idol, suddenly shuddering.

Because, for some reason, he felt the eyes of the idol seemed to have come alive, and were gazing at him.

This feeling was eerie and made the hair on the back of his neck stand up.

"An illusion? Or...?" Li Xiaodancarefully looked at the idol, feeling afraid.

Suddenly, a red light flashed above the idol.

A cold, indifferent voice spoke.

"I am not a god, no need to kneel."

"Ah..."

Hearing this voice, Li Xiaodanfaced turned terrified, and he fled the house as if escaping.

At the same time, in the distant lands of the Luosha Kingdom.

In the imperial palace, a furious and terrified roar echoed.

"A god?!"

"Wasn't it already shattered into countless fragments and thrown into the Yellow Springs and Fierce Tombs?"

"How could a wisp of its aura emerge?"

"Is the Judgment Court reviving it?"

"Quickly send people to Ninghe Kingdom!"

"The Luosha Kingdom does not need a god, only an emperor!"

After all, this Zhifeng was no ordinary maid.

How dare he speak to her like this?
