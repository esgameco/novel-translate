On Twilight and Fire Lotus, the leader of the Ancient God Alliance sensed an unrivaled aura.

This indicated... they were not simply at the fourth step of Treading the Heavens.

How could the three great venerable ones of the Ancient God Alliance not be alarmed, feeling this must be an illusion.

The other ancient gods also watched this scene, shocked in their hearts.

"Kill," Qi Yuan said softly.

At this moment, Venerable Twilight and Venerable Fire Lotus no longer held back.

Facing their former leader, they were decisive in their killing. Their powerful divine domains erupted at this moment, directly pressing down on the three great venerable ones of the Ancient God Alliance.

The leader of the Ancient God Alliance's face changed drastically: "The fifth step of Treading the Heavens!"

"How is this possible!" Another great venerable one was terrified.

"We're all on the same path, all this fighting and killing isn't good!" One of the Ancient God Alliance's great venerable ones said resentfully.

Unfortunately, without instructions from Qi Yuan, Venerable Twilight and Venerable Fire Lotus would not show any mercy.

Two terrifying divine domains pressed down, instantly water and fire directly and forcefully shattered the divine domains of the three great venerable ones of the Ancient God Alliance!

The god Venerable Twilight visualized was the Water Official Great Emperor who Resolves Calamities, controlling all the waters under heaven.

How could these three possibly match the divine domain he condensed? These three didn't even have divine offices, and the divine domains they condensed were like hastily assembled porcelain dolls.

In the eyes of Venerable Twilight and Venerable Fire Lotus, they were full of flaws.

In just one breath, the divine domains of the three great venerable ones directly shattered.

Their entire bodies were unable to move.

Instant kill!

This was not only suppression in realm, but even more so suppression in divine domains.

The difference in strength between great venerable ones lies in their divine domains.

Divine domains with divine offices and those without are completely different concepts.

To make an imperfect analogy, one is like a college student using mathematical formulas to solve problems, mastering the formulas but not knowing their true meaning; the other is like the mathematician who created a certain formula.

The gap between the two can be imagined.

The divine domains of the three great venerable ones of the Ancient God Alliance shattered.

Then powerful divine force swept over, and these three great venerable ones... died on the spot.

Silence.

Fear, shock... various emotions swept across the faces of the ancient gods present.

Looking at the corpses of the three great venerable ones, Qi Yuan couldn't help but sigh: "Sending divine force a thousand miles away, the gift is light but the sentiment is heavy. To repay your kindness, I've decided to eat three extra bowls of white rice today."

The deaths of the three great venerable ones didn't cause any ripples in Qi Yuan's eyes.

He even had a good appetite, wanting to eat three bowls of white rice.

Seeing this, the gazes of the other ancient gods present towards Qi Yuan changed slightly.

Ye Liuyun didn't speak.

Qi Yuan's actions had been constantly breaking his understanding.

Three great venerable ones... killed just like that.

It should be known that even if the three great venerable ones of the Ancient God Alliance had killing intent in their hearts, they didn't directly kill without breaking face.

Qi Yuan, on the other hand, didn't say much and killed directly!

He still had doubts in his heart - how did Venerable Twilight become a great venerable one, and even... at the fifth step of Treading the Heavens?

At this time, Qi Yuan looked at the many ancient gods with fearful faces and said softly: "Everyone, don't be afraid. I'm not a bloodthirsty person. They were people from the main hall of the Palace of Light, and had great enmity with me. When I was at Foundation Establishment, they bullied me in every way! But fortunately, one day I turned from slave to master and sang songs, wiping out the Palace of Light! Who knew they would set up their main hall here again, so I had no choice but to kill them ruthlessly. Don't worry, I'm a good person. I never implicate the innocent, nor do I wipe out entire clans."

Hearing this, everyone present laughed awkwardly.

Ye Liuyun very much wanted to say to Qi Yuan:

Brother Qi, do you want to bring a speech draft when you talk in the future? Do you see how your opening and closing statements match? You just said you wiped out the Palace of Light, then immediately said you don't wipe out entire clans?

Yuan Kun, the clan leader of the Ancient Ape clan, was sweating profusely: "My grandson offended the Origin Venerable One. The Venerable One killed well, killed wonderfully. I, Yuan Kun, apologize to the Origin Venerable One!"

Qi Yuan's gaze suddenly changed, and a bone-chilling chill swept over Yuan Kun.

He then heard: "The leader of the Ancient God Alliance was your grandson? Could it be... you're a hidden expert?"

"Cough cough!" Yuan Kun hurriedly explained.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan finally relaxed.

"So it's the Ancient Ape clan. You scared me. I was thinking today that you haven't come looking for me recently, wondering if you were biding your time to avenge that golden-haired monkey. I was even preparing to bring some great venerable ones and venerable ones to visit your Ancient Ape clan. Don't worry, I have no ill intentions. I was just planning to send a wreath for the golden-haired monkey, and have a meal while I'm at it." Qi Yuan's face was amiable, full of smiles.

The smile on Yuan Kun's face was uglier than crying, and his body couldn't help but tremble.

He didn't believe a word when the Origin Venerable One said he had no ill intentions.

Bringing great venerable ones and venerable ones to send a wreath? Have a meal?

More like wiping out the clan!

Yuan Kun felt grateful in his heart.

It's a good thing he came this time.

"Origin Venerable One, my entire Ancient Ape clan is willing to submit to you and become your subjects!" Yuan Kun gritted his teeth and said.

He was mainly afraid the Origin Venerable One would change his mind and wipe out his Ancient Ape clan.

After all, he killed three great venerable ones of the Ancient God Alliance just like that.

He was scared!

"Ah?" Qi Yuan was a bit surprised.

How come today someone was rushing to be his subjects? In the past, when he recruited true gods as his subjects, he had to beg and plead.

Now, an entire clan directly joining? Could it be that his luck was very good today?

Since that was the case, Qi Yuan blinked his eyes, with a smile in them, and looked at the other ancient gods: "What about you?"

He wanted to test if his luck was good.

Ye Liuyun was stunned for a moment, looking at Qi Yuan's bright and amiable smile. A dense chill arose in his heart.

He couldn't quite understand Qi Yuan.

Was this a fit of madness, or was he threatening them?

He recalled the Candle Dragon, Five-clawed Golden Dragon and others that Qi Yuan had mentioned before. He gritted his teeth and said: "My Well Dragon clan is willing to become subjects of the Venerable One."

Hearing this, Qi Yuan immediately smiled from ear to ear.

His luck today really was quite good.

"What about you all?"

Seeing this, the leaders of the other clans felt grief in their hearts, and also very wronged. Some had other thoughts when they saw Venerable Twilight.

"We are willing to become subjects of the Venerable One!"

The other ancient gods all swore allegiance one after another.

Qi Yuan was very pleased: "It seems my luck today really is quite good. It's a pity there's no lottery shop here, otherwise I'd definitely buy a few tickets."

The many ancient gods present looked at each other, not daring to speak.

"Little Seven," Qi Yuan called out at this time.

Qi Qi in her green dress appeared again. She glanced at the three corpses on the ground, then withdrew her gaze: "Godfather."

"Where are the basic information registration forms? These people say they want to become my subjects. Register their information."

A look of surprise flashed across Qi Qi's face: "Alright."

Immediately, a stack of basic information forms was handed out.

At this time, Qi Yuan added: "Remember to register the true gods in your clans who didn't come today as well."

Hearing this, Yuan Kun hurriedly nodded: "Understood!"

Faced with Qi Yuan, all the ancient gods present were extremely fearful.

This was a god of slaughter.

Killing three great venerable ones at the drop of a hat left a deep impression on them.

After the forms were filled out, they were handed to Qi Yuan.

At this time, an old man came forward and cautiously asked: "Venerable One, now that war is about to break out in the Azure Eternal Continent, where should we go?"
