"Just modifying Zero One is certainly doable.

The house Kuang Huan gave him was full of useful plants and sea monsters.

Currently, Qi Yuan's cultivation level was too low to create storage bags.

"Come in."

Last night, he had even managed to produce a tiny flame from his fingertip.

Although this flame wasn't even as big as that from a lighter, and could be extinguished by a single breath,

it was still the first time he had personally cultivated it.

Even if others asked, he would say he hadn't succeeded.

"Are these the secret materials?"

Qi Yuan opened the box, which contained rows of glass containers.

Inside the glass containers were meat products that looked similar to wind-dried beef jerky.

"Is this... sea monster meat?"

Qi Yuan immediately recognized the main component of the secret materials - it was indeed sea monster meat.

No wonder martial artists' cultivation was so expensive.

Xing Yu spent hundreds of thousands on secret materials every month.

If it was sea monster meat, it was indeed worth the price.

"Mr. Qi has a good eye. I've also investigated some information about secret materials these past few days.

The main component of secret materials is indeed sea monster meat."

Kuang Huan had a basic understanding of martial arts before, but not much.

He only investigated secret materials after Qi Yuan's instructions.

The investigation was eye-opening.

It turned out that the secret materials used in the martial arts world were actually prohibited sea monsters.

This also resolved many of Kuang Huan's doubts.

In the Qin Yuan Kingdom, ordinary martial artists either became internet celebrities or entered the system, with most in the system joining the navy.

Even if the authorities knew about using prohibited sea monsters as secret materials, they wouldn't say anything.

Generally, those who chose to practice martial arts came from families with some money.

Using martial arts to stimulate their consumption and circulate wealth was considered a good thing.

Perhaps the largest producer of these prohibited secret materials was some big shot up there.

After all, how much money could be made from the secret material business across the entire Qin Yuan Kingdom? It was an astronomical figure.

"So martial arts... have a deep connection with sea monsters."

Qi Yuan picked up a piece of dried sea monster and put it in his mouth, chewing slowly.

The taste was somewhat like dried fruit meat, salty with a hint of sweetness.

Clearly, the secret material wasn't simply sea monster meat, but had many additives.

"As expected... it's the blood?"

After consuming the secret material, Qi Yuan felt the greatest improvement was a slight warming of his blood.

Of course, martial artists of this world wouldn't be able to detect this level of warming.

Qi Yuan gained a deeper understanding of this world's martial arts.

"However... everyone's bloodline origin is different. Ordinary secret materials... supplement with all sorts of mixed blood... This can indeed improve martial arts cultivation, but it also... clogs one's own system."

Yang Shan had mentioned that in ancient times, besides innate martial techniques, powerful martial artists also had innate killing moves.

But ordinary people consuming all sorts of messy secret materials led to various bloodlines mixing in their bodies, making their own bloodline impure and unable to form their own innate killing move.

With just this simple reasoning, Qi Yuan had deduced seven or eight tenths of this world's martial arts path below the level of innate killing moves.

Beside him, Kuang Huan listened attentively but didn't understand a thing.

"The taste is not bad, quite nutritious."

Although the bloodlines were mixed, Qi Yuan still ate the secret materials.

Martial arts cultivation was inseparable from secret materials.

Without strengthening the bloodline, cultivation would be extremely difficult, and it would be hard to make progress with techniques.

To avoid mixing bloodlines, one would have to devour mutated sea monsters like last time, and they had to be mutated sea monsters of the same bloodline origin.

Unfortunately, such sea monsters were hard to come by.

So Qi Yuan thought he would consider purifying his bloodline when he reached the limit of martial arts cultivation.
