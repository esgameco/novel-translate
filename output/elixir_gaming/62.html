The young girl finished her forceful words and turned to leave. She didn't give Qi Yuan any time to react.

Qi Yuan narrowed his eyes and watched the girl's retreating back. This was the first time he had seen an NPC in this world display such intense emotions.

Tong Yan's expression, on the other hand, was without sorrow or joy. He already knew that the Five Patriarchs of Gold, Wood, Water, Fire, and Earth were merely remnants of obsession. The five of them had long since perished at the hands of demonic forces from beyond the domain.

The usually rational Tong Yan did not have the melancholy sentimentality of Little Green.

At that moment, Qi Yuan felt a burning sensation. A ball of fiery red flames flew towards him, burning continuously just dozens of centimeters away.

"Ahhh!" With a loud cry, the Fire Patriarch materialized in human form beside Qi Yuan, looking at him with malicious intent. "You may look good, but you don't seem to have anything special. What makes you the complete embodiment of the Five Elements?"

Tong Yan quickly introduced, "This is the Fire Patriarch, who has a rather volatile temper."

"Greetings, Fire Patriarch," Qi Yuan responded respectfully.

Hearing Tong Yan's words, the Fire Patriarch opened his mouth and spewed a ball of flames that ignited Tong Yan's clothes.

Tong Yan chuckled gracefully, "Fire Patriarch, you're being mischievous again."

"Ahhh!" The Fire Patriarch cried out in anger.

At that moment, Qi Yuan felt the presence of the other energies approaching.

The soil on the ground began to flow, as if a mole was moving within. Then, a small girl with a round belly, resembling a roly-poly, materialized.

The roly-poly girl did not speak as she looked at Qi Yuan.

"This is the Earth Patriarch," Tong Yan introduced.

"Greetings, Earth Patriarch," Qi Yuan greeted with a bow.

"Young man, I just took a little nap, and you've already become the complete embodiment of the Five Elements. I thought I had slept for thousands of years, but when I asked, it's only been three days. Truly, it's beyond my expectations," a voice came from the distance as the Gold Patriarch approached.

He was still dressed as meticulously as the first time Qi Yuan had met him, formal and upright.

Beside the Gold Patriarch stood two others - the Water Patriarch, a woman in a blue dress with a mature beauty, and the Wood Patriarch, dressed in vibrant colors, barefoot, and with a cheerful, laughing expression.

"Greetings to the three of you," Qi Yuan greeted them solemnly.

Seeing this, Tong Yan and the other prodigies quietly left the area.

Qi Yuan held Little Bride's hand and faced the Five Patriarchs.

The Gold Patriarch stepped forward, his expression kind and benevolent. "On that day, I made a pact with the Master of the Clothing Faction. Now, I have come to fulfill that promise."

He did not beat around the bush but went straight to the point.

The volatile Fire Patriarch wanted to say something, but the roly-poly Earth Patriarch kicked him in the butt, silencing him. The Fire Patriarch remained silent, though he still looked dissatisfied with Qi Yuan.

Then, the dignified and graceful Water Patriarch spoke, "Master of the Clothing Faction, can you guarantee that you will participate in the Demon Slaying Assembly three hundred years from now and slay the demonic forces from beyond the domain?"

Qi Yuan controlled the Five Elements Forbidden Land. If he did so, the Patriarchs would dissipate in this world, and their remaining obsessions would cease to exist.

"I will slay the demonic forces from beyond the domain!" Qi Yuan did not mention the Demon Slaying Assembly but directly targeted the demonic forces.

He must kill the demonic forces from beyond the domain. They were the final boss of this game, and once Qi Yuan defeated them, he would obtain the complete fragments of the Heavenly Dao and be able to establish the foundation of the Heavenly Dao.

"Good!" The Water Patriarch nodded in satisfaction upon hearing the answer.

The Gold Patriarch, Wood Patriarch, and Earth Patriarch also nodded in agreement.

Only the Fire Patriarch looked unwilling, but after being glared at by the roly-poly Earth Patriarch, he reluctantly nodded as well.

Qi Yuan then looked at the Gold Patriarch and slowly asked, "How strong are the demonic forces from beyond the domain, based on your experiences?"

He was genuinely curious about the combat power of the demonic forces.

The Gold Patriarch fell silent, then said, "The demonic forces from beyond the domain possess a supreme treasure called the Flesh Hook. It is like a fishing line that descends from the heavens and pierces into the bodies of cultivators. When my predecessor was pierced by the Flesh Hook, even with all his might, he could not sever it, nor leave even a trace behind."

Hearing this, Qi Yuan felt a sense of trepidation.

The Gold Patriarch had been a Godly Realm expert in his lifetime, yet he was unable to cut the Flesh Hook or even leave a mark on it.

If Qi Yuan encountered this Flesh Hook, it would likely be an immense challenge.

"In the Demon Slaying Assembly, if you battle the demonic forces, you must stay away from the Flesh Hook," the Wood Patriarch reminded. "Once it pierces into your body, even Celestial Deities cannot escape unharmed."

The volatile Fire Patriarch unexpectedly spoke up as well, "The demonic forces from beyond the domain may not be a single entity, but two, or even more!"

Qi Yuan took in all this information, his eyes flashing with a puzzled expression.

"Just how strong are the demonic forces from beyond the domain? The Five Elements Forbidden Land has around fifty powerful experts, and the Yin-Yang Forbidden Land has dozens as well. If we all work together and attack them, won't we be able to easily cut them down with our combined strength?"

Qi Yuan felt that the power of the Four Great Forbidden Lands was formidable. With these ninety-level experts working together, he would likely be no match for them.

"The demonic forces from beyond the domain have immortal bodies and cannot be killed! Or even if they are killed, they will revive!" the vibrant and colorful Wood Patriarch said.

"Immortal bodies?" Qi Yuan was somewhat surprised, but it was just surprise, nothing more.

How could there be a boss in the game that couldn't be killed? At most, he would just have to kill them a few more times.

However, Qi Yuan's assessment of the demonic forces from beyond the domain had risen further.

Without reaching the Hundred Level, going to confront the demonic forces would be seeking death.

The Gold Patriarch, seeing that Qi Yuan had learned what he wanted to know, smiled benevolently. "Old friends, let us fulfill our promise and empower the Master of the Clothing Faction."

"Okay."

"Okay."

"Okay."

"Damn it, fine!"

As the words fell, the five of them placed their arms on Qi Yuan's shoulders.

The powers of Gold, Wood, Water, Fire, and Earth surged into Qi Yuan's body, more vibrant than the Five Elemental Essences.

They spread through Qi Yuan's body, making his blood seem to change color. Markings of the five colors appeared on his skin, like tattoos.

Qi Yuan watched as the Five Patriarchs' bodies gradually faded away. Except for the reluctant Fire Patriarch, the others seemed calm, even with a sense of liberation.

The four Patriarchs did not speak, but Qi Yuan could read their expressions, seeing them slowly dissipate.

In the final moments, the Fire Patriarch suddenly let out a loud roar, "Kid, I hate you! It's because of you that I'm reminded of the fact that I'm already dead!"

With this final shout, as if using up all his strength, the Fire Patriarch's form disappeared from this world.

The other four Patriarchs also vanished from Qi Yuan's sight, as if they had never existed.

Qi Yuan was momentarily stunned.

They're gone.

He looked down at the Five Element tattoos on his body, which would likely take a day to fully settle and integrate.

He tightened his grip on Little Bride's hand a bit more. "Log out, I'll come back tomorrow."

He felt that today's expenditure had been somewhat excessive.

He took Little Bride to an empty area and dug a hole, as he usually did, and they crawled into it together.

He embraced Little Bride's waist and opened his contact list.

Lonely, with only Jinli.

"It's over."
