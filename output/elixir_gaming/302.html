Xie Xinsu, listening to Zero One's voice nearby, felt indignant.

No cat toys, no cat tree.

What era was this, still thinking about practicing martial arts.

With Gong Xing about to revive spiritual energy, only those who cultivate immortality like her had a future.

Only by cultivating immortality was there hope of attaining the great Dao of longevity.

Of course, now that she was a cat, even if she had these thoughts, she couldn't express them.

Even if she could express them, why would she introduce the immortal way to this cold, heartless, boorish man who didn't know how to appreciate it? Most importantly, if she could demonstrate other talents, no doubt she would be tormented by this damned robot to film videos.

When would this kind of life end?

"Martial arts ruins?" Qi Yuan was slightly stunned.

He hadn't obtained such information from Yang Shan.

But hearing that this news came from Qin Yuan Country's secret database, Qi Yuan understood.

Although Yang Shan was at the limit of martial arts, his understanding of martial arts might not be as deep as that of some professors.

In a society like Qin Yuan Country, all kinds of powerful people would certainly be thinking about longevity.

There must be frighteningly many experiments related to longevity.

It was normal for the powerful to focus their attention on martial arts.

If martial arts ruins truly held hope for breaking through the limits of martial arts, he would have to go take a look after the martial arts competition ended.

Now, there were only three days left until the start of the martial arts competition.

He didn't have time to go to martial arts ruins.

"It seems your hacking skills are quite impressive, to be able to steal such hidden information," Qi Yuan couldn't help but praise.

Zero One was his own creation, and the stronger its abilities, the more gratified Qi Yuan felt.

"Not just that, almost any information online, if I want it, I can obtain it.

While searching online, I also saw many interesting things. I've organized them, and if the master wants to see, I can send them to you," Zero One said proudly.

Now it was becoming more and more human-like.

"What kind of interesting things?" Qi Yuan inquired.

"It's..." Zero One lowered its voice and said.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan's expression became strange: "Spreading pornographic and obscene information is illegal behavior.

Moreover... you're not my friend.

Only sharing between friends is not illegal."

Qi Yuan did not accept this file package.

It was just some electronic data after all.

Even ordinary humans, aren't they just made up of various elements like carbon, hydrogen, oxygen, etc?

The next day.

Qi Yuan went to the martial arts gym again.

This time, he had just started practicing martial arts for a short while when Xu Tiehua came running over to find him.

"Junior Brother Qi Yuan, there are only three days left until the martial arts competition. For the team competition, do you want to discuss it a bit?"

Xu Tiehua was very much looking forward to the team competition.

Now that he had money and power, he wanted to become famous.

Sometimes, fame is also very useful, and some titles and gimmicks can play a huge role.

"No rush, we'll meet the night before the competition," Qi Yuan said casually.

This response confused Xu Tiehua, but he had good manners and slowly said, "Won't that be too late? The rules for the team competition are somewhat complicated.

If we make a strategy first, we might be able to achieve better results."

"Aren't the rules of martial arts just about who has the harder fist?" Qi Yuan asked doubtfully, "When the time comes, I'll just knock down all the opponents, won't that solve the problem?"

This statement choked Xu Tiehua again.

What Qi Yuan said wasn't wrong.

But this was based on having strong abilities.

The four participating members from Ming Wu Martial Arts Gym were not very strong.

Especially Qi Yuan, who probably hadn't even obtained a martial artist certificate yet. How could he have the face to say such things?

But thinking that Qi Yuan did indeed have a certificate, Xu Tiehua could only helplessly say, "What you say makes sense, but... our abilities are not enough, we need to rely on strategy."

"No wonder your abilities are not enough, chatting idly all day. If you practiced martial arts as diligently as I do, would you still worry about lacking ability?" Qi Yuan said casually.

His body was still practicing punches.

Although his progress had slowed after reaching the limit of martial arts,

he still persevered in practicing martial arts.

Qi Yuan's words made Xu Tiehua blush a little.

He hadn't expected Qi Yuan to be a true martial arts fanatic.

While he had always treated martial arts as a calling card, hearing Qi Yuan's words made him feel somewhat ashamed.

"You're right, practicing martial arts should be done properly," Xu Tiehua decided not to disturb Qi Yuan further.

"Martial arts... is very interesting," Qi Yuan said softly, looking at Xu Tiehua.

Over these nearly three months, practicing martial arts daily, his understanding of martial arts had grown deeper and deeper.

He knew that on the path of martial arts, he had not yet even entered the door.

In his view, the potential of martial arts in this world was no weaker than the immortal ways of other worlds.

"Martial arts is interesting?" Hearing Qi Yuan's words, Xu Tiehua was slightly stunned in his heart, seeming somewhat puzzled.

However, he didn't ask anything further and left directly.

Not far away, Wang Shiqiao saw this scene and couldn't help but give Qi Yuan a thumbs up: "You're still fierce, daring to talk to Brother Xu like that."

Although Xu Tiehua was approachable in the gym,

these students still felt full of respect and awe when facing Xu Tiehua.

They couldn't imagine directly confronting Xu Tiehua like Qi Yuan did.

"I was just telling the truth," Qi Yuan glanced at Wang Shiqiao, "But I am indeed braver than you."

Hearing this, the smile on Wang Shiqiao's face froze.

"Your mouth should really be sewn shut with needle and thread," Wang Shiqiao said angrily.

"Why don't you say that to that Gui Ran person?" Qi Yuan shrugged.

Wang Shiqiao's courage was still as small as ever.

Hearing Qi Yuan's words, many of the students present laughed.

Ever since Wang Shiqiao had a verbal fight with a military officer from Wu Gui Country three months ago,

For the past three months, that officer had been sending private messages cursing at Wang Shiqiao every day.

Wang Shiqiao couldn't help but curse back every day.

They had been insulting each other for a full three months.

Of course, only online.
