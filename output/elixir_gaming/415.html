This guy was just like Qin Wuyang.

A real madman! No matter what, daring to make a move against a Yangsheng of the Moluo clan - they were both dead men today!

Mochi Tianzun took a deep breath.

"Mere ancient arrays are not worth mentioning."

This time he had expended quite a bit of energy.

"I never expected that I would be so fortunate as to encounter purple qi. I just wonder whose cave dwelling this used to be."

He scanned around but found nothing unusual.

Evidently, the master of this ancient array had long since left or perished, leaving behind only a wisp of purple qi.

Looking at this wisp of purple qi, he was excited, feeling that destiny was on his side.

However, even so, he remained cautious, scanning the surroundings to avoid any other killing arrays.

At this moment, suddenly, the purple qi in heaven and earth began to fluctuate.

Mochi Tianzun's expression remained unchanged: "Where do you think you're going!"

Though the purple qi had no consciousness, it would instinctively try to escape.

So he quickly gave chase.

After all, with his Yangsheng strength, this purple qi had no hope of escaping his grasp.

But after just a few breaths of time.

Suddenly, his expression froze, his mind violently shaken.

Before him appeared an ancient, profound door radiating the light of wisdom.

When he saw that door, even Mochi Tianzun, as a Yangsheng, couldn't help but tremble, his expression becoming frenzied.

"The... the Door of Ultimate Principles!"

His breathing quickened.

This door was none other than the number one miraculous treasure of the immortal realm - the Door of Ultimate Principles! Legend had it that all the ultimate principles comprehended by the Ultimate Principle realm experts of the Six Heavens were contained within! It was also said that whoever obtained the Door of Ultimate Principles and entered to contemplate the ultimate principles within...

As long as they had enough time, they would certainly attain the Great Ultimate Principle realm.

It was even said that one could comprehend the ultimate principles of other Yangsheng in the Great Ultimate Principle realm.

In that case, he, Mochi Tianzun... would become number one in the immortal realm!

"Hahaha, destiny is on my side!"

At this moment, all of Mochi Tianzun's rationality vanished, replaced by utter frenzy.

The Door of Ultimate Principles before him was his sole target.

Although even Great Ultimate Principle experts had never captured the Door of Ultimate Principles, what if... what if he succeeded?

Just then, suddenly, the Door of Ultimate Principles opened.

Seeing this, Mochi Tianzun was stunned.

Because this was something he had never heard of, not even in rumors.

Even if a Great Ultimate Principle realm expert attacked the Door of Ultimate Principles, they could not open it.

Let alone enter the Door of Ultimate Principles.

Forget about controlling the Door of Ultimate Principles, just entering it... would be the greatest opportunity in the immortal realm.

Before he could think further, that wisp of purple qi suddenly contracted and flew into the Door of Ultimate Principles.

The space around the Door of Ultimate Principles also rippled, as if about to leave.

Seeing this, Mochi Tianzun knew he could hesitate no longer, or this heaven-sent opportunity would be lost.

"We cultivators must forge ahead bravely and dare to fight!"

"Destiny is on my side!"

Without any hesitation, Mochi Tianzun transformed into a streak of light and plunged into the Door of Ultimate Principles.

Awaiting him were infinite ultimate principles!

He would become number one in the immortal realm!

On the other side, seeing this, Qi Yuan's eyes gleamed with a smile: "Fellow Daoist Ji, the fish has taken the bait. Let's go close the door and beat the dog!"

As he spoke, he grabbed Ji Buping by the neck and entered his own Purple Palace.

At this moment, inside the Purple Palace, Mochi Tianzun's expression suddenly changed.

"Death energy?"

"Yin energy?"

"Isn't this supposed to be the Door of Ultimate Principles?"

He was confused and extremely wary.

He had thought that upon entering the Door of Ultimate Principles, he would be greeted by infinite ultimate principles.

But... there wasn't a single ultimate principle to be found.

This place was extremely gloomy and permeated with death energy.

It didn't resemble the interior of the Door of Ultimate Principles at all!

But he had clearly entered the Door of Ultimate Principles.

At this moment, his expression suddenly changed again.

"There are no stars reflecting all of creation, only ten suns?"

At this moment, he caught the scent of danger.
