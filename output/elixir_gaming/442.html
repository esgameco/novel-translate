[The Enlightenment Blood Pill, when taken, can temporarily stimulate enlightenment and enhance bloodline. There are several loopholes, if modified... the effect can be increased threefold.]

For Yang Spirit Celestial Venerables, combat-type magical treasures provided an extremely terrifying boost to combat power.

This made all the Yang Spirits present feel alarmed.

Their strength was formidable, but when facing a Great Ultimate Principle expert, it was not enough to escape.

Could this be Black Magic Abyss's declaration of war against Ghost Dominion Heaven?

Without the Great Ultimate Principle experts emerging, the Three-Faced Celestial Venerable was the actual controller of Ghost Dominion Heaven.

They all wanted to know how the Three-Faced Celestial Venerable viewed this matter.

"The Banner Master says that after the Ultimate Dao Conference ends, we will go to war with several factions of Black Magic Abyss!" the Three-Faced Celestial Venerable spoke frenziedly.

Hearing this, all the Yang Spirit Celestial Venerables present had their expressions change.

Because this news was too unexpected for them.

How did the Banner Master suddenly become so brave?

It was well known that Black Magic Abyss's hard power definitely surpassed Ghost Dominion Heaven's.

Going to war over the death of one Yang Spirit Celestial Venerable seemed too childish.

After all, friction between such major powers was common, but actual declarations of war were rare.

The Yang Spirit Celestial Venerables present were not fools. They instantly sensed that the waters behind this ran deep, and it certainly wasn't so simple.

"As for the reason..." The Three-Faced Celestial Venerable narrowed his eyes, "You'll know after the Ultimate Dao Conference ends."

He looked at these Yang Spirits with pity and regret in his eyes.

Cultivated to the Yang Spirit Celestial Venerable realm, with lifespans as long as heaven and earth, bodies unharmed even as stars fell.

Without powerful enemies, not attempting breakthroughs and encountering the Root Demon Tribulation, they were almost worry-free, with no need to fear perishing.

Unfortunately... they had encountered Lin Ya Tui.

Hearing the Banner Master mention Lin Ya Tui, even the Three-Faced Celestial Venerable was shocked.

Who would have thought that beings as powerful as Yang Spirits... able to destroy the Heavenly Dao with a wave of the hand, able to devour worlds, were in some sense merely pawns.

"This..."

"What exactly is going on?"

The Yang Spirit Celestial Venerables present looked at each other in confusion.

Regarding Lin Ya Tui, they did not know.

Generally, only Great Ultimate Principle experts and some special creatures knew of this matter.

"Going to war with Black Magic Abyss, the battle lines may span ten thousand years, a hundred thousand years.

Recently, Black Magic Abyss has produced several good seedlings, one Black Sword, one Blood Robe."

Mentioning Black Sword, even the Three-Faced Celestial Venerable's expression became solemn.

That one was even singled out by the Banner Master, who wished to kill him as soon as possible.

His talent... was unparalleled, but acceptable, as the Sixth Heaven did not lack for geniuses.

But... his bloodline was very frightening.

Especially Black Magic Abyss's top-tier bloodline, combined with terrifying talent, would be a deadly combination.

The Banner Master even speculated that individual had a chance to become a Great Ultimate Principle expert who grasped the Supreme Ultimate Principle.

This made the Three-Faced Celestial Venerable even more envious and jealous.

Great Ultimate Principle experts who grasped the Supreme Ultimate Principle numbered less than two handfuls in the entire Sixth Heaven.

Their Ghost Dominion Heaven had many Great Ultimate Principle experts, but not one who grasped the Supreme Ultimate Principle.

"When the Ultimate Dao Conference begins, Fog Lord, you will be responsible for killing Black Sword!"

The Three-Faced Celestial Venerable said calmly, killing intent in his eyes.

Hearing this, the Yang Spirit Celestial Venerables present were somewhat surprised.

Because Fog Lord Celestial Venerable was a perfected Great Celestial Position Yang Spirit, only one step away from the Ultimate Principle realm.

This time, he was going to participate in the Ultimate Dao Conference, with a very high probability of entering the Ultimate Principle realm.

How many years had it been since Black Sword entered the Yang Spirit realm?

Was it even 3000 years? In the Yang Spirit realm, he was only considered a newcomer. Sending a perfected Great Celestial Position expert showed how highly they regarded him.

"Alright." Fog Lord Celestial Venerable's expression was calm, his face covered by a layer of mist, making his features unclear.

"As for Blood Robe..." The Three-Faced Celestial Venerable looked at everyone, his expression relaxed, "Who wants this small merit?"

Killing Black Sword would definitely be a great merit for Ghost Dominion Heaven, even the Banner Master would reward it.

As for Blood Robe, it was just a small merit.

After all, Blood Robe was powerful, but in the eyes of these Yang Spirits, anyone not in the Yang Spirit realm was nothing special.

"This old one will personally handle it," Great Joy Celestial Venerable stepped forward, smiling, "Even a mosquito's meat is still meat."

"Very well," the Three-Faced Celestial Venerable nodded.

Originally he had thought to send a few Mythical realm experts to kill Blood Robe, but that wasn't very reliable.

Penglai Celestial Venerable was a newly promoted Great Principle expert of the Black Demon Abyss.

Qi Yuan listened carefully, as this was very important.

"However, if I were a Great Principle expert with an offensive Primordial Treasure, I could injure him," Purple Origin Ancestor said, his expression complex. "This is the second tier of Great Principle experts. Offensive Primordial Treasures are too rare, extremely uncommon."

Offensive Primordial Treasures were mostly improved from ordinary Primordial Treasures by Great Principle experts who once grasped Supreme Principles, through endless years under great opportunities. They were extremely rare.

In the current Black Demon Abyss, only two Great Principle experts possessed offensive Primordial Treasures.
