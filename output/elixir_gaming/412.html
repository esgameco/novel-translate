Compared to the Floating Sky City, the Demon Lord's Sacred Tower was like the countryside versus the city.

The spiritual energy was also much more abundant.

Ordinary people living in the Demon Lord's Sacred Tower could increase their lifespan by nearly double.

During this period, Qi Yuan used celestial spiritual energy to cleanse his body, making his skin increasingly snow-white and crystal clear.

After all, her skin was indeed not as good as Qi Yuan's now.

"Now that you're here, focus on cultivating well," Qi Yuan said, patting Jiang Lingsu's hair.

The group of three also arrived at one of the floors of the Demon Lord's Sacred Tower, called the Domain Gathering Heaven.

This floor was the most prosperous in the Demon Lord's Sacred Tower, and also had the most visiting cultivators.

Many holy lands from other heavenly realms, or from the Fifth Heavenly Realm, had outposts on this floor.

The area where the Demon Lord clan resided produced Milo Light, which was very valuable and could be used to expand divine domains. For Yang Spirit Heavenly Venerable, it also had considerable uses, seemingly related to illuminating the myriad realms.

Therefore, those major powers all established outposts in the Demon Lord clan's territory and arranged people to collect Milo Light.

Now, walking in the Domain Gathering Heaven, streams of light flashed by from time to time, emanating powerful auras.

In the giant city, Qi Yuan and his two companions traversed through, their expressions calm.

This giant city was the central city of the Domain Gathering Heaven, with outposts of major holy lands from various heavenly realms within.

"Futu Mountain is so low-class, they don't even have an outpost here," Qi Yuan sighed.

"Ahem, Futu Mountain is only a holy land of the Second Heavenly Realm, so it's... normal that they don't have an outpost," God of No God laughed awkwardly.

Futu Mountain only had one Yang Spirit, and only at the Heavenly Rank, so how could they extend their reach to the Demon Lord clan?

"It seems I need to choose a stronger power to join this time," Qi Yuan pondered.

Wandering aimlessly in the Demon Lord's Sacred Tower was ultimately not good, as it was easy to attract attention.

Especially since he would be doing some big things later, which would definitely draw the notice of some Yang Spirits.

Casually joining a power as an honorary disciple not only allowed him to freeload on lodging and food, but perhaps his efforts and talent would catch the eye of some great principle sugar mommy, which would be fantastic.

Additionally, having a power would be good for settling his junior sister and God of No God.

At this moment, Qi Yuan stood on a tower, gazing at a wall in the distance.

The wall was covered with many "recruitment advertisements", with many cultivators viewing them below.

"Xuanyun Mountain is recruiting three Yin Spirit honorary disciples, Great Venerable preferred, salary negotiable.

"Jade Immortal Gate recruiting Purple Palace attendants..."

"Tai Huang Palace recruiting attendants, cultivation requirement: Mythical realm..."

"Black Magic Abyss..."

Qi Yuan's eyes fell on Tai Huang Palace's "recruitment ad", but then he thought it was better to stay away from that one for now.

"In this Demon Lord's Sacred Tower, there are outposts for four holy lands from the Sixth Heavenly Realm.

The rest are mostly holy lands from the Fifth Heavenly Realm.

As for the Fourth Heavenly Realm and below, there are even fewer," God of No God was still trying to prove his relevance.

After all, he had been looked down upon by Qi Yuan earlier for not seeming like a native of the immortal realm, so now he had to show his familiarity.

He did know about those famous major powers of the Sixth Heavenly Realm.

"What do you think of the Black Magic Abyss?" Qi Yuan asked casually.

God of No God's expression became solemn, then he said: "Each top holy land in the Sixth Heavenly Realm has its own unique aspects.

The unique aspect of the Black Magic Abyss is that they have many Yang Spirit Heavenly Venerables at the Great Principle realm.

As for exactly how many, this junior doesn't know.

Because of this, the factions within the Black Magic Abyss are complex and intertwined, with many factions even hostile to each other.

But even so, the Black Magic Abyss is definitely among the top ten major powers in the Sixth Heavenly Realm."

"So many ideal women?" Qi Yuan's eyes lit up, but then he calmed down again.

He hadn't even met them yet, so it wasn't good to think of them as ideal women. That would make his ideal women seem cheap.

Just then, a cultured-looking cultivator approached from the side, his eyes seemingly carrying a hint of arrogance: "Fellow Daoist, you must be from a lower heavenly realm. Wanting to become an honorary disciple of the Black Magic Abyss is not so simple."

This cultivator was named Ji Buping, originally from the Fifth Heavenly Realm. He had a sense of superiority towards cultivators from lower realms.

So when he spoke to Qi Yuan, he deliberately emphasized the words "lower heavenly realm".

"Fellow Daoist has good eyesight. I'm from the Second Heavenly Realm and accidentally entered a secret realm, ending up here without even enough celestial jade to get home. I have no choice but to find some work to earn travel expenses to return home," Qi Yuan replied. "May I ask what's not simple about becoming an honorary disciple of the Black Magic Abyss?"

This was the persona he had established for himself - an elder from Futu Mountain.

"The Black Magic Abyss is an extremely exclusive holy land that values bloodline above all," Ji Buping said, flipping his hair to casually reveal a black mark by his ear. "See this? My bloodline would be good enough to be an... steward even in the Sixth Heavenly Realm's Black Magic Abyss. Too bad I don't have a Sky-Reaching Jade, so I can't reach the Sixth Heavenly Realm."

Ji Buping greatly yearned for the Sixth Heavenly Realm's Black Magic Abyss and wanted to go there.

In his view, with his bloodline level, he would definitely live the good life if he went to the Black Magic Abyss.

"I don't have a Sky-Reaching Jade either," Qi Yuan sighed.

Then he heard Ji Buping continue proudly: "The Black Magic Abyss bloodline is one of the most precious bloodlines in the Sixth Heavenly Realm.

Their bloodlines are further divided into grades one through nine, with grade one being the highest and grade nine the lowest.

I humbly possess a grade four bloodline, just short of being a high-grade bloodline. Too bad my mother's bloodline was a bit lacking."

Ji Buping said with a sigh, looking at Qi Yuan: "You don't have the Black Magic Abyss bloodline, so you can't become an honorary disciple of the Black Magic Abyss."

Though he sighed, there was still a hint of superiority in his tone.

However, just then, a playful voice suddenly rang out: "Who says he can't join the Black Magic Abyss?"

A woman in a purple robe appeared, with a graceful figure. But what attracted the most attention were the two horns on her head, resembling the Ox Demon King's bull horns, looking indestructible.

She looked at Qi Yuan, her eyes flashing with sharpness.

Ji Buping saw the newcomer with a shocked expression. He couldn't see through her identity or strength and was extremely wary.

"Handsome fellow Daoist, care to exchange contact jade slips?" the purple-robed woman spoke, her voice quite flirtatious.

Seeing this, Qi Yuan was slightly stunned.

Was he being asked for his "WeChat"?

Indeed, being handsome was troublesome.

"I rarely give out my contact information on first meetings," Qi Yuan said very reservedly and traditionally conservative.

"Give it to me, I'll help you become an honorary disciple of the Black Magic Abyss!" The purple-robed woman's eyes held a smile.

Qi Yuan's nose didn't grow longer, indicating the purple-robed woman was telling the truth.

"Horn Sister, here's my contact info. You can save me as... Blood Robe!" Qi Yuan changed his tune very quickly.

Ji Buping saw this and his expression changed repeatedly. He somewhat disbelieved the purple-robed woman's words.

After all, the Black Magic Abyss valued bloodline above all. Even for an outpost recruiting honorary disciples to collect Milo Light, bloodline was required.
