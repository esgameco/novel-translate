The carriage traveled along the wide street.

The young man had a gentle smile on his face.

"Of the thirteen states under heaven, Dingbo State has the most upright administration, and the people live in peace and prosperity."

Chichangle was slender, with sunken eyes, and his long robe hung on him like a clothes rack.

Qiyuan looked out the window, and although the various peddlers on the streets were in coarse linen clothes, there were few who looked sallow and emaciated.

If he were to take off his clothes, judging by his physique alone, Chichangle would appear to be the poorest.

"It is indeed much better than other places," Qiyuan commented.

Whether it was Ninghe County, Fengshan Prefecture, or Yeshan Lake, they did not seem as good as this prefecture.

"Dingbo State, also known as Liuzhou," Chichangle said meaningfully.

This "Liu" was naturally the Liu surname.

"Originally, the many policies left behind by the Adjudicating Deity were all overturned by the imperial court, only Liuzhou retained some of them," Chichangle sighed, "But ever since the Prince of Zhenyuan took charge of Dingbo State, I don't know how long this kind of life will last."

He spoke with a worried expression in his eyes.

"Is the Prince of Zhenyuan very powerful?" Qiyuan asked.

Chichangle paused, not expecting someone to not know about the Prince of Zhenyuan.

"The Seven Deities of the world, the Prince of Zhenyuan is one of them," Chichangle lowered his voice, "And Lord Liu is also one of the Seven Deities."

Lord Liu, the strongest person in Dingbo State, the only divine-level military commander.

"It seems the Prince of Zhenyuan has trained hard," Qiyuan gave his assessment.

Chichangle smiled: "You should mention the Prince of Zhenyuan's name less in the future, in this Dingbo State, there are spies of the Prince of Zhenyuan everywhere, they are also called the Bloodhands, wherever they appear, there is bloodshed!"

"What is their strength?" Qiyuan asked.

"The four Supervisors are all peak divine-level military commanders, and the Captains under them are all divine-level..."

"It seems they are not trying hard enough."

"Brother Qi, be careful with your words, if you offend the Bloodhands, even the government cannot save you!" Chichangle said urgently.

"What's there to be afraid of? We are already walking fifty thousand strong, and still afraid of offending the Bloodhands?" Qiyuan felt Chichangle lacked self-awareness.

They had already joined the Adjudication, and were directly confronting the court, and still afraid of this?

Chichangle paused, feeling that Qiyuan had a point.

After joining the Adjudication, they could be directly executed if discovered by the court officials, and still afraid of offending the Bloodhands, the lackeys of the Prince of Zhenyuan?

"You're right," Chichangle nodded seriously.

The carriage weaved through the streets, moving at a slow pace.

Peddlers' cries rang out continuously.

"Ice sugar hawthorn..."

"Steamed buns, steamed buns..."

"Golden plums... fresh golden plums..."

Qiyuan's gaze lingered, looking at the overly vibrant fruit.

"What is this?"

Chichangle's eyes lit up with a smile: "This is the Hare Fruit, you're from out of town, so you must be seeing it for the first time!"

"Why is it called Hare Fruit?"

"I don't know, it's just called Hare Fruit."

Qiyuan's gaze was deep.

Did Hare really buy the Hare Fruit? Indeed, this world was deeply influenced by him.

Even the Hare Fruit existed.

He had once told the Voiceless Voice the story of the Hare Fruit.

After settling down at the Adjudication's base, Qiyuan's life entered a period of calm.

Though calm, there was much to do.

On one hand, he had to strive to improve the realm of the Artifact Master, striving to reach the divine-level Artifact Master realm as soon as possible.

But it was too difficult, even with the innate talent of the Blind Artifact Master, his progress was still very slow.

On the other hand, he was also preparing to play the role of the Deity Descendent, slowly seeking out the believers of the Deity Descendent.

The last aspect was that he was waiting for news from the Adjudication, seeking out the Devilish Spirits and evildoers, and then upholding justice.

"Brother Qi, there's a job!"

At this moment, a rough voice came from outside.

A gleam of joy flashed in Qiyuan's eyes.

Finally, there was something to do.

"Wang An, just come in, the door is not locked."

Outside the room, a short and thin man with dark skin, sweating profusely, hesitated a bit, but still pushed the door and entered.

"I just unloaded some cargo at the docks, I'm all sweaty and stinky," Wang An awkwardly touched his shaved head, standing in the room without sitting down, perhaps afraid of dirtying the chair.

"You must be tired after a day's work, have a seat," Qiyuan poured water for Wang An.

Seeing this, Wang An finally sat down, but only half-sitting on the chair.

Wang An was also a member of the Adjudication.

His experience could also be considered somewhat extraordinary.

He came from a family of laborers, specializing in unloading cargo at the docks.

The son of a tiger is still a tiger; the son of a laborer is still a laborer.

A hundred years ago, his grandfather was also a laborer, but not an ordinary one.

In the era of the Deity Descendent, the Luo Sha Kingdom had many strange and special regulations.

One of the regulations was that everyone was equal, and there was no distinction between occupations.

Even as a laborer, as long as one did well, one would still enjoy "rights" and have various "speaking rights", ranking above the nobles.

In addition to laborers, incense burners, and weavers, etc., they were all equal.

Wang An's grandfather was such a laborer, ranking above the nobles.

The real hard workers.

Although these people had uneven "qualities", and their frequent opinions might cause chaotic governance.

But fortunately, there was the intelligent AI-like Deity Descendent, and the Luo Sha Kingdom did not encounter any chaos, and could even be considered "fair" and "just".

At least, wherever the Deity Descendent looked, the darkness was swept away.

Even the story of a brothel girl assassinating the Third Prince could be widely circulated.

However, after the Deity Descendent's demise, all the regulations of the Deity Descendent era were abolished.

According to the imperial court's explanation, Wang An's grandfather was just a laborer, and his contribution was merely carrying goods, how could it be as great as the contributions of the nobles in the court, so these rights were abolished.

"Today at the docks, I got news that the Blood Cloud Bandits... are going to attack Zhao family's caravan!" Wang An gulped down some water.

Ten years ago, a group of bandits suddenly appeared in Dingbo State, known as the Blood Cloud Bandits.

They burned, killed, and plundered, committing all kinds of evil.

The Liu family sent troops to eliminate them once, but they quickly revived.

The court, blaming the Liu family for their failure to eliminate the bandits, sent the Prince of Zhenyuan to take charge of Dingbo State.

In these past few years, the power of the Blood Cloud Bandits had grown larger and larger, and the Prince of Zhenyuan, due to the "bandit suppression", could be considered to have taken root in Dingbo State.

"Is this news reliable?" Qiyuan asked curiously.

"Absolutely reliable!" Wang An nodded, "Among the Blood Cloud Bandits, there are brothers from our Adjudication! The others may not believe it, but the brothers of the Adjudication are all trustworthy!"

"So this time we'll go and kill the Blood Cloud Bandits?" Qiyuan asked.

"With the Adjudicators taking action, you and I... will get to see the world," Wang An said, "Sister Liu said you are too kind-hearted, you don't even dare to kill ants, so you need to go see the world."

Wang An revealed a mouthful of big yellow teeth.

The "Sister Liu" he referred to was naturally Liu Chun'winter.

Sister Liu had said before that this Qiyuan was too kind-hearted, he didn't even dare to kill fish.

Now, he had to let him see blood, understand...
