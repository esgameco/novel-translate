Qi Yuan's expression was somewhat fanatical, even... ferocious.

Just like a cat seeing a mouse.

But Qi Yuan, with his fanatical expression, was a rare sight.

He seemed not to be a person of the Divine Wood Universe, but rather one of the Mysterious Deities.

"This kind of Bai Yue Guang is truly exceptional," Qi Yuan was even about to drool.

This expression left the usually calm Master of the Academy somewhat puzzled.

However, in the next moment, the Master's expression changed slightly.

"The Mysterious Deities... have arrived."

Qi Yuan rose from the recliner and looked up at the sky.

At that moment, the sky and earth suddenly darkened.

Massive shadows replaced the vast expanse of the land.

In the Cloud Blood City, many civilians instinctively looked up at the sky.

When they saw those towering figures, many people were stunned, abruptly sitting on the ground.

Some kept kowtowing, with snot and tears flowing.

"The Mysterious Deities have descended!"

"It's over!"

In the sky above, ninety-nine Mysterious Deities gathered.

The smaller ones were perhaps only a few hundred zhang in size, while the larger ones spanned dozens of li.

The descent of the Mysterious Deities blocked out the sun, giving a sense of the arrival of a vast celestial army.

However, to describe them as a celestial army would be an overestimation.

No one knew, not even the strongest in the Divine Wood Universe, the Primal One, that in the core region of the Mysterious, the Yang Deities... were merely the celestial soldiers.

Qi Yuan looked at these Mysterious Deities, his gaze falling on the familiar Dark-Eyed Mysterious Deity: "So you're still alive!"

The Dark-Eyed Deity looked at Qi Yuan with some trepidation, but remembering the other Mysterious Deities behind him, he regained his arrogance.

"Mere human, how do you know of the greatness of Lord Kai Huang?

If you kill me, with Lord Kai Huang here, I can be revived at any time!"

The Dark-Eyed Deity said arrogantly.

The other Mysterious Deities also eyed Qi Yuan with malicious intent.

As for the Master of the Academy...

In their sight, the Master seemed to not exist at all.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan's expression became even more joyful.

This was an experience package.

He couldn't let them get scared away.

He pondered deeply, trying to think of a way to lure all these Mysterious Deities down.

But no matter how he thought, he couldn't come up with a good plan.

Even if his acting skills were excellent, he would still give the impression that they could easily kill him.

However, they had not cast any debuff, so he could just die a few times and then flee.

"Unexpectedly, upon seeing us, you do not flee, showing that even a withered mythical legend has such confidence," the white-bearded Kai Huang spoke, his face as confident as ever.

"Why should I flee?" Qi Yuan said indifferently.

In fact, he was overjoyed in his heart.

Especially his left eyebrow, it was jumping frantically!

It was probably jumping too fast, almost as if it had stopped.

Qi Yuan knew this was a sign of great fortune and wealth.

"Indeed, with mythical realm strength and the Mandate's favor, you have the qualifications and confidence to not fear us," Kai Huang said calmly.

Indeed, in the fallen regions of other worlds, even if the Mysterious Deities gathered together, they might not be able to subdue Qi Yuan.

"But alas... you have encountered me!"

Kai Huang's face flashed with a sharp gleam.

Suddenly, Qi Yuan felt a powerful, massive force attracting him.

This force was extremely powerful.

His left eyebrow twitched violently again.

Without any hesitation, Qi Yuan shouted, "What is this? Don't pull me in!"

Although he shouted this, Qi Yuan did not resist at all, but rather seemed to actively dive into that treasured object.

The Kai-Wings Deity saw this and flattered, "Indeed, you are a Mysterious Deity who has emerged from the Mysterious, Lord Kai Huang's casual display of a treasure can so quickly draw in a mythical realm expert."

The other Deities echoed in agreement, "Even the mythical cannot resist at all."

Kai Huang was extremely pleased.

The effect of this treasure was even better than he had expected.

"Alright, let us enter the treasure, do not keep that human waiting long."

After saying this, the nearly hundred Mysterious Deities swiftly entered the treasure.

At this moment, Qi Yuan stood quietly in the treasure.

The treasure was ethereal, like a true vacuum, occasionally revealing some meteorite fragments or stellar debris.

As Kai Huang and the ninety-nine Deities entered, a look of apprehension appeared on Qi Yuan's face.

"There's no way out of here?"

His voice trembled, as if he had some fear.

Before Kai Huang could speak, the Kai-Wings Deity said, "This treasure was bestowed by Lord Kai Huang's uncle, even Yang Deities cannot escape from it."

Kai Huang nodded in satisfaction, adding, "Not even the first layer of Yang Deities can escape."

Seemingly dissatisfied with the Kai-Wings Deity's description of the treasure's level.

"So strong? Doesn't that mean... we all have to stay here?" Qi Yuan looked very apprehensive.

But in reality, he was just trying to gather more information.

"Hmph, this treasure has its own sentience... as long as you die, or we are all wiped out, then we can naturally leave," Kai Huang did not mind telling a dead man more.

This behavior was akin to the enemy's crystal being invaded, the jungler not attacking the tower, and taunting in front of the enemy's fountain, waiting for them to revive, while also showing off his famous sword to block the fountain's attacks.

"I don't die, and you can't get out?" Qi Yuan's eyes widened, looking very... fearful and worried.

His nose did not grow longer, which meant the other side was not lying.

"Hmph, now you're afraid, aren't you?" The Dark-Eyed Deity said.

"Look at him, being a mythical legend, his body trembling, not even better than a commoner!"

"But don't underestimate him, after all, he is a mythical legend!"

"Yes, although Lord Kai Huang has many Mysterious sources, we shouldn't waste them."

"No matter, wasting a bit won't hurt, I... have many Mysterious sources, and my uncle has many as well," Kai Huang said confidently.

Obtaining this information, Qi Yuan suddenly burst into laughter, "Hahaha haha haha..."

This behavior startled the Mysterious Deities.

"Has he gone mad?"

"Is he insane?"

Qi Yuan's gaze turned to Kai Huang, his eyes burning with fanaticism: "So your name is Kai Huang, eh? After you die, I'll erect a tomb for you in the Underworld!"

Qi Yuan was always deeply devoted to his Bai Yue Guang.

To ensure their legacy was not devoured by others, he would personally take care of the Bai Yue Guang's inheritance.

As for Kai Huang, he was even more considerate, wanting to erect a tomb and offer sacrifices to him.

Although Kai Huang did not have an elderly aura about him.

But Qi Yuan's eyebrow was twitching, comparable to refining the Heavenly Emperor's Banner, even stronger by a fraction.

"You're courting death!" Kai Huang raged.

The other dozens of Mysterious Deities were also furious.
