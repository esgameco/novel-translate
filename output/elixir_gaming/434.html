Purple Origin Ancestor hung outside the celestial curtain, his gaze deep and profound.

His eyes fell upon Qi Yuan.

When he saw Qi Yuan go to meet Purple Origin Great Snow, the corner of his mouth twitched involuntarily.

"This guy... is there something wrong with him?"

The first thing he said was: "I want to ask you something!"

What is it?

"Does the Ancestor have a long-lost father out there somewhere?"

Does the Ancestor have a father left behind somewhere?

The Ancestor gave me Black Demon Ancestor Blood as a welcome gift!

That's right, he asked any random passerby, frightening many of the cultivators in Purple Origin Heaven.

He even didn't spare unborn children still in the womb.

Through their mothers' bellies, he directly said the Ancestor gave Black Demon Ancestor Blood, and asked if the Ancestor had an ancestor left behind somewhere.

In any case, within half a day, thousands of people in Purple Origin Heaven had been questioned by Qi Yuan.

At this moment, on a steep mountain peak, red magma flowed, emitting thick heat.

The ground was cracked, with magma bubbling up occasionally.

Qi Yuan's figure stood still.

An impolite voice sounded by his ear: "What are you doing here?"

The one speaking was none other than An Xunlu.

Her expression was cold as she crossed her arms, looking at Qi Yuan with some wariness.

After all, she had just offended Qi Yuan a few days ago, so his coming here now easily made her think he wanted revenge.

"I came here to ask you something!" Qi Yuan said secretly.

An Xunlu narrowed her eyes: "What is it?"

"Do you know if the Ancestor has a father left behind somewhere?" Qi Yuan asked seriously.

An Xunlu was very familiar with Black Sword.

And Black Sword was the number one genius of Black Demon Abyss, the light of hope, so he might know many hidden truths.

"What... are you talking about?" An Xunlu didn't quite understand Qi Yuan's words.

"It's like this, didn't the Ancestor give me a welcome gift?" Qi Yuan continued.

An Xunlu turned her head, the firelight from the magma reflecting on her fair face, making it appear especially rosy: "So you've come to brag about this?"

She seemed to have seen through Qi Yuan's little scheme, and didn't care at all.

The Ancestor valuing someone and giving them a treasure might make others jealous and envious, but not her.

Her future man was Black Sword after all.

The person all nine Ancestors of Black Demon Abyss treated with care.

"What bragging, I'm confused and sincerely asking." Qi Yuan couldn't help but retort.

Where was the trust between people? Oh right, he forgot, the other party wasn't human.

"Heh, what gift did the Ancestor give you?" An Xunlu asked with a smile.

It was probably something a little precious, so Blood Robe couldn't help coming to show off.

But she wasn't ordinary, having been by Black Sword's side, she had long seen too many precious treasures.

"The Ancestor gave me Black Demon Ancestor Blood." Qi Yuan said, taking out the Black Demon Ancestor Blood and revealing half of it to An Xunlu.

An Xunlu's pupils suddenly dilated, her breathing became rapid causing her ample chest to heave: "This... how is this possible!"

How could it possibly be Black Demon Ancestor Blood!

This was Black Demon Ancestor Blood!

In Black Demon Abyss, Black Demon Ancestor Blood was even more precious than miraculous treasures.

Even Yang Spirits at the Ultimate Principle realm weren't qualified to obtain it.

Only the Ancestors at the Great Ultimate Principle realm were qualified to obtain it.

Back then, when the Demon Luo clan's Demon Luo Well swallowed some Black Demon Ancestor Blood, they were expelled.

Even now, Black Sword wasn't qualified to obtain Black Demon Ancestor Blood.

How could Blood Robe? "Impossible, Black Demon Ancestor Blood is the Ancestor's prized possession, how could he give it to you!" An Xunlu didn't believe it.

But the aura emanating from the storage bag was clearly that of Black Demon Ancestor Blood.

As someone with Black Demon Abyss bloodline, she might mistake her own father, but could never mistake Black Demon Ancestor Blood.

"What? Prized possession? Is the Ancestor a duck or something?" Qi Yuan was a bit confused.

An Xunlu took a deep breath to calm herself down: "You really did get it, I didn't expect the Ancestor would give you Black Demon Ancestor Blood.

But... even with Black Demon Ancestor Blood, the number one of the younger generation in Black Demon Abyss is still Big Brother Black Sword, not you.

No matter how hard you try, you can only see Black Sword's back!"

An Xunlu thrust out her chest and said proudly.

"Hey, I'm not trying to compete with Black Sword for first place or anything, don't talk nonsense." Qi Yuan was speechless.

As someone with social anxiety, what would he do with an empty title of number one?

"Hmph!" An Xunlu was very proud, directly turning and leaving, not willing to pay any more attention to Qi Yuan.

In fact, she was nearly grinding her teeth to pieces.

That was Black Demon Ancestor Blood, a supreme treasure no cultivator with Black Demon Abyss bloodline could resist.

"Sigh, looks like this is another unsolved mystery of the world."

Qi Yuan sighed.

Logically, it would make the most sense to ask Purple Origin Heaven's Ancestor about this kind of thing.

After all, just asking would reveal the answer.

But Qi Yuan was socially intelligent, he couldn't just go ask Purple Origin Heaven's Ancestor directly, to avoid awkwardness.

...

At this moment, outside the celestial curtain, Purple Origin Ancestor's beard quivered with anger.

"This guy's personality is too erratic!"

Over the past half day, he had completely lost face.

Just then, space rippled, Ultimate Principle energy flowed, and a shadowy figure appeared beside Purple Origin Ancestor.

"Sixth Brother, you actually gave Black Demon Ancestor Blood to Blood Robe!"

The newcomer was none other than Third Elder, who had parted from Purple Origin Ancestor not long ago.

His eyes held a trace of surprise, as well as a puzzled look.

Even if Blood Robe's talent could reach Great Ultimate Principle, even if he could master Supreme Great Ultimate Principle, it still wasn't worth them... sacrificing their own huge benefits to nurture him.

"Oh, we've had a bit too much Black Demon Ancestor Blood lately, couldn't use it all." Purple Origin Ancestor said casually, clearly not wanting to answer directly.

Third Elder's expression changed several times, then he sighed and said: "Sixth Brother, it seems you still resent Master, back then..."

Hearing this, the smile disappeared from Purple Origin Ancestor's face.

"It has nothing to do with Master." He replied flatly.

Third Elder clearly didn't accept Purple Origin Ancestor's words.

"You gave Black Demon Ancestor Blood to Blood Robe, hoping he could defeat... Black Sword... to prove to Master that you were right.

But... Black Sword's origin is Black Demon Ancestor Blood, no matter how talented Blood Robe is, he can't possibly be his match!" Third Elder lamented.

It seemed the events back then had greatly affected Sixth Brother, he was still hung up on it even now.

"Hmph, I'm too lazy to explain, believe whatever you want, don't bother me!"

Purple Origin Ancestor waved his hand, and Third Elder's figure vanished.

In the vast space, only Purple Origin Ancestor remained.

An unsatisfied look appeared on his face: "To think they would take me for such a petty person?"

Then, the dissatisfaction turned to secret joy: "Hmph, this is good too, they won't pay too much attention to Blood Robe."

...

Time flew by.

Inside the steel building, it was especially quiet.

Purple Origin Little Dew, who cleaned, had long finished and disappeared.

In the cold building, only Qi Yuan remained.

"Level 7 of the Ancestor Blood Scripture."

Qi Yuan stood up, feeling refreshed.

In three months, he had reached the 7th level of the Ancestor Blood Scripture. His strength at the Ten Thousand Dao Martial God realm had also made a qualitative leap.

"When I reach level 9 of the Ancestor Blood Scripture, I should be able to withstand Ultimate Principle with just Ten Thousand Dao Martial God cultivation.
