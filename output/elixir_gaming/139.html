Here is my full English translation of the Chinese novel excerpt:

At this moment, in the Cang Lan Realm.

Countless pairs of eyes looked up at the sky.

Next to the vast sun, another... small sun suddenly appeared.

Compared to the large sun, that small sun seemed insignificant.

However, everyone who saw that small sun had shock in the depths of their eyes.

"There's... another sun in the sky!"

"What's going on? What kind of strange phenomenon is this?"

"This isn't a phenomenon, it's real!"

In the arctic ice fields, a sleeping ice dragon opened its eyes, its blue pupils glancing at the sun in the sky: "Did I sleep too long and start hallucinating?"

It rolled over and went back to sleep.

In the Eastern Holy Land, a woman bathed in radiance looked up at the sky, disbelief in her eyes: "Which... deity is illuminating the heavens..."

Whenever a new star appears, it must mean a new exalted position has emerged.

In the Central Celestial Realm, a man in white robes was fishing when suddenly his hand trembled and the fishing rod snapped.

He looked at the newly appeared sun, deep worry on his face.

"There cannot be two suns in the sky. Is some immortal or god trying to challenge that one's position?"

"Troubled times indeed. I hope it doesn't affect us here. There's not much time left, we should migrate... if we don't leave soon, we'll be caught in the crossfire!"

Countless gazes looked at the extra sun that had appeared in the sky, awe and shock in their eyes.

Meanwhile, at the Divine Light Sect.

The various peak masters had gathered together, with Elder Kumo among the crowd.

Everyone was looking at the sun in the sky.

"How did another sun suddenly appear?"

"It's baffling."

"There must be great terror behind this!"

"We shouldn't look at this sun for too long. If cultivators stare at it, their memory will deteriorate."

"What? It can do that?"

The various cultivators in the Divine Light Sect were heatedly discussing the extra sun that had appeared in the sky.

At this time, Sect Leader Elder Kumo's voice rang out: "What use is there in discussing the sun in the sky? Will it affect your cultivation? Will it help you break through?

I summoned you all here to discuss the Hundred Nations Blood War and the matter of the Palace of Light."

The peak masters fell silent upon hearing this, and stopped discussing the sun in the sky.

An extra sun may have appeared, but shouldn't they still eat their meals as usual?

That kind of matter wasn't for small cultivators like them to ponder.

They should consider things directly related to themselves.

"Kang Fulu has already entered the Golden Core realm, and Qi Yuan is the disciple of the Seven Colors Peak Master. We absolutely cannot hand over these two!"

"Right, we can't hand them over!"

"Hmph, the Palace of Light is truly tyrannical to the extreme. Starting the Hundred Nations Blood War, do they have no... morals left?!"

"Sigh, without any powerful cultivators to stand up for us hundred nations, this seems to be a foregone conclusion!"

Everyone present looked dejected.

Weakness was the original sin.

The Palace of Light, one power oppressing a hundred nations.

A hundred nations and over a thousand sects, yet unable to resist.

Allowing the Palace of Light to manipulate them, becoming pawns in the Hundred Nations Blood War.

And the Great Shang Nation had it even worse, becoming the demonic faction in the Hundred Nations Blood War!

Any cultivator who encountered the demonic faction could slaughter them by any means necessary.

Most likely, some powers surrounding the hundred nations were already rubbing their hands in anticipation, preparing to take advantage of the chaos to invade Great Shang and plunder its resources.

This was a massive disaster for Great Shang.

No power in Great Shang could avoid it.

Now, all of Great Shang was in great turmoil, with many rogue cultivators preparing to migrate away from this war-torn land.

"The hundred nations can't even produce a single Purple Palace realm expert. How can they contend against the Palace of Light?

Kang Fulu and Qi Yuan may be talented, but they still need time to grow. Even if Kang Fulu became a Divine Infant and Qi Yuan reached Golden Core right now, it would be useless," an elder said, his words full of helplessness.

The hundred nations were too weak, without even a single Purple Palace realm expert.

Even if they had one, it likely wouldn't change the outcome.

The Palace of Light had dozens of Purple Palace realm experts.

It could be said they were the undisputed hegemon of the Eastern Emerald Continent.

Of course, these elders didn't know that the Palace of Light also possessed the Imperial Dragon Crown, a damaged Yin Spirit artifact.

Otherwise, they would be even more despairing.

Even a damaged Yin Spirit artifact could easily wipe out the entire Great Shang from among the hundred nations with a single strike.

This was a power that even Purple Palace realm experts couldn't resist.

"This old one received news from the Soaring Heaven Pavilion that one of the Blood Robe Sword Saints from the Divine Flower Society originated from our hundred nations.

The people of the Palace of Light humiliated this Blood Robe Sword Saint, forcing his daughter to live in a dog kennel. The two sides seem to have gotten into a dispute. Hopefully this matter can delay the Palace of Light's offensive."

"The Palace of Light is truly despicable! They claim to be the leader of the righteous path in the Emerald Continent, yet they do such inhuman things! As the saying goes, calamity should not extend to wives and children. Forcing someone's young daughter to live in a dog kennel is utterly abhorrent!"

"Sigh, we all know how disgusting they are, but what can we do about it?"

"The Blood Robe Sword Saint's strength is probably only at the late stage of Divine Infant. He's no match for the Palace of Light."

Everyone discussed animatedly, worry clouding their faces.

They were far too weak compared to the Palace of Light.

At this time, on Seven Colors Peak.

Qi Yuan looked at himself in the mirror, feeling somewhat melancholy.

"Baize, Foolish Daoist, Nangong Dago, Chen Huan, Zhang Sheng..."

He recalled the plot of the Flowing Wind Realm game, memorizing the names he needed to remember.

For the other useless memories, he temporarily forgot them using the Great Forgetting Heart Sutra.

"I've finally become a True Person of the Primal Core."

"This time... I thought it was my mouth that awakened a special ability, but it turned out to be... my eyebrows."

After entering Qi Refinement, his eyes could see hidden information.

After Heavenly Dao Foundation Establishment, his ears could hear some strange sounds.

After forming a Golden Core, he gained another ability - eyebrows that could predict fortune and disaster.

When his left eyebrow twitched, it meant good fortune.

When his right eyebrow twitched, disaster was coming.

That's right, not his eyes, but his eyebrows.

This ability was quite good.

After all, if he entered the game in the future, before challenging a boss, he would know whether to fight or not based on which eyebrow twitched.

This was practically a divine tool.

He left his small wooden cabin, sighing, "I'm now a True Person of the Primal Core. Living in a wooden cabin with junior sister like before lowers my status and identity.

I should upgrade to a brick house."

He left the wooden cabin and immediately ran into his junior sister Jiang Lingsu.

Junior sister Jiang Lingsu seemed worried, with concerns in her heart: "Senior brother, did you see? There's an extra sun in the sky?"

Jiang Lingsu's worries stemmed from this.

Back then, in the Qiankun Mirror, she saw living beings in dire straits, endless blood rain.

She didn't see an extra sun appear in the sky.

Now, a sun had suddenly appeared in the sky.

This meant the workings of fate had changed.

She didn't know if this was a good thing or a bad thing.

"Sun?" Qi Yuan had been thinking about how to bring up the topic of the sun in the sky when he saw his junior sister.

He didn't expect her to mention it directly.

Well then, he wouldn't be polite.

"That's not a sun, that's my Golden Core!" Qi Yuan pointed at the small sun in the sky, full of vigor.

If you don't show off your wealth when you return home, it's like wearing brocade in the dark.

He had finally caught an opportunity to boast.

Hearing Qi Yuan's words, Jiang Lingsu was stunned.

The thread of worry in her heart also disappeared. She smiled, her ample chest quivering slightly: "Senior brother, you really know how to joke.

You can't casually say things like that.

What if the deity illuminating that sun hears you? They might come looking for trouble."

"The truth is in the hands of the few." Qi Yuan naturally wouldn't explain. He looked at that sun, a hint of nostalgia on his face. "There's still someone on that sun..."

The golden canary was still on that star.

"Senior brother, your talent is indeed good, but right now you've just broken through to Foundation Establishment.

Golden Core is still far away for you! The vessel for the Golden Core is even more important than Foundation Establishment.

Senior brother, do you want to borrow some spirit stones from me to buy something expensive?" Jiang Lingsu was truly wealthy, with lots of money, and she was genuinely generous towards Qi Yuan.

This time, Qi Yuan was magnanimous, viewing wealth as dirt: "Can spirit stones buy a star?"

They can't! And there were so many stars and planets in the universe waiting for Qi Yuan to devour.

They were all free, at no cost!

Might as well eat them!

"Senior brother, my sister will be coming tomorrow. I'm letting you know in advance." Jiang Lingsu didn't mention the Golden Core matter again.

"She's most welcome!" Qi Yuan said.

The two chatted for a while, then Jiang Lingsu returned to her wooden cabin to cultivate.

Qi Yuan muttered to himself: "True Person of the Primal Core... it's just so-so?"

He felt his life hadn't really changed much.

He took a deep breath and looked towards the palace halls at the top of Seven Colors Peak.

Silent and still, bathed in cold moonlight, like a fairyland.

He started walking up.
