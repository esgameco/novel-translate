This must be a major event.

"If the sky falls, there are tall people to hold it up, this has nothing to do with me."

Blood Tiger calmed himself down.

Now, finding Qi Yuan is the priority.

"The princess has actually been killed by a human slave!"

Either they were the daughters of the Mysterious Spirits, or their sisters.

The daughters or sisters of the Mysterious Spirits had a status even higher than the elders of the Thorny Blood.

This princess particularly liked to hide her identity and cause trouble.

As members of the Thorny Blood, they all left a wisp of their divine soul in their soul tokens.

The shattering of the soul token meant the death of the body.

Upon learning this news, how could Golden Crane not be alarmed?

The other sky-level experts were also rushing towards the place where that sky-level expert had perished.

"What's the situation?" Golden Crane's voice was gloomy and terrible.

The death of the princess, they could not escape responsibility.

Probably not even death would save them, they might have to peel off a layer of skin.

As for that person, he wished he could eat his flesh and drink his bones.

Dragon Food was the sky-level expert protecting the princess, although his strength was not outstanding, he was still a sky-level expert! But he was killed with a single slap? How could Golden Crane not be shocked? Now, the powerful experts of the Cloud Blood City had gathered in one place, their momentum fierce.

"Slapping Dragon Food to death with one hand, his strength must be extraordinary, but no matter what, for killing the princess, he must die today!" Golden Crane said in a deep voice.

"Li Zha is currently contacting him, trying to probe his depth!" said the Tenth Elder.

Li Zha was also a sky-level expert, best at speed and fleeing, so he was the one tasked with probing Qi Yuan.

As for the other powerful experts of the Thorny Blood, they were now gathered in a group, hiding in the dark, preparing to strike with a thunderous blow.

If they did not strike, so be it, but if they did, it would be earth-shattering.

At this moment, on the street, Li Zha looked at the man in front of him, his expression angry.

"Do you know what you've done?"

Qi Yuan stretched lazily, his expression as casual as ever: "I just wanted to come into the city and have breakfast, but someone was blocking the city gate, collecting a toll of a hundred taels!

How many steamed buns could that buy? I complained to the city guards, but they didn't listen to me!

The head of the city guards was standing behind that thief, nodding and fawning like a lackey! Where is the justice, where is the righteous way? Since no one upheld justice for me, as an honest person, I had no choice but to take matters into my own hands!"

Qi Yuan said earnestly.

His original intention was very simple, to go find the Thorny Blood and tell them, I want to become mortal, don't disturb me, otherwise the consequences will be dire.

He was still very good at talking, persuading with reason.

Of course, if reason didn't work, he would flexibly change his approach and use force to prevail! Hearing this, Li Zha felt only ridiculous.

Merely for a few hundred taels of silver, to slay a princess? This person must be sick! "You're done for, killing the princess, you have no chance of survival, you will surely die today!" Li Zha said in a deep voice, "Soon there will be people coming for you, it is your time of death!"
