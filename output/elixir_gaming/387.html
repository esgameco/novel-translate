"Senior Brother!" Those disciples hurriedly bowed their heads in greeting.

The female disciple who had spoken up for Qi Yuan earlier said proudly: "Senior Brother Qi Yuan didn't steal any beautiful female elder's underwear. The beautiful female elders delivered it themselves at night!"

She felt proud of her quick wit, lest Senior Brother Qi Yuan be portrayed as a thief.

"Oh..."

Qi Yuan was speechless.

Such things... seemed to have actually happened.

However, that was when he was cultivating the Five Elements Qi, causing some madness and chaos in Divine Light Sect.

It shouldn't be taken seriously.

"You, you. Haven't seen you in three years, but you've made some progress and become an official disciple. Don't spread baseless rumors anymore! Official disciples are also the face of Divine Light Sect."

Qi Yuan casually said to this fan, then his figure dispersed and vanished.

Hearing this, the young woman's eyes lit up with joy: "Senior Brother Qi Yuan... actually remembers me!"

Back then, she was an errand disciple at Divine Medicine Peak, specifically responsible for reception. When Senior Brother Qi Yuan went to Divine Medicine Peak seeking medicine, she was the one who received him and recommended he find Elder Xuanchong.

Later, Senior Brother Qi Yuan had pointed out issues with her body's cultivation, which she then resolved, increasing her cultivation and becoming an official disciple.

Returning to Seven-Colored Peak, Qi Yuan was in quite a good mood.

After reaching the Purple Palace realm, his status and class naturally had to be elevated.

He couldn't allow there to be any houses in Divine Light Sect more classy than his own.

Withered Wood True Lord had taken Qi Yuan's design plan and was already looking for people to start construction, building a small tower for Qi Yuan.

"With a small tower, it will finally match my status.

My wives can move in without worrying about not having their own private bedrooms!"

Qi Yuan thought to himself.

Of course, there was also his daughter Qi Jianjun who was always running around fishing randomly.

Now, his daughter Qi Jianjun had gone off somewhere unknown to fish.

As for Shen Wushen, he should still be in the upper realm. Calculating the time, he would descend to the lower realm after another period to pass on divine methods to Qi Yuan.

Thinking of Shen Wushen, Qi Yuan's gaze couldn't help but turn towards the palace halls atop Seven-Colored Peak and that huge ancient Qichun tree.

"Shen Wushen once said the letter would take three years to reach Jin Li's hands. Two and a half years have already passed..."

In another half year, Jin Li would receive his letter.

Qi Yuan's mood rippled with faint waves.

He thought of the small figure of Moon Girl by July Lake, and the Empress Jin Li whom he had never met.

"Senior Brother, run quickly!"

Just then, an urgent voice rang out. Jiang Lingsu, dressed conservatively, flew quickly to Qi Yuan's side and tightly hugged his arm.

Jiang Lingsu's voice suddenly jolted Qi Yuan from his melancholy.

"What's wrong?" he asked, "Why are you in such a hurry? Has the sky fallen or something?"

Now that Qi Yuan was a legendary Purple Palace great cultivator, he naturally had to have composure, remaining calm even if Mount Tai collapsed before him.

"My father is coming!" Jiang Lingsu said anxiously.

The image of that enthusiastic uncle immediately appeared in Qi Yuan's mind.

At that time, on the spirit boat, that uncle had been very enthusiastic.

For the sake of his grand plan, Qi Yuan had "tricked" everyone into uploading techniques to the Divine Flower Society, personally making up stories under the name Origin Venerable.

Stories about having a humble background, being looked down upon by senior brothers, and receiving cold shoulders from others.

Then, uploading techniques to the Divine Flower Society, exchanging for powerful techniques, attracting countless admirers, cold senior sisters giving flirtatious glances, busty junior sisters sneaking to his room at night.

At the time, Jiang Ruhua had taken Origin Venerable's story as true, personally using his own experiences to guide Qi Yuan, advising him to choose the busty junior sister.

As for the busty junior sister's father not agreeing? Simply turn the rice into cooked rice, have the busty junior sister appear with a big belly, and her old fogey father would have no choice but to accept it with gritted teeth.

Sharing experiences with juniors and satisfying the desire to teach others was very enjoyable.

But... when that busty junior sister's father was himself, Jiang Ruhua couldn't hold it together anymore.

He stopped managing his businesses and directly moved to Great Shang.

Hearing that Qi Yuan had awakened, he immediately rushed over.

Hearing it was Jiang Ruhua, Qi Yuan also became a bit flustered: "We really should run, otherwise... it would be too awkward!"

Qi Yuan was very socially anxious and didn't dare to face Jiang Ruhua again.

Meeting face to face would be so awkward.

"Run quickly!" Qi Yuan picked up Jiang Lingsu and vanished into a blur.

Outside Seven-Colored Peak, Jiang Ruhua in blue robes looked at that streak of light in the sky and couldn't help cursing: "You little brat, why are you running!"

He wanted nothing more than to slash up there with his sword.

But his daughter was still there!

"You brat, even having me muted on the Divine Flower Society. No respect for elders at all!"

Jiang Ruhua was indignant.

Originally he had planned to spread rumors on the Divine Flower Society forum, but who knew, as soon as he cursed Origin Venerable once, he was muted.

"Sigh, miscalculation, miscalculation. Originally passed on dragon-slaying techniques to that kid, who knew I myself was the dragon!"

Helan Province was located in the north of the Eastern Lands, ranking middle to lower among the thirteen provinces of the Eastern Lands.

At this time, on a tower, a man in a blood-red robe leaned against the railing, his gaze occasionally sweeping over the cultivators passing below, his eyes carrying a thoughtful look.

Beside him, Jiang Lingsu held a communication jade slip, seemingly chattering away with someone, apparently complaining about something.

After who knows how long, Qi Yuan stretched lazily: "I'm so hardworking and diligent."

After arriving in Helan Province, Qi Yuan had been using his eyes every day to "peep" at the cultivators of the world.

Anyone Foundation Establishment or above had a mark inside their body. His eyes would receive an information prompt each time he looked at a person's mark in a day.

So, he "peeped" at the marks inside people's bodies all over the world in order to find ways to remove and seal the marks.
