Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The consumption in the game was huge, but for Qi Yuan, the improvement was also extremely great.

Especially after reaching level 90 in the game, the feedback to the real world made him feel that his cultivation had improved significantly.

Previously, he did not use knives, but relying on the combat skills and various techniques he had honed in the game, he already possessed the battle power of a Foundation Establishment cultivator.

Now, with his improved cultivation, even without using any techniques or skills, Qi Yuan could suppress ordinary Foundation Establishment cultivators with just his own spiritual power.

This kind of improvement was extremely high.

"Unfortunately, the consumption of spiritual awareness is still too great. Is it because my spiritual soul is too weak?" Qi Yuan pondered.

Unfortunately, cultivators in the Qi Refining stage cannot even sense their own spiritual souls, and it is only after reaching the Foundation Establishment stage that they can perceive it.

"After I reach the Foundation Establishment stage, I must cultivate some secret techniques to strengthen my spiritual soul, so that I can stay longer in the game."

Thinking this, Qi Yuan took out the books from his storage bag to read.

He was quite diligent.

However, as soon as he looked at the books in the storage bag, he felt a sharp pain in his head.

Clearly, this was a side effect of staying up late playing games.

"I can't read the books anymore, I need to rest well and get a massage." Qi Yuan felt that he really needed to go out and recuperate.

Otherwise, it would be too ridiculous if he suddenly died.

As Qi Yuan slowly left the thatched hut, he saw Jiang Lingsu flipping through something.

Jiang Lingsu, in a long ice-blue dress, glanced at Qi Yuan and frowned, "Senior brother, have you encountered a problem in your cultivation?"

"No, I just stayed up late playing games, and my body can't take it.

I'm going out to get a massage, and it should be fine. Junior sister, do you want to give me a massage? I'll give you... three spirit stones as a reward."

"...Get lost!" Seeing Qi Yuan's flowery words, the concern Jiang Lingsu had just shown was immediately extinguished.

Qi Yuan chuckled and slowly went down the mountain.

It's a good thing his junior sister didn't agree, or else he would have wasted three spirit stones.

At the foot of the mountain, a proper massage only cost one spirit stone.

If it weren't for the fact that his junior sister was a wealthy lady, he wouldn't have endured the pain of adding three spirit stones.

As Qi Yuan walked on, Jiang Lingsu looked at her senior brother's retreating figure and felt a little self-reproachful. Was her tone just now a bit too harsh?

Qi Yuan went down the mountain and had a proper, all-natural massage.

He also spent one spirit stone to buy a rather rich and nourishing spiritual soup to drink.

After a day of relaxation, Qi Yuan finally felt his body getting better.

"My spiritual soul is still too weak. Unfortunately, the cultivation techniques and spiritual medicines related to the spiritual soul are extremely rare."

In the vast library of the Black Mountain Sect, Qi Yuan had not yet found any cultivation techniques related to the spiritual soul.

As for spiritual medicines, there were even fewer.

Qi Yuan knew that any spiritual medicines or treasures related to the spiritual soul were highly precious.

It was rumored that from the Spirit Infant stage to the Purple Palace realm, the requirements for the spiritual soul were very high.

Therefore, almost all spiritual medicines or cultivation techniques related to the spiritual soul were monopolized by those ancient Spirit Infant cultivators.

Not to mention Qi Yuan was only in the Qi Refining stage, even Foundation Establishment or Elixir cultivators would find it very difficult to obtain spiritual medicines that could enhance their spiritual souls.

"But it's not a big problem. Once I succeed in Foundation Establishment, I'll consider the matter of strengthening my spiritual soul."

Qi Yuan's eyes could see hidden information. This was the ability he had obtained after entering the game and reaching the Qi Refining stage.

Perhaps after reaching the Foundation Establishment stage, he would gain some new abilities.

Qi Yuan was not in a hurry.

He returned to the Seven-Color Peak, ready to continue resting, and once he had recovered, he would re-enter the game.

When passing by the large tree halfway up the mountain, Qi Yuan stopped again.

He looked down at the ground.

[This is a somewhat unusual small grass, it has once again sensed the aura of the moon.]

Qi Yuan looked up at the sky: "The aura of the moon, what is that?"

He lowered his head and looked at the small grass, then said to it, "When I see you tomorrow, will you be able to sense Qi Yuan's aura as well?"

He wanted to find someone without diabetes and revive this unusual small grass.

Then ask it how it went from ordinary to unusual. He also wanted to become unusual.
