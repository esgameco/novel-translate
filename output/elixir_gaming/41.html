As soon as he entered the game, he and the bride were lying on a board.

His bride was a large red wedding dress, in the ancient Chinese style, the kind you see in horror movies.

If you look closely, you can even imagine a beautiful, graceful, and charming bride.

He looked at the dress monster, or rather, the wedding dress monster.

However, Qi Yuan ultimately did not kill this NPC.

At the time, when fighting the Ugly Dress Monster King in the forbidden area, the blood-red gas of the Ugly Dress Monster King entered Qi Yuan's body.

That gas was extremely troublesome for him, continuously weakening his attributes, and even trying to gradually control Qi Yuan's body.

And that red gas was helped to be dispelled by this wedding dress monster, Qi Yuan's new bride.

Everyone helped you? How can you have the heart to turn her into an experience pack? Moreover, this wedding dress monster seems to have some intelligence.

Unlike those who see Qi Yuan and just want to attack him, this one is different.

He looked at the wedding dress monster sitting on the board, only a step away from him, and slowly said, "Who are you? What's your name?"

The wedding dress monster did not respond, motionless.

"No name?"

"Don't remember?"

"Then I'll call you... 'Xiao Hong'?"

"Xiao Monster?"

"Xiao Jia!"

In the end, Qi Yuan gave the wedding dress monster a name - Xiao Jia.

Unfortunately, Xiao Jia has no face, no skin, no legs, no hands... if the sleeves and pant legs count as hands and legs, then she has them.

"Sigh, I've accidentally picked up a wife." Qi Yuan didn't know whether to be happy or worried.

This wife still cannot communicate with him normally.

"Why did you marry me?" Qi Yuan still tried to communicate with Xiao Jia, even though she seemed unable to respond.

This time, Qi Yuan was surprised that Xiao Jia did not remain unresponsive.

From her red sleeves, she threw out a wooden plaque.

The wooden plaque was very ancient in material, with two characters written on it.

"Husband?"

That's right, the two characters written on it were "husband."

"You want me to be your husband? So you took the opportunity when I was offline to marry me?" Qi Yuan was speechless.

He had encountered an existence even more absurd than himself.

And it was an NPC.

"No, we didn't go to the civil affairs bureau, so it doesn't count!" Qi Yuan continued.

He didn't want to marry a piece of clothing.

Although brothers are like hands and feet, and women are like clothes, you can't actually be a real piece of clothing, right?

Otherwise, how would the married life be coordinated?

Xiao Jia's sleeves tightly grasped Qi Yuan's arm and refused to let go.

She couldn't speak, but her actions made it clear.

Qi Yuan sighed helplessly: "Since you can communicate with wooden plaques, at least get more plaques and introduce yourself more."

His eyes looked towards Xiao Jia...

At that moment, Little Jia's entire cold body pressed against Qi Yuan's.

A special change occurred at that moment.

In the bridal chamber, the blood-red wedding dress turned into red light dots, like red fireflies, falling on Qi Yuan's body.

In less than a thousandth of a second, the red fireflies transformed into a red armor, covering Qi Yuan's body, completely concealing his body parts, leaving only his eyes exposed.

From a distance, the armor was blood-red, like flowing blood, but this fluid-type armor gave Qi Yuan an extremely strong sense of security.

Exquisite and beautiful, mysterious and powerful.

Wearing the blood armor, Qi Yuan held a great sword, and his entire height also rose to ten meters.

Now, he was like a terrifying demon walking in hell.

Qi Yuan felt that there was a wedding dress tattoo on his chest.

The tattoo was exactly the same as Little Jia's.

He could feel that the wedding dress pattern was Little Jia's true form.

The blood-colored armor was the defense that Little Jia had bestowed upon him, and not Little Jia's true body.

However, he vaguely felt that this was temporary.

That was 5 levels! A 5-level increase was a huge boost for Qi Yuan, making it much easier for him to grind monsters.

If he encountered the level 95 Ugly Clothes Emperor again, he wouldn't have to directly flee, and he even had a chance to defeat it in a one-on-one fight.

Of course, this one-on-one would be him and Little Jia against the Ugly Clothes Emperor.

At this time, an unexpected thing happened to Qi Yuan.

He activated two skills simultaneously.

One was a major skill, and the other was a minor skill.

Every 30 levels, Qi Yuan could obtain a major skill that could be used in the real world.

Every 10 levels, he would gain a minor skill.

By merging with Little Jia, Qi Yuan's level had broken through 90, and he had gained two skills.

These two skills were the major skill [Comrade-in-Arms] and the minor skill [Rend Clothes].
