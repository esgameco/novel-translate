No words could adequately describe this battle.

Qi Yuan, who had entered the Purple Palace realm, displayed combat prowess at the pinnacle of the Great Celestial Yang Spirit level, regardless of his actual cultivation level.

When he ascended to the stars, he had merged with 4,000 divine infants and slain the East Lord who was half a step into the Ultimate Principle realm.

Of course, this was related to the fact that the East Lord's trump cards were completely useless against him.

In terms of divine power alone and physical manifestation, he was even stronger than Yang Spirits at the minor Ultimate Principle level.

Now, he charged ahead of gods and Buddhas, straight into the Three Thousand Great Calamities.

The slaughter continued!

Blood flowed! Chaos was torn apart! As far as the eye could see, there were only enemies, enemies everywhere!

Since becoming an Elemental Core, Qi Yuan had never experienced such an intense battle.

The demons in the Mountain and Sea Scroll could be dealt with in one sword strike? The fiends outside the City of No Return could block one sword? Now, facing the Three Thousand Great Calamity Elemental Bodies, he fought to the ends of time.

There were no instant kills, no slaying with a single sword!

It was all about the collision and competition of divine power! Qi Yuan, who had not been injured for a long time, was also wounded in this battle.

The chaotic fragments of the Calamity Elemental Bodies continuously fell into the underworld, forming a special area.

Qi Yuan's 4,000 divine infants, once primordial gods, were also surrounded and bloodied by the Calamity Elemental Bodies, falling into the underworld.

"Kill!"

Qi Yuan's eyes had long been dyed red with blood. Clad in blood-red armor, he charged into the Calamity Elemental Bodies, killing gods who blocked his way, slaying Buddhas who stood in his path! Sky-Cleaving Sword Technique! Death's Radiant Light! Tyrannical Power!

He used these three great skills in rotation.

He held nothing back! This battle was extremely brutal.

Perhaps because of the chaotic nature of the Great Calamity, there was no fear of retreat.

Even though Qi Yuan's side held an absolute advantage, with many fighting against many, the strong defeating the weak.

However... not a single Calamity Elemental Body retreated, panicked, or fled.

They were like emotionless war machines, giving their all.

Because of this, even though they were at a disadvantage, the Great Calamity showed no signs of collapse, still facing the primordial gods of all heavens with their strongest stance.

This battle lasted a full ten days.

For ten days, the fighting in heaven and earth did not cease.

All cultivators and mortals could not see even a corner of the battle, only able to judge by the blood color and formless chaos.

The blood color grew more intense, while the chaos diminished.

All cultivators breathed a sigh of relief, while mortals were shocked in their hearts, numb yet accustomed.

In the void, the blood armor on Qi Yuan's body had long since faded away. Clad in a long robe, his entire body was dyed red with blood.

This blood came from both himself and the divine infants.

In front, only the strongest few of the chaotic Great Calamity remained.

There was no fear or despair in their eyes.

"Do you have any last words to say?" Killing intent surged from Qi Yuan's body.

This battle was so intense that even the suona band and the mourners had to take up arms and join the fight.

Qi Yuan's divine infants had also burned out.

The Six Emperors stood behind Qi Yuan, majestic and noble, displaying infinite bodies.

The chaotic Great Calamity finally spoke, their voices in perfect unison.

"Power is calamity!"

"The Nine Heavens have no true future!"

"You... can you really eliminate the Great Calamity?"

There was a hint of gloom in the voices of the chaotic Great Calamity.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan was a bit speechless: "I shouldn't have let you speak at all, still trying to be all mysterious with me?"

The expressions of the chaotic Great Calamity remained unchanged, showing no fear of death.

At this moment, Qi Yuan said: "Hehe, if it were someone else, they might be stunned by your riddles, troubled to no end.

But I'm sorry, I was born... not fond of using my brain.

I've long since stored my brain away! What's the point of thinking so much? As long as I'm strong enough, even the Great Calamity will have to bow at my feet!"

"Kill!"

Countless divine powers erupted at this moment. The slaughter began again, and ended in half a quarter hour.

The entire sky turned blood red.

This blood red was Qi Yuan's long robe, and also the blood of countless divine infants.

The sky became a single red, and chaos finally disappeared without a trace.

Countless cultivators and mortal commoners looked up at the sky.

The sounds of killing and roaring suddenly stopped, all divine power vanished, leaving silence.

The sky seemed to have become an ancient battlefield. The previous slaughter seemed like just a projection of history, a fleeting dream.

"Is it over?"

"Did we... win?"

Countless cultivators and mortals looked up at the sky, some weeping with joy.

When they saw the Great Calamity swallowing other worlds, they had actually become somewhat fearful.

It's not that having more people... means disaster won't befall individuals.

Many mountains crumbled, many cities shattered.

The false sense of security was brutally torn apart, and now, it was held up again by that blood-colored figure!

Countless cultivators celebrated wildly, shouting.

"Blood-Robed Sword God!"

"Qi Yuan, Eliminator of the Great Calamity!"

But there were also great venerable ones and yin spirits whose expressions were shocked, then changed to worry.

Does this Canglang Realm... belong to Qi from now on?

A look of wisdom flashed in the eyes of Great Wisdom True Lord: "People love to worry about the world, not knowing that worldly worries are endless.

We're cultivating immortality, it's better not to worry than to worry."

At this moment, in the sky, Qi Yuan in his blood-colored robe showed weariness in his eyes.

In this battle, 327 divine infant cores were shattered, with over 3,700 wounded.

Now, all the divine infants returned to their original bodies.

As for the Canglang Realm, billions of lives were also lost due to the impact of this battle.

Qi Yuan looked towards the underworld and sighed softly: "Fortunately, marks were left beforehand. Given enough time, the divine infants can be revived, and the dead... can reappear."

Before battling the Great Calamity, Qi Yuan had slowly toured the Canglang Realm, dyeing its sky red.

He had also seen mountains, waters, and insects, leaving marks on all living beings.

This way, even if the living beings of the Canglang Realm were killed by the shockwaves of the great battle, they would be revived.

Of course, this kind of revival could not remove the marks of the Tai Huang Palace.

Even if revived, the marks would still be there.

Moreover, if they died because of the marks, the underworld could not revive them.

So now, the Canglang Realm could only reside within his Purple Palace, separated from the outside world.

"So tired."

At this moment, Qi Yuan felt endless, boundless fatigue.

Four thousand divine infants had fought with all their might.

All the mental consumption was concentrated in his body.

All beings are me, primordial gods transformed into divine infants. In a sense, these divine infants were also him.

So the fatigue from this battle was the combination of all divine infants.

The injuries from this battle were also the combination of all divine infants.

Now, he was more exhausted than ever before, even more tired than when he fought the old man in the Ground of Extinction.

"Time to go back... and take a nap."

Qi Yuan swept his gaze around.

The fragments of the chaotic Great Calamity had transformed into a strange land.

If it were the past him, he would definitely have rushed over to see what was inside.

Now, he was too tired, too sleepy, he needed to go sleep for a while.

The blood-red light disappeared at this moment, and the red color in the sky of the Canglang Realm also vanished.

The sun and moon illuminating all heavens, like countless stars flowing on the blood-colored armor, also disappeared at this moment.

In the sky of the Canglang Realm, only ten suns could be seen.

Jiang Lingsu in the tower seemed to sense something. She lifted her skirt and flew towards that blood-red figure.
