They could even corrode the body of a Yang Shen.

Half a day passed quickly, and Qi Yuan's figure stopped.

The seven Ren Qi Guang were emitting a terrifying aura, like a scorching sun.

Qi Yuan looked ahead and saw a huge battleship traversing the cold void sea.

That battleship was huge, even larger than the Qingshan Realm.

"The Black Sky Black Earth is indeed formidable, such a massive battleship... If it were to fall from the sky, wouldn't it crush ten thousand ants?"

Qi Yuan murmured.

Holding the seven Ren Qi Guang, the void sea environment around Qi Yuan changed, and he seemed like the moon surrounded by stars.

The scorching light, contrasting with him, made him appear like a war god.

"Realm Lord, just confront them, blocking the battleship's advance will do!" Yuan reminded.

The irritable big-bearded man also hurriedly said, "Realm Lord, you must remain calm."

Qi Yuan looked at the ominous battleship, his gaze deep: "I want to confront them, but they have to agree, don't they?"

Hearing this, Yuan's expression suddenly changed.

Could it be... that there is an unexpected situation?

The other ancestral gods' thoughts were also quickly turning, seemingly realizing something.

As Qi Yuan's voice fell.

Suddenly, a towering figure flew out from the battleship.

Eternal burning flames, cracking and popping, the colorful luminous spots in the void sea were all blown away.

The terrifying flame giant roared: "Qi Yuan, die!"

The Flame Realm Lord furiously attacked.

This kind of supremely powerful being attacking, domineering and extraordinary, without any reason.

In just an instant, the surrounding space turned into a sea of flames.

Reaching the Realm Lord realm, one can already influence the environment and rules of heaven and earth.

Even if the Flame Realm Lord were to sneeze, it could turn a cultivation world the size of the Qinglan Realm into a realm of flames that would burn for billions of years without extinguishing.

This is just a casual sneeze, if he were to attack, the might of his flames would be able to devour everything.

A strong scorching sensation came, and even with Qi Yuan's strength, he could feel the heat.

"Eternal Fire, able to burn through heaven and earth, Qi Yuan... where can you run, obediently be burned by my flames!"

The Flame Realm Lord let out a loud roar, and flames were everywhere in the heavens and the earth.

Absolute Adaptation!

At this moment, Qi Yuan unleashed the rules he had mastered.

In an instant, he also transformed into a man of fire.

"Oh, I'm indeed being burned, and have become a man of fire." Qi Yuan said lightly.

He did not use the Ren Qi Guang, but only Absolute Adaptation, fighting fire with fire.

The Flame Realm Lord was stunned, his expression furious: "Die!"

The flames became even more vigorous, ten times, a hundred times more powerful than before! Qi Yuan's kind of rule, the Flame Realm Lord had seen similar ones before.

However, most were just crude imitations.

Qi Yuan shrugged, his gaze shifting: "You're a bit hard to kill, aren't you?"

His eyes could see a lot.

Reaching the Realm Lord realm, they were not that easy to kill.

Unless the power gap between them was too great.

Now that Qi Yuan had just advanced to the Wanwu Realm, to kill the Flame Realm Lord of the Rule Realm, it would take at least a day.

This was too slow for Qi Yuan.

"Hmph, you're hard to kill too." The Flame Realm Lord said seriously.

What he understood as hard to kill was different from Qi Yuan's understanding.

What he understood was that with his strength, even if he stood at the edge of the universe, it would be difficult to defeat each other.

"But... even if you're hard to kill, I, the Sovereign, will kill you!" The Flame Realm Lord's eyes burned even more fiercely, with a sinister expression, "I have the Deviant Source, I do not fear death, what do you have?"

At this time, outside the void sea, Yuan and Sheng Nü and the other ancestral gods' expressions suddenly changed.

"Oh no, this Flame Realm Lord has gone mad, he's going to exchange his life for yours!"

"How could this be, is the one revived by the Deviant Source him? How could he be so unwise?"

"Realm Lord, retreat quickly, don't fight him, stay away from the Flame Realm Lord!" Yuan hurriedly reminded.

The ancestral gods present were on edge.

Between Realm Lords, no one can really harm the other, but if one side doesn't care about their life and consumes their source rules in a self-destructive manner, then one time would be enough to severely injure an opponent of the same level, twice... would definitely lead to mutual destruction.

In the past, the Black Sky Black Earth had not used this method, because no one knew whether the one revived by the Deviant Source was still themselves.

But the Flame Realm Lord didn't care about that.

"Wukong, lend me the Deviant Source to block this person's retreat!" The Flame Realm Lord roared.

The Wukong Realm Lord, with an expression devoid of joy or sorrow, sent the Deviant Source into the Flame Realm Lord's hand, and his towering figure just happened to block Qi Yuan's path.

"With me here, he won't be able to escape." Wukong Realm Lord said indifferently.

"What are you going to do?" Qi Yuan had a terrifying guess in his heart.

"Hmph, don't you know?" The Flame Realm Lord's expression was cold.

"This is too unwise of you." Qi Yuan pretended to be frightened.

But inwardly, he was about to bloom with joy.

Not bad, this is my uncle Qiaohuang, or rather, Qiaohuang's main body, just as foolish as Qiaohuang.

To actually self-destruct, not knowing... do I have a blood-locking pendant?

The more severe the injury, the more terrifying the Absolute Adaptation empowered by the Death Brilliance.

"The seven Ren Qi Guang can help you block seven times of my rule dissolution, but what about after the seventh time?"

Rule dissolution, that is, the Realm Lord self-destructing.

Realm Lord self-destruction is not an explosion of the body, but a dissolution of the rules.

The power released by the dissolution of the rules is no less than a great earthquake in the dimensions of the universe.

"To kill you, I specifically borrowed the Deviant Source from the Wukong Realm Lord, enough to support my rule dissolution thirty times... how will you survive?"

The Flame Realm Lord roared, his eyes bloodshot.

Qi Yuan's expression became serious: "No, don't!"

He originally wanted to act a bit more real, saying something like "Is the one revived by the Deviant Source still you?", but was afraid that the words would be too harsh and scare the Flame Realm Lord.

So he could only keep saying "No, don't!" Inwardly, he was thinking, "Come on, self-destruct! After all, it would take a day to kill me, but self-destruction only takes an instant!"

"Die!" The Flame Realm Lord let out a roar.

Endless and boundless flame rules were dissolved at this moment.

The void sea seemed to sway.

The majestic battleship also seemed to be shaken by this.

Qi Yuan saw this and held the Ren Qi Guang.

"Block for me!"

In fact, all the rule dissolutions were blocked by his own body.

Death.
