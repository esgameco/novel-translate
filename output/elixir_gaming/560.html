"A god has descended?"

Others were amazed, for the overbearing and extraordinary voice in the sky was indeed too familiar.

When he heard "Grandpa Cai", he couldn't help but grimace.

However, being a cultured person, he couldn't be bothered to respond.

His gaze seemed to transcend time and space, falling on Shen Lingfeng in Feng Mountain Prefecture.
