White Marsh was truly the Buddha, truly the Great Demon! While she... was just a demon woman, and demon woman was just one of her identities.

Back then, when Qi Yuan stepped into the Gate of Ten Thousand Demons, the illusion he fell into was the Great Demon's many lifetimes.

Qi Yuan was in the Gate of Ten Thousand Demons, the golden canary was in the Gate of Ten Thousand Demons.

This golden body would be indestructible to gods and indestructible to vajras.

Once she succeeded in cultivating it, she could swallow the dragon like a python swallows a sparrow. The Wind Realm she had schemed for for years would truly be devoured by her, thoroughly becoming her playground.

[Fragment of the Fate Tablet, its spirit has perished, buried in Buddha's hand.]

But they had crossed paths a few times.

If that happened, he would be utterly disgraced.

Hearing Qi Yuan say things he didn't understand, Chen Huan wore a silly smile on his face.
