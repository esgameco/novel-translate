After all, for a Divine Infant True Lord like him, living in the Cang Lan Realm now was no different from before.

The surrounding Yin Gods' expressions all changed at Qi Yuan's words.

"Senior has shown us life-saving grace. How could we dare to complain?"

These Yin Gods were all scared, worried that Qi Yuan was implying something.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan's expression remained calm.

Is this the legendary "accompanying a ruler is like accompanying a tiger"?

He had spoken casually, but these people were all carefully speculating about his meaning.

"Senior's will is the will of the Cang Lan Realm, and our will as well."

Seeing "brothers" driving Range Rovers would make him die of anger.

The Yin God Venerables present looked at each other, bewildered by this scene.

They carefully pondered Qi Yuan's words, but found them difficult to understand.

Regarding the Blood-Robed Sword God's personality and character, these Yin God Venerables had thoroughly discussed it in private recently.

After all, he was the lord of Cang Lan, a mountain looming over their heads.

But despite discussing it until their saliva flew, they couldn't come to any conclusions.

Because they reached a terrifying realization.

This Blood-Robed Sword God behaved strangely...

There seemed to be... something wrong with his mind! But... how could a cultivator at the Yang God realm possibly have mental issues or be crazy? After all, for such a great figure, even farting would have deep meaning and be part of some grand scheme.
