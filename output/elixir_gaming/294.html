Qi Yuan, who had been training in the Mingwu Resonance Fist, paused for a moment.

Then he heard Wang Shiqiao say: "Jealous?"

They were considered to have some money, with no worries about food and clothing, but compared to the real power players of Tianyue, the gap was still very large.

"Xu Tiehua is different from us."

These students saw things clearly. For them, there wasn't much to pursue in life, they were just killing time.

Naturally, they were extremely envious of hardworking geniuses like Xu Tiehua.

Of course, their conversations fell on the ears of the martial arts school staff, seeming somewhat pretentious.

These staff members, with monthly salaries of six or seven thousand, would need to save every penny for 20-30 years to buy a decent house in Tianyue City.

The gap between rich and poor in Qin Yuan country was greater than that between humans and dogs.

An ordinary person working hard their whole life might not live as well as a rich person's dog.

Qi Yuan turned a deaf ear to these voices.

As he swung the Mingwu Resonance Fist, the blood in his entire body began to simmer slightly.

Dense beads of sweat appeared on his back, then disappeared without a trace.

At this moment, Qi Yuan's Mingwu Resonance Fist entered the fourth level.

"Hmm... still a weakling with a battle power of 5."

Qi Yuan didn't notice any obvious improvement in his combat ability.

However, for an ordinary person, relying solely on the strength gained from martial arts training would be enough to handle one or two small-time thugs of equal weight.

Provided that thug didn't have a knife.

"So, I need to reach at least the seventh level to be considered... a certified martial artist?"

Not everyone who practices martial arts is considered a martial artist. To obtain a martial artist certificate, one needs to meet the requirements set by the state.

These requirements vary for different schools or martial arts gyms.

These rules are set by the Martial Arts Association.

For example, with the Mingwu Resonance Fist, one needs to reach the seventh level to be considered a martial artist and obtain a martial artist certificate.

The function of a martial artist certificate is similar to various professional certifications in the previous world of Blue Star.

To become a martial arts instructor or open a martial arts gym, one must have a martial artist certificate.

Mingwu Martial Arts School has over a hundred students, but only fifteen have martial artist certificates.

Although an ordinary person practicing diligently every day could obtain a martial artist certificate in three years,

The problem is... many people are too lazy to practice.

"At this rate, I can get a martial artist certificate in half a month?"

Qi Yuan thought to himself.

After consuming the essence blood of the mutant sea monster, Qi Yuan's martial arts aptitude had improved considerably.

His training speed had also become faster.

Plus, he was originally a cultivator.

What might take an ordinary person one or two years to achieve, he could do in half a month, which was considered normal.

After breaking through to the seventh level and becoming a true martial artist, Qi Yuan would be able to use secret items to make breakthroughs.

Why do things yourself when you can have NPCs do them?

Qi Yuan was constantly practicing martial arts, standing out from the other students present.

Not far away, Wang Shiqiao was sitting on a sofa playing with his phone.

At this time, a female voice came: "Xiao Qiao, you didn't really develop feelings for that guy, did you?"

The woman looked petite and delicate, but her body contained powerful energy. She was one of the few people in the gym with a martial artist certificate.

Wang Shiqiao glanced at Qi Yuan, who was training hard, and smiled: "What do you mean developed feelings? Besides just playing around, can't we be friends? Having such a handsome guy as a friend would be impressive to show off."

At first, Wang Shiqiao was indeed attracted by Qi Yuan's looks and had some designs on him.

Later she found Qi Yuan was different from what she imagined, not like her.

So she withdrew her intentions and treated him as an ordinary friend.

Of course, if they had some drinks one day, she wouldn't mind if something beyond friendship happened.

"It's good that you think this way. He... doesn't come from a wealthy family, he's the child of a martyr.

Coming to the martial arts school has probably used up all his savings.

Wang Shiqiao and Xing Yu had a good relationship and were dormitory roommates.

Wang Shiqiao was from a moderately wealthy family, while Xing Yu's family background was ordinary, even considered poor compared to the other girls in the dorm, coming from a small town.

Through Wang Shiqiao's connections, Xing Yu had managed to get into the bed of a billionaire and successfully became his official partner.

After serving the old man for a few years, Xing Yu's fortunes had finally turned. The old man was about to die.

Team competitions rely not only on individual strength but also on tactics.

He was currently only at the fourth level of Mingwu Resonance Fist, still several levels away from becoming a martial artist.

Without any hesitation, she drove here directly to "coincidentally" encounter him.
