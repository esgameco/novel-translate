Qi Yuan followed the staff member to get his training clothes.

At this moment, his mood was quite unsettled.

Because in that punch just now, he discovered something different.

The punch was very ordinary, not even as good as martial arts from the Cang Lan Realm.

But with Qi Yuan's observation skills that surpassed normal people, he noticed the punch seemed to conceal something.

"When executing that punch... it felt somewhat different."

When throwing that punch, Qi Yuan felt it was distinctly different from martial arts in the Cang Lan Realm and the martial ways of the Wangyue Continent.

Of course, it was just a feeling.

If he had Yin Spirit cultivation, he could examine his own body and instantly discover the internal issues.

"Hey there, handsome!"

After all, with Qi Yuan's looks, he had attracted many fangirls back in school.

Sometimes, when a man is handsome enough, he realizes how forward some girls can be.

Those who could afford to practice martial arts here must all have good family backgrounds.

These men and women were mostly outgoing in personality, with some girls boldly batting their eyelashes at Qi Yuan.

"I'm new here. Name's Qi Yuan. Please guide me in the future."

Qi Yuan said casually, then found a corner and began to study and practice the "Ming Wu Resonance Fist".

"Oh?" Qi Yuan looked at the ponytailed girl. "Have you learned it well?"

Wang Shiqiao looked at Qi Yuan and opened a window: "Look... this is the fourth floor."

Qi Yuan looked down.

From this height, an ordinary person might die if they jumped.

Could it be...? He looked at Wang Shiqiao, his eyes filled with expectation.

"Watch my punch!"

At this moment, Wang Shiqiao shouted loudly and threw a fierce punch at the sandbag next to Qi Yuan.

The sandbag, subjected to tremendous pressure, swung violently, looking quite powerful.

If it was Qi Yuan before coming to this game, already afflicted by the curse, taking such a punch would have killed him.

"Good, not bad!"

"That punch had a year's worth of power!"

The others applauded as if watching a show.

Wang Shiqiao looked at Qi Yuan and said proudly: "Was big sister's punch powerful?"

Qi Yuan looked at Wang Shiqiao with an expectant face: "Powerful. Now shouldn't you demonstrate jumping from the building?"

"Huh?" A smile appeared on Wang Shiqiao's lips. "I was just teasing you. I never said I'd jump from the fourth floor.

Even Big Senior Brother would end up in the hospital if he jumped from this fourth floor.

You didn't really think practicing the 'Ming Wu Resonance Fist' would turn you into Superman who could defeat gene warriors, did you?"

Hearing this, Qi Yuan murmured: "Is martial arts so weak?"

On the way here, Qi Yuan had already heard that the Big Senior Brother of Ming Wu Martial Arts Hall was second only to the hall master.

This person had already practiced the "Ming Wu Resonance Fist" to the eighth level, just below the hall master.

Yet he would still get injured jumping from the fourth floor.

No wonder learning martial arts was just a hobby, similar to fishing or working out on Earth.

"Martial arts isn't weak. Our bodies are much better than ordinary people's... and all natural too!" As Wang Shiqiao spoke, she deliberately thrust out her chest.

Due to technological developments and the clash of ideas on Canxing, the social atmosphere in various Gongxing countries was much more open than before.

Especially among the wealthy.

"What's natural about it?" Qi Yuan glanced at Wang Shiqiao's nose. "Your nose has been operated on."

Wang Shiqiao's expression changed slightly. She covered her nose, her face changing: "You can tell?"

"It's so obvious," Qi Yuan answered honestly.

"Damn it, it's been less than a year since I had it done, and people can already tell. What did that damn hospital promise me when they did it? Said it would look natural and undetectable. I need to go find them and complain!" Wang Shiqiao said angrily as she left, seemingly intending to confront the cosmetic surgery hospital.

Qi Yuan turned his head away, no longer paying attention, and continued to study the "Ming Wu Resonance Fist".

This set of fist techniques had both internal energy cultivation methods and external fist moves.

If it were martial arts moves from the Cang Lan Realm, Qi Yuan could fully master and execute them after just one look.

But the martial arts methods of this world were different.

Qi Yuan could indeed perform a set of "Ming Wu Resonance Fist" moves.

However, he needed to practice frequently.

He realized that executing this martial art was also a form of progress.

For others, progress might mean increased familiarity with the fist techniques.

For Qi Yuan, it was not so.

He discovered there was something deeper within.

It's just that he couldn't discern it yet at his current level.

"Fist strikes like the soul, resonating in all directions... push up and circle right..."

Qi Yuan recited silently as he continued to practice the "Ming Wu Resonance Fist".

The fist techniques flowed from Qi Yuan's hands. He focused intently, seriously practicing, as time quickly passed.

Sweat rolled down his forehead, but Qi Yuan paid no attention, continuing to execute this set of fist techniques.

In the practice room, the other students chatted and laughed, sparring or flirting. Occasionally someone's gaze would fall on Qi Yuan, but never lingered long.

Not far away, Qiao Wu looked through the glass door, scanning the entire practice room.

Finally, his gaze landed on Qi Yuan, a trace of appreciation in his eyes.

"In this era, to still be obsessed with martial arts is quite rare," Qiao Wu couldn't help but sigh.

He was the master of Ming Wu Martial Arts Hall, over fifty years old, but due to taking gene-enhancing drugs, he now stood like a wall, giving people a great sense of impact.

Second Senior Brother stood beside Qiao Wu. Looking at Qi Yuan, he couldn't help but lament: "What a pity he was born in the wrong era. If it were ancient times, with his diligence and talent, he might have become a fierce general who shook the world."

Qiao Wu nodded in agreement.

Indeed, today's society was very different from ancient times.

Martial arts had become merely an embellishment and a small circle.

Take Qiao Wu, who came from a martial arts family.

His ancestors even produced martial arts champions and generals.

But what about now? Decades of hard practice in the "Ming Wu Resonance Fist" brought him less enhancement than a single gene-enhancing drug.

"He wants to participate in the martial arts competition? He's a good seedling.

Our Ming Wu Martial Arts Hall hasn't had students participate for many years. If he participates, it might attract quite a few people to join our hall." Qiao Wu smiled and mentioned casually.
