The hidden information was revealed at this moment.

[Chen Kangbao, 67 years old, was once a Qi Master, physically weak and feeble.]

This white-haired old man, named Chen Kangbao, was actually a Qi Master.

He is now just a minor Purple Mansion cultivator, not even an Yin Deity, how could he be called a divine being?

The white-haired old man smiled: "This old man knows, divine beings who descend to the mortal realm lose all their powers and need to hide their identities! Young master, your servant..."

Chen Kangbao looked at Qi Yuan and also changed his form of address, seemingly truly wanting to hide Qi Yuan's identity.

However, at this moment, the veins on his neck were bulging, as if he wanted to say something important.

"No need to hurry, take your time," Qi Yuan said.

"Your servant... is hungry!" The white-haired old man gritted his teeth and said, and after that, Qi Yuan clearly noticed his expression becoming somewhat distorted, with black lines faintly visible in his veins.

"You really live up to your name, to be honest, I'm a bit hungry too, come, let's go to the kitchen for a late-night snack."

Qi Yuan stretched lazily.

Now that he has come to this game world, he is merely an ordinary mortal.

Even his physique is much weaker.

If he encounters a strong man, with his combat skills, he may be able to defeat the other.

But if it's a Commanding Officer or a Mysterious Spirit, with his thin arms and legs, he may not be able to.

"Let's go, young master, I know where the kitchen is!" Chen Kangbao seemed to quickly get into his role, sneakily leading the way in front, taking Qi Yuan to the kitchen under the cover of night.

The nights in the asylum were particularly pitch-black, with only a few large red lanterns hanging on the road.

The entire asylum was silent and deathly still, appearing particularly eerie.

Especially Chen Kangbao, his face was already very pale, like it was powdered, passing by the large red lanterns, his face could make the cute Sadako cry.

Along the way, Qi Yuan did see a few genuine lunatics - one hanging from a tree sleeping, one fishing in the middle of the night, clearly not normal people.

Soon, Chen Kangbao and Qi Yuan arrived outside the kitchen.

Qi Yuan paused: "Sneaking in like this, aren't we thieves?"

"Young master, the food in the asylum is meant for us to eat, we're just eating it early!" Chen Kangbao said, with drool at the corners of his mouth.

It seems he was truly hungry.

"You have a point there." Qi Yuan nodded in agreement.

The kitchen door was locked, but for Qi Yuan, opening a lock was no problem.

The two entered the kitchen, and Chen Kangbao immediately rushed towards the food like a starving ghost, frantically eating all kinds of steamed buns and cold rice.

Qi Yuan lightly drank some porridge and ate a few buns, already feeling full.

But Chen Kangbao was still eating frantically, like a glutton.

About an hour later, Qi Yuan looked at Chen Kangbao and said indifferently, "Full?"

Now Chen Kangbao's belly was round and bulging, if one didn't know, they would think he was pregnant.

"With the young master's food, hehe... six parts full." Chen Kangbao let out a burp, then said, "Young master, now that we've both eaten our fill, it's time to start... the real work!"

Chen Kangbao had a serious expression, but his pale face made him look too creepy, now speaking like a clown.

"Indeed, we must start the real work. I need to play the role of the son-in-law well, and also... the chef's knife and the Divine Arrival."

The son-in-law and the chef's knife were easy enough, but playing the Divine Arrival, Qi Yuan had no idea.

The most urgent thing now is to return to Jian'an Manor.

But now, because of "defiling" Lady Liu, he has been sent to the asylum.

There must be a conspiracy behind this.

What is the conspiracy? Qi Yuan pondered, feeling a headache. He looked at Chen Kangbao and casually asked, "Old man, are you smart?"

Hearing this, Chen Kangbao hurriedly patted his belly: "I'm very smart when I'm full!"

"Let me test you, what is ninety-seven times ninety-seven?"

"9409!" Chen Kangbao blurted out without any hesitation.

Qi Yuan's eyes lit up: "You're a talent! Old man, your intelligence is impressive! If I put this question to the clear-eyed university students on the Blue Star, they definitely wouldn't be able to solve it immediately. Give Zhuge Liang a thousand university students, and the Northern Expedition would have hope! You're stronger than university students, your brain will be very useful to me. Are you interested in being my strategist?"

Chen Kangbao drooled: "As long as there's food to eat, I'll do it!"

"Okay!" Qi Yuan heaved a sigh of relief.

When playing the game, he needs a teammate.

Chen Kangbao's quick arithmetic skills make him the intelligence pillar of the team, Qi Yuan only needs to kill and level up.

Chen Kangbao glanced at Qi Yuan and suddenly whispered, "Young master, your bird, is it on the left or the right?"

"What bird?" Qi Yuan was puzzled.

Then he realized Chen Kangbao was looking at his crotch.

He understood now, it was referring to that.

"It's just..." Chen Kangbao raised his eyebrows, "Young master, I am your strategist, you are my lord, out of respect for the esteemed, the bird cannot be on the same side as you!"

"...This kind of taboo doesn't need to be observed." Qi Yuan was speechless.

But thinking that talented people often have some quirks, Qi Yuan just accepted it.

"In the future, don't always talk in that vulgar way, it's too low-class." Qi Yuan added.

"Understood, your servant will obey!" Chen Kangbao bent his body, then turned serious, "Since the young master values me, I must also help the young master solve his troubles.

Now, the young master and I are trapped in the asylum, we need to kill the director to get out! This director, he has done all kinds of evil, his head is oozing pus!"

Mentioning the director, Chen Kangbao hated him so much that his teeth were itching, and there was a hint of fear in his eyes.

Clearly, this director had a rather bad reputation in the asylum.

"Oh, say whatever you want." Qi Yuan didn't feel like thinking.

As a qualified lord, he only needs to make decisions.

After all, his eyes, his ears, his nose, etc. can help him make the right judgments.

"I have formulated a detailed plan!" Chen Kangbao said seriously.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan's eyes lit up.

"A talent like you, with excellent qualities like me, making plans before taking action. Tell me about the plan."

Chen Kangbao, receiving the praise, showed a proud expression.

"Before making the plan, we need to understand the enemy.

Director Zhou Mo may be small in stature, but he keeps a fierce beast dog in his courtyard, extremely fierce, this dog has swallowed a strong man whole before." Chen Kangbao mentioned this with great trepidation.

A beast, Qi Yuan also knew about this kind of creature from the memories of the son-in-law Qi Yuan.

These are terrifying beasts born from ordinary animals encountering Mysterious Spirit pollution.

Most of them are bloodthirsty and carnivorous.

It can be said that even with Qi Yuan's skills, if he encounters such a beast, he would probably be no match.

The difference in strength and speed is too great, even containing supernatural powers.

"If I encounter this dog now, it would be a 30-70 chance, with me 30% dead and it 70% full." Qi Yuan said honestly.

With this thin body and limbs, it won't work.

How can a small dog defeat a big dog?

Chen Kangbao looked at Qi Yuan and asked, "Can the young master recover his spiritual powers?"
