A blood-red light hung high in the sky, making the whole world appear especially gloomy and terrifying.

The Thunder God Venerable handed his form to Qi Yuan, with a rather complex expression.

"Do you want to visualize more Divine Visualization Methods and distribute them to these ancient gods?"

Qi Yuan took the form and nodded, "Mm-hmm."

"This may be somewhat problematic."

"Oh?" Qi Yuan was surprised, "What's the problem?"

"Originally there was no problem, but after you appeared... it became an issue.

Because... you're too good at visualization!" The Thunder God Venerable said helplessly, "The Visualization Realm actually relies on the Heavenly Pillar to exist. After the Heavenly Pillar broke, the world changed greatly.

Not only can Purple Mansions not advance to True Gods, but visualizing Divine Visualization Methods in the Visualization Realm is also restricted.

It's just that before, there were too few mythical visualizations, so no one paid attention to this restriction."

The Thunder God Venerable's meaning was that Qi Yuan had visualized too much.

For example, originally the Visualization Realm could visualize 100 Divine Visualization Methods.

People from the Mortal Heart Realm could at most visualize 50, so no one would care about this restriction.

Qi Yuan appeared and directly visualized 50, and even wanted to visualize more.

So, the restriction came into effect.

"The current Visualization Realm can probably only support five mythical-level Divine Visualization Methods," the Thunder God Venerable continued, "Try to leave some for the Mythical Plan... for them."

The Thunder God Venerable knew about the Mythical Plan.

He neither supported nor opposed the Mythical Plan.

But now, those chosen ones of the Mythical Plan had only visualized 2 mythical-level Visualization Methods so far.

"There's such a restriction?" Qi Yuan was not anxious.

In fact, this restriction was like Purple Mansions being unable to break through to True Gods.

He could directly use the Lost Fluorescent Rod, taking the Venerables and people to travel through time and space back to the past.

The last time he went back to the past, Qi Yuan had a sudden idea to try visualizing.

He found he could enter the Visualization Realm.

This also meant that he could take all the villagers and ancient gods to the past, first to visualize, and then break through to True Gods.

In the past before the Heavenly Pillar broke, there should not be such restrictions.

The Thunder God Venerable looked at Qi Yuan, his eyes complex: "So... even if you've seen True Gods outside the Mortal Heart Realm, no matter how many or how strong those True Gods are, they cannot save the Mortal Heart Realm.

I don't need grand gifts, nor do I need powerful Divine Visualization Methods."

The Thunder God Venerable's voice was somewhat wistful, filled with endless hesitation.

For him, Qi Yuan being born in the Mortal Heart Realm was a mistake.

Such an astonishingly brilliant talent, if placed in other worlds, might have stepped above True Gods.

But now, he could only be confined to the Mortal Heart Realm, without any hope.

"Old Thunder, you really underestimate me.

Although this game is full of restrictions, afraid players will clear it too easily.

But I can cheat, you think these little restrictions can stop me?

Just watch how I go into god mode and directly push through the Mortal Heart Realm!" Qi Yuan said confidently.

The restrictions the Thunder God Venerable mentioned were nothing to Qi Yuan.

The only flaw was that the Lost Fluorescent Rod only had two uses left.

He couldn't visualize whenever he wanted.

He needed to make a meticulous plan.

The Thunder God Venerable's beard twitched: "I praise you a few words and you get cocky?

Push through the Mortal Heart Realm? Forget about the Above True Gods in the Heavenly Pillar, how will you deal with just those mythical-level Evil Gods? I just got news that the vanguard under the Evil God Army Commander of Qingheng Continent is already gathering.

These Evil Gods are converging on Sunset Slope.

The Evil God forces are stronger than imagined.

A full thirty-four Venerables, and over a thousand ordinary Evil Gods.

This time, their target is not just us, they even want to sweep through all the ancient gods of the entire Qingheng Continent."

As the Thunder God Venerable said this, the worry on his face grew heavier.

Originally they had guessed there would be ten Venerables.

But it turned out to be a full thirty - the Evil God Army Commander was determined to completely sweep through Qingheng Continent.

"Sunset Slope, nice name." Qi Yuan's voice was quite delighted, his eyes shining, "Thirty-four Venerables, over a thousand Evil Gods, this is too good to be true."

It was truly a case of a pillow being sent when one was sleepy.

He had been wondering, with the Lost Fluorescent Rod only usable twice, he needed to gather more Evil God spirit power to go back to the past together.

As a result, so many Evil Gods came.

The Thunder God Venerable looked at Qi Yuan: "Have you gone mad?"

So many Evil Gods were enough to sweep through all the new gods of Qingheng Continent.

Even if the ancient gods from the Southern Lands came to help, it would be too little too late.

Those Evil Gods wouldn't let them enter in large numbers.

"I think you should just go to bed!" The Thunder God Venerable continued. He didn't mention the Evil God invasion again.

He felt it was like playing the lute to a cow.

He thought of something and asked: "Don't you usually sleep at night? Why aren't you sleeping this time?"

Since coming to Qingshui Village, the Thunder God Venerable had found Qi Yuan to be very strange.

He did strange things, and still needed to sleep at night.

Sleeping was fine, but he ended up sleeping hanging from trees, which was quite creepy.

"Aren't you very curious why I have the confidence to be so reckless?

Even facing the Evil God allied army, I remain unmoved as a mountain." At this point, Qi Yuan spoke with a serious face.

The Thunder God Venerable was silent for a moment, because it was rare to see Qi Yuan so serious: "I am somewhat curious."

"I have a secret to tell you, don't tell anyone else." Qi Yuan said secretly.

The Thunder God Venerable's heart was unsettled.

The Origin Venerable clearly looked like someone with many secrets.

And not just small secrets.

Otherwise, who could visualize eight mythical-level Divine Visualization Methods out of nowhere?

Now, this secret was going to be told to him?

He felt inexplicably nervous: "This old man swears, I will not tell anyone else."

He looked at Qi Yuan expectantly, very curious about Qi Yuan's secret.

"Actually, I've formed a very powerful Golden Core." Qi Yuan's tone was very calm, but he was gloating inside.

It was finally time to show off his Star Golden Core again.

"Hm?" The Thunder God Venerable was a bit confused.

A powerful Golden Core? What nonsense! No matter how powerful, isn't it still just a tiny Golden Core?

He had been a True God for so many years, Golden Cores meant nothing to him.

No matter how powerful, so what?

"My Golden Core is a star. Look up at the sky, see that? That's my Golden Core!" Qi Yuan pointed at the Star Golden Core in the sky, outwardly calm but inwardly showing off.

The Thunder God Venerable couldn't hold back: "Are you mocking this old man?"

"Old Thunder, you're short-sighted." Qi Yuan sighed, "As they say, once people reach middle and old age, they can't accept new things."

Qi Yuan had once read a passage saying that people all have nostalgia bonuses.

Things encountered in youth are all considered the best of the best in their hearts.

But when they get older, they become especially resistant to new things.

"You call this a new thing? No matter how new, how can a Golden Core be a star?" The Thunder God Venerable almost cursed at Qi Yuan - was he toying with him like this?

"You don't believe me?"

"Do you think anyone would believe this?"

"Fine, I'm planning to form another Golden Core. Open your eyes wide and watch carefully. In a moment, there will be another small sun in the sky."
