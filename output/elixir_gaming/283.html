Wang Xu touched his glasses. With his tall stature, he was the center of attention among the three.

The Qin Yuan Kingdom's maritime hegemony was unrivaled under heaven, and maritime trade was flourishing, so a job at the Maritime Affairs Bureau was highly sought after.

Such a respectable job naturally made other classmates envious.

Hearing Wang Xu's words, a smile appeared in her eyes: "I was young and foolish then, but now..."

With such a cautionary tale from that roommate, how could Li Yan really want to have anything to do with Qi Yuan.

A one night stand was fine, but marriage was out of the question.

Otherwise she would be laughed to death.

"Mm, I'm sick." Qi Yuan answered truthfully.

He looked at the three classmates before him, memories flooding back like a tide.

When the primordial seed was still there, they had also come to see Qi Yuan.

Once he started cultivating immortality, would these issues still be issues?

Of course, he couldn't solve the curse-like problem with his body for now, but becoming a normal person and delaying the curse's recurrence was doable.

"How is this a small issue, look at your complexion!" Li Yan couldn't help saying, her eyes reddening as if very sad for Qi Yuan.

Although she looked down on Qi Yuan now, she couldn't bear to see him pass away at such a young age.

"Li Yan, you're thinking too much. Qi Yuan can stand up and talk to us now, so it shouldn't be a big problem." The buzz cut guy said.

Indeed, if it was really serious, he would be lying in a hospital bed.

The fact that he could stand up and talk meant the problem wasn't too big.

Although this kind of pain wasn't much to him, he didn't have masochistic tendencies either, so he naturally did whatever was most comfortable.

Wang Xu revealed his purpose.

Currently, he was in a probationary period at the Maritime Affairs Bureau.

Helping someone like Qi Yuan, the child of a martyr, would earn him a lot of bonus points.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan was stunned: "But don't I have a mental illness? Do people with mental illnesses need to find jobs too?"

Would any company dare hire someone with a mental illness? Was Gongxing so competitive that even the mentally ill needed to find work? Wang Xu was stunned for a moment.

Because this was the first time he had heard someone with a mental illness matter-of-factly ask if they needed to work too.

It sounded particularly strange.

Li Yan was also stunned.

But thinking about it, she understood. Having a mental illness didn't mean being stupid, it just meant having some issues with one's thoughts.

It will be very difficult to get married and have children.

For children of martyrs, the Qin Yuan Kingdom had many protections.

Not only were there various subsidies, but matchmaking services were also arranged.

After all, what woman would want to take on such a burden?
