Zi Yuan Zu stood outside the celestial curtain, his expression extremely complex.

"What could this mean?" he pondered, quite perplexed.

After all, for the star projection to become "female" was too strange.

The star projection was supposed to be the essence of the yang spirit.

How could it inexplicably turn into the character for "woman"? Could even the supreme truth accomplish this?

It was unheard of.

"Could there be a supreme being behind this Blood Robe? Who could it be?" Zi Yuan Zu pondered.

Suddenly, he thought of something.

"Could it be... the Moon Spirit Sovereign?"

"This feat... does fit with the Moon Spirit Sovereign!"

"But wasn't the Moon Spirit Sovereign gravely injured?"

"Could it be that with the Lin Ya Molt approaching, she's showing her hand, telling the world that her injuries... have healed?"

Zi Yuan Zu could only try to convince himself this way.

Blood Robe's relationships in the Sixth Heaven were simple.

He came from the Demon Luo clan and fell in love with the Moon Woman.

So it was very likely he had also met the Moon Spirit Sovereign before.

"No wonder this fellow said he wanted to become dao partners with the Moon Woman back then, and even... probed my thoughts on the Moon Spirit Palace."

Thinking of this, Zi Yuan Zu felt the doubts in his heart resolve.

Since the Moon Spirit Sovereign had intervened, Blood Robe would surely be fine.

Realizing this, his mood improved.

"This guy, making me go beg at Black Magic Mountain and lose face. When he returns, I'll have to teach him a good lesson!" Zi Yuan Zu muttered.

Suddenly, he sensed something and his eyes lit up with joy.

His form vanished in that moment.

When he reappeared, he landed outside the teleportation formation.

The surrounding space froze for a moment as Zi Yuan Zu looked calmly at Qi Yuan in his blood-red robe.

"You lucky fellow, you actually managed to come back alive."

His words were laced with irrepressible joy.

"Sigh, old man, it's too dangerous out there," Qi Yuan said, terrified. "When you go out, there are too many men staring, and slanderous shrimp-headed men. It's simply impossible to guard against!"

Remembering Divine Blood Venerable's gaze, Qi Yuan became very angry.

It was simply killing harassment!

"The Sixth Heaven was never a blessed land, it's extremely perilous. You should understand that now, right?" Zi Yuan Zu said sternly. "Will you still be so bold as to go out recklessly and act willfully in the future?"

"Old man, how can you blame the victim? Just because I dress revealingly, does that mean they can stare at me?"

"Hmph, I'm just telling you, a gentleman doesn't stand beneath a dangerous wall.

Now, with the Lin Ya Molt approaching, the major holy lands will be stirred up. You should go out less, focus on cultivating.

Improve your strength, gain more ability to protect yourself!" Zi Yuan Zu advised earnestly.

Qi Yuan nodded: "What you say makes sense, but... I need to go out once. Moon Radiance Venerable has agreed to let me marry Moon Woman and bring her back to Purple Fate Heaven!"

Hearing this, Zi Yuan Zu's expression tightened.

He became even more certain of his guess.

Indeed, the one behind Blood Robe was the Moon Spirit Sovereign.

"When?" Zi Yuan Zu asked.

"I'll find an auspicious date?" Qi Yuan pondered.

"This time, I'll go with you to Moon Spirit Palace!" Zi Yuan Zu said calmly.

Since there was danger, he would accompany him.

Unless the Banner Master of Ghost Dominion Heaven personally took action, no one could harm him.

Even the Banner Master could at most injure him.

And Ghost Dominion Heaven didn't understand what Blood Robe represented, so they wouldn't use the same level of force against Blood Robe as they would against Black Sword.

Thinking of something, Qi Yuan said: "This matter... Black Magic Abyss won't try to stop it, right?"

Although Qi Yuan didn't like to use his brain, he certainly wasn't stupid.

Now, with the Lin Ya Molt approaching.

The chief of the Five Demons of Purple Fate Heaven marrying a Moon Woman from Moon Spirit Palace.

This couldn't help but make people speculate.

Was Black Magic Abyss going to ally with Moon Spirit Palace?

Or even some forces within Black Magic Abyss, like Ghost Soul Heaven which was at war with Moon Spirit Palace, might try to stop it.

Hearing this, Zi Yuan Zu's expression remained calm: "This matter... is Purple Fate Heaven's business. Others... have no say in it!"

Some time ago, he had gone to Black Magic Mountain to seek an audience with his master, to rescue Blood Robe.

But he was met with a closed door.

He was thoroughly disappointed with that master.

Could it be said that he, Zi Yuan Zu, had failed Black Magic Abyss in all these years?

He had entered the Strange Lands seven times for Black Magic Abyss, fought alien races, reclaimed the Black Magic Ancestor's blood and placed it in the ancestral grounds.

To pave the way for Black Sword, he had also depleted his vital energy four times to refine great pills.

Not to mention other things.

But when he sought an audience with his master to help Blood Robe, his master wouldn't even see him.

His heart was filled with anger.

"This won't make you offend others, will it?" Qi Yuan asked carefully, being emotionally intelligent. "I've long heard that you old man don't get along with the Lord of Black Magic Abyss."

It was no secret among the upper echelons that Zi Yuan Zu and the Lord of Black Magic Abyss didn't get along.

There were both unofficial histories and obscure facts circulating.

Anyway, it was very bizarre.

For example, an unofficial history claimed the Lord of Black Magic Abyss had homosexual tendencies and took a liking to his disciple Zi Yuan Zu, wanting to engage in public acts with Zi Yuan Zu, but Zu refused.

And an obscure fact claimed Zi Yuan Zu fell in love with his master's wife, coveted her, drugged her, and only realized after waking up that he had slept with the wrong person - he had slept with the Lord of Black Magic Abyss.

Zi Yuan Zu's expression was complex as long-buried memories surged: "It's nothing more than our paths not aligning, so we can't work together.

These are small matters. If I can't even decide your marriage, then there's no point in staying in this Black Magic Abyss.

Not to mention..."

A smile appeared in Zi Yuan Zu's eyes.

"Didn't you say marriage should be free? What business is your wedding to them? If they want to meddle, they can stay away from my Purple Fate Heaven!"

Zi Yuan Zu had made up his mind.

In the past, he had been too weak, always yielding.

So in Black Magic Abyss, he was always the one to suffer losses and hardships.

The benefits never fell to him.
