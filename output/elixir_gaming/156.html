The tall man was a great cultivator at the late Purple Palace stage, who had always served Venerable Yiqiong.

After receiving Wind Orchid's message, he was furious.

In the Heaven and Earth Cave, gusts of gloomy wind blew.

A childish voice came: "What do you think should be done?"

Hearing this, the tall man frowned in thought: "First, there's Wind Lord. Second, the great calamity is approaching. The Venerable Ones need to preserve their strength and can't act against the Blood Robe Sword God."

He was pondering how to deal with the Blood Robe Sword God.

Could it be that they really had to let the Blood Robe Sword God succeed, disrupt the Eastern Land Genius Battle, and enter the Wangyue Continent? Suddenly, the tall man's brows twitched and his eyes lit up with joy: "Just let him obtain a spot in the top 20 of the Eastern Land Genius Rankings.

The Blood Robe Sword God is definitely doing this to enter the Wangyue Continent.

The Heavenly Dao of the Wangyue Continent is broken, and its powerful beings have fallen. Not only do we from the Canglan Realm have designs on them, but the Moluo Clan does as well.

The Moluo Clan has left behind a supreme treasure that can sense any cultivator at the Fifth Realm or above.

Once the Blood Robe Sword God enters the Wangyue Continent, he will definitely be discovered by the Moluo Clan. With the Moluo Clan's arrogant and unruly nature, they will certainly kill the Blood Robe Sword God.

The Blood Robe Sword God will certainly perish upon entering the Wangyue Continent!"

The current Wangyue Continent was in a three-way standoff.

The weakest were naturally the natives of the Wangyue Continent.

The strongest was the Moluo Clan.

Even the three Venerable Ones were extremely wary of the Yin God of the Moluo Clan.

The Blood Robe Sword God entering the Wangyue Continent would be like a lamb entering a tiger's den.

There was a moment of silence in the Heaven and Earth Cave. After a long while, the childish voice came again: "Let everything be as you said."

Hearing this, the tall man's eyes were filled with pleasant surprise.

At this time, another childish voice came from the Heaven and Earth Cave.

"If convenient, investigate his background."

"Based on the clues obtained so far, we can basically determine that the Blood Robe Sword God, the Mad Daoist, and the Seven Desires Demon Lord are one and the same person.

They all came from the remote Hundred Countries." The tall man lowered his head to report, "No more information could be found. According to this subordinate's speculation, the Blood Robe Sword God may... not be a being from the Canglan Realm."

The Heaven and Earth Cave fell silent again, with only the howling of the gloomy wind audible.

"Investigate which other members of the Divine Flower Meeting are from the Hundred Countries."

After a long while, the childish voice came again.

"As you command!" The tall man bowed his head, but a troubled expression appeared on his face.

This matter would be difficult to investigate.

In Cloud City, Wind Orchid sat on pins and needles, but kept a smile on his face.

"Senior Blood Robe Sword God, why are you so obsessed with entering the Wangyue Continent? It would be better to casually join some forces of geniuses and obtain the qualification to develop territory in the Wangyue Continent."

Hearing this, Qi Yuan said lazily: "The Wangyue Continent is my home, I need to go back."

He had taken the Heavenly Dao of the Wangyue Continent to build his foundation.

Wasn't the Wangyue Continent his home now? Hearing this, Wind Orchid laughed awkwardly.

The power level of the Wangyue Continent was not high enough to support a powerful expert like the Blood Robe Sword God.

So naturally, he thought the Blood Robe Sword God was joking.

"When I enter the top 20, when will I be qualified to enter the Wangyue Continent? I'm in a hurry." Qi Yuan was eager to enter the Wangyue Continent.

He wanted to see if that Wangyue Continent was the world he had once stayed in.

He also wanted to know if there was a chance to see again the woman he had never met before.

"After the Eastern Land Genius Battle ends, the entrance to the Wangyue Continent will open briefly. Senior can enter at that time."

"Good." Qi Yuan nodded.

At this moment, Wind Orchid's communication jade slip suddenly lit up.

Qi Yuan's eyes stared at him: "A message came?"

Wind Orchid was a bit nervous and didn't dare to look.

He was afraid that the Venerable Ones above would have a big reaction, not only disagreeing with the Blood Robe Sword God, but also being arrogant and angering the Blood Robe Sword God.

The Blood Robe Sword God wouldn't dare to kill a Venerable One, but he would dare to kill him! His divine sense entered the jade slip, and his eyes revealed a look of pleasant surprise: "Blood Robe Sword God, those above have already agreed to let you obtain a spot in the top 20 of the Eastern Land Genius Rankings without testing, and also obtain the qualification to enter the Wangyue Continent."

Wind Orchid breathed a sigh of relief.

He was just afraid that those above wouldn't agree to this matter.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan smiled broadly: "Those above you are quite tolerant and magnanimous. When they have time, I'll treat them to a meal."

Wind Orchid laughed awkwardly.

He definitely wouldn't pass these words up to those above.

He knew about the incident where the Blood Robe Sword God had provoked Venerable Yiqiong at the Divine Flower Meeting some time ago.

If the two were to meet, there would certainly be a fight.

"Now that I've entered the top 20 of the Eastern Land Genius Rankings, do I have a chance to choose the rewards for geniuses?" Qi Yuan looked at Wind Orchid and asked.

Qi Yuan remembered the Sect Master's instructions very clearly.

Great Cultivator Wind Orchid was stunned for a moment, suspecting that there was something wrong with his ears.

The top 100 of the Eastern Land Genius Rankings indeed had rewards.

The rewarded treasures were mostly some powerful divine infant magical treasures.

For young geniuses, they had some use, but what use were they for a powerful expert like the Blood Robe Sword God? "Of course." Though puzzled in his heart, Great Cultivator Wind Orchid didn't dare to hesitate. He waved his hand, and a storage bag flew from the depths of the palace into his hand.

"Blood Robe Sword God can choose any one or two of these magical treasures as you wish."

When the storage bag opened, a dazzling array of magical treasures appeared.

If it were Qi Yuan at the Qi Condensation realm, he would have been dazzled by these.

Now that he had reached the Fixed Star Golden Core, he didn't care for these.

He looked at that purple-gold small gourd: "This one... I'll take it."

This small gourd was the treasure that the Sect Master had instructed Qi Yuan to obtain.

"Senior truly has a good eye." Great Cultivator Wind Orchid carefully examined this small gourd and discovered its hidden secrets, "This gourd is actually a Purple Palace magical artifact."

After saying this, Great Cultivator Wind Orchid regretted it.

An ordinary Purple Palace magical artifact wouldn't mean much to the Blood Robe Sword God.

The fierce person who destroyed the Light Palace wouldn't lack these.

At this time, the usually quiet Cloud Courtyard Lord looked at the purple-gold gourd and boldly asked: "May I ask, Senior, do you have any connection with this gourd?"

"Mm." Qi Yuan looked at him and said, "This is a treasure from an elder's clan, now... it should return to its rightful owner."

"I see." The Cloud Courtyard Lord continued, "Could Senior leave a contact method?"

"This." Qi Yuan didn't refuse, and casually showed a Purple Palace communication jade slip to the Cloud Courtyard Lord.

"All the best. Courtyard Lord, Wind Orchid, Booger Immortal, farewell." Qi Yuan turned into a streak of blood and disappeared.

Seeing this, Wind Orchid breathed a sigh of relief.

The Cloud Courtyard Lord's gaze was deep, lost in thought about something.

Wind Moon Immortal had a wronged expression, speechless.

...

When he returned to the cave dwelling, Kong Fulu's figure had already disappeared.

Jiang Ya was still standing on the Bodhi tree, pondering cultivation techniques.

Qi Yuan looked at Jiang Ya and called out: "I've successfully used connections, so I won't participate in the Eastern Land Genius Battle.

I'm going into closed cultivation for seven days to study cultivation techniques!"

Jiang Ya looked at Qi Yuan with a puzzled expression: "Alright."

With Qi Yuan's strength, participating in the Eastern Land Genius Battle would indeed make him cannon fodder, so it was better not to participate.

After Qi Yuan entered the cave dwelling, he hung another sign outside.

"In closed cultivation for seven days, do not disturb. Violators will be killed!"

Concerning the Dao of the Golden Core, he naturally took it seriously.

He even placed the kitchen knife and the blood-colored divine soul sword inside the door to guard.

Two days later, Kong Fulu looked at the writing outside Qi Yuan's cave dwelling with a strange expression. He couldn't help but sigh: "Junior Brother's talent... is even stronger than mine. What a pity, if this Eastern Land Genius Battle hadn't been brought forward, he might really have had a chance to enter the top 100 of the Eastern Land Genius Rankings and retrieve the Sect Master's clan treasure."

But now, Junior Brother hadn't even formed a Golden Core. Participating in the Eastern Land Genius Battle would be completely suicidal.

Jiang Ya's expression was calm: "Let's go, the Eastern Land Genius Battle has begun."

The two turned into streaks of light and headed towards the depths of Cloud Courtyard.

In the mountains, a secret realm opened, light flowed in all directions, and the spiritual energy in the air also became abundant.

Jiang Ya looked at the young geniuses ahead, her gaze also becoming solemn.

Kong Fulu frowned: "Sigh, my titles as the son-in-law of the Divine Light Sect Master and the closed-door disciple of the Five Light Peak Master won't be of much use here."

He sensed the auras of too many powerful beings.

Looking around, there were a lot he couldn't defeat.

His pride in being a first-grade Golden Core seemed ordinary here.

"Sigh, Junior Brother was right. If my Golden Core was bigger, I wouldn't feel so inferior."

He stood among the crowd, quite inconspicuous.

At this moment, a vast voice entered everyone's ears.

"For this Eastern Land Genius Battle, the rules have changed.

For the top 20, there are only 19 spots. Everyone... don't get it wrong."

All the geniuses who heard this had slight changes in their expressions.

"The top 20... only have 19 spots? What does that mean!"

"For such a grand event as the Eastern Land Genius Battle, someone actually blatantly used connections!"

"It's really despicable!"

"It's really enviable!"

The various geniuses discussed among themselves.

Those who were originally ranked in the twenties had particularly ugly expressions. With one less spot, the competition pressure for them increased.
