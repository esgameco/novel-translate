The Long-Browed Arhat beside him quickly followed suit, his eyes full of longing for the Linmu Immortal Liquid.

The villagers behind were the same.

For those who weren't true gods, drinking the Linmu Immortal Liquid still had many benefits - improving cultivation and enhancing aptitude.

"Drink, all of you have a mouthful," Qi Yuan didn't stop them.

If people wanted to drink bath water, he couldn't be bothered to stop them.

Seeing this, the villagers on the scene rushed forward frantically, reaching their hands into the bath water and cupping it in their palms.

The village chief Yu Lu's aged face showed an entranced expression as he sniffed the strange fragrance of the Linmu Immortal Liquid.

A group of true gods, Purple Palace cultivators, Divine Infant cultivators, and Yuan Dan cultivators drank the bath water with intoxicated looks in their eyes.

"It's a pity I can't livestream this, it would definitely be interesting," Qi Yuan muttered.

However, he still secretly took out a recording stone and recorded this scene.

Who knows, after endless time passes, these people might all become big shots.

When they discover how obsessed they were with bath water back then, it would definitely be interesting.

His strength instantly surged.

The other villagers also became stronger to varying degrees.

The various treasures in the Linmu Immortal Liquid nourished their bodies. The villagers' complexions became ruddy and their bodies more upright.

"Not bad, just the first tower yielded so much," Qi Yuan was naturally excited that Happy Arhat's strength had increased.

As mentioned before, this ancestral land had seven levels and over a hundred towers.

If just the bath water from the first tower was this powerful, if there were even better things later, their overall strength would certainly increase significantly.

"Take these few tons of bath water with us, let's go to the next tower," Qi Yuan commanded.

The villagers present didn't know why Qi Yuan called the Linmu Immortal Liquid bath water, but they didn't mind.

They just treated it as a pronunciation issue of Qi Yuan's.

After all, how could such precious immortal liquid be bath water?

How could anyone use so much precious immortal liquid to bathe?

"Alright!" Happy Arhat happily took out a storage bag and filled it completely with the Linmu Immortal Liquid.

Qi Yuan swept his gaze over the tower. Inside were metal scraps everywhere.

All kinds of broken, strange objects.

To him, these things were almost useless.

"Let's go to the second tower!" Qi Yuan waved his hand.

The villagers present followed Qi Yuan and left the tower.

At this time, outside the tower, a group of ancient gods were still quietly waiting. They were worried and on edge, ready to step in and rescue at any moment.

"It's been so long without any movement, what's going on?" Yan Xi Venerable asked.

"It's really strange!"

They couldn't help but find it odd.

The tower was full of dangers, and could trigger the puppet gods to awaken at any time.

They had been inside for over a hundred breaths, but there was still no movement, which was very rare.

"Could it be that he really can tell fortunes?" the twin sister said in surprise.

The ancient gods present smiled when they heard this, not taking it seriously.

The matter of fate is most unpredictable.

Even fortune-telling masters would die inside the ancestral land towers.

Because the things and rules inside the ancestral land towers were more chaotic compared to the mortal realm.

Just then, a voice suddenly came: "Why are you all here?"

The ancient gods present immediately looked up towards the source of the voice.

They saw Qi Yuan open something like a glass window at the top of the tower and jump out from inside.

The other villagers also imitated Qi Yuan, jumping out from the window.

These villagers jumped out of the tower as easily as an adulterous man jumping out of a woman's house.

The ancient gods present were stunned.

They...

The twin sister spoke frankly: "How did you enter the highest level!"

To be able to come out from the highest level, they must have entered the highest level!

The tower before them had three levels in total.

Many ancient gods had shed blood on the first level, and no ancient god had set foot on the second level until now.

How did these two true gods and a bunch of mortals enter the highest level and walk out from inside! Were the puppet gods inside sleeping in a daze?

The ancient gods present were shocked.

Qi Yuan and the others landed beside everyone: "I can tell fortunes, so naturally I found a way to survive.

It's a pity that this tower doesn't have any good treasures, only a bunch of bath water."

Qi Yuan said leisurely.

The first tower indeed didn't have anything good.

"Bath water? There's actually bath water placed in the tower?"

The ancient gods present looked at each other, quite puzzled by Qi Yuan's words.

"Only bath water?"

"No, whose bath water?"

Seeing Qi Yuan and the others come out like this, many thoughts flashed through their minds.

Either Qi Yuan was lucky, or he had something special, or the tower malfunctioned.

In any case, this made them envious and jealous.

After all, Yan Xi Venerable had entered this first level tower before.

Unfortunately, he only entered for one breath before being chased by a group of puppet gods, and ended up leaving his thigh in the tower.

That time, he worked so hard and suffered heavy losses, only managing to snatch a thin piece of golden gauze from the tower, which he used as a mask since it could conceal auras.

His hard work formed a stark contrast with Qi Yuan's ease.

They felt unbalanced in their hearts.

But when they heard it was bath water, they felt balanced again.

This didn't sound like any treasure.

At this moment, the twin sister suddenly looked at the crowd behind Qi Yuan: "They all seem to have been reborn, especially..."

She looked at Happy Arhat, sensing that his strength had reached another level.

To improve so much in such a short time!

There must be supreme treasures!

"Linmu Immortal Liquid, this old man smells the scent of Linmu Immortal Liquid!" A man with a large mole between his eyebrows looked at the villagers behind Qi Yuan, his eyes shining.

"Linmu Immortal Liquid, I smell it too!"

"It's actually Linmu Immortal Liquid!" Some ancient gods' eyes turned red.

Linmu Immortal Liquid was a supreme treasure even for true gods below the Great Venerable level.

This immortal liquid not only tasted delicious, but also emitted a strange fragrance, truly a delicacy of the mortal world.

Most importantly, after drinking one drop, the speed of condensing divine apertures would increase fivefold for ten years.

If a true god hadn't broken through for a long time, drinking a few drops could even give them a chance to break through.

Such precious Linmu Immortal Liquid, yet... Qi Yuan directly gave it to those mortals to drink!

Based on the scent they smelled, and they drank more than just one or two drops.

What a waste!

Pearls before swine!

The ancient gods present felt heartbroken.

The effects such a treasure would bring them would definitely far exceed giving it to Yuan Dan and Divine Infant mortals.

"You mean this bath water? It is indeed quite extraordinary. Shen Tu has always been skinny, but after drinking it, he's put on some meat.

If he likes drinking bath water in the future, he should drink a bowl every day, otherwise being so weak and frail following me around, people might misunderstand that I like men."

Generally speaking, Qi Yuan didn't care about others' comments about him.

He was always magnanimous, at most he would just chop people up offline.

But saying he liked men was something he couldn't accept.

He was a married man after all.

"Bath water? Linmu Immortal Liquid?"

"Drink a bowl every day..."

The ancient gods present were completely lost.

For a Yuan Dan cultivator, this was too wasteful.

He'll burst soon!

Some ancient gods thought darkly in their hearts.

"May I ask, friend, how much Linmu Immortal Liquid did you obtain?" the twin sister asked seriously, her eyes gleaming.

"Not much, just over ten thousand catties," Qi Yuan said casually.
