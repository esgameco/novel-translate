The leader of Cloud Palace fell silent.

He didn't quite understand.

What did the Dragon King mean by "asking for a crooked mouth"?

No wonder the white moonlight always remained white moonlight, unable to become official - the age gap was too big.

He said, looking at Huang Tu beside him: "Elder, could you do me a favor and spare this young man's life?"

At this moment, deep fear filled Huang Tu's eyes.

How could this man in blood-red robes possibly be the Blood Sword God? The Blood Sword God he viewed as a nightmare and wanted to devour the flesh and crush the bones of was right beside him, yet he didn't dare harbor any resentful thoughts, his body trembling.

Because the Blood Sword God was truly capable of killing.

"Why should I do you a favor? He wanted to kill me, so I'll kill him. It's only natural," Qi Yuan said calmly, glancing at Huang Tu.

The geniuses around Huang Tu felt as if they had fallen into an ice cellar. They all moved away from him, treating him like the plague.

"Elder, if you kill Huang Tu, I'm afraid... it will be very difficult to reconcile with the Palace of Light," the Cloud Palace leader said. "This Genius Battle is crucial, and Huang Tu is the Palace of Light's only hope of entering the top twenty."

"Oh, he's from the Palace of Light? And so important to them?" Qi Yuan was surprised.

No wonder Huang Tu looked familiar - he was a descendant of the white moonlight.

"More important than a Purple Mansion cultivator, even... comparable to a Palace Lord," the Cloud Palace leader emphasized Huang Tu's importance.

He seemed to want to protect Huang Tu.

Hoping the Blood Sword God would spare Huang Tu's life because of this.

Qi Yuan was speechless. Could the people of the Palace of Light not survive without Huang Tu? Couldn't they watch videos?

A cold smile appeared on Qi Yuan's face: "Then he must be killed even more."

His hand flicked, sending a spell flying towards Huang Tu.

The Cloud Palace leader's expression changed slightly, wanting to block it.

Unfortunately, his strength was far inferior to the Blood Sword God's. He simply couldn't block Qi Yuan's attack.

Even if he touched it, he would be injured.

As the spell landed, Huang Tu's eyes froze.

His body went limp and he collapsed to the ground, all life force gone.

The Cloud Palace leader's face looked particularly grim.

The geniuses present were all somewhat terrified.

This Blood Sword God was truly beyond common sense.

He really killed geniuses!

He wasn't afraid of being criticized for bullying the weak at all.

With Huang Tu's death, the Cloud Palace leader sighed: "Elder, why must you make relations with the Palace of Light so tense?"

Qi Yuan ignored the Cloud Palace leader, instead clapping his hands and looking at the Rogue Immortal Lord: "Another one dead, your ranking on the Genius List has improved again. How far are you from the official list? Do you want me to kill some more?"

Hearing this, those geniuses were frightened and their faces changed greatly. They all took several steps back.

This Blood Sword God must be crazy, right?

The Rogue Immortal Lord was completely dumbfounded at this point. He squeezed out a smile uglier than crying: "No... no need."

Beside him, Gao Jien stood still, not daring to move at all.

If not for the two Purple Mansion cultivators here, he would have definitely gone to ask his junior brother what was going on with the Blood Sword God.

The Cloud Palace leader looked at Qi Yuan and slowly said: "Actually, the Palace of Light had some words they wanted this junior to pass on to Elder."

"You're the Palace of Light's lapdog?" A murderous aura swept out from Qi Yuan's body.

This was the white moonlight after all, valuable.

"Not at all." Sweat formed on the Cloud Palace leader's forehead. Just now he had sensed a crisis of death. "I have no relationship with the Palace of Light."

Hearing this, the murderous aura on Qi Yuan's body disappeared.

"Elder, may I have a word in private?" the Cloud Palace leader spoke up at this time.

In public, some things were ultimately not good to transmit via voice.

"Mm, alright, two words is fine too." Qi Yuan nodded, looking at the Rogue Immortal Lord beside him, "Rogue Immortal Lord, when the Fish Dragon Golden Core Dance starts later, remember to record it for me, thanks."

After Qi Yuan finished speaking, he disappeared with the Cloud Palace leader.

Only leaving behind a group of geniuses with faces full of awe and shock.

The Rogue Immortal Lord sat blankly in place, his mind a bit dazed.

The other geniuses all looked at him, their eyes filled with envy.

At this time, Gao Jien hurriedly transmitted: "Junior brother, what's going on exactly? What Blood Sword God? What Palace of Light? Should we run away now and quickly return to Thunder State?"

"Senior brother... I'm actually not very clear either." The Rogue Immortal Lord's mind was still a bit chaotic.

Meanwhile, in the cave dwelling, a faint incense was lit, adding a hint of immortal charm to the entire mansion.

Qi Yuan wore a blood-red robe, the bloody aura on his body permeating. He looked at the Cloud Palace leader: "Speak, what's the matter?"

"Sigh, Elder shouldn't have killed Huang Tu. This has thoroughly offended the Palace of Light," the Cloud Palace leader said.

"I've already offended them before," Qi Yuan said indifferently.

He was going to destroy the Palace of Light anyway, so why worry about offending them? What was there to fear about offending dead people?

Even in this immortal cultivation world, he had completely annihilated his enemies in body and spirit, so they couldn't possibly become ghosts.

"Elder may not know how important it is for the Palace of Light to enter the top twenty in this Eastern Land Genius Battle," the Cloud Palace leader said.

"Isn't it just to gain the qualification to enter a new world?" Qi Yuan was surprised.

The Cloud Palace leader was slightly stunned: "Since Elder knows this, shouldn't you know what it means?"

"Oh? What does it mean?" Qi Yuan looked confused. "Don't give me riddles, speak clearly."

The Cloud Palace leader's heart skipped a beat.

It seemed the Blood Sword God really didn't know.

But... shouldn't all top forces or orthodox major forces in each state know these things?

Could it be... this Blood Sword God was an independent cultivator? Thinking of this, the Cloud Palace leader admired him even more.

His expression became difficult: "Elder, this matter concerns heavenly secrets. I really can't say. Otherwise, if I speak of it, I'm afraid I would die on the spot. I can only reveal eight words to Elder: Great calamity is coming, prepare early."

"Great calamity?" Qi Yuan was surprised.

But he didn't pursue the question further.

Judging by the Cloud Palace leader's appearance, he wouldn't be able to get an answer anyway.

"This time, the Eastern Land has discovered a new world. The three... Yin Gods of the Eastern Land have discussed that any sect or force whose genius ranks in the top twenty of the Eastern Land Genius List will have the opportunity to enter that world and expand their territory," the Cloud Palace leader continued.

"The Eastern Land has three Yin Gods?" Qi Yuan asked.

This was what he cared about.

"Such secrets, this junior does not know," the Cloud Palace leader shook his head, not expecting the Blood Sword God to ask this.

"How strong are Yin Gods? Could ten thousand of you kill a Yin God?" Qi Yuan asked again.

"Cough cough..." The Cloud Palace leader was very embarrassed. "Elder, please don't put this junior in a difficult position. Yin Gods... are already like gods in this world. This level, this junior cannot fathom, and doesn't dare to have any thoughts of comparison at all."

"If I took down the sun from the sky and smashed a Yin God with it, would it kill them?" Qi Yuan asked again.

Cold sweat covered the Cloud Palace leader's head.

Why was this Blood Sword God so fixated on Yin Gods? As soon as he opened his mouth, he wanted to fight Yin Gods.

He had to admit, the Blood Sword God's strength was very powerful, almost on par with the master of the Ascending Heaven Pavilion.

But Yin Gods and Purple Mansion cultivators were on completely different levels.

One was still cultivating immortality, while the other was already an immortal god.

"It should be able to kill them..." the Cloud Palace leader said awkwardly.

Hearing this, Qi Yuan made up his mind: "By the way, that new world, which world is it? How strong is it?"

"The Wangyue Continent. It's very weak, with the strongest beings only comparable to the Divine Infant stage in our Azure Wave Realm. So the top twenty geniuses of the Eastern Land Genius List are enough to sweep across the Wangyue Continent."

"Wangyue Continent?" Qi Yuan's eyelashes twitched.

He hadn't expected to hear about this world.

Wasn't this the world in his game? The place where he had built his Heavenly Dao Foundation?

Jin Li...

Was it just the same name or the same place? His heart stirred with waves, but he looked very calm: "Do I qualify to go to the Wangyue Continent?"

"Elder, the matter decided by the three Yin God Venerable Ones cannot be changed. You must enter the top twenty of the Eastern Land Genius List to have a chance to enter the Wangyue Continent."

"Mm, I understand." There were still some waves in Qi Yuan's heart. "I'm leaving."

His figure flashed and disappeared.

Only leaving behind the Cloud Palace leader with a bitter smile in the room.

"Sigh, it seems the grudge between the Blood Sword God and the Palace of Light cannot be resolved."
