The elderly man with graying hair revealed a shocked expression in his eyes, followed by fear.

He hadn't expected his companion to be so bold as to directly threaten with words in a moment of madness.

He was even more surprised that the ancient god before them had directly killed his companion.

This was severing ties with the Ancient God Alliance!

"You..."

"What? Do you want to observe divine law like him?" Qi Yuan looked at the gray-haired elder with an amused expression.

The elder looked at his companion's corpse on the ground and said resignedly: "The fault was his, he deserved death. But Your Honor's actions have severed ties with the Ancient God Alliance. In the future... you will not be able to join the Alliance."

To be honest, the elder had hoped that ancient gods like Origin Venerable would join the Ancient God Alliance.

Now with the evil gods pressing in step by step, the ancient gods were already in decline.

Unfortunately, even so, the ancient gods were still fighting for power and unable to truly unite.

He had always admired gods like Origin Venerable who dared to take action against the evil gods, and wanted to truly absorb them into the Ancient God Alliance.

Within the Alliance, there were still quite a few gods who wanted to do real work.

"I'm not interested in joining any Ancient God Alliance," said Qi Yuan, looking up at the sky.

Far to the north, an unknown true god had just fallen.

"Five towns in Flying Desolation City are completely under the rule of evil gods. Those five evil gods are all purple mansion late stage or perfected divine beings. Origin Venerable must be careful," the gray-haired elder warned kindly.

Qi Yuan lowered his head.

He was also aware of this information.

The power levels in this world seemed a bit high, and these new gods were also very strong.

For him now, sweeping through these five towns of evil gods would indeed be somewhat troublesome.

"Your Honor should be especially wary of the evil god in Fierce Wind Town. He is no ordinary purple mansion perfected being!"

"Is he Wang Wei's purple mansion perfected?" Qi Yuan couldn't help but ask.

The gray-haired elder was stunned, not understanding Qi Yuan's meaning. He could only continue: "He has already condensed half a divine aperture!"

Hearing this, Qi Yuan's expression became solemn.

The biggest difference between yin spirits and purple mansion beings was that yin spirits wielded divine power.

The serval with half a divine aperture could already be considered a demigod.

He was already able to wield divine power.

"So strong?" Qi Yuan's eyes revealed an astonished look.

He glanced up at his own stellar golden core, feeling a bit more secure.

"Without becoming a true god, it's impossible to defeat him!" said the gray-haired elder.

Qi Yuan fell silent upon hearing this.

Now relying on his origin divine ability, he could easily kill ordinary late stage purple mansion beings.

But... condensing half a divine aperture.

The origin divine ability would be almost ineffective.

He could no longer instantly kill him like he had dealt with other ancient gods before.

Unless he also advanced to purple mansion and reached level 60, but he was only in his 30s now.

Only by summoning the stellar golden core could he easily kill instantly.

But in this game, Qi Yuan still wanted to keep a low profile.

Before figuring out this game, he didn't want to casually summon his stellar golden core.

"Are there any yang spirits among the evil gods?" Qi Yuan asked at this point.

"Yang spirits? You mean above true gods?"

"Mm."

"In ancient times during the Great Flood War, there was one being above true god who participated in battle among the new gods." Mentioning this, the gray-haired elder's face was full of sighs. "It was precisely because that being above true god participated that the Mortal Heart Realm was greatly defeated."

"What happened to that being above true god?"

"Unknown." The gray-haired elder shook his head.

Qi Yuan frowned in thought upon hearing this.

During this time, he had already learned about the Great Flood War through the chat group he entered during contemplation.

That was the war when evil gods invaded the Mortal Heart Realm.

Gods as generals, purple mansion beings as soldiers, killing until heaven collapsed and earth split.

Nearly 90% of the true gods in the Mortal Heart Realm perished.

This was the war that broke the backbone of the Mortal Heart Realm.

Since then, the world greatly changed.

Without special treasures, those at the peak of purple mansion could no longer step into true godhood.

"Mm." Qi Yuan felt that to deal with the serval, there was no need to summon the stellar golden core or Ning Tao.

There was no virtual realm here, yin spirits fought directly in the physical realm.

Summoning would be too conspicuous.

Qingshui Village was still too weak now.

He should now emulate the ancients - store up grain in large quantities and slowly proclaim himself king.

After all, he still had many methods to observe divine law.

"You may go," Qi Yuan did not kill this elder.

He always distinguished clearly between gratitude and grudges.

This gray-haired elder had not offended him, so he would not kill him.

He was not the kind of compatriot who would kill someone's entire family at the drop of a hat!

After the gray-haired elder left, Qingshui Village was especially busy in the following days.

More and more people came to the village suffering from the plague, but fortunately under Little Snow's leadership, the earth gods worked tirelessly, and the plague slowly improved.

Three days later, Qi Yuan was continuing to write the "Qi Yuan Sutra" in the temple when suddenly he heard a girl sobbing.

He opened his eyes and looked at the maid Little Snow in the distance with a puzzled expression: "What's wrong, did someone bully you?"

The maid Little Snow's eyes were red. Seeing Qi Yuan, she rubbed her eyes and quickly said: "No, I just thought of my dead parents."

"Oh, is that so?" Qi Yuan glanced at the maid Little Snow.

"Godfather, it's fine, I'll keep Little Snow company," said Qi Qi.

As she spoke, she grabbed Little Snow and left.

When the two were far from the temple, the maid Little Snow's eyes were red: "Miss I'm sorry, I couldn't hold back. The people of Jinshi Town... are too miserable!"

Thinking of the tragic state of Jinshi Town, Little Snow's eyes were full of grief.

To find out the source of the plague, she had secretly snuck into Jinshi Town herself, but the scene she saw was horrific beyond words.

When she first came to Qingshui Village, she had passed through Jinshi Town.

Jinshi Town was still a relatively peaceful and tranquil small town then.

Now... corpses lay on the ground with no one to collect them.

Every household's doors were tightly shut, as if waiting for death.

It had become a town that was like hell on earth.

"With godfather just starting out and lacking manpower, we can't help Jinshi Town now." Qi Qi's eyes held deep worry.

The plague in Jinshi Town was clearly man-made.

So... who did it?

Obviously, it was those evil gods.

Qingshui Village's strength was very powerful now, but still unable to clash with the remaining five towns.

Qi Qi was afraid the maid Little Snow would leak information about Jinshi Town, causing unnecessary trouble for her godfather.
