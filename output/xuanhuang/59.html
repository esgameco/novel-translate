The perseverance over the years is something everyone can see.

But when Xuanhuang mentioned that Gou Jianren, one of the Twelve Demonic Sect Elders and the Demonic Puppet Elder, was a disciple of the Demon Cult, even he was perplexed.

"Xuanshi, is this truly the case?"

Xu Ruyi asked solemnly.

It was hard for him to believe that the deputy director of his Exorcism Department was the elder of an evil cult.

"Every word I've said is the truth. Director Xu, take a look at what this is."

Xuanhuang then produced a puppet that emanated a strong aura of eeriness.

Controlling it with his mental powers, Xuanhuang slowly brought the puppet over to Xu Ruyi's hand.

Xu Ruyi solemnly accepted it and examined it closely, especially after the eerie contact...

It was indeed the notorious Gou Jianren, one of the Twelve Demonic Sect Elders and the Demonic Puppet Elder.

The eeriness could not be faked.

Xuanhuang said, "This person is so deranged, and he is closely tied to the Demon Cult's Elders. The assassin he hired, Qi Long the Ominous Shadow, has also been eliminated by me. I believe Director Xu can make a fair judgment."

Xu Ruyi was burning with anger. The facts were undeniable, now crystal clear.

"Thank you, Xuanshi, for informing me. My Exorcism Department will handle this matter."

Xu Ruyi then said, "Our purpose in coming here today was twofold - first, to congratulate you on the Wudao Hall; second, we also came for Jia Zhengyi. Now that this is clear, we shall take our leave."

As for the shaman who had followed Director Dong Xingbao to cause a ruckus? After witnessing the unbelievable scene just now, he had completely forgotten about it.

Who was Jia Zhengyi? Ah, he was an Elder of the Demon Cult, good riddance to him.

No wonder I, such a good person, was incited by him. It turns out the problem was not with me, but entirely with Gou Jianren.

Striking at Xuanhuang?

Don't be ridiculous, didn't you see my arm was broken, only sustained by my eerie abilities.

Thus, they had come with a thunderous arrival, and left in a disheveled state.

Not until Xuanhuang saw Xu Ruyi off at the hall's entrance did Xu Ruyi say to Xuanhuang, "I wonder if Xuanshi has time tomorrow? I'd like to discuss something with you."

"Hmm?" Xuanhuang was puzzled, "What does Director Xu wish to discuss?"

"Eeriness!"

"Eeriness?" Hearing this, Xuanhuang became interested: "I will sweep the hall clean to welcome you tomorrow, Director Xu."

"That's good. Lately, a storm is brewing over South City, not to mention your appearance; in terms of eeriness, it is also forming an encirclement over South City. The Exorcism Department alone cannot protect the citizens of South City. Your appearance has given me renewed hope."

"It's that serious?" Xuanhuang asked.

"Even more serious than you imagine. There are quite a few renowned Second-Order Eerie beings, and there are even suspected Third-Order existences."

Xuanhuang nodded.

"Alright, I'll be awaiting your arrival tomorrow."

With that, the two parted ways.

Xu Ruyi left with a group of shamans, some of whom were walking in a peculiar, twisted manner.

"Have a seat, everyone."

Xuanhuang waved his hand, closely examining the group.

He was familiar with the ones from the Wudao Hall, mainly Master Ma, Master Jiu Tong, Venerable Ming Hui, Mr. Yan Lai, and Du Ming and Zhao Long.

A will enveloped the six of them, and they remained motionless and silent. Xuanhuang had a general understanding.

"Well done, well done. Your natural talent is quite good, and your karmic conditions are also profound."
