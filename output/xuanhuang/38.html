The group from the Dao Mansion, their minds heavy with thoughts, returned to the Dao Mansion.

Master Ma asked.

"Hmm, I only learned of this matter today. If Lord Xuan Huang has indeed decided to stay long-term at Qing Yun Mountain, we cannot fall behind either, being close by will give us more opportunities."

After saying that, he said to Xia Qing: "In that case, Deputy Mansion Master Xia, you should send someone to scout the surrounding area tomorrow to see if there are any suitable places for us to station."

"Okay."

Xia Qing nodded, since Master Ma and the Mansion Master both said so, it meant they believed Xuan Huang's words.

"How's the situation?"

"Department Head, the situation is not too good, this Xuan Huang person may not be as simple as we thought."

Jia Zhengyi asked, "Explain in detail."

"Yes, that's right, Department Head. The one-eyed person was mysteriously injured, suddenly disrupting our plan."

Jia Zhengyi nodded and said, "I see, you all go and rest first, let me think it through."

After the few people left, Jia Zhengyi fell into deep thought.

It seemed he had made many mistakes.

As long as Xuan Huang returned, as long as he investigated a little, he would understand Jia Zhengyi's actions.

Such an enemy was not too good.

It seemed he needed to use some methods.

He then dialed a phone number.

"Old friend, I need you to do something, and after the task is accomplished, the puzzle you've been longing for can be taken."

There was a moment of silence on the other end of the phone.

"What's the matter?"

Jia Zhengyi said, "I need you to kill a person, a person who has opened up a superhuman martial arts path."

"What did you say?"

This news obviously startled the person on the other end of the phone.

"I didn't expect Mysterious Shadow Qi Long to also have moments of surprise. That's right, it's a person who has opened up a superhuman martial arts path. As long as you kill him, the Mysterious Shadow Cloak will be yours, and with the Mysterious Shadow Cloak, your advancement to the second stage will be smooth sailing."

The other end pondered for a long time.

"Okay, I'll hurry to South City."

Then he added, "You'd better keep your word, otherwise..."

"Don't worry, I won't cheat an old friend."

"Hehe, forget it, a 'friend' like you, I, Qi Long, can't bear."

Jia Zhengyi didn't get angry, but instead said, "Your words are too hurtful."

"Alright, if there's nothing else, hang up, don't disgust me."

Jia Zhengyi's face darkened as he said, "Okay, see you later, at the old place in South City."

Click, the phone call was hung up.

With such a figure stationed at the Martial Arts Mansion, the development and growth of the Martial Arts Mansion was just around the corner.
