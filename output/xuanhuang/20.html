The only goal now is to transcend the will.

They are non-man-made objects, they are the products of heaven and earth.

The material condensed by this natural force is the most suitable for their true realization.

It doesn't mean that they don't need any materials at all.

If there are suitable ones, it can accelerate the gestation, but if there aren't any, it's just a bit slower for the self-gestation.

The void blade is different, it was bestowed with meaning by Xuanhuang's small world, the physical object came first, and then the true meaning was gradually born through the quality.

The better the material, the faster the progress.

And without material, the progress will stop.

So whenever there is good material, Xuanhuang will let it be devoured and enhance himself.

Currently, the best material is the Anomalous. The centipede is not Anomalous, but it is still extraordinary, a very good material.

Unless Xuanhuang is willing to let the Anomalous Dream Brush be devoured by the void blade.

"Swoosh--"

After a day's effort by Xuanhuang, along with the support of the small world, the centipede scales were finally dissolved into a liquid state.

Removing the impurities, the essence was absorbed by the void blade.

On the originally bumpy blade surface, a layer of flowing light was moving, constantly replenishing the essence of the void blade.

Until it was completely absorbed.

Examining the void blade, the most intuitive feeling was that it had become heavier, and its hardness had also increased.

"It would be best to encounter an Anomalous blade, let the void blade devour and absorb it."

"But this kind of opportunity requires fate."

Xuanhuang struck the void blade, "You'd better bless yourself, be lucky enough to encounter some suitable Anomalous, otherwise you'll be far behind your three companions!"

Putting away the long sword, Xuanhuang simply had a light dinner.

The moon hung high, all things were silent, only the howling north wind swept through the mountains and forests.

Occasionally, the call of an unknown animal could be heard.

If there were no Anomalous, this would be a beautiful world.

This world and the previous Earth are not much different, Xuanhuang would adapt very well.

Without Anomalous, his parents would still be here, he would have a small courtyard worth a few million, and a few million in savings.

Xuanhuang could completely be a happy young man: not humble, not hurried, perhaps this is how life should be.

Away from the internal struggle, seeking the true self, settling the heart, being a gentleman as the ancients said.

Find his other half, support each other and share the fate, work hard to turn love into the 'affection' forged by the two.

Perhaps it would be very beautiful.

Alas!

Hurriedly, hastily, life turns out to be like this?

Damn it!...

Back on the bed in the safe house, Xuanhuang sat cross-legged.

In the consciousness space, the centipede demon, which had been divided into three parts, was in it.

Xuanhuang's goal today is to raise his will to the ninth layer limit.

Grind that transcendent barrier to a point where it's just about to break through, then stay calm and patiently wait for the breakthrough tomorrow.

After all, this is the first time in this world, although the simulation is perfectly executed, but as a pioneer, and the conditions are sufficient, it's still better to be cautious.

It's just a difference of one day.

Using one part of the three parts of the centipede demon, with sufficient vital essence, in his repeated grinding...
