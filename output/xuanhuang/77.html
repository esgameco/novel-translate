The bald head darted about.

Not far from the main peak of Qingyun Mountain, on a mountain peak that was currently under development, in a simple large hall, a group of senior monks were gathered together.

The leader was Monk Minghui.

Currently, there were nearly a thousand monks who had come to Qingyun Mountain in the south, all of them renowned high monks and masters.

They were experts and geniuses in the fields of qi cultivation, body cultivation, and spirit cultivation, as well as some very devout spirit cultivator monks.

"How is your cultivation going, everyone?" Minghui asked.

"Abbot, currently we have too many monks and too little resources. There are queues to participate in the visualization practices, even if one has merit points, it's still not enough. We need to compete with the Taoist and Confucian factions. Furthermore, the Mysterious Master has issued an order, requiring us to give up one-fifth of our shares..."

The monk continued, "Fortunately, our Buddhist faction has produced many elite cultivators. We cover body, qi, and spirit cultivation. We are currently negotiating with the Grand Marshal of the Martial Alliance, Sun Hui, to see if we can exchange our body cultivation shares for their qi and spirit cultivation shares. We can temporarily focus less on body cultivation and emphasize more on spirit and qi cultivation practices."

"But... the Confucian and Taoist factions have been disrupting us. They are also negotiating with the Martial Alliance to exchange shares."

A senior monk said, "Abbot, the Taoists and Confucians are doing this intentionally, they are being too overbearing."

Minghui did not speak for a while, then said, "This kind of situation will be unavoidable in the future. All of you are eminent monks, high cultivators forged by the world. You should understand that our previous alliance and mutual support was an exception. After the Mysterious Master's teachings, that tacit understanding will be broken, and competition will be the norm."

The monks present were all true high monks, so they just complained a bit without getting too worked up.

Minghui continued, "We must strive for what we need, not just strive but give it our all. We need to change, if we don't change, we will be eliminated... Previously, our Buddhist faction was too excessive. This is an opportunity for us to change. Those hypocritical and false monks must be resolutely expelled. Those who have lost their Buddhist compassion and do not understand true Buddhism must also be removed..."

In short, it was a time for reorganization.

Although the Buddhist faction had undergone a strange revival, it was no longer as strong as before, having been greatly weakened.

The previous false monks who had usurped reputations had already been scorned.

But Minghui felt that it was still not enough, they could not overturn the world's perception of them.

The Buddhist faction was too large, with many false monks, but also true high monks. The key was that the false monks had been rampant, while the true high monks were neglected.

This situation had to change, not only for the world's perception, but also for the Mysterious Master's view.

In fact, the views of the upper echelons were the most important, such as the Mysterious Sovereigns.

The views of the common people didn't matter much, because they posed no threat to the monks.

"Anything else?" Minghui asked.

An elder monk said, "There is a rumor targeting us."

"Go on."

The elder monk said, "It's the old claim that the Buddhist faction does not produce anything real, especially in the current situation of population and food crises, etc. This is being spread by the Confucian faction... They've been doing this for thousands of years."

After these words, the assembled high monks fell silent.

The Confucians, they had been at odds with them for too long. Not only the Confucians, even the Taoists were often brought out by them for criticism.

Previously, the Confucians were the dominant school, and even though they couldn't defeat the Buddhists, they didn't dare to curse them, only enduring. But now that the situation had changed, their old tricks had resurfaced.

But what the corrupt Confucians said was also true.

Now with the dwindling population, strange occurrences, and a chaotic world, the Buddhist faction needed to change.

"Then let's change. We will be self-reliant, the Buddhist faction has done this before. To shut others' mouths, it's not about making them stay silent, but us taking action to make them shut up. Stop trying to suppress others, from now on we'll prove ourselves through our actions."

Minghui said, "No matter who it is, no matter which eminent monk, they must actively participate in the frontlines of subduing fierce beasts and dealing with strange phenomena. Our Buddhist faction is not afraid of sacrifice. If the spilled blood and sacrifices of our Buddhist faction can restore our reputation, then starting from this old monk, we will use our own blood and dedication to earn the respect of the world."

Minghui spoke with great solemnity.

Finally, he said gravely, "Some people need to use their own blood to wash away the past disgrace and decline, and also let the world see our change. Do you all understand what I mean?"

The assembled high monks were stirred, waves rippling in their hearts.

Abbot Minghui was going to take drastic measures. "Some people need to sacrifice themselves to redeem the disgrace" - this meant that some monks would have to die.

But it couldn't be just any death, it had to be a death with value. For example, "A high monk vowing to die saving ordinary people, perishing together with a fierce beast." Or "A certain high monk making a great vow, disappearing while exploring a strange phenomenon, leaving no trace."

Or "A certain monastery exhausting its wealth to aid the homeless, the entire monastery's monks vowing to remeasure the earth, sacrificing themselves one by one in the wilderness."

And so on.

Change was necessary, in this era of competition, falling into decay was no longer an option.

...

After discussing the serious matters, the senior monks then explored their cultivation experiences.

After all, that was the most important thing. No matter how one chose to martyr themselves, they still needed the ability to do so, couldn't just be sending themselves to their deaths.

"Abbot, currently we have 300 people who have participated in the visualization practices. The most are 136 who have comprehended the 'Heaven and Earth Law' Soaring Tree visualization. 58 have comprehended the 'Return to Void Law' Great Grindstone visualization. The remaining 104 have comprehended the 'Smelting Law' Heaven and Earth Smelter visualization. Among them, nearly half, 62 people, have entered qi cultivation, 21 have entered spirit cultivation, and 42 have entered body cultivation."

"Furthermore, we have nearly a hundred eminent monks researching the forging of spiritual artifacts, over 200 people researching talismans, and others studying spirit herbs and even formations."

Minghui listened and nodded, "What have you gained?"

"Abbot, you have been resolving our doubts in cultivation, and the eminent monks here are also wise. Our doubts in cultivation have been resolved. But for artifact forging and talismans, we need to conduct experiments."

"Mm-hmm."

Minghui nodded. Artifact forging required obtaining strange phenomena for sealing, then refining them in the Heaven and Earth Smelter, and finally self-tempering.

Talismans required using the Soaring Tree, needing to exchange leaves with the Tree.

These all required hands-on experimentation, mere research was not enough.

"Then go experiment. Whether it's artifact forging or talismans, our Buddhist faction needs to have our own masters. Appropriately raise the merit points of the artifact forging and talisman masters, let them experiment, don't be afraid of failure. We are in the same situation as the Taoist and Confucian factions..."
