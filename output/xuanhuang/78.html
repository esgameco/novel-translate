The sixth refinement of the two pillars of the family, the Xuanhuang consciousness flew out from the furnace, instantly covering the entire sea of qi.

The 60-zhang sea of qi was surging with golden qi, like the turbulent waves of the great sea.

At the center was a surging spring, constantly spewing forth golden qi flow, and the Heavenly-Earth Furnace was situated right above it.

The diffused will that flew into the higher realm had become much stronger compared to when it first entered the Xuan phase.

As the will grew stronger, so did Xuanhuang's abilities, borrowing the Heavenly Will of the small world to expand. Now, with just a gaze, Xuanhuang could instantly disperse the divine soul of an ordinary person if the will was lent to it.

Just a lethal gaze would suffice.

Withdrawing the consciousness, Xuanhuang instantly returned to the small world.

Gazing at the massive Spirit-Locking Stele at the center, Xuanhuang pondered deeply. Ever since the Spirit-Locking Stele appeared, its golden divine soul had been continuously growing stronger.

The strengthening of the previously unimaginable golden divine soul was achieved through a single fortuitous encounter.

Xuanhuang was already very pleased when the white divine soul could be strengthened, but now the golden divine soul was growing stronger without any effort, and even boosting the white divine soul - it was simply unbelievable.

"Could it be that divine souls are scattered throughout the boundless space-time?" Xuanhuang couldn't understand where the strengthening divine souls were coming from.

Previously, Xuanhuang had planned to use spirit stones to absorb the corrupted divine souls of Cultivators to strengthen them. But the Great Grinder seemed to need it more, absorbing the Returning-to-Void force to strengthen itself.

So Xuanhuang temporarily abandoned the idea of using spirit stones to strengthen the white divine soul, letting the Great Grinder strengthen first.

Unexpectedly, without the need for spirit stones, the Spirit-Locking Stele made the spirit stones' transformed divine souls almost negligible.

The Great Grinder was busily refining the Returning-to-Void force condensed from the spirit stones, and each time it did so, it grew stronger.

It was almost catching up to the Wooden Sage.

They all had a promising future, and Xuanhuang was very satisfied.

Xuanhuang took out a damaged Pig-Slaughtering Knife, which was obtained from a previous targeted cleansing of anomalies.

A few days ago, Xuanhuang had a confrontation in the Anomaly Alley and sustained some injuries, so these past two days, Xuanhuang had not continued the targeted cleansing, only dealing with six anomalies.

The Anomaly Bell was temporarily left alone, as if it was refined, the world there would be destroyed, which would be a great loss.

Xuanhuang was still thinking about the Great Bell on South Mountain and the one on the mountain, as well as the Time Bubble River - such opportunities would not be easily encountered, so a backup plan had to be kept.

As for the Pig-Slaughtering Knife, it was bloodthirsty and had divine soul impact, able to drive people into a bloodthirsty frenzy and also absorb fear to grow stronger, with the ability to lock onto a target with certainty.

It was considered special, but its effect on Xuanhuang was not significant, as Xuanhuang's will already covered everything.

Xuanhuang planned to let the Void Sword devour it, as the Void Sword was currently in the mid-Xuan phase and was the slowest in progress among the three.

After devouring the Pig-Slaughtering Knife, due to their similar nature, perhaps the Void Sword could reach the late-Xuan phase and catch up to its three "elder brothers".

Summoning the Void Sword, Xuanhuang let it handle the matter.

The Void Sword quickly wrapped around the sealed Pig-Slaughtering Knife anomaly. Even though it was sealed, when the crisis of complete annihilation arrived, the Pig-Slaughtering Knife still resisted.

But the outcome was predetermined - in the small world, the Void Sword was the child of the Heavenly Will.

Its spiritual intelligence was bestowed by the Heavenly Will of the small world. What's wrong with the parent's child eating something? Instantly, the Heavenly Will lent its power, and the Void Sword's devouring and fusion ability invaded, causing the struggling Pig-Slaughtering Knife anomaly to instantly lose its ability to resist.

Seeing this, Xuanhuang no longer paid attention.

They had all grown up and needed to eat for themselves, just like the White Bone Pagoda, which was diligently refining a section of white bone, and after two days, it still hadn't been completely refined.

However, Xuanhuang looked at it and saw that it was almost done. At the latest by tomorrow, the White Bone Pagoda would be able to completely refine a section of white bone, officially reaching the peak of the mid-Xuan phase.

In this way, the personnel trials on Qingyan Mountain would become even more formidable.

With nothing to do, a raven appeared in Xuanhuang's hand, which was the Death Raven, one of the six anomalies previously dealt with.

This thing could peck at people's divine souls, and once hit by it, their divine souls would shatter, causing them to die from divine soul collapse.

In addition, this raven had the ability to seek out death, and could also bestow death upon people, like a weakened version of the Grim Reaper.

Once it had you in its sights, you could only pray for the best.

Its anomalous abilities were not yet at the level of instantly causing death, but it could achieve complete soul dissipation within three days.

Due to its ability to seek out death, this raven also had some spatial abilities, like spatial teleportation.

However, Xuanhuang found it rather useless for itself.

Who couldn't do spatial teleportation? Xuanhuang could even rely on the small world as the center to instantly teleport between the two worlds, far surpassing the raven's ability to only teleport within a ten-li radius.

As for the death-dealing ability, the Wooden Sage had something to say about that.

And as for the divine soul pecking ability, the Great Grinder found it too weak.

"I'll just refine it again, I can't use it, so I can give it to someone else."

The same old saying, anomalies are definitely treasures, because the refined artifacts all have growth potential.

This was truly extraordinary.

"Open the furnace!"

Xuanhuang summoned the Heavenly-Earth Furnace circling around the Spirit-Locking Stele.

"Rumble!"

The stubborn furnace landed with a loud sound.

Xuanhuang didn't mind, letting out a loud shout: "Open!"

The furnace lid flew up, and Xuanhuang tossed the Death Raven into the Heavenly-Earth Furnace.

"Roar!"

The golden flames began to burn, and the Heavenly Will-forged hammer started swinging.

"Clang, clang!"

It was a gruesome scene, a seemingly innocent little raven being surrounded by golden flames and pounded by the massive hammer.

It was a bit 'cruel', but the raven had brought it upon itself.

The furnace had grown much stronger, and Xuanhuang had also grown stronger recently, with greatly enhanced artifact refining experience.

So compared to the first time refining an artifact, this time it was much simpler and more composed.

Soon, a freshly forged raven emerged from the furnace.

Xuanhuang didn't make any major changes, only shattering the corrupted divine will of the Death Raven, restoring it to factory settings.

Carefully examining the newly forged Death Raven, Xuanhuang was very satisfied.

Xuanhuang disappeared in a flash from the small world.

"Sun Tong, come here."

Arriving at the side hall, Xuanhuang called out to Sun Tong in the room.

The hazy Sun Tong quickly came over.

"You've been with me for a long time, and they say 'the nearest water gets the moon first', but it seems like you haven't really benefited from following me, and you've been kept busy, which has hindered your cultivation. Hmm, your body refinement is barely at the third layer, how about your will refinement?"

Xuanhuang asked.

Hearing this, Sun Tong became very excited, finally Xuanhuang was thinking of him?

"Master Xuan, my natural talent is too poor, I've brought you shame. A few families have been providing me with resources, and my body refinement is at the third layer, my perception and visualization are at 90% completion, but the last 10% just won't budge."

Xuanhuang was taken aback. "What do you mean by 'a few families providing'?"

"Master Xuan, I was initially reluctant, but Sect Leader Jiu, Master Ming Hui, and Master Yan Lai were all very enthusiastic, saying that I've been working hard for you and have fallen behind in my cultivation. So the three of them, along with the Martial Dao Alliance, have been jointly providing me with resources."

I see, so that's how it is.

"From now on, don't use theirs or the Martial Dao Alliance's resources, use mine instead. My people don't need them to provide, after all, it's not good to take from others and eat from their mouths." Xuanhuang said.

Suddenly, Xuanhuang asked, "By the way, do I have any resources?"

Sun Tong paused for a moment, a bit short-circuited, and quickly replied, "You have unlimited resources, Master."

"Good, then just use mine from now on."

"Yes, Master Xuan." Sun Tong hurriedly agreed.

From now on, I'll be eating and drinking what the Master provides.

Hmm, it feels like I'm being kept.
