After sorting through Qi Long's gains, Xuan Huang found nothing particularly noteworthy.

He had expected some kind of sealed, mysterious item, but there was none.

It made sense - who would deliberately collect such strange items? They must have been traded or sold off.

Holding onto something mysterious and useless to oneself is a dangerous thing. If one accidentally triggers the revival of the mystery, even a Demon Subduing Spiritual Master would not be able to withstand it.

Qi Long's most important collection was the suppression medicine known as "Divine Blood", and a stone he had picked up from an underground demonic cave, called the "Demon Stone".

Xuan Huang probed with his mental sense and obtained the small world, activating his transcendent perception.

To his amazement, these two items were quite special.

The Divine Blood could suppress the demonic corruption of the divine soul, while the Demon Stone could absorb the demonic aura invading the divine soul.

One suppresses, the other absorbs.

These two items must have some secrets.

The Divine Blood is a product of the Demonic Sect, and it is said that only the Demonic Sect in the entire world can produce the Divine Blood.

As for the Demon Stone, Qi Long had picked it up by chance when he wandered into the demonic cave.

What surprised Xuan Huang the most was that the Divine Blood contained pure, uncorrupted divine souls.

This means that after consuming it, one's divine soul can be strengthened. Such purity and massive energy, yet untainted - this at least indicates that the source of the Divine Blood is at the Fourth Realm True Self level.

Otherwise, without the support of the True Self, it would be impossible to remain uncorrupted.

Of course, this does not mean the Divine Blood itself is Fourth Realm, but rather its source is Fourth Realm, similar to how the Primal Spiritual Essence condenses the True Self. Therefore, the blood it nurtures or breeds also carries the characteristics of the True Self, remaining uncorrupted.

The Demon Stone is the same - it is not the Demon Stone itself that is Fourth Realm, but the source that imbues its existence is at least Fourth Realm, allowing it to absorb the corrupted divine souls and emotions.

"Quite interesting?" Xuan Huang looked at the Divine Blood and Demon Stone in his hands.

Of the sources he currently knows that bestow true meaning at the Fourth Realm level, there are seven: the Sea of Blood, the Abyss, the Ruins of Return, the Azure Skies and Yellow Springs, the Nightmare World, the Nine Nether Hellfire Realm, and the Bitter Sea.

The Divine Blood and Demon Stone seem to be the eighth and ninth.

Xuan Huang is very interested in these seven places, because if sentient, intelligent beings can be birthed there, they would be at least Fourth Realm at the starting point.

Only existences at the True Self level can establish a foothold in these seven places. If Fourth Realm beings are born, they are not simple demonic entities, but intelligent existences.

Even if demons reach the Fourth Realm, they will give birth to True Self spiritual intelligences.

Existences that have not birthed True Self spiritual intelligences cannot possibly attain the Fourth Realm.

By extension, if a Demon Subduing Cultivator reaches the Fourth Realm and has not lost their sanity, occupying the dominant position, they will completely master the demonic rules and become a True Self existence.

At that point, they need not worry about being corrupted by the demonic, for they will be the sovereign of the demonic rules.

Xuan Huang already knew that the demonic possesses a dual nature.

If you can completely control and refine it, you will ascend to the heavens, becoming an existence that commands part of the rules.

But if you falter in the refinement process, you will become part of it, nourishing the countless slain remnant souls until it births a new spiritual intelligence at the Fourth Realm.

It is similar to the initial opening of the Primordial Chaos - with so much resentful energy and weak souls becoming ferocious beasts, devoid of reason and only driven by slaughter.

While the strong ones who control themselves become the first born sacred gods who establish the heavens and earth.

This is why, in the fusion of the myriad realms, wondrous places can always be birthed.

The Ruins of Return, the Abyss, and the other seven places are equivalent to the Sea of Blood, the Dark Depths, and the like in the Primordial Chaos.

It is practically a remake.

And if sentient beings are birthed in these places, they would start at the Fourth Realm level.

The reason they have not appeared yet is that the fusion of the heavens and earth is still too short, and these wondrous places are still in the process of gestation - not to mention the birthing of spiritual beings.
