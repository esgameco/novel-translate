"How does the Dao companion feel?"

In front of the White Bone Pagoda, the crowd was gathered, watching a young cultivator stagger out of the pagoda.

The young man's face was pale, drenched in sweat, and even his clothes on the autumn mountain were soaked through.

He walked unsteadily, and after exiting the White Bone Pagoda, he looked back at it with lingering fear in his eyes.

Suddenly, he was startled by the inquiry of the people guarding the entrance.

He was clearly still not fully recovered.

"It's... it's fine, it's quite fine!" the young man said, trying to suppress his fear. "Truly worthy of a trial that requires ten points to experience. It was very good inside, and had a great effect on tempering the will. I gained a lot."

His words were incoherent, unable to organize his thoughts properly, only saying that the effect was great.

He was afraid of being laughed at, not knowing what he had truly experienced.

It was a bizarre descent, where his entire body's bones were uncontrollably compressed and ground down.

Temper the will? What will? Due to his lack of experience, this time he only suffered and gained nothing. There was no will tempering at all.

Once the trial began, he immediately lost control of his own will, how could he possibly temper it?

To temper the will, one needs a certain level of willpower to begin with. You must first remain calm and steady your mind.

If you panic the moment you enter, you can't accomplish anything, only suffering and gaining nothing.

"Ah, what a loss!" the young man lamented inwardly.

But was it truly a loss?

Not entirely, at least he now knows what kind of "being" he is - too inexperienced, his will too weak.

Directly uncontrollable, how could he cultivate it?

It seems the direction is clear now, he needs to forge his will, at least take the first step, to avoid losing control the moment he enters.

"Dao companion, please elaborate..." the crowd asked.

"It can only be experienced, not described. Everyone needs to enter the trial themselves. I can only say, those who understand will understand, those who don't, no explanation is needed. I was just in the tower, and just now..."

Suddenly, his tone became more aggressive, with a pungent smell, strangely familiar.

"If you don't gain anything after entering, you can only say that your efforts over the years were in vain. Why didn't your will grow? Have you ever reflected on your own problems? Complaining that the points are expensive? They are already very cheap! After all these years your will didn't grow, and you can't even exchange for points, it's your own problem, you need to reflect on yourself..."

He became even more aggressive, making the crowd feel like hitting him.

A burly man asked, "May I ask this Dao companion's honorable name?"

The young man said, "I'm afraid to say, in case I'm..."

"Hateful, this person, with no experience at all, just rambling nonsense, making me feel like I'm back in the days before the Anomaly's revival, listening to those experts' dogma." the burly man said frustratedly.

The others agreed with his words.

"Who doesn't know? His words are too aggressive. Doesn't he know whether he worked hard or not? I almost died to achieve today's accomplishments, yet he says I didn't work hard. Is it really my problem? I've surpassed so many Dao companions, and this is how it is?"

"Ah, nothing has changed. We wandering cultivators are still just cannon fodder, even with the Anomaly's revival, we're just the ones enjoying the good life for the big powers. Our individual efforts are too insignificant."

"That's why the appearance of Qingyun Mountain is a miracle. I must continue to work hard, I must take on missions, I must earn points, I will definitely rise up."

The burly man vowed, then left without looking back, heading to the mission assignment area to see if there were any points he could exchange or suitable missions for himself.

"...What's going on over there? This pagoda, how strange, Anomalous forces? But without the sense of chaos?"

The newly arrived Xu Ru'one asked the deputy curator High Jian.

"Director Xu, this is a trial tower just refined by Master Xuan, entering it can temper the will, it requires points to enter... Possibly someone has entered the trial, and the others are inquiring about the experience."

High Jian guessed in his response.

"A tower that can temper the will? Master Xuan is truly a scholar of heaven and earth, in such a short time he has created a treasure that can harness the Anomaly's power. If the Anomaly can all be refined into treasures that benefit human progress, that would be a game changer."

Xu Ru'one was very excited.

"Haha, Master Xuan is indeed a scholar of heaven and earth, it seems there is nothing he cannot do. We who are lucky to follow Master Xuan can only say we are fortunate."

High Jian agreed.

Currently, the four hall masters of the Pavilion, and the three deputy hall masters under each master, are all exceptional talents personally selected by Master Xuan as one of the ten to be summoned.

Only himself, no, and Li Tongni'an as well, feel like they are falling behind.

He can only give full play to his other advantages, such as his management skills.

Develop his strengths to make up for his lack of cultivation talent.

After Xun Hui and the others are summoned, cultivation will be their top priority.

The management of Qingyun Mountain will rely on Li Tongni'an, High Jian, Xia Qing and others.

Xu Ru'one and High Xiaolan agreed with High Jian's words.

Looking at the changes in Qingyun Mountain, they felt curious everywhere.

"Why do I feel like the Anomaly I control has become dormant after entering Qingyun Mountain?"

High Xiaolan voiced the doubts of the group.

"Did you all see that giant tree?" High Jian pointed to the Tong Tian Building Wood in the Xuanhuang Villa in the distance.

"Yes, I see it. Ah, it looks familiar, isn't this the previous Anomalous corpse tree? I heard it was sealed and taken away by Master Xuan, and its appearance has changed."

High Xiaolan continued, "Indeed, the previous gloomy and terrifying appearance is gone, replaced by lush greenery. Hmm... and a sense of the cycle of life and death that's hard to describe? Am I seeing things?"

High Xiaolan asked uncertainly.

"Director High's keen observation is correct! It has indeed been suppressed and transformed by Master Xuan, I heard it has been re-cultivated, becoming something akin to a heavenly spiritual root. This tree is called the Tong Tian Building Wood, named after the mythical Building Wood. This tree can regulate the chaotic spiritual mechanism and remove toxic spiritual energy."

High Jian explained to the group, "The area within a ten-li radius of Qingyun Mountain is stable and undistorted spiritual energy. This may be the reason why the Anomaly in your bodies has become dormant. The chaos that gives birth to the Anomaly is naturally suppressed here."

"This?"

Hearing High Jian's words, the cultivators were greatly shocked.

Doesn't this mean that to be able to act in Qingyun Mountain, one must at least surpass the level of the Building Wood?

Those below the Building Wood's level will be suppressed.

Even if they forcefully erupt their power, they still won't be able to reach their true strength.

"Master Xuan's methods are truly extraordinary, I look forward to the upcoming exchange even more."

As a conclusion, Xu Ru'one spoke.

His Anomalous heart within him did not become dormant, but rather became particularly active, seemingly disliking this place very much.

After all, Xu Ru'one, with his Anomalous heart, is a Second-Tier Great Expert.

The First-Tier peak Building Wood, even with the support of the small world's will, would not be able to suppress the dormant Anomalous heart.

The difference between each Tier is heaven and earth.

"Haha, Master Xuan has been awaiting your arrival, Director Xu."

High Jian immediately said.

"Let's go!"

Xu Ru'one, full of anticipation, headed towards Xuanhuang's villa.

There, Xuanhuang was already waiting at the entrance.

"Director Xu, Dao companions."

"Master Xuan!"

The crowd addressed him as Master Xuan, acknowledging his status.

"Please come in, everyone."

Xuanhuang turned and gestured, guiding the cultivators into the villa.
