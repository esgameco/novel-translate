Gazing at the blood-stained calligraphy brush in his hand, Xuanhuang fell into deep contemplation.

He took out the love letter and found that it had changed as well.

There were no sweet words, only a dilapidated paper with a large courtyard drawn in fresh blood.

The eerie brush should be called the "Ominous Dream Brush".

After it lost its uncanny power, Xuanhuang probed its abilities with his spiritual sense and easily uncovered its capabilities.

As long as the user of this brush paints a picture with their own blood and gives it to someone else, they can pull the holder of the painting into a dream through the Ominous Dream Brush.

In the dream, you can freely splash ink and wield the brush to kill the person you've pulled into the dream realm.

However, the ink used to paint in the dream is the spiritual essence of the dream creator, and those who don't know how to use spiritual essence will end up sacrificing their lifespan.

It is an extremely ominous ability.

First, the painting that pulls someone into the dream requires the creator's own fresh blood.

Then, the painting in the dream requires the creator's spiritual essence and lifespan.

Drinking blood and extracting souls.

"Such a powerful dream-creating ability, yet it is used in such an evil way," Xuanhuang shook his head.

"But this thing has great potential, if I use my own will to replace the blood, I can greatly benefit from pulling people into dreams."

As for the spiritual essence ink, that is something Xuanhuang has no shortage of.

Every time he refines the Abyss Grinder, it produces a large amount, which has been accumulating uselessly in his small world.

Whenever he needs it, he can just take it out and use it.

As for whether non-self spiritual essence is suitable or not?

Xuanhuang declared: "Once they enter my world, they belong to me, for I am the will of heaven."

"I'll wait until I reach the First Realm before using it. Using it now would be like reviving its ominous power, which has just barely faded and settled. This is not the best time."

"When my will takes physical form, I won't fear its revival, as my will shall replace it."

Xuanhuang temporarily put away the Ominous Dream Brush and the blood-stained paper.

This paper is also an ominous item, as it is a ready-made dream scene, that of the grand courtyard from the Republican era.

Although the paper appears dilapidated, it is very difficult to destroy. It will be very useful in the future.

Xuanhuang is a self-reliant cultivator.

Any object can become a subject for him to study and analyze.

If he can mass-produce this kind of paper in the future, he can consider using it for talismans, paper-cutting, puppets, contracts, summonings, card-making, and more.

The essence remains the same, only the operating rules differ.

Carefully storing the Ominous Dream Brush and the blood-stained paper in a prepared jade box, Xuanhuang placed them away.

All the ominous beings sealed in this world must be stored in tightly sealed jade boxes.

Afterwards...

He entered his spiritual space.

The Abyss Grinder that had absorbed the Ominous Dream Brush was now floating in the air.

"Haha, it has indeed solidified."

The Heavenly-Earth Furnace, due to constant refinement of his will, was the closest to solidification.

But this time, the "Ominous Dream Brush Iron" one-stop service allowed the Abyss Grinder to be fully nourished.

It can now condense into the physical world.

In other words, he doesn't need to reach the First Realm.

He can now control the Abyss Grinder with his thoughts and manifest it in the real world.

This is the biggest harvest from this experience.

Although Xuanhuang is confident that he can reach the First Realm in ten days at the latest, having more methods is always better.

He has already sealed a ominous being, finally feeling a sense of accomplishment.

He looked at the Heavenly-Earth Furnace and the Heavenly Pillar again.

The Heavenly-Earth Furnace should be able to progress along with him into the First Realm.

The Heavenly Pillar is a bit lacking, being too illusory. It needs an opportunity.

Xuanhuang thought of one of his starting points: the "Blood Pool Ominous Being".

After reaching the First Realm, he will seek an opportunity.

According to the painting seen in the Martial Hall, the Blood Pool Ominous Being is at the peak of the First Realm, and by utilizing the secret power of the Blood Pool Realm, it can reach the Second Realm.

Not an easy problem to solve.

Of course, Xuanhuang has another starting point, which is his own Grinder.

To be precise, this is his "true starting point".

His biggest asset right now is still in the Grinder space.
