"Your secret has ultimately become mine. Wouldn't it be better if you just told me about it?"

"You're joking."

Through the relentless efforts of Da Mo, Qi Long's spiritual soul was completely shattered and absorbed.

Naturally, the secret he had mentioned also appeared in Xuan Huang's memories.

This was the first time Xuan Huang had refined a 'human' being. Previously, it had not checked the memories of the slain creatures, not because it couldn't, but because there were none.

Xuan Huang perfectly assimilated Qi Long's memories.

Through this process, Da Mo also broke through to the mid-stage of the first realm, reaching the same level as the Void Knife, second only to Tong Tian Jian Mu.

As a result, its abilities in the metaphysical realms of soul, spirit, dream, and heart became even stronger.

Exploring Qi Long's memories, Xuan Huang discovered that six years ago, Qi Long had mastered the Deceptive Shadow during a life-and-death trial.

Originally the target of the Deceptive Shadow's invasion, due to luck and unwavering faith, he had transformed into a Spiritual Cultivator.

He did not join the Exorcism Department, but remained a solitary cultivator.

In the following years...

Through Qi Long's memories, Xuan Huang learned many secrets it had not known before.

For example, the Deceptive Society, which was established by a 'Superintendent' - a powerful cultivator at the third realm - who gathered like-minded individuals.

It gradually grew larger and larger, and the Superintendent's Deceptive abilities allowed them to control a Deceptive Tower, which had the nature of binding the earth spirits and could move freely, making it extremely formidable.

The interior of the Deceptive Tower could rival Xuan Huang's domain.

Moreover, it did not require piecing together puzzles; it could simply grow stronger over time, which was quite rare.

In addition, there were other Deceptive Spiritual Cultivator organizations, some of which Xuan Huang was familiar with, such as the 'Salvation Society' and the 'Demon Worshippers'.

There were also righteous organizations like the 'Exorcism Sect' and the 'Golden Messenger'.

It was an eye-opening array of diverse factions.

As for Qi Long's secret...

Half a year ago, in the southern Wu City, Qi Long had discovered a Demonic Lair in an underground fissure.

It was called a Demonic Lair because it was filled with a black mist that could drive people insane.

From the mist, the sounds of ancient battlefield-like clashes could be heard from time to time.

Through the Deceptive Shadow's abilities, Qi Long had barely survived and made a remarkable discovery.

In the depths of the mist, there was an ancient, red-glowing multi-story building.

In front of the door hung a large red lantern that was captivating.

There were ancient-dressed soldiers constantly coming and going.

Those who entered were missing limbs or severely mutilated, but when they came out, they were completely whole.

It seemed that there was something inside the building that could restore them.

The specific and detailed secrets, Qi Long did not know.

This was already a difficult discovery for him.

Even the three-story building was not too deep into the center of the black mist, only at the edge.

Normally, this would not be anything worth paying attention to.

It was just a newly discovered Deceptive place, or perhaps even an entrance to a Deceptive world.

But when he came out of the mist, he had an unexpected harvest.

At the time, due to his prolonged fusion with the Deceptive Shadow, he was in a high-pressure period and at risk of collapse and distortion.

But for some reason, whether it was the black mist's ability to suppress Deceptive powers or the red building's ability, his Deceptive Shadow and Deceptive abilities were suppressed.

This allowed his collapsing mind to be relieved.

Qi Long was overjoyed by this discovery.

If the Demonic Lair had this ability, he would no longer fear the danger of losing control.

Furthermore, if he conducted a second Deceptive fusion here, would it be much easier?

He was eager to experiment, with the goal of obtaining Jia Zhengyi's important puzzle piece, the Deceptive Cloak.

He had been in contact with Jia Zhengyi for a long time, but Jia Zhengyi had not agreed to trade with him.

Until recently, Jia Zhengyi unexpectedly contacted him first.

Qi Long was extremely excited, but to prevent Jia Zhengyi from seeing through his intentions, he had to act rationally.

He had a mentality of 'if I get it, I'll be happy; if I don't get the Deceptive Cloak, it's also fine'.

But in reality, he was desperately craving it.

Forcibly taking it would be difficult. The Exorcism Department in Southern City...
