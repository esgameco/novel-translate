However...

The busiest thing these days was not a person, but a precious treasure.

Its name was the Heavenly-Earth Smelting Furnace.

Yes, this Heavenly-Earth Smelting Furnace had been the busiest these past few days.

The fierce beasts brought back by the four factions and the scattered cultivators were all integrated and refined into essence blood by it.

The cultivators who had fully comprehended the Contemplation Diagram of the Heavenly-Earth Smelting Furnace could also refine them themselves, but the efficiency was very low.

But the Heavenly-Earth Smelting Furnace accepted all comers, no matter how many, and would refine them all at once.

There were two options:

1. Personal refinement, which required the cultivator to operate it themselves.

2. Fully automated, where the Heavenly-Earth Smelting Furnace would refine one-tenth of the beast's essence blood.

No matter how much, it would always be one-tenth.

Many people chose the fully automated option, letting the Heavenly-Earth Smelting Furnace take one-tenth of the essence blood.

This was much faster, as the Heavenly-Earth Smelting Furnace could refine everything in a short time.

Personal refinement was too slow and consumed too much of the cultivator's own essence blood, even though it could be recovered. With the time spent, it was better to go kill more beasts.

It's not that people didn't want to refine it themselves to avoid losing one-tenth, but the queues during peak hours were too long, and everyone was too busy to wait for the slow personal refinement.

Personal refinement could indeed increase one's control, but the time simply didn't allow for it.

Later, Xia Qing in the Merit Hall saw that there were too many scattered cultivators, and each one refining individually was a waste of time.

Therefore, he formulated a plan that required at least 20 people in a group, so that 20 people's beast captures could be resolved at once.

The scattered cultivators also agreed, as individual refinement was too slow, and they were all annoyed by it themselves.

Moreover, the powerful factions of the four groups were also very dissatisfied, thinking that since we were all refining together, the time was being wasted by the scattered cultivators.

The scattered cultivators, in order to avoid conflict and trouble, consciously formed groups of 20 people.

This solved the problem of the Heavenly-Earth Smelting Furnace being too busy.

As an expert at surreptitiously consuming, this Furnace had been improving every moment these past few days, and if it continued for another ten days or so, it would definitely catch up to the Chengmu and Guiqu Great Refiners.

Of course, the Guiqu Great Refiner and Tongtian Chengmu were not idle either. Although there were not many cultivators who had fully comprehended the Contemplation Diagram, even a mosquito's leg was still meat.

Yan Lai, Jiu Tong, Ming Hui and the others, each time they refined a beast, seven-tenths of the true essence was devoured by them. Of the remaining three-tenths, two-tenths were used to condense their sea of consciousness' contemplation objects, and one-tenth was refined to strengthen their will.

A seven-three division was quite a capital.

But this was normal, as they couldn't use any more even if they were given more.

Moreover, these abilities were bestowed by the Guiqu Great Refiner and Tongtian Chengmu, so even if they didn't give the three-tenths, it would be the same. Giving three-tenths was already generous.

So the Chengmu and Guiqu Great Refiner were also growing stronger, just with a much smaller effect compared to the Heavenly-Earth Smelting Furnace.

However, the Guiqu Great Refiner still had a backup plan, continuously refining the Guiqu Essence from the small worlds, and its true essence had already reached 3.9, catching up to the Tongtian Chengmu.

As for the Tongtian Chengmu, apart from the cultivators' offerings, its recent progress was not great, and it was even injured yesterday, which could be considered the most unfortunate.
