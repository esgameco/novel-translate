"Yes, I was summoned by the Deputy Director, who said a major event had occurred, that the path of the Martial Way still had a future, and that humanity's hope had arrived."

"I was informed by the Vice Director of the Martial Arts, who said a transcendent martial arts master would be giving a lecture soon. I'm still a bit confused, as it seems you all didn't know about this either."

The gathered crowd discussed this after assembling in the training hall.

The words "transcendent martial arts" were so powerfully alluring.

Suddenly, the news that this could be achieved left everyone deeply puzzled and shocked.

"That's impossible, how can one achieve transcendent martial arts without losing control? Didn't they say there was still no solution? Wasn't Master Liu, our former senior, at the peak of martial arts mastery, but had to leave our sect because he feared losing control?"

"Indeed, Master Liu was a natural martial arts genius, reaching the peak in just three years, but had to temporarily leave the populated areas because there was no path forward. I heard his strength reached 6,000 kilograms a year ago."

"As for powerful experts, I've also seen some. There was a Taoist priest at the headquarters whose strength reached 7,000 kilograms. I don't know how he did it, but I've heard it's related to willpower."

"Willpower is so mysterious. People have been researching it for many years but still haven't found a solution."

The discussions among the crowd continued back and forth.

Until the three Deputy Directors and the Director arrived.

The crowd then stopped their discussions and looked up at the leaders on the stage.

Director Sun Hui's gaze was resolute, dispelling the lingering confusion.

Wu Gang was the most excited, his excitement written all over his face, his thick beard unable to conceal it.

"Quiet down."

Sun Hui said solemnly, "Now, please welcome the transcendent martial artist, Master Xuanhuang, to guide us on the path forward."

As soon as he said these words, an uproar erupted.

Xuanhuang?

Everyone was very familiar with him. He had just entered the realm of martial artists a mere two weeks ago, and had since shown remarkable progress.

To say he had achieved the transcendent level, it would be more accurate to say that about Master Liu.

Among them was Liu Jinquan, who was completely dumbfounded.

Not just them, even the three Directors who had not yet seen Xuanhuang were filled with anticipation.

Believing it was one thing, but without seeing it, they still had doubts in their hearts.

Suddenly!

A thunderous rumble swept through the entire hall.

A dazzling golden light could be seen emanating from one of the entrances, so brilliant it was intoxicating.

Accompanied by an overwhelming sense of pressure.

All present were martial artists, though mostly at the disciple level. Yet this level of pressure, the stronger the cultivator, the more intensely they felt it.

The four Directors on stage felt it the most strongly.

If Xuanhuang's pressure had been directed at any one of them, they might have collapsed on the spot.

Instantly, the hall fell silent.

No words were needed, this pressure alone conveyed everything.

If this was not transcendent, then what was?

Striding as if through the void, Xuanhuang came to the center and withdrew his pressure.

The intense oppressive feeling immediately vanished.

The Directors were convinced, their excitement evident on their faces as they gazed intently at Xuanhuang.

"Greetings everyone, I am Xuanhuang. I have made some progress in the Martial Way, and have come today to share my insights with you."

His gentle tone was a stark contrast to the overwhelming pressure he had just exuded. It was hard to believe this amiable Xuanhuang was the same person.

"Applaud, applaud!"

Wu Gang's rough voice bellowed, his hands clapping loudly.

"Applause erupted"

The awestruck crowd hurriedly joined in the applause.

Regardless of Xuanhuang's previous state, at this moment he was undoubtedly transcendent.

As for anyone being dissatisfied? Unlikely. Even if he was only at the first level, this was a difference on a fundamental level of existence.

All the people present combined would not be a match for a transcendent.

The distinction between heaven and earth, challenging one's superiors would be impossible.
