Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

What an incredible pair of words.

This was a world beyond the ordinary, but to hear someone say they were extraordinary was still shocking.

"Lord Xuanhuang, how did you do it?"

Wu Gang looked at Xuanhuang still floating in the sky, firmly believing this was the extraordinary.

And Xuanhuang did not seem out of control or frantic.

"I'll explain later."

Xuanhuang slowly descended from the sky.

Thinking of the purpose of their coming here, Xuanhuang knew he needed to go down the mountain for now.

But he had no intention of leaving for good.

This place was truly a geomantic treasure trove, not only having the blood pool, but also the undeveloped secret realm of the Blood Moon World.

Going back to the martial arts hall or going home would not be very convenient.

In the nearby valley, there was an abandoned resort.

A landscape garden, and there were four or five multi-story hotels and inns, if cleaned up properly, it would be a good place to stay.

Since it has already been abandoned, might as well tidy it up and live there himself.

Xuanhuang really liked the ancient architectural scenic area.

As for the millstone at home, he wouldn't go stir it up for now, wait until he was confident enough to deal with it.

"Let's go down the mountain."

Seeing the two still in a daze, Xuanhuang said.

"Yes!"

Wu Gang hurriedly nodded.

This person seemed carefree, but was actually quite smart.

His attitude towards Xuanhuang immediately changed, and he had an obedient look.

Jin Ning was a bit lacking, still looking dazed.

The things that needed to be cleaned up, Xuanhuang had all placed in his spiritual space.

After entering the extraordinary realm, the area he could control reached one kilometer, a full ten times more than before, and very stable.

Furthermore, after his body entered the extraordinary, Xuanhuang felt his physical strength reach 100,000 kilograms.

Of course, at this moment, to boast of strength would no longer be appropriate.

The extraordinary will, and the extraordinary energy that comes with the extraordinary body, are the most important.

In the car...

Xuanhuang closed his eyes and sat in the back seat, the dazed Jin Ning bit his lip to wake himself up.

Wu Gang did not dare let Jin Ning, who was in such a state, drive, so he had to drive himself.

Half an hour later, the three arrived at the Southern City Martial Arts Hall.

"Do you have any orders?"

The car stopped in front of the martial arts hall, Wu Gang did not get out of the car but asked instead.

Xuanhuang opened his eyes and said, "You can gather the current senior members and martial artists at the martial arts hall, just say that the martial way still has a future, and those who want to continue to progress can wait in the training hall."

Hearing this, Wu Gang's breathing became heavy with excitement.

He loved this kind of news so much.

On the way here, he was still thinking about this matter, thinking about how to get Xuanhuang to reveal the secret of extraordinary control without losing control.

He even calculated what kind of price he would have to pay.

But Xuanhuang's words made him feel his vision was too small.

No need for any heavy price, Xuanhuang... no, Lord Xuanhuang's vision was truly broad.

"Jin Ning, you take Lord Xuanhuang to rest in the No. 1 private room."

Wu Gang instructed Jin Ning.

Nothing needs Xuanhuang's involvement, he will handle it himself.

"Okay!"

Jin Ning nodded.

Xuanhuang thought, the big bearded guy really knows how to get things done.

He then followed Jin Ning out of the car, while Wu Gang ran enthusiastically towards the martial arts hall.

The unrestrained smile on his face, one wouldn't know if he thought his wife had just given birth.

Until he had escorted Xuanhuang to the private room to rest, Jin Ning didn't dare say a single unnecessary word.

After closing the door, he finally let out a sigh of relief.

Even though Xuanhuang had suppressed all his aura, the instinctive impulse and the previous shock were not something that could be easily eliminated in a short time.

Calming himself down, he also quickly headed to the senior office of the martial arts hall.

"What?"

"Wu Gang, are you sure what you're saying is true, and not just talking nonsense?"
