After the haunted tree in Fulin Community was dealt with, aside from the recently disappeared mysterious alley, there were no other incidents that required Xuanhuang's intervention.

"Old High, how is the renovation of Qingyun Mountain going?"

"Who?"

"Deputy Director Xia Qing. She said it was the idea of Li Tong and Master Ma, and she didn't hide anything from me. They want to be closer to Master Xuan, to get the advantage of being near the water."

Sun Hui nodded in understanding and asked, "Can we make space for them?"

"Director, Qingyun Mountain is very large, and the tourist area we are renovating is not small. Leaving a little space for the Taoist Hall won't affect us."

High Jian continued, "Moreover, Xia Qing said they will expand, and this is just a temporary location for them. After their expansion, they will return the space to the Martial Arts Hall."

Sun Hui understood and said, "Then you handle it as you see fit. As long as it doesn't affect us, it's fine to provide them with convenience."

After all, they have had a good relationship for many years, and the Taoist Hall and Martial Arts Hall have never had conflicts, but have even been allies in many battles.

"Understood, Director."

Sun Hui then said, "The second matter, in the next ten days or so, how is the progress on the Heavenly Furnace Visualization Diagram?"

He looked at the senior martial artists present at the meeting.

The deputy director in charge of this matter, Song Hongliang, stood up and said, "Director, according to Master Xuan's new classification, our current martial artists at the Refining Body mid-stage, that is, above the fourth layer of martial disciples, are considered the early stage. The seventh layer is the late stage, and the first three layers are the initial stage."

"Currently, our martial artists at the fourth Refining Body layer have the best comprehension of the Visualization Diagram, and some have already reached the stage of condensing the Copper Furnace with three feet, while the slowest ones have at least comprehended one foot of the Heavenly Furnace."

"These are not the fastest ones in our hall. The fastest is Jin Ning, who has already comprehended half of the Visualization Diagram and is making rapid progress. He is confident of reaching 90% in half a month. As for fully comprehending the Visualization Diagram, it depends on luck."

Hearing this, Sun Hui understood.

He himself had not fallen behind, and would occasionally observe the Visualization Diagram when he had time.

Becoming the Director was not only due to his personal abilities, but also his powerful cultivation.

After Xuanhuang's teachings, their system has become more complete.

For example, Sun Hui's own cultivation is at the fifth Refining Body layer, just not reaching the extreme limit of the sixth layer like Master Liu.

In the past week, aside from comprehending the Visualization Diagram, the members of the Martial Arts Hall have been systematically correcting their previous mistakes.

Xuanhuang's "Nine Refinements of the Furnace" is too advanced and more complete in its system.

For example, the book states that if one's strength reaches 7,000 kilograms, they can attempt to break through to the extraordinary realm, but Xuanhuang does not recommend it. It's better to aim for 9,000 kilograms to have a greater grasp.

If one is a genius and doesn't mind spending time, they can even attempt to reach the limit of 10,000, 11,000, or 12,000.

The path has been clearly laid out, the rest is up to one's own destiny.

Of course, the most important thing now is to comprehend the Visualization Diagram. This is the prerequisite for everything. To make physical progress, one's will must keep up.

Even if one cannot catch up with the fusion of techniques, they still need to fully comprehend the Visualization Diagram.

Therefore, each meeting is not only for handling incidents, but also a way to exchange insights.

So Sun Hui asked, "Any insights to share?"
