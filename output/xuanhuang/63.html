Chapter 63

Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

He has already been revered by the Confucianists, Taoists, and Buddhists as the reincarnation of the Buddha, the Taoist Patriarch, and the Sage.

If one knew, they would not admit it. Not to mention whether these claims are true or not.

Even if they are not true, he is just himself, not the reincarnation of anyone else, and does not need to boast about his background.

After seeing off the crowd, he began his cultivation.

In the sea of qi in his dantian.

The will covered the golden sea of qi, and a 30-zhang (about 100 meters) sea of qi was grasped by a large hand.

"Condense!"

A loud shout, and a 6-zhang (about 20 meters) golden celestial pillar condensed.

This process lasted for about 10 minutes.

The will's large hand, grasping the 6-zhang celestial pillar, transformed into a rainbow light and ascended to the peak of the first celestial pillar in the sea of qi mist.

Each celestial pillar needs to be farther than the previous one.

To make the celestial pillars reach farther, one can only ascend the first celestial pillar.

"Go!"

Exerting all his strength, the will's large hand threw the 6-zhang blood-qi celestial pillar into the distance.

"Rumble!"

The 6-zhang celestial pillar thundered as it landed, standing in the sea of qi mist farther than the first celestial pillar.

Immediately, the sea of qi mist surged and churned, trying to topple the blood-qi celestial pillar.

"Stabilize!"

The large hand formed by the Xuanhuang will pressed down on the second 6-zhang celestial pillar.

After a while, the sea of qi mist no longer churned, and the second celestial pillar was firmly rooted in the sea of qi mist.

"Phew!"

Xuanhuang breathed a sigh of relief.

Although the sea of qi was his own, it was also the sea of qi of all living beings.

The sea of qi is endless, and the 30-zhang he controls is his own world.

This endless mist is not controlled by Xuanhuang, but is the sea of qi of all living beings under the laws of heaven and earth.

It's just that each living being's sea of qi is like a separate dimension, and no matter how much it expands, it cannot be contacted.

The large hand formed by the will returned to the 30-zhang golden sea of qi under his control.

"The second celestial pillar is established. As long as I can condense and fully integrate another 3 zhang in the next two days, I can proceed to the second layer of will cultivation."

Seeing this, Xuanhuang withdrew his mind and left the sea of qi.

He has already taken up residence on Qingyun Mountain, and his previous plans have all been completed.

Next, he needs to formulate a plan for the next period of time.

First,

Tomorrow, Xu Ruyi will come to see him to discuss the strange situation in South City.

Xuanhuang actually hopes that the more strangeness, the better, as long as it does not exceed his control, he should be able to resolve it all.

In addition, due to the Linggen Tong Tian Building Wood standing in reality, combined with the blessing of the small world's Tianyi,

He has obtained a spiritual land of ten li (about 5 kilometers) in all directions, where cultivators can cultivate, and some uncontaminated precious herbs can also be planted.

Although it will take time, it is still a good start for alchemy.

Second,

There is the strangeness of the Blood Pool, which is the second place he has established.

It is not covered by the Tong Tian Building Wood's coverage, so after meeting with Xu Ruyi, he should go to the Blood Pool secret realm to investigate.

While at it, he should also tidy it up and use it as a catalyst for fierce beasts.

Third,

The appointment with Village Chief Li Baokang of the Li family in the Blood Moon World.

He had said that he would go to the Blood Moon World at the latest in a month, and that time is almost up. He wonders how the Blood Moon World is doing?

When he went to the Blood Moon World that time, it was just a hurried visit, and it still needs to be explored.

But the Blood Moon World is very large, and it would be a waste of time for him to explore it alone.

Xuanhuang plans to send people from the Wuguan to explore, as long as they are careful.

The Li family village is also doing well, and the strangeness of the Blood Moon only poses a small danger as long as one does not actively make contact with it.

The most dangerous thing is the fierce beasts catalyzed by the Blood Moon.

"Could it be that the Blood Moon is like the moon in legends, able to pour down the divine elixir?"

Xuanhuang wondered.

"Nevermind, I'll explore it slowly."

Fourth,

There is also the underground demon cave that he learned about from Qi Long, which still needs demon stones to solidify.

If there is time, Xuanhuang will go and take a look himself.

This demonic cave that Qi Long discovered was only the surface, and it is said to be very large, but Xuanhuang does not know what is in the deeper mist.

The fourth...

The fourth is the threat of those few cults online, which needs to be paid attention to.

After all, it is not yet at the level of the second-stage Penetrating Mystery, and there are many second-stage spirit masters in the cults.

Although it is not to the point of being assassinated by a third-stage Penetrating Mystery expert, arranging a second-stage Penetrating Mystery one is still possible.

I can survive in the hands of a second-stage Penetrating Mystery spirit master, but I cannot fight them head-on, let alone suppress them.

This needs to be noted.

The fifth is a new problem.

After this, there will be more cultivators coming here out of curiosity.

I don't need to worry about this, as there are people from the martial arts hall and the Tao hall, and there are also the three leaders of Confucianism, Taoism, and Buddhism.

I welcome all who come to seek the Tao here.

"Finally!"

Xuanhuang thought of the last problem.

It is the problem of forging tools and drawing talismans.

Currently, whether it is the martial way or the cultivation of immortality, the methods are too few.

It is unlikely that they can enter the extraordinary realm in a short time, so auxiliary means are needed.

As for auxiliary means, nothing is more direct than magical tools and talismans.

Thinking of this, Xuanhuang took out the Heavenly-Earth Smelting Furnace and the Returning Void Great Grinder.

He also took out a strange and powerful leg bone, which was one of the three strange things preserved by the martial arts hall before.

The blood cloth and light bulb are not suitable for forging into magical tools for now.

This leg bone is not bad.

Xuanhuang threw it into the Heavenly-Earth Smelting Furnace.

"Forge!"

Activating the Heavenly-Earth Smelting Furnace, it began to slowly rotate.

Xuanhuang's will and the will of the small world permeated the furnace, converging on the leg bone.

Strange things are all natural treasures, they are all rare treasure embryos.

They are all formed by the rules of heaven and earth. Even if they look insignificant, they are still precious.

Xuanhuang intends to erase the innate instinct of the leg bone.

However, this so-called instinct is actually a chaotic consciousness born from the absorption of the resentment of heaven and earth.

As long as its chaotic consciousness is erased and a new sound consciousness is endowed, the leg bone can become a treasure.

Moreover, it still retains its ability to devour bones and evolve, making it an evolvable treasure.

In fact, one could even say that strange things are all evolvable treasures.

In the furnace, even the sealed strange thing began to struggle violently before its consciousness was erased.

Being sealed is just being sealed, but erasing the chaotic consciousness is equivalent to a direct rebirth.

The strange leg bone does not want to be reborn, it just wants to remain in a dazed state.

"Still struggling, how many people want the opportunity for rebirth, but you don't cherish it? Being in a dazed state like this is so unproductive," Xuanhuang muttered.

Thinking, why are you struggling, a newly born resentful consciousness? It's better to be reborn quickly.

"Be suppressed!"

"Rumble!"

The cosmic power of the small world began to increase.

How strong can the consciousness of a just-born resentful being be? Although it struggled violently, it was still erased by the will of heaven and earth.

"Come!"

After the chaotic consciousness of the strange thing was erased, Xuanhuang quickly took it out of the furnace.

"Go!"

He quickly threw the leg bone into the Returning Void Great Grinder.

Endowing a new spiritual consciousness is the Returning Void Great Grinder's most skilled technique, as it is an expert in this field.

A thread of subtle true meaning flew out from the Great Grinder and merged into the empty leg bone.

"Sizzle!"

Like the sound of branding, the leg bone was imprinted with the true meaning belonging to the Great Grinder.

It's as if the Returning Void Great Grinder is the source of the leg bone.
