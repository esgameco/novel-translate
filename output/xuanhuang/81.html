As long as they moved quickly, the Confucians, Taoists, and Buddhists would still fall behind.

The active performance of the Confucians, Taoists, and Buddhists was due to the strong reasons of the Martial Dao Alliance.

The rapid refinement of the Esoteric Inscriptions had quickly narrowed the gap. As long as they completed the comprehension of the Observation Diagram, the Confucians, Taoists, and Buddhists would be no match for them.

Although they didn't have very strong offensive abilities, you also couldn't break through their defenses.

Rumor had it that the three old fellows of the Confucians, Taoists, and Buddhists were going to refine the fourth Esoteric Inscription, which was a mid-level Esoteric Inscription with offensive abilities. This would be a headache.

At the same time, the three Confucians, Taoists, and Buddhists, as well as Dou Ming and Ma Dazhang who had joined the Taoist school, also received the message.

The ten people were summoned by the Mysterious Black and Yellow again, and after quickly instructing their subordinates, they hurried to the Mysterious Black and Yellow's villa.

Soon, the ten people gathered.

"Everyone, the Mysterious Master is waiting for you. Please come in," Sun Tong welcomed them at the door.

"Thank you."

"Please sit down."

"Thank you, Mysterious Master."

The group half-sat down, sitting up straight, just like subordinates being summoned by a superior.

Jiu Tong immediately stood up: "It is thanks to your grace, Mysterious Master... I have gained a lot, but I am still unable to achieve the miraculous transformations like you. I can only erase the distorted consciousness, but I have not yet refined the..."

"It's been a while."

Recalling that he had been guarding this place just over a month ago, time had indeed passed quickly, and things had changed.

Liu Jintian, the familiar "Uncle Liu", noticed Xuanhuang's arrival. He was now in charge of the development of the Blood Pool Anomaly.

Adhering to his principle of not causing trouble for others, Xuanhuang called him "Old Liu" instead.

This form of address brought a wide smile to Liu Jintian's face.

"Master Xuan, you've come."

"Mm, let me take a look."

"Please, come in."

To be honest, Xuanhuang was very cautious. He had guarded the pool for over ten days before, but had never actually entered it.

He was afraid that his cultivation would not be enough, and he would be harmed by the Blood Pool Anomaly.

This was his first time entering.

As time passed, the Blood Pool Secret Realm would continuously encroach on the surroundings, until it was completely integrated into the real world, becoming a part of it.

This was the result of the merging of the worlds.

The Blue Star was expanding every day due to the countless spaces merging in.

This was still considered a relatively good outcome.

Some areas directly shattered and transformed into strange domains, adding new land to the Blue Star.

"You all wait outside, I'll go in alone."

He said this to the group.

This was Xuanhuang's first time entering the Blood Pool Secret Realm.

"Early Dao Realm, not quite at the peak."

It was still not as powerful as the previous Corpse Tree Anomaly and the Ominous Bell.

But with the support of being a secret realm, it could reach the peak of the Early Dao Realm.

"As long as the beings who enter can withstand the spiritual soul assault, they can avoid being devoured."

"But ordinary people would find it difficult to resist the assault. A safe path needs to be carved out."

Xuanhuang found the area that needed to be changed.

"But no need to rush. Let's first raise the Void Saber to the Early Dao Realm level."

"Hum hum!"

The appearing Void Saber was still full of enthusiasm as always.

Whenever it was summoned by Xuanhuang, it would vibrate excitedly, as if saying: "Use me and you will be invincible, with me you are unstoppable. Master, let's fight together!"
