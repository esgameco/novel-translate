On the other side.

"What?" Xu Ruyi paused.

"Is this a Second Realm Anomaly?"

Xu Ruyi looked at Xuan Huang gravely.

"I know it's a Second Realm one. My cultivation has improved a bit recently, and I've gained some insights. As a Second Realm Anomaly, I may not be able to suppress it, but it won't pose a danger to me either. Even if I want to escape, I don't think anyone could stop me."

Xuan Huang spoke directly, without joking.

"Are you sure?"

"Yes, I'm very sure. I won't joke around with my own life, nor with the lives of the 300 trapped people."

Gao Xiaolan, who had already heard their conversation, remained silent, only looking at the two men repeatedly.

"Alright."

Saving people is like putting out a fire. Xu Ruyi didn't think too long, hesitating for a moment before agreeing.

Although he hadn't interacted with Xuan Huang for long, Xuan Huang's steadfast demeanor had deeply impressed him.

"Be careful."

Xu Ruyi said to Xuan Huang.

"Don't worry."

Xuan Huang smiled at him, indicating that he would be fine.

Immediately, he flickered and disappeared into the misty world.

"Ah, just his lightning-fast speed alone, I believe he wasn't exaggerating. Ordinary Anomalies won't be able to hold him back." Seeing Xuan Huang vanish in a flash, Gao Xiaolan suddenly felt very confident.

"That's true." Xu Ruyi agreed. That speed, he didn't even notice when Xuan Huang entered the misty realm.

Probing with his will, Xuan Huang could see wisps of energy formed from fear, drifting towards the impenetrable darkness.

Xuan Huang immediately summoned the Void Saber.

Whenever the Void Saber appeared, it trembled, indicating that its master was about to fight alongside it again. As a weapon of slaughter, it greatly yearned for such a scene.

"Absorb the energy of fear."

Xuan Huang commanded the Void Saber.

The Void Saber also had the ability to absorb and strengthen itself with fear. And after being empowered by abilities like Dread, Devour, and Soul Annihilation, it had become exceptionally adept at this.

Suddenly, streams of fear-formed threads, like flowing rivers, came tracing the Void Saber's trail.

"Crackle, crackle!"

At this moment.

A rolling sound stood out particularly clearly, even drowning out the surrounding cries and screams from the mist.

Xuan Huang looked up, only to see a severed head rolling towards him.

The face was bleeding from all seven orifices, looking like a red ball at a glance.

"Can you see my body? It hurts so much, where did my body go, can you see it?"

The words, imbued with spiritual impact and hallucinations, came from the severed head's mouth.

If this were an ordinary person, their spirit would have already been shattered.

"No, I can't."

Xuan Huang replied simply.

"Can you see my body? It hurts so much, where did my body go, can you see it?"

The same words, the same spiritual impact.

It seemed to be a mechanical repetition of the same phrase. Xuan Huang had thought he could have a conversation.

This kind of tactic might work on ordinary people, but it was useless against him.

"I see, you took my body, give it back, give it back..."

Suddenly, the seven bleeding orifices on the head began spewing black smoke, which howled and charged towards Xuan Huang.

"Boom!"

Just as it reached the Void Saber Xuan Huang had manifested, it was blasted apart.

"So it's just about absorbing some fear energy, and this lackey shows up to deal with me? Well, that's easy, keep absorbing."

Xuan Huang knew the appearance of the severed head was due to the Void Saber absorbing the fear energy.

It was like fighting the Mysterious Alley for its food, so the head showed up.

Immediately, Xuan Huang enveloped the Void Saber with his will, letting it continue absorbing.

When he first entered, with it being a Second Realm Anomaly, he couldn't immediately locate the main body of the Mysterious Alley.

The mist was like a maze, and he could hear many agonized cries and pleas for help, but going towards them didn't necessarily lead to real people.

Without a clear target, Xuan Huang was considering using his domain.
