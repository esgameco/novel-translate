Time passed in a flash, three days had gone by.

On this day, Xuanhuang finally refined the complete Heavenly Pillar.

With the control of his will, the final three-zhang Heavenly Pillar completely merged with the previous six-zhang ones.

Boom!

Xuanhuang felt the sea of qi shaking.

It was as if the sealed sea of qi had opened a gap, and the place that connected was the entire heaven and earth.

This kind of connection had certain risks.

If not carefully controlled, the connection of the power of heaven and earth would destroy the sea of qi, and if the sea of qi collapsed, even if the cultivator could survive, they would be almost ruined.

"Stabilize!"

Xuanhuang gritted his teeth and shouted.

His will transformed into the will of heaven, firmly guarding the trembling sea of qi.

About three minutes later, it was as if the two magnetic fields had finally collided and synchronized.

The great magnetic field of heaven and earth and the small magnetic field of the sea of qi finally resonated and synchronized.

Therefore, the Dao of Heaven has a surplus and a deficiency, and the power of heaven and earth found that they had such a weak little brother.

Suddenly, the protective love surged, the strong power of heaven and earth, accompanied by the distortion of the will of heaven, the incompleteness of the Dao and Fa, and other existences, surged through the medium of the Heavenly Pillar.

Fortunately, only one Heavenly Pillar served as the medium, so the power of heaven and earth drawn was not much.

If it were the complete three Heavenly Pillars, Xuanhuang's current will would not be able to support it.

One Heavenly Pillar connecting the power of heaven and earth is like opening a small mountain path, two is a wide cement road, and three is directly a high-speed expressway.

This needs to be done step by step.

But the thick love of the power of heaven and earth, Xuanhuang still felt uncomfortable.

At this time, the importance of the Heavenly Gate on the furnace became crucial.

After entering a certain amount of the power of heaven and earth, Xuanhuang gritted his teeth and controlled it.

With a rumbling sound, he closed the portal connecting heaven and earth, finally stopping the protective love of the great elder of heaven and earth.

Inside the furnace, the great elder of heaven and earth sent everything, not only the power of heaven and earth, but also the distorted rules and so on also entered.

However, these were all what Xuanhuang needed.

To temper the will of the first stage of Xuanbian, he needed these as kindling.

"Refine!"

Xuanhuang did not hesitate, and his will transformed into a rainbow that was invested into the furnace.

The power of blood and qi began to gush from the Bubbling Spring, turning into golden burning flames.

"Roaring!"

The great fire burned, forging Xuanhuang's will, the distorted spiritual mechanism rules in the furnace as the hammer, his divine soul wielding the hammer.

Bit by bit, forging Xuanhuang's will.

Finally, after three hours.

The tempering brought by the first time of drawing the power of heaven and earth was completed.

Xuanhuang clearly felt that his will had grown again.

It had been a long time since it had grown, and the cultivation time of the first stage of Xuanbian was much slower than before.

Just refining the Heavenly Gate for seven days and the Heavenly Pillar for seven days, half a month had already passed.

Xuanhuang divided the first stage of Xuanbian into twelve layers, and each Heavenly Pillar represented one layer.

One layer after another, it required the will of two layers to draw the power of heaven and earth brought by two Heavenly Pillars, otherwise it would be very dangerous.

This time was the first tempering, and Xuanhuang found that after such tempering ten times, he could step into the second layer of Xuanbian.

"If the blood and qi are sufficient, even if it's once a day, it would still take ten days, and the twelve layers would take at least half a year, after all, there's no guarantee it won't slow down later, or there might be something to delay it."

"Adding the engraving of the Heavenly Gate and the refining of the Heavenly Pillar, it would take nearly a year to advance to the second stage of Tong Xuan."

A year is actually not long, and even very short.

After breaking through the first stage of Xuanbian, for Xuanhuang with a lifespan of 300 years of the five great cycles, it is very, very short.

After breaking through the extraordinary Xuanbian, he sensed that his lifespan had reached 300 years.

The reason he didn't mention it before was that it was not representative.

Because currently, he is the only one, and he doesn't know if it will be the same for other cultivators who enter the first stage of Xuanbian.

According to the records of the Martial Pavilion, the cultivators in the previous small world who cultivated the Qi Refining stage had a lifespan of 200 years.

And the martial artists in the small martial world realm, their extraordinary realm was between 120-180 years, in general, it was difficult to exceed 200 years.

Xuanhuang's 300-year lifespan was definitely not the norm, it was a manifestation of his refined essence, qi, and spirit, and his breakthrough of the 12,000 jin giant force limit.

A year is really very short.

Even if Xuanhuang opens the path and completes it, and other cultivators follow his cultivation, those who can advance from the first stage of Xuanbian to the second stage of Tong Xuan within a year would be top-tier geniuses.
