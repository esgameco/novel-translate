Gao Jian nodded to indicate yes.

Gao Jian said, "We've only just started considering this recently. Before, we didn't really need to. But we haven't figured out a solution yet."

He continued, "Whether it's our alchemists or weapon forgers, they are all ordinary people, with not many martial artists among them. Asking them to use ordinary thinking to come up with extraordinary weapons and medicines is beyond their capabilities."

He was right. Without reaching the extraordinary realm, they cannot forge extraordinary medicines and weapons.

The most skilled alchemists and weapon forgers must be true experts.

"Have the spiritual herbs in the small worlds also been damaged by the mysterious invasion?" Xuan Huang asked.

"Yes, Master Xuan. The spiritual herbs in the small worlds have been disrupted by the resurgence of the mysterious forces, with some even turning into mysterious plants."

Xuan Huang nodded, this was about what he had expected.

"How many secret realms do our martial arts school control?"

Hearing this, Gao Jian looked ashamed. "Master Xuan, since our martial arts school didn't play a big role against the mysterious forces before, the secret realms in the southern region and surrounding areas have all been controlled by the Exorcism Department. We currently only have five. And those were only given to us to observe after the Exorcism Department sealed the mysterious forces within."

"However..."

Gao Jian continued, "In recent years, two of them have already collapsed and completely merged with the Blue Star, becoming areas permeated by mysterious forces. The remaining three aren't doing too well either, and will likely collapse within a few years."

Xuan Huang understood now.

They were just the dregs that the Exorcism Department didn't care about - the small secret realms that would merge with reality the fastest during this inter-world fusion.

These numerous small secret realms, even if they had mysterious forces, were better off that way, as they would merge with reality slowly if the mysterious forces were sealed.

Soon after, they would become new regions of the real world.

This means the changes to the Blue Star must be enormous, with its area likely expanded many times over.

Comparatively speaking, this inter-world fusion is relatively gentle, not the kind of world-destroying fusion.

It's just a collision and fusion of rules, with some mysterious fragments forming.

Xuan Huang sometimes wondered, how far will this fusion develop? Why has the Blue Star become a key point?

In the vast universe, are there other key points like the Blue Star? If so, what kind of extraordinary powers will they develop? Or will they become realms of mysterious forces instead.

And what are the mysterious forces on other planets like? Xuan Huang understood that mysterious forces are the inevitable result of the collision of worlds, so they must exist on other planets as well, even if to a lesser degree.

After all, in the broad sense, the universe itself is a living being, as are the planets. As long as there are beings with desires on other planets, mysterious forces will inevitably arise.

He was thinking a bit too far ahead. Reaching the second realm can allow one to fly out of the Blue Star, but to truly traverse the universe requires the third realm transformation, with the true self condensing spatial teleportation.

For now, Xuan Huang's teleportation relies on the power of the Abyss, which doesn't really count.
