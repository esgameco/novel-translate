Without any willpower cultivation techniques, their qi and spiritual cultivation had reached the limits of willpower.

Fortunately, these three were at the fourth layer of willpower, otherwise their seventh layer of qi and spirit would have been out of control.

From Qilong's memories, Xuanhuang roughly determined the identities of these three.

They should be the three directors of the Zhenxie Headquarters, the three legendary figures of the Confucian, Taoist, and Buddhist sects of the Xia Kingdom.

Daoist Master Ma's willpower was only just reaching the third layer.

This time, the three with sufficient accumulation had already grasped 50% of the Visualization Diagram of the Heavenly and Earthly Furnace. Xuanhuang felt that for them to reach the seventh layer by the end would be simple.

As long as he gave them a little time, at most a week, they would definitely be able to fully comprehend the Visualization Diagram.

A love for talent suddenly arose.

With such three geniuses, it would be interesting to provide them with guidance.

Thinking of this, Xuanhuang's will became more inclined towards the three, and the speed of the spiritual resonance flow increased.

The three suddenly felt a tremor in their bodies, and discovered that the speed of the coagulation of the azure qi into the Heavenly and Earthly Furnace was accelerating.

The three's mental state was also continuing to be consumed.

The three understood that they were receiving special care.

The three were both grateful and wholeheartedly admiring, wondering if they could truly achieve this level of ability. They dared not be certain.

But Xuanhuang definitely could, because it would be best for this kind of genius to condense the complete Visualization Diagram as soon as possible.

In this way, their own insights and accumulations would also be greater.

Sometimes the insights of geniuses could be equivalent to those of tens of thousands of ordinary people.

In this way...

As time slowly passed, more and more people could no longer persist.

The remaining ones were all masters above the fourth layer of physical, spiritual, and qi cultivation.

Of course, there were also spirit cultivators, who had all been kicked out just now.

They unconsciously let out a sigh of relief, feeling frustrated that they could see but not learn.

Rather than this, it would be better to be kicked out.

But the seeds had been sown, and this excitement would linger with them for the rest of their lives.

Unable to resist the urge to cultivate, they would need to change their paths and seek guidance from Xuanhuang.

In fact, Xuanhuang was happy to see this, because if the spirit cultivators could cultivate, then that would be a great boon.

Not only would they contribute their insights, but also various strange rules, as well as the media that Xuanhuang could seal them with at any time.

Daoist Master Ma, the three Confucian, Taoist, and Buddhist figures, as well as Sun Hui, Wu Gang, Jin Ning, Song Hongliang, and two previous arrivals - one martial artist and one cultivator.

These two were also fortunate, and their own cultivation was strong, with the qi cultivator at the fifth layer and the martial artist at the fifth layer of physical cultivation. Their willpower was also about the same, approaching the third layer of willpower.

Until another three minutes had passed, they had all withdrawn, leaving only the perfected Jin Ning.

Xuanhuang immediately ceased the performance, and everyone fell silent to adjust their state.

After a while, everyone stood up and respectfully cupped their hands in salute.

"We thank the Venerable Master for bestowing the Dao upon us!"

The harvest was too great, a single transmission that changed their destinies.

Although there was no formal master-disciple relationship, the reality of it had already been established.

"Well done, you all have the strength and the opportunities, you are all rare talents. I hope you will not squander your own potential."
