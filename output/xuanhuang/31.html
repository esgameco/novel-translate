Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

There was a massive building as grand as a martial arts school, but the martial arts school building seemed more rugged.

This building had a more ancient and ethereal charm.

This was the Taoist temple that every city had.

It managed the entire city together with the Exorcism Department and the martial arts school, or even said to be in control of it.

In an office on the third floor.

Xia Qing, the deputy director of the Taoist temple, was handling the temple's expenditures.

"Knock, knock!"

The sound of knocking came.

Xia Qing closed the account book and said, "Come in."

After the door opened, a woman in her early twenties walked in.

"Xiao Zhao, what's the matter?"

Seeing the visitor was Zhao Xi, the socialite of the Taoist temple, Xia Qing asked.

"Master Xia, I have something to report."

"Oh? What is it, tell me."

Xia Qing was quite familiar with this socialite, who was a celebrity in the Taoist temple.

Zhao Xi was very clever, had a wide social network, and was known as an all-knowing person, not just in the entire Taoist temple, but in the whole southern city.

"Master Xia, my boyfriend told me a secret, asked me to keep it confidential, but I think it is related to our Taoist temple, even our current situation, so I want to report it to you."

Xia Qing was taken aback, "Your... I mean, your boyfriend, where does he work, what kind of secret is so important?"

She almost slipped, after all, this woman had too many boyfriends.

Zhao Xi caught the oddity, but didn't mind at all.

If she cared about that, she wouldn't have so many boyfriends.

Those who care about other people's opinions are the ones who want to save face, but those who don't care about face, how can they care about others' opinions?

"My boyfriend works at the martial arts school. They had a big event happen, I heard there appeared a extraordinary martial artist, my boyfriend said the spring of martial arts has come, and said our cultivation has no future, because we don't have the 'Mystic Master', anyway, his words were very strange, and asked me to keep it secret."

Xia Qing remained calm, although Zhao Xi's words sounded absurd.

But she didn't believe it.

What extraordinary, what incomprehensible 'Mystic Master'? What is this? In the martial arts field, not to mention the younger generation, even the elders are just assistants to the Exorcist Masters.

If these words came from someone else, Xia Qing would have paid attention, but coming from Zhao Xi's mouth, the credibility...

"Is it true?"

However, as the deputy director, she still had some social etiquette, so she asked perfunctorily.

"Master Xia, I know you don't believe me, but please believe me, my boyfriend from the martial arts school also assured me that they really have an extraordinary path."

"My boyfriend is always excitedly muttering 'will is king' 'will is king'"

"What: 'Everyone has thought of it, but only the Mystic Master has achieved my will as the will of heaven'."

Seeing Zhao Xi's earnest manner, Xia Qing was a bit confused.

But thinking of the word "extraordinary", she shook her head firmly again.

How can extraordinary be so easy?

"Alright Xiao Zhao, I understand. Later I will have someone investigate, if it's true, your contribution won't be overlooked, it will be converted into corresponding points, how about that?"

Zhao Xi then smiled brightly, "Master, whatever you say goes, the whole Taoist temple knows that Master Xia's words are trustworthy, you are the pillar supporting our temple..."

After sending Zhao Xi away,

Xia Qing let out a bitter smile, almost believing the praise herself.

This Zhao Xi really "knows how to come up with things".

Since it's like this, I'll have someone investigate, can't let my subordinates feel their words are not taken seriously.

After all, I am the pillar supporting the Taoist temple...

Look, the praise rule is starting to take effect.

...

On the martial arts school side, unaware that their hidden secret had already been exposed by the love-crazed man.

In fact, the Mystic Master didn't care at all.

Using bizarre abilities on ordinary people is not allowed openly.

Jia Zhengyi chuckled, not wanting to get involved in this.

"What's the matter? Recently, many people from the martial arts pavilion have been sacrificed, yet these people cannot hide their excitement..."

"What?"

As Wei Zixuan described it, Jia Zhengyi could not sit still.

The bizarre being that Wei Zixuan controlled was a huge bloody mouth in the palm of his hand.

This bizarre mouth could not only devour tangible objects, but also devour the memories, emotions, and other intangible aspects of ordinary people whose willpower had not reached the extraordinary.

Wei Zixuan had not hesitated to use this method to satisfy his "special" desires.

Perhaps he was once a good young man, but after controlling the bizarre, he changed.
