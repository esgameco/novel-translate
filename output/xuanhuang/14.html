As long as he takes one more step, with his qi and blood leaving his body, he can achieve defensive and offensive capabilities.

Until the time comes.

The eerie existence clearly exhibits both "terribleness" and "stupidity".

If it were an intelligent female ghost, it would definitely not provoke Xuanhuang, as there are so many easily crushed beings, why bother to provoke a cultivator.

But the Eerie Letter cannot, it can only follow the rules.

Just like last night, Xuanhuang let down his active defense.

"Whoosh—"

"Heartless man..., damn it..."

The repeated scenes were nothing more than more intense spiritual impacts.

The female eerie being had already moved from the seventh side room to the sixth room.

This time, Xuanhuang did not hurry to take action, planning to just pass the time by using the eerie being's spiritual impacts to temper himself.

He had already squeezed out the essence from the Blood Pool Eerie, so there was no reason for the Eerie Letter to be spared.

This kind of will-tempering method is free, and using the Great Elixir would cost him money, even the "guarding spring" Blood Pool Eerie would take time.

At this point, a surreal scene appeared.

The fluctuating spiritual impacts were heading towards Xuanhuang.

The curses turned into a forge.

It lasted for a long time.

Suddenly,

A pulling force came.

Xuanhuang sensed no danger, so he followed the pull.

"Hehe..."

The silvery laughter came.

A female figure appeared before Xuanhuang's eyes.

Xuanhuang's gaze swept over, and it was no longer the ancient-style courtyard.

But a riverbank in the spring warmth and blooming flowers.

Dreamlike, only the figure of the woman had a graceful figure, walking like a gentle deer, appearing very cute, like a simple and adorable young lady.

The floral dress swayed, the tight-fitting top, her hair half-tied, she looked like a beautiful sight in the world.
