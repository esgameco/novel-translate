The purpose is to gain fame, and on Qingyun Mountain, there are constantly arriving martial artists and cultivators.

Some join the Taoist sect, some join the martial alliance, some join the Confucian sect, some are from the Buddhist sect, and some are independent cultivators.

So it is said that Qingyun Mountain is constantly expanding, the area covered by the buildings has already been planned, but there is no dense gathering, as too dense would not be good.

In the central square, the senior members of the Taoist sect stood together solemnly, as if holding a grand ritual.

As the time came, the Sect Leader Jiu Tong, with a majestic bearing, stepped onto the platform.

"Fellow Taoists, our Taoist sect has been granted the cultivation methods by the Venerable Master, the Taoist sect..."

He spoke grandly.

After finishing, he stepped back, and three more Taoist priests stepped forward to stand beside him.

"Summon the Furnace of Heaven and Earth!"

Jiu Tong shouted.

Xuanhuang waved his hand, and the Furnace of Heaven and Earth in the small world rumbled.

"Today is the beginning of your business, don't mess it up. As long as you do well this time, there will be opportunities for you to sneak a bite in the future, go."

With a hum, the Furnace of Heaven and Earth transformed into a blue rainbow light and headed towards the square.

"Look, it's here, this is the precious Furnace of Heaven and Earth refined by the Venerable Master."

It should be called a Fa Qi (Dharma Implement), but the martial artists prefer to call Xuanhuang's Furnace of Heaven and Earth, the Penetrating Heavenly Tree, and the Great Millstone the "precious treasures".

After all, the archetypes they comprehend are these three implements, which also suppress their spiritual souls.

Calling them Fa Qi is too undignified, it's better to call them precious treasures.

Of course, the implements forged by others are called Fa Qi.

This group of people has a typical double standard, treating them differently.

"So this is the Furnace of Heaven and Earth? I've never seen the actual object before, only the comprehension diagram. Indeed, the Tao flows, it is not an ordinary object, just seeing it makes one feel close to the Tao."

"This precious treasure is refined by the Venerable Master, it is the source that suppresses our spiritual souls, not something ordinary Fa Qi can compare to. And this treasure is adept at forging and elixir refining, it is a blessing for us."

"I've seen the Penetrating Heavenly Tree, the visualization object of the 'Heavenly Earth Law', it is truly extraordinary. I haven't seen the Venerable Master's third precious treasure, the Great Millstone, I can only imagine it must be an extraordinary object as well."

As he spoke, someone reminded him.

"Fellow Daoist, you're mistaken, the Venerable Master has a fourth precious treasure, an apparently dilapidated longsword, according to my investigation, it was the Venerable Master's first weapon, taken from the previous Southern City Martial Arts Hall, and has been constantly refined by the Venerable Master, so it has undergone changes."

His words aroused the curiosity of the crowd.

"Does this fellow Daoist know the name of the Venerable Master's fourth precious treasure?"

But he shook his head, "Not many know, the name of the sword has not been passed down, but it must be extraordinary without a doubt."

This statement was met with disdain from the crowd.

Someone laughed, "Of course the treasure refined by the Venerable Master would not be ordinary."

The crowd chuckled in agreement.

Someone suddenly said, "Oh yes, a few days ago, the Venerable Master defeated the Mysterious Alley, do you all know about that?"

The crowd nodded, and one person replied, "Of course we know, the Exorcism Department in Southern City has been publicizing it these past two days, even hinting that the previous records about the Mysterious Alley were inaccurate."

"Haha," someone laughed, "So the Venerable Master has 'slapped their face', I heard the information the Exorcism Department gave the Venerable Master had a huge discrepancy with how the Venerable Master dealt with the Mysterious Alley. The Exorcism Department's Spiritual Masters' writing was so outrageous that the Venerable Master wanted to exchange ideas with Spiritual Masters who had previous experience dealing with the Mysterious Alley... only to find that the 'Spiritual Master' was just handling the aftermath, haha."

"How do you know all this?"

The person smiled, "I have a fellow Daoist named Gao Jue, his information is quite reliable."

The crowd didn't delve deeper, and someone said, "Previously, the Spiritual Masters' records were the final say, they could write whatever they wanted. Now it's all exposed as full of mistakes, haha."

The person didn't completely reveal it, but those who understood got the gist.

"Look, the Confucian and Buddhist factions have arrived, and the Grand Marshal of the Martial Alliance, Sun Hui, has also come."

"Do you see that woman?"

"Yes, the stunningly beautiful one, she's the Venerable Master's assistant."

"Wow, the Taoist sect is really going all out. This is the first time they're attempting to forge, it's a pioneering move. I was just thinking of joining the Taoist sect, it seems I've made the right decision."

A companion said, "Aren't you a martial artist? Why not join the Martial Alliance, isn't that the highest aspiration of martial artists?"

He replied, "You don't understand. The Martial Alliance has been doing a Genius Plan recently, with my age and cultivation, there's no way I can be selected. Plus, those with martial talent usually choose the Martial Alliance first, the competition will be fierce. The Taoist sect also has a Cultivation Body division, and it's a centuries-old power, joining the Taoist sect is more reliable."

His companion scoffed, "So you're just afraid you can't compete with the geniuses in the Martial Alliance?"

"Cough, cough, my old friend, it's hard to get by, you know." He smiled bitterly, "What can I do? Qingyun Mountain is changing too fast, the situation is different from a few days ago. The Martial Alliance used to welcome people like me warmly, but now they're being very selective."

His companion understood his old friend's thoughts, Qingyun Mountain was indeed evolving too rapidly, changing every moment.

Every day, talented cultivators from all over were gathering here.

At this stage, those who could cross the wilderness to reach Qingyun Mountain were not only powerful, but also steadfast in their Dao. They were not just strong, but had wisdom and experience to match.

"I heard the Martial Alliance has selected their first genius talent?"

He replied, "Yes, a young man named Lin Fan, 15 years old. Not only did he comprehend the visualization diagram on his first try, he comprehended three-tenths of it, a true young prodigy."

His companion sighed, "People are just better than others, I struggled for a long time to comprehend the Penetrating Heavenly Tree visualization, but I couldn't even reach the entry level, let alone three-tenths."

He said, "That's why it's wise to know oneself. I've recognized my own limitations, joining the Martial Alliance's fierce competition is not for me. It's better to join the Taoist sect, where there are many options, I can find a place that suits me in the cultivation of immortality and various arts."

His companion accepted his decision, just as he was about to speak...

They saw the Buddhist, Confucian, and Martial factions finish their congratulations, and the Taoist sect began the forging.

"Begin!"

A loud shout brought the whispering crowd back to attention, all eyes fixed on the Taoist sect's first forging attempt.

"Open!"

Jiu Tong chanted the incantation, and the furnace lid of the Furnace of Heaven and Earth flew up.

Jiu Tong gathered all his spiritual power to activate the Furnace.

"Enter!"

A black chain was thrown into the Furnace of Heaven and Earth.

As soon as it entered, the black chain instinctively struggled and raged, causing the Furnace to tremble, and the controlling Jiu Tong to suffer a huge impact, his spiritual energy thrown into chaos, and a mouthful of fresh blood nearly spurted out.

Jiu Tong dared not be careless, quickly regulating the disrupted spiritual energy, gritting his teeth to prevent the blood from spraying out, only allowing a red line to trickle from the corner of his mouth.

"Not good."

The observing Taoist disciples were alarmed.

"It's fine, I'm alright, don't panic."

Jiu Tong spoke.

The bizarre struggle and distortion of the spiritual consciousness, this was the most violent impact, the first difficult hurdle of forging.

The reason Xuanhuang was unaffected was that the impact did not affect him.

If the Furnace of Heaven and Earth could speak, it would scold Jiu Tong as an amateur. When the Master controlled it, it was as steady as an old...hand.

Even if they call the monks "bald-headed", they still respect Minghui and the others.

Yan Lai said frustratedly, "Not to mention them, we also need to reorganize ourselves a bit, our reputation is not much better than the Buddhists. Although we Confucians often take the blame, we still need to revive our reputation. The most valuable thing about us Confucians is that we are pragmatic and understand how to change and adapt to the trend, this is not something to be ashamed of, we need to continue maintaining this, only those who survive to the end have the right to comment, do you all have this awareness?"

"Yes."

Many people are still wary of the previous overwhelming forum discussions, the Exorcism Department, Golden Token Envoys, and the Confucian, Taoist, and Buddhist factions, they all praise Xuanhuang highly, and even the Confucians, Taoists, and Buddhists have allied themselves with him. With so many forces supporting him, many assassins are also cautious."

She continued, "It's just the Three Mysterious Rats Brothers on the verge of losing control, almost to the point of madness, they don't care. "Let's go, this Qingyun Mountain is really depressing, it's hard to bear staying here. Xuanhuang must die, the place where he is is so hateful."

The man, seeing that the Taoists' artifact was about to succeed, did not want to face this situation.

Plus, it was indeed hard to bear staying in Qingyun Mountain, after all, the Mysterious Anomalies were suppressed by the Demon-Subduing Wood.

Therefore, he chose to leave.
