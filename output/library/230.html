North Hall Cepointedly nodded.

"I've seen it before, in fact, many have seen it, not just me alone.

But so far, those who have seen the Warrior Saint's tombstone are few who have achieved anything. Even I have not grasped even a fraction of its profundity.

So, it is now no longer believed by the world.

However, I think Mr. Ye has such extraordinary natural talent, perhaps he may be able to discern its mysteries."

Ye Xiao fell silent for a moment, then nodded and said:

"Alright, I'll go take a look. But about my studies at the Central Plains Martial Arts University..."

"Don't worry, this old man will take care of it, it won't disturb your normal life."

"Then I'm very grateful to the senior."

"No need to thank me, as long as you can achieve something, at least be able to protect the people of the Nine Provinces, that's what we should be thanking you for!"

After exchanging a few more pleasantries, Ye Xiao followed North Hall Ce's directions to find the Warrior Saint's tombstone.

For him, a mere few hundred kilometers was nothing at all.

Ye Xiao had almost completed the thought, and the distance was already reached.

This was a dilapidated temple, with the life story of the Warrior Saint Han Xin written on the temple door. The inscriptions on the stone tablet had faded over time, and there was even moss growing on it.

Ye Xiao had read the historical books, in the ancient times of this world, the martial arts culture was more prosperous, and there were many figures who could astound the long river of history.

The Demon God Bai Qi, the Warrior Saint Han Xin, the War God Li Jing - it was because of these people that they could easily defeat the star beasts, and the star beasts could not pose an external threat.

So in that era, there was constant warfare between the human tribes.

Ye Xiao entered the temple, which was also in a state of disrepair, with cobwebs everywhere.

It seems that it is indeed as North Hall Ce said, this place has long been disbelieved.

As Ye Xiao stepped into the temple, a dark energy immediately invaded his body.

It was the wandering lonely souls, hiding in this temple, and now they could smell the fresh blood on the human, so they began to gather around.

Ye Xiao's eyes flickered, his spiritual power burst forth, directly shattering the soul bodies of these resentful spirits.

Then, he turned his gaze to the stone statue in front of him.

It was a statue of the Warrior Saint Han Xin, and even after more than a thousand years, when Ye Xiao saw the statue, he couldn't help but feel a slight shock.

The winds of history had aged his sculpture, but Ye Xiao could still feel a powerful and unrestrained aura from that statue.

It was an aura of overlooking the world, treating the world as insignificant as grass.

Ye Xiao was not Han Xin, nor was he a great figure, his realm was definitely not as high as the ancient general Han Xin, but he could still feel and absorb Han Xin's aura, and make it his own.

Mysteriously, Ye Xiao felt that his soul body seemed to have changed, as if his soul power had increased much faster than before.

He took a deep breath and stepped into the temple.

The Warrior Saint's tombstone was behind the temple.

Some say that the remains of the Warrior Saint Han Xin are preserved here, but over the years, countless people have come to dig here in the Nine Provinces, but under the tombstone, there is only three feet of yellow earth.

The tombstone has been probed countless times, it is just an ordinary stone, but it is as heavy as Mount Tai, and no one has been able to take it away.

Over time, it has also been abandoned here.

Ye Xiao used the Taixuan Tiangong technique to scan the tombstone, but it had no effect, and the spiritual power exploration showed that it was just an extremely ordinary large stone.

Thinking about it, it makes sense, with so many people in the Nine Provinces, there must be someone who has cultivated to the Imperial Dao level spiritual techniques.

Like North Hall Ce's eye technique, compared to the Imperial Dao level techniques, it is also strong but not the strongest!

However, he still couldn't see through the mystery of this tombstone.

"All along, I've been relying on the Golden Book, I don't know if the Golden Book has the ability to see through the hidden secrets of the tombstone."

As he spoke, Ye Xiao released the spiritual body of the Golden Book.

This was the second time he had released the spiritual body of the Golden Book outside his body, the previous time was to deal with the Great Dragon.

As the Golden Book emerged, the world changed dramatically, and everything in the heavens and earth seemed to fade.

The surrounding scene began to change rapidly, as if time was rewinding and evolving the grand historical war scenes of the Nine Provinces over a thousand years ago.

Among this, a tall figure in golden armor, wielding a whip in one hand and carrying his hands behind his back, pointing at the rivers and mountains, as if war was just a game for him to play with a flick of his hand.

Ye Xiao could feel that although this was all an illusion, that figure exuded an awe-inspiring power, like a towering giant!

Is this the Warrior Saint Han Xin?

Even as a mere illusion, it contained a momentum that ordinary people could not even approach!

Ye Xiao spoke to the figure:

"Junior Ye Xiao wishes to break through the Kingly Realm, please impart your teachings, senior."

Mysteriously, the illusion seemed to have heard Ye Xiao's words, and swept a indifferent gaze at him, a gaze that contained the vastness of the stars and mountains.

"Without the ability to slay kings, how can one become a king?"

With these eight calm words, all the illusions faded away.
