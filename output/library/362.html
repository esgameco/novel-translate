Ye Xiao: "(_..)!"

He furrowed his brows tightly, and his whole person seemed a bit at a loss.

After his full-force investigation these past few days, he found that the formation techniques were not at the treasure art level.

That's right, the highest rank of formation techniques was only the Nine-Star Divine Formation.

This left him a bit puzzled.

Healing arts, alchemy, and weapon forging - these all seemed to be able to be combined into treasure arts, so why couldn't formation techniques do the same? Analyzing this situation, Ye Xiao could only say that no matter how powerful the formation techniques were, they couldn't surpass the consciousness of people.

Especially those peerless geniuses who possessed treasure arts - to restrain them, what kind of formidable formation techniques would be required?

And how many spirit crystals would it take to set up? If the cost was too high, there might not be many people in this world who could afford it.

If someone could afford to set up a formation that, relying only on a certain amount of spirit crystal resources, could defeat treasure arts, then treasure arts would become worthless.

So this was a very troubling issue.

Perhaps that was precisely why there were no formation techniques at the treasure art level in the heavens and earth?

Oh well, let it be. The heavens didn't produce them, and he couldn't obtain them either.

He should focus on cultivation and try to raise his own strength as much as possible.

Fortunately, a few days later, Ye Xiao had completely cultivated his treasure art to the Great Perfection realm.

At the moment the Annihilation Treasure Art was successfully fused, Ye Xiao's cultivation also broke through.

Boom!

He reached the Second Realm of the Divine Realm.

Above the Divine Realm, just like the Innate Realm, it was divided into nine small realms.

Although they were called "small realms", the increase in each realm was comparable to the amount of spiritual energy required to break through to the Divine Realm previously.

Of course, this did not mean it would take as long as reaching the Divine Realm.

At least not for Ye Xiao.

Because after the increase in strength, the speed of absorbing spiritual energy would also accelerate. And there was another reason related to his cultivation method.

Ordinary people only cultivated divine arts, and the vast majority of them could not even use the Nine-Star Divine Arts, having at most a One-Star Divine Art was already remarkable.

For example, Ning Zhiyuan - why was the Ning Clan willing to let him go out to temper himself? One of the reasons was that his divine blood and divine soul were also possessed by the Ning Clan, so if he encountered any mishaps, the Ning Clan could revive him again.

But the other reason was even more important.

That was, the cultivation method he practiced in the early stage was not the Nine-Star Emperor Art, which meant that even if he was captured by others and had his soul searched, he would not reveal the Ning Clan's secrets.

Not only Ning Zhiyuan, but almost all the tribes in the starry sky used this method.

This could greatly reduce the chance of their heirs being killed.

Killing these heirs was equivalent to slapping their faces, and it was also meaningless.

It was also for this reason that their cultivation speed might be a little slower than breaking through from the Emperor Realm to the Divine Realm, but not much slower, and there was no hope of being faster.

In contrast, Ye Xiao, like others, had strengthened his physical body and increased the speed of absorbing spiritual energy, and he had also cultivated several Nine-Star Divine Arts, even adding an Ancient Treasure Art.

This was equivalent to everyone having the same spirit gathering formation, but others had no spirit stones, so the absorption speed was very slow, while Ye Xiao not only had them, but also a pile of spirit crystals.

The gap between them was naturally self-evident.

It was only natural that Ye Xiao successfully broke through to the Second Realm of the Divine Realm in just over a month after breaking through to the Divine Realm.

This made Ye Xiao's mood much more comfortable.

Although he didn't get good results in the formation techniques, he got some compensation in terms of his own strength. It could be considered quite perfect.

The only thing that was not quite satisfactory was that he currently had no way to test the power of the Ancient Treasure Art - the Annihilation Treasure Art.

This treasure art must be extremely powerful, but how powerful exactly, he did not know.

When he was still a Patriarch, he had caused a huge sword mark, which had attracted the attention of many people.

Now Ye Xiao was much more mature and would not do something so revealing.

He would be more stable in his actions.

Anyway, there would be an opportunity to test it in the future.

He sent a message underground, asking the spirit beasts to speed up the mining progress, abandoning large-scale mining, and focusing on one path.

He wanted to dig a tunnel to enter the Divine Realm's treasure vault first and collect those treasures.

He was not sure if he could take all the spirit crystals, but the treasures in the treasure vault were more valuable than the spirit crystals.

After giving this order, Ye Xiao began to search for the remaining divine art of healing.

This second synthesized divine art, which included both defensive power and healing power, would definitely be beneficial to improving his own combat power or survival chances when it was combined into a treasure art.

"Ye Xiao, still reading books?"

Ye Xiao was startled and looked up, a bit strange.

"Senior North Hall, weren't you all going to the starry sky to dig graves? How come you're here?"
