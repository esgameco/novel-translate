"Her? Whether she comes or not, it doesn't make much difference. After all, she's just a spiritual beast, and her strength is basically at the bottom of the entire Tianmo Cliff."

"That's true. After all, just a few of them would be more than enough for this human race, let alone the entire twenty of them!"

At this moment, in the minds of almost everyone, Ye Xiao had already been declared dead.

Now, he was just a vegetable that could still breathe.

The twenty Shenzong beasts were chatting with each other, like old friends who hadn't seen each other for a long time, completely ignoring the presence of Ye Xiao.

They didn't treat Ye Xiao as an intruder at all.

In their eyes, Ye Xiao was just a delicious piece of meat, just like the distant spectating beasts.

What they were discussing now was how to divide this piece of meat, not how to subdue it.

"Alright, let's not waste too much time. We're all old comrades from the Tianmo Cliff, there's no need to argue over a piece of meat.

This meat, we'll divide it equally among ourselves, consider it as a sacrificial offering, and no one is allowed to hog it for themselves."

As soon as these words were spoken, the other beasts' eyes lit up.

Their strength was definitely not as good as Bazu Jun and the others. If it came down to it, they were the least likely to be able to eat Ye Xiao.

But now that the powerful Bazu Jun had spoken, there was no problem.

"Senior Bazu Jun is so generous!"

"As expected of Senior Bazu Jun!"

The shouts of the beasts made Long Mo and Tongtian Giant Python quite unhappy, but they were helpless.

After all, Bazu Jun's strength was far superior to theirs.

Moreover, not only that, Bazu Jun had an excellent relationship with Leitong Qingniu, whose strength was also extremely powerful.

They couldn't defeat the two of them, otherwise they wouldn't have to swallow their anger here.

Leitong Qingniu slightly raised his thick, muscular arm.

"Alright, all of you be quiet."

The beasts immediately fell silent, not daring to speak.

Leitong Qingniu looked at Ye Xiao and spoke indifferently:

"Human, before I kill you, I have a question I want to ask. What are you doing here in the Tianmo Cliff? Don't tell me you came here specifically to die?"

The Tianmo Cliff was originally a forbidden area for the human race. Suddenly, a human had barged in, so Leitong Qingniu couldn't help but be curious and wanted to ask about the reason.

Ye Xiao had remained silent the entire time, just standing in place.

When he came to the Tianmo Cliff, his Taixuan Tiangong had already thoroughly scanned the entire mountain cliff.

He was already very familiar with the powerful beasts here.

So he was waiting.

Waiting for these beasts to come to him, and then he would slaughter them all, saving time and effort.

But now it seems that the last beast was most likely not going to show up.

Unexpectedly, there was such a steady beast.

It was quite interesting.

But it didn't matter. After he had eliminated these beasts, he would then deal with her.

Thinking about this, Ye Xiao slowly opened his eyes. Although he couldn't see anything at the bottom of the Tianmo Cliff, this did not hinder the expansion of his spiritual sense, allowing him to take in everything around him.

These beasts seemed to have evolved extremely strong vision, able to adapt to this environment after living in it for a long time.

But it didn't matter.

They were just a bunch of medicinal ingredients, why think so much about it?

With a slight movement of his gaze, Ye Xiao struck.

When he killed, he never liked too much unnecessary talk.

A sword beam suddenly appeared in the dark space, like a shining sun, directly slicing through the dark abyss and illuminating the entire Tianmo Cliff.

In an instant, countless beasts were startled by the dazzling light, their shrieks and roars intertwining.

Countless beasts began to flee and fly in chaos.

The collisions and stampedes were countless, and beasts kept falling from the sky like falling dumplings.

But as soon as these falling beasts approached the sword beam, they were shattered to pieces by the spiritual energy ripples on the sword beam.

All of this happened in the blink of an eye.

The Shenzong beasts had never expected Ye Xiao to suddenly strike.

This human had no rules at all.

He actually dared to come here to deal with them!

This was a huge joke! Who did he think he was?

What strength did he have to run wild here? Twenty Shenzong experts!

Altogether twenty super experts! More than enough to kill him hundreds of times over.

What gave him the right? How dare he?

However, just as the Shenzong beasts were about to be enraged, the sword beam had already descended.

Boom! The massive sword beam, in an instant, had split the two Xiantian Seventh-Grade Shenzong beasts in front of Ye Xiao into two halves.

He had restrained his strength, otherwise this one move would have reduced them to powder!
