"Have you heard? The Buddhist disciple Aro from India has come to our Nine Prefectures again."

"Aro? Who is Aro? Is he very powerful?"

"Look at how ignorant and uninformed you are. Aro was a genius Buddhist disciple from India over forty years ago.

Back then, he came to challenge the Buddhist disciples of our Nine Prefectures, and only lost once, winning all other confrontations. For the past few decades, he has been coming every year to challenge them, and except for that one loss, he has not failed even once."

"Wow! Is he really that powerful?"

"Quite powerful indeed. It is said that he has now reached the peak of the Sixth Realm of the Innate, and could break through to the Seventh Realm of the Innate at any time."

"Heavens, to break through to the Seventh Realm of the Innate, wouldn't that make him a legendary Saint-Patriarch expert?"

"Exactly! So you can see how formidable he is, right? I've heard that this time he has already defeated quite a few people. Since he only challenges those of the same generation, the elders and juniors don't dare to challenge him. Unless the Nine Prefectures elder who previously defeated him makes a move again, he will most likely sweep through everything this year as well."

"Whew! He's truly too strong! But wait, aren't the Buddhists of our Nine Prefectures quite powerful? How could they not be able to defeat him?"

"Are your ears just for decoration? Didn't I just say that he only challenges those of the same generation, the top experts of that generation?

As for the more senior Buddhist masters, I won't even mention them. In the past few decades, the Nine Prefectures have also produced some extremely powerful experts.

Two of them, who are a generation or two younger than him, have already broken through to the Saint-Patriarch realm. Aren't they stronger than him? He only challenges those of his own generation, and coincidentally, there aren't many in his generation who can match him. Isn't that frustrating?"

"I see, there's nothing to be done about it then."

Ye Xiao had known beforehand that the Buddhist disciple from India was coming, but he didn't expect him to arrive so soon! And it seems this guy does have some real abilities.

Normally, a prodigy would break through to the Patriarch realm around their 30s or 40s, reach the Grand Patriarch realm in their 50s or 60s, and only break through to the Saint-Patriarch realm after a hundred years.

For this person to have reached the peak of the Sixth Realm of the Innate in his 60s, just a step away from the Seventh Realm of the Saint-Patriarch, his talent is indeed quite remarkable.

However, he wasn't too interested in this.

His own talent was only remarkable compared to ordinary people. If he were to compare himself to true geniuses, not only the Imperial bloodline, but even someone like Pu Jiansheng, he might not be able to match up.

If it weren't for the fact that Fa Zheng was going to fight against him, he probably wouldn't have paid attention to this name at all.

But in any case, based on what they've said, if nothing unexpected happens, Fa Zheng will most likely lose.

Since he has a good relationship with the old monk, he'll see if he can lend a hand. After all, Fa Zheng is from the Nine Prefectures, while Aro, no matter how talented, is from India.

He should help his own people.

He quickly made his way to the fifth floor.

On the fourth floor, there were almost no students, as ordinary students were not allowed up here, unless they had made significant contributions or had exceptional achievements.

Fa Zheng was currently reading books on the fifth floor.

The fifth and sixth floors were where the most Buddhist cultivation techniques donated by Shaolin were stored.

Ye Xiao went up and took a look.

Fa Zheng's cultivation had already reached the peak of the Fifth Realm of the Innate, just one step away from breaking through to the Sixth Realm.

However, his talent was only that of an ordinary genius, so it was doubtful whether he could complete the breakthrough in a few months' time.

Ye Xiao thought for a moment, and then found a copy of the Diamond Sutra from the bookshelf.

The Diamond Sutra was a Patriarch-level internal cultivation technique of the Buddhist sect.

The Buddhist internal cultivation techniques were actually quite numerous and renowned.

If he were to list them all, like the Muscle-Tendon Classic, the Diamond Sutra, the Diamond Bodhisattva Sutra, Ye Xiao could probably go on until he got off work.

But in reality, the Dharma Classic was the true essence of Buddhist cultivation techniques, the overarching principle.

Ye Xiao had already learned the Dharma Classic, so he could see through these Shaolin techniques at a glance, immediately understanding their flaws and optimizing them.

He then used his own power to modify one of the texts from the Diamond Bodhisattva Sutra, incorporating parts of the Dharma Classic to create a new scripture, vastly superior to the original.

While it could not compare to the complete Dharma Classic, it was still several times more powerful than the Diamond Bodhisattva Sutra.

He quietly placed the modified scripture on top of Fa Zheng's unread books.

With his high cultivation, this small action would be unnoticed by Fa Zheng.

"Old Monk, you're here reading so early. Have you had breakfast yet?"

"No, this humble monk can no longer dine with you, Ye Shishi."

Ye Xiao nodded.

"Then continue your studies, I won't disturb you."

"Okay! We'll talk about it after this period. This humble monk will invite you to a meal then."

Ye Xiao smiled.

"When that time comes, don't try to weasel out of it if I order some seafood or something."

"Don't worry, although this humble monk doesn't eat, I will never stop others from enjoying their meals."

The two parted ways, and Ye Xiao returned to his own fourth floor.

As a good friend, he had lent a helping hand. If Fa Zheng could comprehend it, that would be his fortune.

If he could not, then so be it.
