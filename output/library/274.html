"Speak."

"If I can't hold on, kill me! Don't let me harm the world."

The Sage of the Black Tortoise's heart trembled, and he nodded, his heart filled with bitterness.

In every battle against the beast calamities, Li Liushui had expended a great deal of his strength.

He could be considered one of the tiger generals of the human race in the Nine Provinces! But now, he had fallen into this predicament, which couldn't help but make one sigh with sorrow.

This scene made the dragon vein let out a low rumble.
