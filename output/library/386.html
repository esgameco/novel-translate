The spiritual energy ship of the Yun family descended to the ground of the Nine States. A Yun family disciple, holding a cage, came before Cloud Water.

"Elder Cloud, I've brought what you wanted."

Elder Cloud nodded.

"Put it down."

The Yun family disciple placed the cage on the ground and lifted the canvas cover, revealing a Xuanming Wolf with extremely thick blood qi.

"Roar!"

It let out an angry low growl, but after seeing Cloud Water's gaze, it immediately lost its spirit and curled up.

The Yun family disciple couldn't help but marvel inwardly. Even a beast lord comparable to the eighth level of the Divine Realm, seeing Elder Cloud's gaze, was so terrified, just how terrifying must Elder Cloud's strength be?

Cloud Water looked at the trembling Xuanming Wolf, squinting his eyes and smiling lightly.

"The Xuanming Wolf is an excellent star beast for tracking prey. They have an innate sense of bloodline, and can clearly locate those with powerful bloodline power.

In this world, there are so few humans, with it, we should be able to easily find that boy."

The Yun family disciple smiled:

"Elder Cloud really has a plan. As long as we can find him and bring him back to the Yun family to cultivate, in the future, we can cultivate another saint-level existence!"

"Saint-level? Haha, you're thinking too much. The Yun family already has one saint-level disciple, we don't need another one!"

The Yun family disciple's heart couldn't help but tremble as he said, "Does Elder Cloud's meaning mean...?"

"After finding that boy, directly kill him and devour his bloodline, bring it back to us."

"How can this be? He is a genius of our Xuanyuan Clan, if we can cultivate him, it will allow our Xuanyuan Clan's strength to rise to a new level! Why kill him?"

Cloud Water glanced at him and snorted lightly.

"You really have a pig's brain. Do you think that level of existence will bring glory to our Xuanyuan Clan? You're thinking too much.

Remember that guy from more than two thousand years ago?"

The Yun family disciple thought of a name, and his expression immediately became solemn.

The guy from more than two thousand years ago, of course, it must be that person.

He represented an era, an extremely brilliant and dazzling era!

It was he who, in an instant, pulled the Xuanyuan Clan, which was about to fall out of the top thirty in the Starry Sky, back to the nineteenth place! It was he who supported a clan that was about to wither, and restored its former glory.

Seeing that he didn't speak, Cloud Water knew that he had already understood who he was talking about.

"That person back then, shook the heavens and the earth, brilliant and unparalleled.

To cultivate him, the entire Xuanyuan Clan almost exhausted all its excellent resources, concentrating them all on him.

This created an era that belonged to him! But in the end, how did he do it? Cultivating to the Divine Emperor realm...

On the other side, Ye Xiao finished his day's work and returned home to eat fish.

He was quite helpless.

Although the taste of this fish was very delicious, eating it every day was still tiring. No matter how good something is, eating it too much will make you sick of it.

Furthermore, after absorbing the divine blood of that Divine King, enhancing his cultivation and blood qi, the blood qi this fish could provide was no longer as abundant.

Ye Xiao was very troubled.

He knew that at this rate, before he could break through to the Slay God Realm, the blood qi from the big fish would not be enough. And when he reached the Slay God Realm, the blood qi would be even more insufficient.

That would definitely slow down the speed of his cultivation advancement.

The excavation of the Divine Nation's Treasure Vault had reached over 30%, with two-thirds of the project still remaining. It was impossible to obtain the treasures inside in the short term.

He could only rely on the medicinal pills obtained from that Divine King's tomb to support himself for now.

But the medicinal pills were limited. Consuming one meant one less, and they wouldn't last very long.

Unfortunately, Beast Sovereigns were extremely hard to find, and there were none in this star domain. To search for them, he would have to go to higher-level places, which were also dangerous, making it not worthwhile.

Returning to the Xuanyuan Divine Clan and acknowledging his ancestry was absolutely impossible. Not until his strength was sufficient would he ever publicly reveal his identity. Safety came first.

It seemed there was only one path left.

He would have to go out and excavate a few more Divine King tombs. There were the most Divine King tombs in the starry skies, and digging a few should be enough to sustain him for a while, until his cultivation reached the late Slay God Realm, at which point the problem should not be too great.

By then, his cultivation would have improved, and the Divine Nation's Treasure Vault should also be accessible. He could then utilize the resources of the Divine Nation's Treasure Vault to advance to beyond the Divine King Realm, even to the Divine Emperor Realm!

Once he reached the Divine Emperor Realm, the many subsequent problems would be easily resolved.
