If the previous light was like a small sun, then this light was a true existence that could rival the sun in the sky!

It was many times more powerful than the previous light! What was more terrifying was that at this moment, many people were directly suppressed to their knees on the ground because they could not withstand this power.

It was a fear from the depths of their souls, forcing them to submit to that light.

The people were dumbfounded, and the vast majority of them did not understand what was happening at this moment.

Some of the more powerful cultivators began to tremble.

"It's the Emperor Realm! That is an Emperor Realm expert! He has stepped into the realm of the Emperor Realm expert!"

The Emperor Realm! Not to mention the entire Nine States, even if you put the entire human race together, how many would there be?

At the same time, this also made the crowd even more admiring of Ye Xiao.

A young man in his early twenties could surpass these old monsters who had cultivated for hundreds of years, and reach a realm they had never reached before!

What an incredibly powerful and extraordinary talent? Qinglong, feeling the powerful force within himself, sighed with emotion. Even though he was already extremely powerful, and his mind had been tempered for a long time, at this moment, he was still immersed in the power of the Emperor Realm expert, unable to extricate himself.

At this moment, he could truly be called the Sword Emperor Qinglong!

After a while, Qinglong finally collected his mind and aura, descended to the ground, and came before the crowd.

Although he had put away his aura, the people could still feel that overwhelming Emperor Realm pressure, unable to help but feel a bit of apprehension.

"Big brother Qinglong, congratulations on successfully entering the Emperor Realm."

"Brother Qingshan, congratulations on successfully entering the King Realm."

Baihu and the others congratulated Qinglong, and only Li Liushui congratulated Lu Qingshan.

Qinglong nodded and then spoke:

"We two should also go and thank Ye Xiao. After all, it was he who helped us both break through the realms."

Lu Qingshan nodded.

"Yes, we should go and thank Ye Xiao. I've always wanted to meet him before, but never had the chance.

Taking this opportunity, I'll also get to know this extraordinary genius."

Jiang Chen smiled.

"I'll lead the way."

With that, several figures instantly disappeared from the spot, turning into streaks of light, heading towards Ye Xiao's house in Jianghai City.

Because their speed was so fast, the distant onlookers did not notice their direction and had not even seen them clearly before they disappeared.

This left the crowd greatly disappointed.

But the posts online were already heating up.

All kinds of speculations were present, almost occupying the top ten rankings of the hot search list.

A few minutes later, the group appeared at Ye Xiao's villa.

Ye Xiao, who was cultivating spiritual herbs in the courtyard, couldn't help but sigh slightly when he sensed their arrival.

More people had come, and soon the entire Nine States would know his identity, which he could hardly keep track of with just one hand.

From the initial Northern Palace Strategy to the current Venerable Xuanwu, Sword Saint Li Liushui, Venerable Baihu, and now the three who just arrived - Sword Emperor Qinglong, Fire Knife Zhuque, and Sword Sage Lu Qingshan.

Fortunately, they were all top-tier experts in the Nine States.
