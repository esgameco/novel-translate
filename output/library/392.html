"Young Master Ling, that's quite a big mouth you have. Who said we were following you? We've known about this Shen King's Tomb for a long time, we just haven't come to excavate it yet.

In fact, I'd like to ask, where did you get the news from? Was it by using spies to obtain intelligence from within my Hundred Tribes Alliance?"

The moment the words left his mouth, Ling Shaoyu raised his hand and struck out with a palm.

As the young master of the Ling Clan, he cultivated the Seven Star Divine Art! The power of his techniques was extraordinary.

With a single strike, a beam of cold light wrapped in purple lightning like a dragon descended heavily upon Zhu Chunqiu in the distance.

"You insolent wretch, how dare you!"

The few Deity Realm cultivators around Zhu Chunqiu immediately stepped forward to block.

But the cultivation techniques of these few were clearly not on the same level as Ling Shaoyu's.

At most, they were only at the one or two star Divine Art level. By joining forces and relying on their cultivation being slightly higher than Ling Shaoyu's, they were barely able to neutralize his attack.

Boom! The light column exploded in the starry sky, and the ripples of spiritual energy swept in all directions, billowing the clothes and hair of the crowd.

Ling Shaoyu snorted coldly and spoke to the few beside him:

"These despicable and shameless dogs have no sense of propriety at all. Talking to them any more is a waste of time, just attack them directly!"

Ning Zhiyuan said:

"Some of us will fight to hold them off, while the rest go to open the Shen King's Tomb. As long as we can open the entrance, we'll seal it with formations from the inside so they won't be able to get in even if they want to."

"Good idea, kill them!"

With the order given, the powerful experts of the major Xuanyuan Clan families immediately charged into the Hundred Tribes.

Zhu Chunqiu's expression changed, and while fending off the Xuanyuan Clan's experts, he shouted:

"Those who know formations, go quickly and try to open the formations first, don't let them succeed!"

"Yes!"

The two sides engaged in an intense battle, with light beams flashing all over the starry sky and various imperial and divine arts colliding and exploding.

The violent shockwaves even allowed the people in the human world on the ground to see the twinkling stars in the sky during broad daylight.

"What's happening up there?"

"I don't know! Looks like they're fighting.

After all, they found the Shen King's Tomb in the starry sky, the Hundred Tribes Alliance definitely won't just sit and watch without taking action."

"Tsk! These Hundred Tribes Alliance people are really shameless. It was our Xuanyuan Clan that found it first, yet they have the nerve to come and try to profit from it."

"Can't be helped, that is the Shen King's Tomb after all! If it were just an ordinary tomb, do you think they would even bother coming?"

In the library, Ye Xiao looked up at the starry sky through the window, his brow slightly furrowed.

It seems the battle raging in the starry sky is very intense.

However, their speed in breaking the formations is quite slow, and they may not be able to fully open it by nightfall. He doesn't need to be in a hurry either.

Ye Xiao's luck today is quite good.

One is that he found two imperial-level poison arts that can be fused into a divine-level poison art.

This technique itself is quite rare, and it's not easy to obtain normally, but if you find it, you'll basically get several of them at once.

Because they tend to be clustered together.

Ye Xiao isn't too interested in poison arts. Although they have many advantages like low consumption, strong bursts of power, high damage, and even the ability to affect a few experts beyond his own realm, they don't really match his handsome appearance.

That's not the biggest problem though. When it comes to actual combat, both sides' agility, defense, and attack will all reach a peak level.

Poison arts are difficult to use, and that's the biggest issue.

For powerful experts, they aren't very practical.

However, having something is better than having nothing, so he'll keep them.

Now, with his strengthened spiritual sense, the speed at which he cultivates these imperial-level techniques is extremely fast, just like how he used to cultivate the basic techniques.

In less than half an hour, he had completely cultivated the two imperial-level poison arts.

After finishing the cultivation, Ye Xiao immediately started the fusion process.

Soon, a brand new nine-star poison art was synthesized - Poison Deity Absolute! This is an extremely powerful poison technique. Not only can it form a powerful defensive barrier within Ye Xiao's body, but if any poison enters his body, it will be immediately consumed and converted into spiritual energy to continuously nourish Ye Xiao.

At the same time, it will also form a layer of potent poison on Ye Xiao's body surface, which can be used for defensive purposes.
