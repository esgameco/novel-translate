As Ye Xiao entered, a low growl came from the darkness, and then a super-level blue wolf charged towards him, opening its bloody maw in the darkness, the stench of blood assaulting his nostrils. The bloody maw was large enough to swallow three of Ye Xiao's heads.

Thud.

The blue wolf fell heavily to the ground, with a large hole in its head, its breath completely gone.

Ye Xiao didn't even raise his hand, the Taixuan Tiangong controlled by his spiritual power condensed into substance and instantly pierced through the blue wolf's head, ruthlessly killing it.

Arriving at the depths, Ye Xiao found that the cave had been transformed into the blue wolf's den.

However, the techniques were carved on the walls, so they were not damaged.

Ye Xiao glanced around, and the first thing he saw was the Rulai Shenzhang.
