Ye Xiao spoke in a calm tone, and two massive sword beams came crashing down from the canyon above, instantly slaying over ten thousand star beasts.

At the same time, the massive explosive impact force also caused the other star beasts to be shaken, their internal organs dislocated, their bodies flung back, either colliding with each other or crashing into the stone walls.

Thousands of star beasts were killed or stunned on the spot.

Seeing this, the Saint Envoy shuddered, and his entire body's hair stood on end.

Staring at Ye Xiao's gaze fixed on him, he knew that this time, he could not escape.

After taking a deep breath, he resigned himself to his fate.

"I never expected that such a powerful human genius would emerge from this small Jianghai City. I admit defeat."

This was not because he was a coward.

Although even ants can fight desperately for survival, if one knows their fate is sealed, any further resistance would be futile.

Ye Xiao did not speak.

To him, the opponent was just a star beast, even if it could now take on a human form, its essence was still that of an animal.

Killing him would suffice.

However, before killing him, he needed to search the opponent's memories to see what the star beast clan was up to. He did not believe that the star beast clan went to such lengths merely to eat humans or occupy human cities - that would be too idiotic.

But before he could make a move, the opponent spoke again.

"However, today, although I am destined to die by your hand, I will also trap you here for a while.

Without your powerful protection, the human race will find it difficult to resist the star beasts enhanced by the Seven-Star Cluster.

A few hours will be enough for hundreds of thousands in several cities to perish."

As he spoke, the opponent began to form hand seals.

Unfortunately, although his movements were fast, Ye Xiao's speed was even faster.

Pu-chi! With a slight movement of his eyes, two beams of light flashed, directly severing the opponent's arms.

"So fast!" the Saint Envoy of the star beasts exclaimed in shock, but then he smiled.

"But it's useless. The star beasts' cultivation is different from humans - we can directly use our bloodline to unleash our techniques, without the need for hand seals and cultivation paths like humans.

Although I made the hand seals just now, the formation had already activated the moment I had the intent to act."

True enough, a black and white alternating formation suddenly appeared, enveloping Ye Xiao within it.

As the formation emerged, the Saint Envoy of the star beasts roared:

"All star beasts, detonate yourselves and infuse your lifeblood and star pearls into the formation!"

As soon as he spoke, countless star beasts, without the slightest hesitation, began to self-destruct.

The unity of the star beasts was fully displayed at this moment.

Their blood and star pearls all seeped into the formation, nourishing and empowering it.

Although the individual star beasts were not strong, their numerical advantage in accumulating power made the formation extremely terrifying.

"An Imperial Formation!" Ye Xiao exclaimed with a hint of surprise.

Having just cultivated the Heavenly Transformation Formation, he could instantly recognize the level of this formation.

The Saint Envoy of the star beasts smiled slightly, a hint of pride surfacing in his eyes.

"Not bad, you have a good eye to recognize this as an Imperial Formation at a glance.

This is the Earthly Calamity Heavenly Pressure Formation! It is an innate Imperial Formation in the bloodline of our Black Mysterious Tortoise Clan!

It possesses extremely powerful defensive capabilities. Whether from the outside or the inside, it cannot be easily broken.

Outwardly, it is an Imperial-grade defensive formation.

Inwardly, it is an Imperial-grade imprisonment formation.

Even if your strength is extraordinary, it will be difficult for you to break through this formation anytime soon."

As he said this, he expected Ye Xiao to be shocked and grave, but to his surprise, Ye Xiao seemed excited instead.

"This Imperial Formation has defensive properties?"

The Saint Envoy of the star beasts furrowed his brows, somewhat puzzled.

Is this person insane?

Doesn't he know that this is an Imperial Formation, which even Sage Realm cultivators would be trapped within?

Trapping him for a few hours would be enough for massive casualties on the human side.

What he didn't know was that Ye Xiao was not worried about that at all, because he had the Supremely Mysterious Heavenly Pressure, which could analyze the opponent's spiritual power and learn the formation, allowing him to easily break it.

Moreover, by doing so, he could also learn another powerful Imperial Formation, the Earthly Calamity Heavenly Pressure Formation.

The Heavenly Transformation Formation was mainly focused on killing and illusion effects. This would nicely complement its lack in defensive formations.

This was an unexpected windfall, how could Ye Xiao not be delighted?

He had already planned to search the opponent's memories, and now, for the sake of this formation, he had even more reason to do so.

Without further ado, the Supremely Mysterious Heavenly Pressure was activated, the overwhelming spiritual power ruthlessly and unstoppably invading the opponent's mind.

"This Imperial Formation is now mine."

The Saint Envoy of the star beasts' pupils suddenly shrank, his entire being overwhelmed.
