"Unidentified life form?"

The group of big-nosed scientists in the command center couldn't help but raise their eyebrows, exchanging glances with each other, their faces filled with a hint of confusion.

"Could it be a star beast?"

"Unlikely, this is the star battlefield, star beasts couldn't have landed here casually."

"Then could it be a human warrior from the Nine States? This should be the star battlefield of the Nine States, right?"

Everyone looked towards the commander, who pondered for a moment before giving a slight smile.

"Whoever it is, since they've come, let's have them test our battle machines!"

The group's expressions changed slightly.

"Commander, is that really a good idea?"

"What's wrong with it? It's just a star beast, an alien star beast! As long as it's not one of our Meizou warriors, it's fine!"

The group exchanged mischievous smiles.

"Alright then! Immediately execute the battle order, annihilate the star beast!"

"Battle Plane No. 1 received."

"Battle Plane No. 2 received."

"Battle Plane No. 3 received."

The three battle planes, upon receiving the order, immediately rushed towards the figure at their maximum speed.

Ye Xiao, who was in the midst of practicing the Divine Intention Technique, suddenly felt a warning from the Taixuan Heavenly Might within him.

This left him somewhat startled.

"These are... battle planes?"

In the human's fight against star beasts, aside from cultivation, there are other ways to enhance themselves.

The two most famous methods are genetic technology and military technology.

Genetic technology involves unlocking the genes of star beasts and then applying them to human genes, thereby shortening the time for human advancement and rapidly producing high-level masters, although there are many serious side effects, the effects are indeed good.

Military technology, on the other hand, is the research on advanced weaponry, allowing many countries to create extremely powerful large-scale destructive weapons on the star beast battlefields, some of which can even rival the attacks of Sage Realm cultivators.

And judging from the flags on the battle planes, it seems to be the technologically most advanced Meizou.

Just as Ye Xiao was pondering, suddenly, six beams of light were fired from the enemy's battle planes.

These beams were compressed from ultra-high frequency lasers, and the Taixuan Heavenly Might instantly determined that their destructive power was no less than an early Sage Realm master's attack.

Moreover, the speed of these beams was extremely fast, and Ye Xiao hadn't initially thought that the other party would attack a human warrior, so he hadn't taken excessive precautions.

By the time he reacted, the enemy's beams had already reached him.

Ye Xiao instantly unleashed the Tyrannical Saber, and a saber beam slashed through the air, exploding the six beams in a dazzling display of sparks.

Boom, boom, boom...

The sky seemed to have burst into six brilliant fireworks.

Thick smoke rose, and Ye Xiao, who had not cultivated body refinement techniques and had not taken excessive precautions, sustained some light injuries under this sudden Sage Realm-level attack.

The Replenishing Technique activated, quickly healing his wounds, while his eyes turned ice-cold without a trace of warmth.

The other party's technological level was not low, they couldn't have failed to detect that he was a human warrior, yet they still attacked him decisively, clearly intending to use him as a target for practice.

This was unforgivable!

The three battle planes reported to the command center after the initial attack.

"The target has been eliminated, requesting further instructions."

The commander's face revealed a hint of disappointment, he had thought it was a big fish from the Nine States.

Killing that would have been somewhat challenging, but he didn't expect it to be just a small fry.

"Nevermind, come back."

"Understood!"

The battle planes were about to retreat when suddenly...

The pilot of the No. 1 fighter jet felt a golden glow radiate from above, the earth-shattering pressure disrupting all the magnetic fields of his fighter.

Without time to look up, he left behind a final words.

"My God!"

Boom! In the next instant, a golden blade covered his fighter, directly causing it to explode.

"Damn it!"

The other two fighter jets immediately launched an attack towards the explosion center.

But the next second, one of the fighter jets suddenly exploded! It had been attacked by Ye Xiao.

"Damn it!"

The last Grandmaster-level pilot was terrified, no longer daring to stay. He opened the throttle to the max, trying to flee this airspace.

But before he could catch his breath, he saw a bright red dot on the screen rapidly approaching, its speed unbelievable!

"Oh my god, he's flying faster than me! Oh no!"

Boom! The next second, the last fighter jet was completely destroyed.

After destroying the three fighter jets, the air became silent.

Clearly, the other party had already left.

In the command room, all the scientists were collectively dumbfounded.

The commander, hands on his hips, pointed at the screen, his fingers trembling with anger.

"This is what you call fighter jets comparable to Grand Masters?"

A chubby scientist coughed lightly and said, "Perhaps the other party's combat power is even beyond Grand Masters."

"You mean Divine Masters? Don't tell me our radar technology is also fake, the most advanced radar in the entire Midwest, clearly tested him as a Grand Master!"

"Uh..."

The other person opened his mouth but ultimately said nothing.

"The R&D investment for this fighter jet has exceeded 20 billion US dollars, and with the rare alloy materials used, the three fighter jets cost a total of 40 billion US dollars!

On Ye Xiao's side, after destroying the three high-tech fighter jets, he quickly descended back to Jianghai City.

The Midwest's strength is formidable, with many high-level warriors.

After suffering losses, they might dispatch Grand Masters or even Divine Masters! He wasn't afraid, but fighting against such powerful opponents, it would be difficult to decide the outcome quickly.

If he attracted more people over, his identity would be exposed.

The hole would just keep getting bigger, there's no way it would lead to anything good.
