Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

The crowd trembled in their hearts, scared out of their wits, and immediately retreated quickly.

But although their speed was fast, the speed of the black-scaled snake was even faster, more than twice as fast.

The long tail swept across, destroying the trees, and the huge force heavily slammed into the bodies of the three martial artists, directly flattening their chests into rotten mud.

In the blink of an eye, it then spat venom towards a few other figures.

"Ah—!"

A few people let out a miserable scream, and were quickly corroded into ashes.

When the black-scaled snake fights, the venom it spits out can directly corrode low-level cultivators into dregs.

Normally, it would not easily use its venom, but instead preserve the corpses of its opponents as food.

But today, it had become enraged to the extreme.

The leader's legs were trembling.

It's over!

It's completely over now.

This damned intelligence really got them killed.

That little black-scaled snake was indeed of a transcendent level, but the intelligence didn't say that behind it, there was a big black-scaled snake! And this big black-scaled snake's cultivation had already reached the Grandmaster level!

How are they supposed to deal with this? "Retreat quickly, save whoever can be saved! We must convey the situation here, so that other humans don't rashly come in."

The leader shouted, and the crowd, upon receiving the order, instantly scattered like birds and beasts.

Admittedly, abandoning one's companions is certainly wrong from a moral perspective.

But this is in accordance with the alliance's regulations.

When a mercenary company encounters a powerful star beast, they are allowed to flee.

If there are survivors left behind, they can transmit the intelligence out, preventing the entire human race from suffering greater losses.

This is also something unavoidable - since humans are too weak, some sacrifices are destined to be made for survival.

Although the big black-scaled snake was very fast, faced with the martial artists scattering in all directions, it couldn't immediately pursue and capture each one.

This made it even more enraged, and all of this was because of the message sent out by that human leader! "Hiss~!"

It violently inhaled spiritual energy, its massive body twisting and pushing off the ground, rushing straight towards the leader.

The leader stomped his foot and rapidly retreated.

But how could his speed possibly compare to a Grandmaster-level black-scaled snake?

He was just a post-Heavenly Ninth-Grade martial artist!

The black-scaled snake approached in an instant, its huge body destroying everything in its path, making the ground tremble.

Fierce winds blew, and the leader could even smell the foul breath of the black-scaled snake.

It's over.

At this moment, the leader had even unconsciously slowed his pace.

When a human is utterly desperate, their body will no longer put up much resistance.

It was as if he had accepted his fate.

But just as he was in despair, a figure wrapped in golden light suddenly appeared before him.

With a slight raise of the hand, a thousand-zhang sword beam burst forth.

Boom—! In an instant, the entire world was filled with white radiance, illuminating everything, and also swallowing the leader's consciousness into the darkness.

He didn't know how long it had been, it felt like a century had passed, or perhaps just a moment, when the leader felt a stinging pain on his face.

"Captain, wake up, wake up!"

He emerged from his daze, looking bewildered at the teammate embracing him.

If he remembered correctly, this teammate had just been flattened in the chest by the black-scaled snake.

"Are we in hell?"
