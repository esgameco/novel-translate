How did he do it? He's just a great master, a first-grade great master! When he was at this cultivation level, he couldn't even do this, how could he? How could he? Amidst his shock and speechlessness, Ye Xiao's blades had already started to move.

Countless spiritual power blades, covering the sky, with overwhelming force, began to frantically slash at King Aier's soul body clone.

When the blades struck King Aier's body, King Aier instantly awoke.
