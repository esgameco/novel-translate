Here is the full English translation of the Chinese excerpt:

After finishing planting all these heavenly treasures, Ye Xiao scanned through the newly obtained star beasts and extracted new imperial techniques from their star cores to supplement the requirements of his Golden Book.

Ultimately, he found four imperial techniques from these star beasts that could be recorded into the divine soul of the Golden Book.

There was no other choice, as the attack methods of these star beasts were mainly based on the most common sword techniques, claw techniques, fist techniques, and blade techniques.

The vast majority were repetitive.

The divine techniques of the Golden Book only require three techniques to be fused into one, and he already possessed several divine techniques, so there were not many left to synthesize.

However, even so, this was still a huge harvest.

The four imperial techniques were: Asura's Three Blades - an increasingly tyrannical blade technique filled with a bloodthirsty aura, surpassing even the Tyrannical Blade; Frenzied Thunder Refining Technique - a faster and more violent alchemy technique, but with higher cultivation requirements due to its immense consumption; Sovereign Trampling the Rivers and Mountains - an unexpected leg technique that could exert supreme pressure to immobilize enemies, unlike his previous leg techniques that could inflict massive destructive damage; and the Imperial Array - Brilliant Luo Imperial Array, which could move independently and possess a certain degree of self-awareness.

This was Ye Xiao's fourth array technique, in addition to the Heaven-Traversing Causal Array, the Vast Origin Grand Array, and the Earth Desolation Heaven Arrayed Formation.

However, the fusion of arrays was different from the fusion of techniques. It required more arrays, and four were not enough. He would need to find another imperial array of a different attribute in the future to complete the fusion.

Even so, he had not wasted his time last night. Not only had he resolved the issue of the cultivation base, but he had also obtained several excellent techniques, and he could even synthesize a divine-level leg technique.

Perfect!

The next morning, as Ye Xiao emerged from the jade seal, he encountered Ning Yuhen and Youwei at the door.

Ning Yuhen informed Ye Xiao that the human race had gathered the backing of several major divine clans, and it would be safer for Ye Xiao to return to the Silver Fox Valley for the time being, as Ning Yuhen would not be able to protect him here. Ye Xiao instructed Ning Yuhen to notify the Silver Fox Valley to also hide and avoid the spotlight, and to never reveal Ye Xiao's identity, as it could cause him great trouble.

Ning Yuhen promised to keep Ye Xiao's identity a secret and accepted the heavenly treasures Ye Xiao gave him before departing.

After sending off Ning Yuhen, Ye Xiao returned to the library to continue his studies. Meanwhile, a powerful aura began to emerge in the starry sky, causing the star beasts to tremble in fear, sensing that the one who had refined the divine blood of hundreds of billions of the human race was now incredibly formidable, capable of destroying them with a single punch.
