Ye Xiao understood that the power within Fa Xin had been completely exhausted.

This mountain had previously been heavily eroded by vengeful spirits, so it was about to collapse.

If it weren't for the power of the Tathagata Palm within Fa Xin, it probably would have collapsed long ago.

Now that Fa Xin's power had completely disappeared, it was only natural that it had reached its end.

Lin Wan immediately spoke to Ye Xiao:

"You must leave quickly, this mountain is about to collapse."

Ye Xiao looked at her with some confusion.

Lin Wan wiped away her tears and smiled, saying, "I've waited for him for so many years, and now that I've finally found him, where else would I go?"

Ye Xiao did not leave, but instead walked towards Lin Wan's back.

Lin Wan exclaimed in alarm, "Why are you walking back? Have you gone mad? You'll die!"

Ye Xiao spoke calmly:

"Every life bears the hopes of its loved ones, the expectations of its beloved, and the thoughts of its friends. It should be cherished."

As he spoke, he clenched his right fist and punched towards the collapsing mountain, to Lin Wan's astonished gaze. Lin Wan swore she had never witnessed such a scene in her life.

With a single punch, the heavens shattered and the earth split. A 4,000-meter-high mountain was directly reduced to rubble by this seemingly unremarkable human fist!

The fist light shot towards the sky, actually blasting a huge gap in the clouds above, causing the starlight to pour down, mysterious and extraordinary yet also eerie.

Ye Xiao had disappeared from the original spot, with no one knowing when he had vanished - perhaps in the instant of the punch, or perhaps in the moment Lin Wan was dumbfounded.

Lin Wan stood there, her face filled with disbelief at the sight.

Had she encountered a deity?

Three days later, in Jianghai City.

A figure in a pure white robe silently appeared above the surface of the Jianghai River.

He walked with his hands behind his back, floating in the air above the river, and any ordinary person could tell at a glance that he was a powerful expert above the Dazong level.

Because only those at the Dazong level or above would possess the ability to levitate.

Many passing passengers and ship owners couldn't help but take photos and post them on their social media, as powerful experts at the Dazong level were not common in this small town of Jianghai City.

Sword Immortal Senior was very strong, but he was elusive, so people rarely got to see him. Therefore, a Dazong expert could easily become a hot topic.

Soon, the photos began circulating widely on the social media of Jianghai City, causing quite a stir.

At the Jianghai City Martial Arts Academy, Pao Jiansheng had just finished his sword practice when some classmates came over to compliment him:

"Pao, your sword skills have really improved."

Pao Jiansheng took a deep breath, his expression serious:

"It's still not enough, I'm still far behind."

He glanced at the sword marks on the bronze ox in the distance, feeling a deep sense of powerlessness every time he saw them.

The sword intent in that strike was too powerful, far beyond what he could currently comprehend. It was like staring at an insurmountable mountain.

"Oh right, Pao, have you seen the news? Jianghai City has another Dazong expert now."

"Oh? Did someone advance to that level?"

"No, it's a new face. He's wearing white robes and just standing motionless on the river surface. We don't know what he's up to."

Pao Jiansheng's expression changed, and he quickly asked, "What does he look like? Let me see."

One of the classmates immediately handed over his phone.

"Here, he's all over my social media feed today. The whole city is talking about him."

Pao Jiansheng took the phone and took a quick look, his breathing momentarily stilling. His gaze then became extremely serious.

"He's not a Dazong expert."

"Huh? He's not a Dazong expert? Then what is he?"

"Only Dazong experts can stand and float in midair like that."

Pao Jiansheng spoke again, word by word:

"This is my master, the Sword Saint of Hanzhou, Liu Chengxun!"

With that, he immediately returned the phone to his classmate and hurried towards the riverside.

The other classmates, after their initial stunned silence, immediately erupted in excitement.

"What? He said that's his master, the Sword Saint of Hanzhou? A Saint-level expert?"

"Oh my god, it's actually a Saint-level cultivator who has come! I thought he was just a Dazong!"

"What are you waiting for? Hurry up and post the news! This is such an explosive piece of information, it's bound to attract tons of attention!"

"Yes, yes! Post it quickly!"

Soon, the social media and forums of Jianghai City were all abuzz with heated discussions.

The topic "The Mysterious Figure on the River is Actually the Sword Saint of Hanzhou Liu Chengxun!" quickly went viral.
