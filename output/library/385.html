Ning Zhiyuan quickly arrived in the starry sky.

In the starry sky, several massive spiritual energy ships had just arrived, even larger than the ones they had come in before.

Some of the spiritual energy ships were so massive that they could be compared to the size of a star island, reaching tens of thousands or even hundreds of thousands of meters in size.

Accompanying the arrival of these spiritual energy ships were the powerful experts of the previous generation from each family.

This group of experts were mostly at the Slay God Realm and beyond, with some even reaching the middle-to-late stages of the Slay God Realm.

The experts previously sent by the Xuanyuan Clan had a few Slay God Realm elites, but they were only a handful, just enough to hold the fort.

But now it's different.

Now, there are dozens of Slay God Realm experts! This terrifying number is enough to make one tremble in fear.

They are more than enough to sweep away the cultivators sent by the Hundred Clans.

This is the terrifying power of the Xuanyuan Clan - they can casually unleash a large group of extremely powerful experts.

And even this is not the true strength of the Xuanyuan Clan. The clan's true powerhouses have not even arrived yet.

The young masters of a few major clans, including Ning Zhiyuan, had to go up and pay their respects to these newcomers, who were mostly their uncles and a few even their grandfathers' generation.

After some polite exchanges, the starry sky once again stirred.

Soon, several even more massive spiritual energy ships, over 200,000 meters in size, slowly appeared before the crowd.

In addition to the "Xuanyuan" clan flag, the flags on these ships also bore the "Cloud" insignia.

Seeing this flag, the expressions of the crowd suddenly changed.

"This is...the Cloud family?"

"I didn't expect the Cloud family to come as well."

The next moment, several figures slowly flew out from the Xuanyuan-Cloud clan's spiritual energy ships. Leading them was an elder, who cupped his hands and smiled at the crowd: "Fellow daoists, the Cloud family has come uninvited, I hope you don't mind?"

The astonishment on everyone's faces quickly disappeared, replaced by smiles.

"Brother Cloud, don't be so polite. We are all from the Xuanyuan clan, of course we welcome Brother Cloud's arrival. But may I ask, why did you come at this time?"

"Hehe, of course it's for that peerless genius of our Xuanyuan clan."

The expressions of the crowd changed slightly. Indeed, that must be the reason.

Over the years, the Cloud family has been the strongest and purest-blooded branch within the Xuanyuan lineage, with the highest talent. The Cloud family has already cultivated several holy scions who have entered the Xuanyuan Mountain to learn the supreme martial arts of the Xuanyuan Clan.

Even in this generation, the Cloud family has produced a genius talent that could be considered a heaven-defying prodigy.

Normally, if nothing unexpected happened, this person would most likely become the next holy scion of the Xuanyuan Clan, enter the Xuanyuan Mountain, and cultivate the core martial arts of the Xuanyuan alongside the previous generation of experts, enjoying the most precious resources of the Xuanyuan Clan. In the future, they would have a high chance of becoming a God Emperor-level powerhouse!

But unexpectedly, in this human world, a talent has appeared that could surpass even the Cloud family's genius.

This is why the other major clans don't want the Cloud family to come - whoever can properly cultivate and nurture that peerless talent has the chance of making them the new number one of this generation in the Xuanyuan Clan, and the next holy scion. If they succeed in this, the Xuanyuan Mountain will surely bestow tremendous rewards and resources upon the clan that produced the new holy scion.

Those kinds of resources are something these clans can only dream of. The Cloud family has been able to maintain its hegemon status within the Xuanyuan Clan for so long precisely because they have cultivated several Xuanyuan holy scions over the years, receiving abundant rewards and resources from the Xuanyuan Mountain.
