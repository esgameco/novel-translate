On Ye Xiao's side, following what Emperor Jing had said, he released all the martial artists from the Nine Prefectures who were sealed inside the Seal of the Emperor. Then, he immediately used the Yin-Yang Escape Technique to return to the dormitory in the library.

Ning Yuhen and Youwei were not there, they should have each found a room to stay in.

Only Huan Liuli was lying on the bed, her arms and legs spread out in a 'big' shape.

The moonlight poured in through the window, crossing over the high hills, illuminating her body. The pure white stockings under the moonlight were as thin as cicada wings, completely unable to cover the delicate little toes inside.

Ye Xiao was quite speechless, and covered her with a blanket.

Huan Liuli smacked her lips and let out a few giggly murmurs.

"Mm, no, that's yucky, go wash it clean."

Ye Xiao shook his head helplessly.

The little kitten has grown up, it seems time to find her a tomcat.

However, the next moment, Ye Xiao heard Huan Liuli speak again:

"Master, you're so naughty."

Ye Xiao: "(_..)! "

Damn, what kind of dream is this fool of a cat having? Oh well, there's no time to deal with her right now, the main matter is more important.

The divine-grade sword technique 'Crack the Earth' that he cultivated within his body had already reached the Great Perfection realm.

But the Golden Book did not show any indication of being able to synthesize it.

Clearly, just like before, it was because his cultivation had not yet reached the required level.

Thinking about it carefully, it made sense. The divine-grade technique was already a cultivation beyond his level for him.

If he were to further synthesize the divine-grade technique to create an even more powerful one, he definitely wouldn't be able to control it at his current level.

Rome wasn't built in a day, and the path is not completed in a single step. Slowly, step by step.

The main issue he currently needed to focus on was cultivating his foundation.

The technique was one aspect, but he didn't know what the Xuanyuan Clan people who came to this world would choose to do. Ye Xiao felt that since the Nine Prefectures people were their descendants, they wouldn't treat them too harshly.

At least they wouldn't kill them or take them back as slaves.

Ye Xiao felt there were two possibilities, he just didn't know which one.

Would they take them away, or impart some techniques to them? If it was the former, he could consider using the storage ring to take some books from the library, then go to the Xuanyuan Clan's territory to see if he could extract more Emperor-grade techniques from their techniques to fuse with his remaining Emperor-grade techniques, upgrading them to more divine-grade ones!

If it was the latter, that would be even better. He could directly obtain some powerful techniques from them to fuse.

If the other party treated them poorly, he could also choose to first find a place to hide, then use the first method to supplement himself and secretly improve his cultivation.

With the level of the Golden Book's divine spirit, Ye Xiao believed that his strength would one day rise to a realm that could rival the Xuanyuan Clan.

By then, he could be a little more at ease.

However, aside from this, there was one more thing.

That was cultivation resources.

Techniques were only a part of cultivation resources, not the entirety.

A larger part of cultivation resources referred to things like elixirs, blood-rich beast meats, etc.

Since Emperor Jing also had some concerns and was uncertain how the Xuanyuan Clan would treat them, he decided to have Ye Xiao hide himself first and not overly expose his identity.

In the initial stage, he definitely wouldn't be able to obtain too many resources.

Fortunately, with this wave of beast calamity, he had captured many Supernal Realm experts, so he could happily replenish his blood qi in the future.

But this also brought up a new problem - his spirit beasts could no longer be kept under the library.

There were many experts in the Clan, they would definitely be discovered.

That would be a big trouble.

And his spiritual herbs, where should he place them? Storing them all in his storage ring wouldn't work.

Because the spatial range of his storage ring was far from enough for these spirit beasts to live in.

He needed an extremely large storage space.

Ye Xiao carefully thought it through, and silently took out the Seal of the Emperor given to him by Emperor Cloud.

Wasn't this a huge storage space? And now it was his, he could use it however he wanted.

When he had released the martial artists from the Nine Prefectures before, Ye Xiao had noticed that the space inside this jade seal was very large, far larger than his spatial storage ring.

It could even be said that it was like a small world, comparable to a small state.

This made Ye Xiao slightly puzzled and curious.

Were all the treasures of the Clan this powerful?

A spatial storage device could have such a huge effect, then who knows how formidable the other treasures would be?

However, Ye Xiao was not certain about the ranking of this item in the Clan.

Perhaps it was something the Xuanyuan ancestors had painstakingly found from somewhere, and not an ordinary spatial storage device.

Tonight, he would first set up the spirit beast breeding ground.

Thinking of this, Ye Xiao gathered all his spiritual herbs and stored them inside the Seal of the Emperor.

Then, he used the Yin-Yang Escape Technique to go to the spirit beast breeding ground under the library, and moved all those spirit beasts...
