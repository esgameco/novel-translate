But with the Indestructible Divine Skill, Ye Xiao's defensive capabilities can be greatly enhanced. Even if he does not dodge, evade or counterattack, he can still withstand the attacks of a Grand Master through the sheer toughness of his body.

Jin Shu immediately began to cultivate the Indestructible Divine Skill. It was now 2 a.m. at night, and Ye Xiao returned to his room to rest. By tomorrow morning, the Indestructible Divine Skill should have reached the peak realm.

Early the next morning, Ye Xiao looked at the high blanket and fell into deep thought.

The Indestructible Divine Skill had already been cultivated to the peak realm, but he was not considering this problem now, but rather whether this technique would really turn him into steel!

He sat up and tried it out, finally letting out a sigh of relief.

Fortunately, without activating the technique, he was still normal.

Subsequently, he activated the Indestructible Divine Skill, and surprisingly found that his body was enveloped in a golden glow.

This golden glow originated from Jin Shu's divine soul, and then spread from the divine soul to the area one millimeter outside Ye Xiao's body.

Although the distance it spread was not far, it gave Ye Xiao's defensive power an extraordinary boost.

Every inch of Ye Xiao's skin, even every single cell, was protected by the Indestructible Divine Skill and could withstand attacks at the Grand Master level.

He tried punching himself.

Duang~! Duang~! His fist made a crisp metallic sound, with a very clear and pleasant texture.

"Incredible, let's see how strong an attack this technique can withstand."

Taking a deep breath, Ye Xiao unleashed the Severing Heaven Sword on his own arm.

Pu Tong~!

The entire room fell silent. Ye Xiao looked at the arm lying quietly on the floor, deep in thought.

He had been careless.

The Severing Heaven Sword and the Indestructible Divine Skill should be at the same level.

Normally, the most a Grand Master could use would be Patriarch-level techniques, but the Severing Heaven Sword far exceeded that category.

Therefore, the Indestructible Divine Skill could not withstand its attack.

Sighing softly, Ye Xiao picked up his arm, used the Mending Heavens Technique to repair it, and soon his arm was completely healed without even a scar left.

It seems that with the Indestructible Divine Skill, he could not defend against everything. He could only say that defending against Grand Master-level or surpassing Deity Patriarch-level attacks was not a problem.

To obtain even higher defensive power, he would need even greater cultivation. Then, when he activated the Indestructible Divine Skill, his defensive power would be even stronger.

But cultivation was not something that could be achieved overnight. Perhaps he should pick up forging again and try to refine some powerful defensive armors.

Ordinary or superior-grade defensive armors would definitely not work.

They had no special abilities and couldn't even change shape, and their defensive power was not even enough to match a Grand Master's own defense, let alone with his Indestructible Divine Skill.
