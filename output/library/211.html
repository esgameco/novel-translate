This is simply not allowed given the human lifespan.

It seems that I should find a way to cultivate my spiritual power next.

The Taixuan Tiangong is for cultivating mental power, which is not the same as soul power.

Just like cultivation is not the same as physical strength.

Mental power is the power used by the soul, just like spiritual energy is the power used by the physical body.

So spiritual energy can harm the physical body, while mental power can harm the soul.

This is why only those at the Grand Master Realm or above, or those who specialize in mental cultivation techniques, can deal with the existence of ghosts and spirits.

"It seems that I should find a way to improve my soul power next."

After understanding the information about the King Realm, it was also time to get off work. The golden book within Ye Xiao had already cultivated the last claw technique to the Great Perfection Realm, emanating a faint golden glow.

He could go home and refine it now.

After punching out, Ye Xiao used the Divine Will Technique to instantly disappear from the spot and return home, beginning to refine the claw technique.

Activating the divine soul of the golden book, the pages recording the technique gradually merged into a few large characters:

Grab! Knead! Rub! Pinch! Squeeze! Clamp! Pinch! Pull! Press! These were some strange-sounding words, but they made sense since they were claw techniques, after all. The basic skills were nothing more than these methods.

Soon, the basic technique was refined into a brand-new imperial claw technique - the Star-Plucking Palm!

The Star-Plucking Palm can grab objects from a distance and kill people invisibly. As long as the cultivation is strong enough, it can capture people from hundreds, thousands, or even tens of thousands of miles away, as easily as reaching into a bag.

How powerful! It's practically an invincible control skill.

With this technique, even if the enemy runs far away, he can be instantly captured and brought back.

After mastering this technique, could he use it to remotely control and capture some star beasts on the star battlefield, without having to directly face powerful ones like the Tiger God?

After learning the technique, Ye Xiao continued to read some of the books he had brought back from the library. He reckoned that Bee Susanne should have finished her work, so he ordered Illusion Liuli to go to the mountain and meadow where he grew flowers, and pick some flowers to send over for Susanne to continue collecting honey.

As a qualified breeder, he had to make the most of every moment and try to create more benefits for himself from the breeding animals. Moreover, keeping them busy and overwhelmed would prevent them from having the time and energy to resist and try to escape.

Illusion Liuli received the order and quickly went to pick the flowers. She wanted to show the master that she was far more capable than the Great Dragon, who was just a cold, iron lump that could only be used to kill people.

She, on the other hand, could not only do the laundry and cook, but also warm the bed. If she could win Ye Xiao's favor, she estimated that she could become a Divine Ancestor in about three thousand years.

Just thinking about it made her feel happy and blessed.

As for Chen Qingxuan and the others trapped in Ye Xiao's formation, their situation was truly miserable.

Originally, they thought that even if they couldn't break the formation immediately, they would be able to do so within a month. But they never expected that this formation not only had illusion techniques, but also deadly killing power!

In other words, it was a deadly formation.

Starting from the fourth hour after they entered the formation, the killing formation began to activate. Various attacks came at them relentlessly, beating them into a sorry state, with the Divine Ancestors, Grand Masters, and Formation Masters all cowering and struggling to survive in the formation.

Until now, the killing power had subsided and turned into an illusion formation, allowing the group to finally catch their breath. But they were all lying on the ground, their bones aching, and finding it difficult to even stand up.

"I can't go on anymore. Professor Chen, can we really escape this formation?" Chen Qingxuan, lying on the ground, had tears welling up in his eyes, silently flowing down.

Because at this point, he didn't know anymore. He was completely lost in this formation.

The Chen Clan had been cultivating formations for nearly a thousand years, with even a three-year-old child having a basic understanding of a few formations. Yet now, he was trapped in a formation in Jianghai City, unable to escape.

How embarrassing! The ancestors of the Chen Clan would be ashamed.
