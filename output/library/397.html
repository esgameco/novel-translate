This Starbeast Essence Blood was extremely powerful, able to strengthen the Great Dragon's spirit.

In the mortal world, those great masters had just fallen asleep, but suddenly heard the commotion in the courtyard and came out of their respective rooms.

When they saw Ning Zhiyuan, they went forward and asked, "Young Master Ning, what happened? Why are you all making a commotion in the courtyard so late at night?"

Ning Zhiyuan immediately responded:

"Masters, just now, for some unknown reason, the weapons in our hands all started trembling. Is there some kind of danger approaching?"

"What did you say? The weapons started trembling?"

Master Fu Long and the other few weapon refiners' expressions suddenly changed, and then they became solemn.

"Let me see!"

Master Fu Long snatched the divine long sword from Ning Zhiyuan's hand, glanced at it, then bit his two fingers and squeezed out a drop of essence blood.

"Settle!"

He printed that drop of essence blood on the divine long sword, but the divine long sword did not calm down as he had imagined, but continued to tremble. Even more, it began to bend, wanting to kneel towards a certain direction.

"It's as I thought!"

Master Fu Long's eyes shone with excitement, and the other masters were also excited.

"I can't believe this is actually true! We've always thought it was just a legend, but today we actually witnessed this kind of thing."

"Extraordinary, this is truly extraordinary!"

Ning Zhiyuan was somewhat puzzled, frowning slightly, and couldn't help but ask again, "Masters, what does this represent?"

Master Fu Long patted his chest to calm down the excitement in his heart.

"In our world of weapon refiners, there is an ancient legend. When all the weapons start trembling, it means that a precious treasure is about to be born!"

"What did you say?" Ning Zhiyuan's pupils suddenly shrank.

Although he didn't know why these weapons were trembling and bowing, he had definitely heard of precious treasures before.

The Xuanyuan Clan had a precious treasure! The Xuanyuan Divine Sword!

The ancient ancestor of the Xuanyuan Clan had left behind this clan treasure. Precious treasures are powerful existences that can amplify divine arts.

Even the best nine-star divine weapons cannot withstand the repeated casting of divine arts, let alone unleashing divine arts to the extreme.

But with a precious treasure, one can achieve this.

To put it simply, in the same case of having divine arts and reaching the realm of divine emperor, possessing a precious treasure would basically give one a great advantage.

So the preciousness of a precious treasure is self-evident.

"Then the direction they are bowing towards must be the location of that precious treasure, right?"

"Young Master Ning is correct."

"Let's go!"

The other weapon refiners, as well as the experts of the Ning Clan, immediately followed.

At this moment, Ye Xiao had already successfully forged the Great Dragon.

Looking at the brand-new Great Dragon, which was releasing an unprecedented powerful and terrifying aura, Ye Xiao was very satisfied.

He tapped on the weapon, and the Great Dragon emitted a crisp trembling response. The surface of the weapon was as smooth as a mirror, and one could clearly see a giant dragon excitedly roaming inside.

It was evident that the dragon spirit of the Great Dragon had grown even larger than before.

Ye Xiao smiled.

"You seem to really like this new home. I just wonder what level you are now? How strong is your strength?"

Just as he finished saying this, Ye Xiao suddenly frowned.

He felt that from all directions, many auras were flying towards this place.

"This is really strange, I didn't even cause any thunder tribulation, so why are so many people being attracted here?"

But after a moment, he shook his head and turned to leave.

No matter what it was, it had nothing to do with him. He only wanted to cultivate peacefully, and he didn't want others to discover the secrets about him.
