The art of physiognomy is just a very simple and ordinary ocular technique, with a very low level of mastery, and a low entry threshold. Of course, the effect is also low! But there's a saying that goes, "The sword in the hands of the weak can still defeat the small pebble in the hands of the expert."

So, no matter how trashy the art of physiognomy is, it depends on who is using it.

If it's someone else, then it's truly trash.

But Ye Xiao's cultivation has already reached the realm of the Innate Ninth Grade. With such powerful abilities, even a small technique like this can produce extraordinary effects.

When he was by the bedside, practicing the ocular technique, the next moment, he saw that on the buildings of the entire Central State City, there were auras of varying strengths floating about.

Some people had auras as powerful as a dragon, as massive as a mountain.

Some people's auras were a bit weaker, strong like a white rainbow, forming a streak in the sky.

And some were pitifully weak, almost negligible, like a strand of hair.

Those were mostly children, because they had never cultivated before, so the spiritual energy in their bodies was very scarce.

Apart from that, there were also some that were rather special, some were dark red or black in color.

Those were most likely not good things.

But Ye Xiao was not a savior, and he couldn't go find them one by one.

Moreover, to judge a living being as good or bad based solely on physiognomy? Ye Xiao felt that would be a bit too arbitrary.

However, it must be said that the ocular technique is quite useful, combining spiritual power exploration, physical attacks, and illusory techniques and so on.

The art of physiognomy is far inferior to the Supreme Mystery Heaven Gaze, merely because it is an ordinary technique, and not a synthesized imperial-level ocular technique!

But the Golden Book can be used to synthesize ocular techniques, and as long as he learns more basic ocular techniques in the future, he can synthesize an imperial-level ocular technique, which would be sufficient.

From the reflection in the mirror, Ye Xiao saw that the pupils of his eyes had completely turned from black to golden.

This was similar to that Xia Bingyan's method, where using it would also change the color of the eyes.

Just then, his doorbell rang.

Ye Xiao glanced over, this aura should be Yang Qian's.

He put away the ocular technique and went to open the door.

Well, this woman does look better in a dress.

"Manager, I still want to thank you for today's matter!"

"Okay!"

Yang Qian actually didn't like Western food either, but when inviting someone to a meal, she didn't feel comfortable going to a roadside stall.

Unexpectedly, Ye Xiao took the initiative to speak up, so it was a good opportunity to go along with it.

The two of them went out to the street and found a snack street, sampling some delicious snacks.

Just strolling around, a little over two hours had passed.

"Manager, look, my belly has been fattened up by you."

Yang Qian pointed to her small belly, which had become plump and round.

She had thought that when Ye Xiao came to the snack street, he might just politely go through the motions, and not eat too much.

But unexpectedly, he ate from start to finish, not missing out on anything - fried, stir-fried, boiled, fried, cold, hot, sour, sweet, he didn't leave a single thing.

As a result, she had been eaten into the shape of a six-month pregnant woman, round and plump, like a giant panda.

But for some reason, the manager's belly had never changed at all? The manager had eaten much more than her, but his belly remained perfectly fine, still in good shape.

This left Yang Qian feeling very puzzled.

Ye Xiao spoke indifferently:

"That sentence of yours is a bit lacking in conscience. If it weren't for me, could you have picked out the delicious ones yourself?

Many things, I could tell at a glance whether they were good or not, saving you a lot of money."

Yang Qian nodded.

The manager's words were not wrong.

"The street has been walked through, so we should head back now. We still have training and further studies to attend to tomorrow."

Ye Xiao nodded.

"Yes, it's time to go back and rest."

The two of them prepared to walk back, following the route they had come.

At this time, it was already very late, around past 11 o'clock at night, and the street was empty, with not a single person around.

Song Xiaoyan also saw Yang Qian, and a trace of panic flashed in her eyes.

"What I do is none of your business."

Yang Qian's face turned cold.

"What you do may not be my business, but if you harm others, I absolutely cannot allow it. Release her!"

Song Xiaoyan snorted lightly.

"Don't pretend to be a good person, this woman owes me money, so I'm going to catch her. If you keep interfering, don't blame me for not being polite to you."

The woman was also a cultivator of the martial way, and after struggling a bit, she immediately shouted loudly:

"I don't know her, I don't owe her any money at all. Please, please save me!"

Song Xiaoyan's eyes turned cold, and she immediately pressed her down even harder.

"On the verge of death, you still stubbornly refuse. Aren't you tired of living?"

With that, she struck the woman unconscious with a palm.

Yang Qian sneered, "Even if she really did something wrong, that's the defense team's business, you have no right to arrest her."

"I see you're looking for trouble. Da Wen, Xiao Wen, come out and subdue her!"

Yang Qian's face tightened, and with a heavy stomp of her foot, her body suddenly leapt high into the air.

"Hmph! You're just lucky." Song Xiaoyan spat, and immediately prepared to flee.
