Overall, this trip to the ancestral tomb of the human race has yielded a harvest that cannot be described as anything less than massive.

This time, Ye Xiao has obtained so much!

First, he was able to determine the reason why he could not become a god, and with the help of the ancestors, he successfully resolved this issue, raising the divine blood within him to over a million drops, allowing him to achieve perfect harmony with the divine soul.

He then activated the divine eyes, although he doesn't know exactly what they are.

However, since the ancestors have emphasized it so much, it must be something quite remarkable.

In addition to that, he also learned about the Divine Treasure Vault.

Ye Xiao has decided that he must strive to reach the Divine Realm as soon as possible!

Only by reaching the Divine Realm will he have a chance to find the Kunlun Dragon Vein and locate the Divine Treasure Vault.

Many years ago, although the ancestors said they found the Divine Treasure Vault by sheer coincidence, their cultivation at the time was already at the Divine Realm.

With his current Emperor Realm cultivation, there is a 100% chance that he will not be able to find the Kunlun Dragon Vein.

He will set a short-term goal first - to enter the Divine Realm. Then, his long-term goal will be to find the Divine Treasure Vault.

If he can beat the others to it and find the Divine Treasure Vault, and obtain the treasures within, he might just be able to cultivate himself into an existence at the Divine Emperor level.

If that were the case, it would be perfect.

Divine Emperor! Just the thought of it fills Ye Xiao with a sense of beauty.

If his guess is correct, the Divine Emperor should be the mightiest existence in the starry sky.

As long as he can reach that realm, even if he doesn't do much, with the support of the Xuanyuan Clan, it should be enough for him to live a peaceful life in the future.

Currently, there are two issues he needs to overcome to achieve divinity.

The first is the issue of spiritual energy.

The ancestors have said that to succeed in the final breakthrough to become a god, he will need an immense amount of spiritual energy.

Relying solely on absorbing spiritual energy within his body will not be enough, unless he has a supreme-grade cultivation method.

But without reaching the Divine Realm, he cannot further refine the divine scriptures within the Golden Book, so how could he possibly obtain a supreme-grade technique?

This is a paradox.

This is where spirit pills and spiritual gathering formations come into play.

With the star beasts he has, as well as the abundant celestial materials and treasures, refining a divine pill should not be too difficult for him.

The next step is to wait until his pill refining technique is successfully fused and fully cultivated, then he can start making preparations.

The other issue is formations.

He currently has four formations - the Earthly Calamity and Heavenly Talisman Formation, the Grand Heavenly Origin Formation, the Heavenly Transformation Creation Formation, and the Brilliant Luo Emperor Formation.

However, only the Grand Heavenly Origin Formation is a spiritual gathering formation, while the others do not have this effect.

Ye Xiao urgently needs to find another technique to fuse.

As long as he can find another technique to fuse, he will be able to create a divine formation.

At that time, the power of the spiritual gathering formation will rapidly increase.

Perfect!

The days ahead are filled with motivation once again.

After leaving Kunlun, the three parted ways and returned to their respective places.

Ye Xiao also returned to Jianghai City.

The good news is that as soon as he returned to Jianghai City, the three Emperor Realm pill refining techniques he had were all successfully fused.

He has created a Divine Technique-level pill refining method - the Divine Luo Pill.

This pill refining technique has consolidated the characteristics of three pill refining arts - it possesses the ultimate power to refine impurities, the ability to forcefully suppress spiritual energy, and the capacity to perfectly enhance the quality of pills.

These three powerful abilities combined mean that Ye Xiao can refine even a pile of cow dung into a pill, extracting all the nutritional elements and then upgrading the pill.

In this way, the value of the pill can be increased exponentially beyond that of a mere pile of cow dung.

Entering the Jade Seal, Ye Xiao sees that Huan Liuli is diligently cultivating, still wearing her maid outfit with cat ears and black stockings, which are Balenciaga.

This silly cat, she never has a proper demeanor, and even when she's being serious, she still seems a bit unserious.

A combination of silliness and middle-school-level edginess, with a touch of... well, never mind.

The fact that she's able to start cultivating seriously is already quite praiseworthy, considering cats are naturally lazy, and her intelligence is not the highest.

Sensing his presence, Huan Liuli opens her eyes.

"Master!"

Huan Liuli excitedly gets up and comes in front of Ye Xiao, raising her little arms.

"Master, look at me, my cultivation has advanced again. I've finally broken through to the Divine Beast Realm."

Ye Xiao nods and gives a perfunctory compliment.

"Mm, not bad."

"Hey, Master, would you like to test my current strength?"

"How? By letting you hit me?"

"No no no!"

Huan Liuli quickly waves her hands.

"Master is joking, I would never dare to hit you. It should be you hitting me, like giving me a light tap, to test how much I can endure."

"It will hurt."

"It's fine, it won't hurt too much."
