Elder Xuanwu took a deep breath, his gaze resolute as he stepped forward.

He would use his Monarch Realm strength and the defensive power of his Xuanwu energy to suppress Li Liushui, buying a little more time for Li Liushui.

As for the citizens of Jianghai City, they had already been evacuated for the most part over the past few days, so he could go all out in his battle against Li Liushui.

In the underground library, in the breeding grounds, the various star beasts who had been playing small games, chatting, collecting honey, or milking, were suddenly busy when a large hole opened up in the ceiling above them.

Immediately after, a beam of starlight poured down.

All the star beasts were stunned - the formation had actually been broken?

This was understandable.

Ye Xiao had not anticipated that there would be Monarch Realm or near-Monarch Realm experts attacking this place, so the formation he had set up and the number of spirit stones he had placed were only enough to defend against Monarch Realm experts.

This was because the performance of a formation depends on two factors - its upper limit and the spirit stones powering it.

If the formation's upper limit is not high enough, no matter how many spirit stones are used, it can only display effects within that limit. And if there are enough spirit stones to reach the formation's full potential, but the formation's upper limit is not high enough, it still cannot unleash its full power.

But if too many spirit stones are used, it will consume a lot, just like using a large cargo truck to carry a small bag versus a small motorcycle - the fuel consumption will be very different.

Moreover, the number of spirit stones is limited, as is the spiritual energy contained within them. The daily consumption is quite high.

This is why Ye Xiao had planned to wait until the star beasts' strength increased before continuing to add more spirit stones to the formation and enhance its power.

But this time, the attack from Li Liushui, enhanced by demonic energy, was no weaker than a Monarch Realm expert's strike, which is why it was able to break through this imperial formation.

The star beasts looked at each other, suddenly frightened.

"Whoa! The formation has been broken!"

"No way? Who's so powerful? They actually broke the formation? That's insane."

"If the formation is broken, does that mean we can escape now?"

Some star beasts couldn't help but have a gleam in their eyes.

But at this moment, everyone fell silent.

Not a single star beast made a move.

If this had been before, they would have already scattered and fled in all directions.

But now, they found that their feet were somewhat stuck.

It wasn't because they were afraid of Ye Xiao. After all, even though Ye Xiao was strong, there were so many star beasts here, and if they fled separately, Ye Xiao wouldn't be able to catch up to them.

The problem was, if they fled now, where else could they find such a good cultivation ground? Ye Xiao's spirit gathering formation had greatly enhanced the spiritual energy of all of Jianghai City, especially in the breeding grounds.

Being within this spirit gathering formation, their cultivation speed would double.

Because star beasts rely on their own bodies to constantly absorb spiritual energy.

And there was another point - the resources Ye Xiao provided.

He gave them so many precious treasures every day.

Although they had to sacrifice some of their flesh and blood, compared to what Ye Xiao gave them, they had gained countless times more.

Compared to freedom, this didn't seem like much.

Because their so-called freedom was at most just being able to move around outside. But after going out, they would still have to listen to the commands of those top-tier star beasts, and still have to risk their lives.

To truly achieve freedom is practically impossible without sufficient strength.

But here, they could rapidly increase their cultivation, only needing to sacrifice a bit of their flesh and blood, without risking their lives. And Ye Xiao would also guarantee their safety. Calculated this way, it was actually a great deal!

Even though they were star beasts and their minds weren't as clever as humans with all their plots and schemes, they could still discern what was better for themselves, what was more beneficial.

"I don't want to leave."

"But once we miss this chance, there may not be another. Who knows, maybe this was the result of Ye Xiao's battle with someone else, and next time he discovers it, he'll definitely strengthen the formation, and then it'll be even harder for us to escape."

The ones speaking were Feng Long and Qing Niu, the latest arrivals, and they were the only ones among the star beasts who still had the desire to escape.

The other star beasts had already grown accustomed to this tamed lifestyle.

"But what can we do out there? To survive after leaving, we'd have to join some faction, serve under a divine beast as our leader, and still have to charge into battle against human experts. How is that any different from being here?

Here it's so safe, we have food and drink, and an abundance of spiritual energy. Isn't that far better than going out?"
