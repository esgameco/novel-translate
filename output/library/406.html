He sat down at the desk and started reading the book.

This evoked countless emotions within him.

It had been just over two years since the Divine Tribe's arrival, and before their arrival, he and Ye Xiao were both at the Emperor Realm, with only a slight gap between them.

But now, he felt like a primary school student in comparison.

It could now be said without any politeness that Ye Xiao was the second among the younger generation of the Xuanyuan Tribe!

The gap was truly heaven and earth.

In the starry sky, the Xuanyuan Tribe and the Hundred Tribes Alliance had been excavating four Divine King Tombs for a full month.

They did not have Ye Xiao's level of array-breaking skills and methods, and every time they opened a Divine King Tomb, they would fight fiercely with each other over the resources.

In order to obtain the resources from these four Divine King Tombs, they had sacrificed countless lives.

But in the end, the resources they obtained were able to make up for these losses.

Only no one had noticed that the bodies of those who had died would mysteriously disappear from the starry sky battlefield.

After a month, the two major forces had completely stripped the four Divine King Tombs, not leaving a single soft hair behind.

The Xuanyuan Tribe had obtained the most resources, about two and a half Divine Tombs.

While the Hundred Tribes Alliance had obtained about one and a half Divine Tombs.

Now it was time to divide the spoils.

It was clear that the Xuanyuan Tribe had reaped a bountiful harvest, and it was said that even the students of the Academy, like the Emperor, had obtained a certain amount of cultivation resources.

During this one month, Ye Xiao's cultivation had also further advanced to the Slay God Realm Third Layer! Meanwhile, the excavation progress of the Divine National Treasure Vault had also reached 60%.

The tunnel still lacked the last 40% to reach the door of the Divine National Treasure Vault.

After all, this was largely thanks to the participation of the Silver Fox Clan. Their large numbers meant that the excavation of the divine crystals did not require strong strength, but rather a large workforce and endless labor.

Ye Xiao estimated that at this rate, it would take at most four more months for him to completely excavate the divine crystals and enter the Divine National Treasure Vault.

He wondered what kind of treasures would be inside the Divine National Treasure Vault? Would he be able to greatly improve his cultivation at that time?

That day, he continued reading, and managed to cultivate a new body technique divine art - the Spatial Heavenly Escape!

This was the second body technique divine art he had obtained after the Yin-Yang Heavenly Escape.

What surprised Ye Xiao was that this time, it did not require the combination of other types of divine arts, but could be directly self-synthesized.

This was quite novel. After all, so far, all the divine arts Ye Xiao had encountered required the synthesis of multiple types of divine arts to form the ancient precious art.

The body technique class was currently the only one that could be synthesized solely based on its own source arts.

After cultivating it to the Great Perfection Realm, Ye Xiao clicked to synthesize it and then left it there, waiting for the golden book to complete the final synthesis.

It was already near the end of the workday, and Ye Xiao heard his colleagues in the library discussing not far away: "This time, our Xuanyuan Tribe really made a huge profit, obtaining a lot of good things."

"That's right, several major families came down from the starry sky and distributed a large batch of precious resources. My second aunt's daughter-in-law, who is in the Martial Arts Academy, has good talent and suddenly obtained hundreds of divine crystals! And she's just an ordinary student, far inferior to other students with higher talents, but she still got so many resources."

"That's nothing! Although the resources the Ning Family gave to the Martial Arts Academy were not few, in reality, that was just a drop in the bucket. I heard that the Ning Family brought back many coffins from the starry sky, and the treasures inside are probably countless!"

Regarding these matters, Ye Xiao also listened carefully.

He was not particularly interested in those resources, he just wanted to know if anyone would investigate the news about the Divine National Treasure Vault. Aside from that, the rest had little impact on him.

But for now, it seemed very stable, and he had not heard anyone discussing the Divine National Treasure Vault.
