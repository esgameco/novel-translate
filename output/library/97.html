Pao Jiansheng had defeated Qin Yuyan, and he had just fought against Sasaki Taro, so he had more say than anyone else.

On the field, Sasaki Taro stood arrogantly, his eyes half-closed.

Sensing Qin Yuyan's arrival, he slowly opened his eyes, but only halfway, not fully.

He slightly raised his chin and glanced at Qin Yuyan out of the corner of his eye.

He didn't even take Qin Yuyan seriously.

Not only was Qin Yuyan an unknown figure before, but she had also been defeated by Pao Jiansheng.

The disciple of his disciple's disciple, that was certainly his own disciple's disciple.

Sasaki Taro spoke indifferently, his tone revealing a hint of disdain, as if he had completely disregarded Qin Yuyan.

Qin Yuyan seemed to have already seen through his arrogance, but she did not strike first.

With a firm gaze, neither sad nor happy, she calmly said:

"This is my home field, and you are the guest, so naturally you should make the first move."

Sasaki Taro narrowed his eyes slightly.

"You are playing with fire!"

Qin Yuyan no longer spoke, only tightly gripping the hilt of her sword.

Seeing this, a surge of anger rose in Sasaki Taro's eyes.

He had been looked down upon by a woman.

And not just any woman, but the disciple of his disciple's disciple.

This was no different from being looked down upon by a piece of trash. Whoosh! He drew his long sword and coldly said:

"The sword knows no eyes, and if I accidentally maim you, don't blame me!"

With that, he lightly stepped, instantly leaving his original position.

With the same level of martial arts cultivation as Pao Jiansheng, suppressed to the realm of the Posterior Heaven Fifth Grade.

But his speed was even faster than Pao Jiansheng's! The key to the Iga-ryu ninja sword technique lies in this.

This sword technique had already transcended the realm of ordinary sword techniques.

Its terrifying speed and bizarre movement path, like a fierce ghost, sent shivers down the spines of the onlookers.

"Such a fast sword!"

Qin Yuyan stood still, not moving an inch.

Sasaki Taro's speed was extremely fast, almost in an instant, he had already arrived in front of her.

Ten meters, five meters, three meters, one meter! Just as Sasaki Taro reached her side, Qin Yuyan suddenly struck.

A sword drawn out like lightning.

Seeing this sword strike, all the high-level experts on the scene couldn't help but have their hearts suddenly shaken, their eyes wide open, revealing a deep sense of disbelief.

The most unbelievable one was Sasaki Taro!

At this moment, he was closest to Qin Yuyan, so he could best feel the strength of Qin Yuyan's sword intent!

This move almost made his heart struggle to breathe! It was not a suppression of cultivation, but a pure crushing of martial dao intent!

It was only at this moment that he realized he had underestimated this girl.

Completely overlooked her.

Boom! Without giving him time to think further, the next moment, their swords collided!
