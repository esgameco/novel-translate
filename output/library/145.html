The star beasts' mouths curled up, and excited smiles appeared on their faces.

Under the starlight, a golden-brown light beam quickly flew towards the direction of Jianghai City.

The night passed quickly.

In the blink of an eye, it was dawn.

Ye Xiao woke up from the early morning. The quilt was still the same as before, with an overly high slope that was gradually becoming even steeper.

Previously, when Ye Xiao was just cultivating, the height didn't increase, only the rigidity strength.

After eating the Black-scaled Snake, Black Xuanwu Saint Envoy, and Aier King, the height kept increasing.

Could it be that eating these star beasts would bring about physical changes? But thinking about it carefully, it does seem to be the case.

The aura of star beasts is on average higher than that of the human race, as the human race has the weakest aura.

After eating powerful star beasts, Ye Xiao's own aura would continuously rise, so his physical qualities in all aspects would surpass what they were before.

This can be considered a good thing.

Because with the increase of the inner aura, Ye Xiao's cultivation will become stronger.

The aura is of great help for absorbing the spiritual energy of heaven and earth, as well as for physical combat prowess.

After getting up, Ye Xiao measured his height again, feeling like he had grown another two centimeters taller.

This made him slightly worried.

Could it be that his height will also keep increasing? If that's the case, would he turn into a giant? If that happens, Ye Xiao is very averse to it, as he is a normal person who only likes normal human body proportions.

But it should be fine. There are many people who have eaten star beasts, and he hasn't heard of anyone who developed abnormalities from eating their flesh.

However, directly eating star cores can cause some physical changes.

But he doesn't eat star cores.

Ye Xiao measured a bit, and the meat of Noerfeisi from last night provided about a 1% increase in his cultivation.

After all, it was only a detached body of Noerfeisi, not the whole, or even just a small part of the whole.

Being able to increase his cultivation by this much is already quite good.

Looking at this, star beasts are truly treasures for humans.

Star cores can be refined into pills, and their techniques can be extracted. The flesh of star beasts can also be eaten to increase one's cultivation, although not by much.

Their fur, scales, horns, and bones can all be used to refine weapons.

In the future, when Ye Xiao's strength reaches a peak realm, he must consider going to the Star Battlefield.

When Ye Xiao came downstairs, ready to go to work, he couldn't help but be slightly taken aback.

On the sofa was a 1.45-meter cat-eared girl - Huan Liuli!

She had grown taller!

Most importantly, her cultivation had broken through! She had reached the Saint Master realm at the Innate Fourth Grade.

After reacting, the corners of Ye Xiao's mouth curled up slightly.

Very good, worthy of a Dragon-level bloodline.

After eating so many high-grade star beasts, she has grown rapidly.

At this rate, it won't be long before Huan Liuli can become a Divine Sect expert.

By then, combined with himself, there will be two Divine Sect experts.

Should he unseal Noerfeisi's restriction and dig him out, then have the whole of him eaten? That way, both he and Huan Liuli's cultivation should increase a bit more.

But after thinking about it, Ye Xiao rejected this idea.

Noerfeisi is used to provide spiritual energy for the entire Jianghai City. If he digs up Noerfeisi, all the people in Jianghai City will not be able to receive the spiritual energy feedback, which would be a bit too much.

But there's no need to be too anxious. Didn't the previous news say that the Seven-Star Convergence caused several Divine Sect-level star beasts to escape?

If he can capture and eat those Divine Sect-level star beasts, it should also be able to increase his aura.

"Mmm~ Master, why have you been staring at me?" Huan Liuli said with a yawn as she stretched on the sofa.
