With the healing abilities of the doctors at Jianghai City Hospital, combined with medication, they still lacked the capability to stop the bleeding. The damage to the umbilical cord was causing insufficient oxygen supply to the baby, like a drowning person, naturally leading to the fetal kicking and struggling.

With him here, Lu Sister will definitely be fine."

Because just now, while healing Lu Wanwan, his spiritual power had suddenly detected two unusual auras!

The aura of a demihuman!

Huan Liuli looked at them with great vigilance.

"Demihumans?"

"That's right, we have been ordered by the Holy Envoy to come and bring you back, Huan Liuli, the Phantom Cat."

Huan Liuli's cat body trembled, and a layer of mist instantly covered her eyes! After more than fifty years! Finally, finally, a beast organization has come to take her back!
