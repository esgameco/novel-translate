Books about food? What does he want the books about food for? Before they could ponder for more than two seconds, suddenly one of the Grand Masters exploded into a mist of blood.

Damn it, how could their brains be so stupid just now?

He is the Aier King, a beast spirit that kills people, and a Saint Monarch-level one at that. Whatever he wants, they just have to do it, why think so much? Not that they can be fully blamed.

The Aier King's actions are simply too unfathomable.

Soon, the crowd gathered a pile of books and carefully placed them in front of the Aier King, then respectfully stepped aside, afraid that the Aier King might suddenly attack.

As for the cautious and obsequious behavior of the few people, the Aier King merely disdained them with a glance.

A bunch of idiots, he is a Saint Monarch-level beast spirit! He can kill whoever he wants, he doesn't care whether these humans will listen to him or not.

Whether he kills them or not is entirely up to his whim, he can kill them if he wants to, and not kill them if he doesn't want to.

They are being so respectful and hypocritical, does that mean he won't kill people anymore? Ridiculous!

However, at the moment, he really doesn't have the desire to kill anyone.

That Grand Master he just killed with a thought...
