He immediately unleashed the Taixuan Tiangong Scan, and as his spiritual power increased, the range of his Taixuan Tiangong search had expanded far beyond its previous standard.

In the blink of an eye, he found what he was looking for.

"So I've hit something, better hurry down and use the Repair Heaven Technique to save the person."

But before he could go down, he saw the American flag emblazoned on the aircraft.

"Ah, it's the Americans? Forget it then."

Ye Xiao turned and left.

The Martial Dao knows no borders, but martial artists have nationalities.

The Americans bully people everywhere, yet they still preach about benevolence and righteousness - that's just plain idiotic.

Ye Xiao decided not to try it for now. The leap in his realm of cultivation was too great, and he wasn't quite ready to perfectly control it yet. Otherwise, the gap between the Divine Sect and the Grand Masters would be far too small.

He was worried that if he cultivated for too long, Aier Wang might break free on the ground.

Judging from the martial artists in those few cities, they definitely couldn't defeat him.

If that happened, Aier Wang would wreak havoc on the city Ye Xiao was staying in, and that wouldn't be good.

It's better to go back first.

Meanwhile, on the ground, Aier Wang's several avatar manifestations, after being freed from the seal, swiftly converged towards the direction of Jianghai City, as if by prior arrangement.

Several beams of light collided in the sky above Jianghai City, and in an instant, a dazzling radiance erupted, illuminating the night sky of Jianghai City as bright as day.

The crowd couldn't help but close their eyes.

After a while, the people slowly opened their eyes, but still dared not look directly at the brilliant light in the sky, only peeking through their fingers.

"What is that thing?"

"It's so dazzling, ah! My eyes!"

"I'm going blind!"

The citizens had not yet realized the significance of that beam of light.

Only a few people had a grave expression, sensing a terrifying aura of death.

Among them, the feeling was most acute for Du Changfeng and the others.

At this moment, Du Changfeng, Qin Shenglong, and the other powerful martial artists of Jianghai City had gathered together, looking at the scorching white radiance in the sky, unable to suppress their fear and bewilderment.

"What is this exactly?" Nangong Lin couldn't help but ask.

Du Changfeng spoke with a grave expression:

"If I'm not mistaken, this should be Aier Wang."

"What did you say?"

The others heard this and couldn't help but shudder, their pupils involuntarily constricting.

Although they had never fought Aier Wang directly, his name had haunted the nightmares of many of them throughout their childhoods.

"How is this possible? Wasn't Aier Wang sealed? How did he break the seal?"

Du Changfeng took a deep breath.

"He was indeed sealed. But you haven't forgotten that Aier Wang's map was stolen by Yang Zhengyi from the library before.

He even released one of Aier Wang's avatars.

Although that time, one of our senior predecessors in Jianghai City had slain it, Aier Wang's map was never recovered.

And now, with the Seven Stars Converging, if someone intentionally unsealed him, it would make sense."

As soon as he finished speaking, several figures quickly approached from the distance.

Each one was at least at the Sect Master level, and the strongest even reached the Grand Master realm.

"Professor Du, Dean Qin, it's been a while."

The crowd greeted them from afar, and Du Changfeng immediately spoke:

"Brother Zhang, Brother Liu, you two are Grand Masters of your respective cities. Why aren't you guarding your own cities, but have come to Jianghai City instead?"

The crowd exchanged glances and quickly replied:

"The star beasts in our cities have all been slain by a high-level swordsman. There should be news about this on the forums."

"Is that so? I was so preoccupied that I didn't have time to check the news. Anyway, since you've come, is it because of this?"

He pointed to the radiant sphere in the sky, and the crowd nodded solemnly.

"Is that really Aier Wang?" Nangong Lin couldn't help but ask anxiously, and the crowd gravely nodded in confirmation.

"It is Aier Wang! After that senior predecessor slew the star beasts, Aier Wang used the fresh blood to recover much of his power and seized the opportunity to break the seal.

We originally wanted to stop him from fusing his avatars, but it seems we were too late."

Hearing the confirmation, Nangong Lin and the others could no longer hold back, their foreheads beading with cold sweat.

Aier Wang! So it was really him! If that's the case, the entire Jianghai City, with its population of seven to eight million, might be doomed without a trace.

They still remembered that fifty-six years ago, Aier Wang led an army of a million star beasts, invading the cities of Jianghai, Wanghai, Jiangdong, Lingnan, and Jiangxia. The number of participants exceeded ten million.

Just from that single battle, the casualties among the human martial artists were staggering.
