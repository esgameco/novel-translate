Ye Xiao had already gathered three martial arts: the Great Extinction Fist, the Enlightened Emperor Fist, and the Thunder Emperor Fist.

He would directly initiate the fusion, and it should be completed in less than two days.

Ye Xiao's divine blood being fully activated and his true divine physique would also change the magnetic field of the earth, bringing a special transformation to the entire Nine Realms.

At this time, the Nine Realms had truly become an ocean of joy for martial artists!

Beitang Ce's heart trembled, as he knew the implications behind Elder White Tiger's words.

He clenched his fists, his gaze fixed intently on the mountain in front of him.

This time, bringing Ye Xiao here was truly the right decision!

"Shuishui, can you still hold on?"
