In the following days, Jianghai City gradually returned to peace.

Aside from the group discussing the divine king's tomb after meals, everything went back to normal.

But no one noticed that a dark and mysterious power was quietly rising in Jianghai City.

Four days later, Ye Xiao went to work and clearly heard his colleagues discussing some strange things.

"Have you heard? Recently, a number of people have mysteriously disappeared in Jianghai City."

"Of course I've heard about it. My uncle who works in the defense squad specifically told me not to go out at night these days, it's best to stay at home obediently.

Apparently, these dead people were mostly those who came back very late at night. No one knew when they died, they were only found the next day.

I heard that there's not a single trace of injury on them, but their fresh blood and souls have all been drained, not a drop left."

"That's too eerie, isn't it? I don't know if it's the work of those beast spirits or someone cultivating evil methods."

"Who knows? Anyway, it's best to stay at home at night."

Hearing these words, Ye Xiao couldn't help but feel puzzled.

If he remembered correctly, a few days ago, Gu Hai and the others had mentioned that there seemed to be some special situation around Jianghai City, and some poultry had been killed.

Now there's this incident of people dying, could it be zombies?

But that's impossible, right? This world has never heard of such things.

However, there are half-beast blood vampires that feed on human blood! Could it be something similar to that?

Anyway, with the Xuanyuan Clan around, there shouldn't be any big problems.

The other party is obviously not strong, otherwise they wouldn't be hiding and sneakily killing people, but would come out openly to harm people.

At this time, his body technique talisman had just been completed and cultivated.

This was Ye Xiao's fourth ancient talisman, taking a slightly longer time than the previous one, perhaps because the fusion time varies for different techniques.

But overall, it was much faster than before.

The soaring talisman also has its own unique attributes, and Ye Xiao feels that it has already surpassed the realm of body techniques.

Whether it's the Yin-Yang Escape or the Spatial Divine Escape, theoretically they are both dragging one's own body to another place.

But the soaring talisman can already drag other existences to one's own presence.

This is similar to the claw technique in a way.

But it's stronger than the claw technique.

The claw technique relies on the release of one's own powerful force to achieve long-range control of the opponent or pull them to oneself.

But it is still a technique, and has not reached the level of transcending the rules of techniques. As long as the opponent's strength is stronger than oneself, or their technique is somewhat peculiar, they may still be able to break free from one's grasp.

However, the soaring talisman is different, it directly transfers the space to bring the opponent to oneself.

In terms of body techniques, it has also reached the point where it is no longer about using techniques to increase one's own speed, but has transcended that.
