On Ye Xiao's side, after cultivating in the formation, he had perfectly solved the problem of his spiritual energy supply. The remaining time was spent entirely on reading books.

Ye Xiao had finally synthesized the last and strongest imperial art in his physical attack repertoire - the Warrior God's Powerful Kick! Its power surpassed even the Great Annihilation Fist and the Infinite Calamity Finger. Although the Great Annihilation Fist and Infinite Calamity Finger were also single-target attacks, they were mainly focused on instantaneous release. The Warrior God's Powerful Kick, on the other hand, could be charged up, allowing the attack power to exceed one's cultivation level.

This was similar in principle to the Emperor's Extreme Sword Technique.

This move should be considered one of the two final imperial arts Ye Xiao had synthesized.

The other one was an eye technique.

Ye Xiao's cultivation methods could now be divided into two main categories:

One was the auxiliary and enhancement type that used weapons like swords and knives as the attack method.

He had only cultivated two of these - a sword technique and a knife technique.

The other category was the one that used the body, soul, and spirit as the attack method.

This included fist techniques, finger techniques, leg techniques, claw techniques, spiritual techniques, and eye techniques.

Since he had refined his body and spirit to the extreme, if he did not learn these techniques based on the body and spirit as the foundation, it would be a waste.

Moreover, Ye Xiao was considering a problem - after synthesizing techniques above the Slaughter God Art, it might no longer be possible to fuse similar techniques.

In that case, would it be possible to fuse the sword and knife techniques, as well as fuse the fist, leg, finger, and Diamond Indestructible Divine Skill-type techniques focused on body refinement?

It was necessary to make plans for the future in advance. Otherwise, if he had to come back and practice these basic techniques later, that would be torture.

After mastering the final eye technique, Ye Xiao's focus would no longer be on these basic cultivation methods, but on fully refining the imperial arts at the Patriarch level to synthesize the Slaughter God Art!

After continuous cultivation over several days, Ye Xiao finally synthesized the second Slaughter God Art-level cultivation method - the Eternal Life Sutra, a healing technique.

However, when Ye Xiao saw the "Healing God Art" mark in the lower right corner of the record of the cultivation method, he couldn't help but be puzzled.

This thing is called the Healing God Art?

Does that mean it's a healing technique for Healing God Realm cultivators?

The battle technique is called the Slaughter God Art, and the healing technique is called the Healing God Art. Couldn't they just call it the God Art and be done with it? Why come up with so many fancy names, as if trying to show off, when in the end it just falls into the clich√©.

Oh well, let them name it however they want. The cultivation method is still very powerful, so Ye Xiao should try out the Eternal Life Sutra and see how strong it is.

Ye Xiao wouldn't find some low-level cultivators to experiment on, because with their low cultivation, the healing effect would naturally be better, and the experimental data would not be very meaningful for his actual combat effectiveness.

So Ye Xiao decided to use his own body to experiment.
