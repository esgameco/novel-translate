The thunderous roars echoed from the gigantic hurricane channel in the sky above. Flashes of lightning and rumbles of thunder reverberated, caused by the rapid rotation of the clouds, resulting in frictional discharge.

Reaching the realm of the Supernal Divine, if one were to emerge, the world would be transformed. Even without taking action, the sheer power radiating from one's being would be akin to a towering mountain, capable of stirring the spiritual energies of heaven and earth, causing them to tremble.

Ye Xiao stood with his hands clasped behind his back, gazing up at the sky. Since attaining the Emperor Realm, he had never engaged in combat with those of the realms above. If not for the sudden outbreak of the Beast Calamity, he might have sparred with the Sword Emperor, Azure Dragon.

Alas, with the arrival of the Beast Calamity, he could not practice beforehand and had to forgo the opportunity. During the battles in Jianghai City and here, he had not encountered any existence above the Emperor Realm. The mightiest star beasts he had faced were at most Half-Step Supernal Divine, their strength merely comparable to the Half-Step Emperor Realm of the human race, completely unable to compel him to unleash his full power. Such childishly simple battles held no challenge for him; he desired more thrilling confrontations to further test the limits of his own strength, to reach the true Supernal Divine Realm.

Accompanied by flashes of lightning and rumbles of thunder, a massive Sovereign Dragon-Lion descended from the hurricane. Its entire body emanated a faint golden glow, for its spiritual energy had reached the pinnacle, becoming so dense that it manifested as an aura.

The Sovereign Dragon-Lion stepped out from the hurricane, its towering figure exceeding eight meters in length, like a small mountain. As it fully emerged, the hurricane gradually dissipated, for the transfer portal was no longer affected by its presence. In that moment, it shone as brilliantly as the sun high in the heavens.

Gazing at Ye Xiao, its eyes gleamed with the intensity of swords, exuding a chill and battle intent.

"In centuries, who would have thought the human race would produce another Emperor Realm expert! Truly an eye-opening sight."

Ye Xiao remained silent, and the Sovereign Dragon-Lion continued, "If you had broken through to the Emperor Realm sooner, perhaps you could have been of some help to the human race. Alas, your breakthrough now comes too late; you cannot keep up with the times. The human race is doomed, and nothing can stop it."

Ye Xiao finally spoke his first words, "As long as I live, the human race will not perish!"

These simple words were imbued with Ye Xiao's hope and confidence in the people of the Nine Provinces. The Sovereign Dragon-Lion's gaze remained indifferent, unmoved by such "human sentiments." The star beasts revered only the strong and the victorious, the harsh realities of the world.

With a slight shift in its eyes, the Sovereign Dragon-Lion began to move, its speed appearing languid, yet Ye Xiao knew better than to underestimate it. In the blink of an eye, the Sovereign Dragon-Lion had already expanded its domain, and seven powerful forces struck at Ye Xiao from different directions.

Fortunately, Ye Xiao's speed was not inferior, and he swiftly evoked the Yin-Yang Escape Art, easily evading the attack. The spot where he had stood moments ago erupted in a terrifying implosive explosion, a unique technique of the Sovereign Dragon-Lion. Had the attack landed, it would have inflicted severe damage. But for Ye Xiao, this pose no significant threat.

The Sovereign Dragon-Lion glanced at Ye Xiao and once more unleashed its might from its breath.
