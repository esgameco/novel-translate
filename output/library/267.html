Huan Liuli curiously examined Beitang Ce.

"This is... a Xiantianseven-grade Shenzong-level spirit beast? Mr. Ye, you actually happen to be a beast tamer as well?"

Beitang Ce couldn't help but sigh in admiration:

"Mr. Ye is truly remarkable, to even be able to get a Xiantian seven-grade Shenzong spirit beast to serve as a slave.

This level of ability truly impresses this old man."

Although he had already reached Xiantian nine-grade cultivation, and could easily defeat a Xiantian seven-grade spirit beast, even to the point of killing it in an instant, it was extremely difficult for him to get such a high-level spirit beast to serve under him.

As the saying goes, the wild is hard to tame, and spirit beasts of their level are all proud and arrogant, absolutely unwilling to obey your commands just because your cultivation is higher.

Unless you captured them when they were young and gradually trained them up.

But that would take an extremely long time and cost, which is not very worthwhile.

After all, even for dragon-level bloodlines, it would take several hundred years, or even close to a thousand years, to rise from having no cultivation at all in childhood to reaching the Shenzong realm.

"Mr. Ye, do you have any miraculous beast taming techniques? Could you also introduce them to this old man?"

Ye Xiao thought for a moment.

"Hmm, just beat them to death, that's all."

Beitang Ce raised his eyebrows slightly.

"Is it really that simple? But why? When this old man tried beating the spirit beasts he found, it didn't have this effect at all? The mentality of Shenzong-grade spirit beasts has already been trained to an extremely powerful degree, they won't fear being beaten or death."

"Then find spirit beasts with slightly lower cultivation, when I took her in, she was only at the Xiantian one-grade Zongshi realm spirit beast level."

Hearing this, Beitang Ce's face couldn't help but twitch violently.

Ye Xiao took in Huan Liuli when she was only at the Xiantian one-grade cultivation level?

What kind of joke is this? By that logic, Ye Xiao should only be a little over 20 years old, and even if Huan Liuli cultivated diligently, she could at most have only cultivated for 20 years, and would be far from reaching this level, because Ye Xiao had just said that he beat Huan Liuli into submission.

That means Ye Xiao's cultivation had already broken through to the Xiantian one-grade Zongshi realm at that time.

So, calculated this way, Huan Liuli could have only cultivated for a little over 10 years.

From Xiantian one-grade to Xiantian seven-grade Shenzong in just over 10 years? Crossing six realms in one go? This has to be a joke, right? Not even riding a rocket could one ascend that quickly.

However, when he looked at Ye Xiao, he suddenly felt he could understand it a bit.

With such a monstrous master, cultivating such a monstrous spirit pet, is that not normal?

Afterwards, he shook his head and followed Ye Xiao into the courtyard.

"Forget it, this old man is still not quite suited for raising spirit beasts."

However, as soon as he entered the courtyard, he was attracted by a strange feeling.

"Huh? What's this?"

His gaze fell on something in the corner of the courtyard that looked like a potted plant.

"I didn't expect Mr. Ye to also have the leisure and elegance to grow potted plants, and even carve a spiritual gathering array on it."

Ye Xiao glanced at it.

"That's not a potted plant, but I've used a defensive array, so you can't see the wonders hidden inside it."

Ye Xiao snapped his fingers, dispelling the defensive array, and in the next moment, the entire small space revealed its true appearance.

"My goodness!"

Beitang Ce immediately widened his eyes, his jaw almost dropping to the ground in shock.

"This, this... Mr. Ye, you actually created a small mountain with spatial power in your own little courtyard to cultivate heavenly materials and earthly treasures?

My goodness! The abundance of heavenly materials and earthly treasures here, how much must there be? Your scale is simply too grand!"

It's no wonder Beitang Ce was so shocked. Who could have imagined that this seemingly small flower pot actually had such a different world hidden inside, filled with so many precious treasures?

These heavenly materials and earthly treasures, not only are they not easily obtainable for ordinary people, even if it was a large company, they wouldn't be able to collect as comprehensively and as well as Ye Xiao.

Incredible!

Moreover, the pot that cultivates these heavenly materials and earthly treasures has already been refined into a treasure imbued with spatial power.

On its surface, an array has also been inscribed.

Just this one pot alone can already be considered priceless.

It would require the joint efforts of several top-tier forgers and array masters, working together for half a month, to create something like this.

Yet Ye Xiao casually placed it on the ground like a broken flower pot.

This is simply too extravagant!

"Mr. Ye, today you have truly opened the eyes of this old man. This old man is completely amazed..."
