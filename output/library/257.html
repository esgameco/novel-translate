On Ye Xiao's side, there was not much commotion.

Qing Niu and Feng Long, when they had just arrived, he had already sensed their presence.

His current strength was at the King Realm level, and with just a single thought, he could easily cover the entire Jianghai City.

In the past, he could also instantly cover the entire Jianghai City, but at that time, Jianghai City had not yet expanded.

Now, Jianghai City was about five times larger than before.

Moreover, a large number of people had flooded in.

The surrounding several cities, like Wanghai and Jiangdong, had all migrated their populations here.

For him to cover the entire Jianghai City with just a single thought now, was not the same as before when he could cover the original Jianghai City.

It was precisely because of this that he had long known that Jiang Chen, the dean, would make a move.

He could even see it as if it were right in front of him.

Anything that happened in any corner of Jianghai City could not escape his eyes.

It would not be an exaggeration to say that as long as he wanted to know, he could instantly know what every single person in Jianghai City was doing at any given moment.

Therefore, he did not intervene.

Later, although Jiang Chen was a bit casual and did not attack with full force, causing Feng Long to escape by using the Blood Explosion Technique, and Qing Niu to betray Feng Long and flee, Ye Xiao did not stop them.

Because he could also monitor that Feng Long had entered his breeding ground, and as long as he entered the breeding ground, no matter how powerful he was, he would not be able to escape from it.

The defensive formation he had set up was one-way, not two-way, otherwise it would completely isolate the spiritual energy.

The formation needed to constantly absorb spiritual energy to function.

The principle of the Blood Explosion Technique was probably to transform one's own body elements into a substance similar to spiritual energy, and then escape.

That was why Feng Long accidentally stumbled into the breeding ground.

But since the defensive formation was one-way, and it was to prevent the star beasts inside from escaping, when Feng Long tried to use the Blood Explosion Technique again, he naturally could not escape.

As for Qing Niu, it was even simpler.

Ye Xiao had already sensed that the path of Qing Niu's escape was blocked by Dao Shen Li Liushui.

As long as Li Liushui was guarding there, unless Qing Niu's strength had reached the true Deity Beast realm, he would not be able to defeat Li Liushui and escape.

At this moment, Huan Liuli suddenly opened the door, excitedly shouting, "Master, master, come and take a look. I've got something good! A star beast at the God Ancestor level, and it's a bull! Haha, from now on we can have beef, we can have hot pot, and we can even have steak!"

Huan Liuli excitedly brought the unconscious Qing Niu in front of Ye Xiao.

Ye Xiao looked at the muscular bull in front of him and nodded in satisfaction.

In fact, he had encountered a Qing Niu before, the Thunder Qing Niu at the bottom of the Tiemo Cliff.

However, that Qing Niu was not as powerful as the one in front of him now.

Moreover, that Qing Niu had been corroded by the evil energy at the Tiemo Cliff, and could no longer be used for meat.

Ye Xiao had only been able to refine it into a medicinal pill at the time.

Now he had harvested another Qing Niu! And its strength was even greater than the previous one, truly an unexpected and pleasant surprise.

"You've done well."

"Thank you, master, meow~!" Huan Liuli was extremely excited, her little tail wagging rapidly.

"I'll go and put him in the breeding ground and lock him up. You can take a break from the breeding ground today."

"Okay, master."

Ye Xiao nodded, patted her little head, and then, holding the Qing Niu, used the Earth Traversal Technique to instantly arrive at the breeding ground.

Huan Liuli's little head was burning with excitement.

Ah, ah, ah, the master just now, patted her little head.

Ah, ah, ah, it felt so good!

Ye Xiao didn't think much of it, he just casually stroked the little head like one would normally do to a pet.

At this moment, he suddenly appeared in the breeding ground.

The next second, the star beasts quickly gathered around him.

"Master, you're here."

"Master, did you have a good day?"

"Master, are you thirsty? The fresh milk we just milked is still warm."

Seeing the star beasts acting like loyal dogs, Feng Long was completely dumbfounded.

What was this situation? What were these creatures doing? Didn't they have any self-respect at all? They were all star beasts, and even God Ancestor level ones!

Such powerful star beasts, actually acting like a bunch of loyal dogs, licking a human? If their ancestors saw this, they would probably crawl out of their graves and scold them furiously!

But this person was not the one who had defeated him! Who was he?

With these doubts in mind, Feng Long coldly said, "Hey, who are you?"

Ye Xiao glanced at him and threw the Qing Niu next to him.

"Damn it, this bastard!"

Seeing the Qing Niu, Feng Long was so angry that he almost vomited blood.

If it wasn't for this idiot suddenly attacking him from behind, he wouldn't have ended up in this situation.

He might have already escaped, opened the teleportation array, and returned to the Starry Skies.

But anger aside, his mind was still relatively clear now.

The biggest problem was not the Qing Niu, but Ye Xiao!

This person was actually able to capture the escaped Qing Niu, his strength was not ordinary!

Ye Xiao first healed the Qing Niu, and seeing Ye Xiao's powerful healing techniques, Feng Long's pupils couldn't help but shrink violently.

What a terrifying skill, to be able to instantly heal a severely injured demigod beast like this.

"Livestock? Breeding farm? Feng Long, you've been captured too? And Samuels, Susan? You're all here as well?"

The next moment, his gaze immediately fell on Ye Xiao.

"Was it you who captured us?"

It was at this moment that he realized how terrifying this Ye Xiao was! No wonder these star beasts, Samuels and Susan, were all so proud, yet agreed to be livestock here.
