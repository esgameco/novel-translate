"Quick, look! The Talent Pillar below has lit up another layer, no, not just one layer, but ten layers. No, it's still rising, it's getting higher and higher, it's already exceeded a hundred layers!

It's still rising!"

Everyone was stunned and immediately looked down below.

Exceeding a hundred layers meant that the human race of the Nine Realms would cultivate an existence at the Slay God Realm level.

"Brother Ning, it seems your family's luck is quite good."

Ning Zhiyuan nodded.

This time, he did not remain silent. Even for the mighty Ning family, a Slay God Realm expert could be considered a rare talent.

However, the Talent Pillar did not stop there. It continued to climb to even higher layers.

One hundred and ten layers, one hundred and twenty layers, one hundred and thirty layers, in the blink of an eye, it had broken through two hundred layers.

"It's broken through two hundred layers!"

The crowd couldn't help but exclaim in surprise. This time, they could no longer remain unmoved.

Because the one who could break through this level was far superior to their own aptitude.

"Brother Ning, your Ning family is really going to make a big fortune this time."

Ling Shaoyu couldn't help but exclaim in surprise.

And Song Qingxue's face turned solemn as she said, "No, it's still rising, it hasn't ended yet."

Two hundred and ten layers, two hundred and twenty layers, two hundred and thirty layers, two hundred and forty layers, two hundred and fifty layers, the hearts of the crowd began to beat faster.

This level had already surpassed them, and surpassed all of them.

In other words, there would be a genius below who was more powerful than the young masters of the Xuanyuan Clan's main branch families, the most outstanding talents among the major orthodox families.

Two hundred and sixty, two hundred and seventy, two hundred and eighty, two hundred and ninety, three hundred! "It, it's broken through three hundred."

Song Qingxue swallowed hard, her voice starting to tremble.

But the Talent Pillar did not stop there. Its brilliance continued to rise further.

Three hundred and one, three hundred and two, in the blink of an eye, it had broken through four hundred.

"It's already broken through four hundred!"

Song Qingxue spoke softly, her face already starting to numb.

Then the crowd watched as the Talent Pillar lit up layer by layer.

Five hundred!

Six hundred!

Seven hundred!

Eight hundred!

Nine hundred!

Boom! When its power reached its limit, it continued to emit even stronger light! Finally, it seemed to be unable to withstand the other's talent and completely exploded.

A thunderous roar rose from the ground, a mushroom cloud shooting into the sky, and a massive shockwave spread out in all directions, causing countless trees to fall.

"What's happening?"

"What's going on? Why did we suddenly hear an explosion?"

"Could it be that another Clan has invaded the mortal world?"

The warriors on the ground, as well as the powerful experts from the major Xuanyuan Clans stationed here, couldn't help but fly out of their rooms and look towards that direction.

In the sky above, the young masters of the major Xuanyuan Clan branches were completely in disarray.

Nine hundred layers fully lit up, a level like this, even the Xuanyuan Clan's holy sons might not necessarily be able to reach.

It should only be the top-tier existences in the starry sky who could achieve this.

To put it rather arrogantly, if this person's existence is real, he may very well bring the entire Xuanyuan Clan to an unprecedented new height.

He may even change the current order of the starry sky.

This made them find it hard to believe their own eyes, feeling as if they were dreaming or under an illusion.

Otherwise, how could such a strange thing happen? This is just a small human world where the descendants of the Xuanyuan Clan reside, a tiny world.

How could there be such a top-tier genius in this tiny place? What a joke! How could such an existence appear here?

"Well, maybe it's because the Talent Pillar has been activated for too long, so it's malfunctioned and caused this erroneous judgment."

Ling Shaoyu couldn't help but speak up, and the others quickly nodded and echoed in agreement.

"Yes, yes, you're absolutely right."

"I also think that's the case. Ling Junior, don't you still have many Talent Pillars? Why don't you take out a few more and try them?"

"Okay!"

Ling Shaoyu was quick to act, and he soon took out another Talent Pillar and tossed it to the ground.
