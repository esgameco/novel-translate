This black-scaled snake was very large, over 15 meters long and thicker than the slender waist of a dancing girl.

Looking closely, there were faint bumps on its head that resembled small horns.

The legend goes that a snake that cultivates for a thousand years can become a python, a python that cultivates for a thousand years can become a flood dragon, and a flood dragon that cultivates for a thousand years can transcend tribulation and become a true dragon.

This creature's age was estimated to be just over a thousand years old.

If it were on Earth, it would definitely be a top-tier protected animal.

"Its cultivation is at the early Xiantian Third Realm, just a hair's breadth away from reaching the Xiantian Fourth Realm. What if we first revive it, then use spiritual energy to help it cross that final threshold? Then..."

Saying so, he acted on it.

After all, finding a Xiantian Fourth Realm spirit beast is extremely rare, and the armor forged from its skin would surpass that of the Xiantian Third Realm.

The Chenxing Technique was activated, a flash of golden light, and the black-scaled snake's body began to emanate a golden glow.

It was actually evolving during the revival process.

How convenient.

What surprised Ye Xiao even more was that as the black-scaled snake was evolving, it shed its previous skin.

This meant he would obtain two snake skins - one from the Xiantian Third Realm black-scaled snake, and one from the Xiantian Fourth Realm black-scaled snake.

This was a great plan - in the future, when killing spirit beasts, he could first catalyze their evolution, then slay them!

No, wait, if he could keep peeling its skin and then use healing techniques to help it recover, he could obtain multiple snake skins! Perfect!

The black-scaled snake quickly revived, its consciousness emerging from the haze.

"I remember being slain by a human's attack, how am I revived? And my cultivation has even advanced to the Xiantian Fourth Realm! Could it be that I obtained a breakthrough at the brink of death?"

The snake-like master spirit beast could speak the human tongue, which was nothing extraordinary.

As it was laughing, the black-scaled snake suddenly noticed Ye Xiao standing beside it.

An extremely audacious human insect daring to stand by its side.

Nine times out of ten, this was a human cultivator sent to guard its corpse.

This was a blatant provocation of the dignity of a master spirit beast.

In an instant, the black-scaled snake was consumed by rage!

"Damned human, I'll—!"

Seeing Ye Xiao's direction shift in its eyes, and the tearing pain at its abdomen, the snake's massive head fell into confusion.

As it plummeted to the ground with a thud, the intense pain brought it to full awareness.

No, no! This person was not the human cultivator guarding it, but the one who had slain it! In that moment, a sense of immense despair filled the black-scaled snake's heart.

That sword strike was of unparalleled elegance, something it had never witnessed in its entire existence.

The sword techniques of this human cultivator were shockingly powerful! And it had fallen into his hands - what a tragedy!

A profound sense of hopeless sorrow welled up within it.

To think that it had just advanced to the Xiantian Fourth Realm, only to encounter such a formidable opponent, ultimately being slain by his sword. If Lord Aer were still around, it would not have suffered such a humiliating defeat.

The spirit beast's robust life force prevented it from dying instantly, but this only made its suffering more agonizing.

For it watched as this human began to peel off its skin.

Undoubtedly, the human would use its skin to forge armor, to protect himself against other spirit beasts.

What a disgrace!

But alas, this was the way of the world - the strong devour the weak.
