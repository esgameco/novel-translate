High up in the sky, after Ye Xiao made a move with his palm, he did not let his guard down. He just raised his head and looked in another direction.

There, the star beast from earlier was standing, but he was slightly panting, obviously having used a secret technique to instantly boost his speed to the limit.

And he had managed to avoid Ye Xiao's attack.

After taking a couple of deep breaths, his expression became calm again. But his eyes no longer held the same disdainful look as before, replaced instead by an extremely cautious gaze.

"Wow, I underestimated you. I didn't expect you to be a Saint Sovereign of the Ninth Rank as well! No wonder Su Shan and the others were killed! You must have encountered them in Jianghai City, you brat!"

Ye Xiao narrowed his eyes slightly.

The other party had mentioned Su Shan, which meant he was one of Tiger God's people? And he was also a Ninth Rank star beast?! At this moment, Ye Xiao suddenly felt fond of Tiger God.

He had been worrying about where to find a Ninth Rank star beast to raise on his farm.

Unexpectedly, the other had delivered one right to him.

The corners of Ye Xiao's mouth curled up slightly as he launched another attack.

The Infinite Calamity Finger.

The Infinite Calamity Finger was characterized by its swiftness and strength.

It was several times faster than the Tathagata Palm.

Boom! The next moment, the Infinite Calamity Finger exploded at the other's location.

But once again, he had managed to escape.

"Give up, human. You don't know the name of this general. I am Saimuel, the Wind God under Tiger God. Speed is my greatest specialty.

Your attacks may be powerful, but unfortunately, they are no match for my speed, like a child's game of house against me.

Your attacks can't even keep up with my body.

Trying to injure me? Haha, you're just wasting your effort."

However, the moment he finished speaking, Ye Xiao suddenly arrived beside him with lightning-fast speed.

"So fast!"

Saimuel's pupils constricted.

He simply couldn't believe his eyes.

Ye Xiao's speed was almost on par with his own signature movement technique?

But now he dared not be the slightest bit careless, for Ye Xiao's fist was already thundering towards him.

The Great Extinction Fist!

The terrifying fist power instantly drained all the spiritual energy around, leaving only a vacuum.

A thick aura of death shrouded the iron fist.

Saimuel was very clear-headed - he knew that with his cultivation and strength, he could not withstand this punch.

If he fought Ye Xiao head-on, he would undoubtedly lose.

Even more terrifying was that the other's speed was even faster than his own. If they both sustained injuries, the next moment could very well be their death.

His mind was very sharp, his thought processes reaching an extreme speed.

In an instant, he had already come up with a plan.

The next moment, he swung out a fist to meet Ye Xiao's.

Although they were both Ninth Rank Celestial Sovereigns, his fist appeared so pale and powerless before Ye Xiao's.

The moment they collided, his fist exploded into a bloody mist, just like tofu.

A flash of pain crossed his face, but it quickly vanished.

It was replaced by a deep excitement.

The next second, he had appeared hundreds of miles away.

"Human, you're ruthless! But don't be too proud of yourself. When the day of the Calamity of the Beasts arrives, I, Saimuel, will have Tiger God himself come to Jianghai City to destroy you.

I hope you don't commit suicide out of fear by then!"

Ye Xiao raised his eyebrows slightly.

It turned out that Saimuel had deliberately collided fists with him just now in order to unleash the Blood Explosion Technique.

Ye Xiao had read before that the essence blood was the most precious thing in the bodies of humans and star beasts.

When cultivating, refining the body, everyone's essence blood and flesh would be refined by spiritual energy over and over.

After countless refinements, it would contain a considerable amount of spiritual energy.

So in battle, if one felt they couldn't endure, they could choose to detonate their own essence blood, thereby obtaining a large amount of spiritual energy to ensure their technique instantly exceeded their normal power.

He didn't expect the other party to have this kind of technique as well.

At this time, Saimuel was already preparing to leave, but how could Ye Xiao let him go?

The fat meat had come to his doorstep, would he not eat it?

With a wave of the Plucking Star Hand, Saimuel's body was suddenly fixed in place.

He stiffened, revealing a trace of fear and shock.

Ye Xiao actually had a long-range control attack technique as well?

Just how many techniques had this human cultivated? How could he be so monstrous in combat skills, speed, and now even long-range control?

"How is this possible? How can this be? Release me!"

He roared furiously, gathering all his strength to try and break free from the restraint.

But unfortunately, Ye Xiao's spiritual energy was who knows how many levels higher than his own. No matter how he tried to enhance his own power, he could not budge Ye Xiao's power at all.

Ye Xiao's open right hand clenched slightly.

"Crack! Crack!"

"Ahhhhh, no—!"

Saimuel let out a scream of agony as Ye Xiao crushed him in an instant.
