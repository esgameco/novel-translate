"Do you think I would care about these things? I am a noble beast spirit. I am a god-level existence! No matter what you use to tempt me, I will absolutely never become your livestock! Ye Xiao could see that Tiger Shenzhen's gaze was filled with unwavering determination. He was not like the other beast spirits, his inner heart was truly unyielding.

Using torture methods would definitely not be able to tame him.

"Was everything you just said true?"

"Of course it's true."

"That's right."
