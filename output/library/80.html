Early the next morning, Ye Xiao packed up the Illusion Jade.

From now on, he had to keep the Illusion Jade with him at all times.

The day of the Seven Stars Convergence was approaching, and the Illusion Jade could only be suppressed by his spiritual power when it was by his side.

Otherwise, there was no guarantee that it wouldn't go out and do something dangerous.

After finishing breakfast, he came to the library, clocked in for work, and the other department managers had already started issuing tasks and giving morning briefings, but everything was calm on Ye Xiao's end.

He didn't like giving briefings or being high and mighty.

Power and authority, if you only use them when you feel like it, that's just you playing with power.

If you can't bear to let go of it at any moment, then it's power that's playing with you.

Staying calm and peaceful, that's the best way to live.

The administrators under Ye Xiao were also very capable. When they arrived, they would proactively mop the floors, wipe the tables, and take care of the basic cleaning, then check if any books were damaged and needed to be replaced.

As for Ye Xiao, he would calmly read books, continuing to search for the last foundational cultivation technique in his body refining arts.

Before long, a junior administrator came over and couldn't help but ask curiously:

"Manager, is Ning Yuhen on leave today? Why hasn't he come?"

Ye Xiao was a bit taken aback.

"He hasn't come?"

"Yes, he hasn't shown up all morning."

Ye Xiao raised his brow slightly. This Ning Yuhen was usually very diligent and disciplined at work, so what happened today that he skipped work?

"I see, don't mention this to anyone, so his wages won't be deducted."

"Okay!"

Ye Xiao was good to the people under him. Whenever someone was late or left early, he never recorded it, so his words carried a lot of weight.

After the other person left, Ye Xiao immediately activated the Taixuan Tiangong, and in an instant, he found that Ning Yuhen was lying in a daze on his bed in the dormitory, with a slightly elevated body temperature.

Could this kid also be affected by the Seven Stars Convergence, with his star beast bloodline activated, causing him to go berserk?

He wouldn't go crazy and run out to indiscriminately kill people, would he?

With a slight movement of his mind, Ye Xiao activated the Shenyi Jue and instantly appeared outside Ning Yuhen's door.

He knocked on the door, and Ning Yuhen's hazy voice came from inside.

"Who is it?"

"It's me."

Hearing Ye Xiao's voice, Ning Yuhen mustered the strength to say, "Come in, the door isn't locked."

Ye Xiao pushed open the door, his expression momentarily blank.

In that instant, he had sensed a very familiar feeling.

However, he quickly regained his composure and walked in.

"What's going on? Sleeping in the middle of the day?"

Ning Yuhen hugged the blanket, curled up in the bed, shivering.

"I don't know, last night when I came back, I felt a bit cold, like I had a fever. But the healing technique I used didn't work."

Ye Xiao knew that Ning Yuhen didn't dare go to the hospital, afraid that his star beast bloodline would be discovered.

However, his own silver fox bloodline came with a powerful healing technique, not much inferior to the doctors in the hospital.

How could he not even be able to cure a simple cold and fever? And combined with that familiar feeling just now.

"Maybe your healing technique isn't suitable. I've been studying some other healing techniques recently, let me take a look."

Healing techniques were something anyone could cultivate, with low requirements, so Ye Xiao didn't need to worry about revealing anything in front of Ning Yuhen.

"Okay! Thank you, Manager."

Ye Xiao reached out and placed his hand on Ning Yuhen's wrist.

Because it was a spiritual power explosion, only their spiritual sea would feel it, and no one else would be able to hear it.
