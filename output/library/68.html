The other person's words were full of a teasing tone, but Ning Yuthen felt as if he had fallen into an icy pit, his whole body breaking out in cold sweat.

He was truly at a dead end, with no way out.

Ning Yuthen gritted his teeth, thinking that rather than being tortured to death by the two of them, he might as well end it himself.

Thinking this, he immediately raised his hand, preparing to commit suicide.

But just as he raised his hand, the Azure Wolf casually waved his hand, a beam of light directly blasting off Ning Yuthen's arm.

"Ahhh!"

This excruciating pain caused Ning Yuthen to immediately faint.

"Tsk tsk, Azure Wolf, you're too rough. Little Ning is still a junior after all, with such thin arms and legs, you really don't know how to be gentle at all."

The Azure Wolf's face was icy cold.

"Shut up and hurry up and scan his memories, find that bastard who killed Basan and Meg."

"Alright, alright~."

The Charm Fox Master feigned helplessness, spreading her hands, then immediately unleashed her clan's signature technique, her spiritual energy surging into Ning Yuthen's body like a tidal wave.

"???"

Just as her spiritual energy entered Ning Yuthen's mind, she suddenly paused, her expression changing.

Because within Ning Yuthen's spiritual sea, there now stood a young figure.

This figure was formed from condensed spiritual energy, standing there with his hands behind his back, his eyes coldly regarding her, as if he had been waiting for her arrival all along.

"Are you the mastermind behind the killings of Basan and Meg?"

Ye Xiao did not respond to her. He had held back just now because he had been waiting for Ning Yuthen to be knocked unconscious, as it wouldn't have been good to act while he was still awake.

Now that the Charm Fox's spiritual energy had surged in, that meant he could send these two Demon God Sect masters on their way.

With a slight shift of his gaze, an even more powerful spiritual energy began to overwhelm the Charm Fox.

"So strong!"

The Charm Fox's pupils constricted, her very soul beginning to tremble.

Not daring to be careless, she immediately tried to withdraw her spiritual energy. But Ye Xiao's speed was too fast, giving her no chance to react as he ruthlessly crushed her invading spiritual energy, then followed the same path to strike at her soul.

"Pu-chi!"

The Charm Fox, who had been standing there, suddenly spat out blood and was blasted back, flailing like a kite with its string cut in midair.

"Charm Fox!"

The Azure Wolf stomped his foot, his body instantly darting forward to catch the Charm Fox's body, only to find that she was already on the verge of death.

"What happened?"

"Run..." the Charm Fox struggled to say, and the Azure Wolf, not daring to be careless, immediately turned and fled.

But just as he turned, a blade-like light, shining like a brilliant meteor in the darkness, instantly closed in on his forehead.

He had no time to react, and the moment he saw that blade-light, his consciousness sank into darkness.

His physical body and soul were mercilessly shattered by that single strike, leaving no trace behind.

Thud. The Azure Wolf and Charm Fox's bodies fell to the ground once more.

"Azure Wolf!"
