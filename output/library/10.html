Boom! The earth-shattering roar echoed, and a massive chasm over a kilometer long, five meters wide, and four meters deep, suddenly formed.

The entire city trembled from the impact.

Dust and smoke rose, forming a kilometer-long screen that obscured people's vision.

Plop, plop...

The Stellar Beast Sect Master had been split in two, falling into the chasm, its green lifeblood and internal organs spilling out all around.

Until that moment, its eyes still held the crazed look of one facing death, which meant that it hadn't even had time to react to that strike! Qin Shenglong and Ouyang Yunzhong were dumbfounded, one sitting on the ground, the other standing motionless, on either side of the chasm.

And that sword mark was so familiar - wasn't it the mysterious expert from the other night?

Qin Shenglong followed her gaze, his pupils constricting as he shuddered.

"The Grand Sect Master, it must be the Grand Sect Master!"

The Stellar Beast Sect Master had said that its shell had been affected by human genetic technology, mutating to the point that no one below the Grand Sect Master could break through it. That meant the attacker's power must be at the Grand Sect Master level! Qin Shenglong and Ouyang Yunzhong were both excited and awestruck, immediately turning back, but they only saw the empty entrance of the library, with no one there.

Their excited expressions instantly dimmed.

Because the stronger he is, the safer he will be.
