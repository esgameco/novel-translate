Look at that arm, so white and tender. If it were coated in oil and roasted over the fire for a few hours, seasoned with cumin and chili powder, the flavor would be absolutely exquisite.

Look at those long legs, she must exercise regularly! Ye Xiao could clearly feel the elasticity and chewiness of the muscles under that skin.

One leg could be braised, the other stir-fried with chili peppers.

If that's not enough to eat, he could even use a healing spell to revive them and make some shredded rabbit legs.

A 100% delicacy from the human realm.

And that small head, so delicately shaped - a braised rabbit head is also a famous dish.

Mixing all kinds of spices and simmering it, the spicy and sweet flavors would make the neighboring young lady cry from envy.

However, taking action here would not work. After all, the other party is a guest in the Silver Fox Valley, and if he killed them, their powerful backers would definitely come looking for trouble, dragging in all sorts of complications.

If he messed it up, he might even implicate Ning Yuhen, which would not be worth it.

So, he'll wait until they return, then ambush them on the way!

After all, they are star beasts, and not his friends. If he eats them, he won't feel any guilt.

Unlike the Silver Fox clan, they won't enter the human world during the beast calamity to massacre the human race.

Killing them would be preemptively eliminating a threat to the people.

Decided, he'll slaughter them when they return.

Thinking this, Ye Xiao lowered his head and started eating.

Those star beasts naturally noticed him as well. As a human among so many star beasts, he did stand out quite a bit.

But to these star beasts, they weren't too concerned about him.

He might be a big shot from the Silver Fox clan, wanting something new to keep as a pet human.

"Sister Fox Queen, we've missed you dearly and have come to visit. We hope you don't mind our presumption, and will graciously host us," the star beasts said.

The Fox Queen smiled slightly. "My dear sisters, what nonsense is this? I'm delighted that you've come to see me. After all, you only visit once in a blue moon, so of course I must treat you well and fulfill my duties as a host. Otherwise, people would mock the Fox Queen for not knowing how to properly welcome her friends."

"Sister Fox Queen is so kind. By the way, Sister, we've noticed some strange glowing lights in the Silver Fox Valley lately. Could it be that you've obtained some precious treasure?"

A meaningful light flashed in the Fox Queen's eyes. She already knew these fellows had ulterior motives. Now the fox tail was out of the bag.

Since the imperial inheritance was no ordinary matter, it was bound to cause some celestial phenomena, so these star beasts with ill intentions had set their sights on it.

But she was no pushover either. She directly denied it, saying, "No, it's nothing like that. I've just been refining some elixirs lately, which have been emitting some glow."

"Really? Sister Fox Queen, your alchemy skills have reached such a high level. In the future, we'd love to learn more alchemy from you, so please don't be stingy with your teachings."

"Of course, of course. We're all sisters, so this is no problem at all. Whenever you want to learn, you can come to me."

"Why not start today, Sister Fox Queen? We're all eager to learn your amazing alchemy skills!"

"Alright, I'll teach you all this afternoon."

Ye Xiao raised his eyebrows slightly. He wasn't interested in these green tea ladies' pretentious acting.

He wasn't a fool - he could tell from a few words that they were after the imperial inheritance.

That didn't matter, since he was here, they had no chance of obtaining the imperial inheritance.

But the problem was, if they stayed here, how would he be able to hunt? Encountering such good prey, Ye Xiao really didn't want to wait a moment longer.

Headache.

Ye Xiao could only stare at Binluo Yujun a bit more, and think about which method would be most suitable - J, K, C, C, T, G? After the third round of wine, the banquet was dismissed, and the star beasts followed the Fox Queen to learn alchemy, while Ye Xiao returned to his room to continue meditating.

These star beasts delayed until nightfall, so the Fox Queen, out of courtesy, had to let them stay the night in the Silver Fox Valley.

This gave them even more time and opportunity.

As night fell, under the starry sky, several star beasts gathered in a room, their eyes flickering with restlessness.

"That old fox, the Fox Queen, is really cunning. Her mouth is sealed tight, we can't get anything out of her."

"Hmph! The more she refuses to say anything, the more it means there's something fishy going on. She must be hiding something."

"I bet the Silver Fox Valley has definitely obtained some precious inheritance. Otherwise, their strength couldn't have increased so much so quickly."

"Well said. The Silver Fox Valley's power was nowhere near this level before."
