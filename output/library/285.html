Huan Liuli turned and ran into the courtyard. Youwei couldn't help but ask in puzzlement, "Strange, why did Lady Ye say 'this servant'? Isn't that how eunuchs from the human race refer to themselves?"

The next moment, a series of agonized cries suddenly came from the courtyard.

"Ah~!"

Youwei's pretty face flushed red.

Senior Ye, you're really going at it, and in broad daylight too.

A moment later, Ye Xiao walked out of the courtyard. Seeing Ye Xiao, Youwei's legs trembled slightly, but she still bowed respectfully and said, "Junior Youwei of the Silver Fox Clan pays respects to Senior Ye."

Ye Xiao spoke indifferently, "What are you here for?"

Youwei hurriedly replied, "Junior has been entrusted by our Silver Fox Clan's Holy Son to come and deliver a message to Senior Ye."

"Holy Son?"

"That would be Ning Yuheng."

Youwei quickly added, and Ye Xiao was somewhat surprised.

"Oh? Has he already become your clan's Holy Son?"

Youwei explained with a smile, "Yes! The Holy Son has exceptional talents and extraordinary abilities! He has also inherited the legacy of our Silver Fox Clan's elders, gaining profound enlightenment, and has now become a Saint-Emperor Realm cultivator at the Innate Eighth Rank!"

"So fast!"

Ye Xiao was somewhat surprised. After all, before Ning Yuheng left, his cultivation was only at the Mortal Realm, not even reaching the Innate Realm.

But in such a short time, he had actually broken through to the Innate Eighth Rank.

However, since he had received the profound enlightenment from the Silver Fox Clan's elders, having such cultivation was quite normal. Those old monsters had probably cultivated for hundreds or thousands of years, and they directly transmitted all their cultivation to him, which was equivalent to gifting him hundreds or thousands of years' worth of cultivation.

It was not something he had truly cultivated himself.

"What did he send you here to find me for?"

"To reply to Senior Ye, our Silver Fox Clan has discovered a legacy site of the Human Emperor Realm, and it is very likely to open up soon. So the Holy Son wishes to invite you to come, as there may be an opportunity for you."

Ye Xiao's eyes flickered.

"A legacy of the Human Emperor Realm?"

How could a legacy of the Human Emperor Realm be in the Silver Fox Clan? However, since Youwei was in front of him, there was no possibility of her lying. Even if he just casually scanned her with the Taixuan Tiangong, he would clearly know her thoughts.

If that was the case, then he needed to consider it carefully. After all, what he was currently lacking was the enlightenment regarding the Emperor Realm.

As long as he could obtain even a sliver of that enlightenment, he could instantly break through to success.

So this sliver of enlightenment was of utmost importance to him.

"The Silver Fox Clan, huh?"

Ye Xiao looked up towards the sky, it seems he would have to go there.

"But before that, there is one more thing I need to take care of. You wait here, I'll be back shortly."

"Yes!"

Youwei responded, and Ye Xiao disappeared from the spot, his sudden speed startling Youwei.

"Senior Ye's strength seems to have grown even stronger than before."

Just as she finished speaking, the door creaked open, and Huan Liuli walked out, limping and swaying her hips.

"Lady Ye, are you alright?"

"I'm fine, this servant is going out to buy some medicine, you wait here."

"Yes!"

Watching Huan Liuli limp away, a blush once again rose to Youwei's face.

Senior Ye's greatness seems to be manifested not only in his cultivation.

On Ye Xiao's side, he wanted to find Jiang Chen and inquire about whether the Sage Emperor and Cloud Emperor from the Star Battlefield had time to meet him. If he could not gain any enlightenment from the Emperor Realm legacy, he could try to obtain it from the two Emperors.

However, when he arrived at Jiang Chen's office, he did not see the other party. This made him slightly puzzled.

With a thought, Ye Xiao expanded his perception, and his expression changed slightly.

"This is... eleven Monarch Realm experts? Could it be that they want to reclaim the Dragon Vein?"

Ye Xiao's expression darkened, and he was about to hurry over.

However, the next moment, he also sensed another figure heading towards the Western Sea, and after a brief moment of surprise, he shook his head.

It seems he no longer needs to go over there.

Shortly after, Deputy Director Ouyang Yunzhong walked in, and seeing Ye Xiao, his eyes lit up.

"Young Master Ye has come."

Ye Xiao: "(...)"

Why has the Director suddenly become so enthusiastic? And he even called him "Young Master Ye"? "Director, what's going on?"

Ouyang Yunzhong smiled and said, "Young Master Ye, you don't have to hide it, I understand. In front of others, I'll still call you by your name. But in private, I'll address you as Young Master Ye."

"Director, there's no need to be so polite."

"Oh yes, I have a confidential document here for Director Jiang. Before he left, he entrusted me to tell you about it if there was anything. So I'll give this confidential document to you."

Ye Xiao nodded and took it, his divine sense scanning it. The content was a response from the Nine State Alliance.

It was a brief message: "The battle situation is dire, the two Emperors cannot withdraw, please postpone the matter for now."

It seems he cannot go to the Star Battlefield for the time being. In that case, this is a good opportunity to go to the Silver Fox Clan.

No wonder Venerable Xuanwu didn't let him come earlier, this battle was indeed not something he could easily participate in.

However, as a man of the Nine Heavenly Realms, even if his strength was not enough, he could not retreat. This was the bottom line of a Nine Heavenly Realms man!
