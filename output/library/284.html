The changes in my inner cultivation were only because these dragon qi were circulating within my body, causing my dantian and meridians to have a part of their improvement, and thus I could absorb more spiritual energy power.

If such a powerful being could be easily broken through by anyone, wouldn't that be a bit of a joke? Shaking his head, Ye Xiao entered meditation again, continuing to contemplate in the hopes of finding that final thread of opportunity.

And just a few days later, the phone in the director's office of the Jianghai City Martial Arts Library suddenly rang.

The Jiang Chen who answered the phone had his expression instantly freeze.

"Are they coming to demand accountability together? Do they really think the Nine Provinces are easy to bully?"

A tense voice came from the other end of the phone again.

"Master, what should we do? The other side is a full eleven King Realm experts from the West! They're waiting for a response at sea right now? If this matter can't be resolved peacefully, it will definitely be a disaster."

"I understand, you don't need to worry about this matter, continue to notify the other masters, the rest will be left to us."

"Understood."

Jiang Chen hung up the phone and called in Ouyang Yunzhong, who quickly walked in.

"Director! What did you summon me for?"

Jiang Chen spoke with a grave expression:

"I may have to leave Jianghai City for a while."

Ouyang Yunzhong's eyes flickered, with a hint of surprise.

"Yes, but I don't know how long the director will be away?"

"I don't know how long I'll be away either, maybe I'll be back tomorrow, or maybe I won't be able to come back for a long time.

If I'm not here, all the big and small affairs of the library will be handled by you.

I only have one request, whatever Ye Xiao requests, you must fulfill it all.

Remember, any request, you must fulfill it! Absolutely do not cause him any trouble, and do not ask any questions about him. Understand?"

"Understood."

Jiang Chen nodded.

"Alright, that's all, you can leave now."

"Yes."

Ouyang Yunzhong walked out, her heartbeat couldn't help but accelerate a bit.

"Strange, why did the director ask about Ye Xiao? The director's identity is definitely not ordinary. Although I don't know who he really is, his identity must be at the level of the Nine Provinces Alliance.

But why would he care so much about Ye Xiao. Could it be that Ye Xiao is my...!"

Ouyang Yunzhong's heart suddenly pounded violently.

She had always been curious about a question.

Why was Ye Xiao's temperament so calm, seemingly completely unlike an ordinary young man?

Although he didn't have the noble air of a young master from a prominent family, no matter what happened, he was always carefree and indifferent.

His temperament was terrifyingly strong.

In addition to that, every time there was a personnel change in the library, he just happened to be in a position that wouldn't be demoted.

The library required a post-heaven third-grade review, and he immediately broke through to post-heaven third-grade.

The library required a post-heaven fourth-grade review, and he immediately broke through to post-heaven sixth-grade.

And in the past year or so, there have been many big and small events happening in Jianghai City, but he has never been injured.

It's as if the spirit beasts would never hurt him.

This guy seems to not care about anything. But in reality, every step he takes is very stable.

Whether it's work or other matters.

Now she finally understood the problem.

Ye Xiao, he is the director's illegitimate son!

This also explains another thing, the director is already so old, but he was transferred from Jingzhou to Jianghai City to serve as the library director.

Isn't it just for his illegitimate son?

After figuring out this matter, Ouyang Yunzhong instantly felt a sense of joy.

It's a good thing that she has always been very nice to Ye Xiao, unknowingly she has already grasped a thick and powerful backing.

It seems that she will be blessed in the future.

Decided, in the future she must treat Ye Xiao even better.

Even find him a partner! No, find him ten partners, until he likes one of them!

Carry out the matchmaking to the end, tightly grasp the big thigh, absolutely cannot let him run away.

After Ouyang Yunzhong left, Jiang Chen took a deep breath and moved the bookshelf behind him.

Behind the bookshelf was a majestic and imposing wardrobe.

Inside the wardrobe was empty, except for a single robe. The robe was a dark green color, with a huge Xuanwu pattern embroidered on the back.

Jiang Chen gently stroked the robe, his eyes revealing a hint of glory and fighting spirit.

"Over fifty years! Old friend, today you will accompany me to the battlefield once again."

With that, he put on the robe, and the originally gentle old man instantly transformed into a fierce and mighty battle general!

With a thought, Jiang Chen instantly appeared in the sky.
