Since there was no meat of other star beasts, and he wanted to quickly improve his own strength, he had to cut more meat from this big fish star beast.

Even if he gets tired of it, there's no other choice, just like a man, no matter how much he does it, he can take a pill and still have to force himself to do it.

Otherwise, he'll just become a waste.

"Have you had enough? Day after day, can't you have some self-respect? Plucking wool is not supposed to be done like this, can't you find another star beast?"

Since I was captured by you, tell me, have you let me rest for a single day?
