"Above the Divine Realm is the Imperial Realm? The ancient emperors?"

How to put it...

This poor monk does not understand it very clearly, I only know that after reaching the peak of the Imperial Realm, human cultivation will be constrained by certain laws. Either they break through to an even stronger level, or they will fall into ashes.

As for what that level is, this poor monk does not know, for it is beyond my comprehension.

However, it is said that the Patriarch Bodhidharma of our Buddhist sect seems to be an existence that has transcended the Imperial Realm, but he is a very ancient being, so I cannot confirm if it is true or not.

I only know that the emperor seems to be an existence that can transcend that rule constraint, meaning that after surpassing the ordinary Imperial Realm, the emperor's cultivation will not be suppressed by the rules, and they can continue to endlessly improve their cultivation and unleash even greater combat power.

This is also why humanity so yearns for the emperor."

Ye Xiao was shocked, not expecting that above the Divine Realm, there were two great realms - the Royal Realm and the Imperial Realm.

And above the Imperial Realm, there was an even more powerful and mysterious existence.

The Imperial Art can already cultivate one to the Imperial Realm, so wouldn't that mean the cultivation technique practiced by the existence above the Imperial Realm is likely the fusion of the Imperial Art? This made Ye Xiao's heart burn with excitement.

It also gave him a deeper desire to pursue that unknown, mysterious realm.

But Ye Xiao soon reacted.

"Wait, you just said that at the peak of the Imperial Realm, one will be constrained by the rules. But if they can break through to an even higher level, wouldn't they no longer be bound by the rules? Wouldn't they be able to unleash powers even greater than ordinary Imperial Realm cultivators, and protect humanity?"

"In theory, that's how it should be. But there is a rumor that after breaking through that limitation, they will also disappear from this world.

And in fact, those who can cultivate to the Imperial Realm are already extremely few, while those who can break through above the Imperial Realm are even more rare, with the majority turning to ashes.

Perhaps there have been one or two in history who broke through that level, but the records of the human race barely have any about the Imperial Realm, let alone higher realms.

So we really don't know what the situation is above the Imperial Realm."

"I see."

Ye Xiao had a vague idea in his mind.

It seems that breaking through to the realms above the Imperial Realm carries great risks, and even if one succeeds, the future is unknown. However, the ordinary Imperial Realm should only require the Imperial Art.

His Golden Book Spiritual Consciousness, he wonders if he can fuse it into an even higher-level technique.

If he can condense an even higher-level technique, perhaps he can break through that limitation, who knows.

And that emperor, he is truly tempting.

With the great fortune of the human race protecting him, he can cultivate without being bound by the rules, and continuously grow stronger, it's like cheating! The question is, will Ye Xiao have that luck to become an emperor?

Since Ye Xiao doesn't like taking risks much, compared to the ordinary Imperial Realm that requires breaking through trials, he would prefer to become an emperor and win without effort.

In the past few days, Ye Xiao has read some books in the library and has a weak understanding of the emperor.

When a crisis befalls the human race, many saints and saintesses will be born into the world. As long as they break through to beyond the Grandmaster Realm, they will display celestial anomalies.

These people will receive strong support from the nation, go through arduous growth, overcome countless dangers, and need to have the great fortune of the world on their side, in order to have a chance to become the rare emperor of the human race in the end.

The human race has had many saints and saintesses appear in the past few hundred years, but no emperor has emerged.

From this perspective, Ye Xiao doesn't have much confidence in himself.

Because he has already broken through to the Divine Realm, but has never displayed any anomalies.

Not even once.

With such a self, it is almost impossible for him to become an emperor.

It's a bit frustrating, as it means his luck is not good enough, that he has such a good system but doesn't have the qualifications to activate it.

He really wants to know what kind of amazing and extraordinary people can qualify to become saints and saintesses! To have the talent and abilities worthy of becoming the legendary emperor, their gifts must be...

The Indestructible Vajra Body Skill is actually from Shaolin? No, that's not quite right, Shaolin's techniques were also refined from the stellar cores of the stellar beasts.
