Ye Xiao stopped his movements.

"Then let's go."

Ye Xiao used a healing technique to help the big fish recover its body.

Afterwards, he placed his hand on the big fish's body and used the Yin-Yang Escape Technique, disappearing from the spot in an instant.

In a certain place in the starry sky, the air distorted and a figure stumbled out.

It was a powerful cultivator from the Winged God Tribe, with half of its wings already fallen off, leaving only a half-broken wing. Its clothes were also heavily torn.

It panted heavily, its gaze somewhat scattered and dazed, yet also carrying a faint joy of surviving a calamity.

"I've finally escaped! The XuanYuan Clan is indeed not to be trifled with! Luckily I ran fast, or else I would have surely perished at the hands of Ning family's experts."

After a pause, it clenched its fists.

"But this time, I've completely torn off the mask with the Ning family. Fortunately, I've already passed on the message. Next time, it won't just be our three major clans, but also many allies.

When that time comes, even if you are the XuanYuan Clan, you won't be able to remain unscathed, will you?"

Just as it was about to flee and find a place to hide, the next moment, it suddenly discovered something.

"Huh? Isn't that... that damned fish?"

In an instant, its pupils constricted.

Damn it!

It's all because of that damned fish!

If it weren't for him, the three major clans wouldn't have attacked the Ning family, and naturally wouldn't have been wiped out by the reinforcements sent by the Ning family.

It had been puzzled earlier, why that damned fish, since it belonged to the Ning family, did not appear during the previous great battle? It turns out it was leisurely carrying someone and roaming around! Our three major clans had so many of our people annihilated, and you have the nerve to be so carefree?

Go to hell! Driven by the surging anger in his heart, the powerful cultivator from the Winged God Tribe immediately charged towards the fish.

But just as he was about to unleash his spiritual energy, a figure in the distance also sensed his aura.

"This aura, Divine Realm Fourth Realm, should be the Winged God Tribe expert that Zhiyuan mentioned escaped? Let me take care of him first, so I can meet up with Zhiyuan sooner."

With that, he also transformed into a beam of light and rushed over in pursuit.

On Ye Xiao and the big fish's side, they had just arrived in the starry sky. The big fish sensed something and used its fish head to point in a certain direction.

"That's the direction, that's my previous lair."

"That direction..."

Ye Xiao was somewhat taken aback. Why did that direction feel so familiar to him?

That direction seemed to be the former base of the Three Clans, where he had previously wreaked havoc.

The big fish chuckled:

"Correct, it's that place. Previously, I was sealed by your human ancestor, someone called Patriarch Zu Long, with a palm strike for over two thousand years.

Fortunately, recently, I don't know who, killed many people there, allowing me to absorb a lot of fresh blood and finally break the seal."

However, as the big fish said this, the smile on its face faded, as if something was not quite right. Now that it had escaped Patriarch Zu Long's seal, the situation seemed to have become even worse.

Being sliced and diced every day, without an ounce of dignity.

Damn it all!

Ye Xiao's mouth twitched. Sure enough, this big fish was the one he had released.

What a cycle of cause and effect. In the end, it was he who had captured it.

However, what Ye Xiao didn't expect was that the big fish had been sealed by Patriarch Zu Long with a palm strike over two thousand years ago. Patriarch Zu Long's strength was truly terrifying.

At that time, he had just emerged from the human world, right?

To be able to seal this Sovereign-level big fish with a single palm strike was simply astounding.

If Patriarch Zu Long were still alive, Ye Xiao couldn't even imagine how powerful he would be now.

Oh well, even if Patriarch Zu Long were still alive, Ye Xiao had no chance of seeing him now. It's better to first go find that Divine King's Tomb and improve his own cultivation.

However, just as he was about to leave, the next moment, an unexpected incident occurred.

An extremely strong killing intent suddenly surged towards him and the big fish.

The opponent's cultivation was actually at the Divine Realm Fourth Realm!

One realm higher than Ye Xiao's current level.

And it was a powerful cultivator from the Winged God Tribe! "Damned fish, die!"

The opponent roared, its killing intent erupting to the extreme.

Ye Xiao asked, "Do you know this person?"

The big fish shook its head.

"No, I've never seen him before."

Ye Xiao nodded. In that case, there was no need to stay any longer.

He raised his hand, aiming at the opponent, and directly unleashed the Annihilation Treasure Technique.

"Boom!"

The next moment, the Winged God Tribe's Divine Realm Fourth Realm expert with the broken wing was directly blasted into a bloody mist.

Under the Annihilation Treasure Technique, an opponent of a lower realm was easily dealt with by Ye Xiao. After unleashing this move, Ye Xiao directly took the big fish and used the Yin-Yang Escape Technique to disappear from the spot once again.

This scene was just witnessed by Ning Uncle Two, who had just arrived. His pupils instantly constricted, and his heartbeat couldn't help but accelerate.

"So strong!"

In an instant, he was able to instantly annihilate a Divine Realm Fourth Realm expert into a bloody mist. This level of ability, although he could also do it, he was at the peak of the Divine Realm!

He was at the Ninth Realm of the Divine Realm, far surpassing that Winged God Tribe expert by several realms.

But the one he just saw, although he didn't get a clear look at his face, just from the glimpse of his back, he could clearly feel that the other party was only at the Third Realm of the Divine Realm! And he was very young!

Perhaps even younger than Ning Zhiyuan, the number one young genius of the Ning family.

To reach the Third Realm of the Divine Realm at such a young age was already monstrous enough!

This made Ning Zhiyuan even more eager to get to know who this person was.
