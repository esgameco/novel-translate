So he needs to arrange a reasonable cultivation plan for himself, striving to break through as soon as possible.

Ye Xiao summarized his current resources.

What Ye Xiao is calculating are his most important things.

Heart Techniques: Purple Thorn Star Sea, Dharma Sutra.

Forging Techniques: Demonic Smithing.

Alchemy Techniques: Myriad Elements Return to One.

Among them, the three Imperial Healing Techniques have reached the stage of synthesis.

Before, when he was at the Xiantian Eighth Grade, he couldn't synthesize them, and now at the Xiantian Ninth Grade, he still can't.

It seems very likely that when he reaches the King Realm, he will be able to synthesize the Imperial Techniques.

I really don't know what kind of powerful techniques he will be able to synthesize at that time?

Secondly, supplementary resources.

He currently has three Eighth Grade star beasts and seven Xiantian Seventh Grade star beasts, providing him with spiritual energy and blood qi every day in the breeding ground.

And now he has also picked so many flowers to give to Susanne the bee, specifically for making honey.

In the short term, the supplement of spiritual energy and blood qi should not be too lacking.

But he should also consider catching a few more star beasts.

After all, he has now advanced to the Xiantian Ninth Grade realm, and his requirements for star beasts are also higher.

Otherwise, the spiritual energy supplement in the later stage will not be enough.

In addition, he also has the imperial-grade defensive armor Black Scale Armor, as well as the divine weapon that has surpassed the imperial realm - the Great Dragon.

As for Illusion Liuli... well, her strength is too weak now, so there's no need to count her in.

Star beasts cannot be caught in the short term, because he now wants star beasts above the Xiantian Ninth Grade, and such star beasts are not so easy to obtain.

It's not like picking cabbages!

Unless he goes to the Star Battlefield.

But Ye Xiao doesn't want to go to the Star Battlefield.

Liu Chengxun has said that the Star Battlefield is very dangerous, even for Saint Monarch-level experts, they may fall at any time.

Moreover, that Tiger God, as well as Xia Bingning, have made Ye Xiao feel a bit wary of the Star Battlefield.

There must be many powerful experts there.

Speaking of which, Shaolin seems to have Ninth Grade star beasts, and quite a few of them.

It is said that Shaolin has Seventy-two Demon Pits, all of which are Ninth Grade Saint Monarch star beasts.

Should he go to Shaolin and steal one or two back to raise as pigs? But this does not conform to Ye Xiao's principles, as Illusion Liuli has been unclean before, and he has scolded her several times.

It seems that he still needs to find a way to investigate where he can find Xiantian Ninth Grade star beasts more easily.

And his current task is to first plant these spiritual flowers.

These are the spiritual flowers he has obtained with great difficulty. If he plants them well, he can let Susanne the bee collect honey from them every day.

In Jianghai City, which places are suitable for planting spiritual flowers? Ye Xiao thought about it for a while, but it seems there is no suitable place.

Spiritual flowers require a lot of sunlight, so it is impossible to hide and plant them, as that would be ineffective.

Planting them at his own home is also very dangerous, as they are easily discovered.

It's a bit troublesome.

Ye Xiao thought for a while, maybe he should find a mountain hideout and set up the Tianyan Zaohua Array and Disha Tiangong Array? The two arrays working together will definitely have an excellent effect.

The Tianyan Zaohua Array is responsible for illusion and killing, while the Disha Tiangong Array is responsible for defense.

And he has the Divine Will Incantation, which is extremely fast, so he can spend a little time every day to pick the fresh flowers.

It can work, it's perfect.

So in the following time, Ye Xiao will go find a suitable mountain hideout.

Set up the arrays, plant the flowers.

When the time comes, it will be deep in the night.

Under the starry sky, the experimental base in the Americas is beginning the trial of a new type of fighter aircraft.

This time, they didn't mess around with fancy things.
