It's better to be more cautious.

Ye Xiao is pondering a question.

If his cultivation breaks through to the Saint Emperor Realm in the future, what should he do then? At that time, it might not be possible for him to stay here anymore, perhaps that will be the time for him to leave.

"Hello! Senior Beitang, why did you suddenly think to contact me?"

On the other end of the Thought Stone, Beitang Ce's voice sounded somewhat serious.

Beitang Ce rarely contacted him easily, and for him to say they were in big trouble, there must be a very important matter.

It might be something even the XuanYuan Clan could not resolve.

"Don't be anxious, just tell me slowly."

Beitang Ce took a deep breath and then spoke:

"A few days ago, Emperor Jing, Qinglong, Master Xuanwu, and Li Liushui all fell ill one after another, their faces deathly pale like wax, but the cause could not be determined.

In the past two days, they have been on the verge of death.

"I'm not sure about the specific situation. But what I know is that people from the Ning family have also fallen ill.

Ye Xiao's eyes narrowed slightly.

That level of existence actually fell ill, it seems this matter is truly not simple.

And his colleagues who didn't come to work today, they were most likely affected as well.

"Don't be too anxious, I'll go to the hospital and take a look first to see what the situation is."

"Okay! But you have to be careful and protect yourself, you are much more important to us than we are."

Ye Xiao lightly hummed in acknowledgment, ending the communication with the Thought Stone, and instantly disappeared from the spot, reappearing inside the hospital.

Relying on his keen perception, he easily found the ward where Emperor Jing and the others were.

They were indeed as Beitang Ce had described, already in a critical condition.

Ye Xiao approached them, and they were like in a state of suspended animation, their breathing extremely slow and faint, almost like they were on the verge of death.

Ye Xiao used his spiritual power to scan their bodies, but found nothing abnormal, their physical condition was intact, and their blood qi was also unharmed.

This was puzzling, their bodies were completely fine, yet they were displaying such a dire condition. It shouldn't be an illness.

Ye Xiao activated his Elemental Eyes again, and the next moment, he suddenly discovered the crux of the matter.

"This is..."

It turned out that for some unknown reason, their blood qi was being devoured by a type of small worm.

These small worms were extremely minute, their structure identical to that of Godblood, and they could even mimic Godblood, moving within the body.

This way, even if Ye Xiao used spiritual power to scan, he couldn't find any problems, because they were completely indistinguishable from the blood cells, essentially one with the Godblood.

And as they continuously moved and multiplied within the body, they were constantly devouring the Godblood.

If they reached a certain degree, they might replace the original Godblood in the body, thereby gaining complete control over the person.

This was a type of ancient poison!

And it was a very old type of poison.

Ye Xiao had previously obtained the inheritance manual of the Poison King, so he knew clearly what this poison was.
