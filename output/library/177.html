The Left Protector looked at the Imperial Soldier in his hand with a hint of reluctance.

Although the one in his hand was an Imperial Soldier, it was not the kind that could be used continuously. After this one-time use, he would have no more to use in the future.

Otherwise, how powerful would it have to be? But for the sake of Noel Fei's separation, it was worth it.

After all, this one-time Imperial Soldier could not be eaten, nor could it increase his own cultivation.

Noel Fei, on the other hand, could actually and substantially increase his cultivation and improve his bloodline.

With a thought, the spiritual energy had been poured into the Imperial Soldier without end.

At this moment, Ye Xiao, who was eating kimchi fried rice with Liu Cheng Xun in the restaurant, suddenly furrowed his brows.

He had sensed the aura of an Imperial Soldier, and it was on his formation.

Liu Cheng Xun did not cultivate spiritual power like Ye Xiao, so he could not perceive what Ye Xiao could sense with such precision.

He was only slightly puzzled by Ye Xiao's expression.

"What's wrong?"

"Nothing, I just suddenly remembered something I haven't done yet."

Ye Xiao casually made up an excuse and didn't pay much attention to it.

Although the other party had an Imperial Soldier, it was only a one-time use, and it could only expand the formation by a small margin, allowing only one person to enter. So Ye Xiao didn't need to be vigilant.

In fact, the Noel Fei and the others below had many grievances against him, so letting them vent their resentment would make them grow healthier.

As for the possibility of them escaping with Noel Fei?

Hehe.

In the library, the Left Protector used his one-time Imperial Soldier to open a temporary hole in Ye Xiao's Imperial Formation, then took a deep breath and jumped down.

"Noel Fei! Obediently become my food!"

But as soon as he said these words, he suddenly felt something was wrong.

The aura under this sealing formation was so dense! So dense that he felt suppressed and could hardly move his limbs.

Wasn't Noel Fei's separation just a primordial seventh-grade Deity Ancestor realm? With a cultivation similar to his own, how could it exert such great suppression on him?

Just as he was completely puzzled, the next second, he heard several different voices coming from his ears.

"Who is this guy?"

"I don't know, he came to look for Senior Noel Fei. Senior Noel Fei, do you know him?"

"Know him? Hell no, didn't you hear that he wants to eat me?"

"Oh~! Then he's the enemy!"

In the darkness, the Deity Ancestor Beasts began to release their powerful auras, and their bodies started to emit flashing lights, forming several beams of light.

The Left Protector was immediately completely dumbfounded.

What's going on? One, two, three, four, five, six, seven, a total of seven Deity Ancestor Beasts?

The Seven Deities Brothers? Didn't they say there was only Noel Fei's separation here? Moreover, in this small Jianghai City, having so many Deity Ancestor Beasts as formation eyes, what are they playing at? No, this is not the time to think about that. With so many Deity Ancestor Beasts here, the key is how to save his own life.

Quickly use the Beast Soul Incense.

Beast Soul Incense is condensed from the essence blood of countless Beasts, and when burned, it can stimulate the spiritual power of Beasts, making them extremely weak and unable to exert their full strength. It is a specialized tool developed by the human race to deal with Beasts.

As long as he had the Beast Soul Incense, even if there were seven Deity Ancestor Beasts here, he would still have a chance to escape.

But he thought too optimistically.

Just as he was about to take out the Beast Soul Incense, the next second, an extremely powerful force directly hit his shoulder.

Boom! The huge force directly dislocated his shoulder, and then other attacks came one after another, bombarding him.

In just two seconds, hundreds of attacks had already hit his body, leaving him severely injured.

The Deity Ancestor Old Man picked up the Beast Soul Incense that had been knocked out of his hand and sniffed it twice, his expression changing slightly.

"It's Beast Soul Incense, this beast is here to deal with us."

At these words, the group of Beasts instantly became as cold as ice.

"Damn it, bullying the Beasts too much!"

"Ye Xiao treats us like livestock, and you also want to come and slaughter us? Drink our blood?"

"At least Ye Xiao is handsome and powerful in the human race! You're so ugly and weak, and you dare to set your sights on us? Looking for death!"

"Eat him!"

The seven Deity Ancestor Beasts were completely enraged to the extreme.

How much humiliation and anger had they endured in these days? Although they couldn't beat Ye Xiao, they could at least bear it.

But now, this little primordial seventh-grade semi-Beast, also dared to set his sights on them, really treating them like mud to be molded? The Left Protector was torn apart alive by the Beasts, his screams echoing endlessly.

"Stop! Stop! I'm the Left Protector of the Beast God Alliance, I have a coordinate position on me.

If you kill me, the Alliance Master won't let you off, I'm her lover, she'll definitely come to kill you all! Ahhhhh."

After a few more screams, the Left Protector was eventually devoured by the Beasts.

The Beasts looked at each other, their mouths full of fresh blood, revealing cruel smiles.
