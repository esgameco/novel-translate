King Aier's face was cold and stern. He directly punched the precious sword, and in an instant, he shattered the sword on the spot.

Boom! The sword exploded, and the shards flew back like lasers, instantly piercing through Yang Zhengyi's body, causing him to spit out another mouthful of fresh blood.

If it were not for the fact that the Grand Master's physical body was already strong enough, this move alone would have been enough to make him completely perish.

But even so, his meridians were severed, and he had lost all vitality, relying only on the last embers to stubbornly refuse to die.

"Do you see? This is the gap between gods and men!"

King Aier's gaze revealed a trace of mocking, as if looking at an ant.

Yang Zhengyi could no longer move, bound by King Aier's lightning chains, his face full of unwillingness.

He patted Yang Zhengyi's cheek and smiled evilly.

"Don't worry, I won't kill you so quickly, that would be too boring."

He glanced back at the city of Jianghai, his eyes filled with a terrifying bloodthirsty light.

Yang Zhengyi already seemed to have guessed something.

"What are you going to do?"

King Aier licked his lips, his eyes revealing a chilling coldness.

"Hehe, more than a hundred years ago, it was because of this city that I was suppressed. Today, I will destroy this city right before your eyes!

By the way, I haven't tasted the delicious flavor of human flesh for more than a hundred years."

Yang Zhengyi shuddered, and the despair that had not appeared on his face when he was defeated now surfaced.

"No! You can't do this! No!"

"Unfortunately, you have no say in the matter."

King Aier's words made Yang Zhengyi's heart completely freeze, overwhelmed by despair!

He had lost! Lost miserably.

Not only did he fail to destroy one of King Aier's avatars, but he had also dragged the entire Jianghai City with its millions of civilians into it.

He had killed some civilians, but that was driven by his obsession to break the seal and destroy King Aier's avatar.

But to let the entire Jianghai City and its millions of people die, the last shred of conscience in his heart could not bear it.

At this moment, he suddenly regretted! Regretted his own stupidity, hated his own obsession.

Why did he have to be so obsessed with revenge?

The result was harming others and himself!

If time could turn back, he would definitely not do it again.

But unfortunately, time cannot be rewound!

The rain fell silently on Yang Zhengyi's face, indistinguishable from the tears of regret.

King Aier looked at his expression of despair, his face filled with smugness, and turned towards Jianghai City. He noticed a thousand-meter-long sword mark on the riverbank, black and gaping like a giant mouth devouring life.

This made him raise his eyebrows slightly, but he soon ignored it.

A mere thousand-meter-long sword mark was nothing, a Grand Master's full-power strike could easily do that, and his own main body could casually slash it out, there was nothing to be afraid of.

Thinking this, King Aier stepped over the sword mark.

But just as he stepped over it, in an instant, King Aier suddenly felt a terrifying crisis approaching, causing all the hair on his body to stand on end!

The two sides of this sword mark were like two different worlds. "No..."

Before he could finish the word, the next moment, his consciousness plunged into darkness.
