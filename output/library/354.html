After taking everything out, it looked like a small mountain nearly a kilometer high.

The star beasts in the star beast breeding farm were really too many. Every day, they continuously provided flesh and blood, and after accumulating for a long time, it reached the size of a small mountain.

Then Ye Xiao took out the star cores of Lin Qingbei and the others.

There were super divine-level star cores, as well as divine beast-level ones, and more were at the level of grand supreme masters. In short, Ye Xiao killed and slaughtered all the star beasts that were not suitable for eating, and directly dug out their star cores.

The star cores were the souls of the medicinal materials for refining dan, and they were also the core of the medicinal materials. The qi in the flesh and blood was to enhance the existence of the dan.

When the two were combined, they would complement each other.

Ye Xiao practiced the divine Luo dan success method, blending and fusing, and all the star cores were instantly shattered. The essence inside them condensed together like stars, rapidly forming a solidified, highly spiritual core.

Then Ye Xiao refined the mountain-like star beast meat, attracting out all the blood qi within.

The star beast meat without blood qi was like ashes, without the slightest trace of nutrition. Without any wind blowing, it would naturally decay.

Thousands upon thousands of streams of blood qi continuously converged onto that core, adhering to it.

Rumbling, in the sky above, a large expanse of thunder clouds began to condense again. This time, the thunder tribulation was specifically generated for the divine dan. The thunder tribulation just caused by the divine formation had not yet dispersed and was still hovering in the sky.

This sizable commotion was soon transmitted out, quickly attracting the attention of a few rare beings in the surrounding starry skies.

"Up ahead, we're about to reach the Xuanyuan Clan and the Thunder God Clan, who have discovered the location of the divine tomb. I heard that this time, many tomb sites have been discovered, far more than just one or two. We should also go and get a share."

"Before the reinforcements from the major clans arrive, let's first snatch some things and leave."

"Yes, we can't let them take all the good stuff."

As they spoke, several figures soon encountered the disturbance in the asteroid group and couldn't help but be startled.

"This is... thunder tribulation? This feeling, it's dan tribulation! My goodness, there's actually someone refining dan here, and they've even triggered thunder tribulation."

"Judging from the scale of this dan tribulation, this dan is definitely not simple."

"Let's go take a look."

Several figures quickly surrounded the area.

However, when they saw the potent spiritual energy of the dan Ye Xiao was refining, they couldn't help but exclaim in amazement, "Heavens, such a massive spiritual energy! This dan has already transcended the realm of emperor dan and is a divine dan! This is definitely a divine dan!"

"Quick! Snatch this dan away."

However, just as some of them were about to act, their companions next to them promptly stopped them.

"Why the hurry? Can't you see that the dan hasn't been completely refined yet? Don't go disturb him. Let him continue refining the dan, and then we'll go seize this dan when it's finished. That would be more valuable."

"Yes, yes, yes."

The crowd nodded in agreement.

"All of you, hide your auras well. Don't let him discover us."

The crowd hid behind the asteroids, secretly and excitedly watching Ye Xiao refine the dan.

Of course, Ye Xiao knew that people had come, but these people, no matter how strong they were, could not surpass him, especially since he had cultivated the spiritual cultivation method.

It was difficult to find a place in the entire starry sky that was completely devoid of people, so he could only try to find a place with fewer people.

And these few people were not weak either, with their strengths averaging around the Emperor Realm 30-40 dragons, and the strongest one even reaching the Emperor Realm 58 dragons, which was indeed not low.

Being able to detect his abnormal activity was also normal.

He didn't speak, nor did he alert them, completely focused on refining his own dan. He had already thought of a strategy in his heart.

Crackle—! At this moment, the first bolt of dan tribulation lightning began to fall, heavily striking the divine dan.

The dan shook violently, but it also squeezed out some impurities. At the same time, the power of the lightning also greatly enhanced the spiritual energy of the dan.

Then the second, third, fourth, and more bolts of lightning continuously fell on the dan, with each strike making the divine dan even stronger.

The divine dan had begun to emit a faint golden light, and some special patterns unique to divine dans had also appeared on it.

The few figures hiding in the distance couldn't help but sigh with admiration, "This person's dan refining ability is truly too strong!"

"At such a young age, he can possess such powerful dan refining ability. Looking across the entire starry sky, he is absolutely a dan refining genius. His strength may not be inferior to the top-tier dan refining prodigies of the major starry sky clans! He is truly a heaven-blessed talent!"

"I think it's best not to kill him later. We should capture him and have him refine dan for us in the future."

"That's right, we need the dan, and we need him too. Haha haha."

"Keep it down, don't startle him and affect his dan refining."

The speed of the thunder tribulation falling from the sky was getting faster and faster, and the dan was becoming increasingly perfected.

But the purple tribulation, the books had no record of it.
