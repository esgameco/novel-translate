"That person just now, he should be a human, right?"

The captain couldn't help but murmur.

The others silently followed and spoke up:

"Couldn't see clearly, he was shrouded in a golden light, so we couldn't make out his face."

"But he's probably human. He seems to look like a human - one nose, two eyes, two arms, two legs. More or less the same as us.

And if he was a star beast, he should have killed all of us. How could he just leave us alone?"

"But why wouldn't the things he did be done by a human?"

On Ye Xiao's side, in the blink of an eye, he had returned to the Jianghai City.

This trip to the Southwest Forest Sea had allowed him to collect a large amount of heavenly materials and earthly treasures, all of which were the finest of the finest.

It should be enough for these star beasts to use.

However, Ye Xiao also had a concern - although the quantity of these heavenly materials and earthly treasures was large, they were ultimately limited.

Once one is consumed, there is one less.

The star beasts he was raising, not only would their numbers not decrease, but they would continue to increase.

If that was the case, these heavenly materials and earthly treasures might not last very long.

The Southwest Forest Sea could not create another batch of the same heavenly materials and earthly treasures in a short period of time.

The growth cycle of these things is really too long, otherwise they would not have such powerful nutrition.

It's not like raising pigs that can be slaughtered in three months.

Moreover, apart from the Southwest Forest Sea, there are not many places in the Nine Provinces that have so many resources.

In fact, it can be said that it is difficult to find such resources anywhere in the world.

This won't do, he has just reached the King Realm cultivation, if the resource supply is insufficient, how can he accelerate his future cultivation?

It must be known that with the increase in cultivation, the time, effort, and resources required for each advancement increase exponentially.

The reason why he has been able to advance quickly all this time is entirely due to the fact that he has absorbed and plundered a large amount of star beast resources.

Relying solely on resources, he would be far from reaching this level.

Without resources, he would be done for.

He needs to come up with a solution.

Should he open up another farm? That's a pretty good idea.

Only by taking matters into his own hands can one be well-fed.

However, when establishing a farm, the issue of land must be considered.

The star beast breeding ground he has set up can be arranged in the basement of the library, without occupying any space, and it will not be discovered by others.

But the star beast breeding ground is arranged there because they only need to be provided with spiritual energy.

Heavenly materials and earthly treasures won't work, in addition to needing a large amount of spiritual energy, they also need to constantly absorb the essence of the sun and moon in order to mature quickly.

This requires a surface-level land.

And to grow so many things, the required land area will definitely not be small.

In fact, the land he previously used to plant those flowers may not even be enough.

To do it this way, it would be a bit unprofitable.

At this moment, Ye Xiao's gaze fell on his ring.

The spatial storage ring.

Shrinking the space, that's it! He can completely add a thing into the power of space, refine it into a spatial treasure, and then engrave a formation on it to absorb the spiritual energy of heaven and earth and the essence of the sun and moon, wouldn't that allow him to grow a large amount of heavenly materials and earthly treasures? And it can also be carried around conveniently, easy to hide, without worrying about anyone discovering it.

It's perfect! Tomorrow, he'll give Wang Dalong a call and ask him to help search for some obsidian.

The spatial ring he snatched from the Ferocious Beast Alliance Leader's hand has things that can be used to plant heavenly materials and earthly treasures, but it lacks obsidian.

Perhaps because obsidian is too cheap, that Alliance Leader's ring space is limited, so he didn't bother to put it in.

Ye Xiao found a ten-thousand-year meteorite formation base from it, about the size of three cubic meters.

This thing is quite hard and can withstand a lot of spatial power infusion.

Ye Xiao used his divine craftsmanship to forge it into a rectangular base, with an initial land area of only five or six square meters and a thickness of about 20 centimeters.

As long as he adds the spatial power of obsidian to it, it will be able to produce the effect of spatial miniaturization.

Then, he can plant a huge amount of heavenly materials and earthly treasures inside.

But this process will have to wait until he has gathered enough obsidian.

Time flew by, and a night had passed in the blink of an eye.

Fortunately, Ye Xiao no longer needs to rest now.

His spiritual energy is now overflowing, constantly circulating within him, having long surpassed the physical constraints of ordinary humans.

In the morning, Xuanliuli used star beast materials to prepare a sumptuous breakfast for Ye Xiao, allowing him to replenish his blood and energy.

While eating breakfast, Ye Xiao dialed Wang Dalong's phone number.

"Hello! Brother Ye, do you have any more business for your little brother?"

"It's still about obsidian. This time I want a bit more, can you get me a few truckloads?"

"No problem, it's a small matter for your little brother."

"When do you need it? I'll send it over to you right away."

Ye Xiao was slightly taken aback.

"Has the delivery speed become this fast nowadays? Before, you still had to go through a period of collection."

"That was the past, it's different now. Our Jianghai City has been expanded, and we've also established a super-large martial arts resource transfer station.

All kinds of resources can be allocated at any time.

Only a few rare resources need to be procured from other places, but the speed is also very fast, much faster than before when we had to go through second- or third-tier cities. Now it's direct shipping, if you need it in the morning, it can be delivered by the afternoon.

I started a company with a few friends, so please feel free to patronize us in the future.

Ye Xiao then realized that with Jianghai City's continuous expansion and strengthening, this city was no longer the small town he used to live in. Instead, it had become a large city with a massive amount of martial arts resources, and was progressing towards becoming a super-large city! This was a good thing, as in the future, he wouldn't need to go to other cities to find things, he could just stay in Jianghai City and save a lot of trouble.

"Since that's the case, then please send it over when I get off work in the evening. You know the location."

"Don't worry, I'll make sure to get it done for you, President Ye."

After resolving this matter, Ye Xiao left home and went to work.

When he arrived at the library, it was just a little past 7 o'clock, with plenty of time before the official start of work.

Ye Xiao directly used the earth-traversing technique to go underground and collect today's offerings.

Seeing him arrive, all the star beasts enthusiastically rushed over to him.

"Master has come."

"Master, my cultivation level advanced by one layer yesterday, I've already promoted to the Xiantian Eighth Rank. Is the meat I offer even more tender now?"

"Master, have you had breakfast yet? How was the milk I squeezed yesterday? Was it fragrant? Sweet? If it's not sweet enough, I'll eat more sugarcane today."

Among all the star beasts, only Feng Long and Qing Niu did not come forward to fawn over him like the others.

Because they had just arrived the day before and were not yet accustomed to the life of being domesticated animals.

Ye Xiao waved his hand.

"Quiet down, all of you. I've already told you before that whoever can provide me with more blood qi, I will also give them more resources to help them further improve their strength.

Starting today, no, starting right now, this rule is in effect.

I will distribute appropriate materials based on your performance today."

Su Shan was the first to step forward, as she was the simplest of all the star beasts, only needing to brew honey, without having to sacrifice her own blood qi.

"I'm here, I'm here. I've already prepared, this is the honey I harvested last night, a total of twenty-eight barrels."

Ye Xiao nodded.

She had indeed done a very good job. Previously, she could only produce a few barrels of honey each night at most, but this time she had produced several times that amount.

With so much honey, Ye Xiao could use it to brew liquor with even more abundant spiritual energy, and with a little deer blood and deer antler, the blood qi enhancement should be able to reach several times the previous level.

"Here are ten white fragrant herbs, I don't need to explain their uses, do I? Is this enough to make up for the extra barrels of honey you produced?"

Su Shan's eyes lit up and she quickly nodded.

"Of course, of course."

The value of the white fragrant herbs was not something precious to Su Shan.

But the effort she had put in was just producing an extra twenty or so barrels of honey every night.

If calculated in terms of wages, this would be a huge earning, equivalent to a regular worker earning a thousand yuan per day.

More importantly, as a Shenzu-level star beast, she would not feel tired, and could even continuously brew honey, activate her cultivation technique, and even further improve her cultivation.

Calculating it this way, she could both improve her cultivation and earn a huge amount, why wouldn't she be eager to do it?

The other star beasts also had their eyes light up, and they couldn't help but get excited.

"Master, master, I also have good things. Look, I chopped off eight tentacles last night."

"Master, master, I chopped off two wings last night and stripped all the feathers off my body, they are the finest feathers."

"Master, look at how much milk I squeezed yesterday? I filled up several barrels, my udders are all swollen!"

The star beasts one by one offered their tributes.

Ye Xiao also distributed a large amount of resources to them accordingly.

This scene made the following Feng Long and Qing Niu swallow their saliva involuntarily.

"Hey, old mudfish, did you see that? He gave them so many things."

"Not just a lot, but many of them are top-tier treasures. Even we can use some of them to boost our cultivation."

"Do we still need to pretend to be aloof here?"

"I want to, but what he gave is really too much!"
