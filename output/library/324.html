Due to the large-scale movement of the Star Beast Legion, a series of violent winds surged in from the gates of the Nine Provinces, bringing with them a series of ear-piercing howls.

Directly experiencing this massive impact, the people of the Nine Provinces on the battlefield could not help but feel their hearts pounding, and even those watching from the screens, not present on the battlefield, felt an oppressive, suffocating sensation that made their hearts tremble.

This second wave of Star Beasts was far, far stronger than the previous one. In terms of both numbers and strength, they carried an even more overwhelming sense of domination!

The howling winds, like giant waves in the sea, constantly blew Ye Xiao's clothes, making them flutter rapidly backwards, but the roar of the wind was so deafening that the sound of the clothes flapping was completely drowned out and could not be heard.

At this moment, the crowd looked at Ye Xiao with even greater admiration. Even facing such tremendous pressure, he had not retreated a single step. That figure stood firmly guarding the gates of the Nine Provinces, like a god of war descended to earth.

The next moment, he finally moved again. Accompanied by a rising sword intent, a shocking sword beam once again appeared above Ye Xiao's head. It was still the Heavenly Opening.

Because Lie Di had not yet been completely cultivated to the realm of great perfection by the Golden Book Shen Hun.

The sword broke out, piercing straight into the clouds, radiating a distance of over a million zhang. The golden sword beam even illuminated the entire earth, allowing some of the people in the west of the Nine Provinces to see this golden light shooting up into the sky.

The next moment, the sword beam fell.

Boom! Another earth-shattering roar, followed by another burst of light, illuminating everything and making everyone's eyes unable to see clearly.

The light continued for dozens of seconds, and after dozens of seconds, the light disappeared, and all the Star Beasts vanished without a trace.

The earth returned to calm, as if nothing had ever happened. Only a sword mark spreading out for a million zhang, like a bottomless abyss, testified to the stunning power of that previous sword strike.

"Good!" The Xuanwu Venerable and Beitang Ce exclaimed the word "good" in unison. The two elders' eyes were reddened with excitement and emotion. The Nine Provinces had Ye Xiao, and could thus remain safe and secure! At this time, both the strong ones behind and the strong ones watching on the screens could not help but cheer repeatedly.

"Senior Sword Immortal is truly divine!" "I love him!" "To be able to witness such a powerful figure and such a heroic battle in this lifetime, I have no regrets!" "The heavens have blessed the Nine Provinces!"

Amid the chorus of praise, Ye Xiao continued to eliminate wave after wave of Star Beasts, the number of Star Beasts killed by his hand rising to an unimaginable and terrifying degree.

Ten million! Twenty million! Thirty million! Forty million! In the blink of an eye, it had already exceeded a hundred million, and this number was still rising.

One hundred million! Two hundred million! Three hundred million! Four hundred million! Five hundred million!

Ye Xiao had gone berserk in his killing. No matter how many Star Beasts appeared, he would kill them all.

As long as they appeared within his line of sight, not a single one could escape his attacks.

While slaughtering, Ye Xiao's heart was also bleeding. What a waste of food! After all, among these Star Beasts, there were many at the Deity Sect level, half-step Deity Beasts, and even a few that had already reached the Deity Beast level. What a heartbreaking thing.

If he could collect them all, he could make his breeding farm so prosperous that he could eat different kinds of fish and eggs every day. Just thinking about it made him feel extravagant.

However, the people of the Nine Provinces behind him were the truly important ones.

But at this moment, Ye Xiao suddenly thought of something. If he couldn't catch them in front of the national gate, could he create some spiritual energy clones?

Spiritual energy clones had a certain time limit and spiritual energy limit, but at least with his current Emperor Realm Eight Dragons cultivation, the spiritual energy clones he created should be several times stronger than the ones he created when he first reached the Emperor Realm.

They should have reached the level of Emperor Realm entry. This level of spiritual energy clone should have a greatly increased endurance time and considerable strength. They would be a very good choice to go out and capture those super-large Star Beasts.

Ye Xiao's habit was to act as soon as he thought of something. So the next moment, he directly divided out four spiritual energy clones.

The crowd was startled.

"What, the Sword Immortal can also perform the legendary clone technique? Is this a cultivation method? Isn't this already a divine immortal's technique? Could the Sword Immortal really be the legendary immortal?"

Some who had already reached the Deity Sect level were full of reverence and awe.
