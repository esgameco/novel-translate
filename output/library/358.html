But in reality, when they are combined, they can shape their own origin.

Using four or even more divine techniques to synthesize it can truly demonstrate its preciousness.

Long-distance vision, X-ray vision, analysis, illusion, attack - a series of powerful abilities were manifested in his divine eyes.

"Wow, such powerful divine eyes!"
