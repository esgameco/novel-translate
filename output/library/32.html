Afterwards, Qin Yuyan hurried back, and when she passed by the pool, she noticed a few purple fish scales on the edge of the pool, not yet dried.

This made her pupils contract, and her heart suddenly skipped a beat.

Could it be that someone really ate the purple-scaled fish, is it Ye Xiao's pet? Her heart suddenly started beating violently.

Seeing Huan Liuli in good condition, it must not have eaten the purple-scaled fish.

So she was just being ridiculous. How could Huan Liuli be able to eat purple-scaled fish? If that were the case, how powerful would Ye Xiao have to be? To have a spirit pet with the strength of a master or above, Ye Xiao would have to be a great grandmaster or even beyond, so how could he be just a librarian?

And how old is he? In front of her, he's still just a younger brother. Even if he had been cultivating since birth, he couldn't be that monstrous.
