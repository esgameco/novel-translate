Ordinary flowers definitely won't do. Ye Xiao wants good-tasting honey that can provide a huge amount of spiritual energy to help him break through, not just regular honey.

It's like meeting online friends - you can't just meet for the sake of meeting, that would be vulgar. It has to be for the sake of spiritual elevation.

The first flower he decided on was called the Purple Yaolan. This is a flower that blooms at night, and unlike other flowers, it absorbs moonlight instead of sunlight, condensing the essence of heaven and earth. It has a kind of strange, zombie-like feeling to it.

What's so good about this flower? Because it blooms in the early morning, when most people are resting, and the spiritual energy in heaven and earth is at its fullest.

So the spiritual energy it condenses is extremely rich.

There are also not a few cultivators who grow this flower at home, both as an ornamental and to absorb spiritual energy.

Another important reason Ye Xiao chose it is that it's easy to obtain, with abundant supply.

There's also the Blazing Sun Flower, whose nature is the opposite of the Purple Yaolan - it loves the sun instead of the moon.

Its existence is more common, and while its effects aren't as good as the Purple Yaolan's, its abundance and affordability make up for it.

Besides these two, there are also the Linglong Flower, the Flower King - Myriad Purple and Red Leaves, and the Climbing Stone Flower.

They are all flowers with extremely rich spiritual energy, and some are even auctioned off, used as top-grade medicinal materials for refining supreme elixirs!

But Ye Xiao reckons those last few types won't be easy to obtain.

Ye Xiao called up Wang Dalong, the night market stall owner.

This guy has connections, and if there's something hard to find, he can usually get it.

"Hello, President Ye, why did you suddenly call me? Do you have a task for me?"

"I want to buy a few types of flowers."

"Flowers? Are they for elixir refining?"

"Yes."

"Okay, what flowers do you want?"

Ye Xiao listed the flowers he wanted, and Wang Dalong explained:

"President Ye, I can get you the Purple Yaolan and Blazing Sun Flower, but the others will be hard. They're top-grade goods, and the prices are high, tens of thousands per flower, even over a hundred thousand. Buying those flowers to refine elixirs isn't really suitable.

What if it fails? That would be a huge loss."

"Where can I buy the other flowers more cheaply?"

"Cheap is impossible. Unless you go pick them in the mountains yourself. If I remember correctly, you can find them in the Southwest Forest Sea, but it's extremely dangerous there, with powerful star beasts lurking. If you're not careful, you could lose your life."

"Is that so? I see. Then just get me 200,000 worth of the Purple Yaolan and Blazing Sun Flower first."

Ye Xiao had previously sold Jifeng's fur for several million in the Hucheng black market, so he didn't need to worry about money now.

"Okay, I'll have it ready for you by tonight."

"Await your good news."

After hanging up, Ye Xiao started planning.

First, he'll try the effects of the Purple Yaolan and Blazing Sun Flower, see what level they can reach.

If the honey is excellent and has immense spiritual energy, he'll then go to the Forest Sea to look for other flower varieties.

If the honey is poor and lacks spiritual energy, he'll just get rid of Su Shan and fry her up in breadcrumbs.

Ye Xiao won't keep useless things - if they can't provide value, even an eighth-grade star beast is worthless to him.

That night, Wang Dalong prepared the two flowers as promised, two truckloads of each.

Ye Xiao paid the money and threw the flowers into his spatial seal, letting Su Shan start making the honey, then went home to rest.

The next morning, he personally went to the cultivation farm to collect the results, not letting Xuanliuli come.

He wanted to see how yesterday's efforts had turned out.

When Ye Xiao arrived, all the star beasts had their daily offerings ready.

Noel Fis's octopus tentacles, Elder Xian's ginseng threads, deer blood, deer antlers, chicken eggs, turtle eggs...

But what Ye Xiao valued most were the offerings from Su Shan and Moyu, the two eighth-grade star beasts.

Moyu had just finished and was buttoning up.

This left Ye Xiao very puzzled.

Does she not even need to transform back into a star beast form? Is she really that powerful?

The fresh milk was still steaming.

Ye Xiao flicked his finger with spiritual energy to take out a bit, and the taste was very good - warm, slightly sweet, very appetizing.

An extremely rich but gentle spiritual energy spread from his stomach to his limbs.

Wow, jackpot!

The effect of just a sip is almost like eating a supreme-grade elixir pill. Drinking a whole bucket would be worth several master-grade elixir pills at least.

And it can also be used to make cheese, butter, peach yogurt, and so on.

Both in actual effects and in taste, it's far superior to elixir refining.
