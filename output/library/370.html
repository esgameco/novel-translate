"Human race?! Wait a minute, this is..."

A deep sense of resentment and indignation filled his soul!

"Let me first take a look at what kind of fish this is. Not all fish are suitable for stewing."

However, when he approached the fish and took a closer look, his eyes immediately lit up.

"Wow! It's actually this kind of fish!"

Huan Liuli and the other star beasts were a bit puzzled.

"Master, what kind of fish is it?"

Ye Xiao patiently explained:

"This fish is a salmon. Its meat is very tender and delicious, and it's an excellent choice for sashimi."

Huan Liuli and the others' eyes lit up.

"Sashimi? So we can eat it raw."

Ye Xiao nodded.

"There's wasabi and soy sauce in the kitchen. Go get them."

"Okay!"

Huan Liuli quickly slipped into the kitchen and quickly brought out the soy sauce and wasabi.

Ye Xiao used his spiritual energy to form a sword qi, cutting off a small piece of meat, dipping it in soy sauce, and then putting it into Huan Liuli's little mouth.

"How is it?"

Huan Liuli took a bite and immediately exclaimed excitedly, "It's so delicious! This fish is really good! I love eating this fish. The fat and lean are just right, and it's not greasy at all!"

Ye Xiao nodded.

"That's why I said this fish is best eaten raw."

He took out four pieces of meat and handed them to Huan Liuli.

"These four pieces of meat are for you all."

The three-headed Great Emperor Dragon Lion star beasts were completely dumbfounded.

"We also get a share?"

Ye Xiao nodded and continued preparing another piece of fish meat to eat.

"Of course you all get a share. It was you all who brought him here in the first place. You all have made a great contribution, so I naturally have to reward you."
