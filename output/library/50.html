Ye Xiao sipped his soy milk and listened to the news.

"Hello everyone, this is Jianghai City TV, the morning talk show.

"Throughout history, small places can only attract powerful experts for one reason - they are hiding their identities and retiring from the world!

Professor Zhang Daqiang nodded in agreement.

"I agree with what Old Man Zhang said. I've researched many ancient texts and found that Jianghai City has many legends hinting at its extraordinary nature.

It's not that the experts chose Jianghai City, but rather Jianghai City has attracted the experts.

And with the arrival of these experts, Jianghai City will also attract a large number of ordinary martial artists.

"We all hope that Jianghai City will develop better and better. This is very likely to be an opportunity for our city.

He swept them with the Taixuan Tiangong, and they were all filled with books.

This made his eyes light up.

These books... many of them are new, could it be that the library just bought these new books?
