On the other side, Ye Xiao was walking home from the city area after finishing his meal. His expression was not very happy.

Ever since he had eaten the meat of the Star Beasts before, he often found the ordinary dishes to be lacking in taste.

Especially the meat of the Celestial Beast-level Star Beasts.

Whether it was the Aer King or Noel Fist, or even the Black Xuanwu Saint Envoy and the Black Scale Snake, they were all far better than these ordinary dishes.

The meat of the Celestial Beast-level Star Beasts was tender and crisp, and with each bite, not only was there the fresh and soft texture of the meat, but it also contained an extremely powerful blood qi and spiritual qi.

The burst of spiritual qi in the mouth, the sense of satisfaction, and the refreshing feeling that permeated the body were things that no dish could compare to.

This is probably also the reason why cultivators who have reached the Celestial Beast realm no longer enjoy eating ordinary food? Eating regular food can no longer satisfy the needs of the Celestial Beasts, so they might as well not eat at all.

This has also made Ye Xiao more determined to catch more Celestial Beast-level Star Beasts to eat. Not only can it satisfy his own appetite, but it can also eliminate the threat to humanity.

Speaking of which, he has always been puzzled by one problem.

Why don't Celestial Beasts eat food, and why hasn't their anus disappeared or degenerated?

According to normal biological evolution theory, if people don't eat, their digestive system should also become useless, right? Later, Ye Xiao came up with a reasonable explanation - the high-martial world itself is unreasonable, so the occasional unreasonable thing is also understandable.

Just as he had just finished pondering this question, he suddenly stopped in his tracks and looked up towards the rooftop in the distance.

There, a murderous intent was hidden, although it was well concealed, it could not escape his perception.

The Profound Heaven Divine Might was activated, instantly revealing her appearance.

A bewitching figure, wearing a feathered robe, it could only be Yifeng, who else could it be? As soon as he looked up, she had obviously also noticed him.

"So it's this human. Noel Fist senior is waiting here, I'll go and kill him."

"No, no, don't act rashly. We just need to know that he is in Jianghai City. It's better to avoid the limelight and plan carefully.

What if you can't beat him? The Star Beast Clan's plan will be ruined."

"Senior, you don't need to worry too much. You don't know my strength, which is understandable. I, Yifeng, have never been afraid of anyone in a one-on-one battle! Let alone he is just a human, even if he is also a Celestial Beast, he can't possibly defeat me.

And even if I can't kill him, escaping to save my life is not difficult.

Senior, just watch."

Noel Fist still wanted to say something, but Yifeng had already leapt down from the rooftop.

This made Noel Fist somewhat speechless.

Yifeng seems to have an excessive blind self-confidence.
