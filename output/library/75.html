Ye Xiao had no interest in this at all, even if Gu Hai had pestered him to come.

This kind of thing had almost become a standard practice for small martial arts academies like the Jianghai Martial Arts Academy.

It seemed that if they didn't invite a person of status and prestige to give a graduation speech, it would not be considered proper.

Ye Xiao came here mainly to see if he could take the opportunity to obtain some basic cultivation techniques.

Hearing that all martial arts originated from Shaolin, Shaolin must have many fundamental techniques, which should be relatively easy to acquire.

As expected, he did find one.

The grand old monk who gave the speech taught a body tempering technique - the Strengthening Tendons and Bones Technique!

It was a fundamental body tempering technique passed down in the Shaolin Martial Arts Academy.

It had the effects of strengthening the body, significantly enhancing physical abilities, and improving physical flexibility.

It was said that the advanced Shaolin body tempering technique was the Sinew Transformation Classic.

However, in a place like this, it was obvious that they would not teach that, at most they would only teach a technique like the Strengthening Tendons and Bones Technique.
