His previous display had just been an act, a ploy to lure Ye Xiao in close.

This technique was Noel Fis' trump card, only used when he couldn't escape or when facing a powerful enemy he needed to eliminate decisively.

That Ye Xiao had forced him to use this move was already a great honor.

"Human! Now die!" Noel Fis sneered, unleashing his remaining six tentacles in a full-force attack.

Boom! The deafening explosion rang out once more.

This time, Noel Fis was truly going all-out, and although he had lost two tentacles, the power of this strike was still formidable.

Surely this would be enough to finish off Ye Xiao?

However, the triumphant smile on Noel Fis' face quickly froze and vanished, replaced by sheer horror.

His bulging eyes stared at Ye Xiao in disbelief.

"Impossible! How is this possible?"

His six tentacles had failed to crush Ye Xiao as expected, but had instead been firmly withstood.

How could this human's physique be so strong?

Noel Fis recognized the golden glow emanating from Ye Xiao's body - he was cultivating the Tempering Body Art!

Not only was this human fast and powerful in attack, he had also mastered the Tempering Body Art?

What was going on? How was he supposed to deal with this?

Before Noel Fis could fully process his shock, he was hit with another bombshell.

Despite the slight paralysis to his soul, Ye Xiao's three healing arts had activated simultaneously, instantly treating his injuries and dispelling the negative effects.

"Healing arts? You've even learned healing arts?" Noel Fis shrieked.

It was no wonder he was so stunned - Ye Xiao's techniques and abilities were simply too numerous and powerful.

Even human cultivators could only focus on mastering one or two martial arts at most, as the human talent was limited.

But this Ye Xiao before him...

His strength was formidable, and his repertoire of skills was absurdly excessive! This man was truly no ordinary human.
