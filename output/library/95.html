Ning Yuhen's heart shuddered, and he answered solemnly.

"Don't worry, manager."

"Then go."

With that, Ye Xiao turned and walked into the courtyard.

"I don't want my peaceful life to be disturbed. If my identity is known, it will be counted against the Silver Fox Clan."

Ning Yuhen deeply bowed to Ye Xiao.

"Manager, thank you for your care all this time. Rest assured, the Silver Fox Clan will never reveal even a fraction of your secret."

The Dowager Empress smiled faintly. "Child, can we go now?"

Ning Yuhen nodded.

Then the Dowager Empress glanced at Youwei, and Youwei immediately took Ning Yuhen and flew away with the wind.

As for Ye Xiao, he returned to his room to study the healing imperial art of the Silver Fox Clan, the Chenxing Technique.

Ye Xiao had never heard the term "imperial art" before, but he understood that above the Sect Master level techniques, there were other even higher-level techniques.

The techniques he had previously synthesized, like the Tyrant Saber, were all above the Sect Master level.

Now it seems that this technique might be an imperial art!

"Imperial art," Ye Xiao murmured, raising his eyebrows slightly.

"Could it be that the ancient peerless emperors cultivated the so-called imperial arts?"

He could feel that the cultivation momentum of techniques like the Tyrant Saber had not yet reached its limit.

Cultivating these techniques, he would surely be able to break through the limits of the Divine Sect and reach higher levels.

He just didn't know what those higher levels were called.

He quickly finished reviewing the Chenxing Technique, and the Golden Book recorded it.

The night passed quickly.

The next morning, when Ye Xiao woke up from his dream, the quilt had risen above his line of sight, blocking his view of the foot of the bed.

This was not because he had cultivated the Chenxing Technique, but since he had learned the Indestructible Divine Skill, he had been waking up to this scene every morning - he was already used to it.

His consciousness was immersed in the divine soul of the Golden Book, and he was slightly surprised.

The Chenxing Technique had indeed been cultivated, but the imprinted Chenxing Technique and the Supplementary Heaven Technique were beginning to emit a faint golden glow.

This meant that they were missing one or two more techniques to be synthesized again! Could it be that above the imperial arts, even stronger techniques could be synthesized?

This filled Ye Xiao with great joy.

If imperial arts could continue to be synthesized, his future advancement would be remarkable.

Additionally, this also indirectly confirmed that the Supplementary Heaven Technique, Tyrant Saber, Purple Vortex Sea, Heavenly Slash, Indestructible Divine Skill, Divine Will Technique, and Grand Celestial Heavenly Formation were all imperial art-level techniques.

In the future, these other types of techniques might also be able to be merged.

However, one thing was a bit troublesome - ordinary techniques were easy to find, but imperial arts were not.

Where could he obtain some imperial arts?

This thing might not even be found in the library.

And after synthesizing one imperial art from basic techniques, no matter how many ordinary techniques he looked at, the Golden Book would not continue to record them to synthesize another imperial art.

But soon, Ye Xiao shook his head, no longer thinking too much about this matter. Whatever, the path will reveal itself when the time comes. As the saying goes, "When the river reaches the bridge, it will naturally flow straight."

Ye Xiao first came to try the Chenxing Technique.
