"Okay, okay, please come in, please come in."

City Lord Dou was somewhat troubled. These free-spirited experts are usually unwilling to be constrained by the system.

If they wanted to join the Alliance, they would have done so long ago.

But since they have not appeared, it means they are not interested in power and position.
