"Not bad today, made over 200 in just an hour, and even got a spirit stone worth several hundred. Hehe, at this rate, I can buy my son another house by the end of the year."

He carefully tucked the money and spirit stone into his pocket, asking, "What do you want?"

The two smiled slightly.

"Friend, don't be alarmed. We just want to ask you about a few things."

"You gentlemen have good eyes. I know everything that happens in this Jianghai City.

"We don't care about that. We just want to know, did a rare treasure appear in the Nine Realms last night?"

The old beggar slapped his thigh.

"Ah, I know exactly what you're talking about! There's been a great treasure found in our Nine Realms - the Perfected Treasure!"

"Perfected Treasure?!"

The two men's pupils immediately constricted, and their entire bodies broke out in a cold sweat.

They must be afraid of others discovering this Perfected Treasure.

The value of a Perfected Treasure is beyond question.

"Has the Xuanyuan Clan obtained that Perfected Treasure yet?"

"Based on my many years of experience, they definitely haven't found it yet. If they had, they wouldn't be guarding it so tightly and keeping the news secret."

The two brothers exchanged a glance, growing even more excited.

The Perfected Treasure hasn't been found yet - this is a huge stroke of luck for them! Their trip to the Xuanyuan Clan's territory wasn't in vain after all! If they can find this Perfected Treasure first, they'll be set for life!

"Oh, you gentlemen, this is an absolute secret. I'm the only one who knows about this, so you can't let anyone else find out.

"You've reminded us, of course we can't let anyone else know about this. The fewer people who know, the better."

On Ye Xiao's side, he had been reading books all day, feeling utterly speechless.

He didn't know how the Xuanyuan Clan was shielding the news, but why did it feel like everyone knew about it?

Their secrecy work was really too sloppy, wasn't it?

Ye Xiao reckoned that except for the Ning family thinking others didn't know, everyone else already knew.

What made him even more speechless was that the Jing Emperor had secretly contacted him, asking him to find a way to obtain that supreme treasure.

Saying that this item appeared in the Nine Provinces, and it was destined to belong to him.

Well, wasn't it his then? It was something he had refined himself, so who else could it belong to? It seems he couldn't casually play with the Divine Creation Art in the future.

Right now, he really wanted to further improve his cultivation.

He had originally thought that after synthesizing the third Ancient Treasure Art, his cultivation should be able to rise another level, break through to the Second Realm of the Deicide Realm, just like when he reached the Divine Realm, each time he synthesized an Ancient Treasure Art, his strength would quickly rise again.

Regrettably, this time, his cultivation didn't increase much, at least, it was far from enough to advance.

Ye Xiao estimated that it was because his cultivation had risen, and the spiritual energy required for advancement was more and stronger.

So after the synthesis of the Ancient Treasure Art, he was no longer able to advance a realm.

It seems he should use those poisons to refine some pills, and then advance.

However, this time, Ye Xiao didn't want to create something like the Great Dragon again. He considered it and decided to refine the pills separately.

That is to say, not to treat them as a whole and refine an extremely powerful pill, but to refine each poison fog separately into a pill.

This would waste a lot of time, but it would also lose a little bit.

Because in addition to the blood qi and power of the star beasts themselves, the pill refining process will also continuously absorb the spiritual energy of heaven and earth.

The higher the grade of the pill, the more spiritual energy it can absorb, and vice versa.

So in a strict sense, low-grade pills are far inferior to high-grade ones.

But there's no help for it, just refining a weapon has already attracted so much attention, if he refines pills even more impressively, creating some heavenly and earthly anomalies, who knows how many powerful experts it will attract?

So Ye Xiao used the Divine Creation Art to frantically refine pills, working while secretly refining, and perhaps because the energy gathered by the pills he refined was not too high, there was no reaction around, which made Ye Xiao feel a little relieved.

The poisons from the Divine King's Tomb were refined into more than a thousand pills.

Next, Ye Xiao could eat the pills like eating jelly beans, continuously improving his strength.

He roughly estimated that after consuming all these pills, his cultivation would at least break through to the Second Realm of the Deicide Realm within half a month to one month.

This speed is actually quite impressive. If it were someone else, even if their talent was monstrous, it would take them a few years to break through from the First Realm of the Deicide Realm to the Second Realm.

And just before he got off work, two figures quietly came to the vicinity of his house, which were Li Shixiong and his junior.

The two of them were holding a compass, and when they arrived near Ye Xiao's residence, the pointer on the compass started spinning frantically.

"The magnetic field is malfunctioning, it seems there is a very precious treasure here, we two are in luck."

"Could it be that supreme treasure?"

"It doesn't matter what it is, as long as we can get it, it will be a huge fortune. It should be underground here, let's use the Earth Traversal Technique to go down."

"Good!"

The two of them used the Earth Traversal Technique and instantly disappeared from the spot.

The next moment, "Duang~!"

"Ouch!"

"Damn it! It hurts so much!"

"Who's so mean! They've set up a formation underground! I almost broke my neck."

"Since there's a formation, it means there must be something good here. Let me use the talisman to break through this formation."

"As expected, Shixiong has good ideas. This formation is no match for the two of us."

Chuckling, Li Shixiong took out a talisman.
