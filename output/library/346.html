After the Xuanyuan Clan descended to the mortal world, household appliances could no longer be used, so Ye Xiao had thrown them all into the storage room.

"What are you getting that thing out for now?"

Huan Liuli chuckled.

"I just bought some discs, so of course I have to find the TV to watch them."

"But do you have any electricity?"

"Of course! I conveniently bought a hand-cranked generator."

Ye Xiao was rather speechless.

"Do you really have to watch that TV show?"

"Of course! It's a show I've always wanted to watch, but never had the chance. Now that I finally have the opportunity, I can't give it up!"

Ye Xiao shook his head.

"Do whatever you want, just don't make too much noise and disturb my meditation."

"Understood."

Ye Xiao went into his room to begin meditating.

Huan Liuli then started fiddling with her TV.

Soon, she had used the hand-cranked generator to power on the TV that had been gathering dust for over two years.

Huan Liuli: "!"

"After waiting all these years, I can finally continue watching this TV series. Mighty factory boss, come out!"

On the other side, Ye Xiao returned to his own room to continue cultivating.

At this moment, he suddenly discovered a strange thing - as his cultivation progressed, the dragon qi within him was no longer increasing.

Or more accurately, the spiritual energy he was absorbing was no longer increasing either.

It was as if it had reached a state of saturation, and no matter how he tried to improve, he could not reach a higher level.

Ye Xiao's brow furrowed.

"This situation seems a bit off. Even when I reached the peak of the King Realm, the half-step Emperor Realm, and exceeded my limits, I was still able to continue improving my strength.

But now I can no longer continue improving, does this mean there are limits to the Emperor Realm?"

After pondering for a moment, Ye Xiao tried another method.

He attempted to refine the dragon qi to temper his divine soul.

Sure enough, when the dragon qi began to refine the divine soul, there was finally movement within Ye Xiao.

The originally thick dragon qi, at this moment, seemed to have found an outlet, and began to frantically and continuously pour into the Golden Book divine soul.

After being nourished by this dragon qi, Ye Xiao found that the Golden Book had also begun to undergo new changes.

It had grown larger! That's right, it had grown larger, and was continuing to grow larger without limit.

Originally it was about the size of a book, but in the end, it had grown to almost the same size as Ye Xiao's own body.

And Ye Xiao's 108 protective dragon qi had disappeared completely, not a trace remaining.

Ye Xiao couldn't help but be taken aback.

That's it?

In just a few seconds?

It just grew a bit larger? No other special effects occurred? Or did it jump around a bit? It just absorbed one dragon qi and that was it? Well, this divine soul really had Ye Xiao stumped.

However, at this moment, he suddenly discovered that he could continue cultivating again.

The only difference was that while he could continue to absorb spiritual energy into his dantian, he was no longer able to form new protective dragon qi.

"How strange. That's all there was to it? No other reactions? Could it be that the so-called spiritual energy tempering of the divine soul is actually just refining the divine soul to a level close to the physical body?

Then what's next? Fusing the divine soul and physical body together? But how exactly do you fuse them together?"

Ye Xiao once again fell into confusion.

According to common sense, since the divine soul is called a divine soul, it must have a great relationship with the divine realm body.

Even at the King Realm, the human flesh and soul had already fused into one, without distinction between soul and body, only separating at the time of death to remove the soul from the physical body.

The reason for this was most likely to provide a strong enough vessel for the divine soul, so that when reaching the Divine Realm, the divine soul and divine realm body could be merged.

But now, after his divine soul had absorbed 108 protective dragon qi, there were no signs of it wanting to merge.

Could it be that his physical body was still not strong enough? Ye Xiao felt that he really needed to find a divine realm inheritance or manual to study.

He must find a way to understand the difference between the divine soul and physical body.

Time flew by, and the next morning, Ye Xiao came downstairs to see Huan Liuli sitting on the sofa, hugging her knees and looking dazed, her eyes red and swollen, as if she had been crying for a long time.

"What's wrong with you?"

Ye Xiao asked, quite puzzled.

Huan Liuli looked up at him, then began to sob softly.

"Wah, Cao Zhengchun, also known as Cao Eunuch Dog... Wah, I've always idolized him and used 'I' when talking, but it turns out he was a man who was treated that way. I'm so embarrassed after all these years."

Ye Xiao said sympathetically:

"I already told you not to watch that TV show, but..."

Jing Di took a deep breath several times, his excitement overflowing.

As for Blue Dragon, he didn't have much of a reaction.

He had already been shocked by Ye Xiao's genius time and time again.

At this moment, even if Ye Xiao had reached the Godly Monarch Realm, Blue Dragon wouldn't find it too strange.
