Ye Xiao fell silent.

It couldn't be denied that it was because of the quiet perseverance and efforts of people like them that the Nine States had been able to stand tall in the East all this time.

They could be considered the backbone of the Nine States.

While an individual may be insignificant, when many are dedicating themselves quietly for the Nine States, they will eventually uphold a towering giant.

In his previous life, Ye Xiao had greatly admired those unsung heroes and great scientists who wholeheartedly devoted themselves for their country. Now that he was in the Nine States and had some abilities, he should also contribute his meager efforts for the country.

This was not some saintly act, but the responsibility of a true son of the Nine States.

The decline of the martial arts traditions in the Nine States was indeed not good. But there would eventually be opportunities to make up for it, as the foundation was still there.

And martial arts strength could not be changed overnight.

However, the development of the Nine States' military technology was painstakingly slow. If he could contribute some military technology information, it would be a great help...
