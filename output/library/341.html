At the entrance, he encountered Liu Chengxun.

Half a year ago, Liu Chengxun had finally passed the exam and become a student of the elite class, the fourth batch of students, and was now studying at the Divine Wood Academy's branch in Jianghai City.

It is said that the elite classes in other regions are also very difficult to get into, with only one session every half a year, and each time, less than twenty people are recruited, with an average of just a few people per class.

Moreover, the admission to the elite class is not determined by cultivation level, but by talent.

So, countless people have been striving to get in.

"Ye Xiao, long time no see."

Liu Chengxun saw him from afar and greeted him.

Ye Xiao waved his hand in response.

"Aren't you supposed to be in class today? Why do you have time to come here?"

Liu Chengxun explained:

"These two days, the XuanYuan Clan and the other three clans have had some friction, and the seniors responsible for guiding the elite class have gone to discuss countermeasures, so we have been given a half-day off."

Ye Xiao raised his eyebrows slightly.

He had already heard about the friction between the XuanYuan Clan and the other three clans.

All this enmity was rooted in what Lin Qingbei had done before.

Lin Qingbei had devoured all the divine blood of the Western Tribes, and in the end, all this divine blood fell into his own body.

The other three clans currently only know that Lin Qingbei, carrying the divine blood, had perished within the boundaries of the Nine Provinces, and it was in their hands.

But they don't know who he is, so they have no way to trace it.

However, no matter how untraceable it is, they still want to retrieve that divine blood.

So much divine blood has fallen into the hands of the XuanYuan Clan, they must be very unwilling to accept this.

This is also why the Western and Southern Tribes have basically been exterminated, but the three clans behind them have not left and have been waiting in the human world, probably just to reap some benefits.

Ye Xiao couldn't be bothered with these matters.

The divine blood he obtained back then was all through his own efforts.

Why should he return it? If they want to find it, they should go find Lin Qingbei, find the young master behind Lin Qingbei, he didn't directly snatch it from them.

"Anyway, how have you been doing recently?"

"Pretty good, my cultivation has reached the peak of the Xiantian Ninth Grade, just one step away from breaking through to the King Realm.

My old friends have also been doing very well.

My previous disciple, Pu Jiansheng, is now a Grand Master and is preparing to take the elite class exam.

And Fa Zheng, he has just broken through to the Xiantian Seventh Grade, the Divine Sect Realm. He is also preparing to take the elite class exam.

Other friends have also improved to varying degrees, especially your friend Qin Yuyan. She has also reached the Xiantian Seventh Grade Divine Sect Realm, and has just been admitted to the elite class. After I graduate this session, she will be taking over."

Ye Xiao nodded.

The progress of everyone is generally quite good.

Of course, compared to himself, there is still a huge gap.

The One Hundred and Eight Dragons of the Emperor Realm, although it is not exaggerated to the point where each dragon requires the cultivation of an Emperor Realm One Dragon, it is still much more difficult than cultivating the King Realm.

Ye Xiao has already surpassed the distance between the King Realm and the Emperor Realm by eighty or ninety, while their strength is still within the Xiantian Realm.

He really doesn't know if in the future, after hundreds or thousands of years, his strength will become too powerful, and he will live too long.
