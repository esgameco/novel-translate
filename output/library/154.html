"Master, should we eat something else? You only had a little of the fried skewers before throwing them away."

"You can eat, I don't have much appetite."

Ye Xiao found a park bench to sit down on, took out a bottle of water from his storage ring, cleared his throat, and then began to cast a disguise spell.

After all, this was not his hometown of Jianghai City, but Jiuzhou, a place where talented people abounded. It would be best not to expose his identity here if possible.

He had read some information before.

In Jiuzhou, there were some top-tier experts whose strength was not inferior to the peak of the Divine Sect.

In the World Dragon Ranking, Liu Chengxun and Murakami Hitomi only occupied the 49th and 50th places respectively.

But in Jiuzhou alone, there were as many as seventeen people occupying the Dragon and Phoenix Rankings.

Most of them were ranked near the top of the rankings.

Like the Sword Sovereign Qinglong, ranked first in the World Dragon Ranking.

The Fire Blade Zhuque, ranked first in the World Phoenix Ranking.

The Thunder Tiger Baihu, ranked third in the World Phoenix Ranking.

The Diamond Xuanwu, ranked third in the World Dragon Ranking.

A little lower were the Sword Saint Lu Qingshan, ranked 10th in the World Dragon Ranking, and the Sword God Li Liushui, ranked 9th.

In addition, there were several more in the 10th to 20th and 20th to 30th intervals.

It was said that in the past, there were even more of these powerful experts, numerous as the hairs on a cow, to the point of being able to look down on other states. With such a powerful martial arts culture, it's no wonder the other states targeted them! Because they were too strong, completely unrivaled. They had already posed a fatal threat to the others.

It's not that Jiuzhou was wrong, but human hearts are wicked. When you are too dazzling, it's inevitable that you will attract envy.

Now he was the same.

In this world where people only reach the Divine Sect realm after nearly a hundred years of cultivation, a Divine Sect cultivator in his early twenties, who could guarantee that others wouldn't have any ideas about him?

Normally, in the small town of Jianghai City, he didn't have to worry about encountering any powerful experts. But now that he had come out, he naturally had to be more careful.

After Ye Xiao finished casting the disguise spell, he suddenly found a three-year-old little girl standing in front of him, staring at him with wide eyes, full of wonder.

"You must have seen wrong."

"But I just saw you turn into another person!"

"Here, just pretend you didn't see anything, okay? You can't tell anyone about this."

The little girl, now excited after receiving the candy, nodded earnestly.

"Okay."

"Then let's make a pinky promise, and it will last a hundred years without breaking."

The little girl very solemnly hooked her pinky with Ye Xiao's, as if they had just made an important contract.

"Where's your mom? Why are you alone?"

"Mommy went to the restroom."

Just as she said that, a young, attractive mother hurriedly came out of the restroom in the distance, calling out loudly:

"Xiao Yi, where did you go?"

"Mommy, I'm here."

The little girl waved her small hand, and the young mother quickly came over. She glanced at Ye Xiao, then at the candy in her daughter Xiao Yi's hand, and immediately spoke:

"Xiao Yi, did you take something from someone again?"

She then apologized to Ye Xiao while asking her daughter to return the candy.

"I'm very sorry, my daughter is a bit mischievous. Xiao Yi, quickly return the candy to the uncle."

But Xiao Yi hid her hand behind her back, refusing to give it back.

"No, this is the candy the uncle gave me."

Ye Xiao smiled and said, "The candy really is something I gave her."

The mother then stopped her actions and thanked Ye Xiao instead.

"Thank you for giving my daughter the candy."

"You're welcome, she's very cute."

"Xiao Yi, did you thank the uncle?"
