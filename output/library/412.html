If one cannot endure, they will never be able to eat the final, fattest prey.

The Blood Butterflies were quickly advancing forward, while Ye Xiao calmly followed behind them with his hands clasped behind his back.

This made him raise his eyebrows slightly.

Ye Xiao narrowed his eyes, put on his cloak, and slowly stepped into the manor.

Meanwhile, in the deepest part of the Ning family manor, in the room where the obsidian coffin excavated from the Divine King's Tomb was stored, a faint and unusual fluctuation suddenly emerged.

Crack, crack, one of the huge obsidian coffins began to emit cracks.

Shortly after, a few drops of fresh blood seeped out from within.

The blood dripped onto the ground and merged with the Blood Butterflies emerging from the earth, forming a figure with a completely black body but with a white crescent moon mark on its back.

This voice caught the attention of the Ning family disciples guarding outside.

"Who's in there?"

A few figures rushed in, and upon seeing the Alpha King, they were immediately startled.

"Who are you?"

The Alpha King's mouth curled up slightly, his eyes revealing a greedy look.

He did not respond to their words, but merely stomped his foot, instantly transforming into a residual image and disappearing from the spot.

He reappeared in front of the crowd.

"So fast!"

The crowd's pupils constricted, and they couldn't help but exclaim in surprise, but before they could resist, the Alpha King struck like lightning, and in the blink of an eye, he had sent several people to their deaths.

These people were all Deity Realm cultivators, but in the face of the Alpha King, they were utterly powerless.
