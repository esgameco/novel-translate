When the aura reached its peak, Liu Chengxun finally made his move.

He used his finger as a sword, unleashing a sword strike.

In an instant, above his head, a dazzling lightning bolt suddenly erupted, causing all eyes to focus and all breathing to pause.

The powerful sword intent, with an overbearing posture, struck towards the girl.

In this moment, the wind on the river surface seemed to be suppressed, unable to howl.

In this moment, the clouds in the sky also seemed to be frozen, unable to drift.

Between heaven and earth, it was as if only Liu Chengxun's sword strike remained.

One sword strike, like a sudden burst of icy light, arriving in an instant, occupying almost all the advantage and superiority!

Its body first began to emit a golden light, then its power, in an instant, reached a stronger aura than Liu Chengxun's, instantly surpassing him.

And when its aura reached its peak, it charged straight at Liu Chengxun like a bolt of lightning.

Without any preparatory moves like Liu Chengxun, it was just a flash of golden light, simple and casual.

Then the sword shot out like a dragon, piercing the heavens.

Boom! In the instant it appeared, it directly collided with Liu Chengxun's sword beam, and then ruthlessly shattered Liu Chengxun's sword beam, soaring into the sky! The sword beam slashed through the sky, breaking through all of Liu Chengxun's aura!

In this moment, the wind and floating clouds that had been suppressed by Liu Chengxun's aura seemed to be unsealed.

The wind began to blow again.
