Time quickly passed, and it was now night. Ye Xiao was lying on the bed, staring blankly at the ceiling.

The rabbit meat had been completely eaten, leaving only the bones, star pearls, and the soul, which he had stored in his storage ring.

This way, when he returned to the surface, he could revive her and then place her in the breeding farm to continue shearing her wool.

Humans must take the path of sustainable development. Only the path of sustainable development can ensure success.

Unfortunately, Brocade Jade Rabbit's star core did not contain any suitable cultivation methods, only a movement technique, and Ye Xiao currently did not need the movement technique, so it was completely useless.

He didn't know when the Heavenly Emperor's inheritance would be unlocked. Although there were only a few days left, it was still somewhat boring here.

While feeling bored, Ye Xiao's eyes suddenly narrowed.

Someone had arrived.

Accompanied by a slight creaking sound from the window, moonlight poured in, and with the moonlight came a long, slender leg!

It was very long, at least 1.2 meters.

It was also very white, reflecting the moonlight outside and emitting a faint glow.

Ye Xiao's gaze couldn't help but flicker.

So long!

A gentle breeze blew in, carrying a faint, lingering fragrance that entered Ye Xiao's nostrils.

So fragrant!

This scent, without even needing to add any seasoning oil, or even any spices, just slow-braising the meat until it's tender, then sprinkling a little black pepper and cilantro, and squeezing out a few drops of lemon juice to add a refreshing taste and reduce the oiliness, would absolutely make a delicious soup.

The window was fully opened, and the graceful figure and cold, beautiful face of the Crane Immortal Maiden were revealed to Ye Xiao's eyes.

She glanced at the burning desire in Ye Xiao's eyes and a trace of disdain and contempt flashed across her expression.

Of course, men are all useless. Normally, with her temperament, she wouldn't even glance at a man like Ye Xiao.

But at this moment, she had no choice. In order to find the answers, she had to sacrifice her pride.

Thinking about this, she forced herself to squeeze out a faint smile, easily stepping the other leg into the room and slowly approaching Ye Xiao.

"Little human brother, all alone here, feeling lonely? Shall sister keep you company and play with you?"

As soon as she finished speaking, her eyes were suddenly enveloped by a dazzling light.

The blinding light made her involuntarily squint her eyes, and then she inexplicably felt a sharp pain in her neck, and her head heavily crashed to the ground.

Crane Immortal Maiden: "?"

A deep sense of bewilderment enveloped her heart until she saw a headless crane body lying next to her, and she finally understood - she had been killed! She had actually been killed? And so quickly?

This had to be a joke, right? She was a half-step Spiritual Beast, comparable to a half-step Monarch realm expert in the human realm. Yet she had been so easily and swiftly killed? And she hadn't even seen her opponent's attack coming!

How could the speed be so fast? No, wait a minute, she suddenly realized something.

Why was there a human in the Silver Fox Valley? Even though the relationship between the Silver Fox Clan and humans was relatively good, it was still unlikely for a human to appear there so casually.

Apart from that, there was another very important thing.

That is, the vixen of the Fox Queen, seemed to have great respect for this human.

If it was just some pretty boy she was keeping, no matter what, she wouldn't have such deep respect.

That means this human must be extremely powerful, powerful enough to command her deep respect, even though she was a half-step Spiritual Beast, comparable to a half-step Monarch realm expert.

If even she had such great respect for him, how strong must he be? Monarch realm? Or even beyond Monarch realm?

In that instant, the Crane Immortal Maiden felt incredibly stupid, no, they were all stupid!

If her guess was correct, Brocade Jade Rabbit had most likely also suffered a tragic end.

This man was terrifyingly powerful, and even more terrifying was that he didn't seem to like women at all, treating them as prey!

Why did she have to provoke such a monstrous existence? If she hadn't provoked him, she could have at least lived well.

Unfortunately, there are no "ifs" in this world.

She could no longer continue thinking, as her consciousness gradually sank into the darkness and disappeared without a trace.

In contrast to her shock and sorrow, Ye Xiao was feeling quite excited.

"Strange, my luck seems to have been pretty good lately. Whatever I think of seems to just come to me."
