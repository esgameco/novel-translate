Until Ye Xiao slowly descended, the crowd had not fully reacted yet.

The Vixen Queen was slightly surprised and said, "Mr. Ye, just who are you exactly?"

Ye Xiao was rather speechless.

"There's no need to be so shocked, I'm still me, Ning Yuhen's friend. You just need to remember this."

After hearing these words, the Vixen Queen heaved a sigh of relief, and her inner shock turned into joy.

Ye Xiao was right, no matter what kind of strength he had, as long as he was Ning Yuhen's friend, that was enough.

Moreover, for Ning Yuhen to have such a powerful friend was truly fortunate! "Mr. Ye is right. Mr. Ye, the Imperial Relic Site has now fully manifested, you and Yuhen can use your blood to activate and open this Imperial Relic Site."

Ye Xiao nodded and looked at Ning Yuhen, who immediately ran over.

"Master."

"Give me a drop of your blood."

"Okay!"

Ye Xiao willed it, and from Ning Yuhen's fingertip, he took a drop of fresh blood, and also took a drop of his own.

Then, he dripped the two drops of blood into the gaps of the two large doors of the Imperial Relic Site.

After receiving these two different drops of blood, the next moment, the formation on the doors of the Imperial Relic Site began to rapidly radiate golden light, endlessly spreading outwards.

The next moment, the doors slowly opened, as if they had been granted permission.

From within the doors, an ancient and rustic aura burst forth, filled with boundless spiritual energy.

All the people present felt this rich spiritual energy, and their pores all opened up, their cultivation methods automatically starting to run, frantically absorbing the energy within.

"Master, let's go."

Ning Yuhen spoke, and Ye Xiao nodded. Ning Yuhen then took the lead and stepped inside.

But he had only taken a few steps when he had to stop in his tracks.

Because the spiritual energy gushing out from within was simply too much, like a tidal wave surging outwards.

With his innate Saint Monarch cultivation, he was unable to break through this spiritual energy and enter the relic.

The Silver Fox Clan members were not only worried, but also shocked and terrified.

"Such powerful spiritual energy!"

"How terrifying! This Imperial Realm expert has been dead for three thousand years, yet he can still unleash such thick spiritual energy, it's simply unbelievable."

"Is this the legendary Imperial Realm expert? Even after death, his strength is still so powerful! Then how strong would a living Imperial Realm expert be? They must be truly extraordinary!"

"No wonder they are called Imperial Realm experts? Among all the beings in the starry sky, how many can reach the Imperial Realm level?"

That a dead Imperial Realm expert's spiritual energy could prevent a Saint Monarch from entering his relic completely overturned everyone's worldview.

It seems that if one's strength is not enough, even if they encounter a great opportunity and come across the legendary Imperial Realm inheritance, they can only watch with dry eyes and be unable to obtain it.

Just as Ning Yuhen was at an impasse, Ye Xiao stepped forward.

Boom! In an instant, the spiritual energy that collided with Ye Xiao's body was split into two and gushed out in different directions.

He walked up to Ning Yuhen and helped him withstand the assault of the Imperial Realm spiritual energy.

"Walk behind me."

This calm sentence and his towering figure instantly calmed the young sapling that was Ning Yuhen in the raging wind.

He smiled slightly and nodded, silently following behind Ye Xiao.

After the two entered the doors, they thunderously closed, indicating that the Imperial Relic Site could only accommodate two people at a time.

Inside the Imperial Relic Site, there were no fancy or extravagant things, unlike the legends in novels where there were divine weapons and treasures everywhere.

Thinking about it, truly powerful experts are too lazy to carry around important items.

Useful heavenly materials and earthly treasures, they would just consume them directly. As for weapons, unless they were of the highest quality, they would not bother to bring them.

Moreover, most experts would only cultivate one or two martial arts, so they wouldn't have many weapons.

However, despite this, due to the thick spiritual energy nourishing this place for three thousand years, some good things had still been produced.

Ning Yuhen couldn't help but exclaim:

"Master, look, that Blood Spirit Fungus is so big, almost twenty to thirty square meters!

And over there, that Soul Spirit Grass is over ten meters long.

Heavens! If these were outside, others would fight tooth and nail to snatch them."

Ye Xiao calmly said:

"These are just minor things, the real treasures are the inheritances here. You and I need to comprehend different inheritances. I can feel that the inheritance of the Human Realm Imperial expert is on the left, while the inheritance of the Silver Fox Clan's ancestor is on the right. You go to the right."

"Yes!"

The two did not bother with the so-called heavenly materials and earthly treasures, but instead went to seek the inheritances.

Ning Yuhen quickly headed to the right, while Ye Xiao instantly teleported to the left position.
