The two figures following behind saw Ye Xiao walk into the small alley. They exchanged a glance.

"This idiot actually delivered himself right to us."

They had been worrying, as there were people all around and it was not a good opportunity to make a move. But now Ye Xiao had walked right into the alley.

One of them sneered, "Let me go greet him and see if I can get some information out of him."

"Alright! But be quick about it, don't make too much commotion and attract the powerful experts of the XuanYuan Clan."

"Don't worry, he's just a minor character. It'll be easy pickings for me, like catching chickens and killing dogs. It's nothing noteworthy."

His companion didn't say much more. It was clear he agreed with the assessment.

He watched as the man followed Ye Xiao into the alley, while he himself waited alone on the street.

A few seconds later, he saw Ye Xiao emerge from the alley, unharmed. He was dumbfounded.

What was going on? Where was his companion? Why did Ye Xiao come out alone without being interrogated?

At that moment, Ye Xiao glanced at him.

In that single glance, he felt his spiritual power instantly spiral out of control.

Boom! In a flash, he was reduced to a mist of blood.

Even in the moment of his death, he could hardly believe it.

He must have encountered a ghost. Otherwise, how could he have been instantly killed like that?

This thought only lasted half a second, because half a second later, his soul was also reduced to shreds by Ye Xiao's Demon Eyes.

Any enemy who had seen Ye Xiao in action and laid eyes on his face would have to die! Although it had only been a few seconds, Ye Xiao had completely grasped the reason for their presence.

The good news was, they still didn't know about the existence of the Divine King's Tomb.

However, the recent commotion of the XuanYuan Clan had caused some vigilance in the Western Three Tribes, so they had sent spies to investigate the situation in the Lower Nine Heavens.

They had not only placed spies here, but also inserted informants in other places.

Unfortunately, while scanning the opponent's spiritual power, Ye Xiao had also obtained their list of names.

The rest was simple. Ye Xiao directly edited a few messages and transmitted them to Beitang Ce using a Thought Stone.

Let Beitang Ce and his people take care of these guys. After all, they were spies from the other three clans, so they couldn't be left alive.

After receiving the message, Beitang Ce immediately responded to Ye Xiao and began taking action. Under Ye Xiao's name, he arranged for people to search for the spies, so Ye Xiao's identity wouldn't be exposed.

Afterwards, Ye Xiao continued to go to the library to read books.

However, in less than half an hour, a message came from Emperor Jing's Thought Stone.

It only had three words: "Trouble has occurred."

Ye Xiao's eyes narrowed, and he immediately connected with Emperor Jing.

"What happened?"

Emperor Jing's tone was somewhat heavy.

"Beitang Ce has been seriously injured, his eyes gouged out."

Ye Xiao's pupils constricted, and a cold murderous intent instantly surged in his heart.

"Why did this happen? Didn't he go after the spies with people from the XuanYuan Clan?"

"That's not the case. He went with people from the XuanYuan Clan. But as you know, most of the XuanYuan Clan's experts are currently in the Starry Sky. The defense force on the ground is very weak.

The opponent was an Empyrean Realm 27 Dragon expert.

The Xuanyuan Clan also lost two Empyrean Realm experts, and the opponent has already fled back to the Starry Sky. If my guess is correct, he should have already escaped by now."

Ye Xiao clenched his fists, the bones cracking, and the murderous intent in his eyes solidified.

He had been careless.

He hadn't expected that the Xuanyuan Clan had actually withdrawn the majority of their powerful experts to the Starry Sky, weakening the ground defense.

It couldn't be entirely blamed on him, though. The intelligence he had obtained indicated that the opponents were only at the early Empyrean Realm, not an Empyrean Realm 27 Dragon expert.

It seems that among the three clans, they were also harboring malicious intentions towards each other, even deceiving the spies they sent about their true strength.

So the information he obtained by scanning their spiritual power was inaccurate.

"Take good care of Senior Beitang."

"Ye Xiao, don't be rash. I'll immediately notify the Xuanuan Clan. They have high-level experts, Saint Realm experts, who can definitely handle this. You absolutely must not go there yourself. If anything happens to you, that would be a true massive loss for us.

Besides, the spies weren't sent by you, so you don't need to take it too much to heart."

Ye Xiao coldly said, "Even though the spies weren't sent by me, I was the one who notified Senior Beitang. If I hadn't informed him, he wouldn't have encountered this.

Moreover, the Demon Fire Spirit Eyes are a supreme treasure.

When they were in Senior Beitang's possession, the Xuanuan Clan might not have dared to make a move.

But now that they've been taken, if the Xuanuan Clan seizes them back, they may very well keep them for themselves.

When you notify the Xuanuan Clan, don't mention that Senior Beitang's eyes have been taken.

Let me see if I have a chance to retrieve them."
