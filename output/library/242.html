There, they were all powerful grandmaster-level experts.

It was precisely because of the existence of those powerful experts that the ones above did not dare to take a step further down, to cultivate in the lower regions.

But now, they were all exterminated by a single human in just over ten seconds - it was truly terrifying.

Fortunately, they were quite wise earlier and did not attack that human.

Otherwise, it would have been them who died at this moment.

In fact, Ye Xiao had indeed killed all the star beasts that attacked him, around one or two hundred of them.

If they had not come in batches but all together, Ye Xiao would not have even needed ten seconds.

A single thought of his could instantly slay all these star beasts.

Of course, these one or two hundred star beasts were not the only grand-master level star beasts in this Tianhuo Cliff.

The star beasts in this Tianhuo Cliff were truly too many.

Ye Xiao even had the idea of turning this place into his own breeding ground. After all, there were so many star beasts here, with a complete variety and abundant resources.

If he could turn this into a breeding ground, wouldn't he be able to have a sumptuous feast every day? That would be far better than just eating barbecue, roasted meat, and fried eggs, right?

Maybe he could even open a restaurant.

Tianhuo Cliff Farmhouse Delicacy.

In this world of cultivators, a star beast restaurant is also an extremely popular and profitable business.

After all, every cultivator needs to replenish their vital energy.

As long as they don't eat the star cores of the star beasts, there is no problem at all.

It's just like eating ordinary meat, no different from eating domestic poultry.

If one had to give it a definition, it would be wild game versus domestic.

The former is actually more advantageous.

But thinking about it, he's not really interested in money, so what's the use of a profitable restaurant? Moreover, the ordinary star beasts can indeed be eaten.

But not the ones here.

The star beasts here are not just simple energy bodies like spirit herbs.

They have long been corroded by the evil Qi, from their souls to their physical bodies, from the inside out, they have all been contaminated.

If one eats them casually, they might even contract some unclean diseases.

So it's better to be cautious, just refine them into medicinal pills.

Ye Xiao collected their star cores and flesh, but not too much, after all, he still needs to leave space for the grand feast below.

After descending another ten thousand zhang, Ye Xiao finally arrived at the bottom of the Tianhuo Cliff.

Down here, it was all white bones, as if it had become an ocean of accumulated oil-white bones, unfathomable in depth!

Among them were human bones and star beast bones.

Many of the bones were still fresh, one can imagine how many brutal events must happen here every day.

These star beasts were completely savage, whether it was humans or their own kind, as long as they felt hungry, they would not spare anyone.

Ye Xiao could even sense several powerful auras down below.

Each one was not weaker than the Xiantian Sixth-Grade.

They were all Divine Patriarchs.

This made Ye Xiao's eyes light up, revealing an excited expression.

If he could gather all these powerful star beasts and refine them into medicinal pills, how wonderful would that be?

With so much energy, it should be enough for him to break through to the final realm, right?

Thinking of this, Ye Xiao released his powerful blood Qi energy.

He was baiting them.

Using himself as bait to lure these powerful star beasts to approach him.

The scent of a human, for star beasts, is irresistible.

Especially for a very powerful human, with his strong and abundant blood Qi, it is simply a fatal attraction to the star beasts!

It could drive them to madness with envy.

Devouring Ye Xiao could very likely propel them to an even higher level of cultivation.

The moment Ye Xiao released his aura, he sensed several powerful auras approaching, their spiritual senses already searching for his location.

Ye Xiao stood proudly above the sea of bones, the Taixuan Tiangong rotating at a frequency of over a thousand times per second, quietly waiting for the arrival of those star beasts.

Two hundred kilometers away, a blood lake with floating white skulls suddenly erupted, and a huge head emerged, thicker than a truck.

It was a massive Heavenly Python.

It was frantically flicking its huge, deep red, and even slightly purple forked tongue, frantically sniffing Ye Xiao's blood Qi in the air, and letting out a series of hissing sounds.

That was it frantically absorbing massive amounts of air, creating a vacuum area due to the sudden absorption, causing this phenomenon.

"The scent of a human! This blood Qi, so rich! This human, I must have him!"

Three hundred kilometers to the southwest, a huge blue wolf roared as it charged out of a pile of bone mountains, its eerie green pupils flashing ominously in the darkness.

"Such powerful blood Qi! If I can devour this fellow, I will surely be able to step into the Xiantian Ninth-Grade."

In the north, a gale flashed in the darkness, and a massive vulture-like star beast landed on a bone mountain.

"Mingdie, have you caught the scent of that powerful blood Qi?"
