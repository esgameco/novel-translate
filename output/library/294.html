"Grandmother, why did you suddenly summon us here?"

The Vixen Queen's face was grave as she said, "Trouble has occurred. The secret of the Imperial Realm inheritance may have been discovered by someone else."

As soon as she finished speaking, Ning Yuhen and Ye Xiao's faces both tensed up.

"How could this happen? Who leaked the information?"

The Vixen Queen sighed deeply.

"It was those few of them. I don't know how they found out, but the secret has been exposed.

This morning when I went to find them, they flew away without a word, and I didn't even have time to intercept them.

If I'm guessing correctly, they will soon bring other powerful experts here."

"What? What are we going to do?"

Ning Yuhen was somewhat anxious, while Ye Xiao was slightly taken aback. He seemed to understand why those spirit beasts had fled.

Likely, it was because they were terrified after Ye Xiao had slain Liangluo Yuzhu and Hesenzi. That must have been a misunderstanding.

Just as Ye Xiao was about to open his mouth to try and ease their tension, the Vixen Queen spoke up again with a grave expression:

"The only solution now is to utilize the secret techniques of my Silver Fox Clan to forcibly activate the Imperial Realm ruins, so that you two can enter the ruins ahead of time and retrieve the inheritance.

That way, even if we encounter some trouble, as long as the inheritance is not lost, we don't need to worry."

Hearing this, the words of comfort that Ye Xiao had been about to say got stuck in his throat.

Ning Yuhen exclaimed excitedly:

"Grandmother, if you have a way to forcibly activate the Imperial Realm ruins, why didn't you do it earlier?"

The Vixen Queen smiled wryly.

"That is an Imperial Realm ruin after all, it's not as easy as just saying we'll activate it. Do you think your grandmother is an Imperial Realm expert?

Previously, the time for it to fully manifest was still far away, so activating it was difficult. Now, it's almost completely manifested, so the activation should be much more effective.

And at first, I didn't even know we had a technique to activate it. It's only been these past few days that I learned from the inheritance passed down in the Silver Fox Clan that we have an ancestral formation technique specifically for activating this Imperial Realm ruin."

"I see."

The Vixen Queen's expression turned serious as she said:

"Time is of the essence now. I will summon the Clan Patriarchs of the Silver Fox Clan to join hands and begin the formation, accelerating the manifestation of the Imperial Realm ruin.

Once the ruin is manifested, you two will immediately use your blood to open the entrance and enter it."

"Alright!"

They quickly arrived at the location of the Imperial Realm ruin.

Ye Xiao's spiritual sense had not detected this place before, perhaps because it was a true Imperial Realm expert's ruin.

Now, he was seeing this Imperial Realm ruin for the first time.

Overall, it was constructed entirely of bronze, shaped somewhat like an ancient cauldron, covered in formations, all of which were Imperial Formations, and emanating an Imperial Aura that was difficult to suppress, even with Ye Xiao's Yin-Yang Escape Art.

Although the Yin-Yang Escape Art was a divine movement technique, the key was still the level of the practitioner.

If the practitioner's level was strong enough, even an ordinary technique could unleash earth-shattering power. But if the practitioner's level was not high enough, even the best techniques could not be used to their full potential.

Ye Xiao was not yet an Imperial Realm expert, while the former King Zhou had once been a veteran Imperial Realm expert.

Even with the Yin-Yang Escape Art empowering him, Ye Xiao could only surpass his level to a certain extent, not to the extreme.

After briefly instructing the group, the Vixen Queen immediately led the Clan Patriarchs of the Silver Fox Clan to begin deploying the formation.

They transformed into their massive fox spirit beast bodies, each the size of a small mountain, containing terrifying power that would make even peers from the human race tremble in awe.

The fox elders took the formation of the Nine Dragon Cauldron, unleashing their power to activate the formation.

As the formation was activated, the spiritual energy of heaven and earth began to converge towards it.

Under the formation's enhancement, the partially manifested Imperial Realm ruin also began to accelerate its manifestation.

Howling winds, and faintly visible were several purple dragons swimming on top of the giant cauldron, as if they were pulling it out from another dimension.

The so-called Imperial Realm had reached the point where it could place its inheritance in a separate dimension from the current world - a terrifying ability that was a hallmark of the Imperial Realm.

The Imperial Realm was the ultimate pinnacle of all powers in the human race.

Rumble... At this moment, a thundercloud suddenly formed in the sky above, as if the heavens themselves were wary of the appearance of this ruin.

All the Silver Fox Clan members were extremely tense, their hearts pounding in their throats.
