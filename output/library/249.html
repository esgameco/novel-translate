"Fuck!"

"How long has it been? How could this guy have already broken through to that realm? Is he even human? Even if he's a human with exceptional talent, it's normally impossible for him to break through to that realm so quickly, without decades of cultivation, right?"

"Is this guy even human? You're still treating him as a human? You're so naively adorable. I stopped seeing him as human a long time ago."

"Oh no, oh no, even if we cultivate for six thousand more years, we might still not be able to escape from his grasp? Realm King experts not only have immense strength, but their lifespans have also risen to a new peak, with thousands of years at their disposal."

This statement made the crowd start to feel pessimistic.

No wonder he wanted to help them improve their cultivation - they had wondered why he was being so kind, not afraid they would just run away after getting stronger.

The reason was clear now.

Firstly, his own strength had already increased tremendously, so he had no fear of them escaping.

Secondly, with his enhanced strength, their flesh and blood were probably no longer enough to sustain him, so he needed to boost the strength of himself and the other star beasts to have more robust flesh and blood to aid his cultivation.

But just then, the Immortal Celestial Elder suddenly spoke up with a sigh:

"Haven't you all considered another possibility?

If he can boost his own cultivation so quickly, he can surely rapidly improve us star beasts as well.

That means, even though he'll be taking our flesh and blood, if our strength increases greatly, our lifespans will also become longer than his.

If he dies, we'll still be alive.

And by then, our strength may even surpass that of divine beasts!"

These words made the eyes of the star beasts suddenly light up, and some even started breathing more heavily.

They had been so shocked by Ye Xiao's cultivation speed that they had momentarily forgotten another crucial point.

Ye Xiao had said that as long as they offered enough, he could help them break through their current cultivation realms.

For them, this was an extremely lucrative method of cultivation improvement.

After all, for them, improving their cultivation was as difficult as ascending to the heavens. If they had to rely on themselves, they might only be able to increase their strength by one or two realms in thousands of years, let alone breaking through to the divine beast realm.

But with Ye Xiao's cultivation speed and talent, everyone had seen how remarkable he was.

So he must have a way to rapidly boost their strength.

And they didn't need to worry that Ye Xiao would help them improve their cultivation just for the sake of it, because Ye Xiao needed their stronger flesh and blood to further enhance his own power.

This was an unavoidable necessity.

Faced with a method that could definitely increase their strength, if all they had to sacrifice was some of their flesh and blood, what was there to worry about?

After all, they might never be able to reach the divine beast realm through their own cultivation in their entire lifetimes.

Now, all they had to do was cut off their tentacles and hooves every day - it couldn't be simpler!

It was practically a free ride to success.

In this moment, the star beasts looked at each other, all understanding the gleam in each other's eyes.

They had decided - they must continue being this "poultry" for the long haul.

"I've decided, I'll be this poultry for at least ten thousand years! Within these ten thousand years, unless he drives me away, I absolutely will not leave," Noel Fist spoke up first.

Sameul quickly followed suit.

"Well said. Ten thousand years is nothing for us star beasts.

It's just the blink of an eye.

And in this breeding ground, we don't have to worry about safety at all.

As long as our safety is guaranteed, we star beasts can live peacefully here, and with our cultivation improving, our lifespans will be extended even further.

Ten thousand years is nothing."

"Good! It's just ten thousand years, I, Ji Feng, will see it through to the end!"

Susan quickly raised her hand.

"Me too! Me too!"

"Count me in as well."

"Alright then, it's settled. Within these ten thousand years, we'll all be good poultry, and no one is allowed to run away, lest we incur the displeasure of our master and he stops helping us improve our cultivation."

"Right! Whoever dares to run away, the rest of us will band together to kill him."

"If any other star beasts come in the future, we should try our best to persuade them not to cause trouble, so as not to upset our master."

"Yes, yes, yes, we should form a group and strive to become the strongest poultry in the world."

The star beasts looked at each other and burst into hearty laughter.

Ye Xiao noticed that Director Ouyang was no longer standing at the top, but sitting with the others.

According to her previous words, after they returned from their cultivation trip, she would no longer be the director.
